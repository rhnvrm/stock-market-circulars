## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a circular clarifying the process for early pay-in (EPI) of securities of Mangalam Worldwide Limited (MWL) in connection with the company's face value split (sub-division) from Rs 10 per share to Re 1 per share. This is a follow-up to Item 10.18 of the consolidated circular on Early Pay-in of Securities for Margin Exemption (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/73996) dated April 30, 2026. Members and custodians undertaking early pay-in for MWL around the record date/ex-date of July 10, 2026 must use the existing/old ISIN (INE0JYY01011) for trades executed before the record date, without adjusting quantities for the face value conversion.

## Key Points

- Applies to Mangalam Worldwide Limited (Symbol: MWL), undergoing a face value split from Rs 10/- per share to Re 1/- per share.
- Record Date and Ex Date for the corporate action: July 10, 2026.
- Members/custodians must perform early pay-in in the depository system using the old/existing ISIN (INE0JYY01011), without adjusting for the split, for trades in the applicable settlement type.
- Example given: for a sale quantity of 10 shares (post-split), the early pay-in in the depository should be done in the old ISIN for a quantity of 1 share (pre-split equivalent).
- Market participants must adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022 on the SEBI Block Mechanism for client demat accounts undertaking sale transactions; the client-wise early pay-in allocation file should not be uploaded when using block mechanism for early pay-in.
- Custodians providing early pay-in through a pool account must upload client-wise early pay-in allocation details based on actual traded quantity to pass on the benefit to clients.
- After the record date, market participants/custodians must execute pay-in/early pay-in instructions using the New ISIN in the depositories.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this circular operationalizes the existing early pay-in for margin exemption framework (NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) specifically for the MWL face value split corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must ensure early pay-in for MWL is executed in the old ISIN prior to the record date, correctly adjusted for the face value split ratio.
- Custodians using pool accounts must upload accurate client-wise allocation files reflecting actual traded (post-split) quantities.
- Entities using block mechanism for early pay-in must follow NCL/CMPT/53386 and refrain from uploading client-wise allocation files in that scenario.
- Post record date, all pay-in instructions must shift to the New ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex Date: July 10, 2026
- EPI Date July 10, 2026: Settlement Number 2026128 (Old/Existing ISIN)
- EPI Date July 13, 2026: Settlement Numbers 2026128 & 2026129 (New ISIN)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and narrowly scoped to clearing members, custodians, and PCMs handling MWL securities around the face value split. Correct adherence to ISIN usage (old vs. new) across the record date is critical to avoid settlement failures or mismatches in early pay-in processing. No broader market-wide impact is expected beyond entities holding or trading MWL shares.
