## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in privately placed Non-Convertible Securities of three companies effective June 1, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspensions are due to redemption of the respective instruments.

## Key Points

- Trading in three privately placed NCS instruments will be suspended from June 1, 2026
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the securities
- The circular is issued under NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74474 / Circular Ref. No: 0875/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notification under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members must cease trading in the following securities from June 1, 2026:

| Company | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited | INE0L6807088 | June 1, 2026 | Redemption |
| Sammaan Capital Limited | INE148I07EW5 | June 1, 2026 | Redemption |
| Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited | INE507T07062 | June 1, 2026 | Redemption |

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 1, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective for all three securities

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. These are privately placed non-convertible securities being suspended ahead of their redemption date. Holders of these instruments should note the trading suspension and expect redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No broader market disruption is expected.
