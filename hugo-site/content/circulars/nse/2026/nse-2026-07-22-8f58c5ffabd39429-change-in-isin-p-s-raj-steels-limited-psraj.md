## Summary

NSE has informed members that the ISIN code for the equity shares of P S Raj Steels Limited (symbol: PSRAJ) will change effective July 24, 2026, as a result of the sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- per share to Rs. 2/- per share.

## Key Points

- The ISIN for P S Raj Steels Limited (PSRAJ) will change to INE0XUS01020.
- The change is due to a stock split (sub-division) of face value from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share.
- The new ISIN becomes effective for all trades done on and from the ex-date.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational/administrative update reflecting a corporate action (stock split) already approved by the company; no new regulatory policy is introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange must update their trading and settlement systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE0XUS01020) for PSRAJ from the effective date to ensure accurate trade processing and settlement.

## Important Dates

- Effective date/Ex-date: July 24, 2026 — new ISIN applies to all trades from this date onward.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to P S Raj Steels Limited and its shareholders/traders. Members must ensure systems recognize the new ISIN to avoid settlement mismatches. The underlying stock split increases share count and proportionally reduces price per share, with no change in aggregate shareholder value.
