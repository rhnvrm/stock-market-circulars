## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF, a new fund offer (NFO) under Mirae Asset Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- Two plan variants are being launched: Direct Growth (ISIN INF769K01QX8) and Regular Growth (ISIN INF769K01QW0), both categorized as Equity schemes.
- The NFO subscription window is open from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026, till 11:59 pm.
- Allotment for both schemes is scheduled for July 23, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds (via any payment mode) are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 22, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Member intermediaries transacting on behalf of investors must ensure timely fund transfers to the Clearing Corporation account within the specified deadline to enable valid allotment of units.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Period: July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Clear Funds Deadline: July 22, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Allotment Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact, being an operational notice for mutual fund distributors and NSE members regarding a new ETF fund-of-fund offering. It does not affect listed equity trading, surveillance, or settlement systems, and is primarily relevant to those distributing or investing in the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF via the NSE MF Invest Platform.
