## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing earlier circulars dated June 02, 2023, July 18, 2023, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, October 04, 2024, and July 25, 2025. The circular identifies a new security added to ESM Stage I, three securities moving from ESM Stage I to Stage II, and provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework.

## Key Points

- Tirupati Forge Limited (TIRUPATIFL, ISIN: INE319Y01024) has been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I.
- TIRUPATIFL will shift from Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST) w.e.f. July 24, 2026.
- Three securities move from ESM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. July 23, 2026: DCM Shriram Fine Chemicals Limited (DSFCL, INE0OFM01015, as per BSE), Frog Innovations Limited (FROG, INE385O01018), and Gaudium IVF and Women Health Limited (GAUDIUMIVF, INE0P8B01020).
- No securities were identified for movement from Stage II back to Stage I in this cycle.
- No securities are excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle (Annexure II is Nil).
- A consolidated list of all securities under ESM (Annexure III) has been published, headed by Aarey Drugs & Pharmaceuticals.

## Regulatory Changes

No changes to the ESM framework methodology itself; this circular applies the existing framework criteria to identify newly qualifying and exiting securities for the current cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on TIRUPATIFL for all open positions as on July 23, 2026, and new positions created from July 24, 2026.
- Members must ensure TIRUPATIFL trades are settled under the Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment from July 24, 2026 instead of Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM).
- Securities shifting to Stage II (DSFCL, FROG, GAUDIUMIVF) will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 23, 2026.
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures; members must continue complying with those measures concurrently.

## Important Dates

- July 23, 2026: Stage I to Stage II movement and Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band takes effect for DSFCL, FROG, and GAUDIUMIVF; reference date for existing open positions in TIRUPATIFL.
- July 24, 2026: 100% margin and Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment shift take effect for TIRUPATIFL; new positions in TIRUPATIFL also subject to 100% margin from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The shift of TIRUPATIFL to Trade-for-Trade with 100% margin will significantly curb speculative trading and reduce liquidity in the stock, likely increasing price volatility and discouraging short-term trading. Similarly, DSFCL, FROG, and GAUDIUMIVF moving to the stricter Stage II with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction will further restrict intraday price movement and trading flexibility for these securities. These measures are surveillance-driven and not indicative of adverse fundamental developments, but market participants holding or trading these stocks should adjust position sizing and margin planning accordingly.
