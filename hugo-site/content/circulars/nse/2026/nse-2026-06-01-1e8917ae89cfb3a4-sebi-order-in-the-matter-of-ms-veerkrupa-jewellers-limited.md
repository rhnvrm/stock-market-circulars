## Summary

NSE has communicated SEBI Order No. QJA/SS/IVD-2/ID13/32429/2026-27 dated May 29, 2026, which restrains 17 entities — including M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited and associated individuals and companies — from accessing the securities market. The restraint prohibits buying, selling, or dealing in securities directly or indirectly for periods of 3 to 5 years.

## Key Points

- 17 entities restrained from accessing the securities market in the matter of M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited
- 11 entities face a 5-year restraint period; 6 entities face a 3-year restraint period
- Restrained entities are prohibited from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly
- Entities with open derivative positions may close/square off within 3 months from the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier
- Restraint on Entity Nos. 6 (M/s First Overseas Capital Limited), 16 (M/s NNM Securities Private Limited), and 17 (M/s Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited) will commence after expiration of restraints imposed in earlier SEBI orders (October 23, 2025 and May 30, 2024 respectively)

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must take note of the restraint order and ensure none of the 17 listed entities are permitted to trade or deal in securities on the exchange
- Members must check the consolidated list of debarred entities on the NSE website: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities
- For queries, members may contact: dl-invsg-all@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **SEBI Order Date:** May 29, 2026
- **NSE Circular Date:** June 01, 2026
- **Derivative Position Close-out Deadline:** Within 3 months from May 29, 2026 (i.e., by August 29, 2026) or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier
- **Restraint Start for Entities 6, 16 & 17:** After expiration of prior SEBI restraint orders (October 23, 2025 order for Entity 6; May 30, 2024 order for Entities 16 & 17)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action restricts 17 entities from all securities market activity for 3-5 years. The staggered restraint for M/s First Overseas Capital Limited, M/s NNM Securities Private Limited, and M/s Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited — which begins only after completion of prior restraint periods — indicates these entities have been subject to repeated SEBI enforcement actions. NSE members must immediately ensure these entities are blocked from trading. The full SEBI order is available at http://www.sebi.gov.in.
