## Summary

NSE has notified the applicability of the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for a set of securities, pursuant to its earlier circulars on the ASM framework (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558 and NSE/SURV/64066). Six securities — Aarnav Fashions, Eleganz Interiors, Just Dial, Landmark Cars, Shradha Realty, and Tatva Chintan Pharma Chem — have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I, attracting a higher margin requirement effective July 23, 2026. No securities met the criteria for Stage II, and there were no movements between Stage I and Stage II this cycle.

## Key Points

- Six securities added to ST-ASM Stage I: Aarnav Fashions Limited (AARNAV), Eleganz Interiors Limited (ELGNZ), Just Dial Limited (JUSTDIAL), Landmark Cars Limited (LANDMARK), Shradha Realty Limited (SHRADHA), and Tatva Chintan Pharma Chem Limited (TATVA).
- No securities were added to ST-ASM Stage II this cycle.
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I.
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework (established under prior circulars dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024) to the newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must apply the applicable margin rate on all open positions in the listed Stage I securities as on July 22, 2026, and on new positions created from July 23, 2026 onward. Members should monitor client positions in the affected securities and ensure margin collection complies with the ST-ASM requirements.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Circular issued (Ref No. NSE/SURV/75312, Circular Ref. No. 550/2026).
- July 22, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the revised margin.
- July 23, 2026: Effective date for the 50% (or existing, whichever higher, capped at 100%) margin rate on Stage I securities.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirement (50% or existing margin, whichever is higher) raises the cost of trading in the six affected securities and may reduce trading volumes and liquidity in the near term. Just Dial Limited is the most widely held/tracked name among those listed, so its inclusion is likely to draw the most market attention. The measure is a standard surveillance mechanism rather than a substantive regulatory or financial finding, limiting long-term impact absent further escalation (e.g., movement to Stage II or additional surveillance actions).
