## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for eight companies on the Capital Market segment, effective March 13, 2026. The securities include equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Eight companies have new securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from March 13, 2026
- Allotments include ESOP shares (Balaji Telefilms, Billionbrains Garage Ventures/Groww, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance) and preferential warrant conversions (Crown Lifters)
- Billionbrains Garage Ventures (GROWW) has the largest allotment: 100,000,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE0HOQ01053)
- Crown Lifters has two tranches of warrant conversions with lock-in periods expiring September 14–15, 2026
- All new securities are pari passu with existing shares of respective companies
- Market lot for all listed securities is 1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading in newly listed securities must use the designated security codes as specified in the Annexure
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes
- Lock-in restrictions on Crown Lifters shares must be observed until respective expiry dates (September 14–15, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **March 13, 2026**: Effective date for admission to dealings for all listed securities
- **March 9, 2026**: Date of allotment for Balaji Telefilms ESOP shares (51,997 shares)
- **March 6, 2026**: Date of allotment for Billionbrains Garage Ventures ESOP shares (100,000,000 shares)
- **March 10, 2026**: Date of allotment for ICICI Prudential Life Insurance ESOP shares (1,308 shares)
- **July 4, 2025**: Date of allotment for Crown Lifters warrant conversion tranche 1 (234,156 shares)
- **August 18, 2025**: Date of allotment for Crown Lifters warrant conversion tranche 2 (80,000 shares)
- **September 14–15, 2026**: Lock-in expiry dates for Crown Lifters preferential shares

## Impact Assessment

The most significant allotment is Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited (GROWW) with 100 million ESOP shares added to the float, which represents a meaningful increase in tradeable shares for this stock. Balaji Telefilms and ICICI Prudential Life additions are comparatively small (51,997 and 1,308 shares respectively) and are unlikely to materially affect price or liquidity. Crown Lifters' warrant-converted shares carry lock-in periods, limiting immediate market impact. Overall, this is a routine listing circular with no systemic market-wide implications.
