## Summary

NSE has notified all trading members of a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of Coal India Limited (COALINDIA) scheduled for May 27, 2026 and May 29, 2026. The OFS will be conducted on the NSE eOFS platform with a floor price of Rs. 412/- per share. The base offer size is up to 6,16,27,283 equity shares, with an equal oversubscription option and an additional 25,000 shares reserved for eligible employees.

## Key Points

- All eligible trading members of the Capital Market segment can participate via the eOFS platform at https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Three bidding categories: IS (Non-Retail), RS (Retail – bid value ≤ Rs. 2 lacs), and ES (Employee – bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs)
- Floor price set at Rs. 412/- per equity share; face value Rs. 10/-; tick size Re. 0.05; market lot of 1 share
- ISIN: INE522F01014; Symbol: COALINDIA
- Members not yet holding Admin user IDs must register via ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under path: ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS
- Admin user ID requests are processed at 11:00 am, 2:00 pm, and 3:00 pm; requests after 3:00 pm are processed next day by 8:45 am
- Only one request for Admin User creation should be raised and cannot be modified once submitted
- The seller may exercise the oversubscription option on or before 5:00 pm on May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The OFS will be conducted in accordance with the Exchange Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026, which governs implementation of Interoperability among Clearing Corporations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure compliant UCC of self (PRO) and all clients are updated in the Unique Client Identification (UCI) system at least one day prior to the OFS bidding session
- UCCs that become compliant on the day of the OFS bidding session will not be permitted to participate; orders will not be accepted
- UCCs identified as compliant by 22:00 hrs on the previous day will be considered permitted for the next bidding day
- Members must ensure sufficient collateral before initiating carry-forward of orders on T+1 day; orders rejected once due to margin insufficiency cannot be re-placed
- Members should refer to Annexure 3 of Exchange circular NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020 for feature and limit settings for their users
- Queries on the bidding platform may be directed to the helpdesk at toll-free number 1800 266 00 50 (Option 1)

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026**: Circular issued; UCC compliance and platform registration must be completed by end of day
- **May 27, 2026**: Day 1 of OFS bidding session
- **May 27, 2026 (by 5:00 pm)**: Seller to intimate intent to exercise oversubscription option
- **May 29, 2026**: Day 2 of OFS bidding session

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant liquidity event for Coal India Limited, a large-cap public sector undertaking. The combined offer (base + oversubscription) could involve up to ~12.3 crore equity shares, making it one of the larger OFS transactions in the market. Trading members across the Capital Market segment are directly affected and must ensure platform readiness, UCC compliance, and adequate margin/collateral ahead of the bidding sessions. Retail investors (bid value ≤ Rs. 2 lacs) and eligible employees (bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs) have dedicated participation categories, broadening the investor base for this OFS.
