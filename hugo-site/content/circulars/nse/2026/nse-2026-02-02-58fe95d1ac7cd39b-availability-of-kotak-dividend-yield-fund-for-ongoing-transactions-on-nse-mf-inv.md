## Summary

The Kotak Dividend Yield Fund of Kotak Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facility on NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from February 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kotak Dividend Yield Fund will be available for ongoing transactions from February 03, 2026
- Six plan variants will be available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All plans are categorized as Equity funds
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
