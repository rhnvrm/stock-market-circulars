## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the Mirae Asset Nifty200 Momentum 30 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 5050 ETF, a new fund offer (NFO) under Mirae Asset Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- The NFO covers two plans: Direct Growth and Regular Growth, both under ISIN INF769K01RJ5.
- The scheme falls under the Equity category.
- Allotment date for both schemes is July 27, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account before the specified deadline.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 24, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026
- Funds pay-in deadline: July 24, 2026, 11:59 pm
- Allotment date: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It serves as an operational enablement notice for NSE members to facilitate client transactions in the new ETF scheme, with no direct effect on existing listed securities or broader trading activity.
