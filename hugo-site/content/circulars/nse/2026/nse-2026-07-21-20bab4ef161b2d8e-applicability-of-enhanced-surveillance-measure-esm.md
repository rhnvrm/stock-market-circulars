## Summary

NSE has updated the list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Two securities — Consolidated Finvest & Holdings Limited (CONSOFINVT) and Teamtech Formwork Solutions Limited (TEAMTECH) — have been newly added to ESM Stage I effective July 22, 2026, and will move from the Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) segment to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment effective July 23, 2026. Additionally, Nupur Recyclers Limited (NRL) and Shree Vasu Logistics Limited (SVLL) are being moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective July 22, 2026. No securities are moving from Stage II to Stage I, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.

## Key Points

- CONSOFINVT and TEAMTECH newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- Both securities shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) segment w.e.f. July 23, 2026.
- NRL and SVLL move from ESM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage I in this cycle.
- No securities excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.
- Securities shifting to Stage II are placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- A minimum 100% margin applies on all open positions as on July 22, 2026, and new positions created from July 23, 2026, for newly added securities.
- ESM measures operate alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under NSE's existing ESM framework (referencing prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315), applying the standard ESM criteria to reclassify specific securities rather than introducing new regulatory provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on open positions in CONSOFINVT and TEAMTECH as on July 22, 2026, and on new positions from July 23, 2026. Trading in these securities, along with NRL and SVLL, must be conducted per the applicable Trade-for-Trade and price band restrictions from the specified effective dates.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: ESM Stage I inclusions and Stage I-to-Stage II moves take effect; 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction begins for Stage II securities; margin applies to open positions.
- July 23, 2026: CONSOFINVT and TEAMTECH shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment; 100% margin applies to new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The measure significantly restricts trading liquidity and increases margin costs for CONSOFINVT and TEAMTECH shareholders and traders, as Trade-for-Trade status eliminates intraday trading and requires full upfront margin. NRL and SVLL face tighter price band restrictions under Stage II. The broader market impact is limited given the small number of securities affected, but it signals continued regulatory surveillance on stocks exhibiting price/volume anomalies.
