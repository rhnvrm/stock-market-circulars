## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has admitted privately placed commercial paper securities from eight companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective January 23, 2026. The circular includes fresh issues from Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services, Axis Securities, Godrej Industries, EPL Limited, IIFL Finance, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, JM Financial Services, and Aditya Birla Money, along with re-issues from Axis Securities and Blue Star Limited.

## Key Points

- 8 fresh commercial paper issues listed with face values of ₹5 lakh each
- Total fresh issue quantity: 28,400 securities (₹1,420 crore aggregate face value)
- Maturity periods range from 52 days (ABML) to 364 days (IIFL, MOFS, JFSL)
- All securities allotted on January 22-23, 2026
- 2 re-issues also listed (Axis Securities and Blue Star Limited)
- Security codes and ISIN numbers assigned for each instrument
- Listing effective from January 23, 2026

## Regulatory Framework

Listing admitted pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed debt securities available for trading on the debt segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 22-23, 2026
- **Maturity Dates**: Range from March 16, 2026 to January 22, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for the debt market segment with no impact on equity trading or retail investors. The circular provides transparency about newly available debt instruments for institutional investors participating in the commercial paper market. These private placements represent short-term funding arrangements by various financial and non-financial companies.
