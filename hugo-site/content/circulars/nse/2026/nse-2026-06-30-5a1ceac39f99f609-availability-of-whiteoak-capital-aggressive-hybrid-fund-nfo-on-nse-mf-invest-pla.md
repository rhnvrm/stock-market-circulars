## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Whiteoak Capital Aggressive Hybrid Fund NFO, managed by Whiteoak Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- The Whiteoak Capital Aggressive Hybrid Fund NFO will reopen for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 01, 2026.
- Facilities enabled include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Two scheme options are covered: Regular Growth (ISIN INF03VN01AG6) and Direct Growth (ISIN INF03VN01AF8), both categorized as Equity.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling platform availability of an existing scheme, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members and participants using the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems and client-facing information to reflect the availability of these schemes for ongoing transactions from the effective date. No additional filings are required.

## Important Dates

- July 01, 2026: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP for the scheme on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact and is primarily relevant to NSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors transacting through the NSE MF Invest Platform. It expands investor access to ongoing transactions in the Whiteoak Capital Aggressive Hybrid Fund but does not affect listed equity securities or broader market operations.
