## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74647 (Ref. No. 435/2026) dated June 10, 2026, updating the list of securities under the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective June 11, 2026, Impex Ferro Tech Limited (IMPEXFERRO) is added to ASM IBC Stage I. No securities are being excluded or moving between stages in this update.

## Key Points

- **New addition to ASM IBC Stage I (w.e.f. June 11, 2026):** IMPEXFERRO – Impex Ferro Tech Limited (ISIN: INE691G01015)
- No securities shortlisted under "Receipt of corporate announcement / disclosure" criteria
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II
- No securities moving from Stage II to Stage I
- No securities excluded from ASM IBC in this update
- ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges
- Shortlisting under ASM IBC is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the ASM IBC framework first established under NSE/SURV/37979 (June 08, 2018) and subsequently revised via NSE/SURV/52368 (May 20, 2022), NSE/SURV/55582 (February 10, 2023), and NSE/SURV/70074 (September 05, 2025). The current update adds one security to the Stage I list.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must note the revised ASM IBC applicability effective June 11, 2026
- Securities under ASM IBC are subject to additional surveillance conditions; members must ensure compliance with applicable trading restrictions for listed securities
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026 (for new ASM IBC inclusions and exclusions)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to IMPEXFERRO and existing ASM IBC-listed securities. The consolidated ASM IBC list includes at least ABAN (Aban Offshore Limited), NEUEON (Neueon Corporation Limited), PERFECT (Perfect Infraengineers Limited), and AGSTRA (AGS Transact Technologies Limited), all at Stage I. Securities under ASM IBC typically face enhanced surveillance, which may include trade-to-trade settlement or price band restrictions, affecting liquidity and trading activity for retail and institutional participants holding these stocks. The measure is a regulatory safeguard and does not reflect an adverse finding against the company.
