## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of 18,50,287 equity shares of AvenuesAI Limited (trading symbol: CCAVENUE) on the Capital Market segment, effective March 30, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares. The shares carry face value and paid-up value of Re. 1/- each and are pari passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AvenuesAI Limited
- **Symbol:** CCAVENUE
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE483S01020 (permanent); temporary ISIN IN8483S01029 used during dematerialized credit
- **Number of shares listed:** 18,50,287
- **Face value / Paid-up value:** Re. 1/- each
- **Market lot:** 1
- **Pari passu:** Yes — ranks equally with existing equity shares
- **Lock-in:** Not applicable
- **Distinctive numbers:** 3,47,96,19,233 to 3,48,14,69,519

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes
- SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) governing ISIN activation for additional share issuances

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code **CCAVENUE** for all trades in these shares.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of **1 share**.
- Shares credited under temporary ISIN IN8483S01029 will be migrated to the permanent ISIN INE483S01020 upon activation as per SEBI guidelines.

## Important Dates

- **Circular date:** March 27, 2026
- **Effective date for trading:** March 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action involving the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up status for AvenuesAI Limited (the parent entity of CCAvenue payment gateway). The 18,50,287 new shares represent a modest addition to the float and are pari passu with existing shares, so dilution impact is limited. Existing holders of the temporary ISIN will have their holdings migrated to the main ISIN. No adverse market impact is expected; the listing merely formalizes tradability of shares already allotted.
