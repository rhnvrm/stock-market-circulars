## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Debenture issued by ICICI Bank Limited, effective June 19, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the maturity of the instrument under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Instrument: ICICI Bank Limited NCD — Symbol **ICICM58**, ISIN **INE090A08SP8**
- Trading will be suspended with effect from **June 19, 2026**
- Reason for suspension: **Maturity** of the debenture
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74725, Ref. No. 0968/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. The action is routine and governed by the existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.2), which mandates suspension of a security upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in ICICM58 from June 19, 2026
- No further action required for members beyond acknowledging the suspension; settlement of existing trades should follow normal procedures prior to the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative action. The suspension affects only holders of ICICI Bank's NCD (ICICM58 / INE090A08SP8) who will no longer be able to trade the instrument on NSE from June 19, 2026. As the trigger is contractual maturity, debenture holders should expect principal repayment as per the original terms. There is no adverse credit or compliance implication for ICICI Bank or the broader market.
