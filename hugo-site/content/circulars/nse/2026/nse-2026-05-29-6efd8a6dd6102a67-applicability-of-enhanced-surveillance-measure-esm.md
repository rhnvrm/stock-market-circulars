## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/SURV/74472, Circular Ref. No: 402/2026) announcing updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Seven securities are newly included in ESM Stage I, two securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II, and a consolidated list of all securities under ESM has been updated. All changes take effect from June 1–2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Seven securities are newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I effective June 2, 2026, attracting a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions.
- Securities marked with an asterisk (*) will be shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series) w.e.f. June 2, 2026.
- Two securities — APSISAERO and GSTL — are escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II, subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction effective June 1, 2026.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum **100% margin** on all open positions as on June 1, 2026, and all new positions created from June 2, 2026 for newly included ESM Stage I securities.
- Trades in the affected securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules under the BE/ST series.
- Stage II securities (APSISAERO, GSTL) must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a **2% price band**.
- Upon exit from ESM, the price band reverts to the pre-ESM applicable band, unless the scrip is under another surveillance measure.

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: ESM Stage I to Stage II transition effective; 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction applies to Stage II securities.
- **June 1, 2026**: Reference date for open positions attracting 100% margin for new ESM Stage I securities.
- **June 2, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective for all open and new positions in ESM Stage I securities; EQ/SM to BE/ST segment shift effective.

## Impact Assessment

Trading in the seven newly included securities will become significantly more capital-intensive due to the 100% margin requirement. The mandatory shift from rolling settlement to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) eliminates netting benefits and requires full delivery, reducing speculative activity. APSISAERO and GSTL face even stricter restrictions under Stage II with a tight 2% price band in Periodic Call Auction, severely limiting price discovery and liquidity. Market participants holding positions in these scrips need to arrange additional margin by June 1, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation.
