## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members that the face value and paid-up value of equity shares of Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited (POCL) will change from Rs. 5 to Rs. 2 per share, effective July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited (Symbol: POCL)
- Existing face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5 per share
- New face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2 per share
- Change effective from July 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corporate action notification under NSE Listing regulations, informing members of the face value split of POCL equity shares. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a company-specific implementation of a previously approved corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the revised face value and paid-up value of POCL shares effective the stated date. No specific action is required from investors beyond awareness of the adjusted share metrics.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 14, 2026
- Effective Date of Face Value Change: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split will proportionally adjust the number of shares and price per share for POCL, without changing the total investment value for existing shareholders. Trading systems, indices, and derivative contracts referencing POCL (if any) will require corresponding price and lot adjustments from the effective date.
