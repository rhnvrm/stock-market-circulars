## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued Circular 452/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74768) updating the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Eight securities are newly inducted into ESM Stage I, three securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II (stricter controls), and no securities are moving from Stage II back to Stage I. All affected securities will face a minimum 100% margin requirement and a mandatory shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Eight securities newly added to ESM Stage I effective June 19, 2026: CEREBRAINT, JEYYAM, K2INFRA, MADHUCON, PATINTLOG, SHYAMDHANI, SMARTEN, VALIANTORG
- Three securities escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective June 19, 2026: INNOMET, KESORAMIND, VPRPL
- No securities are downgraded from Stage II to Stage I in this cycle
- All newly included and escalated securities attract a minimum 100% margin on open positions as of June 19, 2026 and all new positions from June 22, 2026
- Securities marked with asterisk (*) shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST series) w.e.f. June 22, 2026
- Stage II securities will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 19, 2026
- ESM classification is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the ESM framework originally established under circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315 (June 2023 through July 2025). The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Must ensure clients maintain minimum 100% margin on all open positions in ESM securities as of June 19, 2026 and on new positions from June 22, 2026
- Members must be aware that affected securities will no longer be traded in the normal Rolling Settlement segment and will move to Trade-for-Trade
- Stage II securities (INNOMET, KESORAMIND, VPRPL) will be subject to Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band — members must inform clients accordingly
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Impact Assessment

**Newly added to ESM Stage I** (100% margin + T-for-T from June 22, 2026):
1. CEREBRAINT – Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited (INE345B01019)
2. JEYYAM – Jeyyam Global Foods Limited (INE0RXB01022)
3. K2INFRA – K2 Infragen Limited (INE0DEZ01013)
4. MADHUCON – Madhucon Projects Limited (INE378D01032)
5. PATINTLOG – Patel Integrated Logistics Limited (INE529D01014)
6. SHYAMDHANI – Shyam Dhani Industries Limited (INE1CRP01016)
7. SMARTEN – Smarten Power Systems Limited (INE14GK01016)
8. VALIANTORG – Valiant Organics Limited (INE565V01010)

**Escalated to ESM Stage II** (Periodic Call Auction, 2% price band, Trade-for-Trade from June 19, 2026):
1. INNOMET – Innomet Advanced Materials Limited (INE0S1D01010)
2. KESORAMIND – Kesoram Industries Limited (INE087A01019) — as per BSE
3. VPRPL – Vishnu Prakash R Punglia Limited (INE0AE001013)

Trading in all affected securities will be severely restricted: Trade-for-Trade eliminates the netting of positions, forcing delivery of full traded quantity. The 100% margin requirement eliminates leverage. Stage II securities face additional restriction through Periodic Call Auction with only a 2% price band, drastically limiting intraday price movement. Market participants holding positions in these securities should review margin availability and delivery obligations immediately.
