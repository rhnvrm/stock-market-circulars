## Summary

NSE has issued a circular on the applicability of the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, shortlisting 5 new securities into ESM Stage I effective June 02, 2026. No securities are moving between stages or being excluded from the framework in this update. The consolidated ESM list contains 26 securities in Stage II and multiple securities in Stage I.

## Key Points

- 5 new securities added to ESM Stage I effective June 02, 2026
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II
- No securities moving from Stage II to Stage I
- No securities excluded from the ESM framework
- 3 of the 5 new Stage I entrants will shift from EQ/SM segment to BE/ST segment effective June 03, 2026
- As of this update, 26 securities remain under ESM Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities are newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I w.e.f. June 02, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | BLUECOAST | Blue Coast Hotels Limited | INE472B01011 |
| 2 | GLOBALPET | Global Pet Industries Limited | INE0PS501019 |
| 3 | MOTOGENFIN | The Motor & General Finance Limited | INE861B01023 |
| 4 | SHIVALIK | Shivalik Rasayan Limited | INE788J01021 |
| 5 | TANKUP | Tankup Engineers Limited | INE0Z7N01017 |

Note: MOTOGENFIN, SHIVALIK, and TANKUP will move from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment w.e.f. June 03, 2026. MOTOGENFIN classification is as per BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply ESM Stage I restrictions (trade-for-trade settlement, periodic call auction) for newly listed securities from June 02, 2026
- Securities moving to BE/ST series (MOTOGENFIN, SHIVALIK, TANKUP) must be transacted under the revised segment from June 03, 2026
- Members should update their systems and client communications to reflect the revised ESM applicability

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: 5 new securities enter ESM Stage I
- **June 03, 2026**: MOTOGENFIN, SHIVALIK, and TANKUP shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment

## Impact Assessment

The 5 newly added securities will face enhanced trading restrictions under ESM Stage I, including trade-for-trade settlement. Investors holding positions in BLUECOAST, GLOBALPET, MOTOGENFIN, SHIVALIK, and TANKUP will be subject to stricter margin and settlement requirements. The segment change to BE/ST for three securities further limits intraday trading. The consolidated ESM list continues to include 26 Stage II securities with the most stringent restrictions. No securities have been released from the framework this cycle, indicating sustained regulatory concern over the listed counters.
