## Summary

NSE has announced that Vedanta Power Limited (symbol: VEDPOWER) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (series: BE) to the Rolling segment (series: EQ) with effect from June 30, 2026. This follows an earlier circular (NSE/CML/74668 dated June 11, 2026) and is in accordance with SEBI guidelines issued vide circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- VEDPOWER (Vedanta Power Limited) is being moved from series BE (Trade for Trade) to series EQ (Rolling Settlement).
- The transition is effective from June 30, 2026.
- The transfer is pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.
- This circular is a follow-up to NSE/CML/74668 issued on June 11, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular applies existing SEBI guidelines (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012) governing the movement of securities between the Trade for Trade segment and the Rolling segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed of the segment transfer and should update their systems and client communications accordingly to reflect that VEDPOWER will trade under series EQ (Rolling Settlement) from June 30, 2026 onward.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Earlier NSE circular (NSE/CML/74668) issued regarding VEDPOWER.
- **June 15, 2026**: Date of this circular (NSE/CML/74702, Ref No. 0959/2026).
- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from Trade for Trade (BE) to Rolling segment (EQ).

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade (BE series) to Rolling Settlement (EQ series) is a positive development for VEDPOWER shareholders and traders. Under the Trade for Trade mechanism, each trade must result in actual delivery and no netting of positions is allowed, which restricts liquidity. Moving to the EQ rolling segment allows normal intraday trading and netting, improving trading flexibility and liquidity for the stock. The impact is limited to VEDPOWER and is operationally routine.
