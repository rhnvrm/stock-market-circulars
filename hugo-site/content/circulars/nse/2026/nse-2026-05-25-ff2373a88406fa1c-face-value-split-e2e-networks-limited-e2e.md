## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has announced that E2E Networks Limited (Symbol: E2E) will undergo a face value split. The paid-up value of each equity share will change from Rs. 10 to Re. 1, effective June 05, 2026. This constitutes a 10:1 stock split.

## Key Points

- E2E Networks Limited (NSE Symbol: E2E) is undergoing a face value split.
- Existing face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share.
- New face value and paid-up value: Re. 1 per share.
- Split ratio: 10:1 (each existing share splits into 10 shares).
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74383.

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to this circular, the face value and paid-up value of E2E Networks Limited equity shares will be revised from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 per share with effect from June 05, 2026. Members and market participants are required to update their systems to reflect the new face value.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must take note of the revised face value effective June 05, 2026.
- Trading systems, back-office software, and client records should be updated to reflect the new face value of Re. 1.
- Adjustments to futures and options contracts (if any) on E2E may be issued separately by NSE.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date of Split:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 (a 10:1 ratio) will proportionally increase the number of shares outstanding while reducing the share price by a factor of 10. This improves share affordability and liquidity for retail investors. Existing shareholders' economic value remains unchanged. Derivative contracts on E2E, if any, will require adjustment. ISIN-level changes and demat account credit of additional shares will be managed by depositories in coordination with the company's registrar.
