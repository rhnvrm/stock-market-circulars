## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for twelve companies, arising primarily from allotments of equity shares under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The newly allotted shares of Aarti Pharmalabs Limited, Ather Energy Limited, Axis Bank Limited, The Federal Bank Limited, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited, Kajaria Ceramics Limited, Global Health Limited, Meesho Limited, Niva Bupa Health Insurance Company Limited, Sanstar Limited, SRG Housing Finance Limited and Yes Bank Limited will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 10, 2026, ranking pari passu with existing shares.

## Key Points

- Further issues of securities admitted to dealings under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.
- All listed allotments in the detailed annexure relate to shares issued under ESOP.
- Aarti Pharmalabs Limited (AARTIPHARM): 22,062 equity shares of Rs. 5 each, allotted 24-Jun-2026.
- Ather Energy Limited (ATHERENERG): 189,032 equity shares of Re. 1 each, allotted 01-Jul-2026.
- Axis Bank Limited (AXISBANK): 137,549 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, allotted 07-Jul-2026.
- The Federal Bank Limited (FEDERALBNK): 474,994 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, allotted 05-Jul-2026.
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited (ICICIPRULI): 11,360 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted 07-Jul-2026.
- Kajaria Ceramics Limited (KAJARIACER): equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted under ESOP (allotment date not captured in available excerpt).
- Additional companies covered in the annexure (Global Health, Meesho, Niva Bupa Health Insurance, Sanstar, SRG Housing Finance, Yes Bank) with details not fully available in the provided excerpt.
- All new shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares and carry no lock-in.
- Market lot for all newly listed shares is 1.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of further share issues to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to identify these securities only by their designated security codes and trade in the specified lot sizes as set out in the annexure from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 09, 2026
- Effective date for listing/trading of new shares: July 10, 2026
- Allotment dates range from 24-Jun-2026 to 07-Jul-2026 across companies.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of ESOP-driven equity allotments across several large-cap and mid-cap companies. The incremental share counts are small relative to each company's total outstanding shares, resulting in negligible dilution and minimal market impact. No trading restrictions or lock-ins apply to the newly listed shares.
