## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has announced the change of Market Maker for KATARIA INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Symbol: KATARIA), an SME-listed company. The market maker role transitions from MNM Stock Broking Pvt Ltd to Shreni Shares Limited, effective March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: KATARIA (KATARIA INDUSTRIES LIMITED)
- Old Market Maker: MNM Stock Broking Pvt Ltd (Member Code: 90080)
- New Market Maker: Shreni Shares Limited (Member Code: 14109)
- Effective Date: March 11, 2026
- All NSE members are advised to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the market-making arrangement for an SME scrip on NSE's Capital Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to note the change of market maker for KATARIA INDUSTRIES LIMITED effective March 11, 2026. Trading systems and records should reflect the new market maker, Shreni Shares Limited (Member Code: 14109).

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 09, 2026
- Effective Date of New Market Maker: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational change affecting only the designated market maker for KATARIA INDUSTRIES LIMITED on the SME platform. Liquidity provision responsibilities transfer from MNM Stock Broking Pvt Ltd to Shreni Shares Limited. No broader market or systemic implications are anticipated.
