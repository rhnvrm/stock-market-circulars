## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for nine companies — Abans Financial Services Limited, CARE Ratings Limited, Coforge Limited, Kfin Technologies Limited, Prime Focus Limited, Silly Monks Entertainment Limited, Suzlon Energy Limited, Tejas Networks Limited and Tracxn Technologies Limited — arising primarily from ESOP allotments. These additional securities will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 15, 2026, under the designated security codes and lot sizes specified in the circular's annexure.

## Key Points

- Further equity shares issued under ESOP schemes have been admitted for trading for all nine listed companies.
- Abans Financial Services Limited: 46,919 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, allotted 10-Jun-2026, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- CARE Ratings Limited: 26,733 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted 06-Jul-2026, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Coforge Limited: 19,265 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, allotted 23-Jun-2026, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Kfin Technologies Limited: 18,657 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted 09-Jul-2026, market lot 1, pari passu.
- Details for Prime Focus Limited, Silly Monks Entertainment Limited, Suzlon Energy Limited, Tejas Networks Limited and Tracxn Technologies Limited are provided in the annexure (not fully captured in the excerpt reviewed).
- All new shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares and are identified only by their designated security codes for trading purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of further securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

No action is required from investors. Trading members must recognize the new shares under the applicable designated codes and lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all disclosed allotments) from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for trading of further-issued securities: July 15, 2026.
- Allotment dates range from June 10, 2026 to July 9, 2026 across the various companies.

## Impact Assessment

This circular reflects routine ESOP-related share allotments and has negligible market impact. The additional shares represent a small incremental increase in each company's free float and paid-up capital, with no change to trading mechanics, symbols, or ISINs. No price or volume disruption is expected as a direct result of this listing.
