## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a circular referencing its earlier circular (NSE/CML/74850) dated June 24, 2026, regarding the change in trading symbol of Gujarat Gas Limited. Participants and Custodians are informed that the trading symbol of the company in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Scheme will be changed from GUJENERGY to GUJGASLTD with effect from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- The symbol change applies specifically to the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme.
- Old Symbol: GUJENERGY
- New Symbol: GUJGASLTD
- This follows from the prior circular NSE/CML/74850 dated June 24, 2026, which announced the underlying symbol change.
- All contracts in the security will be traded under the new symbol from the effective date onward.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this circular operationalizes a previously announced symbol change within the SLB Scheme segment specifically.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and Custodians must update their systems and records to reflect the new trading symbol (GUJGASLTD) for all SLB contracts in this security effective July 1, 2026, and ensure no references to the old symbol (GUJENERGY) remain in post-effective-date processing.

## Important Dates

- June 24, 2026: Original circular (NSE/CML/74850) announcing the symbol change.
- June 30, 2026: Date of this circular.
- July 1, 2026: Effective date for the symbol change in the SLB Scheme; all contracts traded under the new symbol from this date.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative/operational change limited to symbol nomenclature in the SLB Scheme. It does not affect the company's fundamentals, listing status, or trading mechanics beyond the symbol identifier. Participants must ensure operational and system readiness to avoid settlement or matching errors due to symbol mismatch.
