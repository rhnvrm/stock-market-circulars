## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts in LICI (Life Insurance Corporation of India) pursuant to NSE Circular No. 93/2026 dated June 24, 2026. A dividend of Rs. 10/- per share will be deducted from futures contract settlement prices and options strike prices, with June 24, 2026 as the last cum-dividend date and June 25, 2026 as the ex-dividend date.

## Key Points

- Dividend amount: Rs. 10/- per share
- Last cum-dividend date: June 24, 2026
- Ex-dividend date: June 25, 2026
- All open futures positions in LICI will be marked-to-market on June 24, 2026 at the daily settlement price, then carried forward at daily settlement price less Rs. 10/-
- All cum-dividend options strike prices will be reduced by Rs. 10/- on the ex-dividend date, adjusted to nearest tick size
- Existing positions in old strike prices will automatically continue in corresponding new adjusted strike prices
- Begin-of-day margins on June 25, 2026 will be computed based on adjusted carry forward values

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to NSE Circular No. 93/2026 (Download No. NSE/FAOP/74864) dated June 24, 2026, clearing and settlement procedures for LICI F&O contracts are adjusted as follows:
- Futures contracts: daily MTM settlement price reduced by Rs. 10/- for carry-forward open positions
- Options contracts: all cum-dividend strike prices reduced by Rs. 10/- (adjusted to nearest tick size) on the ex-dividend date

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members must account for adjusted carry-forward values for LICI futures positions from June 25, 2026
- Intra-day margins from June 25, 2026 onward will be computed based on relevant traded prices at the time intra-day SPAN risk parameter files are generated
- No separate action required for options positions — existing positions automatically migrate to new adjusted strike prices

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Last cum-dividend date; futures positions marked-to-market at daily settlement price and carried forward at price less Rs. 10/-
- **June 25, 2026**: Ex-dividend date; normal MTM settlement resumes for futures; all options strike prices reduced by Rs. 10/-; begin-of-day margins computed on adjusted values

## Impact Assessment

All market participants holding open futures or options positions in LICI as of June 24, 2026 will see their contract values adjusted downward by Rs. 10/- (the dividend amount). For example, a futures position valued at Rs. 436.00 will be carried forward at Rs. 426.00. Options strike prices will similarly be reduced by Rs. 10/- adjusted to the nearest tick size. This is a standard corporate action adjustment and does not represent a loss — it reflects the value transfer from the company to shareholders via the dividend. Members should ensure their risk and margin systems are updated to reflect adjusted carry-forward values for begin-of-day margin computation on June 25, 2026.
