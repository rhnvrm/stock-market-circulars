## Summary

NSE has admitted ten privately placed Commercial Papers (CPs) to dealings on its Debt Market Segment with effect from June 15, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. All securities were allotted on June 12, 2026 and are scheduled to redeem in September 2026.

## Key Points

- Ten Commercial Paper issuances from nine companies admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment effective June 15, 2026.
- All CPs were allotted on June 12, 2026 with face value of ₹5,00,000 each.
- Issuers include public sector institutions (SIDBI, NABARD) and private corporates.
- Redemption dates are September 10–11, 2026 (~91-day tenor).
- Security codes (ISINs) assigned as listed in the Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All Members of NSE are notified of the new security codes for these CPs for trading on the Debt Market Segment. No additional compliance action required from members beyond updating their systems with the new ISINs.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | June 12, 2026 |
| Admission to Debt Market Segment | June 15, 2026 |
| Redemption (most CPs) | September 11, 2026 |
| Redemption (CEAT CP) | September 10, 2026 |
