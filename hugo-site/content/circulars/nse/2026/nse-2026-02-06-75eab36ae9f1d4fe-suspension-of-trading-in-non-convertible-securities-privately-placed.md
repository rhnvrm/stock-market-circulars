## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the suspension of trading in non-convertible securities (privately placed) of MRF Limited under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspension is effective from February 09, 2026, and is due to the redemption of the securities.

## Key Points

- Single non-convertible security of MRF Limited to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE883A08016
- Suspension effective date: February 09, 2026
- Reason: Redemption of securities
- Action taken under NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine notification pursuant to existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified ISIN (INE883A08016) from February 09, 2026
- No further trading activity permitted in this non-convertible security after suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 06, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** February 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a standard administrative action affecting a single privately placed debt security that has reached its redemption date. The suspension is procedural in nature and part of the normal lifecycle of debt instruments. Only holders and traders of this specific ISIN will be affected, with no broader implications for MRF Limited's equity or other securities.
