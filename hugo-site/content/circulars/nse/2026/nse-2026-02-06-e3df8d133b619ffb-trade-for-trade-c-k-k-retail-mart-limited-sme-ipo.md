## Summary

National Stock Exchange has announced the transfer of C K K Retail Mart Limited from the trade-for-trade segment (ST series) to the rolling settlement segment (SM series) effective February 20, 2026. This follows the previous circular NSE/CML/72656 dated February 05, 2026 and is in accordance with SEBI guidelines (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012).

## Key Points

- Stock Symbol: CKKRETAIL
- Company Name: C K K Retail Mart Limited
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (Series: ST)
- Target Segment: Rolling Settlement (Series: SM)
- Transition applicable to SME IPO listing
- Reference to prior circular NSE/CML/72656 dated February 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The security will move from trade-for-trade settlement mechanism to standard rolling settlement mechanism as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012. This is a standard post-IPO transition for SME securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members. The segment change will be automatically implemented by the exchange. Members should note the change in trading series from ST to SM for order placement and settlement purposes.

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: February 06, 2026
- Effective Date of Segment Transfer: February 20, 2026
- Previous Related Circular: NSE/CML/72656 dated February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on overall market operations. This is a routine procedural change affecting only C K K Retail Mart Limited (CKKRETAIL). The transition from trade-for-trade to rolling settlement typically provides improved liquidity and more flexible trading conditions for the security. Market participants trading this stock should update their systems to reflect the new series designation (SM instead of ST) from February 20, 2026.
