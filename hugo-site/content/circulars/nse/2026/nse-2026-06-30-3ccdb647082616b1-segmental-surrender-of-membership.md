## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Muni Broking House Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000316338) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives Segment. The surrender was approved on June 11, 2026. All members are notified to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Muni Broking House Limited has voluntarily surrendered its Currency Derivatives Segment membership.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000316338.
- Surrender approved by the Exchange on June 11, 2026.
- Circular issued by the Compliance Department of NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding the exit of a trading member from a specific segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All members are required to take note of the surrender. Counterparties or clients dealing through Muni Broking House Limited in the Currency Derivatives Segment should be aware that the member is no longer active in that segment as of the surrender approval date.

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender of a single member's Currency Derivatives Segment membership is a routine administrative event. It does not affect broader market operations or other trading members. Clients of Muni Broking House Limited in this segment would need to make alternative arrangements with other registered currency derivatives members.
