## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the revised list of approved securities and empaneled banks applicable for the month of July 2026. This circular (Ref. 076/2026) supersedes the earlier circular 061/2026 dated May 22, 2026 and is addressed to all Members, Custodians, and PCMs in the Futures and Options (F&O) segment.

## Key Points

- Seven annexures detail eligible collateral categories: equity shares (non-cash), ETFs (non-cash and cash), Sovereign Gold Bonds and G-Sec/T-Bills (cash), open-ended mutual funds, corporate bonds (non-cash), and approved banks for BGs/FDRs.
- Applicable haircut rates are VAR-based for equity shares; market-wide and member-specific permitted quantity limits are specified per security.
- A prudential norm applies: exclusion of a single pledged security must not result in a margin shortfall exceeding 25% at the Clearing Member level.
- G-Sec/T-Bills carry a two-day shut period; members must release these securities at least two days before maturity.
- Member-banks must ensure G-Secs pledged as collateral are not counted toward SLR requirements and not used for trading.
- Annexure 7 lists banks empaneled for issuance of Bank Guarantees (BGs) and Fixed Deposit Receipts (FDRs).

## Regulatory Changes

This is a periodic revision to the approved securities and bank list, updating market-wide limits, member-specific limits, and applicable haircut rates for each eligible security across all collateral categories for the F&O segment effective July 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must ensure collateral submitted from July 2026 onwards conforms to the updated lists and limits in Annexures 1–7.
- Members must release G-Sec/T-Bills at least two days before their maturity date.
- Member-banks must not reckon pledged G-Secs for SLR purposes or use them for trading.
- Collateral submissions must respect both market-wide and member-specific quantity limits as stipulated in the annexures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Period:** July 2026
- **Previous Circular:** 061/2026 dated May 22, 2026 (NCL/CMPT/74373)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine monthly operational update with medium impact. It directly affects all F&O clearing members and custodians who use securities as collateral for margin obligations. Changes to the approved list, haircut rates, or quantity limits can require members to substitute or top up collateral. Members should review the updated annexures, particularly for any securities added or removed, and ensure their collateral portfolios remain compliant ahead of July 2026.
