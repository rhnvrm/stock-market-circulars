## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Shadowfax Technologies Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol "SHADOWFAX" with ISIN INE12UN01015. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shadowfax Technologies Limited
- Trading Symbol: SHADOWFAX
- ISIN: INE12UN01015
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/72465, dated January 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement for a new IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are informed to note the new security details for trading purposes once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to the NSE platform, providing investors with access to Shadowfax Technologies Limited shares. Market participants should await the separate circular announcing the actual listing date before commencing trading activities. The addition of this IPO expands investment opportunities in the logistics and technology sector.
