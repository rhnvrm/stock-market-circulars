## Summary

NSE has notified, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, that further issued securities of 12 companies are being admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 06, 2026. The companies are Bliss GVS Pharma Limited, Gayatri Projects Limited, HDB Financial Services Limited, IDFC First Bank Limited, Lloyds Metals And Energy Limited, Max India Limited, Rainbow Childrens Medicare Limited, Renaissance Global Limited, Sansera Engineering Limited, Shah Metacorp Limited, UTI Asset Management Company Limited, and Valiant Organics Limited. The circular specifies designated security codes and lot sizes per Regulation 2.5.5, detailed in an Annexure for each company.

## Key Points

- Bliss GVS Pharma Limited (BLISSGVS): 267,250 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted under ESOP on 11-Jun-2026, lock-in until 11-Dec-2026, market lot 1, pari passu with existing shares.
- Gayatri Projects Limited (GAYAPROJ): 277,100,315 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted on preferential basis on 22-Apr-2026 at an issue price of Rs. 10, market lot 1, pari passu; lock-in tranches expiring 08-Jan-2027 (184,240,515 shares) and 08-Jan-2028 (92,859,800 shares) as per Annexure A.
- HDB Financial Services Limited (HDBFS) and eight other companies (IDFC First Bank, Lloyds Metals And Energy, Max India, Rainbow Childrens Medicare, Renaissance Global, Sansera Engineering, Shah Metacorp, UTI Asset Management, Valiant Organics) also have further securities listed; full allotment details for these were on the continuation sheet not fully captured in the excerpt provided.
- All listed securities are identified solely by their designated security codes for trading purposes, with lot sizes as specified in the Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, not a change to regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new securities using their designated codes and apply the specified lot sizes when trading these securities from the effective date. No other action is required from members.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 03, 2026
- Effective date for listing of further issued securities: July 06, 2026
- Bliss GVS Pharma lock-in expiry: 11-Dec-2026
- Gayatri Projects lock-in expiries: 08-Jan-2027 and 08-Jan-2028 (per Annexure A tranches)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification that incrementally increases the tradeable share count for each named company (via ESOP allotments, preferential issues, or other further issuances). Impact on trading is limited to updated lot sizes and security codes; shareholders should note applicable lock-in expiry dates for shares subject to restrictions, particularly the large preferential allotment for Gayatri Projects Limited, which materially increases its outstanding share count.
