## Summary

NSE has revised the list of 'Specified Stockbrokers' under the framework for addressing Technical Glitches in stock brokers' electronic trading systems, as directed by SEBI circular SEBI/HO/38/44/12(1)2026-MIRSD-TPD1 dated January 09, 2026. Five new members have been added and four have been excluded based on updated criteria.

## Key Points

- Specified Stockbrokers are the top 20 brokers with the highest number of IBT/STWT clients and more than 10,000 registered clients (excluding closed accounts) as on March 31 of the previous financial year.
- Five new stockbrokers have been added to the Specified Stockbrokers list.
- Four stockbrokers have been excluded from the list as they no longer meet the revised criteria.
- Excluded members may continue to adhere to enhanced obligations on a voluntary basis.
- The full revised list now contains 35 Specified Members (Annexure A).

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the revised criteria/framework from SEBI circular ref. SEBI/HO/38/44/12(1)2026-MIRSD-TPD1 dated January 09, 2026, and NSE circular NSE/COMP/72254 dated January 09, 2026, on 'Review of framework to address Technical Glitch in Stock Brokers Electronic Trading Systems'.

## Compliance Requirements

**Newly Added Specified Stockbrokers (Table-1) — must comply with enhanced obligations within two months:**
1. Ashika Stock Services Limited
2. Marketwolf Securities Pvt Ltd
3. Moneywise Finvest Limited
4. PhonePe Wealth Broking Private Limited
5. Share India Securities Limited

**Excluded from Specified Stockbrokers (Table-2):**
1. Indiabulls Securities Limited
2. Profitmart Securities Pvt. Ltd.
3. Ventura Securities Ltd.
4. Zebu Share and Wealth Managements Pvt. Ltd.

All remaining Specified Members in Annexure A must continue to comply with the enhanced obligations and responsibilities outlined in the aforementioned SEBI and NSE circulars.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Date of this circular.
- **July 25, 2026**: Deadline for newly added Specified Stockbrokers to comply with enhanced obligations and responsibilities (two months from circular date).

## Impact Assessment

The revision directly impacts five newly designated stockbrokers — including PhonePe Wealth Broking and Share India Securities — who must upgrade their technical infrastructure and compliance processes within two months. The four excluded firms (Indiabulls, Profitmart, Ventura, Zebu) are relieved of mandatory enhanced obligations but may continue voluntarily. The 35-member revised list covers the largest retail-facing brokers in India, meaning the framework has broad systemic relevance for retail investor protection against trading disruptions.
