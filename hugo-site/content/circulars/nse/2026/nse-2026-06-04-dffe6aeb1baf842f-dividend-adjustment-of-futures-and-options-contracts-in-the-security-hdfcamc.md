## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts for HDFC Asset Management Company Limited (HDFCAMC) in accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments. A dividend of Rs 54 per share (face value Rs 5) has been declared, with an ex-date of June 5, 2026. All option strike prices for HDFCAMC contracts expiring June 30, 2026 are revised downward by Rs 54.

## Key Points

- Symbol: HDFCAMC (HDFC Asset Management Company Limited)
- Corporate action: Dividend of Rs 54 per share (face value Rs 5)
- Ex-date and effective date: June 5, 2026
- All OPTSTK contracts expiring June 30, 2026 have revised (reduced) strike prices effective from ex-date
- Revised strike prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places; these will be rounded to the nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively
- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract files before trading on the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI guidelines on adjustments to futures and options contracts on announcement of corporate actions, all option strike prices for HDFCAMC June 2026 expiry contracts are adjusted downward by the dividend amount of Rs 54. Example adjustments from Annexure 1:

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz and MII contract file (NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) and spread file (NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) on their trading applications **before** trading commences on June 5, 2026 (ex-date).
- Files are available from the directory `faoftp/faocommon` on the Extranet server and on the NSE website under all-reports-derivatives.
- Position adjustments methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Circular issued (NSE/FAOP/74549, Circular Ref. No. 79/2026)
- **June 5, 2026**: Ex-date and effective date — revised strike prices become applicable
- **June 30, 2026**: Expiry date of affected HDFCAMC OPTSTK contracts

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard, SEBI-mandated adjustment that ensures F&O contracts for HDFCAMC remain fairly priced after the dividend distribution. Traders holding long or short positions in HDFCAMC options and futures expiring June 30, 2026 will see their strike prices adjusted downward by Rs 54 effective June 5, 2026. The economic value of existing positions is preserved by the adjustment. Members failing to load updated contract files before the ex-date may face operational issues on their trading platforms.
