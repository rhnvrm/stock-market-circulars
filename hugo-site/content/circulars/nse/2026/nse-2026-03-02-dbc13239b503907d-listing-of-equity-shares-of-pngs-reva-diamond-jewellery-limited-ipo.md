## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73075, Circular Ref. No: 0353/2026) informing all exchange members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited via an IPO. The exact listing date has not been specified and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company name: PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited
- NSE trading symbol: PNGSREVA
- ISIN: INE1RDG01013
- Security type: Equity Shares (IPO)
- Listing date to be announced via a separate circular
- Circular issued by the Listing Department of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard pre-listing notification circular informing members of an upcoming new equity listing on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited and await the subsequent circular confirming the exact listing date before commencing trading in this security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 02, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals the imminent listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited on NSE under the symbol PNGSREVA. Members and investors interested in the IPO should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. The impact is moderate and limited to participants who subscribed to the IPO or plan to trade this new security upon listing.
