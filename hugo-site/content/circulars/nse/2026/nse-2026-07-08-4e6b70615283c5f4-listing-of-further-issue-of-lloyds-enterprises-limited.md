## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 09, 2026. The listing pertains to 2,10,62,615 equity shares arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.
- Company: Lloyds Enterprises Limited; Symbol: LLOYDSENT; Series: EQ.
- ISIN: INE080I01025; Face Value: Re. 1/-; Paid-up Value: Re. 1/-.
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares.
- Number of securities admitted: 2,10,62,615; Market lot: 1; Pari passu with existing shares: Yes.
- Lock-in: Not applicable.
- Distinctive number range: 1503044791 to 1524107405.
- Shares currently allotted in dematerialized mode are credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8080I01024) pending activation, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is a standard listing notification issued under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of further-issued securities and lot size disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize the newly listed shares under the designated security code and trade only in the specified lot size of 1 from the effective date. No additional action required from investors beyond standard trading procedures; the temporary ISIN will be reconciled with the permanent ISIN (INE080I01025) upon activation.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 08, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Lloyds Enterprises Limited shareholders and traders, as this reflects conversion of already-issued partly paid-up shares into fully paid-up status rather than a new capital raise. Trading liquidity in the fully paid-up shares may see a marginal increase, but there is no material impact on broader market operations or other listed securities.
