## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department (Circular Ref. No. 379/2026) updates the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective May 21–22, 2026. Four securities are newly included under ESM Stage I, two securities are escalated from Stage I to Stage II, no securities are downgraded from Stage II to Stage I, and no securities are excluded from the framework.

## Key Points

- Four securities added to ESM Stage I effective May 21, 2026: AUTOIND, HILTON, ONELIFECAP, SUNTECH
- Two securities escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective May 21, 2026: MCLEODRUSS, SHANKARA
- No securities removed from ESM; no Stage II-to-Stage I downgrades
- All newly included and stage-changed securities attract a minimum **100% margin** on open positions as of May 21, 2026 and on new positions from May 22, 2026
- Newly included Stage I securities shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series) w.e.f. May 22, 2026
- Stage II securities trade under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. May 21, 2026
- ESM classification is a surveillance action and should not be interpreted as an adverse finding against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ESM framework established via prior circulars: NSE/SURV/56948 (Jun 2, 2023), NSE/SURV/57609 (Jul 18, 2023), NSE/SURV/63361 (Aug 9, 2024), NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024), NSE/SURV/64400 (Oct 4, 2024), and NSE/SURV/69315 (Jul 25, 2025). The current update operationalises periodic review of securities meeting ESM inclusion criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers:** Must collect a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in ESM securities as of May 21, 2026, and on all new positions created from May 22, 2026 onwards
- **Trading systems:** Must route orders in newly included Stage I securities to the BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) series from May 22, 2026
- **Stage II securities (MCLEODRUSS, SHANKARA):** Orders must be routed through Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from May 21, 2026
- Members may direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026:** Circular issued
- **May 21, 2026:** ESM Stage I inclusions and Stage I→II escalations take effect; 100% margin applies to open positions; Stage II Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band begins
- **May 22, 2026:** Stage I securities shift to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST series); 100% margin applies to all new positions

## Impact Assessment

**Traders and investors** holding positions in AUTOIND, HILTON, ONELIFECAP, SUNTECH, MCLEODRUSS, or SHANKARA face immediate doubling of margin requirements and significant liquidity constraints due to the Trade-for-Trade settlement mechanism, which eliminates netting benefits.

**MCLEODRUSS and SHANKARA** face the more restrictive Stage II conditions — Periodic Call Auction with only a 2% daily price band — severely limiting intraday price movement and exit opportunities.

**Market liquidity** in all six securities will likely decline materially as arbitrageurs and short-term traders exit due to higher capital requirements and settlement restrictions.

The ESM designation is surveillance-driven and does not constitute a formal regulatory action against the companies themselves, but market perception may still exert downward price pressure.
