## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, effective July 15, 2026. Two new securities have been shortlisted for ESM Stage I, one security has moved from Stage I to Stage II, no securities moved from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities were excluded from the ESM framework. A consolidated list of securities currently under ESM Stage II was also published.

## Key Points

- Securities newly added to ESM Stage I (effective July 15, 2026): Hexagon Nutrition Limited (HEXAGON, INE0JUI01012) and Panache Digilife Limited (PANACHE, INE895W01019); both will also move from EQ/SM series to BE/ST series effective July 16, 2026.
- Security moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II: Setco Automotive Limited (SETCO, INE878E01021).
- No securities moved from ESM Stage II to Stage I.
- No securities were excluded from the ESM framework this cycle.
- The consolidated ESM list (Stage II) includes 35 securities such as Kesoram Industries, Peninsula Land, Sigachi Industries, Shivam Autotech, and Setco Automotive (newly added to this stage).

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the ongoing ESM framework administered jointly by NSE and BSE to curb excessive speculation in securities exhibiting high price volatility relative to their fundamentals. Movement between Stage I and Stage II typically reflects continued price/volume anomalies, subjecting securities to progressively stricter surveillance actions such as additional surveillance deposits, price bands, and trade-for-trade settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply revised margin, price band, and settlement (trade-for-trade) requirements to HEXAGON, PANACHE, and SETCO as per their new ESM stage classification.
- Brokers must reclassify HEXAGON and PANACHE from EQ/SM series to BE/ST series effective July 16, 2026, and communicate the change to clients.
- Surveillance and risk teams should update watchlists to reflect the revised Stage I and Stage II lists.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Revised ESM Stage I/Stage II classifications take effect.
- July 16, 2026: HEXAGON and PANACHE move from EQ/SM to BE/ST series.

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification increases trading friction for the affected securities, particularly reduced liquidity and stricter settlement (trade-for-trade in BE/ST series) for HEXAGON and PANACHE, and heightened surveillance for SETCO following its move to Stage II. Investors and traders holding these securities should expect increased margin requirements and potential price band restrictions, warranting closer monitoring of positions in these names.
