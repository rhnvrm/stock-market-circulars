## Summary

NSE has admitted four privately placed debt securities issued by Globe Fincap Limited and Globe Capital Market Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective May 20, 2026. All four securities carry a uniform coupon rate of 9.189% and mature in 2028. Trading is in series N0 with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Four debt securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from May 20, 2026
- All securities carry a coupon rate of 9.189% per annum
- Issuers are Globe Fincap Limited and Globe Capital Market Limited
- Market lot size for all securities is 1 unit
- Series designation: N0 for all four instruments
- Circular Ref. No: 0803/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74294

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for all trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- Securities shall be identified solely by their designated codes on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 19, 2026
- **Effective Date (Trading Commencement):** May 20, 2026
- **Maturity Date (9189GFL28, 9189GCM28A):** May 15, 2028
- **Maturity Date (9189GCM28, 9189GFL28A):** November 15, 2028
