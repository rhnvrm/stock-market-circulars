## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in the partly paid-up equity shares of Krishival Foods Limited (Symbol: KRISHPP, ISIN: IN90GGO01013) with effect from July 13, 2026 (closing hours of trading on July 10, 2026). The suspension is on account of the first and final call on the partly paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- Security suspended: Krishival Foods Limited partly paid-up equity shares, Symbol KRISHPP, ISIN IN90GGO01013
- Reason: Suspension on account of first & final call
- Suspension effective from July 13, 2026 (i.e., closing hours of trading on July 10, 2026)
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational suspension of a specific security carried out under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease trading in the KRISHPP scrip from the effective suspension date and update their systems accordingly. No other member action is specified in the circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 09, 2026
- Suspension effective: July 13, 2026 (closing hours of trading on July 10, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to the partly paid-up equity share class of Krishival Foods Limited, tied to the first and final call money process, and does not affect the company's fully paid-up shares. Impact is confined to holders and traders of the partly paid-up shares, with negligible effect on the broader market.
