## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has approved the listing and trading of equity shares of Orient Technologies Limited (Symbol: ORIENTTECH) issued pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 41,64,174 equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from January 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Orient Technologies Limited (ORIENTTECH)
- ISIN: INE0PPK01015
- Number of securities to be listed: 41,64,174 equity shares
- Distinctive Number Range: 41641743 to 45805916
- Securities issued pursuant to bonus issue
- Listing segment: Capital Market segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should identify the security by its designated code (Symbol: ORIENTTECH, ISIN: INE0PPK01015)
- The securities will be available for trading on the NSE Capital Market segment from the effective date

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 02, 2026
- Record Date: January 05, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: January 06, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: January 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains only to Orient Technologies Limited. The bonus issue will increase the number of outstanding shares of the company, potentially improving liquidity. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares in proportion to their holdings. No impact on broader market operations or trading mechanisms.
