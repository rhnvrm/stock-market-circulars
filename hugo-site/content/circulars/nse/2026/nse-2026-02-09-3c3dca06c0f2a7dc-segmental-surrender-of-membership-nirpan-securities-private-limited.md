## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has approved the request of Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI registration number: INZ000235432) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment. The surrender was approved on February 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Member Name: Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000235432
- Surrendered Segment: Currency Derivatives
- Surrender Approval Date: February 05, 2026
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/72695, Circular No. 11/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary surrender of segment membership.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this segmental surrender. No specific action required from other members.

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: February 09, 2026
- Surrender Approval Date: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action involving a single trading member's voluntary exit from the Currency Derivatives segment. No effect on other market participants or trading operations.
