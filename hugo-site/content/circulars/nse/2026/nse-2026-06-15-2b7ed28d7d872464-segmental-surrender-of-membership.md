## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental surrender of membership by two trading members. Shilpa Stock Broker Private Limited has surrendered its Commodity Derivative segment membership, and Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. has surrendered its Currency Derivatives (including IRF) segment membership. All exchange members are requested to take note of these changes.

## Key Points

- Shilpa Stock Broker Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INZ000201636) surrendered its Commodity Derivative segment membership effective June 09, 2026
- Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg: INZ000274132) surrendered its Currency Derivatives including IRF segment membership effective June 10, 2026
- Both surrenders have been formally approved by NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular serves as a notification of approved membership surrenders under existing exchange membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the segmental membership surrenders. No specific compliance action is required from other members beyond acknowledging the change in membership structure of the two affected firms.

## Important Dates

- Shilpa Stock Broker Private Limited — Commodity Derivative segment surrender approval: June 09, 2026
- Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. — Currency Derivatives including IRF segment surrender approval: June 10, 2026
- Circular issued: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The surrender of segmental memberships by these two brokers affects only their own operational scope within the respective segments. Other market participants in Commodity Derivatives and Currency Derivatives segments are unaffected. Clients of these firms who trade in the surrendered segments will need to make alternative arrangements with other exchange members.
