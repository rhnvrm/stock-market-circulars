## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued a circular (Ref: NSE/SURV/74893, Circular No. 474/2026) updating the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Three new securities are added to ESM Stage I, two securities move from Stage I to Stage II, and two securities move from Stage II back to Stage I, all effective June 29-30, 2026. Securities under ESM will be subject to 100% minimum margin and shifted to the Trade-for-Trade settlement segment.

## Key Points

- Three securities newly included in ESM Stage I: CYBERTECH, RADIOWALLA, and SLONE, effective June 29, 2026
- New ESM Stage I securities will move from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST series) effective June 30, 2026
- 100% minimum margin applies on all open positions as on June 29, 2026, and new positions from June 30, 2026
- GRCL and ZIMLAB are upgraded from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective June 29, 2026
- MCLEODRUSS and SHANKARA are downgraded from ESM Stage II to Stage I effective June 29, 2026
- Stage II securities are subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction effective June 29, 2026
- ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ESM is for market surveillance purposes only and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

The circular updates the ESM framework in reference to earlier circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. Securities exiting the ESM framework will have their price bands reinstated to pre-ESM levels, subject to no other surveillance measure applying.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must ensure 100% margin collection on ESM-listed securities for open positions as of June 29, 2026, and new positions from June 30, 2026
- Members must update their systems to reflect the series change from EQ/SM to BE/ST for newly included Stage I securities effective June 30, 2026
- Members trading Stage II securities must comply with the 2% price band restriction under Periodic Call Auction from June 29, 2026
- Members may direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 29, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusions effective; Stage I to Stage II and Stage II to Stage I movements effective; Stage II Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band begins; reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 30, 2026**: New ESM Stage I securities shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST (Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade); 100% minimum margin applicable on new positions

The mandatory 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding positions in these securities. The shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement eliminates netting benefits and restricts intraday trading, reducing liquidity. Stage II securities face additional restrictions via Periodic Call Auction with a tight 2% price band, further constraining price discovery and exit options for investors.
