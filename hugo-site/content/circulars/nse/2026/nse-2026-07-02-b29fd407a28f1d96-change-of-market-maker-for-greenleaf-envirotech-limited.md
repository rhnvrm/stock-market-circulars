## Summary

NSE has notified members of a change in the Market Maker for GREENLEAF ENVIROTECH LIMITED, an SME-segment listed company. Rikhav Securities Limited will be replaced by Shreni Shares Limited as the Market Maker, effective July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: GREENLEAF
- Company: GREENLEAF ENVIROTECH LIMITED
- Old Market Maker: Rikhav Securities Limited (Member Code - 12804)
- New Market Maker: Shreni Shares Limited (Member Code - 14109)
- Circular Ref. No: 93/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/CMTR/74995

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine administrative update under NSE's SME Market Maker framework; no changes to regulations or listing rules are involved.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, especially those dealing in GREENLEAF shares, should update their systems and records to reflect the new Market Maker effective the given date. No action is required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 3, 2026
- Circular Date: July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to liquidity provisioning mechanics for GREENLEAF ENVIROTECH LIMITED on the SME segment. Market Maker changes typically have minimal effect on trading unless liquidity support is disrupted during the transition; the switch to a new registered Market Maker should ensure continuity of two-way quotes for the scrip.
