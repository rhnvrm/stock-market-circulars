## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. The session includes trading from both the Primary Site and the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site. There is no new software version release for this mock. Members are also reminded of the upcoming mandatory migration to the new NEAT Adapter version and new CA certificate by July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for June 06, 2026 in the Commodity Derivatives segment
- No new software version release for this mock session
- Two trading sessions: Session-1 from Primary Site (10:00–13:00) and Session-2 from DR Site (13:45–14:30)
- New NEAT Adapter (Version 1.0.29 for Windows and Linux) mandatory from July 06, 2026 mock onwards
- New CA certificate available on Extranet at `/comtftp/comtcommon/Encryption_CA_Certificate/2026_Certificate`
- Applicable GR Port for new NEAT Adapter: 10869
- Members using NNF direct connection must download new CA certificate by June 5, 2026
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- Trades during mock sessions carry no financial obligation (no fund pay-in or pay-out)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced in this circular. Members are directed to existing circulars:
- NSE/MSD/74511 dated June 02, 2026 – New CA certificate
- NSE/MSD/74559 dated June 05, 2026 – New NEAT Adapter EXE application version
- NSE/MSD/73993 dated April 30, 2026 – Interactive connectivity details

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the new NEAT Adapter Version 1.0.29 (Windows/Linux) mandatory from July 06, 2026 mock session
- Members using Direct connection via NNF must download and install the new CA certificate
- Members must ensure migration readiness by July 06, 2026
- Members using NNF software must manually clear outstanding orders in their systems before each session
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved by the Exchange are permitted for trading

## Important Dates

- **June 06, 2026**: Mock trading session date
  - 10:00 – Normal Market open (Primary Site)
  - 11:00 – Contingency Start Time
  - 12:30 – Contingency End Time
  - 13:00 – Normal Market close (Primary Site)
  - 13:45 – Normal Market open (DR Site)
  - 14:30 – Normal Market close (DR Site)
  - 14:40 – Position Limit/Collateral value Set up cut off time
  - 14:40 – Trade Modification end time
  - 16:00 – Live Re-login start time
  - 16:30 – Live Re-login close time
- **July 06, 2026**: Mandatory use of new NEAT Adapter Version 1.0.29 and new CA certificate

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it pertains to a routine operational mock trading session with no financial settlement obligations. The primary impact is on Commodity Derivatives segment members who must prepare for the mandatory migration to the new NEAT Adapter version and CA certificate by July 06, 2026. Members using direct NNF connections should prioritize CA certificate installation and connectivity testing during this mock to ensure readiness for the July deadline.
