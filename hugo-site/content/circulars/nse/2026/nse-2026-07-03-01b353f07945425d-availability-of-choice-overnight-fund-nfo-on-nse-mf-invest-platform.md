## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Choice Overnight Fund NFO, managed by Choice Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Choice Overnight Fund NFO reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026.
- Facilities available include subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- Four scheme variants are covered, spanning Direct and Regular plans with Growth and Daily IDCW options.
- All schemes fall under the Overnight fund category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification regarding platform availability of a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure client transactions for the listed schemes are processed only through the NSE MF Invest Platform in accordance with standard mutual fund operating procedures from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 06, 2026 - reopening of ongoing subscriptions/redemptions and SIP/STP/SWP facility.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact, serving primarily as an operational update for NSE members and mutual fund distributors regarding platform availability of an Overnight category fund. No effect on listed equity securities or broader trading activity is expected.
