## Summary

NSE Surveillance (Circular Ref: NSE/SURV/74384, dated May 25, 2026) has announced a fortnightly Trade for Trade review. NIMBUS PROJECTS LIMITED (NIMBSPROJ) will be shifted from the Rolling segment (series: EQ) to the Trade for Trade segment (series: BE) with a price band of 5% or lower, effective May 29, 2026. Additionally, three securities will continue in the Trade for Trade segment (series: BE/BZ) under the current fortnightly surveillance review.

## Key Points

- NIMBSPROJ (NIMBUS PROJECTS LIMITED, ISIN: INE875B01015) shifted from EQ to BE series effective May 29, 2026
- Criteria for NIMBSPROJ: Price Earnings Multiple, Price Variation & Market Capitalization (as per BSE)
- No securities shifted from SM to ST series in this review
- Three securities continue in Trade for Trade (BE/BZ): MORARJEE, ONELIFECAP, SANCO
- No securities continuing in ST/SZ series in this review
- Settlement will be on a trade-to-trade basis; no netting off will be allowed
- Transfer is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse against the companies

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Capital Market Segment Trading Regulations Part - A, 2.6, the following changes take effect:
- NIMBSPROJ moves from Rolling segment (EQ) to Trade for Trade segment (BE) with a 5% or lower price band
- The security will consequently not be available in the rolling segment (series: EQ/SM)

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must note that NIMBSPROJ will no longer be available in the EQ rolling segment from May 29, 2026
- Members must take adequate precaution while trading in the listed securities, as settlement will be done on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off permitted
- For queries, members may contact: surveillance@nse.co.in
- Criteria for shifting securities to/from Trade for Trade segment are available at: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/movement-securities-periodic-review

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Circular issued
- **May 29, 2026 (Friday)**: Effective date for NIMBSPROJ shift from EQ to BE series
