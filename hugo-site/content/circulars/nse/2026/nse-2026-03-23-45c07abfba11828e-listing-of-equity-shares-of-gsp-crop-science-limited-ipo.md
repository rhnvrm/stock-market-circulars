## Summary

NSE has informed its members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of GSP Crop Science Limited on the National Stock Exchange of India. The company will trade under the symbol GSPCROP. The exact listing date has not been specified in this circular and will be communicated separately.

## Key Points

- Company: GSP Crop Science Limited
- Trading Symbol: GSPCROP
- ISIN: INE713R01022
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/73382, Circular Ref. No. 0488/2026
- The specific listing date will be announced via a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard IPO listing notification issued by the NSE Listing Department.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of GSPCROP and prepare for trading in the security once the listing date is confirmed through a subsequent circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of GSP Crop Science Limited adds a new equity security to the NSE. Impact is moderate and primarily relevant to investors and traders interested in the agrochemical/crop science sector. Broader market impact is limited. Members should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the official listing date before initiating any trading activity in GSPCROP.
