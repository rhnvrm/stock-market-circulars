## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has directed that MT Educare Limited (Symbol: MTEDUCARE, ISIN: INE472M01018) will be moved to Stage I of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) effective July 1, 2026. This imposes a mandatory 100% margin rate and a price band of 5% or lower on all trades in the security.

## Key Points

- MTEDUCARE (MT Educare Limited, ISIN: INE472M01018) is being placed under Stage I GSM from July 1, 2026.
- A 100% margin rate will be applicable on all trades in this security.
- Price band will be restricted to 5% or lower as applicable.
- This is a continuation of prior GSM framework circulars dating back to February 23, 2017.
- Members are advised to take adequate precaution while trading in this security.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Regulatory Changes

MT Educare Limited is being brought under Stage I of NSE's Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework as per circular NSE/SURV/74950 (Ref No. 483/2026). The GSM framework was originally established via circular NSE/SURV/34262 dated February 23, 2017, and has been updated through subsequent circulars including NSE/SURV/74795 dated June 19, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply a 100% margin rate on trades in MTEDUCARE from July 1, 2026.
- The applicable price band of 5% or lower must be enforced.
- Members should inform their clients of the enhanced surveillance restrictions on this security.
- For Stage II and beyond, additional obligations (Trade-for-Trade settlement, Additional Surveillance Deposit) would apply — currently not triggered for this security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 1, 2026 (Stage I GSM restrictions apply from this date)

## Impact Assessment

Trading in MTEDUCARE will become significantly more capital-intensive due to the 100% margin requirement, limiting participation to well-capitalised traders. The 5% price band further restricts intraday price movement. Retail investors and short-term traders are most affected. The placement under GSM Stage I signals that the exchange has identified unusual trading patterns or price-volume anomalies in this stock warranting enhanced oversight.
