## Summary

NSE has circulated SEBI's ex-parte interim order (WTM/AS/WRO/WRO-DIV-3/32505/2026-27 dated July 24, 2026) restraining five entities—Surabhi Chauhan, Zahin Ismail Jessani, Stark Investments, Starkblue Ventures LLP, and Shakuntala Davendra Singh—from the securities market for allegedly conducting unregistered Investment Advisory (UIA) and unregistered Portfolio Management Services (UPMS) activities. The order followed 16 unique complaints against the entities.

## Key Points

- SEBI received 16 unique complaints alleging unregistered investment advisory and portfolio management services by Ms. Surabhi Chauhan, Mr. Zahin Ismail Jessani, and/or through Stark Investments and Starkblue Ventures LLP.
- Stark Investments is a partnership firm of Mr. Zahin Ismail Jessani and Ms. Shakuntala Davendra Singh (mother of Ms. Surabhi Chauhan), each holding 50% controlling interest.
- Ms. Surabhi Chauhan and Mr. Zahin Ismail Jessani are designated partners and authorised signatories of Starkblue Ventures LLP.
- SEBI has restrained all five entities from buying, selling, or dealing in securities, or associating with the securities market in any manner, directly or indirectly, until further orders.
- Entities with open derivative positions may close/square off within 3 months from the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Entities are permitted to settle pay-in/pay-out obligations for transactions that took place before close of trading on the order date.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an enforcement action under Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11B(1) and 11D of the SEBI Act, 1992, not a change to trading rules or regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure the named entities (Surabhi Chauhan - PAN AOFPC0570C, Zahin Ismail Jessani - PAN AGNPJ2499P, Stark Investments - PAN AEJFS2830K, Starkblue Ventures LLP - PAN AERFS0106E, Shakuntala Davendra Singh - PAN DVJPS6011Q) are not permitted to trade or deal in securities.
- Members should refer to the consolidated list of debarred entities on the NSE website (https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities).
- Queries can be directed to dl-invsg-all@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: July 24, 2026
- Open derivative positions must be closed/squared off within 3 months of the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct impact on listed stocks or broader market trading, as it targets specific unregistered advisory/portfolio management entities rather than listed companies. Impact is limited to the restrained individuals and entities, and to NSE members who must ensure compliance by not facilitating trading for these debarred parties. The circular serves an informational and compliance-monitoring purpose for member firms.
