## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of a privately placed Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) issued by Muthoot Finance Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from June 16, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Security symbol **865MFL31** (Series: N0) listed under the NSE Capital Market segment.
- Issue description: **MFL 8.65% 2031** — a fixed-coupon NCD with an 8.65% coupon rate.
- ISIN: **INE414G07KB6**; market lot size: **1**.
- Effective date of trading: **June 16, 2026**.
- Lot size and trading codes notified in the Annexure as required under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is routine and carried out under existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must identify and trade the security exclusively by its designated code **865MFL31**.
- Trading must be conducted in the notified lot size of **1**.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date (Trading Commences):** June 16, 2026
- **Date of Maturity:** June 12, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact — this is a standard admission of a single privately placed NCD to the capital market segment. Relevant primarily to fixed-income investors and members dealing in debt securities. Muthoot Finance's 8.65% NCD maturing in 2031 provides a reference yield for NBFC debt instruments in the secondary market.
