## Summary

Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (the Promoter/Seller), a promoter of Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd, has notified BSE and NSE of a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of equity shares via the stock exchange mechanism. The base offer comprises up to 1,963,994 equity shares of face value ₹10 each (7.22% of total paid-up capital), with an oversubscription option for an additional 202,634 shares (0.75%). The OFS is scheduled across two days: T day for non-retail investors and T+1 day for retail investors and non-retail investors carrying forward unallotted bids.

## Key Points

- Seller: Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (formerly Robert Bosch Engineering and Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd.), CIN: U72400KA1997PTC023164
- Company: Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd
- Base Offer Size: up to 1,963,994 equity shares (7.22% of total paid-up equity capital)
- Oversubscription Option: up to 202,634 additional equity shares (0.75% of total paid-up equity capital)
- Face value of equity shares: ₹10 each
- T day (non-retail investors only): Thursday, June 18, 2026
- T+1 day (retail investors + non-retail carry-forward): Friday, June 19, 2026
- OFS conducted through designated windows of BSE and NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is conducted under existing frameworks:
- SEBI OFS Circular: SEBI/HO/MRD2/PoD-2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024
- BSE OFS Circular: Notice No. 20240701-19 dated July 1, 2024
- NSE OFS Circular: No. 25/2026 dated February 24, 2026

## Compliance Requirements

- The offer shall be conducted exclusively through the Seller's designated broker on the stock exchange mechanism
- Non-retail investors must participate on T day (June 18, 2026); retail investors participate on T+1 day (June 19, 2026)
- Unallotted non-retail bids from T day may be carried forward to T+1 day
- Restrictions apply on distribution/release of this notice in the United States (except to Qualified Institutional Buyers) and other restricted jurisdictions

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Date of notice issued to BSE and NSE
- **June 18, 2026 (T day)**: OFS open for non-retail investors only
- **June 19, 2026 (T+1 day)**: OFS open for retail investors and non-retail investors carrying forward unallotted bids

## Impact Assessment

This OFS represents a significant promoter stake sale of 7.22% (up to 7.97% if oversubscription option exercised) in Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd. The dual-day OFS structure provides price discovery for institutional investors on T day before retail participation on T+1 day. Investors should note the potential dilution of promoter shareholding and assess market liquidity impact given the size of the offering. The oversubscription option provides the seller additional flexibility to offload shares if demand is strong.
