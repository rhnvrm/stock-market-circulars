## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange, effective June 30, 2026, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The circular covers fresh issuances of Commercial Papers (CPs) by five issuers and a Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) issuance by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited.

## Key Points

- Commercial Papers admitted to dealings:
  - Tata Communications Limited — TCM CP 28/09/26, ISIN INE151A14313, 7,000 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹491,664.5, Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 28-Sep-2026
  - Tata Capital Limited — TCL CP 12/03/27, ISIN INE976I14RR7, 5,000 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹475,583.5, Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 12-Mar-2027
  - Tata Capital Limited — TCL CP 28/09/26, ISIN INE976I14RQ9, 5,000 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹491,664.5, Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 28-Sep-2026
  - Godrej Industries Limited — GIL CP 28/09/26 Sr 37, ISIN INE233A149H1, 1,500 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹491,676.5, Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 28-Sep-2026
  - Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited — HPCL CP 25/09/26 Sr 50, ISIN INE094A14KG4, 20,000 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹491,970, Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 25-Sep-2026
  - Jio Credit Limited — JCL CP 29/07/26, ISIN INE282H14139, 4,000 units, Face Value ₹500,000, Issue Price ₹497,200.50 (per unit), Allotment 29-Jun-2026, Redemption 29-Jul-2026
- Other Debt Security admitted:
  - Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited — CIFCL 8.88% 2033 Sr SD80, ISIN INE121A08PZ6, 200,000 units, Face Value ₹100,000, Coupon 8.88%, Allotment 25-Jun-2026, Redemption 25-Jun-2033, issued at multiple issue prices (₹1,00,000 to ₹1,00,010 across tranches)

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, formalizing admission of privately placed debt instruments to the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond noting the new ISINs and security codes for trading and settlement on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: June 30, 2026
- Allotment dates: 25-Jun-2026 (CIFCL NCD) and 29-Jun-2026 (all CPs)
- Redemption dates range from 29-Jul-2026 (Jio Credit CP) to 25-Jun-2033 (CIFCL NCD)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is an administrative listing notification relevant primarily to institutional debt market participants and members dealing in these specific privately placed instruments. No impact on equity trading or retail investors.
