## Summary

NSE has issued this circular under the Long-Term ASM (Additional Surveillance Measure) Framework announcing stage changes and exclusions effective **May 25, 2026**. No new securities have been shortlisted for Stage I or Stage IV entry. Three securities are being moved to lower (less restrictive) ASM stages, and at least thirteen securities are being fully excluded from the ASM framework.

## Key Points

- No new securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I or Stage IV.
- No securities escalated to higher ASM stages (all upward-movement lists show Nil).
- One security downgraded from Stage IV to Stage III: **SIGMAADV** (Sigma Advanced Systems Limited, INE933B01012).
- One security downgraded from Stage III to Stage II: **ATLANTAELE** (Atlanta Electricals Limited, INE0Z4F01028).
- One security downgraded from Stage II to Stage I: **RPSGVENT** (RPSG Ventures Limited, INE425Y01011).
- At least thirteen securities excluded from ASM framework entirely (see Annexure II).
- Scrips in Stage IV under Criteria VII are subject to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) settlement on a T+3 basis from the date of circular issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants must update their systems to reflect the revised ASM stage classifications from May 25, 2026.
- Securities moving out of Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade) will revert to normal Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) as applicable to their new stage.
- Excluded securities will no longer be subject to ASM-related margin and trading restrictions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 25, 2026 (all stage changes and exclusions)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **net positive (easing) impact** for affected securities. No securities are being placed under enhanced surveillance or moved to higher-restriction stages. Three securities benefit from stage downgrades, and at least thirteen securities are fully removed from the ASM framework, restoring normal trading conditions including Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) where applicable. Market participants holding positions in excluded or downgraded securities may see improved liquidity and reduced margin requirements from May 25, 2026.
