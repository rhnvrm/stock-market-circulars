## Summary

NSE has implemented a temporary restriction on lumpsum subscriptions in TATA Gold ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) under TATA Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective after cut-off time (3:00 PM) on June 8, 2026. The restriction caps lumpsum purchases and switch-ins at INR 10 lakh per PAN per calendar month at the first holder level. Redemptions, Switch-outs, and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP) continue to operate normally.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum purchases and switch-ins into TATA Gold ETF Fund of Fund are restricted to a maximum of INR 10 lakh per PAN per calendar month (at first holder level).
- Restriction applies to transactions received after cut-off time of 3:00 PM on June 8, 2026.
- Redemptions, Switch-outs, and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP) remain fully operational.
- For Tata Gold Exchange Traded Fund (the underlying ETF), large investor subscriptions directly with Tata Mutual Fund (minimum Rs. 25 crore) are also suspended from June 8, 2026; Market Makers/Authorized Participants are exempt from this restriction.
- The restriction is temporary and will continue until further notice.

## Regulatory Changes

An Addendum to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) has been issued by TATA Mutual Fund dated June 5, 2026. The addendum modifies the subscription terms for:
1. **Tata Gold Exchange Traded Fund**: Large investor direct subscriptions (≥ Rs. 25 crore) suspended effective June 8, 2026. Market Makers/Authorized Participants are exempt.
2. **Tata Gold ETF Fund of Fund (FOF)**: Lumpsum purchases/switch-ins capped at Rs. 10 lakh per PAN per calendar month (first holder level), effective after 3:00 PM on June 8, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must inform clients about the lumpsum subscription restriction in TATA Gold ETF Fund of Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- All transactions submitted after 3:00 PM on June 8, 2026 must comply with the INR 10 lakh per PAN per calendar month limit for lumpsum purchases and switch-ins.
- Members should note that the restriction does not apply to Redemptions, Switch-outs, or SWP transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Addendum Date**: June 5, 2026
- **Effective Date/Time**: After cut-off time (3:00 PM) on June 8, 2026
- **Duration**: Temporary — until further notice from TATA Mutual Fund

## Impact Assessment

Investors seeking to make large lumpsum investments in TATA Gold ETF Fund of Fund via the NSE MF Invest Platform will be restricted to INR 10 lakh per PAN per calendar month. This may affect high-net-worth individuals and investors seeking significant gold exposure through the FOF route. The restriction is driven by broader economic and market conditions as cited by TATA Mutual Fund. Existing investors are not impacted in terms of redemptions or systematic withdrawals. The temporary nature of the restriction suggests it may be lifted once market conditions stabilize.
