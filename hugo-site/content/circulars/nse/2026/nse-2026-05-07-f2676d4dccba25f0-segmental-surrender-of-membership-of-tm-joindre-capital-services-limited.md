## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Joindre Capital Services Ltd (SEBI registration number INZ000174034) to surrender their membership in the Currency Derivatives including Interest Rate Futures (IRF) segment. The surrender was approved on April 30, 2026, and all exchange members have been notified to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Joindre Capital Services Ltd has surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivatives including IRF segment.
- SEBI registration number: INZ000174034.
- Surrender approval date: April 30, 2026.
- The circular is issued under NSE Circular Ref. No. 42/2026 (Download Ref. No.: NSE/COMP/74099).

## Regulatory Changes

No broad regulatory changes. This is an administrative action specific to Joindre Capital Services Ltd's exit from the Currency Derivatives including IRF segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the surrender of Joindre Capital Services Ltd's membership in the Currency Derivatives including IRF segment and update their records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: Surrender approval date for Joindre Capital Services Ltd's Currency Derivatives including IRF segment membership.
- **May 7, 2026**: Date of NSE circular notification to all members.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the operational status of Joindre Capital Services Ltd in the Currency Derivatives including IRF segment. Other market participants and the broader market are not directly affected. Clients of Joindre Capital Services Ltd who traded in Currency Derivatives or IRF through this member should make alternative arrangements if needed.
