## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members that Crown Leasing and Finance Limited, a company exclusively listed on a de-recognized/non-operational/exited stock exchange and placed on NSE's Dissemination Board (DB), will be removed from the Dissemination Board effective July 07, 2026. The removal follows the company's exercise of the Exit Option under applicable SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Crown Leasing and Finance Limited is being removed from NSE's Dissemination Board.
- Reason for removal: Exit Option exercised by the company.
- The removal takes effect from July 07, 2026.
- Action is taken pursuant to SEBI circulars dated October 10, 2016 and August 01, 2017, and Chapter 6 of the SEBI Master Circular dated October 16, 2023.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this circular implements existing SEBI framework provisions governing exclusively listed companies of de-recognized, non-operational, or exited stock exchanges that were placed on the Dissemination Board.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to note the removal of Crown Leasing and Finance Limited from the Dissemination Board and update their records/systems accordingly; no shareholder or trading action is prescribed beyond this notification.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 06, 2026
- Effective Date of Removal from Dissemination Board: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and administrative in nature, affecting only shareholders and members tracking Crown Leasing and Finance Limited on the Dissemination Board. There is no broader market-wide trading or listing impact, as this pertains to a single company's exit from a mechanism meant for exclusively listed entities of de-recognized exchanges.
