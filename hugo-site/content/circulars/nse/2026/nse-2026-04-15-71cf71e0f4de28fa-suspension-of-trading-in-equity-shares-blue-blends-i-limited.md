## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying the suspension of trading in equity shares of Blue Blends (I) Limited (Symbol: BLUEBLENDS) effective April 17, 2026 (i.e., from closing hours of April 16, 2026). The suspension is mandated under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, on account of capital reduction pursuant to orders from the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) and the National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT).

## Key Points

- Trading in equity shares of Blue Blends (I) Limited (BLUEBLENDS) will be suspended from April 17, 2026
- The suspension takes effect from the closing hours of trading on April 16, 2026
- Reason: Capital Reduction pursuant to NCLT Order dated December 06, 2024
- Suspension further upheld by NCLAT Order dated February 18, 2026
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/73742 | Circular Ref. No: 0622/2026

## Regulatory Changes

Under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, NSE is empowered to suspend trading in securities under specified circumstances. This circular invokes that regulation to implement the suspension of BLUEBLENDS equity shares as a consequence of a court-ordered capital reduction process.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified and must ensure no trading in BLUEBLENDS equity shares from April 17, 2026 onwards
- Members should communicate the suspension to their clients holding BLUEBLENDS shares
- No further transactions in this security are permitted on the NSE Capital Market segment post suspension effective date

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** December 06, 2024 (Capital Reduction order)
- **NCLAT Order Date:** February 18, 2026 (Appellate confirmation)
- **Last Trading Day:** April 16, 2026 (up to closing hours)
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Circular Date:** April 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension will have a significant impact on existing shareholders and investors holding equity shares of Blue Blends (I) Limited. Once suspended, the shares will no longer be tradeable on NSE, resulting in complete illiquidity for BLUEBLENDS equity. The capital reduction ordered by NCLT and upheld by NCLAT indicates a court-supervised restructuring of the company's share capital, which may reduce the face value or number of equity shares held by investors. Shareholders should monitor further announcements from the company or regulatory authorities regarding the terms of the capital reduction and any resultant entitlements or compensations.
