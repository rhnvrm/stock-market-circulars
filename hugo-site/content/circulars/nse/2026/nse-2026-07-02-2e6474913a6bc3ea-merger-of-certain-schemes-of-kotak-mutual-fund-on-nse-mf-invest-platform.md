## Summary

NSE has notified members of the merger of Kotak Nifty SDL Jul 2026 Index Fund (Merging Scheme) into Kotak CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9 to 12 Months Debt Index Fund (Surviving Scheme), both plans/options of Kotak Mutual Fund. The merger will take effect after close of business hours on July 31, 2026, as per the notice-cum-addendum issued by Kotak Mutual Fund under Regulation 22(9)(c) of the SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kotak Nifty SDL Jul 2026 Index Fund will merge into Kotak CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9 to 12 Months Debt Index Fund.
- The merger constitutes a change in fundamental attributes under SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026.
- Investors not in favour of the merger may redeem or switch to other Kotak Mutual Fund schemes without payment of exit load during the specified window via the NSE MF Invest platform.
- Existing SIP/XSIP/SWP instructions will continue to be processed in the merging scheme during the exit-load-free period.
- After the merger is effective, the merging scheme will cease to exist, and registered SIP/XSIP/STP/SWP instructions will be shifted to the surviving scheme.
- Details of the schemes/plans/options being merged are provided in Annexure-I to the circular.

## Regulatory Changes

The scheme merger is executed under Regulation 22(9)(c) of the SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026, which governs changes in the fundamental attributes of a mutual fund scheme requiring unitholder notice and an exit option.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE must inform relevant clients holding units in the Merging Scheme about the exit-load waiver window and facilitate redemption or switch requests through the NSE MF Invest platform if clients do not wish to continue in the surviving scheme post-merger.

## Important Dates

- Exit load waiver period: July 2, 2026 to July 31, 2026 (both days inclusive, up to 3:00 PM on July 31, 2026)
- Effective date of merger: After close of business hours on July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund scheme merger with limited market-wide impact, relevant primarily to investors holding units in the Kotak Nifty SDL Jul 2026 Index Fund through the NSE MF Invest platform. Members should ensure timely communication to affected clients regarding the exit window and post-merger transition of systematic transactions to avoid unintended continuation in the surviving scheme.
