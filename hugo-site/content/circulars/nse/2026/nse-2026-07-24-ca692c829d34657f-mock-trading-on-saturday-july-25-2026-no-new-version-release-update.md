## Summary

NSE has issued an updated circular revising the mock trading session schedule for the Currency Derivatives (CD) segment scheduled on Saturday, July 25, 2026. This circular supersedes the earlier mock circular NSE/CD/75370 dated July 23, 2026, and members are required to follow the updated session timings and other participation details outlined below.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session in the Currency Derivatives segment will be conducted on July 25, 2026.
- This circular supersedes and updates NSE/CD/75370 dated July 23, 2026.
- Trading Session-1 will run from the Primary Site.
- Trading Session-2 will run from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site, including a trade modification end time.
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session; NNF software users must manually clear orders.
- Trades executed during the mock session will not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out.
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN records uploaded and approved before cutoff will be permitted; UCC validation will be skipped during contingency order entry time.
- The NOTIS application will not be accessible during the mock session.
- Members should participate using all trading software and complete live re-login to ensure smooth connectivity for the next trading day, Monday, July 27, 2026.
- NEAT Version applicable: 3.5.4.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update revising previously announced mock session timings referenced in SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 regarding software testing requirements via Mock Trading or Simulated Environment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members enabled for CD segment trading in the live market are automatically eligible; no separate registration needed.
- Ensure telnet connectivity to the Exchange host and correct Box ID setup for the segment.
- NNF application users must ensure User ID is dealer type, converted for NNF, and mapped with the IP.
- Members must set appropriate branch/user limits.
- Members are required to participate in the mock session and complete live re-login by the specified close time to ensure readiness for Monday, July 27, 2026 trading.

## Important Dates

- Mock Date: July 25, 2026
- Trading Session-1 (Primary Site) Normal Market open/close: 13:15–15:15 hrs
- Trading Session-2 (DR Site) Normal Market open/close: 16:00–17:00 hrs
- Trade Modification end time: 17:10 hrs
- Live Re-login window: 19:00–19:30 hrs

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to a scheduled system/technical mock trading exercise in the Currency Derivatives segment, not live trading. It is operationally important for member firms to ensure system readiness, connectivity, and compliance ahead of live trading resumption on Monday, July 27, 2026, but carries no financial or price implications for the market or specific stocks.
