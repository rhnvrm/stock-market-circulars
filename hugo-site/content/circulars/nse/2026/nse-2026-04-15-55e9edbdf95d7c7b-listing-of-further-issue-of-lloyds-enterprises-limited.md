## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENT) on the Capital Market segment, effective April 16, 2026. The listing covers 3,21,634 equity shares arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE080I01025.

## Key Points

- 3,21,634 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from April 16, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid-up equity shares (temporary ISIN IN8080I01024) to fully paid-up equity shares (ISIN INE080I01025)
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are Pari Passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- Distinctive numbers range: 1404286895 to 1404608528
- Circular ref: NSE/CML/73756 | Circular Ref. No: 0630/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, and SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 governing ISIN activation for additional share issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol LLOYDSENT and security codes for all trades in these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- Shares were credited under temporary ISIN (IN8080I01024) in dematerialized mode prior to activation; the permanent ISIN INE080I01025 is now applicable

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 15, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up shares increases the tradeable float of LLOYDSENT by 3,21,634 shares. As the shares are Pari Passu with existing equity and carry no lock-in, they are immediately eligible for trading. The impact is confined to existing shareholders who held partly paid-up shares and to traders in LLOYDSENT stock.
