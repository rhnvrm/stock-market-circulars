## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of a privately placed debt security issued by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment, effective March 25, 2026. The security is a non-convertible debenture carrying a coupon rate of 7.94% maturing in March 2029.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to NSE Capital Market segment effective March 25, 2026
- Issuer: Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited
- Symbol: 794CIFCL29, Series: N0
- Issue Description: CIFCL 7.94% 2029 Sr 662
- ISIN: INE121A07SS7
- Market Lot: 1
- Coupon Rate: 7.94%
- Date of Maturity: March 20, 2029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (794CIFCL29) for trading identification on the system.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 as per the Annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 24, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 25, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)
- **Maturity Date:** March 20, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard listing notification for a single privately placed NCD by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited. It enables trading of the security on NSE's capital market segment from March 25, 2026. Relevant primarily to institutional investors and members dealing in debt securities of NBFC issuers.
