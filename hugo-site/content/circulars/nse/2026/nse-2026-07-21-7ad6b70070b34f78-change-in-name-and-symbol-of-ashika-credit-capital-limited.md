## Summary

NSE has notified members that Ashika Credit Capital Limited, currently under the Permitted to Trade category, will undergo a change in its corporate name and trading symbol effective July 27, 2026. The company will be renamed Ashika Global Securities Limited and its symbol will change from ASHIKA to ASHIKAG.

## Key Points

- Existing Symbol: ASHIKA; New Symbol: ASHIKAG
- Existing Name: Ashika Credit Capital Limited; New Name: Ashika Global Securities Limited
- The security is classified under the Permitted to Trade category on NSE
- Change effective from July 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes; this is an administrative update reflecting the company's name and symbol change in NSE's Permitted to Trade category records.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to update their systems and records to reflect the new name (Ashika Global Securities Limited) and new symbol (ASHIKAG) effective July 27, 2026, for all trading and reporting purposes related to this security.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/CML/75304, Circular Ref. No: 1184/2026)
- July 27, 2026: Effective date of name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and purely administrative, affecting only identifier references (name and symbol) for this Permitted to Trade security. Members should ensure their trading and back-office systems are updated to avoid reconciliation errors post the effective date.
