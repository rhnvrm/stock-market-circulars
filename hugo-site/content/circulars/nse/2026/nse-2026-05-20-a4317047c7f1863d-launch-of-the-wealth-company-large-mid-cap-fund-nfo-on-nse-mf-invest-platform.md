## Summary

NSE has informed its members that The Wealth Company Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO (New Fund Offer) will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 21, 2026 to June 4, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The fund belongs to The Wealth Company Mutual Fund and offers six scheme variants spanning direct and regular plans with growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: May 21, 2026 to June 4, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants available across direct and regular plan categories
- All schemes are classified under the Equity category (Large & Mid Cap)
- Allotment date for all schemes: June 10, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by June 9, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Contact for support: 1800 2100 940 / support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch communication under existing NSE MF Invest Platform operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds (via any payment mode) are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 9, 2026 at 11:59 PM.
- Members should make the six scheme options available for client transactions on the platform within the NFO window.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| NFO Opens | May 21, 2026 |
| NFO Closes | June 4, 2026 (11:59 PM) |
| Clear Funds Deadline | June 9, 2026 (11:59 PM) |
| Allotment Date | June 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The six scheme variants cover a broad range of investor preferences (growth vs. dividend, direct vs. regular), which may attract retail and institutional participation in the large & mid cap equity segment. Operational impact is limited to ensuring fund availability and timely payment settlement by the stated deadlines. No market-wide or systemic impact is anticipated.
