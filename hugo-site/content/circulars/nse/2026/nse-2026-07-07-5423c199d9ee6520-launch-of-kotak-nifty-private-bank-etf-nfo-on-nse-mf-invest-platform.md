## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF NFO, launched under Kotak Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 8, 2026 to July 15, 2026. The circular provides scheme details, ISIN, category, and allotment date, along with the funds pay-in deadline for members.

## Key Points

- Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF (Direct Growth and Regular Growth options) will be open for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- ISIN for the scheme: INF174KA1A94.
- Category: Equity.
- Allotment date: July 20, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 17, 2026, 12:00 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting in this NFO must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 17, 2026 till 12:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 8, 2026 to July 15, 2026
- Funds pay-in deadline: July 17, 2026, 12:00 pm
- Allotment date: July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact, being primarily relevant to NSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions on the MF Invest Platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, surveillance measures, or corporate actions for any specific stock.
