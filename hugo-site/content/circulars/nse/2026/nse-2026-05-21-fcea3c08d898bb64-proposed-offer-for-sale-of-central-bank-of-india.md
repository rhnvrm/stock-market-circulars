## Summary

NSE has notified all Capital Market segment trading members of a Proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Central Bank of India, scheduled for May 22, 2026 and May 25, 2026. The OFS will be conducted in accordance with the interoperability framework among Clearing Corporations as per Exchange Scheme Circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- OFS dates: May 22, 2026 (Day 1) and May 25, 2026 (Day 2)
- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate via the NSE eOFS platform
- eOFS platform URL: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Latest eOFS WEB API documentation is available on the NSE website
- Members without Admin user IDs must register via the ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under: ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS
- Admin user ID requests are processed at 11:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., and 3:00 p.m.; requests post 3:00 p.m. are processed the next day by 8:45 a.m.
- Only one Admin user creation request can be raised; requests cannot be modified once submitted

## Regulatory Changes

The OFS is being conducted under the interoperability framework among Clearing Corporations as specified in circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026. No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure compliant UCC of self (PRO) and clients are updated in the Unique Client Identification (UCI) system **at least one day prior** to the OFS bidding session
- Members or clients with UCC updated or becoming compliant on the day of the OFS bidding session will not be permitted to participate, and orders will not be accepted
- Members yet to obtain Admin user IDs must register on the ENIT portal before the OFS dates
- Members should ensure only one Admin user creation request is raised per member

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: OFS bidding session Day 1
- **May 25, 2026**: OFS bidding session Day 2
- **May 21, 2026 (by end of day)**: Deadline to ensure UCC compliance in UCI system for Day 1 participation
- **May 24, 2026 (by end of day)**: Deadline to ensure UCC compliance in UCI system for Day 2 participation

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant primarily to Capital Market segment trading members who intend to participate in the Central Bank of India OFS on behalf of themselves (PRO) or their clients. Members must proactively verify UCC compliance well before the bidding sessions to avoid order rejection. The eOFS platform is the sole participation channel; members without Admin user IDs face exclusion if they do not register in advance. Impact is moderate, confined to members and clients intending to trade in this specific OFS.
