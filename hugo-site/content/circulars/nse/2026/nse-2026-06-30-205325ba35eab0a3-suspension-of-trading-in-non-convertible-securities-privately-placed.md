## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Security issued by National Housing Bank (ISIN: INE557F08FS6) effective July 1, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the security, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- National Housing Bank's privately placed NCD (ISIN: INE557F08FS6) will be suspended from trading on July 1, 2026.
- The reason for suspension is redemption of the security.
- The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- Issued by NSE Listing Department under Circular Ref. No. 1048/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74939).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading upon maturity/redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in ISIN INE557F08FS6 (National Housing Bank NCD) with effect from July 1, 2026.
- No action required beyond halting trades in the specified security from the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** July 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is minimal and limited. The suspension is a standard end-of-life action for a privately placed Non-Convertible Security upon its redemption. Holders of ISIN INE557F08FS6 will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the security. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
