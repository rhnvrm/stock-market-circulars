## Summary

NSE has admitted four privately placed debt securities of Dar Credit & Capital Limited (DCCL) for trading on the capital market segment effective February 05, 2026. The securities include two series with 12% coupon rate maturing in 2029 and two series with 14% coupon rate maturing in 2031. All securities will trade with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Four debt securities of Dar Credit & Capital Limited admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Two securities (12DCCL29A and 12DCCL29) with 12% coupon rate maturing February 03, 2029
- Two securities (14DCCL31A and 14DCCL31) with 14% coupon rate maturing February 03, 2031
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading series: N0 for all four securities
- Four unique ISIN codes assigned: INE04Q907157, INE04Q907124, INE04Q907165, INE04Q907116

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all securities)
- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 05, 2026 - Trading commences for all four securities
- **Maturity Date (12% securities)**: February 03, 2029
- **Maturity Date (14% securities)**: February 03, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities of a single issuer. The securities are likely held by institutional investors and will have limited liquidity given the private placement nature. The listing provides an exit mechanism for investors but does not affect broader market operations or trading activities. No action required by general market participants unless they hold or wish to trade these specific debt instruments.
