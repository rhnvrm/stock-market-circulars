## Summary

NSE has announced, based on communication from UTI Mutual Fund, a temporary restriction on lumpsum subscriptions in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Effective June 9, 2026, lumpsum purchases and switch-ins into the UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) are capped at INR 10 Lakh per PAN per calendar month at the first holder level. Additionally, UTI Gold ETF itself has restricted large investor direct subscriptions (minimum Rs. 25 crore) effective June 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum subscriptions in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund are temporarily restricted effective June 9, 2026
- Maximum lumpsum purchase/switch-in limit: INR 10 Lakh per PAN per calendar month (at first holder level)
- Transactions received before cut-off time (3:00 PM) on June 8, 2026 are exempt from this restriction and will be processed at applicable NAV
- UTI Gold ETF restricts large investor direct subscriptions (minimum Rs. 25 crore) from June 8, 2026
- Restriction is temporary and remains in force until further notice
- All other terms and conditions of the schemes remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

UTI Mutual Fund issued Addendum No. 18/2026-27 dated June 06, 2026, introducing temporary restrictions on lumpsum subscriptions in both UTI Gold ETF and UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund. NSE has implemented these changes on the NSE MF Invest Platform in accordance with the addendum.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members and investors must adhere to the INR 10 Lakh per PAN per calendar month limit for lumpsum purchases/switch-ins into UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund
- Large investors seeking to directly subscribe to UTI Gold ETF with a minimum of Rs. 25 crore must note that such transactions will not be accepted from June 8, 2026
- Distributors and investment advisers should communicate the revised subscription limits to their clients

## Important Dates

- **June 6, 2026**: UTI Mutual Fund issued Addendum No. 18/2026-27
- **June 8, 2026**: Restriction on large investor direct subscriptions in UTI Gold ETF takes effect; transactions received before 3:00 PM cut-off are exempt from the FOF restriction
- **June 9, 2026**: INR 10 Lakh per PAN per calendar month cap on lumpsum purchases/switch-ins in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund takes effect

## Impact Assessment

This restriction moderately impacts investors in UTI Gold ETF and UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund, particularly high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors seeking large lumpsum gold allocations. Retail investors with smaller investment amounts below INR 10 Lakh per month are largely unaffected. The restriction is driven by prevailing economic and market conditions, suggesting UTI Mutual Fund is managing inflow risks in its gold-related schemes. SIP and other non-lumpsum transactions are not mentioned as restricted. The temporary nature of the restriction may be lifted once market conditions normalize.
