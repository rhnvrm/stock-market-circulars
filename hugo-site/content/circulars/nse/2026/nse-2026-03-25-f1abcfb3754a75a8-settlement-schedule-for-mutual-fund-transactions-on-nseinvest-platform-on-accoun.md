## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a revised settlement schedule for mutual fund transactions on the NSEInvest (MFSS) platform due to a clearing holiday (Annual Bank Closing) on April 1, 2026. Participants must adjust their processing expectations for subscription and redemption orders placed around this date.

## Key Points

- Liquid and debt scheme subscription funds received on April 1 will be settled to AMCs on April 2, 2026 (next business day) with applicable NAV.
- Non-liquid/non-debt scheme subscriptions received within cut-off time will be settled to AMCs on April 1, 2026 as usual.
- Subscription units pay-in and pay-out for orders placed on March 30 and April 1, 2026, will both occur on April 2, 2026.
- Redemption units pay-in and pay-out for orders placed on April 1, 2026, will occur on the same day (April 1).
- There will be NO redemption funds pay-out on April 1, 2026.
- No liquid subscription orders or settlements will occur on April 1, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time operational adjustment to the standard settlement calendar due to the Annual Bank Closing holiday on April 1, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All MFSS participants must take note of the revised settlement schedule and plan order processing accordingly.
- Participants should check the scheme category in the scheme master to determine applicable redemption funds settlement timelines (T+1 through T+8).
- Liquid and debt scheme subscription orders placed on April 1 must be processed with awareness that funds will reach AMCs only on April 2, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 30, 2026**: Subscription settlement S 2026059 — funds pay-in/pay-out on March 30; units pay-in/pay-out on April 2; funds pay-in cut-off 14:30 hrs. Liquid settlement K 2026059 — funds and units on March 30; cut-off 13:00 hrs.
- **April 1, 2026**: Clearing holiday (Annual Bank Closing). Subscription settlement S 2026060 — funds pay-in/pay-out on April 1; units pay-in/pay-out on April 2; funds pay-in cut-off 14:30 hrs. Redemption settlement U 2026060 — units transfer on April 1; no funds pay-out on this date.
- **April 2, 2026**: Units pay-out for subscription orders placed March 30 and April 1; funds settlement for liquid/debt scheme subscriptions received on April 1.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is primarily operational for mutual fund participants and investors using the NSEInvest platform. Investors in liquid and debt schemes placing subscriptions on April 1 will face a one-day delay in fund settlement to AMCs, which may affect NAV applicability. Redemption investors will receive units on April 1 but must wait for funds as per their scheme's standard T+N payout schedule — no funds will be disbursed on April 1 itself. Participants need to proactively communicate these changes to clients to manage expectations around NAV and liquidity timelines.
