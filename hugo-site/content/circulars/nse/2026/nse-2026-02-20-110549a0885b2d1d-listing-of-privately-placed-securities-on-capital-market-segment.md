## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a privately placed debt security issued by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) on the capital market segment, effective February 23, 2026. The security is a 7.01% bond maturing in March 2029 (Series 26D), identified by the symbol 701NBRD29 under series N0.

## Key Points

- NABARD's 7.01% bond (NBRD 7.01% 2029 Sr 26D) is admitted to dealings on NSE's capital market segment
- Trading symbol: 701NBRD29, Series: N0, Market Lot: 1
- ISIN: INE261F08ES8
- Coupon rate: 7.01% per annum
- Maturity date: March 16, 2029
- Securities are identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes
- Applicable under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the new security and its designated code (701NBRD29) for trading purposes
- Trading in this security must be conducted only using the designated code
- Lot size of 1 unit applies for all trades in this security

## Important Dates

- Circular date: February 20, 2026
- Effective date for trading: February 23, 2026
- Bond maturity date: March 16, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The addition of a single NABARD bond to the NSE capital market segment provides institutional and retail investors an additional fixed-income instrument to trade. NABARD is a government-backed development finance institution, making this a high-credit-quality bond. The 7.01% coupon and 2029 maturity offer a short-to-medium term fixed income option. No disruption to existing trading operations is expected.
