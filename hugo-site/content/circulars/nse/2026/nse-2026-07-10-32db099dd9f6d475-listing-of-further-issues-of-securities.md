## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for 13 companies under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. These additional securities, arising primarily from ESOP allotments and one QIP issuance, will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 13, 2026. The circular specifies designated security codes and lot sizes for each listing as per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Companies covered: Angel One Limited, Avalon Technologies Limited, Blue Jet Healthcare Limited, Coforge Limited, CreditAccess Grameen Limited, EPL Limited, Lloyds Metals And Energy Limited, One 97 Communications Limited (Paytm), SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited, Baazar Style Retail Limited, Transport Corporation of India Limited, VA Tech Wabag Limited, and Wipro Limited.
- Angel One Limited: 169,561 equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted under ESOP on 30-Jun-2026 (Distinctive Nos. 913349400–913518960), market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Avalon Technologies Limited: 24,058 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on 25-Jun-2026 (Distinctive Nos. 66787698–66811755), market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Blue Jet Healthcare Limited: 15,810,276 equity shares of Rs. 2 each issued under QIP at an issue price of Rs. 506, allotted on 09-Jul-2026 (Distinctive Nos. 173465426–189275701), market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Coforge Limited: further equity shares of Rs. 2 each also listed; full allotment details were not available in the extracted portion of the circular.
- Details for CreditAccess Grameen, EPL, Lloyds Metals And Energy, One 97 Communications (Paytm), SEDEMAC Mechatronics, Baazar Style Retail, Transport Corporation of India, VA Tech Wabag, and Wipro follow the same Annexure format (ESOP/other allotments) but were not fully captured in the extracted text.

## Regulatory Changes

None — this is a routine administrative listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes), not a change to regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants must recognize the newly listed securities only by their designated security codes and trade them in the specified lot sizes effective July 13, 2026. No other member action is required.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: Angel One (30-Jun-2026), Avalon Technologies (25-Jun-2026), Blue Jet Healthcare (09-Jul-2026).
- Effective date of listing for all securities in the Annexure: July 13, 2026.
- Circular date: July 10, 2026 (Circular Ref. No. 1132/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/CML/75159).

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, procedural circular reflecting incremental share admissions from employee stock option exercises and one QIP-based capital raise (Blue Jet Healthcare). Such issuances marginally increase the free-float/outstanding share count of the respective companies but do not materially affect trading dynamics, valuations, or investor decision-making. No broader market or sectoral impact is expected.
