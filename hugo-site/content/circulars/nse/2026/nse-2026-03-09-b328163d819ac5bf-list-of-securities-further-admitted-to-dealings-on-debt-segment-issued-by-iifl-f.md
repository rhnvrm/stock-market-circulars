## Summary

NSE has admitted 9 new debt securities issued by IIFL Finance Limited to dealings on the Debt Segment, effective March 09, 2026. The securities comprise 6 fixed coupon (RDNGDB) and 3 zero coupon (RDNGDC) bonds with maturities ranging from 2028 to 2031, all issued on March 06, 2026, and rated by CRISIL.

## Key Points

- 9 new debt securities of IIFL Finance Limited admitted to NSE Debt Segment effective March 09, 2026
- Securities fall under two instrument types: RDNGDB (fixed coupon, periodic) and RDNGDC (zero coupon, on maturity)
- Maturity tenors: 2-year (2028), 3-year (2029), and 5-year (2031)
- All securities have a face value of Rs. 1,000 and are rated by CRISIL
- Fixed coupon rates range from 8.37% to 9.00% per annum
- Zero coupon bonds carry 0% stated rate with redemption at maturity
- All securities issued under Public issue type

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular references existing frameworks under NSE/CML/23366 (May 8, 2013) and NSE/DS/24832 (October 25, 2013) for admission of securities to the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are informed of the new securities available for trading on the Debt Segment
- No specific compliance action required from members beyond awareness of the new tradeable instruments

## Important Dates

- **Issue Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 09, 2026
- **Next Interest Payment Date (fixed coupon, periodic series):** April 01, 2026 (for ISIN INE530B07658, INE530B07591, INE530B07633) and March 06, 2026 (for ISIN INE530B07617, INE530B07575, INE530B07609)
- **Maturity Dates:** March 06, 2028 / March 06, 2029 / March 06, 2031
