## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has informed all members that due to a settlement holiday on June 26, 2026, three EGR (Electronic Gold Receipt) settlements have been compressed into June 29, 2026. Members must observe revised pay-in and pay-out timings and updated depository cut-off timings.

## Key Points

- Settlement holiday on June 26, 2026 triggers multiple settlements on June 29, 2026
- Three settlements (2026028, 2026029, 2026030) are scheduled on June 29, 2026 in sequence
- Members must note revised cut-off timings for pay-in at depositories
- Auction trading and settlement under revised T+1 cycle have been updated
- UNPAID MTF file upload timings for EGR Normal are revised for June 26 and June 29
- Shortages of settlement 2026030 will be clubbed with 2026031; no separate auction for 2026030

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time operational adjustment to the EGR settlement calendar to accommodate the June 26, 2026 settlement holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to revised pay-in timings for each settlement on June 29, 2026
- Members must observe depository cut-off timings for pay-in under revised settlement schedule
- Members must upload UNPAID MTF files for EGR Normal within the revised windows on June 26 and June 29

## Important Dates

- **June 26, 2026**: Settlement holiday (trigger for multiple settlements)
- **June 29, 2026 – Settlement 2026028 (Type 7)**: Trade date June 25; Pay-in 09:30 hrs, Pay-out 11:30 hrs
- **June 29, 2026 – Settlement 2026029 (Type 6)**: Trade date June 25; Pay-in 14:00 hrs, Pay-out 16:30 hrs
- **June 29, 2026 – Settlement 2026030 (Type 6)**: Trade date June 26; Pay-in 17:30 hrs, Pay-out 20:00 hrs
- **MTF file upload window for 2026029**: June 26 (07:30–23:00), June 29 (07:30–13:00)
- **MTF file upload window for 2026030**: June 29 (07:30–16:30)
- **Auction settlement 2026029**: Auction trade date June 29, settlement date June 30
- **Auction settlement 2026031**: Auction trade date June 30, settlement date July 1

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on EGR segment members. The bunching of three settlements in a single day requires careful time management for pay-in obligations, MTF file uploads, and depository cut-offs. Auction obligations for settlements 2026030 and 2026031 are merged, eliminating a standalone auction for 2026030, which simplifies short-coverage but requires members to be aware of the combined shortage handling. No impact on stocks or long-term regulatory posture.
