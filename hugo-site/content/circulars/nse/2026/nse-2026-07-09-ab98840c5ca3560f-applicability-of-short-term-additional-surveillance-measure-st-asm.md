## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, identifying Thaai Casting Limited (TCL) for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I based on surveillance criteria. No securities were shortlisted for Stage II or for migration between stages in this cycle. Separately, four securities — Agarwal Industrial Corporation Limited (AGARIND), Chandan Healthcare Limited (CHANDAN), IFCI Limited (IFCI), and IZMO Limited (IZMO) — have been excluded from the ASM framework effective July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- TCL (Thaai Casting Limited, ISIN: INE0QJL01014) is placed under ST-ASM Stage I.
- No securities were added to ST-ASM Stage II in this cycle.
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I.
- Four securities (AGARIND, CHANDAN, IFCI, IZMO) are being excluded from the ASM framework w.e.f. July 10, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates alongside other surveillance measures already in force.
- Inclusion under ASM is a surveillance-driven action and not to be construed as adverse to the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the continuing ST-ASM framework established via earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, 46557, 52144, 58558, 64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024). No changes to the framework itself; this is a periodic application of existing criteria to identify newly qualifying and exiting securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE trading members must apply the specified higher margin rate to TCL on all open positions as of July 10, 2026, and new positions created from July 13, 2026.
- Members must update surveillance/margin systems to reflect the ASM stage change for TCL and the removal of margin restrictions for the four excluded securities.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the revised margin and effective date for exclusions from ASM framework.
- July 13, 2026: Effective date for the increased margin rate (50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) on TCL for open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The ST-ASM Stage I inclusion imposes a significant margin increase on TCL, which typically raises trading costs, may reduce short-term liquidity, and can dampen speculative activity in the stock. Market participants trading TCL should account for the higher margin requirement starting July 13, 2026. The removal of ASM restrictions on AGARIND, CHANDAN, IFCI, and IZMO reduces trading friction for these securities, potentially improving liquidity and normalizing margin requirements. Overall market impact is limited given the small number of securities affected.
