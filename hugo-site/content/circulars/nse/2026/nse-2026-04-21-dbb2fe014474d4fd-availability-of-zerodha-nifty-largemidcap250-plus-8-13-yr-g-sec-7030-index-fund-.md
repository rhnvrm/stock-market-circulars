## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Zerodha Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund Direct Growth, offered by Zerodha Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 22, 2026. The scheme supports subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP).

## Key Points

- Zerodha Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund Direct Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01208) is reopened for ongoing transactions.
- The scheme is categorized as Equity and follows a 70:30 allocation between the Nifty LargeMidcap250 index and 8-13 year G-Sec.
- Effective date for reopening: April 22, 2026.
- Facilities available: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members. Members are informed for awareness and to update their systems/platforms to enable transactions in this scheme from April 22, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Zerodha Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund Direct Growth reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is relevant primarily to NSE MF Invest Platform members and investors interested in the Zerodha Mutual Fund scheme. The reopening enables retail and institutional investors to access this hybrid index fund combining large-midcap equity exposure with government securities, providing a diversified investment option. No systemic or regulatory implications are anticipated.
