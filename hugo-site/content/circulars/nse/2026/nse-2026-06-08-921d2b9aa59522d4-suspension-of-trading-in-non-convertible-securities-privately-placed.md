## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in a privately placed non-convertible security issued by Union Bank of India (ISIN: INE692A08144) effective June 9, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the company's exercise of a call option, leading to early redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Union Bank of India's non-convertible security (ISIN: INE692A08144) will be suspended from trading on NSE's Debt Market segment effective June 9, 2026.
- Reason for suspension is redemption through the exercise of a call option by the issuer.
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74573 | Circular Ref. No: 0912/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an operational notification under the existing framework of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs suspension of debt securities prior to redemption or other corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in debt securities must note the suspension and cease trading in ISIN INE692A08144 from June 9, 2026 onwards.
- Holders of this security should coordinate with their custodians/depositories for the redemption process.

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026**: Circular issuance date.
- **June 9, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension for ISIN INE692A08144 (Union Bank of India).

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders of the specific non-convertible security (INE692A08144) issued by Union Bank of India. Trading in this ISIN will cease from June 9, 2026. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the call option terms. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
