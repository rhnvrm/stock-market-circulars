## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of privately issued corporate bonds that are eligible for DVP-3 (Delivery versus Payment - 3) settlement on the Institutional Platform of the Debt Segment. The annexure details instruments from a wide range of public sector undertakings, financial institutions, and banks, specifying their symbols, maturity dates, issue dates, ISIN codes, credit ratings, coupon types, and other key parameters.

## Key Points

- NSE has updated the list of privately placed corporate bonds eligible for DVP-3 settlement on its Institutional Debt Platform.
- The revised list covers bonds from major issuers including IRFC, Axis Bank, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, HPCL, IOC/IOCL, Canara Bank, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance, Export Import Bank of India, FCI, Aditya Birla Capital, Aditya Birla Housing Finance, Aseem Infrastructure Finance, Bharti Telecom, DME Development, and GFL, among others.
- Each instrument is identified by its symbol, expiry/maturity date, issue date, ISIN code, credit rating, coupon type, coupon frequency, face value, issued capital, and tick size.
- Both IINGDB/IINGPT/IINGBB (NSE-IING series) and IONGDB/IONGPT/IONGBB (NSE-IONG series) variants are listed, reflecting institutional-grade bond segments.
- Instruments include fixed and variable coupon bonds with maturities ranging from 2027 to 2036.

## Regulatory Changes

- This circular supersedes or revises the previous list of corporate bonds eligible under DVP-3 settlement on the Institutional Platform.
- DVP-3 settlement means securities and funds are settled on a net basis on the settlement date, which reduces counterparty risk for institutional participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians operating on the NSE Institutional Debt Platform must refer to this revised list to confirm eligibility of bonds for DVP-3 settlement.
- Transactions in bonds not appearing on this list should not be processed under the DVP-3 settlement mechanism.
- Members should update their internal systems and settlement workflows to reflect the revised eligible bond list.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: 2026-06-12
- Effective date: As communicated by NSE; members should treat this list as operative from the date of circular issuance.
- Instrument maturities in the list range from June 2027 (IRFC27) to April 2036 (ABCL36, GFL36).

## Impact Assessment

- **Institutional Investors and Custodians:** Directly affected; must verify bond eligibility before executing DVP-3 settlements on the NSE Institutional Debt Platform.
- **Corporate Bond Market Liquidity:** Inclusion in the DVP-3 eligible list enhances liquidity and settlement certainty for listed bonds, potentially supporting tighter bid-ask spreads.
- **Issuers:** Bonds of major PSUs (IRFC, HPCL, IOC, EXIM Bank, FCI) and banks (Axis, HDFC, ICICI, Canara) are included, underscoring the platform's focus on high-credit-quality institutional paper.
- **Retail Market:** No direct impact; this is an institutional debt market operational update.
- **Credit Quality:** Bonds listed span AAA-rated and high-grade issuers, consistent with the institutional platform's risk framework.
