## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership by Phoenix Share & Stockbrokers Pvt. Ltd. The Exchange issued this circular (Ref. No. NSE/COMP/74719, Circular No. 55/2026) on June 15, 2026, informing all members of the approved surrender.

## Key Points

- Phoenix Share & Stockbrokers Pvt. Ltd. applied for and received approval to surrender their NSE membership.
- The member held SEBI registration numbers INB231082935 (cash segment) and INF231082935 (F&O segment).
- The member's last trade date on NSE was February 15, 2006, indicating over 20 years of inactivity prior to formal surrender.
- The Exchange formally approved the surrender on June 4, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative order reflecting the exit of a single trading member from NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All existing NSE members are requested to take note of the surrender. No direct compliance action is required from other members as a result of this circular.

## Important Dates

- **Last Trade Date:** February 15, 2006
- **Date of Approval of Surrender:** June 4, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Phoenix Share & Stockbrokers Pvt. Ltd. had not conducted any trades since February 2006, making this a formalization of a long-inactive membership. No disruption to market operations, client funds, or trading activity is anticipated.
