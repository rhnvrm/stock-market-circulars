## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has issued Circular No. 459/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74800) dated June 19, 2026, notifying the applicability of the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) framework. Sical Logistics Limited (Symbol: SICALLOG, ISIN: INE075B01020) is being shortlisted under ASM IBC Stage I effective June 22, 2026. No securities are being moved between stages or excluded from the framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Sical Logistics Limited (SICALLOG) is newly added to ASM IBC Stage I with effect from June 22, 2026.
- No securities qualify under the 'Receipt of Corporate Announcement/Disclosure' shortlisting category in this circular.
- No securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage I.
- No securities are being excluded from the ASM IBC framework (Annexure II lists Nil).
- The ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM IBC is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing ASM IBC framework established via prior circulars:
- NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018
- NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022
- NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023
- NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of securities under the existing ASM IBC structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE Members must note the revised list of securities under ASM IBC and apply the applicable price band restrictions from June 22, 2026.
- For securities moving out of the ASM IBC framework, the price band shall revert to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless the scrip is also under another surveillance measure (in which case, that framework's price band prevails).
- Members with clients holding SICALLOG positions should communicate the surveillance status and associated trading restrictions as applicable.
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Date of circular issuance.
- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date for ASM IBC Stage I applicability for SICALLOG.

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is limited in scope, affecting only Sical Logistics Limited (SICALLOG). Entry into ASM IBC Stage I typically results in reduced price bands and may shift the security to a trade-for-trade settlement mechanism, limiting intraday speculative activity. Market participants holding or trading SICALLOG should be aware of potentially tighter price movement limits from June 22, 2026. The broader market is unaffected as no other securities are added, removed, or transitioned between stages in this update.
