## Summary

NSE is introducing Futures & Options contracts on two individual securities — GE Vernova T&D India Limited (GVT&D) and Radico Khaitan Limited (RADICO) — effective May 27, 2026. This circular (continuation of NSE/FAOP/74151 dated May 11, 2026) specifies lot sizes, tick sizes, and the strike scheme for both securities.

## Key Points

- GE Vernova T&D India Limited (GVT&D): Lot size of 125
- Radico Khaitan Limited (RADICO): Lot size of 150
- Options contracts tick size: Re 0.05
- Futures contracts tick size: Same as the underlying security in the CM Segment
- Strike scheme and step values are detailed in Annexure A for both near, mid, and far expiries
- Updated contract files (contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) must be loaded before trading on May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

Two new securities are being added to the F&O segment. The strike scheme (Annexure A) defines step values and reserve strikes across narrow and wide ranges for near, mid, and far expiry contracts:

| Symbol | Narrow Step (Near/Mid/Far) | Wide Step (Near/Mid/Far) |
|--------|--------------------------|-------------------------|
| GVT&D  | 100 / 100 / 200          | 200 / 200 / 400         |
| RADICO | 50 / 50 / 100            | 100 / 100 / 200         |

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz files in their trading applications before trading on May 27, 2026.
- Contract files are available from the faoftp/faocommon directory on the Extranet server.
- Contract files are also accessible on the NSE website: https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Applicable quantity freeze details are contained within the contract file.

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Original circular NSE/FAOP/74151 issued
- **May 26, 2026**: This circular issued
- **May 27, 2026**: F&O contracts on GVT&D and RADICO available for trading

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard derivative segment expansion impacting traders and brokers dealing in GE Vernova T&D India Limited and Radico Khaitan Limited. Members must ensure their trading systems are updated with the new contract files before market open on May 27, 2026. The addition of F&O contracts on these securities increases liquidity options and allows hedging for holders of the underlying shares.
