## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of three privately placed debt securities on its Capital Market segment, effective July 03, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The circular specifies the security codes, lot sizes, coupon rates, and maturity dates for each newly listed instrument.

## Key Points

- Three fresh debt issues have been admitted to dealings on NSE's Capital Market segment.
- Lodha Developers Limited: Symbol LDL30, Series N0, Market Lot 1, Issue "LDL RESET 2030", Coupon Rate Reset, Maturity 15-Feb-2030, ISIN INE670K07364.
- IIFL Samasta Finance Limited: Symbol 920ISF28, Series N0, Market Lot 1, Issue "ISF 9.20% 2028", Coupon Rate 9.2%, Maturity 30-Dec-2028, ISIN INE413U07475.
- Profectus Capital Private Limited: Symbol 975PCPL27A, Series N0, Market Lot 1, Issue "PCPL 9.75% 2027", Coupon Rate 9.75%, Maturity 30-Sept-2027, ISIN INE389Z07120.
- Trading in these securities on NSE's system will be identified solely by their designated security codes, with dealings restricted to the specified lot sizes.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of privately placed securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new security codes and adhere to the specified market lot size of 1 unit each when transacting in these instruments from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 02, 2026
- Effective Date: July 03, 2026 (listing and trading commence)
- Maturity Dates: 30-Sept-2027 (Profectus Capital), 30-Dec-2028 (IIFL Samasta Finance), 15-Feb-2030 (Lodha Developers)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing notification affecting the debt market segment. It enables trading members to deal in these newly issued privately placed debt securities but has no direct implications for equity markets or broader investor sentiment.
