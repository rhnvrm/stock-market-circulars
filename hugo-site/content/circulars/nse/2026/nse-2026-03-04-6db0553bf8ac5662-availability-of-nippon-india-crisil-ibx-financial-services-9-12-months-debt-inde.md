## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund (by Nippon India Mutual Fund) will be available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 05, 2026
- Facilities available: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Six scheme variants are available across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout/Reinvestment) options
- All six variants are categorised as Debt funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification regarding the reopening of a mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of the above schemes for processing client transactions (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) from March 05, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of the fund for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice is relevant only to NSE members and investors using the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to transact in the Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund. The fund targets short-duration debt instruments in the financial services segment, tracked against the CRISIL-IBX index. No impact on equity markets or trading operations.
