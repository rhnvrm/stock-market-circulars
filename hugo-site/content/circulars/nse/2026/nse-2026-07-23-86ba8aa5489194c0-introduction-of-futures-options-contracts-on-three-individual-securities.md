## Summary

NSE has notified that futures and options (F&O) contracts on three individual securities - Ather Energy Limited (ATHERENERG), Bank of Maharashtra (MAHABANK) and Sagility Limited (SAGILITY) - will be available for trading with effect from August 26, 2026. The introduction follows the stock selection criteria prescribed by SEBI under master circular SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024, and is subject to fulfilment of the eligibility criteria under the Quarter sigma computation cycle of August 2026.

## Key Points

- F&O contracts to be introduced on three securities: Ather Energy Limited (ATHERENERG), Bank of Maharashtra (MAHABANK) and Sagility Limited (SAGILITY)
- Introduction is based on SEBI's stock selection criteria under master circular SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024
- Contracts are subject to fulfilment of eligibility criteria of the Quarter sigma computation cycle of August 2026
- Market lot and scheme of strikes will be communicated via a separate circular on August 25, 2026
- Applicable quantity freeze details will be available in the contract file for trading on August 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements SEBI's existing stock selection criteria under master circular SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024, following SEBI's approval for these securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz files in their trading applications before trading on the effective date
- These files can be obtained from the faoftp/faocommon directory on the Extranet server, or the MII contract file from NSE's website (all-reports-derivatives section)
- Members should monitor for the separate circular on August 25, 2026 detailing market lot and strike scheme

## Important Dates

- December 30, 2024: SEBI master circular prescribing stock selection criteria
- August 25, 2026: Market lot and scheme of strikes to be notified via separate circular
- August 26, 2026: F&O contracts on ATHERENERG, MAHABANK and SAGILITY become available for trading, subject to eligibility criteria

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of F&O contracts provides market participants with new derivatives-based tools for hedging and speculative exposure to Ather Energy, Bank of Maharashtra and Sagility. This typically increases trading volumes and liquidity in the underlying cash market and may attract institutional and retail derivatives traders. The impact is contract-specific and does not alter the fundamentals of the underlying companies; members need to ensure operational readiness (updated contract files) ahead of the August 26, 2026 launch.
