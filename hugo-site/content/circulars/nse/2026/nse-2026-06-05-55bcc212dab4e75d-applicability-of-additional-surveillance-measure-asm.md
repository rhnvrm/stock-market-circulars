## Summary

NSE has shortlisted four securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) Stage-I framework effective June 08, 2026, pursuant to the ASM framework circulars. A 100% margin requirement will apply on all open positions as of June 09, 2026 and new positions from June 10, 2026. Three of the four securities are being moved from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework to the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) framework.

## Key Points

- Four securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I w.e.f. June 08, 2026
- 100% margin applicable w.e.f. June 10, 2026 on all open and new positions
- Three securities (CEMPRO, PARACABLES, WEBELSOLAR) moved from STASM to LTASM
- No securities shortlisted under Stage-IV (Trade-for-Trade shift) in this circular
- No stage upgrades (Stage-I to II or Stage-II to III) in this circular
- ASM shortlisting is purely a market surveillance action and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I are subject to enhanced surveillance under the LTASM framework as outlined in circular NSE/SURV/39265 (October 27, 2018) and subsequent amendments through NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024). Securities qualifying under criteria VII (Stage-IV) would be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE); however, no securities qualify for Stage-IV in this instance.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must collect 100% margin on all open positions in the listed securities as on June 09, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 10, 2026 onwards
- **Market Participants**: Must note that ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- **Price Band**: Upon exit from the ASM framework, the price band will revert to the pre-shortlisting level, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | CEMPRO | Cemindia Projects Limited | INE686A01026 | Moved from STASM |

| 3 | UTLSOLAR | Fujiyama Power Systems Limited | INE12UR01024 | Fresh inclusion |
| 4 | WEBELSOLAR | Websol Energy System Limited | INE855C01023 | Moved from STASM |

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital required to trade or hold these securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes. Investors holding these securities will face higher margin calls. The absence of Stage-IV inclusions means no security is being shifted to the Trade-for-Trade segment in this circular, which limits the most restrictive impact. For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.
