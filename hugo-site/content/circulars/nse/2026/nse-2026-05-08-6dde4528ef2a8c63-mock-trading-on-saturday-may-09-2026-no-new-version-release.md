## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock trading session in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Market (SLBM) on Saturday, May 09, 2026. The session covers two trading sessions — one from the Primary Site and one from the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site — followed by live re-login timings. No new NEAT version will be released for this mock session (current version: NEAT 1.3.3).

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be held on May 09, 2026 in the SLBM segment
- No new software version release; NEAT version remains 1.3.3
- Two trading sessions: Session-1 from Primary Site and Session-2 from DR Site
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- Trades during mock sessions carry no financial obligation (no fund pay-in or pay-out)
- UCC validation will be skipped during contingency time for order entry
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during the mock session
- Members must re-login to the live environment after the mock to ensure smooth connectivity for Monday, May 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock trading circular referencing existing guidelines under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 and NSE circular NSE/MSD/46441 for software testing compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- All SLBM-enabled members are automatically eligible and expected to participate
- Members using Non-NEAT Frontend (NNF) must manually clear outstanding orders in their systems before each session
- NNF users must ensure dealer-type User IDs converted for NNF and mapped with the correct IP
- Only valid UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be permitted
- Members must re-login to the live trading environment after the mock session to confirm connectivity for the next trading day
- Connectivity parameters per circular NSE/MSD/73993 dated April 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** May 09, 2026 (Saturday)
- **Session-1 (Primary Site):** Normal Market Open 10:15 | Contingency Start 12:00 | Contingency Close 13:30 | Normal Market Close 14:00
- **Session-2 (DR Site):** Normal Market Open 15:00 | Normal Market Close 15:30
- **Live Re-login Window:** 17:30 – 18:00
- **Extranet/Connect2NSE Unavailable:** 13:00 – 22:00
- **Extranet API Unavailable:** 13:15–14:15 and 18:00–22:00
- **Next Live Trading Day:** May 11, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is minimal and confined to SLBM segment members. This is a standard operational drill to test system resilience and member connectivity, including switchover to the DR site. No live trades, financial settlements, or market prices are affected. Members who do not participate may face connectivity issues on the following Monday. General investors and listed companies are unaffected.
