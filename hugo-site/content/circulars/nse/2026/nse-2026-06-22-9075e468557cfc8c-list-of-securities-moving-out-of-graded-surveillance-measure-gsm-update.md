## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued an update to circular NSE/SURV/74795 (dated June 19, 2026), specifying the applicable trading series and price bands for 8 securities that will exit the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective June 23, 2026. Three of the eight securities are currently suspended.

## Key Points

- Eight securities are being moved out of GSM with effect from June 23, 2026.
- This circular supplements NSE/SURV/74795 (June 19, 2026) by specifying the exact series and price bands applicable post-exit.
- Three securities — OMKARCHEM, SAHAJ, and SANCO — are currently suspended and will remain so after exiting GSM.
- Price bands assigned are either 5% or 10% depending on the security.

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities will exit the GSM framework on June 23, 2026 with the specified series and price bands:

| Symbol | Security Name | ISIN | Series | Price Band |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| GTL | GTL LIMITED | INE043A01012 | EQ | 10% |
| LCCINFOTEC | LCC INFOTECH LIMITED | INE938A01021 | EQ | 5% |
| OMKARCHEM* | OMKAR SPECIALITY CHEMICALS LIMITED | INE474L01016 | BZ | 5% |
| SADBHAV | SADBHAV ENGINEERING LIMITED | INE226H01026 | EQ | 10% |
| SADBHIN | SADBHAV INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECT LIMITED | INE764L01010 | BE | 5% |
| SAHAJ* | SAHAJ FASHIONS LIMITED | INE013901017 | SZ | 5% |
| SANCO* | SANCO INDUSTRIES LIMITED | INE782L01012 | BZ | 5% |
| SETUINFRA | SETUBANDHAN INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED | INE023M01027 | BZ | 5% |

*Suspended

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must note the revised series and price band applicable for each security from June 23, 2026.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Original circular NSE/SURV/74795 issued announcing GSM exit for these securities.
- **June 22, 2026**: This update circular (NSE/SURV/74806) issued with series and price band details.
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date — securities move out of GSM framework with new series and price bands.

## Impact Assessment

For the five non-suspended securities (GTL, LCCINFOTEC, SADBHAV, SADBHIN, SETUINFRA), removal from GSM relaxes the heightened surveillance constraints they were subject to. Trading resumes under standard EQ/BE/BZ series with price bands of 5% or 10%, which may improve liquidity and reduce trading friction. The three suspended securities (OMKARCHEM, SAHAJ, SANCO) are unaffected in practice as trading remains halted.
