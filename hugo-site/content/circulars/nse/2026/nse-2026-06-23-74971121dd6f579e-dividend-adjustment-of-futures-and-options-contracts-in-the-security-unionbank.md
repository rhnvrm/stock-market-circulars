## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts for Union Bank of India (symbol: UNIONBANK) pursuant to a dividend declaration. The dividend of Rs 5.00/- per share (face value Rs 10/-) has an ex-date of July 3, 2026. Revised option strike prices will be communicated one day prior to the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Symbol: UNIONBANK (Union Bank of India)
- Corporate action: Dividend of Rs 5.00/- per share on face value of Rs 10/-
- Ex-date / Effective date: July 3, 2026
- Revised option strike prices will be provided one day before the ex-date
- Revised strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places, rounded to the nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively
- Updated contract files (contract.gz / MII contract and spread files) will be available one day prior to ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are being made in pursuance of SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments to futures and options contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading application before trading on the ex-date.
- Files are available from the directory `faoftp/faocommon` on the Extranet server and also on the NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately intimated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- Ex-date / Effective date: July 3, 2026
- Revised strike prices and contract file availability: July 2, 2026 (one day prior to ex-date)

## Impact Assessment

Traders holding Futures and Options positions in UNIONBANK will see adjusted strike prices and contract parameters effective from July 3, 2026. The adjustment is a standard SEBI-mandated process to account for the dividend payout. Members must ensure their trading systems are updated with the revised contract files before the ex-date to avoid discrepancies in order placement and position management.
