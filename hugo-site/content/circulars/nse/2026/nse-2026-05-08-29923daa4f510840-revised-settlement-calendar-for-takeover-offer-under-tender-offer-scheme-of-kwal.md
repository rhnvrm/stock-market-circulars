## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a revised settlement calendar for the Takeover offer under the Tender Offer scheme of Kwality Wall's (India) Limited. This supersedes the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73781 dated April 16, 2026, with updated obligation download and settlement dates for Settlement No. 2026038.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: X, Settlement No. 2026038
- Trade window runs from April 23, 2026 to May 07, 2026
- Obligation Download date revised to May 13, 2026
- Settlement Date revised to May 14, 2026
- All other terms from circular NCL/CMPT/73781 dated April 16, 2026 remain unchanged
- Clearing Corporation may further revise schedule in case of delay in RTA acceptance details or funds pay-in by buying trading member

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the settlement schedule originally communicated via NCL/CMPT/73781 (April 16, 2026) and NSE/CMTR/73768 (April 16, 2026). The revision extends the obligation download and settlement dates by approximately one week compared to the original schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must note and adhere to the revised settlement calendar
- Buying trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid further schedule revisions
- Members should contact the NSE Clearing help desk (ncl_tenderoffer@nsccl.co.in or Toll Free: 1800 266 00 50, Option 2) for clarifications

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | April 23, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | May 07, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | May 13, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | May 14, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The revised settlement calendar directly impacts members, custodians, and PCMs involved in the Kwality Wall's (India) Limited takeover tender offer. The settlement date has been pushed to May 14, 2026, giving participants additional time for obligation processing. The impact is limited to parties engaged in this specific tender offer transaction. Further revisions remain possible if RTA acceptance details are delayed or if the buying trading member's funds pay-in is not received on time.
