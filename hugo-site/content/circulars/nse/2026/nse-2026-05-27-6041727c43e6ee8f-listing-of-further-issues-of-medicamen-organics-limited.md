## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 465,910 additional equity shares of Medicamen Organics Limited (Symbol: MEDIORG) on the SME EMERGE segment of NSE, effective May 29, 2026. The shares were allotted on January 24, 2026, pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 64.39 per share.

## Key Points

- Symbol: MEDIORG, Series: SM (SME EMERGE platform)
- ISIN: INE0PE401018 (currently credited under temporary ISIN in dematerialized mode)
- 465,910 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 allotted at issue price Rs. 64.39
- Distinctive number range: 11,700,001 to 12,165,910
- Market lot size: 2,000 shares
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period applies until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine further listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (MEDIORG) for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 2,000 shares
- The temporary ISIN will be activated to the permanent ISIN (INE0PE401018) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: January 24, 2026
- Effective listing date on NSE: May 29, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a small-cap SME EMERGE listing involving 465,910 shares from preferential warrant conversion. The lock-in until December 31, 2027 restricts near-term liquidity of these shares. The addition increases the total paid-up capital of Medicamen Organics Limited marginally. Relevant only to existing investors and traders active in the SME EMERGE segment.
