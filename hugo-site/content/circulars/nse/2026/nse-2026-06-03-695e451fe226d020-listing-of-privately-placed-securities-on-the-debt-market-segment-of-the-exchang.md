## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of privately placed Commercial Papers (CPs) from 10 companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange effective June 03, 2026, under Circular Ref. No. 0892/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/CML/74525). All instruments were allotted on June 02, 2026, with most maturing on September 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 Commercial Papers admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment effective June 03, 2026
- All instruments allotted on June 02, 2026
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit across all securities
- Most CPs mature on September 01, 2026; one HDFC Securities CP matures July 30, 2026
- Issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations
- Signed by Manisha Vyas, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine admission notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Regulation 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. Members are notified of the new security codes assigned to the listed CPs as specified in the Annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** June 02, 2026 (all securities)
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Redemption Dates:**
  - July 30, 2026 — HDFC Securities Limited (HSL CP 30/07/2026, ISIN: INE700G14UB3)
  - September 01, 2026 — all remaining nine CPs
