## Summary

NSE has informed all members that SBI Mutual Fund's CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index - Dec 2029 Index Fund NFO will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 14, 2026 to May 19, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The fund is a debt category index fund tracking the CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index with a target maturity in December 2029. Six scheme variants are being launched covering direct and regular plans with growth and IDCW options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: May 14, 2026 to May 19, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Fund house: SBI Mutual Fund
- Benchmark: CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index - Dec 2029
- Category: Debt (target maturity index fund)
- Six scheme variants available across direct/regular plans and growth/IDCW payout/IDCW reinvestment options
- Allotment date for all variants: May 20, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by May 20, 2026 at 3:00 PM
- NSE Circular Ref. No: 1368/2026 | Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74193

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO launch notification from NSE's NMF TM Segment to inform members of a new mutual fund product available on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 20, 2026 at 3:00 PM.
- Members should facilitate investor transactions within the NFO window (May 14–19, 2026).

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| May 14, 2026 | NFO subscription opens |
| May 19, 2026 (11:59 PM) | NFO subscription closes |
| May 20, 2026 | Allotment date for all six scheme variants |
| May 20, 2026 (3:00 PM) | Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine product launch notification relevant to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions. Investors seeking fixed-income exposure with a December 2029 maturity horizon in government securities (Gilts) and State Development Loans (SDLs) in a 10:90 ratio may find this NFO relevant. The six scheme variants (ISINs: INF200KB1BK6, INF200KB1BG4, INF200KB1BI0, INF200KB1BF6, INF200KB1BJ8, INF200KB1BH2) provide flexibility across investment plan types. No impact on equity markets or existing listed securities.
