## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74490 (Ref. 405/2026) announcing the applicability of Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for multiple securities. Nine securities have been shortlisted for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I, two securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II, and Stage II has no new direct inclusions. Enhanced margin requirements take effect from June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9 securities newly included under ST-ASM Stage I effective June 02, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage II new inclusions: Nil
- 2 securities moving from ST-ASM Stage I to Stage II effective June 02, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max capped at 100%)
- ST-ASM Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max capped at 100%)
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and not an adverse finding against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ST-ASM framework established via prior circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; the circular applies existing ST-ASM criteria to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply the enhanced margin rates to all open positions in listed securities as on June 02, 2026
- New positions created from June 03, 2026 in affected securities must also carry the applicable ST-ASM margin rates
- Members should refer to FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for detailed provisions
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 02, 2026**: Securities shortlisted; ST-ASM Stage I & Stage I-to-II movement effective date
- **June 03, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements effective for all open positions as on June 02, 2026 and new positions

The mandatory 50% margin for Stage I and 100% margin for Stage II will significantly increase the cost of carrying positions in these securities, likely reducing liquidity and potentially suppressing trading volumes. Wockhardt (WOCKPHARMA) is the highest-profile name affected as a large-cap pharmaceutical company. Traders with existing positions in any of these securities must ensure sufficient capital to meet the enhanced margin obligations by June 03, 2026.
