## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of HMA Agro Industries Limited. The OFS spans two order dates — April 9, 2026 (non-retail) and April 10, 2026 (retail and non-retail) — with settlements on April 10 and April 13, 2026 respectively. Settlement follows existing secondary market transaction rules as per circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- OFS settlement is governed by existing secondary market transaction rules
- Two settlement numbers: 2026066 (order date April 9) and 2026067 (order date April 10)
- Non-retail (IS) category requires 100% margin with custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM; 0% margin variant has confirmation by 7:30 AM next day
- Retail (RS) category under settlement 2026067 requires 100% margin with no custodial confirmation (NA)
- Funds pay-in deadline: T+1 day by 11:00 AM
- Securities and funds pay-out: T+1 day by 1:30 PM
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes existing OFS settlement rules (per NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025) for this specific OFS transaction.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must adhere to the specified settlement calendar and timings
- Custodians must confirm non-retail (IS) transactions by the specified confirmation times (3:30 PM or 7:30 AM as applicable)
- Funds pay-in must be completed by T+1 day 11:00 AM
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement days
- For queries, contact the NSE Clearing helpdesk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

| Settlement Date for 2026067 | April 13, 2026 |

| Securities & Funds Pay-out | T+1 by 1:30 PM |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a targeted operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs (Professional Clearing Members) participating in the HMA Agro Industries Limited OFS. Retail investors are subject to 100% upfront margin with no custodial confirmation required. Non-retail/institutional participants have two margin options (100% with same-day custodial confirmation, or 0% with next-day morning confirmation). The short settlement window (T+1) requires prompt action from all participating entities. Broader market impact is limited to participants in this specific OFS.
