## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a revised settlement calendar for the takeover offer under the tender offer scheme of Stylam Industries Limited. This circular supersedes the earlier settlement schedule communicated via circular NCL/CMPT/73802 dated April 20, 2026, and updates the obligation download and settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Revised settlement calendar issued for Stylam Industries Limited takeover tender offer
- Settlement Type: X, Settlement No.: 2026039
- Trade period runs from April 22, 2026 to May 06, 2026
- Obligation download date revised to May 12, 2026
- Settlement date set for May 13, 2026
- Clearing Corporation may further revise the schedule in case of delays in RTA acceptance details or funds pay-in by buying trading members

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the settlement schedule originally announced in circular NCL/CMPT/73802 dated April 20, 2026, and referenced in NSE/CMTR/73761 dated April 16, 2026. All other terms and conditions from the April 20 circular continue to apply unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs (Professional Clearing Members) must note and adhere to the revised settlement calendar
- Buying trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid further schedule revision
- Participants should monitor for any further notifications from the Clearing Corporation regarding schedule changes

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | April 22, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | May 06, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | May 12, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | May 13, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This revision directly impacts members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Stylam Industries Limited takeover tender offer. The updated settlement date of May 13, 2026 gives participants a clear deadline for funds and securities settlement. The conditional clause allowing further revision introduces a degree of uncertainty, requiring participants to stay alert for any subsequent communications from NSE Clearing Limited.
