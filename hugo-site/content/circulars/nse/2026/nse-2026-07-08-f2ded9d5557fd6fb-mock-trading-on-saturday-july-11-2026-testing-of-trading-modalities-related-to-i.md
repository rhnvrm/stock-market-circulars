## Summary

NSE has announced a mock trading session on Saturday, July 11, 2026, to test trading modalities related to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, as referenced in an earlier circular (NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026). The mock will involve a new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) specifically for CAS-related testing during Trading Session-1, with members required to revert to the earlier NEATPlus version (7.8.8) for Trading Sessions 2 and 3.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 11, 2026, across three trading sessions.
- New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 will be introduced solely for Trading Session-1, related to CAS testing.
- After Trading Session-1, members must reinstall and log in with the earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for Trading Sessions 2 and 3.
- Trading Sessions 2 and 3 will follow existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes.
- Trading Session-1 includes new CAS-specific timings: CAS open time 12:30, CAS market order restriction start 12:45, CAS close time 12:53 (with random closure in the last two minutes), followed by Normal Market close at 12:45 and a Closing Session from 13:00 to 13:15.
- Trades from Trading Session-1 will not carry over to Trading Sessions 2 and 3, but trades from Session 2 will be available in Session 3.
- Trading Session-3 will be conducted from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site.
- Live re-login timings for the mock are from 17:30 to 18:00.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulatory changes but operationalizes testing for the previously announced introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment (per circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must participate in the mock trading session as scheduled.
- Members must install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 for Trading Session-1 and then reinstall version 7.8.8 before Trading Sessions 2 and 3.
- Members should ensure systems are configured according to the detailed session timings provided in the circular.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Date: July 11, 2026 (Saturday)
- Trading Session-1: 07:45 to 13:15 (includes CAS testing window from 12:30 to 12:53)
- Trading Session-2 (Primary Site): 13:00 to 15:00
- Trading Session-3 (DR Site): 15:30 to 16:45
- Live Re-login window: 17:30 to 18:00

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a market-operations impact as it prepares NSE members' trading systems for the upcoming Closing Auction Session (CAS) feature in the Equity Cash segment. While the mock session itself is a controlled, non-live test with no direct financial or stock-specific impact, it signals an important infrastructural change to trading close mechanics that member firms must adapt to, including software version management and adherence to revised session timings.
