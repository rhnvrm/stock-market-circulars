## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of various privately placed securities on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange, effective July 03, 2026, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The securities admitted include fresh issues and re-issues of commercial papers, non-convertible debentures, and securitized debt instruments from multiple issuers.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue Commercial Papers admitted include: Godrej Agrovet Limited (GAL CP 31/08/26 Sr 6, ISIN INE850D14VO4), ICICI Securities Limited (ISEC CP 29/03/27, ISIN INE763G14L60), IIFL Finance Limited (IIFL CP 15/09/26, ISIN INE530B14HY4), L&T Finance Limited (LTFL CP 10/06/27, ISIN INE498L14GA5), and Axis Securities Limited (ASL CP 15/03/27 Sr 26, ISIN INE110O14IX8)
- Fresh issue Other Debt Securities include: Ambium Finserve Limited (AFL 11.16% 2028, ISIN INE0RU307262), L&T Finance Limited (LTFL 8.20% 2036 Sr C, ISIN INE498L08069), and India Residential Mortgage Trust 2025 02 Limited (IRMT 8.10% 2045 Sr A, ISIN INE2XAN15011, a securitized debt instrument)
- Re-issue Commercial Paper includes HDFC Securities Limited (HSL CP 30/07/2026, ISIN INE700G14UB3)
- Designated security codes for each instrument are specified in the annexure to the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations governing admission of privately placed securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from listed companies or investors beyond the standard settlement and trading procedures applicable to debt market instruments. Members are notified for informational purposes regarding the security codes assigned.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 03, 2026
- Date of allotment for various securities ranges from July 01, 2026 to July 03, 2026
- Redemption dates range from August 31, 2026 (Godrej Agrovet CP) through February 15, 2045 (India Residential Mortgage Trust securitized debt)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to administrative listing of privately placed debt instruments, which are typically traded among institutional investors. It does not affect equity markets, retail investors, or broader trading operations. The listing enables secondary market trading of these debt instruments on NSE's Debt Market Segment for eligible participants.
