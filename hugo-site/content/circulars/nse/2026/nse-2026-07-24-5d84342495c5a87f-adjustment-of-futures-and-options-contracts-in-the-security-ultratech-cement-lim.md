## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a circular detailing the mechanics of adjusting Futures and Options (F&O) contracts in UltraTech Cement Limited (ULTRACEMCO) to account for a corporate dividend action. This follows the earlier NSE Circular No. 104/2026 (Download No. NSE/FAOP/75343) dated July 22, 2026, which announced the underlying corporate action adjustment. The circular specifies how futures positions will be marked-to-market and carried forward, and how options strike prices will be adjusted to reflect the dividend amount of Rs. 240 per share.

## Key Points

- Futures contracts in ULTRACEMCO will be marked-to-market on July 29, 2026 (last cum-dividend date) based on the daily settlement price.
- After mark-to-market processing, open futures positions will be carried forward at the daily settlement price reduced by Rs. 240 (the dividend amount).
- From July 30, 2026 (ex-dividend date), normal daily mark-to-market settlement procedures resume.
- Begin-of-day margins on July 30, 2026 will be computed using the adjusted carry-forward value; intra-day margins will use relevant traded prices per standard intra-day span risk parameter files.
- For options contracts, the full dividend value of Rs. 240 will be deducted from all cum-dividend strike prices on the ex-dividend date, adjusted to the nearest tick size to determine new strike prices.
- Existing positions at old strike prices will continue to exist under the corresponding new adjusted strike prices.
- An illustrative example shows a futures contract valued at Rs. 11,760 adjusted down to Rs. 11,520 (Rs. 11,760 – Rs. 240) post-adjustment.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulations but operationalizes a previously announced corporate action adjustment (per NSE Circular No. 104/2026 dated July 22, 2026) within NSE Clearing Limited's settlement and risk management framework for F&O contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members and trading members with open positions in ULTRACEMCO futures and options contracts should:
- Ensure margin and position management systems account for the adjusted contract values from July 30, 2026 onward.
- Recognize that options positions at old cum-dividend strike prices will map automatically to new ex-dividend adjusted strike prices — no member action is required for this remapping.
- Monitor begin-of-day and intra-day margin requirements computed on the adjusted carry-forward values.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Original NSE Circular No. 104/2026 announcing the corporate action.
- July 24, 2026: Date of this NSE Clearing circular (Download Ref No. NCL/CMPT/75377).
- July 29, 2026: Last cum-dividend date; futures contracts marked-to-market at this date's settlement price.
- July 30, 2026: Ex-dividend date; adjusted carry-forward values and new option strike prices take effect; normal settlement procedures resume.

## Impact Assessment

The adjustment is a routine but necessary operational step to neutralize the impact of the Rs. 240 dividend on derivative contract valuations for ULTRACEMCO, preventing distortion of futures and options pricing around the ex-dividend date. Market participants holding ULTRACEMCO F&O positions should account for the adjusted values in their risk and margin calculations, though the overall market impact is limited to this single security's derivatives segment.
