## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in the equity shares of JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited (Symbol: JBCHEPHARM, Series: EQ) will be suspended with effect from July 17, 2026 (i.e., from the closing hours of trading on July 16, 2026). The suspension is being carried out under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, pursuant to a Scheme of Amalgamation.

## Key Points

- Security: JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited
- Symbol: JBCHEPHARM, Series: EQ
- Reason for suspension: Scheme of Amalgamation
- Suspension effective from July 17, 2026 (closing hours of trading on July 16, 2026 is the last trading session)

## Regulatory Changes

Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE trading members must cease trading in JBCHEPHARM equity shares from the effective suspension date and update their systems/client communications accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Last day of trading: July 16, 2026 (closing hours)
- Suspension effective: July 17, 2026
- Circular date: July 13, 2026 (Ref No: 1137/2026)

## Impact Assessment

Investors holding JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals shares will be unable to trade the stock on NSE from July 17, 2026 onward due to the ongoing Scheme of Amalgamation. This is a significant corporate action event requiring shareholders to track further updates on the amalgamation process and any subsequent relisting or share exchange arrangements.
