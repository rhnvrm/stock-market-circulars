## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced a change in the market maker for DURLAX TOP SURFACE LIMITED. The market maker responsibility is being transferred from Globalworth Securities Limited (Member Code: 13994) to Nikunj Stock Brokers Ltd. (Member Code: 06913), effective January 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company affected: DURLAX TOP SURFACE LIMITED (Symbol: DURLAX)
- Old Market Maker: Globalworth Securities Limited (Member Code: 13994)
- New Market Maker: Nikunj Stock Brokers Ltd. (Member Code: 06913)
- This is a change for an SME segment company
- Members have been notified to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an operational change in market maker assignment.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are required to note this change in market maker assignment for the specified security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 05, 2026
- Effective Date: January 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The change in market maker does not affect trading operations, investor rights, or the liquidity obligations for the security. Market making services will continue uninterrupted under the new market maker.
