## Summary

NSE Circular No. 0683/2026 (Ref: NSE/CML/73885) notifies all exchange members that Sastasundar Ventures Limited will undergo a corporate name change and corresponding trading symbol change effective April 30, 2026. The company will be renamed Health X Platform Limited and its NSE symbol will change from SASTASUNDR to HEALTHX.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol **SASTASUNDR** will be replaced by **HEALTHX** from April 30, 2026.
- Existing company name **Sastasundar Ventures Limited** will change to **Health X Platform Limited**.
- All exchange members are required to take note and update their systems accordingly.
- The circular is issued by the Listing Department of NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulations are introduced. This circular communicates a corporate action — a name and symbol change — that members must reflect in their trading and back-office systems.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update internal records, trading systems, and client-facing platforms to reflect the new symbol (HEALTHX) and company name (Health X Platform Limited) by April 30, 2026.
- Client communications referencing the old symbol or name should be updated accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 24, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 30, 2026 — new name (Health X Platform Limited) and new symbol (HEALTHX) become active.

## Impact Assessment

Traders, investors, and brokers holding or tracking positions in SASTASUNDR must update their watchlists, order templates, and records to use the new symbol HEALTHX after April 29, 2026. The underlying security and shareholding remain unchanged; only the identifier and corporate name are updated. This is a routine corporate rebranding event with moderate operational impact on systems that reference the old symbol or name.
