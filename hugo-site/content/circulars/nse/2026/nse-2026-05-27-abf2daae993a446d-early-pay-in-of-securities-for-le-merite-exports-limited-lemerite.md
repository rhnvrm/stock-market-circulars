## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued guidance for members, custodians, and PCMs regarding the early pay-in (EPI) of securities for Le Merite Exports Limited (Symbol: LEMERITE, ISIN: INE0G1L01017) in connection with a face value split from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share. The record date and ex-date for this corporate action are both May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Le Merite Exports Limited (LEMERITE) is undergoing a face value sub-division from Rs 10/- to Rs 2/- per share.
- Record date and ex-date are both May 29, 2026.
- Members doing EPI on May 29, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026099) must use the **old/existing ISIN** (INE0G1L01017) without adjusting for the conversion ratio.
- Members doing EPI on June 1, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026099 & 2026100) must use the **new ISIN**.
- For a sale of 10 shares, EPI in the depository should be made in the old ISIN for a quantity of 2 shares (reflecting the pre-split ratio).
- Custodians providing EPI through a pool account must upload client-wise early pay-in allocation details based on actual traded quantity (e.g., 10 shares in the example).
- In case of EPI through block mechanism, the client-wise EPI allocation file should **not** be uploaded.
- After the record date, all pay-in/early pay-in instructions must be executed in the new ISIN.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references Item 10.18 ('Early Pay-in of Securities for Margin Exemption') of the consolidated circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and also directs members to adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022, concerning SEBI's block mechanism in demat accounts for clients undertaking sale transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/custodians must submit EPI in the **old ISIN** for trades settling on May 29, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026099), using pre-split quantities.
- For settlements on June 1, 2026, EPI must be submitted in the **new ISIN**.
- Custodians using pool accounts for EPI must upload client-wise allocation files reflecting actual traded (post-split) quantities.
- EPI through block mechanism must not be accompanied by a client-wise allocation file upload.
- All participants must transition to the new ISIN for pay-in instructions after the record date.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a focused operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs holding or trading LEMERITE around the face value split date. Failure to follow the prescribed ISIN-switching procedure could result in settlement failures or incorrect margin exemptions. The impact is contained to participants with positions in LEMERITE and is time-bound around the May 29, 2026 record date. General market impact is low.
