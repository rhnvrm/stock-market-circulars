## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the monthly settlement schedule for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for June 2026. The circular (Ref. No. 52/2026) details the T+1 settlement cycle applicable to all trade dates across the month, with the final trade date of 30-Jun-2026 settling on 01-Jul-2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement follows a T+1 cycle for all trade dates in June 2026.
- 21 trade dates are listed, spanning 01-Jun-2026 through 30-Jun-2026.
- The last settlement of the month (for trades on 30-Jun-2026) falls on 01-Jul-2026.
- No trading is scheduled on 06-Jun-2026, 07-Jun-2026, 13-Jun-2026, 14-Jun-2026, 20-Jun-2026, 21-Jun-2026, 26-Jun-2026, 27-Jun-2026, and 28-Jun-2026 (weekends/holidays).
- Issued by Anil Suvarna, Associate Vice President, NSE Clearing Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational notification of the standard T+1 settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- All F&O segment members must adhere to the published settlement dates for trade date obligations.
- Members should plan funds and securities availability in line with the settlement schedule to avoid settlement failures.
- For queries, members may contact NSE Clearing at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or fao_clearing_ops@nsccl.co.in.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium operational impact for F&O segment members as it governs settlement timing for the entire month of June 2026. Members need to ensure liquidity and securities availability on each settlement date. The gap between 25-Jun-2026 (trade) and 29-Jun-2026 (settlement) reflects a weekend skip. The end-of-month spillover (30-Jun settlement on 01-Jul) has minor cross-month accounting implications for members and clearing participants.
