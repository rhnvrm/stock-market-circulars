## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited on its Capital Market segment, effective July 1, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Security: Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited NCD, issue description "CIFCL 8.88% 2033"
- Symbol: 888CIFCL33, Series: N0
- Market Lot: 1
- Coupon Rate: 8.88%
- Date of Maturity: 25-Jun-2033
- ISIN Code: INE121A08PZ6
- The security will be identified for trading purposes only by its designated security code

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of privately placed securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the designated security code (888CIFCL33, series N0) and adhere to the specified market lot of 1 when trading this security.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 1, 2026
- Maturity Date: June 25, 2033
- Circular Date: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact. This circular facilitates exchange-based trading of a single privately placed NCD series for institutional/debt investors holding Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited paper. No impact on equity markets or broader trading activity.
