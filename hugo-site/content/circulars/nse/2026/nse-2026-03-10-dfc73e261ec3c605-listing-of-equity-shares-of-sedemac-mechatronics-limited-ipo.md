## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited through an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol SEDEMAC with ISIN INE00XB01019. The specific listing date has not yet been confirmed and will be communicated via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited is set to list its equity shares on NSE via IPO
- Trading symbol assigned: **SEDEMAC**
- ISIN: **INE00XB01019**
- The exact listing date will be announced through a separate circular
- Circular issued by the Listing Department (Ref No: NSE/CML/73214, Circular Ref. No: 0412/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an informational notice to NSE members regarding an upcoming IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited
- Members should watch for the subsequent circular that will confirm the official listing date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited on NSE will provide investors with a new equity instrument to trade. As a mechatronics company, it may attract interest from investors in the engineering and industrial technology sector. The medium-term market impact is contingent on the final listing date, IPO pricing, and overall market conditions at the time of listing. Members should prepare for the onboarding of this new security once the listing date is confirmed.
