## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of 1,68,825 equity shares of AvenuesAI Limited (symbol: CCAVENUE, ISIN: INE483S01020) on the Capital Market segment, effective July 23, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: AvenuesAI Limited
- Symbol: CCAVENUE
- Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE483S01020
- Face Value: Re. 1/-
- Paid-up Value: Re. 1/-
- Security Description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Number of securities: 1,68,825
- Market lot: 1
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Lock-in details: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (further admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. No new regulatory framework is introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed shares under the designated security codes and trade them at the specified market lot of 1 share. Shares allotted in dematerialized mode have been credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8483S01029) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012), pending activation of the permanent ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a low market-wide impact, being specific to AvenuesAI Limited. It formalizes the conversion of 1,68,825 partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status, marginally increasing the fully paid-up share count available for trading. No broader market or sectoral implications are expected.
