## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding the applicability of Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for select securities. Nine securities have been shortlisted for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I, and one security (SAIFL) has been moved from Stage I to Stage II, all effective May 29, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9 securities added to ST-ASM Stage I w.e.f. May 29, 2026
- 1 security (SAIFL - Sameera Agro And Infra Limited) moved from ST-ASM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. May 29, 2026
- Stage II list reports Nil new additions
- ST-ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to Exchange Circular nos. NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558 and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023 and September 20, 2024, respectively, concerning the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework.

## Compliance Requirements

**ST-ASM Stage I:**
- Applicable margin rate: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher
- Maximum margin capped at 100%
- Applies to all open positions as on May 29, 2026 and new positions from June 1, 2026

**ST-ASM Stage II (SAIFL):**
- Applicable margin rate: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher
- Maximum margin capped at 100%
- Applies to all open positions as on May 29, 2026 and new positions from June 1, 2026

All NSE members must ensure compliance with the enhanced margin requirements for the listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion/movement in ST-ASM framework; open positions subject to new margins
- **June 1, 2026**: New margin rates applicable to all new positions created
