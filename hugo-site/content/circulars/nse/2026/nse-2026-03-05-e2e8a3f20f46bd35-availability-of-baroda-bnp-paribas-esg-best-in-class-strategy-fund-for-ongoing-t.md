## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best in Class Strategy Fund (of Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective March 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund house: Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund
- Fund: Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best in Class Strategy Fund
- Effective date for reopening: March 06, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities available: Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants are being made available across Regular and Direct plans

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to update their systems and clients accordingly to reflect the availability of the following six scheme variants from March 06, 2026:

| 3 | Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best in Class Strategy Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment | INF251K01VO6 | Equity |

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 06, 2026**: Fund reopens for ongoing transactions (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact. It restores transactional access for investors in the Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best in Class Strategy Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in ESG-focused equity funds will now be able to invest in or redeem from this fund via NSE's mutual fund platform. The six ISIN variants cover both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options, providing flexibility to investors. No systemic or broad market implications are expected.
