## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has approved the request of Smart Equity Brokers Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000177636) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives & Interest Rate Futures (IRF) segment, effective April 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Smart Equity Brokers Private Limited has voluntarily surrendered its Currency Derivatives & IRF segment membership.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000177636.
- Surrender approval date: April 06, 2026.
- All NSE members are notified to take note of this change.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update reflecting a member's voluntary exit from a specific trading segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are required to note that Smart Equity Brokers Private Limited is no longer an active member in the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment as of April 06, 2026.
- No action required from other members beyond acknowledgement.

## Important Dates

- **April 06, 2026**: Surrender approval date for Smart Equity Brokers Private Limited's Currency Derivatives & IRF segment membership.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine membership administrative action limited to a single trading member exiting one segment. It does not affect other members, trading operations, or broader market participants in the Currency Derivatives or IRF segments.
