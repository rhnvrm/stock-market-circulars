## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of 73,04,331 equity shares of Silgo Retail Limited (Symbol: SILGO) to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from May 21, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and carry no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 73,04,331 equity shares of Silgo Retail Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from May 21, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Trading symbol: SILGO | Series: EQ | ISIN: INE01II01013
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- Distinctive number range: 24604530 to 31908860

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governing admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governing lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (SILGO, Series EQ) for all trading in these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1
- Note: Shares were temporarily credited under ISIN IN801II01014 (temporary ISIN) in demat mode per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012); the permanent ISIN INE01II01013 is now activated

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market-wide impact. The addition of 73,04,331 shares to the tradeable float of Silgo Retail Limited increases the company's listed equity. Existing shareholders holding partly paid-up shares under the temporary ISIN (IN801II01014) will see those migrated to the permanent ISIN (INE01II01013), enabling normal trading from May 21, 2026. No impact on broader market operations or other listed entities.
