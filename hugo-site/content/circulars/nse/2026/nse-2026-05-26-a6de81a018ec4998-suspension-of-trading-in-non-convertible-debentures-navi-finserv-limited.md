## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Navi Finserv Limited, effective May 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of both instruments, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two NCDs of Navi Finserv Limited will be suspended from trading effective May 29, 2026.
- Symbol **1040NFL26** (ISIN: INE342T07437) is suspended due to maturity.
- Symbol **1090NFL26** (ISIN: INE342T07452) is suspended due to maturity.
- Circular Ref. No: NSE/CML/74409, dated May 26, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. The suspension is executed under the existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities upon maturity or other qualifying events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of NSE are notified to cease trading in the above-mentioned NCD symbols from May 29, 2026.
- No specific action is required from members beyond acknowledging the suspension and updating their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is a routine operational event triggered by the natural maturity of two NCD instruments issued by Navi Finserv Limited. Holders of these debentures should expect settlement or redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. There is no adverse regulatory or compliance implication for the broader market. Impact is limited to investors holding these specific debentures.
