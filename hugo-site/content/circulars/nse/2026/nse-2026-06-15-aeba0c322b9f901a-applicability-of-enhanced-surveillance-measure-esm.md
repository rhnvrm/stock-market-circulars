## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 442/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/SURV/74707) announcing the inclusion of three securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Stage I framework effective June 17, 2026. These securities will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement and will be moved from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST).

## Key Points

- Three securities newly shortlisted for ESM Stage I: AMANTA, BIKEWO, and FLEXITUFF
- Minimum 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 16, 2026, and new positions from June 17, 2026
- Securities shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST series) w.e.f. June 17, 2026
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage I
- No securities excluded from the ESM framework in this update
- Stage II securities are subject to Trade for Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 16, 2026
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

The ESM framework update references prior circulars: NSE/SURV/56948 (June 02, 2023), NSE/SURV/57609 (July 18, 2023), NSE/SURV/63361 (August 09, 2024), NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024), NSE/SURV/64400 (October 04, 2024), and NSE/SURV/69315 (July 25, 2025). The current circular applies those established ESM criteria to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers/Members**: Must collect a minimum 100% margin on open positions in AMANTA, BIKEWO, and FLEXITUFF as on June 16, 2026, and for all new positions from June 17, 2026
- **Trading systems**: Must route orders in these securities to the BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) series from June 17, 2026 onward
- **Participants holding Stage II securities**: Must comply with the 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction mechanism effective June 16, 2026
- ESM classification is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Impact Assessment

**Newly added ESM Stage I securities:**

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | AMANTA | Amanta Healthcare Limited | INE084K01015 |
| 2 | BIKEWO | Bikewo Green Tech Limited | INE0SQH01013 |
| 3 | FLEXITUFF | Flexituff Ventures International Limited | INE060J01017 |

**Market Impact:** Inclusion under ESM significantly restricts trading in these securities. The shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates the benefit of netting in settlement, meaning each trade must result in compulsory delivery. The 100% margin requirement increases the cost of holding or taking positions, likely reducing liquidity and increasing volatility in the short term. Retail investors and traders should note that intraday positions in these securities will require full upfront margins and cannot be squared off without actual delivery obligations.
