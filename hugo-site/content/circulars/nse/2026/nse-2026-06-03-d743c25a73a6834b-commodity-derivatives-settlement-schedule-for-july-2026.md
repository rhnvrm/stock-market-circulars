## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for the month of July 2026. Each trade date is mapped to its T+1 settlement date, with the final trade date of July 31 settling on August 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement follows a T+1 cycle for all trade dates in July 2026.
- The schedule covers 23 trade dates from July 1 to July 31, 2026.
- The last trade date (July 31, 2026) settles on August 3, 2026, skipping weekends.
- Issued under Circular Ref. No. 198/2026 by NSE Clearing Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational calendar notification.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members participating in the Commodity Derivatives Segment must align their settlement obligations with the published schedule for July 2026.
