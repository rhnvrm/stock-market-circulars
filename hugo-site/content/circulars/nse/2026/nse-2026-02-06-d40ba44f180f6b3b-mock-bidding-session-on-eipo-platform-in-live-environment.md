## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock bidding session on the eIPO platform in live environment on February 07, 2026. The mock session will run from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM and will feature a mock government security (MCKGS). Members are encouraged to participate actively, and no financial obligations will arise from bids placed during this session.

## Key Points

- Mock bidding session scheduled for February 07, 2026
- Session will be conducted on live eIPO platform at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- Active participation from members is encouraged
- No obligations for funds and securities will arise from mock session bids
- Mock security: Mock Govt Security (MCKGS) in G-sec series
- Market lot: 10,000 units at Rs. 100 per unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a testing and training exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock bidding session
- Members should monitor www.nseindia.com for any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session
- For queries, members can contact NSE Toll Free number: 1800 26600 50 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **February 07, 2026**: Mock bidding session from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a mock session with no impact on actual trading operations or financial markets. The session is designed for testing the eIPO platform functionality and providing members with hands-on experience. No real financial obligations, fund transfers, or securities transactions will occur.
