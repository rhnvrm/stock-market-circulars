## Summary

NSE has notified all members that Excel Realty N Infra Limited will undergo a change in its corporate name and trading symbol effective April 02, 2026. The company will be renamed Landsmill Green Limited and its NSE trading symbol will change from EXCEL to LANDSMILL.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Excel Realty N Infra Limited
- New company name: Landsmill Green Limited
- Existing NSE symbol: EXCEL
- New NSE symbol: LANDSMILL
- Change effective date: April 02, 2026
- Circular issued by Listing Department under Ref No NSE/CML/73456

## Regulatory Changes

The name and symbol change is a corporate action processed by NSE's Listing Department. All market references, trading terminals, and exchange systems will reflect the updated symbol LANDSMILL and the new name Landsmill Green Limited from April 02, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their trading systems and references to reflect the new symbol LANDSMILL from April 02, 2026.
- Investors holding positions under symbol EXCEL should be aware that the same holding will be accessible under LANDSMILL post the effective date.
- No specific action is required by existing shareholders regarding their holdings; the change is administrative.

## Important Dates

- **March 25, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **April 02, 2026**: Effective date for name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate name and symbol change with medium operational impact. Traders and investors tracking Excel Realty N Infra Limited under the symbol EXCEL will need to update their watchlists, alerts, and order references to the new symbol LANDSMILL from April 02, 2026. The change does not affect the underlying shares, ownership, or trading continuity. Automated trading systems and screeners referencing the old symbol may need reconfiguration prior to the effective date.
