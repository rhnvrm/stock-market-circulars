## Summary

NSE issued circular ref. 0896/2026 (Download Ref. No. NSE/CML/74532) notifying the listing of further issued securities for 15 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 04, 2026. The securities listed are primarily equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) by the respective companies.

## Key Points

- Securities of 15 companies admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective June 04, 2026
- Majority of new securities are ESOP allotments
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares and carry no lock-in
- Market lot is 1 for all listed securities
- Trading shall be identified by designated security codes as per the Annexure
- Lot sizes specified in the Annexure apply for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern the admission of further securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members beyond recognizing the new security codes and lot sizes as per the Annexure for trading purposes effective June 04, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 04, 2026
- Allotment dates per company:
  - Aadhar Housing Finance Limited: May 26, 2026 (736,334 shares, ISIN INE883F01010)
  - Dr. Agarwal's Health Care Limited: May 21, 2026 (25,240 shares, ISIN INE943P01029)
  - Coforge Limited: May 17, 2026 (352,818 shares, ISIN INE591G01025)
  - ICICI Bank Limited and remaining companies: dates in Annexure

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP-related further issuances that modestly increase the outstanding share count for each company. All new shares are pari passu with existing equity and carry no lock-in restrictions. The additional float from ESOP shares is small relative to total market capitalisation for large-cap names like ICICI Bank, Coforge, Marico, and Polycab. No price band adjustments or special trading conditions are indicated.
