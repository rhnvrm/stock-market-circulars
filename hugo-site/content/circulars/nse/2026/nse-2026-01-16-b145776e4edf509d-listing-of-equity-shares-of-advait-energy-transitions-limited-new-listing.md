## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing of equity shares of Advait Energy Transitions Limited on the Capital Market segment with effect from January 20, 2026. The company will trade under the symbol 'ADVAIT' with ISIN INE0ALI01010. A total of 10,936,777 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each will be listed and traded in compulsory demat mode in the Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement). The company is registered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat and operates in the energy transitions sector.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: ADVAIT
- ISIN: INE0ALI01010
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Total Securities: 10,936,777 equity shares
- Distinctive Number Range: 1 to 10,936,777
- Market Lot Size: 1 share
- Trading Mode: Compulsory demat for all investors
- Trading Segment: Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Registrar: Accurate Securities & Registry Private Limited
- Company Financial Year: April 1 to March 31

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification in accordance with Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in ADVAIT shares must be in demat form only
- Market participants must use the designated security code (ADVAIT) for trading on the system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- Lock-in restrictions apply to certain shares as per annexure

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**:
  - 10,825,707 shares: Free (no lock-in)
  - 104,031 shares: Locked until March 31, 2026
  - 5,631 shares: Locked until April 30, 2026
  - 1,408 shares: Locked until June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a new listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds a new company in the energy transitions sector to the NSE platform. Approximately 99% of shares (10,825,707 out of 10,936,777) are free from lock-in restrictions and available for trading immediately. The remaining shares (approximately 111,070 shares or 1%) have lock-in periods ranging from 2 to 5 months. The compulsory demat requirement is standard for all new listings and ensures transparent settlement.
