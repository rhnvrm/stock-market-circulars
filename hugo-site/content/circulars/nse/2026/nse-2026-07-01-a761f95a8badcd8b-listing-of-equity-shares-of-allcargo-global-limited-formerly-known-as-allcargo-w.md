## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Allcargo Global Limited (formerly Allcargo Worldwide Limited, originally incorporated as Allcargo ECU Limited) on the Capital Market segment, effective July 03, 2026. The listing follows a Scheme of Arrangement approved by the NCLT Mumbai Bench, under which the international logistics services undertaking of Allcargo Logistics Limited was demerged and vested into Allcargo Global Limited, previously an unlisted wholly-owned subsidiary of Allcargo Logistics Limited.

## Key Points

- Security: Allcargo Global Limited, symbol **AGL**, ISIN INE1YPB01014
- Shares will initially trade in Series 'BE' (Trade for Trade) before shifting to Series 'EQ' per SEBI circular dated September 2, 2010
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2/- each
- Total securities admitted: 982,782,096 equity shares (distinctive number range 1 to 982,782,096)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Shares carry pari passu rights; no lock-in applicable
- Shares will be included in the pre-open trading session per SEBI circulars dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The listing arises from an NCLT-approved Scheme of Arrangement (order dated October 10, 2025, NCLT Mumbai Bench, Court V) among Allcargo Logistics Limited (Demerged Company), Allcargo Supply Chain Private Limited, Gati Express & Supply Chain Private Limited, Allcargo Gati Limited (Transferor Companies), and Allcargo Global Limited (Resulting Company). The scheme demerges the international logistics services undertaking of Allcargo Logistics Limited into Allcargo Global Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must recognize AGL under its designated security code and trade only in the specified lot size of 1
- Initial trading restricted to Trade-for-Trade (Series 'BE') segment before migration to normal 'EQ' series
- Shares are subject to pre-open session trading norms as per applicable SEBI circulars

## Important Dates

- NCLT approval of Scheme of Arrangement: October 10, 2025
- Circular issue date: July 01, 2026
- Listing/effective date: July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing formalizes the demerger of Allcargo Logistics Limited's international logistics business into a separately listed entity, Allcargo Global Limited. Shareholders of Allcargo Logistics Limited who received AGL shares under the scheme will now be able to trade them, though initially only in the Trade-for-Trade segment, which may limit liquidity and intraday trading until the shift to the 'EQ' series. The large share count (~982.8 million) indicates a broad shareholder base impacted by this corporate restructuring.
