## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers (CPs) from nine companies to the Debt Market Segment with effect from June 18, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. All securities were allotted on June 17, 2026, and mature between September 2026 and February 2027.

## Key Points

- Nine fresh commercial paper issuances admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment effective June 18, 2026
- All CPs carry a face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit
- Issuers include major financial institutions and corporates: Godrej Properties, Angel One, Reliance Retail Ventures, ICICI Securities, NABARD, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Godrej Industries, IIFL Finance, and Motilal Oswal Finvest
- Maturity dates range from September 7, 2026 to February 11, 2027
- Aggregate quantity across listed issues exceeds 1,06,000 units

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for members. Members dealing in debt market securities should note the assigned security codes from the Annexure for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 17, 2026 (all issues)
- **Effective Listing Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Earliest Redemption:** September 7, 2026 (GPL CP 07/09/26 Sr 39, ISIN INE484J14D73)
- **Latest Redemption:** February 11, 2027 (IIFL CP 11/02/27, ISIN INE530B14HW8)
