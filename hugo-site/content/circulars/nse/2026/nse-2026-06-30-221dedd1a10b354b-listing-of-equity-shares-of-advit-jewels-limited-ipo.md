## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/74930, Circular Ref. No: 1042/2026) informing all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Advit Jewels Limited on the Exchange. The listing is pursuant to the company's IPO. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company name: Advit Jewels Limited
- Trading symbol: RAMBHAJO
- ISIN: INE1SJO01012
- Listing date: To be announced via a separate circular
- Circular issued by the Listing department, signed by Aarti Parmar (Manager)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard pre-listing notification to exchange members.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of Advit Jewels Limited and await the subsequent circular confirming the exact listing date before executing any related transactions.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 30, 2026
- Listing date: To be notified through a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine pre-listing notification with low immediate market impact. The circular serves as advance notice to exchange members about Advit Jewels Limited's impending debut on NSE under the symbol RAMBHAJO. Actual trading activity and price discovery will commence only upon the formal listing date being announced and the shares being admitted to trading.
