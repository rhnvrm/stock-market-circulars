## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73554, Circular Ref. No: 0553/2026) informing members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited on the Exchange. The exact date of listing has not been specified and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited
- Exchange Symbol: AMIRCHAND
- ISIN: INE05TO01019
- Security Type: Equity Shares (IPO)
- Listing date to be announced via a subsequent circular
- Circular issued by the Listing Department, NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under NSE's Listing Department.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of AMIRCHAND and await a further circular from NSE confirming the exact date of listing before facilitating trades.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 01, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It pertains to a single company IPO listing (Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited) on NSE. Market participants and brokers dealing in SME/new listings should monitor NSE for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date.
