## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the QSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund NFO by Quant SIF Mutual Fund will be open for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from April 02, 2026 to April 16, 2026 (until 11:59 PM). The fund is categorized under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework. Allotment for all six scheme variants is scheduled for April 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: April 02, 2026 to April 16, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Fund house: Quant SIF Mutual Fund
- Fund type: Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF)
- Six scheme variants offered across Direct and Regular plans
- All variants classified under the Debt category
- Allotment date: April 21, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by April 21, 2026, 03:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard NFO availability notice under the existing SIF framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before April 21, 2026, 03:00 PM.
- Members should facilitate client transactions within the NFO window (April 02–16, 2026).

## Important Dates

- **April 02, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **April 16, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **April 21, 2026 (03:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **April 21, 2026**: Allotment date for all six scheme variants
