## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities of five SME EMERGE companies — Grill Splendour Services Limited, Cellecor Gadgets Limited, Polysil Irrigation Systems Limited, RNFI Services Limited, and Renol Polychem Limited — effective from July 15, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The circular also specifies applicable market lot sizes under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Grill Splendour Services Limited (Symbol: BIRDYS, ISIN: INE0PC901019): 380,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under Preferential allotment at issue price Rs. 85.61; allotment date 03-Jun-2026; market lot 1200; lock-in until 18-Jan-2027.
- Cellecor Gadgets Limited (Symbol: CELLECOR, ISIN: INE0OMO01025): 1,649,138 equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted under FCCB conversion at issue price Rs. 29; allotment date 14-May-2026; market lot 3000; no lock-in.
- Polysil Irrigation Systems Limited (Symbol: POLYSIL, ISIN: INE517M01028): 4,000,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under Preferential allotment at issue price Rs. 17.55; allotment date 21-May-2026; market lot 500; lock-in until 31-Jan-2027.
- RNFI Services Limited (Symbol: RNFI, ISIN: INE0SA001017): equity shares of Rs. 10 each at issue price Rs. 270; further details truncated in source document.
- Renol Polychem Limited: mentioned in the subject line; detailed annexure data not available in the extracted content.
- All securities are admitted to dealings on the SME EMERGE segment of NSE Capital Market, identified only by their designated security codes for trading purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, confirming admission of further issued securities to trading and specifying corresponding lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure trading in the specified securities is conducted only using the designated security codes and in the specified lot sizes effective July 15, 2026. No other member action is required.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (Grill Splendour Services Limited): 03-Jun-2026
- Date of Allotment (Cellecor Gadgets Limited): 14-May-2026
- Date of Allotment (Polysil Irrigation Systems Limited): 21-May-2026
- Lock-in expiry (Grill Splendour Services Limited): 18-Jan-2027
- Lock-in expiry (Polysil Irrigation Systems Limited): 31-Jan-2027
- Circular effective date: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification affecting trading mechanics (lot size, security codes) for the specified SME EMERGE stocks. It has limited broad market impact but is directly relevant to existing and prospective shareholders of BIRDYS, CELLECOR, POLYSIL, RNFI, and Renol Polychem Limited, as it formalizes the tradability of newly issued shares from preferential allotments and FCCB conversions.
