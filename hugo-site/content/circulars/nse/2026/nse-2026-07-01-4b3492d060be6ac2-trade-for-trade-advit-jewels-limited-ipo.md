## Summary

NSE has issued a circular announcing that the equity shares of Advit Jewels Limited (Symbol: RAMBHAJO), which were trading in the trade for trade segment (Series: BE) following its IPO, will be transferred to the normal rolling settlement segment (Series: EQ) with effect from July 15, 2026. This follows an earlier Exchange circular (NSE/CML/74954 dated June 30, 2026) and is issued pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular reference CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Security: Advit Jewels Limited, Symbol: RAMBHAJO
- Moving from trade for trade segment (Series: BE) to rolling segment (Series: EQ)
- Effective date: July 15, 2026
- Issued under SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Follow-up to Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74954 dated June 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements existing SEBI guidelines governing the transition of newly listed IPO securities from trade-for-trade settlement to normal rolling settlement after an initial monitoring period.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants should update their systems to reflect RAMBHAJO's series change from BE to EQ effective July 15, 2026, and ensure trades are settled under normal rolling settlement rules from that date.

## Important Dates

- July 01, 2026: Circular issued
- July 15, 2026: Effective date of transfer from Series BE to Series EQ

## Impact Assessment

The transfer to the rolling (EQ) segment typically increases liquidity and trading flexibility for RAMBHAJO shares by removing trade-for-trade settlement restrictions, which is a routine and positive development for shareholders and traders of this specific stock, with no broader market impact.
