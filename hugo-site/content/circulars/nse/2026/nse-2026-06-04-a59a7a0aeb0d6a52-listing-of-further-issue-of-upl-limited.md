## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 57 additional equity shares of UPL Limited (Symbol: UPL, Series: EQ) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- 57 equity shares of UPL Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from June 05, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- ISIN: INE628A01036 (Face Value: Rs. 2/-, Paid-up Value: Rs. 2/-)
- Market lot size: 1; Pari Passu: Yes; Lock-in: Not Applicable
- Temporary ISIN IN8628A01019 was used for dematerialized credit per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must identify the security by its designated code (UPL) and trade in the specified lot size of 1. No additional compliance action required from members.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 04, 2026
- Effective Date (listing on NSE): June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Only 57 shares are being listed across three distinctive number ranges (890707351–890707400: 50 shares; 890734697–890734701: 5 shares; 890789252–890789253: 2 shares). This is a routine administrative listing arising from the partly paid-up to fully paid-up conversion process and does not materially affect UPL Limited's total share capital or market dynamics.
