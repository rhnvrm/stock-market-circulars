## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for eleven companies - Aarti Surfactants Limited, Asian Energy Services Limited, Eris Lifesciences Limited, The Federal Bank Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, Kernex Microsystems (India) Limited, Manorama Industries Limited, Metropolis Healthcare Limited, PC Jeweller Limited, Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited and Vishal Mega Mart Limited. These securities, mostly allotted under respective ESOP schemes, will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective July 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Further issues of securities for 11 listed companies admitted to trading effective July 07, 2026
- Aarti Surfactants Limited (AARTISURF): 5,910 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP, allotment date 18-Jun-2026
- Asian Energy Services Limited (ASIANENE): 18,641 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP, allotment date 19-May-2026
- Eris Lifesciences Limited (ERIS): 11,233 equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted under ESOP, allotment date 26-Jun-2026
- The Federal Bank Limited (FEDERALBNK): further equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted (details continued in annexure)
- All securities carry pari passu rights with existing shares and market lot of 1
- No lock-in applicable for the disclosed allotments (Aarti Surfactants, Asian Energy Services, Eris Lifesciences)

## Regulatory Changes

None - this is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of further securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify these securities only by their designated security codes and trade in the specified lot sizes (1 share per lot for the disclosed entries) as per the Annexure from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 06, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: July 07, 2026
- Allotment Dates: 18-Jun-2026 (Aarti Surfactants), 19-May-2026 (Asian Energy Services), 26-Jun-2026 (Eris Lifesciences)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing update reflecting ESOP-related share allotments across multiple companies. The additional shares represent a small incremental increase in the free float/paid-up capital of each company and are unlikely to have a material market impact given the modest quantities involved relative to overall market capitalization.
