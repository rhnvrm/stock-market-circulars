## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Zerodha Mutual Fund has revised the minimum amount for Daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) in two of its schemes — Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF and Zerodha Gold ETF FOF — effective May 21, 2026, on the NSE MF Invest platform. The minimum daily SIP installment has been reduced from ₹100 to ₹10.

## Key Points

- Minimum daily SIP amount for Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF revised from ₹100 to ₹10.
- Minimum daily SIP amount for Zerodha Gold ETF FOF revised from ₹100 to ₹10.
- Minimum number of SIP installments remains unchanged at 1.
- Change applies specifically to the Daily frequency under the SIP facility.
- NSE circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/74596, Circular Ref. No. 1417/2026.
- Addendum reference: Addendum No. 03/2026-27 issued by Zerodha Asset Management Private Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

Zerodha Mutual Fund has issued Addendum No. 03/2026-27 to the Scheme Information Document (SID) of both Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF and Zerodha Gold ETF FOF, revising the minimum daily SIP amount from ₹100 to ₹10. All other terms and conditions of the schemes remain unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members operating on the NSE MF Invest platform are informed to note the revised minimum SIP amount.
- Members should update their systems and inform investors of the new minimum daily SIP threshold for these two schemes.
- No specific compliance action is required beyond acknowledging the change.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 21, 2026 — Revised minimum daily SIP amount of ₹10 comes into effect.
- **NSE Circular Date:** June 08, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This change lowers the entry barrier for retail investors wishing to invest via daily SIP in Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF and Zerodha Gold ETF FOF. By reducing the minimum from ₹100 to ₹10, the fund house aims to make micro-investing more accessible. The operational impact on the NSE MF Invest platform is minimal and routine. There is no broader market or systemic impact.
