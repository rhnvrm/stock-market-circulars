## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the suspension of trading in three privately placed Non-Convertible Securities effective May 29, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspensions are triggered by the redemption of the respective securities.

## Key Points

- Three Non-Convertible Securities will be suspended from trading on May 29, 2026
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the underlying securities
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74426 | Circular Ref. No: 0856/2026
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The suspensions are routine actions pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs the suspension of debt securities upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the listed ISINs from May 29, 2026
- No further action required from members beyond halting trades in these securities

## Important Dates

| Company | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited | INE033L07HF1 | May 29, 2026 | Redemption |
| HDFC Bank Limited | INE040A08757 | May 29, 2026 | Redemption |
| LIC Housing Finance Limited | INE115A07JQ2 | May 29, 2026 | Redemption |

## Impact Assessment

The suspensions are standard end-of-life events for debt securities reaching their redemption date. Investors holding these securities will receive their principal repayment as per the terms of the respective instruments. There is no broader market impact; only holders of these specific ISINs are directly affected. Members should update their systems to prevent trading in these three ISINs from May 29, 2026 onwards.
