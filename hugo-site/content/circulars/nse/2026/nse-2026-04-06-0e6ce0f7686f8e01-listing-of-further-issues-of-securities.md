## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further securities for 10 companies under the Capital Market segment, effective April 07, 2026. All securities listed are equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing shares. No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the allotted securities.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref No.: NSE/CML/73626 / 0579/2026, issued April 06, 2026
- Securities admitted to dealings effective **April 07, 2026**
- All allotments are under ESOP schemes; all shares are pari passu with no lock-in
- 10 companies covered across varying share counts and face values
- Trading will use designated security codes and lot sizes as specified in the Annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The notification is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading these newly listed securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes per the Annexure
- No additional compliance actions required from members beyond standard trading procedures
