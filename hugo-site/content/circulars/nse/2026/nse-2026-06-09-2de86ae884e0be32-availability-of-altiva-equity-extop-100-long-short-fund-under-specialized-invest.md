## Summary

NSE has informed all members that Altiva Equity ExTop 100 Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) offered by Edelweiss Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 10, 2026. SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will also be available.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants are being made available across Direct and Regular plans, covering Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Effective date: June 10, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Fund house: Edelweiss Mutual Fund
- Category: Equity (all six variants)
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are included

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling transactions for an existing SIF scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members on the NMF TM Segment should update their systems to reflect the availability of these six scheme variants for client transactions starting June 10, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for reopening ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP for all six variants on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low broad market impact. It is relevant specifically to NSE MF Invest Platform members and investors interested in the Altiva Equity ExTop 100 Long-Short Fund under the SIF category from Edelweiss Mutual Fund. The six ISINs enabled are INF754K30094, INF754K30102, INF754K30110, INF754K30136, INF754K30144, and INF754K30151.
