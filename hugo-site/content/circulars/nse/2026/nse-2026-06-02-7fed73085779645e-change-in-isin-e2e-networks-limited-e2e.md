## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the ISIN for the equity shares of E2E Networks Limited (Symbol: E2E) will be changed to **INE255Z01027** with effect from June 05, 2026. The change is consequent to a sub-division (stock split) of the face value of shares from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-.

## Key Points

- The ISIN for E2E Networks Limited equity shares is being changed as part of a face value sub-division.
- New ISIN: **INE255Z01027**
- The new ISIN is effective for all trades done on and from the ex-date, i.e., June 05, 2026.
- Symbol remains **E2E** on NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN change is mandated by the corporate action of sub-division of shares. All references to the old ISIN must be replaced with INE255Z01027 in trading systems, back-office platforms, and depository records from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE255Z01027) for E2E Networks Limited before June 05, 2026.
- All trades executed on or after June 05, 2026 must use the new ISIN.
- Members should communicate this change to their clients and ensure back-office and settlement systems are updated accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Ex-Date / Effective Date for New ISIN:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The ISIN change is a direct result of the stock split (sub-division from Rs. 10/- face value to Re. 1/-), which will increase the number of shares outstanding by a factor of 10. Members and investors holding E2E Networks Limited shares must ensure their depository accounts and trading records reflect the new ISIN post the ex-date. No trading disruption is expected if systems are updated timely. The stock's market price per share will adjust proportionately on the ex-date.
