## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,14,22,378 equity shares of California Software Company Limited (symbol: CALSOFT) under the BE series on the Capital Market segment, effective June 29, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company:** California Software Company Limited
- **Symbol:** CALSOFT | **Series:** BE
- **ISIN:** INE526B01014 (permanent); temporary ISIN IN8526B01013 used during dematerialisation credit
- **Number of shares listed:** 1,14,22,378
- **Face value / Paid-up value:** Rs. 10/- each
- **Market lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Distinctive numbers:** From 23612933 to 35035310
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

This listing is pursuant to:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification

The temporary ISIN (IN8526B01013) used for dematerialised credit of allotted shares will be replaced by the permanent ISIN (INE526B01014) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code CALSOFT (series BE) and ISIN INE526B01014 for all trades in these shares on and after June 29, 2026.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 1.14 crore fully paid-up equity shares of CALSOFT to the Capital Market segment. Existing holders of the partly paid-up shares (under temporary ISIN IN8526B01013) will see their holdings migrated to the permanent ISIN upon activation. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares with no lock-in, making them freely tradeable from the effective date. Market impact is moderate and confined to CALSOFT shareholders and traders.
