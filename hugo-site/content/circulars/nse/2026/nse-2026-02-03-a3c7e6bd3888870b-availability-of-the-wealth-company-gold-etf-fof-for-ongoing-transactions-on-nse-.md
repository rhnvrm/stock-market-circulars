## Summary

The Wealth Company Gold ETF FOF of Wealth Mutual Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform effective February 04, 2026. The fund will be available with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. Six plan variants are available covering Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: February 04, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Fund Manager: Wealth Mutual Fund
- Fund Type: Gold ETF Fund of Fund (FOF)
- Available Facilities: Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants available with unique ISINs
- All schemes categorized as Debt category
- Both Direct and Regular plan options available
- IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options: Payout and Reinvestment
- Growth option available for both Direct and Regular plans

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.
