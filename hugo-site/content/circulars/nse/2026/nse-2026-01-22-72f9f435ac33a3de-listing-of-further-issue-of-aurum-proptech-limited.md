## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 54,390 equity shares of Aurum PropTech Limited (Symbol: AURUM) on the Capital Market segment, resulting from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares. The listing will be effective from January 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Aurum PropTech Limited (Symbol: AURUM)
- **Number of Securities**: 54,390 equity shares
- **ISIN**: INE898S01029 (permanent ISIN)
- **Temporary ISIN**: IN8898S01010 (for dematerialized shares)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5 per share
- **Paid-up Value**: Rs. 5 per share
- **Security Description**: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Lock-in**: Not Applicable
- **Distinctive Numbers**: From 72747564 to 72801953

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should note the designated security codes for trading purposes
- Trading in the securities shall be in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- Shares allotted in dematerialized mode have been credited under temporary ISIN (IN8898S01010) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The conversion of 54,390 partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up shares represents a small capital event for Aurum PropTech Limited. The listing does not affect existing trading arrangements and has no lock-in restrictions. Market participants can trade these shares from January 23, 2026 onwards in the standard market lot of 1 share.
