## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in three Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited, effective May 22, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the maturity of these debt securities, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Three NCD instruments of Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited are being suspended from trading.
- Reason for suspension is maturity of the respective securities.
- Circular issued under NSE Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74327, Circular Ref. No: 0819/2026.
- Suspension is effective from May 22, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This action is taken in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the suspension of securities upon maturity or other qualifying events.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in the three listed NCD symbols from May 22, 2026.
- Holders of these NCDs should follow applicable redemption/maturity settlement procedures with Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited and the relevant depositories.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects three specific NCD instruments of Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | ISIN | Reason |
|---------|--------|------|--------|
| 1 | 872MMFSL26 | INE774D08LU6 | Maturity |
| 2 | 9MMFS-ML | INE774D08MA6 | Maturity |
| 3 | M&MFIN | INE774D08MG3 | Maturity |

This is a routine operational action with limited broader market impact. Investors holding these debentures will receive maturity proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No trading in these symbols will be permitted on NSE from May 22, 2026 onwards.
