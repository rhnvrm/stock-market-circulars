## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance activity that will result in complete downtime of the NSE MF Invest Platform on January 24, 2026, from 04:00 hrs to 07:00 hrs (3-hour window). During this period, the platform will be completely unavailable for all activities and transactions.

## Key Points

- Complete platform downtime scheduled for January 24, 2026
- Downtime window: 04:00 hrs to 07:00 hrs (3 hours)
- All activities and transactions will be unavailable during this period
- Members connecting via API must inform their technical teams and vendors
- Members should plan their activities accordingly to avoid disruption

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions during the maintenance window
- Members using API connections must notify their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled downtime
- Members should plan their mutual fund investment activities to avoid the maintenance window

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 24, 2026, 04:00 hrs**: Maintenance downtime begins
- **January 24, 2026, 07:00 hrs**: Maintenance downtime ends, platform expected to resume

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - The 3-hour maintenance window will temporarily halt all mutual fund investment activities on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members and investors will be unable to execute transactions during this period.

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - Impact is limited to the mutual fund segment and is temporary in nature. The early morning timing (04:00-07:00 hrs) may minimize disruption for most retail investors.

**Technical Impact**: Members using API integrations need to ensure their systems handle the downtime gracefully and avoid transaction failures or errors during the maintenance window.
