## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 130 (92 days) NFO of Axis Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Four variants of the scheme are being launched, with an allotment date of March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Four scheme variants offered: Direct IDCW Payout, Direct Growth, Regular Growth, and Regular IDCW Payout
- All four variants have an allotment date of March 12, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by March 12, 2026, 12:30 PM
- Category listed as Equity in the circular (likely a data error; this is a Fixed Maturity Plan, a debt instrument)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO launch communication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before March 12, 2026, 12:30 PM.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Open Date:** March 09, 2026
- **NFO Close Date:** March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Funds Deadline:** March 12, 2026, 12:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact. This is a standard NFO announcement for a short-duration (92-day) fixed maturity plan. It is relevant primarily to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions and retail/institutional investors seeking short-term debt investments. The four scheme variants (INF846KA1507, INF846KA1481, INF846KA1473, INF846KA1499) provide flexibility in payout and plan type.
