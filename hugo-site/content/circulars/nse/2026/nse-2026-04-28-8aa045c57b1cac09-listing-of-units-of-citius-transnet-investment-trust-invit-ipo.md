## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members about the forthcoming listing of units of Citius TransNet Investment Trust, an Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT), under the IPO segment. The security will trade under the symbol CITIUSINVT with ISIN INE2Q7823014. The precise date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Citius TransNet Investment Trust is being listed on NSE under the InvIT IPO category
- Trading symbol: **CITIUSINVT**
- ISIN: **INE2Q7823014**
- The listing date has not yet been specified and will be announced separately
- Circular issued by the Listing Department (Ref No: NSE/CML/73931, Circular Ref No: 0696/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification circular issued by NSE's Listing Department to inform members of an upcoming InvIT IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should take note of the forthcoming listing of CITIUSINVT
- Members should await the separate circular that will confirm the exact listing date before facilitating trades in this security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate market impact as it signals the imminent listing of a new Infrastructure Investment Trust on NSE. InvIT listings attract interest from institutional and retail investors seeking infrastructure-linked yield instruments. Members and investors should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date to prepare for trading in CITIUSINVT.
