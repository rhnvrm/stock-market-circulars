## Summary

NSE has informed its members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund is renaming one of its schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The change takes effect from April 01, 2026 and is purely administrative in nature — the scheme's underlying structure and mandate may be adjusted as reflected in the new name.

## Key Points

- The scheme currently named **ICICI Prudential Thematic Advantage Fund (FOF)** will be renamed to **ICICI Prudential Aggressive Hybrid Active FOF**.
- The name change is effective **April 01, 2026** on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- The change is based on a communication received directly from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund.
- A notice cum addendum issued by ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund is enclosed with the circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is an administrative update communicated by ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund to NSE, resulting in a scheme name update on the NSE MF Invest platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members should update their records and systems to reflect the revised scheme name **ICICI Prudential Aggressive Hybrid Active FOF** effective April 01, 2026.
- Members should refer to the enclosed notice cum addendum from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund for detailed information.

## Important Dates

- **April 01, 2026**: Effective date of the scheme name change on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and operational in nature. Investors and members transacting in this scheme on the NSE MF Invest platform will see the updated scheme name from April 1, 2026. No change in NAV computation, investment objective, or fund management is implied by this circular alone. Members should consult the ICICI Prudential notice cum addendum for any substantive changes to the scheme.
