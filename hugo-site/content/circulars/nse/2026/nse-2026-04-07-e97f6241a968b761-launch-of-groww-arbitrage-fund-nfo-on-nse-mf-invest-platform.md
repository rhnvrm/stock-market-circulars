## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Groww Arbitrage Fund NFO by Groww Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from April 08, 2026 to April 22, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The fund offers six scheme variants across direct and regular plans with an allotment date of April 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: April 08, 2026 to April 22, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants available: Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Payout, Regular IDCW Reinvestment
- All schemes are categorised as Equity (Arbitrage)
- Allotment date for all schemes: April 29, 2026
- Clear funds must reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by April 24, 2026 at 6:00 PM
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/73633, Circular Ref. No. 1314/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of a new mutual fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before April 24, 2026 by 6:00 PM.
- Members should facilitate client transactions within the NFO window (April 08–22, 2026).

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| April 08, 2026 | NFO subscription opens |
| April 22, 2026 (11:59 PM) | NFO subscription closes |
| April 24, 2026 (6:00 PM) | Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account |
| April 29, 2026 | Allotment date for all six schemes |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch communication with low market-wide impact. It is relevant to NSE members who participate in mutual fund transactions via the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in arbitrage equity strategies can subscribe to any of the six variants of the Groww Arbitrage Fund. No trading restrictions or systemic changes are introduced by this circular.
