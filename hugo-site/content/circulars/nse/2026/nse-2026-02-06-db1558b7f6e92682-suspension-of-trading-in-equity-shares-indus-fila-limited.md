## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular suspending trading in the equity shares of Indus Fila Limited (Symbol: INDUSFILA) effective from February 09, 2026 (closing hours of February 06, 2026). The suspension is implemented under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, and is specifically due to capital reduction pursuant to an NCLT (National Company Law Tribunal) Order dated May 10, 2019.

## Key Points

- Trading in Indus Fila Limited equity shares will be suspended from February 09, 2026
- The suspension takes effect from closing hours of February 06, 2026
- Reason: Capital reduction pursuant to NCLT Order dated May 10, 2019
- Stock Symbol: INDUSFILA
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/72671, Circular Ref. No: 0195/2026

## Regulatory Changes

This action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in securities under specific circumstances including corporate restructuring activities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in INDUSFILA equity shares from February 09, 2026
- Market participants holding positions in this security should note that no further trading will be permitted
- Members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 09, 2026 (from closing hours of February 06, 2026)
- **NCLT Order Date**: May 10, 2019 (original order for capital reduction)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension prevents all buy and sell transactions in this security on NSE.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders of Indus Fila Limited will not be able to trade their shares on the NSE platform. The suspension is linked to a capital reduction process ordered by NCLT, which typically involves restructuring of the company's share capital.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants, brokers, and trading systems must update their systems to block trading in this security. The suspension remains in effect until further notice from NSE.
