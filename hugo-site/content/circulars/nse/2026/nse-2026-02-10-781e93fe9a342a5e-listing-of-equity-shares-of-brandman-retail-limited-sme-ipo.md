## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing of equity shares of Brandman Retail Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective February 11, 2026. The company's shares will trade under the symbol BRANDMAN with ISIN INE0XUD01014, initially in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') before transitioning to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment).

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: BRANDMAN
- **ISIN**: INE0XUD01014
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 176/- per share
- **Total Securities**: 18,457,186 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 800 equity shares
- **Trading Series**: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - TFTS, Settlement Type 'W')
- **Maximum Permissible Spread**: Within 10%
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 18,457,186
- **Market Type**: Continuous Market, Pari Passu

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI regulations:
- SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 (pre-open session for IPO)
- Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018 (continuous market trading)

## Compliance Requirements

- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Security available for trading in continuous market
- Initially listed in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series 'ST')
- Will subsequently shift to Normal Rolling Segment (Series 'SM') as per SEBI circulars
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I (specific details not provided in circular)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: February 11, 2026
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine SME listing on the EMERGE platform with standard surveillance measures. The Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment classification indicates regulatory oversight for new listing.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard IPO listing process with established market maker arrangements (Gretex Share Broking Limited and Shree Bahubali Stock Broking Limited).
