## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Takeover Offer under the Tender Offer scheme of Restaurant Brands Asia Limited (RBA). This follows earlier circulars NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74539 dated June 4, 2026. Members, custodians, and PCMs are required to adhere to the prescribed settlement schedule and instructions for tendering shares via early pay-in.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X, Settlement No. 2026065 has been established for this takeover offer.
- Shares must be tendered exclusively through the Early Pay-in mechanism of depositories under Market Type Buyback.
- On the trade end date (June 19, 2026), early pay-in of shares must be completed well before 3:30 PM.
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur only after receipt of funds pay-in on the settlement day.
- A Tender Offer Acceptance Report will be provided to members detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders along with rejection reason codes.
- Applicable Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day.
- The Clearing Corporation may revise the settlement schedule in case of delays in RTA acceptance details or funds pay-in by buying trading members.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the settlement mechanics for the previously announced takeover offer as per the scheme document referenced in circular NCL/CMPT/73996.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must place Early Pay-in of Shares through the depository mechanism (Market Type: Buyback) — no other mode of share tendering is permitted.
- Early pay-in must be completed before 3:30 PM on the trade end date (June 19, 2026).
- Members must refer to Annexure I for the list of rejection reason codes (codes 01–20 covering issues such as signature discrepancies, invalid transfer deeds, stop transfer flags, demat account name mismatches, and excess shares tendered beyond eligibility).
- For queries, members must contact the helpdesk: Toll Free 1800 266 00 50 (Option 2) or ncl_tenderoffer@nsccl.co.in.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | June 5, 2026 |
| Trade Start Date | June 8, 2026 |
| Trade End Date (Early pay-in deadline: 3:30 PM) | June 19, 2026 |
| Obligation Download Date | July 3, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 6, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is directly relevant to members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Restaurant Brands Asia Limited takeover tender offer. The early pay-in mechanism restricts how shares can be tendered, requiring participants to plan depository instructions ahead of the June 19 deadline. The potential for a revised settlement schedule introduces some operational uncertainty. Broader market impact is limited as this applies only to eligible shareholders and trading members involved in this specific offer.
