## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of additional equity shares of V Marc India Limited, issued pursuant to a bonus issue, on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 09, 2026. The shares will trade under the existing symbol VMARCIND.

## Key Points

- Additional equity shares of V Marc India Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment.
- Trading symbol remains VMARCIND.
- ISIN: INE0GXK01018.
- Number of securities allotted: 12,21,03,480.
- Distinctive number range: 24420697 to 146524176.
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification pursuant to existing NSE trading regulations following a corporate bonus action.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize the new bonus shares under the designated security code and ISIN for trading purposes from the effective date. No additional action required from investors beyond standard corporate action processing.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 07, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: July 08, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing & Trading: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables shareholders of V Marc India Limited to trade the newly allotted bonus shares on NSE, increasing the free float and total outstanding shares of the company. Market impact is limited to VMARCIND and its shareholders, with no broader systemic implications.
