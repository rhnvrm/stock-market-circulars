## Summary

NSE has admitted various privately placed securities to dealings on its Debt Market Segment effective February 03, 2026, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The circular lists fresh issues and re-issues of commercial papers and other debt securities from multiple companies.

## Key Points

- Effective date: February 03, 2026
- Fresh issues include 5 commercial papers and 2 other debt securities
- Re-issues include 3 commercial papers
- Commercial papers issued by Godrej Industries Limited, Tata Capital Limited, ICICI Securities Limited, and HDB Financial Services Ltd
- Debt securities issued by Fortis 01 2026 and Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited
- All securities allotted on February 02, 2026 (except debt securities allotted Jan 29-30, 2026)
- ISIN codes assigned for all listed securities

## Compliance Requirements

- All listed securities are available for trading on NSE's Debt Market Segment
- Securities are identified by their designated security codes as specified in the annexure
- Trading subject to NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 29-30, 2026 (debt securities), February 02, 2026 (commercial papers)
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from February 09, 2026 to January 30, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notification with minimal market impact. The circular provides transparency regarding newly listed debt instruments available for trading. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants and institutional investors
- Specific companies mentioned as issuers
- Enhanced liquidity for listed debt securities
- No changes to trading regulations or compliance requirements
