## Summary

NSE notifies members that PNB Housing Finance Limited's privately placed non-convertible debenture (NCD) — PHFL 8.35% 2031 Sr LXXVI — has been admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market Segment with effect from June 11, 2026. Trading will be identified solely by the designated security code with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to NSE Capital Market Segment effective June 11, 2026
- Symbol: 835PHFL31, Series: N0, Market Lot: 1
- Issue: PHFL 8.35% 2031 Sr LXXVI issued by PNB Housing Finance Limited
- Coupon Rate: 8.35% per annum
- Maturity Date: June 9, 2031
- ISIN: INE572E07290
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine admission notification pursuant to existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size specification).

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the new security code 835PHFL31 for trading purposes
- Trading in this security must use the designated code only and must conform to the specified lot size of 1

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date (trading commencement):** June 11, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** June 9, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard private placement debt listing for a single NCD series by PNB Housing Finance Limited. Relevant primarily to institutional and fixed-income investors who trade debt securities on NSE. No equity market implications.
