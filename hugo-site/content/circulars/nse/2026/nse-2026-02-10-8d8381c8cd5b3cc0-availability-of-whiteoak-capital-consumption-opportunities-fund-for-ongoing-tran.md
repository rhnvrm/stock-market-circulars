## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Whiteoak Capital Consumption Opportunities Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective February 11, 2026. The fund will allow subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Whiteoak Capital Consumption Opportunities Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from February 11, 2026
- Both Regular Growth and Direct Growth options available
- Facilities enabled: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Fund category: Equity
- Two ISINs: INF03VN01AE1 (Regular Growth) and INF03VN01AD3 (Direct Growth)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed of the fund's availability for processing investor transactions through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **February 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 11, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Whiteoak Capital Consumption Opportunities Fund for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notification affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for existing and prospective investors of Whiteoak Capital Consumption Opportunities Fund. Investors can now resume or initiate investments in this equity fund through various transaction modes including systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: NSE members and distributors can process transactions for this fund starting February 11, 2026. The platform will support all standard transaction types including lump sum investments, systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP).
