## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has approved the request of Wealth Discovery Securities Pvt. Ltd. to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment. The surrender was approved on June 23, 2026, and all members are notified accordingly.

## Key Points

- Wealth Discovery Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000199032) has surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment.
- The surrender was approved by NSE on June 23, 2026.
- Circular Ref. No. 59/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/COMP/74844) issued by NSE Compliance Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notification of a trading member's voluntary segmental surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of the surrender. No direct compliance action is required from other members.

## Important Dates

- Surrender Approval Date: June 23, 2026
- Circular Date: June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender affects only Wealth Discovery Securities Pvt. Ltd.'s ability to trade in the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment. Other market participants and the broader market are unaffected.
