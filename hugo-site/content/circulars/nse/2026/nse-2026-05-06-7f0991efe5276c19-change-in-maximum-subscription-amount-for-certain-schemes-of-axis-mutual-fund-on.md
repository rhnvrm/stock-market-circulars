## Summary

NSE has notified all members about a restriction on the maximum subscription limit for three Axis Mutual Fund international schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Effective from 9:30 AM on May 5, 2026, lumpsum purchases, switch-ins, and fresh systematic registrations (SIP/STP) exceeding Rs. 1 lakh per PAN per month will be rejected for these schemes. The restriction is temporary and will remain in effect until further notice from Axis AMC.

## Key Points

- Maximum subscription limit of INR 1,00,000 per month per PAN is now applicable for three Axis international fund-of-fund schemes
- Transactions exceeding this limit (lumpsum purchase, switch-in, additional purchase, fresh STP/SIP registrations) will be rejected
- Redemptions, switch-outs, and existing systematic transactions are not affected and will continue normally
- The restriction originated from Axis AMC's addendum dated May 4, 2026, and was further clarified on May 5, 2026
- NSE has implemented the changes on the MF Invest Platform with immediate effect

## Regulatory Changes

As per the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Axis Asset Management Company Limited (No. 13/2026-27), the following changes are effective from May 5, 2026 (9:30 AM):

- **Restricted transactions**: Lumpsum subscription, switch-ins, fresh registrations of Systematic Transactions (SIP/STP) above Rs. 1 lakh per PAN per month are suspended
- **Permitted transactions**: Subscriptions up to Rs. 1 lakh per PAN per month, redemptions, switch-outs, and continuation of existing systematic transactions remain unaffected
- The restriction modifies and supersedes the earlier addendum dated May 4, 2026

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure that transactions submitted on the MF Invest Platform for the three affected schemes comply with the Rs. 1 lakh per PAN per month ceiling
- Any transactions exceeding the limit will be system-rejected; members should inform their clients accordingly
- Members should communicate this restriction to investors to avoid failed transactions

## Important Dates

- **May 4, 2026**: Original addendum issued by Axis AMC announcing temporary subscription suspension
- **May 5, 2026, 9:30 AM**: Effective date of the Rs. 1 lakh per PAN per month restriction
- **May 6, 2026**: NSE circular issued to all members (Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74068, Circular Ref. No: 1354/2026)
- **End date**: Not specified; restriction is temporary and will continue until further notice from Axis AMC

## Impact Assessment

**Affected Schemes:**
1. Axis Global Equity Alpha Fund of Fund
2. Axis Global Innovation Fund of Fund
3. Axis Greater China Equity Fund of Fund

**Investor Impact:** Investors seeking to deploy large lump-sum amounts (above Rs. 1 lakh) into these international fund-of-funds will be unable to do so through the NSE MF Invest Platform until the restriction is lifted. This is particularly relevant to high-net-worth investors and those seeking international equity exposure via these schemes.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers and distributors using the NSE MF Invest Platform must update their systems and client communication to reflect the new subscription cap. Existing SIP/STP investors are unaffected.

**Market Context:** The restriction likely stems from regulatory limits on overseas investments by Indian mutual funds under SEBI/RBI guidelines on the aggregate industry-level cap for international fund investments. This is a precautionary measure by Axis AMC to manage inflows within permissible overseas investment limits.
