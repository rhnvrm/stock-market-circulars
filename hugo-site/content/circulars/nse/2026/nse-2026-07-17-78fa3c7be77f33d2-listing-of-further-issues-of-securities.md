## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities of thirteen companies — Bosch Limited, ICICI Bank Limited, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited, Jtekt India Limited, Vedant Fashions Limited, Markolines Pavement Technologies Limited, Nitco Limited, FSN E-Commerce Ventures Limited, Pidilite Industries Limited, Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Limited and Virinchi Limited — under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The new securities, mostly allotted under ESOP schemes (with Bosch Limited's allotment under a Preferential Issue), will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 20, 2026, ranking pari passu with existing shares.

## Key Points

- Bosch Limited: 2,460 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under Preferential Issue on 10-Jun-2026 at an issue price of Rs. 35,200; locked in until 30-Jan-2028.
- ICICI Bank Limited: 94,547 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on 16-Jul-2026; no lock-in.
- ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited: 8,186 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP on 14-Jul-2026; no lock-in.
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited: equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP on 14-Jul-2026 (further details in Annexure).
- Remaining companies (Jtekt India, Vedant Fashions, Markolines Pavement Technologies, Nitco, FSN E-Commerce Ventures, Pidilite Industries, Sona BLW Precision Forgings, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank, Virinchi) also have further issues listed per the Annexure, largely under ESOP allotments.
- All new securities carry Market Lot of 1 and are identified by their existing designated security codes.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A regarding admission of further issued securities and applicable lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional securities as tradable using the existing designated codes and applicable lot size (1) from the effective date; no separate action is required by investors.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (Bosch Limited - Preferential Issue): June 10, 2026
- Date of Allotment (ICICI Lombard, ICICI Prudential Life): July 14, 2026
- Date of Allotment (ICICI Bank): July 16, 2026
- Effective date of listing for all securities: July 20, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (Bosch Limited preferential allotment): January 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular reflecting incremental share issuances, primarily via ESOP exercises, across multiple large and mid-cap companies. Given the small size of these allotments relative to each company's total outstanding share capital, the market and price impact is expected to be minimal. It marginally increases the free-float/paid-up capital of the respective companies and is relevant mainly for accurate shareholding and dilution tracking rather than trading strategy.
