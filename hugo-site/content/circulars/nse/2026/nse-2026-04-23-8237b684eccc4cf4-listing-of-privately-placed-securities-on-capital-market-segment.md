## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of privately placed securities of L&T Finance Limited on the Capital Market segment of the National Stock Exchange, effective April 24, 2026. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- L&T Finance Limited bond series (LTFL 7.7942% 2031 Sr A) admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from April 24, 2026
- Trading symbol: 7794LTFL31, Series: N0, Market Lot: 1
- Coupon Rate: 7.7942% per annum
- Maturity Date: June 27, 2031
- ISIN: INE498L07202
- Securities shall be identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Circular is issued in routine compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to recognize the designated security code 7794LTFL31 for trading
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- Members must use designated codes exclusively to identify these securities on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 24, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)
- **Maturity Date:** June 27, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with low market impact. The admission of L&T Finance Limited's fixed-rate bond (7.7942% coupon, maturing 2031) to NSE's capital market segment provides debt market investors with an additional tradeable instrument. The market lot size of 1 allows flexible trading. No systemic or broad market implications are anticipated.
