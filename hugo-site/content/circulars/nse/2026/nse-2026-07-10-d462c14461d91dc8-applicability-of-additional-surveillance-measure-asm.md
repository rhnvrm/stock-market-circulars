## Summary

NSE has notified that National Standard (India) Limited (NATIONSTD) has been shortlisted under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long Term - ASM) Framework Stage I, having moved from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework. No securities were shortlisted for Stage IV, or for movement between Stage I to Stage II, Stage II to Stage III, or Stage I to Stage IV in this cycle.

## Key Points

- NATIONSTD (National Standard (India) Limited, ISIN: INE166R01015) has moved from STASM to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I, effective July 13, 2026.
- No securities were added to Long-Term ASM Stage IV in this cycle.
- No securities moved between other ASM stages (I→II, II→III, I→IV) in this cycle.
- A 100% margin will apply on all open and new positions in the affected security from July 15, 2026.
- This circular references and continues the framework established under prior NSE circulars dated October 27, 2018, July 22, 2020, December 04, 2020, June 04, 2021, April 22, 2022, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes were introduced; this circular applies the existing Long-Term ASM Framework criteria to update the list of shortlisted securities. Long-Term ASM Stage I imposes a 100% margin requirement, and Stage IV shifts a scrip from the Rolling Settlement (EQ series) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE series). This cycle involves only a Stage I inclusion; no Stage IV shifts occurred.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply 100% margin on all open positions as of July 14, 2026, and on new positions created from July 15, 2026 onwards, for NATIONSTD.
- Members should ensure the ASM measures are applied in conjunction with any other prevailing surveillance measures on the security.
- Price bands for any scrip exiting the framework should be reinstated to pre-shortlisting levels, unless another surveillance framework applies.

## Important Dates

- **July 13, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of NATIONSTD in Long-Term ASM Stage I.
- **July 14, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to the new margin.
- **July 15, 2026**: 100% margin becomes applicable on all open and new positions in NATIONSTD.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to traders and investors holding or trading NATIONSTD, who will face significantly higher margin requirements (100%) starting July 15, 2026, which may reduce liquidity and trading volume in the stock. The broader market impact is minimal since this is a routine, security-specific surveillance action rather than a market-wide measure, and the shortlisting is explicitly stated to not be an adverse reflection on the company itself.
