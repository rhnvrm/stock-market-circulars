## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) has notified members that Orissa Bengal Carrier Limited (symbol: OBCL) will change its registered name to OBCL Limited, effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- The company currently trading under the symbol OBCL will change its name from "Orissa Bengal Carrier Limited" to "OBCL Limited".
- The trading symbol OBCL remains unchanged; only the company name is updated.
- The change takes effect from July 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory or structural changes are involved; this is an administrative update to reflect the company's new registered name in exchange records.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their internal records, client communications, and back-office systems to reflect the new company name effective July 17, 2026. No action is required from investors regarding their existing holdings.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** July 17, 2026
- **Circular Date:** July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine name change with no impact on trading, share value, or corporate structure. The trading symbol OBCL remains the same, minimizing disruption for market participants. Impact on investors and trading operations is negligible.
