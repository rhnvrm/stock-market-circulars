## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Indian Railway Finance Corporation Limited (IRFC). The OFS spans two order dates — June 24, 2026 (non-retail) and June 25, 2026 (retail and non-retail) — with corresponding settlements on June 25 and June 29, 2026. This circular supplements the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and confirms that OFS settlements follow existing secondary market transaction rules.

## Key Points

- OFS order date for non-retail (IS) category: June 24, 2026; settlement date: June 25, 2026
- OFS order date for retail (RS) and non-retail (IS) categories: June 25, 2026; settlement date: June 29, 2026
- Non-retail (IS) trades carry 100% margin (H-type) or 0% margin (T-type)
- Retail (RS) trades carry 100% margin with no custodial confirmation required
- Custodial confirmation deadline for non-retail IS (June 24 orders): June 24, 2026 by 03:30 PM
- Custodial confirmation deadline for non-retail IS (June 25 orders): June 25, 2026 by 03:30 PM; T-type by June 29 at 07:30 AM
- Funds pay-in: T+1 by 11:00 AM; Securities and funds pay-out: T+1 by 01:30 PM
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises existing OFS settlement rules (per NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) for the specific IRFC OFS tranches.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the specified settlement dates and custodial confirmation deadlines
- Non-retail (IS) participants must complete custodial confirmation by 03:30 PM on the order date (H-type) or by 07:30 AM on the settlement date (T-type)
- Ensure funds pay-in is completed by T+1 at 11:00 AM
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement day
- For queries, contact the help desk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: OFS order date for non-retail (IS) category; custodial confirmation by 03:30 PM
- **June 25, 2026**: Settlement date for June 24 orders; OFS order date for retail (RS) and non-retail (IS); custodial confirmation for June 25 IS orders by 03:30 PM
- **June 29, 2026**: Settlement date for June 25 orders; T-type custodial confirmation by 07:30 AM

## Impact Assessment

This circular is directly relevant to members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the IRFC OFS. The bifurcated schedule (non-retail on June 24, retail and non-retail on June 25) requires participants to track two separate settlement cycles. The 100% margin requirement for H-type settlements increases liquidity demands for non-retail participants. Retail investors (RS category) settle on June 29 with no custodial confirmation step, simplifying their process. Overall market impact is limited to IRFC OFS participants; broader market operations are unaffected.
