## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Groww Nifty PSU Bank Index Fund of Groww Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facility on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective March 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Groww Nifty PSU Bank Index Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 27, 2026
- Both Direct and Regular plan variants are included across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will also be available
- All six scheme variants fall under the Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the availability of these schemes for client transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **March 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Groww Nifty PSU Bank Index Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in six variants of the Groww Nifty PSU Bank Index Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The schemes are:
