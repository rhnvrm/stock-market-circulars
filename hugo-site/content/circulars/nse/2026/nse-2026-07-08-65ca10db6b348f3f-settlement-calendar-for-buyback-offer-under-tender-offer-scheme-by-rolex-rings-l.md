## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the buyback offer under the tender offer scheme by Rolex Rings Limited. This follows earlier circulars NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/75083 dated July 7, 2026. The circular specifies the trade window, obligation download, and settlement dates for Settlement Type X, Settlement No. 2026085.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: X, Settlement No. 2026085
- Trade start date: July 9, 2026
- Trade end date: July 15, 2026
- Obligation download date: July 21, 2026
- Settlement date: July 22, 2026
- Demat shares must be tendered only via Early Pay-in of Shares through the depositories' early pay-in mechanism for Market Type Buyback
- On trade end date, early pay-in of shares must be completed well in advance of order acceptance cutoff at 3:30 p.m.
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur only after receipt of funds pay-in on settlement day
- A Tender Offer Acceptance Report detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders (with reason codes) will be provided to members
- Applicable Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day
- Clearing Corporation may revise the settlement schedule in case of delays in receiving acceptance details from RTA or delays in funds pay-in by the buying trading member

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes the settlement schedule for an already-approved buyback tender offer scheme by Rolex Rings Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must ensure demat shares are tendered strictly through the early pay-in mechanism of depositories
- Early pay-in of shares must be completed ahead of the 3:30 p.m. order acceptance cutoff on the trade end date
- Buying trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid settlement delays
- Members should refer to the rejection codes in Annexure I to address partial allocation or rejection of tendered shares
- Members must refer to the original scheme document for further procedural details

## Important Dates

- Trade start date: July 9, 2026
- Trade end date: July 15, 2026
- Obligation download date: July 21, 2026
- Settlement date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a procedural/operational impact primarily on clearing members, custodians, and PCMs handling the Rolex Rings Limited buyback tender offer, as well as shareholders tendering shares. It does not affect broader market pricing or trading activity but is important for ensuring smooth settlement of the buyback transaction within the prescribed timeline.
