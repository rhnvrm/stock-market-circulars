## Summary

NSE has updated the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) framework, adding four securities to Stage I and moving one security from Stage I to Stage II, all effective June 05, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply on all open and new positions from June 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4 securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 05, 2026 (all moved from Short-Term ASM to Long-Term ASM)
- 1 security (VENUSREM) moved from Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. June 05, 2026
- No securities shortlisted for Stage IV; no Stage II to Stage III movements
- 100% margin applicable from June 09, 2026 on all open positions as on June 08, 2026 and all new positions thereafter
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and operates under the framework established by earlier NSE circulars: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). Scrips shifted to Stage IV would move from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE); however, no such movement has been announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Must note the updated ASM list and apply 100% margin on affected securities from June 09, 2026.
- **Market Participants**: Must comply with increased margin requirements for open positions as on June 08, 2026 and all new positions from June 09, 2026 onward.
- ASM classification is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the listed company.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 05, 2026 | LTASM Stage I inclusion effective for BBOX, GTECJAINX, NITTAGELA, ONYX |
| June 05, 2026 | VENUSREM moves from LTASM Stage I to Stage II |
| June 08, 2026 | Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin |
| June 09, 2026 | 100% margin requirement comes into effect for all open and new positions |

## Impact Assessment

**Securities Entering Long-Term ASM Stage I (moved from STASM):**
- **BBOX** – Black Box Limited (INE676A01027)
- **GTECJAINX** – G-TEC JAINX EDUCATION LIMITED (INE586X01012)
- **NITTAGELA** – Nitta Gelatin India Limited (INE265B01019)
- **ONYX** – Onyx Biotec Limited (INE0WVU01018)

**Security Moving to Long-Term ASM Stage II:**
- **VENUSREM** – Venus Remedies Limited (INE411B01019)

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or taking new positions in these securities, effectively reducing leverage to zero. Stage II placement for Venus Remedies indicates continued or worsening surveillance concerns beyond the initial stage. Retail and institutional traders holding these securities should plan for higher capital requirements or position adjustments before June 09, 2026.
