## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has notified that Standard Glass Lining Technology Limited will undergo a corporate name change to Standard Engineering Technology Limited, accompanied by a change in trading symbol from SGLTL to SETL, effective January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol SGLTL will be changed to SETL
- Company name will change from "Standard Glass Lining Technology Limited" to "Standard Engineering Technology Limited"
- Changes will be effective from January 19, 2026
- NSE Circular Reference: 0048/2026
- NSE Download Reference: NSE/CML/72266

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving name and symbol modification for a listed company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- Members should ensure proper identification of the security under its new symbol SETL from the effective date
- Trading and settlement systems must be updated to recognize both the old and new identifiers during the transition

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 - Name and symbol change becomes operational

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name and symbol changes are administrative in nature and do not affect the underlying business operations or shareholder equity.

**Trading Impact**: Investors and traders must use the new symbol SETL for trading from January 19, 2026. Existing holdings will automatically reflect under the new name and symbol.

**Operational Impact**: Exchange members need to update internal systems, databases, and client communications to reflect the new company identifier to ensure seamless trading and settlement operations.
