## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for 20 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective May 27, 2026, under Circular Ref. No. 0853/2026. The newly listed shares arise primarily from Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) allotments and, in the case of Emami Realty, from the conversion of warrants allotted on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- Securities for 20 companies admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective May 27, 2026.
- Majority of new shares result from ESOP allotments across multiple companies.
- Emami Realty Limited (EMAMIREAL) listing arises from conversion of preferential warrants (8,200,000 shares at Rs. 128.5 issue price, allotted 08-Apr-2026).
- All new securities are pari passu with existing shares and carry no lock-in.
- Trading is to be conducted using designated security codes and specified lot sizes as per the Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of further securities to dealings and specification of lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading in the newly listed securities.
- Trading must be conducted in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure.
- No additional compliance action is required from listed companies or members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Selected Allotment Dates:**
  - Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC (ABSLAMC): 20-May-2026 — 78,697 ESOP shares
  - Anthem Biosciences (ANTHEM): 19-May-2026 — 1,481,250 ESOP shares
  - Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions (CLEANMAX): 20-May-2026 — 4,800 ESOP shares
  - Eicher Motors (EICHERMOT): 22-May-2026 — 183,326 ESOP shares
  - Emami Realty (EMAMIREAL): 08-Apr-2026 — 8,200,000 shares (warrant conversion)

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a moderate market impact due to the large number of companies involved and the significant share counts in certain cases (notably Anthem Biosciences at 1.48 million shares and Emami Realty at 8.2 million shares). ESOP-related listings represent minor dilution and are standard corporate events. The Emami Realty warrant conversion is the most notable entry given the volume and preferential-basis origin. No market disruption is expected as these are routine further-issue listings with immediate pari passu status and no lock-in restrictions.
