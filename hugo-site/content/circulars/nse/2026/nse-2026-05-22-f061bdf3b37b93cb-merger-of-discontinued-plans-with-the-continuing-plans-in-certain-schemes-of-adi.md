## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited (ABSLAMC), Investment Manager for Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund, and Aditya Birla Sun Life Trustee Private Limited (ABSLTPL) have approved the merger of Discontinued Plans (Transferor Plans) into Continuing Plans (Transferee Plans) across five ABSL Mutual Fund schemes. The merger is effective from the close of business hours on Friday, June 19, 2026. This is notified via Addendum No. 28/2026 to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM).

## Key Points

- Five ABSL Mutual Fund schemes are consolidating discontinued legacy plans (Retail and Discipline Advantage Plans) into their respective Regular and Direct Growth/IDCW plans.
- The merger is effective from close of business hours on June 19, 2026.
- Unitholders in the discontinued plans will be given an exit option window before the effective date.
- Updated SID and KIM will be available at ISCs and on the ABSL Mutual Fund website after the exit option period concludes.
- This addendum (No. 28/2026) forms an integral part of the SID/KIM read with earlier addenda.

## Regulatory Changes

The Board of Directors of ABSLAMC and ABSLTPL have approved this scheme-level consolidation under SEBI mutual fund regulations. The Notice-cum-Addendum modifies the SID and KIM for each of the affected schemes to reflect the merger of discontinued plans.

## Compliance Requirements

- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC must update the SID and KIM for all affected schemes and make them available at ISCs and on the website post the exit option period.
- Existing unitholders in the discontinued plans should review the notice and exercise their exit option before June 19, 2026, if they do not wish to be transferred to the continuing plans.
- Investors can locate nearest ISCs at https://mutualfund.adityabirlacapital.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 14, 2026
- **NSE Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Merger Effective Date:** Close of business hours, Friday, June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine plan consolidation exercise that affects unitholders currently invested in legacy Retail and Discipline Advantage Plans of five ABSL Mutual Fund schemes. The schemes affected are:

1. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Savings Fund** — Discipline Advantage Plan (Growth), Retail Daily Dividend, Retail Growth, Retail Weekly Dividend plans merging into Regular/Direct Growth and IDCW variants.
2. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Dynamic Bond Fund** — Discipline Advantage Plan (Growth) merging into Regular/Direct Growth.
3. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Floating Rate Fund** — Retail Growth and Retail Weekly Dividend merging into Regular/Direct Growth and Weekly IDCW.
4. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Money Manager Fund** — Retail Daily Dividend, Retail Growth, and Retail Weekly Dividend merging into Regular/Direct IDCW and Growth variants.
5. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Low Duration Fund** — Institutional plan(s) merging into continuing plans (details partially available).

The operational impact is limited to existing investors in these discontinued plans. Post-merger, units will be transferred to the continuing plans at NAV-based ratios. Broader market impact is minimal as this is an internal AMC restructuring with no new fund launch or regulatory change.
