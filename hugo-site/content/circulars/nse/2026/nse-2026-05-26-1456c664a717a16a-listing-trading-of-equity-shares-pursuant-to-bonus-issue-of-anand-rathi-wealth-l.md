## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of Anand Rathi Wealth Limited (Symbol: ANANDRATHI) issued pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 8,30,20,634 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8,30,20,634 bonus equity shares of Anand Rathi Wealth Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: ANANDRATHI; ISIN: INE463V01026
- Distinctive number range: 83390635 to 166411268
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs admission of securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are informed of the new securities admitted to dealings
- Members must use the designated security codes (Symbol: ANANDRATHI) for trading the bonus shares on the system

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date for Listing & Trading:** June 05, 2026
- **Circular Date:** May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Anand Rathi Wealth Limited will receive bonus shares credited to their accounts following the record date of June 03, 2026. The listing of 8,30,20,634 new shares on June 05, 2026 will increase the total share count, which typically results in a proportional price adjustment. This is a routine corporate action with medium market impact, primarily affecting current shareholders of ANANDRATHI.
