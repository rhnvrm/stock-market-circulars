## Summary

NSE has admitted Rights Entitlements (REs) of AVG Logistics Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment with effect from June 1, 2026. The REs will trade under the symbol AVG-RE in the Trade to Trade (BE) series in compulsory demat mode. A total of 3,645,742 rights entitlements are available for trading or renunciation during the specified window.

## Key Points

- Symbol: AVG-RE; Series: BE; ISIN: INE680Z20018
- Total Rights Entitlements: 3,645,742
- Market lot size: 1
- REs trade in the Trade to Trade segment (compulsory demat for all investors)
- Trading members must prominently inform clients that REs are not ordinary shares
- Purchase of REs confers the right to participate in the rights issue or renounce before closing; unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after the Issue Closing Date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, admitting the REs to dealings and specifying lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of trading in REs, including the ISIN, and clarify these are not ordinary shares.
- Contract notes and bills for RE purchases must prominently carry a disclaimer explaining that REs give only the right to participate in the rights issue or renounce before closure; REs neither subscribed nor renounced will lapse after the Issue Closing Date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective date of trading:** June 01, 2026
- **Trading period for renunciation:** June 01, 2026 to June 04, 2026
- **Date of suspension of RE trading:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational notification for a small-cap company's rights issue. Impact is limited to existing shareholders of AVG Logistics Limited who hold rights entitlements and trading members facilitating RE transactions. The short 4-day trading window (June 1–4) and Trade to Trade settlement restrict speculative activity. Investors must act promptly to subscribe or renounce, as unactioned REs will lapse after June 5, 2026.
