## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Iware Supplychain Services Limited (Symbol: IWARE, SME EMERGE segment) under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The shares were allotted on a preferential basis and will be admitted to dealings with effect from July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Iware Supplychain Services Limited
- Symbol: IWARE; Series: SM
- ISIN: INE1AII01014 (currently under temporary ISIN pending activation)
- Face Value: Rs. 10; Paid-up Value: Rs. 10; Issue Price: Rs. 255
- Security Description: Equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted on Preferential Basis
- No. of Securities: 790,800
- Distinctive Number Range: 10716001 to 11506800
- Market Lot: 600
- Pari Passu status: Yes

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and lot size specification under Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security only by its designated codes and trade in the specified lot size of 600.
- The temporary ISIN will be activated per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 05, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 09, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry Date: January 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME segment listing update with limited market-wide impact. It primarily affects trading members dealing in IWARE shares, who must note the new market lot and pari passu status of the newly listed shares, and existing shareholders subject to the lock-in until January 10, 2027.
