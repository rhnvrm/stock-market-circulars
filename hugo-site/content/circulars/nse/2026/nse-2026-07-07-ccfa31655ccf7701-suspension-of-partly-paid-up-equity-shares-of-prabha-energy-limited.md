## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in the partly paid-up equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited (Symbol: PRABHAPP1, ISIN: IN90I0M01022) with effect from July 09, 2026 (i.e., from the closing hours of trading on July 08, 2026). The suspension is on account of the second and final call on these partly paid shares, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Security suspended: Prabha Energy Limited partly paid-up equity shares (Symbol: PRABHAPP1)
- ISIN: IN90I0M01022
- Reason: Suspension on account of second and final call
- Suspension effective from July 09, 2026, i.e., from the close of trading on July 08, 2026
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No broad regulatory changes; this is a company-specific trading action under existing NSE listing/trading regulations governing partly paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease trading in the PRABHAPP1 scrip from the specified suspension date. No further action is required from other listed entities.

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2026: Last day of trading (closing hours)
- July 09, 2026: Suspension of trading takes effect

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders and traders holding the partly paid-up shares of Prabha Energy Limited. Trading in this specific scrip will halt following the second and final call, which is a standard process for partly paid shares transitioning toward full payment. No wider market impact is expected.
