## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. The session covers two trading phases — from the Primary Site (BKC) and from the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site — along with re-login timings. No new software version is being released; members should continue using NEAT+ v1.0.0 and must update to NEAT Adapter v1.0.25.

## Key Points

- Mock trading is scheduled for June 06, 2026 in the EGR segment only
- Two trading sessions: Primary Site (10:00–13:00) and DR Site (14:00–14:30)
- No new NEAT+ version; current version remains 1.0.0 (download path: `/egftp/egcommon/NEATPlus100`)
- Members must uninstall the older NEAT Adapter and install updated NEAT Adapter v1.0.25 (Windows & Linux) from Extranet path via GR PORT 11887
- Trades during mock sessions carry no financial obligation (no fund pay-in or pay-out)
- Extranet/Member portal and Extranet API will be unavailable from 11:30 am to 1:00 pm
- Live re-login window: 16:00–16:30

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock trading circular issued in continuation of circular Download No. 74203 dated May 13, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must uninstall the previous NEAT Adapter version and re-download and install NEAT Adapter v1.0.25 (available for both Windows and Linux)
- Members should refer to circular NSE/EGR/74591 dated June 05, 2026 for NEAT Adapter Exe application details
- Default login password for mock session: `Neat@EG1`
- Members should review all five annexures for instructions on mock trading, login parameters, market data broadcast parameters, NEATPlus installation, and DR site switchover guidelines

## Important Dates

- **June 06, 2026**: Mock trading session date
  - Primary Site Pre-open: 09:45–09:53
  - Primary Site Normal Market: 10:00–13:00
  - Block Deal Window: 11:05–11:20
  - Auction Market: 11:30–12:15
  - DR Site Pre-open: 13:45–13:53
  - DR Site Normal Market: 14:00–14:30
  - Trade modification end: 14:40
  - Live Re-login: 16:00–16:30

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a scheduled operational drill for EGR segment members only. No trades executed during the mock session will result in financial obligations. Members need to ensure their NEAT Adapter is updated to v1.0.25 prior to the session. Connectivity to Extranet and the Member portal will be disrupted between 11:30 am and 1:00 pm during the mock session window.
