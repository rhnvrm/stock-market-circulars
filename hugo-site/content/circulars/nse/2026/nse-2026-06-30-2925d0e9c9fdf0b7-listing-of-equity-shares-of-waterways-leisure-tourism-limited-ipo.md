## Summary

NSE has informed its members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited on the Exchange pursuant to its IPO. The company will trade under the symbol **CORDELIA**. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited
- **Trading Symbol:** CORDELIA
- **ISIN:** INE0LZF01013
- **Exchange:** National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
- **Circular Reference:** NSE/CML/74931 | Circular Ref. No: 1043/2026
- Listing date to be notified via a subsequent circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational circular notifying members of a new security to be listed on the Exchange following a completed IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should take note of the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular specifying the exact date of listing before facilitating trading in the security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate NSE circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact limited to investors and members who participated in the IPO of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited. The stock will be available for trading on NSE under the symbol CORDELIA once the listing date is officially announced. No immediate trading activity is triggered by this circular.
