## Summary

National Stock Exchange has announced the launch of Groww BSE Hospitals ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from February 11, 2026 to February 25, 2026 till 11:59 PM. The fund offers both Direct Growth and Regular Growth options under the Equity category, with allotment scheduled for March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: February 11, 2026 to February 25, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Two scheme options available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF666M01OD9) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF666M01OD9)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity
- Allotment date: March 5, 2026
- Fund house: Groww Mutual Fund
- Clearing deadline: February 26, 2026 at 7:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 26, 2026 at 7:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: February 11, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: February 25, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Clearing Deadline**: February 26, 2026 (7:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The fund focuses on the healthcare/hospitals sector through BSE Hospitals index tracking. Members can offer this investment option to clients interested in sector-specific equity ETF exposure. The NFO is available through the NSE MF Invest Platform, providing access to both retail and institutional investors.
