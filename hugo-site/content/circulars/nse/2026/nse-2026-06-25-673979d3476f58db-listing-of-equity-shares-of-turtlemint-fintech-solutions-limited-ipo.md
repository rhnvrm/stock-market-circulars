## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has informed all exchange members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited via an IPO. The company will trade under the symbol **TURTLEMINT** with ISIN **INE0OC301013**. The exact listing date has not been specified in this circular and will be communicated through a separate notification.

## Key Points

- Company: Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited
- Exchange Symbol: TURTLEMINT
- ISIN: INE0OC301013
- Listing date to be announced via a separate circular
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Ref: NSE/CML/74882, Circular No. 1022/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard pre-listing notification informing exchange members of an upcoming IPO listing on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular confirming the exact listing date before executing trades in this security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 25, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited represents a new entrant in the fintech segment on NSE. Investors who participated in the IPO and market participants interested in the fintech sector should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. This announcement has high relevance for IPO allottees, trading members, and retail/institutional investors tracking new listings.
