## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department issued Circular 327/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/SURV/74055) on May 05, 2026, updating the list of securities subject to Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective May 06, 2026, no new securities are being added under any ASM IBC category, while Embassy Developments Limited (EMBDL) is being excluded from the ASM IBC framework.

## Key Points

- No new securities have been shortlisted under ASM IBC Stage I, Stage II, or any transition category with effect from May 06, 2026.
- Embassy Developments Limited (EMBDL, ISIN: INE069I01010) is removed from ASM IBC effective May 06, 2026.
- The ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM IBC is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.
- Five securities remain on the consolidated ASM IBC list under Stage I as of this circular.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and builds upon prior NSE circulars:
- NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018
- NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022
- NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023
- NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025

No structural changes to the ASM IBC framework itself have been introduced in this circular; it is a periodic securities list update.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must take note of the updated ASM IBC lists (Annexure I and Annexure II).
- Members must ensure trading in the listed securities complies with applicable ASM restrictions.
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 06, 2026 (changes to the ASM IBC securities list take effect)
