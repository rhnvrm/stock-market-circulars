## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective June 16, 2026. Beta Drugs Limited (BETA) is newly added to ST-ASM Stage I. Seven securities are excluded from the ASM framework, of which two — Kernex Microsystems and Yasho Industries — are moved to the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) framework. No securities are moved between Stage I and Stage II. A consolidated list of 29 securities remaining under the ST-ASM framework is also published.

## Key Points

- **1 security added to ST-ASM Stage I:** Beta Drugs Limited (BETA, INE351Y01019), effective June 16, 2026
- **No securities** added to or moved between ST-ASM Stage II
- **7 securities excluded** from ASM framework effective June 16, 2026
- 2 of the excluded securities (KERNEX, YASHO) are moved to the LTASM framework rather than being fully released
- Consolidated ST-ASM list contains 29 securities, all in Stage I

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework is a surveillance mechanism used by NSE to monitor and restrict trading in securities exhibiting abnormal price or volume movement. Securities placed under ASM are subject to additional margin requirements and may be moved to trade-for-trade settlement. This circular reflects the periodic review and rebalancing of the surveillance list.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable additional surveillance margins are collected from clients holding or transacting in ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II securities
- Members must update client-facing disclosures and risk parameters for BETA (newly added) effective June 16, 2026
- For securities removed from ASM (AVALON, GRWRHITECH, PANACEABIO, SOTL, STRIDERS), normal trading conditions and margin norms apply from June 16, 2026
- KERNEX and YASHO, though removed from ST-ASM, remain under LTASM surveillance — members must apply LTASM norms accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Effective date for all changes:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Newly added (Stage I):** Beta Drugs Limited (BETA) will be subject to ST-ASM Stage I restrictions including higher margin requirements, potentially reducing speculative activity and liquidity in the counter.

**Excluded from ASM:** Avalon Technologies, Garware Hi-Tech Films, Panacea Biotec, Savita Oil Technologies, and Striders Impex are fully released from surveillance restrictions, which may improve trading liquidity and lower margin burden for market participants in these securities. Kernex Microsystems and Yasho Industries move to the stricter LTASM framework, indicating continued regulatory scrutiny.

**Consolidated ST-ASM list:** 29 securities remain under Stage I surveillance. No securities are currently in Stage II, indicating no securities face the most restrictive tier of ST-ASM at this time.
