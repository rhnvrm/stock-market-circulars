## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in one Non-Convertible Security (privately placed) issued by NTPC Limited will be suspended effective July 6, 2026, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspension is on account of redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 1078/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75040
- Issued by NSE Listing Department, addressed to all members
- Security affected: NTPC Limited, ISIN INE733E07IU8
- Suspension date: July 6, 2026
- Reason for suspension: Redemption

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification issued under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A governing suspension of trading in listed non-convertible securities upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease trading in the specified ISIN (INE733E07IU8) from the suspension date. No further action is required from investors beyond awaiting redemption proceeds as per the security's terms.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 3, 2026
- Suspension effective date: July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific privately placed NCD ISIN of NTPC Limited in the debt market segment. This is a standard, low-impact procedural suspension tied to scheduled redemption and does not affect NTPC's equity shares or broader market operations.
