## Summary

NSE has announced adjustments to Futures and Options contracts on Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (symbol: HINDPETRO) pursuant to SEBI guidelines on corporate action adjustments. The adjustment is triggered by a dividend of Rs 19.25 per share (face value Rs 10) with an ex-date of August 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: HINDPETRO (Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited)
- Corporate action type: Dividend
- Dividend amount: Rs 19.25 per share on a face value of Rs 10
- Ex-date / Effective date: August 14, 2026
- Revised option strike prices will be provided one day prior to the ex-date
- Revised strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places and will be rounded to nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively

## Regulatory Changes

In accordance with SEBI guidelines for adjustments to futures and options contracts on corporate actions, NSE will revise option strike prices, futures base prices, lot sizes, and quantity freeze limits for HINDPETRO contracts effective from the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications before trading commences on the ex-date (August 14, 2026).
- Updated files are available from the `faoftp/faocommon` directory on the Extranet server and on the NSE website at the derivatives reports section.
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- **Ex-date / Effective date:** August 14, 2026
- **Revised strike price details available:** August 13, 2026 (one day prior to ex-date)

## Impact Assessment

Traders and members holding Futures and Options positions in HINDPETRO must ensure their trading systems are updated with the revised contract files before the ex-date. Failure to load updated files may result in incorrect pricing or settlement. The adjustment is a standard corporate action process and does not indicate any extraordinary market event; however, open positions in HINDPETRO derivatives will be subject to revised strike prices and contract parameters effective August 14, 2026.
