## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of 9,48,37,920 equity shares of ZF Commercial Vehicle Control Systems India Limited (Symbol: ZFCVINDIA) on the Capital Market segment, arising from a bonus issue. The shares will be available for trading effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9,48,37,920 new equity shares of ZFCVINDIA to be listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- ISIN: INE342J01019
- Distinctive number range: 18967585 to 113805504
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Security to be identified only by its designated code (Symbol: ZFCVINDIA) for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of securities to dealings on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security code (ZFCVINDIA) for trading the bonus shares on the system
- Members should update client records to reflect the increased share count post-bonus allotment

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 22, 2026
- **Record Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 25, 2026
- **Listing & Trading Effective Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of ZF Commercial Vehicle Control Systems India Limited (ZFCVINDIA) will receive bonus shares allotted as of June 25, 2026. The bonus shares will be tradeable on NSE from June 29, 2026. The stock price is expected to adjust proportionally on the ex-bonus date. The addition of approximately 9.48 crore shares will increase the free float and may improve liquidity in the counter.
