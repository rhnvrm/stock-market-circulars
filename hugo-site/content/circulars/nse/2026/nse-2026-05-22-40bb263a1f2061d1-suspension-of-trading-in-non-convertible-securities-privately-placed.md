## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two privately placed Non-Convertible Securities issued by NTPC Limited and Sammaan Capital Limited, effective May 25, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of these instruments under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two non-convertible securities (privately placed) will be suspended from trading effective May 25, 2026
- Suspension is due to redemption of the instruments
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74347 dated May 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of debt securities from trading upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed that trading in the following ISINs will not be permitted from May 25, 2026 onwards:

| Sr. No. | Company | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---------|---------|------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | NTPC Limited | INE733E07FO7 | May 25, 2026 | Redemption |
| 2 | Sammaan Capital Limited | INE148I07ES3 | May 25, 2026 | Redemption |

Members must ensure no trades are executed in these ISINs on or after the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 25, 2026 (for both securities)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The suspension of trading in these two privately placed NCDs is a standard procedure upon their redemption. Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
