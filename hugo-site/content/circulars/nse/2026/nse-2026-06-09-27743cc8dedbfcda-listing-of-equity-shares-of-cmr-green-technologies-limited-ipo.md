## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of CMR Green Technologies Limited on the Exchange. The company will trade under the symbol CMRGREEN with ISIN INE00WV01027. The specific listing date has not yet been announced and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: CMR Green Technologies Limited
- Trading Symbol: CMRGREEN
- ISIN: INE00WV01027
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
- Listing date to be announced via a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under NSE's listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular confirming the exact date of listing before facilitating trading in the security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 09, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of CMR Green Technologies Limited will introduce a new equity security to NSE. Market participants should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. Brokers and members should prepare their systems to handle trading in CMRGREEN once the listing date is confirmed. The IPO listing may attract investor interest in the green technologies sector.
