## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock bidding session on the eOFS (electronic Offer for Sale) platform in a live environment on March 07, 2026, from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The session uses test securities and does not create any fund or securities obligations for participants.

## Key Points

- Mock bidding session scheduled on the eOFS live platform on March 07, 2026
- Session timing: 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Live Web URL: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Two test securities available — one for Non-Retail Category (IS series) and one for Retail Category (RS series)
- Bids placed during the mock session will not result in any actual fund or securities obligations
- Members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock session
- Any new/revised announcements will be available on www.nseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational mock/test activity to familiarise members with the eOFS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged (not mandated) to actively participate in the mock bidding session
- No compliance obligations arise from participation or non-participation
- For queries, members may contact the Toll Free No: 1800 266 0050 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **March 07, 2026**: Mock bidding session on eOFS platform, 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational activity. The mock session is designed to let exchange members test and familiarise themselves with the eOFS bidding platform using dummy securities (TESTOFS1 and TESTOFS2, floor price Rs. 100, issue size 10,00,000 shares each). There is no real market, financial, or regulatory impact. Members dealing in OFS transactions may benefit from participating to ensure readiness for live OFS sessions.
