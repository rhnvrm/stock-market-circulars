## Summary

NSE has admitted further issues of securities for two SME EMERGE platform companies - Proventus Agrocom Limited and Transteel Seating Technologies Limited - to trading effective January 12, 2026. Proventus Agrocom allotted 6,725 equity shares under ESOP, while Transteel Seating allotted 170,500 equity shares pursuant to warrant conversion on preferential basis.

## Key Points

- **Proventus Agrocom Limited (PROV)**: 6,725 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on December 23, 2025
- **Transteel Seating Technologies Limited (TRANSTEEL)**: 170,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each from warrant conversion on December 2, 2025
- Trading commences on SME EMERGE platform from January 12, 2026
- Both issues are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes notified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Temporary ISIN allocation per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The additional shares represent small allotments for SME segment companies - an ESOP allotment for Proventus Agrocom and warrant conversion for Transteel Seating. The lock-in period on Transteel shares (expiring July 2026) will restrict immediate supply. Trading will be limited to SME EMERGE platform participants.
