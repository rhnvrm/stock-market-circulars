## Summary

NSE has issued an updated circular for the mock trading session scheduled on Saturday, July 25, 2026, which supersedes the earlier mock circular NSE/CMTR/75366 dated July 23, 2026. The mock session is being conducted to test trading modalities related to the upcoming introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, as previously outlined in circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026. The exchange will also introduce a new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) specifically for CAS-related testing during Trading Session-1.

## Key Points

- This circular supersedes NSE/CMTR/75366 issued on July 23, 2026, with updated mock session timings and details.
- The mock trading session will be held on Saturday, July 25, 2026, across three trading sessions (Trading Session-1, Trading Session-2 from Primary Site, and Trading Session-3 from DR Site).
- A new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) will be introduced for CAS-related testing, applicable only during Trading Session-1.
- After Trading Session-1, members must reinstall and log in with the earlier NEATPlus version (7.8.8) for Trading Sessions 2 and 3, which will run per existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes.
- Trading Session-1 includes a new CAS window with market order restrictions, along with standard pre-open, normal market, auction, and closing session timings.
- Trading Session-2 and Trading Session-3 follow the standard existing structure including block deal windows, pre-open sessions, T+0 market sessions, and closing sessions.
- Live re-login timing is specified from 16:15 to 17:00 hrs.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulatory requirements but provides updated operational/testing details ahead of the formal introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, which was originally announced via NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate in the mock trading session on July 25, 2026, per the specified schedule.
- Members must install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 for Trading Session-1 to test CAS-related functionality.
- Members must reinstall NEATPlus version 7.8.8 after Trading Session-1 to participate in Trading Sessions 2 and 3.
- Members should refer to the detailed session-wise timings provided in the circular for block deal windows, pre-open sessions, normal market hours, auction sessions, CAS timings, and closing sessions.

## Important Dates

- **July 23, 2026**: Original mock circular NSE/CMTR/75366 issued (now superseded).
- **May 29, 2026**: Circular NSE/CMTR/74466 on introduction of CAS in Equity Cash segment issued.
- **July 24, 2026**: This updated circular (NSE/CMTR/75401) issued.
- **July 25, 2026 (Saturday)**: Mock trading session date, including Trading Session-1 (CAS testing), Trading Session-2 (Primary Site), and Trading Session-3 (DR Site).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but important operational circular for NSE trading members, requiring participation in a mock session to test new trading infrastructure ahead of the CAS rollout in the Equity Cash segment. There is no direct impact on listed stocks or investors; the impact is confined to trading members who must ensure proper software version installation and adherence to the specified mock session schedule to ensure readiness for the eventual live implementation of CAS.
