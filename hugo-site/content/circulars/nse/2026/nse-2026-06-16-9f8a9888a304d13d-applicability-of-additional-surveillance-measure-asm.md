## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework announcing changes effective June 17, 2026. Seven securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) Framework Stage I, while four securities have been excluded from the ASM framework — all four being moved to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework.

## Key Points

- 7 securities added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I w.e.f. June 17, 2026
- 2 of the 7 newly added securities (BLUSPRING, NIBE) were moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to LTASM
- No securities moved between existing LTASM stages (Stage I→II, II→III, II→IV, III→IV, IV→III, III→II, II→I all show Nil)
- 4 securities excluded from ASM Framework, all transitioning to ESM framework
- Stage IV securities (if any) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade for Trade (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis

## Regulatory Changes

The ASM framework is a surveillance mechanism used by NSE to monitor securities exhibiting abnormal price and volume behaviour. Placement under LTASM Stage I subjects securities to enhanced scrutiny and typically involves higher margin requirements for trading. Securities moved from ASM to ESM face stricter trading restrictions under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure framework.

Stage IV securities under LTASM (none applicable in this circular) are subject to Trade for Trade settlement (Series: BE) shifted from Rolling Settlement on a T+3 basis from the date of circular issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and trading members must update margin requirements for securities newly placed under LTASM Stage I
- Clients holding or trading ADDICTIVE, APOLLO, BABAFP, BLUSPRING, LOTUSEYE, NIBE, and SHADOWFAX should be informed of the ASM placement
- KHADIM, NAVKARURB, SUPERSPIN, and SYSTMTXC will now be governed under the ESM framework — applicable trading restrictions under ESM apply from June 17, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 17, 2026 — all inclusions, exclusions, and stage movements take effect
- **Circular Issued:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of 7 securities in LTASM Stage I signals heightened regulatory concern about price volatility or trading irregularities in these stocks. Investors and traders in ADDICTIVE, APOLLO, BABAFP, BLUSPRING, LOTUSEYE, NIBE, and SHADOWFAX should expect increased margin requirements and additional scrutiny. The transition of KHADIM, NAVKARURB, SUPERSPIN, and SYSTMTXC from ASM to ESM indicates these securities are facing more severe surveillance conditions, with stricter trading restrictions under ESM. Market participants should review their positions and risk exposure in all 11 affected securities before June 17, 2026.
