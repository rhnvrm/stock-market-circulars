## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in one Non-Convertible Security (privately placed) issued by Godrej Industries Limited, effective May 27, 2026. The suspension is being enforced under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, in connection with the upcoming redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Godrej Industries Limited's NCS (ISIN: INE233A08071) will be suspended from trading effective May 27, 2026
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the security
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74402 | Ref. No: 0847/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Action taken under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of debt securities from trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure no trading occurs in ISIN INE233A08071 from May 27, 2026 onwards
- Existing holders of the security should prepare for redemption proceedings as per the terms of the instrument

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine suspension applicable to a single privately placed non-convertible security of Godrej Industries Limited ahead of its redemption. The impact is limited to holders of this specific instrument (ISIN: INE233A08071), who will no longer be able to trade it on NSE from May 27, 2026. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the security. Broader market impact is minimal.
