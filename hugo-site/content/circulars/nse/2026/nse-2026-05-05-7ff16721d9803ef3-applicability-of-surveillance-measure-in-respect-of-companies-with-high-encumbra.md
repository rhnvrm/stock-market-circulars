## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department (Circular Ref. No. 324/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/SURV/74052) issued on May 05, 2026, updates the list of securities under its high-encumbrance surveillance framework as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011. Three securities are added to the framework with a minimum 35% margin requirement, while nine securities are removed. A consolidated list of 10 securities currently under the framework is also provided.

## Key Points

- 3 securities added (Annexure I): BLKASHYAP, NRBBEARING, RTNPOWER – subject to minimum 35% margin in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments from May 08, 2026
- 35% margin applies to all open positions as on May 07, 2026, and all new positions created from May 08, 2026
- 9 securities excluded (Annexure II): AFCONS, COHANCE, EUREKAFORB, HINDZINC, MSPL, SAGCEM, THYROCARE, VERANDA, VIKRAMSOLR – exit effective May 06, 2026
- Consolidated active list (Annexure III) contains 10 securities: AAVAS, BLKASHYAP, GENUSPOWER, HEXT, NRBBEARING, PATELENG, RTNPOWER, SAGILITY, TRUALT, VENTIVE
- This measure operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures and is subject to periodic review
- Inclusion is purely a surveillance action and must not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular continues the framework established under NSE/SURV/42507 (October 24, 2019) and NSE/SURV/44169 (April 17, 2020). The update reflects a periodic review of promoter encumbrance disclosures filed under Reg. 28(3) of the SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, which requires promoters to disclose pledging or encumbrance of shares above specified thresholds.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure minimum 35% margin is collected/maintained for the three newly added securities (BLKASHYAP, NRBBEARING, RTNPOWER) in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments from May 08, 2026
- Margin applies to both open positions held as of end-of-day May 07, 2026, and new positions initiated from May 08, 2026
- Members may accept company representations regarding inclusion; the circular notes a process exists for such representations
- This surveillance measure is additive – it does not replace other applicable surveillance margins

## Important Dates

- **May 05, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **May 06, 2026**: Effective date for exclusion of 9 securities (Annexure II)
- **May 07, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to the new 35% margin
- **May 08, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of 3 new securities (Annexure I) with 35% minimum margin
