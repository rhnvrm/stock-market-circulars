## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Angel One Silver ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from February 09, 2026 to February 19, 2026. Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plans are available under the Debt category with ISIN INF1J2R01171. The allotment date is scheduled for February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: February 09, 2026 to February 19, 2026
- Two plan options available: Direct Growth and Regular Growth
- Both plans share the same ISIN: INF1J2R01171
- Category: Debt
- Allotment Date: February 26, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by February 26, 2026 at 11:59 PM
- Support available via toll-free number 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 26, 2026 11:59 PM
- Standard NFO transaction and settlement procedures to be followed

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date:** February 09, 2026
- **NFO End Date:** February 19, 2026
- **Allotment Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Fund Settlement Deadline:** February 26, 2026 at 11:59 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product launch with minimal market impact. The circular is informational in nature, enabling NSE members to facilitate client transactions in the Angel One Silver ETF during the NFO period. The launch expands the commodity ETF offerings available on the NSE MF Invest Platform, providing investors with another silver-focused investment option. No operational changes or compliance burden beyond standard NFO settlement procedures.
