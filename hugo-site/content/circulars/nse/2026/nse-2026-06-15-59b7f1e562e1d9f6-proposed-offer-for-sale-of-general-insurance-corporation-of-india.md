## Summary

NSE has notified all trading members of the Capital Market segment about a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of equity shares of General Insurance Corporation of India (Symbol: GICRE, ISIN: INE481Y01014) scheduled on June 16, 2026, and June 17, 2026. The OFS will be conducted via the NSE eOFS platform in accordance with the Interoperability among Clearing Corporations framework under circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- OFS bidding sessions are scheduled for June 16, 2026, and June 17, 2026.
- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members may participate via the eOFS platform at https://eofs.nseindia.com.
- Three participation categories: IS (non-retail), RS (Retail — bid value ≤ Rs 2 lacs), and ES (Employee — bid value ≤ Rs 5 lacs).
- Issue size: up to 35,088,000 equity shares (Base Offer), up to 52,632,000 equity shares (Oversubscription Option), and 20,000 equity shares (Eligible Employee Option); face value Rs. 5/- each.
- Members without Admin user IDs must register via ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS.
- ENIT registration requests are processed at 11:00 am, 2:00 pm, and 3:00 pm; requests after 3:00 pm are processed the next day by 8:45 am.
- Only one Admin User creation request must be raised; requests cannot be modified once submitted.
- UCCs identified as compliant by 22:00 hrs the previous day are permitted to trade in the next bidding session.
- Members must ensure sufficient collaterals before carrying forward orders to T+1 day; rejected orders due to margin insufficiency cannot be re-placed.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The OFS is being conducted under the existing interoperability framework (NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026) and member limits guidelines (NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020, Annexure 3).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all participating client UCCs are updated and marked compliant in the Unique Client Identification (UCI) system at least one day prior to the OFS bidding session.
- UCCs updated or made compliant on the day of the bidding session will not be eligible to participate.
- Members must verify margin/collateral sufficiency before initiating carry-forward of orders on T+1.
- Members yet to obtain Admin user IDs must complete ENIT portal registration before the bidding session.
- Members should review Annexure 3 of NSE/CMTR/43529 (February 13, 2020) for feature and limits configuration for their users.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Circular issued; deadline to ensure UCC compliance updated by 22:00 hrs for June 16 session.
- **June 16, 2026**: Day 1 of OFS bidding session.
- **June 17, 2026**: Day 2 of OFS bidding session.
- **On or before 5:00 pm on Day 1**: Seller to intimate exchange on exercise of oversubscription option.

## Impact Assessment

This OFS involves a significant volume of shares in General Insurance Corporation of India, a major public sector general insurer. The combined oversubscription size of up to 52.6 million shares makes this a high-visibility event. Trading members face operational deadlines around UCC compliance, margin sufficiency, and eOFS platform access that must be met before June 16, 2026. Members who have not yet set up eOFS Admin user IDs must act immediately given the same-day processing cutoff of 3:00 pm. Retail and employee categories have separate bid-value caps, requiring members to route orders through appropriate series (RS/IS/ES).
