## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Vinit Mobile Limited under its SME IPO on the EMERGE SME Platform of the Capital Market segment, effective July 07, 2026. The shares will trade under the symbol VMOBILE, initially in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series 'ST'), before shifting to the normal rolling segment (Series 'SM').

## Key Points

- Company: Vinit Mobile Limited, listed on NSE EMERGE SME Platform
- Symbol: VMOBILE
- ISIN: INE27V201011
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share; Issue Price: Rs. 158/- per share
- Total securities admitted: 6,170,000 equity shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 6,170,000)
- Market lot: Minimum 800 equity shares
- Market Maker: Comfort Securities Limited, with permissible spread within 10%
- Initial trading series: 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, Settlement Type 'W'), later shifting to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment, Settlement Type 'N')
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Bigshare Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

This is a listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. No new regulatory framework is introduced; the circular applies existing SEBI pre-open session norms (per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012) and continuous market trading rules (per NSE Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018) to this new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize and trade the security only under its designated symbol (VMOBILE) and specified lot size (800 shares).
- The security will initially be subject to Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) settlement (Type 'W') before transitioning to normal rolling settlement (Type 'N').
- Members must ensure the security participates in the pre-open session applicable to IPO and other newly listed scrips.
- Lock-in restrictions on certain shares apply as detailed in Annexure I of the circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 06, 2026
- Listing/Effective Date: July 07, 2026
- Financial Year of the company: April 2026 - March 2027

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME listing. It is directly relevant to trading members, market makers, and investors dealing in Vinit Mobile Limited shares, who must adhere to the specified trading series, lot size, and settlement type from the effective date. Broader market operations remain unaffected.
