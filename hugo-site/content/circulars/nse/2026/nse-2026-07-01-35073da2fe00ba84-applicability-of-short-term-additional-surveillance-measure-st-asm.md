## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has issued a circular identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM). Five securities have been shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I. No securities were shortlisted for Stage II, and no securities were moved between stages or excluded from the ASM framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Five securities identified for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I: Chandan Healthcare Limited (CHANDAN), GNG Electronics Limited (EBGNG), Readymix Construction Machinery Limited (READYMIX), Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited (TICL), and Tirupati Forge Limited (TIRUPATIFL).
- No securities were added to ST-ASM Stage II in this cycle.
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or vice versa.
- No securities were excluded from the ASM framework in this update.
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing ST-ASM framework established by earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, NSE/SURV/64066). It applies the framework's Stage I margin criteria to the newly identified securities; no changes to the framework's rules or thresholds are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must apply the mandated margin rate on all open and new positions in the identified Stage I securities as specified below, and factor this into client-facing margin communications and risk management systems.

## Important Dates

- **July 02, 2026**: Securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I as of this date; margin applies to all open positions as on this date.
- **July 03, 2026**: Applicable margin rate of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) becomes effective for all open positions as of July 02, 2026, and new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirement (50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) will raise the cost of trading and reduce leverage for the five identified securities, likely dampening trading volumes and volatility in these names. The impact is limited to these specific securities and their traders/investors rather than the broader market. No Stage II inclusions or exclusions were made, indicating no securities faced escalated restrictions or relief in this cycle.
