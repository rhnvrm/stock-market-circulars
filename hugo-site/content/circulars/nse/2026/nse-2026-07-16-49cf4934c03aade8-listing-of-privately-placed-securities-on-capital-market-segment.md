## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of four privately placed non-convertible debt securities on the Capital Market segment, effective July 17, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The circular specifies the designated security codes and lot sizes for trading under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Four fresh NCD issues are being admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment.
- Nomura Fixed Income Securities Limited: symbol 820NOM28, series N0, coupon 8.20%, maturing 11-Aug-2028, ISIN INE127K08025.
- Akme Fintrade (India) Limited: symbol 115AFIL28A, series N0, coupon 11.50%, maturing 14-Jul-2028, ISIN INE916Y07099.
- Globe Capital Market Limited: symbol 938GCML28, series N0, coupon 9.38%, maturing 14-Sep-2028, ISIN INEO81J07036.
- Globe Fincap Limited: symbol 938GFL29, series N0, coupon 9.38%, maturing 14-Mar-2029, ISIN INE013U07010.
- All four securities have a market lot of 1 and will be identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure trading in these securities uses only the specified designated codes and adheres to the notified lot sizes (1 unit each) once trading commences.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 16, 2026
- Effective date for listing and trading: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification affecting a limited set of privately placed NCD instruments and their holders/traders. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
