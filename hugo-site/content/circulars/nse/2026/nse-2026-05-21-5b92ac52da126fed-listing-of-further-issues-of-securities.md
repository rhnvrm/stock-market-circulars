## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No. 0824/2026) notifies members that further equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) by 14 companies are admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment with effect from May 22, 2026. Trading will use the designated security codes and lot sizes specified in the Annexure.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have additional ESOP-allotted equity shares admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies
- Market lot for all securities is 1 share
- No lock-in applicable for any of the listed ESOP tranches
- Effective date: May 22, 2026
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance obligations for members. This is an informational notice. Members should update their systems to recognise the newly admitted shares under the existing security codes and ISINs of each company.
