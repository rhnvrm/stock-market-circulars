## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited under its Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund scheme. Six unit classes covering Regular and Direct plans across Growth, Reinvestment, and Payout of Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six unit series listed under Mirae Asset Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund: PHLSRG, PHLSRDI, PHLSRDP, PHLSDG, PHLSDDI, PHLSDDP
- All units carry a face value and issue price of Rs. 10/- each
- Date of allotment: June 10, 2026
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISINs assigned: INF769K30019 (PHLSRG), INF769K30027 (PHLSRDI), INF769K30035 (PHLSRDP), INF769K30043 (PHLSDG), INF769K30050 (PHLSDDI), INF769K30068 (PHLSDDP)
- Trustee: Mirae Asset Trustee Company Private Limited, Mumbai
- AMC: Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, which govern the admission of securities and specification of lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must identify these securities solely by their designated codes (PHLSRG, PHLSRDI, PHLSRDP, PHLSDG, PHLSDDI, PHLSDDP) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: June 10, 2026
- Effective date for trading on NSE: June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with limited market impact. It enables retail and institutional investors to trade Mirae Asset Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund units on the NSE secondary market across all six plan-option combinations. The hybrid long-short fund structure may attract investors seeking a hedged equity-debt product. Market lot of 1 unit ensures accessibility for all investor categories.
