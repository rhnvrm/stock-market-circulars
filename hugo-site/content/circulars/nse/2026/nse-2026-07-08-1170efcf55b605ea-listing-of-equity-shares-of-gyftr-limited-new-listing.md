## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Gyftr Limited under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 10, 2026, under the symbol GYFTR, series EQ, with a designated lot size as specified in the circular's annexure.

## Key Points

- Company: Gyftr Limited (formerly associated with LKP Finance, per website references)
- Symbol: GYFTR, Series: EQ, ISIN: INE724A01017
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share; fully paid up
- Total securities admitted: 7,68,08,250 equity shares
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 7,81,37,716 (13,29,466 shares cancelled pursuant to buy-back are included in this range)
- Market lot: 1
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Lock-in: Not applicable
- Trading will be in Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement), compulsory demat for all investors
- Registrar: Adroit Corporate Services Private Limited, Mumbai
- Company Secretary: Mr. Rishi Arya

## Regulatory Changes

This is a listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. No broader regulatory policy change is involved; it is company-specific.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize GYFTR as a newly admitted security from the effective date and ensure trading occurs only in the specified lot size (1) and in compulsory demat form. No other member action is required beyond system updates to reflect the new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 08, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables trading in Gyftr Limited shares on NSE's Capital Market segment, providing liquidity and price discovery for the stock. Impact is confined to GYFTR and its shareholders/investors; no systemic market impact is expected. This is a routine new listing notification of moderate relevance to market participants tracking newly listed securities.
