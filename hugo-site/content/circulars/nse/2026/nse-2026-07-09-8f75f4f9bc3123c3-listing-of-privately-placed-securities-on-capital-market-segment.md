## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Jio Credit Limited and L&T Finance Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 10, 2026. The circular specifies the designated security codes and lot sizes for trading these instruments under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two privately placed NCD issues admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Jio Credit Limited: Symbol 778JCL29, Series N0, Market Lot 1, Coupon 7.78%, Maturity 07-Jul-2029, ISIN INE282H07075 (JCL 7.78% 2029 Sr VII)
- L&T Finance Limited: Symbol 783LTFL29, Series N0, Market Lot 1, Coupon 7.8384%, Maturity 28-Sep-2029, ISIN INE498L07228 (LTFL 7.8384% 2029 Sr D)
- Securities to be identified on the trading system solely by designated security codes and traded in the specified lot sizes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative listing notification issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the designated security codes and market lot of 1 for trading these NCDs from the effective date; no other compliance action required.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 09, 2026
- Effective date of listing: July 10, 2026
- Maturity of Jio Credit Limited NCD: July 07, 2029
- Maturity of L&T Finance Limited NCD: September 28, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational notification enabling secondary market trading of two privately placed debt instruments. It affects debt market participants and institutional investors holding these NCDs but has no bearing on equity share prices or broader market operations.
