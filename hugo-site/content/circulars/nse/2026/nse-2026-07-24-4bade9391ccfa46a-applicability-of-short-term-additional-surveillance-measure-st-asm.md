## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion in ST-ASM Stage I and separately listing securities to be excluded from the ASM framework altogether, effective July 27-28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Gandhar Oil Refinery (India) Limited (GANDHAR), Sungarner Energies Limited (SEL) and TechNVision Ventures Limited (TECHNVISN) have been shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I.
- No securities have been shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage II, or for movement between Stage I and Stage II, in this cycle.
- Fermenta Biotech Limited (FERMENTA), Hardwyn India Limited (HARDWYN), Vraj Iron and Steel Limited (VRAJ) and at least one further security (list truncated in source, beginning with "WANB") are being excluded from the ASM framework.
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Shortlisting under ASM is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing ASM framework established via NSE circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558 and NSE/SURV/64066 (dated October 27, 2018, December 4, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023 and September 20, 2024 respectively). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of securities under ST-ASM.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%, on all open positions in GANDHAR, SEL and TECHNVISN as on July 27, 2026, and on new positions created from July 28, 2026.
- Members should update systems to reflect the revised price band for securities exiting the framework, reinstating the price band applicable before ASM shortlisting, unless the scrip remains subject to another surveillance measure.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: Circular issued (Ref. No. 558/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/SURV/75395).
- July 27, 2026: Effective date for exclusions from the ASM framework and for open positions subject to the enhanced margin in Stage I securities.
- July 28, 2026: Enhanced margin requirement (50% or higher, capped at 100%) becomes applicable to new positions in the newly included Stage I securities.

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of GANDHAR, SEL and TECHNVISN under ST-ASM Stage I raises the cost of carrying and creating positions in these stocks due to the elevated margin requirement, which typically reduces speculative trading volumes and can pressure short-term price movement. Conversely, the exclusion of FERMENTA, HARDWYN, VRAJ and other listed securities removes the additional margin burden and associated trading constraints, potentially improving liquidity and price band flexibility for these stocks. The overall market impact is limited to the specific securities named, with no broad-based effect on the wider market.
