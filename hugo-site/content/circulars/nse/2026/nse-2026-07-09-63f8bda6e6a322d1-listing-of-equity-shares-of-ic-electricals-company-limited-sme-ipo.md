## Summary

IC Electricals Company Limited has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern (Annexure-I, Regulation 31(1)(a)) in connection with the listing of its equity shares under the SME IPO segment. The disclosure covers the position as on 08 July 2026, following completion of the initial public offering.

## Key Points

- Total post-issue paid-up equity capital stands at 1,82,61,600 shares held by 1,083 shareholders.
- Promoter & Promoter Group hold 1,11,29,100 shares, representing 60.94% of total equity.
- Public shareholders hold 71,32,500 shares, representing 39.06% of total equity.
- Of the public shareholding, 36,60,900 shares (51.33%) are locked-in.
- The company has no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, or differential voting rights.
- No promoter shares are pledged, subject to non-disposal undertaking, or otherwise encumbered.
- The company has no significant beneficial owner reported.
- 100% of promoter shareholding is held in dematerialized form.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine post-issue disclosure required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, Regulation 31(1)(a), filed in the prescribed shareholding pattern format ahead of listing.

## Compliance Requirements

IC Electricals Company Limited was required to submit the shareholding pattern in the standard format to the exchange as part of the listing process for its SME IPO, disclosing category-wise holding, locked-in shares, and encumbrance status of promoter holdings.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern reporting date: 08 July 2026 (Post-Issue)

## Impact Assessment

This filing formalizes the shareholder base of IC Electricals Company Limited ahead of the listing of its equity shares on the SME platform. The disclosure confirms a concentrated promoter holding (60.94%) with a broad public shareholder base of 1,073 investors, and no encumbrances, warrants, or convertible instruments that could dilute existing shareholders. The information is primarily relevant to investors participating in or tracking the SME IPO listing rather than to the broader market.
