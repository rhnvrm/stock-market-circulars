## Summary

NSE has disclosed the post-issue shareholding pattern of CSM Technologies Limited as of 01.07.2026, filed under Regulation 31(1)(a), following the listing of its equity shares pursuant to its IPO. The disclosure covers the Format of Holding of Specified Securities for the equity shares class.

## Key Points

- Category: Promoter & Promoter Group holds 2 shareholders with 36,730,332 fully paid-up equity shares, representing 71.18% of total shares.
- Category: Public holds 40,465 shareholders with 14,873,140 fully paid-up equity shares, representing 28.82% of total shares.
- Voting rights mirror the shareholding percentages for both categories (71.18% and 28.82% respectively).
- The company has not issued any partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts.
- Promoter shares are not encumbered under pledge or non-disposal undertaking.
- The company does not have differential voting rights shares or a significant beneficial owner declared.
- Some shares are held in locked-in status (flagged "YES" in the declaration), with 36,730,332 promoter shares (100.00%) and 3,742,260 public shares (25.16%) shown as locked-in.
- All disclosed promoter and public shares are held in dematerialized form.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine post-listing shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI (LODR) Regulation 31(1)(a).

## Compliance Requirements

CSM Technologies Limited, as a newly listed entity, is required to submit the Format of Holding of Specified Securities to the stock exchange in the prescribed tabular format, including details of promoter and public shareholding, encumbrances, and lock-in status.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: 01 July 2026 (post-issue, following IPO listing).

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard regulatory disclosure accompanying a new listing and carries limited direct market impact beyond providing transparency on ownership structure. It is relevant to prospective and existing investors in CSM Technologies Limited for assessing promoter commitment (71.18% holding, fully locked-in) and free float availability (public float of 28.82%, partly locked-in).
