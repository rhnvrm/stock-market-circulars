## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Choice Overnight Fund, a new fund offer (NFO) from Choice Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 1, 2026 to July 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular lists the four scheme/plan variants along with their ISINs and specifies the allotment date and fund pay-in deadline.

## Key Points

- Choice Mutual Fund is launching the Choice Overnight Fund, an Overnight category scheme, via the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Four scheme variants are available for subscription:
  - Choice Overnight Fund - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF2KCX01087)
  - Choice Overnight Fund - Direct Plan - Daily IDCW (ISIN: INF2KCX01061)
  - Choice Overnight Fund - Regular Growth (ISIN: INF2KCX01095)
  - Choice Overnight Fund - Regular Plan - Daily IDCW (ISIN: INF2KCX01079)
- All four variants have an allotment date of July 4, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds (via any payment mode) are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for transaction on the platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members transacting on behalf of clients must ensure subscription orders for the NFO are placed within the offer window (July 1 - July 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm).
- Members must ensure clear funds reach the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm to ensure successful allotment.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 1, 2026 to July 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Fund pay-in deadline: July 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm
- Allotment date: July 4, 2026 (all four scheme variants)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low direct market impact as it pertains to the operational onboarding of a new mutual fund scheme onto NSE's MF distribution infrastructure. It is primarily relevant to NSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions and to investors considering the Choice Overnight Fund NFO. There is no impact on listed equity securities or trading in the cash/derivatives market.
