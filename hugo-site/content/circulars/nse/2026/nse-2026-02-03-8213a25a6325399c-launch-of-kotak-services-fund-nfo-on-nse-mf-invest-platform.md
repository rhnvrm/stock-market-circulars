## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Kotak Services Fund NFO (New Fund Offer) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from February 04, 2026 to February 18, 2026, with the allotment date scheduled for February 25, 2026. The fund offers six plan variants covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: February 04, 2026 to February 18, 2026
- Allotment date: February 25, 2026
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with multiple IDCW options)
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Clear funds must be received by February 23, 2026 at 06:00 PM
- Funds to be deposited in designated Clearing Corporation account

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account
- Payment deadline: February 23, 2026 at 06:00 PM
- Members should facilitate client transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: February 04, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Payment Deadline**: February 23, 2026 (06:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new equity fund option focused on the services sector through Kotak Mutual Fund. The launch expands investment choices on the NSE MF Invest Platform but does not affect existing market operations or trading activities. Members need to be prepared to facilitate client subscriptions during the NFO period and ensure timely fund settlements.
