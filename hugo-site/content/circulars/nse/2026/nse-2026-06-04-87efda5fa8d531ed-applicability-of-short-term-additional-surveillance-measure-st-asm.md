## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has issued Circular No. 416/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74548) dated June 04, 2026, announcing the inclusion of 6 securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I framework, effective June 05, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 08, 2026. No securities have been shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage II, and there are no movements between stages.

## Key Points

- 6 securities added to ST-ASM Stage I effective June 05, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on June 05, 2026 and new positions from June 08, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage II list: Nil (no securities added)
- Stage I to Stage II movement: Nil
- Stage II to Stage I movement: Nil
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned companies

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ST-ASM framework established by earlier circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). The inclusion of securities triggers mandatory margin enhancements as per the ST-ASM framework provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply enhanced margin rates for ST-ASM Stage I securities
- **ST-ASM Stage I**: Collect margin at 50% or the existing applicable margin rate, whichever is higher (subject to a maximum cap of 100%)
- Enhanced margins must be collected on all open positions as on June 05, 2026
- Enhanced margins must be collected on all new positions created from June 08, 2026 onwards
- Members should refer to the ASM FAQs on the NSE website for detailed framework information
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Impact Assessment

**Securities under ST-ASM Stage I (effective June 05, 2026):**

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | EMKAY | Emkay Global Financial Services Limited | INE296H01011 |
| 2 | GULFPETRO | GP Petroleums Limited | INE586G01017 |
| 3 | IZMO | IZMO Limited | INE848A01014 |
| 4 | JAYBARMARU | Jay Bharat Maruti Limited | INE571B01036 |
| 5 | KPL | Kwality Pharmaceuticals Limited | INE552U01010 |
| 6 | THANGAMAYL | Thangamayil Jewellery Limited | INE085J01014 |

Traders and investors holding positions in these 6 securities will face significantly higher margin requirements from June 08, 2026, which may necessitate additional capital infusion or position reduction. The mandatory 50% minimum margin (compared to standard margins which may be lower) increases the cost of holding or creating new positions in these securities, potentially reducing liquidity and trading volumes. This surveillance action signals that these securities have exhibited abnormal trading patterns warranting regulatory oversight.
