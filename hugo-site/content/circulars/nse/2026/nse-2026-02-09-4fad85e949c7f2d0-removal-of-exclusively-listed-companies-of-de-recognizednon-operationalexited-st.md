## Summary

NSE has announced the removal of Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited (formerly Montage Housing Finance Limited) from the Dissemination Board effective February 11, 2026. The company is being removed because it has secured listing on another stock exchange with nationwide trading terminals, and therefore no longer qualifies as an exclusively listed company of de-recognized, non-operational, or exited stock exchanges.

## Key Points

- Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited (erstwhile Montage Housing Finance Limited) will be removed from NSE's Dissemination Board
- Removal reason: Company is now listed on another stock exchange having nationwide trading terminals
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI circulars dated October 10, 2016, August 01, 2017, and October 16, 2023
- The company will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action is in compliance with existing SEBI circulars:
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2017/92 dated August 01, 2017
- Chapter 6 of Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD2/PoD-2/CIR/P/2023/171 dated October 16, 2023

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members. This is an informational circular regarding the administrative removal of a company from the Dissemination Board.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 09, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 11, 2026 - Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited will no longer be available on NSE's Dissemination Board

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single company that was previously on the Dissemination Board for exclusively listed companies of de-recognized or non-operational stock exchanges. The removal indicates positive development for the company as it has secured listing on another recognized exchange with nationwide trading terminals. No trading disruptions or member actions are required.
