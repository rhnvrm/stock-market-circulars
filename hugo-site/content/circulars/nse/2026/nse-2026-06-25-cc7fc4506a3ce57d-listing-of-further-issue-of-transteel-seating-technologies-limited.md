## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 3,48,000 equity shares of Transteel Seating Technologies Limited (Symbol: TRANSTEEL) on the SME Emerge segment (Series: SM), effective June 29, 2026. The shares were allotted under a preferential issue at an issue price of Rs. 80 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.

## Key Points

- 3,48,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under preferential issue at Rs. 80 per share
- Shares will be listed on NSE SME Emerge (Series: SM) with effect from June 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE0NVI01020 (temporary ISIN IN8********* assigned pending activation per SEBI circular)
- Distinctive number range: 22092011 to 22440010
- Market lot size: 1,000 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period applies until December 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol TRANSTEEL and series SM for all trades
- All trades must be executed in the specified market lot size of 1,000 shares
- The temporary ISIN (IN8*********) is in use pending activation of the permanent ISIN (INE0NVI01020) per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 13, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME Emerge listing with limited market impact. The preferential allotment of 3,48,000 shares is a relatively small issue for a SME-segment company. The lock-in period extending to December 31, 2026 restricts tradability of these shares until expiry, limiting near-term supply pressure. Market participants trading TRANSTEEL should note the updated total share count and the applicable lot size of 1,000 shares.
