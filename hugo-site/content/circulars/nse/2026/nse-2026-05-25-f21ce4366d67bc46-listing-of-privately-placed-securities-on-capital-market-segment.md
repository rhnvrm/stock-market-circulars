## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities of Viviana Power Tech Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective May 26, 2026. The security is a 12% Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) maturing in May 2028, admitted under symbol 12VPT28 in Series N0.

## Key Points

- Viviana Power Tech Limited's NCD (ISIN: INE0MEG07029) admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Symbol: 12VPT28, Series: N0, Market Lot: 1
- Coupon rate: 12% per annum
- Maturity date: May 21, 2028
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of the same regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code 12VPT28 for trading identification
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- All trades in this security must adhere to NSE Capital Market segment rules

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 25, 2026
- Effective Date: May 26, 2026
- Maturity Date: May 21, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing notification with limited market impact. The admission of this privately placed 12% NCD of Viviana Power Tech Limited provides investors with a tradeable debt instrument on NSE's capital market segment. The market lot of 1 allows flexible trading. Impact is confined to participants interested in this specific debt security.
