## Summary

BCPL Railway Infrastructure Limited (Symbol: BCPL, ISIN: INE00SW01015) has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange of India Capital Market segment effective March 27, 2026. The company is listed under NSE Circular Ref. No. 0500/2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** BCPL
- **Company:** BCPL Railway Infrastructure Limited
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE00SW01015
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10 per share
- **Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10 per share
- **Total Securities:** 16,723,638 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 16,723,638
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- Trading is in Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement) in compulsory demat for all investors

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies the listing under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in BCPL shares must use the designated symbol and security codes
- Trading must occur in the specified lot size of 1 share
- All investors must hold and trade in compulsory demat form

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 24, 2026
- **Effective Date / Trading Commencement:** March 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a new listing event for BCPL Railway Infrastructure Limited, a Kolkata-based company (CIN: L51109WB1995PLC075801) in the railway infrastructure sector. With 16.72 million shares at Rs. 10 face value, the listing provides a new investment opportunity in the railway/infrastructure space. The compulsory demat requirement and market lot of 1 share ensures retail investor accessibility. The registrar is Maheshwari Datamatics Private Limited, Kolkata.
