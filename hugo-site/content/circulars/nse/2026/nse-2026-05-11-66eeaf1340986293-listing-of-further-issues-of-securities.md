## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for seven companies effective May 12, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. All securities were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP), carry no lock-in, and rank pari passu with existing equity shares. Lot size for all securities is 1.

## Key Points

- Seven companies admitted to further dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from May 12, 2026
- All allotments are under ESOP schemes with no lock-in applicable
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and have a market lot of 1
- Angel One Limited: 1,139,549 equity shares of Re. 1 each (ISIN: INE732I01021), allotted Apr 30, 2026
- Godrej Agrovet Limited: 30,973 equity shares of Rs. 10 each (ISIN: INE850D01014), allotted Apr 30, 2026
- ICICI Bank Limited: 66,061 equity shares of Rs. 2 each (ISIN: INE090A01021), allotted May 8, 2026
- IndusInd Bank Limited: 4,900 equity shares of Rs. 10 each (ISIN: INE095A01012), allotted Apr 30, 2026
- Mahindra EPC Irrigation Limited: 8,284 equity shares of Rs. 10 each (ISIN: INE215D01010), allotted Apr 21, 2026
- Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited: 10,700 equity shares of Rs. 10 each (ISIN: INE073K01018), allotted Apr 30, 2026
- Wipro Limited: details partially available in source document

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes and prescribed lot sizes for all transactions in these securities
- No additional compliance obligations for members beyond standard trading rules

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 11, 2026
- Effective Date: May 12, 2026 (all seven securities admitted to trading)
- ESOP Allotment Dates: April 21, 2026 (Mahindra EPC); April 30, 2026 (Angel One, Godrej Agrovet, IndusInd Bank, Sona BLW); May 8, 2026 (ICICI Bank)

## Impact Assessment

Market impact is minimal. These are routine ESOP-related further listings representing small additions to each company's existing share capital. No lock-in periods apply, so shares are immediately tradeable, but volumes are unlikely to cause notable price movements given the relatively small quantities involved. Members should update their systems with the new distinctive number ranges and confirm security codes before May 12, 2026 trading commencement.
