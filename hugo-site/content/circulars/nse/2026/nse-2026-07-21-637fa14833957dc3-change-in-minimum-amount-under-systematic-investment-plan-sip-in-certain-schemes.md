## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Bank of India Mutual Fund has revised the minimum instalment amount under its Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for both Monthly and Weekly frequencies, effective July 21, 2026, on the NSE MF Invest platform. The minimum SIP amount has been reduced from Rs. 1,000/- (and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter) to Rs. 250/- (and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter). This change follows a Notice-cum-Addendum (No. 19/2026-27) issued by Bank of India Investment Managers Private Limited on July 17, 2026, which also revised the list of schemes eligible for the "Choti SIP" facility.

## Key Points

- Revised minimum SIP amount applies to Monthly and Weekly SIP frequencies.
- The change applies to three specific schemes: Bank of India Balanced Advantage Fund, Bank of India Multi Cap Fund, and Bank of India Multi Asset Allocation Fund.
- Choti SIP facility, earlier available across most equity schemes (excluding Debt, Sectoral & Thematic, Small Cap, Mid Cap, and ELSS schemes), will now be continued only in the above three schemes and discontinued for other applicable schemes.
- The AMC reserves the right to modify the list of schemes eligible for the Choti SIP facility from time to time.
- The revised minimum SIP amount applies to prospective investors going forward.

## Regulatory Changes

Minimum SIP instalment amount reduced from Rs. 1,000/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter, to Rs. 250/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter, for both Monthly and Weekly SIP frequencies in the specified Bank of India Mutual Fund schemes. This aligns with SEBI guidelines on the Choti SIP facility introduced via an earlier addendum (No. 09/2025-26 dated July 8, 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members dealing in mutual fund units via the NSE MF Invest platform must update their systems to reflect the revised minimum SIP amounts for the affected Bank of India Mutual Fund schemes and ensure investors are informed of the applicable Choti SIP facility scheme list.

## Important Dates

- July 8, 2025: Original Choti SIP facility introduced via Notice-cum-Addendum No. 09/2025-26.
- July 17, 2026: Notice-cum-Addendum No. 19/2026-27 issued revising applicable schemes and minimum SIP amounts.
- July 21, 2026: Revised minimum SIP instalment amount effective on NSE MF Invest platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational update primarily relevant to mutual fund distributors and investors using the NSE MF Invest platform. Lowering the minimum SIP amount to Rs. 250/- is intended to broaden retail participation in the specified Bank of India Mutual Fund schemes by making systematic investing more accessible. There is no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader market trading operations.
