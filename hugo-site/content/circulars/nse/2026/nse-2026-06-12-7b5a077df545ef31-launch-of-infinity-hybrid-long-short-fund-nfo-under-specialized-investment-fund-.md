## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund NFO under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category of Kotak Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 15, 2026 to June 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The fund offers six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans. Allotment is scheduled for July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- The NFO is categorized as a Debt fund under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework of Kotak Mutual Fund.
- Six scheme variants are available: Regular Plan Growth, Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment, Regular Plan IDCW Payout, Direct Plan Growth, Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment, and Direct Plan IDCW Payout.
- ISINs assigned: INF174K30046 (Regular-Growth), INF174K30061 (Regular-IDCW Reinvestment), INF174K30053 (Regular-IDCW Payout), INF174K30012 (Direct-Growth), INF174K30038 (Direct-IDCW Reinvestment), INF174K30020 (Direct-IDCW Payout).
- Clear funds via any payment mode must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 3, 2026 till 12:00 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the SIF framework by enabling a specific Kotak Mutual Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members transacting in this NFO must ensure that clear funds are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before **July 3, 2026 till 12:00 pm**.
- Members should process subscriptions only within the NFO window: **June 15, 2026 to June 29, 2026 till 11:59 pm**.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard NFO availability notification for NSE MF Invest Platform members. It introduces a new SIF-category hybrid long-short debt fund by Kotak Mutual Fund, expanding investment options for eligible investors. No changes to existing trading rules, margins, or compliance frameworks are involved. Members need only ensure timely fund transfers if participating in this NFO.
