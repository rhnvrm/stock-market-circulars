## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Aurum PropTech Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 23, 2026. The further issue arises from the conversion of 1,170 partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- Symbol: AURUM; Series: EQ; ISIN: INE898S01029
- Face value: Rs. 5/- per share; Paid-up value: Rs. 5/- per share (fully paid)
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Number of securities admitted: 1,170
- Market lot: 1
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Lock-in details: Not applicable
- Distinctive number ranges cover three blocks totaling 1,170 shares (660 + 10 + 500)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the 1,170 newly listed fully paid-up shares under the existing AURUM symbol and ISIN (INE898S01029) with a market lot of 1 from the effective date. Shares were previously credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8898S01010) per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 pending activation.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 23, 2026
- Circular date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the small size of the further issue (1,170 shares) relative to Aurum PropTech Limited's overall float. This is a routine corporate action completing the conversion of previously partly paid-up shares into fully paid, freely tradable equity shares under the existing ISIN, with no changes to trading regulations or broader market structure.
