## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the AlphaGrep Multi Asset Allocation Fund NFO, offered under AlphaGrep Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The fund is a Debt category scheme offered across six plan/option variants, with allotment scheduled for July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- New Fund Offer: AlphaGrep Multi Asset Allocation Fund, categorized as Debt scheme.
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular-Growth, Regular-IDCW Reinvestment, Regular-IDCW Payout, Direct-Growth, Direct-IDCW Reinvestment, Direct-IDCW Payout.
- ISINs assigned: INF2VOA01046, INF2VOA01053, INF2VOA01061, INF2VOA01012, INF2VOA01020, INF2VOA01038.
- Allotment date for all variants: July 24, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by the NFO closing deadline.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme to be transacted on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting in this NFO must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 20, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Period: July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: July 20, 2026, till 11:59 pm
- Allotment Date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is a routine operational notification enabling NSE members to offer a new debt-oriented mutual fund scheme to investors via the NSE MF Invest Platform. There is no effect on listed equities, trading mechanisms, or existing securities. Impact is limited to members and investors participating in this specific NFO.
