## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India announces the launch of Axis BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund New Fund Offer (NFO) by Axis Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from January 23, 2026 to February 06, 2026 till 11:59 PM. Two plan options are available: Direct Growth and Regular Growth, both under the Equity category with allotment scheduled for February 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Axis BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund NFO launching on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two scheme options available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1424) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1416)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Subscription period: January 23, 2026 to February 06, 2026 till 11:59 PM
- Allotment date: February 11, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account by February 11, 2026 12:30 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to:
- Ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 11, 2026 12:30 PM
- Process transactions for the NFO during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: January 23, 2026
- **NFO End Date**: February 06, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Funds Receipt Deadline**: February 11, 2026 (12:30 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch announcement with minimal market impact. It provides investment opportunities in the BSE India Sector Leaders Index through Axis Mutual Fund. The circular is informational in nature, targeting NSE members and investors interested in equity index funds. No significant operational or compliance burden is imposed on market participants beyond standard NFO transaction processing requirements.
