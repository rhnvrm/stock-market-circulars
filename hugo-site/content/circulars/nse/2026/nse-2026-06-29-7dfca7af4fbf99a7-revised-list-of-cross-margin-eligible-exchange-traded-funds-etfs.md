## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a revised list of Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) eligible for cross-margining in the Futures & Options segment. This circular supersedes the previous list issued under circular no. 0062/2026 dated May 26, 2026, and the updated list takes effect from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 23 ETFs are eligible for cross-margin benefit in the F&O segment effective July 01, 2026.
- Each eligible ETF has a specified minimum quantity (and multiples thereof) required to avail cross-margin benefit.
- The list includes ETFs from major AMCs: Nippon India, SBI, ICICI Prudential, HDFC, Kotak Mahindra, Mirae Asset, and CPSE ETF.
- This update supersedes the list from NCL circular 0062/2026 (NCL/CMPT/74400) dated May 26, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The revised eligible ETF list replaces the previously communicated list. Changes may include additions, removals, or modifications to minimum quantity requirements for specific ETFs.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of NSE Clearing Limited must use the updated list for cross-margin calculations from July 1, 2026.
- Cross-margin benefit is applicable only when ETF holdings meet or exceed the specified minimum quantity (or multiples thereof).

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 1, 2026
- **Previous Circular:** NCL/CMPT/74400, dated May 26, 2026 (circular no. 0062/2026)
