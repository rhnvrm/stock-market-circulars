## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund NFO (New Fund Offer) of JM Financial Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- The circular covers six scheme options under JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund, spanning Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- All listed schemes fall under the Equity category with distinct ISINs.
- Facilities enabled include ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Scheme ISINs: INF192K01NS0, INF192K01NR2, INF192K01NQ4, INF192K01NV4, INF192K01NU6, INF192K01NT8.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding platform availability of an existing fund scheme, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in mutual fund units via the NSE MF Invest Platform should ensure their systems recognize the scheme ISINs and enable order placement (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) for these schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions: July 20, 2026
- Circular issue date: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with low market impact. It affects mutual fund distributors and members transacting on NSE's MF platform by enabling continued investor access to the JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund, but does not affect listed equity trading or broader market operations.
