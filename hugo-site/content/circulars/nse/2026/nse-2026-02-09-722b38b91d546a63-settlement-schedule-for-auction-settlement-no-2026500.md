## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement schedule for Auction settlement number 2026500. This circular is specifically for NCL members who participated in BSE auction 2526814. The settlement date is February 10, 2026, with a pay-in deadline of 07:00 AM at the Clearing Corporation.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: A2026500
- Trade date: February 09, 2026
- Custodial confirmation date: February 09, 2026
- Settlement date: February 10, 2026
- Pay-in time for auction at CC: 07:00 AM
- Participants must note cut-off timings for pay-in at depositories end

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NCL members who participated in BSE auction 2526814 must adhere to the settlement schedule
- Members must ensure timely pay-in by 07:00 AM on February 10, 2026
- Participants must be aware of and comply with cut-off timings at depositories end

## Important Dates

- **February 09, 2026**: Trade date and custodial confirmation date
- **February 10, 2026**: Settlement date
- **07:00 AM (February 10, 2026)**: Pay-in deadline for auction at Clearing Corporation

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It affects only NCL members who participated in the specific BSE auction 2526814.
