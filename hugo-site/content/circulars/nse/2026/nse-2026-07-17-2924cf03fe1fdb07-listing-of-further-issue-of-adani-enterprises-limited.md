## Summary

NSE has notified the further listing of 4,80,344 equity shares of Adani Enterprises Limited under the symbol ADANIENT (Series EQ, ISIN INE423A01024), arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares. These shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: Adani Enterprises Limited, Symbol ADANIENT, Series EQ, ISIN INE423A01024
- Face value: Re. 1/-; Paid-up value: Re. 1/-
- Nature of issue: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Number of securities: 4,80,344
- Market lot: 1
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Lock-in: Not applicable
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8423A01015 pending activation, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify and trade these securities using the designated codes and lot size (1) as specified in the annexure once dealings commence.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 17, 2026
- Effective date for listing/dealings: July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing action reflecting conversion of already-issued partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status. It does not represent new capital raising or a change in overall issued capital structure beyond the conversion, and is not expected to materially affect ADANIENT's trading or valuation.
