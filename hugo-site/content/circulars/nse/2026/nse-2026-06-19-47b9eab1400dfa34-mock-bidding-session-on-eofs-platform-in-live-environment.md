## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock bidding session on the eOFS (electronic Offer for Sale) platform in the Live environment on June 20, 2026. The session runs from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM and involves three test securities. Bids placed during this mock session carry no fund or securities obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for June 20, 2026 from 11:00 AM to 01:00 PM on the Live eOFS platform (https://eofs.nseindia.com)
- Three test securities available: TESTOFS1 (Retail + Employee categories), TESTOFS2 (Retail + Employee categories), and TESTOFS3 (Institutional category)
- All bids placed during the mock session are non-binding — no funds or securities obligations will arise
- NSE will perform internal order/bidding activity as a sanity check under symbol TESTOFS1 (Employee Category)
- Members with employee PAN details shared with NSE can bid in TESTOFS1 using existing credentials
- Members are requested to place bids in Employee Category under symbol TESTOFS2
- Floor price for all three test securities is Rs. 100; face value is Rs. 10

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock session to test platform readiness for OFS bidding.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock bidding session
- Members should monitor www.nseindia.com for any new or revised announcements related to the June 20, 2026 mock session
- For queries, members may call the NSE Toll Free number: 1800 266 0050 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026**: Mock bidding session on eOFS Live platform, 11:00 AM to 01:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to a test/mock exercise on the eOFS platform. No real securities or funds are involved. The session is intended to help exchange members familiarise themselves with the eOFS bidding system, particularly for Offer for Sale transactions. Participation is voluntary and carries no financial consequence. Relevant only to NSE members who participate in OFS bidding.
