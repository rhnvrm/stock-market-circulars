## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of 68,50,095 equity shares of Foce India Limited (Symbol: FOCE) on the Capital Market segment pursuant to a bonus issue. The shares will be admitted to dealings effective March 05, 2026, under ISIN INE0I7D01019.

## Key Points

- 68,50,095 new equity shares of Foce India Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Symbol: FOCE; ISIN: INE0I7D01019
- Distinctive number range: 4892926 to 11743020
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Issued by Manager Ruchi Jaiswal on behalf of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of securities to dealings on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security solely by its designated code (FOCE) for the purpose of trading on the system
- No separate action required from members beyond acknowledging the new securities admitted to trading from March 05, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** March 04, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action listing. Existing shareholders of Foce India Limited who hold shares as of the record date (March 02, 2026) will receive bonus shares, increasing the total share count by 68,50,095. Trading in the bonus shares on NSE commences March 05, 2026. The bonus issue dilutes the per-share price proportionally but does not affect overall shareholder value. Market members should update their systems to reflect the expanded share count from the effective date.
