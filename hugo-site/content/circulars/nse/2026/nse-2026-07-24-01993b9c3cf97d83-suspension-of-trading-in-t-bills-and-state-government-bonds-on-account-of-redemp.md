## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in 14 debt securities—12 Treasury Bills (T-Bills) and 2 State Government Bonds (SDLs)—effective their respective suspension dates, all on account of maturity/redemption. This is a routine regulatory notification under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 14 debt securities will be suspended from trading due to maturity/redemption
- Securities include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills, and 2 State Development Loans (SDLs)
- Suspension dates range from July 31, 2026 to August 25, 2026
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification implementing existing regulation 3.1.2 for suspending securities nearing maturity/redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the listed securities from their respective suspension dates. No other action is required from market participants beyond ensuring no trades are executed in these securities post-suspension.

## Important Dates

- 91D060826, 182D060826, 364D060826: Suspended from August 4, 2026
- 91D130826, 182D130826, 364D130826: Suspended from August 11, 2026
- 91D200826, 364D200826: Suspended from August 18, 2026
- 182D210826: Suspended from August 19, 2026
- 182D270826: Suspended from August 24, 2026
- 91D280826, 364D280826: Suspended from August 25, 2026
- 625AS26 (SDL): Suspended from July 31, 2026
- 624MH26 (SDL): Suspended from August 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative action affecting only holders and traders of these specific short-term government debt instruments, which are being suspended as they approach their natural maturity/redemption dates. No impact on equity markets or broader trading activity is expected.
