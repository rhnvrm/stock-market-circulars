## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Dhariwalcorp Limited on the Capital Market segment under SME EMERGE, effective March 25, 2026. The listing follows a rights issue allotment dated March 20, 2026, with 47,007,000 equity shares admitted to dealings under symbol DHARIWAL.

## Key Points

- Symbol: DHARIWAL, Series: SM
- ISIN: INE0YRN01025
- Face Value: Rs. 2, Paid-up Value: Rs. 2, Issue Price: Rs. 2.5
- Total securities listed: 47,007,000 equity shares
- Distinctive number range: 47,007,001 to 94,014,000
- Market lot size: 3,000 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is made pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol DHARIWAL and series SM for all transactions.
- Trading must be conducted in the prescribed market lot size of 3,000 shares.
- Shares allotted in dematerialized form are credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) pending activation per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 20, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing and Trading: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME EMERGE listing with limited broader market impact. It provides liquidity to rights issue allottees of Dhariwalcorp Limited. The market lot of 3,000 shares at an issue price of Rs. 2.5 implies a minimum trading value of Rs. 7,500, consistent with SME segment norms.
