## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying the suspension of trading in six Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited. The suspension is effective from May 18, 2026, and is triggered by the maturity of these debt instruments.

## Key Points

- Six NCDs of Sammaan Capital Limited are being suspended from trading effective May 18, 2026
- The reason for suspension is maturity of each of the listed securities
- The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74248, Circular Ref. No: 0792/2026
- Issued by Ruchi Jaiswal, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The suspension is a routine action under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the suspension of securities upon maturity or other qualifying events.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in the six listed NCD symbols from May 18, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of these securities
- No additional compliance action is required beyond awareness and system updates

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 15, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 18, 2026
