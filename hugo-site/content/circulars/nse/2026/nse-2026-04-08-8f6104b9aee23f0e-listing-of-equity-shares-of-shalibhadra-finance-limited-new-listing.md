## Summary

Shalibhadra Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 511754, ISIN: INE861D01011) has been newly listed on NSE. The company has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(ab) for the quarter ended 30-03-2026 as part of the pre-listing/allotment disclosure requirements.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Shalibhadra Finance Ltd
- **Scrip Code:** 511754
- **ISIN:** INE861D01011
- **NSE Symbol:** SAHLIBHFI (new listing)
- **Class of Security:** Equity Shares
- **SME Company:** No
- **PSU:** No
- **Report Type:** Quarterly (Regulation 31(1)(ab) – Pre-listing/Allotment)
- **Quarter Ended / Listing Date Reference:** 30-03-2026
- Promoter shareholding has locked-in shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or outstanding ESOPs
- No depository receipts, pledged shares, or non-disposal undertakings
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner declared

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular pertains solely to the new listing and associated mandatory shareholding pattern disclosure under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shalibhadra Finance Limited is required to maintain and update shareholding pattern disclosures on a quarterly basis post-listing.
- Promoter lock-in compliance must be observed as indicated in the shareholding pattern.
- The company must adhere to all continuing listing obligations under SEBI LODR Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Shareholding Pattern Reference Date:** 30-03-2026
- **Listing Date:** On or around 08-04-2026 (circular issuance date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a new listing of a small non-SME finance company. Market impact is limited to investors specifically tracking new NSE listings or the NBFC/finance sector. No unusual capital structures (no warrants, convertibles, or differential voting rights) are present, suggesting a straightforward equity listing.
