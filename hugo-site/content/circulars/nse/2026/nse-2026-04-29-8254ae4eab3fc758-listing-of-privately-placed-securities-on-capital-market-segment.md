## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities of Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective April 30, 2026. The notification is issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, specifying the designated security codes and lot sizes for trading.

## Key Points

- Securities of Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective April 30, 2026
- Security identified by designated code `82907AIF29` under Series `N0`
- Market lot size specified as 1 unit
- ISIN: INE0AD507218
- Coupon rate: 8.2907% per annum
- Maturity date: June 27, 2029
- Issue description: AIFL 8.2907% 2029 Sr A1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued under the existing framework of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security code `82907AIF29` for trading this security on the system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members must ensure trades are executed in Series `N0`

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Effective Date (Listing):** April 30, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** June 27, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing notification with limited market impact. The listing of a single NCD (Non-Convertible Debenture) by Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited at a coupon of 8.2907% maturing in 2029 is relevant primarily to institutional investors and bond market participants. The private placement nature restricts broad retail participation. No systemic or broad market impact is expected.
