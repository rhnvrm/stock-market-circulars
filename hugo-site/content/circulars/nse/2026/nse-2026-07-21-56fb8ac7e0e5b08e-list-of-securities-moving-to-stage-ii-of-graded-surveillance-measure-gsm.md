## Summary

NSE has announced that the security CBAZAAR (Net Avenue Technologies Limited) will move to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective July 22, 2026. This continues the surveillance actions previously communicated across several earlier circulars dating back to February 2017.

## Key Points

- Security moving to GSM Stage II: CBAZAAR (Net Avenue Technologies Limited), ISIN INE518X01015
- Trading will be restricted to Trade for Trade (T2T) segment
- Price band capped at 5% or lower as applicable
- Buyers must deposit Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value
- Circular references continuation of prior GSM circulars: NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/74254, NSE/SURV/74795

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this circular applies the existing GSM framework (Stage I through IV) to a specific security. Stage II actions involve trade-for-trade settlement, restricted price bands, and mandatory ASD collection from buyers to curb speculative and unauthorized market practices.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure ASD of 50% of trade value is collected from buyers of CBAZAAR shares
- Members must apply trade-for-trade settlement and the applicable 5% or lower price band
- Members should exercise adequate precaution while trading in this security
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- Effective date of move to GSM Stage II: July 22, 2026
- Circular issued: July 21, 2026 (Circular Ref. No: 546/2026)

## Impact Assessment

The move to Stage II GSM significantly restricts trading liquidity and flexibility for CBAZAAR shares, as trades will be limited to trade-for-trade settlement (no intraday trading) with a capped price band and a substantial 50% ASD requirement on buyers. This is likely to reduce trading volumes and increase transaction costs for market participants dealing in this security, while serving the intended purpose of curbing speculative or manipulative trading activity.
