## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued an update on the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies subject to the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective July 1, 2026, Aban Offshore Limited (ABAN), Neueon Corporation Limited (NEUEON), and Perfect Infraengineers Limited (PERFECT) are added to the 'Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure' category, while Sical Logistics Limited (SICALLOG) is fully excluded from ASM-IBC. No securities were newly shortlisted under Stage I or Stage II in this update.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref No: NSE/SURV/74946 (Ref: 486/2026), dated June 30, 2026, effective July 1, 2026.
- Three securities — ABAN, NEUEON, PERFECT — satisfy the criteria for 'Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure from the company' under ASM-IBC.
- SICALLOG satisfies exclusion criteria and is removed from ASM-IBC effective July 1, 2026.
- No new securities are shortlisted under ASM IBC Stage I, Stage I-to-II upgrade, or Stage II-to-I downgrade in this update (all Nil).
- The ASM-IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM IBC is purely for market surveillance purposes and must not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the ASM-IBC list in accordance with prior circulars NSE/SURV/37979 (June 8, 2018), NSE/SURV/52368 (May 20, 2022), NSE/SURV/55582 (February 10, 2023), and NSE/SURV/70074 (September 5, 2025). The consolidated ASM-IBC list continues to include AGSTRA (AGS Transact Technologies Limited, Stage I) and CLCIND (CLC Industries Limited, Stage I), among others.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must take note of the revised ASM-IBC applicable securities effective July 1, 2026.
- Members should apply the relevant trading restrictions as per ASM-IBC rules to ABAN, NEUEON, and PERFECT from July 1, 2026.
- Trading in SICALLOG should reflect its exclusion from ASM-IBC from July 1, 2026.
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Circular issued.
- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date for all changes — addition of ABAN, NEUEON, PERFECT and exclusion of SICALLOG from ASM-IBC.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to a small set of securities. Traders and investors holding or trading ABAN, NEUEON, or PERFECT should note enhanced surveillance measures under ASM-IBC from July 1. Holders of SICALLOG benefit from its removal from the ASM-IBC framework. No Stage I or Stage II additions in Annexure I means the overall surveillance burden has not materially increased for the broader market. The ASM-IBC framework continues to impose higher scrutiny on IBC-affected companies without signaling any regulatory adverse action.
