## Summary

NSE has informed members that the NSE MF Invest Platform will undergo a complete downtime due to maintenance activity on August 1, 2026, from 18:00 hrs to 21:00 hrs. During this window, no orders will be accepted on the platform through any mode, including APIs.

## Key Points

- Complete downtime scheduled for the NSE MF Invest Platform due to maintenance activity.
- No transactions or orders will be accepted during the downtime window, including via API access.
- Members using API connectivity are advised to inform their technical teams or vendors about the downtime.
- Members are requested to plan their mutual fund transaction activities accordingly to avoid disruption.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational/maintenance notice and does not involve any regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the downtime window and should proactively inform relevant technical teams/vendors connecting via API about the scheduled maintenance.

## Important Dates

- Downtime Start: August 1, 2026, 18:00 hrs
- Downtime End: August 1, 2026, 21:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to a 3-hour window on a single day and is confined to the NSE MF Invest Platform used for mutual fund transactions. Equity and other securities trading segments are unaffected. Members and their clients should plan mutual fund order placements around this window to avoid transaction disruptions.
