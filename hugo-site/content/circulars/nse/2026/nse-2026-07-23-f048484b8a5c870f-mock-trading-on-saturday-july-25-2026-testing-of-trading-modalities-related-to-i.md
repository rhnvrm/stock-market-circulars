## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session on Saturday, July 25, 2026, in the Capital Market segment to test trading modalities related to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, as previously notified under circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026. The mock will run across three trading sessions with a new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) required only for Trading Session-1, related to CAS testing.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: Saturday, July 25, 2026
- Three trading sessions will be conducted: Session-1 (Primary Site, CAS testing), Session-2 (Primary Site, existing modalities), and Session-3 (DR Site)
- New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 is applicable only during Trading Session-1 for CAS-related testing
- After Trading Session-1, members must reinstall and log in with the earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for Sessions 2 and 3
- Sessions 2 and 3 will be conducted per existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes
- Trading Session-1 includes new CAS-specific timings: CAS Open (13:15), CAS Market order restriction start (13:23), CAS close (14:00), following Normal Market close for CAS securities (13:15)
- Trades from Trading Session-1 will not be available in Sessions 2 or 3; trades from Session-2 will be available in Session-3
- Members are required to actively participate in all trading sessions
- Live re-login window for the mock is between 19:30 and 20:00

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operationalizes the CAS implementation previously announced in NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026, by scheduling a dedicated mock test of the new Closing Auction Session mechanism and associated NEATPlus software version.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must participate in the mock trading session on July 25, 2026
- Members must install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 for Trading Session-1
- Members must reinstall and log in with NEATPlus version 7.8.8 after Session-1 concludes, before Sessions 2 and 3
- Members must adhere to the specified session timings for block deal windows, pre-open, normal market, auction market, CAS, and closing sessions

## Important Dates

- Mock trading date: July 25, 2026 (Saturday)
- Trading Session-1: Normal Market opens 09:00, CAS Open 13:15, CAS Close 14:00, Closing Session ends 14:40
- Trading Session-2: Normal Market opens 09:15, Normal Market closes 16:00, Closing Session ends 16:15
- Trading Session-3 (DR Site): Normal Market opens 18:30, closes 19:00, Closing Session ends 19:20
- Live re-login window: 19:30 to 20:00

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/technical exercise required for all NSE trading members to validate readiness for the upcoming Closing Auction Session (CAS) feature in the Equity Cash segment. Members must ensure their systems support both the new NEATPlus version 7.8.9 (for CAS testing) and revert capability to version 7.8.8 for subsequent sessions. Failure to properly test could result in technical issues when CAS goes live in production. No direct impact on listed securities or investors is expected from this mock exercise itself.
