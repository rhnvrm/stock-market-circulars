## Summary

NSE has admitted units of two SBI Mutual Fund ETFs to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective May 25, 2026. The two schemes — SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF and SBI Nifty200 Value 30 ETF — were allotted on May 20, 2026, and will trade under symbols SBISMLETF and SBIVALETF respectively.

## Key Points

- **SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF (SBISMLETF):** 3,775,464 units, ISIN INF200KB1AY9, issue price Rs. 16.68, face value Rs. 10/-, market lot 1.
- **SBI Nifty200 Value 30 ETF (SBIVALETF):** 5,813,252 units, ISIN INF200KB1AX1, issue price Rs. 15.32, face value Rs. 10/-, market lot 1.
- Both series are EQ, allotted on May 20, 2026, with a paid-up value of Rs. 10/- per unit.
- Trading in these securities must use the designated NSE symbol codes; lot size is 1 unit for both.
- Thirteen market makers are designated for SBIVALETF, including Parwati Capital Market, East India Securities, Sbicap Securities, Motilal Oswal, and others.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is made in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade these securities using only the designated NSE symbol codes (SBISMLETF, SBIVALETF).
- Trading must be in the specified lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers listed in the annexure are obligated to provide liquidity as per applicable market-making obligations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 20, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of new ETF units with minimal market disruption. Investors and members gain access to two additional index ETF products tracking NSE's Nifty Smallcap 250 and Nifty200 Value 30 indices. The presence of 13 designated market makers for SBIVALETF suggests an expectation of reasonable liquidity. No impact on existing securities or compliance frameworks.
