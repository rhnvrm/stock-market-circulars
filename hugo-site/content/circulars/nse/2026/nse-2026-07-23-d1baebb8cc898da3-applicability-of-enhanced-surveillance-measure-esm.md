## Summary

NSE has issued its periodic update on the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Three securities — Emami Paper Mills Limited (EMAMIPAP), Forcas Studio Limited (FORCAS), and Investment & Precision Castings Limited (INVPRECQ) — have been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I effective July 24, 2026. No securities were moved between Stage I and Stage II, and no securities were excluded from the framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Three securities added to ESM Stage I: Emami Paper Mills Limited (INE830C01026), Forcas Studio Limited (INE0U2501017), and Investment & Precision Castings Limited (INE155E01016).
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I in this cycle.
- No securities excluded from the ESM framework in this update.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework (across Stage I and Stage II) is provided as Annexure III.
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ESM is based purely on market surveillance criteria and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine update under the existing ESM framework, referencing prior circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; the update applies existing ESM criteria to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in the newly added securities as on July 24, 2026, and on new positions created from July 27, 2026.
- The three newly added securities (EMAMIPAP, FORCAS, INVPRECQ) shall be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective July 27, 2026.
- Any securities shifting to Stage II (none in this cycle) would additionally trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: ESM Stage I inclusion effective; 100% margin applies to open positions as on this date.
- July 27, 2026: New positions in added securities also attract 100% margin; affected securities shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment.

## Impact Assessment

The move imposes a 100% margin requirement and a shift to the trade-for-trade (BE/ST) segment for EMAMIPAP, FORCAS, and INVPRECQ, which will reduce trading leverage and liquidity for these specific stocks. The broader market impact is limited since only three relatively small-cap securities are affected and no changes occurred to Stage II or exclusion lists in this cycle.
