## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Aditya Birla Sun Life Fixed Term Plan – Series TI (1837 days) (the Merging Scheme) will be merged into Aditya Birla Sun Life Corporate Bond Fund (the Surviving Scheme) effective after the close of business hours on **July 9, 2026**. The merger has been approved by the Board of Directors of Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited and the Trustees. Unitholders who do not wish to participate in the merger may exit without exit load during the specified window.

## Key Points

- **Merging Scheme:** Aditya Birla Sun Life Fixed Term Plan – Series TI (1837 days)
- **Surviving Scheme:** Aditya Birla Sun Life Corporate Bond Fund
- **Effective Date:** After close of business hours on Thursday, July 9, 2026
- Unitholders of the Merging Scheme who are not in favour of the merger may redeem or switch to other eligible Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund schemes without payment of exit load
- The exit/switch window runs from **June 10, 2026 to July 9, 2026** (both days inclusive, up to 3:00 PM on July 9, 2026) via the NSE MF Invest platform
- No change to the name, investment objective, asset allocation, or recurring expenses of the Surviving Scheme post-merger
- No new scheme will come into existence as a result of this merger

## Regulatory Changes

- All references to the Merging Scheme will be deleted from the SID (Scheme Information Document) and KIM (Key Information Memorandum) of Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund schemes
- Updated SID and KIM of the Surviving Scheme will be made available at ISCs and on the AMC website after the exit option period concludes
- This Notice-cum-Addendum (Addendum No. 31/2026) forms an integral part of the SID/KIM issued for the schemes

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members:** Take note of the scheme merger effective July 9, 2026 on the NSE MF Invest platform
- **Unitholders of Merging Scheme:** May redeem investments or switch to other available Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund schemes without exit load during the designated window
- All transactions must be submitted through the NSE MF Invest platform within the specified exit window

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 9, 2026 | NSE circular issued; notice cum addendum published |
| June 10, 2026 | Exit option window opens (no-load redemption/switch begins) |
| July 9, 2026 (up to 3:00 PM) | Last day for no-load redemption or switch by dissenting unitholders |
| July 9, 2026 (after close of business) | Merger effective date |

## Impact Assessment

- **Unitholders of Merging Scheme:** Existing investors in Aditya Birla Sun Life Fixed Term Plan – Series TI (1837 days) will have their units converted into units of the Surviving Scheme (Aditya Birla Sun Life Corporate Bond Fund) unless they opt to exit during the window. This represents a change from a fixed-term plan to an open-ended corporate bond fund.
- **Surviving Scheme Unitholders:** No adverse impact; the investment objective, asset allocation, and expense structure of the Corporate Bond Fund remain unchanged.
- **Platform Operations:** NSE members and the NSE MF Invest platform will need to update scheme listings to reflect the merger and removal of the Merging Scheme post-effective date.
- **Market Impact:** Limited broader market impact; this is an internal fund reorganisation by one AMC, though unitholders transitioning from a closed-ended fixed-term plan to an open-ended fund gain liquidity.
