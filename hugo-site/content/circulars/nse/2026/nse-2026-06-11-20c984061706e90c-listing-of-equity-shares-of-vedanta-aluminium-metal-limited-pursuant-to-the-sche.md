## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (symbol: VAML) on the Capital Market segment effective June 15, 2026. The listing arises from the NCLT-approved Scheme of Arrangement under which the Aluminium Undertaking of Vedanta Limited (VEDL) has been demerged and vested into VAML, an unlisted wholly owned subsidiary of VEDL. A total of 3,910,388,057 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each are being admitted to dealings.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** VAML | **Series:** BE (Trade for Trade) initially, to be shifted to EQ per SEBI circular SEBI/CIR/ISD/1/2010
- **ISIN:** INE1CDF01017
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Re. 1/- each
- **Total shares listed:** 3,910,388,057 (distinctive numbers 1 to 3,910,388,057)
- **Market lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes with existing shares
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- **Allotment ratio:** 1 equity share of VAML for every 1 equity share of VEDL held
- VAML will participate in the pre-open session per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

The Scheme of Arrangement was approved by the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench, Court V vide order dated December 16, 2025 under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The scheme provides for demerger of Vedanta Limited's Aluminium Undertaking into VAML, along with separate demergers of Power, Oil & Gas, and Iron & Steel undertakings into respective resulting companies. Trading commences in BE (Trade for Trade) series per SEBI surveillance norms and will subsequently migrate to the EQ series.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to recognize VAML under symbol VAML, series BE, with effect from June 15, 2026
- Trading must use designated security codes and the specified market lot of 1
- Members must comply with Trade for Trade settlement obligations for BE series securities
- Pre-open session participation applies as per SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Scheme Approval:** December 16, 2025
- **NSE Circular Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing / Trading Commencement:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact listing event. Vedanta Limited's existing shareholders automatically receive VAML shares on a 1:1 basis, making this a large-scale distribution event affecting the entire VEDL shareholder base. With nearly 3.91 billion shares listed, VAML enters as a large-cap entity in the aluminium sector. Initial trading in the BE (Trade for Trade) series means settlement is on a gross basis with no netting, potentially impacting liquidity in early trading sessions. The migration to the EQ series will occur once SEBI surveillance conditions are satisfied, normalizing trading thereafter. This listing is part of a broader Vedanta group restructuring that also includes separate listings for power, oil & gas, and iron & steel businesses.
