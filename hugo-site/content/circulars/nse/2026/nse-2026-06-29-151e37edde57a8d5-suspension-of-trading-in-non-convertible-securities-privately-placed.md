## Summary

NSE's Department of Listing has notified, under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, that the privately placed non-convertible securities of Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (ISIN: INE752E07NZ7) will be suspended from trading effective June 30, 2026, on account of redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE752E07NZ7 (Power Grid Corporation of India Limited) will be suspended from June 30, 2026.
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the non-convertible security.
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- Reference: NSE/CML/74876, Circular Ref. No. 1032/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is a standard procedural action under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.2) triggered by the upcoming redemption of the security.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are notified to cease trading in ISIN INE752E07NZ7 from June 30, 2026. No additional compliance action is required beyond ceasing trading in the specified security on and after the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine end-of-life suspension for a privately placed NCD of Power Grid Corporation of India Limited upon its redemption. Holders of the security would have received or will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or operational disruption is expected.
