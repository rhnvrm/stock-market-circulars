## Summary

NSE Circular No. NSE/CML/74693 (Ref: 0954/2026) dated June 12, 2026 notifies the admission of further equity shares to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective June 15, 2026, for 16 companies. All new shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Effective date for trading of newly listed shares: **June 15, 2026**
- All allotments are under ESOP schemes with no lock-in applicable
- All newly listed shares rank pari passu with existing shares
- Market lot for all securities is 1
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification under existing NSE listing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes as specified in the Annexure for trading
- Lot sizes as specified in the Annexure must be adhered to for all listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 15, 2026
- ESOP Allotment dates: May 28, 2026 (Apar Industries); June 4, 2026 (Almondz Global); June 5, 2026 (Aarti Industries)
