## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 2,42,15,722 equity shares of SEPC Limited (Symbol: SEPC, Series: EQ) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 11, 2026. The securities arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares, and will trade under the permanent ISIN INE964H01014 replacing the temporary ISIN IN8964H01013.

## Key Points

- 2,42,15,722 equity shares of SEPC Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from June 11, 2026
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Symbol: SEPC | Series: EQ | Face Value: Rs. 10/- | Paid-up Value: Rs. 10/-
- Permanent ISIN: INE964H01014 (replaces temporary ISIN IN8964H01013)
- Market lot: 1 | Pari Passu: Yes | Lock-in: Not Applicable
- Distinctive number ranges: 1890868399–1906601128 (1,57,32,730 shares) and 1906604380–1915087371 (84,82,992 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, governing admission of securities to dealings and lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade these securities using the designated NSE symbol (SEPC) and security codes only
- Trading must be in the specified lot size of 1
- The temporary ISIN (IN8964H01013) under which dematerialised shares were credited per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 is superseded by the permanent ISIN INE964H01014

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative listing consequent to the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up shares. The converted shares (2,42,15,722) are now fully fungible with existing SEPC equity shares and eligible for normal trading. No change in the company's total authorised capital or business fundamentals is implied by this circular.
