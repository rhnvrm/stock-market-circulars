## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on its Debt Market Segment effective June 24, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The circular covers eight fresh-issue commercial papers and one market-linked debt security across nine issuers, along with a re-issue by ICICI Securities Limited.

## Key Points

- Eight commercial papers (fresh issue) admitted to dealings from June 24, 2026
- One market-linked debt security by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited maturing January 2032
- Issuers include major financial institutions: Export Import Bank of India (60,000 units), Cholamandalam Investment and Finance (12,000 units), PNB Housing Finance (10,000 units)
- All commercial papers have face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit
- Redemption tenors range from short-term (HDB Financial: 06-Jul-2026) to medium-term (Angel One: 22-Dec-2026)
- At least one re-issue by ICICI Securities Limited also included (details truncated in source)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All Members of NSE are notified of the new security codes assigned per the Annexure. No specific compliance action required from members beyond taking note of the new listings and designated security codes.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing circular with low market impact. It provides liquidity to holders of these privately placed debt instruments by admitting them to exchange-traded dealings on the NSE Debt Market Segment. The total issuance is significant in size—notably Export Import Bank of India's 60,000 units at ₹5,00,000 face value represents a ₹3,000 crore issuance—but the listing itself is a standard procedural step. Equity market participants are not directly affected. Debt market members and institutional investors in these commercial papers gain a secondary market trading avenue.
