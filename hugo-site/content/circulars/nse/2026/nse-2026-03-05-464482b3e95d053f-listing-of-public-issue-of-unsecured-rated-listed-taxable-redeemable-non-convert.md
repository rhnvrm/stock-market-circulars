## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of 8 tranches (STRPP A through H) of 8.05% Unsecured, Rated, Listed, Taxable, Redeemable, Non-Convertible Green Municipal Bonds in the nature of Debentures issued by Nashik Municipal Corporation. All bonds were allotted on March 05, 2026, at an issue price of Rs. 125 per unit with a nominal value of Rs. 125. Each tranche comprises 20,00,000 securities totalling Rs. 25 crores per tranche (Rs. 200 crores in aggregate). The bonds carry half-yearly interest payments and have been rated Provisional IND AA+/Stable by India Ratings and Research and Provisional CRISIL AA+/Stable by CRISIL Ratings.

## Key Points

- 8 tranches (STRPP A to H) of green municipal bonds listed on NSE
- Coupon rate: 8.05% per annum, paid half-yearly (March 5 & September 5 each year)
- Issue price and nominal value: Rs. 125 per bond
- Each tranche: 20,00,000 securities; Rs. 25 crores redemption amount
- Total issuance: Rs. 200 crores across all 8 tranches
- Date of allotment: March 05, 2026
- Distinctive numbers for all tranches: 1 to 20,00,000
- Rated Provisional IND AA+/Stable (India Ratings, January 14, 2026) and Provisional CRISIL AA+/Stable (CRISIL, January 13, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing circular for newly issued debt securities on NSE's debt market segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the listing of these bonds and update their systems accordingly
- The securities are available for trading in the debt market segment of NSE from the listing date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 05, 2026
- **Interest Payment Dates:** March 5 and September 5 every year
- **STRPP A Maturity:** March 5, 2029 (ISIN: INE579F24099)
- **STRPP B Maturity:** March 5, 2030 (ISIN: INE579F24065)
- **STRPP C Maturity:** March 5, 2031 (ISIN: INE579F24032)
- **STRPP D Maturity:** March 5, 2032 (ISIN: INE579F24057)
- **STRPP E Maturity:** March 5, 2033 (ISIN: INE579F24040)
- **STRPP F Maturity:** March 5, 2034 (ISIN: INE579F24081)
- **STRPP G Maturity:** March 5, 2035 (ISIN: INE579F24073)
- **STRPP H Maturity:** March 5, 2036 (ISIN: INE579F24107)
- **Rating Date:** January 13–14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Nashik Municipal Corporation's green bonds on NSE provides retail and institutional investors access to AA+ rated green municipal debt instruments with staggered maturities from 2029 to 2036. The 8.05% coupon offers a competitive yield for investment-grade municipal paper. As green bonds, they support environmentally sustainable urban infrastructure projects. The half-yearly coupon frequency and structured redemption profile (STRPP structure) offer predictable cash flows. The high credit rating (AA+) from two agencies signals low default risk, making these bonds suitable for conservative investors seeking fixed income exposure in the municipal bond space.
