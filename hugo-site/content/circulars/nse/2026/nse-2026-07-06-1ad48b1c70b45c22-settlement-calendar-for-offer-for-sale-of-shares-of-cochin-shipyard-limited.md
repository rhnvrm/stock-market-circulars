## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has notified the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Cochin Shipyard Limited, referencing an earlier circular dated April 30, 2026. Settlement for the OFS securities will follow existing secondary market transaction rules, with specific dates provided for order placement, custodial confirmation, and final settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement is being conducted as per existing rules for secondary market transactions, per earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026.
- Two settlement types apply: H (Hold) and T (Trade-for-Trade), across two settlement series (2026125 and 2026126).
- Series 2026125: Order date July 7, 2026, settlement date July 8, 2026 for both 100% margin (IS non-retail) and 0% margin (T-type IS non-retail) categories.
- Series 2026126: Order date July 8, 2026, settlement date July 9, 2026, covering Retail (RS) and Institutional/Non-retail (IS) categories.
- Custodial confirmation timing is 03:30 PM for H-type settlements and 07:30 AM (T+1) for T-type settlements.
- Applicable Securities Transaction Tax (STT) and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes settlement timelines under existing OFS and secondary market settlement rules previously communicated via circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the specified settlement calendar, ensuring timely order confirmation, custodial confirmation, and funds/securities pay-in and pay-out as per the prescribed schedule. Funds pay-in is due by T+1 day 11:00 am, and securities & funds pay-out by T+1 day 1:30 pm.

## Important Dates

- Series 2026125: Order date July 7, 2026; Settlement date July 8, 2026.
- Series 2026126: Order date July 8, 2026; Settlement date July 9, 2026.
- Custodial confirmation deadlines: 03:30 PM (same day for H-type) and 07:30 AM (next day for T-type).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has operational relevance primarily for NSE members, custodians, and PCMs involved in the Cochin Shipyard Limited OFS transaction, ensuring smooth settlement processing. It does not have a broader market-wide impact but is important for entities directly participating in the OFS to avoid settlement delays or penalties.
