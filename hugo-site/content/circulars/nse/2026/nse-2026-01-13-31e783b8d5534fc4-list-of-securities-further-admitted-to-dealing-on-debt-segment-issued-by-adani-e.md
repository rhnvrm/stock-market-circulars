## Summary

NSE has admitted 8 new debt securities (non-convertible debentures) issued by Adani Enterprises Limited to trading on the Debt Segment. The securities include both fixed coupon and zero-coupon debentures with maturities in 2028, 2029, and 2031. Total issued capital across all securities is Rs. 1,00,000 lakhs approximately. The listing becomes effective from January 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8 debt securities of Adani Enterprises Limited admitted to NSE Debt Segment
- Mix of fixed coupon (rates: 8.60%, 8.48%, 8.75%, 8.62%, 8.90%) and zero-coupon debentures
- Maturity dates: January 12, 2028 (2 securities), January 12, 2029 (3 securities), January 12, 2031 (3 securities)
- All securities rated by CARE
- Face value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture for all securities
- Periodic coupon payment frequency for fixed coupon debentures
- Issue date: January 21, 2026
- Public issue type

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing circular pursuant to existing regulations referenced in NSE/CML/23366 (May 8, 2013) and NSE/DS/24832 (October 25, 2013).

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed debt securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 14, 2026 (trading commencement)
- **Issue Date**: January 21, 2026
- **First Interest Payment Dates**: April 12, 2026 (for 2029 and 2031 maturities) and January 12, 2027 (for 2028 maturity)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine debt listing for institutional and retail investors seeking fixed income exposure to Adani Enterprises Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Members can trade these securities on the Debt Segment from January 14, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional debt investment options with 2-5 year tenors and fixed/zero coupon structures for investors seeking Adani Enterprises credit exposure.
