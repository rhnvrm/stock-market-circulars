## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by IRB Infrastructure Developers Limited (Symbol: IRB). The new shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective April 06, 2026, under ISIN INE821I01022.

## Key Points

- Bonus issue equity shares of IRB Infrastructure Developers Limited to be listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- Symbol: IRB | ISIN: INE821I01022
- Total number of new securities: 6,03,90,00,000 (approximately 6.039 billion shares)
- Distinctive number range: 6039000001 to 12078000000
- Trading effective from April 06, 2026
- Circular Ref. No: 0520/2026 | Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73475

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of securities to dealings on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated security codes for trading purposes.
- This circular is effective from April 06, 2026; members should update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** April 01, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** April 02, 2026
- **Listing & Trading Effective Date:** April 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue adds approximately 6.039 billion new equity shares of IRB Infrastructure Developers Limited to the NSE Capital Market segment. This is a standard corporate action (bonus issue) and is unlikely to have an adverse market impact. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares, and the stock price is expected to adjust proportionally on the ex-bonus date. Members and trading systems should be updated to handle the increased share count and the new distinctive number range prior to April 06, 2026.
