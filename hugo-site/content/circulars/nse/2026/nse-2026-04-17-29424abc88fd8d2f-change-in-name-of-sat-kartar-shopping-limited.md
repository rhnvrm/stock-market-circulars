## Summary

NSE has informed members that Sat Kartar Shopping Limited, listed under the symbol SATKARTAR, will officially change its name to Sat Kartar Life Limited with effect from April 23, 2026. The stock symbol remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Symbol SATKARTAR will retain its ticker but the company name changes
- Existing name: Sat Kartar Shopping Limited
- New name: Sat Kartar Life Limited
- Effective date: April 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a straightforward corporate name change communicated via NSE Listing Department circular ref. 0640/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73777).

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the name change and update records accordingly. No additional compliance action is specified.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: April 17, 2026
- Effective date of name change: April 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The name change reflects a rebranding from a shopping-focused identity to a life-focused identity. The stock symbol SATKARTAR remains the same, so no trading disruption is expected. Members should update internal systems to reflect the new company name from April 23, 2026.
