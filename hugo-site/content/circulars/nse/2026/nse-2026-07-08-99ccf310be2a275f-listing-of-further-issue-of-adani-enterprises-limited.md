## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Adani Enterprises Limited on the Capital Market segment. The shares were allotted under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) and will be admitted to dealings effective July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.
- Company: Adani Enterprises Limited, Symbol: ADANIENT, Series: EQ.
- ISIN: INE423A01024 (shares currently credited under a temporary ISIN pending activation per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012).
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1 each; Issue price: Rs. 2883 per share.
- Number of securities allotted: 52,029,136 equity shares.
- Distinctive number range: 1356525481 to 1408554616.
- Date of allotment: July 7, 2026.
- Market lot: 1; shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares.
- No lock-in applicable to these shares.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize the additional shares as admitted to dealings from the effective date and identify the security by its designated code and specified market lot for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 7, 2026
- Effective date of listing/trading: July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Adani Enterprises Limited by approximately 5.2 crore shares issued via QIP at Rs. 2883 per share, representing a capital raise of roughly Rs. 15,000 crore. This is a standard post-QIP listing formality with no immediate market operational impact, though it modestly increases free float and may be a data point for investors tracking dilution and capital-raising activity at ADANIENT.
