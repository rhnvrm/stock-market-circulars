## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Central Mine Planning & Design Institute Limited (CMPDI) on the Exchange under an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Central Mine Planning & Design Institute Limited
- Stock Symbol: CMPDI
- ISIN: INE05HV01027
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/73483 (Circular Ref. No: 0524/2026)
- The listing date has not yet been announced and will be notified via a separate circular
- Circular issued by Aarti Parmar, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It serves as a pre-listing notification to Exchange members regarding an upcoming IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing. No specific compliance actions are required at this stage. Members should monitor for the subsequent circular that will confirm the exact listing date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals the imminent listing of Central Mine Planning & Design Institute Limited (CMPDI), a public sector entity involved in mine planning and design. The listing will make CMPDI shares tradeable on NSE. Market participants and investors interested in the mining/infrastructure sector should watch for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. The impact is moderate as it opens a new investment avenue but has no immediate effect on existing market operations or trading rules.
