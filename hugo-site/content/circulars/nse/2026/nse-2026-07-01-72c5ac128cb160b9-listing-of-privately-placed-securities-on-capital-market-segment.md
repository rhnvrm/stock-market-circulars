## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities on the Capital Market segment, effective July 02, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Fresh issues from REC Limited, Aditya Birla Capital Limited, and Hero FinCorp Limited have been admitted to dealings, with designated security codes and lot sizes as specified in the Annexure.

## Key Points

- Three new debt securities admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective July 02, 2026
- REC Limited: Symbol 717RECL31, Series N0, 7.17% coupon, Sr 255, maturing 01-Jul-2031, ISIN INE020B08GF9
- Aditya Birla Capital Limited: Symbol 0ABCL31, Series N0, 0% coupon (zero-coupon), Sr C2, maturing 25-Jun-2031, ISIN INE674K07150
- Hero FinCorp Limited: Symbol 0HFL28, Series N0, 0% coupon (zero-coupon), Sr 79, maturing 30-Jun-2028, ISIN INE957N07880
- Market lot for all three securities is 1

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify these securities only by their designated codes and trade them in the specified lot size (1) once dealings commence.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 01, 2026
- Effective date for dealings: July 02, 2026
- REC 717RECL31 maturity: 01-Jul-2031
- Aditya Birla Capital 0ABCL31 maturity: 25-Jun-2031
- Hero FinCorp 0HFL28 maturity: 30-Jun-2028

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to the routine listing of privately placed, non-convertible debt securities for three already well-known issuers (REC, Aditya Birla Capital, Hero FinCorp). It primarily affects debt market participants and trading members who need to update security master lists; there is no impact on equity trading, pricing, or broader market operations.
