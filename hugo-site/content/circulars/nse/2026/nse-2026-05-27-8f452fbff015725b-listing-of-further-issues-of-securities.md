## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 15 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective May 29, 2026. The shares were allotted through various mechanisms including ESOP, preferential allotment, and other corporate actions. Each security has been assigned designated codes and lot sizes for trading.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have further securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective May 29, 2026
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes for all new securities are set at 1 (market lot)
- All newly listed shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Securities include allotments via ESOP, preferential allotment, and other mechanisms

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes
- Lock-in restrictions apply where noted (e.g., Affordable Robotic & Automation Limited shares locked in until November 30, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Affordable Robotic):** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The addition of further securities for 15 companies increases the total float marginally. ESOP-related listings reflect employee compensation activity and are non-dilutive in terms of operational impact. The preferential allotment for Affordable Robotic & Automation Limited carries a lock-in until November 30, 2026, restricting near-term tradability of those shares. No material impact on broader market operations is expected.
