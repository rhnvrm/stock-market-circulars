## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for 14 companies — Aavas Financiers, Axis Bank, Biocon, Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions, CreditAccess Grameen, Five-Star Business Finance, HDFC Bank, Karur Vysya Bank, Kirloskar Oil Engines, PC Jeweller, Restaurant Brands Asia, RMC Switchgears, Tata Motors, and Triveni Engineering & Industries — effective from July 24, 2026. Most of the new securities arise from ESOP (Employee Stock Option Plan) allotments, while Biocon's tranche was allotted on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 376.41 per share. All newly listed shares rank pari passu with existing shares and carry no lock-in restrictions, except where otherwise specified.

## Key Points

- New equity shares from ESOP exercises and a preferential allotment become eligible for trading on NSE's Capital Market segment from July 24, 2026.
- Aavas Financiers Limited: two ESOP tranches of 648 and 1,596 shares (Rs. 10 face value) allotted on 07-Jul-2026, both pari passu, no lock-in.
- Axis Bank Limited: 116,524 ESOP shares (Rs. 2 face value) allotted on 21-Jul-2026, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Biocon Limited: shares allotted on a preferential basis at Rs. 376.41 per share (Rs. 5 face value); further allotment details were not fully captured in the available circular text.
- The remaining companies (Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions, CreditAccess Grameen, Five-Star Business Finance, HDFC Bank, Karur Vysya Bank, Kirloskar Oil Engines, PC Jeweller, Restaurant Brands Asia, RMC Switchgears, Tata Motors, Triveni Engineering & Industries) are covered under the same annexure, with individual lot sizes, ISINs, and distinctive number ranges specified per company.
- Each security continues to trade under its existing designated code and lot size as specified in the annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (further admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. No new regulatory requirement is introduced; it formalizes admission of incrementally issued shares to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize the newly allotted shares under the existing designated security codes and lot sizes as specified in the annexure.
- No separate member action is required beyond system-level trading recognition of the additional shares effective the stated date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 23, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading of New Securities: July 24, 2026
- Aavas Financiers allotment date: July 7, 2026
- Axis Bank allotment date: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular is administrative in nature and reflects routine ESOP-driven and preferential share issuances that marginally increase the free float of the respective companies. The share quantities involved (e.g., a few hundred to just over 100,000 shares) are immaterial relative to the total outstanding share capital of these large-cap and mid-cap companies, resulting in negligible market or price impact. No trading halts, restrictions, or investor action are required.
