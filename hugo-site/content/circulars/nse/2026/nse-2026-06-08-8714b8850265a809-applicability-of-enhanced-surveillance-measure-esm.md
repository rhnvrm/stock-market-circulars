## Summary

NSE has announced the applicability of Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) for a new set of securities effective June 09-10, 2026. Six securities have been newly included in ESM Stage I, and one security (CL Educate Limited) has been moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II. No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this circular.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly included in ESM Stage I effective June 09, 2026
- 1 security (CLEDUCATE) escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective June 09, 2026
- No securities moving from Stage II to Stage I; no securities excluded from ESM
- Securities marked with * will shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST series) from June 10, 2026
- Minimum 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 09, 2026 and new positions from June 10, 2026
- Stage II securities placed under Trade for Trade with 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction from June 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ESM framework established via prior NSE circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply a minimum 100% margin on open positions in the listed securities as on June 09, 2026 and on all new positions created from June 10, 2026
- Members must ensure trading in affected securities transitions to the Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment from June 10, 2026
- Stage II security (CLEDUCATE) must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from June 09, 2026
- Members may direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusions and Stage I-to-II escalation take effect; Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band for Stage II securities begins
- **June 10, 2026**: Shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) for marked securities; 100% margin requirement on new positions
