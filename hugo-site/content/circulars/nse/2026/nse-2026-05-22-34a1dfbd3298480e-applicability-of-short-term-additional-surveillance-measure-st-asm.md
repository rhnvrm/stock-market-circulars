## Summary

NSE has announced changes to the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective May 25, 2026. Eight securities have been added to ST-ASM Stage I, while six securities have been excluded from the ASM framework entirely. There are no securities moving between Stage I and Stage II in this update.

## Key Points

- 8 securities newly placed under ST-ASM Stage I effective May 25, 2026
- No securities added to ST-ASM Stage II
- No inter-stage movements (Stage I ↔ Stage II)
- 6 securities excluded from the ASM framework effective May 25, 2026
- SHETHJI (Shreeji Global FMCG Limited) moved from ST-ASM to ESM framework rather than a clean exclusion

## Regulatory Changes

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I are subject to enhanced surveillance measures including trade-for-trade settlement and applicable margin requirements as prescribed by NSE/SEBI. The consolidated ASM list has been updated accordingly.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and trading members must apply ST-ASM Stage I conditions to the newly listed securities from May 25, 2026
- Positions in excluded securities revert to normal trading and settlement conditions from May 25, 2026
- Members should note that SHETHJI transitions to ESM framework and ESM-specific conditions apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 25, 2026 — all additions and exclusions take effect
