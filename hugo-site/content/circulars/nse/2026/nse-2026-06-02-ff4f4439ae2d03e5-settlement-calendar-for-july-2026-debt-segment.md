## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (formerly National Securities Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Debt Segment for the month of July 2026. The calendar covers two settlement types: Debt Settlement (Type 'I') on a rolling T+1 basis and Debt Auction Settlement (Type '2'), also on a rolling T+1 basis. Settlement numbers run from 2026121 through 2026143 for Type 'I' and 2026121 through 2026141 for Type '2'.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type 'I' (Debt Settlement) covers trade dates from 01-Jul-26 to 31-Jul-26, with settlement dates from 02-Jul-26 to 03-Aug-26.
- Settlement Type '2' (Debt Auction Settlement) covers trade dates from 02-Jul-26 to 30-Jul-26, with settlement dates from 03-Jul-26 to 31-Jul-26.
- Both settlement types follow a rolling T+1 schedule.
- Weekends and holidays result in settlement on the next available business day (e.g., trades on 03-Jul-26 settle on 06-Jul-26).
- Issued under Download Ref No: NCL/DS/74513 and Circular Ref. No: 196/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational calendar notification issued in pursuance of Regulation 6.2 of NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs should note and adhere to the published settlement schedule for the Debt Segment during July 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact the NSE Clearing help desk at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or email securities_ops@nsccl.co.in.

## Important Dates

- First settlement (Type 'I'): Trade Date 01-Jul-26, Settlement Date 02-Jul-26 (Sett No. 2026121)
- Last settlement (Type 'I'): Trade Date 31-Jul-26, Settlement Date 03-Aug-26 (Sett No. 2026143)
- First settlement (Type '2'): Trade Date 02-Jul-26, Settlement Date 03-Jul-26 (Sett No. 2026121)
- Last settlement (Type '2'): Trade Date 30-Jul-26, Settlement Date 31-Jul-26 (Sett No. 2026141)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard monthly operational circular with no market-moving implications. It serves as a reference for debt segment participants to plan their settlement obligations for July 2026. No action is required beyond noting the published schedule.
