## Summary

NSE (Department: Listing) has issued circular NSE/CML/74894 (Ref. No. 1024/2026) dated June 25, 2026, notifying the admission of 8 privately placed debt securities to dealings on the NSE Capital Market Segment with effect from **June 29, 2026**. The issuers are Western Capital Advisors Private Limited, Torrent Power Limited (4 tranches), Godrej Industries Limited (2 tranches), and Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency Limited (IREDA). All securities carry a market lot of 1 and trade under Series N0.

## Key Points

- 8 fresh-issue debt securities admitted to NSE Capital Market Segment effective June 29, 2026.
- All securities carry a uniform market lot of 1 under Series N0.
- Coupon rates range from 7.34% (IREDA) to 10.75% (Western Capital Advisors).
- Maturities span from June 2029 to June 2036.
- Securities are identified solely by their designated NSE codes for trading purposes.
- Lot sizes are governed by Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The listings are made pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use only the designated NSE security codes when trading these instruments.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all securities).
- No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard debt segment trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date (Admitted to Dealings):** June 29, 2026
