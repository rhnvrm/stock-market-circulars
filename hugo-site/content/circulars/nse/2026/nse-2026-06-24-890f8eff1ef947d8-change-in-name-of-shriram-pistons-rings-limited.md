## Summary

NSE has notified all members that Shriram Pistons & Rings Limited, trading under the symbol **SHRIPISTON**, will officially change its corporate name to **SPR Auto Technologies Limited** with effect from July 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol **SHRIPISTON** remains unchanged; only the company name is being updated.
- Existing name: Shriram Pistons & Rings Limited.
- New name: SPR Auto Technologies Limited.
- Circular issued by Jeetendra Rangnani, Manager, NSE Listing Department.

## Regulatory Changes

The circular updates the registered name of the company on NSE records from Shriram Pistons & Rings Limited to SPR Auto Technologies Limited. No changes to the trading symbol, ISIN, or series have been announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the name change and update their internal records accordingly. No specific action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the updated company name.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change:** July 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with minimal market impact. The trading symbol SHRIPISTON remains the same, so day-to-day trading operations are unaffected. Investors and members should update records, reports, and client-facing materials to reflect the new company name SPR Auto Technologies Limited from July 01, 2026 onward.
