## Summary

NSE has shortlisted four securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective June 30, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) will apply to all open positions as of June 30, 2026 and new positions from July 1, 2026. No securities have been added to ST-ASM Stage II, and none are being moved between stages or excluded from the framework.

## Key Points

- Four securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I effective June 30, 2026: AEGISLOG, INVPRECQ, SPRL, and TEAMTECH
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%
- ST-ASM Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%
- No securities added to Stage II; no inter-stage movements; no exclusions
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to earlier NSE circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (October 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (December 4, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (April 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (September 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024) regarding the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework. The current circular applies the ST-ASM provisions to four newly shortlisted securities under Stage I.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must apply enhanced margin requirements on the shortlisted securities from July 1, 2026
- Margin of 50% or existing margin (whichever is higher, max 100%) must be collected on all open positions as of June 30, 2026 and new positions created from July 1, 2026
- Members should refer to the NSE FAQs on ASM for detailed provisions: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date of securities shortlisting under ST-ASM Stage I
- **June 30, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margin
- **July 1, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become applicable on open positions and new positions

## Impact Assessment

**ST-ASM Stage I Securities (effective June 30, 2026):**
| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|
| 1 | AEGISLOG | Aegis Logistics Limited | INE208C01025 |
| 2 | INVPRECQ | Investment & Precision Castings Limited | INE155E01016 |
| 3 | SPRL | Sp Refractories Limited | INE0JW501011 |
| 4 | TEAMTECH | Teamtech Formwork Solutions Limited | INE2OL601013 |

Traders holding positions in the above securities will face significantly higher margin requirements (minimum 50%, up to 100%), increasing the cost of carry and potentially triggering forced liquidations for those unable to meet enhanced margin calls. The restriction is likely to reduce liquidity and may cause price volatility in these counters. The measure applies uniformly to all open positions as of June 30, 2026, giving market participants one trading day to assess their exposure before July 1, 2026 implementation.
