## Summary

NSE's Managed Security Services Provider for Market-SOC services, M/s. Aujas Cybersecurity Limited, has officially changed its name to NuSummit Cybersecurity Limited effective July 15, 2025. This follows from the original partnership announced in NSE circular NSE/MSD/66154 dated January 16, 2025. Members are requested to update their records accordingly.

## Key Points

- Aujas Cybersecurity Limited has been renamed to NuSummit Cybersecurity Limited, effective July 15, 2025.
- The company continues to serve as NSE's Managed Security Services Provider for Market-SOC services to SEBI-regulated entities.
- Updated contact details are provided for both NSE and NuSummit personnel across PAN India regions.
- New email for Market-SOC services: market.soc@nusummit.com
- New website: https://cybersecurity.nusummit.com/msoc/

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative name change of NSE's cybersecurity partner.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are requested to take note of the name change and update contact details for Market-SOC service interactions. No formal compliance action is required.

## Important Dates

- **July 15, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Aujas Cybersecurity Limited to NuSummit Cybersecurity Limited.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. Members using Market-SOC services should update their contact records to reflect the new company name and updated contact personnel. Service continuity is expected as the provider entity remains the same under a new name.
