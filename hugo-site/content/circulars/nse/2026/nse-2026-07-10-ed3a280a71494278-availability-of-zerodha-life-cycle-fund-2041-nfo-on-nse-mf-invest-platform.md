## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 NFO, offered by Zerodha Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme covered is Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01216), categorized as an Equity scheme.
- The fund will support ongoing subscriptions and redemptions after reopening.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will also be available.
- Circular issued under NSE's NMF TM Segment (Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/75148, Circular Ref. No: 1488/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/product availability notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure their systems and processes support transactions (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) for this scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 13, 2026 — scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Circular Date: July 10, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational update relevant to NSE members and distributors dealing in mutual fund products via the NSE MF Invest Platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, market structure, or broader compliance obligations, and is primarily informational for facilitating continued investor access to the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 scheme.
