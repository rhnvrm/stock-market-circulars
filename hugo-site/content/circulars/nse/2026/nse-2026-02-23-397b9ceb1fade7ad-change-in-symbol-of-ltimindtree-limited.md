## Summary

NSE has announced that the trading symbol for LTIMindtree Limited will be changed from **LTIM** to **LTM** with effect from February 27, 2026. All exchange members are advised to update their systems and references accordingly.

## Key Points

- The existing symbol **LTIM** will be replaced by the new symbol **LTM**
- The change applies to LTIMindtree Limited listed on NSE
- Effective date is February 27, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE's Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/72948

## Regulatory Changes

The NSE Listing Department has formally notified all exchange members of the symbol change. No change in the underlying company, its securities, or listing status is implied â€” only the trading symbol identifier is being updated.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their trading systems, order management systems (OMS), and back-office systems to reflect the new symbol **LTM** before February 27, 2026
- Market data vendors, data aggregators, and financial platforms should update their symbology databases accordingly
- Investors and traders using symbol-based alerts or watchlists should update their references from LTIM to LTM

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **February 27, 2026**: New symbol LTM becomes effective; old symbol LTIM ceases to be valid

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with moderate operational impact. Traders, brokers, and institutional investors tracking LTIMindtree Limited under the symbol LTIM must update their systems before the effective date to avoid failed orders or missed alerts. Automated trading systems and algorithmic strategies referencing the old symbol LTIM will need to be reconfigured. The change does not affect the company's fundamentals, share price, or listing status.
