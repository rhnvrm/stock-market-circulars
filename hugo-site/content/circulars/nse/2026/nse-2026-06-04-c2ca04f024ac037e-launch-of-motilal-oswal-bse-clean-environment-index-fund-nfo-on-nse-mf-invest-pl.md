## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Motilal Oswal BSE Clean Environment Index Fund NFO by Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 05, 2026 to June 19, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The fund offers two plan options — Direct Growth and Regular Growth — both categorised as equity schemes with allotment on June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 05, 2026 to June 19, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Two plans available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HC6) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HD4)
- Both plans are categorised under the Equity category
- Allotment date for both plans: June 25, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by June 24, 2026 till 06:00 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 24, 2026 till 06:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: NFO opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **June 19, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: NFO closes
- **June 24, 2026 (06:00 pm)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **June 25, 2026**: Allotment date for both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plans

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund NFO launch notification with limited broader market impact. Members facilitating MF transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform should ensure payment processing is completed ahead of the June 24 deadline. The fund tracks the BSE Clean Environment Index, targeting the ESG/clean energy thematic equity segment.
