## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in four non-convertible debt securities (privately placed) effective January 02, 2026. The suspension is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. All four securities are being suspended due to redemption.

## Key Points

- Four non-convertible securities will be suspended from trading effective January 02, 2026
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the securities
- Suspension applies to privately placed debt instruments
- Action taken under NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Securities Affected

1. **Jharkhand Road Projects Implementation Company Limited**
   - ISIN: INE746N07796
   - Suspension Date: 02-Jan-2026
   - Reason: Redemption

2. **Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited**
   - ISIN: INE033L07HC8
   - Suspension Date: 02-Jan-2026
   - Reason: Redemption

3. **Hero FinCorp Limited**
   - ISIN: INE957N07567
   - Suspension Date: 02-Jan-2026
   - Reason: Redemption

4. **IDFC First Bank Limited**
   - ISIN: INE092T08014
   - Suspension Date: 02-Jan-2026
   - Reason: Redemption

## Regulatory Framework

This action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date**: January 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension has minimal market impact as it affects only privately placed non-convertible debt securities that are reaching maturity. This is a routine administrative action following the redemption schedule of these instruments. Trading members should note these ISINs will no longer be available for trading from the suspension date.
