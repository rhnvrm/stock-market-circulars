## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares of Baheti Recycling Industries Limited (Symbol: BAHETI) on the SME EMERGE (Capital Market) segment, effective April 23, 2026. A total of 78,375 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 592.5, have been admitted for trading.

## Key Points

- 78,375 equity shares of Baheti Recycling Industries Limited admitted to dealings on NSE SME EMERGE from April 23, 2026
- Shares allotted on preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 592.50 per share
- ISIN: INE029Q01017 (temporary dematerialized ISIN IN8********* per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012)
- Market lot size: 375 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period applies until October 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes (Symbol: BAHETI, Series: SM) for all trades
- All trading must be in the prescribed market lot of 375 shares
- Lock-in on allotted shares must be observed until October 31, 2026
- ISIN activation subject to SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) for additional issue of shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Circular Date:** April 22, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 23, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 78,375 shares to the tradeable float of Baheti Recycling Industries Limited on the NSE SME EMERGE platform. At the issue price of Rs. 592.50, the total value of newly listed shares is approximately Rs. 4.64 crore. The lock-in until October 31, 2026 restricts immediate liquidity for these preferential allottees. Impact is confined to this SME-listed company and its investors; no broader market implications are anticipated.
