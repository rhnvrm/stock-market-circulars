## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has released the funds payout that was previously withheld for certain trades executed on May 5, 2026. This release follows directions received on May 15, 2026 from the relevant authority, concluding the withholding action initiated under circular NSE/SURV/74066 dated May 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Funds payout withheld for certain trades dated May 5, 2026 has been released effective May 15, 2026
- Release was directed by the relevant authority via directions dated May 15, 2026
- This circular is an update to prior circular NSE/SURV/74066 (May 6, 2026)
- Respective counterparties whose funds were withheld will receive the payout
- Trading members are requested to take note of the development

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular concludes the withholding action described in the earlier circular NSE/SURV/74066 dated May 6, 2026, following receipt of directions from the relevant authority to release the withheld funds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the release of withheld funds for affected counterparties
- For further queries, members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **May 5, 2026**: Date of the trades for which payout was withheld
- **May 6, 2026**: Original circular (NSE/SURV/74066) announcing the withholding of payout
- **May 15, 2026**: Directions received from relevant authority; funds payout released on this date

## Impact Assessment

The release of withheld funds resolves the settlement uncertainty for affected counterparties involved in the May 5, 2026 trades. Trading members with exposure to the relevant counterparties will see their withheld payouts credited. The resolution limits any broader market disruption and restores normal settlement flow for those specific trades. No ongoing compliance burden is imposed on trading members beyond awareness of the update.
