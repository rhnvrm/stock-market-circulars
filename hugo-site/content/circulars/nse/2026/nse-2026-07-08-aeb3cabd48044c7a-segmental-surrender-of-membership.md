## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) has approved the request of SPFL Securities Limited (SEBI registration number INZ000214237) to surrender its trading membership in the Currency Derivatives segment, including Interest Rate Futures (IRF). The surrender approval was granted effective June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- SPFL Securities Limited has surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment (including IRF).
- The surrender request was approved by the Exchange.
- SEBI registration number of the member: INZ000214237.
- Surrender approval date: June 30, 2026.
- All members are requested to take note of this development.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced; this circular documents the administrative approval of a single member's segmental membership surrender under existing NSE membership rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Other trading members should note that SPFL Securities Limited is no longer registered to operate in the Currency Derivatives (including IRF) segment. No action is required from other members beyond taking note.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Surrender approval date for SPFL Securities Limited's Currency Derivatives (including IRF) segment membership.
- July 08, 2026: Circular issued (Ref. No. NSE/COMP/75094, Circular Ref. No. 64/2026).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact, as it pertains solely to the membership status of a single trading member in one derivatives segment. There is no direct effect on listed securities, trading operations, or investors at large. Other members and market participants should simply note that SPFL Securities Limited can no longer transact in the Currency Derivatives (including IRF) segment.
