## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares of Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCAPITAL), admitted for dealing on the Capital Market segment effective July 06, 2026. The shares were allotted across three tranches under a preferential issue on June 23, 2026, totaling 112,353,236 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each, at an issue price of Rs. 356.02 per share.

## Key Points

- Three tranches of equity shares allotted under preferential issue to Aditya Birla Capital Limited are being listed:
  - Tranche 1: 80,894,331 shares (distinctive numbers 2621853065 to 2702747395), lock-in until 11-Jan-2028
  - Tranche 2: 25,841,244 shares (distinctive numbers 2708365057 to 2734206300), lock-in until 11-Jan-2027
  - Tranche 3: 5,617,661 shares (distinctive numbers 2702747396 to 2708365056), lock-in until 11-Jan-2028
- Symbol: ABCAPITAL, Series: EQ, ISIN: INE674K01013
- Face value Rs. 10/-, Paid-up value Rs. 10/-, Issue price Rs. 356.02
- Market lot for all tranches: 1
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- Shares are currently credited under a temporary ISIN pending activation, per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize the additional securities and lot sizes for trading purposes, identifying them only by the designated security codes specified in the circular.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (all tranches): June 23, 2026
- Effective/listing date: July 06, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: January 11, 2027 (Tranche 2) and January 11, 2028 (Tranches 1 and 3)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases ABCAPITAL's total outstanding share count by over 112 million shares, which may have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders' earnings per share, though the shares carry lock-in restrictions limiting immediate tradeable float. The preferential allotment reflects capital raised at Rs. 356.02 per share. Market impact is expected to be limited in the near term given the lock-in periods, but investors should track the eventual unlock dates for potential supply overhang.
