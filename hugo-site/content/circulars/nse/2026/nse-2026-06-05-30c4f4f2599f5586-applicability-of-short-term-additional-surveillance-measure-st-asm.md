## Summary

NSE has invoked the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, shortlisting six securities under Stage I effective June 08, 2026 (margin applicable from June 09, 2026). No securities have been added to Stage II, and no securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II under this circular.

## Key Points

- Six securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I effective June 08, 2026
- Applicable margin for Stage I: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- No securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage II
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Upon exit from framework, price band reverts to pre-shortlisting level (unless subject to another surveillance measure)
- Shortlisting is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to ST-ASM Stage I are subject to enhanced margin requirements per the ST-ASM framework established via circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018; December 04, 2020; April 28, 2022; September 25, 2023; and September 20, 2024 respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 50% margin (or existing margin, whichever is higher, maximum 100%) on all open positions in Stage I securities as on June 08, 2026
- The enhanced margin applies to all new positions created from June 09, 2026
- Members should monitor for any subsequent circulars regarding movement of these securities between stages or removal from the framework

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 08, 2026**: Securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I (reference date for open positions)
- **June 09, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become effective for open positions (as on June 08, 2026) and all new positions

**Affected Securities (ST-ASM Stage I):**

Traders and investors holding positions in these six securities will face increased margin requirements from June 09, 2026. This may trigger margin calls for leveraged positions and could reduce liquidity in these counters as the cost of holding positions increases. The 50% minimum margin (capped at 100%) under Stage I is a significant constraint, particularly for retail participants with limited capital. Participants should review their positions in these securities ahead of June 08, 2026 and ensure adequate margins are maintained.
