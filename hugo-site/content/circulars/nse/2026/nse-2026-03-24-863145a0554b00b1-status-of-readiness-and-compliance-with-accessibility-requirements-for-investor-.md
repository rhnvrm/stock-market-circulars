## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder circular requiring all Trading Members to submit their status of readiness and compliance with accessibility requirements for investor-facing digital platforms by **March 31, 2026**. This is mandated under SEBI Circular No. HO/13/19/13(2)2025-ITD-1_VIAP/I/187/2025 dated December 8, 2025, and aligns with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD) Act, 2016.

## Key Points

- All Trading Members must submit compliance status via the Member Portal (New ENIT > Compliance > Compliance with RPwD Act, 2016 > RPwD Submission)
- Members engaged only in Proprietary Trading can submit a simplified declaration
- Members with client-facing business must report compliance status for their website and mobile applications
- Members must declare whether they have appointed an IAAP Certified Accessibility Professional as Auditor
- Submission deadline is March 31, 2026
- MIS/reporting functionality is available for members to review and download their submissions

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references a series of prior NSE circulars (NSE/COMP/71706, 70464, 69938, 69870, 69612) and SEBI's December 2025 circular on mandatory accessibility compliance for regulated entities under the RPwD Act, 2016. No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a compliance reminder with a firm deadline.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Proprietary-only Trading Members:** Select "Yes" for proprietary trading flag and submit via ENIT portal
- **Members with client business:** Must report compliance status for all digital platforms (website URLs and mobile applications previously submitted to the Exchange)
- **IAAP Auditor Appointment:** Members must declare if an IAAP Certified Accessibility Professional has been appointed as auditor; this field can be updated later from "No" to "Yes" once an auditor is appointed
- **Digital Platform details** once submitted cannot be changed; only the IAAP auditor response can be updated
- Submission portal: Member Portal – New ENIT > Compliance > Compliance with RPwD Act, 2016 > RPwD Submission

## Important Dates

- **March 31, 2026** – Deadline for submitting readiness and compliance status for investor-facing digital platforms
- **December 8, 2025** – SEBI Circular mandating compliance (HO/13/19/13(2)2025-ITD-1_VIAP/I/187/2025)
- **March 24, 2026** – Date of this NSE circular (NSE/COMP/73451, Circular Ref. No. 22/2026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily impacts Trading Members who operate investor-facing digital platforms (websites and mobile applications). Members must ensure their platforms meet accessibility standards under the RPwD Act, 2016 and complete the ENIT portal submission before the deadline. Non-compliance could result in regulatory action from NSE/SEBI. There is no direct impact on market trading, stock prices, or listed securities. The operational burden is moderate — members need to assess platform accessibility, potentially appoint an IAAP-certified auditor, and complete the online submission.
