## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL), formerly the National Securities Clearing Corporation Ltd., has notified the settlement calendar for the Debt Segment for the month of August 2026, in pursuance of Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997). The calendar covers both Debt Settlement (Settlement Type 'I') under Annexure 'A' and Debt Auction Settlement (Settlement Type '2') under Annexure 'B', both following a rolling T+1 settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Circular applies to all Members, Custodians, and PCMs (Professional Clearing Members) operating in the Debt Segment.
- The settlement schedule is issued under Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).
- Two separate settlement calendars are provided: Debt Settlement (Type 'I') and Debt Auction Settlement (Type '2').
- Both settlement types follow a rolling T+1 (Trade Date + 1 business day) cycle.
- Settlement numbers run from 2026144 through 2026163 for Debt Settlement, and 2026144 through 2026163 for Auction Debt Settlement.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine periodic notification of the operational settlement calendar as mandated under existing NSE Clearing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, Custodians, and PCMs must align their back-office and funds/securities pay-in/pay-out processes with the published trade date and settlement date schedule for both Debt Settlement and Debt Auction Settlement segments during August 2026.
