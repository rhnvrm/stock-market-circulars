## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the 360 ONE MSCI India ETF NFO, offered by 360 Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 18, 2026 to May 20, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Allotment is scheduled for May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO window: May 18, 2026 to May 20, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two scheme variants available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF579M01BP5) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF579M01BP5)
- Both schemes are categorized as Equity
- Allotment date: May 22, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by May 20, 2026, 11:59 PM
- Contact: support@nseinvest.com | Toll Free: 1800 2100 940

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 20, 2026, 11:59 PM.

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **May 20, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes; fund transfer deadline
- **May 22, 2026**: Allotment date for both scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO announcement with low market impact. It provides NSE members access to a new MSCI India-tracking ETF from 360 Mutual Fund. The offering is limited to the NSE MF Invest Platform and affects only members who facilitate mutual fund transactions for their clients.
