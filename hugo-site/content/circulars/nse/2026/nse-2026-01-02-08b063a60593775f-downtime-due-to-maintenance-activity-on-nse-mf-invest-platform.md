## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance activity on the NSE MF Invest Platform that will result in system downtime on January 02-03, 2026. The platform will be unavailable for a 2-hour window from 22:00 hrs on January 02, 2026 to 00:00 hrs on January 03, 2026. Members are advised to refrain from all activities and transactions during this period.

## Key Points

- NSE MF Invest Platform will be unavailable due to maintenance
- Downtime window: January 02, 2026, 22:00 hrs to January 03, 2026, 00:00 hrs (2 hours)
- All members must refrain from carrying out activities and transactions during this time
- Members using API connections should inform their technical teams and vendors
- Support available via toll-free number 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must suspend all activities and transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified downtime window
- Members connecting via API must notify their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled maintenance
- Members should plan their mutual fund activities accordingly to avoid disruption

## Important Dates

- **Maintenance Start**: January 02, 2026 at 22:00 hrs
- **Maintenance End**: January 03, 2026 at 00:00 hrs
- **Duration**: 2 hours

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this maintenance activity is minimal as it occurs during late evening hours (10 PM to midnight) when mutual fund transaction volumes are typically low. The 2-hour maintenance window is relatively short and scheduled outside regular market hours. Members have been given advance notice to plan their activities accordingly. API-connected members need to coordinate with their technical teams to ensure systems are not attempting transactions during the downtime window.
