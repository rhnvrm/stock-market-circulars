## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has informed all members that Le Merite Exports Limited (symbol: LEMERITE) will undergo a face value split effective May 29, 2026. The face value and paid-up value of the equity shares will be reduced from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share.

## Key Points

- Le Merite Exports Limited (LEMERITE) is undergoing a 5:1 face value split (Rs. 10 to Rs. 2)
- Both the face value and paid-up value of equity shares are being changed
- The circular is issued by NSE's Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/74348 (Circular Ref: 0826/2026)
- Signed by Ruchi Jaiswal, Manager, on behalf of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

The face value and paid-up value of equity shares of Le Merite Exports Limited will change as follows:

| Company | Symbol | Existing Face Value & Paid-up Value (Rs.) | New Face Value & Paid-up Value (Rs.) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Le Merite Exports Limited | LEMERITE | 10 | 2 |

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are informed to take note of the face value change for LEMERITE effective May 29, 2026
- Systems and records referencing LEMERITE's face value should be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 29, 2026 (face value and paid-up value change takes effect)

## Impact Assessment

The face value split from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 represents a 5:1 split ratio. Shareholders will receive 5 shares for every 1 share held, with the market price per share adjusting proportionally downward. This typically improves liquidity and affordability of the stock for retail investors. Futures and options contracts on LEMERITE, if any, may also require adjustment. Traders and investors holding positions in LEMERITE should account for the change in face value when evaluating per-share metrics.
