## Summary

NSE has announced that Wipro Limited will conduct a buyback offer via the Exchange's web-based bidding platform under the Tender Offer Scheme (as per NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022). The fixed-price buyback is set at Rs. 250 per share for up to 60,00,00,000 (60 crore) equity shares, with live bidding from June 11 to June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Wipro Limited (NSE symbol: WIPRO, Series: BB, ISIN: INE075A01022) is conducting a buyback under the Tender Offer Scheme
- Offer price is fixed at Rs. 250 per share; face value is Re. 2 per share
- Issue size is up to 60,00,00,000 equity shares
- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Members already on the web-based IPO bidding platform can use existing credentials at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- Members without access must log in to the admin terminal (Adminxxxxx, where xxxxx is Member Code) to create branches and users
- Merchant Banker: JM Financial Limited; Registrar: Kfin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer follows the existing Tender Offer Scheme framework established under NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to participate must ensure access to the web-based bidding platform at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- Members without existing access must set up branches and users via the admin terminal before the bidding window opens
- Custodial confirmations must be completed within the specified time windows each day during the issue period
- Custodial confirmation on issue closure day extends to 4.00 p.m.

## Important Dates

- **Issue Period:** June 11, 2026 to June 17, 2026
- **Daily Bidding Time:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Custodial Confirmation (regular days):** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Custodial Confirmation (issue closure day):** 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact event for WIPRO shareholders and trading members. The buyback at Rs. 250 per share for up to 60 crore shares represents a significant capital return to shareholders. Eight investor categories are eligible to participate including FIIs, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Banks & FIs, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors. Trading members must prepare their systems in advance to ensure smooth participation during the bidding window.
