## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has notified all members that Laurel Securities Pvt. Ltd (SEBI Registration No. INZ000216837) has been approved to surrender its membership in the Interest Rate Futures Segment. The surrender was approved on March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Member: Laurel Securities Pvt. Ltd
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000216837
- Surrendered Segment: Interest Rate Futures Segment
- Surrender Approval Date: March 5, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Compliance Department (Circular Ref. No: 16/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes. This is an administrative action specific to the surrendering member's participation in the Interest Rate Futures Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of Laurel Securities Pvt. Ltd's exit from the Interest Rate Futures Segment. No specific action is required from other members beyond acknowledging this notice.

## Important Dates

- Surrender Approval Date: March 5, 2026
- Circular Date: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The exit of a single member from the Interest Rate Futures Segment is an isolated administrative event. Other market participants and segment operations are unaffected. Counterparties or clients of Laurel Securities in this segment should make alternative arrangements if applicable.
