## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75177, Circular Ref. No: 1141/2026) dated July 14, 2026, informing members of the Exchange about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Kusumgar Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO).

## Key Points

- Company Name: Kusumgar Limited
- Trading Symbol: KUSUMGAR
- ISIN: INE0ISX01025
- The actual date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational circular from the Listing Department notifying members of an upcoming security listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the new symbol and ISIN for Kusumgar Limited in preparation for trading once the listing date is confirmed. No immediate action is required until the listing date circular is issued.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 14, 2026
- Listing Date: To be notified via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited immediate market impact as it only announces the forthcoming listing without specifying a trading start date. Once listed, Kusumgar Limited's shares will become available for trading on NSE under the symbol KUSUMGAR, expanding the exchange's listed universe. Members and investors should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the exact listing date.
