## Summary

NSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework effective June 09, 2026. Vadivarhe Speciality Chemicals Limited (VSCL) is newly included under Stage I, while Fujiyama Power Systems Limited (UTLSOLAR) is moved from a lower stage to Stage IV. Stage IV triggers a shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade settlement (Series: BE) effective June 11, 2026, along with a 100% margin requirement.

## Key Points

- VSCL (Vadivarhe Speciality Chemicals Limited, ISIN: INE551Q01028) added to Long Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 09, 2026
- UTLSOLAR (Fujiyama Power Systems Limited, ISIN: INE12UR01024) moved to Long Term ASM Stage IV w.e.f. June 09, 2026
- Stage IV securities shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE series) effective June 11, 2026
- 100% margin applicable from June 11, 2026 on all open positions as on June 10, 2026 and new positions from June 11, 2026
- UTLSOLAR qualifies under Criteria VII (Stage IV shift); it satisfies criteria at BSE
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage III in this update
- ASM shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

The Long Term ASM framework is being updated per provisions established under Exchange Circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) are moved from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade settlement on a T+3 basis from the date of circular issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply 100% margin on all open positions in Stage IV securities (UTLSOLAR) as on June 10, 2026 and on new positions from June 11, 2026 onwards
- Trading members must ensure proper settlement handling as UTLSOLAR transitions from EQ series to BE series on June 11, 2026
- ASM framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of VSCL in Long Term ASM Stage I and UTLSOLAR in Stage IV
- **June 10, 2026**: Last date for open positions in UTLSOLAR under current margin structure
- **June 11, 2026**: 100% margin applicable on UTLSOLAR; shift of UTLSOLAR from EQ (Rolling Settlement) to BE (Trade-for-Trade) series effective

## Impact Assessment

VSCL traders face a 100% margin requirement under Stage I from June 11, 2026, significantly increasing capital requirements for holding positions. UTLSOLAR faces higher impact: the Stage IV classification and Trade-for-Trade shift eliminates netting benefits and restricts speculative activity, which typically leads to reduced liquidity and higher volatility in the scrip. Existing long/short holders in UTLSOLAR must ensure adequate margins by June 10, 2026. The move to BE series means intraday trading becomes unavailable, and each trade must result in delivery, making the stock less accessible for short-term traders.
