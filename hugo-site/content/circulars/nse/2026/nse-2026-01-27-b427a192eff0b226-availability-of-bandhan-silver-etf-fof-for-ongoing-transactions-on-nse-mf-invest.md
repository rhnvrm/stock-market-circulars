## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Bandhan Silver ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) from Bandhan Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective January 28, 2026. Both Regular and Direct plan variants with Growth option will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Bandhan Silver ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from January 28, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities included: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KM1) and Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KN9)
- Both schemes categorized under Equity category
- Issued by NMF TM Segment Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification about fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the availability of investment options.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - Bandhan Silver ETF FOF available for ongoing transactions
- **Circular Date:** January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme on the NSE platform. It provides investors with additional investment options in silver-focused ETF funds of funds through both regular and direct plans. The availability of systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) enhances investment flexibility for retail and institutional investors interested in silver exposure through the fund-of-fund route.
