## Summary

NSE has announced that NFP Sampoorna Foods Limited (symbol: NFPSAMPOOR), which was listed under the SME IPO segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (series: ST) to the Rolling segment (series: SM) with effect from June 09, 2026. This transition follows the standard post-IPO regulatory process under SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- NFPSAMPOOR will move from trade for trade segment (series ST) to rolling segment (series SM) effective June 09, 2026.
- The transfer is pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.
- This circular is a follow-up to NSE circular NSE/CML/74362 dated May 22, 2026.
- The circular was issued by the NSE Listing department under ref. no. NSE/CML/74380 (Circular Ref. No: 0837/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular implements the existing SEBI framework (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012) governing the migration of newly listed SME IPO securities from the trade-for-trade segment to normal rolling settlement after the mandatory initial period.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are informed to take note of the segment change for NFPSAMPOOR effective June 09, 2026. Members should update their trading systems to reflect the series change from ST to SM for this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: Previous NSE circular NSE/CML/74362 issued regarding this matter.
- **May 25, 2026**: Date of this circular (NSE/CML/74380).
- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from trade for trade (series ST) to rolling segment (series SM).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-IPO transition that enables normal rolling settlement trading in NFPSAMPOOR shares. The move from trade-for-trade to rolling segment typically improves liquidity as intraday trading and netting of positions becomes possible. Investors and traders in this SME stock should be aware that settlement mechanics will change on June 09, 2026.
