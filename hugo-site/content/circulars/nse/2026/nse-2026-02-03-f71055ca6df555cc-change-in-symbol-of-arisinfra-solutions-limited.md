## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced a change in the trading symbol for Arisinfra Solutions Limited. The symbol will change from ARISINFRA to ARIS effective February 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Arisinfra Solutions Limited
- Current Symbol: ARISINFRA
- New Symbol: ARIS
- Effective Date: February 09, 2026
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/72592, Circular Ref. No: 0166/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative update to the company's trading symbol on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems to reflect the new symbol ARIS for Arisinfra Solutions Limited from February 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is a routine symbol change that will require system updates by exchange members and trading platforms. There is no impact on the company's operations, share ownership, or investor rights. Trading will continue normally under the new symbol from the effective date.
