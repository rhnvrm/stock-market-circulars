## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has informed all members that the trading symbol for LTIMindtree Limited in the Futures & Options segment will change from **LTIM** to **LTM**, effective February 27, 2026. This follows Circular No. 28/2026 (Download No. NSE/FAOP/72935) issued on February 24, 2026. All existing open positions in F&O contracts under symbol LTIM will be automatically carried forward under the new symbol LTM.

## Key Points

- The symbol for LTIMindtree Limited in the F&O segment changes from **LTIM** to **LTM** effective February 27, 2026.
- All open futures and options positions in LTIM will be carried forward under the new symbol LTM without any disruption.
- Reports dated February 26, 2026 will still reference the old symbol LTIM.
- From February 27, 2026 onwards, all reports and portfolio files will reflect the new symbol LTM.
- This circular is issued by NSE Clearing Limited under the Futures & Options segment (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/72983, Circular Ref. No: 27/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

The symbol change is mandated by NSE Clearing Limited in pursuance of NSE Circular No. 28/2026 (NSE/FAOP/72935) dated February 24, 2026. The change is administrative in nature â€” the underlying company (LTIMindtree Limited) and all contract specifications remain unchanged; only the ticker symbol is being updated.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must update their internal systems, trading terminals, and reporting workflows to recognize the new symbol **LTM** for LTIMindtree Limited effective February 27, 2026.
- Members should ensure that portfolio files submitted on or after February 27, 2026 use the new symbol LTM.
- Members should note the transitional period: February 26, 2026 reports will still carry the old symbol LTIM.
- For queries, members may contact NSE Clearing at **18002660050 (IVR Option 2)** or email **fao_clearing_ops@nsccl.co.in**.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to medium. This is a purely administrative symbol change with no impact on contract terms, margin requirements, or settlement procedures. All open F&O positions are automatically migrated to the new symbol.

**Operational Impact:** Medium. Members need to update trading systems, back-office software, risk management tools, and any automated systems that reference the symbol LTIM to avoid reconciliation issues or order rejections post-February 27, 2026.

**Affected Participants:** All members active in LTIMindtree F&O contracts, including proprietary traders, institutional clients, and retail participants trading via brokers.
