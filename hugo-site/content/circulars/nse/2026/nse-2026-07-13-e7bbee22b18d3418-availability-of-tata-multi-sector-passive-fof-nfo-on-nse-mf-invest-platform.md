## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Tata Multi Sector Passive FOF NFO, managed by Tata Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme is offered by Tata Mutual Fund under the Multi Sector Passive FOF (Fund of Funds) category.
- Six plan/option variants are covered, spanning Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout/Reinvestment) options.
- All variants are classified under the Equity category.
- Facilities enabled include ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational circular enabling platform-level trading facilities for a specific fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members offering NSE MF Invest Platform services should update their systems to reflect availability of the Tata Multi Sector Passive FOF scheme codes/ISINs for ongoing transactions from the effective date, and inform investors accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for reopening of subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP: July 14, 2026
- Circular date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact and is relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors, members, and investors using the NSE MF Invest Platform for transacting in this specific Tata Mutual Fund scheme. It does not affect listed equities or broader market operations.
