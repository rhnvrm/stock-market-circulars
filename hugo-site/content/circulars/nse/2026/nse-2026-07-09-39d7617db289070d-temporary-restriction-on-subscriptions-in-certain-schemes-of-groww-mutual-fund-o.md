## Summary

NSE has notified members that, as communicated by Groww Mutual Fund, fresh subscriptions (including lumpsum purchases, switches, and new SIP/STP registrations) will be temporarily restricted in certain plans/options of multiple Groww Mutual Fund schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective July 10, 2026. The restriction primarily targets the Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) Reinvestment and IDCW Payout options, along with specific frequency-based IDCW variants, across a wide list of Groww index funds, ETF FOFs, and other schemes.

## Key Points

- Fresh subscriptions (Lumpsum purchase, Switches, and fresh SIP/STP registration) restricted in certain Groww Mutual Fund schemes effective July 10, 2026.
- Fresh subscription discontinued entirely in IDCW Reinvestment and IDCW Payout Options for 21 listed schemes, including Groww Nifty 50 Index Fund, Groww Nifty Next 50 Index Fund, Groww Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund, Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund, various sectoral/thematic ETF FOFs (BSE Hospitals ETF FOF, BSE Power ETF FOF, Nifty PSU Bank Index Fund, Nifty India Defence ETF FOF, Gold ETF FOF, Silver ETF FOF, etc.), and Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF.
- For a separate set of schemes (Groww Multicap Fund, Groww Banking & Financial Services Fund, Groww Small cap Fund, and others per the addendum), fresh subscription is suspended only under the IDCW Reinvestment Facility; subscriptions continue to be accepted under the IDCW Payout Facility.
- Fresh subscription in Monthly, Quarterly, and Half-yearly frequency variants under IDCW Reinvestment and IDCW Payout options is discontinued for both Direct and Regular plans.
- Change is implemented via Notice cum Addendum No. 13/2026-2027 issued by Groww Asset Management Ltd., approved by Groww Trustee Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational/product-level change communicated by the mutual fund (Groww Mutual Fund) and relayed by NSE to its members via circular NSE/NMFTM/75121 (Ref. No. 1482/2026). It amends the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of the affected schemes to suspend fresh subscriptions in specific IDCW options.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members dealing in the NSE MF Invest Platform must update their systems/processes to reject fresh lumpsum purchases, switches, and new SIP/STP registrations in the affected IDCW options of the listed Groww Mutual Fund schemes from July 10, 2026.
- Members should inform clients holding or intending to invest in these schemes about the restricted options and redirect eligible fresh subscriptions to the IDCW Payout Facility where applicable.
- Members should refer to the full Notice cum Addendum issued by Groww Mutual Fund (enclosed with the circular) for the complete list of affected schemes and options.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 10, 2026 — restriction on fresh subscriptions begins.
- Circular Date: July 09, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and limited to investors and distributors using the NSE MF Invest Platform for the specified Groww Mutual Fund schemes. Existing investors are not affected for redemptions or already-held units; the restriction applies only to fresh subscriptions in specific IDCW options. No direct impact on listed equity trading or broader market operations is expected, but mutual fund distributors and RTAs handling Groww schemes need to adjust subscription processing to avoid failed transactions from July 10, 2026 onward.
