## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Vedanta Power Limited (formerly Talwandi Sabo Power Limited), symbol VEDPOWER, effective June 15, 2026. The shares are being listed pursuant to an NCLT-approved Scheme of Arrangement involving the demerger of Vedanta Limited into four resulting companies. Trading will commence in Series BE (Trade for Trade) and will subsequently be shifted to Series EQ as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** VEDPOWER on NSE Capital Market segment
- **Series:** BE – Trade for Trade (to be shifted to EQ subsequently per SEBI circular SEBI/CIR/ISD/1/2010 dated September 2, 2010)
- **ISIN:** INE694L01019
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Number of Securities:** 3,910,388,057 (approx. 3.91 billion shares)
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 3,910,388,057
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- Security will participate in the pre-open session as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The listing is pursuant to the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench, Court V order dated January 09, 2026, approving the Scheme of Arrangement between Vedanta Limited (Demerged Company) and four resulting companies:
1. Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (Resulting Company 1)
2. Vedanta Power Limited, formerly Talwandi Sabo Power Limited (Resulting Company 2)
3. Vedanta Oil & Gas Limited, formerly MALCO Energy Limited (Resulting Company 3)
4. Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (Resulting Company 4)

The scheme was approved under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/brokers must use the designated symbol VEDPOWER and Series BE for trading from June 15, 2026.
- Trading in the security must be in the specified lot size of 1.
- The security will be subject to Trade for Trade settlement rules under Series BE until shifted to Series EQ.
- Members should note pre-open session applicability for this security.

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** January 09, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact listing event involving a major corporate demerger. With approximately 3.91 billion shares being listed, VEDPOWER represents one of the larger new listings on NSE. The initial Trade for Trade (Series BE) designation means all trades must result in delivery — intraday squaring off is not permitted — which may limit initial liquidity but protects against excessive speculation on day one. Investors holding shares allotted under the Scheme of Arrangement can begin trading on June 15, 2026. The eventual migration to Series EQ will allow normal trading. Registrar and Share Transfer Agent is KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad.
