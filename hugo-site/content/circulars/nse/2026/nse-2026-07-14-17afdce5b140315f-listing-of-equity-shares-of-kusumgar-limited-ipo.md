## Summary

NSE has disseminated the post-issue Shareholding Pattern (Holding of Specified Securities) of Kusumgar Limited in connection with the listing of its equity shares following its Initial Public Offering (IPO), effective 14 July 2026. The disclosure is filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Key Points

- Filing relates to KUSUMGAR LIMITED equity shares, post-issue basis, as of 14.07.2026.
- The company has not issued partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or depository receipts against its shares.
- The company has outstanding ESOPs (Employee Stock Options).
- Certain shares of the company are under lock-in.
- No promoter shares are encumbered by way of pledge, non-disposal undertaking, or other means.
- The company does not have differential voting rights shares.
- The company has a significant beneficial owner as per the declaration.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this filing; it is a standard post-listing disclosure required under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations for newly listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

As a newly listed entity, Kusumgar Limited is required to submit periodic shareholding pattern disclosures to the stock exchange, including details of promoter holdings, public holdings, locked-in shares, ESOPs, and beneficial ownership, in the prescribed format.

## Important Dates

- Effective/reporting date: 14 July 2026 (post-issue shareholding pattern).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing-related compliance disclosure providing transparency on the ownership structure of Kusumgar Limited immediately after its IPO listing. It is primarily relevant to investors and analysts tracking the stock's promoter holding, lock-in status, and beneficial ownership, with limited broader market impact.
