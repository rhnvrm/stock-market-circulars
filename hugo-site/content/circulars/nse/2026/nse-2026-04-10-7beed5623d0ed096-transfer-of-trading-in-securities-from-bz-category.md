## Summary

NSE has announced the transfer of trading in BF Utilities Limited (Symbol: BFUTILITIE) from the 'BZ' series to the 'EQ' series, effective April 21, 2026. This move is pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (last updated January 30, 2026).

## Key Points

- BFUTILITIE (BF Utilities Limited) will be transferred from the BZ series to the EQ series.
- The transfer is effective from **April 21, 2026**.
- The action is taken in accordance with the SEBI Master Circular on surveillance measures (originally SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024).
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73702, Circular Ref. No: 0607/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The transfer is governed by SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 issued July 11, 2023 and last updated January 30, 2026. The BZ (Trade-for-Trade, Z category) series typically carries additional surveillance restrictions; migration to EQ indicates the company has met the conditions for restoration to normal trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed to update their systems and client communications to reflect the series change for BFUTILITIE from April 21, 2026.
- No specific action required from investors, but brokers should note the series change for order routing and reporting purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 21, 2026 — BFUTILITIE moves from BZ to EQ series.

## Impact Assessment

Moving BFUTILITIE from BZ to EQ is a positive development for the stock. The BZ series subjects securities to trade-for-trade settlement and heightened surveillance, which can restrict liquidity and investor participation. Restoration to the EQ series allows normal rolling settlement (T+1), potentially improving liquidity and broadening the eligible investor base. Existing holders may see improved tradability. The impact is confined to participants in BF Utilities Limited.
