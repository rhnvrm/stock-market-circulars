## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies — Muthoot Finance Limited and Akme Fintrade (India) Limited — on the capital market segment. The securities are effective for trading from April 22, 2026, under designated symbols and lot sizes as specified.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective April 22, 2026
- Muthoot Finance Limited: 8.45% NCD maturing June 26, 2029 (Symbol: 845MFL29, Series: N0, Lot: 1, ISIN: INE414G07JX2)
- Akme Fintrade (India) Limited: 11.25% NCD maturing April 20, 2029 (Symbol: 1125AFIL29, Series: N0, Lot: 1, ISIN: INE916Y07081)
- Market lot size for both securities is 1
- Securities must be identified and traded only by their designated codes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Circular is issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities and lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes (845MFL29 and 1125AFIL29) for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (1 unit per lot for both securities)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 22, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)
- **Muthoot Finance NCD Maturity:** June 26, 2029
- **Akme Fintrade NCD Maturity:** April 20, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with low market impact. It provides NSE members with operational details needed to trade two newly listed privately placed debt instruments. Investors in Muthoot Finance Limited (8.45% coupon) and Akme Fintrade India Limited (11.25% coupon) NCDs can now trade these securities on the NSE capital market segment from April 22, 2026. The higher coupon on Akme Fintrade reflects its smaller, higher-risk profile compared to the established NBFC Muthoot Finance.
