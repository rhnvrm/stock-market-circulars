## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the list of securities under Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for Companies as per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective July 23, 2026, Neueon Corporation Limited (Symbol: NEUEON, ISIN: INE333I01044) has been placed under ASM IBC Stage I. No securities have been moved between stages or excluded from the framework in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Neueon Corporation Limited (NEUEON) added to ASM for Companies under IBC – Stage I, effective July 23, 2026.
- No securities were moved from Stage I to Stage II or vice versa in this update.
- No securities were excluded from the ASM IBC framework in this cycle.
- The consolidated ASM (IBC) list currently includes AGS Transact Technologies (AGSTRA), CLC Industries (CLCIND), Gensol Engineering (GENSOL), Impex Ferro Tech (IMPEXFERRO), and now Neueon Corporation (NEUEON), all under Stage I.
- The ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM IBC is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is an update under the existing ASM IBC framework established via earlier circulars (NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018; NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022; NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023; and NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine periodic shortlisting update identifying securities meeting IBC-related ASM criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must note the updated ASM status for Neueon Corporation Limited and apply the applicable trading restrictions and surveillance measures associated with ASM Stage I classification. Members should ensure client communications and risk management systems reflect this change ahead of the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 23, 2026 – Neueon Corporation Limited placed under ASM IBC Stage I.

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of Neueon Corporation Limited in ASM Stage I will likely result in additional trading restrictions such as higher margin requirements and price band constraints for the stock, potentially reducing liquidity and increasing volatility for NEUEON. The impact is company-specific and does not have broader market-wide implications, as no other securities were added, excluded, or moved between stages in this circular.
