## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of several privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 16, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The listed instruments include fresh issues of commercial papers (CPs) from Minda Corporation Limited and CEAT Limited, and other debt securities (non-convertible debentures) from Nomura Fixed Income Securities Limited, Akme Fintrade (India) Limited, Globe Capital Market Limited, and Globe Fincap Limited.

## Key Points

- **Minda Corporation Limited**: Commercial Paper "MCL CP 22/09/26 Sr 3" (ISIN INE842C14222), 2,000 units, face value ₹500,000, issue price ₹493,378, allotted 15-Jul-2026, redemption 22-Sep-2026.
- **CEAT Limited**: Commercial Paper "CL CP 14/08/26" (ISIN INE482A14GT2), 1,000 units, face value ₹500,000, issue price ₹497,343, allotted 15-Jul-2026, redemption 14-Aug-2026.
- **Nomura Fixed Income Securities Limited**: Debenture "NOM 8.20% 2028 Sr 01" (ISIN INE127K08025), 30,000 units, face value ₹100,000, coupon 8.20%, allotted 14-Jul-2026, redemption 11-Aug-2028, call option date 13-Aug-2027; multiple issue prices applied across tranches (₹100,040 and ₹100,000).
- **Akme Fintrade (India) Limited**: Debenture "AFIL 11.50% 2028" (ISIN INE916Y07099), 25,000 units, face value ₹10,000, coupon 11.50%, allotted 14-Jul-2026, redemption 14-Jul-2028.
- **Globe Capital Market Limited**: Debenture "GCML 9.38% 2028 Sr III" (ISIN INEO81J07036), 20,000 units, face value ₹100,000, coupon 9.38%, allotted 14-Jul-2026, redemption 14-Sep-2028.
- **Globe Fincap Limited**: Debenture "GFL 9.38% 2029 Sr III" (ISIN INE013U07010), 10,000 units, face value ₹100,000, coupon 9.38%, allotted 14-Jul-2026 (details partially available in source).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations governing admission of privately placed securities to the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from trading members beyond noting the new security codes and ISINs for the purposes of dealing in these instruments on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective date of listing**: July 16, 2026
- **Minda Corporation CP**: Allotment 15-Jul-2026, Redemption 22-Sep-2026
- **CEAT Limited CP**: Allotment 15-Jul-2026, Redemption 14-Aug-2026
- **Nomura Fixed Income Securities NCD**: Allotment 14-Jul-2026, Call Option 13-Aug-2027, Redemption 11-Aug-2028
- **Akme Fintrade NCD**: Allotment 14-Jul-2026, Redemption 14-Jul-2028
- **Globe Capital Market NCD**: Allotment 14-Jul-2026, Redemption 14-Sep-2028

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market-wide impact and primarily concerns debt market participants, institutional investors, and bondholders dealing in these specific instruments. It provides transparency on new debt issuances and their trading eligibility on NSE's Debt Market Segment, supporting price discovery and secondary market liquidity for these privately placed securities. No impact on equity trading of the underlying issuers is expected from this listing notification alone.
