## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members of the Exchange about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Knack Packaging Limited, which is going public via an IPO. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Knack Packaging Limited
- Trading Symbol: KNACK
- ISIN: INE17QZ01016
- Circular Ref. No: 1097/2026
- Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75077

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under the Listing Department.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the new symbol and ISIN for Knack Packaging Limited in preparation for trading once listing commences.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 07, 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains solely to the onboarding of a new listed entity (Knack Packaging Limited) following its IPO. Impact is primarily relevant to investors and members trading in the new symbol KNACK once listing is confirmed.
