## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued instructions for early pay-in (EPI) of securities of Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Limited (JLHL, ISIN INE682M01012) in connection with a face value split (sub-division) from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share. The record date and ex-date for the corporate action are both July 24, 2026. Members and custodians undertaking early pay-in during the transition period must use the old/existing ISIN without adjusting for the split ratio, for trades falling in specified settlement numbers.

## Key Points

- Applies to Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Limited (Symbol: JLHL, existing ISIN: INE682M01012).
- Corporate action: Face Value Split (Sub-Division) from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share.
- Record Date and Ex Date: July 24, 2026.
- For EPI on July 24, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026138) and July 27, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026138 & 2026139), members must pay in using the old/existing ISIN without adjusting for the conversion ratio.
- Example: for a sale quantity of 10 shares (pre-split), early pay-in in the depository should be made for 2 shares in the old ISIN.
- Custodians using the pool account for early pay-in must upload client-wise EPI allocation details based on actual traded (pre-split) quantity, not the adjusted quantity.
- In case of early pay-in through block mechanism, per NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022, the client-wise early pay-in allocation file should not be uploaded.
- After the record date, members/custodians must execute pay-in and early pay-in instructions in the New ISIN.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rule is introduced; this circular operationalizes the existing early pay-in and margin exemption framework (Item 10.18 of consolidated circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) specifically for the JLHL face value split corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must ensure early pay-in for JLHL trades on July 24 and July 27, 2026 in the specified settlement numbers is made in the old/existing ISIN, unadjusted for the split.
- Custodians providing early pay-in via pool accounts must upload accurate client-wise allocation based on actual (pre-split) traded quantities.
- Block mechanism early pay-in must follow NCL/CMPT/53386 (August 22, 2022) and must not include a client-wise allocation file upload.
- Post record-date pay-in/early pay-in must shift to the New ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex Date: July 24, 2026
- EPI in old ISIN: July 24, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026138) and July 27, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026138 & 2026139)
- Post record-date: pay-in/early pay-in to shift to New ISIN

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational circular targeted at clearing members, custodians, and PCMs handling settlement of JLHL trades during the face value split transition. It carries low market-moving impact but is operationally important to ensure correct depository handling and avoid settlement mismatches during the ISIN transition window.
