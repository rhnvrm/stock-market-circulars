## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities issued by Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), Godrej Finance Limited, and IFL Finance Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 14, 2026. The circular specifies the designated security codes and market lot sizes for trading in these instruments under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- Three privately placed debt securities have been admitted for dealing on NSE's Capital Market segment.
- SIDBI 7.29% 2029 Series IV bonds (Symbol: 729SIDBI29, Series: N0, ISIN: INE556F08LG1) maturing 09-Nov-2029.
- Godrej Finance Limited 7.94% 2028 Series E1 bonds (Symbol: 794GFL28, Series: N0, ISIN: INE02KN07113) maturing 05-Jul-2028.
- IFL Finance Limited 11.80% 2028 bonds (Symbol: 1180IFL28B, Series: N0, ISIN: INE01XO07074) maturing 09-Jul-2028.
- All three securities carry a market lot size of 1.
- Trading in these securities will be identified only by their designated security codes.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A regarding admission of privately placed securities and lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the designated security codes and specified lot sizes when trading these instruments on the Capital Market segment from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 14, 2026
- SIDBI bond maturity: 09-Nov-2029
- Godrej Finance bond maturity: 05-Jul-2028
- IFL Finance bond maturity: 09-Jul-2028

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the technical listing of privately placed debt instruments on the Capital Market segment. It primarily affects institutional bondholders and members trading in these specific fixed-income securities, with no bearing on equity trading or broader market sentiment.
