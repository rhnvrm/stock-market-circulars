## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of five privately placed debt securities to dealings on the National Stock Exchange Capital Market Segment with effect from June 18, 2026. The securities are issued by Aditya Birla Capital Limited, National Bank For Financing Infrastructure And Development (NABFID), LIC Housing Finance Limited, and Sundaram Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Five debt securities (NCDs) admitted to NSE capital market segment effective June 18, 2026
- All securities carry Market Lot of 1 and are listed under Series N0
- Coupon rates range from 7.37% to 8.26% per annum
- Maturities range from July 2029 to June 2036
- Trading will be identified solely by the designated security codes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Admission is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, governing lot sizes and security identification codes for newly listed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for all trading in these instruments
- Trading must be conducted in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure (Market Lot: 1 for all five securities)

- **Effective Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Maturity Dates:**

  - 766NBFID36 (NABF 7.66% 2036 Sr 3): June 18, 2036 | ISIN: INE0KUG08159
  - 759LICH31 (LICH 7.59% 2031 Tr 457): June 12, 2031 | ISIN: INE115A07RI2

  - 795SFL29 (SFL 7.95% 2029 Sr AA1): August 14, 2029 | ISIN: INE660A07SE4

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The admission of these five privately placed NCDs expands the tradeable debt universe on NSE. Instruments are from well-rated issuers (Aditya Birla Capital, NABFID, LIC Housing Finance, Sundaram Finance), and the coupon rates (7.37%–8.26%) reflect prevailing institutional debt market conditions. No equity securities or derivative contracts are affected.
