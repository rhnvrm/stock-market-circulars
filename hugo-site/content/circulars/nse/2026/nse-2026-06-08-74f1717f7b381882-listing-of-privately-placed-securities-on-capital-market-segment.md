## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of two privately placed NCD securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on the capital market segment, effective June 09, 2026. Both securities carry fixed coupon rates and are admitted to dealings under the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- Two fresh-issue NCD securities of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited admitted to dealings on NSE capital market segment
- Effective date: June 09, 2026
- Both securities carry market lot size of 1 and are in Series N0
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes governed by Regulation 2.5.5 of the same regulations

## Securities Details

| Symbol | Description | Coupon Rate | Maturity Date | ISIN |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------------|------|
| 825ABHFL31 | ABHFL 8.25% 2031 Sr C1 | 8.25% | June 05, 2031 | INE831R07649 |
| 822ABHFL29 | ABHFL 8.22% 2029 Sr C2 | 8.22% | June 05, 2029 | INE831R07656 |

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations for admission of new debt securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security codes (825ABHFL31 and 822ABHFL29) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- Securities are identifiable only by their designated codes on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 08, 2026
- **Effective / Trading Commencement Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Maturity of 825ABHFL31:** June 05, 2031
- **Maturity of 822ABHFL29:** June 05, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The securities are privately placed NCDs of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited, providing debt market investors access to fixed-income instruments with tenors of approximately 3 and 5 years. The addition of these two securities expands the tradeable debt universe on NSE's capital market segment but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
