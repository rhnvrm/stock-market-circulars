## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the DSP Nifty FMCG ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) from DSP Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 12, 2026 to May 14, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The scheme tracks the Nifty FMCG index and is offered in two plans — Direct Growth and Regular Growth — both under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: May 12, 2026 to May 14, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two scheme variants: DSP Nifty FMCG ETF - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1ZA4) and DSP Nifty FMCG ETF - Regular Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1ZA4)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity
- Allotment date: May 18, 2026
- Payment deadline: May 17, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Issued by NSE NMF TM Segment (Circular Ref. No: 1363/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74140)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product launch notification for a new mutual fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds — through any payment mode — are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 17, 2026 at 11:59 PM.
- Members facilitating NFO transactions must adhere to the platform's standard transaction procedures.

## Important Dates

- **May 12, 2026**: NFO subscription window opens
- **May 14, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription window closes
- **May 17, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to reach the Clearing Corporation account
- **May 18, 2026**: Allotment date for both scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant primarily to NSE members and investors interested in gaining passive exposure to the FMCG sector via an index-tracking ETF. Members transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the tight subscription and payment timelines (3-day window, fund deadline one day before allotment). No systemic or regulatory impact is anticipated.
