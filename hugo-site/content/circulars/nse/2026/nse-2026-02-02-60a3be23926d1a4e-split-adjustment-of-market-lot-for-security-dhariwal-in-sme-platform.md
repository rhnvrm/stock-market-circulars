## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of market lot for DHARIWALCORP LIMITED (Symbol: DHARIWAL) listed on the SME Platform due to a 5:1 stock split. The adjusted market lot will be 3000 shares effective February 06, 2026. Members are required to load updated security files in their trading applications before the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: DHARIWALCORP LIMITED (Symbol: DHARIWAL)
- Corporate Action: Stock Split
- Split Ratio: 5:1
- Adjustment Factor: 5
- Adjusted Market Lot: 3000 shares
- Platform: SME

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action processing circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load updated security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII - Security File.gz in trading applications before trading on February 06, 2026
- Files available from directory common/NTNEAT on Extranet server
- MII - Security File.gz also available on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports
- Members must ensure trading systems reflect new market lot before effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Stock split increases share count by 5x and adjusts market lot to 3000 shares, making the stock more accessible to retail investors on SME platform.

**Operational Impact**: Members must update their trading systems with new security files before the effective date to ensure proper order processing. Failure to update may result in trading disruptions.
