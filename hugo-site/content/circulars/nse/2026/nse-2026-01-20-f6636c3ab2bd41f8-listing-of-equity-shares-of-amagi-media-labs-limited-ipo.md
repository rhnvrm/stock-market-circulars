## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Amagi Media Labs Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol "AMAGI" with ISIN INE121R01077. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Amagi Media Labs Limited
- Trading Symbol: AMAGI
- ISIN: INE121R01077
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Listing Type: IPO
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/72400, Circular Ref. No: 0102/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular that will specify the actual listing date before commencing trading in these securities.

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new tradeable security to the NSE platform. Market participants and investors interested in the media technology sector should prepare for trading opportunities once the listing date is announced. The company operates in the media labs/technology space, expanding sectoral diversity on the exchange.
