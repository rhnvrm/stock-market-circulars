## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 NFO (New Fund Offer) from Zerodha Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 19, 2026 to July 07, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The scheme is an equity fund with allotment scheduled for July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 19, 2026 to July 07, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Scheme: Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 Direct Plan Growth
- ISIN: INF0R8F01224
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: July 10, 2026
- Payment deadline: Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 10, 2026 till 11:59 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO availability notification for NSE members on the MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 10, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **July 07, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: NFO subscription closes
- **July 10, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: Deadline for clear funds to reach Clearing Corporation account
- **July 10, 2026**: Allotment date for Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 Direct Plan Growth

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact; this is a routine notification enabling NSE members to facilitate client investments in a new Zerodha Mutual Fund equity scheme through the NSE MF Invest Platform. No changes to existing market operations, regulations, or trading rules are involved. Members dealing in mutual fund transactions should note the subscription and payment deadlines.
