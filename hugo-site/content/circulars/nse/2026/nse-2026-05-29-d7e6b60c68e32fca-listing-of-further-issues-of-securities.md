## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for 25 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 01, 2026. The issuances predominantly relate to Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and warrant conversions on a preferential basis. Securities will be identified by their designated codes and traded in the specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 25 companies have further securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Effective date: June 01, 2026
- Securities governed under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.1)
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- All further issues are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable for ESOP allotments; warrant conversion shares may differ

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying routine admission of further securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for all transactions in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure (Market Lot: 1 for all mentioned securities)
- No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 01, 2026
- **360 ONE WAM LIMITED (360ONE):** 191,376 shares allotted on May 23, 2026 under ESOP (ISIN: INE466L01038)
- **Arvind Limited (ARVIND):** 25,000 shares allotted on May 11, 2026 under ESOP (ISIN: INE034A01011)
- **Associated Alcohols & Breweries Ltd. (ASALCBR):** 1,100,000 shares allotted on March 12, 2026 via warrant conversion on preferential basis at Rs. 679/share (ISIN: INE073G01016)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine further listings of small tranches of equity shares issued under existing ESOP schemes or warrant conversions already approved by shareholders. The incremental share counts are small relative to total outstanding shares for large-cap companies like Infosys and ICICI Lombard. For smaller companies such as Associated Alcohols & Breweries, the 1.1 million share warrant conversion represents a more meaningful addition to float but was pre-disclosed at the time of warrant issuance. All securities become tradeable from June 01, 2026.
