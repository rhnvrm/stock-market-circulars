## Summary

NSE has informed members that Capitalmind Multi Asset Allocation Fund, a New Fund Offer (NFO) by Capitalmind Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from February 23, 2026 to March 09, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Six scheme variants are being launched across direct and regular plans.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: February 23, 2026 to March 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans, each with Dividend Payout, Dividend Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All schemes categorised as Equity
- Allotment date for all schemes: March 16, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by March 16, 2026 at 12:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before March 16, 2026 by 12:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **March 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **March 16, 2026 (12:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **March 16, 2026**: Allotment date for all six schemes

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market-wide impact. It is relevant to NSE members and distributors who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in a multi-asset allocation strategy from Capitalmind Mutual Fund can subscribe during the NFO window. The six ISINs introduced are: INF226401067, INF226401075, INF226401059, INF226401091, INF226401109, and INF226401083.
