## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework, identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion under the Long-Term ASM. Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (VISL) has been shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I, having moved from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework. No securities were shortlisted for Stage IV, or for movement between Stage I to II, Stage II to III, or Stage I to IV in this cycle.

## Key Points

- VISL (Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited, ISIN: INE1CLE01013) shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I, having moved from STASM to LTASM.
- No securities shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage IV in this cycle.
- No securities shortlisted for transition from Stage I to Stage II, Stage II to Stage III, or Stage I to Stage IV.
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and not to be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing ASM framework established by prior circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, NSE/SURV/64066). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine periodic update identifying securities meeting existing Long-Term ASM criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply 100% margin on all open positions in VISL as on July 07, 2026, and on new positions created from July 08, 2026 onwards.
- Members must monitor client positions in the identified security for compliance with Long-Term ASM margin requirements.
- No securities under this cycle require shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE series), as no securities qualified under Stage IV criteria.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Effective date for shortlisting of VISL under Long-Term ASM Stage I.
- July 07, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 08, 2026: 100% margin applicable on all open and new positions in VISL.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is contained to a single security, VISL, which will face a 100% margin requirement effective July 8, 2026, significantly increasing the cost of holding or creating positions and likely reducing trading liquidity and volumes in the stock. Since no securities qualified for Stage IV this cycle, there is no immediate move to Trade-for-Trade settlement. Broader market impact is minimal as this is a routine, security-specific surveillance action rather than a systemic or market-wide measure.
