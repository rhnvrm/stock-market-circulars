## Summary

NSE has informed members that the JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund NFO by JioBlackRock Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 24, 2026 to April 07, 2026 (till 06:00 PM). The fund is an equity category scheme with allotment scheduled for April 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform: March 24, 2026 to April 07, 2026 (till 06:00 PM)
- Scheme: JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund - Direct - Growth (ISIN: INF22M001176)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: April 10, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by April 09, 2026 at 08:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before April 09, 2026 at 08:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **March 24, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **April 07, 2026 (06:00 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **April 09, 2026 (08:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **April 10, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine announcement for the launch of a new mutual fund offering on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members acting as distributors or facilitators should note the fund availability window and payment deadline to ensure timely processing for investors.
