## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in 7 privately placed non-convertible securities effective March 13, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspensions are triggered by redemptions and exercise of call options across five issuers.

## Key Points

- 7 non-convertible securities suspended from trading effective March 13, 2026
- Suspensions cover two categories: outright redemption and redemption for exercise of call option
- L&T Finance Limited has 3 securities being suspended (INE027E07CM5, INE027E08079, INE476M08055)
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73259, Circular Ref. No: 0437/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an operational notification under the existing framework of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs suspension of debt securities upon maturity or call option exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in the listed securities from March 13, 2026
- No further transactions should be executed for the 7 listed ISINs post the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 13, 2026 (all 7 securities)
