## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, June 13, 2026 for the Debt Segment. The session covers the Order Matching Platform, Negotiated Trade Reporting Platform, Tri-Party Repo Market Platform, Electronic Bidding Platform (EBP), CBRICS, and RFQ. Members are not required to make any changes to their connectivity configuration.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for Saturday, June 13, 2026 from the DR site
- No configuration changes required at the member end; existing URLs remain valid
- Order Matching Platform URL: https://ndm.nseindia.com
- EBP URL: https://www.nse-ebp.com
- CBRICS and RFQ URL: https://bricsonline.nseindia.com
- Members are encouraged to participate actively to verify connectivity
- Trades from this session carry no funds or securities pay-in/pay-out obligations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational DR readiness exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should participate actively in the mock session to verify connectivity to the DR site
- Do not transfer any data files generated during the mock session
- Trades reported on the Negotiated Trade Reporting Platform on June 13, 2026 will not be considered
- Any new UCC uploaded during the mock session will be removed after completion and must be re-uploaded post mock activity
- Monitor www.nseindia.com for any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard DR preparedness drill for debt segment members. No trading obligations arise from mock trades. Members should treat it as a connectivity and operational readiness check only. No securities or funds will be transferred as a result of this session.
