## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that the Motilal Oswal Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective January 29, 2026. Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plan options will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Motilal Oswal Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds reopening for ongoing transactions
- Effective date: January 29, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two plan options available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GL9) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GM7)
- Both schemes categorized under Equity category
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/72480, Circular No. 1147/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed of the scheme availability for facilitating investor transactions.

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Effective date for scheme availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational update. It affects investors interested in the Motilal Oswal Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds by providing access to ongoing subscription and redemption facilities along with systematic investment options (SIP, STP, SWP) through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening indicates the fund is now available for regular transactions after a period of closure or initial offering.
