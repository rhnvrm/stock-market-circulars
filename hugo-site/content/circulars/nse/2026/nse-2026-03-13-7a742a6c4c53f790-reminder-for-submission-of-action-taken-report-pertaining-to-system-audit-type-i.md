## Summary

NSE's Inspection Department has issued a reminder to all trading members to submit their Action Taken Reports (ATR) for non-compliances identified during System Audit (Type III) covering the half-yearly audit period of April 2025 to September 2025. The deadline for submission is March 31, 2026. This circular references the original audit circular NSE/INSP/70900 dated October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading members must take corrective action for each non-compliance reported by auditors in the System Audit Report (Type III)
- ATRs must be submitted for non-compliances in the audit period ending September 30, 2025
- After review of corrective actions, auditors will mark compliance status as Compliant or Non-Compliant on ENIT
- ATR submission link is available on ENIT portal
- Trading members are also reminded of circular NSE/INSP/70746 dated October 10, 2025, on rationalization and standardization of penalties

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a reminder for compliance with the existing framework established under NSE/INSP/70900 dated October 17, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members with non-compliances in the System Audit (Type III) for April 2025 – September 2025 must submit ATRs by March 31, 2026
- ATR submission must be made via ENIT portal: NEW-TRADE → Trade → System Audit → System Audit Help File → ATR
- Auditors must also update compliance status (Compliant/Non-Compliant) on ENIT via the same ATR path

## Important Dates

- **Audit Period:** April 2025 – September 2025 (Half-Yearly)
- **ATR Submission Deadline:** March 31, 2026
- **Reference Circular:** NSE/INSP/70900 dated October 17, 2025
- **Penalty Rationalization Circular:** NSE/INSP/70746 dated October 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts all trading members who received non-compliance findings in their Type III System Audit for the half-year ending September 2025. Failure to submit ATRs by the March 31, 2026 deadline may result in penalties as outlined in the rationalization circular NSE/INSP/70746. The compliance process is managed through the ENIT platform, and regional NSE offices are available to assist with clarifications.
