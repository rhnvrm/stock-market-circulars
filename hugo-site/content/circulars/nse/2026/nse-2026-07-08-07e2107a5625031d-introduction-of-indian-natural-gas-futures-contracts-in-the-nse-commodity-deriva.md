## Summary

NSE has informed members that it has received SEBI approval to launch Indian Natural Gas Futures contracts in the NSE Commodity Derivatives segment. The contracts will be available for trading with effect from July 27, 2026. The circular provides detailed contract specifications and a launch calendar as Annexure A. Risk management, clearing, and settlement details will be communicated separately by NSE Clearing Ltd.

## Key Points

- Product: Indian Natural Gas Futures Contract (symbol: FUTENR / NATGASIND, format NATGASINDYYMMM)
- Monthly contracts as per the launch calendar; trading commences July 27, 2026
- Contract commencement day: business day immediately following the last trading day
- Last trading day (contract expiry): last business day of the contract expiry month
- Trading period: Monday to Friday; trading session 9:00 am to 11:30/11:55 pm (based on US daylight saving time)
- Trading unit: 250 mmBtu; quotation in ₹ per mmBtu on GCV basis (excluding fees, transport charges, taxes) at the IGX Gujarat (Dahej) delivery hub
- Maximum order size: 12,500 mmBtu; tick size: ₹1
- Base daily price limit: 6%, relaxable to 9% after a 15-minute cooling-off period, with further 3% relaxations possible if international price movement exceeds the limit
- Initial margin: minimum based on volatility category or SPAN, whichever is higher; extreme loss margin: 1%
- Additional/special margins may be imposed during periods of additional volatility
- Maximum allowable open position: members - 6,00,00,000 mmBtu or 20% of market-wide open position (whichever higher); individual clients - 60,00,000 mmBtu or 5% of market-wide open position (whichever higher)
- Settlement: cash-settled; quality per PNGRB (Access Code for Common Carrier or Contract Carrier Natural Gas Pipelines) Regulations, 2008
- Final Settlement Price: monthly weighted average price of actual deliveries traded on Indian Gas Exchange (IGX) for the contract month, excluding gas traded at ceiling price, ssLNG trades, and long duration contracts

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements SEBI's approval for introduction of a new commodity derivatives product (Indian Natural Gas Futures) on NSE, establishing contract specifications, margining framework, position limits, and settlement methodology for the new product.

## Compliance Requirements

Members trading in the NSE Commodity Derivatives segment must familiarize themselves with the new contract specifications, position limits, and margin requirements ahead of the launch. Risk management, clearing, and settlement details will follow in a separate circular from NSE Clearing Ltd., which members should monitor for further compliance obligations.

## Important Dates

- July 27, 2026: Trading commences for Indian Natural Gas Futures (first contract launch month)
- Subsequent monthly contracts to be launched through July 2027 as per the published launch calendar

## Impact Assessment

The launch expands NSE's commodity derivatives offering, providing market participants a new hedging and trading instrument linked to domestic natural gas prices referenced to the IGX Gujarat (Dahej) hub. Impact is primarily on commodity derivatives trading members, energy sector participants, and hedgers rather than listed equities. No direct impact on individual equity stocks is indicated in this circular.
