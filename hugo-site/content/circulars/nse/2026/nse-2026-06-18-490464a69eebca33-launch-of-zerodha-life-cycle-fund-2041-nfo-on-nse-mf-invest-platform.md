## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 NFO from Zerodha Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 19, 2026 to July 07, 2026. Members can transact in the Direct Plan Growth option of this equity scheme.

## Key Points

- NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 19, 2026 to July 07, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Scheme: Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01216)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment date: July 10, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 10, 2026 till 11:59 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational circular announcing a new fund offer on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 10, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **July 07, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: NFO subscription closes
- **July 10, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **July 10, 2026**: Allotment date for units

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine NFO announcement for members using the NSE MF Invest Platform. It provides brokers and members with the operational details needed to facilitate client transactions in the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 scheme.
