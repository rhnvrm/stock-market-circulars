## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued circular 79/2026 (Ref: NCL/CMPT/74886) on June 25, 2026, detailing the clearing and settlement adjustments to be made for Futures and Options contracts in HINDUSTAN PETROLEUM CORPORATION LIMITED (HINDPETRO) in connection with an upcoming dividend of Rs. 19.25 per share. This is pursuant to NSE Circular No. 91/2026 dated June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Dividend amount: Rs. 19.25/- per share
- Last cum-dividend date: August 13, 2026
- Ex-dividend date: August 14, 2026
- All open HINDPETRO futures positions will be marked-to-market on August 13, 2026 and carried forward at the daily settlement price less Rs. 19.25/-
- From August 14, 2026, daily MTM settlement of HINDPETRO futures resumes under normal procedures
- Begin-of-day margins on August 14, 2026 will be computed based on the adjusted carry-forward value
- For options, the full dividend amount of Rs. 19.25/- will be deducted from all cum-dividend strike prices on the ex-dividend date, adjusted to the nearest tick size to arrive at new strike prices
- All positions in existing strike prices will continue in the corresponding adjusted strike prices

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operationalises the dividend adjustment mechanism for exchange-traded derivatives under NSE Circular No. 91/2026 (NSE/FAOP/74828) dated June 23, 2026. The clearing corporation will make ex-dividend adjustments to both futures carry-forward prices and options strike prices to ensure neutrality of value for existing position holders.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should note that open HINDPETRO futures positions will be automatically adjusted by the clearing corporation on August 13, 2026 (cum-dividend date)
- No manual action is required from members for the price adjustment itself
- Members should communicate the adjustment details to their clients holding HINDPETRO F&O positions
- Margin requirements on August 14, 2026 will reflect the adjusted carry-forward values; members should ensure adequate margins are in place

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026** – NSE Circular No. 91/2026 (reference circular for F&O adjustment)
- **June 25, 2026** – Date of this clearing circular (NCL/CMPT/74886)
- **August 13, 2026** – Last cum-dividend date; futures positions marked-to-market and carried forward at adjusted price (settlement price minus Rs. 19.25/-)
- **August 14, 2026** – Ex-dividend date; options strike prices reduced by Rs. 19.25/- (adjusted to nearest tick size); normal MTM settlement resumes for futures

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding open HINDPETRO futures or options contracts should note that the adjustment is value-neutral by design — the reduction in futures carry-forward price and options strike prices mirrors the dividend outflow. However, margin requirements on August 14 will be recalculated on the adjusted basis, which may affect margin utilisation. The adjustment example in the circular illustrates futures long/short value moving from Rs. 8,01,900 (at Rs. 396.00) to Rs. 7,62,918.75 (at Rs. 376.75, i.e., Rs. 396.00 – Rs. 19.25). Members with clients in HINDPETRO F&O should proactively communicate these changes to avoid confusion around position values on and after the ex-dividend date.
