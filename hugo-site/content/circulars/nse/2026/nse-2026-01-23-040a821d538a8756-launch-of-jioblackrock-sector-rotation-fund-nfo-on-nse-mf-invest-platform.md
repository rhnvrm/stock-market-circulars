## Summary

National Stock Exchange has announced the launch of JioBlackRock Sector Rotation Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The equity scheme will be available for subscription from January 27, 2026 to February 09, 2026 until 6:00 PM, with allotment scheduled for February 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme Name: JioBlackRock Sector Rotation Fund
- ISIN: INF22M001168
- Category: Equity
- Subscription Period: January 27, 2026 to February 09, 2026 (till 6:00 PM)
- Allotment Date: February 13, 2026
- Fund House: JioBlackRock Mutual Fund
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 13, 2026 at 8:00 PM
- Members should facilitate transactions for clients on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **February 09, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: NFO closing date and time
- **February 13, 2026 (8:00 PM)**: Deadline for receipt of clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **February 13, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with limited market impact. The circular is informational for NSE members to enable client transactions on the MF Invest Platform. The sector rotation equity fund adds to the product offerings available to investors but does not affect trading operations or compliance requirements beyond the specified payment timeline for participating members.
