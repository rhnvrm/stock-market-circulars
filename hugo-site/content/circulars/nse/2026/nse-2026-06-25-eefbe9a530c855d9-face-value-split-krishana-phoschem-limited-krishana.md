## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified that Krishana Phoschem Limited (symbol: KRISHANA) will undergo a face value split effective July 03, 2026. The face value and paid-up value of its equity shares will be reduced from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2, implying a 5-for-1 split ratio.

## Key Points

- Krishana Phoschem Limited (KRISHANA) will split its equity share face value from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share.
- The split ratio is effectively 5:1 — each existing share of Rs. 10 will become five shares of Rs. 2.
- The number of outstanding shares will increase fivefold while the market price per share is expected to adjust proportionally.
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref. No. NSE/CML/74883 (Circular Ref. No. 1023/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

The face value and paid-up value of KRISHANA equity shares is being changed from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share pursuant to the company's board/shareholder approval for the stock split. NSE members are required to note the revised face value for all settlement and margin purposes from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must update their records to reflect the new face value of Rs. 2 per share for KRISHANA from July 03, 2026.
- Any futures and options contracts (if active) on this scrip may require corresponding adjustment per NSE's F&O adjustment methodology.
- Members should communicate the change to clients holding KRISHANA shares in their demat accounts.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date of Split:** July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 (a 5:1 ratio) will increase the total number of equity shares of Krishana Phoschem Limited fivefold. The market price per share is expected to adjust downward proportionally on the ex-date (July 03, 2026), keeping the overall market capitalisation unchanged. The split typically enhances share liquidity and retail investor participation by making the per-share price more accessible. Members and investors holding KRISHANA in physical or demat form will see their share count updated automatically by the depository.
