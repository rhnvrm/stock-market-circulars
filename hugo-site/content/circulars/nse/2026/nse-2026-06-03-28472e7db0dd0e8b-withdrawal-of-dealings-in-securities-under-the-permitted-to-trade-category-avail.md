## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has issued Circular Ref. No. 0893/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74524) notifying members that Available Finance Limited (Symbol: AVAILFC, ISIN: INE325G01010) is being withdrawn from the Permitted to Trade (PTT) Category effective June 04, 2026. Dealings in the security will cease from the closing hours of trading on June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Available Finance Limited (AVAILFC, INE325G01010) will no longer be available for trading under the Permitted to Trade category.
- The withdrawal takes effect from the closing hours of June 03, 2026.
- The circular is formally effective from June 04, 2026.
- All NSE members are advised to take note of this withdrawal.

## Regulatory Changes

The security's status in the Permitted to Trade (PTT) Category is being revoked. Once withdrawn, the security will not be available for trading on NSE under this category.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members must cease dealing in Available Finance Limited (AVAILFC) under the PTT category from the close of trading on June 03, 2026. Members should update their systems and client communications accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: Last day of trading; withdrawal effective from closing hours.
- **June 04, 2026**: Formal effective date of withdrawal; no dealings permitted from this date.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or intending to trade Available Finance Limited through the NSE PTT Category must be aware that the window for dealing in this security closes on June 03, 2026. After this date, transactions in AVAILFC through the PTT route on NSE will not be possible. The impact is limited to participants specifically trading this security on NSE's PTT platform.
