## Summary

NSE has informed members about the merger of certain schemes of Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Baroda BNP Paribas Nifty SDL December 2026 Index Fund (Merging scheme) will merge into Baroda BNP Paribas Nifty SDL December 2028 Index Fund (Surviving scheme), and Baroda BNP Paribas Credit Risk Fund (Merging scheme) will merge into Baroda BNP Paribas Short Duration Fund (Surviving scheme). The merger is effective from August 21, 2026, pursuant to a notice cum addendum issued by the AMC.

## Key Points

- Merger 1: Baroda BNP Paribas Nifty SDL December 2026 Index Fund merges into Baroda BNP Paribas Nifty SDL December 2028 Index Fund
- Merger 2: Baroda BNP Paribas Credit Risk Fund merges into Baroda BNP Paribas Short Duration Fund
- Investors not in favour of the merger can redeem or switch to other Baroda BNP Paribas schemes without exit load during the specified window
- No inflows permitted into merging schemes effective from August 18, 2026
- Existing SIP/STP/SWP will trigger in the surviving scheme from August 21, 2026
- Existing SIP/XSIP/SWP will continue processing in merging schemes during the exit load waiver period
- Merging schemes cease to exist after business hours on August 20, 2026, with registered SIP/XSIP/STP/SWP shifting to surviving schemes

## Regulatory Changes

This is a scheme merger/consolidation action by Baroda BNP Paribas Asset Management, approved by the AMC's Board of Directors and Trustees, communicated to NSE members via Notice cum Addendum No. 60/2026, amending the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of the affected schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/distributors must inform investors of the merger and the exit load waiver window
- No new subscriptions/inflows should be processed for merging schemes after August 18, 2026
- Existing SIP/STP/SWP registrations must be tracked for automatic transition to surviving schemes
- Full details of schemes and plans/options being merged are provided in Annexure-I of the circular

## Important Dates

- **July 22, 2026 to August 20, 2026 (up to 3:00 PM)**: Exit load waiver period for redemption/switch by dissenting investors
- **August 18, 2026**: No further inflows allowed into merging schemes
- **August 20, 2026 (after business hours)**: Merging schemes cease to exist; SIP/XSIP/STP/SWP registrations shift to surviving schemes
- **August 21, 2026**: Merger effective date; SIP/STP/SWP begin triggering in surviving schemes

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to mutual fund investors and distributors using the NSE MF Invest platform who hold units in the merging schemes (Baroda BNP Paribas Nifty SDL December 2026 Index Fund and Baroda BNP Paribas Credit Risk Fund). Investors need to actively decide whether to redeem/switch during the exit-load-free window or allow their holdings and systematic transactions to transition automatically to the surviving schemes. There is no direct impact on listed equity securities or trading operations.
