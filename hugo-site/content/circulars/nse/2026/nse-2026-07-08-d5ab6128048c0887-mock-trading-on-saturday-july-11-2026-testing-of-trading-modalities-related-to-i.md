## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Futures & Options segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026, to test trading modalities related to the upcoming introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment. The mock involves three trading sessions, including a new NEATPlus software version (7.8.9) for Session 1, followed by a reversion to the existing version (7.8.8) for Sessions 2 and 3, which will be conducted from the Primary Site and DR Site respectively.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: Saturday, July 11, 2026, referencing earlier circular NSE/FAOP/74467 dated May 29, 2026 on CAS introduction.
- Trading Session 1 uses new NEATPlus version 7.8.9, applicable only for this session.
- After Session 1, members must reinstall and log in with the earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for Sessions 2 and 3, which follow existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes.
- Session 2 runs from the Primary Site; Session 3 runs from the DR Site.
- Trades from Session 1 are not carried forward to Sessions 2 or 3; trades from Session 2 carry forward to Session 3.
- During Session 1, stock futures price bands will be aligned with CAS in the Cash Market segment, with reference price based on VWAP of stock futures trades between 09:00 a.m. and 09:15 a.m.
- NEATPlus versions 7.8.8 and 7.8.9 are compatible with new broadcast parameters referenced in circular NSE/MSD/74448 dated May 27, 2026.
- Additional mock trading sessions for CAS testing in the CM segment are scheduled for July 18, 2026, and July 25, 2026, with timings to be announced later.
- Live trading from July 13, 2026 onward should use NEATPlus version 7.8.8.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulatory requirements but operationalizes the previously announced Closing Auction Session (CAS) framework for the Equity Cash segment by testing its impact on Equity Derivatives trading modalities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download and install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 (available July 11, 2026, 07:00 hours) from the NSE Extranet path for Session 1 only.
- Members must reinstall NEATPlus version 7.8.8 from the NSE Extranet path before Sessions 2 and 3.
- Members are required to actively participate in all three trading sessions.
- Members must use NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for live trading starting July 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- July 11, 2026: Mock trading day (Sessions 1, 2, and 3)
- July 13, 2026: Live trading resumes with NEATPlus version 7.8.8
- July 18, 2026 and July 25, 2026: Additional mock trading sessions for CAS testing

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/technical circular primarily affecting trading members' IT systems and testing procedures ahead of the CAS rollout in the Equity Cash segment. It has no direct bearing on listed companies or specific stocks, and low market impact, but requires trading members to actively coordinate software updates and participate in scheduled mock sessions to ensure readiness for the CAS transition.
