## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Edelweiss Nifty Metal ETF NFO, under Edelweiss Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The scheme includes both Direct Plan Growth and Regular Plan Growth options.

## Key Points

- New Fund Offer (NFO): Edelweiss Nifty Metal ETF under Edelweiss Mutual Fund
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 till 11:59 pm
- Two scheme variants: Edelweiss Nifty Metal ETF - Direct Plan Growth and Edelweiss Nifty Metal ETF - Regular Plan Growth, both under ISIN INF754K01XG1
- Category: Equity
- Allotment date for both schemes: July 27, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for trading on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 23, 2026 till 06:00 pm, to facilitate settlement of the NFO subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Clear funds deadline: July 23, 2026 (till 06:00 pm)
- Allotment date: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to the operational enablement of a single new ETF scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It is primarily relevant to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions and investors interested in subscribing to the Edelweiss Nifty Metal ETF NFO. No impact on listed equity trading or existing securities is expected.
