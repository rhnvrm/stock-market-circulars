## Summary

NSE has admitted 11 fresh privately placed Commercial Paper (CP) issuances from 9 corporates and financial institutions to dealings on the Debt Market Segment, effective June 23, 2026. The action is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Key Points

- All securities are fresh issues of Commercial Papers (CPs) allotted on June 22, 2026.
- 9 issuers covered: Reliance Retail Ventures Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, HDFC Securities Limited, Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Tata Projects Limited, SIDBI, HDB Financial Services Ltd, and Astec LifeSciences Limited.
- Largest issue by quantity: SIDBI CP 21/09/26 Sr 7 — 74,500 units at face value ₹5,00,000 each (ISIN: INE556F14MM5).
- Reliance Retail Ventures Limited issued 52,000 CPs (ISIN: INE929O14FF4) at an issue price of ₹4,91,797 each, redeeming September 21, 2026.
- Most CPs mature on September 21, 2026; exceptions are HDFC Securities (one series maturing July 13, 2026) and HDB Financial Services (maturing July 2, 2026) and IOCL (maturing September 18, 2026).
- Designated security codes are specified in the Annexure attached to the circular.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a standard operational notification under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of NSE are notified of the admission of these securities to the Debt Market Segment.
- No specific compliance action is required of members beyond awareness of the new security codes and ISINs for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment (all issues):** June 22, 2026
- **Effective date for dealings:** June 23, 2026
- **Redemption dates:**
  - July 2, 2026 — HDB Financial Services Ltd (HFSL CP 02/07/26 Sr 339)
  - July 13, 2026 — HDFC Securities Ltd (HSL CP 13/07/2026)
  - September 18, 2026 — Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (IOCL CP 18/09/26 Sr 773)
  - September 21, 2026 — Reliance Retail Ventures, Godrej Properties, Godrej Industries, HDFC Securities (second series), Tata Projects, SIDBI, Astec LifeSciences (both series)
  - September 17, 2026 — Astec LifeSciences (ALSL CP 17/09/26 Sr 197)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with no material market-wide impact. The listings provide liquidity to institutional investors holding these CPs in the secondary debt market. The aggregate notional value is significant (SIDBI alone: ₹74,500 × ₹5,00,000 = ~₹3,725 crore; RRVL: ~₹2,557 crore), but the listings are standard short-term money market instruments. Equity investors and retail participants are not directly affected.
