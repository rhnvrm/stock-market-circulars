## Summary

NSE announces the launch of DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from February 05, 2026 to February 19, 2026 until 11:45 PM, with six scheme options across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: February 05, 2026 to February 19, 2026 (until 11:45 PM)
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Allotment date: February 25, 2026
- Payment deadline: February 24, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Fund house: DSP Mutual Fund

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 24, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- All transactions to be conducted through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opens**: February 05, 2026
- **NFO Closes**: February 19, 2026 (11:45 PM)
- **Payment Deadline**: February 24, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new multi-asset fund of funds investment option through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund offers diversification across asset classes through a fund of funds structure. No impact on equity market operations or existing securities.
