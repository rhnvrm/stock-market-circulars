## Summary

NSE has admitted five privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from March 12, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The securities include bonds from Torrent Power Limited (three tranches), Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Company Limited, and Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited. All securities have a market lot size of 1 and trade under the N0 series.

## Key Points

- Five debt securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment effective March 12, 2026
- Torrent Power Limited lists three tranches (797TOPO34, 797TOPO35, 797TOPO36) at 7.97% coupon maturing in 2034, 2035, and 2036 respectively
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Company Limited lists one bond (790ABSLI36) at 7.90% coupon maturing March 9, 2036
- Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited lists one market-linked bond (AAFS31) maturing January 28, 2031
- All securities have a market lot size of 1 under Series N0
- Securities are identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for all trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- No additional compliance actions required from members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 12, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)
- **Maturity Dates:**
  - 797TOPO34 (Torrent Power Tr 14A): March 9, 2034
  - 797TOPO35 (Torrent Power Tr 14B): March 9, 2035
  - 790ABSLI36 (Aditya Birla Sun Life): March 9, 2036
  - 797TOPO36 (Torrent Power Tr 14C): March 9, 2036
  - AAFS31 (Alpha Alternatives): January 28, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing circular with limited market impact. The five securities are privately placed instruments now made available for secondary market trading on NSE. Torrent Power's three tranches (INE813H07424, INE813H07408, INE813H07416) at 7.97% offer fixed-income investors exposure to the power sector. Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance's bond (INE951F08085) at 7.90% adds insurance sector debt to the market. Alpha Alternatives' market-linked bond (INE0L6807294) offers a variable return instrument. Impact is confined to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in these specific instruments.
