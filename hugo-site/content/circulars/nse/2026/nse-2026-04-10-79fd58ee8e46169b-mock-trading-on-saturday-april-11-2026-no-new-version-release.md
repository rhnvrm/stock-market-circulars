## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Market (SLBM) on Saturday, April 11, 2026. The session covers trading from both the Primary Site and the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site. No new NEAT version is being released; members should continue using NEAT 1.3.3. Trades executed during the mock session carry no financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be held on **April 11, 2026** in the SLBM segment
- **No new NEAT version** is being released for this session; existing NEAT 1.3.3 applies
- Trading session from Primary Site: Normal Market 10:15 – 14:00 hrs (Contingency: 12:00 – 13:30 hrs)
- Trading Session-2 from DR Site: Normal Market 15:00 – 15:30 hrs
- Live re-login window: 17:00 – 18:00 hrs
- All outstanding orders will be purged before each session
- NOTIS application will **not** be accessible during the mock session
- Connect2NSE and Extranet facility unavailable from 12:30–17:30 hrs and 18:00–21:30 hrs
- Extranet API unavailable from 12:30–14:00 hrs and 17:30–18:30 hrs
- Members must re-login into the live environment post-mock to ensure readiness for **Monday, April 13, 2026**

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock session. Members may use either Mock Trading or Simulated Environment to meet SEBI regulatory requirements as per circulars NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members enabled for SLBM trading in the live market are automatically eligible; no separate registration required
- Participation uses existing User IDs, IPs, and Box ID mappings from the live environment
- Members using Non-NEAT Frontend (NNF) must ensure: User ID is dealer type, converted for NNF, and mapped with the IP
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be permitted (UCC validation skipped during contingency time)
- For UCC/PAN queries, contact uci@nse.co.in
- Members should test all trading software and confirm live re-login before end of session

## Important Dates

- **April 11, 2026**: Mock trading session in SLBM
  - Primary Site Normal Market: 10:15 – 14:00 hrs
  - DR Site Trading Session-2: 15:00 – 15:30 hrs
  - Live Re-login Window: 17:00 – 18:00 hrs
- **April 13, 2026**: Live trading resumes (Monday); members must ensure connectivity readiness

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it pertains to a scheduled mock/test session with no financial settlement, no new software release, and no changes to trading rules or regulations. The primary operational impact is on SLBM segment members who must participate in the mock session and confirm live re-login to ensure smooth Monday operations. Temporary unavailability of Connect2NSE, Extranet, and NOTIS during specified windows should be planned for by technology teams.
