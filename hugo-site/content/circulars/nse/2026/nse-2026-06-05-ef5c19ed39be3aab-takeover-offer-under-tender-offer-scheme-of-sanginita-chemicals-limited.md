## Summary

NSE has announced a takeover offer for Sanginita Chemicals Limited (Symbol: SANGINITA, Series: TO, ISIN: INE753W01010) under the Tender Offer Scheme. The acquirers, B N G Investment LLC (Acquirer 1) and Anubhav Agarwal (Acquirer 2), are using NSE's web-based bidding platform for this fixed-price open offer at Rs. 13.55 per equity share.

## Key Points

- Offer is for up to 1,56,89,957 equity shares of Sanginita Chemicals Limited at a fixed price of Rs. 13.55 per share
- Offer type: Takeover; Issue type: Fixed Price
- Security symbol: SANGINITA, Series: TO, Face value: Rs. 10/-, Lot size: 1 equity share
- Merchant Banker: Finshore Management Services Limited; Registrar: Purva Sharegistry (India) Private Limited
- Bidding platform: https://eipo.nseindia.com (same as web-based IPO platform)
- Order modification (quantity) and cancellation are NOT permitted for takeover offers
- All trading members of the Capital Market segment are eligible to participate
- Members without existing access must use the admin terminal (User ID: Adminxxxxx where xxxxx is Member Code) to create branches and users

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operates under the Tender Offer Scheme Exchange circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022. No new regulatory changes are introduced; the existing tender offer framework applies.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to participate must ensure they have access to the web-based IPO bidding platform; existing users can reuse their credentials
- Members without access must log in via the admin terminal to set up branches and users before bidding opens
- Order modification (quantity) or cancellation is strictly prohibited once submitted
- Members must adhere to bidding timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on regular days; custodial confirmation on closure day until 4:00 p.m.

## Important Dates

- Issue (Bidding) Period: June 10, 2026 to June 23, 2026
- Bidding Time: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (daily)
- Custodial Confirmation on Issue Closure Day: 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders of Sanginita Chemicals Limited have a structured exit opportunity through this takeover offer at Rs. 13.55 per share. The offer targets up to 1,56,89,957 equity shares. Eligible investor categories span FIIs, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Banks & Financial Institutions, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors. Trading members acting as intermediaries should prepare their systems and credentials ahead of June 10, 2026. The prohibition on order modification/cancellation demands careful order placement by participants.
