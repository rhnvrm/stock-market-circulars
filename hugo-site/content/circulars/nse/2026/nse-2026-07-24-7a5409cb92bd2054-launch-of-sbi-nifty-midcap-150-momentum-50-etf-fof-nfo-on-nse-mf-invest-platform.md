## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the SBI Nifty Midcap 150 Momentum 50 ETF FOF New Fund Offer (NFO) from SBI Mutual Fund will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 27, 2026 to August 5, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular lists six scheme plan options with their respective ISINs and specifies the allotment date and clearing fund deadlines.

## Key Points

- Scheme: SBI Nifty Midcap 150 Momentum 50 ETF FOF, offered by SBI Mutual Fund
- NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform for subscription
- Six plan/option variants listed, each with a unique ISIN:
  - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout: INF200KB1BV3 (Debt)
  - Direct Growth: INF200KB1BU5 (Debt)
  - Direct Plan - IDCW: INF200KB1BW1 (Debt)
  - Regular Plan - IDCW: INF200KB1BT7 (Debt)
  - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout: INF200KB1BS9 (Debt)
  - Regular Growth: INF200KB1BR1 (Equity)
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by the subscription close date/time

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/administrative notification, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members transacting this NFO must ensure clear funds (via any payment mode) are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 5, 2026, till 11:59 pm.
- Members should process client orders for this scheme only within the specified subscription window.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription open: July 27, 2026
- NFO subscription close: August 5, 2026, till 11:59 pm
- Allotment date: August 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact and is primarily operational in nature, informing NSE members and MF distributors about platform availability for a new fund offer. It does not affect listed equity trading, surveillance measures, or broader compliance obligations, and is relevant mainly to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions and investors interested in subscribing to this specific ETF FOF scheme.
