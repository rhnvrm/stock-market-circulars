## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all members that Deepak Builders & Engineers India Limited (symbol: DBEIL) will undergo a face value split. The existing face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10 per equity share will be reduced to Re. 1 per share, effective June 19, 2026. This represents a 10:1 split ratio.

## Key Points

- Company: Deepak Builders & Engineers India Limited
- NSE Symbol: DBEIL
- Split ratio: 10:1 (face value reduced from Rs. 10 to Re. 1)
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74677 | Circular Ref. No. 0950/2026
- Issued by: Dhwani Shah, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to this circular, the face value and paid-up value of DBEIL equity shares will change from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 per share with effect from June 19, 2026. Members and market participants must update their systems to reflect the revised face value and the consequent increase in the number of outstanding shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are required to take note of the revised face value effective June 19, 2026.
- Trading systems and back-office records should be updated to reflect the new face value of Re. 1 per equity share.
- Relevant adjustments to positions, margins, and contract parameters (if any) should be made in accordance with NSE guidelines.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Effective Date of Face Value Split:** June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 (10:1) will result in a proportional increase in the number of equity shares held by each shareholder, with a corresponding reduction in the market price per share (in theory, by a factor of 10 at the ex-date). This enhances share liquidity and accessibility for retail investors. Existing shareholders are not financially disadvantaged as the total value of their holding remains unchanged at the ex-date. Market participants should update lot sizes, EPS calculations, book value per share, and any derivative contract parameters linked to DBEIL as applicable.
