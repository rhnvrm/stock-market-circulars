## Summary

National Stock Exchange notifies the listing of further equity shares for 13 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective February 01, 2026. The circular provides details of securities admitted to trading including allotment dates, number of securities, distinctive number ranges, and lock-in details where applicable.

## Key Points

- Total of 13 companies issued further securities being listed
- Majority are ESOP (Employee Stock Option Plan) allotments
- One preferential issue by Emkay Global Financial Services Limited
- All securities will trade with market lot size of 1
- Securities identified by designated codes specified in annexure
- Effective date: February 01, 2026

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- All securities are pari passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 01, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from December 12, 2025 to January 28, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry** (Emkay Global only): July 31, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for these securities
- Securities to trade in specified lot sizes (Market Lot: 1 for all listed securities)
- Lock-in restrictions applicable only to Emkay Global Financial Services Limited preferential issue

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine further issues primarily from employee stock option exercises. The quantities are relatively small compared to the total outstanding shares of respective companies. The preferential issue by Emkay Global has appropriate lock-in until July 2027, preventing immediate dilution impact. Trading will commence normally from February 01, 2026 with standard market lot of 1 share.
