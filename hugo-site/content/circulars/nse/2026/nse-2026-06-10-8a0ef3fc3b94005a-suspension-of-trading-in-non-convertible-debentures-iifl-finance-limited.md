## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by IIFL Finance Limited, effective June 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of these securities, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two NCD securities of IIFL Finance Limited will be suspended from trading on NSE effective June 12, 2026.
- Reason for suspension is maturity of the instruments.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74639, Circular Ref. No. 0929/2026.
- Issued by the Listing Department of NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension is being effected under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed that trading in the following securities must cease from June 12, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Company | Symbol | ISIN | Reason |
|---------|---------|--------|------|--------|
| 1 | IIFL Finance Limited | 85IFL26 | INE530B07344 | Maturity |
| 2 | IIFL Finance Limited | IIFLZC26B | INE530B07351 | Maturity |

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is a routine action upon maturity of the NCD instruments and has limited market impact. Holders of these securities (INE530B07344 and INE530B07351) will no longer be able to trade them on NSE from June 12, 2026 onwards. Redemption proceeds will be settled as per the terms of the respective debentures.
