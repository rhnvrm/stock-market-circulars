## Summary

Aarnav Fashions Limited has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange of India (Capital Market segment) under the symbol **AARNAV** effective **February 25, 2026**. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Trading will occur in the Normal Market segment under Rolling Settlement in compulsory demat mode for all investors.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** AARNAV
- **ISIN:** INE750R01016
- **Series:** EQ
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Total Securities Listed:** 42,238,628 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 42,238,628
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- **Segment:** Normal Market (Rolling Settlement), compulsory demat for all investors
- The company is **not** classified as an SME listed entity.
- Pre-listing shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a); date of allotment: February 13, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is in pursuance of existing NSE Trading Regulations (Part A), specifically:
- **Regulation 3.1.1** — admission of securities to dealings
- **Regulation 2.5.5** — specification of lot sizes for admitted securities

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in AARNAV securities must use the designated NSE symbol and security code.
- Trading must be in the specified lot size of 1 share.
- All investors must hold and transact shares in dematerialised (demat) form only.
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: MCS Share Transfer Agent Limited, Ahmedabad.
- Company Secretary: Ms. Milee Kamdar (cs@aarnavgroup.com).

## Important Dates

- **Date of Circular:** February 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing / Trading Commencement:** February 25, 2026
- **Date of Allotment:** February 13, 2026
- **Financial Year:** April 1 to March 31

## Impact Assessment

This is a new equity listing on NSE's Capital Market segment. Market participants — particularly those tracking new listings in the textile and fashion sector — should note the commencement of trading in AARNAV from February 25, 2026. With 42,238,628 shares listed at a face value of Rs. 10 each, the listing adds a mid-size company to the exchange. No lock-in applies, and shares are pari passu. The broader market impact is limited, but traders and investors interested in new listings or the apparel/fashion industry should monitor price discovery in the initial trading sessions.
