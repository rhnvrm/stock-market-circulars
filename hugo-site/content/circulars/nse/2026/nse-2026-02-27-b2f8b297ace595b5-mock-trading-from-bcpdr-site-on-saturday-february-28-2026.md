## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) and the National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE) have issued circulars notifying all members of a mock trading session to be conducted from the BCP/DR site on Saturday, February 28, 2026. The session covers the Currency Derivatives Segment and uses NEAT version 3.5.4. No financial obligations arise from trades executed during this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session will be conducted in the **Currency Derivatives Segment** from the DR site on **February 28, 2026**.
- Trading session runs from **09:00 to 17:00 hrs**; Trade Modification ends at **17:30 hrs**.
- Re-login window: **18:30 to 19:00 hrs**.
- Required NEAT version: **3.5.4** (Download path on NSE Extranet: `/cdsftp/cdscommon/NEATCDS354`).
- Old NEAT Adapter (v1.0.20) must use GR PORT **10877**; Old NEAT Adapter (v1.0.22) and New NEAT Adapter (v1.0.23) must use GR PORT **10879**.
- All outstanding orders will be **purged/cleared** before each session; NNF software users must manually clear orders.
- Trades executed during the mock session will **not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out**.
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before the cutoff will be allowed; UCC validation is skipped during contingency time for order entry.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. Members are directed to existing circulars:
- NSE/MSD/67674 dated April 24, 2025 and NSE/CD/70422 dated September 25, 2025 for interactive connectivity details.
- NSE/MSD/70421 dated September 25, 2025 and NSE/MSD/71601 dated December 02, 2025 for NEAT Adapter Exe application versions.
- NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 for software testing requirements.
- NSE/ISC/51754 dated March 24, 2022 and NSE/ISC/52722 dated June 23, 2022 for UCC/PAN compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members participating in the mock session must ensure they are using **NEAT version 3.5.4**.
- Members must use the correct NEAT Adapter version and corresponding GR PORT (10877 or 10879 as applicable).
- NNF software users must **manually purge outstanding orders** in their systems before the session.
- Members must ensure all UCC/PAN data is valid and uploaded before the applicable cutoff.
- Members may use either the Mock Trading environment or Simulated Environment to meet regulatory software testing requirements per SEBI/NSE guidelines.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine business continuity / disaster recovery (BCP/DR) drill with no market-wide financial impact. Only members of the Currency Derivatives Segment who choose or are required to participate will be affected. There are no fund pay-in or pay-out obligations arising from mock trades. Members should verify their NEAT version and adapter compatibility ahead of Saturday to avoid connectivity issues during the drill.
