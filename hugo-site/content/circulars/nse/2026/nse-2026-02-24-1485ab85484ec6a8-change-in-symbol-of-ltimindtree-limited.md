## Summary

NSE's Futures & Options department has announced a change in the trading symbol of LTIMindtree Limited. The symbol will be updated from **LTIM** to **LTM** effective February 27, 2026. All derivative contracts in the security will be traded under the new symbol from that date.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol changes from **LTIM** to **LTM** for LTIMindtree Limited
- Change applicable across all Futures & Options contracts in the security
- Effective date: February 27, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE's Futures & Options department (Ref: NSE/FAOP/72935, Circular 28/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The exchange has mandated a symbol rename for LTIMindtree Limited in the Futures & Options segment. Old symbol LTIM will be retired and replaced with LTM across all F&O contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/brokers must update their trading systems, order management systems (OMS), and algorithmic strategies to reference the new symbol **LTM** before February 27, 2026
- Risk management systems, watchlists, and client-facing platforms should be updated accordingly
- Confirmations or queries can be directed to NSE at msm@nse.co.in or Toll Free: 1800-266-0050 (Option 1)

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change with moderate impact on market participants trading LTIMindtree derivatives. Traders, algorithmic systems, and data vendors referencing the old symbol LTIM must update their configurations before February 27, 2026 to avoid order failures or data mismatches. The underlying security and contracts themselves remain unchanged; only the ticker symbol is affected.
