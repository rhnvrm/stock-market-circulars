## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (privately placed) of Toyota Financial Services India Limited (ISIN: INE692Q07407) effective June 4, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the security, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE692Q07407 (Toyota Financial Services India Limited) will be suspended from June 4, 2026
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the non-convertible security
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74517 | Ref No: 0891/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is a standard operational action under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.2, Part A).

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are notified to refrain from trading in ISIN INE692Q07407 (Toyota Financial Services India Limited) from June 4, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 3, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only a single privately placed non-convertible security of Toyota Financial Services India Limited, and is a standard procedure upon maturity and redemption of debt instruments. Holders of this security would receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
