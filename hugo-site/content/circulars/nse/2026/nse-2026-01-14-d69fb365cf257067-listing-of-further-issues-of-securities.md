## Summary

NSE notifies the listing of further equity shares of nine companies on the Capital Market segment effective January 16, 2026. The securities include shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and preferential allotment. All securities will trade with market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Effective date: January 16, 2026
- Nine companies listed: Black Box Limited, Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited, Indegene Limited (2 allotments), The Karnataka Bank Limited, Mahindra Logistics Limited, Moschip Technologies Limited, Suzlon Energy Limited, Tarmat Limited, and Yes Bank Limited
- Most securities allotted under ESOP schemes
- Black Box Limited shares issued under preferential allotment with lock-in until July 25, 2026
- All securities pari passu with existing shares
- Market lot size: 1 for all securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- Trading in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all securities)
- Securities admitted pursuant to NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations

## Important Dates

- Circular date: January 14, 2026
- Listing effective date: January 16, 2026
- Black Box Limited lock-in expiry: July 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine further issues representing employee compensation (ESOP) and small preferential allotments. The number of securities being listed is relatively small compared to existing equity base for most companies. Only Black Box Limited shares have lock-in restrictions. Trading will commence in normal course with standard lot sizes.
