## Summary

NSE has notified members that the face value and paid-up value of the equity shares of P S Raj Steels Limited (PSRAJ) will be split from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share, effective July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: P S Raj Steels Limited
- Symbol: PSRAJ
- Existing Face Value & Paid-up Value: Rs. 10
- New Face Value & Paid-up Value: Rs. 2
- Split ratio: 1 share of Rs. 10 face value into 5 shares of Rs. 2 face value
- Effective date: July 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is a corporate action notification pursuant to the company's approved face value split, communicated by NSE Listing Department under Circular Ref. No. 1161/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75235).

## Compliance Requirements

Members and depository participants must ensure trading systems, order entry, and corporate action processing reflect the revised face value and adjusted share quantities/prices from the effective date. No action is required from retail investors as the adjustment is processed automatically by depositories.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 16, 2026
- Effective Date of Face Value Change: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split will proportionately increase the number of shares held by existing shareholders and adjust the market price accordingly, with no change in the overall value of holdings. This is a routine corporate action that may improve stock liquidity and affordability for retail investors but does not alter the company's fundamentals.
