## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has announced a change of market maker for Ideal Technoplast Industries Limited (Symbol: IDEALTECHO), an SME-listed company. Sunflower Broking Pvt. Ltd. will be replaced by Gainn Fintech Private Limited as the designated market maker effective June 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Ideal Technoplast Industries Limited (IDEALTECHO)
- Old Market Maker: Sunflower Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 13586)
- New Market Maker: Gainn Fintech Private Limited (Member Code: 90261)
- Effective Date: June 9, 2026
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/CMTR/74590, Circular Ref. No: 78/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update to the designated market maker assignment for an SME scrip under NSE's Capital Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are advised to take note of the change in market maker for IDEALTECHO. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond awareness of the updated market maker entity.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 5, 2026
- Effective Date of New Market Maker: June 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The change affects only the designated market maker for a single SME-listed scrip. Continuity of liquidity provision is maintained through the appointment of Gainn Fintech Private Limited as the replacement market maker. Members trading in IDEALTECHO should be aware of the new market maker from June 9, 2026.
