## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/SURV/74773, Circular Ref No: 455/2026) updating the list of securities under the high promoter and non-promoter encumbrance surveillance measure as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. Elpro International Limited (ELPROINTL) has been newly added to the framework and will attract a minimum 75% margin in the equity and equity derivatives segments from June 23, 2026. No securities have been removed from the framework in this update.

## Key Points

- **New addition (Annexure I):** Elpro International Limited (ELPROINTL, ISIN: INE579B01039) is shortlisted effective June 23, 2026; it also satisfies criteria at BSE.
- **Minimum 75% margin** will be applicable on all open positions as on June 22, 2026 and all new positions created from June 23, 2026 in both the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- **No exclusions (Annexure II):** No securities have been removed from the framework effective June 19, 2026.
- **Consolidated list (Annexure III):** 8 securities are now under the framework — AQYLON, BEDMUTHA, BLKASHYAP, ELPROINTL, JAYNECOIND, RKEC, STEELXIND, THYROCARE.
- This measure operates in conjunction with all other prevailing exchange-level measures and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion in this measure should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a continuation of Exchange circular NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. The update adds one security to the framework under SEBI (SAST) Regulation 28(3), which mandates additional margin requirements for listed companies where promoter and/or non-promoter encumbrance exceeds prescribed thresholds.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure the minimum 75% margin is collected on all open and new positions in ELPROINTL (equity and equity derivatives) from June 23, 2026.
- Members must apply the margin requirement on all open positions existing as of June 22, 2026 and on any new positions opened from June 23, 2026.
- Members must continue to apply existing margin requirements to all other securities in the consolidated list (Annexure III).
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026:** Circular issued.
- **June 19, 2026:** Effective date referenced for Annexure I shortlisting eligibility (per circular text); no exclusions effective this date.
- **June 22, 2026:** Last date for existing open positions in ELPROINTL before enhanced margin applies.
- **June 23, 2026:** Minimum 75% margin becomes applicable for ELPROINTL in equity and equity derivatives segments.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or planning positions in **Elpro International Limited (ELPROINTL)** face a significantly higher margin requirement (minimum 75%) from June 23, 2026 in both equity and derivatives segments, increasing the capital required to maintain or enter positions. This may reduce liquidity and increase the cost of trading in the stock. The 7 other securities already on the consolidated list (AQYLON, BEDMUTHA, BLKASHYAP, JAYNECOIND, RKEC, STEELXIND, THYROCARE) continue to be subject to the same enhanced margin regime. The measure is a regulatory risk-management tool and does not constitute a finding against the companies involved.
