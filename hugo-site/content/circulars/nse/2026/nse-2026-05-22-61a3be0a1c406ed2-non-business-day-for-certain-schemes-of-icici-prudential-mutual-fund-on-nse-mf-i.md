## Summary

NSE has informed members that certain schemes of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund will observe non-business days on **May 25, 2026** and **May 29, 2026** on the NSE MF Invest platform. During these dates, no subscriptions, redemptions, switches, SIPs, STPs, or SWPs will be permitted in the affected schemes through the platform.

## Key Points

- Non-business days designated for select ICICI Prudential schemes on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two separate non-business dates: **May 25, 2026** and **May 29, 2026**
- All transaction types suspended on those dates: Subscriptions, Redemptions, Switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs
- Notification issued pursuant to communication received from ICICI Prudential AMC
- Reference: NSE/NMFTM/74345 | Circular Ref. No: 1389/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification based on ICICI Prudential AMC's communication regarding non-business days for specific international/overseas fund-of-funds and index fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are advised to note the non-business days and ensure no transactions are processed in the listed schemes on May 25, 2026 and May 29, 2026 via the NSE MF Invest platform.
- Members should communicate this to relevant clients investing in the affected schemes.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Stable Equity Fund (FOF) — all plans/options, ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund (FOF) — all plans/options, ICICI Prudential Nasdaq 100 Index Fund — all plans/options, ICICI Prudential Passive Multi-Asset Fund of Funds — all plans/options, ICICI Prudential Strategic Metal and Energy Fund (truncated in source)
- **May 29, 2026**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Stable Equity Fund (FOF) — all plans/options, ICICI Prudential US Bluechip Equity Fund — all plans/options

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to investors transacting in the affected ICICI Prudential schemes (primarily international/overseas FOFs and index funds tracking US and global markets) via the NSE MF Invest platform on the specified dates. Investors should plan redemptions, subscriptions, or SIP/STP/SWP instructions around these non-business days to avoid transaction failures or delays. No broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
