## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in three Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) series issued by Nido Home Finance Limited, effective July 3, 2026, on account of maturity of these instruments. The action is taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations, Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in three NCD series of Nido Home Finance Limited
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the debentures
- Suspension applies from July 3, 2026
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine application of existing NSE trading regulations governing suspension of matured debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and market participants must cease trading in the following securities from the effective date:

| Symbol | ISIN | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 957NIDO26 | INE530L07210 | Maturity |
| 10NIDO26 | INE530L07228 | Maturity |
| NIDOZC26 | INE530L07236 | Maturity |

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Suspension:** July 3, 2026
- **Circular Date:** July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these specific Nido Home Finance NCD series, which will no longer be tradable on the exchange following their maturity. This is a standard, low-impact procedural notification affecting a narrow set of debt market participants rather than the broader equity market.
