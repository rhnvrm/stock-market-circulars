## Summary

NSE has notified all members that MIRC Electronics Limited will change its trading symbol from MIRCELECTR to ONIDA and its registered name to Onida Electronics Limited, effective June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing trading symbol MIRCELECTR will be replaced with ONIDA
- Company name changes from MIRC Electronics Limited to Onida Electronics Limited
- Change is effective from June 19, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/74716 (Circular Ref No: 0964/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The stock exchange is formally updating the company's name and symbol in its systems as notified by the Listing Department. All trading references, order books, and member systems must reflect the new symbol ONIDA and new name Onida Electronics Limited from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of the Exchange must update their systems and records to reflect the new symbol (ONIDA) and new company name (Onida Electronics Limited)
- Any references, reports, or client communications using the old symbol MIRCELECTR or old name MIRC Electronics Limited should be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 19, 2026 (name and symbol change comes into force)

## Impact Assessment

Traders, brokers, and investors holding or tracking MIRCELECTR must update their watchlists, alerts, and portfolio systems to use the new symbol ONIDA from June 19, 2026. The change is purely administrative in nature — the underlying security, ISIN, and company operations remain the same. Automated trading systems and data feeds should be updated before market open on June 19, 2026 to avoid disruption.
