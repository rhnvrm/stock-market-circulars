## Summary

HDFC Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF (Fund of Funds) under HDFC Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective March 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- HDFC Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 18, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform for subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available across Direct and Regular plans in Growth and IDCW options
- All six variants are classified under the Debt category
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/73314, Circular Ref. No: 1268/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond awareness. Members may facilitate client transactions in HDFC Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 18, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 17, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 18, 2026**: HDFC Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This circular provides routine operational information enabling members to offer six variants of HDFC Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF to their clients on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The schemes span Direct and Regular plan options with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) sub-options, all under the Debt category. ISINs range from INF179KC1KB2 to INF179KC1KG1. No disruption to existing market operations is expected.
