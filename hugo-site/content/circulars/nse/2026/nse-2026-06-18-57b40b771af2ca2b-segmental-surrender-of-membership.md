## Summary

NSE has approved requests from two trading members to surrender their membership in specific segments. J M Global Equities Private Limited has surrendered its Currency Derivatives (including IRF) segment membership, and R B K Share Broking Limited has surrendered its Futures & Options segment membership.

## Key Points

- J M Global Equities Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INZ000203636) surrendered Currency Derivatives including IRF segment, approved June 09, 2026
- R B K Share Broking Limited (SEBI Reg: INZ000225939) surrendered Futures & Options segment, approved June 11, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Compliance department (Ref: NSE/COMP/74757, Circular 57/2026)
- All members are requested to take note of these changes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular serves as a notification of approved membership segment surrenders in accordance with existing NSE membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from other members. This is an informational circular for all NSE members to note the change in membership status of the two firms listed.

## Important Dates

- J M Global Equities Private Limited – Currency Derivatives surrender approved: June 09, 2026
- R B K Share Broking Limited – Futures & Options surrender approved: June 11, 2026
- Circular date: June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender of segment memberships by these two brokers is an administrative matter. Clients of these firms trading in the respective segments would need to migrate to other brokers for those segment-specific activities. No broader market or systemic implications are expected.
