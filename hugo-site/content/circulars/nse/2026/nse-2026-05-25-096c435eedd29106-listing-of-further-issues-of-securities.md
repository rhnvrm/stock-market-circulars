## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 11 companies effective May 26, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The securities arise from ESOP allotments, a bonus issue abeyance resolution, and preferential allotments.

## Key Points

- **Effective date**: May 26, 2026 for trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- **Cipla Limited (CIPLA)**: 17,915 equity shares (Rs. 2 FV) allotted under ESOP on May 14, 2026; ISIN INE059A01026; distinctive nos. 807782632–807800546
- **Dixon Technologies (DIXON)**: 284,028 equity shares (Rs. 2 FV) allotted under ESOP on May 20, 2026; ISIN INE935N01020; distinctive nos. 60801894–61085921
- **ICICI Bank (ICICIBANK)**: 945 equity shares (Rs. 2 FV) allotted pursuant to bonus issue previously kept under abeyance, date of allotment June 24, 2017; ISIN INE090A01021
- **Power & Instrumentation Gujarat (PIGL)**: 354,339 equity shares (Rs. 10 FV, issue price Rs. 83.75) allotted under preferential issue on March 16, 2026; partial lock-in applies
- **PIGL (second tranche)**: 543,531 equity shares (Rs. 10 FV, issue price Rs. 83.75) allotted under preferential issue on March 18, 2026 (details truncated in source)
- Additional companies listed in subject line: Rossari Biotech, Tata Consumer Products, Tejas Networks, Tracxn Technologies, Trent Limited, VA Tech Wabag, Yes Bank
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares; market lot is 1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a standard notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify securities only by their designated NSE security codes for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (lot size: 1 for all listed securities)
- PIGL preferential allotment shares are subject to lock-in: 300,000 shares locked until December 1, 2027; 54,339 shares locked until December 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Cipla & Dixon**: Small ESOP-related dilution (17,915 and 284,028 shares respectively) relative to their large outstanding share bases; minimal market impact expected.

**ICICI Bank**: Resolution of 945 bonus shares previously held in abeyance since 2017; negligible dilution.

**PIGL**: Preferential allotment of approximately 897,870 shares (combined two tranches) at Rs. 83.75 per share represents a more significant relative addition for this smaller-cap company. Lock-in restrictions on the majority of shares until December 2027 limit near-term selling pressure.

**Other companies** (Rossari Biotech, Tata Consumer Products, Tejas Networks, Tracxn Technologies, Trent, VA Tech Wabag, Yes Bank): Details are referenced in the annexure but were not fully reproduced in the available content; similar ESOP or corporate action-related allotments are anticipated based on the circular's subject line.
