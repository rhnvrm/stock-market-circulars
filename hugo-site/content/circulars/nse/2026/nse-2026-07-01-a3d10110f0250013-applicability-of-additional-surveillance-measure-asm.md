## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying that five securities have met the criteria for inclusion under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Framework Stage I. This action is part of NSE's ongoing surveillance framework aimed at monitoring securities exhibiting high volatility, price movements not commensurate with fundamentals, or other risk indicators, and is not to be construed as adverse action against the concerned companies.

## Key Points

- Five securities have been shortlisted under Long Term ASM Framework Stage I effective July 02, 2026: Astra Microwave Products Limited (ASTRAMICRO), Exicom Tele-Systems Limited (EXICOM), Premier Explosives Limited (PREMEXPLN), Raghav Productivity Enhancers Limited (RPEL), and Teerth Gopicon Limited (TGL).
- No securities were shortlisted under Long Term ASM Stage IV, nor were any securities moved from Stage I to Stage II, Stage II to Stage III, or Stage I to Stage IV in this circular.
- A 100% margin will be applicable on all open positions as of July 03, 2026 and on new positions created from July 06, 2026 onwards for the listed securities.
- Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (shifted to Stage IV) would be moved from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) w.e.f. July 06, 2026; however, no securities fall under this category in the current circular.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this circular applies existing Long Term ASM Framework criteria (as established via prior circulars referenced: NSE/SURV/39265, 45111, 46557, 48506, 52090, 63362, 64066) to newly identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on the specified securities for open positions as of July 03, 2026 and new positions created from July 06, 2026 onwards.
- Members should factor these securities into their surveillance and risk management systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Effective date of inclusion of securities under Long Term ASM Stage I.
- July 03, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 06, 2026: Effective date for 100% margin applicability on open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion under Long Term ASM Stage I significantly increases the cost of trading in the affected securities (ASTRAMICRO, EXICOM, PREMEXPLN, RPEL, TGL) due to the 100% margin requirement, which may reduce trading volumes and liquidity in these stocks. The impact is limited to the specific securities named and does not affect the broader market, though it serves as a signal to investors regarding elevated volatility or surveillance concerns in these stocks.
