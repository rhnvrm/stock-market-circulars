## Summary

NSE has issued updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective May 25, 2026. Six securities are newly shortlisted into ESM Stage I (with series change to BE/ST from May 26, 2026), no securities move from Stage I to Stage II, three securities are downgraded from Stage II back to Stage I, and nine securities are excluded from the ESM framework entirely (reverting to EQ/SM series from May 25, 2026).

## Key Points

- 6 securities added to ESM Stage I w.e.f. May 25, 2026: ABSMARINE, BODALCHEM, MODINATUR, SHETHJI, TECHD, WOL3D
- All 6 new Stage I additions will move from EQ/SM to BE/ST series w.e.f. May 26, 2026 (trade-for-trade settlement)
- 0 securities moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II (Nil)
- 3 securities moved from ESM Stage II to Stage I w.e.f. May 25, 2026: BOHRAIND, DCMFINSERV, TGL
- 9 securities excluded from ESM framework w.e.f. May 25, 2026 (reverting to EQ/SM series)
- Consolidated ESM list contains 22 securities remaining in Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

The ESM framework is a surveillance mechanism applied by NSE to monitor and manage trading in securities exhibiting abnormal price or volume behaviour. Stage I imposes enhanced monitoring and trade-for-trade settlement (BE/ST series). Stage II involves stricter measures. Securities excluded from ESM revert to normal settlement (EQ/SM series).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants holding or trading the newly added ESM Stage I securities must note the series change from EQ/SM to BE/ST effective May 26, 2026
- Brokers must update client margins and settlement obligations for affected securities accordingly
- Securities excluded from ESM revert to normal EQ/SM settlement from May 25, 2026 — brokers should update trading systems
- No intraday trading is permitted for securities in BE/ST series; all trades are settled on a trade-for-trade basis

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: ESM Stage I additions, Stage II-to-Stage I movements, and ESM exclusions all take effect
- **May 26, 2026**: Newly added ESM Stage I securities (ABSMARINE, BODALCHEM, MODINATUR, SHETHJI, TECHD, WOL3D) move from EQ/SM to BE/ST series

## Impact Assessment

**Newly Added to ESM Stage I (High Impact):** ABSMARINE (ABS Marine Services), BODALCHEM (Bodal Chemicals), MODINATUR (Modi Naturals), SHETHJI (Shreeji Global FMCG), TECHD (TechD Cybersecurity), WOL3D (Wol 3D India) — these stocks will face trade-for-trade settlement, reducing liquidity and restricting intraday trading.

**Downgraded from Stage II to Stage I (Moderate Positive Impact):** BOHRAIND (Bohra Industries), DCMFINSERV (DCM Financial Services), TGL (Teerth Gopicon) — eased surveillance restrictions, though still under ESM monitoring.

**Excluded from ESM (Positive Impact):** BLUEPEBBLE, FRESHARA, LAXMICOT, MEGAFLEX, NIRMAN, ORTINGLOBE, PRESSTONIC, QUESTLAB, SIDDHICOTS — these 9 securities return to normal EQ/SM series from May 25, 2026, restoring full trading and intraday facility.

**Consolidated Stage II Securities (22 stocks remain under stricter measures):** AKSHOPTFBR, AVATAR, BANKA, EMAMIREAL, EMPOWER, EXCELLENT, GLFL, INSPIRE, LLOYDS, LOYALTEX, MCLEODRUSS, NAHARSPING, S&SPOWER, SADHNANIQ, SANGINITA, SETCO, SHANKARA, SHYAMTEL, SWASTIK, UCL, UNIVPHOTO, VILINBIO.
