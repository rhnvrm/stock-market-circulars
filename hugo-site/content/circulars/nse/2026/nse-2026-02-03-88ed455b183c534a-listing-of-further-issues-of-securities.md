## Summary

NSE has admitted further issues of equity shares to trading on the Capital Market segment for eight companies, all allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The listing becomes effective from February 04, 2026. Companies include Aditya Birla Capital Limited (116,888 shares), The Federal Bank Limited (61,552 shares), Lupin Limited (169,140 shares), Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (91,607 shares), Ramco Systems Limited (28,322 shares), Rossari Biotech Limited (2,000 shares), SIS Limited, and Tube Investments of India Limited.

## Key Points

- Total of 469,509 equity shares being listed across six companies (details provided)
- All shares issued under ESOP schemes
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size: 1 for all securities
- No lock-in period applicable
- Allotment dates range from January 17, 2026 to February 01, 2026

- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot sizes (Market Lot: 1)

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine administrative listing of ESOP-allotted shares. The number of shares being listed represents a small fraction of the total outstanding shares for each company. No lock-in restrictions apply, allowing immediate trading. This circular is procedural in nature and supports employee compensation programs while maintaining market transparency.
