## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in privately placed Non-Convertible Securities issued by Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ISIN: INE860H07CS9), effective March 05, 2026. The suspension is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A and is triggered by the redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE860H07CS9 (Aditya Birla Capital Limited) will be suspended from March 05, 2026.
- The suspension is due to redemption of the non-convertible security.
- This is a privately placed debt instrument traded on the NSE Debt Market segment.
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73119, Circular Ref. No: 0376/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension is invoked under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in debt securities upon events such as redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in ISIN INE860H07CS9 with effect from March 05, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and inform relevant clients holding or intending to trade this security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific ISIN (INE860H07CS9) of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. Since this is a privately placed NCS approaching redemption, the suspension is a standard procedural step. Investors holding this security should note the cessation of secondary market trading from March 05, 2026 and await redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
