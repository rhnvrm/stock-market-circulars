## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced a live trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Sunday, February 01, 2026, due to the presentation of the Union Budget. Trading will be conducted during morning session hours only, with standard market timings from 09:00 hrs to 17:00 hrs.

## Key Points

- Live trading session scheduled for Sunday, February 01, 2026
- Applies to Commodity Derivatives segment only
- Morning session timings: 09:00 hrs to 17:00 hrs
- Position limit/collateral value setup cut-off: 17:00 hrs
- Trade modification end time: 17:15 hrs
- Special session conducted on account of Union Budget presentation

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time special trading arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure readiness for Sunday trading operations
- Standard position limits and collateral requirements apply
- Members should note the cut-off times for position limits and trade modifications

## Important Dates

- **February 01, 2026 (Sunday)**: Live trading session for commodity derivatives
  - Trading hours: 09:00 hrs - 17:00 hrs
  - Position limit/collateral setup cut-off: 17:00 hrs
  - Trade modification end time: 17:15 hrs

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Members need to arrange for Sunday operations including staff availability and system readiness.

**Market Impact**: Medium - Provides opportunity for market participants to respond to Union Budget announcements in commodity derivatives segment on the same day.

**Segment Coverage**: Limited to Commodity Derivatives segment only; equity and other segments not mentioned in this circular.
