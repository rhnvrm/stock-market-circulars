## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 12 companies under Circular Ref. No. 0349/2026 (Download Ref. No.: NSE/CML/73065). All newly allotted securities are admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective March 02, 2026. The allotments are primarily under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP).

## Key Points

- Effective date for trading of all newly listed securities: **March 02, 2026**
- All securities are in the **EQ series** with a market lot of **1**
- All newly issued shares rank **pari passu** with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable for any of the listed allotments
- Companies covered: 360 ONE WAM LIMITED, Aadhar Housing Finance Limited, BLACKBUCK LIMITED (two separate allotments), BlueStone Jewellery and Lifestyle Limited, Fedbank Financial Services Limited, HDFC Asset Management Company Limited, Ideaforge Technology Limited, India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited, ITC Limited, Lenskart Solutions Limited, Viyash Scientific Limited, and Optiemus Infracom Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- **Regulation 3.1.1** of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governing admission of securities to dealings
- **Regulation 2.5.5** of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governing lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade securities using their designated NSE symbol codes only
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (lot size: 1 for all listed securities)
- No additional compliance actions required from trading members beyond standard listing protocols

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The newly issued ESOP shares represent marginal dilution for existing shareholders across all 12 companies. Notable share volumes include:
- **Aadhar Housing Finance**: 796,299 shares (ISIN: INE883F01010)
- **360 ONE WAM**: 384,008 shares (ISIN: INE466L01038)
- **BLACKBUCK**: 126,859 + 54,212 = 181,071 shares (ISIN: INE0UIZ01018)
- **BlueStone Jewellery**: 65,494 shares (ISIN: INE304W01038)

No disruption to normal trading is expected. Allotted shares become tradeable from March 02, 2026 with no lock-in restrictions.
