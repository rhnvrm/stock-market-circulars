## Summary

NSE has issued an amendment to its earlier circular (NSE/CML/73474 dated March 25, 2026) regarding the trading of Rights Entitlements for TIL Limited's Rights Issue. The amendment corrects the number of Rights Entitlements from 12,086,491 to 12,086,520, as requested by the company.

## Key Points

- Amendment to NSE circular NSE/CML/73474 dated March 25, 2026
- Symbol affected: TIL-RE1 (TIL Limited)
- Revised number of Rights Entitlements: 12,086,520 (previously 12,086,491)
- Net increase in Rights Entitlements: 29 units
- All other terms of the original circular remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory policy changes. This circular solely corrects a numerical figure in the Rights Entitlements count for TIL Limited as per the company's amendment and rectification request.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the updated Rights Entitlements figure (12,086,520) for TIL Limited (Symbol: TIL-RE1) and disregard the previously communicated figure of 12,086,491 for all relevant purposes.

## Important Dates

- Original circular date: March 25, 2026 (NSE/CML/73474)
- Amendment circular date: March 27, 2026 (NSE/CML/73484)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. The correction involves a difference of only 29 Rights Entitlements out of over 12 million, representing a negligible adjustment of approximately 0.00024%. This is an administrative rectification with no material market or operational consequences for exchange members or investors.
