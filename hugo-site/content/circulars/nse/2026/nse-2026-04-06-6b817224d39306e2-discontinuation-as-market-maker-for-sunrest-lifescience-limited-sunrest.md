## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has announced the discontinuation of SVCM Securities Private Limited (Member Code 90216) as the Market Maker for SUNREST LIFESCIENCE LIMITED (Symbol: SUNREST) on the SME platform. The change takes effect from April 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- SVCM Securities Private Limited (Member Code 90216) is the outgoing Market Maker for SUNREST LIFESCIENCE LIMITED
- The discontinuation is effective April 07, 2026
- The stock symbol affected is SUNREST on the NSE SME (Emerge) segment
- An updated list of Market Makers mapped to SME securities is available on the NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational update under the existing NSE SME Market Maker program, reflecting the removal of a specific market maker for one SME-listed company.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are advised to take note of this discontinuation
- Members dealing in SUNREST should refer to the updated Market Maker list on the NSE website for current market maker information
- Updated list available at: https://www.nseindia.com/static/products-services/emerge-sme-market-makers-list

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 06, 2026
- **Effective Date of Discontinuation:** April 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The discontinuation of SVCM Securities Private Limited as market maker for SUNREST LIFESCIENCE LIMITED may temporarily affect liquidity and bid-ask spreads for the stock on the NSE SME Emerge platform. Market participants trading in SUNREST should be aware of potential reduced liquidity from April 07, 2026 onwards, unless a new market maker is assigned. Members and investors in this SME stock should monitor the NSE's updated market maker list for any replacement appointment.
