## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Futures & Options segment on Saturday, July 18, 2026, to test trading modalities related to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, as previously announced in circular NSE/FAOP/74467 dated May 29, 2026. The mock will use a new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) during Trading Session-1, with members required to revert to the earlier version (7.8.8) for Trading Session-2, which follows existing (non-CAS) trading modalities. An additional mock session is scheduled for July 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 18, 2026, in the F&O segment.
- New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 will be used exclusively for Trading Session-1, available from 07:00 hours on July 18, 2026, via NSE Extranet path /faoftp/faocommon/Mock/18JULY2026/NEATPlus789.
- After Trading Session-1, members must reinstall and log in with the earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for Trading Session-2, which will follow existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes.
- Trading Session-2 will be conducted from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site.
- Trades executed in Trading Session-1 will not carry over to Trading Session-2; members must participate actively in all sessions.
- During Trading Session-1, stock futures price bands will be updated for alignment with CAS in the Cash Market segment, with the reference price based on VWAP of trades executed between 10:25 a.m. and 10:40 a.m.
- NEATPlus versions 7.8.8 and 7.8.9 are compatible with new broadcast parameters per circular NSE/MSD/74448 dated May 27, 2026.
- For Trading Session-2 and live trading from July 20, 2026, members must use NEATPlus version 7.8.8, downloadable from /faoftp/faocommon/NEATPlus788.
- An additional mock trading session for CAS testing in the Cash Market segment is scheduled for July 25, 2026; timings to be communicated later.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulations but operationalizes the previously announced introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment (per circular NSE/FAOP/74467 dated May 29, 2026) by scheduling mock testing of related trading modalities in the F&O segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download and install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 before Trading Session-1 on July 18, 2026.
- Members must reinstall NEATPlus version 7.8.8 after Trading Session-1 to participate in Trading Session-2.
- Members must use NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for live trading from July 20, 2026 onward.
- Members should refer to circular NSE/MSD/74511 dated June 2, 2026 for interactive connectivity details and follow general guidelines in the annexures for mock environment participation.
- Active participation in all mock trading sessions is required.

## Important Dates

- July 18, 2026 (Saturday): Mock trading session — Trading Session-1 (10:00–12:35 hrs) and Trading Session-2 from DR Site (16:45–19:30 hrs).
- July 20, 2026: Resumption of live trading using NEATPlus version 7.8.8.
- July 25, 2026: Additional mock trading session for CAS testing in Cash Market segment (timings to be announced).

## Impact Assessment

This circular is primarily an operational/technical notice for NSE trading members and does not directly affect listed securities, investors, or market pricing. Impact is limited to trading members needing to manage software version changes and participate in scheduled mock sessions to ensure readiness for the broader CAS rollout in the Equity Cash segment.
