## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited on the capital market segment, effective April 23, 2026. The security is a reset-rate bond maturing in April 2041, trading under symbol SDIL41 with ISIN INE507T07203.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective April 23, 2026
- Company: Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited
- Symbol: SDIL41, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1
- Issue description: SDIL RESET 2041
- Coupon rate: RESET (variable/reset rate)
- ISIN: INE507T07203
- Maturity date: April 19, 2041

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Circular is issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern the admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to identify this security only by its designated code (SDIL41) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- Members must ensure trades comply with the applicable series (N0) designation

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 23, 2026**: Circular comes into effect; security admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- **April 19, 2041**: Maturity date of the security

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with low market-wide impact. It enables trading of a single privately placed reset-rate bond issued by Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited. The security is relevant to institutional investors and bond market participants. The reset coupon structure means the interest rate will be periodically revised as per the terms of the instrument. No broad market or compliance impact is expected for general equity market participants.
