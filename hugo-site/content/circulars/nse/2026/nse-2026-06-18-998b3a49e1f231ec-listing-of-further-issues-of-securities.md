## Summary

NSE circular (Ref. No. 0988/2026, Download Ref. NSE/CML/74779) dated June 18, 2026 notifies the admission of further securities to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment for seven companies effective June 19, 2026. The allotments relate primarily to ESOP grants, with Ceinsys Tech Limited also listing shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- Seven companies are listing further securities on NSE Capital Market segment from June 19, 2026.
- Aether Industries Limited (AETHER): 3,677 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP on June 15, 2026 (ISIN: INE0BWX01014; distinctive numbers 132708900–132712576).
- Axis Bank Limited (AXISBANK): 148,481 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on June 16, 2026 (ISIN: INE238A01034; distinctive numbers 3110251194–3110399674).
- Ceinsys Tech Limited (CEINSYS): 3,096,515 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted on March 18, 2026 at Rs. 559.9 per share pursuant to conversion of warrants on a preferential basis (ISIN: INE016Q01014); subject to lock-in restrictions (see below).
- Emkay Global Financial Services Limited (EMKAY), Go Digit General Insurance Limited (GODIGIT), Mufin Green Finance Limited (MUFIN), and Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited (VIJAYALAB) are also included in the annexure (details partially truncated in source document).
- All newly listed securities are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies.
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities listed.
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission of securities; Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes and approved lot sizes for all trading in the newly admitted securities.
- Ceinsys Tech shareholders subject to lock-in must observe the restrictions as specified in Annexure A (see Important Dates below).

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Allotment date for Aether Industries ESOP shares.
- **June 16, 2026**: Allotment date for Axis Bank ESOP shares.
- **March 18, 2026**: Allotment date for Ceinsys Tech warrant conversion shares.
- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date for admission to dealings on NSE for all seven companies.
- **Ceinsys Tech lock-in schedule (Annexure A)**:
  - 1,489,086 shares (distinctive nos. 17841147–19330232): lock-in expiry December 30, 2026.
  - 1,428,826 shares (distinctive nos. 19330233–20759058): lock-in expiry December 31, 2027.
  - 178,603 shares (distinctive nos. 20759059–20937661): lock-in expiry December 31, 2027.

## Impact Assessment

The listing is routine in nature for most companies—ESOP shares represent small additions to existing share capital for Aether Industries (3,677 shares) and Axis Bank (148,481 shares). The Ceinsys Tech listing is more significant at 3,096,515 shares (warrant conversions at Rs. 559.9) with a meaningful portion subject to lock-in until end of 2026 and 2027, limiting near-term float impact. Traders and investors in the seven named companies should note the incremental dilution and, for Ceinsys Tech, the lock-in schedule before the free float changes materially.
