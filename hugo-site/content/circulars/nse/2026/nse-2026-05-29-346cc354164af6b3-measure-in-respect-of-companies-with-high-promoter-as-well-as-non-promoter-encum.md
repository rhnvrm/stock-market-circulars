## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued its periodic update (Circular Ref. 401/2026) to the encumbrance surveillance measure applicable to companies with high Promoter and non-Promoter encumbrance under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. This update is a continuation of Exchange circular NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. No new securities are being added in this cycle, one security exits the framework, and six securities remain on the consolidated list subject to a minimum 75% margin requirement.

## Key Points

- No securities are being newly added to the encumbrance surveillance measure in this update (Annexure I is Nil).
- Gayatri Highways Limited (GAYAHWS, ISIN: INE287Z01012) is removed from the framework effective June 1, 2026.
- Six securities remain on the consolidated list and continue to attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The measure applies to all open positions as on June 2, 2026 and all new positions from June 3, 2026 onward.
- This measure operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion in this framework should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the powers of NSE's Surveillance department in furtherance of SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, Regulation 28(3), which mandates monitoring of companies where promoters or non-promoters have pledged or encumbered a significant portion of their shareholding. The framework itself is unchanged; only the list of covered securities is updated.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure a minimum 75% margin is collected on all positions in the listed securities in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The 75% margin requirement applies to all open positions as on June 2, 2026 and all new positions created from June 3, 2026.
- Members are advised to inform their clients about the applicable margin requirements for the securities on the consolidated list.
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Gayatri Highways Limited (GAYAHWS) exits the encumbrance measure framework.
- **June 2, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to the 75% margin requirement.
- **June 3, 2026**: Enhanced 75% margin becomes effective for open positions as of June 2, 2026 and all new positions.
