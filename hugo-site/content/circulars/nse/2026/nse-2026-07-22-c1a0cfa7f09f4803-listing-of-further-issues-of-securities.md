## Summary

NSE has notified, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, the listing of further issues of securities of eleven companies effective July 23, 2026: Apollo Micro Systems Limited, Ather Energy Limited, Balu Forge Industries Limited, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited, The Federal Bank Limited, ICICI Bank Limited, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited, IndoStar Capital Finance Limited, SBI Life Insurance Company Limited, Sterlite Technologies Limited and Tata Elxsi Limited. The designated security codes and lot sizes for these newly admitted securities are specified in the Annexure to the circular, per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Symbol: APOLLO, ISIN: INE713T01028) had three separate preferential allotment tranches of equity shares (Re. 1 face value, issue price Rs. 114) admitted to trading:
  - 2,143,095 shares allotted 23-Jun-2026 (distinctive numbers 369456418–371599512)
  - 3,385,362 shares allotted 08-Jun-2026 (distinctive numbers 357305441–360690802)
  - 8,765,615 shares allotted 17-Jun-2026 (distinctive numbers 360690803–369456417)
  - All tranches are pari passu with existing equity, market lot 1, and locked in until 23-Jan-2027
- Ather Energy Limited (Symbol: ATHERENERG) further issue shares are also listed; specific allotment details for Ather Energy and the remaining companies (Balu Forge Industries, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance, Federal Bank, ICICI Bank, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, IndoStar Capital Finance, SBI Life Insurance, Sterlite Technologies, Tata Elxsi) are provided in the full Annexure but were not captured in this excerpt
- Trading in these securities on the NSE system will be identified only by their designated security codes and traded in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine administrative listing notification under existing NSE Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5), not a change to rules or regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure that trading in the newly listed securities is conducted using the designated security codes and lot sizes specified in the Annexure effective July 23, 2026. No other action is required from members beyond system-level recognition of the new listings.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Circular issued (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75344; Circular Ref. No: 1201/2026)
- July 23, 2026: Effective date for listing of the further-issued securities on NSE Capital Market segment
- Apollo Micro Systems lock-in for the preferential allotment shares expires 23-Jan-2027

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact as it is a routine procedural listing of further-issued shares (largely preferential allotments) for existing NSE-listed companies rather than new IPO listings. For Apollo Micro Systems shareholders and traders, over 14.2 million additional shares from preferential allotments become tradable (subject to lock-in until January 2027), which increases the free-float base incrementally once the lock-in lifts. Similar further-issue listings for the other ten companies (Ather Energy, Balu Forge Industries, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance, Federal Bank, ICICI Bank, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, IndoStar Capital Finance, SBI Life Insurance, Sterlite Technologies, and Tata Elxsi) modestly expand each company's tradable share base. Overall market impact is low, though it is relevant for shareholders tracking dilution and share capital changes in these specific stocks.
