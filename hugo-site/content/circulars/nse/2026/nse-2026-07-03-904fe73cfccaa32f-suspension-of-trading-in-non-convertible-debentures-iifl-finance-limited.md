## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) series issued by IIFL Finance Limited, effective July 06, 2026. The suspension is being carried out under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, with maturity of the securities cited as the reason.

## Key Points

- Trading in two IIFL Finance Limited NCD series will be suspended from July 06, 2026.
- Symbol 9IIFL26 (ISIN INE530B07559) is being suspended due to maturity.
- Symbol IIFL210425 (ISIN INE530B07542) is being suspended due to maturity.
- The action applies to the debt market segment and does not affect IIFL Finance's equity shares.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine administrative action taken under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the specified ISINs (INE530B07559 and INE530B07542) from the effective date and ensure any open positions or settlement obligations related to these NCDs are appropriately closed out prior to suspension.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Suspension: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these specific NCD series, which are being suspended as a natural consequence of reaching their maturity date. There is no indication of credit or operational distress at IIFL Finance Limited, and the equity segment is unaffected. Debenture holders should expect redemption proceeds in line with the maturity terms of these instruments.
