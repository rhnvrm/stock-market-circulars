## Summary

NSE has issued its periodic circular updating the list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. PPAP Automotive Limited has been newly included in ESM Stage I, while Peninsula Land Limited has been moved from Stage I to Stage II. No securities have been shifted from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities have been excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM (covering both Stage I and Stage II) is also provided.

## Key Points

- PPAP Automotive Limited (Symbol: PPAP, ISIN: INE095I01015) has been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I.
- Peninsula Land Limited (Symbol: PENINLAND, ISIN: INE138A01028) has moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II.
- No securities moved from Stage II back to Stage I this cycle.
- No securities were excluded from the ESM framework this cycle.
- A consolidated list of all ESM securities (including names such as Aarey Drugs & Pharmaceuticals, Accord Synergy, Aksh Optifibre, and others) continues to remain in force under either Stage I or Stage II.
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ESM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine update under the existing ESM framework referenced in prior circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; only the list of applicable securities and their stages is updated.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in PPAP Automotive Limited as of July 14, 2026, and on new positions created from July 15, 2026.
- PPAP Automotive Limited's trading series will shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) with effect from July 15, 2026.
- Peninsula Land Limited, moving to Stage II, will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction with effect from July 14, 2026.
- Members should refer to the NSE FAQs on ESM for further procedural guidance and direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Effective date for 100% margin on open positions in PPAP; effective date for Peninsula Land's Stage II Trade-for-Trade/Periodic Call Auction treatment.
- July 15, 2026: Effective date for PPAP's shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment; margin applies to new positions created on/after this date.

## Impact Assessment

The move to ESM Stage I imposes a 100% margin requirement and shifts PPAP Automotive Limited to trade-for-trade settlement, significantly restricting trading flexibility and liquidity for the stock. Peninsula Land Limited's progression to Stage II adds a 2% price band under periodic call auction, further tightening trading conditions and reducing intraday volatility potential. These measures are targeted at the specific securities named and do not have broader market-wide implications, though investors and traders holding these stocks should adjust their strategies given the reduced liquidity and stricter margin/settlement requirements.
