## Summary

NSE Clearing Ltd. (NCL) has issued the CBRICS (Corporate Bond Reporting and Integrated Clearing System) settlement calendar for August 2026. The circular specifies settlement dates and their corresponding settlement numbers for corporate bond transfers to be executed via NSDL/CDSL using 'On-market' settlement mode.

## Key Points

- Applicable to Participants/Custodians dealing in CBRICS corporate bond settlements
- Market Type: Corporate Bond
- Clearing Member Name: NSE Clearing Ltd.
- CM-BP ID: IN568177
- 20 settlement dates listed for August 2026, each mapped to a specific settlement number (e.g., 03-Aug-26 → 2608003, through 31-Aug-26 → 2608031)
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) are not valid for CBRICS platform settlement, effective since September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic settlement calendar issuance under the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and Custodians must transfer securities for CBRICS settlement strictly as per the listed settlement dates and settlement numbers, using the 'On-market' settlement mode and the correct CM-BP ID (IN568177).

## Important Dates

Settlement dates for August 2026: 03, 04, 05, 06, 07, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, 24, 25, 27, 28, and 31 August 2026, each with a corresponding settlement number as detailed in the circular.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact and is operational in nature, relevant primarily to Participants and Custodians involved in corporate bond settlement via the CBRICS platform. It ensures continuity and proper scheduling of debt segment settlements for the upcoming month with no effect on trading, listing, or broader compliance matters.
