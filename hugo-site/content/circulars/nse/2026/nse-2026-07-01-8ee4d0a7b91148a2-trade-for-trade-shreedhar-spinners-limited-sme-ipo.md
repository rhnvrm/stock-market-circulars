## Summary

NSE has notified that Shreedhar Spinners Limited (Symbol: SHREEDHAR), an SME IPO listed security, will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to the rolling settlement segment (Series: SM) with effect from July 15, 2026. This follows an earlier Exchange circular (NSE/CML/74940 dated June 30, 2026) and is in line with SEBI guidelines under circular reference no. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Security: Shreedhar Spinners Limited (Symbol: SHREEDHAR)
- Moving from trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to rolling segment (Series: SM)
- Effective date: July 15, 2026
- Issued pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Follow-up to Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74940 dated June 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational segment transfer implementing existing SEBI guidelines on trade for trade classification, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants should update their systems to reflect the change in series (from ST to SM) for the SHREEDHAR symbol effective July 15, 2026, and ensure trades are settled under the applicable rolling settlement norms thereafter.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Prior Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74940 issued
- July 1, 2026: Current circular issued
- July 15, 2026: Effective date for transfer to rolling segment (Series: SM)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from trade for trade to rolling settlement segment reduces settlement restrictions on SHREEDHAR shares, potentially improving liquidity and trading flexibility for market participants holding or trading this SME-listed stock. The impact is limited to this single security and its stakeholders.
