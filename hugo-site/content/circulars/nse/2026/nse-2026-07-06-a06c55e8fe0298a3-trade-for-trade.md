## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has issued a circular under the periodic (fortnightly) review of securities eligible for Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement. Effective July 9, 2026 (Thursday), four securities currently trading in the Rolling (EQ) segment — GRADIENTE, VIPULLTD, BTTL and GANGAFORGE — and one SME security (CBAZAAR, series SM) will move to the Trade for Trade segment (series BE and ST respectively) with a price band of 5% or lower. These securities will no longer be available in the Rolling/EQ or SM segments. Separately, four securities already under surveillance action (VIJIFIN, GOLDTECH in series BE/BZ, and DIVYADHAN, ACCORD in series ST/SZ) will continue in the T2T segment as they remain eligible under the current fortnightly review.

## Key Points

- Securities moving from Rolling segment (EQ) to Trade for Trade (BE) with 5% or lower price band: GRADIENTE (Gradiente Infotainment Limited), VIPULLTD (Vipul Limited), BTTL (Bhilwara Technical Textiles Limited), GANGAFORGE (Ganga Forging Limited).
- Security moving from Rolling segment (SM) to Trade for Trade (ST) with 5% or lower price band: CBAZAAR (Net Avenue Technologies Limited).
- Criteria applied for shifting: Price Earnings Multiple, Price Variation & Market Capitalization.
- Securities continuing in T2T under existing surveillance action: VIJIFIN (Viji Finance Limited) and GOLDTECH (Aion-Tech Solutions Limited) in series BE/BZ; DIVYADHAN (Divyadhan Recycling Industries Limited) and ACCORD (Accord Synergy Limited) in series ST/SZ.
- Settlement for T2T securities will be done on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off allowed.
- The shift is a surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned companies.

## Regulatory Changes

Under Capital Market Segment Trading Regulations Part-A, Clause 2.6, NSE is reclassifying the listed securities from the Rolling segment (EQ/SM series) to the Trade for Trade segment (BE/ST series), subject to a price band of 5% or lower. This is part of NSE's periodic (fortnightly) surveillance review process for identifying securities meeting criteria such as price-earnings multiple, price variation, and market capitalization thresholds.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in series for the affected securities effective July 9, 2026, and ensure trades in these securities are settled on a trade-to-trade basis without netting. Members should exercise adequate precaution while trading in these securities given the settlement restriction. Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **July 6, 2026**: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/SURV/75058, Circular Ref. No: 504/2026).
- **July 9, 2026 (Thursday)**: Effective date for shift of GRADIENTE, VIPULLTD, BTTL, GANGAFORGE and CBAZAAR to the Trade for Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification restricts intraday trading and netting for the affected securities, requiring full delivery-based settlement, which typically reduces trading volumes and liquidity in these stocks. Investors and traders holding or transacting in GRADIENTE, VIPULLTD, BTTL, GANGAFORGE and CBAZAAR should note the settlement change from July 9, 2026. The measure is a routine surveillance action rather than a reflection of any adverse development at the companies, but it can affect short-term price behavior and trading strategies for these names.
