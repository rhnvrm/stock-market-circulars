## Summary

NSE has implemented a temporary restriction on lumpsum subscriptions in Axis Gold Fund of Fund (a Fund of Fund investing in Axis Gold ETF) on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective June 8, 2026. This follows an addendum issued by Axis Asset Management Company limiting lumpsum purchase and switch-in transactions to INR 10 lakh per PAN per month.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum subscriptions and switch-ins into Axis Gold Fund of Fund are restricted to a maximum of INR 10 lakh per PAN per month.
- The restriction applies to transactions received after cut-off time (3:00 PM) on June 8, 2026.
- Transactions exceeding this limit are liable to be rejected.
- The suspension is temporary and will remain in effect until further notice from Axis AMC.
- Separately, Axis Gold ETF restricts large investor subscriptions exceeding Rs. 25 crores directly with Axis Mutual Fund; Market Makers/Authorized Participants are exempt.

## Regulatory Changes

Axis AMC issued Notice-cum-Addendum No. 25/2026-27 dated June 5, 2026, amending the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) for Axis Gold ETF and Axis Gold Fund to temporarily restrict subscriptions. NSE has implemented corresponding changes on the NSE MF Invest Platform per Circular Ref. No. 1419/2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members on NSE MF Invest Platform must ensure that lumpsum subscription/switch-in orders for Axis Gold Fund of Fund do not exceed INR 10 lakh per PAN per month for transactions post cut-off time.
- Orders exceeding the limit will be rejected; members should inform investors accordingly.
- All other terms and conditions of the SID and KIM remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- **June 5, 2026**: Addendum issued by Axis AMC (No. 25/2026-27).
- **June 8, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Effective cut-off time after which the lumpsum subscription restriction applies.
- **Until further notice**: Restriction remains in force until Axis AMC lifts it.

## Impact Assessment

The restriction primarily affects retail and HNI investors seeking to make large lumpsum investments in gold via the Fund of Fund route on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) and smaller lumpsum purchases below the threshold are unaffected. This is a temporary liquidity management measure by Axis AMC, likely triggered by elevated demand for gold-linked investments. Market Makers and Authorized Participants in Axis Gold ETF are explicitly exempt, so secondary market liquidity of the ETF itself should remain unaffected.
