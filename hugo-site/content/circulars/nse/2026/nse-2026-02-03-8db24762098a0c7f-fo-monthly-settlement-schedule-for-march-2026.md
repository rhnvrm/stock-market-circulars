## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has released the settlement schedule for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for March 2026. The circular provides a comprehensive list of 19 trading days and their corresponding settlement dates, enabling members to plan their clearing and settlement activities.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers the period from March 2, 2026 to March 30, 2026
- Total of 19 trading days scheduled for March 2026
- Most settlements occur T+2 days (two business days after trade date)
- Notable exceptions include March 18 and 19 trades settling on March 20, 2026
- Final March trading day (March 30) settles on April 2, 2026
- Circular issued by Associate Vice President Anil Suvarna
- Members can contact clearing operations at 1800 266 0050 or fao_clearing_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular. This is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members should note the settlement dates for operational and cash management purposes.
