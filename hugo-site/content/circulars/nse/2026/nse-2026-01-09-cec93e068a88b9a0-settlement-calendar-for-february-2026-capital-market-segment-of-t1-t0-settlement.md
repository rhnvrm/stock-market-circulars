## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has published the settlement calendar for February 2026 for the Capital Market Segment. The circular provides detailed schedules for five settlement types: Normal Rolling (T+1) settlement type M, Normal Auction settlement type A, Trade for Trade (T+1) settlement type Z, ITP for SME (T+1) settlement type B, and Normal Rolling (T+0) settlement type 5. This is issued in pursuance of Regulation 6.2 of the National Securities Clearing Corporation (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers February 2026 for Capital Market Segment
- Five distinct settlement types with separate schedules provided in annexures
- Normal Rolling (T+1) settlement type M covers 20 settlements from 2026022 to 2026041
- Auction settlements run from settlement numbers 2026022 to 2026041
- Trade period spans from February 2nd to March 2nd, 2026
- Special note: Shortages of 2025035 will be merged with 2025036 with no auction in 2025035
- Custodial confirmation dates align with trade end dates for most settlements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a routine notification under existing Regulation 6.2 of the National Securities Clearing Corporation (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the published settlement schedules
- Custodial confirmations must be completed by specified dates
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled according to the dates specified in each annexure
- Members should note the merger of shortages from settlement 2025035 into 2025036
- For clarifications, contact the help desk at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or securities_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Calendar Period**: February 2026
- **First Trade Start Date**: February 2, 2026 (Settlement 2026022)
- **Last Trade End Date**: March 2, 2026 (Settlement 2026041)
- **First Settlement Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Last Settlement Date**: March 4, 2026
- **Note**: Settlement schedules account for weekends and the trading holiday on January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine administrative circular providing operational schedules.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard monthly calendar that enables market participants to plan their settlement activities, fund management, and custodial operations for February 2026. The calendar maintains the existing T+1 and T+0 settlement framework without introducing changes to settlement cycles or processes.

**Stakeholder Impact**: All clearing members, custodians, and PCMs need to reference these schedules for their February 2026 operations, but this represents business-as-usual activity with no new compliance burdens or operational changes.
