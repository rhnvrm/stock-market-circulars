## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENGG) on the Capital Market segment, effective May 21, 2026. The listing involves 6,21,82,965 equity shares converted from partly paid-up to fully paid-up status under ISIN INE093R01011.

## Key Points

- 6,21,82,965 equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Shares are a conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Symbol: LLOYDSENGG, Series: EQ, ISIN: INE093R01011
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are Pari Passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- Previously allotted under temporary ISIN IN8093R01010 in dematerialized mode per SEBI circular guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)
- SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 regarding ISIN activation for additional share issues

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code LLOYDSENGG for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The permanent ISIN INE093R01011 replaces the temporary ISIN IN8093R01010 for dematerialized shares

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 20, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 6.22 crore fully paid-up equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited to the NSE Capital Market segment. This increases the tradable float for LLOYDSENGG and may have a moderate impact on liquidity and price discovery. The conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up shares is a standard corporate action and is not expected to cause significant market disruption. Distinctive numbers range from 1,39,27,57,519 to 1,45,48,73,367, covering two tranches of shares.
