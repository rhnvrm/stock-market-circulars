## Summary

NSE has conducted its periodic review of securities under the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, in coordination with other exchanges, and identified six securities eligible for relaxation to a lower GSM stage. This relaxation takes effect from July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- The review was conducted under the GSM framework established via earlier circulars (NSE/SURV/34262 dated February 23, 2017; NSE/SURV/42790 dated November 29, 2019; NSE/SURV/59425 dated November 17, 2023; NSE/SURV/64066 dated September 20, 2024; and NSE/SURV/74254 dated May 15, 2026).
- Six securities have been moved to a lower (relaxed) GSM stage:
  - ANSALAPI (Ansal Properties & Infrastructure Limited) — Stage II to I
  - BLUECHIP (Blue Chip India Limited) — Stage III to II
  - WINSOME (Winsome Yarns Limited) — Stage II to I
  - GFSTEELS (Grand Foundry Limited) — Stage IV to III
  - MORARJEE (Morarjee Textiles Limited) — Stage II to I
  - UNIVAFOODS (Univa Foods Limited) — Stage IV to III
- Further details on the GSM framework are available via NSE's published FAQs.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework was introduced; this circular implements a periodic relaxation review under the existing GSM framework, reducing surveillance stage levels for the listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should update their trading systems and client communications to reflect the revised (lower) GSM stage for the six listed securities effective the stated date. Trading restrictions applicable to each new stage (e.g., reduced price bands, trade-for-trade settlement, additional surveillance deposits) should be applied per the updated stage.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of relaxation: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The relaxation reduces surveillance-related trading restrictions on the six affected securities, potentially improving liquidity and investor sentiment for these specific stocks. The overall market impact is limited given the small number and relatively low profile of the securities involved.
