## Summary

LIC Mutual Fund's LIC MF Technology Fund is being reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from March 19, 2026. Members are informed that subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) will be available across all six scheme variants.

## Key Points

- LIC MF Technology Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions from March 19, 2026
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will also be available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants are covered under this circular
- Schemes are equity category funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard platform availability notification for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members using the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes for client transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **March 19, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of LIC MF Technology Fund for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This circular enables retail and institutional investors to transact in LIC MF Technology Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investors access to an equity technology sector fund across Direct and Regular plan options with Growth and IDCW variants.
