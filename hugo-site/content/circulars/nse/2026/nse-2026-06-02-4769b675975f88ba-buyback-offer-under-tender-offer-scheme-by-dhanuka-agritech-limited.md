## Summary

NSE has notified all Capital Market segment members of Dhanuka Agritech Limited's upcoming buyback offer under the Tender Offer scheme. The offer will use NSE's web-based bidding platform (https://eipo.nseindia.com) and runs from June 4 to June 10, 2026, at a fixed price of Rs. 1400/- per share for up to 5,00,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Offer type: Buyback via Tender Offer (fixed price) at Rs. 1400/- per share
- Issue size: Up to 5,00,000 equity shares (ISIN: INE435G01025)
- Bidding platform: NSE web-based IPO bidding system at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- Existing IPO platform user IDs are valid for this offer
- Members without access must use the admin terminal (login: Adminxxxxx where xxxxx is Member Code)
- All trading members of the Capital Market segment are eligible to participate
- Merchant Banker: Sundae Capital Advisors Private Limited
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The offer is conducted per the existing Tender Offer Scheme framework under NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NSE web-based bidding platform for participation
- Members without existing access must log in via admin terminal and create branch/user accounts before bidding
- Custodial confirmations must be submitted by 4:00 p.m. on the issue closure date
- All bids must be submitted during the designated bidding window (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Issue Open Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Issue Close Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Bidding Hours:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (daily)
- **Custodial Confirmation on Closure Day:** 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to trading members of the NSE Capital Market segment and shareholders of Dhanuka Agritech Limited (DHANUKA). The buyback at Rs. 1400/- per share for up to 5,00,000 shares represents a liquidity event for existing shareholders across all investor categories (FII, MF, Insurance, Banks/FIs, QIBs, Corporates, Individuals, and other non-institutional investors). Market impact is moderate and confined to DHANUKA's stock and its shareholder base.
