## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Navi Finserv Limited, effective July 3, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the maturity of both securities, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two NCDs of Navi Finserv Limited are being suspended from trading due to maturity.
- Symbol 1102NFL26 (ISIN: INE342T07403) — suspended effective July 3, 2026.
- Symbol 1050NFL26 (ISIN: INE342T07387) — suspended effective July 3, 2026.
- The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard operational action under existing NSE trading regulations (Regulation 3.1.2, Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A) for securities reaching their maturity date.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are notified of the suspension. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond ceasing to trade in these two NCD symbols from July 3, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of these two specific Navi Finserv NCD instruments. As the suspension is due to maturity, investors will receive their principal repayment as per the debenture terms. There is no broader market or systemic impact. This is a routine end-of-lifecycle action for debt securities.
