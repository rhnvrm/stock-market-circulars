## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Apex Hybrid Long-Short Fund NFO by Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from **March 06, 2026 to March 18, 2026**. The fund comprises six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options. Allotment is scheduled for March 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: March 06, 2026 to March 18, 2026
- Fund house: Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund
- Fund name: Apex Hybrid Long-Short Fund
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as **Equity**
- Allotment date: March 25, 2026
- Payment deadline: March 18, 2026 by 11:59 PM (clear funds must be in designated Clearing Corporation account)
- Reference: NSE/NMFTM/73137, Circular Ref. No. 1247/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account **on or before March 18, 2026, 11:59 PM**.
- Members should facilitate investor transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform within the specified NFO window.

## Important Dates

- **March 06, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **March 18, 2026, 11:59 PM**: NFO subscription closes; payment deadline
- **March 25, 2026**: Allotment date for all six scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact, affecting only NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in a hybrid long-short equity strategy from Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund can subscribe during the NFO window. The fund's equity category classification and hybrid long-short strategy may appeal to investors seeking market-neutral or hedged equity exposure.
