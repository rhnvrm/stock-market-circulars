## Summary

NSE has admitted further equity shares of 10 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective January 14, 2026. All shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) with market lot size of 1 share and no lock-in restrictions. The listings are pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 10 companies listing additional equity shares from ESOP allotments
- Total securities: 386,600+ shares across all companies
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size: 1 share for all securities
- No lock-in period applicable
- Trading effective from January 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to note the new ISIN codes and distinctive number ranges
- Securities to be identified by designated codes on trading system
- Trading permitted only in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 share)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 13, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 14, 2026 (trading commences)
- **Allotment Dates:** December 30, 2025 to January 11, 2026 (company-specific)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine ESOP allotments representing small percentages of total outstanding shares for each company.

**Liquidity Impact:** Negligible increase in tradable float.

**Investor Impact:** ESOP recipients can trade shares from effective date with no restrictions. All shares rank pari passu with existing equity, maintaining uniform shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update systems with new distinctive number ranges for accurate settlement and depository operations.
