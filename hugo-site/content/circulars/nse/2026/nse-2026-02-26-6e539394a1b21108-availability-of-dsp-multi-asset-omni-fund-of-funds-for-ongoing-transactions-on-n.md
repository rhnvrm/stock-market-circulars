## Summary

NSE has informed its members that DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds, managed by DSP Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with effect from February 27, 2026. The fund will also be available for SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Key Points

- DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds reopens for ongoing transactions from February 27, 2026
- Available for subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants are available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/73000, Circular Ref. No: 1223/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members may facilitate client transactions in the listed schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard availability notice for mutual fund schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors and distributors using the platform can transact in DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds from February 27, 2026. No impact on equity markets or existing trading operations.
