## Summary

NSE has issued a circular placing 8 securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I framework effective June 12, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 15, 2026 on all open positions as on June 12, 2026 and new positions created thereafter. No securities have been added to ST-ASM Stage II, nor have any securities moved between stages.

## Key Points

- 8 securities added to ST-ASM Stage I effective June 12, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- ST-ASM Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- No securities added to Stage II; no inter-stage movements
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria are subject to elevated margin requirements as per the ST-ASM framework previously established under circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members/Brokers**: Must collect enhanced margins (minimum 50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) from clients on all open and new positions in the listed securities from June 15, 2026.
- **Market Participants**: Must ensure sufficient margin coverage for open positions as on June 12, 2026 before the enhanced margin regime takes effect on June 15, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of securities in ST-ASM Stage I
- **June 15, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become applicable on all open positions (as on June 12, 2026) and new positions
