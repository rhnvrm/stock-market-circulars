## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of additional equity shares of Goldiam International Limited, issued pursuant to a bonus issue, on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 14, 2026. The shares will trade under the existing symbol GOLDIAM.

## Key Points

- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.
- Company: Goldiam International Limited.
- Symbol: GOLDIAM.
- ISIN: INE025B01025 (unchanged, as bonus shares carry the same ISIN as existing equity shares).
- Number of new securities admitted: 3,76,39,281 equity shares.
- Distinctive number range: 115097338 to 152736618.
- New shares will be identified for trading purposes only by their designated security codes.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural admission of bonus shares to trading under existing listing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to note the effective trading date and ensure systems reflect the additional share quantity for settlement and trading purposes from July 14, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 10, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: July 13, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing & Trading: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradable share count of Goldiam International Limited following the bonus issue, which typically improves liquidity and affordability per share without affecting overall market capitalization. Impact is limited to GOLDIAM shareholders and traders; no broader market-wide implications.
