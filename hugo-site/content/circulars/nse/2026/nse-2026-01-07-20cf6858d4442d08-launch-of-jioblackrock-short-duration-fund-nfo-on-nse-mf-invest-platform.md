## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of JioBlackRock Short Duration Fund, a debt category scheme from JioBlackRock Mutual Fund. The NFO will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from January 08, 2026 to January 13, 2026 until 06:00 PM, with allotment scheduled for January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme Name: JioBlackRock Short Duration Fund
- ISIN: INF22M001143
- Category: Debt
- NFO Period: January 08, 2026 to January 13, 2026 (until 06:00 PM)
- Allotment Date: January 19, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Fund House: JioBlackRock Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 19, 2026 at 08:00 PM
- Members should facilitate NFO transactions for investors during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **January 08, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **January 13, 2026 (06:00 PM)**: NFO closing date and time
- **January 19, 2026 (08:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds receipt in Clearing Corporation account
- **January 19, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It provides members with the necessary details to facilitate investor participation in the JioBlackRock Short Duration Fund NFO. The circular is purely informational for NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions and does not affect equity markets or trading operations. Contact support is available via toll-free number 1800 2100 940 or email at support@nseinvest.com.
