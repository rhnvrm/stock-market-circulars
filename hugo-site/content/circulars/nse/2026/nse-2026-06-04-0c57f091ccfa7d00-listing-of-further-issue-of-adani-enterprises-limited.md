## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 766 additional equity shares of Adani Enterprises Limited (symbol: ADANIENT) on the Capital Market segment, arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares. The listing is effective from June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- 766 equity shares of Adani Enterprises Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- ISIN: INE423A01024 (Face Value: Re. 1/-, Paid-up Value: Re. 1/-)
- Market lot: 1; Pari Passu: Yes; Lock-in: Not Applicable
- Shares credited under temporary ISIN IN8423A01015 as per SEBI circular guidelines pending activation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the designated security code ADANIENT (Series: EQ) for all trades in these securities. Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 766 shares represents a negligible increase to Adani Enterprises Limited's total share capital. The conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up shares is a routine corporate action. Total distinctive numbers range from 1,212,444,339 to 1,212,445,089 (751 shares) and 1,347,245,234 to 1,347,245,248 (15 shares).
