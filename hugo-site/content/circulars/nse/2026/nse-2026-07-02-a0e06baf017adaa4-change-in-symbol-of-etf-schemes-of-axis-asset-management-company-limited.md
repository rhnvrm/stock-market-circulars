## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a circular informing all Participants and Custodians about a change in trading symbols for multiple ETF schemes of Axis Asset Management Company Limited within the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS). This follows an earlier circular (NSE/CML/74923) dated June 29, 2026, regarding the same symbol change on the exchange. The new symbols will take effect from July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- The symbol change applies specifically to the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS)
- This is a follow-up action referencing the original circular NSE/CML/74923 dated June 29, 2026
- Seven ETF schemes of Axis Asset Management Company Limited are affected
- All contracts in these securities will trade under the new symbols from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

The following symbol changes will be implemented in the SLBS segment:

| Old Symbol | New Symbol |
|---|---|
| NIFTYAXIS | AXISNIFTY |
| ITAXIS | AXISTECETF |
| AXISCETF | CONSUMAXIS |
| AXISHCETF | HEALTHAXIS |
| SENSEXAXIS | AXSENSEX |
| AXISVALUE | VALUEAXIS |
| AXISBNKETF | BNKETFAXIS |

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and Custodians must update their internal systems, records, and trading/settlement processes to reflect the new trading symbols in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme. No further action or filing is required beyond internal system updates.

## Important Dates

- June 29, 2026: Original circular (NSE/CML/74923) announcing the symbol change on the exchange
- July 02, 2026: This follow-up circular issued for SLBS
- July 03, 2026: Effective date for the symbol change in SLBS; all contracts trade under new symbols from this date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and limited to identification and reference data changes. Participants and custodians dealing in these Axis ETF schemes under the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme need to ensure their systems recognize the new symbols to avoid settlement or reconciliation errors. There is no change to the underlying value, structure, or trading mechanics of the ETF schemes themselves.
