## Summary

NSE has admitted two privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective May 21, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The securities are debentures issued by Sundaram Home Finance Limited and IFL Finance Limited, each with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Two privately placed securities listed on NSE Capital Market segment from May 21, 2026
- Both securities carry a market lot size of 1 unit
- Securities are identified by designated codes: 796SHFL31 and 1175IFL28A
- Trading series for both securities: N0
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74317, Circular Ref. No: 0815/2026

| ISIN | INE667F07JG2 | INE01XO07066 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (796SHFL31 and 1175IFL28A) for system identification and trading
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (1 unit each)
- No additional compliance actions required for members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 21, 2026 (securities admitted to dealings)
- **Maturity Date (SHFL):** May 19, 2031
- **Maturity Date (IFL):** May 18, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of two privately placed debt instruments. Sundaram Home Finance Limited's debenture offers a 7.96% coupon maturing in 2031, while IFL Finance Limited's debenture offers a higher 11.75% coupon maturing in 2028, reflecting different credit risk profiles. Both are accessible with a market lot of 1, allowing flexible trading. Impact is confined to investors and members dealing specifically in these debt instruments.
