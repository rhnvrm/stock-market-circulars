## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Capitalmind Arbitrage Fund, a new fund offering (NFO) by Capitalmind Mutual Fund, on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription window runs from February 23, 2026 to March 9, 2026 (till 11:59 PM), with allotment scheduled for March 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants are available under the Capitalmind Arbitrage Fund umbrella
- Schemes span Direct and Regular plans, each with Dividend Payout, Dividend Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All six schemes are categorised as Equity
- Allotment date for all schemes is March 16, 2026
- NSE Circular Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/72903 | Circular Ref. No: 1212/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product availability notification for NSE members on the MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before **March 16, 2026 by 12:00 PM**.
- Members facilitating transactions must make the fund available to investors within the NFO window.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Open Date:** February 23, 2026
- **NFO Close Date:** March 9, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Funds Receipt Deadline:** March 16, 2026 by 12:00 PM
- **Allotment Date:** March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact. This notification is operationally relevant to NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in arbitrage strategies gain access to a new fund option from Capitalmind Mutual Fund. The six ISIN codes (INF226401125, INF226401133, INF226401117, INF226401158, INF226401166, INF226401141) are now available for transaction processing.
