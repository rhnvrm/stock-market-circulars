## Summary

NSE has admitted Rights Entitlements (REs) of Ravindra Energy Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment (Trade to Trade / BE series) effective June 16, 2026. The REs will trade under the symbol RELTD-RE with ISIN INE206N20018. Trading members are required to inform clients that purchasing REs confers only the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce the entitlements before the issue closes.

## Key Points

- Symbol: RELTD-RE (Series: BE)
- ISIN: INE206N20018
- Total Rights Entitlements: 19,832,834
- Market lot: 1
- Traded in Trade to Trade segment in compulsory demat mode for all investors
- Trading members must prominently display a disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities to dealings and lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of trading in REs, including the ISIN, and clarify that REs are not ordinary shares of Ravindra Energy Limited.
- Trading members must include a prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases stating that: (1) purchase of REs only gives the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce before closing; and (2) unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after the Issue Closing Date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 16, 2026 (commencement of trading)
- **Trading Period for Renunciation of REs:** June 16, 2026 to June 19, 2026
- **Date of Suspension:** June 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium, time-limited impact affecting existing shareholders of Ravindra Energy Limited and market participants who may trade the Rights Entitlements during the 4-day renunciation window (June 16–19, 2026). The Trade to Trade (BE series) designation means no intraday trading is permitted; all trades must result in delivery, reducing speculative activity. With 19,832,834 REs admitted and a market lot of 1, participation is accessible to retail investors. The short trading window and compulsory demat requirement are standard safeguards for RE trading.
