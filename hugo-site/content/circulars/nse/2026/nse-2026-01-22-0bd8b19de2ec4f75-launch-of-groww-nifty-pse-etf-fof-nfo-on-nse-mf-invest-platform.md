## Summary

Groww Mutual Fund is launching the Groww Nifty PSE ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from January 23, 2026 to February 06, 2026 till 11:59 PM. The scheme offers six plan options across Direct and Regular categories with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: January 23, 2026 to February 06, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: February 13, 2026 for all schemes
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Category: Equity schemes
- Payment deadline: Clear funds must be received by February 10, 2026 at 07:00 PM in the designated Clearing Corporation account

This is a routine NFO launch with limited market impact. The launch expands investment options for investors seeking exposure to public sector enterprises through an ETF fund of fund structure. No impact on existing securities or trading operations.
