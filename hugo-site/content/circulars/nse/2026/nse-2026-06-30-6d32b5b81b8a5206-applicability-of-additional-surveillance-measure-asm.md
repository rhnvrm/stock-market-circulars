## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued Circular 484/2026 placing 7 securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long-Term ASM) Stage-I framework effective July 1, 2026. Additionally, one security (BLISSGVS - Bliss GVS Pharma Ltd) is being elevated to Stage-IV, which triggers a mandatory shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) effective July 3, 2026. A 100% margin requirement applies to all affected securities.

## Key Points

- 7 securities newly included in Long-Term ASM Stage-I w.e.f. July 1, 2026
- Of these, 4 securities (ELECTHERM, GICL, RAMCOSYS, SETL) are moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM)
- 100% margin applicable from July 3, 2026 on all open positions as of July 2, 2026 and all new positions thereafter
- BLISSGVS (Bliss GVS Pharma Ltd) moved from Stage-I to Stage-IV, shifting to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) from July 3, 2026
- No securities moved from Stage-I to Stage-II or Stage-II to Stage-III in this revision
- ASM actions are in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must ensure 100% margin collection on all positions in ASM-listed securities from July 3, 2026
- **Clearing members**: Must apply enhanced margin requirements on open positions as of July 2, 2026 and all new positions from July 3, 2026
- **For BLISSGVS**: Trades must be executed on a trade-for-trade basis (Series: BE) from July 3, 2026; netting of positions not permitted
- Members should refer to the NSE ASM FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for detailed framework provisions
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **July 1, 2026**: New ASM inclusions and stage changes become effective
- **July 2, 2026**: Last date for open positions before the 100% margin applies
- **July 3, 2026**: 100% margin requirement kicks in for all open and new positions; BLISSGVS shifts to Trade-for-Trade (BE series)

## Impact Assessment

**High impact** on trading in the 8 affected securities:

- **Liquidity reduction**: 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital cost of holding or initiating positions, likely reducing trading volumes in all 7 Stage-I securities
- **BLISSGVS - Trade-for-Trade restriction**: Moving to BE series eliminates intraday netting, forcing delivery-based settlement only. This typically causes sharp decline in volumes and may lead to price discovery disruption
- **ELECTHERM, GICL, RAMCOSYS, SETL**: Already under STASM; escalation to LTASM signals continued surveillance concern and further tightens trading conditions
- **MEDIORG, MEGASTAR, SUVEN**: Fresh inclusions may see significant price and volume impact as market participants reassess risk
- The circular explicitly clarifies that ASM inclusion is a surveillance action and does not constitute an adverse finding against the listed company
