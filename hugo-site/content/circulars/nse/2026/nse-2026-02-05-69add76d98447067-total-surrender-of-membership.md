## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has approved the total surrender of membership for Manipal Stock & Share Brokers Ltd. The broker had ceased trading operations on April 21, 2022, and the formal surrender approval was granted on February 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Manipal Stock & Share Brokers Ltd.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000188032
- Last Trade Date: April 21, 2022
- Surrender Approval Date: February 02, 2026
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/72640 (Circular 08/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding membership status.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership surrender. No specific action items required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Last Trade Date**: April 21, 2022
- **Surrender Approval Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Circular Date**: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The broker had already ceased trading operations nearly four years ago (April 2022), so this formal surrender approval has no operational impact on current market activities.

**Operational Impact**: None for other market participants. This is an informational notice about the completion of an exit process for a broker that was already inactive.
