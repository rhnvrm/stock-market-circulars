## Summary

NSE has issued a modification to the previously announced suspension date for trading of rights entitlements pursuant to the rights issue of Silgo Retail Limited. The suspension date has been revised from February 02, 2026 to February 01, 2026, as per NSE Circular Ref No. NSE/CMTR/72349 dated January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension date for SILGO-RE1 (Silgo Retail Limited rights entitlements) has been moved forward by one day
- Previous suspension date: February 02, 2026
- Revised suspension date: February 01, 2026
- All other aspects of the original circular (NSE/CML/72258 dated January 12, 2026) remain unchanged
- Symbol affected: SILGO-RE1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative modification to a previously announced trading suspension schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to:
- Note the revised suspension date of February 01, 2026
- Update their systems and processes accordingly
- Ensure trading in rights entitlements of Silgo Retail Limited is suspended on the revised date

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Original circular issued (NSE/CML/72258)
- **January 16, 2026**: Modification circular issued (NSE/CMTR/72349)
- **January 19, 2026**: Current circular date (NSE/CML/72396)
- **February 01, 2026**: Revised date of suspension (New)
- **February 02, 2026**: Original date of suspension (Old)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this modification is minimal and limited to:
- Traders and investors holding or trading rights entitlements of Silgo Retail Limited
- One-day advancement of the suspension date may require minor adjustments to trading strategies
- No broader market impact expected
- Administrative change affecting a single security's trading schedule
