## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares issued by Life Insurance Corporation of India (LICI) pursuant to a bonus issue. Over 6.32 billion new shares will be admitted to dealings on NSE's Capital Market segment effective June 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: **LICI** | ISIN: INE0J1Y01017
- Total new bonus shares to be listed: **6,32,49,97,701**
- Distinctive number range: 6324997702 to 12649995402
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74382 | Ref. No. 0839/2026
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulations introduced. The circular operationalises the listing of bonus shares under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Part A, Regulation 3.1.1), designating security codes for system-level identification.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to identify the security exclusively by its designated security codes for trading purposes.
- No additional member-level compliance action is specified beyond awareness of the new securities and their codes.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Record Date | May 29, 2026 |
| Deemed Date of Allotment | June 01, 2026 |
| Listing & Trading effective from | June 02, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action. Life Insurance Corporation of India is one of the largest companies by market capitalisation on Indian exchanges. The bonus issue nearly doubles the share count (adding ~6.32 billion shares), which will lead to a proportional price adjustment on the ex-date. Index constituents and derivatives contracts referencing LICI will be subject to standard adjustment procedures. Investors should account for the increased float and adjusted price levels post-record date.
