## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers from nine issuers to trading on the debt market segment with effect from February 04, 2026. The circular provides details of security codes, ISINs, face values, issue prices, allotment dates, and redemption dates for each instrument.

## Key Points

- Total of 10 commercial paper instruments listed across 9 issuers
- All securities allotted on February 03, 2026
- Redemption dates range from February 10, 2026 to February 03, 2027
- Face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit across all instruments
- Largest issue: SIDBI with 124,000 securities (Rs. 62 billion face value)
- Issue prices range from Rs. 462,321 to Rs. 499,349 per unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly admitted debt securities for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 04, 2026 (trading commencement)
- **Allotment Date**: February 03, 2026 (all securities)
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from February 10, 2026 to February 03, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification of privately placed commercial paper listings. The securities are admitted for trading on the debt segment and provide short-term funding instruments for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. The listings expand the available debt instruments for institutional trading but represent standard money market operations.
