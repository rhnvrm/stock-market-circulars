## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the UTI Nifty 1D Rate Liquid ETF NFO, under UTI Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 08, 2026 to July 15, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular provides scheme details and payment deadlines for members facilitating this NFO.

## Key Points

- The NFO covers UTI Nifty 1D Rate Liquid ETF - Growth (Regular Plan and Direct Plan)
- Both plans share ISIN INF789F1AG68
- Category: Debt
- Allotment date for both schemes: July 16, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account before the specified deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification regarding a new fund offer being made available on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 15, 2026 till 03:00 pm
- Members facilitating transactions must ensure investor orders are processed within the NFO subscription window

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 08, 2026 to July 15, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Fund pay-in deadline: July 15, 2026 till 03:00 pm
- Allotment date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it pertains to the operational launch of a new debt-category ETF (liquid fund) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It is primarily relevant to NSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions, with no direct bearing on listed equity securities or broader market trading activity.
