## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units of HSBC Mutual Fund's RedHex Hybrid Long-Short Fund on the Capital Market segment, effective June 25, 2026. Three options are being listed — Regular Growth, Direct Growth, and Direct IDCW Reinvestment — with units allotted on June 19, 2026 at a face value and issue price of Rs. 10/- each.

## Key Points

- Three scheme options listed: RHEXHDRG (Regular Growth), RHEXHDDG (Direct Growth), and RHEXHDDIR (Direct IDCW Reinvestment)
- Total units listed: ~354.86 million (Regular Growth), ~202.76 million (Direct Growth), and ~99,995 (Direct IDCW Reinvestment)
- Face value, paid-up value, and issue price all set at Rs. 10/- per unit
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Series: SF
- Trading effective from June 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes (RHEXHDRG, RHEXHDDG, RHEXHDDIR) to identify the securities on the trading system
- Trading must be carried out in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 19, 2026
- Listing effective date: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard mutual fund unit listing on NSE. The RedHex Hybrid Long-Short Fund is managed by HSBC Asset Management (India) Private Limited, with HSBC Trustees (India) Private Limited as trustee and Computer Age Management Services (CAMS) as the Registrar and Share Transfer Agent. The listing enables secondary market trading of the fund units on NSE.
