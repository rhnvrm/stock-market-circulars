## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members that LTIMindtree Limited will officially change its name to LTM Limited effective April 17, 2026. The trading symbol LTM on NSE remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- LTIMindtree Limited will be renamed to LTM Limited
- Trading symbol LTM remains unchanged on NSE
- Change is effective from April 17, 2026
- Circular issued by the Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/73700

## Regulatory Changes

The company name change is a corporate action recognised and communicated by NSE. No changes to the trading symbol or ISIN are indicated in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should update their records to reflect the new company name (LTM Limited) effective April 17, 2026
- All documentation, client communications, and system references to LTIMindtree Limited should be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **April 17, 2026**: Effective date of name change from LTIMindtree Limited to LTM Limited

## Impact Assessment

This is a name-change-only corporate action. The trading symbol (LTM) is unaffected, so day-to-day trading operations will continue without disruption. Market participants, brokers, and data vendors will need to update company name references in their systems and client-facing materials. The change may have minor branding implications for the company but no direct impact on share price or trading mechanics.
