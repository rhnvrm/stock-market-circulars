## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,606,500 equity shares of Integrated Personnel Services Limited (Symbol: IPSL) on the SME EMERGE platform (Capital Market segment), effective June 30, 2026. The shares were allotted under a preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 96 per share with a face value of Rs. 10, and are subject to varying lock-in periods as detailed in Annexure A.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Integrated Personnel Services Limited
- **Symbol:** IPSL | **Series:** SM | **ISIN:** INE02EE01019
- **Segment:** NSE SME EMERGE (Capital Market)
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 96 per share; Face Value: Rs. 10
- **Total Shares Listed:** 1,606,500 equity shares (allotted under Preferential allotment)
- **Date of Allotment:** May 5, 2026
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 8,606,511 to 10,213,010
- **Market Lot:** 500 shares
- **Pari Passu:** Yes — new shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- Shares initially credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*) per SEBI circular nos. CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 pending ISIN activation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol **IPSL** and series **SM** for all trades in these securities.
- Trading must be conducted in market lot sizes of **500 shares**.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed per Annexure A; the locked-in shares cannot be traded until the respective expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 5, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing/Trading:** June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates:**
  - 803,250 shares (Distinctive Nos. 8,606,511–9,409,760): **December 31, 2027**
  - 706,500 shares (Distinctive Nos. 9,409,761–10,116,260): **December 31, 2026**
  - 39,750 shares (Distinctive Nos. 10,116,261–10,156,010): **December 30, 2026**
  - 57,000 shares (Distinctive Nos. 10,156,011–10,213,010): **December 31, 2026**

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine further-issue listing on the SME EMERGE segment. The addition of ~1.6 million shares under preferential allotment increases the share count for IPSL. The bulk of the new shares (803,250, approximately 50%) are locked in until December 2027, limiting near-term float impact. The remaining shares (~803,250) are locked in until end of December 2026. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the SME segment's lower liquidity and the significant lock-in constraints on the newly listed shares.
