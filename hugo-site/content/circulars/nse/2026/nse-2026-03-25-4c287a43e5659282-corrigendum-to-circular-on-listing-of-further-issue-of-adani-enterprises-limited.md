## Summary

NSE has issued a corrigendum to Circular No. NSE/CML/73453 dated March 24, 2026, regarding the listing of a further issue of Adani Enterprises Limited. The corrigendum revises the Distinctive Number Range communicated earlier, with the total number of shares remaining at 13,74,58,427.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum reference: NSE/CML/73447, Circular Ref. No. 0510/2026, dated March 25, 2026
- Original circular: NSE/CML/73453 dated March 24, 2026
- Adani Enterprises Limited informed the Exchange of a revised Distinctive Number Range
- Total shares in the further issue: 13,74,58,427
- The revised ranges span four tranches with specific from/to distinctive numbers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular solely corrects the Distinctive Number Range data published in the original listing circular for Adani Enterprises Limited's further issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should update their records to reflect the revised Distinctive Number Range as provided in this corrigendum
- The corrected ranges are as follows:
  - 1209786112 to 1212444338 — 26,58,227 shares
  - 1212445112 to 1212447618 — 2,507 shares
  - 1212447636 to 1347245233 — 13,47,97,598 shares
  - 1347245285 to 1347245379 — 95 shares
  - **Total: 13,74,58,427 shares**

## Important Dates

- Original circular date: March 24, 2026
- Corrigendum date: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has a moderate operational impact on members dealing in Adani Enterprises Limited shares. The correction to the Distinctive Number Range is important for accurate settlement, depository records, and share transfer processing. Members should ensure their systems reflect the revised ranges to avoid discrepancies in transaction processing. There is no change to the total number of shares listed or any broader market impact.
