## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Rajputana Stainless Limited (symbol: RSL) on the Capital Market segment effective March 19, 2026. The company completed its IPO at an issue price of Rs. 122 per share with a total of 83,567,658 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value admitted to trading.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** RSL | **Series:** EQ | **ISIN:** INE313L01016
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- | **Issue Price:** Rs. 122/-
- **Total shares listed:** 83,567,658 equity shares
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- RSL will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated code (RSL) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- Members to note RSL's inclusion in the pre-open IPO session category

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 18, 2026
- **Effective Date (listing):** March 19, 2026
- **Lock-in dates:**
  - 6,250,000 shares: Free (no lock-in)
  - 45,953,154 shares: Locked until September 26, 2026
  - 16,714,504 shares: Locked until September 26, 2027
  - 14,650,000 shares: Free (no lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing circular for Rajputana Stainless Limited, a Gujarat-based stainless steel company. With 83.57 million shares at an issue price of Rs. 122, the implied market cap at listing is approximately Rs. 10,195 million (~Rs. 1,020 crore). A significant portion of shares (approximately 75%) are subject to lock-in periods extending to September 2026 and September 2027, limiting near-term float. Impact is primarily relevant to IPO allottees and traders monitoring new listings; no broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
