## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of Best Agrolife Limited (Symbol: BESTAGRO) pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 11,82,23,700 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective January 20, 2026. The securities are identified by ISIN INE052T01021 with distinctive number range from 236447401 to 354671100.

## Key Points

- 11,82,23,700 new equity shares to be listed
- Trading symbol: BESTAGRO
- ISIN: INE052T01021
- Distinctive number range: 236447401 to 354671100
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes to identify securities for trading purposes
- Trading permitted only from the effective date specified

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: January 19, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date**: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue significantly increases the outstanding share count of Best Agrolife Limited. The listing of 11.82 crore additional shares will increase liquidity and adjust the share price proportionately. Existing shareholders benefit from receiving bonus shares as per the bonus ratio. Market participants should note the trading commencement date and updated share capital structure for accurate valuation and trading decisions.
