## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Two securities — AVG Logistics Limited and Fairchem Organics Limited — have been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I. Divyadhan Recycling Industries Limited has been moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II. No securities have been excluded from the ESM framework in this update. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM (both stages) is provided in Annexure III of the circular.

## Key Points

- AVG Logistics Limited (AVG, ISIN INE680Z01018) and Fairchem Organics Limited (FAIRCHEMOR, ISIN INE0DNW01011) newly added to ESM Stage I.
- Both newly added securities will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective July 06, 2026.
- Divyadhan Recycling Industries Limited (DIVYADHAN, ISIN INE0QYI01019) moves from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective July 03, 2026.
- No securities are being moved from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.
- Securities moving to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, effective July 03, 2026.
- A minimum 100% margin applies on all open positions as of July 03, 2026, and on new positions created from July 06, 2026, for the newly shortlisted securities.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ESM is purely a surveillance measure and should not be viewed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under NSE's existing ESM framework (originally established via earlier circulars including NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of the existing ESM criteria to specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in AVG and FAIRCHEMOR as on July 03, 2026, and on new positions created from July 06, 2026.
- Members must ensure trading in AVG and FAIRCHEMOR shifts to the Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment from July 06, 2026.
- Members must apply the 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction for DIVYADHAN from July 03, 2026, as it moves to Stage II.
- Members should refer to the consolidated ESM list (Annexure III) to track all securities currently under surveillance.

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Effective date for Divyadhan Recycling Industries' move to Stage II and applicability of 100% margin on existing open positions in newly added Stage I securities; 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction begins for Stage II move.
- July 06, 2026: Effective date for AVG Logistics and Fairchem Organics shifting from EQ/SM to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) segment; margin applies to new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The move to ESM Stage I/II significantly restricts trading in the affected securities by imposing a 100% margin requirement and shifting them to the Trade-for-Trade segment, which eliminates intraday trading and requires actual delivery for all transactions. This typically reduces liquidity and trading volumes in AVG Logistics, Fairchem Organics, and Divyadhan Recycling Industries shares. Investors holding these stocks should be aware of the increased margin burden and settlement restrictions. The measure is a surveillance action rather than a reflection of any regulatory violation by the companies, but it can still negatively affect short-term price discovery and trading activity in these names.
