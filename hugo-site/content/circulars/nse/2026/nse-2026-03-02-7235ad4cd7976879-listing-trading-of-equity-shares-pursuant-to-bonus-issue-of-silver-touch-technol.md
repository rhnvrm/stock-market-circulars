## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by Silver Touch Technologies Limited. A total of 6,34,05,000 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings on NSE's Capital Market segment effective March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: SILVERTUC
- ISIN: INE625X01026
- Total bonus shares issued: 6,34,05,000
- Distinctive number range: 63405001 to 126810000
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying admission of new securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (SILVERTUC) for all trading on the system.
- Trading in these bonus shares may commence only from March 10, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: March 6, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: March 9, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue increases the total share count of Silver Touch Technologies Limited significantly. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares, which typically results in a proportional adjustment to the share price on the ex-bonus date. Market liquidity for SILVERTUC may improve due to the increased float. The impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders in this specific scrip.
