## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for two SME EMERGE segment companies — Destiny Logistics & Infra Limited (DESTINY) and Ushanti Colour Chem Limited (UCL) — with effect from June 02, 2026. The shares were issued through preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- Both companies are listed on the NSE Capital Market segment under the SME EMERGE platform with Series SM.
- Destiny Logistics & Infra Limited is listing 10,54,500 equity shares (face value Rs. 10, issue price Rs. 33) from conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis; market lot is 1,500.
- Ushanti Colour Chem Limited is listing 16,57,000 equity shares (face value Rs. 10, issue price Rs. 58) under preferential allotment; market lot is 2,000.
- Both ISINs are subject to temporary ISIN (IN8\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*) until activation per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes (DESTINY and UCL) for all trades.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes: 1,500 for DESTINY and 2,000 for UCL.
- Lock-in restrictions apply: DESTINY shares locked in until December 05, 2027; UCL shares locked in until December 31, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment (DESTINY):** April 13, 2026
- **Date of Allotment (UCL):** March 31, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 02, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (DESTINY):** December 05, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (UCL):** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. Both listings are routine further-issue notifications for SME EMERGE companies. Destiny Logistics adds 10.54 lakh shares at Rs. 33 (warrant conversion), and Ushanti Colour Chem adds 16.57 lakh shares at Rs. 58 (preferential allotment). The lock-in periods restrict near-term liquidity for these newly admitted securities.
