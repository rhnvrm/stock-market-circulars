## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for three SME EMERGE companies — Magson Retail and Distribution Limited (MAGSON), Sar Televenture Limited (SARTELE), and Utssav CZ Gold Jewels Limited (UTSSAV) — effective March 30, 2026. All shares were allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis and are admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market (SME EMERGE) segment.

## Key Points

- Three SME EMERGE companies are listing further issues of equity shares effective March 30, 2026
- All new shares were allotted via conversion of warrants on a preferential basis
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies
- Lock-in restrictions apply on portions of the newly listed shares
- Trading must use designated security codes and specified market lot sizes
- Temporary ISINs (IN8*) apply per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 until activation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The listing is made in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes for securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only the designated security codes (MAGSON, SARTELE, UTSSAV) for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot sizes: 1000 (MAGSON), 500 (SARTELE), 600 (UTSSAV)
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified per company

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 30, 2026 — securities admitted to dealings on NSE SME EMERGE
- **MAGSON allotment date:** February 6, 2026 | Lock-in expiry: November 10, 2026
- **SARTELE allotment date:** January 9, 2026 | Lock-in expiry: September 29, 2026 (495,500 shares) and September 30, 2027 (1,000,000 shares) and September 29, 2026 (475,000 shares)
- **UTSSAV allotment date:** August 29, 2025 | Lock-in expiry: October 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**MAGSON (Magson Retail and Distribution Limited):**
- 875,460 new equity shares (face value Rs. 10, issue price Rs. 93.25) listed
- ISIN: INE0O1S01012 | Series: SM | Market lot: 1,000
- Distinctive numbers: 11449709 to 12325168

**SARTELE (Sar Televenture Limited):**
- 1,970,500 new equity shares (face value Rs. 2, issue price Rs. 242) listed
- ISIN: INE0PUC01020 | Series: SM | Market lot: 500
- Distinctive numbers: 47107501 to 49078000
- Staggered lock-in: portions expire September 2026 and September 2027

**UTSSAV (Utssav CZ Gold Jewels Limited):**
- 330,000 new equity shares (face value Rs. 10, issue price Rs. 222) listed
- ISIN: INE06IJ01010 | Series: SM | Market lot: 600
- Distinctive numbers: 23818001 to 24148000

The listing increases the tradeable float for all three SME companies. The lock-in periods limit near-term selling pressure from allottees. Impact is limited to investors and traders in these specific SME securities.
