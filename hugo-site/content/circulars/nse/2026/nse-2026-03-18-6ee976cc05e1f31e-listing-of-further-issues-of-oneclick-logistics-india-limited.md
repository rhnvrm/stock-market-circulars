## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares of Oneclick Logistics India Limited (Symbol: OLIL) on the NSE Capital Market segment under SME Emerge, effective March 19, 2026. The shares were issued under a Rights issue (Final) at Rs. 313 per share, with allotment dated March 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,118,560 additional equity shares of Oneclick Logistics India Limited admitted to NSE SME Emerge segment
- Symbol: OLIL | Series: SM | ISIN: INE0OPS01011
- Issue price: Rs. 313 per share; Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Market lot size: 400 shares
- Distinctive number range: 5,593,164 to 6,711,723
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in dematerialized mode per SEBI circular guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (OLIL, Series SM) for all trades
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 400 shares
- Activation of permanent ISIN (INE0OPS01011) from temporary ISIN is subject to SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 17, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing/Trading:** March 19, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine further-issue listing notification for a small-cap SME Emerge company. The addition of ~1.12 million shares at Rs. 313 each (~Rs. 35 crore) is modest in scale. Impact is limited to existing shareholders of Oneclick Logistics India Limited and market participants trading on the SME Emerge platform. No broad market implications.
