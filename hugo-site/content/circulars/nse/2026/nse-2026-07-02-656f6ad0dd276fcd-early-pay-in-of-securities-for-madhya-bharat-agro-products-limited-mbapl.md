## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued instructions for early pay-in (EPI) of securities for Madhya Bharat Agro Products Limited (MBAPL) in connection with a face value split (sub-division) of the stock from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share. The record date and ex-date for the corporate action are both July 3, 2026. Members and custodians undertaking early pay-in during the specified settlement window must use the existing/old ISIN (INE900L01010) without adjusting for the conversion ratio, and switch to the new ISIN for pay-in after the record date.

## Key Points

- Circular references Item 10.18 'Early Pay-in of Securities for Margin Exemption' of the consolidated circular (NCL/CMPT/73996) dated April 30, 2026.
- Corporate action: Face Value Split (Sub-Division) from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share for MBAPL (ISIN: INE900L01010).
- Record Date and Ex-Date: July 3, 2026.
- EPI on July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123) must be done in the Old/Existing ISIN.
- EPI on July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124) must be done in the New ISIN.
- Example given: for a sale quantity of 10 shares, early pay-in in the old ISIN should be for a quantity of 2 shares (reflecting the 5:1 split ratio).
- Members must adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022 on the SEBI Block Mechanism for client demat accounts.
- In case of early pay-in through block mechanism, the client-wise early pay-in allocation file should not be uploaded.
- Custodians providing early pay-in through the pool account must upload client-wise early pay-in allocation details as per the actual traded quantity to pass the benefit to clients.
- Market participants/custodians must execute pay-in/early pay-in instructions in the New ISIN after the record date in the depositories.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes the existing early pay-in and block mechanism framework (per NCL/CMPT/73996 and NCL/CMPT/53386) specifically for the MBAPL face value split corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians performing early pay-in for MBAPL must use the correct ISIN (old vs. new) based on the settlement date as specified.
- Quantities pledged for early pay-in in the old ISIN must be adjusted for the split ratio (5:1) as illustrated in the example.
- Custodians using pool accounts must upload accurate client-wise allocation details reflecting actual traded quantities.
- Block mechanism early pay-in must not include a client-wise allocation file upload, per existing SEBI/NCL block mechanism rules.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex-Date: July 3, 2026
- EPI in Old ISIN: July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123)
- EPI in New ISIN: July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/settlement circular limited to members, custodians, and PCMs handling trades in MBAPL around its face value split. Correct adherence ensures smooth settlement and margin exemption processing during the ISIN transition; failure to follow the old/new ISIN distinction could result in settlement mismatches or failed pay-ins for affected trades. Impact on the broader market is minimal, but operationally important for entities with MBAPL exposure during the transition window.
