## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Limited under its Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF scheme. The units will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective July 22, 2026, under the symbol PVTBANK.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF, managed by Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Limited
- Symbol: PVTBANK; ISIN: INF174KA1A94
- Number of units allotted: 7,458,765
- Date of allotment: July 17, 2026
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 28.54 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Series: EQ
- Designated market makers include Kotak Securities, Parwati Capital Market, Kanjalochana Finserve, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Irage Broking Services, Fyers Securities, Choice Equity Broking, Marwadi Chandarana Intermediaries Brokers, Junomoneta Finsol, and Rikhav Securities

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations, Part A. It does not amend any regulation but formalizes the listing of a new ETF security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize the security only by its designated code (PVTBANK) and trade in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- No additional action required from listed companies or investors beyond standard trading protocols

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 17, 2026
- Circular date: July 21, 2026
- Effective date of listing/trading: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing circular introducing a new ETF product tracking the Nifty Private Bank index to the exchange. It expands investment options for market participants seeking exposure to private sector banks but does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations. Impact is limited to investors and market makers involved with this specific ETF scheme.
