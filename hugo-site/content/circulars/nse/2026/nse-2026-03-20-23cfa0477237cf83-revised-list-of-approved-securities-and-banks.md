## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of equity shares approved as non-cash components of liquid assets for the Commodity (COMM) segment. The list covers 39 large-cap securities with overall permitted quantities across all segments. A VAR-based haircut is applicable for all listed securities, and a value-based prudential norm applies such that the exclusion of any pledged security must not result in a margin shortfall exceeding 25% at the Clearing Member level.

## Key Points

- 39 equity shares are approved as non-cash liquid assets for the Commodity segment
- Applicable haircut for all securities is VAR (Value at Risk)
- Prudential norm: exclusion of a pledged security must not cause margin shortfall of more than 25% at Clearing Member level
- Memberwise permitted quantity for the COMM segment is listed as N.A. for all securities, meaning segment-specific caps do not apply separately
- Overall permitted quantities vary significantly across securities, from ~20.7 million (APOLLOHOSP) to ~2.67 billion (HDFCBANK)

## Regulatory Changes

This circular supersedes the previous approved securities list for the Commodity segment. The updated list reflects current Nifty 50 constituents and large-cap securities eligible for margin collateral purposes. ETERNAL LIMITED (INE758T01015) appears as a notable inclusion with the highest overall permitted quantity of ~1.82 billion shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing Members must ensure their pledged securities for non-cash liquid assets are drawn only from the approved list
- Clearing Members must monitor that removal of any single pledged security does not cause a margin shortfall exceeding 25% at their level
- Clearing Members should update their collateral management systems to reflect the revised permitted quantities

## Important Dates

- Circular date: 2026-03-20
- Effectiveness: As communicated by NSE; Clearing Members should treat this list as operative immediately upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update affecting Clearing Members participating in the Commodity segment. The revision ensures the approved securities list remains aligned with current market conditions and eligible large-cap equities. The N.A. memberwise permitted quantities for COMM segment indicate no separate segment-level caps, giving Clearing Members flexibility within the overall permitted limits. The inclusion of high-liquidity Nifty 50 stocks ensures adequate collateral availability for members.
