## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all exchange members that Ambey Laboratories Limited will undergo a corporate name change. The company will be renamed Dhansa Labs Limited and its NSE trading symbol will be updated from AMBEY to DHANSA, effective June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol **AMBEY** will change to new symbol **DHANSA**.
- Existing company name **Ambey Laboratories Limited** will change to **Dhansa Labs Limited**.
- All exchange members are required to take note of the updated name and symbol.
- The change applies to the NSE Listing segment.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular formalises the change in listed entity name and trading symbol on NSE. Members and market participants must refer to the stock by its new symbol DHANSA and name Dhansa Labs Limited from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update internal systems, order management systems, and client-facing platforms to reflect the new symbol (DHANSA) and company name (Dhansa Labs Limited) before June 01, 2026.
- Investors holding shares under the old name/symbol will have their holdings automatically updated; no separate action is required by investors, but they should note the updated identifier in their portfolio statements.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name and Symbol Change:** June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The renaming and symbol change is primarily an administrative and identification update. Trading in the security will continue uninterrupted; however, all market participants — brokers, data vendors, research platforms, and investors — need to update their references from AMBEY/Ambey Laboratories Limited to DHANSA/Dhansa Labs Limited. There is no impact on the underlying shareholding, ISIN, or corporate rights of existing shareholders.
