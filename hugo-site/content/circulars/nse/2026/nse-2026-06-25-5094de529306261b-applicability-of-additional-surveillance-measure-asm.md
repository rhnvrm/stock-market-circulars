## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74900 (Ref. No. 476/2026) updating the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework. Gradiente Infotainment Limited (GRADIENTE) is shortlisted to move from Stage I to Stage II effective June 29, 2026. A 100% margin requirement applies to all open and new positions in ASM-listed securities from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- GRADIENTE (Gradiente Infotainment Limited, ISIN: INE361K01017) moves from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. June 29, 2026
- No new securities added to Stage I or Stage IV
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage III or from Stage I to Stage IV
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 30, 2026 and new positions from July 1, 2026
- ASM is a purely surveillance-driven action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) w.e.f. July 1, 2026. However, no securities qualified for Stage IV in this update. The price band of a scrip exiting the ASM framework is reinstated to its pre-ASM price band, subject to no other surveillance measure being in force.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure 100% margin is collected on all open positions as on June 30, 2026 and on all new positions in ASM-shortlisted securities from July 1, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges
- Members should refer to NSE FAQs on ASM at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for detailed provisions

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: GRADIENTE moves from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II
- **June 30, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **July 1, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes effective for open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a targeted impact, affecting traders and investors holding or planning to take positions in GRADIENTE (Gradiente Infotainment Limited). The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital required to hold or enter positions, potentially reducing liquidity and trading volumes in this stock. The Stage II escalation under Long-Term ASM indicates continued regulatory concern about the security's trading patterns. Other market participants are unaffected by this specific update.
