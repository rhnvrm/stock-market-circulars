## Summary

NSE has notified members that trading in a privately placed Non-Convertible Security issued by NTPC Limited will be suspended effective July 14, 2026, on account of redemption, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- The suspension applies to a single Non-Convertible Security (privately placed) issued by NTPC Limited.
- ISIN affected: INE733E07GD8.
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the security.
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification implementing existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in ISIN INE733E07GD8 from the suspension date.
- No further trading activity should be recorded in this security on the debt market segment after suspension.

## Important Dates

- Suspension effective date: July 14, 2026
- Circular date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific privately placed NCD (ISIN INE733E07GD8) of NTPC Limited. As the suspension is due to redemption, it reflects the natural maturity/redemption process of the debt instrument rather than any adverse event, and does not affect NTPC's equity trading or broader market operations.
