## Summary

NFP Sampoorna Foods Limited has listed its equity shares on NSE following a SME IPO. The post-issue shareholding pattern (filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) as of 21 May 2026) shows a total of 12,634,128 shares held across 969 shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total shares outstanding: 12,634,128 equity shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 5 shareholders holding 8,173,828 shares (64.70%)
- Public shareholders: 964 shareholders holding 4,460,300 shares (35.30%)
- No partly paid-up shares, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged promoter shares reported
- All shares held in dematerialised form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, Regulation 31(1)(a).

## Compliance Requirements

- NFP Sampoorna Foods Limited has submitted the required shareholding pattern disclosure as a newly listed entity.
- The company confirmed no convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, pledged promoter shares, or differential voting rights are outstanding.

## Important Dates

- Post-issue shareholding pattern date: 21 May 2026
- Listing effective: 22 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with limited broader market impact. The promoter holding of 64.70% is above the minimum threshold, indicating reasonable promoter commitment. Public float of 35.30% (4,460,300 shares across 964 shareholders) is standard for an SME listing. No special securities structures (warrants, convertibles, ESOPs) that could dilute existing shareholders.
