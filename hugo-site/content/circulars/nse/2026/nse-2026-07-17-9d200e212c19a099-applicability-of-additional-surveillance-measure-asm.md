## Summary

NSE has issued its periodic circular on the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework, effective July 20-22, 2026. GNA Axles Limited (GNA) has been newly shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage I. Teerth Gopicon Limited (TGL) has been moved from Stage II to Stage III. No securities were shortlisted or moved into Stage IV in this cycle, and no securities moved from Stage I directly to Stage IV under Criteria VII.

## Key Points

- GNA Axles Limited (ISIN: INE934S01014) newly added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I, effective July 20, 2026.
- Teerth Gopicon Limited (ISIN: INE0K6601012) moved from Stage II to Stage III of the Long-Term ASM Framework, effective July 20, 2026.
- No securities were added to Stage IV, and none moved from Stage I to Stage IV under Criteria VII in this cycle.
- A 100% margin applies on all open positions as of July 21, 2026, and on new positions created from July 22, 2026 onwards, for securities under the framework.
- Securities shortlisted under Criteria VII (Stage IV) are shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) — not applicable in this cycle as no scrips qualified.
- ASM shortlisting operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures and does not imply any adverse action against the company.
- Price bands revert to pre-ASM levels once a scrip exits the framework, unless it remains subject to another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is a routine periodic update under the existing Long-Term ASM framework established via prior circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, 45111, 46557, 48506, 52090, 63362, and 64066).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on all open and new positions in GNA Axles Limited and Teerth Gopicon Limited from the specified dates.
- Members must monitor the applicable ASM stage for these securities and adjust risk management and client communication accordingly.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 20, 2026: Effective date for stage shortlisting/movement (GNA to Stage I; TGL from Stage II to Stage III).
- July 21, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 22, 2026: 100% margin applies to open positions and all new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement significantly raises the cost of trading GNA Axles and Teerth Gopicon shares, likely reducing trading volumes and liquidity in these counters. This is a recurring, formulaic surveillance action rather than a company-specific development, and does not reflect any adverse finding against either company. Market participants holding these securities should expect increased margin funding requirements and potential price volatility around the effective dates.
