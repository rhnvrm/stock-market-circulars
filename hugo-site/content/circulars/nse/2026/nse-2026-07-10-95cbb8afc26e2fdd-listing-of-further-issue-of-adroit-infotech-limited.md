## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Adroit Infotech Limited (Symbol: ADROITINFO), arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares. The newly admitted securities will be available for trading on the Capital Market segment effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.
- Security: Adroit Infotech Limited, Symbol ADROITINFO, Series EQ.
- ISIN: INE737B01033; shares currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8737B01016 pending activation, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10/- each.
- Number of securities admitted: 4,51,312.
- Market lot: 1.
- Pari passu status: Yes.
- Lock-in details: Not applicable.
- Distinctive number ranges: 51077209–51083516 (6,308 shares) and 51083517–51528520 (4,45,004 shares), totaling 4,51,312 shares.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy changes; this is an administrative listing action under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of further-issued securities and applicable lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify the security only by its designated NSE codes and trade in the specified lot size (1) from the effective date. No other member action is required.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 10, 2026
- Effective Date for Listing/Trading: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact listing update pertaining solely to Adroit Infotech Limited. It reflects conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status rather than a new capital raise, and has no material implications for the broader market or other listed entities.
