## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) offered by ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund (Regular Growth) — ISIN: INF109K30075 — reopened on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 10, 2026.
- ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund (Direct Growth) — ISIN: INF109K30083 — reopened on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 10, 2026.
- Both schemes are categorized as Equity under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework.
- Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility are all available.
- Fund house: ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notice about fund availability on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE's NMF TM Segment are expected to take note of the availability of these schemes and facilitate investor access through the NSE MF Invest Platform accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund (Regular and Direct Growth) for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in ICICI Prudential's ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund via the NSE MF Invest Platform. Relevant primarily to distributors, advisers, and investors interested in the SIF equity category. No changes to fund structure, NAV computation, or regulatory obligations.
