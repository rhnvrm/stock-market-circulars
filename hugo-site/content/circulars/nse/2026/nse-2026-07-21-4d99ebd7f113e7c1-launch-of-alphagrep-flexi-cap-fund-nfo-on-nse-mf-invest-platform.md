## Summary

NSE has informed members that the AlphaGrep Flexi Cap Fund NFO, launched under AlphaGrep Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 21, 2026 to August 4, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The scheme is an equity flexi cap fund offered across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- New Fund Offer (NFO) for AlphaGrep Flexi Cap Fund opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Reinvestment, Regular IDCW Payout, Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, Direct IDCW Payout
- Category: Equity (Flexi Cap)
- ISINs range from INF2VOA01079 to INF2VOA01129 across variants
- Allotment date for all schemes is August 11, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds reach the designated Clearing Corporation account before the funds pay-in deadline

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational circular enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting in this NFO on behalf of clients must ensure clear funds through any permitted payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 4, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Period: July 21, 2026 to August 4, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: August 4, 2026, till 11:59 pm
- Allotment Date: August 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily operational in nature, relevant to NSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions. It does not affect listed equity trading, market structure, or existing investor holdings, but enables investors to subscribe to a new equity mutual fund scheme through the NSE MF Invest Platform within the specified window.
