## Summary

NSE has issued Circular Ref. No. 189/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/73256) dated March 12, 2026, moving LCC Infotech Limited (Symbol: LCCINFOTEC, ISIN: INE938A01021) to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, effective March 13, 2026. The series for this security will also change from EQ to BE.

## Key Points

- **Security Affected:** LCC Infotech Limited (LCCINFOTEC), ISIN: INE938A01021
- **Effective Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Series Change:** EQ to BE
- **Settlement Mode:** Trade for Trade (no netting off allowed)
- **Price Band:** 5% or lower as applicable
- **Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD):** 50% of trade value, to be deposited by buyers
- This circular is in continuation of earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, and NSE/SURV/71670

## Regulatory Changes

LCCINFOTEC is being escalated to Stage II of the GSM framework. Under Stage II, trading is permitted daily but under enhanced restrictions — Trade for Trade settlement with a 5% price band and a mandatory 50% ASD from buyers. This is an upgrade in surveillance intensity compared to Stage I, which only required 100% margin and a 5% price band.

### GSM Stage-wise Actions (for reference):
| Stage | Actions |
|---|---|
| I | 100% margin, 5% price band |
| II | Trade for Trade, 5% price band, 50% ASD from buyers |
| III | Trade for Trade, 5% price band, trading once a week (Monday), 100% ASD from buyers |
| IV | Trade for Trade, 5% price band, trading once a week (Monday), 100% ASD, no upward movement |

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members** must note the change and ensure clients are informed of the new trading conditions for LCCINFOTEC effective March 13, 2026.
- **Buyers** of LCCINFOTEC must deposit an Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of the trade value.
- Members must take adequate precautions while trading in this security as settlement will be on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off.
- Queries may be directed to NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Effective Date of Stage II GSM:** March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The move to Stage II GSM significantly impacts the liquidity and tradability of LCC Infotech Limited (LCCINFOTEC). The shift from EQ to BE series, combined with Trade for Trade settlement and a mandatory 50% ASD from buyers, will substantially increase the cost of taking positions in this stock. The absence of netting off means each trade requires full settlement, further deterring speculative activity. Investors holding or considering positions in LCCINFOTEC should be aware of the increased settlement obligations and restricted price movement. This action signals heightened regulatory concern about the trading patterns or fundamentals of this security.
