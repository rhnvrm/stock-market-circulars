## Summary

NSE has extended the trading period for Rights Entitlement (RE) of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (Symbol: ONECAP-RE) pursuant to its ongoing Rights Issue. The last date for on-market renunciation has been revised to March 10, 2026, and the date of suspension of RE trading to March 11, 2026. This update supersedes the earlier circular NSE/CML/72898 dated February 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: ONECAP-RE (Onelife Capital Advisors Limited)
- New last date for on-market renunciation: March 10, 2026
- New date of suspension of RE trading: March 11, 2026
- Rights Issue closing date extended from March 06, 2026 to March 16, 2026
- Previous last date for on-market renunciation was March 02, 2026
- Issue opening date of February 23, 2026 remains unchanged
- No change in terms and conditions of the Letter of Offer (LOF) or Abridged Letter of Offer (ALOF)

## Regulatory Changes

This circular amends NSE/CML/72898 (dated February 19, 2026) solely with respect to the revised dates for on-market renunciation and suspension of Rights Entitlement trading. All other terms of the original circular remain unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed of the updated schedule and must adhere to the new trading and suspension timelines for ONECAP-RE.
- Eligible shareholders wishing to renounce their rights entitlement on-market must do so by March 10, 2026.
- The addendum to the LOF with revised dates will be available on the company website (www.onelifecapital.in), registrar's website (www.kfintech.com), BSE (www.bseindia.com), and NSE (www.nseindia.com).

## Important Dates

| Event | Original Date | Revised Date |
|---|---|---|
| Issue Opening Date | February 23, 2026 | February 23, 2026 (unchanged) |
| Last Date for On-Market Renunciation | March 02, 2026 | March 10, 2026 |
| Last Date for Application Form / RE Letter Requests | March 02, 2026 | March 10, 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | March 06, 2026 | March 16, 2026 |
| Date of Suspension of RE Trading | — | March 11, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides additional time for eligible shareholders of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (NSE: ONELIFECAP, BSE: 533632) to apply for the rights issue or renounce their entitlements on the market. This is a company-specific operational update with moderate impact, primarily affecting existing shareholders and Rights Entitlement (ONECAP-RE) holders who now have until March 10, 2026 to act on their entitlements.
