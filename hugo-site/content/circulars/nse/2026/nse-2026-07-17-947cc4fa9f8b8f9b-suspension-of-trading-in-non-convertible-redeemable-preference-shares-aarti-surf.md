## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in the Non-Convertible Redeemable Preference Shares (NCRPS) of Aarti Surfactants Limited will be suspended effective July 21, 2026, following redemption of the instrument on account of maturity, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Security: Aarti Surfactants Limited
- Symbol: AARTISURF
- ISIN: INE09EO04017
- Series: P1
- Reason for suspension: Redemption on account of maturity
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an administrative action suspending trading in a specific security series under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the AARTISURF P1 series from the effective date. No further action required from the listed company or investors beyond the scheduled redemption process.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 17, 2026
- Effective date of suspension: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific Non-Convertible Redeemable Preference Shares series of Aarti Surfactants Limited, which is being redeemed at maturity. There is no impact on the company's listed equity shares or broader market operations.
