## Summary

NSE Surveillance Department circular (Ref No: NSE/SURV/74624, Circular Ref. No: 430/2026) announces changes to the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective June 10-11, 2026. Two securities are newly included under ST-ASM Stage I, one security moves from Stage II to Stage I, and two securities are excluded from the ASM framework entirely.

## Key Points

- **ST-ASM Stage I inclusions**: SYNOPTICS (Synoptics Technologies Limited, INE0BTR01012) and TCIFINANCE (TCI Finance Limited, INE911B01018) added effective June 10, 2026.
- **ST-ASM Stage II inclusions**: Nil — no new securities added to Stage II.
- **Stage II to Stage I movement**: PRAMARA (Pramara Promotions Limited, INE0O7A01017) moves down from Stage II to Stage I effective June 10, 2026.
- **ASM exclusions**: BALAMINES (Balaji Amines Limited, INE050E01027) and NRL (Nupur Recyclers Limited) excluded from ASM framework effective June 10, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to earlier ASM circulars: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). The current update reflects periodic review and rebalancing of securities under the ST-ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **ST-ASM Stage I margin**: 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- **ST-ASM Stage II margin**: 100% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Margins are applicable on all open positions as on June 10, 2026 and all new positions created from June 11, 2026.
- NSE members must ensure margin collection is enforced as per the above provisions for the affected securities.

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026**: Circular issued.
- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for ST-ASM inclusions, exclusions, and stage movements.
- **June 11, 2026**: Enhanced margin rates come into effect on all open positions (as of June 10) and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or planning to trade in SYNOPTICS and TCIFINANCE will face a minimum 50% margin requirement under ST-ASM Stage I, significantly increasing the cost of maintaining or initiating positions. PRAMARA's downgrade from Stage II to Stage I reduces its margin burden from 100% to 50% (minimum), providing some relief to participants. BALAMINES and NRL being excluded from ASM removes their enhanced margin requirements entirely, potentially improving liquidity and reducing trading costs for those securities. Members should update their risk management systems accordingly before June 11, 2026.
