## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares issued by City Union Bank Limited pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 24,76,96,809 bonus equity shares (ISIN: INE491A01021, Symbol: CUB) will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus equity shares of City Union Bank Limited (Symbol: CUB) admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Total bonus shares allotted: 24,76,96,809
- ISIN: INE491A01021
- Distinctive number range: 743090429 to 990787237
- Effective date for trading: June 16, 2026
- Circular Ref. No: 0924/2026 | Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74630

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs admission of new securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must identify the security only by its designated security code (CUB) for trading purposes.
- This circular is effective from June 16, 2026; members should update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Record Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date for Listing & Trading:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 247.7 million bonus shares will increase the total share count of City Union Bank Limited, potentially diluting per-share metrics while keeping overall market capitalisation stable. Existing shareholders as of the record date (June 12, 2026) will receive bonus shares credited by June 15, 2026. Trading in the bonus shares will commence on June 16, 2026. Market participants should account for the price adjustment that typically accompanies bonus share listings.
