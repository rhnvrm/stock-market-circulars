## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 9,20,500 equity shares of Swaraj Suiting Limited (Symbol: SWARAJ) on the SME EMERGE platform, effective June 04, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 236 per share pursuant to conversion of warrants on a preferential basis, allotted on March 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9,20,500 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market (SME EMERGE) from June 04, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 236 per share; market lot size: 500 shares
- ISIN: INE0GMR01016; Series: SM
- Distinctive number range: 25389661 to 26310160
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Shares temporarily credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8*) pending ISIN activation per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard further listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol SWARAJ and series SM for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the prescribed lot size of 500 shares
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per Annexure A details

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: March 31, 2026
- Effective date for trading: June 04, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for 8,00,000 shares (25389661–26189660): December 10, 2027
- Lock-in expiry for 1,20,500 shares (26189661–26310160): December 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine further listing on the SME EMERGE platform for a small textile company. The addition of 9,20,500 shares increases the tradable equity base. The majority of newly listed shares (800,000 shares, ~86.9%) are subject to a longer lock-in until December 2027, limiting near-term float impact. Investors should note the temporary ISIN status pending full activation.
