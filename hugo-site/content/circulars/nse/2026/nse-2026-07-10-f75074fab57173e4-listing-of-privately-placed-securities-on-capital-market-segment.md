## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed non-convertible debt securities of PNB Housing Finance Limited and NTPC Green Energy Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective from July 13, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- PNB Housing Finance Limited: Symbol 783PHFL29, Series N0, Issue "PHFL 7.83% 2029 LXXVII", Coupon 7.83%, Maturity 07-Aug-2029, ISIN INE572E07308
- NTPC Green Energy Limited: Symbol 727NGEL36, Series N0, Issue "NGEL 7.27% 2036 Sr 2", Coupon 7.27%, Maturity 09-Jul-2036, ISIN INE0ONG08024
- Market lot for both securities is 1
- Securities will be identified only by their designated security codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A regarding admission of privately placed securities and lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the specified designated security codes and market lot sizes when trading these securities on the system.

## Important Dates

- Circular effective date: July 13, 2026
- PHFL 7.83% 2029 LXXVII maturity: August 7, 2029
- NGEL 7.27% 2036 Sr 2 maturity: July 9, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing circular with minimal market impact. It enables trading of these privately placed NCDs on the NSE Capital Market segment, primarily relevant to debt market participants, bondholders, and members dealing in fixed income securities of PNB Housing Finance and NTPC Green Energy. No impact on equity shareholders or broader market operations.
