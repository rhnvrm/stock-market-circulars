## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective July 03, 2026, Supreme Engineering Limited (SUPREMEENG) has been added to the ASM IBC list under the 'Receipt of corporate announcement / disclosure from the company' category, while KSS Limited (KSERASERA) has been excluded from the ASM IBC framework. No securities moved between Stage I and Stage II during this update.

## Key Points

- Supreme Engineering Limited (SUPREMEENG, ISIN: INE319Z01021) added to ASM IBC list upon receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure, effective July 03, 2026.
- KSS Limited (KSERASERA, ISIN: INE216D01026) excluded from the ASM IBC framework effective July 03, 2026.
- No new additions or movements recorded in ASM IBC Stage I or Stage II for this cycle.
- The consolidated ASM IBC list continues to include securities such as AGS Transact Technologies Limited (AGSTRA), CLC Industries Limited (CLCIND), Gensol Engineering Limited (GENSOL), and Impex Ferro Tech Limited (IMPEXFERRO), among others, at Stage I.
- ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM IBC is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is an update under the existing ASM framework for IBC-related companies, referencing prior circulars NSE/SURV/37979 (June 08, 2018), NSE/SURV/52368 (May 20, 2022), NSE/SURV/55582 (February 10, 2023), and NSE/SURV/70074 (September 05, 2025). No new regulatory framework was introduced; this is a routine periodic update to the shortlisted/excluded securities.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must apply the applicable ASM restrictions to securities newly added to the list (SUPREMEENG) and remove restrictions for excluded securities (KSERASERA) effective July 03, 2026. Members should ensure trading systems reflect the updated surveillance status for affected securities and continue to apply other prevailing surveillance measures concurrently.

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Effective date for addition of SUPREMEENG to ASM IBC and exclusion of KSERASERA from ASM IBC.

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact is limited to the specific securities involved. Supreme Engineering Limited (SUPREMEENG) shareholders and traders will face additional surveillance restrictions (such as higher margins, price bands, or trade-for-trade settlement) starting July 03, 2026. Conversely, KSS Limited (KSERASERA) will see restrictions eased upon exclusion from the ASM IBC framework, potentially improving trading liquidity for that stock. Broader market impact is minimal as this is a routine, narrowly targeted surveillance update.
