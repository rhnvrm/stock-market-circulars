## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Motilal Oswal Contra Fund NFO of Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants of Motilal Oswal Contra Fund are being made available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- Both Direct and Regular plan options are available, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment sub-options
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be available
- Members can reach NSE MF Invest support at Toll Free No: 1800 2100 940 or support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding platform availability of existing fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of these schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform and may facilitate transactions accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact and is limited to NSE MF Invest Platform operations. Investors and distributors using the NSE MF Invest Platform will gain access to Motilal Oswal Contra Fund schemes for regular transactions. The six schemes and their ISINs are:
