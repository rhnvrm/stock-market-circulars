## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by R&B Denims Limited (Symbol: RNBDENIMS, ISIN: INE012Q01021). A total of 8,99,73,440 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective April 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8,99,73,440 bonus equity shares of R&B Denims Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: RNBDENIMS; ISIN: INE012Q01021
- Distinctive number range: 179946881 to 269920320
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Issued by Dhwani Shah, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of new securities to dealings on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security exclusively by its designated code (RNBDENIMS) for the purpose of trading on the NSE system.
- Trading in the bonus shares is permitted only from April 07, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 01, 2026
- **Record Date:** April 03, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** April 06, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for bonus shares. Existing shareholders of R&B Denims Limited who held shares as of the record date (April 03, 2026) will receive bonus equity shares, which become tradeable on NSE from April 07, 2026. The bonus issue increases the total share count significantly (8,99,73,440 new shares), which may result in a proportional adjustment to the share price on the ex-bonus date. No broader market impact is anticipated.
