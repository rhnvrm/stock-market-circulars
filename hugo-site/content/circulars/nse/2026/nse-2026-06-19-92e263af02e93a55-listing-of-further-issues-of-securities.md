## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the admission to dealings of further equity shares for 12 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 22, 2026. All new shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are listed under the EQ series, pari passu with existing shares and with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 12 companies receive listing of further ESOP-allotted equity shares effective June 22, 2026
- All securities are admitted under the EQ series with a market lot of 1
- All new shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Each security is identified by its designated code for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required for members beyond recognising the new distinctive number ranges for the listed securities. Trading must use the designated security codes as specified in the Annexure.

Routine operational circular. The additional shares from ESOP allotments represent a marginal increase in the share capital of the respective companies and have no material impact on market operations, pricing, or trading conditions.
