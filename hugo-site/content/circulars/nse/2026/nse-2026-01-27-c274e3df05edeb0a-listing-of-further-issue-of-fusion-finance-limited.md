## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted 5,66,562 equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment with effect from January 28, 2026. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares. The shares will trade under the symbol 'FUSION' with ISIN INE139R01012, face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10 each, with a market lot size of 1 share.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 5,66,562 equity shares
- Security type: Conversion of partly paid-up to fully paid-up equity shares
- Trading symbol: FUSION
- ISIN: INE139R01012 (permanent ISIN)
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8139R01011 pending activation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI regulations regarding activation of ISIN in case of additional issue of shares/securities as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code 'FUSION' for trading these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- Shares to be identified only by designated codes on the trading system
- Dematerialized shares currently under temporary ISIN will need to be transferred to permanent ISIN INE139R01012

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing notification for conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up shares, affecting existing shareholders of Fusion Finance Limited. The conversion adds 5.67 lakh shares to the tradable float.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal operational impact. Trading members need to ensure they use the correct symbol and ISIN for trading. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and carry no lock-in restrictions, allowing immediate trading.

**Shareholder Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders as partly paid-up shares become fully paid-up, enhancing liquidity and eliminating any pending payment obligations on these shares.
