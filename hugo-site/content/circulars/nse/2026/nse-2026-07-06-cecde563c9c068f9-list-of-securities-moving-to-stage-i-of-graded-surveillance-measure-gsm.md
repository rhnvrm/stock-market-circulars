## Summary

NSE has issued a circular placing Net Avenue Technologies Limited (Symbol: CBAZAAR, ISIN: INE518X01015) into Stage I of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, effective July 07, 2026. This continues the exchange's ongoing GSM monitoring program referenced across multiple prior circulars dating back to 2017.

## Key Points

- Net Avenue Technologies Limited (CBAZAAR) moves to GSM Stage I effective July 07, 2026.
- Trading in the security will be subject to 100% applicable margin.
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower, as applicable.
- Circular references continuation of GSM framework established via prior circulars: NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/74254, and NSE/SURV/74795.
- Members advised to exercise adequate precaution while trading in this security.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced; this circular applies the existing GSM framework to a newly identified security. Stage I actions under the framework include 100% margin and a 5% (or lower) price band, distinct from more severe Stage II-IV actions which involve trade-for-trade settlement, additional surveillance deposits, and restricted trading frequency.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply 100% margin on trades in CBAZAAR from July 07, 2026.
- Members must enforce the applicable 5% (or lower) price band for the security.
- Members should advise clients of the heightened surveillance status and associated trading restrictions.
- Queries to be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **July 06, 2026**: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/SURV/75054, Circular Ref. No: 503/2026).
- **July 07, 2026**: GSM Stage I restrictions take effect for CBAZAAR.

## Impact Assessment

The move to GSM Stage I significantly increases trading friction for CBAZAAR, with 100% margin requirements likely to reduce liquidity and trading volumes in the stock. This is a stock-specific surveillance action with no broader market impact, but is high-severity for holders and traders of Net Avenue Technologies Limited given the immediate effective date and margin escalation.
