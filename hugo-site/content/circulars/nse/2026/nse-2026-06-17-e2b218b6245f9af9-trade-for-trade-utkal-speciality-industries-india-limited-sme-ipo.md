## Summary

NSE has notified that Utkal Speciality Industries India Limited (Symbol: UTKAL), which was listed under the trade for trade segment (Series: ST) following its SME IPO, will be transferred to the rolling segment (Series: SM) effective July 2, 2026. This follows Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74714 dated June 16, 2026 and is in accordance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- UTKAL shares will move from trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to rolling segment (Series: SM) on July 2, 2026.
- The transfer is pursuant to SEBI guidelines for SME IPO listed securities.
- This circular is a follow-up to NSE/CML/74714 issued on June 16, 2026.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74736, Ref No: 0973/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012), SME IPO securities initially traded under the trade for trade mechanism are moved to the regular rolling settlement segment after the mandatory period. This transition allows normal buy/sell netting and settlement for market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members dealing in UTKAL must update their systems and processes to reflect the series change from ST to SM effective July 2, 2026. Members should ensure proper settlement handling post-transition.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Prior circular NSE/CML/74714 issued regarding UTKAL listing.
- **June 17, 2026**: This circular issued (NSE/CML/74736).
- **July 2, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from trade for trade (ST) to rolling segment (SM).

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from trade for trade to rolling segment is a routine, positive development for UTKAL shareholders and traders. It improves liquidity by allowing netting of buy and sell trades within a settlement cycle, reducing capital requirements for trading. This is a standard post-SME-IPO procedural step and does not indicate any adverse action against the company or its securities.
