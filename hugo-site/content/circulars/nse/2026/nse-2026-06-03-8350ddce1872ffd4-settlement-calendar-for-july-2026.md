## Summary

NSE has published the settlement calendar for the Mutual Fund Service System (MFSS) for July 2026. The calendar covers two settlement types: normal subscription (Type S, T+1 settlement) and liquid subscription (Type K, T-day settlement). The calendar spans all 23 trading days in July 2026 (July 1–31), with settlement numbers running from 2026120 to 2026142.

## Key Points

- **Normal Subscription (Annexure A — Type S):** Follows T+1 settlement. Funds payin/payout occurs on the subscription order date (T), while units payin/payout occurs on the next business day (T+1).
- **Liquid Subscription (Annexure B — Type K):** Follows same-day (T-day) settlement. Both funds and units payin/payout occur on the subscription order date itself.
- Settlement numbers run from **2026120** (July 1) to **2026142** (July 31) for both settlement types.
- The last subscription date in July (July 31, Settlement 2026142) has units payout on **August 3, 2026** under normal subscription, due to the weekend.
- No trading days are skipped within July (weekends excluded), indicating no exchange holidays fall in July 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational calendar notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members, AMCs, RTAs, and other MFSS participants should update their operational systems with the July 2026 settlement schedule to ensure timely payin and payout of funds and units.

## Important Dates

- **First settlement day:** July 1, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026120)
- **Last settlement day:** July 31, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026142)
- **Cross-month units payout:** August 3, 2026 (for July 31 normal subscription orders)

## Impact Assessment

Routine calendar update with no market-moving implications. Operational teams at fund houses, RTAs, and clearing members should note the T+1 vs T-day distinction when processing July 2026 subscription orders on the MFSS platform. The absence of any July holidays simplifies reconciliation for the month.
