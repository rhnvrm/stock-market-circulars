## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF NFO (New Fund Offer) of Edelweiss Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 4, 2026 to May 14, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The scheme tracks the Nifty Next 50 index and is offered in both Regular and Direct Plan Growth options, with allotment scheduled for May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: May 4, 2026 to May 14, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Fund house: Edelweiss Mutual Fund
- Scheme: Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF (Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth)
- ISIN for both plans: INF754K01XF3
- Category: Equity
- Allotment date: May 21, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by May 19, 2026 at 6:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds (through any payment mode) are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 19, 2026 by 6:00 PM.
- Members should make the NFO available for client transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform within the subscription window.

## Important Dates

- **May 4, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **May 14, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **May 19, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **May 21, 2026**: Allotment date for Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF units

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is a routine NFO launch notification. Members on the NSE MF Invest Platform gain access to a new ETF product tracking the Nifty Next 50 index. The offering provides investors with equity exposure to the next 50 large-cap companies beyond the Nifty 50. No trading disruptions or compliance burdens are expected beyond standard NFO processing procedures.
