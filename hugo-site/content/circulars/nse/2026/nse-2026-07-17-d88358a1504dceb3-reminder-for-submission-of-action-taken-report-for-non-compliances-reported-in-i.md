## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder to all trading members regarding submission of the Action Taken Report (ATR) for non-compliances identified in the Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026. This follows the Exchange's earlier circular (NSE/INSP/74566 dated June 5, 2026) on the same subject.

## Key Points

- Members must close all non-compliances reported in the Internal Audit Report for H2 ended March 31, 2026.
- Status of compliance must be provided through the Inspection module in the Member Portal, in the prescribed format.
- Failure to close observations or submit the ATR within the due date will attract monetary penalties and/or disciplinary action.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a reminder of an existing requirement under Exchange Circular No. NSE/INSP/74566 dated June 5, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must submit the Action Taken Report for non-compliances reported in the Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026, through the Inspection module in the Member Portal, in the prescribed format, on or before July 31, 2026.

## Important Dates

- June 5, 2026: Original circular (NSE/INSP/74566) issued.
- July 31, 2026: Deadline for submission of Action Taken Report / closure of non-compliances.

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily affects trading members' compliance operations. Non-compliance with the July 31, 2026 deadline may result in monetary penalties or disciplinary action as per Exchange Circular No. NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026. There is no direct impact on listed securities or broader market activity.
