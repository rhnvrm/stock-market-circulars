## Summary

NSE has informed all members that Groww Nifty Private Bank Index Fund, an NFO by Invesco Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective May 27, 2026. Six scheme variants are available across Direct and Regular plans.

## Key Points

- Effective date for ongoing transactions: May 27, 2026
- Fund: Groww Nifty Private Bank Index Fund (Invesco Mutual Fund)
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six equity scheme variants across Direct and Regular plan options
- All schemes fall under the Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement enabling a mutual fund NFO for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems to support transactions in the six Groww Nifty Private Bank Index Fund schemes from May 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Groww Nifty Private Bank Index Fund NFO for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low impact. This is a standard platform onboarding announcement for a new index fund NFO. It expands the product universe available to investors via NSE MF Invest Platform by adding six Groww Nifty Private Bank Index Fund scheme variants (ISINs: INF666M01OU3, INF666M01OV1, INF666M01OW9, INF666M01OX7, INF666M01OY5, INF666M01OZ2). Members distributing mutual funds through NSE's platform should enable these schemes for their clients from the effective date.
