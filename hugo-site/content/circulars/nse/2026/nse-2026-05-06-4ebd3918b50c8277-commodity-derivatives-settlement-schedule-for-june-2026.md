## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for the month of June 2026. The schedule covers all trading days from June 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026, with settlements typically occurring on T+1 basis, with some exceptions around weekends and holidays.

## Key Points

- Settlement follows a T+1 cycle for most trading days in June 2026.
- Trade date June 25, 2026 settles on June 29, 2026 (skipping June 26-28 weekend/holiday).
- Trade date June 26, 2026 also settles on June 29, 2026.
- Trade date June 30, 2026 settles on July 1, 2026 (next business day).
- The circular covers 22 trading days in June 2026.
- Circular reference: NCL/COM/74073, Circular Ref. No. 162/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification providing the monthly settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members participating in the Commodity Derivatives Segment must adhere to the published settlement dates.
- Members should plan their funds and securities obligations in accordance with the schedule.
- For queries, members may contact NSE Clearing at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or support@nsccl.co.in.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on all NSE members active in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. Members must align their back-office systems, fund flows, and margin obligations with the published schedule. The T+1 settlement cycle is standard, but exceptions around weekends (e.g., June 25 and 26 both settling on June 29) require attention for cash and collateral management. No market-wide or systemic risk implications are associated with this circular.
