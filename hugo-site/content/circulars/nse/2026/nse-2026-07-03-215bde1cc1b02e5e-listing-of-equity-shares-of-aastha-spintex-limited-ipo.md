## Summary

NSE has informed members of the Exchange about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Aastha Spintex Limited, which recently completed its IPO. The company will trade under the symbol AASTHA.

## Key Points

- Company: Aastha Spintex Limited
- Symbol: AASTHA
- ISIN: INE2FMX01012
- The actual date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under the Exchange's listing department procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems and records to recognize the new symbol AASTHA and ISIN INE2FMX01012 in preparation for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

Date of circular: July 03, 2026. Date of listing: To be announced via a separate circular.

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects trading members who will need to prepare for trading in the newly listed equity shares. Market impact is limited to investors and traders interested in Aastha Spintex Limited, with no broader market-wide implications.
