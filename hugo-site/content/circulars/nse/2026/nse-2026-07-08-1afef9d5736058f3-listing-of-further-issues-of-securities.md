## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for five companies effective July 09, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The additional shares arise mainly from ESOP allotments (ICICI Bank, InterGlobe Aviation, Solara Active Pharma Sciences) and a preferential allotment (Bharti Airtel). Lot sizes for trading are also specified per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- **Bharti Airtel Limited (BHARTIARTL)**: 146,761,335 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each allotted on a preferential basis on 23-Jun-2026, at an issue price of Rs. 1,923. Distinctive number range 6094392982 to 6241154316. Shares are locked in until 09-Jan-2028.
- **ICICI Bank Limited (ICICIBANK)**: 237,850 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted under ESOP on 07-Jul-2026. Distinctive number range 7173615745 to 7173853594. No lock-in.
- **InterGlobe Aviation Limited (INDIGO)**: 9,099 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 07-Jul-2026. Distinctive number range 386657499 to 386666597. No lock-in.
- **Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited (SOLARA)**: 70,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 16-Jun-2026. Distinctive number range 48176013 to 48246012. No lock-in.
- **Tata Consumer Products Limited**: Listed in the annexure heading, but detailed allotment figures are cut off in the source content.
- All securities carry a market lot of 1 and rank pari passu with existing equity shares.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification of further listing under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for listing, Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants must recognize the additional securities and updated distinctive number ranges as forming part of each company's listed equity capital from the effective date, and trade the additional shares in the specified lot size of 1.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: 23-Jun-2026 (Bharti Airtel, preferential); 07-Jul-2026 (ICICI Bank, ESOP); 07-Jul-2026 (InterGlobe Aviation, ESOP); 16-Jun-2026 (Solara Active Pharma, ESOP)
- **Effective Date of Listing**: July 09, 2026
- **Circular Date**: July 08, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Bharti Airtel preferential shares)**: 09-Jan-2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing additions are largely incremental and driven by routine ESOP exercises for ICICI Bank, InterGlobe Aviation, and Solara Active Pharma Sciences, representing negligible dilution relative to each company's outstanding share capital. The Bharti Airtel preferential allotment of ~146.76 million shares is more significant in absolute terms but is subject to a lock-in until January 2028, limiting near-term tradable float impact. Overall market impact is low, with no immediate trading or price implications expected beyond standard capital base updates.
