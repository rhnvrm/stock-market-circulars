## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department (Circular Ref. No: 295/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/73874) has updated the list of securities under the encumbrance-based surveillance measure prescribed by SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011, Regulation 28(3). This measure targets companies where both promoter and non-promoter encumbrance levels are high. One security (STEELXIND) has been added; none have been removed. The consolidated list now contains nine securities.

## Key Points

- **New addition (Annexure I):** STEELXIND (Steel Exchange India Limited, INE503B01021) is newly included under the measure effective April 28, 2026.
- **Exclusions (Annexure II):** No securities are being removed from the framework in this review cycle.
- **Margin requirement:** A minimum 75% margin will apply to STEELXIND in both Equity and Equity Derivatives segments on all open positions as of April 27, 2026, and on all new positions created from April 28, 2026.
- **Consolidated list (Annexure III):** Nine securities remain under the framework after this update.
- This measure is applied in conjunction with all other prevailing exchange surveillance measures and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion under this measure should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in continuation of earlier circular NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. The framework under SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, Reg. 28(3), requires exchanges to impose enhanced surveillance on securities where promoter and non-promoter encumbrance levels exceed prescribed thresholds. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of covered securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure that clients trading in STEELXIND maintain a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments effective April 28, 2026.
- Members must apply the margin requirement to all open positions in STEELXIND as of April 27, 2026, and to all new positions from April 28, 2026 onwards.
- For queries, members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in.
