## Summary

NSE's Investigation Department has provisionally empaneled M/s Optibase Financial Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider, authorized to offer Whitebox Algorithmic Trading Solutions to trading members of the Exchange. The empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Vendor empaneled: M/s Optibase Financial Technologies Private Limited
- Type of algo offered: Whitebox
- Address: 315 & 317, 3rd Floor, DLF Galleria, DLF City, Phase IV, Gurugram, Haryana - 122002
- Contact: +91 7455951966
- Circular Ref. No: 43/2026 | Download Ref No: NSE/INVG/74750
- Full list of empaneled Algo Providers is available on the NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine update to the list of provisionally empaneled Algo Providers under NSE's existing algorithmic trading empanelment process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members intending to use algorithmic trading solutions from Optibase Financial Technologies must ensure they engage with the provider within the framework of NSE's guidelines for empaneled Algo Providers. Members should refer to the updated empaneled Algo Provider list on the NSE website before onboarding any algo vendor.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: June 17, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact; this is an administrative update expanding the pool of NSE-empaneled Algo Providers by one Whitebox algo vendor. Trading members seeking Whitebox algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally empaneled option. No market-wide or systemic implications.
