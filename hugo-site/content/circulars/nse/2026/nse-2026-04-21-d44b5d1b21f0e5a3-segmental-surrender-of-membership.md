## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department (Circular Ref. No. 35/2026) has approved the request of Victory Financial Services Ltd. to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment. The surrender was approved on April 21, 2026. All members are directed to take note of this development.

## Key Points

- Victory Financial Services Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INZ000255831) has surrendered its Currency Derivatives segment membership.
- The surrender was approved by the Exchange on April 21, 2026.
- This is a segmental surrender, meaning the member may retain membership in other segments.
- All NSE members are requested to acknowledge this notification.

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative notification of an approved membership surrender for a specific segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the surrender of Currency Derivatives segment membership by Victory Financial Services Ltd. No further action is required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Surrender approval date for Victory Financial Services Ltd.'s Currency Derivatives segment membership.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the operational status of Victory Financial Services Ltd. in the Currency Derivatives segment. Other market participants and overall market operations in the Currency Derivatives segment are unaffected. This is a routine administrative update with no broader market or systemic implications.
