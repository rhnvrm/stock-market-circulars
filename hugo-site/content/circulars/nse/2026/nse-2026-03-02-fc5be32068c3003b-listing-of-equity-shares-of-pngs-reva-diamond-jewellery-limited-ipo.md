## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited (Symbol: PNGSREVA) on the Capital Market segment of the National Stock Exchange effective March 04, 2026. A total of 31,698,400 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each have been admitted to dealings at an IPO issue price of Rs. 386/- per share. The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** PNGSREVA | **Series:** EQ | **ISIN:** INE1RDG01013
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- | **Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- | **Issue Price:** Rs. 386/-
- **Total Securities Listed:** 31,698,400 equity shares fully paid up
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai; Contact Person: Mr. Babu Rapheal C.
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Ms. Kirti Suryakant Vaidya

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is effected under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code **PNGSREVA** for all trading on the system.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1.
- Members must observe lock-in restrictions on specified tranches of shares as detailed in Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Effective Date (Listing):** March 04, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates (various tranches):**
  - 28-Mar-2026 (2,209,600 shares)
  - 27-May-2026 (2,209,600 shares)
  - 01-Sep-2026 (multiple tranches: 207,000 + 152,100 + 250,650 + 1,840,050 + 223,650 + 71,550 shares)
  - 01-Mar-2027 (multiple tranches: 450,000 + 4,775,000 + 4,775,000 + 2,207,500 + 508,500 + 5,400 shares)
  - 01-Mar-2029 (multiple tranches: 2,200,000 + 2,200,000 + 2,000,000 shares)
  - **Free float (no lock-in):** 5,412,800 shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing notification impacting NSE members and investors in PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited. The company, headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, is engaged in the diamond jewellery retail segment. With an issue price of Rs. 386/- and 31.7 million total shares, the market capitalisation at listing is approximately Rs. 12,235 crore. A significant portion of shares (approximately 9.4 million shares) are subject to lock-in periods extending up to March 2029, limiting near-term supply. The free float of approximately 5.41 million shares will be available for unrestricted trading from day one. Members should prepare trading systems to include PNGSREVA in pre-open session processing from March 04, 2026.
