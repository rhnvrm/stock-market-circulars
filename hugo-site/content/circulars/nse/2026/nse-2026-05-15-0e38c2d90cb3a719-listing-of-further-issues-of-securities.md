## Summary

NSE Circular Ref. No. 0791/2026 (Download Ref. NSE/CML/74246), issued May 15, 2026, notifies the listing and admission to trading of further issued equity securities for 11 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective **May 18, 2026**. Allotments arise from ESOP schemes, preferential issues, and conversion of warrants on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- Securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from **May 18, 2026**.
- Five companies detailed in the annexure: Aditya Birla Capital (ABCAPITAL), Bodhi Tree Multimedia (BTML), Camlin Fine Sciences (CAMLINFINE), Fedbank Financial Services (FEDFINA), and Five-Star Business Finance (FIVESTAR); remaining six (ICICI Lombard, ICICI Prudential, Indiamart Intermesh, Mastek, Pearl Global Industries, Axis Bank) listed in the subject line.
- All newly listed securities are equity shares with market lot of 1 and are pari passu with existing shares.
- Lock-in is **not applicable** for ESOP-allotted shares (ABCAPITAL, CAMLINFINE, FEDFINA).
- Lock-in expiry **November 29, 2026** for BTML preferential issue shares.
- Lock-in for FIVESTAR warrant-conversion shares as per Annexure A (details in circular).
- Securities identified solely by designated NSE codes for trading purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. Notification is issued under:
- **Regulation 3.1.1** of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings.
- **Regulation 2.5.5** of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes for the respective securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the **designated security codes** for trading; no alternative identification is permitted.
- Trading must be conducted in the **specified lot sizes** (all at market lot of 1 for the detailed securities).
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for BTML (until Nov 29, 2026) and FIVESTAR (per Annexure A).

## Impact Assessment

**ABCAPITAL**: 272,002 equity shares (₹10 face value, ESOP) added; minor dilution, no lock-in.

**BTML**: 1,200,000 equity shares (Re. 1 face value, issue price ₹10, preferential issue) added; locked in until Nov 29, 2026, limiting near-term trading supply.

**CAMLINFINE**: 5,000 equity shares (Re. 1 face value, ESOP) added; negligible dilution, no lock-in.

**FEDFINA**: 71,125 equity shares (₹10 face value, ESOP) added; minor dilution, no lock-in.

**FIVESTAR**: 310,000 equity shares (Re. 1 face value, issue price ₹770, warrant conversion on preferential basis) added; partial lock-in per Annexure A. Issue price of ₹770 signals significant premium over face value.

Overall market impact is routine and low; these are standard corporate actions. The lock-in conditions on BTML and FIVESTAR preferential/warrant tranches reduce near-term float impact for those counters.
