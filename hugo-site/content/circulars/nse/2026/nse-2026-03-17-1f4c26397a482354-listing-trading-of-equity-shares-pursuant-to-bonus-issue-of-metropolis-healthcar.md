## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares of Metropolis Healthcare Limited (symbol: METROPOLIS) issued pursuant to a bonus issue. These shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment with effect from March 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus issue shares of Metropolis Healthcare Limited will be listed on NSE's Capital Market segment.
- Total number of new shares being listed: 1,55,495,826 (approx. 15.55 crore shares).
- Shares are identified by ISIN INE112L01020 and symbol METROPOLIS.
- Distinctive number range: 51,831,943 to 207,327,768.
- Trading shall use the designated security codes as specified.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs admission of securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the new security codes for METROPOLIS bonus shares for trading purposes.
- Trading on the system must reference only the designated security codes.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** March 20, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** March 23, 2026
- **Listing & Trading Effective Date:** March 24, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 15.55 crore bonus shares will increase the total share count of Metropolis Healthcare Limited, potentially leading to a proportional adjustment in share price on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders benefit from additional shares without cash outflow. Market members should update their systems to recognize the new shares under the existing METROPOLIS symbol and ISIN INE112L01020. The circular is routine in nature with no adverse regulatory or compliance implications.
