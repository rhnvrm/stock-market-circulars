## Summary

Knack Packaging Limited has submitted its Shareholding Pattern under Regulation 31 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the listing of its equity shares post-IPO. The disclosure is filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) and reflects the post-issue shareholding structure as on 07.07.2026.

## Key Points

- The filing pertains to the post-issue shareholding pattern of Knack Packaging Limited's equity shares.
- The company has not issued any partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts.
- Certain shares held by the company are in lock-in as on the disclosure date, consistent with standard post-IPO promoter/anchor lock-in requirements.
- No promoter shares are encumbered by pledge or non-disposal undertaking.
- The company has a significant beneficial owner as declared in the filing.
- The company does not have equity shares with differential voting rights.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine post-listing compliance disclosure mandated under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

As a newly listed entity, Knack Packaging Limited is required to file periodic shareholding pattern disclosures under Regulation 31(1)(a), detailing promoter, public, and other shareholder categories, along with lock-in and encumbrance status.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: 07.07.2026 (date of listing/post-issue disclosure)

## Impact Assessment

This filing formalizes the listing of Knack Packaging Limited's equity shares on the exchange, providing transparency on ownership structure to investors. The disclosure of locked-in shares and beneficial ownership is relevant for investors assessing float and potential future selling pressure once lock-in periods expire, but has limited impact beyond the specific stock.
