## Summary

NSE has issued a corrigendum revising the effective date for admission of equity shares pursuant to the bonus issue of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited. The new effective date for listing and trading on the Capital Market segment is January 16, 2026, instead of the previously announced January 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Correction to earlier circular NSE/CML/72210 dated January 08, 2026
- Related to bonus issue equity shares of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- Effective date postponed by one day due to Exchange Circular NSE/NCL/CMPT/72260 dated January 12, 2026
- Change made in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a procedural correction to align with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

All members should note the revised effective date for trading in the bonus issue equity shares of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Original Effective Date**: January 15, 2026 (now superseded)
- **Revised Effective Date**: January 16, 2026 (new date for admission to dealings)
- **Reference Circular Date**: January 08, 2026 (NSE/CML/72210)
- **Related Circular Date**: January 12, 2026 (NSE/NCL/CMPT/72260)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative correction affecting only Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited's bonus issue shares. The one-day postponement allows proper alignment with exchange regulations and provides additional preparation time for market participants. Trading members should update their systems with the corrected effective date.
