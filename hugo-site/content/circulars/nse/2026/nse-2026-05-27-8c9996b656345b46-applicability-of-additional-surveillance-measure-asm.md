## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 395/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74443) dated May 27, 2026, notifying the applicability of the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Limited (SPARC, ISIN: INE232I01014) has been moved from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework to the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) Stage I, effective May 29, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from June 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- SPARC (Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Limited) is moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM) Stage I effective May 29, 2026.
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 01, 2026 and new positions from June 02, 2026 onwards.
- No securities were shortlisted for LTASM Stage IV in this circular (Nil).
- No securities were moved between Stage I→II, II→III, I→IV, or II→IV (all Nil).
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued with reference to prior NSE circulars on ASM: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis, though no securities have been placed in Stage IV under this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a 100% margin on all open positions in SPARC as on June 01, 2026 and on new positions created from June 02, 2026 onwards.
- Members should note that ASM actions are supplementary to all other prevailing surveillance measures and must be complied with accordingly.
- Members may refer to NSE FAQs on ASM at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for further guidance.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Circular issuance date.
- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of SPARC in Long-Term ASM Stage I.
- **June 01, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- **June 02, 2026**: 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 01, 2026 and new positions created onwards.

## Impact Assessment

The primary market impact is on SPARC (Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Limited, INE232I01014), which has been escalated from Short-Term ASM to Long-Term ASM Stage I. Traders and investors holding or planning to take positions in SPARC will be required to maintain 100% margin from June 02, 2026, significantly increasing the capital requirement for trading in this security. This could reduce liquidity and trading volumes in SPARC. No other securities are affected by this circular as all other ASM stage transitions list 'Nil' entries.
