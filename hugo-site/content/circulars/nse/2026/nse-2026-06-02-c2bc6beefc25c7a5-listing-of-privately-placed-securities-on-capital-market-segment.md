## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of 12+ privately placed debt securities to dealings on the National Stock Exchange Capital Market segment with effect from June 03, 2026. The securities comprise multiple Strip bonds issued by Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA) at 8.50% coupon and one bond issued by Dar Credit & Capital Limited at 12.75% coupon, all with market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Effective date for trading: June 03, 2026
- All securities listed under Series N0 with market lot size of 1
- HMDA has issued 12 STRPP (Strip) bonds ranging from 2030 to 2043 maturities, all at 8.50% coupon rate
- Dar Credit & Capital Limited listed one bond (DCCL 12.75% 2028) maturing November 29, 2028
- Securities are identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Lot sizes are as specified in the Annexure per Regulation 2.5.5

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for all trades in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (market lot: 1)
- No additional compliance obligations beyond standard debt market trading rules

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 02, 2026
- Effective Date of Trading: June 03, 2026
- HMDA bond maturities: June 2030 through June 2043 (various dates)
- DCCL bond maturity: November 29, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational listing circular with low market impact. The HMDA STRPP bonds represent a structured series of strip securities with staggered maturities (2030–2043) at a uniform 8.50% coupon, providing long-duration investment options for debt market participants. The DCCL bond at 12.75% offers a higher-yield shorter-duration option maturing in 2028. No impact on equity markets or existing securities. Applicable only to debt market participants interested in trading these specific instruments on NSE.
