## Summary

NSE has listed units of Groww Nifty PSE ETF managed by Groww Asset Management Limited on the Capital Market segment. The ETF will commence trading from February 11, 2026, under the symbol GROWWPSE with ISIN INF666M01NQ3. A total of 624,188 units were allotted on February 9, 2026, at an issue price of Rs. 103.54 per unit with a market lot size of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: GROWWPSE
- ISIN: INF666M01NQ3
- Total Units Allotted: 624,188
- Date of Allotment: February 09, 2026
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Paid Up Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 103.54/- per unit
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading Members: Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited (TM: 90246), East India Securities (TM: 08134), Irage Broking Services LLP (TM: 90068)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code GROWWPSE for identification on the trading system
- All transactions must adhere to the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ETF listing effective February 11, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: February 09, 2026
- **Listing Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Circular Effective Date**: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with a new investment vehicle tracking the Nifty PSE (Public Sector Enterprises) index. The ETF is managed by Groww Asset Management Limited with KFin Technologies Limited as the Registrar and Share Transfer Agent. Impact is limited to investors seeking exposure to public sector enterprises through an exchange-traded fund structure.
