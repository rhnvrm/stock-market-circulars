## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement calendar for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment for July 2026. The circular covers two settlement types: Normal T+1 settlement (Type 6) and Auction settlement (Type 7), both effective throughout July 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar issued under Regulation 6.2 of NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997)
- Normal EGR settlement (Type 6, T+1) runs from settlement number 2026033 to 2026055, covering July 1 to July 31, 2026
- Auction EGR settlement (Type 7) runs from settlement number 2026033 to 2026049, covering July 2 to July 24, 2026
- Weekends result in settlement dates falling on the next trading day (e.g., trades on July 3 settle on July 6)
- Last Normal settlement (2026055) for July 31 trades settles on August 3, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification issued pursuant to Regulation 6.2 of NSE Clearing Limited Capital Market Regulations, 1997.

## Compliance Requirements

All EGR segment members are required to observe the published settlement schedules for Normal (Type 6) and Auction (Type 7) settlement types during July 2026.

## Important Dates

- **July 1, 2026**: First trade date for Normal settlement (2026033); settles July 2
- **July 2, 2026**: First trade date for Auction settlement (2026033); settles July 3
- **July 31, 2026**: Last trade date of the month for Normal settlement (2026055); settles August 3, 2026
- **July 24, 2026**: Last trade date listed for Auction settlement (2026049)

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative circular with no market-moving impact. EGR segment members should update their systems with the July 2026 settlement schedule to ensure timely settlement of Electronic Gold Receipt trades. The calendar reflects standard T+1 settlement for normal trades and next-day settlement for auction trades, with adjustments for weekends.
