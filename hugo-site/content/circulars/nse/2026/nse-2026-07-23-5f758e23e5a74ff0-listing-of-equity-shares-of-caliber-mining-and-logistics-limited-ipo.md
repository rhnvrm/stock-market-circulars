## Summary

This circular pertains to the listing of equity shares of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited on NSE following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). It includes the post-issue Shareholding Pattern (Form under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR) as of the listing date, effective through 23.07.2027.

## Key Points

- Post-issue shareholding pattern filed for Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited under Regulation 31(1)(a).
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, or significant beneficial owner declared.
- The company has shares under lock-in and no promoter shares are pledged or under non-disposal undertaking.
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 16 shareholders holding 48,495,677 shares (74.18% of total equity).
- Public shareholders: 109,625 holders holding 16,880,108 shares (25.82% of total equity).
- Total shares outstanding: 65,375,785, with 85.45% held in dematerialized form.
- No shares held by Non-Promoter-Non-Public category (including employee trusts or DR-underlying shares).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a standard post-IPO disclosure filing required under SEBI LODR Regulation 31(1)(a) for newly listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited is required to maintain and periodically update this shareholding pattern disclosure as per SEBI LODR requirements applicable to listed entities.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern filed with reference validity noted through 23.07.2027 (post-issue).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing and disclosure circular with limited immediate market impact. It confirms the company's formal entry into public markets and establishes baseline shareholding data for investors and analysts tracking promoter versus public float, which is relevant for assessing liquidity and free-float based index eligibility going forward.
