## Summary

NSE has informed members that the ISIN code for the equity shares of Mangalam Worldwide Limited (MWL) will change effective July 10, 2026. The change is on account of the sub-division (stock split) of the company's equity shares from a face value of Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Mangalam Worldwide Limited (Symbol: MWL)
- New ISIN: INE0JYY01029
- Reason for change: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- The new ISIN will be effective for all trades done on and from the ex-date, i.e., July 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirement is introduced; this is an administrative/operational update to reflect the ISIN change resulting from the corporate action (stock split) undertaken by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange must update their systems and records to reflect the new ISIN (INE0JYY01029) for MWL equity shares effective July 10, 2026, to ensure accurate trade processing and settlement.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date (Ex-date): July 10, 2026 — new ISIN applicable for all trades from this date
- Circular Date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The ISIN change is a direct consequence of the stock split (sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-) for Mangalam Worldwide Limited. Market participants, custodians, and depository participants need to ensure systems are updated with the new ISIN to avoid settlement mismatches. The impact on trading is operational rather than price-driven, though the underlying stock split itself would already reflect in adjusted share prices and quantities.
