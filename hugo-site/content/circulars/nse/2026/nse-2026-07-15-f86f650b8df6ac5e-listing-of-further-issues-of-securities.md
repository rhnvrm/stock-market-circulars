## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for eleven companies, arising primarily from allotments made under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). These additional shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment of NSE with effect from July 16, 2026, under their existing symbols, series and ISINs. Details of each allotment, including the number of securities, distinctive number ranges, market lot and lock-in status, are specified in the Annexure to the circular.

## Key Points

- Companies covered: Dr. Agarwal's Health Care Limited (AGARWALEYE), Entero Healthcare Solutions Limited (ENTERO), Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited (EQUITASBNK), The Federal Bank Limited (FEDERALBNK), ICICI Bank Limited (ICICIBANK), Insolation Energy Limited (INSOLATION), Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited (KOTAKBANK), The Karnataka Bank Limited (KTKBANK), NIIT Learning Systems Limited (NIITLS), Polycab India Limited (POLYCAB) and Restaurant Brands Asia Limited (RBA).
- Dr. Agarwal's Health Care Limited: 43,155 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted under ESOP on 09-Jul-2026 (ISIN INE943P01029), market lot 1, no lock-in.
- Entero Healthcare Solutions Limited: 1,475 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 26-Jun-2026 (ISIN INE010601016), market lot 1, no lock-in.
- Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited: 174,562 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 02-Jul-2026 (ISIN INE063P01018), market lot 1, no lock-in.
- The Federal Bank Limited: further issue under ISIN INE171A01029; full allotment details continue beyond the excerpted content.
- All new shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies and carry no lock-in restrictions (as specified for the companies detailed).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this circular implements existing provisions under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of further securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize these securities by their designated codes and trade them in the specified market lot (1 share) from the effective date. No additional action is required from investors; the shares will automatically reflect in demat accounts of allottees.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 15, 2026
- Effective date of listing: July 16, 2026
- Allotment dates vary by company: Dr. Agarwal's Health Care (09-Jul-2026), Entero Healthcare (26-Jun-2026), Equitas Small Finance Bank (02-Jul-2026), and others as per the full Annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing update reflecting ESOP-driven share allotments across eleven companies. The incremental share quantities are small relative to each company's total outstanding shares, resulting in negligible dilution and no material effect on trading dynamics, though it marginally increases free-float share counts for the affected stocks.
