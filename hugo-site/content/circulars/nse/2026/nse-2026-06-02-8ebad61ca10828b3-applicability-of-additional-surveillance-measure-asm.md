## Summary

NSE Surveillance has issued Circular No. 408/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/SURV/74506) dated June 02, 2026, updating the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long-Term ASM) framework. Wockhardt Limited (WOCKPHARMA) is newly added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I (transitioned from Short-Term ASM), while Vidya Wires Limited (VIDYAWIRES) is escalated from Stage-I to Stage-II. A 100% margin requirement will apply to all affected securities effective June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- WOCKPHARMA (Wockhardt Limited, ISIN: INE049B01025) added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I w.e.f. June 03, 2026; moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM) framework.
- VIDYAWIRES (Vidya Wires Limited, ISIN: INE14UN01029) moved from Long-Term ASM Stage-I to Stage-II w.e.f. June 03, 2026.
- No securities shortlisted for Long-Term ASM Stage-IV in this circular.
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage-IV) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) — not applicable in this circular as Stage-IV list is nil.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the Long-Term ASM list under the framework established by earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. The escalation of WOCKPHARMA from STASM to LTASM represents a tightening of surveillance classification for that security.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure 100% margin collection on WOCKPHARMA and VIDYAWIRES positions effective June 05, 2026, covering all open positions as of June 04, 2026 and any new positions thereafter.
- Members should communicate the ASM status and associated margin obligations to their clients holding or intending to trade in these securities.
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: ASM framework stage changes take effect (inclusion of WOCKPHARMA in Stage-I; VIDYAWIRES moves to Stage-II).
- **June 04, 2026**: Last date for existing open positions before enhanced margin regime.
- **June 05, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes applicable on all open positions and new positions in affected securities.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in WOCKPHARMA will face significantly increased capital requirements due to the 100% margin rule, potentially forcing position reductions and creating short-term selling pressure. WOCKPHARMA's transition from Short-Term ASM to Long-Term ASM signals sustained regulatory scrutiny and may weigh on market sentiment for the stock. VIDYAWIRES' escalation to Stage-II further restricts trading in that security. No securities are moved to the Trade-for-Trade segment in this circular, limiting the most severe liquidity impact. Overall market impact is contained to these two specific securities.
