## Summary

NSE has informed members of the adjustment to Futures and Options contracts in UltraTech Cement Limited (ULTRACEMCO) following the company's announcement of a Rs 240/- per share dividend on a face value of Rs 10/-. The adjustment will take effect from the ex-date/effective date of July 30, 2026, in line with SEBI guidelines on corporate action adjustments.

## Key Points

- Corporate action type: Dividend
- Company: UltraTech Cement Limited (Symbol: ULTRACEMCO)
- Face value: Rs 10/-
- Dividend amount: Rs 240/-
- Revised option strike prices will be provided one day prior to the ex-date/effective date
- Adjustment methodology available at NSE's equity derivatives corporate actions adjustments page

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are being made pursuant to SEBI guidelines prescribed for corporate action adjustments to futures and options contracts. Revised option strike prices, futures base prices, lot size, and quantity freeze limits will be reflected in the updated contract.gz and MII contract/spread files.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz/MII contract file (NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) and spread file (NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) onto their trading applications before trading on the ex-date/effective date
- These files are available via the faoftp/faocommon directory on the Extranet server, or on the NSE website at nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation

## Important Dates

- Ex-date & effective date: July 30, 2026
- Revised contract files: available one day prior to the ex-date (July 29, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, mechanical adjustment required to keep ULTRACEMCO F&O contracts economically consistent following the dividend payout. Members and traders with open positions in ULTRACEMCO derivatives need to account for revised strike prices and contract specifications ahead of the ex-date to avoid mismatches in trading systems. No broader market impact is expected beyond ULTRACEMCO derivatives participants.
