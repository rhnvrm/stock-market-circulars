## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73386, Circular Ref. No: 0477/2026) informing all Exchange members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Innovision Limited via an IPO. The company will be listed under the symbol INNOVISION with ISIN INE0ADB01012. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Innovision Limited is set to list its equity shares on NSE via an IPO
- Trading symbol: **INNOVISION**
- ISIN: **INE0ADB01012**
- The exact listing date has not been announced yet and will be provided in a subsequent circular
- Circular issued by the Listing Department of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard informational notice regarding an upcoming IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from members at this stage. Members should watch for the follow-up circular that will announce the official listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 20, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact as it signals the imminent listing of Innovision Limited on NSE. Traders and investors interested in the IPO should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. No immediate trading actions are required as the listing date remains unconfirmed.
