## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued an advisory to all Members, Custodians and PCMs regarding the process for early pay-in (EPI) of securities for DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (HEALTHADD), which is undergoing a face value split (sub-division) from Rs 10 per unit to Re 1 per unit. The circular references the consolidated EPI circular (NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) and provides specific instructions for handling the ISIN transition during the record/ex-date period.

## Key Points

- Security involved: DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (Symbol: HEALTHADD), Existing/Old ISIN: INF740KA1UF4
- Corporate action: Face Value Split (Sub-Division) from Rs 10/- per unit to Re 1/- per unit
- Record Date and Ex-Date: July 3, 2026
- Members doing EPI on July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123) must use the Old/Existing ISIN
- Members doing EPI on July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124) must use the New ISIN
- Early pay-in must be done without adjusting for the conversion ratio for the applicable settlement type on the trade date
- Example: for a sale quantity of 10 shares, early pay-in in the depository should be made in the old ISIN for a quantity of 1 share (reflecting the 10:1 split ratio)
- Members must adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022 on the SEBI Block Mechanism for client demat accounts undertaking sale transactions
- In case of early pay-in through block mechanism, the client-wise early pay-in allocation file should not be uploaded
- Custodians providing early pay-in through pool accounts must upload client-wise early pay-in allocation details as per actual traded quantity
- After the record date, market participants/custodians must execute pay-in/early pay-in instructions in the New ISIN

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes the face value split corporate action under the existing consolidated early pay-in framework (NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) and references the SEBI Block Mechanism circular (NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must use the Old ISIN for early pay-in on the ex-date/record date (July 3, 2026) without adjusting for the split ratio
- Members and custodians must switch to the New ISIN for early pay-in from July 6, 2026 onward
- Custodians using pool accounts must upload client-wise allocation details based on actual traded quantities to pass on early pay-in benefits to clients
- Entities using block mechanism for early pay-in must not upload the client-wise early pay-in allocation file

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex-Date: July 3, 2026
- EPI in Old ISIN: July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123)
- EPI in New ISIN: July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational and procedural circular affecting only clearing/settlement mechanics for DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (HEALTHADD) around its face value split. It does not affect the fund's underlying value or investor holdings materially, but non-compliance with ISIN-specific EPI instructions could cause settlement failures for members and custodians handling trades in this security around the record date.
