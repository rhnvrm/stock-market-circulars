## Summary

NSE has issued the further contract launch calendar for calendar year 2027 for WTI Crude Oil/WTI Crude Oil Mini Futures, WTI Crude Oil Options on Futures, Natural Gas (Henry Hub)/Natural Gas Mini (Henry Hub) Futures, and Natural Gas Options on Futures in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. This follows the contract specifications laid out in earlier Exchange circular no. 73930 dated April 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 44/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/COM/75171, dated July 14, 2026.
- Provides contract launch and expiry dates for four annexures covering the calendar year 2027:
  - Annexure A: WTI Crude Oil / WTI Crude Oil Mini Futures
  - Annexure B: WTI Crude Oil Options on Futures
  - Annexure C: Natural Gas (Henry Hub) / Natural Gas Mini (Henry Hub) Futures
  - Annexure D: Natural Gas Options on Futures
- WTI Crude Oil/Mini Futures for Jan-27 through Dec-27 will be launched progressively between 21-Jul-26 and 22-Jun-27, with expiries between 19-Jan-27 and 17-Dec-27.
- WTI Crude Oil Options on Futures for Jan-27 through Dec-27 will be launched between 20-Oct-26 and 21-Sep-27, with expiries between 08-Jan-27 and 08-Dec-27.
- Natural Gas (Henry Hub)/Mini Futures contracts for Jan-27 through at least Jul-27 will be launched between 29-Jul-26 and 27-Jan-27, with expiries between 25-Jan-27 and 27-Jul-27 (data for later months not included in the extracted content).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular is an operational extension of the contract specifications previously notified via circular no. 73930 dated April 28, 2026, announcing forward-looking launch dates for the next annual cycle of contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in Commodity Derivatives Segment products (WTI Crude Oil, WTI Crude Oil Mini, Natural Gas Henry Hub, and related options) should note the specified launch and expiry dates for planning trading, risk management, and client communication purposes. No specific action beyond awareness is mandated.

## Important Dates

- WTI Crude Oil/Mini Futures: first 2027 contract (Jan-27) launches 21-Jul-26, expires 19-Jan-27; last listed contract (Dec-27) launches 22-Jun-27, expires 17-Dec-27.
- WTI Crude Oil Options on Futures: first contract (Jan-27) launches 20-Oct-26, expires 08-Jan-27; last listed contract (Dec-27) launches 21-Sep-27, expires 08-Dec-27.
- Natural Gas (Henry Hub)/Mini Futures: first contract (Jan-27) launches 29-Jul-26, expires 25-Jan-27.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular relevant primarily to commodity derivatives members and traders for forward planning of contract cycles. It does not affect equity listings, corporate actions, or broader market operations.
