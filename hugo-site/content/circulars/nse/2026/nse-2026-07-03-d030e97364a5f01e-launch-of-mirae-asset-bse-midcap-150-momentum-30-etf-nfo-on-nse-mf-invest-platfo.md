## Summary

NSE's NMF TM Segment has notified members that the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF NFO, under Mirae Asset Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 06, 2026 to July 13, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- The NFO covers the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF, offered in both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plans.
- Both plans share the ISIN INF769K01RC0 and fall under the Equity category.
- Allotment date for the scheme is July 16, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 15, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification regarding platform availability for a new fund offer, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting on behalf of clients must ensure clear funds through any payment mode reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 15, 2026, 11:59 pm, to complete the subscription process.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 06, 2026 to July 13, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Clear funds deadline: July 15, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions and investors interested in subscribing to this new ETF offering. It does not affect existing listed securities or trading operations.
