## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder circular referencing its earlier circulars (NSE/INSP/74185 dated May 14, 2026 and NSE/INSP/74775 dated June 18, 2026) regarding submission of the Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) Report for FY 2025-26. This applies to Self-certification REs, Small-size REs, Mid-size REs, and Qualified REs (excluding those categorized as QSBs). The circular reiterates the due dates for conducting the VAPT audit through a CERT-In empanelled auditor and for submitting the IT Committee-approved report.

## Key Points

- Applicable to: Self-certification REs, Small-size REs, Mid-size REs, and Qualified REs (not categorized as QSBs).
- VAPT must be conducted through a CERT-In empanelled auditor.
- The final report must be submitted only after approval from the respective IT Committee.
- Operating guidelines for auditor registration and report submission are available on ENIT under: Inspection → VAPT → VAPT Help Documents (for both Trading Members and Auditors).
- Members previously classified as 'Exempted from CSCRF' (per SEBI circular dated April 30, 2025 criteria/thresholds) must submit a declaration via the member portal at: Inspection → VAPT → Display VAPT Auditor Registration → New Auditor → select 'Exempted' under Member Category → submit declaration.
- Members are directed to Annexure 1.2 of Exchange Circular No. NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026 for details on actions/penalties for non-compliance in periodic VAPT submissions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a reminder of previously communicated requirements under circulars NSE/INSP/74185 (May 14, 2026), NSE/INSP/74775 (June 18, 2026), and NSE/INSP/73792 (April 17, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible trading members/REs must conduct VAPT through a CERT-In empanelled auditor.
- The VAPT report must be reviewed and approved by the member's IT Committee before submission.
- Auditor registration and report submission must follow the operating guidelines available on ENIT.
- Members exempted from CSCRF must file a declaration on the member portal as per the specified path.
- Non-compliant members are subject to actions outlined in Annexure 1.2 of circular NSE/INSP/73792.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Due date for conduct of VAPT through a CERT-In empanelled auditor.
- July 31, 2026: Due date for submission of the VAPT report after IT Committee approval.
- Related prior circulars: NSE/INSP/74185 (May 14, 2026), NSE/INSP/74775 (June 18, 2026), NSE/INSP/73792 (April 17, 2026).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact and does not concern any listed securities. It primarily affects the compliance operations of trading members/REs, who must ensure timely completion of cybersecurity audits and IT Committee approvals to avoid penalties or regulatory action as outlined in prior circulars. The reminder underscores NSE's continued focus on cybersecurity resilience (VAPT/CSCRF) across its member base.
