## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) for 25 companies on the Capital Market segment. The new securities will be available for trading effective June 29, 2026, with designated security codes and lot sizes as specified in the annexure.

## Key Points

- 25 companies have additional equity shares admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Effective date for trading: June 29, 2026
- All allotments are under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Securities identified by existing designated codes; market lot is 1 for listed entries
- Aadhar Housing Finance Limited (AADHARHFC): 38,218 shares (ISIN INE883F01010), allotted June 18, 2026, distinctive numbers 437298900–437337117
- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited (ABSLAMC): 28,452 shares (ISIN INE404A01024), allotted June 19, 2026, distinctive numbers 289175043–289203494
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable for AADHARHFC shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities and lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading in the newly listed securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the annexure
- No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 25, 2026
- Effective Trading Date: June 29, 2026
- Allotment dates vary by company (June 18–19, 2026 confirmed for first two entries)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of ESOP-allotted shares for 25 companies. The quantities involved are small relative to existing share capital. No change in trading rules, circuit breakers, or market structure. Existing shareholders experience slight dilution from ESOP allotments, but this is a pre-approved and expected corporate action for each company.
