## Summary

NSE has admitted the equity shares of TCC Concept Limited (Symbol: TCC, ISIN: INE887D01016) to dealings on the Capital Market segment (Rolling Settlement) effective February 25, 2026. A total of 47,528,061 fully paid-up equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value are listed under series EQ with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** TCC
- **Company:** TCC Concept Limited
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE887D01016
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Total Shares Listed:** 47,528,061
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 47,528,061
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Trading Mode:** Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement), compulsory demat for all investors
- **Registrar:** MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying admission of securities and their designated lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in TCC shares must be conducted using the designated symbol **TCC** and the specified security codes.
- Trading is in compulsory demat mode for all investors.
- Members must adhere to the market lot size of 1 as specified in the Annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (partial — 7,136,502 shares):** April 3, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (partial — 11,855,553 shares):** July 14, 2026
- **Free (no lock-in):** 22,734,060 shares + 5,801,946 shares (total 28,536,006 shares)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of TCC Concept Limited introduces a new tradeable equity on NSE from February 25, 2026. Approximately 60% of shares (28,536,006 out of 47,528,061) are free from lock-in at the time of listing, while the remaining ~40% are subject to staggered lock-in periods expiring in April and July 2026. This lock-in structure may affect near-term liquidity. Investors and members should note the lock-in schedule when assessing tradeable float. The company is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, and operates in the concept/services sector based on available information.
