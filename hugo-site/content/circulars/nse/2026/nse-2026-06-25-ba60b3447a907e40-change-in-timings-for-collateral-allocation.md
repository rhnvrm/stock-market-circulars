## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced a change in timings for collateral allocation on July 03, 2026, for clearing members in the Currency Derivatives Segment. This is issued in reference to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/DOP/P/CIR/2022/101 dated July 27, 2022, on Settlement of Running Account of Client's Funds.

## Key Points

- The collateral up-streaming and down-streaming window for cash and fixed deposit receipts (FDR) will remain open until **8:00 PM** on July 03, 2026.
- The collateral allocation window will remain open until **10:00 PM** on July 03, 2026.
- NSE Clearing will endeavor to release Cash/FDR/BG within one hour of receiving a request from the clearing member, subject to collateral availability after necessary margin checks.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rules are introduced. This circular implements an operational timing change on a specific date, consistent with the framework established under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/DOP/P/CIR/2022/101 (July 27, 2022) regarding settlement of running accounts of client funds held with Trading Members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members in the Currency Derivatives Segment must note the revised timings for July 03, 2026.
- Members should plan collateral up-streaming, down-streaming, and allocation requests accordingly to ensure completion within the extended windows.

## Important Dates

- **July 03, 2026**: Revised collateral timing windows are effective for this date.
  - Collateral up-streaming/down-streaming (cash and FDR): open until 8:00 PM
  - Collateral allocation window: open until 10:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational circular affecting clearing members in the Currency Derivatives Segment on a single trading date. The extended windows provide additional time for collateral management and are unlikely to disrupt normal operations. Members should update their internal processes for July 03, 2026, to align with the revised schedule.
