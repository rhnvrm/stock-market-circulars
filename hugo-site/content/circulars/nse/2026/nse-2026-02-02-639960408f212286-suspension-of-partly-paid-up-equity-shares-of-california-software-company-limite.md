## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in partly paid-up equity shares of California Software Company Limited (Symbol: CALSOFTPP, ISIN: IN9526B01012) effective from February 05, 2026. The suspension is implemented under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, and is attributed to the first and final call money on these shares.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies only to partly paid-up equity shares (Symbol: CALSOFTPP)
- ISIN of suspended security: IN9526B01012
- Suspension reason: First and final call money
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/72586, Circular No. 0165/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action is taken in pursuance of existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in the specified partly paid-up equity shares from February 05, 2026
- Trading will be suspended from closing hours of February 04, 2026
- Shareholders holding partly paid-up shares are expected to respond to the call money notice from the company

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Last Trading Day**: February 04, 2026 (until closing hours)
- **Suspension Effective Date**: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of partly paid-up equity shares of California Software Company Limited. This suspension does not affect the company's fully paid-up equity shares if listed separately. 

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding partly paid-up shares (CALSOFTPP) will not be able to trade these securities from February 05, 2026 onwards. They will need to pay the first and final call money as demanded by the company to convert their partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up shares.

**Operational Impact**: Standard suspension procedure; trading members should update their systems to block trading in ISIN IN9526B01012 from the specified date.
