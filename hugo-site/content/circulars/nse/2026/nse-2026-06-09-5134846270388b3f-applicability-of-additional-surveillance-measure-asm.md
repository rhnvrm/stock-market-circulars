## Summary

NSE has issued Circular Ref. No. 429/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/74623) placing four securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) Stage I effective June 10, 2026. A mandatory 100% margin requirement applies on all open positions as on June 11, 2026 and all new positions from June 12, 2026 onwards. No securities are being shifted to Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade) under this circular.

## Key Points

- Four securities added to LTASM Stage I effective June 10, 2026
- 100% margin applicable from June 12, 2026 on open and new positions
- BALAMINES is being moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to LTASM framework
- No securities shortlisted for LTASM Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade shift) under this circular
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage III
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities have been shortlisted under LTASM Stage I w.e.f. June 10, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|
| 1 | ACUTAAS | Acutaas Chemicals Limited | INE00FF01025 |
| 2 | BALAMINES | Balaji Amines Limited* | INE050E01027 |
| 3 | MONOLITH | Monolithisch India Limited | INE1DV401010 |
| 4 | VINYAS | Vinyas Innovative Technologies Limited | INE0OLS01010 |

*BALAMINES moved from STASM to LTASM framework.

No securities are shortlisted under Stage IV, nor are any securities moving across stages under this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Must apply 100% margin on all open positions in the listed securities as on June 11, 2026 and on all new positions created from June 12, 2026 onwards.
- Members are advised to note that the ASM framework is applied in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting is purely on account of market surveillance and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned companies.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: LTASM Stage I inclusion effective date for ACUTAAS, BALAMINES, MONOLITH, VINYAS
- **June 11, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions to which 100% margin applies
- **June 12, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes effective for all open positions (as of June 11) and all new positions

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding or planning positions in ACUTAAS, BALAMINES, MONOLITH, and VINYAS will face significantly higher capital requirements due to the 100% margin mandate. This effectively increases the cost of maintaining positions and may lead to forced liquidation of under-margined positions. BALAMINES, being escalated from STASM to LTASM, faces a more stringent surveillance regime. Since no securities are being moved to Stage IV, there is no immediate shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement, which would have further restricted trading. Market participants should review their exposure to these four securities ahead of June 11, 2026.
