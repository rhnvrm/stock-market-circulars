## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) is introducing a new Margins service within the NMASS-Margins module for the Securities Lending & Borrowing (SLB) Scheme. This enhancement allows members to access detailed margin information at multiple levels, improving transparency and user experience for risk and operations teams.

## Key Points

- New Margin service launched for SLB within NMASS-Margins module
- Members can view total margins at member and client level
- Members can view settlement level margins
- Members can view security level margins
- An Annexure manual is provided detailing how super admins can create new roles or update existing roles to grant users access to this service

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational enhancement to NCL's clearing member-facing systems adding a new margin visibility feature to the existing NMASS-Margins module.

## Compliance Requirements

- Super admins at member firms must create a new role or update existing roles in NMASS to grant relevant users access to the new Margins service information
- Members should familiarize their risk/operations teams with the new margin views (total, settlement-level, security-level)

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 13, 2026
- Circular Date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational update aimed at enhancing member access to margin-related data for SLB. It does not affect trading operations, market pricing, or listed securities, but improves risk monitoring capability for clearing members participating in the SLB segment.
