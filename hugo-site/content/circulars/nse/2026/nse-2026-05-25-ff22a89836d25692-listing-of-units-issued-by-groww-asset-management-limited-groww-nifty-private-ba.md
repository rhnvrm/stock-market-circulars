## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units of Groww Nifty Private Bank ETF, a scheme of Groww Mutual Fund managed by Groww Asset Management Limited, on the NSE Capital Market segment. The units will be available for trading from May 26, 2026 under the symbol PVTBKGROWW with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** PVTBKGROWW
- **ISIN:** INF666M01OT5
- **Security Description:** Units of Rs. 10/- each of Groww Mutual Fund – Groww Nifty Private Bank ETF
- **Total Units Listed:** 2,106,136
- **Series:** EQ
- **Date of Allotment:** May 22, 2026
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 26.39/- per unit
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Market Makers:** Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd (TM: 90246), East India Securities (TM: 08134), Irage Broking Services LLP (TM: 90068), Parwati Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (TM: 11890)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code PVTBKGROWW for all system transactions.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers listed above are designated to provide liquidity for this ETF.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 22, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Circular Date:** May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new ETF listing with limited broader market impact. The Groww Nifty Private Bank ETF tracks private sector banks and adds another index ETF option for investors seeking exposure to this segment. With four designated market makers assigned, adequate liquidity support is in place from day one. The issue price of Rs. 26.39 against a face value of Rs. 10 reflects the underlying NAV at allotment. Impact is confined to investors and trading members interested in the private banking sector ETF space.
