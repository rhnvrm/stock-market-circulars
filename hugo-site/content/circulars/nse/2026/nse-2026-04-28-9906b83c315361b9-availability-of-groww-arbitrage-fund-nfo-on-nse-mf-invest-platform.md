## Summary

NSE has informed all members that Groww Arbitrage Fund, offered by Groww Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from April 29, 2026. The platform will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities for all six scheme variants.

## Key Points

- Groww Arbitrage Fund becomes available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform from April 29, 2026
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are included
- Six scheme variants are offered across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options
- All six schemes are categorized as Equity
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment (Ref: NSE/NMFTM/73937, Circular Ref: 1339/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members are informed for awareness so they can facilitate client transactions in Groww Arbitrage Fund through NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **April 29, 2026**: Groww Arbitrage Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic plans (SIP/STP/SWP) on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine platform onboarding notification for a single AMC's arbitrage fund. Members using NSE MF Invest Platform can now offer Groww Arbitrage Fund transactions to their clients. Arbitrage funds are equity-categorized but carry relatively low risk, targeting returns from price differentials between cash and futures markets. No systemic or broad market implications.
