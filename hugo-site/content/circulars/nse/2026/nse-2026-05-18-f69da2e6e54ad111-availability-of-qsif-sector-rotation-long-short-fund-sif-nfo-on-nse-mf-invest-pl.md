## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Qsif Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) by Quant Mutual Fund, will be available on the NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with effect from May 18, 2026. The fund will also support SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Qsif Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund (SIF) by Quant Mutual Fund is reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform from May 18, 2026.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also available on the platform.
- Six scheme variants are available across Direct and Regular plans, with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options.
- All six schemes are categorized as Equity.
- ISINs assigned: INF966L30290, INF966L30282, INF966L30274, INF966L30324, INF966L30316, INF966L30308.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding platform availability of an existing fund category.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members wishing to offer this fund to clients can do so via the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2026**: Effective date for availability of all six scheme variants on NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP/STP/SWP registrations.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This circular is a routine operational update notifying NSE members of the availability of a new Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It expands investment options for retail and institutional investors interested in a sector rotation long-short equity strategy offered by Quant Mutual Fund. No trading halts, compliance deadlines, or systemic changes are involved.
