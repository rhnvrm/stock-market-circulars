## Summary

NSE has notified a change in ISIN for V2 Retail Limited (symbol: V2RETAIL) as a result of a share sub-division (stock split) from a face value of Rs. 10/- per share to Re. 1/- per share. The new ISIN INE945H01021 will be effective from the ex-date of March 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- V2 Retail Limited (V2RETAIL) is undergoing a share sub-division from face value Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- New ISIN assigned: INE945H01021
- Change is effective for all trades on and from the ex-date: March 25, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/73431, Circular Ref. No: 0497/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN code for equity shares of V2 Retail Limited is being changed in accordance with the corporate action (share sub-division). All trading, settlement, and depository records must reflect the new ISIN from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE945H01021) for V2RETAIL from March 25, 2026
- All trades executed on or after the ex-date must use the new ISIN
- Back-office, risk, and depository systems should be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 23, 2026
- **Ex-Date / Effective Date:** March 25, 2026 — new ISIN applicable for all trades from this date

## Impact Assessment

The share sub-division reduces the face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- (a 10:1 split ratio implied), which will proportionally increase the number of shares held by investors while reducing the per-share price. The ISIN change requires operational updates by trading members, depositories, and custodians. Market liquidity for V2RETAIL may improve post-split due to a lower per-share price. No broader market impact is expected beyond the single stock.
