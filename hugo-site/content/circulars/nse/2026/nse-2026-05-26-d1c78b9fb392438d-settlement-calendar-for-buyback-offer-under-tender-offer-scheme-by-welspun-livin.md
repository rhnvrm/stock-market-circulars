## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the buyback offer under the tender offer scheme by Welspun Living Limited, referencing circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74415 dated May 26, 2026. The buyback window opens May 29, 2026 and closes June 4, 2026, with final settlement on June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X, Settlement No. 2026058
- Trade window: May 29, 2026 to June 4, 2026
- Obligation download date: June 10, 2026
- Settlement date: June 11, 2026
- Demat shares must be tendered exclusively via Early Pay-in of Shares through depository mechanism under Market Type Buyback
- On trade end date, early pay-in of shares must be completed well before 3:30 PM for order acceptance
- Securities and funds pay-out will be processed after receipt of funds pay-in on settlement day
- STT (Securities Transaction Tax) will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular provides operational settlement schedule details in continuation of the previously announced buyback offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must use the Early Pay-in mechanism of depositories for tendering demat shares; no other method is permitted
- Early pay-in of shares on trade end date (June 4, 2026) must be submitted well in advance of the 3:30 PM cut-off
- Members should review the Tender Offer Acceptance Report which details accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders along with reason codes (Annexure I lists 26 rejection codes covering issues such as signature mismatch, invalid documents, PAN mismatch, excess shares tendered, bids above exit price, and duplicate bids)
- NCL may notify a revised settlement schedule in case of delays from the RTA or delays in funds pay-in by the buying trading member

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | May 29, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | June 4, 2026 (early pay-in cut-off: 3:30 PM) |
| Obligation Download | June 10, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 11, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Welspun Living Limited buyback tender offer. The settlement timeline is standard for buyback tender offers. Participants must ensure timely early pay-in of demat shares through the depository mechanism. The inclusion of Annexure I with 26 rejection reason codes provides transparency on order rejection criteria, helping members pre-validate submissions and reduce rejection risk. Broader market impact is limited to Welspun Living Limited (WELSPUNLIV) shareholders and trading members involved in this specific offer.
