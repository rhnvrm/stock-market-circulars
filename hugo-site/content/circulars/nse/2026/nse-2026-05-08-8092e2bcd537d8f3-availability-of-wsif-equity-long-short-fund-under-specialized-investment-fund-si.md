## Summary

NSE has informed all members that WSIF Equity Long-Short Fund, a scheme under The Wealth Company SIF Mutual Fund (Specialized Investment Fund), will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective May 11, 2026. Systematic facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP will also be available.

## Key Points

- WSIF Equity Long-Short Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from May 11, 2026
- Six scheme variants are being made available across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options
- All six variants fall under the Equity category within the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) structure
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be available
- The fund house is The Wealth Company SIF Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed of the scheme availability. No specific compliance actions are required; members may facilitate client transactions in these schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of WSIF Equity Long-Short Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This circular pertains to the availability of a specific SIF mutual fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in equity long-short strategies under the SIF framework will gain access to six variants of this fund. The scheme ISINs are:
