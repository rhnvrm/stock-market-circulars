## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74475 (Ref. 399/2026) announcing the inclusion of four securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I framework, effective June 1, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 2, 2026. No securities have been added to ST-ASM Stage II.

## Key Points

- Four securities included in ST-ASM Stage I effective June 1, 2026: ATGL (Adani Total Gas), JAYKAY (Jaykay Enterprises), NOVARTIND (Novartis India), and SURYALA (Suryalata Spinning Mills)
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- ST-ASM Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (no new additions in this circular)
- No securities moved between Stage I and Stage II in this update
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to prior ASM circulars: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 4, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). The ST-ASM framework applies enhanced margin requirements to securities exhibiting abnormal market behavior, as determined by NSE surveillance criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply the enhanced margin rates to all open positions in the listed securities as on June 1, 2026
- New positions in these securities created from June 2, 2026 onward must also comply with the applicable ST-ASM margin rates
- Price band applicable to any scrip exiting the framework will revert to its pre-ASM band, unless subject to another surveillance measure
- Shortlisting under ASM is a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 1, 2026**: Effective date for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion of four securities
- **June 2, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements apply on all open positions as on June 1, 2026 and new positions created thereafter

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in ATGL, JAYKAY, NOVARTIND, and SURYALA will face a minimum 50% margin requirement (Stage I) from June 2, 2026, which may necessitate additional capital allocation or position reduction. The absence of Stage II additions and inter-stage movements limits broader market disruption. The inclusion is based purely on market surveillance criteria and does not reflect fundamental company issues. Members should review their exposure to these four stocks and ensure adequate margin funding ahead of June 2, 2026.
