## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of units of 360 ONE MSCI India ETF (managed by 360 ONE Asset Management Limited) to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective May 29, 2026. The ETF trades under the symbol MSCI360 with ISIN INF579M01BP5, with a face value of Rs. 10/- per unit and a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** MSCI360
- **ISIN:** INF579M01BP5
- **Security Description:** Units of Rs. 10/- each of 360 ONE Mutual Fund – 360 ONE MSCI India ETF
- **Number of Units Listed:** 6,200,118
- **Series:** EQ
- **Date of Allotment:** May 22, 2026
- **Face Value / Paid-Up Value / Issue Price:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Market Makers:** Irage Broking Services LLP (TM code: 90068) and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited (TM code: 90246)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code MSCI360 for all trades in this ETF.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers (Irage Broking Services LLP and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited) are designated to provide liquidity.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing / Trading Commencement:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing notification with low broader market impact. The 360 ONE MSCI India ETF tracks the MSCI India index, providing investors access to a passively managed index product. Listing on NSE enables secondary market trading in these units. The presence of two designated market makers should support liquidity from the listing date.
