## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Commodity Derivatives (CD) segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. The session involves two trading sessions — one from the Primary Site and one from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site. No new NEAT software version will be released for this mock.

## Key Points

- Mock trading covers two sessions: Primary Site (09:00–10:05) and DR Site (10:45–11:30)
- NEAT version in use: **NEAT 1.5.4**, available at `/comtftp/comtcommon/NEATCO154` on NSE Extranet
- NEAT Adapter version: **1.0.27** (both Windows and Linux); applicable GR PORT: **10859**
- All outstanding orders will be purged before each session
- Trades in the mock session carry no financial obligation (no fund pay-in or pay-out)
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during the mock session
- UCC/PAN validation applies; only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded before cutoff will be allowed
- Members should re-login into the live environment post-mock to ensure smooth connectivity for Monday, May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Members may use this mock trading session to meet SEBI's software testing requirements as per circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 and NSE circular NSE/MSD/46441.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members enabled for CD segment trading in the live market are automatically eligible and expected to participate
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their own systems before each session
- Connectivity details available in circular NSE/MSD/73993 dated April 30, 2026
- Refer to NEAT Adapter circular NSE/MSD/73674 dated April 09, 2025 for the latest adapter application

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, May 23, 2026
- Session 1 (Primary Site): 09:00 – 10:05
- Session 2 (DR Site): 10:45 – 11:30
- Position Limit/Collateral value setup cutoff: 11:40
- Trade Modification end time: 11:40
- Live Re-login: 13:00 – 13:30
- **Live Market Resumes:** Monday, May 26, 2026 (members must ensure connectivity by then)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational mock session with no market or financial impact. It provides members an opportunity to test their trading infrastructure, NEAT software, and DR connectivity. No new version is being released, so the version-upgrade burden is absent. Members are advised to participate fully to ensure seamless live trading on May 26, 2026.
