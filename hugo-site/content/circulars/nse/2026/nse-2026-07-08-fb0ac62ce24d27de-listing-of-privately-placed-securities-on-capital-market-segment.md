## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of a privately placed debt security issued by Tata Capital Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment, effective July 09, 2026. The circular specifies the designated security code, market lot, coupon rate, and maturity date for the security as per Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- NSE has admitted a fresh issue of privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs) from Tata Capital Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment.
- The security is identified by symbol 788TACA31, series N0, with a market lot of 1.
- Issue description: TACA 7.88% 2031 Series D, carrying a coupon rate of 7.88%.
- Date of maturity is July 07, 2031.
- ISIN Code: INE976I07DG4.
- Trading in the security will be identified solely by its designated security code and traded in the specified lot size.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements existing Regulation 3.1.1 (listing of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A for a newly admitted privately placed debt security.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the designated security code (788TACA31, series N0) and the specified market lot of 1 when trading this security on the Capital Market segment.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 08, 2026
- Effective Date: July 09, 2026 (listing and trading commencement)
- Maturity Date: July 07, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification affecting a single privately placed debt instrument of Tata Capital Limited. The impact is limited to trading members and investors dealing in this specific NCD series, with no broader implications for equity markets or other listed securities.
