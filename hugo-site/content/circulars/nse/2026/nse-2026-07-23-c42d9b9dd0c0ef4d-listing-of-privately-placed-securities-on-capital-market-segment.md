## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a privately placed non-convertible debt security issued by Kisetsu Saison Finance (India) Private Limited on the Capital Market segment. The security will be admitted to dealings with effect from July 24, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, with the lot size specified per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue listing by Kisetsu Saison Finance (India) Private Limited
- Security: KSFP 8.20% 2036 Sr SD01
- Symbol: 820KSFP36A, Series: N0
- Market Lot: 1
- Coupon Rate: 8.2%
- Date of Maturity: 21-Jul-2036
- ISIN Code: INE0DZE08042

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size determination).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify and trade the security using the designated security code (820KSFP36A) and series (N0), and transact only in the specified market lot size of 1.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 24, 2026
- Maturity date of the security: July 21, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the listing of a single privately placed debt instrument from an unlisted NBFC. It is primarily relevant to institutional and debt market participants trading in this specific ISIN, with no direct implications for equity markets or broader listed companies.
