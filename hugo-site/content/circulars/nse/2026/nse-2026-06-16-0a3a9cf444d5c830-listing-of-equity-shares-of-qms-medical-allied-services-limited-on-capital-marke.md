## Summary

NSE has announced that the equity shares of QMS Medical Allied Services Limited (Symbol: QMSMEDI) will be migrated from the SME Emerge platform to the Capital Market Segment (Main Board) effective June 18, 2026. The company was originally listed on the SME Emerge platform via circular NSE/CML/53997 dated October 10, 2022.

## Key Points

- Trading in QMSMEDI equity shares migrates from SME Platform (EMERGE) to NSE Capital Market Segment – Main Board
- Effective date: June 18, 2026
- NSE Symbol: QMSMEDI; Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE0FMW01018
- Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Total securities listed: 19,337,443 equity shares (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 19,337,443)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Shares are Pari Passu; no lock-in details applicable
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/74728, Circular Ref. No. 0969/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The security migrates from the SME Emerge segment to the Main Board Capital Market Segment. Trading norms, price band rules, and circuit breaker thresholds applicable to the Main Board will now govern QMSMEDI rather than SME-specific rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new segment classification for QMSMEDI effective June 18, 2026.
- Registrar (Bigshare Services Private Limited) and the company (QMS Medical Allied Services Limited) should ensure shareholder records are aligned with the Main Board listing.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 18, 2026**: Effective date of migration to Main Board; trading commences on Capital Market Segment
- **Pre-listing Shareholding Pattern Date**: June 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The migration to the Main Board increases visibility and liquidity potential for QMSMEDI shares, as Main Board listings are accessible to a broader investor base including institutional investors. The market lot of 1 share (down from typical SME lot sizes) may increase retail trading activity. No lock-in or encumbrance on promoter shares, and no outstanding warrants, convertible securities, or ESOPs were reported in the pre-listing shareholding pattern, indicating a clean capital structure. The company (QMS Medical Allied Services Limited) is a healthcare/medical allied services firm based in Mumbai.
