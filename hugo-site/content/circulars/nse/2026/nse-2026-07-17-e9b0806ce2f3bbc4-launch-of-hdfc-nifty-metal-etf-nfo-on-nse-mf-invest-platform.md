## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the HDFC Nifty Metal ETF NFO under HDFC Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 20, 2026 to July 24, 2026 (till 6:00 pm).

## Key Points

- Scheme: HDFC Nifty Metal ETF - Direct Growth and Regular Growth, ISIN INF179KC1KT4, category Equity.
- NFO subscription window open on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 20, 2026 to July 24, 2026 till 6:00 pm.
- Allotment date for the scheme is July 27, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 27, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational circular enabling transactions for a new fund offer on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure timely remittance of clear funds to the designated Clearing Corporation account by the specified deadline to complete allotment processing.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: July 20, 2026
- NFO Close: July 24, 2026 (till 6:00 pm)
- Allotment Date: July 27, 2026
- Fund Pay-in Deadline: July 27, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily operational, informing NSE members and distributors about the availability window and settlement requirements for subscribing investors to the new ETF scheme on the MF Invest Platform.
