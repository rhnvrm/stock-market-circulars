## Summary

NSE has issued changes to the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) framework effective June 16, 2026. Three securities have been newly shortlisted under LTASM Stage I, one security moves from Stage I to Stage II, and no securities are excluded from the ASM framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Three securities added to LTASM Stage I w.e.f. June 16, 2026: KERNEX (Kernex Microsystems India Ltd), NGLFINE (NGL Fine-Chem Limited), and YASHO (Yasho Industries Limited)
- KERNEX and YASHO are moved from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework to the LTASM framework
- SHEEL (Sheel Biotech Limited) moves from LTASM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. June 16, 2026
- No securities shortlisted in LTASM Stage IV in this update
- No securities moving between Stages II, III, or IV in either direction
- No securities excluded from the ASM framework
- Consolidated ASM list continues to carry a large number of securities across all stages

## Regulatory Changes

Securities shortlisted under Stage IV (Criteria VII) are subject to a shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade for Trade settlement (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis, where T is the date of issuance of the circular. No securities are being moved to Stage IV in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and investors holding or trading in KERNEX, NGLFINE, YASHO, and SHEEL must note the applicable LTASM stage and associated trading restrictions effective June 16, 2026
- Brokers and trading members should update their systems to reflect the revised LTASM stage classifications
- Entities dealing in securities on the consolidated ASM list must comply with applicable margin and trading restrictions per the respective stage

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 16, 2026 — all LTASM changes (new additions, stage movements) take effect
- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders in KERNEX, NGLFINE, and YASHO will face LTASM Stage I restrictions starting June 16, 2026, which typically include enhanced surveillance margins. SHEEL, moving to Stage II, will face stricter trading conditions. KERNEX and YASHO transitioning from STASM to LTASM indicates prolonged surveillance concern. The absence of any Stage IV additions means no new Trade for Trade (BE series) shifts are triggered by this circular. Overall market impact is limited to holders of the four affected securities.
