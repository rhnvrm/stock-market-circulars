## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 76,190,476 further equity shares of JSW Energy Limited (symbol: JSWENERGY) allotted under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 525 per share. The shares are admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 76,190,476 new equity shares of Rs. 10 face value allotted under QIP at Rs. 525 per share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- ISIN: INE121E01018 (temporary ISIN IN8********* credited in demat per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012)
- Distinctive number range: 1,757,292,261 to 1,833,482,736
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74421, Ref No. 0854/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified to use the designated security code (JSWENERGY, Series EQ) for all trading in these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- Temporary ISIN (IN8*********) is used in demat until the permanent ISIN (INE121E01018) is activated per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 25, 2026
- Listing effective date: May 27, 2026
- Circular date: May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The QIP raises approximately Rs. 40,000 crore (76,190,476 shares × Rs. 525), representing a significant capital raise for JSW Energy. Existing shareholders will experience equity dilution. As the shares are pari passu with no lock-in, they are immediately tradeable from May 27, 2026, which may exert short-term selling pressure. The large size of the issuance makes this a notable event for energy sector watchers and JSWENERGY investors.
