## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the NSE MF Invest Platform will undergo a complete downtime due to scheduled maintenance activity on March 22, 2026, from 20:30 hrs to 23:30 hrs. Members and API-connected vendors are advised to plan activities accordingly and avoid transactions during this period.

## Key Points

- Complete downtime scheduled on **March 22, 2026, from 20:30 hrs to 23:30 hrs**
- All activities and transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform must be avoided during the downtime window
- Members using API connectivity should inform their technical teams and vendors
- Issued by the NMF TM Segment department under Circular Ref. No: 1285/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notice for a scheduled maintenance window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities or transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the downtime window (20:30–23:30 hrs, March 22, 2026)
- Members connecting via API must inform their technical team or vendor about the scheduled downtime

## Important Dates

- **Downtime Start:** March 22, 2026, 20:30 hrs
- **Downtime End:** March 22, 2026, 23:30 hrs

## Impact Assessment

The downtime is limited to a 3-hour evening window on March 22, 2026, and affects all members transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Since the window is outside normal trading hours and is scheduled in advance, operational disruption is expected to be minimal provided members plan accordingly. API users face additional responsibility to coordinate with vendors.
