## Summary

NSE has admitted units of ICICI Prudential iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective February 10, 2026. Two schemes are listed: Growth Plan (ISIFHLSG) with 238,678,214 units and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIFHLSDG) with 197,561,096 units. Units have a face value and issue price of Rs. 10/- each with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Two schemes listed: iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund - Growth (ISIFHLSG) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIFHLSDG)
- ISIFHLSG: ISIN INF109K30018, 238,678,214 units
- ISIFHLSDG: ISIN INF109K30026, 197,561,096 units
- Date of allotment: February 04, 2026
- Face value per unit: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 10/-
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Series: SF

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes (ISIFHLSG and ISIFHLSDG) for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (1 unit per lot)
- Securities identified only by designated codes on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: February 04, 2026
- **Listing Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Circular Effective Date**: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of mutual fund units that provides investors with exchange-traded access to ICICI Prudential's iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund. The listing expands investment options on NSE's Capital Market segment but does not affect existing securities or market operations.
