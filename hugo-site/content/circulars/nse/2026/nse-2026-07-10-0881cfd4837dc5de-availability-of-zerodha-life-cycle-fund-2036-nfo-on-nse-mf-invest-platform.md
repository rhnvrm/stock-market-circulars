## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing all members that the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 New Fund Offer (NFO) of Zerodha Mutual Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- The circular pertains to the NMF TM Segment (NSE Mutual Fund platform) for members transacting in mutual fund units.
- Scheme covered: Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01224), categorized as an Equity scheme.
- The scheme will be available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions as well as SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notification about fund availability on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members offering mutual fund transaction services must ensure their systems reflect the scheme's availability for ongoing transactions from the effective date and route client orders accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 13, 2026 — Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2036 Direct Plan Growth reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational update relevant primarily to NSE members and distributors handling mutual fund transactions. It does not affect listed equity trading, market structure, or broader compliance obligations, and has no direct impact on stock prices or trading activity.
