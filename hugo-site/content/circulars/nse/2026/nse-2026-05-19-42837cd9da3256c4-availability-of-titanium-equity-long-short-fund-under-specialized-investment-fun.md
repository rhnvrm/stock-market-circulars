## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) offered by TATA Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective May 20, 2026. Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities will also be available.

## Key Points

- Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund (TATA Mutual Fund) reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform from May 20, 2026.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are included.
- Six scheme variants are made available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notice regarding platform availability of an existing SIF product.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the availability of these schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform for onward facilitation to investors.

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in TATA Mutual Fund's Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The six scheme variants cover all major plan/option combinations (Regular/Direct × Growth/IDCW Reinvestment/IDCW Payout), providing full investor choice.
