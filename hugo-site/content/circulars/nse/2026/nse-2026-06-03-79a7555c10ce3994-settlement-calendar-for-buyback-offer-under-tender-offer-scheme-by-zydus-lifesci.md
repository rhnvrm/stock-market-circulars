## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Buyback Offer under Tender Offer scheme by Zydus Lifesciences Limited. This circular (ref. NCL/CMPT/74526) follows the earlier circulars NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74522 dated June 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X with Settlement No. 2026063 has been designated for this buyback offer.
- Trade window opens on June 4, 2026 and closes on June 10, 2026.
- Obligation download date is June 16, 2026 and settlement date is June 17, 2026.
- Demat shares must be tendered via Early Pay-in of Shares through depository mechanisms under Market Type Buyback.
- Early pay-in of shares on the trade end date must be completed well in advance of 3:30 PM for order acceptance.
- Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day.
- The Clearing Corporation may revise the settlement schedule in case of delays in RTA acceptance details or funds pay-in.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the existing buyback tender offer framework under the previously issued circulars NCL/CMPT/73996 and NSE/CMTR/74522.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must tender demat shares only through the Early Pay-in mechanism of depositories.
- Early pay-in of shares on the trade end date (June 10, 2026) must be completed before 3:30 PM.
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur only after receipt of funds pay-in on settlement day.
- Members will receive a Tender Offer Acceptance Report detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders with rejection reason codes.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | June 4, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | June 10, 2026 |
| Obligation Download Date | June 16, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 17, 2026 |
| Early Pay-in Deadline (Trade End Day) | 3:30 PM on June 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a focused operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Zydus Lifesciences buyback tender offer. Participants must ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements and the specified settlement timeline. Rejection codes (01–20) are provided in Annexure I and cover scenarios such as signature mismatches, invalid transfer deeds, shares not eligible as on record date, demat account/name mismatch, and excess shares tendered beyond eligibility, among others. The Clearing Corporation retains discretion to revise dates in case of operational delays.
