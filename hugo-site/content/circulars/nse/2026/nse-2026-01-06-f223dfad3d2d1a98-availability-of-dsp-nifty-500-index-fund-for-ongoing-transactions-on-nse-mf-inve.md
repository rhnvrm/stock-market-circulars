## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that the DSP Nifty 500 Index Fund from DSP Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective January 07, 2026. This includes subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP).

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty 500 Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from January 07, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity
- Facilities include: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed of the availability for facilitating client transactions through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 06, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 07, 2026 - DSP Nifty 500 Index Fund becomes available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement affecting only investors interested in the DSP Nifty 500 Index Fund.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Platform members can resume offering this fund to clients for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment plans.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking exposure to Nifty 500 Index through DSP Mutual Fund, as they can now make ongoing investments and redemptions through the NSE platform.
