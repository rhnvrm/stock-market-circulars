## Summary

NSE has issued a revision to its earlier circular dated January 09, 2026 regarding Shankara Buildpro Limited's transfer from Trade for Trade segment to rolling segment. The only change is the effective date, which has been postponed from January 23, 2026 to January 27, 2026. All other terms of the original circular remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- This circular revises NSE Circular Ref. No. NSE/CML/72229 dated January 09, 2026
- Transfer date from BE series (Trade for Trade) to EQ series (rolling segment) revised
- New effective date: January 27, 2026
- Previous date: January 23, 2026
- All other conditions remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a date revision for an existing measure.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the revised date for segment transfer and adjust their trading operations accordingly for Shankara Buildpro Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 12, 2026
- **Original Transfer Date:** January 23, 2026 (superseded)
- **Revised Transfer Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Reference Circular Date:** January 09, 2026 (NSE/CML/72229)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative revision extending the Trade for Trade period for Shankara Buildpro Limited by 4 trading days. Traders and investors dealing in this stock will have the stock remain in the BE series (Trade for Trade segment) for a slightly longer period before moving to the EQ series (normal rolling settlement). The extension provides additional time before normal trading resumes.
