## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) has notified the suspension of trading in equity shares of Auri Grow India Limited (AURIGROW) effective July 08, 2026. The action was taken under Chapter IV of the Exchange's Byelaws pertaining to dealings in securities, following the company's non-availability at its registered office and its failure to respond to show cause notices issued by the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Trading in AURIGROW shares will be suspended effective July 08, 2026
- The Company was not found to be available at its registered office
- The Company failed to provide a response to show cause notices issued by NSE
- ISIN of the company: INE925Y01036
- Action taken under Chapter IV of the Byelaws of the Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this circular applies existing Byelaw provisions (Chapter IV) governing suspension of trading for non-compliant listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

All Members of the Exchange must cease trading in AURIGROW shares from the effective date. Members should update their systems and inform clients holding positions in this security about the suspension.

## Important Dates

- **July 06, 2026**: Circular issued (Ref. No. NSE/CML/75070, Circular Ref. No. 1094/2026)
- **July 08, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension

## Impact Assessment

The suspension directly affects shareholders and traders of Auri Grow India Limited, who will be unable to buy or sell the stock on the Exchange from July 08, 2026. The company's non-responsiveness to regulatory notices signals potential governance or compliance issues, which could further affect investor confidence. The broader market impact is limited given the company's small size, but it serves as a compliance signal for other listed entities regarding the consequences of registered office unavailability and non-response to Exchange notices.
