## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, placing four new securities under ESM Stage I and escalating one security from Stage I to Stage II, effective June 03–04, 2026. Affected securities will attract a minimum 100% margin and be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST).

## Key Points

- Four securities newly included in ESM Stage I: BAWEJA (Baweja Studios), BIRLACABLE (Birla Cable), QUICKHEAL (Quick Heal Technologies), and UHTL (United Heat Transfer).
- SOFTTECH (Softtech Engineers Limited) escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II.
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage I; no securities excluded from ESM this cycle.
- Stage II securities placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing exchange surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely a surveillance action and does not imply adverse findings against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities newly entering ESM Stage I will shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective June 04, 2026.
- SOFTTECH's escalation to Stage II triggers a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction effective June 03, 2026.
- Minimum 100% margin applicable on all open positions as of June 03, 2026, and on new positions from June 04, 2026.
- Circular references prior ESM circulars: NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must ensure 100% margin collection on positions in affected securities from the specified effective dates.
- Members must update their systems to reflect the segment reclassification (EQ/SM → BE/ST) for the four Stage I entrants.
- Members dealing in SOFTTECH must apply the 2% Periodic Call Auction price band from June 03, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: 100% margin applicable on all open positions in newly included ESM securities; SOFTTECH moves to Stage II with 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- **June 04, 2026**: Segment shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) for BAWEJA, BIRLACABLE, QUICKHEAL, and UHTL; 100% margin on all new positions in ESM securities.

## Impact Assessment

- **BAWEJA, BIRLACABLE, QUICKHEAL, UHTL**: Shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and intraday squaring off, reducing liquidity. Mandatory 100% margin significantly increases the cost of holding positions, likely leading to reduced trading volumes and increased volatility around the effective dates.
- **SOFTTECH**: Escalation to Stage II with 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction is a more restrictive measure than Stage I, severely limiting price discovery and liquidity for this security.
- No securities were removed from ESM this cycle, maintaining or expanding the restricted list.
- Market participants holding positions in these securities should review margin adequacy and risk exposure ahead of the June 03–04, 2026 effective dates.
