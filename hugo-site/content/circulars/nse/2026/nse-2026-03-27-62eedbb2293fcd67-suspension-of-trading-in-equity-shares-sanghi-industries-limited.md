## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying the suspension of trading in equity shares of Sanghi Industries Limited (Symbol: SANGHIIND, Series: EQ) effective April 06, 2026. The suspension is triggered by a Scheme of Amalgamation. The last day of trading will be during closing hours of April 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Sanghi Industries Limited (SANGHIIND, EQ series) will be suspended from trading on NSE effective April 06, 2026
- The last trading session will be on April 02, 2026 (closing hours)
- Reason for suspension: Scheme of Amalgamation
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73501, Circular Ref. No: 0535/2026

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, the exchange is exercising its authority to suspend a security from trading upon the occurrence of a qualifying corporate event — in this case, a Scheme of Amalgamation involving Sanghi Industries Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in SANGHIIND (EQ series) from April 06, 2026 onwards
- Members should inform their clients holding or intending to trade in SANGHIIND equity shares about the impending suspension
- Any open positions or pending orders in SANGHIIND should be managed before closing hours on April 02, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 27, 2026
- **Last Trading Day:** April 02, 2026 (closing hours)
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension of SANGHIIND on the NSE will have a direct and significant impact on shareholders and active traders of Sanghi Industries Limited. Investors holding the stock must be aware that after April 02, 2026, the shares will no longer be tradeable on NSE. The amalgamation scheme underlying the suspension may result in share swaps or other corporate actions that will affect the value and form of existing holdings. Market participants are advised to review the terms of the amalgamation scheme for details on how their holdings will be treated post-suspension.
