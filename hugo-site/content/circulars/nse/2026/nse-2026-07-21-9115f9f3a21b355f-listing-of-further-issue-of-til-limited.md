## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of TIL Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 22, 2026. The issue relates to conversion of 97,39,346 partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares under the symbol TIL, series EQ, ISIN INE806C01018, with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- TIL Limited's converted shares will be admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from July 22, 2026.
- Security description: conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares.
- Number of securities: 97,39,346; Face value: Re. 10; Paid-up value: Re. 10; Market lot: 1.
- The shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable.
- Distinctive numbers for the shares range from 70352057 to 80091402.
- Shares allotted in dematerialized mode are currently credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8806C01017) pending activation of the permanent ISIN, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. No new regulatory policy is introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify and trade the security using its designated codes and specified lot size (1) as per the Annexure.
- No specific action required from investors beyond awareness of the temporary ISIN arrangement until permanent ISIN activation.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 22, 2026.
- Circular issued: July 21, 2026 (Ref. No. NSE/CML/75313; Circular Ref. No. 1188/2026).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to TIL Limited shareholders and traders, as it reflects conversion of previously partly paid-up shares into fully paid-up tradable equity shares. This increases the fully paid-up float of TIL Limited but does not represent a new capital raise or corporate action affecting the broader market.
