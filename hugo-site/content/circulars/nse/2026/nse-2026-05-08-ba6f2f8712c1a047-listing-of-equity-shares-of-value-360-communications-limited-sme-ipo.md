## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Value 360 Communications Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform under the Capital Market segment, effective May 11, 2026. The shares will trade under the symbol VALUE360 in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment – Settlement Type 'W') initially, with a subsequent shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment – Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI circular guidelines.

## Key Points

- Symbol: VALUE360; ISIN: INE1E7Y01018
- Listed on NSE EMERGE SME Platform effective May 11, 2026
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price for current public issue: Rs. 98/- per share
- Total securities: 1,60,90,008 equity shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 16090008)
- Market lot and Market Maker minimum quantity: 1,200 equity shares
- Market Maker maximum permissible spread: within 10%
- Type of market: Continuous Market; shares are Pari Passu
- Market Maker: Aikyam Capital Private Limited, Mumbai
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Kfin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, and in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, and Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated codes (VALUE360) for trading purposes.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1,200 equity shares.
- The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips as per SEBI guidelines.
- Lock-in details apply as per Annexure I of the circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: May 08, 2026
- Effective date for trading on NSE EMERGE SME Platform: May 11, 2026
- Financial year of the company: April 2026 – March 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides market access to Value 360 Communications Limited shares for retail and institutional investors via the NSE EMERGE SME Platform. Initial trading in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series ST) limits speculative activity and ensures delivery-based settlement. The market maker (Aikyam Capital Private Limited) is mandated to provide liquidity within a 10% spread. Impact is limited to participants interested in this SME scrip; no broader market-wide implications are anticipated.
