## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund, offered under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) structure by 360 Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP and STP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- The fund is managed under 360 Mutual Fund SIF as a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) product
- Reopening covers subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), and STP (Systematic Transfer Plan)
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All six variants are categorized as Debt schemes
- Transactions will be available on the NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational circular notifying the availability of an existing SIF fund for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are required to take note of the fund availability and communicate the same to relevant clients interested in transacting in the DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **March 30, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, and STP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited market impact, applicable only to investors and members participating in the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides liquidity and ongoing transaction access for investors in the DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund. The six scheme variants (ISINs: INF579M30075, INF579M30083, INF579M30091, INF579M30109, INF579M30117, INF579M30125) under both Regular and Direct plans offer flexibility to investors. As a Debt-categorized SIF product, impact is confined to participants in this niche fund category.
