## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the HDFC Nifty Auto Index Fund NFO, launched by HDFC Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- The circular pertains to the NMF TM Segment (NSE Mutual Fund platform for members).
- Two scheme options are covered: HDFC Nifty Auto Index Fund Regular Growth and HDFC Nifty Auto Index Fund Direct Growth.
- Both schemes fall under the Equity category and track the Nifty Auto Index.
- ISIN for Regular Growth plan: INF179KC1KP2.
- ISIN for Direct Growth plan: INF179KC1KQ0.
- Ongoing transaction facilities enabled include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an operational notification enabling continuous transaction facilities for an already-launched NFO scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members dealing in mutual fund transactions must ensure their systems and client-facing platforms recognize the scheme ISINs and enable subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 13, 2026 — schemes reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Circular Issued: July 10, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular facilitating investor access to an existing NFO scheme through NSE's mutual fund distribution platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, market structure, or impose new compliance burdens beyond standard platform enablement for member firms.
