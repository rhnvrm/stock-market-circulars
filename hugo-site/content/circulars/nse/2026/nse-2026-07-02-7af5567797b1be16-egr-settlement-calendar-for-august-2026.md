## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has notified the settlement calendar for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment for August 2026, pursuant to Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997). The circular provides separate schedules for EGR Normal T+1 settlement (Type '6') and EGR Auction settlement (Type '7').

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 238/2026; Download Ref No: NCL/EGR/75017, dated July 02, 2026
- Applies to all Clearing Members trading in the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment
- Two annexures provided: Annexure 'A' for Normal T+1 settlement (Type 6) and Annexure 'B' for Auction settlement (Type 7)
- 21 settlement cycles listed for Normal (Type 6) settlement covering 03-Aug-26 to 31-Aug-26 trade dates
- 21 settlement cycles listed for Auction (Type 7) settlement covering 04-Aug-26 to 01-Sep-26 trade dates

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine periodic notification under Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997) and does not introduce new regulatory requirements; it simply publishes the settlement schedule for the upcoming month.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing Members must align their back-office, funds, and securities pay-in/pay-out processes with the published settlement and trade dates for both Normal (T+1) and Auction settlement types in the EGR segment for August 2026.
