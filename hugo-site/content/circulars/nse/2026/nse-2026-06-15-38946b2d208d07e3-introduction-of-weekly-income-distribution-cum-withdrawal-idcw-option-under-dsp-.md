## Summary

NSE has communicated that DSP Mutual Fund, with the approval of DSP Trustee Private Limited, has introduced a Weekly Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) option under DSP Savings Fund. The new option is effective from June 15, 2026, and is available on the NSE MF Invest Platform for physical mode transactions only. Both the Regular Plan and Direct Plan will offer this new weekly IDCW frequency alongside existing frequencies.

## Key Points

- Weekly IDCW option introduced under DSP Savings Fund for both Regular Plan and Direct Plan
- Record date is every Wednesday; if it falls on a non-Business Day, the immediate next Business Day applies
- Units available at ₹10/- per unit on the effective date (June 15, 2026); NAV-based pricing on subsequent Business Days
- Minimum IDCW payout threshold is ₹10,000 (gross, including statutory deductions); amounts below this are compulsorily reinvested at ex-IDCW NAV regardless of investor's payout/reinvestment preference
- Default option of the scheme remains unchanged
- Available only in physical mode on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

The Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of DSP Savings Fund have been amended to include the Weekly IDCW option. The addendum overrides any conflicting provisions in the existing SID/KIM. All other provisions remain unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are required to take note of the availability of the new Weekly IDCW schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform
- Unitholders are advised to update their PAN, KYC details, email address, mobile number, and nominee details with the AMC
- Unitholders must link their PAN with Aadhaar Number
- Investors can check for unclaimed redemptions, IDCW payments, or inactive folios on the DSP Mutual Fund website

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for the introduction of the Weekly IDCW option; subscription available at ₹10/- per unit
- **June 08, 2026**: Date of DSP AMC's notice-cum-addendum
- **Every Wednesday**: Recurring record date for Weekly IDCW (next Business Day if Wednesday is a non-Business Day)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product enhancement adding a new distribution frequency to an existing debt scheme (DSP Savings Fund). The impact is limited to current and prospective investors in this fund seeking a weekly income distribution option. Two new ISINs are now active on NSE MF Invest Platform:

- DSP Savings Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Weekly: INF740KA1ZB2
- DSP Savings Fund - Direct Plan IDCW Weekly: INF740KA1ZD8

The physical-mode-only restriction on NSE MF Invest Platform limits accessibility. The ₹10,000 minimum payout threshold for cash distribution means smaller investors will have IDCW amounts automatically reinvested. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
