## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in units of four plans/options of Aditya Birla Sun Life Fixed Term Plan - Series TI (1837 days), effective July 8, 2026, on account of redemption of the scheme upon maturity, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading will be suspended in four schemes/plans of Aditya Birla Sun Life FTP - Series TI (1837 days)
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the fixed term plan on maturity
- Suspension date: July 8, 2026
- Last day of trading (closing hours): July 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A governing suspension of trading in securities/units upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in the specified units from the suspension date
- Members holding positions in these units should settle or close out positions before the last trading day (July 7, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **July 7, 2026**: Last day (closing hours) of trading in all four units
- **July 8, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective; units redeemed

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and trading members holding units of these four specific plans of Aditya Birla Sun Life FTP - Series TI (1837 days). As this is a scheduled maturity-driven redemption of a closed-end fixed term plan, it is a routine administrative action with no impact on the broader market or other securities.
