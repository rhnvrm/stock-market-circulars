## Summary

NSE has listed nine series of Secured, Rated, Listed, Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by IIFL Finance Limited under a public issue. All nine tranches were allotted on March 06, 2026, with an issue price and nominal value of Rs. 1,000 each. The debentures carry coupon rates from 8.37% to 9.00% (for interest-bearing series) or are cumulative (for Series VII–IX), with redemption dates ranging from March 06, 2028 to March 06, 2031. The securities are rated CRISIL AA/Stable.

## Key Points

- Nine series of NCDs listed on NSE's debt market segment, all allotted on March 06, 2026.
- Total securities issued across all series: approximately 1,02,16,391 debentures.
- Issue price, nominal value, and paid-up value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture for all series.
- Coupon rates: 8.37% (Series I), 8.52% (Series II), 8.65% (Series III), 8.70% (Series IV), 8.85% (Series V), 9.00% (Series VI); Series VII–IX are cumulative.
- Coupon frequency: Monthly (Series I–III), Annual (Series IV–VI), Cumulative at maturity (Series VII–IX).
- Redemption dates: March 06, 2028 (Series I, IV, VII), March 06, 2029 (Series II, V, VIII), March 06, 2031 (Series III, VI, IX).
- Redemption amounts: Rs. 1,000 for Series I–VI; Rs. 1,181.85 (Series VII), Rs. 1,290.00 (Series VIII), Rs. 1,539.00 (Series IX).
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA/Stable (as per Annexure I).
- No put/call options on any series.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under NSE's debt market segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- IIFL Finance Limited must comply with NSE's ongoing listing obligations for listed debt securities.
- Interest/coupon payments must be made on scheduled dates as specified for each series.
- Redemption must be executed on the respective maturity dates per the terms disclosed.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 06, 2026 (all series)
- **Interest Payment Start (Series I–III):** April 01, 2026 (monthly thereafter)
- **Annual Interest Payment (Series IV–VI):** Every year on March 06 until maturity
- **Redemption Dates:**
  - Series I, IV, VII: March 06, 2028
  - Series II, V, VIII: March 06, 2029
  - Series III, VI, IX: March 06, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides fixed-income investors on NSE access to nine tranches of IIFL Finance NCDs with varying tenors (2, 3, and 5 years) and payment structures (monthly, annual, cumulative). The CRISIL AA rating signals strong credit quality. The issuance of over 1 crore debentures at Rs. 1,000 each represents a substantial capital raise for IIFL Finance. Impact is primarily on debt market participants and IIFL Finance investors; no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
