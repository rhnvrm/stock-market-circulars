## Summary

NSE has informed all members that it has approved the segmental surrender of membership by Shareway Securities Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000208134), specifically for the Interest Rate Futures segment, with the surrender approved on July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Member: Shareway Securities Private Limited
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000208134
- Segment surrendered: Interest Rate Futures
- Surrender approval date: July 22, 2026
- All members are requested to take note of this development

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative approval of a single member's request to surrender its Interest Rate Futures trading segment registration.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from other members beyond noting the change. Shareway Securities Private Limited will no longer be authorized to operate in the Interest Rate Futures segment following the surrender.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Surrender approval date
- July 24, 2026: Circular issued (Circular Ref. No. 68/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/COMP/75364)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the membership status of a single trading member in one segment. It does not affect listed securities, trading operations of other members, or broader market functioning.
