## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the list of securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework, effective July 8, 2026. Elgi Rubber Company Limited (ELGIRUBCO, ISIN: INE819L01012) has been shortlisted to move from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II. No securities were newly added to Stage I or Stage IV, and no securities moved from Stage II to Stage III, Stage I to Stage IV, or Stage II to Stage IV in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Elgi Rubber Company Limited (ELGIRUBCO) moves from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II effective July 8, 2026.
- No new securities added to Stage I or Stage IV in this circular.
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage III, Stage I to Stage IV, or Stage II to Stage IV.
- Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) are shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE); not applicable to any security in this circular.
- ASM framework operates alongside other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and not an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the applicability of the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure framework as per prior circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, NSE/SURV/64066). ELGIRUBCO transitions to Stage II of the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply a 100% margin rate effective July 10, 2026 on all open positions as of July 9, 2026 and on new positions created from July 10, 2026 onwards for securities under the applicable ASM stage. Members should ensure client communication and risk management systems reflect the updated surveillance status of ELGIRUBCO.

## Important Dates

- July 8, 2026: Effective date for stage movement of shortlisted securities.
- July 9, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margin.
- July 10, 2026: 100% margin rate becomes applicable on open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is concentrated on ELGIRUBCO, whose trading will now attract a 100% margin requirement under Stage II of the Long-Term ASM framework, likely increasing the cost of trading and reducing speculative activity in the stock. Broader market impact is limited since no other securities were added or escalated in this cycle.
