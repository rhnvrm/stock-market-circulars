## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members of an ISIN change for Deepak Builders & Engineers India Limited (symbol: DBEIL). The change is a result of a sub-division of the equity share face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- (a 10:1 stock split). The new ISIN INE0OPA01027 becomes effective from the ex-date, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: DBEIL (Deepak Builders & Engineers India Limited)
- Old ISIN replaced by new ISIN: INE0OPA01027
- Reason: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- The new ISIN applies to all trades executed on and after the ex-date
- Circular issued by NSE Department of Listing (Ref No: NSE/CML/74738, Circular Ref: 0975/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN code for DBEIL equity shares is being changed in accordance with the corporate action (stock sub-division). All references to the security in trading and settlement systems must reflect the new ISIN from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their systems, records, and client communications to use the new ISIN INE0OPA01027 for DBEIL effective June 19, 2026.
- Trades placed on or after the ex-date must reference the new ISIN only.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Ex-date / Effective Date:** June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change has a moderate operational impact on brokers and members trading DBEIL. Settlement systems, back-office software, and depository records must be updated to reflect the new ISIN before the ex-date. Failure to update could lead to settlement mismatches. The face value sub-division (10:1 split) will proportionally increase the number of shares outstanding while reducing the per-share face value, with no change in the overall market capitalisation at the time of the split.
