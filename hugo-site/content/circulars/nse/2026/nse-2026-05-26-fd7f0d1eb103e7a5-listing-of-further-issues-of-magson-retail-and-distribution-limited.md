## Summary

NSE has admitted 603,286 additional equity shares of Magson Retail and Distribution Limited to dealings on the Capital Market (SME Emerge) segment effective May 27, 2026. The shares were allotted on March 30, 2026, pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 93.25 per share.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** MAGSON | **Series:** SM (SME Emerge)
- **ISIN:** INE0O1S01012 (shares initially credited under temporary ISIN in dematerialised form per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012)
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 93.25 per share; Face Value / Paid-up Value: Rs. 10
- **Shares Listed:** 603,286 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 12,325,169 to 12,928,454
- **Market Lot:** 1,000 shares
- **Pari Passu:** Yes — rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** March 30, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is effected under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security exclusively by its designated code (MAGSON / SM) on the trading system.
- All trades must be executed in the specified market lot of 1,000 shares.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specified tranches; members and clients should verify lock-in status before transacting in restricted shares.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine incremental listing for an SME Emerge-listed company. The 603,286 new shares represent a warrant-conversion allotment at Rs. 93.25, adding to the existing float of MAGSON. The majority of newly listed shares (320,286 shares, ~53%) are locked in until December 2027, limiting near-term selling pressure. The remaining ~283,000 shares have staggered lock-in expiries through late 2026. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the SME segment's lower liquidity and the phased lock-in structure.
