## Summary

NSE has updated the list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Vital Chemtech Limited (VITAL) has been shortlisted to move from ESM Stage I to Stage II, attracting stricter surveillance and a minimum 100% margin on open and new positions. Conversely, five securities — CL Educate Limited (CLEDUCATE), DCM Financial Services Limited (DCMFINSERV), Excellent Wires and Packaging Limited (EXCELLENT), FCS Software Solutions Limited (FCSSOFT), and Softtech Engineers Limited (SOFTTECH) — have been moved down from Stage II to Stage I, indicating a relative easing of surveillance restrictions. No securities are newly added to Stage I or excluded from the ESM framework entirely in this circular.

## Key Points

- VITAL (Vital Chemtech Limited, ISIN INE0L4K01016) moves from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective July 13, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II are placed under Trade for Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 13, 2026.
- Five securities move down from Stage II to Stage I: CLEDUCATE, DCMFINSERV, EXCELLENT, FCSSOFT, and SOFTTECH.
- Securities under ESM (Series: EQ/SM) are shifted to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) w.e.f. July 14, 2026.
- A minimum 100% margin applies on all open positions as on July 13, 2026, and new positions created from July 14, 2026, for securities newly qualifying under the measure.
- ESM classification is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.
- The framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures (e.g., ASM, GSM).

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is an update under NSE's existing ESM framework (originally circulated via NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic reclassification of securities within the existing ESM stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a minimum 100% margin on VITAL for all open positions as on July 13, 2026, and new positions from July 14, 2026.
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded in the correct segment (Trade-for-Trade, Series BE/ST) as applicable.
- Price bands must be adjusted per each security's new stage classification; securities moving out of a stricter framework revert to their prior applicable price band, unless subject to another surveillance measure.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Stage transitions effective (VITAL to Stage II; five securities to Stage I); 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction begins for Stage II entrants.
- July 14, 2026: Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST) shift effective; 100% margin applies to open positions as on July 13, 2026, and new positions from this date.

## Impact Assessment

VITAL faces materially tighter trading conditions — 100% margin, Trade-for-Trade settlement, and a restrictive 2% price band — likely reducing liquidity and increasing cost of trading in the stock. The five securities moved from Stage II to Stage I see a relative easing of restrictions, which may modestly improve trading conditions and investor sentiment, though they remain under surveillance. Overall market impact is contained to these specific stocks rather than broader market structure.
