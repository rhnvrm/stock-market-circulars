## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF (Fund of Fund) by Edelweiss Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 06, 2026. Both Direct Plan and Regular Plan (Growth option) will be available, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 06, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Both Direct Plan (ISIN: INF754K01WP4) and Regular Plan (ISIN: INF754K01WQ2) - Growth options are included
- Subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will all be available
- Both schemes are categorized under Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the availability of these schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 06, 2026 — Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF available for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This notice is relevant primarily to distributors and investors using the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to transact in Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF. The reopening restores normal transaction access for the fund, enabling investors to subscribe or redeem units and set up systematic plans.
