## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for 18 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective March 27, 2026. The securities include shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and preferential allotment basis across companies ranging from large-cap banks and pharma to mid-cap financials.

## Key Points

- 18 companies are having additional securities listed on NSE effective March 27, 2026
- Securities issued under ESOP allotments include: Aadhar Housing Finance (867,247 shares), Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands (12,140 shares), AU Small Finance Bank (42,604 shares)
- Aeroflex Industries issued 3,010,398 equity shares on preferential basis at Rs. 182.7 per share (allotted February 3, 2026)
- Aeroflex preferential shares carry partial lock-in expiring September 30, 2026
- All securities are listed in the EQ series with market lot size of 1
- All further issues are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only the designated security codes for trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- Lock-in restrictions on Aeroflex Industries preferential allotment shares must be observed until September 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **March 25, 2026** – Circular issued
- **March 27, 2026** – Effective date; all listed securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- **September 30, 2026** – Lock-in expiry for Aeroflex Industries preferential allotment shares
- Notable allotment dates: Aadhar Housing Finance (March 20, 2026), AU Small Finance Bank (March 21, 2026), Aeroflex Industries (February 3, 2026), Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands (March 4, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification. The share additions represent incremental dilution for existing shareholders, primarily from ESOP vesting across multiple companies. The most notable issue is Aeroflex Industries' preferential allotment of ~3 million shares at Rs. 182.7, which constitutes a meaningful capital raise. Large-cap names (ICICI Bank, HDFC Life, ITC, Dr. Reddy's) likely have small ESOP tranches with negligible dilution impact. Market impact is expected to be minimal for most companies given the small quantities relative to their total outstanding shares.
