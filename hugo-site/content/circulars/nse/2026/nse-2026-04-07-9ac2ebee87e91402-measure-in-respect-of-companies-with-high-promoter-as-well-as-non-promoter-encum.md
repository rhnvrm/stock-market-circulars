## Summary

NSE has updated the surveillance measure applicable to companies with high Promoter and non-Promoter encumbrance under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011. Vishnu Prakash R Punglia Limited (VPRPL) has been added to the framework effective April 10, 2026, attracting a minimum 75% margin requirement in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments. No securities are being removed in this update.

## Key Points

- Vishnu Prakash R Punglia Limited (VPRPL, ISIN: INE0AE001013) is newly included under the encumbrance surveillance measure
- A minimum 75% margin will be applicable in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments for VPRPL
- The 75% margin applies to all open positions as on April 09, 2026 and new positions created from April 10, 2026
- No securities are being excluded from the framework in this update (Annexure II: Nil)
- The consolidated list now contains 8 securities under this framework
- This measure operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under this measure should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in continuation of NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. The update reflects a periodic review of securities satisfying the inclusion criteria under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, which governs disclosures related to encumbrance of shares by promoters and non-promoters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure a minimum 75% margin is collected for VPRPL positions in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments
- Members must apply margin requirements to all open positions in VPRPL as on April 09, 2026
- Members must apply margin requirements to all new VPRPL positions created from April 10, 2026 onwards
- For queries, members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **April 07, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 08, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of VPRPL under the framework (Annexure I reference)
- **April 09, 2026**: Cutoff date for identifying open positions subject to 75% margin
- **April 10, 2026**: 75% margin requirement becomes effective for VPRPL in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments
