## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No. 0645/2026, Download Ref. No. NSE/CML/73797) notifies that 19 companies' securities have been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange Capital Market segment effective April 20, 2026. The circular specifies designated security codes and lot sizes for each security under Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 19 securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from April 20, 2026
- Each security assigned a designated symbol and ISIN code for system identification
- Trading must use designated codes; lot sizes as specified in the Annexure
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. The circular is issued in pursuance of existing NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size specification.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use only the designated security codes when trading these securities
- Trading in each security must conform to the lot sizes specified in the Annexure
- Members should update their systems to reflect new listings effective April 20, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 20, 2026 (all 19 securities admitted to dealings)
