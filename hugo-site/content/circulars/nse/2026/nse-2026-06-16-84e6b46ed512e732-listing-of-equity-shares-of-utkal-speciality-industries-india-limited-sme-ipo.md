## Summary

Utkal Speciality Industries India Limited has listed its equity shares on NSE following completion of its SME IPO. The shareholding pattern has been filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as on 16 June 2026 (post-issue of IPO).

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Utkal Speciality Industries India Limited listed on NSE as an SME IPO.
- Shareholding pattern filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015, effective 16 June 2026 (post-IPO).
- Promoter and Promoter Group shares are subject to lock-in; public and non-promoter/non-public shares are not locked in.
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or outstanding ESOPs have been issued.
- No shares held against depository receipts.
- No pledged or encumbered promoter shares (including NDU or other encumbrances).
- No equity shares with differential voting rights.
- No significant beneficial owners identified.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Compliance is pursuant to existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, Regulation 31.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity has submitted the shareholding pattern declaration as required under Reg. 31 of SEBI LODR.
- Lock-in applicable to Promoter and Promoter Group shares post-IPO as per applicable SEBI norms.
- All declarations regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, pledge, and differential voting rights have been filed as 'No'.

## Important Dates

- **16 June 2026**: Date of listing and shareholding pattern filing (post-issue of IPO).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing on NSE. The listing provides liquidity to investors who participated in the IPO and marks the commencement of trading in Utkal Speciality Industries India Limited equity shares. Promoter lock-in ensures promoter shareholding stability in the near term. Impact is contained to the company's investors and does not affect broader market operations.
