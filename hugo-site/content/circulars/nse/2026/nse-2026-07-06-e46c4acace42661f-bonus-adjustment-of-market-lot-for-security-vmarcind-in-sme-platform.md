## Summary

NSE has announced an adjustment to the market lot for V Marc India Limited (VMARCIND), listed on the SME platform, following a corporate action involving a bonus issue. The revised market lot takes effect from July 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: VMARCIND (V Marc India Limited), SME Platform
- Corporate action type: Bonus issue
- Bonus ratio: 5:1
- Adjustment factor: 6
- Adjusted market lot: 1500 shares
- Ex-date/Effective date: July 07, 2026
- Record date: July 07, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine operational adjustment to the market lot resulting from a corporate action (bonus issue) on an SME-listed security.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must update their trading systems with the revised security master files (security.gz, nnf_security.gz, or MII - Security File.gz) before trading begins on the effective date. These files are available via the common/NTNEAT directory on the Extranet server or from the NSE website (https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports).

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 07, 2026
- Ex-date/Effective Date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This adjustment affects trading members dealing in VMARCIND on the SME platform, who must ensure their systems reflect the new market lot of 1500 shares prior to the effective date to avoid order placement errors. The impact is limited to this single SME security and does not affect the broader market.
