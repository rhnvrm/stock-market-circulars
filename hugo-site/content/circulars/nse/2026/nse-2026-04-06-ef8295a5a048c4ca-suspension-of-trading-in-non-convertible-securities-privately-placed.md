## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited (ISIN: INE148I07EL8), effective April 7, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of these securities under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading in Sammaan Capital Limited's NCS (ISIN: INE148I07EL8) will be suspended from April 7, 2026
- Suspension reason: Redemption of the securities
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Applies to all NSE members trading in the debt market segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs trading suspensions.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in ISIN INE148I07EL8 (Sammaan Capital Limited) from April 7, 2026 onwards
- No further orders should be placed or executed for this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 6, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a limited and targeted market impact, affecting only holders and traders of Sammaan Capital Limited's specific privately placed NCS (INE148I07EL8). As the suspension is due to redemption, investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds per the terms of the instrument. No broader debt market disruption is expected.
