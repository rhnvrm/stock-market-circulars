## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 privately placed commercial paper securities to dealings on the debt market segment with effect from February 10, 2026. The securities are from Tata Capital Limited, Aditya Birla Money Limited, Blue Star Limited, 5Paisa Capital Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), IIFL Finance Limited, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Muthoot Finance Limited (2 securities).

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities admitted to debt market segment
- Allotment date: February 09, 2026 for all securities
- Effective trading date: February 10, 2026
- Face value: â‚¹5,00,000 for all securities
- Maturity periods range from 28 days to 365 days
- Total quantity: 47,200 securities across all issuances
- All securities issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations

## Securities Details

**Short-term (28-91 days):**
- Blue Star Limited: 2,000 securities, redemption March 09, 2026 (28 days)
- 5Paisa Capital Limited: 500 securities, redemption March 31, 2026 (50 days)
- Aditya Birla Money Limited: 2,000 securities, redemption May 11, 2026 (91 days)
- SIDBI: 13,000 securities, redemption May 11, 2026 (91 days)
- IIFL Finance Limited: 4,000 securities, redemption May 11, 2026 (91 days)
- Motilal Oswal Financial Services: 1,200 securities, redemption May 11, 2026 (91 days)

**Long-term (365 days):**
- Tata Capital Limited: 10,000 securities, redemption February 09, 2027
- Godrej Industries Limited: 1,500 securities, redemption February 04, 2027
- Muthoot Finance Limited: 6,000 securities, redemption February 02, 2027
- Muthoot Finance Limited: 7,000 securities, redemption February 04, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members should note the designated security codes and ISINs for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** February 09, 2026 (all securities)
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** February 10, 2026
- **Redemption Dates:** Range from March 09, 2026 to February 09, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine commercial paper listings that provide additional debt instruments for trading in the debt market segment.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard listing procedure with designated security codes allocated for trading.

**Investor Impact:** Provides institutional investors access to short-term and medium-term debt instruments from diverse issuers across financial services, manufacturing, and development finance sectors.
