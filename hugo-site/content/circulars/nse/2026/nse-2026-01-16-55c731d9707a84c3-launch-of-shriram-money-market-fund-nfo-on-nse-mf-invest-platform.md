## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Shriram Money Market Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from January 19, 2026 to January 21, 2026 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for January 27, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan growth options will be available.

## Key Points

- Shriram Money Market Fund NFO launching on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two scheme options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01489) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01471)
- Subscription period: January 19-21, 2026 (until 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: January 27, 2026
- Category listed as Equity in circular (likely classification error for Money Market Fund)
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by January 27, 2026 at 10:00 AM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 27, 2026 at 10:00 AM
- Members should facilitate investor transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: NFO opens for subscription
- **January 21, 2026**: NFO closes at 11:59 PM
- **January 27, 2026**: Allotment date and payment deadline (10:00 AM)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine announcement for a new mutual fund offering. It provides investment opportunities in the money market segment for investors using the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members and distributors need to be aware of the subscription timeline and payment requirements to facilitate client transactions.
