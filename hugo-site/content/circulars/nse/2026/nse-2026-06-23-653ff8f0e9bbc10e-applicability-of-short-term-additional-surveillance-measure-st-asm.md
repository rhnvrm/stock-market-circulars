## Summary

NSE has issued Circular Ref. No. 470/2026 placing 10 securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective June 24, 2026. The enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 25, 2026 on both existing open positions (as on June 24, 2026) and new positions. No securities have been placed under ST-ASM Stage II or moved between stages.

## Key Points

- 10 securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I effective June 24, 2026
- No securities placed under ST-ASM Stage II
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to exit
- ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the companies

## Regulatory Changes

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I are subject to enhanced margin requirements under the ASM framework originally introduced via NSE/SURV/39265 (October 27, 2018) and subsequently updated through NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066.

## Compliance Requirements

- **ST-ASM Stage I**: Applicable margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- Margin applicable on all open positions as on June 24, 2026
- Margin applicable on all new positions created from June 25, 2026
- Members must ensure sufficient margins are collected from clients holding these securities

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 24, 2026**: Effective date for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion
- **June 25, 2026**: Date from which enhanced margin rates apply on open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The 10 securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I will face significantly increased margin requirements (minimum 50%, capped at 100%), raising the cost of holding and trading these stocks. This may lead to reduced liquidity and forced unwinding of leveraged positions in these securities.
