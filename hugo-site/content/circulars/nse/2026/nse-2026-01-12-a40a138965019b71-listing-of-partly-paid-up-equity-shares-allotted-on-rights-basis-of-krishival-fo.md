## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of partly paid-up equity shares of Krishival Foods Limited allotted on rights basis. The securities will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from January 14, 2026, under the symbol KRISHPP with a temporary ISIN.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Krishival Foods Limited
- **Symbol**: KRISHPP
- **Series**: E1
- **Temporary ISIN**: IN90GGO01013
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Paid-up Value**: Rs. 3.50 per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 300 (Rs. 105 paid on application)
- **Number of Securities**: 3,333,160 shares
- **Date of Allotment**: January 8, 2026
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 3,333,160
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Lock-in**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should note the new security code (KRISHPP) for trading purposes
- Trading in these securities shall be in the specified market lot size of 1
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 regarding activation of ISIN in case of additional issue of shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Listing Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Circular Effective Date**: January 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific company's rights issue. The listing of partly paid-up shares allows existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue to trade their allotted shares. Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new symbol and ISIN. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions, providing liquidity to rights issue subscribers.
