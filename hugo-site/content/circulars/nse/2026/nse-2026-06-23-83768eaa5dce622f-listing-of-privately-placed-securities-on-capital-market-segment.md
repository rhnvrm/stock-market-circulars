## Summary

NSE has admitted two fresh privately placed debt securities to dealings on its Capital Market segment with effect from June 24, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The securities are issued by Bajaj Auto Credit Limited and SMFG India Credit Company Limited.

## Key Points

- Two fresh issue privately placed NCDs listed effective June 24, 2026
- Both securities assigned Series N0 with a market lot size of 1
- Bajaj Auto Credit Limited NCD: 8.10% coupon, maturing June 19, 2029 (ISIN: INE18UV07020, Symbol: 810BACL29)
- SMFG India Credit Company Limited NCD (Sr 118): 8.15% coupon, maturing June 19, 2029 (ISIN: INE535H07CU3, Symbol: 815SICC29)
- Securities must be identified and traded only by their designated codes and specified lot sizes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A for admitting privately placed securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (810BACL29 and 815SICC29) exclusively for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit per lot
- Effective date compliance required from June 24, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date (Listing):** June 24, 2026
- **Maturity Date (Both Securities):** June 19, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational listing notification with minimal market impact. Both NCDs are privately placed instruments with a market lot of 1, targeting eligible investors in the debt segment. The coupon rates (8.10% and 8.15%) are in line with prevailing corporate debt market rates for 3-year tenors. No disruption to existing trading or settlement operations is expected.
