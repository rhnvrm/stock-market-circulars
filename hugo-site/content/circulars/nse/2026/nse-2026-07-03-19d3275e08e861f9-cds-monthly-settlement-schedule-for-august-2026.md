## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the Mark to Market (MTM) settlement schedule for the Currency Derivatives (CDS) segment for August 2026. The circular lists the trade date to settlement date mapping for all trading days in the month, along with special final settlement dates for specific contract types.

## Key Points

- Standard MTM settlement follows a T+1 cycle (settlement occurs one trading day after the trade date).
- The schedule covers all 20 trading days in August 2026, from 03-Aug-2026 to 31-Aug-2026.
- Four contract types have special final settlement dates marked with asterisks: 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures, Cash Settled Interest Rate Contracts, Currency Futures and Options Contracts, and Overnight Call Rate (MIBOR) Contracts.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational schedule circular, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members and trading members in the Currency Derivatives segment must align their funds and settlement processes with the published schedule to ensure timely MTM settlement obligations are met.

## Important Dates

- 27-Aug-2026: Final settlement for August 2026 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures
- 28-Aug-2026: Final settlement for August 2026 Cash Settled Interest Rate Contracts
- 31-Aug-2026: Final settlement for August 2026 Currency Futures and Options Contracts
- 01-Sep-2026: Final settlement for August 2026 Overnight Call Rate (MIBOR) Contracts

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is a routine administrative notification providing advance visibility into the settlement calendar for the Currency Derivatives segment. It helps clearing members and trading members plan liquidity and funds pay-in/pay-out for MTM settlements, with no effect on individual stocks or broader market sentiment.
