## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts for Bank of Baroda (symbol: BANKBARODA) in accordance with SEBI guidelines on corporate action adjustments. The adjustment is triggered by a dividend of Rs 8.50 per share (face value Rs 2/-), with the ex-date and effective date set for June 5, 2026. All 17 option strike prices for the June 30, 2026 expiry have been revised downward by Rs 8.50.

## Key Points

- Symbol: BANKBARODA (Bank of Baroda)
- Corporate action: Dividend of Rs 8.50/- per share (face value Rs 2/-)
- Ex-date / Effective date: June 5, 2026
- All affected contracts are OPTSTK instruments expiring June 30, 2026
- Strike prices revised downward by Rs 8.50 across 17 strikes (range: 220.00–265.00 old; 211.50–256.50 revised)
- Members must load updated contract.gz and spread files before trading on the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

Strike prices for 17 BANKBARODA option contracts (OPTSTK, expiry 30-JUN-2026) have been revised as follows (selected entries):

| Old Strike | Revised Strike |
|------------|----------------|
| 220.00 | 211.50 |
| 230.00 | 221.50 |
| 240.00 | 231.50 |
| 250.00 | 241.50 |
| 260.00 | 251.50 |
| 265.00 | 256.50 |

All strikes reduced uniformly by Rs 8.50. Full details available in Annexure 1 of the circular and at the NSE corporate actions adjustments page.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications **before trading on June 5, 2026**.
- Files are available from the `faoftp/faocommon` directory on the Extranet server and on the NSE website under all-reports-derivatives.
- Revised strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places; these will be rounded to the nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively.
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 5, 2026**: Ex-date and effective date for dividend adjustment; revised strike prices come into force
- **June 30, 2026**: Expiry date of affected option contracts

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard, SEBI-mandated adjustment to prevent economic distortion in F&O contracts caused by the dividend payout. Traders holding open positions in BANKBARODA options expiring June 30, 2026 will see their strike prices automatically revised downward by Rs 8.50. The adjustment is value-neutral in principle but requires members to update contract files to avoid trading errors. No new contracts are introduced or removed; only existing strike prices are revised.
