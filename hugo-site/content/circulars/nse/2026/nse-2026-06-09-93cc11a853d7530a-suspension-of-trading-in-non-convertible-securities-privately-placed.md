## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed) issued by PNB Housing Finance Limited, effective June 10, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the security with ISIN INE572E07100, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- PNB Housing Finance Limited's NCS (ISIN: INE572E07100) will be suspended from trading on NSE effective June 10, 2026.
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the security.
- Action is taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74616 | Circular Ref. No: 0920/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is executed under the existing framework of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which mandates suspension of trading upon redemption of privately placed non-convertible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in INE572E07100 from June 10, 2026 onwards.
- No further action required from members beyond acknowledging the suspension.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. The suspension affects a single privately placed NCS issued by PNB Housing Finance Limited. Redemption is a standard lifecycle event for debt instruments, and the suspension ensures no trading occurs post-redemption. Holders of this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
