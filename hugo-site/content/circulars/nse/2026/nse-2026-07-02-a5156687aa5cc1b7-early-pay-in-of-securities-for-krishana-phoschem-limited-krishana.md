## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued instructions for members, custodians, and PCMs regarding the process of early pay-in (EPI) of securities for Krishana Phoschem Limited (KRISHANA), in connection with a face value split (sub-division) of the stock from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share. The record date and ex-date for the corporate action are both July 3, 2026. Members undertaking early pay-in during the specified settlement window must use the existing/old ISIN (INE506W01012) without adjusting for the conversion ratio, before switching to the new ISIN after the record date.

## Key Points

- Circular relates to Item 10.18 'Early Pay-in of Securities for Margin Exemption' of NCL's consolidated circular (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/73996) dated April 30, 2026.
- Corporate action: Face Value Split (Sub-Division) from Rs 10 per share to Rs 2 per share for Krishana Phoschem Limited (KRISHANA).
- Existing/Old ISIN: INE506W01012.
- Early pay-in on July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123) and July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124) must be done in the Old/Existing ISIN.
- Example given: for a sale quantity of 10 shares (post-split), the early pay-in in the depository should be made for a quantity of 2 shares in the old ISIN (pre-split equivalent).
- Members must adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022, on SEBI's Block Mechanism for early pay-in of securities from client demat accounts; the client-wise early pay-in allocation file should not be uploaded when using block mechanism.
- Custodians providing early pay-in through the pool account must upload client-wise early pay-in allocation details based on actual traded quantity to pass on the benefit to clients.
- After the record date, market participants and custodians must execute pay-in/early pay-in instructions in the New ISIN.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this circular operationalizes the existing early pay-in process (per the April 30, 2026 consolidated circular) for the specific corporate action affecting Krishana Phoschem Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must execute early pay-in instructions in the Old ISIN for trades dated on/around the ex-date/record date, without adjusting quantities for the split ratio.
- Custodians using block mechanism must not upload client-wise early pay-in allocation files.
- Custodians using pool account early pay-in must upload accurate client-wise allocation details reflecting actual traded quantities.
- Post record date, all pay-in and early pay-in instructions must shift to the New ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex-Date: July 3, 2026
- Early Pay-in Date 1: July 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026123, Old ISIN)
- Early Pay-in Date 2: July 6, 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026123 & 2026124, Old ISIN)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular primarily impacting clearing members, custodians, and PCMs handling settlement logistics for Krishana Phoschem Limited around its face value split. Incorrect ISIN usage or quantity adjustment during the transition window could lead to settlement mismatches or shortages, making adherence to the prescribed old/new ISIN timeline important for smooth settlement. Direct market/price impact on the stock is minimal, as this addresses only the mechanics of security pay-in during the corporate action.
