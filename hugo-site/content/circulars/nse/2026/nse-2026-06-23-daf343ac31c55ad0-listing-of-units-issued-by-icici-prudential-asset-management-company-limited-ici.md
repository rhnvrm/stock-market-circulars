## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 4,968,883 units of ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF on the Capital Market segment, effective June 24, 2026. The units were allotted on June 19, 2026 at an issue price of Rs. 17.73 per unit and will trade under the symbol SMALLIETF with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** SMALLIETF
- **ISIN:** INF109K1A476
- **Units Listed:** 4,968,883
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 17.73 per unit
- **Face Value / Paid-Up Value:** Rs. 10 per unit
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Date of Allotment:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 24, 2026
- Eleven market makers have been designated, including ICICI Securities, HDFC Securities, Kotak Securities, Motilal Oswal, and Mirae Asset Capital Markets

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code SMALLIETF for all transactions on the NSE Capital Market segment.
- Trades must be executed in the specified lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers listed in the Annexure are expected to provide liquidity as per their obligations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 19, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine incremental listing of ETF units for an existing fund (ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF). The addition of 4,968,883 units expands the tradeable float. With eleven designated market makers, liquidity support is broad. Impact on the broader market is negligible; relevant only to investors tracking or trading this ETF and to participating market makers.
