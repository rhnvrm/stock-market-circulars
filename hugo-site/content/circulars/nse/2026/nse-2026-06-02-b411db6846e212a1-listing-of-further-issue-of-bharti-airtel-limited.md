## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 4,366 additional equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited (symbol: BHARTIARTL) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 03, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares, under permanent ISIN INE397D01024.

## Key Points

- 4,366 equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited admitted to trading on NSE Capital Market segment from June 03, 2026
- Series: EQ | Face Value: Rs. 5/- | Paid-up Value: Rs. 5/- | Market Lot: 1
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Permanent ISIN: INE397D01024; shares being migrated from temporary ISIN IN8397D01015
- Two tranches of distinctive numbers: 5748678797–5748682950 (4,154 shares) and 5883201890–5883202101 (212 shares)
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, governing admission of securities and lot size specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code BHARTIARTL and series EQ for trading these shares
- Trading shall be in lot sizes of 1 as specified
- Shares were previously held under temporary ISIN IN8397D01015 per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012); they are now activated under the permanent ISIN

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing involves a very small number of shares (4,366) relative to Bharti Airtel's total share capital, resulting in negligible dilution. This is a routine administrative action converting partly paid shares to fully paid status. No material impact on price, liquidity, or market structure is expected. Existing shareholders are unaffected, and the newly listed shares rank pari passu with all other equity shares.
