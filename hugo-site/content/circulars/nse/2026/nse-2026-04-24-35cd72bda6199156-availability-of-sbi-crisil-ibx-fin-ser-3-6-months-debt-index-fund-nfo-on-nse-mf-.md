## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the SBI CRISIL IBX Fin Ser 3-6 Months Debt Index Fund NFO by SBI Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 27, 2026. The platform will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities for this fund.

## Key Points

- SBI CRISIL IBX Fin Ser 3-6 Months Debt Index Fund NFO will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform from April 27, 2026
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled on the platform
- Fund category is Debt for all six scheme variants
- Issued under NSE circular reference NSE/NMFTM/73868 (Circular Ref. No: 1337/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform availability notification for an existing NFO.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of the fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform and facilitate client transactions accordingly from April 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a low market-wide impact. It is an administrative notice to NSE members about the addition of a new debt index fund NFO to the NSE MF Invest Platform. The six scheme variants (ISIN: INF200KB1AL6, INF200KB1AM4, INF200KB1AP7, INF200KB1AO0, INF200KB1AQ5, INF200KB1AN2) cover Regular and Direct plan options, providing flexibility for different investor preferences. Impact is limited to members and investors interested in short-duration debt index fund products from SBI Mutual Fund.
