## Summary

NSE has announced a change in the designated Market Maker for LAMOSAIC INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: LAMOSAIC), an SME-listed company on the Capital Market Segment. The existing market maker SVCM SECURITIES PVT LTD is being replaced by KALPALABDHI FINANCIALS PRIVATE LIMITED, effective April 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: LAMOSAIC
- Company: LAMOSAIC INDIA LIMITED
- Old Market Maker: SVCM SECURITIES PVT LTD (Member Code: 90216)
- New Market Maker: KALPALABDHI FINANCIALS PRIVATE LIMITED (Member Code: 90149)
- Effective Date: April 10, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Capital Market Segment (Ref No: NSE/CMTR/73653, Circular Ref. No: 46/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update to the market-making arrangement for an SME-listed security.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are advised to take note of the change in market maker for LAMOSAIC INDIA LIMITED. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond awareness of the updated market maker details.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 08, 2026
- Effective Date of New Market Maker: April 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change has minimal market impact. It is a routine operational update confined to the SME segment. The transition from SVCM SECURITIES PVT LTD to KALPALABDHI FINANCIALS PRIVATE LIMITED as market maker may affect liquidity provision for LAMOSAIC shares on a short-term basis, but such transitions are standard practice and closely monitored by NSE.
