## Summary

NSE has informed its members that SBI Mutual Fund's SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF NFO will be available to transact on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 07, 2026 to May 18, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The NFO offers two plan options — Direct Growth and Regular Growth — both categorized as equity schemes, with allotment scheduled for May 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF NFO opens on NSE MF Invest Platform from May 07, 2026 to May 18, 2026
- Two scheme variants: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF200KB1AY9) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF200KB1AY9)
- Both schemes are classified under the Equity category
- Allotment date for both schemes is May 20, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by May 19, 2026 at 3:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational circular announcing the availability of a new NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds (via any payment mode) are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 19, 2026 by 3:00 PM
- Members facilitating transactions in this NFO must adhere to the specified subscription window dates

## Important Dates

- **May 07, 2026**: NFO subscription window opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **May 18, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription window closes
- **May 19, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be received in the Clearing Corporation account
- **May 20, 2026**: Allotment date for both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plans

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions via the NSE MF Invest Platform. It introduces a new equity ETF tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 index by SBI Mutual Fund, expanding investment options for retail and institutional investors in the small-cap space. The operational impact is limited to ensuring timely fund collection and transaction processing within the NFO window. No significant market-wide impact is anticipated.
