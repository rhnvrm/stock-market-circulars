## Summary

NSE has notified all members of a change in the designated market maker for EMA Partners India Limited (Symbol: EMAPARTNER), an SME-listed company. Effective April 02, 2026, R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd. will replace Alacrity Securities Ltd. as the market maker.

## Key Points

- Company: EMA Partners India Limited (NSE Symbol: EMAPARTNER)
- Old Market Maker: Alacrity Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 09098)
- New Market Maker: R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd. (Member Code: 12310)
- Effective Date: April 02, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Capital Market Segment department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to market maker assignment under the SME market maker framework.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are advised to take note of the change in market maker for EMAPARTNER. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond awareness of the new market maker.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 01, 2026
- Effective Date of New Market Maker: April 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The change affects liquidity provision for EMAPARTNER on the SME platform. R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd. assumes market-making obligations from April 02, 2026, ensuring continued liquidity support for the stock. No disruption to trading is expected.
