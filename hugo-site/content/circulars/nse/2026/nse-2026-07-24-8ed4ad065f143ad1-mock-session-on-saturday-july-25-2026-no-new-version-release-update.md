## Summary

NSE has issued an update superseding its earlier mock circular (NSE/COM/75373 dated July 23, 2026), revising the timings for a mock trading session in the Commodity Derivatives (CD) segment to be held on Saturday, July 25, 2026. All members enabled for trading in the CD segment in the live market are automatically eligible to participate using their existing live environment User IDs, IPs, and Box ID mappings.

## Key Points

- This circular supersedes NSE/COM/75373 dated July 23, 2026 with updated mock session timings.
- Trading Session-1 (Primary Site): Normal Market open 13:15 hrs, close 15:15 hrs.
- Trading Session-2 (DR Site): Normal Market open 16:00 hrs, close 17:00 hrs.
- Position Limit/Collateral value set up cut-off time: 17:10 hrs.
- Trade modification end time: 17:10 hrs.
- Live re-login window: 19:00 hrs to 19:30 hrs.
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session; NNF software users must manually clear orders.
- No fund pay-in or pay-out results from trades executed during the mock session.
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN records uploaded and approved before the cutoff will be permitted to trade; UCC validation is skipped during contingency order entry time.
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during the mock session.
- Members should participate using all trading software and re-login to the live environment to ensure smooth connectivity ahead of trading on Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirements are introduced. The circular references existing requirements under NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234, noting that members may use either Mock Trading or a Simulated Environment to meet applicable regulatory testing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure valid connectivity (telnet access) from the intended IP address and confirm Box ID setup with appropriate segment messages.
- NNF application users must ensure their User ID is of dealer type, converted for NNF, and properly mapped.
- Members using NNF software must manually clear outstanding orders before each session.
- UCC/PAN records must be valid and approved before the cutoff time to enable trading during the mock session.

## Important Dates

- Mock trading session: Saturday, July 25, 2026
- Trading Session-1: 13:15 - 15:15 hrs
- Trading Session-2 (DR Site): 16:00 - 17:00 hrs
- Position limit/collateral cut-off and trade modification end time: 17:10 hrs
- Live re-login window: 19:00 - 19:30 hrs
- Live trading resumes: Monday, July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular relevant only to NSE trading members participating in the Commodity Derivatives segment. It requires members to test system connectivity and participate in the mock session to ensure readiness for live trading resumption on July 27, 2026. There is no impact on listed securities, market pricing, or investor-facing activity since no financial settlement occurs during mock sessions.
