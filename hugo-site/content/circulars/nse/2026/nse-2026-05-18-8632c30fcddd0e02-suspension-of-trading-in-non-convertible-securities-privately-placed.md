## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in one privately placed Non-Convertible Security issued by L&T Finance Limited (ISIN: INE476M08063) effective May 19, 2026. The suspension is triggered by redemption due to the exercise of a call option, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- L&T Finance Limited NCS (ISIN: INE476M08063) will be suspended from trading on NSE effective May 19, 2026
- Suspension is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Reason for suspension: Redemption for exercising of call option
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref No. NSE/CML/74263, Circular Ref. No. 0794/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is executed under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs trading suspensions for non-convertible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in ISIN INE476M08063 (L&T Finance Limited NCS) from May 19, 2026 onwards
- Members with open positions or pending orders in this security should take necessary action prior to the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 18, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only holders and traders of L&T Finance Limited's specific privately placed non-convertible security (INE476M08063). As this is a call option redemption event, it is a planned corporate action rather than a punitive or regulatory measure. Impact is limited to counterparties holding or trading this instrument in the NSE debt market. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
