## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in privately placed Non-Convertible Securities of Toyota Financial Services India Limited, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspension takes effect on June 18, 2026, ahead of redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE692Q07423 (Toyota Financial Services India Limited) will be suspended effective June 18, 2026
- Suspension is triggered by redemption of the non-convertible security
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74752 (Ref. No. 0981/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notification under the existing framework of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs suspension of trading in debt securities upon maturity or redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members dealing in debt market instruments should note the suspension of trading in the specified ISIN
- Members must ensure no trades are executed in ISIN INE692Q07423 on or after June 18, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 18, 2026 (redemption date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific privately placed NCD (ISIN: INE692Q07423) issued by Toyota Financial Services India Limited. The suspension is routine and expected, as it coincides with the security's redemption. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
