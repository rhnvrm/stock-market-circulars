## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to dealings on its Debt Market Segment effective January 21, 2026. The listing includes 5 commercial papers totaling Rs 665 crore and 4 debenture issues totaling Rs 4,990 crore from six companies across financial services, pharmaceuticals, and NBFC sectors.

## Key Points

- 5 commercial papers listed with maturities ranging from 64 days to 364 days
- 4 debenture issues listed with tenures of 2-3 years
- Commercial paper issue prices range from Rs 4,60,505 to Rs 4,93,296.50 per unit (face value Rs 5 lakh)
- Debenture coupon rates range from 7.14% to 11.50% per annum
- All securities allotted on January 19-20, 2026

## Regulatory Framework

Listing authorized under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 19-20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with no impact on equity trading or market operations. Provides investors with additional fixed-income investment options across various credit profiles and maturities. The securities are privately placed and restricted to qualified institutional buyers.
