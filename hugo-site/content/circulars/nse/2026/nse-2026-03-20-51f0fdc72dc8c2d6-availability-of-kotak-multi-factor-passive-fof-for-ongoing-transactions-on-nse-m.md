## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Kotak Multi Factor Passive FOF of Kotak Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 23, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options will be available, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Kotak Multi Factor Passive FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 23, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities include: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two schemes available: Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1ZH4) and Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1ZG6)
- Both schemes are categorized under Equity
- Issued under Circular Ref. No: 1283/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/73384

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification regarding the resumption of ongoing transaction facilities for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability and facilitate client transactions in the Kotak Multi Factor Passive FOF through the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **March 23, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Kotak Multi Factor Passive FOF for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular enables retail and institutional investors to transact in the Kotak Multi Factor Passive FOF via NSE MF Invest Platform. It restores normal transaction access after the NFO period and is a routine operational update with no broader regulatory or market implications.
