## Summary

NSE has informed members that Sundaram Mutual Fund is renaming the 'Monthly IDCW' option to 'IDCW' for two of its schemes — Sundaram Arbitrage Fund and Sundaram Overnight Fund — on the NSE MF Invest platform. The change is purely nomenclatural and takes effect from July 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Sundaram Arbitrage Fund: 'Monthly IDCW' option renamed to 'IDCW'
- Sundaram Overnight Fund: 'Monthly IDCW' option renamed to 'IDCW'
- Change applies on the NSE MF Invest platform
- Notice-cum-addendum issued by Sundaram Asset Management Company Ltd is enclosed with the circular
- All other terms and conditions of the Scheme Information Document (SID), Key Information Memorandum (KIM), and Statement of Additional Information (SAI) remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory change; this is a scheme-level administrative update by the AMC (renaming of an existing IDCW option), communicated to NSE members for platform-level reflection.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members and the NSE MF Invest platform must update the scheme option name from 'Monthly IDCW' to 'IDCW' for the two affected schemes. Relevant changes will also be carried out in the SID, KIM, and SAI of the schemes by the AMC.

## Important Dates

- Addendum date: July 2, 2026
- Circular date: July 7, 2026
- Effective date of option name change: July 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change limited to renaming an existing dividend/IDCW option label. It does not affect fund NAV, investment strategy, unit holders' rights, or trading mechanics, and requires no action from investors beyond awareness of the new option name.
