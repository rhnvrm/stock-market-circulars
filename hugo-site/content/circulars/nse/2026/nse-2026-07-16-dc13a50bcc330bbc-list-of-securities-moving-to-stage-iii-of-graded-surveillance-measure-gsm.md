## Summary

NSE has issued a circular moving the security MORARJEE (Morarjee Textiles Limited, ISIN INE161G01027) to Stage III of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, effective July 17, 2026. This follows a series of earlier GSM circulars dating back to February 2017. Under Stage III, the security will trade on a trade-for-trade basis with a price band of 5% or lower, trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or the first trading day of the week), and buyers must deposit an Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) equal to 100% of trade value.

## Key Points

- Security moved to GSM Stage III: MORARJEE (Morarjee Textiles Limited), ISIN INE161G01027
- Effective date: July 17, 2026
- Trading restricted to trade-for-trade basis only (no netting off allowed; settlement is trade-to-trade)
- Price band capped at 5% or lower, as applicable
- Trading permitted only once a week (every Monday, or first trading day of the week if Monday is a holiday)
- Buyers must deposit Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 100% of trade value
- Circular references a continuing series of GSM actions: NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/74254, and NSE/SURV/74795

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular applies the existing GSM framework (Stages I through IV) to move a specific security into a stricter surveillance stage. Stage III combines trade-for-trade settlement, reduced trading frequency (weekly), a capped price band, and a full (100%) ASD requirement on buyers, with no netting permitted.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure trades in MORARJEE are executed on a trade-for-trade basis only, with no netting off
- Members must collect 100% ASD from buyers before settlement
- Trading in the security should only be facilitated once a week, per the Stage III schedule
- Members must apply the applicable 5% or lower price band
- Any queries should be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- July 16, 2026: Circular issued (Circular Ref. No. 536/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/SURV/75229)
- July 17, 2026: Effective date for MORARJEE moving to GSM Stage III

## Impact Assessment

This action significantly reduces liquidity and trading flexibility for Morarjee Textiles Limited shareholders and prospective investors, as trading is limited to once weekly with a high capital deposit requirement (100% ASD) for buyers. This is a targeted surveillance measure aimed at curbing potential price manipulation or unusual trading activity in the specific security, and does not have broader market-wide implications beyond MORARJEE and its market participants.
