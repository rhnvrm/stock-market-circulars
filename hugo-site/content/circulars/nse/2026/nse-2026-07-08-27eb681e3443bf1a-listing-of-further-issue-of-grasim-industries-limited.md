## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Grasim Industries Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 09, 2026. The issue relates to the conversion of 5,967 partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- Symbol: GRASIM; Series: EQ; ISIN: INE047A01021
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share; Paid-up value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Number of securities newly admitted to dealings: 5,967
- Market lot: 1
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Lock-in: Not applicable
- Distinctive numbers range: 680463939 to 680469905 (5,967 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (listing of further issued securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed shares under the designated security code (GRASIM-EQ) and lot size of 1 for trading purposes from the effective date. Shares were credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8047A01020) pending activation per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 09, 2026
- Circular date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The additional 5,967 shares represent a negligible increase to Grasim Industries' total listed equity capital and are pari passu with existing shares. The impact on market float, liquidity, or share price is expected to be minimal given the small quantum relative to Grasim's total outstanding shares.
