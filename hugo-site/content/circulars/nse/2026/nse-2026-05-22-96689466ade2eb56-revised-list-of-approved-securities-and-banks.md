## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of equity shares eligible as the non-cash component of liquid assets for the Currency Derivatives (CD) segment. The circular specifies overall permitted quantities across all segments and memberwise permitted quantities for the CD segment, along with applicable haircut (VAR-based) and value-based prudential norms.

## Key Points

- Eligible equity shares forming the non-cash component of liquid assets for the CD segment are listed in Annexure 1 & 2.
- Applicable haircut for listed securities is Value at Risk (VAR)-based.
- Value-based prudential norm: exclusion of a pledged security must not result in a margin shortfall of more than 25% at a Clearing Member level.
- Memberwise permitted quantity for the CD segment is listed as "N.A" for all securities in Annexure 1, indicating no separate member-level cap beyond the overall limit.
- The list covers 39+ major Nifty 50 constituent stocks including HDFCBANK, RELIANCE, ICICIBANK, ITC, KOTAKBANK, INFY, SBIN, BHARTIARTL, and others.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular supersedes the previous approved securities list for the CD segment. The overall permitted quantities for each security have been updated to reflect current market capitalisation and liquidity parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing Members must ensure that collateral pledged as non-cash liquid assets conforms to the revised permitted quantities specified in the circular.
- Pledging of a security that would cause margin shortfall exceeding 25% at the Clearing Member level upon exclusion is not permitted.
- Members must review and rebalance their collateral portfolios if any security's permitted quantity has changed.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: 2026-05-22
- Effective Date: As notified (typically immediate or next trading day upon issuance).

## Impact Assessment

Clearing Members active in the Currency Derivatives segment who use equity shares as non-cash collateral are directly impacted. The revised permitted quantities determine the maximum exposure a member can back with each listed security. No stocks have been removed in the visible data; however, quantity limits may have changed. Members holding securities close to previous limits should verify compliance with the updated quantities to avoid margin shortfalls.
