## Summary

Teamtech Formwork Solutions Limited has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform with effect from May 26, 2026. The equity shares will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment) as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Symbol: TEAMTECH; ISIN: INE2OL601013
- Listed on NSE EMERGE SME Platform effective May 26, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 63 per share; Face value: Rs. 5 per share
- Total securities listed: 2,99,76,000 shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 29976000)
- Market lot and Market Maker minimum quantity: 2,000 equity shares
- Market Maker maximum permissible spread: within 10%
- Market Maker: SMC Global Securities Limited, Mumbai
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Kfin Technologies Limited, Mumbai
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Available for continuous market trading as per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5, and applicable SEBI circulars governing SME IPO listings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (TEAMTECH) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 2,000 equity shares
- Lock-in restrictions apply as per Annexure I of the circular
- The security will initially trade in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series ST) before migration to Normal Rolling Segment (Series SM)

## Important Dates

- Circular date: May 25, 2026
- Effective date for trading: May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing on the NSE EMERGE platform. Impact is limited to participants in the Teamtech Formwork Solutions Limited IPO and active market makers. The initial Trade for Trade segment placement (Series ST) means delivery is mandatory on each trade, reducing speculative activity in early trading. The 2,000-share market lot is consistent with SME platform norms. Broader market impact is low.
