## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 1,439,399 equity shares of Khfm Hospitality and Facility Management Services Limited on the SME EMERGE platform, effective January 21, 2026. These shares were allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants allotted on preferential basis on November 12, 2025. The shares have a face value of Rs. 10 and were issued at Rs. 52 per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Khfm Hospitality and Facility Management Services Limited
- Trading Symbol: KHFM
- Number of securities listed: 1,439,399 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 52 per share
- ISIN: INE00UG01014 (Series: SM)
- Market lot size: 1,550 shares
- Date of allotment: November 12, 2025
- Distinctive number range: 21671101 to 23110499
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Trading platform: NSE Capital Market segment - SME EMERGE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new security codes and lot sizes for trading purposes
- Securities will be identified only by designated codes on the trading system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1,550 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 regarding activation of ISIN for additional issues

## Important Dates

- **Circular date:** January 20, 2026
- **Effective date for trading:** January 21, 2026
- **Date of allotment:** November 12, 2025
- **Lock-in expiry dates (various tranches):**
  - 64,102 shares: July 30, 2027
  - 148,800 shares: July 30, 2026
  - 46,500 shares: July 29, 2026
  - 449,500 shares: July 30, 2026
  - 271,540 shares: July 30, 2027
  - 458,957 shares: July 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine listing of additional shares for an SME company on the EMERGE platform. The volume of 1.44 million shares represents a further issue following warrant conversion.

**Company Impact:** The listing provides liquidity to warrant holders who converted their warrants at Rs. 52 per share. Various lock-in periods apply to different tranches, with some shares locked until July 2027, indicating long-term commitment from certain investors.

**Investor Impact:** Existing shareholders should note the increase in outstanding shares. The pari passu status means these new shares carry the same rights as existing equity shares. The market lot size of 1,550 shares should be considered for trading purposes.
