## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Groww BSE Hospitals ETF FOF (Fund of Fund) by Groww Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective March 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Groww BSE Hospitals ETF FOF will be available for ongoing transactions from March 09, 2026
- Six scheme variants are covered across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options
- Facilities available: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category

| Groww BSE Hospitals ETF FOF - Regular - IDCW Reinvestment | INF666M01OC1 | Equity |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed to take note of the availability of these schemes for facilitating investor transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Groww BSE Hospitals ETF FOF for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP/STP/SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact. It is operationally relevant to NSE members and investors who wish to transact in the Groww BSE Hospitals ETF FOF. The reopening provides investors access to a healthcare-sector-focused ETF Fund of Fund, which may attract interest given the BSE Hospitals index exposure. No disruption to existing market operations is anticipated.
