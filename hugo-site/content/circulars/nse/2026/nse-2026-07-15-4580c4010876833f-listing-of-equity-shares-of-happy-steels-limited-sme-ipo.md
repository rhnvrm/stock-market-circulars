## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Happy Steels Limited following its SME IPO. Along with the listing, the company has filed its shareholding pattern (Holding of Specified Securities, Annexure-I) as required under SEBI LODR Regulation 31, covering the reporting period ending 15-07-2026.

## Key Points

- Happy Steels Limited's equity shares are being listed on NSE's SME platform pursuant to its SME IPO.
- The company has submitted its Shareholding Pattern declaration under Regulation 31(1)(a)/(b)/(c) of SEBI LODR, for the quarter ending 15-07-2026.
- Declaration confirms: no partly paid-up shares, no outstanding convertible securities, no warrants, no outstanding ESOPs, and no depository receipts issued.
- The company has shares that are currently locked-in.
- No promoter shares are encumbered via pledge or non-disposal undertaking.
- No differential voting rights shares exist.
- The entity has a significant beneficial owner as declared.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing and disclosure filing under existing SEBI LODR Regulation 31 requirements for newly listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

Happy Steels Limited is required to continue periodic shareholding pattern disclosures as per Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR following its listing, including updates on any changes to locked-in shares, promoter holdings, and beneficial ownership status.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern reporting period: quarter ending 15-07-2026.
- Listing date: 15-07-2026 (same-day as circular date).

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides a new trading avenue for Happy Steels Limited's equity shares on NSE's SME segment. Impact is primarily relevant to investors in the SME IPO and does not have broader market-wide implications. Presence of locked-in shares limits immediate free-float liquidity.
