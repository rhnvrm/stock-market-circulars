## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying the listing of further issues of securities for 14 companies — AVRO India Limited, Axis Bank Limited, Cyient DLM Limited, ICICI Bank Limited, Ideaforge Technology Limited, Kirloskar Pneumatic Company Limited, Lodha Developers Limited, NAVA Limited, NIIT Learning Systems Limited, Onward Technologies Limited, The South Indian Bank Limited, Sterlite Technologies Limited, Suven Life Sciences Limited, and Tirupati Forge Limited. These additional securities, arising from preferential allotments, ESOP allotments, and bonus share allotments, will be admitted to dealings on NSE's Capital Market segment with effect from July 03, 2026, under Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 14 companies had further issues of equity shares admitted for trading on NSE's Capital Market segment.
- AVRO India Limited: 1,060,900 equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted on a preferential basis on 18-Apr-2026 at an issue price of Rs. 12.73; market lot 1; lock-in expiry 05-Jan-2027.
- Axis Bank Limited: 129,056 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on 30-Jun-2026; market lot 1; no lock-in.
- Cyient DLM Limited: 18,972 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP on 04-Jun-2026; market lot 1; no lock-in.
- ICICI Bank Limited: 325 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted pursuant to a bonus issue earlier kept under abeyance, originally allotted 24-Jun-2017.
- Additional details for Ideaforge Technology, Kirloskar Pneumatic, Lodha Developers, NAVA, NIIT Learning Systems, Onward Technologies, South Indian Bank, Sterlite Technologies, Suven Life Sciences, and Tirupati Forge are specified in the circular's annexure (not fully captured in this excerpt).
- All securities will be identified by their designated security codes, and trading will occur in the specified lot sizes.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for listing of securities and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes) confirming admission of further-issue shares to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed securities by their designated codes and trade only in the specified lot sizes as detailed in the annexure from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 02, 2026
- Effective Date: July 03, 2026 (further issues admitted to dealings)
- Company-specific allotment dates: AVRO India (18-Apr-2026), Axis Bank (30-Jun-2026), Cyient DLM (04-Jun-2026), ICICI Bank (original bonus allotment 24-Jun-2017, abeyance release date per annexure)
- AVRO India lock-in expiry: 05-Jan-2027

## Impact Assessment

This circular reflects routine incremental additions to the listed equity capital of several companies through ESOP exercises, preferential allotments, and bonus share releases from abeyance. The share quantities involved are small relative to the overall market capitalization and free float of these companies (particularly for large-cap names like Axis Bank and ICICI Bank), so the market impact is minimal. The notice is primarily operational, ensuring smooth trading system recognition of the additional shares under existing security codes and lot sizes.
