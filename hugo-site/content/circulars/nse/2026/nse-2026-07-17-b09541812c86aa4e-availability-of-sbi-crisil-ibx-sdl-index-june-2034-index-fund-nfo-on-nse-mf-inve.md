## Summary

NSE has notified members that the SBI CRISIL-IBX SDL Index - June 2034 Index Fund NFO, managed by SBI Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme is a debt index fund tracking the CRISIL-IBX SDL Index maturing June 2034.
- Six plan/option variants of the scheme will be available on the platform, covering Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- Facilities enabled include ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP and SWP.
- ISINs for each variant are listed in the circular for reference by members.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling platform availability of an existing fund scheme, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members offering mutual fund distribution services should update their systems/platforms to reflect availability of these scheme codes and ISINs for client transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 20, 2026 - reopening of NFO for subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Circular Date: July 17, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to operational availability of a debt mutual fund scheme on NSE's MF distribution platform. It does not affect listed equities, trading mechanisms, or broader market structure. Relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors, NSE members facilitating MF transactions, and investors interested in this specific debt index fund scheme.
