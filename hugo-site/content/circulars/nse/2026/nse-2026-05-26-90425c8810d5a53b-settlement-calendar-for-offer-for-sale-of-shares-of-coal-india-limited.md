## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Coal India Limited, in reference to earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026. Settlement for OFS securities follows existing secondary market transaction rules.

## Key Points

- Two OFS order dates are covered: May 27, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026098) and May 29, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026099).
- Settlement No. 2026098 settles on May 29, 2026; Settlement No. 2026099 settles on June 1, 2026.
- Non-retail (IS) orders with 100% margin category require custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM on the order date; 0% margin category confirmations are due by 7:30 AM on the settlement date.
- Retail (RS) orders under Settlement No. 2026099 carry 100% margin with no custodial confirmation requirement.
- Funds Pay-in is due by 11:00 AM on T+1; Securities and Funds Pay-out occurs by 1:30 PM on T+1.
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the OFS settlement process as per existing secondary market rules, referencing the framework established in circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must adhere to the custodial confirmation deadlines: 3:30 PM (IS, 100% margin) and 7:30 AM on settlement date (IS, 0% margin).
- Funds pay-in must be completed by 11:00 AM on T+1.
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement day.
- Queries may be directed to the help desk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: OFS order date for Settlement No. 2026098 (non-retail).
- **May 29, 2026**: Settlement date for Settlement No. 2026098; also OFS order date for Settlement No. 2026099 (retail and non-retail).
- **June 1, 2026**: Settlement date for Settlement No. 2026099.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Coal India OFS. Participants must ensure timely custodial confirmations and funds pay-in to avoid settlement failures. The two-tranche structure (non-retail on May 27 and retail + non-retail on May 29) requires careful coordination of confirmation and pay-in workflows. No broader market impact is anticipated beyond OFS participants.
