## Summary

NSE's Futures & Options segment circular (Ref. No. 58/2026) notifies members that four indices will be disseminated via NEAT+ terminals under the multiple index inquiry screen effective May 11, 2026. This circular is consequent to Capital Market circular NSE/CMTR/74097 dated May 07, 2026 covering the same indices for the Capital Market segment.

## Key Points

- Four indices will be broadcast to F&O members via NEAT+ terminals with specific broadcast names.
- Index broadcast names differ slightly from official index names in some cases (e.g., 'Nifty REITs & Realty' broadcasts as 'Nifty REITs Realty').
- Members using Non-Neat Front End (NNF) systems must configure or develop their systems independently to receive the index broadcast.
- Factsheets for the indices are provided as annexures to the circular.
- The Nifty Cement Index was launched February 16, 2026, tracks up to 20 cement & cement products stocks from Nifty Total Market, and currently has 16 constituents with Grasim Industries Ltd. as a top weighted constituent.

## Index Broadcast Name Mapping

| Sr. No. | Index Name | Broadcast Name |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| 1 | Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 | Nifty MidSmall 50 50 |
| 2 | Nifty Cement | Nifty Cement |
| 3 | Nifty Smallcap 500 | Nifty Smallcap 500 |
| 4 | Nifty REITs & Realty | Nifty REITs Realty |

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rules introduced. This circular operationalises the extension of index dissemination to the F&O segment following the parallel Capital Market segment circular (NSE/CMTR/74097).

## Compliance Requirements

- **NEAT+ users**: No action required; index data will be automatically available under the multiple index inquiry screen.
- **NNF (Non-Neat Front End) users**: Must configure or develop systems to receive the index broadcast feed for the four new indices.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: May 07, 2026
- **Effective Date**: May 11, 2026
- **Reference Circular**: NSE/CMTR/74097 dated May 07, 2026 (Capital Market segment)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-severity operational update. F&O members using NEAT+ terminals will gain automatic access to real-time broadcast data for four indices, supporting index-based strategy development and monitoring. NNF members face a moderate operational requirement to update their systems before May 11, 2026. The Nifty Cement Index, tracking up to 20 cement sector stocks with a 15% single-stock weight cap, is newly launched (February 16, 2026) and its inclusion in F&O dissemination may support future derivative product launches on the index.
