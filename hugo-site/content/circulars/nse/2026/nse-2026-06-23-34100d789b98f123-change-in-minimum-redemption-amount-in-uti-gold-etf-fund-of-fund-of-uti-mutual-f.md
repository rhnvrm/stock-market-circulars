## Summary

NSE has informed its members that UTI Mutual Fund has revised the minimum redemption amount for the UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform, effective June 23, 2026. The change is communicated via Addendum No. 22/2026-27 dated June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Minimum redemption amount reduced from Rs.25/- (in multiples of Re.1/-) to Re.1/- (in multiples of Re.1/-)
- Change applicable on the NSE MF Invest platform
- All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged
- Addendum forms an integral part of the Scheme Information Document (SID) / Key Information Memorandum (KIM)

## Regulatory Changes

The minimum redemption threshold for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund has been lowered, making redemptions accessible at a significantly smaller unit value. This is a scheme-level amendment under Addendum No. 22/2026-27 issued by UTI Asset Management Company Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform should update their systems and processes to reflect the revised minimum redemption amount of Re.1/- effective June 23, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Addendum No. 22/2026-27 issued by UTI Mutual Fund
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date of revised minimum redemption amount

## Impact Assessment

The reduction in minimum redemption amount from Rs.25/- to Re.1/- broadens investor accessibility for redemptions in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund. The operational impact on members is minimal, limited to updating redemption validation parameters on the NSE MF Invest platform. No impact on trading, settlement, or broader market operations.
