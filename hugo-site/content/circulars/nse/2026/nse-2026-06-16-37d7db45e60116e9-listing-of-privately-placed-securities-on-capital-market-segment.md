## Summary

NSE has admitted L&T Finance Limited's privately placed Non-Convertible Debenture (8.12% coupon, Series B, maturing June 29, 2029) to dealings on the National Stock Exchange Capital Market segment effective June 17, 2026. The security trades under symbol `812LTFL29` in series `N0` with a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Security admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Market lot size of 1 unit per Regulation 2.5.5
- Trading must reference the designated security code `812LTFL29` exclusively
- ISIN: INE498L07210
- Coupon rate: 8.12% per annum
- Maturity date: June 29, 2029

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a standard notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of Part A).

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security code `812LTFL29` for trading this instrument
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** June 29, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing notification for a single privately placed NCD issued by L&T Finance Limited. It provides liquidity to holders of the INE498L07210 debenture by enabling secondary market trading on NSE. Relevant to debt market participants and existing holders of this instrument; no broader equity or regulatory implications.
