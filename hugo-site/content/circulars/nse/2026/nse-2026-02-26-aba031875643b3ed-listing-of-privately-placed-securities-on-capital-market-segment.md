## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of Angel One Limited's privately placed Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective February 27, 2026. The securities are listed under symbol 875AOL27 (Series N0) with a market lot of 1 and carry a coupon rate of 8.75%, maturing on August 25, 2027.

## Key Points

- Angel One Limited NCDs admitted to Capital Market segment from February 27, 2026
- Trading symbol: **875AOL27**, Series: **N0**, Market Lot: **1**
- Issue Description: AOL 8.75% 2027
- Coupon Rate: **8.75%**
- ISIN: **INE732I07010**
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in compliance with existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must use the designated security code **875AOL27** to identify and trade these securities on the NSE system.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of **1** unit.
- No additional compliance action is required from members beyond recognising the new security code.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Effective Date (trading commencement):** February 27, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** August 25, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The admission of a single NCD issue by Angel One Limited provides investors on NSE's Capital Market segment access to a short-duration (approx. 18-month) debt instrument with an 8.75% coupon. The market lot of 1 supports retail participation. No disruption to existing trading activity is anticipated.
