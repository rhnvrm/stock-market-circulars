## Summary

NSE's Listing department (Circular Ref. No: 1016/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74861) notifies the admission of further equity shares of three SME companies to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment under SME Emerge, effective June 25, 2026. The three companies are Grill Splendour Services Limited, Delaplex Limited, and Integrity Infrabuild Developers Limited.

## Key Points

- Three SME Emerge-listed companies are adding further securities under various issue types (preferential allotment and ESOP).
- All new shares are in Series SM with a face value of Rs. 10 each.
- Lot sizes and designated security codes are specified in the annexure for trading purposes.
- Shares allotted in dematerialised form are initially credited under temporary ISINs (IN8*) per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012, pending activation of the permanent ISIN.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission of securities to dealings).
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes for trading).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only the designated security codes (BIRDYS, DELAPLEX, INTEGRITY) and the specified lot sizes when trading these securities.
- Shares under lock-in (applicable to BIRDYS and INTEGRITY preferential allotments) cannot be traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 25, 2026 (admission to dealings on NSE SME Emerge).
- **Grill Splendour Services (BIRDYS):** Date of Allotment — May 22, 2026; Lock-in expiry — December 31, 2026.
- **Delaplex (DELAPLEX):** Date of Allotment — June 15, 2026; No lock-in applicable.
- **Integrity Infrabuild Developers (INTEGRITY):** Date of Allotment — April 29, 2026; Lock-in expiry — December 31, 2026.
