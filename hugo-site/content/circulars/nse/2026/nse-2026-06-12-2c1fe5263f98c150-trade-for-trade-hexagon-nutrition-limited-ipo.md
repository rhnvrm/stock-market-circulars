## Summary

NSE has announced the transfer of Hexagon Nutrition Limited (symbol: HEXAGON) from the Trade for Trade segment (series: BE) to the Rolling segment (series: EQ) effective June 29, 2026. This follows the company's IPO listing and is pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- HEXAGON (Hexagon Nutrition Limited) will move from series BE (Trade for Trade) to series EQ (Rolling segment)
- Transfer is effective from June 29, 2026
- This circular is a follow-up to NSE/CML/74667 dated June 11, 2026
- The change is made pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The security will transition from compulsory Trade for Trade settlement (where every trade must result in delivery and no netting is permitted) to the normal Rolling settlement (EQ series), enabling regular trading with netting benefits.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members must note the series change for Hexagon Nutrition Limited and update their systems accordingly. Trades executed on or after June 29, 2026 will be settled under the Rolling segment (EQ series) rules.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Reference Circular:** NSE/CML/74667 dated June 11, 2026
- **Effective Date of Transfer:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard post-IPO procedural change. Moving from Trade for Trade (BE) to Rolling (EQ) improves liquidity and trading flexibility for HEXAGON, as traders will no longer be required to give/take mandatory delivery on every trade. Intraday trading and netting will become permissible, which typically increases trading volumes and price discovery efficiency for the stock.
