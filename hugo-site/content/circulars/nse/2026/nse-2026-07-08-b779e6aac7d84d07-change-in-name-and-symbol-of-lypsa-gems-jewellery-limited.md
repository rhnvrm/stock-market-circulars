## Summary

NSE has notified members that Lypsa Gems & Jewellery Limited will undergo a change in its company name and trading symbol, effective July 14, 2026. The existing symbol LYPSAGEMS will be replaced with AURUS, and the company will be renamed AURUS GEM CORPORATION LIMITED.

## Key Points

- Existing Symbol: LYPSAGEMS; New Symbol: AURUS
- Existing Name: Lypsa Gems & Jewellery Limited; New Name: AURUS GEM CORPORATION LIMITED
- Change applicable from July 14, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75101, Circular Ref. No: 1107/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to the company's registered name and trading symbol on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange must update their trading and back-office systems to reflect the new symbol (AURUS) and company name (AURUS GEM CORPORATION LIMITED) effective July 14, 2026, to ensure accurate order routing and reporting.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date of Change: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is largely operational, requiring members to update static data and trading systems to avoid order rejection or misrouting. Investors and traders should be aware of the new symbol to correctly identify and trade the security post-change. No change to underlying business operations, shareholding, or corporate structure is indicated.
