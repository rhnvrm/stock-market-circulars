## Summary

NSE has notified members that the ISIN code for the equity shares of Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited (POCL) will change with effect from July 21, 2026. The change follows a sub-division (stock split) of the face value of equity shares from Rs. 5/- per share to Rs. 2/- per share. The new ISIN will be INE063E01061.

## Key Points

- Symbol: POCL
- Company: Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited
- New ISIN: INE063E01061
- Reason: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 5/- to Rs. 2/- per equity share
- Effective date: July 21, 2026 (Ex-date)

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative/operational update reflecting a change in the ISIN following a corporate action (stock split), not a change in regulatory policy.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN for POCL equity shares for all trades executed on and after the ex-date. No action is required from investors beyond awareness of the ISIN change for record-keeping and demat purposes.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 17, 2026
- Effective/Ex-date for new ISIN: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The ISIN change is a direct consequence of the face value sub-division (split) of POCL shares from Rs. 5/- to Rs. 2/-, which increases the number of outstanding shares proportionately while keeping overall market capitalization unchanged. Members and depository participants must ensure accurate mapping of the old and new ISIN to avoid settlement or reconciliation errors. The impact on trading is procedural rather than affecting the company's fundamentals.
