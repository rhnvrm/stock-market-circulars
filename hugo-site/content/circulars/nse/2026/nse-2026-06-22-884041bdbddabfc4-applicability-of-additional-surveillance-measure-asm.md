## Summary

NSE has shortlisted three securities under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) Stage I framework effective June 23, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply to all open positions and new positions from June 25, 2026. No securities were added to Stage IV, and no stage progressions (Stage I→II, II→III, or I→IV) were recorded in this circular.

## Key Points

- Three securities added to Long Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 23, 2026
- PARAS (Paras Defence and Space Technologies Limited) moved from Short Term ASM (STASM) to Long Term ASM (LTASM)
- SBC (SBC Exports Limited) and WALCHANNAG (Walchandnagar Industries Limited) newly included in LTASM Stage I
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions from June 25, 2026
- No securities shortlisted under Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade shift) in this circular
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to the Long Term ASM framework are subject to enhanced surveillance margins. The shortlisting is based purely on market surveillance criteria and does not constitute an adverse action against the concerned company. Reference circulars include NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure 100% margin is collected on open positions in PARAS, SBC, and WALCHANNAG as on June 24, 2026
- 100% margin must be collected on all new positions in these securities created from June 25, 2026 onwards
- Members should refer to the NSE ASM FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for detailed provisions
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of securities in Long Term ASM Stage I
- **June 24, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 25, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes applicable on open positions (as of June 24, 2026) and all new positions

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding or trading PARAS, SBC, and WALCHANNAG will face significantly increased margin requirements (100%), raising the capital needed to maintain or establish positions. This may reduce liquidity and trading volumes in these securities. PARAS was already under Short Term ASM and its migration to Long Term ASM signals continued surveillance concern. Since no securities were moved to Stage IV, there is no immediate series change from EQ to BE (Trade-for-Trade settlement) in this update.
