## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol BHARATCOAL with ISIN INE05XR01022. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Bharat Coking Coal Limited
- Trading Symbol: BHARATCOAL
- ISIN: INE05XR01022
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular about a new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 16, 2026
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new equity security in the coal sector to the NSE platform. The impact is moderate as it provides investors with a new investment opportunity in the coking coal industry. Market participants and investors interested in the coal and energy sector should watch for the subsequent circular announcing the actual listing date. The listing will expand the universe of publicly traded coal companies available on NSE.
