## Summary

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited has issued a Notice-cum-Addendum to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of the ICICI Prudential Equity – Arbitrage Fund. The changes, effective May 27, 2026, include a scheme name change and updates to scheme features to align with SEBI's updated Master Circular for Mutual Funds dated March 20, 2026, and the SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme name change for ICICI Prudential Equity – Arbitrage Fund effective May 27, 2026
- Changes to investment provisions to align with SEBI Master Circular for Mutual Funds (March 20, 2026)
- Updated investment universe includes: coupon-bearing bonds, zero coupon bonds, treasury bills, debt securities by domestic government agencies and statutory bodies, units of debt-oriented mutual fund schemes with Macaulay Duration less than one year, permitted derivative instruments, and any other SEBI/RBI-permitted securities
- Both regulatory alignment changes and other scheme feature changes are included
- Updates reflected in SID and KIM documents

## Regulatory Changes

The scheme is being updated to comply with:
- SEBI's updated Master Circular for Mutual Funds dated March 20, 2026
- SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026

Investment provisions have been revised, including clarification on permissible debt instruments and debt-oriented mutual fund scheme units with Macaulay Duration less than one year.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE MF Invest platform to reflect the updated scheme name and features from May 27, 2026
- Existing investors are notified via Notice-cum-Addendum to SID and KIM
- ICICI Prudential AMC to update all scheme documentation accordingly
- Distributors and investment advisers should communicate changes to clients invested in or considering investment in the scheme

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 27, 2026 — New scheme name and updated features come into force
- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026 — Addendum issued by ICICI Prudential AMC
- **SEBI Master Circular Reference Date:** March 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The changes primarily affect investors in the ICICI Prudential Equity – Arbitrage Fund and participants on the NSE MF Invest platform. The scheme name change may require updates in portfolio management systems, distributor platforms, and investor records. The investment provision changes are largely regulatory housekeeping to align with SEBI's 2026 Master Circular, and do not represent a fundamental shift in the scheme's arbitrage-oriented strategy. Impact is moderate and operational in nature, with no expected disruption to NAV or investor returns.
