## Summary

NSE has placed three securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Stage I effective June 11, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from June 15, 2026 on all open positions as of June 12, 2026 and all new positions created thereafter. No securities were added to Stage IV, and no stage progressions were announced.

## Key Points

- Three securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 11, 2026
- 100% margin applicable from June 15, 2026 on open positions as of June 12, 2026 and new positions
- Stage IV list is Nil — no securities shifted to Trade-for-Trade segment this cycle
- No stage progressions (Stage I→II, II→III, I→IV) announced
- ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I are subject to enhanced surveillance under the framework established via circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE), but no such securities were identified this cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Ensure 100% margin collection on open positions in JNKINDIA, OLAELEC, and TECHERA as of June 12, 2026
- **NSE Members**: Apply 100% margin on all new positions in these securities from June 15, 2026
- Market participants must comply with ASM provisions in conjunction with all other surveillance measures
- For queries, contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Impact Assessment

**JNKINDIA (JNK India Limited | INE0OAF01028):** Inclusion in ASM Stage I will require traders to post 100% margin, significantly increasing the cost of holding positions and likely reducing liquidity.

**OLAELEC (Ola Electric Mobility Limited | INE0LXG01040):** As a high-profile EV company, this inclusion may attract significant market attention. The 100% margin requirement will materially impact leveraged trading activity in this widely tracked stock.

**TECHERA (TechEra Engineering (India) Limited | INE0JQS01019):** Smaller-cap inclusion; ASM placement will restrict speculative activity and enforce price discovery discipline.

Overall, the 100% margin requirement across all three securities will reduce leveraged positions, may compress volumes, and signal elevated surveillance concern over price/volume behavior. The absence of Stage IV additions means no forced segment shifts to Trade-for-Trade this cycle.
