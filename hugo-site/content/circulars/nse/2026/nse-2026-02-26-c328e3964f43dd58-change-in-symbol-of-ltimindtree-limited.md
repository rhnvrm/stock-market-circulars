## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a circular informing all Participants and Custodians of a change in the trading symbol of LTIMindtree Limited within the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Scheme. The symbol changes from **LTIM** to **LTM**, effective February 27, 2026. This follows an earlier NSE circular (NSE/CML/72948) dated February 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol of LTIMindtree Limited changes from **LTIM** to **LTM** in the SLB Scheme.
- Change is effective from **February 27, 2026**.
- All existing contracts in the security will be traded under the new symbol post the effective date.
- This circular is issued by the Department of Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme under NSE Clearing Limited.
- Reference circular: NSE/CML/72948 dated February 23, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The trading symbol for LTIMindtree Limited in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme is being updated:

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Old Symbol | LTIM |
| New Symbol | LTM |
| Effective Date | February 27, 2026 |

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians are advised to update their internal systems, references, and records to reflect the new trading symbol **LTM** for LTIMindtree Limited in the SLB Scheme.
- Ensure all SLB contracts created or carried forward on or after February 27, 2026 use the new symbol LTM.

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: Original NSE circular (NSE/CML/72948) issued regarding the symbol change.
- **February 26, 2026**: This NSE Clearing circular (NCL/CMPT/73010) issued.
- **February 27, 2026**: Symbol change takes effect; all SLB contracts will trade under the new symbol LTM.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change affecting participants and custodians active in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme for LTIMindtree Limited. While the underlying security and its fundamentals are unaffected, firms must ensure timely updates to trading platforms, risk systems, and internal records to avoid confusion or failed transactions. The change is limited in scope to the SLB segment and does not affect equity cash or derivatives markets directly.
