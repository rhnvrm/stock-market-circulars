## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced the settlement schedule for the Futures & Options segment for February 2026. The circular provides trade dates and corresponding settlement dates for all trading days in the month, enabling members to plan their settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers the period from February 2 to February 27, 2026
- Most settlements occur on T+1 basis (next business day)
- February 19, 2026 settlement is scheduled for February 20, 2026
- Final settlement for February 27 trades scheduled for March 2, 2026
- Total of 20 trading days in February 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the F&O segment must:
- Follow the specified settlement schedule for all trades executed in February 2026
- Ensure adequate funds and securities availability as per settlement dates
- Adhere to standard T+1 settlement cycle for daily settlements

## Important Dates

**Trading Period:** February 2-27, 2026

**Key Settlement Dates:**
- February 2, 2026 trades: Settlement on February 3, 2026
- February 27, 2026 trades: Settlement on March 2, 2026
- February 19, 2026 trades: Settlement on February 20, 2026 (monthly expiry)

**Contact:** NCL F&O Clearing Operations - 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or fao_clearing_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine settlement schedule announcement

**Operational Impact:** Standard settlement operations continue as per established T+1 cycle. No disruptions to regular trading or settlement processes expected. Members should note the monthly expiry settlement on February 20 for February 19 trades.
