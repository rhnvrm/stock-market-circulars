## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework effective March 04, 2026. Three securities have been newly shortlisted under the Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I. No securities were moved between stages or excluded from the framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Three securities added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I effective March 04, 2026
- No securities shortlisted for Long-Term ASM Stage-IV
- No stage transitions (Stage-I to II, II to III, III to IV, or reverse movements)
- No securities excluded from the ASM framework
- Scrips shortlisted under Criteria VII for Stage-IV would shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade for Trade (Series: BE) on T+3 basis — not applicable in this circular
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ASM framework is published as part of this circular

## Regulatory Changes

No structural regulatory changes. This is a periodic update to the existing Long-Term ASM framework, adjusting the list of securities subject to enhanced surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants trading in MAHASTEEL, SETCO, and TARMAT must note the applicable ASM Stage-I restrictions effective March 04, 2026
- Brokers and members must ensure clients are informed of the enhanced surveillance status of these securities
- Applicable margin and trading restrictions associated with Long-Term ASM Stage-I apply to the newly added securities

## Important Dates

- **March 02, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 04, 2026**: Effective date for all ASM changes listed in this circular

## Impact Assessment
