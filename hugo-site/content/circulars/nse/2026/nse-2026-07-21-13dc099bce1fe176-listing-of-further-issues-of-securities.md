## Summary

NSE Circular Ref. No. 1191/2026 (Download Ref. No. NSE/CML/75325, dated July 21, 2026) notifies the listing of further issues of securities of nine companies — Belrise Industries Limited, Emkay Global Financial Services Limited, ESAF Small Finance Bank Limited, IDFC First Bank Limited, Jana Small Finance Bank Limited, Max Estates Limited, Metro Brands Limited, Shriram Finance Limited and Zim Laboratories Limited — for dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 22, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Lot sizes for the newly listed securities are specified per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Belrise Industries Limited (BELRISE, ISIN INE894V01022): 77,272,727 equity shares of Rs. 5 each issued under QIP at an issue price of Rs. 220, allotted 17-Jul-2026; market lot 1; no lock-in.
- Emkay Global Financial Services Limited (EMKAY, ISIN INE296H01011): 100,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under Preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 239.5, allotted 27-May-2026; market lot 1; lock-in expiry 23-Jan-2028.
- ESAF Small Finance Bank Limited (ESAFSFB, ISIN INE818W01011): 51,391 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP, allotted 14-Jul-2026; market lot 1; no lock-in.
- IDFC First Bank Limited (IDFCFIRSTB, ISIN INE092T01019): 2,167,979 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP, allotted 14-Jul-2026; market lot 1.
- Additional further-issue listings also cover Jana Small Finance Bank Limited, Max Estates Limited, Metro Brands Limited, Shriram Finance Limited and Zim Laboratories Limited (full annexure details for these five were not present in the extracted content).
- All shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes) rather than a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional securities/quantities under the respective existing designated codes and lot sizes from the effective date; no separate member action is required beyond system recognition of the increased share counts.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: Emkay Global – 27-May-2026; IDFC First Bank and ESAF Small Finance Bank – 14-Jul-2026; Belrise Industries – 17-Jul-2026.
- Effective date of listing/dealings: July 22, 2026.
- Emkay Global preferential shares lock-in expiry: 23-Jan-2028.

## Impact Assessment

The further issues increase the listed equity base (and free float, where applicable) of the nine companies. Belrise Industries' QIP of ~7.73 crore shares at Rs. 220 is the most significant in absolute size and could have a modest dilutive effect; the ESOP and preferential allotments for the other companies are comparatively small relative to their existing share capital and are unlikely to materially affect trading dynamics.
