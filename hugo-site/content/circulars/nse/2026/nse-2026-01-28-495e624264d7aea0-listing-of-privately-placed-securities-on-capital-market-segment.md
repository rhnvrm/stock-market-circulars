## Summary

National Stock Exchange notifies the listing of privately placed market-linked securities issued by SMFG India Home Finance Company Limited on the capital market segment. The security will be available for trading from January 29, 2026, with the symbol SIHF29 in N0 series.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to dealings on NSE capital market segment effective January 29, 2026
- Issuer: SMFG India Home Finance Company Limited
- Symbol: SIHF29
- Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Issue description: SIHF Market Linked 2029
- Coupon rate: Market linked (variable)
- ISIN code: INE213W07368
- Date of maturity: January 23, 2029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code SIHF29 for identification on the trading system
- All trades must be executed in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- Circular date: January 28, 2026
- Effective date for trading: January 29, 2026
- Security maturity date: January 23, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a single privately placed debt security listing. The market-linked coupon structure makes returns variable and linked to market performance. Relevant primarily for debt market participants and investors interested in SMFG India Home Finance Company Limited's debt instruments.
