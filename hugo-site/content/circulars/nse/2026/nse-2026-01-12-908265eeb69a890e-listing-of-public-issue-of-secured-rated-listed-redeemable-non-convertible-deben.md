## Summary

National Stock Exchange has admitted eight series of secured, rated, listed, redeemable non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by Adani Enterprises Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment effective January 13, 2026. The NCDs have face value of Rs. 1,000 each, carry credit ratings of Care AA- (Stable) and ICRA AA- (Stable), and will trade in compulsory demat mode with a market lot of one NCD.

## Key Points

- Total of 8 series of NCDs issued with varying tenures (2-5 years) and interest payment structures
- Series I: 15,74,640 NCDs with 8.60% annual coupon, maturing January 12, 2028
- Series II: 20,41,505 NCDs with zero coupon (cumulative), maturing January 12, 2028, redemption value Rs. 1,179.4
- Series III: 10,87,272 NCDs with 8.48% quarterly coupon, maturing January 12, 2029
- Series IV: 39,63,588 NCDs with 8.75% annual coupon, maturing January 12, 2029
- Series V: 1,99,225 NCDs with zero coupon (cumulative), maturing January 12, 2029, redemption value Rs. 1,286.45
- Series VI: 5,09,630 NCDs with 8.62% quarterly coupon, maturing January 12, 2031
- Series VII: 2,91,160 NCDs with 8.90% annual coupon, maturing January 12, 2031
- Series VIII: 3,32,980 NCDs with zero coupon (cumulative), maturing January 12, 2031, redemption value Rs. 1,531.95
- Date of allotment: January 12, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 1,000 per NCD across all series
- All debentures are secured and will trade in Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies listing in pursuance of existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading in these NCDs shall be in compulsory demat mode for all investors
- Securities identified by designated codes only for trading purposes
- Trading in specified market lot of one NCD
- Compliance Officer: Jatin Jalundhwala
- Registered Office: Adani Corporate House, Shantigram, Near Vaishno Devi Circle, S. G. Highway, Khodiyar, Ahmedabad 382 421, Gujarat

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 12, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: January 13, 2026
- Date of Allotment: January 12, 2026
- First Interest Payment Dates:
  - Series I, IV, VII: January 12, 2027
  - Series III, VI: April 12, 2026
  - Series II, V, VIII: NA (cumulative, paid at maturity)
- Maturity Dates:
  - Series I, II: January 12, 2028
  - Series III, IV, V: January 12, 2029
  - Series VI, VII, VIII: January 12, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This represents a substantial debt capital raising by Adani Enterprises Limited through public issuance of NCDs across multiple series with aggregate value exceeding Rs. 1,000 crores. The staggered maturity profile (2028, 2029, 2031) and variety of coupon structures (annual, quarterly, cumulative) provide investors with diverse options. The AA- credit rating indicates adequate safety with moderate credit risk. The listing enhances liquidity for NCD holders and provides retail investors access to corporate debt instruments. The different series cater to varying investor preferences for interest payment frequency and investment horizon.
