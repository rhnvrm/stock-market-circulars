## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members that Gujarat Gas Limited will undergo a corporate name change to Gujarat Energy Limited. Alongside the name change, the company's trading symbol on NSE will be updated from GUJGASLTD to GUJENERGY. Both changes take effect from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Gujarat Gas Limited → New name: Gujarat Energy Limited
- Existing NSE symbol: GUJGASLTD → New symbol: GUJENERGY
- Effective date: July 1, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Ref No: NSE/CML/74850, Circular Ref No: 1009/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The Exchange is formally notifying members of the updated name and symbol as required under NSE listing regulations. The change is initiated by the listed company and approved through the applicable corporate and regulatory process.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their systems, order management platforms, and client-facing interfaces to reflect the new symbol GUJENERGY from July 1, 2026.
- References to GUJGASLTD in watchlists, algorithms, and reports should be updated prior to the effective date to avoid disruption.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date for name change (Gujarat Gas Limited → Gujarat Energy Limited) and symbol change (GUJGASLTD → GUJENERGY)

## Impact Assessment

This change has a medium operational impact on traders, brokers, and portfolio management systems that reference the stock by its current symbol GUJGASLTD. Automated trading systems, screeners, and analytics tools will need to be updated. There is no change to the underlying ISIN or shareholding structure, so the economic impact on existing shareholders is minimal. Market participants should ensure all downstream references are updated before market open on July 1, 2026.
