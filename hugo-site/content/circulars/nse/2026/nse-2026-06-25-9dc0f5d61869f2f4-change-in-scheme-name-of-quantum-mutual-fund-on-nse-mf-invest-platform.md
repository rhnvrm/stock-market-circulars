## Summary

Quantum Asset Management Company Private Limited has issued Addendum No. 10/2026 notifying changes to several Quantum Mutual Fund schemes to align with SEBI's Master Circular for Mutual Funds dated March 20, 2026. The changes include scheme name changes, revised investment objectives, and updated scheme type descriptions, all effective June 29, 2026. These changes will be reflected on the NSE MF Invest platform.

## Key Points

- Changes are mandated under SEBI Master Circular for Mutual Funds (March 20, 2026) for categorization and rationalization
- All changes are effective from **June 29, 2026**
- Two schemes are being renamed; multiple schemes are receiving updated investment objectives
- Quantum Dynamic Bond Fund is renamed to Quantum Dynamic Term Fund
- Quantum Gold Fund is renamed to Quantum Gold ETF
- Quantum Value Fund and Quantum ELSS Tax Saver Fund receive revised investment objectives

## Regulatory Changes

| Scheme | Change Type | Existing | Revised |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quantum Value Fund | Investment Objective | Long-term capital appreciation investing in BSE 200 Index companies | Long-term capital appreciation by investing in equity and equity related securities following value investing strategy |
| Quantum ELSS Tax Saver Fund | Type of Scheme | Open-ended Equity Linked Saving Scheme with statutory lock-in of 3 years and tax benefit | Open-Ended Scheme with attributes in accordance with Equity Linked Saving Scheme, 2005 notified by Ministry of Finance |
| Quantum ELSS Tax Saver Fund | Investment Objective | Long-term capital appreciation investing in BSE 200 Index companies | Long-term capital appreciation by investing primarily in equity and equity related securities |
| Quantum Dynamic Bond Fund | Name | Quantum Dynamic Bond Fund | Quantum Dynamic Term Fund |
| Quantum Gold Fund | Name | Quantum Gold Fund | Quantum Gold ETF |
| Quantum Gold Fund | Investment Objective | Returns in line with gold and gold related instruments subject to tracking errors | Returns in line with performance of physical gold in domestic prices, subject to tracking error |

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE MF Invest platform will update scheme names and details accordingly effective June 29, 2026
- Existing investors in the affected schemes should take note of the revised scheme names and investment objectives
- Distributors and advisers should update client-facing documentation to reflect the new scheme names and objectives
- No action is required from investors to continue existing investments or SIPs

## Important Dates

- **Addendum Date:** June 2026 (Addendum No. 10/2026)
- **SEBI Master Circular Reference:** March 20, 2026
- **Effective Date of All Changes:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The changes are primarily administrative and regulatory in nature, driven by SEBI's scheme categorization and rationalization mandate. Existing investors are not required to take any action; however, they should be aware of the revised scheme names when monitoring their portfolios or placing new transactions. The renaming of Quantum Gold Fund to Quantum Gold ETF more precisely reflects the fund's structure. The updated investment objectives across schemes are aligned with SEBI's standardized categorization language and do not represent material changes to the underlying investment strategies.
