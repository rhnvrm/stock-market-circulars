## Summary

NSE has declared SIC Stocks & Services Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000289933) as a defaulter with immediate effect, in line with BSE's declaration of the same member as defaulter vide its circular ref. no. 20260708-25 dated July 08, 2026. The declaration is made under SEBI Circular No. SEBI/MIRSD/Master Cir-04/2010 dated March 17, 2010, and under the Rules, Byelaws & Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- SIC Stocks & Services Private Limited has been declared a defaulter by NSE following a similar declaration by BSE on the same date (July 08, 2026, BSE circular ref. no. 20260708-25).
- The declaration takes immediate effect and is made under the provisions of the Rules, Byelaws & Regulations of NSE and SEBI Circular No. SEBI/MIRSD/Master Cir-04/2010.
- Directors of the firm — Sidharth Handa (PAN: AAOPH2609C), Nupur Handa (PAN: ABDPH0433N), and Rajinder Handa (PAN: AACPH4025Q) — are listed in Annexure A.
- Under sub-rules 4(A) and 5 of Rule 8 of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 (SCRR), these individuals may be disqualified from holding director/partner positions in any company/firm that is a stock exchange member, due to their prior association with a defaulter member.
- All trading members are directed to comply with the relevant provisions of Chapter IV of the NSEIL Rules regarding transactions with SIC Stocks & Services Private Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is an enforcement action applying existing SEBI and NSE member-default provisions to a specific trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with Chapter IV of the NSEIL Rules in respect of any transactions with SIC Stocks & Services Private Limited.
- Members and investors should take note of the defaulter status and the associated director disqualification implications under SCRR Rule 8.

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2026: BSE declaration of default (circular ref. no. 20260708-25).
- July 08, 2026: NSE declaration of default, effective immediately (Download Ref No. NSE/COMP/75118, Circular Ref. No. 65/2026).

## Impact Assessment

The circular has limited direct market impact but is significant for counterparties, clients, and creditors of SIC Stocks & Services Private Limited, who must cease normal dealings with the defaulting member per exchange rules. It also carries governance implications, as the listed directors may face disqualification from serving as directors/partners in other exchange-member entities, which could affect their broader involvement in the securities industry.
