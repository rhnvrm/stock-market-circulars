## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (Circular Ref. No. 064/2026, Download Ref No. NCL/CMPT/74398) has issued instructions for the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts in LIFE INSURANCE CORPORATION OF INDIA (LICI) on account of a Bonus Issue in the ratio of 1:1. The adjustment factor is 2 and the ex-date is May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus issue ratio: 1:1 (adjustment factor = 2)
- Ex-date for corporate action: May 29, 2026
- Contracts expiring on May 27, 2026 will NOT be adjusted
- All open futures and options positions in LICI existing after End of Day on May 27, 2026 will be adjusted
- Adjusted market lot is as per Circular No. 69/2026 (Download No. 74367) dated May 22, 2026
- Daily MTM settlement of LICI futures on May 27, 2026 will proceed normally before adjustments are applied

## Regulatory Changes

In pursuance of NSE Clearing Byelaws on Clearing and Settlement and SEBI circular SMDRP/DC/CIR-8/01 dated June 21, 2001, NSE Clearing is implementing contract adjustments for all LICI derivatives positions. This circular supersedes or supplements Circular No. 50 (NCL/CMPT/73997) dated April 30, 2026 and Circular No. 69/2026 (Download No. 74367) dated May 22, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must note that LICI F&O positions open after EOD May 27, 2026 will be automatically adjusted by NSE Clearing
- No manual action is required for the adjustment itself, but members should inform their clients of the contract adjustments
- Begin-of-day margins on May 29, 2026 will be computed based on the adjusted carry-forward value
- From May 29, 2026, daily MTM settlement resumes under normal procedures

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Last trading day for unadjusted contracts; MTM settlement based on daily settlement price; positions expiring on this date are NOT adjusted
- **May 27, 2026 (EOD)**: Cutoff for determining positions to be adjusted
- **May 29, 2026**: Ex-date for the bonus issue; adjusted contracts begin trading; BOD margins computed on adjusted carry-forward values

## Impact Assessment

All market participants holding open futures or options positions in LICI beyond May 27, 2026 will have their positions adjusted. The futures price adjustment divides the pre-adjustment settlement price by the adjustment factor (2), halving the price to reflect the doubled share count from the bonus issue. To avoid rounding discrepancies, the carry-forward value is computed by multiplying pre-adjusted quantity by pre-adjusted settlement price, ensuring no monetary gain or loss purely from the mechanical adjustment. Members and clients should review their LICI F&O exposure ahead of May 27, 2026 to understand the effect on margin requirements and position sizes.
