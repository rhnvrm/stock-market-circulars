## Summary

Mirae Asset Silver ETF FOF (Fund of Fund) of Mirae Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 25, 2026. The facility includes SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) options across six scheme variants.

## Key Points

- Mirae Asset Silver ETF FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 25, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform for all members
- Transactions supported: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants available across Direct and Regular plans
- All six schemes are categorized as Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding platform availability of an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members may facilitate client transactions in the listed Mirae Asset Silver ETF FOF schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 25, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 25, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Mirae Asset Silver ETF FOF for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This circular enables retail and institutional investors to transact in Mirae Asset Silver ETF FOF via the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investors access to silver commodity exposure through a fund-of-fund structure. No changes to trading rules, fees, or compliance obligations are introduced. Members may see incremental transaction flow in these six scheme variants.
