## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73029, Circular Ref. No: 0335/2026) informing members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited on the Exchange under the IPO category. The specific listing date has not been announced yet and will be communicated via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shree Ram Twistex Limited
- Trading Symbol: SRTL
- ISIN: INE19GK01015
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification circular issued by NSE's Listing Department.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are informed to take note of the forthcoming listing of Shree Ram Twistex Limited (SRTL) and prepare accordingly for trading once the listing date is confirmed via a subsequent circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals the imminent listing of Shree Ram Twistex Limited on NSE. Market participants and brokers should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the exact listing date. The listing will make SRTL shares available for trading on NSE, providing liquidity to IPO investors. Impact is moderate and confined to investors who participated in the IPO and brokers handling post-listing activity.
