## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of C K K Retail Mart Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective February 06, 2026. The security will trade under symbol CKKRETAIL in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment). The IPO consists of 19,368,000 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 10/- issued at Rs. 163/- per share.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: CKKRETAIL
- ISIN: INE0SMX01019
- Face value: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 163/- per share
- Total shares: 19,368,000 equity shares
- Market lot size: 800 equity shares
- Initial trading series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W')
- Market Maker: SVCM Securities Private Limited
- Maximum permissible spread: Within 10%
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Available for continuous market trading as per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for SME IPO under existing SEBI and NSE regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (800 equity shares)
- Security will be part of pre-open session for IPO category
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I apply
- Pari Passu: Yes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Listing Date**: February 06, 2026
- Financial Year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine SME IPO listing on the EMERGE platform. The security will initially trade under surveillance measures (Trade for Trade segment) before moving to normal rolling settlement. The Rs. 163/- issue price and 800-share market lot indicate this is targeted at retail investors. Market maker arrangement with 10% spread provides liquidity support. Lock-in provisions apply as per standard IPO norms.
