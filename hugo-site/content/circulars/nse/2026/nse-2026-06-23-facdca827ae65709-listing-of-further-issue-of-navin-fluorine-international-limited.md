## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 860 additional equity shares of Navin Fluorine International Limited (Symbol: NAVINFLUOR, Series: EQ) effective June 24, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE048G01026.

## Key Points

- 860 equity shares of Navin Fluorine International Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from June 24, 2026
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Pari passu with existing equity shares: Yes
- Lock-in: Not applicable
- Shares distributed across 18 distinctive number ranges
- Temporary ISIN IN8048G01025 used for dematerialised credit per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012; permanent ISIN is INE048G01026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission of securities to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security only by its designated codes (Symbol: NAVINFLUOR, Series: EQ)
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 1
- Members should note the temporary ISIN (IN8048G01025) is used for dematerialised credits pending activation of permanent ISIN (INE048G01026)

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 23, 2026
- Effective date for trading: June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 860 shares is negligible relative to Navin Fluorine International Limited's total equity base. This is a routine administrative listing arising from conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status. Existing shareholders and traders are unaffected in terms of price discovery or liquidity.
