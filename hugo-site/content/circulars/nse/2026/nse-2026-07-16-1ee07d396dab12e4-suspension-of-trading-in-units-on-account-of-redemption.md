## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in units of four Aditya Birla Sun Life Fixed Term Plan (FTP) - Series TJ (1838 days) schemes, effective from July 24, 2026, on account of redemption. This action is taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Four schemes of Aditya Birla Sun Life FTP - Series TJ (1838 days) are being suspended from trading due to redemption.
- Affected schemes/symbols: ABSLFTTJRG (ISIN INF209KB14A8) - RP-Growth; ABSLFTTJRN (ISIN INF209KB15A5) - RP-IDCW; ABSLFTTJDG (ISIN INF209KB16A3) - DP-Growth; ABSLFTTJDN (ISIN INF209KB17A1) - DP-IDCW.
- Reason for suspension across all four schemes is redemption of the fixed term plan.
- The circular is issued by NSE's Listing Department, referencing Circular Ref. No. 1156/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants must cease trading in the specified units from the suspension date and ensure no orders are placed in these securities after the last trading day.

## Important Dates

- Last day of trading (closing hours): July 23, 2026
- Suspension effective date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to unit holders of these specific Aditya Birla Sun Life FTP Series TJ (1838 days) schemes, which are closed-end fixed term plans reaching redemption. There is no broader market impact as this pertains to a niche fixed maturity mutual fund product rather than listed equity securities.
