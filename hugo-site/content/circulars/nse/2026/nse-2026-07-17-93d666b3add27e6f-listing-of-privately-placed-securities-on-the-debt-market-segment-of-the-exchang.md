## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on its Debt Market Segment effective July 17, 2026, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The listing covers two fresh commercial paper issuances by Godrej Properties Limited and Godrej Industries Limited, and a re-issue of secured non-convertible debentures by Muthoot Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Fresh Issue - Commercial Paper:
  - Godrej Properties Limited: GPL CP 19/08/26 Sr 59, ISIN INE484J14E98, 3,000 securities, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,96,765, allotted 16-Jul-2026, redemption 19-Aug-2026
  - Godrej Industries Limited: GIL CP 15/10/26 Sr 41, ISIN INE233A149L3, 500 securities, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,91,484, allotted 16-Jul-2026, redemption 15-Oct-2026
- Re-issue - Other Debt Securities:
  - Muthoot Finance Limited: MUTF 8.78% 2029 debentures, ISIN INE414G07JJ1 (temporary ISIN under SEBI circular dated October 05, 2022), 50,000 securities, face value ₹1,00,000, coupon rate 8.78%, allotted 15-Jul-2026, redemption 04-Oct-2029, multiple issue prices ranging around ₹1,07,785.0616 per unit across tranches

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations admitting the listed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from members beyond noting the admission of these securities to the Debt Market Segment for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (Commercial Papers): July 16, 2026
- Date of Allotment (Muthoot Finance NCD): July 15, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 17, 2026
- Redemption - GPL CP: August 19, 2026
- Redemption - GIL CP: October 15, 2026
- Redemption - Muthoot Finance NCD: October 4, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification with limited market-wide impact. It formalizes trading access for privately placed debt instruments (commercial papers and NCDs) issued by Godrej Properties, Godrej Industries, and Muthoot Finance on NSE's Debt Market Segment, primarily relevant to institutional debt market participants and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.
