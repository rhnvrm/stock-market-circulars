## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Summit Equity Long-Short Fund NFO, launched under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework by Invesco Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 2, 2026 to July 16, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular lists six scheme variants (Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) along with their respective ISINs, all falling under the Equity category, with a common allotment date of July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 1461/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74962, dated July 01, 2026
- Scheme: Summit Equity Long-Short Fund NFO under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) of Invesco Mutual Fund
- Six scheme variants available:
  - Regular-Growth (ISIN: INF205K30014)
  - Regular-IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF205K30022)
  - Regular-IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF205K30030)
  - Direct-Growth (ISIN: INF205K30048)
  - Direct-IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF205K30055)
  - Direct-IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF205K30063)
- All schemes categorized as Equity
- Allotment date for all schemes: July 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational circular under the existing NSE MF Invest Platform framework enabling transactions in a new SIF scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 16, 2026, till 12:00 pm.
- Members must facilitate transactions in the NFO strictly within the specified subscription window.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 02, 2026 to July 16, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Fund pay-in deadline: July 16, 2026, till 12:00 pm
- Allotment date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to operational enablement of a single mutual fund NFO for transaction processing on NSE's MF platform. It is relevant primarily to NSE members, distributors, and investors interested in subscribing to Invesco's new Specialized Investment Fund scheme, with no direct effect on listed equities or broader trading activity.
