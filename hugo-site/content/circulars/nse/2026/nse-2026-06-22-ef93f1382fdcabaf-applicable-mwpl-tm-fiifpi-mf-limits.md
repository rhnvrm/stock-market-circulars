## Summary

NSE has published the updated Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) and corresponding trading position limits applicable to Trading Members (TM), FPI Category I, FPI Category II, and Mutual Funds (MF) for all stocks eligible for trading in the Futures & Options (F&O) segment. These limits govern the maximum permissible open interest that various participant categories can hold in individual stock derivatives.

## Key Points

- MWPL specifies the total market-wide open interest cap (in number of shares) for each F&O stock.
- Overall Stock Limit for Clients is set at approximately 10% of MWPL.
- Overall Stock Limit for Proprietary trading is set at approximately 20% of MWPL.
- TM (Client + Proprietary), FPI Category I, and MF combined limit is approximately 30% of MWPL.
- FPI Category II (excluding individuals, family offices, and corporates) limit is approximately 20% of MWPL.
- FPI Category II (individuals, family offices, and corporates) limit is approximately 10% of MWPL.
- Limits are published as absolute share counts derived from the applicable percentage of each stock's MWPL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a periodic recalculation and publication of position limits based on current share capital and MWPL methodology as prescribed by SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure client and proprietary positions in individual stock F&O contracts do not exceed the specified limits.
- FPIs must monitor their open interest to stay within the applicable Category I or Category II thresholds.
- Mutual Funds must ensure their combined F&O positions remain within prescribed limits.
- Breaches of MWPL at the market level trigger additional restrictions on new position creation under SEBI guidelines.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: 2026-06-22 (date of circular issuance; limits apply to current F&O positions and new trades).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with medium market impact. Participants with large existing positions in high-concentration F&O stocks must verify compliance against the updated limits. Stocks with reduced MWPL (due to changes in free-float or share capital) may see forced position unwinding. The update covers a broad universe of F&O stocks including large-caps such as AXISBANK, BHARTIARTL, BAJFINANCE, ADANIPORTS, BPCL, BEL, and CANBK, among others.
