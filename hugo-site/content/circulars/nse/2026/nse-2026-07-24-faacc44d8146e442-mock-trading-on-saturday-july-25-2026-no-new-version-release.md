## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026. This mock session does not involve release of any new NEAT+ version; the existing NEAT+ EG Version 1.0.0 will be used. The circular provides the detailed session schedule, login parameters, and market data broadcast parameters for members to test their systems.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 25, 2026 in the EGR segment.
- No new NEAT+ version release; existing NEAT+ EG Version 1.0.0 continues to apply.
- Session includes trading from both the Primary Site (Trading Session-1) and DR Site (Trading Session-2).
- Trades executed during the mock session will not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out.
- Default password for mock session login is "Neat@EG1".
- Live re-login window provided in the evening (19:00–19:30 Hrs) for members.
- Detailed annexures cover login parameters, market data broadcast parameters, and installation procedures for the EGR trading system.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational circular for a scheduled mock trading exercise in the EGR segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure their trading systems are configured per the login and broadcast parameters in Annexures 2 and 3.
- Members should refer to Annexure 1 for general mock trading instructions and Annexure 4 for installation procedures (if applicable).
- Members must complete live re-login between 19:00 and 19:30 Hrs on the mock date.
- For queries, members can contact NSE's toll-free number (1800-266-0050, Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Date: Saturday, July 25, 2026
- Trading Session-1 (Primary Site): Pre-open 13:00–13:08 Hrs, Normal Market open 13:15 Hrs, Normal Market close 15:15 Hrs
- Block Deal Window: 14:05–14:20 Hrs
- Auction Market: 14:00–14:45 Hrs
- Trading Session-2 (DR Site): Pre-open 16:00–16:08 Hrs, Normal Market open 16:15 Hrs, Normal Market close 17:00 Hrs
- Trade modification end time: 17:05 Hrs
- Live Re-login window: 19:00–19:30 Hrs

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a scheduled, non-financial mock trading exercise in the EGR segment. Trades executed during the session carry no financial obligation. The impact is limited to members' technical and operational preparedness for system testing, including a switchover test to the DR site.
