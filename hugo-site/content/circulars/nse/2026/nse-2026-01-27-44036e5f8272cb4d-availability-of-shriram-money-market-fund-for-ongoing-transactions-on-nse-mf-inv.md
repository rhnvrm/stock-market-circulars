## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Shriram Money Market Fund from Shriram Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The facility will include SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) options.

## Key Points

- Shriram Money Market Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from January 28, 2026
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01489) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01471)
- Subscriptions and redemptions will be enabled
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Both schemes are categorized under Equity (note: this appears to be an error in the circular as money market funds are typically debt instruments)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding fund availability.

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: Circular issue date
- **January 28, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Shriram Money Market Fund for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact and is relevant primarily to investors interested in money market funds through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investment opportunities in the Shriram Money Market Fund with various transaction facilities. No impact on stock trading or broader market operations.
