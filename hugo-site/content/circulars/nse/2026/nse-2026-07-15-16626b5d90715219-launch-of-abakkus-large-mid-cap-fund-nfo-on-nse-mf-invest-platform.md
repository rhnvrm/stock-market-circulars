## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Abakkus Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO, launched under Abakkus Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The scheme is offered in Direct Growth and Regular Growth options, both categorized as Equity schemes, with allotment scheduled for August 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Abakkus Large & Mid Cap Fund (Direct Growth - ISIN INF2JJD01193; Regular Growth - ISIN INF2JJD01201)
- Category: Equity
- NFO subscription window: July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026, till 11:59 pm
- Allotment date: August 4, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform (NMF TM Segment)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational circular enabling transaction of a newly launched NFO scheme on NSE's mutual fund distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm, to facilitate timely settlement and allotment of units.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: July 15, 2026
- NFO Close: July 29, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Clear Funds Deadline: August 3, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Allotment Date: August 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact and is primarily operational in nature, informing NSE members and mutual fund distributors of the availability of a new equity scheme for subscription. It enables retail participation in the Abakkus Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO through the NSE MF Invest Platform but does not affect listed equity trading, indices, or existing securities.
