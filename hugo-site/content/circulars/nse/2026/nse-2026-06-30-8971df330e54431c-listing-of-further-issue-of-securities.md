## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for 21 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective July 1, 2026. The allotments span multiple corporate action types including ESOP grants, warrant conversions, and preferential allotments.

## Key Points

- Securities of 21 companies admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from July 1, 2026
- Aarti Industries Limited (AARTIIND): 78,641 equity shares of Rs. 5 each allotted under ESOP (allotment date: June 24, 2026; distinctive numbers: 386666143–386744783)
- Asian Energy Services Limited (ASIANENE): 3,662,702 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted via conversion of warrants on preferential basis (allotment date: May 5, 2026; lock-in expiry: December 31, 2026; issue price: Rs. 335)
- Balrampur Chini Mills Limited (BALRAMCHIN): 9,316,771 equity shares of Re. 1 each allotted on preferential basis (allotment date: June 3, 2026; issue price: Rs. 483)
- Other companies listed include BLACKBUCK LIMITED, CREDITACCESS GRAMEEN LIMITED, Emcure Pharmaceuticals, Entero Healthcare Solutions, The Federal Bank, Happy Forgings, Intellect Design Arena, JSW Infrastructure, Karur Vysya Bank, Laurus Labs, Lokesh Machines, One Mobikwik Systems, Moschip Technologies, Ravindra Energy, Sai Life Sciences, SBI Cards and Payment Services, SPML Infra, and UFO Moviez India
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares of the respective companies
- Each security is identified by its designated NSE code; trading in specified lot sizes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Notification is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading the newly listed securities
- Trading in these securities must be conducted in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure
- Lock-in restrictions apply where indicated (e.g., Asian Energy Services lock-in expiry: December 31, 2026)

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 30, 2026
- Effective date: July 1, 2026
- Aarti Industries ESOP allotment date: June 24, 2026
- Asian Energy Services warrant conversion allotment date: May 5, 2026; lock-in expiry: December 31, 2026
- Balrampur Chini Mills preferential allotment date: June 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine further listing circular. The newly admitted shares will be available for trading from July 1, 2026, increasing the free float for the respective companies. The ESOP allotment for Aarti Industries is relatively small (78,641 shares). The preferential allotment for Balrampur Chini Mills (9.3 million shares at Rs. 483) and warrant conversion for Asian Energy Services (3.66 million shares) are more substantial and may have minor dilution impact. The Asian Energy Services shares carry a lock-in until December 31, 2026, limiting near-term tradeable supply. No systemic market risk is introduced.
