## Summary

NSE has announced the transfer of Visesh Infotecnics Limited (symbol: VISESHINFO) to the Dissemination Board of the Exchange with effect from April 15, 2026, following its compulsory delisting under Regulation 22(6) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2009. This action is in continuation of NSE circular NSE/CML/73566 dated April 02, 2026, which notified the formal delisting. Once on the Dissemination Board, the securities will remain available for buying and selling in a restricted capacity.

## Key Points

- Visesh Infotecnics Limited (VISESHINFO) has been compulsorily delisted from NSE under SEBI Delisting Regulations 2009
- SEBI has directed NSE to move the company to the Dissemination Board
- Trading on the Dissemination Board will be available from April 15, 2026
- This is a continuation of circular NSE/CML/73566 dated April 02, 2026
- Securities on the Dissemination Board allow existing shareholders a limited exit mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting is governed by Regulation 22(6) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2009, which mandates that compulsorily delisted securities be made available on the Dissemination Board to provide shareholders an opportunity to exit their positions. The Dissemination Board is a platform maintained by exchanges specifically for securities of compulsorily delisted companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are informed of the migration and must update their systems accordingly
- Members facilitating trades in VISESHINFO must route orders through the Dissemination Board segment effective April 15, 2026
- Normal exchange trading in VISESHINFO on the main board will cease; all transactions must occur via the Dissemination Board

## Important Dates

- **April 02, 2026**: Original delisting circular NSE/CML/73566 issued
- **April 13, 2026**: Current circular (NSE/LIST/C/2026/0441) issued announcing Dissemination Board migration
- **April 15, 2026**: Effective date — Visesh Infotecnics Limited moved to Dissemination Board; buying and selling becomes available on the new platform

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Visesh Infotecnics Limited are directly impacted as the stock will no longer trade on the main NSE board. The Dissemination Board provides a last-resort liquidity window, though volumes and price discovery are typically limited compared to the main exchange. Investors holding VISESHINFO shares should be aware that the Dissemination Board operates under different conditions and that liquidity may be significantly constrained. The compulsory delisting signals serious non-compliance by the company with listing obligations, and investors should exercise caution and consider their exit options promptly.
