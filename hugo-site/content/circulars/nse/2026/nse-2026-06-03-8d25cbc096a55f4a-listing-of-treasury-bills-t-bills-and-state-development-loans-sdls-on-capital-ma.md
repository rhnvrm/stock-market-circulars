## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 9 government securities — 8 State Development Loans (SDLs) and 1 GOI 91-day Treasury Bill — on the Capital Market segment, effective June 05, 2026. This is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. All securities carry a standard lot size of 100 units.

## Key Points

- 9 securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective June 05, 2026
- 8 SDLs covering states: Rajasthan (RJ), Gujarat (GJ), Tamil Nadu (TN), Jammu & Kashmir (JK), and Andhra Pradesh (AP)
- 1 GOI 91-day Treasury Bill maturing September 03, 2026 (Symbol: 91D030926)
- All securities carry a uniform lot size of 100
- SDL coupon rates range from 7.37% (RJ 2030) to 7.91% (JK 2046)
- SDL maturities range from 2030 to 2046; T-bill matures September 2026
- Trading to be conducted using designated security codes as specified in the Annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is a routine admission notification under Regulation 3.1.1 (securities admitted to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (symbols) for trading these securities on the NSE system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 100 units for all 9 securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 05, 2026
- **T-bill Maturity (91D030926):** September 03, 2026
- **SDL Maturities:** 2030 (RJ), 2033 (TN), 2036 (TN, GJ), 2038 (AP), 2041 (GJ, TN), 2046 (JK)
