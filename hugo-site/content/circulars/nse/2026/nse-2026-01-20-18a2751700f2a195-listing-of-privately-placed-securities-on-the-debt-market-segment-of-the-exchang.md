## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective January 20, 2026. The listing includes six commercial papers (CPs) totaling Rs 25,250 crore face value from major corporates and three debenture issues totaling Rs 40,800 crore face value.

## Key Points

- Total of 6 commercial papers and 3 debenture issues listed
- Commercial papers have maturity ranging from 16 days to 365 days
- Debentures have long-term tenure of 10-20 years
- All securities allotted on January 19, 2026 (except Muthoot Finance on January 16, 2026)
- Listing pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment as per Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations
- Designated security codes assigned as specified in the annexure
- No specific compliance actions required from members

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 16, 2026 (Muthoot Finance); January 19, 2026 (all others)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine listing of privately placed debt securities with no impact on equity markets or trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Securities now available for trading on NSE Debt Market Segment with assigned security codes.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional investment opportunities in debt instruments for institutional and qualified investors in the secondary market.

**Liquidity**: Limited secondary market liquidity expected as these are privately placed securities primarily held until maturity.
