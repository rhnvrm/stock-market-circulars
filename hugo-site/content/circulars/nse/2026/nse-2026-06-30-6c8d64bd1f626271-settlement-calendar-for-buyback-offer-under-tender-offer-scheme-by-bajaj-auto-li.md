## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has announced the settlement calendar for the Bajaj Auto Limited buyback offer under the Tender Offer scheme, referencing earlier circulars dated April 30, 2026 and June 30, 2026. Members, custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the specified trade and settlement dates and follow early pay-in procedures for demat shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X, Settlement No. 2026079 has been assigned to this buyback offer.
- Trade start date: July 1, 2026; Trade end date: July 7, 2026.
- Obligation download date: July 13, 2026; Settlement date: July 14, 2026.
- Demat shares must be tendered exclusively via Early Pay-in of Shares through depository mechanisms under Market Type Buyback.
- Early pay-in of shares on trade end date must be completed well in advance of 03:30 PM for order acceptance.
- Securities and funds pay-out will be made only after receipt of funds pay-in on the settlement day.
- A Tender Offer Acceptance Report will be provided to members detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders with rejection reason codes.
- STT (Securities Transaction Tax) will be collected on the respective settlement day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the previously announced buyback offer by Bajaj Auto Limited under the Tender Offer scheme (ref. circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74937 dated June 30, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must tender demat shares only through the Early Pay-in mechanism of the depositories for Market Type Buyback.
- Early pay-in of shares on the trade end date (July 7, 2026) must be completed before 03:30 PM.
- Buying trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in on the settlement day (July 14, 2026).
- Members should refer to the scheme document for additional details.
- For queries, members must contact the help desk: Toll Free 1800 266 00 50 (Option 2) or ncl_tenderoffer@nsccl.co.in.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | July 1, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | July 7, 2026 |
| Obligation Download Date | July 13, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 14, 2026 |
| Early Pay-in Deadline (Trade End Day) | July 7, 2026, 03:30 PM |

Note: The Clearing Corporation may notify a revised settlement schedule in case of delays in acceptance details from RTA or delays in funds pay-in by buying trading members.

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Bajaj Auto Limited buyback tender offer. The operational requirements—particularly the early pay-in mechanism and the 03:30 PM deadline on the trade end date—require timely action from participants. The inclusion of Annexure I (20 rejection reason codes) indicates a structured process for handling partial or non-acceptance of orders, reducing ambiguity for participants. Overall market impact is limited to parties directly involved in the buyback offer.
