## Summary

NSE has notified admission of the Rights Entitlements (REs) of Ganga Forging Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 10, 2026. The REs will trade under the symbol GANGAFO-RE, series BE, ISIN INE691Z20015, in the Trade to Trade segment in compulsory demat mode for all investors.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements of Ganga Forging Limited admitted for trading under symbol GANGAFO-RE (display name: Ganga Forging Ltd-RE).
- Series: BE; ISIN: INE691Z20015; Market lot: 1.
- Total Rights Entitlements available: 202,196,727.
- REs will trade in the Trade to Trade segment under compulsory demat for all investors.
- Trading members must inform clients that REs are not to be mistaken for ordinary equity shares.
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must carry a disclaimer that buying REs only confers the right to apply for the rights issue or renounce the entitlement before issue closure.
- Unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse and be extinguished after the Issue Closing Date.

## Regulatory Changes

Admission of the security is made under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, with lot size specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of the same regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must notify all clients of the commencement of RE trading and clarify the ISIN pertains to REs, not ordinary shares.
- Trading members must include a prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases regarding the nature and lapse conditions of REs.

## Important Dates

- Renunciation trading period: July 10, 2026 to July 15, 2026
- Date of suspension of RE trading: July 16, 2026
- Circular effective from: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular facilitates renunciation trading for shareholders of Ganga Forging Limited who wish to sell or transfer their rights entitlements ahead of the rights issue subscription deadline. The impact is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of Ganga Forging Limited and market participants trading in REs during the short renunciation window; there is no broader market-wide effect.
