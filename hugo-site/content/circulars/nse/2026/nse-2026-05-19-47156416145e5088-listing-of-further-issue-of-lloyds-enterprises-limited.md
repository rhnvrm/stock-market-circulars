## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 6,094 additional equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENT) on the Capital Market segment, effective May 20, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE080I01025.

## Key Points

- 6,094 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Shares represent conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Trading symbol: LLOYDSENT, Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE080I01025 (Face Value: Re. 1/-, Paid-up Value: Re. 1/-)
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers range: 1500434039 to 1500440132
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (LLOYDSENT) and series (EQ) for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- Shares were temporarily credited under ISIN IN8080I01024 (temporary ISIN) in dematerialized mode per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012; the permanent ISIN INE080I01025 is now activated

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 19, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 6,094 shares is a small incremental increase to the outstanding share capital of Lloyds Enterprises Limited. The conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up status is a routine corporate action. Existing shareholders are not affected beyond the pari passu ranking of these new shares.
