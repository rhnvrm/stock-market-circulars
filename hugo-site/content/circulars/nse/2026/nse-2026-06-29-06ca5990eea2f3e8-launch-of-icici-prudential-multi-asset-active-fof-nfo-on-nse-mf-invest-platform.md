## Summary

NSE has informed its members that ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF, a fund-of-funds scheme from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, will be open for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 30, 2026 to July 14, 2026 (till 11:30 PM). Allotment is scheduled for July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO window: June 30, 2026 to July 14, 2026 (till 11:30 PM)
- Two plan variants available: Regular Growth (ISIN: INF109K1A542) and Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF109K1A559)
- Both plans are classified under the Equity category
- Allotment date: July 17, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 16, 2026 till 11:59 AM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product launch notification for a new mutual fund NFO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 16, 2026 by 11:59 AM.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Open Date:** June 30, 2026
- **NFO Close Date:** July 14, 2026 (till 11:30 PM)
- **Fund Transfer Deadline:** July 16, 2026 (till 11:59 AM)
- **Allotment Date:** July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant primarily to NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in a multi-asset active fund-of-funds strategy from ICICI Prudential can subscribe during the NFO window via the platform.
