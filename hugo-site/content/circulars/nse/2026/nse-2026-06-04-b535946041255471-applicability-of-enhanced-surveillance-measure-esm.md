## Summary

NSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective June 05–08, 2026. Three securities (EFFWA, MAHESHWARI, VIGOR) are newly added to ESM Stage I, one security (FCSSOFT) is escalated from Stage I to Stage II, no securities are moved down from Stage II to Stage I, and no securities are excluded from the framework.

## Key Points

- Three securities newly shortlisted for ESM Stage I effective June 05, 2026: EFFWA (Effwa Infra & Research Limited), MAHESHWARI (Maheshwari Logistics Limited), and VIGOR (Vigor Plast India Limited).
- FCSSOFT (FCS Software Solutions Limited) moves from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective June 05, 2026.
- No securities are moving from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework.
- A minimum 100% margin will apply on all open positions as on June 05, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 08, 2026.
- All newly included/escalated securities shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective June 08, 2026.
- FCSSOFT (Stage II escalation) will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction effective June 05, 2026.
- ESM shortlisting is a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ESM framework established by prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. The update triggers series reclassification from EQ/SM to BE/ST for affected securities and imposes enhanced margin and price band controls consistent with the existing ESM framework rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must collect a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in EFFWA, MAHESHWARI, VIGOR, and FCSSOFT as on June 05, 2026, and on all new positions from June 08, 2026.
- **Trading systems**: Must reflect the series change from EQ/SM to BE/ST for the affected securities effective June 08, 2026.
- **FCSSOFT specifically**: Must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from June 05, 2026.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.

## Impact Assessment

**EFFWA, MAHESHWARI, VIGOR (New ESM Stage I)**: Trading liquidity will be materially reduced as these securities move to the Trade-for-Trade segment, eliminating netting benefits and requiring 100% upfront margin. Retail and institutional participants will face significantly higher capital requirements.

**FCSSOFT (Stage I → Stage II escalation)**: The most restrictive outcome — trading shifts to Periodic Call Auction with a tight 2% price band, drastically limiting price discovery and liquidity. This is a significant escalation in surveillance intensity.

**No exclusions**: The absence of any securities being removed from the ESM framework indicates the exchange is tightening surveillance rather than relaxing it in the current review cycle.

**Overall**: Four securities are affected by this circular, all facing increased trading restrictions. Members should update risk and margin systems before June 05, 2026 for the Stage II security and before June 08, 2026 for the Stage I securities.
