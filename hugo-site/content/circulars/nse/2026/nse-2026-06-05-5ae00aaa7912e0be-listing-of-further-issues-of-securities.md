## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 13 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 08, 2026. The securities include allotments under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and a large Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) by Acme Solar Holdings Limited.

## Key Points

- Effective date for trading in newly listed shares: **June 08, 2026**
- Securities governed under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- All newly listed shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable on the allotments disclosed in the annexure
- Market lot for all securities is 1 share
- Companies covered: Aavas Financiers, Acme Solar Holdings, Amber Enterprises India, Ather Energy, Aurum PropTech, Emcure Pharmaceuticals, The Federal Bank, InterGlobe Aviation, Niva Bupa Health Insurance, Ola Electric Mobility, SBI Cards and Payment Services, Suzlon Energy, Vikram Solar

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard listing notification pursuant to existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading in these securities on the NSE system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- No specific compliance action required from members beyond standard trading protocols

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

## Impact Assessment

**Acme Solar Holdings (ACMESOLAR):** Largest allotment in this circular — 100,178,890 equity shares (face value Rs. 2) allotted via QIP at an issue price of Rs. 279.5 per share, with distinctive numbers 605971805 to 706150694. This represents a significant capital raise and will increase the company's free float substantially upon trading commencement.

**Aavas Financiers (AAVAS):** Two separate ESOP tranches totalling 5,039 shares (3,816 + 1,223) allotted on May 26, 2026. Minimal dilution impact.

**Amber Enterprises India (AMBER):** ESOP allotment details partially disclosed; face value Rs. 10 per share.

**Other companies (Ather Energy, Aurum PropTech, Emcure Pharmaceuticals, Federal Bank, InterGlobe Aviation, Niva Bupa, Ola Electric, SBI Cards, Suzlon, Vikram Solar):** Specific allotment details were in the truncated portion of the circular but are routine further issue listings.

Overall market impact is low for most companies given the small ESOP allotments, but moderate for Acme Solar due to the large QIP quantum.
