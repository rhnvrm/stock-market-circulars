## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of Rights Entitlements (REs) of Sumeet Industries Limited (Symbol: SUMEET-RE, Series: BE, ISIN: INE235C20010) for trading on the Capital Market segment effective June 22, 2026. The REs will trade in the Trade to Trade (T2T) segment in compulsory demat mode for all investors, with a renunciation trading window from June 22 to July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: SUMEET-RE; ISIN: INE235C20010; Series: BE
- Total Rights Entitlements available for trading: 168,413,258
- Market lot size: 1
- REs will trade in the Trade to Trade segment in compulsory demat form
- Trading members must prominently inform clients that purchase of REs only confers the right to participate in the rights issue or renounce the REs before issue closes
- REs neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date shall lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, Sumeet Industries Limited's Rights Entitlements are admitted to dealings with designated security codes and specified lot sizes as per the Annexure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of RE trading, clearly stating the ISIN and that REs are not ordinary shares
- Contract notes and bills for purchase of REs must carry a prominent disclaimer explaining that REs only entitle the holder to participate in the rights issue or renounce before closure
- Trading members must ensure clients understand that unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after the Issue Closing Date

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (SUMEET-RE)
- **June 22, 2026 – July 15, 2026**: Trading period for renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **July 16, 2026**: Date of suspension of RE trading

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts existing shareholders of Sumeet Industries Limited who have been allotted Rights Entitlements and secondary market investors looking to buy or sell REs during the renunciation window. With 168.4 million REs in circulation and a market lot of 1, the REs are accessible to retail investors. The Trade to Trade settlement mechanism limits intraday speculation and ensures delivery-based settlement, reducing speculative risk. Investors must act before July 15, 2026 to either subscribe to the rights issue or renounce their entitlements, as any unactioned REs will be extinguished post Issue Closing Date.
