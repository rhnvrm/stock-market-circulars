## Summary

JioBlackRock Mutual Fund is launching the JioBlackRock Low Duration Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The subscription period runs from January 08, 2026 to January 13, 2026 until 6:00 PM, with allotment scheduled for January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme Name: JioBlackRock Low Duration Fund
- ISIN: INF22M001150
- Category: Debt Fund
- Subscription Period: January 08, 2026 to January 13, 2026 (until 6:00 PM)
- Allotment Date: January 19, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/72184, Circular Ref. No: 1103/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 19, 2026 at 8:00 PM
- Members should facilitate transactions for the NFO during the subscription window

## Important Dates

- **January 08, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **January 13, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **January 19, 2026 (8:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **January 19, 2026**: Allotment Date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a standard NFO launch notification. It provides NSE members with information about a new debt fund offering from JioBlackRock Mutual Fund, allowing them to facilitate investor subscriptions through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The low duration debt fund category is a standard mutual fund product with no special market implications.
