## Summary

Groww Mutual Fund is launching the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription window opens May 29, 2026 and closes June 12, 2026 at 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform from May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Fund category: Equity (Index Fund tracking Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index)
- Six scheme variants offered: Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Payout, Regular IDCW Reinvestment
- Allotment date for all variants: June 19, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by June 16, 2026 at 6:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification for members of the NSE NMF TM Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 16, 2026 at 6:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **June 12, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **June 16, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **June 19, 2026**: Allotment date for all scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with limited market impact. The fund provides investors exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index through six plan/option combinations. Members on the NSE MF Invest Platform should ensure timely fund collection to meet the June 16 clearing deadline. No impact on existing securities or trading operations.
