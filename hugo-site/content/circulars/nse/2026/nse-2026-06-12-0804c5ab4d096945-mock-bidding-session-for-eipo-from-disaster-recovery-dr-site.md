## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock bidding session for its eIPO platform from the Disaster Recovery (DR) site on June 13, 2026. Members do not need to make any configuration changes — the same login URL (https://eipo.nseindia.com/) applies. Bids placed during the mock session carry no obligation in terms of funds or securities.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for June 13, 2026 from the DR site
- No connectivity or configuration changes required at the member end
- Login URL remains: https://eipo.nseindia.com/
- Mock symbol: MCKGS (Series: GS) — Mock Govt Security (G-sec type)
- Market lot: 10,000 units at Rs. 100; Normal market hours 11:45–17:00 hrs
- Bids from the mock session will not attract any fund or securities obligations
- Revised announcements, if any, will be published on www.nseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notice for a scheduled DR drill.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate actively to verify connectivity to the DR site
- No system or configuration changes are required at the member end

## Important Dates

- **June 13, 2026**: Mock bidding session from DR site (Normal Market: 11:45–17:00 hrs)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine disaster recovery readiness exercise for the eIPO platform. Active participation helps members confirm their systems can connect to the DR site, but there are no financial obligations or regulatory consequences tied to participation or non-participation.
