## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of a Government of India loan security — 6.94% GOI 2036 — to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective May 13, 2026. The security carries the designated symbol 694GS2036 under series GS and must be traded in lot sizes of 1.

## Key Points

- New government security admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from May 13, 2026
- Security: GOI Loan 6.94% maturing May 11, 2036
- Designated symbol: 694GS2036, Series: GS
- ISIN: IN0020260025
- Lot size: 1
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is effected pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use only the designated code 694GS2036 for system identification and trading of this security
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- No additional compliance action required beyond standard G-Sec trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 11, 2026
- **Effective Date (trading commencement):** May 13, 2026
- **Security Maturity Date:** May 11, 2036

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of a single government security providing an additional fixed-income instrument for NSE Capital Market participants. The 6.94% coupon and 10-year tenor (maturing 2036) may attract institutional investors seeking sovereign debt exposure. No operational disruption expected; members need only update their securities master with the new symbol and ISIN.
