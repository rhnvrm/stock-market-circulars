## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the DYNA SIF Equity Long Short Fund by 360 SIF Mutual will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund house: 360 SIF Mutual
- Fund name: DYNA SIF Equity Long Short Fund
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Effective date: February 27, 2026
- Transactions enabled: Subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, and STP
- Six scheme variants are being made available across Direct and Regular plans
- All six schemes are categorized under the Equity asset class

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding the reopening of an existing Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE using the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of these schemes from February 27, 2026, and ensure their systems are updated to process transactions for the listed ISINs. For queries, members may contact support@nseinvest.com or call the toll-free number 1800 2100 940.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: DYNA SIF Equity Long Short Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, and STP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact. It is relevant only to members and investors transacting through the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to invest in 360 SIF Mutual's equity long-short strategy. The six available schemes cover Direct and Regular plan variants with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options:
