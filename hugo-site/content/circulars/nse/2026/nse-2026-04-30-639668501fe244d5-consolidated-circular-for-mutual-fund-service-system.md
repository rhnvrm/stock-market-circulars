## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued Circular Ref. No. 152/2026 (Download Ref No: NCL/MFSS/74000) on April 30, 2026, consolidating all Mutual Fund Service System (MFSS) circulars issued from April 1, 2025 to March 31, 2026. The circular is structured in three parts: Part A (summary of important circulars), Part B (detailed consolidated circular covering settlement schedules, clearing and settlement procedures, mode of payments, and limitation of liability), and Part C (list of payment aggregators).

## Key Points

- Consolidated circular covering MFSS circulars from April 1, 2025 to March 31, 2026
- Settlement schedule for HLIQUID (liquid fund) subscriptions: market timings 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM, funds cut-off at 1:00 PM on T day, units settled on T day
- Normal subscription settlement: market timings 9:00 AM to 2:30 PM, funds cut-off at 2:30 PM on T day, units on T+1 day
- NFO (Normal/New Fund Offer) subscriptions: market timings 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, funds cut-off at 2:30 PM on T day
- Normal redemptions: market timings 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, cut-off at 4:30 PM on T day, units payout T+1 to T+8 day per AMC scheme master
- Overnight scheme redemptions: market timings 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, cut-off at 7:15 PM on T day, units on T+1 day
- UPI payment standardisation and handle change incorporated from earlier circulars
- Contact for clarifications: mfss_clearing@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

- **Revised Settlement Schedule for Overnight Mutual Fund Schemes** (NCL/MFSS/68163, May 23, 2025): Updated settlement timelines specifically for overnight schemes
- **Standardised UPI IDs for Payment Collection** (NCL/MFSS/68641, June 19, 2025): Adoption of standardised, validated, and exclusive UPI IDs for payment collection by SEBI-registered intermediaries from investors
- **Change in UPI Handle** (NCL/MFSS/70469, September 26, 2025): Updated UPI handle for MFSS transactions
- **Settlement of MF Transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform** (NCL/MFSS/70797, October 14, 2025 and NCL/MFSS/71454, November 25, 2025): Two updates to settlement procedures on the NSE MF Invest Platform

## Compliance Requirements

- All MFSS participants must adhere to the settlement schedules specified in Part B of this consolidated circular
- Participants must use standardised and exclusive UPI IDs as prescribed by SEBI for payment collection from investors
- Redemption payouts must be made in accordance with the scheme master of respective AMCs within the T+1 to T+8 day window
- Participants should refer to Part C for the updated list of approved payment aggregators
- Any changes to timelines will be communicated by the Clearing Corporation via separate circulars

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: Date of issuance of this consolidated circular
- **Coverage period**: April 1, 2025 to March 31, 2026 (circulars consolidated herein)
- **NCL/MFSS/68163** – May 23, 2025: Revised overnight fund settlement schedule
- **NCL/MFSS/68641** – June 19, 2025: UPI standardisation circular
- **NCL/MFSS/70469** – September 26, 2025: UPI handle change
- **NCL/MFSS/70797** – October 14, 2025: NSE MF Invest Platform settlement update
- **NCL/MFSS/71454** – November 25, 2025: NSE MF Invest Platform settlement update

## Impact Assessment

This consolidated circular serves as a comprehensive reference document for all MFSS participants including mutual fund distributors, brokers, and clearing members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The operational impact is moderate — participants must ensure compliance with the specified settlement schedules across all mutual fund categories (liquid, normal, NFO, and overnight schemes). The UPI standardisation requirements affect payment collection processes for SEBI-registered intermediaries. No new extraordinary changes are introduced by this consolidation itself; however, participants who may have missed any of the five key circulars from FY2025-26 should review them via this document. Redemption timelines vary significantly by AMC scheme master (T+1 to T+8), requiring participants to monitor individual fund house guidelines.
