## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has notified the adjusted market lot for DIVINEHIRA (Divine Hira Jewellers Limited) on the SME Platform, effective July 2, 2026, as a result of a 2:1 bonus corporate action. The adjustment factor is 3, and the revised market lot is 1200.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** DIVINEHIRA
- **Company:** Divine Hira Jewellers Limited
- **Corporate Action:** Bonus (2:1 ratio)
- **Adjustment Factor:** 3
- **Adjusted Market Lot:** 1200
- **Ex-date / Effective Date:** July 2, 2026
- **Record Date:** July 2, 2026
- Members must load the updated security files (`security.gz` / `nnf_security.gz` / `MII - Security File.gz`) before trading on the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification to inform trading members of the revised market lot following a bonus corporate action under existing SME Platform rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must update their trading applications with the revised security files prior to the effective date of July 2, 2026.
- Updated files (`security.gz`, `nnf_security.gz`, `MII - Security File.gz`) are available on the Extranet server under `common/NTNEAT` and on the NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

| Circular Date | June 29, 2026 |
| Ex-date / Effective Date | July 2, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME-listed security. Trading members dealing in DIVINEHIRA on the SME Platform must ensure their systems reflect the new market lot of 1200 before trading begins on July 2, 2026. Failure to update security files may cause order rejection or mismatched lot sizes. The adjustment factor of 3 reflects the combined effect of the 2:1 bonus on the pre-existing market lot.
