## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, March 21, 2026. The session covers two trading phases — Session 1 from the Primary Site and Session 2 from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site. NEAT+ version 7.8.7 will be used, with no new version release for this mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, March 21, 2026 in the Capital Market Segment
- Two trading sessions: Session 1 from Primary Site (10:45–14:00) and Session 2 from DR Site (14:45–15:30)
- NEAT+ version 7.8.7 in use; download path: `/common/NEATPlus787` on NSE Extranet
- No new version release for this mock session
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- Trades during mock sessions will not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out
- Members must re-login into the live environment after the mock to ensure connectivity for Monday, March 23, 2026
- NOTIS application will be accessible during the mock session
- Only valid UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed; UCC validation skipped during contingency order entry

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading exercise. Members may use the Mock Trading or Simulated Environment to meet regulatory requirements per circular NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate using all trading software during the mock session
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems before each session
- Members must re-login into the live environment post-mock to ensure readiness for Monday, March 23, 2026
- Refer to Annexure 1 (important instructions), Annexure 2 (prerequisites/general guidelines), and Annexure 3 (DR site switchover guidelines)
- For UCC/PAN queries, contact uci@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **March 21, 2026**: Mock trading session
  - Session 1 (Primary Site): 10:45 – 14:00
  - Session 2 (DR Site): 14:45 – 15:30
  - Closing Session: 15:40 – 15:50
  - Trade Modification End: 16:00
  - Live Re-login Window: 16:30 – 17:00
- **March 23, 2026**: Live trading resumes; members must ensure connectivity

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational exercise for NSE members to test connectivity and trading systems. No financial transactions are processed, and there are no changes to trading rules or software versions. Members who fail to participate risk connectivity issues on the following live trading day (Monday, March 23, 2026).
