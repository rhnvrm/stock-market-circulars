## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the iSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 19, 2026 to June 02, 2026 (till 11:30 PM). Two plan variants are offered — Regular Growth and Direct Growth — both classified under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- Fund: iSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund (SIF) by ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- NFO subscription window: May 19, 2026 to June 02, 2026 (till 11:30 PM)
- Regular Growth ISIN: INF109K30059
- Direct Growth ISIN: INF109K30067
- Both schemes are categorized as Equity
- Allotment date for both plans: June 05, 2026
- Fund contact: support@nseinvest.com | Toll Free: 1800 2100 940

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an operational notification under the NMF TM Segment, informing members of a new Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) NFO availability on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before **June 04, 2026 by 12:00 PM**.
- Members facilitating transactions must process subscriptions within the NFO window ending June 02, 2026 at 11:30 PM.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Deadline |
|---|---|
| NFO Opens | May 19, 2026 |
| NFO Closes | June 02, 2026 (11:30 PM) |
| Clear Funds Deadline | June 04, 2026 (12:00 PM) |
| Allotment Date | June 05, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact on NSE members who participate in the mutual fund investment segment. The NFO introduces a long-short equity strategy under the SIF framework, which may attract investors seeking alternative/hedged equity exposure. Members need to ensure timely fund settlement by June 4, 2026 to facilitate smooth allotment. No systemic or broad market impact is expected.
