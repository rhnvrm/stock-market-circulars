## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has notified all members that due to a settlement holiday on May 28, 2026, multiple Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) settlements have been scheduled for May 29, 2026. Members must adhere to revised pay-in/pay-out timings and updated auction cycle schedules.

## Key Points

- Three settlements are compressed into May 29, 2026 due to the May 28, 2026 settlement holiday
- Settlement 2026007 (Trade Date: May 27): Pay-in at 09:30 hrs, Pay-out at 11:30 hrs
- Settlement 2026008 (Trade Date: May 27): Pay-in at 14:00 hrs, Pay-out at 16:30 hrs
- Settlement 2026009 (Trade Date: May 28): Pay-in at 17:30 hrs, Pay-out at 20:00 hrs
- Depository cut-off timings for pay-in are revised accordingly
- Shortages of settlement 2026009 will be clubbed with settlement 2026010; no separate auction in 2026009

## Regulatory Changes

The auction trading and settlement under the revised T+1 auction cycle is restructured as follows:
- Settlement 2026008 (Trade Date May 27, Settlement May 29): Auction settlement 2026008, Auction Trade Date May 29, Auction Settlement Date June 1
- Settlement 2026009 (Trade Date May 28, Settlement May 29): Auction settlement 2026010, Auction Trade Date June 1, Auction Settlement Date June 2
- Settlement 2026010 (Trade Date May 29, Settlement June 1): No separate auction; shortages merged with 2026010

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the revised pay-in timings for each settlement on May 29, 2026
- Members must note the revised depository cut-off timings for pay-in at depositories
- UNPAID MTF files for EGR Normal must be uploaded within the following windows:
  - Settlement 2026008 (Trade Date May 27, Settlement May 29): 07:30–23:00 on May 28; 07:30–13:00 on May 29
  - Settlement 2026009 (Trade Date May 28, Settlement May 29): 07:30–16:30 on May 29

## Important Dates

- **May 28, 2026**: Settlement holiday; no settlements conducted
- **May 29, 2026**: Multiple settlements (2026007, 2026008, 2026009) scheduled
- **June 1, 2026**: Auction settlement date for settlements related to 2026008 and 2026010
- **June 2, 2026**: Auction settlement date for 2026010

## Impact Assessment

EGR segment members face an operationally intensive day on May 29, 2026 with three back-to-back settlements within a single trading day. The compressed timelines require careful monitoring of pay-in deadlines and depository cut-off times. The merging of 2026009 shortages into 2026010 auction simplifies shortage handling but requires members to plan positions accordingly. This is a one-time adjustment with no broader market-wide impact beyond the EGR segment.
