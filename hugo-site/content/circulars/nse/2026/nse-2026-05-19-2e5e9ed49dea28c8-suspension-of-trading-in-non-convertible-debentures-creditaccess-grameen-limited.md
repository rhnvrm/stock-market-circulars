## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by CreditAccess Grameen Limited, effective May 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of both debenture series, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of CreditAccess Grameen Limited are being suspended from trading on NSE
- Symbol CAGL26 (ISIN: INE741K07538) is suspended due to maturity
- Symbol 925CAGL26 (ISIN: INE741K07595) is suspended due to maturity
- Suspension is effective from May 22, 2026
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74285, Circular Ref. No. 0801/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This action is taken under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in the two specified NCD symbols (CAGL26 and 925CAGL26) from May 22, 2026
- No further trading activity should be initiated in these securities post the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 19, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of the two matured NCD series issued by CreditAccess Grameen Limited. As this is a scheduled maturity event, it represents a routine lifecycle closure of these debt instruments. Investors holding these debentures should expect redemption proceedings per the terms of the NCD issuance. There is no broader negative market signal associated with this action.
