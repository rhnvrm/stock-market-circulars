## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref: NSE/CMTR/74495, Circular No. 72/2026) announcing the Offer for Sale (OFS) of NHPC Limited (Symbol: NHPC, ISIN: INE848E01016) scheduled for June 02-03, 2026. The OFS will be conducted through the NSE eOFS platform with a floor price of Rs. 71/- per share, covering non-retail, retail, and employee categories.

## Key Points

- OFS dates: June 02, 2026 and June 03, 2026
- Floor price: Rs. 71/- per equity share (face value Rs. 10/-)
- Base offer size: Up to 30,13,51,044 equity shares
- Oversubscription option: Up to additional 30,13,51,044 equity shares
- Eligible Employee option: Additionally 45,20,265 equity shares
- Series available: IS (non-retail), RS (retail, bid value ≤ Rs 2 lacs), ES (employee, bid value ≤ Rs 5 lacs)
- Market lot: 1 equity share; Tick size: Re. 0.01
- Platform: NSE eOFS (https://eofs.nseindia.com)
- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate
- OFS conducted under Interoperability framework per NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The OFS is conducted in accordance with the existing Interoperability among Clearing Corporations framework and Exchange Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members not yet registered on eOFS must request Admin user IDs via the ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under path: ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS
- Admin user ID requests processed at 11:00 am, 2:00 pm, and 3:00 pm; requests after 3:00 pm processed next day by 8:45 am; only one request per member allowed
- Members must ensure compliant UCCs for self (PRO) and clients are updated in the UCI system at least one day prior to the OFS bidding session
- UCCs updated or becoming compliant on the day of the OFS session will not be permitted to participate
- UCCs identified as compliant by the Exchange at 22:00 hrs on the previous day are permitted for the next bidding day
- Members must ensure adequate collateral sufficiency before carrying forward orders on T+1 day; orders rejected for insufficient margins cannot be re-placed
- Members to refer to Annexure 3 of NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020 for features and limits settings

## Important Dates

- June 01, 2026: Circular issued; UCC compliance deadline for Day 1 bidding (UCCs must be updated by 22:00 hrs)
- June 02, 2026: Day 1 OFS bidding session (non-retail and retail/employee categories)
- June 02, 2026 (on or before 5:00 pm): Seller to intimate Exchange regarding exercise of oversubscription option
- June 03, 2026: Day 2 OFS bidding session

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant government disinvestment event involving NHPC Limited, a major public sector hydroelectric power company. The combined issue size (base + oversubscription + employee) could reach up to approximately 60.72 crore equity shares at a floor price of Rs. 71/-, implying a potential transaction value exceeding Rs. 4,300 crores. All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate. The three-tier structure (non-retail, retail, employee) broadens participation. Members must act promptly on UCC compliance and eOFS registration to avoid exclusion from bidding sessions.
