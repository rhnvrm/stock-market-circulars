## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has approved the request of Competent Finman Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INZ000221138) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives and Interest Rate Futures (IRF) segment. The surrender was approved on March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Competent Finman Pvt. Ltd. has surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivatives and IRF segment.
- SEBI Registration Number of the member: INZ000221138
- Surrender approval was granted on March 6, 2026.
- All NSE members are requested to take note of this change.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative action reflecting the approved exit of a trading member from specific segments per NSE's membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All existing NSE members are required to note the surrender and update their records accordingly.
- No direct compliance action required from other members beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender is limited to a single trading member (Competent Finman Pvt. Ltd.) exiting the Currency Derivatives and IRF segment. This is a routine administrative action with no effect on broader market operations, securities, or other market participants.
