## Summary

NSE's Compliance department has approved the request of R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd (SEBI Registration No. INZ000211932) to surrender its membership in three trading segments. The surrender was approved on June 9, 2026, and all NSE members have been notified to take note.

## Key Points

- R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd has surrendered membership in the Currency Derivatives & IRF, Futures & Options, and Commodity Derivatives segments.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000211932
- Surrender approval date: June 9, 2026
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/COMP/74690 (Circular Ref. No: 53/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a notification of an approved membership surrender under existing NSE membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the surrender. Members and market participants should update their records to reflect that R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd is no longer an active member in the Currency Derivatives & IRF, Futures & Options, and Commodity Derivatives segments.

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date:** June 9, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to clients and counterparties of R.K. Stockholding (P) Ltd who traded through them in the affected segments. Broader market impact is negligible. Affected clients would need to transfer their positions or accounts to another registered member for continued trading in Currency Derivatives, IRF, Futures & Options, and Commodity Derivatives.
