## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the admission of further equity securities to dealings on the Capital Market segment for 14 companies, effective June 03, 2026. The issuances span ESOP allotments, preferential warrant conversions, and bonus shares.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have new securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from June 03, 2026
- Avalon Technologies (AVALON): 21,534 equity shares (Rs. 2 face value) allotted under ESOP on May 06, 2026; ISIN INE0LCL01028; distinctive numbers 66766164–66787697
- Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions (CLEANMAX): 3,240 equity shares (Re. 1 face value) allotted under ESOP on May 28, 2026; ISIN INE647U01026; distinctive numbers 117083975–117087214
- Gretex Corporate Services (GCSL): 1,519,999 equity shares (Rs. 10 face value) comprising 8,00,000 shares on preferential basis via warrant conversion at Rs. 379 (premium Rs. 369) and 7,19,999 bonus shares (9:10 ratio) allotted January 13, 2026; ISIN INE199P01028; distinctive numbers 22639348–24159346; lock-in applicable as per Annexure A
- Additional companies listed in the circular title (HDB Financial Services, Indegene, Le Travenues Technology, Navin Fluorine International, RBL Bank, Safari Industries, The South Indian Bank, TATA Consumer Products, Tube Investments of India, Vaibhav Global, Wakefit Innovations) have further details in the full annexure
- Market lot for all securities is 1; all are pari passu with existing shares
- Trading identification is by designated security codes as specified in the annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1: governs admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5: governs lot sizes for admitted securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes when trading these newly listed securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes
- Lock-in restrictions apply to Gretex Corporate Services (GCSL) shares as detailed in Annexure A of the circular

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 02, 2026
- Effective Date (trading commencement): June 03, 2026
- GCSL allotment date: January 13, 2026
- Avalon Technologies ESOP allotment date: May 06, 2026
- Clean Max ESOP allotment date: May 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine further-issue listing notification with low systemic market impact. The addition of ESOP shares increases float marginally for Avalon Technologies and Clean Max Enviro. Gretex Corporate Services sees a more significant capital structure change with 15.2 lakh new shares (warrant conversion + bonus), which could modestly affect near-term price discovery given lock-in conditions on some shares. Existing shareholders of all 14 companies should note the expanded share counts for dilution assessment.
