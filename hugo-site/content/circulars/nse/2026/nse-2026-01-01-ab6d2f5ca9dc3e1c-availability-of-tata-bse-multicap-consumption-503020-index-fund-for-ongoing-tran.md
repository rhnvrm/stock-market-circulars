## Summary

Tata BSE Multicap Consumption 50:30:20 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from January 02, 2026. The fund will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) across all six plan variants (Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options).

## Key Points

- Effective date: January 02, 2026
- Fund reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available
- Six plan variants available: Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes categorized under Equity
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
