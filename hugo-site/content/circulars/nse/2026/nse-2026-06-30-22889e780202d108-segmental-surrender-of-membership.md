## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Manu Stock Broking Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000201738) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives Segment, including Interest Rate Futures (IRF). The surrender was approved on June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Manu Stock Broking Private Limited has voluntarily surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivatives Segment (including IRF).
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000201738
- Surrender approval date: June 17, 2026
- All NSE members are directed to take note of this change.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding a member's voluntary exit from a specific trading segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All members are required to note that Manu Stock Broking Private Limited is no longer an active member in the Currency Derivatives Segment (including IRF) with effect from the surrender approval date.

## Important Dates

- Surrender Approval Date: June 17, 2026
- Circular Date: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action involving a single broker's voluntary withdrawal from the Currency Derivatives Segment. Other members and market participants in the segment are unaffected.
