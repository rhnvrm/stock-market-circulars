## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited (symbol: LLOYDSENGG) on the Capital Market segment, effective July 14, 2026. The further issue arises from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares, adding 14,25,192 shares to the exchange's dealings list.

## Key Points

- Company: Lloyds Engineering Works Limited
- Symbol: LLOYDSENGG, Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE093R01011 (permanent); shares currently held under temporary ISIN IN8093R01010 pending activation
- Face value: Re. 1/-; Paid-up value: Re. 1/-
- Nature of issue: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Number of securities: 14,25,192
- Market lot: 1
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Lock-in: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations, Part A. No new regulatory policy is introduced; it is an administrative listing notification for an existing security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize the newly admitted securities only by their designated codes and trade them in the specified lot size of 1.
- Temporary ISIN IN8093R01010 will be used for dematerialized shares until activation of the permanent ISIN INE093R01011, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 13, 2026
- Effective date of listing: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited by 14,25,192 shares following conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status. This is a routine corporate action/listing update with limited immediate market impact beyond a modest increase in free float and total shares available for trading in LLOYDSENGG.
