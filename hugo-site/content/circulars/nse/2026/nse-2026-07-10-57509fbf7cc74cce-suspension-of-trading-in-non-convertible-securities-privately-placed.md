## Summary

NSE has notified members that trading in two privately placed Non-Convertible Securities (NCDs) issued by Aditya Birla Capital Limited will be suspended with effect from July 13, 2026, on account of redemption of the securities.

## Key Points

- Circular issued by NSE's Listing Department, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Two ISINs of Aditya Birla Capital Limited are affected: INE860H08DU1 and INE860H08DT3
- Reason for suspension in both cases is redemption of the securities
- Suspension applies to privately placed Non-Convertible Securities traded on the debt market segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A governing suspension of NCDs upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the specified ISINs (INE860H08DU1 and INE860H08DT3) of Aditya Birla Capital Limited from the suspension date and update their systems/records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **July 10, 2026**: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/CML/75145, Circular Ref. No: 1128/2026)
- **July 13, 2026**: Effective date of suspension of trading for both ISINs

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these two specific privately placed NCD ISINs of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. As the suspension is due to redemption—a routine, scheduled event—there is minimal market disruption. Debt market participants holding these securities should ensure settlement and redemption processes are completed ahead of the suspension date.
