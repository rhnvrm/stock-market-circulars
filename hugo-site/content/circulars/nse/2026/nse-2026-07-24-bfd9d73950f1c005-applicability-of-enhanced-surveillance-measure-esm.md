## Summary

NSE has notified updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Pioneer Investcorp Limited (PIONRINV) has been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage-I and will attract a minimum 100% margin effective July 28, 2026, along with a shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST). Separately, several securities including ATC Energies System Limited (ATCENERGY), Gujarat Lease Financing Limited (GLFL), Kesoram Industries Limited (KESORAMIND), Newjaisa Technologies Limited (NEWJAISA), Palred Technologies Limited (PALREDTEC), R. S. Software (India) Limited (RSSOFTWARE), and Sahasra Electronic Solutions (SAHASRA, list possibly truncated) have moved from ESM Stage-II back to Stage-I effective July 27, 2026. No securities were listed as moving from Stage-I to Stage-II in this cycle.

## Key Points

- PIONRINV (Pioneer Investcorp Limited, ISIN INE746D01014) is newly added to ESM Stage-I.
- No securities are moving from ESM Stage-I to Stage-II in this cycle.
- Multiple securities are moving from ESM Stage-II to Stage-I, including ATCENERGY, GLFL, KESORAMIND, NEWJAISA, PALREDTEC, RSSOFTWARE, and SAHASRA.
- Securities newly qualifying under ESM shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST).
- Securities shifting to Stage-II are subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction (not applicable to the current Stage-I additions).
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is a surveillance action only and is not to be construed as adverse against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No change to the ESM framework itself; this circular applies the existing framework (referenced from earlier circulars NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315) to a fresh set of securities based on periodic review criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in PIONRINV as on July 27, 2026, and on new positions created from July 28, 2026. Members must also ensure PIONRINV trades are routed under the BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) series from July 28, 2026, instead of EQ/SM. Price bands for securities exiting ESM revert to their pre-ESM levels unless another surveillance framework applies.

## Important Dates

- July 27, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin; effective date for securities moving from Stage-II to Stage-I.
- July 28, 2026: Effective date for 100% margin on new positions and shift of PIONRINV from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment.

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a targeted impact limited to the specific securities named. PIONRINV shareholders and traders face significantly higher margin requirements and reduced liquidity due to the shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement. Securities moving back to Stage-I from Stage-II see relatively eased restrictions compared to Stage-II. Overall market-wide impact is low, but affected stock-specific impact is high for trading activity and liquidity in the concerned names.
