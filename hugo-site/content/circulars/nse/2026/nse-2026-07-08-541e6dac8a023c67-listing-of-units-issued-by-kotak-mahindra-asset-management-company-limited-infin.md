## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Limited under its Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund scheme. The units are admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment of NSE effective July 09, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund by Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund
- Five plan variants listed with distinct symbols and ISINs:
  - INSIFHLSDG (Direct Plan - Growth), ISIN INF174K30012
  - INSIFHLSDR (Direct Plan - Reinvestment of IDCW), ISIN INF174K30038
  - INSIFHLSRG (Regular Plan - Growth), ISIN INF174K30046
  - INSIFHLSRD (Regular Plan - Payout of IDCW), ISIN INF174K30053
  - INSIFHLSRR (Regular Plan - Reinvestment of IDCW), ISIN INF174K30061
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: July 06, 2026
- Series: SF
- Trustee: Kotak Mahindra Trustee Company Limited
- AMC: Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Limited
- RTA: Computer Age Management Services Private Limited (CAMS), Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings, Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size notification).

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members must recognize the new security codes and use the specified market lot size of 1 unit for trading these instruments from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 06, 2026
- Effective/Listing Date: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative listing affecting only investors and distributors of the specific Kotak Mahindra mutual fund scheme. No implications for broader equity markets or existing listed securities.
