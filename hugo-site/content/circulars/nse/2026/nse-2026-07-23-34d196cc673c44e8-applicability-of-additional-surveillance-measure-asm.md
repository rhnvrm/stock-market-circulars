## Regulatory Changes

No changes to the ASM Framework methodology itself; this is a routine periodic update applying existing surveillance criteria to identify securities exhibiting high volatility, price movement, or other risk parameters warranting additional monitoring.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and stock brokers must apply the enhanced surveillance measures applicable to Long-Term ASM Stage I securities for NURECA and TEJA, including any applicable additional margin requirements and trading restrictions as prescribed under the ASM framework, effective from July 24, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the two newly added securities, Nureca Limited and Teja Engineering Industries Limited, which will face increased margin requirements and heightened surveillance, potentially reducing trading liquidity and increasing volatility in these counters. There is no broader market impact, as no stage escalations, de-escalations, or exclusions occurred among previously listed ASM securities in this cycle.
