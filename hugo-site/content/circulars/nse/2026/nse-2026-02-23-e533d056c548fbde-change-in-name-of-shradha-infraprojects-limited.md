## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members that Shradha Infraprojects Limited will officially change its name to **Shradha Realty Limited** effective February 27, 2026. The NSE trading symbol (SHRADHA) remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Shradha Infraprojects Limited** to **Shradha Realty Limited**
- NSE trading symbol **SHRADHA** is retained without modification
- Change is effective from **February 27, 2026**
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under ref. NSE/CML/72949

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a corporate action (name change) communicated via a listing circular as required under exchange disclosure norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are expected to take note of the name change and update internal records accordingly
- No specific action required from investors; existing holdings under the SHRADHA symbol remain unaffected

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The company's NSE symbol (SHRADHA) remains the same, so trading operations are not disrupted. Investors and members should update reference data to reflect the new legal name — Shradha Realty Limited — from February 27, 2026 onwards.
