## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of partly paid-up equity shares of GVP Infotech Limited (Symbol: GVPTECHPP, ISIN: IN9382T01012) from trading with effect from June 25, 2026. The suspension is on account of first and final call money, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- GVP Infotech Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (GVPTECHPP) will be suspended from trading effective June 25, 2026
- Trading in the security will cease at the closing hours of June 24, 2026
- The suspension reason is the first and final call money on the partly paid shares
- ISIN of the affected security: IN9382T01012
- Issued under Circular Ref. No: 1001/2026 (NSE/CML/74825)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is invoked under the existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities from trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified not to trade in GVPTECHPP from June 25, 2026 onward
- Investors holding partly paid-up shares of GVP Infotech Limited should note the trading cessation and ensure call money obligations are met as applicable

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 24, 2026**: Last day of trading (suspension effective from closing hours)
- **June 25, 2026**: Suspension effective date — shares cease trading on NSE

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders of GVP Infotech Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (GVPTECHPP). Investors will be unable to trade this security on NSE from June 25, 2026. The trigger is the first and final call money event, which typically marks the conversion or settlement of partly paid shares. Market impact is limited to holders of this specific instrument; there is no broader market or systemic impact.
