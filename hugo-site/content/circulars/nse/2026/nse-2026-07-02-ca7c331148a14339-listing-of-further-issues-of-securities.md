## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities of two SME EMERGE-listed companies — Sharp Chucks and Machines Limited (SCML) and Swaraj Suiting Limited (SWARAJ) — effective July 03, 2026, under Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Sharp Chucks and Machines Limited: 2,412,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted on preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 76.12, ISIN INE460Q01014, market lot 1000, allotment date 17-Nov-2025.
- Swaraj Suiting Limited: 33,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on preferential basis, issue price Rs. 236, ISIN INE0GMR01016, market lot 500, allotment date 03-May-2026.
- Both securities trade in the SM series on the SME EMERGE platform.
- Securities allotted in dematerialized mode are currently credited under a temporary ISIN pending activation, per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative listing notification issued under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations governing admission of further issues to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify these securities only by their designated codes and trade them in the specified lot sizes (1000 for SCML, 500 for SWARAJ).
- Lock-in restrictions apply: for SCML, 800,000 shares locked in until 01-Feb-2028 and 1,612,000 shares locked in until 01-Feb-2027 (per Annexure A); for SWARAJ, all 33,000 shares locked in until 10-Jan-2027.

## Important Dates

- Allotment date (SCML): 17-Nov-2025
- Allotment date (SWARAJ): 03-May-2026
- Effective date of listing: July 03, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (SCML): 01-Feb-2027 and 01-Feb-2028 (tranche-wise)
- Lock-in expiry (SWARAJ): 10-Jan-2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market-wide impact. This is a routine administrative listing update affecting trading eligibility and lot sizes for two SME EMERGE-listed companies only; existing shareholders and prospective traders in SCML and SWARAJ should note the new share count, lock-in schedule, and lot sizes effective July 03, 2026.
