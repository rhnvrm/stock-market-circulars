## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying all trading members of a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of NLC India Limited (Symbol: NLCINDIA, ISIN: INE589A01014). The OFS will be conducted on June 09, 2026 and June 10, 2026 via the NSE eOFS platform, with a floor price of Rs. 303/- per equity share. The offer is being implemented under the Interoperability among Clearing Corporations framework.

## Key Points

- OFS bidding sessions scheduled for **June 09, 2026** and **June 10, 2026**
- Floor price set at **Rs. 303/-** per equity share (face value Rs. 10/- each)
- Base offer size: up to **2,77,32,732 equity shares**; oversubscription option: up to **1,38,66,366 equity shares**; employee option: **25,000 equity shares**
- Seller will communicate intention to exercise oversubscription option on or before 5:00 PM on June 09, 2026
- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate via the **eOFS platform** (https://eofs.nseindia.com)
- Three bidding categories: IS (Non-Retail), RS (Retail — bid value ≤ Rs. 2 lacs), ES (Employee — bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs)
- Market lot: 1 equity share; tick size: Re. 0.05

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is conducted in accordance with the existing Interoperability among Clearing Corporations framework under Exchange circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members not yet having Admin user IDs must register via ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under: ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS. Registration requests are processed at 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, and 3:00 PM; requests post 3:00 PM are processed next day by 8:45 AM. Only one request per member is allowed and cannot be modified.
- Members must ensure compliant UCC (for self/PRO and clients) is updated in the UCI system **one day prior** to the OFS bidding session. UCCs updated on the day of bidding will not be permitted to participate.
- UCCs identified as compliant by 22:00 hrs on the previous day will be considered permitted for the next bidding day.
- Members must ensure **sufficient collateral** before initiating carry-forward of orders on T+1 day. Orders rejected once due to insufficient margins cannot be re-placed.
- Members should refer to Annexure 3 of circular NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020 for features and limits settings.

## Impact Assessment

This OFS impacts trading members and their clients who wish to participate in the NLC India Limited share sale. NLC India Limited is a government-owned public sector undertaking (PSU), and the OFS represents a potential stake dilution by the seller (likely the Government of India). Members must proactively ensure UCC compliance and collateral adequacy ahead of the bidding sessions. The relatively large base offer size (approximately 2.77 crore shares) at a floor price of Rs. 303/- implies an offer worth over Rs. 840 crore at minimum, indicating significant market activity on the eOFS platform on June 09-10, 2026.
