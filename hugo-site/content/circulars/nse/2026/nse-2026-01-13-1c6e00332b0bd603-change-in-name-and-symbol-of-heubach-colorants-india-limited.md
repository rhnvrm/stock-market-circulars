## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Heubach Colorants India Limited will undergo a name and trading symbol change effective January 20, 2026. The company's trading symbol will change from HEUBACHIND to SUDARCOLOR, and the company name will change to Sudarshan Colorants India Limited.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Heubach Colorants India Limited" to "Sudarshan Colorants India Limited"
- Trading symbol changing from "HEUBACHIND" to "SUDARCOLOR"
- Change is effective from January 20, 2026
- Circular reference number: NSE/CML/72287 (Circular Ref. No: 0059/2026)
- Issued by the Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving a name and symbol change for an existing listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- All trading activities post January 20, 2026 must use the new symbol SUDARCOLOR
- Members need to inform their clients about the name and symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name and symbol change may cause temporary confusion among market participants. Traders and investors need to update their watchlists and systems to track the stock under its new symbol SUDARCOLOR.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Brokers, trading terminals, and market data systems need to be updated to reflect the new name and symbol. There may be a transition period where both identifiers are referenced.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Existing shareholders are not affected in terms of holdings, but must be aware of the new company name and symbol for future trading and corporate communications.
