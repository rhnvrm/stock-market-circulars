## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Edelweiss BSE LargeMid (60:40) Stable Dividend 50 ETF, a new fund offer (NFO) from Edelweiss Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 27, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- The NFO covers both Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth options of the Edelweiss BSE LargeMid (60:40) Stable Dividend 50 ETF.
- ISIN for the scheme: INF754K01XI7.
- Scheme category: Equity.
- Allotment date for the scheme: August 5, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds (via any payment mode) are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by August 3, 2026, 6:00 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for subscription on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting on behalf of investors must ensure timely fund settlement in the designated Clearing Corporation account before the specified deadline to complete valid subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 27, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Clear funds deadline: August 3, 2026, 6:00 pm
- Allotment date: August 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to NSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors interested in subscribing to this specific ETF NFO. It does not affect listed equities, trading rules, or broader market operations.
