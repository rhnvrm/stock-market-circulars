## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 426/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74607) dated June 08, 2026 placing 7 securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective June 09, 2026, with enhanced margin requirements kicking in from June 10, 2026. No securities have been placed under ST-ASM Stage II or moved between stages.

## Key Points

- 7 securities added to ST-ASM Stage I effective June 09, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I: Applicable margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- ST-ASM Stage II: Applicable margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%) — no securities placed here in this circular
- Margin enhancement applies to all open positions as on June 09, 2026 and new positions created from June 10, 2026
- No securities shortlisted for Stage II, Stage I to Stage II movement, or Stage II to Stage I movement
- This circular is further to prior ASM circulars: NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066

## Regulatory Changes

Securities meeting the ST-ASM criteria are subject to enhanced margin requirements under the ASM framework. The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges. Shortlisting of securities under ASM is purely on account of market surveillance and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must apply the enhanced margin rates to the listed securities from June 10, 2026
- For ST-ASM Stage I securities: collect margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%)
- Applicable on all open positions as on June 09, 2026 and all new positions created from June 10, 2026
- Members may refer to NSE FAQs on ASM for detailed framework provisions

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of securities in ST-ASM Stage I
- **June 10, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become applicable on open positions (as on June 09) and new positions

Traders and investors holding or intending to take positions in these 7 securities will face significantly higher margin requirements. The mandatory minimum margin of 50% (ST-ASM Stage I) will reduce leveraged trading capacity and may affect liquidity. Ramco Systems Limited (RAMCOSYS) is the most widely tracked of these names, while the others are smaller-cap or SME-segment securities. No securities were excluded from ASM in this circular.
