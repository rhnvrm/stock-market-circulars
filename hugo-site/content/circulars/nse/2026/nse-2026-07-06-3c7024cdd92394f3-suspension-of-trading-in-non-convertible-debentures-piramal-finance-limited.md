## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in two Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) series issued by Piramal Finance Limited will be suspended effective July 08, 2026, as both securities have reached their maturity date. The suspension is made under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two Piramal Finance Limited NCD series are being suspended from trading.
- Symbol 85PFL26 (ISIN INE516Y07360) — reason: Maturity.
- Symbol 875PF26 (ISIN INE516Y07410) — reason: Maturity.
- Suspension is effected under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine action under existing NSE trading regulations to suspend trading in matured debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the specified NCD symbols from the effective date and update systems accordingly. No other action required from listed entities beyond standard maturity settlement processes.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 06, 2026
- Suspension Effective From: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these two specific NCD series, which will no longer be tradable on the exchange following their scheduled maturity. There is no broader market or equity impact; this is a standard administrative action tied to debt instrument maturity.
