## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF (Fund of Funds) of Sundaram Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective January 19, 2026. The scheme will also support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP) facilities.

## Key Points

- Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF will be available for ongoing transactions from January 19, 2026
- Six variants of the scheme are being reopened across Direct and Regular plans
- All variants support IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, and Growth options
- Facility includes SIP, STP, and SWP options
- All six variants are categorized under Equity category
- Applicable to NSE MF Invest Platform members

## Scheme Details

### Direct Plan Options:
1. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Direct IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF903JA1MB6)
2. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Direct IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF903JA1MA8)
3. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF903JA1LZ7)

### Regular Plan Options:
4. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Regular IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF903JA1ME0)
5. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Regular IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF903JA1MD2)
6. **Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF Fund - Regular Growth** (ISIN: INF903JA1MC4)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding the reopening of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE MF Invest Platform members should:
- Update their systems to enable transactions in the listed scheme variants from January 19, 2026
- Ensure all six ISINs are activated for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities
- Make the schemes available to investors through their platforms

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 16, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 19, 2026 - Sundaram Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with SIP, STP, SWP facility

## Impact Assessment

### Market Impact
Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational update affecting only investors interested in Sundaram Mutual Fund's Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF scheme.

### Investor Impact
Positive for investors seeking to invest in or redeem from this arbitrage fund of funds. The availability of SIP, STP, and SWP facilities provides flexible investment and withdrawal options.

### Operational Impact
NSE MF Invest Platform members need to ensure their systems are ready to process transactions in these schemes from January 19, 2026. Minor operational update with no significant changes to existing processes.
