## Summary

Choice Nifty 50 Index Fund, offered by Choice Mutual Fund, has been reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 09, 2026. The fund is also available for Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP).

## Key Points

- Choice Nifty 50 Index Fund reopened for ongoing transactions from April 09, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform for subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- Both plans categorized under Equity
- Regular Plan ISIN: INF2KCX01038
- Direct Plan ISIN: INF2KCX01020

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational circular informing members of the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed of the availability and may facilitate client transactions in Choice Nifty 50 Index Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform from April 09, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **April 09, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Choice Nifty 50 Index Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This announcement enables retail and institutional investors to transact in Choice Nifty 50 Index Fund via the NSE MF Invest Platform. It expands the available product suite for investors seeking passive equity exposure tracking the Nifty 50 index through Choice Mutual Fund. No systemic or broad market implications.
