## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued this circular to inform members of the adjustment procedure for Futures and Options contracts in TRENT LIMITED (TRENT) due to a Bonus Issue in the ratio of 1:2. The adjustment factor is 1.5, and the ex-date is June 04, 2026. Contracts expiring on June 03, 2026 will not be adjusted; all other open positions will be adjusted after end-of-day on June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus issue ratio: 1:2 (one bonus share for every two held)
- Adjustment factor: **1.5**
- Ex-date: **June 04, 2026**
- Positions in contracts expiring **June 03, 2026 will NOT be adjusted**
- All open futures and options positions in TRENT existing after EOD June 03, 2026 will be adjusted
- Adjusted market lot is as per Circular No. 76/2026 (Download No. 74486) dated June 01, 2026
- Adjusted futures price = Settlement price on June 03, 2026 ÷ adjustment factor (1.5)
- Carry-forward/adjusted value computed by multiplying pre-adjusted quantity with pre-adjusted settlement price to avoid rounding differences
- From June 04, 2026, daily MTM settlement resumes under normal procedures

## Regulatory Changes

The adjustment is made pursuant to:
- NSE Clearing Byelaws on Clearing and Settlement
- SEBI circular SMDRP/DC/CIR-8/01 dated June 21, 2001
- Circular No. 50 (NCL/CMPT/73997) dated April 30, 2026
- Circular No. 76/2026 (Download No. 74486) dated June 01, 2026 (lot size change)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their systems to reflect adjusted positions, prices, and lot sizes effective June 04, 2026
- Begin-of-day margins on June 04, 2026 will be computed based on adjusted carry-forward values
- Intra-day margins will subsequently be computed based on traded prices at the time span risk parameter files are generated
- Members should refer to Circular No. 76/2026 for the revised market lot applicable post-adjustment

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 01, 2026 | Circular issued; lot size change circular (No. 76/2026) also issued |
| June 03, 2026 | Last day before ex-date; contracts expiring this day will NOT be adjusted; MTM based on daily settlement price |
| June 04, 2026 | Ex-date for bonus issue; adjusted positions effective; normal MTM and margin procedures resume |

## Impact Assessment

All market participants holding open futures or options positions in TRENT LIMITED beyond June 03, 2026 will see their positions, prices, and lot sizes adjusted by a factor of 1.5. This is a mechanical corporate-action adjustment and does not affect the economic value of positions. However, members must ensure their back-office and risk systems correctly apply the new adjusted lot size and carry-forward values to avoid margin miscalculations at the start of June 04, 2026. The adjustment example provided in the circular (long 100 contracts at Jun-2026 expiry, short 150 at Jul-2026 expiry) illustrates the position multiplication mechanism members should validate against their own books.
