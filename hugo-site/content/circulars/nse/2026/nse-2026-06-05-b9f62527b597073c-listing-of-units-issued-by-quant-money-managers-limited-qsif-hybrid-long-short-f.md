## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by quant Money Managers Limited under its scheme qsif Hybrid Long-Short Fund (Regular Plan - IDCW Payout Option) on the Capital Market segment. The units will be available for trading under the symbol QSIFHRP with effect from June 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- 98,086 units of qsif Hybrid Long-Short Fund admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: **QSIFHRP**; ISIN: **INF966L30118**
- Face value and paid-up value per unit: Rs. 10/-; Issue price: Rs. 10.19/-
- Market lot size: 1 unit; Series: SF
- Date of allotment: April 09, 2026
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular acts under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities and specification of lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security code **QSIFHRP** for trading these units.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Effective date for commencement of trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- **April 09, 2026**: Date of allotment of units

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of a small tranche (98,086 units) of a mutual fund scheme on NSE. The hybrid long-short fund structure may attract interest from investors seeking alternative mutual fund products. No changes to existing trading mechanisms or member obligations beyond recognising the new security code.
