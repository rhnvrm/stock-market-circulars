## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, identifying four securities that have met the criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I: Ind-Swift Laboratories Limited (INDSWFTLAB), S. P. Apparels Limited (SPAL), Tirupati Forge Limited (TIRUPATIFL), and Unichem Laboratories Limited (UNICHEMLAB). No securities were shortlisted under Stage II, and no securities moved between stages in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Four securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I: INDSWFTLAB, SPAL, TIRUPATIFL, UNICHEMLAB
- No securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage II
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or vice versa
- Applicable margin rate for Stage I securities: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at maximum 100%
- Measure applies to all open positions as on July 13, 2026 and new positions created from July 14, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and not to be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a periodic update under the existing ST-ASM framework established by prior NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024 respectively). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular applies the existing framework to newly identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must apply the specified margin rates to the listed securities for all open positions as of July 13, 2026 and new positions created from July 14, 2026 onward. Members should monitor price band reinstatement rules if a scrip exits the framework, subject to it not being part of any other surveillance measure.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of ST-ASM Stage I measures: July 14, 2026
- Reference date for open positions: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirement (50% or higher, capped at 100%) for INDSWFTLAB, SPAL, TIRUPATIFL, and UNICHEMLAB will raise the cost of taking or holding positions in these securities, likely reducing speculative trading activity and liquidity in these names. This is a routine surveillance action rather than a signal of any adverse corporate development, but traders and investors in these specific stocks should account for higher margin obligations starting July 14, 2026.
