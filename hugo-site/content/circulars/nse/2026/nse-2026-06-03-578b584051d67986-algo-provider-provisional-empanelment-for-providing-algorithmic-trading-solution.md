## Summary

NSE's Investigation Department has provisionally empaneled M/s Koti Labs Private Limited as an Algo Provider authorized to offer Whitebox algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. The empanelment is effective immediately as of June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- M/s Koti Labs Private Limited has been provisionally empaneled with NSE for providing Algorithmic Trading Solutions.
- The type of algo offered is **Whitebox**.
- Registered address: F0130, Juniper Block Bridge Orchards, Devanhalli, Bengaluru, Karnataka - 562110.
- Contact: +91 9167949733.
- The circular is issued by NSE's Investigation Department under Circular Ref. No. 40/2026.
- The updated list of all empaneled Algo Providers is available on the NSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine empanelment notification adding a new vendor to the list of NSE-approved algo providers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to use Koti Labs Private Limited's algorithmic trading solutions may now do so under NSE's empanelment framework.
- Trading members should refer to the full list of empaneled Algo Providers on the NSE website before engaging any vendor.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 03, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact; this is an administrative update expanding the pool of NSE-approved algo providers by one vendor. Trading members seeking whitebox algorithmic trading solutions have an additional empaneled option. No changes to existing trading rules, margins, or compliance obligations for the broader market.
