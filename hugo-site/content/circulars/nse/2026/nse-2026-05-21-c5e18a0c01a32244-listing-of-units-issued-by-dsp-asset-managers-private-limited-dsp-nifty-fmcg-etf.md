## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of 1,061,077 units of DSP Nifty FMCG ETF, managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited, to dealings on the Capital Market segment under the symbol **FMCGADD** (ISIN: INF740KA1ZA4), effective May 22, 2026. The units were allotted on May 18, 2026 at an issue price of Rs. 50.90 per unit with a face value of Rs. 10/-.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** FMCGADD | **ISIN:** INF740KA1ZA4
- **Number of units listed:** 1,061,077
- **Issue price:** Rs. 50.90 per unit
- **Face value / Paid-up value:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Market lot:** 1 unit
- **Series:** EQ
- **Date of allotment:** May 18, 2026
- Ten market makers appointed, including Kotak Securities, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Share India Securities, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is executed under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code **FMCGADD** for all transactions in this ETF.
- Trading must be conducted in the prescribed market lot of 1 unit.
- Market makers listed (TM codes: 90246, 10798, 08081, 13773, 90068, 10412, 90207, 11890, 08134, 12151) are required to fulfil their market-making obligations from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment:** May 18, 2026
- **Effective trading date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact; this is a standard new ETF unit listing. Investors tracking FMCG sector indices via mutual fund ETFs gain an additional exchange-traded option. The appointment of ten market makers should ensure adequate liquidity from day one. No impact on existing listed securities or trading norms.
