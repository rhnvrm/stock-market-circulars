## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has approved the request of CRIZAC Technologies Private Limited (SEBI Registration: INZ000175032) to surrender their membership in the Currency Derivatives Segment. The surrender was approved on January 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member: CRIZAC Technologies Private Limited
- SEBI registration number: INZ000175032
- Surrendered segment: Currency Derivatives Segment
- Approval date: January 29, 2026
- Circular reference: NSE/COMP/05/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative approval of a voluntary membership surrender request.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this segmental surrender. CRIZAC Technologies Private Limited will no longer be able to trade in the Currency Derivatives Segment.

## Important Dates

- **January 29, 2026**: Surrender approval date and effective date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to a single trading member's voluntary decision to exit the Currency Derivatives Segment. Other members should update their records to reflect that CRIZAC Technologies Private Limited is no longer an active participant in currency derivatives trading.
