## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has announced that multiple Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) settlements have been scheduled on April 02, 2026, due to a settlement holiday on April 01, 2026. Participants and custodians must adhere to revised pay-in and pay-out timings for the compressed settlement schedule.

## Key Points

- Settlement holiday on April 01, 2026 necessitates multiple SLBS settlements on April 02, 2026
- Three settlement types are scheduled: L 2026060, P 2026756, and L 2026061
- Each settlement has specific pay-in and pay-out timings starting from 08:00 hrs
- Participants must note cut-off timings for pay-in at depositories due to revised settlement schedule
- Circular issued by NSE Clearing Limited under Circular Ref. No: 034/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational scheduling notice to manage settlements around the April 01, 2026 holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and custodians must follow the revised settlement schedule on April 02, 2026
- Pay-in submissions must comply with updated cut-off timings at depositories
- Adherence to the specific pay-in and pay-out windows for each settlement number is mandatory

## Important Dates

- **April 01, 2026**: Settlement holiday (no SLBS settlements)
- **April 02, 2026**: Multiple settlements scheduled
  - L 2026060: Transaction Date 30-Mar-26 | Pay-in 08:00 hrs | Pay-out 09:30 hrs
  - P 2026756: Settlement Date 02-Apr-26 | Pay-in 08:30 hrs | Pay-out 09:45 hrs
  - L 2026061: Transaction Date 01-Apr-26 | Pay-in 10:15 hrs | Pay-out 12:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on SLBS participants and custodians who must plan for compressed, back-to-back settlements on April 02, 2026. The early morning start times (from 08:00 hrs) require participants to ensure timely pay-in submissions at depositories. No impact on general equity or debt market participants unless they are active in the SLBS segment.
