## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Megasoft Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective February 09, 2026. The company will be renamed to Sigma Advanced Systems Limited, and its trading symbol will change from MEGASOFT to SIGMAADV.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from "Megasoft Limited" to "Sigma Advanced Systems Limited"
- Trading symbol changes from "MEGASOFT" to "SIGMAADV"
- Changes effective from February 09, 2026
- Circular reference number: NSE/CML/72598 (Circular Ref. No: 0167/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action involving company name and symbol modification as per standard listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are required to:
- Update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Ensure all trading and reporting systems use the new symbol SIGMAADV from the effective date
- Communicate the change to clients holding positions in this security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 09, 2026 - Name and symbol change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that affects only the company's trading identity on the exchange.

**Trading Impact**: No impact on trading operations. The security will continue to trade normally under the new symbol from the effective date.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders are not affected in terms of their holdings. Only the company name and trading symbol change; share ownership and ISIN remain intact.
