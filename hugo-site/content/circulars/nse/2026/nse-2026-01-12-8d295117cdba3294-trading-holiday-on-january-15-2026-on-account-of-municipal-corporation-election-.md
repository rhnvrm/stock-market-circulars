## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has declared Thursday, January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday in the Futures & Options segment due to Municipal Corporation Election in Maharashtra. This circular (NSE/FAOP/72262) partially modifies the earlier circular reference no. NSE/FAOP/71777.

## Key Points

- Trading holiday declared for January 15, 2026
- Applies to Futures & Options segment only
- Reason: Municipal Corporation Election in Maharashtra
- This is a partial modification to earlier circular NSE/FAOP/71777
- All members are requested to take note

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding trading schedule modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must note the trading holiday
- No F&O trading will be conducted on January 15, 2026
- Members should inform their clients and stakeholders accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Trading Holiday**: Thursday, January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete closure of F&O segment trading for one day affects all derivative market participants, including retail traders, institutional investors, and hedgers. Market participants need to adjust their positions and strategies accordingly.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure all systems and operations account for this holiday. Open positions will carry forward, and margin requirements will need to be managed considering the holiday.
