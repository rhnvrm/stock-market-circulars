## Summary

NSE requires all trading members utilizing Software as a Service (SaaS) based solutions/applications to submit half-yearly compliance reports through the Member Portal. This circular facilitates submission for the period July 01, 2025 to December 31, 2025, with a deadline of January 31, 2026. The requirement is based on SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020. Members not using SaaS services must also submit a declaration selecting 'NO' option.

## Key Points

- Half-yearly SaaS compliance reporting required for period July 01, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- Submission deadline: January 31, 2026
- Reports must be submitted through NSE Member Portal via path: Log in to Member Portal → Inspection → SaaS → Submit SaaS Report
- Members not utilizing SaaS services must select 'NO' option and provide appropriate comment
- User manual provided as Annexure-A with step-by-step submission guidelines
- NSE will share submissions with other exchanges (BSE/MSEI/MCX/NCDEX) where member is also registered
- Reference to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI advisory on Software as a Service (SaaS) based solutions for financial sector organizations through a dedicated portal submission mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members Using SaaS:**
- Submit detailed SaaS compliance report through Member Portal
- Follow submission path: Member Portal → Inspection → SaaS → Submit SaaS Report
- Select reporting period (July 01, 2025 to December 31, 2025)
- Click 'Yes' when asked about SaaS usage
- Add rows and fill required details for each SaaS solution/application used
- Submit on or before January 31, 2026

**For Trading Members Not Using SaaS:**
- Access same submission portal
- Select 'NO' option in SaaS report menu
- Provide appropriate comment or remark explaining non-usage
- Submit declaration by deadline

**Data Sharing:**
- Submissions will be shared with other exchanges (BSE/MSEI/MCX/NCDEX) where member is registered (as per NSE Circular Ref no- NSE/INSP/70505 dated September 29, 2025)

## Important Dates

- **Reporting Period:** July 01, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **Submission Deadline:** January 31, 2026
- **Circular Issue Date:** January 02, 2026
- **Reference SEBI Circular Date:** November 03, 2020
- **Previous NSE Circular Date:** September 29, 2025 (NSE/INSP/70505)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading members using SaaS solutions must allocate resources for compliance reporting
- Members must maintain documentation regarding SaaS vendors and usage details
- Portal-based submission streamlines compliance process compared to manual submissions

**Compliance Impact:**
- Mandatory submission for all trading members (either affirmative report or negative declaration)
- Non-compliance may result in regulatory action from Inspection Department
- Enhanced visibility for exchanges and SEBI on SaaS usage in financial sector

**Market Impact:**
- No direct market impact
- Strengthens cybersecurity and operational risk oversight in trading ecosystem
- Aligns with SEBI's focus on technology risk management in financial services
