## Summary

NSE has informed members that DYNA SIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund, a New Fund Offer (NFO) under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category of 360 Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 05, 2026 to June 19, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Allotment is scheduled for June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund: DynaSIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund by 360 Mutual Fund
- NFO window: June 05, 2026 to June 19, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: June 24, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six plan variants available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Category: Equity (Specialized Investment Fund)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification for the launch of a new SIF scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 24, 2026 till 11:59 PM.
- Transactions are to be processed via the NSE MF Invest Platform during the NFO subscription window.

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: NFO subscription window opens
- **June 19, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription window closes
- **June 24, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account; Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broad market impact and is primarily relevant to NSE MF Invest Platform members facilitating transactions in 360 Mutual Fund products. The six scheme variants (ISINs: INF579M30133, INF579M30141, INF579M30158, INF579M30166, INF579M30174, INF579M30182) cover Regular and Direct plan options under an equity long-short strategy targeting securities outside the top 100 stocks. Members should update their systems to enable transactions for these new ISINs within the NFO window.
