## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has approved the surrender of segment membership for Elite Wealth Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000186539) from the Interest Rate Futures segment. The surrender was approved on June 18, 2026, and all members are notified to take note.

## Key Points

- Elite Wealth Limited has surrendered its membership in the Interest Rate Futures segment.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000186539
- Surrender approved by NSE on June 18, 2026.
- This is an informational notice to all NSE members.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative approval of a voluntary membership surrender for a specific segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of Elite Wealth Limited's exit from the Interest Rate Futures segment. No specific action is required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Surrender approval date for Elite Wealth Limited's Interest Rate Futures membership.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender of a single member from the Interest Rate Futures segment is a routine administrative event. Other market participants and counterparties who may have had dealings with Elite Wealth Limited in this segment should be aware of their changed membership status.
