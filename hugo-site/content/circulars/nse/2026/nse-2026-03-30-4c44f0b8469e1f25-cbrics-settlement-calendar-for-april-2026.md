## Summary

NSE Clearing Ltd. has issued the CBRICS (Corporate Bond Repo in International Clearing Settlement) settlement calendar for April 2026. Participants and custodians are directed to transfer securities using 'On-market' settlement mode under the Corporate Bond market type. The circular covers 19 settlement dates across April 2026 with corresponding settlement numbers for NSDL/CDSL transfers.

## Key Points

- CM Name: NSE Clearing Ltd.; CM-BP ID: IN568177
- Market Type: Corporate Bond
- Settlement mode: On-market
- 19 settlement dates scheduled in April 2026
- Applicable for both NSDL and CDSL transfers
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) are invalid for CBRICS settlements since September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular publishing the monthly settlement schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and custodians must use Settlement mode 'On-market' for all CBRICS securities transfers
- Must use CM-BP ID IN568177 for NSE Clearing Ltd.
- Must not use the deprecated DP details (IN001002-10000617) for any CBRICS settlement

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate operational impact for debt segment participants and custodians involved in corporate bond settlements via CBRICS. It provides the necessary schedule to plan securities transfers for the entire month of April 2026. No market-wide or equity segment impact. Non-compliance with settlement dates or use of outdated DP details could result in settlement failures.
