## Summary

NSE Clearing Ltd. has issued the CBRICS (Corporate Bond) settlement calendar for July 2026. Participants and custodians are instructed to transfer securities for CBRICS settlement using the 'On-market' settlement mode. The calendar covers 23 trading days in July 2026 with corresponding settlement numbers.

## Key Points

- Market Type: Corporate Bond
- Clearing Member: NSE Clearing Ltd. (CM-BP ID: IN568177)
- Settlement mode: 'On-market'
- Covers 23 settlement days in July 2026 (01-Jul-26 to 31-Jul-26)
- Settlement numbers follow the format 260700X for each trading day
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) have been discontinued since September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly operational circular for the CBRICS settlement platform in the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and custodians must:
- Use the 'On-market' settlement mode for all CBRICS transfers
- Transfer securities according to the published settlement calendar using the correct settlement number for each trade date
- Not use the deprecated DP details (IN001002-10000617) for any CBRICS settlement
