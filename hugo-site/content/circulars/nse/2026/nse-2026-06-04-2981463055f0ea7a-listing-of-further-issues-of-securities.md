## Summary

NSE has admitted 6,00,000 equity shares of Sar Televenture Limited (Symbol: SARTELE) to dealings on the Capital Market segment under SME EMERGE with effect from June 05, 2026. The shares were allotted on May 07, 2026 pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 242 per share.

## Key Points

- 6,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 2 face value admitted to NSE SME EMERGE segment
- Symbol: SARTELE, Series: SM, ISIN: INE0PUC01020
- Issue price: Rs. 242 per share; allotment date: May 07, 2026
- Distinctive number range: 49547501 to 50147500
- Market lot size: 500 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in on these shares expires December 31, 2027
- Shares temporarily credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8*********) pending activation per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code SARTELE (Series: SM) for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 500 shares
- The temporary ISIN will be activated and converted to the permanent ISIN (INE0PUC01020) in accordance with applicable SEBI circulars

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 07, 2026
- Effective date for trading: June 05, 2026
- Lock-in expiry date: December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME EMERGE listing of additional equity shares resulting from warrant conversion on a preferential basis. The addition of 6,00,000 shares increases the tradable float of SARTELE marginally. The lock-in until December 31, 2027 restricts immediate selling by allottees. Impact is limited to existing investors and traders in SARTELE on the SME EMERGE platform.
