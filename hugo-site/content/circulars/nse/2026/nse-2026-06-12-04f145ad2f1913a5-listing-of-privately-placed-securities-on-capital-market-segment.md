## Summary

NSE has admitted two privately placed debt securities — one from Tata Capital Limited and one from Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited — to dealings on the National Stock Exchange capital market segment with effect from June 15, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two fresh issue NCDs admitted to NSE capital market segment effective June 15, 2026
- Both securities carry a coupon rate of 8.15% per annum
- Market lot size is 1 for both securities
- Both trade under Series N0
- Securities identified exclusively by designated codes for trading purposes
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only designated security codes when trading these instruments
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (market lot: 1)
- No additional compliance actions required for members beyond standard debt segment trading norms

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Effective Date (trading commencement):** June 15, 2026
- **Maturity — Tata Capital (815TACA29):** June 11, 2029
- **Maturity — Tata Capital Housing Finance (815TCHFL31):** June 11, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative listing notification for two investment-grade NCDs from Tata group NBFCs. Both instruments offer 8.15% coupon with medium-term tenors (3 and 5 years respectively). Impact is confined to institutional and debt market participants interested in Tata Capital or Tata Capital Housing Finance paper. No equity market implications.
