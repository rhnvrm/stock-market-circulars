## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective January 08, 2026. The circular notifies fresh issues from Godrej Industries Limited, L&T Finance Limited, and Tata Steel Limited, as well as re-issues from ICICI Securities Limited, L&T Finance Limited, and IIFL Finance Limited. All securities have been assigned designated security codes and ISINs.

## Key Points

- Fresh commercial paper issues from 3 companies totaling 30,000 securities worth ₹15 billion face value
- Re-issues from 3 companies totaling 36,000 securities worth ₹18 billion face value
- All securities allotted on January 07, 2026
- Redemption periods ranging from 22 days to 91 days
- Securities listed pursuant to NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed debt securities on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** January 07, 2026 (all securities)
- **Listing Effective Date:** January 08, 2026
- **Redemption Dates:** Range from January 29, 2026 to April 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine listing of commercial papers in the debt market segment. These are short-term instruments with tenors under 91 days.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard debt market operations. Members can trade these securities starting January 08, 2026.

**Investor Impact:** Provides additional short-term debt investment options from established issuers including financial services companies and industrial conglomerates.
