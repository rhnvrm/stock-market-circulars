## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund NFO, offered under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category by Kotak Mutual Fund, will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme is classified under the SIF category and falls under the Debt asset class.
- Six plan/option variants of the scheme are covered, spanning Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.
- Ongoing transactions will be enabled via the NSE MF Invest Platform, including SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling continued platform access for an existing fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in mutual fund units via the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems and client-facing information to reflect the reopening of subscriptions and redemptions for this scheme, along with its associated ISINs:

- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular Plan - Growth: INF174K30046
- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment: INF174K30061
- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan - Growth: INF174K30012
- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment: INF174K30038
- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular Plan - IDCW Payout: INF174K30053
- Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan - IDCW Payout: INF174K30020

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 09, 2026 — scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact, as it pertains solely to the operational availability of a single mutual fund scheme's transaction facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It is relevant primarily to NSE members and distributors handling mutual fund transactions and investors in the Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund SIF.
