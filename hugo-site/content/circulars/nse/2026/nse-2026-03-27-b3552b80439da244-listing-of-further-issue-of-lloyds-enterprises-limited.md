## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 14,47,739 additional equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENT) on the Capital Market segment with effect from March 30, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE080I01025.

## Key Points

- 14,47,739 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited admitted to trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- Security type: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Trading symbol: LLOYDSENT | Series: EQ | ISIN: INE080I01025
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1/- each
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable
- Shares were temporarily credited under ISIN IN8080I01024 (temporary ISIN for additional issues per SEBI circulars)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Circular is issued under:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes for securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (LLOYDSENT) and ISIN (INE080I01025) for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in market lot size of 1 share
- The temporary ISIN (IN8080I01024) will be replaced by the permanent ISIN upon activation per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 27, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact. The 14,47,739 shares (comprising two tranches: 10,56,284 and 3,91,455 shares with respective distinctive number ranges) are added to the tradeable float of LLOYDSENT following conversion from partly paid-up status. Existing shareholders holding the converted shares will be able to trade them on NSE from March 30, 2026. No impact on broader market operations or other listed entities.
