## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Aastha Spintex Limited pursuant to its IPO. The shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment of NSE with effect from July 06, 2026, under the symbol AASTHA, initially trading in Series 'BE' before subsequent shift to Series 'EQ'.

## Key Points

- Company: Aastha Spintex Limited
- Symbol: AASTHA; ISIN: INE2FMX01012
- Series: BE (to be shifted to EQ later as per SEBI circulars dated January 20, 2012)
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share; Paid-up Value: Rs. 10/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 136/- per share
- Total securities admitted: 44,142,190 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Security will be part of the pre-open session for IPO and Other category of scrips
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine admission of newly issued securities to trading pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security only by its designated codes and trade in the specified lot size.
- Pre-open session norms for IPO and Other category scrips (per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012) apply.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specified tranches of shares as detailed in Annexure I, with release dates ranging from January 04, 2027 to July 04, 2029; 12,500,000 shares are free of lock-in.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing and trading: July 06, 2026
- Circular date: July 03, 2026
- Lock-in release dates: January 04, 2027; July 04, 2027; January 04, 2027 (partial); July 04, 2029 (various tranches per Annexure I)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing notification enabling trading members to trade Aastha Spintex Limited shares on NSE from July 06, 2026. Impact is primarily operational for trading members needing to update systems for the new security code and lot size; market impact is limited to investors in the specific IPO allotment and interested new investors.
