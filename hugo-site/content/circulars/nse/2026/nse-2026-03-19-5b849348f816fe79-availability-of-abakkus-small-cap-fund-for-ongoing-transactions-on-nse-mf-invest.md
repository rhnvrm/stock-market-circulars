## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Abakkus Small Cap Fund of Abakkus Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 20, 2026. Both Direct and Regular Growth plans will be available along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Abakkus Small Cap Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 20, 2026
- Both Direct-Growth (INF2JJD01177) and Regular-Growth (INF2JJD01185) plans are available
- Facilities include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Fund category: Equity (Small Cap)
- AMC: Abakkus Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine platform availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. NSE members facilitating MF transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes from March 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 20, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Abakkus Small Cap Fund for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This circular enables retail and institutional investors to transact in Abakkus Small Cap Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors previously unable to subscribe or redeem due to the fund being closed can now do so. The reopening provides additional liquidity options for existing unit holders and new investment access for prospective investors in the small cap equity category.
