## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 409/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74508) dated June 02, 2026, adding 9 securities to the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I framework effective June 04, 2026. No securities have been added to Stage II, and no securities are moving between stages. The action follows earlier ASM circulars from 2018 through 2024.

## Key Points

- 9 securities have been included under ST-ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 04, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage II inclusions: Nil
- Securities moving from Stage I to Stage II: Nil
- Securities moving from Stage II to Stage I: Nil
- Enhanced margin requirements apply to all open positions as on June 03, 2026 and new positions from June 04, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to ST-ASM Stage I are subject to heightened margin requirements as prescribed under the ST-ASM framework. This circular follows the established ASM framework circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, NSE/SURV/64066) issued between October 2018 and September 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

**For ST-ASM Stage I securities:**
- Applicable margin rate: 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher
- Maximum margin cap: 100%
- Applies to: all open positions as on June 03, 2026 and all new positions created from June 04, 2026

**For ST-ASM Stage II securities (currently Nil):**
- Applicable margin rate: 100% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher
- Maximum margin cap: 100%

All NSE members must ensure compliance with enhanced margin collection for the listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Effective Date for Enhanced Margins:** June 04, 2026
- **Reference Date for Open Positions:** June 03, 2026 (positions open on this date subject to new margins from June 04, 2026)
