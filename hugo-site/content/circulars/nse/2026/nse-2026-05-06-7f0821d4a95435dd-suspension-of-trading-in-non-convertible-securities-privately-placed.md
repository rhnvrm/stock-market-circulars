## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No: 0741/2026) has notified the suspension of trading in privately placed non-convertible securities of Trent Limited under the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2. The suspension takes effect from May 07, 2026, due to the redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Trent Limited's non-convertible security with ISIN INE849A08082 will be suspended from trading effective May 07, 2026.
- The reason for suspension is redemption of the security.
- The suspension is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- The circular is addressed to all NSE members.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular is an operational notification under the existing framework of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.2), which governs trading suspensions for non-convertible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in Trent Limited's non-convertible security (ISIN: INE849A08082) from May 07, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and notify relevant clients holding or trading this security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 06, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is a routine administrative action triggered by the upcoming redemption of Trent Limited's privately placed non-convertible security. Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debt instrument (ISIN: INE849A08082) on the NSE debt market segment. No broader equity market impact is expected. Holders of this security should be aware that trading will no longer be available post-suspension and should plan accordingly ahead of the redemption.
