## Summary

NSE has announced that Avience Biomedicals Limited (symbol: AVIENCE), which was listed under the trade-for-trade segment (series ST) following its SME IPO, will be transferred to the rolling segment (series SM) with effect from July 10, 2026. This is a standard transition mandated under SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- AVIENCE will move from series ST (trade-for-trade) to series SM (rolling segment) on July 10, 2026.
- The transfer follows Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74863 dated June 24, 2026 and SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.
- This is applicable to Avience Biomedicals Limited, an SME IPO listed on NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, newly listed SME IPO securities are initially placed in the trade-for-trade segment and subsequently moved to the rolling segment after the mandatory period. This circular effectuates that transition for AVIENCE.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members should note the change in trading series for AVIENCE from ST to SM effective July 10, 2026, and update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Prior circular NSE/CML/74863 issued regarding AVIENCE's listing.
- **June 25, 2026**: This circular issued.
- **July 10, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from trade-for-trade (ST) to rolling segment (SM).

## Impact Assessment

The transition from trade-for-trade to rolling settlement is a routine post-IPO milestone for SME listings. From July 10, 2026, intraday trading will be permitted in AVIENCE, improving liquidity and price discovery. Members and investors holding or trading AVIENCE should be aware of the series change to avoid order entry errors.
