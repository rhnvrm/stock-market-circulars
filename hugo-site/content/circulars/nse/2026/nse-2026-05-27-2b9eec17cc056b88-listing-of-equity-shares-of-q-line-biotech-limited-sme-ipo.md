## Summary

Q-Line Biotech Limited will be listed on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform effective May 29, 2026 under the ticker symbol QLINE. The equity shares will trade in Series ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, Settlement Type 'W') initially and will subsequently be shifted to Series SM (Normal Rolling Segment) as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** QLINE
- **ISIN:** INE1G2W01011
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 343/- per share
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/-
- **Total Securities:** 2,33,28,199 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 23328199
- **Market Lot (Minimum):** 400 equity shares
- **Market Maker Maximum Permissible Spread:** Within 10%
- **Market Maker:** Hem Finlease Private Limited, Jaipur
- **Pre-open session:** Applicable under SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent:** Purva Sharegistry (India) Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission of securities and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes) and applicable SEBI circulars governing SME IPO listings and pre-open sessions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security solely by its designated code (QLINE) for trading purposes.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 400 equity shares.
- Members must observe the pre-open session requirements for IPO and Other category scrips.
- Lock-in restrictions apply as per Annexure I: various tranches of promoter/pre-IPO shares are locked in up to May 31, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (multiple tranches):** May 31, 2027
- **Financial Year:** April 2026 – March 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing on the NSE EMERGE platform. The initial placement in Series ST (Trade for Trade) limits speculative activity in early trading days and reduces settlement risk. The mandatory market lot of 400 shares at Rs. 343 per share implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,37,200, which is typical for SME listings. The market maker (Hem Finlease Private Limited) provides liquidity support with a maximum spread of 10%. Lock-in provisions on promoter shares until May 2027 ensure promoter commitment. Overall impact is contained to investors specifically tracking SME/EMERGE listings.
