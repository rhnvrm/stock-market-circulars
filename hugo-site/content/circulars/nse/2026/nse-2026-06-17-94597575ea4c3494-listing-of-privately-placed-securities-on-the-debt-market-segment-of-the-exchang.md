## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed Commercial Papers (CPs) from 10 issuers on its Debt Market Segment with effect from June 17, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. All securities were allotted on June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 fresh Commercial Paper issuances admitted to the NSE Debt Market Segment effective June 17, 2026
- All CPs carry a face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit
- Allotment date for all securities: June 16, 2026
- Redemption dates range from August 26, 2026 to September 15, 2026
- Issuers span financial institutions, housing finance, cement, and property sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, which mandates notification of newly admitted debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required for market participants beyond standard debt market trading procedures. Members are notified of the designated security codes (ISINs) for the newly listed CPs.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment (all issuances):** June 16, 2026
- **Effective listing date:** June 17, 2026
- **Redemption – TPL CP 26/08/26 (Tata Projects):** August 26, 2026
- **Redemption – UCL CP 11/09/26 (UltraTech Cement, Sr -):** September 11, 2026
- **Redemption – all remaining CPs:** September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with no material market or regulatory impact. The newly listed CPs provide short-term debt instruments (approximately 3-month tenor) from well-known issuers including NABARD, SIDBI, UltraTech Cement, Godrej Properties, Godrej Industries, PNB Housing Finance, Aditya Birla Money, Motilal Oswal Finvest, and Tata Projects. Collectively, the issuances represent significant institutional short-term funding activity (e.g., SIDBI alone issued 70,000 units; NABARD issued 36,000 units). No equity market or retail investor action is required.
