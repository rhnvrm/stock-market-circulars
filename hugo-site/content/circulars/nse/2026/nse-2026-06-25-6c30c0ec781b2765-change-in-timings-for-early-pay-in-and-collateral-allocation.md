## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the Early Pay-in (EPI) and collateral allocation timings for July 03, 2026, in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/DOP/P/CIR/2022/10 dated July 27, 2022, pertaining to the settlement of running accounts of client funds held with Trading Members.

## Key Points

- EPI of Funds final cut-off time moved earlier from 07:45 PM to 07:00 PM.
- EPI of Securities final cut-off time moved earlier from 09:00 PM to 07:00 PM.
- EPI of Securities for clients clearing directly with UPI block facility extended from 08:00 PM to 10:30 PM.
- Download of margin report window extended from 06:00 PM to 09:00 PM.
- Collateral up-streaming and down-streaming window for cash and fixed deposit receipts (FDR) remains open till 8:00 PM.
- Collateral allocation window remains open till 10:00 PM.

## Regulatory Changes

The revisions are made pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/DOP/P/CIR/2022/10 (July 27, 2022) on settlement of running accounts of client funds lying with Trading Members. The specific cut-off times for EPI of funds and securities are being brought forward for the July 03, 2026 settlement cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the revised EPI cut-off timings for July 03, 2026.
- Members should ensure EPI of Funds is completed by 07:00 PM (revised from 07:45 PM).
- Members should ensure EPI of Securities is completed by 07:00 PM (revised from 09:00 PM), except for clients using the UPI block facility (extended to 10:30 PM).
- Collateral placement/release requests should be submitted within the updated windows.
- NSE Clearing will endeavour to release Cash/FDR/BG within one hour of receiving a request from a clearing member, subject to post-margin-check collateral availability.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 03, 2026 (revised timings apply to this settlement date only)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on Members, Custodians, and PCMs active in the Capital Market Segment. The earlier EPI of Funds and Securities cut-offs (both moved to 07:00 PM) require members to ensure client funds and securities are submitted before the revised deadlines on July 03, 2026. The extension of the UPI block facility EPI window to 10:30 PM provides additional flexibility for clients using that mechanism. The margin report download window has been extended to 09:00 PM, giving members more time for reconciliation. No broad market or stock-specific impact is anticipated.
