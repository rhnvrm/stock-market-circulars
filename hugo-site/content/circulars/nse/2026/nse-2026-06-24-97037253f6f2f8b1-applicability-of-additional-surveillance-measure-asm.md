## Summary

NSE has issued a circular on the Applicability of Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM), announcing changes to the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) Framework effective June 25, 2026. Four securities are newly shortlisted under LTASM Stage-I, while one security is excluded from the ASM framework (moved to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure framework). No stage escalations or de-escalations occurred this period.

## Key Points

- 4 securities added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I effective June 25, 2026
- 2 of the 4 additions (AEROENTER, JAYBARMARU) were moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to LTASM framework
- No securities shortlisted for LTASM Stage-IV in this circular
- No inter-stage movements (Stage I→II, II→III, II→IV, III→IV, IV→III, III→II, II→I) — all Nil
- RAMASTEEL excluded from ASM Framework and moved to the ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure) framework

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities are newly shortlisted under the **Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I** w.e.f. June 25, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN | Note |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|------|
| 1 | AEROENTER | Aeroflex Enterprises Limited | INE065D01027 | Moved from STASM to LTASM |
| 2 | DENORA | De Nora India Limited | INE244A01016 | Fresh inclusion |
| 3 | JAYBARMARU | Jay Bharat Maruti Limited | INE571B01036 | Moved from STASM to LTASM |
| 4 | KIRLOSENG | Kirloskar Oil Engines Limited | INE146L01010 | Fresh inclusion |

The following security is **excluded from the ASM Framework** w.e.f. June 25, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN | Note |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|------|
| 1 | RAMASTEEL | Rama Steel Tubes Limited | INE230R01035 | Moved from LTASM to ESM framework |

**Note on Stage-IV:** Securities shortlisted under LTASM Stage-IV (Criteria VII) are shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade for Trade (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis (T-day being the date of circular issuance).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and brokers must apply the applicable ASM trading restrictions for newly included securities from June 25, 2026 onwards
- AEROENTER and JAYBARMARU, transitioning from STASM to LTASM, will be subject to LTASM-specific margin and surveillance requirements
- RAMASTEEL participants should note the transition to the stricter ESM framework with corresponding ESM-applicable trading restrictions
- No immediate Trade-for-Trade (BE series) migration is triggered in this circular as no security is placed in Stage-IV

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 25, 2026 — all inclusions, exclusions, and stage changes take effect
- **Circular Date:** June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a **moderate impact** on the affected securities:

- **AEROENTER (Aeroflex Enterprises)** and **JAYBARMARU (Jay Bharat Maruti)**: Escalation from STASM to LTASM implies prolonged surveillance concern; investors should exercise caution. LTASM carries additional margin obligations.
- **DENORA (De Nora India)** and **KIRLOSENG (Kirloskar Oil Engines)**: Fresh entrants into LTASM Stage-I signal NSE-flagged anomalies in price/volume behavior. Trading activity may face heightened scrutiny.
- **RAMASTEEL (Rama Steel Tubes)**: Moving to ESM represents a more serious escalation than ASM removal suggests; ESM imposes stricter trade-for-trade and additional surveillance conditions.
- No broad market impact; all changes are security-specific. The consolidated ASM list continues to cover a large number of securities across various stages.
