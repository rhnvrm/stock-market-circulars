## Summary

UTI Mutual Fund has issued Addendum No. 17/2026-27 announcing changes to the scheme provisions of UTI Liquid Fund, effective June 24, 2026. Key changes include a reduction in the minimum initial investment for the Growth Option, introduction of a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility, revised minimum additional purchase amounts, and significantly lowered minimum redemption amounts under the Growth Option.

## Key Points

- Minimum initial investment for Growth Option reduced from Rs. 500/- to Rs. 100/-
- SIP facility introduced with minimums of Rs. 100/- for Daily/Weekly/Monthly plans and Rs. 1,500/- for Quarterly plans
- Minimum additional (lumpsum) purchase for Growth Option reduced from Rs. 500/- to Rs. 100/-
- Minimum redemption amount for Growth Option reduced from Rs. 500/- to Re. 1/-
- All changes are effective June 24, 2026, on a prospective basis
- Instant Access Facility (IAF) provisions remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

| Parameter | Existing | Revised |
|---|---|---|
| Min. Initial Investment – Growth Option | Rs. 500/- | Rs. 100/- |
| Min. Initial Investment – Other Options | Rs. 20,000/- | Rs. 20,000/- (unchanged) |
| Min. Additional Purchase – Growth Option | Rs. 500/- | Rs. 100/- |
| Min. Additional Purchase – Other Options | Rs. 500/- | Rs. 500/- (unchanged) |
| SIP – Daily/Weekly/Monthly | Not available | Rs. 100/- & multiples of Re. 1/- |
| SIP – Quarterly | Not available | Rs. 1,500/- & multiples of Re. 1/- |
| Min. Redemption – Growth Option | Rs. 500/- | Re. 1/- |
| Min. Redemption – Other Options | Rs. 500/- | Rs. 500/- (unchanged) |

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE MF Invest platform must update its transaction processing rules to reflect the new minimum amounts effective June 24, 2026
- Distributors and investment advisers should update client communication materials to reflect the revised thresholds
- Investors wishing to utilise the new SIP facility may register from June 24, 2026 onwards

## Important Dates

- **Addendum Date:** June 5, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 24, 2026 (prospective basis)

## Impact Assessment

The changes lower the barrier to entry for retail investors in the Growth Option of UTI Liquid Fund, making it more accessible with a reduced minimum investment of Rs. 100/-. The introduction of SIP makes it easier for investors to systematically invest in a liquid fund, which is typically used for short-term cash management. The near-zero minimum redemption (Re. 1/-) for the Growth Option provides greater flexibility for partial withdrawals. Overall impact is limited to investors and distributors dealing specifically with UTI Liquid Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform.
