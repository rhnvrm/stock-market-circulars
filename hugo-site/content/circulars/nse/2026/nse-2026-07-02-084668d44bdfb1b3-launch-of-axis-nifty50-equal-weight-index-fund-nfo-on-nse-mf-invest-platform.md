## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Axis Nifty50 Equal Weight Index Fund New Fund Offer (NFO), sponsored by Axis Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular provides scheme details including ISIN codes, category, and allotment date, along with the deadline for funds settlement.

## Key Points

- Two scheme variants are being launched: Axis Nifty50 Equal Weight Index Fund Direct Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1564) and Axis Nifty50 Equal Weight Index Fund Regular Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1556)
- Both schemes fall under the Equity category
- Allotment date for both schemes is July 23, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account before the settlement deadline

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notification enabling a new mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting on behalf of investors in this NFO must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 23, 2026, till 12:30 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Period: July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: July 23, 2026, till 12:30 pm
- Allotment Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to a standard NFO launch on the NSE MF Invest distribution platform. It is primarily operational, informing NSE members of the process and timelines for facilitating investor subscriptions to the new index fund scheme.
