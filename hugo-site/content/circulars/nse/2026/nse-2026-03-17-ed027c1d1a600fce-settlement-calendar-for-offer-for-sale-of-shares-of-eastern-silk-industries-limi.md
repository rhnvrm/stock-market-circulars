## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has published the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Eastern Silk Industries Limited. The OFS is scheduled across two order dates — March 18, 2026 (non-retail) and March 19, 2026 (retail and non-retail) — with all settlements due on March 20, 2026. This circular references the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025, which established that OFS settlements follow existing secondary market transaction rules.

## Key Points

- Two settlement numbers apply: 2026053 (order date March 18) and 2026054 (order date March 19)
- Settlement date for both is March 20, 2026
- Non-retail (IS) category: 100% margin with custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM on the order date; 0% margin with confirmation by 7:30 AM on settlement day
- Retail (RS) category: 100% margin, no custodial confirmation required (NA)
- Funds pay-in deadline: T+1 day by 11:00 AM
- Securities and funds pay-out: T+1 day by 1:30 PM
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Settlement follows existing secondary market transaction rules as established by circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the specified settlement calendar
- Custodial confirmation for non-retail (IS) orders must be completed by the stated deadlines
- Funds pay-in must be completed by T+1 day 11:00 AM
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement day

## Important Dates

| Event | Date / Time |
|---|---|
| OFS Order Date (Non-Retail) | March 18, 2026 |
| OFS Order Date (Retail & Non-Retail) | March 19, 2026 |
| Custodial Confirmation (IS, Settlement 2026053) | March 18, 2026 by 3:30 PM |
| Custodial Confirmation (IS, Settlement 2026054) | March 19, 2026 by 3:30 PM |
| Settlement Date (Both 2026053 & 2026054) | March 20, 2026 |
| Funds Pay-in Deadline | March 20, 2026 by 11:00 AM |
| Securities & Funds Pay-out | March 20, 2026 by 1:30 PM |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Eastern Silk Industries OFS. Participants must ensure timely custodial confirmations and fund transfers to meet the T+1 settlement obligations. The retail segment has no custodial confirmation requirement, simplifying participation for retail investors. No broader market impact is anticipated beyond the specific OFS participants.
