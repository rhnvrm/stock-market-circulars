## Summary

NSE has informed members that the ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) scheme of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund under SIF of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 10, 2026
- Both Regular Growth and Direct Growth plan variants will be available
- Facility covers ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment (Ref: NSE/NMFTM/74621)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding the reopening of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members facilitating transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of this scheme from June 10, 2026, and ensure their systems are updated accordingly to support subscription, redemption, and SIP transactions.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has limited broader market impact. It provides investors access to ICICI Prudential's ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund through NSE's MF Invest Platform. The two available variants are:

| Sr. No | Scheme Name | ISIN | Category |
|--------|-------------|------|----------|
| 1 | ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund - Regular Growth | INF109K30059 | Equity |
| 2 | ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund - Direct Growth | INF109K30067 | Equity |

The Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category targets sophisticated investors; operational impact is confined to NSE members offering mutual fund investment services through the platform.
