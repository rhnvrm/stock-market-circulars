## Summary

NSE has informed members about a scheme name change by Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform. The scheme "Motilal Oswal Gold and Silver ETFs Fund of Funds" has been renamed to "Motilal Oswal Gold and Silver Passive Fund of Funds" effective January 14, 2026. This change is to align the scheme name with its category classification.

## Key Points

- Previous scheme name: Motilal Oswal Gold and Silver ETFs Fund of Funds
- New scheme name: Motilal Oswal Gold and Silver Passive Fund of Funds
- Change is effective from January 14, 2026
- Name change is for alignment with scheme category
- No changes to scheme operations or investment strategy

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative nomenclature update to better reflect the scheme's classification as a passive fund of funds.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members should update their systems with the revised scheme name
- Investors should note the new scheme name for all future transactions and communications
- All future references on the NSE MF Invest platform will reflect the updated name

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: Effective date of scheme name change
- **January 15, 2026**: Non-business day for all Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund schemes due to Maharashtra municipal elections
- **January 16, 2026**: Circular date; transactions from January 15 processed on this date

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change with no effect on:
- Investment strategy or portfolio composition
- Scheme operations or management
- Investor holdings or unit values
- Transaction processing

The name change is purely for better alignment with SEBI's scheme categorization norms. Existing investors are not required to take any action.
