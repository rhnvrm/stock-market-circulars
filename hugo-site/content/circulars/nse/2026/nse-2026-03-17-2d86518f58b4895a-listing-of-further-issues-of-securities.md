## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 13 companies, effective March 18, 2026. The shares are primarily allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment. Each security is identified by its designated symbol and ISIN, and trading will be conducted in the specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have further issues of securities listed on NSE effective March 18, 2026
- Most allotments are under ESOP schemes
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable on any of the listed further issues
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of further issues to dealings and lot size specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- Securities are identified only by their designated codes on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 17, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 18, 2026
- **Allotment Dates by Company:**
  - AU Small Finance Bank Limited (AUBANK): March 12, 2026
  - Devyani International Limited (DEVYANI): February 10, 2026
  - Globus Spirits Limited (GLOBUSSPR): February 11, 2026
  - Go Digit General Insurance Limited (GODIGIT): March 9, 2026
  - IDFC First Bank Limited (IDFCFIRSTB): March 12, 2026
  - Ivalue Infosolutions Limited (IVALUE): Not specified in extracted content

## Impact Assessment

**AU Small Finance Bank (AUBANK):** 133,465 equity shares of Rs. 10 allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE949L01017). Minor dilution impact.

**Devyani International (DEVYANI):** 67,500 equity shares of Re. 1 allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE872J01023). Minor dilution impact.

**Globus Spirits (GLOBUSSPR):** 95,834 equity shares of Rs. 10 allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE615I01010). Minor dilution impact.

**Go Digit General Insurance (GODIGIT):** 19,975 equity shares of Rs. 10 allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE03JT01014). Minimal dilution impact.

**IDFC First Bank (IDFCFIRSTB):** 2,016,620 equity shares of Rs. 10 allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE092T01019). Largest issuance in this batch; modest dilution given the bank's large share capital.

**Ivalue Infosolutions (IVALUE):** Details partially available; face value Rs. 2 (ISIN: INE056801025).

Overall, these are routine ESOP-related further issues. The additions are relatively small compared to total outstanding shares for each company and represent standard employee compensation-related dilution. Existing shareholders may see minor dilution effects.
