## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 10, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The annexure lists fresh issues of commercial papers (CPs) from eight issuers and one other debt security (non-convertible debenture) from PNB Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Nine fresh debt instruments admitted to trading on NSE's Debt Market Segment.
- Commercial Papers listed: ICICI Securities Limited (ISEC CP 08/10/26, ISIN INE763G14L94), Godrej Properties Limited (GPL CP 27/08/26 Sr 52, ISIN INE484J14E64), CESC Limited (CESC CP 31/08/26, ISIN INE486A14GF2), Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited (AAFS CP 08/07/2027, ISIN INE0L6814167), UltraTech Cement Limited (UCL CP 07/10/26, ISIN INE481G14FV9), CEAT Limited (CL CP 07/08/26, ISIN INE482A14GS4), Godrej Industries Limited (GIL CP 29/09/26 Sr 39, ISIN INE233A149J7), Nu Vista Limited (NVL CP 22/09/26, ISIN INE973U14300), and HDFC Securities Limited (HSL CP 30/09/2026, ISIN INE700G14UU3).
- Other debt security listed: PNB Housing Finance Limited debenture (PHFL 7.83% 2029 LXXVII, ISIN INE572E07308) with a 7.83% coupon rate, allotted at multiple issue prices, redeemable on August 7, 2029.
- Face value of all commercial papers is Rs. 500,000 per unit, issued at a discount reflecting the implied yield; quantities range from 500 to 7,000 units across issuers.
- The PNB Housing Finance debenture has a face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit with 50,000 units allotted (partly at Rs. 100,018.18 and partly at Rs. 100,000 for a 4,500-unit tranche).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations rather than a change to rules or regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from trading members beyond noting the new security codes and ISINs for the listed instruments to enable trading on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 10, 2026
- Dates of allotment: July 9, 2026 (most issues) and July 10, 2026 (HDFC Securities CP)
- Redemption dates range from August 7, 2026 (CEAT Limited CP) to July 8, 2027 (Alpha Alternatives Financial Services CP), with the PNB Housing Finance debenture maturing August 7, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it is an administrative notification enabling trading of privately placed debt securities on NSE's Debt Market Segment. It is primarily relevant to debt market participants, institutional investors, and trading members dealing in commercial paper and corporate debentures, with no direct effect on equity markets or listed stock prices.
