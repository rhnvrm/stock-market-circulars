## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Mirae Asset Nifty India Infrastructure & Logistics ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from January 27, 2026 to February 09, 2026 until 11:59 PM. Both Regular and Direct plan variants will be available with the same ISIN (INF769K01QB4) under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: January 27, 2026 to February 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Scheme: Mirae Asset Nifty India Infrastructure & Logistics ETF
- Two plans available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan
- Both plans share ISIN: INF769K01QB4
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: February 12, 2026
- Fund House: Mirae Asset Mutual Fund
- Platform: NSE MF Invest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 11, 2026 11:59 PM
- Members need to facilitate transactions for this NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 23, 2026
- **NFO Opening Date**: January 27, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: February 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Fund Payment Deadline**: February 11, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO launch announcement. The impact is limited to:

- Investors interested in infrastructure and logistics sector exposure through ETF route
- NSE members who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform
- Expands investment options in the thematic/sectoral ETF space focusing on infrastructure and logistics sectors
- No impact on existing securities or trading operations
- Operational impact limited to members processing NFO subscriptions during the specified period
