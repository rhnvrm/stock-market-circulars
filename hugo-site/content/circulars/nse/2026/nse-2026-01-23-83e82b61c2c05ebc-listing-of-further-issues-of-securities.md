## Summary

NSE has admitted further securities of 10 companies to the Capital Market segment with effect from January 27, 2026. The securities comprise equity shares allotted primarily under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) for companies including Axis Bank Limited, Genesys International Corporation Limited, HDFC Bank Limited, Home First Finance Company India Limited, SRG Housing Finance Limited, TATA Consumer Products Limited, Urban Company Limited, Zota Health Care Limited, IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust, and Som Distilleries & Breweries Limited.

## Key Points

- Further securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Effective date: January 27, 2026
- Market lot size: 1 for all securities
- All shares are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in period applicable
- Securities allotted primarily under ESOP schemes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading on the system
- Securities must be traded in specified market lot sizes (1 share per lot)
- All trading participants to note the effective date of January 27, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: 
  - January 13, 2026: Genesys International, SRG Housing Finance
  - January 20, 2026: Axis Bank, Home First Finance
  - January 21, 2026: HDFC Bank

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine listings of further securities issued under ESOP schemes. The number of shares being listed represents a small fraction of the existing float for most companies. HDFC Bank has the largest allotment at 2.29 million shares. All securities are pari passu with existing shares and have no lock-in restrictions, making them immediately tradeable.
