## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on its Capital Market segment, admitting a fresh non-convertible debenture (NCD) issue by Ambium Finserve Limited for dealing effective July 06, 2026. The circular specifies the designated security code, lot size, coupon rate, and maturity date for the instrument as per Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue: Ambium Finserve Limited NCD listed under symbol "1116AFL28", series N0
- Market lot fixed at 1
- Issue description: AFL 11.16% 2028
- Coupon rate: 11.16%
- Date of maturity: 06-Mar-2028
- ISIN Code: INE0RU307262
- Trading in the security will be identified solely by its designated security code and traded in the specified lot size

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (listing of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trading in this security is conducted only by its designated security code and in the specified lot size of 1 from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 03, 2026
- Effective date of listing and trading: July 06, 2026
- Maturity date of the NCD: March 06, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a narrow, instrument-specific impact, formalizing exchange-level trading access for a single privately placed NCD issue. It does not affect broader market operations, existing listed securities, or trading members beyond enabling dealings in this new debt instrument. Relevant primarily to institutional and private investors holding or trading Ambium Finserve Limited's privately placed debentures.
