## Summary

NSE will conduct mock bidding sessions on the eOFS (Offer for Sale) platform in live environment on January 03, 2026. Two sessions are scheduled: Session 1 from 11:00 AM to 02:00 PM and Session 2 from 02:45 PM to 03:30 PM. Members are encouraged to participate actively. The mock session uses test securities (TESTOFS1 and TESTOFS2) and will not create any actual obligations for funds or securities.

## Key Points

- Mock bidding sessions scheduled for January 03, 2026 in live environment
- Two sessions: 11:00 AM - 02:00 PM and 02:45 PM - 03:30 PM
- Platform URL: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Test securities include TESTOFS1 (Non-Retail) and TESTOFS2 (Retail and Employee categories)
- No actual obligations for funds or securities from mock bids
- Members encouraged to participate actively
- Support available via toll-free number 1800 26600 50 (Option 1)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock testing exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock bidding session
- Members should monitor www.nseindia.com for any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session
- For queries, members should contact the toll-free helpline at 1800 26600 50 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **January 01, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 03, 2026**: Mock bidding sessions
  - Session 1: 11:00 AM to 02:00 PM
  - Session 2: 02:45 PM to 03:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock testing session with no real market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members can use this opportunity to test their systems and familiarize themselves with the eOFS platform in a live environment without any financial or securities obligations.
