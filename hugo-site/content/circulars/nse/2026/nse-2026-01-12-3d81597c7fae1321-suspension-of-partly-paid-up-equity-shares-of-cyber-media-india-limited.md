## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in partly paid-up equity shares of Cyber Media (India) Limited (Symbol: CYBERPP, ISIN: IN9278G01019) effective from January 14, 2026. The suspension is being implemented on account of first and final call money and will take effect from the closing hours of trading on January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to partly paid-up equity shares only
- Symbol affected: CYBERPP
- ISIN: IN9278G01019
- Reason: First and final call money
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: 0052/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action is taken in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and investors holding partly paid-up shares of Cyber Media (India) Limited must settle first and final call money obligations
- Trading members should ensure no further trades are executed in CYBERPP from January 14, 2026 onwards
- Shareholders must respond to the call money notice from the company to convert partly paid shares to fully paid shares

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 13, 2026**: Last day of trading (suspension effective from closing hours)
- **January 14, 2026**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Investors holding partly paid-up shares will be unable to trade these securities on the exchange, limiting liquidity options until call money is settled.

**Investor Impact**: High - Shareholders must pay the outstanding call money to the company to maintain their shareholding. Failure to respond to the call may result in forfeiture of shares as per company law provisions.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in CYBERPP and inform clients holding these securities about the suspension and call money obligations.
