## Summary

Teja Engineering Industries Limited (Scrip Code: TEIN) has listed its equity shares on the NSE SME platform following its initial public offering. The company has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern (Format of Holding of Specified Securities) as required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, disclosing the distribution of equity ownership between promoters and public shareholders immediately after listing.

## Key Points

- Total post-issue equity shares outstanding: 6,417,300
- Total number of shareholders: 289
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 5 shareholders holding 4,310,270 shares (67.17% of total)
- Public shareholders: 284 shareholders holding 2,107,030 shares (32.83% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities/warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged/encumbered promoter shares have been declared
- 100% of promoter shareholding (4,310,270 shares) is held in dematerialized form
- Of public shareholding, 409,030 shares (19.41%) are held in dematerialized form as reported
- No shares held under Non-Promoter Non-Public category (including employee trusts or depository receipts)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine post-listing disclosure filed under Regulation 31(1) of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, applicable to the quarter ending 03/07/2026.

## Compliance Requirements

The listed entity is required to maintain and periodically update its shareholding pattern disclosures under Regulation 31(1)(a)/(b)/(c), including declarations on partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and promoter share encumbrances. Any future changes to capital structure (e.g., ESOP conversions, warrant exercises) will require updated filings.

## Important Dates

- Listing/reporting reference date: 03/07/2026 (quarter-end reporting date referenced in the filing)
- Circular publication date: 2026-07-06

## Impact Assessment

This filing formalizes the entry of Teja Engineering Industries Limited into the NSE SME segment, providing transparency on ownership concentration. With promoters holding a majority stake (67.17%) and no encumbrances, pledges, or locked-in shares reported, the disclosure signals a relatively straightforward ownership structure at listing. Market impact is limited to investors and stakeholders tracking this specific SME stock, with no broader market-wide implications.
