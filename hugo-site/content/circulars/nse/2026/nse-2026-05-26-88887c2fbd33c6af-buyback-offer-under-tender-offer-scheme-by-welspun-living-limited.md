## Summary

NSE has notified all Capital Market segment members of Welspun Living Limited's upcoming buyback offer via the web-based tender offer bidding platform. The offer is a fixed-price buyback of up to 1,44,00,000 equity shares at Rs. 175 per share, open for bidding from May 29 to June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Welspun Living Limited (NSE symbol: WELSPUNLIV, Series: BB) is conducting a buyback under the Tender Offer Scheme per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.
- Issue size: Up to 1,44,00,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 175 per share.
- The bidding platform is available at https://eipo.nseindia.com for all eligible trading members.
- Members already using the web-based IPO bidding platform can use existing credentials; others must log in via the admin terminal (Adminxxxxx format, where xxxxx is the Member Code).
- Eight investor categories are eligible to participate, including FII, MF, IC, FI, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer follows the existing Tender Offer Scheme framework per NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of the Capital Market segment are eligible to participate in the Tender Offer Facility.
- Members without existing platform access must log into the admin terminal and create branches and users prior to participation.
- Custodial confirmations must be submitted within specified timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. during the issue period, and 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on the issue closure date.

## Important Dates

- **Issue Period:** May 29, 2026 to June 4, 2026
- **Bidding Hours:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (daily during issue period)
- **Custodial Confirmation on Closure Day:** 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to NSE trading members participating on behalf of clients in the WELSPUNLIV buyback. Shareholders of Welspun Living Limited across all investor categories (retail, institutional, FII, MF, etc.) may tender shares at the fixed price of Rs. 175. The offer is of moderate market impact, limited to existing shareholders deciding whether to participate in the buyback at the stated price.
