## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed Commercial Papers (CPs) from multiple companies on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange, effective June 01, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. All CPs were allotted on May 29, 2026, with redemptions ranging from June to August 2026.

## Key Points

- 12 Commercial Papers from 10+ companies admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment effective June 01, 2026
- All securities allotted on May 29, 2026
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit across all CPs
- Redemption dates span June 2026 to August 2026
- Security codes assigned as specified in the Annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. Members are notified of the designated security codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 29, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** June 01, 2026
- **Earliest Redemption:** June 18, 2026 (Godrej Properties GPL CP 18/06/26 Sr 28)
- **Latest Redemption:** August 28, 2026 (multiple issuers)
