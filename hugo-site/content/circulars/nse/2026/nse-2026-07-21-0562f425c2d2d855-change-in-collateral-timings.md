## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a revision to collateral addition and release timings, updating its earlier circular (Ref No. 0153/2026 dated April 30, 2026). Based on member feedback, the cut-off times for cash and FDR collateral addition/release have been extended, and a detailed batch-wise timing schedule for release of pledged securities has been introduced. The changes take effect from July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Cash and FDR Addition cut-off extended from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (Intraday) cut-off extended from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (End of Day) cut-off remains unchanged at 8:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (Allocation) cut-off extended from 8:00 PM to 11:30 PM.
- New batch-wise release timings introduced for pledge securities, spanning 16 batches from 8:30 AM to 8:00 PM, plus an End-of-Day (EOD) batch at 9:00 PM.
- Members seeking release of repledged securities for pay-in requirements must submit requests one day prior to the pay-in date, not on the pay-in date itself.

## Regulatory Changes

NCL has revised the operational timelines governing collateral addition and release for cash, FDR, and pledged securities. This supersedes the timing framework set out in the earlier circular (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

All Members, Custodians, and Professional Clearing Members (PCMs) must align their internal collateral management and back-office processes with the revised cut-off times and the new 16-batch pledge securities release schedule. Requests for release of repledged securities needed for pay-in must be submitted at least one day in advance of the pay-in date.

## Important Dates

- Circular issued: July 20, 2026
- Revised timings effective from: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely operational change affecting the back-office and treasury workflows of clearing members, custodians, and PCMs. It extends flexibility for collateral addition and release, potentially improving liquidity management for members, but has no direct bearing on listed securities, trading activity, or investor-facing outcomes.
