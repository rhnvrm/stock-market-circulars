## Summary

National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) has revised the NACH (National Automated Clearing House) mandate form vide Notice No. NPCI/2026-27/NACH/001. NSE's NMF TM Segment has communicated this revision to all members through Circular Ref. No. 1410/2026 and advised them to take note and initiate necessary actions.

## Key Points

- NPCI has issued a revised NACH mandate form under Notice No. NPCI/2026-27/NACH/001.
- The revised NPCI notice is enclosed as Annexure I to the circular for member reference.
- All NSE members are advised to review the revised form and initiate applicable actions.
- Further updates, if any, will be communicated separately by NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

The NACH mandate form has been revised by NPCI. The specific changes to the form are detailed in the attached Annexure I (NPCI Notice No. NPCI/2026-27/NACH/001). Members should refer to the annexure for the exact modifications made to the mandate form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must review the revised NACH mandate form provided in Annexure I.
- Members should discontinue use of the old mandate form and adopt the revised version as applicable.
- Necessary internal processes and documentation should be updated in line with the revised form.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 05, 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned; members are advised to act promptly upon receipt of the circular.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/administrative update with low immediate market impact. It affects members who facilitate NACH-based transactions (e.g., SIP mandates, redemption payouts) in the mutual fund segment. Members will need to update their forms and back-office processes to comply with the revised NACH mandate format issued by NPCI. No direct impact on trading, settlement schedules, or market prices.
