## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to trading on its Debt Market Segment effective February 09, 2026. The securities include commercial papers from eight issuers and municipal bonds from Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation. All securities have been assigned designated security codes and ISINs for trading purposes.

## Key Points

- Total of 9 commercial paper issues listed from various financial institutions and corporates
- NABARD issued 2 CPs totaling 190,000 securities (face value â‚¹5 lakh each)
- SIDBI issued CP of 47,000 securities with maturity on May 08, 2026
- HDFC Securities issued 2 CPs totaling 2,000 securities
- Blue Star Limited issued CP of 2,000 securities maturing May 07, 2026
- Godrej Consumer Products issued CP of 11,000 securities
- Motilal Oswal Financial Services issued 2 CPs (one with 1-year tenure to Feb 05, 2027)
- Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation listed 8.50% bonds maturing in 2036
- All securities allotted between February 06-09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members to note the designated security codes and ISINs for trading purposes
- Securities available for trading on NSE Debt Market Segment from February 09, 2026
- All securities are privately placed and subject to applicable debt market trading regulations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 09, 2026 (listing and trading commencement)
- **Allotment Dates**: February 06-09, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from April 17, 2026 to February 05, 2027 (CPs) and 2036 (municipal bonds)
- **Nearest Maturity**: NABARD CP on April 17, 2026
- **Longest Maturity**: Tiruchirappalli Municipal Corporation bonds in 2036

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing of these privately placed securities does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: No changes to trading systems or procedures. Members can trade these securities using the designated codes starting February 09, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional debt investment options for institutional investors and high net worth individuals participating in private placements. The securities offer tenures ranging from short-term (2-3 months) to long-term (10+ years).

**Issuer Profile**: Mix of development financial institutions (NABARD, SIDBI), established corporates (Blue Star, Godrej Consumer Products), and financial services firms (HDFC Securities, Motilal Oswal), indicating healthy debt market activity across sectors.
