## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by IIFL Samasta Finance Limited, effective June 05, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the maturity of both debenture series.

## Key Points

- Two NCD securities of IIFL Samasta Finance Limited are being suspended from trading on NSE
- Symbol `920IIFLS26` (ISIN: INE413U07285) suspended due to maturity
- Symbol `960IIFLS26` (ISIN: INE413U07335) suspended due to maturity
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

In pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, these securities are being removed from active trading upon reaching their maturity date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified to cease trading in these two NCD symbols effective June 05, 2026
- No further transactions in these securities should be facilitated post suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine end-of-lifecycle action for two maturing NCD series of IIFL Samasta Finance Limited. Holders of these debentures will receive maturity proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. There is no broader market disruption expected; the suspension is a standard administrative step following NCD maturity.
