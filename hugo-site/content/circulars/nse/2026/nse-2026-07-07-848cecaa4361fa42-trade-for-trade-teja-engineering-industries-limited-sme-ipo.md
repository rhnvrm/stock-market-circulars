## Summary

NSE has notified that the securities of Teja Engineering Industries Limited (SME IPO), currently trading under the trade for trade segment (Series: ST), will be transferred to the rolling settlement segment (Series: SM) with effect from July 21, 2026. This follows an earlier Exchange circular (NSE/CML/75069 dated July 06, 2026) and is issued pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular reference no. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Symbol: TEJA
- Company: Teja Engineering Industries Limited
- Security moves from trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to rolling segment (Series: SM)
- Effective date: July 21, 2026
- Issued under Circular Ref. No. 1095/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/CML/75051

## Regulatory Changes

This action is taken pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, which governs the framework for trade for trade segment classification and transfer of securities between trading segments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in trading series for TEJA from ST to SM effective July 21, 2026. No other member action is specified in the circular.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Prior Exchange circular NSE/CML/75069 referenced
- July 07, 2026: Date of this circular
- July 21, 2026: Effective date of transfer to rolling segment (Series: SM)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from trade for trade to rolling settlement segment is generally a positive development for a stock, as it typically indicates reduced regulatory restriction on trading and improved liquidity conditions. Impact is limited to shareholders, prospective investors, and trading members dealing in TEJA shares.
