## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members about the forthcoming listing of units of Bagmane Prime Office REIT on the Exchange under the REITs IPO category. The security will trade under the symbol BAGMANE with ISIN INE2OVN25015. The specific listing date has not been disclosed in this circular and will be communicated separately.

## Key Points

- Bagmane Prime Office REIT units are scheduled to be listed on NSE
- Trading symbol: BAGMANE
- ISIN: INE2OVN25015
- Security type: REIT (Real Estate Investment Trust) units via IPO
- Exact listing date to be announced through a separate circular
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref No. NSE/CML/74171

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural listing announcement informing exchange members of an upcoming security listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are advised to note the forthcoming listing of Bagmane Prime Office REIT units and prepare accordingly for trading once the listing date is formally announced via a subsequent circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 13, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Bagmane Prime Office REIT on NSE expands the available REIT instruments for investors seeking exposure to commercial office real estate in India. Members and investors should monitor NSE communications for the formal listing date announcement. The REIT structure offers regulated, exchange-traded access to real estate assets, which may attract institutional and retail investors diversifying into alternative asset classes.
