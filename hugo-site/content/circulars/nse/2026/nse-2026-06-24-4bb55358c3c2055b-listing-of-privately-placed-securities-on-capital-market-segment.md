## Summary

NSE's Listing Department notifies the admission of a privately placed market-linked security issued by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment, effective June 25, 2026, under Circular Ref. No. 1010/2026.

## Key Points

- Security admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Trading symbol: **AAFS32**, Series: – (not applicable), Market Lot: 1
- Security name: AAFS Market Linked 2032
- ISIN: INE0L6807344
- Coupon Rate: Reset (variable/market-linked)
- Date of Maturity: January 13, 2032
- Securities must be identified by their designated codes for trading purposes
- Lot sizes as specified in the Annexure apply per Regulation 2.5.5

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a routine notification under existing NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Parts A (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5).

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must trade the admitted security using its designated code (AAFS32) only
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size (1 unit per lot)

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026** – Circular issued
- **June 25, 2026** – Effective date; security admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment
- **January 13, 2032** – Maturity date of the security

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with low market impact. The admission of a single privately placed market-linked debt instrument (AAFS Market Linked 2032) from Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited adds a new fixed-income product to the NSE Capital Market segment. Given the private placement nature, the instrument is targeted at institutional/qualified investors and has a market lot of 1. No disruption to existing trading operations is expected.
