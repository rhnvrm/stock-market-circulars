## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74813 (Ref. 464/2026) announcing the applicability of Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), effective June 23, 2026. Vikram Solar Limited (VIKRAMSOLR) has been newly shortlisted under the "Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure" criteria. No securities are moving between Stage I and Stage II, and no securities are being excluded from ASM-IBC in this update.

## Key Points

- Vikram Solar Limited (VIKRAMSOLR, ISIN: INE078V01014) is newly shortlisted under ASM-IBC due to receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure, effective June 23, 2026.
- No securities are being moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I under ASM-IBC in this circular.
- No securities are being excluded from ASM-IBC (Annexure II entries are Nil).
- ASM-IBC framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM-IBC is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.
- The consolidated ASM-IBC list continues to include: ABAN (Aban Offshore Limited, Stage I), AGSTRA (AGS Transact Technologies Limited, Stage I), CLCIND (CLC Industries Limited, Stage I), GENSOL (Gensol Engineering Limited, Stage I), IMPEXFERRO, and others.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in continuation of earlier circulars on ASM-IBC:
- NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018
- NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022
- NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023
- NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025

No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this is a routine update to the list of securities under the existing ASM-IBC framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must take note of the securities listed in Annexure I (newly added under ASM-IBC) and apply applicable surveillance-related trading restrictions effective June 23, 2026.
- Market participants trading in VIKRAMSOLR and other ASM-IBC listed securities must comply with the applicable ASM trading conditions (e.g., trade-for-trade settlement, additional margins as prescribed).
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 23, 2026 (for all changes in Annexure I and Annexure II)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of Vikram Solar Limited (VIKRAMSOLR) under ASM-IBC will subject it to heightened surveillance trading conditions, likely including trade-for-trade settlement and potentially higher margin requirements. This restricts normal trading and may reduce liquidity in the stock. The consolidated ASM-IBC list remains largely unchanged as there are no inter-stage movements or exclusions. Impact is moderate and confined to the specific securities on the list; broader market impact is minimal.
