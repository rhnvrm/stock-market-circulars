## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF New Fund Offer (NFO), under ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 20, 2026 to July 28, 2026 (till 11:30 pm). The circular provides scheme details, ISIN, category, and allotment date, along with the funds pay-in deadline for members.

## Key Points

- Scheme: ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF - Direct Growth and Regular Growth
- ISIN: INF109K1A567 (common to both plans as listed)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: July 31, 2026
- NFO subscription window on NSE MF Invest Platform: July 20, 2026 to July 28, 2026, till 11:30 pm
- Members must ensure clear funds reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 30, 2026, till 12:00 pm

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling transaction of a new ETF NFO scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting on behalf of clients must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 30, 2026, till 12:00 pm, to ensure successful allotment processing.

## Important Dates

- NFO Opens: July 20, 2026
- NFO Closes: July 28, 2026, 11:30 pm
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: July 30, 2026, 12:00 pm
- Allotment Date: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to the operational launch of a single new ETF scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It is primarily relevant to NSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions and investors interested in subscribing to the ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF NFO. No impact on existing listed securities or trading of other instruments is expected.
