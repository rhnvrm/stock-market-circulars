## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of several privately placed debt securities on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange, effective July 14, 2026, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The annexure specifies the newly admitted securities, including commercial papers (CPs) and other debt instruments (NCDs/bonds), along with their designated security codes, ISINs, issue details, allotment and redemption dates.

## Key Points

- Fresh issues of commercial papers admitted include: Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited (TCHFL CP 09/10/26 Sr 1, ISIN INE033L14PC7), SRF Limited (SRF CP 07/10/26 Sr 2, ISIN INE647A14BB9), Tata Projects Limited (TPL CP 09/10/26 Sr 220, ISIN INE725H14EG1), Godrej Properties Limited (GPL CP 31/08/26 Sr 54, ISIN INE484J14E31), Alembic Pharmaceuticals Limited (APL CP 25/09/26, ISIN INE901L14CN0), Aditya Birla Money Limited (ABML CP 23/09/26, ISIN INE865C14QQ9), IIFL Samasta Finance Limited (ISFL CP 08/01/27, ISIN INE413U14372), and Idbi Capital Markets & Securities Limited (ICMSL CP 11/09/2026 Sr 3, ISIN INE835F14187).
- Other debt securities admitted include Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI 7.29% 2029 Sr IV, ISIN INE556F08LG1, face value Rs. 100,000, maturing 09-Nov-2029) and IFL Finance Limited (IFL 11.80% 2028, ISIN INE01XO07074), with the IFL Finance details truncated in the source content.
- All commercial papers were allotted on 10-Jul-2026, with redemption dates ranging from 31-Aug-2026 to 08-Jan-2027.
- Face value for commercial papers is Rs. 500,000 per unit, issued at a discount reflecting the money-market pricing convention.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations for admission of privately placed securities to the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in these securities should update their systems with the designated security codes and ISINs as specified in the annexure to enable trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 14, 2026
- Date of allotment (commercial papers): July 10, 2026
- Redemption dates: range from August 31, 2026 to January 8, 2027 for commercial papers; November 9, 2029 for SIDBI bonds

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is an administrative listing notification for privately placed debt instruments, relevant primarily to institutional debt market participants, custodians, and the issuing companies rather than retail equity investors.
