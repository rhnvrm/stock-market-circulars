## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance activity on the NSE MF Invest Platform that will result in a complete downtime on March 21, 2026 from 01:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs. All members and their API-connected technical teams/vendors must plan their activities around this outage window.

## Key Points

- Complete downtime of NSE MF Invest Platform scheduled for March 21, 2026
- Outage window: 01:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs (4-hour duration)
- All activities and transactions on the platform must be paused during this period
- Members using API connectivity must notify their technical teams and vendors
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment under Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/73294

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities or transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the downtime window (March 21, 2026, 01:00–05:00 hrs)
- Members connecting via API are required to inform their technical team and/or vendor about the scheduled downtime in advance

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 16, 2026
- **Downtime Start:** March 21, 2026, 01:00 hrs
- **Downtime End:** March 21, 2026, 05:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

The maintenance window is a planned 4-hour outage during early morning hours (01:00–05:00 hrs), which minimizes impact on regular trading hours. All members and API-based participants accessing the NSE MF Invest Platform will be affected during this period. Members should schedule any pending transactions or activities either before 01:00 hrs or after 05:00 hrs on March 21, 2026. The off-peak timing of the maintenance window reduces broader market operational impact.
