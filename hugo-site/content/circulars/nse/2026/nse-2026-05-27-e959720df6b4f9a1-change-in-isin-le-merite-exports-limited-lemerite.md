## Summary

NSE has notified a change in ISIN for the equity shares of Le Merite Exports Limited (Symbol: LEMERITE), effective May 29, 2026. The change is a consequence of a sub-division of the face value of equity shares from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share. The new ISIN INE0G1L01025 will apply to all trades executed on and from the ex-date.

## Key Points

- ISIN for LEMERITE equity shares will change to INE0G1L01025 with effect from May 29, 2026.
- The ISIN change is triggered by a stock sub-division (stock split) reducing face value from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share.
- The new ISIN is applicable to all trades done on and from the ex-date of May 29, 2026.
- NSE Circular Ref. No.: 0860/2026; Download Ref. No.: NSE/CML/74438.

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN code for Le Merite Exports Limited equity shares is being updated in the exchange records to reflect the corporate action (sub-division). The old ISIN will be superseded by the new ISIN INE0G1L01025 from the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members are required to note the new ISIN and update their back-office and settlement systems accordingly before May 29, 2026.
- Depository participants and custodians should ensure trades and holdings reflect the new ISIN from the ex-date onwards.
- Orders, trades, and settlement instructions referencing LEMERITE equity shares must use the new ISIN on and after May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Ex-Date / ISIN Change Effective Date:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The stock sub-division will increase the number of equity shares proportionally (5x, given the face value reduction from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2), making each share more affordable and potentially improving liquidity. The ISIN change is a technical necessity accompanying the corporate action. Members, depositories, and investors holding LEMERITE shares must ensure their records are updated to the new ISIN to avoid settlement failures or discrepancies from May 29, 2026 onwards.
