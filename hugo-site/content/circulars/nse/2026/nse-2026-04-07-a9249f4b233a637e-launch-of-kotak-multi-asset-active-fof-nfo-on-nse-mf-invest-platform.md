## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF (Fund of Funds) NFO by Kotak Mutual Fund will be open for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from April 08, 2026 to April 22, 2026. Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Reinvestment/Payout) options. Allotment is scheduled for April 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: April 08, 2026 to April 22, 2026
- Fund: Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF (Kotak Mutual Fund)
- Six scheme variants available: Regular/Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All six schemes are categorized as Equity
- Allotment date for all schemes: April 27, 2026
- Clear funds must reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by April 24, 2026 at 12:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO availability notification under the NMF TM Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before April 24, 2026 by 12:00 PM.
- Members should make the six scheme variants available for client transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform within the NFO window.

## Important Dates

- **April 08, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **April 22, 2026**: NFO subscription closes
- **April 24, 2026, 12:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds to reach Clearing Corporation account
- **April 27, 2026**: Allotment date for all six scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This announcement is relevant to NSE members and investors interested in multi-asset fund-of-funds products. The Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF offers six variants (ISIN: INF174KA1ZR3, INF174KA1ZT9, INF174KA1ZO0, INF174KA1ZQ5, INF174KA1ZS1, INF174KA1ZP7), providing flexibility across plan types and income distribution options. The impact is primarily operational for members who need to facilitate transactions and ensure timely fund transfers. No broader market or regulatory impact is expected.
