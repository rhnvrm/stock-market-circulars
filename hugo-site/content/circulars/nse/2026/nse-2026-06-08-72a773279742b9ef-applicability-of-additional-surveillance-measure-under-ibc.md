## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (NSE/SURV/74608, Ref No. 427/2026) placing four securities under Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for Companies as per Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) Stage I, effective June 09, 2026. No securities are being moved between stages or excluded in this update.

## Key Points

- Four securities shortlisted under ASM-IBC Stage I effective June 09, 2026
- No securities shortlisted via corporate announcement/disclosure criteria in this update
- No stage upgrades (Stage I to Stage II) or downgrades (Stage II to Stage I)
- No securities excluded from ASM-IBC in this update
- ASM-IBC framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in reference to earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/37979 (June 08, 2018), NSE/SURV/52368 (May 20, 2022), NSE/SURV/55582 (February 10, 2023), and NSE/SURV/70074 (September 05, 2025), which established and updated the ASM framework for IBC companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the updated list of securities under ASM-IBC
- Trading in affected securities is subject to enhanced surveillance measures per the ASM-IBC framework
- Market participants must comply with all prevailing surveillance measures applicable alongside ASM-IBC
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 08, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 09, 2026 (ASM-IBC Stage I applicability)
