## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued equity shares of Sabar Flex India Limited (Symbol: SABAR) on the Capital Market segment under SME Emerge, effective March 23, 2026. The circular covers two separate preferential allotments with associated lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Two tranches of further issued equity shares of Sabar Flex India Limited admitted for trading on NSE SME Emerge from March 23, 2026
- ISIN: INE0DZ101013; Series: SM; Face Value: Rs. 10; Market Lot: 5,000
- First allotment: 1,869,157 shares at Rs. 16.05 per share, allotted on December 20, 2023 (Distinctive Nos. 17250208–19119364)
- Second allotment: 100,000 shares at Rs. 24 per share, allotted on February 18, 2025 (Distinctive Nos. 19119365–19219364)
- Both allotments are pari passu with existing shares
- Shares are in dematerialized form under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) pending activation per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A governing admission of securities and lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol SABAR and series SM for all trades
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 5,000 shares
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the specified share tranches until their respective expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **March 23, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading
- **Lock-in expiry dates (First allotment tranches)**:
  - 124,610 shares (17250208–17374817): September 30, 2026
  - 280,373 shares (17374818–17655190): September 30, 2026
  - 124,610 shares (17655191–17779800): September 29, 2026
  - 93,458 shares (17779801–17873258): September 30, 2026
  - 1,246,106 shares (17873259–19119364): September 29, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry date (Second allotment)**: September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for a small-cap SME Emerge company. The addition of 1,969,157 equity shares increases the tradeable float of Sabar Flex India Limited from March 23, 2026, though the majority of shares remain under lock-in until late September 2026. Market impact is minimal and limited to participants trading in this specific scrip on the SME platform.
