## Summary

NSE has notified members of a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of Cochin Shipyard Limited shares, to be conducted on July 07 and July 08, 2026, via the eOFS platform, in accordance with the Interoperability among Clearing Corporations scheme (circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026).

## Key Points

- Eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate via the NSE eOFS platform at https://eofs.nseindia.com.
- Members without Admin user IDs must request them via the ENIT portal (ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS); requests are processed at 11:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., and 3:00 p.m., with only one request permitted per member and no modifications allowed once raised.
- Members must ensure UCC compliance of self (PRO) and clients in the UCI system a day prior to the bidding session; UCCs updated on the bidding day itself will not be permitted to trade.
- UCCs confirmed compliant by the Exchange at 22:00 hrs on the previous day are permitted for the next bidding day.
- Sufficient collateral must be maintained before carrying forward orders to T+1 day; rejected orders due to margin insufficiency cannot be re-placed.
- Issue details: ISIN INE704P01025, up to 66,29,636 equity shares as base offer size, up to 66,29,636 shares as oversubscription option, and an additional 26,308 shares under the Eligible Employee Option (face value Rs 5/- each).
- Seller must intimate intention to exercise oversubscription option by 5:00 p.m. on July 07, 2026.
- Categories include IS (non-retail), RS (retail, bid value ≤ Rs 2 lakh), and ES (employee, bid value ≤ Rs 5 lakh).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this circular implements the OFS mechanism under the existing Interoperability among Clearing Corporations scheme (NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026) and references user/system setup guidance from NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure UCC compliance for self and clients a day prior to bidding.
- Only one Admin User creation request per member; requests cannot be modified once submitted.
- Members must ensure adequate collateral before initiating T+1 carry-forward orders.

## Important Dates

- OFS bidding session: July 07, 2026, and July 08, 2026.
- Oversubscription exercise intimation deadline: on or before 5:00 p.m., July 07, 2026.
- Admin ID request processing times: 11:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., and 3:00 p.m. daily; requests after 3:00 p.m. processed by 8:45 a.m. next day.

## Impact Assessment

The circular is operationally significant for trading members participating in the Cochin Shipyard OFS, requiring timely UCC compliance and platform readiness, but has limited broader market impact beyond the specific bidding window and affected stock.
