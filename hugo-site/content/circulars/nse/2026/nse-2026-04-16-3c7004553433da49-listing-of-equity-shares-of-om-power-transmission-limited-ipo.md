## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Om Power Transmission Limited (symbol: OMPOWER) on the Capital Market segment effective April 17, 2026. The IPO was priced at Rs. 175 per share with a face value of Rs. 10. A total of 34,245,000 equity shares will be admitted to trading, initially in Series 'BE' before being shifted to Series 'EQ'.

## Key Points

- Symbol: **OMPOWER**, ISIN: INE25E901019
- Issue price: Rs. 175 per share; face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Total shares listed: 3,42,45,000 (34.245 million)
- Trading series: Initially 'BE', subsequently shifted to 'EQ' per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu
- Security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (OMPOWER) for all trading activity
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes
- Members are advised to note the pre-open session applicability for this scrip

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2026**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading
- **May 14, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 4,57,512 shares and 8,28,622 shares
- **July 13, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 4,57,513 shares and 8,28,623 shares
- **October 16, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 1,67,47,570 shares and 20,70,000 shares
- **October 16, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for 68,52,430 shares
- **Free (no lock-in)**: 9,75,000 shares (from distinctive nos. 915026–1000000) and 59,17,755 shares (from 28327246–34245000)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of OMPOWER provides market participants with access to a new equity scrip in the power transmission sector. The stock will initially trade in Series 'BE' under the IPO pre-open session framework, ensuring price discovery. The significant portion of shares under lock-in (approximately 71% locked until at least October 2026) limits near-term float, which may affect liquidity. Investors should note the staggered lock-in release schedule when assessing trading volumes and price stability post-listing.
