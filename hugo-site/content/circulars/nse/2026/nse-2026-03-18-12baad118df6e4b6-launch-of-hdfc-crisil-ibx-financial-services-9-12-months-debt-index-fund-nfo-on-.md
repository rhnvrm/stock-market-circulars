## Summary

NSE has informed its members that HDFC CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund NFO by HDFC Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 19, 2026 to March 23, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Allotment is scheduled for March 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: March 19, 2026 to March 23, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KH9) and Direct Plan - Growth Option (ISIN: INF179KC1KI7)
- Both plans fall under the Debt category
- Allotment date for both plans: March 24, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by March 24, 2026 till 11:59 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification for members of NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before March 24, 2026 till 11:59 PM.

## Important Dates

- **March 19, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **March 23, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **March 24, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **March 24, 2026**: Allotment date for both Regular and Direct plans

## Impact Assessment

This circular is informational and operationally routine. It enables NSE members to offer the HDFC CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund NFO to investors via the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund targets short-term debt instruments in the financial services sector, tracked against the CRISIL-IBX index. Impact is limited to members facilitating MF transactions and their clients interested in short-duration debt index products.
