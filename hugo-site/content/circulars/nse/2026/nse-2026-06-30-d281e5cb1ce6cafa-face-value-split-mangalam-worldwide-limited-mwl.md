## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing market participants that the face value and paid-up value of the equity shares of Mangalam Worldwide Limited (MWL) will be changed (split) from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 per share, effective July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Mangalam Worldwide Limited
- Symbol: MWL
- Existing face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share
- New face value and paid-up value: Re. 1 per share
- The split represents a 1:10 sub-division of shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rules introduced; this is a corporate action notification regarding the change in face value of MWL equity shares, implemented in accordance with the company's approved corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants must update their systems to reflect the revised face value and adjusted price/quantity for MWL shares effective the stated date. No specific action required from investors beyond awareness of the adjusted share count and price.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 10, 2026 (face value change takes effect)

## Impact Assessment

The face value split will increase the number of outstanding shares proportionally while reducing the per-share price, with no change to the overall market capitalization of the company. Trading systems, price bands, and historical data references for MWL will require adjustment from the effective date. This is a routine corporate action with limited broader market impact, primarily affecting MWL shareholders and traders.
