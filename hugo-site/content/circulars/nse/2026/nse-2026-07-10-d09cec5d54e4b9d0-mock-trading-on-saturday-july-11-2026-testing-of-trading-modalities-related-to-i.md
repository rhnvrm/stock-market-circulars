## Summary

NSE has issued an update to its earlier circular (NSE/FAOP/75098 dated July 08, 2026) regarding mock trading scheduled for Saturday, July 11, 2026, which is being conducted to test trading modalities related to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment. This update revises the market close timing for Trading Session-1. All other details from the original circular remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- This circular amends only the Trading Session-1 timings within the previously announced mock trading schedule for July 11, 2026.
- The mock trading exercise tests systems and modalities ahead of the planned introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash Market.
- Three trading sessions are scheduled: Session-1, Session-2 (from Primary Site), and Session-3 (from DR Site).
- Trades executed in Session-1 will not carry over into Sessions 2 or 3, but trades from Session-2 will be available in Session-3.
- Members are required to actively participate in all trading sessions.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory change; this is an update to operational timings for a member-facing mock trading/system testing exercise ahead of the CAS rollout in the Equity Cash segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members must note and adhere to the updated Session-1 timings and participate in the mock trading exercise on July 11, 2026, per NSE's instructions. Members should also refer to the original circular (NSE/FAOP/75098) for unchanged session and re-login details.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Date: July 11, 2026 (Saturday)
- Trading Session-1: Pre-Open Open 08:00, Pre-Open Close 08:08, Normal Market Open 08:15, Stock future price band update 09:15, Normal Market Close 10:30, Trade Modification End 10:40
- Trading Session-2 (Primary Site): Pre-Open Open 12:45, Pre-Open Close 12:53, Normal Market Open 13:00, Normal Market Close 15:00
- Trading Session-3 (DR Site): Pre-Open Open 15:30, Pre-Open Close 15:38, Normal Market Open 15:45, Normal Market Close 16:15, Trade Modification End 16:25
- Live Re-login window: 17:30 to 18:00

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, operational circular affecting only trading members participating in the scheduled mock trading exercise. It has no direct bearing on listed securities, investors, or market pricing, but is procedurally important for members to ensure smooth system readiness ahead of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) go-live in the Equity Cash segment.
