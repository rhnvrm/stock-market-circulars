## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of a privately placed security issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) on the Capital Market segment, effective April 29, 2026. The security is a Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) with a coupon rate of 7.6164% maturing in January 2029.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to NSE Capital Market segment effective April 29, 2026
- Issuer: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- Symbol: 7616ABHF29, Series: N0
- Issue description: ABHFL 7.6164% 2029 Sr A1
- Coupon rate: 7.62%
- Date of maturity: January 25, 2029
- ISIN: INE831R07631
- Market lot size: 1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of privately placed securities and specification of lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to use the designated security code (7616ABHF29) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- Trading in this security is only permitted using the designated code as specified

## Important Dates

- Circular date: April 28, 2026
- Effective date for trading: April 29, 2026
- Maturity date of security: January 25, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with limited broad market impact. It enables trading of a single ABHFL NCD (fixed income, 7.62% coupon) on NSE's capital market segment. Relevant primarily to debt market participants, fixed income traders, and investors seeking exposure to Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader compliance obligations for members beyond recognition of the new security code.
