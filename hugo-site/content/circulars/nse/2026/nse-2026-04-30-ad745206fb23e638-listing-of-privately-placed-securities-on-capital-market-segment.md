## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of three privately placed debt securities on the Capital Market segment effective May 04, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The securities belong to Mangalam Worldwide Limited, NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited, and Godrej Finance Limited, each with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Three debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from May 04, 2026
- All securities are in Series N0 with a market lot size of 1
- Securities span maturities from 2029 to 2036
- Coupon rates range from 7.88% to 10%
- Trading must use designated security codes and specified lot sizes per Regulation 2.5.5

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is pursuant to existing Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must trade these securities only using their designated codes (10MWL29, 788NIIF31, 85GFL36)
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit per security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 04, 2026 (commencement of dealings)
- **Maturity Dates:**
  - Mangalam Worldwide Limited (10MWL29): April 28, 2029
  - NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited (788NIIF31): August 28, 2031
  - Godrej Finance Limited (85GFL36): April 28, 2036
