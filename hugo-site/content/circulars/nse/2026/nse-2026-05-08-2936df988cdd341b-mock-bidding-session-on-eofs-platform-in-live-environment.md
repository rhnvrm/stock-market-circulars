## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock bidding session on its eOFS (Electronic Offer for Sale) platform in the Live environment on May 09, 2026. The session runs from 10:00 AM to 3:30 PM and is intended for members to familiarise themselves with the platform. No financial or securities obligations will be incurred from bids placed during the mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock session is scheduled for May 09, 2026, on the live eOFS platform at https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Session timing: Market starts at 10:00 AM, Contingency window 11:00 AM–2:00 PM, Switchover to DR site 2:00 PM–2:45 PM, Market ends at 3:30 PM
- Bids placed during the mock session carry no obligation in terms of funds or securities
- NSE will internally place orders/bids in symbol **TESTOFS1** (Employee Category) as a sanity check
- Members are requested to place bids in Employee Category under symbol **TESTOFS2**
- Three test securities are available: TESTOFS1, TESTOFS2 (Retail + Employee categories), and TESTOFS3 (Institutional category)
- Members are encouraged to participate actively to validate platform readiness

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notification for a scheduled mock drill on the eOFS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members participating in OFS sessions should actively participate in the mock bidding session on May 09, 2026
- Members should place bids under symbol **TESTOFS2** in the Employee Category
- Any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session will be published on www.nseindia.com

## Important Dates

| Market Start Time | 10:00 AM |

| Contingency End Time | 11:00 AM |

| Market End Time | 3:30 PM |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it concerns a mock/test exercise with no real financial obligations. It is operationally relevant for NSE members who participate in Offer for Sale (OFS) transactions, as it provides an opportunity to test platform connectivity and bidding workflows. The three test securities (TESTOFS1, TESTOFS2, TESTOFS3) are dummy instruments with floor prices of Rs. 100 and varying tick sizes. Members should use this session to validate their systems ahead of actual OFS events.
