## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued equity shares of Kody Technolab Limited (Symbol: KODYTECH) on the SME Emerge (Capital Market) segment, effective April 22, 2026. The shares, totalling 153,900 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each, were allotted on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 975 per share and carry a lock-in period expiring October 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 153,900 equity shares of Kody Technolab Limited admitted to NSE SME Emerge dealings from April 22, 2026
- Shares allotted on preferential basis at Rs. 975 per share (face value Rs. 10)
- ISIN: INE0Q7P01013 (temporary ISIN IN8********* applies until permanent ISIN activation per SEBI circular)
- Distinctive number range: 12751561 to 12905460
- Market lot size: 100 shares
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in expiry date: October 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is effected in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (lot size specification)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol KODYTECH and series SM for all trades in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 100 shares
- Shares are subject to lock-in and cannot be transferred/sold until October 30, 2026 by the respective allottees
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in dematerialized form per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012; permanent ISIN INE0Q7P01013 will be activated upon completion of the process

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 19, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** October 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME Emerge listing notification with minimal broader market impact. The addition of 153,900 shares at issue price Rs. 975 represents a preferential allotment that increases the tradeable float of KODYTECH on the SME platform. The lock-in period until October 30, 2026 restricts immediate secondary market availability of these shares. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and potential investors of Kody Technolab Limited on the SME Emerge segment.
