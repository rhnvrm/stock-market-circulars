## Summary

NSE issued its periodic update on the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing proceedings under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). No new securities have been added to the ASM-IBC list in this cycle (Annexure I is Nil across all stages), while one security, Vikram Solar Limited (VIKRAMSOLR), has been excluded from the ASM-IBC framework effective July 02, 2026. The consolidated list of securities currently under ASM-IBC (Stage I) includes AGS Transact Technologies Limited, CLC Industries Limited, Gensol Engineering Limited, Impex Ferro Tech Limited, and others.

## Key Points

- No securities were newly shortlisted under ASM-IBC (Stage I, or Stage I-to-II/II-to-I transitions) in this update.
- Vikram Solar Limited (ISIN INE078V01014) has been excluded from ASM for Companies under IBC with effect from July 02, 2026.
- The consolidated ASM-IBC list continues to include AGS Transact Technologies Limited, CLC Industries Limited, Gensol Engineering Limited, Impex Ferro Tech Limited, and other securities under Stage I.
- The ASM-IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- NSE clarifies that shortlisting under ASM-IBC is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under the existing ASM-IBC framework established via earlier circulars (NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018, NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022, NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023, and NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025). No new regulatory framework changes were introduced; this update reflects only the periodic reassessment of securities against existing ASM-IBC inclusion and exclusion criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply the ASM-IBC framework in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures for the securities listed in the consolidated annexure. No additional securities require fresh compliance action in this cycle beyond the removal of Vikram Solar Limited from the framework.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Effective date for the exclusion of Vikram Solar Limited from ASM-IBC.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited in scope. The exclusion of Vikram Solar Limited from the ASM-IBC framework is a mildly positive development, potentially easing trading restrictions on that stock. Securities remaining on the consolidated ASM-IBC list continue to face enhanced surveillance measures, which may affect their trading liquidity and margin requirements. Overall market impact is low given the absence of any new additions in this cycle.
