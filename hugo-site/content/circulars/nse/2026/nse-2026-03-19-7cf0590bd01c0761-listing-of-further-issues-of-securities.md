## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for nine companies on the Capital Market segment, effective March 20, 2026. All allotments are under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing equity shares. This is a routine administrative circular under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Nine companies are listing additional equity shares allotted under their respective ESOPs
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and carry no lock-in
- Effective date for trading of new shares: March 20, 2026
- Market lot for all securities is 1
- Security codes and lot sizes as specified in the Annexure apply for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading the newly listed shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 19, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 20, 2026 (new shares admitted to dealings)
