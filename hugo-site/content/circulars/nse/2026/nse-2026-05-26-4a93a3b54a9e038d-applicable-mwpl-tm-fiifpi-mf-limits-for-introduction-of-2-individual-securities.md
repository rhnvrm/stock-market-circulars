## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has announced the applicable Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL), Trading Member (TM) limits, FII/FPI (Category I & II), and Mutual Fund position limits for two individual securities — GVT&D and RADICO — being introduced in the Futures & Options segment. These limits are effective from trade date May 27, 2026, in pursuance of earlier circulars 050/2026 and 034/2026.

## Key Points

- Two securities introduced in the F&O segment: **GVT&D** and **RADICO**
- Limits cover: Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL), client-level limits, proprietary trading limits, TM-level limits, FII/FPI Category I & II limits, and Mutual Fund limits
- All limits are expressed in number of shares
- Effective date: **May 27, 2026** (trade date)
- Issued by: NSE Clearing Limited, Futures & Options Department

## Regulatory Changes

The following position limits are prescribed under Circular Ref. No. 065/2026 (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/74418), building on the framework established in circulars 050/2026 (dated April 30, 2026) and 034/2026 (dated March 20, 2026).

| Symbol | MWPL (Shares) | Client Limit | Proprietary Limit | TM + Client + FPI Cat I + MF | FPI Cat II (Excl. Individuals) | FPI Cat II (Individuals/Family Offices/Corporates) |
|--------|--------------|--------------|-------------------|------------------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| GVT&D  | 18,819,420   | 1,881,875    | 3,763,875         | 5,645,750                    | 3,763,875                     | 1,881,875                                          |
| RADICO | 12,010,217   | 1,200,900    | 2,401,950         | 3,603,000                    | 2,401,950                     | 1,200,900                                          |

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of NSE Clearing Limited must note and adhere to the above position limits for GVT&D and RADICO from May 27, 2026
- Trading Members must ensure client-level and proprietary positions remain within the prescribed limits
- FII/FPI participants (both Category I and Category II) and Mutual Funds must monitor and maintain positions within their respective limits
- Client-level limits apply individually per client; TM-level limits aggregate client and proprietary positions

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Effective trade date for all position limits specified in this circular
- **May 26, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- Reference: Circular 050/2026 dated April 30, 2026 and Circular 034/2026 dated March 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of F&O contracts for GVT&D and RADICO expands derivative market coverage. Market participants — including trading members, FPIs, and mutual funds — holding or planning to take positions in these securities must ensure compliance with the prescribed limits from the effective date. The limits are tiered by participant type, with MWPL serving as the aggregate cap across all participants. Non-compliance with these limits could attract regulatory action from NSE Clearing Limited.
