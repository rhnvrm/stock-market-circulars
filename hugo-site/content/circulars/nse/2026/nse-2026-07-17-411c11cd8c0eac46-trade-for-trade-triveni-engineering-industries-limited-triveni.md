## Summary

NSE has informed members that, pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement, Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited (TRIVENI) will be moved to the Trade for Trade (T) segment under Series: BE with effect from July 22, 2026 (Wednesday). The scrip will also be included in the call auction mechanism during the pre-open session, in accordance with SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited (TRIVENI) shifts to Trade for Trade segment, Series: BE.
- The move is effective July 22, 2026 (Wednesday).
- The change is being made pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement involving the company.
- TRIVENI will be subject to the pre-open call auction session applicable to Trade for Trade securities.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the Trade for Trade mechanism for TRIVENI in line with SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, which mandates call auction in the pre-open session for securities under this segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure that all trades in TRIVENI are settled on a trade-for-trade (delivery) basis, with no intraday squaring-off permitted, and must incorporate the security into pre-open call auction session workflows starting the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 22, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Circular Date: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The shift to Trade for Trade restricts intraday trading in TRIVENI, requiring all transactions to result in actual delivery, which may reduce short-term liquidity and speculative trading activity in the scrip while investors adjust to the scheme of arrangement-related changes.
