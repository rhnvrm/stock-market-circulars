## Summary

NSE has moved Ansal Properties & Infrastructure Limited (Symbol: ANSALAPI, ISIN: INE436A01026) to Stage I of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, effective May 8, 2026. Trading in this security will be subject to a 100% applicable margin rate and a price band of 5% or lower.

## Key Points

- ANSALAPI (Ansal Properties & Infrastructure Limited, ISIN: INE436A01026) is placed under GSM Stage I effective May 8, 2026
- Applicable margin rate will be 100% for trading in this security
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower as applicable
- Members are advised to take adequate precaution while trading in this security
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/74111, Circular Ref. No: 338/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The security is being moved to GSM Stage I in continuation of earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/34262 (Feb 23, 2017), NSE/SURV/38389 (Jul 20, 2018), NSE/SURV/42790 (Nov 29, 2019), NSE/SURV/59425 (Nov 17, 2023), NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024), and NSE/SURV/73167 (Mar 06, 2026).

**GSM Stage-wise Actions:**

- **Stage I:** 100% margin rate; price band of 5% or lower
- **Stage II:** Trade-for-Trade; 5% price band; Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value by buyers
- **Stage III:** Trade-for-Trade; 5% price band; trading once a week (every Monday); ASD of 100% of trade value by buyers
- **Stage IV:** Trade-for-Trade; 5% price band; trading once a week (every Monday); ASD of 100% of trade value by buyers; no upward movement permitted

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure 100% margin is collected from clients trading in ANSALAPI
- Members must enforce the 5% price band restriction
- Members are required to take adequate precautions while trading in this security
- For queries, members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 7, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The placement of ANSALAPI under GSM Stage I significantly increases the cost and risk for traders and investors. The mandatory 100% margin requirement means buyers must have full trade value as margin, substantially reducing leverage. The 5% price band limits daily price movement. Existing holders may face difficulty exiting positions quickly. This action signals regulatory concern about the trading pattern or fundamentals of Ansal Properties & Infrastructure Limited and may lead to reduced liquidity in the stock.
