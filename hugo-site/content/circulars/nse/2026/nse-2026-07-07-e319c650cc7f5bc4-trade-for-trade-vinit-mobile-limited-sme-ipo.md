## Summary

NSE has issued a circular transferring Vinit Mobile Limited (Symbol: VMOBILE), an SME IPO listed company, from the trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to the rolling settlement segment (Series: SM), effective July 21, 2026. This follows an earlier Exchange Circular no. NSE/CML/75068 dated July 06, 2026, and is issued pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular reference no. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Vinit Mobile Limited (Symbol: VMOBILE) will move from trade-for-trade (Series: ST) to rolling settlement (Series: SM) segment.
- The transfer is effective from July 21, 2026.
- This follows the earlier circular NSE/CML/75068 dated July 06, 2026.
- Action is based on SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements existing SEBI guidelines on trade-for-trade segment classification for newly listed SME securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in trading series for VMOBILE from ST to SM effective July 21, 2026, and ensure trades are settled under normal rolling settlement rules going forward.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Prior circular NSE/CML/75068 issued.
- July 07, 2026: Current circular issued.
- July 21, 2026: Effective date of transfer to rolling (SM) segment.

## Impact Assessment

The transition from trade-for-trade to rolling settlement typically indicates the stock has completed its mandatory settlement surveillance period following listing, allowing normal netting of trades. This reduces settlement friction for VMOBILE shareholders and traders but has minimal impact beyond this single SME-listed stock.
