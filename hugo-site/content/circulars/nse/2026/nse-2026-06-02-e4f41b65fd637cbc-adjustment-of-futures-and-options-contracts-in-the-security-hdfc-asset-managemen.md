## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued guidelines for the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts in HDFC Asset Management Company Limited (HDFCAMC) in response to a declared dividend of Rs. 54 per share. The adjustment applies to all open F&O positions with HDFCAMC as the underlying, effective June 04, 2026 (last cum-dividend date) and June 05, 2026 (ex-dividend date). This circular supplements NSE Circular No. 77/2026 (NSE/FAOP/74489) dated June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Dividend amount: Rs. 54/- per share
- Last cum-dividend date: June 04, 2026
- Ex-dividend date: June 05, 2026
- All open futures positions in HDFCAMC will be marked-to-market on June 04, 2026 at the daily settlement price
- Carry-forward open positions will be reduced by Rs. 54/- (dividend amount) from June 05, 2026
- The full dividend value of Rs. 54/- will be deducted from all options strike prices for HDFCAMC contracts
- Begin-of-day margins on June 05, 2026 will be computed based on the adjusted carry-forward value

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to NSE Circular No. 77/2026, the clearing corporation will perform the following adjustments:
- **Futures contracts**: Open positions carried forward at daily settlement price less Rs. 54/- from June 05, 2026
- **Options contracts**: Strike prices reduced by the full dividend value of Rs. 54/-

Example of futures adjustment:
- Pre-adjustment: 300 long/short positions in HDFCAMC futures valued at Rs. 2,600.00 per unit (total: Rs. 7,80,000)
- Post-adjustment: Same positions valued at Rs. 2,546.00 per unit (Rs. 2,600 – Rs. 54 dividend), totalling Rs. 7,63,800

## Compliance Requirements

- All clearing members must be aware that open HDFCAMC F&O positions will be automatically adjusted by the clearing corporation on June 04, 2026
- No specific action is required by members for the adjustment; the clearing corporation will process it
- Members should communicate position valuation changes to their clients accordingly
- Intra-day margins from June 05, 2026 will revert to normal procedures based on traded prices

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Last cum-dividend date; daily MTM settlement price reduced by Rs. 54/-; F&O positions marked-to-market
- **June 05, 2026**: Ex-dividend date; normal daily MTM settlement resumes; begin-of-day margins computed on adjusted carry-forward values

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action adjustment affecting all market participants holding open Futures and Options positions in HDFCAMC. The adjustment ensures fair pricing by neutralising the dividend effect on derivative contracts. Traders with long futures positions will see their carry-forward values reduced by Rs. 54/- per unit, while those with short positions will be correspondingly adjusted. Options holders will see strike prices reduced by Rs. 54/-. The impact is moderate and confined to HDFCAMC F&O traders; no broader market disruption is expected.
