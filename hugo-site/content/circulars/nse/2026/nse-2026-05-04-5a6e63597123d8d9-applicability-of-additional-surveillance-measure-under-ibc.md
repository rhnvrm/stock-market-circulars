## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has updated the list of securities subject to Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) framework, effective May 05, 2026. One security — Osia Hyper Retail Limited (OSIAHYPER) — has been newly shortlisted under the 'Receipt of Corporate Announcement/Disclosure' category. No securities have been added to or moved between Stage I and Stage II, and no securities have been excluded from the ASM-IBC framework in this update.

## Key Points

- OSIAHYPER (Osia Hyper Retail Limited, ISIN: INE06IR01021) is newly shortlisted under ASM-IBC in the 'Receipt of Corporate Announcement/Disclosure' category effective May 05, 2026.
- No securities have been added to ASM-IBC Stage I or moved between stages (Stage I to II or II to I) in this update.
- No securities have been excluded from ASM-IBC in this update.
- The ASM-IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- The shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and must not be construed as adverse action against any company or entity.
- The consolidated ASM-IBC list currently includes: AGSTRA (AGS Transact Technologies Limited, Stage I), BILVYAPAR (BIL VYAPAR LIMITED, Stage I), CLCIND (CLC Industries Limited, Stage I), DHARAN (DHARAN INFRA-EPC LIMITED, Stage I), EMBDL (Embassy Developers — details truncated in source), among others.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the operational list under the existing ASM-IBC framework established through prior circulars:
- NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018 (original framework)
- NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022
- NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023
- NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025

No changes to the ASM-IBC framework rules themselves; this is a periodic list update.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Take note of the updated ASM-IBC security lists (Annexure I and Annexure II) and apply applicable surveillance restrictions for the listed securities.
- **Market Participants**: Ensure awareness that trading in OSIAHYPER and other consolidated ASM-IBC securities is subject to heightened surveillance measures.
- **Queries**: Direct any further queries to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: May 04, 2026
- **Effective Date**: May 05, 2026 (all changes in Annexure I and Annexure II take effect)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited in scope, affecting traders and members dealing in the specific securities listed under ASM-IBC. The primary new addition is OSIAHYPER (Osia Hyper Retail Limited), which will now be subject to additional surveillance requirements due to corporate announcements related to insolvency proceedings. Existing ASM-IBC securities such as AGSTRA, BILVYAPAR, CLCIND, and DHARAN continue under Stage I restrictions. The ASM-IBC designation increases scrutiny but is not a trading halt; however, it typically results in additional margin requirements, price bands, or other restrictions that may affect liquidity and volatility in the affected securities. No securities were removed from the ASM-IBC list in this update, indicating no improvement in IBC status for any previously listed company.
