## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts for Punjab National Bank (symbol: PNB) in accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments. PNB has declared a dividend of Rs 3/- per share (face value Rs 2/-), with the ex-date and effective date set as June 12, 2026. All option strike prices for contracts expiring June 30, 2026 have been revised downward by Rs 3/-.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** PNB (Punjab National Bank)
- **Corporate Action:** Dividend of Rs 3/- per share on face value of Rs 2/-
- **Ex-Date / Effective Date:** June 12, 2026
- Strike prices for all OPTSTK PNB contracts expiring June 30, 2026 are revised downward by Rs 3/- (e.g., 86.00 → 83.00, 100.00 → 97.00)
- Members must load the updated contract.gz and spread files before trading on the ex-date
- Revised strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places and will be rounded to the nearest tick size / nearest integer

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI guidelines on adjustments to futures and options contracts on announcement of corporate actions, strike prices for PNB option contracts (instrument type: OPTSTK) expiring June 30, 2026 have been revised. Full revised strike price details are available in Annexure 1 of the circular and at the NSE corporate actions adjustments page.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated **contract.gz** file (`faoftp/faocommon` on the Extranet server) and the MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications **before trading on June 12, 2026**.
- Updated contract and spread files are also available on the NSE website under the All Reports – Derivatives section.
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Date / Effective Date:** June 12, 2026 — revised strike prices and contract files become operative
- **Contract Expiry (affected contracts):** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

All market participants holding or trading PNB options contracts expiring June 30, 2026 are directly affected. Strike prices are uniformly reduced by Rs 3/- (the dividend amount) across the contract series. Traders must ensure their trading systems reflect the updated contract files prior to market open on June 12, 2026 to avoid order entry errors. This is a standard SEBI-mandated adjustment and does not alter the economic value of existing positions at the time of adjustment.
