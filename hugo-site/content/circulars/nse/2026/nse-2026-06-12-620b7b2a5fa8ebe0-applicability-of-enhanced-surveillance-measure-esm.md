## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework (Circular Ref. No. 439/2026) effective June 15–16, 2026. The circular specifies securities being added to ESM Stage I, securities moving between stages, and those eligible to exit the framework. Nine securities are moving from ESM Stage II back to Stage I effective June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities newly included under ESM will attract a **minimum 100% margin** on all open positions as on June 15, 2026, and on new positions from June 16, 2026.
- Securities under ESM are shifted from Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to **Trade-for-Trade segment** (Series: BE/ST) w.e.f. June 16, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage II** will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 15, 2026.
- Annexure I lists no new securities for ESM Stage I inclusion (Nil).
- Nine securities are moving from ESM Stage II back to Stage I.
- Annexure II lists securities eligible to move out of the ESM framework entirely.
- Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- Price band reinstatement applies to securities exiting the framework, subject to no other active surveillance measures.
- ESM shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and is not an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and updates the ESM framework established under earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315 (dated June 2023 through July 2025). The current update adjusts stage classifications for specific securities and reaffirms the margin and settlement rules applicable to the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members** must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in newly included ESM securities as on June 15, 2026, and on new positions from June 16, 2026.
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded in the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series) from June 16, 2026.
- For Stage II securities, trading must occur under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from June 15, 2026.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Stage II price band (2% under Periodic Call Auction) and Stage II-to-I reclassification take effect; 100% margin applies to open positions as of this date.
- **June 16, 2026**: Shift to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series) takes effect; 100% margin applies to new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The circular directly affects nine securities transitioning from ESM Stage II to Stage I — a positive development as Stage I carries less restrictive conditions than Stage II. No new securities are being added to Stage I (Nil in Annexure I). The affected securities include small/mid-cap stocks across sectors (optics, biotech, realty, films, textiles, chemicals, telecom, pipes, photo imaging). Traders holding positions in these securities must maintain 100% margin and will face Trade-for-Trade settlement restrictions, limiting intraday speculation and increasing capital requirements. The move is routine and does not indicate regulatory action against the companies involved.
