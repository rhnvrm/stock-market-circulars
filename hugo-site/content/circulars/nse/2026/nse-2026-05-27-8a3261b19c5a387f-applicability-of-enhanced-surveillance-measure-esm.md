## Summary

NSE has invoked the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, adding 5 securities to Stage I and moving Kirloskar Electric Company Limited from Stage I to Stage II, effective May 29, 2026. All newly included Stage I securities will be shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade settlement (BE/ST series) effective June 1, 2026, and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions from that date.

## Key Points

- 5 securities added to ESM Stage I: CORDSCABLE, ECOSMOBLTY, KOPRAN, MODISONLTD, NRVANDANA (effective May 29, 2026)
- KECL (Kirloskar Electric Company Limited) moves from ESM Stage I to Stage II (effective May 29, 2026)
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage I; no securities excluded from the ESM framework
- Minimum 100% margin applicable on all open positions as of May 29, 2026, and all new positions from June 1, 2026
- Stage I securities shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) w.e.f. June 1, 2026
- Stage II security (KECL) placed under Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. May 29, 2026
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

The circular is issued under the ESM framework established via prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315 (June 2023 – July 2025). No new framework changes are introduced; this circular applies existing ESM criteria to the newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/brokers** must ensure 100% margin is collected on all open positions in ESM-listed securities as on May 29, 2026, and on any new positions created from June 1, 2026.
- **Market participants** must note that affected securities will trade only in Trade-for-Trade mode (BE/ST series) from June 1, 2026; intraday positions cannot be netted off.
- **Stage II security (KECL)** trades under Periodic Call Auction with a strict 2% price band from May 29, 2026.
- Participants may refer to NSE's ESM FAQs or write to surveillance@nse.co.in for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusion and Stage I→II migration effective; 100% margin applicable on open positions; Stage II price band (2% PCA) begins for KECL
- **June 1, 2026**: Stage I securities shift from EQ/SM (Rolling Settlement) to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade); 100% margin applies to all new positions

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding positions in CORDSCABLE, ECOSMOBLTY, KOPRAN, MODISONLTD, or NRVANDANA face immediate margin pressure (100% requirement) and loss of intraday trading flexibility due to the Trade-for-Trade shift. Liquidity in these counters is expected to decline significantly. KECL faces the most severe restriction, moving to Stage II with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, which heavily limits daily price movement and liquidity. No securities were removed from the ESM framework in this update, indicating continued or escalating surveillance pressure across the consolidated list.
