## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74534 (Ref. 412/2026) placing 8 securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and moving 1 security (BBOX) from Stage I to Stage II, effective June 04, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements take effect from June 05, 2026 under the ASM framework.

## Key Points

- 8 securities newly included under ST-ASM Stage I effective June 04, 2026
- No new securities directly added to ST-ASM Stage II
- Black Box Limited (BBOX) moves from ST-ASM Stage I to Stage II effective June 04, 2026
- Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Margin applicability covers open positions as on June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026
- ASM classification is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to earlier ASM circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024 respectively. Securities meeting ST-ASM criteria are subjected to enhanced surveillance margins in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply enhanced margin rates to the affected securities from June 05, 2026
- Stage I securities: collect minimum 50% margin (or existing margin if higher, capped at 100%) on all open positions as of June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026
- Stage II securities (including BBOX): collect 100% margin (or existing margin if higher) on all open positions as of June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026
- Members must ensure compliance with these margins in conjunction with all other surveillance measures

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 04, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion/stage change of securities under ST-ASM
- **June 05, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become applicable on open positions (as of June 04, 2026) and new positions
