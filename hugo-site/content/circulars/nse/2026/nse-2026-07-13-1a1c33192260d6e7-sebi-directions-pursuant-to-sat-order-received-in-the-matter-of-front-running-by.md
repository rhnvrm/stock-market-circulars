## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members of SEBI directions pursuant to a Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) order in the matter of front running by Ashok Maheshwari and others. This follows NSE Circular No. NSE/INVG/73920 dated April 27, 2026, referencing SEBI Order No. WTM/AS/ISD/ISD-SEC-6/32384/2026-27 dated April 27, 2026, which had originally restrained certain noticees from accessing the securities market. The Hon'ble SAT, vide order dated June 22, 2026, has stayed the debarment and recovery proceedings against two of the noticees—CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd. and Chirag Mahendra Shah—subject to deposit of the penalty amount. SEBI has communicated that the original order in respect of these entities has been set aside by SAT, and has advised the Exchange to comply immediately.

## Key Points

- The original SEBI order (WTM/AS/ISD/ISD-SEC-6/32384/2026-27 dated April 27, 2026) restrained noticees from accessing the securities market and prohibited them from buying, selling, or dealing in securities.
- Hon'ble SAT, via order dated June 22, 2026, stayed the debarment and recovery proceedings for CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AADCC7495F) and Chirag Mahendra Shah (PAN: AAQPS1679F), subject to deposit of the penalty amount.
- The stay effectively sets aside the debarment for these two entities pending compliance with the penalty deposit condition.
- SEBI has directed NSE to comply with the SAT order immediately.
- The broader SEBI order covered eight noticees in total, including Ashok Maheshwari, Darshan Bakul Shah, Khusboo Darshan Shah, Darshan Bakul Shah (HUF), Benzer Department Stores Pvt. Ltd., Mihir Dhirajalal Savla, CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd., and Chirag Mahendra Shah, related to alleged front running under Sections 11C(3) and 12A of the SEBI Act and related PFUTP Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements a SAT appellate order staying enforcement/debarment action against two specific noticees, conditional on penalty deposit.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their records to reflect the stay of debarment for CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd. and Chirag Mahendra Shah.
- Members should refer to the consolidated list of debarred entities maintained on the NSE website (https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities) for the current status.
- Queries can be directed to dl-invsg-all@nse.co.in.
- The detailed SAT order is available at https://satweb.sat.gov.in/orders.

## Important Dates

- April 26/27, 2026: Original SEBI Interim Order/Final Order issued.
- June 22, 2026: Hon'ble SAT order staying debarment and recovery proceedings, subject to penalty deposit.
- July 13, 2026: NSE circular communicating compliance directive to members.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact as it pertains to the debarment status of two specific entities in an ongoing front running enforcement matter. Its primary significance is procedural/compliance-related for NSE members who must maintain accurate records of market access restrictions. There is no broader effect on listed securities, trading operations, or market-wide compliance frameworks.
