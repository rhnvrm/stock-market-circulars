## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by Kotyark Industries Limited (Symbol: KOTYARK) on the Capital Market segment. The new shares will be available for trading effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kotyark Industries Limited (Symbol: KOTYARK, ISIN: INE0J0B01017) bonus shares admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Total bonus shares issued: 10,27,91,160 (approximately 102.79 million shares)
- Distinctive number range: 10279117 to 113070276
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular Ref. No: 0995/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74810

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs admission of securities to dealings on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the bonus shares solely by their designated security codes for trading purposes.
- No additional compliance obligations imposed beyond standard trading norms.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Kotyark Industries Limited will receive bonus shares, increasing their holdings proportionately. The listing of approximately 102.79 million new shares on NSE may affect short-term liquidity and price dynamics for KOTYARK. No impact on broader market operations or other listed securities.
