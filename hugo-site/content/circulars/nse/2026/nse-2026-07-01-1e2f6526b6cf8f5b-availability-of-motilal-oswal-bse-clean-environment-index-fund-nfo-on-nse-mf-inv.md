## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Motilal Oswal BSE Clean Environment Index Fund NFO, offered by Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- The circular pertains to two schemes: Motilal Oswal BSE Clean Environment Index Fund Direct Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HC6) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HD4).
- Both schemes fall under the Equity category.
- Ongoing transactions including subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) will be enabled on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational update enabling continued transaction facilities for an existing NFO scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems to reflect the reopening of these schemes for ongoing transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 02, 2026 — schemes reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice relevant to mutual fund distributors and investors using the NSE MF Invest Platform. It does not affect listed equities or broader market trading activity.
