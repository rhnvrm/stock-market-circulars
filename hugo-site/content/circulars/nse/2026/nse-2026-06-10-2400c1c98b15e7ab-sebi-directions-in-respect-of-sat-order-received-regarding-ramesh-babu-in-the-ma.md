## Summary

NSE has communicated SEBI's directions following a Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) order dated May 5, 2026, which set aside the earlier SEBI debarment order (QJA/AA/WRO/WRO/26660/2023-24 dated May 25, 2023) against Mr. Ramesh Babu (PAN: BGNPB5216K) in the matter of alleged unregistered investment advisory activities by Investocare Financial Research. SEBI has advised NSE to comply with the SAT order immediately.

## Key Points

- SAT vide order dated May 5, 2026 allowed the appeal filed by Mr. Ramesh Babu and set aside SEBI's original order dated May 25, 2023 against him.
- The matter has been remitted to SEBI for fresh disposal in accordance with law.
- SEBI's original order had debarred Mr. Ramesh Babu from accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of two years.
- SEBI has now directed the exchange to comply with the SAT order immediately, effectively lifting the debarment as of this communication.
- The consolidated list of debarred entities is available on the NSE website under the SEBI-debarred entities section.

## Regulatory Changes

The SEBI debarment order (QJA/AA/WRO/WRO/26660/2023-24 dated May 25, 2023) against Mr. Ramesh Babu has been set aside by SAT. The individual is no longer under the securities market access restrictions imposed by that order. SEBI will conduct fresh proceedings as directed by SAT.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must take note of the SAT order and update their compliance records accordingly.
- Members should no longer treat Mr. Ramesh Babu as a debarred entity under the original May 2023 SEBI order.
- Members should refer to the updated consolidated list of debarred entities on the NSE website: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities
- For queries, members may email: dl-invsg-all@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2023**: Original SEBI debarment order issued (QJA/AA/WRO/WRO/26660/2023-24)
- **May 26, 2023**: NSE circular NSE/INVG/56843 issued in relation to the original order
- **May 5, 2026**: SAT order allowing appeal and setting aside the SEBI debarment order
- **June 10, 2026**: NSE circular issued directing members to comply with SAT order immediately

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact as it concerns a single individual, Mr. Ramesh Babu, associated with Investocare Financial Research. The practical effect is that NSE members must remove him from their list of debarred entities. The matter being remitted to SEBI for fresh disposal means a new regulatory proceeding may follow. Members in direct contact with or monitoring debarred entities should update their compliance systems promptly.
