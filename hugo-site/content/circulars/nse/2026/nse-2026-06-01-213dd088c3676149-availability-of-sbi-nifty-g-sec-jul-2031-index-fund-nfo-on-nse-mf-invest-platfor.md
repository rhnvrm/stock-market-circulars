## Summary

NSE has informed all members that SBI Nifty G-Sec Jul 2031 Index Fund NFO of SBI Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants of SBI Nifty G-Sec Jul 2031 Index Fund are being made available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Effective date for platform availability: June 01, 2026
- Facilities available: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- All six variants fall under the Debt category
- Circular Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74460 / 1403/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond ensuring their systems support transactions in the newly listed scheme variants on the NSE MF Invest Platform as of June 01, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: Effective date for availability of SBI Nifty G-Sec Jul 2031 Index Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP/STP/SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with low market impact. It expands investor access to a debt index fund tracking Government Securities maturing in July 2031, via the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members and investors who wish to invest in G-Sec-based index products through the NSE platform will benefit from the added transaction flexibility (SIP, STP, SWP). No disruption to existing trading or settlement operations is anticipated.
