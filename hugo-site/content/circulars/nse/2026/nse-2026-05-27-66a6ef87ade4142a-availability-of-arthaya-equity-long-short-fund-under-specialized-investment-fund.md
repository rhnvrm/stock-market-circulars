## Summary

NSE has informed members that Arthaya Equity Long Short Fund under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) of Union Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants of Arthaya Equity Long Short Fund (Regular and Direct, across Growth and IDCW options) will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category within the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) structure.
- Facilities available include ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- The fund is managed under Union Mutual Fund (AMC code: INF582M).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update enabling trading access to existing SIF schemes on NSE's MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of these schemes on the platform. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond enabling client transactions in these schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 29, 2026 — Arthaya Equity Long Short Fund schemes go live for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine platform onboarding circular enabling investor access to Union Mutual Fund's SIF equity schemes via NSE MF Invest. It expands distribution reach for the fund but does not introduce new regulatory requirements or affect broader market operations. Relevant primarily to distributors and investors interested in long-short equity strategies under the SIF framework.
