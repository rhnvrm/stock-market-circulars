## Summary

NSE has informed its members that The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund, a New Fund Offer (NFO) under The Wealth Company Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular lists six scheme variants of the fund along with their ISINs and allotment date.

## Key Points

- The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund NFO opens for subscription via NSE MF Invest Platform from July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).
- The fund is an Equity category scheme offered under The Wealth Company Mutual Fund.
- Six plan/option variants are listed:
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Direct IDCW Payout — ISIN INF2F0001669
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Direct IDCW Reinvestment — ISIN INF2F0001677
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Direct Growth — ISIN INF2F0001651
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Regular IDCW Payout — ISIN INF2F0001636
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Regular IDCW Reinvestment — ISIN INF2F0001644
  - The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund Regular Growth — ISIN INF2F0001628
- All six variants have a common allotment date of August 4, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a change to regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 3, 2026, till 11:59 pm, to facilitate settlement of NFO subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Clear funds deadline: August 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment date: August 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily operational in nature, relevant to NSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions. It enables investor access to a new equity mid-cap fund offering through the NSE MF Invest Platform but does not affect listed securities, trading mechanisms, or broader compliance frameworks.
