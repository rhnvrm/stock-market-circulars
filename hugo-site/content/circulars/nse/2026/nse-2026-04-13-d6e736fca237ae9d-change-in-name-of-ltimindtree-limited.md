## Summary

NSE has informed all exchange members that LTIMindtree Limited will officially change its name to **LTM Limited** effective April 17, 2026. The trading symbol **LTM** remains unchanged. This circular is issued by the Futures & Options department (Ref No: NSE/FAOP/73724, Circular Ref. No: 45/2026).

## Key Points

- The company's name changes from **LTIMindtree Limited** to **LTM Limited**
- The NSE trading symbol **LTM** remains the same
- The change is effective from **April 17, 2026**
- This circular applies to the Futures & Options segment
- Circular issued by Khushal Shah, Associate Vice President, NSE

## Regulatory Changes

The name change is a corporate action by LTIMindtree Limited. NSE is notifying all members of the official name update as required under exchange regulations to ensure accurate representation in trading systems and documentation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their internal records, trading systems, and client-facing documentation to reflect the new company name **LTM Limited**
- All existing contracts, positions, and references to LTIMindtree Limited in the F&O segment should be updated accordingly
- No change in trading symbol is required; symbol LTM continues to be used

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change:** April 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is moderate and primarily administrative. Since the trading symbol LTM remains unchanged, there is no disruption to active trading or open positions in the F&O segment. However, members, brokers, and investors should update records, contract notes, and any documentation referencing the old name LTIMindtree Limited to LTM Limited from April 17, 2026 onwards. LTIMindtree (LTM) is a significant large-cap IT company in the Nifty indices, so the name change may have minor branding implications but no fundamental market impact.
