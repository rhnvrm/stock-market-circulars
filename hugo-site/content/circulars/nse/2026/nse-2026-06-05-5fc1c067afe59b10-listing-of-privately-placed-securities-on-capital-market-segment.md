## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of two privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective June 08, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Both securities carry a market lot size of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from June 08, 2026
- Market lot size for both securities is 1 unit (Series: N0)
- Securities must be identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes
- Lot sizes as specified in the Annexure govern all trading in these instruments

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security codes (855MFL36 and SIDBI29) when trading these instruments
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date (Trading Admission):** June 08, 2026
- **Muthoot Finance NCD Maturity:** June 03, 2036
- **SIDBI Bond Maturity:** June 04, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification. Two debt instruments are now available for trading on NSE:

1. **Muthoot Finance Limited — 855MFL36 (INE414G07KA8):** Fixed coupon NCD at 8.55% maturing June 2036, offering a long-duration fixed-income instrument.
2. **Small Industries Development Bank of India — SIDBI29 (INE556F08LE6):** Market-linked bond maturing June 2029, providing a medium-term instrument with returns tied to market benchmarks.

Impact is limited to fixed-income traders and institutional participants active in the debt segment. No equity market implications.
