## Summary

NSE has announced a mock bidding session on the eOFS (electronic Offer for Sale) platform in the Live environment, scheduled for July 25, 2026, from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM. The mock session will use test securities TESTOFS2 and TESTOFS3 and does not create any financial obligation for participants.

## Key Points

- Mock bidding session to be conducted on the eOFS platform via the live web URL https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Members are requested to place bids in the Employee Category under the symbol "TESTOFS2"
- Bids placed during the mock session shall not attract any obligation in terms of funds or securities
- Test security parameters are detailed in Annexure 1 covering TESTOFS2 and TESTOFS3
- Any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session will be published on www.nseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/technical mock exercise, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are requested to actively participate in the mock bidding session to test the eOFS platform's live environment functionality. No mandatory compliance action beyond participation is required.

## Important Dates

- Mock session date: July 25, 2026
- Mock session timing: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a technical mock/test exercise involving fictitious securities (TESTOFS2 and TESTOFS3) with no real financial consequences. It is primarily relevant for exchange members' operational readiness in using the eOFS platform for future actual offer-for-sale transactions.
