## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has notified the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) settlement calendar for August 2026, pursuant to circular no. NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026. The circular specifies settlement schedules for First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'), Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'), and Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q') transactions.

## Key Points

- The circular covers three annexures: Annexure 'A' (First Leg Settlement calendar), Annexure 'B' (Reverse Leg Settlement calendar), and Annexure 'C' (Reverse Leg Auction Settlement calendar).
- First Leg Settlements (Type 'L') are scheduled almost daily through August 2026, with trade dates from 03-Aug-26 to 31-Aug-26, settlement numbers 2026144 through 2026164.
- Custodial confirmation dates align with trade dates, and settlement dates generally fall one business day after (T+1), adjusted for weekends/holidays.
- Reverse Leg Settlements (Type 'P') for various expiry series (e.g., 08/X8, 09/X9 through 07/X7, 08/X8) are also scheduled, extending through August 2027.
- The 08/X8 series settlement dated 03-Aug-27 will be available for trading from 05-Aug-26, following expiry of the current 08/X8 series on 04-Aug-26.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic notification of the operational settlement calendar under the existing SLBS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

All SLBS participants and custodians must adhere to the specified trade dates, custodial confirmation dates, and settlement dates for First Leg, Reverse Leg, and Reverse Leg Auction settlements in August 2026 to ensure timely completion of securities lending and borrowing transactions.

## Important Dates

- First Leg Settlement trade dates: 03-Aug-26 to 31-Aug-26 (settlement numbers 2026144–2026164), with settlements typically completed the following business day.
- Reverse Leg Settlement (08/X8 series): 04-Aug-26.
- New 08/X8 series (expiring 03-Aug-27) available for trading from 05-Aug-26.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is a routine operational notification providing advance visibility into the SLBS settlement calendar, enabling participants and custodians to plan trade confirmations and fund/security movements accordingly. No stock-specific or broader market impact is expected.
