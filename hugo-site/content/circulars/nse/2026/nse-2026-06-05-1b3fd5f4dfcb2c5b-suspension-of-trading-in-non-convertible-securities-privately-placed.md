## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the suspension of trading in two privately placed Non-Convertible Securities effective June 08, 2026. The suspension applies to instruments issued by Aditya Birla Capital Limited and HDFC Bank Limited, both being suspended due to redemption under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Two non-convertible securities are being suspended from trading on June 08, 2026
- Both suspensions are triggered by redemption of the respective instruments
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74560, Ref No. 0907/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The suspension is a routine procedural action under the existing framework — Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A — which mandates suspension of trading in securities upon their redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in the specified ISINs from June 08, 2026 onwards
- Members holding or dealing in these securities should account for the imminent redemption and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | June 05, 2026 |
| Suspension Effective Date (both ISINs) | June 08, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. Both instruments are privately placed non-convertible securities approaching their redemption date. Investors holding these ISINs will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No broader market disruption is expected.
