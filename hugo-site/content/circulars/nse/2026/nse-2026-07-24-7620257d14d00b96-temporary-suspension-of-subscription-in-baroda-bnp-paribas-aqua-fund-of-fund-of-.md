## Summary

Baroda BNP Paribas Asset Management India Private Limited has issued Notice-cum-Addendum No. 63/2026, temporarily suspending the sale of units and acceptance of fresh subscriptions in the Baroda BNP Paribas Aqua Fund of Fund, effective July 23, 2026. This action follows prior addenda (No. 46/2022, No. 48/2022, and No. 40/2026) and is intended to avoid breaching SEBI-mandated overseas investment limits at the Fund level, as permitted by SEBI's letter dated June 17, 2022.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum subscriptions (including switch-ins) into the Scheme will not be accepted from July 23, 2026.
- Fresh registrations of Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) and Systematic Transfer Plans (STPs) into the Scheme will not be accepted from July 23, 2026.
- Existing SIP/STP instalments registered on or before July 22, 2026 will continue uninterrupted.
- Switch-outs, redemptions, and fresh SWP registrations are unaffected.
- Existing SWP instalments where the Scheme is the source scheme are unaffected.
- Intra-Scheme switches (Regular to Direct Plan and vice versa) and Intra-Plan switches (Growth to IDCW and vice versa) are unaffected.
- All lumpsum subscription and fresh SIP/STP transactions received after the applicable cut-off time on July 22, 2026 will not be accepted or processed.
- The suspension is temporary; the AMC will review overseas investment headroom periodically and resume subscriptions once adequate headroom is available, with a separate notice-cum-addendum to be issued at that time.
- All other terms and conditions of the SID, KIM, and SAI of Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an AMC-level operational action taken to comply with SEBI's existing framework (per SEBI letter dated June 17, 2022) governing overseas investment limits for mutual funds at the Fund level, aimed at preventing a potential breach of those limits.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE MF Invest Platform participants (distributors, RTAs, investors) must stop accepting/processing lumpsum subscriptions and new SIP/STP registrations for this Scheme from July 23, 2026.
- Existing SIP/STP instalments, redemptions, switch-outs, SWPs, and intra-scheme/intra-plan switches should continue to be processed normally.
- Market participants should monitor for a further notice-cum-addendum from the AMC announcing resumption of subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Cut-off date; transactions received after the applicable cut-off time on this date for lumpsum subscriptions and fresh SIP/STP registrations will not be accepted.
- July 23, 2026: Effective date of suspension of sale of units and fresh subscriptions.
- Date of Notice: July 22, 2026 (issued from Mumbai).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and distributors dealing in the Baroda BNP Paribas Aqua Fund of Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Existing investors are not required to take any action for ongoing SIPs/STPs, redemptions, or switches. New investors and those seeking to increase exposure via lumpsum or new SIP/STP registrations will be unable to do so until the AMC announces resumption. There is no impact on broader market operations, listed securities, or other mutual fund schemes.
