## Summary

NSE has notified that Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited will undergo a corporate name change to Aqylon Nexus Limited, along with a trading symbol change from SABTNL to AQYLON, effective February 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited
- New company name: Aqylon Nexus Limited
- Existing trading symbol: SABTNL
- New trading symbol: AQYLON
- Change is effective from February 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving name and symbol modification on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- Market participants should note the symbol change for trading and settlement purposes
- All references to the company should use the new name and symbol from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard corporate name and symbol change with moderate operational impact. Market participants, brokers, and trading systems must update their databases and systems to reflect the new identity. Existing shareholders are not affected in terms of holdings, but all future communications and trading will use the new name AQYLON. The change represents a corporate rebranding initiative with no direct impact on business operations or shareholder rights.
