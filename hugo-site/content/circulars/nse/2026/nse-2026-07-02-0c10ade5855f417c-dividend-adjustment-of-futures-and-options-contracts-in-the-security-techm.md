## Summary

NSE has announced adjustments to Futures and Options (F&O) contracts in the security TECHM (Tech Mahindra Limited) following the company's declaration of a dividend of Rs 36 per share on a face value of Rs 5/-. The adjustment is being made in accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments to derivative contracts, and will take effect from the ex-date of July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: TECHM (Tech Mahindra Limited)
- Corporate action type: Dividend
- Face value: Rs 5/-
- Dividend amount: Rs 36/- per share
- Ex-date and effective date: 03-Jul-2026
- Revised option strike prices are detailed in Annexure 1 of the circular, covering the 28-JUL-2026 expiry series with strikes revised downward (e.g., 1140.00 to 1104.00, 1160.00 to 1124.00, and so on through the range of listed strikes)
- Full adjustment details are also available on the NSE website's equity derivatives corporate actions adjustments page

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational adjustment to existing derivative contracts mandated by SEBI's standard corporate action adjustment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz/MII contract file (NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) and spread file (NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) onto their trading applications before trading commences on the ex-date/effective date
- These files can be obtained from the faoftp/faocommon directory on the Extranet server, or from the NSE website's all-reports-derivatives page
- The methodology for position adjustments will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation

## Important Dates

- Ex-date & effective date: July 3, 2026 (adjustments take effect)
- Affected expiry: 28-JUL-2026 options series

## Impact Assessment

This adjustment affects traders and members holding or trading TECHM options contracts, as strike prices are being revised downward to account for the Rs 36 dividend payout. Failure by members to update contract and spread files before the ex-date could result in trading discrepancies or errors. The impact is limited to TECHM derivatives market participants and does not affect the broader market or cash equity trading.
