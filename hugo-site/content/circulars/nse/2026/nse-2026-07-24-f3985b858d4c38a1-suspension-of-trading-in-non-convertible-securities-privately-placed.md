## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Security (privately placed) of Tata Capital Limited, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspension is on account of redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75378, Circular Ref. No: 1210/2026)
- Applicable to Tata Capital Limited's Non-Convertible Security bearing ISIN INE306N08193
- Trading suspension is triggered by redemption of the security
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational action under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A governing suspension of trading in privately placed non-convertible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members must cease trading in the specified ISIN (INE306N08193) from the suspension date. No further action is required from issuers or investors beyond acknowledging the redemption-driven suspension.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 24, 2026
- Suspension Date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific Tata Capital Limited NCD ISIN in the debt market segment. As the suspension follows redemption, it reflects routine closure of the instrument's trading lifecycle rather than a distress or regulatory action, resulting in minimal market-wide impact.
