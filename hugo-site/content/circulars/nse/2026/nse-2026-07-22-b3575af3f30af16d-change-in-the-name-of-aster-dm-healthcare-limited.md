## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) has issued a circular informing members that Aster DM Healthcare Limited (symbol: ASTERDM) will officially change its registered name to Aster DM Quality Care Limited, effective July 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Symbol: ASTERDM
- Existing Name: Aster DM Healthcare Limited
- New Name: Aster DM Quality Care Limited
- The trading symbol ASTERDM remains unchanged; only the company's registered name changes
- Circular Ref. No: 1200/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75342

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory or structural changes are involved; this is an administrative update to reflect the company's new legal name in exchange records.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their internal records and systems to reflect the new company name from the effective date. No action is required from investors regarding their existing holdings.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 28, 2026
- Circular Date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, administrative name change with no bearing on trading symbol, share structure, or financials. Market participants should simply note the updated corporate name for reference and reporting purposes going forward.
