## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members that Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund (KMMF) is introducing a Weekly frequency under the Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) option for both Regular and Direct plans in select schemes, effective July 16, 2026. This is available on the NSE MF Invest Platform (NMF TM Segment) based on an addendum received from Kotak Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- Kotak Mahindra Trustee Company Limited's Board approved introduction of Weekly IDCW frequency in addition to existing options.
- Applies to: Kotak Savings Fund, Kotak Corporate Bond Fund, Kotak Money Market Fund, and Kotak Bond Short Term Fund (Regular and Direct plans).
- For Kotak Savings Fund, Kotak Corporate Bond Fund and Kotak Money Market Fund: Payout and Reinvestment of IDCW options revised from Monthly-only to Monthly and Weekly frequency.
- For Kotak Bond Short Term Fund: Payout and Reinvestment of IDCW options revised from Trustee-discretion-only to Trustee discretion and Weekly frequency.
- IDCW Record Date for Weekly Frequency will be every Thursday (or the next Business Day if Thursday is not a Business Day).
- The addendum forms an integral part of the Scheme Information Document (SID), Key Information Memorandum (KIM), and Statement of Additional Information (SAI) of the affected schemes.
- All other terms and conditions of the SID/KIM/SAI remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes from SEBI/NSE; this is a mutual fund scheme-level feature enhancement communicated by the AMC and disseminated by NSE to its NMF TM Segment members for platform readiness.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members operating on the NMF Invest Platform should update their systems/processes to support the new Weekly IDCW option for the specified schemes and inform investors accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Addendum date: July 3, 2026
- NSE circular date: July 15, 2026
- Effective date of Weekly IDCW frequency: July 16, 2026
- Weekly Record Date: Every Thursday (or next Business Day)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, operational update relevant only to investors and distributors of the specified Kotak debt schemes, providing more frequent (weekly) income distribution options. It does not affect listed equities, trading mechanisms, or broader market operations.
