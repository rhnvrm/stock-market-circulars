## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced that the trading symbol of Infibeam Avenues Limited will change from INFIBEAM to CCAVENUE in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) effective February 03, 2026. This change follows the earlier NSE circular (NSE/CML/72519) dated January 29, 2026. All existing contracts in the security will be traded under the new symbol from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Old trading symbol: INFIBEAM
- New trading symbol: CCAVENUE
- Effective date: February 03, 2026
- Applies to Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme segment
- All contracts will automatically trade under new symbol from effective date
- Reference circular: NSE/CML/72519 dated January 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative symbol change following corporate action by Infibeam Avenues Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- All participants and custodians must update their systems to recognize the new symbol CCAVENUE
- Ensure trading and settlement systems are configured for the new symbol before February 03, 2026
- Update internal records and client communications regarding the symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 03, 2026 - New symbol CCAVENUE becomes active for SLBS trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Symbol changes require system updates by all participants but do not affect underlying security ownership or contracts. 

**Operational Impact**: Participants must ensure their trading systems, risk management tools, and reporting mechanisms are updated to handle the new symbol. The one-day notice period requires immediate action by market infrastructure participants.

**Trading Impact**: All open positions and new contracts will seamlessly transition to the new symbol. Market participants should communicate the change to clients who hold positions in SLBS segment.
