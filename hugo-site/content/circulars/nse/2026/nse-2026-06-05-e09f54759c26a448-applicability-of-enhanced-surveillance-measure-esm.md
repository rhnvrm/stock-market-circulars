## Summary

NSE has announced the applicability of Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) for a new set of securities effective June 08-09, 2026. Six securities have been newly included under ESM Stage I, while certain securities are moving between ESM stages. All newly included securities will attract a minimum 100% margin and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM series) to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series).

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly included under ESM Stage I effective June 08, 2026
- Minimum 100% margin applicable w.e.f. June 09, 2026 on all open and new positions
- Securities shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) w.e.f. June 09, 2026
- Securities moving to Stage II will be under Trade for Trade with 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 08, 2026
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

The circular references prior ESM framework circulars: NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. Securities qualifying under ESM are shifted from Rolling Settlement segment to Trade-for-Trade segment. Price bands for securities moving out of the framework will be reinstated to their pre-ESM levels, subject to them not being part of any other surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure 100% margin collection on all open positions as on June 08, 2026 for newly included securities
- Members must ensure 100% margin on new positions created from June 09, 2026 in these securities
- Trading in affected securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules under BE/ST series
- Members should inform clients about the changed trading conditions for affected securities

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 08, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusion effective date; Stage II price band (2%) under Periodic Call Auction effective
- **June 09, 2026**: Shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST series effective; 100% margin on new positions effective

The imposition of ESM significantly restricts trading in affected securities — liquidity will be reduced due to Trade-for-Trade settlement (no netting), higher margin requirements will increase the cost of carrying positions, and price bands may limit daily price movement. Retail and institutional investors holding these securities may face difficulty exiting positions. Market participants are advised to review their exposure to these securities before the effective dates.
