## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of three privately placed debt securities on the capital market segment effective June 30, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. All securities are assigned a market lot of 1 and will trade under Series N0.

## Key Points

- Three new debt instruments admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from June 30, 2026
- All securities have a market lot size of 1 under Series N0
- Securities are privately placed bonds/NCDs from ESAF Small Finance Bank, Tata Projects, and LIC Housing Finance
- Trading will use designated security codes as specified in the annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing provisions under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must trade these securities only by their designated codes and in the prescribed lot sizes as specified in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 30, 2026 (all three securities admitted from this date)
- **Maturity Dates:**
  - ESAF Small Finance Bank (1165ESF32): July 25, 2032
  - Tata Projects (827TPL29): June 22, 2029
  - LIC Housing Finance (757LICH29A): November 23, 2029
