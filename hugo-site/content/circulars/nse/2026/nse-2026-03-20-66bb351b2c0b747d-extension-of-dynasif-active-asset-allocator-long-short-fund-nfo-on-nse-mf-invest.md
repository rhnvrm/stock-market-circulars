## Summary

NSE has informed its members that 360 SIF Mutual Fund has extended the New Fund Offer (NFO) for the DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund. The NFO closure has been extended to 11:59 PM on March 20, 2026 on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Key Points

- NFO extended: DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund
- New NFO closure deadline: March 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on the NFO closure date
- Clear funds via any payment mode must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by 11:59 PM on March 20, 2026
- Extension communicated by 360 SIF Mutual Fund to NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update extending the NFO subscription window for a specific mutual fund product.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client SIP/XSIP registration authentication is completed before 3:00 PM on March 20, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before 11:59 PM on March 20, 2026

## Important Dates

- NFO Closure Date: March 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM
- SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline: March 20, 2026 at 3:00 PM
- Clear Funds Receipt Deadline: March 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This extension provides existing and prospective investors a final opportunity to subscribe to the DynaSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund NFO on the same day. Members need to act promptly to meet the intraday deadlines for SIP/XSIP authentication and fund transfers. No broader market or regulatory implications.
