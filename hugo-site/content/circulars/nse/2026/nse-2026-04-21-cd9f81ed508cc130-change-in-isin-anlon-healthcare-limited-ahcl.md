## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members that the ISIN code for equity shares of Anlon Healthcare Limited (Symbol: AHCL) will be changed effective April 24, 2026. The change is a result of a sub-division of the face value of shares from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share. The new ISIN is INE0Y8W01025.

## Key Points

- The ISIN for Anlon Healthcare Limited (AHCL) is being changed to INE0Y8W01025
- The change is triggered by a stock sub-division from Rs. 10/- face value to Rs. 2/- face value
- The new ISIN is effective for all trades on and from the ex-date of April 24, 2026
- All exchange members are required to update their systems with the new ISIN

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN reassignment is a standard procedural action following a corporate action (sub-division/stock split). The old ISIN will be replaced by INE0Y8W01025 for all trading and settlement purposes from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their trading and back-office systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE0Y8W01025) for AHCL
- Trades executed on or after April 24, 2026 must use the new ISIN for settlement and reporting
- Custodians, depositories, and clearing members should ensure holdings are migrated to the new ISIN before the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Ex-date / Effective Date:** April 24, 2026 — new ISIN applicable for all trades from this date

## Impact Assessment

The sub-division reduces the face value of each share from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/-, resulting in a 5x increase in the number of shares outstanding with a proportional reduction in share price. The new ISIN (INE0Y8W01025) must be used in all downstream systems including order management, settlement, and portfolio reporting. Investors holding AHCL shares will see their holdings updated automatically by the depository. Market participants with automated trading or risk systems referencing the old ISIN must update their configurations before April 24, 2026 to avoid settlement failures.
