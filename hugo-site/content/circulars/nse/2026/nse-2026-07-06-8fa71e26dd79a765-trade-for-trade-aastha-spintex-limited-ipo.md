## Summary

NSE has announced that Aastha Spintex Limited, which listed via IPO and was placed under the trade for trade segment (Series: BE), will be transferred to the rolling settlement segment (Series: EQ) with effect from July 20, 2026. This follows the exchange's earlier circular (NSE/CML/75036 dated July 3, 2026) and is in line with SEBI guidelines under circular reference CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Symbol AASTHA (Aastha Spintex Limited) moves from trade for trade segment (Series BE) to rolling segment (Series EQ).
- The transfer takes effect from July 20, 2026.
- The action follows Exchange Circular no. NSE/CML/75036 dated July 3, 2026.
- Undertaken pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational application of existing SEBI guidelines governing newly listed IPO securities transitioning out of the trade for trade segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect AASTHA's series change from BE to EQ effective July 20, 2026, and ensure trades are settled under the rolling (T+1/T+2) settlement mechanism applicable to Series EQ rather than trade-for-trade settlement.

## Important Dates

- July 3, 2026: Prior circular (NSE/CML/75036) referenced.
- July 6, 2026: Current circular issued.
- July 20, 2026: Effective date for transfer to rolling segment (Series EQ).

## Impact Assessment

The move to the rolling segment typically increases liquidity and trading flexibility for AASTHA shares by removing the trade-for-trade restriction (which mandates delivery-based settlement without intraday squaring off). This is a routine post-IPO transition and carries limited broader market impact, though it is relevant for market participants and investors holding or trading AASTHA.
