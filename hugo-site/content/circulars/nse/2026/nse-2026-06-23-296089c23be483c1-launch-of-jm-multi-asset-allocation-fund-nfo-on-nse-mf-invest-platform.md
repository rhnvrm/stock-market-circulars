## Summary

NSE has informed its members that JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund of JM Mutual Fund is now available for subscription on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO window runs from June 24, 2026 to July 08, 2026 (till 11:59 pm), with allotment scheduled for July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO window: June 24, 2026 to July 08, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Six scheme options available across direct and regular plans
- All schemes classified under the Equity category
- Allotment date for all schemes: July 14, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 08, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Contact: Toll Free 1800 2100 940 | support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 08, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **July 08, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: NFO subscription closes; deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **July 14, 2026**: Allotment date for all six scheme options

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with limited market impact. Members using the NSE MF Invest Platform can facilitate transactions in the six JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund schemes during the NFO window. The schemes span direct and regular plans with IDCW (payout and reinvestment) and growth options.
