## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by quant Money Managers Limited under the qsif Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund scheme on the Capital Market segment, effective April 24, 2026. Four unit series across Direct and Regular plans (Growth and IDCW options) have been admitted to dealings, with a total of approximately 7.56 million units allotted on April 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four unit series listed: QSIFAADG, QSIFAADP, QSIFAARG, and QSIFAARR
- All units carry a face value of Rs. 10/- each with a market lot size of 1
- Date of allotment: April 21, 2026
- Listing effective from: April 24, 2026
- Governed under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5
- Asset Management Company: quant Money Managers Limited, Mumbai
- Trustee: quant Capital Trustee Limited, Mumbai
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade these securities using their designated NSE codes only
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit per lot
- No additional compliance obligations imposed on members beyond standard listing rules

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Date of allotment of units
- **April 23, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **April 24, 2026**: Effective date — securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
