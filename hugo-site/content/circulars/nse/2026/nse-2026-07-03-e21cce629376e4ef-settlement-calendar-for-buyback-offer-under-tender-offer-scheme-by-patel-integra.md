## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Buyback Offer under the Tender Offer scheme by Patel Integrated Logistics Limited. This follows earlier circulars NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/75030 dated July 3, 2026. Members and custodians are required to follow the prescribed settlement timeline and early pay-in procedures for tendering demat shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: X, Settlement No. 2026083
- Trade Start Date: July 6, 2026
- Trade End Date: July 10, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: July 16, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 17, 2026
- Demat shares must be tendered only via Early Pay-in of Shares through depositories' early pay-in mechanism for Market Type Buyback
- On trade end date, early pay-in of shares must be completed well in advance for order acceptance up to 3:30 p.m.
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur only after receipt of funds pay-in on Settlement Day
- A Tender Offer Acceptance Report will be issued to members detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders, along with rejection reason codes (Annexure I)
- Applicable Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes the settlement calendar for an already-approved buyback scheme referenced in prior circulars.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs must ensure early pay-in of demat shares through depository mechanisms before the trade end date cutoff
- Members must monitor the Tender Offer Acceptance Report for order status and rejection reasons
- Funds pay-in must be completed to enable securities and funds pay-out on the settlement date
- STT collection must be accounted for on the settlement day

## Important Dates

- Trade Start Date: July 6, 2026
- Trade End Date: July 10, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: July 16, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 17, 2026
- Note: Clearing Corporation may revise this schedule in case of delays in receipt of Acceptance details from RTA or delay in funds pay-in

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited but direct operational impact on members and custodians participating in the Patel Integrated Logistics Limited buyback offer, requiring adherence to specific settlement timelines and early pay-in procedures. The broader market impact is minimal as it pertains to a single company's tender offer settlement process.
