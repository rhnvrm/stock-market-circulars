## Summary

NSE issued this circular to inform members that the Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT), vide order dated June 24, 2026, has stayed the SEBI debarment order against M/s NNM Securities Private Limited and M/s Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited. These entities were originally restrained by SEBI Order No. QJA/SS/IVD-2/ID13/32429/2026-27 dated May 29, 2026, in the matter of M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited. The stay is conditional on deposit of 50% of the penalty amount within four weeks from the SAT order date. SEBI has directed the Exchange to comply with the SAT order immediately.

## Key Points

- Original SEBI order (May 29, 2026) barred NNM Securities Private Limited (PAN: AAACN8070G) and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited (PAN: AAECM1798G) from securities market access for 3 years each.
- SAT order dated June 24, 2026 (Appeal No. 181 of 2026) granted stay of debarment and recovery proceedings.
- Stay is conditional: 50% of the penalty amount must be deposited within four weeks of the SAT order (i.e., by approximately July 22, 2026).
- NNM Securities argued it is a market maker required to place buy/sell orders, which is a legally permitted activity.
- SEBI has advised NSE to comply with the SAT order immediately, effectively lifting the trading restriction on these entities.

## Regulatory Changes

The SAT stay sets aside the SEBI debarment order in the instant matter, reversing the restriction on the two named entities from accessing or dealing in the securities market. This is a partial and conditional reversal pending final disposal of Appeal No. 181 of 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members should note that the debarment against NNM Securities Private Limited and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited has been stayed and these entities may now access the securities market.
- Members should refer to the updated consolidated list of debarred entities on NSE's website: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities
- For queries, members may contact dl-invsg-all@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- May 29, 2026: Original SEBI order restraining the entities for 3 years
- June 01, 2026: Earlier NSE circular (NSE/INVG/74494) communicating the SEBI order
- June 24, 2026: SAT order granting stay of debarment (Appeal No. 181 of 2026)
- ~July 22, 2026: Deadline for deposit of 50% of penalty amount (four weeks from SAT order)
- June 29, 2026: NSE circular instructing members to comply with SAT order immediately

## Impact Assessment

The impact is primarily limited to the two named entities — NNM Securities Private Limited and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited — and their clients. With the stay granted, these entities can resume securities market activities including their market-making role. The broader market impact is low. Members who had restricted dealings with these entities based on the earlier NSE circular should update their records accordingly. The matter remains sub-judice before SAT and the debarment could be reinstated depending on the final outcome of Appeal No. 181 of 2026.
