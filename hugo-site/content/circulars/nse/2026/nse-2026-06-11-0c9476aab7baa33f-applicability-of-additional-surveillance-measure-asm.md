## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 437/2026 (NSE/SURV/74657) dated June 11, 2026, announcing updates to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework. Kandarp Digi Smart BPO Limited (symbol: KANDARP) is the sole security added to Long Term ASM Stage I effective June 12, 2026. No securities have been shortlisted for Stage IV or for any inter-stage movements in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Kandarp Digi Smart BPO Limited (KANDARP, ISIN: INE0MOT01016) is newly included in Long Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 12, 2026.
- A 100% margin rate will apply on all open positions as of June 15, 2026, and all new positions created from June 16, 2026 onwards.
- No securities have been shortlisted under Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade shift) in this circular.
- All inter-stage movement lists (Stage I→II, Stage II→III, Stage I→IV, Stage II→IV) are Nil for this cycle.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the Long Term ASM participant list under the framework established by previous circulars: NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066 (dated October 27, 2018 through September 20, 2024). Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis; however, no such securities are identified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a 100% margin rate on KANDARP for all open positions as on June 15, 2026 and for all new positions created from June 16, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ASM Stage I classification for KANDARP effective June 12, 2026.
- Market participants must ensure compliance with the ASM framework provisions in addition to any other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: KANDARP added to Long Term ASM Stage I (effective date for inclusion).
- **June 15, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to the 100% margin requirement.
- **June 16, 2026**: 100% margin rate becomes applicable on all open positions as of June 15, 2026 and all new positions created on or after this date.

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a targeted impact, affecting only one security — Kandarp Digi Smart BPO Limited (KANDARP). Traders and investors holding positions in KANDARP will face a significantly increased margin requirement of 100%, which may reduce liquidity and increase the cost of holding positions. No securities are being moved to the Trade-for-Trade segment this cycle, so there is no broader settlement-segment disruption. The overall market impact is limited given the single-stock scope and the absence of Stage IV assignments.
