## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73028, Circular Ref No: 0334/2026) informing members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited via an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The exact listing date has not been specified and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited
- **Exchange Symbol:** CLEANMAX
- **ISIN:** INE647U01026
- **Circular Reference:** NSE/CML/73028 | 0334/2026
- **Department:** Listing
- The listing date will be announced via a separate circular from NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing announcement under NSE's Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular that will confirm the official listing date for CLEANMAX equity shares.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited (CLEANMAX) adds a clean/renewable energy company to NSE. Market participants interested in the clean energy sector should monitor NSE communications for the confirmed listing date. The IPO listing will enable secondary market trading of CLEANMAX shares. Impact is moderate and sector-specific, primarily relevant to investors who participated in the IPO or are interested in the clean energy space.
