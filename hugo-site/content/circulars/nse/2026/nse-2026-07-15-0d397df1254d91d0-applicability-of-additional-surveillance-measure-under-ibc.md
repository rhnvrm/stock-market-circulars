## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), effective July 16, 2026. No new securities were added to the ASM IBC list under Annexure I. Under Annexure II, Prime Focus Limited (PFOCUS) has been excluded from the ASM IBC framework. The consolidated list of securities currently under ASM IBC Stage I includes AGS Transact Technologies (AGSTRA), CLC Industries (CLCIND), Gensol Engineering (GENSOL), Impex Ferro Tech (IMPEXFERRO), and Reliance Infrastructure (RELINFRA), among others.

## Key Points

- No securities were newly shortlisted under ASM IBC Stage I, Stage II, or stage transitions per Annexure I (all Nil).
- Prime Focus Limited (PFOCUS, ISIN INE367G01038) has been excluded from ASM IBC effective July 16, 2026.
- The consolidated ASM IBC list includes AGSTRA, CLCIND, GENSOL, IMPEXFERRO, RELINFRA and possibly other securities under Stage I surveillance.
- ASM IBC operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM IBC is a surveillance measure only and does not constitute an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under the existing ASM IBC framework established via earlier circulars (NSE/SURV/37979 dated June 08, 2018, NSE/SURV/52368 dated May 20, 2022, NSE/SURV/55582 dated February 10, 2023, and NSE/SURV/70074 dated September 05, 2025). No new regulatory framework changes were introduced; this update reflects revised shortlisting/exclusion of specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply the ASM IBC framework provisions to the updated list of securities effective July 16, 2026, in conjunction with other applicable surveillance measures. No action required for PFOCUS given its exclusion; continued compliance required for securities remaining on the consolidated ASM IBC list.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 16, 2026 (all inclusions/exclusions effective from this date)
- Circular Date: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The exclusion of PFOCUS from ASM IBC is likely to be viewed positively for that stock, as it removes additional trading restrictions and surveillance scrutiny associated with the IBC framework. Securities remaining on the ASM IBC list (AGSTRA, CLCIND, GENSOL, IMPEXFERRO, RELINFRA and others) continue to face enhanced surveillance measures including potentially higher margins and price bands, which may affect trading liquidity and investor sentiment for these stocks.
