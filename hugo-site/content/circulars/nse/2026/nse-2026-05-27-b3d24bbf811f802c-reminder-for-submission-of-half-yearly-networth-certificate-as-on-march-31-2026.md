## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder to all trading members to submit the Half Yearly Networth Certificate as on March 31, 2026. This follows the original circular NSE/COMP/73822 dated April 21, 2026. The deadline for submission is May 31, 2026, and non-compliance may attract penal or disciplinary action.

## Key Points

- Trading members must submit their Half Yearly Networth Certificate as on March 31, 2026 by May 31, 2026.
- Submission must be made through the ENIT portal: ENIT-NEW-COMPLIANCE > Compliance > New Half Yearly Networth Submission.
- The certificate must be digitally signed by the auditor.
- Failure to submit by the deadline may result in penal or disciplinary action as per NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a reminder referencing the existing requirement communicated via NSE/COMP/73822 dated April 21, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must obtain a digitally signed networth certificate from their auditor.
- Submission to be completed via the ENIT portal at https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/ under: ENIT-NEW-COMPLIANCE > Compliance > New Half Yearly Networth Submission.
- Detailed submission manual is available in the original circular NSE/COMP/73822 dated April 21, 2026.
- For support: helpdesk at 1800 266 0050 (IVR option 3) or email memcompliance_support@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **March 31, 2026**: Reference date for the networth certificate.
- **May 31, 2026**: Deadline for submission of the networth certificate.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance reminder with no direct market or trading impact. Only trading members who have not yet submitted their half yearly networth certificate are affected. Non-compliance could result in penalties or disciplinary proceedings against defaulting members.
