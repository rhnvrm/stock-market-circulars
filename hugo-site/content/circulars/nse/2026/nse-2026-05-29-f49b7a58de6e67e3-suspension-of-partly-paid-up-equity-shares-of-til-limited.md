## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of partly paid-up equity shares of TIL Limited (Symbol: TILPP, ISIN: IN9806C01016) from trading on the Capital Market segment effective June 03, 2026 (i.e., from the closing hours of trading on June 02, 2026). The suspension is on account of first and final call money.

## Key Points

- TIL Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (symbol: TILPP) will be suspended from trading effective June 03, 2026
- Trading in TILPP will cease after closing hours on June 02, 2026
- The suspension is triggered by first and final call money on the partly paid shares
- The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74464, Circular Ref. No: 0873/2026

## Regulatory Changes

In pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, the security TILPP is being suspended from trading. No broader regulatory framework changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and market participants must cease trading in TILPP (ISIN: IN9806C01016) after the closing hours of June 02, 2026
- Brokers should inform clients holding or trading in TILPP of the impending suspension
- Investors holding partly paid shares should be aware of the call money obligation that has triggered the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Last Trading Day:** June 02, 2026 (until closing hours)
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

All holders and active traders of TIL Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (TILPP) will be directly affected as trading will no longer be permitted from June 03, 2026. The suspension is a standard procedural action tied to the call money event on partly paid shares. Investors holding TILPP should be prepared to respond to the call money demand; failure to pay may result in forfeiture of the partly paid shares. Market liquidity in TILPP will be zero post-suspension.
