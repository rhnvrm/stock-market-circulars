## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited under the scheme "Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF" on the Capital Market segment, effective July 22, 2026. The units carry the trading symbol MOMMIDCAP.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF
- Symbol: MOMMIDCAP
- ISIN: INF769K01RC0
- Number of units admitted: 5,747,986
- Series: EQ
- Date of allotment: July 16, 2026
- Face value per unit: Rs. 10/-
- Paid-up value per unit: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 12.62/-
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Designated market makers include East India Securities Limited, Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited, Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited, Parwati Capital Markets Private Limited, Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited, and IRage Broking Services LLP

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. No changes to existing regulations; this is a listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify the security only by its designated code (MOMMIDCAP) and trade in the specified market lot of 1 unit. Market makers listed in the circular are required to provide liquidity for the ETF as per exchange norms.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 16, 2026
- Circular date: July 21, 2026
- Effective date of listing: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of a new passive ETF product tracking the BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 index. Impact is limited to investors in the fund, designated market makers, and the AMC. No impact on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
