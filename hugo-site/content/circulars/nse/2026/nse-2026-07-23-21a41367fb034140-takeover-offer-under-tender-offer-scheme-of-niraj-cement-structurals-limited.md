## Summary

NSE has informed all trading members that acquirer Mr. Gulshankumar Vijaykumar Chopra will conduct a takeover offer for Niraj Cement Structurals Limited using NSE's web-based bidding platform, in accordance with the Tender Offer Scheme under circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Mr. Gulshankumar Vijaykumar Chopra
- Target company: Niraj Cement Structurals Limited (Security symbol: NIRAJ, Series: TO)
- Issue size: Up to 1,55,20,529 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 29/- per share
- Offer type: Takeover (Fixed price)
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each
- Lot size: 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE368I01016
- Merchant Banker: Navigant Corporate Advisors Limited
- Registrar: Mufg Intime India Private Limited
- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Existing users of the web-based IPO bidding platform can use their current login credentials
- New members must use the admin terminal to create branches and users, with login ID format Adminxxxxx (xxxxx = Member Code)
- Order modification (quantity) and cancellation are not permitted for takeover offers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes the existing Tender Offer Scheme framework (NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022) for this specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure only eligible investors participate through the designated bidding categories (FII, MF, IC, FI, OTH, CO, IND, NOH)
- Members without existing platform access must set up admin terminal access, branches, and users prior to bidding
- Custodial confirmations must be completed within specified windows, including an extended window (9.15 a.m. to 4.00 p.m.) on the issue closure day
- No quantity modification or cancellation of orders is allowed once placed

## Important Dates

- Issue Period: July 31, 2026 to August 13, 2026
- Bidding and Custodial Confirmation Time: 9.15 a.m. to 3.30 p.m. (daily during issue period)
- Custodial Confirmation on Issue Closure Day: 9.15 a.m. to 4.00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

The circular enables operational readiness for trading members to facilitate shareholder participation in the Niraj Cement Structurals takeover offer. Impact is primarily procedural for trading members and directly relevant to NIRAJ shareholders considering tendering shares at the fixed offer price of Rs. 29/-. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
