## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) and the associated Participant, Institutional Client, and Non-Institutional Client limits for the symbol ANANDRATHI under the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Scheme. The revision is effective from June 4, 2026, and approximately doubles all existing limits.

## Key Points

- Circular reference: NCL/CMPT/74538 dated June 3, 2026 (Circular Ref. No: 057/2026)
- Partial modification of earlier circular NCL/CMPT/74480 dated May 29, 2026, which itself was issued in pursuance of NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026
- All four position limit categories for ANANDRATHI have been revised upward, roughly doubling the previous limits
- Applicable for the month of June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

Revised limits for ANANDRATHI in the SLB Scheme:

| Limit Type | Old Limit (Shares) | New Limit (Shares) |
|---|---|---|
| Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) | 4,722,705 | 9,445,410 |
| Institutional Client Limit | 472,270 | 944,541 |
| Participant Limit | 472,270 | 944,541 |
| Non-Institutional Client Limit | 47,227 | 94,454 |

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians are advised to take note of the revised limits
- Positions in ANANDRATHI under the SLB Scheme must be managed within the new limits effective June 4, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 3, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 4, 2026
- **Applicable Month:** June 2026

## Impact Assessment

The doubling of all SLB position limits for ANANDRATHI increases capacity for securities lending and borrowing activity in this stock. Participants and institutional clients who may have been constrained by the previous limits now have greater headroom to lend or borrow ANANDRATHI shares. This revision may improve liquidity in the SLB segment for this counter and could facilitate increased short-selling or hedging activity.
