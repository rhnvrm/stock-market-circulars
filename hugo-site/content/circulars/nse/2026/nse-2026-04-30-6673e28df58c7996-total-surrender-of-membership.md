## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership for three trading members effective April 29, 2026. The affected members are HR Finance & Investments, RSG Share & Stock Brokers Ltd., and Fortune Capital Market. All NSE members are notified to take note of these changes.

## Key Points

- Three trading members have surrendered their NSE membership with exchange approval
- Surrender approval date for all three members is April 29, 2026
- Last trade dates vary significantly, with RSG Share & Stock Brokers having ceased trading as far back as November 2020
- Circular issued by the Compliance Department (Ref No: NSE/COMP/73982)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an administrative notification of approved membership surrenders under existing membership surrender procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of the surrender of membership of the following entities:
- HR Finance & Investments (SEBI Reg: INZ000249526)
- RSG Share & Stock Brokers Ltd. (SEBI Reg: INB230814535)
- Fortune Capital Market (SEBI Reg: INZ000278225)

Members should ensure they do not route trades through or rely on these entities for any exchange-related activities post their surrender approval dates.

## Important Dates

| Member | Last Trade Date | Surrender Approval Date |
|---|---|---|
| HR Finance & Investments | January 14, 2025 | April 29, 2026 |
| RSG Share & Stock Brokers Ltd. | November 12, 2020 | April 29, 2026 |
| Fortune Capital Market | August 21, 2025 | April 29, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The three surrendering members appear to have been largely inactive, with RSG Share & Stock Brokers last trading in November 2020 and the others in 2025. This is a routine administrative action with no systemic implications for market operations or other participants.
