## Summary

NSE has notified all members that June 19, 2026 is a non-business day for certain mutual fund schemes managed by Nippon India Mutual Fund and ICICI Mutual Fund. All transactions including subscriptions, redemptions, switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs will be blocked for the listed schemes on this date through the NSE MF Invest platform.

## Key Points

- Non-business day applies on **June 19, 2026** for selected schemes of Nippon India Mutual Fund and ICICI Mutual Fund
- Affected transaction types: Subscriptions, Redemptions, Switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs
- Restriction applies specifically on the **NSE MF Invest platform**
- Notification issued per communication received from the respective AMCs
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/74761 | Ref No: 1436/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a temporary operational restriction for a single non-business day as communicated by the AMCs to NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure no transactions are processed for the listed schemes on June 19, 2026 via the NSE MF Invest platform
- Members should inform their clients of the temporary unavailability of transaction services for these schemes on the said date

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 19, 2026**: Non-business day; all transactions suspended for listed schemes

## Impact Assessment

Investors holding or intending to transact in the following international equity fund schemes will be unable to place orders on June 19, 2026:

**Nippon India Mutual Fund (Non-business date: 19.06.2026):**
- Nippon India Taiwan Equity Fund — Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Nippon India Taiwan Equity Fund — Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Nippon India US Equity Opportunities Fund — Growth Plan, Dividend Plan (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Nippon India US Equity Opportunities Fund — Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)

**ICICI Mutual Fund (Non-business date: 19.06.2026):**
- ICICI Prudential US Bluechip Equity Fund — Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout variants)
- ICICI Prudential US Bluechip Equity Fund — Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout variants)
- ICICI Prudential Global Stable Equity Fund (FOF) — Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)

The impact is limited to one trading day and affects predominantly international/overseas equity funds, likely tied to a market holiday in the underlying geographies (Taiwan or US markets). Investors should plan transactions around this date.
