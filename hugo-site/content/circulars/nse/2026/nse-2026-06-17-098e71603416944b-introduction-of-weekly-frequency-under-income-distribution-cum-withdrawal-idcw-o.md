## Summary

NSE has notified all members that Invesco Mutual Fund has introduced a Weekly frequency under the Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) option for Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund (an open-ended ultra-short term debt scheme). This change is effective from June 16, 2026, and is now available on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The weekly IDCW option is offered under both Regular and Direct plans, with payout and reinvestment facility.

## Key Points

- Weekly IDCW option introduced under Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund effective June 16, 2026.
- Available under both Regular Plan and Direct Plan of the scheme.
- Offers both Payout and Reinvestment sub-options.
- If IDCW payable under the Payout option is Rs. 100 or less, it will be compulsorily reinvested.
- Record date for weekly IDCW is every Monday, or the immediately succeeding Business Day if Monday is a non-business day.
- IDCW Transfer Plan is not available under the Weekly IDCW option.
- Applications received without a payout/reinvestment choice will default to reinvestment.
- Four new scheme variants added to NSE MF Invest Platform: Regular Plan Weekly IDCW Payout, Regular Plan Weekly IDCW Reinvestment, Direct Plan Weekly IDCW Payout, and Direct Plan Weekly IDCW Reinvestment.

## Regulatory Changes

Invesco Asset Management (India) Pvt. Ltd. has issued a Notice-cum-Addendum to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund to incorporate the new Weekly IDCW frequency. All other terms and conditions of the SID and KIM remain unchanged. This addendum forms an integral part of the SID/KIM as amended from time to time.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are requested to take note of the addition of new scheme variants on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- No specific compliance action is required from members beyond awareness and system readiness to process transactions under the new weekly IDCW option.
- Investors/unit holders are notified of the new option and should update their preferences as applicable.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Date of addendum issued by Invesco Asset Management (India) Pvt. Ltd.
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of introduction of Weekly IDCW option on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- **Record Date**: Every Monday (or immediately succeeding Business Day if Monday is a non-business day) for weekly IDCW distribution.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product enhancement with low market impact. Investors in Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund seeking more frequent income distributions now have an additional weekly option alongside existing frequencies. The change is limited to a single fund and does not affect trading mechanisms, margins, or broader market operations. NSE members dealing in mutual fund transactions on the MF Invest Platform should ensure their systems reflect the four newly added scheme codes/ISINs for smooth processing of subscription and redemption requests.
