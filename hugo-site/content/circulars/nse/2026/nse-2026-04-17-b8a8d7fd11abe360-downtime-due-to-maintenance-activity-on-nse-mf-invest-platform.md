## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance window for the NSE MF Invest Platform on April 25, 2026. The platform will be completely unavailable from 01:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs (a 4-hour window). All members and their technical teams/vendors should plan accordingly and avoid scheduling any transactions during this period.

## Key Points

- Complete downtime on NSE MF Invest Platform from 25 April 2026, 01:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs
- No orders or transactions will be accepted during the downtime window
- All modes of access including APIs will be unavailable
- Members connecting via API must notify their technical teams and vendors
- Contact: support@nseinvest.com | Toll Free: 1800 2100 940

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities or transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the downtime window (25 April 2026, 01:00–05:00 hrs)
- API-connected members are required to inform their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled downtime

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Maintenance Start:** April 25, 2026 at 01:00 hrs
- **Maintenance End:** April 25, 2026 at 05:00 hrs
- **Duration:** 4 hours

## Impact Assessment

The maintenance window will temporarily suspend all mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform for 4 hours in the early morning of April 25, 2026. The early morning timing (1 AM to 5 AM) minimizes disruption to regular trading hours. Members using automated API-based systems must ensure their systems are configured to handle the outage gracefully. No trades or orders will be processed during this period, so members should plan any time-sensitive transactions before or after the window.
