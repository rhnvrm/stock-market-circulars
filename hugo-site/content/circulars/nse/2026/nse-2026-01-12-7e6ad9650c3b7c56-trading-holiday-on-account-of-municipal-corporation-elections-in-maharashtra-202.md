## Summary

NSE has notified Thursday, January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday for Mutual Fund schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra. This circular partially modifies the earlier circular NSE/NMF/71897 dated December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading holiday declared for January 15, 2026 (Thursday)
- Applies specifically to NSE MF Invest Platform
- All Mutual Fund schemes affected
- Reason: Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/72281 (Circular No. 1111/2026)
- Modifies previous circular NSE/NMF/71897 dated December 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This is a partial modification to the previous circular regarding trading holidays for the year 2026. The addition of January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday was not included in the original holiday calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of NSE MF Invest Platform must suspend trading in Mutual Fund schemes on January 15, 2026
- Members should inform their clients about the trading holiday
- No orders will be processed for mutual fund schemes on this date

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026 (Thursday)**: Trading holiday for Mutual Fund schemes on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **Circular Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Reference Circular Date**: December 19, 2025 (NSE/NMF/71897)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Trading in mutual fund schemes will be suspended for one day on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members and investors will need to plan their transactions accordingly.

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as the holiday is specific to the mutual fund segment and is for a single day. Equity and other segments are not mentioned in this circular.

**Investor Impact**: Investors will not be able to place buy/sell orders for mutual fund schemes on January 15, 2026 through the NSE MF Invest Platform. Alternative platforms may remain operational depending on their respective holiday calendars.
