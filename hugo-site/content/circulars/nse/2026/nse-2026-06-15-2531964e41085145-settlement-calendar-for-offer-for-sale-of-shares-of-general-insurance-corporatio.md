## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of General Insurance Corporation of India. This circular (NCL/CMPT/74726) references the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and confirms that settlement for OFS securities will follow existing rules for secondary market transactions.

## Key Points

- OFS order dates are June 16, 2026 (non-retail) and June 17, 2026 (retail and non-retail)
- Non-retail (IS) category requires 100% margin for H-type settlements; 0% margin for T-type settlements
- Retail (RS) category has 100% margin with no custodial confirmation requirement
- Settlement dates are June 17, 2026 (for June 16 orders) and June 18, 2026 (for June 17 orders)
- Custodial confirmation deadline for non-retail IS on June 16 is 3:30 PM; on June 17 is 3:30 PM and 7:30 AM (next day)
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the OFS framework per existing secondary market settlement rules as referenced in circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must adhere to the specified settlement calendar for GIC Re OFS transactions
- Funds pay-in must be completed by 11:00 AM on T+1 day
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur by 1:30 PM on T+1 day
- For non-retail IS orders placed on June 16, custodial confirmation must be completed by 3:30 PM on June 16
- For non-retail IS orders placed on June 17, custodial confirmation must be completed by 3:30 PM on June 17 or by 7:30 AM on June 18
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement days

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Order date for non-retail (IS) category; custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM; settlement on June 17
- **June 17, 2026**: Order date for retail (RS) and non-retail (IS) categories; custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM (or 7:30 AM June 18 for T-type); settlement on June 18
- **June 17, 2026**: Settlement date for June 16 orders
- **June 18, 2026**: Settlement date for June 17 orders

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the GIC Re OFS. The settlement structure follows standard secondary market norms, with 100% upfront margin for H-type (high margin) settlements and 0% for T-type (trade-based) non-retail orders. Retail investors (RS category) are not subject to custodial confirmation. The two-day OFS window (June 16-17) and next-day settlement (T+1) align with standard equity OFS practices. Participants should ensure timely funds pay-in by 11:00 AM on T+1 to avoid settlement failures. Contact: ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in or 1800 266 0050 (Option 2).
