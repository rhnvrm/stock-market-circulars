## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in equity shares of Sanco Industries Limited (Symbol: SANCO, Series: BZ) effective June 05, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The suspension is consequent to a Scheme of Amalgamation.

## Key Points

- Trading in SANCO (Series: BZ) will be suspended from June 05, 2026 (i.e., from the closing hours of June 04, 2026)
- Reason for suspension: Scheme of Amalgamation
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74543, Circular Ref. No. 0899/2026

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, NSE has exercised its authority to suspend trading in the specified security pending the completion of the amalgamation scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified and must ensure no trading orders are placed or executed in SANCO (BZ series) from June 05, 2026 onwards
- Members should inform their clients holding or intending to trade in this security about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Last day of trading; suspension effective from closing hours
- **June 05, 2026**: Trading suspended effective this date

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Sanco Industries Limited will be unable to trade their shares on NSE from June 05, 2026. The suspension is tied to a Scheme of Amalgamation, meaning the company is undergoing a merger/amalgamation process. Investors should monitor further announcements regarding the amalgamation terms, share swap ratios, and any subsequent listing of the merged entity's shares.
