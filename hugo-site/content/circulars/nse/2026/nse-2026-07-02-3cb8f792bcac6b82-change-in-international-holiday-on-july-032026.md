## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a circular cancelling the previously declared international holiday for Friday, July 03, 2026. This follows up on an earlier circular (download reference no. NCL/CMPT/72059 dated December 30, 2025) that listed international holidays applicable for confirmation of institutional trades. Trading Members and Custodians must now treat July 03, 2026 as a regular working day for institutional trade confirmation purposes.

## Key Points

- The international holiday earlier scheduled for Friday, July 03, 2026 stands cancelled.
- This change amends the international holiday calendar circulated on December 30, 2025 (Ref No. NCL/CMPT/72059).
- Custodial rejections of institutional trades on this date will attract applicable penalties, as the day is no longer treated as a holiday.
- The circular is addressed to all Members and Custodians operating in the Capital Market segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is a correction/update to the previously notified international holiday calendar for institutional trade confirmations under the Capital Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must ensure institutional trade confirmations are processed normally on July 03, 2026, since it is no longer recognized as an international holiday. Failure to confirm trades (custodial rejections) on this date will be subject to applicable penalties.

## Important Dates

- **July 03, 2026 (Friday):** International holiday cancelled; normal institutional trade confirmation processing applies.
- **December 30, 2025:** Original circular (NCL/CMPT/72059) that had designated this date as an international holiday.
- **July 02, 2026:** Date of this circular.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and limited to custodians and trading members handling institutional trade confirmations. There is no direct effect on listed securities, trading volumes, or market pricing. However, custodians must update internal calendars promptly to avoid custodial rejections and associated penalties on July 03, 2026.
