## Summary

NSE has informed members of the Exchange that the ISIN code for the equity shares of Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Limited (JLHL) will change with effect from July 24, 2026, following a sub-division of the face value of the equity shares from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Limited (Symbol: JLHL)
- New ISIN: INE682M01020
- Reason for change: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share
- The new ISIN will be effective for all trades done on and from the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational/administrative change arising from a corporate action (stock split/sub-division) rather than a regulatory policy change. The ISIN code is being updated to reflect the new face value of the equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange must update their trading and settlement systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE682M01020) for JLHL shares effective from the ex-date. All trades in JLHL shares from July 24, 2026 onwards must reference the new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Ex-date / Effective date of new ISIN: July 24, 2026
- Circular date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading, clearing, and settlement systems that reference ISIN codes for Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Limited. Members need to ensure their systems are updated to avoid settlement mismatches. Shareholders will see the sub-divided shares (face value Rs. 2/- instead of Rs. 10/-) reflected under the new ISIN, consistent with the stock split corporate action.
