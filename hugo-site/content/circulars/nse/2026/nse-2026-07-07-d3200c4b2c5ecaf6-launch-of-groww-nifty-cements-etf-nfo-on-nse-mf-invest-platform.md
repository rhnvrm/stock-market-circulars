## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Groww Nifty Cements ETF NFO under Groww Mutual Fund will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 08, 2026 to July 22, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Groww Nifty Cements ETF - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF666M01PH7), Category: Equity, Allotment Date: 29.07.2026
- Scheme: Groww Nifty Cements ETF - Regular Growth (ISIN: INF666M01PH7), Category: Equity, Allotment Date: 29.07.2026
- NFO subscription window open on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 08, 2026 to July 22, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account before the specified deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification regarding availability of a new fund offer on the platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 23, 2026 till 15:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Opens: July 08, 2026
- NFO Subscription Closes: July 22, 2026, 11:59 pm
- Clear Funds Deadline: July 23, 2026, 15:00 pm
- Allotment Date: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact, serving primarily as an operational notice to NSE members enabling them to facilitate client transactions in the new ETF scheme during its NFO window. It does not affect existing listed securities or trading operations.
