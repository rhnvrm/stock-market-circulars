## Summary

NSE has issued an update to the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework effective May 21, 2026. The key changes include escalation of Future Market Networks Limited (FMNL) from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II, and removal of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (ONELIFECAP) from the ASM framework as it is being moved to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework.

## Key Points

- Future Market Networks Limited (FMNL, INE360L01017) is moved from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II effective May 21, 2026
- Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (ONELIFECAP, INE912L01015) is excluded from the ASM framework and moved to the ESM framework effective May 21, 2026
- No new securities have been added to Long-Term ASM Stage I or Stage IV
- No other stage transitions (Stage II→III, I→IV, II→IV, III→IV, IV→III, III→II, II→I) have any securities listed
- A large consolidated list of securities remains under the ASM framework across various stages

## Regulatory Changes

- **Stage Escalation:** FMNL moves from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II, implying stricter surveillance and higher margin requirements for trading in this security.
- **Framework Migration:** ONELIFECAP is removed from the Long-Term ASM (LTASM) framework and transferred to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, which typically carries more stringent trading restrictions.
- **Note on Stage IV:** Securities shortlisted under Criteria VII for Stage IV are shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade for Trade (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis from the date of circular issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers and members** must update margin requirements for FMNL in accordance with Long-Term ASM Stage II norms from May 21, 2026.
- **Brokers and members** must apply ESM framework trading restrictions for ONELIFECAP from May 21, 2026.
- **Investors** holding or trading in FMNL or ONELIFECAP should be aware of the changed surveillance status and associated trading conditions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 21, 2026 — All ASM framework changes (stage transitions and exclusions) come into force.
- **Circular Date:** May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is moderate and stock-specific. FMNL's move to Stage II under the Long-Term ASM framework will result in higher margin requirements and enhanced monitoring, potentially reducing liquidity and increasing trading costs for market participants in that scrip. ONELIFECAP's shift to the ESM framework represents a more significant escalation in regulatory oversight, as ESM typically entails stricter trading controls than LTASM. The overall market impact is low since the changes are confined to two securities. Investors and traders in these specific stocks should review the revised surveillance conditions before placing orders from May 21, 2026.
