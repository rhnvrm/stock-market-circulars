## Summary

NSE has circulated SEBI Order WTM/AS/WRO/32479/2026-27 dated July 09, 2026, in the matter of unregistered investment advisory activities by Zara Portal, Proprietor Mr. Anurag Jaiswal (PAN: BGEPJ2695F). The matter arose from complaints against a SEBI-registered investment advisory, Sai Proficient Research Investment Advisory (SPRIA), during examination of which Zara Portal's unregistered advisory activities came to light. SEBI has restrained the Noticee from accessing the securities market and from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner, for a period of 1 year from the date of completion of the refund directed under paragraph 29(a) of the Order.

## Key Points

- SEBI Order No. WTM/AS/WRO/32479/2026-27 dated July 09, 2026 debars Zara Portal, Proprietor Mr. Anurag Jaiswal (PAN: BGEPJ2695F), from the securities market.
- Debarment period: 1 year, commencing from the date of completion of the refund directed under paragraph 29(a) of the Order.
- The Noticee is prohibited from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner.
- The proceedings stemmed from complaints originally filed against SPRIA, a SEBI-registered investment advisory, which led to examination of Zara Portal's unregistered advisory activities.
- Noticee may close out/square off any open derivative positions within 3 months from the date of the Order or at expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Pay-in and pay-out obligations for transactions prior to the Order date are permitted to be settled.
- The consolidated list of SEBI-debarred entities is available on the NSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an enforcement action under Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A), 11B(1) and 11B(2) of the SEBI Act, 1992, against a specific individual/entity for unregistered investment advisory activities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must take note of the debarment and ensure they do not facilitate securities market access or dealings for the Noticee during the restraint period.
- Members should refer to the consolidated list of SEBI-debarred entities on the NSE website (https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/member-sebi-debarred-entities) for compliance screening.
- Any open derivative positions of the Noticee must be closed out or squared off within 3 months of the Order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: July 09, 2026
- Circular Date: July 09, 2026 (Circular Ref. No. 47/2026)
- Debarment Duration: 1 year from completion of refund under paragraph 29(a) of the Order
- Derivative position close-out deadline: within 3 months from the Order date or at expiry, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited direct market impact as it pertains to an individual/proprietorship (Zara Portal) engaged in unregistered investment advisory activities rather than a listed company or broad market segment. The primary impact is on NSE members, who must ensure compliance by not facilitating market access for the debarred entity. It reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement against unregistered investment advisors and serves as a reminder for investors to verify SEBI registration before engaging advisory services.
