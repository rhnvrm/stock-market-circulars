## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF NFO from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the subscription window of June 9 to June 16, 2026 (until 11:30 pm). Two variants are offered — Regular Growth and Direct Growth — both under the Equity category with a common ISIN.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 09, 2026 to June 16, 2026 (till 11:30 pm)
- Both Regular Growth and Direct Growth plans are available under ISIN INF109K1A476
- Fund category: Equity
- Allotment date: June 19, 2026
- Clear funds must reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by June 18, 2026 till 12:00 pm
- Contact for support: Toll Free 1800 2100 940 / support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard product launch notification for a new ETF NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds via any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 18, 2026 by 12:00 pm.
- Members should make the NFO available to clients on the NSE MF Invest Platform within the subscription window.

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **June 16, 2026 (11:30 pm)**: NFO subscription closes
- **June 18, 2026 (12:00 pm)**: Deadline for clear funds to reach Clearing Corporation account
- **June 19, 2026**: Allotment date for both Regular and Direct Growth plans

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it is a routine NFO launch notification. Members using the NSE MF Invest Platform need to make the scheme available to investors and ensure timely fund settlement. Investors interested in gaining exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 index via an ETF vehicle can subscribe during the window. No impact on existing securities or trading activities.
