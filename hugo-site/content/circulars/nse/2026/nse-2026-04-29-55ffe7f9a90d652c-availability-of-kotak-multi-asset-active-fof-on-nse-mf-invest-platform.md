## Summary

Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF schemes of Kotak Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 30, 2026. The platform will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities for these schemes.

## Key Points

- Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform from April 30, 2026
- Six scheme variants are being made available, covering Regular and Direct plans across Growth and IDCW (Reinvestment and Payout) options
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available alongside standard subscription and redemption
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment (Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/73950, Circular Ref. No: 1340/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding platform availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of these schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform and communicate the same to relevant clients as applicable.

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF schemes on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact and is relevant primarily to NSE members and investors interested in Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF. It restores access to six scheme variants (Regular/Direct, Growth/IDCW Reinvestment/IDCW Payout) with ISINs INF174KA1ZP7, INF174KA1ZR3, INF174KA1ZT9, INF174KA1ZO0, INF174KA1ZQ5, and INF174KA1ZS1. No new regulatory obligations are introduced.
