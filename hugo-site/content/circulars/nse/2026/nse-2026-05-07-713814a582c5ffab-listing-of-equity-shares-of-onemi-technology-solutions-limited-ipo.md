## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/74093, Circular Ref. No: 0743/2026) informing all exchange members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited on the National Stock Exchange of India. The company will trade under the symbol **KISSHT**. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited
- Trading Symbol: KISSHT
- ISIN: INE12F801023
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/74093 / 0743/2026
- Listing date to be announced via a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification circular issued by the Listing department of NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All members of the Exchange are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited (KISSHT). Members should await the separate circular that will confirm the exact date of listing before enabling trading in this security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: May 07, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited (KISSHT) represents a new equity security being introduced to the NSE. Market participants, brokers, and investors interested in the fintech/EMI financing sector should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. The impact is moderate — it adds a new tradeable instrument but does not affect existing market operations or regulations.
