## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in five Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited, effective March 11, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the maturity of these debt instruments, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Five NCDs of Sammaan Capital Limited are suspended from trading effective March 11, 2026
- Suspension reason for all five instruments: Maturity
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73227, Circular Ref. No. 0418/2026
- Issued by the Listing Department of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities upon maturity or other qualifying events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of NSE must cease trading in the listed NCD symbols from March 11, 2026
- Affected instruments and their ISINs:
  - 925SCL26A — INE148I07SA1
  - 965SCL26 — INE148I07SD5
  - 888SCL26 — INE148I07SB9
  - 925SCL26B — INE148I07SC7
  - 0SCL26 — INE148I07SF0

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only the five maturing NCD instruments of Sammaan Capital Limited. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of each instrument. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
