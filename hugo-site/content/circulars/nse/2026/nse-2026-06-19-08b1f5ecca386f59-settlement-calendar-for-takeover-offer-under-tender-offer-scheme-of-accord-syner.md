## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Takeover Offer under the Tender Offer scheme of Accord Synergy Limited (Circular Ref. No: 0214/2026, Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/74763). This follows the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74729 dated June 16, 2026. Members, Custodians, and PCMs are required to note and comply with the settlement schedule.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X (Settlement No. 2026075) is designated for this Takeover Offer.
- Trade window opens on June 24, 2026 and closes on July 8, 2026.
- Obligation download is scheduled for July 22, 2026; settlement date is July 23, 2026.
- The Clearing Corporation may revise the settlement schedule if there are delays in receipt of Acceptance details from the RTA or delays in funds pay-in by the buying trading member.
- Demat shares must be tendered exclusively via the Early Pay-in mechanism of depositories under Market Type Buyback.
- On the trade end date (July 8, 2026), early pay-in of shares must be completed well in advance of the 03:30 p.m. deadline for order acceptance.
- Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operationalises the settlement framework for the Accord Synergy Limited takeover tender offer as previously notified.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must tender demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism via depositories for Market Type Buyback.
- Early pay-in of shares on the trade end date must be submitted well before 03:30 p.m.
- Securities and funds pay-out will be processed only after receipt of funds pay-in on the Settlement Day.
- Members should review the Tender Offer Acceptance Report, which details accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders, along with rejection reason codes (see Annexure I).
- STT obligations must be fulfilled on the applicable settlement day.
- For further details, members must refer to the scheme document cited in circular NCL/CMPT/73996 and NSE/CMTR/74729.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Trade Start Date | June 24, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | July 8, 2026 |
| Obligation Download Date | July 22, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 23, 2026 |

*Note: Dates may be revised by the Clearing Corporation in case of delays from RTA or buying trading member.*

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a focused operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Accord Synergy Limited takeover tender offer. Participants must ensure timely early pay-in of shares and funds to avoid rejection or partial allocation. The Annexure I rejection code list (covering 20 codes including signature mismatches, invalid documents, stop transfers, and ineligible shares) provides guidance on common pitfalls. Broader market impact is limited to entities directly involved in this specific offer.
