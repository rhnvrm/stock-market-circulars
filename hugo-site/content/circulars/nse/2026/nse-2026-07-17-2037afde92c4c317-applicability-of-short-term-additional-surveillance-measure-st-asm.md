## Summary

NSE Circular Ref. No. 540/2026 (Download Ref No. NSE/SURV/75258), dated July 17, 2026, notifies members that certain securities have satisfied the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM). Crop Life Science Limited (CLSL), Global Surfaces Limited (GSLSU) and Identical Brains Studios Limited (IDENTICAL) have been shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I. No securities have been shortlisted under Stage II, and no securities are moving between Stage I and Stage II in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I effective July 20, 2026:
  - Crop Life Science Limited (CLSL) — ISIN INE00NH01017
  - Global Surfaces Limited (GSLSU) — ISIN INE0JSX01015
  - Identical Brains Studios Limited (IDENTICAL) — ISIN INE0TD101011
- No securities were added to ST-ASM Stage II in this cycle.
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I in this cycle.
- This action is issued under the framework established by earlier circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558 and NSE/SURV/64066.
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a surveillance mechanism and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly identified securities based on surveillance criteria (price/volume volatility, volatility, client concentration, etc.).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the applicable additional margin on all open and new positions in the listed securities from the effective date.
- Members should monitor the price band reinstatement rules: once a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band reverts to the pre-shortlisting band, unless it remains under another surveillance measure.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 17, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I shortlisting effective: July 20, 2026
- Applicable margin rate effective: July 21, 2026, on all open positions as of July 20, 2026 and new positions created from July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Trading in CLSL, GSLSU and IDENTICAL will require a minimum 50% margin (or existing margin if higher, capped at 100%) from July 21, 2026, which is likely to reduce leverage, dampen speculative trading, and could reduce short-term liquidity in these three securities. The overall market impact is limited given the small number and scale of securities involved.
