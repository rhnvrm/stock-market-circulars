## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Part A, notifying that trading in five privately placed Non-Convertible Securities will be suspended effective July 16, 2026, on account of redemption of the underlying securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to five specific ISINs across five issuers.
- The reason for suspension in all cases is redemption of the securities.
- Suspended securities:
  - REC Limited — ISIN INE020B08EI8
  - L&T Finance Limited — ISIN INE027E07AQ0
  - Power Finance Corporation Limited — ISIN INE134E08DS2
  - National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) — ISIN INE261F08DX0
  - Muthoot Finance Limited — ISIN INE414G07IG9

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational action taken under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in these Non-Convertible Securities must cease trading in the listed ISINs effective the suspension date and update their systems/records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Suspension effective date: July 16, 2026 (for all five securities)
- Circular date: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact notification affecting a limited set of privately placed debt securities that are being redeemed. It does not affect equity trading or broader market operations, and only concerns bondholders/members dealing in these specific NCDs.
