## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in privately placed non-convertible securities of Sammaan Capital Limited (ISIN: INE148I07EM6) effective April 24, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the security, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading in Sammaan Capital Limited's non-convertible security (ISIN: INE148I07EM6) will be suspended from April 24, 2026
- Suspension reason: Redemption of the security
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73878 | Ref No: 0675/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is issued under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs trading suspensions in debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in ISIN INE148I07EM6 (Sammaan Capital Limited) from April 24, 2026
- No further trades should be executed in this security on or after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 23, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of Sammaan Capital Limited's specific non-convertible security (INE148I07EM6). Since the suspension is due to redemption, investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. There is no broader market impact. Members dealing in debt securities should update their systems to block trading in this ISIN from April 24, 2026.
