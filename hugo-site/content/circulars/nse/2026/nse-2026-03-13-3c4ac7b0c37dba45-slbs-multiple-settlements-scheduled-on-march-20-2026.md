## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced that multiple Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) settlements have been scheduled on March 20, 2026, owing to a settlement holiday on March 19, 2026. Participants and custodians must adhere to revised pay-in and pay-out timings and note corresponding depository cut-off times.

## Key Points

- Settlement holiday on March 19, 2026 has resulted in multiple SLBS settlements being clubbed on March 20, 2026.
- Three settlement batches are scheduled on March 20, 2026 with distinct timings.
- Participants and custodians must observe revised depository cut-off timings for pay-in.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational scheduling notice in response to a settlement holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians must note the revised settlement schedule and ensure timely pay-in within the specified windows.
- Participants must also account for depository-end cut-off timings applicable due to the revised settlement schedule on March 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 19, 2026**: Settlement holiday (no SLBS settlement).
- **March 20, 2026**: Multiple SLBS settlements scheduled:
  - **L 2026053** (Transaction Date: March 18, 2026) — Pay-in: 08:00 hrs, Pay-out: 09:30 hrs
  - **P 2026750** (Settlement Date: March 20, 2026) — Pay-in: 08:30 hrs, Pay-out: 09:45 hrs
  - **L 2026054** (Transaction Date: March 19, 2026) — Pay-in: 10:15 hrs, Pay-out: 12:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on SLBS participants and custodians. The compressed settlement schedule on March 20, 2026 requires careful management of pay-in obligations across three settlement windows within a single trading day. Failure to meet any of the specified pay-in timings could result in settlement failures. No impact on equity cash or derivatives markets broadly.
