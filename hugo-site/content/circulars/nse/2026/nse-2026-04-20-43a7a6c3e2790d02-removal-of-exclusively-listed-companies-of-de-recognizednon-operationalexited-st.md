## Summary

NSE has announced the removal of Sita Bhagwat Investments and Finance Limited from its Dissemination Board (DB) with effect from April 21, 2026. The removal is pursuant to SEBI circulars governing exclusively listed companies of de-recognized, non-operational, or exited stock exchanges.

## Key Points

- Sita Bhagwat Investments and Finance Limited is being removed from the NSE Dissemination Board
- The reason for removal is that the company's status on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) website is listed as 'strike off'
- The removal is effective from April 21, 2026
- The action is taken under SEBI circulars dated October 10, 2016, August 01, 2017, and October 16, 2023

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued pursuant to:
- SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016
- SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2017/92 dated August 01, 2017
- Chapter 6 of Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD2/PoD-2/CIR/P/2023/171 dated October 16, 2023

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are requested to note that Sita Bhagwat Investments and Finance Limited will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board of NSE from April 21, 2026 onwards. No further trading or dissemination activity should be expected for this company on the NSE platform.

## Important Dates

- **April 20, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 21, 2026**: Effective date of removal from NSE Dissemination Board

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and localized. The Dissemination Board is a platform for exclusively listed companies from de-recognized or exited stock exchanges, not a primary trading platform. The removal of Sita Bhagwat Investments and Finance Limited, which has been struck off by MCA, has negligible market impact. Investors holding shares in this company should be aware that no dissemination service will be available on NSE for this company post April 21, 2026.
