## Summary

NSE has notified the applicability of Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) on select securities, effective July 16, 2026. Five securities — Fino Payments Bank Limited (FINOPB), JITF Infralogistics Limited (JITFINFRA), Master Components Limited (MASTER), Shreedhar Spinners Limited (SHREEDHAR), and Swaraj Suiting Limited (SWARAJ) — have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I. No securities were added to Stage II, and there were no movements between Stage I and Stage II in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Five securities newly qualify for ST-ASM Stage I: FINOPB, JITFINFRA, MASTER, SHREEDHAR, and SWARAJ.
- No securities have been shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage II in this circular.
- No securities are shortlisted to move from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I.
- Stage I securities attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The ASM framework operates alongside all other existing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be viewed as an adverse action against the listed company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing ST-ASM framework established via earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update applying existing ST-ASM criteria to identify newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE trading members must apply the applicable margin rate of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) on all open positions in the listed Stage I securities as of July 16, 2026, and on new positions created from July 17, 2026 onward. Members should ensure their risk management and margin collection systems are updated to reflect these enhanced margin requirements for the affected securities.

## Important Dates

- **July 15, 2026**: Circular issue date.
- **July 16, 2026**: ST-ASM Stage I applicability date; margin applies to all open positions as on this date.
- **July 17, 2026**: Effective date from which the 50% (or higher) margin rate applies to open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of ST-ASM Stage I on FINOPB, JITFINFRA, MASTER, SHREEDHAR, and SWARAJ will increase the cost of trading in these securities due to the higher margin requirement (50% or existing margin, whichever is higher), potentially reducing speculative activity and liquidity. This is a routine, security-specific surveillance action rather than a broad market event, so the overall market impact is limited to the affected stocks and their existing shareholders/traders.
