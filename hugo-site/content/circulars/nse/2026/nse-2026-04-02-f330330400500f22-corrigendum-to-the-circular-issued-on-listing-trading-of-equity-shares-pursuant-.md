## Summary

NSE has issued a corrigendum to Circular No. NSE/CML/73561 dated April 01, 2026, which pertained to the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to the bonus issue of R&B Denims Limited. The correction clarifies that, following a change in ISIN for R&B Denims Limited (per NSE/CML/73536), the bonus shares will be listed and traded under the new ISIN INE012Q01039.

## Key Points

- Original circular reference: NSE/CML/73561 dated April 01, 2026 (Listing & Trading of Bonus Shares — R&B Denims Limited)
- The ISIN for R&B Denims Limited has changed as per circular NSE/CML/73536
- Bonus shares of R&B Denims Limited will now be listed and traded under new ISIN: **INE012Q01039**
- All exchange members are required to take note of this correction

## Regulatory Changes

The listing and trading of R&B Denims Limited bonus shares has been updated to reflect the new ISIN INE012Q01039, superseding any ISIN referenced in the earlier circular NSE/CML/73561.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must update their records to use the new ISIN INE012Q01039 for R&B Denims Limited bonus shares
- Any orders, settlements, or references tied to the bonus issue must reference the corrected ISIN

## Important Dates

- Corrigendum issued: April 02, 2026
- Original circular date: April 01, 2026 (NSE/CML/73561)
- ISIN change reference circular: NSE/CML/73536

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has a moderate operational impact on members and investors dealing in R&B Denims Limited bonus shares. Using an incorrect ISIN could result in settlement failures or mismatched trades. Members should promptly update internal systems and ensure all transactions for the bonus shares reference the new ISIN INE012Q01039.
