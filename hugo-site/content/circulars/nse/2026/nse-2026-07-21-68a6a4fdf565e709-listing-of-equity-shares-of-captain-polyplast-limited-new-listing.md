## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of Captain Polyplast Limited's equity shares to dealings on the Capital Market segment under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The shares will be listed and available for trading with effect from July 23, 2026, under the symbol CPL.

## Key Points

- Symbol: CPL
- Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE536P01021
- Face Value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Total number of securities: 60,178,790 equity shares
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 60,178,790
- Market lot: 1 share
- Trading in compulsory demat form for all investors in the Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- Pari passu status: Yes
- Registered Office: Upper Level - 25, Royal Complex, Dhebar Road, Bhutkhana Chowk, Rajkot - 360002, Gujarat
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular implements admission of the security under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (listing) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must identify the security only by its designated NSE codes (Symbol CPL, Series EQ) and trade only in the specified lot size of 1 share. A portion of the shareholding is subject to lock-in as per Annexure-I, with tranches locked until September 15, 2026, November 15, 2026, and January 31, 2027 respectively; the remainder is free for trading.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective/Listing Date: July 23, 2026
- Lock-in expiry dates: September 15, 2026; November 15, 2026; January 31, 2027 (for respective share tranches)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing notification enabling trading of Captain Polyplast Limited shares on NSE. Impact is limited to investors and market participants interested in this specific stock, with no broader market-wide implications. Trading liquidity may be constrained initially by the lock-in restrictions on a portion of the total share count.
