## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Buyback Offer under the Tender Offer scheme by Dhanuka Agritech Limited (NSE: DHANUKA). This follows circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026 and NSE/CMTR/74503 dated June 02, 2026. Members, custodians, and PCMs are required to adhere to the specified timeline and operational instructions for tendering demat shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X (Settlement No. 2026060) applies to the Dhanuka Agritech buyback offer
- Trade window runs from June 4, 2026 to June 10, 2026
- Early Pay-in of Shares is the only accepted mechanism for tendering demat shares (Market Type: Buyback)
- Early pay-in on the trade end date must be completed well before 03:30 p.m.
- Securities and funds pay-out will be made only after receipt of funds pay-in on settlement day
- Tender Offer Acceptance Report will detail accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders with rejection reason codes
- Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day
- NSE Clearing may revise the schedule if RTA acceptance details are delayed or buying trading member funds pay-in is delayed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the previously announced buyback offer by publishing the specific settlement calendar and operational procedures for members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must tender demat shares exclusively via the Early Pay-in mechanism of depositories under Market Type: Buyback
- Early pay-in of shares on trade end date (June 10, 2026) must be submitted well in advance of the 03:30 p.m. cut-off
- Members should review the Tender Offer Acceptance Report for order status (accepted, partially accepted, rejected) and act on rejection/partial allocation reason codes (Annexure I)
- Buying trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid revised settlement schedules

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | June 02, 2026 |
| Trade Start Date | June 04, 2026 |
| Trade End Date | June 10, 2026 (Early pay-in cut-off: 03:30 p.m.) |
| Obligation Download Date | June 16, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 17, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Dhanuka Agritech buyback offer. The early pay-in requirement through depositories (Market Type: Buyback) and the 03:30 p.m. cut-off on the trade end date require timely action. Rejection codes covering issues such as signature discrepancies, demat account mismatches, excess tendering beyond eligibility, and record date eligibility (codes 16–18 specifically for buyback offers) highlight the need for members to verify shareholder details before submission. Delays in RTA acceptance data or fund pay-in by buying members could trigger a revised settlement schedule, adding operational risk.
