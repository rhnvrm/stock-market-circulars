## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on its Capital Market segment, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The circular specifies that Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by TVS Motor Company Limited will be admitted to dealings effective July 22, 2026, along with their designated security code and trading lot size as required under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Security listed: TVS Motor Company Limited NCDs, symbol "730TMCL29", series N0
- Issue description: TMCL 7.30% 2029
- Coupon rate: 7.30% per annum
- Date of maturity: July 17, 2029
- ISIN Code: INE494B08044
- Market lot: 1
- Effective date of listing: July 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size specification), not a change to the regulatory framework itself.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify and trade this security using its designated security code (730TMCL29, series N0) and must adhere to the specified market lot size of 1 unit when transacting on the system from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/CML/75314, Circular Ref. No: 1189/2026)
- July 22, 2026: Effective date for listing and trading of the security
- July 17, 2029: Maturity date of the NCD

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact, being a routine administrative listing of a single privately placed debt instrument. It is primarily relevant to institutional or private investors holding TVS Motor Company's 7.30% 2029 NCDs and to trading members who need to update their systems with the new security code and lot size to facilitate trading from July 22, 2026. There is no impact on TVS Motor Company's equity shares or broader operations.
