## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the Futures & Options (F&O) segment settlement schedule for August 2026. The circular specifies the settlement date corresponding to each trading day of the month, enabling clearing members to plan pay-in and pay-out obligations.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 083/2026, Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/75000, issued by NSE Clearing Limited's Futures & Options Department.
- Settlement generally follows a T+1 cycle, with trade date settlements occurring the next business day.
- Notable exception: trades on 24-Aug-2026 and 25-Aug-2026 both settle on 27-Aug-2026, reflecting an intervening holiday/non-settlement day.
- The final trading day of the month, 31-Aug-2026, settles on 01-Sep-2026.
- Applicable to all members of the F&O segment.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational schedule notification, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members must align their funds and securities pay-in/pay-out processes with the specified settlement dates for each trade date in August 2026.

## Important Dates

- Schedule covers trade dates from 03-Aug-2026 to 31-Aug-2026.
- Settlement dates range from 04-Aug-2026 to 01-Sep-2026.
- Combined settlement date of 27-Aug-2026 applies to trades from both 24-Aug-2026 and 25-Aug-2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a standard administrative schedule required for clearing operations. It affects operational planning for NSE Clearing members but does not influence trading activity, stock prices, or investor decisions.
