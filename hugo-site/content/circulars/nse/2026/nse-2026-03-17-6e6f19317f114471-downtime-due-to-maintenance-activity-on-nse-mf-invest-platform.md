## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the NSE MF Invest Platform will undergo a complete maintenance downtime on March 17, 2026, between 18:00 hrs and 19:00 hrs. During this one-hour window, the platform will be entirely unavailable, and members are requested to avoid any activities or transactions.

## Key Points

- Complete downtime scheduled on **March 17, 2026, from 18:00 hrs to 19:00 hrs**
- The NSE MF Invest Platform will be fully unavailable during this window
- Members must refrain from all activities and transactions on the platform during downtime
- Members using API connectivity should inform their technical teams and/or vendors in advance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational maintenance notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must not carry out any activities or transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform between 18:00 and 19:00 hrs on March 17, 2026
- Members connecting via API are required to notify their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled downtime

## Important Dates

- **Downtime Start:** March 17, 2026, 18:00 hrs
- **Downtime End:** March 17, 2026, 19:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and temporary. The one-hour maintenance window affects only the NSE MF Invest Platform (NMF TM Segment). Members and their API vendors should plan mutual fund investment activities outside this window. No broader market or trading operations are affected.
