## Summary

NSE has notified the applicability of the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework on select securities. Aequs Limited (AEQUS) has been newly shortlisted under Long Term ASM Stage I, while Teerth Gopicon Limited (TGL) has been moved from Stage I to Stage II. No securities were shortlisted for Stage IV or for moves into Stage III or Stage IV in this cycle. These actions take effect from July 10, 2026, with the associated 100% margin requirement applicable from July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Aequs Limited (AEQUS, ISIN: INE947N01017) shortlisted under Long Term ASM Framework Stage I w.e.f. July 10, 2026
- Teerth Gopicon Limited (TGL, ISIN: INE0K6601012) moved from Long Term ASM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. July 10, 2026
- No securities shortlisted under Stage IV (Criteria VII) in this circular
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage III, Stage I to Stage IV, or Stage II to Stage IV in this cycle
- Applicable margin rate is 100% on all open and new positions from July 14, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under ASM is a surveillance measure only and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is an update under the existing Long Term ASM framework established via prior circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, NSE/SURV/64066). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic shortlisting update identifying which securities meet the existing ASM criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/brokers must apply 100% margin on all open positions in AEQUS (as on July 13, 2026) and new positions created from July 14, 2026 onwards. For TGL, members must apply the Stage II ASM surveillance actions as per the framework. No securities require shifting from Rolling Settlement (EQ series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE series) in this circular since no Stage IV/Criteria VII inclusions were identified.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Effective date for ASM stage shortlisting/movement (AEQUS to Stage I, TGL to Stage II)
- July 13, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- July 14, 2026: 100% margin becomes applicable on all open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The ASM inclusion will increase trading costs and reduce leverage for market participants holding or trading AEQUS and TGL due to the 100% margin requirement, which may reduce trading volumes and liquidity in these securities. The impact is confined to the two named securities and does not affect the broader market or other listed companies.
