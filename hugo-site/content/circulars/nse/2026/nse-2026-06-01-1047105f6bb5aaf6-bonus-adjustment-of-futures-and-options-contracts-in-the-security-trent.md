## Summary

NSE has announced adjustments to Futures and Options contracts for TRENT LIMITED (symbol: TRENT) pursuant to a 1:2 bonus issue. The adjustments are effective from the ex-date of June 4, 2026, with an adjustment factor of 1.5. Market lot sizes will be revised for both June and July expiry contracts, and revised option strike prices will be communicated one day prior to the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Symbol: TRENT (TRENT LIMITED)
- Corporate action type: BONUS, ratio 1:2, face value Re 1/-
- Ex-date / effective date: June 4, 2026
- Adjustment factor: 1.5
- Revised market lot — June Expiry: 150; July Expiry onwards: 225 (per circular 73549 dated March 30, 2026)
- Revised option strike prices will be provided one day prior to the ex-date
- Adjusted prices/lot sizes may appear in decimal places and will be rounded to the nearest tick size / nearest integer

## Regulatory Changes

In line with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments to F&O contracts, the existing contract parameters for TRENT (lot size, futures base prices, option strike prices) are being revised to reflect the bonus issue. The methodology for position adjustments will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications before trading on the ex-date (June 4, 2026).
- Files are available from the `faoftp/faocommon` directory on the Extranet server and also on the NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives.
- Members should refer to the adjustment details at https://www.nseindia.com/products-services/equity-derivatives-corporate-actions-adjustments.

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 3, 2026**: Revised option strike prices to be provided (one day prior to ex-date)
- **June 4, 2026**: Ex-date / effective date — all F&O contract adjustments come into force

## Impact Assessment

TRENT F&O traders will see their contracts adjusted effective June 4, 2026. The June expiry lot size becomes 150 and July expiry onwards becomes 225, reflecting the 1.5 adjustment factor from the 1:2 bonus. Option strike prices will be proportionally adjusted and communicated on June 3, 2026. Members who fail to load updated contract files before trading on the ex-date risk using stale contract parameters, which could lead to order-entry errors or incorrect position calculations.
