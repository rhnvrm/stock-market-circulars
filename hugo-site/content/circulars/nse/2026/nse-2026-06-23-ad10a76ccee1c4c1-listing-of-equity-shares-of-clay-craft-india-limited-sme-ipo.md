## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Clay Craft India Limited on the NSE EMERGE SME Platform with effect from June 24, 2026. The shares will trade under the symbol CLAYCRAFT in Series ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment – Settlement Type 'W') with a market lot of 600 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** CLAYCRAFT
- **ISIN:** INE1QGM01024
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 203/- per share
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Total Securities:** 2,05,70,280 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 20,570,280
- **Series:** ST – Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS), Settlement Type 'W'
- **Market Lot (Minimum):** 600 equity shares
- **Market Maker:** Hem Finlease Private Limited, Jaipur
- **Market Maker Maximum Permissible Spread:** Within 10%
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent:** Kfin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

The shares will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment – Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment – Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI circular nos. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

The security will be included in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips per SEBI circulars dated January 20, 2012, and available for continuous market trading per NSE Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated symbol CLAYCRAFT and series ST for trading.
- All trades must be in the specified lot size of 600 equity shares.
- Market Maker (Hem Finlease Private Limited) must maintain spread within 10%.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specified tranches of shares as per Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (multiple tranches):** June 24, 2027 for promoter/anchor shares (5,048,760 + 576,925 + 5,220,595 shares and others as per Annexure I)

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces Clay Craft India Limited, a Jaipur-based company (Vishwakarma Industrial Area), to the NSE EMERGE SME Platform. Investors and market participants should note the initial trade-for-trade (T2T) settlement restriction, which prevents intraday trading and requires delivery on both buy and sell sides. The minimum market lot of 600 shares at an issue price of Rs. 203 implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,21,800. Lock-in on a significant portion of shares until June 24, 2027 limits free float in the near term.
