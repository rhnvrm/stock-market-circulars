## Summary

Whiteoak Capital Mutual Fund is launching the Whiteoak Capital Consumption Opportunities Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for subscription from January 20, 2026 to February 03, 2026 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two scheme options available: Regular Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AE1) and Direct Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AD3)
- Both schemes fall under the Equity category
- NFO period: January 20, 2026 to February 03, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: February 10, 2026
- Fund clearing deadline: February 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Available through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account through any payment mode on or before February 09, 2026 at 11:59 PM
- Standard NFO subscription and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: January 20, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: February 03, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Fund Payment Deadline**: February 09, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine NFO launch that expands investment options for mutual fund investors on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The consumption opportunities theme targets equity investments in consumer-oriented sectors. No impact on existing trading operations or compliance frameworks.
