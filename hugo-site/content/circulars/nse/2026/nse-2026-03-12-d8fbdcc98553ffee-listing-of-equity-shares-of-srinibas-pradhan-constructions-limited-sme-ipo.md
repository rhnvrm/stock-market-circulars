## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Srinibas Pradhan Constructions Limited (ticker: SPCON) on the SME platform. The shareholding pattern has been filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as a post-issue IPO filing dated 11-Mar-2026.

## Key Points

- Srinibas Pradhan Constructions Limited is listed on NSE's SME platform following its IPO
- Shareholding pattern filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) as a post-IPO disclosure (11-Mar-2026)
- Security type: Equity Shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Shares are locked-in for both promoter and public shareholder categories
- No pledged or encumbered promoter shares
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner declared

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular pertains to compliance with existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 31 governing the submission of shareholding patterns upon listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has submitted the required shareholding pattern declaration under Regulation 31(1)(a)
- All applicable fields declared as 'No' will be treated as 'Zero' by default on the exchange's dissemination platform
- Lock-in provisions apply to both promoter group and public shareholder shares as declared

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern filing date: 11-Mar-2026 (post-IPO)
- Listing date: 12-Mar-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing announcement. The impact is limited to investors and traders interested in the SME segment. Srinibas Pradhan Constructions Limited's shares are now tradeable on NSE's SME platform. Lock-in on shares for both promoters and public shareholders limits near-term liquidity. No encumbrances or complex capital structures (warrants, convertibles, DRs) are present, indicating a straightforward equity listing.
