## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities of four companies on the SME EMERGE platform of the Capital Market segment, effective July 20, 2026. The shares were allotted under preferential issue/allotment and are subject to lock-in restrictions. This circular specifies the ISIN, face value, issue price, number of securities, distinctive number ranges, market lots and lock-in details for each company as per Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Further securities of Abhishek Integrations Limited, Madhusudan Masala Limited, Moxsh Overseas Educon Limited and On Door Concepts Limited admitted to dealings on NSE SME EMERGE effective July 20, 2026.
- Abhishek Integrations Limited (Symbol: AILIMITED, ISIN: INE0CAJ01017): 316,500 equity shares of Rs. 10 each at issue price Rs. 55, allotted under Preferential Issue on 13-Jun-2026; market lot 1500; locked in until 12-Feb-2028.
- Madhusudan Masala Limited (Symbol: MADHUSUDAN, ISIN: INE0P6701019): 895,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each at issue price Rs. 181, allotted under Preferential Issue on 13-Jun-2026; market lot 1000; lock-in split as 773,000 shares until 31-Jan-2028 and 122,000 shares until 31-Jan-2027.
- Moxsh Overseas Educon Limited (Symbol: MOXSH, ISIN: INE0N6D01014): 38,400 equity shares of Rs. 10 each at issue price Rs. 80, allotted on Preferential Basis on 12-May-2026; market lot 1600; locked in until 09-Mar-2027.
- On Door Concepts Limited (Symbol: ONDOOR): further details (ISIN, issue price, allotment date, quantity, lock-in) were cut off in the source document and are not available in this circular extract.
- All securities are stated to rank pari passu with existing shares of the respective companies.
- Trading in these securities will be identified only by designated security codes and conducted in the specified market lots.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (listing of further securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed securities only by their designated security codes and trade them strictly in the specified market lots (1500 for AILIMITED, 1000 for MADHUSUDAN, 1600 for MOXSH). Lock-in restrictions on the allotted shares must be observed as per the dates specified until the shares can be freely traded.

## Important Dates

- July 20, 2026: Effective date for listing of further securities on SME EMERGE.
- 12-Feb-2028: Lock-in expiry for Abhishek Integrations Limited shares.
- 31-Jan-2028: Lock-in expiry for 773,000 Madhusudan Masala Limited shares.
- 31-Jan-2027: Lock-in expiry for 122,000 Madhusudan Masala Limited shares.
- 09-Mar-2027: Lock-in expiry for Moxsh Overseas Educon Limited shares.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing update affecting the free float and share capital structure of four small-cap SME EMERGE companies. The impact is limited to shareholders and traders of these specific stocks; broader market impact is negligible. The lock-in provisions restrict immediate tradability of the newly allotted preferential shares, which may modestly affect near-term float and price discovery for the respective securities.
