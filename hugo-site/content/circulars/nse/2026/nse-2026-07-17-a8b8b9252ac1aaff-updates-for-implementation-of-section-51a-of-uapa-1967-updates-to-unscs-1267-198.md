## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No. 32/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/ISC/75243) forwarding a communication from SEBI regarding updates to the United Nations Security Council's (UNSC) ISIL (Da'esh) and Al-Qaida Sanctions List under the Committee established pursuant to resolutions 1267 (1999), 1989 (2011) and 2253 (2015). The update pertains to Press Release SC/16407 dated July 8, 2026, which reflects an amendment to one entry on the Sanctions List. This is issued in the context of implementation of Section 51A of the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act (UAPA), 1967, which mandates freezing of funds, financial assets, or economic resources of individuals/entities identified under UNSC sanctions measures.

## Key Points

- SEBI forwarded UNSC Security Council Committee Press Release SC/16407 (dated July 8, 2026) to NSE regarding an amendment to one entry on the ISIL (Da'esh) and Al-Qaida Sanctions List.
- The amended entry (QDi.439) relates to an individual, Hamidah Nabaggala, with updates to date of birth, passport number, and national identification number.
- The amendment was listed originally on 30 March 2026 and amended on 8 July 2026.
- Members are directed to refer to the updated UNSC Consolidated List and the ISIL (Da'esh) and Al-Qaida Sanctions List available on the UN Security Council's official website.
- The press release is available at https://press.un.org/en/2026/sc16407.doc.htm.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a procedural update under the existing Section 51A of UAPA, 1967 framework, requiring trading members to stay current with amendments to the UNSC ISIL (Da'esh) and Al-Qaida Sanctions List for purposes of client due diligence, KYC screening, and asset-freeze compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their sanctions screening databases/systems to reflect the amended entry (QDi.439 - Hamidah Nabaggala).
- Members should cross-reference client records against the updated UNSC Consolidated List and the ISIL (Da'esh) and Al-Qaida Sanctions List.
- Continued adherence to asset freeze, travel ban, and arms embargo provisions under Security Council resolution 2734 (2024) for any matched individuals/entities.

## Important Dates

- Original listing date of entry QDi.439: March 30, 2026
- Amendment date: July 8, 2026
- Circular issue date: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact and does not pertain to any listed company, stock, or trading activity. It is a routine regulatory/compliance pass-through communication requiring NSE members to update internal sanctions screening records as part of ongoing anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing (AML/CFT) obligations under UAPA, 1967. No operational disruption to trading or settlement is expected.
