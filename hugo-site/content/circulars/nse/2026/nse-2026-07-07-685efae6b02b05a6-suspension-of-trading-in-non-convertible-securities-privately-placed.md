## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in one privately placed Non-Convertible Security will be suspended effective July 8, 2026, on account of redemption. The affected security is issued by National Housing Bank (ISIN INE557F08FR8).

## Key Points

- NSE Circular Ref. No. 1096/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75072, dated July 07, 2026
- Issued by the Listing Department under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Trading in the security of National Housing Bank (ISIN: INE557F08FR8) will be suspended effective July 8, 2026
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the security

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification issued under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A governing suspension of trading in privately placed non-convertible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in the listed debt security (ISIN INE557F08FR8) of National Housing Bank must cease trading in the security from the suspension date and settle any outstanding positions in line with the redemption process.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 07, 2026
- Suspension effective date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific privately placed debt instrument of National Housing Bank. As the suspension is due to scheduled redemption rather than a credit or compliance event, it represents routine debt market operations with no broader market implications.
