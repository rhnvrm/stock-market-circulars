## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has declared Thursday, January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday in the Capital Market (CM) Segment. This holiday is on account of Municipal Corporation Election in Maharashtra. This circular partially modifies the earlier Exchange circular reference no. NSE/CMTR/71775.

## Key Points

- Trading holiday declared for Capital Market (CM) Segment only
- Date: Thursday, January 15, 2026
- Reason: Municipal Corporation Election in Maharashtra
- This is a partial modification to circular NSE/CMTR/71775
- Circular reference number: 07/2026
- Department: Capital Market Segment

## Regulatory Changes

This circular modifies the previously announced trading schedule by declaring January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday specifically for the Capital Market segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must take note of the trading holiday
- Members should adjust their trading schedules accordingly
- No trading activity will be conducted in the CM Segment on January 15, 2026

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday in Capital Market Segment
- **Circular Date**: January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This trading holiday will impact all trading activity in the Capital Market segment for the entire day. Market participants, brokers, and investors should plan their trading strategies accordingly. The holiday is localized to Maharashtra's electoral process but affects the entire NSE Capital Market segment operations. Other segments may continue operations as per their respective schedules unless separately notified.
