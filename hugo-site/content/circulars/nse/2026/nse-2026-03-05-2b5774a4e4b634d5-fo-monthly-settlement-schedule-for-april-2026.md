## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the Futures & Options (F&O) segment settlement schedule for April 2026 (Circular Ref. No: 30/2026). The schedule maps each trade date in April 2026 to its corresponding settlement date, with the final trade date of 30-Apr-2026 settling on 04-May-2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers all F&O segment trade dates for April 2026
- 20 trade dates are listed from 01-Apr-2026 to 30-Apr-2026
- Standard T+1 settlement cycle applies for most trade dates
- The last settlement of the month (30-Apr-2026 trade date) settles on 04-May-2026 due to weekend/holiday
- Notably, 03-Apr-2026, 05-Apr-2026, 11-Apr-2026, 12-Apr-2026, 14-Apr-2026, 18-Apr-2026, 19-Apr-2026, 25-Apr-2026, and 26-Apr-2026 are not listed as trade dates (weekends/holidays)
- 15-Apr-2026 (Sunday falls on Saturday) is included as a trade date, settling on 16-Apr-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule notification.

## Compliance Requirements

All F&O segment members must adhere to the published settlement schedule for April 2026. Members should plan their settlement obligations accordingly and ensure funds/securities are available on the respective settlement dates.
