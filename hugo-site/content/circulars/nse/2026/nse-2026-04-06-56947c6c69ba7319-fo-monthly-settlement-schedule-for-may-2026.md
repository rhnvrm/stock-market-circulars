## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the Futures & Options (F&O) segment settlement schedule for the month of May 2026. The circular (Ref. No: 040/2026) outlines 19 trade dates and their corresponding T+1 settlement dates, with the last trade date of May 29, 2026 settling on June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement follows a T+1 cycle for all trade dates in May 2026
- 19 trading sessions scheduled for May 2026 in the F&O segment
- May 28, 2026 is absent from the schedule, indicating a non-trading day
- The final settlement of the month (trade date May 29) rolls into June 1, 2026
- Circular issued by Anil Suvarna, Associate Vice President, NSE Clearing Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule circular.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members participating in the F&O segment must adhere to the prescribed settlement dates. Members should ensure adequate funds and securities are available on the respective settlement dates to meet their obligations.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational circular with medium impact on F&O segment participants. Members and clearing participants must align their back-office systems and fund management processes with the published schedule. The skip from May 27 settlement to May 29 (bypassing May 28) and the month-end settlement spilling into June 1 are notable operational considerations for fund managers and clearing members managing end-of-month positions.
