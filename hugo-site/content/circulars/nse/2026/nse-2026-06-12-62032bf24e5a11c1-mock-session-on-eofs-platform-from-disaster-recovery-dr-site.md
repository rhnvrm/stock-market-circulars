## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock bidding session for the eOFS (Electronic Offer for Sale) platform from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site on June 13, 2026. Members are encouraged to participate actively to test connectivity. No financial obligations arise from bids placed during this mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock bidding session will be conducted from NSE's DR site on June 13, 2026
- No configuration changes are required at member end; the same login URL applies: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Two test securities (TESTOFS1 and TESTOFS2) will be used for the mock session
- Switchover to DR site is scheduled between 11:00 hrs and 11:45 hrs
- Bids placed during the mock session carry no obligation in terms of funds or securities
- Any revised announcements will be published on www.nseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational drill to validate DR site readiness for the eOFS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to participate actively in the mock bidding session to verify connectivity
- No system-side changes are required at the member end
- For queries, members may contact the Toll Free number: 1800 266 0050 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 13, 2026**: Mock bidding session from DR site
  - Normal Market Open: 09:15 hrs
  - DR Switchover Window: 11:00 hrs – 11:45 hrs
  - Normal Market Close: 15:30 hrs

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a scheduled disaster recovery test for the eOFS platform involving two synthetic test securities (TESTOFS1 – non-retail category; TESTOFS2 – retail and employee categories). Floor price for both is Rs. 100 per share. All bids are simulated and attract no real financial settlement obligations. Members should treat this as a connectivity and readiness check only.
