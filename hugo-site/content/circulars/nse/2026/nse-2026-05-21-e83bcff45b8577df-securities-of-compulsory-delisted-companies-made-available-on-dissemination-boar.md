## Summary

NSE has notified that Varanium Cloud Limited (symbol: CLOUD) will be moved to the Dissemination Board (DB) of NSE with effect from May 22, 2026, following its compulsory delisting. The company was delisted under Regulation 32(1) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021, as notified in circular NSE/CML/73740 dated April 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Varanium Cloud Limited (CLOUD) has been compulsorily delisted from NSE.
- SEBI has directed NSE to move the company to the Dissemination Board (DB).
- Effective date for transfer to the Dissemination Board: May 22, 2026.
- Trading (buying and selling) in the company's shares will remain available on the DB of NSE.
- This circular is a continuation of NSE/CML/73740 dated April 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting was carried out under Regulation 32(1) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021, read with Section 21A of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Act, 1956, and the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules, 1957, along with the Bye-Laws and Regulations of NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to note that Varanium Cloud Limited (CLOUD) will no longer trade on the main board and will be moved to the Dissemination Board from May 22, 2026. Members should update their systems and inform clients accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **April 15, 2026**: Original delisting circular issued (NSE/CML/73740).
- **May 21, 2026**: This circular issued directing transfer to Dissemination Board.
- **May 22, 2026**: Effective date — Varanium Cloud Limited moved to NSE Dissemination Board.

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders of Varanium Cloud Limited will retain the ability to buy and sell shares on the NSE Dissemination Board, providing a limited exit mechanism post-delisting. However, liquidity will be significantly lower than on the main board. This circular has a moderate impact on existing shareholders and traders who hold or deal in CLOUD shares.
