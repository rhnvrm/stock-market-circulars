## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund (offered by Edelweiss Mutual Fund) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective April 09, 2026. Ten scheme variants — covering Direct and Regular plans with Growth, Monthly IDCW, and Quarterly IDCW options — are included.

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from **April 09, 2026**.
- Facility includes subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- Ten scheme variants are available across Direct and Regular plans.
- All schemes are classified under the **Equity** category.
- Issued under Circular Ref. No. 1315/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/NMFTM/73647).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling ongoing transaction access for a re-opened mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance obligations are imposed. Members using the NSE MF Invest Platform may now facilitate transactions in the listed Edelweiss fund schemes for their clients effective April 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 08, 2026**: Circular issued.
- **April 09, 2026**: Fund reopened for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This circular enables standard mutual fund transactions for existing and new investors in the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund through NSE's MF platform. The ten scheme variants provide flexibility across investment styles (Direct/Regular) and payout preferences (Growth, Monthly IDCW Payout/Reinvestment, Quarterly IDCW Payout/Reinvestment). The ISINs covered are: INF754K01WR0, INF754K01WS8, INF754K01WT6, INF754K01WZ3, INF754K01XA4, INF754K01WV2, INF754K01WW0, INF754K01WX8, INF754K01XC0, INF754K01XD8.
