## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 14 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These securities will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment with effect from May 13, 2026. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have had additional equity shares listed on NSE following ESOP allotments
- All newly listed shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies
- Market lot for all listed securities is 1 share
- No lock-in applicable on any of the newly listed ESOP shares
- Effective date for trading: May 13, 2026
- Securities are identified by designated NSE codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Circular is issued under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5), which govern the admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required for general market participants or members. Listing is effective automatically from May 13, 2026. Trading members must use the designated security codes when executing trades in these newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 13, 2026
- **ESOP Allotment Dates by Company:**

  - Brigade Enterprises Limited (BRIGADE): May 6, 2026 — 28,321 shares (ISIN: INE791I01019)
  - CARE Ratings Limited (CARERATING): May 4, 2026 — 300 shares (ISIN: INE752H01013)
  - Digitide Solutions Limited (DIGITIDE): May 5, 2026 — 99,056 shares (ISIN: INE0U4701011)
  - EPL Limited (EPL): April 22, 2026 — 2,500 shares (ISIN: INE255A01020)

  - India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited, Larsen & Toubro Limited, Marico Limited, MphasiS Limited, One 97 Communications Limited, Sai Life Sciences Limited, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Limited, Vardhman Polytex Limited: Details in annexure (content truncated in source)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. ESOP-allotted shares represent small incremental additions to the outstanding share capital of each company and are pari passu with existing shares, meaning no dilution in rights or entitlements. The share counts involved are modest relative to the total paid-up capital of these large and mid-cap companies. No price band changes or surveillance actions are associated with this circular. Investors and traders can trade these shares normally from May 13, 2026 using existing NSE symbols.
