## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued revised Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) and position limits for Participants, Institutional Clients, and Non-Institutional Clients under the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for the month of June 2026. This is a routine monthly update pursuant to NCL Circular No. NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Revised MWPL and position limits are effective for June 2026
- Limits cover securities and ETFs eligible under SLBS
- Four limit categories are specified per security: Market Wide Position Limit, Participant Limit, Institutional Client Limit, and Non-Institutional Client Limit
- Participant and Institutional Client limits are generally set at 10% of MWPL; Non-Institutional Client limits at 1% of MWPL
- The circular covers a broad universe of equity securities and ETFs listed on NSE
- Issued under authority of NCL Circular No. NCL/CMPT/73999 (April 30, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy changes are introduced. This circular implements the standard monthly revision of position limits as mandated under the existing SLBS framework established by the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73999.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians in the SLBS must adhere to the revised limits specified in Annexure I
- Participants must ensure their own positions, as well as those of their Institutional and Non-Institutional Clients, do not breach the applicable limits for June 2026
- SLBS participants should update their internal risk and position monitoring systems with the new limits before June 2026 begins

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Period:** June 2026
- **Reference Circular:** NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular that affects all participants in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme. The revised limits reflect updated share counts for the MWPL universe and must be operationally incorporated by SLBS participants. The non-institutional client limits (at approximately 1% of MWPL) remain the most restrictive tier. No market-wide disruption is anticipated; however, participants whose current SLBS positions approach revised limits must take note to avoid breaches. The circular covers 50+ securities and ETFs including large-caps such as ADANIPORTS, ADANIPOWER, ADANIENT, ABCAPITAL, and ABFRL, as well as several ETFs like ABSLBANETF and ALPHAETF.
