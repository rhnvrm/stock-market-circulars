## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No: 0551/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73551) informs all Exchange members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Powerica Limited on the National Stock Exchange of India. The specific listing date has not yet been confirmed and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Powerica Limited
- Trading Symbol: POWERICA
- ISIN: INE921L01032
- Listing Type: Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- Exact listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification circular ahead of the IPO listing of Powerica Limited on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All Exchange members are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of Powerica Limited equity shares and await the subsequent circular confirming the exact date of listing before executing any related trading activity.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 01, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact as it signals the imminent listing of Powerica Limited (POWERICA) on NSE following its IPO. Market participants should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date. The stock will be available for trading under symbol POWERICA with ISIN INE921L01032 once listed.
