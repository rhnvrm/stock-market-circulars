## Summary

NSE has notified, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, that a set of privately placed securities have been admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 06, 2026. The securities include fresh issues of commercial papers and other debt securities, as well as re-issues of previously listed commercial papers, from a range of corporate and financial institution issuers.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue commercial papers listed: NABARD CP 01/10/26 (ISIN INE261F14PX2), JFSL CP 01/10/26 Sr 12 by JM Financial Services Limited (ISIN INE012I14SS7), and KSL CP 31/07/26 Sr 118 by Kotak Securities Limited (ISIN INE028E14XH5)
- Fresh issue other debt securities listed: AAFS Mkt lkd 2032 CAR M by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited (ISIN INE0L6807351, market-linked coupon), NIIF 7.80% 2031 Sr PP3 by NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited (ISIN INE246R07913), and NIIF 7.95% 2036 Sr PP3 by NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited (ISIN INE246R07921)
- Re-issue commercial papers listed: GPL CP 23/09/26 Sr 46 and GPL CP 24/09/26 Sr 47 by Godrej Properties Limited (ISIN INE484J14E07 and INE484J14E15), and LTFL CP 10/06/27 by L&T Finance Limited (ISIN INE498L14GA5)
- Designated security codes for each instrument are as specified in the circular's annexure

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations rather than a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from members beyond awareness that these securities are now available for dealing on the Debt Market Segment under their respective designated security codes and ISINs.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 06, 2026
- NABARD CP: allotted 03-Jul-2026, redemption 01-Oct-2026
- JFSL CP Sr 12: allotted 03-Jul-2026, redemption 01-Oct-2026
- KSL CP Sr 118: allotted 06-Jul-2026, redemption 31-Jul-2026
- Alpha Alternatives (AAFS32A): allotted 01-Jul-2026, redemption 24-Feb-2032, call/put option date 27-Jan-2032
- NIIF 7.80% 2031 Sr PP3: allotted 02-Jul-2026, redemption 27-Nov-2031
- NIIF 7.95% 2036 Sr PP3: allotted 02-Jul-2026, redemption 29-May-2036
- Godrej Properties CP Sr 46: allotted 03-Jul-2026, redemption 23-Sep-2026
- Godrej Properties CP Sr 47: allotted 03-Jul-2026, redemption 24-Sep-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular is a routine operational disclosure of standard significance for debt market participants and members dealing in privately placed instruments. It does not affect equity trading, retail investors, or listed company operations, and primarily serves to formalize exchange-level admission and reference codes for institutional debt instruments.
