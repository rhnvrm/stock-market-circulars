## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 14 companies admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective June 05, 2026. All securities were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing shares, with no lock-in applicable.

## Key Points

- 14 companies' ESOP-allotted shares are being admitted to trading on NSE from June 05, 2026
- All securities carry a market lot of 1 and are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Each security is identified by its designated code for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a standard further-issue admission notice under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members. Trading members should note the new security codes and lot sizes as specified in the Annexure for the 14 companies. Securities must be traded using their designated codes only.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 05, 2026
- **Angel One ESOP Allotment Date:** May 27, 2026 (1,350,620 shares; ISIN INE732I01021; range 911998780–913349399)
- **Axis Bank ESOP Allotment Date:** June 02, 2026 (125,057 shares; ISIN INE238A01034; range 3109931194–3110056250)
- **Black Box ESOP Allotment Date:** May 26, 2026 (103,950 shares; ISIN INE676A01027; range 177495256–177599205)
- **ICICI Lombard ESOP Allotment Date:** June 02, 2026 (82,675 shares; ISIN INE765G01017; range 499024984–499107658)
- Allotment dates for remaining 10 companies (ICICI Prudential Life, Infosys, Jana Small Finance Bank, Kirloskar Oil Engines, L&T Finance, Global Health, Quick Heal Technologies, Ramco Systems, UNO Minda, WeWork India Management) are specified in the full Annexure

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP share listings representing incremental increases in the outstanding share count for each company. The volumes are small relative to each company's total float. No price adjustments, new series, or trading restrictions are introduced. Market participants can trade these shares from June 05, 2026 under the existing symbols and series (EQ) for each company.
