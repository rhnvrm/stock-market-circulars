## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has announced the discontinuation of SVCM Securities Private Limited (TM Code 90216) as the Market Maker for SHREEJI GLOBAL FMCG LTD (NSE symbol: SHETHJI), an SME-listed company. The change is effective April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- SVCM Securities Private Limited (TM Code 90216) is being discontinued as Market Maker for SHETHJI
- The outgoing market maker's last effective date is April 22, 2026
- SHREEJI GLOBAL FMCG LTD trades under the symbol SHETHJI on the NSE SME segment
- An updated list of active Market Makers for SME securities is available on the NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the market maker assignment for an SME security.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are advised to take note of the change in Market Maker assignment for SHETHJI. No specific action is required from members beyond updating their records.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** April 22, 2026 — SVCM Securities Private Limited ceases to function as Market Maker for SHETHJI

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the SME stock SHETHJI. The discontinuation of a market maker may temporarily reduce liquidity for this SME security until a new market maker is assigned. Members trading in SHETHJI should refer to the updated Market Makers list on the NSE website for the current mapping.
