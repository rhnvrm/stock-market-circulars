## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited under the symbol **AEPL** on the Capital Market segment (Rolling Settlement) effective March 12, 2026. The company is listed with 251,036,900 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each, fully paid up, with ISIN INE757T01025.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** AEPL
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE757T01025
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Re. 1/-
- **Total Securities:** 251,036,900 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 251,036,900
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** NA
- Trading will be in compulsory demat form for all investors
- Traded in the Normal Market segment under Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot sizes for securities

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in AEPL shares must be conducted using the designated security code and symbol
- Trading is mandatory in demat form for all investors
- Lot size of 1 share applies for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Date (Trading Commencement):** March 12, 2026
- **Shareholding Pattern Reference Date:** March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a new IPO listing of Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited, an electrical company based in Vasai East, Thane. The listing of 251 million shares at Re. 1 face value signals a relatively large share count for a small-cap company. Impact is limited to investors participating in the IPO and those tracking new listings in the electricals/infrastructure sector. Registrar services are handled by Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai.
