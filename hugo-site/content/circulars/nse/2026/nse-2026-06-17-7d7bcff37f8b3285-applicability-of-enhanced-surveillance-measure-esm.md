## Summary

NSE has invoked the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework adding 13 securities to ESM Stage I effective June 18, 2026. These securities will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions from June 19, 2026, and will be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective June 19, 2026. No securities are being moved from Stage I to Stage II in this update.

## Key Points

- 13 securities added to ESM Stage I effective June 18, 2026
- Minimum 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 18, 2026, and new positions from June 19, 2026
- All 13 securities (marked with *) shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) w.e.f. June 19, 2026
- Oil Country Tubular Limited (OILCOUNTUB) is included but not marked for EQ/SM to BE/ST shift
- Securities shifting to Stage II (none in this circular) would be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction from June 18, 2026
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ESM framework established by prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. The ESM framework is designed to identify and impose additional surveillance on securities showing unusual price or volume movements. Key changes include reclassification of settlement series from EQ/SM to BE/ST, mandating trade-for-trade settlement to prevent netting and reduce speculative activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in the listed securities as on June 18, 2026
- Members must collect minimum 100% margin on all new positions in these securities created from June 19, 2026
- Trading systems must reflect the series change from EQ/SM to BE/ST for 12 of the 13 securities effective June 19, 2026
- Members should inform their clients about the enhanced margin requirements and settlement changes
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 18, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusion effective; 100% margin applicable on existing open positions; Stage II price band (2%) under Periodic Call Auction effective (if applicable)
- **June 19, 2026**: 12 securities shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST); 100% margin applicable on all new positions

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of 13 securities under ESM Stage I carries significant trading restrictions. The mandatory 100% margin requirement substantially increases the capital required to hold positions, which will likely reduce liquidity and trading volumes in these scrips. The shift to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) settlement eliminates the ability to net off positions intraday, further constraining speculative activity. Investors holding these securities may face increased margin calls on existing positions as of June 18, 2026. The ESM designation typically signals elevated regulatory scrutiny due to unusual market behavior, and market participants should review their exposure to these securities.
