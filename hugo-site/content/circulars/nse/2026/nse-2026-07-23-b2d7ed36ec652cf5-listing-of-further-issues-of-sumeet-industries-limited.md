## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of Sumeet Industries Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 24, 2026. The listing pertains to 168,424,217 equity shares of face value Rs. 2 each allotted under a Rights Issue at an issue price of Rs. 11.86 per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Sumeet Industries Limited (Symbol: SUMEETINDS, Series: EQ)
- ISIN: INE235C01036 (shares currently credited under temporary ISIN pending activation)
- Face Value: Rs. 2 per share; Paid-up Value: Rs. 2 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 11.86 per share
- Security Description: Equity shares allotted under Rights Issue
- Date of Allotment: July 22, 2026
- Number of Securities: 168,424,217
- Distinctive Number Range: 526325681 to 694749897
- Market Lot: 1
- Pari Passu status: Yes
- Lock-in details: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

This is a notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, admitting the newly allotted rights issue shares to dealings on the Capital Market segment and specifying the applicable market lot.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must identify the security only by its designated codes and ensure trading occurs in the specified lot size (Market Lot: 1). No additional action is required beyond adherence to standard trading and settlement procedures for the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Sumeet Industries Limited following the rights issue, which may affect share liquidity and price dynamics as the newly allotted shares become available for trading. The temporary ISIN status until activation may cause minor settlement distinctions for these shares in the near term. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of SUMEETINDS rather than the broader market.
