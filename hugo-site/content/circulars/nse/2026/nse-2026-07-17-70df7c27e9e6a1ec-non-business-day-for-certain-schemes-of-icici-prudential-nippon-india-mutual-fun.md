## Summary

NSE has informed members that, based on communication received from ICICI Prudential and Nippon India Mutual Fund AMCs, July 20, 2026 has been designated as a non-business day for certain schemes of these two fund houses on the NSE MF Invest platform. During this non-business day, subscriptions, redemptions, switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs will not be permitted for the schemes listed in the circular's Annexure-I.

## Key Points

- Applies to specific schemes of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund and Nippon India Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform.
- Affected ICICI Prudential scheme: ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund (FOF), across Direct Plan and regular Growth/IDCW Payout/IDCW Reinvestment options.
- Affected Nippon India scheme: Nippon India Japan Equity Fund, across Direct Plan, Dividend Plan, and Growth Plan options (including Bonus and IDCW variants).
- Transaction types blocked on the non-business day: Subscriptions, Redemptions, Switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs.
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment department (Circular Ref. No. 1502/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/NMFTM/75250).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification passing through AMC-communicated non-business day information to NSE MF Invest platform members.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in the listed schemes must ensure no subscription, redemption, switch, SIP, STP, or SWP transactions are processed for the affected ISINs on the designated non-business day, and should inform relevant clients/systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Non-Business Day: July 20, 2026 (applicable to schemes listed in Annexure-I)
- Circular issued: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and operational in nature, affecting only mutual fund transaction processing for a small set of ICICI Prudential and Nippon India schemes on the NSE MF Invest platform for a single day. There is no impact on listed equity trading, broader market operations, or investor holdings beyond a one-day pause in transaction processing for the affected schemes.
