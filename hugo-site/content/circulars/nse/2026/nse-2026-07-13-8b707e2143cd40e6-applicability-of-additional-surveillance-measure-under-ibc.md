## Summary

NSE has issued its periodic update on securities under the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing proceedings under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), effective July 14, 2026. No new securities were added to Stage I or Stage II shortlists this cycle. One security, Sab Events & Governance Now Media Limited (SABEVENTS), has been excluded from the ASM (IBC) list. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ASM due to IBC-related proceedings has been provided, categorized by stage.

## Key Points

- No new securities shortlisted for ASM under IBC Stage I or Stage II effective July 14, 2026 (Annexure I shows 'Nil' for all new shortlisting categories)
- One security excluded from ASM (IBC) list: SABEVENTS (Sab Events & Governance Now Media Limited, ISIN INE860T01019)
- Consolidated list includes securities at Stage I (e.g., AGSTRA, CLCIND, GENSOL, IMPEXFERRO, RELINFRA) and those under 'Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure from the company' stage (e.g., ABAN, AKSHOPTFBR, ALCHEM, ANKITMETAL, ANSALAPI, ARSHIYA, ARTEDZ, ASTRON, BANSAL, BGRENERGY, BILVYAPAR, CMICABLES, and others)
- The ASM framework under IBC monitors securities of companies undergoing insolvency resolution proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No structural changes to the ASM-IBC framework; this is a routine periodic (fortnightly/scheduled) update reflecting movements in and out of the surveillance list based on IBC case status and corporate disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants dealing in the listed securities must comply with applicable ASM trading restrictions (such as higher margins, price bands) for securities remaining on the Stage I/II lists
- SABEVENTS will move out of ASM-specific trading restrictions effective July 14, 2026
- Depositories, brokers, and RTAs should update surveillance flags in their systems for the excluded security

## Important Dates

- Effective date: July 14, 2026 (for both shortlisting review and exclusion of SABEVENTS from ASM)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and security-specific. SABEVENTS shareholders and traders benefit from removal of ASM-related trading restrictions. The remaining consolidated list continues to signal elevated risk for stocks of companies under IBC proceedings, with trading in AGSTRA, GENSOL, RELINFRA, and other listed names subject to continued additional surveillance requirements including higher margins and potential price band restrictions.
