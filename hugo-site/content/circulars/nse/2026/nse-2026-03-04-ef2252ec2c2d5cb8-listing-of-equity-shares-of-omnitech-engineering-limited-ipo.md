## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73097, Circular Ref. No: 0365/2026) informing all exchange members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited via an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol **OMNI** with ISIN **INE0UH301010**. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited is being listed on NSE through an IPO.
- Trading symbol assigned: **OMNI**
- ISIN: **INE0UH301010**
- The actual listing date has not yet been announced and will be notified via a subsequent circular.
- Circular issued by the Listing Department of NSE on March 04, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification circular under NSE's Listing Department.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are informed to take note of the forthcoming listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited.
- Members should await the follow-up circular from NSE confirming the specific listing date before executing trades in the security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact as it signals the imminent market debut of Omnitech Engineering Limited. Investors who participated in the IPO should monitor NSE communications for the confirmed listing date. No trading is yet possible in the security until the listing date is formally announced. Market participants interested in this IPO scrip should be prepared for the upcoming listing event.
