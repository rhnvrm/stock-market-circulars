## Summary

NSE has announced that Teamtech Formwork Solutions Limited (Symbol: TEAMTECH) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (Series: ST) to the Rolling segment (Series: SM) effective June 10, 2026. This follows the company's SME IPO listing and is in accordance with SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- TEAMTECH will move from Trade for Trade series (ST) to Rolling segment series (SM) on June 10, 2026
- This transfer is pursuant to Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74393 dated May 25, 2026
- The action is in compliance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- The company was listed under the SME IPO framework

## Regulatory Changes

The segment migration is governed by SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012), which prescribes the framework for transitioning newly listed SME IPO securities from the trade-for-trade segment to normal rolling settlement after an initial period.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members should update their systems and be aware that from June 10, 2026, TEAMTECH will trade under Series SM (rolling settlement) instead of Series ST (trade for trade). Orders and positions should be managed accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Previous exchange circular NSE/CML/74393 issued
- **May 26, 2026**: This circular issued (NSE/CML/74401)
- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for transfer from Series ST to Series SM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural change with limited market impact. The transition from trade-for-trade to rolling settlement typically signals the end of the mandatory surveillance period for a newly listed SME IPO stock. Post-migration, TEAMTECH will be subject to normal rolling settlement (T+1), which may improve liquidity and reduce settlement risk for market participants trading in this scrip.
