## Summary

NSE has announced the transfer of Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (symbol: VISL) from the Trade for Trade segment (series: BE) to the Rolling segment (series: EQ), effective June 30, 2026. This follows Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74664 dated June 11, 2026 and is pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- VISL shares will move from the BE (Trade for Trade) series to the EQ (Rolling) series
- Effective date of transfer: June 30, 2026
- Action is pursuant to SEBI surveillance framework circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Follows earlier NSE circular NSE/CML/74664 dated June 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The reclassification from Trade for Trade (BE series) to Rolling segment (EQ series) means that VISL shares will no longer be subject to the mandatory delivery-based settlement restrictions of the Trade for Trade segment. Under the rolling segment, normal intraday trading and netting of positions will be permitted.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members are required to take note of the series change for VISL effective June 30, 2026 and update their systems accordingly. Orders placed in the BE series will not be valid post the transition date.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Prior NSE circular NSE/CML/74664 issued placing VISL in Trade for Trade
- **June 15, 2026**: This circular issued (NSE/CML/74701, Ref: 0958/2026)
- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date — VISL transferred from BE to EQ series

## Impact Assessment

The move from Trade for Trade (BE) to the Rolling segment (EQ) is generally positive for VISL's liquidity, as it removes the mandatory physical delivery constraint and allows normal intraday trading. This is a routine outcome of the NSE/SEBI surveillance review process, indicating the stock no longer warrants the additional restrictions of the Trade for Trade segment. Impact is moderate and primarily affects traders and market participants dealing in VISL shares.
