## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. The session covers two trading periods — Session 1 from the Primary Site and Session 2 from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site. No new software version is being released for this mock. Members are reminded of the upcoming mandatory migration to NEAT Adapter version 1.0.29 and a new CA certificate, both mandatory from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mock trading scheduled for Saturday, June 06, 2026 in the Capital Market segment
- Two sessions: Session 1 from Primary Site (morning) and Session 2 from DR Site (afternoon)
- No new version release accompanying this mock
- Members must use NEAT Adapter version 1.0.29 (Windows and Linux) with GR PORT 10831
- New CA certificate available by June 5, 2026 (EOD) on Extranet path `/common/Encryption_CA_Certificate/2026_Certificate`
- NNF (direct connection) users must download and use the new CA certificate
- Live Re-login window: 16:00 to 16:30

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced by this circular. The circular references NSE/MSD/74511 (June 02, 2026) regarding new CA certificate and NSE/MSD/74559 (June 05, 2026) regarding new NEAT Adapter EXE application.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use NEAT Adapter version 1.0.29 (Windows or Linux) with GR PORT 10831 during the mock session
- Members using direct connection via NNF users must download the new CA certificate from Extranet by June 5, 2026 (EOD)
- Members must ensure readiness of their migration plan by July 06, 2026 (mandatory go-live date)

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: New CA certificate available on Extranet (EOD)
- **June 06, 2026**: Mock trading session (Capital Market segment)
- **July 06, 2026**: Mandatory use of NEAT Adapter version 1.0.29 and new CA certificate goes live

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading exercise with low market impact. It primarily affects trading members who need to test their infrastructure using the updated NEAT Adapter and CA certificate. The session schedule provides ample time for members to validate connectivity from both Primary and DR sites. The mandatory deadline of July 06, 2026 for NEAT Adapter 1.0.29 and CA certificate migration requires members to plan and test their systems promptly.
