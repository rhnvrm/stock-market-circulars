## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has revised the cut-off timings for collateral addition and release in the Commodity Derivatives Segment, based on feedback received from members. This update follows the earlier circular ref. no. 0156/2026 (Download Ref No: NCL/COM/74020 dated April 30, 2026) that had established the previous timings. The revised timings for Cash and FDR release, along with pledge securities release batches, will take effect from July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Cash and FDR Addition cut-off remains unchanged at 10:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (Intraday) cut-off moved from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (End of Day) cut-off remains unchanged at 8:00 PM.
- Cash and FDR Release (Allocation) cut-off remains unchanged at 11:30 PM.
- Pledge Securities release timings are structured across 16 batches, from 8:30 AM (Batch 1) through 8:00 PM (Batch 16), with an End-of-Day (EOD) batch at 9:00 PM.
- Requests for release of re-pledged securities towards pay-in requirements must be made one day prior to the pay-in date, not on the pay-in date itself.

## Regulatory Changes

The only substantive change from the prior circular (0156/2026) is the extension of the Cash and FDR Intraday Release cut-off time from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM. All other cut-off timings, including Cash and FDR Addition, End of Day Release, Allocation Release, and the pledge securities release batch schedule, remain the same as previously notified.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and Professional Clearing Members (PCM) in the Commodity Derivatives Segment must align their internal collateral management processes with the revised intraday release cut-off time of 9:00 PM.
- Members must continue to submit release requests for re-pledged securities required for pay-in one day in advance of the pay-in date.
- Members should route queries to the collaterals operations desk at collaterals_ops@nsccl.co.in or via the toll-free IVR (1800 266 0050, Option 2).

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 22, 2026 — revised collateral timings come into force.
- Reference Circular: 0156/2026 (Download Ref No: NCL/COM/74020) dated April 30, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational update affecting back-office and treasury functions of clearing members, custodians, and PCMs dealing in commodity derivatives. The one-hour extension of the intraday cash/FDR release cut-off (8:00 PM to 9:00 PM) provides members additional flexibility for same-day collateral release, potentially easing liquidity management. There is no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or specific stocks; the circular is relevant primarily to clearing member operations and risk management workflows.
