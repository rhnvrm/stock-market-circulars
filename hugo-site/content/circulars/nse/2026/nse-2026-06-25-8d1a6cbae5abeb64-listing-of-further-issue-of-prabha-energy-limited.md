## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 88,97,316 partly paid equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 02, 2026. The shares carry a face value of Re. 1/- each with Re. 0.67/- paid-up and will trade under symbol PRABHAPP1 in the E1 series.

## Key Points

- 88,97,316 partly paid equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: PRABHAPP1, Series: E1
- Face value: Re. 1/- each; paid-up amount: Re. 0.67/- per share
- ISIN: IN90I0M01022 (permanent); temporary ISIN IN80I0M01032 used for initial demat credit
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 88,97,316

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Temporary ISIN usage is in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) governing ISIN activation for additional share issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code PRABHAPP1 and series E1 for trading these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- The permanent ISIN IN90I0M01022 will replace the temporary ISIN IN80I0M01032 upon activation

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** July 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of a further issue of partly paid shares for Prabha Energy Limited. Traders and members should note the new symbol PRABHAPP1 and the E1 series designation to distinguish these partly paid shares from any fully paid shares. The pari passu status and absence of lock-in restrictions are standard features for such listings.
