## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of equity shares of Algoquant Fintech Limited (Symbol: ALGOQUANT) with effect from January 06, 2026. The security will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment in compulsory demat mode for all investors. The company has issued 281,096,028 equity shares of Re. 1/- each, fully paid up, with ISIN INE598D01035.

## Key Points

- **Company Name**: Algoquant Fintech Limited
- **Symbol**: ALGOQUANT
- **ISIN**: INE598D01035
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Face Value**: Re. 1/-
- **Total Securities**: 281,096,028 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 281,096,028
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Lock-in**: NA
- **Trading Mode**: Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- **Demat Requirement**: Compulsory for all investors
- **Circular Reference**: NSE/CML/72145, Circular 0014/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (ALGOQUANT) for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- All transactions must be in demat form (compulsory demat applicable)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 02, 2026
- **Listing Date/Effective Date**: January 06, 2026
- **Date of Allotment**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine new listing that expands the universe of tradeable securities on NSE. No impact on existing listed securities or market operations.

**Investor Impact**: New investment opportunity available for market participants starting January 06, 2026. The compulsory demat requirement ensures standardized settlement procedures.
