## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session on Saturday, June 20, 2026, to test trading modalities related to the introduction of Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment. This follows the earlier circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026. The mock comprises three trading sessions, with Sessions 1 and 3 conducted from the DR site and Session 2 from the Primary site.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: Saturday, June 20, 2026
- Three trading sessions are scheduled across DR and Primary sites
- New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 will be introduced for CAS-related testing in Trading Session-1 only
- After Trading Session-1, members must reinstall and log in with earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8 for Sessions 2 and 3
- Sessions 2 and 3 will follow existing trading modalities without CAS-related changes
- Trades from Trading Session-1 (DR site) shall not be carried forward

## Regulatory Changes

This mock tests the implementation of Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, which includes:
- A dedicated CAS window with market order restriction period before CAS close
- Modified normal market close time for CAS securities
- CAS open, market order restriction start, and CAS close timings introduced alongside existing closing session timings

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of the Capital Market segment must participate in the mock trading session
- Members must install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 before Trading Session-1
- Members must reinstall NEATPlus version 7.8.8 after Trading Session-1 and before Trading Session-2 and Trading Session-3
- Members should test their systems against CAS-related changes during Session-1

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 20, 2026**: Mock trading day
  - **Session-1 (DR Site)**: 08:45 – 11:30 (includes CAS testing with NEATPlus 7.8.9)
  - **Session-2 (Primary Site)**: 13:30 – 15:45 (existing modalities, NEATPlus 7.8.8)
  - **Session-3 (DR Site)**: 16:30 – 17:35 (existing modalities, NEATPlus 7.8.8)
  - **Live Re-login Window**: 18:30 – 19:00

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational mock exercise with medium impact on NSE members in the Capital Market segment. Members need to manage two different NEATPlus versions during the day and plan system changeovers between sessions. The introduction of CAS-specific timings (CAS open at 10:30, market order restriction at 10:45, CAS close at 11:00 in Session-1) signals upcoming live changes to end-of-day trading mechanics. No direct market or investment impact; trades from Session-1 will not be settled. Members should treat this as a mandatory system readiness test ahead of the live CAS rollout.
