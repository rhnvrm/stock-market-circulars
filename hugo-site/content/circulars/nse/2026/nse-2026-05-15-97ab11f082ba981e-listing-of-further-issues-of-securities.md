## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares of **Dynamic Services & Security Limited** (Symbol: DYNAMIC) and **Visaman Global Sales Limited** on the Capital Market segment under **SME EMERGE**, effective **May 18, 2026**. The securities are admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, with designated lot sizes specified in the Annexure.

## Key Points

- Effective date for new listings: **May 18, 2026**
- Segment: **SME EMERGE** (Capital Market segment)
- Market lot size: **500 shares** for Dynamic Services & Security Limited
- ISIN for Dynamic Services & Security Limited: **INE0DZ701010**
- Series: **SM**
- Face Value: **Rs. 10/-**; Issue Price: **Rs. 60/-** per share
- All new securities rank **pari passu** with existing shares
- Multiple tranches of equity shares allotted via conversion of warrants on preferential/preference basis

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations:
- **Regulation 3.1.1**: Admission of securities to dealings
- **Regulation 2.5.5**: Lot size specification for admitted securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify securities **only by their designated codes**
- Trading must occur in the **specified lot size of 500 shares**
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per Annexure A details for each tranche

## Impact Assessment

**Dynamic Services & Security Limited (DYNAMIC):** Four tranches of equity shares arising from conversion of warrants (preferential/preference basis) are being listed. Combined, these tranches add approximately **5,308,112 shares** to the tradeable universe on SME EMERGE. The dual-tiered lock-in (Nov 2026 and Nov 2027) limits immediate free float impact.

**Visaman Global Sales Limited:** Listed in the circular title but details were not fully captured in the available content; traders should refer to the full PDF for complete Annexure data.

**Overall market impact is low**, as this is a routine SME segment listing action affecting niche, lower-liquidity securities. No broader market or compliance obligations are created for general trading members beyond adhering to the specified lot sizes and lock-in schedules.
