## Summary

NSE Surveillance has issued a periodic update under its measure for companies with high Promoter and non-Promoter encumbrance as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. This circular (Ref. No. 487/2026) is a continuation of NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. No new securities are being added this cycle; two securities are being removed; and six securities remain on the consolidated list subject to a minimum 75% margin requirement.

## Key Points

- **No new inclusions**: Annexure I lists nil securities for addition under the measure effective July 3, 2026.
- **Two securities excluded**: BEDMUTHA (Bedmutha Industries Limited, INE844K01012) and RKEC (RKEC Projects Limited, INE786W01010) are removed from the framework effective July 1, 2026.
- **Minimum 75% margin**: Securities remaining in the framework attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments on all open positions as on July 2, 2026, and new positions from July 3, 2026.
- **Periodic review**: The measure is subject to periodic review and operates alongside all other prevailing exchange measures.
- **Not adverse action**: Inclusion in this measure should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the list under the existing encumbrance-based surveillance measure framework established via NSE/SURV/51189 (January 31, 2022), which implements Reg. 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011. The framework targets companies where promoter and/or non-promoter shareholding is subject to high encumbrance levels.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must take note of the updated security lists and ensure minimum 75% margin is collected on positions in the six securities remaining in Annexure III.
- Margin requirement applies to **all open positions as on July 2, 2026** and **all new positions from July 3, 2026** in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **July 1, 2026**: BEDMUTHA and RKEC exit the framework (Annexure II).
- **July 2, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to the 75% margin requirement.
- **July 3, 2026**: Minimum 75% margin becomes effective for the consolidated list securities.

## Impact Assessment

The six securities remaining under this framework (Annexure III) continue to face elevated margin requirements of at least 75%, which increases the cost of holding or initiating positions and may reduce liquidity in these counters. The removal of BEDMUTHA and RKEC provides relief for traders in those securities. The consolidated list of six securities — AQYLON (INE416A01051), BLKASHYAP (INE350H01032), ELPROINTL (INE579B01039), JAYNECOIND (INE854B01010), STEELXIND (INE503B01021), and THYROCARE (INE594H01019) — remain subject to enhanced scrutiny due to high encumbrance levels. Market participants holding positions in these securities should plan their margin requirements accordingly ahead of July 3, 2026.
