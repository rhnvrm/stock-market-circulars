## Summary

NSE has issued its periodic update on the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. No new securities have been added to ESM Stage I, and no securities have been excluded from the framework this cycle. However, Amanta Healthcare Limited (AMANTA) has been shortlisted to move from ESM Stage I to ESM Stage II, effective July 10, 2026. The consolidated list of securities currently under the ESM framework includes multiple stocks such as Aarey Drugs & Pharmaceuticals, Accord Synergy, Aksh Optifibre, Amanta Healthcare, and Apsis Aerocom, among others.

## Key Points

- No new securities have been added to ESM Stage I under Annexure I.
- Amanta Healthcare Limited (AMANTA, ISIN: INE084K01015) has been shifted from ESM Stage I to ESM Stage II.
- No securities have moved from Stage II back to Stage I.
- No securities have been excluded from the ESM framework (Annexure II is nil).
- Securities qualifying under ESM will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) effective July 13, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, effective July 10, 2026.
- ESM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references prior ESM circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315) and updates the applicability list per the ongoing ESM framework. Amanta Healthcare Limited is reclassified from Stage I to Stage II of the ESM framework based on the exchange's surveillance criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in AMANTA as of July 10, 2026, and on new positions created from July 13, 2026 onward.
- Members must ensure trading in AMANTA is conducted under the Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) from July 13, 2026.
- Members must account for the 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction applicable to AMANTA from July 10, 2026.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Open positions in AMANTA as of this date subject to 100% margin; Stage II price band and Periodic Call Auction trading begins.
- July 13, 2026: New positions in AMANTA subject to 100% margin; AMANTA shifts to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST).

## Impact Assessment

The move of Amanta Healthcare Limited to ESM Stage II imposes stricter trading conditions, including a minimum 100% margin requirement and a shift to the Trade-for-Trade segment with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction. This is likely to reduce liquidity and increase the cost of trading in AMANTA shares. The absence of any new stocks entering or exiting the ESM framework this cycle limits the broader market impact, but market participants holding or trading AMANTA should adjust their positions and margin requirements accordingly ahead of the effective dates.
