## Summary

NSE has notified suspension of trading in the non-convertible securities (privately placed) of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspension takes effect on June 22, 2026, and is triggered by the upcoming redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited's NCD (ISIN: INE831R08027) will be suspended from trading on NSE with effect from June 22, 2026.
- The reason for suspension is redemption of the non-convertible security.
- The suspension is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- The circular is issued by the Listing Department, signed by Manager Vishakha Kine.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The suspension is a routine procedural action mandated under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs the suspension of trading in debt securities prior to or upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of NSE are notified to cease trading in the specified ISIN (INE831R08027) from June 22, 2026.
- No action is required by the company beyond the redemption process already in progress.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|

| Trading Suspension Effective | June 22, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to existing holders of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited's NCD (ISIN: INE831R08027). Post-suspension, holders will no longer be able to trade the security on NSE's debt market platform. The suspension ahead of redemption is a standard process ensuring orderly wind-down of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
