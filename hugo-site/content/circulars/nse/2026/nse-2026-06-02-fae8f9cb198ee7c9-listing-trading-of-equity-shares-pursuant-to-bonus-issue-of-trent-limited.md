## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of bonus equity shares of Trent Limited (Symbol: TRENT) on the Capital Market segment effective June 08, 2026. A total of 17,77,24,420 new shares will be admitted to dealings, allotted pursuant to the company's bonus issue.

## Key Points

- 17,77,24,420 bonus equity shares of Trent Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: TRENT | ISIN: INE849A01020
- Distinctive number range: 355488202 to 533212621
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade these bonus shares using the designated security codes only.
- No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Trent Limited will receive bonus shares as of the record date (June 04, 2026). The new shares become tradable on NSE from June 08, 2026. The addition of 17,77,24,420 shares will increase the free-float and total share count, potentially impacting per-share metrics. Impact is moderate and confined to Trent Limited shareholders and traders.
