## Summary

NSE has shortlisted 9 securities for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective June 19, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 22, 2026. No securities have been added to Stage II, and no inter-stage movements (Stage I to II or Stage II to I) have been announced in this circular.

## Key Points

- 9 securities included in ST-ASM Stage I effective June 19, 2026
- Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max capped at 100%), applicable from June 22, 2026
- Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max capped at 100%) — no new Stage II inclusions in this circular
- Margin applies to all open positions as on June 19, 2026 and new positions from June 22, 2026
- ST-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the ST-ASM framework, further to previous Exchange Circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (October 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (December 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (April 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (September 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; this circular applies existing ST-ASM provisions to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply the prescribed margin rates (50% minimum for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on positions in listed securities from June 22, 2026
- Margins apply to open positions as on June 19, 2026 and all new positions created from June 22, 2026
- Members should refer to FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for further guidance
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date for securities inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I; reference date for open positions
- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements on open positions (as on June 19) and new positions
