## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL), under its Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme department, has issued revised timings for collateral up-streaming, down-streaming, and allocation windows for July 3, 2026. The change is linked to SEBI's running account settlement circular from July 2022.

## Key Points

- Collateral up-streaming and down-streaming window for cash and fixed deposit receipts (FDR) will remain open until **8:00 pm** on July 3, 2026.
- Collateral allocation window will remain open until **10:00 pm** on July 3, 2026.
- NSE Clearing will endeavor to release Cash/FDR/Bank Guarantee (BG) within one hour of receiving a request from the clearing member, subject to available collateral post margin checks.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in reference to SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MIRSD/DOP/P/CIR/2022/101 dated July 27, 2022, concerning the Settlement of Running Account of Client's Funds lying with Trading Members. The extended timings are an operational adjustment under that regulatory framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members must note the revised collateral window timings applicable on July 3, 2026.
- Members should ensure collateral requests are submitted within the extended windows to benefit from same-day processing.
- Release of Cash/FDR/BG will be processed within one hour of request, contingent on sufficient collateral availability after margin checks.

## Important Dates

- **July 3, 2026**: Revised collateral timings are effective for this specific date.
  - Up-streaming/down-streaming window (Cash/FDR): open until 8:00 pm
  - Collateral allocation window: open until 10:00 pm

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational adjustment with medium impact on clearing members who manage collateral positions. The extended windows provide additional time for collateral management, which may ease settlement pressures associated with the running account settlement obligations under the referenced SEBI circular. No stock-specific or broad market impact is anticipated.
