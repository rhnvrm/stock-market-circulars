## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of NHPC Limited. The circular (NCL/CMPT/74496) references the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and confirms that settlement for OFS securities will follow existing rules for secondary market transactions.

## Key Points

- OFS order dates are June 2, 2026 (non-retail) and June 3, 2026 (retail and non-retail)
- Settlement dates are June 3, 2026 and June 4, 2026 respectively
- 100% upfront margin applies for H-type settlements; 0% for T-type settlements
- Custodial confirmation deadline for non-retail orders placed on June 2 is 03:30 PM on June 2, 2026
- Custodial confirmation for T-type non-retail orders is by 07:30 AM on June 3, 2026
- STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the OFS framework established under circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, applying standard secondary market settlement rules to the NHPC OFS.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the specified settlement calendar
- Funds pay-in must be completed by T+1 day, 11:00 AM
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur by T+1 day, 01:30 PM
- Applicable STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement day
- For queries, contact the NSE Clearing help desk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the NHPC OFS. Participants must align their back-office processes, margin arrangements, and custodial confirmation workflows with the specified timelines. Retail investors (RS category) are not subject to custodial confirmation requirements. The standard T+1 funds pay-in and pay-out schedule applies, consistent with secondary market norms.
