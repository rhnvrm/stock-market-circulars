## Summary

NSE has admitted Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited's privately placed debt securities for trading on the capital market segment effective February 04, 2026. The security is a 7.45% coupon bond maturing in January 2036, with trading symbol 745SDIP36 and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Security admitted: SDIP 7.45% 2036 bonds of Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited
- Trading symbol: 745SDIP36
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Series: N0
- ISIN Code: INE507T07195
- Coupon rate: 7.45% per annum
- Maturity date: January 30, 2036
- Effective date: February 04, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot size specification)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code 745SDIP36 for identification on the trading system
- All trades must be executed in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to recognize the new security from February 04, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular date**: February 03, 2026
- **Effective date**: February 04, 2026
- **Maturity date**: January 30, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The listing of a single privately placed debt security adds to the available fixed-income investment options but does not affect broader market operations or existing securities. Impact is limited to specific investors and trading members dealing in Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited's debt instruments.
