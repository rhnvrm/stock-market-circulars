## Summary

NSE has informed its members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of **Om Power Transmission Limited** on the National Stock Exchange of India. The company will trade under the symbol **OMPOWER** with ISIN **INE25E901019**. The exact listing date has not yet been specified and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Om Power Transmission Limited is set to list its equity shares on NSE via an IPO
- Trading symbol assigned: **OMPOWER**
- ISIN: **INE25E901019**
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/73758 | Circular Ref. No: 0632/2026
- The specific date of listing will be announced in a subsequent circular
- Issued by Aarti Parmar, Manager, Listing Department, NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard pre-listing notification informing Exchange members of an upcoming new security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing of OMPOWER
- Members should await the separate circular that will confirm the official listing date before executing any listing-day operations or settlement preparations

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 16, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing notification with moderate market impact. The listing of Om Power Transmission Limited will introduce a new equity security to NSE, providing investors an opportunity to trade shares post-IPO. Market participants, particularly those who subscribed to the IPO, should monitor NSE for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date to plan their trading activity accordingly.
