## Summary

NSE's Investigation Department (Circular Ref. No: 020/2026) has provisionally empaneled M/s Stockwiz Technologies LLP as an Algorithmic Trading Solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. The empanelment is effective immediately and covers both Whitebox and Blackbox algo types.

## Key Points

- M/s Stockwiz Technologies LLP has been provisionally empaneled with NSE to provide Algorithmic Trading Solutions
- The empanelment covers both Whitebox and Blackbox types of algorithms
- The vendor is headquartered at 3rd Floor, 979, Mehta Sonography Center Building, Jodhpur, Rajasthan - 342001
- Contact number: +91 9829837880
- The circular is effective with immediate effect from March 02, 2026
- The full updated list of empaneled Algo Providers is available on the NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update to the list of provisionally empaneled Algo Providers maintained by NSE under its algorithmic trading oversight program.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to use M/s Stockwiz Technologies LLP's algorithmic trading solutions may now do so under the provisional empanelment
- Trading members should refer to the updated NSE empaneled Algo Providers list on the NSE website for the complete and current roster of approved providers

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 02, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This empanelment expands the pool of NSE-approved algo solution providers by one vendor, giving trading members an additional option for deploying Whitebox and Blackbox algorithmic trading strategies. No existing workflows or compliance obligations for current market participants are affected.
