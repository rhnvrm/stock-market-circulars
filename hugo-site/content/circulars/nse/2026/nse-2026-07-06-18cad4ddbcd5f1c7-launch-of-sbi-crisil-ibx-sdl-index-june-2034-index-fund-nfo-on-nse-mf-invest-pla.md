## Summary

NSE has informed members that the SBI CRISIL - IBX SDL Index - June 2034 Index Fund NFO, under SBI Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 07, 2026 to July 14, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The scheme is a debt-oriented index fund tracking state development loans (SDL) maturing around June 2034, offered across six plan/option variants.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 1473/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/75049, dated July 06, 2026.
- NFO subscription window: July 07, 2026 to July 14, 2026 (till 11:59 pm) on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Six scheme variants offered: Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN INF200KB1BO8), Regular Plan-Growth (INF200KB1BL4), Direct Plan-IDCW Payout (INF200KB1BP5), Regular Plan-IDCW Reinvestment (INF200KB1BN0), Regular Plan-IDCW Payout (INF200KB1BM2), Direct Plan-IDCW Reinvestment (INF200KB1BQ3).
- All variants are categorized as Debt schemes with an allotment date of July 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new fund offer on the trading platform, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds, through any payment mode, are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 15, 2026 by 3:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO opens: July 07, 2026
- NFO closes: July 14, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Allotment date: July 15, 2026
- Clear funds deadline: July 15, 2026 (3:00 pm)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact and is primarily operational, informing NSE members and MF distributors of a new debt index fund NFO. It affects mutual fund transaction workflows on the NMF platform but does not impact equity trading, listed securities, or broader market structure.
