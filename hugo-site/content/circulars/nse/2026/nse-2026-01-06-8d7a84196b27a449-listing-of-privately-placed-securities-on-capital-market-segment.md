## Summary

National Stock Exchange has admitted Vedika Credit Capital Limited's privately placed debentures (VCCL 12.5% 2031) to trading on the capital market segment. The security will be available for trading from January 07, 2026, with symbol 125VCCL31 in N0 series and a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Company: Vedika Credit Capital Limited
- Security: 12.5% debentures maturing December 05, 2031
- Trading Symbol: 125VCCL31
- Series: N0
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE04HY08011
- Coupon Rate: 12.5% per annum
- Effective Date: January 07, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (125VCCL31) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- All trading regulations applicable to the capital market segment apply to this security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 06, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 07, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: December 05, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard private placement listing of a single debt security. The listing provides an additional trading instrument for debt market participants but does not affect broader market operations or existing securities. Only relevant to investors interested in corporate debt instruments with a 12.5% coupon rate and 5+ year maturity profile.
