## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for 13 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 12, 2026. The securities include equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes, conversion of warrants on a preferential basis, and other further issues. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Securities of 13 companies admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from June 12, 2026
- Axis Bank (AXISBANK): 194,943 equity shares (Rs. 2 each) allotted under ESOP on June 9, 2026; ISIN INE238A01034
- Balu Forge Industries (BALUFORGE): 20,60,000 equity shares (Rs. 10 each, issue price Rs. 360) issued via warrant conversion on preferential basis on March 6, 2026; ISIN INE011E01029; partial lock-in applies
- Dabur India (DABUR): 2,62,216 equity shares (Re. 1 each) allotted under ESOP on May 28, 2026; ISIN INE016A01026
- DCB Bank (DCBBANK): Further securities details in the original PDF
- Other companies include Diamines & Chemicals, Avenue Supermarts, Godrej Properties, Marico, Moschip Technologies, RBL Bank, Ramkrishna Forgings, Shadowfax Technologies, and Windsor Machines
- All securities are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes for trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for Balu Forge Industries shares: 5,60,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2026; 15,00,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2027

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 11, 2026
- Effective Date: June 12, 2026
- Axis Bank ESOP allotment date: June 9, 2026
- Balu Forge warrant conversion allotment date: March 6, 2026
- Dabur India ESOP allotment date: May 28, 2026
- Balu Forge lock-in expiry (560,000 shares): December 31, 2026
- Balu Forge lock-in expiry (1,500,000 shares): December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of further issues with minimal market impact. ESOP allotments represent small additions to the existing share capital of large-cap companies (Axis Bank, Dabur, etc.) and are unlikely to cause significant price movement. The Balu Forge warrant conversion of 20.6 lakh shares at Rs. 360 each is a relatively larger event for that mid-cap company but was a previously announced preferential allotment. Lock-in restrictions on Balu Forge shares limit near-term supply. Overall, this is a standard administrative notification with low market disruption.
