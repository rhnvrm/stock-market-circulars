## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Mehta Equities Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000175334) to surrender its membership in the Debt segment. The surrender was approved on March 30, 2026, and all NSE members have been notified to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Mehta Equities Limited has voluntarily surrendered its membership in the Debt segment of NSE
- SEBI Registration Number of the member: INZ000175334
- The Exchange has formally approved the surrender request
- Circular issued by NSE Compliance Department (Circular Ref. No: 29/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No broad regulatory changes. This is an administrative action specific to Mehta Equities Limited's membership status in the Debt segment. The member will no longer be permitted to trade in the Debt segment following the approved surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are required to take note of this surrender
- No action is required from other members beyond awareness of the change in membership status of Mehta Equities Limited in the Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date:** March 30, 2026
- **Circular Date:** April 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and localized. Mehta Equities Limited's clients trading in the Debt segment through this member would need to migrate to another broker. There is no broader market or systemic impact. This is a routine membership administration event with no effect on market operations or other trading members.
