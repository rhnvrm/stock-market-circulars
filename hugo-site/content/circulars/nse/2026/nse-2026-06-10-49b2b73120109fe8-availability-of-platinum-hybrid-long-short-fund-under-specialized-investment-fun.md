## Summary

NSE has informed its members that Mirae Mutual Fund's Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund, categorized under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund (Mirae Mutual Fund) reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 11, 2026
- SIP facility will also be available from the same date
- Fund falls under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category
- Six scheme variants available across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout/Reinvestment) options
- All six schemes are classified as Equity category

| Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout | INF769K30035 | Equity |
| Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | INF769K30027 | Equity |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is an operational notification about the availability of an existing fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members who offer mutual fund investment services via the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of these schemes for client transactions from June 11, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund schemes for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact. This is a routine platform availability update for a single mutual fund offering under the SIF framework. Investors and distributors using NSE MF Invest Platform will gain access to Mirae Mutual Fund's Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund variants from June 11, 2026. The SIF classification indicates this is a relatively specialized product. No changes to trading rules, settlement, or broader market operations.
