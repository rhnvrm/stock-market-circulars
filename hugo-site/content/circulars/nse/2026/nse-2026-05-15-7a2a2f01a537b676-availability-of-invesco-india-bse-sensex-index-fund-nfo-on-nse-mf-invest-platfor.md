## Summary

NSE has informed members that Invesco India BSE Sensex Index Fund NFO by Invesco Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting May 18, 2026. The platform will also support systematic investment options including SIP, STP, and SWP for both plan variants.

## Key Points

- Invesco India BSE Sensex Index Fund NFO becomes available on NSE MF Invest Platform effective **May 18, 2026**
- Both Direct Plan (Growth) and Regular Plan (Growth) are offered
- Facility covers ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular issued by NSE NMF TM Segment (Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74205, Circular Ref: 1371/2026)

## Scheme Details

| Sr. No | Scheme Name | ISIN | Category |
|--------|-------------|------|----------|
| 1 | Invesco India BSE Sensex Index Fund Direct Plan Growth | INF205KA1BM8 | Equity |
| 2 | Invesco India BSE Sensex Index Fund Regular Plan Growth | INF205KA1BL0 | Equity |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform availability notification enabling transactional access to an existing NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed to note the availability of these schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform and may facilitate client transactions accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low impact — this is a routine operational announcement enabling investors to transact in Invesco India BSE Sensex Index Fund (a passive equity index fund tracking the BSE Sensex) via the NSE MF Invest platform. It expands accessibility for retail and institutional investors seeking Sensex-linked passive exposure but carries no systemic market or compliance implications. Members with clients interested in BSE Sensex index funds should note the May 18, 2026 go-live date.
