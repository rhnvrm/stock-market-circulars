## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued updated formats for Bank Guarantees (BGs) to be furnished by Clearing Members towards margin deposits in the Futures and Options (F&O) segment. The circular provides revised annexure formats, including Annexure 1(A) - Format of Bank Guarantee for Margin Deposit (Fungible), to be used by banks approved by NCL when issuing guarantees on behalf of Clearing Members.

## Key Points

- The circular prescribes a standardized format for Bank Guarantees furnished for margin deposits in the F&O segment.
- The BG format applies to Clearing Members operating as sole proprietorships, partnership firms/LLPs, or companies incorporated under the Companies Act.
- The guarantee must be issued by a bank approved by NCL and is governed by the terms of a Master Agreement executed between the bank and NCL.
- The format specifies the guarantee amount, validity, and liability cap of the issuing bank.

## Regulatory Changes

NCL has updated the annexures (Annexure 1(A) and 1(B)) governing the format of Bank Guarantees accepted for margin deposits, replacing or revising prior formats used by Clearing Members and approved banks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing Members must ensure that Bank Guarantees submitted for margin deposits in the F&O segment conform to the revised formats specified in the annexures.
- Approved banks issuing guarantees on behalf of Clearing Members must use the updated format and reference the applicable Master Agreement with NCL.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/documentation update impacting Clearing Members and their banks with respect to margin deposit collateral formats in the F&O segment. It does not directly affect trading activity, listed securities, or market pricing, but non-compliance with the revised format could affect the acceptance of margin deposit guarantees by NCL.
