## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed) of Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited (ISIN: INE121A07QV5), effective March 12, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the instrument, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE121A07QV5 (Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited) will be suspended from March 12, 2026.
- The reason for suspension is redemption of the Non-Convertible Security.
- The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- This applies to all NSE members trading in the debt market segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is a standard operational action under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in ISIN INE121A07QV5 from March 12, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems and inform relevant clients about the suspension of this security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific Non-Convertible Security (INE121A07QV5) issued by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited. As this is a privately placed debt instrument undergoing redemption, the suspension is routine and expected. No broader market or equity segment impact is anticipated.
