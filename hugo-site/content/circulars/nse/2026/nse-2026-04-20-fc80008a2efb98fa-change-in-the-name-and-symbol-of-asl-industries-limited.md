## Summary

NSE has announced that ASL Industries Limited will change its name to AVATAR Industries Limited and its trading symbol from ASLIND to AVATAR, effective April 24, 2026. All members of the Exchange are required to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: ASL Industries Limited
- New company name: AVATAR Industries Limited
- Existing trading symbol: ASLIND
- New trading symbol: AVATAR
- Change is effective from April 24, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department, Ref No: NSE/CML/73804, Circular Ref. No: 0648/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The name and symbol change is a listing-level update on the National Stock Exchange. The company will no longer be identifiable under its previous name or symbol post the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must update their systems, records, and client communications to reflect the new name (AVATAR Industries Limited) and symbol (AVATAR) from April 24, 2026.
- Brokers and trading members should ensure their order management and back-office systems are updated prior to the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **April 20, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **April 24, 2026**: Effective date for name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

The renaming from ASL Industries Limited (ASLIND) to AVATAR Industries Limited (AVATAR) is primarily an administrative and branding change. Market participants, algorithmic trading systems, data vendors, and investor portals will need to update references. There is no direct financial or operational impact on the underlying business, but confusion may arise during the transition period if systems are not updated promptly.
