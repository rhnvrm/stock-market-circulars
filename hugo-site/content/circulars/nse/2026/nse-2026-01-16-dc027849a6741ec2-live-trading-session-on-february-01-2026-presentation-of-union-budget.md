## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India will conduct a live trading session on Sunday, February 01, 2026, coinciding with the presentation of the Union Budget. The session will follow standard market timings with pre-open, normal market hours, and trade modification periods.

## Key Points

- Live trading session scheduled for Sunday, February 01, 2026
- Session conducted on account of Union Budget presentation
- Standard market timings will be followed
- Pre-open session: 09:00 hrs to 09:08 hrs with random closure in last one minute
- Normal market hours: 09:15 hrs to 15:30 hrs
- Trade modification end time: 16:15 hrs
- Circular issued by Futures & Options Department

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time special trading session arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must note the special trading session on Sunday, February 01, 2026
- Members should ensure systems and personnel are available for the Sunday trading session
- Standard trading protocols and timings apply

## Important Dates

- **February 01, 2026 (Sunday)**: Live trading session
  - Pre-Open: 09:00 hrs - 09:08 hrs
  - Normal Market: 09:15 hrs - 15:30 hrs
  - Trade Modification End: 16:15 hrs

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This is an unusual Sunday trading session that will allow market participants to react to the Union Budget announcements in real-time. All trading members need to ensure operational readiness for weekend trading.

**Operational Impact**: High - Trading on a Sunday requires special arrangements for staffing, systems, and infrastructure. Members must coordinate with their teams to ensure availability and readiness for the special session.

**Participant Impact**: All futures and options market participants are affected as this provides an opportunity to trade immediately following the Budget presentation, potentially leading to increased volatility and trading volumes.
