## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of 14,08,604 additional equity shares of AvenuesAI Limited (trading symbol: CCAVENUE) on the Capital Market segment, effective March 06, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE483S01020.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AvenuesAI Limited
- **Symbol:** CCAVENUE
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE483S01020 (permanent); temporary ISIN IN8483S01029 was used for dematerialized credits pending activation
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** ₹1/- per share
- **Number of shares listed:** 14,08,604
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes — new shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- **Security Description:** Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is executed under:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission of securities to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) governing ISIN activation for additional share issues

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol **CCAVENUE** and security codes for all trades in these shares.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of **1 share**.
- The permanent ISIN INE483S01020 replaces the temporary ISIN IN8483S01029 for dematerialized holdings upon activation.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The addition of 14,08,604 shares represents a modest increase to the existing float of CCAVENUE. No new funds are raised; this is purely a conversion event. The shares are pari passu with existing equity, so no dilution differential applies. Market members should update their systems to recognise the newly listed distinctive number ranges from March 06, 2026.
