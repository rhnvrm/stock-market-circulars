## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of 9 new government securities — 3 Treasury Bills (T-bills) and 6 State Development Loans (SDLs) — for trading on the Capital Market segment effective June 19, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. All securities carry a lot size of 100.

## Key Points

- 3 Government of India Treasury Bills admitted: 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities
- 6 State Development Loans admitted from Gujarat, Rajasthan, Assam, Punjab, Andhra Pradesh, and Uttarakhand
- All securities have a uniform lot size of 100
- Effective date of trading: June 19, 2026
- Securities to be identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular is issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use only the designated security codes for trading these instruments, and must adhere to the specified lot size of 100 for each security.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 19, 2026
- **T-bill Maturities:** September 17, 2026 (91D) | December 17, 2026 (182D) | June 17, 2027 (364D)
- **SDL Maturities:** June 17, 2032 (AP) | June 17, 2033 (PN) | June 17, 2034 (GJ) | June 17, 2035 (RJ, UK) | June 17, 2036 (AS)
