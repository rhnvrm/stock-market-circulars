## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the Mark to Market (MTM) settlement schedule for the Currency Derivatives Segment (CDS) for the month of July 2026. The schedule covers all 23 trading days in July 2026, with settlements occurring on the next trading day.

## Key Points

- Settlement for each trade date is the immediately following trading day (T+1).
- Three special final settlement dates apply for specific contract types at month-end.
- Final settlement for July 2026 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures is on 30-Jul-2026.
- Final settlement for July 2026 Cash Settled Interest Rate Contracts, Currency Futures, and Options Contracts is on 31-Jul-2026.
- Final settlement for July 2026 Overnight Call Rate (MIBOR) Contracts is on 03-Aug-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Currency Derivatives Segment are required to adhere to the published settlement schedule for all Mark to Market settlements in July 2026.

## Important Dates

- First trade date: 01-Jul-2026 (settlement: 02-Jul-2026)
- Last trade date: 31-Jul-2026 (settlement: 03-Aug-2026)
- Final settlement — 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures: 30-Jul-2026
- Final settlement — Cash Settled Interest Rate & Currency Futures/Options: 31-Jul-2026
- Final settlement — MIBOR Contracts: 03-Aug-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is a routine monthly schedule publication. Currency Derivatives Segment members should update their back-office and settlement systems with the July 2026 schedule. The MIBOR final settlement date of 03-Aug-2026 extends into August and may require attention for month-end reconciliation purposes.
