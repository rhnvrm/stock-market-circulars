## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index Fund NFO of Groww Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants of Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index Fund will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform from June 19, 2026.
- All six schemes are categorised as Equity funds.
- Both Direct and Regular plan options are available, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment sub-options.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available from the effective date.
- Circular Ref. No: 1437/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74762.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling trading and investment in the fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the fund's availability on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited and routine impact. It expands the fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform, providing NSE members and their clients an additional channel to invest in the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index Fund. The six ISIN-mapped scheme variants (INF666M01PA2 through INF666M01PF1) cover all standard plan/option combinations, ensuring broad accessibility for investors through the NSE MF route.
