## Summary

NSE has notified the inclusion of four securities under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) Stage-I framework effective June 4, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from June 8, 2026 on all open positions as of June 5, 2026 and all new positions created thereafter. No securities have been shortlisted for Stage-IV, nor any movements between higher stages.

## Key Points

- Four securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I effective June 4, 2026
- Three securities (CPPLUS, OWAIS, SASKEN) moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM)
- One security (NAVKARURB) included as it satisfies criteria at BSE
- 100% margin applicable w.e.f. June 8, 2026 on open positions as on June 5, 2026 and new positions from June 8, 2026
- No securities shortlisted under Stage-IV (Trade-for-Trade shift)
- No inter-stage movements (Stage-I to II, Stage-II to III, or Stage-I to IV) in this cycle
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

Inclusion under Long-Term ASM Stage-I is based on the criteria defined in Exchange Circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. The shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute an adverse finding against the concerned companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply 100% margin on open positions in the listed securities as on June 5, 2026
- 100% margin must be collected on all new positions in these securities from June 8, 2026 onwards
- Members should refer to NSE FAQs on ASM for further guidance: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 3, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 4, 2026**: Securities included in Long-Term ASM Stage-I framework effective date
- **June 5, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 8, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes effective on all open and new positions

**Securities Included in LTASM Stage-I:**

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN | Note |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|------|
| 1 | CPPLUS | Aditya Infotech Limited | INE819V01029 | Moved from STASM |

| 3 | OWAIS | Owais Metal And Mineral Processing Limited | INE0R8M01017 | Moved from STASM |
| 4 | SASKEN | Sasken Technologies Limited | INE231F01020 | Moved from STASM |

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or initiating positions in these securities, reducing leverage for traders and potentially suppressing trading volumes. Existing holders as of June 5, 2026 must ensure adequate margin is available by June 8, 2026 or risk forced liquidation of positions. Institutional and retail investors should review their exposure to these four securities ahead of the effective date.
