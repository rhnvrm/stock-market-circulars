## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No. 0835/2026, Download Ref. No. NSE/CML/74366) notifies the admission to dealings of further issued equity shares for 15 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective May 25, 2026. The securities were allotted under various schemes including preferential issues, warrant conversions, and ESOPs.

## Key Points

- Effective date for trading: May 25, 2026
- Issued under NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations, Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5
- Securities covered include allotments via ESOP, preferential issue, and conversion of warrants
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies
- Market lot for all securities: 1
- Certain preferential allotments carry lock-in restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes specified in the Annexure for all trades in these securities
- Trades must be executed in the specified lot sizes
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for applicable allotments (ADVAIT lock-in until Jan 31, 2027; BALUFORGE lock-in until Nov 30, 2026)

## Important Dates

| Company | Allotment Date | Effective Trading Date | Lock-in Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

**ADVAIT (Advait Energy Transitions Limited):** 5,630 equity shares (ISIN: INE0ALI01010, face value Rs. 10) allotted at Rs. 1,776 via warrant conversion under preferential issue. Negligible dilution; lock-in until Jan 31, 2027 limits near-term supply.

**ALIVUS (Alivus Life Sciences Limited):** 3,100 equity shares (ISIN: INE03Q201024, face value Rs. 2) allotted under ESOP. Very small quantum; no lock-in.

**BALUFORGE (Balu Forge Industries Limited):** Two tranches totalling 2,630,000 equity shares (ISIN: INE011E01029, face value Rs. 10) allotted at Rs. 360 under preferential issue. Larger quantum but subject to lock-in until Nov 30, 2026, limiting immediate float impact.

**FEDERALBNK (The Federal Bank Limited):** 215,417 equity shares (ISIN: INE171A01029, face value Rs. 2) allotted under ESOP. Modest addition relative to Federal Bank's large outstanding share capital; no lock-in.

Overall market impact is minimal. These are routine further-issue listings with no changes to business fundamentals. The full list of 15 companies in the subject line (including ICICI Bank, SBI Life, Tata Elxsi, etc.) is partially detailed in the Annexure excerpt provided.
