## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Baroda BNP Paribas Service Fund NFO, launched under Baroda Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 14, 2026 to July 28, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular lists six scheme-plan combinations (Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) along with their respective ISINs and allotment date.

## Key Points

- Baroda BNP Paribas Service Fund is an equity category scheme under Baroda Mutual Fund.
- NFO subscription window on NSE MF Invest Platform: July 14, 2026 to July 28, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).
- Six scheme-plan variants offered:
  - Regular Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF251K01VV1)
  - Regular Plan – IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF251K01VW9)
  - Regular Plan – IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF251K01VX7)
  - Direct Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF251K01VY5)
  - Direct Plan – IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF251K01VU3)
  - Direct Plan – IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF251K01VZ2)
- Common allotment date for all schemes: August 3, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new fund offer on the trading platform, not a change to regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 31, 2026, till 03:00 pm, to facilitate settlement of NFO subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription period: July 14, 2026 to July 28, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Clear funds deadline: July 31, 2026, till 03:00 pm
- Allotment date: August 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The circular has limited direct market impact and is primarily operational, informing NSE members and mutual fund distributors of a new scheme becoming available for transaction. It affects mutual fund distributors, members handling MF transactions, and investors interested in subscribing to this equity-category NFO, but does not affect listed equity securities or broader trading operations.
