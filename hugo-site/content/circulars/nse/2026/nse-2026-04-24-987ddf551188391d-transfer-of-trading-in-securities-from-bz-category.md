## Summary

NSE has announced the transfer of trading in securities of Supreme Infrastructure India Limited (symbol: SUPREMEINF) from the 'BZ' series to the 'EQ' series, effective May 05, 2026. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, originally issued on July 11, 2023 and last updated on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Supreme Infrastructure India Limited (SUPREMEINF) will be moved from the 'BZ' trading series to the 'EQ' trading series.
- The transfer is effective from May 05, 2026.
- The action is pursuant to SEBI's Master Circular on surveillance and trading category classifications.
- The circular is addressed to all NSE members.

## Regulatory Changes

The reclassification is carried out under the framework of SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026. The 'BZ' series typically denotes securities under a restricted or surveillance category (companies that have not complied with listing requirements), while 'EQ' is the standard equity trading series, indicating the company has met the necessary compliance conditions for reinstatement to normal trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are informed to update their systems and processes to reflect the series change for SUPREMEINF from May 05, 2026.
- No specific action is required from retail investors, but brokers and trading members should ensure accurate categorization in their platforms.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 24, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 05, 2026 — SUPREMEINF transfers from BZ to EQ series.

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from 'BZ' to 'EQ' is generally a positive development for Supreme Infrastructure India Limited, as it indicates the company has addressed prior listing non-compliances that led to its placement in the BZ category. Investors and traders in SUPREMEINF will benefit from improved liquidity and normal trading conditions associated with the EQ series. The impact is limited to holders and prospective traders of this specific stock.
