## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,15,36,824 equity shares of Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited (Symbol: SOLARA) on the Capital Market segment, effective May 29, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares, and will trade under the permanent ISIN INE624Z01016 replacing the temporary ISIN IN8624Z01015.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** SOLARA
- **Company:** Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited
- **Series:** EQ
- **Permanent ISIN:** INE624Z01016 (replacing temporary ISIN IN8624Z01015)
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/-
- **Nature of Issue:** Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- **Number of Shares Listed:** 1,15,36,824
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- Distinctive numbers span two ranges: 36005268–46056074 (1,00,50,807 shares) and 46066743–47552759 (14,86,017 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, governing admission of securities to dealings and lot sizes respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shares previously credited under temporary ISIN IN8624Z01015 (in dematerialized mode) will transition to permanent ISIN INE624Z01016 per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012).
- Members and depositories should ensure the ISIN activation and transfer is reflected correctly in client demat accounts.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. Existing holders of partly paid-up shares of SOLARA will see their holdings converted to fully paid-up shares tradeable under the permanent ISIN. The addition of ~1.15 crore shares to the tradeable float may have a marginal dilutive effect, but as these shares were already partly paid and tracked under a temporary ISIN, the market impact is expected to be negligible.
