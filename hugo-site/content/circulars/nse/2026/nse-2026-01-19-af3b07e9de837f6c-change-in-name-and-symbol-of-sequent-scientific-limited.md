## Summary

NSE has announced that Sequent Scientific Limited will undergo a corporate name and trading symbol change effective January 23, 2026. The company will be renamed to Viyash Scientific Limited and its trading symbol will change from SEQUENT to VIYASH.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from Sequent Scientific Limited to Viyash Scientific Limited
- Trading symbol changing from SEQUENT to VIYASH
- Change effective from January 23, 2026
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving rebranding of an existing listed company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Trading platforms and market data systems need to be updated before the effective date
- Investors should note the new symbol for trading and tracking purposes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name and symbol change will require updates across trading systems, demat accounts, and portfolio tracking systems. Existing shareholders are not affected in terms of holdings, but need to be aware of the new identity for future transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Brokers and market participants must ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new symbol and name to avoid confusion or trading errors. Historical data and charts will need to maintain continuity under the new symbol.
