## Summary

NSE has announced the applicability of the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) effective May 22, 2026. Five securities have been newly shortlisted for ESM Stage I. No securities are moving between Stage I and Stage II, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework this cycle.

## Key Points

- 5 new securities added to ESM Stage I effective May 22, 2026
- Securities marked with `*` will migrate from EQ/SM series to BE/ST series effective May 25, 2026
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage I
- No securities excluded from the ESM framework
- Consolidated ESM list contains 25 securities in Stage II and multiple securities in Stage I

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities are newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I effective May 22, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|
| 1 | FMNL | Future Market Networks Limited* | INE360L01017 |
| 2 | GLOTTIS | Glottis Limited* | INE0TQE01026 |
| 3 | NEPHROCARE | Nephro Care India Limited* | INE0SUN01013 |
| 4 | NOIDATOLL | Noida Toll Bridge Company Limited* | INE781B01015 |
| 5 | UNITEDPOLY | United Polyfab Gujarat Limited* | INE368U01029 |

*\* EQ/SM series to be migrated to BE/ST series effective May 25, 2026*

**Stage I to Stage II movements:** Nil

**Stage II to Stage I movements:** Nil

**Exclusions from ESM:** Nil

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ESM trading conditions for all shortlisted securities
- Newly added securities (FMNL, GLOTTIS, NEPHROCARE, NOIDATOLL, UNITEDPOLY) will be subject to ESM Stage I restrictions including trade-for-trade settlement
- Series migration from EQ/SM to BE/ST for the 5 new securities must be factored into order management systems by May 25, 2026

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026:** ESM applicability effective date for newly shortlisted Stage I securities
- **May 25, 2026:** Migration of newly added securities from EQ/SM series to BE/ST series

## Impact Assessment

The 5 newly added securities will be subject to Enhanced Surveillance Measure Stage I restrictions, which typically include trade-for-trade settlement (no netting), higher margins, and periodic review. The series migration to BE/ST on May 25, 2026 will further restrict intraday trading and require upfront delivery of securities. The consolidated ESM list includes 25 securities in Stage II (stricter restrictions) and a larger number in Stage I, covering a broad set of small and mid-cap companies. Investors and trading members holding or transacting in these securities should review their risk exposure and settlement obligations accordingly.
