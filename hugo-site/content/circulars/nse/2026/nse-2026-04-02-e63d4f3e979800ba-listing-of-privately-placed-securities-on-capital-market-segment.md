## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 8 privately placed debt securities on the Capital Market segment effective April 06, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The securities span multiple issuers including NBFC, housing finance, real estate, and InvIT sectors, with lot sizes of 1 unit each under Series N0.

## Key Points

- 8 new debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from April 06, 2026
- All securities listed under Series N0 with a market lot of 1
- Issuers include Dar Credit & Capital, Alpha Alternatives Financial Services, PNB Housing Finance, Aditya Birla Capital, Lodha Developers, SMFG India Home Finance, and NDR InvIT Trust
- Coupon structures vary: fixed rate (12.75%, 7.7173%, 8.52%), market-linked, and reset rate instruments
- Maturities range from September 2028 to July 2031
- Securities must be identified solely by their designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular is issued pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only the designated security codes when trading these instruments
- All trades must be executed in the specified lot sizes (1 unit per lot for all listed securities)
- Members are advised to update their systems with the new ISIN codes and symbols before April 06, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 02, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 06, 2026
