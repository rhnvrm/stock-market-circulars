## Summary

NSE has placed Lloyds Engineering Works Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENGG, ISIN: INE093R01011) under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Framework at Stage-I, effective June 19, 2026. A 100% margin rate will be applicable from June 23, 2026 on all open positions as of June 22, 2026 and all new positions created thereafter. No securities were moved to Stage-IV in this circular, so no Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) shifts apply this time.

## Key Points

- LLOYDSENGG (Lloyds Engineering Works Limited, ISIN: INE093R01011) added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I effective June 19, 2026
- 100% margin rate applicable from June 23, 2026 on all open and new positions
- No securities shortlisted for Stage-II, Stage-III, or Stage-IV transitions in this circular
- No securities shifted to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) segment in this instance
- ASM framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and is not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the Long-Term ASM framework established via prior circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. The ASM framework provides for graduated surveillance stages with increasing restrictions. Stage-IV securities qualifying under Criteria VII would additionally be shifted from Rolling Settlement (EQ series) to Trade-for-Trade (BE series), though no such shift is triggered in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must ensure 100% margin collection on LLOYDSENGG positions from June 23, 2026
- Members must apply the margin requirement to all open positions held as of June 22, 2026 and all new positions from June 23, 2026 onwards
- Members should inform clients holding or intending to take positions in LLOYDSENGG about the enhanced margin requirement
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 19, 2026**: LLOYDSENGG inclusion in Long-Term ASM Stage-I effective date
- **June 22, 2026**: Last date for open positions before 100% margin kicks in
- **June 23, 2026**: 100% margin rate applicable on all open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of LLOYDSENGG in Long-Term ASM Stage-I with a 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or initiating positions in the stock. Traders will need to post full value as margin, effectively eliminating leverage and making the stock less attractive for speculative trading. This typically leads to reduced volumes, increased volatility, and potential price correction as leveraged positions are unwound. The ASM action signals that the stock has satisfied NSE's surveillance criteria — likely related to price movement, volume, or other market integrity parameters. Investors with existing positions should factor in the higher capital requirement, and those on leverage must arrange additional funds by June 22, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation.
