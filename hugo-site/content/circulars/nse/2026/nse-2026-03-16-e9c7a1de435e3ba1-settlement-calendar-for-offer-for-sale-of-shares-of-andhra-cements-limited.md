## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Andhra Cements Limited. This circular, referencing the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025, specifies that OFS settlements will follow existing secondary market transaction rules.

## Key Points

- Settlement for OFS of Andhra Cements Limited shares follows secondary market transaction rules as per circular NCL/CMPT/67751 (April 29, 2025).
- Two settlement numbers apply: 2026052 (non-retail/IS orders on March 17, 2026) and 2026053 (retail/RS and non-retail/IS orders on March 18, 2026).
- Non-retail (IS) investors face 100% margin requirement with custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM; a 0% margin category is also available with next-day morning confirmation.
- Retail (RS) investors in settlement 2026053 have 100% margin with no custodial confirmation required.
- STT and stamp duty will be collected on the respective settlement day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the existing OFS settlement framework established under circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must adhere to the specified settlement calendar for Andhra Cements Limited OFS transactions.
- Funds pay-in must be completed by T+1 day at 11:00 AM.
- Securities and funds pay-out will occur by T+1 day at 1:30 PM.
- Applicable STT and stamp duty to be paid on the respective settlement date.
- For queries, contact the help desk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **March 17, 2026**: Order date for Settlement No. 2026052 (non-retail/IS category); custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM; settlement date March 18, 2026.
- **March 18, 2026**: Order date for Settlement No. 2026053 (retail RS and non-retail IS); settlement date March 20, 2026.
- **March 20, 2026**: Final settlement date for Settlement No. 2026053.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to members, custodians, and PCMs (Professional Clearing Members) participating in the Andhra Cements Limited OFS. Retail investors using the RS series have a simplified process with no custodial confirmation required. Non-retail investors must ensure timely custodial confirmations (3:30 PM on order date for T+1 settlement) or utilize the 0% margin route with next-day morning confirmation. The T+1 settlement timeline aligns with standard secondary market practices, minimizing operational disruption.
