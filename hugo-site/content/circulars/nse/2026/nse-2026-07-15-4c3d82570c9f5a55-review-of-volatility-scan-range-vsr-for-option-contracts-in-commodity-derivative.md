## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a circular reviewing the Volatility Scan Range (VSR) for option contracts in the Commodity Derivatives Segment, applicable for the month of August 2026. This is a routine monthly update issued under the framework prescribed by SEBI circular SEBI/HO/CDMRD/DRMP/CIR/P/2021/08 dated January 11, 2021, and follows on from NCL's previous circular no. 0208/2026 dated June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No. 0250/2026, Download Ref No. NCL/COM/75204, dated July 15, 2026.
- Applicable VSR (%) for August 2026 by commodity (including variants):
  - Copper: 5%
  - Gold: 5%
  - Silver: 6%
  - Zinc: 5%
  - Crude Oil: 30%
  - Natural Gas: 6%
- Issued by the Chief Risk Officer, NSE Clearing Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the VSR parameters used to compute margins for option contracts in the specified commodities, in continuation of the periodic review mandated by SEBI circular SEBI/HO/CDMRD/DRMP/CIR/P/2021/08 dated January 11, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

All clearing members must apply the revised VSR percentages for margining option contracts in Copper, Gold, Silver, Zinc, Crude Oil, and Natural Gas (including their variants) for the month of August 2026.

## Important Dates

- Circular issued: July 15, 2026
- Applicable period: August 2026
- Previous circular referenced: June 15, 2026 (Circular no. 0208/2026, Download Ref no. NCL/COM/74705)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine risk management update affecting margin computation for commodity option contracts. The impact is limited to clearing members and traders active in these commodity derivatives; no listed equity stocks are directly affected. Crude Oil retains a notably higher VSR (30%) reflecting its higher volatility profile relative to metals.
