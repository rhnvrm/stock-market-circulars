## Summary

NSE has issued an updated circular (superseding NSE/FAOP/75367 dated July 23, 2026) detailing revised timings for a mock trading session in the Futures & Options segment to be held on Saturday, July 25, 2026. The mock session is designed to test trading modalities associated with the upcoming introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment, as previously outlined in circular NSE/FAOP/74467 dated May 29, 2026. The session will also involve the rollout of a new NEATPlus trading terminal version (7.8.9) for CAS-specific testing, followed by a reversion to the existing version (7.8.8) for subsequent sessions and live trading.

## Key Points

- This circular supersedes NSE/FAOP/75367 issued July 23, 2026, with updated mock session timings.
- Three trading sessions will be conducted: Session 1 (testing CAS-related changes with new software), Session 2 (Primary Site, existing modalities), and Session 3 (DR Site, existing modalities).
- New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 is required exclusively for Trading Session 1; members must reinstall version 7.8.8 for Sessions 2 and 3.
- Trades executed in Session 1 will not carry over to Sessions 2 or 3; trades from Session 2 will be available in Session 3.
- During Session 1, reference prices for stock futures will be based on VWAP of trades executed between 08:45 a.m. and 09:00 a.m. to align price bands with CAS in the Cash Market segment.
- Version 7.8.8 and 7.8.9 are compatible with new broadcast parameters referenced in circular NSE/MSD/75324 dated July 21, 2026.
- Existing broadcast parameters for members using direct NNF connections will be discontinued from September 5, 2026, requiring migration planning.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulations but operationalizes the previously announced change in trading modalities in the Equity Derivatives segment tied to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment (per circular NSE/FAOP/74467 dated May 29, 2026). It also references a related change to market data broadcast parameters (NSE/MSD/75324 dated July 21, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download and install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 from the NSE Extranet path (/faoftp/faocommon/Mock/25JULY2026/NEATPlus789) prior to 07:00 hours on July 25, 2026, for use only in Trading Session 1.
- After Session 1, members must reinstall NEATPlus version 7.8.8 (available at /faoftp/faocommon/NEATPlus788) before participating in Sessions 2 and 3, and for live trading resuming July 27, 2026.
- Members are required to actively participate in all three trading sessions.
- Members using direct NNF connections must plan migration to new market data broadcast parameters ahead of the September 5, 2026 discontinuation of existing parameters.

## Important Dates

- **July 24, 2026**: Circular issued (supersedes July 23, 2026 circular).
- **July 25, 2026**: Mock trading day — Session 1 (08:00–10:55 hrs), Session 2 (13:15–15:25 hrs), Session 3 (16:00–17:10 hrs), with live re-login testing from 19:00–19:30 hrs.
- **July 27, 2026**: Resumption of live trading using NEATPlus version 7.8.8.
- **September 5, 2026**: Discontinuation of existing broadcast parameters for members using direct NNF connections.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low immediate market impact as it pertains to a single-day mock trading exercise affecting only exchange members' operational readiness, not investors or listed securities. However, it carries medium longer-term operational significance as it forms part of the phased rollout of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment — a structural change to trading mechanics that members and their technology teams must prepare for, including software upgrades and adaptation to new market data broadcast standards ahead of the September 2026 deadline.
