## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued securities for three companies on the NSE Capital Market SME EMERGE segment, effective June 18, 2026. All three securities — De Neers Tools Limited, Ducol Organics and Colours Limited, and Swaraj Suiting Limited — have been allotted equity shares under preferential allotment and will trade in the SM series.

## Key Points

- Three SME EMERGE companies have new preferential allotment shares admitted to trading effective June 18, 2026.
- All issues are equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each, in the SM series.
- Shares were allotted on different dates; lock-in periods extend through December 2026 for all three.
- Temporary ISINs (IN8*) were used for dematerialized credit per SEBI circulars; permanent ISINs are now activated.
- Trading will be identified solely by designated security codes, in specified lot sizes.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot sizes for each scrip.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to allotted shares until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 18, 2026 (trading commences)
- **De Neers Tools — Date of Allotment:** May 4, 2026 | **Lock-in Expiry:** December 25, 2026
- **Ducol Organics — Date of Allotment:** January 22, 2025 | **Lock-in Expiry:** December 30, 2026
- **Swaraj Suiting — Date of Allotment:** April 16, 2026 | **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2026
