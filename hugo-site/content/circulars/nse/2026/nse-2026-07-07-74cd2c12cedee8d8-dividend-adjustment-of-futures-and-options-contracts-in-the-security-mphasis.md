## Summary

NSE has notified members of an adjustment to Futures and Options contracts in MPHASIS Limited following the company's declaration of a Rs 62.00 per share dividend on a face value of Rs 10/-. The adjustment, made in accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments, will be effective from the ex-date of July 8, 2026, with revised option strike prices detailed in the circular's annexure.

## Key Points

- Symbol: MPHASIS (MPHASIS LIMITED)
- Corporate action type: Dividend
- Face value: Rs 10/-
- Dividend amount: Rs 62.00 per share
- Ex-date and effective date: 08-Jul-2026
- Revised option strike prices are listed in Annexure 1, covering OPTSTK contracts expiring 28-JUL-2026, with strikes adjusted downward (e.g., 1800.00 to 1738.00, 1840.00 to 1778.00, up to 2000.00 to 1938.00 and beyond)
- Adjustment methodology available at NSE's equity derivatives corporate actions adjustments page

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine corporate action adjustment applied under SEBI's prescribed guidelines for adjusting derivative contracts upon dividend announcements, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz/MII contract file (NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) and spread file (NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) onto their trading applications before trading on the ex-date/effective date.
- These files can be obtained from the faoftp/faocommon directory on the Extranet server, or from the NSE website's all-reports-derivatives page.
- Position adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Important Dates

- Ex-date and effective date of adjustment: July 8, 2026
- Relevant contract expiry referenced in annexure: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The adjustment affects only market participants holding or trading MPHASIS Futures and Options contracts. Revised strike prices and lot sizes will be rounded to the nearest tick size and integer respectively, and will be reflected in updated contract and spread files. Members and traders must ensure their systems reflect the revised strikes before the ex-date to avoid mispricing or settlement discrepancies. The overall market impact is limited to MPHASIS derivatives and does not affect other securities or broader market operations.
