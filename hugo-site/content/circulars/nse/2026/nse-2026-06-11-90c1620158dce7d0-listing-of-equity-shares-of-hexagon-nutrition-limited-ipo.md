## Summary

NSE has notified all Exchange members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Hexagon Nutrition Limited on the National Stock Exchange of India. The company will trade under the symbol **HEXAGON** with ISIN **INE0JUI01012**. The specific listing date has not been announced and will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Hexagon Nutrition Limited
- Exchange Symbol: HEXAGON
- ISIN: INE0JUI01012
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date: To be announced via a separate circular
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74636, Circular Ref. No. 0934/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under NSE's Listing department circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing and prepare accordingly for trading in HEXAGON once the listing date is officially confirmed in a subsequent circular.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 11, 2026
- Listing Date: To be notified separately

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Hexagon Nutrition Limited as a new scrip on NSE will expand the investable universe for Exchange members and retail investors. As a nutrition-sector company, it may attract interest from FMCG and healthcare-focused investors. No immediate market impact until the listing date circular is issued and trading commences.
