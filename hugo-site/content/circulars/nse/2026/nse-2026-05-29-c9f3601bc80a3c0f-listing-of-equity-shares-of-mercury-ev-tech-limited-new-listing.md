## Summary

NSE has admitted the equity shares of Mercury Ev-Tech Limited to dealings on its Capital Market segment effective June 02, 2026. The scrip will trade under the symbol **MERCURYEV** in Series **BE**, which places it in the Trade-for-Trade segment until further notice. A total of 189,973,058 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each are listed.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** MERCURYEV
- **ISIN:** INE763M01028
- **Series:** BE (Trade-for-Trade segment)
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Total shares listed:** 189,973,058
- **Distinctive number range:** 1 to 189,973,058
- **Market lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Effective date:** June 02, 2026
- **Registrar & Share Transfer Agent:** Accurate Securities and Registry Private Limited, Ahmedabad

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, the security is admitted to dealings with designated codes and lot sizes as specified. The scrip will remain in the Trade-for-Trade (BE series) segment till further notice from NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (MERCURYEV) and series (BE) for all transactions.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 1 share.
- Members should note the lock-in details per Annexure I before facilitating delivery or transfer of locked shares.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective / Listing Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Partial Lock-in Expiry:** July 31, 2026 (for 3,400,000 shares; distinctive nos. 175,547,393 to 178,947,392)

## Impact Assessment

The listing introduces Mercury Ev-Tech Limited — an EV technology company based in Vadodara, Gujarat — to NSE's Capital Market segment. With 189,973,058 shares outstanding and a Re. 1/- face value, the float is large. However, placement in the BE (Trade-for-Trade) series restricts intraday trading and requires compulsory delivery settlement, reducing speculative activity in the near term. Approximately 3.4 million shares (around 1.8% of total) are locked until July 31, 2026, posing minimal liquidity impact. Investors should exercise caution typical of newly listed, Trade-for-Trade securities.
