## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities of Profectus Capital Private Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective February 24, 2026. The security is a 9% bond maturing in February 2028 and will trade under the symbol 9PCPL28A in the N0 series.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to NSE Capital Market segment with effect from February 24, 2026
- Issuer: Profectus Capital Private Limited
- Symbol: 9PCPL28A, Series: N0
- Coupon rate: 9% per annum
- Maturity date: February 20, 2028
- ISIN: INE389Z07104
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in routine compliance with existing NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A â€” Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification).

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to use the designated security code (9PCPL28A) for trading identification
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members must adhere to the designated series (N0) for this security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Effective Date (Trading Commencement):** February 24, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** February 20, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing notification for a single privately placed corporate bond by Profectus Capital Private Limited. The security (PCPL 9% 2028) offers a fixed 9% coupon and has a short remaining tenor of approximately two years. Relevant primarily to debt market participants and investors in Profectus Capital instruments.
