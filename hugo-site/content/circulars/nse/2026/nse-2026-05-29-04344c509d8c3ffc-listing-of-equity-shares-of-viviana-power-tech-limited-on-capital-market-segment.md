## Summary

Viviana Power Tech Limited (ISIN: INE0MEG01014, NSE Symbol: VIVIANA) has been listed on the Capital Market Segment (Main Board) of NSE following migration from the SME Emerge platform. The shareholding pattern was filed under Regulation 31(1)(b) with a report date of May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Viviana Power Tech Limited migrates from NSE SME Emerge to the NSE Main Board (Capital Market Segment).
- NSE Symbol: VIVIANA; ISIN: INE0MEG01014.
- The company is classified as an SME and issues Equity Shares as the class of security.
- Shareholding pattern report filed for the quarter/period ended May 25, 2026.
- The company is not a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU).
- Promoter shares are locked-in; no pledging, NDU, or other encumbrances reported.
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, or depository receipts issued.
- No equity shares with differential voting rights; no significant beneficial owner reported.

## Regulatory Changes

Migration from the SME Emerge platform to the Main Board subjects Viviana Power Tech Limited to the full compliance and disclosure requirements applicable to Main Board listed entities under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must comply with ongoing Main Board listing obligations including periodic disclosures, shareholding pattern filings under Regulation 31, and continuous listing requirements.
- Promoter lock-in conditions remain in effect as reported.

## Important Dates

- Listing / Report Date: May 25, 2026
- Circular Date: May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The migration to the Main Board increases the visibility and accessibility of Viviana Power Tech Limited's shares to a broader set of investors, including institutional participants who may be restricted from trading SME-platform securities. Existing shareholders benefit from improved liquidity potential. The promoter lock-in and absence of encumbrances indicate stable shareholding structure at the time of migration.
