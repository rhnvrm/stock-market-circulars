## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: NSE/CML/73932, Circular Ref. No: 0697/2026) notifying the suspension of trading in equity shares of Omkar Speciality Chemicals Limited (Symbol: OMKARCHEM) effective from the closing hours of April 28, 2026. The suspension is mandated under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, triggered by a capital reduction pursuant to an NCLT (National Company Law Tribunal) order dated July 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading in OMKARCHEM equity shares is suspended effective April 29, 2026 (i.e., from closing hours of April 28, 2026)
- Suspension is under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Reason: Capital Reduction pursuant to NCLT Order dated July 31, 2025
- Circular issued by Ruchi Jaiswal, Manager, NSE Listing Department
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73932 | 0697/2026

## Regulatory Changes

Under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, NSE is empowered to suspend securities from trading. This action is a regulatory consequence of the NCLT-ordered capital reduction in Omkar Speciality Chemicals Limited, which necessitates halting normal equity trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified and must cease trading in OMKARCHEM equity shares from the closing hours of April 28, 2026
- Members should inform their clients holding OMKARCHEM shares about the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders for OMKARCHEM equity shares should be placed or accepted after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** July 31, 2025 (underlying order for capital reduction)
- **Circular Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** April 29, 2026 (from closing hours of April 28, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension of OMKARCHEM equity shares will have a significant impact on existing shareholders and investors:
- Shareholders will be unable to trade (buy or sell) OMKARCHEM equity shares on NSE from April 29, 2026
- The capital reduction under the NCLT order likely implies a restructuring of the company's equity, which may affect the value of shares held by investors
- Investors holding OMKARCHEM shares should review the NCLT order details and the company's announcements to understand the implications of the capital reduction on their holdings
- The suspension may remain in effect until the capital reduction process is formally completed and NSE lifts the suspension
