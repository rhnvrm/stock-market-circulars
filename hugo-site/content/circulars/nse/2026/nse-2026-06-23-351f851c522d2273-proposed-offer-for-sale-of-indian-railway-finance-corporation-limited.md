## Summary

NSE has announced a proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Indian Railway Finance Corporation Limited (IRFC, symbol: IRFC, ISIN: INE053F01010) scheduled for June 24–25, 2026. The OFS will be conducted via the NSE eOFS platform at a floor price of Rs. 91/- per equity share of face value Rs. 10/-. The offer includes a base size, an oversubscription option of equal size, and a small employee category tranche.

## Key Points

- OFS dates: June 24, 2026 (Day 1) and June 25, 2026 (Day 2)
- Floor price: Rs. 91/- per equity share
- Base offer size: Up to 13,06,85,060 equity shares
- Oversubscription option: Up to an additional 13,06,85,060 equity shares (seller to intimate intention to exercise by 5:00 PM on June 24, 2026)
- Employee category: Additionally 25,000 equity shares (bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs)
- Bidding categories: IS (Non-retail), RS (Retail — bid value ≤ Rs. 2 lacs), ES (Employee — bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs)
- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate via the eOFS platform (https://eofs.nseindia.com)
- The OFS is in accordance with interoperability among Clearing Corporations per circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026
- Market lot: 1 equity share; Tick size: Re. 0.01

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced; the OFS is conducted under existing interoperability guidelines per Exchange Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/72975 dated February 24, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure compliant UCCs of self (PRO) and clients are updated in the Unique Client Identification (UCI) system at least one day prior to the OFS bidding session.
- UCCs that become compliant on the day of the bidding session will not be permitted to participate.
- UCCs identified as compliant by 22:00 hrs on the previous day will be considered eligible for the next bidding day.
- Members not yet having Admin user IDs must register via the ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/) under path: ENIT-NEW-TRADE → Membership → Enablement → eOFS. Requests are processed at 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, and 3:00 PM; post-3:00 PM requests are processed next day by 8:45 AM. Only one request per member should be raised.
- Members must ensure only a single Admin User creation request is submitted (cannot be modified once raised).
- Members must ensure sufficiency of collaterals before initiating carry-forward of orders on T+1 day; orders rejected for insufficient margins cannot be re-placed.
- Members should refer to Annexure 3 of circular NSE/CMTR/43529 dated February 13, 2020 for feature and limit settings.

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Circular issued; members must update UCCs in UCI system by end of day
- **June 24, 2026**: Day 1 of OFS bidding session; seller to intimate intention on oversubscription option by 5:00 PM
- **June 25, 2026**: Day 2 of OFS bidding session

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact event for Capital Market segment participants. IRFC is a major public sector enterprise, and the combined maximum offer size (base + oversubscription) of approximately 26.14 crore shares at a floor price of Rs. 91/- represents a significant liquidity event. All trading members must proactively verify UCC compliance, collateral adequacy, and eOFS platform access ahead of June 24. Retail investors (bid value ≤ Rs. 2 lacs) and employees (bid value ≤ Rs. 5 lacs) have dedicated bidding categories. Members with pending Admin ID registration on the eOFS platform should act immediately to meet the processing cut-off times.
