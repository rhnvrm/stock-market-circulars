## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all exchange members that Phoenix ARC Private Limited will officially change its name to Phoenix ARC Limited with effect from May 19, 2026. This is a straightforward corporate name change with no impact on trading operations.

## Key Points

- Existing name: Phoenix ARC Private Limited
- New name: Phoenix ARC Limited
- Effective date: May 19, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74223

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative notification informing members of a corporate name change for Phoenix ARC Private Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their records to reflect the new name — Phoenix ARC Limited — effective May 19, 2026. No specific compliance action is required beyond acknowledging the name change.

## Important Dates

- **May 14, 2026**: Circular issued
- **May 19, 2026**: Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal. The name change from Phoenix ARC Private Limited to Phoenix ARC Limited is a corporate administrative action. It does not affect trading, securities listings, or compliance obligations for exchange members beyond updating internal records and references to the entity.
