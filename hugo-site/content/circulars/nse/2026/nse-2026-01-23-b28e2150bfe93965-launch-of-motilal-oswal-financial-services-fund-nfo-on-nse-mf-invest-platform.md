## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Motilal Oswal Financial Services Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The new fund offer will be open for subscriptions from January 27, 2026 to February 10, 2026, with allotment scheduled for February 16, 2026. The fund offers six plan variants across Direct and Regular categories with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- Fund House: Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund
- Scheme Type: Equity Category
- NFO Period: January 27, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- Allotment Date: February 16, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes have unique ISINs ranging from INF247L01GN5 to INF247L01GS4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 16, 2026 at 06:00 PM
- Members should make the NFO available to their clients on the NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **February 10, 2026**: NFO closing date
- **February 16, 2026**: Allotment date and payment deadline (06:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine announcement of a new mutual fund offering. The impact is limited to:

- Investors seeking exposure to financial services sector through equity mutual funds
- NSE MF Invest Platform members who will facilitate transactions
- Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund's product portfolio expansion

No direct impact on stock market operations or trading activities. The fund focuses on financial services sector equity investments and provides standard plan options for different investor preferences.
