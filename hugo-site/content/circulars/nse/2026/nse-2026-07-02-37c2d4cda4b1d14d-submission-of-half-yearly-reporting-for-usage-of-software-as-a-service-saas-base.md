## Summary

NSE has issued a circular reminding trading members availing Software as a Service (SaaS) based solutions/applications to submit their half-yearly compliance reporting, in line with SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020. For the half-yearly period January 01, 2026 to June 30, 2026, members must submit the required details through the Member Portal on or before July 31, 2026. Members not availing any SaaS services must still submit a 'NO' declaration with appropriate remarks.

## Key Points

- Applicable to all trading members availing/utilizing SaaS-based solutions or applications.
- Submission is to be made via NSE Member Portal: Login → Inspection → SaaS → Submit SaaS Report.
- Members not using any SaaS service must select 'NO' in the SaaS report menu and provide an appropriate comment/remark.
- A detailed user manual for submission is provided as Annexure – A to the circular.
- Non-submission or delayed submission attracts financial disincentives as per Annexure 1.1 of Exchange Circular No. NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026, detailed in Annexure – B.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirement is introduced; this circular operationalizes the existing SEBI advisory (SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020) on SaaS usage by financial sector organizations, requiring periodic half-yearly compliance confirmation through NSE's Member Portal.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must log in to the Member Portal and navigate to Inspection → SaaS → Submit SaaS Report.
- If availing SaaS services, members must add row(s) with the required details and submit.
- If not availing SaaS services, members must select 'NO' and provide a remark/comment before submitting.
- Members should refer to Annexure – A for step-by-step submission guidance.
- Members should be aware of financial disincentives under Annexure 1.1 of Circular NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026 for non-adherence.

## Important Dates

- Reporting period covered: January 01, 2026 to June 30, 2026
- Submission deadline: July 31, 2026
- Reference SEBI circular: November 03, 2020
- Reference Exchange circular on financial disincentives: April 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/compliance circular affecting trading members' internal reporting processes with no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or stock prices. Non-compliant members face financial disincentives, making timely submission important from a member-level regulatory risk perspective, though the overall market impact is minimal.
