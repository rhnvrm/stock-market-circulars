## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No: 1033/2026) has notified that the face value and paid-up value of the Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF (Symbol: PSUBANK) will be reduced from Rs. 10 to Re. 1, effective July 10, 2026. This is a standard face value split that will increase the number of units proportionally while reducing the per-unit price.

## Key Points

- Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF (PSUBANK) face value split from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 (10:1 split ratio)
- Both face value and paid-up value are being changed
- Effective date: July 10, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74911

## Regulatory Changes

The face value per unit of Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF will change as follows:

| ETF Name | Symbol | Existing Face Value & Paid-up Value | New Face Value & Paid-up Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF | PSUBANK | Rs. 10 | Re. 1 |

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of NSE are informed and must take note of the change effective July 10, 2026
- Trading systems and back-office records should be updated to reflect the new face value of Re. 1 per unit from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 10, 2026 (face value and paid-up value change takes effect)

## Impact Assessment

The face value split of PSUBANK ETF from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 will result in a 10x increase in the number of units held by investors, with a corresponding reduction in per-unit NAV/price. This improves affordability and liquidity of the ETF for retail investors. The total portfolio value of existing unitholders remains unchanged. Brokers and depository participants should ensure their systems are updated before July 10, 2026 to correctly reflect the new unit structure.
