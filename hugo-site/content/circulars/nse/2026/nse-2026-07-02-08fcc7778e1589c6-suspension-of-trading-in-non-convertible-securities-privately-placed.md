## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in two privately placed Non-Convertible Securities (NCDs) issued by Jharkhand Road Projects Implementation Company Limited, effective from July 3, 2026, on account of redemption of the securities.

## Key Points

- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Part A
- Two ISINs of Jharkhand Road Projects Implementation Company Limited are being suspended from trading
- ISIN INE746N07812 - suspended effective 03-July-2026 due to Redemption
- ISIN INE746N07390 - suspended effective 03-July-2026 due to Redemption
- Circular issued by Vishakha Kine, Manager, on behalf of NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations governing suspension of securities upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members must cease trading in the specified ISINs (INE746N07812 and INE746N07390) of Jharkhand Road Projects Implementation Company Limited from the suspension date and update their systems/records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 2, 2026
- Suspension Effective Date: July 3, 2026 (both ISINs)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these two specific privately placed debt ISINs of Jharkhand Road Projects Implementation Company Limited. As the suspension is due to redemption (maturity/repayment of the securities), this represents a routine, expected market operations event with no adverse implications for the issuer's broader debt program or the equity market.
