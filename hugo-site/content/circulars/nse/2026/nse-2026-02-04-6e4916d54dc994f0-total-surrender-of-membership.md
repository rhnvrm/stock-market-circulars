## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has approved the total surrender of membership for Vinod Shares Ltd. (SEBI registration number INZ000175235). The member's last trade date was November 14, 2024, and the surrender was approved on February 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Vinod Shares Ltd.
- SEBI registration number: INZ000175235
- Last trade date: November 14, 2024
- Approval date: February 2, 2026
- Circular reference: NSE/COMP/72603, Circular No. 07/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership surrender. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2024**: Last trade date for Vinod Shares Ltd.
- **February 2, 2026**: Date of approval for membership surrender
- **February 4, 2026**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action involving a single trading member who had already ceased trading operations over a year prior to the formal surrender approval. The action does not affect market operations or other trading members' activities.
