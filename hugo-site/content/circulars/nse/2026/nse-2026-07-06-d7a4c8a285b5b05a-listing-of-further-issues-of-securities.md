## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities (preferential allotments) for four SME EMERGE-listed companies — Anlon Technology Solutions Limited, Krishca Strapping Solutions Limited, Sat Kartar Life Limited, and Z-Tech (India) Limited — effective July 07, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. Corresponding market lot sizes have also been notified under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Anlon Technology Solutions Limited (Symbol: ANLON, Series: SM, ISIN: INE0LR101013): 1,254,400 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 398.5; date of allotment 03-Jun-2026; market lot 400; lock-in until 14-Jan-2027.
- Krishca Strapping Solutions Limited (Symbol: KRISHCA, Series: SM, ISIN: INE0NR701018): 1,037,750 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 190; date of allotment 02-Jun-2026; market lot 500; lock-in until 15-Jan-2027.
- Sat Kartar Life Limited (Symbol: SATKARTAR, Series: SM, ISIN: INE0NB801022): 50,400 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued under preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 172; date of allotment 27-May-2026; market lot 800; lock-in until 29-Jan-2027.
- Z-Tech (India) Limited (Symbol: ZTECH, Series: SM, ISIN: INE0ISZ01012): Face value and paid-up value Rs. 10; further details (issue price, allotment date, quantity, lock-in) truncated in source but company is included in this listing action.
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares (where specified) and shall be identified by their designated security codes for trading.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes), not a new regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must trade these securities only under their designated codes and in the specified market lot sizes effective from the listing date. No additional action required from investors beyond awareness of new tradable quantities and lock-in restrictions on the preferentially allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: Anlon – 03-Jun-2026; Krishca – 02-Jun-2026; Sat Kartar Life – 27-May-2026.
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: July 07, 2026.
- Lock-in Expiry: Anlon – 14-Jan-2027; Krishca – 15-Jan-2027; Sat Kartar Life – 29-Jan-2027.

## Impact Assessment

This circular increases the tradable float for the four SME-listed companies as preferentially allotted shares become eligible for dealing on NSE's SME EMERGE platform, subject to lock-in for non-promoter/other categories where applicable. Market impact is limited given the small-cap, SME nature of these issuers, but existing shareholders and prospective investors should note the additional share count and lock-in expiry timelines for future supply considerations.
