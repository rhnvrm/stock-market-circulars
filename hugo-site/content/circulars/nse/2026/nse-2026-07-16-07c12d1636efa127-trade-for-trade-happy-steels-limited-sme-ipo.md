## Summary

NSE has announced that Happy Steels Limited (Symbol: HAPPY), an SME IPO listed company, will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to the rolling settlement segment (Series: SM) with effect from July 30, 2026. This follows an earlier circular (NSE/CML/75214 dated July 15, 2026) and is issued pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular reference CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Security: Happy Steels Limited, Symbol HAPPY
- Moving from trade for trade segment (Series: ST) to rolling segment (Series: SM)
- Effective date: July 30, 2026
- Issued under Download Ref No. NSE/CML/75225, Circular Ref. No. 1157/2026
- Follows up on prior circular NSE/CML/75214 dated July 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This transition is undertaken pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012), which governs the framework for trade for trade segment transitions for SME-listed securities as part of the post-IPO trading normalization process.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to note the change in trading series for HAPPY from ST to SM effective July 30, 2026, and update their systems/records accordingly for order routing and settlement processes.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Prior circular NSE/CML/75214 issued
- July 16, 2026: Current circular issued
- July 30, 2026: Effective date of transfer from ST to SM series

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural change common to SME IPOs following their initial trade-for-trade period. The shift to the rolling (SM) segment allows for normal T+1 settlement and potentially increased trading liquidity for HAPPY. Impact on the broader market is minimal, though it is relevant for members and investors trading in this specific security.
