## Summary

NSE has admitted Rights Entitlements (REs) of Integra Essentia Limited to trading on the Capital Market segment effective May 29, 2026. The REs trade under the symbol ESSEN-RE3 (Series: BE, ISIN: INE418N20043) in the Trade to Trade segment in compulsory demat mode. A total of 687,592,710 Rights Entitlements are available for trading.

## Key Points

- Symbol ESSEN-RE3 admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from May 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE418N20043; Series: BE; Market lot: 1
- Total Rights Entitlements: 687,592,710
- Trading in Trade to Trade (T2T) segment; compulsory demat for all investors
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished
- Trading members must prominently include a disclaimer in contract notes/bills about the nature of REs

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs, mentioning the ISIN and clarifying that REs are not ordinary shares.
- Trading members must include a prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for purchases of REs, stating that purchase only grants the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce before the issue closes.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (ESSEN-RE3)
- **May 29, 2026 – June 5, 2026**: Trading window for renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **June 8, 2026**: Date of suspension of trading in Rights Entitlements

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts existing shareholders of Integra Essentia Limited who have received Rights Entitlements. The T2T segment classification means each trade results in mandatory delivery, reducing speculative activity. With 687,592,710 REs and a market lot of 1, participation is accessible to retail investors. Shareholders must act before June 5, 2026 to subscribe or renounce; failure to do so will result in lapse of entitlements on June 8, 2026.
