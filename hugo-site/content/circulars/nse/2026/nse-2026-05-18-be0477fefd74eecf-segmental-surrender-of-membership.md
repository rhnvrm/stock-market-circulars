## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has notified all members that Saurin Investments (P) Ltd has been approved for surrender of its membership in the Currency and IRF (Interest Rate Futures) Segment. The surrender was approved on May 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Member: Saurin Investments (P) Ltd
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000285637
- Surrendered Segment: Currency and IRF Segment
- Surrender Approval Date: May 13, 2026
- This is a segmental surrender only; membership in other segments (if any) may be retained

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an administrative notification of an approved membership surrender in a specific segment.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the surrender approval. No direct compliance action is required from other members beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- Surrender Approval Date: May 13, 2026
- Circular Date: May 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action involving a single member exiting the Currency and IRF Segment. No disruption to market operations or other participants is anticipated.
