## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all Exchange members that Madhav Copper Limited, trading under the symbol MCL, will change its name to M TEK COPPER LIMITED effective June 09, 2026. The stock symbol MCL remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company symbol MCL is unaffected by the name change.
- Existing name: Madhav Copper Limited; new name: M TEK COPPER LIMITED.
- Change is effective from June 09, 2026.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74529 | Circular Ref. No: 0895/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new legal name in Exchange records.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the updated company name and ensure their systems, records, and client communications reflect the new name M TEK COPPER LIMITED from June 09, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026** — Circular issued.
- **June 09, 2026** — Name change becomes effective on NSE.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is administrative and low. The trading symbol MCL remains the same, so no disruption to order entry or settlement is expected. Investors holding shares of MCL should be aware that the company name in their demat statements and trading terminals will reflect M TEK COPPER LIMITED after the effective date.
