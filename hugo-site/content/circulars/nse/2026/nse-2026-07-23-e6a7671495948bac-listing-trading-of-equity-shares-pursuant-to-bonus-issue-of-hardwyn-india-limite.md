## Summary

NSE has notified that the bonus equity shares of Hardwyn India Limited will be admitted for dealing on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 30, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Company: Hardwyn India Limited (Symbol: HARDWYN)
- ISIN: INE626Z01029
- Number of bonus securities admitted: 19,53,73,621
- Distinctive number range: 488434055 to 683807675
- Securities will be identified only by their designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notification under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize and process the newly admitted bonus securities of Hardwyn India Limited using the designated security codes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 28, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: July 29, 2026
- Listing & Trading Effective Date: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue increases Hardwyn India Limited's outstanding share count and public float, which may improve liquidity and lower the per-share price without affecting overall shareholder value. Market impact is limited to the company's stock and its existing shareholders.
