## Summary

NSE has notified that Bio Medica Laboratories Limited (symbol: BMLL), which was listed under the SME IPO segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (series: ST) to the Rolling segment (series: SM) effective June 12, 2026. This follows the earlier Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74442 dated May 27, 2026 and is in accordance with SEBI guidelines under circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- BMLL (Bio Medica Laboratories Limited) will move from series ST (Trade for Trade) to series SM (Rolling segment)
- Effective date of transfer: June 12, 2026
- This is a follow-up to NSE circular NSE/CML/74442 dated May 27, 2026
- Transfer is pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- The scrip is categorized as an SME IPO listing

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular implements the standard transition procedure for SME IPO securities from the trade-for-trade settlement mechanism to normal rolling settlement, as prescribed by SEBI's existing guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members are advised to take note of the segment transfer and update their systems accordingly. Trades in BMLL after June 12, 2026 will be settled under the rolling settlement mechanism (series: SM) instead of the trade-for-trade mechanism (series: ST).

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date of Transfer:** June 12, 2026
- **Reference Circular:** NSE/CML/74442 dated May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade (ST) to Rolling Segment (SM) is a standard post-IPO milestone for SME listings. Under the Trade for Trade mechanism, each trade must result in delivery and cannot be squared off intraday. Moving to the rolling segment allows normal intraday trading and netting of positions, generally improving liquidity and reducing settlement friction for BMLL shareholders. The impact is limited to participants trading in BMLL.
