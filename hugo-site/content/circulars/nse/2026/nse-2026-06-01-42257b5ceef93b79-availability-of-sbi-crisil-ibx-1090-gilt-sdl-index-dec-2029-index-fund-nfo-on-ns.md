## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the SBI CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index - Dec 2029 Index Fund NFO of SBI Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Effective date: June 01, 2026
- Fund house: SBI Mutual Fund
- Scheme type: Debt Index Fund tracking CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index - Dec 2029
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants across Direct and Regular plans in Growth and IDCW (Payout/Reinvestment) options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform availability announcement for an existing NFO being reopened for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of this fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform and facilitate client transactions accordingly from June 01, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 01, 2026 — Scheme available for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational announcement with low market impact. The circular notifies NSE members of the reopening of the SBI CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt+SDL Index - Dec 2029 Index Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing transactions. Investors interested in a target-maturity debt index fund with a December 2029 maturity horizon and a 10:90 allocation between Government Securities and State Development Loans may now transact through the NSE MF Invest Platform. No impact on equities or broader market operations.
