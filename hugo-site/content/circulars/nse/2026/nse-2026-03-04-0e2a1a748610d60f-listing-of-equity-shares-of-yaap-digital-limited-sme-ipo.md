## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Yaap Digital Limited (scrip code: YAAP) pursuant to its SME IPO. The shareholding pattern has been filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as on the post-issue date of 02.03.2026.

## Key Points

- Yaap Digital Limited equity shares listed on NSE under the SME IPO segment with scrip code YAAP
- Shareholding pattern filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) with post-issue date of 02.03.2026
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, pledged promoter shares, NDU encumbrances, or differential voting rights
- The company has at least one significant beneficial owner (declared YES)
- All shares held in dematerialised form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Filing is in compliance with existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 31 governing shareholding pattern disclosures post-listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Yaap Digital Limited is required to maintain and update shareholding pattern disclosures as per SEBI LODR Regulation 31
- The company must disclose significant beneficial ownership as indicated in the declaration
- All standard post-listing compliance obligations under SEBI LODR apply to the newly listed entity

## Important Dates

- Post-issue shareholding pattern date: 02.03.2026
- Listing date: on or around 04.03.2026 (circular date)

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces Yaap Digital Limited to NSE's SME platform, making its equity shares available for trading. The impact is limited to investors and market participants interested in this SME scrip. No adverse regulatory flags (no pledged shares, no encumbrances, no warrants or convertible securities outstanding), which is generally a positive indicator for new investors.
