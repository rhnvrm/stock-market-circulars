## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debentures of Emerald Haven Properties Private Limited on the Capital Market segment. The 7% debentures (ISIN: INE29PK08010) will be available for trading from January 16, 2026, with symbol 7EHPP46 in N0 series and a market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Company: Emerald Haven Properties Private Limited
- Symbol: 7EHPP46
- Series: N0
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Description: EHPP 7% 2046
- Coupon Rate: 7%
- Maturity Date: January 13, 2046
- ISIN Code: INE29PK08010
- Segment: Capital Market (privately placed securities)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code 7EHPP46 for identification on the trading system
- Trading shall be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- Members must ensure compliance with all applicable trading regulations for debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 14, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 16, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** January 13, 2046

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing expands the debt securities universe available on NSE but is limited to a single issuer with a 20-year maturity period. The impact is primarily relevant to institutional investors interested in long-term debt instruments.
