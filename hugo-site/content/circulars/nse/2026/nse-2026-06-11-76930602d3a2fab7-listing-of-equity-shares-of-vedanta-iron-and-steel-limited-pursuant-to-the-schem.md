## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (VISL) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 15, 2026. The listing arises from the NCLT-approved Scheme of Arrangement under which Vedanta Limited was demerged into four resulting companies, with VISL being Resulting Company 4.

## Key Points

- Symbol: VISL; Series: BE (Trade for Trade) — to be subsequently shifted to EQ series per SEBI circular SEBI/CIR/ISD/1/2010
- ISIN: INE1CLE01013; Face Value: Re. 1/-; Paid-up Value: Re. 1/-
- Total securities listed: 3,910,388,057 equity shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 3,910,388,057)
- Market lot: 1; Pari Passu: Yes; Lock-in: Not Applicable
- Security will participate in the pre-open session as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

The listing is governed by Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. Shares are initially admitted in the BE (Trade for Trade) series per SEBI's surveillance framework, with eventual migration to the EQ series upon satisfaction of applicable conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must recognize VISL under its designated security code for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size (1 share)
- Members should note the pre-open session applicability for this security
- Subsequent series change from BE to EQ will be communicated separately

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 11, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing / Trading Commencement: June 15, 2026
- NCLT Order Date (approving Scheme): December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing introduces a significant new equity with over 3.91 billion shares, representing the iron and steel business demerged from Vedanta Limited. Initial trading in the BE (Trade for Trade) series means transactions are settled on a gross basis without netting, which limits speculative activity and reduces counterparty risk in the initial phase. Investors in Vedanta Limited who received VISL shares pursuant to the scheme can begin trading from June 15, 2026. The eventual shift to EQ series will enable normal rolling settlement and broader participation.
