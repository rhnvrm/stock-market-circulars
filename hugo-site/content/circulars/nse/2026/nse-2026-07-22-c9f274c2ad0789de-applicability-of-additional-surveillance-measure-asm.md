## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework, identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion in or movement within the Long-Term ASM (LT-ASM) framework. Global Surfaces Limited (GSLSU) has been shifted from the Short-Term ASM (STASM) framework into LT-ASM Stage-I, and Shalimar Paints Limited (SHALPAINTS) has moved from LT-ASM Stage-I to Stage-II. No securities were shortlisted for Stage-IV in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Global Surfaces Limited (GSLSU, ISIN: INE0JSX01015) shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage-I, having moved from the STASM framework.
- Shalimar Paints Limited (SHALPAINTS, ISIN: INE849C01026) shortlisted to move from LT-ASM Stage-I to Stage-II.
- No securities were shortlisted under LT-ASM Stage-IV, or for movement from Stage-I/II to Stage-III/IV, in this circular.
- Applicable margin rate under LT-ASM shall be 100% on all open positions as of July 24, 2026 and new positions created from July 27, 2026 onwards.
- Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage-IV shift) would move from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE), though none qualified in this cycle.
- The ASM framework operates alongside all other existing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is a market surveillance action and does not reflect an adverse view of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this circular applies the existing Long-Term ASM framework (per earlier circulars NSE/SURV/39265, 45111, 46557, 48506, 52090, 63362, and 64066) to a fresh set of securities based on periodic review of surveillance criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply 100% margin on GSLSU (Stage-I) and SHALPAINTS (Stage-II) positions as specified.
- Members must ensure the additional margin is collected on all open positions as on July 24, 2026 and on new positions from July 27, 2026.
- No segment shift (EQ to BE) compliance action required this cycle since no securities were shortlisted under Stage-IV/Criteria VII.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 23, 2026: Effective date for inclusion/movement of securities within LT-ASM stages.
- July 24, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the new margin rate.
- July 27, 2026: Date from which 100% margin applies to open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of GSLSU in LT-ASM Stage-I and the escalation of SHALPAINTS to Stage-II will increase margin requirements to 100%, likely reducing trading volumes and liquidity in these two stocks as leveraged positions become costlier. The overall market impact is limited given only two securities are affected and no stocks are being moved to Trade-for-Trade this cycle.
