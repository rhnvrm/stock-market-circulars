## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of four privately placed debt securities on the capital market segment under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The securities will be admitted to dealings effective **June 22, 2026**, with lot sizes of 1 unit each.

## Key Points

- Four new debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE capital market segment from June 22, 2026
- All securities carry a market lot size of 1 and trade under Series N0
- Securities span coupon rates ranging from 7.4% (SIDBI) to 12% (Viviana Power Tech)
- Maturities range from March 2028 (Viviana Power Tech) to July 2036 (REC)
- Trading must use designated security codes as specified in the Annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify and trade each security exclusively by its designated symbol/code
- Trading in these securities must adhere to the specified market lot sizes (1 unit each)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 22, 2026 (all four securities admitted to dealings)

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of privately placed NCD instruments from two public-sector financial institutions (REC, SIDBI) and two private entities (Tata Capital Housing Finance, Viviana Power Tech). The listing provides secondary market liquidity to existing holders. Broad market impact is minimal; relevant only to fixed-income investors and members dealing in debt securities on the NSE capital market segment.
