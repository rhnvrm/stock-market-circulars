## Summary

NSE's Inspection Department has issued a reminder to all trading members to submit Risk Based Supervision (RBS) data for the financial year April 01, 2025 – March 31, 2026. Submission is mandatory for all members who executed at least one trade during the period and must be completed electronically via the Inspection module in the NSE Member Portal by May 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- This is a follow-up reminder to the original circular NSE/INSP/73795 dated April 17, 2026.
- Submission is mandatory for every active trading member — defined as any member who executed even a single trade between April 01, 2025 and March 31, 2026.
- Submissions must be made electronically through the Inspection module in the NSE Member Portal.
- Non-compliance will attract penal charges and/or disciplinary action as prescribed in circular NSE/INSP/73795.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular reiterates and reinforces the RBS data submission obligation originally communicated via NSE/INSP/73795 dated April 17, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All active trading members (those with at least one trade in FY 2025-26) must submit RBS data.
- Submission channel: Inspection module on the NSE Member Portal.
- Ensure submission is completed on or before May 31, 2026.
- Members requiring assistance may contact the relevant regional office or the central help desk.

## Important Dates

- **Reference circular date:** April 17, 2026 (NSE/INSP/73795)
- **This reminder date:** May 22, 2026
- **Submission deadline:** May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects all NSE trading members who were active during FY 2025-26. The impact is administrative — members must log in to the Member Portal and complete the RBS data submission. Failure to do so by May 31, 2026 will result in penal charges or disciplinary proceedings as defined in the original circular. No market-wide or stock-specific trading impact is expected.
