## Summary

Angel One Mutual Fund's Angel One Silver ETF FOF has been reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform with immediate effect from March 06, 2026. Both Regular-Growth and Direct-Growth plans are available, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Angel One Silver ETF FOF is reopened for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Both Regular-Growth (ISIN: INF1J2R01163) and Direct-Growth (ISIN: INF1J2R01155) plans are available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also enabled along with subscriptions and redemptions
- Effective immediately from the date of the circular (March 06, 2026)
- Category of both schemes is listed as Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding scheme availability on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members are informed to facilitate transactions in the Angel One Silver ETF FOF schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 06, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in Angel One Silver ETF FOF schemes via the NSE MF Invest Platform. It provides investors with access to silver-backed fund-of-fund products and associated systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal options.
