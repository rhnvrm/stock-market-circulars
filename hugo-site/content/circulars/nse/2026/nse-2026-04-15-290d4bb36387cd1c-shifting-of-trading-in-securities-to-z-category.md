## Summary

NSE has identified Vikas Lifecare Limited (VIKASLIFE) as non-compliant with Regulation 33 (Submission of Financial Results) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for two consecutive quarters ending September 30, 2025 and December 31, 2025. Trading in VIKASLIFE will be transferred to 'Z' Series (BZ) effective April 27, 2026 unless the company achieves compliance by April 22, 2026. Additionally, 24 other companies already trading in the Z category have also been confirmed as non-compliant under the same regulation for the same two quarters.

## Key Points

- VIKASLIFE (Vikas Lifecare Limited) will move to Z Series (BZ) from April 27, 2026 if it does not comply with Regulation 33 by April 22, 2026
- Non-compliance relates to Regulation 33 — failure to submit Financial Results for Q2 FY26 (Sep 30, 2025) and Q3 FY26 (Dec 31, 2025)
- 24 additional companies already in Z category are confirmed non-compliant for the same two consecutive quarters
- All trades in Z category securities will be settled on Trade for Trade (T2T) basis
- This circular is issued pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, last updated January 30, 2026
- Total of 25 companies impacted by this circular

## Regulatory Changes

This circular enforces provisions of SEBI's Master Circular on SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. Regulation 33 mandates listed companies to submit financial results on a quarterly/half-yearly basis within prescribed timelines. Non-compliance for two consecutive quarters triggers transfer of trading to the Z category (BZ series) under NSE's market surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Vikas Lifecare Limited (VIKASLIFE)**: Must comply with Regulation 33 (submit outstanding Financial Results for Sep 30, 2025 and Dec 31, 2025 quarters) on or before **April 22, 2026** to avoid Z category transfer
- All 24 companies already in Z category must similarly fulfil Regulation 33 compliance to be considered for reclassification out of Z category
- Members of the Exchange are informed to note the settlement change to Trade for Trade basis for all affected Z category securities

## Impact Assessment

**VIKASLIFE (Vikas Lifecare Limited):** High impact. The stock faces transfer to Z category within 12 days unless it files overdue financial results. Z category designation typically results in reduced liquidity, investor caution, and potential de-listing risk if non-compliance continues.

**24 Companies Already in Z Category:** These companies' Z category status is reaffirmed and extended due to continued non-compliance. Companies include notable names such as Gensol Engineering Limited, Future Enterprises Limited, Future Lifestyle Fashions Limited, Reliance Home Finance Limited, IL&FS Transportation Networks Limited, Arshiya Limited, and Educomp Solutions Limited, among others.

**Market Impact:** Trade-for-trade settlement in Z category means no netting of positions, requiring full upfront funds/securities for each trade. This significantly reduces speculative trading and liquidity in these stocks. Institutional investors and certain fund categories may be restricted from holding Z category securities.
