## Summary

NSE has extended the migration deadline for self-service links at the Jivan Vihar, Delhi POP (operated by TNI Sify) from March 21, 2026 to March 28, 2026. This is a continuation of earlier circulars (NSE/MSD/73042 dated February 27, 2026 and NSE/MSD/73282 dated March 13, 2026) regarding the phasing out of existing Sify POP facilities at Kohinoor (Kurla, Mumbai) and Jivan Vihar (Delhi) in favour of new POP locations.

## Key Points

- The migration deadline for self-service links at Jivan Vihar, Delhi POP has been extended to EOD March 28, 2026.
- The extension applies only to **self-service links**; managed links are not mentioned as receiving an extension.
- Substantial progress has already been made by members for self-service link migrations, per confirmation from TNI Sify.
- After March 28, 2026, the existing Jivan Vihar POP will be permanently discontinued by Sify.
- Members must migrate to the new Sify Okhla POP to maintain uninterrupted connectivity to NSE's trading system.
- For queries, members can contact Sify using the Annexure 1 – Sify Handbook details.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an operational update extending a previously announced infrastructure migration timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members subscribed to **self-service** links at the Jivan Vihar, Delhi POP must complete migration to the new Sify Okhla POP by **EOD March 28, 2026**.
- Failure to migrate will result in loss of connectivity to NSE's trading system.
- Members are solely responsible for procuring and maintaining their own network architecture and connectivity.
- NSE acts only as a facilitator; SLAs, uptime, and service quality are the responsibility of Sify (TNI), not NSE.

## Impact Assessment

- **Affected members**: Trading members using self-service connectivity links via the Jivan Vihar, Delhi POP.
- **Operational risk**: Members who do not complete migration by March 28, 2026 will lose connectivity to NSE's trading systems, potentially disrupting trading operations.
- **Market-wide impact**: Low — this affects only a subset of members using a specific POP location in Delhi.
- **Action required**: Immediate — affected members have approximately 8 days from the date of this circular to complete migration.
