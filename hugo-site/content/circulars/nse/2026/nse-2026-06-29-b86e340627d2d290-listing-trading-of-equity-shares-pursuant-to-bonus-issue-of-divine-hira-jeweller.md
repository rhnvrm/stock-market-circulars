## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares issued by Divine Hira Jewellers Limited pursuant to a Bonus Issue. The new shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment with effect from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: DIVINEHIRA
- ISIN: INE0NA501011
- Record Date for the bonus issue: July 02, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: July 03, 2026
- Number of bonus shares issued: 2,60,94,200
- Distinctive Number Range: 13047101 to 39141300
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of new securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified that the bonus shares of Divine Hira Jewellers Limited will be tradeable from July 06, 2026.
- For trading purposes, the security must be identified only by its designated code (DIVINEHIRA).

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 02, 2026
- Deemed Date of Allotment: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing & Trading: July 06, 2026
- Circular Date: June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Divine Hira Jewellers Limited as of the record date (July 02, 2026) will receive bonus shares. The 2,60,94,200 new shares will increase the total share capital and may have a dilutive effect on the share price post-listing on July 06, 2026. The impact is primarily relevant to existing shareholders and traders in DIVINEHIRA stock.
