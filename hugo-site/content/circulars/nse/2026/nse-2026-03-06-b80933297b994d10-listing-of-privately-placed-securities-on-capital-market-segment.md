## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of two privately placed securities on the Capital Market segment effective March 09, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The securities are bonds issued by Sammaan Capital Limited and Bank of Baroda.

## Key Points

- Two privately placed debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from March 09, 2026
- Lot size for both securities is 1 unit
- Securities are identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Circular effective from March 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Circular is issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes when trading these instruments and adhere to the specified lot sizes as per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 09, 2026
