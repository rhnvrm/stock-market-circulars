## Summary

NSE has notified its members that the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF NFO, managed by Mirae Asset Mutual Fund, will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme reopens for ongoing transactions (subscriptions and redemptions) on NSE MF Invest Platform from July 24, 2026.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are also being enabled for the scheme.
- Two plan variants are covered: Direct Growth (ISIN INF769K01QX8) and Regular Growth (ISIN INF769K01QW0), both categorized as Equity.
- The circular is issued under NSE's NMF TM Segment (Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/75352, Circular Ref. No: 1510/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational enablement notice for an existing mutual fund scheme on NSE's mutual fund distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members dealing in mutual funds should ensure their systems and client-facing platforms reflect the reopening of this scheme for ongoing transactions and systematic facilities (SIP/STP/SWP) from the effective date. No additional filings are required from members beyond standard operational readiness.

## Important Dates

- **July 23, 2026**: Circular issue date.
- **July 24, 2026**: Effective date from which the scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP/STP/SWP facilities on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market-wide impact. It primarily affects mutual fund distributors, NSE members handling MF transactions, and investors interested in the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF scheme. There is no direct effect on listed equities, trading mechanisms, or broader market structure.
