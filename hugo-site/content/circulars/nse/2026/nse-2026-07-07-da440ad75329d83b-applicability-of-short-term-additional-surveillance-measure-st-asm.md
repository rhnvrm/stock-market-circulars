## Summary

NSE has notified the applicability of the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework under Circular Ref. No. 510/2026, referencing earlier circulars dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023 and September 20, 2024. Seven securities have been shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I. No securities were shortlisted under Stage II, and no securities moved between Stage I and Stage II in this cycle.

## Key Points

- 7 securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I: DIGITIDE (Digitide Solutions Limited), EEPL (Eppeltone Engineers Limited), GRANDOAK (Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Limited), IFGLEXPOR (IFGL Refractories Limited), NATIONSTD (National Standard (India) Limited), SACHEEROME (Sacheerome Limited), SHAH (Shah Metacorp Limited).
- No securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage II this cycle.
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I this cycle.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework criteria to identify newly qualifying securities as per Exchange circular nos. NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558 and NSE/SURV/64066.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply the applicable margin rate of 50% or the existing margin (whichever is higher), capped at a maximum of 100%, on all open positions in Stage I securities as of July 08, 2026, and on new positions created from July 09, 2026.
- Members must ensure compliance across all other prevailing surveillance measures applicable to these securities.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- Securities list effective: July 08, 2026 (positions as on this date)
- Margin rate applicability: July 09, 2026 (on all open positions as on July 08, 2026 and new positions from July 09, 2026)
- Circular date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Traders and members holding positions in the 7 newly listed Stage I securities face increased margin requirements (50% or existing, whichever higher, capped at 100%) starting July 09, 2026, which will raise the cost of holding or initiating positions in these stocks and may reduce trading volumes and liquidity. The absence of Stage II additions or stage migrations limits the scope of impact to Stage I entrants only. Overall market-wide impact is limited given the small number of securities affected, though it is significant for investors and traders directly holding these specific stocks.
