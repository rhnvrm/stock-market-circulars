## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 ETF NFO from Groww Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The ETF tracks the Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 Index and is offered in both Direct Growth and Regular Growth variants under the equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Two scheme variants available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF666M01PG9) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF666M01PG9)
- Both variants fall under the Equity category
- Allotment date for both schemes: June 19, 2026
- Fund house: Groww Mutual Fund
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/74430, Circular Ref. No: 1397/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 15, 2026 by 3:00 PM.
- Members should facilitate client transactions during the NFO window as per platform norms.

## Important Dates

- NFO Opens: May 29, 2026
- NFO Closes: June 12, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Fund Clearance Deadline: June 15, 2026 by 3:00 PM
- Allotment Date: June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with limited market-wide impact. It provides investors access to a new passive equity ETF focused on smallcap momentum and quality factors via the NSE MF Invest Platform. Members need to operationally prepare for transaction processing and ensure timely fund transfers to the Clearing Corporation account before the June 15 deadline.
