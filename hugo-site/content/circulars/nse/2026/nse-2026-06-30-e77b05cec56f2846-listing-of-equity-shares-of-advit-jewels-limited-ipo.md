## Summary

Advit Jewels Limited (NSE symbol: RAMBHAJO) has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange of India Limited (Capital Market segment) effective July 1, 2026, pursuant to its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shares will initially trade in Series 'BE' and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'EQ' per SEBI circular. The issue price is Rs. 138 per share with a total of 45,810,000 equity shares listed.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** RAMBHAJO
- **ISIN:** INE1SJO01012
- **Series:** BE (to be shifted to EQ per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012)
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 138/- per share
- **Total Securities Listed:** 45,810,000 equity shares
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Effective Date:** July 1, 2026
- The security will be part of the pre-open session for IPO and Other category of scrips

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 — pre-open session and series shifting from BE to EQ

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to use the designated security code (RAMBHAJO) for trading
- Trading must be conducted in the prescribed market lot of 1 share
- Members must observe the pre-open session rules for IPO category scrips
- Lock-in restrictions as per Annexure I must be respected by concerned shareholders

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date:** July 1, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates (various tranches):**
  - July 28, 2026 — 1,794,350 shares
  - September 26, 2026 — 1,794,350 shares
  - November 15, 2026 — 280,000 shares (240,000 + 40,000)
  - January 1, 2027 — 25,361,300 shares (multiple tranches)
  - January 1, 2028 — 8,161,000 shares (multiple tranches)
  - Free (no lock-in) — 8,379,300 shares

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces a new equity scrip (RAMBHAJO) on NSE for Advit Jewels Limited, a jewellery company based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. With 45.81 million shares at an issue price of Rs. 138, the implied market capitalisation at listing is approximately Rs. 6,321.8 million (~Rs. 632 crore). The initial trading series 'BE' (trade-for-trade settlement) limits speculative activity until the shift to 'EQ'. A significant portion of shares (~80%) are under lock-in until at least January 1, 2027, which limits free float in the near term. The registrar is Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai.
