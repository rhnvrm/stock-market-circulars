## Summary

NSE has invoked the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework to include two securities — ASHWINI (Ashwini Container Movers Limited) and BTTL (Bhilwara Technical Textiles Limited) — under Stage I, effective June 30, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from July 2, 2026. No securities were included in Stage IV, and no stage movements (Stage I to II, II to III, I to IV, or II to IV) were recorded in this circular.

## Key Points

- Two securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I: ASHWINI and BTTL, effective June 30, 2026
- 100% margin applicable from July 2, 2026 on all open positions as on July 01, 2026 and on new positions created from July 2, 2026 onwards
- No securities included under Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade segment shift) in this circular
- No stage transitions recorded (Stage I→II, II→III, I→IV, or II→IV are all Nil)
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the Long-Term ASM framework as established by earlier NSE circulars: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). Securities satisfying Stage IV criteria (Criteria VII) are shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis; no such shift applies in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure collection of 100% margin on all positions in ASHWINI and BTTL from July 2, 2026
- Members must apply the margin requirement to open positions held as on July 1, 2026 as well as any new positions created from July 2, 2026 onwards
- Members should communicate applicable surveillance restrictions to their clients holding or intending to take positions in these securities
- For queries, members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of ASHWINI and BTTL in Long-Term ASM Stage I
- **July 1, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **July 2, 2026**: 100% margin becomes applicable on all open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in ASHWINI (Ashwini Container Movers Limited, ISIN: INE1A6Q01010) and BTTL (Bhilwara Technical Textiles Limited, ISIN: INE274K01012) will face a 100% margin requirement, significantly increasing the capital cost of holding these securities. This is likely to reduce liquidity and may cause increased price volatility around the effective date. Since no securities are moved to Stage IV in this circular, there is no immediate segment shift from EQ to BE series. The impact is contained to participants exposed to these two small-cap stocks.
