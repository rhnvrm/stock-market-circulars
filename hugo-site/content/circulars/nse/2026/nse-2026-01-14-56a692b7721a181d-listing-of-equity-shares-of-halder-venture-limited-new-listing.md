## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of equity shares of Halder Venture Limited on the Capital Market segment effective January 19, 2026. The securities will trade under the symbol 'HALDER' in Series 'BE' and will remain in the Trade-for-Trade segment until further notice. A total of 12,438,135 equity shares with face value of Rs. 10/- each will be available for trading with a market lot size of 1 share.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: HALDER
- ISIN: INE115S01010
- Total equity shares: 12,438,135
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Paid-up value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Trading series: BE (Trade-for-Trade segment)
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 12,438,135
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Lock-in details: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code for the stock
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- Securities will remain in Trade-for-Trade segment until further notice from NSE

## Important Dates

- Circular date: January 14, 2026
- Effective date: January 19, 2026
- Financial year: April 1 to March 31

## Impact Assessment

Low impact event. This is a routine new listing that adds one more security to the NSE Capital Market segment. The Trade-for-Trade classification indicates enhanced surveillance, limiting speculative activity. Impact is limited to investors and traders interested in this specific stock. No broader market implications or changes to trading infrastructure.
