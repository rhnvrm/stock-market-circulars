## Summary

HSBC Mutual Fund is launching a Gold ETF NFO (New Fund Offer) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The subscription window is open from March 16, 2026 to March 18, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). Two plan variants are available — Direct and Regular — both under the Growth option. Allotment is scheduled for March 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- HSBC Gold ETF NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 16 to March 18, 2026
- Two schemes offered: HSBC Gold ETF - Growth Direct Plan (ISIN: INF336L01RX2) and HSBC Gold ETF - Growth Regular Plan (ISIN: INF336L01RX2)
- Both schemes categorized under Equity
- Allotment date: March 23, 2026
- Payment deadline: March 20, 2026 by 11:59 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification for the launch of a new mutual fund product on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before March 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM, regardless of payment mode used.

## Important Dates

- **March 16, 2026**: NFO subscription window opens
- **March 18, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription window closes
- **March 20, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **March 23, 2026**: Allotment date for both schemes

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard NFO launch notification relevant only to members of the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to facilitate transactions in HSBC Gold ETF. Investors interested in gold as an asset class have a short 3-day window to subscribe. No broader market or compliance implications.
