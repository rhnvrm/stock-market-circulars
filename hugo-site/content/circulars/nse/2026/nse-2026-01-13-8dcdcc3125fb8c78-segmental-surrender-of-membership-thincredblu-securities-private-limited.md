## Summary

NSE has approved Thincredblu Securities Private Limited's request to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives including IRF segment. The surrender was approved on January 09, 2026. The member's SEBI registration number is INZ000191839.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Thincredblu Securities Private Limited
- SEBI registration number: INZ000191839
- Surrendered segment: Currency Derivatives including IRF
- Approval date: January 09, 2026
- Circular reference: NSE/COMP/72288, Circular 04/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative approval of a voluntary membership surrender request.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this membership change.

## Important Dates

- **January 09, 2026**: Surrender approval date
- **January 13, 2026**: Circular issuance date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving one member's voluntary exit from a specific trading segment. No operational changes required for other market participants beyond awareness of the membership status change.
