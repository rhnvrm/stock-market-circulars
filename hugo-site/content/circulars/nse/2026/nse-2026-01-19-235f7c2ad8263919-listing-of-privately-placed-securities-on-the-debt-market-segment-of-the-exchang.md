## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment with effect from January 19, 2026. The circular lists fresh issues of commercial papers from Godrej Industries Limited, Aditya Birla Capital Limited, Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited, Kisetsu Saison Finance (India) Private Limited, and a debt security from Sammaan Capital Limited. It also includes a re-issue of commercial paper by ICICI Securities Limited.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue of 5 commercial papers totaling Rs 8,000 crore (16,000 securities of Rs 5 lakh face value)
- Fresh issue of 1 debt security (non-convertible debenture) worth Rs 115 crore
- Re-issue of 1 commercial paper worth Rs 50 crore
- All securities admitted to trading on NSE Debt Market Segment effective January 19, 2026
- Allotment date for all securities: January 16, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed debt securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 16, 2026 (all securities)
- **Listing/Trading Date**: January 19, 2026 (effective date)
- **Maturity Dates**: Range from February 17, 2026 to January 16, 2036

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing notification affecting only the debt market segment. The securities are privately placed and targeted at institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Members can now trade these debt instruments on the NSE debt platform. No changes to trading systems or procedures required.

**Liquidity**: The securities are privately placed and typically have limited secondary market trading activity compared to public issues.
