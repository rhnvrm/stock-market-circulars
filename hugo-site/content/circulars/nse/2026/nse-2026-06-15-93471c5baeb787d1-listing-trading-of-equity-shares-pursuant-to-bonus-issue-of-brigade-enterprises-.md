## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares allotted pursuant to a bonus issue by Brigade Enterprises Limited (Symbol: BRIGADE) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 19, 2026. A total of 8,15,40,595 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings under ISIN INE791I01019.

## Key Points

- 8,15,40,595 bonus equity shares of Brigade Enterprises Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading commences on June 19, 2026
- Symbol: BRIGADE | ISIN: INE791I01019
- Distinctive number range: 244621786 to 326162380
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74687 | Circular Ref. No: 0953/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the admission of securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (BRIGADE) for all trading on the NSE system.
- No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** June 18, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 19, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Brigade Enterprises Limited who held shares as of the record date (June 17, 2026) will receive bonus shares credited on June 18, 2026. The bonus shares become tradeable from June 19, 2026. The addition of over 81.5 million shares increases the total float and may result in a proportional price adjustment on the ex-bonus date. No impact on non-shareholders or broader market operations.
