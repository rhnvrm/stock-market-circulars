## Summary

NSE has identified Salasar Exteriors and Contour Limited (Symbol: SECL) as non-compliant with Regulation 76 (Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report) under SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018 for two consecutive quarters ending December 31, 2025 and March 31, 2026. If the company does not comply by June 23, 2026, its securities will be transferred to Z Series (SZ) with trade-for-trade settlement from June 29, 2026. Additionally, 14 other companies already in Z category have been flagged for the same non-compliance.

## Key Points

- SECL (Salasar Exteriors and Contour Limited) is being moved to 'Z' Series (SZ) from June 29, 2026, subject to compliance by June 23, 2026
- Non-compliance pertains to Regulation 76 — Submission of Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report — for two consecutive quarters: Q3 (Dec 31, 2025) and Q4 (Mar 31, 2026)
- 14 other companies already trading in Z category are also non-compliant under the same regulation; they will continue in BZ Series from June 29, 2026
- All trades in Z category securities will be settled on a Trade for Trade (TFT) basis
- Action is pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI's Master Circular on Depositories and Participants, NSE is invoking the provision to transfer non-compliant securities to the Z category. The shift to Z (SZ) Series imposes Trade for Trade settlement, eliminating netting benefits and increasing settlement obligations for traders.

## Compliance Requirements

- **SECL (Salasar Exteriors and Contour Limited)** must submit the Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report as required under Regulation 76 of the SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018 on or before **June 23, 2026** to avoid transfer to Z category
- The following 14 companies are already in Z category for the same non-compliance and must similarly address their Regulation 76 obligations:
  1. Ankit Metal & Power Limited (NSE/CML/64541)
  2. Dharan Infra-Epc Limited (NSE/CML/70317)
  3. Future Enterprises Limited (NSE/CML/55207)
  4. Future Lifestyle Fashions Limited (NSE/CML/56081)
  5. Gensol Engineering Limited (NSE/CML/70808)
  6. Housing Development and Infrastructure Limited (NSE/CML/43626)
  7. Impex Ferro Tech Limited (NSE/CML/64541)
  8. Lakshmi Precision Screws Limited (NSE/CML/38892)
  9. Morarjee Textiles Limited (NSE/CML/64541)
  10. Ortel Communications Limited (NSE/CML/42146)
  11. Rajvir Industries Limited (NSE/CML/49924)
  12. Reliance Home Finance Limited (NSE/CML/70317)

  14. SKIL Infrastructure Limited (NSE/CML/62406)

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE
- **June 23, 2026**: Deadline for SECL to comply with Regulation 76 to avoid Z category transfer
- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date for transfer of SECL to Z Series (SZ), and continuation of 14 other companies in BZ Series, if non-compliance persists

## Impact Assessment

For **SECL investors and traders**: A transfer to Z category means trade-for-trade settlement, significantly reducing liquidity and preventing intraday trading or netting of positions. This typically leads to reduced market participation, wider bid-ask spreads, and downward price pressure.

For the **14 companies already in Z category**: Their continued listing under BZ Series reaffirms existing trading restrictions. No additional impact beyond what is already in effect, but underscores unresolved compliance issues.

For **market participants broadly**: This circular is a reminder of NSE's active enforcement of SEBI depository compliance norms. Members should monitor their portfolio holdings for Z category transfers ahead of June 29, 2026.
