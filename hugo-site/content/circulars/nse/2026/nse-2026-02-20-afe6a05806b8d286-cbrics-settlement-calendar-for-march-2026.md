## Summary

NSE Clearing Ltd. has published the CBRICS (Corporate Bond Reporting and Integrated Clearing System) settlement calendar for March 2026. Participants and custodians are directed to transfer securities using Settlement mode 'On-market' for the listed dates.

## Key Points

- Market Type: Corporate Bond; CM Name: NSE Clearing Ltd.; CM-BP ID: IN568177
- Settlement covers 18 trading days in March 2026 via NSDL/CDSL transfer
- Settlement mode must be set as 'On-market'
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) are no longer valid for CBRICS settlement effective September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine monthly operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and custodians must use the published settlement dates and corresponding settlement numbers when transferring securities on the CBRICS platform
- Settlement mode must be selected as 'On-market'
- The deprecated DP ID (IN001002-10000617) must not be used
