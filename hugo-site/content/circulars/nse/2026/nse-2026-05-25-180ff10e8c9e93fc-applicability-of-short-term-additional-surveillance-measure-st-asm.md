## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 388/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74391) dated May 25, 2026 announcing the inclusion of 12 securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I, effective May 26, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements apply from May 27, 2026. No securities have been added to ST-ASM Stage II, and no securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II in this circular.

## Key Points

- 12 securities included under ST-ASM Stage I with effect from May 26, 2026
- Enhanced margin of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) applies from May 27, 2026
- Applies to all open positions as on May 26, 2026, and all new positions created from May 27, 2026
- No securities added to ST-ASM Stage II (Nil)
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II (Nil)
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against any company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to earlier NSE circulars on ASM: NSE/SURV/39265 (October 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (December 4, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (April 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (September 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024). Securities satisfying the ST-ASM criteria are subject to the prescribed margin enhancements as detailed below.

**ST-ASM Stage I:** Minimum margin of 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.

**ST-ASM Stage II:** Minimum margin of 100% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply the enhanced margin requirements for the listed Stage I securities on open positions as on May 26, 2026, and on new positions from May 27, 2026
- Members must ensure margin collection is aligned with the higher of 50% or existing margin for all Stage I securities
- Members should communicate relevant implications to clients holding or intending to trade in these securities
- For queries, members may contact surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026:** Circular issued
- **May 26, 2026:** Securities included under ST-ASM Stage I (effective date for shortlisting)
- **May 27, 2026:** Enhanced margin requirements apply on all open positions (as of May 26, 2026) and on new positions created from this date

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of 12 securities under ST-ASM Stage I significantly increases the cost of carrying positions in these stocks. Traders and investors holding these securities will face a mandatory minimum 50% margin (capped at 100%), which may trigger margin calls for those with existing leveraged positions.
