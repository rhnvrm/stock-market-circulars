## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the list of securities under its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Three securities — Aartech Solonics Limited (AARTECH), DB (International) Stock Brokers Limited (DBSTOCKBRO), and RFBL Flexi Pack Limited (RFBL) — have been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I for satisfying the applicable criteria. No securities have moved between Stage I and Stage II, and no securities have been excluded from the framework in this update. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM (including existing Stage I and Stage II names such as AAREYDRUGS and ACCORD Synergy) has also been published.

## Key Points

- AARTECH, DBSTOCKBRO, and RFBL are newly added to ESM Stage I.
- These three securities will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST) effective July 10, 2026.
- No securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I in this cycle.
- No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.
- A minimum 100% margin will apply to all open positions as of July 09, 2026, and to new positions created from July 10, 2026, for the newly added securities.
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ESM is a market surveillance action and is not to be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a periodic update under NSE's existing ESM framework (referencing prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational update applying existing ESM criteria to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions in AARTECH, DBSTOCKBRO, and RFBL from the effective dates.
- Members must ensure trading in these securities is conducted only in the Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment from July 10, 2026, rather than the Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) segment.
- Members should refer to the NSE ESM FAQ page for procedural guidance and direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 09, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin; Stage II periodic call auction price band effective (if applicable).
- July 10, 2026: AARTECH, DBSTOCKBRO, and RFBL shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment; 100% margin applies to new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The move to Trade-for-Trade with 100% margin significantly reduces intraday trading flexibility and liquidity for AARTECH, DBSTOCKBRO, and RFBL, likely increasing price volatility and discouraging speculative trading in these names. The impact is contained to these specific small-cap/micro-cap securities and does not affect the broader market, but shareholders and traders in these three stocks should expect reduced liquidity and higher capital requirements for trading.
