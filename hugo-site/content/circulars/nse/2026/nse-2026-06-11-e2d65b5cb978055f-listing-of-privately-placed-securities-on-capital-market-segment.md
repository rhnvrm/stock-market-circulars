## Summary

NSE notifies admission of three privately placed Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective June 12, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A. The securities are issued by Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited (two tranches) and REC Limited.

## Key Points

- Three NCDs admitted to NSE Capital Market segment effective June 12, 2026
- All securities trade in Series N0 with a market lot size of 1
- Issuers: Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited (two series) and REC Limited (one series)
- Coupon rates range from 7.38% to 8.00%
- Maturities span 2029 to 2036
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes for trading. Trading in these securities must be conducted in the specified lot sizes as per the Annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 12, 2026 (all three securities admitted to dealings)

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing notification with limited market impact. The three securities are:
