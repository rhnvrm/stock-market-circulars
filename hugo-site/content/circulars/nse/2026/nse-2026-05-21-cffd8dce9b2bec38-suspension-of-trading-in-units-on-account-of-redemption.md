## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in four SBI Mutual Fund Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) Series 74 – 1243D scheme units on account of maturity redemption. Trading in these units will be suspended effective May 29, 2026, with the last trading session on May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four SBI FMP Series 74 – 1243D scheme units will be suspended from trading on NSE
- Suspension is triggered by maturity redemption (MF Redemption)
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- All four schemes share the same suspension date and last trading date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension is carried out in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities from trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note that trading in the listed units will cease after May 27, 2026 (closing hours)
- No further orders should be placed in these securities on or after the suspension date of May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational action affecting unitholders of four SBI FMP Series 74 – 1243D schemes listed on NSE. Fixed maturity plans have a predetermined tenure, and suspension upon redemption is an expected lifecycle event. Investors holding these units should be aware that the last opportunity to trade on the exchange is during closing hours of May 27, 2026. Redemption proceeds will be disbursed by SBI Mutual Fund as per the scheme terms.
