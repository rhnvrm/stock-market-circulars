## Summary

PPFAS Mutual Fund's Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund NFO will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from January 19, 2026 to January 30, 2026 until 11:59 PM. The fund offers six plan variants across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options. The allotment date is scheduled for February 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: January 19, 2026 to January 30, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: February 4, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- ISINs assigned: INF879O01332, INF879O01365, INF879O01357, INF879O01340, INF879O01381, INF879O01373
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by February 3, 2026 (11:59 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before February 3, 2026 by 11:59 PM
- Members should facilitate NFO transactions for investors during the subscription period

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **January 30, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closes
- **February 3, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds receipt
- **February 4, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new large-cap equity investment option through PPFAS Mutual Fund. The circular is informational for NSE members to enable transactions on the platform. No impact on stock market operations or trading activities.
