## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a privately placed security on its Capital Market segment, effective July 08, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The circular specifies the designated security code and lot size for the newly admitted security as required under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Security admitted: NABARD 7.16% 2029 Series 27A bonds (Fresh Issue)
- Issuer: National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD)
- Symbol: 716NBRD29, Series: N0
- Market Lot: 1
- Coupon Rate: 7.16%
- Date of Maturity: 14-Dec-2029
- ISIN Code: INE261F08EV2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify and trade the security only by its designated code (716NBRD29-N0) and in the specified lot size (1) from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date: July 08, 2026
- Bond Maturity Date: December 14, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of a single privately placed debt instrument with minimal market lot (1) and no equity implications. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors trading NABARD bonds; no broader market or retail investor impact expected.
