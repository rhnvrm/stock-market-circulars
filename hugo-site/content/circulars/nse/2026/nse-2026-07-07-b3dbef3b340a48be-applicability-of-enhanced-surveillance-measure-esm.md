## Summary

NSE has notified the applicability of the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework for a fresh set of securities. Seven securities have been newly added to ESM Stage-I, and two securities have moved from Stage-I to Stage-II. No securities are being excluded from the framework in this cycle, and no securities are moving from Stage-II back to Stage-I.

## Key Points

- 7 securities added to ESM Stage-I effective July 08, 2026: BTTL (Bhilwara Technical Textiles Limited), GINNIFILA (Ginni Filaments Limited), IVP (IVP Limited), KAUSHALYA (Kaushalya Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited), MADHUSUDAN (Madhusudan Masala Limited), MAZDA (Mazda Limited), and SRIVASAVI (Srivasavi Adhesive Tapes Limited).
- These 7 securities will shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST) effective July 09, 2026.
- 2 securities move from ESM Stage-I to Stage-II effective July 08, 2026: ACCORD (Accord Synergy Limited) and SHIVAMAUTO (Shivam Autotech Limited, as per BSE).
- No securities are moving from Stage-II to Stage-I in this cycle.
- No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle (Annexure II is nil).
- ESM Stage-II securities will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, effective July 08, 2026.
- ESM classification is a surveillance measure only and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under the existing ESM framework established via earlier circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of existing criteria to update the list of covered securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of July 08, 2026, and on new positions created from July 09, 2026, for the 7 newly added Stage-I securities.
- Members must ensure the 7 newly added securities are moved from EQ/SM to BE/ST series effective July 09, 2026.
- Members must apply Trade-for-Trade settlement with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction for the 2 securities moving to Stage-II, effective July 08, 2026.
- The ESM framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2026: New ESM Stage-I and Stage-II classifications take effect; 100% margin applies to open positions as of this date; Stage-II price band/call auction rules take effect.
- July 09, 2026: Newly added Stage-I securities shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) segment; 100% margin applies to new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement and imposition of 100% margins will significantly reduce trading liquidity and increase transaction costs for the 7 newly listed Stage-I securities (BTTL, GINNIFILA, IVP, KAUSHALYA, MADHUSUDAN, MAZDA, SRIVASAVI). The move of ACCORD and SHIVAMAUTO to Stage-II adds further trading restrictions via price band and call auction mechanisms, likely increasing price discovery friction and reducing intraday volatility for these names. Investors and traders holding positions in these securities should account for reduced exit liquidity and higher capital requirements going forward.
