## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued equity shares of two SME EMERGE segment companies — Sar Televenture Limited (SARTELE) and Viaz Tyres Limited (VIAZ) — effective April 16, 2026. Both listings arise from preferential allotments and include lock-in periods restricting immediate trading of the newly listed shares.

## Key Points

- 469,500 equity shares of Sar Televenture Limited (SARTELE) admitted to SME EMERGE segment with effect from April 16, 2026
- 870,000 equity shares of Viaz Tyres Limited (VIAZ) admitted to SME EMERGE segment with effect from April 16, 2026
- Both issuances are from preferential allotments and carry lock-in expiry dates
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of respective companies
- Trading in these securities will use designated security codes with specified lot sizes
- ISIN activation follows SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 for additional share issues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is executed under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes (SARTELE, VIAZ) for all trading in these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes: 500 for SARTELE and 2,000 for VIAZ
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed — SARTELE shares locked until November 14, 2026; VIAZ shares locked until December 14, 2027

## Important Dates

| Event | SARTELE | VIAZ |
|---|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | January 22, 2026 | February 18, 2026 |
| Effective Listing Date | April 16, 2026 | April 16, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry Date | November 14, 2026 | December 14, 2027 |

- ISIN: INE0PUC01020 | Series: SM | Market Lot: 500
- Distinctive numbers: 49078001 to 49547500

**Viaz Tyres Limited (VIAZ):**
- 870,000 new shares listed at an issue price of Rs. 70 (face value Rs. 10)
- ISIN: INE0MO401019 | Series: SM | Market Lot: 2,000
