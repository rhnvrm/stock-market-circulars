## Summary

Singer India Limited (BSE: 505729, ISIN: INE638A01035) is being newly listed on NSE. A pre-listing shareholding pattern has been filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations. The report date is 06-03-2026, with the original listing date on BSE being 28-04-1982. The company is not classified as an SME or PSU.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 62,219,117
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 18,967,940 shares (30.49%)
- Public shareholding: 43,251,177 shares (69.51%)
- Outstanding ESOPs: 1,250,000 shares (held by public shareholders)
- Total diluted share capital: 63,469,117 shares
- Shares held in dematerialized form: 62,154,652 out of 62,219,117
- Locked-in shares: 563,578 (0.91% of total)
- Significant beneficial owners exist (Yes)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, pledged shares, or differential voting rights

## Regulatory Changes

Pre-listing shareholding pattern filed as required under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations prior to commencement of trading on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Singer India Limited has filed the mandatory pre-listing shareholding pattern disclosure.
- The company must ensure compliance with NSE listing requirements before trading commences.
- Demat conversion is near-complete; remaining physical shares (64,465 shares) may need to be resolved.

## Important Dates

- Report Date (Pre-listing): 06-03-2026
- Original BSE Listing Date: 28-04-1982
- NSE Listing: New listing as of circular date (2026-03-17)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Singer India Limited on NSE provides existing shareholders and new investors an additional exchange for trading. With 69.51% public float and near-complete dematerialization, liquidity is expected to be reasonable. The presence of outstanding ESOPs (1,250,000 shares) could result in modest future dilution. No pledging or encumbrances on promoter shares is a positive governance indicator.
