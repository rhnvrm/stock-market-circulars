## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a privately placed debt security issued by Tata Capital Limited on the capital market segment, effective May 25, 2026. The security is a T-bill linked NCD (symbol: TACA29, Series N0) maturing on February 21, 2029, with ISIN INE976I07DE9.

## Key Points

- Tata Capital Limited's NCD (TACA29) admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from May 25, 2026
- Issue description: TACA Tbill lkd 2029 Sr B
- Coupon rate is T-bill linked (floating)
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Security to be identified by its designated code TACA29 for all trading purposes
- Governed under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular acts as a standard admission notification pursuant to existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must use the designated security code TACA29 (Series N0) for trading this instrument
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new security effective May 25, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date (Listing):** May 25, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** February 21, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact. This is a routine listing of a single privately placed NCD by Tata Capital Limited. The T-bill linked coupon structure makes it relevant to institutional investors seeking floating-rate debt exposure. No changes to existing trading rules or settlement processes are introduced.
