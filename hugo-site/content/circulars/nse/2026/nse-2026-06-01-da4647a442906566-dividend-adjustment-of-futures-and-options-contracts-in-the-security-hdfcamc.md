## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts for HDFC Asset Management Company Limited (symbol: HDFCAMC) following a dividend declaration of Rs 54 per share (face value Rs 5). The ex-date and effective date for this adjustment is June 5, 2026. Members are required to load updated contract files before trading commences on the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Symbol: HDFCAMC (HDFC Asset Management Company Limited)
- Corporate action: Dividend of Rs 54/- per share on face value of Rs 5/-
- Ex-date / Effective date: June 5, 2026
- Revised option strike prices will be communicated one day prior to the ex-date
- Adjusted strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places, rounded to nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively
- Updated contract details will be available in the latest contract.gz file

## Regulatory Changes

In accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments to futures and options contracts, revised option strike prices, futures prices, lot size, and quantity freeze limits will be recalculated. The adjustment methodology will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications **before trading on June 5, 2026**.
- Files are available from the `faoftp/faocommon` directory on the Extranet server.
- Files are also available on the NSE website at: https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Adjustment details can be referenced at: https://www.nseindia.com/products-services/equity-derivatives-corporate-actions-adjustments

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 4, 2026** (one day prior to ex-date): Revised option strike prices and updated contract/spread files to be made available
- **June 5, 2026**: Ex-date and effective date — all F&O contract adjustments become effective

## Impact Assessment

Traders and members holding open Futures and Options positions in HDFCAMC will see revised strike prices and contract parameters effective June 5, 2026. The dividend of Rs 54/- (10.8x face value) is significant, meaning the strike price adjustments could be material. Members must proactively update their trading systems with the new contract files to avoid trading on stale contract parameters, which could lead to erroneous orders or position mismatches.
