## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Groww Small Cap Fund NFO by Groww Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from January 08, 2026 to January 22, 2026 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for January 30, 2026. Six scheme variants are being offered covering both Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.

## Key Points

- Groww Mutual Fund launching Small Cap Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under Equity category
- Subscription period: January 08, 2026 to January 22, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: January 30, 2026 for all variants
- Clear funds must be received by January 27, 2026 at 07:00 PM

## Scheme Details

**Direct Plans:**
1. Groww Small Cap Fund Direct - Growth (INF666M01NJ8)
2. Groww Small Cap Fund Direct - IDCW Payout (INF666M01NK6)
3. Groww Small Cap Fund Direct - IDCW Reinvestment (INF666M01NL4)

**Regular Plans:**
4. Groww Small Cap Fund Regular - Growth (INF666M01NM2)
5. Groww Small Cap Fund Regular - IDCW Payout (INF666M01NN0)
6. Groww Small Cap Fund Regular - IDCW Reinvestment (INF666M01NO8)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a new product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 27, 2026 at 07:00 PM
- Funds must be available for the NFO subscription process

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: January 08, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: January 22, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Fund Payment Deadline**: January 27, 2026 (07:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The circular provides investment opportunity for investors interested in small-cap equity exposure through Groww Mutual Fund. No impact on existing securities, trading operations, or market structure. Members need to ensure operational readiness for processing NFO subscriptions and timely fund settlements.
