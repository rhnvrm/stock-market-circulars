## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in five series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited, effective July 13, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The suspension is due to the maturity of these debt securities.

## Key Points

- Five NCD series of Sammaan Capital Limited will be suspended from trading
- Reason for suspension in all five cases: Maturity of the securities
- Suspension takes effect from July 13, 2026
- Affected securities and ISINs:
  - 940SCL26C — INE148I07ON3
  - 990SCL26I — INE148I07OR4
  - 902SCL26I — INE148I07OQ6
  - 948SCL26I — INE148I07OP8
  - SCLZC26B — INE148I07OS2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notification issued under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A governing suspension of trading in securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease trading in the listed NCD ISINs of Sammaan Capital Limited from the effective date. No further action is required from investors beyond standard maturity redemption processes through the company/registrar.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 09, 2026
- Effective Date of Suspension: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of these specific Sammaan Capital Limited NCD series, as trading suspension upon maturity is a standard, procedural exchange action. There is no impact on equity trading of Sammaan Capital Limited or on the broader market. Debenture holders should expect redemption proceeds through the company's normal maturity payout process rather than continued exchange trading.
