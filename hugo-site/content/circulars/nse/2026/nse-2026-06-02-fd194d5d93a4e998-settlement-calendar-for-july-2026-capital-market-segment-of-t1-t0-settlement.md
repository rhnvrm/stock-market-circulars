## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement calendar for July 2026 for the Capital Market Segment, covering five settlement types: Normal Rolling T+1 (Type M), Normal Auction T+1 (Type A), Trade for Trade T+1 (Type Z), ITP for SME T+1 (Type B), and Normal Rolling T+0 (Type 5). This calendar is issued pursuant to Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers the full month of July 2026 across five settlement types
- Normal Rolling T+1 (Type M) settlements run from Settlement No. 2026121 (trades on 1-Jul-26) through 2026143 (trades on 31-Jul-26, settling 3-Aug-26)
- Each T+1 settlement sees custodial confirmation on the day after the trade date, with settlement the following business day
- Weekends are excluded; settlement skips over non-trading days (e.g., trades on 3-Jul-26 settle on 6-Jul-26)
- T+0 settlement schedule (Type 5) is provided in Annexure E for same-day settlement eligible trades
- Auction settlement (Type A), Trade for Trade (Type Z), and ITP for SME (Type B) schedules are provided in Annexures B, C, and D respectively
- Contact for queries: securities_ops@nsccl.co.in or 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular issued under Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997) to notify members of scheduled settlement dates for the upcoming month.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the published settlement schedules for July 2026
- Custodial confirmations must be submitted by the dates specified for each settlement number
- Participants should plan securities and funds obligations in line with the published settlement dates

## Important Dates

- **First settlement of July 2026:** Settlement No. 2026121 — Trade Date: 1-Jul-26, Settlement Date: 2-Jul-26
- **Last settlement of July 2026:** Settlement No. 2026143 — Trade Date: 31-Jul-26, Settlement Date: 3-Aug-26
- Settlements span 23 trading days across July 2026 (Settlement Nos. 2026121–2026143 for Type M)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notification with medium operational impact for market participants. Members, custodians, and PCMs must update their internal systems and settlement planning processes to reflect the July 2026 calendar. The inclusion of both T+1 and T+0 settlement schedules reflects the ongoing dual-track settlement framework. No market-wide disruptions or special provisions are indicated for July 2026.
