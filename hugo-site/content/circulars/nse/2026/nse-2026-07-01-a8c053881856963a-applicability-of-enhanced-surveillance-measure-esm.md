## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Sixteen securities have been newly shortlisted for ESM Stage I, and additional securities (led by AVATAR Industries Limited) are moving from Stage I to Stage II. The measures take effect from July 02-03, 2026, and securities qualifying under ESM will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST).

## Key Points

- 16 securities newly added to ESM Stage I: ARMOUR, BHADORA, DELPHIFX, DHARARAIL, EMMBI, GEECEE, KILITCH, MICEL, NECCLTD, OMFREIGHT, SHYAMCENT, SUTLEJTEX, SYNOPTICS, URAVIDEF, VARDHACRLC, and VELJAN (VELJAN as per BSE data).
- These securities shall attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of July 02, 2026, and on new positions created from July 03, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure I are shifting from EQ/SM series to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) series effective July 03, 2026.
- A separate list of securities (including AVATAR Industries Limited) is moving from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective July 02, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, effective July 02, 2026.
- Securities eligible to move out of the ESM framework are listed in Annexure II (not detailed in extracted content).
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ESM is based purely on market surveillance criteria and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under the existing ESM framework established via earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315). No new regulatory framework is introduced; it applies existing ESM criteria to a revised list of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions in the newly added ESM Stage I securities from the specified dates.
- Members must ensure trading in shifted securities occurs under the BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) series rather than EQ/SM.
- Members must apply the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism for securities moved to Stage II.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Minimum 100% margin applies to all open positions in newly added ESM Stage I securities; Stage II price band and call auction mechanism becomes effective.
- July 03, 2026: Minimum 100% margin applies to new positions created in ESM Stage I securities; shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment takes effect.

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of a 100% margin requirement and shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement significantly restricts trading activity, increases capital requirements for holding positions, and reduces liquidity for the affected securities. Investors and traders in ARMOUR, BHADORA, DELPHIFX, DHARARAIL, EMMBI, GEECEE, KILITCH, MICEL, NECCLTD, OMFREIGHT, SHYAMCENT, SUTLEJTEX, SYNOPTICS, URAVIDEF, VARDHACRLC, VELJAN, and AVATAR should expect reduced trading volumes and higher transaction costs. This measure is a defensive surveillance action and does not reflect a fundamental assessment of the companies involved, but it can still negatively affect market perception and short-term price behavior.
