## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of 7,244 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (Symbol: LLOYDSENT) on the Capital Market segment, effective May 13, 2026. The shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE080I01025.

## Key Points

- 7,244 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from May 13, 2026
- Shares represent conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- Symbol: LLOYDSENT | Series: EQ | ISIN: INE080I01025
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Pari passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 1,494,194,859 to 1,494,202,102
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which govern admission of securities to dealings and lot size specifications respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code LLOYDSENT (Series: EQ) for all transactions in these shares
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The temporary ISIN IN8080I01024 (used for dematerialized credit at allotment) will be migrated to the permanent ISIN INE080I01025 per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. Only 7,244 shares are being listed, which is a negligible addition to the total share capital of Lloyds Enterprises Limited. This is a procedural listing arising from conversion of partly paid shares and does not indicate fresh fundraising or dilution of significance. Market participants trading in LLOYDSENT should note the marginal increase in freely tradable share count from May 13, 2026.
