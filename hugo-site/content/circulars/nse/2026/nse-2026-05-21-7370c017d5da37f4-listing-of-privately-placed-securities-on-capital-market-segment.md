## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of privately placed securities of Toyota Financial Services India Limited to dealings on its Capital Market segment effective May 22, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Toyota Financial Services India Limited NCD (Symbol: 761TFSIL28, Series: N0) is listed on the NSE Capital Market segment from May 22, 2026.
- The issue is described as TFSIL 7.61% 2028 Sr S60 with a coupon rate of 7.61%.
- Market lot size is 1 unit.
- ISIN: INE692Q07647.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, governing admission and lot sizes of privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security code (761TFSIL28) for trading and transact only in the specified lot size of 1 unit as outlined in the Annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** May 19, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact. It enables trading in a single privately placed NCD of Toyota Financial Services India Limited on NSE's Capital Market segment. Investors in fixed-income instruments can now trade this security with a 7.61% coupon maturing in May 2028.
