## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of various privately placed debt securities on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange, effective July 09, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The securities include fresh issues of commercial papers (CPs) from Godrej Properties Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Tata Projects Limited, and Axis Securities Limited, as well as non-convertible debentures from L&T Finance Limited and Jio Credit Limited.

## Key Points

- **Commercial Papers admitted:**
  - Godrej Properties Limited – GPL CP 07/10/26 Sr 50 (ISIN: INE484J14E49), 2,000 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,91,749, allotted 08-Jul-2026, redemption 07-Oct-2026
  - Godrej Properties Limited – GPL CP 25/08/26 Sr 51 (ISIN: INE484J14E56), 2,500 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,95,665.50, allotted 08-Jul-2026, redemption 25-Aug-2026
  - Godrej Industries Limited – GIL CP 07/10/26 Sr 38 (ISIN: INE233A149I9), 1,500 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,92,147, allotted 08-Jul-2026, redemption 07-Oct-2026
  - Tata Projects Limited – TPL CP 06/10/26 Sr 219 (ISIN: INE725H14EF3), 4,000 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,91,611.50, allotted 08-Jul-2026, redemption 06-Oct-2026
  - Axis Securities Limited – ASL CP 26/10/26 Sr 27 (ISIN: INE110O14IY6), 6,000 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,90,048.50, allotted 09-Jul-2026, redemption 26-Oct-2026
- **Other Debt Securities (Debentures) admitted:**
  - L&T Finance Limited – LTFL 7.8384% 2029 Sr D (ISIN: INE498L07228), 50,000 units, face/paid-up value ₹1,00,000, multiple issue prices (15,000 units at ₹1,00,000 and 35,000 units at ₹1,00,100), coupon rate 7.8384%, allotted 07-Jul-2026, redemption 28-Sep-2029, no call/put option
  - Jio Credit Limited – JCL 7.78% 2029 Sr VII (ISIN: INE282H07075), 96,500 units, face/paid-up value ₹1,00,000, issue price ₹1,00,006.54, coupon rate 7.78%, allotted 07-Jul-2026, redemption 07-Jul-2029, no call/put option

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations for admission of privately placed securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from listed companies or investors as a result of this circular. Members dealing in the Debt Market Segment should note the security codes and terms for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: July 09, 2026
- Godrej Properties CP (Sr 50 & 51) and Godrej Industries CP (Sr 38): allotted 08-Jul-2026
- Tata Projects CP (Sr 219): allotted 08-Jul-2026
- Axis Securities CP (Sr 27): allotted 09-Jul-2026
- L&T Finance and Jio Credit debentures: allotted 07-Jul-2026
- Redemption dates range from 25-Aug-2026 (earliest CP) to 07-Jul-2029 (latest debenture)

## Impact Assessment

This circular is a procedural listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. It primarily concerns debt market participants and members dealing in privately placed commercial papers and non-convertible debentures, providing them with security codes and terms needed for trading on the Debt Market Segment. There is no direct impact on equity markets or listed company stock prices.
