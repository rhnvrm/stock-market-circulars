## Summary

NSE has notified all members of an adjustment to the market lot for P S Raj Steels Limited (Symbol: PSRAJ), an SME-platform listed company, on account of a stock split. The split ratio is 5:1 with an adjustment factor of 5, effective July 24, 2026, which is also the record date. The adjusted market lot has been set at 2500 shares.

## Key Points

- Symbol: PSRAJ (P S Raj Steels Limited)
- Corporate action type: Stock Split
- Split ratio: 5:1
- Adjustment factor: 5
- New adjusted market lot: 2500 shares
- Ex-date/Effective date and Record date: July 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is a routine corporate action adjustment under NSE's Capital Market Segment procedures to align market lot size with the stock split.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members must load the updated security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII-Security File.gz in their trading applications before trading commences on the effective date (July 24, 2026). These files are available in the common/NTNEAT directory on the Extranet server, and the MII-Security File.gz is also published on the NSE website under the all-reports section.

## Important Dates

- Ex-date/Effective Date: July 24, 2026
- Record Date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and shareholders of P S Raj Steels Limited on the SME platform. Members must update their trading systems with the revised security file ahead of the effective date to ensure accurate order lot sizes and avoid trading disruptions for this security. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
