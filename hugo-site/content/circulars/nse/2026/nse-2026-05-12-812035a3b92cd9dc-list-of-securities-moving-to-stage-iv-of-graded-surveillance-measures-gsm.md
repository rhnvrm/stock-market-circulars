## Summary

NSE has moved two securities — GRAND FOUNDRY LIMITED (GFSTEELS, ISIN: INE534A01028) and UNIVA FOODS LIMITED (UNIVAFOODS, ISIN: INE275F01019) — to Stage IV of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework, effective May 13, 2026. Stage IV represents the most stringent level of surveillance, prohibiting upward price movement and restricting trading to once per week.

## Key Points

- Two securities elevated to GSM Stage IV: GFSTEELS (Grand Foundry Limited) and UNIVAFOODS (Univa Foods Limited)
- Effective date: May 13, 2026
- Trading restricted to Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement — no netting off allowed
- Price band capped at 5% or lower as applicable
- Trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or the 1st trading day of the week)
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 100% of trade value must be deposited by buyers
- No upward price movement permitted
- Members advised to exercise adequate caution while trading these securities

## Regulatory Changes

These securities are being moved to Stage IV under the GSM framework, building on the series of prior circulars (NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, and NSE/SURV/73167). Stage IV imposes the maximum available restrictions within the GSM framework, adding a "no upward movement" condition on top of Stage III requirements.

**GSM Stage Summary:**

| Stage | Key Restrictions |
|-------|------------------|
| I | 100% margin, 5% price band |
| II | Trade for Trade, 5% price band, 50% ASD |
| III | Trade for Trade, weekly trading, 100% ASD |
| IV | Trade for Trade, weekly trading, 100% ASD, no upward movement |

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members** must ensure all trades in GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS are settled on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off
- **Buyers** must deposit ASD equivalent to 100% of trade value prior to trading
- Members must inform their clients of the restrictions and ensure compliance with the 5% price band
- Trading is only permissible on Mondays or the first trading day of the week
- Members should direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **May 12, 2026** — Circular issued
- **May 13, 2026** — Stage IV GSM restrictions effective for GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS

## Impact Assessment

Both securities face severely curtailed liquidity and trading activity under Stage IV GSM. The combination of weekly-only trading, full trade-value ASD, and prohibition on upward price movement effectively freezes price discovery on the upside and discourages speculative activity. Existing holders face significant exit challenges given limited trading windows. The move signals serious regulatory concern about the price behavior or fundamentals of these companies. Investors and members should treat these securities with extreme caution, as Stage IV is the terminal stage within the GSM framework.
