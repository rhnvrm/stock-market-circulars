## Summary

National Stock Exchange has admitted 14 series of secured, rated, listed, redeemable non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by Power Finance Corporation Limited (PFCL) for trading on the Capital Market segment effective February 04, 2026. The debentures span five series (I through V) with varying coupon rates from 6.85% to 7.30% and maturity dates ranging from 2031 to 2041. Series III and V include zero-coupon bonds (ZCBs). All debentures will trade in compulsory demat mode in the Normal Market segment with rolling settlement.

## Key Points

- 14 NCD series admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Coupon-bearing debentures: Series I (6.85%-7.00%, maturity 2031), Series II (7.00%-7.20%, maturity 2036), Series IV (7.05%-7.30%, maturity 2041)
- Zero-coupon bonds: Series III (maturity 2036) and Series V (maturity 2041)
- Trading symbols range from 685PFCL31 to PFCL41A
- Market lot size: 1 debenture per lot
- All securities trade in compulsory demat for all investors
- Date of allotment: February 03, 2026 (for at least some tranches)
- First interest payment date for Series I Tranche 1: varies by category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading these NCDs
- All trades must be executed in market lot size of 1 debenture
- Debentures must be held and traded in dematerialized form only
- Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement) procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 04, 2026 (trading commences)
- **Date of Allotment**: February 03, 2026
- **Maturity Dates**:
  - Series I: February 03, 2031
  - Series II: February 02, 2036
  - Series III: 2036 (ZCB)
  - Series IV: 2041
  - Series V: 2041 (ZCB)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of NCDs following a public issue by Power Finance Corporation Limited, a government-owned financial institution in the power sector. The listing expands debt market options for institutional and retail investors seeking fixed-income securities with varying tenures (5 to 15 years) and yields.

**Investor Impact**: Provides debt investment opportunities with secured, rated debentures offering coupon rates between 6.85% and 7.30% per annum, suitable for conservative investors seeking stable returns. The inclusion of zero-coupon bonds (Series III and V) offers tax-efficient options for long-term investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems with the new security codes and ISIN codes. No significant operational changes required as this follows standard listing procedures.

**Credit Profile**: The debentures are secured and rated, issued by a Maharatna CPSE, suggesting strong credit quality suitable for institutional and retail fixed-income portfolios.
