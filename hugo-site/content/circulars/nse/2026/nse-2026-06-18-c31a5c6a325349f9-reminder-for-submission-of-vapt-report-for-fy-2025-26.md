## Summary

NSE's Inspection Department has issued a reminder to all trading members for submission of Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) reports for FY 2025-26, referencing the original circular NSE/INSP/74185 dated May 14, 2026. Deadlines vary by member category, with the earliest due date being June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Half-yearly VAPT (Oct 2025 – Mar 2026) applies to Qualified Stock Brokers (QSBs) and Regulated Entities (REs) classified as 'Protected Systems' and/or Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) by NCIIPC
- Annual VAPT (Apr 2025 – Mar 2026) applies to Self-certification REs, Small-size REs, Mid-size REs, and Qualified REs not categorized as QSBs
- VAPT must be conducted through a CERT-In empanelled auditor
- Reports must be submitted after approval from the respective IT Committee
- Operating guidelines and help documents are available on ENIT under: Inspection → VAPT → VAPT Help Documents
- Members classified as 'Exempted from CSCRF' must submit a declaration via the member portal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a reminder referencing existing obligations under NSE/INSP/74185 (May 14, 2026) and NSE/INSP/73792 (April 17, 2026). Non-compliance actions are governed by Annexure 1.2 of NSE/INSP/73792.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must submit VAPT reports within the applicable deadlines
- VAPT must be performed by a CERT-In empanelled auditor
- Reports require prior approval from the member's IT Committee before submission
- Members exempted from CSCRF must submit a self-declaration via: Inspection → VAPT → Display VAPT Auditor Registration → New Auditor → select 'Exempted' under Member Category
- Members should refer to ENIT for detailed operating guidelines on auditor registration and report submission

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Deadline for half-yearly VAPT report (QSBs & CII/Protected System REs) and for annual VAPT conduct/report submission by Yearly category members
- **July 31, 2026**: Final deadline for annual VAPT report submission (after IT Committee approval) for Self-certification, Small-size, Mid-size, and Qualified REs

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative compliance circular with no direct market or price impact. However, trading members who fail to comply risk regulatory action as outlined in NSE/INSP/73792. Members should ensure their VAPT audits are already complete or in progress, given the June 30 deadline is imminent. Exempted members must proactively file declarations to avoid unnecessary scrutiny.
