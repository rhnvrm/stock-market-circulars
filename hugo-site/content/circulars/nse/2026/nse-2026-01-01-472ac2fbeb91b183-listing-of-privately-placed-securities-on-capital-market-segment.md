## Summary

NSE has admitted two privately placed debt securities issued by IIFL Finance Limited to trading on the Capital Market segment effective January 02, 2026. The securities are IIFL 9.30% 2035 Sr 35 (ISIN: INE530B08235) and IIFL 9.25% 2032 Sr 35 (ISIN: INE530B08227), both with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of IIFL Finance Limited listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- Security 1: IIFL 9.30% 2035 Sr 35 (Symbol: 930IIFL35A, Series: N0)
- Security 2: IIFL 9.25% 2032 Sr 35 (Symbol: 925IIFL32A, Series: N0)
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading to commence January 02, 2026
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading: 930IIFL35A and 925IIFL32A
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for both securities)
- Applicable to all NSE members trading in Capital Market segment

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 02, 2026
- **Maturity Date (930IIFL35A)**: December 30, 2035
- **Maturity Date (925IIFL32A)**: December 30, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides additional trading avenues for investors holding these IIFL Finance bonds but does not affect broader market operations or introduce new compliance obligations beyond standard trading procedures.
