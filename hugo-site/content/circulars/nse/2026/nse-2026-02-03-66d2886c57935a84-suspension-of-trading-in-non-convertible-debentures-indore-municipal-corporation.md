## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures issued by Indore Municipal Corporation (Symbol: IMC1, ISIN: INE00QS24019) effective February 05, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debentures, in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Indore Municipal Corporation's Non-Convertible Debentures
- Security Symbol: IMC1
- ISIN Number: INE00QS24019
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational action following existing regulations regarding matured securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members. This is a notification of trading suspension due to instrument maturity.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 05, 2026 - Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting a single municipal corporation debenture issue that has reached maturity. The suspension is expected and part of normal debt instrument lifecycle management.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for market participants. Investors holding these debentures will follow standard maturity and redemption procedures as per the original issuance terms.
