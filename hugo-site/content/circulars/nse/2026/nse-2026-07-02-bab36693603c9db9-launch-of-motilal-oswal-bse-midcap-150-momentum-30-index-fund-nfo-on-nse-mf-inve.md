## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Motilal Oswal BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 Index Fund NFO, launched by Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund, will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 03, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- New Fund Offer (NFO): Motilal Oswal BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 Index Fund
- Two plans available: Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HE2) and Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HF9)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: July 23, 2026
- Available for subscription on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification of a new fund offer becoming tradable on the platform, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 23, 2026, till 06:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Period: July 03, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment Date: July 23, 2026
- Clear Funds Deadline: July 23, 2026, till 06:00 pm

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to NSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors interested in subscribing to this specific index fund NFO. It does not affect existing listed securities, trading mechanisms, or broader market operations.
