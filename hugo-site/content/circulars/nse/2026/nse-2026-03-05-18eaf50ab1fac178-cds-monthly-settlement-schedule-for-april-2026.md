## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (Circular Ref. No: 12/2026) has published the Mark to Market (MTM) settlement schedule for the Currency Derivatives Segment (CDS) for April 2026. The schedule covers 19 trade dates from April 2 to April 30, 2026, with corresponding settlement dates. Two special final settlement dates apply to specific contract types beyond the standard schedule.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers trade dates from 02-Apr-2026 to 30-Apr-2026
- Standard MTM settlement follows T+1 pattern, adjusted for exchange holidays
- April 14 (Dr. Ambedkar Jayanti) is a holiday — no trading or settlement on that date
- Final settlement for April 2026 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures and Cash Settled Currency Futures and Options Contracts: **30-Apr-2026**
- Final settlement for April 2026 Overnight Call Rate (MIBOR) Contracts and Cash Settled Interest Rate Contracts: **04-May-2026**

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- All CDS members must adhere to the published settlement schedule for MTM obligations
- Members with open positions in 91-Day GOI T-Bill Futures, Cash Settled Currency Futures, and Options must settle by 30-Apr-2026
- Members with open positions in MIBOR and Cash Settled Interest Rate Contracts must settle by 04-May-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on CDS members who must align their back-office and risk systems with the published schedule. The extended final settlement date of 04-May-2026 for MIBOR and Interest Rate Contracts means members should account for cross-month settlement obligations. No market-wide disruption is expected; this is standard monthly scheduling.
