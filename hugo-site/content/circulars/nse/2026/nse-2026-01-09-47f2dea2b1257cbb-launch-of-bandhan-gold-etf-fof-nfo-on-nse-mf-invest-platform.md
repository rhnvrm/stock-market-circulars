## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Bandhan Gold ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The new fund offer will be open for subscription from January 12, 2026 to January 20, 2026, with allotment scheduled for January 23, 2026. Both Regular and Direct plan options with Growth variants will be available under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- Two schemes available: Bandhan Gold ETF FOF Regular Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KK5) and Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KL3)
- Both schemes categorized under Equity category
- NFO subscription period: January 12-20, 2026
- Allotment date: January 23, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by January 22, 2026, 11:59 PM
- Payment accepted through any payment mode

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 22, 2026, 11:59 PM
- Members need to facilitate transactions for investors during the NFO period on the NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **January 20, 2026**: NFO closing date
- **January 22, 2026, 11:59 PM**: Deadline for clear funds receipt
- **January 23, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the launch of a new mutual fund scheme. The impact is limited to:

- **Investors**: New investment option in gold ETF fund of funds category for those seeking gold exposure through mutual funds
- **Distributors/Members**: Operational requirement to facilitate NFO transactions during the specified period
- **Market**: Marginal impact; expands product offerings in the gold investment space through fund of funds structure

The circular is informational in nature and provides routine procedural details for a new fund offering on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
