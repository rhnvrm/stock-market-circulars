## Summary

NSE has announced changes to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective June 30 and July 1, 2026. Two securities — Pradeep Metals Limited (PRADPME) and Rajoo Engineers Limited (RAJOOENG) — are newly included under ESM Stage I. G-TEC Jainx Education Limited (GTECJAINX) is escalated from Stage I to Stage II. No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this round.

## Key Points

- PRADPME and RAJOOENG are included in ESM Stage I w.e.f. June 30, 2026.
- GTECJAINX moves from ESM Stage I to Stage II w.e.f. June 30, 2026.
- No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework.
- A minimum 100% margin will be applicable from July 1, 2026 on all open positions as of June 30, 2026 and new positions created from July 1, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE/ST) w.e.f. July 1, 2026.
- Stage II securities will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 30, 2026.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Regulatory Changes

The circular is issued under the ESM framework established by prior NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315). The key regulatory change is the addition of new securities to Stage I and the escalation of one security to Stage II, with associated margin and settlement segment changes.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure a minimum 100% margin is collected on all open and new positions in ESM-listed securities from July 1, 2026.
- Members must note the shift of qualifying securities from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM series) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST series).
- For Stage II securities (GTECJAINX), trading is restricted to Trade-for-Trade under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from June 30, 2026.
- Members should refer to NSE's ESM FAQs for detailed framework guidelines.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Circular issued.
- **June 30, 2026**: ESM Stage I inclusions (PRADPME, RAJOOENG) and Stage I→II escalation (GTECJAINX) effective; Stage II Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band effective for GTECJAINX.
- **July 1, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective on open positions (as of June 30, 2026) and new positions; shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST settlement series effective.

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding positions in PRADPME, RAJOOENG, and GTECJAINX will face significantly restricted trading conditions. The mandatory 100% margin requirement increases capital requirements for all positions. Migration to the Trade-for-Trade segment eliminates netting benefits and requires delivery of each trade individually. For GTECJAINX, the additional constraint of a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction will severely limit price discovery and liquidity. These measures reflect heightened regulatory scrutiny of these securities due to market surveillance concerns, though NSE has clarified the inclusion is not an adverse action against the companies themselves.
