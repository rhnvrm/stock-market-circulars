## Summary

NSE has announced a change in ISIN code for Dhariwalcorp Limited (Symbol: DHARIWAL) effective February 06, 2026, due to a sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/-. The new ISIN code will be INE0YRN01025 and will apply to all trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Company: Dhariwalcorp Limited
- Stock Symbol: DHARIWAL
- New ISIN: INE0YRN01025
- Reason: Sub-division of shares from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- face value
- Ex-date: February 06, 2026
- The new ISIN will be effective for all trades done on and from the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding ISIN code modification following share sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their systems with the new ISIN code INE0YRN01025 for Dhariwalcorp Limited
- Members must ensure the new ISIN is used for all trades executed on or after February 06, 2026
- Trading and settlement systems should be configured to recognize the new ISIN from the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Ex-Date (Effective Date)**: February 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Share sub-division is a corporate action that increases the number of outstanding shares while reducing the face value proportionally. This typically makes shares more affordable for retail investors without changing the company's market capitalization.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Exchange members and market participants must update their systems and records to reflect the new ISIN code. The three-day notice period between circular date and ex-date provides adequate time for system updates.

**Trading Impact**: All trades in DHARIWAL stock from February 06, 2026 onwards must use the new ISIN code INE0YRN01025. Pre-existing holdings will be automatically adjusted to reflect the new face value and increased quantity.
