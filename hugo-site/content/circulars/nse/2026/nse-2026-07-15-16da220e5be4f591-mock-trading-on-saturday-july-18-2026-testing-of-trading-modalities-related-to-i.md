## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session on Saturday, July 18, 2026 to test trading modalities associated with the upcoming introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment. This follows the earlier circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026 on CAS introduction. The mock involves two trading sessions, including a new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) specifically for CAS-related testing in Trading Session-1, with members required to revert to the earlier NEATPlus version (7.8.8) for Trading Session-2.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 18, 2026, with two distinct trading sessions.
- Trading Session-1 will test CAS-related modalities using new NEATPlus version 7.8.9, available from 07:00 hours on July 18, 2026.
- Trading Session-2 will run from the DR (Disaster Recovery) Site using the earlier NEATPlus version 7.8.8, following existing modalities without CAS changes.
- Members must reinstall and log in with NEATPlus 7.8.8 after Trading Session-1 concludes, before Trading Session-2 begins.
- Master files for Trading Session-1 (security.gz, nnf_security.gz, NSE_CM_security_18072026.csv.gz) will be available on the NSE Extranet by 8:00 hours on July 18, 2026.
- Trades from Trading Session-1 will not carry over to Trading Session-2; members are urged to actively participate in both sessions.
- Trading Session-1 includes a new CAS window between Normal Market close (11:30) and the final Closing Session (12:15-12:35), with CAS open time at 11:30, market order restriction start, and CAS close (with random closure in the last two minutes).

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operationalizes the previously announced introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment (per circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026) by scheduling a dedicated mock test. No new regulatory change is introduced here beyond testing procedures for the CAS rollout.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must download and install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 from the NSE Extranet path /cmftp/common/Mock/CM/18JULY2026/NEATPlus789, available from 07:00 hours on July 18, 2026, exclusively for use in Trading Session-1.
- Members must load the specified master files into their trading applications before Trading Session-1 begins.
- After Trading Session-1, members must uninstall NEATPlus 7.8.9 and reinstall/login with NEATPlus 7.8.8 for Trading Session-2.
- Members are required to actively participate in both trading sessions of the mock.
- Re-login for further testing is permitted between 19:00 and 19:30 hours.

## Important Dates

- May 29, 2026: Original circular (NSE/CMTR/74466) announcing CAS introduction.
- July 17, 2026: End-of-day master files generated for use in the mock (except CAS-specific files).
- July 18, 2026, 07:00 hours: New NEATPlus version 7.8.9 available for download.
- July 18, 2026, 8:00 hours: CAS-specific master files available on NSE Extranet.
- July 18, 2026: Mock trading day, with Trading Session-1 (approx. 09:45–12:45) and Trading Session-2 from DR Site (16:45–19:30, including re-login window).
- July 20, 2026: Reference date for live trading transition mentioned in the circular (content on subsequent instructions is truncated in the source).

## Impact Assessment

The mock session carries medium operational impact for trading members, who must coordinate software updates (NEATPlus version switch), master file downloads, and dual-session participation within a compressed weekend timeframe. Failure to properly test CAS modalities could pose readiness risks ahead of the live CAS rollout in the Equity Cash segment. There is no direct impact on listed securities, investor trading, or specific stocks, as this is an internal systems and process testing exercise for exchange members.
