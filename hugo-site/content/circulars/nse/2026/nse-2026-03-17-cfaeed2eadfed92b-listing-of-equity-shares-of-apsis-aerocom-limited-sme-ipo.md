## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Apsis Aerocom Limited on the NSE EMERGE SME Platform effective March 18, 2026. The shares will trade under symbol APSISAERO in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment – Settlement Type 'W') initially, before being shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment – Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Symbol: APSISAERO on NSE EMERGE SME Platform
- ISIN: INE1OOJ01011
- Issue price: Rs. 110/- per share
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Total securities listed: 12,051,921 equity shares
- Market lot and Market Maker minimum quantity: 1,200 equity shares
- Market Maker maximum permissible spread: within 10%
- Trading type: Continuous Market, Pari Passu
- Market Maker: Basan Equity Broking Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (securities admitted to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)
- SEBI circular nos. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 (pre-open session and TFTS-to-SM shift)
- NSE circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018 (continuous market trading)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code APSISAERO for all trading
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1,200 equity shares
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Shares are subject to lock-in as detailed in Annexure I
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Integrated Registry Management Services Private Limited, Bengaluru; Contact: Mr. S Giridhar; Email: smeipo@integratedindia.in

## Important Dates

- Circular date: March 17, 2026
- Effective date for trading: March 18, 2026
- Financial year of company: April 2025 – March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing on the NSE EMERGE platform. The scrip will initially trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series ST, Settlement Type W), which restricts speculative trading and requires delivery-based settlements. Impact is limited to investors participating in the Apsis Aerocom IPO and the designated market maker Basan Equity Broking Limited. The company is an industrial/aerospace sector firm based in Peenya Industrial Area, Bangalore. With 12,051,921 shares at an issue price of Rs. 110, the implied market cap at listing is approximately Rs. 132.57 crore (SME scale). Lock-in restrictions apply to a portion of shares as per Annexure I.
