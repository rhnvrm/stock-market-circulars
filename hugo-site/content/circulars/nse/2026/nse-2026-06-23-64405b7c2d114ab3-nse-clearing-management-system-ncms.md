## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has released NCMS (NSE Clearing Management System) EXE version 4.2.2 for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment. Members and custodians are advised to download the updated version and note the prescribed activity timelines.

## Key Points

- NCMS EXE version 4.2.2 is now available for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment.
- The software can be downloaded from the NSE extranet at path: `/common/C2N/NCMS_Member_Exe_EGR_Setup.zip`.
- CP code modification is permitted on Trade Day (T) until 23:45 hrs / 23:59 hrs (as per market timings).
- Custodians can confirm Custodial Participant (CP) trades by 10:00 AM on T+1 via the NCIS EGR portal.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a system software update notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are required to download and install NCMS EXE version 4.2.2 for EGR operations.
- Custodians must submit trade confirmations for Custodial Participant trades by 10:00 AM on T+1 using the NCIS EGR platform at https://www.connect2nse.com/NCISEGR/.
- CP code modifications must be completed by 23:45 hrs / 23:59 hrs on trade day (T).

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 23, 2026
- No specific implementation deadline mentioned; members should update promptly.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update limited to members and custodians participating in the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment. The new NCMS version (4.2.2) is an incremental software release. Operational impact is minimal, restricted to the EGR segment, and requires members to update their local NCMS installation. No changes to trading rules, margins, or broader market structure.
