## Summary

NSE has informed its members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Rajputana Stainless Limited on the National Stock Exchange of India. The company will trade under the symbol RSL with ISIN INE313L01016. The specific listing date has not been announced in this circular and will be communicated separately.

## Key Points

- Rajputana Stainless Limited is scheduled to list its equity shares on NSE via IPO
- Trading symbol: **RSL**
- ISIN: **INE313L01016**
- The exact date of listing will be announced through a separate circular
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department under Ref No: NSE/CML/73344

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements outlined for members in this circular. Members are advised to note the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular confirming the exact listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 18, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Rajputana Stainless Limited (RSL) introduces a new equity security in the stainless steel sector to NSE. Market participants, particularly those who subscribed to the IPO, should monitor NSE communications for the confirmed listing date. The impact is limited at this stage as the circular is purely informational and does not trigger any immediate trading or compliance obligations.
