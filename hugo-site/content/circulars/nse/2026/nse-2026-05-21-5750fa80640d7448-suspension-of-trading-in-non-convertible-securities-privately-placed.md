## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in one privately placed Non-Convertible Security issued by Export Import Bank of India, effective May 22, 2026. The suspension is being effected under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, due to the redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Trading in ISIN INE514E08FZ5 (Export Import Bank of India) will be suspended from May 22, 2026.
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the security.
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74306, dated May 21, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension is a standard operational action under existing NSE Debt Market regulations when a non-convertible security reaches redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are informed not to execute trades in the specified ISIN (INE514E08FZ5) from the suspension date. Members should ensure their systems and order management processes reflect this suspension.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single privately placed debt security (INE514E08FZ5) of Export Import Bank of India that is being redeemed. Existing holders of this security should note that secondary market trading will cease from May 22, 2026, and they will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the security.
