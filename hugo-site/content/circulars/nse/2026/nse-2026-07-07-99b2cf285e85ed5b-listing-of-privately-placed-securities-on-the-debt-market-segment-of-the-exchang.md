## Summary

NSE has notified members that a set of privately placed commercial papers (CPs) have been admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 07, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. The circular lists the security codes, ISINs, issue sizes, and redemption dates for CPs issued by ten corporate/financial entities.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue of Commercial Papers admitted to the Debt Market Segment effective July 07, 2026.
- Issuers include: HDFC Securities Limited (HSL CP 23/09/2026), ICICI Securities Limited (ISEC CP 15/03/27), Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI CP 28/09/26 Sr 8), NTPC Limited (NTPC CP 05/10/26 Sr 162), Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCL CP 28/09/26 Sr 60), Godrej Consumer Products Limited (GCPL CP 05/10/26 Sr 9), Reliance Retail Ventures Limited (RRVL CP 23/09/26), Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (two CPs: Sr 52 and Sr 51), and L&T Finance Limited (LTFL CP 29/07/26).
- All CPs have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit, issued at a discount to face value.
- Date of allotment for all listed securities is 06-Jul-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from members beyond updating records to reflect the new security codes and ISINs now admitted for dealing on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- Effective/Listing Date: July 07, 2026
- Date of Allotment (all securities): July 06, 2026
- Redemption Dates: ranging from August 04, 2026 (HPCL Sr 52) to March 15, 2027 (ICICI Securities CP)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative circular listing newly admitted commercial papers on NSE's debt segment. It affects debt market participants and institutional investors trading in these specific CP instruments but has no direct impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
