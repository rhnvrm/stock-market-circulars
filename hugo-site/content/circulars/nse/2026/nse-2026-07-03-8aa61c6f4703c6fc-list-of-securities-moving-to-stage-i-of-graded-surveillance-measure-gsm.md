## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No. NSE/SURV/75031, Circular Ref. No. 499/2026) dated July 3, 2026, notifying that Globale Tessile Limited (Symbol: GLOBALE, ISIN: INE0URU01010) will be moved to Stage I of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) with effect from July 6, 2026. This action continues the Exchange's ongoing surveillance framework established through prior circulars dated February 23, 2017, July 20, 2018, November 29, 2019, November 17, 2023, September 20, 2024, May 15, 2026, and June 19, 2026. The security is being added to the GSM list as per BSE's classification.

## Key Points

- Globale Tessile Limited (GLOBALE, ISIN: INE0URU01010) moves to GSM Stage I effective July 6, 2026.
- The security is added to GSM as per BSE's parallel classification.
- Under Stage I, applicable margin rate will be 100% and price band will be 5% or lower as applicable.
- Members are advised to exercise adequate precaution while trading in this security.
- This is a continuation of the GSM framework circular series, referencing seven prior circulars dating back to 2017.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular applies the existing GSM framework (Stages I through IV) to an additional security. The Annexure reiterates the surveillance actions applicable at each GSM stage:
- Stage I: 100% margin and price band of 5% or lower.
- Stage II: Trade-for-Trade with 5% or lower price band and 50% Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) on buyers.
- Stage III: Trade-for-Trade, weekly trading only (first trading day of the week), and 100% ASD on buyers.
- Stage IV: Same as Stage III with no upward price movement permitted.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE trading members must apply 100% margin and a price band of 5% or lower on trades in GLOBALE from July 6, 2026.
- Members must exercise due caution and inform relevant desks/clients about the GSM classification.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 3, 2026
- Effective Date of GSM Stage I applicability: July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The GSM Stage I classification for GLOBALE will tighten trading conditions through higher margin requirements (100%) and a restricted price band (5% or lower), likely reducing trading volumes and increasing cost of trading for this security. Market participants holding or trading GLOBALE shares should reassess exposure given the increased surveillance and margin costs. The broader market impact is limited since this action affects a single, likely illiquid or high-risk security rather than systemic market segments.
