## Summary

HDFC Mutual Fund is launching the HDFC Nifty Auto Index Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be open for transactions from June 22, 2026 to July 3, 2026, with allotment scheduled for July 7, 2026. Two scheme variants are available — Regular Growth and Direct Growth.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 22, 2026 to July 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two schemes offered: Regular Growth (INF179KC1KP2) and Direct Growth (INF179KC1KQ0)
- Both schemes are categorised as Equity
- Allotment date: July 7, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 7, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product availability notification for a new fund offer on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 7, 2026 till 11:59 pm.
- Members facilitating transactions in this NFO should adhere to standard NSE MF Invest Platform operating procedures.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **July 3, 2026**: NFO subscription closes (11:59 pm)
- **July 7, 2026**: Allotment date; deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account (11:59 pm)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with minimal market impact. Members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to facilitate client investments in HDFC Nifty Auto Index Fund need to be aware of the subscription window and fund transfer deadlines. The fund tracks the Nifty Auto Index, providing equity exposure to the automobile sector. No disruption to existing operations is expected.
