## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has notified all members of a change in the designated Market Maker for C K K Retail Mart Limited (Symbol: CKKRETAIL), an SME-listed company. The outgoing market maker, SVCM Securities Pvt Ltd, is being replaced by Basan Equity Broking Limited, effective April 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: CKKRETAIL
- Company: C K K Retail Mart Limited
- Old Market Maker: SVCM Securities Pvt Ltd (Member Code: 90216)
- New Market Maker: Basan Equity Broking Limited (Member Code: 12964)
- Effective Date: April 20, 2026
- Circular Reference: NSE/CMTR/73790, Circular Ref. No. 52/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational update to the market maker assignment for an SME-listed security.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the change in market maker for CKKRETAIL effective April 20, 2026. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness of the new market maker assignment.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Market Maker Change: April 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to liquidity provision for CKKRETAIL on the NSE SME platform. Basan Equity Broking Limited (Member Code: 12964) will assume market-making responsibilities from April 20, 2026. This change may result in minor short-term adjustments to bid-ask spreads or liquidity during the transition, but no significant market disruption is expected.
