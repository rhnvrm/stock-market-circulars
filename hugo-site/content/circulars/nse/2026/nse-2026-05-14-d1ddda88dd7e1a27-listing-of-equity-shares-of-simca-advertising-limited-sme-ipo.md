## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding the listing of equity shares of Simca Advertising Limited (ticker: SIMCA) under the SME IPO segment. The shareholding pattern has been filed post-issue under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as of May 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Simca Advertising Limited (scrip: SIMCA) is being listed on NSE under the SME IPO category
- Shareholding pattern filed post-issue under Reg. 31(1)(a) as on May 13, 2026
- All equity shares are fully paid-up; no partly paid-up shares exist
- No convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts have been issued
- No promoter shares are pledged, under non-disposal undertaking, or otherwise encumbered
- No shares are locked-in post-issue
- No significant beneficial owner declared
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- All shares are held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The circular follows existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 requirements for shareholding pattern disclosure at the time of listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Simca Advertising Limited has submitted the required shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Listed entity has declared 'NO' for all applicable disclosure items (partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, pledged shares, locked-in shares, differential voting rights, significant beneficial owner)
- All 'NO' declarations result in corresponding values being treated as zero on the stock exchange platform

## Important Dates

- Post-issue shareholding pattern date: May 13, 2026
- Listing date: May 14, 2026 (circular date)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Simca Advertising Limited on NSE's SME platform provides a new investment opportunity in the SME segment. The clean shareholding structure with no encumbrances, locked-in shares, or complex securities simplifies the equity story for investors. Impact is limited to participants in the SME IPO segment and direct investors in SIMCA.
