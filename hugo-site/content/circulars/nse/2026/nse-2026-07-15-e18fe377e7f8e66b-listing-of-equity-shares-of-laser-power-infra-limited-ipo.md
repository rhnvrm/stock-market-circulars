## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Laser Power & Infra Limited on the Capital Market segment, effective July 16, 2026, following completion of its IPO. The circular specifies the security's trading identifiers, lot size, and lock-in details for pre-IPO shareholders under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- Symbol: LASERPOWER; Series: EQ; ISIN: INE17IR01028
- Face value: Rs. 5/- per share; Issue price: Rs. 214/- per share
- Total number of securities listed: 140,368,342 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares
- Security will be included in the pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Lock-in schedule applies to distinctive number ranges: shares locked in until 12-Aug-2026, 11-Oct-2026, 15-Jan-2027, and 15-Jan-2028, with certain tranches free of lock-in
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size specification).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify the security only by its designated codes (Symbol: LASERPOWER, Series: EQ) and trade only in the specified lot size of 1 share. The security will be subject to pre-open session procedures applicable to IPO listings.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Circular issued
- July 16, 2026: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading
- 12-Aug-2026: First lock-in release for certain distinctive number ranges
- 11-Oct-2026: Second lock-in release
- 15-Jan-2027: Third lock-in release
- 15-Jan-2028: Final lock-in release (anchor/promoter category shares)

## Impact Assessment

This listing enables secondary market trading of Laser Power & Infra Limited shares on NSE from July 16, 2026, providing liquidity to IPO allottees for unlocked shares. The impact is contained to investors and traders interested in this specific stock; the phased lock-in schedule limits available float in the near term, which may contribute to price volatility for the newly listed scrip during initial trading sessions.
