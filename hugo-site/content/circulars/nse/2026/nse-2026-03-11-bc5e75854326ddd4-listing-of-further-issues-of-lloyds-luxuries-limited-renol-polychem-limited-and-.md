## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of further issues of equity shares to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment (SME EMERGE) for three companies: Lloyds Luxuries Limited (ESOP allotment), Renol Polychem Limited (Preferential allotment), and Presstonic Engineering Limited (Rights issue — Final). The circular is effective from March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three SME EMERGE companies are listing additional equity shares effective March 12, 2026.
- Lloyds Luxuries Limited (LLOYDS): 2,57,026 equity shares allotted under ESOP at face value of Rs. 10/-, allotment date March 6, 2026; market lot 1,000; ISIN INE0DTQ01028.
- Renol Polychem Limited (RNPL): 34,54,462 equity shares issued under Preferential allotment at issue price Rs. 134/-, face value Rs. 10/-, allotment date January 24, 2026; market lot 1,200; lock-in until September 18, 2026; ISIN INE0VZX01015.
- Presstonic Engineering Limited (PRESSTONIC): 75,33,600 equity shares issued under Rights — Final at issue price Rs. 35/-, face value Rs. 10/-, allotment date March 9, 2026; market lot 800; ISIN INE0R1601012.
- All allotments are pari passu with existing equity shares.
- Shares are credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in dematerialized mode pending activation per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes (LLOYDS, RNPL, PRESSTONIC) for trading.
- Trading in these securities must be conducted in the specified lot sizes: 1,000 (LLOYDS), 1,200 (RNPL), 800 (PRESSTONIC).
- Renol Polychem preferential allotment shares are subject to lock-in until September 18, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for trading: March 12, 2026.
- Lloyds Luxuries ESOP allotment date: March 6, 2026.
- Renol Polychem Preferential allotment date: January 24, 2026; lock-in expiry: September 18, 2026.
- Presstonic Engineering Rights allotment date: March 9, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to the three SME EMERGE segment companies and their shareholders. The additions increase the floating share capital of each company. The Renol Polychem preferential allotment is the largest by volume (34.5 lakh shares at Rs. 134/- issue price) and carries a lock-in period restricting near-term liquidity. Presstonic Engineering's rights issue adds the highest number of shares (75.3 lakh). Lloyds Luxuries' ESOP allotment is the smallest (2.57 lakh shares). No broad market impact is anticipated.
