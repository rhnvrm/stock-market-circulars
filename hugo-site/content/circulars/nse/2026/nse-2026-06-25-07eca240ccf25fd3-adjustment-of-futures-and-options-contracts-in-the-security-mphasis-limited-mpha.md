## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued guidelines for the adjustment of Futures and Options contracts in MPHASIS LIMITED (MPHASIS) consequent to a dividend of Rs. 62/- per share. The adjustment will be carried out on July 07, 2026 (last cum-dividend date), with the ex-dividend date being July 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- Dividend amount: Rs. 62/- per share
- Last cum-dividend date: July 07, 2026
- Ex-dividend date: July 08, 2026
- Futures contracts will be adjusted by reducing the daily mark-to-market settlement price by Rs. 62/-
- All open futures positions will be carried forward at daily settlement price less Rs. 62/-
- Options strike prices will be reduced by Rs. 62/- (adjusted to nearest tick size) on the ex-dividend date
- This circular is pursuant to NSE Circular No. 92/2026 (NSE/FAOP/74829) dated June 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The clearing corporation will apply mandatory adjustments to all MPHASIS F&O contracts:
- **Futures**: Positions marked-to-market on July 07, 2026 at daily settlement price; open positions carried forward at adjusted price (settlement price minus Rs. 62/-)
- **Options**: All cum-dividend strike prices reduced by Rs. 62/- on the ex-dividend date, adjusted to nearest tick size

## Compliance Requirements

- All clearing members must be aware of the adjusted carry forward values effective July 08, 2026
- Begin-of-day margins on July 08, 2026 will be computed based on the adjusted carry forward value
- Intra-day margins post July 08, 2026 will be computed based on relevant traded prices at the time intra-day span risk parameter files are generated
- Normal daily mark-to-market settlement procedures resume from July 08, 2026 onwards

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Circular issued
- **July 07, 2026**: Last cum-dividend date; futures positions marked-to-market and adjusted
- **July 08, 2026**: Ex-dividend date; adjusted carry forward values and strike prices take effect; normal settlement procedures resume

## Impact Assessment

Traders and clearing members holding long or short positions in MPHASIS futures and options contracts will see automatic adjustments to their position values and strike prices. For example, a futures position previously valued at Rs. 2,244/- per unit will be adjusted to Rs. 2,182/- (Rs. 2,244 minus Rs. 62 dividend). This is a standard corporate action adjustment ensuring fair treatment across all F&O participants and preventing arbitrage opportunities around the dividend date. The impact is moderate and limited to those with active MPHASIS F&O positions.
