## Summary

NSE has notified its members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of CSM Technologies Limited on the Exchange, following completion of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol CSM. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: CSM Technologies Limited
- Trading Symbol: CSM
- ISIN: INE0ZK601013
- This circular pertains to the pre-listing notification for the company's IPO shares
- The actual listing date is yet to be confirmed and will follow in a subsequent circular

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard listing notification circular issued under NSE's Listing Department procedures for newly IPO'd companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems and records to recognize the new symbol CSM and ISIN INE0ZK601013 ahead of the listing date. No further action is required until the listing date circular is issued.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 01, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The impact is currently limited as trading has not yet commenced; however, once listed, CSM Technologies Limited shares will become available for trading on NSE, providing investors a new avenue for participation. Members should prepare their trading and settlement systems for the addition of this new security.
