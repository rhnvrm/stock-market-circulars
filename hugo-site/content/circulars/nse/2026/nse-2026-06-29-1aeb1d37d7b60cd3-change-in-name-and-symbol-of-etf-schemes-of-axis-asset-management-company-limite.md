## Summary

NSE has notified all members that the names and trading symbols of nine ETF schemes managed by Axis Asset Management Company Limited will be changed effective July 03, 2026. The underlying ISINs remain unchanged; only the scheme names and NSE ticker symbols are being updated.

## Key Points

- Nine Axis Mutual Fund ETF schemes will undergo name and symbol changes on NSE.
- Effective date: **July 03, 2026**.
- ISINs of all schemes remain unchanged.
- Old symbols will be replaced by new symbols as detailed below.
- Members and trading systems referencing these ETFs must update their records to use new symbols.

## Regulatory Changes

The following ETF scheme name and symbol changes have been announced:

| Existing Scheme Name | ISIN | Existing Symbol | New Symbol |
|---|---|---|---|
| AXIS MUTUAL FUND - Axis NIFTY 50 ETF | INF846K01W98 | AXISNIFTY | NIFTYAXIS |
| AXIS MUTUAL FUND - AXIS NIFTY IT ETF | INF846K01Y96 | AXISTECETF | ITAXIS |
| AXIS MUTUAL FUND - Axis NIFTY Bank ETF | INF846K01X63 | AXISBNKETF | BNKETFAXIS |
| AXIS MUTUAL FUND - Axis NIFTY India Consumption ETF | INF846K016C7 | AXISCETF | CONSUMAXIS |
| AXIS MUTUAL FUND - Axis NIFTY Healthcare ETF | INF846K01Z12 | AXISHCETF | HEALTHAXIS |
| Axis Mutual Fund - AXIS S&P BSE SENSEX ETF | INF846K010Q0 | AXSENSEX | SENSEXAXIS |
| Axis Mutual Fund - Axis Silver ETF | INF846K011K1 | AXISILVER | SILVERAXIS |
| Axis Mutual Fund - Axis Nifty500 Value 50 ETF | INF846KA1119 | AXISVALUE | VALUEAXIS |
| Axis Mutual Fund - Axis Gold ETF | INF846K01W80 | AXISGOLD | GOLDAXIS |

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must update all internal systems, order management platforms, and client-facing tools to reference the new ETF symbols from July 03, 2026.
- Existing holdings are unaffected as ISINs remain unchanged; only trading symbol references need to be updated.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Circular issued by NSE.
- **July 03, 2026**: Name and symbol changes become effective on NSE.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change affecting how these 9 Axis Mutual Fund ETFs are identified on NSE. Investors holding these ETFs are not directly impacted as ISINs remain the same. However, brokers, trading platforms, screeners, and algorithmic systems that rely on NSE ticker symbols must update their references before July 03, 2026 to avoid order routing failures or data mismatches. The change does not affect NAV, fund strategy, or investor holdings.
