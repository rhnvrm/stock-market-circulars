## Summary

NSE has revoked the temporary suspension on fresh subscriptions in Tata Small Cap Fund under Tata Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. All fresh inflows through lumpsum and switch-in applications have been resumed with effect from April 06, 2026, as per communication received from Tata Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- Tata Small Cap Fund is now open for all fresh inflows via lumpsum and switch-in applications on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- The resumption is effective April 06, 2026.
- The decision was communicated by Tata Mutual Fund to NSE via a Notice cum Addendum.
- The addendum forms an integral part of the SID/KIM cum Application Form.
- All other terms, conditions, and risk parameters of the scheme remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular revokes a previously imposed temporary suspension on fresh subscriptions in Tata Small Cap Fund, restoring normal subscription operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are requested to take note of the resumption and update their systems/platforms accordingly.
- The Notice cum Addendum issued by Tata Mutual Fund is enclosed and forms an integral part of the SID/KIM.
- Members should ensure that lumpsum and switch-in applications for Tata Small Cap Fund are processed from April 06, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **April 02, 2026**: Addendum date issued by Tata Mutual Fund.
- **April 06, 2026**: Effective date for resumption of fresh inflows (lumpsum and switch-in) in Tata Small Cap Fund.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on investors, distributors, and NSE members using the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors who were previously unable to make fresh investments or switch into Tata Small Cap Fund can now do so. The resumption may attract fresh capital inflows into the small-cap segment. No compliance burden is introduced; members simply need to enable processing of subscriptions for this scheme. The revision applies prospectively and remains in force until further notice.
