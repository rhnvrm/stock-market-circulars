## Summary

NSE has informed members that Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund has revised the minimum instalment amount under the Daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) for its Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform. The minimum instalment amount has been reduced from Rs. 100/- to Rs. 21/-, with the change effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Change applies specifically to the Daily SIP frequency of Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund.
- Existing minimum instalment amount: Rs. 100/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter.
- Revised minimum instalment amount: Rs. 21/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter.
- Minimum number of instalments remains 1 month (30 days).
- Change communicated via a Notice cum Addendum to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) issued by Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

The Notice cum Addendum modifies the SID and KIM of the Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund to reflect the lower minimum Daily SIP instalment amount. All other terms and conditions of the scheme, including the Statement of Additional Information (SAI), remain unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members and NSE MF Invest platform participants must update their systems/processes to reflect the revised minimum Daily SIP amount for this scheme. No other member action is required beyond platform-level configuration updates.

## Important Dates

Effective immediately from July 01, 2026, the date of both the NSE circular and the Motilal Oswal notice cum addendum.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational update affecting only the Daily SIP minimum instalment threshold for a single mutual fund scheme. It lowers the entry barrier for retail investors wishing to start a Daily SIP in the scheme but has no bearing on broader market operations, trading, or listed securities.
