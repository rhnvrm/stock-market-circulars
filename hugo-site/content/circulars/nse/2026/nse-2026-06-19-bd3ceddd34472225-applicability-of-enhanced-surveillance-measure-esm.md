## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department (Circular Ref. No: 456/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/74789) updates the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective June 22–23, 2026. Two new securities are added to ESM Stage I, three securities escalate from Stage I to Stage II (with stricter restrictions), and three securities de-escalate from Stage II back to Stage I.

## Key Points

- **New ESM Stage I additions (effective June 22, 2026):** EIFFL (Euro India Fresh Foods Limited) and PRITIKAUTO (Pritika Auto Industries Limited) — both shifting from EQ/SM to BE/ST series w.e.f. June 23, 2026.
- **Stage I → Stage II escalations (effective June 22, 2026):** NEWJAISA (Newjaisa Technologies Limited), PALREDTEC (Palred Technologies Limited), SAHASRA (Sahasra Electronic Solutions Limited) — subjected to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- **Stage II → Stage I de-escalations (effective June 22, 2026):** AVATAR (AVATAR Industries Limited), EMPOWER (Empower India Limited), LOYALTEX (Loyal Textile Mills Limited).
- Minimum 100% margin applies on all open positions as on June 22, 2026, and on new positions created from June 23, 2026.
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities qualifying under ESM are shifted from Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST) w.e.f. June 23, 2026.
- Securities in ESM Stage II are placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 22, 2026.
- Upon exit from ESM, price bands revert to those applicable before shortlisting, subject to no other surveillance measure being active.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must note the revised ESM securities list and ensure applicable margin collection of minimum 100% for affected securities.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the series change from EQ/SM to BE/ST for newly included and escalated securities effective June 23, 2026.
- For Stage II securities under Periodic Call Auction, members must adhere to the 2% price band constraint.
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026:** Circular issuance date.
- **June 22, 2026:** Effective date for ESM Stage movements (Stage I additions, Stage I↔II transitions) and Periodic Call Auction price band of 2% for Stage II securities.
- **June 23, 2026:** Effective date for series change (EQ/SM → BE/ST) and 100% margin on new positions.
- **June 22, 2026 (end of day):** Reference date for open positions attracting 100% margin.

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities face significantly reduced liquidity and tighter trading conditions. EIFFL and PRITIKAUTO investors will see their holdings restricted to Trade-for-Trade settlement, eliminating intraday trading benefits and netting. NEWJAISA, PALREDTEC, and SAHASRA face the most severe restrictions with a 2% daily price band under Periodic Call Auction, severely limiting price discovery and exit options. AVATAR, EMPOWER, and LOYALTEX see partial relief as they move back to Stage I, though they remain under ESM surveillance. The 100% margin requirement increases the cost of holding or creating positions in all newly added Stage I securities, likely reducing market participation and volumes in these stocks.
