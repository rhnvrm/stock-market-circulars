## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has notified the settlement schedules for the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for February 2026. The circular provides three annexures detailing First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'), Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'), and Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q') schedules.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers February 2026 for all SLBS settlement types
- First Leg Settlement: 20 settlements scheduled from Feb 2-27, 2026
- Reverse Leg Settlement: 13 series from 02/X2 to 02/X2 (next year)
- Reverse Leg Auction: Same 13 series with one-day settlement lag
- Settlement Type 'L' for First Leg, 'P' for Reverse Leg, 'Q' for Auction Reverse Leg
- Reference circular: NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational calendar notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- All participants and custodians must adhere to the published settlement schedules
- Custodial confirmations required on same day as trade date for First Leg settlements
- Settlement dates are T+1 for most First Leg trades, with some T+2 on Feb 19-20, 2026

## Important Dates

**First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'):**
- Trading period: Feb 2-27, 2026
- 20 settlement numbers: 2026022 through 2026041
- Settlement dates: Feb 3, 2026 through Mar 2, 2026

**Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'):**
- Series 02/X2: Settlement on Feb 3, 2026
- Series 03/X3: Settlement on Mar 4, 2026
- Series 04/X4 through 01/X1: Monthly settlements through Jan 5, 2027
- Series 02/X2 (new): Settlement on Feb 2, 2027

**Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q'):**
- Same series as Reverse Leg with settlement one day after transaction date

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Low - This is a standard monthly settlement calendar notification that market participants routinely follow.

**Market Impact:** Minimal - The calendar provides operational clarity for SLBS participants and ensures smooth functioning of securities lending and borrowing activities.

**Participant Action:** Participants and custodians should note the settlement schedules and ensure their systems and operations align with the published dates, particularly noting the T+2 settlement on Feb 19-20, 2026.
