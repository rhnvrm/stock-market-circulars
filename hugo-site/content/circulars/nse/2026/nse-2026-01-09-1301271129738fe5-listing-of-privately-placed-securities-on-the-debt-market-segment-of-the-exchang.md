## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers from five companies to trading on its Debt Market Segment effective January 09, 2026. The listing includes three fresh issues totaling ₹2,875 crores and two re-issues totaling ₹1,925 crores across Tata Capital Housing Finance, Godrej Properties, Godrej Industries, Nxtra Data, and ICICI Securities.

## Key Points

- Total of 5 commercial paper securities listed on debt market segment
- Fresh issues: 3 securities from Tata Capital Housing Finance, Godrej Properties, and Godrej Industries
- Re-issues: 2 securities from Nxtra Data and ICICI Securities
- All securities allotted on January 08, 2026
- Maturity periods range from 69 days to 365 days
- Total face value across all issues: ₹4,800 crores

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members to note the designated security codes for the listed commercial papers
- Securities available for trading on the debt market segment from January 09, 2026
- ISIN codes have been assigned for each security for identification and trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 09, 2026 (listing and trading commencement)
- **Allotment Date**: January 08, 2026 (all securities)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Nxtra Data CP: March 11, 2026 (62 days)
  - Godrej Properties CP: March 18, 2026 (69 days)
  - ICICI Securities CP: March 18, 2026 (69 days)
  - Godrej Industries CP: April 09, 2026 (91 days)
  - Tata Capital Housing Finance CP: January 08, 2027 (365 days)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt market listing that adds liquidity to the commercial paper market for these specific issuers. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt market participants who may trade these instruments. The listing provides secondary market liquidity for institutional investors holding these commercial papers.

**Issuer Details**:
- **Tata Capital Housing Finance**: ₹500 crores (10,000 securities @ ₹5 lakh face value, issue price ₹4.68 lakh)
- **Godrej Properties**: ₹200 crores (4,000 securities @ ₹5 lakh face value, issue price ₹4.94 lakh)
- **Godrej Industries**: ₹75 crores (1,500 securities @ ₹5 lakh face value, issue price ₹4.92 lakh)
- **Nxtra Data**: ₹75 crores re-issue (1,500 securities @ ₹5 lakh face value, issue price ₹4.95 lakh)
- **ICICI Securities**: ₹310 crores re-issue (6,200 securities @ ₫5 lakh face value, issue price ₹4.94 lakh)
