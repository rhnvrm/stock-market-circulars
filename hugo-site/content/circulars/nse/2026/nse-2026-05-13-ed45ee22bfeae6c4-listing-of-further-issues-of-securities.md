## Summary

NSE Circular No. 0777/2026 (Download Ref. NSE/CML/74202) notifies the listing of further issued securities for 16 companies on the NSE Capital Market segment, effective May 14, 2026. The new securities are primarily equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) across the listed companies.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have further securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective May 14, 2026
- All new securities are equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares; market lot size is 1 for all
- No lock-in applies to any of the listed securities
- Securities are identified by their designated codes for trading purposes
- Lot sizes for all securities are as specified in the Annexure (market lot: 1)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governs admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — governs lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. Members are notified to use designated security codes for trading. Trading in the newly listed securities must be conducted in the specified lot sizes.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 13, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 14, 2026 (all newly listed securities admitted to dealings)
- **Allotment Dates (by company):**
  - Alicon Castalloy Limited: April 17, 2026
  - Delhivery Limited: May 9, 2026
  - EPack Prefab Technologies Limited: March 31, 2026
  - Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited: May 5, 2026
  - Gokaldas Exports Limited: May 7, 2026
  - Greaves Cotton Limited: (not fully disclosed in extracted content)
  - ICICI Bank Limited, Kamdhenu Ventures Limited, Maha Rashtra Apex Corporation Limited, Nippon Life India Asset Management Limited, NIIT Learning Systems Limited, Ravindra Energy Limited, Shadowfax Technologies Limited, Tech Mahindra Limited, Fujiyama Power Systems Limited, W S Industries (I) Limited: dates per Annexure

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The listing of ESOP shares increases the floating stock for each of the 16 companies slightly, which could have a marginal dilutive effect on existing shareholders. Key details for the partially disclosed companies:

- **Alicon Castalloy (ALICON):** 75,000 shares (ISIN: INE062D01024, Face Value: Rs. 5)
- **Delhivery (DELHIVERY):** 23,166 shares (ISIN: INE148O01028, Face Value: Re. 1)
- **EPack Prefab Technologies (EPACKPEB):** 163,294 shares (ISIN: INE0MLS01022, Face Value: Rs. 2)
- **Equitas Small Finance Bank (EQUITASBNK):** 287,860 shares (ISIN: INE063P01018, Face Value: Rs. 10)
- **Gokaldas Exports (GOKEX):** 23,000 shares (ISIN: INE887G01027, Face Value: Rs. 5)

No trading restrictions, surveillance measures, or regulatory actions are associated with these listings. Overall market impact is negligible.
