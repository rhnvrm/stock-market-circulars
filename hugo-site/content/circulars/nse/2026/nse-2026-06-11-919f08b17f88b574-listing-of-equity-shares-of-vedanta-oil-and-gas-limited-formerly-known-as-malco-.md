## Summary

Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly known as MALCO Energy Limited) is being listed on NSE pursuant to a scheme of arrangement. The post-scheme shareholding pattern has been prepared based on allotment data as of May 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly MALCO Energy Limited) is listing equity shares pursuant to a scheme of arrangement
- Post-scheme shareholding pattern is based on allotment date of May 5, 2026
- The company has at least one significant beneficial owner
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities/warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged/encumbered promoter shares
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- Scrip code/ISIN at time of filing: NA (to be listed pursuant to scheme)

## Regulatory Changes

This listing arises from a scheme of arrangement under which Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited has been created as a resulting company. The company was formerly known as MALCO Energy Limited and has been renamed as part of the restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Post-scheme shareholding pattern disclosure under Regulation 31(1)(c) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Shareholding pattern reflects allotment as on May 5, 2026
- Company Secretary and Compliance Officer (Shivangni Dhanuka) has certified the shareholding pattern

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** May 5, 2026
- **Shareholding Pattern Reference Date:** May 5, 2026
- **Listing Circular Date:** June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing creates a new publicly traded entity on NSE for Vedanta's oil and gas operations, previously held under MALCO Energy Limited. Investors in the parent/demerged entity who received shares in Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited as part of the scheme will now be able to trade these shares. The absence of pledged promoter shares, locked-in securities, and convertible instruments at listing provides a clean capital structure. The presence of significant beneficial owners warrants monitoring for future disclosure obligations.
