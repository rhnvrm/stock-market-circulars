## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has issued a circular informing members that Madhya Bharat Agro Products Limited (symbol: MBAPL) will undergo a face value split, reducing its equity share face value and paid-up value from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share. The change is effective from July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Madhya Bharat Agro Products Limited (MBAPL)
- Existing face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per equity share
- New face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2 per equity share
- Split ratio: 5:1 (each existing share of Rs. 10 splits into 5 shares of Rs. 2)
- Effective date: July 03, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Ref No: NSE/CML/74880, Circular Ref: 1020/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The face value and paid-up value of MBAPL equity shares will officially change from Rs. 10 to Rs. 2 per share with effect from July 03, 2026, as notified under NSE Circular Ref No. 1020/2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are informed and are expected to update their records accordingly.
- Trading systems, margin calculations, and position limits linked to MBAPL should be adjusted to reflect the new face value of Rs. 2 per share from the effective date.
- Members should communicate this corporate action to their clients holding MBAPL shares.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 25, 2026
- Effective Date of Face Value Split: July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split (5:1 ratio) will result in a proportionate increase in the number of equity shares held by each shareholder, with the share price adjusting downward by a factor of 5 on the ex-date. This makes the stock more accessible to retail investors by reducing the per-share price. Members should review any outstanding F&O contracts on MBAPL for potential adjustments, and ensure margin and position tracking systems are updated ahead of July 03, 2026.
