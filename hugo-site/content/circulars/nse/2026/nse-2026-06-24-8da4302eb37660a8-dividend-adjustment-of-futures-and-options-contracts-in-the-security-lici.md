## Summary

NSE's Futures & Options department has announced the adjustment of options strike prices for Life Insurance Corporation of India (LICI) in accordance with SEBI guidelines for corporate action adjustments. A dividend of Rs 10.00/- per share (equivalent to Rs 20/- per equity share on a pre-bonus issue basis) has been declared, triggering a downward revision of all LICI options strike prices by Rs 10, effective from the ex-date of June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Symbol: LICI (Life Insurance Corporation of India)
- Corporate action type: Dividend
- Face value: Rs 10/-
- Dividend amount: Rs 10.00/- per share (Rs 20/- per share on pre-bonus issue basis)
- Ex-date and effective date: June 25, 2026
- All OPTSTK contracts expiring June 30, 2026 have revised (reduced) strike prices
- Strike prices are adjusted downward by Rs 10 across all listed strikes
- Revised strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places and will be rounded to nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively

## Regulatory Changes

In pursuance of SEBI guidelines for adjustments to futures and options contracts upon announcement of corporate actions, NSE has revised the option strike prices for LICI. The adjustment methodology follows standard SEBI-prescribed procedures. Full adjustment details are available at: https://www.nseindia.com/products-services/equity-derivatives-corporate-actions-adjustments

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated contract.gz / MII contract file (`NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) and spread file (`NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz`) on their trading applications before trading on the ex-date (June 25, 2026)
- These files are available from the directory `faoftp/faocommon` on the Extranet server
- MII contract and spread files are also available on the NSE website at: https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- The methodology for position adjustments will be separately communicated by the respective Clearing Corporation

## Important Dates

- **Circular date:** June 24, 2026
- **Ex-date / Effective date:** June 25, 2026
- **Options expiry date affected:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

All market participants holding open LICI options positions expiring June 30, 2026 will have their strike prices adjusted downward by Rs 10. For example, the 330.00 strike is revised to 320.00, the 335.00 strike to 325.00, and so on across all listed strikes. Members must ensure trading systems are updated with the revised contract files before the market opens on June 25, 2026 to avoid trading on stale strike prices. This is a standard corporate action adjustment with no change to overall economic exposure for position holders.
