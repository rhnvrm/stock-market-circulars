## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of privately placed securities issued by Sammaan Capital Limited on the capital market segment. The security, a 9.05% coupon bond maturing in 2036, will be available for trading from January 20, 2026, with the designated symbol 905SCL36 and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Sammaan Capital Limited's bond admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Trading symbol: 905SCL36 (Series N0)
- ISIN: INE148I07YJ0
- Coupon rate: 9.05%
- Maturity date: January 16, 2036
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Effective date: January 20, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security by its designated code (905SCL36)
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new security listing

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 19, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 20, 2026
- **Bond Maturity:** January 16, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single privately placed debt security. The listing provides investors with a tradable platform for Sammaan Capital Limited's bonds. The market lot size of 1 provides flexibility for retail and institutional investors. No disruption to existing trading operations is expected.
