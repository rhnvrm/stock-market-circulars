## Summary

NSE has informed members that the NSE MF Invest Platform will undergo scheduled maintenance and will be completely unavailable on January 17, 2026, between 01:00 hrs and 02:00 hrs (1-hour window). Members are advised to refrain from all activities and transactions during this period.

## Key Points

- Complete platform downtime scheduled for maintenance activity
- Downtime window: January 17, 2026, from 01:00 hrs to 02:00 hrs
- Duration: 1 hour
- All transactions and activities must be suspended during this period
- Members connecting via API should inform their technical teams/vendors
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a system maintenance notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified downtime window
- Members should plan their activities accordingly to avoid disruptions
- API-connected members must inform their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled maintenance

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Maintenance Start**: January 17, 2026, 01:00 hrs
- **Maintenance End**: January 17, 2026, 02:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Low - The one-hour maintenance window is scheduled during early morning hours (01:00-02:00 hrs) when trading activity is typically minimal or non-existent. This timing minimizes disruption to normal business operations.

**Market Impact**: Negligible - The downtime affects only the NSE MF Invest Platform for mutual fund transactions, not equity or derivatives trading. The brief duration and off-hours timing ensure minimal impact on members and investors.

**Member Actions Required**: Members should plan mutual fund investment activities outside the maintenance window and ensure their technical teams are aware if using API connectivity.
