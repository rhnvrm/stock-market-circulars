## Summary

CMR Green Technologies Limited has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) following its IPO listing on June 8, 2026. The total issued equity shares stand at approximately 219.06 million, with the promoter group holding 84% and the public holding the remaining 16%.

## Key Points

- Total shares outstanding: 219,055,489 equity shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 10 shareholders holding 184,006,611 shares (84.00%)
- Public shareholders: 151,915 holders with 35,048,878 shares (16.00%)
- All shares are held in dematerialized form
- Locked-in shares exist (applicable to promoter and post-IPO lock-in norms)
- Outstanding ESOPs granted by the company
- Significant beneficial owner(s) identified
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or depository receipts issued
- No promoter shares pledged or subject to Non-Disposal Undertakings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine statutory disclosure under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 31(1)(a), filed post-IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- CMR Green Technologies Limited has submitted the shareholding pattern as required under Reg. 31(1)(a) on the date of listing.
- The company has confirmed the absence of partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, and depository receipts.
- Locked-in shares have been disclosed as applicable under SEBI IPO lock-in norms.
- Outstanding ESOPs and significant beneficial ownership have been duly declared.

## Important Dates

- **IPO Listing / Record Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Shareholding Pattern Filing Date:** June 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of CMR Green Technologies Limited introduces a new equity scrip on NSE. With 84% promoter holding and only 16% public float (approximately 35.05 million shares across 151,915 public shareholders), the free float is relatively limited, which may affect liquidity and price discovery in early trading sessions. The presence of locked-in shares further constrains near-term supply. Investors should note the outstanding ESOPs, which could dilute shareholding upon future exercise. Overall market impact is moderate and localized to this scrip.
