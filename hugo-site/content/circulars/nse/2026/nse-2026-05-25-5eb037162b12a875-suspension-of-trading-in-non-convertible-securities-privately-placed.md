## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in three privately placed Non-Convertible Securities (NCS) effective May 26, 2026. The suspension applies to debt instruments issued by NTPC Limited, TVS Credit Services Limited, and LIC Housing Finance Limited, all due to redemption on that date.

## Key Points

- Trading in three NCS instruments will be suspended from May 26, 2026
- All three suspensions are due to scheduled redemption of the instruments
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Affects the debt market segment only; no impact on equity trading

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension is a standard procedural action under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in NCS are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in the listed ISINs on or after May 26, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 26, 2026 (for all three securities)

| Sr. No. | Company Name | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---------|-------------|------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | NTPC Limited | INE733E07DG8 | 26-May-2026 | Redemption |
| 2 | TVS Credit Services Limited | INE729N08030 | 26-May-2026 | Redemption |
| 3 | LIC Housing Finance Limited | INE115A07JP4 | 26-May-2026 | Redemption |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt market participants holding positions in these three specific NCS instruments. The suspensions are routine end-of-life actions triggered by scheduled redemptions and do not indicate any financial distress or regulatory concern. Holders of these securities will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
