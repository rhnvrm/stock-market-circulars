## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all members that International Gemmological Institute (India) Limited, listed under the symbol **IGIL**, will undergo a name change effective **May 04, 2026**. The new name will be **International Gemological Institute Limited**.

## Key Points

- Symbol **IGIL** remains unchanged.
- Existing name: International Gemmological Institute (India) Limited.
- New name: International Gemological Institute Limited.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73911 | Circular Ref. No: 0691/2026.
- Issued by Jeetendra Rangnani, Manager, NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No structural regulatory changes. This is a straightforward corporate name change as communicated by NSE's Listing Department to all exchange members.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are required to take note of the name change and update their records accordingly. No additional compliance action is specified in this circular.

## Important Dates

- **May 04, 2026**: Effective date of the name change.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The trading symbol IGIL is unchanged, so no disruption to trading is expected. Investors and members should update company name references in their systems and documentation ahead of May 04, 2026.
