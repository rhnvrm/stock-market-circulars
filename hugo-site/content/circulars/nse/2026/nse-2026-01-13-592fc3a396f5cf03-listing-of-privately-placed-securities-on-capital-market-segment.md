## Summary

NSE has admitted market-linked debentures issued by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited to trading on the capital market segment. The securities will be available for trading from January 14, 2026, under the symbol AAFS30C with a market lot size of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited
- Trading Symbol: AAFS30C
- Series: N0
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- Issue Description: AAFS Market Linked 2030
- Coupon Rate: Market Linked (variable)
- Maturity Date: September 24, 2030
- ISIN Code: INE0L6807278
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (AAFS30C) for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- All trading regulations applicable to capital market segment securities apply

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: January 13, 2026
- Listing Effective Date: January 14, 2026
- Securities Maturity Date: September 24, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative circular affecting only trading members dealing with privately placed debt securities. The listing provides an additional trading instrument on NSE's capital market segment. Market-linked coupon structure means returns are variable and tied to underlying market conditions. Impact is limited to participants interested in this specific debt instrument.
