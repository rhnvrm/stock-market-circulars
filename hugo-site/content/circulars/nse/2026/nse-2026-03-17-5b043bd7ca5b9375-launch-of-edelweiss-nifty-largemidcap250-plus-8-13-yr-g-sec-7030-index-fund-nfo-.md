## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund NFO by Edelweiss Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 18, 2026 to April 1, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). This is a hybrid index fund combining large and mid-cap equity exposure (Nifty LargeMidcap250) with medium-to-long duration government securities (8-13 yr G-Sec) in a 70:30 ratio.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: March 18, 2026 to April 1, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: April 9, 2026
- 10 scheme variants offered across Direct and Regular plans with Growth, Monthly IDCW Payout, Monthly IDCW Reinvestment, Quarterly IDCW Payout, and Quarterly IDCW Reinvestment options
- Category: Equity
- Fund house: Edelweiss Mutual Fund
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification for NSE members facilitating transactions on the MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before **April 7, 2026 by 6:00 PM**.
- Members should facilitate client transactions within the NFO window per standard NSE MF Invest Platform procedures.

## Important Dates

- **March 18, 2026**: NFO opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **April 1, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO closes
- **April 7, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account
- **April 9, 2026**: Allotment date for all 10 scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low broader market impact. It is an operational notification to NSE members about the availability of a new mutual fund NFO on the MF Invest Platform. The fund's 70:30 equity-to-G-Sec structure targets investors seeking blended exposure to large/mid-cap equities and government bonds. Members acting as distributors or facilitators on the NSE MF Invest Platform need to ensure timely fund collection before the April 7 clearing deadline.
