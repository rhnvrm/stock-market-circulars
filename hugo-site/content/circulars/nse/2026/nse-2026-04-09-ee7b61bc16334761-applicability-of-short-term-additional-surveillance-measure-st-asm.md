## Summary

NSE has announced the list of securities shortlisted under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Framework Stage I, effective April 10, 2026. This circular identifies 59+ securities that have been placed under enhanced surveillance due to unusual price/volume movements or other market-related concerns. Securities under ST-ASM are subject to additional margin requirements and trading restrictions.

## Key Points

- 59+ securities have been shortlisted under ST-ASM Framework Stage I effective April 10, 2026
- Securities span multiple sectors including pharmaceuticals, technology, infrastructure, energy, and financial services
- Notable inclusions: EASEMYTRIP (Easy Trip Planners), HTMEDIA (HT Media), EMUDHRA, DREAMFOLKS, JTLIND (JTL Industries), KESORAMIND (Kesoram Industries)
- Securities are identified by Symbol, Security Name, and ISIN in the annexure
- ST-ASM Stage I typically involves enhanced surveillance with additional margin requirements

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply additional margin requirements for all securities listed under ST-ASM Stage I
- Clients/investors holding or trading these securities must be aware of enhanced surveillance status
- Brokers must inform clients about the ST-ASM applicability and associated trading conditions
- All trades in these securities on and after April 10, 2026 will be subject to ST-ASM Stage I conditions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** April 10, 2026 — ST-ASM Stage I applicability begins for all listed securities
- **Circular Date:** April 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a high market impact as it places 59+ securities under the ST-ASM framework, which typically results in:
- **Increased margin requirements** for traders and investors in these securities
- **Reduced liquidity** as higher margins discourage speculative trading
- **Potential price corrections** as leveraged positions may be unwound
- **Investor awareness** about unusual price/volume activity in these securities

The list includes a mix of small-cap and mid-cap companies across diverse sectors. Notably, some well-known names like EASEMYTRIP, HTMEDIA, EMUDHRA, DREAMFOLKS, and KESORAMIND are included, which may attract market attention. Securities placed under ST-ASM are subject to periodic review, and may be moved to higher stages or removed based on subsequent price/volume behavior.
