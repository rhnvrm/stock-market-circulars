## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited (trading symbol: CORDELIA) on the Capital Market segment effective July 01, 2026. The company operates in the leisure tourism space under the Cordelia Cruises brand. The IPO was priced at Rs. 808 per share with a face value of Rs. 10 each, and a total of 72,394,543 equity shares are admitted to dealings.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: CORDELIA, Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE0LZF01013
- Issue price: Rs. 808 per share (face value Rs. 10)
- Total shares listed: 72,394,543
- Market lot: 1 share
- Pari Passu: Yes
- The scrip will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited), Mumbai
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Mr. Ankit Satish Shah

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying the admission of securities to dealings and specifying lot sizes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated code (CORDELIA) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1
- Members must observe pre-open session requirements for IPO category scrips as mandated by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 30, 2026
- Effective date for trading: July 01, 2026
- Lock-in periods:
  - 50,203,071 shares locked until July 02, 2027
  - 14,478,909 shares locked until July 02, 2029
  - 472,464 shares locked until January 02, 2027
  - 1,629,022 shares locked until July 28, 2026
  - 1,629,023 shares locked until September 26, 2026
  - 3,982,054 shares free (no lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited (Cordelia Cruises) on NSE opens trading in a new leisure/tourism sector equity. The IPO issue price of Rs. 808 per share implies a significant market capitalisation. The majority of shares (approximately 89.7%) are subject to lock-in restrictions ranging from mid-2026 to mid-2029, limiting the free float to approximately 3,982,054 shares initially. This relatively tight free float may lead to heightened price volatility in early trading sessions. Investors should note the pre-open session participation for price discovery on listing day.
