## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced an auction (A 2026026) to address shortages in settlement numbers M 2026025 and M 2026026. The auction will be conducted on February 09, 2026 with settlement on February 10, 2026. Pay-in time for the auction at Clearing Corporation is 08:00 AM on February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Shortages from M 2026025 (trade date: February 05, 2026) and M 2026026 (trade date: February 06, 2026) will be assigned to Auction A 2026026
- Both CC (Clearing Corporation) and internal shortages will be included in the auction
- Settlement M 2026025 had custodial confirmation on February 06, 2026 with settlement date February 06, 2026
- Settlement M 2026026 had custodial confirmation on February 09, 2026 with settlement date February 09, 2026
- This circular follows NCL/CMPT/72664 dated February 6, 2026 regarding auctions for settlement nos. 2026023 and 2026024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard auction process for handling settlement shortages.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians/PCMs with shortages must participate in Auction A 2026026
- Participants must adhere to auction cut-off timings for pay-in at depositories
- Pay-in must be completed by 08:00 AM on February 10, 2026 at Clearing Corporation
- Special attention required to revised settlement time on February 10, 2026

## Important Dates

- **February 05, 2026**: Trade date for M 2026025
- **February 06, 2026**: Trade date for M 2026026; Custodial confirmation for M 2026025; Settlement date for M 2026025
- **February 07, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 09, 2026**: Custodial confirmation for M 2026026; Settlement date for M 2026026; Auction trade date (A 2026026)
- **February 10, 2026**: Auction settlement date; Pay-in time at CC: 08:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to members with delivery shortages in the specified settlements. This is a standard operational procedure to resolve settlement failures.

**Operational Impact**: Members with shortages must arrange funds and securities for auction participation. The revised settlement timing on February 10, 2026 requires careful attention to cut-off times at depositories to avoid further penalties.

**Contact**: Members can reach NSE Clearing at 18002660050 (Option 2) or securities_ops@nsccl.co.in for queries.
