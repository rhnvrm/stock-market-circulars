## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing members about the forthcoming listing of units of Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust (InvIT IPO) on the Exchange. The security details have been provided, but the actual listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust (InvIT) is scheduled to be listed on NSE
- Trading symbol: **RIIT**
- ISIN: **INE2PB023011**
- The listing date has not been specified in this circular and will be announced separately
- Circular issued by the Listing Department of NSE (Ref No: NSE/CML/73411, Circular Ref. No: 0490/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It serves as an informational notice to NSE members about an upcoming listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing of Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust units and await the separate circular that will confirm the exact listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 23, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust (RIIT) as an InvIT IPO on NSE expands the infrastructure investment trust segment on the exchange. Investors seeking exposure to infrastructure assets through a regulated trust structure will have a new option once the listing is formalized. The actual market impact will depend on the listing date and subscription details to be communicated in the subsequent circular.
