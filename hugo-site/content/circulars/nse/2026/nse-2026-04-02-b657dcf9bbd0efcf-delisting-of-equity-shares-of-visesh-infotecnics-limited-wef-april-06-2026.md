## Summary

NSE has notified the compulsory delisting of equity shares of Visesh Infotecnics Limited (Symbol: VISESHINFO) effective April 06, 2026. The delisting is being effected under Rule 21(2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957, following the compulsory delisting of the company by BSE Limited.

## Key Points

- Visesh Infotecnics Limited (VISESHINFO) will be delisted from NSE effective April 06, 2026
- Delisting is compulsory, initiated following BSE Limited's earlier compulsory delisting action
- Action is taken under Rule 21(2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957
- Trading in VISESHINFO equity shares on NSE will cease from April 06, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Rule 21(2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957, NSE is withdrawing admission to dealings in the equity shares of Visesh Infotecnics Limited. This is a consequential action following the company's compulsory delisting by BSE Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and brokers must cease accepting or placing orders in VISESHINFO equity shares on NSE from the effective date
- Investors holding VISESHINFO shares should be aware that the shares will no longer be tradeable on NSE or BSE post April 06, 2026
- Depositories and clearing corporations should update their records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 02, 2026
- **Effective Delisting Date:** April 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The compulsory delisting of Visesh Infotecnics Limited from NSE (following its delisting from BSE) results in a complete loss of exchange-traded liquidity for shareholders. Investors holding VISESHINFO shares will be unable to sell their holdings through normal exchange mechanisms after April 06, 2026. Shareholders may need to explore off-market transactions or await any exit opportunity that may be provided under applicable SEBI delisting regulations. This is a high-impact event for existing shareholders of the company.
