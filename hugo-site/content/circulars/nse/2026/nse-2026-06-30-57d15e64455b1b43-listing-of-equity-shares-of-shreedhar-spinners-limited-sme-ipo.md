## Summary

NSE has disseminated the post-issue shareholding pattern of Shreedhar Spinners Limited under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, in connection with the listing of its equity shares following an SME IPO. The shareholding pattern is filed as on 29.06.2026 under the scrip code/name SHREEDHAR (Equity).

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Shreedhar Spinners Limited are being listed on the NSE SME platform pursuant to completion of its SME IPO.
- The shareholding pattern disclosure is filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) as the post-issue shareholding statement.
- The company has declared no partly paid-up shares, no outstanding convertible securities, no warrants, no outstanding ESOPs, and no depository receipts.
- The company has confirmed the presence of locked-in shares, consistent with standard promoter/anchor lock-in requirements applicable to SME IPOs.
- No promoter shares are pledged or subject to non-disposal undertakings or other encumbrances.
- The company does not have equity shares with differential voting rights and has no significant beneficial owner declared.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing and disclosure filing under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, applicable to newly listed SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Shreedhar Spinners Limited is required to continue periodic shareholding pattern disclosures under Regulation 31, including disclosure of any future changes to locked-in shares, promoter holdings, or encumbrances.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: 29.06.2026
- Circular/listing update date: 30.06.2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables public trading of Shreedhar Spinners Limited's equity shares on the NSE SME platform, providing liquidity and price discovery for investors who participated in the IPO. The impact is largely confined to SME-segment investors and the company itself, with limited relevance to the broader market.
