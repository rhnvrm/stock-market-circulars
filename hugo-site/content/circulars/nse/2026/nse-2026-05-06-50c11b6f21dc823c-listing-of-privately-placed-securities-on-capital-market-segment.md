## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities of Clix Capital Services Private Limited on the capital market segment, effective May 07, 2026. The security is a 9.7% coupon non-convertible debenture maturing in May 2029, admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Clix Capital Services Private Limited's debt security admitted to dealings on NSE capital market segment from May 07, 2026
- Security symbol: 97CCSP29, Series: N0, Market Lot: 1
- ISIN: INE157D07EQ9
- Coupon rate: 9.7% per annum
- Maturity date: May 05, 2029
- Issue description: CCSP 9.7% 2029
- Trading shall be conducted using the designated security code only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is pursuant to existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size specification) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (97CCSP29) for all trading in this security
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1
- All trades must comply with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- Circular date: May 06, 2026
- Effective date for trading: May 07, 2026
- Security maturity date: May 05, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal broad market impact. It provides liquidity for holders of Clix Capital Services' privately placed 9.7% debentures by enabling secondary market trading on NSE. Impact is limited to participants interested in this specific debt instrument.
