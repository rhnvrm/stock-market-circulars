## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has notified the applicability of the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long Term-ASM) framework for several securities effective June 22, 2026. Three securities are newly included in Stage I, one security is moved from Stage I to Stage II, and no securities appear in Stage IV (Trade-for-Trade shift) or Stage II to Stage III movement in this circular. A 100% margin requirement will be imposed on all affected securities from June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3 securities added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I effective June 22, 2026: DAICHI, KODYTECH, and RPTECH (DAICHI and RPTECH moved from Short-Term ASM to Long-Term ASM)
- 1 security moved from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II effective June 22, 2026: MTARTECH (Mtar Technologies Limited)
- No securities shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage IV in this circular (Nil)
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage III in this circular (Nil)
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 23, 2026 and new positions from June 24, 2026
- Securities qualifying under criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted from EQ (Rolling Settlement) to BE (Trade-for-Trade) series — not applicable this round
- ASM shortlisting is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued in reference to earlier NSE circulars on ASM: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). The Long-Term ASM framework provisions apply, including staged escalation of surveillance actions and margin requirements.

Price band reinstatement policy: When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band reverts to the pre-ASM applicable band, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Must note and comply with the 100% margin requirement for the listed securities from June 24, 2026 on all open positions as of June 23, 2026 and on new positions thereafter
- **Market Participants**: Must factor increased margin requirements into risk and capital planning for DAICHI, KODYTECH, RPTECH, and MTARTECH
- **Clearing Members**: Must ensure adequate margin collection from clients trading in ASM-listed securities
- ASM framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges

## Impact Assessment

**Securities Affected:**

| Symbol | Company | Action | ISIN |
|--------|---------|--------|------|
| DAICHI | Dai-Ichi Karkaria Limited | Added to LT-ASM Stage I (moved from ST-ASM) | INE928C01010 |
| KODYTECH | Kody Technolab Limited | Added to LT-ASM Stage I | INE0Q7P01013 |
| RPTECH | Rashi Peripherals Limited | Added to LT-ASM Stage I (moved from ST-ASM) | INE0J1F01024 |
| MTARTECH | Mtar Technologies Limited | Moved from LT-ASM Stage I to Stage II | INE864I01014 |

**Trading Impact:** The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or taking positions in these securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes. Retail and leveraged traders will be most impacted as full cash/collateral will be required upfront.

**Stage IV Impact:** No securities are being shifted to Trade-for-Trade (BE series) in this round, so the most restrictive surveillance action is not triggered currently.

**Escalation Risk for MTARTECH:** Movement to Stage II indicates continued surveillance concerns; further non-compliance with criteria could result in progression to higher stages with more restrictive measures.
