## Summary

NSE has suspended the trading rights of OPG Securities Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000192935) with effect from May 21, 2026, before market hours. The suspension is for a period of one day and applies to the Capital Market segment. The action was taken due to non-adherence to regulatory provisions and directions issued by the relevant authority of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- OPG Securities Private Limited is suspended from trading effective May 21, 2026, before market hours
- Suspension duration: one day
- Segment affected: Capital Market
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000192935
- Reason: Non-adherence to regulatory provisions and exchange directions
- All members and investors are requested to take note

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing NSE compliance framework, invoking the Exchange's authority to suspend trading rights for regulatory non-compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and investors who route trades through OPG Securities Private Limited should make alternative arrangements for May 21, 2026
- OPG Securities must rectify the non-compliance with regulatory provisions and exchange directions to resume trading rights

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 21, 2026 (before market hours)
- **Suspension Duration:** One day

## Impact Assessment

The suspension directly affects clients of OPG Securities Private Limited who will be unable to trade through this member on May 21, 2026, in the Capital Market segment. The broader market impact is limited given the short one-day duration and single-member scope. Investors with open positions or pending orders through OPG Securities should take immediate note and arrange alternative trading access if required.
