## Summary

NSE has moved two securities — GRAND FOUNDRY LIMITED (GFSTEELS, ISIN: INE534A01028) and UNIVA FOODS LIMITED (UNIVAFOODS, ISIN: INE275F01019) — to Stage IV of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) effective June 10, 2026. Stage IV is the most stringent level of GSM, imposing the harshest trading restrictions including no upward price movement.

## Key Points

- Two securities upgraded to Stage IV GSM: GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS
- Effective date: June 10, 2026
- Trading will be on a Trade-for-Trade (TFT) basis — no netting off allowed
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower as applicable
- Trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or 1st trading day of the week)
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 100% of trade value to be deposited by buyers
- No upward price movement allowed
- Circular Ref No: NSE/SURV/74620 | 428/2026

## Regulatory Changes

These securities are being escalated to Stage IV of GSM in continuation of the GSM framework established by earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/73167, and NSE/SURV/74254). Stage IV introduces the additional restriction of no upward movement on top of Stage III conditions.

**GSM Stage-wise Actions:**
| Stage | Key Actions |
|-------|-------------|
| I | 100% applicable margin, 5% price band |
| II | Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, 50% ASD by buyers |
| III | Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, weekly trading (Monday), 100% ASD by buyers |
| IV | Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, weekly trading (Monday), 100% ASD by buyers, **no upward movement** |

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must take adequate precaution while trading in GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS
- Settlement shall be done strictly on a trade-to-trade basis
- No netting off is permitted
- Buyers must deposit ASD equal to 100% of the trade value before trading
- Members should communicate these restrictions to their clients
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 10, 2026**: Stage IV GSM restrictions become effective for GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS
- **Ongoing**: Trading permitted only on Mondays (or first trading day of the week)

## Impact Assessment

Stage IV GSM represents the maximum level of surveillance-based trading restriction available on NSE. The combination of weekly-only trading, 100% ASD requirement, 5% price band, and prohibition on upward price movement effectively renders these securities near-illiquid. Investors holding positions in GFSTEELS or UNIVAFOODS face significantly reduced exit opportunities and heightened settlement risk. The no-upward-movement restriction means these securities can only trade sideways or decline, making them unsuitable for most retail and institutional participants. Members should alert clients with exposure to these stocks immediately.
