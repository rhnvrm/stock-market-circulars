## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for six companies — Aditya Birla Capital Limited, Kalpataru Limited, The Karnataka Bank Limited, R Systems International Limited, Sanginita Chemicals Limited, and SEPC Limited — all arising from shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). These additional shares will be admitted to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment effective July 27, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, with lot sizes specified per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCAPITAL, ISIN INE674K01013): 290,715 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 17-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 2735816481 to 2736107195; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Kalpataru Limited (KALPATARU, ISIN INE227J01012): 8,100 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 10-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 205913994 to 205922093; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- The Karnataka Bank Limited (KTKBANK, ISIN INE614B01018): 74,042 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP on 16-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 378229886 to 378303927; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- R Systems International Limited (RSYSTEMS, ISIN INE411H01032): 6,213 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted under ESOP on 27-Jun-2026; distinctive number range 166501453 to 166507665; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Sanginita Chemicals Limited (SANGINITA) and SEPC Limited: further issue details listed in the annexure (content truncated in source; core allotment terms follow the same ESOP-based pattern as above).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

Members must recognize the newly listed shares by their designated security codes and trade them only in the specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all listed allotments) effective from the stated date.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 24, 2026
- Effective date for trading of further-issued shares: July 27, 2026
- Allotment dates: 27-Jun-2026 (R Systems), 10-Jul-2026 (Kalpataru), 16-Jul-2026 (Karnataka Bank), 17-Jul-2026 (Aditya Birla Capital)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative circular reflecting incremental increases in listed share capital due to ESOP exercises. It does not affect trading restrictions, pricing, or investor rights, and pari passu status ensures the new shares rank equally with existing shares. No lock-in applies to any of the allotments.
