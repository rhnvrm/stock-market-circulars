## Summary

Bio Medica Laboratories Limited (Symbol: BMLL) has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform with effect from May 29, 2026. The equity shares will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment, Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI circular guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** BMLL | **ISIN:** INE1BKA01015
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 139/- per share | **Face Value:** Rs. 10/-
- **Total Securities Listed:** 1,25,75,001 equity shares
- **Market Lot:** Minimum 1,000 equity shares
- **Market Maker:** Prabhat Financial Services Limited (spread within 10%)
- Shares will be available in the pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips
- Trading in continuous market as per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes) and SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code BMLL for all trading activity
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1,000 equity shares
- Market Maker (Prabhat Financial Services Limited) must maintain spread within 10%
- Lock-in restrictions on promoter shares must be observed as per Annexure I

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Listing Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Period 1:** 2,550,000 shares (Distinctive Nos. 1–25,50,000) locked in until May 28, 2029
- **Lock-in Period 2:** 3,000,000 shares (Distinctive Nos. 25,50,001–55,50,000) — lock-in end date partially available in source document

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new SME company to the NSE EMERGE platform. Initial trading under Trade for Trade Surveillance (Series ST) limits speculative activity, as each trade results in mandatory delivery settlement. Market Maker participation by Prabhat Financial Services Limited provides liquidity support. The issue price of Rs. 139 against a face value of Rs. 10 reflects a significant premium. Investors and members dealing in SME segment securities should note the 1,000-share minimum lot size and the lock-in restrictions applicable to a significant portion of promoter holdings through May 2029.
