## Summary

NSE has notified members that the trading symbol of Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited will change from "AMIRCHAND" to "AEROPLANE" with effect from July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Symbol: AMIRCHAND
- New Symbol: AEROPLANE
- Company Name: Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited
- Circular Ref. No: 1145/2026
- Download Ref No: NSE/CML/75196

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative change in the trading symbol under which the company's shares are listed and traded on NSE; no change to ISIN, corporate structure, or shareholding is indicated.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems, order management tools, and market watch lists to reflect the new symbol "AEROPLANE" before July 20, 2026.
- Back-office and reconciliation systems referencing the old symbol "AMIRCHAND" should be updated to avoid settlement or reporting mismatches.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 20, 2026 (symbol change effective from this date)
- Circular Date: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational rather than financial. Trading members and investors need to ensure orders are placed using the new symbol "AEROPLANE" post the effective date to avoid failed or rejected orders. No impact on underlying share value, ownership, or corporate actions is indicated by this circular.
