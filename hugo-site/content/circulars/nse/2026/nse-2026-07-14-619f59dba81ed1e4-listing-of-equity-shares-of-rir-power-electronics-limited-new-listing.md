NSE has issued a circular pertaining to the listing of equity shares of RIR Power Electronics Limited on the exchange as a new listing. The accompanying document (SHP_RIR.pdf) provides the company's shareholding pattern disclosure under Regulation 31(1)(b), covering the quarter ended 30-06-2026.

- Scrip code: 517035

- NSE Symbol: Not yet listed (NOTLISTED) at the time of this filing
- MSEI Symbol: Not yet listed (NOTLISTED)

- Company classified as: Non-SME
- Type of report: Quarterly shareholding pattern
- Quarter ended: 30-06-2026
- Not classified as a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, or locked-in shares reported
- ESOPs have been granted and are outstanding for the promoter group and public categories
- No pledged or encumbered promoter shareholding reported
- No differential voting right shares or significant beneficial owners reported

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular; it documents the listing event and associated shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, specifically Regulation 31(1)(b).

## Compliance Requirements

RIR Power Electronics Limited is required to maintain and periodically update its shareholding pattern disclosures in compliance with LODR Regulation 31(1)(b). Market participants and depositories should update records to reflect the company's new listing status on NSE.

The listing of RIR Power Electronics Limited's equity shares on NSE expands the tradable universe for market participants and provides a new avenue for price discovery and liquidity in the stock.
