## Summary

Trust Mutual Fund's TrustMF Mid Cap Fund will reopen for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 24, 2026. Both the Direct Plan and Regular Plan (Growth option) will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- TrustMF Mid Cap Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions from March 24, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Two schemes covered: Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF0GCD01875) and Regular Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF0GCD01883)
- Both schemes are classified under the Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding platform availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the availability of these schemes for client transactions from March 24, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 24, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of TrustMF Mid Cap Fund for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular informs NSE members that TrustMF Mid Cap Fund schemes are being made available for ongoing transactions after a closure period. Investors and members can transact in these two equity mid-cap schemes via the NSE MF Invest Platform starting March 24, 2026. No systemic or broad market implications.
