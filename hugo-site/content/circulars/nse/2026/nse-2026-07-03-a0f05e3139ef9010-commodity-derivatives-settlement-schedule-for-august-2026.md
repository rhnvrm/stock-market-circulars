## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for the month of August 2026. The circular lists each trading date in August 2026 along with its corresponding settlement date, enabling NSE members to plan their settlement obligations accordingly.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No: 0239/2026, Download Ref No: NCL/COM/75025, issued on July 03, 2026
- Applicable to all NSE members trading in the Commodity Derivatives Segment
- Settlement generally follows a T+1 cycle, with trade date and settlement date mapped for every trading day in August 2026
- Some dates reflect adjustments for weekends/holidays (e.g., trade date 26-Aug-2026 settles on 27-Aug-2026, same as 25-Aug-2026's settlement)
- Final trade date of the month (31-Aug-2026) settles on 01-Sep-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic publication of the settlement calendar for the Commodity Derivatives Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members must align their back-office, funds, and securities settlement processes with the published schedule to ensure timely settlement of commodity derivatives trades executed in August 2026.
