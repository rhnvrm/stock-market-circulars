## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued Circular Ref. No. 0252/2026 (Download Ref No. NCL/COM/75212) dated July 15, 2026, notifying members of the Settlement Calendar for Compulsory Delivery contracts and the GST details reporting calendar applicable to the Commodity Derivatives Segment for contracts expiring in August 2026. The calendars cover delivery-based commodities including gold variants (GOLDM, GOLD1G, GOLD), silver and related variants (SILVERM, SILVER100, GOLDGUINEA, GOLD10G), and base metals (ALUMINIUM, ALUMINI, COPPER, LEAD, LEADMINI, NICKEL, ZINC, ZINCMINI).

## Key Points

- Annexure 1 sets out the delivery settlement schedule (pay-in/pay-out of commodities and funds) for compulsory delivery-based futures contracts expiring in August 2026.
- Annexure 2 sets out the GST details confirmation schedule, specifying seller and buyer member GST details uploading windows for each expiry batch.
- Applicable to gold products (GOLDM, GOLD1G, GOLD), silver and related products (SILVERM, SILVER100, GOLDGUINEA, GOLD10G), and base metals (ALUMINIUM, ALUMINI, COPPER, LEAD, LEADMINI, NICKEL, ZINC, ZINCMINI).
- Members must have valid GST registration applicable to the jurisdiction of the relevant delivery centers and obtain any other necessary licenses.
- Sellers raising invoices must include GST registration number, HSN code, and other statutory particulars, and must timely file all statutory GST returns.
- Seller Members/Clients must ensure timely delivery of the GST invoice to Buyer Members/Clients as per the confirmed details.
- The prescribed timelines are maximum timelines; members are advised to complete the invoicing process at the earliest.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic notification of the settlement and GST reporting calendar for the upcoming expiry month, issued under existing Commodity Derivatives Segment clearing and settlement norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must maintain valid GST registration for applicable delivery center jurisdictions and required licenses for accepting/giving delivery of commodities.
- Sellers must raise GST-compliant invoices with registration number, HSN code, and other mandated particulars, and file statutory GST returns within stipulated timelines.
- Members must adhere to the specified GST details uploading windows (separate for seller and buyer members) for each commodity/contract expiry batch.
- Timely delivery of GST invoices from Seller Member/Client to Buyer Member/Client is required as per confirmed details.

## Important Dates

- GOLDM/GOLD1G/GOLD contracts: expiries on 03-Aug-26, 04-Aug-26, and 05-Aug-26, with GST uploading windows beginning 29-Jul-26 through 31-Jul-26 (seller) and 30-Jul-26 through 03-Aug-26 (buyer).
- SILVERM/SILVER100/GOLDGUINEA/GOLD10G contracts: expiries on 04-Aug-26, 05-Aug-26, and 06-Aug-26, with corresponding GST uploading windows.
- ALUMINIUM/ALUMINI/COPPER/LEAD/LEADMINI/NICKEL/ZINC/ZINCMINI contracts: expiries on 05-Aug-26, 06-Aug-26, and 07-Aug-26 (per Annexure 2), with GST uploading windows starting 29-Jul-26.
- Full delivery pay-in/pay-out schedule (dates and times) is detailed in Annexure 1 for each commodity group.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular affecting clearing members and clients participating in compulsory delivery of commodity derivatives contracts (bullion and base metals) on NSE's Commodity Derivatives Segment. It does not directly impact listed equity securities or their trading but is important for members to ensure smooth physical settlement and GST compliance for August 2026 expiries, avoiding penalties or settlement delays.
