## Summary

NSE Circular Ref. No. 1104/2026 (Download Ref. No. NSE/CML/75089, dated July 07, 2026) notifies the listing of further issues of securities for ten companies, effective July 08, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The further issues predominantly relate to equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP).

## Key Points

- Ten companies have further equity issues admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment: Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited, Dixon Technologies (India) Limited, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited, Intense Technologies Limited, Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited, L&T Finance Limited, Pine Labs Limited, Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited, Tata Communications Limited, and Varun Beverages Limited.
- Aptus Value Housing Finance (APTUS): 50,000 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on 26-Jun-2026; distinctive number range 500742737 to 500792736; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Dixon Technologies (DIXON): 56,210 equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP on 03-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 61085922 to 61142131; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- ICICI Lombard General Insurance (ICICIGI): 57,538 equity shares of Rs. 10 each allotted under ESOP on 03-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 499272165 to 499329702; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Intense Technologies (INTENTECH): Equity shares of Rs. 2 each allotted under ESOP (details truncated in source document).
- Details for Kotak Mahindra Bank, L&T Finance, Pine Labs, Solara Active Pharma Sciences, Tata Communications, and Varun Beverages were not fully captured in the available excerpt but are covered under the same circular and effective date.
- All listed securities will be identified by their designated security codes and traded in the specified lot sizes from the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative notification under existing NSE Trading Regulations governing admission of further security issues to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize the additional shares under the existing designated security codes and lot sizes from the effective date.
- No separate action required from investors; the newly allotted ESOP shares become fungible and pari passu with existing listed equity shares.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date: July 08, 2026 (listing of further issues effective from this date)
- Allotment dates: 26-Jun-2026 (Aptus), 03-Jul-2026 (Dixon, ICICI Lombard, and others as applicable)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative circular reflecting standard ESOP-related share allotments across ten well-established, actively traded companies. The additional share quantities are small relative to each company's total outstanding shares, so no material effect on stock price, liquidity, or shareholding structure is expected. No trading halts or restrictions are imposed; all new shares are pari passu and freely tradable with market lot of 1.
