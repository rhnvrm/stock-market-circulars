## Summary

NSE Circular NSE/CML/74648 (Ref. 0933/2026) dated June 10, 2026 notifies the listing of further issued securities for 15 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective June 11, 2026. The additional shares are primarily allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and are pari passu with existing equity shares with no lock-in.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have further securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from June 11, 2026
- All allotments are pari passu with existing equity shares with no lock-in restrictions
- Securities are identified by designated codes; trading lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- All allotments are under ESOP schemes with allotment dates ranging from May to June 2026
- Issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification and trading
- Trading in these securities must be in the specified lot sizes (1 for all securities)
- This circular is effective from June 11, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Allotment Dates:** May 7, 2026 (EPack Prefab); May 25, 2026 (Awfis Space); May 29, 2026 (CG Power); other dates for remaining companies

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP-related further issues with small quantities relative to total outstanding share capital. All shares are immediately tradeable with no lock-in. The 15 companies span diversified sectors including financial services (ESAF Small Finance Bank, Fedbank Financial Services, ICICI Bank, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, Laxmi India Finance), industrials (CG Power, EPack Prefab, Godawari Power, Polycab, Shaily Engineering), consumer (Awfis Space Solutions, Motisons Jewellers, Parag Milk Foods, Havells India), and energy (KPI Green Energy). No material dilution or trading disruption expected.
