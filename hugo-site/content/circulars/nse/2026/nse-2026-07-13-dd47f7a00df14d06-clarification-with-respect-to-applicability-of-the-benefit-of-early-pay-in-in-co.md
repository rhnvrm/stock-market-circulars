## Summary

NSE has forwarded a SEBI circular (dated June 19, 2026) clarifying the applicability of the benefit of early pay-in in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. The clarification revises paragraph 11.3.1 of the SEBI Master Circular for the Commodity Derivatives Segment (dated August 4, 2023), specifying the treatment of margins for positions against which early pay-in of certified goods has been made to Clearing Corporation accredited warehouses.

## Key Points

- SEBI circular reference: HO/47/16/13(4)2026-MRD-POD1/I/14266/2026 dated June 19, 2026
- Revises para 11.3.1 of Chapter 11 of the SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2023/136 for Commodity Derivatives Segment (dated August 4, 2023)
- Revision follows recommendations from the Working Group (WG) on Review of regulatory framework for delivery/settlement in Agricultural Commodity Derivatives, and the Commodity Derivatives Advisory Committee (CDAC)
- Clearing Corporations must continue to provide early pay-in facility allowing market participants to deposit certified goods at accredited warehouses against derivatives contracts
- Based on risk perception, Clearing Corporations may exempt all types of margins for positions with early pay-in, but must continue to collect mark-to-market (MTM) margins on such positions

## Regulatory Changes

Paragraph 11.3.1 of the Master Circular is revised to clarify that while Clearing Corporations may exempt all types of margins (based on risk perception) for positions against which early pay-in has been made, mark-to-market margins must still be collected on such positions. This removes ambiguity on whether MTM margins are covered under the general margin exemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Stock Exchanges and Clearing Corporations with Commodity Derivatives Segments must make necessary system changes to implement the revised provision, notify their members, and disseminate the circular on their websites.

## Important Dates

- SEBI circular date: June 19, 2026
- NSE circular date: July 13, 2026
- Effective date: September 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The clarification primarily affects Clearing Corporations' margin frameworks and trading members dealing in Commodity Derivatives who utilize the early pay-in facility. It ensures continued collection of MTM margins even when other margin types are exempted, reducing risk exposure for Clearing Corporations while preserving the incentive of margin relief for early pay-in. Impact on broader equity markets or listed stocks is minimal, as this pertains specifically to commodity derivatives settlement mechanics.
