## Summary

NSE's Listing (CML) department has notified the admission of further equity shares to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from **June 2, 2026**. The shares were allotted to employees under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) by 16 listed companies. The notification is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Sixteen companies have had additional ESOP-allotted shares admitted to NSE trading from June 2, 2026.
- All securities are in the EQ series with a market lot of 1 and are pari passu with existing shares.
- Lock-in is not applicable for any of the newly listed tranches.
- Each security is identified by its designated NSE symbol and ISIN for trading purposes.
- Lot sizes for all newly listed securities are specified as 1 (standard market lot).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The circular is a routine operational notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated NSE security codes when trading these securities.
- Trading must be conducted in the lot sizes specified in the Annexure (Market Lot: 1 for all listed securities).
- No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | June 1, 2026 |
| Effective Date (Listing) | June 2, 2026 |
| ESOP Allotment – Aditya Birla Capital, Axis Bank, BLACKBUCK | May 26, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with minimal market impact. The newly admitted ESOP shares represent incremental dilution across 16 companies and are available for trading from June 2, 2026. Key allotments include:

- **Aditya Birla Capital (ABCAPITAL):** 756,007 equity shares (₹10 face value), ISIN INE674K01013
- **AU Small Finance Bank (AUBANK):** 183,972 equity shares (₹10 face value), ISIN INE949L01017
- **Axis Bank (AXISBANK):** 326,279 equity shares (₹2 face value), ISIN INE238A01034
- **BLACKBUCK LIMITED (BLACKBUCK):** Shares (₹1 face value), ISIN INE0UIZ01018
- Additional allotments for DCB Bank, Globus Spirits, Gopal Snacks, HDFC Bank, HDFC Life Insurance, IDFC First Bank, Neogen Chemicals, Sai Life Sciences, S.A.L. Steel, Tata Motors, Veranda Learning Solutions, and V-Mart Retail as per the Annexure.

Given the ESOP nature of these allotments and pari passu status, no price adjustments or trading restrictions apply. Overall market impact is negligible.
