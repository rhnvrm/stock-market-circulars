## Summary

NSE issued circular 0602/2026 on April 9, 2026 notifying the admission of further securities to dealings on the Capital Market segment for 12 companies with effect from April 10, 2026. The allotments span ESOP exercises and a preferential issue, covering both large-cap and mid/small-cap companies.

## Key Points

- Securities of 12 companies admitted to NSE Capital Market segment effective April 10, 2026
- Aurionpro Solutions: 48,134 equity shares allotted on preferential basis at Rs. 1,454.3 per share (allotment date: December 19, 2025); lock-in expiry October 24, 2026
- Axis Bank: 1,49,462 equity shares allotted under ESOP (allotment date: April 7, 2026); no lock-in
- AYM Syntex: 33,000 equity shares allotted under ESOP (allotment date: March 27, 2026); no lock-in
- Fedbank Financial Services: 21,000 equity shares allotted under ESOP (allotment date: April 1, 2026); no lock-in
- InterGlobe Aviation (IndiGo): 43,000 equity shares allotted under ESOP (allotment date: April 8, 2026); no lock-in
- Moneyboxx Finance: Equity shares at issue price Rs. 76 (full details in PDF annexure)
- Additional companies in annexure: NACL Industries, One 97 Communications (Paytm), Sarveshwar Foods, Tata Consumer Products, TREJHARA Solutions, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares of respective companies
- Market lot is 1 for all listed securities
- Circular issued by Srishti Soni, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission of securities to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot size specification)

## Compliance Requirements

- Member brokers must use the designated NSE security codes for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (1 share per lot for all securities)
- Aurionpro Solutions shares allotted on preferential basis are subject to lock-in until October 24, 2026; trading restrictions apply accordingly
- No lock-in restrictions apply to ESOP-allotted shares of Axis Bank, AYM Syntex, Fedbank Financial Services, and InterGlobe Aviation

## Important Dates

- **April 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 10, 2026**: Effective date — all listed securities admitted to dealings on NSE
- **October 24, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for Aurionpro Solutions preferential allotment shares
- **Allotment dates by company**: Aurionpro (Dec 19, 2025), Axis Bank (Apr 7, 2026), AYM Syntex (Mar 27, 2026), Fedbank Financial Services (Apr 1, 2026), InterGlobe Aviation (Apr 8, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The overall market impact is moderate and localized to the individual stocks. The largest volume addition is Axis Bank with 1,49,462 ESOP shares, though this is negligible relative to Axis Bank's multi-billion share float. Aurionpro's preferential allotment of 48,134 shares at Rs. 1,454.3 is more meaningful proportionally for a mid-cap stock and carries a lock-in until October 2026, limiting immediate supply. ESOP listings for AYM Syntex, Fedbank Financial Services, and InterGlobe Aviation involve small quantities with no lock-in, posing minor near-term selling pressure. Investors should monitor Moneyboxx Finance and the remaining companies (NACL Industries, Paytm, Sarveshwar Foods, Tata Consumer Products, TREJHARA, Ujjivan SFB) via the full PDF annexure for complete allotment details.
