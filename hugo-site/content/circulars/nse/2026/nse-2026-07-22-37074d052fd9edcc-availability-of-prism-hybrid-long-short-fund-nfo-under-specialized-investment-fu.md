## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Prism Hybrid-Long Short Fund NFO, launched under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework by Jioblackrock Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Prism Hybrid-Long Short Fund NFO under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF), offered by Jioblackrock Mutual Fund.
- Two plan variants are available for ongoing transactions:
  - Prism Hybrid-Long Short Fund Direct Growth (ISIN: INF22M030027)
  - Prism Hybrid-Long Short Fund Regular Growth (ISIN: INF22M030019)
- Both schemes are categorized as Equity.
- Facilities enabled include ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Available on the NSE MF Invest Platform for member transactions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an operational notice enabling ongoing transaction facilities for a scheme previously launched under the SIF framework.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members offering mutual fund transaction services should update their systems and client-facing platforms to reflect availability of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities for the specified ISINs from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 23, 2026 — ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities become available for the scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a low market-wide impact as it pertains to operational enablement of a specific mutual fund scheme on NSE's MF distribution platform. It is primarily relevant to NSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors interested in the Prism Hybrid-Long Short Fund, facilitating continued access to investment and redemption facilities post the NFO period.
