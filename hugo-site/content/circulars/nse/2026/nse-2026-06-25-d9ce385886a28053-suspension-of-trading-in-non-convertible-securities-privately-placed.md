## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the suspension of trading in four privately placed Non-Convertible Securities issued by Piramal Finance Limited and Power Finance Corporation Limited. The suspension takes effect from June 29, 2026, in all cases due to redemption of the instruments, as per Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Four NCS instruments across two issuers are being suspended from trading on June 29, 2026
- All suspensions are triggered by redemption of the respective securities
- The action is taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Piramal Finance Limited: 1 ISIN affected
- Power Finance Corporation Limited: 3 ISINs affected

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is a standard enforcement action under the existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs the suspension of debt securities upon maturity or redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the listed ISINs from June 29, 2026
- No further buy/sell orders should be placed for these securities on or after the suspension date
- Investors holding these securities should follow up with their respective depositories or registrars for redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | June 25, 2026 |
| Suspension Effective Date | June 29, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The suspension impacts holders of these four specific ISINs who will lose the ability to trade the securities on NSE from June 29, 2026. As the reason is redemption, investors will receive redemption proceeds through the normal settlement process. The impact is limited to the holders of these instruments and does not affect the broader equity or debt markets. Power Finance Corporation holders should note that three separate ISINs are being redeemed simultaneously.
