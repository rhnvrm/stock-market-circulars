## Summary

NSE's Capital Market Segment has announced a change of market maker for Paramatrix Technologies Limited (Symbol: PARAMATRIX), an SME-listed company. Effective April 01, 2026, Kalpalabdhi Financials Private Limited (Member Code: 90149) will replace SVCM Securities Pvt Ltd (Member Code: 90216) as the designated market maker.

## Key Points

- Market maker for PARAMATRIX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED is being changed on the NSE SME platform.
- Outgoing market maker: SVCM Securities Pvt Ltd (Member Code: 90216).
- Incoming market maker: Kalpalabdhi Financials Private Limited (Member Code: 90149).
- The change is effective from April 01, 2026.
- All NSE members are advised to take note of this change.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update to the designated market maker assignment for an SME-listed security.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members trading in PARAMATRIX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED should update their records to reflect the new market maker — Kalpalabdhi Financials Private Limited (Member Code: 90149) — effective April 01, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 30, 2026
- **Effective Date of Change:** April 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading activity in PARAMATRIX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED on the NSE SME platform. The change of market maker is a routine operational matter and does not affect the company's listing status, trading rules, or broader market operations. Continuity of liquidity provision for the scrip should be maintained under the new market maker.
