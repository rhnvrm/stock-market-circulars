## Summary

NSE has announced that Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly known as MALCO Energy Limited), trading under symbol VOGL, will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (Series: BE) to the rolling segment (Series: EQ) effective June 30, 2026. This follows Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74666 dated June 11, 2026 and is pursuant to SEBI guidelines under circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- VOGL shares will move from BE (trade for trade) series to EQ (rolling) series
- Effective date: June 30, 2026
- This is a follow-up to NSE circular NSE/CML/74666 dated June 11, 2026
- The transfer is in accordance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The security transitions out of the trade for trade (T+1 compulsory delivery) segment into the standard rolling settlement segment, restoring normal intraday trading and netting capabilities for VOGL.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members should note the series change for VOGL and update their systems accordingly prior to June 30, 2026. No action is required from investors beyond awareness of the segment change.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Previous circular NSE/CML/74666 issued placing VOGL in trade for trade
- **June 15, 2026**: This circular issued
- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from BE to EQ series

## Impact Assessment

The transfer back to the rolling segment (EQ) is a positive development for market participants trading VOGL, as it restores normal settlement mechanics including intraday trading and netting of positions. The stock was previously placed in the trade for trade segment as a surveillance measure. This change has low broader market impact but is operationally relevant for members and investors holding or trading VOGL.
