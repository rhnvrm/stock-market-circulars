## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issued equity securities for nine companies on the Capital Market segment, effective April 08, 2026. The securities have been allotted primarily under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs), with one case involving equity shares allotted pursuant to a bonus issue previously kept under abeyance (ICICI Bank). Trading in these securities will be identified by their designated codes as specified in the Annexure.

## Key Points

- Nine companies have new securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment effective April 08, 2026
- Allotments are primarily under ESOP schemes; ICICI Bank shares were allotted pursuant to a Bonus Issue (date of allotment: June 24, 2017) previously kept under abeyance
- All new securities are pari passu with existing equity shares of the respective companies
- No lock-in applies to any of the listed securities
- Market lot for all securities is 1
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must trade these securities using only the designated security codes as specified in the Annexure
- Trading must be conducted in the lot sizes specified
- No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **April 07, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 08, 2026**: Effective date — securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- **Allotment dates of underlying securities**:
  - Angel One Limited (ANGELONE): March 30, 2026 — 1,804,070 shares (ESOP)
  - E2E Networks Limited (E2E): March 31, 2026 — 1,500 shares (ESOP)
  - ICICI Bank Limited (ICICIBANK): June 24, 2017 — 240 shares (Bonus Issue, abeyance resolved)
  - L&T Finance Limited (LTF): April 02, 2026 — 313,000 shares (ESOP)
  - Meesho Limited (MEESHO): March 27, 2026 — 36,312,662 shares (ESOP)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these further issues represents a modest increase in tradeable float for the affected companies. The largest volume addition is Meesho Limited with over 36.3 million new shares, followed by Angel One Limited with approximately 1.8 million shares and L&T Finance with 313,000 shares. ICICI Bank's addition of 240 shares from a 2017 bonus issue abeyance resolution is negligible in market terms. Overall market impact is low-to-medium; ESOP-related listings are routine and typically well-anticipated by the market. The Meesho listing is the most notable given the large share volume.
