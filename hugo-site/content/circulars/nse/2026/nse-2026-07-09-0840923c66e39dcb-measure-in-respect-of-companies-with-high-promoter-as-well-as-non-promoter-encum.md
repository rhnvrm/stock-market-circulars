## Summary

NSE has issued a surveillance circular updating the list of securities subject to the measure targeting companies with high Promoter as well as non-Promoter 'Encumbrance' under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011. This is a continuation of Exchange circular NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022. Share India Securities Limited (SHAREINDIA) has been newly included under this framework, while no securities have been excluded in this cycle. A consolidated list of seven securities currently under this measure has been provided.

## Key Points

- Share India Securities Limited (SHAREINDIA, ISIN: INE932X01026) has been newly added to the encumbrance surveillance framework (Annexure I).
- No securities have been excluded from the framework in this cycle (Annexure II shows 'Nil').
- The consolidated list of securities under this measure (Annexure III) now comprises seven companies: Aqylon Nexus Limited (AQYLON), B. L. Kashyap and Sons Limited (BLKASHYAP), Elpro International Limited (ELPROINTL), Jayaswal Neco Industries Limited (JAYNECOIND), Share India Securities Limited (SHAREINDIA), Steel Exchange India Limited (STEELXIND), and Thyrocare Technologies Limited (THYROCARE).
- This measure operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion under this measure is explicitly stated not to be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

No change to the regulatory framework itself; this circular is a periodic update under the existing measure established via NSE/SURV/51189 dated January 31, 2022, which tracks companies with high promoter and non-promoter share encumbrance as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must apply a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments on all open positions in SHAREINDIA as of July 13, 2026, and on new positions created from July 14, 2026 onward. Members should also account for the consolidated list of securities under this framework when assessing margin and risk requirements for client trading activity.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Reference date for open positions in newly included securities.
- July 14, 2026: Minimum 75% margin requirement becomes effective for SHAREINDIA (on open positions as of July 13 and new positions thereafter).
- July 10, 2026: Effective date for exclusions from the framework (none this cycle).

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of SHAREINDIA under this high-margin surveillance framework significantly raises the cost of trading and holding leveraged positions in the stock, likely reducing speculative activity and derivative exposure. Market participants with existing positions in SHAREINDIA should reassess margin requirements ahead of the July 14, 2026 effective date. The impact is limited to the affected securities and does not indicate adverse findings against the companies, but it does signal elevated promoter/non-promoter share encumbrance risk that investors may wish to monitor.
