## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited is conducting a mock trading session in the Electronic Gold Receipts (EG) Segment on January 17, 2026. The session is designed to help members familiarize themselves with the trading system before live operations. The mock session will run from 16:30 to 18:40 hours with various trading windows including pre-open, normal market, and block deal sessions.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for January 17, 2026 in EG Segment
- Members to use Capital Market segment credentials (user ID, password, box IDs, IPs) for login
- Trading session runs from 16:30 to 18:40 hours from primary site
- Pre-open session: 16:30 to 16:38 (random closure in last minute)
- Normal market: 16:45 to 18:30
- Block deal window: 17:40 to 17:55
- Trade modification end time: 18:40
- Password credentials replicated from Capital Market segment as on January 14, 2026 EOD
- Default password on reset: "Neat@CM1"
- Co-location facility not available for EG Segment
- Members required to use dummy client codes during order entry

## Impact Assessment

This is a practice session with no impact on live markets. The mock trading exercise allows members to test their systems and familiarize themselves with the Electronic Gold Receipts trading platform before actual trading commences. No real trades will be executed, and no compliance obligations arise from this circular beyond voluntary participation for preparedness.
