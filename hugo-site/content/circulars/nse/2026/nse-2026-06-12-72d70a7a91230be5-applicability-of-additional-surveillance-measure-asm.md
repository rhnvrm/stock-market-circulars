## Summary

NSE has issued a circular (Ref No: 440/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/74696) regarding the applicability of the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework. The circular reaffirms the surveillance actions applicable under Long Term ASM but no new securities have been shortlisted or moved between stages in this update — all annexure lists show "Nil".

## Key Points

- Applicable margin rate under Long Term ASM is 100% effective June 17, 2026, on all open positions as of June 16, 2026, and all new positions created thereafter.
- Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) are to be shifted from Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) effective June 17, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Upon exit from the ASM framework, the price band of a scrip reverts to the band applicable before it was shortlisted, subject to no other surveillance measure being active.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular references and reaffirms the ASM framework established under earlier circulars: NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024).

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members**: Note that no securities are being added to or moved within the Long Term ASM framework in this circular update.
- **Market Participants**: Maintain 100% margin on positions in any securities that are or become subject to Long Term ASM.
- For FAQs on ASM, refer to: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure
- Queries may be directed to: surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date referenced in Annexure for any ASM stage changes (all lists are Nil for this update).
- **June 16, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement.
- **June 17, 2026**: Effective date for 100% margin applicability and Trade-for-Trade segment shift for Criteria VII securities.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate market impact as no securities have been added to or moved within any Long Term ASM stage (all annexure lists are Nil). It is a routine periodic update reiterating the existing ASM framework rules. Market participants with positions in Long Term ASM-listed securities should already be compliant with the 100% margin requirement. No specific stocks are affected by this particular issuance.
