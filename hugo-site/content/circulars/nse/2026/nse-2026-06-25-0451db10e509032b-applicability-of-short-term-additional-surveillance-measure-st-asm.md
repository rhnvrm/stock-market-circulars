## Summary

NSE has shortlisted five securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I framework effective June 29, 2026, with enhanced margin requirements applicable from June 30, 2026. No securities have been added to Stage II, and none are moving from Stage I to Stage II.

## Key Points

- Five securities included under ST-ASM Stage I w.e.f. June 29, 2026: CCCL, ICIL, PGIL, POKARNA, SPECIALITY
- Stage II inclusions: Nil; Stage I to Stage II migrations: Nil
- Stage I margin requirement: 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Stage II margin requirement: 100% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Margin requirements apply to all open positions as on June 29, 2026 and all new positions from June 30, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to NSE circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 (dated October 27, 2018; December 4, 2020; April 28, 2022; September 25, 2023; and September 20, 2024 respectively) governing the ASM framework. No structural change to the ASM framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must collect a minimum margin of 50% (or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) on all positions in the five ST-ASM Stage I securities from June 30, 2026
- Members must apply the enhanced margin to open positions as of June 29, 2026 and to any new positions created from June 30, 2026 onwards
- Members should note that price band reinstatement for any scrip exiting the framework will revert to the pre-ASM price band, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date for shortlisting of securities under ST-ASM Stage I
- **June 30, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements (50% minimum for Stage I) become applicable on all open positions as of June 29, 2026 and new positions

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in CCCL, ICIL, PGIL, POKARNA, or SPECIALITY will face increased margin obligations starting June 30, 2026. The 50% minimum margin requirement for Stage I scrips can significantly increase the cost of carrying leveraged positions, potentially leading to forced position reductions for margin-constrained participants. The absence of Stage II additions and Stage I-to-Stage II migrations limits the severity of this circular's overall market impact. The measure is a standard surveillance intervention; the underlying companies are not subject to any regulatory action.
