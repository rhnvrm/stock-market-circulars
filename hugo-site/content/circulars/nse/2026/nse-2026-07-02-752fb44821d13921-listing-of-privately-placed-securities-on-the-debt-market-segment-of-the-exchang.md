## Summary

NSE has notified, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, that a set of privately placed securities have been admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 02, 2026. The annexure lists newly issued commercial papers (CPs) and other debt securities (NCDs) along with their designated security codes and issue details.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue commercial papers admitted for: Tata Steel Limited, Godrej Agrovet Limited, ICICI Securities Limited (two CPs), and IIFL Capital Services Limited (two CPs).
- Other debt securities (NCDs) admitted for: Lodha Developers Limited (LDL RESET 2030), IIFL Samasta Finance Limited (ISF 9.20% 2028), and Profectus Capital Private Limited (PCPL 9.75% 2027 Sr RR).
- Each security carries a designated ISIN, face value, issue price, and dates of allotment and redemption as specified in the annexure.
- Lodha Developers' NCD (INE670K07364) has a reset coupon with call/put option date of 15-Feb-2029 and redemption on 15-Feb-2030.
- IIFL Samasta Finance's NCD (INE413U07475) carries a fixed coupon of 9.20% maturing 30-Dec-2028.
- Profectus Capital's NCD (INE389Z07120) carries a fixed coupon of 9.75% maturing 30-Sep-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations confirming admission of privately placed securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from members beyond noting the new security codes for trading purposes on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 02, 2026
- Tata Steel CP (INE081A14HE2): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 29-Sep-2026
- Godrej Agrovet CP (INE850D14VN6): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 31-Jul-2026
- ICICI Securities CP (INE763G14L52): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 31-Mar-2027
- ICICI Securities CP (INE763G14L45): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 14-Jun-2027
- IIFL Capital Services CP (INE489L14967): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 31-Aug-2026
- IIFL Capital Services CP (INE489L14975): Allotted 01-Jul-2026, redeems 30-Sep-2026
- Lodha Developers NCD (INE670K07364): Allotted 30-Jun-2026, redeems 15-Feb-2030
- IIFL Samasta Finance NCD (INE413U07475): Allotted 30-Jun-2026, redeems 30-Dec-2028
- Profectus Capital NCD (INE389Z07120): Allotted 30-Sep-2024, redeems 30-Sep-2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with low market impact. It primarily concerns debt market participants and institutional investors who trade in commercial papers and privately placed NCDs on NSE's Debt Market Segment. There is no direct impact on equity share trading or the listed companies' equity stock prices.
