## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued guidance to all Members, Custodians, and PCMs regarding the early pay-in (EPI) of securities for E2E Networks Limited (Symbol: E2E) in connection with an upcoming face value split. The split reduces the face value from Rs 10 per share to Re 1 per share, with the record date and ex-date both set for June 5, 2026. Members must follow specific ISIN usage rules depending on the EPI date and settlement number.

## Key Points

- E2E Networks Limited (E2E) is undergoing a face value split (sub-division) from Rs 10/- per share to Re 1/- per share.
- Record date and ex-date are both June 5, 2026.
- Existing/Old ISIN: INE255Z01019.
- For EPI on June 5, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026104): use old/existing ISIN.
- For EPI on June 8, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026104 & 2026105): use new ISIN.
- For a sale of 10 shares, the EPI in the depository should be in the old ISIN for a quantity of 1 share (pre-split equivalent).
- Custodians providing EPI through pool accounts must upload client-wise EPI allocation details based on actual traded quantity (e.g., 10 shares).
- In case of EPI through block mechanism, the client-wise EPI allocation file should NOT be uploaded.
- Members must adhere to NCL circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022 regarding SEBI's block mechanism in demat accounts.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular references existing procedures under the consolidated circular NCL/CMPT/73996 (dated April 30, 2026), Item 10.18, pertaining to early pay-in of securities for margin exemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must execute EPI instructions in the old ISIN (INE255Z01019) for the ex-date/record date (June 5, 2026) without adjusting for the conversion ratio.
- After the record date, EPI/pay-in instructions must be executed in the new ISIN.
- Custodians using pool accounts for EPI must upload client-wise EPI allocation details reflecting actual traded quantities.
- Members using block mechanism for EPI must not upload the client-wise allocation file.

## Important Dates

- **June 5, 2026**: Ex-date and record date for the face value split; EPI in old ISIN for Settlement No. 2026104.
- **June 8, 2026**: EPI in new ISIN for Settlement Nos. 2026104 and 2026105.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on members and custodians trading E2E Networks Limited around the corporate action date. The face value split requires careful ISIN management during the transition period to avoid settlement errors. The quantity adjustment rule (1 share in old ISIN for every 10 shares sold) is critical for compliance. Failure to follow the prescribed procedure could result in settlement failures or incorrect margin exemption claims. The impact is limited to participants with positions in E2E Networks Limited.
