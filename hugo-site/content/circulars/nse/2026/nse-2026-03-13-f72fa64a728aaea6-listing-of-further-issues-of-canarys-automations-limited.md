## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,548,780 additional equity shares of Canarys Automations Limited (Symbol: CANARYS, Series: SM) on the NSE SME Emerge platform. These shares were allotted on November 20, 2025, under a Preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 41 per share (face value Rs. 2). Trading in these securities is effective from March 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Canarys Automations Limited
- **Symbol:** CANARYS | **Series:** SM | **Segment:** SME Emerge
- **ISIN:** INE0QG301017 (permanent; currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8* per SEBI circular)
- **Face Value:** Rs. 2 per share | **Issue Price:** Rs. 41 per share
- **Number of Shares Listed:** 1,548,780
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 57,196,114 to 58,744,893
- **Market Lot:** 4,000 shares
- **Pari Passu:** Yes — new shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** November 20, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 16, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is carried out in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specification

ISIN activation follows SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) for additional issue of shares in dematerialized mode.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol CANARYS and series SM for all trades in these securities.
- Trading must be conducted in the prescribed market lot of 4,000 shares.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the applicable tranches of shares (see Important Dates below).

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 20, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 16, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry — 512,194 shares** (Distinctive Nos. 57,196,114–57,708,307): September 15, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry — 975,610 shares** (Distinctive Nos. 57,708,308–58,683,917): September 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry — 60,976 shares** (Distinctive Nos. 58,683,918–58,744,893): September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,548,780 new shares to the tradable pool for Canarys Automations Limited on the SME Emerge platform from March 16, 2026. However, a significant portion of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions — approximately 512,194 shares are locked in until September 2027, and the remaining ~1,036,586 shares until September 2026. This limits the immediate free-float increase. The circular has minimal broader market impact and is relevant primarily to existing shareholders and prospective investors in this SME-listed company.
