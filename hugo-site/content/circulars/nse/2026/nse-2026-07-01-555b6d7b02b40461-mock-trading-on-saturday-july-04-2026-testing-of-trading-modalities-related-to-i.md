## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market segment on Saturday, July 04, 2026, to test trading modalities related to the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment. This follows the earlier circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026 on CAS trading modalities, and continues testing initiated via the December 24, 2025 circular. A new NEATPlus version (7.8.9) will be introduced specifically for CAS-related testing during Trading Session-1 only; members must reinstall the earlier NEATPlus version (7.8.8) for Trading Sessions 2 and 3, which will run under existing (non-CAS) trading modalities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading conducted on Saturday, July 04, 2026 across three trading sessions.
- Trading Session-1 uses new NEATPlus version 7.8.9 to test CAS-related functionality.
- Trading Sessions 2 and 3 require reinstallation of NEATPlus version 7.8.8 and run without CAS changes.
- Trading Session-3 will be conducted from the Disaster Recovery (DR) site.
- Session-1 introduces new CAS-specific timings: CAS open time, CAS market order restriction start time, and CAS close time, in addition to the standard Normal Market close time.
- Live re-login window for members is from 18:00 to 19:30 hrs.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced by this circular itself; it operationalizes testing for the previously announced introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash segment (per circular NSE/CMTR/74466 dated May 29, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate in the mock trading session on July 04, 2026.
- Members must install NEATPlus version 7.8.9 prior to Trading Session-1 for CAS testing.
- Members must uninstall/reinstall and log in with NEATPlus version 7.8.8 before Trading Sessions 2 and 3.
- Members should complete live re-login between 18:00 and 19:30 hrs as specified.

## Important Dates

- Mock trading date: July 04, 2026 (Saturday)
- Trading Session-1: 07:45 to 10:30 hrs (Normal Market close 10:00 hrs; Closing Session 10:00–10:30 hrs)
- Trading Session-2 (Primary Site): 12:30 to 16:00 hrs
- Trading Session-3 (DR Site): 16:30 to 18:00 hrs
- Live re-login window: 18:00 to 19:30 hrs

## Impact Assessment

This is a controlled, one-day mock testing exercise with no impact on live trading or listed securities. Its purpose is to validate system readiness (NEATPlus 7.8.9) and trading modalities ahead of the planned rollout of the Closing Auction Session in the Equity Cash segment. Trading members and their technology teams need to ensure timely software version switches between sessions to participate correctly; failure to do so could affect their ability to test CAS functionality but has no bearing on regular market operations.
