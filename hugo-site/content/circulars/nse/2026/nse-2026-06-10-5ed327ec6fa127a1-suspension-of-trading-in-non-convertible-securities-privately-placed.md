## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed) of TVS Credit Services Limited effective June 11, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the redemption of the security under ISIN INE729N07057, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- TVS Credit Services Limited NCD (ISIN: INE729N07057) will be suspended from trading effective June 11, 2026.
- Suspension reason: Redemption of the security.
- Issued under Circular Ref. No. 0927/2026 (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74634).
- Applies to the NSE Debt Market segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notification is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of trading in debt securities upon redemption or other triggering events.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified to cease trading in ISIN INE729N07057 (TVS Credit Services Limited NCD) from June 11, 2026 onwards.
- Members should ensure no new orders are placed or executed in this security on or after the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine lifecycle event for a privately placed NCD approaching redemption. The suspension affects only holders and traders of this specific security (ISIN: INE729N07057) issued by TVS Credit Services Limited. Existing holders should expect redemption proceeds as per the terms of the security. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
