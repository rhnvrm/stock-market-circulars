## Summary

NSE has announced adjustments to Futures and Options contracts for ITC Limited (Symbol: ITC) pursuant to SEBI guidelines on corporate action adjustments. ITC Limited has declared a dividend of Rs 8.00 per share on a face value of Re 1/-, with an ex-date and effective date of May 27, 2026. Revised option strike prices and other contract parameters will be communicated one day prior to the ex-date.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** ITC (ITC Limited)
- **Corporate Action:** Dividend of Rs 8.00/- per share (face value Re 1/-)
- **Ex-date / Effective date:** May 27, 2026
- Revised option strike prices will be published one day before the ex-date
- Adjusted strike/futures base prices and lot sizes may appear in decimal places and will be rounded to the nearest tick size and nearest integer respectively
- Updated contract files (contract.gz / MII contract and spread files) will be available one day prior to the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are being made in pursuance of SEBI guidelines for futures and options contracts upon announcement of corporate actions. The methodology for position adjustments will be separately intimated by the respective Clearing Corporation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated `NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz` and `NSE_FO_spdcontract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz` files on their trading applications **before trading on the ex-date (May 27, 2026)**
- Files are available from the `faoftp/faocommon` directory on the Extranet server
- Files are also available on the NSE website at: https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Adjustment details can be referred to at: https://www.nseindia.com/products-services/equity-derivatives-corporate-actions-adjustments

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | May 22, 2026 |
| Revised strike prices published | May 26, 2026 (one day prior to ex-date) |
| Ex-date / Effective date | May 27, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

All members with open Futures and Options positions in ITC will be subject to contract adjustments on account of the Rs 8.00 dividend. The dividend represents 800% of the face value, which is a substantial corporate action likely to cause a meaningful downward adjustment to option strike prices and futures base prices. Members must ensure trading systems are updated with revised contract files before the market opens on May 27, 2026 to avoid trading on stale contract parameters.
