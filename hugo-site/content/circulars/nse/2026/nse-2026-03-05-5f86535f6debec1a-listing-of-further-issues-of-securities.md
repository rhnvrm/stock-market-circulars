## Summary

NSE's Listing Department (Circular Ref. No. 0389/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73151) notifies admission of further equity shares to dealings on the Capital Market segment for nine companies, effective March 06, 2026. All securities were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing shares with no lock-in.

## Key Points

- Nine companies have had additional equity shares admitted to NSE's Capital Market segment effective March 06, 2026
- All allotments are under ESOP schemes and rank pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in applies to any of the newly listed shares
- Market lot for all securities is 1
- Trading will use the existing designated security codes for each company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A — lot size specifications

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members or market participants. The circular is informational, notifying that these securities are now admitted to dealings under their existing trading symbols and codes.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. ESOP allotments represent routine share capital increases for employee compensation purposes. The volumes involved are small relative to existing share capital for large-cap companies like ICICI Bank and Aditya Birla Capital. No change in trading mechanics, surveillance, or member obligations. Existing shareholders experience marginal dilution, which is typical and expected for ESOP-based allotments.
