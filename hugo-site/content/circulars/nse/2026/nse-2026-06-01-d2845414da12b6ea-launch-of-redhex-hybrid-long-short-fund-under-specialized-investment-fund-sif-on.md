## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the RedHex Hybrid Long-short Fund under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category of HSBC Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 02, 2026 to June 16, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The fund offers 10 scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans, with allotment scheduled for June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- RedHex Hybrid Long-short Fund (SIF) by HSBC Mutual Fund is now available on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Transaction window: June 02, 2026 to June 16, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).
- Allotment date for all 10 scheme variants: June 19, 2026.
- All 10 scheme variants are categorised as **Debt** by the exchange.
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 19, 2026 by 11:30 am.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling a new SIF scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure clear funds via any accepted payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before **June 19, 2026 by 11:30 am**.
- Members should make the scheme available for client transactions on the platform during the subscription window.

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Subscription window opens on NSE MF Invest Platform.
- **June 16, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: Subscription window closes.
- **June 19, 2026 (11:30 am)**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account.
- **June 19, 2026**: Allotment date for all 10 scheme variants.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to NSE members who distribute or facilitate mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in hybrid long-short strategies under the SIF framework from HSBC Mutual Fund should note the narrow subscription window of approximately two weeks. No existing securities or market operations are affected.
