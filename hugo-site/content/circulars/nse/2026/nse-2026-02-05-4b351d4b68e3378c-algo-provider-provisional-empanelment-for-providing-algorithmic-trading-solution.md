## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has provisionally empaneled M/s Quick Algoplus Private Limited as an Algo Provider to provide algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and the provider will offer Whitebox type algorithmic solutions.

## Key Points

- **Vendor Name**: M/s Quick Algoplus Private Limited
- **Type of Algo**: Whitebox
- **Address**: TT Nagar, Pipliya kumar, Nipaniya, Indore- 452010
- **Contact**: Phone: +918357000107
- Empanelment is on provisional basis
- Complete list of empaneled Algo Providers available on NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification regarding empanelment of a new algo provider.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members seeking to use algorithmic trading solutions may engage with the provisionally empaneled vendor. No mandatory compliance requirements imposed by this circular.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 05, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It expands the choice of algo providers available to trading members but does not mandate any changes to existing trading operations. Trading members interested in whitebox algorithmic solutions now have an additional empaneled vendor option. The provisional nature of the empanelment suggests the vendor is in an evaluation period.
