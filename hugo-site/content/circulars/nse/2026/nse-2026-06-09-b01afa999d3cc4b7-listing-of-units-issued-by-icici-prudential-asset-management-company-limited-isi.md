## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund - iSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund on the Capital Market segment, effective June 10, 2026. Two plan variants are listed: Growth Plan (symbol: ISIFAAFG) and Direct Plan Growth (symbol: ISIFAAFDG), both with a face value of Rs. 10/- per unit.

## Key Points

- Listing effective from June 10, 2026 on NSE Capital Market segment
- Growth Plan (ISIFAAFG): 348,651,891 units, ISIN INF109K30059
- Direct Plan Growth (ISIFAAFDG): 129,510,473 units, ISIN INF109K30067
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10/- per unit; Issue price: Rs. 10/-
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: June 05, 2026
- Trustee and AMC: ICICI Prudential Trust Limited / ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited, Nirlon Knowledge Park, Mumbai
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: Computer Age Management Services Private Limited (CAMS), Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to use the designated security codes (ISIFAAFG, ISIFAAFDG) for trading and adhere to the specified market lot size of 1 unit.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 05, 2026
- Listing Effective Date: June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund unit listing with no broad market impact. It enables secondary market trading in units of the ICICI Prudential iSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund on NSE. The combined listed units total approximately 478 million across both plans.
