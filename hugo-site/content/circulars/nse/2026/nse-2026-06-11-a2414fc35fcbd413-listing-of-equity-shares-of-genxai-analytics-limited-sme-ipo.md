## Summary

GenXAI Analytics Limited has been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform effective June 12, 2026. The equity shares will trade under Symbol GENXAI in Series ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, Settlement Type 'W') at an issue price of Rs. 116/- per share. The company is engaged in analytics and is headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan.

## Key Points

- Symbol: GENXAI; ISIN: INE1W0N01014
- Issue price: Rs. 116/- per share; Face value: Rs. 10/-
- Total securities listed: 1,79,43,610 equity shares
- Market lot: 1,200 equity shares (minimum)
- Market Maker: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited; maximum permissible spread within 10%
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai
- Shares initially trade in Series ST (TFTS), subsequently to be shifted to Series SM (Normal Rolling Segment)
- Pre-open session applicable per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1 (securities admitted to dealings) and 2.5.5 (lot sizes). Continuous market trading enabled per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated code GENXAI for trading purposes.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1,200 equity shares.
- Market Maker (Choice Equity Broking Private Limited) must maintain spread within 10%.
- Lock-in conditions as per Annexure I of the circular apply to promoter/pre-issue shares.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: June 12, 2026
- Financial year: April 2026 – March 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing on NSE EMERGE with moderate market impact. The trade-for-trade surveillance mechanism (Series ST) limits speculative activity in early trading days and reduces settlement risk. The market lot of 1,200 shares at Rs. 116/- implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,39,200, consistent with SME segment norms. Investors should note the mandatory shift to Series SM (normal rolling settlement) will occur post the initial surveillance period per SEBI guidelines.
