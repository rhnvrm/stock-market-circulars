## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, identifying 10 securities that have satisfied the criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I effective July 23, 2026. No securities were shortlisted under Stage II, and no securities moved between Stage I and Stage II in either direction. This continues the surveillance framework established in prior circulars dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024.

## Key Points

- 10 securities have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I effective July 23, 2026: Ashima Limited (ASHIMASYN), Mrs. Bectors Food Specialities Limited (BECTORFOOD), C2C Advanced Systems Limited (C2C), Chavda Infra Limited (CHAVDA), eClerx Services Limited (ECLERX), Globesecure Technologies Limited (GSTL), IKIO Technologies Limited (IKIO), Jainik Power Cables Limited (JAINIK), Praveg Limited (PRAVEG), and Vinit Mobile Limited (VMOBILE)
- No securities were shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage II
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II, or from Stage II to Stage I
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

No change to the ST-ASM framework itself; this circular is a routine application of the existing framework to newly identified securities based on surveillance criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must apply the following margin requirements:

- For Stage I securities: Applicable margin rate shall be 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%, effective July 24, 2026 on all open positions as of July 23, 2026 and new positions created from July 24, 2026
- Members should monitor client positions in the listed securities and ensure margin collection complies with the revised rates

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Circular issued (Ref No. NSE/SURV/75335, Circular Ref. No. 552/2026)
- July 23, 2026: Effective date for inclusion of securities under ST-ASM Stage I
- July 24, 2026: Effective date for applicable margin rates on open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (minimum 50%, capped at 100%) for the 10 identified securities will raise trading costs and may reduce speculative activity and liquidity in these stocks, particularly affecting leveraged and intraday positions. Market participants holding these securities should expect increased capital requirements starting July 24, 2026. The impact is company-specific and does not indicate broader market or sector-wide risk.
