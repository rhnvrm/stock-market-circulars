## Summary

NSE has published the Mutual Fund Service System (MFSS) settlement calendar for August 2026, detailing settlement numbers, subscription order dates, and payin/payout schedules for funds and units under both Normal Subscription (T+1) and Liquid Subscription (T-day) categories.

## Key Points

- Annexure 'A' covers Normal Subscription settlements (T+1 cycle) with 21 settlement numbers (2026143 to 2026163) spanning August 3 to August 31, 2026.
- Annexure 'B' covers Liquid Subscription settlements (T-day units settlement) where funds and units payin/payout occur on the same day as the subscription order date.
- Each settlement has a distinct settlement number used for reconciliation between subscription order date and corresponding payin/payout dates.
- Normal subscription funds payin/payout occurs same day as order date, while units payin/payout is settled the next business day (T+1).
- Liquid subscription settles both funds and units on the same day as the order date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic publication of the operational settlement calendar for MFSS transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and MFSS participants must align their subscription processing and fund/unit settlement operations with the published calendar dates for August 2026.

## Important Dates

- Normal Subscription settlement cycle: August 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026143) through August 31, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026163), with final units payout on September 1, 2026.
- Liquid Subscription settlement cycle: August 3, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026143) through late August 2026, with same-day fund and unit settlement.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact and primarily serves as an operational reference for mutual fund distributors, clearing members, and RTAs participating in NSE's MFSS platform to ensure timely and accurate settlement of subscription transactions during August 2026.
