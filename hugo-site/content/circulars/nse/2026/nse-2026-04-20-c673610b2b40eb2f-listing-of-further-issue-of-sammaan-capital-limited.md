## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of 3,497 equity shares of Sammaan Capital Limited (symbol: SAMMAANCAP, series: EQ) on the Capital Market segment, effective April 21, 2026. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE148I01020.

## Key Points

- 3,497 equity shares of Sammaan Capital Limited admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment from April 21, 2026
- Shares are converted from partly paid-up to fully paid-up equity shares
- Symbol: SAMMAANCAP | Series: EQ | ISIN: INE148I01020
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Distinctive number range: 733942452 to 733945948
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares; no lock-in applicable
- Temporary ISIN IN8148I01011 was used at allotment per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify these securities only by the designated code SAMMAANCAP in the EQ series
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The temporary ISIN (IN8148I01011) used at dematerialized allotment will transition to the permanent ISIN (INE148I01020) per SEBI activation guidelines

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 20, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 21, 2026 (shares admitted to dealings)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. Only 3,497 shares are being listed, representing a very small addition to the existing float of Sammaan Capital Limited. This is an administrative listing action following conversion of partly paid-up shares and carries no significant market or price impact.
