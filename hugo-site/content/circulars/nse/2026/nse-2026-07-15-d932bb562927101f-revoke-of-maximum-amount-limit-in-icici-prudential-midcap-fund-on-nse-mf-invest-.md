## Summary

NSE has informed members that, based on communication from ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, the existing limit of ₹2,00,000 per PAN per month on fresh registrations of Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Transfer Plan (STP) for the ICICI Prudential Midcap Fund has been removed on the NSE MF Invest Platform, effective July 16, 2026. The revocation also extends to other systematic transactions and special products/features such as Freedom SIP, SIP Top Up Facility, Booster SIP, Flex STP, Booster STP, Capital Appreciation STP, IDCW transfer-in, and Trigger Facility.

## Key Points

- The ₹2,00,000 per PAN per month restriction on fresh SIP/STP registrations for ICICI Prudential Midcap Fund has been revoked.
- The revocation covers SIP, STP, and other systematic transactions and special features including Freedom SIP, SIP Top Up Facility, Booster SIP, Flex STP, Booster STP, Capital Appreciation STP, IDCW transfer-in, and Trigger Facility.
- The change applies on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
- The existing restriction on fresh/additional lumpsum purchases and switch-ins into the scheme from other ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund schemes continues until further notice.
- All other provisions of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

ICICI Prudential Trust Limited approved acceptance of fresh SIP/STP and related systematic transaction registrations in the ICICI Prudential Midcap Fund without the previous ₹2,00,000 per PAN per month cap, per the notice-cum-addendum to the Scheme's SID and KIM dated July 13, 2026 (Addendum No. 011/07/2026).

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform should update their systems/processes to reflect removal of the monthly cap for fresh SIP/STP registrations in this scheme, while continuing to observe the existing restriction on lumpsum purchases and switch-ins.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Notice-cum-addendum issued by ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company.
- July 16, 2026: Effective date for revocation of the ₹2,00,000 per PAN per month limit on fresh SIP/STP registrations.

## Impact Assessment

The change removes a barrier for investors seeking to start new SIP/STP investments above ₹2,00,000 per month in the ICICI Prudential Midcap Fund, potentially increasing systematic inflows into the scheme. The impact is operational and scheme-specific rather than market-wide, as lumpsum purchase and switch-in restrictions remain in place, indicating continued caution around large one-time inflows into this midcap scheme.
