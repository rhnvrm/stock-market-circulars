## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of additional equity shares for three SME Emerge-listed companies — Grill Splendour Services Limited (BIRDYS), E Factor Experiences Limited (EFACTOR), and Womancart Limited (WOMANCART) — effective March 20, 2026. The new shares arise from preferential allotments, an ESOP grant, and warrant conversions respectively.

## Key Points

- All three securities are listed on the NSE Capital Market segment under the SME Emerge platform in the SM series.
- Grill Splendour Services Limited (BIRDYS) is listing 2,39,745 equity shares allotted under a preferential issue at Rs. 85.61 per share, with a lock-in until October 14, 2026.
- E Factor Experiences Limited (EFACTOR) is listing 43,300 equity shares allotted under an ESOP scheme at face value (Rs. 10), with no lock-in applicable.
- Womancart Limited (WOMANCART) is listing 36,000 equity shares allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on a preferential basis at Rs. 312 per share, with a lock-in until September 30, 2026.
- Equity shares allotted in dematerialised mode are temporarily credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8*********) per SEBI circulars dated August 02, 2012 and September 11, 2012 pending ISIN activation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading in these securities must use the designated security codes and specified lot sizes.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to Grill Splendour Services shares (until October 14, 2026) and Womancart shares (until September 30, 2026).
- ISIN activation must follow the SEBI circular process before permanent ISIN is assigned.

## Important Dates

- **March 20, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading in the further issued shares.
- **February 20, 2026**: Date of allotment for Grill Splendour Services (BIRDYS) shares.
- **February 12, 2026**: Date of allotment for E Factor Experiences (EFACTOR) ESOP shares.
- **December 23, 2025**: Date of allotment for Womancart (WOMANCART) warrant-conversion shares.
- **October 14, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for BIRDYS further issue shares.
- **September 30, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for WOMANCART further issue shares.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders and investors of the three individual SME companies. The addition of new shares increases the floating stock modestly for each company. Lock-in provisions on BIRDYS and WOMANCART shares restrict near-term selling pressure from allottees. EFACTOR's ESOP shares have no lock-in, which may see marginal near-term supply. Overall, there is no broader market impact given the small scale of these SME issuances.
