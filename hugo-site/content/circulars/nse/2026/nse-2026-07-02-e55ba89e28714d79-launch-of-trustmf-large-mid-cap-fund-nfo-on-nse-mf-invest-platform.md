## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Trustmf Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO, launched by Trust Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). The circular details the scheme variants, their ISINs, category, and allotment date, along with the funds pay-in deadline for clearing corporation settlement.

## Key Points

- New Fund Offer (NFO) for Trustmf Large & Mid Cap Fund by Trust Mutual Fund opens for subscription via NSE MF Invest Platform.
- Two scheme variants are available: Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF0GCD01891) and Regular Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF0GCD01909).
- Both variants fall under the Equity category.
- Allotment date for both schemes is July 24, 2026.
- Members must ensure clear funds reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 22, 2026, 12:00 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for trading on the NSE MF Invest Platform; it does not introduce any new regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members transacting on behalf of clients must ensure funds for units subscribed are cleared and credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account through any permitted payment mode on or before July 22, 2026, by 12:00 pm.
- Members should ensure the NFO is offered to clients only within the specified subscription window.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Funds pay-in deadline: July 22, 2026, 12:00 pm
- Allotment date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is a routine operational notification enabling members to offer a new equity mutual fund scheme (Trustmf Large & Mid Cap Fund) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It affects NSE members and investors interested in subscribing to this NFO, and Trust Mutual Fund as the issuing AMC, but does not affect listed securities, indices, or broader trading operations.
