## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Adisoft Technologies Limited on the NSE EMERGE SME Platform effective April 30, 2026. The shares will trade under symbol ADISOFT in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment – Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment – Settlement Type 'N') per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** ADISOFT
- **ISIN:** INE20PL01012
- **Platform:** NSE EMERGE SME Platform
- **Series:** ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment, TFTS – Settlement Type 'W')
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 172/- per share
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Total Securities Listed:** 16,318,000 equity shares
- **Market Lot & Market Maker Minimum Quantity:** 800 equity shares
- **Market Maker Maximum Permissible Spread:** Within 10%
- **Market Maker:** Hem Finlease Private Limited, Jaipur
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent:** Kfin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad
- Shares are available for pre-open session for IPO category scrips per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Shares are available for continuous market trading per NSE Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A – admission to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A – lot sizes
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 – pre-open session and TFTS to SM series transition

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must identify and trade the security using its designated symbol (ADISOFT) and series codes only.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 800 equity shares.
- Members should note that ADISOFT will initially trade in Series 'ST' (TFTS) and will be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment) subsequently as per SEBI guidelines.
- Market Maker (Hem Finlease Private Limited) must maintain spread within 10%.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Effective Date / Listing Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Financial Year:** April 2026 – March 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing on the NSE EMERGE platform with limited broader market impact. The listing involves 16,318,000 equity shares at an issue price of Rs. 172/- per share, implying a total issue size of approximately Rs. 280.67 crore. Initial trading is restricted to the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS), which limits speculative activity and ensures delivery-based settlement. The stock will later migrate to the normal rolling segment. Impact is confined to retail and institutional investors who participated in the IPO and members facilitating trades in this scrip.
