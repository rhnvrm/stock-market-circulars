## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department has shortlisted 5 securities for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective July 1, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from July 2, 2026, on both existing open positions and new positions. No securities were added to Stage II, and no inter-stage movements or exclusions were announced.

## Key Points

- 5 securities added to ST-ASM Stage I effective July 1, 2026
- No securities added to ST-ASM Stage II
- No inter-stage movements (Stage I to II or II to I)
- No securities excluded from ASM framework
- Margin enhancement applies to all open positions as on July 1, 2026, and new positions from July 2, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to ST-ASM Stage I are subject to a minimum margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%. This is pursuant to earlier circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 4, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024 respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply the enhanced margin rate of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, up to 100%) on all open positions in the listed securities as on July 1, 2026
- Enhanced margins must be collected on all new positions in these securities created from July 2, 2026 onwards
- Members should communicate relevant margin changes to their clients holding or trading in the shortlisted securities

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **July 1, 2026**: Securities included in ST-ASM Stage I; margin rules applicable on open positions
- **July 2, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements effective for new positions

## Impact Assessment

The following 5 securities are now under ST-ASM Stage I:

| Sr. No. | Symbol | Security Name | ISIN |
|---------|--------|---------------|------|
| 1 | GNRL | Gujarat Natural Resources Limited | INE207H01018 |
| 2 | MHLXMIRU | Mahalaxmi Rubtech Limited | INE112D01035 |
| 3 | SAKSOFT | Saksoft Limited | INE667G01023 |
| 4 | SKMEGGPROD | SKM Egg Products Export (India) Limited | INE411D01023 |
| 5 | VISL | Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited | INE1CLE01013 |

Traders and investors holding positions in these securities will face significantly higher margin requirements, which may lead to forced liquidations if margin top-up requirements cannot be met. The higher margin cost is likely to reduce trading volumes and increase bid-ask spreads in these securities. No securities were moved to the more restrictive Stage II (100% margin) in this circular.
