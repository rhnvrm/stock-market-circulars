## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has published the Mark to Market (MTM) settlement schedule for the Currency Derivatives Segment (CDS) for the month of June 2026. The schedule covers all trade dates in June 2026 and specifies the corresponding settlement dates, generally following a T+1 settlement cycle. Special final settlement dates are noted for specific contract types.

## Key Points

- Settlement follows a T+1 cycle for all regular trade dates in June 2026
- Trade dates span June 1 to June 30, 2026 (excluding weekends and holidays)
- Four distinct final settlement dates apply to different contract types
- June 26-27 are excluded, indicating non-trading days
- Issued by Anil Suvarna, Associate Vice President, NSE Clearing Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule circular (Circular Ref. No: 20/2026).

## Compliance Requirements

All members of the Currency Derivatives Segment must adhere to the published settlement schedule for Mark to Market obligations. Members should note the special final settlement dates for specific contract types to ensure timely settlement.
