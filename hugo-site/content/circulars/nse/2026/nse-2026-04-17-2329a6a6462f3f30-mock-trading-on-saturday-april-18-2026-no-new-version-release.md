## Summary

NSE is conducting a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, April 18, 2026. The session covers two trading sessions — one from the Primary Site and one from the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site. This mock is linked to the earlier circular on New Encryption Mechanism of Interactive Messages & Immediate Order Acknowledgement (NSE/CMTR/72769 dated February 12, 2026). No new software version is being released for this mock.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: Saturday, April 18, 2026
- Two trading sessions: Session-1 from Primary Site, Session-2 from DR Site
- NEAT+ version applicable: **7.8.7** (download path: `/common/NEATPlus787`)
- No new version release for this mock
- All outstanding orders will be purged before each session
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- NEAT Adapter reference circular: NSE/MSD/73674 dated April 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock trading exercise tied to the rollout of the New Encryption Mechanism for Interactive Messages & Immediate Order Acknowledgement.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Capital Market segment members must take note of session timings and software version requirements
- Members must use NEAT+ version 7.8.7 for this mock
- Members using NNF software must manually purge outstanding orders before each session
- Members should refer to Annexures 1, 2, and 3 for detailed instructions on mock participation and DR site switchover guidelines
- Connectivity details available in circular NSE/MSD/67674 dated April 24, 2025
- Installation guide available on NSE Extranet at `/common/InstallationProcedure`

## Important Dates

- **April 18, 2026**: Mock trading session date
- **April 09, 2026**: NEAT Adapter reference circular (NSE/MSD/73674)
- **February 12, 2026**: Original encryption mechanism circular (NSE/CMTR/72769)

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant for all NSE Capital Market segment members. Participation in the mock session allows members to validate connectivity, software compatibility (NEAT+ 7.8.7), and DR site switchover procedures. No financial or regulatory impact; no new software version is being released. Members not participating may face operational risks during live market sessions if system readiness is not verified.
