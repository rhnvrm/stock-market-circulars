## Summary

NSE has admitted 10,662,786 equity shares of EFC (I) Limited (Symbol: EFCIL) to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from May 27, 2026. These shares were allotted under a Rights Issue (Final) at an issue price of Rs. 150 per share, with a face value of Rs. 2 each.

## Key Points

- Symbol: EFCIL, Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE886D01026
- Issue Price: Rs. 150 per share; Face Value: Rs. 2 per share
- 10,662,786 new equity shares admitted to trading
- Distinctive number range: 137283377 to 147946162
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size: 1 share
- No lock-in applicable
- Shares temporarily credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*) per SEBI circular guidelines pending ISIN activation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, governing admission of further securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the designated security code EFCIL for trading purposes. Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1. The temporary ISIN (IN8*) will be activated in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 25, 2026
- Effective date for trading: May 27, 2026
- Circular date: May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event following completion of a rights issue by EFC (I) Limited. The addition of approximately 10.66 million shares to the tradeable float may have a modest dilutive effect on existing shareholders. No broad market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
