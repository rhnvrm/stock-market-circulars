## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified all exchange members that Bartronics India Limited, trading under the symbol **ASMS**, will officially change its name to **Avio Smart Market Stack Limited** effective June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company trading under symbol **ASMS** is undergoing a name change
- Existing name: **Bartronics India Limited**
- New name: **Avio Smart Market Stack Limited**
- The trading symbol **ASMS** remains unchanged
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department (Ref No: NSE/CML/74628, Circular Ref. No: 0923/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

The name change is a corporate administrative action. NSE has formally notified all exchange members in accordance with listing obligations. No changes to the trading symbol (ASMS) have been indicated.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should update their records to reflect the new company name: **Avio Smart Market Stack Limited**
- No specific compliance action is required from members beyond updating internal records

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative name change with minimal market impact. The trading symbol (ASMS) remains the same, so trading operations will not be disrupted. Investors and members should update company references from Bartronics India Limited to Avio Smart Market Stack Limited from June 15, 2026 onwards.
