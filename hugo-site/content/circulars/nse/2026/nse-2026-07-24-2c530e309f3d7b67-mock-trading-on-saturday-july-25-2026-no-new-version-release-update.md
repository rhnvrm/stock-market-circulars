## Summary

NSE issued a revised circular (Circular Ref. No. 22/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/SLBS/75393) announcing updated timings for a mock trading session in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Market (SLBM) segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026. This circular supersedes the earlier mock circular NSE/SLBS/75374 dated July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 25, 2026 in the SLBM segment.
- Trading Session-1 from Primary Site: Normal Market open 13:30 hrs, close 15:15 hrs.
- Trading Session-2 from DR (Disaster Recovery) Site: Normal Market open 16:15 hrs, close 17:00 hrs.
- Live Re-login window: start 19:00 hrs, close 19:30 hrs.
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session; NNF software users must manually clear orders.
- Trades in mock sessions carry no financial obligation (no pay-in/pay-out).
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN records uploaded and approved before cutoff will be permitted; UCC validation is skipped during contingency time.
- NOTIS application will be accessible during mock session-1.
- Members are advised to participate using all trading software and re-login to the live environment to ensure smooth connectivity for Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update revising mock session timings previously circulated under NSE/SLBS/75374 dated July 23, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members enabled for SLBM trading in the live market are automatically eligible for mock participation using existing User IDs, IPs, and Box ID mappings.
- Members must verify Telnet connectivity to the Exchange host and confirm Box ID setup with appropriate segment messages.
- NNF (Non-NEAT Frontend) users must ensure the User ID is dealer type, converted for NNF, and mapped with the IP.
- Members should set appropriate branch/user limits ahead of the session.
- Members may use either Mock Trading or a Simulated Environment to meet SEBI regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Session: Saturday, July 25, 2026 (Session-1: 13:30–15:15 hrs; Session-2 from DR Site: 16:15–17:00 hrs).
- Live Re-login window: 19:00–19:30 hrs on July 25, 2026.
- Live market resumption to be confirmed via re-login ahead of Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/technical circular limited to NSE trading members participating in the SLBM segment. It has no direct impact on listed securities, investors, or broader market operations, and serves primarily to ensure system readiness and disaster recovery preparedness ahead of the next live trading session.
