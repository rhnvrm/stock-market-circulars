## Summary

NSE has informed members that Shriram Mutual Fund has revised the face value of the Shriram Overnight Fund from Rs. 10 per unit to Rs. 1,000 per unit on the NSE MF Invest platform, effective May 30, 2026. The scheme underwent a temporary closure period for two working days prior to the change.

## Key Points

- Face value of Shriram Overnight Fund revised from Rs. 10/- per unit to Rs. 1,000/- per unit
- Change effective from May 30, 2026 on the NSE MF Invest platform
- Scheme unavailable for transactions during the closure period: May 27–29, 2026
- Normal transactions resume on NSE MF Invest platform from June 01, 2026
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/74456, Circular Ref. No. 1401/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The face value per unit of the Shriram Overnight Fund has been revised upward by 100x, from Rs. 10 to Rs. 1,000 per unit, as communicated by Shriram Mutual Fund to NSE. This is an AMC-initiated change applicable specifically on the NSE MF Invest platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are informed to take note of the revised face value for the Shriram Overnight Fund
- Members should ensure that transaction systems and data reflect the updated face value of Rs. 1,000/- per unit from May 30, 2026
- No transactions should be processed for this scheme on the NSE MF Invest platform during the closure period (May 27–29, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **May 27–29, 2026**: Closure period — scheme not available for transactions (two working days)
- **May 30, 2026**: Revised face value of Rs. 1,000/- per unit takes effect
- **June 01, 2026**: Scheme resumes full transaction availability on NSE MF Invest platform

## Impact Assessment

This change has limited market impact as it is a purely administrative revision to the face value of a single overnight (liquid) fund scheme. Investors in the Shriram Overnight Fund will see the per-unit face value increase 100-fold, which may affect the unit count for existing holdings, but NAV-based returns are unaffected. The brief closure window (May 27–29) restricts new subscriptions and redemptions temporarily. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
