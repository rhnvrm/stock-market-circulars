## Summary

NSE has notified, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, the admission of various privately placed commercial paper (CP) issuances to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange with effect from July 23, 2026. The circular lists the security codes (ISINs) and issue details for each fresh commercial paper issue.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref. No. 1205/2026, Download Ref No. NSE/CML/75356, dated July 23, 2026, issued by the Listing Department.
- Securities admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective July 23, 2026.
- Fresh commercial paper issues listed include:
  - HDFC Securities Limited – HSL CP 15/10/2026 (ISIN INE700G14VA3), 15,000 units, face value ₹5,00,000, issue price ₹4,91,473.5, allotted 22-Jul-2026, redemption 15-Oct-2026
  - HDFC Securities Limited – HSL CP 26/10/2026 (ISIN INE700G14UW9), 2,000 units, issue price ₹4,90,454.5, redemption 26-Oct-2026
  - Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited – MMFSL CP 25/01/27 Sr 17 (ISIN INE774D14TV5), 4,500 units, issue price ₹4,81,736.5, redemption 25-Jan-2027
  - CEAT Limited – CL CP 12/10/26 (ISIN INE482A14GW6), 1,000 units, issue price ₹4,92,237, redemption 12-Oct-2026
  - Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited – TCHFL CP 21/10/26 (ISIN INE033L14PD5), 8,000 units, issue price ₹4,91,484, redemption 21-Oct-2026
  - Godrej Properties Limited – GPL CP 21/08/26 Sr 60 (ISIN INE484J14F06), 2,500 units, issue price ₹4,97,221, redemption 21-Aug-2026
  - Angel One Limited – AOL CP 14/06/27 Sr 15 (ISIN INE732I14DO1), 500 units, issue price ₹4,64,309.5, redemption 14-Jun-2027
  - Angel One Limited – AOL CP 22/07/27 Sr 16 (ISIN INE732I14DP8), 500 units, issue price ₹4,60,066, redemption 22-Jul-2027
  - Varroc Engineering Limited – VEL CP 21/08/26 Sr 1 (ISIN INE665L14459), 1,000 units, issue price ₹4,97,058.5, redemption 21-Aug-2026
  - Kotak Securities Limited – KSL CP 30/10/26 Sr 128 (ISIN INE028E14XK9), 4,000 units, issue price ₹4,90,227, allotted 23-Jul-2026, redemption 30-Oct-2026
- All issues have a face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit, consistent with standard commercial paper denomination norms.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations governing admission of privately placed debt securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from members or investors beyond awareness of the new security codes for trading purposes; the issuing companies and their registrars/RTAs are responsible for ensuring accurate ISIN allotment and redemption records.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 23, 2026
- Date of allotment (most issues): July 22, 2026 (Kotak Securities: July 23, 2026)
- Redemption dates range from August 21, 2026 to July 22, 2027 depending on the specific CP series

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to the administrative listing of privately placed commercial papers on NSE's Debt Market Segment. It enables secondary market trading eligibility for these instruments among institutional and qualified investors but does not affect equity markets, retail investors, or the issuing companies' listed equity shares.
