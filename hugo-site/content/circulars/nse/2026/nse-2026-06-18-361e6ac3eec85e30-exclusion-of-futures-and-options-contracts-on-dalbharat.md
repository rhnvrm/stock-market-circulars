## Summary

NSE has announced the exclusion of Futures and Options (F&O) contracts on DALBHARAT (Dalmia Bharat Limited) from the derivatives segment. In accordance with SEBI's eligibility criteria circular dated August 30, 2024, no new expiry month contracts will be introduced for DALBHARAT once existing contract months expire. All F&O trading in the security will effectively cease from August 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- DALBHARAT (Dalmia Bharat Limited) is being excluded from the NSE Futures & Options segment.
- No new expiry month contracts will be issued after the existing contract months expire.
- Existing contracts for June 2026, July 2026, and August 2026 expiries will continue to be available for trading until their respective expiry dates.
- New strikes will also be introduced in the existing unexpired contract months as applicable.
- This action is pursuant to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2024/116 dated August 30, 2024 on stock eligibility criteria.

## Regulatory Changes

This exclusion is driven by SEBI's eligibility criteria for stocks in the derivatives segment (Circular Ref. No: SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2024/116, dated August 30, 2024). DALBHARAT no longer meets the criteria required to maintain its presence in the F&O segment, resulting in its phased withdrawal.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note that no fresh contracts for new expiry months will be issued for DALBHARAT.
- Members may continue to trade existing unexpired contracts (June 2026, July 2026, August 2026) until their respective expiry.
- Members should inform their clients holding positions in DALBHARAT F&O contracts of the exclusion and the final trading deadline.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Existing contracts available for trading:** June 2026, July 2026, August 2026 expiries
- **Last date for F&O trading in DALBHARAT:** August 26, 2026 (expiry of the August 2026 contract)
- **Effective cessation of all F&O contracts:** August 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The exclusion of DALBHARAT from the F&O segment will have a significant impact on traders and investors who use derivatives for hedging or speculative positions in Dalmia Bharat Limited. Open interest holders in the June, July, and August 2026 contracts must manage or close their positions before each respective expiry. After August 26, 2026, no derivatives-based exposure will be possible through NSE for this security. Market participants should reassess hedging strategies for DALBHARAT exposure and transition to alternative instruments if required.
