## Summary

NSE has admitted 80,344,000 units of Anzen India Energy Yield Plus Trust (Series IV, ISIN: INE0MIZ23019) to trading on the Capital Market segment effective May 22, 2026. The units were allotted on a preferential basis at an issue price of ₹125 per unit on May 19, 2026, and carry staggered lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Symbol: ANZEN; Series: IV; ISIN: INE0MIZ23019
- 80,344,000 units allotted via preferential issue at ₹125 per unit on May 19, 2026
- Distinctive number range: 255718901 to 336062900
- Units rank pari passu with existing units
- Three tranches of units have different lock-in expiry dates
- Trading effective from May 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is made in pursuance of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1 (admission of securities) and 2.5.5 (lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (ANZEN) and applicable lot size for trading
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the respective unit tranches until their specified expiry dates
- ISIN activation is subject to SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 for dematerialised allotments

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 19, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry — Tranche 1:** May 21, 2027 (43,454,855 units; range 255718901–299173755)
- **Lock-in Expiry — Tranche 2:** November 22, 2027 (16,803,145 units; range 299173756–315976900)
- **Lock-in Expiry — Tranche 3:** May 21, 2029 (20,086,000 units; range 315976901–336062900)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 80.34 million units to the tradeable float of ANZEN on the NSE Capital Market segment, though the full quantum is subject to staggered lock-in restrictions with ~54% locked until at least May 2027 and ~25% locked until May 2029. Market impact is expected to be limited and localised to existing and prospective ANZEN unit holders. No systemic or broad market implications.
