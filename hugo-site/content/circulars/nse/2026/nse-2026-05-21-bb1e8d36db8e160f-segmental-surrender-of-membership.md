## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental membership surrender request of Gogia Capital Growth Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000202733). The member has surrendered their membership in the Currency Derivatives & Interest Rate Futures (IRF) segment, with the surrender approved on May 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Gogia Capital Growth Limited (SEBI Reg: INZ000202733) has surrendered membership in the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment
- Surrender approval date: May 15, 2026
- NSE Circular Ref. No: 49/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/COMP/74303
- All NSE members are advised to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary segment surrender approved by the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of Gogia Capital Growth Limited's exit from the Currency Derivatives & IRF segment. Counterparties and clearing members dealing with this entity should update their records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date:** May 15, 2026
- **Circular Date:** May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine membership surrender affecting only the Currency Derivatives and IRF segments for a single trading member. Other market participants should note that Gogia Capital Growth Limited is no longer an active member in these segments and update their counterparty records as needed.
