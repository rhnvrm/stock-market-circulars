## Summary

Axis Mutual Fund is launching Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 130 (92 days) as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO window is open from March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM), with allotment scheduled for March 12, 2026. Four variants of the scheme are available across Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Four scheme variants available: Direct IDCW Payout, Direct Growth, Regular Growth, and Regular IDCW Payout
- Allotment date for all variants: March 12, 2026
- Clear funds must reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by March 12, 2026 at 12:30 PM
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/73187, Circular Ref. No: 1253/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO availability notification for NSE members.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before March 12, 2026 by 12:30 PM.
- Members should facilitate client transactions within the specified NFO window.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Open Date:** March 09, 2026
- **NFO Close Date:** March 11, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Fund Transfer Deadline:** March 12, 2026 by 12:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is specific to NSE members participating on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The four scheme variants (ISINs: INF846KA1507, INF846KA1481, INF846KA1473, INF846KA1499) are short-duration debt instruments with a 92-day maturity, primarily relevant to investors seeking fixed-income products over a short horizon. No broader equity market or systemic impact is anticipated.
