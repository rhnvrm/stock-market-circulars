## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, notifying the suspension of trading in two privately placed non-convertible securities. The securities issued by Can Fin Homes Limited and NTPC Limited will be suspended from trading effective May 11, 2026, in anticipation of their redemption.

## Key Points

- Two non-convertible securities are being suspended from trading on NSE's debt market segment
- Suspension is effective from May 11, 2026
- Reason for suspension is redemption of both securities
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Download Ref No: NSE/CML/74123 | Circular Ref No: 0750/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is executed in accordance with the existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which provides the framework for suspending securities ahead of scheduled redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified and must cease trading in the listed ISINs from the suspension date
- Members holding or facilitating trades in INE477A07373 (Can Fin Homes) and INE733E07KC2 (NTPC) must ensure no transactions are executed on or after May 11, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** May 08, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 11, 2026 (for both securities)

| Sr. No. | Company | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---------|---------|------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | Can Fin Homes Limited | INE477A07373 | 11-May-2026 | Redemption |
| 2 | NTPC Limited | INE733E07KC2 | 11-May-2026 | Redemption |

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and traders holding these specific privately placed non-convertible securities. This is a routine administrative action preceding scheduled redemptions, ensuring an orderly wind-down of these debt instruments. Holders of INE477A07373 and INE733E07KC2 should prepare for redemption proceeds rather than secondary market exit. No broader market or equity segment impact is anticipated for Can Fin Homes Limited or NTPC Limited.
