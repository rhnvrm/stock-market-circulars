## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for three companies on the SME EMERGE segment of the Capital Market, effective May 25, 2026. The companies are Kody Technolab Limited (KODYTECH), Osel Devices Limited (OSELDEVICE), and Vinsys IT Services India Limited (VINSYS). The shares have been issued through preferential allotment, warrant conversion, and ESOP, respectively, with applicable lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Three SME EMERGE companies are admitting further issued securities to dealings on NSE from May 25, 2026.
- Kody Technolab Limited issued 1,388,000 equity shares at Rs. 1,400 per share via preferential allotment; allotted February 21, 2026.
- Osel Devices Limited issued 100,000 equity shares at Rs. 340 per share via conversion of warrants on preferential basis; allotted April 23, 2026.
- Vinsys IT Services India Limited issued 71,930 equity shares under ESOP; allotted April 28, 2026.
- All securities have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10 each and are pari passu with existing shares.
- Temporary ISINs (IN8*) are in use per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 pending ISIN activation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes (KODYTECH, OSELDEVICE, VINSYS) for all trades in these securities.
- Trades must be executed in the specified market lot sizes: KODYTECH — 100 shares, OSELDEVICE — 200 shares, VINSYS — 250 shares.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as detailed in the annexure for Kody Technolab (two tranches with different lock-in expiry dates) and Osel Devices and Vinsys IT Services.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading in the further issued shares.
- **Kody Technolab Lock-in**: 1,088,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2027; 300,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2026.
- **Osel Devices Lock-in**: 100,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2026.
- **Vinsys IT Services Lock-in**: 71,930 shares locked in until April 27, 2027.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to existing and prospective investors in the three SME EMERGE counters. The listing increases the float marginally for each company, but significant portions are under lock-in. Kody Technolab's large preferential allotment at Rs. 1,400 may be notable given the high issue price relative to SME norms. Osel Devices' warrant conversion and Vinsys' ESOP issuance are routine corporate actions. No broad market or systemic impact is anticipated.
