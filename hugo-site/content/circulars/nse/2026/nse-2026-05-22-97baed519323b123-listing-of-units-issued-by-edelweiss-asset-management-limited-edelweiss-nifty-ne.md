## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,029,421 units of the Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF, managed by Edelweiss Asset Management Limited, on the Capital Market segment. The units will be available for trading effective May 25, 2026, under the symbol ENEXT50 with an issue price of Rs. 69.36 per unit.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** ENEXT50
- **ISIN:** INF754K01XF3
- **Number of Units Listed:** 1,029,421
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Paid-Up Value:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 69.36 per unit
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Series:** EQ
- **Date of Allotment:** May 20, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 25, 2026
- Six market makers designated, including Choice Equity Broking Private Limited, East India Securities Limited, Parwati Capital Market Private Limited, Irage Broking Services LLP, Neo Wealth Management Private Limited, and Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code ENEXT50 for all transactions.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers are obligated to provide liquidity as per their respective TM Codes (13773, 08134, 11890, 90068, 90296, 90246).

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 20, 2026
- **Circular Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF unit listing with limited market impact. The Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF tracks the Nifty Next 50 Index and the listing provides investors with exchange-traded access to this index. With six designated market makers, adequate liquidity support is in place. The low issue price of Rs. 69.36 and market lot of 1 unit makes it accessible to retail investors. No disruption to existing trading or settlement operations is expected.
