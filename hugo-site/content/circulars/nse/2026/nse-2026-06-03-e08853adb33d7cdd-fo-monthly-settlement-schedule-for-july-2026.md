## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (Circular Ref. No: 68/2026) has published the Futures & Options (F&O) segment settlement schedule for July 2026. The schedule maps each trade date in July to its corresponding settlement date, with the final trade date (31-Jul-2026) settling on 03-Aug-2026.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers all F&O trade dates from 01-Jul-2026 through 31-Jul-2026.
- Settlement occurs on T+1 for all listed trade dates.
- The last trade date of the month (31-Jul-2026) settles on 03-Aug-2026 due to weekends/holidays.
- Issued by Anil Suvarna, Associate Vice President, NSE Clearing Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule notice.

## Compliance Requirements

All F&O segment members must adhere to the published settlement dates when planning fund and securities deliveries for July 2026 trades.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally important for all F&O segment members as it defines settlement obligations for the entire month of July 2026. The T+1 settlement cycle applies throughout, with the month-end trade date rolling into August due to the weekend. No market-wide disruption is expected; members should update their internal settlement systems accordingly.
