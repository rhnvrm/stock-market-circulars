## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has notified the settlement calendar for August 2026 for the Capital Market Segment, covering both T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles. The circular, issued under Regulation 6.2 of the NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997), provides the detailed settlement schedules across five settlement types via separate annexures.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule for Normal Rolling (T+1) settlement type M is detailed in Annexure 'A'
- Settlement schedule for Normal Auction settlement type A is detailed in Annexure 'B'
- Settlement schedule for Trade for Trade (T+1) settlement type Z is detailed in Annexure 'C'
- Settlement schedule for ITP for SME (T+1) settlement type B is detailed in Annexure 'D'
- Settlement schedule for Normal Rolling (T+0) settlement type 5 is detailed in Annexure 'E'
- Circular applies to all Members, Custodians, and Professional Clearing Members (PCMs)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic notification of the settlement calendar under existing NSE Clearing Limited Capital Market Regulations, 1997.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, Custodians, and PCMs must align their settlement operations, custodial confirmations, and pay-in/pay-out processes with the trade dates, custodial confirmation dates, and settlement dates specified in the annexures for August 2026.

## Important Dates

The settlement calendar covers the entire month of August 2026, with settlement numbers 2026144 through 2026164 for Normal Rolling (T+1) settlement type M, beginning with trade date 03-Aug-26 (settlement date 04-Aug-26) and concluding with trade date 31-Aug-26 (settlement date 01-Sep-26). Corresponding auction settlement dates begin from 04-Aug-26.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with low market impact, primarily relevant to clearing members, custodians, and back-office operations for pay-in/pay-out scheduling. It does not affect any specific listed company or trading strategy but is important for ensuring smooth settlement operations and avoiding custodial confirmation delays during August 2026.
