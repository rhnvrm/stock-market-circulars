## Summary

NSE has informed members that the ISIN for Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF (symbol PSUBANK) will change effective July 10, 2026, due to a sub-division of face value from Rs. 10 per unit to Re. 1 per unit. The new ISIN is INF174KA1A86.

## Key Points

- Symbol: PSUBANK
- Company/Scheme: Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF
- New ISIN: INF174KA1A86
- Reason: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- Effective date (ex-date): July 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN code representing the units of Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF is being changed as a consequence of the face value sub-division. This is a standard identifier update mandated when a fund's unit face value is altered.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members must update their trading, clearing, and settlement systems to reflect the new ISIN (INF174KA1A86) for PSUBANK from the effective date. All trades executed on or after the ex-date must reference the new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Ex-date / Effective date for new ISIN: July 10, 2026
- Circular date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to operational and system-level changes for exchange members dealing in this ETF. Investors' holdings are unaffected in value, but back-office and depository records must reflect the new ISIN post sub-division. No trading disruption is expected if systems are updated in time for the effective date.
