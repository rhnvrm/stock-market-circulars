## Summary

NSE has notified that Gujarat Energy Limited (GUJENERGY), currently trading in the trade for trade segment under series BE, will be shifted to the rolling segment under series EQ with effect from July 16, 2026. This follows an earlier circular (NSE/CML/74908 dated June 25, 2026) and is issued pursuant to SEBI circular reference CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Gujarat Energy Limited (GUJENERGY) will transition out of the trade for trade segment.
- The security moves from series BE to series EQ.
- Change takes effect from July 16, 2026 (Thursday).
- Action follows up on an earlier NSE circular dated June 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The transfer is carried out under SEBI's framework for trade for trade segment classification as per circular reference CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, which governs periodic review and movement of securities between trade-for-trade and normal rolling settlement segments.

## Compliance Requirements

Members/trading participants must update their systems to reflect GUJENERGY's series change from BE to EQ and ensure trades executed on or after the effective date are processed under normal rolling settlement rather than trade-for-trade settlement.

## Important Dates

- June 25, 2026: Prior circular reference (NSE/CML/74908).
- July 6, 2026: Circular issue date.
- July 16, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of transfer to rolling segment (series EQ).

## Impact Assessment

The move to the rolling segment typically indicates reduced surveillance concern and restores normal settlement (T+1 rolling) for the stock, which may improve liquidity and ease of trading for GUJENERGY compared to the trade-for-trade regime, which required delivery-based settlement to curb speculation.
