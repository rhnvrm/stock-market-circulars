## Summary

NSE has notified all exchange members that the ISIN for equity shares of Madhya Bharat Agro Products Limited (Symbol: MBAPL) will change to INE900L01028 with effect from July 03, 2026. The change is consequent to a sub-division of the face value of equity shares from Rs. 10/- per share to Rs. 2/- per share (a 5:1 split).

## Key Points

- New ISIN assigned to MBAPL: **INE900L01028**
- Reason: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- per share
- The new ISIN is applicable to all trades executed on and from the ex-date
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department; Ref No: NSE/CML/74938, Circular Ref. No: 1047/2026

## Regulatory Changes

The existing ISIN for MBAPL equity shares is being replaced with INE900L01028 to reflect the post-split security identity. The sub-division reduces the face value from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/-, implying shareholders will receive 5 shares for every 1 share held as of the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- All exchange members must update their back-office and trading systems to use the new ISIN (INE900L01028) for MBAPL from July 03, 2026.
- Members should ensure correct ISIN mapping for settlement, margin, and collateral purposes from the ex-date onwards.
- Any positions, orders, or references using the old ISIN must be migrated to the new ISIN prior to the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective / Ex-date: July 03, 2026** — New ISIN applicable for all trades from this date.

## Impact Assessment

This change has a medium operational impact on members and depository participants. Systems referencing the old ISIN for MBAPL will need to be updated before market open on July 03, 2026. Retail and institutional investors holding MBAPL shares will see their depository holdings reflected under the new ISIN post-split. Trading activity in MBAPL may see elevated volumes around the ex-date as is typical with stock splits, given the lower per-share price post-division.
