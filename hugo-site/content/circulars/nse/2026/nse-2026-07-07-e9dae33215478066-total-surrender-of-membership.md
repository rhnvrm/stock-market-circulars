## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of trading membership by Money Krishna Financial Services (SEBI registration number INZ000294819). The member's last trade date was March 27, 2025, and the surrender was approved by the Exchange on July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Member: Money Krishna Financial Services
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000294819
- Last Trade Date: March 27, 2025
- Surrender Approval Date: July 2, 2026
- All members are requested to take note of the surrender

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification of membership surrender approval under NSE's membership administration process.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members are requested to take note of the surrender for record-keeping and counterparty verification purposes. No action is required from other members beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- Last Trade Date: March 27, 2025
- Surrender Approval Date: July 2, 2026
- Circular Issued: July 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the administrative exit of a single trading member from the Exchange. It does not affect listed securities, trading operations, or investor holdings. Other members should note the surrender for counterparty due diligence purposes.
