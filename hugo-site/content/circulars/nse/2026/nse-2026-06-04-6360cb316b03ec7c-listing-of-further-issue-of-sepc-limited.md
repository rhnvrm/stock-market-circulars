## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and admission to dealings of 7,00,000 additional equity shares of SEPC Limited (Symbol: SEPC, Series: EQ) on the Capital Market segment, effective June 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- 7,00,000 equity shares of SEPC Limited admitted to trading on NSE Capital Market segment from June 05, 2026
- Security description: Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- ISIN: INE964H01014; Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Market lot size: 1; Pari Passu with existing equity shares: Yes
- Distinctive number range: 1707680718 to 1708380717
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Admission is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 (securities admitted to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot sizes) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol (SEPC) and series (EQ) codes for all trades in these securities
- Trades must be executed in the specified lot size of 1
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8964H01013 in demat mode; final ISIN activation is INE964H01014, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 04, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of shares converted from partly paid to fully paid status. The addition of 7 lakh shares increases the tradeable float of SEPC modestly. Existing partly paid shareholders will have their holdings activated under the permanent ISIN (INE964H01014) once the temporary ISIN (IN8964H01013) is deactivated per SEBI guidelines.
