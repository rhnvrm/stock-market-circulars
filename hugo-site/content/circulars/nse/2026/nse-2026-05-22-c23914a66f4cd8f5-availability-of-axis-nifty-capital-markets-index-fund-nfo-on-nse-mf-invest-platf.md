## Summary

Axis Mutual Fund's Axis Nifty Capital Markets Index Fund NFO will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective May 25, 2026. Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plan variants will be available, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Axis Nifty Capital Markets Index Fund NFO reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions from May 25, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Two scheme variants available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1549) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1531)
- Both schemes are categorised as Equity funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification informing members of platform availability for a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This circular is informational, notifying NSE members of the availability of the fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine notification about a mutual fund scheme becoming available on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in Nifty Capital Markets Index exposure through Axis Mutual Fund can subscribe via the platform starting May 25, 2026. No broader market or trading implications.
