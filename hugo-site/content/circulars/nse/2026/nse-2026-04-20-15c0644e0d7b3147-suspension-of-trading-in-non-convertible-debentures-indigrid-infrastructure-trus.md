## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, notifying the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures issued by IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust, effective April 21, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of both instruments.

## Key Points

- Two NCD instruments of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust are being suspended from trading on NSE.
- Symbol **76IGT26** (ISIN: INE219X07207) suspended due to maturity.
- Symbol **745IGT26** (ISIN: INE219X07199) suspended due to maturity.
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/73803 | Circular Ref. No: 0647/2026.
- Issued by Ruchi Jaiswal, Manager, NSE Listing Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Action taken under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities from trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified to cease trading in the two listed NCD symbols (76IGT26 and 745IGT26) from April 21, 2026 onwards.
- No further action required from members beyond acknowledging the suspension.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 20, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** April 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of the two specific IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust NCD instruments. As the reason is maturity, investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. This is a routine administrative action following the lifecycle completion of the debt securities.
