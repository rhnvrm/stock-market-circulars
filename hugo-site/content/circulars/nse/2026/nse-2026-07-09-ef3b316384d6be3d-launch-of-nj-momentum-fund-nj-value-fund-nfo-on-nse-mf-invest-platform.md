## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the NJ Momentum Fund and NJ Value Fund, new fund offers (NFOs) under NJ Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 10, 2026 to July 24, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). Both are equity category schemes offered in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- Two new equity schemes launched: NJ Momentum Fund and NJ Value Fund, under NJ Mutual Fund.
- Each scheme offers 4 plan/option combinations: Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Payout — totaling 8 ISINs.
- NFO subscription window: July 10, 2026 to July 24, 2026, till 11:59 pm.
- Allotment date for all 8 schemes/options: July 30, 2026.
- Clear funds must reach the designated Clearing Corporation account by July 29, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification enabling a new product launch on the NSE MF Invest Platform, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members transacting on behalf of investors must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 29, 2026, till 11:59 pm, to ensure successful allotment.

## Important Dates

- NFO Subscription Opens: July 10, 2026
- NFO Subscription Closes: July 24, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: July 29, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment Date: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact, being a routine notification of new mutual fund scheme availability on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It is operationally relevant for NSE members, distributors, and RTAs handling mutual fund transactions, requiring them to update systems and ensure timely fund settlement for investor subscriptions to the NJ Momentum Fund and NJ Value Fund NFOs. No impact on listed equities or broader trading activity is expected.
