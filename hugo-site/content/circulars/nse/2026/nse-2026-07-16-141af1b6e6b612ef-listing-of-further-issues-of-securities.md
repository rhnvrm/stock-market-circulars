## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares of eight companies — Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited, Axis Bank Limited, Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited, Coromandel International Limited, ICICI Bank Limited, Manorama Industries Limited, Northern Arc Capital Limited and Zodiac Energy Limited — under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The new shares, largely allotted under ESOP schemes, will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 17, 2026, along with their designated lot sizes as per Regulation 2.5.5.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited (ABSLAMC): 60,252 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each allotted under ESOP on 14-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 289203495–289263746; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Axis Bank Limited (AXISBANK): 50,993 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted under ESOP on 14-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 3110915679–3110966671; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited (CLEANMAX): 8,740 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted under ESOP on 13-Jul-2026; distinctive number range 117217411–117226150; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Coromandel International Limited (COROMANDEL): 1,800 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted under ESOP on 23-Jun-2026; market lot 1; pari passu; no lock-in.
- Further details for ICICI Bank Limited, Manorama Industries Limited, Northern Arc Capital Limited and Zodiac Energy Limited are listed in the circular's Annexure (not fully captured in the extracted content) but are covered by the same listing notification.
- All securities will be identified on the trading system by their designated codes, and trading will occur in the specified lot sizes.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notification under existing NSE Trading Regulations governing admission of further-issued securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional shares as distinct allotments identified by the stated distinctive number ranges and ISINs, and trade them per the specified lot size of 1 share from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: 23-Jun-2026 (Coromandel International), 13-Jul-2026 (Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions), 14-Jul-2026 (Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC, Axis Bank)
- Effective date of listing/trading: July 17, 2026
- Circular date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing of ESOP-driven equity share issuances for already-listed, well-established companies. The additional share counts are marginal relative to each company's outstanding capital and pari passu with existing shares, so no material price or trading impact is expected.
