## Summary

NSE has admitted further equity shares of eight companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective February 03, 2026. All shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) during January 2026, are pari passu with existing shares, have a market lot of 1, and carry no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Eight companies listing additional equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes
- Effective date: February 03, 2026
- All securities admitted under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1
- Market lot size: 1 share for all securities
- All shares are pari passu (equal in all respects) with existing securities
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified market lot sizes (1 share)
- All regulatory requirements under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine ESOP allotments representing small dilutions to existing share capital. The shares are immediately tradable with no lock-in restrictions.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members will identify securities by designated codes with market lot of 1.

**Investor Impact**: The newly listed shares are pari passu with existing shares, carrying equal rights and privileges. No lock-in restrictions enhance liquidity for ESOP recipients.
