## Summary

NSE has informed members that the Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund NFO by Zerodha Mutual Fund will be open for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from March 23, 2026 to April 6, 2026 (until 11:59 PM). Allotment is scheduled for April 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO window: March 23, 2026 to April 6, 2026 (11:59 PM)
- Scheme: Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund - Direct Growth
- ISIN: INF0R8F01190
- Category: Equity (Index Fund)
- Allotment Date: April 9, 2026
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before April 9, 2026 by 11:59 PM.

## Important Dates

- **March 23, 2026**: NFO opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **April 6, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: NFO closes
- **April 9, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be received in designated Clearing Corporation account
- **April 9, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch announcement with low market impact. It provides NSE members with a new passive equity index fund option tracking the Nifty MidSmallcap400 index with a 50:50 weighting methodology. Members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the subscription and payment deadlines.
