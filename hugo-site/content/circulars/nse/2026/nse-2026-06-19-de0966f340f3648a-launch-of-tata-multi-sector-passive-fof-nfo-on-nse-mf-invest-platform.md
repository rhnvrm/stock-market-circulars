## Summary

NSE has informed members that Tata Multi Sector Passive FOF NFO of TATA Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 22, 2026 to July 06, 2026 (till 11:59 pm). Six scheme variants are being launched across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Reinvestment and Payout) options, all classified under the Equity category with a common allotment date of July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 22, 2026 to July 06, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Fund House: TATA Mutual Fund
- Scheme Type: Multi Sector Passive Fund of Funds (FOF), Equity category
- Six scheme variants launched covering Regular/Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- Allotment date for all variants: July 09, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 09, 2026 till 11:59 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO availability notification for members of the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 09, 2026 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: NFO opens for transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **July 06, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: NFO subscription window closes
- **July 09, 2026 (11:59 pm)**: Deadline for clear funds to be received in Clearing Corporation account
- **July 09, 2026**: Allotment date for all six scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard NFO launch notification with limited broad market impact. The six scheme variants and their ISINs are:

| 2 | Tata Multi Sector Passive FOF Regular IDCW Payout | INF277KA1EU8 |
| 3 | Tata Multi Sector Passive FOF Direct IDCW Reinvestment | INF277KA1EW4 |

Impact is limited to NSE MF platform members and retail/institutional investors seeking exposure to a multi-sector passive fund-of-funds structure. No change to trading rules, margins, or surveillance frameworks.
