## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 198/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/73321) dated March 17, 2026, announcing that two securities — GFSTEELS (Grand Foundry Limited, ISIN: INE534A01028) and UNIVAFOODS (Univa Foods Limited, ISIN: INE275F01019) — will be moved to Stage IV of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective March 18, 2026. Stage IV represents the most stringent level of surveillance under GSM, imposing severe trading restrictions on the affected securities.

## Key Points

- Two securities moving to GSM Stage IV effective March 18, 2026: GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS
- Trading will be on a Trade-for-Trade (T2T) basis — no netting off is permitted
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower as applicable
- Trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or the 1st trading day of the week)
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 100% of trade value must be deposited by buyers
- No upward price movement allowed at Stage IV
- Members advised to take adequate precaution while trading in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

The securities are being elevated to Stage IV under the GSM framework, which is the highest and most restrictive surveillance stage. This is in continuation of earlier circulars establishing the GSM framework (NSE/SURV/34262, NSE/SURV/38389, NSE/SURV/42790, NSE/SURV/59425, NSE/SURV/64066, and NSE/SURV/73167). The GSM framework has four stages with progressively stricter surveillance actions:

- **Stage I:** 100% applicable margin rate, 5% or lower price band
- **Stage II:** Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, 50% ASD by buyers
- **Stage III:** Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, weekly trading (Mondays), 100% ASD by buyers
- **Stage IV:** Trade-for-Trade, 5% price band, weekly trading (Mondays), 100% ASD by buyers, **no upward movement**

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members:** Must note the Stage IV classification for GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS and ensure compliance with all applicable trading restrictions from March 18, 2026
- **Buyers:** Must deposit 100% of trade value as Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) before executing trades
- **Settlement:** Must be done on a trade-to-trade basis; no netting off is allowed
- **Queries:** Members may contact NSE Surveillance at surveillance@nse.co.in for further clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 17, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 18, 2026 (Stage IV restrictions apply from this date)
- **Trading Schedule:** Only on Mondays (or first trading day of each week)

## Impact Assessment

The move to Stage IV GSM has a high impact on investors and traders in GFSTEELS and UNIVAFOODS. Liquidity is severely curtailed as trading is limited to once a week, making it difficult to enter or exit positions quickly. The 100% ASD requirement ties up significant capital for buyers. The prohibition on upward price movement effectively caps any potential gains, signalling that regulators have identified significant concerns with these securities. Investors holding these stocks face reduced exit options and heightened settlement risk. Members and investors should exercise extreme caution when dealing in these securities.
