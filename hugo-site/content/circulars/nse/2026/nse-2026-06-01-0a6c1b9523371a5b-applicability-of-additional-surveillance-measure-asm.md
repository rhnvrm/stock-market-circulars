## Summary

NSE Surveillance department has issued Circular 404/2026 announcing changes to the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LTASM) framework. Four securities are newly included in Stage I and one security (HFCL) is escalated from Stage I to Stage II, all effective June 2, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage I effective June 2, 2026: FCL, GRADIENTE, LEMERITE, and OBSCP
- Three of the four newly added securities (FCL, GRADIENTE, LEMERITE) are moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM)
- HFCL Limited escalated from LTASM Stage I to Stage II effective June 2, 2026
- No securities shortlisted for Stage IV, Stage II to III, or Stage I to IV transitions
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 3, 2026 and new positions from June 4, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

The circular is issued under the ASM framework established by prior circulars NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066 (dated October 27, 2018 through September 20, 2024). Securities qualifying under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would additionally be shifted from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ) to Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE) on a T+3 basis, though no securities qualify for Stage IV in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply 100% margin on all open positions in the listed securities as on June 3, 2026
- 100% margin must be collected on all new positions in these securities created from June 4, 2026 onwards
- Members should note that ASM shortlisting is purely a surveillance action and does not constitute an adverse finding against the companies

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 2, 2026**: Effective date for ASM stage inclusions and changes
- **June 3, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 4, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes effective for open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

**FCL (Fineotex Chemical Limited)**: Upgraded from STASM to LTASM Stage I, signaling sustained surveillance concern. 100% margin increases cost of holding positions.

**GRADIENTE (Gradiente Infotainment Limited)**: Upgraded from STASM to LTASM Stage I. Higher margin burden expected to reduce liquidity.

**LEMERITE (Le Merite Exports Limited)**: Upgraded from STASM to LTASM Stage I. Similar impact as above.

**OBSCP (OBSC Perfection Limited)**: Freshly included in LTASM Stage I. Traders will face 100% margin requirements.

**HFCL (HFCL Limited)**: Escalated from Stage I to Stage II within LTASM, indicating continued surveillance concern with potentially stricter conditions. This is a notable development for a relatively prominent listed company.

Overall, the 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of carrying or initiating positions in all five affected securities, likely resulting in reduced trading volumes and liquidity in these counters.
