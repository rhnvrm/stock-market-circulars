## Summary

NSE has informed its members that DYNA SIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category of 360 Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- DYNA SIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund (360 Mutual Fund) reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest from June 29, 2026.
- Six scheme variants are available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- All standard transaction modes are supported: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category within the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice informing members of platform availability for an existing SIF scheme that is being reopened for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of these schemes from June 29, 2026, and update their systems or client-facing portals accordingly if they offer SIF products.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of DYNA SIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This circular is a routine platform availability update for a specialized long-short equity fund targeting the mid/small-cap segment (ex-Top 100 stocks). It is relevant primarily to distributors and investors who use the NSE MF Invest Platform and are interested in SIF products from 360 Mutual Fund. No systemic or broad market implications.
