## Summary

NSE has issued Circular NSE/SURV/74646 (Ref. 434/2026) placing six securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I effective June 11, 2026. Enhanced margin requirements of 50% (or existing, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) apply to all open and new positions from June 12, 2026. No securities were added to Stage II, and no stage transitions occurred.

## Key Points

- Six securities shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion w.e.f. June 11, 2026
- Applicable margin for Stage I: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max cap: 100%)
- Margin requirement applies on open positions as on June 11, 2026 and new positions from June 12, 2026
- Stage II list is Nil; no Stage I to Stage II or Stage II to Stage I transitions
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is for surveillance purposes only and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

Securities added to ST-ASM Stage I are subject to enhanced margin requirements under the ASM framework previously established via circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 4, 2020), NSE/SURV/52144 (Apr 28, 2022), NSE/SURV/58558 (Sep 25, 2023), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum margin of 50% (or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher) on ST-ASM Stage I securities
- Margin capped at a maximum of 100%
- Applicable to all open positions as of June 11, 2026 and all new positions created from June 12, 2026
- Members should refer to FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/additional-surveillance-measure for further guidance
- Queries may be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 11, 2026**: Securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I; margin applicable on open positions as of this date
- **June 12, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements effective for all open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the six Stage I securities — DHARARAIL (Dhara Rail Projects Limited, INE2HJL01019), GRANDOAK (Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Limited, INE926B01016), GRMOVER (GRM Overseas Limited, INE192H01020), NFPSAMPOOR (NFP Sampoorna Foods Limited, INE0T1501010), NRL (Nupur Recyclers Limited, INE0JM501013), and SUPRIYA (Supriya Lifescience Limited, INE07RO01027) — must ensure sufficient margin from June 12, 2026. The 50% margin requirement may reduce liquidity and increase the cost of carrying positions in these scrips, potentially leading to forced unwinding of leveraged positions.
