## Summary

NSE's Member Service Department has announced a revision in the real-time dissemination frequency for four major Nifty indices. The frequency will be increased from 500 milliseconds (0.5 second) to 250 milliseconds (0.25 second) for Nifty Bank, Nifty Next 50, Nifty Financial Services, and Nifty Midcap Select indices on the trading system in the CM and FO segments. This follows a similar upgrade made to Nifty 50 effective May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Dissemination frequency for four major indices will be doubled from 500ms to 250ms
- Affected indices: Nifty Bank, Nifty Next 50, Nifty Financial Services (Nifty Fin Service), and Nifty Midcap Select
- Change applies to both CM (Capital Market) and FO (Futures & Options) segments
- This is a continuation of the upgrade previously applied to Nifty 50 (effective May 11, 2026)
- Issued by NSE Member Service Department vide Download Ref No: NSE/MSD/74455, Circular Ref. No: 41/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework change is involved. This is a technical enhancement to NSE's trading system infrastructure, increasing the granularity of live index feeds for the listed indices from 0.5-second intervals to 0.25-second intervals.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members consuming real-time index data for Nifty Bank, Nifty Next 50, Nifty Financial Services, and Nifty Midcap Select are required to:
- Take note of the change in dissemination frequency
- Ensure their systems can handle the increased data rate (doubled frequency)
- Participate in the mock session on June 6, 2026 to test readiness before the live rollout

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 6, 2026**: Mock session planned to test the new 250ms dissemination frequency
- **June 8, 2026**: Effective date for the change to 250ms dissemination frequency

## Impact Assessment

This change doubles the rate at which Nifty Bank, Nifty Next 50, Nifty Financial Services, and Nifty Midcap Select index values are broadcast on NSE's trading system. Members and algorithmic traders relying on real-time index feeds for hedging, arbitrage, or index-based strategies will receive more granular data, potentially enabling faster and more accurate responses to market movements. Members must verify their systems can sustain the higher data throughput before June 8, 2026. The mock session on June 6 provides an opportunity to validate readiness. The operational impact is moderate for technology-reliant participants.
