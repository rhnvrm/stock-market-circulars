## Summary

NSE Clearing has released the settlement calendar for CBRICS (Corporate Bond Repository for Institutional and Corporate Securities) for February 2026. The circular provides settlement dates and numbers for corporate bond transactions to be settled through NSDL/CDSL using on-market settlement mode. The calendar includes 19 settlement dates throughout February 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers February 2026 for CBRICS platform
- Market Type: Corporate Bond
- Clearing Member: NSE Clearing Ltd.
- CM-BP ID: IN568177
- Settlement mode to be used: On-market
- Total of 19 settlement dates scheduled for the month
- Applies to both NSDL and CDSL transfers
- Previous DP details (IN001002-10000617) not valid since September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and Custodians must transfer securities for CBRICS settlement based on the provided calendar
- Settlement mode must be set as 'On-market'
- Use the current CM-BP ID IN568177 for NSE Clearing Ltd.
- Do not use the deprecated DP details (IN001002-10000617)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Low - This is a routine monthly calendar that enables participants to plan their corporate bond settlement activities. It provides clarity on settlement dates and technical requirements.

**Market Impact:** Minimal - Standard operational schedule with no changes to existing settlement processes or procedures.

**Affected Parties:** Participants and custodians involved in corporate bond trading on the CBRICS platform, including those using NSDL and CDSL for depository services.
