## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL), under the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS), has revised the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) and the applicable position limits for Participants, Institutional Clients, and Non-Institutional Clients for the stock **BRIGADE**. The revision is effective from **June 18, 2026** and represents an increase of approximately 33% across all limit categories.

## Key Points

- Circular issued by NSE Clearing Limited, Department of Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/74744; Circular Ref No: 060/2026)
- Limits apply to BRIGADE in the SLBS for the month of June 2026
- All four limit categories — Market Wide, Institutional Client, Participant, and Non-Institutional Client — have been revised upward
- This revision partially modifies the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/74480 dated May 29, 2026, which itself was issued in pursuance of NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

**Old Limits for BRIGADE:**

| Limit Type | Shares |
|---|---|
| Market Wide Position Limit | 14,405,068 |
| Institutional Client Limit | 1,440,506 |
| Participant Limit | 1,440,506 |
| Non-Institutional Client Limit | 144,050 |

**New Limits for BRIGADE:**

| Limit Type | Shares |
|---|---|
| Market Wide Position Limit | 19,206,753 |
| Institutional Client Limit | 1,920,675 |
| Participant Limit | 1,920,675 |
| Non-Institutional Client Limit | 192,067 |

The increase across all categories is approximately 33.3%, likely reflecting changes in the underlying MWPL computation based on updated shareholding or free-float data.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians in the SLBS must take note of the revised limits effective June 18, 2026
- Existing positions in BRIGADE under SLBS must be evaluated against the new limits to ensure compliance
- No positions should breach the revised Market Wide Position Limit of 19,206,753 shares

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 18, 2026**: Effective date for revised limits

## Impact Assessment

The upward revision of MWPL and associated limits for BRIGADE allows greater participation in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme for this stock. Institutional and non-institutional clients, as well as participants, will have more headroom to lend or borrow BRIGADE shares. This is a routine administrative update triggered by changes in the stock's MWPL computation and has limited broader market impact. Participants currently near the old limits should ensure their positions are recalibrated against the new thresholds from June 18, 2026.
