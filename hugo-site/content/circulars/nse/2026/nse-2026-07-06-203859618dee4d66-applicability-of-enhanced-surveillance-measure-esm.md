## Summary

NSE has issued a circular under its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, identifying securities that newly qualify for ESM Stage I, securities moving from Stage I to Stage II, and providing a consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework. No securities are being excluded from the framework in this cycle.

## Key Points

- Damodar Industries Limited (DAMODARIND), Lamosaic India Limited (LAMOSAIC), and Vipul Limited (VIPULLTD) have been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I.
- Lokesh Machines Limited (LOKESHMACH) is moving from ESM Stage I to Stage II.
- No securities are moving from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.
- Newly added Stage I securities (DAMODARIND, LAMOSAIC, VIPULLTD) will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST).
- Securities moving to Stage II (LOKESHMACH) will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM reflects market surveillance criteria only and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No change to the ESM policy framework itself; this circular applies the existing ESM criteria (per earlier circulars NSE/SURV/56948, 57609, 63361, 64066, 64400, and 69315) to update the list of securities under Stage I and Stage II surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure a minimum 100% margin is applied on all open positions in the newly added ESM Stage I securities (DAMODARIND, LAMOSAIC, VIPULLTD) as on July 07, 2026, and on new positions created from July 08, 2026. Members must also update trading systems to reflect the shift of these securities to the BE/ST series and apply the 2% price band/Periodic Call Auction mechanism for LOKESHMACH.

## Important Dates

- July 07, 2026: Stage reclassifications take effect (Stage I additions, Stage I to Stage II move); 100% margin applies to open positions as on this date; 2% price band Periodic Call Auction begins for Stage II movers.
- July 08, 2026: Newly added Stage I securities shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST segment; 100% margin applies to new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The shift to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) segment and imposition of 100% margin will significantly reduce speculative trading and intraday liquidity in DAMODARIND, LAMOSAIC, and VIPULLTD, likely increasing price volatility and widening bid-ask spreads. LOKESHMACH's move to Stage II with a tighter 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction further restricts price movement and trading flexibility. These measures increase compliance burden for brokers handling these securities but do not reflect any adverse fundamental news about the companies themselves.
