## Summary

NSE Clearing Ltd. (CM-BP ID: IN568177) has issued the CBRICS settlement calendar for May 2026. Participants and custodians are directed to transfer securities for CBRICS settlement using the 'On-market' settlement mode in the Corporate Bond market segment. The calendar covers 19 settlement dates from May 4 to May 29, 2026, with corresponding settlement numbers for NSDL and CDSL transfers.

## Key Points

- Settlement mode must be set to 'On-market' for all CBRICS transfers
- Market Type: Corporate Bond; Clearing Member: NSE Clearing Ltd.; CM-BP ID: IN568177
- 19 settlement dates scheduled in May 2026, ranging from May 4 to May 29
- Settlement numbers follow the format 260XXXX corresponding to each settlement date
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) are not valid for CBRICS settlement effective September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine periodic calendar issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and custodians must use Settlement mode 'On-market' for all CBRICS security transfers
- The deprecated DP details (IN001002-10000617) must not be used; these have been invalid since September 3, 2019
- Transfers must reference the correct settlement number for each settlement date as listed in the calendar

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact for participants and custodians active in the CBRICS (Corporate Bond Repo in International Clearing System) segment. Back-office and settlement teams must update their systems with the May 2026 schedule to ensure timely and accurate securities transfers. No trading disruption or market-wide impact is expected; non-compliance with settlement dates or use of obsolete DP details could result in settlement failures.
