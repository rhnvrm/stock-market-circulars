## Summary

Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited is being listed on the Capital Market Segment (Main Board) of the National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE) effective 29 June 2026, pursuant to its migration from the SME Platform of BSE. The company's ISIN is INE0I0T01010 and its BSE scrip code is 543597.

## Key Points

- Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited (ISIN: INE0I0T01010, BSE scrip code: 543597) migrates from BSE SME Platform to NSE Main Board.
- The company is classified as an SME entity with equity shares as the class of security.
- The listing/reporting date is 29 June 2026.
- The company has issued warrants (held by promoter/promoter group).
- Certain shares are under lock-in for both promoters and public shareholders.
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, ESOPs, or depository receipts are outstanding.
- No shares held by promoters are encumbered by pledge, NDU, or other means.
- The company does not have equity shares with differential voting rights.
- No significant beneficial owner has been identified.

## Regulatory Changes

The migration from BSE SME Platform to NSE Main Board (Capital Market Segment) brings the company under the full disclosure and compliance framework applicable to main board listed entities on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(b) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.
- The company must comply with all NSE Main Board listing obligations going forward.
- Lock-in restrictions on shares must continue to be observed by applicable shareholders.
- Warrants outstanding must be disclosed and managed per applicable SEBI regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Listing/Report Date:** 29 June 2026
- **Quarter Ended (Shareholding Pattern):** 29 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

The migration to NSE Main Board increases the company's visibility and liquidity by giving it access to a larger pool of investors on NSE. Existing BSE SME shareholders should note the change in trading venue. The presence of warrants and lock-in shares means the fully diluted share count and tradable float will evolve as these restrictions lapse or warrants are exercised. Overall market impact is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited.
