## Summary

NSE's Surveillance department has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Circular Ref. No. 467/2026. Five securities are newly added to ESM Stage I, four securities are escalated from Stage I to Stage II, and no securities are excluded from or moved down within the framework. All changes take effect on June 24–25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted for ESM Stage I effective June 24, 2026: GCHOTELS, SIGNORIA, SONUINFRA, THESL, VELS
- 4 securities escalated from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective June 24, 2026: AKSHOPTFBR, SADHNANIQ, SHYAMTEL, UHTL
- No securities moved from Stage II to Stage I; no securities excluded from the ESM framework
- All ESM-covered securities attract a minimum 100% margin on open positions as of June 24, 2026 and new positions from June 25, 2026
- ESM securities shift from Rolling Settlement (Series: EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE/ST) effective June 25, 2026
- Stage II securities placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction effective June 24, 2026
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and updates the ESM framework originally established under circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315 (dated June 2023 through July 2025). The current update modifies the list of securities under the framework and their respective stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in ESM-listed securities as on June 24, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 25, 2026
- Members must note the shift of EQ/SM series securities to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) effective June 25, 2026
- Stage II securities must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band from June 24, 2026
- Members are advised that ESM classification is solely a market surveillance action and should not be interpreted as adverse action against the listed company

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026** — Circular issued
- **June 24, 2026** — ESM Stage I inclusions and Stage I→II escalations take effect; Stage II Periodic Call Auction with 2% price band commences; 100% margin applies to open positions
- **June 25, 2026** — Shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) for ESM securities; 100% margin applies to new positions

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding positions in these securities, effectively discouraging speculative activity. The move to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and requires full delivery, further reducing liquidity. Stage II securities face the additional constraint of a 2% daily price band under Periodic Call Auction, severely limiting intraday price discovery.
