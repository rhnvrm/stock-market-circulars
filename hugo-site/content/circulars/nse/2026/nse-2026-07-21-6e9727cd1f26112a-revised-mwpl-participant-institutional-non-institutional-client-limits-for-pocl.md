## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a revision to the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) and the applicable Participant, Institutional Client, and Non-Institutional Client limits for the symbol POCL under the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Scheme for July 2026. This follows an earlier circular dated April 30, 2026 (NCL/CMPT/73999) and a partial modification dated June 30, 2026 (NCL/CMPT/74959). The revised limits take effect from July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Circular Ref No: 077/2026; Download Ref No: NCL/CMPT/75326, dated July 21, 2026.
- Applies to all Participants and Custodians in the SLB Scheme.
- Symbol affected: POCL.
- MWPL raised from 1,800,412 shares to 4,501,030 shares.
- Institutional Client Limit raised from 180,041 shares to 450,103 shares.
- Participant Limit raised from 180,041 shares to 450,103 shares.
- Non-Institutional Client Limit raised from 18,004 shares to 45,010 shares.

## Regulatory Changes

The revision substantially increases (by roughly 2.5x) all four limit categories - MWPL, Institutional Client Limit, Participant Limit, and Non-Institutional Client Limit - for POCL within the SLB framework, superseding the limits set in the prior April 30, 2026 and June 30, 2026 circulars.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and Custodians dealing in POCL under the SLB Scheme must ensure their positions conform to the new limits effective July 22, 2026 and update internal risk/position monitoring systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Circular issued: July 21, 2026
- New limits effective: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The higher limits expand the capacity for lending and borrowing activity in POCL shares, potentially increasing liquidity and participation in the stock's SLB segment. The impact is operational and limited to participants and clients active in POCL's securities lending market rather than the broader market.
