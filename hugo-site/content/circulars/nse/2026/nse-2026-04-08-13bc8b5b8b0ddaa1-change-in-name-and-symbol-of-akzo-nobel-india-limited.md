## Summary

NSE has announced that Akzo Nobel India Limited will undergo a name and stock symbol change effective April 15, 2026. The company will be renamed to JSW Dulux Limited, and its trading symbol on NSE will change from AKZOINDIA to JSWDULUX.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol **AKZOINDIA** will be replaced by **JSWDULUX**
- Existing company name **Akzo Nobel India Limited** will change to **JSW Dulux Limited**
- The change reflects a rebranding under the JSW Group
- All members of the Exchange are formally notified via this circular

## Regulatory Changes

The company's listing details on NSE will be updated to reflect the new name and symbol. No change in ISIN or other identifiers was mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems and references to use the new symbol **JSWDULUX** from April 15, 2026
- Traders and brokers should ensure client-facing systems are updated to reflect the new name and symbol before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **April 08, 2026** — Circular issued by NSE
- **April 15, 2026** — Effective date for name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

Existing holders of AKZOINDIA shares will automatically hold JSWDULUX shares post the name change with no action required. Traders using the old symbol for orders or watchlists must update to JSWDULUX. This rebranding signals a strategic shift under JSW Group ownership of the former Akzo Nobel India paint and coatings business.
