## Summary

NSE has admitted further issued securities of four SME EMERGE-listed companies to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective April 10, 2026. All securities were issued via preferential allotment and are assigned designated trading symbols with specified lot sizes and lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Four companies' preferential allotment shares listed on NSE SME EMERGE (Series: SM) effective April 10, 2026
- All securities carry lock-in periods expiring between October 2026 and October 2027
- Trading in all securities is subject to designated lot sizes as specified in the annexure
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in routine compliance with:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot size specification)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use only designated security codes (KODYTECH, SWARAJ, VILINBIO, WOMANCART) for trading in the respective securities
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes for each security
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the respective distinctive number ranges as per the annexures

## Important Dates

- **April 09, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 10, 2026**: Effective date — all four securities admitted to trading on NSE SME EMERGE
- **15-Oct-2026**: Lock-in expiry for KODYTECH and SWARAJ preferential shares
- **20-Oct-2026**: Lock-in expiry for VILINBIO preferential shares
- **31-Oct-2026 / 31-Oct-2027**: Staggered lock-in expiry for WOMANCART tranches (as per Annexure A)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational listing notification with limited broad market impact. The affected securities are all SME EMERGE-listed companies with relatively small float additions. The Swaraj Suiting allotment is the largest by volume (33.71 lakh shares), while Kody Technolab has the highest issue price (Rs. 1,400). Womancart has two tranches with differing issue prices (Rs. 128 and Rs. 326) and complex staggered lock-in schedules. Market participants trading in these SME securities should note the applicable lot sizes and lock-in expiry dates before executing transactions.
