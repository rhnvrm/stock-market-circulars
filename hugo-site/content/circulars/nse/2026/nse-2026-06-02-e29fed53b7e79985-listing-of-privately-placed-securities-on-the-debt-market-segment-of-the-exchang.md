## Summary

NSE has admitted several privately placed debt securities — predominantly commercial papers (CPs) — to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective June 02, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations. Issuers include major financial institutions and corporates such as NABARD, IIFL Finance, ICICI Securities, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Godrej Industries, HDFC Securities, Kotak Securities, Alpha Alternatives Financial Services, and Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority.

## Key Points

- Eight commercial papers from seven issuers listed with effect from June 02, 2026.
- CPs carry 91-day to ~6-month tenors, all with face value of ₹5,00,000 each.
- NABARD CP (Sr 270): 40,000 units, ISIN INE261F14PL7, allotted June 01, redeemable August 31, 2026.
- IIFL Finance CP: 500 units, ISIN INE530B14HO5, allotted June 01, redeemable August 31, 2026.
- ICICI Securities CP (31/08/26): 6,500 units, ISIN INE763G14J64, allotted June 01, redeemable August 31, 2026.
- ICICI Securities CP (25/11/26): 736 units, ISIN INE763G14J72, allotted June 01, redeemable November 25, 2026.
- Motilal Oswal Financial Services CP: 10,000 units, ISIN INE338I14MQ3, allotted June 01, redeemable August 31, 2026.
- Godrej Industries CP (Sr 18): 1,500 units, ISIN INE233A148O9, allotted June 01, redeemable August 31, 2026.
- HDFC Securities CP: 200 units, ISIN INE700G14TZ4, allotted June 01, redeemable July 31, 2026.
- Kotak Securities CP (Sr 68): 3,500 units, ISIN INE028E14WS4, allotted June 02, redeemable September 01, 2026.
- Alpha Alternatives Financial Services: Market-linked debt security (ISIN INE0L6807336), 19,003 units at ₹1,18,000 issue price, allotted May 27, 2026, maturing November 04, 2031, with put/call option on October 14, 2031.
- Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority: 8.50% bond (STRPP A), details partially listed in Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listings are in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for members. The circular is informational, notifying all NSE members of the designated security codes and admission of these securities to the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Dealings:** June 02, 2026
- **CP Redemption Dates:** July 31, 2026 (HDFC Securities); August 31, 2026 (NABARD, IIFL, ICICI Securities ×2, Motilal Oswal, Godrej Industries); September 01, 2026 (Kotak Securities)
- **Alpha Alternatives Maturity:** November 04, 2031; Put/Call Option: October 14, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, operationally low-impact circular. It expands the tradeable universe on NSE's Debt Market Segment with fresh commercial paper issuances from well-known financial institutions and corporates. These CPs represent short-term funding instruments for the issuers and provide liquidity options for institutional investors. The market-linked security from Alpha Alternatives and the HMDA bond cater to longer-duration debt investors. No impact on equity markets or existing trading members' obligations.
