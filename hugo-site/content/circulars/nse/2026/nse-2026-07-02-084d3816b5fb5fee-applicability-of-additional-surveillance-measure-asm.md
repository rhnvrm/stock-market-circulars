## Summary

NSE Circular NSE/SURV/75002 dated July 02, 2026 (Circular Ref. No. 496/2026) announces updates to the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long-Term ASM) framework. Aegis Logistics Limited (AEGISLOG) and Net Avenue Technologies Limited (CBAZAAR) have been newly shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage I, with Aegis Logistics having moved from the Short-Term ASM framework. Vipul Limited (VIPULLTD) has been moved from Long-Term ASM Stage I to Stage II. No securities were shortlisted under Stage IV (which triggers a shift to Trade-for-Trade/BE series) in this round.

## Key Points

- Long-Term ASM Stage I additions (w.e.f. July 03, 2026): AEGISLOG (Aegis Logistics Limited, ISIN INE208C01025) — moved from STASM to LTASM; CBAZAAR (Net Avenue Technologies Limited, ISIN INE518X01015)
- Stage I to Stage II movement (w.e.f. July 03, 2026): VIPULLTD (Vipul Limited, ISIN INE946H01037)
- Stage IV shortlisting (Criteria VII, Rolling to Trade-for-Trade shift): Nil
- Stage II to Stage III movement: Nil
- Stage I to Stage IV movement: Nil
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and not an adverse comment on the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this circular applies the existing Long-Term ASM framework (per prior circulars NSE/SURV/39265, 45111, 46557, 48506, 52090, 63362, and 64066) to a fresh set of securities based on periodic screening criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must apply 100% margin on all open positions (as on July 06, 2026) and new positions created from July 07, 2026 onwards in the newly/re-classified ASM securities.
- Members must monitor and comply with applicable surveillance actions for securities moving between ASM stages.
- No securities require shifting from Rolling Settlement (EQ) to Trade-for-Trade (BE) segment in this circular, as no scrips met Criteria VII (Stage IV).

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Circular issued
- July 03, 2026: Effective date for ASM stage shortlisting/movement of listed securities
- July 06, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- July 07, 2026: 100% margin becomes applicable on open and new positions in the identified securities

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement significantly raises the cost of trading in AEGISLOG, CBAZAAR, and VIPULLTD, likely reducing trading volumes and liquidity in these stocks. Investors and traders holding or considering positions in these securities should account for the increased capital requirement effective July 07, 2026. Since no securities were shortlisted under Stage IV, there is no immediate Trade-for-Trade (BE series) transition impact in this circular.
