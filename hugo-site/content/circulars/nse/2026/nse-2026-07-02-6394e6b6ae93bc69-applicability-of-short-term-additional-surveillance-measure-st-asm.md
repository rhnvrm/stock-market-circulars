## Summary

NSE circular NSE/SURV/75003 dated July 02, 2026 (Circular Ref. No: 497/2026) provides an update on the applicability of the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Per the attached annexures, no securities were newly shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I or Stage II, and none moved between stages, for the review period. Separately, four securities — Aegis Logistics Limited, Denta Water and Infra Solutions Limited, EMS Limited, and Kirloskar Industries Limited (list may include additional entries beyond the truncated excerpt) — are being excluded from the ASM framework effective July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- No securities satisfied the criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I in this cycle.
- No securities satisfied the criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage II in this cycle.
- No securities are moving from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage I.
- Four securities (Aegis Logistics, Denta Water and Infra Solutions, EMS Limited, Kirloskar Industries, and potentially more) are being excluded from the ASM framework altogether, effective July 03, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance mechanism and should not be interpreted as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the continuing ST-ASM framework established via earlier NSE circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/52144, NSE/SURV/58558, and NSE/SURV/64066 dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, April 28, 2022, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024 respectively). No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this is a routine periodic update of the shortlisted/excluded securities list.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply the margin rate of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) for any Stage I securities — none listed this cycle.
- Members must apply the margin rate of 100% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) for any Stage II securities — none listed this cycle.
- Margin requirements for the four excluded securities are relieved effective July 03, 2026, subject to no other surveillance measures applying.
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Circular issued.
- July 03, 2026: Effective date for exclusion of listed securities from the ASM framework (Annexure II) and reference date for open positions.
- July 06, 2026: Effective date for applicable margin rates on any ST-ASM Stage I/II positions (open as of July 03, 2026, and new positions from this date) — not applicable this cycle as no securities were added.

## Impact Assessment

This cycle's update is largely neutral to mildly positive for the market as no new securities face additional margin burdens under ST-ASM. However, the exclusion of Aegis Logistics, Denta Water and Infra Solutions, EMS Limited, and Kirloskar Industries from the ASM framework is a positive development for those specific stocks, as it removes enhanced margin requirements and associated trading friction, potentially improving liquidity and trading volumes for these names.
