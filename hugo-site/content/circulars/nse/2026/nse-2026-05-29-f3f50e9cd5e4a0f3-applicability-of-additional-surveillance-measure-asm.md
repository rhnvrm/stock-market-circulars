## Summary

NSE has announced updates to the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (Long-Term ASM) framework effective June 01, 2026. Five securities are newly shortlisted under Stage-I, one security is moving from Stage-I to Stage-II, and the applicable margin rate of 100% will be enforced from June 03, 2026. Securities qualifying under criteria VII (Stage-IV) will additionally be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ series) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE series).

## Key Points

- 5 securities added to Long-Term ASM Stage-I w.e.f. June 01, 2026: BLISSGVS, GVPIL, KSHINTL, SUNDRMBRAK, VINDHYATEL
- BLISSGVS (Bliss GVS Pharma Limited) moved from Short-Term ASM (STASM) to Long-Term ASM (LTASM)
- 1 security (MUKTAARTS - Mukta Arts Limited) moves from Stage-I to Stage-II w.e.f. June 01, 2026
- No securities shortlisted under Stage-IV; no securities moving from Stage-II to Stage-III
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on June 02, 2026 and new positions from June 03, 2026
- ASM shortlisting is a surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

The circular is issued under the Long-Term ASM framework as established by prior circulars NSE/SURV/39265 (Oct 27, 2018), NSE/SURV/45111 (Jul 22, 2020), NSE/SURV/46557 (Dec 04, 2020), NSE/SURV/48506 (Jun 04, 2021), NSE/SURV/52090 (Apr 22, 2022), NSE/SURV/63362 (Aug 09, 2024), and NSE/SURV/64066 (Sep 20, 2024). The price band for any security exiting the framework will revert to the band applicable before its inclusion, unless the security remains under another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must apply a 100% margin on open positions in the listed securities as of June 02, 2026 and on all new positions created from June 03, 2026 onwards
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded in accordance with the applicable ASM stage provisions
- Securities under Stage-IV (if any) must be handled under the Trade-for-Trade (BE series) settlement segment from June 03, 2026
- Members may direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: New securities effective under Long-Term ASM Stage-I and Stage-II movements
- **June 02, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 03, 2026**: 100% margin enforcement begins; Trade-for-Trade segment shift effective for Stage-IV securities

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or taking new positions in the affected securities (BLISSGVS, GVPIL, KSHINTL, SUNDRMBRAK, VINDHYATEL, and MUKTAARTS at Stage-II). This typically leads to reduced liquidity, increased volatility, and potential forced liquidation by margin-constrained traders. MUKTAARTS faces heightened restrictions as it advances to Stage-II. No securities are being shifted to Trade-for-Trade in this notification (Stage-IV list is nil), which limits the most severe liquidity impact. The ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures.
