## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Infibeam Avenues Limited will undergo a corporate name and trading symbol change. The company name will change from "Infibeam Avenues Limited" to "AvenuesAI Limited" and the trading symbol will change from "INFIBEAM" to "CCAVENUE". These changes will be effective from February 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from Infibeam Avenues Limited to AvenuesAI Limited
- Trading symbol changing from INFIBEAM to CCAVENUE
- Changes effective from February 03, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department
- All exchange members have been informed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving name and symbol modification for an existing listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Trading systems should be configured to recognize CCAVENUE as the new symbol from February 03, 2026
- Market participants should update their records and databases accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Existing shareholders will see their holdings automatically transferred to the new symbol. Trading will continue under the new symbol CCAVENUE from the effective date.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Brokers, trading members, and market systems need to update configurations to accommodate the symbol and name change. Investors need to be aware of the new symbol for placing orders.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors holding INFIBEAM shares should be aware that their holdings will be reflected under CCAVENUE symbol post-change. No action required from shareholders as the change is automatic.
