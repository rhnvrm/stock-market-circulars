## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental surrender of membership request from R.L.P. Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INZ000166638). The member has surrendered its Derivatives segment registration, with the surrender approved effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member: R.L.P. Securities Pvt. Ltd.
- SEBI registration number: INZ000166638
- Segment surrendered: Derivatives
- Surrender approval date: July 17, 2026
- Circular Ref. No: 67/2026; Download Ref No: NSE/COMP/75259

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative approval under NSE's membership regulations, not a change to rules or regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All members are requested to take note of the segmental surrender. R.L.P. Securities Pvt. Ltd. will no longer be permitted to operate in the Derivatives segment following this approval.

## Important Dates

- Surrender approval date: July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact, being limited to the membership status of a single trading member in the Derivatives segment. It does not affect listed securities, trading in other segments, or broader market operations.
