## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Futures & Options (F&O) segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. The session covers two phases: Trading Session-1 from the Primary Site and Trading Session-2 from the Disaster Recovery (DR) Site. No new NEAT+ version will be released for this mock; existing versions 7.8.7 and 7.8.8 remain in use.

## Key Points

- Mock trading in F&O segment scheduled for Saturday, May 23, 2026
- No new software version release for this mock session
- Two trading sessions: Primary Site (Session-1) and DR Site (Session-2)
- NEAT+ versions 7.8.7 (compatible with existing broadcast parameters) and 7.8.8 (compatible with new broadcast parameters) both remain applicable
- NEATPlus 7.8.7 discontinuation date: June 06, 2026 (mock)
- New NEAT Adapter Version 1.0.27 (Windows and Linux) applies; applicable GR PORT is 10833
- All outstanding orders cleared before each session; NNF software users must manually clear orders
- No fund pay-in or pay-out obligations arising from mock trades
- Members encouraged to participate across all trading software and re-login to live environment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. Members are directed to refer to:
- NSE/MSD/74133 dated May 08, 2026 for new broadcast/market data parameters
- NSE/MSD/73993 dated April 30, 2026 for interactive connectivity details
- NSE/MSD/73674 dated April 09, 2026 for NEAT Adapter Exe application details
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 and NSE/MSD/46441 for software testing and regulatory compliance

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use NEATPlus 7.8.7 or 7.8.8 as applicable based on broadcast parameter compatibility
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed during the mock session; UCC validation is skipped during contingency time for order entry
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders before each session
- Members should use the mock session to verify software and connectivity; either Mock Trading or Simulated Environment may be used to meet SEBI regulatory testing requirements
- For UCC/PAN queries, contact: uci@nse.co.in

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it pertains solely to a routine mock/test trading session with no financial settlement obligations. The primary audience is member firms (brokers) in the F&O segment who need to verify their trading software and connectivity prior to live trading resuming on Monday, May 25, 2026. Members running NEATPlus 7.8.7 should note its upcoming discontinuation in the June 6, 2026 mock session and plan migration to 7.8.8 accordingly.
