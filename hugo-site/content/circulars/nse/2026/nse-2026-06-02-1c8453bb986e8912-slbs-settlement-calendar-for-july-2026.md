## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) settlement calendar for July 2026 via circular ref. 056/2026. The calendar covers three settlement types: First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'), Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'), and Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q').

## Key Points

- Circular issued pursuant to NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026.
- First Leg Settlement (Type 'L') runs from July 1, 2026 to July 31, 2026, with settlement numbers 2026121 through 2026143.
- Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P') covers series from 07/X7 (settlement on Jul 7, 2026) through 07/X7 of 2027 (settlement on Jul 6, 2027).
- The 07/X7 series expiring on Jul 6, 2027 will be available from Jul 8, 2026 after the current 07/X7 series expires on Jul 7, 2026.
- Settlement is on T+1 basis for First Leg trades throughout July 2026.
- Final First Leg settlement (2026143) on Jul 31, 2026 settles on Aug 3, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic notification of settlement schedules in continuation of the framework established under circular NCL/CMPT/73999 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians must adhere to the published settlement schedules for SLBS transactions in July 2026.
- Participants must ensure timely custodial confirmation as per the dates specified in Annexure 'A'.
- Reverse Leg settlements (Annexure 'B') must be settled on the designated dates for each series.

## Important Dates

- **Jul 1, 2026**: First SLBS First Leg trade date (Settlement No. 2026121); settles Jul 2, 2026.
- **Jul 7, 2026**: Reverse Leg settlement for 07/X7 series (Settlement No. 2026507); current 07/X7 series expires.
- **Jul 8, 2026**: New 07/X7 series (expiring Jul 6, 2027) becomes available.
- **Jul 31, 2026**: Last First Leg trade date for July (Settlement No. 2026143); settles Aug 3, 2026.
- **Aug 4, 2026**: Reverse Leg settlement for 08/X8 series.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with limited market impact. It is directly relevant to participants and custodians active in the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme on NSE. Compliance with the specified settlement dates is mandatory for smooth settlement operations in July 2026. No new products, restrictions, or policy changes are introduced.
