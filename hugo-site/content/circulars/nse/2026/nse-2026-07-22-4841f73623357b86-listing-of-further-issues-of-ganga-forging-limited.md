## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issue of equity shares of Ganga Forging Limited under its Final Rights Issue, admitting the new shares to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from July 23, 2026, under the existing symbol GANGAFORGE.

## Key Points

- Company: Ganga Forging Limited; Symbol: GANGAFORGE; Series: EQ
- ISIN: INE691Z01023
- Security Description: Equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued under Rights - Final
- Face Value: Re. 1; Paid-up Value: Re. 1; Issue Price: Rs. 1.63
- Date of Allotment: July 21, 2026
- Number of Securities: 202,203,345
- Distinctive Number Range: 134802231 to 337005575
- Market Lot: 1
- Pari Passu status: Yes
- Lock-in: Not Applicable
- Shares allotted in dematerialized mode are currently credited under a temporary ISIN pending activation, per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify and trade the newly listed shares using the designated security codes and the specified market lot of 1 share. No additional action is required from investors beyond standard settlement processes.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 21, 2026
- Effective/Listing Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 202,203,345 new equity shares to GANGAFORGE's tradable float following the final tranche of its rights issue, pari passu with existing shares. This is a standard post-rights-issue listing action with limited immediate market impact beyond an increase in the company's outstanding share count and liquidity.
