## Summary

NSE has informed its members of the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited following its IPO. The company will trade under the symbol CMLL, with ISIN INE11XY01018. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited
- Trading Symbol: CMLL
- ISIN: INE11XY01018
- Listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification following an IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems and records to recognize the new symbol CMLL and ISIN INE11XY01018 ahead of the listing date, once announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new tradable security to the NSE Capital Market segment. Market impact is limited until the formal listing date is announced, at which point trading members will need to enable the symbol for trading.
