## Summary

NSE's Compliance Department has approved the request of Star Finvest Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INZ000160735) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment. The surrender was approved on April 17, 2026, and all members are notified accordingly.

## Key Points

- Star Finvest Pvt. Ltd. has voluntarily surrendered its trading membership in the Currency Derivatives segment.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000160735
- Surrender approval date: April 17, 2026
- Circular issued by NSE Compliance Department under Circular Ref. No. 33/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a membership surrender under existing NSE membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this surrender. No active compliance action is required from other members. Star Finvest Pvt. Ltd. is no longer authorized to trade in the Currency Derivatives segment.

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2026**: Surrender approval date for Star Finvest Pvt. Ltd. in Currency Derivatives segment
- **April 20, 2026**: Date of circular notification to all members

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action involving a single member exiting one segment. Other members and market operations in the Currency Derivatives segment remain unaffected. Counterparties or clients of Star Finvest Pvt. Ltd. in the Currency Derivatives segment should arrange alternative membership if required.
