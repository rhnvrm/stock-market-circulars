## Summary

NSE has informed all members that the Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund (Zerodha Mutual Fund) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 13, 2026. The fund will also be available for systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP).

## Key Points

- Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from April 13, 2026
- Facility covers subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Scheme available: Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01190)
- Fund category: Equity
- Circular issued by NSE NMF TM Segment (Ref: NSE/NMFTM/73688, Circular Ref. No: 1321/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification enabling transaction facilities for a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed of the availability so they can facilitate client transactions in this fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. No specific compliance action required beyond awareness.

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 13, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notification is limited to investors seeking to transact in the Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund via the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides access to an equity index fund tracking midcap and smallcap segments (Nifty MidSmallcap400), which may benefit retail investors looking for passive exposure to that market segment. No systemic or broad market implications.
