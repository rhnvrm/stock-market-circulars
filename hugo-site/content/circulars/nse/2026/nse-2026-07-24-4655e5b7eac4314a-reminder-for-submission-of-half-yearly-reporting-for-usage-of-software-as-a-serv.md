## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder to all trading members regarding the half-yearly reporting requirement for usage of Software as a Service (SaaS) based solutions/applications. This obligation stems from SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020, and NSE circular no. NSE/INSP/75018 dated July 02, 2026. Trading members must submit their compliance report by July 31, 2026, via the Member Portal.

## Key Points

- Trading members using SaaS-based solutions/applications must submit a half-yearly compliance report.
- Members not utilizing any SaaS services must still submit the report, selecting 'NO' and providing an appropriate comment/remark.
- Submission path: Member Portal → Inspection → SaaS → Submit SaaS Report.
- Non-adherence to SaaS guidelines is subject to financial disincentives as per Annexure 1.1 of NSE Circular No. NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this circular is a reminder of existing SEBI and NSE requirements regarding SaaS-based solution usage reporting by trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members, regardless of whether they use SaaS-based solutions, must log into the Member Portal and submit the SaaS Report under the Inspection section. Non-users must explicitly select 'NO' with a remark. Failure to comply may attract financial disincentives as specified in the referenced April 2026 circular.

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2026**: Deadline for submission of half-yearly SaaS usage report.
- Reference circulars: SEBI circular dated November 03, 2020; NSE/INSP/75018 dated July 02, 2026; NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily affects the operational compliance workflow of trading members and does not have direct market impact. However, non-compliance could result in financial disincentives, making timely submission important for affected trading members.
