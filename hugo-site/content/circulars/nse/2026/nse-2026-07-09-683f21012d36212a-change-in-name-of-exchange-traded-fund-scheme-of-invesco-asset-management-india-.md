## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Exchange Traded Fund scheme 'Invesco India Nifty Exchange Traded Fund', managed by Invesco Asset Management (India) Private Limited and traded under the symbol IVZINNIFTY, will be renamed to 'Invesco India Nifty 50 Exchange Traded Fund' with effect from July 15, 2026. The ISIN remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Scheme symbol IVZINNIFTY retains its existing trading symbol.
- Existing scheme name: Invesco India Nifty Exchange Traded Fund.
- New scheme name: Invesco India Nifty 50 Exchange Traded Fund.
- ISIN INF205K01DA9 remains unchanged.
- Change is effective July 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a scheme name change notification issued by NSE's Listing Department under Download Ref No. NSE/CML/75135, Circular Ref. No. 1124/2026. No change to the fund's investment objective, ISIN, or trading symbol accompanies this update.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should update internal systems, client-facing materials, and reference data to reflect the new scheme name from the effective date. No action is required from investors as the ISIN and trading symbol remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 09, 2026
- Effective date of name change: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and purely administrative, as trading under the existing symbol IVZINNIFTY continues uninterrupted and the ISIN is unaffected. Members and data vendors should ensure the updated scheme name is reflected in reports and disclosures post effective date to avoid discrepancies.
