## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed) issued by IDFC First Bank Limited (ISIN: INE688I08152) effective May 21, 2026. The suspension is triggered by the issuer exercising its call option, resulting in redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Trading in IDFC First Bank Limited NCS (ISIN: INE688I08152) will be suspended from May 21, 2026
- Reason for suspension: Redemption for exercising call option
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/74312 | Circular Ref. No: 0810/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is executed under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in ISIN INE688I08152 (IDFC First Bank Limited NCS) effective May 21, 2026
- Members holding or facilitating client positions in this instrument should communicate the suspension and upcoming redemption to affected clients

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026**: Circular issued
- **May 21, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective date; call option redemption date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific privately placed non-convertible security (INE688I08152). Secondary market liquidity in this instrument ceases from May 21, 2026. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the call option terms. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
