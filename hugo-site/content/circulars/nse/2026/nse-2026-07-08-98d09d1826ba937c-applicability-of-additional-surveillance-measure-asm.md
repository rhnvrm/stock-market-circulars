## Summary

NSE has issued a circular dated July 08, 2026, identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion under the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework. As per the attached Annexure, IOL Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals Limited (Symbol: IOLCP, ISIN: INE485C01029) has been shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage I effective July 09, 2026. No securities were shortlisted under Stage IV, and no securities were identified for movement between existing ASM stages (I to II, II to III, I to IV, or II to IV) in this circular.

## Key Points

- IOLCP (IOL Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals Limited) shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Framework Stage I w.e.f. July 09, 2026
- No new securities added to Stage IV
- No securities shifted between ASM stages in this circular
- 100% margin applicable on all open and new positions from July 13, 2026
- ASM framework operates alongside other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under ASM is a surveillance measure only and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under the existing Long-Term ASM framework established via prior circulars (NSE/SURV/39265, 45111, 46557, 48506, 52090, 63362, and 64066). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine periodic update identifying newly qualifying securities under existing ASM criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE trading members must apply 100% margin on all open positions in IOLCP as on July 10, 2026, and on new positions created from July 13, 2026 onwards
- Members should update risk management systems to reflect the revised margin requirement for the affected security
- No Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE) shift is applicable in this circular since no securities were shortlisted under Criteria VII (Stage IV)

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2026: Circular issued
- July 09, 2026: IOLCP shortlisted under Long-Term ASM Stage I
- July 10, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- July 13, 2026: 100% margin becomes effective on open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margin significantly increases the cost of trading in IOLCP, likely reducing speculative activity and liquidity in the stock. This is a security-specific measure with limited broader market impact, as it affects only one stock and does not involve a shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement. Investors and traders holding or trading IOLCP should account for the higher margin requirement starting July 13, 2026.
