## Summary

NSE's Listing Department has notified the suspension of trading in three privately placed Non-Convertible Securities effective June 25, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A. The suspensions are due to scheduled redemption of the respective instruments.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in three NCD instruments across LIC Housing Finance Limited, Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited, and NHPC Limited
- Suspension effective date: June 25, 2026
- Reason for all suspensions: Redemption
- Circular issued under Ref No: NSE/CML/74845, Circular Ref. No: 1007/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The suspension is invoked under the existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, which governs trading suspensions for securities reaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified to cease trading in the listed ISINs from the suspension date
- No further action required from members beyond acknowledging the suspension of these instruments

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension for all three securities

| Sr. No. | Company Name | ISIN | Suspension Date | Reason |
|---------|-------------|------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | LIC Housing Finance Limited | INE115A07JW0 | 25-June-2026 | Redemption |
| 2 | Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited | INE831R08035 | 25-June-2026 | Redemption |
| 3 | NHPC Limited | INE848E07864 | 25-June-2026 | Redemption |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders of the specific NCD instruments being redeemed. This is a routine operational action with no broader market implications. Investors in these instruments will receive redemption proceeds as per the respective security terms. No equity trading is affected.
