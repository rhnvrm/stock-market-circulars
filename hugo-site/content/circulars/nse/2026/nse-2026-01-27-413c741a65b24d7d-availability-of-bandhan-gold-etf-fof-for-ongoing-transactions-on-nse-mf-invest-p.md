## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Bandhan Gold ETF FOF (Fund of Fund) of Bandhan Mutual Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting January 28, 2026. Both Regular and Direct plan Growth options will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- Bandhan Gold ETF FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from January 28, 2026
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options are available
- Full systematic facility support: SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Bandhan Gold ETF FOF Regular Plan-Growth ISIN: INF194KB1KK5
- Bandhan Gold ETF FOF Direct Plan-Growth ISIN: INF194KB1KL3
- Category: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This circular is informational for members of NSE regarding the availability of the mutual fund scheme on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - Bandhan Gold ETF FOF available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions
- **Circular Date:** January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational update. It provides investors with access to Bandhan's Gold ETF Fund of Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The availability of both Regular and Direct plans with systematic investment facilities offers flexibility to investors seeking gold exposure through ETF fund-of-funds structure. The impact is limited to investors and distributors interested in this specific mutual fund product.
