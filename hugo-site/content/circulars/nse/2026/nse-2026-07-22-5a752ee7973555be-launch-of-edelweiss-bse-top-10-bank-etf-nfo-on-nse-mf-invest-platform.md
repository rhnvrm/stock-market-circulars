## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Edelweiss BSE Top 10 Bank ETF NFO, launched by Edelweiss Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 23, 2026 to July 30, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- Scheme: Edelweiss BSE Top 10 Bank ETF - Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth
- ISIN: INF754K01XH9 (common for both plans as listed)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: August 06, 2026
- NFO subscription window on NSE MF Invest Platform: July 23, 2026 to July 30, 2026, till 11:59 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 04, 2026, till 06:00 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: July 23, 2026
- NFO Close: July 30, 2026 (11:59 pm)
- Funds Pay-in Deadline: August 04, 2026 (06:00 pm)
- Allotment Date: August 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact; relevant primarily to NSE members and distributors handling mutual fund transactions who need to ensure timely fund settlement for investor subscriptions during the NFO window.
