## Summary

NSE's Debt Segment (Circular Ref. No: 03/2026) has announced a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, February 28, 2026. The session covers three platforms: the Order Matching Platform, the Negotiated Trade Reporting Platform, and the Tri-Party Repo Market Platform. Members are encouraged to participate actively to test connectivity. No actual financial obligations will arise from trades executed during this session.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled from DR site on Saturday, February 28, 2026
- No changes required at member end for connecting to Primary/DR site
- Login URL remains the same: https://ndm.nseindia.com
- Trades from the mock session carry no fund or securities settlement obligations
- Trades reported on the Negotiated Trade Reporting Platform on February 28, 2026 will not be considered
- Members should not transfer any data files for this session
- Updates/announcements available at www.nseindia.com
- Queries: Toll Free 1800-266-0050 (Option 1) | Email: msm@nse.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational drill to test DR site connectivity and readiness.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must actively participate in the mock session to verify connectivity
- No data files should be transferred for this mock session
- No fund transfers or securities transfers should be made in relation to mock trades

## Important Dates

- **February 28, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session from DR site
  - **Order Matching Platform:** Normal Market 09:00–17:00 hrs; Auction Market 14:00–14:35 hrs
  - **Negotiated Trade Reporting Platform:** Normal Market 09:00–17:15 hrs (Govt & Non-Govt Securities)
  - **Tri-Party Repo Market Platform:**
    - T+0 Settlement: 09:00–13:00 hrs
    - T+1 Settlement: 09:00–17:00 hrs
    - Repo Auction Market: 15:00–15:35 hrs

## Impact Assessment

Impact is minimal. This is a scheduled DR drill with no real market or financial consequences. Members in the Debt Segment should ensure connectivity checks are completed during the session windows. No portfolio, settlement, or compliance impact is expected. The exercise is operational in nature and serves to validate business continuity preparedness of NSE's Debt Segment platforms.
