## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited (symbol: TURTLEMINT, series: EQ) on the Capital Market segment effective June 29, 2026. The IPO issue price is Rs. 152 per share with a total of 294,478,906 equity shares of face value Re. 1 each admitted to dealings. The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** TURTLEMINT | **Series:** EQ | **ISIN:** INE0OC301013
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **IPO Issue Price:** Rs. 152/- per share
- **Total Shares Listed:** 294,478,906 equity shares
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Registrar:** KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai
- **Company Secretary & Compliance Officer:** Mr. Prashant Saini (022-68387400)
- **Registered Office:** The ORB Sahar, 4 and 4A, 1st Floor, A Wing, Marol Village, Andheri East, Mumbai – 400 099

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. The security will be part of the pre-open session per SEBI circular nos. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security solely by its designated code (TURTLEMINT) for trading purposes.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 1 share.
- Members must observe lock-in restrictions applicable to specified tranches of shares as detailed in Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Listing Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Financial Year:** April 2026 – March 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates:**
  - 59,200 shares: December 25, 2026
  - 160,030,330 shares: December 25, 2026
  - 59,383,684 shares: December 25, 2027
  - 13,065,840 shares: July 23, 2026
  - 13,065,840 shares: September 21, 2026
  - 48,874,012 shares: Free (no lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

The IPO listing of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited introduces a new fintech scrip to NSE's Capital Market segment. With 294 million total shares at an issue price of Rs. 152, the implied market capitalisation at listing is approximately Rs. 44,760 crore, making this a significant new entrant. A substantial portion of shares (~73%) are subject to lock-in periods ranging from July 2026 to December 2027, which limits near-term free float and may affect initial liquidity. Members and investors should factor in the phased lock-in schedule when assessing tradeable supply post-listing.
