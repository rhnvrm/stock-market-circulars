## Summary

NSE Capital Market segment circular (Ref: NSE/CMTR/74729, Circular 84/2026) notifies all trading members of a takeover offer for Accord Synergy Limited (Symbol: ACCORD, Series: TO). Dr. Farukbhai Gulambhai Patel (Acquirer 1) along with Mr. Muinulhaque Iqbalhusen Kadva (PAC) will use the Exchange's web-based bidding platform for this offer, conducted in accordance with the Tender Offer Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Dr. Farukbhai Gulambhai Patel along with PAC Mr. Muinulhaque Iqbalhusen Kadva
- Target company: Accord Synergy Limited (ISIN: INE113X01015)
- Offer price: Rs. 42.35 per share (fixed price)
- Issue size: Up to 9,72,500 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share; Lot size: 2,000 equity shares
- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Order modification (quantity) and cancellation are NOT permitted for takeover offers
- Merchant Banker: Rarever Financial Advisors Private Limited
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under the existing Tender Offer Scheme framework per NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members already on the web-based IPO bidding platform can use existing user IDs at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- Members without access must log in to the admin terminal using Adminxxxxx (where xxxxx is the Member Code), then create branches and users to participate
- Custodial confirmation must be completed within the specified windows each day
- Bidding categories supported: FII, MF, IC, FI, Other QIBs (OTH), Bodies Corporate (CO), Individuals (IND), Other Non-Institutional Investors (NOH)

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 16, 2026
- Issue / Bidding period: June 24, 2026 to July 8, 2026
- Daily bidding and custodial confirmation time: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Custodial confirmation on issue closure day: 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant primarily to trading members of NSE's Capital Market segment and investors holding or interested in Accord Synergy Limited shares. The offer at Rs. 42.35 per share for up to 9,72,500 equity shares is a company-specific event with limited broader market impact. Members not yet registered on the IPO bidding platform should set up admin access before June 24, 2026 to ensure timely participation.
