## Summary

NSE notifies the admission of units of Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 ETF to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective June 18, 2026. The ETF, managed by Groww Asset Management Limited, will trade under the symbol SMALLGROWW with ISIN INF666M01PG9.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** SMALLGROWW
- **ISIN:** INF666M01PG9
- **Units Listed:** 1,266,762 units of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 45.68 per unit
- **Face Value / Paid-Up Value:** Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Series:** EQ
- **Date of Allotment:** June 16, 2026
- **Market Makers:** Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd (TM: 90246), East India Securities Ltd (TM: 08134), Irage Broking Services LLP (TM: 90068), Parwati Capital Market Pvt Ltd (TM: 11890)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, governing the admission of new securities and lot size designation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code SMALLGROWW for all trades in this ETF.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit.
- Market makers listed above are obligated to provide liquidity as per their obligations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 16, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective From:** June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of a new passively managed ETF tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 index. The listing adds a new instrument to the NSE Capital Market segment for investors seeking smallcap exposure with a momentum-quality factor tilt. With four designated market makers, liquidity support is in place from day one. The relatively small initial unit count (1.27 lakh units) and low market lot size of 1 suggest it is designed for retail accessibility. Impact on broader markets is minimal.
