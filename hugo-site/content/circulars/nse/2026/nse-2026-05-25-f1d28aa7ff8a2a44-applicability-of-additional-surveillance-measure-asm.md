## Summary

NSE has issued Circular No. 387/2026 (Ref: NSE/SURV/74389) dated May 25, 2026, notifying the inclusion of Norben Tea & Exports Limited (Symbol: NORBTEAEXP) under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Framework at Stage I, effective May 26, 2026. A 100% margin requirement will apply from May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Norben Tea & Exports Limited (NORBTEAEXP, ISIN: INE369C01017) is included in Long Term ASM Stage I w.e.f. May 26, 2026
- No securities have been moved to Stage II, Stage III, or Stage IV in this circular
- 100% margin is applicable on all open positions as on May 27, 2026, and on new positions created from May 29, 2026 onwards
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and operates under the framework established by earlier NSE circulars: NSE/SURV/39265, NSE/SURV/45111, NSE/SURV/46557, NSE/SURV/48506, NSE/SURV/52090, NSE/SURV/63362, and NSE/SURV/64066. No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this is an application of the existing Long Term ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure 100% margin is collected on all open positions in NORBTEAEXP as on May 27, 2026
- 100% margin must be collected on all new positions in NORBTEAEXP created from May 29, 2026 onwards
- Members should communicate ASM implications to their clients holding or intending to trade in the affected security
- Securities under Criteria VII (Stage IV) would be shifted to Trade-for-Trade (Series: BE); however, no securities qualify under this criteria in this circular

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **May 26, 2026**: Effective date for Long Term ASM Stage I inclusion of NORBTEAEXP
- **May 27, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **May 29, 2026**: Date from which 100% margin becomes applicable on open positions (as of May 27) and all new positions

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is limited to a single security — Norben Tea & Exports Limited (NORBTEAEXP). The imposition of 100% margin under Long Term ASM Stage I significantly increases the cost of holding or taking new positions in this stock, which may reduce trading volumes and liquidity. Traders and investors holding positions in NORBTEAEXP must arrange additional capital to meet the elevated margin requirement by May 29, 2026. No securities were shifted to Trade-for-Trade (Stage IV) settlement, so overall market settlement operations remain unaffected. The broader market impact is minimal given the single-stock scope.
