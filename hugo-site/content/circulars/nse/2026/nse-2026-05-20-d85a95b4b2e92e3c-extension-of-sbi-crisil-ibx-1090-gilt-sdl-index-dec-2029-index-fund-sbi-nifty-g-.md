## Summary

NSE has communicated a revision to the NFO Close Date and Allotment Date for two SBI Mutual Fund NFOs listed on the NSE MF Invest Platform: the SBI CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt +SDL Index - Dec 2029 Index Fund and the SBI Nifty G-Sec Jul 2031 Index Fund. Both dates have been extended by six days following communication received from SBI Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- NFO Close Date revised from May 19, 2026 to May 25, 2026 for both funds
- Allotment Date revised from May 20, 2026 to May 26, 2026 for both funds
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 11:59 PM on the NFO closure date (May 25, 2026)
- Clear funds via any payment mode must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by May 26, 2026 at 3:00 PM
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment (Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/74302, Circular Ref. No: 1384/2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update reflecting revised operational dates communicated by SBI Mutual Fund to NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before May 26, 2026 by 3:00 PM
- Clients wishing to register SIP/XSIP must complete authentication before 11:59 PM on May 25, 2026 (the revised NFO closure date)

## Important Dates

| Event | Previous Date | Revised Date |
|---|---|---|
| NFO Close Date | May 19, 2026 | May 25, 2026 |
| Allotment Date | May 20, 2026 | May 26, 2026 |
| Funds receipt deadline | — | May 26, 2026, 3:00 PM |
| SIP/XSIP auth deadline | — | May 25, 2026, 11:59 PM |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to investors and members participating in the NFOs for SBI CRISIL-IBX 10:90 Gilt +SDL Index - Dec 2029 Index Fund and SBI Nifty G-Sec Jul 2031 Index Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The extension gives investors additional time to subscribe. No broader market or regulatory impact is anticipated. Members should update their systems and inform clients of the revised dates promptly.
