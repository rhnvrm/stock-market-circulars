## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the takeover offer under the tender offer scheme of Sanginita Chemicals Limited. This circular (ref. NCL/CMPT/74565, Circular No. 0203/2026) is issued in reference to the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and NSE/CMTR/74585 dated June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type X, Settlement No. 2026068 is designated for this takeover offer.
- Trade window opens on June 10, 2026 and closes on June 23, 2026.
- Obligation download date is June 29, 2026; settlement date is June 30, 2026.
- Demat shares must be tendered exclusively via Early Pay-in of Shares through the depository mechanism for Market Type Buyback.
- On the trade end date, early pay-in of shares must be completed well before 03:30 p.m. for order acceptance.
- Securities and funds pay-out will be made after receipt of funds pay-in on the settlement day.
- A Tender Offer Acceptance Report will be provided to members detailing accepted, partially accepted, and rejected orders along with rejection reason codes.
- Applicable Securities Transaction Tax (STT) will be collected on the respective settlement day.
- The Clearing Corporation may notify a revised settlement schedule if there are delays in receipt of acceptance details from RTA or delays in funds pay-in by the buying trading member.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operationalises the settlement schedule pursuant to previously issued circulars regarding the Sanginita Chemicals Limited takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, Custodians, and PCMs must tender demat shares only through the Early Pay-in mechanism via depositories under Market Type Buyback.
- Early pay-in of shares on the trade end date must be completed well in advance of 03:30 p.m.
- Members should refer to the original scheme document (NCL/CMPT/73996 and NSE/CMTR/74585) for all other details.
- For clarifications, members should contact the NCL help desk at ncl_tenderoffer@nsccl.co.in or Toll Free: 1800 266 00 50 (Option 2).

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operational in nature and directly affects members, custodians, and PCMs participating in the Sanginita Chemicals Limited takeover offer. The settlement calendar provides clear timelines for tendering, obligation download, and final settlement. The inclusion of a comprehensive rejection code list (Annexure I covering 20 rejection reasons such as signature discrepancies, invalid transfer deeds, demat account mismatches, and excess share tendering) ensures transparency for all participants. The impact is limited to parties involved in this specific takeover offer and does not affect broader market operations.
