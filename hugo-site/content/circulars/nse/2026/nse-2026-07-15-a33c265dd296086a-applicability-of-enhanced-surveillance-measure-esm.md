## Summary

NSE has issued a circular updating the list of securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Two securities, MADHAVBAUG (Vaidya Sane Ayurved Laboratories Limited) and SHARDUL (Shardul Securities Limited), have been newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I. One security, VLEGOV (VL E-Governance & IT Solutions Limited), is being moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II. No securities are moving from Stage II back to Stage I, and no securities are being excluded from the ESM framework in this cycle.

## Key Points

- MADHAVBAUG and SHARDUL have been newly included under ESM Stage I and will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (EQ/SM) to the Trade-for-Trade segment (BE/ST) effective July 17, 2026.
- VLEGOV is being moved from ESM Stage I to Stage II effective July 16, 2026, and will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- No securities are moving from Stage II to Stage I this cycle (Nil).
- No securities are being excluded from the ESM framework this cycle (Nil).
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework (including AAREYDRUGS, ACCORD, and others) is provided in Annexure III of the circular.
- The ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ESM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under NSE's existing ESM framework, referencing prior circulars NSE/SURV/56948, NSE/SURV/57609, NSE/SURV/63361, NSE/SURV/64066, NSE/SURV/64400, and NSE/SURV/69315. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this update applies existing ESM criteria to reclassify specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply a minimum 100% margin on all open positions in MADHAVBAUG and SHARDUL as on July 16, 2026, and on new positions created from July 17, 2026.
- Members must ensure trading in MADHAVBAUG and SHARDUL shifts from EQ/SM to BE/ST series w.e.f. July 17, 2026.
- Members must apply the Trade-for-Trade mechanism with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction for VLEGOV w.e.f. July 16, 2026, following its move to Stage II.
- Queries regarding the ESM framework should be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- July 16, 2026: Stage transitions take effect (Stage I to Stage II for VLEGOV); 100% margin applies on open positions in newly added securities.
- July 17, 2026: MADHAVBAUG and SHARDUL shift from EQ/SM to BE/ST (Trade-for-Trade) segment; 100% margin applies on new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under ESM face significantly reduced liquidity due to the shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement (eliminating intraday trading) and the imposition of 100% margin requirements, which substantially raises the cost of taking positions. For MADHAVBAUG and SHARDUL, this represents a new and material trading restriction. VLEGOV's move to Stage II tightens restrictions further with a 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction. These measures are targeted at curbing speculative trading in specific securities and do not reflect a broader market-wide impact, but are highly consequential for holders and traders of the specific stocks named.
