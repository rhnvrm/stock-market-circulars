## Summary

NSE has announced that Clay Craft India Limited (symbol: CLAYCRAFT), listed under its SME IPO, will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (series: ST) to the rolling segment (series: SM) effective July 09, 2026. This is a routine transition that follows the mandatory trade-for-trade period applicable to newly listed SME IPO securities.

## Key Points

- Symbol CLAYCRAFT will move from series ST (trade for trade) to series SM (rolling segment)
- Effective date: July 09, 2026
- The transfer is pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Further to Exchange Circular NSE/CML/74823 dated June 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the existing SEBI framework (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012) governing the transition of newly listed SME IPO securities from trade-for-trade to rolling settlement after the mandatory surveillance period.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are notified to update their systems and trading practices accordingly for CLAYCRAFT from July 09, 2026, when normal rolling settlement (series SM) applies instead of the trade-for-trade mechanism (series ST).

## Important Dates

- Circular date: June 24, 2026
- Effective date of transfer to rolling segment: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard post-IPO operational step for SME-listed securities. The move to rolling settlement improves liquidity and trading flexibility for CLAYCRAFT, removing the trade-for-trade restriction that requires delivery-based settlement for every transaction. Retail and institutional traders in this scrip will benefit from normal netting and settlement conveniences available in the rolling segment.
