## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of units issued by Jio BlackRock Asset Management Private Limited under its Prism Hybrid Long-Short Fund scheme. The units, admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, will be available for dealing on the Capital Market segment effective July 23, 2026, with designated security codes and lot sizes specified in the circular's annexure.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Prism Hybrid Long-Short Fund by Jio BlackRock Asset Management Private Limited
- Two plans listed: Regular Plan Growth (Symbol: PSIFHLSRG, ISIN: INF22M030019) and Direct Plan Growth (Symbol: PSIFHLSDG, ISIN: INF22M030027)
- Series classification: SF
- Face value and paid-up value per unit: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 10/-
- Date of allotment: July 17, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trustee: Jio BlackRock Trustee Private Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: Computer Age Management Services Private Limited (CAMS)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must identify the security only by its designated security codes and trade in the specified lot size (1 unit) once dealings commence. No other specific action is required from members beyond standard trading system compliance.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 17, 2026
- Effective date for listing and commencement of dealings: July 23, 2026
- Circular issue date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and primarily affects investors and distributors dealing in the Prism Hybrid Long-Short Fund. It enables secondary market trading of the fund's units on NSE, providing liquidity access for unit holders. No impact on existing listed securities or broader trading operations is expected.
