## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a circular excluding JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited (JBCHEPHARM) from the Securities Lending & Borrowing (SLB) segment, effective July 17, 2026. This action follows an earlier circular (NSE/CML/75178 dated July 13, 2026) regarding the suspension of trading in the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- JBCHEPHARM shall not be available for transactions in the SLB segment with effect from July 17, 2026.
- The exclusion is a direct consequence of the suspension of trading in JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited's equity shares, as notified in an earlier circular.
- The circular is addressed to all Participants and Custodians in the SLB segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an operational action implementing the consequences of a prior trading suspension by excluding the security from SLB segment eligibility.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and Custodians must ensure no new SLB transactions are initiated in JBCHEPHARM from the effective date, and must account for the exclusion in their existing SLB positions and operations.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Original circular (NSE/CML/75178) on suspension of trading in JBCHEPHARM equity shares.
- July 16, 2026: Date of this circular (Ref No. NCL/CMPT/75241, Circular Ref. No. 073/2026).
- July 17, 2026: Effective date of exclusion of JBCHEPHARM from the SLB segment.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to participants holding or trading JBCHEPHARM in the SLB segment, who will need to unwind or settle existing positions and refrain from new SLB transactions in this security. This follows from the broader trading suspension already in effect, so the incremental market impact of this specific circular is moderate.
