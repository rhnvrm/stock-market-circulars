## Summary

NSE has informed its members that SBI CRISIL IBX Fin Ser 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund, an NFO by SBI Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions — including SIP, STP, and SWP facilities — on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective April 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants of SBI CRISIL IBX Fin Ser 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform from April 27, 2026.
- Facilities available: ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- All six variants are classified under the Debt category.
- The fund tracks the CRISIL IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding platform availability of a new fund offering.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed to note the availability of this fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform and facilitate investor transactions accordingly from April 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of SBI CRISIL IBX Fin Ser 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact routine announcement. The addition of six debt index fund variants to the NSE MF Invest Platform provides retail and institutional investors with additional fixed-income investment options targeting the 9-12 months financial services debt segment.
