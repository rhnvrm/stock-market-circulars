## Summary

NSE has updated the list of securities under Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for companies under Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Gensol Engineering Limited (Symbol: GENSOL, ISIN: INE06H201014) has been added to ASM IBC Stage I effective February 02, 2026. No securities are being excluded from the framework. The consolidated list now includes 4 securities in Stage I: NEUEON, SWANDEF, GENSOL, and EASTSILK.

## Key Points

- Gensol Engineering Limited added to ASM IBC Stage I effective February 02, 2026
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or vice versa
- No securities excluded from ASM IBC framework in this update
- ASM IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and not an adverse action against the company
- Reference circulars: NSE/SURV/37979 (June 08, 2018), NSE/SURV/52368 (May 20, 2022), NSE/SURV/55582 (February 10, 2023), NSE/SURV/70074 (September 05, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the existing ASM IBC framework established through previous circulars by updating the list of affected securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the updated ASM IBC classification for affected securities
- Members should apply surveillance measures as per the ASM IBC framework guidelines
- Trading in affected securities subject to applicable ASM IBC Stage I conditions
- Queries should be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: February 01, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 02, 2026 - ASM IBC measures applicable for newly shortlisted securities

## Impact Assessment

**Affected Securities:**
- **Stage I New Entries**: Gensol Engineering Limited (GENSOL - INE06H201014)
- **Consolidated ASM IBC List** (4 securities in Stage I):
  1. Neueon Corporation Limited (NEUEON - INE333I01044)
  2. Swan Defence and Heavy Industries Limited (SWANDEF - INE542F01020)
  3. Gensol Engineering Limited (GENSOL - INE06H201014) - NEW
