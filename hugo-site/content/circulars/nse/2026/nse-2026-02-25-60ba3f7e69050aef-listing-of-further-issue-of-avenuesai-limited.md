## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of a further issue of 79,26,820 equity shares of AvenuesAI Limited (Symbol: CCAVENUE) on the Capital Market segment, effective February 26, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up equity shares under ISIN INE483S01020.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AvenuesAI Limited
- **Symbol:** CCAVENUE
- **Series:** EQ
- **ISIN:** INE483S01020 (permanent); temporary ISIN IN8483S01029 was used during allotment
- **Total shares listed:** 79,26,820
- **Face Value / Paid-up Value:** Re. 1/- each
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Passu:** Yes â€” shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable
- **Security Description:** Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The listing is made pursuant to:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission of securities to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

Shares were originally credited under a temporary ISIN (IN8483S01029) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 2, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) governing activation of ISIN for additional share issuances.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated symbol **CCAVENUE** and security codes as specified in the Annexure for all trades.
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of **1 share**.
- Members holding securities under the temporary ISIN (IN8483S01029) should note the migration to the permanent ISIN (INE483S01020).

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 79,26,820 fully paid-up equity shares of AvenuesAI Limited to the NSE Capital Market segment. These shares are pari passu with existing shares and carry no lock-in restrictions, meaning they are immediately tradeable from February 26, 2026. The conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up status increases the company's paid-up capital and expands the freely tradeable float. The distinctive number ranges are:
- 3,47,16,77,963 to 3,47,61,01,627 (44,23,539 shares)
- 3,47,61,01,501 to 3,47,96,04,907 (35,03,281 shares)

This is a routine corporate action with moderate market impact limited to existing and prospective shareholders of CCAVENUE.
