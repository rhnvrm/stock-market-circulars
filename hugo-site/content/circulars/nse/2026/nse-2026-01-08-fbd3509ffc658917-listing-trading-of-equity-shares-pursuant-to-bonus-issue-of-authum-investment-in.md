## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited (AIIL) pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 67,93,80,400 new equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective January 15, 2026. The new shares have been assigned ISIN INE206F01022 with distinctive numbers ranging from 169845101 to 849225500.

## Key Points

- Company: Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited (Symbol: AIIL)
- Number of new shares: 67,93,80,400 equity shares
- New ISIN: INE206F01022
- Distinctive Number Range: 169845101 to 849225500
- Trading segment: Capital Market segment of NSE
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of securities admission pursuant to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated codes for trading purposes
- All trading members should update their systems to reflect the new shares and ISIN

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 08, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: January 14, 2026
- **Effective Date for Listing & Trading**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue will significantly increase the outstanding share count of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares as per the bonus ratio. The increased float may improve liquidity in the stock. The market capitalization remains unchanged, but the per-share price will adjust proportionately. This is a standard corporate action with medium impact on existing shareholders and the trading activity of the stock.
