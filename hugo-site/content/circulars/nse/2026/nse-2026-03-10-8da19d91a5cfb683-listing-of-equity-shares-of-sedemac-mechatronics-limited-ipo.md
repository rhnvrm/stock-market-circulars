## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited on the Capital Market segment effective March 11, 2026. The company is admitted to dealings under symbol SEDEMAC (Series: EQ) with a total of 44,161,500 equity shares at an issue price of Rs. 1352 per share (face value Rs. 10 each). The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips.

## Key Points

- Symbol: SEDEMAC, Series: EQ, ISIN: INE00XB01019
- Total shares listed: 44,161,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each fully paid up
- Issue price: Rs. 1352/- per share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Shares are Pari Passu with existing shares
- Security will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited), Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (SEDEMAC / EQ / INE00XB01019) for all trading activity
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- Members should note the applicable lock-in periods for various tranches of shares

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- **Lock-in dates for specific tranches**:
  - 11,163 shares locked until April 7, 2026
  - 3 shares locked until April 7, 2026
  - 1,194,032 shares locked until June 6, 2026
  - 25,500 shares locked until March 12, 2026
  - 8,849,600 shares locked until September 9, 2026
  - 9,771,300 shares locked until September 9, 2026
  - 562,500 shares locked until September 9, 2027
  - Remaining shares are free (no lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited marks a new entrant on NSE's Capital Market segment. With 44.16 million shares at an issue price of Rs. 1352, the company enters with a significant implied market capitalisation. Lock-in restrictions on portions of the shareholding (notably promoter/anchor tranches) limit near-term selling pressure on those tranches. Market participants should note the pre-open session applicability on listing day (March 11, 2026). The company is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, and operates in the mechatronics sector.
