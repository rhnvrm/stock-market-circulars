## Summary

NSE has notified that trading in a privately placed Non-Convertible Security issued by Piramal Finance Limited will be suspended effective July 2, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Part A. The suspension is on account of redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Security: Non-Convertible Security (Privately Placed) of Piramal Finance Limited
- ISIN: INE140A07211
- Suspension effective date: July 2, 2026
- Reason for suspension: Redemption
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations, Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations governing suspension of debt securities upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in this NCD must cease trading in ISIN INE140A07211 from the suspension date. No further compliance action required from other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 1, 2026
- Suspension effective date: July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this pertains to a single privately placed debt security being suspended due to redemption, a routine and expected event affecting only holders of this specific ISIN.
