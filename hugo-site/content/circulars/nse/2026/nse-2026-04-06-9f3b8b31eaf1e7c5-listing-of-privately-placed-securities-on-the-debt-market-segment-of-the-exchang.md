## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities issued by Corbett 03 2026 Trust to dealings on the Debt Market Segment of the Exchange effective April 06, 2026. This is a fresh issue under the 'Other Debt Securities' category, notified under Circular Ref. No. 0576/2026 (Download Ref: NSE/CML/73618).

## Key Points

- Securities of Corbett 03 2026 Trust admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment with effect from April 06, 2026
- Security description: CT 12% 2029 Sr A1 (Series: Sr A1)
- ISIN: INE2TD115019
- Security code: CT29
- Total quantity: 19,755 securities
- Face value, paid-up value, and issue price: ₹10,000 each
- Coupon rate: 12% per annum
- Date of allotment: March 27, 2026
- Date of redemption: July 17, 2029
- No call or put option dates applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is executed in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are notified of the new security codes and ISIN for reference and trading purposes
- Members must use the designated security code (CT29) for dealings in this instrument on the Debt Market Segment

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing:** April 06, 2026
- **Date of Redemption:** July 17, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market-wide impact. The securities are privately placed and targeted at institutional investors participating in NSE's Debt Market Segment. The 12% coupon rate and ~3.3-year tenor (to July 2029) positions this as a medium-term fixed-income instrument. Broader retail market participants are unlikely to be directly affected.
