## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of NLC India Limited. This circular (ref. NCL/CMPT/74611) supplements the earlier circular NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026, and confirms that OFS settlements will follow existing secondary market transaction rules.

## Key Points

- Settlement covers two order dates: June 9, 2026 and June 10, 2026
- Both H-type (100% margin) and T-type (0% margin) settlements are applicable for non-retail (IS) category
- Retail category (RS) applies only to H-type settlement on June 10, 2026
- Custodial confirmation deadline for H-type non-retail: 3:30 PM on order date
- Custodial confirmation deadline for T-type non-retail: 7:30 AM on T+1 day
- Applicable STT and stamp duty will be collected on respective settlement days

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the earlier OFS framework circular (NCL/CMPT/73996 dated April 30, 2026) by providing specific settlement dates for the NLC India Limited OFS.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must adhere to the specified custodial confirmation timings
- Funds pay-in must be completed by 11:00 AM on T+1 day
- STT and stamp duty obligations must be met on the respective settlement day
- For queries, contact the NCL help desk at 1800 266 0050 (Option 2) or ofs_ncl@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

| Settlement No. | Category | Order Date | Custodial Confirmation | Settlement Date |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2026106 (H) | IS – Non-Retail (100% margin) | 09-Jun-2026 | 09-Jun-2026, 3:30 PM | 10-Jun-2026 |
| 2026106 (T) | IS – Non-Retail (0% margin) | 09-Jun-2026 | 10-Jun-2026, 7:30 AM | 10-Jun-2026 |
| 2026107 (H) | RS – Retail (100% margin) | 10-Jun-2026 | NA | 11-Jun-2026 |
| 2026107 (H) | IS – Non-Retail (100% margin) | 10-Jun-2026 | 10-Jun-2026, 3:30 PM | 11-Jun-2026 |
| 2026107 (T) | IS – Non-Retail (0% margin) | 10-Jun-2026 | 11-Jun-2026, 7:30 AM | 11-Jun-2026 |

**Settlement Timing:**
- Funds Pay-in: T+1 day by 11:00 AM
- Securities & Funds Pay-out: T+1 day by 1:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact on members, custodians, and PCMs (Professional Clearing Members) participating in the NLC India OFS. Participants must ensure timely custodial confirmations and fund pay-ins to avoid settlement failures. The two-day OFS window (June 9-10) with next-day settlement (June 10-11) is a standard OFS structure. Non-retail investors face a 100% upfront margin for H-type settlements, while T-type settlements carry no margin requirement but require next-morning custodial confirmation.
