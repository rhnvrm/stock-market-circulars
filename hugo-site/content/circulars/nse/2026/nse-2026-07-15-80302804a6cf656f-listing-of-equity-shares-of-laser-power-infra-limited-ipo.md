## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing all members of the Exchange about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Laser Power & Infra Limited, which is coming to market via an IPO. The security will trade under the symbol LASERPOWER with ISIN INE17IR01028. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Laser Power & Infra Limited
- Trading Symbol: LASERPOWER
- ISIN: INE17IR01028
- This circular pertains to the IPO listing on NSE
- The specific listing date is yet to be announced

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under NSE's standard process for admitting new securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems and records to recognize the new symbol LASERPOWER and ISIN INE17IR01028 ahead of the security becoming available for trading.

## Important Dates

Date of circular: July 15, 2026. The actual date of listing and commencement of trading will be announced via a separate circular.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to members and investors tracking the Laser Power & Infra Limited IPO. Once listed, trading in LASERPOWER will begin, providing a new investment avenue; broader market impact is expected to be minimal given this is a single new listing.
