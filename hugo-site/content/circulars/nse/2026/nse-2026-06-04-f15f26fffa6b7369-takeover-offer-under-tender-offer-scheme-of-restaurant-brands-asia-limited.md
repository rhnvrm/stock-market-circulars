## Summary

NSE has announced the activation of its web-based bidding platform for a takeover offer of Restaurant Brands Asia Limited (NSE symbol: RBA, Series: TO). The acquirers — Lenexis Foodworks Private Limited, Aayush Agrawal Trust, Inspira Foodworks Private Limited, and Mr. Aayush Madhusudan Agrawal, along with PAC Inspira Agro Trading LLC — are offering to acquire up to 20,80,61,717 equity shares at a fixed price of Rs. 70.39 per share. The offer runs from June 8 to June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Offer price: Rs. 70.39 per share (fixed price)
- Issue size: Up to 20,80,61,717 equity shares
- Bidding period: June 8, 2026 to June 19, 2026
- Bidding hours: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily; 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on the closure day
- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Members already on the NSE web-based IPO bidding platform can use existing credentials
- Order modification (quantity) and cancellation are NOT permitted once submitted
- Face value: Rs. 10/-; Lot size: 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE07T201019
- Merchant Banker: Motilal Oswal Investment Advisors Limited
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under the existing Tender Offer Scheme as per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members without access to the web-based IPO bidding system must log in to the admin terminal (user ID: Adminxxxxx, where xxxxx is the Member Code) to create branches and users before participating.
- Members must use the live URL https://eipo.nseindia.com for bidding.
- Order modification or cancellation of quantity is strictly not permitted in takeover offers.
- Custodial confirmations must be completed within the specified time windows.

## Important Dates

- **Issue Open Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Issue Close Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Daily Bidding Window:** 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Custodial Confirmation (closure day):** 9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action for shareholders of Restaurant Brands Asia Limited. With an offer to acquire up to ~208 million shares at Rs. 70.39, this represents a major ownership change in the company. Trading members need to ensure platform access is in place before June 8. The fixed-price nature and no-modification rule require careful order placement. Eligible investor categories include FIIs, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Banks & FIs, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors.
