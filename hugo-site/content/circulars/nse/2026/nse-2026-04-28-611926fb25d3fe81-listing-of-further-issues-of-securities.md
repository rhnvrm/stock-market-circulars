## Summary

NSE Circular Ref. No. 0705/2026 (Download Ref. NSE/CML/73945), dated April 28, 2026, notifies the admission to dealings on the NSE Capital Market segment of further issued securities for 12 companies, effective April 29, 2026. The additional securities are predominantly equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs).

## Key Points

- Securities of 12 companies admitted to NSE Capital Market segment from April 29, 2026
- All listings are further issues (not IPOs), primarily ESOP allotments
- All new shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot for all securities is 1
- No lock-in applicable for any of the listed allotments
- Issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for admission to dealings and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes and specified lot sizes for all listed securities
- Securities are identifiable only by their designated codes on the trading system

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 29, 2026
- **Allotment Dates by Company:**
  - Abans Financial Services Limited (AFSL): April 8, 2026 — 1,34,000 shares (ISIN: INE00ZE01026)
  - BlackBuck Limited (BLACKBUCK): April 23, 2026 — 81,197 shares + 18,596 shares = 99,793 shares total (ISIN: INE0UIZ01018)
  - CreditAccess Grameen Limited (CREDITACC): April 20, 2026 — 6,800 shares (ISIN: INE741K01010)
  - DCB Bank Limited (DCBBANK): April 22, 2026 — 16,000 shares (ISIN: INE503A01015)
  - Gulf Oil Lubricants India Limited (GULFOILLUB): April 13, 2026 — shares allotted under ESOP (ISIN: INE635Q01029)
  - ICICI Bank Limited, ICICI Lombard General Insurance, Global Health Limited, Meesho Limited, Moschip Technologies Limited, FSN E-Commerce Ventures Limited (Nykaa), SPML Infra Limited: Details in Annexure

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. These are routine ESOP share listings representing incremental additions to existing float for each company. The largest single issuance visible in the circular is 1,34,000 shares for AFSL and ~99,793 shares for BlackBuck — both small relative to total outstanding shares. No regulatory or structural changes are involved. Traders and investors should update holdings data to reflect updated distinctive number ranges and share counts for the affected ISINs.
