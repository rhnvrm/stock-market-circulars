## Summary

NSE's Inspection Department has issued a reminder to all trading members to submit the Cyber Security and Cyber Resilience Audit Report for the period ended March 31, 2026. The submission is mandatory for all Qualified REs, Mid-size REs, and Small-size REs and must be completed electronically via the ENIT Member Portal by June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Reminder issued under circular ref no. 29/2026, referencing original circular NSE/INSP/73849 dated April 22, 2026
- Submission is mandatory for all Trading Members classified as Qualified REs, Mid-size REs, and Small-size REs
- Report must be submitted electronically through the ENIT Member Portal
- Failure to comply may result in penalty or disciplinary action as prescribed in NSE/INSP/73792 dated April 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a reminder of existing requirements established under NSE/INSP/73849 (April 22, 2026) and NSE/INSP/73792 (April 17, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members categorized as Qualified REs, Mid-size REs, or Small-size REs must submit the Cyber Security and Cyber Resilience Audit Report for the period ending March 31, 2026
- Submission must be made electronically through the ENIT Member Portal
- Members must comply by the due date to avoid penalty or disciplinary action

## Important Dates

- **Audit Period:** April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2026
- **Submission Deadline:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no direct impact on markets or individual stocks. However, it carries significant compliance implications for trading members who have not yet filed the mandatory cyber audit report. Non-compliance may attract financial penalties or disciplinary proceedings as defined in earlier NSE circulars. Members across all regional offices (Ahmedabad, Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai) are equally bound by this requirement.
