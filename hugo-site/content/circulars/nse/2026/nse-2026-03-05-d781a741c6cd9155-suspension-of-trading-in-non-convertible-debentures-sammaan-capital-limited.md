## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in three Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited, effective March 09, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of these debenture instruments, in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Three NCDs of Sammaan Capital Limited will be suspended from trading effective March 09, 2026
- Suspension reason: Maturity of all three securities
- Securities affected: 990SCL26D (INE148I07OB8), 948SCL26D (INE148I07NZ9), and 990SCFL26 (INE148I07NY2)
- Circular issued under Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73143, Circular Ref. No: 0382/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The suspension is carried out under the existing framework of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs suspension of securities upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of NSE must note the suspension of these three NCD symbols and cease facilitating trades in them from March 09, 2026
- Holders of these debentures should follow the standard redemption/maturity settlement process with Sammaan Capital Limited

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension is a routine, lifecycle-driven action resulting from the maturity of three NCDs issued by Sammaan Capital Limited. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. Trading in these symbols (990SCL26D, 948SCL26D, 990SCFL26) will no longer be available on NSE from March 09, 2026. The impact is limited to existing holders of these specific securities and does not affect equity or other instruments of Sammaan Capital Limited.
