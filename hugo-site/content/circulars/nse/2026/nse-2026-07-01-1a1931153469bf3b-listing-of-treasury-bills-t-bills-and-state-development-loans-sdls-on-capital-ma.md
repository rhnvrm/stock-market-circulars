## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of new Treasury Bills (T-bills) and State Development Loans (SDLs) on the Capital Market segment, effective July 03, 2026. The circular specifies the security codes, lot sizes, coupon rates, maturity dates, and ISIN codes for 3 T-bills and 8 SDLs newly admitted to dealings under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- 3 new Treasury Bills admitted for trading: 364-day, 182-day, and 91-day T-bills
- 8 new State Development Loans (SDLs) admitted for trading across multiple states (Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Punjab, Rajasthan)
- All securities carry a lot size of 100
- Trading and identification will be based on designated security codes and specified lot sizes
- Effective date: July 03, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes for identification and trade only in the specified lot sizes (100 units) for all listed securities effective July 03, 2026.

## Important Dates

- July 01, 2026: Circular issued
- July 03, 2026: Effective date for trading of listed T-bills and SDLs
- Maturity dates range from October 01, 2026 (91-day T-bill) to July 01, 2055 (SDL TS 7.65% 2055)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular that expands the list of government debt securities (T-bills and SDLs) available for trading on NSE's Capital Market segment. It does not affect equity markets, existing listed stocks, or trading operations beyond providing additional debt investment options for market participants.
