## Summary

NSE has informed its members that the Whiteoak Capital Aggressive Hybrid Fund NFO of Whiteoak Mutual Fund will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from June 08, 2026 to June 22, 2026 (till 11:59 PM). The fund offers both Regular Growth and Direct Growth options under the Equity category, with allotment scheduled for June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 08, 2026 to June 22, 2026 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two scheme variants available: Regular Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AG6) and Direct Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AF8)
- Both schemes are categorized under Equity
- Allotment date for both schemes: June 30, 2026
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 29, 2026 till 06:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure that clear funds (through any payment mode) are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before June 29, 2026 till 06:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: NFO subscription opens on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **June 22, 2026**: NFO subscription closes (till 11:59 PM)
- **June 29, 2026**: Deadline for clear funds in Clearing Corporation account (till 06:00 PM)
- **June 30, 2026**: Allotment date for both Regular Growth and Direct Growth schemes

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with limited market-wide impact. Members operating on the NSE MF Invest Platform should note the subscription window and payment deadline to facilitate investor transactions in the new Whiteoak Capital Aggressive Hybrid Fund schemes.
