## Summary

NSE's Surveillance Department (Circular Ref. No. 482/2026, Download Ref No: NSE/SURV/74943) announces the latest revision to its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Fourteen securities are newly included under ESM Stage I, one security moves from Stage I to Stage II, and certain securities are eligible to exit the framework. The changes take effect July 1–2, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14 securities added to ESM Stage I will attract a minimum **100% margin** on all open positions as on July 01, 2026, and on new positions created from July 02, 2026.
- All Stage I securities marked with an asterisk (*) will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment (Series: EQ/SM) to the **Trade-for-Trade segment (Series: BE/ST)** w.e.f. July 02, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage II** will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** under Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 01, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is available in Annexure III of the circular.
- The ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ESM is based purely on market surveillance criteria and does not constitute an adverse action against the company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the ESM applicability list in line with the framework established under prior circulars: NSE/SURV/56948 (June 02, 2023), NSE/SURV/57609 (July 18, 2023), NSE/SURV/63361 (August 09, 2024), NSE/SURV/64066 (September 20, 2024), NSE/SURV/64400 (October 04, 2024), and NSE/SURV/69315 (July 25, 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

- **NSE Members** must ensure a minimum 100% margin is collected on all open positions in the Annexure I securities as on July 01, 2026, and on all new positions created from July 02, 2026.
- Members must facilitate the segment shift for applicable securities from EQ/SM to BE/ST effective July 02, 2026.
- For Stage II securities (e.g., ASCOM), members must note the 2% price band restriction under Periodic Call Auction effective July 01, 2026.
- Members with further queries may write to surveillance@nse.co.in.

## Important Dates

- **July 01, 2026:** ESM Stage I inclusion effective; Stage I → Stage II shift effective; Stage II price band (2%, Periodic Call Auction) effective.
- **July 01, 2026:** 100% margin applicable on all open positions as on this date.
- **July 02, 2026:** Segment shift from Rolling Settlement (EQ/SM) to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST) for marked Stage I securities; 100% margin on new positions effective from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 14 securities to ESM Stage I imposes a 100% margin requirement and forces a series shift to Trade-for-Trade (BE/ST), significantly restricting speculative activity and limiting intraday trading in these scrips. Investors holding or wishing to take positions in these securities will face higher capital requirements and reduced liquidity. The Stage II shift for ASCOM introduces an even more restrictive 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction, further limiting price discovery and exit options for holders. Overall market impact on the broader indices is minimal, but retail investors with concentrated positions in these small/mid-cap names may be materially affected.
