## Summary

Victory Electric Vehicles International Limited has been listed on the NSE SME segment following the completion of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shareholding pattern has been filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, reflecting the post-IPO shareholding structure as of January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company listed: Victory Electric Vehicles International Limited
- Security type: Equity Shares
- Filing basis: Post-issue of IPO (January 13, 2026)
- Regulation: SEBI LODR Regulation 31(1)(a)
- Promoter shares are locked-in as per IPO requirements
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners declared

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing and shareholding pattern disclosure following SME IPO completion.

## Compliance Requirements

- The listed entity has submitted shareholding pattern as required under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR
- Promoter lock-in requirements applicable as per SME IPO regulations
- Ongoing disclosure obligations under listing regulations will apply

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: IPO allotment date and listing date
- **January 13, 2026**: Shareholding pattern filing date (post-IPO)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds Victory Electric Vehicles International Limited to the NSE SME platform, providing liquidity to IPO investors. The shareholding pattern indicates compliance with promoter lock-in requirements and no encumbrances, suggesting a clean capital structure post-IPO. Impact is limited to investors and stakeholders of this specific company in the electric vehicle sector.
