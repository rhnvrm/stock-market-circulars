## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 20 companies – Aditya Birla Capital, Aeroflex Enterprises, AU Small Finance Bank, Godrej Industries, ICICI Bank, Lupin, Max Healthcare Institute, Physicswallah, Quick Heal Technologies, Radico Khaitan, Renaissance Global, Rishabh Instruments, Shadowfax Technologies, Suprajit Engineering, UTI Asset Management Company, Vimta Labs, HDFC Life Insurance, HMT, RBL Bank and Ideaforge Technology – with effect from July 14, 2026. The shares were predominantly allotted under respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) and will be admitted to dealings in the Capital Market segment under their existing security codes, ISINs, series and market lot of 1, ranking pari passu with existing shares.

## Key Points

- 20 companies have further equity issues (mostly ESOP allotments) listed for trading from July 14, 2026.
- Aditya Birla Capital (ABCAPITAL): 16,10,180 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted 08-Jul-2026.
- Aeroflex Enterprises (AEROENTER): 79,200 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, allotted 26-Jun-2026.
- AU Small Finance Bank (AUBANK): 3,13,485 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, allotted 06-Jul-2026.
- All new shares carry pari passu rights with existing listed shares and have a market lot of 1.
- No lock-in applicable on the ESOP-allotted shares listed for Aditya Birla Capital, Aeroflex Enterprises and AU Small Finance Bank.
- Full company-wise details (ISIN, distinctive number ranges, allotment dates) are provided in the circular's Annexure for all 20 companies.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative notification issued under Regulation 3.1.1 (admission of further securities to dealings) and Regulation 2.5.5 (lot size notification) of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional shares under the existing designated security codes and trade them only in the specified market lot (1 share) from the effective date. No separate member action beyond system recognition is required.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 13, 2026 (NSE/CML/75181, Circular Ref. No. 1139/2026)
- Effective date of listing: July 14, 2026
- Allotment dates vary by company (e.g., Aditya Birla Capital: 08-Jul-2026; Aeroflex Enterprises: 26-Jun-2026; AU Small Finance Bank: 06-Jul-2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative circular reflecting incremental equity dilution from ESOP exercises across 20 listed companies. The share quantities involved are small relative to each company's total outstanding shares, so the effect on market capitalization, float and share price is expected to be negligible. No trading halts or special mechanisms are triggered; existing shareholders and traders need only note the marginal increase in listed share count for the affected symbols.
