## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Mirae Asset Nifty200 Momentum 30 Plus 8-13 yr G-Sec 7525 Index Fund, a new fund offer (NFO) under Mirae Asset Mutual Fund, will be available for transacting on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- The NFO covers six plan/option variants of the scheme: Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Payout, and Regular IDCW Reinvestment.
- All variants are classified under the Equity category with allotment date of July 28, 2026.
- ISINs assigned: INF769K01RG1 (Direct Growth), INF769K01RI7 (Direct IDCW Payout), INF769K01RH9 (Direct IDCW Reinvestment), INF769K01RD8 (Regular Growth), INF769K01RF3 (Regular IDCW Payout), INF769K01RE6 (Regular IDCW Reinvestment).
- Members must ensure clear funds via any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by the specified deadline.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notification enabling a new scheme for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members transacting in this NFO must ensure clear funds through any approved payment mode are credited to the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before July 27, 2026 till 11:59 pm.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new scheme details and ISINs for order placement during the NFO window.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026
- Funds pay-in deadline: July 27, 2026, 11:59 pm
- Allotment date: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact, serving primarily as an operational notice for NSE members and distributors to enable transactions in the new index fund scheme. It does not affect listed equities, trading mechanisms, or broader compliance frameworks, but is relevant for mutual fund distributors and platform users facilitating this NFO.
