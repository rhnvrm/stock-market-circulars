## Summary

NSE has admitted additional debt securities issued by TVS Motor Company Limited to dealings on the Debt Segment, effective March 11, 2025. This circular (Download Ref No: NSE/CML/73228, Circular Ref. No: 0419/2026) references earlier circulars NSE/CML/23366 dated May 8, 2013 and NSE/DS/24832 dated October 25, 2013, and provides the Annexure detailing the newly admitted instrument.

## Key Points

- One new debt instrument (TVSM26RDNGPS01092025T1) admitted to NSE Debt Segment.
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (CP) issued by TVS Motor Company Limited.
- Symbol: RDNGPS TVSM26; Issue Date: September 1, 2025; Expiry/Maturity Date: September 1, 2026.
- Face Value: Rs. 10; Total Issue Capital: Rs. 190,034.85 Lakhs.
- ISIN: INE494B04019.
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ (Stable Outlook); Public issue type.
- Coupon: Fixed, Periodic; Last Interest Payment Date: August 31, 2026; Next Interest Payment Date: September 1, 2026.
- Tick Size: 0.01; Multiplier: 1.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is an operational listing notification under the existing framework established by NSE/CML/23366 (May 8, 2013) and NSE/DS/24832 (October 25, 2013).

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are informed of the new security admitted to dealings and should update their systems accordingly.
- No specific compliance action required from listed companies or investors beyond standard debt segment trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 10, 2026
- Effective Date: March 11, 2025 (note: as stated in circular)
- Issue Date: September 1, 2025
- Maturity/Expiry Date: September 1, 2026
- Last Interest Payment Date: August 31, 2026
- Next Interest Payment Date: September 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The admission of TVS Motor Company Limited's commercial paper (CARE A1+ rated) to NSE's Debt Segment provides increased liquidity and a formal trading avenue for institutional investors in this short-term fixed-income instrument. The high credit rating (A1+, Stable) indicates low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants trading TVS Motor securities.
