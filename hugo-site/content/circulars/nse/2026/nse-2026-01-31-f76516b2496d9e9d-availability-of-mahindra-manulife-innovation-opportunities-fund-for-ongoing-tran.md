## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective February 02, 2026. The fund will also support Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities.

## Key Points

- Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund to be available for ongoing transactions from February 02, 2026
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes classified under Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Circular issued to all NSE members on January 30, 2026

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund will be available:

1. Regular - Growth (ISIN: INF174V01CX8)
2. Regular - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01CY6)
3. Regular - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01CZ3)
4. Direct - Growth (ISIN: INF174V01DA4)
5. Direct - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01DB2)
6. Direct - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01DC0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. NSE members are informed of the fund's availability for processing investor transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 02, 2026 - Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with SIP, STP, SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it is specific to investors interested in the Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund. The reopening provides investment opportunities in an equity-focused fund with systematic investment options. NSE members can facilitate transactions for clients seeking exposure to innovation-themed equity investments through this fund.
