## Summary

NSE has informed all members that Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund, offered by Old Bridge Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective March 04, 2026. Systematic facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) will also be available from the same date.

## Key Points

- Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both subscription (including SIP, STP) and redemption (including SWP) facilities will be available.
- Six scheme variants are covered under this circular, spanning Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.
- All six variants are classified under the Equity category.
- Issued under Circular Ref. No: 1236/2026 by the NMF TM Segment department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from members. Members should ensure their systems and platforms reflect the availability of these schemes for client transactions from March 04, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 04, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a low market impact. It affects investors and distributors using the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to transact in Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund. The six scheme variants and their ISINs are as follows:
