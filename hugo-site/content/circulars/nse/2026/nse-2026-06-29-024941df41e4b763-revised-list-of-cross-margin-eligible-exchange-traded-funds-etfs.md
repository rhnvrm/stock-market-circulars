## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has issued a revised list of Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) eligible for cross margining in the Capital Market Segment. This circular supersedes the previous circular no. 0186/2026 (NCL/CMPT/74390) dated May 26, 2026, and is effective from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Revised cross margin eligible ETF list effective July 1, 2026
- Supersedes circular 0186/2026 dated May 26, 2026
- Specifies minimum quantity (and multiples thereof) required for each eligible ETF
- Covers ETFs from major fund houses including Nippon, SBI, ICICI Prudential, HDFC, Kotak, Mirae Asset, and Aditya Birla Sun Life
- Applies to all members of NSE Clearing Limited in the Capital Market Segment

## Compliance Requirements

- All members of NSE Clearing Limited must note the updated eligible ETF list and revised minimum quantity requirements for cross margining purposes
- Cross margin positions in ETFs must comply with the minimum quantity thresholds specified for each scheme

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 1, 2026
- **Supersedes:** Circular 0186/2026 (NCL/CMPT/74390) dated May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update for market participants using ETFs for cross margining in the Capital Market Segment. Members holding or planning cross margin positions in the listed ETFs must ensure compliance with the revised minimum quantity requirements from July 1, 2026. The update affects margin calculations and collateral eligibility for the listed ETF symbols. No new ETFs have been identified as added or removed from the list based on the available content, though the full list may include additional entries.
