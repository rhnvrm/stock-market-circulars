## Summary

NSE has notified members that the Summit Equity Long-Short Fund NFO, offered under the Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category by Invesco Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Summit Equity Long-Short Fund (SIF category) by Invesco Mutual Fund
- Reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions from July 27, 2026
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities also available from the same date
- Applicable plans/options: Regular-Growth, Regular-IDCW Payout, Regular-IDCW Reinvestment, Direct-Growth, Direct-IDCW Payout
- Category classified as Equity for all listed plans
- ISINs provided for each plan/option variant

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational circular enabling continuous transaction facilities for an existing NFO scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members dealing in mutual fund units must ensure the scheme details (ISINs, plan/option codes) are correctly updated in their systems to facilitate client transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: Circular issued
- July 27, 2026: Scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact and is primarily operational in nature, facilitating continued investor access to the Summit Equity Long-Short Fund scheme via NSE's mutual fund distribution platform. It is relevant mainly to NSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors tracking Invesco Mutual Fund's SIF offerings.
