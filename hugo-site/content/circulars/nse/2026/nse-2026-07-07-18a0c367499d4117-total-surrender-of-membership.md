## Summary

NSE has issued a circular informing all members that it has approved the total surrender of trading membership of Global Vision Securities Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000204136). The member's last trade date was November 26, 2025, and the surrender was approved on July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Private Limited has surrendered its trading membership with NSE.
- The member's SEBI registration number was INZ000204136.
- Last trade date recorded for the member was 26-Nov-2025.
- Surrender approval was granted by the Exchange on 02-Jul-2026.
- All NSE members are requested to take note of this development.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a notification of an individual member's exit from the exchange, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from other members or market participants; the circular is issued for informational purposes only.

## Important Dates

- Last Trade Date: November 26, 2025
- Surrender Approval Date: July 2, 2026
- Circular Issue Date: July 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It solely documents the exit of one trading member (Global Vision Securities Private Limited) from NSE membership following exchange approval. There are no implications for listed stocks, trading operations, or other market participants beyond administrative record-keeping.
