## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities of eleven companies - Adani Enterprises Limited, Arisinfra Solutions Limited, AYM Syntex Limited, Easy Trip Planners Limited, India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited, Jamna Auto Industries Limited, Max Estates Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, Shadowfax Technologies Limited, Signatureglobal (India) Limited and Tube Investments of India Limited - effective from July 02, 2026, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Adani Enterprises Limited: 9,011,048 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement, allotment date 15-Apr-2026, distinctive number range 1347514433 to 1356525480, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Arisinfra Solutions Limited: 32,600 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted under ESOP, allotment date 23-Jun-2026, distinctive number range 81761247 to 81793846, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- AYM Syntex Limited: 120,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP, allotment date 18-Jun-2026, distinctive number range 58614092 to 58734091, market lot 1, pari passu, no lock-in.
- Easy Trip Planners Limited: equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued under Preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 9.19.
- Further details for India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited, Jamna Auto Industries Limited, Max Estates Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, Shadowfax Technologies Limited, Signatureglobal (India) Limited and Tube Investments of India Limited are provided in the circular's Annexure (not fully captured in the extracted content).
- Trading in these securities shall be identified only by their designated codes, with lot sizes as specified in the Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular applies existing provisions under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A for listing and lot size notification of further issues.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional securities under their respective designated codes and apply the specified lot sizes when trading these securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: July 01, 2026
- Effective date for listing of further securities: July 02, 2026
- Adani Enterprises allotment date: 15-Apr-2026
- Arisinfra Solutions allotment date: 23-Jun-2026
- AYM Syntex allotment date: 18-Jun-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification enabling trading of additional shares issued via ESOP, preferential allotment, or scheme of arrangement. It does not alter company fundamentals materially but marginally increases the tradeable equity base for the listed companies, particularly Adani Enterprises Limited given the relatively larger share count involved.
