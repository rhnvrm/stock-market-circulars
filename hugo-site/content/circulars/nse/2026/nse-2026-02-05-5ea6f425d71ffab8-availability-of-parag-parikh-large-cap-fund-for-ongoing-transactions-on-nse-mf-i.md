## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund of PPFAS Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective February 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund reopening for transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Effective date: February 06, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund will be available:

1. **Direct Plan Growth** (ISIN: INF879O01332)
2. **Regular Plan Growth** (ISIN: INF879O01365)
3. **Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF879O01357)
4. **Direct Plan IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF879O01340)
5. **Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF879O01381)
6. **Regular Plan IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF879O01373)

All schemes are classified under the Equity category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed of the scheme availability for processing investor transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 06, 2026 - Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with minimal market impact. The reopening of the Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund provides investors with renewed access to this equity fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The announcement affects only investors interested in this specific PPFAS Mutual Fund scheme and does not have broader market implications.
