## Summary

NSE has informed members that the HDFC Nifty Metal ETF FOF NFO, under HDFC Mutual Fund, will be available for transaction on the NSE MF Invest Platform from July 20, 2026 to August 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm).

## Key Points

- Two schemes are being launched: HDFC Nifty Metal ETF FOF Regular Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KR8) and HDFC Nifty Metal ETF FOF Direct Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KS6).
- Both schemes fall under the Equity category.
- The allotment date for both schemes is August 6, 2026.
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment (Download Ref No: NSE/NMFTM/75246, Circular Ref. No: 1499/2026).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification regarding availability of a new fund offer on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before August 6, 2026, till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- NFO subscription window: July 20, 2026 to August 3, 2026 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment date: August 6, 2026
- Fund pay-in deadline: August 6, 2026, till 11:59 pm

## Impact Assessment

This circular is procedural in nature and primarily affects NSE members and mutual fund distributors handling transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. It does not directly impact listed equity securities, trading operations, or broader market structure. Impact is limited to operational readiness of members to process subscriptions and fund settlement for the new HDFC ETF FOF schemes.
