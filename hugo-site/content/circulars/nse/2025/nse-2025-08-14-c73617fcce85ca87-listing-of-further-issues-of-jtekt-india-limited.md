## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 23,116,407 additional equity shares of Jtekt India Limited (Symbol: JTEKTINDIA) under a rights issue. The shares will be available for trading from August 18, 2025, with an issue price of Rs. 108.1 per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Jtekt India Limited (JTEKTINDIA)
- Securities: Equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Number of shares: 23,116,407
- Issue price: Rs. 108.1 per share
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE643A01035
- Market lot: 1
- Pari Passu: Yes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN in dematerialized mode as per SEBI guidelines

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commencement date and circular effective date

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact with addition of over 23 million shares to tradeable float. Rights issue structure indicates existing shareholder participation. No lock-in restrictions provide immediate liquidity. Temporary ISIN arrangement follows standard SEBI protocol for additional share issues.
