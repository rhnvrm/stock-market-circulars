## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Axis Multi-Asset Active Fund of Funds (FOF) of Axis Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 17, 2025. The reopening includes subscriptions, redemptions, and various investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF will be available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions from December 17, 2025
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be enabled
- Six scheme variants are being made available across Regular and Direct plans
- All variants are categorized under the Debt category
- The schemes include Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options

## Scheme Details

The following six schemes of Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF are being reopened:

1. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Regular Growth** - ISIN: INF846KA1333
2. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Regular IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF846KA1358
3. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Regular IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF846KA1341
4. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Direct Growth** - ISIN: INF846KA1366
5. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Direct IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF846KA1374
6. **Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF Direct IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF846KA1382

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is an operational announcement regarding the availability of existing mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are outlined for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the reopening of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 - Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF schemes will be available for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement that affects only investors interested in Axis Multi-Asset Active FOF schemes.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors in these schemes, as they will regain access to subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities.

**Operational Impact**: NSE members and intermediaries should update their systems to enable transactions in these six scheme variants from December 17, 2025.
