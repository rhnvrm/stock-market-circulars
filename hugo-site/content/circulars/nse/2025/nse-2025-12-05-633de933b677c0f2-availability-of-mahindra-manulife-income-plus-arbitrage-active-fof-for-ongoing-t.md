## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active Fund of Funds (FOF) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 08, 2025. The fund will support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants of Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF will be available
- Both Regular and Direct plans will be offered
- Three options available: Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout
- All schemes are categorized under Debt category
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment (Reference: NSE/NMFTM/71642, Circular No. 1039/2025)

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plans
1. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Regular Growth** (ISIN: INF174V01CR0)
2. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Regular IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF174V01CT6)
3. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Regular IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF174V01CS8)

### Direct Plans
4. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF174V01CU4)
5. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Direct IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF174V01CW0)
6. **Mahindra Manulife Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF - Direct IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF174V01CV2)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed of the fund reopening and should ensure their systems are updated to process transactions for these schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It affects only investors interested in Mahindra Manulife's Income Plus Arbitrage Active FOF. The reopening provides investment opportunities in a debt-categorized arbitrage fund of funds structure. Members and investors can now resume transactions in this fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform.
