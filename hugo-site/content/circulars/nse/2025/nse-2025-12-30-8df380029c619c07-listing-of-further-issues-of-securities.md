## Summary

NSE has admitted further equity shares of 22 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 31, 2025. All shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The circular provides details of ISIN codes, distinctive number ranges, face values, and market lot sizes for each issuance.

## Key Points

- Effective date: December 31, 2025
- 22 companies have issued further equity shares under ESOP schemes
- All securities admitted under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1
- Market lot size: 1 share for all listed securities
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable
- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading these securities
- Market lot size of 1 share applies to all listed securities
- Securities to be traded in specified lot sizes as per annexure

## Important Dates

- Circular date: December 30, 2025
- Effective date for trading: December 31, 2025
- Various allotment dates ranging from December 9, 2025 to December 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are small ESOP allotments representing minor dilution to existing shareholders.

**Trading Impact:** No impact on trading operations. Securities will trade with same codes and market lot of 1.

**Investor Impact:** Negligible dilution from employee stock option exercises. All shares rank pari passu with existing equity, maintaining equal rights for all shareholders.
