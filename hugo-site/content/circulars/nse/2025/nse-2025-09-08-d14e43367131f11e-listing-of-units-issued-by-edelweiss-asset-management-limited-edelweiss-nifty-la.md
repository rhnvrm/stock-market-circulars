## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of units issued by Edelweiss Asset Management Limited for the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap 250 ETF. The listing includes 35,487,628 units with trading symbol ELM250, effective from September 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: ELM250
- Total Units: 35,487,628
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 16.06
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- ISIN: INF754K01VV4
- Date of Allotment: September 3, 2025

## Market Maker

- Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited (TM Code: 90246)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing that expands investment options for market participants interested in the Nifty LargeMidcap 250 index. The impact is primarily beneficial for investors seeking diversified exposure to large and mid-cap stocks through a single ETF instrument.
