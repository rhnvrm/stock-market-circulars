## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has notified all members about the name change of Hercules Hoists Limited to Hercules Investments Limited, effective from November 24, 2025. The company will continue to trade under the same symbol 'HERCULES'.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Hercules Hoists Limited (existing) â†’ Hercules Investments Limited (new)
- Trading Symbol: HERCULES (remains unchanged)
- Effective Date: November 24, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71341, Circular No. 2236/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving administrative name change of a listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should update their systems and records to reflect the new company name
- Trading will continue under the existing symbol 'HERCULES'
- Members should ensure proper communication to clients regarding the name change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The name change is administrative in nature and does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The trading symbol remains unchanged, minimizing disruption to trading operations. Members need to update internal records and systems to reflect the new company name.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible. Existing shareholders retain all their rights and holdings; only the company's legal name changes.
