## Summary

NSE has announced that DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 15, 2025. The fund will also support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP).

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund to reopen for transactions on December 15, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Ongoing subscriptions and redemptions will be enabled
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- All schemes fall under the Equity category

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund will be available:

1. Regular Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XE1)
2. Regular IDCW (ISIN: INF740KA1XF8)
3. Regular IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XG6)
4. Direct Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XH4)
5. Direct IDCW (ISIN: INF740KA1XI2)
6. Direct IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XJ0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members are informed of the availability for facilitating investor transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 - DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Circular Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact and is primarily operational in nature. It provides investors with renewed access to DSP's Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund, allowing exposure to smallcap stocks through a passive index investment strategy. The reopening enables both lump sum and systematic investment options, benefiting retail investors seeking diversified smallcap exposure.
