## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 67,040 equity shares of TAC Infosec Limited allotted under Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) on the SME EMERGE platform. The shares will commence trading from December 15, 2025, with symbol 'TAC' under temporary ISIN IN8*********.

## Key Points

- 67,040 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP
- Date of allotment: November 26, 2025
- Trading symbol: TAC
- Series: SM (SME)
- Temporary ISIN: IN8********* (permanent ISIN: INE0SOY01013)
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share
- Distinctive number range: 20959201 to 21026240
- Market lot size: 200 shares
- Pari passu: Yes
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing of further issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security code 'TAC' for identification on the trading system
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot size of 200 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: November 26, 2025
- Effective date for trading: December 15, 2025
- Circular date: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a small ESOP allotment representing additional shares for an existing SME EMERGE listed company. The 67,040 shares will be traded in lot sizes of 200 shares. No lock-in restrictions apply, allowing immediate trading liquidity. The use of temporary ISIN is standard procedure for additional share issues until permanent ISIN activation.
