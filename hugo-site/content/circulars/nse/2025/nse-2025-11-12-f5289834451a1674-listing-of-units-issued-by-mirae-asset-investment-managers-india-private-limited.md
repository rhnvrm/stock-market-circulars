## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of units issued by Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited for two Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) - Mirae Asset Nifty Energy ETF and Mirae Asset Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF. The securities will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective November 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Mirae Asset Nifty Energy ETF (Symbol: ENERGY)**
  - ISIN: INF769K01PR2
  - Number of Units: 2,109,879
  - Face Value: Rs. 10/-
  - Issue Price: Rs. 35.79
  - Date of Allotment: November 07, 2025
  - Market Lot: 1 unit

- **Mirae Asset Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF (Symbol: SMALL250)**
  - ISIN: INF769K01PQ4
  - Number of Units: 3,940,666
  - Face Value: Rs. 10/-
  - Issue Price: Rs. 17.02
  - Date of Allotment: November 07, 2025
  - Market Lot: 1 unit

- **Market Makers** for both ETFs:
  - East India Securities Limited (TM Code: 8134)
  - Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited (TM Code: 90246)
  - Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited (TM Code: 90144)
  - Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited (TM Code: 11890)
  - Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited (TM Code: 12151)
  - IRage Broking Services LLP (TM Code: 90068)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identifying these securities on the trading system
- Trading shall be conducted in the specified lot sizes (1 unit for both ETFs)
- Securities will be traded on the Capital Market segment in the EQ series

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 07, 2025
- **Circular Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard ETF listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with access to two new passive investment vehicles tracking Nifty Energy and Nifty Smallcap 250 indices. The presence of six market makers ensures adequate liquidity for these ETFs. Impact is confined to:

- Investors seeking exposure to energy sector or small-cap stocks through ETF route
- Market makers designated for these securities
- Mirae Asset Mutual Fund scheme investors

No material impact on broader market operations or trading infrastructure.
