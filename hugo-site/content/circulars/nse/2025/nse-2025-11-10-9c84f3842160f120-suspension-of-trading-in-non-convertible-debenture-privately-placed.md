## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed) issued by Sundaram Finance Limited, effective November 11, 2025. The suspension is being implemented due to the redemption of the debenture instrument.

## Key Points

- Single NCD of Sundaram Finance Limited to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE660A07QP4
- Suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the instrument
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71211, Circular No. 2178/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action for debt instrument lifecycle management under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in the specified NCD (ISIN: INE660A07QP4) from November 11, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Debenture holders should note that trading will not be available post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one privately placed NCD instrument of Sundaram Finance Limited. The suspension is due to normal redemption process, not any adverse event.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No trading impact on equity shares of Sundaram Finance Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Routine operational matter for trading members dealing in debt securities. Standard system updates required to prevent trading in the suspended ISIN.
