## Summary

NSE has removed Kavi Commercial Company Limited from its Dissemination Board effective November 22, 2025. The removal follows SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, as the company is listed on another stock exchange with nationwide trading terminals.

## Key Points

- Kavi Commercial Company Limited removed from NSE Dissemination Board
- Reason: Listed on other stock exchange having nationwide trading terminals
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI Circular dated October 10, 2016
- Company was exclusively listed on de-recognized/non-operational/exited stock exchanges
- Effective date: November 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI guidelines from October 2016 regarding exclusively listed companies on de-recognized stock exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed that:
- Kavi Commercial Company Limited will not be available on NSE Dissemination Board
- No trading actions available for this security on NSE platform from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 22, 2025 (removal from Dissemination Board)
- **Reference SEBI Circular**: October 10, 2016 (SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative action affecting one company that was already on the Dissemination Board (limited trading platform). The company remains accessible through other stock exchanges with nationwide trading terminals.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members will no longer see this security on NSE's Dissemination Board, but trading access continues through other exchanges where the company is listed.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible. Investors can continue trading through other stock exchange platforms with nationwide coverage.
