## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has provisionally empanelled M/s. AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to NSE trading members. The empanelment is effective immediately as of November 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- M/s. AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with NSE
- The provider offers White Box type algorithmic trading solutions
- Registered office located at A/2102, Kanchangiri, Ashok Nagar, Kandivali East, Mumbai - 400101
- Contact phone number: +917703935116
- Circular is applicable with immediate effect from November 26, 2025
- Updated list of all empanelled Algo Providers available on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/static/trade/empanelled-algo-providers-exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative circular announcing the addition of one new algo provider to NSE's empanelled list.

## Compliance Requirements

No mandatory compliance requirements. Trading members interested in using algorithmic trading solutions may choose to engage with the newly empanelled provider. The empanelment is on a provisional basis.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 26, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular provides trading members with an additional option for algorithmic trading solutions through a provisionally empanelled White Box algo provider. Trading members can voluntarily choose to engage with AlgoBulls Technologies for their algorithmic trading needs. The provisional status suggests the provider will undergo further evaluation. No impact on trading rules, compliance obligations, or market structure.
