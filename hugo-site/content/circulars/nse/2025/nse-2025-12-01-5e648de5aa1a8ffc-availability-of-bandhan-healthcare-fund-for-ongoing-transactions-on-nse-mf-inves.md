## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Bandhan Healthcare Fund of Bandhan Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Bandhan Healthcare Fund will be available for ongoing transactions from December 02, 2025
- All facilities including subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP will be enabled
- Six plan variants are available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment department

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of Bandhan Healthcare Fund will be available:

1. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Regular Plan Growth** (ISIN: INF194KB1KC2)
2. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF194KB1KD0)
3. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF194KB1KE8)
4. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Direct Plan Growth** (ISIN: INF194KB1KF5)
5. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Direct Plan IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF194KB1KG3)
6. **Bandhan Healthcare Fund - Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF194KB1KH1)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the availability of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 - Bandhan Healthcare Fund reopened for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to a single mutual fund scheme becoming available for transactions. The impact is limited to:

- Investors seeking exposure to healthcare sector through mutual funds
- NSE MF Invest Platform users interested in Bandhan Mutual Fund products
- Distributors and advisors recommending healthcare-focused equity funds

The reopening provides investors access to systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) in addition to regular subscriptions and redemptions.
