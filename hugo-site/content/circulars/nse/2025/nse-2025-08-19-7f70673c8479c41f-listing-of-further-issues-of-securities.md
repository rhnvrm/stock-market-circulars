## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of further equity shares for 10 companies effective August 20, 2025. These shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment with designated security codes and specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 10 companies are listing additional equity shares under ESOP schemes
- Effective date: August 20, 2025
- All shares are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions apply
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities

## Securities Details

**Axis Bank Limited (AXISBANK)**
- 121,145 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Date of Allotment: August 13, 2025
- ISIN: INE238A01034

**CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited (CGPOWER)**
- 52,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE067A01029

**Chalet Hotels Limited (CHALET)**
- 90,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Date of Allotment: July 31, 2025
- ISIN: INE427F01016

**D.B.Corp Limited (DBCORP)**
- 16,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Date of Allotment: August 4, 2025
- ISIN: INE950I01011

**Gulf Oil Lubricants India Limited (GULFOILLUB)**
- 15,173 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Date of Allotment: July 31, 2025
- ISIN: INE635Q01029

**Infosys Limited (INFY)**
- 97,651 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each
- Date of Allotment: August 5, 2025
- ISIN: INE009A01021

## Regulatory Framework

- Pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings with relatively small quantities. The shares being pari passu means they carry the same rights as existing shares, ensuring no dilution of shareholder rights.
