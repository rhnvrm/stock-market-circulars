## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Gujarat Kidney And Super Speciality Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol GKSL with ISIN INE0V0W01025. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Gujarat Kidney And Super Speciality Limited
- Trading Symbol: GKSL
- ISIN: INE0V0W01025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/72027, dated December 29, 2025
- Circular Number: 2525/2025
- Department: LISTING

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond awareness of the forthcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 29, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing adds one new equity security to the NSE platform. Market participants should note the company symbol and ISIN for trading purposes once the listing date is announced. The circular is informational in nature and affects primarily those interested in trading the new security.
