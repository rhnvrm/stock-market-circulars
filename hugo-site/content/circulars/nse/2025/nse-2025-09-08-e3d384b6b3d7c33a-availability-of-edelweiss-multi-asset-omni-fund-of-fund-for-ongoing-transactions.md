## Summary

Edelweiss Multi Asset Omni Fund of Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective September 09, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions after temporary closure
- Six different plan options available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW variants)
- All plans categorized under Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Effective date: September 09, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - this is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE MF Invest Platform are informed of the fund's availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **September 09, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for mutual fund investors seeking multi-asset allocation options. The reopening provides investment opportunities in a diversified fund of fund structure with flexible transaction options through systematic plans. Limited broader market impact as this affects only specific fund investors on the NSE MF platform.
