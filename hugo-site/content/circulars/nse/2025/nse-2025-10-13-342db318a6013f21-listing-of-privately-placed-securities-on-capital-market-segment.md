## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of two privately placed debt securities on the Capital Market segment effective October 14, 2025. The securities are debentures issued by L&T Finance Limited (7.2190% maturing November 2028) and Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited (7.6825% maturing October 2027). Both securities will trade with market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- L&T Finance Limited: LTFL 7.2190% 2028 Sr I (Symbol: 72190LTFL2, ISIN: INE498L07194)
- Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited: AIFL 7.6825% 2027 Sr G1 (Symbol: 76825AIFL2, ISIN: INE0AD507192)
- Both securities designated to Series N0 with market lot size of 1
- Trading to commence October 14, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading system purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must use designated security codes for trading these instruments
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for both securities)
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A applicable to debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 13, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 14, 2025 (trading commencement)
- **L&T Finance Maturity:** November 10, 2028
- **Aseem Infrastructure Maturity:** October 8, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine administrative notification for privately placed debt securities listing. The impact is limited to investors and entities dealing specifically with these debt instruments.

**Operational Impact:** Neutral. Market participants trading in debt securities need to note the new symbols and ISINs for trading purposes.

**Investor Impact:** Low. Provides additional investment options in the debt segment with coupon rates of 7.22% and 7.68% respectively, offering moderate yield opportunities for debt investors.
