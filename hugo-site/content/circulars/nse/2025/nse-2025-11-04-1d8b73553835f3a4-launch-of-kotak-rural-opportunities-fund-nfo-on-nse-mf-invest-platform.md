## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription period runs from November 06, 2025 to November 20, 2025. Six scheme options are available covering both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants. Allotment is scheduled for November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants launched: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Unique ISIN codes assigned to each scheme variant
- NFO subscription period: November 06, 2025 to November 20, 2025
- Allotment date: November 27, 2025
- Payment deadline: November 26, 2025 by 12:00 PM

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1XZ1
2. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1YB0
3. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1YA2

### Direct Plan Options
1. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1XW8
2. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1XY4
3. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1XX6

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 26, 2025 by 12:00 PM
- Funds can be received through any payment mode
- Members responsible for facilitating investor transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date:** November 06, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date:** November 20, 2025
- **Payment Deadline:** November 26, 2025 (12:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date:** November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It expands investment options for retail and institutional investors interested in rural-themed equity opportunities. The fund aims to capture growth potential in India's rural economy. Impact is limited to Kotak Mutual Fund investors and NSE MF Invest Platform participants. No effect on secondary market trading or existing securities.
