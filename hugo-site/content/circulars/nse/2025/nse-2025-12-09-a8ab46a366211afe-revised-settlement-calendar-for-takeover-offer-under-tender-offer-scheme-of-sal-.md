## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a revised settlement calendar for the takeover offer under the Tender Offer scheme of SAL Steel Limited. This circular updates the previous schedule communicated on November 26, 2025, with new obligation download and settlement dates while maintaining the same trading period.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: X
- Settlement Number: 2025127
- Trading Period: November 28, 2025 to December 11, 2025 (unchanged)
- Revised Obligation Download Date: December 12, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: December 15, 2025
- All other details from the original circular NCL/CMPT/71485 dated November 26, 2025 remain applicable
- Settlement schedule may be further revised in case of delays in acceptance details from RTA or funds pay-in by buying trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a schedule revision for an existing tender offer process.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must note the revised settlement calendar
- Trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid further schedule revisions
- Participants should monitor for any additional schedule changes due to RTA or payment delays

## Important Dates

- **Trade Start Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Trade End Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Obligation Download Date:** December 12, 2025 (revised)
- **Settlement Date:** December 15, 2025 (revised)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects participants in the SAL Steel Limited takeover offer

**Operational Impact:** Trading members and custodians participating in this tender offer must adjust their settlement operations to align with the revised obligation download and settlement dates. The three-day extension (from the original schedule) provides additional time for processing but requires updated internal scheduling and reconciliation processes.
