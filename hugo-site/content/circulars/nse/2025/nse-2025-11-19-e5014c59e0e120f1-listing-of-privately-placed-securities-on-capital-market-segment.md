## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on the capital market segment. The securities will be available for trading with effect from November 20, 2025. The circular provides details of the security code, market lot size, and other trading parameters.

## Key Points

- ABHFL 7.2841% 2030 Sr H1 debentures admitted to trading on NSE capital market segment
- Trading symbol: 72841ABH30, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Coupon rate: 7.28% per annum
- ISIN Code: INE831R07623
- Maturity date: November 18, 2030
- Circular issued pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable. This is an informational circular for market participants regarding newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 20, 2025 (trading commencement)
- **Maturity Date:** November 18, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities for a single issuer. The listing provides additional liquidity options for investors holding these debentures.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members need to note the new security code (72841ABH30) and market lot size (1 unit) for order placement and execution.

**Investor Impact:** Low - Provides secondary market trading facility for holders of ABHFL's privately placed debentures with a 7.28% coupon rate maturing in 2030.
