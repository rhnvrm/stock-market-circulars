## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has informed its members about the name change of Iris Business Services Limited. The company will be renamed to IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited with effect from December 16, 2025. The trading symbol 'IRIS' will remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company: Iris Business Services Limited
- New Name: IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited
- Trading Symbol: IRIS (unchanged)
- Effective Date: December 16, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71727, Circular No. 2399/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are informed to note the name change for their records and systems. No specific compliance action required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative change with minimal market impact. The trading symbol remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading operations. Investors and market participants should update their records to reflect the new company name. No impact on stock holdings, trading mechanisms, or shareholder rights.
