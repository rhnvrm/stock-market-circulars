## Summary

NSE has suspended trading in equity shares of ARSS Infrastructure Projects Limited (Symbol: ARSSINFRA) effective September 04, 2025. The suspension is pursuant to an NCLT Order dated August 29, 2025, under which the company is suspended immediately under the approved Resolution Plan.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from September 04, 2025
- Company symbol: ARSSINFRA
- Suspension is immediate and pursuant to NCLT Order dated August 29, 2025
- Action taken under approved Resolution Plan
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must cease trading in ARSSINFRA shares from September 04, 2025
- No new positions can be created in the suspended security
- Existing positions will be subject to NCLT Resolution Plan proceedings

## Important Dates

- **August 29, 2025**: NCLT Order date
- **September 03, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **September 04, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

High impact on existing shareholders and investors in ARSS Infrastructure Projects Limited. Complete halt of trading activities means no liquidity for current shareholders. The NCLT Resolution Plan will determine the future course of action for the company and its securities. This suspension protects market integrity during insolvency proceedings.
