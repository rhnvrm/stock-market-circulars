## Summary

NSE has announced that DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 15, 2025. The fund will allow subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund to reopen on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity
- Facility includes ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Scheme Details

The following six ISIN variants will be available:

1. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Regular Growth** (INF740KA1WY1)
2. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Regular IDCW** (INF740KA1WZ8)
3. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Regular IDCW Reinvestment** (INF740KA1XA9)
4. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Direct Growth** (INF740KA1XB7)
5. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Direct IDCW** (INF740KA1XC5)
6. **DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Direct IDCW Reinvestment** (INF740KA1XD3)

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members should update their systems to enable transactions for the listed scheme variants
- Members must ensure availability of all six scheme options on the NSE MF Invest Platform
- Support queries can be directed to support@nseinvest.com or toll-free number 1800 2100 940

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with minimal market impact. It affects only investors interested in DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund products on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investors access to midcap equity exposure through an index fund structure with multiple investment and withdrawal options. No broader market or regulatory implications.
