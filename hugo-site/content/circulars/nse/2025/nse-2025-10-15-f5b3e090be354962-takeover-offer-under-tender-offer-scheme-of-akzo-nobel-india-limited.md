## Summary

JSW Paints Limited (Acquirer) is conducting a takeover offer for Akzo Nobel India Limited through NSE's web-based bidding platform. The offer is for up to 1,18,40,482 equity shares (26% of voting share capital) at a fixed price of Rs. 3,231.77 per share. The bidding period runs from October 23, 2025 to November 6, 2025. The offer is conducted under the Tender Offer Scheme in accordance with SEBI (SAST) Regulations, with provisions for proportionate reduction to ensure the acquirer's post-offer shareholding does not exceed 75% of voting share capital.

## Regulatory Changes

This offer follows the Tender Offer Scheme outlined in NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022. The offer includes a proportionate reduction mechanism as per the first proviso to Regulation 7(4) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations to ensure post-acquisition shareholding does not exceed 75% of voting share capital.

**Proportionate Reduction Mechanism**: If 1,18,40,482 or more equity shares are tendered, the open offer size will be reduced to 92,73,902 equity shares (~20.36% of voting capital) and shares from sellers will be reduced to 2,48,81,333 equity shares (~54.64% of voting capital).
