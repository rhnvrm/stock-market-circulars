## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Axis Multi-Asset Active Fund of Funds (FOF) New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from November 21, 2025, to December 05, 2025, until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- NFO subscription period: November 21, 2025 to December 05, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- All schemes categorized under Debt category
- Allotment date: December 11, 2025
- Payment deadline: December 11, 2025 (12:30 PM)
- Funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account before payment deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account
- Payment deadline: December 11, 2025 at 12:30 PM
- Members responsible for facilitating transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: November 21, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: December 05, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Payment Deadline**: December 11, 2025 (12:30 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific mutual fund NFO launch. The impact is limited to:

- Investors interested in multi-asset fund of funds products
- NSE MF Invest Platform members who facilitate mutual fund transactions
- Axis Mutual Fund distribution network

The offering provides investors with diversified multi-asset allocation options through both regular and direct plans with various dividend and growth options. No impact on equity markets or trading operations.
