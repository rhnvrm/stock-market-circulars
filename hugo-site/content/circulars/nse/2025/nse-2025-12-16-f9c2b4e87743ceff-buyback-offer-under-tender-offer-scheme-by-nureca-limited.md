## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has enabled its web-based bidding platform for Nureca Limited's buyback offer under the Tender Offer scheme. The buyback will be conducted from December 18-24, 2025, for up to 5,80,000 equity shares at a fixed price of Rs. 330 per share. All Capital Market segment trading members can participate using the eIPO platform at https://eipo.nseindia.com.

## Key Points

- Buyback of up to 5,80,000 equity shares of Nureca Limited
- Fixed offer price: Rs. 330 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Lot size: 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE0DSF01015
- Security symbol: NURECA (Series: BB)
- Merchant Banker: Mefcom Capital Markets Limited
- Registrar: Alankit Assignments Limited
- Conducted under Tender Offer scheme per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes an existing Tender Offer framework for a specific company buyback.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use existing web-based IPO bidding platform credentials or create new access via admin terminal
- Members without system access must log in using Admin terminal with user ID format: Adminxxxxx (where xxxxx is Member Code)
- Members must create branches and users to participate in bidding
- Custodial confirmations required during bidding hours and extended until 4:00 PM on issue closure date

## Important Dates

- **Issue Period**: December 18-24, 2025 (7 days)
- **Bidding Time**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- **Custodial Confirmation Window**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (extended on issue closure date)
- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025

## Eligible Investor Categories

1. Foreign Institutional Investors (FII)
2. Mutual Funds (MF)
3. Insurance Companies (IC)
4. Banks & Financial Institutions (FI)
5. Other QIBs (OTH)
6. Bodies Corporate (CO)
7. Individuals (IND)
8. Other Non-Institutional Investors (NOH)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only Nureca Limited shareholders. The buyback size of 5,80,000 shares is relatively small.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating trading members who need to ensure system access and user credentials are configured before the bidding window opens on December 18, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for Nureca shareholders who wish to tender their shares. The fixed price of Rs. 330 provides clarity for participation decisions.
