## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities (commercial papers) from 9 companies to trading on the Debt Market Segment effective September 01, 2025. The circular lists both fresh issues and re-issues of commercial papers with their respective security codes, ISINs, and maturity dates.

## Key Points

- 5 fresh commercial paper issues admitted to trading
- 5 re-issues of commercial papers admitted to trading
- All securities effective for trading from September 01, 2025
- Face value of â‚¹5,00,000 for all listed securities
- Maturity periods range from 18 days to 90 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Securities are now available for trading on the debt market segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 01, 2025 - All listed securities admitted to trading
- **Allotment Date**: August 29, 2025 - All securities were allotted on this date
- **Maturity Dates**: Range from September 18, 2025 to November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents routine listing activity. Provides additional investment options in short-term debt instruments for institutional investors. No operational changes required for trading members.
