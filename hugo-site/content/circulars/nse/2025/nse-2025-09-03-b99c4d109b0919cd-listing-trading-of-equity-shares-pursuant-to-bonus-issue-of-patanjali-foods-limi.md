## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to bonus issue of Patanjali Foods Limited. The bonus shares will be available for trading on NSE Capital Market segment from September 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Patanjali Foods Limited (Symbol: PATANJALI)
- ISIN: INE619A01035
- Number of bonus securities: 72,52,18,830 shares
- Distinctive number range: 362609416 to 1087828245
- Trading effective from September 15, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Admission of additional equity shares under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- All members must note the new security codes and trading arrangements

## Important Dates

- Record Date: September 11, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: September 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 15, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: September 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the total number of tradeable shares of Patanjali Foods Limited, potentially affecting liquidity and market capitalization calculations. Standard bonus issue with no operational disruptions expected.
