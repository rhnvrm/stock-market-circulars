## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Mirae Asset BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription period is from December 02, 2025 to December 10, 2025 (till 11:59 PM), with allotment scheduled for December 15, 2025. Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth options will be available under the same ISIN.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: December 02-10, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two scheme options available: Direct Growth and Regular Growth
- Both schemes share the same ISIN: INF769K01PY8
- Category: Equity ETF
- Allotment date: December 15, 2025
- Fund house: Mirae Asset Mutual Fund
- Underlying index: BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 13, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Standard NFO subscription and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: December 02, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: December 10, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Fund Settlement Deadline**: December 13, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a new ETF launch that provides investors with another investment option focused on dividend-paying stocks from the BSE 500 index. No immediate market impact expected.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard NFO procedures apply. Members of NSE MF Invest Platform need to facilitate subscriptions during the NFO period and ensure timely fund settlement.

**Investor Impact**: Provides retail and institutional investors access to a dividend-focused equity ETF tracking BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 index through both direct and regular plans.
