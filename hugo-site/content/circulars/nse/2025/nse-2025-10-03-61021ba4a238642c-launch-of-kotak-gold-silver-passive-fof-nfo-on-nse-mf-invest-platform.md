## Summary

Kotak Mutual Fund is launching Kotak Gold Silver Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from October 06, 2025 to October 20, 2025, with allotment scheduled for October 28, 2025. Two plan options are available: Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth, both categorized under Equity.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: October 06, 2025 to October 20, 2025
- Allotment Date: October 28, 2025
- Two schemes available: Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XU2) and Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XT4)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity
- Fund Type: Gold Silver Passive Fund of Funds
- Platform: NSE MF Invest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before October 27, 2025 till 11:59 PM
- Standard NFO transaction procedures apply through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: October 06, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Funds Receipt Deadline**: October 27, 2025 (11:59 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine NFO launch announcement affecting only participants of the NSE MF Invest Platform who wish to offer this new mutual fund scheme to their clients. The circular provides operational information for platform members to facilitate transactions during the NFO period. No impact on equity markets or trading operations.
