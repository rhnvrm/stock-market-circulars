## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Kotak Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 18, 2025. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- Six plan options available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- Unique ISIN codes assigned for each plan variant
- Regular Plan Growth ISIN: INF174KA1YF1
- Direct Plan Growth ISIN: INF174KA1YC8
- Support available via toll-free number 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the availability of the mutual fund schemes for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 18, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions and redemptions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular is specific to investors and distributors interested in the Kotak Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index Fund. The reopening provides access to systematic investment and withdrawal facilities for this particular scheme, benefiting existing and prospective investors seeking exposure to momentum-based equity strategies tracking the Nifty 500 index.
