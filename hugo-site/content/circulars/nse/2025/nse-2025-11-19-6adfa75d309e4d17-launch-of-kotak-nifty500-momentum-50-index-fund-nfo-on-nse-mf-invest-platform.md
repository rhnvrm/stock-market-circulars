## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Kotak Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from November 20, 2025 to December 04, 2025. Six plan variants are being offered including Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options. The allotment date is scheduled for December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: November 20, 2025 to December 04, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes classified under Equity category
- Allotment date: December 11, 2025
- Clear funds must be received by December 10, 2025 till 12:00 PM
- ISINs allocated: INF174KA1YF1, INF174KA1YH7, INF174KA1YG9, INF174KA1YC8, INF174KA1YE4, INF174KA1YD6

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 10, 2025 till 12:00 PM
- Funds can be received through any payment mode
- Members should facilitate investor transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date:** November 20, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Funds Payment Deadline:** December 10, 2025 (12:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new equity index fund option tracking the Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index. The launch expands product offerings on the NSE MF Invest Platform but does not affect existing market operations or trading activities. Impact is limited to investors interested in momentum-based index investing strategies.
