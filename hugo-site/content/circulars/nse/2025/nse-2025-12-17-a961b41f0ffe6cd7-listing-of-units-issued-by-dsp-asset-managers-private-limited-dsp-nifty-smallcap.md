## Summary

NSE has admitted two new ETF units issued by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 18, 2025. The DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF (symbol: SMALLADD) and DSP Nifty Midcap 150 ETF (symbol: MIDCAPADD) will be available for trading with designated security codes and lot sizes as specified.

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF: 3,487,401 units allotted on December 12, 2025
- DSP Nifty Midcap 150 ETF: 2,801,276 units allotted on December 12, 2025
- Both ETFs have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10/- per unit
- Trading lot size: 1 unit for both ETFs
- Nine market makers designated for both ETFs
- ISIN for Smallcap 250 ETF: INF740KA1WX3
- ISIN for Midcap 150 ETF: INF740KA1WW5

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the designated symbols (SMALLADD and MIDCAPADD) for identification
- Securities to be traded in specified lot sizes (1 unit)
- Trading permitted in EQ series only
- Issue prices: Rs. 16.40/- for Smallcap 250 ETF and Rs. 22.15/- for Midcap 150 ETF

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of new ETF units that provides investors with additional investment options in smallcap and midcap segments through passive index-tracking instruments. The presence of nine designated market makers ensures adequate liquidity. No changes to existing trading rules or member obligations.
