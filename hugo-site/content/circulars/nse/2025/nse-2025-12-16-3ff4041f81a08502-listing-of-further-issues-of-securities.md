## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares issued by 27 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) schemes. The securities will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from December 17, 2025. All shares are issued on a pari passu basis with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 27 companies have issued further equity shares under ESOP schemes
- Effective date for trading: December 17, 2025
- All shares carry market lot of 1
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable on any of the issues
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notification for listing of further issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes for trading purposes
- Securities to be traded in market lot sizes as specified (market lot of 1 for all listed securities)
- Trading systems must be updated to reflect the new securities from December 17, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** December 17, 2025
- **Allotment Dates:** Various dates in November-December 2025 (company-specific)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine ESOP-based further issues representing small dilutions to existing share capital of the respective companies.

**Trading Impact:** Negligible impact on trading volumes or prices as ESOP shares are typically small in number relative to total outstanding shares.

**Investor Impact:** No specific impact on existing shareholders. The shares are issued at par with existing equity shares with full voting and dividend rights.

**Operational Impact:** Exchange members need to update their systems to reflect the additional shares in circulation for these 27 companies.
