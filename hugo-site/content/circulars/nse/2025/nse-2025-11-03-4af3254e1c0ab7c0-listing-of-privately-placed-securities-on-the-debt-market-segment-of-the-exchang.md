## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective November 03, 2025. The circular lists 8 commercial papers and 2 other debt securities issued by various companies, providing details of security codes, ISINs, face values, issue prices, allotment dates, and redemption dates.

## Key Points

- Total 10 debt instruments listed: 8 commercial papers and 2 debentures
- Commercial papers issued by Blue Star Limited (INE472A14OR1), Godrej Industries Limited (INE233A144Z4), Godrej Agrovet Limited (INE850D14VB1), JM Financial Services Limited (2 series: INE012I14SD9, INE012I14SC1), Welspun Living Limited (INE192B14794), HDB Financial Services Ltd (INE756I14FN7), and Kotak Securities Limited (INE028E14TI1)
- Debt securities issued by Toyota Financial Services India Limited (INE692Q07597) with 7.3% coupon rate maturing January 15, 2029, and Profectus Capital Private Limited with 9.75% coupon rate maturing 2028
- All commercial papers have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 with varying issue prices and tenures
- Allotment dates: October 31, 2025 (6 instruments) and November 03, 2025 (1 instrument)
- Shortest tenure: HDB Financial Services CP maturing November 07, 2025 (7 days)
- Longest tenure: JM Financial Services CP maturing August 28, 2026 (10 months)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular notifying the admission of securities to trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: November 03, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: October 31, 2025 and November 03, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from November 07, 2025 to January 15, 2029

### Commercial Paper Redemption Schedule:
- November 07, 2025: HDB Financial Services (Sr 308)
- November 28, 2025: Kotak Securities (Sr 158)
- January 29, 2026: Welspun Living
- January 30, 2026: Blue Star (Sr 25), Godrej Industries (Sr 108), Godrej Agrovet (Sr 43)
- February 25, 2026: JM Financial Services (Sr 26)
- August 28, 2026: JM Financial Services (Sr 27)

### Debenture Redemption Schedule:
- 2028: Profectus Capital (9.75%)
- January 15, 2029: Toyota Financial Services (7.3%)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing of privately placed debt securities. The securities are already issued and allotted; this circular simply notifies their admission to exchange trading.

**Operational Impact**: Provides liquidity options for holders of these privately placed instruments through secondary market trading on NSE's Debt Market Segment.

**Investor Impact**: Institutional investors holding these securities can now trade them on the exchange platform with designated security codes and ISINs as specified in the annexure.
