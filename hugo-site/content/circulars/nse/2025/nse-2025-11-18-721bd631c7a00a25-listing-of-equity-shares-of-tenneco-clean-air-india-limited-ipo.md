## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of Tenneco Clean Air India Limited's equity shares following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol TENNIND with ISIN INE19RI01016. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Tenneco Clean Air India Limited
- Trading Symbol: TENNIND
- ISIN: INE19RI01016
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71337, Circular No. 2235/2025
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for exchange members. This is an informational circular about an upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate impact on market operations. It serves as an advance notification to exchange members about a new stock listing. Trading members should prepare their systems for the new security once the listing date is announced. The listing will add one more equity security to the NSE trading platform.
