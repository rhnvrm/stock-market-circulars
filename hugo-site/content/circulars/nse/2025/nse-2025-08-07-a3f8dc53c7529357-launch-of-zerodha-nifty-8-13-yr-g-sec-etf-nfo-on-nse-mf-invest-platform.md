## Summary

Zerodha Mutual Fund is launching the Zerodha Nifty 8-13 Yr G-Sec ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on NSE MF Invest Platform. The scheme will be available for transactions from August 8, 2025, to August 12, 2025, with allotment scheduled for August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- New ETF launch: Zerodha Nifty 8-13 Yr G-Sec ETF
- ISIN: INF0R8F01133
- Category: Debt scheme
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Direct Growth option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds can be received through any payment mode

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: August 8, 2025
- NFO End Date: August 12, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine ETF launch. Provides investors with additional debt investment option focused on 8-13 year government securities segment.
