## Summary

NSE will make the Nifty50 USD index available to Futures & Options members through NEAT+ terminals under the multiple index inquiry screen. This follows the Capital Market circular NSE/CMTR/70716 dated October 10, 2025 regarding index dissemination in the Capital Market Segment. Members using Non-NEAT Front end (NNF) can configure their systems to receive the index broadcast.

## Key Points

- Nifty50 USD index will be broadcast to F&O members in NEAT+ terminals
- Available under multiple index inquiry screen with broadcast name "Nifty50 USD"
- NNF users can configure/develop systems to receive the index broadcast
- Nifty 50 is a well-diversified 50-company index computed using free float market capitalization method
- Index has 50 constituents with base date November 03, 1995 and base value 1000
- Real-time calculation frequency with semi-annual rebalancing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement to make additional index data available to F&O segment members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members using Non-NEAT Front end (NNF) may need to configure or develop their systems to receive the Nifty50 USD index broadcast
- No mandatory compliance actions required

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is an informational update providing additional index data to F&O members.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Members will have access to Nifty50 USD index data through their trading terminals. NNF users may optionally configure their systems to receive the broadcast.
