## Summary

National Stock Exchange has admitted 37,96,800 equity shares of Viviana Power Tech Limited (Symbol: VIVIANA) for trading on the Capital Market segment pursuant to a bonus issue. The securities will be available for trading from November 21, 2025, under ISIN INE0MEG01014.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares issued: 37,96,800
- Trading symbol: VIVIANA
- ISIN: INE0MEG01014
- Distinctive number range: 6328001 to 10124800
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading on the system
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification of securities admission to trading following a corporate action (bonus issue).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading the securities on the NSE system
- Trading participants should update their systems to reflect the increased share count

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The bonus issue will increase the total outstanding shares of Viviana Power Tech Limited, likely resulting in proportional price adjustment. Existing shareholders will see their holdings increase without dilution of ownership percentage.

**Liquidity Impact**: The increased share count may improve liquidity and make the stock more accessible to retail investors due to lower per-share price post-adjustment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to handle the new share count and distinctive number ranges. The ISIN remains unchanged, simplifying the transition.
