## Summary

NSE has removed 12 exclusively listed companies from its Dissemination Board (DB) effective August 28, 2024, following SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. The removal is based on the companies' updated status on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) website.

## Key Points

- 12 companies removed from NSE Dissemination Board
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI circular from October 2016
- Companies had various inactive statuses on MCA website
- Removal effective from August 28, 2024

## Companies Removed

**Strike Off Status (1 company):**
- Accord Cotsyn Limited

**Dissolved (Liquidation) Status (10 companies):**
- Invogue Furnishers & Builders Limited
- Karnataka Chem Ind Corp Limited
- Mercury Phytochem Limited
- Narain Jewels International Limited
- Tulip Finance Limited
- Anjani Solvents India Limited
- Monarch Laboratories Limited
- Rajratan Synthetices Limited
- Rishi Oil & Fats Limited
- Vishal Footwear Limited

**Dissolved under Section 54 Status (1 company):**
- Rave Globls Limited

**Dormant under Section 455 Status:**
- None explicitly listed with this status

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action follows existing SEBI guidelines for handling companies with changed corporate status.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are advised to note that these companies will no longer be available on NSE's Dissemination Board for information or trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 28, 2024
- **Circular Date:** August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these companies were already inactive with changed corporate status. The removal is an administrative cleanup measure to maintain accuracy of the Dissemination Board database.
