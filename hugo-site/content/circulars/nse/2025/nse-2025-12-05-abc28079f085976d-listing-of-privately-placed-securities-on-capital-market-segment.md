## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of Hero FinCorp Limited's privately placed debentures on the Capital Market segment effective December 08, 2025. The security is a 7.80% coupon rate debenture maturing on June 02, 2027, designated as Series 75, with trading symbol 780HFL27 and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Hero FinCorp Limited
- **Trading Symbol**: 780HFL27
- **Series**: N0
- **Issue Description**: HFL 7.80% 2027 Sr 75
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.8% per annum
- **Maturity Date**: June 02, 2027
- **ISIN Code**: INE957N07856
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 unit
- Securities admitted pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code 780HFL27 for identification on the trading system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members must comply with NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A regarding privately placed securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: June 02, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of a single privately placed debt security.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems with the new security code and parameters, which is a standard operational process.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional liquidity option for holders of Hero FinCorp's privately placed debentures through exchange trading facility.
