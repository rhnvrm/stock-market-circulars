## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has provisionally empanelled M/s. AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider for offering algorithmic trading solutions to trading members. The vendor is authorized to provide White Box algorithmic trading solutions. This circular is effective immediately and the updated list of empanelled Algo Providers is available on the NSE website.

## Key Points

- M/s. AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with NSE
- The vendor is authorized to provide White Box type algorithmic trading solutions
- Contact office located at A/2102, Kanchangiri, Ashok Nagar, Kandivali East, Mumbai - 400101
- Contact phone number: +917703935116
- Circular applicable with immediate effect
- Complete list of empanelled Algo Providers available at NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update adding one vendor to the existing empanelled Algo Provider list maintained by NSE's Investigation Department.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. Trading members seeking to use algorithmic trading solutions may engage with the newly empanelled provider. Trading members should refer to the NSE website for the complete updated list of empanelled Algo Providers at: https://www.nseindia.com/static/trade/empanelled-algo-providers-exchange

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Immediate effect from date of circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification that expands the options available to trading members for algorithmic trading solutions. The provisional empanelment adds one vendor (AlgoBulls Technologies) offering White Box algorithmic trading technology. Trading members seeking to implement or enhance algorithmic trading capabilities now have an additional empanelled provider to consider. No changes to trading rules, compliance requirements, or market operations.
