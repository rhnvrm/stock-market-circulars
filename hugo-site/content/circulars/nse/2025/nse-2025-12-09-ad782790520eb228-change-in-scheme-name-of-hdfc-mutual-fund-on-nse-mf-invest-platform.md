## Summary

NSE has informed members about a scheme name change for HDFC Mutual Fund. The scheme 'HDFC Multi-Asset Fund' will be renamed to 'HDFC Multi-Asset Allocation Fund' effective December 10, 2025 on the NSE MF Invest platform. This is an administrative update communicated through a notice cum addendum issued by HDFC Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- HDFC Multi-Asset Fund will be renamed to HDFC Multi-Asset Allocation Fund
- Change applies to NSE MF Invest platform
- Name change is effective from December 10, 2025
- Notice cum addendum issued by HDFC Mutual Fund attached with circular
- All NSE members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheme name modification by the mutual fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members should update their systems to reflect the new scheme name
- Members should ensure the revised name is used for all transactions and communications from December 10, 2025
- Reference the notice cum addendum issued by HDFC Mutual Fund for complete details

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 9, 2025 (Note: Circular shows November 09, 2025 but context indicates December 9, 2025)
- **Effective Date**: December 10, 2025 - New scheme name becomes applicable

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Negligible. This is purely an administrative name change with no impact on the fund's investment strategy, portfolio composition, or investor holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Members need to update their systems and records to reflect the new scheme name. No changes to fund operations, NAV calculations, or transaction processing.

**Investor Impact**: None. Existing investors retain their holdings with no action required. The fund's fundamental attributes remain unchanged except for the name.
