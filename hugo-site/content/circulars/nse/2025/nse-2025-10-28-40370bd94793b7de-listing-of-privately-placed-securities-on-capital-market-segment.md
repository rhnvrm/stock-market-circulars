## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited on the capital market segment. The security (TCHFL 7.35% 2035 Sr D) with symbol 735TCHFL35 will be available for trading from October 29, 2025, with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Symbol**: 735TCHFL35
- **Series**: N0
- **Market Lot Size**: 1
- **Issue Description**: TCHFL 7.35% 2035 Sr D
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.35%
- **Maturity Date**: October 26, 2035
- **ISIN Code**: INE033L07IQ6
- Trading identification will be by designated security codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (735TCHFL35) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1
- All trading activities must comply with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 29, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: October 26, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine listing notification for a single privately placed debt security. The listing provides an additional investment avenue for debt market participants interested in housing finance sector exposure. The security offers a 7.35% coupon rate with a 10-year maturity period. Impact is limited to investors specifically interested in Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited debt instruments.
