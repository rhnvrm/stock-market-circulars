## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Latin Manharlal Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration: INZ000208631) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment, including Interest Rate Futures (IRF). The surrender was approved on December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Member Name: Latin Manharlal Securities Pvt. Ltd.
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000208631
- Surrendered Segment: Currency Derivatives including IRF
- Approval Date: December 10, 2025
- All NSE members are requested to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action concerning voluntary surrender of segment membership by a single member.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this membership surrender. No specific action items for other members.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Surrender approval date
- **December 16, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving one member's voluntary exit from the Currency Derivatives segment. Does not affect trading operations or other market participants' activities.
