## Summary

JioBlackRock Mutual Fund is launching 5 new index funds as New Fund Offerings (NFOs) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO period runs from August 5, 2025, to August 12, 2025, until 5:00 PM, with allotment scheduled for August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5 new JioBlackRock index funds being launched as NFOs
- All funds are Direct Growth variants
- Investment period: August 5-12, 2025 (until 5:00 PM)
- Allotment date: August 18, 2025
- Available through NSE MF Invest Platform
- Clear funds must be received before NFO closing date

## Fund Details

1. **JioBlackRock Nifty 50 Index Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF22M001044)
2. **JioBlackRock Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF22M001051)
3. **JioBlackRock Nifty 8-13 yr G-Sec Index Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF22M001069)
4. **JioBlackRock Nifty Midcap 150 Index Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF22M001077)
5. **JioBlackRock Nifty Next 50 Index Fund - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF22M001085)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds can be received through any payment mode
- All transactions to be conducted through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening**: August 5, 2025
- **NFO Closing**: August 12, 2025 (5:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The launch expands investment options for retail and institutional investors seeking index fund exposure across different market segments including large-cap, mid-cap, small-cap, and government securities. The diverse fund offerings cater to various risk profiles and investment strategies within the equity and debt markets.
