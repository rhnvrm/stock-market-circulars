## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers from 10 issuers to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective December 08, 2025. The circular lists fresh issuances and re-issuances of commercial papers with tenures ranging from 21 days to 180 days, with face values of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit.

## Key Points

- **Fresh Issues**: 9 commercial paper issuances from various companies
- **Re-Issues**: ICICI Securities Limited CP with 26,000 securities
- **Allotment Date**: Primarily December 05, 2025 (one on December 08, 2025)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security across all issuances
- **Total Fresh Issue Volume**: 44,500 securities worth approximately Rs. 22,250 crores
- **Issuers Include**: Aditya Birla Money, Blue Star, Aditya Birla Capital, ICICI Securities, Sundaram Home Finance, Godrej Industries, SIDBI, L&T Finance, and HDFC Securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can trade these securities on the debt market segment using the designated security codes and ISINs provided
- Securities are available for trading from December 08, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 05, 2025 and December 08, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from December 26, 2025 to June 03, 2026
- **Shortest Tenure**: L&T Finance CP - 21 days (December 26, 2025)
- **Longest Tenure**: Sundaram Home Finance CP - 180 days (June 03, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing of debt instruments that expands the debt market inventory. Commercial papers are money market instruments used by corporates for short-term funding needs.

**Operational Impact**: Provides additional debt trading opportunities for institutional investors and debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.

**Issuer Profile**: Mix of NBFCs (Aditya Birla Money, L&T Finance, Sundaram Home Finance), securities firms (ICICI Securities, HDFC Securities), industrial conglomerates (Blue Star, Godrej Industries, Aditya Birla Capital), and development financial institutions (SIDBI).
