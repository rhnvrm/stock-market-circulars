## Summary

NSE has admitted further equity shares of 12 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 16, 2025. All shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will trade pari passu with existing shares. The circular specifies security codes, distinctive number ranges, and lot sizes for each listing.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Number of Companies**: 12 companies listing further equity shares
- **Issuance Type**: All shares allotted under ESOP schemes
- **Trading Status**: Pari passu with existing equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share for all securities
- **Lock-in**: Not Applicable for any of the listed securities

## Regulatory Framework

- **Regulation**: National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
  - Regulation 3.1.1: Admission of securities to dealings
  - Regulation 2.5.5: Lot size specifications
- **Trading Identification**: Securities identified by designated codes only
- **Lot Size**: All securities have market lot of 1 share

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- No special compliance requirements as these are routine ESOP allotments

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Varied between October 31, 2025 and December 8, 2025 (company-specific)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine ESOP share listings with relatively small quantities that will not materially affect the tradeable float or market dynamics of the respective companies.

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard listing process with no changes to trading mechanisms or procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral. Shares trade pari passu with existing equity, no lock-in restrictions, and market lot of 1 ensures easy tradability.
