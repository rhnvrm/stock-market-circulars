## Summary

NSE has admitted further equity shares of 15 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective August 8, 2025. All shares are issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and are pari passu with existing shares.

## Key Points

- 15 companies issuing further equity shares under ESOP
- All shares effective from August 8, 2025
- Market lot size of 1 for all securities
- No lock-in restrictions applicable
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares

## Companies and Share Details

- **AU Small Finance Bank**: 241,350 shares of Rs. 10 each (Aug 2, 2025)
- **Cholamandalam Investment**: 30,840 shares of Rs. 2 each (Jul 22, 2025)
- **Hero MotoCorp**: 52,152 shares of Rs. 2 each (Aug 5, 2025)
- **ICICI Lombard General Insurance**: 198,153 shares of Rs. 10 each (Aug 4, 2025)
- **InterGlobe Aviation**: 23,166 shares of Rs. 10 each (Aug 6, 2025)
- **Infibeam Avenues**: Details in continuation sheet

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Various Allotment Dates**: July 22 - August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP allotments representing small percentages of existing share capital. No trading restrictions or lock-in periods apply.
