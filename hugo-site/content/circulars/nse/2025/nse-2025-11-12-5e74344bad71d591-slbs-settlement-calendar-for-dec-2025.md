## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement calendar for Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for December 2025. This circular notifies all participants and custodians of the settlement schedules for three types of settlements: First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'), Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'), and Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q'). The circular references NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers December 2025 SLBS operations
- Three settlement types outlined: First Leg (L), Reverse Leg (P), and Auction Reverse Leg (Q)
- First Leg settlements run from December 1-31, 2025 with 22 settlement numbers (2025228-2025249)
- Reverse Leg settlements span from December 2025 to December 2026 across 13 series (12/XD through 12/XD)
- Auction Reverse Leg settlements follow same series structure as Reverse Leg
- All First Leg settlements follow T+1 settlement cycle
- New 12/XD series expiring December 1, 2026 will be available from December 3, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational calendar notification in pursuance of existing circular NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Participants and custodians should note the settlement dates for operational planning and execution of SLBS transactions during December 2025.

## Important Dates

**First Leg Settlements (Type L):**
- Settlement period: December 1-31, 2025
- 22 trading days with corresponding settlement dates (T+1)
- Settlement numbers: 2025228 to 2025249
- Final settlement of the month: December 31, 2025 (settles January 1, 2026)

**Reverse Leg Settlements (Type P):**
- 12/XD series: December 2, 2025
- 01/X1 series: January 6, 2026
- 02/X2 series: February 3, 2026
- 03/X3 series: March 3, 2026
- 04/X4 series: April 7, 2026
- 05/X5 series: May 5, 2026
- 06/X6 series: June 2, 2026
- 07/X7 series: July 7, 2026
- 08/X8 series: August 4, 2026
- 09/X9 series: September 1, 2026
- 10/XO series: October 6, 2026
- 11/XN series: November 3, 2026
- 12/XD series: December 1, 2026

**Special Note:** New 12/XD series (expiring December 1, 2026) available from December 3, 2025 after current 12/XD series expires on December 2, 2025.

**Auction Reverse Leg Settlements (Type Q):**
- Same transaction dates as Reverse Leg settlements
- Settlement dates are T+1 from transaction dates

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational calendar that enables proper planning and execution of securities lending and borrowing transactions.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Participants and custodians can plan their SLBS activities for December 2025 based on the published settlement schedules. The calendar ensures transparency and predictability in settlement cycles.

**Participant Impact:** Informational only. No changes to existing processes or requirements. Participants should incorporate these dates into their operational calendars for smooth settlement processing.
