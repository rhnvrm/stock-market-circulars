## Summary

NSE has issued a corrigendum to circular NSE/CML/71747 dated December 10, 2025, correcting the ISIN details for listing and trading of bonus shares of Bharat Rasayan Limited. Following the ISIN change notified in circular NSE/CML/71625, the bonus shares will now be listed and traded under the new ISIN INE838B01021 instead of the previously communicated ISIN.

## Key Points

- This circular corrects information in earlier circular NSE/CML/71747 dated December 10, 2025
- Relates to listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to bonus issue of Bharat Rasayan Limited
- Bonus shares will be listed and traded under new ISIN INE838B01021
- The ISIN change was previously communicated via circular NSE/CML/71625
- Circular reference number: 2411/2025
- Download reference number: NSE/CML/71750

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a correction to previously issued information regarding the applicable ISIN for bonus share trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must note the corrected ISIN (INE838B01021) for listing and trading of bonus shares of Bharat Rasayan Limited. All trading and settlement activities related to the bonus issue should use the new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Original circular date: December 10, 2025 (NSE/CML/71747)
- Corrigendum circular date: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The correction ensures accurate identification of bonus shares in trading and settlement systems. Members and market participants dealing with Bharat Rasayan Limited's bonus issue must update their systems to reflect the correct ISIN (INE838B01021) to avoid processing errors. The impact is limited to this specific corporate action and company.
