## Summary

Angel One Mutual Fund is launching the Angel One Nifty Total Market Momentum Quality 50 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from November 03, 2025 to November 17, 2025, with allotment scheduled for November 21, 2025. The scheme is offered in both Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth options under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform from November 03 to November 17, 2025
- Two plan options: Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF1J2R01148) and Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF1J2R01148)
- Category: Equity
- Allotment date: November 21, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by November 17, 2025 till 11:59 pm
- Payment accepted through any payment mode

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 17, 2025 till 11:59 pm
- Members should facilitate investor transactions during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: November 03, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Payment Deadline**: November 17, 2025 (11:59 pm)
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO launch notification. It provides investment opportunities for investors seeking exposure to Nifty Total Market Momentum Quality 50 index through an ETF structure. The impact is limited to Angel One Mutual Fund distributors, NSE members, and potential investors interested in this specific thematic ETF.
