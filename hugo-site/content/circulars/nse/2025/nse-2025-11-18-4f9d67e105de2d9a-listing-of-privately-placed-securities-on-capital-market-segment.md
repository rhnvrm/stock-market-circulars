## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of SMFG India Home Finance Company Limited on the capital market segment. The security (7.71% 2035 Sr 33) will be available for trading from November 19, 2025, with designated security code 771SIHF35 under series N0.

## Key Points

- Security admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Trading symbol: 771SIHF35 (Series N0)
- Market lot size: 1
- Issue description: SIHF 7.71% 2035 Sr 33
- Coupon rate: 7.71%
- Maturity date: November 16, 2035
- ISIN Code: INE213W07350
- Issuer: SMFG India Home Finance Company Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should identify the security by its designated code 771SIHF35
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members must ensure compliance with NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Circular date:** November 18, 2025
- **Effective date:** November 19, 2025
- **Maturity date:** November 16, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to the routine listing of a single privately placed debt security. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment for market participants. The security is a long-term debt instrument with approximately 10-year tenure, offering a 7.71% coupon rate to investors.
