## Summary

The Groww BSE Power ETF Fund of Funds (FOF) will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 08, 2025. The fund will offer subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- Groww BSE Power ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Includes 6 scheme variants (Direct/Regular with Growth/IDCW options)
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available
- All schemes categorized under Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational announcement for fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members to note the availability of schemes for client transactions
- Ensure proper processing of subscription and redemption requests from August 08, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 08, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine reopening of an existing fund for ongoing transactions. Provides additional investment options for investors interested in power sector exposure through ETF fund of funds structure.
