## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers (CPs) from 10 companies to the debt market segment effective October 17, 2025. The circular lists commercial papers totaling ₹39,500 crores across various issuers with maturity dates ranging from December 2025 to January 2026.

## Key Points

- **Total Securities Listed**: 10 commercial paper issues from different companies
- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025 for all listed securities
- **Trading Start Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Face Value**: ₹5,00,000 per security for all CPs
- **Total Quantity**: 97,500 securities
- **Largest Issue**: Hindalco Industries Limited - 40,000 securities (₹2,000 crores)
- **Issue Price Range**: ₹4,89,863 to ₹4,95,077 per security

## Listed Securities Details

### Short-term Maturities (December 2025)
- **Tata Steel Limited** (TSL CP 16/12/25): 15,000 securities, redemption Dec 16, 2025
- **Angel One Limited** (AOL CP 31/12/25 Sr 52): 6,000 securities, redemption Dec 31, 2025

### Medium-term Maturities (January 2026)
- **Tata Communications Limited** (TCM CP 15/01/26): 10,000 securities
- **Godrej Properties Limited** (GPL CP 15/01/26 Sr 76): 3,000 securities
- **Axis Securities Limited** (ASL CP 15/01/26 Sr 43): 8,000 securities
- **Godrej Industries Limited** (GIL CP 15/01/26 Sr 99): 1,500 securities
- **HDFC Securities Limited** (HSL CP 15/01/2026): 11,000 securities
- **Aditya Birla Money Limited** (ABML CP 15/01/26): 2,000 securities
- **Hindalco Industries Limited** (HIL CP 15/01/26): 40,000 securities
- **IIFL Finance Limited** (IIFL CP 19/01/26 Sr 2544): 1,000 securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The listed securities have been admitted to dealings on the debt market segment with designated security codes and ISINs provided in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Start Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: 
  - December 16, 2025 (Tata Steel)
  - December 31, 2025 (Angel One)
  - January 15, 2026 (7 issuers)
  - January 19, 2026 (IIFL Finance)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine operational notification for debt market participants. The listing of commercial papers provides additional trading instruments in the debt segment but does not affect equity markets or introduce any market-wide changes.

**Investor Impact**: Information is relevant only for debt market investors and institutional participants interested in short-term commercial paper investments from these corporate issuers.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for members. The securities are now available for trading with designated codes as per the annexure.
