## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of further issues of securities for 14 companies effective September 5, 2025. The securities include equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes and preferential allotments, with specific security codes, lot sizes, and trading regulations as per NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have further securities admitted for trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- Effective date: September 5, 2025
- Securities include ESOP allotments and preferential issues
- Notable companies: ICICI Bank, Adani Green Energy, Cartrade Tech, Brainbees Solutions (FirstCry)
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities admission under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specifications under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Trading to be conducted only using designated security codes

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use only designated security codes for these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (all securities have lot size of 1)
- Securities are pari passu with existing shares
- Lock-in requirements vary by company and allotment type

## Important Dates

- **September 5, 2025**: Effective date for listing and trading of further securities
- Various allotment dates ranging from April 29, 2025 to September 1, 2025
- Lock-in expiry for Adani Green Energy: March 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

- Increased liquidity potential for affected securities
- Significant volume additions, particularly Adani Green Energy (4.49 million shares) and ICICI Bank details pending
- ESOP allotments indicate employee participation and company growth
- Market participants should note new security ranges and trading parameters
