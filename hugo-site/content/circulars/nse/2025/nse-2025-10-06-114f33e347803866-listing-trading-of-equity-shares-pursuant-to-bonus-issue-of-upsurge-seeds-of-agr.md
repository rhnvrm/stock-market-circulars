## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by Upsurge Seeds of Agriculture Limited. The securities will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective October 14, 2025. A total of 30,25,628 bonus shares will be listed under the symbol USASEEDS.

## Key Points

- Company: Upsurge Seeds of Agriculture Limited
- Symbol: USASEEDS
- ISIN: INE0CBM01019
- Number of bonus shares: 30,25,628
- Distinctive Number Range: 7059801 to 10085428
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the new securities and update their systems accordingly. Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 6, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 13, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: October 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The bonus issue increases the number of shares outstanding for Upsurge Seeds of Agriculture Limited but does not affect overall market operations. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares according to the bonus ratio, improving liquidity in the stock.
