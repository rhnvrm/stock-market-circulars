## Summary

NSE has admitted three non-convertible debentures of Piramal Finance Limited to trading on the Debt Segment effective November 10, 2025. These securities were transferred from Piramal Enterprises Limited pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement. All three debentures are publicly issued, fixed-rate instruments with ICRA AA rating.

## Key Points

- Three debentures with symbols RDNGDB PFL26, RDNGDB PFL28, and RDNGDB PFL33 admitted to trading
- Original issue date: November 3, 2023
- Maturity dates: November 3, 2026, 2028, and 2033 respectively
- Coupon rates: 9.05%, 9.2%, and 9.35% with periodic frequency
- All instruments rated ICRA AA with multiplier 1
- Securities transferred from Piramal Enterprises Limited through Scheme of Arrangement
- Trading effective from November 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing following corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should update their systems to reflect the new listings and ensure proper trading and settlement of these securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 7, 2025
- Effective Date: November 10, 2025
- Issue Date (all debentures): November 3, 2023
- Maturity Dates:
  - PFL26: November 3, 2026
  - PFL28: November 3, 2028
  - PFL33: November 3, 2033

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is an administrative listing following transfer of securities from Piramal Enterprises Limited to Piramal Finance Limited under a corporate restructuring scheme. The instruments continue trading with same terms and ratings. Impact is confined to debt market participants dealing with Piramal group securities.
