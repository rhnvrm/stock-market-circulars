## Summary

NSE has admitted Motilal Oswal Nifty Services Sector ETF units to trading on the Capital Market segment effective November 27, 2025. The ETF will trade under symbol MOSERVICE with ISIN INF247L01GJ3, comprising 1,500,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10/- each and issue price of Rs. 33.09.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: MOSERVICE
- ISIN: INF247L01GJ3
- Number of Units: 1,500,000
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 33.09 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading Effective Date: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (MOSERVICE) for identification
- Trading shall be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Market makers listed above are authorized to provide liquidity

- Circular Issue Date: November 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement Date: November 27, 2025
