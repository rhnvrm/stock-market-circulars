## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Kotak Nifty Next 50 ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscriptions from December 18, 2025 to January 01, 2026, with allotment scheduled for January 07, 2026. Both Direct and Regular plans with Growth options are available under the Equity category.

## Key Points

- Two scheme options available: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth
- Both schemes have identical ISIN: INF174KA1YO3
- NFO subscription period: December 18, 2025 to January 01, 2026
- Allotment date: January 07, 2026
- Category: Equity ETF
- Fund House: Kotak Mutual Fund

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 06, 2026 at 12:00 PM
- Funds can be transferred through any payment mode
- Payment deadline is one day before allotment date

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: December 18, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Fund Payment Deadline**: January 06, 2026 (12:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: January 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product launch with minimal market impact. The circular provides operational information for NSE members to facilitate ETF subscriptions. Investors seeking exposure to Nifty Next 50 index will have a new passive investment option through this ETF.
