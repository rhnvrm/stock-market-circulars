## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing of equity shares of Sundrex Oil Company Limited (Symbol: SOCL) on the NSE EMERGE SME Platform with effect from December 30, 2025. The security will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment). The IPO issue price is Rs. 86/- per share with a face value of Rs. 10/- and total securities of 13,393,552 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company Symbol**: SOCL
- **ISIN**: INE0CTB01014
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **IPO Issue Price**: Rs. 86/- per share
- **Total Securities**: 13,393,552 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 13,393,552
- **Market Lot Size**: 1,600 equity shares
- **Trading Series**: Initially ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - TFTS), later to be shifted to SM (Normal Rolling Segment)
- **Settlement Type**: 'W' initially, then 'N'
- **Market Type**: Continuous Market
- **Maximum Permissible Spread**: Within 10%
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable for IPO category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5) for admitting new securities to trading on the EMERGE SME Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members**: Must note the designated security code (SOCL) and lot size (1,600 equity shares) for trading purposes
- **Trading Availability**: Security will be available for trading in continuous market as per Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018
- **Lock-in Details**: As per Annexure I (specific shares with distinctive numbers have lock-in restrictions)
- **Market Maker**: Sunflower Broking Private Limited appointed as market maker

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 – March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard SME IPO listing on the EMERGE platform with limited market-wide implications. The security will trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment initially, indicating enhanced monitoring.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Routine listing procedure for NSE members dealing in SME securities. The lot size of 1,600 shares and initial TFTS placement are standard risk management measures for new SME listings.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for SME investors - New investment opportunity in the oil processing sector. Initial TFTS placement provides price discovery mechanism while limiting speculative activity. Investors should note the 1,600 share minimum lot size requirement.
