## Summary

NSE has announced market lot adjustment for Jakharia Fabric Limited (Symbol: JAKHARIA) in the SME platform following a 2:1 bonus issue. The adjusted market lot will be 2400 shares, with an adjustment factor of 3, effective from October 08, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Jakharia Fabric Limited (Symbol: JAKHARIA)
- Corporate Action: Bonus Issue
- Bonus Ratio: 2:1
- Adjustment Factor: 3
- Adjusted Market Lot: 2400 shares
- Members must load updated security files before trading on the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a technical adjustment to trading parameters following a corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII - Security File.gz file in their trading application before trading on October 08, 2025
- Files can be obtained from the directory common/NTNEAT on the Extranet server
- MII - Security File.gz is also available on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Ex-date/Effective Date**: October 08, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects trading in a single SME security. The 2:1 bonus will increase the number of shares outstanding by 3x (shareholders receive 2 additional shares for every 1 held). The market lot adjustment to 2400 shares ensures proper trading unit alignment post-bonus.

**Operational Impact**: Members must ensure their trading systems are updated with the new security files before market opening on October 08, 2025 to avoid trading disruptions.
