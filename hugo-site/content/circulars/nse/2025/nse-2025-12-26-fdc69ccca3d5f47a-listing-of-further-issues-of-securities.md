## Summary

NSE has admitted further issues of equity shares for 23 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment, effective December 29, 2025. All securities are equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) with varying allotment dates between December 16-22, 2025. The shares will be identified by designated security codes and traded in specified lot sizes as per NSE regulations.

## Key Points

- 23 companies have issued equity shares under ESOP schemes
- Total securities listed include 212,637 shares by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED (largest allotment mentioned)
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- Companies include major financial institutions: ICICI Bank, ICICI Lombard, ICICI Prudential, RBL Bank
- Technology sector: Wipro, NIIT Learning Systems
- Industrial sector: Alkyl Amines, Windsor Machines, Walchandnagar Industries
- Financial services: 360 ONE WAM, Motilal Oswal, Nippon Life India Asset Management

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - lot size specifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification on trading system
- Trading must be conducted in lot sizes as specified in the annexure (market lot of 1 for all securities)
- Securities are available for trading from December 29, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: 
  - December 16, 2025: Borosil Limited
  - December 18, 2025: 360 ONE WAM LIMITED, Arvind Fashions Limited
  - December 19, 2025: Alkyl Amines Chemicals Limited
  - December 22, 2025: Bajel Projects Limited

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine ESOP allotments represent small percentages of total equity and do not materially affect share capital or trading dynamics.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Shares are pari passu with existing equity, maintaining equal rights for all shareholders. No lock-in restrictions provide immediate liquidity.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard listing process with existing trading infrastructure and lot sizes.
