## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Park Medi World Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol PARKHOSPS with ISIN INE119201023. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Park Medi World Limited
- Trading Symbol: PARKHOSPS
- ISIN: INE119201023
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing and await the separate circular specifying the exact listing date before commencing trading in this security.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing will provide a new investment opportunity in the healthcare/hospital sector. Impact is limited to investors interested in the IPO and the company itself. Trading members should prepare their systems to handle the new security once the listing date is announced.
