## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has approved the listing of units issued by Groww Asset Management Limited under the Groww Nifty Realty ETF scheme. The ETF will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from October 09, 2025. A total of 6,434,490 units with face value of Rs. 10/- each will be listed under the symbol GROWWRLTY with ISIN INF666M01MN2.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: GROWWRLTY
- Total Units Listed: 6,434,490 units
- Face Value per Unit: Rs. 10/-
- Paid Up Value per Unit: Rs. 10/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 10/-
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Series: EQ
- Effective Date: October 09, 2025
- Market Makers: East India Securities (TM code: 08134) and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Ltd. (TM code: 90246)
- Registrar: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to identify the security by its designated code (GROWWRLTY)
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot size of 1 unit
- Market makers East India Securities and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Ltd. to provide liquidity

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: October 08, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: October 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with a new passive investment vehicle tracking the Nifty Realty Index. The ETF offers exposure to the real estate sector through a diversified basket of realty stocks. Impact is limited to investors seeking sector-specific exposure and market makers providing liquidity. The listing expands the ETF product suite available on NSE and provides an additional investment option for realty sector allocation.
