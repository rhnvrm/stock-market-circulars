## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced a change in the ISIN code for equity shares of Websol Energy System Limited (symbol: WEBELSOLAR). The new ISIN INE855C01023 will be effective from November 14, 2025, following a sub-division of shares from face value Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-. The new ISIN will apply to all trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Company: Websol Energy System Limited
- Trading Symbol: WEBELSOLAR
- New ISIN: INE855C01023
- Reason: Sub-division of face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- (1:10 split)
- Effective Date: November 14, 2025 (ex-date)
- The new ISIN applies to all trades from the ex-date onwards

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard ISIN update procedure following a corporate action (share sub-division).

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN code INE855C01023 for WEBELSOLAR
- All trading and settlement systems must recognize the new ISIN effective November 14, 2025
- Members must ensure proper identification of the security using the new ISIN for all transactions on or after the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Ex-Date:** November 14, 2025 (new ISIN becomes effective)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change resulting from a share sub-division (stock split). The face value reduction from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 represents a 1:10 split, making shares more affordable and potentially improving liquidity. Market participants must update their systems and records to use the new ISIN for all transactions from the ex-date. The change affects trading systems, depository records, and portfolio management systems that track securities by ISIN.
