## Summary

Mark AB Capital Investment India Private Limited is conducting a takeover offer for Bohra Industries Limited through NSE's web-based bidding platform. The offer is for up to 70,19,667 equity shares at Rs. 32 per share under the Tender Offer Scheme.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Mark AB Capital Investment India Private Limited
- Target: Bohra Industries Limited (BOHRAIND)
- Offer size: Up to 70,19,667 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 32 per share (fixed price)
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Lot size: 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE802W01023
- Merchant Banker: Saffron Capital Advisors Private Limited
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer follows existing Tender Offer Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Members with existing IPO bidding platform access can use current credentials
- New members must use admin terminal (Adminxxxxx format) to create branches and users
- Order modification or cancellation not permitted for takeover offers
- Multiple investor categories supported: FII, MF, IC, FI, OTH, CO, IND, NOH

## Important Dates

- Issue Period: July 31, 2025 to August 13, 2025
- Bidding Time: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- Custodial confirmation on closure day: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM
- Live URL: https://eipo.nseindia.com

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to Bohra Industries shareholders and trading members participating in the tender offer. The takeover could result in change of control for the company. Trading members need to prepare for web-based bidding process during the specified period.
