## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 75,000 additional equity shares of Committed Cargo Care Limited on the SME EMERGE platform, effective September 09, 2025. The shares were issued under preferential basis at Rs. 81 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.

## Key Points

- 75,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each issued under preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 81 per share
- Trading symbol: COMMITTED
- ISIN: INE597Z01014 (temporary ISIN currently in use)
- Market lot size: 800 shares
- Date of allotment: July 23, 2025
- Distinctive number range: 11384601 to 11459600

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security codes for system trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 800 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circulars

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 09, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 23, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: March 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small preferential issue for a single SME company. The lock-in period until March 2026 limits immediate trading volume impact.
