## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has provisionally empaneled M/s. Simply Algo Fintech Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately from December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- M/s. Simply Algo Fintech Private Limited has been provisionally empaneled as an Algo Provider
- The provider offers Whitebox type algorithmic trading solutions
- Located at No.418, First Floor, G.V. Residency, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu-641038
- Contact number: +919751597579
- Circular applicable with immediate effect
- Complete list of empaneled Algo Providers available on NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine empanelment notification adding one more provider to the existing list of empaneled algo providers.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational in nature, providing trading members with details of a newly empaneled algo provider they may choose to engage with for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 23, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Circular Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Circular Reference**: NSE/INVG/71974, 536/2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative action expanding the pool of empaneled algo providers available to trading members.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members now have an additional option for sourcing algorithmic trading solutions. The empanelment is provisional, indicating the provider is subject to ongoing evaluation.

**Affected Parties**: Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions may consider M/s. Simply Algo Fintech Private Limited as a vendor option.

**Reference**: The updated list of all empaneled Algo Providers is maintained at https://www.nseindia.com/static/trade/empanelled-algo-providers-exchange
