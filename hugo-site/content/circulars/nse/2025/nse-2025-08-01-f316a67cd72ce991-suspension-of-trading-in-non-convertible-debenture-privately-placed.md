## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Debenture issued by Tata Capital Limited (ISIN: INE306N07NB4) effective August 4, 2025, due to redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Tata Capital Limited's Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed)
- ISIN: INE306N07NB4
- Suspension effective from August 4, 2025
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the debenture
- Action taken under NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must cease trading in the specified debenture from the suspension date
- No additional compliance requirements for market participants

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves suspension of a single privately placed debenture due to normal redemption process. Routine administrative action with no broader market implications.
