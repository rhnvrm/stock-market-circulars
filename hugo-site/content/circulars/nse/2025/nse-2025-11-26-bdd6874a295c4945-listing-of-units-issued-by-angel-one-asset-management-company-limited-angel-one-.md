## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted Angel One Nifty Total Market Momentum Quality 50 ETF units for trading on the Capital Market segment with effect from November 27, 2025. The ETF is issued by Angel One Asset Management Company Limited with symbol AONETMMQ50 and ISIN INF1J2R01148. A total of 5,316,342 units have been allotted at face value and issue price of Rs. 10/- per unit.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: AONETMMQ50
- ISIN: INF1J2R01148
- Number of Units Allotted: 5,316,342 units
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to update their systems with the new security code AONETMMQ50
- Trading permitted only in designated lot size of 1 unit
- Security to be identified by designated code on trading systems
- All trading members and market participants to note the listing for order placement and execution

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Circular Issue Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it represents a new ETF product introduction. The ETF provides investors with exposure to momentum and quality factors across the Nifty Total Market universe. The product expands investment options for market participants seeking systematic factor-based investment strategies. Trading members include Parwati Capital Market Private Limited, East India Securities Limited, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, Share India Securities Limited, Irage Broking Services LLP, and Fyers Securities Private Limited.
