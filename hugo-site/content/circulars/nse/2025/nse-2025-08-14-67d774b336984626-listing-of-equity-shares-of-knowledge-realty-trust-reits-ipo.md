NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of Knowledge Realty Trust equity shares under the REITs IPO category. The security will trade with symbol KRT and ISIN INE1JAR25012. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

- Company Name: Knowledge Realty Trust
- Trading Symbol: KRT
- ISIN: INE1JAR25012
- Category: REITs IPO
- Listing date to be announced separately
