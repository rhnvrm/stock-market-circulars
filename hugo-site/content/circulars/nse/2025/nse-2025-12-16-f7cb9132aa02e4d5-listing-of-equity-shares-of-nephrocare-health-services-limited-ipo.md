## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Nephrocare Health Services Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol NEPHROPLUS with ISIN INE428V01029. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Nephrocare Health Services Limited
- Trading Symbol: NEPHROPLUS
- ISIN: INE428V01029
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71817, 2445/2025
- Department: LISTING

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying exchange members about the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate market impact as it is a standard listing notification. The announcement adds Nephrocare Health Services Limited to the NSE's listed companies, expanding investment opportunities in the healthcare services sector. Market participants and investors interested in healthcare stocks should watch for the subsequent circular announcing the actual listing date. The company will be available for trading under the symbol NEPHROPLUS once listing is completed.
