## Summary

National Stock Exchange has admitted Suba Hotels Limited's equity shares for trading on the EMERGE SME Platform effective October 07, 2025. The security will trade under symbol SUBAHOTELS in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') initially, and will subsequently shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment). The IPO was priced at Rs. 111/- per share with a face value of Rs. 10/-.

## Key Points

- Symbol: SUBAHOTELS
- ISIN: INE0RYR01018
- Face Value: Rs. 10/-
- IPO Price: Rs. 111/- per share
- Total Securities: 24,242,516 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 1,200 equity shares
- Trading Series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W')
- Market Maker: NNM Securities Private Limited
- Market Maker Maximum Permissible Spread: Within 10%
- Registrar & Transfer Agent: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing circular following SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 for SME listings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading permitted only in specified lot sizes (1,200 equity shares)
- Security initially available in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS)
- Will subsequently shift to Normal Rolling Segment as per SEBI guidelines
- Pre-open session applicable as per Exchange regulations

Minimal market impact as this is a standard SME IPO listing. The security is placed under Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment initially, indicating cautious regulatory oversight.
