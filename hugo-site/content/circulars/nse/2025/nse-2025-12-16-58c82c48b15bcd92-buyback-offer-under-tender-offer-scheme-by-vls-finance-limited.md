## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced that VLS Finance Limited will conduct a buyback offer under the Tender Offer scheme. The buyback will utilize NSE's web-based bidding platform for up to 26,31,578 equity shares at a fixed price of Rs. 380 per share. The offer period runs from December 18-24, 2025, with bidding hours from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM and custodial confirmation until 4:00 PM on the issue closure date.

## Key Points

- Buyback of up to 26,31,578 equity shares of VLS Finance Limited
- Fixed offer price: Rs. 380 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Lot size: 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE709A01018
- Series: BB (Buyback)
- Merchant Banker: New Berry Capitals Private Limited
- Registrar: RCMC Share Registry Private Limited
- Platform: NSE web-based tender offer facility (https://eipo.nseindia.com)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This buyback follows the established Tender Offer Scheme as per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of the Capital Market segment are eligible to participate
- Members with existing web-based IPO bidding platform access can use their current user IDs
- Members without access must log into the admin terminal using Adminxxxxx (where xxxxx is the Member Code) to create branches and users
- Bidding categories include: FII, Mutual Funds, Insurance Company, Banks & Financial Institutions, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Issue Period**: December 18-24, 2025
- **Bidding Time**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (each day)
- **Custodial Confirmation**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (on issue closure date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific corporate action with limited broader market impact. The buyback affects only VLS Finance Limited shareholders and interested investors. The use of NSE's standardized web-based tender offer platform ensures transparent and efficient execution. Trading members already familiar with the IPO bidding platform can seamlessly participate. The fixed price mechanism at Rs. 380 per share provides clarity to shareholders considering participation in the buyback offer.
