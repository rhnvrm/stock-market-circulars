## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Diviniti Equity Long Short Fund of ITI Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for transactions including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities starting December 03, 2025. This specialized investment fund offers six plan variants across Direct and Regular plans with different payout options.

## Key Points

- Diviniti Equity Long Short Fund will be available for ongoing transactions effective December 03, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Direct Plan and Regular Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Fund operates under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category
- Circular issued by NSE's NMF TM Segment department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members of NSE MF Invest Platform are informed of the availability of this fund for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 03, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific mutual fund product becoming available on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The impact is primarily operational, providing investors access to ITI Mutual Fund's Diviniti Equity Long Short Fund for transactions. Members and distributors can now facilitate client investments in this specialized equity fund with various plan options and systematic transaction facilities.
