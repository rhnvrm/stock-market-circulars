## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Timex Group India Ltd. scheduled for December 29-30, 2025. The settlement will follow existing rules for secondary market transactions with specific timings for different investor categories (retail and non-retail).

## Key Points

- Settlement follows existing secondary market transaction rules
- Two order dates: December 29 and 30, 2025
- Separate settlement numbers: 2025213 and 2025214
- Different margin categories: 100% and 0% for institutional non-retail; 100% for retail
- Funds pay-in deadline: T+1 day by 11:00 AM
- Securities and funds pay-out: T+1 day by 01:30 PM
- Securities Transaction Tax (STT) and stamp duty applicable on settlement day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing secondary market settlement rules for the OFS transaction.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Members/Custodians/PCMs:**
- Adhere to specified settlement calendar timings
- Ensure custodial confirmation by specified deadlines (varies by category)
- Complete funds pay-in by T+1 day 11:00 AM
- Note that STT and stamp duty will be collected on respective settlement days

**Settlement Schedule Details:**

**December 29, 2025 Orders (Settlement 2025213):**
- IS (non-retail) 100%: Custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM same day, settlement on Dec 30
- IS (non-retail) 0%: Custodial confirmation by 7:30 AM Dec 30, settlement on Dec 30

**December 30, 2025 Orders (Settlement 2025214):**
- RS (Retail) 100%: No custodial confirmation required, settlement on Dec 31
- IS (non-retail) 100%: Custodial confirmation by 3:30 PM same day, settlement on Dec 31
- IS (non-retail) 0%: Custodial confirmation by 7:30 AM Dec 31, settlement on Dec 31

## Important Dates

- **December 26, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 29, 2025**: First order date for institutional non-retail categories
- **December 30, 2025**: Second order date (retail and institutional non-retail); Settlement date for Dec 29 orders
- **December 31, 2025**: Settlement date for Dec 30 orders
- **Funds Pay-in**: T+1 day by 11:00 AM
- **Pay-out**: T+1 day by 01:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects participants in Timex Group India OFS only

**Operational Impact:** Medium - requires members and custodians to follow specific timelines for different investor categories with tight custodial confirmation deadlines (some as early as 7:30 AM)

**Participant Groups Affected:**
- All NSE members participating in the OFS
- Custodians handling institutional orders
- Professional Clearing Members (PCMs)
- Retail and institutional investors in Timex Group India OFS

**Reference:** This circular refers to NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025 for the underlying OFS framework.
