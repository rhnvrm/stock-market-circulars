## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of further equity shares for 10 companies admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment effective November 14, 2025. All shares were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) with allotment dates ranging from October 29 to November 11, 2025. The shares are pari passu with existing equity and have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- **Astral Limited**: 15,436 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted on November 5, 2025
- **Axis Bank Limited**: 167,367 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted on November 11, 2025
- **Balaji Telefilms Limited**: 86,100 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted on November 5, 2025
- **Capri Global Capital Limited**: 518,840 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted on November 6, 2025
- **Cipla Limited**: 5,518 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted on October 29, 2025
- **Delhivery Limited**: 122,840 equity shares of Re. 1/- each allotted on November 10, 2025
- All securities have market lot of 1
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify securities by designated codes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for all securities)
- Companies have completed allotment formalities under their respective ESOP schemes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**:
  - Cipla Limited: October 29, 2025
  - Astral Limited, Balaji Telefilms Limited: November 5, 2025
  - Capri Global Capital Limited: November 6, 2025
  - Delhivery Limited: November 10, 2025
  - Axis Bank Limited: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine ESOP allotments representing fractional increases to existing equity capital. The number of shares being listed is small relative to the outstanding equity of each company.

**Operational Impact**: No significant operational changes for trading members. Standard trading procedures apply with market lot of 1 for all securities.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible impact on liquidity as the additional shares represent minor dilution and will be available for trading without restrictions.
