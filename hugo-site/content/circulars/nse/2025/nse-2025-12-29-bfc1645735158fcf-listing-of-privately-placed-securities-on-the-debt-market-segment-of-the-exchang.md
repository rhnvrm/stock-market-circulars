## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective December 29, 2025. The circular lists fresh issues and re-issues of commercial papers and other debt securities from six companies with their respective ISINs, face values, and maturity details.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue of commercial papers from Godrej Industries Limited (1,500 securities, face value ₹5,00,000) and HDB Financial Services Ltd (10,000 securities, face value ₹5,00,000)
- Fresh issue of debt securities from IIFL Samasta Finance Limited (9.25% coupon, maturing 2028) and Bank of India (7.23% coupon, maturing 2035)
- Re-issue of commercial paper from Tata Capital Limited (9,000 securities, maturing March 23, 2026)
- Re-issue of debt securities from Aditya Birla Capital Limited (7.3789% coupon, maturing February 14, 2028)
- All securities listed with effect from December 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Issuers' debentures/bonds allotted in dematerialized mode have been credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/DDHS/CIR/P/2020/198 dated October 05, 2022
- All securities have been assigned designated security codes as specified in the annexure

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: December 29, 2025
- **Godrej Industries CP Allotment**: December 26, 2025; Redemption: February 26, 2026
- **HDB Financial CP Allotment**: December 26, 2025; Redemption: January 5, 2026
- **IIFL Samasta Finance Allotment**: December 24, 2025; Redemption: December 24, 2028
- **Bank of India Allotment**: December 26, 2025; Redemption: December 26, 2035
- **Tata Capital CP Allotment**: December 26, 2025; Redemption: March 23, 2026
- **Aditya Birla Capital Allotment**: December 26, 2025; Redemption: February 14, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing provides additional investment opportunities in the debt segment but does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Relevant only for debt market participants, institutional investors, and the specific issuers mentioned. Provides liquidity options for holders of these privately placed securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and qualified buyers in the debt market segment who may trade these securities on the exchange platform.
