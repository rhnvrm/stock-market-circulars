## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to bonus issue of Aaron Industries Limited. The securities will be available for trading on NSE Capital Market segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Aaron Industries Limited
- Symbol: AARON
- ISIN: INE721Z01010
- Number of bonus securities: 1,04,73,239
- Distinctive number range: 10473240 to 20946478
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes for trading the bonus shares on the system.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 25, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 28, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine bonus issue listing that will increase the tradable float of Aaron Industries Limited. No significant market disruption expected as this follows standard corporate action procedures.
