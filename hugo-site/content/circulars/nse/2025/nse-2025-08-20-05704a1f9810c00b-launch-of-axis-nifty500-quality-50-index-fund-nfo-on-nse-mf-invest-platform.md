## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Axis Nifty500 Quality 50 Index Fund NFO (New Fund Offer) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for investment from August 21, 2025, to September 4, 2025, until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two plan options available: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth
- Fund category: Equity
- Investment period: August 21 - September 4, 2025 (until 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: September 10, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by NFO closing date

## Fund Details

| Plan Type | ISIN | Category | Allotment Date |
|-----------|------|----------|----------------|
| Direct Plan - Growth | INF846KA1200 | Equity | 10.09.2025 |
| Regular Plan - Growth | INF846KA1192 | Equity | 10.09.2025 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before the NFO closing date
- All transactions to be conducted through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening**: August 21, 2025
- **NFO Closing**: September 4, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with another index fund option tracking the Nifty500 Quality 50 Index, expanding investment choices on the NSE MF Invest Platform.
