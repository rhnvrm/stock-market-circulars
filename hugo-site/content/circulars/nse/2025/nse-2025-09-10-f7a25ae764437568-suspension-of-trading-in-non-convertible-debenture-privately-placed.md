## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Non-Convertible Debentures (Privately Placed) effective September 11, 2025, due to redemption. The affected securities are from Canara Bank, Toyota Financial Services India Limited, and L&T Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Three NCDs will be suspended from trading on September 11, 2025
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular effective from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified NCDs from September 11, 2025
- No specific compliance actions required beyond cessation of trading

## Important Dates

- **Suspension Date**: September 11, 2025 - Trading suspension effective for all three NCDs
- **Circular Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine redemption-related suspensions. The suspensions affect privately placed debt securities with limited trading activity. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
