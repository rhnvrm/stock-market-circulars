## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the trading symbol for Creative Newtech Limited will be changed from "CREATIVE" to "CNL" effective December 10, 2025. This is an administrative change that will affect how the stock is identified on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Creative Newtech Limited
- Existing Symbol: CREATIVE
- New Symbol: CNL
- Effective Date: December 10, 2025
- Circular applies to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a symbol nomenclature update only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new symbol "CNL" from December 10, 2025
- All trading in Creative Newtech Limited shares must use the new symbol CNL after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 10, 2025**: New symbol "CNL" becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a purely administrative change affecting only the trading symbol identification. The company's securities, ISIN, and all other identifiers remain unchanged.

**Operational Impact**: Exchange members and market participants need to update their systems and databases to reflect the new symbol to ensure seamless trading operations from December 10, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: No impact on existing shareholdings or trading operations. Investors will need to use the new symbol "CNL" when placing orders after the effective date.
