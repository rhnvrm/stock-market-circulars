## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares issued by 18 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The securities will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 05, 2025. The circular provides details of allotment dates, number of securities, distinctive number ranges, and lock-in periods for each company.

## Key Points

- 18 companies have issued further equity shares under ESOP schemes
- Listing effective date: December 05, 2025
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- Lock-in periods vary by company - some have no lock-in while others have specific lock-in dates
- Major companies include ICICI Bank, Wipro, Federal Bank, ICICI Lombard, ICICI Prudential Life, Mankind Pharma, and RBL Bank

## Companies and Allotment Details

### Bajaj Electricals Limited (BAJAJELEC)
- Face Value: Rs. 2
- Securities allotted: 18,301 equity shares
- Allotment date: November 24, 2025
- Lock-in: Not Applicable

### Bliss GVS Pharma Limited (BLISSGVS)
- Face Value: Re. 1
- Securities allotted: 33,670 equity shares
- Allotment date: November 05, 2025
- Lock-in: May 05, 2026

### Campus Activewear Limited (CAMPUS)
- Face Value: Rs. 5
- Securities allotted: 58,138 equity shares
- Allotment date: November 21, 2025
- Lock-in: Not Applicable

### The Federal Bank Limited (FEDERALBNK)
- Face Value: Rs. 2
- Securities allotted: 259,532 equity shares
- Allotment date: December 01, 2025
- Lock-in: Not Applicable

### Globus Spirits Limited (GLOBUSSPR)
- Face Value: Rs. 10
- Securities allotted: 6,400 equity shares

### ICICI Bank Limited (ICICIBANK)

### Other Companies
The circular also covers: ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, Ind-Swift Laboratories Limited, Jyoti Structures Limited, L&T Finance Limited, Mankind Pharma Limited, PC Jeweller Limited, RBL Bank Limited, Renaissance Global Limited, V-Mart Retail Limited, and Wipro Limited.

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specifications under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading these securities
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot sizes as per the annexure
- All shares are eligible for trading on the Capital Market segment from the effective date

## Important Dates

- Circular date: December 04, 2025
- Listing effective date: December 05, 2025
- Various allotment dates ranging from November 05, 2025 to December 01, 2025
- Lock-in end date for Bliss GVS Pharma: May 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The further issues represent ESOP allotments which are typically small in number relative to the total issued capital of these companies. The listing of these securities maintains transparency and allows ESOP recipients to trade their allotted shares. No material impact on stock prices or trading volumes is expected from this notification.
