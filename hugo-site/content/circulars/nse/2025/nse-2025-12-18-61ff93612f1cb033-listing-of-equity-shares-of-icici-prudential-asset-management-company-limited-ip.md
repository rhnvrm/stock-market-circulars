## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular informing members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol ICICIAMC and ISIN INE346A01027. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited
- Trading Symbol: ICICIAMC
- ISIN: INE346A01027
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular about an upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited represents the addition of a significant financial services entity to the NSE. As part of the ICICI Group, this listing may attract investor interest in the asset management sector. The actual market impact will depend on the listing date, offer size, and pricing details which will be communicated separately. Members should prepare for trading activity once the listing date is announced.
