## Summary

NSE has admitted Om Freight Forwarders Limited equity shares for trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 08, 2025. The company's IPO of 33,675,442 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each at an issue price of Rs. 135/- will commence trading under the symbol OMFREIGHT in the BE series. The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO category scrips as per SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: OMFREIGHT
- **ISIN**: INE1BZC01019
- **Series**: BE (will subsequently shift to EQ series)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 135/-
- **Total Shares**: 33,675,442 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Listing Date**: October 08, 2025
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new logistics and freight forwarding company to the NSE platform. With an issue price of Rs. 135/- and over 33 million shares, the IPO represents significant capital raising for the company. The staggered lock-in periods indicate a mix of promoter holdings and public float. Trading will initially occur in the BE (Book Entry) series before transitioning to the regular EQ series, providing price discovery mechanisms for a newly listed security. Investors in the logistics sector will have a new investment opportunity.
