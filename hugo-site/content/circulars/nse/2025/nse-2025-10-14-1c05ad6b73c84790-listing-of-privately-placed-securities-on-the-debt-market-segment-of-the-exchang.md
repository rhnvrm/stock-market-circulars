## Summary

NSE circular 2016/2025 notifies the admission of privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective October 14, 2025. The circular covers 6 commercial paper issuances and 3 other debt security listings with designated security codes and ISINs as specified in the annexure.

## Key Points

- 6 commercial paper (CP) issues listed from ICICI Securities (2 issues), Axis Securities, Welspun Corp, Godrej Industries, and CEAT
- 3 debt security issues listed from Piramal Finance, Clix Capital Services, and Jio Credit
- All securities effective for trading from October 14, 2025
- Total CP value approximately Rs 520 crore (10,400 securities x Rs 5 lakh face value)
- Debt securities include Rs 307.5 crore from Piramal Finance, Rs 75 crore from Clix Capital, and Rs 500 crore from Jio Credit

## Regulatory Framework

Listing approved under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: October 13-14, 2025
- **CP Maturity Dates**: Range from Nov 28, 2025 to Apr 10, 2026
- **Debt Security Maturity Dates**: Range from 2027 to Apr 2028

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing enables secondary market trading for these privately placed securities. No immediate impact on equity markets or broad market operations. Provides additional investment options for debt market investors across short-term (CP) and medium-term (bonds) instruments.
