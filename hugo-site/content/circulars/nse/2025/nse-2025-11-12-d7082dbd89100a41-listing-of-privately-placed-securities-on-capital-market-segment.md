## Summary

NSE has admitted NTPC Green Energy Limited's privately placed debentures to dealings on the Capital Market segment. The 7.01% coupon rate debentures maturing on November 12, 2035 (Series 1) will be available for trading with effect from November 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: NTPC Green Energy Limited
- Security Type: Debentures (Series 1)
- Symbol: 701NGEL35
- Coupon Rate: 7.01%
- Maturity Date: November 12, 2035
- ISIN Code: INE0ONG08016
- Market Lot: 1
- Series: N0
- Type: Privately placed securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should identify the security by its designated code: 701NGEL35
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Lot size specification per Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: November 12, 2025
- Effective Date: November 13, 2025
- Maturity Date: November 12, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides liquidity options for existing holders of NTPC Green Energy Limited debentures but does not affect broader market operations or trading in equity segments. The securities are likely held by institutional investors given the private placement nature.
