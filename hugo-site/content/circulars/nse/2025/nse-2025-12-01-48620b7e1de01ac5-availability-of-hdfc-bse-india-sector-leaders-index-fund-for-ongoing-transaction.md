## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting December 02, 2025. Both Regular Growth and Direct Growth options will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP).

## Key Points

- HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund reopened for transactions effective December 02, 2025
- Available in two variants: Regular Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1JX8) and Direct Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1JY6)
- Both schemes classified under Equity category
- Full support for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions
- Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities enabled
- Applicable to all NSE members on the MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members can facilitate transactions in these schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform starting from the effective date. No additional compliance requirements specified.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 02, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions with all systematic facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement affecting a specific mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in this index fund, as it provides access to the HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund with full transaction capabilities including systematic investment options.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Standard processing of mutual fund transactions through the NSE MF Invest Platform.
