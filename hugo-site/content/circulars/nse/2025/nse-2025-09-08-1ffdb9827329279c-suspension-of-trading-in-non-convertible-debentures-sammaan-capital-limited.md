## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in 5 Non-Convertible Debentures of Sammaan Capital Limited effective September 11, 2025. The suspension is due to maturity of these debt securities.

## Key Points

- 5 Non-Convertible Debentures of Sammaan Capital Limited will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from September 11, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension is due to natural maturity of debt instruments rather than any adverse corporate action or regulatory concern.
