## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 14,054 fully paid-up equity shares of Suzlon Energy Limited (Symbol: SUZLON) effective August 21, 2025. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares with face value and paid-up value of Rs. 2/- each.

## Key Points

- 14,054 equity shares of Suzlon Energy Limited admitted to trading
- Conversion of partly paid-up to fully paid-up equity shares
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- ISIN: INE040H01021
- Pari Passu rights with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading
- Securities identified only by designated codes on trading system
- Trading in specified lot sizes as per annexure

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to small number of shares (14,054) and routine nature of conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up shares. No dilution effect as these are conversions rather than new issuances.
