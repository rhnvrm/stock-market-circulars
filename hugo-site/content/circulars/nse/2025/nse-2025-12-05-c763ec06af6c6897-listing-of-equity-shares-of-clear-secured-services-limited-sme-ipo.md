## Summary

Clear Secured Services Limited's equity shares are being listed on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform effective December 08, 2025. The stock will trade under symbol CSSL with ISIN INE1EF801010. Initially, trading will occur in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') before shifting to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment). The IPO issue price is Rs. 132/- per share with face value of Rs. 10/- and market lot size of 1000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Stock Symbol**: CSSL
- **ISIN**: INE1EF801010
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **IPO Issue Price**: Rs. 132/- per share
- **Total Securities**: 24,047,112 equity shares
- **Market Lot Size**: 1000 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 24,047,112
- **Maximum Permissible Spread**: Within 10%
- **Trading Series**: Initially ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W'), later to shift to SM (Normal Rolling Segment - Settlement Type 'N')
- **Market Type**: Continuous Market
- **Market Maker**: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent**: Bigshare Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, and Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stock will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category of scrips as per SEBI circular requirements
- Trading will be available in continuous market as per Exchange regulations
- Stock initially under Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with Settlement Type 'W'
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I (specific details in company documentation)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 – March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has limited impact on the broader market as it pertains to a single SME company entering the EMERGE platform. The impact is primarily relevant to:

- **IPO Investors**: New investment opportunity in security services sector
- **Market Makers**: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited designated as market maker
- **Trading Members**: New security available for trading with specific lot sizes and spread restrictions

The initial placement in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment indicates enhanced monitoring, which is standard practice for new SME listings. The market lot of 1000 shares at Rs. 132/- per share represents a minimum investment of Rs. 132,000.
