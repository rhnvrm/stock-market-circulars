## Summary

NSE has admitted PNB Housing Finance Limited's privately placed debentures to dealings on the Capital Market segment. The security, designated as 728PHFL28 (Series N0), will commence trading from December 10, 2025, with a market lot size of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Security: PHFL 7.28% 2028 Sr LXXII debentures
- Issuer: PNB Housing Finance Limited
- Symbol: 728PHFL28
- ISIN: INE572E07258
- Coupon Rate: 7.28% per annum
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Series: N0
- Maturity Date: June 5, 2028
- Trading commencement: December 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security by its designated code (728PHFL28) on the trading system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new security before trading commencement

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: June 5, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a single privately placed debt security listing. The admission of these debentures provides investors with a trading platform for PNB Housing Finance Limited's 7.28% coupon bonds maturing in 2028. The lot size of 1 unit ensures accessibility for retail and institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or existing trading operations.
