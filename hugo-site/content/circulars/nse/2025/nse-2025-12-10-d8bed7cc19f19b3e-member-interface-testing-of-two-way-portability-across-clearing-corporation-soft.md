## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has scheduled a mock session on December 13, 2025 to test the Two-way portability across Clearing Corporations using the Software as a Service (SaaS) model. This is in compliance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/TPD/P/CIR/2023/192 dated December 20, 2023, which mandates quarterly mock sessions to familiarize and train members on SaaS functionalities for business continuity purposes.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for December 13, 2025 from 10:00 hrs to 13:45 hrs
- NCL will invoke its SaaS setup between 10:00 hrs - 10:30 hrs
- NCL will be operational using SaaS setup (NCL@ICCL) between 11:15 hrs to 13:45 hrs
- All clearing members are requested to actively participate and test all functionalities
- Members who have existing login credentials can use them for the mock session
- New members need to request login credentials via email to support@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the ongoing compliance requirement from SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/TPD/P/CIR/2023/192 dated December 20, 2023, which mandates quarterly mock sessions for Business Continuity through SaaS model across Clearing Corporations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must participate in the mock session on December 13, 2025
- Members should test all functionalities associated with SaaS setup during the scheduled time
- Members without login credentials must request them from support@nsccl.co.in before the mock session
- Members should refer to previous NCL circulars for guidelines and SOPs:
  - NCL circular no 056/2023 (Download No: NCL/CMPT/56351) dated April 13, 2023
  - NCL circular no 059/2024 (Download No: NCL/CMPT/61959) dated May 10, 2024

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 13, 2025**: Mock session date
  - 10:00 hrs - 10:30 hrs: NCL SaaS setup invocation period
  - 10:00 hrs - 13:45 hrs: Full testing window for members
  - 11:15 hrs - 13:45 hrs: NCL operational using SaaS setup (NCL@ICCL)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All clearing members need to allocate resources and personnel to participate in the mandatory mock session. This is a routine quarterly requirement but essential for ensuring business continuity readiness.
