## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has notified members that the Zerodha BSE SENSEX Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective November 10, 2025. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- Zerodha BSE SENSEX Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available scheme: Zerodha BSE SENSEX Index Fund - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF0R8F01174)
- Category: Equity fund
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/71190, Circular No. 977/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed about the availability of the fund for client transactions. Members should update their systems and inform clients about the reopening of the fund for ongoing transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 07, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members and distributors on NSE MF Invest Platform can now facilitate ongoing transactions in the Zerodha BSE SENSEX Index Fund for their clients.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors interested in this specific index fund, as they can now make ongoing investments, redemptions, and set up systematic transaction facilities through the NSE platform.
