## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that LIC MF Consumption Fund of LIC Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective from November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- LIC MF Consumption Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective November 25, 2025
- All subscription and redemption facilities will be available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six scheme variants covered: Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All schemes fall under Equity category

## Scheme Details

| Scheme Name | ISIN | Category |
|-------------|------|----------|
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Direct IDCW Payout | INF767K01SF5 | Equity |
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Direct IDCW Reinvestment | INF767K01SE8 | Equity |
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Regular IDCW Payout | INF767K01SC2 | Equity |
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Regular IDCW Reinvestment | INF767K01SB4 | Equity |
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Direct Growth | INF767K01SD0 | Equity |
| LIC MF Consumption Fund Regular Growth | INF767K01SA6 | Equity |

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note the availability of these schemes for transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- All transaction facilities (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) to be enabled from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational update affecting only investors in LIC MF Consumption Fund.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard reopening of a mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions. No new regulatory requirements or compliance changes introduced.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for existing and potential investors in LIC MF Consumption Fund who can now resume or initiate transactions through NSE MF Invest Platform with full facility options including systematic investment and withdrawal plans.
