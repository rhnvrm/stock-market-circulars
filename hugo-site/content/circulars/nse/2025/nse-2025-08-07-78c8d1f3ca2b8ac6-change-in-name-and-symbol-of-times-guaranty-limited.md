## Summary

Times Guaranty Limited will undergo a corporate name change to Team India Guaranty Limited, with corresponding symbol change from TIMESGTY to TEAMGTY on NSE, effective August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from Times Guaranty Limited to Team India Guaranty Limited
- Trading symbol changing from TIMESGTY to TEAMGTY
- Changes effective from August 13, 2025
- Administrative notification to all exchange members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is a corporate administrative action for name and symbol modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- All trading and settlement activities should use new symbol TEAMGTY from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Effective date for name and symbol change
- **August 07, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is purely an administrative change. Trading operations will continue normally under the new symbol. Members need to update internal systems and client communications to reflect the new corporate identity.
