## Summary

National Stock Exchange has listed equity shares of Ashwini Container Movers Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective December 19, 2025. The company's shares will trade under symbol ASHWINI with ISIN INE1A6Q01010. Trading will initially be in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') before shifting to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment). The IPO was priced at Rs. 142 per share with face value of Rs. 10.

## Key Points

- Symbol: ASHWINI
- ISIN: INE1A6Q01010
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 142 per share
- Total Securities: 15,000,000 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 1,000 equity shares
- Market Maker: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited
- Market Maker Maximum Permissible Spread: Within 10%
- Initial Trading Series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W')
- Will subsequently shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment - Settlement Type 'N')
- Available in pre-open session for IPO category
- Available for continuous market trading
- Distinctive Number Range: 1 to 15,000,000
- Lock-in applies to certain shares as per Annexure I

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies a new listing in compliance with:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes (1,000 equity shares)
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category
- Security available for continuous market trading
- Market makers must maintain spread within 10%

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 18, 2025
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025
- Financial Year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds a new container moving and logistics company to the EMERGE SME platform. Initial placement in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (Series 'ST') indicates enhanced surveillance measures typical for new SME listings. The company operates in the logistics sector providing container movement services. Limited immediate impact expected beyond participants in the IPO and SME segment traders.
