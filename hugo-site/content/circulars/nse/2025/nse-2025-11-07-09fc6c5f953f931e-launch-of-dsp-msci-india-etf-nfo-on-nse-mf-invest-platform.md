## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India announces the launch of DSP MSCI India ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from November 10, 2025 to November 17, 2025 till 11:45 PM. Both Regular and Direct plan variants under the Equity category will be offered with allotment scheduled for November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- DSP MSCI India ETF NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two plan variants: Regular Plan and Direct Plan (both with same ISIN: INF740KA1WV7)
- NFO transaction period: November 10, 2025 to November 17, 2025 (till 11:45 PM)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Allotment date: November 21, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by November 20, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/71170, Circular No. 972/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 20, 2025 till 11:59 PM
- Members should make the NFO available to investors during the specified transaction window

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: November 10, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: November 17, 2025 (till 11:45 PM)
- **Fund Settlement Deadline**: November 20, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO launch notification. The impact is limited to NSE MF Invest Platform members who will facilitate investor participation in the DSP MSCI India ETF offering. The ETF provides exposure to MSCI India index constituents, offering investors a passive investment vehicle. Standard operational procedures apply for fund collection and allotment processing.
