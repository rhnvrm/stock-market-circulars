## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares issued by 21 companies on the Capital Market segment effective December 09, 2025. The shares were primarily allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will be admitted to trading with specified security codes and lot sizes. All listed securities are pari passu with existing shares and have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 21 companies issuing further equity shares for listing
- Primary issuance mode: Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP)
- Effective listing date: December 09, 2025
- All securities trading with market lot size of 1
- All issues are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable on any of the securities
- Regulation: NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (3.1.1 and 2.5.5)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify securities by designated codes only
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- All securities eligible for trading from December 09, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 09, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from November 12, 2025 to December 04, 2025 (varies by company)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is routine listing of ESOP shares representing dilution from employee compensation programs. The number of shares being listed is relatively small compared to the existing equity base of these companies.

**Trading Impact**: No impact on existing trading operations. All securities will trade with standard market lot size of 1 share.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders experience minor dilution. New shares are fully fungible with existing shares (pari passu) with no restrictions, ensuring market liquidity is not affected.
