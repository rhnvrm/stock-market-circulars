## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Angel One Gold ETF Fund of Fund New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from August 20, 2025, to September 3, 2025, with allotment scheduled for September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Angel One Gold ETF Fund of Fund NFO launching on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan Growth and Direct Plan Growth
- Both plans categorized under Debt category
- ISIN codes: INF1J2R01106 (Regular) and INF1J2R01098 (Direct)
- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received before NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds must be received through any approved payment mode

## Important Dates

- NFO Opening Date: August 20, 2025
- NFO Closing Date: September 3, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The offering provides investors with another investment option in gold ETF fund of funds category through Angel One Mutual Fund. The impact is primarily operational for NSE members facilitating transactions during the NFO period.
