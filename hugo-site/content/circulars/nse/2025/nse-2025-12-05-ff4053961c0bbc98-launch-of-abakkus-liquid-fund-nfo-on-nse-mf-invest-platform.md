## Summary

Abakkus Mutual Fund is launching its Abakkus Liquid Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription period runs from December 08, 2025 to December 10, 2025 (till 11:59 PM), with allotment scheduled for December 12, 2025. The fund offers 10 plan variants across Direct and Regular categories with different dividend options (Growth, Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, and Weekly IDCW).

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: December 08-10, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: December 12, 2025
- Fund house: Abakkus Mutual Fund
- Fund category: Liquid Fund
- Total plan variants: 10 (5 Direct plans and 5 Regular plans)
- Payment deadline: Clear funds must be received by December 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Platform: NSE MF Invest

## Scheme Details

**Direct Plans:**
1. Abakkus Liquid Fund Direct - Growth (ISIN: INF2JJD01029)
2. Abakkus Liquid Fund Direct Daily IDCW - Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01011)

4. Abakkus Liquid Fund Direct Quarterly IDCW - Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01052)
5. Abakkus Liquid Fund Direct Weekly IDCW - Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01045)

**Regular Plans:**

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Standard NFO subscription and payment processing procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opens:** December 08, 2025
- **NFO Closes:** December 10, 2025 (11:59 PM)
