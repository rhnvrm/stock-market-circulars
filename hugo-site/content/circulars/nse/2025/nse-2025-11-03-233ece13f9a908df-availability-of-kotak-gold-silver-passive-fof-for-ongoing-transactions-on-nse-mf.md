## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Kotak Gold Silver Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective November 04, 2025. Both Regular and Direct plan options with Growth variant will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities.

## Key Points

- Kotak Gold Silver Passive FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from November 04, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Facilities include: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XU2) and Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XT4)
- Both plans categorized under Equity category
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/71070, Circular No. 963/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about fund availability.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 04, 2025 - Reopening date for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational announcement affecting only investors interested in gold and silver exposure through fund of funds structure on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investors access to a passive investment vehicle for precious metals allocation through mutual fund route with systematic transaction facilities.
