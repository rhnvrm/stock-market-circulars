## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has admitted units of Quant Money Managers Limited's QSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment Option for trading on the Capital Market segment. The units will commence trading from November 06, 2025, under the symbol QSIFHRR with ISIN INF966L30126.

## Key Points

- **Fund Name**: QSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment Option
- **Trading Symbol**: QSIFHRR
- **ISIN**: INF966L30126
- **Number of Units Listed**: 99,995 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 29, 2025
- **Series**: SF

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new security code QSIFHRR
- The security will be identified by its designated code for trading purposes
- Trading shall be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 29, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of mutual fund units with minimal market impact. The listing provides investors access to trade units of the QSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund on the exchange platform. The fund is managed by Quant Money Managers Limited with KFin Technologies Limited as the registrar and transfer agent.
