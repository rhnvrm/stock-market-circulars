## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced a change in the trading symbol of Addictive Learning Technology Limited. The company's symbol will change from LAWSIKHO to ADDICTIVE effective December 17, 2025. This is an administrative update to the company's ticker symbol on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Current Symbol: LAWSIKHO
- New Symbol: ADDICTIVE
- Company Name: Addictive Learning Technology Limited
- Effective Date: December 17, 2025
- All exchange members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative symbol change only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new symbol ADDICTIVE from December 17, 2025
- All trading and reference to this security should use the new symbol after the effective date
- Market participants should update their watchlists and trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Circular issued by NSE
- **December 17, 2025**: New symbol ADDICTIVE becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The symbol change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or trading mechanics. Investors and traders will need to search for the stock using the new symbol ADDICTIVE instead of LAWSIKHO after December 17, 2025. The change likely reflects a broader rebranding effort by the company from its previous identity as LawSikho to its current corporate name Addictive Learning Technology Limited.
