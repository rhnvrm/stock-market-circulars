## Summary

This addendum circular provides the shareholding pattern for Invicta Diagnostic Limited as part of its SME IPO listing on NSE. The disclosure filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated December 5, 2025, shows total issued capital of 12,571,973 equity shares distributed between promoters (66.70%) and public shareholders (33.30%). The company has confirmed no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, or encumbrances on promoter holdings.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 12,571,973 shares (100% fully paid up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 8,384,920 shares (66.6953%) held by 16 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 4,187,053 shares (33.3047%) held by 533 shareholders
- 100% of promoter shares are in dematerialized form (8,384,920 shares)
- 40.04% of public shares are locked-in (1,676,653 shares)
- Total dematerialized shares: 10,061,573 shares (80.03% of total capital)
- No convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters
- No depository receipts issued against shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure filing pursuant to existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity has submitted shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in promoter shares, pledged shares, or non-disposal undertakings
- All voting rights aligned with equity shareholding (no differential voting rights)
- No significant beneficial owner reported

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Circular Publication Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine disclosure for a newly listed SME company establishing baseline shareholding information.

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure with no unusual features or compliance concerns.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency on ownership structure. Promoters retain comfortable majority at 66.70%, ensuring management stability. Public float of 33.30% meets minimum public shareholding requirements for SME segment. Significant portion of public shares (40%) locked in suggests promoter-aligned stakeholders or anchor investors.
