## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of Power Grid Corporation of India Limited on the capital market segment, effective August 13, 2025. The security is a 6.98% coupon bond maturing in 2035.

## Key Points

- Power Grid Corporation of India Limited bond admitted to NSE capital market dealings
- Security symbol: 698PGCIL35 (Series N0)
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Coupon rate: 6.98% per annum
- Maturity date: August 12, 2035
- ISIN Code: INE752E08791

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security code 698PGCIL35 for system trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot size of 1 unit
- Security identification only through designated codes on trading system

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 12, 2025
- Effective date: August 13, 2025
- Bond maturity: August 12, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of debt securities. Provides additional investment option for institutional and retail investors in government-backed infrastructure bonds with attractive 6.98% coupon rate and 10-year tenure.
