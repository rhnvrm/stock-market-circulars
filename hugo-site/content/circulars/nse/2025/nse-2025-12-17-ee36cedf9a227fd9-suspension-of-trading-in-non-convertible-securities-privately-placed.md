## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible securities (privately placed) of NHPC Limited with ISIN INE848E07AT3, effective December 18, 2025. The suspension is due to the redemption of these securities.

## Key Points

- NHPC Limited's debt security (ISIN: INE848E07AT3) will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective date: December 18, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the securities
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational action pursuant to existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. Trading members should note the suspension and cease trading in the affected ISIN from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date:** December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension related to scheduled redemption of privately placed debt securities. Only holders of this specific ISIN (INE848E07AT3) are affected. The suspension is procedural and expected as part of the normal redemption process for debt instruments.
