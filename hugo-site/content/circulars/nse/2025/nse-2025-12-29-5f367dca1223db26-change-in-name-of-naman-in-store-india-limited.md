## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular informing members about the name change of Naman In-Store (India) Limited. The company will be renamed to Naman Industries Proxima Limited, with the change becoming effective from January 02, 2026. The stock symbol NAMAN will remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Naman In-Store (India) Limited" to "Naman Industries Proxima Limited"
- Stock symbol remains: NAMAN
- Effective date: January 02, 2026
- Circular issued by Listing Department
- NSE Circular Reference: 2527/2025
- Download Reference: NSE/CML/72030

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting a corporate name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are required to note the name change in their systems and records. No specific compliance actions are mandated for members beyond updating company identification information.

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Circular issued
- **January 02, 2026**: Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Trading symbol (NAMAN remains unchanged)
- Stock holdings or valuations
- Trading operations
- Investor rights or obligations

Investors and market participants should update their records to reflect the new company name from January 02, 2026 onwards.
