## Summary

NSE announces the listing of six debt securities from Godrej group companies on the capital market segment. The securities include bonds from Godrej Seeds & Genetics Limited (4 series) and Godrej Finance Limited (2 series) with coupon rates of 7.68% and 7.5% respectively.

## Key Points

- Six new debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Securities from Godrej Seeds & Genetics Limited: 4 series with 7.68% coupon rate
- Securities from Godrej Finance Limited: 2 series with 7.5% coupon rate
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading to be identified by designated security codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing process under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading shall be conducted using designated security codes only
- Securities must be traded in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**: 
  - GSGL securities: January 28, 2028 to October 27, 2028
  - GFL securities: September 29, 2028 and July 29, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options for institutional investors in Godrej group debt instruments with competitive coupon rates ranging from 7.5% to 7.68%.
