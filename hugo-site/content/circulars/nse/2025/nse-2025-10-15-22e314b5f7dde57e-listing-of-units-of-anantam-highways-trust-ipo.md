## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted Units of Anantam Highways Trust to trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 16, 2025. The InvIT issued 217.5 million units at Rs. 100/- per unit with ISIN INE1UA823019. The units will participate in a 60-minute pre-open session from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM before normal trading. Lock-in provisions apply to various unit holder categories with periods ranging from free tradability to lock-in until October 2028.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: ANANTAM
- **Security Type**: Units of InvIT Rs. 100/- each fully paid up
- **ISIN**: INE1UA823019
- **Series**: IV
- **Total Units Listed**: 217,500,000 units
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100/- per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Commencement**: October 16, 2025
- **Pre-open Session**: 60 minutes (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM)
  - Order entry/modification: 45 minutes
  - Order matching: 10 minutes
  - Buffer period: 5 minutes

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the InvIT segment on NSE with a highways infrastructure trust. The Rs. 2,175 crore issue (at face value) provides retail and institutional investors access to infrastructure investment opportunities. The staggered lock-in structure ensures orderly market development while the pre-open session mechanism will facilitate price discovery on the first trading day. Market participants should note the specific trading protocols for InvIT units including the extended pre-open session format.
