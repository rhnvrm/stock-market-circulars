## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of KSH International Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol KSHINTL with ISIN INE987S01020. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: KSH International Limited
- Trading Symbol: KSHINTL
- ISIN: INE987S01020
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued by Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members of the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 22, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact as it introduces a new tradeable security to the NSE platform. Market participants and investors can prepare for trading opportunities in KSH International Limited once the listing date is announced. The announcement provides advance notice to members to update their systems and inform clients about the new listing.
