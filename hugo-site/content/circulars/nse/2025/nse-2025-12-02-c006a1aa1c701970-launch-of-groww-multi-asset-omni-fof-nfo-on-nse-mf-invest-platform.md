## Summary

National Stock Exchange announces the launch of Groww Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds (FOF) NFO by Groww Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from December 03, 2025 to December 17, 2025 till 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for December 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available: 3 Direct plans and 3 Regular plans (Growth and IDCW options)
- NFO subscription period: December 03, 2025 to December 17, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: December 24, 2025
- All schemes categorized as Equity
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by December 19, 2025 (07:00 PM)

| Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF-Regular-IDCW Reinvestment | INF666M01NH2 | Equity |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 19, 2025 at 07:00 PM.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: December 03, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: December 17, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Payment Deadline**: December 19, 2025 (07:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new multi-asset fund of funds option through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The launch expands investment opportunities but does not affect trading operations or existing compliance frameworks. Impact is limited to members participating in mutual fund distribution through NSE platform.
