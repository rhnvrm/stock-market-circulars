## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of equity shares of Orkla India Limited on the Capital Market segment effective November 06, 2025. The company issued 136,989,230 equity shares of Re. 1/- face value each at an issue price of Rs. 730 per share. The stock will trade under the symbol ORKLAINDIA with ISIN INE16NZ01023 and a market lot size of 1 share.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: ORKLAINDIA
- ISIN: INE16NZ01023
- Face Value: Re. 1/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 730 per share
- Total Shares Issued: 136,989,230 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Pari Passu: Yes
- The security will be part of pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Lock-in Details

The issued shares have varying lock-in periods:

- 86,748,366 shares: Locked until May 07, 2026
- 27,397,860 shares: Locked until May 07, 2027
- 3,421,950 shares: Locked until December 02, 2025
- 3,421,950 shares: Locked until January 31, 2026
- 15,999,104 shares: Free (no lock-in)

## Regulatory Compliance

- Listed pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes notified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new equity security to NSE's Capital Market segment. The IPO pricing at Rs. 730 per share with a face value of Re. 1 represents a significant premium. The majority of shares (approximately 63%) are subject to lock-in periods ranging from 2 months to 2 years, which will limit immediate trading float. The stock will be available for trading by all NSE members from November 06, 2025 and will participate in the pre-open session designed for IPO securities.
