## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited on the Capital Market segment effective December 19, 2025. The company issued 494,258,520 equity shares of Re. 1/- face value at an issue price of Rs. 2165 per share. The security will trade under the symbol 'ICICIAMC' with ISIN INE346A01027 and will be part of the pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- Total securities listed: 494,258,520 equity shares of Re. 1/- each fully paid up
- Trading symbol: ICICIAMC
- ISIN: INE346A01027
- Issue price: Rs. 2165 per share
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category
- Shares are pari passu with existing shares

## Lock-in Details

- 343,939,593 shares locked in until June 18, 2026
- 2,494,227 shares locked in until June 9, 2026
- 98,851,706 shares locked in until June 18, 2027
- 6,978,651 shares locked in until January 15, 2026
- 6,978,652 shares locked in until March 16, 2026
- 35,015,691 shares are free from lock-in

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (ICICIAMC) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot: 1)
- Security will participate in pre-open session as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Members must comply with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The listing adds a significant asset management company to the exchange, providing investors access to a leading mutual fund player in India. With an issue size of over 494 million shares at Rs 2165, this represents a substantial public offering in the financial services sector.

**Trading Impact:** The security will be available for trading from December 19, 2025, with approximately 70% of shares under various lock-in periods ranging from 9 months to 30 months, which may impact liquidity in the initial trading period. Only 35 million shares (approximately 7% of total) are immediately free for trading.

**Investor Impact:** Retail and institutional investors can now trade in ICICI Prudential AMC shares on the exchange. The company operates in the asset management business, managing mutual funds and other investment products.
