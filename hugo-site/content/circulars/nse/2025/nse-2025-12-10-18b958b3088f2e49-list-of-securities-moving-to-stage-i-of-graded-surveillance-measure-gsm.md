## Summary

NSE has announced that NOIDA TOLL BRIDGE COMPANY LIMITED (Symbol: NOIDATOLL, ISIN: INE781B01015) will be moved to Stage I of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective December 11, 2025. This action is part of NSE's ongoing surveillance measures to monitor securities exhibiting specific characteristics that warrant enhanced scrutiny.

## Key Points

- NOIDA TOLL BRIDGE COMPANY LIMITED (NOIDATOLL) moved to GSM Stage I
- Effective date: December 11, 2025
- Applicable margin rate: 100% of trade value
- Price band restriction: 5% or lower as applicable
- Members advised to take adequate precaution while trading
- Circular reference: NSE/SURV/71726, Circular No. 956/2025

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements Stage I surveillance actions under the GSM framework for the identified security. The GSM framework has four progressive stages (I through IV), each with increasingly stringent trading restrictions. Stage I is the initial level of enhanced surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Apply 100% margin requirement on all trades in NOIDATOLL
- Enforce 5% price band or lower as applicable
- Exercise adequate precaution when executing trades in this security
- Ensure clients are aware of the enhanced surveillance status

**For Investors:**
- Full margin (100% of trade value) required upfront for trading
- Price movements limited to 5% per day

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Reduced liquidity in NOIDATOLL due to higher margin requirements
- Limited intraday price volatility due to 5% price band
- Potential reduction in speculative trading activity

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading members must update margin and price band parameters
- Risk management systems need adjustment for the affected security
- Increased capital requirements for traders holding positions

**GSM Framework Overview:**
The circular includes an annexure detailing all four stages of GSM:
- **Stage I:** 100% margin, 5% price band
- **Stage II:** Trade-for-trade, 50% Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD), 5% price band
- **Stage III:** Trade-for-trade, 100% ASD, weekly trading only (Mondays), 5% price band
- **Stage IV:** Trade-for-trade, 100% ASD, weekly trading only, 5% price band, no upward movement

Members can direct queries to surveillance@nse.co.in or refer to NSE's GSM FAQs at https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/graded-surveillance-measure
