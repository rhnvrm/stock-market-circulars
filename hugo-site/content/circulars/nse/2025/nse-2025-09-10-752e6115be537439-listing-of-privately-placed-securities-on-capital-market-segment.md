## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of privately placed securities by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited on the capital market segment. The AAFS Market Linked Series Q security will commence trading from September 11, 2025, with designated symbol AAFS30.

## Key Points

- New security listing: AAFS Market Linked Sr Q by Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited
- Trading symbol: AAFS30, Series N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE0L6807245
- Maturity date: January 22, 2030
- No coupon rate specified (market linked structure)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security code AAFS30 for system identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 1 unit
- All trading regulations under NSE Capital Market segment apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 11, 2025 - Trading commencement
- **Maturity Date**: January 22, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves a single privately placed security by a financial services company. The listing expands available investment options in market-linked instruments but represents routine capital market activity with limited broader implications.
