## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 commercial papers from 7 companies to trading on the Debt Market Segment with effect from November 19, 2025. All securities were allotted on November 18, 2025, with redemption dates ranging from February 2026 to November 2026. Total value of securities listed is ₹4,305 crore (86,100 units at ₹5 lakh face value each).

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers listed across 7 issuers
- All allotments dated November 18, 2025
- Face value: ₹5,00,000 per unit for all securities
- Maturity periods: 87 days to 365 days
- Total quantity: 86,100 securities worth ₹4,305 crore
- Issue prices range from ₹4,68,246.50 to ₹4,92,626 per unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Standard debt market trading rules apply to listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025 (effective date for trading)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - February 13, 2026: TTEL CP
  - February 17, 2026: GIL CP, HSL CP, LTFL CP
  - February 27, 2026: HSL CP
  - March 18, 2026: AIFL CP
  - April 27, 2026: SFL CP
  - November 17, 2026: TTML CP, TTEL CP
  - November 18, 2026: SFL CP

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing provides liquidity options for investors holding these commercial papers. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Relevant only to institutional investors and debt market participants trading in short-term corporate debt instruments.
