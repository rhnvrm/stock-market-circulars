## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of further equity shares of Capital Trust Limited (Symbol: CAPTRUST) on the Capital Market segment. The listing pertains to 17,009,702 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under Rights Issue on November 12, 2025, with trading commencing from November 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Capital Trust Limited (Symbol: CAPTRUST)
- **Security Type**: Equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value
- **ISIN**: INE707C01018 (Series: EQ)
- **Number of Securities**: 17,009,702 shares
- **Issue Type**: Rights Issue
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 14 per share
- **Paid-up Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 17011703 to 34021404
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Pari Passu**: Yes (ranking equally with existing shares)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Lock-in**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - for admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - for specification of lot sizes

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security by its designated symbol code: **CAPTRUST**
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of **1 share**
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in dematerialized mode as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) regarding activation of ISIN for additional issues

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: November 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing notification for rights issue shares of Capital Trust Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can commence trading in the newly listed shares from November 14, 2025 using symbol CAPTRUST with market lot of 1 share.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their allotted shares available for trading. The shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions, providing immediate liquidity to rights issue subscribers.
