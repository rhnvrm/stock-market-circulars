## Summary

NSE has announced that Johnson Controls - Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited will undergo a name and trading symbol change effective December 24, 2025. The company will be renamed to BOSCH HOME COMFORT INDIA LIMITED and its trading symbol will change from JCHAC to BOSCH-HCIL.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol: JCHAC
- New symbol: BOSCH-HCIL
- Existing name: Johnson Controls - Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited
- New name: BOSCH HOME COMFORT INDIA LIMITED
- Change is effective from December 24, 2025
- All exchange members have been notified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action resulting in administrative changes to the company's listing details on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- All trading and settlement systems should be updated before the effective date of December 24, 2025
- Members should ensure proper communication to clients regarding the symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name and symbol change will affect all market participants trading this security. Trading systems, watchlists, and reporting systems will need to be updated.

**Operational Impact**: Members need to update internal systems, databases, and client communications to reflect the new symbol (BOSCH-HCIL) and company name. Investors holding positions should be aware that their holdings will automatically transition to the new symbol.

**Business Continuity**: No disruption to trading is expected. The change is administrative in nature and represents a corporate rebranding, likely reflecting ownership or strategic changes in the company's business focus.
