## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced the listing of equity shares of Shilchar Technologies Limited (Symbol: SHILCTECH) on the Capital Market segment. The securities will be admitted to trading with effect from November 24, 2025, in the Normal Market segment under compulsory demat mode for all investors.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: SHILCTECH
- ISIN: INE024F01011
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Total Number of Securities: 11,440,200 equity shares
- Distinctive Number Range: 1 to 11,440,200
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading Mode: Compulsory demat for all investors
- Market Segment: Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Lock-in: NA (No lock-in)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (SHILCTECH) for trading purposes
- All trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- Compulsory demat requirement applies to all investors

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: November 20, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 24, 2025
- Financial Year: April 1 to March 31
