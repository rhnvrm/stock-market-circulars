## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Fabtech Technologies Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol FABTECH with ISIN INE0HF201011. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Fabtech Technologies Limited
- Trading Symbol: FABTECH
- ISIN: INE0HF201011
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70624 (1922/2025)
- Listing date to be announced separately

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 06, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

This is a routine IPO listing announcement with minimal immediate impact on existing market operations. The listing will add a new equity instrument to the NSE platform. Members should note the new symbol and ISIN for trading purposes once the listing date is announced.
