## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective November 10, 2025. The IPO consists of 1,734,870,187 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each, issued at Rs. 402/- per share. The security will trade under symbol 'LENSKART' with ISIN INE956O01016 and will be part of pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: LENSKART
- **ISIN**: INE956O01016
- **Face Value**: Rs. 2/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 402/- per share
- **Total Shares**: 1,734,870,187 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Trading Start Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Pari Passu**: Yes

## Regulatory Framework

- Listing pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specification under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Pre-open session applicability as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Lenskart Solutions Limited adds a significant eyewear retail company to NSE. With an issue size of over 1.73 billion shares at Rs. 402 per share, this represents a substantial IPO. The staggered lock-in periods indicate a mix of promoter holdings, pre-IPO investors, and public float.
