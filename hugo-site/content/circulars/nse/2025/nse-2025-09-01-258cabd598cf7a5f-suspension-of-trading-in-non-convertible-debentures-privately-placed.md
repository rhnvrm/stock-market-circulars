## Summary

NSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Power Finance Corporation Limited (ISIN: INE134E08HX3) effective September 2, 2025, due to redemption of the securities.

## Key Points

- Single NCD security affected: Power Finance Corporation Limited
- ISIN: INE134E08HX3
- Suspension effective from September 2, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Standard suspension procedure under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified NCD from the suspension date
- Comply with NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **September 2, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only one privately placed debt security. Normal course redemption process with minimal disruption to broader debt market operations.
