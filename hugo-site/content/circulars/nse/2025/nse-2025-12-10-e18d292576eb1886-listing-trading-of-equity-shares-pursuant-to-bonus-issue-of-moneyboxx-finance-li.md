## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular notifying the listing and admission to trading of equity shares arising from the bonus issue of Moneyboxx Finance Limited. The bonus shares will be listed and available for trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 17, 2025. A total of 3,27,04,600 bonus equity shares with ISIN INE296Q01012 will be credited to eligible shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company: Moneyboxx Finance Limited (Symbol: MONEYBOXX)
- Number of bonus shares: 3,27,04,600
- ISIN: INE296Q01012
- Record date: December 15, 2025
- Deemed date of allotment: December 16, 2025
- Trading commencement date: December 17, 2025
- Distinctive number range: 32704601 to 65409200
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/71740, Circular No. 2405/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which governs the listing of securities on NSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes (Symbol: MONEYBOXX, ISIN: INE296Q01012) for trading the bonus shares on the system
- Securities will be identified only by their designated codes for trading purposes
- Members must be aware that trading in these bonus shares will commence from December 17, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 15, 2025**: Record date for bonus issue eligibility
- **December 16, 2025**: Deemed date of allotment of bonus shares
- **December 17, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date (circular effective date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action specific to Moneyboxx Finance Limited. The bonus issue increases the number of outstanding shares but does not change the overall market capitalization or ownership structure of the company.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to be aware of the increased share count and ensure their systems reflect the correct security details. The bonus shares will be automatically credited to eligible shareholders' demat accounts.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders holding shares as of the record date (December 15, 2025) will receive bonus shares in their demat accounts. The bonus ratio is not explicitly mentioned in this circular but can be calculated as approximately 1:1 based on the distinctive number range.
