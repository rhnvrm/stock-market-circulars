## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Old Bridge Arbitrage Fund NFO (New Fund Offer) by Old Bridge Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from November 06, 2025 to November 10, 2025, with allotment scheduled for November 13, 2025. Six scheme options are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment variants.

## Key Points

- Old Bridge Arbitrage Fund NFO launching on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme options available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as Equity with unique ISINs assigned
- NFO subscription period: November 06-10, 2025
- Allotment date: November 13, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in Clearing Corporation account by November 12, 2025 till 01:00 PM
- Contact support available at 1800 2100 940 or support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account
- Payment deadline: November 12, 2025 by 01:00 PM
- Members should make the NFO available for transactions during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opens**: November 06, 2025
- **NFO Closes**: November 10, 2025
- **Payment Deadline**: November 12, 2025 by 01:00 PM
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO launch announcement. It provides investment opportunities for investors interested in arbitrage strategies through Old Bridge Mutual Fund. The launch does not affect existing securities, trading operations, or compliance requirements for market participants beyond standard NFO processing procedures. Members need to facilitate transactions during the specified period and ensure timely fund settlement.
