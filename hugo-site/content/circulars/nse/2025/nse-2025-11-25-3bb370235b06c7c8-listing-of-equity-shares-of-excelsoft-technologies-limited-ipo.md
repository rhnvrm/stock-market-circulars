## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has informed its members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Excelsoft Technologies Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol EXCELSOFT with ISIN INE606N01019. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Excelsoft Technologies Limited
- Trading Symbol: EXCELSOFT
- ISIN: INE606N01019
- Listing Date: To be announced separately
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71447, dated November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular regarding a new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The addition of one new equity security to the NSE does not affect existing market operations or trading rules. Market participants should note the new symbol EXCELSOFT for trading purposes once the listing date is announced.
