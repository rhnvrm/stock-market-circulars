## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of Knowledge Realty Trust REIT units on the Capital Market segment with effect from August 18, 2025. The REIT will trade under symbol "KRT" with ISIN INE1JAR25012, featuring 4,434,399,541 units at Rs. 100 per unit issue price.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: KRT (Knowledge Realty Trust)
- ISIN: INE1JAR25012
- Total units: 4,434,399,541
- Issue price: Rs. 100 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Trading effective from: August 18, 2025
- Will be part of pre-open session for IPO category

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes introduced. Standard REIT listing procedures applied under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- REIT will participate in pre-open session as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Important Dates

- Circular effective date: August 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 18, 2025
- Lock-in periods vary: ranging from September 11, 2025 to August 17, 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new REIT investment option to the market, managed by Knowledge Realty Office Management Services Private Limited. With sponsors including Sattva Developers and BREP Asia, this provides institutional-grade real estate exposure to retail investors. The significant unit count (4.4+ billion) indicates substantial asset base backing the trust.
