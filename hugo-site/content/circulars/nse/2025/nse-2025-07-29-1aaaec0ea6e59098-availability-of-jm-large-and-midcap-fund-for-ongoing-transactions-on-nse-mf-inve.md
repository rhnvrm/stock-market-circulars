## Summary

NSE announces that JM Large and Midcap Fund of JM Financial Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facility on NSE MF Invest Platform effective from July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- JM Large and Midcap Fund reopening for all transaction types
- Effective date: July 30, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six variants of the fund available (Direct/Regular with IDCW Payout/Reinvestment/Growth options)
- All variants categorized as Equity funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational announcement about fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can resume transactions in the specified mutual fund schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **July 30, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact - routine operational matter enabling investor access to JM Financial's Large and Midcap Fund through NSE's mutual fund platform. Provides additional investment options for platform users.
