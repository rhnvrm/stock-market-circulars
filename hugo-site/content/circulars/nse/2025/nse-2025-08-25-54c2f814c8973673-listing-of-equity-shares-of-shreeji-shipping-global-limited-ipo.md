## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Shreeji Shipping Global Limited following completion of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shares will commence trading on August 26, 2025 under the symbol SHREEJISPG with ISIN INE1B6101010.

## Key Points

- Company: Shreeji Shipping Global Limited
- Trading Symbol: SHREEJISPG
- ISIN: INE1B6101010
- Face Value: Rs. 10/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 252/-
- Total Shares: 162,918,254 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)

## Trading Information

- Shares will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Trading regulations as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Security identification by designated codes only

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new shipping company to NSE's equity segment, providing investors access to the maritime logistics sector. With an issue price of Rs. 252 and significant lock-in periods for promoter holdings, the stock may see initial volatility as free float is limited to approximately 11.4 million shares.
