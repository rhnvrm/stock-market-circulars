## Summary

Sree Metaliks Limited is conducting a takeover offer for SAL Steel Limited through NSE's web-based bidding platform. The offer allows acquisition of up to 3,76,39,342 equity shares at a fixed price of Rs. 25 per share. The bidding period runs from November 28, 2025 to December 11, 2025, with specific timing windows for bid submission and custodial confirmation.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer**: Sree Metaliks Limited
- **Target Company**: SAL Steel Limited (Symbol: SALSTEEL)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 3,76,39,342 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 25 per share (fixed price)
- **Face Value**: Re. 10 per share
- **Lot Size**: 1 equity share
- **ISIN**: INE658G01014
- **Merchant Banker**: Vivro Financial Services Private Limited
- **Registrar**: KFin Technologies Limited
- **Platform**: NSE web-based bidding platform at https://eipo.nseindia.com
- **Order Restrictions**: Order modification (quantity) and cancellation NOT permitted

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This offer follows the existing Tender Offer Scheme as per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members:
- All Capital Market segment trading members are eligible to participate
- Members with existing web-based IPO bidding platform access can use their current user IDs
- New members must log into admin terminal (User ID: Adminxxxxx where xxxxx is Member Code) to create branches and users
- Members must facilitate bidding for eligible shareholders during the offer period

### For Investors:
- Eight investor categories eligible: FII, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Banks & Financial Institutions, Other QIBs, Bodies Corporate, Individuals, and Other Non-Institutional Investors
- Each category has designated status codes for bidding
- Custodial confirmation required during specified time windows

## Important Dates

- **Issue Period**: November 28, 2025 to December 11, 2025
- **Bidding Time**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- **Custodial Confirmation Time**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- **Custodial Confirmation on Issue Closure Day**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (December 11, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

### Direct Impact:
- SAL Steel Limited shareholders have opportunity to tender shares at Rs. 25 per share
- Significant premium or discount depends on current market price (not disclosed in circular)
- Potential change in ownership structure of SAL Steel Limited

### Operational Impact:
- Trading members need to ensure platform access and user credentials are configured
- Members must support investor participation during the 14-day offer window
- No order modification flexibility requires investors to be certain before bidding

### Market Impact:
- Limited to SAL Steel Limited shareholders and the acquirer
- No broader market implications beyond this specific corporate action
