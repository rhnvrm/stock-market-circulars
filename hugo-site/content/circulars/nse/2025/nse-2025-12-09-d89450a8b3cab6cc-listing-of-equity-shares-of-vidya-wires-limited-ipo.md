## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Vidya Wires Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol VIDYAWIRES and ISIN INE14UN01029. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Vidya Wires Limited
- Trading Symbol: VIDYAWIRES
- ISIN: INE14UN01029
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued by Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are informed to note the security details for trading purposes once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 09, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing adds a new equity security to the NSE platform. Trading members should update their systems with the new security details. Actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile, IPO size, and market conditions at the time of listing.
