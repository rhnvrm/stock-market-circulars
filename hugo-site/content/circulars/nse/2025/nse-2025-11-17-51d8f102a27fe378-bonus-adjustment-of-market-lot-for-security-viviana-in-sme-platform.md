## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of market lot for VIVIANA POWER TECH LIMITED (Symbol: VIVIANA) listed on the SME platform following a bonus issue. The market lot will be adjusted to 200 shares with an adjustment factor of 1.6, corresponding to a 3:5 bonus ratio, effective November 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security Symbol: VIVIANA
- Company Name: VIVIANA POWER TECH LIMITED
- Corporate Action Type: Bonus Issue
- Bonus Ratio: 3:5 (3 new shares for every 5 existing shares)
- Adjustment Factor: 1.6
- New Market Lot: 200 shares
- Ex-date: November 19, 2025
- Record Date: November 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard market lot adjustment following corporate action procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII - Security File.gz file in their trading application before trading on November 19, 2025
- Updated files can be obtained from the directory common/NTNEAT on the Extranet server
- MII - Security File.gz is also available on NSE website at: https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Ex-date and Record Date for bonus issue
- **November 19, 2025**: Adjusted market lot becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The market lot adjustment affects trading in VIVIANA shares on the SME platform. The 3:5 bonus will increase share count by 60% (adjustment factor 1.6), requiring traders to adjust their lot sizes accordingly.

**Operational Impact**: Members must update their trading systems before the effective date to ensure proper order execution with the new market lot of 200 shares.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive 3 additional shares for every 5 shares held, increasing liquidity but diluting share price proportionally.
