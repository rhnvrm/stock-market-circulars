## Summary

NSE has approved the listing and trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by Laxmi Goldorna House Limited (Symbol: LGHL). A total of 2,92,20,660 new equity shares with ISIN INE258Y01016 will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective October 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Laxmi Goldorna House Limited (Symbol: LGHL)
- ISIN: INE258Y01016
- Number of bonus shares: 2,92,20,660
- Distinctive Number Range: 20871901 to 50092560
- Trading effective date: October 14, 2025
- Securities to be identified by designated codes for trading on the system

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A for admission of securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. The securities will be available for trading using the designated security codes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 09, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 13, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date**: October 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard corporate action affecting only shareholders of Laxmi Goldorna House Limited. The bonus issue increases the number of outstanding shares but does not change the overall market capitalization of the company.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Members need to be aware of the new shares available for trading from October 14, 2025. The securities will be identified by their designated codes in the trading system.
