## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in partly paid-up equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Symbol: FUSIONPP, ISIN: IN9139R01028) effective from November 11, 2025. The suspension is being implemented on account of first and final call on these partly paid-up shares, as per NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Key Points

- Trading in FUSIONPP (partly paid-up equity shares) will be suspended from November 11, 2025
- Suspension takes effect at closing hours of trading on November 10, 2025
- Reason for suspension: First and final call on partly paid-up shares
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- ISIN of suspended security: IN9139R01028

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements a trading suspension in accordance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2, which governs the suspension of securities for specific corporate actions including calls on partly paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must ensure no trading occurs in FUSIONPP partly paid-up shares from November 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension status of the security
- Investors holding partly paid-up shares should respond to the first and final call as per company notifications

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 10, 2025**: Last day of trading (suspension effective at closing hours)
- **November 11, 2025**: Suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a high impact on investors holding partly paid-up equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited. Once suspended, these shares cannot be traded on the NSE until further notice. Investors must comply with the company's first and final call payment requirements. The suspension is a standard regulatory measure when companies make final calls on partly paid-up shares, ensuring orderly settlement of such corporate actions.
