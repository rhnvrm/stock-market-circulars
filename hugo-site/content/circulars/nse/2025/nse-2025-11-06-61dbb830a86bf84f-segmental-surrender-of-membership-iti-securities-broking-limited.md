## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has approved the segmental surrender of membership by ITI Securities Broking Limited (SEBI Registration: INZ000005835). The broker has surrendered its membership in the Currency (including IRF) and Derivatives segments with effect from November 03, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: ITI Securities Broking Limited
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000005835
- Surrendered Segments: Currency (including IRF) and Derivatives
- Approval Date: November 03, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/71141, Circular No. 94/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding a member's voluntary surrender of trading membership in specific segments.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership change. Members should update their records regarding ITI Securities Broking Limited's reduced segment participation.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 06, 2025
- **Surrender Approval Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 03, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a single broker's voluntary decision to exit Currency and Derivatives segments. No impact on market operations or other trading members. ITI Securities Broking Limited may continue operations in other segments not mentioned in the surrender.
