## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025. Six scheme variants are being offered covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW, and IDCW Reinvestment options. All variants are categorized as Equity funds with allotment scheduled for December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Allotment date: December 11, 2025
- Fund house: DSP Mutual Fund
- Benchmark: Nifty Smallcap 250 Index
- Clear funds deadline: December 10, 2025 till 11:59 pm in designated Clearing Corporation account

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plans
1. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Regular Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XE1)
2. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Regular IDCW (ISIN: INF740KA1XF8)
3. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Regular IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XG6)

### Direct Plans
4. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XH4)
5. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Direct IDCW (ISIN: INF740KA1XI2)
6. DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund - Direct IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XJ0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 10, 2025 till 11:59 pm.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date:** November 24, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date:** December 08, 2025
- **Funds Payment Deadline:** December 10, 2025 (11:59 pm)
- **Allotment Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard NFO launch notification with minimal market impact. The circular provides operational information for members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. As a passive index fund tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index, it offers investors exposure to the small-cap segment of the Indian equity market. The impact is limited to DSP Mutual Fund distributors and investors interested in smallcap index funds.
