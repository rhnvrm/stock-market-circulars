## Summary

NSE has admitted 600,000 units of Motilal Oswal Gold ETF (symbol: MOGOLD) to trading on the Capital Market segment effective July 31, 2025. The ETF units have a face value of Rs. 10/- each with an issue price of Rs. 98.05.

## Key Points

- 600,000 units of Motilal Oswal Gold ETF listed on NSE
- Trading symbol: MOGOLD
- ISIN: INF247L01FY4
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 98.05 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: July 28, 2025

## Market Makers

- East India (TM code: 8134)
- MOFSL (TM code: 10412)
