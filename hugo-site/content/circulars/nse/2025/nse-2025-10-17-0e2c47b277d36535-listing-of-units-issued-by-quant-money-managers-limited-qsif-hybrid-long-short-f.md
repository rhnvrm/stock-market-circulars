## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of units issued by Quant Money Managers Limited for qsif Hybrid Long-Short Fund on the Capital Market segment. Two schemes are being listed: Direct Plan - Growth Option (QSIFHDG) and Regular Plan - Growth Option (QSIFHRG), both effective from October 20, 2025. The units were allotted on October 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two schemes listed: Direct Plan (QSIFHDG) with 12,299,385 units and Regular Plan (QSIFHRG) with 1,049,947 units
- Face value, paid-up value, and issue price: Rs. 10/- per unit for both schemes
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both schemes
- Series: SF (Securities/Funds)
- ISIN codes: INF966L30076 (Direct Plan), INF966L30084 (Regular Plan)
- Date of allotment: October 15, 2025
- Registrar: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes (QSIFHDG and QSIFHRG) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (1 unit)
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** October 15, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to specific mutual fund units. The listing provides investors access to trade qsif Hybrid Long-Short Fund units on NSE's Capital Market segment. The hybrid long-short strategy indicates the fund employs both long and short positions, which may appeal to investors seeking market-neutral or absolute return strategies. Standard market lot of 1 unit ensures accessibility for retail investors.
