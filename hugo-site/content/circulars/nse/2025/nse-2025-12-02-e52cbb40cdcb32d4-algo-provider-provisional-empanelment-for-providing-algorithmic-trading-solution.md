## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has provisionally empanelled M/s. Quantiply Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members. The company will provide Whitebox type algorithmic solutions. This empanelment is effective immediately from December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantiply Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an Algo Provider
- Type of algorithmic solution offered: Whitebox
- Office location: Plot 344, Dharmashi Bhuvan, 4th Floor, Chandavarkar Cross Road, Matunga (E), Mumbai- 400019
- Contact number: +919920266899
- Effective date: Immediate effect from December 02, 2025
- Complete list of empanelled Algo Providers available on NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification about vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational for members who may wish to engage with the newly empanelled Algo Provider for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative empanelment that expands the options available to trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members looking for Whitebox algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. The complete list of empanelled providers is maintained at https://www.nseindia.com/static/trade/empanelled-algo-providers-exchange.

**Contact Information**: For queries, trading members can contact NSE at toll-free number 1800-266-0050 (Option 1) or via email at nnfreg@nse.co.in or invg@nse.co.in.
