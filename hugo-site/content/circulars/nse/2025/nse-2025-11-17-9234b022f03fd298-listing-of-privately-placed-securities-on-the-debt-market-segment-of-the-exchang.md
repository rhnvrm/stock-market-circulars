## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to the Debt Market Segment with effect from November 17, 2025. The circular lists 9 commercial papers and 1 debt security from various issuers including financial institutions and corporates, with redemption dates ranging from November 2025 to June 2026.

## Key Points

- 9 commercial papers listed from companies including Bajaj Auto Credit Limited, Export Import Bank of India, Afcons Infrastructure Limited, ICICI Securities Limited, Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited, L&T Finance Limited, Nuvoco Vistas Corporation Limited, and Kotak Securities Limited
- 1 debt security listed from National Bank For Financing Infrastructure And Development (6.86% 2030 Tr 1)
- All securities allotted on November 14-17, 2025
- Face value for commercial papers is Rs 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue prices range from Rs 4,80,754.5 to Rs 4,98,756.5 for commercial papers
- Shortest maturity: Nuvoco Vistas CP maturing November 28, 2025 (14 days)
- Longest maturity: ICICI Securities CP maturing June 15, 2026 (7 months)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members are notified of the newly listed securities and their designated security codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: November 14-17, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - November 28, 2025: Nuvoco Vistas Corporation Limited CP
  - February 13, 2026: Bajaj Auto Credit, EXIM Bank, Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services, L&T Finance CPs
  - February 16, 2026: Kotak Securities Limited CP
  - March 2, 2026: ICICI Securities Limited CP
  - March 25, 2026: Afcons Infrastructure Limited CP
  - June 15, 2026: ICICI Securities Limited CP

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing notification that provides additional debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt market segment.

**Operational Impact**: Exchange members can now trade these securities using the designated security codes and ISINs provided in the annexure.

**Investor Impact**: Adds liquidity options for institutional investors seeking short-term debt instruments with varying maturities from 14 days to 7 months.
