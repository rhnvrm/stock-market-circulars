## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of Aaradhya Disposal Industries Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 11, 2025. The company will trade under symbol 'AARADHYA' in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with Settlement Type 'W' before moving to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: AARADHYA
- ISIN: INE124401014
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 116 per share
- Market lot: 1200 equity shares
- Total securities: 14,138,000 shares
- Market maker spread: within 10%

## Regulatory Changes

- Initial trading in Series 'ST' (TFTS - Settlement Type 'W')
- Subsequently will shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment - Settlement Type 'N')
- Part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Available for continuous market trading

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading only in specified lot sizes (1200 shares)
- Lock-in provisions apply as per Annexure I
- Market maker obligations through Prabhat Financial Services Limited
