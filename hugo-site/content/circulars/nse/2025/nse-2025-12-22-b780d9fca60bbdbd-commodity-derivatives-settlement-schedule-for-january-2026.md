## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivative Segment for January 2026. The schedule covers 21 trading days with corresponding settlement dates following a T+1 settlement cycle. The circular is issued by the Commodity Derivatives Segment department.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- Standard T+1 settlement cycle applies (settlement occurs one business day after trade date)
- Total of 21 trading days scheduled for January 2026
- Circular reference number: 403/2025
- NCL download reference: NCL/COM/71931
- Support contact: 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or support@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members trading in commodity derivatives should note the settlement dates for January 2026 and ensure adequate arrangements for settlements on specified dates. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal settlement obligations.

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: Trade date, settles January 2, 2026
- **January 2, 2026**: Trade date, settles January 5, 2026
- **January 26, 2026**: Republic Day - No trading scheduled
- **January 30, 2026**: Trade date, settles February 2, 2026
- **Trading days**: 21 days in January 2026

Note: Settlement follows T+1 cycle throughout the month with weekend and holiday adjustments.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational schedule that confirms standard settlement practices.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members should update their internal calendars and settlement systems with these dates. The schedule follows the standard T+1 settlement cycle with no deviations from normal operations.

**Member Action Required**: Informational only - members should note the settlement schedule for planning purposes and ensure adequate liquidity for settlements on specified dates.
