## Summary

National Stock Exchange notifies the listing and trading of equity shares of Sikko Industries Limited (Symbol: SIKKO) pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 21,84,00,000 (21.84 crore) equity shares with ISIN INE112X01025 will be admitted to trading on the NSE Capital Market segment effective December 10, 2025. The shares have a distinctive number range from 218400001 to 436800000.

## Key Points

- Company: Sikko Industries Limited
- Trading Symbol: SIKKO
- ISIN: INE112X01025
- Number of bonus shares: 21,84,00,000 (21.84 crore)
- Distinctive number range: 218400001 to 436800000
- Trading effective date: December 10, 2025
- Securities will be identified by designated codes on the trading system

## Regulatory Changes

None. This circular is issued in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A for admission of further securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must recognize the new equity shares for trading purposes using the designated security codes
- The securities shall be identified only by designated codes on the trading system
- Members must ensure trading systems are updated to reflect the increased share capital

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 09, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue represents a capital restructuring for Sikko Industries Limited, increasing the number of outstanding equity shares. The impact is primarily on existing shareholders who will receive additional shares proportionate to their holdings. The increased float may improve liquidity in the stock. Trading members need to update their systems to accommodate the expanded share capital. The market capitalization remains unchanged, but the share price will adjust proportionately to reflect the increased number of shares.
