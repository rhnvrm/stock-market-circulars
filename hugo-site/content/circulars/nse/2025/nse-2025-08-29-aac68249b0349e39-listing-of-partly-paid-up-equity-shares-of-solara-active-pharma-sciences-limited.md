## Summary

NSE announces the listing of 3,45,923 partly paid-up equity shares of Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited on the Capital Market segment. The shares will trade under symbol SOLARAPP1 with ISIN IN9624Z01022, effective September 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3,45,923 partly paid-up equity shares to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each (Rs. 7/- paid up)
- Trading symbol: SOLARAPP1
- Market lot size: 1 share
- No lock-in shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN IN8624Z01031

## Regulatory Changes

Admission of securities under Regulation 3.1.1 and lot size specification under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use designated security codes for trading and adhere to specified lot sizes as per the annexure.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: September 01, 2025
- Circular Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact limited to Solara Active Pharma Sciences shareholders and traders. Provides additional liquidity avenue for partly paid-up shares with specific trading parameters.
