## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) and position limits for Participants, Institutional clients, and Non-Institutional clients for ADANIENT in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme for November 2025. The new limits will be effective from November 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Market Wide Position Limit increased from 30,040,977 shares to 30,942,206 shares
- Institutional client limit increased from 3,004,097 shares to 3,094,220 shares
- Participant limit increased from 3,004,097 shares to 3,094,220 shares
- Non-Institutional client limit increased from 300,409 shares to 309,422 shares
- Changes apply to ADANIENT symbol only
- Circular issued by Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme Department

## Regulatory Changes

This circular modifies position limits previously established in circular NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025, and partially modifies circular NCL/CMPT/71091 dated October 31, 2025. All position limits for ADANIENT have been increased by approximately 3%.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Participants and Custodians must comply with the revised position limits
- Limits are applicable for the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme
- Participants must ensure their positions and client positions remain within the new thresholds
- Contact support@nsccl.co.in or call 18002660050 (IVR Option 2) for queries

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Applicable Period**: November 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revision represents a modest increase in position limits across all categories, providing slightly more capacity for Securities Lending and Borrowing activities in ADANIENT. This change affects market participants, institutional investors, and retail clients engaged in securities lending/borrowing of ADANIENT shares. The uniform percentage increase maintains the proportional structure of limits across different participant categories.
