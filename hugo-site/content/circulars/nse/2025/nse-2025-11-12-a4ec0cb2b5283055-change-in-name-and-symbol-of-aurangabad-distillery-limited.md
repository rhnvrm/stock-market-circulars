## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Aurangabad Distillery Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective November 18, 2025. The company will be renamed to Alcokraft Distilleries Limited and its trading symbol will change from AURDIS to ALCODIS.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from Aurangabad Distillery Limited to Alcokraft Distilleries Limited
- Trading symbol changes from AURDIS to ALCODIS
- Changes will be effective from November 18, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department
- Circular Reference Number: 2195/2025
- NSE Reference Number: NSE/CML/71248

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving name and symbol modification for an existing listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Market participants should use the new symbol ALCODIS for trading from November 18, 2025
- All references to the company should be updated in trading and settlement systems

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. Investors holding shares of Aurangabad Distillery Limited will continue to hold the same securities under the new name Alcokraft Distilleries Limited with the updated symbol ALCODIS. No action is required by shareholders as the ISIN and other security identifiers remain unchanged. Trading systems and member platforms will need to update company name and symbol references by the effective date.
