## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Kaytex Fabrics Limited equity shares on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 05, 2025. The company's shares will initially trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W' before moving to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: KAYTEX
- ISIN: INE13ZD01013
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 180 per share
- Total Securities: 14,699,200 equity shares
- Lot Size: 800 equity shares
- Market Maker: Gretex Share Broking Limited
- Maximum Permissible Spread: 10%

## Regulatory Changes

- Initial trading in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment)
- Subsequently will shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment)
- Subject to pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes of 800 shares
- Market maker obligations with 10% spread limit
- Lock-in requirements as per Annexure I
