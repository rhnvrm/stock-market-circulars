## Summary

Medistep Healthcare Limited will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (series: ST) to the rolling segment (series: SM) effective September 2, 2025, following SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Transfer from trade for trade segment (ST) to rolling segment (SM)
- Effective date: September 2, 2025
- References Exchange Circular NSE/CML/69676 dated August 14, 2025
- Follows SEBI guidelines CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

Segment classification change for MEDISTEP from restrictive trade-for-trade to normal rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members to note the segment transfer and adjust trading accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **September 2, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine segment transfer for a single SME stock, allowing more flexible trading conditions for investors.
