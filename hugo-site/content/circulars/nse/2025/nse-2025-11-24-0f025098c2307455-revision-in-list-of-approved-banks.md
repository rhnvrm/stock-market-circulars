## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the list of approved banks for the Capital Market Segment by adding Credit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank (India) to the existing approved banks list. This is an addition to the list previously published in circular no. 0370/2025 dated November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Credit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank (India) added to approved banks list
- This is an addition to the existing list, not a replacement
- Circular reference: 373/2025 dated November 24, 2025
- Download reference: NCL/CMPT/71430
- Related to previous circular no. 0370/2025 (NCL/CMPT/71395) dated November 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update expanding the list of approved banks that members can use for capital market segment operations and collateral purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are advised to take note of the addition of Credit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank (India) to the approved banks list. No specific action required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Effective Date:** Immediate (upon circular issuance)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. This circular provides members with an additional banking option for their capital market segment operations and collateral management. The addition of Credit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank (India) expands the choice of approved banks available to members without affecting existing arrangements or requiring any changes to current banking relationships.
