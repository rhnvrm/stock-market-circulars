## Summary

NSE has revised the expiry date and maturity date for Futures & Options contracts scheduled to expire in March 2026. The date has been changed from March 31, 2026 to March 30, 2026. This change follows an earlier circular (NSE/FAOP/71777 dated December 12, 2025) and requires members to update their trading systems with new contract files before trading commences on December 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Expiry date and maturity date changed from March 31, 2026 to March 30, 2026
- Change applies to all F&O contracts for March 2026 expiry month
- Members must load updated contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz files
- Contract files available on Extranet server at faoftp/faocommon directory
- MII contract file also accessible at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives
- Settlement schedule to be communicated separately by clearing corporations
- Circular reference: NSE/FAOP/71876, Circular No. 217/2025

## Regulatory Changes

**Contract Specification Amendment:**
- **Current Expiry Date:** March 31, 2026
- **Revised Expiry Date:** March 30, 2026
- **Current Maturity Date:** March 31, 2026
- **Revised Maturity Date:** March 30, 2026

This technical amendment affects the contract specifications for all derivatives contracts expiring in March 2026, including stock futures, index futures, and options contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
1. Download updated contract files from NSE Extranet server (faoftp/faocommon directory)
2. Load contract.gz file into trading application before December 29, 2025
3. Load NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz file into trading application before December 29, 2025
4. Verify MII contract file (NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz) from NSE website if needed
5. Ensure all trading systems reflect the revised expiry and maturity dates
6. Await settlement schedule communication from respective clearing corporations

**Critical Deadline:** All system updates must be completed before trading begins on December 29, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Mandatory System Update Deadline:** Before trading on December 29, 2025
- **Original Expiry/Maturity Date:** March 31, 2026 (now obsolete)
- **Revised Expiry/Maturity Date:** March 30, 2026
- **Related Circular:** NSE/FAOP/71777 dated December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:**
- High impact on trading members' technical infrastructure requiring immediate system updates
- All members trading F&O contracts must update their systems to prevent trading disruptions
- Failure to update could result in incorrect contract processing and settlement issues

**Market Impact:**
- Affects all market participants holding or trading March 2026 expiry contracts
- One-day advancement of expiry requires adjustment of trading and hedging strategies
- Settlement timelines will be impacted, with details to follow from clearing corporations

**Risk Considerations:**
- Non-compliance with update deadline could lead to system errors on December 29, 2025
- Trading members must coordinate with their technical teams to ensure timely implementation
- Portfolio management systems and risk management tools must reflect the revised dates

**Stakeholder Actions Required:**
- Brokers and trading members: Update trading systems and inform clients
- Institutional investors: Adjust portfolio management systems and hedging calendars
- Risk managers: Update risk models and position monitoring tools
- Clearing members: Prepare for revised settlement schedule upon receipt of further instructions
