## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced the relocation of its Common Investor Services Centre in Chennai to a new address effective December 01, 2025. The new office will be located at Centre Point-3, 4th Floor, No. 2/4, Mount Poonamallee High Road, Manapakkam, Porur, Chennai – 600089. This move is part of NSE's initiative to enhance access and reach to investors and trading members across India.

## Key Points

- Common Investor Services Centre in Chennai is relocating to a new address
- New location: Centre Point-3, 4th Floor, Mount Poonamallee High Road, Manapakkam, Porur, Chennai – 600089
- Contact person: Mr. Mathew Rijo Rajeev
- Contact number: 044 6630 9900
- Email: nseiscchen@nse.co.in
- Toll-free number for investor grievances: 1800-266-0050 (select option 5)
- General investor grievance email: ignse@nse.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding office relocation.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. Members and participants are informed of the new address for future reference when accessing investor services.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 01, 2025 - Chennai Common Investor Services Centre begins operations at new address

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change that does not affect trading operations, market regulations, or compliance requirements. The relocation is intended to improve accessibility for investors and trading members seeking in-person services at the Chennai location. Stakeholders should update their records with the new contact information and address for future correspondence or visits to the Chennai Investor Services Centre.
