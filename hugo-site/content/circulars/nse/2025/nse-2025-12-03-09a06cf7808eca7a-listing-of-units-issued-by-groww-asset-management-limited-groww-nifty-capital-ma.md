## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF units for trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 04, 2025. The ETF, managed by Groww Asset Management Limited, will trade under the symbol GROWWCAPM with 6,183,954 units listed at a face value of Rs. 10 per unit.

## Key Points

- Total units listed: 6,183,954 units
- Trading symbol: GROWWCAPM
- ISIN: INF666M01MM4
- Face value: Rs. 10 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 10 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: December 02, 2025
- Three market makers appointed: Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt. Ltd (TM code 90246), East India Securities (TM code 08134), and Irage Broking Services LLP (TM code 90068)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code GROWWCAPM for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Security identification on the trading system will be by designated codes only

## Important Dates

- **Circular date**: December 03, 2025
- **Date of allotment**: December 02, 2025
- **Listing effective date**: December 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine ETF listing that adds another investment option for investors seeking exposure to Nifty Capital Markets index. The listing is primarily relevant for ETF investors and the three designated market makers.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure with clear trading parameters. Market lot of 1 unit provides flexibility for retail investors.
