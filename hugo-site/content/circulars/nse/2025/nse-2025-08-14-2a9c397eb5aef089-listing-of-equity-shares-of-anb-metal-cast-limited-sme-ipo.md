## Summary

ANB Metal Cast Limited (Symbol: AMCL) will be listed on the NSE EMERGE SME Platform effective August 18, 2025. The company's 11,834,780 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each will initially trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with 'W' settlement type before moving to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Symbol: AMCL (ANB Metal Cast Limited)
- ISIN: INE0VG001016
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 156/- per share
- Total Securities: 11,834,780 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 800 equity shares
- Trading Series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment)
- Settlement Type: 'W' initially, then 'N' in normal rolling segment

## Regulatory Changes

- Initial trading in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Subsequent shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment) as per regulatory guidelines
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category scrips
- Continuous market trading available as per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202

## Compliance Requirements

- Market makers must maintain spread within 10%
- Market maker minimum quantity: 800 equity shares
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I
- Company must comply with SME platform listing requirements

Limited market impact as this is a new SME listing. The trade-for-trade surveillance mechanism and specific lot sizes will ensure controlled trading.
