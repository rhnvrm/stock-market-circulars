## Summary

NSE has moved EUROTEX INDUSTRIES AND EXPORTS LIMITED (Symbol: EUROTEXIND, ISIN: INE022C01012) to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective November 04, 2025. The security's series will change from EQ to BE, and trading will be restricted to trade-for-trade basis with a 5% price band and 50% Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) requirement for buyers.

## Key Points

- EUROTEX INDUSTRIES AND EXPORTS LIMITED moved to Stage II of GSM
- Security series changes from EQ to BE
- Trade-for-trade settlement mandatory with no netting off allowed
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower
- Buyers must deposit 50% ASD of trade value
- Members advised to take adequate precautions while trading

## Regulatory Changes

The security transitions from Stage I to Stage II of the GSM framework, which implements stricter surveillance measures:

**Stage II Surveillance Actions:**
- Trade for Trade settlement basis
- Price band of 5% or lower as applicable
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value to be deposited by buyers
- No netting off allowed in settlement

**GSM Framework Overview:**
- Stage I: 100% margin rate with 5% price band
- Stage II: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band and 50% ASD
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band, weekly trading (Mondays only), and 100% ASD
- Stage IV: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band, weekly trading, 100% ASD, and no upward movement

## Compliance Requirements

**For NSE Members:**
- Ensure trade-for-trade settlement for EUROTEXIND
- Collect 50% ASD from buyers before execution
- Apply 5% or lower price band restrictions
- Disable netting off for this security in settlement systems
- Inform clients about enhanced surveillance measures
- Take adequate precautions while facilitating trades

**For Buyers:**
- Deposit 50% of trade value as Additional Surveillance Deposit
- Accept trade-for-trade settlement terms
- Comply with 5% price band restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 04, 2025 (Stage II GSM implementation)
- **Series Change:** Effective November 04, 2025 (EQ to BE)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Significant reduction in liquidity due to trade-for-trade requirement
- Higher capital requirement for buyers (50% ASD)
- Limited price movement (5% band) reduces volatility
- No intraday netting increases settlement obligations
- Series change to BE indicates surveillance category

**Market Impact:**
- Increased transaction costs for participants
- Potential decline in trading volumes
- Higher barriers to entry for retail investors
- Enhanced risk perception for the security

**Operational Impact:**
- Members must update risk management systems
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements
- Client communication and disclosure obligations
- Settlement process modifications required

This circular continues NSE's surveillance framework established through multiple circulars since February 2017, demonstrating ongoing regulatory oversight of securities exhibiting concerning trading patterns or corporate governance issues.
