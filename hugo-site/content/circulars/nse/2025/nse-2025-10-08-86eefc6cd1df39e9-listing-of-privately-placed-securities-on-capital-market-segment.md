## Summary

NSE has admitted two series of privately placed debentures issued by Vedika Credit Capital Limited for trading on the capital market segment with effect from October 09, 2025. The securities include VCCL 11.8% 2027 Sr B and VCCL 11.6% 2027 Sr A debentures with respective ISIN codes INE04HY07336 and INE04HY07328.

## Key Points

- Two debenture series of Vedika Credit Capital Limited listed on NSE
- Trading symbols: 118VCCL27B (11.8% coupon) and 116VCCL27A (11.6% coupon)
- Both securities trade in market lot size of 1 unit
- Series N0 classification for both instruments
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must identify securities by designated codes only
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Members should update their systems to recognize new security codes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 08, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 09, 2025
- **Maturity Date (118VCCL27B):** October 07, 2027
- **Maturity Date (116VCCL27A):** April 07, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities that will primarily affect institutional investors who participated in the private placement.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Members need to update trading systems with new security codes and lot sizes.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to existing debenture holders seeking secondary market liquidity for these instruments.
