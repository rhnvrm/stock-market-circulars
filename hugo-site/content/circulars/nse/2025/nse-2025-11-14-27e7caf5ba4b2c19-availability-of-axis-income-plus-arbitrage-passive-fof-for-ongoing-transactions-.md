## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Axis Income Plus Arbitrage Passive FOF (Fund of Funds) will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting November 17, 2025. The fund will accept subscriptions and redemptions with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities across six plan variants.

## Key Points

- Axis Income Plus Arbitrage Passive FOF reopening for ongoing transactions effective November 17, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six plan variants available: Direct Growth, Regular Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Regular IDCW Payout, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, and Regular IDCW Reinvestment
- All variants categorized as Debt funds
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Circular reference number: 990/2025

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational announcement. The reopening enables members to offer these schemes to their clients through the NSE MF Invest Platform with systematic investment facilities.
