## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in equity shares of Allcargo Gati Limited (Symbol: ACLGATI, Series: EQ) effective from November 12, 2025. The suspension is being implemented in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, and is necessitated by a Scheme of Amalgamation involving the company.

## Key Points

- Trading in Allcargo Gati Limited equity shares (ACLGATI) will be suspended from closing hours of November 11, 2025
- Suspension applies to the EQ (Equity) series
- Action taken under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2
- Circular Reference Number: NSE/CML/71156, Circular Ref. No: 2155/2025
- Reason for suspension: Scheme of Amalgamation

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements a trading suspension under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations. The suspension is a standard regulatory action taken when companies undergo corporate restructuring through amalgamation schemes. Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Trading Regulations Part A provides the authority for such suspensions.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must cease trading in ACLGATI equity shares from November 12, 2025
- No buy or sell orders for Allcargo Gati Limited shares will be accepted after November 11, 2025 closing hours
- Members should inform their clients holding ACLGATI shares about the trading suspension
- Existing positions and shareholdings remain valid; only trading activity is suspended

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Last Trading Day**: November 11, 2025 (up to closing hours)
- **Suspension Effective Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders of Allcargo Gati Limited will not be able to trade their shares on NSE from November 12, 2025. Shareholders will need to wait for the completion of the amalgamation scheme and subsequent listing arrangements of the merged entity.

**Market Impact**: The suspension removes liquidity for ACLGATI shares from the market. Investors with positions in this stock will face restricted exit options until the amalgamation process concludes and new arrangements are announced.

**Corporate Action Context**: Trading suspensions due to amalgamation schemes are standard procedure during corporate restructuring. The suspension typically remains in effect until the scheme is implemented and the resulting entity's shares are listed, or alternative arrangements are communicated to shareholders through subsequent circulars.
