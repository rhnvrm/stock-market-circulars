## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has notified the settlement holidays for the Debt Segment for calendar year 2026. The circular lists 19 weekday holidays and 4 additional holidays falling on weekends (Saturday/Sunday). This notification is issued in pursuance of Regulation 6.2 of NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Key Points

- Total of 19 settlement holidays on weekdays during 2026
- Additional 4 holidays falling on weekends
- Applies to all Members, Custodians, and PCMs in the Debt Segment
- Circular reference number: 0399/2025
- NCL download reference: NCL/DS/71918

## Weekday Settlement Holidays 2026

1. January 26, 2026 (Monday) - Republic Day
2. February 19, 2026 (Thursday) - Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti
3. March 3, 2026 (Tuesday) - Holi (Second Day)
4. March 19, 2026 (Thursday) - Gudhi Padwa
5. March 26, 2026 (Thursday) - Ram Navami
6. March 31, 2026 (Tuesday) - Mahavir Jayanti
7. April 1, 2026 (Wednesday) - Annual Bank Closing
8. April 3, 2026 (Friday) - Good Friday
9. April 14, 2026 (Tuesday) - Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti
10. May 1, 2026 (Friday) - Maharashtra Din / Buddha Pournima
11. May 28, 2026 (Thursday) - Bakri ID (Id-Uz-Zuha)
12. June 26, 2026 (Friday) - Moharum
13. August 26, 2026 (Wednesday) - Id-E-Milad
14. September 14, 2026 (Monday) - Ganesh Chaturthi
15. October 2, 2026 (Friday) - Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti
16. October 20, 2026 (Tuesday) - Dussehra
17. November 10, 2026 (Tuesday) - Diwali (Bali Pratipada)
18. November 24, 2026 (Tuesday) - Guru Nanak Jayanti
19. December 25, 2026 (Friday) - Christmas

## Weekend Holidays

1. February 15, 2026 (Sunday) - Mahashivratri
2. March 21, 2026 (Saturday) - Ramzan-Id (Id-Ul-Fitr)
3. August 15, 2026 (Saturday) - Independence Day/Parsi New Year (Shahenshahi)
4. November 8, 2026 (Sunday) - Diwali Amavasya (Laxmi Pujan)

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs in the Debt Segment must note these settlement holidays
- Plan settlement operations accordingly for the calendar year 2026
- No debt segment settlements will occur on the notified dates

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 22, 2025
- Applicable Period: Calendar Year 2026 (January 1 to December 31, 2026)
- First Holiday: January 26, 2026 (Republic Day)
- Last Holiday: December 25, 2026 (Christmas)

## Impact Assessment

- Operational Impact: Medium - Members need to adjust settlement schedules and operational planning for 2026
- Market Impact: Low - This is a routine annual notification providing advance notice of holidays
- Planning Benefit: High advance notice allows members to plan operations, liquidity management, and resource allocation
- Scope: Applies specifically to Debt Segment operations
