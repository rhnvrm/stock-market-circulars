## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing of equity shares of Vidya Wires Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company's shares will commence trading on the Capital Market segment with effect from December 10, 2025. The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Company Name**: Vidya Wires Limited
- **Symbol**: VIDYAWIRES
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **ISIN**: INE14UN01029
- **Face Value**: Re. 1/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 52/-
- **Total Securities**: 212,692,307 equity shares of Re. 1/- each fully paid up
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 share
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Registered Office**: Plot No. 8/1-2, GIDC, Opposite SLS Industries, Vithal Udyognagar, Anand - 388 121, Gujarat
- **Contact**: +91 74340 38300
- **Website**: www.vidyawire.com
- **Email**: cs@vidyawire.com
- **Company Secretary**: Mr. Alpesh Makwana
- **Registrar**: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (VIDYAWIRES) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size (1 share)
- The security will participate in pre-open session as per SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Lock-in provisions apply as per Annexure I with various lock-in periods ranging from January 6, 2026 to December 10, 2028 for different tranches of shares

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates** (various tranches):
  - January 6, 2026 (multiple tranches)
  - March 7, 2026 (multiple tranches)
  - June 10, 2026 (2 tranches)
  - December 10, 2026 (4 tranches)
  - December 10, 2028 (2 tranches)
  - Some shares have no lock-in (free)

## Impact Assessment

This listing has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a single company's IPO admission to trading. The impact is primarily relevant to:

- Investors who participated in the Vidya Wires Limited IPO
- Trading members facilitating transactions in this security
- Market participants interested in the wire manufacturing sector

The company operates in the wire manufacturing industry based in Gujarat. With an issue price of Rs. 52 and over 212 million shares listed, the listing provides liquidity to IPO investors and access to public markets for the company. The various lock-in periods indicate different categories of shareholders (promoters, anchor investors, etc.) with staggered release schedules to ensure market stability.
