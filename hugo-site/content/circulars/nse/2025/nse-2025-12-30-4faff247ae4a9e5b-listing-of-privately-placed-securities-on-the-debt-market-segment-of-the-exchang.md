## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed debt securities to its Debt Market Segment effective December 30, 2025. The listing includes commercial papers from Phoenix Arc Private Limited and HDFC Securities Limited, along with debentures from Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited and Gaursons India Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Four issuers' debt securities admitted to NSE Debt Market Segment
- Phoenix Arc CP: ₹100 crore (2,000 units), redemption December 15, 2026
- HDFC Securities CP: ₹250 crore (5,000 units), redemption January 20, 2026
- Trust Investment Advisors DB: ₹125 crore (12,500 units), 8.8% coupon, redemption November 21, 2029
- Gaursons India DB: ₹440 crore (44,000 units), 10.96% coupon, redemption December 26, 2028 with put option on June 25, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. The securities are available for trading on the Debt Market Segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Commercial Paper Redemptions**: January 20, 2026 (HSL), December 15, 2026 (PAPL)
- **Debenture Put Option**: June 25, 2027 (Gaursons India)
- **Debenture Redemptions**: December 26, 2028 (Gaursons India), November 21, 2029 (Trust Investment Advisors)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities totaling ₹915 crore across four issuers. The securities enhance debt market liquidity and provide additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
