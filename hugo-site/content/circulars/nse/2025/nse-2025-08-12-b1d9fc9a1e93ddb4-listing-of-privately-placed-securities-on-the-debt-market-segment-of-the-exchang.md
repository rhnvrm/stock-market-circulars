## Summary

NSE has admitted various privately placed commercial papers to trading on its Debt Market Segment effective August 12, 2025. The circular lists 10 commercial paper securities from 8 different companies with face values of ₹5,00,000 each and varying maturity dates.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities listed from 8 companies
- All securities have uniform face value of ₹5,00,000
- Maturity periods range from September 2025 to November 2025
- Securities admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations
- Each security assigned unique ISIN codes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must comply with ongoing debt market segment regulations
- Securities subject to standard debt market trading rules
- Issuers must maintain compliance with private placement norms

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: August 7-11, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: September 8, 2025 to November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on debt market liquidity with addition of ₹20.9 crore worth of commercial papers. Provides short-term funding options for listed companies and investment opportunities for institutional investors. No significant market disruption expected as these are routine debt listings.
