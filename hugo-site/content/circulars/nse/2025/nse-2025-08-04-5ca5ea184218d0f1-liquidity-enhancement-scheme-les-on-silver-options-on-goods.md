## Summary

NSE has extended the Liquidity Enhancement Scheme (LES) on Silver (30 kgs) Options on Goods contracts for an additional 3-month period. The scheme will now remain effective until November 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- LES on Silver Options extended for 3 months
- New expiry date: November 26, 2025
- All existing provisions from previous circulars remain applicable
- NSE reserves right to amend or discontinue with 15 days advance notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes - this is purely an extension of the existing scheme with all previous terms and conditions remaining unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must continue to comply with existing LES provisions
- No additional compliance requirements introduced
- Members should refer to previous circulars for detailed scheme provisions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 04, 2025
- **Scheme Expiry**: November 26, 2025
- **Notice Period**: 15 days advance notice required for any amendments

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides continued liquidity support for silver options trading, maintaining market stability and trading opportunities for participants. The 3-month extension allows for sustained market participation without disruption to existing trading strategies.
