## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debentures of two companies on the capital market segment effective December 19, 2025. IIFL Finance Limited's 8.50% Senior Debentures maturing in 2028 (Series D34) and Tata Chemicals Limited's 7.06% Debentures maturing in 2028 will be available for trading with market lot size of 1 unit each.

## Key Points

- Two new debenture series admitted to NSE capital market segment
- IIFL Finance Limited: 8.50% coupon, maturity December 18, 2028, Symbol: 850IIFL28, ISIN: INE530B08219
- Tata Chemicals Limited: 7.06% coupon, maturity December 15, 2028, Symbol: 706TCHE28, ISIN: INE092A08089
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Trading series: N0 for both securities
- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new security codes and symbols for trading purposes
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes (850IIFL28 and 706TCHE28)
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (1 unit each)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 19, 2025
- **IIFL Debenture Maturity:** December 18, 2028
- **Tata Chemicals Debenture Maturity:** December 15, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine administrative notification for listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides liquidity avenue for existing debenture holders but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Investor Impact:** Provides secondary market trading facility for holders of these privately placed debentures, enabling price discovery and exit options before maturity.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems with new security codes and symbols for these debenture series.
