## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of 213 securities that are eligible for the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS). This comprehensive list includes major stocks across various sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, technology, consumer goods, and ETFs.

## Key Points

- Total 213 securities are now eligible for SLBS
- List includes major blue-chip stocks like ADANIPORTS, ASIANPAINT, AXISBANK, BAJFINANCE, BHARTIARTL, BRITANNIA, COALINDIA, CIPLA
- Multiple ETFs are included such as BANKBEES, ALPHAETF, AXISNIFTY, BSE500IETF
- List covers diverse sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, automotive, technology, and consumer goods
- Both equity shares and ETFs are eligible for securities lending and borrowing

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the previous list of SLBS eligible securities, expanding or revising the scope of securities available for lending and borrowing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must refer to this updated list for SLBS transactions
- Only securities mentioned in this list are eligible for securities lending and borrowing
- Participants should ensure compliance with SLBS regulations for the listed securities

## Important Dates

Effective immediately upon circular issuance.

## Impact Assessment

This update provides expanded opportunities for institutional investors, mutual funds, and other market participants to engage in securities lending and borrowing activities. The inclusion of 213 securities offers better liquidity and flexibility for SLBS transactions, potentially improving market efficiency and providing additional revenue streams for securities holders.
