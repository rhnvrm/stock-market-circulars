## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited (NCL) has scheduled a mock session on December 13, 2025, to test the Software as a Service (SaaS) setup for two-way portability across clearing corporations. This is conducted in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/TPD/P/CIR/2023/192 dated December 20, 2023, which mandates quarterly mock sessions to ensure business continuity and familiarize members with SaaS functionalities.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for December 13, 2025 from 10:00 hrs to 13:45 hrs
- NCL will invoke SaaS setup between 10:00 hrs - 10:30 hrs
- NCL will operate using SaaS setup (NCL@ICCL) between 11:15 hrs to 13:45 hrs
- Members must test all functionalities associated with SaaS setup
- Login credentials previously issued remain valid for this session
- New members requiring credentials should email support@nsccl.co.in
- Three access links provided: RTRMS, Collateral, and Extranet

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements existing SEBI requirements from circular SEBI/HO/MRD/TPD/P/CIR/2023/192 dated December 20, 2023 regarding quarterly mock sessions for business continuity through SaaS model.

## Compliance Requirements

- All clearing members are required to actively participate in the mock session
- Members must test all functionalities during the designated timeframe
- Members without login credentials must obtain them by contacting support@nsccl.co.in
- Members should refer to previous NCL circulars for guidelines:
  - NCL circular no 0138/2023 (Download No: NCL/CMPT/56360) dated April 13, 2023
  - NCL circular no 088/2024 (Download No: NCL/CMPT/61960) dated May 10, 2024

## Important Dates

- **December 13, 2025**: Mock session testing date
  - 10:00 hrs - 10:30 hrs: SaaS setup invocation period
  - 10:00 hrs - 13:45 hrs: Overall testing window for members
  - 11:15 hrs - 13:45 hrs: NCL operational using SaaS setup (NCL@ICCL)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Members need to allocate resources and technical staff to participate in the mock session and test interfaces. This is a routine quarterly exercise for business continuity preparedness.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a testing exercise with no impact on live trading or settlement operations. The mock session is designed to prepare members for potential failover scenarios.

**Technical Impact**: Medium - Members must ensure their systems can connect to and operate through the SaaS infrastructure, testing RTRMS, Collateral, and Extranet interfaces.

**Business Continuity**: High importance for ensuring seamless operations in case primary clearing infrastructure needs to be switched to backup SaaS model during actual contingency situations.
