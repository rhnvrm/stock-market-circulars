## Summary

NSE has empaneled M/s. Trading Technologies Private Limited as an approved vendor for providing Non-NEAT Front End Solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and allows trading members to use this vendor's front-end trading solutions.

## Key Points

- M/s. Trading Technologies Private Limited has been added to NSE's list of approved vendors
- The vendor will provide Non-NEAT Front End Solutions to trading members
- Vendor office located at The Pavilion Mall, 3rd Floor, Pune - 411016
- Contact number: +9987506687
- Applicable with immediate effect from December 15, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update adding a new vendor to the Exchange's empaneled list for Non-NEAT Front End Solutions.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. This circular provides information about an additional vendor option available for trading members who wish to use Non-NEAT Front End Solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 (Immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative empanelment that expands the list of approved vendors for trading solutions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members now have an additional vendor option for Non-NEAT Front End Solutions. No mandatory changes required for existing members.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Affects trading members who may choose to engage with this newly empaneled vendor for their front-end trading technology needs.

**Contact Information**: For queries, trading members can contact NSE at toll-free number 1800-266-0050 (Option 1) or email nnfreg@nse.co.in
