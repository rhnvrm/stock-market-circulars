## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing of equity shares of Paushak Limited (Symbol: PAUSHAKLTD) on the Capital Market segment effective December 01, 2025. The company has issued 24,656,912 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each fully paid up with ISIN INE111F01024. Trading will be conducted in the Normal Market segment under rolling settlement with compulsory demat for all investors.

## Key Points

- Symbol: PAUSHAKLTD
- ISIN: INE111F01024
- Face Value: Rs. 5/- per share
- Total Securities: 24,656,912 equity shares
- Distinctive Number Range: 1 to 24,656,912
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Lock-in Details: NA (No lock-in)
- Trading Mode: Compulsory demat for all investors
- Segment: Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- Shares are fully paid up

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a new listing announcement in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in PAUSHAKLTD equity shares must be in compulsory demat mode
- Securities identified only by designated codes on trading system
- Trading in specified lot size (1 share) as per Annexure
- Pre-listing shareholding pattern filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) dated November 14, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Report Date (Shareholding Pattern)**: November 14, 2025
- **Financial Year**: April 1 to March 31

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard new listing announcement that adds one more security to the NSE Capital Market segment. Impact is limited to investors specifically interested in Paushak Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Members and investors can trade PAUSHAKLTD shares from December 01, 2025 in standard rolling settlement mode with compulsory demat.
