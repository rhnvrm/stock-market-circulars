## Summary

NSE has admitted Kotak Nifty 200 Momentum 30 ETF units to trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 17, 2025. The ETF comprises 1,762,750 units with ISIN INF174KA1XS6, allotted on October 10, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 31.15 per unit. Four market makers have been designated for the ETF.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: MOMENTUM30
- **ISIN**: INF174KA1XS6
- **Total Units**: 1,762,750
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Paid Up Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 31.15 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: October 17, 2025

## Market Makers

Four entities have been designated as market makers:

1. **Kotak Securities Limited** (TM Code: 8081)
2. **Parwati Capital Market Private Limited** (TM Code: 11890)
3. **Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited** (TM Code: 90246)
4. **Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited** (TM Code: 10412)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code MOMENTUM30 for trading identification
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- All trading regulations applicable to ETF securities apply

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands investment options for investors seeking exposure to momentum strategies based on the Nifty 200 universe. The presence of four market makers should ensure adequate liquidity. The ETF targets investors interested in systematic momentum-based investing strategies within large and mid-cap stocks.
