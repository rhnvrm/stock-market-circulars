## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares issued pursuant to the bonus issue by Ram Ratna Wires Limited. A total of 4,66,74,536 new equity shares with ISIN INE207E01023 will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective December 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Ram Ratna Wires Limited (Symbol: RAMRAT)
- Total bonus shares to be listed: 4,66,74,536
- ISIN: INE207E01023
- Distinctive Number Range: 46674537 to 93349072
- Security identification will be through designated codes only
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for bonus shares under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must update their systems to reflect the new securities effective December 30, 2025
- Trading participants must use designated security codes for identification purposes
- Members must be prepared to handle trading in the enlarged share capital from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 23, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 26, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** December 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The bonus issue will increase the total outstanding shares of Ram Ratna Wires Limited, improving liquidity and potentially making shares more accessible to retail investors due to adjusted price levels post-bonus.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard corporate action requiring routine system updates by trading members. No changes to trading rules or procedures.

**Shareholder Impact:** Positive for existing shareholders who receive additional shares in proportion to their holdings as of the record date, though the proportional ownership remains unchanged.
