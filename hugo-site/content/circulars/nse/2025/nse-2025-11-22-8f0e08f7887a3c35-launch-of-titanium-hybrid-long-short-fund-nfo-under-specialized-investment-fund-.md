## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund NFO by TATA Mutual Fund under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category. The NFO will be available for transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025 until 11:59 PM. Six scheme variants are offered covering Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- Fund: Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund by TATA Mutual Fund SIF
- NFO Period: November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Total Schemes: 6 variants (Direct/Regular with Growth/IDCW Payout/IDCW Reinvestment)
- Category: Equity (Specialized Investment Fund)
- Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Payment Deadline: December 10, 2025 (11:59 PM)

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 10, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- All transactions must be completed through NSE MF Invest Platform during the NFO period
