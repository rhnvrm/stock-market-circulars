## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted equity shares of Park Medi World Limited to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 17, 2025. The company issued 431,930,864 equity shares at Rs. 162 per share with a face value of Rs. 2. The security will trade under symbol PARKHOSPS with ISIN INE119201023 and market lot size of 1 share. The listing follows completion of the company's Initial Public Offering in the healthcare sector.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: PARKHOSPS
- ISIN: INE119201023
- Total shares listed: 431,930,864 equity shares
- Face Value: Rs. 2 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 162 per share
- Market Lot Size: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading commences: December 17, 2025
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Registrar: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code PARKHOSPS for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot size of 1 share
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category scrips
- Lock-in requirements apply to specific share tranches as per Annexure I

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Trading Commencement: December 17, 2025
- Lock-in Expiry Dates:
  - 86,386,180 shares: December 16, 2028 (3-year lock-in)
  - 271,662,327 shares: December 16, 2026 (1-year lock-in)
  - 17,092,234 shares: June 16, 2026 (6-month lock-in)
  - 8,518,517 shares: January 13, 2026
  - 8,518,519 shares: March 14, 2026
  - Remaining shares: Free (no lock-in)
- Financial Year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The listing adds a healthcare sector company to NSE with substantial market capitalization (approximately Rs. 6,997 crores at issue price). The healthcare sector listing provides investors with additional investment opportunities in the medical services space.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard IPO listing with straightforward trading parameters. Market lot of 1 share provides good liquidity and accessibility for retail investors.

**Lock-in Structure:** Significant portion (approximately 88% of shares) subject to lock-in periods ranging from 6 months to 3 years, which may impact near-term liquidity but demonstrates promoter and early investor commitment.
