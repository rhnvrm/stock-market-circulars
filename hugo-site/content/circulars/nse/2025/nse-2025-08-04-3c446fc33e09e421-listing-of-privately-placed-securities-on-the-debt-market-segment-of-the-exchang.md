## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 commercial papers and 1 debenture to trading on the debt market segment effective August 04, 2025. The securities include commercial papers from major companies like Reliance Industries Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Welspun Corp Limited, and others, with redemption periods ranging from September 2025 to January 2026.

## Key Points

- 11 privately placed debt securities admitted to NSE debt market trading
- 10 commercial papers with face values of ₹5 lakh each
- 1 debenture from Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited with 7.73% coupon
- Securities allotted between July 31, 2025 and August 04, 2025
- Redemption dates range from September 25, 2025 to January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Securities listed under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 04, 2025
- **Earliest Redemption**: September 25, 2025 (RIL CP)
- **Latest Redemption**: January 30, 2026 (IIFL Capital CP)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is routine administrative listing of debt securities. Provides additional trading options for institutional investors in the debt market segment. The securities represent approximately ₹2.85 crores in total face value across all instruments.
