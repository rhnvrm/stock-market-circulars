## Summary

Flywings Simulator Training Centre Limited has been listed on NSE SME platform with its shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated December 11, 2025. The company has 10,177,128 total equity shares with promoter holding at 59.87% and public shareholding at 40.13%. The company has confirmed no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares (except those encumbered), pledged shares, or differential voting rights.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 10,177,128 (all fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 6,093,414 shares (59.87%)
- Public shareholding: 4,083,714 shares (40.13%)
- Number of public shareholders: 948
- Dematerialized shares: 7,978,800 (78.40% of total)
- Locked-in shares: 4,086,432 shares (40.15% of total, held by promoters at 67.06% of promoter holding)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No depository receipts or differential voting rights

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure following SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Listed entity required to submit declaration regarding various types of securities and encumbrances
- All required disclosures have been completed as per SCRR, 1957

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern filing date: December 11, 2025
- Listing date: December 11, 2025 (as per circular)

This is a routine listing disclosure for a new SME IPO with minimal market-wide impact. The shareholding pattern shows:
