## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued a revised settlement calendar for the takeover offer under the Tender Offer scheme of J.B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited. This circular updates the previous schedules communicated on November 14, 2025 and November 11, 2025, specifically modifying the obligation download and settlement dates for Settlement No. 2025121.

## Key Points

- Trading period: November 18, 2025 to December 01, 2025
- Revised obligation download date: December 04, 2025
- Revised settlement date: December 05, 2025
- Settlement Type: X
- Settlement Number: 2025121
- Schedule may be further revised if there is delay in receiving acceptance details from RTA or delay in funds pay-in by buying trading member
- All other details from circular NCL/CMPT/71296 dated November 14, 2025 remain applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular only revises the settlement timeline for an existing takeover offer process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, custodians, and PCMs must note the revised settlement calendar
- Trading members must ensure timely funds pay-in to avoid further schedule revisions
- Participants should refer to previous circular NCL/CMPT/71296 (dated Nov 14, 2025) for all other applicable details and requirements

## Important Dates

- **Trade Start Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Trade End Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Obligation Download Date**: December 04, 2025 (revised)
- **Settlement Date**: December 05, 2025 (revised)
- **Circular Issue Date**: December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This revision affects parties participating in the takeover offer of J.B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited. The delayed settlement schedule provides additional time for processing obligations.

**Operational Impact**: The revised dates require participants to adjust their internal settlement processes and timelines. Members involved in the tender offer must update their systems and procedures to reflect the new obligation download and settlement dates. The conditional nature of the schedule (subject to RTA acceptance details and funds pay-in) requires participants to remain vigilant for potential further revisions.
