## Summary

Optivalue Tek Consulting Limited has completed its SME IPO and filed its post-issue shareholding pattern as of September 09, 2025. The company has 898 total shareholders with 23,411,400 equity shares outstanding, comprising 2 promoter shareholders and 896 public shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company name: Optivalue Tek Consulting Limited
- Total equity shares: 23,411,400
- Total shareholders: 898 (2 promoters, 896 public)
- Filing under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern as of September 09, 2025
- Shares are locked-in as declared in the pattern

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes mentioned. This is a standard post-IPO shareholding pattern filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has filed shareholding pattern under SEBI LODR Regulation 31
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- Locked-in shares present as per IPO requirements
- No pledging, encumbrance, or differential voting rights

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: September 09, 2025
- Filing represents post-IPO shareholding structure

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine post-IPO compliance filing for an SME listing. The disclosure provides transparency on the company's shareholding structure following its public listing on the SME platform.
