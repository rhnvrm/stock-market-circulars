## Summary

NSE announces adjustment of futures and options contracts for NESTLE INDIA LIMITED (NESTLEIND) due to a bonus issue or similar corporate action.

## Key Points

- Contract adjustments for NESTLEIND futures and options
- Effective from specified date
- New contract specifications to be implemented
- Trading members to be notified of changes

## Regulatory Changes

Contract terms modified to reflect the bonus issue impact on underlying security.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and inform clients about the contract adjustments.

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 1, 2025
- Adjustment effective date: As specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

All existing NESTLEIND futures and options positions will be adjusted proportionally based on the bonus ratio.
