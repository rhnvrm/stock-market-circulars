## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has released the settlement calendar for December 2025 for the Capital Market Segment. The circular provides detailed schedules for five settlement types: Normal Rolling T+1 (Type M), Normal Auction (Type A), Trade for Trade T+1 (Type Z), ITP for SME T+1 (Type B), and Normal Rolling T+0 (Type 5). The schedules cover settlement numbers 2025228 through 2025249, spanning from December 1, 2025 to January 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar published for December 2025 under Capital Market Segment
- Five settlement types covered with separate annexures for each
- Settlement numbers range from 2025228 to 2025249 (22 settlements)
- Circular issued pursuant to Regulation 6.2 of NSCCC Capital Market Regulations, 1997
- Trade dates run from December 1, 2025 through December 31, 2025
- Settlement dates extend to January 1, 2026 for last December trades
- Normal Rolling T+1 settlements continue on all trading days except holidays
- Auction settlements follow T+1 trade dates with settlement one day later

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular issued under existing Regulation 6.2 of the National Securities Clearing Corporation (Capital Market) Regulations, 1997.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members, Custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the published settlement schedules
- Custodial confirmation dates must be observed as specified in each annexure
- Settlement obligations must be met on designated settlement dates
- For clarifications, members should contact the helpdesk at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or email securities_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

**Normal Rolling T+1 Settlements (Type M):**
- First settlement: Trade date December 1, 2025; Settlement date December 2, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025228)
- Last settlement: Trade date December 31, 2025; Settlement date January 1, 2026 (Settlement No. 2025249)
- December 25, 2025 appears to be a holiday (no settlement scheduled)

**Auction Normal Settlements (Type A):**
- Follow T+1 schedule with auction trade dates one day after corresponding regular trade dates
- Settlement dates align with T+1 regular settlements
- First auction: Trade December 2, Settlement December 3, 2025
- Last auction: Trade January 1, 2026, Settlement date not fully specified in excerpt

**Other Settlement Types:**
- Trade for Trade T+1 (Type Z) - Annexure C
- ITP for SME T+1 (Type B) - Annexure D
- Normal Rolling T+0 (Type 5) - Annexure E

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - This calendar is essential for all market participants to plan their settlement operations, manage liquidity, and ensure timely fulfillment of obligations. The calendar allows members to prepare for year-end settlements extending into January 2026.

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine administrative circular that does not affect trading rules, market structure, or participant eligibility. It provides necessary operational clarity for the month ahead.

**Participant Impact:** All clearing members, custodians, and Professional Clearing Members need to align their back-office operations, funding arrangements, and custodial confirmation processes with these schedules. The T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles require precise coordination to meet settlement obligations on specified dates.
