## Summary

HOV Services Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective September 03, 2025. The company will be renamed to HandsOn Global Management (HGM) Limited, with the trading symbol changing from HOVS to HGM.

## Key Points

- Current name: HOV Services Limited
- New name: HandsOn Global Management (HGM) Limited
- Current symbol: HOVS
- New symbol: HGM
- Change effective from September 03, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name and symbol change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

All exchange members are informed of the change. Trading systems and records should be updated to reflect the new name and symbol from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **September 03, 2025**: Effective date for name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative change that will require updating of trading systems, portfolios, and documentation to reflect the new corporate identity.
