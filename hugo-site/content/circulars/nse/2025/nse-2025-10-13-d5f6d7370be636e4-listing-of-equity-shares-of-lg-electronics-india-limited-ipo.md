## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of LG Electronics India Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol LGEINDIA with ISIN INE324D01010. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: LG Electronics India Limited
- Trading Symbol: LGEINDIA
- ISIN: INE324D01010
- Listing Type: Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- Exchange members have been notified of the forthcoming listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for exchange members. This is an informational circular regarding a new security listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 13, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a major consumer electronics company to the NSE platform, expanding investment opportunities in the consumer durables sector. The listing of LG Electronics India Limited, a well-established brand in the Indian market, is expected to attract investor interest. Members should prepare their systems to handle trading in this new security once the listing date is announced. The impact on overall market operations is minimal as this is a routine new listing.
