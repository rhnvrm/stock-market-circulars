## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Zerodha Nifty Smallcap 100 ETF NFO (New Fund Offer) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The ETF will be available for transactions from August 25, 2025 to September 5, 2025, with allotment scheduled for September 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Zerodha Nifty Smallcap 100 ETF-Direct Growth variant launching as NFO
- ISIN: INF0R8F01141
- Category: Equity ETF
- Trading period: August 25 - September 5, 2025
- Allotment date: September 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard new product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- All payment modes accepted for fund collection
- Standard NSE member compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: August 25, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine ETF launch. The product provides investors access to Nifty Smallcap 100 index through Zerodha Mutual Fund. Expected to contribute to smallcap market liquidity and provide passive investment options for retail investors.
