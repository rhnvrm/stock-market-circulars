## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of Choice Gold ETF units issued by Choice AMC Private Limited on the Capital Market segment effective November 10, 2025. The ETF with symbol CHOICEGOLD consists of 4,749,787 units with a face value of Rs.100/- each and issue price of Rs.119.94/-. Three market makers have been designated for the ETF.

## Key Points

- Security Symbol: CHOICEGOLD
- ISIN: INF2KCX01012
- Total Units: 4,749,787
- Face Value: Rs.100/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.119.94/- per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: November 04, 2025
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Market Makers: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (TM code 13773), IRAGE Broking Services LLP (TM code 90068), and RiddiSiddhi Bullions Limited (TM code 06074)

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify the security by its designated code (CHOICEGOLD/INF2KCX01012)
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- The circular provisions are effective from November 10, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 04, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Circular Date:** November 07, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a standard ETF listing that expands investment options for gold investors but does not materially affect broader market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking gold exposure through exchange-traded instruments. The ETF provides a regulated, transparent avenue for gold investment with three designated market makers ensuring liquidity.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Market makers are in place to facilitate trading and maintain liquidity in the ETF units.
