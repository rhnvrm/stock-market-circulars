## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further equity shares issued by 13 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The securities will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment effective November 6, 2025. All shares are issued at face value, have a market lot of 1, are pari passu with existing shares, and have no lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 13 companies issuing further equity shares under ESOP schemes
- Total securities listed across all companies: 575,079+ shares
- Effective date for trading: November 6, 2025
- All shares issued at face value with market lot of 1
- All securities are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Regulatory Framework

This listing is pursuant to:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - securities admitted to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - lot size specification

## Trading Details

- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system
- Market lot: 1 share for all listed securities
- Trading in specified lot sizes only
- All shares eligible for immediate trading

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 4, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: November 6, 2025
- Allotment Dates: October 16-28, 2025 (varies by company)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading permitted only in specified lot sizes
- No additional compliance actions required from market participants

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as:
- Routine ESOP allotments with small quantities relative to outstanding shares
- No lock-in restrictions, allowing immediate liquidity
- Shares issued at face value under established ESOP schemes
- Standard listing procedure with no material dilution for most companies
- Market lot of 1 maintains accessibility for retail investors
