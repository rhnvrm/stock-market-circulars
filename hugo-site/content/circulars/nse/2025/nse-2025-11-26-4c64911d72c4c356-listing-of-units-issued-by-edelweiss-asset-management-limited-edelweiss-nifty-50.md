## Summary

NSE notifies the admission of two new Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) to dealings on the Capital Market segment. Edelweiss Asset Management Limited has listed Edelweiss Nifty 50 ETF (Symbol: ENIFTY) with 6,169,376 units and Edelweiss BSE Sensex ETF (Symbol: ESENSEX) with 1,839,028 units. Both ETFs were allotted on November 20, 2025, and will commence trading from November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new ETFs by Edelweiss Asset Management Limited admitted to NSE trading
- Edelweiss Nifty 50 ETF: Symbol ENIFTY, ISIN INF754K01VX0, 6,169,376 units
- Edelweiss BSE Sensex ETF: Symbol ESENSEX, ISIN INF754K01VY8, 1,839,028 units
- Both have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue prices: Rs. 26.19 for Nifty 50 ETF and Rs. 85.63 for BSE Sensex ETF
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both ETFs
- Date of allotment: November 20, 2025
- Market makers appointed: Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited (TM Code: 90246) and Parwati Capital Market Private Limited (TM Code: 11890)
- Registrar: Kfin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new security codes (ENIFTY and ESENSEX)
- Securities to be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (1 unit for both ETFs)
- Market makers must fulfill their obligations as per NSE regulations

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Date of allotment for both ETFs
- **November 26, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date for trading commencement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing that expands investment options for market participants. The addition of index-tracking ETFs provides investors with passive investment vehicles for Nifty 50 and BSE Sensex exposure.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure requiring system updates by trading members to accommodate new securities. Market makers have been appointed to ensure liquidity.

**Investor Impact**: Positive - Provides retail and institutional investors with cost-effective index tracking instruments with low market lot size (1 unit), making them accessible to small investors.
