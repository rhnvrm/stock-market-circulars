## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers (CPs) from ten companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective November 24, 2025. The circular lists security codes, ISINs, issue details, and redemption dates for commercial papers issued by financial services companies and corporations with allotment dates of November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total of 10 commercial paper issuances listed on NSE debt market segment
- All securities allotted on November 21, 2025
- Redemption dates range from December 5, 2025 to June 5, 2026
- Face value of â‚¹5,00,000 per security across all issues
- Total quantity ranges from 1,000 to 32,000 securities per issuer
- Issuers include Godrej Industries, Tata Communications, HDB Financial Services, IIFL entities, Motilal Oswal, PNB Housing Finance, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations for admission of privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed debt securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025 (all securities)
- **Redemption Dates**: 
  - December 5, 2025 (HDBFSL CP)
  - February 20, 2026 (GIL, TCM, GPL, ABML, AOL, IIFL, PHFL CPs)
  - May 22, 2026 (ISL CP)
  - June 5, 2026 (MOFS CP)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed commercial papers. The circular provides transparency to market participants about newly available debt securities for trading. These are privately placed instruments with specific investors and do not affect broader market operations or require any action from trading members.
