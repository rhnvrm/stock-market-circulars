## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Wakefit Innovations Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol WAKEFIT with ISIN INE0E7301029. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Wakefit Innovations Limited
- Trading Symbol: WAKEFIT
- ISIN: INE0E7301029
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements outlined. This is an informational circular notifying members about the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium market impact as it introduces a new equity security for trading on the NSE. Members and market participants should prepare for the addition of WAKEFIT to the exchange. The actual trading impact will depend on the listing date and initial market response to the IPO. Investors interested in the home and lifestyle products sector should note this upcoming listing opportunity.
