## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of Indiqube Spaces Limited equity shares on the exchange following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol INDIQUBE with ISIN INE06ST01018.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Indiqube Spaces Limited
- Stock Symbol: INDIQUBE
- ISIN: INE06ST01018
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exact listing date to be communicated via separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced in separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will provide a new investment and trading opportunity for market participants. The impact is company-specific and creates additional liquidity in the real estate/workspace sector given Indiqube's business model.
