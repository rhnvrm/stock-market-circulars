## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities on its Debt Market Segment effective December 9, 2025. The circular covers fresh issues and re-issues of Commercial Paper (CP) from various companies with face values of ₹5,00,000 each, along with one Non-Convertible Debenture issue from PNB Housing Finance Limited. Total issuances include 7 fresh CP issues, 1 fresh NCD issue, and 2 re-issues of CP.

## Key Points

- **Fresh Commercial Paper Issues:** 7 companies listed CPs with redemption dates ranging from December 31, 2025 to March 10, 2026
- **Largest CP Issue:** Tata Steel Limited issued 25,000 units (₹12,500 crore) with redemption on March 6, 2026
- **Kotak Securities:** Issued 9,500 CP units (₹4,750 crore) with March 10, 2026 redemption
- **Blue Star:** Short-term CP with 22-day tenure, redeeming December 31, 2025
- **PNB Housing Finance:** Issued 24,500 NCDs (₹2,450 crore) at 7.28% coupon rate with 2.5-year tenure (June 2028 redemption)
- **Re-issues:** Aditya Birla Capital (4,000 units) and Tata Steel CPs
- All securities admitted to dealings under NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Market participants can trade these securities on the NSE Debt Market Segment using the designated security codes and ISINs provided in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** December 9, 2025 (effective date for all securities)
- **Allotment Dates:** December 5, 2025 (PNB Housing Finance); December 8, 2025 (majority of CPs); December 9, 2025 (Kotak Securities, Blue Star)
- **Redemption Dates Range:** December 31, 2025 to June 5, 2028
- **Nearest Redemption:** Blue Star CP on December 31, 2025
- **Longest Tenure:** PNB Housing Finance NCD on June 5, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - routine debt market listing activity. Provides additional short-term and medium-term debt instruments for institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard listing procedure. Trading members can transact in these securities through normal debt market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant primarily for debt market investors, mutual funds, and institutions seeking commercial paper and debenture exposure to these corporate issuers. The PNB Housing Finance NCD offers a 7.28% fixed coupon rate for medium-term investors.

**Liquidity:** Fresh issuances totaling approximately ₹23,425 crore across all securities, contributing to debt market depth.
