## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies on the capital market segment. IIFL Home Finance Limited's 8.00% 2027 bonds and Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited's 7.1450% 2032 bonds will be available for trading from August 07, 2025, with designated security codes and standard market lot sizes.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment trading
- IIFL Home Finance Limited: 8.00% coupon rate, maturing August 04, 2027
- Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited: 7.1450% coupon rate, maturing August 05, 2032
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Securities identified by designated codes: 8IHFL27 and 714SDIL32

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system trading
- Trading must comply with specified lot sizes as per Regulation 2.5.5
- Securities trading subject to NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 07, 2025 - Securities available for trading
- **IIFL Maturity**: August 04, 2027
- **SDIL Maturity**: August 05, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard listing notification for debt securities. Provides additional investment options for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market segment. No operational changes required for existing market participants beyond updating security master data.
