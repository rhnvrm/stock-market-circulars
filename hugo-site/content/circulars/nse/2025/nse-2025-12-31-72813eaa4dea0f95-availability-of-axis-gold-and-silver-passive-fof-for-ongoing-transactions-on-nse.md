## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has informed members that Axis Gold and Silver Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) of Axis Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective January 01, 2026. Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth variants will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities.

## Key Points

- Axis Gold and Silver Passive FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from January 01, 2026
- Available facilities: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two scheme variants covered: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1408) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1390)
- Both schemes categorized under Debt category
- Applicable on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should:
- Update their systems to enable transactions for the specified schemes from January 01, 2026
- Ensure all transaction facilities (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) are operational for both scheme variants

## Important Dates

- **January 01, 2026**: Effective date for resumption of ongoing transactions in Axis Gold and Silver Passive FOF

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact affecting only Axis Mutual Fund investors who wish to invest in gold and silver passive fund of funds through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening provides investors access to systematic investment and withdrawal facilities that were previously unavailable. No broader market impact expected.
