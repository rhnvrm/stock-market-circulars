## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of The Wealth Company Gold ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from December 16, 2025 to December 22, 2025, with allotment scheduled for December 29, 2025. Two plan options are available: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth, both categorized under Equity with the same ISIN INF2F0001370.

## Key Points

- The Wealth Company Gold ETF NFO will be available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- NFO subscription period: December 16-22, 2025
- Two plans available: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth
- Both plans share ISIN: INF2F0001370
- Category: Equity
- Allotment Date: December 29, 2025
- Fund house: The Wealth Company Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 26, 2025, 11:59 PM
- Funds must be transferred for NFO subscriptions processed through the platform

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 22, 2025**: NFO closing date
- **December 26, 2025, 11:59 PM**: Deadline for clear funds to reach Clearing Corporation account
- **December 29, 2025**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine notification about a new fund offering. The launch provides investors with an additional Gold ETF investment option through The Wealth Company Mutual Fund. Members of NSE MF Invest Platform need to ensure timely fund settlement for client subscriptions during the NFO period. The circular does not affect trading operations, compliance requirements, or existing market participants beyond those involved in mutual fund distribution.
