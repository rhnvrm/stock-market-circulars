## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Aequs Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol AEQUS with ISIN INE947N01017. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Aequs Limited
- Trading Symbol: AEQUS
- ISIN: INE947N01017
- Listing Type: IPO
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71694, Circular No. 2380/2025
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 09, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO listing announcement with limited immediate market impact. The circular provides advance notice to exchange members about the new security that will be available for trading. Impact will primarily affect:

- Investors interested in participating in the new listing
- Market participants preparing for trading in AEQUS shares
- IPO subscribers awaiting listing date confirmation

The actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile, IPO details, and market conditions at the time of listing.
