## Summary

Hercules Investments Limited has applied for voluntary delisting under Regulation 5 & 6 of SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021. The company's equity shares (Symbol: HERCULES, Series: EQ) will be suspended from trading effective January 02, 2026, and will be completely delisted from NSE effective January 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Hercules Investments Limited (Symbol: HERCULES)
- Series affected: EQ (Equity)
- Delisting type: Voluntary delisting
- Regulatory framework: SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021 - Regulation 5 & 6
- Trading suspension date: January 02, 2026
- Final delisting date: January 09, 2026
- Circular issued by: Varsha Gandhi, Manager, NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

This action is taken pursuant to Regulation 5 and 6 of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021. The company has formally applied for voluntary delisting in compliance with these regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must note the suspension of trading effective January 02, 2026
- No trading will be permitted in HERCULES equity shares after the suspension date
- Members must inform clients holding HERCULES shares about the delisting timeline
- Admission to dealings in the security will be completely withdrawn from January 09, 2026

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Circular issued (Ref No: NSE/CML/71892, Circular Ref. No: 2025/2485)
- **January 02, 2026**: Trading suspension begins
- **January 09, 2026**: Complete delisting and withdrawal of admission to dealings

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Shareholders of Hercules Investments Limited will no longer be able to trade the stock on NSE after January 02, 2026. This is a permanent removal from the exchange.

**Investor Impact**: High - All existing shareholders must be aware of the delisting timeline. The voluntary delisting process typically involves an exit opportunity for shareholders who wish to sell their holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Moderate - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the suspension and delisting of HERCULES equity shares and ensure proper communication with affected clients.
