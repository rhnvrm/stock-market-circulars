## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Baroda BNP Paribas Gold ETF Fund of Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from August 04, 2025, to August 14, 2025, till 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two scheme variants available: Direct-Growth and Regular-Growth
- Both schemes categorized under Debt category
- NFO period: August 04-14, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account before NFO closing date

## Scheme Details

- **Baroda BNP Paribas Gold ETF Fund of Fund-Direct-Growth**: ISIN INF251K01VD9
- **Baroda BNP Paribas Gold ETF Fund of Fund-Regular-Growth**: ISIN INF251K01UY7

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Members need to facilitate transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: August 04, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: August 14, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The addition of gold ETF fund of fund options provides investors with diversified gold exposure through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The impact is limited to mutual fund segment participants and investors interested in gold-linked investment products.
