## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of privately placed securities issued by Vedika Credit Capital Limited on the capital market segment. The security is an 11.5% coupon bond maturing in April 2028, which will be available for trading from December 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- New debt security listed: VCCL28A 11.5% 2028 bonds
- Trading symbol: 115VCCL28A (Series N0)
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Coupon rate: 11.5% per annum
- ISIN: INE04HY07385
- Issuer: Vedika Credit Capital Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code 115VCCL28A for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Security will be identified on the trading system only by its designated code

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: April 22, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of a privately placed debt security. The listing provides an additional trading instrument for debt market participants. The security will trade in the capital market segment with a market lot size of 1, offering flexibility for investors in this corporate bond.
