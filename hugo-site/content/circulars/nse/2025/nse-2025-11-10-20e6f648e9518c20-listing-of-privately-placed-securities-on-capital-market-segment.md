## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities of NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited on the Capital Market segment effective November 11, 2025. The security is a 7.41% coupon rate senior privately placed debenture maturing on February 20, 2031, with symbol 741NIIF31 (Series N0) and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited
- **Security**: NIIF 7.41% 2031 Sr PP 5
- **Symbol**: 741NIIF31
- **Series**: N0
- **Market Lot Size**: 1
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.41%
- **Maturity Date**: February 20, 2031
- **ISIN Code**: INE246R07830
- Trading to be conducted using designated security codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified only by designated codes (741NIIF31) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1)
- Members must comply with Regulation 2.5.5 regarding lot sizes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: February 20, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard private placement listing that adds one debt security to the trading platform. The security is accessible to institutional and qualified investors who participated in the private placement. No impact on broader market operations or existing listed securities.
