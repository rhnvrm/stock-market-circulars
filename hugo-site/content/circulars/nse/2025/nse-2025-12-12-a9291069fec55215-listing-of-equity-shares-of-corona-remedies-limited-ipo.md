## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of CORONA Remedies Limited following its Initial Public Offering. The company will trade under the symbol CORONA with ISIN INE02ZQ01018. The specific listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: CORONA Remedies Limited
- Trading Symbol: CORONA
- ISIN: INE02ZQ01018
- Listing Type: Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding a new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate impact on market operations. This is a routine IPO listing announcement. The new equity shares will become available for trading once the listing date is formally announced. Members should update their systems to recognize the new security symbol CORONA and its associated ISIN.
