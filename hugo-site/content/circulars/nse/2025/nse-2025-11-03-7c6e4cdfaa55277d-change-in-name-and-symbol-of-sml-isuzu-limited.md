## Summary

NSE has announced that SML Isuzu Limited will undergo a corporate name change to SML Mahindra Limited, along with a corresponding change in trading symbol from SMLISUZU to SMLMAH. The changes will be effective from November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: SML Isuzu Limited
- New company name: SML Mahindra Limited
- Existing trading symbol: SMLISUZU
- New trading symbol: SMLMAH
- Effective date: November 10, 2025
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/71094, Circular Ref. No: 2126/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving name and symbol change of a listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- Trading under the new symbol SMLMAH will commence from November 10, 2025
- All stakeholders should update their records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name and symbol change will require updates to trading systems, databases, and investor portfolios. Investors holding shares should be aware of the new symbol for trading purposes.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and market participants must ensure their systems are updated before the effective date to prevent any trading disruptions. The change from SMLISUZU to SMLMAH indicates a potential corporate restructuring or partnership change with Mahindra group.
