## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed non-convertible debentures issued by CEAT Limited on the capital market segment. The 7.20% debentures maturing on December 30, 2030 will be admitted to trading with effect from January 01, 2026 under the symbol 720CL30 (Series N0) with ISIN INE482A08033.

## Key Points

- CEAT Limited's 7.20% debentures (maturity: Dec 30, 2030) listed on capital market segment
- Trading symbol: 720CL30, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE482A08033
- Listing effective from January 01, 2026
- Securities admitted pursuant to NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security code 720CL30 for system trading
- Trading shall be conducted in market lot size of 1 unit as specified
- Lot size compliance as per Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: December 30, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for listing of a single privately placed debt security. The listing provides an additional trading avenue for investors holding CEAT Limited's debentures but does not affect broader market operations or trading regulations. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in CEAT Limited's fixed-income securities.
