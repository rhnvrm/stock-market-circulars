## Summary

Fujiyama Power Systems Limited's equity shares have been admitted to dealings on the National Stock Exchange Capital Market segment effective November 20, 2025. The company issued 306,410,934 equity shares of Re. 1/- each at an issue price of Rs. 228/-. Trading will commence under the symbol UTLSOLAR (ISIN: INE12UR01024) with a market lot size of 1 share. The security will be part of the pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: UTLSOLAR
- **Company Name**: Fujiyama Power Systems Limited
- **ISIN**: INE12UR01024
- **Face Value**: Re. 1/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 228/-
- **Total Shares**: 306,410,934 equity shares
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 share
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Trading Commencement**: November 20, 2025
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies the listing pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must use designated security code UTLSOLAR for identifying and trading the security
- **Lot Size Compliance**: Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- **Pre-open Session**: Members must note that the security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing adds a new tradeable security in the solar/power systems sector to the NSE Capital Market segment. With an issue size of over 306 million shares at Rs. 228/- per share, this represents a significant capital raise.

**Investor Impact**: The IPO provides investment opportunity in the solar power systems sector. The presence of multiple lock-in periods indicates various categories of shareholders (promoters, anchor investors, etc.) with staggered release schedules.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new security code UTLSOLAR and ensure compliance with pre-open session requirements for IPO category scrips.
