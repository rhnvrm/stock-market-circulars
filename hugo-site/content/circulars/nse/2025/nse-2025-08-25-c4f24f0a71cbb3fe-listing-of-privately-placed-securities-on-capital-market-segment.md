## Summary

NSE has admitted two privately placed debt securities to trading on the capital market segment effective August 26, 2025. The securities are from Demuric Holdings Private Limited (12.15% coupon, maturing August 22, 2027) and Akme Fintrade (India) Limited (12% coupon, maturing August 22, 2027).

## Key Points

- Two new privately placed debt securities admitted for trading
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading to commence August 26, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes: 1215DHPL27 and 12AFIL27
- Both securities mature on August 22, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified market lot sizes
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 26, 2025
- **Maturity Date (both securities):** August 22, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The addition provides more investment options in the debt segment but is unlikely to significantly affect broader market dynamics.
