## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Tata BSE Multicap Consumption 50:30:20 Index Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from December 09, 2025 to December 23, 2025 till 11:59 PM. The fund offers six plan variants covering Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Reinvestment/Payout) options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: December 09, 2025 to December 23, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants available: Direct Plan and Regular Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Allotment date: December 29, 2025
- Clear funds deadline: December 26, 2025 (11:59 PM) in designated Clearing Corporation account
- Six unique ISINs allocated for different plan variants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 26, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Members should facilitate NFO transactions for the specified period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: December 09, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: December 23, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Funds Settlement Deadline**: December 26, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO announcement. It provides an additional investment option for investors interested in consumption-focused multicap equity index funds. The fund follows a 50:30:20 allocation strategy aligned with the BSE Multicap Consumption Index. Members and distributors on the NSE MF Invest Platform can facilitate subscriptions during the NFO period.
