## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of SMC Global Securities Limited pursuant to a bonus issue. The new shares will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment effective November 18, 2025. A total of 10,47,00,000 (10.47 crore) equity shares with ISIN INE103C01036 will be listed.

## Key Points

- Company: SMC Global Securities Limited
- Trading Symbol: SMCGLOBAL
- ISIN: INE103C01036
- Number of new securities: 10,47,00,000 (10.47 crore shares)
- Distinctive Number Range: 113134451 to 217834450
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a bonus issue approved by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the listing of additional equity shares
- Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new securities
- Securities must be identified only by designated codes for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 17, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The bonus issue will increase the number of outstanding shares of SMC Global Securities Limited, improving liquidity. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares proportionate to their holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Routine listing procedure for trading members. Systems need to be updated to reflect the new share count and distinctive number range.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who will receive bonus shares. The increased float may improve trading liquidity and accessibility for retail investors.
