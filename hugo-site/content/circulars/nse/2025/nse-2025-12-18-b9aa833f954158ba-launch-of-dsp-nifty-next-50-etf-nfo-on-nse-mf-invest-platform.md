## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of DSP Nifty Next 50 ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from December 19, 2025 to December 30, 2025 until 11:59 PM. Both Direct Plan and Regular Plan growth options are being offered under the equity category with allotment scheduled for January 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Next 50 ETF NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two plan options: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth
- Both plans share the same ISIN: INF740KA1XU7
- Category: Equity
- NFO subscription period: December 19, 2025 to December 30, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: January 5, 2026
- Members must ensure clear funds are received by January 4, 2026 (11:59 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before January 4, 2026 at 11:59 PM
- Members should facilitate transactions for investors during the NFO period

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 30, 2025 (11:59 PM)**: NFO closing date
- **January 4, 2026 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds receipt
- **January 5, 2026**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new passive investment option tracking the Nifty Next 50 index. The ETF offers exposure to the next 50 largest companies after the Nifty 50, providing diversification opportunities. Impact is limited to investors interested in index-based equity investments and members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the NSE platform.
