## Summary

National Stock Exchange has moved NOIDA TOLL BRIDGE COMPANY LIMITED (NOIDATOLL) to Stage I of Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) effective September 04, 2025. This action implements enhanced surveillance measures including 100% margin requirements and 5% price band restrictions.

## Key Points

- NOIDATOLL (ISIN: INE781B01015) moved to GSM Stage I
- Effective date: September 04, 2025
- Trading margin rate increased to 100%
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower
- Members advised to take adequate precaution while trading

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Stage I GSM framework with specific trading restrictions and margin requirements for the identified security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin rate for NOIDATOLL
- Price band of 5% or lower must be enforced
- Members should exercise additional caution in trading activities
- Queries can be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **September 04, 2025**: GSM Stage I measures become effective for NOIDATOLL

## Impact Assessment

The GSM Stage I classification will significantly impact trading in NOIDATOLL by requiring full margin coverage and limiting price movements, potentially reducing trading volume and liquidity while providing enhanced investor protection through stricter surveillance measures.
