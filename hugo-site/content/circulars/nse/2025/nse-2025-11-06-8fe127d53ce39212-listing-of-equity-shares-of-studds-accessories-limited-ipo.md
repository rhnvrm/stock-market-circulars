## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has admitted equity shares of Studds Accessories Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment effective November 07, 2025. The IPO consists of 39,353,400 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 5 and an issue price of Rs. 585 per share. The security will trade under the symbol "STUDDS" with ISIN INE00Q601028 and a market lot size of 1 share. The scrip will be part of the pre-open session for IPO category.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares admitted: 39,353,400 shares of Rs. 5 face value each
- Issue price: Rs. 585 per share
- Trading symbol: STUDDS
- ISIN: INE00Q601028
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Listing date: November 07, 2025
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Shares issued on Pari Passu basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is issued in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (STUDDS) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The security will be part of pre-open session for IPO category scrips
- Lock-in periods must be observed as per Annexure I

## Important Dates

- Circular issue date: November 06, 2025
- Listing effective date: November 07, 2025
- Lock-in expiry dates vary:
  - Free: 9,817,020 shares (immediately tradeable)
  - December 04, 2025: 1,167,915 shares
  - February 02, 2026: 1,167,921 shares
  - May 06, 2026: Multiple tranches totaling approximately 26.6 million shares
  - May 06, 2027: 16,000 shares
- Financial year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The listing adds a new equity security in the helmet and accessories manufacturing sector. With an IPO size of 39.35 million shares at Rs. 585, the total issue size is approximately Rs. 2,302 crores, representing a significant mid-cap listing.

**Trading Impact:** Approximately 25% of shares (9.8 million) are immediately free for trading, while the majority are under lock-in until May 2026, which will limit initial liquidity. The market lot size of 1 share provides flexibility for retail investors.

**Operational Impact:** Members need to configure trading systems for the new symbol STUDDS. The pre-open session requirement means special handling for price discovery on the listing day.
