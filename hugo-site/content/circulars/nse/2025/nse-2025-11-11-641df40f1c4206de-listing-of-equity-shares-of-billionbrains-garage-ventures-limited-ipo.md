National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol GROWW with ISIN INE0HOQ01053. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

- Company Name: Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited
- Trading Symbol: GROWW
