## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Securities (privately placed) of Toyota Financial Services India Limited with ISIN INE692Q07399, effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is being implemented under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A due to the redemption of these securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ISIN: INE692Q07399
- Issuer: Toyota Financial Services India Limited
- Suspension effective date: December 4, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of securities
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Security type: Non-Convertible Securities (Privately Placed)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular implementing existing regulations for suspension of trading upon redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure no trading occurs in the specified ISIN from the suspension date
- Market participants holding these securities should be aware of the redemption process
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension status

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal and limited to holders of the specific privately placed Non-Convertible Securities. This is a standard administrative action taken when debt securities reach maturity and are redeemed. The suspension affects only one ISIN and does not impact equity trading or other securities of Toyota Financial Services India Limited. Market participants holding these securities will follow normal redemption procedures.
