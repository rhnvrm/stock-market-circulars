## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) NFO by Groww Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from November 14, 2025 to November 28, 2025 until 11:59 pm. Six scheme variants are being launched covering Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 14, 2025 to November 28, 2025 (till 11:59 pm)
- Fund type: Equity Fund of Fund investing in Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF
- Six scheme variants launched with separate ISINs
- Allotment date: December 5, 2025
- Payment deadline: December 2, 2025 (till 7:00 pm)
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Scheme Details

### Direct Plans
1. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MG6)
2. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MH4)
3. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01MI2)

### Regular Plans
4. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MJ0)
5. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MK8)
6. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01ML6)

All schemes are categorized as Equity funds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account through any payment mode
- Funds must be received on or before December 2, 2025 till 7:00 pm
- Transactions to be processed through NSE MF Invest Platform only

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: November 14, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: November 28, 2025 (11:59 pm)
- **Payment Deadline**: December 2, 2025 (7:00 pm)
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new fund offering with minimal market impact. The launch expands mutual fund product offerings on the NSE MF Invest Platform, providing investors access to capital markets sector exposure through a fund of fund structure. No impact on existing securities or trading operations. Members need to ensure operational readiness for processing NFO transactions during the specified period.
