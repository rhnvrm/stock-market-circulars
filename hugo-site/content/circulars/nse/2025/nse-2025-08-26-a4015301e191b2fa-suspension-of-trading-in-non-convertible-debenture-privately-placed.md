## Summary

NSE has issued a circular suspending trading in the Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed) of Clix Capital Services Private Limited (ISIN: INE157D07DX7) effective August 28, 2025, due to redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Clix Capital Services Private Limited's Non-Convertible Debenture
- ISIN: INE157D07DX7
- Suspension effective from August 28, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of the debenture
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is implementation of existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is a notification of administrative action by NSE.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: August 26, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects a single privately placed debt instrument. Routine administrative action due to redemption. No impact on equity markets or broader debt trading operations.
