## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies on the capital market segment, effective August 20, 2025. The circular provides details of three debt instruments with their trading symbols, coupon rates, and maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Three debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment trading
- All securities have market lot size of 1 unit
- Trading to commence August 20, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Listed Securities

**Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited:**
- AIFL 7.6377% 2029 Sr E1 (Symbol: 76377AIF29, ISIN: INE0AD507176)
- AIFL 7.73% 2030 Sr E2 (Symbol: 773AIFL30, ISIN: INE0AD507184)

**IIFL Samasta Finance Limited:**
- ISF 9.75% 2035 (Symbol: 975ISF35, ISIN: INE413U08176)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 20, 2025
- **Maturity Dates:** January 18, 2029; August 16, 2030; August 20, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative action with minimal market impact. Provides additional debt investment options for institutional and qualified investors in the privately placed securities market.
