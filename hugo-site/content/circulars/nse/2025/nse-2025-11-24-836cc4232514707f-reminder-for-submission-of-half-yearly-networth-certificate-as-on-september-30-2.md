## Summary

NSE has issued a reminder circular to all trading members regarding the mandatory submission of Half Yearly Networth Certificate as on September 30, 2025. This is a follow-up to the original circular NSE/COMP/70884 dated October 16, 2025. Members must submit the certificate through the ENIT portal by November 30, 2025 to avoid penal or disciplinary action.

## Key Points

- Reminder for submission of Half Yearly Networth Certificate as on September 30, 2025
- Submission must be done through ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/)
- Detailed submission manual available in original circular NSE/COMP/70884 dated October 16, 2025
- Non-compliance will result in penal/disciplinary action as per circular NSE/INSP/70746 dated October 10, 2025
- Helpdesk support available at 1800 266 0050 (IVR option 3) or memcompliance_support@nse.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a reminder for existing compliance requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Applicable to:** All Trading Members of NSE
- **Requirement:** Submit Half Yearly Networth Certificate as on September 30, 2025
- **Submission method:** ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/)
- **Documentation:** Follow detailed manual provided in circular NSE/COMP/70884 dated October 16, 2025
- **Consequences:** Penal/disciplinary action for non-compliance as prescribed in circular NSE/INSP/70746 dated October 10, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Reference Date:** September 30, 2025 (networth calculation date)
- **Submission Deadline:** November 30, 2025
- **Circular Issue Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Original Circular Date:** October 16, 2025 (NSE/COMP/70884)
- **Penal Guidelines Circular:** October 10, 2025 (NSE/INSP/70746)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** High - All trading members must complete this mandatory filing within the specified deadline. Failure to comply will result in penal/disciplinary action that could affect trading privileges.

**Market Impact:** Medium - While this is a routine compliance requirement, non-compliance by members could lead to trading restrictions or penalties that may affect their operations.

**Member Impact:** High - Trading members need to ensure timely submission through ENIT portal. Those who have not yet submitted need to act immediately to meet the November 30, 2025 deadline and avoid penalties.

**Compliance Burden:** Medium - Members need to prepare and submit networth certificates through the online portal. Detailed manual and helpdesk support are provided to facilitate the process.
