## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of Tata Communications Limited's 6.77% debentures maturing in 2028 on the capital market segment, effective from August 12, 2025. The securities will trade under symbol 677TCL28 with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Single debt security being listed: TCL 6.77% 2028 debentures
- Trading symbol: 677TCL28, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE151A08356
- Issuer: Tata Communications Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify securities by designated codes only
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 7, 2028
- **Circular Date**: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of a single debt security. The addition provides investors with another fixed-income investment option from Tata Communications Limited with a 6.77% coupon rate and approximately 3-year maturity period.
