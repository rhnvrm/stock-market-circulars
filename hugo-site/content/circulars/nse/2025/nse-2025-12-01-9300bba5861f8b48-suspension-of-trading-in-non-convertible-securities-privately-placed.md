## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in one Non-Convertible Security (privately placed debenture) of Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited effective December 2, 2025. The suspension is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A and is due to the redemption of the security.

## Key Points

- Single debenture with ISIN INE033L08247 to be suspended from trading
- Issuer: Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited
- Suspension effective date: December 2, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the security
- Action taken under NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notification regarding suspension of a specific debt security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should note the suspension and ensure no trading occurs in the specified ISIN from December 2, 2025
- Market participants holding positions in this security should be aware of the redemption

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects a single privately placed debt security. The suspension is due to normal course redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Security holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the debenture. No trading disruption for other securities.

**Operational Impact**: Routine - Standard suspension process for redeemed debt securities with no broader market implications.
