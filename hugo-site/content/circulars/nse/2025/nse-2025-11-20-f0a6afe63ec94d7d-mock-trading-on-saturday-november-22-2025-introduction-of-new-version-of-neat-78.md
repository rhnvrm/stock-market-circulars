## Summary

NSE is conducting a mandatory mock trading session on Saturday, November 22, 2025 for the Futures & Options segment to test the new pre-open session feature. The exchange will discontinue NEAT+ version 7.8.5 and introduce version 7.8.6 effective from the mock session. All trading members must download and use the latest NEAT+ version 7.8.6 from November 22, 2025 onwards.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for November 22, 2025 with three trading sessions
- NEAT+ version 7.8.6 will be introduced and version 7.8.5 will be discontinued
- Pre-open session will be tested only in Trading Session-1 (09:00 to 11:00 hrs)
- Pre-open session applicable only for November & December month futures contracts (FUTIDX & FUTSTK)
- Members must actively participate in all three trading sessions
- New NEAT+ 7.8.6 available for download from November 21, 2025 at 19:00 hrs
- Download path: /faoftp/faocommon/NEATPlus786 on NSE Extranet
- After November 22, 2025, only NEAT+ versions 7.8.4 and 7.8.6 will be available

## Regulatory Changes

Introduction of Pre-Open Session in Equity Derivatives (F&O) Segment as per circular NSE/FAOP/71092 dated November 03, 2025. This represents a significant change to the trading workflow for futures and options contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**

1. Download NEAT+ version 7.8.6 from NSE Extranet before November 22, 2025
2. Participate actively in all three mock trading sessions on November 22, 2025
3. Use NEAT+ version 7.8.6 for placing orders in pre-open session
4. Load master files (contract.gz, NSE_FO_contract_22112025.csv) available on NSE Extranet (Path: /faoftp/faocommon/Mock/22Nov2025) before 8:30 hrs on November 22, 2025
5. For live trading on November 24, 2025, download master files generated after end-of-day activities on November 21, 2025
6. Discontinue use of NEAT+ version 7.8.5 from November 22, 2025

## Important Dates

**November 21, 2025:**
- 19:00 hrs - NEAT+ version 7.8.6 available for download

**November 22, 2025 (Mock Trading):**
- **Trading Session-1 (Primary Site with Pre-Open):**
  - 09:00 hrs - Pre-open session opens
  - 09:45 hrs - Pre-open session closes (random closure in last one minute)
  - 10:00 hrs - Normal market opens
  - 11:00 hrs - Normal market closes

- **Trading Session-2 (Primary Site without Pre-Open):**
  - 16:30 hrs - Normal market opens
  - 17:30 hrs - Normal market closes

- **Trading Session-3 (DR Site without Pre-Open):**
  - 18:15 hrs - Normal market opens
  - 18:45 hrs - Normal market closes
  - 18:55 hrs - Trade modification end time

- **Live Re-login:**
  - 19:30 hrs - Live re-login start time
  - 20:00 hrs - Live re-login close time

- NEAT+ version 7.8.5 discontinued (mock session)

**November 24, 2025:**
- Live trading resumes with new pre-open session functionality

**December 06, 2025:**
- NEAT+ version 7.8.4 discontinuation date (mock)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:**
- All F&O trading members must upgrade their trading systems to NEAT+ 7.8.6
- Members using version 7.8.5 will be unable to trade after November 22, 2025
- Introduction of pre-open session will change order placement workflows for November and December futures contracts
- Three separate mock trading sessions require member participation and system testing
- Orders and trades from Trading Session-1 will not carry over to Trading Session-2

**Market Impact:**
- Pre-open session will improve price discovery for equity derivatives
- Random closure mechanism in pre-open session (last one minute) introduces new trading dynamics
- Limited to index futures (FUTIDX) and stock futures (FUTSTK) for November and December expiries initially
- No impact on options contracts during initial pre-open implementation

**Technical Impact:**
- Members must ensure compatibility with new NEAT+ version 7.8.6
- Master file loading requirements add operational steps before mock trading
- Separate master files required for mock session versus live trading on November 24, 2025
