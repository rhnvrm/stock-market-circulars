## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 privately placed commercial paper securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective December 22, 2025. The securities include commercial paper issued by major financial institutions and corporates including Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited, HDFC Securities Limited, HSBC Investdirect Financial Services, The Ramco Cements Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Axis Securities Limited, Welspun Corp Limited, Aditya Birla Capital Limited, IIFL Finance Limited, and Small Industries Development Bank of India.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities admitted to NSE debt market segment
- All securities allotted on December 19, 2025
- Redemption dates range from March 18-20, 2026 to December 18, 2026
- Face values of Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity ranges from 1,000 to 111,500 units across different issuances
- Issue prices reflect discount to face value based on tenor and yield
- ISIN codes assigned for each security for trading and settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for members. Standard debt market trading and settlement procedures apply.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 19, 2025 (all securities)
- **Redemption Dates:** March 18-20, 2026 (9 securities), December 18, 2026 (1 security - ABCL)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative circular informing members of new debt instruments available for trading. The listing provides additional commercial paper options for debt market participants and institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or trading operations.
