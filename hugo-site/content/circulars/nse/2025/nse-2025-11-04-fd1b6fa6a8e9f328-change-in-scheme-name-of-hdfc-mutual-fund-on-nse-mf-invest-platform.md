## Summary

HDFC Mutual Fund has changed the name of one of its schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The scheme previously known as "HDFC Multi-Asset Fund" has been renamed to "HDFC Multi-Asset Allocation Fund" with immediate effect.

## Key Points

- Scheme name change is effective immediately on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Old name: HDFC Multi-Asset Fund
- New name: HDFC Multi-Asset Allocation Fund
- Change communicated by HDFC Mutual Fund to NSE members
- Notice cum addendum issued by HDFC Mutual Fund attached with circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting a scheme name modification by HDFC Mutual Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed to note the scheme name change in their systems and communications. No specific compliance actions required beyond updating scheme name references.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 04, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Circular Date**: November 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is purely an administrative name change for a mutual fund scheme. The underlying investment strategy, portfolio, and operations remain unchanged. Investors and members only need to update their records with the new scheme name. No trading, listing, or compliance implications for equity market participants.
