## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Ugro Capital Limited effective August 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of these debt securities.

## Key Points

- Two NCDs of Ugro Capital Limited will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from August 12, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the securities
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Securities Details

- **1025UCL25** (ISIN: INE583D07414) - Ugro Capital Limited NCD
- **1075UCL25** (ISIN: INE583D07430) - Ugro Capital Limited NCD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action for matured securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. The securities will no longer be available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **August 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **August 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to maturity. Investors holding these NCDs will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No impact on equity shares of Ugro Capital Limited.
