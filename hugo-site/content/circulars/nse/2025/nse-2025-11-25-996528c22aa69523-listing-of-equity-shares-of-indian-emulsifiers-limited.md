## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of equity shares of Indian Emulsifiers Limited (Symbol: IEML) on the Capital Market segment - SME EMERGE platform. The listing includes 6,111,000 equity shares allotted under a Rights Issue, effective from November 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Indian Emulsifiers Limited
- **Symbol**: IEML
- **Series**: SM (SME)
- **ISIN**: INE0RRU01016 (temporary ISIN: IN8*********)
- **Number of Shares**: 6,111,000 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Paid-up Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 80 per share
- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 12222223 to 18333222
- **Market Lot**: 500 shares
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Lock-in**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (IEML) for all trading activities
- Trading in the security must be in the specified market lot size of 500 shares
- Shares allotted in dematerialized mode have been credited under temporary ISIN in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Effective Date of Listing**: November 26, 2025
- **Circular Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for an SME company on the EMERGE platform. The impact is limited to investors and traders interested in the SME segment. The rights issue allotment of approximately 6.11 million shares provides existing shareholders the opportunity to increase their holdings. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions, allowing immediate trading once listed.
