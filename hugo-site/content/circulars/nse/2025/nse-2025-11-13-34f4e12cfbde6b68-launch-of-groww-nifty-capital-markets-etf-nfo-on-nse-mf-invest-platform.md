## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The new fund offer will be available for transactions from November 14, 2025 to November 28, 2025 until 11:59 pm. Two plan options are available - Direct Growth and Regular Growth, both under the equity category with an allotment date of December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 14, 2025 to November 28, 2025 (until 11:59 pm)
- Two scheme options available: Direct Growth (ISIN: INF666M01MM4) and Regular Growth (ISIN: INF666M01MN2)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity
- Allotment date: December 5, 2025
- Payment deadline: December 2, 2025 (until 07:00 pm)
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account through any payment mode

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before December 2, 2025 until 07:00 pm for NFO transactions.

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: November 14, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: November 28, 2025 (11:59 pm)
- **Payment Deadline**: December 2, 2025 (07:00 pm)
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine announcement for a new ETF launch. The impact is limited to members and investors interested in participating in the Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF NFO through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The scheme provides investors with exposure to the Nifty Capital Markets index through an exchange-traded fund structure.
