## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Physicswallah Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol PWL with ISIN INE0LP301011. The specific listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Physicswallah Limited
- Trading Symbol: PWL
- ISIN: INE0LP301011
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71307, Circular No. 2226/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding an upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact for market participants:

- **New Investment Opportunity**: Introduction of Physicswallah Limited shares to the equity market
- **Market Expansion**: Addition of an education technology company to NSE listings
- **Trading Preparation**: Members should prepare systems and infrastructure for trading the new security
- **Investor Interest**: Expected to attract investors interested in the education and technology sectors

No immediate operational changes or compliance actions are required from exchange members at this time.
