## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol EMMVEE with ISIN INE1C6T01020. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited
- Trading Symbol: EMMVEE
- ISIN: INE1C6T01020
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71313, Circular No. 2227/2025
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for exchange members. This is an informational circular about an upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new equity security in the renewable energy/solar sector to NSE. The impact is limited to investors interested in this specific company and the photovoltaic/solar energy sector. No immediate market-wide implications. Members are informed to prepare their systems for trading this new security once the listing date is announced.
