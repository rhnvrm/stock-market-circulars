## Summary

NSE announces the listing of units issued by Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited under the Motilal Oswal Silver ETF scheme. The listing becomes effective from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Symbol: MOSILVER
- Total units listed: 465,000 units
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 114/- per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- ISIN: INF247L01FZ1
- Date of allotment: August 11, 2025

## Market Makers and Intermediaries

- **Market Makers**: Motilal Oswal Financial Services Ltd (TM Code: 10412), Kanjalochana Finserve Private Ltd (TM Code: 90246), East India Securities Ltd (TM Code: 08134)
- **Registrar**: Kfin Technologies Limited

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: August 11, 2025

## Contact Information

- **Asset Management Company**: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited, Mumbai
- **Trustee**: Motilal Oswal Trustee Company Limited, Mumbai
- **Registrar**: Kfin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Impact Assessment

Routine ETF listing that provides investors with exposure to silver through exchange-traded units. Minimal impact on overall market operations.
