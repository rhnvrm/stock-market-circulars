## Summary

DSP Mutual Fund is launching the DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from August 08, 2025, to August 22, 2025, until 11:45 PM with allotment scheduled for August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme options available: Regular and Direct variants with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Transaction window: August 08-22, 2025 (until 11:45 PM)
- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account before NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Standard NFO transaction procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: August 08, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: August 22, 2025 (11:45 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. Provides investors with additional investment option in equity index funds focused on quality stocks from Nifty 500 universe. No direct impact on existing securities or trading operations.
