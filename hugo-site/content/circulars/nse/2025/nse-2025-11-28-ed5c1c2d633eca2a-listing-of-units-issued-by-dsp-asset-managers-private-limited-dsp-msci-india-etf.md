## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of units issued by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited for DSP MSCI India ETF on the Capital Market segment effective December 01, 2025. A total of 2,129,862 units with face value of Rs. 10/- each will be available for trading under the symbol MSCIADD with ISIN INF740KA1WV7. The units were allotted on November 25, 2025 at an issue price of Rs. 30.37 per unit.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: MSCIADD
- ISIN: INF740KA1WV7
- Total Units Listed: 2,129,862
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 30.37 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Nine market makers appointed for liquidity provision

## Market Makers

The following entities have been designated as market makers for MSCIADD:
- Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd. (TM Code: 90246)
- Parwati Capital Market Pvt Ltd. (TM Code: 11890)
- Share India Securities Ltd. (TM Code: 10798)
- East India Securities Ltd. (TM Code: 8134)
- Kotak Securities Limited (TM Code: 8081)
- Achintya Securities Pvt Ltd. (TM Code: 13302)
- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (TM Code: 13773)
- Irage Broking Services LLP (TM Code: 90068)
- Vaibhav Stock Broking and Derivatives Pvt Ltd. (TM Code: 12151)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security code MSCIADD for all transactions
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot of 1 unit
- Market makers must fulfill their liquidity provision obligations

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Date of unit allotment
- **November 28, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 01, 2025**: Effective date for trading commencement

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with another passive investment vehicle tracking the MSCI India Index. The appointment of nine market makers ensures adequate liquidity for trading. The ETF offers diversified exposure to Indian equities through MSCI India constituent stocks. Impact is limited to investors seeking index-based investment strategies and does not affect broader market operations.
