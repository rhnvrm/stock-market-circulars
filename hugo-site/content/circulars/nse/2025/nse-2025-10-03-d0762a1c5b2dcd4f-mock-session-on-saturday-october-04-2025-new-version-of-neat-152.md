## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Commodity Derivatives Segment on Saturday, October 04, 2025, introducing NEAT version 1.5.2. The session includes trading from both Primary Site (11:00-14:00) and BCP site (14:45-16:00), with live re-login window from 18:00-18:30. NEAT version 1.5.2 will be available from October 03, 2025 at 18:00 hrs, while version 1.5.1 will be discontinued on November 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 04, 2025
- New NEAT version 1.5.2 available from October 03, 2025 at 18:00 hrs
- Download path: /comtftp/comtcommon/NEATCO152
- Trading Session-1 (Primary Site): 11:00-14:00 with contingency period 12:00-13:30
- Trading Session-2 (BCP site): 14:45-16:00
- Live re-login window: 18:00-18:30 on October 04, 2025
- NEAT version 1.5.1 discontinuation date: November 01, 2025 (mock)
- Connect2NSE and Extranet unavailable from 11:00 am to 02:00 pm
- Extranet API unavailable from 11:00 am to 11:30 am
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during mock session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a technical system upgrade and testing session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate using all trading software and re-login into live environment to ensure smooth connectivity for Monday, October 06, 2025
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed (UCC validation skipped during contingency time)
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- Members may use Mock Trading or Simulated Environment to meet SEBI regulatory requirements per circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234

## Important Dates

- **October 03, 2025, 18:00 hrs**: NEAT version 1.5.2 available for download
- **October 04, 2025, 11:00-14:00**: Mock trading Session-1 from Primary Site
- **October 04, 2025, 14:45-16:00**: Mock trading Session-2 from BCP site
- **October 04, 2025, 16:10**: Position Limit/Collateral value setup cutoff and Trade modification end time
- **October 04, 2025, 18:00-18:30**: Live re-login window
- **November 01, 2025 (mock)**: NEAT version 1.5.1 discontinuation date

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Members must prepare for NEAT version migration and participate in mock session to ensure readiness for live trading on October 06, 2025. System downtime expected for Connect2NSE/Extranet (11:00 am - 02:00 pm) and Extranet API (11:00 am - 11:30 am).

**Market Impact**: None - Mock session trades have no financial obligations and will not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out.

**Technical Impact**: Members should test all trading software, connectivity parameters, and disaster recovery procedures during the mock session to ensure operational continuity.
