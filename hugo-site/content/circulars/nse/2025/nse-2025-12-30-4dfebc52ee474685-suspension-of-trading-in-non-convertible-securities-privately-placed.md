## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular notifying the suspension of trading in non-convertible securities (privately placed) of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. The suspension is effective from December 31, 2025, and is being implemented due to the redemption of these securities. This action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ISIN: INE860H07IE6 of Aditya Birla Capital Limited
- Suspension effective date: December 31, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Redemption of securities
- Applies only to non-convertible securities in the debt market segment
- Securities were privately placed
- Circular reference number: 2544/2025
- NSE reference number: NSE/CML/72063

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notification under existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified ISIN (INE860H07IE6) from December 31, 2025
- All pending orders and positions in these securities must be settled as per exchange regulations
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension status

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting only privately placed non-convertible securities (debt instruments) of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. The suspension does not impact the company's equity shares or other listed securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific ISIN (INE860H07IE6) who will not be able to trade these securities post-suspension. Since the reason is redemption, investors will receive their principal and applicable interest as per the terms of the securities.

**Operational Impact**: Standard operational adjustment for exchange members to update trading systems and halt trading in the specified security.
