## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities issued by Jio Credit Limited on the capital market segment. The securities will be admitted to trading with effect from November 21, 2025. The listing is made pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Jio Credit Limited
- **Security Symbol**: 726JCL30
- **Issue Description**: JCL 7.26% 2030 Sr IV
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.26%
- **Date of Maturity**: November 18, 2030
- **ISIN Code**: INE282H07042
- **Market Lot Size**: 1
- **Series**: N0
- **Trading Commencement**: November 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code **726JCL30** for trading purposes on the system
- Trading in these securities must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Members must comply with Regulation 2.5.5 regarding lot sizes for the listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 21, 2025 (trading commencement)
- **Maturity Date**: November 18, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors access to Jio Credit Limited's debt securities on the exchange platform. The impact is limited to investors interested in this specific corporate bond issue and does not affect broader market operations or trading regulations.
