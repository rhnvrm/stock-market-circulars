## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance activity on the NSE MF Invest Platform on December 27, 2025. The platform will be unavailable for one hour from 04:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs. Members are advised to refrain from conducting any activities or transactions during this maintenance window.

## Key Points

- NSE MF Invest Platform will be offline for maintenance on December 27, 2025
- Downtime duration: 04:00 hrs to 05:00 hrs (1 hour)
- No activities or transactions should be carried out during this period
- Members using API connections should inform their technical teams/vendors
- Support available via toll-free number 1800 2100 940 or email support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform during the scheduled maintenance window
- Members connecting via API must inform their technical team/vendor about the scheduled downtime
- Members should plan their activities to accommodate this maintenance window

## Important Dates

- **December 27, 2025, 04:00 hrs**: Maintenance window starts - NSE MF Invest Platform unavailable
- **December 27, 2025, 05:00 hrs**: Maintenance window ends - Platform expected to resume normal operations

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this maintenance activity is minimal as it is scheduled during early morning hours (04:00-05:00) when trading and investment activities are typically low. The one-hour duration is relatively short and should not significantly affect member operations. Members have been given advance notice to plan their activities accordingly and avoid scheduling critical transactions during this window. Members using API integrations should coordinate with their technical teams to ensure systems are prepared for the brief outage.
