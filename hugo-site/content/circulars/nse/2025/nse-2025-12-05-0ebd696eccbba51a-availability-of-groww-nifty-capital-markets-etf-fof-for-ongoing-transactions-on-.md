## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF Fund of Funds (FOF) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 08, 2025. The fund will offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities across six scheme variants.

## Key Points

- Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF reopening for transactions from December 08, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Facilities include ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment (Circular Ref. No: 1038/2025)

## Scheme Details

### Direct Plans
1. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MG6)
2. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MH4)
3. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Direct IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01MI2)

### Regular Plans
4. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MJ0)
5. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MK8)
6. **Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF - Regular IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01ML6)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure the schemes are available for transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform from December 08, 2025
- All transaction facilities (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) must be operational

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025 - Schemes reopened for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of specific mutual fund schemes on the NSE platform. It provides investors access to Groww Nifty Capital Markets ETF FOF schemes with various investment and redemption options. The reopening allows both retail and institutional investors to participate in these equity-focused fund of funds offerings through systematic investment facilities.
