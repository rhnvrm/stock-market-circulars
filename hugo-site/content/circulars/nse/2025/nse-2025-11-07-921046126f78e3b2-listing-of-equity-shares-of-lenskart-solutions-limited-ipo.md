## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol LENSKART with ISIN INE956O01016. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Lenskart Solutions Limited
- Trading Symbol: LENSKART
- ISIN: INE956O01016
- Listing date to be announced via separate circular
- Circular issued by Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members at this stage. Members are informed for awareness purposes and should await the subsequent circular announcing the actual listing date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 07, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is an informational circular regarding a new IPO listing. The impact is primarily relevant for market participants interested in trading Lenskart Solutions Limited shares. The company operates in the eyewear and optical retail sector. No immediate operational or trading changes are required from exchange members. The listing will expand the available universe of tradable securities on NSE.
