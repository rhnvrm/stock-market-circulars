## Summary

NSE has announced that Mirae Asset Gold Silver Passive Fund of Fund (FOF) will be available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting September 01, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across six different plan variants.

## Key Points

- Mirae Asset Gold Silver Passive FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Effective date: September 01, 2025
- Available facilities: Subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants available (Direct/Regular plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- All schemes categorized under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational announcement for fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure proper implementation of the fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **September 01, 2025**: Effective date for ongoing transactions availability

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine reopening of a mutual fund scheme. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking exposure to gold and silver through fund of fund structure on NSE platform.
