## Summary

NSE has notified the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (Privately Placed) of Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited effective November 19, 2025. The suspension is being implemented under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A due to the redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Single NCD (ISIN: INE0L6807054) of Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited will be suspended from trading
- Suspension is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- The suspension applies to privately placed non-convertible debentures
- Reason for suspension is redemption of the instrument
- Circular reference number: 2240/2025, NSE/CML/71349

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must cease trading in the specified NCD (ISIN: INE0L6807054) from the suspension date. No other compliance actions required as this is a standard suspension notification.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects a single privately placed NCD instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific NCD who will not be able to trade the instrument post-suspension. The redemption indicates investors will receive their principal and applicable interest.

**Operational Impact**: Routine administrative change with no significant operational implications for market infrastructure or trading members.
