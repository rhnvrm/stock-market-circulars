## Summary

NSE has issued a correction to circular NSE/CML/71371 dated November 19, 2025, modifying only the distinctive number range for equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited listed pursuant to Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP). All other details of the original circular remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Corrects distinctive number range for ICICI Bank ESOP allotment
- Number of securities remains 1,271,557 equity shares
- Shares allotted on November 13, 2025
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2 per share
- ISIN: INE090A01021 (Symbol: ICICIBANK)
- Revised distinctive number range: 7146107105 to 7147378661
- Market lot: 1 share
- Pari passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative correction to previously issued circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should note the corrected distinctive number range for record-keeping purposes
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Circular Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Original Circular Reference:** NSE/CML/71371 dated November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is purely an administrative correction to the distinctive number range for already allotted ESOP shares. No changes to the number of securities, allotment date, or other material terms. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions. Market participants should update their records with the corrected distinctive number range.
