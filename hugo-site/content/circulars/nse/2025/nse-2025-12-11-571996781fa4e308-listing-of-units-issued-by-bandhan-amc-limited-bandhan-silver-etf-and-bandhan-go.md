## Summary

NSE has admitted Bandhan Silver ETF (Symbol: SILVERBND) and Bandhan Gold ETF (Symbol: GOLDBND) units to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 12, 2025. The circular provides security codes, lot sizes, and contact details for the trustee, asset management company, and registrar.

## Key Points

- Bandhan Silver ETF (SILVERBND) with 527,061 units listed, ISIN: INF194KB1KI9
- Bandhan Gold ETF (GOLDBND) with 710,898 units listed, ISIN: INF194KB1KJ7
- Both ETFs have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10/- per unit
- Silver ETF issue price: Rs. 178.35/-
- Gold ETF issue price: Rs. 129.05/-
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both ETFs
- Date of allotment: December 5, 2025
- Series: EQ for both securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security codes for identification: SILVERBND and GOLDBND
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot sizes (1 unit each)
- Securities admitted to dealings as per NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Circular Issue Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard ETF listing. The addition of these precious metals ETFs expands investment options for retail and institutional investors seeking exposure to gold and silver. The low market lot size of 1 unit makes these ETFs accessible to small investors. Contact persons and operational details provided ensure smooth trading operations.
