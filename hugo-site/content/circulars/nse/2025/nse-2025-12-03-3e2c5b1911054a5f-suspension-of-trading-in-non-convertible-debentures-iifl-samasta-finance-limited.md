## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in two series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by IIFL Samasta Finance Limited, effective December 05, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of these debt securities.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of IIFL Samasta Finance Limited will be suspended from trading
- Symbol 921ISFL25 (ISIN: INE413U07244) - suspended due to maturity
- Symbol 960ISFL25 (ISIN: INE413U07228) - suspended due to maturity
- Suspension effective date: December 05, 2025
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action for debt securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified of the suspension and should cease trading in the specified NCDs from December 05, 2025
- No action required from market participants beyond awareness of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 05, 2025
- **Reason:** Maturity of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting two specific NCD series that have reached maturity. Bondholders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the debentures.

**Operational Impact:** No significant operational impact on market participants. Standard procedure for matured debt instruments.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to holders of these specific NCDs who will no longer be able to trade these securities on the exchange after December 05, 2025, as they will be redeemed upon maturity.
