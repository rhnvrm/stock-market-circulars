## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership for two trading members: Asset Alliance Securities Pvt. Ltd. and Pentagon Stock Brokers Private Limited, both effective July 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Asset Alliance Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg: INZ000256836) surrendered membership on July 25, 2025
- Pentagon Stock Brokers Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INZ000068338) surrendered membership on July 25, 2025
- Both surrenders were approved by NSE
- All members have been notified of these changes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine administrative action for membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are required to take note of these membership surrenders
- No specific action required from other members

## Important Dates

- **July 25, 2025**: Surrender approval date for both trading members
- **August 5, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative process. The surrender of these two trading members does not affect overall market operations or other members' trading activities.
