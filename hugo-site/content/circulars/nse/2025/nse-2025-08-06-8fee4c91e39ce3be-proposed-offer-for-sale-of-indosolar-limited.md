## Summary

NSE announces the proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of Indosolar Limited shares scheduled for August 07-08, 2025. Up to 12,50,000 equity shares with face value of Rs. 10 each will be offered at Rs. 500 per share through the eOFS platform.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Up to 12,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each
- Offer price: Rs. 500 per share
- Trading symbol: WAAREEINDO
- ISIN: INE866K01023
- Market lot: 1 equity share
- Tick size: Rs. 0.05
- Two series available: IS (Non-retail) and RS (Retail - bid value â‰¤ Rs. 2 lacs)

## Regulatory Changes

OFS implementation follows Interoperability among Clearing Corporations along with Exchange Scheme as per circular NSE/CMTR/62927 dated July 12, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate via eOFS platform
- Members must ensure compliant UCC of self (PRO) and clients are updated in UCI system one day prior to OFS bidding
- UCCs updated on bidding day will not be permitted to participate
- Members must ensure sufficient collaterals before carry forward orders on T+1
- Admin user IDs must be obtained through ENIT portal if not already received

## Important Dates

- **August 07, 2025**: First bidding session (09:15 hrs to 15:30 hrs)
- **August 08, 2025**: Second bidding session with carry forward option (09:15 hrs to 15:30 hrs)
- **22:00 hrs previous day**: UCC compliance verification deadline
- **Admin ID processing times**: 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 3:00 PM (requests after 3:00 PM processed next day by 8:45 AM)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on trading operations as it involves specific participation requirements for members and affects Indosolar Limited shareholders. The two-day OFS structure with carry forward mechanism may influence trading volumes and requires operational readiness from participating members. Platform access through eOFS at https://eofs.nseindia.com is mandatory for participation.
