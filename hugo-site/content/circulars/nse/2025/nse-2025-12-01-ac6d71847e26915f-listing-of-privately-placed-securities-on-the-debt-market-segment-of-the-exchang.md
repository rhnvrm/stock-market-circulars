## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has admitted privately placed commercial paper securities from 10 issuers to dealings on its Debt Market Segment effective December 01, 2025. The securities were allotted on November 28, 2025, with redemption dates ranging from December 24, 2025 to November 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 11 commercial paper instruments listed across 10 issuers
- All securities allotted on November 28, 2025
- Trading commences on December 01, 2025
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 per security across all instruments
- Total aggregate value approximately ₹43,500 crore (87,000 securities)
- Tenure ranges from 26 days to 364 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are notified of the new security codes for trading purposes
- Securities are available for trading on the debt market segment from December 01, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing/Trading Start Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from December 24, 2025 to November 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The listing of commercial paper on the debt segment provides:

- Additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors
- Liquidity for privately placed commercial paper
- Diversified funding sources for issuing corporations across sectors (banking, finance, manufacturing, infrastructure)

No impact on equity markets or trading operations. The notification is informational for NSE members to enable trading in these debt securities.
