## Summary

NSE has notified that Shriram Mutual Fund will accept transactions from Non-Resident Indian (NRI) clients based in the United States of America and Canada on the NSE MF Invest Platform in Non-Demat mode with immediate effect. Members and distributors must ensure NRI investors from these countries submit a physical declaration form to CAMS collection centers or AMC branches on the transaction date. Non-compliance may result in transaction rejection.

## Key Points

- Shriram Mutual Fund now accepts transactions from NRIs residing in USA and Canada
- Applicable to NSE MF Invest Platform transactions in Non-Demat mode
- Mandatory submission of "Shriram Declaration Form for US Investors"
- Physical copy of duly signed declaration form required
- Declaration form must be submitted to CAMS collection centers or AMC branch
- Submission must occur on the date of transaction
- Failure to comply will result in transaction rejection
- Effective immediately from November 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement by Shriram Mutual Fund to expand NRI investor access from previously restricted jurisdictions (USA and Canada).

## Compliance Requirements

**For Members/Distributors:**
- Download the "Shriram Declaration Form for US Investors" (PDF) attached to the circular
- Ensure NRI clients from USA and Canada complete and sign the declaration form
- Submit physical copy of signed declaration to CAMS collection centers or AMC branch on transaction date

**Declaration Requirements:**
NRI investors must declare:
- Status as NRI/PIO residing in United States of America
- Understanding that Shriram Mutual Fund schemes are not registered outside India
- Confirmation of no solicitation/marketing in their jurisdiction of residence
- Confirmation of compliance with all applicable laws
- Authorization for Shriram AMC to disclose/share information as needed
- Acknowledgment that investments may be rejected or redeemed at AMC's discretion

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Effective Date:** Immediate effect from November 10, 2025
- **Declaration Submission:** Required on the date of each transaction

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - affects only NRI investors from USA and Canada interested in Shriram Mutual Fund schemes

**Operational Impact:** 
- Expands investor base for Shriram Mutual Fund to include US and Canada based NRIs
- Additional compliance burden on members/distributors to collect and submit declaration forms
- Risk of transaction rejection if documentation requirements not met

**Beneficiaries:**
- NRI investors from USA and Canada gain access to Shriram Mutual Fund schemes
- Shriram Mutual Fund AMC expands potential investor pool
