## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has notified the settlement holidays for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for the calendar year 2026. The notification is issued under clause 13(2) of chapter VI of the Byelaws of NSE Clearing Limited and Regulation 3.10 of the NSE Clearing Limited (F&O Segment). A total of 19 weekday holidays and 4 weekend holidays have been declared.

## Key Points

- Circular Reference: NCL/CMPT/71923, Circular No. 164/2025
- Segment: Futures & Options
- Total weekday settlement holidays: 19
- Additional weekend holidays: 4 (falling on Saturday/Sunday)
- Applicable to all F&O segment members

- March 21, 2026 (Saturday) - Ramzan-Id (Id-Ul-Fitr)
- August 15, 2026 (Saturday) - Independence Day/Parsi New Year (Shahenshahi)
- November 08, 2026 (Sunday) - Diwali Amavasya (Laxmi Pujan)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard annual notification of settlement holidays as per existing byelaws and regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All F&O segment members should note the settlement holiday schedule for 2026
- Members should plan their settlement operations and risk management activities accordingly
- No settlement activities will be conducted on the notified holidays

## Important Dates

- Calendar Year: 2026
- First Holiday: January 26, 2026 (Republic Day)
- Last Holiday: December 25, 2026 (Christmas)
- Total Settlement Days Impacted: 23 days (19 weekdays + 4 weekends)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Operations:**
- F&O settlements will not be processed on the 19 notified weekday holidays
- Market participants should plan positions and margin requirements considering the holiday schedule
- Weekend holidays provide additional non-settlement days for specific festivals

**Planning Impact:**
- Critical for institutional investors and traders for year-long planning
- Important for managing derivatives positions around expiry dates that may coincide with holidays
- Settlement cycles and fund availability should be planned considering these holidays
