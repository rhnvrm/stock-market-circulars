## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund of TATA Mutual Fund SIF will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will also support systematic facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) effective from December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund of TATA Mutual Fund SIF reopening for transactions
- Six scheme options available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Facilities available: Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Applicable to all NSE members

## Scheme Details

The following six scheme options are available:

1. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Growth** - ISIN: INF277K30047
2. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular Growth** - ISIN: INF277K30013
3. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF277K30062
4. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF277K30039
5. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF277K30054
6. **Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Regular IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF277K30021

All schemes fall under the Equity category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed to note the availability of these schemes for client transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing transactions with all systematic facilities

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme. Investors interested in TATA Mutual Fund's Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund can now access the fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment/withdrawal plans. The availability of both Direct and Regular plan options with different IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) structures provides flexibility for different investor preferences.
