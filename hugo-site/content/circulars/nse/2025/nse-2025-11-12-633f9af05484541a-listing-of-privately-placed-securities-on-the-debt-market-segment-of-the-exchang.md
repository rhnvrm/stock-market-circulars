## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 privately placed commercial paper securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective November 12, 2025. The securities include commercial papers from Aseem Infrastructure Finance Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, HDFC Securities Limited, Tata Projects Limited, Aditya Birla Money Limited, IIFL Finance Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, Tata Capital Limited, Astec LifeSciences Limited, and Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited. All securities were allotted on November 11, 2025, with redemption dates ranging from February 2026 to September 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities listed on NSE debt market segment
- All securities allotted on November 11, 2025
- Listing effective from November 12, 2025
- Total quantity: 44,500 securities across all issuances
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security for all issuances
- Redemption periods: 3 months to 10 months
- Issuers span infrastructure finance, manufacturing, real estate, and financial services sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular notifying members of new debt securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 11, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Dates:**
  - February 10, 2026: AIFL, GIL, HSL, ABML, IIFL, GPL, TCL, CIAFCL
  - April 15, 2026: ALSL
  - September 11, 2026: TPL

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed commercial paper securities that adds liquidity to the debt market segment. The securities are privately placed and therefore have limited immediate market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact:** Provides additional investment opportunities in the debt market for eligible investors. The securities offer short to medium-term investment options with varying redemption dates.

**Operational Impact:** None for general market participants. The designated security codes and ISINs enable trading and settlement of these securities through standard NSE debt market operations.
