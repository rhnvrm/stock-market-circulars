## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Navi Nifty Midsmallcap 400 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 11, 2025. This includes subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Navi Nifty Midsmallcap 400 Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available facilities: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan options available: Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF959L01HR4) and Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF959L01HS2)
- Both plans categorized under Equity schemes
- Effective date: December 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of this fund for client transactions from December 11, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 11, 2025**: Navi Nifty Midsmallcap 400 Index Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a single mutual fund scheme becoming available for transactions. It provides investors with an additional equity index fund option on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening suggests the fund may have been temporarily closed for subscriptions and is now ready to accept new investments and facilitate systematic transactions.
