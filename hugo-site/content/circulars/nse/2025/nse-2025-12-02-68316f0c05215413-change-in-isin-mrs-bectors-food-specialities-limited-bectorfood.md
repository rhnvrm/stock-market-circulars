## Summary

NSE has announced a change in ISIN code for Mrs. Bectors Food Specialities Limited (BECTORFOOD) effective December 12, 2025. The new ISIN INE495P01020 will be applicable following a share sub-division from face value of Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/-.

## Key Points

- ISIN code will change for BECTORFOOD equity shares
- New ISIN: INE495P01020
- Reason: Share sub-division from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 2/- face value
- The new ISIN will be effective for all trades from the ex-date
- Symbol: BECTORFOOD remains unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action requiring ISIN modification to reflect the new share denomination.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure systems are updated to reflect the new ISIN code
- Trading systems should be configured to use the new ISIN from the ex-date
- Settlement and back-office systems must be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Ex-Date**: December 12, 2025 (new ISIN becomes effective)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The ISIN change affects all trading, clearing, and settlement activities for BECTORFOOD shares. Market participants must update their systems and databases.

**Operational Impact**: Member brokers and participants need to ensure their trading and back-office systems recognize the new ISIN from December 12, 2025. The share sub-division will increase the number of shares proportionally while reducing the face value, making shares more accessible to retail investors.
