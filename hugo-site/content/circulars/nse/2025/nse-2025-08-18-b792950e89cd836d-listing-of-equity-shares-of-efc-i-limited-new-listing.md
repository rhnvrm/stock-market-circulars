## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of EFC (I) Limited with trading symbol EFCIL, effective from August 20, 2025. The company will be listed in the Capital Market segment under normal market operations with compulsory demat trading.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: EFCIL
- ISIN: INE886D01026
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Total securities: 99,553,376 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading mode: Compulsory demat for all investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard new listing notification under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading shall be conducted only through designated security codes
- All trading must be in specified lot sizes
- Compulsory demat trading for all investors
- Rolling settlement basis in Normal Market segment

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 18, 2025
- Effective trading date: August 20, 2025
- Allotment/Listing date: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a standard new listing. Provides additional investment option for equity market participants. Company operates from Pune, Maharashtra with registrar services provided by MUFG Intime India Private Limited in Ahmedabad.
