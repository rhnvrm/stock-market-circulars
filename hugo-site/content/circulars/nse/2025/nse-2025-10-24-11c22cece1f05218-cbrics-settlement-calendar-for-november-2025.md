## Summary

NSE Clearing has released the settlement calendar for Corporate Bond Repository for Integrated Clearing and Settlement (CBRICS) for November 2025. The circular provides settlement dates and numbers for participants and custodians to transfer securities using 'On-market' settlement mode through NSDL/CDSL.

## Key Points

- 19 settlement dates scheduled for November 2025
- Market Type: Corporate Bond
- Clearing Member: NSE Clearing Ltd.
- CM-BP ID: IN568177
- Settlement mode to be used: On-market
- Transfers applicable for both NSDL and CDSL
- Previously used DP details (IN001002-10000617) discontinued since September 3, 2019

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants and Custodians must transfer securities for CBRICS settlement based on the provided calendar
- Settlement mode must be set as 'On-market'
- Old DP details (IN001002-10000617) must not be used for settlement on CBRICS platform

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: November 2025
- **First Settlement Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Last Settlement Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Circular Date**: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a routine monthly settlement calendar for corporate bond clearing operations.

**Market Impact**: Low - Informational circular that facilitates normal clearing and settlement operations for corporate bonds.

**Affected Parties**: Participants and custodians engaged in corporate bond trading on CBRICS platform.
