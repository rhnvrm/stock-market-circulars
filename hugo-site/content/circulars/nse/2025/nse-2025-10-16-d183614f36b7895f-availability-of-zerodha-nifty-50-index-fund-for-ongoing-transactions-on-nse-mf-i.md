## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Zerodha Nifty 50 Index Fund of Zerodha Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 17, 2025. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- Zerodha Nifty 50 Index Fund - Direct Growth will be available for transactions
- ISIN: INF0R8F01158
- Category: Equity
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Support contact: 1800 2100 940 (Toll-free) and support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members are informed of the scheme availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 - Zerodha Nifty 50 Index Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Circular Date**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a single mutual fund scheme. Investors who were previously unable to transact in this fund will now have access to subscriptions and redemptions along with systematic investment facilities. This is a routine operational announcement that enhances product availability on the NSE MF Invest Platform but does not materially affect broader market operations or trading activities.
