## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Dhanuka Realty Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective January 02, 2026. The company name will change to Dhanuka Infra Realty Limited and the trading symbol will change from DRL to DIRL.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Dhanuka Realty Limited" to "Dhanuka Infra Realty Limited"
- Trading symbol changing from "DRL" to "DIRL"
- Change effective from January 02, 2026
- Circular issued by Listing Department
- Circular reference number: NSE/CML/72032, 2530/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action involving administrative change of company name and trading symbol.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Trading in the security will continue under the new symbol DIRL from January 02, 2026
- Members should ensure proper identification and processing of orders under the new symbol

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The change is purely administrative, affecting only the company name and trading symbol. Investors and traders will need to use the new symbol DIRL for all transactions from the effective date. No impact on shareholding, trading rights, or company operations. Market participants should update their databases and trading systems accordingly before the effective date.
