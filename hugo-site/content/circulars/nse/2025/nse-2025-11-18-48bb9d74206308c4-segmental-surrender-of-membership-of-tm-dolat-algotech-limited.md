## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited has approved the request of Dolat Algotech Limited (SEBI Registration Number: INZ000285038) for surrender of their membership in the Currency Derivative segment, effective November 13, 2025. This is a routine administrative action communicated to all members.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: Dolat Algotech Limited
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000285038
- Surrendered Segment: Currency Derivative
- Surrender Approval Date: November 13, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/71338, Circular Ref. No: 95/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a voluntary membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this segmental membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Surrender approval date for Currency Derivative segment membership
- **November 18, 2025**: Circular issuance date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a voluntary surrender of membership in a single segment by one trading member. It does not affect other members' operations or broader market functioning. The action is purely administrative in nature.
