## Summary

NSE notifies the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies on the capital market segment, effective September 1, 2025. The circular specifies security codes, lot sizes, and trading parameters for the newly admitted securities.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Toyota Financial Services India Limited: 7.38% coupon rate, maturing August 28, 2028
- Patel Engineering Limited: 10.25% coupon rate, maturing August 25, 2028
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading identification by designated security codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must comply with specified lot sizes
- Securities to be traded according to NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 1, 2025
- **TFSIL Maturity**: August 28, 2028
- **PATE Maturity**: August 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of debt securities. Provides additional investment options for institutional investors in privately placed debt instruments. No direct impact on equity markets or retail trading.
