## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock bidding session on the electronic Offer for Sale (eOFS) platform in live environment on November 08, 2025. The session will run from 09:15 AM to 11:00 AM and will use three test securities (TESTOFS1, TESTOFS2, and TESTOFS3) with predefined parameters. Members are encouraged to participate actively, and bids placed during this session will not attract any obligations in terms of funds or securities.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for November 08, 2025, from 09:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Platform: Live environment at https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Three test securities available: TEST OFS 1 Limited (TESTOFS1), TEST OFS 2 Limited (TESTOFS2), and TEST OFS 3 Limited (TESTOFS3)
- TESTOFS1 and TESTOFS2 offer Retail (RS) and Employee (ES) categories
- TESTOFS3 offers only Institutional (IS) category
- All test securities priced at floor price of Rs. 100 with face value of Rs. 10
- Market lot is 1 equity share for all securities
- No real obligations for funds or securities from mock session bids
- Active participation from members is encouraged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine testing exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to participate actively in the mock bidding session
- Members should monitor www.nseindia.com for any new or revised announcements regarding the mock session
- For queries, members can contact Toll Free no: 1800 26600 50 (Option 1) or email msm@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025**: Mock bidding session from 09:15 AM to 11:00 AM
