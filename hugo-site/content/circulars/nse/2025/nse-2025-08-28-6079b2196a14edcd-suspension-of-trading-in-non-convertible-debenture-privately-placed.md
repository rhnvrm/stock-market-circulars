## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in four Non-Convertible Debentures (privately placed) effective August 29, 2025. The suspensions are due to the redemption of these instruments.

## Key Points

- Four companies' debentures will be suspended from trading
- All suspensions are effective from August 29, 2025
- Reason for all suspensions is redemption of the debentures
- Circular issued under NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Action taken in pursuance of existing Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants beyond noting the suspension of trading in the specified instruments.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** August 28, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date:** August 29, 2025 for all four debentures

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative action following the natural redemption cycle of debt instruments. Only affects holders and potential traders of these specific privately placed debentures. No broader market implications expected.
