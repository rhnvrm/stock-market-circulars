## Summary

NSE has announced that DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index Fund of DSP Mutual Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting September 02, 2025. The fund will offer subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment plans.

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from September 02, 2025
- Offers SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities
- Six different scheme variants available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options
- All schemes classified under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements outlined beyond standard mutual fund transaction procedures.

## Important Dates

- **September 02, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with minimal market impact. It provides investors with additional investment options through the NSE platform but does not affect existing market operations or trading activities. The availability of systematic investment facilities may attract retail investors seeking regular investment options in the Nifty500 index space.
