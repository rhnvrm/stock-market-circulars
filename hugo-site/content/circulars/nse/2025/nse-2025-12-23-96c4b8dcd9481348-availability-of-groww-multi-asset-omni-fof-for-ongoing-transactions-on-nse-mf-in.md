## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has informed members that the Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF (Fund of Funds) of Groww Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective December 24, 2025. The reopening includes facility for Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) transactions.

## Key Points

- Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF will be available for ongoing transactions from December 24, 2025
- Six scheme variants are covered: Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- Facilities include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment department (Circular Ref. No: 1077/2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding the reopening of previously unavailable mutual fund schemes for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

NSE members are informed to update their systems to enable transactions in the listed Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF schemes on the NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 24, 2025 - Schemes available for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions with SIP, STP, SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational update affecting only specific mutual fund schemes of Groww Mutual Fund.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for Groww Multi Asset Omni FOF investors - Investors will regain ability to make subscriptions, redemptions, and set up systematic plans for these schemes.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - NSE members need to ensure the schemes are available for transactions on their platforms from December 24, 2025.
