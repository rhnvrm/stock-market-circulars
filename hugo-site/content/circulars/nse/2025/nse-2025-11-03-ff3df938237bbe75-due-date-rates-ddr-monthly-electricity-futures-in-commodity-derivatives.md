## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced the Due Date Rate (DDR) for the Monthly Electricity Futures contract (ELECMBL) that expired on October 30, 2025. The DDR has been fixed at ₹2800.00 per MWh (base quantity: 1 MWh). This circular is issued in reference to previous circulars NCL/COM/69054 dated July 10, 2025, and NCL/COM/69390 dated July 30, 2025, regarding the Final Settlement (DDR) Procedure for Monthly Electricity Futures.

## Key Points

- DDR Price fixed at ₹2800.00 per MWh for ELECMBL contract
- Contract expiry date: October 30, 2025
- Base quantity: 1 MWh
- Circular reference number: 352/2025
- Download reference: NCL/COM/71093

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular publishing settlement rates for expired futures contracts.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are requested to take note of the published Due Date Rate for settlement purposes. The DDR will be used for final settlement of the expired ELECMBL futures contract.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Contract Expiry Date:** October 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The DDR publication is part of the standard settlement procedure for expired electricity futures contracts. Members who held positions in the ELECMBL contract expiring October 30, 2025, will use this rate for final settlement calculations. No broader market or trading implications.
