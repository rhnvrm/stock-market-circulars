## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted Wakefit Innovations Limited's equity shares to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 15, 2025. The company's IPO comprises 326,828,675 equity shares of Re. 1/- face value each, issued at Rs. 195 per share. The stock will trade under the symbol 'WAKEFIT' with ISIN INE0E7301029 and will participate in the pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: WAKEFIT
- **ISIN**: INE0E7301029
- **Face Value**: Re. 1/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 195/-
- **Total Shares**: 326,828,675 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- **Registered Office**: Umiya Emporium, 97-99, 2nd and 4th Floor, Adugodi, Bengaluru - 560029, Karnataka
- **Company Secretary**: Ms. Surbhi Sharma
- **Registrar**: MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security only by its designated code (WAKEFIT) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category as mandated by SEBI
- Lock-in periods apply to various tranches of shares as detailed in Annexure I

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates** (varying by shareholder category):
  - January 9, 2026
  - March 10, 2026
  - June 2, 2026
  - June 11, 2026
  - June 11, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing adds a new consumer-focused company (furniture and mattress sector) to NSE with significant public float. The IPO size of 326.83 million shares at Rs. 195 represents substantial market capitalization.

**Trading Impact**: The stock will be available for trading from December 15, 2025, with a market lot of 1 share, providing high liquidity and accessibility to retail investors. Various lock-in periods ranging from January 2026 to June 2027 will gradually release shares into the market.

**Investor Impact**: New investment opportunity in the home furnishing/sleep solutions sector. Contact details for investor complaints and registrar services are provided for shareholder servicing.
