## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has approved the voluntary surrender of Debt segment membership by Mansukh Securities & Finance Limited (SEBI Registration Number: INZ000164537) effective November 26, 2025. This is an administrative change affecting the member's trading permissions in the debt segment only.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: Mansukh Securities & Finance Limited
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000164537
- Surrendered Segment: Debt
- Surrender Approval Date: November 26, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/71490, Circular No. 99/2025
- Department: Compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a member-specific administrative action.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of this surrender
- Mansukh Securities & Finance Limited will no longer be authorized to trade in the Debt segment
- The member's SEBI registration for other segments (if any) remains unaffected

## Important Dates

- **Surrender Approval Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Circular Issue Date**: November 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a voluntary administrative action by a single trading member affecting only the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to Mansukh Securities & Finance Limited, which will cease debt segment trading operations. Other members and market participants are not operationally affected.

**Compliance Impact**: Informational notice only. No action required by other members beyond awareness of the change in membership status.
