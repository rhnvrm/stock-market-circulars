## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced a change in the trading symbol for Worth Peripherals Limited. The existing symbol WORTH will be changed to WORTHPERI with effect from October 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Existing trading symbol: WORTH
- New trading symbol: WORTHPERI
- Company name remains unchanged: Worth Peripherals Limited
- Change effective from October 10, 2025
- Circular reference number: NSE/CML/70638 (1930/2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading symbol for identification purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the Exchange are informed to note the new symbol for trading purposes
- All trading systems and references should be updated to reflect the new symbol WORTHPERI from October 10, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 06, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. Traders and market participants will need to use the new symbol WORTHPERI for all trading activities related to Worth Peripherals Limited from October 10, 2025. The change does not affect the company's listing status, trading rights, or any other operational aspects.
