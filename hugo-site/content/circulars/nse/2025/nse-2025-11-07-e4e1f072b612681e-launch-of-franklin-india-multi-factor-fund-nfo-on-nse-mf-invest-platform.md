## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from November 10, 2025 to November 24, 2025 till 11:59 pm. Six scheme variants are being offered covering regular and direct plans with growth, IDCW reinvestment, and IDCW payout options. The allotment date is November 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: November 10, 2025 to November 24, 2025 (till 11:59 pm)
- Allotment date: November 28, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- Scheme category: Equity
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by November 28, 2025 till 11:00 am

## Scheme Details

1. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund Regular - Growth (INF090I01YN8)
2. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund Regular - IDCW Reinvestment (INF090I01YO6)
3. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund Regular - IDCW Payout (INF090I01YP3)
4. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct Growth (INF090I01YQ1)
5. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct IDCW Reinvestment (INF090I01YR9)
6. Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct IDCW Payout (INF090I01YS7)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 28, 2025 till 11:00 am
- All transactions must be completed through NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified subscription period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date:** November 10, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date:** November 24, 2025 (till 11:59 pm)
- **Allotment Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Funds Settlement Deadline:** November 28, 2025 (till 11:00 am)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with minimal market impact. It provides investors with a new equity mutual fund option focusing on multi-factor investment strategy through Franklin India Mutual Fund. The circular is informational for NSE members operating on the MF Invest Platform and does not affect stock market trading or operations.
