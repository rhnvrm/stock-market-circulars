## Summary

Pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement, DCM Shriram Industries Limited (DCMSRIND) will be moved to the Trade for Trade segment (Series: BE) effective December 19, 2025. The stock will also be part of the call auction in the pre-open session as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- DCMSRIND will trade in Trade for Trade segment (Series: BE)
- Change effective from December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- Stock will be part of call auction in pre-open session
- Change implemented pursuant to Scheme of Arrangement
- Governed by SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The stock is being moved to Trade for Trade segment, which means:
- All trades must result in delivery (no intraday trading)
- Settlement on a trade-for-trade basis
- Participation in call auction during pre-open session
- This is in compliance with SEBI's framework for trade-for-trade segment

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must be aware that DCMSRIND can only be traded on a delivery basis
- No intraday square-off is permitted
- Trades will be settled through compulsory delivery
- Investors must have sufficient funds/securities before placing orders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The move to Trade for Trade segment significantly impacts trading in DCMSRIND:
- **Liquidity Impact**: Expected reduction in trading volumes due to compulsory delivery requirement
- **Trading Restrictions**: Eliminates intraday trading opportunities
- **Investor Impact**: Only delivery-based investors can participate; speculators and day traders will exit
- **Price Discovery**: Call auction mechanism may lead to different price discovery dynamics
- **Risk Management**: Reduces speculative activity and volatility

This change is typically implemented for stocks undergoing corporate actions like mergers, demergers, or scheme of arrangements to ensure orderly trading and proper settlement.
