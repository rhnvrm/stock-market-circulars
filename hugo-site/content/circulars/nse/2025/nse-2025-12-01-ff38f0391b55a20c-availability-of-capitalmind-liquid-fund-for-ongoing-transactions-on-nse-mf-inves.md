## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Capitalmind Liquid Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective from December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Capitalmind Liquid Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Both Direct Growth and Regular Growth plans available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Two ISINs covered: INF226401034 (Direct Growth) and INF226401042 (Regular Growth)
- Both schemes categorized as Liquid Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE MF Invest Platform should note the availability of these schemes for client transactions starting December 02, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 - Capitalmind Liquid Fund available for ongoing transactions
- **Circular Date**: December 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It is a routine operational notification informing members about the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform. Investors interested in liquid funds will have access to Capitalmind Liquid Fund options for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities. The reopening suggests the fund had been temporarily unavailable and is now ready for regular operations.
