## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Meesho Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol MEESHO and ISIN INE0VDM01015. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Meesho Limited
- Trading Symbol: MEESHO
- ISIN: INE0VDM01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should note the trading symbol and ISIN for Meesho Limited
- Members should await separate circular for actual listing date announcement
- Members should prepare their systems for trading the new security

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 09, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact on the market as it announces a new listing opportunity for investors and traders. Meesho Limited, being a significant e-commerce platform, may attract considerable investor interest. Exchange members need to update their systems and prepare for trading activity once the listing date is announced. The actual market impact will depend on the IPO pricing, size, and market conditions at the time of listing.
