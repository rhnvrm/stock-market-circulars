## Summary

NSE has decided not to proceed with the suspension of trading in securities of Dharan Infra-EPC Limited (Symbol: DHARAN) effective December 15, 2025. This cancellation follows the company's previous non-compliance with Regulation 33 of SEBI Listing Regulations regarding submission of financial results for two consecutive quarters (March 31, 2025 and June 30, 2025).

## Key Points

- Trading suspension in Dharan Infra-EPC Limited securities cancelled
- Original suspension was due to non-compliance with Regulation 33 (Financial Results submission)
- Non-compliance related to two consecutive quarters: Q4 FY2025 (March 31, 2025) and Q1 FY2026 (June 30, 2025)
- Cancellation effective from December 15, 2025
- References previous circular 2196/2025 dated November 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular reverses a previously announced suspension action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company appears to have resolved the non-compliance issue with financial results submission
- Members should note the restoration of normal trading status for the security

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date for cancellation of suspension
- **November 12, 2025**: Date of original suspension circular (Ref. 2196/2025)

## Impact Assessment

Positive development for Dharan Infra-EPC Limited as trading suspension is cancelled, restoring market access for the security. This indicates the company has likely submitted the required financial results or resolved the compliance issues. Trading members can resume normal trading activities in this security from December 15, 2025.
