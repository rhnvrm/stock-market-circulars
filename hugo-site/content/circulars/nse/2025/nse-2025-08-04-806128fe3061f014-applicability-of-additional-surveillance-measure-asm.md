## Summary

NSE has updated the Long-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Framework with Nelcast Limited being added to Stage-I effective August 05, 2025. No securities are being moved between stages or excluded from the framework.

## Key Points

- Nelcast Limited (INE189I01024) added to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I
- No securities moving between different ASM stages
- No securities being excluded from ASM Framework
- All other stage transition lists show 'Nil' entries

## Regulatory Changes

- Addition of one new security to Long-Term ASM Framework Stage-I
- Consolidated ASM list continues to include multiple securities across different stages
- Stage-IV securities subject to Trade for Trade (Series: BE) on T+3 basis

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must apply appropriate surveillance measures for Nelcast Limited
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for Stage-I securities
- Compliance with ASM framework criteria and restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 05, 2025
- All changes applicable from market opening on the effective date

## Impact Assessment

- Limited impact as only one security added to surveillance framework
- Enhanced monitoring may affect trading volumes and liquidity for Nelcast Limited
- Existing ASM securities continue under current stage requirements
- No immediate changes to trading mechanisms for most securities
