## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement calendar for Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for January 2026. The circular provides three settlement schedules: First Leg Settlement (Type 'L'), Reverse Leg Settlement (Type 'P'), and Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q'). This is issued in pursuance of circular NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers all trading days in January 2026
- First Leg Settlement: 21 settlements scheduled from January 1-30, 2026
- Reverse Leg Settlement: 12 monthly series (01/X1 through 12/XD) with settlement dates ranging from January 6, 2026 to January 5, 2027
- Reverse Leg Auction: Same 12 series with settlement dates one day after transaction dates
- Settlement Type 'L' follows T+1 settlement cycle
- New 01/X1 series expiring on January 5, 2027 will be available from January 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational calendar publication.

## Compliance Requirements

All SLBS participants and custodians must follow the published settlement schedules for their lending and borrowing transactions during January 2026.

## Important Dates

**First Leg Settlements (Type 'L'):**
- First settlement: January 2, 2026 (for trades on January 1, 2026)
- Last settlement: February 2, 2026 (for trades on January 30, 2026)
- Total settlements: 21

**Reverse Leg Settlements (Type 'P'):**
- Series 01/X1: January 6, 2026
- Series 02/X2: February 3, 2026
- Series 03/X3: March 3, 2026
- Series 04/X4: April 7, 2026
- Series 05/X5: May 5, 2026
- Series 06/X6: June 2, 2026
- Series 07/X7: July 7, 2026
- Series 08/X8: August 4, 2026
- Series 09/X9: September 1, 2026
- Series 10/XO: October 6, 2026
- Series 11/XN: November 3, 2026
- Series 12/XD: December 1, 2026
- New Series 01/X1: January 5, 2027 (available from January 7, 2026)

**Reverse Leg Auction (Type 'Q'):**
- Settlement dates are one day after transaction dates for all series

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Routine publication with no operational disruptions. Participants should note the settlement schedules for planning their SLBS activities.

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a standard monthly calendar publication that enables participants to plan their securities lending and borrowing activities for January 2026.

**Participant Action Required:** Review and incorporate settlement dates into operational systems and processes.
