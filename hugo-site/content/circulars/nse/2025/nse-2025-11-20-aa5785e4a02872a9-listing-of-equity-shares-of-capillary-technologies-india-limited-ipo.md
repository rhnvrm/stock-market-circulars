## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited on the Capital Market segment effective November 21, 2025. The company has issued 79,311,773 equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each at an issue price of Rs. 577/-. The stock will trade under the symbol "CAPILLARY" with ISIN INE0ILV01024 and market lot size of 1 share. The security will be part of pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: CAPILLARY
- **ISIN**: INE0ILV01024
- **Face Value**: Rs. 2/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 577/- per share
- **Total Shares Listed**: 79,311,773 equity shares
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 share
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable for IPO category

- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026
- **Registrar**: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (Formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)

## Lock-in Details

Various tranches of shares have different lock-in periods:
- Multiple tranches locked until December 18, 2025
- Some tranches locked until February 16, 2026
- Certain shares locked until April 27, 2026
- Significant portion locked until May 19, 2026 and May 19, 2027
- Several tranches marked as "Free" (no lock-in)

## Regulatory Framework

- Listed pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot size specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Various Lock-in Expiry Dates**: Range from December 18, 2025 to May 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a technology sector company to NSE's Capital Market segment. With an issue price of Rs. 577 and over 79 million shares, this represents a significant IPO. The staggered lock-in periods suggest a mix of promoter, anchor, and public investor participation. Market participants should note the pre-open session applicability and the specific lock-in schedules for different share tranches when assessing liquidity and trading patterns post-listing.
