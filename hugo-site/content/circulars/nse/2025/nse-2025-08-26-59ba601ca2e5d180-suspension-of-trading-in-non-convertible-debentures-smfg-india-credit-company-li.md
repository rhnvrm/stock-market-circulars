## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of SMFG India Credit Company Limited (Symbol: 830SMF25, ISIN: INE535H07CG2) effective August 29, 2025, due to maturity of the securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to SMFG India Credit Company Limited's Non-Convertible Debentures
- Security symbol: 830SMF25
- ISIN: INE535H07CG2
- Reason for suspension: Maturity
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension procedure under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. This is an administrative suspension due to security maturity.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025 - Trading suspension comes into effect
- **Circular Date**: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to debenture maturity. Investors holding these securities should be aware that trading will cease from the effective date. The suspension is procedural and does not indicate any operational or financial issues with the company.
