## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Frog Cellsat Limited (Symbol: FROG) will undergo a name change to Frog Innovations Limited, effective from November 12, 2025. This is an administrative corporate action with no impact on the trading symbol or operations.

## Key Points

- Company: Frog Cellsat Limited
- Trading Symbol: FROG (remains unchanged)
- New Name: Frog Innovations Limited
- Effective Date: November 12, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71142, Circular No. 2148/2025
- Department: Listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change that requires notification to exchange members and market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed of the name change
- No action required from members or investors
- Trading will continue under the same symbol (FROG)

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: November 06, 2025
- Effective Date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The trading symbol remains FROG, and there are no changes to business operations, shareholding patterns, or trading arrangements. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for record-keeping purposes, but no trading disruption is expected.
