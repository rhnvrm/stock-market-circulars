## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the trading symbol UTLSOLAR with ISIN INE12UR01024. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Fujiyama Power Systems Limited
- Trading Symbol: UTLSOLAR
- ISIN: INE12UR01024
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular Reference Number: 2245/2025
- NSE Reference Number: NSE/CML/71357

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from members at this time. Members are informed to note the forthcoming listing and await the separate circular announcing the actual listing date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate impact on market operations. Members should be aware of the new security for trading purposes once the listing date is announced. The listing will add one more stock to the NSE equity segment under the power systems sector.
