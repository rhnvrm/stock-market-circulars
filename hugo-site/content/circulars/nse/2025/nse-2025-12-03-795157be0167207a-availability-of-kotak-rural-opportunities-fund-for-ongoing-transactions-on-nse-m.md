## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has notified members that Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund of Kotak Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from December 03, 2025.

## Key Points

- Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund reopened for transactions effective December 03, 2025
- Available facilities include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six plan variants are available across Regular and Direct plans
- All variants are equity category funds
- Applicable on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund are now available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF174KA1XZ1) - Equity
2. **Direct Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF174KA1XW8) - Equity
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF174KA1YB0) - Equity
4. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF174KA1XY4) - Equity
5. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF174KA1YA2) - Equity
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF174KA1XX6) - Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of a previously suspended mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members should update their systems to enable transactions in the specified ISINs
- Members should inform their clients about the availability of these schemes for investment

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 03, 2025 - Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund reopened for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact and is primarily relevant to:
- Existing and potential investors in Kotak Rural Opportunities Fund
- NSE MF Invest Platform members facilitating mutual fund transactions
- Kotak Mutual Fund distributors and advisors

The reopening provides investment access to a rural-focused equity fund, allowing investors to resume or initiate investments through various systematic and lump-sum modes. The availability of both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options provides flexibility to different investor profiles.
