## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Groww Money Market Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective November 20, 2025. The fund will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. Six scheme variants are being made available, including Direct and Regular Growth options, as well as IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) Payout and Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- Groww Money Market Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective November 20, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six scheme variants available: Direct Growth, Regular Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, Regular IDCW Payout, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, and Regular IDCW Reinvestment
- All schemes categorized under Debt category
- ISINs assigned: INF666M01MW3, INF666M01MZ6, INF666M01MX1, INF666M01NA7, INF666M01MY9, INF666M01NB5

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members of the fund's availability for transactions.

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational reopening of a single mutual fund scheme on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The impact is limited to investors interested in the Groww Money Market Fund. The reopening provides liquidity options for existing and prospective investors through multiple transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) across six scheme variants. This is a routine operational matter with no broader implications for equity markets or trading activities.
