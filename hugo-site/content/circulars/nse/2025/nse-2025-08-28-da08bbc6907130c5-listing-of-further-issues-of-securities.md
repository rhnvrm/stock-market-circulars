## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) by 15 companies, effective from August 29, 2025. The circular provides detailed information about security codes, ISIN numbers, allotment dates, and distinctive number ranges for each company's newly issued shares.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have allotted equity shares under ESOP schemes
- All shares will be listed on NSE Capital Market segment from August 29, 2025
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable to these ESOP allotments
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities

## Company-wise Details

### Major Allotments
- **Aptus Value Housing Finance**: 222,439 shares of Rs. 2 each (allotted Aug 22, 2025)
- **Fedbank Financial Services**: 168,500 shares of Rs. 10 each (allotted Aug 22, 2025)
- **Hindustan Construction Company**: 92,937 shares of Re. 1 each (allotted Aug 7, 2025)
- **Indegene Limited**: 69,596 shares of Rs. 2 each (allotted Jul 31, 2025)
- **ICICI Prudential Life Insurance**: 33,756 shares of Rs. 10 each (allotted Aug 26, 2025)

### Other Companies
The circular also covers ESOP allotments for IndoStar Capital Finance, Marico, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Nupur Recyclers, Poonawalla Fincorp, Sakar Healthcare, SBI Life Insurance, UTI Asset Management, Vishal Mega Mart, and Wipro.

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of the same regulations
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Various dates from July 31 to August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The ESOP share listings represent small incremental additions to existing share capital and do not affect trading patterns or market capitalization significantly. Investors should note the increased share count for dilution calculations.
