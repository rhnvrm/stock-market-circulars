## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 17, 2025. The fund will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from October 17, 2025
- Supports subscriptions and redemptions
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Fund details: JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund - Direct Growth
- ISIN: INF22M001093
- Category: Equity
- Circular Reference: NSE/NMFTM/70859, Circular No. 927/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for NSE members regarding mutual fund platform operations.

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 17, 2025**: JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund becomes available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This circular affects only investors interested in JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Routine operational update enabling transaction facilities for a specific mutual fund scheme. Members and investors can resume or initiate investments in this fund through the platform.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of JioBlackRock Flexi Cap Fund who use NSE MF Invest Platform, as they can now access full transaction capabilities including systematic investment and withdrawal plans.
