## Summary

NSE has announced that the SBI Nifty100 Low Volatility 30 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 31, 2025. The fund will offer subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- SBI Nifty100 Low Volatility 30 Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available facilities: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options)
- All schemes categorized under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a fund availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for members.

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine mutual fund availability announcement. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking low volatility equity exposure through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund offers diversified investment plans catering to different investor preferences.
