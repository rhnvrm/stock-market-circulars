## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of units issued by Groww Asset Management Limited for Groww Nifty Metal ETF on the Capital Market segment. The units will be available for trading from December 23, 2025, under the symbol GROWWMETAL. A total of 7,863,510 units with face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10/- each have been allotted on December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- **ETF Name**: Groww Nifty Metal ETF
- **Symbol**: GROWWMETAL
- **ISIN**: INF666M01NI0
- **Total Units Issued**: 7,863,510 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Paid Up Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 unit
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Allotment Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: December 23, 2025

## Market Makers

Three market makers have been designated for this ETF:

1. **Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd** (TM Code: 90246)
2. **East India Securities** (TM Code: 8134)
3. **Irage Broking Services LLP** (TM Code: 90068)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new ETF listing that expands investment options for market participants interested in the metal sector. The ETF provides investors with a passive investment vehicle tracking the Nifty Metal index. The designation of three market makers ensures adequate liquidity for trading. The market lot size of 1 unit makes it accessible to retail investors. This listing has minimal systemic impact and is relevant primarily to investors seeking exposure to the metals sector through exchange-traded funds.
