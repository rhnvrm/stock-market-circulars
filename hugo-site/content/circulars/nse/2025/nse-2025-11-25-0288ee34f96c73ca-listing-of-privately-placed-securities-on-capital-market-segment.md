## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed securities on the capital market segment effective November 26, 2025. The circular covers the listing of ICICI Securities Limited's 7.45% debentures maturing in 2028 and provides corrections to previously notified Government Treasury Bill symbols and details.

## Key Points

- ICICI Securities Limited 7.45% Senior 1 debentures (Symbol: 745ISL28) listed with market lot of 1
- Maturity date: November 24, 2028
- ISIN: INE763G08967
- Correction issued for three Government T-Bill symbols previously notified on November 19, 2025
- Trading to be conducted using designated security codes only
- Lot sizes as specified in the annexure must be followed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing regulations under:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes only
- Securities identified by codes: 745ISL28 (ICICI Securities), 91D190226, 182D210526, 364D191126 (T-Bills)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity**: November 24, 2028
- **T-Bill Maturities**: 
  - 91D T-Bill: February 19, 2026
  - 182D T-Bill: May 21, 2026
  - 364D T-Bill: November 19, 2026
- **Reference Circular**: NSE/CML/71370 dated November 19, 2025 (corrected)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing notification affecting debt securities markets. The listing of ICICI Securities debentures provides additional investment options for debt market participants but does not significantly alter market dynamics.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Members need to update their systems with new security codes and correct T-Bill symbols. The corrections to previously notified T-Bill details ensure accurate trading and settlement operations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt investors interested in ICICI Securities fixed-income instruments or Government T-Bills. The 7.45% coupon rate on a 3-year tenor provides clarity for investment decisions.
