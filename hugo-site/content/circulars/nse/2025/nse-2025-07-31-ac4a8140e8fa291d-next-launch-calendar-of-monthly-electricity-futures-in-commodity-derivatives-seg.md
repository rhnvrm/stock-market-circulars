## Summary

NSE announces the launch calendar for Monthly Electricity Futures contract for November 2025 in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. The contract will be available for trading from August 1, 2025, with the November 28, 2025 contract becoming available.

## Key Points

- Monthly Electricity Futures contract for November 2025 to be launched
- Contract launch date: August 1, 2025
- Contract available for trading: November 28, 2025
- Part of ongoing Monthly Electricity Futures program introduced via circular NSE/COM/68867 dated July 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an implementation of previously announced Monthly Electricity Futures program.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the availability of the new contract for trading purposes and update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Contract launch month
- **November 28, 2025**: Contract available for trading

## Impact Assessment

Low impact routine announcement. Provides trading opportunities in electricity futures for November 2025 period. Supports the commodity derivatives market expansion into electricity trading.
