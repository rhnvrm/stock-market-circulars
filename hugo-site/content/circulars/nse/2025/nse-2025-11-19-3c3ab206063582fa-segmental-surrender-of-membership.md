## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has approved the surrender of membership by FLUX CAPITAL PRIVATE LIMITED (SEBI Registration Number: INZ000297535) in the Currency Derivative and IRF (Interest Rate Futures) segments, effective November 17, 2025. This is an informational circular notifying all members of the membership change.

## Key Points

- Trading member: FLUX CAPITAL PRIVATE LIMITED
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000297535
- Surrendered Segments: Currency Derivative & IRF
- Surrender Approval Date: November 17, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Compliance Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a voluntary membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required. Members are requested to take note of the membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Surrender approval date for FLUX CAPITAL PRIVATE LIMITED's Currency Derivative & IRF segment membership
- **November 19, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving one trading member's voluntary exit from specific trading segments. No action required by other market participants. The surrender only affects FLUX CAPITAL PRIVATE LIMITED's ability to trade in Currency Derivative and IRF segments.
