## Summary

NSE has notified the admission of Rights Entitlements (REs) of Mahindra Logistics Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment. The REs will be traded under symbol MAHLOG-RE from July 31, 2025 to August 11, 2025, with suspension on August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Symbol: MAHLOG-RE (Mahindra Logistics Limited Rights Entitlements)
- ISIN: INE766P20016
- Total Rights Entitlements: 27,008,004
- Market lot: 1
- Trading in Trade to Trade segment with compulsory demat
- Series: BE

## Regulatory Changes

Admission of new securities (Rights Entitlements) for trading under Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1 and lot size specification under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform clients about commencement of RE trading
- Must specify ISIN of RE and clarify these are not ordinary shares
- Contract notes must include disclaimer about RE purchase rights
- Must inform clients that REs give right to participate in Rights Issue or renounce before closure
- Warning that unsubscribed/unrenounced REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: July 31, 2025
- **Renunciation Period**: July 31, 2025 to August 11, 2025
- **Suspension Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: July 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to Mahindra Logistics shareholders and investors interested in the rights issue. Creates temporary trading opportunity for rights entitlements with specific compliance obligations for trading members.
