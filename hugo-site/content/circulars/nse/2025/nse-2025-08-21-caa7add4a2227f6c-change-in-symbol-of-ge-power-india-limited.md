## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the trading symbol of GE Power India Limited will be changed from GEPIL to GVPIL, effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: GE Power India Limited
- Current Symbol: GEPIL
- New Symbol: GVPIL
- Effective Date: August 28, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes, this is an administrative symbol modification for trading purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their trading systems to reflect the new symbol
- All trading activities for GE Power India Limited must use the new symbol GVPIL from August 28, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for symbol change from GEPIL to GVPIL

## Impact Assessment

This symbol change will require updates to trading systems, market data feeds, and investor portfolios. While operationally significant for market participants, it does not affect the underlying business or financial position of GE Power India Limited.
