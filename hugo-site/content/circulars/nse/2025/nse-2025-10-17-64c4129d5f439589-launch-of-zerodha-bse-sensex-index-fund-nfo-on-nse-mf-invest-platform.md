## Summary

Zerodha Mutual Fund is launching its BSE SENSEX Index Fund through a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The subscription period runs from October 20, 2025 to November 03, 2025, with allotment scheduled for November 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Zerodha BSE SENSEX Index Fund - Direct Growth
- ISIN: INF0R8F01174
- Category: Equity
- NFO period: October 20, 2025 to November 03, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 06, 2025
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 06, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure that clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 06, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: NFO opens for subscription
- **November 03, 2025**: NFO closes
- **November 06, 2025**: Allotment date and deadline for clear funds receipt

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. It provides investors with an additional passive investment option tracking the BSE SENSEX index through Zerodha Mutual Fund. The circular is informational in nature and affects only NSE MF Invest Platform members facilitating transactions for this scheme.
