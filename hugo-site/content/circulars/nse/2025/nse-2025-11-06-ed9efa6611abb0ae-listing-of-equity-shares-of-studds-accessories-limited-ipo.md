## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Studds Accessories Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol STUDDS with ISIN INE00Q601028. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Studds Accessories Limited
- Trading Symbol: STUDDS
- ISIN: INE00Q601028
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71111 (2147/2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular about the forthcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 06, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate impact. This is a routine notification of a new IPO listing. Members and market participants should note the new security details for trading purposes once the listing date is announced. The listing will add one more equity share to the NSE platform.
