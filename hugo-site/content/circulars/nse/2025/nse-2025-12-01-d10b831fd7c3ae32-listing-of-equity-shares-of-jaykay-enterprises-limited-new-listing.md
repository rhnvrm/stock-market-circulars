## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted equity shares of Jaykay Enterprises Limited to trading on the Capital Market segment effective December 03, 2025. The company will trade under symbol JAYKAY with ISIN INE903A01025. Total 130,287,066 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each fully paid up will be listed in compulsory demat mode with market lot of 1 share.

## Key Points

- Symbol: JAYKAY
- ISIN: INE903A01025
- Face Value: Re. 1/-
- Total Securities: 130,287,066 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Distinctive Number Range: 1 to 130,287,066
- Trading Mode: Compulsory demat for all investors
- Trading Segment: Normal Market segment (Rolling Settlement)
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Registrar: Alankit Assignments Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard new listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in JAYKAY shares must be in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in market lot of 1 share as specified
- Security to be identified by designated code JAYKAY on trading system

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 01, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 03, 2025
- Lock-in periods vary by shareholder category (see detailed annexure):
  - Some shares are free from lock-in
  - Lock-in dates range from February 25, 2026 to March 15, 2027 for different tranches

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine new listing that adds one company to NSE's Capital Market segment. The company operates from Kanpur and Delhi with registered office at Kamla Tower, Kanpur. Financial year runs from April 1 to March 31. Company Secretary is Ms. Shikha Rastogi. The listing provides additional investment opportunity for market participants but does not affect existing market operations or other listed securities.
