## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has published the settlement calendar for the Debt Segment for January 2026. The circular covers two settlement types: Debt Settlement (Settlement Type 'I') with T+1 rolling settlement and Debt Auction Settlement (Settlement Type '2') with T+1 rolling settlement. The schedule is issued in pursuance of Regulation 6.2 of NSE Clearing Limited (Capital Market Regulations, 1997).

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers the entire month of January 2026
- Two settlement types are covered: Type 'I' (Debt Settlement) and Type '2' (Debt Auction Settlement)
- Both follow T+1 rolling settlement mechanism
- Total 21 settlement numbers for January 2026 (2026001 to 2026021)
- Trading starts from January 1, 2026 and continues through January 30, 2026
- Settlement dates account for weekends (no settlements on January 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-26, 31)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational calendar notification as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, Custodians, and PCMs must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Settlement Type 'I' (Debt Settlement) follows the schedule in Annexure 'A'
- Settlement Type '2' (Debt Auction Settlement) follows the schedule in Annexure 'B'
- For clarifications, members should contact the help desk at 1800 266 0050 (IVR Option 2) or email securities_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

**Debt Settlement (Type 'I') - Key Dates:**
- First Trade Date: January 1, 2026 (Settlement: January 2, 2026)
- Last Trade Date: January 30, 2026 (Settlement: February 2, 2026)
- Republic Day holiday observed: January 26, 2026 (no trading)

**Debt Auction Settlement (Type '2') - Key Dates:**
- First Trade Date: January 2, 2026 (Settlement: January 5, 2026)
- Last Trade Date: February 2, 2026 (Settlement: February 3, 2026)
- Auction settlements occur 2 business days after trade date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine administrative circular providing operational clarity for debt segment participants.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard settlement calendar following established T+1 mechanism. Members can plan their operations and liquidity requirements for January 2026.

**Stakeholder Impact:** Debt segment members, custodians, and PCMs need to note the settlement dates for operational planning. The calendar helps in managing cash flows and settlement obligations for the month.
