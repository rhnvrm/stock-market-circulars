## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the settlement calendar for the takeover offer under tender offer scheme of Kolte-Patil Developers Limited. The key change is an extension of the trade end date to September 09, 2025, with corresponding adjustments to obligation download and settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: X, Settlement No: 2025073
- Trade period: August 22, 2025 to September 09, 2025
- Obligation download date: September 19, 2025
- Final settlement date: September 22, 2025
- Revision follows previous circulars NCL/CMPT/69724 and NSE/CMTR/70046

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes - this is a schedule revision for an existing tender offer process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members, custodians, and PCMs must note the revised settlement calendar
- All other details from circular NCL/CMPT/69724 dated August 19, 2025 remain applicable
- Contact help desk for clarifications at ncl_tenderoffer@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Trade End Date**: September 09, 2025 (revised)
- **Obligation Download**: September 19, 2025
- **Settlement Date**: September 22, 2025
- Note: Dates may be further revised if there are delays in acceptance details from RTA or funds pay-in

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact specific to Kolte-Patil Developers takeover participants. The extension provides additional time for tender submissions, potentially affecting the takeover timeline and participant strategies. Market operations remain normal with standard settlement procedures applying.
