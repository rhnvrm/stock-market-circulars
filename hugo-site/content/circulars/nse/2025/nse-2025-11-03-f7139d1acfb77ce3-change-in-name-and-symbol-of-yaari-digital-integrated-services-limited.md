## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Yaari Digital Integrated Services Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective November 10, 2025. The company will be renamed to Indiabulls Limited and the trading symbol will change from YAARI to IBULLSLTD.

## Key Points

- Current company name: Yaari Digital Integrated Services Limited
- New company name: Indiabulls Limited
- Current symbol: YAARI
- New symbol: IBULLSLTD
- Change effective date: November 10, 2025
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/71055, Circular No. 2123/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving company name and trading symbol modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the exchange must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- All trading, clearing, and settlement activities should use the new symbol IBULLSLTD from the effective date
- Market participants should inform their clients about the name and symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The name change from Yaari Digital to Indiabulls represents a significant corporate rebranding. Traders and investors must update their watchlists and trading systems to reflect the new symbol IBULLSLTD.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Exchange members need to ensure their trading systems, client communications, and records are updated to reflect the new name and symbol. Any automated trading strategies or alerts based on the old symbol will need reconfiguration.

**Investor Impact:** Existing shareholders should note that their holdings will automatically be updated to reflect the new company name, but they should verify this with their brokers after the effective date.
