## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing of further issues of Womancart Limited on the SME EMERGE platform. A total of 384,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued under preferential allotment at an issue price of Rs. 128 per share, will be admitted to trading effective December 04, 2025. The shares were allotted on September 09, 2025 and carry different lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- 384,400 equity shares of Womancart Limited to be listed on NSE SME EMERGE
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share, Issue price: Rs. 128 per share
- Security symbol: WOMANCART, ISIN: INE0Q9601016, Series: SM
- Date of allotment: September 09, 2025
- Distinctive number range: 6307201 to 6691600
- Market lot size: 400 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Two tranches with different lock-in periods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012)
- Lock-in compliance required as per Annexure A:
  - 114,800 shares (DN 6307201 to 6422000): Lock-in expiry June 30, 2027
  - 269,600 shares (DN 6422001 to 6691600): Lock-in expiry June 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 09, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates:**
  - June 30, 2026 (for 269,600 shares)
  - June 30, 2027 (for 114,800 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment adds 384,400 shares to the existing equity base of Womancart Limited on the SME platform. The staggered lock-in periods (June 2026 and June 2027) will restrict immediate liquidity of these shares. Impact is limited to Womancart Limited shareholders and potential investors interested in SME stocks. No broader market implications are anticipated.
