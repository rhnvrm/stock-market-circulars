## Summary

NSE has issued a circular listing securities that will be moved to Stage I of Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM). This measure is implemented to monitor securities exhibiting unusual price movements or trading patterns.

## Key Points

- Securities listed will be subject to Stage I GSM monitoring
- Enhanced surveillance measures will be applied to monitor trading activities
- Regular review process will determine continuation or progression to higher stages
- Trading remains permitted but under increased oversight

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Stage I GSM monitoring for specified securities with enhanced surveillance protocols.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must be aware of GSM status when trading these securities
- Additional disclosure requirements may apply for transactions in GSM securities
- Brokers should inform clients about GSM status of securities

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 25, 2025
- Implementation: As specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities with potential for increased volatility monitoring and regulatory scrutiny. Trading continues but under enhanced surveillance framework.
