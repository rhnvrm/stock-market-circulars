## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further equity shares of Lloyds Luxuries Limited on the SME EMERGE platform. The company has allotted 14,184 equity shares under its Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP), which will be admitted to trading with effect from November 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- 14,184 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP
- Date of allotment: October 15, 2025
- Trading symbol: LLOYDS
- Series: SM (SME)
- ISIN: INE0DTQ01028
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share
- Distinctive number range: 23863664 to 23877847
- Market lot: 1000 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine further issue listing under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to note the updated security details for Lloyds Luxuries Limited
- Securities to be identified by designated codes on the trading system
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot size of 1000 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012) and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 (September 11, 2012) regarding activation of ISIN for additional share issues

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment:** October 15, 2025
- **Effective date for trading:** November 06, 2025
- **Circular date:** November 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of ESOP shares for an SME company with a relatively small number of shares (14,184) being added to the tradable float. The shares are pari passu with existing equity and have no lock-in restrictions. The listing follows standard regulatory procedures for further issues in the SME segment.
