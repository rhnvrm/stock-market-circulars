## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Orkla India Limited through an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed with the symbol ORKLAINDIA and ISIN INE16NZ01023. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Orkla India Limited
- Trading Symbol: ORKLAINDIA
- ISIN: INE16NZ01023
- Listing Date: To be announced separately
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71112, Circular No. 2133/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular notifying exchange members about the forthcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 04, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO listing announcement with minimal immediate impact on market operations. Members are informed about the new security that will be available for trading once the listing date is announced. No action is required from members at this stage.
