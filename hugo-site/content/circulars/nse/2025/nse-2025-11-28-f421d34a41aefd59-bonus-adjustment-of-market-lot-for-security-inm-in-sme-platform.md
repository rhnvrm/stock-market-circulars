## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of market lot for Interiors & More Limited (Symbol: INM) listed on the SME platform due to a 1:1 bonus issue. The market lot will be adjusted from the previous lot size to 600 shares with an adjustment factor of 2, effective December 05, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Interiors & More Limited (INM)
- Corporate Action: Bonus Issue
- Bonus Ratio: 1:1
- Adjustment Factor: 2
- New Market Lot: 600 shares
- Ex-date: December 05, 2025
- Record Date: December 05, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action adjustment for bonus issue implementation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load the updated security.gz, nnf_security.gz, and MII - Security File.gz files in their trading applications before trading on December 05, 2025
- Files are available in the common/NTNEAT directory on the Extranet server
- MII - Security File.gz is also accessible on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports
- Members must ensure trading systems reflect the new market lot of 600 shares before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 05, 2025**: Ex-date and Record Date for bonus issue
- **December 05, 2025**: Effective date for adjusted market lot

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts trading in INM on the SME platform. Members and investors trading in this security must be aware of the new market lot size of 600 shares. The 1:1 bonus ratio with an adjustment factor of 2 means shareholders will receive one additional share for every share held. Trading members must update their systems to prevent order rejection or trading disruptions on and after the effective date. The impact is limited to this single SME security and does not affect broader market operations.
