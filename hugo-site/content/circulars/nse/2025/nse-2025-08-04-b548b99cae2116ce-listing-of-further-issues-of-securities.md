## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of further issues of securities for 13 companies effective August 5, 2025. The securities include equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and pursuant to scheme arrangements. All securities will trade with market lot size of 1 and are pari passu with existing shares.

## Key Points

- 13 companies getting additional securities listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- Securities include ESOP allotments and scheme arrangement shares
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- No lock-in restrictions applicable
- Trading effective from August 5, 2025

## Companies and Details

**Brand Concepts Limited (BCONCEPTS)**
- 1,204,077 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- Allotted under Scheme of Arrangement on June 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Members to note the effective date and security details

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine listings of additional shares for existing listed companies. The securities represent small percentages of total share capital and include employee benefit allocations and corporate restructuring shares. Trading liquidity may see marginal increase for affected counters.
