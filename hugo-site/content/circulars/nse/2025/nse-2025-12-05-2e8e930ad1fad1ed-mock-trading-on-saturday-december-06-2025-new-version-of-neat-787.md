## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, December 06, 2025 to test the new NEAT+ version 7.8.7. The mock session will simulate three trading sessions from primary and DR sites with specific timings for pre-open, normal market, T+0 market, block deals, auction market, and call auction sessions. This circular provides the complete schedule and important instructions regarding version management and algorithmic trading compliance.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for December 06, 2025 (Saturday)
- New NEAT+ version 7.8.7 will be tested during mock session
- Three trading sessions planned: Two from Primary Site and one from DR Site
- Trading Session-1: 08:45 to 10:00 hrs
- Trading Session-2: 12:15 to 17:15 hrs (includes contingency testing from 14:30 to 15:30)
- Trading Session-3 (DR Site): 16:00 to 17:15 hrs
- Live re-login window: 18:30 to 19:00 hrs
- NEAT+ version 7.8.4 will be discontinued on December 06, 2025 (mock day)
- NEAT+ version 7.8.6 will be discontinued on January 03, 2026
- Version 7.8.7 download path: /common/NEATPlus787

## Regulatory Changes

- Reference made to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/132 dated September 30, 2025 regarding safer participation of retail investors in algorithmic trading
- Trading members must adhere to the glide path provided by SEBI for algorithmic trading compliance
- Continuation of block trading session updates from circular NSE/CMTR/71394 dated November 20, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must participate in the mock trading session
- Members must upgrade to NEAT+ version 7.8.7 before the discontinuation dates
- Members must ensure compliance with SEBI's algorithmic trading requirements as per the prescribed glide path
- Members must test all trading functionalities during the three mock sessions including:
  - Block Deal Windows (Morning and Afternoon sessions)
  - Pre-Open sessions with random closure
  - Special Pre-Open sessions for IPO and relisted securities
  - T+0 market trading and trade modifications
  - Call Auction sessions for illiquid securities
  - Auction market operations
  - Contingency trading (Session-2: 14:30-15:30)
  - DR Site failover operations (Session-3)

## Important Dates

- **December 05, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 06, 2025**: Mock trading session date; NEAT+ version 7.8.4 discontinuation
- **January 03, 2026**: NEAT+ version 7.8.6 discontinuation deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All trading members must participate in the mock session to ensure their systems are compatible with NEAT+ 7.8.7. The mock session provides crucial testing opportunity for system upgrades and contingency procedures.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a mock trading session on Saturday with no actual market transactions. No impact on live trading or investor positions.

**Technical Impact**: Medium - Members must coordinate system upgrades, test multiple trading scenarios including DR site failover, and ensure readiness for version discontinuation deadlines. The three-session format allows comprehensive testing of all trading mechanisms including T+0 settlement, block deals, and auction markets.

**Compliance Impact**: Medium - Members must ensure alignment with SEBI's algorithmic trading guidelines while implementing the new system version.
