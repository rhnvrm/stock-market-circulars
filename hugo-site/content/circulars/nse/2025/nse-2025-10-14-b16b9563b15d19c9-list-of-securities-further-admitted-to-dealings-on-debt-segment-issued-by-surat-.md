## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted two new debt securities issued by Surat Municipal Corporation for trading on the Debt Segment. Both securities are fixed-rate bonds with 8% coupon, rated CRISIL AA, with maturity dates in 2029 and 2030 respectively. The securities will be available for trading effective October 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new municipal bonds admitted to NSE Debt Segment
- Issuer: Surat Municipal Corporation
- Both securities have issue date of October 13, 2025
- Issue size: Rs. 100 crore each (Rs. 10,000 lakhs)
- Face value: Rs. 500 per bond
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA for both securities
- Coupon rate: 8% fixed for both bonds
- Coupon frequency: Periodic
- Securities symbols: SMC29 (maturing 2029) and SMC30 (maturing 2030)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under existing framework referenced in circular NSE/CML/23366 dated May 8, 2013 and NSE/DS/24832 dated October 25, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Standard debt segment trading rules apply.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 14, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Issue Date:** October 13, 2025
- **First Interest Payment:** April 12, 2026
- **Next Interest Payment:** April 13, 2026
- **Maturity Dates:**
  - SMC29: October 13, 2029
  - SMC30: October 13, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of municipal bonds that provides additional fixed-income investment options for institutional and retail investors. The securities add liquidity to the municipal bond market segment. Rating of CRISIL AA indicates strong creditworthiness of Surat Municipal Corporation.
