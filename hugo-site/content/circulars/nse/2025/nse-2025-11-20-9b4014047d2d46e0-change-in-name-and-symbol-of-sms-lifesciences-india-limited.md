## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that SMS Lifesciences India Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective November 26, 2025. The company will be renamed to HALEOS LABS LIMITED and its trading symbol will change from SMSLIFE to HALEOSLABS.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: SMS Lifesciences India Limited
- New company name: HALEOS LABS LIMITED
- Existing trading symbol: SMSLIFE
- New trading symbol: HALEOSLABS
- Change effective from: November 26, 2025
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/71379, Circular No. 2259/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate name and symbol change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

All exchange members are informed to update their systems and records to reflect the new company name and trading symbol effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 26, 2025**: Effective date for name and symbol change

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. Investors and market participants should note the symbol change when placing orders or tracking the stock from November 26, 2025 onwards. The name change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or existing shareholdings.
