## Summary

Exim Routes Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as of December 18, 2025, following the listing of its equity shares through an SME IPO. The filing is submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The company has declared standard capital structure with no complex securities outstanding.

## Key Points

- Post-IPO shareholding pattern filed for Exim Routes Limited
- Filing date: December 18, 2025
- Submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Promoter shares are locked-in as per IPO requirements
- No pledged shares or non-disposal undertakings by promoters
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners identified
- All equity shares held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard compliance disclosure required post-listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR within prescribed timelines
- Declaration of capital structure including partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, and encumbrances
- Disclosure of locked-in shares, pledged shares, and beneficial ownership details
- Maintain updated records in dematerialized form

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: December 18, 2025 (Post-IPO shareholding pattern)
- **Reporting Period**: As of December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine post-IPO compliance filing for an SME company listing on NSE. The shareholding pattern disclosure provides transparency to investors about the ownership structure.

**Operational Impact**: Standard compliance requirement with no operational changes for the company or market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Provides initial shareholding information to investors post-listing, including confirmation of promoter lock-in and clean capital structure without complex securities.
