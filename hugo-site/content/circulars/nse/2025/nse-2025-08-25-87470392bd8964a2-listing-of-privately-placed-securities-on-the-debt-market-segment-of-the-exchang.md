## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial paper securities from 10 companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective August 25, 2025. The securities include commercial paper from major companies with redemption dates ranging from November 2025 to March 2026.

## Key Points

- 13 commercial paper securities listed from 10 different companies
- All securities allotted on August 22, 2025
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 for all securities
- Issue prices range from ₹480,516 to ₹492,873
- Total securities quantity: 73,500 units

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must comply with ongoing debt market segment requirements
- Securities are now available for trading on NSE debt platform

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: November 19-21, 2025 (most securities), March 10, 2026 (Piramal Enterprises)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for debt market participants as it adds liquidity and investment options. Companies include:
- **Piramal Finance Limited**: 3 CP securities (18,000 units)
- **NABARD**: Largest single issue (34,000 units)
- **Tata Communications**: 14,000 units
- **Godrej Industries, Blue Star, ABML, HSBC Investdirect, Piramal Enterprises**: Smaller issues

Provides short-term investment opportunities for institutional investors in high-quality commercial paper.
