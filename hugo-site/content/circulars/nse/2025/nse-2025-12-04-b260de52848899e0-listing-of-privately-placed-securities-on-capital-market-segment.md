## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of two companies on the capital market segment. Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited is listing bonds with 7.36% coupon maturing in 2028, and TATA PROJECTS LIMITED is listing bonds with 7.39% coupon also maturing in 2028. The securities will be available for trading from December 05, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities admitted to trading on NSE capital market segment
- Cholamandalam Investment: 7.36% bonds maturing December 02, 2028 (Symbol: 736CIFL28, ISIN: INE121A07SP3)
- TATA PROJECTS: 7.39% bonds maturing December 01, 2028 (Symbol: 739TPL28, ISIN: INE725H08253)
- Market lot size: 1 for both securities
- Trading series: N0 for both securities
- Securities to be identified by designated codes on trading system

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (market lot of 1 for both securities)
- Securities identified as Series N0 for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 05, 2025
- **Cholamandalam Bond Maturity:** December 02, 2028
- **TATA PROJECTS Bond Maturity:** December 01, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for listing privately placed debt securities. The circular affects only those members and investors interested in trading these specific debt instruments. The bonds are private placements with institutional characteristics, and the listing provides a secondary market avenue for existing bondholders. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
