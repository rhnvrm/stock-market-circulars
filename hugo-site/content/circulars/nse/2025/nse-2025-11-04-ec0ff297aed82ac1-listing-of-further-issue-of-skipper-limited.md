## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has approved the listing of 19,176 equity shares of Skipper Limited (Symbol: SKIPPER) arising from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares. The securities will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment with effect from November 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Skipper Limited (Symbol: SKIPPER)
- **Number of securities:** 19,176 equity shares
- **Transaction type:** Conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares
- **ISIN:** INE439E01022 (permanent ISIN)
- **Temporary ISIN:** IN8439E01013 (for dematerialized mode)
- **Face value:** Re. 1/-
- **Paid-up value:** Re. 1/-
- **Market lot:** 1 share
- **Series:** EQ (Equity)
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Lock-in:** Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in pursuance of existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the designated security codes for trading purposes
- Securities will be identified only by designated codes on the trading system
- Trading shall be conducted in the specified lot size (1 share)
- Dematerialized shares have been credited under temporary ISIN (IN8439E01013) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012

## Important Dates

- **Circular issue date:** November 04, 2025
- **Effective date for listing:** November 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The conversion involves a small number of shares (19,176) spread across five distinctive number ranges. The shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions. The listing facilitates completion of the capital structure by converting partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up status, providing full liquidity to the shareholders concerned.
