## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Pine Labs Limited (Symbol: PINELABS, ISIN: INE15B701018) following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The circular notifies all exchange members about this upcoming listing, though the specific listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Pine Labs Limited
- Trading Symbol: PINELABS
- ISIN: INE15B701018
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71268, Circular No. 2206/2025
- Department: LISTING

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are informed to take note of the forthcoming listing and prepare their systems accordingly. Specific compliance requirements related to trading will be communicated when the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through a separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to the NSE platform. Pine Labs Limited's shares will be available for trading once the listing date is confirmed. Market participants should prepare for the new security's trading commencement. The actual market impact will depend on the IPO subscription details, pricing, and market conditions at the time of listing.
