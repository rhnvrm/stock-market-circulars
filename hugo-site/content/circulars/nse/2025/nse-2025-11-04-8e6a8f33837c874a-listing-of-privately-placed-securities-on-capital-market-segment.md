## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities of Power Finance Corporation Limited on the capital market segment. The securities, designated as PFCL 7.10% 2040 Series 254, will be available for trading with effect from November 06, 2025. The circular specifies the security codes, lot sizes, and trading parameters as per NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- Power Finance Corporation Limited's debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- Security Symbol: 710PFCL40, Series: N0
- Coupon Rate: 7.10% per annum
- Maturity Date: November 12, 2040
- ISIN Code: INE134E08NX1
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- Effective Date: November 06, 2025
- Issue Description: PFCL 7.10% 2040 Sr 254

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies the listing pursuant to existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - admission of securities to dealings
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A - lot size specifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (710PFCL40) for identification on the trading system
- Trading in these securities must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to accommodate the new security listing before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: November 06, 2025
- **Maturity Date of Securities**: November 12, 2040

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with a trading avenue for Power Finance Corporation Limited's privately placed debt securities on the NSE platform. The 15-year tenure debt instrument offers a 7.10% coupon rate, providing a long-term investment option. Impact is limited to investors and traders interested in debt securities of Power Finance Corporation Limited.
