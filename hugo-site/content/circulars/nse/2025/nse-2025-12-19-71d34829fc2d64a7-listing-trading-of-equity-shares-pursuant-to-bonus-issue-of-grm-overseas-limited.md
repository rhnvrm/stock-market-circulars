## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of GRM Overseas Limited (Symbol: GRMOVER) pursuant to a bonus issue. A total of 12,27,04,000 equity shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective December 29, 2025. The securities are identified by ISIN INE192H01020.

## Key Points

- Company Name: GRM Overseas Limited
- Trading Symbol: GRMOVER
- ISIN: INE192H01020
- Number of Securities: 12,27,04,000 (12.27 crore shares)
- Distinctive Number Range: 61352001 to 184056000
- Record Date: December 24, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: December 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement Date: December 29, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/71894, Circular No. 2486/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities shall be identified only by their designated codes on the trading system
- All members must note the effective date of December 29, 2025 for trading commencement
- The bonus shares will be credited to shareholders as per the record date of December 24, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Circular issuance date
- **December 24, 2025**: Record date for bonus issue eligibility
- **December 26, 2025**: Deemed date of allotment
- **December 29, 2025**: Effective date for listing and trading commencement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing of 12.27 crore bonus shares will increase the free float and liquidity of GRM Overseas Limited stock. Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares as per the ratio determined by the company.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard corporate action processing. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who hold shares as of the record date (December 24, 2025). The bonus issue increases shareholding without additional investment, though the stock price will adjust proportionally.
