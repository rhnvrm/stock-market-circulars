## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of two privately placed non-convertible debentures issued by Bharti Telecom Limited on the capital market segment. The securities will be available for trading from December 04, 2025, with designated security codes and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Bharti Telecom Limited admitted to NSE capital market segment
- BTL 7.40% 2029 Series XXVIII (Symbol: 740BTL29, ISIN: INE403D08298)
- BTL 7.30% 2027 Series XXVII (Symbol: 730BTL27, ISIN: INE403D08306)
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Trading series: N0 for both issues
- Securities to be identified by designated codes on trading system

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes (740BTL29 and 730BTL27) for identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified market lot sizes (1 unit)
- Securities traded in N0 series as per NSE regulations

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Maturity Date (740BTL29):** February 01, 2029
- **Maturity Date (730BTL27):** December 01, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine listing of privately placed debt securities affects only specific institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact:** Low - standard listing procedure with no special trading requirements beyond normal NSE debt market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to existing debenture holders of Bharti Telecom who can now trade these securities on the exchange platform, providing liquidity for private placement investors.
