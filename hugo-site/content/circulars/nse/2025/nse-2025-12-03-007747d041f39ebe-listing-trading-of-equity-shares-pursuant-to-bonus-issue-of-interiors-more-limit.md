## Summary

NSE has admitted equity shares of Interiors & More Limited (Symbol: INM) to trading on the Capital Market segment pursuant to a bonus issue. The securities will commence trading from December 09, 2025. A total of 69,95,400 shares with ISIN INE0OPC01015 have been allotted under this bonus issue.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Interiors & More Limited
- Trading Symbol: INM
- ISIN: INE0OPC01015
- Number of bonus shares issued: 69,95,400
- Distinctive Number Range: 6995401 to 13990800
- Trading effective date: December 09, 2025
- Record date: December 05, 2025
- Deemed date of allotment: December 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated codes (Symbol: INM, ISIN: INE0OPC01015) for trading purposes
- Trading systems should be updated to reflect the additional shares before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 05, 2025**: Record date for bonus issue
- **December 08, 2025**: Deemed date of allotment
- **December 09, 2025**: Trading commences for bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The bonus issue increases the total number of tradable shares of Interiors & More Limited, which may improve liquidity. Existing shareholders receive additional shares proportional to their holdings. No immediate impact on broader market operations or other securities.
