## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued instructions for early pay-in of securities for Dev Information Technology Limited (DEVIT) during its face value split from Rs 5 per share to Rs 2 per share. Members must follow specific procedures for early pay-in using old ISIN before record date and new ISIN after record date.

## Key Points

- Face value split from Rs 5 per share to Rs 2 per share
- Record date and Ex-date: August 21, 2025
- Early pay-in must be done in existing/old ISIN (INE060X01026) without conversion adjustment
- Conversion ratio example: For sale of 5 shares, early pay-in should be 2 shares in old ISIN
- Block mechanism rules apply as per previous circulars
- Client-wise allocation files required for custodians using pool accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Circular references existing framework from consolidated circular NCL/CMPT/67751 dated April 29, 2025, and block mechanism circular NCL/CMPT/53386 dated August 22, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must execute early pay-in in old ISIN for specified settlement dates
- Custodians using pool accounts must upload client-wise allocation details based on actual traded quantity
- Client-wise early pay-in allocation files should not be uploaded when using block mechanism
- Switch to new ISIN for pay-in instructions after record date
- Adhere to SEBI block mechanism requirements for client demat accounts

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Ex-Date**: August 21, 2025
- **EPI Dates**: August 21-22, 2025
- **Settlement Numbers**: 2025160 (Aug 21), 2025160 & 2025161 (Aug 22)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on DEVIT trading operations during corporate action period. Members and custodians must adjust their settlement processes temporarily. Proper execution of early pay-in procedures is essential to maintain margin exemption benefits and ensure smooth settlement of trades during the face value split transition.
