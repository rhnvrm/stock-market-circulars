## Summary

NSE has provisionally empaneled M/s. Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider. The vendor will offer Black Box algorithmic trading solutions to NSE trading members. This empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Vendor empaneled: M/s. Stratzy Fintech Private Limited
- Type of algorithmic solution: Black Box
- Empanelment status: Provisional
- Location: Flat No.1501, Center Point, Mulund East, Mumbai-400081
- Contact: +91 8588029967
- Effective date: Immediate
- Complete list of empaneled algo providers available on NSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification of a new vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for trading members. This is informational for members who may wish to utilize algorithmic trading solutions from empaneled providers.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Immediate effect from December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative empanelment that expands the pool of algorithmic trading solution providers available to trading members.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally empaneled vendor option. No mandatory actions required.

**Target Audience**: NSE trading members interested in deploying algorithmic trading strategies through empaneled vendors.

**Reference**: Complete list of empaneled algo providers maintained at https://www.nseindia.com/static/trade/empanelled-algo-providers-exchange
