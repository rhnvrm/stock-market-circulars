## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of one government security on its capital market segment. The GOI LOAN 7.24% 2055 bond will be available for trading starting August 20, 2025, with designated security code 724GS2055 and ISIN IN0020250075.

## Key Points

- Single government security being listed: GOI LOAN 7.24% 2055
- Trading symbol: 724GS2055
- Coupon rate: 7.24%
- Maturity date: August 18, 2055
- ISIN code: IN0020250075
- Lot size: 1
- Market series: GS (Government Securities)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing announcement under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, specifically Regulation 3.1.1 for security admission and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot size specification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security code 724GS2055 for system identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot size of 1
- Standard capital market segment trading regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Bond Maturity**: August 18, 2055

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine government securities listing. Provides additional investment option for institutional and retail investors seeking long-term government bonds with 7.24% coupon rate. No impact on equity markets or existing trading operations.
