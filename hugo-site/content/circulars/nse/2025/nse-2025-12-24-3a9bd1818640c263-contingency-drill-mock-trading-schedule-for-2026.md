## Summary

NSE has notified the calendar schedule for Contingency Drills and Mock Trading sessions for the year 2026. These sessions are conducted periodically to test trading infrastructure and recovery & response mechanisms across Capital Market (CM), Futures & Options (F&O), Currency Derivatives (CD), and Commodity Derivatives (CO) segments. The calendar enables market participants to plan and schedule their own contingency plans and testing activities.

## Key Points

- Contingency drills scheduled monthly throughout 2026 for all trading segments
- Sessions cover CM, F&O, Currency Derivatives, and Commodity Derivatives segments
- Large-scale member participation is essential for session success
- Operational instructions and timings will be communicated separately for each session
- Members can plan their own contingency testing based on this schedule

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a notification of scheduled operational testing sessions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are expected to actively participate in the scheduled contingency drills
- Members should plan and schedule their own contingency plans and testing aligned with NSE's calendar
- Await separate operational instructions and timings for each session

## Important Dates

**2026 Contingency Drill Schedule:**
- January 3, 2026
- February 7, 2026
- March 7, 2026
- April 11, 2026
- May 9, 2026
- June 6, 2026
- July 4, 2026
- August 1, 2026
- September 5, 2026
- October 10, 2026
- November 14, 2026
- December 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Members need to allocate resources and plan participation in monthly drills throughout 2026.

**Market Impact:** Low - These are scheduled testing sessions that do not affect regular market operations but ensure infrastructure resilience.

**Member Impact:** Medium - Requires coordination and participation from trading members across all segments to ensure effective testing of recovery mechanisms and contingency infrastructure.
