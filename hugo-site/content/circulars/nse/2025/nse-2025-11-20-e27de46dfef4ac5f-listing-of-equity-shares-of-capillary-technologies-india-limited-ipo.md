## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol CAPILLARY with ISIN INE0ILV01024. The specific listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Capillary Technologies India Limited
- Trading Symbol: CAPILLARY
- ISIN: INE0ILV01024
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements outlined. This is an informational circular for market participants regarding the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to the NSE exchange. Market participants, particularly those interested in technology sector companies, should note the new trading symbol and ISIN for trading purposes once the listing date is announced. The impact is moderate as it expands investment opportunities but does not affect existing market operations or regulations.
