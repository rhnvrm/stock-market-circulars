## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of six privately placed debt securities on the capital market segment, effective December 02, 2025. The securities include debentures from Bajaj Auto Credit Limited (7.25% 2028), NIIF Infrastructure Finance Limited (7.48% 2033), IIFL Home Finance Limited (7.90% 2027), Muthoot Finance Limited (7.85% 2029), ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited (7.69% 2035), and B.N. Agritech Limited (9.75% 2028). All securities will trade with a market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- Six debt securities admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment
- All securities have a market lot size of 1 unit
- Trading to commence from December 02, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system
- Coupon rates range from 7.25% to 9.75%
- Maturity dates range from May 2027 to November 2035

## Regulatory Framework

This listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. Lot sizes are specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of the same regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification on the trading system
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot sizes (1 unit for all listed securities)
- All securities trade in N0 series

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The addition of these debt securities provides investors with additional fixed-income investment options across various maturity profiles (2027-2035) and coupon rates (7.25%-9.75%). The securities are from established financial institutions and corporates, offering diversification in the debt market segment. No changes to trading systems or operational procedures are required.
