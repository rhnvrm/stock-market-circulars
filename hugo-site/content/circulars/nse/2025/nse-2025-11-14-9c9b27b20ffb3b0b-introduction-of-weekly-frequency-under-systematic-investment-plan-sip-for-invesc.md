## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced the introduction of weekly frequency under Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for Invesco India Consumption Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. This new feature will be available from November 17, 2025, and is offered only under the Growth Option of the fund.

## Key Points

- Weekly SIP frequency introduced for Invesco India Consumption Fund
- Minimum investment amount: Rs. 100 with multiples of Re. 1 thereafter
- Minimum number of instalments required: 12 (including first instalment)
- SIP days: Monday to Friday
- Available only under Growth Option
- Effective date: November 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product enhancement by Invesco Mutual Fund to provide additional investment frequency options to investors.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for members or investors. The AMC reserves the right to change or modify the frequency, minimum amount, instalments, dates, or other terms and conditions. All existing SIP terms and conditions applicable to other schemes of the Fund will apply to this weekly SIP facility.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Weekly SIP facility becomes available for Invesco India Consumption Fund

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to operational enhancements for mutual fund investments. The introduction of weekly SIP frequency provides investors with greater flexibility in their investment planning, particularly for those who prefer more frequent, smaller investments aligned with weekly cash flows. This change is specific to one mutual fund scheme and does not affect trading operations, market infrastructure, or regulatory compliance for exchange members.
