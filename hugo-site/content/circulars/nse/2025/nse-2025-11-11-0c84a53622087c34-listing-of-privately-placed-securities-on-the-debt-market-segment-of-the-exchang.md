## Summary

NSE notifies the listing of privately placed securities on the debt market segment with effect from November 11, 2025. The circular lists commercial papers from seven companies and debt securities from two companies that have been admitted to dealings under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Key Points

- Total of 7 commercial paper issues listed from Angel One Limited (2 series), IIFL Capital Services Limited, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, Astec LifeSciences Limited, Kotak Securities Limited, and HSBC Investdirect Financial Services (I) Limited
- 2 debt securities listed from Aegis Vopak Terminals Limited and ESAF Small Finance Bank Limited
- All securities assigned designated security codes and ISIN numbers
- Face value of commercial papers: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Face value of debt securities: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Allotment dates: November 10-11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Listing conducted in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: November 11, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: November 7, 10-11, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from February 2026 to August 2031 depending on security

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors access to debt instruments from financial services companies and other corporates. The securities are privately placed and primarily relevant to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in these specific instruments.
