## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has approved the listing of equity shares of Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited (trading symbol: GROWW) on the Capital Market segment effective November 12, 2025. The company issued 6,173,596,631 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each at an issue price of Rs. 100/- per share through an Initial Public Offering (IPO).

## Key Points

- **Trading Symbol**: GROWW
- **ISIN**: INE0HOQ01053
- **Face Value**: Rs. 2/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100/- per share
- **Total Shares Issued**: 6,173,596,631 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable for IPO category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Lock-in Details

The shareholding includes multiple tranches with varying lock-in periods:
- Lock-in expiry dates range from December 9, 2025 to May 11, 2027
- Certain shares are free from lock-in restrictions
- Lock-in periods of 1 year and 2 years apply to different share blocks as per regulatory requirements

## Regulatory Changes

None. This circular notifies the admission of new securities to trading under existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (GROWW) and ISIN (INE0HOQ01053) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- The security will participate in the pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI guidelines
- All trading members must update their systems to recognize the new listing

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 12, 2025
- **First Lock-in Expiry**: December 9, 2025
- **Subsequent Lock-in Expiries**: February 7, 2026; May 11, 2026; May 11, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This is a significant listing of a major fintech platform (Groww) with over 6.17 billion shares being admitted to trading. Given Groww's prominent position in the Indian investment and trading ecosystem, this listing is expected to generate substantial trading interest and liquidity.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems to accommodate the new security code and ensure compliance with pre-open session requirements for IPO category securities.

**Investor Impact**: High - The listing provides public market access to shares of a widely-used fintech platform, offering investment opportunities to retail and institutional investors. The staggered lock-in periods indicate a phased release of promoter and investor holdings over the next 1-2 years.
