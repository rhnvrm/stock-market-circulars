## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of JSW Cement Limited equity shares following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol JSWCEMENT with ISIN INE718I01012.

## Key Points

- Company Name: JSW Cement Limited
- Trading Symbol: JSWCEMENT
- ISIN: INE718I01012
- Listing exchange: National Stock Exchange of India
- Security type: Equity Shares (IPO)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing for trading preparation purposes.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact as this adds a new equity security to NSE trading platform. The actual listing date will be communicated separately, allowing market participants to prepare for trading commencement.
