## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of 10,000 equity shares of S J Logistics (India) Limited on the SME EMERGE platform. The shares were allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants issued on preferential basis and will commence trading from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: S J Logistics (India) Limited (Symbol: SJLOGISTIC)
- Number of shares: 10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 576 per share
- Trading platform: NSE Capital Market segment - SME EMERGE
- Market lot size: 250 shares
- ISIN: INE0F3301020
- Distinctive number range: 15283131 to 15293130

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for the listed securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (250 shares)
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circular guidelines

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 1, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025
- Lock-in expiry date: February 28, 2027
- Circular effective date: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (10,000) for an SME company. The shares are subject to lock-in period until February 2027, limiting immediate trading volume. This is a routine administrative listing following warrant conversion on preferential basis.
