## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India announces the launch of HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The New Fund Offer will accept subscriptions from November 07, 2025 to November 21, 2025 (till 11:59 PM), with allotment scheduled for November 26, 2025. Both Regular and Direct Growth options are available for investment.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: November 07, 2025 to November 21, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Two plan options available: Regular Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1JX8) and Direct Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1JY6)
- Fund category: Equity
- Allotment date: November 26, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in the designated Clearing Corporation account by November 25, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Fund tracks the BSE India Sector Leaders Index
- Available exclusively on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 25, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Standard KYC and investor onboarding requirements apply for mutual fund investments

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: NFO subscription opening date
- **November 21, 2025 (11:59 PM)**: NFO subscription closing date
- **November 25, 2025 (11:59 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds receipt in Clearing Corporation account
- **November 26, 2025**: Allotment date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine mutual fund NFO launch that expands investment options for retail and institutional investors on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for interested investors. Provides a new equity index fund option tracking the BSE India Sector Leaders Index, offering diversified exposure to sector-leading companies. Investors have a 15-day window to subscribe.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard NFO processing procedures apply for exchange members and the clearing corporation.

**Contact Support**: Toll-free helpline 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com available for queries.
