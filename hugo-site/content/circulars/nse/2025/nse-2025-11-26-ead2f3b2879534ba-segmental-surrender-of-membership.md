## Summary

NSE has approved the voluntary surrender of membership in Currency Derivative and IRF (Interest Rate Futures) segments by Ashlar Securities Private Limited (SEBI Registration: INZ000203739). The surrender was approved on November 21, 2025. This is a routine administrative action with no broader market implications.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: Ashlar Securities Private Limited
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000203739
- Surrendered Segments: Currency Derivative & IRF (Interest Rate Futures)
- Surrender Approval Date: November 21, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/COMP/71483, Circular No. 98/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary segment surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are requested to take note of this segmental surrender. No specific action required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Surrender approval date
- **November 26, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This affects only the specific trading member's participation in Currency Derivative and IRF segments.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on other market participants. Ashlar Securities Private Limited will no longer operate in the surrendered segments but may continue in other segments where it holds membership.

**Scope**: Single member administrative action with no systemic implications.
