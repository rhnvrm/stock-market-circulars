## Summary

BSE announces the addition of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544621) to the BSE SME IPO INDEX effective at the open of Wednesday, December 3, 2025. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Tuesday, December 2, 2025.

## Key Points

- SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED listed on BSE on December 2, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on December 3, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544621
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolios may need to adjust holdings to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED listing effective on BSE
- **December 3, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index maintenance activity following a new SME listing. Index funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to include this stock in their portfolios. The action primarily affects passive investment strategies and index rebalancing activities.
