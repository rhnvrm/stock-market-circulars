## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading of new equity shares of Madhusudan Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 511000) effective December 10, 2025. A total of 68,57,334 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each were issued at a premium of Rs. 8/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 68,57,334 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 18/- per share (premium of Rs. 8/-)
- Allotted to: Non-promoters on preferential basis
- Method: Conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 14562154 to 21419487
- ISIN: INE856D01011
- Trading commencement: December 10, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should note:
- New shares are available for trading from December 10, 2025
- All 68,57,334 shares are subject to lock-in until June 20, 2025
- Trading systems should reflect updated share capital and distinctive number ranges

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 10, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** June 20, 2025
- **Notice Date:** December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific listing event with minimal broader market implications.

**Operational Impact:** The listing increases the total tradable shares of Madhusudan Securities Ltd, though all new shares remain under lock-in until June 20, 2025. The preferential allotment to non-promoters through warrant conversion represents dilution to existing shareholders but follows standard regulatory procedures. Market participants and investors in this specific scrip should note the updated share capital structure.
