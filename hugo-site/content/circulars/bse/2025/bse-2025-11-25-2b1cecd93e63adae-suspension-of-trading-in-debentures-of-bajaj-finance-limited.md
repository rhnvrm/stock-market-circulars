## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Bajaj Finance Limited effective December 9, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to BFL 6% debentures maturing on December 24, 2025 (Private Placement)
- ISIN: INE296A07RO8, Code: 960354
- Record date set for December 9, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 9, 2025
- Notice number: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 9, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Only debenture holders on record as of December 9, 2025 will be eligible for redemption proceeds and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This affects only holders of the specific debenture series (BFL-6%-24-12-25-PVT). The suspension is temporary and related to routine redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of December 9, 2025 will receive their principal redemption amount plus final interest payment. No impact on equity shareholders of Bajaj Finance.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN from December 9, 2025. Standard operational procedure for debenture redemptions.
