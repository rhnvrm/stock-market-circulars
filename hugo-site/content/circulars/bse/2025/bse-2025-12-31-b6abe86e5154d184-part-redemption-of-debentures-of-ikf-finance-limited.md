## Summary

IKF Finance Limited has announced part redemption of its 9% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing on October 17, 2027. The record date for payment of interest and part redemption is January 2, 2026. Following the part redemption, the debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 58,333.35 per debenture effective January 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: IKF Finance Limited
- Debenture Code: IKFFL-9%-17-10-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE859C07196 (Security Code: 976110)
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption and Interest Payment
- Record Date: January 2, 2026
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 58,333.35 per debenture
- Effective Date: January 2, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-790/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value from the effective date
- All trading systems should reflect the updated face value of Rs. 58,333.35 per debenture starting January 2, 2026
- Debenture holders on record as of January 2, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 2, 2026 - Determination of eligible debenture holders for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date**: January 2, 2026 - Trading commences with reduced face value (Settlement: DR-790/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only the debenture holders of IKF Finance Limited. The part redemption is a standard debt servicing activity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value. The debenture will continue trading on the debt segment with modified parameters.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for debenture holders who will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment on the record date. The reduced face value reflects the return of principal to investors.
