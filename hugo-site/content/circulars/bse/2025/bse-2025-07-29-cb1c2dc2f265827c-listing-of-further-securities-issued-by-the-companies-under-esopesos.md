## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total securities being listed vary by company, ranging from 2,221 shares (Oracle Financial Services) to 1,207,200 shares (Dhani Services)
- All securities have face values between ₹1 to ₹10
- None of the securities are under lock-in period
- Effective trading date: July 30, 2025

## Companies and Securities Details

- **AFFLE (India) Limited**: 37,000 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Avantel Limited**: 7,600 shares (₹2 face value)
- **AXIS Bank Ltd**: 67,820 shares (₹2 face value)
- **CEINSYS Tech Limited**: 400,000 shares (₹10 face value)
- **DCB Bank Limited**: 55,656 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Dhani Services Limited**: 1,207,200 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Eris Lifesciences Limited**: 9,468 shares (₹1 face value)
- **ICICI Lombard General Insurance**: 162,038 shares (₹10 face value)
- **L&T Technology Services**: 4,500 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Oracle Financial Services Software**: 2,221 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Ujjivan Small Finance Bank**: 289,961 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Zinka Logistics Solutions**: 184,545 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Zodiac Energy Limited**: 27,550 shares (₹10 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP listings. The additional shares may slightly increase the floating stock of the respective companies but volumes are relatively small compared to existing share capital for most companies.
