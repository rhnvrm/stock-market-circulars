## Summary

Standard Chartered Capital Limited has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 250 crore (25,000 units at Rs. 1,00,000 face value) on BSE's Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with allotment on December 10, 2025, carrying a 7.3% annual interest rate and maturing on January 9, 2029. The securities hold dual AAA ratings from CRISIL (Stable) and ICRA (Stable).

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977366 | **Scrip ID**: 73SCCL29 | **Detail Name**: SCCL-7.3%-9-1-29-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE403G07111
- **Issue Size**: 25,000 securities at Rs. 1,00,000 each (Rs. 250 crore total)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Interest Rate**: 7.3% per annum (paid annually)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable
- **Face Value & Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Trading Format**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G07111
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 10, 2026 to January 9, 2029
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: January 9, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with no impact on secondary market trading or equity segments.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need only note the new scrip details for potential trading activity in the debt segment. The securities are available for dematerialized trading with standard settlement procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and qualified buyers participating in private placements. The AAA rating indicates high credit quality and low default risk.
