## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited on private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The securities comprise 15,000 units with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each, totaling ₹150 crore. The debentures carry a 7.95% interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on November 27, 2030.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977324
- Scrip ID: 795AVHFL30
- ISIN: INE852O07188
- Quantity: 15,000 units
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: ₹150 crore
- Interest Rate: 7.95% per annum
- Interest Payment: Monthly (27th of each month)
- Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Maturity Date: November 27, 2030
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Put/Call Option: Not Available
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE852O07188
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 27, 2025
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: 27th of each month until November 27, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 27, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to trading members dealing in debt securities who may trade these instruments on BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and qualified investors access to CARE AA-rated debt instruments from Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited with monthly income option and 5-year tenure.
