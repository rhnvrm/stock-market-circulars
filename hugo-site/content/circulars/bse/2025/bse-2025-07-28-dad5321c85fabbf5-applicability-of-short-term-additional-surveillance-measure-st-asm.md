## Summary

BSE has announced updates to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective July 29, 2025. Four securities are being added to the ST-ASM framework, while no securities are moving out or changing stages. The framework includes consolidated listings of all securities currently under various stages of ST-ASM.

## Key Points

- 4 new securities added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving out of ST-ASM framework
- Consolidated list shows 21+ securities currently under ST-ASM Stage I
- Framework includes SME scrips and T+0 scrips based on parent company inclusion

## Regulatory Changes

Securities newly included in ST-ASM framework will be subject to additional surveillance measures including enhanced monitoring of trading activities and potential trading restrictions during specified periods.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ST-ASM framework requirements
- Enhanced due diligence required for transactions in listed securities
- Monitoring of unusual price and volume movements
- Adherence to position limits and margin requirements as applicable

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025 - New ST-ASM framework changes take effect

## Impact Assessment

The addition of four securities to ST-ASM framework will result in enhanced surveillance and potential trading restrictions. This may impact liquidity and trading volumes for the affected securities. Market participants should adjust their trading strategies accordingly and ensure compliance with enhanced surveillance requirements.
