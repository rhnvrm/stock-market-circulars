## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed 20,000 new debt securities (debentures) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 3, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crore. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.04% per annum with annual interest payments and mature on August 8, 2035. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/Stable and IND AAA/Stable, indicating highest safety.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977005, Scrip ID: 804AFL35
- ISIN: INE891K08224 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 20,000 debentures with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,443.40 (at premium)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crore
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, IND AAA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 8.04% per annum (paid annually)
- Tenor: Approximately 10 years
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options attached

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891K08224
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Annual
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: August 8, 2035

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing on the BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for debt investors seeking highly-rated fixed income instruments. The AAA rating indicates highest credit quality and lowest credit risk. The 8.04% interest rate provides attractive returns for a top-rated security in the current interest rate environment.

**Issuer Impact**: Provides Axis Finance Limited with long-term funding at competitive rates, supporting its lending and financing operations.

**Liquidity**: The securities are listed and tradeable on BSE Debt segment, providing secondary market liquidity to investors, though debt securities typically have lower trading volumes compared to equity.
