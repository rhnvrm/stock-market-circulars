## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) on its Debt segment. The listing consists of 11,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued on private placement basis. Trading commenced on November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD)
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (short-term debt)
- **Quantity**: 11,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,618.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 5,500 crore (11,000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakh)
- **Scrip Code**: 730524
- **Scrip ID**: NBAR071125
- **ISIN**: INE261F14ON6
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE261F14ON6
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 6, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing for a government-owned development bank. The instrument is a short-term debt security with high credit rating (A1+), indicating low default risk.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on trading members beyond awareness of the new debt instrument available for trading. The securities follow standard BSE Debt segment trading protocols.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term investment option backed by NABARD with high credit quality and approximately 3-month maturity.
