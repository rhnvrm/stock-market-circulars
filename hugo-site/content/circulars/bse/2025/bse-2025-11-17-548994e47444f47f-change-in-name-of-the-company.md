## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for company with CIN L62099MH1993PLC074167. The company name has been officially changed from VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED to IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED effective October 1, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED
- **New Name**: IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- **CIN**: L62099MH1993PLC074167
- **Original Name**: GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED (at incorporation)
- **Effective Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Registered Office**: Z1-3092, Akshar Business Park, Sector 25, Vashi, Navi Mumbai, Sanpada, Thane-400703, Maharashtra
- **Approving Authority**: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display its old name (VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED) along with the new name (IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 1, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Until October 1, 2027 (2 years from effective date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or trading status.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company structure, CIN, and legal entity remain unchanged. Only the corporate name has been modified.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Informational only. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders should note the new name for future correspondence and documentation. All rights and liabilities continue unchanged.

**Verification**: Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
