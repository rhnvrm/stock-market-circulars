## Summary

AVASARA FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511730) has announced a Rights Issue of equity shares with a record date of January 1, 2026. Existing shareholders will be entitled to subscribe to 2 new equity shares of Rs. 10 each for every 1 equity share held. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from January 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: AVASARA FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511730)
- Rights Entitlement: 2 equity shares for every 1 equity share held (2:1 ratio)
- Issue Price: Rs. 10 per share at par
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per equity share
- Payment Terms: Entire rights issue price payable at the time of application
- Record Date: January 1, 2026
- Ex-Rights Date: January 1, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-789/2025-2026)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities - Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification following existing rights issue framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure transactions in AVASARA FINANCE LIMITED shares are executed on ex-rights basis from January 1, 2026
- Full payment of Rs. 10 per share must be collected at the time of application submission
- All trades must be settled under settlement number DR-789/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 1, 2026 - Shareholders on this date eligible for rights entitlement
- **Ex-Rights Date**: January 1, 2026 - Trading begins on ex-rights basis
- **Circular Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-789/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders as of the record date will receive rights to subscribe to additional shares at a 2:1 ratio, providing opportunity to maintain or increase their stake at par value.

**Trading Impact**: From January 1, 2026, the stock will trade ex-rights, meaning buyers will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue. This typically results in a temporary price adjustment reflecting the dilution impact.

**Capital Structure Impact**: If fully subscribed, the rights issue will triple the company's equity capital (2 new shares for every 1 existing share), significantly increasing the equity base of AVASARA FINANCE LIMITED.
