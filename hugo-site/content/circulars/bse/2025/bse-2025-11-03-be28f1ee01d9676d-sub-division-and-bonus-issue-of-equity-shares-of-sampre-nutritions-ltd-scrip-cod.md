## Summary

SAMPRE NUTRITIONS LIMITED (BSE Scrip Code: 530617) has revised the record date for its equity share sub-division and bonus issue from 4 November 2025 to 11 November 2025. The company will allot 43,693,710 bonus equity shares with deemed allotment date of 12 November 2025, and the bonus shares will be available for trading from 13 November 2025.

## Key Points

- Original record date of 4 November 2025 (intimated on 27 October 2025) has been revised to 11 November 2025
- Purpose: Split/sub-division and bonus issue of equity shares
- Total bonus equity shares to be allotted: 43,693,710
- Distinctive Number Range (DNR): 43693711 to 87387420
- Deemed date of allotment: 12 November 2025 (T+1 day from record date)
- Circular issued in compliance with SEBI Circular SEBI/CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated 16 September 2024
- Regulation 42 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular is issued in compliance with existing regulations:
- Regulation 42 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- SEBI Circular SEBI/CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated 16 September 2024

## Compliance Requirements

**For the Company:**
- Credit of bonus shares must be completed in the depository system by 12:00 PM on 12 November 2025 (T+1 day)
- Ensure bonus shares are available for trading at Beginning of the Day (BOD) on 13 November 2025 (T+2 day)

**For Shareholders:**
- Shareholders holding equity shares as of the revised record date (11 November 2025) will be eligible for the bonus issue and sub-division

## Important Dates

- **Original Record Date:** 4 November 2025 (now revised)
- **Revised Record Date:** 11 November 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** 12 November 2025 (T+1 day)
- **Credit Deadline:** 12 November 2025 by 12:00 PM
- **Trading Commencement:** 13 November 2025 (T+2 day, BOD)
- **Circular Dated:** 1 November 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Existing shareholders as of 11 November 2025 will receive bonus shares, effectively doubling their shareholding or proportionate to the bonus ratio
- Stock split/sub-division will increase liquidity and reduce per-share price
- Trading in bonus shares will commence from 13 November 2025

**Shareholder Impact:**
- Medium impact for existing shareholders who need to track the revised record date
- Shareholders must hold shares on 11 November 2025 to be eligible for corporate action benefits
- Date revision provides additional time for investors to participate

**Operational Impact:**
- Depositories and trading systems need to process 43,693,710 new equity shares
- Market participants must adjust for the revised timeline
- Company must ensure timely credit of shares to maintain compliance with SEBI timelines
