## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Bajaj Housing Finance Limited (50,000 units) on the Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. These securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, carry a 7.10% annual interest rate, and mature on October 16, 2028. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977122, Scrip ID: 71BHFL28
- ISIN: INE377Y07607 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,352 per security
- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Interest Rate: 7.10% per annum paid annually
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Redemption Date: October 16, 2028
- No put or call options available
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE377Y07607
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **November 14, 2025**: Listing and admission to dealings on BSE Debt segment
- **October 16, 2026 to October 16, 2028**: Interest payment dates (annually)
- **October 16, 2028**: Redemption Date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands Bajaj Housing Finance Limited's debt offerings on the BSE platform and provides institutional and eligible investors with additional investment options in AAA-rated debt instruments. The high credit rating indicates low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors specifically interested in Bajaj Housing Finance securities.
