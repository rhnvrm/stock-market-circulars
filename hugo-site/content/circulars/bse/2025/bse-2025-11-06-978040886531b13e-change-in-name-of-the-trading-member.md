## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD. (Member No. 3281) will undergo a name change to "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD." effective from Friday, November 7, 2025. This is an administrative update with no impact on trading operations.

## Key Points

- Trading Member No. 3281 will change its name
- Old Name: LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD.
- New Name: LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD.
- Change reflects conversion from Private Limited to Public Limited company structure
- No impact on member number or trading operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative name change reflecting a change in the corporate structure of the trading member entity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their records to reflect the new name of Member No. 3281
- Use the new name in all future correspondence and dealings with this member
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 6, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 7, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Nil - This is an administrative change with no effect on market operations, trading systems, or settlement processes.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members dealing with Member No. 3281 should update their internal records. The member number remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in all system-level identifications and processes.
