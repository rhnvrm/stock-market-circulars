## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED for public review. The document was filed on December 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The exchange is inviting public comments on the draft offer document within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED on December 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and interested parties may review the draft offer document at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments or feedback on the draft offer document should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- The issuer company must keep the document publicly available for at least 21 days as per ICDR requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Public Comment Deadline**: January 21, 2026 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability Period**: Minimum 21 days from December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate market impact as it represents an early stage in the SME IPO process. The draft offer document stage precedes final regulatory approval and actual listing. Impact is limited to:

- **Potential Investors**: Opportunity to review company disclosures and provide feedback before final offer document
- **SME Segment**: Indicates continued activity in the SME IPO pipeline
- **SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED**: Company moves forward in its public listing journey

The actual market impact will be determined after the IPO is completed and the company lists on the SME platform. This notice is primarily procedural and informational in nature.
