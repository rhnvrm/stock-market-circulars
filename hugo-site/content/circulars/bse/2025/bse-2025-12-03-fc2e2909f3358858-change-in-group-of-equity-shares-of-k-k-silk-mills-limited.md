## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of K K SILK MILLS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544624), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment and shifted to M Group effective December 17, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing.

## Key Points

- K K SILK MILLS LIMITED scrip code: 544624
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date of transfer: December 17, 2025
- Notice issued in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251202-48 dated December 02, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading classification for K K SILK MILLS LIMITED equity shares will change from MT Group (Trade for Trade segment) to M Group (Rolling segment). This represents a shift from a restricted trading mechanism where each transaction requires upfront delivery to a more liquid rolling settlement system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment transfer effective December 17, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **December 02, 2025**: Previous related notice (No. 20251202-48) issued
- **December 03, 2025**: Current notice issued
- **December 17, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This segment change is positive for market liquidity as it moves K K SILK MILLS shares from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment. The Trade for Trade segment typically applies to securities requiring closer monitoring, while the Rolling segment allows normal trading with T+2 settlement. This upgrade indicates improved trading status post-IPO and should facilitate easier buying and selling for investors with better price discovery.
