## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding changes in the scheme names of HDFC mutual funds on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform. This is an administrative update to reflect updated naming conventions or branding changes for HDFC mutual fund schemes.

## Key Points

- Changes apply to HDFC mutual fund scheme names on BSE StAR MF platform
- Administrative nomenclature update for mutual fund schemes
- No impact on scheme characteristics, holdings, or investor positions
- Update effective from the date specified in the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to mutual fund scheme naming on the BSE trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to note the updated scheme names on BSE StAR MF platform
- System updates may be required for platforms displaying HDFC mutual fund schemes
- No action required from investors

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 09, 2025
- Effective Date: As specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is purely an administrative change affecting the display names of HDFC mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform. There is no impact on:
- Scheme characteristics or investment objectives
- Investor holdings or NAV calculations
- Trading operations or settlement
- Regulatory compliance requirements

Market participants and platforms may need to update their systems to reflect the new scheme names for accurate display and reporting purposes.
