## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Annapurna Finance Private Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 26,000 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 10.02% per annum with a maturity date of December 10, 2030. The securities are rated CRISIL A-/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977360, Scrip ID: 1002AFPL30, ISIN: INE515Q07665
- Quantity issued: 26,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Interest Rate: 10.02% per annum, payable half-yearly
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A-/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE515Q07665
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Standard tick size of 1 paise applies to all trades in these securities

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Half-yearly from June 10, 2026 to December 10, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The impact is limited to the debt market segment and specific to investors and trading members dealing in Annapurna Finance Private Limited securities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The securities follow standard trading protocols with dematerialized trading requirements.

**Investor Impact**: The listing provides an exit option for investors who were allotted these securities in the private placement, allowing them to trade on the BSE Debt platform. The CRISIL A-/Stable rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate safety for timely payment of financial obligations.
