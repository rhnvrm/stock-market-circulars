## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 4, 2025, due to the maturity of the debentures on December 5, 2025. Trading members are advised to cease dealings in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from December 4, 2025
- Debt security: ESPL-11%-5-12-25-PVT
- Scrip Code: 975669
- ISIN: INE01YL07284
- Maturity Date: December 5, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026
- Issuer: Earlysalary Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension due to debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new trades should be executed in the affected debt security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only one privately placed debt security that is reaching its scheduled maturity date. Trading members holding positions in this debenture should be aware that no further trading will be permitted after December 3, 2025. The impact is limited to holders of this specific instrument and does not affect broader market operations or other securities of the company.
