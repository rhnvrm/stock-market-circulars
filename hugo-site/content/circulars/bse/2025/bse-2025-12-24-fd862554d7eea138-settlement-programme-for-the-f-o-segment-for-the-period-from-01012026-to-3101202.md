## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for January 2026. The circular provides the daily settlement dates corresponding to each trade date during the month, following the standard T+1 settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- Follows daily settlement mechanism with T+1 settlement cycle
- Trading days: 20 trading days in January 2026
- Settlement handled by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.
- No holidays observed within the January 2026 trading period listed
- Notice issued by Tajindersingh Virdi, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly settlement calendar announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants must:
- Adhere to the published settlement schedule for F&O trades
- Ensure timely payment and delivery obligations as per settlement dates
- Note the settlement dates for margin and position management
