## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 18, 2025. The issue consists of 4,500 securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000/- each (total Rs. 225 crores), with maturity of 120 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 4,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,091/- per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 225 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730589
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF171125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FM1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 17, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FM1
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 17, 2026
- **Tenure**: 120 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides short-term debt market participants with an additional A1+ rated investment avenue from Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited. The high credit rating (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. The impact is limited to debt segment trading members and institutional investors interested in short-term commercial paper instruments. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at the provided contact numbers.
