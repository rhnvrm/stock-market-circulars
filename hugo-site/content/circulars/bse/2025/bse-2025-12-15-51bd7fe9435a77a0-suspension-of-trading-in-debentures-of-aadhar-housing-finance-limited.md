## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited effective December 22, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture series from the suspension date.

- Company: Aadhar Housing Finance Limited
- Debenture Series: AHFL-9.6%-6-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE538L07163
- Scrip Code: 953177
- Record Date: December 22, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 22, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-782/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice following established procedures for debt securities management and trading suspension protocols.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 22, 2025
- Trading members should ensure their systems and records reflect the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE538L07163 from the suspension date
- Members must communicate the suspension to affected clients holding or interested in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and localized to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and relates to the scheduled maturity/redemption event.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. Secondary market trading opportunity ceases from December 22, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended security. Back-office operations should process the redemption and interest payment for client holdings.

**Liquidity Impact**: No further liquidity available in this debenture series post-suspension date, which is expected as the instrument approaches redemption.
