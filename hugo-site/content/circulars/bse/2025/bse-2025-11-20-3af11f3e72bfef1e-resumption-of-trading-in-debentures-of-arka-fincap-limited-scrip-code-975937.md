## Summary

BSE announces the resumption of trading in debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED (Scrip Code 975937) effective November 21, 2025. The resumption follows the debenture holders' decision not to exercise the put option that was available with a record date of November 4, 2025. Trading had been suspended pending the put option exercise period.

## Key Points

- Debenture holders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED did not exercise the put option for redemption
- Trading in debentures with Scrip Code 975937 will resume from November 21, 2025
- Previous notice (20251030-60) dated October 30, 2025 had set November 4, 2025 as the record date for put option exercise
- Debenture holders have requested resumption of trading
- Reference number: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural notice regarding the resumption of trading following the completion of a put option exercise period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note that the debentures will be available for trading from November 21, 2025
- No additional compliance actions required from market participants

## Important Dates

- **October 30, 2025**: Exchange notice issued setting record date for put option
- **November 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption on exercise of put option
- **November 20, 2025**: Notice issued for trading resumption
- **November 21, 2025**: Trading resumes in debentures (Scrip Code 975937)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice affects only holders and potential traders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures (Scrip Code 975937). The decision by debenture holders not to exercise the put option indicates continued confidence in holding the securities. Trading resumption restores normal market liquidity for these debt instruments. No broader market implications expected.
