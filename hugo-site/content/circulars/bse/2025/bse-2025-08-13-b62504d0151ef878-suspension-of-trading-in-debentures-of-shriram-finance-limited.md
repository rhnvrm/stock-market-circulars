## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures of Shriram Finance Limited effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date of August 15, 2025 for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE721A08AH3 (Code: 946997)
- Debenture series: SFL-11%-30-08-2025-PVT
- No dealings allowed from August 14, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard procedural suspension for corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Members advised to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 15, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 14, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension is temporary and procedural, affecting only specific debenture series. Normal trading expected to resume after corporate action completion.
