## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544656, symbol HRS, and ISIN No. INE1E1V01017. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544656
- Trading Symbol: HRS
- ISIN Number: INE1E1V01017
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes associated with this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing will add one more equity to the SME segment. Trading members should prepare for the new listing once the specific date is announced. No immediate action required from market participants.
