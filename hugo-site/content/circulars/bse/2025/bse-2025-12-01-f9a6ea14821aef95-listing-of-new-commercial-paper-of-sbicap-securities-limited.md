## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 1, 2025. The instrument comprises 2,000 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a 90-day tenure maturing on February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: SBICAP Securities Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730670
- **Scrip ID**: SSL281125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CU9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CU9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to SBICAP Securities Limited and offers debt market participants an additional A1+ rated investment option with 90-day maturity. No impact on equity markets or broader regulatory framework.
