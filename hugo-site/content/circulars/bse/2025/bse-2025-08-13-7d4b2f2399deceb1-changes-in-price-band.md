## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 12 securities with effect from August 14, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 10%, with most securities having a 5% price band limit.

## Key Points

- 12 securities affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from August 14, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 10%
- Most securities (9 out of 12) set at 5% price band
- NDL Ventures Ltd and Yatra Online Ltd have highest price band at 10%
- Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd has lowest price band at 2%

## Regulatory Changes

Price band modifications implemented as surveillance measure to control price volatility in identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to revised price bands from August 14, 2025
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Price band changes indicate enhanced surveillance for these securities, potentially limiting intraday price movements and reducing speculative trading activity. The varying percentages suggest different risk levels assigned to each security.
