## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 23,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Total Securities**: 23,000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,522 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730562
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL131125
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EG5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EG5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarifications should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt financing options for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited. The 91-day tenure commercial paper is a standard money market instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members can trade these debt securities through normal BSE Debt segment procedures. The securities carry high credit ratings (A1+) indicating low credit risk.

**Investor Impact**: The securities are suitable for institutional and high-net-worth investors seeking short-term debt instruments with strong credit ratings from a major retail company.
