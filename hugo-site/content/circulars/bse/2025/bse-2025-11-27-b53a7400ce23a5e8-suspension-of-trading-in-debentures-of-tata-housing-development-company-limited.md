## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of TATA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED (code: THDCL-8.2175%-17-9-27-PVT, ISIN: INE582L08052, scrip code: 976013) effective December 2, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: TATA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (8.2175% coupon)
- ISIN: INE582L08052
- Scrip Code: 976013
- Record Date: December 2, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 2, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-768/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 2, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in the affected debentures (ISIN: INE582L08052) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 2, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 2, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings permitted from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium impact on debt market participants holding or intending to trade these specific TATA HOUSING debentures. The suspension prevents any trading activity from December 2, 2025, affecting liquidity for this security.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the record date. Investors planning to buy or sell these debentures must complete transactions before December 2, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and alert clients holding or interested in these debentures about the trading suspension. Standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.
