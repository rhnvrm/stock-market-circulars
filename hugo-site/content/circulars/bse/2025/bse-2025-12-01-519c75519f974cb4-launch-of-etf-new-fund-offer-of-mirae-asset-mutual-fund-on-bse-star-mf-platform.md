## Summary

MIRAE ASSET Mutual Fund will launch the MIRAE ASSET BSE 500 DIVIDEND LEADERS 50 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from December 02-10, 2025, with tentative allotment on December 15, 2025. Both regular and direct plan variants will be available under the equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: December 02, 2025 to December 10, 2025
- Two scheme variants: Regular and Direct Plan (both equity category)
- Tentative allotment date: December 15, 2025
- Extended trading hours available till midnight on December 10, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a new product launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 12, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt by December 12, 2025 at 6:00 PM
- Members must ensure client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received on or before allotment date
- Switch transactions with funds received after allotment date will not be considered by AMC/RTA

## Important Dates

- **December 02, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 10, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **December 12, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **December 15, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1 day, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (post-transaction)
- **T+5 or last payout day, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for pending transactions (whichever is earlier)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine NFO launch announcement. The impact is limited to:

- **Members/Distributors**: Need to familiarize themselves with NFO timelines and operational requirements for client transactions
- **Investors**: New investment opportunity in dividend-focused equity ETF based on BSE 500 index
- **Operational Impact**: Standard NFO processing requirements with specific fund settlement deadlines
- **Market Impact**: Negligible - routine mutual fund product launch with no effect on broader market operations or existing securities
