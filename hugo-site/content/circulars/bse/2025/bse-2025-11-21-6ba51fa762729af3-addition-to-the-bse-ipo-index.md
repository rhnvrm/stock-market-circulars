## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Capillary Technologies India Ltd (Exchange ticker: 544614) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on Friday, November 21, 2025, and will be included in the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Capillary Technologies India Ltd (ticker: 544614) listed on BSE on November 21, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective November 25, 2025 at market open
- This is in reference to BSE Notice No: 20251120-42
- The action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index inclusion action following an IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Listing date for Capillary Technologies India Ltd on BSE
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective date for inclusion in BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine index inclusion following an IPO. The impact is limited to:
- Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE IPO Index will need to purchase the stock
- Potential minor increase in trading volume and liquidity for the stock
- No broader market-wide implications

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Market participants tracking or trading based on the BSE IPO Index should update their systems to reflect the new constituent.
