## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of Rajasthan Gases Ltd. This is a corporate disclosure relating to administrative changes in company identification.

## Key Points

- Company: Rajasthan Gases Ltd
- Action: Change in company name
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Notification Date: December 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of corporate administrative action.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should update their records with the new company name once effective. The scrip code will remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact on trading operations. Company name changes are routine administrative actions that do not affect the underlying business operations or stock trading. The scrip code remains the same, ensuring continuity in trading systems.
