## Summary

METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the completion of its IPO. The filing dated December 11, 2025 shows the post-issue shareholding structure with Promoter & Promoter Group holding 5,061,885 shares and Public Shareholders holding 13,791,516 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total of 2,060 shareholders (4 Promoter & Promoter Group + 2,056 Public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holds 5,061,885 fully paid-up equity shares
- Public shareholders hold 13,791,516 fully paid-up equity shares
- Locked-in shares exist for Promoter & Promoter Group and Public shareholders
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No depository receipts issued
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners
- All shares held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure requirement under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity required to file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) post-IPO
- Declaration submitted confirming absence of partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, and depository receipts
- Confirmation provided regarding locked-in shares and absence of encumbrances

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** December 11, 2025 (Post Issue of IPO)
- **Report Filed Under:** Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine post-IPO disclosure with no unusual elements or red flags.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard compliance filing for newly listed entity establishing baseline shareholding structure.

**Investor Impact:** Informational - Provides transparency on ownership structure post-IPO with confirmed locked-in provisions and clean shareholding (no pledges or encumbrances).

**Notable Observations:**
- Clean capital structure with no complex securities or encumbrances
- Presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with IPO lock-in requirements
- Broad public shareholding base with 2,056 public shareholders
