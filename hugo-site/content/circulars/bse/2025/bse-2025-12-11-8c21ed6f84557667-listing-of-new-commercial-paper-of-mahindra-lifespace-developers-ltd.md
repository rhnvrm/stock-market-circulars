## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd. on private placement basis. The listing includes 1500 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, totaling Rs. 75 crores. The securities are rated IND A1+ and will be traded in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd.
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (privately placed)
- **Quantity**: 1500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,721/- per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 75 crores (at face value)
- **Scrip Code**: 730745
- **Scrip ID**: MLDL101225
- **ISIN**: INE813A14391
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: YES BANK LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE813A14391
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- All trades must comply with debt market trading regulations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 6, 2026
- **Tenure**: 86 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term financing to Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd. The securities are available only to institutional and high net-worth investors given the Rs. 5 lakh denomination requirement.

**Operational Impact**: None for retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt platform.

**Liquidity**: Limited secondary market liquidity expected given the private placement nature and short 86-day tenure until redemption.

**Credit Quality**: The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and low credit risk for short-term instruments.
