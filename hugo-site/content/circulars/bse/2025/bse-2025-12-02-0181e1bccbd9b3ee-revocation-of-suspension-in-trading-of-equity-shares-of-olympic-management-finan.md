## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension of trading in equity shares of Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 511632). The company is listed on both BSE and CSE, with ISIN number INE091N01014. The company is engaged in management and financial services business and has paid listing fees to all applicable stock exchanges.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited
- Scrip Code: 511632
- ISIN: INE091N01014
- CIN: L65990MH1984PLC033825
- Registered Office: 42, 199 Floor, Gopal Bhavan, Princess Street, Mumbai - 400 002
- Listed on: BSE and CSE
- Status: Trading suspension revoked
- Business: Management and Financial Services
- Directors: Pawan Kr Agarwal (Chairman), Satyanarayan Ramchandra Agarwal (Whole-Time Director), Prafulla Rao Benitto, Laxmi Dhar Shankar Irke, Narsingh Bhola, Thomas Yangal Kumar Neethiraya Nadar
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Prashant Chandrakant Vipani
- Statutory Auditors: M/S K.R. Khandelwal & Co (appointed on 30.09.2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular represents a revocation of previously imposed trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has confirmed listing fees are paid to all applicable stock exchanges
- Company profile information submitted to BSE
- Auditor appointment completed as per Companies Act requirements (statutory auditor rotation)

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 2, 2025
- Auditor Appointment Date: September 30, 2025
- Trading resumption effective immediately upon revocation

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of trading suspension allows investors to resume buying and selling shares of Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited on BSE. This is positive for existing shareholders who were unable to exit their positions during the suspension period. The company appears to have met compliance requirements that led to the revocation. Market participants should note the company's financial services business nature and review updated disclosures before trading.
