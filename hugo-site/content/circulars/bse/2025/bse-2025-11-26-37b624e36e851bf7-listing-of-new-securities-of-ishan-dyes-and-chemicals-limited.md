## Summary

BSE has listed 51,72,270 new equity shares of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 531109) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade on the exchange from November 27, 2025, with a lock-in period until June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 51,72,270 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 53/-
- Issue price: Rs. 63/- per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE561M01018
- Distinctive Numbers: 20968148 to 26140417
- Date of Allotment: September 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification following preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 51,72,270 shares until June 15, 2026
- Shares must be tracked under distinctive numbers 20968148 to 26140417

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a company-specific listing event. The preferential allotment represents new capital raised by Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited from non-promoters. The lock-in period until June 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded freely until then, limiting immediate supply. Trading members can commence trading in these securities from November 27, 2025.
