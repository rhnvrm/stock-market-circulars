## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Limited (Scrip Code: 531033). The company is an NBFC engaged in providing Micro Enterprise Loans, SME Loans, Personal Loans and other structured business financing. The company is listed only on BSE with ISIN INE101E01010.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension has been revoked for Regal Entertainment & Consultants Limited
- Scrip Code: 531033
- ISIN: INE101E01010
- Company is an active NBFC providing micro-enterprise loans, SME loans, and personal loans
- Registered office located in Mumbai, Maharashtra
- CIN: L65923MH1992PLC064689
- Auditors: M/s. DBS & Associates Chartered Accountants, Mumbai
- No action taken by any other exchange in last five years
- Company is listed only on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The revocation of suspension restores trading liquidity for existing shareholders and allows market participants to trade the scrip. This is positive for shareholders who were unable to exit their positions during the suspension period.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Limited can now trade their holdings on BSE. The company's operational status is confirmed as active, and there are no pending actions from other exchanges.

**Compliance Impact:** The company has met the requirements necessary for revocation of suspension and can continue normal listing operations on BSE.
