## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis, effective November 11, 2025. The instrument consists of 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying an IND A1+ credit rating. The securities will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 730535, Scrip ID: NST101125, ISIN: INE0NDH14106
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,741.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ 
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NDH14106
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers are issued on private placement basis and will be traded only on the debt segment. The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. The listing provides short-term financing options for Nexus Select Trust with a redemption period of approximately 3 months. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is expected.
