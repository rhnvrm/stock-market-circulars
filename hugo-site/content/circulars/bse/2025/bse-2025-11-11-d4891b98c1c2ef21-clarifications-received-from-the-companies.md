## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from three companies - Gallantt Ispat Limited (Scrip Code: 532726), Indigo Paints Limited (Scrip Code: 543258), and National Aluminium Co. Ltd. - regarding significant increases in trading volume of their securities. All companies responded that they have no undisclosed price-sensitive information, are compliant with SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015, and attribute the volume increases to market-driven conditions.

## Key Points

- **Gallantt Ispat Limited** (Reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/669) confirmed all necessary disclosures made under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- **Indigo Paints Limited** (Reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/670) stated no impending announcement or corporate action pending
- All three companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligation and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Management of the companies stated they are not connected with the price/volume movement
- Volume increases attributed to market conditions and combination of various factors
- Companies committed to continue making timely disclosures as required

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance matter under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies to continue making applicable disclosures within stipulated time
- No additional compliance requirements imposed

## Important Dates

- **November 11, 2025**: Date of BSE surveillance queries
- **November 11, 2025**: Date of company responses (Gallantt Ispat and Indigo Paints)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are standard surveillance clarifications that confirm no undisclosed material information exists. The volume increases are attributed to normal market dynamics rather than company-specific developments.

**Operational Impact**: None. Companies are already compliant with disclosure requirements and no new actions are required.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency that recent trading activity is market-driven and not based on undisclosed corporate developments. Investors can be assured that all material information has been disclosed per regulatory requirements.
