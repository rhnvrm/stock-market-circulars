## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 7 companies effective December 29-30, 2025, due to upcoming redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 7 commercial paper securities suspended from trading
- 5 securities have no dealings from December 29, 2025
- 2 securities have no dealings from December 30, 2025
- Suspensions linked to record dates for redemption purposes
- Notice issued under Debt Segment Corporate Actions category

## Affected Securities

### No Dealings from December 29, 2025:
- **Dolat Algotech Limited** (DAL-30-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE966A14025, Code: 728539)
- **Muthoot Fincorp Limited** (MFL-14-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE549K14BU6, Code: 728611)
- **Navi Finserv Limited** (NFL-30-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE342T14DI8, Code: 729755)
- **Kalpataru Projects International Limited** (KPIL-30-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE220B14BM0, Code: 730403)
- **J.K. Cement Ltd** (JKCL-30-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE823G14AZ4, Code: 730469)

### No Dealings from December 30, 2025:
- **Navi Finserv Limited** (NFl-31-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE342T14DJ6, Code: 729775)
- **Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited** (NWFL-31-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE918K14CS5, Code: 730358)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular implementing existing procedures for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the listed commercial papers from their respective suspension dates
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction for affected securities
- Record date compliance for redemption processing

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Record Date (Batch 1)**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From (Batch 1)**: December 29, 2025
- **Record Date (Batch 2)**: December 30, 2025
- **No Dealings From (Batch 2)**: December 30, 2025
- **Redemption Purpose**: As per individual CP terms

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects specific debt market participants holding these commercial papers. Limited broader market impact as suspensions are instrument-specific and related to scheduled redemptions.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these commercial papers cannot trade them from the suspension dates but will receive redemption proceeds as scheduled.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to block trading in these 7 securities from their respective suspension dates to ensure compliance.
