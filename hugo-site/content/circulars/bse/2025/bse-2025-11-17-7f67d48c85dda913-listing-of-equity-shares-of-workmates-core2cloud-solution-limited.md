## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Workmates Core2Cloud Solution Limited. The circular provides details of securities being listed with distinctive numbers, lock-in periods, and demat status. A total of 9,495,200 fully paid-up equity shares are being listed with varying lock-in periods expiring between November 20, 2026 and November 20, 2028.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Workmates Core2Cloud Solution Limited
- Total securities listed: 9,495,200 fully paid-up equity shares
- All securities are in demat form
- Lock-in periods: Three different lock-in expiry dates (20/11/2026, 20/11/2027, and 20/11/2028)
- Securities range from distinctive numbers 1 to 10,010,000
- Multiple tranches with different lock-in durations based on shareholder categories

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed securities must comply with BSE listing regulations
- Lock-in provisions must be adhered to until respective expiry dates
- Shareholders subject to lock-in restrictions cannot transfer shares until lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- Lock-in expiry for first tranche: November 20, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for second tranche: November 20, 2027
- Lock-in expiry for third tranche: November 20, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine new listing announcement. The staggered lock-in periods (2026, 2027, and 2028) indicate promoter/pre-IPO investor holdings with standard restrictions. Investors should note the lock-in schedules when considering liquidity. No immediate impact on broader market operations.
