## Summary

BSE has released the schedule for Registrar & Transfer Agent visits for August 2025. The circular includes a comprehensive list of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd, one of the RTAs that will be visiting BSE premises during the month.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule published for August 2025
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd services over 50 listed companies
- Visit facilitates coordination between BSE and RTAs for investor services
- Companies span across various sectors including finance, manufacturing, and trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- RTAs to coordinate with BSE during scheduled visits
- Ensure proper documentation and service delivery for listed companies
- Maintain updated records for all serviced companies

## Important Dates

- Visit Month: August 2025
- Specific visit dates to be coordinated between BSE and individual RTAs

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative process. The visits help ensure smooth functioning of investor services and maintain proper coordination between the exchange and transfer agents. Companies listed under Adroit Corporate Services will benefit from enhanced service coordination during the visit period.
