## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Northern Arc Capital Ltd. effective October 29, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in the affected debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for Northern Arc Capital Ltd. debentures with ISIN INE850M07483 (Code: 975911)
- Debenture series: NACL-9.17%-13-11-25-PVT
- Record date set for October 29, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from October 29, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-745/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension implemented as per existing BSE procedures for debt securities approaching record dates for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 29, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Members must ensure their systems and operations reflect the trading suspension for ISIN INE850M07483

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: October 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 13, 2025 (as indicated by debenture series name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension affects a single ISIN and is temporary in nature, related to the scheduled redemption and interest payment process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment. Investors cannot trade these securities from October 29, 2025 onwards, effectively freezing positions until settlement.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this specific security. The suspension is standard practice for debt securities approaching maturity and should not cause operational disruptions.
