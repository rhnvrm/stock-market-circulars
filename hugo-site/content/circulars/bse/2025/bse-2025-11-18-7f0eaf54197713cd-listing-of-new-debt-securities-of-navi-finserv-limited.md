## Summary

BSE has listed three series of non-convertible debentures issued by Navi Finserv Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 18, 2025. The securities comprise 175,000 units of 10.30% debentures maturing September 2027, 75,000 units of 10.75% debentures maturing August 2028, and 5,000 units of 10.50% debentures maturing August 2027. All securities are CRISIL rated and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Three series of NCDs listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 18, 2025
- Series 1: 175,000 units at 10.30% p.a., face value Rs. 10,000, maturing September 30, 2027 (ISIN: INE342T07635)
- Series 2: 75,000 units at 10.75% p.a., face value Rs. 10,000, maturing August 19, 2028 (ISIN: INE342T07601)
- Series 3: 5,000 units at 10.50% p.a., face value Rs. 100,000, maturing August 27, 2027 (ISIN: INE342T07536)
- All securities allotted on November 14, 2025 (except Series 3 allotted November 14, 2024)
- Monthly interest payment frequency for all series
- Credit rating: CRISIL A/STABLE for Series 1 and 2, CRISIL A for Series 3
- No put/call options available
- Market lot size: 1 unit for all series
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 18, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Series 1 & 2):** November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Series 3):** November 14, 2024
- **Redemption Date (Series 1):** September 30, 2027
- **Redemption Date (Series 2):** August 19, 2028
- **Redemption Date (Series 3):** August 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities that adds liquidity to the debt market segment but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these NCDs through BSE's debt platform.

**Investor Impact:** Provides institutional and qualified investors with additional fixed-income investment options offering yields between 10.30% to 10.75% per annum with monthly interest payments and tenures ranging from approximately 21 to 33 months.
