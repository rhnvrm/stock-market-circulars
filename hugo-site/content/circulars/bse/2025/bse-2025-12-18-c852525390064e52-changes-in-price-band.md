## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 19 equity scrips effective December 19, 2025. The revised bands range from 2% (tightest) to 20% (widest), reflecting different levels of surveillance measures. Trading members have been notified to ensure compliance with the new price movement limits.

## Key Points

- 19 scrips will have revised price bands effective December 19, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 20% depending on the scrip
- Tightest bands (2%): S.A.L. Steel Ltd and TVS Electronics Ltd
- Widest bands (20%): Purple Wave Infocom Ltd and SKF India (Industrial) Ltd
- Most scrips (8 out of 19) have 10% price bands
- 5 scrips have 5% bands, and 3 have 10% bands
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This represents operational implementation of existing surveillance measures through price band adjustments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands from December 19, 2025
- All trading systems must be updated to reflect new price movement limits
- Members should monitor orders and trades to ensure compliance with revised bands
- Any queries should be directed to BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revised price bands will restrict intraday price movements for these 19 scrips, potentially reducing volatility and liquidity. Tighter bands (2%-5%) suggest enhanced surveillance concerns.

**Trading Impact**: Traders and investors in these scrips will face restricted price movements, with circuits triggering at lower thresholds. High-frequency and day traders may find reduced opportunities.

**Scrip-Specific Impact**: 
- Very tight bands (2%): S.A.L. Steel and TVS Electronics face severe trading restrictions
- Moderate bands (5%): 5 scrips including Ambo Agritec, Bhatia Colour Chem, Epsom Properties, Omni AXs Software, and Pan Electronics
- Standard bands (10%): 8 scrips indicating moderate surveillance
- Relaxed bands (20%): Purple Wave Infocom and SKF India (Industrial) show easing of previous restrictions
