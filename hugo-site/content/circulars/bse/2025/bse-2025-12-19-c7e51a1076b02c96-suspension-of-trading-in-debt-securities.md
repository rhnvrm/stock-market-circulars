## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Solar Industries India Limited effective December 22, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debentures (SIIL-8.20%-23-12-25-PVT) on December 23, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture: Solar Industries India Limited 8.20% Private Placement
- ISIN: INE343H08016
- Scrip Code: 974468
- Maturity Date: December 23, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: December 22, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-782/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from December 22, 2025
- No new trades should be executed in ISIN INE343H08016 after the suspension date
- Members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 23, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and standard for maturing debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the suspended security. The maturity is on schedule with no disruptions indicated.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debenture holders who will receive maturity proceeds as scheduled. No impact on equity shareholders of Solar Industries India Limited.
