## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 11, 2025. The issue consists of 5,000 units with CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Quantity: 5,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,60,887.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730534
- Scrip ID: MFL101125
- ISIN: INE549K14CE8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Ltd
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CE8
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Muthoot Fincorp Limited with short-term financing through commercial paper with approximately 12-month tenure. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates strong credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect broader equity markets or trading operations.
