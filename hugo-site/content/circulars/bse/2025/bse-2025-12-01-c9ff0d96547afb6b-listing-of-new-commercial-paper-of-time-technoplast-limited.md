## Summary

Time Technoplast Limited has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 25 crore (500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective 01 December 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with a tenure of approximately 114 days, maturing on 25 March 2026. The instruments carry dual ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore (500 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 488,555.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Scrip Code: 730317
- Scrip ID: TTL26925
- ISIN: INE508G14HZ6 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE508G14HZ6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 01 December 2025
- **Listing Date**: 01 December 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 25 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper has strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating high credit quality. The listing provides Time Technoplast Limited with short-term funding while offering investors a highly-rated money market instrument. No significant impact on broader market operations or trading members' activities.
