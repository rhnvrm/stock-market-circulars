## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 500 units of commercial paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total issuance of Rs. 25 crore), rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with a tenure of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: IGH Holdings Private Limited
- **Securities**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore total)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,749 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 729003
- **Scrip ID**: IGHP17325
- **Detail Name**: IGHHPL-10-03-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE02FN14440 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions occur only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14440
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to IGH Holdings Private Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate high credit quality and short-term debt servicing capability. This listing does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
