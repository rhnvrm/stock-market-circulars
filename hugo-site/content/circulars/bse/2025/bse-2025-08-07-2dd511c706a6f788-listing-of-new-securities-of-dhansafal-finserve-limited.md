## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new equity shares of Dhansafal Finserve Limited. The listing involves multiple tranches of shares with different distinctive numbers and varying lock-in periods extending from March 2026 to March 2027.

## Key Points

- Total of 19,470,000 new equity shares being listed
- Shares distributed across 9 different tranches with specific distinctive number ranges
- Lock-in periods vary from March 30, 2026 to March 31, 2027
- Largest tranche consists of 7,730,000 shares (distinctive numbers 195170001 to 202900000)

## Share Distribution Details

- **Tranche 1**: 7,730,000 shares (195170001-202900000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2027
- **Tranche 2**: 2,500,000 shares (202900001-205400000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026
- **Tranche 3**: 2,300,000 shares (205400001-205980000) - Lock-in until March 30, 2026
- **Tranche 4**: 2,300,000 shares (205980001-208280000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026
- **Tranche 5**: 1,160,000 shares (208280001-210580000) - Lock-in until March 30, 2026
- **Tranche 6**: 1,160,000 shares (210580001-211740000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026
- **Tranche 7**: 580,000 shares (211740001-212900000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026
- **Tranche 8**: 1,160,000 shares (212900001-213480000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026
- **Tranche 9**: 580,000 shares (213480001-214640000) - Lock-in until March 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- Lock-in expiry dates range from March 30, 2026 to March 31, 2027
- Different tranches have different lock-in periods

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The staggered lock-in periods indicate a structured release of shares to the market over time, which may affect liquidity and trading patterns for Dhansafal Finserve Limited shares.
