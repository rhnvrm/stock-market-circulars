## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in ILFS debt securities (SCRIP CODE: 952658, ISIN: INE871D07OI1) due to maturity on August 24, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- ILFS 8.69% private debentures will mature on August 24, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from August 21, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-699/2025-2026
- SCRIP CODE: 952658, ISIN: INE871D07OI1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified ILFS debentures from August 21, 2025
- All trading members required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **August 24, 2025**: Maturity date of ILFS debentures
- **August 20, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension for maturing debt securities. Only affects holders and potential traders of the specific ILFS 8.69% debentures. Standard corporate action with no broader market implications.
