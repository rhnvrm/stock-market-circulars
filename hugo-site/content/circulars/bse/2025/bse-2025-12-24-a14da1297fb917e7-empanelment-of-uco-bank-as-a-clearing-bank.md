## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has empaneled UCO Bank as a designated Clearing Bank for settlement of funds obligations of members across various ICCL segments. This addition expands the available clearing bank options for ICCL members.

## Key Points

- UCO Bank empaneled as designated Clearing Bank of ICCL
- Bank will handle settlement of funds obligations for members in various ICCL segments
- Complete list of ICCL empaneled Clearing Banks available at https://www.icclindia.com/Static/membership/List_of_Clearing_Banks.aspx
- Notice Number: 20251224-34
- Category: Settlement/RMS, General segment

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Low - Members now have an additional clearing bank option for settlement of funds obligations.

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine operational enhancement that provides members with more flexibility in choosing clearing banks.
