## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on a private placement basis. The instruments comprise 6,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 300 crore, and are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. The securities carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 300 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,431.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730531
- Scrip ID: PFL101125
- ISIN: INE511C14ZA4
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE511C14ZA4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Tenor**: 85 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The listing provides Poonawalla Fincorp with working capital financing through the commercial paper route.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net worth individuals participating in the debt market, given the minimum trading denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs. The dual A1+ ratings from CARE and CRISIL indicate high credit quality and safety of principal.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes for general market participants. Only relevant for debt market traders dealing with commercial paper instruments.
