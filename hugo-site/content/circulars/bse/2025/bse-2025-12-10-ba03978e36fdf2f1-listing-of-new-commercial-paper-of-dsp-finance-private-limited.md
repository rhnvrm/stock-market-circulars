## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by DSP Finance Private Limited on a private placement basis. A total of 4,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (aggregate value of Rs. 200 crore) have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The instrument carries an ICRA A1+ credit rating and has a tenure of 91 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: DSP Finance Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 4,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730729
- **Scrip ID**: DFP091225
- **ISIN**: INE422H14156
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE422H14156
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: March 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to DSP Finance Private Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and strong repayment capacity. The issuance is part of normal money market operations and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
