## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 30 crore issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each (600 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,868.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730147, Scrip ID: BFSL3925
- ISIN: INE01C314CN4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Start Date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with 24-day maturity. The commercial paper provides additional short-term funding option for institutional investors in BSE's debt segment.
