## Summary

Promoter Shri Madhav Jayesh Valia proposes to sell up to 7,38,711 equity shares (face value Rs. 10 each) of East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 523874), representing 5% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital. The Offer for Sale (OFS) will be conducted through BSE's Stock Exchange Mechanism on December 29, 2025 (T Day - non-retail investors only) and December 30, 2025 (T+1 Day - retail and non-retail investors). The primary objective is achieving minimum public shareholding as prescribed under Rule 9(2)(b) of Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 and Regulation 38 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Seller: Shri Madhav Jayesh Valia (Promoter)
- Offer Size: 7,38,711 equity shares (5% of total capital)
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per equity share
- Company: East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Ltd (BSE Scrip Code: 523874)
- ISIN: IN191C01023
- Stock Exchange: BSE Limited
- Clearing Corporation: Indian Clearing Corporation Limited
- Regulatory Framework: SEBI OFS Circular SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024-00181 dated December 30, 2024
- BSE Operational Guidelines: Notice 20240701-19 dated July 1, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is conducted in compliance with existing regulations:

- SEBI Master Circular bearing no. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/ dated November 1, 2024
- Rule 9(2)(b) of Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957
- Regulation 38 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- OFS mechanism as per paragraph 19.1.5.2 of Chapter I of Master Circular for Stock Exchanges

## Compliance Requirements

- Seller's Broker and prospective investors must read the Notice in entirety along with OFS Guidelines before participating
- Separate bidding window to be provided by BSE for the Offer
- Non-retail investors can bid on T Day and carry forward unallotted bids to T+1 Day
- Retail investors can bid on T+1 Day only
- Compliance with minimum public shareholding norms is the stated objective

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **T Day (December 29, 2025)**: Bidding for non-retail investors only
- **T+1 Day (December 30, 2025)**: Bidding for retail investors and non-retail investors (for carry-forward unallotted bids)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The sale of 5% equity stake will increase public float and improve liquidity in East India Drums & Barrels shares. The two-tier bidding structure allows price discovery across investor categories.

**Regulatory Impact**: Positive - Helps the company achieve minimum public shareholding compliance as mandated by SEBI, avoiding potential regulatory action or delisting risks.

**Investor Impact**: The OFS provides opportunity for retail and institutional investors to acquire shares through a transparent Stock Exchange Mechanism. Non-retail investors get first access on T Day with option to carry forward unallotted bids.

**Promoter Impact**: Reduces promoter holding while maintaining compliance with listing requirements. The structured mechanism ensures fair price discovery and orderly execution.
