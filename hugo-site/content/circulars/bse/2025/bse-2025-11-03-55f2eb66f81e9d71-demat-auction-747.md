## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 747 dated November 3, 2025. The auction covers 118 scrips across various sectors where delivery obligations remain pending. Quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 8,159 shares depending on the scrip. This is a standard settlement mechanism used by the exchange to fulfill pending delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 747
- Date: November 3, 2025
- Total scrips involved: 118 companies
- Quantities vary significantly across scrips (1 to 8,159 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, technology, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, and energy
- Notable companies include Reliance Industries (100 shares), Axis Bank (379 shares), HCL Technologies (5 shares), and Yes Bank (281 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for settlement auction process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with pending delivery obligations must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanism
- Clients and brokers should ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 3, 2025
- Auction Settlement Number: 747

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 118 scrips with pending delivery obligations. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of trades where delivery has failed.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers with shortages in these scrips will need to purchase shares through the auction mechanism, potentially at premium prices. Clients with pending delivery obligations may face additional costs.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary price impact possible in scrips with larger auction quantities, particularly Fineotex Chemicals (8,159 shares), Wardwizard Innovations (2,994 shares), and Srigee DLM (2,400 shares).
