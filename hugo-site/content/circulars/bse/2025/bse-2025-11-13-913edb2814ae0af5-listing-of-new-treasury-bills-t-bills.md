## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category effective November 14, 2025. The T-Bills have tenures of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with maturity dates in 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be listed on BSE Debt segment from November 14, 2025
- 91-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805142, ISIN: IN002025X331) matures February 12, 2026
- 182-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805143, ISIN: IN002025Y339) matures May 14, 2026
- 364-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805144, ISIN: IN002025Z336) matures November 12, 2026
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Securities classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- Cutoff calculation: Maturity Date (T) minus two Trading Days (excluding Bank Holidays)
- Trading members should contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **91TB120226 Maturity**: February 12, 2026 (trading stops February 10, 2026)
- **182TB14526 Maturity**: May 14, 2026 (trading stops May 12, 2026)
- **364T121126 Maturity**: November 12, 2026 (trading stops November 10, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of government treasury bills that provides additional short-term investment options for debt market participants. The listing expands the available universe of government securities on BSE's debt platform with standard tenor T-Bills. No impact on equity markets or existing trading members' operations beyond awareness of the new instruments.
