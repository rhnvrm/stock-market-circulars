## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED (CIN: L46498GJ2011PLC064964). The company name has been officially changed to VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED effective November 27, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Old name: VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED
- New name: VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L46498GJ2011PLC064964
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Originally incorporated as: VEERAM T.V. NETWORK PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sabari Raj, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a name change filing in compliance with the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED) and new name (VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest company status on the MCA portal at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **November 27, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change with no impact on business operations, financial position, or trading activities.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update its name on all official documents, signage, and communications. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal obligations continue under the new name.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights, liabilities, or ownership structure. The change is purely nomenclature-based.
