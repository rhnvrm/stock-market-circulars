## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis. The listing includes 3000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 3000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,741.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730535
- Scrip ID: NST101125
- ISIN Number: INE0NDH14106
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE0NDH14106
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at specified numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 10, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low impact notification affecting only BSE Debt segment participants. This is a standard commercial paper listing with no broader market implications. The instrument provides short-term funding avenue for Nexus Select Trust with a 87-day maturity period. Trading is restricted to institutional participants dealing in debt securities with minimum investment of Rs. 5 Lakhs.
