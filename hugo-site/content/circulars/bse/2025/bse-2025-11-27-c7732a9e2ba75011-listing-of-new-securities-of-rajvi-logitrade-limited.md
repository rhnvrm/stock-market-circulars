## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 30,74,100 new equity shares of Rajvi Logitrade Limited (Scrip Code: 511185) effective November 28, 2025. These shares were issued at par value to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- 30,74,100 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Issued to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion
- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 28, 2025
- ISIN: INE381N01019
- Distribution numbers: 1000001 to 4074100
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for Rajvi Logitrade Limited
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the specified schedule
- Shares cannot be traded freely until lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 29, 2025
- **Trading Start Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (15,94,775 shares):** June 29, 2027 (Distribution Nos. 1000001-2594775)
- **Lock-in Expiry (14,79,325 shares):** May 29, 2026 (Distribution Nos. 2594776-4074100)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The lock-in restrictions limit immediate market supply.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard listing process for trading members. The shares are subject to lock-in periods preventing immediate free trading.

**Investor Impact:** Company-specific impact only. Existing shareholders should note the dilution from the preferential allotment. New shares represent capital raised through warrant conversion at par value of Rs. 10/-.
