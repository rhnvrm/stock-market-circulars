## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Stanbik Agro Limited for its SME IPO, filed on September 5, 2025. The document is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed on September 5, 2025
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period from notice date
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: September 5, 2025
- Notice date: September 8, 2025
- Comment deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO process. Impact is limited to potential investors and market participants interested in Stanbik Agro Limited. The public comment period is a regulatory requirement that allows stakeholders to provide feedback before the final offer document approval.
