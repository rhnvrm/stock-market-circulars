## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Samtel India Ltd (Scrip Code: 500371). Trading in the company's equity shares has been resumed on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Suspension in trading of Samtel India Ltd equity shares has been revoked
- Scrip Code: 500371
- Trading has been resumed on BSE
- This action restores normal trading activity for the stock

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension that was previously imposed on Samtel India Ltd equity shares has been lifted, allowing normal trading operations to resume.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned. Normal trading rules and regulations apply.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: December 16, 2025
- Trading resumed from this date

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of suspension has a significant positive impact on shareholders and market participants who can now trade the stock freely. This restores liquidity and market access for Samtel India Ltd equity shares.
