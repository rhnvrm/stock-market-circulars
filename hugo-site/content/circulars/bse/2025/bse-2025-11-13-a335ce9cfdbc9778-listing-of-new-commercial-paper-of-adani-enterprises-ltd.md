## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis with effect from November 13, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crores), with redemption scheduled for March 25, 2026. The securities carry ACUITE A1+ and CARE A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25,00,00,000 (Rs. 25 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729140
- Scrip ID: AEL28325
- ISIN: INE423A14WX5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,87,010 per unit
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A1+, CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14WX5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 25, 2026
- **Tenure:** Approximately 132 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis with limited market-wide impact.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard debt instrument listing. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.

**Investor Impact:** Low - Relevant only to institutional investors participating in debt markets and commercial paper instruments. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
