## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted 5500 new Commercial Papers issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 7, 2025. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5500 Commercial Papers of Rs.5,00,000 face value each
- Scrip Code: 730506, Scrip ID: IHF061125
- ISIN Number: INE071G14HE0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs.492,627 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialised form only in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE071G14HE0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- Contact debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 5, 2026
- **Notice Date:** November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt instruments (90-day tenure) for institutional investors. The listing expands available debt investment options but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Only relevant to debt segment participants dealing with ICICI Home Finance Company Limited instruments.
