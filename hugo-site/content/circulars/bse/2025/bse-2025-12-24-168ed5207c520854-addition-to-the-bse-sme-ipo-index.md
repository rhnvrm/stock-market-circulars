## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544665) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, December 26, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Wednesday, December 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (ticker: 544665) listed on BSE on December 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on December 26, 2025
- Addition effective at market open on December 26, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to Notice No. 20251223-35

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index inclusion following listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should note the inclusion for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Listing of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited on BSE
- **December 26, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index (effective at open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition for a newly listed SME company. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust holdings accordingly. The action has been communicated through standard corporate action files to facilitate smooth processing by market participants.
