## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and admission for trading of 1,997,235 additional equity shares issued by 6 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). The securities will be available for trading from Thursday, November 13, 2025. None of the listed shares are subject to lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- 6 companies have issued shares under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 1,997,235 shares to be listed
- Effective trading date: November 13, 2025
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the shares
- All shares are in the equity segment
