## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and permission to trade 198 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, December 23, 2025. The shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and are under ISIN INE090A01021.

## Key Points

- Total of 198 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each kept in abeyance now listed
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Scrip Code: 532174
- Shares divided into 5 distinct blocks with specific distinctive numbers
- Block 1: 60 shares (Distinctive No. 6412627299 to 6412627358)
- Block 2: 100 shares (Distinctive No. 6412492908 to 6412493007)
- Block 3: 10 shares (Distinctive No. 6412647190 to 6412647199)
- Block 4: 15 shares (Distinctive No. 6412611781 to 6412611795)
- Block 5: 13 shares (Distinctive No. 6412503183 to 6412503195)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities for trading purposes effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: Tuesday, December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of bonus shares previously kept in abeyance. The quantity of 198 shares is negligible compared to ICICI Bank's total outstanding equity capital, resulting in minimal to no impact on the stock's trading or market capitalization. The listing resolves pending corporate action items and provides liquidity to shareholders entitled to these abeyance shares.
