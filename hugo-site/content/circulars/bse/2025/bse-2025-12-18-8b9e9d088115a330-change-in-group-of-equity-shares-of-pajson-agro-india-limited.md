## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544657), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective January 02, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's Notice No. 20251217-55 dated December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, January 02, 2025
- Contact Person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822
- Notice Number: 20251218-2
- Reference Notice: 20251217-55 dated December 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for intraday trading and standard T+1 settlement cycle instead of mandatory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment change effective January 02, 2025
- All future trades in PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED must be executed under M Group classification
- Trading Members requiring clarification should contact Mr. Anurag Jain at BSE

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date:** January 02, 2025 (Friday)
- **Previous Notice Date:** December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This migration from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment represents a positive development for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED shareholders and traders. The shift enables intraday trading, improved liquidity, and standard settlement cycles. Investors will gain greater flexibility in trading the stock, while the move from MT Group to M Group indicates successful completion of the initial stabilization period post-SME IPO listing. This is a routine progression for SME IPO listings that meet BSE's criteria for normal segment trading.
