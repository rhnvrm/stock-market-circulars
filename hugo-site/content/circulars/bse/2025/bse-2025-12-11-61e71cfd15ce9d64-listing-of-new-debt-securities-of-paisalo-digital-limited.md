## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 11, 2025. The securities consist of 4,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an 8.5% annual interest rate and maturing on December 9, 2028. The debentures are rated IVR AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977358 (Scrip ID: 85PDL28)
- **ISIN**: INE420C07163
- **Quantity**: 4,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Interest Rate**: 8.5% per annum (paid quarterly)
- **Credit Rating**: IVR AA/Stable
- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 9, 2028
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C07163
- Members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: March 9, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly (from March 9, 2026 to December 9, 2028)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 9, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing affects only investors in Paisalo Digital Limited's debt instruments and provides an additional trading avenue for privately placed debentures. The IVR AA/Stable rating indicates good credit quality. The listing enhances liquidity for existing debenture holders but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
