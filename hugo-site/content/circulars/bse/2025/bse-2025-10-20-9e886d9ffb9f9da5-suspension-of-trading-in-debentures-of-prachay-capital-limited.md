## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE0IID07579, Code: 976842) of PRACHAY CAPITAL LIMITED effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for payment of interest and redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: PCL-15%-8-5-29-UL-PVT (INE0IID07579)
- BSE scrip code: 976842
- Record date: October 24, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- All market participants must note the suspension to avoid inadvertent trading attempts
- Compliance with record date requirements for determining eligible holders for interest payment and redemption

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **October 24, 2025**: Record date and commencement of trading suspension

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects a single privately placed debenture series of PRACHAY CAPITAL LIMITED. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to determining eligible holders for corporate action benefits.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading will be possible from October 24, 2025 onwards in this specific series.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trading in scrip code 976842 from the specified date.
