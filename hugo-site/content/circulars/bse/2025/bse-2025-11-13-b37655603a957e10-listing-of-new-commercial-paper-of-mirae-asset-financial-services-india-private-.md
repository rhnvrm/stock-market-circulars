## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on a private placement basis. The instrument consists of 1,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crores), issued at a discount with CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crores (1,000 securities Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,423.50 per security (issued at discount)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Scrip Code**: 730549
- **Scrip ID**: MAFS121125
- **ISIN**: INE0JRU14339
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0JRU14339
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instrument on private placement basis with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity traders. Only relevant for debt segment participants interested in high-rated commercial paper with 3-month maturity.

**Investor Relevance**: Limited to institutional investors and debt market participants. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and low credit risk for short-term obligations.
