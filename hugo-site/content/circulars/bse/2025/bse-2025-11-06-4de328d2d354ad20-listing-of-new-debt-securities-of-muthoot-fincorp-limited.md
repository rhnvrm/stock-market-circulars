## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis, effective November 6, 2025. The first security (ISIN: INE549K07HA9) is a 9.15% bond maturing on October 29, 2028, with 10,000 bonds of face value Rs. 1,00,000 each. The second security (ISIN: INE549K07HC5) is a 9% bond maturing on February 4, 2028, with 7,500 bonds of face value Rs. 1,00,000 each. Both securities carry CRISIL AA-/Positive rating.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- First series: 10,000 bonds at 9.15% p.a., maturing October 29, 2028 (Scrip Code: 977252)
- Second series: 7,500 bonds at 9% p.a., maturing February 4, 2028 (Scrip Code: 977273)
- Both securities rated CRISIL AA-/Positive
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 1,00,000 per bond for both series
- Allotment date: November 4, 2025
- Interest payment: Annually for both securities
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Market lot: 1 bond, Tick size: 1 paise

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The private placement debt securities are institutional in nature with high face values (Rs. 1 lakh per bond), indicating limited retail participation. The CRISIL AA-/Positive rating reflects strong credit quality. The listing provides additional funding avenue for Muthoot Fincorp Limited and offers investment options for debt market participants.
