## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on its Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The issue consists of 1500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each on a private placement basis, with a tenure of approximately 89 days maturing on March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Quantity: 1500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,814 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 75 crore (approx.)
- Scrip Code: 730741
- Scrip ID: DCL101225
- ISIN: INE755K14FR6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FR6
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 11, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issuance is limited to institutional investors through private placement. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong short-term creditworthiness of Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited. Trading is restricted to the debt segment with standard compliance requirements for dematerialized trading.
