## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis in the debt segment effective November 12, 2025. The instrument consists of 500 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Security type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 500 units
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,64,684 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip code: 730536
- Scrip ID: AEL111125
- Detail name: AEL-11-11-26-CP
- ISIN: INE423A14YN2
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YN2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice date: November 12, 2025
- Listing effective date: November 12, 2025
- Date of allotment: November 11, 2025
- Date of redemption: November 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to Adani Enterprises Ltd with a one-year maturity period. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate high creditworthiness. The listing adds to the debt market liquidity but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Only relevant to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers.
