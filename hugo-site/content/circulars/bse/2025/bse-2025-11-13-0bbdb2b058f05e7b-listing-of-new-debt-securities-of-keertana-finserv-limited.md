## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Keertana Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective November 13, 2025. The securities comprise 90,000 units with 11.20% annual interest rate, maturing on April 3, 2027. The securities are rated IND BBB+/Stable and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 90,000 units listed
- Scrip Code: 976943, Scrip ID: 1120KFPL27
- ISIN: INE0NES07246 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,340.60
- Interest Rate: 11.20% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07246
- Members must use the prescribed tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's website for complete details
- Members requiring clarifications may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 3, 2025
- Interest Payment Frequency: Quarterly (from December 3, 2025 to April 3, 2027)
- Redemption Date: April 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities expand the debt segment offerings but are relevant primarily to institutional investors and debt market participants. The BBB+ stable rating indicates moderate credit quality. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Standard administrative circular for debt segment participants.
