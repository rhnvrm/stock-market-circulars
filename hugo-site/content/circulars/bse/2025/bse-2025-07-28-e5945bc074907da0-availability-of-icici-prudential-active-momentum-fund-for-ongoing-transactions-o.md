## Summary

ICICI Prudential Active Momentum Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective July 29, 2025. This follows previous notice 20250707-21 and affects both regular and direct plan growth options of the equity fund.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions after previous closure
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from July 29, 2025
- Includes subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP facilities
- Covers both regular and direct plan growth options
- Equity category fund with ISINs INF109K1A377 and INF109K1A385

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a platform availability update.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the availability of the fund for client transactions from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine fund availability announcement. Provides additional investment options for BSE StAR MF Platform users in the equity fund category.
