## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited on private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The issue comprises 1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, totaling Rs. 75 crore, with ICICI Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 730697
- Scrip ID: NWML31225
- ISIN: INE531F14FL4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,061.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE531F14FL4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 5, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 4, 2025
- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt securities. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate low credit risk. The 88-day tenor commercial paper provides short-term funding to Nuvama Wealth Management Limited with no significant implications for broader market operations.
