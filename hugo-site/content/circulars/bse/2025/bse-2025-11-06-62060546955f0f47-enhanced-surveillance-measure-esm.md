## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Framework effective November 7, 2025. Continental Controls Ltd (Scrip Code: 531460, ISIN: INE545B01022) has been newly shortlisted and placed under ESM Stage I. No securities are being moved to higher or lower ESM stages, and no securities are exiting the ESM framework in this update.

## Key Points

- Continental Controls Ltd added to ESM Stage I effective November 7, 2025
- Scrip Code: 531460, ISIN: INE545B01022
- No securities moved to higher ESM stages
- No securities moved to lower ESM stages
- No securities removed from ESM framework
- Consolidated list shows 30+ securities under various ESM stages
- ESM framework includes securities from AAA Technologies Ltd to Aryaman Capital and beyond

## Regulatory Changes

The Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) is a market surveillance mechanism to monitor securities with abnormal price movements or other concerns. Securities under ESM may face:
- Additional surveillance and monitoring
- Trading restrictions including price bands
- Increased margin requirements
- Potential move to Trade-for-Trade segment

Securities can progress through multiple ESM stages based on continued surveillance concerns.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must be aware of ESM status when trading Continental Controls Ltd
- Brokers should inform clients about ESM securities and associated restrictions
- Additional due diligence required for transactions in ESM securities
- Traders must comply with any additional margin or trading requirements imposed on ESM securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 7, 2025 - Continental Controls Ltd enters ESM Stage I

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The addition of Continental Controls Ltd to ESM Stage I indicates BSE has identified surveillance concerns with the security. This typically results from unusual price movements, volatility, or other market integrity concerns.

**Trading Impact**: Securities under ESM face enhanced monitoring and may have restricted trading conditions, which can impact liquidity and investor participation. Continental Controls Ltd investors should expect potential trading restrictions and increased scrutiny.

**Investor Impact**: Existing and prospective investors in Continental Controls Ltd should exercise heightened caution and conduct thorough due diligence before trading. The ESM designation serves as a warning signal for potential risks associated with the security.
