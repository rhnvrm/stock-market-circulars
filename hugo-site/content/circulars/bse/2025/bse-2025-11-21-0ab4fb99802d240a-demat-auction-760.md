## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 760 dated November 21, 2025. The document lists 91 securities across various scrip codes with their respective quantities to be auctioned due to settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 760
- Settlement Date: November 21, 2025
- Total securities listed: 91 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 25,500 shares per scrip
- Highest quantity: South Indian Bank (25,500 shares)
- Other significant quantities: ZEE Learn (16,259), Sumuka Agro (6,192), Danube Industries (5,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular. This is a routine settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding settlement obligations must participate in the auction process
- Securities listed are subject to auction to fulfill settlement obligations under BSE regulations
- Members must ensure timely delivery/payment as per settlement requirements

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 21, 2025
- Settlement No: 760

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 91 securities with varying quantities. The auction could cause temporary price movements in affected stocks, particularly those with larger quantities like South Indian Bank, ZEE Learn, and Sumuka Agro.

**Operational Impact:** Routine settlement process affecting members with outstanding obligations. Members involved must arrange for funds or securities to settle their positions through the auction mechanism.

**Investor Impact:** Limited direct impact on retail investors. The auction is primarily a mechanism to ensure settlement discipline among trading members.
