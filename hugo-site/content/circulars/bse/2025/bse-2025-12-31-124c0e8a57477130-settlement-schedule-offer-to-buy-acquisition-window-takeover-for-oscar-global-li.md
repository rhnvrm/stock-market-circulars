## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED. The takeover window will be conducted from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026, with settlement number 2025138. The obligation download date is January 29, 2026, and the settlement date is January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025138
- Bid Start Date: January 2, 2026
- Bid End Date: January 15, 2026
- Obligation Download: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement no. 2025138 to be used in depository system
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts in CDSL & NSDL
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

Members and custodians are required to follow the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Participants must ensure that the effect of their early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirmation of bids
- Complete the process of early pay-in of securities in the Depository system sufficiently in advance before the prescribed timelines for closure of BSE OTB Bidding window
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement no. 2025138 in the depository system
- Members are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date**: January 2, 2026
- **Bid End Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Obligation Download**: January 29, 2026
- **Settlement Date**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts members and custodians participating in the takeover offer for Oscar Global Limited. The early pay-in mechanism ensures that securities are properly earmarked and blocked in client accounts before the bidding process. Members must coordinate with CDSL and NSDL to ensure timely completion of early pay-in procedures.
