## Summary

Housing & Urban Development Corporation Ltd. (HUDCO) has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment effective December 15, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 190,500 units, face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 6.98% p.a. with maturity on December 12, 2032.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 977370 (Scrip ID: 698HUDC32)
- **ISIN:** INE031A08AA2
- **Security Name:** HUDCL-6.98%-12-12-32-PVT
- **Quantity Listed:** 190,500 units (total value: Rs. 1,905 crore)
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Interest Rate:** 6.98% per annum (payable annually)
- **Credit Rating:** CARE AAA, ICRA AAA, IND AAA (triple-A rated)
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Tick Size:** 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option:** Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE031A08AA2
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Annually from December 12, 2026 to December 12, 2032
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** December 12, 2032

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing for institutional/qualified investors on private placement basis.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to debt market participants. The triple-A credit rating indicates high credit quality. The 6.98% coupon rate provides a fixed income opportunity for debt investors with a 7-year maturity period.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated with the new scrip code and ISIN for trading in dematerialized form only.
