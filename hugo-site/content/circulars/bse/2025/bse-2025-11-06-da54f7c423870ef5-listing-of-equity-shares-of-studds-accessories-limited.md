## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Studds Accessories Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544599, symbol STUDDS, and ISIN number INE00Q601028. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Studds Accessories Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544599
- Symbol: STUDDS
- ISIN: INE00Q601028
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Studds Accessories Limited and await further notice regarding the listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members will need to update their systems with the new scrip information once the listing date is confirmed. The announcement provides advance notice to prepare for trading in the new security.
