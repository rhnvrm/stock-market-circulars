## Summary

Walking Tree Technologies Private Limited has initiated an open offer to acquire up to 9,34,400 equity shares of Octaware Technologies Limited, representing 26.02% of the voting share capital. The offer price is set at ₹30 per fully paid-up equity share of face value ₹10 each, payable in cash. This offer is made pursuant to Regulation 3(1) and Regulation 4 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer**: Walking Tree Technologies Private Limited
- **Target Company**: Octaware Technologies Limited
- **Offer Size**: Up to 9,34,400 equity shares (26.02% of voting capital)
- **Offer Price**: ₹30 per equity share (face value ₹10)
- **Payment Mode**: Cash
- **Not conditional**: Offer is not subject to minimum acceptance level
- **Not a competing offer**: No competing offer exists as per SEBI (SAST) Regulations
- **No statutory approvals required**: As on date of Letter of Offer
- **Manager to the Offer**: Fintellectual Corporate Advisors Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INM000012944)
- **Registrar to the Offer**: Bigshare Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INR000001385)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a disclosure document under SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011. No new regulatory changes are introduced; the offer complies with existing takeover regulations requiring mandatory open offer upon substantial acquisition of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- **For Public Shareholders**: Review the Letter of Offer and decide whether to tender shares during the Tendering Period
- **For Recent Sellers**: Hand over the Letter of Offer and Form of Acceptance to the stock exchange member through whom sale was effected
- **For Acquirer**: Must inform about any upward revision in Offer Price/Size up to 1 working day prior to commencement of Tendering Period (by November 26, 2025) via public announcement
- **Price Revision**: Any revision in offer price applies to all shares validly tendered during the Tendering Period
- **Document Availability**: Public Announcement, Detailed Public Statement, Draft Letter of Offer and Letter of Offer must be available on SEBI website (www.sebi.gov.in)

## Important Dates

- **Last Date for Price/Size Revision**: Wednesday, November 26, 2025 (1 working day prior to commencement of Tendering Period)
- **Tendering Period**: To be announced (subsequent to November 27, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This takeover offer affects 26.02% of Octaware Technologies Limited's voting capital, representing a significant stake acquisition. Shareholders have the opportunity to exit at ₹30 per share.

**Shareholder Impact**: High - Public shareholders must evaluate whether the offer price of ₹30 per share (3x face value) represents fair value for their holdings. The unconditional nature of the offer (no minimum acceptance threshold) provides certainty of completion.

**Corporate Control**: Significant change in shareholding pattern expected, potentially leading to change in management control or strategic direction of Octaware Technologies Limited.

**Liquidity**: Provides exit opportunity for shareholders who wish to divest their holdings at the stated price during the Tendering Period.
