## Summary

BSE has listed 221,223 equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511644) for trading with effect from November 26, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 128/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 118/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd.
- **Scrip Code**: 511644
- **ISIN**: INE113B01029
- **Number of Shares**: 221,223 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 128/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 118/- premium)
- **Nature of Issue**: Preferential allotment to promoters pursuant to warrant conversion
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 2276277 to 2497499
- **Date of Allotment**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 26, 2025
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these securities for trading operations
- The newly listed shares are available for trading from November 26, 2025
- All shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions as specified

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: June 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a preferential allotment to promoters with complete lock-in until June 2027, meaning these shares cannot be traded during this period. The listing is procedural and does not affect immediate market liquidity or trading volumes.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and distinctive number ranges. The pari-passu ranking ensures these shares have identical rights as existing equity shares.
