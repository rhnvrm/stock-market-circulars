## Summary

BSE has notified that BAID FINSERV LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511724) has fixed November 17, 2025 as the record date for its rights issue. The company is offering 1 equity share of Rs. 2/- face value for every 4 equity shares held, at a premium of Rs. 8/- per share (total issue price of Rs. 10/- per share). Trading in the company's equity shares will be on ex-rights basis from November 17, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-757/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: BAID FINSERV LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511724)
- Rights ratio: 1 equity share for every 4 equity shares held
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 8/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share (payable on application)
- Record date: November 17, 2025
- Ex-rights date: November 17, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-757/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in BAID FINSERV LIMITED equity shares on ex-rights basis from November 17, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-rights arrangements
- Issue price of Rs. 10/- per share is payable on application by subscribing shareholders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 17, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date:** November 17, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-757/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Existing shareholders holding shares as of the record date will be eligible to subscribe to the rights issue at a 1:4 ratio. Shareholders must decide whether to exercise their rights, sell them, or let them lapse.

**Trading Impact:** From November 17, 2025, the stock will trade ex-rights, meaning new buyers will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue. This may result in price adjustment to reflect the rights entitlement separation.

**Capital Structure Impact:** If fully subscribed, the rights issue will increase the company's equity base by 25% (1 new share for every 4 existing shares), raising funds at Rs. 10/- per share.
