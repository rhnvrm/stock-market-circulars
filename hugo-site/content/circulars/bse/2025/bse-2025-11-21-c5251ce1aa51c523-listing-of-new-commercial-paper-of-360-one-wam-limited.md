## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each totaling Rs. 125 crore (2500 units) on BSE Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are admitted to trading with effect from 21st November 2025. HDFC Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 2500 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 125 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730605
- **Scrip ID**: OWL191125
- **ISIN**: INE466L14FF3 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,373.50/-
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE466L14FF3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be settled through the standard debt segment settlement process

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: 21st November 2025
- **Allotment Date**: 20th November 2025
- **Listing Date**: 21st November 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 18th February 2026
- **Tenure**: 89 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides short-term funding to 360 ONE WAM LIMITED and offers investment opportunities for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities can participate, while equity market participants remain unaffected. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE's debt platform.
