## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a government T-bill (GS17DEC25C) effective December 15, 2025, as it approaches its redemption/maturity date. Trading members are instructed not to deal in this security from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with ISIN IN001225C035 (Scrip Code 800395) designated as GS17DEC25C will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from December 15, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-777/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified T-bill (ISIN: IN001225C035, Scrip Code: 800395) from December 15, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 17, 2025 (implied from GS17DEC25C nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects single government T-bill approaching natural maturity

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard procedure for maturing securities; trading members should update their systems accordingly

**Investor Impact**: Low - investors holding this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date; no trading disruption for other securities
