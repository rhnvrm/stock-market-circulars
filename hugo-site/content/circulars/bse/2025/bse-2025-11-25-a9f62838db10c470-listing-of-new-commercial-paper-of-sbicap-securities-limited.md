## Summary

SBICAP Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,000 units of Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis, with a total value of Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units x Rs. 5,00,000 face value). The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will mature on February 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore
- Scrip Code: 730637
- Scrip ID: SSL241125
- ISIN: INE212K14CS3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE212K14CS3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 25, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 25, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 23, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issuance is part of normal treasury operations for SBICAP Securities Limited to meet short-term funding requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing is restricted to the debt segment and has no impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Trading will be limited to institutional participants in the debt market given the minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.
