## Summary

Ravelcare Limited (formerly Ravelcare Private Limited) announced the allocation of 5,25,000 equity shares to anchor investors at ₹130 per share (face value ₹10, premium ₹120) on November 28, 2025. The total amount raised from anchor investors is ₹6,82,50,000. Six anchor investors participated, with NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund receiving the largest allocation of 1,40,000 shares (26.66%). The company confirmed no applications were received from domestic mutual funds.

## Key Points

- Total anchor investor allocation: 5,25,000 equity shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: ₹130 per share (face value ₹10 + premium ₹120)
- Total funds raised from anchors: ₹6.82 crore
- Number of anchor investors: 6
- Largest investor: NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund (1,40,000 shares, 26.66%)
- Five investors received equal allocation of 77,000 shares each (14.67% each)
- No domestic mutual fund participation
- Board approval date: November 28, 2025

## Anchor Investor Details

1. **NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund**: 1,40,000 shares (26.66%) - ₹1.82 crore
2. **Lighthouse Canton Special Opportunities Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crore
3. **Viney Growth Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crore
4. **Finvesta First**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crore
5. **J4S Venture Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crore
6. **NAVbharat Investment Opportunities Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crore
