## Summary

BSE has listed 9,00,000 new equity shares of Uday Jewellery Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 539518) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from December 23, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. All shares are subject to a lock-in period until August 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 9,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 144/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 154/- per share
- Allotment made to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: September 25, 2025
- Trading commences: December 23, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 26084801 to 26984800
- ISIN: INE551B01012
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and permitted trading of these new securities. The shares are subject to standard lock-in requirements applicable to preferential allotments to promoters.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** September 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 23, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a routine preferential allotment to promoters following warrant conversion. With all 9,00,000 shares locked-in until August 31, 2027, there is no immediate impact on tradable float or market liquidity.
