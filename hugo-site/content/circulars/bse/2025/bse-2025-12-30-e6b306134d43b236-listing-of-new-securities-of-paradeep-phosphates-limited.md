## Summary

BSE has listed 22,16,23,331 equity shares of Paradeep Phosphates Limited (Scrip Code: 543530) effective December 31, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement between Mangalore Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited (Transferor Company) and Paradeep Phosphates Limited (Transferee Company) under sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 22,16,23,331 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each face value
- Listed and permitted for trading from December 31, 2025
- Issued under Composite Scheme of Arrangement with Mangalore Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE088F01024
- Distinctive Numbers: 816345659 to 1037968989
- Allotment date: December 3, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing following corporate restructuring approved under Companies Act, 2013 sections 230-232.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the addition of 22.16 crore shares to the traded float
- Updated shareholding pattern will reflect the merger impact
- Trading members to update client holdings and corporate action records

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 31, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Notice Date:** December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Significant increase in share count (22.16 crore shares) will impact liquidity and shareholding percentages. Existing shareholders will see dilution in their ownership percentage post-merger.

**Operational Impact:** The composite scheme consolidates operations between Mangalore Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited and Paradeep Phosphates Limited, potentially creating operational synergies in the fertilizer sector.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of Mangalore Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited receive Paradeep Phosphates shares as per the approved swap ratio. All new shares have equal rights with existing shares.
