## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. Orkla India Limited (ticker 544595) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective November 10, 2025, following its listing on November 06, 2025. Safecure Services Limited (ticker 544596) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective November 07, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on November 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- Orkla India Limited (544595) listed on BSE on November 06, 2025
- Orkla India Limited to be added to BSE IPO index on November 10, 2025
- Safecure Services Limited (544596) listed on BSE SME platform on November 06, 2025
- Safecure Services Limited to be added to BSE SME IPO index on November 07, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20251104-47 for Orkla India Limited
- References Notice No: 20251104-60 for Safecure Services Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational update regarding index composition.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and fund managers tracking these indices should note the additions for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **November 06, 2025**: Listing date for both Orkla India Limited and Safecure Services Limited
- **November 07, 2025**: Safecure Services Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **November 10, 2025**: Orkla India Limited added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine index inclusion following IPO listings, affecting only the specific indices mentioned.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Index tracking funds and ETFs following BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to include these stocks in their portfolios as per their rebalancing schedules.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors tracking or investing in the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices. The staggered addition dates allow for orderly inclusion.
