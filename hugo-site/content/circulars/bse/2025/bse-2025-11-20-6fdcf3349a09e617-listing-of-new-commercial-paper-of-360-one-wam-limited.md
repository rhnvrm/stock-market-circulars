## Summary

360 One Wam Limited has listed new Commercial Papers (CP) on BSE Debt segment effective November 20, 2025. Two tranches totaling Rs. 155 crores have been issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each. The securities carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature in February 2026. HDFC Bank Ltd is the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two tranches of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Tranche 1: 3000 units (Rs. 150 crores) - ISIN: INE466L14FF3, Scrip Code: 730605, maturity February 18, 2026
- Tranche 2: 100 units (Rs. 5 crores) - ISIN: INE466L14FD8 (further listing), Scrip Code: 730593, maturity February 17, 2026
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue prices: Rs. 491,279 (Tranche 1) and Rs. 491,516.50 (Tranche 2)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot is 1 unit per transaction

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 1):** February 18, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 2):** February 17, 2026
- **Notice Date:** November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instruments are institutional debt securities issued on private placement basis and are not accessible to retail investors. The short tenure (approximately 90 days) and high credit ratings (A1+) indicate these are standard money market instruments for corporate treasury operations. The listing provides additional short-term financing options for 360 One Wam Limited and offers institutional investors rated short-term debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
