## Summary

Three companies - Tainwala Chemicals and Plastics (India) Limited, Dolphin Offshore Enterprises (India) Limited, and India Cements Limited - submitted clarifications to BSE and NSE in response to surveillance queries regarding significant price and volume movements in their securities on December 17-18, 2025. All companies confirmed they have disclosed all material information as required under SEBI regulations and have no undisclosed information that could affect price/volume behavior.

## Key Points

- **Tainwala Chemicals** (BSE: 507785, NSE: TAINWALCHM) responded to queries about significant price movement dated December 17, 2025
- **Dolphin Offshore Enterprises** (BSE: 522261) addressed surveillance concerns via NSE email ref. NSE/CM/Surveillance/16186 dated December 17, 2025
- **India Cements** (BSE: 530005) clarified spurt in trading volume across exchanges
- All three companies confirmed full compliance with disclosure requirements under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated no hidden or undisclosed material information exists
- Price and volume fluctuations attributed to market-driven behavior

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine surveillance and disclosure matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies responded to stock exchange surveillance queries as required
- Confirmed ongoing compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- All material events and price-sensitive information have been disclosed within stipulated timelines
- Companies maintain they have no additional information requiring disclosure at this time

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: BSE and NSE issued surveillance queries to companies
- **December 18, 2025**: Companies submitted clarification responses

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are standard regulatory clarifications with no new material information. The companies' confirmations that movements are market-driven and that all disclosures are current should not materially affect trading.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral - Investors are informed that recent price/volume movements are not due to undisclosed corporate developments. The companies' statements provide transparency but no actionable new information.

**Compliance Impact**: Routine - Companies demonstrated adherence to surveillance cooperation requirements and reiterated their compliance with continuous disclosure obligations under SEBI regulations.
