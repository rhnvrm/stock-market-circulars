## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (total 1500 units) issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis, effective August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1500 units (Rs. 75 crore total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730117
- ISIN: INE755K14FO3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard commercial paper listing procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025
- Listing Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding option for Dalmia Cement with 3-month tenure. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
