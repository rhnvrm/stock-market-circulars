## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by DSP Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 3,500 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 175 crore) with ICRA A1+ rating. The securities were allotted on December 10, 2025, and will mature on March 11, 2026. Trading commenced on December 11, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: DSP Finance Private Limited
- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 3,500 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 175 crore
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730746
- **Scrip ID**: DSPF101225
- **ISIN**: INE422H14149
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Trading Specifications

- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Listed and admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from December 11, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE422H14149
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples only
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise applies

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to DSP Finance Private Limited for a 3-month period. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates highest credit quality and minimal credit risk. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
