## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 29, 2025. This testing session will include specific index futures and stock futures contracts with December 2025 expiry to ensure system readiness and operational continuity.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 29, 2025
- Covers Equity Derivatives segment only
- Includes SENSEX and BANKEX index futures (current month expiry: December 24, 2025)
- Includes stock futures for 5 major stocks: KOTAKBANK, AXISBANK, BHARTIARTL, HDFCBANK, and ICICIBANK
- All contracts have December 24, 2025 as expiry date
- Session is for testing and system validation purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational testing activity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are expected to participate in the mock trading session to:
- Test their systems and connectivity
- Validate order placement and execution mechanisms
- Ensure readiness for regular trading operations
- Verify risk management systems

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: Saturday, November 29, 2025
- **Contract Expiry Date**: December 24, 2025 (for all listed instruments)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None - This is a mock trading session with no real transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to allocate resources for participation in the mock session on a Saturday. The session helps ensure system stability and readiness, reducing potential technical issues during actual trading.

**Participant Impact**: Trading members and market participants should use this opportunity to test their systems, connectivity, and operational procedures in a simulated environment without financial risk.
