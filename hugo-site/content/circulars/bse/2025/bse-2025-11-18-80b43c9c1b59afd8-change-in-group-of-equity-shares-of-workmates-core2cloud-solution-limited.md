## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544610) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group effective December 01, 2025. This change follows Exchange Notice No. 20251117-60 dated November 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544610
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, December 01, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822
- Notice Number: 20251118-4

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED will shift from Trade for Trade settlement (MT Group) to Rolling settlement under M Group. This represents a change in the trading and settlement methodology applicable to the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the segment transfer effective December 01, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications
- Ensure compliance with Rolling segment trading rules for this scrip

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Previous notice (20251117-60) issued
- **November 18, 2025**: Current notice issued
- **December 01, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment generally indicates improved market standing and increased liquidity potential for the stock. This change allows for intraday trading and standard T+2 settlement cycles instead of mandatory delivery-based trading. Trading members and investors will benefit from enhanced trading flexibility and potentially improved price discovery mechanisms. The move to M Group suggests the stock has met certain criteria set by the exchange for graduation from the more restrictive Trade for Trade segment.
