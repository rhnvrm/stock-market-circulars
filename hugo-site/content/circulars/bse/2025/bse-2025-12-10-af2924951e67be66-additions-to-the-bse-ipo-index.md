## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective December 12, 2025. The companies are MEESHO LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544632), VIDYA WIRES LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544633), and AEQUS LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544634), all of which commenced trading on BSE on December 10, 2025. This follows the standard procedure for including new IPO listings into the BSE IPO Index.

## Key Points

- Three companies listed on BSE on December 10, 2025 will be added to the BSE IPO Index
- MEESHO LIMITED (ticker: 544632)
- VIDYA WIRES LIMITED (ticker: 544633)
- AEQUS LIMITED (ticker: 544634)
- Index inclusion effective at market open on December 12, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References previous listing notices: 20251209-49, 20251209-51, and 20251209-62

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity following standard procedures for adding newly listed IPO companies to the BSE IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolio managers who track or replicate the BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their holdings to reflect these additions.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)
- Corporate action file updated on December 10, 2025 (evening)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects BSE IPO Index composition and performance calculations.

**Investor Impact**: Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE IPO Index will need to purchase shares of these three companies to maintain alignment. This may result in increased trading volumes for these stocks around the effective date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine index maintenance handled through standard corporate action files. Market participants can query bseindex@bseindia.com for additional information.
