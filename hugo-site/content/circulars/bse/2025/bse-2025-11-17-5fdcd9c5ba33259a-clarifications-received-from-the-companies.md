## Summary

BSE Surveillance sought clarifications from three companies - Ipca Laboratories Limited, Jubilant FoodWorks Limited, and Muthoot Finance Limited - regarding significant increase in trading volumes of their securities. All three companies responded stating that there are no material events or price sensitive information that required disclosure, and attributed the volume increase to market conditions.

## Key Points

- Ipca Laboratories (Scrip Code: 524494/IPCALAB) clarified no material information or events requiring disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- Jubilant FoodWorks (Scrip Code: 533155/JUBLFOOD) stated the volume increase is purely due to market conditions and market driven
- Muthoot Finance (Scrip Code: 533398) confirmed prompt disclosure of all material events to stock exchanges
- All companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated they have been regularly disseminating relevant information to stock exchanges from time to time
- Jubilant FoodWorks referenced their financial results submission dated November 13, 2025 for Q2 and H1 FY2025-26

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond to BSE Surveillance queries regarding significant volume movements
- Companies must disclose material information and events under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- Regular dissemination of relevant information to stock exchanges is mandatory

## Important Dates

- November 17, 2025: Date of clarification letters from all three companies
- November 13, 2025: Jubilant FoodWorks submitted unaudited financial results for Q2 and H1 ended September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it represents routine surveillance activity. All three companies confirmed no undisclosed material events, indicating the volume movements were driven by normal market forces. The clarifications provide transparency to market participants but do not reveal any new information affecting the companies' operations or performance. This is standard regulatory oversight ensuring proper disclosure compliance.
