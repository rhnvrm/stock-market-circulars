## Summary

LODHA DEVELOPERS LIMITED has announced part redemption of two series of non-convertible debentures with record date of December 15, 2025. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values effective from the same date. The two affected series are: (1) LDL-Reset Rate-21-01-28-PVT with new face value of Rs. 91,341 per debenture, and (2) LDL-TBILLS-13-11-28-PVT with new face value of Rs. 99,600 per debenture along with interest payment.

## Key Points

- Two series of LODHA DEVELOPERS LIMITED debentures undergoing part redemption
- Record date fixed as December 15, 2025
- Trading with reduced face values effective from December 15, 2025 under settlement DR-777/2025-2026
- First series: INE670K07307 (Code: 976923) - Reset Rate debenture with reduced face value of Rs. 91,341 per debenture
- Second series: INE670K07349 (Code: 977293) - TBILLS linked debenture with reduced face value of Rs. 99,600 per debenture
- Second series includes both part redemption and interest payment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notice under existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values effective December 15, 2025
- Debenture trading must reflect new face values from the effective date
- Members must process transactions under settlement number DR-777/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: December 15, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-777/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Material impact on holders of the two specified debenture series who will receive partial redemption proceeds. The face value reduction indicates substantial redemption amounts being returned to investors.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members must update systems to reflect new face values from December 15, 2025. This affects pricing, valuation, and settlement processes for these specific ISINs.

**Market Segment**: Limited to debt segment with impact confined to these two specific debenture series of LODHA DEVELOPERS LIMITED. No broader market implications.
