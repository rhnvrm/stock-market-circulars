## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of the debenture series KMPL-7.90%-13-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE916DA7SB4, Code: 974532). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Specific debenture affected: KMPL-7.90%-13-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE916DA7SB4)
- Debenture code: 974532
- Record date for redemption: December 29, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: December 29, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-786/2025-2026
- This is a private placement debenture series

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption process

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a specific series of privately placed debentures of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, not the broader market or equity securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this particular debenture series (ISIN: INE916DA7SB4) who will undergo the redemption process as per the terms of the instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in these specific debentures from December 29, 2025. This is a routine operational adjustment for corporate action processing.
