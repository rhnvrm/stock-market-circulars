## Summary

BSE has issued updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective December 04, 2025. Four securities are being newly shortlisted into ESM Stage I: Arigato Universe Ltd, Parmeshwar Metal Ltd (SME), Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd, and TVS Electronics Ltd. Additionally, two securities - IEL Ltd and SPEL Semiconductor Ltd - will move to higher ESM stages while continuing in the framework. No securities are exiting the ESM framework or moving to lower stages in this update.

## Key Points

- 4 securities newly added to Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Stage I framework
- 2 securities moving to higher ESM stages while remaining in framework
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages
- No securities exiting ESM framework in this cycle
- Changes effective from December 04, 2025
- Consolidated list includes 26+ securities across different ESM stages

## Securities Added to ESM Stage I

**New Entries (Effective Dec 04, 2025):**

1. **Arigato Universe Ltd** (Scrip Code: 530267, ISIN: INE021N01011)
2. **Parmeshwar Metal Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544330, ISIN: INE0QQJ01021) - SME Scrip
3. **Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd** (Scrip Code: 511557, ISIN: INE732K01027)
4. **TVS Electronics Ltd** (Scrip Code: 532513, ISIN: INE236G01019)

## Securities Moving to Higher ESM Stages

**Escalations (Effective Dec 04, 2025):**

1. **IEL Ltd** (Scrip Code: 524614, ISIN: INE056E01024)
2. **SPEL Semiconductor Ltd** (Scrip Code: 517166, ISIN: INE252A01019)

## Regulatory Framework

The Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework is designed to:

- Alert investors about securities exhibiting abnormal price movements
- Enhance market surveillance and investor protection
- Impose stricter trading conditions on identified securities
- Progress securities through multiple stages (I, II, etc.) based on continued surveillance concerns
- Apply additional disclosure and monitoring requirements

## Trading Implications

Securities under ESM framework are subject to:

- Enhanced monitoring and disclosure requirements
- Potential trading restrictions or additional surveillance
- Higher scrutiny of price and volume movements
- Possible impact on liquidity and trading participation
- Progressive escalation to higher stages if concerns persist

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 04, 2025
- All changes to ESM classifications become applicable from market opening on December 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Moderate - affects 6 securities with surveillance measure changes

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or trading these securities should be aware of enhanced surveillance status, which may affect trading conditions and liquidity. The ESM framework serves as a cautionary measure for abnormal price behavior.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members and market participants must update their systems and risk management frameworks to reflect the new ESM classifications and stages for affected securities.
