## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Papers issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 15,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 7,500 crore, effective November 26, 2025. The securities will trade only in dematerialized form with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 15,000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,881.50 per security
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730653
- **Scrip ID**: PFL251125
- **ISIN**: INE511C14ZC0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Trading Specifications

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading segment: BSE Debt Market

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides Poonawalla Fincorp Limited with short-term funding through the commercial paper route. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can trade these securities. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness and low credit risk for investors.
