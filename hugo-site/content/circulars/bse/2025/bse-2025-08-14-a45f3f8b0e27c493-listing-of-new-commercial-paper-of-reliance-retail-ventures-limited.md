## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth ₹1,425 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value ₹5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 28,500 units (₹1,425 crore)
- Issue price: ₹4,92,727.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE929O14DT0
- Scrip Code: 730029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited with high credit ratings indicating low default risk.
