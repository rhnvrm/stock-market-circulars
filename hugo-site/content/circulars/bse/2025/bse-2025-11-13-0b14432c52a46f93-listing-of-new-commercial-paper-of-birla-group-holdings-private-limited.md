## Summary

BSE has listed 3000 units of Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings with a 90-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each
- Quantity: 3000 units
- Scrip Code: 730543
- Scrip ID: BGH121125
- ISIN: INE09OL14IC0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,754.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IC0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 11, 2026
- Notice Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper listing provides short-term funding for Birla Group Holdings Private Limited and offers investment opportunities for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
