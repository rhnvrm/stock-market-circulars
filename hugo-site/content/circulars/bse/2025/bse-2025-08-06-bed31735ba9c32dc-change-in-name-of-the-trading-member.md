## Summary

BSE has announced that Trading Member INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6779) will change its name to "INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Member Number 6779 remains unchanged
- Name change from INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED to INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective date: August 7, 2025
- Administrative change only - no impact on trading operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is purely an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other market participants. Trading members requiring clarification should contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **August 7, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact - this is an administrative name change that does not affect trading operations, member number, or any other operational aspects of the trading member's activities on BSE.
