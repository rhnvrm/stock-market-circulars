## Summary

BSE announces that KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 04, 2025. This notice reverses the previous suspension announced in Notice No. 20251104-57. All transaction facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) will be available.

## Key Points

- KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND reopening for transactions from December 04, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- Full transaction facilities restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Applies to all plan types (Regular and Direct) across all option variants
- Reference to previous suspension notice: 20251104-57

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1XZ1
2. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1YA2
3. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1YB0

### Direct Plan Options
4. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1XW8
5. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1XX6
6. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1XY4

All schemes are categorized under EQUITY fund category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update reversing a previous temporary suspension of transactions in the fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF Platform members should update their systems to enable transactions in KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND from December 04, 2025
- All six ISIN codes listed should be activated for trading
- Members must ensure all transaction types (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) are operational

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 04, 2025 - Fund reopens for all ongoing transactions
- **Previous Suspension Notice**: November 04, 2025 (Notice No. 20251104-57)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a fund-specific operational update affecting only investors and distributors dealing with KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors who can now resume transactions in this equity fund focusing on rural opportunities theme.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform participants need to enable the fund for transactions before December 04, 2025. No broader operational changes required beyond this specific fund reactivation.
