## Summary

BSE has announced that units of Anantam Highways Trust, an Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) Initial Public Offering, will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange. The trust has been assigned scrip code 544579, trading symbol ANANTAM, and ISIN number INE1UA823019. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Trust Name: Anantam Highways Trust
- Scrip Code: 544579
- Trading Symbol: ANANTAM
- ISIN Number: INE1UA823019
- Type: Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) IPO
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for a new InvIT IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the new listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures once listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands investment opportunities in the infrastructure sector through the InvIT structure. InvITs provide investors access to infrastructure assets with potential for regular income distribution. The announcement has medium impact as it adds a new investment vehicle to the market, particularly relevant for investors seeking exposure to highway infrastructure assets. Trading members should prepare systems for the new security once the listing date is confirmed.
