## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by six companies on the debt market segment effective December 24, 2025. These securities will be traded only in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial papers on BSE debt segment
- All securities to be traded in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Total quantity listed: 24,200 units across all issuers
- Contact: BSE Debt Department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples must be maintained
- Tick size of 1 paise applies to all listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 23-24, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: March 6, 2026 to March 24, 2026 (varies by issuer)
- **Interest Payment**: Cumulative at redemption

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular announcing the listing of privately placed commercial papers. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in these specific securities. No broad market impact expected. Trading members requiring clarification can contact the BSE Debt Department.
