## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies - All Time Plastics Limited (ticker: 544479) and JSW Cement Limited (ticker: 544480) - to the BSE IPO Index. Both stocks, which were listed on August 14, 2025, will be added to the index effective at the open of Tuesday, August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- All Time Plastics Limited (Exchange ticker: 544479) to be added to BSE IPO Index
- JSW Cement Limited (Exchange ticker: 544480) to be added to BSE IPO Index
- Both stocks listed on BSE on Thursday, August 14, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on Tuesday, August 19, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index maintenance activity following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the index changes.

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **August 19, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (market open)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of these two stocks to the BSE IPO Index will primarily impact index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE IPO Index. Fund managers will need to purchase shares of both companies to maintain their index tracking accuracy. This could create additional buying pressure for both stocks on the effective date.
