## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis effective December 19, 2025. The listing comprises 9000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 9000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,874 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730798
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL171225
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DZ6 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DZ6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective from December 19, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides an additional short-term debt investment option (3-month tenure) for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in commercial paper investments. No broader market or compliance implications for general trading members beyond awareness of the new listing.
