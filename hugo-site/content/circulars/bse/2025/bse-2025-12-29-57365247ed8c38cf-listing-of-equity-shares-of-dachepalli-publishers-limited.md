## Summary

BSE has listed 14,976,000 equity shares of Dachepalli Publishers Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The listing includes shares allocated to various investor categories with different lock-in periods ranging from 90 days to approximately 3 years. All shares are fully paid and in demat form.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 14,976,000 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid and in demat form
- Multiple lock-in periods apply based on investor category
- Anchor investor allocation: 3,398,400 shares (564,000 + 2,834,400)
- Anchor investor lock-in: 90 days (until January 24-25, 2026)
- Regular allotment lock-in periods vary from 1 year to 3 years
- Lock-in start dates: December 17, 2025 (regular) and December 26, 2025 (anchor)
- Lock-in end dates range from January 2026 to January 2029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard IPO listing circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shares must comply with specified lock-in periods based on allocation category
- Promoter and investor holdings subject to SEBI lock-in regulations
- Trading permitted only after respective lock-in periods expire

## Important Dates

- **Regular Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Anchor Investor Allotment**: December 26, 2025
- **Anchor Lock-in Expiry**: January 24-25, 2026 (90 days)
- **1-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 4, 2027
- **2-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 4, 2028
- **3-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 4, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard IPO listing for a single company. The listing adds liquidity to the market but has limited broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Relevant for IPO subscribers and anchor investors who need to track lock-in periods. Different categories have varying holding requirements ranging from 90 days to 3 years.

**Operational Impact**: The staggered lock-in periods will affect trading volumes as different tranches of shares become tradeable at different times between January 2026 and January 2029.
