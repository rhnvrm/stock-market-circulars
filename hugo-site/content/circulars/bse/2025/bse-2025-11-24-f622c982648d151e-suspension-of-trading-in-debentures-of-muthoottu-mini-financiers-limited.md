## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (ISIN: INE101Q07607, Code: 937127) effective December 2, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (MMFL-ZC-17-12-25-NCD)
- ISIN: INE101Q07607
- BSE Code: 937127
- Record date set for redemption purpose
- Trading members explicitly instructed not to deal in these debentures
- Circular reference: DR-768/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 2, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate accordingly to clients
- No new trades should be executed in these debentures on or after the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 2, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 2, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: December 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only holders of specific debenture series of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited. The suspension is temporary and tied to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these debentures (INE101Q07607) will not be able to trade them from December 2, 2025. Investors should plan any required transactions before the no-dealings date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN and communicate the suspension to relevant clients holding these debentures. This is a routine operational procedure for corporate actions in the debt segment.
