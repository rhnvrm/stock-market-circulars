## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 50,000 equity shares of Comrade Appliances Limited (Scrip Code: 543921) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are listed and permitted to trade with effect from November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- 50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 99/-
- Issue price: Rs. 109/- per share
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: November 22, 2024
- Distinctive Numbers: 7728796 to 7778795
- ISIN: INE0NXA01015
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences: November 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listing
- All 50,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions
- Locked-in shares cannot be transferred until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 22, 2024
- **Trading Commencement**: November 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 15, 2027 (for all 50,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a small preferential allotment to promoters representing warrant conversion. The entire issue of 50,000 shares is locked-in until May 15, 2027, preventing immediate trading of these shares. The listing is part of routine corporate actions for capital raising through preferential issues.
