## Summary

BSE has listed and permitted trading of 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. The shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and will be available for trading from December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited listed
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- Two batches: 15 shares (distinctive nos. 6412616651-6412616665) and 30 shares (distinctive nos. 6412615697-6412615726)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for bonus shares that were held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 45 bonus equity shares representing a negligible fraction of ICICI Bank's total outstanding shares. This is an administrative action to release previously abeyance shares into the tradable pool. Normal trading operations continue without disruption.
