## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD (Scrip Code 973204) effective December 09, 2025. This follows the partial exercise of put option by debenture holders after the record date of October 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD debentures (Scrip Code 973204) to resume from December 09, 2025
- Put option was partially exercised by debenture holders
- Previous notice dated October 17, 2025 had set record date as October 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption
- Reference number: DR-773/2025-2026
- Trading members are requested to take note of the resumption

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural notification regarding the resumption of trading following a corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures with Scrip Code 973204 will be available for trading from December 09, 2025
- No additional compliance actions required from market participants

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Original exchange notice issued
- **October 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 08, 2025**: Notice date for resumption announcement
- **December 09, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of trading

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific notification with minimal market-wide impact. The resumption of trading affects only holders and potential traders of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD debentures with Scrip Code 973204. The partial exercise of put option indicates selective redemption by some debenture holders while others chose to continue holding the securities. Market participants interested in this specific debt instrument can resume trading from December 09, 2025.
