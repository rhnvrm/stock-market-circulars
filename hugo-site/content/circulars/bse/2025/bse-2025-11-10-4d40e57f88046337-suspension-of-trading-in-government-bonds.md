## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Government Bond 812MHSDL25 (Scrip Code: 810716, ISIN: IN2220150121) effective November 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the bond reaching its redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Government Bond 812MHSDL25 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810716
- ISIN: IN2220150121
- Trading suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-753/2025-2026
- Trading members must cease all dealing in this bond

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for bonds reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the suspended Government Bond (Scrip Code: 810716) from November 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Bond Redemption/Maturity Date**: November 11, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting a single government bond upon maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their trading systems to prevent transactions in this specific security. Only bondholders of 812MHSDL25 are affected, as they will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond terms.

**Segment Affected**: Debt segment only, specifically the government bonds category.
