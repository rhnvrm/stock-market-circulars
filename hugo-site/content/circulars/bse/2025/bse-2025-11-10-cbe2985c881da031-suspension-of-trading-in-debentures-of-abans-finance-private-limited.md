## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE00ZD07595, Code: 974011) effective from November 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing November 15, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to AFPL-NIFTY 50-30-11-25-PVT debentures
- ISIN: INE00ZD07595
- BSE Code: 974011
- Record date: November 15, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 14, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-756/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard trading suspension notice related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 14, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and comply with the no-dealings directive
- All pending orders and positions in these debentures should be managed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 14, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **November 15, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only specific debentures of a private limited company. The debt instrument appears to be linked to NIFTY 50 with a maturity date of November 30, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders registered as of November 15, 2025 will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading opportunity available from November 14, 2025.

**Liquidity Impact**: Temporary suspension for one trading day before record date, following standard market practice for corporate actions. Normal procedure to prevent settlement issues during corporate action processing.
