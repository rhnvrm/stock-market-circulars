## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by CaratLane Trading Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from 2nd December 2025. The issue comprises 3000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 150 crore, with a tenor of approximately 88 days maturing on 27th February 2026. The instruments carry ICRA A1+ credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,811.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Allotment Date: 1st December 2025
- Redemption Date: 27th February 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE015Y14641
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: 2nd December 2025
- **Allotment Date**: 1st December 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 27th February 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine short-term debt listing for working capital or operational funding purposes. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates highest credit quality for short-term instruments. The issue is privately placed and represents standard corporate treasury operations. Trading will be limited to institutional investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination requirement.
