## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Mother Nutri Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544623, symbol MNFL, and ISIN INE0UH001016. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Mother Nutri Foods Limited
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Symbol: MNFL
- ISIN: INE0UH001016
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the scrip details for the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to trading members who will need to prepare for trading once the listing date is confirmed. No broader market impact expected.
