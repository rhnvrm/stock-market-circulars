## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis effective December 12, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.95% per annum with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling 20,000 units (Rs. 200 crores). The bonds have a tenure until September 10, 2027, with annual interest payments.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited
- Scrip Code: 977362
- Scrip ID: 895NWIL27
- ISIN: INE523L07AS9
- Credit Rating: CARE AA
- Quantity: 20,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crores
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AS9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 10, 2026 to September 10, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: September 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing with no impact on secondary market operations or retail investors.

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard trading procedures apply for debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors who participated in the private placement. The CARE AA rating indicates high credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of debt obligations.
