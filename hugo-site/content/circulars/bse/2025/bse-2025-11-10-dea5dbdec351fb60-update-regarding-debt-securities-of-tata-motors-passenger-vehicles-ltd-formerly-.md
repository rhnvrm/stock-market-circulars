## Summary

Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing shows the company's shareholding structure with Promoter & Promoter Group holding 42.56% and Public shareholders holding 57.44% of the total equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares held: 3,682,331,373 equity shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 1,567,369,812 shares (42.56%)
- Public shareholding: 2,114,961,561 shares (57.44%)
- Number of public shareholders: 6,647,789
- Outstanding ESOPs exist and are pending (2,666,563 shares)
- Company Secretary: Sudipto Kumar Das
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares
- Tata Sons Private Limited (TSPL) is exempted from SBO Rules as a core investment company registered with RBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing made under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Standard shareholding pattern disclosure requirement for listed entities
- Declaration confirms no significant encumbrances or special share structures

## Important Dates

- Filing date: November 10, 2025
- No specific compliance deadlines mentioned in this routine disclosure

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a stable ownership structure with no significant changes. The presence of outstanding ESOPs (2,666,563 shares representing less than 0.1% on a fully diluted basis) indicates ongoing employee stock option plans. The promoter holding of 42.56% demonstrates continued majority influence while maintaining adequate public float as per SEBI requirements.
