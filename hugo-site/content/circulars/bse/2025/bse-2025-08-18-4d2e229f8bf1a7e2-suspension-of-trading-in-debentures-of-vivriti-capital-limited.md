## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in three series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED effective August 22, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing August 22, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to three specific debenture series of VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED
- All three series have the same record date of August 22, 2025
- Suspension is for both redemption of debentures and interest payment purposes
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 22, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **August 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine suspension for corporate action processing. The suspension affects only three specific debenture series of VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED and is temporary in nature. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and interest payments as per the record date eligibility.
