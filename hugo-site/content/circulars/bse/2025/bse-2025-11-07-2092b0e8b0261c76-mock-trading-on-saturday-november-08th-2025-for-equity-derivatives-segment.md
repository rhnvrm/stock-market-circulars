## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. The mock trading will simulate various market conditions and functionalities. No actual trading obligations will be created from this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 08, 2025
- Available for Equity Derivatives segment only
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Trading members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems through ETI APIs
- Session will test various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Mock trades will not create any rights, liabilities, margin obligations, or pay-in/pay-out obligations
- Feedback must be submitted by 5:00 PM on the session day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading session for system testing and familiarization.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Feedback should be submitted via contact numbers or email provided (BSE Tech Support: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com; Help Desk: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com)
- Members may contact their respective Relationship Manager for queries or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - Log-in: 11:15 AM to 12:15 PM
  - Continuous Trading: 12:15 PM to 04:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 04:00 PM to 04:15 PM
- **November 08, 2025 by 5:00 PM**: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. This is a voluntary testing opportunity for trading members to validate their systems and familiarize themselves with platform functionalities. Members using third-party vendors or in-house developed systems can test various scenarios including exceptional market conditions. No trading obligations or settlements will result from this session.
