## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the takeover offer of Kolte-Patil Developers Limited. The acquisition window will be conducted from August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025, with settlement number 2025073.

## Key Points

- Takeover bid period: August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025
- Settlement number: 2025073
- Obligation download: September 19, 2025
- Settlement date: September 22, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Market type "Takeover" to be used in depository systems

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Process follows existing guidelines for acquisition of shares through stock exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "Takeover" and settlement number 2025073 in depository systems
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- Securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Bid start date
- **September 8, 2025**: Bid end date
- **September 19, 2025**: Obligation download
- **September 22, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on shareholders and market participants of Kolte-Patil Developers Limited. Standard takeover procedure with specific settlement requirements affecting trading and custody operations during the specified period.
