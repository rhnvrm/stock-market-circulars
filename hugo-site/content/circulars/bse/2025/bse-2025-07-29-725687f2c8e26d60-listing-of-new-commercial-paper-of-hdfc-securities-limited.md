## Summary

HDFC Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 150 crores (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are available for trading from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 150 crores (3,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,280 per security (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE700G14PC1
- Scrip Code: 729919
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Trading Start Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: Approximately 3 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term funding for HDFC Securities Limited and offer investors a 3-month investment option with high credit ratings (A1+). The issue represents standard corporate funding activity with no broader market implications.
