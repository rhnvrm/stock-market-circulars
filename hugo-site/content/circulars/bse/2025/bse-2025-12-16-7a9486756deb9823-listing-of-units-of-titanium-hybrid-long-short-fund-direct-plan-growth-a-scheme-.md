## Summary

BSE has listed units of Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan Growth under Tata Mutual Fund for trading effective December 17, 2025. This is a close-ended equity scheme that invests in equity and equity-related instruments, debt and money market instruments, with limited short exposure through derivatives. The units will trade in dematerialized form only in the 'B' Group.

## Key Points

- Fund Name: Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan Growth
- Investment Manager: Tata Asset Management Private Limited
- Number of Units Listed: 62,491,849 units
- Scrip Code: 544649
- ISIN: INF277K30047
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10.03 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 11, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: December 17, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular is a standard listing notification complying with SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to trade only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Members should note the scrip details for their trading systems: Scrip ID/Abbreviated name: TSIFDG

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **December 16, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 17, 2025**: Effective date for listing and trading commencement

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine addition of a mutual fund scheme to the exchange. Impact is limited to:
- Investors seeking exposure to hybrid long-short strategies through Tata Mutual Fund
- Trading members who need to update their systems with the new scrip details
- The scheme provides investors access to a close-ended fund with equity, debt, and derivative exposure including short positions
