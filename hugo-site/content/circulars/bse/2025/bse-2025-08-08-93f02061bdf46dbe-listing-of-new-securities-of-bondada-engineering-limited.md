## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 15,66,000 new equity shares of Bondada Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543971) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 15.66 lakh equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 349.20/-
- Issue price: Rs. 351.20 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0Q8P01029
- Distinctive Numbers: 110027486 to 111593485

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 31, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period Expires: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares by allottees.
