## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 3000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, trading under scrip code 730644 (IFL241125) with ISIN INE879F14MB4, effective November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 3000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each
- **Scrip Code**: 730644
- **Scrip ID**: IFL241125
- **ISIN**: INE879F14MB4
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 485,020.50 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MB4
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the BSE Debt segment. The instruments are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term debt. Trading members dealing in debt securities and institutional investors in commercial papers may utilize this instrument for short-term investment (approximately 5-month tenure). No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
