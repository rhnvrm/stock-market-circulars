## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 7, 2025. The listing comprises 17,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each on the BSE Debt segment. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings with a redemption period of one year.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 17,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,68,384 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730512
- **Scrip ID**: BFL61125
- **ISIN Number**: INE296A14D21
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 6, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the Commercial Papers only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE296A14D21
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper by Bajaj Finance Limited with no significant market impact. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. Trading members can contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers for clarifications. The listing expands the debt market offerings available on BSE's platform for institutional investors.
