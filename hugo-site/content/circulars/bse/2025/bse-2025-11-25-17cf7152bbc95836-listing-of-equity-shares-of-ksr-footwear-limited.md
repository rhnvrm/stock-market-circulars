## Summary

KSR Footwear Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as required under Regulation 31(1)(b) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for the quarter ended September 30, 2025. The company has a total of 18,378,382 fully paid equity shares with promoter and promoter group holding 59.83% and public holding 40.17%.

## Key Points

- Total number of shareholders: 32,100 (5 promoters + 32,095 public)
- Total equity shares: 18,378,382 fully paid shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 10,995,177 shares (59.83%)
- Public holding: 7,383,205 shares (40.17%)
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants outstanding
- No depository receipts issued
- Locked-in shares: 164,384 shares (0.89% of total)
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered
- Report generated on October 8, 2025 based on NSDL and CDSL data as of September 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes reported. This is a standard compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filed under Regulation 31(1)(b) for quarterly shareholding pattern disclosure
- Required submission for listed entities to maintain transparency in shareholding structure
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, or pledged promoter shares

## Important Dates

- Quarter ended: September 30, 2025
- NSDL data date: September 30, 2025
- CDSL data date: September 30, 2025
- Report generation date: October 8, 2025
- Circular publication date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine quarterly disclosure showing stable promoter holding at 59.83% with no pledging or encumbrance of promoter shares. The shareholding pattern indicates healthy compliance with minimum public shareholding norms and transparent ownership structure. The presence of minimal locked-in shares (0.89%) suggests normal post-listing status.
