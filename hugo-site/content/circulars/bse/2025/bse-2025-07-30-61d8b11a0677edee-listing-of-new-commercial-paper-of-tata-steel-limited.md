## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by TATA STEEL LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with a 90-day maturity period and ICRA A1+/IND A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (Total value: Rs. 500 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729929, Scrip ID: TSL29725
- ISIN: INE081A14FZ1
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides Tata Steel with short-term funding at competitive rates given the high credit rating. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
