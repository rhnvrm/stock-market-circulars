## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Tirupati Tyres Ltd. The company name has been officially changed to Tirupati Innovar Limited with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate confirms that this name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L46209MH1988PLC285197
- Old Name: TIRUPATI TYRES LTD
- New Name: TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED
- Effective Date: September 29, 2025
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Original Incorporation Name: TIRUPATI TYRES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate issued by: Prerna Panwar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Registered Address: Unit No 606, Reliables Pride, Anand Nagar, opp Heera Panna, Jogeshwari West, Mumbai- 400102, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative corporate action for company name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display its old name (TIRUPATI TYRES LTD) along with the new name (TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All company documents, letterheads, and communications must reflect the name change
- The name change does not affect existing contracts, rights, or liabilities of the company

## Important Dates

- **September 29, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Tirupati Tyres Ltd to Tirupati Innovar Limited
- **September 29, 2027**: End of two-year period for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect business operations or financial performance.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative only. The company must update its branding, documentation, and signage to reflect the new name while displaying both names for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts and agreements remain valid under the new name.

**Trading Impact**: No impact on trading. The stock will continue to trade under its existing scrip code with updated company name.
