## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the initial public offering (IPO) of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. Anchor investor allocation is conducted one day prior to the IPO opening and provides an indication of institutional demand and helps in price discovery.

## Key Points

- Company: EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- Event: Allocation to anchor investors in public issue
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Circular Date: November 27, 2025
- Anchor allocation conducted as per SEBI guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification as per SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- Disclosure of anchor investor details as mandated by SEBI
- Trading members and market participants to note the allocation for IPO processing

## Important Dates

- Anchor Allocation Date: November 27, 2025 (or one day prior to IPO opening)
- IPO opening date to follow anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation provides price stability and institutional validation for the IPO. The allocation indicates demand from qualified institutional buyers and can influence retail investor sentiment.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard IPO process notification requiring no special operational changes from market participants.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Important information for IPO applicants regarding institutional demand and potential pricing indicators.
