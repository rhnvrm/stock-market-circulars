## Summary

BSE has published the comprehensive trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for December 2025. The circular provides the complete schedule covering 22 settlement cycles (NDS-162/25-26 through NDS-183/25-26) from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025, including trading dates, 6A/7A entry and confirmation dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, auction dates, and auction pay-in/pay-out dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers December 1-31, 2025
- Total of 22 settlement cycles scheduled (NDS-162/25-26 to NDS-183/25-26)
- Regular pay-in time: 11:00 a.m. (members must submit instructions by 10:50 a.m.)
- Auction pay-in time: 9:30 a.m. (members must submit instructions by 9:20 a.m.)
- Settlement follows T+1 cycle for regular pay-in/pay-out
- Auction pay-out follows on T+2
- Instructions must be submitted to Depositories/banks before specified cut-off times

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 10:50 a.m. for regular settlements
- Members must submit auction pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:20 a.m. for auction settlements
- All participants must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Timely submission of instructions is mandatory to ensure smooth settlement operations

## Important Dates

- **Programme Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **First Settlement**: NDS-162/25-26 (Trading: December 1, 2025)
- **Last Settlement**: NDS-183/25-26 (Trading: December 31, 2025)
- **Year-end Processing**: Settlements extending into January 2026 (December 30-31 trades settle in early January)
- **Pay-in Cut-off**: 10:50 a.m. daily for regular settlements
- **Auction Pay-in Cut-off**: 9:20 a.m. daily for auction settlements

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine monthly schedule publication that enables market participants to plan their debt segment trading and settlement activities.

**Operational Impact**: Low to medium for NDS participants who need to plan their operations, fund management, and settlement instructions according to the published schedule.

**Participant Action Required**: NDS segment participants should update their internal systems and calendars with the settlement schedule to ensure timely submission of pay-in instructions and adequate fund/securities availability on settlement dates.

**Year-end Considerations**: Participants should note that settlements for trades executed on December 30-31, 2025 will be completed in January 2026, requiring appropriate planning for year-end accounting and reconciliation processes.
