## Summary

Capillary Technologies India Limited has finalized the allocation of 68,28,001 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs 577 per share (including share premium of Rs 575) on November 13, 2025. The anchor investor portion raised approximately Rs 393.97 crores. The IPO comprises a fresh issue of up to Rs 3,450 million and an offer for sale of up to 9,228,796 equity shares. The allocation was finalized by the IPO Committee in consultation with Book Running Lead Managers - JM Financial Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, and Nomura Financial Advisory and Securities (India) Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 68,28,001 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs 577 per equity share (face value Rs 2, share premium Rs 575)
- Total anchor book size: Approximately Rs 393.97 crores
- Number of anchor investors: 21 institutional investors
- Largest allocation: SBI Technology Opportunities Fund (30,00,111 shares - 43.94%)
- Other major allocations: ICICI Prudential Innovation Fund, Axis Multicap Fund, and Aditya Birla Sun Life Digital India Fund (6,58,600 shares each - 9.65% each)
- IPO structure: Fresh issue up to Rs 3,450 million + OFS of up to 9,228,796 shares
- Allocation date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This anchor investor allocation represents strong institutional interest in Capillary Technologies' IPO. The participation of major mutual funds including SBI, ICICI Prudential, Axis, Kotak, Aditya Birla, and international funds indicates confidence in the company's prospects.
