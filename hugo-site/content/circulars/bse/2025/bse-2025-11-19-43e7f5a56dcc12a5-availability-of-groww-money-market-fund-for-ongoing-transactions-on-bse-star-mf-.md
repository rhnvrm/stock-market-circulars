## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 20, 2025. This notice follows the earlier Notice No. 20251107-54 and covers six scheme variants including Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND reopening for ongoing transactions from November 20, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants will be available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes are categorized as DEBT funds
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- This follows previous Notice No. 20251107-54

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF666M01MZ6)
2. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF666M01NA7)
3. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF666M01NB5)

### Direct Plan Options
4. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF666M01MW3)
5. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF666M01MX1)
6. **GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF666M01MY9)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational notification regarding the resumption of trading activities for specific mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND schemes
- Ensure all six scheme variants are available for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions from November 20, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 20, 2025 - Reopening of GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors dealing with GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking to transact in GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND schemes. The reopening provides access to money market fund investment options through the BSE platform with flexibility of regular investing through SIP and systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Platform members need to ensure technical readiness to process transactions in these schemes from the effective date.
