## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for Government Security GS19DEC25 (Scrip Code: 800591, ISIN: IN001225C092) due to its maturity on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this security effective from December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Government Security GS19DEC25 to mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from December 17, 2025
- Scrip Code: 800591
- ISIN: IN001225C092
- Notice issued under reference DR-779/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with the specified Government Security from December 17, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** December 19, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only one specific government security nearing maturity

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - routine suspension procedure for maturing debt securities

**Affected Parties:** Trading members and holders of GS19DEC25 government security

**Business Continuity:** No disruption to broader debt market operations; standard maturity process
