## Summary

Aarti Industries Limited (BSE Code: 524208) responded to BSE Surveillance's inquiry regarding a significant increase in trading volume of its security across exchanges. The company confirmed that it has been promptly disclosing all material information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, and stated that there is no undisclosed information that could affect the stock's price or volume behavior. The company attributes the volume increase to market-driven factors.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance inquiry reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/667 dated November 10, 2025
- Company has been promptly disclosing all required information under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- No undisclosed material information or pending announcements exist that could impact price/volume
- Volume increase is confirmed to be market-driven
- Response authorized by Company Secretary Raj Sarraf (ICSI M. NO. A1552)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

The company confirms ongoing compliance with:
- Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Timely disclosure of material events and information to stock exchanges
- Response to stock exchange surveillance queries

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: BSE Surveillance email received at 01:39 PM
- **November 10, 2025**: Company's clarification response issued

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine clarification confirming no undisclosed material information. The response should provide clarity to market participants that the volume spike is not related to any corporate developments.

**Operational Impact**: None - No operational changes or requirements imposed on the company or market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral - Investors are informed that the trading volume increase is market-driven with no fundamental company news pending disclosure. This transparency helps maintain market integrity and investor confidence.
