## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective December 12, 2025. The listing comprises four tranches totaling 7,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 350 crores). All securities are listed under ISIN INE212K14CX3 with scrip code 730752 and redemption date of March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total of 7,000 Commercial Paper units listed (2,000 + 2,000 + 2,000 + 1,000 units across four tranches)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 350 crores
- Scrip Code: 730752
- Scrip ID: SSL111225
- ISIN: INE212K14CX3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issue prices: Rs. 4,91,954 and Rs. 4,92,026.50 (depending on tranche)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 12, 2026 (91-day maturity)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper issuance by SBICAP Securities Limited provides short-term funding for the company and offers debt market participants an investment option with high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA). The listing is relevant only to debt segment traders and investors. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations. Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE's debt department.
