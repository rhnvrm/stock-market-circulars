## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED effective December 24, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are instructed not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE01I507AD2 (Code: 939937)
- Series: KLMAFL-9.50%-08-1-26-NCD (9.50% coupon rate)
- Record date for redemption: December 24, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-784/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension implemented as per existing debenture redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures (INE01I507AD2) from December 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in the affected security from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 24, 2025**: Record date for redemption
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension ensures orderly redemption process and prevents trading complications arising from the record date determination.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in INE01I507AD2 from December 24, 2025. Investors holding these debentures will have their holdings redeemed as per the company's redemption schedule.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of December 24, 2025 will be eligible for redemption. No secondary market liquidity available from suspension date onwards.
