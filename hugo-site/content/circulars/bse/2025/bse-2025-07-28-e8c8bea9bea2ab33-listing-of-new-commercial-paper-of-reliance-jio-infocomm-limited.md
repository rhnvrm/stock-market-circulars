## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited worth Rs. 1,450 crores (29,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ ratings and will mature on October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, total 29,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,812.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 729907, Scrip ID: RJIL25725
- ISIN: INE110L14UF6
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 25, 2025
- Listing date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 24, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a large corporate entity. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The short-term nature (91 days) provides liquidity management option for institutional investors.
