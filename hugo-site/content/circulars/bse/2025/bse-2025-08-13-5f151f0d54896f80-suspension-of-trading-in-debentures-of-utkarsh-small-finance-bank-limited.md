## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of UTKARSH SMALL FINANCE BANK LIMITED effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to UTKARSH SMALL FINANCE BANK LIMITED debentures (Code: USFB-10.5777%-30-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE735W08020)
- Record date set for August 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings allowed from August 14, 2025
- Reference: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and comply with the restriction

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension begins
- **August 15, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **August 30, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (implied from code)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one company's debt securities. Routine corporate action that temporarily restricts trading to facilitate proper record-keeping for interest payment and redemption process.
