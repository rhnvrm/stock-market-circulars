## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L46498GJ2011PLC064964. The company name has been officially changed from VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED to VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED with effect from November 27, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Old Name: VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED
- New Name: VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED
- CIN: L46498GJ2011PLC064964
- Original incorporation name: VEERAM T.V. NETWORK PRIVATE LIMITED
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Sabari Raj, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Registered office: Ground & First Floor, 7, Natvarshyam Co Op Ho S Ld Opp. Orchid Park, Ramdevnagar Road, Satellite, Ahmedabad-380051, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative corporate action reflecting a name change approved under the Companies Act, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 27, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: November 27, 2025, 18:31:26 IST
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal impact on trading or operations. The name change does not affect:
- Company's CIN
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Existing contracts and obligations
- Share certificates (though reissuance may be requested)

Investors and market participants should note the new name for identification purposes. All legal and financial obligations continue unchanged under the new name.
