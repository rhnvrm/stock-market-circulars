## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE219O07362, Scrip Code: 974946) effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for payment of interest and redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE219O07362
- Scrip Code: 974946
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (GIPL-ZC-30-4-26-PVT)
- Trading suspension effective from December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action pursuant to existing debt securities regulations requiring trading suspension around record dates for interest payment and redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealings restriction
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 2, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 4, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** December 4, 2025
- **Purpose Date:** December 4, 2025 (Interest payment and redemption)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity for debenture holders of Goswami Infratech Private Limited. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade until the suspension is lifted post the corporate action.

**Investor Impact:** High - Existing debenture holders are locked in until redemption is processed. No entry or exit possible for market participants during the suspension period.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members must ensure compliance systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended security. The suspension is temporary and linked to the corporate action completion timeline.
