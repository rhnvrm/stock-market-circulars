## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of MAX MARKETING LIMITED for public comments. The document was filed on August 27, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public comments are being sought for a 21-day period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MAX MARKETING LIMITED on August 27, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard SME IPO process following existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from August 28, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: August 27, 2025
- Notice Date: August 28, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from August 28, 2025 (until September 18, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only MAX MARKETING LIMITED's IPO process. Provides opportunity for public scrutiny of the company's offer document before final approval. Standard regulatory process for SME segment listings with no broader market implications.
