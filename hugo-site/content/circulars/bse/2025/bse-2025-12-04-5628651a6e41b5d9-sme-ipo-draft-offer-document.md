## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED's SME IPO filed on December 03, 2025. The document is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED on December 03, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document hosted at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments on the Draft Offer Document should be submitted within 21 days from December 04, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED
- **December 04, 2025**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **December 25, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only potential investors and stakeholders interested in VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED's SME IPO.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to the issuer company and their IPO process. Standard procedure for SME segment listings on BSE. Public comment period allows for transparency and stakeholder input before final offer document approval.
