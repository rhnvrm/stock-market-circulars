## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth ₹100 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading in the BSE Debt segment effective August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each (20,000 units)
- Total issue size: ₹100 crore
- Issue price: ₹4,92,788 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730114, ISIN: INE929O14DZ7
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Listing date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Trading Details

- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and paying agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Contact: BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited operations.
