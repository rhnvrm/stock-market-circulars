## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ECL Finance Limited (ISIN: INE804I07H49, Code: 953154) effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading in ECL Finance Limited debentures (ECL-9.81%-22-12-25-PVT) will be suspended from December 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE804I07H49, BSE Code: 953154
- Record date set for December 8, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date
- Notice number: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension aligned with corporate action procedures for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of ECL Finance Limited from December 8, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- Debenture holders should ensure their holdings are properly recorded as of the record date to receive interest and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective From**: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, affecting only the ECL-9.81%-22-12-25-PVT series.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption. No trading will be possible from the record date, which is standard for maturing debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN from December 8, 2025. Minimal operational disruption expected as this is a routine corporate action.
