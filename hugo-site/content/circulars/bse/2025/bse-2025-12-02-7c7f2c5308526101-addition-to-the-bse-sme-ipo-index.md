## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544621) to the BSE SME IPO INDEX effective December 3, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE on December 2, 2025, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on December 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (ticker: 544621) listed on BSE on December 2, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO INDEX on December 3, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: Listing date for SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED on BSE
- **December 3, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO INDEX (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME stock to the BSE SME IPO INDEX. Index funds or investment products tracking the BSE SME IPO INDEX may need to adjust their portfolios to include this stock. The action has been included in the corporate action file for automated processing by market systems.
