## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Filtron Engineers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531191) will be moved to the 'X' group classification effective August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Filtron Engineers Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531191
- New classification: 'X' group
- Effective date: August 18, 2025
- Contact person: Ms. Sonali Kurhade (+91-8779628213)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group, which typically indicates securities with specific trading restrictions or surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this change and should adjust their systems and processes accordingly for trades in Filtron Engineers Ltd. shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 14, 2025
- Effective Date: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The move to 'X' group classification may result in increased surveillance and potentially different trading conditions for Filtron Engineers Ltd. shares, affecting liquidity and trading patterns.
