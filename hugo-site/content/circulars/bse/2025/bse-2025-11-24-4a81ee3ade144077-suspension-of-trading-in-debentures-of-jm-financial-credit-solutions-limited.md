## Summary

BSE has issued a circular suspending trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited (ISIN: INE651J07952, Code: 974918) effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to JMFCSL 8.80% debentures maturing on December 19, 2025
- ISIN: INE651J07952, BSE Code: 974918
- Record date fixed: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 4, 2025 onwards
- Circular reference: DR-770/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational measure implemented when a company sets a record date for debt security redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease all trading activities in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive
- All market participants must note the record date for redemption and interest payment purposes
- Settlement of any existing positions must be completed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension begins
- **December 19, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (based on security nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and relates to the natural lifecycle of the debt instrument approaching maturity.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will be eligible for final interest payment and redemption proceeds. No new positions can be taken from December 4, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security. The suspension ensures proper identification of eligible holders for redemption and interest payment.
