## Summary

BSE has issued a notice for suspension of trading in securities of Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) effective January 30, 2026, due to non-compliance with Regulation 34 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 for two consecutive financial years (March 2024 and March 2025). The suspension follows SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024. All promoter shareholdings and securities in their demat accounts will be frozen during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) to face trading suspension from January 30, 2026
- Non-compliance relates to Regulation 34 (annual report filing) for FY 2024 and FY 2025
- Entire promoter shareholding and all securities in promoter demat accounts will be frozen during suspension
- Company can avoid suspension by complying with all LODR provisions by January 27, 2026
- After 15 days of suspension, trade-for-trade basis trading allowed in Z group only on first trading day of each week for six months
- Revocation requires compliance with all prescribed procedures and payment of fines

## Regulatory Changes

This action is pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024, which prescribes penal actions for non-compliance with certain provisions of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 and establishes Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd:**
- Must comply with all provisions of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 to satisfaction of BSE by January 27, 2026 to avoid suspension
- Must file pending annual reports for FY 2024 and FY 2025 under Regulation 34
- Must pay applicable fines before revocation of suspension
- Must follow prescribed revocation procedure and comply with all extant norms

**For Market Participants:**
- Note that trading will be suspended from January 30, 2026 if company fails to comply
- Post-suspension, limited trading only on trade-for-trade basis in Z group on first trading day of each week
- Contact bse.soplodr@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Notice issued
- **January 27, 2026**: Last date for company to comply and avoid suspension
- **January 30, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension (if non-compliance continues)
- **15 days after January 30, 2026**: Trade-for-trade basis trading begins (first trading day of each week only for six months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Complete halt in normal trading for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd shareholders
- Severe liquidity constraints with trade-for-trade mechanism limiting exit opportunities
- Limited trading window (one day per week) significantly restricts market access

**Investor Impact:**
- Existing shareholders face inability to exit positions during suspension period
- Promoter shareholding completely frozen, preventing any transactions
- Price discovery mechanism severely impaired due to restricted trading
- Six-month restricted trading period creates prolonged uncertainty

**Company Impact:**
- Reputational damage from regulatory non-compliance
- Capital raising capabilities completely restricted
- Additional financial burden from fines and penalties
- Complex revocation process requiring full compliance and procedural adherence
