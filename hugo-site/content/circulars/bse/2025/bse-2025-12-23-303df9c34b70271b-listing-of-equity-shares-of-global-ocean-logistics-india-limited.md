## Summary

Global Ocean Logistics India Limited has listed its equity shares on BSE and filed the shareholding pattern as of December 22, 2025. The company has a total of 14,442,691 equity shares with promoter holding at 59.86% and public holding at 40.14%.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 14,442,691
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 8,645,531 shares (59.86%)
- Public shareholding: 5,797,160 shares (40.14%)
- Total number of shareholders: 539 (6 promoters + 533 public shareholders)
- Dematerialized shares: 11,652,291 (80.68% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, or locked-in shares
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure pursuant to listing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1) of SEBI LODR
- Declaration requirements regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and pledged shares
- Regular quarterly updates required going forward

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: December 22, 2025
- Circular date: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure following new listing with minimal market impact. The promoter holding of 59.86% indicates strong promoter commitment while public float of 40.14% meets minimum listing requirements. The high dematerialization rate of 80.68% facilitates trading liquidity. No encumbrances or convertible securities reduce complexity for investors.
