## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 856,872 new equity shares of Vrundavan Plantation Limited (Scrip Code: 544011) issued on preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading from Tuesday, November 18, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Total Shares Listed**: 856,872 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 51/- per share (including premium of Rs. 41/-)
- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Distribution Numbers**: 5332729 to 6189600
- **ISIN**: INE0Q6901013
- **Issuance Type**: Preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters
- **Basis**: Conversion of warrants
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI regulations for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

**Lock-in Requirements**:
- **148,800 shares** (Dist. Nos. 5332729-5481528): Locked-in until May 31, 2026
- **288,072 shares** (Dist. Nos. 5481529-5769600): Locked-in until May 30, 2027
- **420,000 shares** (Dist. Nos. 5769601-6189600): Locked-in until May 30, 2026

Trading members must ensure compliance with lock-in restrictions for the specified share categories.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**:
  - May 30, 2026 (420,000 shares)
  - May 31, 2026 (148,800 shares)
  - May 30, 2027 (288,072 shares)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting only Vrundavan Plantation Limited shareholders and does not impact broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members should update systems to reflect the new shares and associated lock-in restrictions. The preferential allotment represents capital raising activity by the company through warrant conversion, increasing the total equity base by 856,872 shares at Rs. 51/- per share.
