## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on its Debt segment effective November 24, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with 40,000 units carrying a 12% per annum interest rate and maturing on May 21, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Listed**: 40,000 units
- **Scrip Code**: 977309
- **Scrip ID**: 12ACAPL27B
- **ISIN**: INE08XP07423
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 12% per annum (paid monthly)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: May 21, 2027
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XP07423
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Redemption Date**: May 21, 2027
