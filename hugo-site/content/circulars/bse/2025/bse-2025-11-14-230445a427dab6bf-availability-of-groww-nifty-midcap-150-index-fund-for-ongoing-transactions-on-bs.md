## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 17, 2025. This follows the previous notice 20251027-42 and includes facilities for SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- GROWW NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from November 17, 2025
- Includes subscription and redemption facilities
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Six plan options available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All plans are categorized under EQUITY scheme category

## Scheme Details

The following six plan options are available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MS1)
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MT9)
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01MU7)
4. **Direct Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF666M01MP7)
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF666M01MQ5)
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF666M01MR3)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transactions for a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice regarding the availability of mutual fund transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption with SIP, STP & SWP facility

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational reopening of a single mutual fund scheme.
