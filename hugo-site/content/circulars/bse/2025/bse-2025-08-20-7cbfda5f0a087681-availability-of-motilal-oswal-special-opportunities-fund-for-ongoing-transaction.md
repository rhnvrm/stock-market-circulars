## Summary

MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities across six different plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective August 21, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Offers subscription and redemption facilities
- Includes SIP, STP & SWP facility
- Six scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY

## Available Schemes

1. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH (INF247L01FV0)
2. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (INF247L01FW8)
3. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (INF247L01FX6)
4. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (INF247L01FS6)
5. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (INF247L01FT4)
6. MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (INF247L01FU2)

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 21, 2025
- Notice Date: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice regarding mutual fund platform availability. The reopening provides investors with additional investment options through BSE's mutual fund platform but has minimal direct impact on equity markets or trading operations.
