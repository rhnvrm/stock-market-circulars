## Summary

NACL Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 524709) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with December 12, 2025 as the record date. The company will issue 5 equity shares of Re. 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share (total issue price Rs. 76.70) for every 31 equity shares held by existing shareholders. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 5 new equity shares for every 31 existing shares held
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 75.70 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 76.70 per share (payable on application)
- Record date: December 12, 2025
- Ex-rights date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialised securities - Rolling settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in NACL Industries shares are conducted on ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025
- Members should note the record date and settlement details for proper execution of trades
- Shareholders holding shares as on record date will be eligible to participate in the rights issue

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders as on record date will receive rights entitlements to subscribe to new shares at Rs. 76.70 per share. This provides an opportunity to maintain proportional ownership or monetize rights entitlements.

**Trading Impact**: Share price will be adjusted for ex-rights from December 12, 2025. Trading members and investors should account for the rights factor in pricing and order placement.

**Dilution**: The rights issue will result in equity dilution for shareholders who do not participate, as the equity base will expand by approximately 16.13% (5/31 ratio).

**Liquidity**: Rights entitlements trading may increase market activity in the stock during the rights issue period.
