## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED (Scrip Code 975937) effective from November 21, 2025. This follows the company's earlier notice regarding a put option exercise opportunity with a record date of November 4, 2025. Since debenture holders did not exercise the put option, trading will resume as normal.

## Key Points

- Trading in ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures (Scrip Code 975937) was previously suspended for put option exercise
- Record date for redemption on exercise of put option was November 4, 2025
- Debenture holders chose not to exercise the put option
- Debenture holders requested resumption of trading
- Trading will resume on November 21, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the resumption of trading in specific debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note that the debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED having Scrip Code 975937 will be available for trading from November 21, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **October 30, 2025**: Initial exchange notice (No. 20251030-60) regarding put option
- **November 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption on exercise of put option
- **November 20, 2025**: Notice issued for resumption of trading
- **November 21, 2025**: Trading resumes in ARKA FINCAP debentures (Scrip Code 975937)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it affects only the specific debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED with Scrip Code 975937. The decision by debenture holders not to exercise the put option indicates their preference to continue holding the instruments. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should update their systems to reflect the availability of these debentures for trading from November 21, 2025. The resumption allows normal market operations and liquidity for these specific debt securities.
