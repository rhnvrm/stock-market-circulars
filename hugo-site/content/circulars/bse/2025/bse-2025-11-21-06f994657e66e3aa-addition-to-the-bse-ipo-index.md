## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Capillary Technologies India Ltd (Exchange ticker: 544614) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on November 21, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on November 25, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- Capillary Technologies India Ltd (ticker: 544614) listed on BSE on Friday, November 21, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective Tuesday, November 25, 2025 at market open
- Addition is referenced in Notice No: 20251120-42
- Corporate action included in tonight's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index tracking funds and passive investment vehicles benchmarked to BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to include the new constituent
- No compliance requirements for other market participants

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Listing date for Capillary Technologies India Ltd on BSE
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine index addition following a new IPO listing. 

**Index Impact**: The BSE IPO Index will include one additional constituent. Index funds tracking this index will need to purchase shares of Capillary Technologies India Ltd.

**Trading Impact**: May result in incremental buying activity from passive funds on or before the effective date. Limited impact on broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for investors tracking or investing in the BSE IPO Index and for shareholders of Capillary Technologies India Ltd.
