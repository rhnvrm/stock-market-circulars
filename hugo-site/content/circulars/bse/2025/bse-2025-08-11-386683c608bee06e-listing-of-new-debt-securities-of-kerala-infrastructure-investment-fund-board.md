## Summary

BSE has listed seven new debt securities issued by Kerala Infrastructure Investment Fund Board (KIIFB) on private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025. All securities carry a 9.67% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each.

## Key Points

- Seven debt securities listed with scrip codes 977006 to 977012
- All securities have identical terms: 9.67% interest rate, Rs. 1,00,000 face value
- Maturity periods range from August 8, 2029 to August 8, 2035
- Each security has 21,429 quantity with market lot of 1
- Credit rating: Acuite AA(CE), INDIA AA(CE)
- Date of allotment: August 8, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 8, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: August 11, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**: August 8, 2029 to August 8, 2035 (staggered)
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of infrastructure debt securities. The securities provide institutional investors with AA-rated investment options in Kerala's infrastructure development projects. The staggered maturity structure from 2029-2035 offers varied investment horizons for debt market participants.
