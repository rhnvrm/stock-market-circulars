## Summary

MIRAE ASSET MUTUAL FUND is launching MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND as a New Fund Offering (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be available from November 17, 2025 to December 01, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 05, 2025. Six plan variants are being offered including regular and direct plans with growth, IDCW payout, and IDCW reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes categorized as EQUITY with unique ISINs ranging from INF769K01PS0 to INF769K01PW2
- NFO extended till midnight on December 01, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 04, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to fund clearance deadline
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by 3:00 PM on NFO closure date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification for a new mutual fund offering.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds are received by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Transactions with client-initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 04, 2025 for unit allotment
- Switch transactions must receive funds on or before allotment date; late funds will not be considered for allotment
- SIP/XSIP registrations require client authentication before 3:00 PM on December 01, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 01, 2025**: NFO closure date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **December 01, 2025, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **December 04, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **December 05, 2025**: Tentative allotment date for all scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

This NFO launch has minimal market impact as it is a routine mutual fund offering announcement. The impact is limited to:

- **Platform Impact**: Operational update for BSE StAR MF Platform members and participants
- **Investor Access**: Provides investment opportunity in infrastructure-focused equity fund through both regular and direct plans
- **Operational Impact**: Members need to coordinate payment timelines and authentication processes for successful NFO participation
- **Market Segment**: Affects mutual fund distribution segment only, with no broader equity market implications
