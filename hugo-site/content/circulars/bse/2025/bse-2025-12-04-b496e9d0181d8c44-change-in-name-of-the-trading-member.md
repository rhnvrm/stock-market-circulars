## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 91) to "ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED" effective from Friday, December 5, 2025. This is an administrative update with no operational implications.

## Key Points

- Trading member ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. will be renamed to ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED
- Member Number remains unchanged: 91
- Effective date: Friday, December 5, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the name change in their records. No specific compliance action required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is an administrative name change affecting only the identification of one trading member. No impact on trading operations, client accounts, or market activities. Other trading members are informed for record-keeping purposes only.
