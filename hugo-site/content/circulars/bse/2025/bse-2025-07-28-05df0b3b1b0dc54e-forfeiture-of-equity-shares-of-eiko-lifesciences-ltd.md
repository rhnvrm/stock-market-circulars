## Summary

Eiko Lifesciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 540204) has forfeited 1,39,591 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to shareholders' failure to pay required allotment or call monies.

## Key Points

- Company: Eiko Lifesciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 540204)
- Number of shares forfeited: 1,39,591 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 13720855 to 13860445
- Shares were partly paid-up before forfeiture

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action under existing share forfeiture provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for record-keeping and trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 28 July 2025
- Effective Date: Immediate

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only existing shareholders who failed to meet payment obligations. The forfeited shares reduce the company's issued capital and may affect shareholding patterns.
