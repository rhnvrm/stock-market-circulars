## Summary

BSE has listed 2,24,39,360 fully paid equity shares of K K Silk Mills Limited. The shares are held in dematerialized form with NSDL as the depository. The listing includes securities with different lock-in periods ranging from December 8, 2026 to December 28, 2028, and some securities that are free from lock-in.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 2,24,39,360 fully paid equity shares
- All shares are in dematerialized form with NSDL
- Four tranches with different lock-in periods
- First tranche: 61,56,860 shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 61,56,860) - Lock-in until December 8, 2026
- Second tranche: 42,82,500 shares (distinctive numbers 61,56,861 to 1,04,39,360) - Lock-in until December 8, 2027
- Third tranche: 45,00,000 shares (distinctive numbers 1,04,39,361 to 1,49,39,360) - Lock-in until December 8, 2028
- Fourth tranche: 75,00,000 shares (distinctive numbers 1,49,39,361 to 2,24,39,360) - Free from lock-in
- Listing date: November 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. The company must adhere to standard listing obligations and lock-in provisions as specified.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 1)**: December 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 2)**: December 8, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 3)**: December 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of equity shares for K K Silk Mills Limited. The staggered lock-in periods (2026, 2027, and 2028) will ensure gradual release of locked-in shares into the market, preventing sudden supply shocks. The 75 lakh shares that are free from lock-in are immediately available for trading. Investors should note the lock-in restrictions when considering these securities.
