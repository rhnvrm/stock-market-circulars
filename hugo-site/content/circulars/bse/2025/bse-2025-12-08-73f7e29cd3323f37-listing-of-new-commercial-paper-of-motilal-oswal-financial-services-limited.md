## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new Commercial Paper securities issued by Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 8, 2025. The issuance consists of 3,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 3,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 729774
- **Scrip ID**: MOFSL30725
- **ISIN**: INE338I14KT1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE338I14KT1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 6, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The listing provides additional trading options for debt market participants interested in highly-rated commercial papers.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade MOFSL commercial papers with the specified parameters. The high credit ratings (ICRA A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.

**Investor Impact**: Institutional and qualified investors in the debt segment gain access to short-term investment opportunities in MOFSL commercial papers with a tenure of approximately 3 months and competitive credit ratings.
