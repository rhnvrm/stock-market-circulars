## Summary

Aarti Industries Limited (BSE Code: 524208) submitted a clarification to BSE Surveillance on November 10, 2025, in response to an exchange query regarding significant increase in trading volume of the company's security across exchanges. The company confirmed that it has been promptly disclosing all material information as required under SEBI LODR Regulation 30, and stated there is no undisclosed information that may affect price or volume behavior. The company attributed the volume increase to market-driven factors.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification via email (Ref. No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/667) on November 10, 2025 at 1:39 PM regarding increased trading volume
- Company confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No undisclosed information or pending announcements that could impact price or volume behavior
- Volume increase confirmed as absolutely market-driven
- Clarification signed by Raj Sarraf, Company Secretary (ICSI M. No. A1552)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The circular reaffirms the company's ongoing compliance with:
- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015 regarding timely disclosure of material events
- Standard disclosure obligations to stock exchanges

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Date of BSE surveillance query (received at 1:39 PM)
- **November 10, 2025**: Date of company's clarification response

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine surveillance clarification with no material information disclosed. The confirmation that volume increase is market-driven and not based on undisclosed information should provide transparency to market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral. Investors are informed that the recent trading activity is not due to any corporate developments that haven't been publicly disclosed.

**Regulatory Impact**: None. Standard surveillance procedure with satisfactory response from the company confirming compliance with disclosure norms.
