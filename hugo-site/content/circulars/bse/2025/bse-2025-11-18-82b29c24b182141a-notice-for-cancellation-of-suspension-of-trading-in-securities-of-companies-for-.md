## Summary

BSE has announced the cancellation of trading suspension for two companies - Retro Green Revolution Ltd (Scrip Code: 519191) and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291). These companies were previously suspended for non-compliance with provisions of SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018. The suspension will be lifted effective November 20, 2025, allowing normal trading to resume.

## Key Points

- Two companies will be removed from suspension list: Retro Green Revolution Ltd and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd
- Suspension cancellation is in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19 dated October 20, 2025
- Companies were suspended for non-compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018
- All other provisions of the original notice remain unchanged
- Market participants can contact bse.soplodr@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

This notice reverses the trading suspension previously imposed on the two companies, indicating they have rectified their non-compliance issues with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018. The lifting of suspension restores normal trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the change in trading status for the affected securities
- Normal trading operations can resume for these securities from the effective date
- All other compliance requirements from the original suspension notice remain applicable to other affected entities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Suspension Cancellation Effective Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19 dated October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact for Affected Companies**: The cancellation of suspension is a positive development for Retro Green Revolution Ltd and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd, as it restores investor confidence and allows shareholders to trade their securities freely.

**Limited Broader Market Impact**: The impact is limited to shareholders and investors of these two specific companies. The broader market will not be significantly affected.

**Compliance Signal**: The lifting of suspension indicates that both companies have successfully addressed their non-compliance issues, demonstrating improved regulatory adherence.
