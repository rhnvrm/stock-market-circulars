## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of SPANDANA SPHOORTY FINANCIAL LIMITED with ISIN INE572J07513 (Scrip Code: 974502) effective December 15, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for payment of interest and redemption of the debentures.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: SSFL-11.35%-30-12-25-PVT (INE572J07513)
- Scrip Code: 974502
- Interest rate: 11.35%
- Record date: December 15, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-777/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard suspension procedure for debentures undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 15, 2025
- Trading members are advised to note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No new positions should be created in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 30, 2025 (as indicated by security name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension affects liquidity for these securities but is a routine corporate action.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders identified as on the record date will receive the final interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading activity permitted from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security and communicate the suspension to clients holding or tracking these debentures.
