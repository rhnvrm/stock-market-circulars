## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by J.K. Cement Limited on private placement basis, effective December 3, 2025. The instrument consists of 1,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, and carries a CARE A1+ credit rating. IDBI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730690
- **Scrip ID**: JKCL021225
- **ISIN**: INE823G14BB3
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,710.50 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 1,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+
- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE823G14BB3
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that affects only participants in the BSE debt segment who trade short-term debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Debt segment traders can now trade J.K. Cement's commercial paper with specified parameters.

**Issuer Impact**: Provides J.K. Cement Limited with short-term funding through the commercial paper route, demonstrating strong credit quality with A1+ rating.
