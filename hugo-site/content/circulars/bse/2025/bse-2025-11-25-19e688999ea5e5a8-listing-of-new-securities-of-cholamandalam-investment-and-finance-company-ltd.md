## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 22,42,436 equity shares of Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Ltd (Scrip Code: 511243, ISIN: INE121A01024) with effect from November 26, 2025. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Placements (QIPs) pursuant to the conversion of warrants issued on a QIP basis.

## Key Points

- 22,42,436 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each listed and admitted for trading
- Shares allotted to QIPs following warrant conversion
- Scrip Code: 511243
- ISIN: INE121A01024
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issue price: Rs. 1,369.05 per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 841399033 to 843641468

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing procedure for shares issued under QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities for trading purposes on the BSE platform.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 8, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Notice Date:** November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine QIP conversion and listing with minimal dilution impact.

**Operational Impact:** Standard listing procedure requiring trading members to update their systems for the new shares. The shares are fungible with existing equity shares and trade under the same scrip code.

**Shareholder Impact:** Minimal dilution from warrant conversion completed at premium pricing (Rs. 1,369.05), indicating positive investor sentiment.
