## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the Scrip ID for DSM Fresh Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 544568) in the BOLT Plus trading system. The Scrip ID will be changed from 'DSM' to 'ZAPPFRESH' effective October 31, 2025, following a request from the company.

## Key Points

- Company: DSM Fresh Foods Ltd (Scrip Code: 544568)
- Old Scrip ID: DSM
- New Scrip ID: ZAPPFRESH
- Change applies to BOLT Plus trading system only
- Effective Date: October 31, 2025
- Trading members must update their systems accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading system identifier at the company's request.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the Scrip ID change
- Update internal systems and references to reflect the new Scrip ID 'ZAPPFRESH'
- Ensure trading systems are configured for the new identifier before October 31, 2025
- Contact BSE Listing Operations for clarifications:
  - Mr. Tushar Shetty: +91 22 2272 8634
  - Ms. Asmita Chavan: +91 22 2272 8266
  - Email: Listing.crd@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 31, 2025**: Effective date for Scrip ID change

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a technical change to the trading symbol identifier and does not affect the underlying security, trading operations, or investor holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new Scrip ID 'ZAPPFRESH' instead of 'DSM'. The scrip code (544568) remains unchanged, minimizing disruption to automated systems that may rely on the numeric identifier.
