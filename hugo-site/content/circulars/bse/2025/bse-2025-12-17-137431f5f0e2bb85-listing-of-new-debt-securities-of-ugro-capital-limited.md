## Summary

UGRO Capital Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 150,000 units at face value of Rs. 10,000 each, representing a total value of Rs. 150 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 9.99% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on June 16, 2029. The securities are rated IND A+ and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: 150,000 units (Rs. 150 crore)
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 977382, Scrip ID: 999UCL29
- ISIN: INE583D07638
- Credit rating: IND A+
- Interest rate: 9.99% per annum (monthly payment)
- Allotment date: December 16, 2025
- Maturity date: June 16, 2029
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D07638
- Trading members must apply the tick size of 1 paise for these securities
- Members requiring clarifications should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Detailed information available in the Placement Memorandum on BSE website

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: January 16, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly from January 16, 2026 to June 16, 2029
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: June 16, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt securities listing on private placement basis with limited market-wide implications. The listing expands fixed-income investment options for debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for investors in debt securities and those tracking UGRO Capital's debt instruments. The 9.99% interest rate with IND A+ rating provides a specific risk-return profile for fixed-income investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details and ensure compliance with trading parameters (tick size, dematerialized trading requirement).
