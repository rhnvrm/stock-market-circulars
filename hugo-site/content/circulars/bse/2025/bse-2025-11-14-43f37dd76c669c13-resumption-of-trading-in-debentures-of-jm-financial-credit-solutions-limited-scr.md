## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited (Scrip Code 974918) effective November 17, 2025. This follows a partial redemption wherein 5,000 debentures out of 10,000 were redeemed through buyback at investor request, while 5,000 debentures remain outstanding and will continue trading.

## Key Points

- Trading in JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited debentures (Scrip Code 974918) will resume from November 17, 2025
- Partial redemption completed: 5,000 debentures out of 10,000 total redeemed through buyback
- 5,000 debentures remain unredeemed and will be available for trading
- Record date for the buyback was November 8, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-757/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a resumption of trading following a corporate action (partial buyback redemption).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the resumption of trading for Scrip Code 974918
- Trading members should be aware that only 5,000 debentures remain outstanding after the partial redemption

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Original exchange notice issued regarding record date
- **November 8, 2025**: Record date for buyback redemption
- **November 14, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption announcement
- **November 17, 2025**: Trading resumes in the debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The partial redemption reduces the outstanding debentures by 50%, which may affect liquidity in this instrument. The resumption allows remaining debenture holders to trade their securities.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced outstanding amount and prepare for trading resumption. The security will be available for trading after a suspension period related to the corporate action.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding the unredeemed 5,000 debentures can resume trading, while those whose debentures were redeemed received their redemption proceeds.
