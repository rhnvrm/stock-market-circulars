## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited on private placement basis effective November 17, 2025. The issue consists of 3,000 units of Rs.5,00,000 each (total Rs.150 crore) with CRISIL A1+ rating, maturing on February 12, 2026. ICICI Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs.5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 3,000 units (Rs.150 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730579
- Scrip ID: ABLB141125
- ISIN: INE14LE14046
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs.4,92,531 per unit
- Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE14LE14046
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective November 17, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing announcement affecting only the debt segment. The instrument has a short tenure of approximately 3 months and is rated CRISIL A1+, indicating high credit quality. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade this instrument on BSE. For clarifications, trading members can contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
