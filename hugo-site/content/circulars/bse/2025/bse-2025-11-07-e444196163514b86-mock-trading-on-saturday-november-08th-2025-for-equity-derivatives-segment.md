## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The session will cover various functionalities including exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity Derivatives segment only
- Session date: Saturday, November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Opportunity to test third-party platforms, empaneled vendor systems, and in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will include exceptional market conditions (risk-reduction mode, trading halt, etc.)
- Trades from mock session will not create any obligations or liabilities
- No margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations will arise
- Feedback requested from all participants by 5:00 PM on the session day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback for the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Feedback should be submitted via the contact numbers/email addresses provided

## Important Dates

**Saturday, November 08, 2025 - Mock Trading Session Schedule:**
- **Login:** 11:15 AM to 12:15 PM
- **Continuous Trading:** 12:15 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Trade Modification:** 04:00 PM to 04:15 PM
- **Feedback Deadline:** 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a testing session only with no real trading obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Optional participation for trading members to test and familiarize themselves with their trading systems and various market condition scenarios.

**Member Impact:** The session provides a risk-free environment for members to:
- Test trading application functionality
- Familiarize themselves with exceptional market conditions
- Validate third-party and in-house trading systems
- Practice response to risk-reduction modes and trading halts
