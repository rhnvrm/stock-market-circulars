## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD (ISIN: INE07HK07734, Code: 975736) effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- Debenture: KSL-12.5%-12-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE07HK07734
- Code: 975736
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure linked to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from November 27, 2025
- Trading Members must ensure no transactions are executed in the affected security (ISIN: INE07HK07734)
- Members must take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record Date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 27, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings allowed)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium - affects only holders of specific KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature, linked to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent any transactions in the affected debenture from November 27, 2025. Investors holding these debentures should be aware that they cannot trade during the suspension period. The suspension facilitates the orderly processing of the redemption and interest payment to eligible holders as of the record date.
