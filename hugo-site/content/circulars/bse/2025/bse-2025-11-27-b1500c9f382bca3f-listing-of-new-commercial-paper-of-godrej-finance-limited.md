## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The issue comprises 6,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, with ISIN INE02KN14655, rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities will be redeemed on February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730665, Scrip ID: GFL271125
- ISIN: INE02KN14655
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,725
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: November 27, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14655
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with immediate effect

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Date of allotment and listing effective date
- **February 26, 2026**: Date of redemption

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt financing for Godrej Finance Limited with a 91-day tenure. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. The total issue size is Rs. 300 crores (6,000 x Rs. 5 lakhs). This listing is relevant primarily for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper instruments.
