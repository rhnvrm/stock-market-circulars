## Summary

BSE announces that NAVI NIFTY MIDSMALLCAP 400 INDEX FUND will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 11, 2025. This reopening follows the previous notice (20251121-66) and includes both Regular and Direct plan variants with full SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions after temporary closure
- Available for both subscription and redemption
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- Regular Plan ISIN: INF959L01HS2
- Direct Plan ISIN: INF959L01HR4
- Both plans classified under EQUITY scheme category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE should note the availability of this fund for processing investor transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Reference Notice**: 20251121-66 (previous closure notice)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to a single mutual fund scheme's operational status. The reopening provides investors access to invest in or redeem from the NAVI NIFTY MIDSMALLCAP 400 INDEX FUND, which tracks the Nifty Midsmallcap 400 index. Impact is limited to existing and prospective investors of this specific fund who can now resume regular investment activities including systematic plans.
