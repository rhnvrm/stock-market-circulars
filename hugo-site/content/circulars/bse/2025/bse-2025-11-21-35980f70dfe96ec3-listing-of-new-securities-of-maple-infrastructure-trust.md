## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 12,06,03,200 new units of Maple Infrastructure Trust (Scrip Code: 543925) issued on a preferential basis to Sponsor and Non-Sponsor entities at Rs. 145.60 per unit. The units will commence trading on November 24, 2025, and are ranking pari-passu with existing units. The allotment includes lock-in restrictions with 1.36 crore units locked until November 23, 2028, and 10.70 crore units locked until November 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total units issued: 12,06,03,200 units
- Issue price: Rs. 145.60 per unit
- Allotment date: November 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 24, 2025
- ISIN: INE0M5S23019
- Distinctive Numbers: 352170001 to 472773200
- Units rank pari-passu with existing units
- Preferential allotment to Sponsor and Non-Sponsor entities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the lock-in restrictions on specific units
- 1,35,67,860 units (Dist. Nos. 352170001 to 365737860) are locked-in until November 23, 2028
- 10,70,35,340 units (Dist. Nos. 365737861 to 472773200) are locked-in until November 23, 2026
- These units cannot be traded until their respective lock-in periods expire

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **November 24, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **November 23, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 10.70 crore units
- **November 23, 2028**: Lock-in expiry for 1.36 crore units

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event with limited market impact. The preferential allotment increases the outstanding units of Maple Infrastructure Trust by 12.06 crore units. The staggered lock-in periods (2026 and 2028) will control the supply of tradable units in the market. Investors should note the substantial lock-in periods which indicate long-term commitment by allottees. The preferential pricing at Rs. 145.60 provides a reference point for market participants.
