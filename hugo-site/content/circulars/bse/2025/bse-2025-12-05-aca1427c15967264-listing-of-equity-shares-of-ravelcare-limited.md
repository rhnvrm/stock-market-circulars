## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Ravelcare Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544629, symbol RAVEL, and ISIN number INE0W4D01018. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Ravelcare Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544629
- Trading Symbol: RAVEL
- ISIN Number: INE0W4D01018
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the company details for future trading purposes once the listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The circular primarily serves as an informational notice to trading members about an upcoming SME listing. Impact is limited to:

- Potential investors interested in the SME segment
- Trading members who need to update their systems with the new scrip details
- Existing stakeholders of Ravelcare Limited

The announcement does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations.
