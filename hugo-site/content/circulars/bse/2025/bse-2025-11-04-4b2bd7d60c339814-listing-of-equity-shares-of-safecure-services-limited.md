## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Safecure Services Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544596, symbol SAFECURE, and ISIN INE0SVZ01015. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Safecure Services Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544596
- Trading Symbol: SAFECURE
- ISIN: INE0SVZ01015
- Listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the listing date and security specifications before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. Impact is limited to trading members who will need to update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is confirmed. No immediate action required until the separate listing notice is issued with specific dates and security details.
