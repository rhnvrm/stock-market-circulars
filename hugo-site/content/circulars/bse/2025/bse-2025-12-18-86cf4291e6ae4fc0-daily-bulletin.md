## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 179/2025-2026 dated December 18, 2025, announces book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin covers bonus issues, stock splits, commercial paper redemptions, and non-convertible debenture interest payments and redemptions across multiple companies including A-1 Limited, Adani Enterprises, Avigna Housing, and Aye Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- A-1 Limited: Record date December 31, 2025 for bonus issue and stock split
- Adani Enterprises: Commercial paper redemptions scheduled for December 23 and 24, 2025
- Avigna Housing Private Limited: Interest payment record date December 20, 2025
- Aye Finance Limited: Multiple NCD interest payment and redemption dates spanning January to March 2026
- All securities have trading flag set to 1 on respective record dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the record dates for determining eligible shareholders/debenture holders
- Securities will trade ex-entitlement basis one trading day before the record date
- Investors must hold securities in demat form as of record date to be eligible for corporate actions

## Important Dates

- **December 20, 2025**: Avigna Housing - Interest payment record date
- **December 23, 2025**: Adani Enterprises - CP redemption
- **December 24, 2025**: Adani Enterprises - CP redemption
- **December 31, 2025**: A-1 Limited - Bonus issue and stock split record date
- **January 2, 10, 13, 17, 23, 2026**: Aye Finance - Various NCD interest/redemption dates
- **February 2, 13, 21, 27, 2026**: Aye Finance - Various NCD interest/redemption dates
- **March 2, 5, 23, 2026**: Aye Finance - Various NCD interest/redemption dates

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects investors holding specific securities listed in the bulletin. The bonus issue and stock split for A-1 Limited may impact liquidity and trading patterns for that stock.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - routine corporate action processing for brokers and depositories. Multiple NCDs from Aye Finance require tracking of various interest payment and redemption schedules.

**Investor Impact**: High for affected security holders who must ensure their holdings are properly recorded by the respective record dates to receive entitlements.
