## Summary

BSE has announced a revision in the constituents of the BSE BANKEX index, effective from December 26, 2025. This change follows SEBI Circular No. HO/47/15/11(1)2025-MRD-TPD1/I/63/2025 dated October 30, 2025, and is in continuation of previous exchange notices 20251119-37 and 20251121-50.

## Key Points

- Notice Number: 20251125-55
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Segment: Derivatives
- Category: Trading
- Regulatory Reference: SEBI Circular No. HO/47/15/11(1)2025-MRD-TPD1/I/63/2025 dated October 30, 2025
- Previous Notices: 20251119-37 and 20251121-50

## Regulatory Changes

This revision is implemented pursuant to SEBI guidelines on index constituent changes, ensuring compliance with updated regulatory frameworks for index management and derivative trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants trading in BSE BANKEX derivatives should note the change in index composition
- Portfolio managers and fund houses tracking the BANKEX index need to rebalance their holdings accordingly
- Derivative traders should adjust their positions considering the new constituent stocks from December 26, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 26, 2025
- **SEBI Circular Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Previous Notices**: November 19, 2025 (20251119-37) and November 21, 2025 (20251121-50)

## Impact Assessment

The reconstitution of BSE BANKEX will impact:

- **Derivative Markets**: Existing derivative contracts based on the index may need adjustments
- **Index Funds**: Passive funds tracking BANKEX will need to rebalance portfolios
- **Trading Strategy**: Market participants using BANKEX for hedging or speculation should review positions
- **Liquidity**: Stocks being added may see increased liquidity, while removed stocks may experience reduced trading activity
- **Banking Sector Representation**: The revised constituents will better reflect the current banking sector landscape
