## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L62099MH1993PLC074167. The company name has been officially changed from VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED to IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED with effect from October 1, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED.

## Key Points

- Previous name: VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED
- New name: IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L62099MH1993PLC074167
- Effective date of name change: October 1, 2025
- Original incorporation name: GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered address: Z1-3092, Akshar Business Park, Sector 25, Vashi, Navi Mumbai, Sanpada, Thane-400703, Maharashtra, India
- Company type: Company limited by shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a certificate confirming the administrative change of company name in accordance with the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED) along with the new name (IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **October 1, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 1, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name
- **November 17, 2025**: Circular notification date (BSE)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or stakeholder rights and liabilities. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for identification purposes. The requirement to display both old and new names for two years ensures continuity and prevents confusion during the transition period.
