## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544600, symbol LENSKART, and ISIN number INE956O01016. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Lenskart Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544600
- Trading Symbol: LENSKART
- ISIN Number: INE956O01016
- Listing announcement made on November 7, 2025
- Exact listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to prepare for trading in these securities
- Members should await separate notice for exact listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Lenskart Solutions Limited, a well-known eyewear retail brand, will become available for trading once the listing is formalized. Trading members should prepare their systems and inform clients about this upcoming opportunity. The actual market impact will depend on the listing date, offering size, and price band, which will be communicated in a subsequent circular.
