## Summary

BSE has announced that KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 04, 2025. This follows the previous suspension notice dated November 04, 2025 (Notice No. 20251104-57).

## Key Points

- KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND reopening for all transaction types effective December 04, 2025
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes are classified under Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Both subscription and redemption transactions permitted

## Scheme Details

**Available Plans:**

1. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XZ1)
2. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174KA1YA2)
3. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174KA1YB0)
4. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XW8)
5. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174KA1XX6)
6. KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174KA1XY4)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of trading in a previously suspended mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and investors can resume transactions in the specified fund schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform
- All standard BSE StAR MF Platform transaction procedures and compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 04, 2025 - Reopening for ongoing transactions
- **Reference Notice:** 20251104-57 (previous suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single mutual fund scheme. The impact is limited to:

- **Investors:** Existing and prospective investors in KOTAK RURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND can now resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transactions
- **Distributors/Brokers:** BSE StAR MF Platform members can facilitate transactions in this fund
- **Market Impact:** Negligible broader market impact; routine operational matter for mutual fund trading platform

The reopening provides liquidity and transaction flexibility to fund investors after the temporary suspension.
