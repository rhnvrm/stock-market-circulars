## Summary

BSE has announced changes in group classification for equity shares of Investment Companies (ICs) and Investment Holding Companies (IHCs). Vardhman Holdings Limited will be transferred from trade-for-trade segment to Group B effective November 20, 2025. Four other companies will continue trading in trade-for-trade segment with call auction until further notice.

## Key Points

- Vardhman Holdings Limited (Scrip Code: 500439) to be moved from trade-for-trade to Group B
- Effective date for Vardhman Holdings group change: November 20, 2025
- Four companies to remain in trade-for-trade segment with call auction: Kama Holdings Limited, Elcid Investments Limited, Haryana Capfin Limited, and Easy Fincorp Limited
- This notice continues previous communications dated November 3, 2025 and October 15, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The exchange is implementing differential treatment for investment companies based on their trading characteristics. Companies meeting certain criteria are being moved from restrictive trade-for-trade segments to regular trading groups, while others remain under enhanced surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for Vardhman Holdings Limited effective November 20, 2025
- Members must continue treating the four specified companies under trade-for-trade with call auction rules
- Members should contact Shyam Bhagirath (022-22728013) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 6, 2025
- **Effective Date for Group Change:** November 20, 2025 (Vardhman Holdings Limited)
- **Trade-for-Trade Continuation:** Until further notice (for the four specified companies)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The reclassification of Vardhman Holdings to Group B will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for that stock. The continued trade-for-trade status for the other four companies maintains existing restrictions on their trading.

**Companies Affected:**
1. **Moving to Group B:** Vardhman Holdings Limited (500439)
2. **Remaining in Trade-for-Trade:**
   - Kama Holdings Limited (532468) - Group T
   - Elcid Investments Limited (503681) - Group T
   - Haryana Capfin Limited (532855) - Group XT
   - Easy Fincorp Limited (511074) - Group Z

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems and processes to reflect the group change for Vardhman Holdings by November 20, 2025.
