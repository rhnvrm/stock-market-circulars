## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kogta Financial (India) Limited on private placement basis effective November 3, 2025. The securities have a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, carry an interest rate of 9.5% per annum, and mature on October 30, 2027. The issue is rated CARE A+/Positive and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977263
- **Scrip ID**: 95KFIL27
- **ISIN**: INE192U07376
- **Quantity**: 10,500 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A+/Positive
- **Interest Rate**: 9.5% per annum (paid annually)
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE192U07376
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for complete details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on October 30 (2026, 2027)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement debt listing with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are restricted to institutional and qualified investors given the high face value of Rs. 1 lakh per unit.

**Credit Quality**: The CARE A+/Positive rating indicates adequate safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations, with positive outlook suggesting potential for upgrade.

For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at the provided phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
