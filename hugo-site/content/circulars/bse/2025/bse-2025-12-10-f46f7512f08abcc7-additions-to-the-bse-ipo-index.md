## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, December 12, 2025. The companies being added are MEESHO LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544632), VIDYA WIRES LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544633), and AEQUS LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544634), all of which were listed on BSE on Wednesday, December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three companies will be added to the BSE IPO Index on December 12, 2025
- MEESHO LIMITED (ticker: 544632) - listed on December 10, 2025
- VIDYA WIRES LIMITED (ticker: 544633) - listed on December 10, 2025
- AEQUS LIMITED (ticker: 544634) - listed on December 10, 2025
- Changes are effective at market open on Friday, December 12, 2025
- Corporate action details included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- For queries, contact bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index rebalancing activity following new listings on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index constituent changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The additions reflect the standard process of including newly listed companies in the BSE IPO Index. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to include these three stocks. The action may result in modest buying activity from passive index trackers but is not expected to significantly impact broader market operations or the individual stocks' trading patterns.
