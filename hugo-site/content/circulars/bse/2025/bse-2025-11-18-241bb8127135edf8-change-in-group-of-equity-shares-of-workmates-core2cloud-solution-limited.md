## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544610) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, with dealings shifted to M Group. This change follows a previous notice dated November 17, 2025 (Notice No. 20251117-60) and will take effect from Monday, December 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544610
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: December 01, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20251118-4
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment represents a relaxation of trading restrictions. Trade for Trade segment requires compulsory delivery of shares and does not allow intraday trading, while Rolling segment permits normal T+2 settlement with intraday trading allowed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the segment change effective December 01, 2025
- All dealings in the equity shares will be conducted under M Group from the effective date
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- Previous Notice Date: November 17, 2025 (Notice No. 20251117-60)
- Current Notice Date: November 18, 2025
- Effective Date of Change: December 01, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact**: The migration from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment is generally favorable for shareholders as it:
- Improves liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Enables T+2 settlement instead of compulsory delivery
- Reduces trading restrictions
- May attract more investor participation

**Market Impact**: Medium - This change affects trading mechanisms and settlement processes for all market participants dealing in this SME stock. The shift indicates improved compliance or trading behavior that warranted removal from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment.
