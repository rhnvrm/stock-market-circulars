## Summary

BSE has listed 6,000 Commercial Papers issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment with effect from November 11, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,92,431.50, with ICICI Bank Ltd as the Issuing and Paying Agent. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 6,000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,431.50
- Scrip Code: 730531
- Scrip ID: PFL101125
- ISIN: INE511C14ZA4
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE511C14ZA4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** February 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds short-term debt instruments (85-day maturity) to the BSE Debt segment for institutional investors. As a private placement issue with standard commercial paper characteristics, it has no direct impact on retail investors or broader market operations. Trading members can contact BSE's debt department for any clarifications.
