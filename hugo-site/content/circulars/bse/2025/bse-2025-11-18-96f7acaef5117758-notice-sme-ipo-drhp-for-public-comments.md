## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Black Opal Consultants Limited filed on November 17, 2025 for public comments. The document is available on BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Black Opal Consultants Limited on November 17, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard IPO filing process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public can review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days as per ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **November 18, 2025**: Notice issued and public comment period begins
- **December 9, 2025**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine SME IPO filing notice. The circular is informational for prospective investors and market participants interested in the upcoming SME IPO of Black Opal Consultants Limited. No immediate action required except for those wishing to provide feedback on the draft offer document.
