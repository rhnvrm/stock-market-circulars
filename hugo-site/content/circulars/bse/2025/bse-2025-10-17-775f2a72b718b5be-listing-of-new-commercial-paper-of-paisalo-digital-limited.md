## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on private placement basis with effect from October 17, 2025. The listing comprises 100 units of Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5 crores. The instruments carry IVR A1+ credit rating and will mature on March 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100 units of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,82,147.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730411, Scrip ID: PDL161025
- ISIN: INE420C14235
- Credit rating: IVR A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C14235
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The announcement affects primarily debt market participants, institutional investors, and entities dealing with short-term commercial papers. The listing provides an additional investment avenue for debt investors seeking IVR A1+ rated instruments with approximately 5-month maturity. For queries, trading members can contact the BSE debt department at the provided contact numbers.
