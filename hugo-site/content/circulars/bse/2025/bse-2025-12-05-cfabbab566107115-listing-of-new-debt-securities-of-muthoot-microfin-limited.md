## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Muthoot Microfin Limited on private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The issue comprises 75,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of ₹10,000 each, carrying a coupon rate of 9.70% per annum with monthly interest payments. The securities are rated CRISIL A+/Positive and will mature on December 3, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoot Microfin Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977342
- **Scrip ID**: 970MML27
- **ISIN**: INE046W07339
- **Quantity**: 75,000 debentures (₹750 million total issue size)
- **Face Value**: ₹10,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: ₹10,000 per debenture
- **Coupon Rate**: 9.70% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A+/Positive outlook
- **Date of Allotment**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from January 1, 2026 to December 3, 2027
- **Maturity Date**: December 3, 2027
- **Market Lot**: 1 debenture
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities on the BSE Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE046W07339
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all trades
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: January 1, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: December 3, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (from January 1, 2026 to December 3, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with limited impact on broader market operations. The listing adds liquidity options for investors holding these securities.

**Investor Impact**: The securities offer institutional and qualified investors access to Muthoot Microfin's debt instruments with a competitive 9.70% annual return, monthly income stream, and investment-grade rating (CRISIL A+/Positive).

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members have access to placement memorandum and BSE debt department support for any operational queries.
