## Summary

Catalyst Trusteeship Limited has informed BSE of the partial redemption of Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) of SHANKS 01 2025 SERIES A2 (ISIN: INE1O3X15025, Scrip Code: 976447). Following this partial redemption, the face value per PTC has been reduced to Rs. 639.30. The PTCs will trade at this reduced face value from December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security: SHANKS 01 2025 Series A2 PTCs (12% coupon, maturity 28-10-27)
- ISIN: INE1O3X15025
- Scrip Code: 976447
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of PTCs
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 639.30 per PTC
- Effective Trading Date: December 10, 2025
- Reference: DR-774/2025-2026
- Trustee: Catalyst Trusteeship Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the reduced face value of Rs. 639.30 per PTC
- All subsequent trading in these PTCs must reflect the new reduced face value
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the face value change effective December 10, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 09, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 10, 2025 (PTCs will trade at reduced face value from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The partial redemption reduces the outstanding face value of the PTCs, which affects the principal amount and potentially future cash flows for investors. Existing holders receive partial capital return.

**Investor Impact**: PTC holders of SHANKS 01 2025 SERIES A2 will see a reduction in their investment's face value to Rs. 639.30 per certificate. This represents a return of capital to investors through partial redemption.

**Trading Impact**: Trading Members need to ensure their systems reflect the updated face value for accurate pricing and settlement from December 10, 2025 onwards.
