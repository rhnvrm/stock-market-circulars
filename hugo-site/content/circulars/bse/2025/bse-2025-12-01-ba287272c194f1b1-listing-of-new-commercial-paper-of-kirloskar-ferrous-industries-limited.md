## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Kirloskar Ferrous Industries Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 1, 2025. The listing comprises 2000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crores. The securities carry an ICRA A1+ credit rating and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 2000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,291.92 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730673
- **Scrip ID**: KFIL281125
- **ISIN**: INE884B14713
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE884B14713
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment with limited market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments. The short 90-day tenure indicates this is a standard working capital financing instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term debt investment option rated ICRA A1+ (highest short-term rating), indicating strong safety of principal and timely payment capacity.
