## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in T-bill 815GUJ2025 (Scrip Code: 810586, ISIN: IN1520150088) effective November 24, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 815GUJ2025 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810586
- ISIN: IN1520150088
- Trading suspension effective from November 24, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in T-bill 815GUJ2025 from November 24, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Positions in this T-bill should be settled before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption date**: T-bill maturity date (specific date not mentioned in circular)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single T-bill instrument approaching maturity. Such suspensions are standard practice in debt markets.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects trading members holding or planning to trade in this specific T-bill. Members have advance notice to close positions or prepare for redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Holders of this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds as per standard maturity procedures. No trading disruption for other debt instruments.
