## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 772 dated December 9, 2025. The auction includes 107 securities across various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 258,928 shares. This auction is conducted to settle delivery obligations where shares were not delivered by the selling broker.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 772
- Auction Date: December 9, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 107 scrips
- Highest Quantity: SPRIGHT AGRO (258,928 shares)
- Second Highest: AVANCE TECHN (15,000 shares)
- Multiple blue-chip stocks included: Nestle India, Adani Enterprises, Bank of India, JSW Energy, Sun Pharma, Coromandel International

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine demat auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and market participants with delivery obligations in Settlement 772 must participate in the auction
- Buying brokers must ensure funds availability for auction settlement
- Selling brokers who failed to deliver must participate to close out obligations

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Number:** 772
- **Auction Notice Date:** December 9, 2025
- Actual auction timing and settlement date to be as per standard BSE auction calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 107 securities with potential price impact during auction execution

**Operational Impact:** Brokers with short delivery positions in these securities must participate in the auction to fulfill delivery obligations. Largest impact on SPRIGHT AGRO (258,928 shares), CENTRAL BANK (10,000 shares), and SOUTH INDIAN (6,000 shares).

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of these securities may see temporary price volatility during auction hours. The auction mechanism ensures settlement integrity in the market.
