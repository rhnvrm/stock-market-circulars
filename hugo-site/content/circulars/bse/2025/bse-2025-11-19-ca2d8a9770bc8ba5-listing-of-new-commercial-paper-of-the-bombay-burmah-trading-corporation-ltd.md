## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1100 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+, and will trade on the BSE Debt segment. Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1100 Commercial Paper units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,90,031.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730592
- Scrip ID: TBBT181125
- ISIN: INE050A14895
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE050A14895
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 19, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Date of Allotment:** November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it is a routine private placement of commercial paper in the debt segment. The instrument provides short-term funding option for The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. with a tenor of approximately 90 days. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to institutional investors and debt market participants who trade commercial paper instruments. No effect on equity markets or broader market operations.
