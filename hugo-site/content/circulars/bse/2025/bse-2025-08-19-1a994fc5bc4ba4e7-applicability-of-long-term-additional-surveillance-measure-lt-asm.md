## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 20, 2025. The circular includes new securities being added to LT-ASM, securities moving between ASM stages, and securities moving out of the framework.

## Key Points

- Cargosol Logistics Ltd (543621) newly shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework
- Jeena Sikho Lifecare Ltd (544476) moved to ASM Stage II
- Retina Paints Ltd (543902) moving out of Long Term ASM Framework
- Consolidated list contains 18+ securities across different ASM stages
- Framework includes provisions for T+0 scrips and parent company considerations

## Regulatory Changes

- Updated list of securities under Long Term ASM surveillance
- Stage progression for existing securities in the framework
- Removal of securities that no longer meet LT-ASM criteria
- Integration with other surveillance frameworks (GSM, ESM, IBC)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with LT-ASM trading restrictions for listed securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for securities under surveillance
- Adherence to stage-specific trading limitations and margin requirements

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025 - All LT-ASM changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

- Trading activity in affected securities will be subject to enhanced surveillance measures
- Investors may face additional margin requirements and trading restrictions
- Market participants should review their positions in the affected securities
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM will have reduced trading restrictions
