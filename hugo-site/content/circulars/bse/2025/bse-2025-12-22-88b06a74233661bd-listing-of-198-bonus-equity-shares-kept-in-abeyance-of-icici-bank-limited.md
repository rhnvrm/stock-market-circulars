## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade 198 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were kept in abeyance. These shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and will be available for trading from December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total of 198 bonus equity shares being listed
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Shares distributed across 5 distinctive number ranges
- Trading permitted from December 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for bonus shares that were previously kept in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities and may permit trading in accordance with standard exchange procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing of 198 bonus shares represents a negligible quantity relative to ICICI Bank's total outstanding shares. This is an administrative action to release shares that were previously held in abeyance, likely due to resolution of pending shareholder claims or documentation issues. No significant impact on stock price, trading volumes, or market capitalization anticipated.
