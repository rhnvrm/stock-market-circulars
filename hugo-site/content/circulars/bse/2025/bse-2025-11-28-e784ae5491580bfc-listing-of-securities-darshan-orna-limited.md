## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 50,029,335 new equity shares of Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884) issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on December 1, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884)
- Number of shares: 50,029,335 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2.00 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 3.00 per share (Rs. 2.00 face value + Rs. 1.00 premium)
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE671T01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 50029336 to 100058670
- Allotment date: November 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 1, 2025 (Monday)
- Rights: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities in their systems
- The new shares are available for trading from December 1, 2025
- All trading and settlement procedures apply as per standard equity share norms

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 1, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific rights issue listing with limited broader market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital. The rights issue increases the total equity base of Darshan Orna Limited.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue. The new shares carry identical rights as existing shares (pari-passu status).
