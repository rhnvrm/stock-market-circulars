## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Timex Group India Limited, along with an oversubscription notice. This circular provides details about the OFS process and subscription status for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale (OFS) opened for Timex Group India Limited
- Oversubscription notice issued indicating strong investor demand
- Trading members and investors to take note of the OFS schedule and process
- Timex Group India Limited is a prominent player in the watch and timepiece industry

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular. This is a notification of market activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure proper handling of OFS orders
- Investors participating in the OFS must follow the bidding process and timelines
- Settlement and allotment procedures to be followed as per SEBI guidelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 29, 2025
- OFS opening date and closing date as specified in the detailed notice
- Settlement schedule to follow standard T+2 process

## Impact Assessment

High impact on market participants and investors interested in Timex Group India Limited. The oversubscription status indicates strong investor appetite for the company's shares. This OFS provides liquidity to existing shareholders while allowing new investors to acquire stakes in an established consumer brand. Market participants should monitor the final subscription numbers and allotment details.
