## Summary

BSE has admitted three entities as trading members effective December 15, 2025. Two members (Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited and Allvest Securities Private Limited) have been granted new memberships, while Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has been granted additional segment membership for commodity derivatives.

## Key Points

- **Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited** (Member No. 6972) - New membership for Cash and Commodity Derivatives segments
- **Allvest Securities Private Limited** (Member No. 6973) - New membership for Cash, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, and Debt segments
- **Bajaj Financial Securities Limited** (Member No. 6706) - Additional segment membership for Commodity Derivatives
- All memberships are subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations
- Members granted trading rights on BSE Ltd. only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The admission of new trading members increases market participation but does not affect existing trading operations or member obligations.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing members. New members will operate under standard BSE trading framework.
