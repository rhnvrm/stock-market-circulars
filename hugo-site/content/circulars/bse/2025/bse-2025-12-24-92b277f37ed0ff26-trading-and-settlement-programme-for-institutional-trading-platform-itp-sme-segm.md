## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME segment for the period from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026. The notice details 21 settlement cycles (ITP-189/25-26 through ITP-209/25-26) with corresponding trading dates and pay-in/pay-out dates. This is a routine operational schedule published by the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers 21 trading days in January 2026
- Settlement numbers range from ITP-189/25-26 to ITP-209/25-26
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2526189 to 2526209
- Pay-in timing: 10:00 a.m. (members must submit instructions by 9:50 a.m.)
- Pay-out timing: Funds and securities will be effected by 11:00 a.m.
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:50 a.m.
- Pay-in must be completed at 10:00 a.m.
- Pay-out of funds and securities will be effected by 11:00 a.m.
- Members should plan their operations according to the published settlement schedule

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period:** January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- **Notice Date:** December 24, 2025
- **First Settlement:** ITP-189/25-26 (Trading: Jan 1, Settlement: Jan 2)
- **Last Settlement:** ITP-209/25-26 (Trading: Jan 30, Settlement: Feb 2)
- **Non-trading days:** Weekends (3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-25, 31 Jan) and Republic Day (26 Jan)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine monthly settlement schedule with no changes to existing procedures or timings.

**Operational Impact:** Low. The schedule follows standard T+1 settlement cycle for ITP-SME segment, with weekend adjustments. Market participants can plan their settlement operations accordingly.

**Stakeholder Impact:** Informational for institutional traders, clearing members, and depositories operating in the SME segment through the Institutional Trading Platform. No action required beyond normal operational adherence to settlement timelines.
