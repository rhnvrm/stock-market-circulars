## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 18, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with a quantity of 5,000 units and market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976753, Scrip ID: 9HLFL35
- ISIN: INE146O08340 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 104,857.5342
- Quantity: 5,000 units with market lot of 1
- Credit Rating: CARE AA+/Stable, Crisil AA+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 9% per annum
- Interest Payment: Annually from June 4, 2026 to June 4, 2035
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE146O08340
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: June 4, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: June 4, 2035
- **Interest Payment Schedule**: Annually from June 4, 2026 to June 4, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides additional debt instruments for institutional and qualified investors participating in private placement securities. The AA+ credit rating from both CARE and Crisil indicates strong creditworthiness. The 10-year tenure and 9% annual interest rate may attract investors seeking fixed-income instruments with stable returns.
