## Summary

BSE has announced a group change for Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd (Scrip Code: 539226). The company will be moved from Group X to Group B effective December 19, 2025. This is a routine reclassification that trading members need to note for their records.

## Key Points

- Company: Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd
- Scrip Code: 539226
- Current Group: X
- New Group: B
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251218-10

## Regulatory Changes

The company is being reclassified from Group X to Group B. Group B typically includes companies that have completed listing requirements and meet BSE's criteria for this classification. Group X is generally for companies under specific surveillance or restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note this group change and update their systems accordingly to reflect the new group classification effective December 19, 2025.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a positive administrative change indicating the company's transition from a restricted or surveillance group (X) to a regular trading group (B). The impact on trading and market operations is minimal, but the reclassification may improve the company's trading status and accessibility for investors.
