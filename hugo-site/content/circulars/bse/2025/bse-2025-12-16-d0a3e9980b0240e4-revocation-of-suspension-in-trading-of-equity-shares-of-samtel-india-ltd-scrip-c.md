## Summary

BSE has announced the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371). This circular reinstates normal trading operations for the company's shares on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Samtel India Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 500371
- Action: Revocation of trading suspension
- Trading Status: Resumed
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading suspension previously imposed on Samtel India Ltd. equity shares has been lifted, restoring normal market operations for the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can resume trading in Samtel India Ltd. shares
- Normal exchange trading rules and regulations apply
- Brokers and traders should update their systems to reflect the active trading status

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Effective Date: As per the circular notification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The revocation allows shareholders to trade their holdings and provides liquidity to the scrip. This is positive for existing shareholders who were unable to exit their positions during the suspension period.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and investors can now place orders for buying and selling Samtel India Ltd. shares through regular market mechanisms.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders regain the ability to transact in the stock, potentially affecting price discovery based on market demand and supply dynamics.
