## Summary

Neptune Logitek Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544663) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, December 23, 2025. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Monday, December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited listed on BSE on December 22, 2025
- Stock ticker: 544663
- Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective December 23, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index reconstitution following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required. This is an informational notice about index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025**: Neptune Logitek Limited listing effective on BSE
- **December 23, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME stock to the specialized BSE SME IPO Index. The change affects index tracking products and investors following the BSE SME IPO Index composition. No impact on broader market operations or trading rules.
