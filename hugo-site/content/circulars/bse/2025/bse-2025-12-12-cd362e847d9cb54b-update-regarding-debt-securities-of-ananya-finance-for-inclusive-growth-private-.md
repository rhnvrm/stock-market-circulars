## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited. In continuation of notice number 20250620-29 dated June 20, 2025, the exchange has announced that specific debt securities will be suspended from trading effective December 17, 2025 due to ongoing restructuring of the company.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976510 with ISIN INE774L08022 will be suspended for trading
- Suspension effective from Wednesday, December 17, 2025
- Action taken due to restructuring of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited
- This is a continuation of earlier notice 20250620-29 dated June 20, 2025
- Trading members can contact BSE officials for further clarification

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements trading suspension measures in accordance with existing restructuring provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the suspension and cease trading in the specified scrip code from December 17, 2025
- For queries or clarifications, trading members should contact:
  - Ms. Pooja Trivedi: +91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com
  - Issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Reference to Previous Notice**: June 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20250620-29)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Investors holding these debt securities will be unable to trade them from December 17, 2025, affecting liquidity and exit options.

**Investor Impact**: Significant for bondholders of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited as the restructuring process and trading suspension may affect debt servicing and recovery timelines.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to reflect the suspension and inform clients holding these securities about the inability to trade.
