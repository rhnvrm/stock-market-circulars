## Summary

Wardwizard Innovations and Mobility Limited (Scrip Code: 538970) has listed 4,01,23,546 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- per share on a rights basis. These shares will commence trading on BSE from November 11, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 4,01,23,546 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued on Rights basis
- Issue price: Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 10/- premium = Rs. 11/- per share
- ISIN: INE945P01024
- Distinctive Numbers: 262052304 to 302175849
- Date of Allotment: October 30, 2025
- Trading commencement date: November 11, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the new securities from November 11, 2025
- Recognize the new distinctive number range (262052304 to 302175849)
- Ensure proper settlement processes for the newly listed shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Trading Commencement**: November 11, 2025
