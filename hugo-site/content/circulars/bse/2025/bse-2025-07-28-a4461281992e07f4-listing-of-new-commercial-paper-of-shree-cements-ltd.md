## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Shree Cements Ltd issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with 3000 units totaling Rs. 150 crores, available for trading on BSE Debt segment from July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 3000 units (total value Rs. 150 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729917, Scrip ID: SCL280725
- ISIN: INE070A14810
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,527 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Trading Start Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors. HDFC Bank serves as Issuing and Paying agent for the instruments.
