## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from three listed companies - Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited (Scrip Code: 539551), Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited (Scrip Code: 544530), and B.C. Power Controls Limited (Scrip Code: 537766) - regarding unusual movements in their securities' price and/or volume on November 18, 2025. All companies responded confirming that they have no pending price-sensitive information or announcements that could have influenced the price behavior, and affirmed compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 30 disclosure requirements.

## Key Points

- BSE surveillance department observed significant price/volume movements in securities of three companies
- Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited confirmed all disclosures made timely under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited clarified no pending price-sensitive information exists
- B.C. Power Controls Limited response included but document appears truncated
- All companies attributed price movements to market-driven conditions
- Companies reaffirmed commitment to timely disclosure of material events

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine surveillance activity under existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015 for disclosure of material events
- Companies must confirm whether any pending information could impact price behavior
- Timely and accurate disclosure of all price-sensitive information remains mandatory

## Important Dates

- **November 17-18, 2025**: BSE issued surveillance queries to respective companies
- **November 18, 2025**: Companies submitted clarifications to BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications with no material information disclosed. The price/volume movements were confirmed to be market-driven with no undisclosed corporate developments.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Investors can be assured that companies are compliant with disclosure norms and no material information is being withheld.

**Operational Impact**: None. This is standard exchange surveillance procedure to ensure market integrity and prevent information asymmetry.
