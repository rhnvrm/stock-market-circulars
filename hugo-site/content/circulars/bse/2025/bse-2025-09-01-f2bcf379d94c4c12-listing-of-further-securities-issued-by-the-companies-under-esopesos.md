## Summary

BSE announces the listing of additional securities issued by 6 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These new shares will be available for trading starting September 2, 2025.

## Key Points

- 6 companies are listing additional shares from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- Total of 186,611 new shares across all companies
- All shares are immediately tradeable with no lock-in period
- Face values range from ₹2 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

- **Barbeque-Nation Hospitality Limited**: 5,624 shares (Face Value: ₹5)
- **Coforge Limited**: 12,230 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **ICICI Bank Ltd**: 68,621 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Kaynes Technology India Limited**: 77,561 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Lodha Developers Limited**: 18,583 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Metro Brands Limited**: 4,192 shares (Face Value: ₹5)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these represent small quantities of additional shares from employee stock option exercises. The shares have no lock-in restrictions and will trade normally alongside existing shares.
