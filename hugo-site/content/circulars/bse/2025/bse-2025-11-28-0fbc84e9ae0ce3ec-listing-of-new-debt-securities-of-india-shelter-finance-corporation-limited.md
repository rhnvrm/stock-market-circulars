## Summary

India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 28, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with 15,000 units of face value ₹1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 7.85% per annum with quarterly payments and a maturity date of November 26, 2030. The securities carry an ICRA AA- credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 15,000 units (total value: ₹150 crore)
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 977323
- **Scrip ID**: 785ISFCL30
- **ISIN**: INE922K07112
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-
- **Interest Rate**: 7.85% per annum (quarterly payment)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE922K07112
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: February 27, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly (from February 27, 2026 to November 26, 2030)
- **Redemption Date**: November 26, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing that adds liquidity to the debt market segment. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors access to ICRA AA- rated debt instruments from India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited.

**Investor Impact**: Low. The securities are available for trading in the debt segment for interested investors seeking fixed-income instruments with quarterly interest payments and approximately 5-year tenure.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Debt segment trading members can now trade these securities through the BSE Debt platform.
