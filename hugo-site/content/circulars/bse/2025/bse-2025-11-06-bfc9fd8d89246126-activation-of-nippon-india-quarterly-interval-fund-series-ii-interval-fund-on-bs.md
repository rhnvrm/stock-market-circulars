## Summary

NIPPON INDIA Mutual Fund is activating NIPPON INDIA QUARTERLY INTERVAL FUND - SERIES II on the BSE StAR MF Platform for a Specified Transaction Period (STP) from November 07-10, 2025. The fund offers six plan variants across IDCW (payout and reinvestment) and Growth options in both regular and direct plans. Important payment restrictions apply during this period - MICR Cheque, Nodal and OTM payments will not be accepted, and clear funds must be received before 2:30pm on transaction dates to avoid rejection.

## Key Points

- Transaction period (Subscription/Redemption): November 07-10, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth (each in Regular and Direct plans)
- All schemes categorized as DEBT funds
- Transaction requests accepted during normal business hours within STP period
- NAV application based on cut-off timing and date/time of transaction receipt
- Subject to fund confirmation uploaded/received
- Specific ISINs assigned for each plan variant

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational activation notice for a new interval fund series.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must not accept MICR Cheque, Nodal, or OTM payments for this fund from November 07-10, 2025
- Clear funds must be received by the Exchange before 2:30pm on transaction dates
- Funds received after 2:30pm or without clear status will be refunded to investors after AMC/RTA rejection
- Transaction requests must adhere to scheme cut-off times available on BSE StAR MF platform

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: STP opening date - start of transaction period
- **November 10, 2025**: STP closing date - end of transaction period
- **Daily 2:30pm deadline**: Cut-off for clear funds receipt during STP period

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Affects mutual fund distributors and investors interested in interval funds. The restricted payment methods (no MICR Cheque, Nodal, OTM) require advance planning for payment modes.

**Market Impact**: Low - Limited to specific interval fund transactions during a 4-day window. Does not affect broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors must use approved payment methods and ensure funds clear by 2:30pm daily to avoid rejection and refund delays. The short transaction window (4 days) requires timely action for those interested in subscribing.
