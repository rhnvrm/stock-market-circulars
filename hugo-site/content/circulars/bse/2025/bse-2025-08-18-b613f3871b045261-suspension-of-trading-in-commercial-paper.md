## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 20 companies effective from their respective record dates set for redemption purposes. The suspensions range from August 19-24, 2025, with most companies having record dates of August 21-22, 2025.

## Key Points

- 20 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension effective from respective record dates
- Most suspensions occur on August 21-22, 2025
- Covers various sectors including financial services, real estate, and industrial companies
- Standard procedure for commercial paper redemption

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is standard market practice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified commercial papers from suspension dates
- Standard redemption procedures to be followed by issuing companies
- Record date compliance for redemption purposes

## Important Dates

- **August 19, 2025**: Standard Chartered Securities commercial paper suspension
- **August 21, 2025**: Suspension for 13 companies (Muthoot Capital, Mindspace REIT, Trust Investment Advisors, Godrej Finance, Pilani Investment, IGH Holdings, Julius Baer Capital, Birla Group Holdings, Motilal Oswal Finvest, Reliance Retail Ventures, 360 ONE Prime, Aditya Birla Real Estate, Vertis Infrastructure Trust)
- **August 22, 2025**: Suspension for 6 companies (BOBCARD, Axis Finance, CNH Industrial Capital, Titan Company, Tata Steel, Navi Finserv)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as these are scheduled redemptions of commercial papers. Affects debt market liquidity temporarily for specific instruments. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Routine corporate action with minimal disruption to overall market functioning.
