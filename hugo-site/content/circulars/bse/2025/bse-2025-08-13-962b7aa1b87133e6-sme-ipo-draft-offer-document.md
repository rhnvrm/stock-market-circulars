## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED for public comments. The document was filed on August 11, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document will remain available on BSE's website for at least 21 days for public review and comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED on August 11, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public viewing
- 21-day public comment period from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard SME IPO draft document filing process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments within 21 days from August 13, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with all Schedule VI disclosures under ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Document Filed: August 11, 2025
- Notice Date: August 13, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from August 13, 2025 (until September 3, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This filing represents a standard step in the SME IPO process for SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED. The impact is primarily limited to potential investors and stakeholders of the company. The public comment period allows for regulatory scrutiny and transparency in the IPO process.
