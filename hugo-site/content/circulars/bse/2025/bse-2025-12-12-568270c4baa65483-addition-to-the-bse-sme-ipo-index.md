## Summary

BSE announces the addition of METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544637) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Monday, December 15, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE effective Friday, December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (ticker: 544637) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Company listing on BSE: Friday, December 12, 2025
- Index inclusion effective: Monday, December 15, 2025 (at market open)
- This follows reference to Notice No. 20251211-50
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index constituent addition following new listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants should note the index composition change for portfolio management and tracking purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED listing on BSE
- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date of addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition following the listing of a new SME company. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their holdings accordingly. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.
