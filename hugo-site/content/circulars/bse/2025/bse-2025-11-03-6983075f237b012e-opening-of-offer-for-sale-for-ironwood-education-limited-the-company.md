## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of an Offer for Sale (OFS) for Ironwood Education Limited. The OFS mechanism allows existing shareholders to sell their shares to the public through the stock exchange platform in a transparent and efficient manner.

## Key Points

- Company: Ironwood Education Limited
- Event: Opening of Offer for Sale (OFS)
- Platform: BSE Stock Exchange
- Announcement Date: November 3, 2025

## OFS Mechanism

The Offer for Sale is conducted through the stock exchange platform where:
- Eligible investors can place bids during the specified bidding period
- Separate windows are typically available for retail and non-retail investors
- Allocation is based on the bidding process and allocation methodology

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: November 3, 2025
- OFS Opening Date: As specified in the circular
- Bidding Period: To be confirmed from detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

The OFS provides:
- Liquidity to existing shareholders of Ironwood Education Limited
- Opportunity for investors to acquire shares through a structured mechanism
- Price discovery through the bidding process
- Compliance with regulatory requirements for share divestment
