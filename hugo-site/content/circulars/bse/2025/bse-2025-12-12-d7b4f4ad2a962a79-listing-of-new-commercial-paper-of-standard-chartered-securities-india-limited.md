## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis effective December 12, 2025. The issue consists of 400 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (total Rs. 20 crore), with maturity on December 11, 2026. The instrument carries an ICRA A1+ credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 20 crore (400 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,66,938 per unit (discounted)
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE472H14755
- Scrip code: 730758
- Scrip ID: SCSL121225
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Allotment date: December 12, 2025
- Maturity date: December 11, 2026
- Tenure: 364 days
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the instrument only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Participants should contact BSE debt department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption date**: December 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited with short-term financing for approximately one year. The A1+ rating indicates highest credit quality for short-term debt. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors specifically interested in high-rated commercial paper with 364-day tenor. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
