## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 12, 2025. The instrument comprises 400 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 20 crores) with ICRA A1+ rating, maturing on December 11, 2026. HDFC Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Size**: 400 units (Rs. 20 crores total)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,66,938 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730758
- **Scrip ID**: SCSL121225
- **ISIN**: INE472H14755
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE472H14755
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment under the specified scrip code and ID

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine Commercial Paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term debt funding to Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited for approximately one year. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term instruments. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in Commercial Paper. For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
