## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities, totaling 1500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 28, 2025. The Commercial Papers carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with Federal Bank acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730668 | Scrip ID: FFSL271125
- ISIN Number: INE007N14EK4
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,460.00 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EK4
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 27, 2025
- Listing Date: November 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026
- Notice Date: November 28, 2025
