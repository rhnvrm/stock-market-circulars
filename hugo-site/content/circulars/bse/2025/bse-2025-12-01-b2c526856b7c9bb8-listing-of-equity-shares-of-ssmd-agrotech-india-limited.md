## Summary

SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of November 30, 2025. The company has a total of 86,66,129 equity shares held by 1,128 shareholders. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 67.49% while Public shareholders hold 32.51% of the total shares. The company has confirmed no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 86,66,129 shares held by 1,128 shareholders
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 58,49,019 shares (67.49%)
- Public shareholders: 1,122 shareholders holding 28,17,110 shares (32.51%)
- 110 promoter shares (0.0039%) are locked-in
- All shares are fully paid up equity shares
- 86,66,129 shares held in dematerialized form
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

This filing represents compliance with Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, requiring listed entities to submit shareholding pattern disclosures.

## Important Dates

- Filing date: November 30, 2025
- Circular publication date: December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding above 67%, indicating strong promoter control. The absence of pledged shares, convertible securities, or encumbrances suggests healthy financial position of promoters. No immediate impact on trading or investor decisions expected from this disclosure.
