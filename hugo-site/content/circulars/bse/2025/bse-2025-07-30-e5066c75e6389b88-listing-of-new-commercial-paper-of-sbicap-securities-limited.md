## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 150 crores issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are effective for trading from July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,292 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14BW7
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- State Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Trading effective date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term financing needs. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
