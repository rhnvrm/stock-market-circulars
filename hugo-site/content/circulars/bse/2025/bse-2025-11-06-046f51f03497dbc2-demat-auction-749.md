## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 749 dated November 6, 2025. This circular lists 195 securities from various companies that are subject to demat auction settlement, covering a wide range of sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, energy, and technology companies. The quantities range from single shares to thousands of shares depending on the security.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 749
- Settlement Date: November 6, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 195 securities across multiple companies
- Largest quantities include: YES BANK LTD (18,992 shares), POWER GRID (19,774 shares), STEEL AUTHORITY (14,762 shares), INDUS TOWERS (13,288 shares)
- Smallest quantities: Multiple securities with single share quantities
- Covers major blue-chip stocks including Infosys, NTPC, BPCL, State Bank of India, and Reliance group companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must fulfill demat auction obligations for the listed securities
- Physical delivery settlements must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants must adhere to the standard demat auction settlement timeline for Settlement No. 749

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 6, 2025
- Settlement Number: 749

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine demat auction settlement that occurs regularly as part of standard market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Affects specific brokers and clearing members who have pending physical delivery obligations for the listed securities. The wide variety of stocks listed indicates this is a standard settlement cycle covering multiple trading members' obligations.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. This primarily concerns institutional participants and brokers involved in physical settlement of securities where demat delivery could not be completed through normal settlement processes.
