## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of OM METALLOGIC LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544559), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment under M Group effective October 23, 2025. This is a continuation of the exchange's notice dated October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: OM METALLOGIC LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544559
- Current Status: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Status: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Thursday, October 23, 2025
- Previous Notice: Reference to Notice No. 20251006-53 dated October 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for OM METALLOGIC LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for intraday trading and standard settlement cycles instead of the restrictive Trade for Trade framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the effective date of October 23, 2025 for the group change
- Update internal systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain at 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 6, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251006-53)
- **October 7, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued
- **October 23, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for OM METALLOGIC LIMITED shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment. Investors will be able to engage in intraday trading and benefit from standard settlement cycles. This is a positive development typically occurring after an SME IPO completes its initial stabilization period under Trade for Trade restrictions.
