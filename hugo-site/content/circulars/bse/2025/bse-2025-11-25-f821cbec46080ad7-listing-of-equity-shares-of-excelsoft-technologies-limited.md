## Summary

BSE has listed equity shares of Excelsoft Technologies Limited (CIN: U72900KA2000PLC027256), a Karnataka-based technology company. The circular provides detailed lock-in information for 11,50,84,164+ equity shares with varying lock-in periods ranging from one month to three years, including shares issued under ESOP schemes and IPO.

## Key Points

- Company registered office located at 1-B, Hootagalli Industrial Area, Mysuru â€“ 570 018, Karnataka
- Total shares covered: Approximately 11.5 crore equity shares
- Multiple lock-in categories including promoter shares, ESOP shares, and IPO public shares
- Lock-in periods vary: 1 month (till 23-Dec-2025), 3 months (till 21-Feb-2026), 1 year (till 25-Nov-2026), and 3 years (till 25-Nov-2028)
- Majority of shares are in demat form with some physical shares
- Significant portion of shares under lock-in starting from 24-Nov-2025

### IPO Public Shares

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders must adhere to the specified lock-in periods before trading locked-in shares
- Physical shares must be converted to demat form before trading on exchange
- Lock-in restrictions apply from the dates specified (primarily starting 24-Nov-2025)
- ESOP shares are freely tradable without lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start Date**: 24-Nov-2025 (for most locked-in shares)
- **First Lock-in Expiry**: 23-Dec-2025 (1-month lock-in)
- **Second Lock-in Expiry**: 21-Feb-2026 (3-month lock-in)
- **One-year Lock-in Expiry**: 25-Nov-2026
- **Three-year Lock-in Expiry**: 25-Nov-2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This is a new listing with substantial shares under lock-in, which will restrict supply and potentially support price stability in the near term. The staggered lock-in expiry dates (1 month, 3 months, 1 year, and 3 years) will gradually increase tradable float over time.

**Liquidity Impact**: Initial trading liquidity will be limited to IPO public shares (~2.5 crore) and ESOP shares (~1.03 crore), representing approximately 30% of total shares. Full liquidity will only be available after the final lock-in expires in November 2028.

**Investor Impact**: Long-term investors and promoters are locked in for extended periods (up to 3 years), demonstrating commitment. ESOP holders have immediate trading flexibility, which may create near-term selling pressure.
