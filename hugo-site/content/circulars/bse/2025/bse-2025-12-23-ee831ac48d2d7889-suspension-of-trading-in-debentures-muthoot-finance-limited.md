## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Muthoot Finance Limited (Code: MFL-IV-11-1-26-NCD, ISIN: INE414G07FM3, Scrip Code: 937355) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is in connection with the company fixing December 27, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from December 26, 2025
- Record date for redemption and interest payment: December 27, 2025
- Debenture ISIN: INE414G07FM3
- Scrip Code: 937355
- Notice Number: DR-785/2025-2026
- Issuer: Muthoot Finance Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from December 26, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of the suspension and comply accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the specified debenture series during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 26, 2025**: No dealings commence (trading suspension begins)
- **December 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. Does not affect Muthoot Finance equity shares or other debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive the redemption amount and interest payment. Trading suspension is temporary and standard practice for record date determination.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems do not process trades in this specific debenture from December 26, 2025. Standard corporate action processing for debt securities.
