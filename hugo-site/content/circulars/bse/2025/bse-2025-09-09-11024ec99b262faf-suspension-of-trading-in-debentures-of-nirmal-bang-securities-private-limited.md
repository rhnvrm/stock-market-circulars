## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED effective September 11, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (Code: NBSPL-10.75%-26-25-PVT, ISIN: INE681R08075, BSE Code: 975547)
- Record date for redemption set for September 11, 2025
- No dealings permitted from September 11, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-713/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for debenture redemption process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in specified debentures from September 11, 2025
- Members required to take note of the suspension and comply with trading restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 11, 2025 (for redemption purpose)
- **Trading Suspension Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a private limited company's debentures with limited trading activity. Routine corporate action with standard suspension procedures to facilitate redemption process.
