## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) on private placement basis. The listing comprises 7,000 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 350 crores. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 7,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,627 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 350 crores (face value)
- **Scrip Code**: 730508
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF061125
- **Detail Name**: ABHFL-5-2-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FJ7 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FJ7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025 (Actual/Deemed)
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term funding to Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited and offer investment opportunities to institutional investors in the debt market. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE but does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.
