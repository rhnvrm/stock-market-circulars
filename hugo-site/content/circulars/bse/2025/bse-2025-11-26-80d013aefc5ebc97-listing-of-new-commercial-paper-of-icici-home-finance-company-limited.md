## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 26, 2025, and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 730647
- Scrip ID: IHF251125
- ISIN: INE071G14HG5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,606.50/-
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 24, 2026
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and offers investment opportunity for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Standard trading procedures apply with no special compliance requirements beyond normal debt segment operations.
