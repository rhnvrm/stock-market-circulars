## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has announced the settlement holidays for calendar year 2026. There will be 19 settlement holidays on weekdays and 4 additional holidays falling on weekends. Members and custodians are informed to plan their settlement operations accordingly.

## Key Points

- Total of 19 settlement holidays scheduled for 2026 on working days
- 4 additional holidays fall on Saturday/Sunday: Mahashivratri (Feb 15), Ramzan-Id (Mar 21), Independence Day/Parsi New Year (Aug 15), and Diwali Amavasya (Nov 8)
- Holidays cover major national, religious, and regional festivals
- Notice issued by Tajindersingh Virdi, Assistant Vice President, ICCL
- Notice Number: 20251224-1 dated December 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational circular announcing the settlement holiday calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must note these holidays for settlement planning
- No settlement operations will be conducted on these dates
- All market participants should adjust their operational calendars accordingly

## Important Dates

**2026 Settlement Holidays (Weekdays):**
- January 26 (Monday) - Republic Day
- February 19 (Thursday) - Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti
- March 3 (Tuesday) - Holi (Second Day)
- March 19 (Thursday) - Gudi Padwa
- March 26 (Thursday) - Ram Navami
- March 31 (Tuesday) - Mahavir Jayanti
- April 1 (Wednesday) - Annual Bank Closing
- April 3 (Friday) - Good Friday
- April 14 (Tuesday) - Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti
- May 1 (Friday) - Maharashtra Din/Buddha Pournima
- May 28 (Thursday) - Bakri ID (Id-Uz-Zuha)
- June 26 (Friday) - Moharum
- August 26 (Wednesday) - Id-E-Milad
- September 14 (Monday) - Ganesh Chaturthi
- October 2 (Friday) - Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti
- October 20 (Tuesday) - Dussera
- November 10 (Tuesday) - Diwali (Bali Pratipada)
- November 24 (Tuesday) - Guru Nanak Jayanti
- December 25 (Friday) - Christmas

**Holidays on Weekends:**
- February 15 (Sunday) - Mahashivratri
- March 21 (Saturday) - Ramzan-Id
- August 15 (Saturday) - Independence Day/Parsi New Year
- November 8 (Sunday) - Diwali Amavasya

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Market participants must plan settlement cycles around these holidays. The holidays are distributed throughout the year with clusters in March-April (6 holidays) and October-November (4 holidays).

**Planning Consideration:** Members and custodians should adjust their cash flow planning, settlement schedules, and operational resources to account for these non-settlement days.

**No Trading Impact:** This circular only addresses settlement holidays for ICCL. Trading holidays may differ and should be referred to separately.
