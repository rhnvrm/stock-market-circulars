## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd on private placement basis, effective December 9, 2025. The securities are zero-coupon bonds with a 10-year tenure, maturing on October 30, 2035. A total quantity of 10,994 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each have been listed under ISIN INE094I07098, carrying a CARE AA- credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977351
- **Scrip ID**: 0KPDL35
- **Detail Name**: KPDL-ZC-30-10-35-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE094I07098
- **Quantity**: 10,994 securities
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AA-
- **Interest Rate**: 0% p.a. (zero-coupon)
- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2035
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN (INE094I07098)
- Securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of a single company. The zero-coupon structure indicates these are discount bonds that will be redeemed at face value upon maturity in 2035. The CARE AA- rating indicates high credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of financial obligations. Impact is limited to:

- Debt segment trading members who may facilitate secondary market trading
- Existing and potential investors in Kolte-Patil Developers debt instruments
- The company's ability to raise debt capital through private placements
