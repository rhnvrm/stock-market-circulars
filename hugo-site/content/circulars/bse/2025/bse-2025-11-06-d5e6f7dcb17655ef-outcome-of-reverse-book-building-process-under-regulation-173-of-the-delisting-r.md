## Summary

BSE has published the outcome of the reverse book building process conducted for the voluntary delisting of Manphool Exports Limited under Regulation 17(3) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021.

## Key Points

- Reverse book building process completed for Manphool Exports Limited
- Process conducted under SEBI Delisting Regulations 17(3)
- Circular published on November 6, 2025
- Results determine the final delisting price or outcome

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a disclosure of process outcome under existing SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must comply with the outcome of the reverse book building process
- Shareholders who participated in the process will be informed of final price/outcome
- Further steps as per SEBI Delisting Regulations to be followed

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 6, 2025
- Process completion and outcome announcement

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Manphool Exports Shareholders:** High - Determines exit price for shareholders wanting to tender shares

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - Limited to company-specific impact; affects shareholders of Manphool Exports Limited who wish to exit through the delisting process

**Process Significance:** The reverse book building mechanism allows price discovery through shareholder participation, determining whether delisting proceeds and at what price
