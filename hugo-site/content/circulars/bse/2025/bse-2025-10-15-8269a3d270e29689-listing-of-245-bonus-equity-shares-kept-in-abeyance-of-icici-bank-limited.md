## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and permission to trade 245 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and will be available for trading from Thursday, October 16, 2025. The shares are distributed across four distinct number ranges.

## Key Points

- Total of 245 bonus equity shares being listed
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Shares distributed in four batches: 30 shares, 15 shares, 100 shares, and 100 shares
- Distinctive number ranges provided for each batch

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of these securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 245 bonus shares that were previously kept in abeyance, representing a negligible volume relative to ICICI Bank's overall outstanding shares. This is an administrative action to release previously held shares and does not affect the company's capital structure or market operations in any meaningful way.
