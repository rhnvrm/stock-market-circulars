## Summary

MAS Financial Services Limited has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 15 crore on BSE Debt segment effective August 20, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each and carry an interest rate of 9.25% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976696, Scrip ID: 925MASFS27
- ISIN: INE348L07316 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,076.03
- Date of Allotment: August 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from August 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 16, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a rated investment option (CARE AA-/Stable) offering 9.25% annual returns with quarterly interest payments over a 2-year tenure.
