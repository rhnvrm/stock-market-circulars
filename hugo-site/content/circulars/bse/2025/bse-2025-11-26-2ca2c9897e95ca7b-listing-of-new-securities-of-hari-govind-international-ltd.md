## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 6,67,500 new equity shares of Hari Govind International Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531971) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from November 27, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until May 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,67,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Trading commences on November 27, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 7857501 to 8525000
- ISIN: INE167F01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities available for trading
- All 6,67,500 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Lock-in compliance must be observed until the specified end date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 16, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in End Date**: May 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine preferential allotment representing a relatively small equity issuance. The shares are subject to standard lock-in provisions preventing immediate sale by allottees. The pari-passu ranking ensures equal rights with existing shareholders.
