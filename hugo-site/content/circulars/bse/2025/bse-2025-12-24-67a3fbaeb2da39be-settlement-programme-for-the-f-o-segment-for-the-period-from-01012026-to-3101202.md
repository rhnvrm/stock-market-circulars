## Summary

BSE has published the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for January 2026. The circular provides the daily settlement schedule mapping each trade date to its corresponding settlement date for the entire month.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers trading period from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- Daily settlement follows T+1 pattern (settlement one business day after trade date)
- Last trade date of January 30, 2026 settles on February 2, 2026
- Schedule accounts for weekend gaps in trading days
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine settlement schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should:
- Note the settlement dates for planning fund management and obligations
- Ensure adequate margins and funds availability as per the published schedule
- Align internal settlement processes with the published timeline

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- **First Settlement**: January 2, 2026 (for January 1 trades)
- **Last Settlement of Month**: February 2, 2026 (for January 30 trades)
- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine schedule publication that market participants use for planning purposes.

**Market Impact**: None - this is a standard operational circular providing settlement dates without any changes to settlement mechanisms or timelines.

**Affected Parties**: All F&O segment participants including traders, brokers, and clearing members need to reference this schedule for settlement planning and fund management.
