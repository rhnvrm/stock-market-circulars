## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on private placement basis with effect from December 12, 2025. The CP has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with 5,000 units totaling Rs. 250 crore, maturing on March 12, 2026. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: 5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 250 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730756, Scrip ID: GIL121225
- ISIN: INE047A14AR0
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE047A14AR0
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt funding to Grasim Industries for approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.
