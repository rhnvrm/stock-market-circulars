## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures issued by Aadhar Housing Finance Limited, effective January 2, 2026. The suspension is in connection with the scheduled redemption of debentures and payment of interest, with the record date set for January 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two series of 9.60% debentures of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited will be suspended from trading
- ISIN INE538L07189 (Code: 953250) - AHFL-9.60%-19-1-26-PVT
- ISIN INE538L07197 (Code: 953262) - AHFL-9.60%-19-1-26-PVT
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date
- Circular reference: DR-790/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 2, 2026 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 2, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 2, 2026
- **Circular Date**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific debenture series. This is a routine corporate action involving the maturity and redemption of debt instruments. The suspension ensures orderly settlement of final interest payment and principal redemption to eligible debenture holders as per the record date. No broader market impact is anticipated.
