## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Mindspace Business Parks REIT on a private placement basis, effective November 18, 2025. The securities are worth Rs. 70 lakh (70,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each) and carry an interest rate of 7.1485% per annum with quarterly interest payments. The securities have received CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE ratings and will mature on November 15, 2030.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 70,000 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977297 | Scrip ID: 71485MBR30
- ISIN Number: INE0CCU07173
- Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,081.2860
- Date of Allotment: November 17, 2025
- Interest Rate: 7.1485% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0CCU07173
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from December 31, 2025 to November 15, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 15, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt listing for a REIT on private placement basis with limited quantity (Rs. 70 lakh total). The securities have strong credit ratings (AAA from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicating high creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members need to ensure compliance with dematerialized trading requirements and be aware of the tick size and ISIN for trade execution.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional/qualified investors given the high face value (Rs. 1 lakh per unit) and private placement nature. The securities offer attractive interest rates with top-tier credit ratings for investors seeking stable income over a 5-year tenure.
