## Summary

KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE094I07080 (Code: 977231). The record date has been fixed as December 26, 2025, and trading will commence with reduced face value from the same date in settlement DR-785/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD (KPDL)
- ISIN: INE094I07080
- Scrip Code: 977231 (KPDL-15-10-35-PVT)
- Record Date: December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Redemption Amount: Rs. 98,974/- per Debenture
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-785/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251224-13
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action notification for part redemption of existing debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from December 26, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for the specified debentures
- The debentures will trade ex-redemption from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** December 26, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-785/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption will result in a reduction of the outstanding face value of the debentures. Debenture holders on record as of December 26, 2025, will receive Rs. 98,974/- per debenture as part redemption. Post-redemption, the debentures will continue to trade at the reduced face value. The impact is limited to holders of these specific non-convertible debentures and will affect the valuation and trading of these debt instruments going forward.
