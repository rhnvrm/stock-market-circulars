## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The commercial paper with scrip code 730472 and ISIN INE01C314DC5 is maturing and will be suspended from trading effective November 06, 2025. This is a routine administrative action pursuant to the instrument's scheduled redemption.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730472) will be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE01C314DC5
- Suspension effective date: November 06, 2025
- Reason: Maturity/Redemption of the instrument
- Notice reference: DR-750/2025-2026
- Trading members advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension due to instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited from November 06, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and adjust their systems accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in scrip code 730472 from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 04, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 06, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** November 07, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine suspension of a single commercial paper instrument due to scheduled maturity. No systemic or broader market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members holding or dealing in this specific commercial paper need to ensure no transactions are executed after November 05, 2025. Standard maturity processing applies.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms. No adverse impact expected as this is a scheduled maturity event.
