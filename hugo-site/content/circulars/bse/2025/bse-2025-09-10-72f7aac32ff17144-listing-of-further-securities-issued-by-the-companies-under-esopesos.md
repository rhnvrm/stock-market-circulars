## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional securities issued by 15 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 15 companies are listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total securities being listed range from 500 shares (Vardhman Textiles) to 291,253 shares (Fedbank Financial Services)
- All securities will be available for trading from September 11, 2025
- Face values vary from ₹1 to ₹10 per share across different companies
- None of the securities are under lock-in period

## Companies and Details

- **Brainbees Solutions Limited**: 76,000 shares at ₹2 face value
- **City Union Bank Ltd**: 40,780 shares at ₹1 face value
- **Cyient Limited**: 2,106 shares at ₹5 face value
- **Fedbank Financial Services Limited**: 291,253 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Five-Star Business Finance Ltd**: 19,990 shares at ₹1 face value
- **Godawari Power and Ispat Ltd**: 213,355 shares at ₹1 face value
- **ICICI Prudential Life Insurance**: 11,476 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Intellect Design Arena Limited**: 42,489 shares at ₹5 face value
- **Max Healthcare Institute Limited**: 9,588 shares at ₹10 face value
- **NIIT Ltd**: 83,359 shares at ₹2 face value
- **Quick Heal Technologies Limited**: 56,575 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Star Health and Allied Insurance**: 5,300 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Stove Kraft Limited**: 15,000 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Tejas Networks Limited**: 108,699 shares at ₹10 face value
- **Vardhman Textiles Limited**: 500 shares at ₹2 face value

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The listing of ESOP securities typically represents employee stock option exercises and does not materially affect the companies' market capitalization or trading dynamics. The securities are immediately available for trading without any lock-in restrictions.
