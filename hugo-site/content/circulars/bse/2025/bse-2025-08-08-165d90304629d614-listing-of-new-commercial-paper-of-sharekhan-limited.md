## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Sharekhan Limited worth Rs. 300 crore (6,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each
- Total quantity: 6,000 units (Rs. 300 crore)
- Scrip Code: 729991, Scrip ID: SL70825
- ISIN Number: INE211H14AD7
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- No credit rating specified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- No issuing and paying agent specified

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 7, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The securities provide short-term funding instrument for Sharekhan Limited with 3-month maturity period.
