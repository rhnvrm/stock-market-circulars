## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED on its Debt segment effective December 01, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, bearing interest rate of 10.20% p.a. with monthly interest payments and maturity date of November 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- Security Name: MFL-10.20%-28-11-27-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE296Q07159
- Scrip Code: 977330
- Quantity: 25,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore
- Interest Rate: 10.20% p.a. (payable monthly)
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Maturity Date: November 28, 2027
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296Q07159
- Trading members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise
- Members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly
- **Redemption Date**: November 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on private placement basis and will be traded only in dematerialized form on BSE Debt segment. The CRISIL BBB/Stable rating indicates adequate safety for investors. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular income to debenture holders. Market-wide operational impact is negligible as this affects only participants dealing with Moneyboxx Finance debt instruments.
