## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of ECL Finance Limited effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- ECL Finance Limited has set record date for redemption of debentures
- Trading suspension applies to debenture code ECL-9.60%-13-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE804I07J05, Code: 953285)
- No dealings permitted from December 29, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine suspension related to corporate action (redemption) of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- Only applies to the specific ISIN mentioned: INE804I07J05

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (9.60% series maturing January 13, 2026). The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will not be able to trade these securities from the suspension date. Investors on record as of December 29, 2025 will be eligible for redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified debenture code effective from the suspension date.
