## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD (Scrip Code 976843) effective December 08, 2025. The resumption follows confirmation that none of the debenture holders exercised the put option during the record date period of November 19, 2025. This notice is issued in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251113-74 dated November 13, 2025, which had informed about the record date for redemption and interest payment.

## Key Points

- Trading in PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD debentures (Scrip Code 976843) to resume from December 08, 2025
- No debenture holders exercised the put option on the securities
- Previous notice (20251113-74) dated November 13, 2025 had set record date as November 19, 2025
- Record date was for redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice issued under reference DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice regarding resumption of trading following completion of put option exercise period.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note that the debentures will be available for trading from the specified date. No additional compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Original exchange notice issued (20251113-74)
- **November 19, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 05, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption
- **December 08, 2025**: Trading resumption effective date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and limited to holders and potential traders of PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD debentures. Since no debenture holders exercised the put option, there will be no redemption activity, and normal trading can resume. This indicates debenture holders' confidence in continuing to hold the securities. The resumption restores normal market liquidity for these debt instruments.
