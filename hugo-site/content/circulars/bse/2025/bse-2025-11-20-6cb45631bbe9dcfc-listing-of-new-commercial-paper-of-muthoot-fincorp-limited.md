## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 20, 2025. The instrument consists of 4000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ rating, with allotment date of November 19, 2025 and redemption date of November 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoot Fincorp Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 4000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,61,295.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs.200 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730601
- **Scrip ID**: MFL191125
- **ISIN**: INE549K14CF5
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IndusInd Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CF5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025 (effective date)
- **Allotment Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 4, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 350 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment affecting only participants in the debt market interested in short-term money market instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities will have access to trade this new commercial paper instrument. The listing provides an additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for investors seeking CRISIL A1+ rated short-term debt instruments with approximately 11-month tenure. The implicit yield based on issue price and face value is approximately 9.09% per annum for the 350-day tenure.
