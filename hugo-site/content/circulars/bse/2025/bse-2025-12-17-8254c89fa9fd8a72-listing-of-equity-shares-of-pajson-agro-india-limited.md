## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Pajson Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544657, symbol PAJSON, and ISIN number INE14LM01012. The exact date of listing and further details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Pajson Agro India Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Trading Symbol: PAJSON
- ISIN Number: INE14LM01012
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice for an SME company. Impact is limited to investors and trading members interested in this specific security. The announcement does not affect broader market operations or existing listed securities. Once listed, PAJSON shares will be available for trading on the BSE SME platform.
