## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. A total of 2000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been listed with ISIN INE007N14EN8.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Fedbank Financial Services Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Quantity: 2000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730720
- Scrip ID: FFSL81225
- ISIN: INE007N14EN8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE007N14EN8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 9, 2026
- Notice Date: December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for commercial paper on the debt segment. The impact on broader market operations is minimal. The listing provides short-term funding instrument (91-day maturity) for Fedbank Financial Services Limited. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the specific trading parameters and contact details for clarifications.
