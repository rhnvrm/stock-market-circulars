## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 771 dated December 8, 2025. The auction involves 93 scrips across a wide range of companies, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 57,078 shares. This auction is conducted to close out positions where sellers have failed to deliver securities in previous trades.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 771
- Auction Date: December 8, 2025
- Total Scrips Involved: 93 companies
- Quantity Range: 1 to 57,078 shares
- Highest quantity auction: ERAAYA LIFES (531035) - 57,078 shares
- Notable large quantity auctions: IKOMA TECHNO (10,763 shares), BANK OF BARODA (2,000 shares), HFCL LIMITED (1,900 shares), ONWARD TECHN (1,800 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members involved in the auction must participate in the settlement process
- Buyers must ensure payment and delivery obligations are met
- Sellers who failed to deliver must either deliver securities or pay auction differential charges
- All auction transactions must be settled as per BSE auction settlement norms

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 771

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects specific trading members and shareholders who failed to deliver securities in earlier settlements. The auction covers 93 scrips, indicating moderate delivery failures across the market.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to specific members and scrips listed. Members with short positions in these scrips will need to participate in the auction to close out their obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Direct impact on sellers who failed to deliver and must now purchase from auction at potentially higher prices. May result in financial penalties through auction differential charges.
