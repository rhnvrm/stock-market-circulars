## Summary

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) submitted a clarification to BSE and NSE on November 24, 2025, responding to a news article on Moneycontrol about HAL shares dropping up to 9% following a Tejas fighter jet crash at Dubai Air Show. The company confirmed there was no undisclosed information requiring announcement as the aircraft was in customer service, and referred to a previous communication (Letter No. CO/SEC/4(7)/2025-26/BSE & NSE Filing/56) dated November 24, 2025, regarding the Dubai Air Show incident.

## Key Points

- HAL shares dropped up to 9% following news of Tejas fighter jet crash at Dubai Air Show
- Company confirmed no negotiations were taking place (marked as "Not Applicable")
- No undisclosed information that could explain the trading movement
- Aircraft was in service of customer at the time of incident
- Company had already issued clarification about the Dubai Air Show incident via separate letter
- No regulatory or legal proceedings initiated
- Scrip Code: 541154 (BSE), Symbol: HAL (NSE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Standard disclosure requirement under stock exchange regulations to clarify news items affecting share price
- Company fulfilled obligation to respond to exchange queries regarding price movement
- Reference made to prior disclosure letter (Filing/56) demonstrating compliance with continuous disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Date of clarification letter to exchanges
- **November 24, 2025**: Date of news article on Moneycontrol
- **November 24, 2025**: Date of previous clarification letter (Filing/56) about Dubai Air Show incident

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Significant intraday price movement of up to 9% drop in HAL shares following news of aircraft incident, though company clarified no material undisclosed information exists.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Incident involved aircraft already in customer service; company maintains no announcement was required from its perspective, suggesting limited direct operational impact on HAL.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Clarification provides transparency to investors regarding the price movement and confirms no material undisclosed information, helping to address market concerns and speculation.
