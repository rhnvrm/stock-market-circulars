## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of non-convertible debentures issued by ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET II PRIVATE LIMITED effective October 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Two debenture series affected: EAIIPL-10.15%-RESET RATE-5-2-2 (ISIN: INE12WW07019, Code: 976154) and EAIII-10.15%-RESET RATE-5-2-29 (ISIN: INE12W807011, Code: 976155)
- Record date for early redemption: October 27, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: October 27, 2025
- Notice number: DR-743/2025-2026
- Both debentures carry 10.15% reset rate interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from October 27, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in these securities from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 27, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only two debenture series of a private limited company with limited trading volume.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for debenture holders - early redemption will return principal to investors ahead of maturity.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard suspension process for early redemption corporate action. Trading members need to update systems to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended securities.
