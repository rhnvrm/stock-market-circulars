## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Bhanu Infrabuild Private Limited on private placement basis, effective September 1, 2025. The securities carry a face value of ₹1 lakh each with a total quantity of 1,900 units, representing ₹19 crore in value.

## Key Points

- Security: BIPL-16.90%-31-8-29-PVT (Scrip Code: 977067)
- ISIN: INE247407013
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1,900 units
- Interest Rate: 16.90% per annum
- Credit Rating: IVR BB+ Stable
- Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for additional details

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from September 1, 2025 to August 31, 2029
- Redemption Date: August 31, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing for debt securities. The securities are available for trading on BSE's debt segment for institutional and eligible investors.
