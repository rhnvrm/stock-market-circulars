## Summary

Physicswallah Limited announced the completion of anchor investor allocation for its initial public offering on November 10, 2025. The IPO Committee, in consultation with Book Running Lead Managers, allocated 14,33,80,733 equity shares (face value ₹1 each) to 28 anchor investors at ₹109 per share, raising approximately ₹1,563.24 crores. The anchor book witnessed participation from major domestic and international institutional investors.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 14,33,80,733 equity shares
- Anchor investor offer price: ₹109 per equity share
- Face value: ₹1 per equity share
- Total funds raised from anchors: ₹1,563.24 crores (approximately)
- Number of anchor investors: 28 institutional investors
- Allocation finalized on November 10, 2025
- Major participants include ICICI Prudential, Kotak, Nippon India, Franklin Templeton, Goldman Sachs, Fidelity, and domestic mutual funds
