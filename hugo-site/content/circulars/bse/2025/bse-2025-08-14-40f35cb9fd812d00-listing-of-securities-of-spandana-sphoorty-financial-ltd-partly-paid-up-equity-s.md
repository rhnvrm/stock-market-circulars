## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,73,34,362 partly paid-up equity shares of Spandana Sphoorty Financial Ltd issued through rights offering. The shares will be listed in B Group and commence trading from August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,73,34,362 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value, partly paid-up at Rs. 5/-
- Scrip code: 890221, ISIN: IN9572J01019
- Issue price: Rs. 230/- (Rs. 115/- paid on application, balance in calls)
- Trading symbol: SSFLPP
- Market lot: 1 share
- Allotment date: August 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Temporary ISIN No. IN8572J01028 suspended as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 regarding activation of ISIN for additional share issues.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the new listing parameters and temporary ISIN suspension for proper trade settlement and reporting.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 18, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 71305145 to 88639506

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact limited to existing shareholders participating in rights issue. Increases tradeable securities count for Spandana Sphoorty Financial with dividend entitlement proportionate to paid-up amount.
