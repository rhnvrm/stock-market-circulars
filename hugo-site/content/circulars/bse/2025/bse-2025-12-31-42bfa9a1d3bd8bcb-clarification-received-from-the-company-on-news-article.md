## Summary

Vodafone Idea Limited has issued a clarification under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 in response to exchanges' query about a news article reporting that the Cabinet has frozen the company's AGR dues of Rs. 87,695 crore. The company has confirmed that it has not received any official communication from the Government regarding this matter and will make appropriate disclosures when any development occurs.

## Key Points

- News article reported Cabinet freezing Vodafone Idea's AGR dues of Rs. 87,695 crore
- Stock exchanges sought clarification due to material price movement on 31 December 2025
- Company confirmed no official communication received from Government on the reported matter
- Disclosure issued under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Company committed to disclose any future developments requiring disclosure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company fulfilled its obligation to clarify news reports causing material price movement
- Disclosure made to both NSE and BSE as required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Company committed to making timely disclosures when actual developments occur

## Important Dates

- **31 December 2025**: Date of news article publication
- **31 December 2025**: Date of material price movement in scrip
- **31 December 2025**: Date of clarification disclosure to exchanges

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The news article caused material price movement in Vodafone Idea's stock (IDEA - Scrip code 532822), though the company's clarification indicates no official development has occurred. Initial gains were given up following the reports.

**Operational Impact**: No immediate operational impact as no official government communication has been received. The clarification prevents market speculation based on unconfirmed reports.

**Investor Impact**: Investors should note that the reported AGR dues freeze has not been officially communicated to the company. The clarification provides transparency and prevents trading based on unverified information.
