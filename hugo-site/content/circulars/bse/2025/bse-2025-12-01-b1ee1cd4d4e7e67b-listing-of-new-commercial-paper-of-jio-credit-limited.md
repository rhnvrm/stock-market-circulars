## Summary

BSE has admitted Commercial Paper issued by Jio Credit Limited to trading on the Debt segment effective December 1, 2025. The issue consists of 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 5,000 crores, issued on private placement basis. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ ratings and will mature on February 27, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 5,000 crores aggregate)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,087 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730675
- Scrip ID: JCL281125
- ISIN: INE282H14071
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 27, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE282H14071
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding (approximately 3 months tenor) to Jio Credit Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading will be limited to institutional participants given the high denomination (Rs. 5 lakhs minimum). No impact on equity markets or retail investors. For clarifications, BSE debt department contacts are provided (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
