## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6795) will change its name to "SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED" effective December 18, 2025. This is an administrative update to inform all trading members of the exchange about the name change.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED to SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number: 6795
- Effective date: Thursday, December 18, 2025
- Contact email for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural update related to trading member identification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational circular for awareness purposes only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is purely an administrative name change for a single trading member. Other market participants should update their records to reflect the new name for future reference and communication purposes. No operational changes or disruptions are expected.
