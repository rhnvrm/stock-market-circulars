## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 11,23,200 new equity shares of Times Green Energy (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 543310) issued on rights basis. The shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, November 27, 2025. These shares were issued at a face value of Rs. 10/- each with a premium of Rs. 70/-, making the total issue price Rs. 80/- per share.

## Key Points

- Company: Times Green Energy (India) Limited
- Scrip Code: 543310
- Number of shares: 11,23,200 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 70/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 80/- per share
- Type of issue: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE0B6101012
- Distinctive Numbers: 1664001 to 2787200
- Date of Allotment: November 19, 2025
- Listing date: November 27, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes starting November 27, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **November 19, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **November 26, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 27, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The rights issue results in an increase in the company's equity capital by 11,23,200 shares. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning they carry the same rights and privileges. The impact is limited to Times Green Energy (India) Limited shareholders and does not affect broader market operations or trading rules.
