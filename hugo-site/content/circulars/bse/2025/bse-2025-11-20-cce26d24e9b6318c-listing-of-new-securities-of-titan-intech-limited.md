## Summary

BSE has listed 80,00,000 equity shares of TITAN INTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 521005) effective November 21, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to a lock-in period until August 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 80,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed on BSE
- Shares issued at premium of Rs. 4.50/- (total issue price Rs. 5.50/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: October 13, 2025
- Trading commences: November 21, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 327603911 to 335603910
- ISIN: INE807M01031
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes effective November 21, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** November 21, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The addition of 80 lakh shares increases the equity base of TITAN INTECH LIMITED. All shares are subject to lock-in until August 20, 2026, preventing immediate liquidity impact. The preferential allotment to non-promoters does not dilute promoter holding directly but increases the overall share capital.
