## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party platforms or in-house developed systems to test their trading applications and familiarize themselves with various functionalities including exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS release is planned. All trades executed during this mock session will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on November 08, 2025
- Available for members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs
- Testing opportunity for various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- No new BOLT TWS release scheduled
- Mock trades will not attract any real obligations or create rights/liabilities
- Members required to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Session Schedule

- **Login**: 11:15 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Pre-Open Order Entry**: 12:00 PM - 12:08 PM
- **Matching Period**: 12:08 PM - 12:15 PM
- **Continuous Trading**: 12:15 PM - 4:00 PM
- **Block Deal Window**: 1:00 PM - 1:15 PM
- **Closing**: 4:00 PM - 4:10 PM
- **Post-Closing**: 4:10 PM - 4:20 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback for the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Contact BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) or Help Desk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: November 08, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Feedback Deadline**: November 08, 2025 by 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine testing session with no impact on actual market operations or trading obligations. The mock trading is purely for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing members to validate their trading systems and prepare for various market scenarios. No financial obligations, margins, or settlements will arise from this session.
