## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds issued by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) with ISIN INE906B07EH2 (Code: 935580) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Bond affected: NHAI 7.39% Tax Free NCD (INE906B07EH2)
- Scrip code: 935580
- Record date: December 26, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational measure for bond redemption and interest payment processes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified NHAI bonds from December 26, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and market access for these specific tax-free bonds. Bondholders will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption.

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors holding these bonds will have their positions redeemed. No trading opportunity available from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block transactions in this security and inform clients holding these bonds about the redemption process.
