## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment, effective November 14, 2025. The T-Bills have tenures of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with specific maturity dates in 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be listed with effect from November 14, 2025
- All securities listed in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category
- Market lot size is 1 for all three instruments
- Trading will be suspended two working days before maturity/redemption date
- ISINs assigned: IN002025X331, IN002025Y339, IN002025Z336

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Standard debt instrument trading procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 14, 2025
- **91-Day T-Bill Maturity:** February 12, 2026 (Scrip Code: 805142, Symbol: 91TB120226)
- **182-Day T-Bill Maturity:** May 14, 2026 (Scrip Code: 805143, Symbol: 182TB14526)
- **364-Day T-Bill Maturity:** November 12, 2026 (Scrip Code: 805144, Symbol: 364T121126)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - routine addition of government securities to the debt market segment providing short-term investment options.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - trading members can trade these instruments through standard debt trading platforms with no special procedures required beyond the T-2 trading suspension rule before maturity.
