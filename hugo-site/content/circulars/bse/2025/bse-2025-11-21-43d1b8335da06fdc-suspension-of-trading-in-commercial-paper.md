## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 730597, ISIN: INE01C314DK8) issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, effective from November 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument's maturity date of November 25, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730597
- ISIN: INE01C314DK8
- Maturity/Redemption Date: November 25, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: November 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Cease all trading activities in the specified Commercial Paper from November 24, 2025
- Ensure no new deals are executed in scrip code 730597 (ISIN: INE01C314DK8)
- Update their trading systems to reflect the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension preceding the natural maturity of a commercial paper instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to trading members dealing in this specific debt security. No broader market implications as this is standard procedure for maturing debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No trading impact for other securities of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited.
