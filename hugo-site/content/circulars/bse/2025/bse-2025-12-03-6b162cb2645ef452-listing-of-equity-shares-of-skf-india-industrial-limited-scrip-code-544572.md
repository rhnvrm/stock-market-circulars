## Summary

SKF India (Industrial) Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing shows the company's shareholding structure as of October 15, 2025 (both NSDL and CDSL), with total equity shares of 49,437,963 distributed between promoter group and public shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares held: 49,437,963 shares (fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 25,992,059 shares (52.58%)
- Public shareholding: 23,445,904 shares (47.42%)
- Total number of shareholders: 64,256 (1 promoter entity, 64,255 public shareholders)
- All shares are in dematerialized form
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, or employee trust shares
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or otherwise encumbered

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard disclosure filing as required under existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- This filing satisfies the requirement under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- The company has declared no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares
- Shareholding pattern is filed based on records as of October 15, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** October 15, 2025 (NSDL and CDSL)
- **Filing Date:** December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a balanced distribution between promoter (52.58%) and public (47.42%) holdings, meeting SEBI's minimum public shareholding requirements. No changes in control or significant shifts in ownership structure are indicated. All shares are dematerialized with no encumbrances, reflecting good corporate governance practices.
