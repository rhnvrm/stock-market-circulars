## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited has announced the resumption of fresh subscriptions, additional purchases, and switch-in applications for the Aditya Birla Sun Life Silver ETF Fund of Fund effective Thursday, October 23, 2025. The decision follows changes in market conditions where silver is trading at a discount relative to international prices, leading to Silver ETFs trading at discounts on exchanges.

## Key Points

- Fresh subscriptions and additional purchases will be accepted from October 23, 2025
- Switch-in applications will also be accepted from the effective date
- The resumption is driven by silver trading at discount relative to international prices
- Silver ETFs are currently trading at discounts on exchanges
- The scheme is an open-ended fund of fund investing in units of Aditya Birla Sun Life Silver ETF
- Changes will be incorporated in the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)
- All other features and terms of the SID and KIM remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update modifying the acceptance policy for subscriptions in the fund based on market conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited will update the SID and KIM to reflect the resumption of subscriptions
- The addendum forms an integral part of the SID and KIM issued for the scheme
- All scheme-related documents must be read carefully by investors as per standard mutual fund disclaimer

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, October 23, 2025 - Resumption of fresh subscriptions, additional purchases, and switch-in applications
- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Addendum Number**: 45/2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors interested in the Aditya Birla Sun Life Silver ETF Fund of Fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking exposure to silver through this fund of fund route, as they can now make fresh investments and additional purchases.

**Operational Impact**: The fund will need to process incoming subscription requests and update marketing materials to reflect the acceptance of new investments.

**Strategic Context**: The resumption indicates improved market conditions with silver trading at attractive valuations relative to international prices, making it opportune for the fund to accept fresh capital.
