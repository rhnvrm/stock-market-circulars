## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the applicability of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. The circular lists 8 securities being newly shortlisted in ST-ASM framework, 6 securities moving out of the framework, and provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under ST-ASM. These changes will be effective from December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- 8 securities newly shortlisted for Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 6 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stage ASM within the framework
- Changes effective from December 02, 2025
- Some securities moving out due to inclusion in other frameworks (ESM Framework)
- Includes both regular and SME scrips
- Some scrips marked as per NSE alignment

## Regulatory Framework

The Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) is implemented to monitor securities exhibiting abnormal price movements or unusual trading patterns. Securities may be placed in different stages (I, II, III) with varying surveillance intensity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the securities under ST-ASM framework
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to listed securities
- Investors should be aware of additional monitoring on these securities
- Market participants must comply with specific trading requirements for ST-ASM securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Securities newly added to ST-ASM may experience increased scrutiny and potential trading restrictions
- Securities moving out of ST-ASM gain relief from enhanced surveillance measures
- Two securities moving to ESM Framework face continued enhanced monitoring under different framework

**Trading Impact:**
- ST-ASM securities may have additional margin requirements
- Potential impact on liquidity for affected securities
- Investors holding these securities should monitor their positions

**Investor Impact:**
- Enhanced due diligence required for ST-ASM securities
- Possible restrictions on intraday trading
- Increased awareness needed for price volatility and trading patterns
