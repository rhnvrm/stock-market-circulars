## Summary

BSE has listed 1,80,000 equity shares of Jayant Infratech Limited (Scrip Code: 543544) issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from December 3, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,80,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 103/-
- Issue price: Rs. 113/- per share
- Allotted to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive Numbers: 10156687 to 10336686
- ISIN: INE0KR801019
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in period must be observed until June 15, 2026
- All 1,80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 10156687 - 10336686) are subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 3, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential allotment represents a small issue size with lock-in restrictions preventing immediate trading. The shares are issued to Non-Promoters through warrant conversion, representing routine corporate fundraising activity. Trading members can facilitate transactions post-listing, but liquidity will be limited until lock-in expiry.
