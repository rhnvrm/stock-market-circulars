## Summary

BSE has released the comprehensive trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for December 2025. The schedule covers 22 settlement cycles (NDS-162/25-26 through NDS-183/25-26) from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025, detailing trading dates, 6A/7A entry and confirmation dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, auction dates, and auction pay-in/pay-out dates for each settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers December 1-31, 2025 with 22 settlement cycles
- Settlement numbers range from NDS-162/25-26 to NDS-183/25-26
- DP settlement numbers range from 2526162 to 2526183
- Regular pay-in at 11:00 a.m. with members required to submit instructions by 10:50 a.m.
- Auction pay-in at 9:30 a.m. with members required to submit instructions by 9:20 a.m.
- T+1 settlement cycle maintained throughout the period
- Pay-in instructions must be submitted to Depositories/banks before specified cut-off times
- Last settlement of December 2025 (NDS-183/25-26) has pay-out on January 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule announcement maintaining existing settlement procedures and timelines for the NDS segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members** must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 10:50 a.m. for regular settlements (pay-in at 11:00 a.m.)
- **Members** must submit auction pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:20 a.m. for auction settlements (pay-in at 9:30 a.m.)
- **Trading participants** must adhere to the published settlement schedule for all NDS transactions
- **Clearing members** must ensure timely submission of 6A/7A entries and confirmations on specified dates

## Important Dates

- **December 1-31, 2025**: Trading period covered by this schedule
- **December 25, 2025**: Market holiday (no settlement on this date; settlement skips from December 24 to December 26)
- **January 1, 2026**: New Year holiday reflected in settlement schedule (settlement NDS-182/25-26 pay-out)
- **January 2, 2026**: Pay-out date for last December settlement (NDS-183/25-26)
- **Cut-off time for regular pay-in instructions**: 10:50 a.m. daily
- **Cut-off time for auction pay-in instructions**: 9:20 a.m. daily

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - This schedule is essential for all NDS market participants including brokers, clearing members, and institutional investors to plan their debt securities trading and settlement activities for December 2025.

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - Routine schedule announcement with no changes to existing settlement procedures. However, participants must note the holiday adjustments and plan liquidity accordingly.

**Participant Action Required**: All NDS trading members must update their settlement calendars, ensure adequate funding arrangements for settlements spanning year-end, and coordinate with their back-office systems to reflect the schedule, particularly noting the December 25 holiday adjustment and settlements extending into January 2026.
