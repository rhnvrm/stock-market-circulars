## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 769 dated December 4, 2025. The auction includes 92 securities from various companies with specified quantities that are subject to auction due to short delivery or other settlement obligations. This is a routine market operations circular listing securities and their respective quantities that will be auctioned to complete settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 769
- Settlement Date: December 4, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 92 scrips
- Auction covers securities from diverse sectors including banking, pharma, infrastructure, technology, and manufacturing
- Notable quantities include Srestha Finvest (41,484 shares), Tilak Venture (14,066 shares), and Hikal Ltd (9,692 shares)
- Major companies included: Adani Ports, ICICI Bank, Infosys, Tata Power, Vedanta, Biocon, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery obligations must participate in the auction process
- Settlement of obligations must be completed as per BSE auction mechanism
- Buyers and sellers must fulfill their respective obligations for the listed securities
- Participants must adhere to BSE's demat auction settlement procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 769
- Auction execution and settlement timelines as per BSE standard auction procedures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 92 securities across multiple sectors

**Operational Impact:** Entities with short delivery positions in the listed securities must participate in the auction to fulfill their delivery obligations. The auction ensures orderly settlement and completion of trades where physical delivery was not made on the settlement date.

**Trading Impact:** Securities listed in the auction may experience temporary price volatility during the auction process. Members with obligations must arrange funds/securities to participate in the auction settlement.

**Sector Coverage:** Broad impact across banking, pharmaceuticals, power, technology, real estate, and manufacturing sectors.
