## Summary

BSE has listed 14,976,000 equity shares of Dachepalli Publishers Limited following the company's Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shares are being listed with various lock-in periods ranging from 1 month to over 3 years depending on the category of allotment. All shares are fully paid and in demat form.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 14,976,000 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid and in demat form
- Multiple lock-in categories with varying tenure
- Anchor investor shares: 1,125,600 shares with shorter lock-in periods
- Regular IPO allotment: 13,850,400 shares with lock-in periods ranging from 1 to 3+ years
- Listing dates: December 17, 2025 and December 26, 2025 (for anchor investors)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. This is an informational circular regarding the listing of a new security.

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Listing date for majority of shares
- **December 26, 2025**: Listing date for anchor investor shares
- **January 24, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for first category of anchor shares (564,000 shares)
- **March 25, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for second category of anchor shares (561,600 shares)
- **January 4, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for multiple categories of regular shares
- **January 4, 2028**: Lock-in expiry for multiple categories with 2+ year lock-in
- **January 4, 2029**: Lock-in expiry for categories with longest lock-in period (over 3 years)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine IPO listing. The security will now be available for trading on BSE subject to the specified lock-in periods. The staggered lock-in releases may affect liquidity, with only anchor investor shares becoming free for trading in the short term (1-3 months), while the majority of shares remain locked in for 1-3+ years.
