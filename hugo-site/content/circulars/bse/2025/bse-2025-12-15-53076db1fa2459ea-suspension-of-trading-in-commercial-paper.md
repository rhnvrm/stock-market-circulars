## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper of 13 companies effective December 17-18, 2025, due to upcoming redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified no-dealings dates. The suspension covers major corporate issuers including Adani Enterprises, Reliance Retail Ventures, Bajaj Financial Securities, and others.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension effective from December 17-18, 2025 depending on issuer
- One company (Adani Enterprises) has no-dealings from December 17, 2025
- Twelve companies have no-dealings from December 18, 2025
- Circular reference numbers: DR-779/2025-2026 and DR-780/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice implementing existing rules for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the no-dealings dates
- Adherence to record date requirements for redemption processing
- Compliance with debt segment trading regulations during suspension period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Record Date & No Dealings Start (Adani Enterprises):** December 17, 2025
- **Record Date & No Dealings Start (12 other companies):** December 18, 2025
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Commercial Paper

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects debt market liquidity for 13 commercial paper instruments temporarily. Investors holding these instruments should be aware of the trading suspension.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard redemption process with predetermined timelines. Trading members have advance notice to adjust positions.

**Issuer Impact:** Neutral - Routine redemption process for maturing commercial paper instruments. Part of normal treasury operations for these companies.
