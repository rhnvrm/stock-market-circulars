## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 100 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 5 crore) with scrip code 730679 and ISIN INE423A14YP7. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 100 securities
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 5 crore
- Scrip Code: 730679
- Scrip ID: AEL281125
- Detail Name: AEL-27-11-26-CP
- ISIN: INE423A14YP7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,64,774
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YP7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 1, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Listing Date: December 1, 2025
- Date of Redemption: November 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term financing by Adani Enterprises Ltd. The instrument has high credit ratings (CARE A1+, ICRA A1+) indicating strong creditworthiness. The listing provides additional debt market liquidity and short-term investment options for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or trading members' operations beyond standard debt trading procedures.
