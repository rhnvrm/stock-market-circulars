## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement details for Settlement No. 759 dated November 20, 2025. The circular lists 104 scrips (52 pairs) with their respective scrip codes, names, and quantities to be settled through the demat auction process. This is a routine operational notice for settling failed delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 759
- Settlement Date: November 20, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 104 scrips across various sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share to 54,435 shares depending on the security
- Notable quantities include: Shanti Education (54,435 shares), Spright Agro (10,002 shares), Emcure Pharma (15,000 shares)
- Covers securities from banking, telecom, power, pharma, engineering, and various other sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members involved in the demat auction must settle the listed securities as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Parties with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the settlement process
- Settlement to be completed as per the standard T+2 or applicable settlement cycle norms

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 20, 2025
- Settlement Number: 759

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine demat auction settlement affecting specific members who have failed delivery obligations. The quantities are relatively small for most securities and will not materially impact market liquidity.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to members participating in the auction settlement. Brokers and clearing members with positions in the listed securities need to ensure compliance with auction settlement procedures.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. This administrative process ensures orderly settlement of failed trades and maintains market integrity.
