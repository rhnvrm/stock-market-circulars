## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Tenneco Clean Air India Limited. Anchor investor allocation typically occurs one day before the IPO opens to the public and helps establish institutional confidence in the offering.

## Key Points

- Company: Tenneco Clean Air India Limited
- Event: Allocation to anchor investors in public issue
- Circular issued by BSE on November 11, 2025
- Anchor allocation is part of the IPO book-building process
- This allocation precedes the public offering period

- Public issue opening date: To be announced (typically one day after anchor allocation)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation demonstrates institutional interest and provides price discovery before retail participation. Successful anchor allocation typically indicates positive market sentiment.

**Company Impact:** Medium - Anchor allocation is a critical step in the IPO process, helping establish a credible investor base and pricing benchmark.

**Investor Impact:** The anchor allocation results provide insights into institutional appetite and can influence retail investor participation decisions when the public issue opens.
