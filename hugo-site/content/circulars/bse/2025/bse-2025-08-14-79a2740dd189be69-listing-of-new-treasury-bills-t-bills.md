## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 18, 2025, in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category. The new instruments include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day Treasury Bills with specific ISIN codes and maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills will be listed on BSE from August 18, 2025
- All T-Bills will trade in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS with market lot size of 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to maturity date
- Securities include short-term (91-day), medium-term (182-day), and long-term (364-day) options

## New Treasury Bills Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|
| 805103 | IN002025X208 | 91TB131125 | November 13, 2025 |
| 805104 | IN002025Y206 | 182TB12226 | February 12, 2026 |
| 805105 | IN002025Z203 | 364TB13826 | August 13, 2026 |

## Trading Restrictions

- Securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- Trading suspension applies on Maturity Date (T) minus two trading days (excluding bank holidays)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 18, 2025
- **91-Day T-Bill Maturity**: November 13, 2025
- **182-Day T-Bill Maturity**: February 12, 2026
- **364-Day T-Bill Maturity**: August 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these Treasury Bills expands the debt instrument options available on BSE, providing investors with government-backed securities across different maturity periods. This enhances liquidity in the debt market and offers risk-free investment alternatives for various investment horizons.
