## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 1, 2025. The listing comprises 600 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total issue size Rs. 30 crores) on the BSE Debt segment. The securities will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Navi Finserv Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,77,305 per unit
- **Quantity**: 600 units
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 30 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730669
- **Scrip ID**: NFL281125
- **ISIN**: INE342T14FE2
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities must be traded under ISIN Number INE342T14FE2

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds to the available short-term debt instruments on BSE's debt segment. With a tenure of approximately 6 months and CARE A1 rating, this represents a standard money market instrument for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is expected.
