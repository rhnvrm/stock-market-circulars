## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE296Q07043, Scrip Code: 975220) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE296Q07043 (Scrip Code: 975220)
- Debenture series: MFL-13.15%-23-11-25-PVT (13.15% coupon rate)
- No dealings permitted from November 7, 2025
- Suspension triggered by record date for redemption and interest payment
- Notice issued under reference DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure implemented when a company fixes a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE296Q07043 from the effective date
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 4, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 7, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** November 7, 2025
- **Purpose Date:** November 7, 2025 (for redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity and market access for this debenture series. Existing holders cannot exit positions through the exchange from November 7, 2025.

**Investor Impact:** High - Debenture holders should note that redemption and interest payment will be processed based on holdings as of the record date. No trading opportunity available after suspension date.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members must update their systems to block orders for the specified ISIN and communicate the suspension to affected clients and dealers.

**Debt Market Impact:** The suspension is isolated to this specific debenture series of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED and does not affect other debt securities in the market.
