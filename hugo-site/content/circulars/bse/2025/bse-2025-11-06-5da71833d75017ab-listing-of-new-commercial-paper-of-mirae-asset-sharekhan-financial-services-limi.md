## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited on its Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The issuance comprises 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, issued on private placement basis with a tenor of 91 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crore (1000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,303.50 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **ISIN**: INE550X14AL8
- **Scrip Code**: 730505
- **Scrip ID**: MASFS61125
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE550X14AL8
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine Commercial Paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited and offers debt market participants an investment option rated CRISIL A1+. The listing affects only BSE Debt segment participants and has no impact on equity markets or broader trading regulations.
