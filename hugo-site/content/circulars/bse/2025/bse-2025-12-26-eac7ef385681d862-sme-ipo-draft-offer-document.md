## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Advance Technoforge Limited, filed on December 24, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be hosted on the SME website for at least 21 days. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments via email within this period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Advance Technoforge Limited on December 24, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- PDF file: 26AdvanceTechnoforgeDraftProspectus_20251224211343.pdf
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document hosted on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 26, 2025 (deadline: January 16, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must address or consider public feedback as part of IPO process

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Advance Technoforge Limited
- **December 26, 2025**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **January 16, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)
- **Minimum hosting period**: 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the SME IPO process of Advance Technoforge Limited. The primary impact is on:

- **Potential Investors**: Opportunity to review draft prospectus and investment details before final offer
- **Company (Advance Technoforge Limited)**: Regulatory milestone in IPO process; public feedback may require revisions to offer document
- **Market Participants**: Standard transparency mechanism allowing stakeholders to provide input on proposed SME listing
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensures adherence to ICDR Regulations for public disclosure and transparency in IPO process

No immediate trading or operational impact on existing market participants or listed securities.
