## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 20, 2025. The issue consists of 30,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 1,500 crores, with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Reliance Retail Ventures Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 1,500 crores (30,000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,582.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730610
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL191125
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EI1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN Number INE929O14EI1

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Reliance Retail Ventures Limited and offers investment opportunity to institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong credit quality. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
