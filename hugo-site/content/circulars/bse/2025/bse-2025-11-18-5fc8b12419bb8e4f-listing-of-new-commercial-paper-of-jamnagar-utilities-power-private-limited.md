## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited on its Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The issue comprises 8000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued on private placement basis. The securities carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 8000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,558 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 400 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730591
- **Scrip ID**: JUPL181125
- **ISIN**: INE936D14394
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE936D14394
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 17, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment. The impact is limited to institutional investors and debt market participants dealing with short-term debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade this instrument following normal BSE Debt segment protocols.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and qualified buyers who participate in commercial paper markets. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate high credit quality and low default risk for the 91-day tenure.
