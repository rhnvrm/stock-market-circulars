## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the part redemption of non-convertible debentures issued by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd. Four debenture series will undergo part redemption with a record date of November 28, 2025. Trading in these debentures will be conducted at reduced face values effective from the same date in settlement DR-766/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Part redemption affects four debenture series of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd
- Record date for part redemption: November 28, 2025
- Trading at reduced face values effective from November 28, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-766/2025-2026
- All four debentures are privately placed non-convertible debentures

## Debenture Details

### KPDL-ZC-17-4-33-PVT (INE094I07049)
- Security Code: 974771
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 65,345 per debenture

### KPDL-21-12-33-PVT (INE094I07064)
- Security Code: 975276
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 63,705 per debenture

### KPDL-ZC-20-9-34-PVT (INE094I07072)
- Security Code: 976030
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 68,438 per debenture

### KPDL-15-10-35-PVT (INE094I07080)
- Security Code: 977231
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 99,580 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: November 28, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-766/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in these debentures will be conducted at reduced face values from November 28, 2025
- Debenture holders as on the record date will be eligible for part redemption

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action affects holders of four privately placed debenture series of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd. The part redemption reduces the outstanding face value of each debenture series, returning capital to debenture holders while maintaining the securities in circulation at lower face values. This is a routine debt management action with limited impact on broader market operations.
