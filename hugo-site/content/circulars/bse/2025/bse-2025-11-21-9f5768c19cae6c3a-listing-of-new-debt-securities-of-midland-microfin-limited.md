## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited on its Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 50,000 units at a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 12% per annum with quarterly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977306, Scrip ID: 12MML27
- ISIN Number: INE884Q07806
- Quantity: 50,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000.00 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000.00
- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Interest Rate: 12% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Redemption Date: April 4, 2027
- No Put/Call option available
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE884Q07806
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Quarterly from February 20, 2026 to April 4, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: April 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited. The listing provides liquidity options for holders of these debentures on the BSE Debt segment. The ACUITE A- credit rating indicates moderate credit quality. This is primarily an informational circular for trading members to facilitate trading of these newly listed debt instruments.
