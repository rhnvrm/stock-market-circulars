## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Godrej Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 24, 2025, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- 4000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each listed
- Scrip Code: 730628, Scrip ID: GFL241125
- ISIN Number: INE02KN14630
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,664.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14630
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments (91-day commercial paper) for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. The commercial paper has strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating low credit risk.
