## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Gallard Steel Limited. This announcement is part of the IPO process where shares are allocated to qualified institutional buyers prior to the issue opening to the general public.

## Key Points

- Company: Gallard Steel Limited
- Event: Allocation to Anchor Investors
- Type: Public Issue (IPO)
- Exchange: BSE
- Circular Date: November 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- This circular is informational for market participants
- Anchor investors must comply with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Lock-in period applicable as per SEBI regulations for anchor investor allocations

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 18, 2025
- Anchor allocation date would precede the public issue opening

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - IPO anchor allocation is a standard pre-IPO procedure that helps gauge institutional interest and price discovery. This impacts potential retail investors and market sentiment toward the issue.

**Investor Impact:** Anchor allocation typically indicates institutional confidence in the issue and provides price stability during the IPO process.

**Operational Impact:** Information circular for market awareness regarding the upcoming listing of Gallard Steel Limited on BSE.
