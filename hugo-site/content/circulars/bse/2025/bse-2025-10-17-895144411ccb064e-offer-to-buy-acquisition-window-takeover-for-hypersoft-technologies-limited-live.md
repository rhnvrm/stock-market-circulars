## Summary

BSE has announced the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for HYPERSOFT TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. The offer period runs from Monday, October 20, 2025 to Tuesday, November 4, 2025. The acquirer is offering to purchase up to 42,25,442 equity shares at Rs. 11.00 per share through BSE's OTB (Offer to Buy) platform.

## Key Points

- **Security**: HYPERSOFT TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Symbol: HYPERSOFT, ISIN: INE039D01014, Scrip Code: 539724)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 42,25,442 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 11.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: October 20, 2025 to November 4, 2025
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Multiple investor categories eligible: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals

## Session Timings

### Bid Offering Period
- **Duration**: October 20, 2025 to November 4, 2025
- **Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

### Give-up / Take-up
- **Regular Days**: October 20, 2025 to November 3, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: November 4, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: November 4, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This is a takeover offer for Hypersoft Technologies Limited affecting up to 42,25,442 equity shares. The offer price of Rs. 11.00 per share is slightly above the face value of Rs. 10.00.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and custodians need to ensure their systems and processes are ready for the OTB session. They must verify investor UCC details and demat holdings before participating. The 16-day offer window provides adequate time for shareholders to respond.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders of Hypersoft Technologies have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 11.00 per share during the acquisition window. This corporate action may indicate a change in control or significant stake acquisition in the company.
