## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED with effect from December 18, 2025. The suspension is necessitated by the company's scheduled redemption of debentures and payment of interest, with the record date set for December 18, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE01I507190, Code: 936906)
- Debenture series: KAFL-ZC-2-1-26-NCD
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date
- Notice reference: DR-780/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED from December 18, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- All transactions in the affected debenture series must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 18, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only a specific debenture series of a single issuer in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard corporate action procedure for scheduled redemption. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent transactions in the affected securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (KAFL-ZC-2-1-26-NCD) who will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the schedule. The suspension protects investors by preventing trades during the redemption process.
