## Summary

BSE has updated its Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 20, 2025. Eight securities are being added to the ST-ASM framework at Stage I, while three securities are moving out of the framework. The circular provides consolidated lists of all securities currently under ST-ASM surveillance.

## Key Points

- 8 securities added to ST-ASM framework at Stage I
- 3 securities moving out of ST-ASM framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stages within ST-ASM
- One SME scrip (DRA Consultants Ltd) among new additions
- One SME scrip (Clinitech Laboratory Ltd) among removals
- Changes effective from August 20, 2025

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

1. Annvrridhhi Ventures Ltd (538539)
2. Blue Cloud Softech Solutions Ltd (539607)
3. Cybele Industries Ltd (531472)
4. DRA Consultants Ltd (540144) - SME scrip
5. GTN Textiles Ltd (532744)
6. Palco Metals Ltd (539121)
7. Pradeep Metals Ltd (513532)
8. Sayaji Industries Ltd (540728)

## Securities Removed from ST-ASM Framework

1. Clinitech Laboratory Ltd (544220) - SME scrip
2. Datamatics Global Services Ltd (532528)
3. Patels Airtemp India Ltd (517417)

## Regulatory Changes

Securities under ST-ASM framework are subject to enhanced surveillance measures with specific trading restrictions and monitoring parameters. The framework operates in stages (I, II, III) with increasing levels of surveillance.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The ST-ASM framework implementation will result in enhanced monitoring and potential trading restrictions for the affected securities. Investors and traders dealing in these securities should be aware of the surveillance measures that may include price bands, trade-to-trade settlement, or other restrictions as applicable.
