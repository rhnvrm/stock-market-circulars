## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of AEQUS LIMITED. This announcement is part of the standard IPO process where shares are allocated to qualified institutional buyers before the issue opens to the general public.

## Key Points

- Company: AEQUS LIMITED
- Event: Allocation to Anchor Investors for Public Issue
- Circular Date: December 2, 2025
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural announcement as per SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

This is an informational circular. No specific compliance actions are required from market participants. The circular serves to inform the market about the anchor investor allocation ahead of the public offering.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 2, 2025
- Anchor Investor Allocation: Completed as per the circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The anchor investor allocation is a key milestone in the IPO process. It provides price discovery and demonstrates institutional interest in the offering. The allocation details help retail investors gauge demand and institutional confidence in the issue.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant for prospective IPO applicants who should review the anchor allocation details to assess institutional demand and pricing before the retail opening.
