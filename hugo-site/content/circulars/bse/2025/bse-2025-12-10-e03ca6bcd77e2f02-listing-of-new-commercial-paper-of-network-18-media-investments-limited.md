## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has successfully listed new Commercial Paper (CP) worth Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on the BSE Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The CP was issued on a private placement basis with a tenure of 91 days, maturing on March 11, 2026. The securities carry dual credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, indicating highest degree of safety for timely payment. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total Issue Size: Rs. 250 crores (5,000 Commercial Paper units)
- Face Value/Paid-up Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,643 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,357 per unit)
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Scrip Code: 730727
- Scrip ID: NMIL101225
- ISIN: INE870H14WK9
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE debt segment regulations for commercial paper issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WK9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades subject to tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 10, 2025 (effective today)
- **Date of Redemption:** March 11, 2026
- **Tenure:** 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The CP provides short-term funding for the company's working capital requirements.

**Investor Impact:** The high credit ratings (ICRA A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and minimal credit risk. The discount of approximately 1.47% over 91 days provides investors with returns commensurate with the short tenure.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members have access to trade these securities on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with standard lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs. The listing expands the debt securities available for institutional and high net-worth investors in the secondary market.
