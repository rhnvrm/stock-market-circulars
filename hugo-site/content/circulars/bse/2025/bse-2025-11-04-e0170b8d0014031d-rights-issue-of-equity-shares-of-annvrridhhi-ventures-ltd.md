## Summary

ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD (BSE Code: 538539) has announced a rights issue with a record date of November 7, 2025. The company will issue 7 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each for every 3 equity shares held by shareholders. The shares will trade on an ex-rights basis from November 7, 2025 (Settlement DR-751/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 7 new equity shares for every 3 existing shares held
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share (at par)
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per equity share
- Payment structure: Rs. 2.50 on application and Rs. 7.50 on one or more subsequent calls
- Security type: Dematerialized securities under rolling settlement
- BSE scrip code: 538539

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD equity shares on an ex-rights basis from November 7, 2025
- Trading members are required to note the record date and ex-rights date for proper settlement
- Transactions must comply with dematerialized securities rolling settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-751/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard corporate action affecting only the shareholders of ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD. The rights issue will result in dilution for non-participating shareholders.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to 7 new shares for every 3 shares held, maintaining or adjusting their stake in the company. The installment payment structure (Rs. 2.50 upfront and Rs. 7.50 on calls) provides flexibility in payment.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal broader market impact. Trading members need to ensure proper ex-rights basis trading from November 7, 2025.
