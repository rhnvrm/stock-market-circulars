## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 1000 units (Total value: Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729913
- ISIN: INE101Q14223
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,370.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis. The listing provides additional debt instrument options for institutional investors in the short-term money market segment.
