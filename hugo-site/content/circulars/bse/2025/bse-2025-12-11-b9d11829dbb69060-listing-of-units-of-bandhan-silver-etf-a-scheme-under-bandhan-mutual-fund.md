## Summary

BSE has listed units of Bandhan Silver ETF, an open-ended Exchange Traded Fund that replicates/tracks the domestic price of Silver. The listing becomes effective from Friday, December 12, 2025. The ETF is managed by Bandhan AMC Limited under Bandhan Mutual Fund and will trade in the 'E' Group with scrip code 544639.

## Key Points

- 527,061 units of Bandhan Silver ETF listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544639, ISIN: INF194KB1KI9
- Trading name on BOLT System: SILVERBND
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 178.35 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 5, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: E
- Units will only be traded in dematerialized form
- Market Maker: Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, which mandate that mutual fund units be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that units will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Members requiring clarification should contact the specified persons at the AMC or BSE

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** December 5, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands investment options for market participants interested in silver exposure through exchange-traded instruments. The ETF provides investors with an opportunity to gain exposure to domestic silver prices through a dematerialized, exchange-traded format. Impact is limited to investors specifically interested in silver ETF products.
