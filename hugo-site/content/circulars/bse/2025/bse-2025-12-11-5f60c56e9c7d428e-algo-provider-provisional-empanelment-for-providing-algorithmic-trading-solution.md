## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Quantiply Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to exchange trading members. This empanelment is effective immediately. The complete list of approved Algo Providers is available on BSE's website.

## Key Points

- Quantiply Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Empanelment is on a provisional basis
- Circular is effective immediately from December 11, 2025
- Updated list of all Algo Providers available on BSE website

## Vendor Details

**Vendor Name:** Quantiply Technologies Private Limited

**Address:** Plot no: 344 Dharamshi Bhuvan, 4th floor, Chandavarkar crossroad, Matunga E, Mumbai – 400019

**Contact:** +91 99202 66899

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an addition to the list of approved vendors for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members. This is an informational notice about vendor availability. Trading members may choose to engage with the newly empanelled Algo Provider for their algorithmic trading needs.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 11, 2025 (Immediate effect)
- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine vendor empanelment that expands the options available to trading members for algorithmic trading solutions.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional approved vendor option. No mandatory changes required for existing operations.

**Member Impact:** Optional benefit for trading members who may require or wish to evaluate new algorithmic trading solution providers.

## Additional Information

The complete and updated list of BSE-empanelled Algo Providers can be accessed at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
