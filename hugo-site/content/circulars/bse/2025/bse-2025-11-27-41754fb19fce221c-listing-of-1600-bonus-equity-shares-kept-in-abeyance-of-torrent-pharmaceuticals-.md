## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of 1,600 bonus equity shares of Torrent Pharmaceuticals Ltd (Scrip Code: 500420) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from November 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,600 bonus equity shares of Rs. 5/- each being released from abeyance
- Face value: Rs. 5 per share
- ISIN: INE685A01028
- Company: Torrent Pharmaceuticals Ltd
- Scrip Code: 500420
- Two tranches: 800 shares (distinctive nos. 338437041-338437840) and 800 shares (distinctive nos. 338411241-338412040)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing of previously held abeyance shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance actions required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 28, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The release of 1,600 bonus shares from abeyance represents a negligible addition to Torrent Pharmaceuticals' total share capital. This is an administrative action to complete the distribution of bonus shares that were previously kept pending due to specific shareholder circumstances. Trading members are simply notified of the availability of these shares for normal trading activities.
