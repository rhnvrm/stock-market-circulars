## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The issue comprises 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 300 crores, with a tenor of 91 days from allotment to redemption.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 6,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 300 crores
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730588
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL171125
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DJ0
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DJ0
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 16, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offers investment opportunity to institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. There is no impact on equity markets or general trading operations. Trading members seeking clarifications may contact BSE debt department.
