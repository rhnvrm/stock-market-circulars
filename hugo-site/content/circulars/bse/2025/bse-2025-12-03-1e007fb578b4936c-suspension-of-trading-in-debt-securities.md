## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities (debentures) of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 04, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the 11% debentures (ISIN: INE01YL07284, Scrip Code: 975669) which mature on December 05, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 975669
- ISIN: INE01YL07284
- Security: ESPL-11%-5-12-25-PVT (11% debentures)
- Maturity Date: December 05, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: December 04, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action related to debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 04, 2025
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No trading activity should be undertaken in scrip code 975669 after the effective date

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| December 03, 2025 | Notice issued |
| December 04, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date |
| December 05, 2025 | Debenture maturity date |

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has minimal market impact as it affects only a specific private placement of debentures from a private limited company. The suspension is procedural and routine, occurring one day before maturity to prevent trading in securities that are about to mature. Impact is limited to existing debenture holders of Earlysalary Services Private Limited who will receive their principal and final interest payment upon maturity. No broader market implications or systemic concerns.
