## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Muthoot Microfin Limited on private placement basis effective November 14, 2025. The instrument consists of 500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating, and maturing in 6 months on May 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoot Microfin Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,79,317 per unit (discount of Rs. 20,683)
- **Scrip Code**: 730570
- **Scrip ID**: MML141125
- **ISIN**: INE046W14129
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE046W14129
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment
- Market participants must use the designated tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 13, 2026
- **Tenor**: 180 days (approximately 6 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing representing short-term borrowing of Rs. 25 crores by Muthoot Microfin Limited. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors participating in private placement. The instrument offers a 6-month maturity with an implicit yield based on the discount to face value.

**Operational Impact**: None for broader market. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now transact in this instrument through BSE Debt segment. For clarifications, members can contact BSE debt department.
