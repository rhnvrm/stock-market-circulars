## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The listing includes 2000 securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 18, 2025. The securities are rated ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 2000 Commercial Paper securities listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 729763, Scrip ID: MOFSL2725A
- ISIN: INE338I14KP9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue price: Rs. 481,579 per security
- Market lot: 1 security
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE338I14KP9
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applies

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 18, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment. The securities provide short-term financing instruments for Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited with approximately 6-month maturity. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can access these highly-rated commercial papers for their clients.
