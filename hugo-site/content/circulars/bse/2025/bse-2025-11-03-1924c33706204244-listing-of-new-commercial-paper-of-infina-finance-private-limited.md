## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 03, 2025. The instrument carries credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 Commercial Papers of Rs.5,00,000 face value each
- Scrip Code: 730477
- Scrip ID: IFPL311025
- Detail Name: IFPL-30-1-26
- ISIN: INE879F14LT8
- Issue Price: Rs.491,484.00 per unit
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14LT8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** October 31, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of commercial paper on private placement basis in the debt segment. The listing provides short-term funding instrument for Infina Finance Private Limited with a tenor of approximately 3 months. Trading members can now trade these instruments on BSE's debt platform.
