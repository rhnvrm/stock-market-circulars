## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) of Rs. 5,00,000/- each on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment. The listing consists of 5,000 units with a total value of Rs. 250 crore, effective from December 10, 2025. The instruments are rated ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,643/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730727
- Scrip ID: NMIL101225
- ISIN: INE870H14WK9
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE870H14WK9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instruments are short-term debt securities with a maturity of 91 days. Trading members are informed for awareness purposes. The listing provides short-term funding options for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited and investment opportunities for debt market participants. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
