## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED on private placement basis effective November 25, 2025. The securities comprise 23,000 units with face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying 11.5% p.a. interest rate with quarterly payment and maturing on March 5, 2027. The securities are rated CARE BBB- and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED
- **Quantity**: 23,000 units (Rs. 23 crore total face value)
- **Scrip Code**: 976467
- **Scrip ID**: 115AFL27
- **ISIN**: INE0RU307155 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 9,965.34 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE BBB-
- **Interest Rate**: 11.5% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from June 5, 2025 to March 5, 2027
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0RU307155
- Securities to be traded with tick size of 1 paise on BSE Debt segment
- Members can refer to Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: June 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from June 5, 2025 to March 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with limited broader market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members can trade these securities on BSE Debt segment with standard settlement cycles.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt market participants and institutional investors seeking fixed-income instruments. The CARE BBB- rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate safety for timely payment obligations. The 11.5% interest rate reflects the credit risk profile and current market conditions for such instruments.
