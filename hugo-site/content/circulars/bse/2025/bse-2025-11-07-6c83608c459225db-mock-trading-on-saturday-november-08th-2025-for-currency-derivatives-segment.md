## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. Trading members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs can test their trading applications during this session. No new BOLT TWS Release is planned. The mock trading is for testing and familiarization only, with no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or creation of rights and liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 08, 2025
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- Members can test trading applications including exceptional market conditions
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Trades will not attract any real obligations or create rights and liabilities
- Feedback requested from all members by 5:00 PM on the session day

## Trading Session Timings

- **Log-in**: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives)**: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives)**: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Trade Modification (Currency)**: 04:10 PM onwards
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency)**: 04:10 PM onwards

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the session day
- Contact BSE Tech Support or Help Desk for queries or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025 (Saturday)**: Mock trading session

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine testing session with minimal impact on regular operations. No real trading obligations or liabilities will be created. The session allows members to test their systems and familiarize themselves with trading functionalities in a simulated environment. Regular trading activities are not affected as this occurs on a Saturday.
