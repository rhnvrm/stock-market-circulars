## Summary

Ravelcare Limited (formerly Ravelcare Private Limited) has finalized the allocation of 5,25,000 equity shares to anchor investors at ₹130 per share (face value ₹10, premium ₹120) on November 28, 2025. The allocation raised ₹6.82 crores from 6 institutional investors. The company confirmed no applications were received from domestic mutual funds.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 5,25,000 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: ₹130 per equity share (face value ₹10 + premium ₹120)
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: ₹6,82,50,000 (₹6.82 crores)
- Number of anchor investors: 6 institutional investors
- Largest allocation: NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund received 1,40,000 shares (26.66%)
- No applications received from domestic mutual funds
- Board meeting date for allocation finalization: November 28, 2025

## Anchor Investor Details

1. **NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund**: 1,40,000 shares (26.66%) - ₹1.82 crores
2. **Lighthouse Canton Special Opportunities Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
3. **Viney Growth Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
4. **Finvesta First**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
5. **J4S Venture Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
6. **NAVbharat Investment Opportunities Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
