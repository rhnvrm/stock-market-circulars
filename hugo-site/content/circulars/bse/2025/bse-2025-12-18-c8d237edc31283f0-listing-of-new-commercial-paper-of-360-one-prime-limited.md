## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper on BSE's Debt segment on December 18, 2025. The instrument consists of 4000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, issued on private placement basis. The paper carries dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a 91-day maturity period.

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard commercial paper listing notification.

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U14SR4
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price quotations

- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in commercial paper. The instrument provides a short-term investment option for institutional investors seeking highly-rated paper.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for debt market participants and investors in 360 ONE Prime Limited's short-term instruments. The A1+ rating from both CRISIL and ICRA indicates highest safety for a commercial paper instrument.

**Liquidity Consideration**: Standard lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs makes this accessible primarily to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals in the debt market.
