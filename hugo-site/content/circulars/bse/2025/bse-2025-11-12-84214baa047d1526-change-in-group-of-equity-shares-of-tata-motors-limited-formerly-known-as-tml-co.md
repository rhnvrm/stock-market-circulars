## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Tata Motors Limited (formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) with Scrip Code 544569 will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Tata Motors Limited (Formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Scrip Code: 544569
- New Trading Group: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Wednesday, November 26, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251112-15
- Notice Date: November 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Tata Motors Limited will be moved to Trading Group 'B' on BSE. This reclassification changes the trading parameters and surveillance framework applicable to the security. Group 'B' securities typically have different margin requirements, settlement procedures, and surveillance measures compared to other groups.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for their trading systems and risk management frameworks
- Updated trading parameters for Group 'B' securities will apply to this scrip from the effective date
- Trading members should ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 26, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The reclassification to Group 'B' may affect trading dynamics, margin requirements, and settlement processes for this scrip. However, trading will continue without interruption.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems and risk parameters to accommodate the group change. The two-week notice period provides adequate time for necessary adjustments.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or trading this security should be aware of any changes in margin requirements or trading conditions that may apply under Group 'B' classification.
