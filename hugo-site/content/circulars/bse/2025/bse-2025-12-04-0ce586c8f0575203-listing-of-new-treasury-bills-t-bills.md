## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective December 05, 2025. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities with specific ISINs and trading symbols. Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each maturity date.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills to be listed on BSE Debt segment from December 05, 2025
- All securities listed under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category
- Market lot size: 1 unit for all three T-Bills
- Trading suspension applies two working days before maturity/redemption date
- Contact numbers provided for clarifications: 2272 8352/5753/8597

## New Treasury Bills Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenure |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|--------|
| 805151 | IN002025X364 | 91TB260226 | March 05, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805152 | IN002025Y362 | 182TB28526 | June 04, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805153 | IN002025Z369 | 364T261126 | December 03, 2026 | 364 days |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing effective date of December 05, 2025
- Trading members must be aware that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date (T-2)
- Bank holidays are excluded from the T-2 calculation

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 05, 2025
- **91-Day T-Bill Maturity**: March 05, 2026 (Trading stops March 03, 2026)
- **182-Day T-Bill Maturity**: June 04, 2026 (Trading stops June 02, 2026)
- **364-Day T-Bill Maturity**: December 03, 2026 (Trading stops December 01, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The listing of government Treasury Bills provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional and retail investors in the debt segment. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets. Government securities like T-Bills are considered risk-free instruments and their listing is a regular market activity.
