## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective indices. LG Electronics India Limited (ticker 544576), listed on October 14, 2025, will be added to the BSE IPO index effective at market open on October 16, 2025. Mittal Sections Limited (ticker 544575), listed on the SME platform on October 14, 2025, will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective at market open on October 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- LG Electronics India Limited (544576) listed on BSE main board effective October 14, 2025
- LG Electronics India Limited to be added to BSE IPO index effective October 16, 2025
- Mittal Sections Limited (544575) listed on BSE SME platform effective October 14, 2025
- Mittal Sections Limited to be added to BSE SME IPO index effective October 15, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20251013-65 for LG Electronics India listing
- References Notice No: 20251013-59 for Mittal Sections listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational notice regarding index composition updates.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice about index additions following standard procedures after new listings.

## Important Dates

- **October 14, 2025**: Listing date for both LG Electronics India Limited and Mittal Sections Limited
- **October 15, 2025**: Mittal Sections Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **October 16, 2025**: LG Electronics India Limited added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. These are routine index additions following new listings. The additions will primarily affect index funds and passive investment vehicles tracking the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Index fund managers tracking these indices will need to adjust their portfolios to include these securities at the specified effective dates. The staggered implementation dates allow for orderly portfolio rebalancing.
