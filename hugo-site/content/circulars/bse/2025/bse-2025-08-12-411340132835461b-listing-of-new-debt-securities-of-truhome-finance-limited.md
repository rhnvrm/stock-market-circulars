## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Truhome Finance Limited worth Rs. 20 crores on private placement basis, effective from August 12, 2025. The securities carry a 7.90% annual interest rate and will mature on August 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 20 crores
- Interest rate: 7.90% per annum
- Credit rating: IND AA/Stable
- ISIN: INE432R07489
- Scrip Code: 977014
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from August 8, 2026 to August 8, 2028
- Maturity Date: August 8, 2028
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The listing provides additional debt instrument options for institutional investors but does not affect broader market operations or retail trading significantly.
