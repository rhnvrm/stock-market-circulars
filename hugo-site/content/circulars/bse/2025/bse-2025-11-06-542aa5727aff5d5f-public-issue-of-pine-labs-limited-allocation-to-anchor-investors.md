## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation to anchor investors for the public issue of Pine Labs Limited. This circular provides details of the anchor investor allocation process, which is conducted prior to the opening of the public issue to gauge institutional demand and establish price discovery.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation conducted for Pine Labs Limited's public issue
- Allocation details include institutional investor participation and amounts allocated
- Anchor allocation typically happens one day before the issue opens to retail investors
- Provides price stability and institutional validation for the upcoming IPO

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification as per SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must disclose anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018
- Information to be made available to all investors before the issue opens

## Important Dates

- Anchor investor allocation date: November 6, 2025
- Public issue is expected to open shortly following the anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation for Pine Labs Limited indicates institutional interest in the IPO. Successful anchor allocation typically provides:
- Price discovery mechanism for the public issue
- Institutional validation and confidence for retail investors
- Market indication of demand levels
- Reduced volatility on listing day through institutional participation
