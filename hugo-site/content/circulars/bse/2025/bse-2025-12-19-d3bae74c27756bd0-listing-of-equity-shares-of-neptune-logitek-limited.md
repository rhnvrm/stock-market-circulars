## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Neptune Logitek Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544663, symbol NEPLOG, and ISIN number INE1NQ501012. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544663
- Trading Symbol: NEPLOG
- ISIN: INE1NQ501012
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details for Neptune Logitek Limited
- Members should await separate notice for exact listing date and trading commencement details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine listing notification for a single SME company. Trading members need to update their systems with the scrip details but no immediate action is required until the actual listing date is announced. The listing will add one more security to the SME segment but is unlikely to have broader market implications.
