## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544643 and symbol PRODOCS with ISIN number INE10XK01019. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544643
- Symbol: PRODOCS
- ISIN Number: INE10XK01019
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Details of securities to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the assigned scrip code and symbol for the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required as listing date pending.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement for an SME company. Impact is limited to trading members who need to be aware of the new security for when trading commences. The listing will add one more equity security to the BSE SME platform. Market-wide impact is minimal as this is a routine corporate action. Investors interested in the SME segment should await the detailed listing notice for trading commencement date.
