## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544669, symbol ADMACH, and ISIN number INE0XLO01010. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544669
- Symbol: ADMACH
- ISIN: INE0XLO01010
- Segment: SME
- Listing to be completed in due course
- Specific listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in the SME segment. No immediate trading is possible until the formal listing date is announced through a subsequent circular. The announcement provides preliminary information to prepare trading members for the upcoming listing.
