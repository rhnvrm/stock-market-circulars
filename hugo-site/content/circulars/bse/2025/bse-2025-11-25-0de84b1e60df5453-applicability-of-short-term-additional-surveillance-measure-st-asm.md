## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective November 26, 2025. Four securities are being added to the ST-ASM framework (Stage I), while six securities are being removed from the framework. No securities are being moved to higher or lower stages within the ST-ASM framework.

## Key Points

- 4 securities added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework (Stage I)
- 6 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- Changes effective from November 26, 2025
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Framework includes both mainboard and SME scrips
- Consolidated list provided with current ASM stage classifications

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

**New Entries (Stage I) effective November 26, 2025:**

1. **Jet Freight Logistics Ltd** (Scrip Code: 543420, ISIN: INE982V01025)
2. **Mayur Leather Products Ltd** (Scrip Code: 531680, ISIN: INE799E01011)
3. **Mish Designs Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544015, ISIN: INE0QI101019) - SME Scrip
4. **Olatech Solutions Ltd** (Scrip Code: 543578, ISIN: INE0M3901015) - SME Scrip

## Securities Removed from ST-ASM Framework

**Moving Out effective November 26, 2025:**

1. **Anand Rathi Share And Stock Brokers Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544530, ISIN: INE549H01021)
2. **GB Logistics Commerce Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544348, ISIN: INE0RWU01016) - SME Scrip
3. **Kanungo Financiers Ltd** (Scrip Code: 540515, ISIN: INE453S01015)
4. **Ladderup Finance Ltd** (Scrip Code: 530577, ISIN: INE519D01015)
5. **Shree Hari Chemicals Export Ltd** (Scrip Code: 524336, ISIN: INE065E01017)
6. **Southern Magnesium & Chemicals Ltd** (Scrip Code: 513498, ISIN: INE308N01012)

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework continues to operate with its existing structure of Short Term 5/15/30 Days stages. Securities can be removed from ST-ASM due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks including:

- LT-ASM Framework (Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure)
- Trade for Trade Framework
- GSM Framework (Graded Surveillance Measure)
- Pledge Framework
- ESM Framework (Enhanced Surveillance Measure)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adjust their risk management systems for securities entering ST-ASM
- Enhanced surveillance and monitoring required for securities in ST-ASM Stage I
- Investors should be aware of additional trading restrictions applicable to ST-ASM securities
- Securities in ST-ASM may have stricter price bands and position limits

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 26, 2025
- All changes to ST-ASM framework become applicable from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Securities entering ST-ASM may experience reduced liquidity due to enhanced surveillance measures
- Price volatility may be contained through stricter monitoring
- Securities exiting ST-ASM framework should see improved liquidity conditions

**Operational Impact:**
- Brokers and trading members need to update risk management systems
- Additional monitoring and reporting requirements for securities in ST-ASM
- Potential impact on margin requirements for positions in affected securities

**Investor Impact:**
- Increased scrutiny on trading patterns in newly added securities
- Improved trading conditions for securities moving out of ST-ASM
- Investors should review positions in affected securities before effective date
