## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Finnable Credit Private Limited on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 12.25% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Quantity listed: 3,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977363
- Scrip ID: 1225FCPL28
- ISIN: INE14H407074
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 12.25% per annum
- Interest Payment: Monthly from January 11, 2026 to June 11, 2028
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE14H407074
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Listing Date: December 12, 2025
- First Interest Payment: January 11, 2026
- Redemption Date: June 11, 2028
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from January 11, 2026 to June 11, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors access to Finnable Credit Private Limited's debt instruments rated CARE BBB+/Stable. The securities offer a competitive 12.25% annual return with monthly interest payments over a tenure extending to June 2028. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in private placement securities.
