## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251124-52 directing trading members to suspend dealing in Non-Convertible Debentures (MMFL-ZC-17-12-25-NCD, ISIN: INE101Q07607, Scrip Code: 937127) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited effective December 2, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of these debentures on the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific NCD series: MMFL-ZC-17-12-25-NCD
- ISIN: INE101Q07607, BSE Scrip Code: 937127
- No dealings permitted from December 2, 2025
- Record date for redemption: December 2, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-768/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension implemented as per existing BSE rules for debenture redemption processes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified debentures from December 2, 2025
- Trading members are advised not to execute any buy or sell orders for ISIN INE101Q07607
- Members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive for corporate action processing

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **December 2, 2025**: Record date for redemption
- **December 2, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects single debenture series of one company only

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures will have their holdings redeemed as per the terms. No trading will be possible from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified scrip code to ensure compliance with the suspension directive. The suspension is temporary and specific to the redemption corporate action.
