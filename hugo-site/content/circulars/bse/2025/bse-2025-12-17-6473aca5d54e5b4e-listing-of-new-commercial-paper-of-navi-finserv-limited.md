## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 510 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- 510 Commercial Paper securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue price: Rs. 4,76,715 per security
- Market lot size: 1 security
- Credit rating: IND A1
- Scrip Code: 730786
- Scrip ID: NFL161225
- ISIN: INE342T14FF9
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE342T14FF9

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Navi Finserv Limited with a 6-month maturity period. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in IND A1 rated commercial paper instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
