## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that BSE exclusive scrips in the Equity segment will be available for trading on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) at 2:39 PM. This notice references exchange notice no. 20251211-75 dated December 11, 2025, regarding the invocation of an alternate trading venue.

## Key Points

- BSE exclusive scrips in Equity segment will be available on NSE alternate trading venue
- Trading will commence at 2:39 PM on the alternate venue
- This action is in reference to prior notice no. 20251211-75 dated December 11, 2025
- All trading members are notified of this arrangement

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is an operational measure to invoke alternate trading venue provisions for BSE exclusive scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must be prepared to handle orders for BSE exclusive scrips on NSE platform
- Members should ensure systems and processes are ready for dual-venue trading from 2:39 PM
- Members should refer to earlier notice 20251211-75 for complete context and requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 13, 2025 at 2:39 PM**: BSE exclusive scrips become available for trading on NSE
- **December 11, 2025**: Reference date of original notice (20251211-75)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - BSE exclusive scrips gaining access to NSE platform provides alternate liquidity venue and ensures market continuity. This is a significant operational measure that affects trading patterns and member operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure readiness for handling BSE scrips on NSE platform, including order routing, risk management, and reconciliation processes across both exchanges.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional trading venue and potentially better price discovery for BSE exclusive scrips through alternate market access.
