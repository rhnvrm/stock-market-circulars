## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 97,561 new equity shares of Quest Flow Controls Limited (Scrip Code: 543982) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on December 5, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 97,561 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 400/-
- Issue price: Rs. 410/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0OYE01013
- Distinctive numbers: 10160063 to 10257623
- Date of allotment: August 7, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities
- Lock-in period must be observed for all 97,561 shares until June 30, 2026
- These shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing with minimal market-wide impact. The preferential allotment represents a relatively small number of shares with lock-in restrictions, limiting immediate trading activity. The shares are issued to non-promoters and will dilute existing shareholding marginally. Trading members dealing in Quest Flow Controls Limited should update their records to reflect the expanded share capital.
