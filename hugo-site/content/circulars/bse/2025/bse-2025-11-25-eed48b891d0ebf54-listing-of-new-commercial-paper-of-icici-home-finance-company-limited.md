## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new commercial papers issued on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The listing consists of 4,500 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 225 crores. The commercial papers carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a tenure of 91 days maturing on February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4,500 commercial papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- **Scrip Code**: 730647
- **Scrip ID**: IHF251125
- **ISIN Number**: INE071G14HG5
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,606.50 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI BANK LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HG5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and enhances liquidity in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. The implicit yield of approximately 6% per annum reflects current money market conditions. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or retail investors.
