## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of Choice Gold ETF units effective Monday, November 10, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended Exchange Traded Fund that replicates/tracks the domestic price of gold. A total of 4,749,787 units will be listed in the E Group with scrip code 544601, trading in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Choice Gold ETF (CHOICEGOLD)
- Mutual Fund: Choice Mutual Fund, managed by Choice AMC Private Limited
- Total Units Listed: 4,749,787 units
- Scrip Code: 544601
- ISIN: INF2KCX01012
- Face Value: Rs. 100.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 119.94 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: E
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market Makers: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited, IRAGE Broking Services LLP, RiddiSiddhi Bullions Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to trade in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Units will trade in market lot of 1 unit
- Trading Members should note the E Group classification for this ETF

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025 (Monday)
