## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 748 dated November 4, 2025. The auction includes 108 securities from various companies with specified quantities that need to be settled through the auction mechanism. This is a routine settlement process for securities that failed regular delivery.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 748
- Settlement Date: November 4, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 108 securities from 108 different companies
- Quantities range from 1 share to 31,060 shares depending on the security

## Auction Mechanism

Demat auctions are conducted when sellers fail to deliver securities on the settlement date. The exchange conducts an auction to procure the securities to complete settlement obligations. Members who have failed to deliver must participate in the auction or arrange for delivery.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with short positions in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities are available for auction settlement
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Non-compliance may result in penalties and close-out charges

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 4, 2025
- Auction execution and settlement to follow BSE standard timelines

## Impact Assessment

This auction affects trading members who have failed to deliver the specified securities. The impact is specific to the 108 listed securities and their respective market participants. Members with obligations must either deliver the securities or purchase them through the auction mechanism. The wide range of securities (from 1 share to over 31,000 shares) indicates varying degrees of settlement failures across different market segments.
