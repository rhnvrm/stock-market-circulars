## Summary

The circular includes the updated company profile with details of directors, registered address, and recent changes including appointment of new statutory auditors.

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** The revocation of suspension allows normal trading to resume in the equity shares of Sidh Automobiles Limited. Shareholders can now buy and sell shares on the BSE platform.

**Market Significance:** This is a positive development for existing shareholders who were unable to trade during the suspension period. The resumption indicates the company has met BSE's requirements for lifting the trading suspension.

**Investor Considerations:** Investors should review the company's financial statements and recent developments, particularly the auditor change, before making investment decisions.
