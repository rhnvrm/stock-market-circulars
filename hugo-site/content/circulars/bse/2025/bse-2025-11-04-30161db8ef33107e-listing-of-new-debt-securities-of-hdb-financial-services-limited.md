## Summary

HDB Financial Services Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 5,000 units (market lot of 1), representing Rs. 50 crore in face value. These non-convertible debentures carry a 7.95% annual interest rate and mature on June 4, 2035.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 976817
- **Scrip ID**: 795HDBFS35
- **ISIN**: INE756I08306 (further listings under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,233 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: 5,000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AAA/Stable and CRISIL AAA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 7.95% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annual payments from June 23, 2026 to June 4, 2035
- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: June 4, 2035
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE756I08306
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: June 23, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: June 4, 2035

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing that affects only debt market participants. The securities are AAA-rated, indicating high credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip for trading purposes but no operational changes are required beyond standard debt security trading procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to institutional and qualified investors who participated in the private placement or those interested in trading these securities in the secondary market.
