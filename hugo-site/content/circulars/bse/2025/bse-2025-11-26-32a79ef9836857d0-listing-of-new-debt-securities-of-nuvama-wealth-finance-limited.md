## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The securities are gold-linked bonds with a face value of Rs. 100,000, rated CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable, and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 750 securities
- Scrip Code: 977266 (NWFL301025)
- ISIN: INE918K07QE9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,346 (issued at discount)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading Mode: Demat only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Put/Call Options: As per Key Information Document (KID)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QE9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Date of Redemption: April 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notification for debt securities on private placement basis. The impact is limited to:

- Debt segment participants who may trade these securities
- Existing investors in Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited debt instruments
- No impact on equity markets or broader market operations

The securities have a strong credit rating (AA/Stable) and will provide an additional debt instrument option for institutional and high-net-worth investors in the debt segment.
