## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The securities consist of 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at a discount with a 91-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 3000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,702 (discount of Rs. 9,298)
- **Scrip Code**: 730559
- **Scrip ID**: NVF131125
- **ISIN**: INE918K14CT3
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CT3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt financing instrument for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments. The 91-day tenor and competitive discount rate provide investors with a short-term investment option. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers.
