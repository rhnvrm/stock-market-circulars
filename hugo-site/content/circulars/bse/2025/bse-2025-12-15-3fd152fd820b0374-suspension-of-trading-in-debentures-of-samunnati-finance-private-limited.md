## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE551U07340, Code: 976216) effective December 19, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing December 21, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture: SFPL-11.25%-5-1-26-GREEN-PVT
- ISIN: INE551U07340
- BSE Code: 976216
- Record Date: December 21, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 19, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-781/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice regarding trading suspension for corporate action purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 19, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- All dealings in the debenture code 976216 (ISIN: INE551U07340) must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **December 19, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **December 21, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders and traders of this specific debenture issue. As a private placement, the impact is restricted to institutional and qualified investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trades in this security from December 19, 2025. The suspension is temporary and routine for redemption purposes.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of December 21, 2025 will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. The 11.25% coupon rate debenture is being redeemed as per its maturity schedule.
