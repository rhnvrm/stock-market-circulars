## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in JSW Steel Limited's non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE019A08033, Code: 974466) effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company's redemption on exercise of call option with the record date fixed for December 8, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for JSW Steel's 8.25% NCDs maturing December 27, 2027 (privately placed)
- ISIN: INE019A08033, BSE Code: 974466
- Suspension effective from December 8, 2025
- Reason: Redemption on exercise of call option
- Record date for redemption: December 8, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 8, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All trading activity in these NCDs must cease from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 8, 2025
- **Original Maturity**: December 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific series of JSW Steel NCDs. The early redemption via call option exercise will return principal to debenture holders before the 2027 maturity date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these securities. Debenture holders should prepare for early redemption and plan for reinvestment of proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds. Those holding these securities should not expect further trading opportunities after December 8, 2025.
