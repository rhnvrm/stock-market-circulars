## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 3, 2025. The issuance consists of 1000 units of Commercial Paper issued on a private placement basis, with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730460
- Scrip ID: OPL291025A
- Detail Name: OPL-2-6-26-CP
- ISIN: INE248U14SM5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs.4,78,000
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 3, 2025
- Listing Date: November 3, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: October 31, 2025
- Date of Redemption: June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it involves Commercial Paper issued on a private placement basis for institutional investors. The securities carry strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating high creditworthiness. The listing expands short-term debt market options for qualified investors and provides liquidity for the issuer's working capital requirements. The 7-month maturity period (October 2025 to June 2026) indicates short-term financing needs.
