## Summary

MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions from August 21, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Includes SIP, STP & SWP facility
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options)
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members need to be aware of the fund availability for client transactions starting August 21, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Fund reopening date for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund availability announcement. Provides additional investment options for investors through BSE StAR MF Platform with systematic investment and withdrawal facilities.
