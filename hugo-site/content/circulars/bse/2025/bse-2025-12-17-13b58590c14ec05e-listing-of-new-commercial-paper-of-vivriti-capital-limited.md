## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The instrument consists of 1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 50 crore) with ISIN INE01HV14AH6, issued at a discount price of Rs. 4,77,392 per unit against face value of Rs. 5,00,000, maturing on June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Vivriti Capital Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 730790 (Scrip ID: VCL161225)
- **ISIN**: INE01HV14AH6
- **Quantity**: 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Total: Rs. 50 crore)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,77,392 per unit (discount to face value)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: The Federal Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarifications may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 25, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 6 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and debt market participants. The CARE A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and strong capacity for timely payment. The discount pricing (Rs. 4,77,392 vs Rs. 5,00,000 face value) reflects the implicit interest rate for the 6-month tenure. This listing is relevant only to debt market participants and has no impact on equity markets or retail investors.
