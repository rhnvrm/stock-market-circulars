## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific debentures of MAHINDRA & MAHINDRA FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED (MMFSL) effective September 11, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MMFSL debentures with ISIN INE774D07VC5 (Code: 974993)
- Debenture series: MMFSL-7.9585%-26-9-25-PVT
- Record date set for September 11, 2025
- No dealings permitted from September 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures
- Members must take note of the suspension effective date
- Standard record date compliance procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 11, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as suspension affects only one specific debenture series of MMFSL. Trading will resume after the corporate action is completed. Standard procedure for debt securities with scheduled redemption and interest payments.
