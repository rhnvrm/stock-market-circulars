## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has published a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 754 dated November 13, 2025. The notice lists 104 scrip codes representing various securities that are subject to demat auction proceedings. The auction covers a wide range of companies across different sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 39,798 shares depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 754
- Settlement Date: November 13, 2025
- Total number of scrips listed: 104 different securities
- Quantities vary significantly across securities (minimum 1 share, maximum 39,798 shares for MONEY MASTER)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, automobiles, technology, pharmaceuticals, metals, and more
- Major blue-chip stocks included: State Bank of India (30 shares), Infosys (1 share), Asian Paints (3 shares), Bajaj Auto (1 share), Hero Motocorp (2 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Concerned trading members and clearing members must settle their obligations for the listed securities
- Parties who failed to deliver securities in the normal settlement cycle must participate in the auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's auction settlement procedures
- Members must ensure availability of securities or funds as per auction requirements

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 754
- Specific auction date and time would be communicated separately by BSE as per standard procedures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects specific parties who have outstanding delivery obligations. The auction process ensures orderly settlement and protects market integrity.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members and clearing members with exposure to these securities must arrange for settlement. Larger quantities like MONEY MASTER (39,798 shares), Murae Organic (16,258 shares), and Union Bank (4,200 shares) may require significant operational attention.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Direct impact limited to parties involved in failed settlements. Indirect impact through potential price volatility during auction process for affected securities.

**Liquidity Impact**: The auction involves 104 different securities with varying quantities, which may temporarily affect liquidity in these specific scrips during the auction period.
