## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251113-32 announcing the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment for the period from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025. The circular provides a detailed schedule of 22 settlement cycles (SL-167/25-26 through SL-188/25-26) covering all trading days in December 2025, including corresponding depository settlement numbers, trading dates, entry and confirmation dates, and pay-in/pay-out dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers 22 trading days in December 2025
- Settlement numbers range from SL-167/25-26 to SL-188/25-26
- Corresponding depository settlement numbers: 2526167 to 2526188
- SLB trading, 6A7A entry, and 6A7A confirmation occur on the same day
- Pay-in/Pay-out occurs on T+1 basis (next trading day)
- Pay-in time is 08:00 a.m., with members required to submit instructions by 07:50 a.m.
- Market holidays observed: December 6-7, 13-14, 20-21, 25, 27-28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks latest by 07:50 a.m. on the pay-in/pay-out date
- Pay-in must be completed by 08:00 a.m. on the specified pay-in/pay-out date
- All participants in SLB segment must adhere to the published settlement schedule

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **First Settlement**: SL-167/25-26 - Trading date December 1, 2025, Pay-in/Pay-out December 2, 2025
- **Last Settlement**: SL-188/25-26 - Trading date December 31, 2025, Pay-in/Pay-out January 1, 2026
- **Holiday Dates**: December 6, 7, 13, 14, 20, 21, 25, 27, 28, 2025 (no settlements scheduled)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard operational circular providing advance notice of settlement schedules to facilitate planning and operations.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The settlement cycle follows the established T+1 pattern with same-day entry and confirmation, maintaining consistency with existing SLB segment operations.

**Participant Action Required**: Routine - participants should note the settlement schedule and ensure timely submission of pay-in instructions as per the specified deadlines. No changes to existing processes or systems are required.
