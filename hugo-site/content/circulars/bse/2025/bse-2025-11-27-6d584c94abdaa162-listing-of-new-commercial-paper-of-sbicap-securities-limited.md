## Summary

SBICAP Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The issue consists of 2000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 100 crore) issued on private placement basis with a maturity period of 90 days. The instruments carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: SBICAP Securities Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore (2000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730666
- **Scrip ID**: SBIC271125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CT1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CT1
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE for institutional and high-net-worth investors. The dual A1+ ratings from CRISIL and ICRA indicate strong creditworthiness. The relatively short 90-day tenor is typical for commercial paper. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term highly-rated debt instruments of SBICAP Securities.
