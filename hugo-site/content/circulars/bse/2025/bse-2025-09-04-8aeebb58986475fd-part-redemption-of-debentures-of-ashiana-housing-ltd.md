## Summary

BSE has informed trading members about the partial redemption of debentures issued by Ashiana Housing Ltd. The company has reduced the face value per debenture from the original amount to Rs. 733,484.91 per debenture following the partial redemption process.

## Key Points

- Company: Ashiana Housing Ltd
- Scrip Code: AHL-8%-31-5-41-PVT
- ISIN: INE365D08026 (973220)
- New reduced face value: Rs. 733,484.91 per debenture
- Action type: Part redemption of debentures
- Trading effective date: September 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action following partial redemption procedures for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- Debentures will trade with the new reduced face value from the effective date
- All trading systems must be updated to reflect the new face value

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: September 5, 2025
- Document Reference: DR-709/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on debenture holders and trading members. The significant reduction in face value (to Rs. 733,484.91) indicates a substantial partial redemption. Trading members need to update their systems and inform clients about the change in face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
