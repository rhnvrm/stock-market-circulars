## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Edel Finance Company Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 21, 2025. Two series of securities with different ISINs have been admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities series listed: EFCL30725 (2000 quantity) and EFCL300725 (3000 quantity)
- Both securities have CRISIL A+/Stable credit rating
- Face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each with different issue prices
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size set at 1 paise

## Security Details

**Series 1 (EFCL30725):**
- ISIN: INE836K07304
- Scrip Code: 976967
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,069
- Quantity: 2,000
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2028

**Series 2 (EFCL300725):**
- ISIN: INE836K07312
- Scrip Code: 976968
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,245
- Quantity: 3,000
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2028

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2028

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for further details
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The securities are privately placed with institutional investors and represent standard corporate funding activities.
