## Summary

Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited has announced part redemption of two series of non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date is set for January 1, 2026, with trading in these debentures continuing at reduced face values from the same date.

## Key Points

- Part redemption involves two debenture series: EARCL-2%-7-10-28-PVT (ISIN: INE015L07576, Code: 958306) and EARC-2%-22-7-29-PVT (ISIN: INE015L07683, Code: 958889)
- Both debentures will trade at reduced face values effective January 1, 2026
- The action includes payment of interest and part redemption of principal
- Settlement number DR-789/2025-2026 applies to both securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action as per existing debenture terms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for both debenture series
- Trading will continue with reduced face values from January 1, 2026
- Members must ensure proper settlement under DR-789/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 1, 2026 - For determining debenture holders eligible for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date**: January 1, 2026 - Trading commences with reduced face values
- **Settlement**: DR-789/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**EARCL-2%-7-10-28-PVT (958306)**:
- New reduced face value: Rs. 186,644.43 per debenture

**EARC-2%-22-7-29-PVT (958889)**:
- New reduced face value: Rs. 21,174.06 per debenture

Impact is limited to holders of these specific debenture series. Part redemption reduces outstanding principal while maintaining the securities in active trading with reduced face values. This is a scheduled event as per the debenture terms and does not indicate any credit issues or early redemption triggers.
