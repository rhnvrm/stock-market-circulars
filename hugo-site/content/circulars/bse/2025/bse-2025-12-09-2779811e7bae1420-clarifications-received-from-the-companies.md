## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from three companies - West Leisure Resorts Limited (Scrip Code: 538382), Latent View Analytics Limited (Scrip Code: 543398), and Eldeco Housing and Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 523329) - in response to BSE's surveillance queries regarding significant price and volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed that the movements were market-driven and that they have no undisclosed information that could impact their share prices.

## Key Points

- **West Leisure Resorts Limited** clarified that price movement is purely market-driven with no specific reasons known to the company
- Company confirmed full compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No undisclosed information or impending announcements that may affect price behavior
- **Latent View Analytics Limited** responded to queries about significant increase in trade volume across exchanges
- Confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Stated that volume increase was due to market conditions and market-driven forces
- **Eldeco Housing and Industries Limited** provided clarification on significant price movement (reference incomplete in source document)
- All companies maintain good corporate governance practices and timely disclosures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed ongoing compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Timely disclosure of material events to stock exchanges as required under Regulation 30
- Companies committed to keeping exchanges informed and adhering to compliance requirements in the interest of investors

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: BSE surveillance email sent to West Leisure Resorts Limited (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3188)
- **December 8, 2025**: BSE surveillance email sent to Eldeco Housing (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3186)
- **December 9, 2025**: BSE surveillance email sent to Latent View Analytics (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/745)
- **December 9, 2025**: Companies submitted their clarifications to BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications confirming that price and volume movements are market-driven without any undisclosed material information.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Provides transparency that no material undisclosed information exists that could affect investment decisions. Confirms companies are compliant with disclosure norms.

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard surveillance procedure by BSE to ensure market integrity and compliance with listing regulations. No action required from investors or other market participants.
