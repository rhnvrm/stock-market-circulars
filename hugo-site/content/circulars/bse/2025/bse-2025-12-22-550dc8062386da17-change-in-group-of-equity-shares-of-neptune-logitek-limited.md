## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544663), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group effective January 06, 2026. This change follows the Exchange's Notice No. 20251219-70 dated December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED shares currently trading in MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- Scrip Code: 544663
- Transfer to M Group (Rolling segment) effective from January 06, 2026
- This is a continuation of earlier notice dated December 19, 2025 (Notice No. 20251219-70)
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED equity shares will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) typically has stricter settlement requirements where each trade is settled individually, while Rolling segment (M Group) allows for normal T+2 settlement cycles with netting of positions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Trading members should inform their clients holding or trading NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED shares about the segment transfer
- Risk management parameters and position limits may need adjustment based on the new trading group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Previous Notice Date:** December 19, 2025 (Notice No. 20251219-70)
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment is generally positive for market participants as it provides:
- Enhanced liquidity through normal rolling settlement
- Ability to net positions within the settlement cycle
- Reduced settlement burden compared to individual trade settlement
- Potential for increased trading volumes due to improved flexibility

**Market Participants Affected:** Trading members, institutional investors, and retail investors holding or trading NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED shares will benefit from improved trading conditions and reduced settlement constraints.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members need to ensure proper system configuration and client communication before the effective date.
