## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of MAS Financial Services Limited (ISIN: INE348L07191, Code: 975719) effective November 21, 2025. The suspension is imposed due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific NCD series: MFSL-8.55%-6-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE348L07191
- BSE Code: 975719
- Record date fixed: November 21, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 21, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 21, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension
- Members should update their systems to block trading in ISIN INE348L07191

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Circular issued by BSE
- **November 21, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of this specific debenture series of MAS Financial Services Limited. The suspension is temporary and related to corporate action processing.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine procedure for debt securities reaching maturity. Trading members need to ensure compliance systems prevent trades in the affected security.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debenture holders of this specific series who will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the terms of issuance.
