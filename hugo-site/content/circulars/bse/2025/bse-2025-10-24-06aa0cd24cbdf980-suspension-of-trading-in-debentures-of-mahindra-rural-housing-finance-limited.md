## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE950O07412, Code: 974352) of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited effective October 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from October 30, 2025
- Debenture ISIN: INE950O07412 (Code: 974352)
- Series: MRHFL-GSEC-14-11-25-PVT
- Record date set for October 30, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-746/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from October 30, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE950O07412 (Code: 974352) from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 30, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: October 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The trading suspension is a routine corporate action related to debenture maturity and does not indicate financial distress or regulatory concerns.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the record date. Investors will not be able to trade these securities from October 30, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this ISIN from the specified date to ensure compliance with the suspension directive.
