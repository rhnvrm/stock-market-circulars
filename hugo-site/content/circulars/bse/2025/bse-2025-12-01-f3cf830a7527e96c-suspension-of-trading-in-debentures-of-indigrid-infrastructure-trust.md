## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE219X07132 (IGT-7.4%-26-12-25-INVIT, Code: 960382)
- Record Date: December 11, 2025
- Trading Suspension Start Date: December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard procedural suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE219X07132 from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; Trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The 7.4% debenture maturing on December 26, 2025 will not be tradeable from December 11, 2025. 

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will be eligible for final interest payment and redemption proceeds. Secondary market liquidity ceases 15 days prior to maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems prevent trading in suspended securities to avoid regulatory breaches.
