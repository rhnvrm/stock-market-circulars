## Summary

BSE has issued updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Framework effective December 10, 2025. The circular includes the addition of 2 new securities to the ESM framework, movement of 3 securities to higher ESM stages, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM. No securities are moving out of the ESM framework or being downgraded to lower stages in this update.

## Key Points

- 2 securities newly shortlisted for ESM framework: Kiran Syntex Ltd and Eurotex Industries and Exports Ltd
- 3 securities moving to higher ESM stages: Phaarmasia Ltd, Prima Industries Ltd, and Systematix Securities Ltd
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages or exiting the framework
- Consolidated list includes 28+ securities across various ESM stages
- Changes effective from December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

### Trading Impact
- Securities added to ESM framework will face enhanced surveillance with potential trading restrictions
- Securities moving to higher ESM stages will face stricter surveillance measures
- Investors in affected securities should expect reduced liquidity and additional monitoring

### Market Participants
- Traders and investors holding positions in the 5 affected securities should review their exposure
- Enhanced surveillance may include measures like trade-for-trade settlement, price bands, or additional margin requirements
- Continued presence in ESM framework indicates ongoing concerns about price volatility or other risk factors

### Compliance Requirements
- Market participants must comply with applicable ESM restrictions for listed securities
- Brokers should ensure proper risk management for clients trading in ESM securities
- Enhanced disclosure and monitoring requirements apply to all ESM securities
