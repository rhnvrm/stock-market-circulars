## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with 50,000 units carrying a 12% per annum interest rate, maturing on April 4, 2027.

## Key Points

- 50,000 units of debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 977306, Scrip ID: 12MML27
- ISIN: INE884Q07806
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 12% per annum (quarterly payment)
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Maturity Date: April 4, 2027
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE884Q07806
- Members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: November 21, 2025
- First Interest Payment: February 20, 2026
- Interest Payment Schedule: Quarterly from February 20, 2026 to April 4, 2027
- Redemption Date: April 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it pertains to a private placement of debt securities in a specific microfinance company. The impact is limited to:

- Debt investors and institutional participants interested in microfinance sector debt instruments
- Trading members dealing with debt securities on BSE
- The securities offer a 12% coupon rate with ACUITE A- rating, providing moderate-risk investment opportunity in the microfinance sector
- The relatively small issue size (50,000 units) indicates limited market breadth
