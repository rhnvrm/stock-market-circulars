## Summary

BSE has announced that the KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 18, 2025. The scheme will support various transaction facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan). This follows the earlier notice No. 20251119-30. Six plan variants are available covering both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- Scheme reopening date: December 18, 2025
- All six plan variants will be available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- Subscription and redemption facilities enabled
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available
- Scheme category: Equity
- Platform: BSE StAR MF
- Reference to previous notice: 20251119-30

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems to enable transactions for this scheme
- Investors can access the scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform from December 18, 2025
- All transaction types (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) must be enabled for the listed ISIN codes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Scheme Reopening Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date for Ongoing Transactions:** December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement affecting only mutual fund transactions on the BSE platform.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in momentum-based index investing. Provides access to the Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index through a passive investment vehicle with both regular and direct plan options.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF platform participants need to ensure their systems are updated to facilitate transactions in this scheme from December 18, 2025. The availability of systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) enhances convenience for investors seeking automated investment strategies.
