## Summary

BSE received clarifications from four companies - Venky's (India) Limited, AVT Natural Products Limited, Lodha Developers Limited, and Megastar Foods Limited - in response to surveillance queries regarding unusual volume movements in their shares. All companies confirmed that there is no undisclosed price-sensitive information or pending announcements that could have caused the volume fluctuations. The companies stated that the movements are purely market-driven and all material information has been disclosed in compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Venky's (India) Limited responded to BSE email dated October 31, 2025, confirming no undisclosed information or impending announcements affecting price behavior
- AVT Natural Products Limited clarified on November 3, 2025, that all events bearing on operations/performance have been disclosed under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- Lodha Developers Limited (formerly Macrotech Developers Limited) stated the volume movement is not attributable to any internal event and is market-driven
- Megastar Foods Limited also provided clarification regarding volume movements
- All companies emphasized prompt disclosure practices and compliance with regulatory requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance responses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual volume or price movements
- Companies must confirm whether there is any undisclosed price-sensitive information under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- Companies must clarify if pending announcements could have bearing on share price behavior

## Important Dates

- October 31, 2025: BSE surveillance email to Venky's (India) Limited
- November 1, 2025: Response from Venky's (India) Limited
- November 3, 2025: BSE surveillance email to AVT Natural Products Limited and Lodha Developers Limited
- November 3, 2025: Responses from AVT Natural Products Limited and Lodha Developers Limited

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications that confirm no material undisclosed information exists for the affected companies.

**Operational Impact:** No operational changes required. Companies have confirmed compliance with existing disclosure norms.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency that volume movements are market-driven rather than based on insider information or pending material announcements. Reinforces that companies are current with their disclosure obligations under SEBI regulations.
