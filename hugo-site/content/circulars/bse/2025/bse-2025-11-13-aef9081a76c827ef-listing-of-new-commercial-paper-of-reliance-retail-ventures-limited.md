## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis on its Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The instrument consists of 45,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 45,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,522
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730556
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL121125
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EH3
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading Denomination**: Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EH3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited and offers debt investment opportunities for institutional investors. Trading is restricted to dematerialized form only.
