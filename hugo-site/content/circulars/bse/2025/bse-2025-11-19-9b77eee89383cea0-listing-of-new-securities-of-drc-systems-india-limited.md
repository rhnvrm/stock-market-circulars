## Summary

BSE has listed 1,00,00,000 (1 crore) new equity shares of DRC Systems India Limited (Scrip Code: 543268) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from November 20, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until May 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares**: 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 25.00 per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 24/- premium)
- **Allotment Type**: Preferential basis to Non-Promoters
- **Date of Allotment**: October 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 20, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE03RS01027
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 134081331 to 144081330
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the listing of new securities for DRC Systems India Limited
- **Lock-in Compliance**: All 1 crore shares are locked-in until May 23, 2026 and cannot be transferred during this period
- **Market Operations**: Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine preferential allotment listing for a single company. The shares will increase the total equity base of DRC Systems India Limited by 1 crore shares.

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until May 23, 2026, preventing their sale in the market until that date.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders should note the dilution from the preferential allotment. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning they have equal rights and privileges.
