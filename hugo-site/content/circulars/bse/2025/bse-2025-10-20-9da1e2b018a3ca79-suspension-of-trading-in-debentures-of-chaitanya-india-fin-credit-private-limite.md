## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of CHAITANYA INDIA FIN CREDIT PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE140R08106, Code: 975194) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of October 26, 2025 for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Company: CHAITANYA INDIA FIN CREDIT PRIVATE LIMITED
- Security: 9.75% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing November 10, 2025
- ISIN: INE140R08106
- Scrip Code: 975194
- Record Date: October 26, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard trading suspension procedure for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Debenture holders as of the record date (October 26, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **October 26, 2025**: Record date for determining eligible debenture holders
- **November 10, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. No broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - existing debenture holders need to be aware of the record date to ensure eligibility for final interest payment and redemption proceeds. Trading liquidity is temporarily suspended but this is a standard pre-redemption procedure.

**Operational Impact**: Low - standard corporate action process. Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this security from October 24, 2025.
