## Summary

360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with an allotment date of December 5, 2025. A total of 7,500 securities with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each have been admitted to trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Listed**: 7,500 securities
- **Scrip Code**: 977352
- **Scrip ID**: 0360OAM27
- **Detail Name**: 360OAM-3-12-27-PVT
- **ISIN Number**: INE0TQI07016
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000 per security
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA
- **Rate of Interest**: 0% per annum (zero coupon)
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued through private placement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0TQI07016
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE's debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: December 3, 2027
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Not applicable (zero coupon)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing with no immediate impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to BSE Debt segment trading members dealing with 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited securities. The zero coupon structure indicates these are discount bonds with no periodic interest payments.

**Investor Relevance**: Relevant only to institutional investors and qualified buyers participating in the private placement debt market. The high face value (Rs. 100,000) and ICRA AA rating indicate these are investment-grade institutional securities.
