## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on its debt segment. The securities are market-linked instruments issued on private placement basis with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each and total quantity of 5,000 units.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976954 (NWFL24725)
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: ₹96,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 5,000 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 24, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (Cumulative)
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities are market-linked instruments with a 4-year tenure and carry investment grade rating. Trading is restricted to institutional and qualified investors due to high face value.
