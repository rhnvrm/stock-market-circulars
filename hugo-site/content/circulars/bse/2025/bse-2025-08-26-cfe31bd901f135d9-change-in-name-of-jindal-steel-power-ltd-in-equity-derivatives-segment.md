## Summary

BSE announces a name change for Jindal Steel & Power Ltd in the equity derivatives segment. The underlying scrip name will change from "Jindal Steel & Power Ltd" to "Jindal Steel Limited" effective August 29, 2025, while all other trading parameters remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name change in derivatives segment only
- Symbol remains JINDALSTEL (scrip code 532286)
- No changes to any other trading parameters
- Administrative update with no operational impact

## Regulatory Changes

Name change in equity derivatives segment:
- Old name: Jindal Steel & Power Ltd
- New name: Jindal Steel Limited
- Scrip code and symbol unchanged

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the name change for reference purposes. No action required as trading parameters remain the same.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations, scrip codes, symbols, and all other parameters remain unchanged, ensuring continuity in derivatives trading.
