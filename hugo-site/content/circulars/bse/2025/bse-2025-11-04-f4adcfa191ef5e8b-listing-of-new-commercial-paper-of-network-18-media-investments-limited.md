## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis effective November 4, 2025. The instrument consists of 6000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,92,473.50 per unit with a redemption date of February 3, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730488
- **Scrip ID**: N18MIL41125
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WD4
- **Quantity**: 6000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,473.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WD4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the BSE Debt segment for short-term financing by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited. The instrument provides the company with working capital funding for approximately three months. The A1+ credit rating indicates high credit quality and lowest credit risk for short-term debt obligations. The listing expands debt market offerings but has no impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
