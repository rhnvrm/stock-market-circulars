BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Aureate Tradde Limited filed on December 23, 2025 for SME IPO. The document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations and is available on BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Aureate Tradde Limited on December 23, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments solicited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes. Standard process for SME IPO draft offer document filing as per ICDR Regulations.
