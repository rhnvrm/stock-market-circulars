## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Dugar Housing Developments Limited (CIN: L62013TN1992PLC023689). The company name has been officially changed to Axentra Corp Limited with effect from November 22, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on November 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company original name: Dugar Housing Development Finance India Limited
- Previous name: Dugar Housing Developments Limited
- New name: Axentra Corp Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L62013TN1992PLC023689
- Certificate issued by: Dhirendra Singh, Assistant Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered office: Dugar Towers, 123 Marshalls Road, Egmore, Chennai-600008, Tamil Nadu, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative procedure for recording a company name change as per the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Dugar Housing Developments Limited) for a period of two years along with its new name (Axentra Corp Limited) at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **November 22, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Dugar Housing Developments Limited to Axentra Corp Limited
- **Two-year period**: Duration for which the company must display both old and new names (from November 22, 2025 to November 22, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Company operations or business activities
- Rights and obligations of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders
- Existing contracts, agreements, or legal proceedings
- The company's Corporate Identification Number (CIN)

Investors and market participants should note the new name for identification purposes. The company will continue to operate under the same CIN and legal entity structure.
