## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper securities issued by Godrej Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The listing comprises 4000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,664.50
- **Scrip Code**: 730628
- **Scrip ID**: GFL241125
- **ISIN**: INE02KN14630
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Trading Parameters

- Trading will be conducted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Listed on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with no material impact on broader market operations. The Commercial Paper issuance is part of Godrej Finance Limited's regular short-term funding activities. Trading members can access these instruments through the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with standard trading parameters.
