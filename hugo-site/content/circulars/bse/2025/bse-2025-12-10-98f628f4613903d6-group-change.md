## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Modi Naturals Ltd (Scrip Code 519003) will be reclassified from Group X to Group B effective December 11, 2025. This is a routine group change notification issued on December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Modi Naturals Ltd
- Scrip Code: 519003
- Current Group: X
- New Group: B
- Effective Date: December 11, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251210-3

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a classification adjustment of the company from Group X to Group B within BSE's group structure.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note the group change and update their systems accordingly before the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 10, 2025
- Effective Date: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. Group B typically represents securities with better liquidity and trading characteristics compared to Group X. This reclassification may indicate improved trading parameters for Modi Naturals Ltd. Trading members should update their internal systems to reflect the new group classification.
