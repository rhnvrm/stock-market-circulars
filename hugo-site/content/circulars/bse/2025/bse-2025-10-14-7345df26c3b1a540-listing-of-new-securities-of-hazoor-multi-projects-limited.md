## Summary

BSE has listed 527,500 new equity shares of Hazoor Multi Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 532467) for trading with effect from Wednesday, October 15, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 29/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares**: 527,500 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 30/- (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 29/- premium)
- **Allotment Type**: Preferential basis to non-promoters
- **Purpose**: Conversion of warrants
- **Distinguished Numbers**: 224433911 to 224961410
- **ISIN**: INE550F01049
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Scrip Code**: 532467

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification following SEBI regulations for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in period must be observed for all 527,500 shares until April 20, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: June 24, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 15, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: April 20, 2026
- **Notice Date**: October 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a standard listing of preferential shares for a single company. The number of shares (527,500) represents a modest increase to the existing share capital.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders should note the dilution from the preferential allotment. New shares are subject to lock-in until April 20, 2026, preventing immediate sale by allottees.

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all newly listed shares are locked-in for approximately 18 months from the notice date. Post lock-in expiry, there will be additional floating stock available for trading.
