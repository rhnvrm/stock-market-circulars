## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 2,700 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,700 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,906 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730649
- Scrip ID: BFSL251125
- ISIN: INE01C314DM4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DM4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides an additional short-term debt instrument (91-day tenure) for investors in the BSE Debt segment. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk. No operational changes are required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
