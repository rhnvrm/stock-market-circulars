## Summary

Krishival Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 544416) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 17, 2025. The company will issue 45 equity shares of Rs. 10 each at a premium of Rs. 290 per share (total issue price Rs. 300) for every 301 equity shares held. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 17, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-779/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 45 equity shares for every 301 existing shares held
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Premium: Rs. 290 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 300 per share
- Scrip Code: 544416
- Payment structure: Partly paid with application money of Rs. 105 per share and balance Rs. 195 per share in one or more calls within one year
- Ex-rights trading effective from December 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action announcement in accordance with existing SEBI guidelines for rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in Krishival Foods Limited shares on ex-rights basis from December 17, 2025
- Settlement number DR-779/2025-2026 applies for the ex-rights transactions
- Shareholders on record as of December 17, 2025 will be eligible for the rights entitlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-779/2025-2026
- **Notice Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Call payment deadline:** Balance Rs. 195 per share payable within one year from issuance of partly paid equity shares

## Impact Assessment

**For Shareholders:** Existing shareholders will receive rights to subscribe to new equity shares at a ratio of 45:301. The partly paid structure allows shareholders to pay Rs. 105 on application and the balance Rs. 195 over time within one year, reducing immediate cash outflow.

**For Trading:** Shares will trade ex-rights from December 17, 2025, which may result in price adjustment to reflect the value of the rights being separated from the share price.

**Market Impact:** Medium - This is a standard rights issue that will increase the share capital of Krishival Foods Limited. The impact is limited to existing shareholders and those trading the stock around the record date.
