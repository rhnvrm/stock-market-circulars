## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SKF India (Industrial) Limited (Scrip Code - 544572) will be moved to 'B' group effective Friday, December 19, 2025. This change in group classification will affect the trading and settlement parameters for the stock.

## Key Points

- SKF India (Industrial) Limited scrip code: 544572
- Equity shares will be permitted to trade under 'B' group
- Effective date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- All trading members and market intermediaries have been notified
- Notice issued by Kinnar Mehta (Assistant Vice President) and Toshika Sharma (Deputy Manager)

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will transition from its current group to 'B' group classification. Group 'B' on BSE typically indicates stocks that are traded on a rolling settlement basis with specific eligibility criteria related to trading volumes and compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Market intermediaries should adjust their internal processes for handling trades in this scrip
- All participants must ensure compliance with 'B' group trading norms from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The move to 'B' group may affect:
- Trading settlement cycles for the stock
- Margin requirements for traders
- Liquidity characteristics
- System configurations for trading members and intermediaries

Trading members should prepare for this transition in advance to ensure smooth operations from the effective date.
