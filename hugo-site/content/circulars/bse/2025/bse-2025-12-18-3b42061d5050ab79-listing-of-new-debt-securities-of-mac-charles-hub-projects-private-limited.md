## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Mac Charles Hub Projects Private Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025. The securities are zero-coupon bonds with a 5-year maturity period and carry an IND BB+/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 21,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 215 crores
- Scrip Code: 977385
- Scrip ID: 0MCHP30
- ISIN: INE09T907019
- Credit Rating: IND BB+/Stable
- Issue Type: Zero Coupon (no periodic interest payments)
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE09T907019
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: December 15, 2030
- Payment of Interest Dates: Not applicable (zero-coupon bond)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine private placement debt listing with limited liquidity and institutional investor participation. The securities are not equity-linked and will trade only in the debt segment. Trading members in the debt segment should note the new listing for their records. For clarifications, members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
