## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Gallard Steel Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544616, symbol GALLARD, and ISIN INE0BUQ01012. The specific listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Gallard Steel Limited shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544616
- Trading Symbol: GALLARD
- ISIN Number: INE0BUQ01012
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notification regarding the listing date and complete security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The circular serves as preliminary notification to trading members about the upcoming addition of Gallard Steel Limited to the SME segment. Trading members and investors interested in this SME stock should monitor for the follow-up notice containing the actual listing date and complete trading details.
