## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 221,223 new equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511644) effective from November 26, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 128 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 118) pursuant to conversion of warrants. The allotment date was August 21, 2025, and all shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 221,223 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 118
- Issue price: Rs. 128 per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 2276277 to 2497499
- ISIN: INE113B01029
- Trading permitted from November 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 21, 2025
- **Trading Start Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** June 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment to promoters. All 221,223 shares are locked-in until June 29, 2027, preventing immediate trading by allottees. The shares represent warrant conversions and are pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining uniformity in shareholder rights.
