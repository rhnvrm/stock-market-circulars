## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544599) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on Friday, November 07, 2025, and will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, November 11, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED (scrip code 544599) listed on BSE on November 07, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index on November 11, 2025
- Addition will be effective at the open of trading on November 11, 2025
- This follows Notice No: 20251106-50 issued previously
- Corporate action included in end-of-day SDE file

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking funds and investors may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the index composition change.

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: Listing date of STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED on BSE
- **November 11, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition affects:

- **Index Funds**: Funds tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to include STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED in their portfolios
- **Passive Investors**: May see automatic inclusion in index-tracking investments
- **Market Impact**: Limited, as this is a standard procedure following new listings
- **Liquidity**: May see slight increase in trading volume due to index fund rebalancing
