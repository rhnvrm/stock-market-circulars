## Summary

BSE Surveillance sought clarifications from three companies regarding recent price and volume movements in their securities. Hindware Home Innovation Limited responded to a volume increase query, Jay Ushin Ltd clarified media reports about rare earth magnet imports from China, and Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited addressed price movement concerns. All companies confirmed compliance with disclosure requirements and stated no undisclosed material information.

## Key Points

- **Hindware Home Innovation Limited (Scrip Code: 542905)**: Responded to BSE query about increased trading volume on November 4, 2025
- Company confirmed no material events/information affecting operations or performance
- Stated commitment to transparent and timely disclosure per SEBI regulations
- Attributed volume movement to market forces beyond company control

- **Jay Ushin Ltd (Scrip Code: 513252)**: Clarified media reports about rare earth magnet imports from China
- Company has been importing rare earth magnets for automotive product manufacturing for several years
- Sourcing through authorized Indian trader to ensure supply continuity and mitigate shortages
- Clarification issued under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015

- **Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 531126)**: Responded to price movement query dated November 3, 2025
- Confirmed all material information has been regularly disclosed to stock exchange
- Stated compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Affirmed timely and accurate disclosure practices

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents company responses to routine surveillance queries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond promptly to BSE Surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements
- Disclosures must be made under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed material events exist that could affect security prices
- Transparent and timely dissemination of price-sensitive information is mandatory

## Important Dates

- **November 3, 2025**: BSE surveillance query sent to Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited
- **November 4, 2025**: BSE surveillance query sent to Hindware Home Innovation Limited and Jay Ushin Ltd
- **November 4, 2025**: All three companies submitted their clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine surveillance clarifications with no material undisclosed events reported by any company.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Jay Ushin's clarification confirms ongoing business practices (rare earth magnet imports) rather than new developments. Hindware and Kati Patang confirmed no operational changes.

**Investor Impact**: Low - The clarifications provide transparency but do not reveal new material information that would significantly affect investment decisions. Price and volume movements attributed to normal market forces.

**Sectoral Relevance**: 
- Jay Ushin (automotive components sector) - clarified supply chain practices for critical materials
- Hindware Home Innovation (home improvement sector) - routine volume increase response
- Kati Patang Lifestyle (lifestyle/retail sector) - routine price movement response
