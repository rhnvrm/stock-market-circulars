## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Pajson Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544657, symbol PAJSON, and ISIN number INE14LM01012. This listing will occur on the SME segment of the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Pajson Agro India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Trading Symbol: PAJSON
- ISIN: INE14LM01012
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details to be communicated via separate notice

Trading members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the subsequent notice containing the exact listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.
