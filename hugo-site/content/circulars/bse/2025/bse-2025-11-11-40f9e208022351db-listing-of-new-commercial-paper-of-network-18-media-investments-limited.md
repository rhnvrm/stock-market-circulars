## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 3,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 150 crore) with a 91-day tenor, issued at a discount and rated CARE A1+ and IND A1+. Trading commenced on November 11, 2025, in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Network 18 Media & Investments Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crore (3,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit (issued at discount)
- **Scrip Code**: 730528
- **Scrip ID**: N18M111125
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WF9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WF9
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will trade on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing that provides Network 18 Media & Investments Limited with working capital financing for approximately 3 months. The commercial paper market is primarily accessed by institutional investors and does not affect retail equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade this instrument on the BSE platform. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate high credit quality and low default risk for short-term obligations.
