## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The issue comprises 40,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 20,000 crore. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total Quantity: 40,000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,583 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730629
- Scrip ID: RRVL211125
- ISIN Number: INE929O14EJ9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EJ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 24, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 20, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance is part of normal short-term funding operations for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. The 91-day maturity makes this a standard short-term money market instrument. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
