## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 08, 2025. The fund, which completed its NFO period (as per Notice No. 20251114-10), will now be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as EQUITY funds
- Unique ISINs assigned to each plan variant
- Both Regular and Direct plans offer identical investment options
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251114-10 regarding NFO period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. BSE StAR MF Platform members should update their systems to enable transactions for the listed ISINs from December 08, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025 - MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: December 05, 2025

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan
1. **Growth** - ISIN: INF769K01PS0
2. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF769K01PU6
3. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF769K01PT8

### Direct Plan
4. **Growth** - ISIN: INF769K01PV4
5. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF769K01PX0
6. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF769K01PW2

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard post-NFO reopening announcement for a single mutual fund scheme.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Affects only participants of BSE StAR MF Platform who need to enable the fund for client transactions. No impact on equity or debt markets.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors access to an infrastructure-focused equity fund with systematic investment options, expanding investment choices in the sectoral fund category.
