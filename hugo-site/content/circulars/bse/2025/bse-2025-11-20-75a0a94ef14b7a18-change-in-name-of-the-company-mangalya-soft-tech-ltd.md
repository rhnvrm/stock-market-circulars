## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for company with CIN L66120GJ1992PLC017564. The company name has been officially changed from MANGALYA SOFT-TECH LIMITED to RATNAKAR SECURITIES LIMITED effective October 24, 2025. This certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L66120GJ1992PLC017564
- Old Name: MANGALYA SOFT-TECH LIMITED
- New Name: RATNAKAR SECURITIES LIMITED
- Effective Date: October 24, 2025
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved and digitally signed by: Sunidhi Matroja, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure conducted under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (MANGALYA SOFT-TECH LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 24, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financials, or shareholding structure.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all documentation, letterheads, and signage to reflect the new name while maintaining the old name for two years as required.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. Investors should note the new company name for all future communications and transactions.
