## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific bonds of REC LIMITED (Rural Electrification Corporation Limited) effective November 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for bond redemption. The affected bond is REC LIMITED 7.79% bonds maturing on November 29, 2025 (ISIN: INE020B08ET5, Scrip Code: 975241). Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: REC LIMITED
- Bond Details: RECL-7.79%-29-11-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE020B08ET5
- Scrip Code: 975241
- Record Date: November 14, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Bonds
- No Dealings From: November 14, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-756/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251107-70

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified REC LIMITED bonds from November 14, 2025
- Trading members must ensure their systems and processes reflect the trading suspension
- Members should inform their clients holding or interested in these bonds about the suspension
- All pending orders for the affected bond should be cancelled or withdrawn before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 14, 2025**: Record date for redemption
- **November 14, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 29, 2025**: Bond maturity date

## Impact Assessment

The suspension has a targeted impact on debt market participants:

**Direct Impact:**
- Bondholders of the specified REC LIMITED bond will not be able to trade from November 14, 2025
- Liquidity in this specific bond will cease from the suspension date
- Settlement of any pending trades must be completed before November 14, 2025

**Market Impact:**
- Limited broader market impact as this affects only one specific ISIN
- Standard procedure for bonds approaching redemption
- Bondholders as on record date will receive redemption proceeds

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this ISIN
- Client communication required for existing bondholders
- No impact on other REC LIMITED bonds or securities
