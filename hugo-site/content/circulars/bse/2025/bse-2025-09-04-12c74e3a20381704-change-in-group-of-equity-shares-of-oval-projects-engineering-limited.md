## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of OVAL PROJECTS ENGINEERING LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544498) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment, shifting the company under M Group effective September 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: OVAL PROJECTS ENGINEERING LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544498
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: September 18, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250903-50 dated September 03, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism will change from Trade for Trade basis to normal rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading arrangements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment change
- Updated trading procedures will apply from the effective date
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Important Dates

- **September 18, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **September 04, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity for the stock by moving it from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal Rolling segment, potentially benefiting investors with better trading flexibility.
