## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from 13 November 2025. A total of 500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been issued, with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,68,384 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730557
- **Scrip ID**: BFL121125
- **Detail Name**: BFL-12-11-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE296A14D39
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Allotment Date**: 12 November 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 12 November 2026
- **Tenor**: 1 year
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296A14D39
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading commences on 13 November 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions are conducted in dematerialized form only
- Trading must adhere to standard denomination requirements (Rs. 5 lakhs minimum)
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 12 November 2025
- **Listing Date**: 13 November 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: 12 November 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine Commercial Paper listing by Bajaj Finance Limited, a regular issuer in the debt market. The securities are issued on private placement basis with strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating minimal credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt listing with no special trading conditions. Trading members can trade these securities following normal BSE Debt segment procedures with dematerialized holdings and standard lot sizes.
