## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (Scrip Code: 728373, ISIN: INE523L14856) effective December 04, 2025, due to the instrument's upcoming maturity on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper: NWIL-5-12-25-CP
- Scrip Code: 728373
- ISIN: INE523L14856
- Issuer: Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited
- Trading suspension effective from December 04, 2025
- Reference: DR-770/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251203-5

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper from December 04, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with suspension is mandatory for all exchange participants

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 05, 2025 (implied as maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument ahead of its scheduled maturity. Only holders and potential traders of this specific Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited commercial paper are affected. Standard procedure ensures orderly redemption process without trading activity interfering with settlement.
