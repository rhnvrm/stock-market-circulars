## Summary

BSE has announced that AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 17, 2025. The reopening includes facility for SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan). This follows the previous notice No. 20251027-59 regarding this scheme.

## Key Points

- AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available in both Regular Plan and Direct Plan options
- Each plan offers three variants: IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth
- Total of 6 scheme options available with distinct ISINs
- All schemes categorized under DEBT category
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. BSE members and investors can utilize the BSE StAR MF Platform for transactions in the specified schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Reopening of scheme for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a scheme-specific operational update affecting only investors interested in AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors in this fund, as it restores transaction capabilities including systematic investment options.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can process transactions for all 6 variants of the scheme from November 17, 2025.
