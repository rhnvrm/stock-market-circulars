## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 773 dated December 10, 2025. The auction covers securities shortages across 115 scrips from various companies. Quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 49,000 shares for B.C. Power C and 10,000 shares for Varun Bevera. This is a standard market operation to settle securities shortages arising from trading activities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 773
- Date: December 10, 2025
- Total scrips affected: 115
- Largest quantity: B.C. Power C (49,000 shares)
- Other significant quantities: Varun Bevera (10,000), Worth Invest (8,614), CIAN Agro (3,691), GMR Airports (3,700)
- Wide range of sectors affected including banking, power, auto, pharmaceuticals, technology, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine market operation circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members with securities shortages must participate in the auction
- Securities must be delivered as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants must ensure availability of funds for auction purchases
- Settlement obligations must be met as per exchange guidelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 10, 2025
- Auction execution as per BSE settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 115 scrips but this is a routine settlement process. Localized impact on specific securities with shortages.

**Broker Impact**: Medium - brokers with short positions must participate in auction, potentially at premium prices.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - may cause temporary price volatility in affected scrips during auction period, particularly for scrips with larger quantities like B.C. Power C, Varun Bevera, and Worth Invest.

**Operational Impact**: Routine - standard demat auction settlement process managed through BSE clearing system.
