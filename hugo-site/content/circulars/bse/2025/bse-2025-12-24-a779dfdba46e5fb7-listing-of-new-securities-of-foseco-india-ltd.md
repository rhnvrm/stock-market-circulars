## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 11,50,800 new equity shares of Foseco India Ltd (Scrip Code: 500150) effective Friday, December 26, 2025. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 5,674.12 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 5,664.12). The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in restrictions until June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total new securities: 11,50,800 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 5,674.12 per share (including premium of Rs. 5,664.12)
- Allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 26, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 6386460 to 7537259
- ISIN: INE519A01011
- Issued to: Non-promoters on preferential basis
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period: Until June 30, 2026 for all 11,50,800 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- New securities permitted for trading from December 26, 2025
- All 11,50,800 shares subject to lock-in restrictions until June 30, 2026
- Shares rank equally with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: December 26, 2025 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares for a single company. The lock-in period until June 30, 2026 prevents immediate selling pressure. The preferential issue to non-promoters at a significant premium (Rs. 5,664.12 over face value of Rs. 10) indicates capital raising for corporate purposes. Total issue size approximately Rs. 65.3 crores.
