## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to dealings 3000 Commercial Papers issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis with effect from November 11, 2025. The securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers of face value Rs.5,00,000/- each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 730535, Scrip ID: NST101125
- ISIN: INE0NDH14106
- Issue price: Rs.492,741.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ 
- Date of allotment: November 10, 2025
- Redemption date: February 5, 2026
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NDH14106
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- ICICI Bank Limited is the designated Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The listing provides short-term debt financing option for Nexus Select Trust and offers investment opportunity for debt market participants. The IND A1+ credit rating indicates strong credit quality. The 87-day tenure (November 10, 2025 to February 5, 2026) represents a typical short-term commercial paper maturity.
