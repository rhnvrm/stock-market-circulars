## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531033) will be moved to the 'X' group effective November 19, 2025. This classification change indicates the security will be subject to specific trading restrictions and conditions applicable to X group securities.

## Key Points

- Company: Regal Entertainment & Consultants Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531033
- New Classification: X Group
- Effective Date: November 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251112-10
- Notice Date: November 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved from their current group to the 'X' group. The X group typically includes securities with specific trading restrictions, which may include:
- Trade-to-trade settlement (no intraday trading)
- Additional surveillance measures
- Enhanced disclosure requirements
- Possible restrictions on derivatives trading

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- All trades in this security will be subject to X group rules and regulations from the effective date
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact Mrs. Kinjal More at +91-7304278788

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Effective date for inclusion in X group

## Impact Assessment

The movement to X group will impact trading in the following ways:
- Reduced liquidity due to trade-to-trade settlement requirements
- No intraday trading or squaring off positions
- Increased scrutiny and surveillance on trading activity
- Potential impact on investor sentiment and trading volumes
- Existing shareholders will need to be aware of changed trading conditions
