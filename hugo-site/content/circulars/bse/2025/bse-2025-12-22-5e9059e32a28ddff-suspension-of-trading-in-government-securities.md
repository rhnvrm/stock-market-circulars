## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Government Security SGBDEC2513 (Scrip Code: 800281, ISIN: IN0020170158) effective December 23, 2025, as the security is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Government Security SGBDEC2513 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 800281
- ISIN: IN0020170158
- Trading suspension effective from December 23, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified government security from December 23, 2025
- Trading members should not execute any deals involving SGBDEC2513 after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 2025 (specific date as per security terms)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single government security (Sovereign Gold Bond series) approaching maturity. Standard procedure with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members simply need to avoid trading this specific security. No system changes or complex compliance actions required.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Holders of this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond's terms. No action required from investors beyond the standard redemption process.
