## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Inbrew Beverages Private Limited, continuing from notice 20251014-9 dated October 14, 2025. Effective December 23, 2025, two scrip codes will be available for trading with revised terms including new ISINs, increased coupon rates from 12.50% to 13.25%, and extended maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Inbrew Beverages Private Limited are being updated with revised terms
- Scrip Code 976716: ISIN changes from INE696R07026 to INE696R07042
- Scrip Code 974276: ISIN changes from INE696R07034 to INE696R07059
- Both securities will have coupon rates increased from 12.50% to 13.25%
- Scrip IDs and abbreviated names updated to reflect new terms
- Changes are effective from Tuesday, December 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

**Scrip Code 976716:**
- Old ISIN: INE696R07026 → New ISIN: INE696R07042
- Old Scrip ID: 1250IBPL26 → New Scrip ID: 1325IBPL26
- Old Coupon Rate: 12.50% → New Coupon Rate: 13.25%
- Old Maturity: May 23, 2026 → New Maturity: September 25, 2026
- Old Scrip Name: IBPL-12.50%-23-5-26-PVT → New Scrip Name: IBPL-13.25%-25-9-26-PVT

**Scrip Code 974276:**
- Old ISIN: INE696R07034 → New ISIN: INE696R07059
- Old Scrip ID: 125IBPL25 → New Scrip ID: 1325IBP26A
- Old Coupon Rate: 12.50% → New Coupon Rate: 13.25%
- Old Maturity: September 27, 2025 → New Maturity: September 25, 2026
- Old Scrip Name: IBPL-12.50%-27-9-25-PVT → New Scrip Name: IBPLD-13.25%-25-9-26-PVT

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised terms for the specified scrip codes
- Update systems to reflect new ISINs, coupon rates, and maturity dates
- Ensure proper identification and handling of securities under new terms
- Contact BSE officials for clarification: Ms. Pooja Trivedi (+91 22 2272 8844) or email debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 23, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **New Maturity Date (Both Securities):** September 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects bondholders and traders of Inbrew Beverages Private Limited debt securities. The increase in coupon rate from 12.50% to 13.25% represents improved returns for investors. The extension of maturity dates provides longer tenure for both instruments. The ISIN changes require administrative updates in trading and settlement systems. This is a continuation of previously communicated restructuring, suggesting ongoing refinancing activities by the issuer.
