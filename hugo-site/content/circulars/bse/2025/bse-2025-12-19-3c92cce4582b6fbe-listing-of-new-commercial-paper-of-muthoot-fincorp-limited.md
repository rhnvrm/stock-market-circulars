## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 19, 2025. The instrument comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with CRISIL A1+ rating and maturity period of approximately 12 months.

## Key Points

- Securities listed: Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 2,000 units (Rs. 100 crore aggregate)
- Scrip Code: 730812
- Scrip ID: MFL181225
- ISIN: INE549K14CH1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,59,973 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CH1
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: December 19, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Muthoot Fincorp Limited and offers institutional investors a rated debt instrument with approximately 12-month maturity. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term corporate debt instruments.
