## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of LUMBINI EDUCATION PVT LTD (ISIN: INE0QVA07019, Code: 975730) effective December 16, 2025. The suspension is due to early redemption of the debentures, with the record date fixed for December 16, 2025. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture Series: LEPL-16%-31-3-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE0QVA07019
- BSE Code: 975730
- Record Date: December 16, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 16, 2025
- Purpose: Early Redemption of Debentures
- Circular Reference: DR-778/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 16, 2025
- Trading members should inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and early redemption
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings instruction

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only a specific series of non-convertible debentures of a private limited company with limited trading activity in the debt market.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive early redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. The 4-day notice period allows holders to prepare for the redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN from December 16, 2025. No significant operational burden expected given the narrow scope of the suspension.
