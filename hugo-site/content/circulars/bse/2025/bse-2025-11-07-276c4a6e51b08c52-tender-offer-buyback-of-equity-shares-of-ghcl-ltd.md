## Summary

GHCL LTD (Scrip Code: 500171) has announced a buyback offer for its equity shares and has fixed November 14, 2025 as the record date to determine the entitlement and names of shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback. The shares will trade on an ex-entitlement basis from November 14, 2025 under settlement number DR-756/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: GHCL LTD (Scrip Code: 500171)
- Record Date: November 14, 2025
- Ex-Entitlement Date: November 14, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-756/2025-2026

- Purpose: Determine shareholders eligible to participate in buyback offer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Only shareholders holding equity shares as of the record date (November 14, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade ex-entitlement from November 14, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Ex-Entitlement Basis From**: November 14, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-756/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Shareholders**: Shareholders holding GHCL equity shares on the record date will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback offer. This provides an exit opportunity at potentially favorable terms.

**Trading Impact**: Shares will trade on an ex-entitlement basis from November 14, 2025, meaning shares purchased on or after this date will not be eligible for the buyback offer. This may impact trading volumes and price movement around the ex-date.

**Market Impact**: Limited to GHCL shareholders. The buyback may result in reduced equity capital and potentially higher EPS for remaining shareholders.
