## Summary

BSE has announced revised terms for two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd. The securities will be available for trading under new ISINs with extended maturity dates effective December 01, 2025. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251001-60 dated October 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd will have revised terms
- New ISINs will be assigned to both securities
- Maturity dates extended from October 01, 2025 to December 01, 2025
- Scrip Code 975859: ISIN changing from INE0I0R07045 to INE0I0R07078 (975 quantity)
- Scrip Code 973453: ISIN changing from INE0I0R07052 to INE0I0R07086 (2150 quantity)
- Trading Members can contact debt.listing@bseindia.com for further details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative update implementing revised terms for existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must:
- Note the new ISINs for the affected scrip codes
- Update their systems to reflect the revised maturity dates
- Ensure trading in these securities uses the correct ISINs from December 01, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 01, 2025 (Monday) - Securities available for trading with revised terms
- **Original Maturity Date**: October 01, 2025
- **New Maturity Date**: December 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact affecting only holders and traders of these specific debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd. The two-month maturity extension provides additional time for redemption. Trading Members need to update their systems with new ISINs. No broader market impact expected.
