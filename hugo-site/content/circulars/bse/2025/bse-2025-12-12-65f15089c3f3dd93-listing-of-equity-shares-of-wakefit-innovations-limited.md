## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Wakefit Innovations Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544642 and trading symbol WAKEFIT with ISIN number INE0E7301029. The specific listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Wakefit Innovations Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544642
- Trading Symbol: WAKEFIT
- ISIN: INE0E7301029
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Wakefit Innovations Limited and await further notification regarding the exact listing date and complete security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip code and symbol. The actual market impact will depend on the company's fundamentals, offering size, and pricing, which will be detailed in the subsequent listing notice. Investors and traders should await the final listing date announcement before executing any trades.
