## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Anubhav Plast Limited for its SME IPO, filed on September 9, 2025. The document will remain accessible on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days to allow public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Anubhav Plast Limited on September 9, 2025
- Contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comment period of 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public invited to review and provide comments on the draft offer document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from September 10, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: September 9, 2025
- Notice date: September 10, 2025
- Comment submission deadline: October 1, 2025 (21 days from notice)
- Document availability period: Minimum 21 days on exchange website

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural notification for an SME IPO process. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in Anubhav Plast Limited. No immediate market-wide implications expected from this announcement.
