## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 29th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs, under various market conditions. No new BOLT TWS Release is planned.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for November 29th, 2025
- Applicable to Commodity Derivatives segment only
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Designed for testing trading applications and familiarization
- Testing includes exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- Mock trades will not create any actual obligations or liabilities
- Feedback required by 3:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in:** 09:15 AM to 10:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading:** 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM
- **Trade Modification:** 01:00 PM to 01:10 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems should actively participate
- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on November 29th, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **November 29th, 2025:** Mock trading session
- **3:00 PM (same day):** Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading session with no market impact. Mock trades do not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or create any rights and liabilities. The session is purely for testing and familiarization purposes to ensure robust trading systems.
