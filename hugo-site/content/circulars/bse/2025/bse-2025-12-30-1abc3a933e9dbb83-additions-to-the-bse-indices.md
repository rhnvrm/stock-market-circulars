## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited (ticker: 544666) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 1, 2026, following its listing on December 30, 2025. Dachepalli Publishers Limited (ticker: 544667) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective December 31, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on December 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited (544666) listed on BSE effective December 30, 2025
- Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited to be added to BSE IPO index at open on January 1, 2026
- Dachepalli Publishers Limited (544667) listed on BSE SME platform effective December 30, 2025
- Dachepalli Publishers Limited to be added to BSE SME IPO index at open on December 31, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference notices: 20251229-15 and 20251229-31

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index maintenance action following new company listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required. Market participants tracking these indices should note the additions for portfolio rebalancing and index tracking purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Both companies commence trading on BSE
- **December 31, 2025**: Dachepalli Publishers Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at open)
- **January 1, 2026**: Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited added to BSE IPO index (at open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions for newly listed companies. Impact is limited to:

- Index fund managers and passive investors tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to adjust holdings
- Potential buying pressure from index-tracking funds on the effective dates
- Increased visibility for the newly listed companies within the index ecosystem
- No impact on broader market operations or trading rules
