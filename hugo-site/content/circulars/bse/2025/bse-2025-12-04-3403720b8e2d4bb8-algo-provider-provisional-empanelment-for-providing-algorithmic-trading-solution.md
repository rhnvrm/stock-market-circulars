## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Oraph Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Oraph Private Limited has been empanelled on a provisional basis
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Contact: D-393, Third Floor, Defence Colony, New Delhi – 110024, Phone: +91 9810992306
- Complete list of Algo Providers is available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members may choose to engage with the newly empanelled Algo Provider if they require algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It provides trading members with an additional option for algorithmic trading solutions. The provisional empanelment expands the choice of vendors available to members seeking algo trading infrastructure. The updated list of all Algo Providers is maintained at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
