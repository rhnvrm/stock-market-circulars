## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited (formerly IIFL Wealth Prime Limited) on private placement basis. The securities comprise 12,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 1,200 crores. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.75% per annum and will mature on October 7, 2027. Trading commences on November 26, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: 360 ONE Prime Limited
- **Security Type**: Privately placed debentures
- **Quantity**: 12,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,01,059.38 per debenture (at premium)
- **Total Issue Size**: Approximately Rs. 1,212.71 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 977193
- **Scrip ID**: 875360OP27
- **ISIN**: INE248U07GC5 (further listings under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-
- **Coupon Rate**: 8.75% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually on October 7 (2026 to 2027)
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: October 7, 2027
- **Tenure**: Approximately 2 years
- **Market Lot**: 1 debenture
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07GC5
- The tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Detailed information available in the Placement Memorandum at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: October 7, 2026
- **Second Interest Payment**: October 7, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 7, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis, primarily affecting institutional and high-net-worth investors. The securities are not available to retail investors and will trade on the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Neutral. Trading members can facilitate trading in these debentures through the BSE Debt platform. The securities offer a competitive 8.75% annual return with ICRA AA- rating, indicating high credit quality.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and qualified buyers who participated in the private placement. The premium pricing (Rs. 1,01,059.38 vs Rs. 1,00,000 face value) indicates strong demand. The 2-year tenure provides a medium-term fixed income investment option with relatively low credit risk.
