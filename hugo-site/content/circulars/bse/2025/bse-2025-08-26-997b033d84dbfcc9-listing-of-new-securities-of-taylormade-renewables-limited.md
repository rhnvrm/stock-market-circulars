## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 5,15,000 new equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) issued through conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Thursday, August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5,15,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 170/- premium
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 11609604 to 12124603
- Issue price: Rs. 180/- per share
- ISIN: INE459Z01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,00,000 shares)**: March 1, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (15,000 shares)**: March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of preferentially issued shares. The lock-in periods provide stability by restricting immediate trading of the majority of new shares until 2027.
