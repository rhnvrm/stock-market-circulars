## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the system enhancement/upgradation activity on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The maintenance activity that was originally scheduled for Friday, December 12, 2025, from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM has been deferred. As a result, the BSE StAR MF Platform will continue to be available to members for transaction execution during the previously announced maintenance window.

## Key Points

- System enhancement/upgradation activity scheduled for December 12, 2025 has been deferred
- Previously scheduled maintenance window was 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM on December 12, 2025
- BSE StAR MF Platform will remain available for normal operations
- Members can continue to execute transactions without interruption
- This notice supersedes Exchange Notice No. 20251208-27 dated December 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members should note that the platform will be available for normal trading activities during the time slot previously blocked for maintenance.

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Date of circular announcement
- **December 12, 2025**: Originally scheduled maintenance date (now deferred)
- **Original maintenance window**: 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM (no longer applicable)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Positive - The deferment of the system maintenance means there will be no disruption to mutual fund trading operations on December 12, 2025. Members who had planned around the downtime can now conduct business as usual.

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is purely an operational update with no impact on market participants' ability to execute transactions. The platform's continuous availability ensures uninterrupted service for mutual fund transactions.

**Member Impact**: Low - Members will benefit from uninterrupted access to the BSE StAR MF Platform during normal business hours on December 12, 2025.
