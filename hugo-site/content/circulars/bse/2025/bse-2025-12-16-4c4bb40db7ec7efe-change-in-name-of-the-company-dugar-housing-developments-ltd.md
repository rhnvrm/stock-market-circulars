## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Dugar Housing Developments Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Axentra Corp Limited with effect from November 22, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dugar Housing Development Finance India Limited.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L62013TN1992PLC023689
- Old Name: DUGAR HOUSING DEVELOPMENTS LIMITED
- New Name: AXENTRA CORP LIMITED
- Original Name: DUGAR HOUSING DEVELOPMENT FINANCE INDIA LIMITED
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Approved and digitally signed by Dhirendra Singh, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company registered address: Dugar Towers, 123 Marshalls Road, Egmore, Chennai-600008, Tamil Nadu

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (Dugar Housing Developments Limited) along with the new name (Axentra Corp Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 22, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Dugar Housing Developments Limited to Axentra Corp Limited
- **December 2, 2025**: Certificate digitally signed
- **Two-year period**: Duration for which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The certificate explicitly states that the name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for identification purposes. The company's CIN remains unchanged (L62013TN1992PLC023689), ensuring continuity in corporate identification.
