## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Tata Power Co. Ltd worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (Total value: Rs. 500 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730119
- Scrip ID: TPCL29825
- ISIN: INE245A14KD9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading to be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Trading Start Date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term financing option for Tata Power and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly rated commercial paper.
