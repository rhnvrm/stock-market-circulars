## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Papers issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis. Series 1 comprises 1000 CPs of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 50 crores) maturing on May 11, 2026. Series 2 comprises 200 CPs of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 10 crores) maturing on May 12, 2026. Both series are rated IND A1+ and listed on the BSE Debt segment with HDFC Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Series 1 (ISIN: INE342T14EY3): 1000 CPs, Issue Price Rs. 4,76,949.50, Redemption: May 11, 2026
- Series 2 (ISIN: INE342T14EX5): 200 CPs, Issue Price Rs. 4,77,392.00, Redemption: May 12, 2026
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per CP for both series
- Allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Credit rating: IND A1+ for both series
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing announcement for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members must use the specified scrip codes (730552 for Series 1, 730553 for Series 2)
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: May 11, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: May 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing that affects only debt segment participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments of Navi Finserv Limited. The instruments have a 6-month maturity and provide additional liquidity options in the debt market. The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and safety of principal.
