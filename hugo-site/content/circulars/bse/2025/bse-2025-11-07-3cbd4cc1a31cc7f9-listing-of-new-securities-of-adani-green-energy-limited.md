## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 55,27,802 equity shares of Adani Green Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 541450) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, November 10, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until May 14, 2027.

## Key Points

- 55,27,802 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,470.75/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 1,480.75/- per share
- Shares issued to Promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distribution Numbers: 2266757434 to 2272285235
- ISIN: INE364U01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of Allotment: May 27, 2025
- All shares subject to complete lock-in until May 14, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- All 55,27,802 shares are locked-in and cannot be traded until May 14, 2027
- Shares must be treated as ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: May 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 10, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 14, 2027
- **Circular Issue Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal immediate market impact as all shares are locked-in until May 2027 and cannot be traded. The preferential allotment to Promoters through warrant conversion represents standard corporate action. The shares increase the total equity base of Adani Green Energy Limited but do not affect current trading activity due to the lock-in restriction.
