## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in YES BANK LTD. bonds (9.05% series maturing 2026, ISIN: INE528G08329) effective January 5, 2026. The suspension is due to the record date being set for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: YES BANK LTD. 9.05%-20-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE528G08329
- Scrip Code: 953235
- Record date: January 5, 2026
- No dealings from: January 5, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-791/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified YES BANK bonds from January 5, 2026
- All trading members are required to take note of the suspension
- No new positions should be created in these bonds

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **January 5, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 5, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High for bondholders of this specific series. Trading suspension prevents secondary market transactions, but bondholders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this ISIN. Bondholders holding positions as of the record date will receive redemption payment and accrued interest. This is a terminal event for this bond series as it approaches maturity and redemption.
