## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has formally applied for resignation of its exchange membership in accordance with BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations. Trading members are provided a 15-day window to submit any objections to the Membership department.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has applied to resign from BSE membership
- Application submitted as per BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Trading members can raise objections in writing within 15 days
- Objection deadline: December 15, 2025
- Queries should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a membership status update notice.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members with Objections:**
- Submit objections in writing to BSE Membership department
- Must be submitted within 15 days from December 1, 2025
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

**No Action Required:**
- Trading members without objections need not take any action

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Objection Deadline:** December 15, 2025
- **Objection Period:** 15 days from notice date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is an administrative matter affecting one trading member's resignation from the exchange.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Only trading members with pending transactions or disputes with Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. need to take action. The 15-day objection period provides adequate time for members to review any outstanding matters.

**Member Impact:** Specific to Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and any members with direct business relationships. No broader market implications expected.
