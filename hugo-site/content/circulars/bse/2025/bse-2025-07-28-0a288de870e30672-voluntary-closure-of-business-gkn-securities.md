## Summary

GKN Securities (Member No. 6507) has voluntarily decided to cease its trading operations across Cash, Equity derivative and Currency derivative segments effective July 29, 2025. This is a business closure decision by the trading member.

## Key Points

- GKN Securities will stop trading operations from Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- Closure affects Cash, Equity derivative and Currency derivative segments
- Decision is voluntary, not regulatory enforcement
- Member number: 6507

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a voluntary business decision by the trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for awareness. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of trading operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves only one trading member voluntarily closing operations. Other trading members and market participants can continue normal operations. Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications.
