## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment effective December 30, 2025. The securities include papers from Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited (22,000 units), Aarti Industries Ltd (3,000 units), J.K. Cement Ltd (1,500 units), Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (400 units), and Time Technoplast Limited (200 units). All securities will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- All commercial papers will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Face value for all papers: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Interest rates range from 6.08% p.a. (J.K. Cement) to 9.25% p.a. (Muthoottu Mini Financiers)
- All papers carry A1+ credit ratings from agencies like CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, and IND
- Maturity periods range from 49 days to 182 days
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Adherence to standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Compliance with 1 paise tick size for all listed commercial papers

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 29-30, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Reliance Jio: March 13, 2026 (6.19% p.a.)
  - J.K. Cement: February 17, 2026 (6.08% p.a.)
  - Aarti Industries: March 30, 2026 (6.70% p.a.)
  - Muthoottu Mini Financiers: June 29, 2026 (9.25% p.a.)
  - Time Technoplast: June 30, 2026 (7.60% p.a.)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing of debt securities on the BSE debt segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Relevant only to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals participating in the debt market segment for short-term commercial paper investments.

**Trading Impact**: No impact on equity trading. These instruments trade separately on the debt segment with specific participants and settlement mechanisms.
