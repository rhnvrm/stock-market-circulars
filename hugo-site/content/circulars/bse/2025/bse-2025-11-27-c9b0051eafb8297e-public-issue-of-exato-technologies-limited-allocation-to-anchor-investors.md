## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of EXATO Technologies Limited. Anchor investor allocation is conducted one day prior to the issue opening and helps in price discovery and generating interest among retail and institutional investors.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation completed for EXATO Technologies Limited IPO
- Allocation details include investor names, number of shares allotted, and allocation amount
- Anchor allocation conducted as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Price determined through anchor book building process

## Issue Details

**Company**: EXATO Technologies Limited
**Issue Type**: Public Issue (IPO)
**Segment**: Anchor Investor Allocation
**Process**: Conducted one day prior to issue opening

## Anchor Allocation Information

The circular contains detailed information about:
- Names of anchor investors
- Category of investors (Domestic/Foreign Institutional Investors)
- Number of shares allocated to each investor
- Allocation amount per investor
- Price per share for anchor allocation

- **Issue Opening Date**: Expected to follow anchor allocation
