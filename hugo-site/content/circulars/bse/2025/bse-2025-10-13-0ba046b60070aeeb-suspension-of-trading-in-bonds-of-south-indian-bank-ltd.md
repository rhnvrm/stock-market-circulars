## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of South Indian Bank Ltd. effective October 16, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: SIBL-10.25%-31-10-25-PVT (ISIN: INE683A08028, Code: 952810)
- Record date set for October 16, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from October 16, 2025
- Notice number: DR-737/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension notice for bond redemption and interest payment processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified bonds from October 16, 2025
- Trading members should note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealings directive

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Circular issuance date
- **October 16, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **October 16, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 31, 2025**: Expected maturity date (based on bond nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific bond series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to the bond's maturity process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the affected bond. Investors holding these bonds will receive redemption proceeds and interest as per the terms.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders as of the record date (October 16, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption. No secondary market trading available from suspension date until settlement completion.
