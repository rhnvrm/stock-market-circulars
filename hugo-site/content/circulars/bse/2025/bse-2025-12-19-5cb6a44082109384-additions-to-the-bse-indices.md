## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed stocks to its indices. ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (ticker: 544658) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective December 23, 2025, following its listing on December 19, 2025. Stanbik Agro Limited (ticker: 544659) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective December 22, 2025, following its SME platform listing on December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (544658) listed on BSE mainboard on December 19, 2025
- Addition to BSE IPO index effective at market open on December 23, 2025
- Stanbik Agro Limited (544659) listed on BSE SME platform on December 19, 2025
- Addition to BSE SME IPO index effective at market open on December 22, 2025
- Both actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20251218-56 for ICICI Prudential AMC listing
- References Notice No: 20251218-50 for Stanbik Agro listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action for index composition management.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index addition is an administrative process managed by BSE Index Services Private Limited.

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Listing date for both ICICI Prudential AMC (mainboard) and Stanbik Agro Limited (SME platform)
- **December 22, 2025**: Stanbik Agro Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **December 23, 2025**: ICICI Prudential AMC added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions following new listings. The additions may result in passive index-tracking funds purchasing these securities to maintain index alignment. Index constituents are updated in the end-of-day corporate action file for system updates. No trading restrictions or special conditions apply. Market participants tracking these indices should adjust portfolios accordingly.
