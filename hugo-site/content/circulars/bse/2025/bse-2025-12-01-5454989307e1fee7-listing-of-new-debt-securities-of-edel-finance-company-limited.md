## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Edel Finance Company Limited issued on private placement basis effective December 01, 2025. Two tranches of debentures totaling 600 units with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each have been admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment. Both securities carry CRISIL A+/STABLE rating and have a redemption date of October 30, 2028.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities issued by Edel Finance Company Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Security 1 (EFCL30725): 500 units with ISIN INE836K07304, allotted on November 28, 2025 at Rs. 1,01,192 issue price
- Security 2 (EFCL300725): 100 units with ISIN INE836K07312, allotted on July 30, 2025 at Rs. 1,00,352 issue price
- Both securities have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Credit rating: CRISIL A+/STABLE for both tranches
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Interest payment structure: Cumulative for both securities
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Allotment Date (EFCL30725)**: November 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date (EFCL300725)**: July 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Both Securities)**: October 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing affecting only specific debt market participants interested in Edel Finance Company Limited's securities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members dealing in debt securities need to be aware of the new scrip codes (976967 and 976968) and ISIN numbers for trading purposes.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors specifically interested in CRISIL A+/STABLE rated debt securities with approximately 3-year maturity. The cumulative interest structure and put/call options (as per KID) should be reviewed by potential investors in the placement memorandum.
