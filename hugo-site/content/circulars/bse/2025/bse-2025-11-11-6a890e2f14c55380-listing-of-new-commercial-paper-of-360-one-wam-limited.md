## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment on November 11, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 5 crore (100 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The Commercial Paper carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities issued: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 100 units (Rs. 5 crore total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,423.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730533
- Scrip ID: OWL101125
- ISIN: INE466L14FC0
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE466L14FC0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Deemed date of allotment**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing date**: November 11, 2025
- **Date of redemption**: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper has a short tenure of approximately 3 months and carries strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA). The listing provides 360 ONE WAM LIMITED with access to short-term funding through the commercial paper market. For debt market participants, this offers a highly-rated short-term investment option with a 91-day maturity period.
