## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by PCBL Chemical Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 27, 2025. The instrument consists of 2,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a 90-day maturity period from November 6, 2025 to February 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issue Size**: 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (Total: Rs. 100 crore)
- **Scrip Code**: 730663
- **Scrip ID**: PCL061125
- **ISIN**: INE602A14489
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 492,447.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only in BSE Debt segment
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: YES BANK LIMITED
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE602A14489
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper has a 90-day maturity and is rated ICRA A1+ (highest rating for short-term debt). Trading is restricted to the debt segment with no impact on equity markets. The instrument provides short-term funding for PCBL Chemical Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
