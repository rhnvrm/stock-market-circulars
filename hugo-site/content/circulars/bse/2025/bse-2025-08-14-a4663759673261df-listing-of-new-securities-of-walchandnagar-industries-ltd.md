## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,23,681 new equity shares of Walchandnagar Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 507410) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3,23,681 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 112/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 67459596 to 67783276
- Issue price: Rs. 114/- per share
- ISIN: INE711A01022

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period applicable until February 14, 2026
- All 3,23,681 shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: May 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 18, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a small increase in share capital through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 restricts immediate trading of these shares, limiting any potential dilution effect on existing shareholders.
