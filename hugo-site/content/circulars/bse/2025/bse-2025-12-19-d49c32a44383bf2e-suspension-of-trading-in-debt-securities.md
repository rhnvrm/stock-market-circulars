## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of Solar Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 974468, ISIN: INE343H08016) due to maturity. The 8.20% debentures issued on a private placement basis are set to mature on December 23, 2025, and trading will be suspended from December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Solar Industries India Limited debentures (SIIL-8.20%-23-12-25-PVT) to mature on December 23, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from December 22, 2025
- Scrip Code: 974468
- ISIN: INE343H08016
- Coupon rate: 8.20%
- Circular reference: DR-782/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251219-2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 22, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members are advised to take note of the maturity schedule and suspension timeline
- No trading activity permitted in these securities after the effective suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series of Solar Industries India Limited. This is a routine corporate action related to debt security maturity and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems to prevent trades in this security after December 22, 2025. No impact on the company's equity shares or other debt instruments.
