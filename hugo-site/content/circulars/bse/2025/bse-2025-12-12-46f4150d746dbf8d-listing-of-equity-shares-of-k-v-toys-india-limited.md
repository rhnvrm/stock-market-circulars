## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544641, trading symbol KVTOYS, and ISIN number INE1IE301018. This is a SME segment listing, with the exact listing date and security details to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544641
- Trading Symbol: KVTOYS
- ISIN Number: INE1IE301018
- Segment: SME
- Listing date will be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in a subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing for their awareness and preparation for trading activities once the listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will add one more company to the BSE SME segment. Trading members need to prepare their systems for the new scrip once the listing date is confirmed. Impact is limited to investors interested in SME stocks and the toy manufacturing sector.
