## Summary

BSE has notified that KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 18, 2025. This follows the earlier notice No. 20251119-30. The scheme includes six plan options covering both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants.

## Key Points

- KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions from December 18, 2025
- Available facilities: ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme options available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY category
- Transaction facility on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference to previous notice No. 20251119-30

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of transaction facilities for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice for BSE members and investors interested in transacting in the specified Kotak mutual fund scheme.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 18, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only a specific mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for existing and potential investors in KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND. Investors can now resume or initiate transactions including lump sum investments, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact**: Low. BSE members and distributors can facilitate transactions for this scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform starting December 18, 2025.
