## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 11, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP and SWP facilities across four plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective August 11, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Four scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout and Growth options
- SIP, STP and SWP facilities included
- References previous notice 20250718-19

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine fund reopening announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must process transactions through BSE StAR MF Platform
- Standard mutual fund compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening and transaction availability

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on mutual fund trading segment. Provides investors with access to BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund with flexible investment options including systematic investment plans. Standard operational impact for BSE members handling mutual fund transactions.
