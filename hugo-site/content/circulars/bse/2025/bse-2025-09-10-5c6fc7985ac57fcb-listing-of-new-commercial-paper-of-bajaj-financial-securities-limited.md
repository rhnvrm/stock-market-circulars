## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper issues by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective September 10, 2025. The issues have face values of Rs. 5 lakh each with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings, and ICICI Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- First CP issue: 2000 units, maturity September 29, 2025, issue price Rs. 498,293.50
- Second CP issue: 6000 units, maturity December 9, 2025, issue price Rs. 491,809.50
- Both issues have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading only in dematerialized form under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers and scrip codes

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: September 10, 2025
- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- First CP redemption: September 29, 2025
- Second CP redemption: December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding options for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
