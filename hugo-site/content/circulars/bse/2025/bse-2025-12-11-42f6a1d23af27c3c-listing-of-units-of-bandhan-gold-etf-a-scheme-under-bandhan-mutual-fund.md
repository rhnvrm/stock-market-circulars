## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Bandhan Gold ETF units effective December 12, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended exchange traded fund that replicates and tracks the domestic price of gold. A total of 710,898 units will be listed in the E Group with scrip code 544638, trading in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Mutual Fund**: Bandhan Mutual Fund
- **Investment Manager**: Bandhan AMC Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 544638
- **Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name**: GOLDBND
- **ISIN**: INF194KB1KJ7
- **Group**: E Group
- **Total Units**: 710,898 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 129.05 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Market Maker**: Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade units only in dematerialized form
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Members should note the listing in E Group segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025 (Friday)
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine ETF listing that adds another gold-tracking investment vehicle to the market. The ETF provides investors with an additional option to gain exposure to domestic gold prices through the stock exchange.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new scrip code and ISIN. The ETF will trade alongside other mutual fund units in the E Group.

**Investor Impact**: Provides retail and institutional investors with a dematerialized, exchange-traded option to invest in gold with a relatively low entry barrier (market lot of 1 unit).
