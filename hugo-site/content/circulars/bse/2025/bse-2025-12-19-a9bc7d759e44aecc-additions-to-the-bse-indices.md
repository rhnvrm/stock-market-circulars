## Summary

BSE announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (ticker: 544658) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective December 23, 2025, following its listing on December 19, 2025. Stanbik Agro Limited (ticker: 544659) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective December 22, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (544658) listed on BSE on December 19, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO index at market open on December 23, 2025
- Stanbik Agro Limited (544659) listed on BSE SME platform on December 19, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO index at market open on December 22, 2025
- Corporate action changes included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20251218-56 for ICICI Prudential and Notice No: 20251218-50 for Stanbik Agro

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. These are routine index additions following new listings.

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Listing date for both ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited and Stanbik Agro Limited
- **December 22, 2025**: Stanbik Agro Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **December 23, 2025**: ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. Index additions following new listings are standard procedures. Investors tracking these specific indices will see the new constituents reflected after the respective effective dates. For queries, market participants can contact BSE Index Services at bseindex@bseindia.com.
