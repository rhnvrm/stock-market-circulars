## Summary

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of December 18, 2025. The total share capital comprises 494,258,520 equity shares with Promoter & Promoter Group holding 87.59% and Public holding 12.41%. No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or encumbered shares exist.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares held: 494,258,520 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 432,929,855 shares (87.59%)
- Public shareholding: 61,328,665 shares (12.41%)
- Total number of shareholders: 2,958,295
- Dematerialized shares: 459,242,829 shares (92.92% of total)
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or encumbered
- No convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Listed entity has declared no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or encumbrances
- Shareholding pattern format follows prescribed regulatory template

## Important Dates

- Post-issue date: December 18, 2025
- Filing date: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure requirement with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows concentrated promoter holding at 87.59%, indicating strong promoter control. Public shareholding of 12.41% meets minimum public shareholding requirements. The high dematerialization rate (92.92%) indicates good market infrastructure adoption. No immediate action required by market participants.
