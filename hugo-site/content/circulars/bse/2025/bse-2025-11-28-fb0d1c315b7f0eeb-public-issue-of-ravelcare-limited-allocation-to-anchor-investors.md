## Summary

Ravelcare Limited (formerly Ravelcare Private Limited) has finalized the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors as part of its public issue. The Board of Directors, in consultation with the Book Running Lead Manager, allocated 5,25,000 equity shares to 6 anchor investors at an allocation price of ₹130 per share (face value ₹10, premium ₹120), raising a total of ₹6.82 crores. The company confirmed that no applications were received from domestic mutual funds in the anchor investor category.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 5,25,000 equity shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: ₹130 per equity share (face value ₹10 + premium ₹120)
- Total funds raised from anchor investors: ₹6,82,50,000 (₹6.82 crores)
- Number of anchor investors: 6
- Largest allocation: NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund (1,40,000 shares, 26.66%)
- Equal allocation of 77,000 shares each to 5 other investors (14.67% each)
- No applications received from domestic mutual funds
- Board meeting date: November 28, 2025

## Anchor Investor Details

1. **NAV Capital VCC - Nav Capital Emerging Star Fund**: 1,40,000 shares (26.66%) - ₹1.82 crores
2. **Lighthouse Canton Special Opportunities Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
3. **Viney Growth Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
4. **Finvesta First**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
5. **J4S Venture Fund - I**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
6. **NAVbharat Investment Opportunities Fund**: 77,000 shares (14.67%) - ₹1.00 crores
