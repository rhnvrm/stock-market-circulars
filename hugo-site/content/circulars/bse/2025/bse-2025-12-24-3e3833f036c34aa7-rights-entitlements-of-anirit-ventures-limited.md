## Summary

BSE has announced that trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED will commence on December 26, 2025. The rights entitlements will be tradable in dematerialized form only on a T+1 rolling settlement basis with trade-for-trade settlement. The Rights Issue itself will remain open from December 26, 2025 to January 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading permitted for ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED from December 26, 2025
- Scrip Code: 751070, Scrip ID: ANIRIT-RE, ISIN: INE161F20011
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Trading exclusively in dematerialized form
- T+1 Rolling Settlement basis with trade-for-trade settlement
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced before issue closing date will lapse and be extinguished
- Trading members must inform clients that REs are not ordinary shares and provide proper disclaimers in contract notes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes existing framework for rights entitlements trading as per previous notices (20200305-21 dated March 05, 2020 and 20200518-34 dated May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of RE trading and provide the ISIN number
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must include prominent disclaimer stating that:
  - Purchase of REs only grants right to participate in ongoing Rights Issue or renounce the REs
  - REs are not ordinary shares of the company
  - Unsubscribed/unrenounced REs will lapse after issue closing date
- Trading members must ensure clients understand the nature and implications of RE trading

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 26, 2025**: Commencement of RE trading period and Rights Issue opening date
- **December 31, 2025**: Last date for RE renunciation trading
- **January 05, 2026**: Rights Issue closing date
- **Settlement**: T+1 basis for all RE trades

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED shareholders. The impact is limited to existing shareholders who wish to trade their rights entitlements.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for trading members handling ANIRIT clients - Trading members must ensure proper client communication, implement disclaimer requirements in contract notes, and manage T+1 settlement for RE trades during the specified trading period.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for ANIRIT shareholders - Shareholders have the option to trade their rights entitlements during the 4-day trading window (Dec 26-31, 2025) or subscribe to the rights issue by January 05, 2026. Failure to act will result in lapse of entitlements.
