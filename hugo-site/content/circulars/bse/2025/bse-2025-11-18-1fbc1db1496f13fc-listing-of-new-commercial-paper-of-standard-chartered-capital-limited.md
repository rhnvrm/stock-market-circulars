## Summary

Standard Chartered Capital Limited has listed new commercial papers on BSE Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The issuance comprises 2000 commercial papers issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on September 4, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 2000 commercial papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,73,122.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730590
- **Scrip ID**: SCCL181125
- **ISIN**: INE403G14TU6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE403G14TU6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides short-term funding to Standard Chartered Capital Limited and offers debt investment opportunities to institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
