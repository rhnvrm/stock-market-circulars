## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1,000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crores. The securities are admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment effective December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth Management Limited
- **Securities**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crores (1,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,961.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730697
- **Scrip ID**: NWML31225
- **ISIN**: INE531F14FL4
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE531F14FL4
- Maintain standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Contact BSE Debt Department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Tenure**: 89 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment participants dealing with short-term debt instruments. The securities are investment-grade rated (A1+ by both CARE and CRISIL), indicating low credit risk.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term investment opportunity in high-rated commercial paper maturing in approximately 3 months.
