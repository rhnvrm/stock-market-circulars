## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544636, symbol WOSAL, and ISIN number INE1MF401019. This listing is in the SME segment. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company Name: Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited
- Scrip Code: 544636
- Symbol: WOSAL

- Segment: SME

- Security details will be provided in subsequent notice
