## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 60,752 new equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares will be available for trading from Friday, November 28, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 60,752 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 415/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 417/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 170044486 to 170105237
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Date of allotment: October 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in period applies to all 60,752 shares until June 10, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The number of shares (60,752) represents a relatively small addition to Black Box Limited's equity capital. The shares are subject to lock-in until June 10, 2026, preventing immediate sale pressure. This is a routine preferential allotment following warrant conversion and does not indicate any significant corporate action or market development.
