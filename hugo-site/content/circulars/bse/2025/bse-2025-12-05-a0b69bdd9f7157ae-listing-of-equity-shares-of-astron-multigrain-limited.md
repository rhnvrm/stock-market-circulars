## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Astron Multigrain Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544628, symbol ASTRONMULT, and ISIN INE0RUY01012. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Astron Multigrain Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544628
- Trading Symbol: ASTRONMULT
- ISIN Number: INE0RUY01012
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Astron Multigrain Limited and await the separate notice containing the specific listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing will add one more company to the BSE SME segment. Trading members need to update their systems with the scrip details once the final listing date is announced. Impact is limited to parties interested in this specific SME stock.
