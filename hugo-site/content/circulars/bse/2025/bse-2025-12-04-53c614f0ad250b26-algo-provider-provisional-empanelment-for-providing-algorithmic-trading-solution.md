## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Oraph Private Limited as an approved algorithmic trading solutions provider for trading members. This empanelment is effective immediately and the updated list of approved Algo providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Oraph Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an Algo provider
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Company location: D-393, Third Floor, Defence Colony, New Delhi – 110024
- Contact number: +91 9810992306
- Circular is effective immediately from December 4, 2025
- Updated list of all Algo providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification adding a new vendor to the approved Algo providers list.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for trading members. This is an informational circular notifying members of an additional approved vendor option for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative notification that expands the choice of approved algorithmic trading solution providers available to trading members.

**Operational Impact**: No immediate operational impact on trading members. Members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally approved vendor option. The provisional status indicates the vendor is under evaluation while being allowed to provide services.

**Reference**: Complete list of approved Algo providers maintained at https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
