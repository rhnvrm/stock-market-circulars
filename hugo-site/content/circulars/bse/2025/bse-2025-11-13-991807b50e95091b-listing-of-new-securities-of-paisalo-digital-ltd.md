## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of 74,03,585 equity shares of Paisalo Digital Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532900) effective from Friday, November 14, 2025. These shares were issued at Re. 1/- each with a premium of Rs. 44.74/- pursuant to the conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs). The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 74,03,585 equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 45.74 per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 44.74 premium)
- Issued pursuant to conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs)
- Scrip Code: 532900
- ISIN: INE420C01059
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 902118290 to 909521874

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading from November 14, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025 (Friday)
- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The conversion of FCCBs to equity shares increases the paid-up capital of Paisalo Digital Ltd. by approximately 74 lakh shares. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing shares, ensuring equal rights for all equity shareholders. Trading members can facilitate trading in these securities from the effective date.
