## Summary

HDFC Securities Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis and is available for trading with effect from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 5,000 units (Rs. 25 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730031
- ISIN: INE700G14PH0
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
