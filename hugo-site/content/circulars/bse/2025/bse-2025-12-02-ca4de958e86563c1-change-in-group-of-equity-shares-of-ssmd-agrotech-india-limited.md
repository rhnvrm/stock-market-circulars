## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544621), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment under MT Group, will be transferred to the Rolling segment under M Group effective December 16, 2025. This follows the exchange's previous notice dated December 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544621
- Current Status: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Status: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: December 16, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251201-60 dated December 01, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for intraday trading and T+1 settlement cycle instead of delivery-based trading only.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Adjust trading strategies as the stock will now be available for intraday trading
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further clarifications or details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Reference Notice**: No. 20251201-60 dated December 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED shares. Investors will be able to engage in intraday trading, which was not permitted under the MT Group.

**Market Participants**: Trading members and investors in this SME stock will benefit from enhanced trading options and potentially tighter bid-ask spreads due to increased trading activity.

**Risk Profile**: The move to rolling settlement typically indicates improved confidence in the stock's trading behavior and reduced settlement risk concerns.
