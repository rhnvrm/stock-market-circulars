## Summary

BSE has issued a suspension notice for trading in commercial papers of 8 companies effective from their respective record dates in early January 2026. The suspensions are due to scheduled redemptions of commercial papers, with record dates ranging from January 1 to January 6, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific commercial papers from the no-dealings date onwards.

## Key Points

- 8 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Suspensions effective from January 1-6, 2026 depending on the security
- Trading members must cease dealing in these commercial papers from respective no-dealings dates
- Notice issued under Corporate Actions category for Debt segment
- All affected securities have specific ISIN codes and BSE codes listed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for scheduled commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates
- Members should update their systems to reflect trading suspension for these 8 commercial paper ISINs
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption purposes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to debt market participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in these commercial papers must halt trading activities from the specified dates. Investors holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds as per the redemption schedule.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers should prepare for redemption proceeds. The suspension prevents new market transactions but does not affect the redemption process for existing holders.
