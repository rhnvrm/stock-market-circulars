## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (SGBNOV25) effective November 11, 2025, as the bond is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this government bond from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Applies to Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (SGBNOV25)
- ISIN: IN0020170091
- Scrip Code: 800275
- Reference: DR-753/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for bond maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified government bond from November 11, 2025
- Trading members are requested to take note of the suspension
- Applies to all trading activity in the debt segment for the mentioned security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: On or around November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII. This is a routine administrative action associated with bond maturity and redemption. No broader market implications expected. Trading members dealing in government bonds in the debt segment should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in this security.
