## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis effective December 3, 2025. The issue comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crore) with a tenure of 181 days, maturing on June 1, 2026. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Adani Enterprises Ltd.
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,82,301.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730686
- **Scrip ID**: AEL21225
- **ISIN**: INE423A14YQ5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YQ5
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 1, 2026
- **Tenure**: 181 days (approximately 6 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Commercial Paper is typically used for working capital requirements and does not affect the company's equity structure. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong short-term creditworthiness. Trading will be limited to institutional investors due to the high denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
