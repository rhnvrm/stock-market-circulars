## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Paisalo Digital Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 16, 2025. The listing comprises 3,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 30 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.45% per annum with a maturity date of December 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977371 (Scrip ID: 845PDL2027)
- ISIN Number: INE420C07171
- Quantity: 3,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: IVR AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 8.45% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of Allotment: December 15, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities on BSE Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C07171
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE website for complete details
- All trades must comply with the tick size of 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on December 15 (2026 to 2027)
- **First Interest Payment**: December 15, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it involves debt securities issued on private placement basis. The impact is limited to:

- **Debt Market**: Adds liquidity to BSE Debt segment with IVR AA/Stable rated securities
- **Investor Base**: Provides investment opportunity for institutional and high net-worth investors in Paisalo Digital debt instruments
- **Company**: Enhances Paisalo Digital's fundraising capabilities and market presence in debt markets
- **Trading Members**: Routine operational update requiring no significant system or process changes

The securities are investment-grade rated (IVR AA/Stable) with a relatively short tenure of 2 years, offering moderate yield for debt investors.
