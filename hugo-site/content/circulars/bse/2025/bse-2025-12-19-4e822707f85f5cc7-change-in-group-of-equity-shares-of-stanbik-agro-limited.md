## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of STANBIK AGRO LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544659), an SME IPO, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective January 05, 2026. This change follows the exchange's previous notice dated December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- STANBIK AGRO LIMITED equity shares (Scrip Code: 544659) will move from MT Group to M Group
- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment
- Change is in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251218-50 dated December 18, 2025
- The stock is listed under SME segment
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for STANBIK AGRO LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade settlement (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group). This represents a shift to a more liquid trading framework typically applied after the initial post-listing period for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group) for Scrip Code 544659
- Ensure compliance with Rolling segment trading rules from the effective date
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on 022-2272 8822 for clarifications or further details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Previous Notice**: December 18, 2025 (Notice No. 20251218-50)
- **Effective Date**: January 05, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This segment transfer is positive for market participants as it moves STANBIK AGRO LIMITED from a restrictive Trade for Trade segment to a more liquid Rolling settlement mechanism. This change typically indicates successful completion of the initial stabilization period post-SME IPO and allows for intraday trading and greater liquidity. Trading members need to update their risk management systems and clients should be informed about the enhanced trading flexibility available from January 05, 2026.
