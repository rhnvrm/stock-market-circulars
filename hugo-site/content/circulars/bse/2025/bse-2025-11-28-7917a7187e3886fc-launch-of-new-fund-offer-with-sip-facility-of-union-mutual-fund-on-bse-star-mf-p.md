## Summary

BSE announces the launch of UNION CONSUMPTION FUND NFO on the BSE StAR MF Platform from December 1-15, 2025. The fund offers 6 scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options. Allotment is scheduled for December 22, 2025, with specific payment and settlement timelines for members.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: December 1-15, 2025
- 6 scheme variants available: 3 Regular Plan options and 3 Direct Plan options
- All schemes categorized as EQUITY funds
- Allotment Date: December 22, 2025
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 22, 2025
- Extended trading till midnight on December 15, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last NFO payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds by allotment date
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date

## Scheme Details

**Regular Plan Options:**
1. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF582M01MD2)
2. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF582M01MF7)
3. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF582M01ME0)

**Direct Plan Options:**
4. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF582M01MH3)
5. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF582M01MJ9)
6. UNION CONSUMPTION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF582M01MI1)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Clear funds must be deposited in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 22, 2025 for allotment
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on December 15, 2025
- Switch transactions require funds receipt on or before allotment date for NFO consideration

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date:** December 1, 2025
- **NFO End Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Extended Trading (Lumpsum only):** Till midnight on December 15, 2025
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline:** 3:00 PM on December 15, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Clear Funds Deadline:** 6:00 PM on December 22, 2025

## Payment and Settlement Details

- Transactions without initiated funds by 9:00 AM T+1 will be auto-cancelled
- Pending transactions remain active till 3:00 PM on last payout day or T+5
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period
- Switch transactions accepted subject to funds receipt before allotment date
- Late switch funds (post-allotment) will not be considered for NFO allotment

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to mutual fund platform operations. The impact is primarily operational for BSE StAR MF Platform members and beneficial for investors seeking exposure to consumption-themed equity funds. The structured timeline and clear payment requirements ensure orderly NFO processing.
