## Summary

BSE has announced the cancellation of suspension of trading in securities of two companies - Retro Green Revolution Ltd (Scrip Code: 519191) and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291). These companies were previously suspended for non-compliance with provisions of SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018. The suspension cancellation will be effective from November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies will have their trading suspension lifted effective November 20, 2025
- Retro Green Revolution Ltd (Scrip Code: 519191)
- Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291)
- This notice is in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19 dated October 20, 2025
- Companies have apparently remedied their non-compliance with SEBI Depositories regulations
- All other provisions of the previous notice remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice reverses previous suspensions, indicating the companies have now complied with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note that trading in the securities of the two listed companies will resume
- No specific action required from market participants except to be aware of the resumption
- For clarification, participants can contact bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Effective Date of Suspension Cancellation**: November 20, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19 dated October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited market impact as this affects only two companies with relatively small scrip codes. Trading will resume for shareholders and investors in these securities.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants can resume normal trading operations for these two securities. The cancellation of suspension indicates successful compliance remediation by the companies.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders as liquidity will be restored to these securities. Investors will be able to buy and sell shares of these companies starting November 20, 2025.
