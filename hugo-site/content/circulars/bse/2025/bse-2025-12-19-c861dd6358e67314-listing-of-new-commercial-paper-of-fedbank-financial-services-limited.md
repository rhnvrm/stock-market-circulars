## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of new Commercial Paper issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The issue comprises 2,500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 125 crore.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 125 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,576 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730815
- Scrip ID: FFSL181225
- ISIN: INE007N14EO6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in these securities are conducted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EO6
- Trading must be executed in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 18, 2026
- **Tenure:** 90 days (3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term debt financing.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form.

**Credit Quality:** The Commercial Paper carries high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating strong credit quality and low credit risk.

**Liquidity:** Trading will occur in standard lots of Rs. 5 lakhs, making these instruments accessible primarily to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.
