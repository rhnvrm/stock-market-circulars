## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Shriram Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 2, 2025. The securities consist of 12,500 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, maturing on May 3, 2029. The securities are rated CRISIL AA+ and IND AA+/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 12,500 debt securities
- Scrip Code: 977335, Scrip ID: SFL011225
- ISIN: INE721A07SQ8
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+, IND AA+/Stable
- Date of Allotment: December 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 3, 2029
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE721A07SQ8
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's website for complete details
- For clarifications, members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 2, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **May 3, 2029**: Redemption Date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing adds to the debt securities available on BSE's debt segment and provides investors with access to AA+ rated corporate bonds from Shriram Finance Limited with a maturity period of approximately 3.4 years. The high face value (Rs. 1 lakh) indicates these are institutional-grade securities.
