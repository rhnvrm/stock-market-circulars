## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new official name in the exchange records.

## Key Points

- Official notification of company name change for Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Ltd
- The change is effective as per the date mentioned in the circular
- All stakeholders should use the updated company name in official communications and records
- Scrip code and other trading identifiers remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new official name as per corporate actions approved by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their records with the new company name
- All future communications and documentation should reference the updated company name
- Trading members and brokers should inform clients about the name change
- The company's scrip code and ISIN will remain the same for continuity

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 18, 2025 (as per circular date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - Name changes are administrative in nature and do not affect the company's operations, financials, or trading.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Market participants need to update internal systems and records with the new company name. No impact on trading activities or settlement processes.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible - Shareholders retain their holdings with the same scrip code. This is a formal notification that does not require any action from investors.
