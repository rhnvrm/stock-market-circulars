## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by KEC International Ltd. on private placement basis with effect from December 2, 2025. The instrument consists of 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a maturity period of 90 days.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 per unit listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,397 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730680, Scrip ID: KIL21225
- ISIN: INE389H14LP2
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LP2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding to KEC International Ltd. through debt markets. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
