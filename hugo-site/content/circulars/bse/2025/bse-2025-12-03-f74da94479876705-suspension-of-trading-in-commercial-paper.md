## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a commercial paper issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (Scrip Code: 728373, ISIN: INE523L14856) effective December 04, 2025, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited to mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 728373
- ISIN: INE523L14856
- Trading suspension effective from December 04, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-770/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper (ISIN: INE523L14856) from December 04, 2025 onwards
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 05, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument approaching maturity. The suspension is a standard market practice to prevent trading in securities that are about to be redeemed.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Only affects holders and potential traders of this specific commercial paper. No broader market implications. Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this instrument from December 04, 2025.
