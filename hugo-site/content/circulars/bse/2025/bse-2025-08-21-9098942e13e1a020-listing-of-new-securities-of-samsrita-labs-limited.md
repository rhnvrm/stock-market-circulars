## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 79,41,004 new equity shares of SAMSRITA LABS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 539267) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will commence trading on August 22, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until February 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 79,41,004 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 14808707 to 22749710
- ISIN: INE579N01018
- Scrip Code: 539267

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 79,41,004 shares until February 28, 2026
- Shares subject to standard trading regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date**: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine preferential issue listing for a single company. The lock-in period until February 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, limiting short-term market effects. Standard procedure for companies raising capital through preferential allotment to non-promoter investors.
