## Summary

DSP Finance Private Limited has listed two new Commercial Paper securities on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 17, 2025. Both securities were issued on a private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, ICRA A1+ credit rating, and ICICI Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper securities listed by DSP Finance Private Limited
- Both securities issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ for both instruments
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Quantity: 2,000 units each

## Security Details

**Security 1 (DFPL-13-2-26-CP):**
- Scrip Code: 730574
- Scrip ID: DFPL141125
- ISIN: INE422H14099
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,183.00
- Date of Allotment: November 14, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 13, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

**Security 2 (DFPL-14-5-26-CP):**
- Scrip Code: 730575
- Scrip ID: DFP141125
- ISIN: INE422H14081
- Issue Price: Rs. 482,186.00
- Date of Allotment: November 14, 2025
- Date of Redemption: May 14, 2026
- Tenure: 181 days

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Security 1)**: February 13, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Security 2)**: May 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in short-term debt instruments. No significant market-wide implications. Trading members requiring clarification can contact the BSE debt department.
