## Summary

Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited has listed three new Commercial Paper instruments on the BSE Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 450 crores across all three instruments. All instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, with maturity periods ranging from 14 to 49 days. Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Three Commercial Paper instruments listed with total value of Rs. 450 crores (4000 + 3000 + 2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each)
- First instrument: 4000 units maturing on December 19, 2025 (14 days), Issue Price Rs. 498,794.50, Scrip Code 730711, ISIN INE916D146E5
- Second instrument: 3000 units maturing on December 24, 2025 (19 days), Issue Price Rs. 498,340.00, Scrip Code 730712, ISIN INE916D146G0
- Third instrument: 2000 units maturing on January 23, 2026 (49 days), Issue Price Rs. 495,674.50, Scrip Code 730713, ISIN INE916D146F2
- All instruments carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading exclusively in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot of 1 unit with tick size of 1 paise
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd appointed as Issuing and Paying agent for all three instruments

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025 (all three instruments)
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025 (effective immediately)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - KMP-19-12-25-CP: December 19, 2025
  - KMP-24-12-25-CP: December 24, 2025
  - KMP-23-1-26-CP: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of short-term debt instruments with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers are privately placed securities with short maturity periods (14-49 days) and will only be traded among institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. There is no impact on equity markets or general retail investors. The listing provides Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited with short-term funding options and adds liquidity to the debt market for qualified institutional participants.
