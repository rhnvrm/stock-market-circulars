## Summary

BSE has listed seven series of Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited. The NCDs were allotted on November 10, 2025, with face value of Rs. 1,000 per NCD across all series. The securities include both monthly interest-paying and cumulative options with coupon rates ranging from 10.50% to 12.00% per annum and maturity dates extending from 2026 to 2031.

## Key Points

- Total of 7 NCD series listed with aggregate 935,377 securities
- Face value: Rs. 1,000 per NCD; Issue price: Rs. 1,000 per NCD
- Four monthly interest-paying series (I, II, III, IV) with coupons of 10.50%, 10.90%, 11.30%, and 12.00%
- Three cumulative series (V, VI, VII) with effective yields of 9.50%, 11.00%, and 12.62%
- Date of allotment: November 10, 2025
- Credit rating: IND BBB-/Stable
- Market lot: One NCD
- No put/call options available

This is a routine listing notification with limited market-wide impact. The NCDs provide investment opportunities in debt securities of Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited rated at IND BBB-/Stable. The monthly interest-paying series suit income-seeking investors, while cumulative options appeal to those preferring lump-sum returns at maturity.
