## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Ambium Finserve Limited issued on private placement basis effective September 10, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.16% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on December 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities (Rs. 20 crores)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 11.16% per annum
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-(Stable)
- ISIN: INE0RU307205
- Scrip Code: 977092
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard debt security listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members must refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms
- Contact debt department for clarifications: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from October 12, 2025 to December 12, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: December 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a medium-term debt investment option with monthly interest payments. The BBB- rating indicates moderate credit risk.
