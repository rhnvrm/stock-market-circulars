## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of 360 ONE WAM LIMITED worth Rs. 75 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total quantity: 1,500 securities (Rs. 75 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 491,604.50 per security
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Listing date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for institutional investors. The short-term nature (3-month tenure) and private placement basis limits broader market participation.
