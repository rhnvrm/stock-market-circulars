## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 17, 2025. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment. Total issuance is 1000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crore), with a credit rating of CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Capri Global Capital Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Scrip Code**: 730794
- **Scrip ID**: CGCL171225
- **ISIN**: INE180C14631
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,86,081 per unit
- **Quantity**: 1000 units (Total: Rs. 50 crore)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: April 30, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 4.5 months
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Bank of Maharashtra

## Trading Specifications

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14631
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading available on BSE Debt segment from December 17, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades are executed only in dematerialized form
- Adherence to standard denomination requirements (Rs. 5 lakhs minimum)
- Compliance with debt segment trading regulations

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term debt market participants an additional CRISIL A1+ rated investment option. The listing expands the debt securities available on BSE's platform but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Impact is limited to debt segment traders and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper.
