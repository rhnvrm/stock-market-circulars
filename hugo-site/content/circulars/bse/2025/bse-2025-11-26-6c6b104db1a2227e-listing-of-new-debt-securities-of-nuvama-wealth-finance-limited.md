## Summary

BSE has listed 750 new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The securities (Scrip Code: 977266, Scrip ID: NWFL301025) are gold futures-linked debentures with a maturity date of April 30, 2029, carrying a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- Quantity listed: 750 securities
- Scrip Code: 977266
- Scrip ID: NWFL301025
- ISIN Number: INE918K07QE9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00 per security
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000.00 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,346.00 (issued at discount)
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE918K07QE9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members can contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: April 30, 2029
- **Put/Call Options**: As per Key Information Document (KID)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors access to CARE AA-rated debt securities from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited with approximately 3.4 years to maturity. The securities were issued at a discount (Rs. 97,346 vs. face value of Rs. 100,000), providing yield enhancement. The gold futures linkage may offer specific hedging or investment opportunities for debt market participants. Impact is limited to institutional and high net-worth investors participating in the private placement debt market.
