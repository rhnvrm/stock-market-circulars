## Summary

K.V. Toys India Limited has finalized the allocation of 4,68,000 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs. 239 per equity share (including share premium of Rs. 229) on December 05, 2025. The Board of Directors approved this allocation in consultation with the Book Running Lead Manager. A total of 11 anchor investors participated in this allocation, with no mutual fund schemes participating.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated: 4,68,000 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs. 239 per equity share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 229)
- Number of anchor investors: 11
- Each of 10 investors received 42,000 shares (8.97% each)
- Alpha Investment Fund received 48,000 shares (10.26%)
- No mutual fund schemes participated in the anchor investor portion
- CIN: U32409MH2023PLC400074
