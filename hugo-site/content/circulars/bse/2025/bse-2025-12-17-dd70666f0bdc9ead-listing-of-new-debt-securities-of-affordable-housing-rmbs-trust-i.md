## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities issued by Affordable Housing RMBS Trust-I on private placement basis to trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 17, 2025. The securities carry a CRISIL AAA(SO) credit rating and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977378
- Scrip ID: AHRT151225
- Detail Name: AHRT-3M-MIBOR-20-12-33-PTC
- ISIN Number: INE2O9N15014
- Quantity: 2,677,086 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,000.00
- Date of Allotment: December 15, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA(SO)
- Rate of Interest: As per Disclosure Document
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE2O9N15014
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 15, 2025
- Listing Date: December 17, 2025
- Date of Redemption: December 20, 2033
- Payment of Interest: As per Disclosure Document

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands the debt segment offerings on BSE and provides institutional investors with access to RMBS (Residential Mortgage-Backed Securities) backed by affordable housing loans. The AAA(SO) rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in structured debt products.
