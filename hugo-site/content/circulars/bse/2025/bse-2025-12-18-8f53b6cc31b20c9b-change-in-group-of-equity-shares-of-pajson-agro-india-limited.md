## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544657), currently listed under the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Friday, January 02, 2026. This change follows the Exchange's previous notice dated December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, January 02, 2026
- Reference Notice: 20251217-55 dated December 17, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading segment classification for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED is being upgraded from the more restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment. This change represents a transition from MT Group to M Group classification, typically indicating improved trading liquidity and reduced settlement restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the new segment classification for Scrip Code 544657
- Trading Members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further operational details regarding the transition
- All market participants must be prepared for the segment change by the effective date

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20251217-55)
- **December 18, 2025**: Current notice issued (Notice No. 20251218-2)
- **January 02, 2026**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

This segment change is positive for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED shareholders and traders. The transition from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment will:

- **Improve Liquidity**: Rolling segment allows for intraday trading and T+2 settlement versus same-day settlement requirements in Trade for Trade
- **Reduce Trading Restrictions**: Removes the restrictive delivery-based trading requirement
- **Enhance Price Discovery**: Better liquidity typically leads to more efficient price discovery
- **Increase Trading Flexibility**: Market participants will have more flexibility in their trading strategies
- **Lower Transaction Costs**: Rolling segment typically has lower impact costs due to better liquidity

Trading Members should prepare their systems and inform clients about the enhanced trading conditions that will be available from January 02, 2026.
