## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) on private placement basis. The listing is effective from December 11, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment. A total of 10,000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (totaling Rs. 500 crores) have been listed with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Quantity**: 10,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730747
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF101225
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FQ2
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FQ2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing of commercial paper provides short-term funding to Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited and offers investment opportunities for debt market investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and ABHFL stakeholders. No broader market implications expected.
