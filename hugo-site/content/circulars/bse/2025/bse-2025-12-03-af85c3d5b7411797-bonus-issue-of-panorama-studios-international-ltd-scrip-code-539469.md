## Summary

Panorama Studios International Limited (Scrip Code: 539469) has provided an undertaking to BSE regarding the credit and trading schedule for its bonus shares. The company will credit 18,60,96,875 bonus shares (ISIN: INE258R01028) to the depository system by 12:00 PM on December 8, 2025 (T+1 day after record date), with trading commencing at the beginning of day on December 9, 2025 (T+2 day).

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be issued: 18,60,96,875 shares
- ISIN: INE258R01028
- Distinctive number range: 7,44,38,751 to 26,05,35,625
- Deemed date of allotment: December 8, 2025
- Credit to depository system: By 12:00 PM on December 8, 2025 (T+1)
- Trading availability: Beginning of day on December 9, 2025 (T+2)
- Company Secretary: Yatin Vilas Chaphekar (Mem No. 72316)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action undertaking as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must complete credit of bonus shares in depository system by 12:00 PM on December 8, 2025
- Company must ensure bonus shares are available for trading at BOD on December 9, 2025
- All necessary actions must be taken for smooth and timely credit to eligible shareholders' accounts

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: Deemed date of allotment and credit completion deadline (by 12:00 PM)
- **December 9, 2025**: Effective listing date and trading commencement (Beginning of Day)

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue will significantly increase the outstanding shares of Panorama Studios International Limited by 18.60 crore shares. The action affects all eligible shareholders as of the record date. The structured timeline ensures orderly credit and trading, minimizing market disruption. Shareholders will see their holdings increase proportionately, with immediate trading liquidity available from December 9, 2025.
