## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis effective November 28, 2025. The issuance comprises 1000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a one-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Scrip Code: 730662, Scrip ID: MFL261125
- ISIN Number: INE522D14OY3 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A+
- Issue Price: Rs. 464,515.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: AXIS BANK
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OY3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** November 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of commercial paper on private placement basis. The instrument carries strong credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A+) indicating low credit risk. Trading members dealing in debt securities can trade these instruments on BSE Debt segment. The one-year maturity provides short-term investment option for institutional investors.
