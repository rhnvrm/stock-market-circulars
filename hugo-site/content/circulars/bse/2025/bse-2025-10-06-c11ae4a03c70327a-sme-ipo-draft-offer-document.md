BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED, filed on September 30, 2025, for public review. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days to solicit public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date (October 06, 2025)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.
