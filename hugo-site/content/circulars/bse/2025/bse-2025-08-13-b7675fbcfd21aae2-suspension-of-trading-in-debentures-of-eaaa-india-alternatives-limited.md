## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of EAAA INDIA ALTERNATIVES LIMITED (Code: EAAAL-GSEC-21-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE528L07040, Security Code: 974377) effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Non-Convertible Debentures of EAAA INDIA ALTERNATIVES LIMITED
- Record date fixed: August 17, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Trading Members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Notice reference: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date procedures for interest payment and redemption

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **August 17, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to holders and traders of EAAA INDIA ALTERNATIVES LIMITED debentures. The suspension ensures proper settlement of corporate actions but temporarily restricts trading liquidity for this specific debt instrument. Impact is contained to specific security and does not affect broader market operations.
