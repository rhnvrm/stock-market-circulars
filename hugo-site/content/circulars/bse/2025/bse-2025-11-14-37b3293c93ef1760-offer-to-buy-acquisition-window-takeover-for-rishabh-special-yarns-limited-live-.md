## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for shares of RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED. The offer is for up to 9,25,782 equity shares at Rs. 13.00 per share, running from Friday, November 14, 2025 to Thursday, November 27, 2025. The scrip is only listed at the Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through iBBS and RTRMS platforms during the specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED
- **Symbol**: RISHYRN
- **ISIN**: INE351D01013
- **Scrip Code**: 514177
- **Offer Size**: Up to 9,25,782 Equity Shares
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 13.00 per share
- **Opening Date**: Friday, November 14, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Stock is only listed at Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Members and Custodians can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- Members should refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12 for details on accessing Leased Line URLs

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: November 14-27, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: November 14-26, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: November 27, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: November 27, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine acquisition window for a single stock with limited offer size. The stock is only listed on Metropolitan Stock Exchange (not on BSE), indicating limited trading activity and market participation.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members. Trading Members and Custodian Members need to ensure their systems and investor UCC details are properly configured for OTB participation through iBBS and RTRMS platforms. Access URLs provided for both internet and leased line connections.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: FII (Foreign Institutional Investors), FI (Financial Institutions), MF (Mutual Funds), IC (Insurance Companies), OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other), CO (Company), IND (Individual)

**Technical Support**: Multiple contact points provided for iBBS login issues, password resets, RTRMS access, and general OTB module queries.
