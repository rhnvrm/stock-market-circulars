## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation confirming the name change of SMS Lifesciences India Limited (CIN: L74930TG2006PLC050223) to Haleos Labs Limited, effective August 20, 2025. The company is a company limited by shares and was originally incorporated as Potluri Real Estate Private Limited. The certificate was digitally signed by Brijesh Kain, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies at the Central Processing Centre.

## Key Points

- Former name: SMS Lifesciences India Limited
- New name: Haleos Labs Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L74930TG2006PLC050223
- Effective date of name change: August 20, 2025
- Original incorporation name: Potluri Real Estate Private Limited
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered address: Plot No. 19-III, Road No. 71, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad-500096, Telangana

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative procedure for corporate name change pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SMS Lifesciences India Limited) along with the new name (Haleos Labs Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **August 20, 2025**: Effective date of name change from SMS Lifesciences India Limited to Haleos Labs Limited
- **Two-year period**: Company must display both old and new names during this period

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all corporate stationery, signage, and documentation to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal obligations continue under the new name.

**Trading Impact**: Investors and traders should note the new company name for identification purposes. The stock symbol may be updated by the exchange in due course.
