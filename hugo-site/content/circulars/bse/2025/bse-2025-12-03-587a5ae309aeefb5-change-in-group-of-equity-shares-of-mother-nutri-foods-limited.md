## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544623), an SME IPO company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment effective December 17, 2025. Following this transfer, the company's shares will be shifted to M Group for regular trading operations.

## Key Points

- Company: MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: December 17, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251203-1
- Reference: Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251202-47 dated December 02, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading methodology for MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade basis (which requires delivery-based trading only) to Rolling settlement (which allows intraday trading and regular market operations). This represents a progression in the company's trading status on the BSE platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must:
- Note the segment change effective December 17, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Prepare for rolling settlement trading operations for this scrip
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Reference Notice Date**: December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This segment change will enhance liquidity and trading flexibility for MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED shares. The move from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment indicates the company's graduation to more liquid trading conditions, allowing intraday trading and potentially attracting more market participants. Trading Members should prepare their systems and inform clients about the enhanced trading capabilities available from December 17, 2025.
