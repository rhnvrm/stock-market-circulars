## Summary

The Board of Directors of Helloji Holidays Limited approved the allocation of 2,64,000 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs. 118 per equity share (including share premium of Rs. 108) on December 01, 2025. The total allocation amounted to Rs. 3,11,52,000 and was distributed equally between two anchor investors.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 2,64,000 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs. 118 per equity share
- Share premium: Rs. 108 per equity share
- Total allocation amount: Rs. 3,11,52,000
- Two anchor investors participated with equal 50% allocation each
- Rajasthan Global Securities Private Limited: 1,32,000 shares (Rs. 1,55,76,000)
- Longthrive Capital VCC-Trendview Capital Fund: 1,32,000 shares (Rs. 1,55,76,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

- Information to be made public on BSE website

## Important Dates

- Board Meeting Date: December 01, 2025
- Circular Issue Date: December 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard anchor investor allocation for an SME IPO. The allocation represents the anchor investor portion of the public issue, which typically occurs one day prior to the opening of the public subscription period. The equal distribution between two institutional investors demonstrates balanced anchor support. The share premium of Rs. 108 on a face value of Rs. 10 indicates the issue price reflects market expectations for the company's valuation. This allocation provides stability and credibility to the upcoming public offering.
