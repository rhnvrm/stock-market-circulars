## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of PURPLE WAVE INFOCOM LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544627) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, with the group classification changing to M Group. This change will be effective from Friday, December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PURPLE WAVE INFOCOM LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544627
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025
- Notice follows previous Exchange Notice No. 20251204-47 dated December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to Rolling settlement, which allows for intraday trading and T+1 settlement cycle instead of the stricter Trade for Trade requirement where each trade must be settled individually.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the change in trading segment and group classification
- All dealings in the company's equity shares will be conducted under M Group from the effective date
- Trading Members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 05, 2025
- Previous Notice Date: December 04, 2025 (Notice No. 20251204-47)
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:**
- Enhanced liquidity as the stock moves to Rolling segment allowing intraday trading
- Greater trading flexibility for market participants
- Reduced settlement restrictions compared to Trade for Trade segment

**Trading Impact:**
- Trading Members need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the segment change
- Investors will benefit from improved trading mechanisms and potential price discovery
