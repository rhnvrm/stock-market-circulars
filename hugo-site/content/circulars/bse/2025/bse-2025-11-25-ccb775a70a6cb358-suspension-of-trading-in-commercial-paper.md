## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers of 13 companies due to upcoming redemptions. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified dates. The suspensions range from November 27, 2025 to December 4, 2025, corresponding to the respective record dates set for commercial paper redemption.

## Key Points

- 13 commercial paper instruments suspended for trading
- Suspensions effective from November 27, 2025 to December 4, 2025
- All suspensions are due to commercial paper redemptions
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these securities from suspension dates
- Record dates set one day before trading suspension in most cases

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the "No Dealings From" dates
- Adherence to record dates for redemption purposes
- Compliance with notice numbers DR-765/2025-2026 through DR-770/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants dealing in commercial papers of these issuers

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in these instruments from specified dates. The suspensions are temporary and routine, tied to scheduled redemptions.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from suspension dates until redemption completion.

**Sector Coverage:** Diverse - includes real estate (REIT), industrial, financial services, media, and retail sectors.
