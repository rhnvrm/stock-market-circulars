## Summary

YUG DECOR LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540550) has announced a rights issue with a record date of December 23, 2025. Eligible shareholders will be offered 1 new equity share of Rs. 10 face value for every 2 equity shares held, at an issue price of Rs. 10 per share (at par). The shares will trade on ex-rights basis from December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: YUG DECOR LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540550)
- Rights ratio: 1:2 (1 new share for every 2 existing shares)
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 10 per share (at par)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Existing shareholders as of the record date will be eligible to subscribe to new shares at par value, providing an opportunity to increase their stake at Rs. 10 per share.

**Market Impact:** The stock will trade ex-rights from December 23, 2025, which typically results in price adjustment to reflect the rights entitlement separation. The 1:2 ratio indicates a moderate dilution of 50% if fully subscribed.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must ensure their systems reflect the ex-rights status from the specified date and process transactions accordingly in the rolling settlement segment.
