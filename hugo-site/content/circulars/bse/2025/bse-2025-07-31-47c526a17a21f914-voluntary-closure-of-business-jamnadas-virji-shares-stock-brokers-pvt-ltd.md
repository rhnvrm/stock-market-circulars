## Summary

BSE announces that Jamnadas Virji Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 339) will voluntarily cease business operations in equity derivative segments effective July 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member Jamnadas Virji Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 339) ceasing operations
- Voluntary closure affects equity derivative segments only
- Effective date: July 31, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a member-specific voluntary closure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for awareness and operational adjustments as needed.

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2025**: Effective date of voluntary closure

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single trading member's voluntary decision to cease operations in equity derivative segments. Other market participants remain unaffected.
