## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Infinity Fincorp Solutions Pvt Ltd. The company has received call money from debenture holders, resulting in changes to the ISIN and paid-up value of listed debt securities. The revised terms will be effective from Friday, October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has received call money from debenture holders on specified debt securities
- ISIN will change from INE00VD07024 to INE00VD07032
- Paid-up value will increase from Rs. 10,000 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code remains 976594
- Changes effective from October 24, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250806-43 dated August 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The regulatory update involves modification of debt security parameters:

**Existing Terms:**
- ISIN: INE00VD07024
- Paid-up value: Rs. 10,000 per Debenture

**Revised Terms (effective October 24, 2025):**
- ISIN: INE00VD07032
- Paid-up value: Rs. 1,00,000 per Debenture

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are requested to note the changes in ISIN and paid-up value
- Systems and records must be updated to reflect the new ISIN INE00VD07032
- Trading Members should ensure proper tracking of the revised paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 24, 2025 (Friday) - New ISIN and paid-up value become applicable
- **Reference:** Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250806-43 dated August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This update has moderate impact on:

- **Debenture Holders:** The call money payment increases the paid-up value tenfold from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Trading Members:** Must update systems to reflect new ISIN and paid-up value for accurate trading and settlement
- **Market Operations:** Technical change requiring system updates but not affecting broader market operations
- **Liquidity:** The change in ISIN may temporarily affect tracking and trading until systems are updated
