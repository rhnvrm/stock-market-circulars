## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis, effective December 16, 2025. The instruments comprise 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each maturing in March 2026 (total Rs. 100 crores) and 5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each maturing in December 2026 (total Rs. 250 crores). Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper series listed on BSE Debt segment
- First series: 2,000 units (Rs. 100 crores) maturing March 16, 2026 - ISIN: INE879F14MD0, Scrip Code: 730776
- Second series: 5,000 units (Rs. 250 crores) maturing December 14, 2026 - ISIN: INE879F14ME8, Scrip Code: 730777
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Issue prices: Rs. 4,91,652.50 (first series) and Rs. 4,65,421.00 (second series)
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ for both series
- Allotment date: December 15, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the tick size of 1 paise for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date (First Series)**: March 16, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Second Series)**: December 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it involves private placement of commercial paper in the debt segment. The instruments are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment. The listing provides short-term to medium-term debt instruments for institutional investors seeking highly rated commercial paper. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
