## Summary

DSP Finance Private Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective December 1, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 1000 papers, each with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000. The papers carry an ICRA A1+ credit rating and will be redeemed on February 27, 2026. Trading will occur exclusively in dematerialized form through ICICI Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,423.50 per paper
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730677
- Scrip ID: DSPF281125
- ISIN Number: INE422H14131
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 27, 2026
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE422H14131
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment using the designated scrip code 730677
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it represents a routine addition of commercial paper to the BSE Debt segment. The impact is limited to:

- **Market Participants**: Trading members dealing in debt securities now have access to an additional short-term debt instrument with high credit quality (ICRA A1+ rating)
- **Liquidity**: Addition of Rs. 50 crore worth of commercial paper to the debt market
- **Issuer**: Provides DSP Finance Private Limited with short-term funding at competitive rates
- **Investors**: Offers institutional investors a 3-month investment opportunity with high credit rating

The private placement nature and institutional denomination (Rs. 5 lakh minimum) limit participation to qualified institutional buyers and high-net-worth investors.
