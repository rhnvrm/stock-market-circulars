## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. The instrument comprises 10,000 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with maturity on February 9, 2026. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form only.

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Issuer: Bajaj Housing Finance Limited

- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,546 per unit

- Scrip ID: BHFL11825
- Detail Name: BHFL-09-02-26-CP

- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Allotment Date: November 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 9, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Central Bank of India
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE377Y14BP3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Bajaj Housing Finance Limited for approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in short-term debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
