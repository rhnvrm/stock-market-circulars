## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 1060 new Commercial Papers issued by Krazybee Services Ltd on private placement basis, effective December 12, 2025. The instruments are denominated at Rs.5,00,000/- each with a 6-month tenure, carrying CARE A1 credit rating, and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 1060 Commercial Papers of face value Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Scrip Code: 730763, Scrip ID: KSL111225
- ISIN Number: INE07HK14417
- Issue Price: Rs.474,899.50/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1
- Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 11, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Yes Bank Limited
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE07HK14417
- Transactions must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment affecting only institutional investors and trading members dealing in short-term debt instruments of Krazybee Services Ltd. The 6-month tenor commercial paper provides short-term funding options for the issuer and investment opportunities for debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
