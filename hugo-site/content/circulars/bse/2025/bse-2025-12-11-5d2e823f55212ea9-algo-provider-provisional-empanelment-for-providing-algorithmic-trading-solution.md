## Summary

BSE has granted provisional empanelment to Quantiply Technologies Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider. This enables the vendor to offer algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members. The empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Quantiply Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The vendor is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to trading members
- Located at Plot no: 344 Dharamshi Bhuvan, 4th floor, Chandavarkar crossroad, Matunga E, Mumbai – 400019
- Contact number: +91 99202 66899
- The complete list of algo providers is available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational in nature, notifying members of an additional approved vendor for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is an administrative notification. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally approved vendor option. The empanelment expands the choice of technology providers available to members but does not mandate any action or change in existing operations.
