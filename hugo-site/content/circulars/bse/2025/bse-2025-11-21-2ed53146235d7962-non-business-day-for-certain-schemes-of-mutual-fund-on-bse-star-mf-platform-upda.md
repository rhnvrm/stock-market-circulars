## Summary

BSE has notified that ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will observe a non-business day on November 24, 2025 on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Trading and transactions for this specific mutual fund scheme will not be available on this date.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will be closed for business on November 24, 2025
- This applies only to transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- This is an update to the non-business day schedule for November 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and distributors should note that transactions for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will not be processed on November 24, 2025
- Alternative trading days should be planned for transactions in this scheme

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This affects only one specific mutual fund scheme for a single day. Investors planning transactions in ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund should schedule them for other business days. No impact on other mutual fund schemes or market operations.
