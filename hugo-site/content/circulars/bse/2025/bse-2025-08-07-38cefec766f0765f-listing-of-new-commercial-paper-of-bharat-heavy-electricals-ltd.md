## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) on private placement basis. The listing includes 20,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 1,000 crores, with trading effective August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 20,000 units (total value: Rs. 1,000 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729975
- ISIN: INE257A14AB9
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,631.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities in dematerialized form only
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 7, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for investors with approximately 53-day maturity period.
