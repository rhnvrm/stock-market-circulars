## Summary

BSE has announced that LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 25, 2025. The reopening includes facility for SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan). This follows the earlier suspension referenced in Notice No 20251030-26.

## Key Points

- LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- All transaction types available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options
- Regular Plan variants: Growth (INF767K01SA6), IDCW Payout (INF767K01SC2), IDCW Reinvestment (INF767K01SB4)
- Direct Plan variants: Growth (INF767K01SD0), IDCW Payout (INF767K01SF5), IDCW Reinvestment (INF767K01SE8)
- Scheme category: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update reversing a previous suspension of transactions in this specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND schemes
- Members must ensure all six ISIN variants are available for trading on the platform
- Transaction facilities (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) must be enabled for the specified schemes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 25, 2025 - LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Reference Notice**: Notice No 20251030-26 (previous suspension notice dated October 30, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This notice affects only investors and distributors dealing with LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members need to enable the six scheme variants for trading. The reopening restores normal transaction capabilities that were previously suspended.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors in LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND, as they can now resume or initiate investments, redemptions, and systematic transaction plans through the BSE platform.
