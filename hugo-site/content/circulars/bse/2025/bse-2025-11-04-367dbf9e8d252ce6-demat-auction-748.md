## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement details for Settlement No. 748 dated November 4, 2025. The circular lists 105 securities from various companies along with their scrip codes and auction quantities. This is a routine operational circular for settling demat delivery obligations through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 748
- Settlement Date: November 4, 2025
- Total securities listed: 105 (spanning multiple companies)
- Auction quantities range from 1 share to 31,060 shares depending on the scrip
- Includes major companies like State Bank of India, Maruti Suzuki, Exide Industries, Indian Hotels, Federal Bank, and various mutual fund securities
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, automotive, chemicals, technology, and financial services

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/brokers with delivery obligations in the listed securities must participate in or settle through the demat auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's standard auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 4, 2025
- Settlement Number: 748

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market-wide impact. The auction mechanism is used to close out pending delivery obligations for specific transactions. Impact is limited to:

- Specific members/brokers with outstanding delivery obligations in the listed securities
- Shareholders of the 105 listed companies who may be involved in the settlement process
- No broader market implications or trading restrictions

The quantities involved are relatively small for most scrips, indicating this is normal course settlement activity rather than any systemic issue.
