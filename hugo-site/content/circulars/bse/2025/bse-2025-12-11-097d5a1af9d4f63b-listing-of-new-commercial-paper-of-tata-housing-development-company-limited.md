## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The issue comprises 3,100 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CARE A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Tata Housing Development Company Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Quantity: 3,100 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,474 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730751
- Scrip ID: THDC101225
- ISIN: INE582L14JB8
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 11, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14JB8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 11, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing Date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 11, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers are short-term debt instruments with a 3-month tenure, providing working capital financing to Tata Housing Development Company Limited. The issuance is rated A1+ (highest short-term rating), indicating strong credit quality. Trading is restricted to the debt segment with a minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs, limiting participation to institutional and high net-worth investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
