## Summary

BSE has issued a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 776 dated December 15, 2025. The auction includes approximately 110+ securities from various companies with specified quantities available for auction. The list includes major companies like CIPLA, Hindalco, Indian Oil Corporation, Mahindra & Mahindra, Ultratech Cement, and Vedanta, as well as numerous mutual fund units and smaller companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 776
- Auction Date: December 15, 2025
- Total securities listed: Approximately 110+ scrips
- Includes equity shares and mutual fund units
- Quantities range from 1 share to 25,695 shares depending on the security
- Notable large quantity auctions: Rajnish Wellness (25,695 shares), Harshil Agro (6,000 shares), New Light India (5,000 shares), RO Jewels (4,898 shares)
- Major companies included: CIPLA, Hindalco Industries, Indian Oil Corporation, Mahindra & Mahindra, Ultratech Cement, Vedanta, CESC, Dixon Technologies
- Multiple mutual fund units included from 360 ONE, HDFC, ICICI Prudential, Kotak, Motilal Oswal, Nippon India, SBI, and UTI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants interested in the auctioned securities must follow BSE's demat auction procedures
- Participants must comply with settlement and auction guidelines for Settlement No. 776
- Standard auction participation requirements and settlement obligations apply

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 15, 2025 (Settlement No. 776)
- Auction participants should refer to BSE's auction schedule for bidding timelines

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This is a routine demat auction affecting multiple securities. The auction mechanism helps resolve delivery shortfalls in the settlement process.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Affects brokers and clearing members involved in Settlement No. 776 who have delivery obligations. The auction provides a mechanism to close out failed trades.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Direct impact limited to parties involved in the specific settlement; indirect impact on price discovery for the listed securities during auction.

**Sectoral Distribution:** The auction covers diverse sectors including pharmaceuticals (CIPLA, Lincoln Pharma, Senores Pharma), metals (Hindalco, Vedanta, National Aluminium), energy (Indian Oil, JSW Energy), infrastructure, technology, mutual funds, and various other industries, indicating broad-based settlement issues rather than sector-specific concerns.
