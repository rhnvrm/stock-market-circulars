## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544613, trading symbol UTLSOLAR, and ISIN number INE12UR01024. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Fujiyama Power Systems Limited
- Scrip Code: 544613
- Trading Symbol: UTLSOLAR
- ISIN Number: INE12UR01024
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Segment: Equity
- Notice Category: Company related

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the assigned scrip code and symbol for Fujiyama Power Systems Limited in preparation for the upcoming listing. Members should await the separate notice containing the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement adds a new equity security to the BSE platform under the solar/power sector (as indicated by the UTLSOLAR symbol). Trading members and investors interested in the power systems sector should monitor for the follow-up notice that will provide the actual listing date and complete security details. The listing will provide investment opportunities in Fujiyama Power Systems Limited through the BSE equity segment.
