## Summary

BSE has listed 31,00,000 new equity shares of Kay Power And Paper Ltd (Scrip Code: 530255) issued on preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from December 12, 2025, with specified lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- 31,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 20.10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 30.10 per share
- Allotment date: October 11, 2025
- Trading start date: December 12, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 31840001 to 34940000
- ISIN: INE961B01013
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in requirements must be observed as per specified schedule
- Shares subject to lock-in cannot be traded before respective lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (30 lakh shares)**: June 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (1 lakh shares)**: June 30, 2026

## Lock-in Details

- 30,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 31840001 - 34840000): Locked until June 30, 2027
- 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 34840001 - 34940000): Locked until June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares. The lock-in periods ensure gradual entry of these shares into the tradable float. The issuance represents capital raising through warrant conversion, diluting existing equity by approximately 31 lakh shares.
