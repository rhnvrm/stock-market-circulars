## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SK MINERALS & ADDITIVES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544584) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, and will be shifted under M Group effective from Tuesday, November 04, 2025. This follows the Exchange's Notice No. 220251016-69 dated October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SK MINERALS & ADDITIVES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544584
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, November 04, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20251017-3
- Reference Notice: 220251016-69 dated October 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement mechanism, which allows for more flexible trading with T+2 settlement cycles instead of mandatory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the change in trading segment
- Adjust trading and settlement processes for the scrip accordingly from November 04, 2025
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Original notice issued (220251016-69)
- **October 17, 2025**: Current notice issued (20251017-3)
- **November 04, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This change is generally positive for the stock as it moves from the restricted Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment. This will:
- Enable intraday trading in the scrip
- Improve liquidity and trading volumes
- Allow margin trading facilities
- Make the stock more accessible to retail and institutional investors
- Remove the mandatory delivery requirement that exists in Trade for Trade segment
