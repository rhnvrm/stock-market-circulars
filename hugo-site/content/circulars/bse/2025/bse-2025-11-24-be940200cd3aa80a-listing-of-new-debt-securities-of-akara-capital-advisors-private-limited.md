## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited for trading on the Debt segment effective November 24, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, totaling 40,000 units (Rs. 40 crore). The debentures carry a 12% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on May 21, 2027. The securities are rated BBB (Stable) by ICRA and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977309
- **Scrip ID**: 12ACAPL27B
- **ISIN**: INE08XP07423
- **Quantity**: 40,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 40 crore
- **Interest Rate**: 12% per annum (paid monthly)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: May 21, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XP07423
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- The tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all trades
- Market lot of 1 unit applies to all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly intervals
- **Redemption Date**: May 21, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with limited market-wide impact. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard trading procedures apply. Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip code and ISIN for order placement and settlement.

**Investor Impact**: The securities offer a 12% annual return with monthly interest payments, providing regular income over an 18-month tenure. The BBB (Stable) rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate capacity for timely payment of financial commitments.
