## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of CREATIVE NEWTECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544631, symbol CNL, and ISIN number INE985W01018. The exact date of listing and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: CREATIVE NEWTECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544631
- Symbol: CNL
- ISIN Number: INE985W01018
- Listing to occur in due course with date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing of CREATIVE NEWTECH LIMITED equity shares for their records and systems.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement that will enable trading of CREATIVE NEWTECH LIMITED equity shares on BSE. The impact is moderate, primarily affecting investors interested in this specific company and trading members who need to update their systems. The listing will provide liquidity and price discovery for the company's shares once trading commences.
