## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Studds Accessories Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544599, symbol STUDDS, and ISIN No. INE00Q601028. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Studds Accessories Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544599
- Trading Symbol: STUDDS
- ISIN Number: INE00Q601028
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued to all trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the scrip code and symbol for the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The circular provides advance notice to trading members about the upcoming listing of Studds Accessories Limited. Investors interested in the company should await the separate notice containing the actual listing date and complete security details before trading can commence.
