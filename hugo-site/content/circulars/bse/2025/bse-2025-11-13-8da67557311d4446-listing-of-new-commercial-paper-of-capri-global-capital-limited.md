## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The issuance consists of 200 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form with YES Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 200 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 730554, Scrip ID: CGC131125
- ISIN Number: INE180C14615
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,88,663 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- YES Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14615
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- Clarifications can be sought from BSE debt department on specified contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 13, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing provides Capri Global Capital Limited access to debt market funding through commercial paper issuance. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments with approximately 116-day tenure. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs makes these instruments accessible primarily to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.
