## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 977322, Scrip ID: NWFL251125) on its Debt segment effective November 28, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with 7,405 units, each having a face value and issue price of Rs. 1,00,000. The debentures carry a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable rating and mature on October 26, 2035.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 7,405 units
- **Scrip Code**: 977322
- **Scrip ID**: NWFL251125
- **ISIN**: INE918K07QG4
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QG4
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Tick size compliance: 1 paise minimum price movement

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 26, 2035
- **Circular Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement debt listing that adds new tradable securities to BSE's Debt segment. Impact limited to institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems with new scrip details. The securities expand investment options in the debt market for investors seeking AA-rated long-term instruments with approximately 10-year maturity.

**Issuer Profile**: Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, a financial services entity, has successfully placed debt securities with strong credit rating, indicating healthy market confidence.
