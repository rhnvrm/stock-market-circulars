## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Treasury Bill 91TB010825 (ISIN: IN002025X059) effective July 30, 2025, as the security is approaching its redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Single T-Bill affected: 91TB010825 (Scrip Code: 805058)
- ISIN: IN002025X059
- Trading suspension effective from July 30, 2025
- Reason: Security maturity on redemption date
- Reference: DR-684/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified T-Bill from July 30, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in the affected security

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Notice date
- **July 30, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: T-Bill maturity (specific date not mentioned in circular)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one T-Bill security approaching natural maturity. This is a routine administrative action that occurs regularly in debt markets. Trading members holding positions should prepare for settlement at maturity value.
