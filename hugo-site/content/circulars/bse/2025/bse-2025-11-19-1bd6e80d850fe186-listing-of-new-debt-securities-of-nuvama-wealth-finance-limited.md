## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 19, 2025. The securities consist of 277 units with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable credit rating, and are set to mature on April 30, 2029.

## Key Points

- Security Details: NWFL-GOLDFUTURE-30-4-29-PVT (Scrip Code: 977266, Scrip ID: NWFL301025)
- ISIN Number: INE918K07QE9 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 277 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,391 (issued at discount)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QE9
- Members must adhere to the tick size of 1 paise for these securities
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 14, 2025
- Listing Date: November 19, 2025
- Date of Redemption: April 30, 2029
- Notice Date: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing adds 277 units of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited's debt securities to the BSE Debt segment, providing additional fixed-income investment options for institutional and qualified investors. The AA/Stable rating indicates good credit quality. The impact is primarily relevant to debt market participants and investors specifically interested in Nuvama Wealth Finance's debt instruments. No put/call options are available on these securities.
