## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of non-competitive bidding facility for participating in the RBI auction of Government of India dated securities on January 02, 2026. Trading members can bid for 6.48% GS 2035 (re-issue) worth Rs. 32,000 crores through BSE's iBBS web-based NCB-GSec module.

## Key Points

- Security offered: 6.48% GS 2035 (Symbol: 648GS35J26) - Re-issue
- Issue size: Rs. 32,000 crores
- Minimum subscription: 100 units with minimum amount Rs. 10,000
- Maximum bid amount: Rs. 2 crores
- Auction date: January 02, 2026
- Settlement date: January 05, 2026
- Bidding platform: BSE iBBS web-based system NCB-GSec module (https://ibbs.bseindia.com)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular continues the existing non-competitive bidding facility launched via Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit bids through BSE's iBBS NCB-GSec module
- Members can bid from December 30, 2025 (10:00 am) until January 02, 2026 (8:00 am)
- Direct investors can bid until January 01, 2026 (5:00 pm)
- Bids must be in multiples of 100 units with minimum Rs. 10,000 and maximum Rs. 2 crores

## Important Dates

- **Bid Collection Start**: December 30, 2025, 10:00 am onwards (24-hour availability)
- **Bid Collection End (Members)**: January 02, 2026, 8:00 am
- **Bid Collection End (Direct Investors)**: January 01, 2026, 5:00 pm
- **Auction Date**: January 02, 2026
- **Settlement Date**: January 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular for debt market participants. It provides trading members access to participate in RBI's G-sec auction through non-competitive bidding. The impact is limited to members interested in investing in government securities. No changes to existing processes or requirements. Contact details provided for technical support (Trading Operations: 022-2272 8005/8215) and bidding queries (022-2272 8376/5626/8226).
