## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Treasury Bill (T-Bill) with scrip code 805006 (ISIN: IN002024Z370, identified as 364TB261225) effective December 23, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this T-Bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill scrip code 805006 (364TB261225) with ISIN IN002024Z370 is maturing
- Trading suspension effective from December 23, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-783/2025-2026
- Applies to Debt segment trading members
- Single instrument affected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified T-Bill (scrip code 805006) from December 23, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No new positions should be created in this instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 23, 2025
- **T-Bill Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 26, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only holders and traders of this specific T-Bill instrument

**Operational Impact**: Low - routine maturity process for debt instruments with no broader market implications

**Affected Parties**: Trading members dealing in government securities and T-Bills on BSE Debt segment, specifically those holding or trading scrip code 805006
