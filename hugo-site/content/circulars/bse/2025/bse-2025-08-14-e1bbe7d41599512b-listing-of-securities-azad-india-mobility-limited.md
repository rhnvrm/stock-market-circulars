## Summary

BSE has listed 5,317,529 new equity shares of AZAD INDIA MOBILITY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 504731) for trading effective August 18, 2025. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 98 per share and carry a lock-in period until February 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: AZAD INDIA MOBILITY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 504731)
- New shares listed: 5,317,529 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- Issue price: Rs. 98 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 88 premium)
- Allotment date: May 20, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 35175626 to 40493154
- ISIN: INE566M01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 5,317,529 shares until February 15, 2026
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply for unlocked shares

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **February 15, 2026**: Lock-in period expiry date
- **May 20, 2025**: Original allotment date

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as all shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until February 15, 2026. The preferential allotment increases the company's share capital and provides additional liquidity post lock-in expiry. No immediate trading volume expected from these shares.
