## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crore issued by OXYZO Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (500 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,88,967.50 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730011, ISIN: INE04VS14293
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Federal Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for OXYZO Financial Services and investment option for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
