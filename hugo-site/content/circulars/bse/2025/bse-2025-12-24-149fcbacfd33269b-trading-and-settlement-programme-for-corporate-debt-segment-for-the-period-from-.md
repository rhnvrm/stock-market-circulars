## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Corporate Debt Segment (ICDM) for January 2026. The circular provides detailed settlement schedules for three settlement cycles: T+0, T+1, and T+2, covering all trading days from January 1 to January 31, 2026. Settlement numbers range from 2526185 to 2526205.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedules provided for T+0, T+1, and T+2 cycles in the ICDM segment
- Trading days exclude weekends and Republic Day (January 26, 2026)
- Total of 21 settlement numbers allocated for the month (2526185-2526205)
- Participants must check depository settlement deadline times for respective market types
- Securities must be transferred to ICCL account within specified timelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Securities/funds pay-in and pay-out must occur on specified dates
- Participants should refer to ICCL Notice No.20091206-01 dated December 6, 2009 for depository settlement deadline details
- Timely transfer of securities to ICCL account is mandatory

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- **Trading Holiday**: January 26, 2026 (Republic Day)
- **First Settlement**: January 1, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526185)
- **Last Settlement Pay-in**: January 30, 2026 (T+0), February 2, 2026 (T+1), February 3, 2026 (T+2)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard monthly settlement calendar with no changes to existing processes or procedures.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Market participants can plan their corporate debt segment operations based on the published schedule. The settlement cycles follow established patterns with T+0 (same-day), T+1 (next-day), and T+2 (two-day) settlement options.

**Participant Action**: Information only - No special action required beyond following the published settlement calendar for ICDM transactions during January 2026.
