## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are prohibited from dealing in the affected debenture series.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MRHFL 8.315% debentures maturing December 12, 2025
- ISIN affected: INE950O08287 (Security Code: 974833)
- Suspension effective from November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members must not execute any deals in this debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational measure applied when companies fix record dates for debenture servicing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified debenture (ISIN: INE950O08287) from November 27, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades
- Investors holding these debentures should be informed of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **November 27, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **December 12, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. Affects only holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and procedural, ensuring proper identification of eligible investors for redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders. They cannot trade but will receive scheduled redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Exit liquidity removed but offset by imminent maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard corporate action procedure with clear timelines for compliance by trading members.
