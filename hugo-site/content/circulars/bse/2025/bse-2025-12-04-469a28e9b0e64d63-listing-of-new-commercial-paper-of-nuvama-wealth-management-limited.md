## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited on private placement basis. The securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each (total 1000 units) are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 4, 2025. ICICI Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crore total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,961.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730697
- Scrip ID: NWML31225
- ISIN: INE531F14FL4
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE531F14FL4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment using the assigned scrip code and ISIN

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 4, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 3, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term debt financing for Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and offers trading members another debt instrument option. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+) indicate low credit risk. Impact limited to debt market participants interested in short-term corporate debt instruments.
