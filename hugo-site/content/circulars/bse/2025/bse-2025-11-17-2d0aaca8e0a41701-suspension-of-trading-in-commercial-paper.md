## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251117-6 suspending trading in commercial paper (CP) of 25 companies effective from November 20-21, 2025. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption of these commercial papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified securities from the designated dates.

## Key Points

- 25 companies affected by trading suspension for CP redemption
- Record dates set for November 20, 21, and 23, 2025
- No dealings permitted from respective record dates
- All suspensions fall under Debt Register references DR-760/2025-2026 and DR-761/2025-2026
- Notice applies to Debt Segment under Corporate Actions category

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the stated "No Dealings From" dates
- Members must ensure their systems reflect the trading suspension for these securities
- All transactions related to these CPs must cease from the respective dates

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **First Suspension Date**: November 20, 2025 (13 CPs)
- **Second Suspension Date**: November 21, 2025 (12 CPs)
- **Record Dates**: November 20, 21, and 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action affecting the debt market segment. The trading suspension is temporary and specifically related to the redemption process of commercial papers. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants holding or trading these specific CPs
- Trading members who must update their systems and processes
- Investors in these commercial papers who need to be aware of redemption timeline

The suspension ensures orderly settlement and redemption of these debt instruments. No broader market impact is anticipated as this is standard procedure for maturing commercial papers.
