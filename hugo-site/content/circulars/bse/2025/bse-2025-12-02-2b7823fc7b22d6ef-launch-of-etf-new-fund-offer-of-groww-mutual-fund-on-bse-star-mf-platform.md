## Summary

Groww Mutual Fund is launching GROWW NIFTY METAL ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from December 03, 2025 to December 17, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 24, 2025. Both regular and direct plan variants will be offered under the equity scheme category.

## Key Points

- NFO comprises two schemes: GROWW NIFTY METAL ETF and GROWW NIFTY METAL ETF - DIRECT PLAN, both categorized under equity
- Tentative allotment date: December 24, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled with CC and N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last NFO fund payout day to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 19, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt by December 19, 2025 at 6:00 PM
- NFO extended till midnight on December 17, 2025 for lumpsum purchases

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Members must report cancelled transactions with CC and N flag to RTA/AMC
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 19, 2025 for allotment eligibility
- All cheque and OTM payments must result in clear funds by the December 19, 2025 deadline

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: December 03, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: December 17, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: December 19, 2025 by 6:00 PM
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Fund Initiation Deadline**: 9:00 AM on T+1 day for each transaction
- **Payment Pending Window**: Until 3:00 PM on last NFO payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine NFO launch for a sector-specific ETF tracking Nifty Metal index. It provides investors with exposure to the metal sector through an exchange-traded fund structure.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members. Members need to monitor client fund initiation timelines closely to avoid automatic cancellations and ensure compliance with the strict payment deadlines for successful allotment.

**Investor Impact**: Provides new investment opportunity in metal sector through ETF route with both regular and direct plan options available during the 15-day NFO window.
