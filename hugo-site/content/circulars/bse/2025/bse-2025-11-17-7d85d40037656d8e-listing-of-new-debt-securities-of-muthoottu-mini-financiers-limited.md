## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (MMFL) on its Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 5,000 units at face value of ₹1,00,000 per unit, totaling ₹50 crore. The non-convertible debentures carry a 9.25% per annum interest rate with monthly payments and mature on November 13, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977296
- **Scrip ID**: 925MMFL027
- **ISIN**: INE101Q07BT9
- **Quantity**: 5,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: ₹50 crore
- **Interest Rate**: 9.25% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly (10th of each month from December 10, 2025 to November 13, 2027)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A (Stable)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: November 13, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE101Q07BT9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 13, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (10th of each month)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with limited broad market impact.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Relevant only for debt market investors and those specifically interested in Muthoottu Mini Financiers' debt instruments. The ICRA A (Stable) rating indicates adequate credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for debt segment with existing trading infrastructure and protocols in place.
