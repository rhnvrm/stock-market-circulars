## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd effective December 31, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures with ISIN INE860H07IE6 (Code: 974393). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ABCL 7.93% debentures maturing on January 15, 2026 (Private)
- ISIN: INE860H07IE6
- Scrip Code: 974393
- Record date set as December 31, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 31, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 31, 2025
- Members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension to avoid regulatory violations
- Appropriate systems and processes should be updated to reflect the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited, affects only holders of this specific debenture issue. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading will be possible from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified debenture scrip from December 31, 2025.
