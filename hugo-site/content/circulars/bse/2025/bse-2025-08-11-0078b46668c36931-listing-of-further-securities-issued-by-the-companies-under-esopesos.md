## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities from eight companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These shares will be available for trading starting August 12, 2025, with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 8 companies issued additional shares under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 416,529 new shares across all companies
- All shares have no lock-in period restrictions
- Trading commences August 12, 2025
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **Amber Enterprises India Limited** (540902): 8,150 shares, ₹10 face value
- **ESAF Small Finance Bank Ltd** (544020): 42,464 shares, ₹10 face value  
- **Jagsonpal Pharmaceuticals Limited** (507789): 38,774 shares, ₹2 face value
- **NIIT Learning Systems Limited** (543952): 243,332 shares, ₹2 face value
- **PDS LIMITED** (538730): 10,000 shares, ₹2 face value
- **Restaurant Brands Asia Limited** (543248): 9,799 shares, ₹10 face value
- **Tech Mahindra Limited** (532755): 26,185 shares, ₹5 face value
- **Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited** (543350): 37,825 shares, ₹1 face value

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings representing small percentages of companies' total share capital. No trading restrictions apply, allowing immediate liquidity for option holders.
