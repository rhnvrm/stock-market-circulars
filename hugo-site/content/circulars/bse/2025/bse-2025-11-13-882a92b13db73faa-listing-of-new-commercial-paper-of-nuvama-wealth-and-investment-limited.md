## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1500 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 13, 2025. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- 1500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 730555, Scrip ID: NWIL121125
- ISIN: INE523L14AQ9
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,702.00 per security
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L14AQ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for pricing

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** November 12, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors with an additional short-term debt instrument (91-day tenor) from Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
