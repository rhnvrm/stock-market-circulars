## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 3, 2025. The issue comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a 3-month tenure maturing on January 30, 2026. The paper is rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

- **Scrip Code**: 730477
- **Scrip ID**: IFPL311025
- **ISIN**: INE879F14LT8
- **Credit Ratings**: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for commercial paper.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14LT8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price quotations
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 3 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting only institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities will have a new instrument available for trading on BSE's debt platform. The A1+ ratings from both CRISIL and ICRA indicate high credit quality and lowest credit risk for short-term debt.

**Investor Impact**: Provides debt market participants with a short-term investment option with high credit rating, suitable for treasury management and short-term deployment of funds.
