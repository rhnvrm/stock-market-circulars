## Summary

BSE has announced that OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 14, 2025. This follows the previous notice (20251104-9) and restores full transaction capabilities including Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP) for all six plan variants of the fund.

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND reopened for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: November 14, 2025
- All transaction types enabled: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans
- Three investment options: Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- Fund categorized under Equity segment
- Follows previous suspension notice (20251104-9)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update restoring normal trading operations for the OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice regarding the availability of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 14, 2025 - OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND reopened for all ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Notice Number**: 20251113-50
- **Reference Notice**: 20251104-9 (previous suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational update affecting only a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for existing and prospective investors in OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND. Investors can now resume normal transactions including fresh subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can now process all transaction types for the six OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND scheme variants.

## Scheme Details

All six variants of OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND are now available:

**Regular Plans:**
1. Growth Option (ISIN: INF0S5R01091)
2. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF0S5R01109)
3. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF0S5R01117)

**Direct Plans:**
4. Growth Option (ISIN: INF0S5R01125)
5. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF0S5R01075)
6. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF0S5R01083)

All schemes are categorized under the Equity segment and classified as Arbitrage Funds.
