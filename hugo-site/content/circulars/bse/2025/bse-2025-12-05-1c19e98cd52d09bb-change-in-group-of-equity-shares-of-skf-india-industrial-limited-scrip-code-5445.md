## Summary

BSE has notified that equity shares of SKF India (Industrial) Limited (Scrip Code - 544572) will be permitted to trade under 'B' group effective Friday, December 19, 2025. This represents a change in the group classification for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: SKF India (Industrial) Limited
- Scrip Code: 544572
- New Trading Group: B Group
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Notice applies to all Trading Members and market intermediaries

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to 'B' group, which is BSE's standard group for actively traded securities. This change affects the trading parameters and market segment under which the stock operates.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and market intermediaries must:
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Ensure trading in these equity shares is conducted under 'B' group parameters from December 19, 2025
- Familiarize themselves with B group trading rules and requirements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The change to 'B' group classification generally indicates the stock meets BSE's criteria for regular trading group. This may affect:
- Trading parameters and margins
- Settlement procedures
- Surveillance mechanisms
- Investor access and participation

The reclassification typically reflects the stock's liquidity, market capitalization, and compliance status. Trading members should prepare their systems and inform clients about the group change before the effective date.
