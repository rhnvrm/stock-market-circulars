## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying a 10% annual interest rate and maturing on August 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 5,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977065
- Scrip ID: 10KFLD27
- ISIN: INE403Q07GF7 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,465.75
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Interest Rate: 10% per annum (quarterly payments)
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE403Q07GF7
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: December 19, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Quarterly from March 1, 2026 to August 29, 2027
- Redemption Date: August 29, 2027
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds to the available debt instruments on BSE's platform. The securities carry an IND A/Stable rating, indicating adequate safety with moderate credit risk. The 10% coupon rate and quarterly interest payments provide income opportunities for debt investors. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in Kosamattam Finance Limited's instruments.
