## Summary

Prodocs Solutions Limited has listed its equity shares on BSE with scrip code PRODOCS. The shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) shows a total of 7,050,000 fully paid-up equity shares held by 434 shareholders, with promoters holding 51.77% and public shareholders holding 48.23%.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 7,050,000 (all fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 3,650,000 shares (51.77%)
- Public shareholders: 428 shareholders holding 3,400,000 shares (48.23%)
- All shares are in dematerialized form
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or under any encumbrance
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Company does not have equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or other encumbrances
- All equity shares held in dematerialized form as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 12, 2025
- Shareholding pattern represents post-issue holdings

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine disclosure for a newly listed company showing standard shareholding distribution.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - The shareholding structure is straightforward with clear promoter and public shareholding, no complex securities or encumbrances.

**Investor Impact:** The promoter holding of 51.77% provides controlling stake while maintaining adequate public float of 48.23%, meeting minimum public shareholding norms. Clean shareholding structure with no pledges or encumbrances indicates healthy promoter commitment.
