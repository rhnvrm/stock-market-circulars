## Summary

Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894) has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. Trading members are informed that equity shares will trade on ex-rights basis from November 19, 2025. The rights issue offers 1 equity share of Rs 10/- face value at a premium of Rs 5.35 per share (total Rs 15.35) for every 4 equity shares held.

## Key Points

- Company: Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894)
- Rights entitlement ratio: 1 equity share for every 4 shares held
- Face value: Rs 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs 5.35 per share
- Issue price: Rs 15.35 per share (face value + premium)
- Settlement number: DR-759/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action announcement following existing rights issue procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in Indowind Energy Ltd equity shares on ex-rights basis from November 19, 2025
- Shareholders holding shares as of the record date will be entitled to participate in the rights issue
- Only shareholders on record as of November 19, 2025 are eligible for rights entitlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-759/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to additional shares proportionate to their holdings at a discounted price. Shareholders must hold shares before the ex-rights date to be eligible.

**Trading Impact**: From November 19, 2025, the stock will trade ex-rights, meaning new buyers after this date will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue. This typically results in stock price adjustment to reflect the rights value.

**Capital Structure**: The rights issue will result in equity dilution and increase in share capital, subject to shareholder subscription levels. The issue provides existing shareholders the opportunity to maintain their proportionate ownership.
