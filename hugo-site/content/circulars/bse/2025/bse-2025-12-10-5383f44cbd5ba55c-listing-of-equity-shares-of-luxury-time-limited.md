## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Luxury Time Limited on the exchange. This circular provides details regarding the listing parameters, trading information, and other relevant details for market participants.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Luxury Time Limited are now listed on BSE
- New security available for trading on the exchange
- Market participants can now trade in these shares subject to BSE regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to include the new security
- Standard BSE trading and settlement procedures apply
- Investors should conduct due diligence before investing

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing effective from the date specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new investment opportunity for market participants. The impact is moderate as it expands the universe of available securities on BSE. Investors interested in the luxury goods or watch industry may find this listing relevant. Trading volumes and market reception will determine the long-term significance of this listing.
