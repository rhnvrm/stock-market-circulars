## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (formerly Edelweiss Financial Services Limited) issued on private placement basis. The securities, worth Rs. 100 crore (10,000 units of Rs. 100,000 face value each), carry a 9.20% p.a. interest rate and are rated CARE AA/Stable. The securities are listed under ISIN INE523L07AM2 and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 10,000 units with market lot of 1
- **Scrip Code**: 976911 (Scrip ID: 920NWIL27)
- **ISIN**: INE523L07AM2 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 103,596.16 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 9.20% per annum (payable annually)
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AA/Stable
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 20, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN Number INE523L07AM2
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective date of listing and admission to dealings
- **July 13, 2026**: First interest payment date
- **December 20, 2027**: Maturity and redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with limited market impact. The listing expands the debt securities available for trading on BSE's debt segment but is primarily relevant to institutional investors and debt market participants. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates high credit quality. The 9.20% interest rate reflects current market conditions for AA-rated corporate debt. No immediate impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is expected.
