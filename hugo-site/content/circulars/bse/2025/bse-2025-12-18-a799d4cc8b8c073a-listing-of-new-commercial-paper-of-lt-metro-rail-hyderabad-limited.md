## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 12,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 6,000 crore in aggregate. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025, under scrip code 730802 and ISIN INE128M14AX1.

## Key Points

- Issuer: L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Quantity: 12,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,96,802 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730802
- Scrip ID: LTMR171225
- ISIN: INE128M14AX1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE128M14AX1
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 23, 2026
- Tenure: 37 days (from allotment to redemption)

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it pertains to a private placement of Commercial Paper by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited. The short-term debt instrument (37-day tenure) is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and India Ratings, indicating high credit quality. The listing provides institutional investors with a trading avenue for this short-term debt security but does not affect broader market operations or retail investors.
