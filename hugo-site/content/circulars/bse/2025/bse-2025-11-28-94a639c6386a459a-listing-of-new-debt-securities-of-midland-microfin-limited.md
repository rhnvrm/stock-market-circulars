## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Midland Microfin Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 28, 2025. The listing comprises 5,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 10.75% per annum with monthly interest payments. The securities are rated ACUITE A- and will mature on November 27, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Midland Microfin Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977325
- **Scrip ID**: 1075MML27B
- **ISIN**: INE884Q07814
- **Quantity**: 5,000 debentures
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,00,000 per debenture
- **Interest Rate**: 10.75% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-
- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 27, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Demat only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds liquidity options for investors holding Midland Microfin's debt securities on the BSE platform. The ACUITE A- rating indicates adequate credit quality. With monthly interest payments and a relatively short tenure of approximately 2 years, these securities are suitable for investors seeking regular income with moderate credit risk. The private placement nature suggests limited retail participation, with impact primarily on institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.
