## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of new Commercial Paper securities issued by STANDARD CHARTERED CAPITAL LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities comprise 3000 Commercial Papers of face value Rs.5,00,000 each, issued at a discount price of Rs.4,78,644. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective October 17, 2025, with a redemption date of June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 3000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,78,644 per unit (discount of Rs.21,356)
- Scrip Code: 730407
- Scrip ID: SCCL171025
- Detail Name: SCCL-5-6-26-CP
- ISIN: INE403G14TQ4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members beyond standard trading procedures for debt securities.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 17, 2025
- Listing Date: October 17, 2025
- Date of Redemption: June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for debt securities with minimal market impact. The listing adds liquidity to the debt segment for Standard Chartered Capital Limited's Commercial Paper. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members can contact the debt department at BSE for clarifications on contact numbers 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
