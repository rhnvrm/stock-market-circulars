## Summary

Mindspace Business Parks REIT has listed 120,000 new debt securities on BSE Debt segment through private placement, effective December 9, 2025. The securities carry a 6.9601% annual interest rate with ICRA AAA/STABLE rating, face value of Rs. 100,000, and mature on December 8, 2028. Trading will be conducted only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 120,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977350, Scrip ID: 69601MBP28
- ISIN: INE0CCU07181
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,008.9640
- Interest Rate: 6.9601% per annum (quarterly payment)
- Credit Rating: ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only
- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0CCU07181
- Members must apply tick size of 1 paise for these securities
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Further details available in Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 9, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 31, 2025
- Interest Payment Frequency: Quarterly from December 31, 2025 to December 8, 2028
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The ICRA AAA/STABLE rating indicates high credit quality. The securities are issued through private placement and will provide institutional investors access to REIT debt instruments. No put/call options are attached. The quarterly interest payment structure provides regular income to debenture holders. Impact is limited to participants in the debt market segment.
