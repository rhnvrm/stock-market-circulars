## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,495,000 equity shares of Sunrise Efficient Marketing Limited (Scrip Code: 543515) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Friday, November 14, 2025. These new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until May 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,495,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 96/-
- Issue price: Rs. 106/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 15665001 to 19160000
- ISIN: INE0KPY01017
- Allotment dates: May 27, 2025 and June 10, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commencement: November 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for all 3,495,000 shares until May 15, 2026
- No trading permitted in the locked-in shares during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Dates**: May 27, 2025 and June 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The shares represent warrant conversion and will increase the free float gradually after the lock-in period expires.

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all shares are locked-in until May 15, 2026. Post lock-in, approximately 3.5 million shares will become available for trading.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders should note the dilution effect of 3,495,000 additional shares. The preferential issue price of Rs. 106/- provides a reference point for recent company valuation.
