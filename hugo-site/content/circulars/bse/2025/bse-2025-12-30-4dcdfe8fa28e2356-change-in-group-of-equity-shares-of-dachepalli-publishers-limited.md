## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of DACHEPALLI PUBLISHERS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544667), an SME IPO stock, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective January 13, 2026. This change follows the earlier notice dated December 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: DACHEPALLI PUBLISHERS LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544667
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- Notice Number: 20251230-2
- Previous Related Notice: 20251229-31 dated December 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from MT Group (Trade for Trade segment) to M Group (Rolling segment), which changes the trading mechanism from delivery-based trade-for-trade to regular rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment change effective January 13, 2026
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications
- Adjust trading systems and processes accordingly for the new segment

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Previous related notice issued
- **December 30, 2025**: Current notice date
- **January 13, 2026**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) will improve trading flexibility and liquidity for DACHEPALLI PUBLISHERS LIMITED shares. Trade for Trade segments typically have stricter settlement requirements with mandatory delivery, while Rolling segments allow for more flexible intraday and settlement mechanisms. This change should positively impact market participants by enabling standard trading practices for this SME IPO stock.
