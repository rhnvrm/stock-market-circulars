## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd (Scrip Code 975892) effective December 03, 2025. Trading was previously suspended to facilitate the payment of interest and redemption of debentures with a record date of July 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading in Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd debentures (Scrip Code 975892) will resume from December 03, 2025
- The suspension was implemented following Exchange Notice No. 20250721-46 dated July 21, 2025
- Record date for interest payment and redemption was set as July 23, 2025
- The company has requested resumption of trading after completion of the corporate action
- Reference number: DR-769/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notification regarding the resumption of trading following a corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the debentures will be available for trading from December 03, 2025
- Market participants should update their systems to reflect the availability of these securities for trading

## Important Dates

- **July 21, 2025**: Original suspension notice issued
- **July 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 02, 2025**: Resumption notice issued
- **December 03, 2025**: Trading resumes in the debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects investors and traders in Nuvama Wealth Finance debentures. The resumption allows normal trading activity to continue in these debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard corporate action completion. Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the trading status change.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors who hold or wish to trade these debentures can resume normal trading activities from December 03, 2025.
