## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Earnest Constructions Pvt Ltd effective November 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of NCDs and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Earnest Constructions Pvt Ltd
- Debenture ISIN: INE0HCI07011
- Security Code: ECPL-12%-25-6-27-PVT (974942)
- Purpose: Early Redemption of NCD and Payment of Interest
- Record Date: November 12, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 12, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-754/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for suspension of trading due to early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Earnest Constructions Pvt Ltd from November 12, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No trading activity should be conducted in the debenture with ISIN INE0HCI07011 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 12, 2025**: Record date for early redemption and interest payment
- **November 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of specific debentures of a private limited company

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard procedure for corporate actions involving early redemption

**Investor Impact**: Moderate for affected debenture holders who will receive early redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the record date. Trading members must ensure no inadvertent trades occur in the suspended security.
