## Summary

Standard Chartered Capital Limited has listed new Commercial Papers aggregating Rs.150 crores (3000 units of Rs.5 lakhs each) on the BSE Debt segment on November 24, 2025. The instruments are issued on a private placement basis with ISIN INE403G14TW2, Scrip Code 730636, and carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Standard Chartered Capital Limited
- Instrument Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Total Issue Size: Rs.150 crores (3000 units)
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,85,324.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730636
- Scrip ID: SCCL241125
- ISIN: INE403G14TW2
- Credit Ratings: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G14TW2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for these securities

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 24, 2025
- Redemption Date: April 30, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 5 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding to Standard Chartered Capital Limited and offers investment opportunities for debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or broader regulatory framework. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers for clarifications.
