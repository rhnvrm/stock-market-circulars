## Summary

BSE issued a correction notice updating the transfer date of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) to the "X" group. The corrected transfer date is December 17, 2025, superseding the date mentioned in the original Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025. All other contents of the original notice remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- This is a correction to Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025
- Company affected: Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572)
- Corrected transfer date to X group: December 17, 2025
- All other contents of the original notice remain unchanged
- Trading members are requested to take note of the corrected date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice only corrects the effective date previously communicated for the group transfer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the corrected transfer date of December 17, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the accurate group transfer date
- Participants should refer to the original notice for all other unchanged requirements and details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Corrected Transfer Date to X Group**: December 17, 2025
- **Original Notice Reference**: Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The correction has medium impact as it affects the categorization timeline for Ranjit Securities Ltd. Transfer to the X group typically indicates securities with specific risk characteristics or compliance issues. Trading members and investors need to be aware of the correct effective date (December 17, 2025) for proper risk assessment and trading decisions. The one-day notice period before the effective date requires immediate attention from market participants.
