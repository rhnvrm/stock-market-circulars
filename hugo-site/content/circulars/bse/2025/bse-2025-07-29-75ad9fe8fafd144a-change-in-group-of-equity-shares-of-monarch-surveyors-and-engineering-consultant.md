## Summary

BSE has announced that trading in equity shares of MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544453) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment with effect from August 12, 2025. The dealings will be shifted under M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544453
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: August 12, 2025
- This is a continuation of exchange notice no. 20250728-56 dated July 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment, allowing for normal trading operations under M Group classification.
