## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed 1500 Commercial Papers on BSE Debt segment on December 8, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, total issue size of Rs. 75 crore. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will mature on March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1500 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730704
- Scrip ID: OWL41225
- ISIN: INE466L14FL1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Only in dematerialised form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE466L14FL1
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 8, 2025 (effective from today)
- Allotment Date: December 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 5, 2026
- Tenure: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with limited implications for broader equity markets. The instrument is accessible only to institutional and high net-worth investors due to the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk. The 90-day tenure classifies this as a short-term money market instrument primarily used for working capital management by the issuer.
