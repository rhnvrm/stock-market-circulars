## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 10, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730100 and ISIN INE918K14CO4. The Commercial Papers are valued at Rs. 5,00,000 each with a total quantity of 1000 units, issue price of Rs. 487,354, and redemption date of March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed: Commercial Paper of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited
- Quantity: 1000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Face Value)
- Scrip Code: 730100, Scrip ID: NWFL26825
- ISIN: INE918K14CO4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 487,354 per unit
- Allotment Date: November 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 10, 2026
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt instrument listing that adds short-term commercial paper to the debt segment. The listing provides additional debt market options for investors interested in highly-rated (A1+) commercial paper.

**Issuer Impact**: Neutral to positive for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, providing short-term funding through the commercial paper market with a maturity period of approximately 4 months.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Relevant primarily for debt market participants and institutional investors interested in short-tenure, high-rated commercial paper. The dual A1+ rating from CARE and CRISIL indicates high credit quality.
