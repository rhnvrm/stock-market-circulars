## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Peshwa Wheat Limited filed on December 16, 2025, on its SME platform for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 and will be available for at least 21 days for public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Peshwa Wheat Limited with BSE SME Exchange
- Document filed on December 16, 2025
- Contains all mandatory disclosures per Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document hosted on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 requirements for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 16, 2025 (by January 6, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company: Peshwa Wheat Limited must address public comments as per regulatory framework

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed and made available
- **January 6, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)
- Document will remain available for at least 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a procedural notice for a single SME company's draft IPO document. It does not affect existing market operations or listed securities.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for potential SME IPO investors - Provides opportunity to review Peshwa Wheat Limited's offer document and provide feedback before final IPO launch.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing market participants. Standard IPO process step for the issuing company and underwriters.
