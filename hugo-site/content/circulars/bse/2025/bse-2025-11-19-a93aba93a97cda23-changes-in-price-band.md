## Summary

BSE has announced revisions to price bands for 13 securities, effective from November 20, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20%, indicating different levels of trading restrictions based on surveillance measures. These changes will limit the maximum price movement allowed for these securities during trading sessions.

## Key Points

- 13 securities will have revised price bands effective November 20, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20% (least restrictive)
- Two securities (GSB Finance Ltd and Prerna Infrabuild Ltd) receive the tightest 2% band
- Vardhman Holdings Ltd receives the widest 20% band
- Changes are part of BSE's surveillance measures
- Trading members are required to implement these changes in their systems

## Regulatory Changes

The price band changes represent surveillance measures applied to specific securities. The varying levels of restriction (2%, 5%, 10%, 20%) indicate different degrees of volatility concerns or surveillance requirements for each security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands in their trading systems before November 20, 2025
- All trades in the affected securities must comply with the new price band limits
- Members requiring clarifications should contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revised price bands will restrict intraday price movements for 13 securities, potentially reducing volatility but also limiting trading opportunities. Securities with tighter bands (2% and 5%) will experience more significant trading restrictions.

**Affected Securities and Price Bands**:
- **2% Band**: GSB Finance Ltd (511543), Prerna Infrabuild Ltd (531802)
- **5% Band**: Comrade Appliances Ltd (543921), DAPS Advertising Ltd (543651), Phaarmasia Ltd (523620), Shardul Securities Ltd (512393)
- **10% Band**: Danube Industries Ltd (540361), Garnet International Ltd (512493), Safecure Services Ltd (544596), Shanti Educational Initiatives Ltd (539921), Shreyas Intermediates Ltd (526335)
- **20% Band**: Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited (751056), Vardhman Holdings Ltd (500439)

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems must be updated to reflect new price bands, and investors should be aware of the increased or decreased trading flexibility depending on the security.
