## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED
- Security Code: 977318
- ISIN: INE08KJ07159
- Debenture Series: MGFL-10.85%-26-12-26-PVT
- Record Date: December 11, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 11, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| December 9, 2025 | Notice issued |
| December 11, 2025 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| December 11, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date) |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and relates to a standard corporate action.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders on the record date will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading will be possible from December 11, 2025.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in these debentures and communicate the suspension to affected clients.

**Liquidity Impact:** Trading liquidity for this debenture series will be completely halted from the specified date, preventing any secondary market transactions.
