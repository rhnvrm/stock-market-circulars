## Summary

Unisem Agritech Limited (CIN: U01100KA2016PLC096390) has allocated 9,36,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 5 each to anchor investors at a price of Rs. 65.00 per share (including share premium of Rs. 60.00) on December 9, 2025. The Board of Directors finalized this allocation in consultation with the Book Running Lead Managers to the public issue.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 9,36,000 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs. 65.00 per equity share
- Face value: Rs. 5.00 per share
- Share premium: Rs. 60.00 per share
- Board meeting date for allocation approval: December 9, 2025
- Four anchor investors participated in the allocation

## Anchor Investor Details

### Allocation Breakdown

1. **Universal Golden Fund**
   - Shares allocated: 3,08,000
   - Percentage of anchor portion: 32.91%
   - Bid price: Rs. 65.00 per share

2. **Resonance Opportunities Fund**
   - Shares allocated: 1,54,000
   - Percentage of anchor portion: 16.45%
   - Bid price: Rs. 65.00 per share

3. **J4S Venture Fund - I**
   - Shares allocated: 1,54,000
   - Percentage of anchor portion: 16.45%
   - Bid price: Rs. 65.00 per share

4. **Getfive Opportunity Fund - I**
   - Shares allocated: 3,20,000
   - Percentage of anchor portion: 34.19%
   - Bid price: Rs. 65.00 per share

## Regulatory Requirements

- As per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2009 (as amended), if the offer price discovered through book building is higher than the anchor investor allocation price, anchor investors will be required to pay the difference by the pay-in date specified in the revised Confirmation of Allocation Note (CAN)
- Information disclosed to BSE for public dissemination on the exchange website
- Copy sent to SEBI Corporation Finance Department, Division of Issues and Listing
