## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 29, 2025. This session is designed to allow trading members using third-party trading platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will simulate various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. All trades executed during this session are for testing purposes only and will not create any actual margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or legal rights and liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for November 29, 2025 for Electronic Gold Receipts segment
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be deployed
- Session designed for testing third-party platforms, vendor systems, and in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Tests will cover various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- All mock trades will not attract any real obligations or create legal rights/liabilities
- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the mock trading day
- Active participation from all members is requested

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback for the mock trading session by 3:00 PM on November 29, 2025
- Feedback should be provided via contact numbers or email IDs specified in the circular
- Members should contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

**November 29, 2025 - Mock Trading Session Schedule:**
- Log-in: 09:15 AM - 10:00 AM
- Pre-Open Order Entry Period: 10:00 AM - 10:08 AM
- Matching Period: 10:08 AM - 10:15 AM
- Continuous Trading: 10:15 AM - 01:00 PM
- Block Deal Window: 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM
- Closing: 01:00 PM - 01:10 PM
- Post-Closing: 01:10 PM - 01:20 PM

**Feedback Deadline:** 3:00 PM on November 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a mock trading session with no impact on actual market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Low - The session provides an opportunity for trading members to test and validate their trading systems, platforms, and applications in a simulated environment. This helps ensure system readiness and identify potential issues before live trading.

**Member Impact:** Optional participation for testing and familiarization purposes. Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can use this session to verify functionality and prepare for various market scenarios including exceptional conditions.
