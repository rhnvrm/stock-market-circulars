## Summary

BSE announces the Offer for Sale (OFS) schedule for IRONWOOD EDUCATION LIMITED (Security Code: 508918, ISIN: INE791H01011) with bidding scheduled for November 4th, 2025 (T Day) for institutional and non-institutional investors, and November 6th, 2025 (T+1 Day) for retail investors and unallocated bidders from T Day. Total offer size is 2,46,977 shares with 10% reserved for retail investors.

## Key Points

- **Total Offer Size**: 2,46,977 shares
- **Retail Offer Size**: 24,698 shares (10% reservation)
- **Non-Retail Offer Size**: 2,22,279 shares
- **Floor Price**: Rs. 39.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Tick Size**: Rs. 0.01
- **Allocation Method**: Proportionate
- **T Day Bidding**: November 4th, 2025 for IC/MF/OTH/NII categories
- **T+1 Day Bidding**: November 6th, 2025 for RI/RIC categories and carried forward bids
- **Bid Entry Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM on both days
- **Give up/Take up Window**: Until 7:30 AM on T+1 and T+2 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational details for an upcoming OFS event following existing OFS framework and procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must note the OFS schedule and facilitate client participation
- **Retail Investors** (bid value up to Rs. 2 lakhs) must provide 100% upfront margin in cash and cash equivalents
- **Non-Retail Investors** must provide 100% upfront margin in cash
- **Institutional Investors** can bid with either 100% upfront margin or 0% margin (with subsequent settlement obligations)
- **Multiple bidding** is allowed across categories
- Retail investors bidding in NII category are not eligible for seller discounts
- If cumulative retail investor bids exceed Rs. 2 lakhs across exchanges, RI/RIC category bids become ineligible

## Important Dates

- **November 4th, 2025 (T Day)**: Bid entry, modification, and cancellation for IC/MF/OTH/NII categories (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **November 6th, 2025 (T+1 Day)**: 
  - Give up/Take up for institutional 0% margin bids (until 7:30 AM)
  - Bid entry, modification, and cancellation for RI/RIC categories (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
  - Unallocated bidders from T Day can carry forward bids
- **November 7th, 2025 (T+2 Day)**: Give up/Take up window (until 7:30 AM)
- **Settlement**: T+1 for most categories, T+2 for retail investors

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to IRONWOOD EDUCATION LIMITED stock with moderate offer size of 2.47 lakh shares. The OFS may create temporary price pressure depending on demand-supply dynamics.

**Investor Categories Affected**:
- Retail Investors (individuals, HUF, NRI with bids up to Rs. 2 lakhs)
- Non-Institutional Investors (individuals, HUF, trusts, body corporates, PMS with bids above Rs. 2 lakhs)
- Institutional Investors (mutual funds, insurance companies, FIIs, FIs, banks)

**Operational Impact**: Members must ensure systems are configured to access iBBS platform, RTRMS, and Extranet through internet or leased line URLs. The two-day bidding structure requires coordination for retail investor participation and carry-forward bid management.

**Liquidity**: The proportionate allocation method ensures fair distribution across investor categories with retail reservation providing specific access for smaller investors.
