## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 779 dated December 18, 2025. The circular lists 87 securities across various companies that are subject to demat auction along with their respective quantities. This auction is conducted to fulfill delivery obligations where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 779
- Settlement Date: December 18, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 87 scrips
- Largest quantity auctioned: Meesho Limited (544632) - 283,122 shares
- Second largest: Mangalam Industries (537800) - 89,634 shares
- Includes major companies like HFCL, IIFL Finance, Tata Motors, SRF, Vodafone Idea
- Covers diverse sectors including technology, finance, manufacturing, and infrastructure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine auction settlement circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with short delivery positions in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities for auction settlement
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's demat auction procedures and timelines
- Failed delivery obligations must be closed out through this auction process

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 18, 2025
- Auction Date: As per BSE auction schedule for Settlement No. 779

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 87 securities with varying quantities. The high volume for Meesho Limited (283,122 shares) and Mangalam Industries (89,634 shares) may cause temporary price volatility in these scrips during the auction.

**Trading Member Impact:** Direct impact on members with failed delivery obligations who must participate in the auction to close out their positions, potentially at unfavorable prices.

**Investor Impact:** Limited direct impact on regular investors. May cause temporary liquidity issues or price movements in the affected securities during auction execution.
