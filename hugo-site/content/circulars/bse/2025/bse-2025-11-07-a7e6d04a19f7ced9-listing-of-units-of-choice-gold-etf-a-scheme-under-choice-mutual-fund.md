## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Choice Gold ETF units effective Monday, November 10, 2025. This open-ended Exchange Traded Fund replicates and tracks the domestic price of gold. A total of 4,749,787 units will be listed in the E Group with scrip code 544601, trading in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Total Units**: 4,749,787 units
- **Scrip Code**: 544601
- **ISIN**: INF2KCX01012
- **Face Value**: Rs.100.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.119.94 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: November 4, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit (unit market lot trading)
- **Group**: E Group
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Investment Manager**: Choice AMC Private Limited
- **Registered Office**: Sunil Patodia Tower, Plot No 156-158, J.B. Nagar, Andheri East, Mumbai 400099

## Market Makers

- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited
- IRAGE Broking Services LLP
- RiddiSiddhi Bullions Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in Choice Gold ETF units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Standard ETF trading and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides investors with another gold investment vehicle through the exchange-traded fund route. The availability of three market makers ensures adequate liquidity. Impact is limited to investors seeking gold exposure through ETF instruments rather than physical gold or gold futures.
