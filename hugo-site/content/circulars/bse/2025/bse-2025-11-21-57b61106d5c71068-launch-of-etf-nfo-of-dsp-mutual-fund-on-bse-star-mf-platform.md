## Summary

DSP Mutual Fund is launching DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be available from November 24, 2025, to December 08, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 12, 2025. Both regular and direct plan options will be available under the equity scheme category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025
- Two scheme options available: DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 ETF and DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 ETF - DIRECT PLAN
- Tentative allotment date: December 12, 2025
- NFO extended till midnight on December 08, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending till 3:00 PM on last NFO payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable during NFO period, subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Clients must initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Members must process transactions according to specified timelines to ensure unit allotment

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 08, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **December 11, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **December 12, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1 day, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation to avoid cancellation
- **T+5 or last NFO payout day, 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction deadline (whichever is earlier)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch that provides investors with a new ETF investment option tracking the NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 index.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for participating members - Members need to ensure compliance with fund settlement timelines and manage client transactions according to specified cut-off times. The NFO provides an additional investment product on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Investor Impact**: Positive - Investors gain access to a new passive investment vehicle tracking small-cap stocks through the NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 index, with both regular and direct plan options available.
