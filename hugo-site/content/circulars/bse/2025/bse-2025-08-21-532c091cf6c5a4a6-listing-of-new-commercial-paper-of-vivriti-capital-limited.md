## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Vivriti Capital Limited worth Rs. 10 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and will trade in dematerialized form with a redemption period of 3 months.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper worth Rs. 10 crore (200 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,88,848 per unit against face value of Rs. 5,00,000
- CRISIL A1+ credit rating assigned
- Securities traded only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE01HV14920

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Vivriti Capital Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
