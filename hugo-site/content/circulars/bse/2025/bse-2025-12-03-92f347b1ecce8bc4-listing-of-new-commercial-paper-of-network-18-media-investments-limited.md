## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new commercial papers on BSE's Debt segment effective December 3, 2025. The issue comprises 5000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, issued on private placement basis, with a 3-month tenor maturing on March 3, 2026. The securities carry ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Securities listed: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 5000 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Scrip Code: 730688
- Scrip ID: N18MI31225
- ISIN: INE870H14WH5
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,758.50 (discount to face value)
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading format: Dematerialized only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WH5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730688
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 3, 2025
- Listing Date: December 3, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 3, 2026
