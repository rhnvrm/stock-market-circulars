## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The listing consists of 4500 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 225 crores. The instruments carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt. Trading commenced on November 25, 2025, under scrip code 730647.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED
- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 4500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,606.50 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,393.50)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 225 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730647
- **Scrip ID**: IHF251125
- **ISIN**: INE071G14HG5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ (dual ratings)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI BANK LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued under existing framework for private placement of commercial papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HG5
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard BSE debt segment trading rules and procedures apply
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment exclusively

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Standard debt trading procedures apply. Trading members can access the instrument through BSE Debt segment with standard lot sizes.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and high net-worth investors access to short-term debt instrument from ICICI HOME FINANCE with top-tier credit ratings (A1+), offering relatively safe investment option for 91-day tenor.

**Liquidity**: The issue price of Rs. 4,92,606.50 against face value of Rs. 5,00,000 implies an approximate yield of 6.0% per annum for the 91-day tenure. Market lot of 1 unit allows flexible trading in Rs. 5 lakh increments.
