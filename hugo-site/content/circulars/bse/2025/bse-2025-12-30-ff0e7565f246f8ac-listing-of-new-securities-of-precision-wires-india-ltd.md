## Summary

BSE has listed 27,67,000 new equity shares of Precision Wires India Ltd (Scrip Code: 523539) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading from December 31, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until December 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 27,67,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 150/-
- Issue price: Rs. 151/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive Numbers: 180040976 to 182807975
- Date of allotment: October 20, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE372C01037

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until December 19, 2026
- All 27,67,000 shares are subject to the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 31, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: December 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard preferential allotment listing with limited dilution impact. The shares represent capital raised through warrant conversion to non-promoter investors.

**Shareholder Impact**: The allotment increases the equity base of Precision Wires India Ltd. The lock-in period of approximately 12 months ensures no immediate selling pressure from these allottees.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal impact on trading operations. The shares will be available for secondary market trading from December 31, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions.
