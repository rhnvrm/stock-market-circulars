## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544608, symbol EMMVEE, and ISIN number INE1C6T01020. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited
- Scrip Code: 544608
- Trading Symbol: EMMVEE
- ISIN Number: INE1C6T01020
- Listing status: Approved, pending final date announcement
- Sector: Photovoltaic/Renewable Energy

- **Trading Commencement**: To be announced with listing date
