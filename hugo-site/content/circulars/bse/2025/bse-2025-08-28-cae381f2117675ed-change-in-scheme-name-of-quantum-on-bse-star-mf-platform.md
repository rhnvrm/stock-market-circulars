## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the scheme name of QUANTUM Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Systematic Trading and Real-time) platform.

## Key Points

- Scheme name modification for QUANTUM on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Administrative update to mutual fund platform
- No impact on fund operations or investor holdings

## Regulatory Changes

None - this is an administrative update to platform naming conventions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the updated scheme name for trading purposes
- System updates may be required for platforms interfacing with BSE StAR MF

## Important Dates

Effective Date: As per BSE circular dated August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact - this is purely an administrative change affecting scheme naming on the BSE StAR MF platform. No changes to fund management, investment strategy, or investor holdings.
