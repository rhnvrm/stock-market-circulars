## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Jagsonpal Finance and Leasing Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Jagsonpal Services Limited with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC under CIN L65929DL1991PLC043182.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from "Jagsonpal Finance and Leasing Limited" to "Jagsonpal Services Limited"
- Certificate effective date: September 29, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L65929DL1991PLC043182
- Company originally incorporated as "Jagsonpal Finance and Leasing Private Limited"
- Company is limited by shares
- Registered office: Level 3B/DLF Centre, Connaught Place, Sansad Marg, New Delhi, Central Delhi-110001
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard administrative process under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29 for name change certification.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Mandatory Display Requirement**: The company must display its old name "Jagsonpal Finance and Leasing Limited" along with the new name "Jagsonpal Services Limited" for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 29, 2025 (digitally signed at 23:36:47 IST)
- **Display Period**: Old name must be displayed alongside new name until September 29, 2027 (two years from effective date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative corporate action that does not affect the company's business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Stakeholder Impact**: 
- Existing shareholders retain all rights and interests
- No impact on share certificates or holdings
- All legal rights and liabilities continue unaffected
- Stakeholders should note the name change for future correspondence and documentation

**Operational Impact**: 
- Company must update all stationery, signage, and official documents
- Two-year transition period allows gradual implementation
- No disruption to business operations expected

**Verification**: Stakeholders are advised to verify the company's current status and view public documents on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
