## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by ARKA FINCAP LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2000 units totaling Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730105, Scrip ID: ARFL26825
- ISIN Number: INE03W114716
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,73,490/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- IndusInd Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. Adds to BSE's debt segment offerings and provides short-term funding instrument for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED with 262-day maturity period.
