## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251104-5 announcing the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 730472, ISIN: INE01C314DC5) of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The suspension is effective from November 06, 2025, as the instrument is approaching its maturity/redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730472) will be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE01C314DC5
- Suspension effective date: November 06, 2025
- Reason: Instrument approaching maturity/redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-750/2025-2026
- Trading members must cease dealing in this instrument from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine operational notice for suspension of a maturing debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE01C314DC5) from November 06, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No new positions should be created in this instrument from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: Not explicitly specified (indicated as approaching)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine administrative action affecting a single commercial paper instrument approaching maturity. Such suspensions are standard practice in debt markets and do not indicate any credit issues or market disruptions.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects holders and potential traders of this specific ISIN. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this instrument.

**Investor Impact**: None for general investors. Holders of this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as per the instrument's terms.
