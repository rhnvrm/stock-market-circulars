## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD (Scrip Code 976843) effective December 08, 2025. The trading was previously suspended due to a put option exercise period with a record date of November 19, 2025 for redemption and interest payment. The company has confirmed that none of the debenture holders exercised the put option, allowing trading to resume.

## Key Points

- Trading in PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD debentures (Scrip Code 976843) will resume from December 08, 2025
- Previous suspension was related to put option exercise period for redemption
- Record date for redemption and interest payment was November 19, 2025
- No debenture holders exercised the put option
- Company requested resumption of trading after put option period concluded
- Reference: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine trading resumption notice following the conclusion of a corporate action period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to note the resumption of trading in these debentures
- Normal trading operations to resume from December 08, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Original notice issued regarding record date
- **November 19, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 05, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption
- **December 08, 2025**: Trading resumes in the debentures

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and limited to holders and traders of PRACHAY CAPITAL LTD debentures. Since no debenture holders exercised the put option, there is no redemption activity and normal trading will continue. This is a routine corporate action conclusion with no broader market implications. The resumption provides liquidity to existing debenture holders who may wish to trade these securities.
