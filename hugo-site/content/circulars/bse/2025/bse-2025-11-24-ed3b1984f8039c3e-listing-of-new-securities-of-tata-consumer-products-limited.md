## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading approval of 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Tata Consumer Products Limited (Scrip Code: 500800) with effect from Tuesday, November 25, 2025. These shares were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme and are now being listed with ISIN INE192A01025. The newly listed shares are pari passu (rank equally) with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Tata Consumer Products Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 500800
- **ISIN**: INE192A01025
- **Total Shares Listed**: 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Reason**: Shares kept in abeyance pursuant to Scheme
- **Trading Commencement**: November 25, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Distinctive Number Ranges**:
  - 185 shares: 632473163 to 632473347
  - 91 shares: 632706589 to 632706679
- **Status**: Pari passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a routine listing notification for shares previously kept in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the listing of these new securities and ensure systems are updated to reflect the additional shares available for trading from November 25, 2025.
- **Market Participants**: Should be aware that these 276 shares carry the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares (pari passu status).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Effective Date**: November 25, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Negligible. The listing of only 276 equity shares represents an extremely minimal addition to the outstanding share capital of Tata Consumer Products Limited, a large-cap company. This will have no material impact on liquidity, price discovery, or market capitalization.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new distinctive number ranges, but this is a routine administrative task with no operational complexity.

**Shareholder Impact**: None for existing shareholders. The newly listed shares are pari passu, maintaining equal rights across all equity shares.
