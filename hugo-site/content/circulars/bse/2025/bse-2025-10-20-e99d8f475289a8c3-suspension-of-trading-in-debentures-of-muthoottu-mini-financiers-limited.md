## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE101Q07AN4, Code: 975197) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is being implemented ahead of the record date set for October 26, 2025, for the purpose of debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: MMFL-10%-10-11-25-PVT (INE101Q07AN4)
- BSE scrip code: 975197
- Record date: October 26, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- Members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **October 26, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific debenture series (INE101Q07AN4) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited. This is a routine corporate action with minimal market-wide impact. Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the terms of the instrument. The two-day trading suspension ensures proper record-keeping for identifying eligible investors as of the record date.
