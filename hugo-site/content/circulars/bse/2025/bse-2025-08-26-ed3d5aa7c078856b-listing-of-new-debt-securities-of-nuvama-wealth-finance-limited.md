## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis effective August 26, 2025. The securities carry a 9% annual interest rate and are set to mature on July 22, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,030 per security
- Credit Rating: CARE AA
- Securities tradeable only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- Scrip Code: 977055
- Scrip ID: 9NWFL027
- ISIN: INE918K07PZ6
- Detail Name: NWFL-9%-22-7-27-PVT

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 22, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from August 24, 2026 to July 22, 2027
- Redemption Date: July 22, 2027

## Trading Information

- Trading begins: August 26, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07PZ6
- No put/call options available
- Placement Memorandum available on BSE website

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional investment option for debt market participants seeking AA-rated corporate bonds with 9% yield.
