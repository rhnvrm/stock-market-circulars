## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing consists of 9000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, effective from December 04, 2025. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Total quantity of Commercial Papers listed: 9000 units
- Face value per unit: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,749/-
- Scrip Code: 730698
- Scrip ID: IGHP41225
- ISIN Number: INE02FN14705
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14705
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: December 04, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 04, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 05, 2026
- Notice Date: December 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific debt instrument on private placement basis. The impact is limited to:

- Trading members operating in the BSE Debt segment who may trade these Commercial Papers
- Investors seeking short-term debt instruments with CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+ rating
- The securities have a 91-day maturity period (December 04, 2025 to March 05, 2026)

No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Contact debt department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
