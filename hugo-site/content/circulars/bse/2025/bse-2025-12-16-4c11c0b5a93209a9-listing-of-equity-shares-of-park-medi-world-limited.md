## Summary

PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a). The filing dated December 16, 2025 shows total equity capital of 431,930,864 shares with promoter and promoter group holding 82.89% (358,048,507 shares) and public holding 17.11% (73,882,357 shares). The company has 222,731 total shareholders including 3 promoters and 222,728 public shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 431,930,864 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 358,048,507 shares (82.89%)
- Public shareholding: 73,882,357 shares (17.11%)
- Total number of shareholders: 222,731 (3 promoters + 222,728 public)
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- 90.80% of total shares held in dematerialized form (392,177,777 shares)
- 100% of promoter shares (358,048,507) held in dematerialized form
- 46.19% of public shares (34,129,270) held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Listed entity confirmed no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or non-disposal undertakings
- No shares held by promoters are encumbered
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners identified

## Important Dates

- Filing date: December 16, 2025 (Post-Issue)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-listing compliance filing with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows:

- Concentrated promoter holding (82.89%) indicates strong promoter control
- Minimum public shareholding requirement of 25% not met, suggesting this may be initial post-IPO filing with promoter lock-in periods applicable
- Wide public shareholder base (222,728 shareholders) despite relatively low public float
- High dematerialization rate among promoters (100%) and moderate among public shareholders (46.19%)
- No encumbrances, pledges, or lock-ins disclosed, indicating clean shareholding structure
- Total of 358,048,507 promoter shares appear as locked-in shares based on the data, representing 100% of promoter holding
