## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Seshachal Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 531794) will be transferred to the 'X' group effective Wednesday, December 10, 2025. This change in grouping classification will affect the trading parameters and requirements for this security.

## Key Points

- Company: Seshachal Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 531794
- New Group: 'X' group
- Effective Date: December 10, 2025 (Wednesday)
- Notice Number: 20251126-1
- Notice Date: November 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Seshachal Technologies Limited will be moved from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically has specific trading restrictions and is used for securities under surveillance or with certain regulatory considerations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Be aware of the group change effective December 10, 2025
- Ensure trading in these shares complies with 'X' group trading rules and parameters
- Update their systems and client communications regarding the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 10, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group indicates enhanced surveillance or specific trading conditions for Seshachal Technologies Limited. This may result in:
- Modified trading parameters (tick size, price bands, etc.)
- Potential impact on liquidity
- Additional disclosure or compliance requirements for trades
- Investors and traders should review the specific trading rules applicable to 'X' group securities before transacting
