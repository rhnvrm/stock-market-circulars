## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Satin Creditcare Network Limited on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The issue comprises 500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore. The securities are rated ICRA A1+ and will be traded in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Satin Creditcare Network Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 500 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,57,146 per security
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 25 crore
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Scrip Code**: 730634
- **Scrip ID**: SCN211125
- **ISIN**: INE836B14333
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE836B14333
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment with the specified scrip code and parameters

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: November 20, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 365 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that expands short-term debt instruments available on BSE's debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these Commercial Papers in the debt segment. The securities follow standard trading norms with dematerialized trading requirements.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and eligible investors with an additional A1+ rated short-term debt investment option from Satin Creditcare Network Limited with approximately one-year maturity.
