## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction for Settlement No. 758 dated November 19, 2025. The auction covers 98 scrips across various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 500,000 shares. This auction is conducted to settle cases where sellers have failed to deliver securities in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 758
- Settlement Date: November 19, 2025
- Total number of scrips affected: 98
- Largest quantity: Spright Agro Limited (499,754 shares) and Reliance Power (200,000 shares)
- Smallest quantity: Multiple scrips with 1 share each
- Covers diverse sectors including power, infrastructure, banking, technology, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members must note the securities and quantities listed for auction
- Affected parties must participate in the demat auction process as per BSE regulations
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 758

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 98 different securities across multiple sectors. While this is a routine auction, the large quantities in certain scrips (particularly Spright Agro with ~500K shares and Reliance Power with 200K shares) may create temporary price pressure.

**Operational Impact**: Specific to members who have failed to deliver securities and must now participate in the auction process to close out their obligations. Other market participants are not directly affected.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders whose brokers failed to deliver may experience settlement delays. The auction mechanism ensures completion of trades but potentially at different prices.
