## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 10, 2025. The securities comprise 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.80% per annum with quarterly interest payments and will mature on December 6, 2027. The securities are rated CRISIL AA-/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977353, Scrip ID: 880IFSL27, ISIN: INE321N07590
- Total quantity: 10,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Allotment date: December 8, 2025
- Interest rate: 8.80% per annum (quarterly payments)
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Maturity date: December 6, 2027
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07590
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE's website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: March 9, 2026
- **Interest Payment Period**: Quarterly from March 9, 2026 to December 6, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: December 6, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of InCred Financial Services Limited. The impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants and institutional investors who may trade these securities
- Existing and potential investors in InCred Financial Services' debt instruments
- The securities provide an investment option rated CRISIL AA-/Stable with 8.80% annual return
- No impact on equity markets or broader market operations
- Routine addition to BSE's debt segment trading platform
