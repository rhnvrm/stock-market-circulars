## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ with a maturity period of 24 days.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 497,953.5 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730147, Scrip ID: BFSL3925
- ISIN: INE01C314CN4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 5, 2025
- Listing date: September 9, 2025
- Redemption date: September 29, 2025
- Maturity period: 24 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding requirements. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk, and the short maturity period limits exposure duration.
