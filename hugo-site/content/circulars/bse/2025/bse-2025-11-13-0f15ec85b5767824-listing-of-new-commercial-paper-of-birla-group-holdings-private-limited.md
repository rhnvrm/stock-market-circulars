## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. A total of 3000 commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been listed under scrip code 730543.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 3000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,754.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730543
- Scrip ID: BGH121125
- ISIN: INE09OL14IC0 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN number
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to Birla Group Holdings Private Limited with a tenor of approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the dematerialized trading requirement and minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.
