## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for CIN L82990MH1940PLC003151. The company name has been officially changed from "THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD" to "E & E ENTERPRISES LIMITED" with effect from October 16, 2025. The certificate was digitally signed by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L82990MH1940PLC003151
- Previous Name: THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD
- New Name: E & E ENTERPRISES LIMITED
- Effective Date: October 16, 2025
- Company Type: Limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Authorized by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered Address: 1st Floor, Piramal Tower, Peninsula Corporate Park, Ganpatrao Kadam Marg, Lower Parel, Mumbai-400013, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard company name change procedure conducted under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name and new name for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing obligations, contracts, and liabilities continue under the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 16, 2027**: End of mandatory dual-name display period (two years from effective date)
- **December 11, 2025**: BSE circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Stakeholders, including shareholders, creditors, and business partners, should update their records to reflect the new company name. All legal rights and obligations remain unchanged.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update its corporate stationery, signage, banking details, and all official documentation to reflect the new name while maintaining dual-name display for the mandatory two-year period.

**Trading Impact**: No impact on trading operations. The stock will continue to trade under updated identification with the new company name.
