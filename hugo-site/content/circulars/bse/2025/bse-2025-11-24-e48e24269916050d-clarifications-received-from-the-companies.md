## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three companies - BAMPSL Securities Limited, DCM Shriram Limited, and Motisons Jewellers Limited - regarding significant price and volume movements in their securities. All companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015, stated they are not aware of any unpublished price-sensitive information causing the movements, and attributed the fluctuations to market-driven factors.

## Key Points

- BAMPSL Securities Limited (22 Nov 2025): Responded to BSE query on significant price movement, confirmed compliance with disclosure obligations
- DCM Shriram Limited (Scrip Code: 523367, 24 Nov 2025): Responded to volume increase query, stated all requisite disclosures have been made
- Motisons Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 544053, 24 Nov 2025): Clarified significant volume increase across exchanges, attributed to market conditions
- All companies confirmed timely disclosure of material and price-sensitive information as per regulatory requirements
- No unpublished price-sensitive information identified by any company
- Companies remain committed to maintaining transparency and ensuring timely disclosures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular contains company responses to routine surveillance queries under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015, specifically Regulation 30 regarding disclosure of material events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond promptly to stock exchange queries regarding unusual price or volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed material or price-sensitive information exists
- Companies must reiterate their commitment to disclosure obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015
- Companies should provide explanations for market movements when possible

## Important Dates

- 21 November 2025: BSE sent query to BAMPSL Securities Limited
- 22 November 2025: BAMPSL Securities Limited submitted clarification
- 24 November 2025: DCM Shriram Limited submitted clarification (received BSE query at 12:45 PM)
- 24 November 2025: Motisons Jewellers Limited submitted clarification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications with no new material information disclosed. The companies' responses indicate normal market activity without any specific corporate events driving the price/volume movements.

**Investor Impact:** Low. No material events or price-sensitive information revealed. Investors can continue relying on previously disclosed information. The clarifications provide assurance that companies are compliant with disclosure norms.

**Operational Impact:** None. No operational changes or requirements for market participants. Standard surveillance and disclosure process functioning as designed to ensure market transparency.
