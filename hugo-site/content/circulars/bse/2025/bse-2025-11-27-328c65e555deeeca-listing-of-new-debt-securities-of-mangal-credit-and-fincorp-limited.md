## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Mangal Credit and Fincorp Limited on private placement basis, effective November 27, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976597
- Scrip ID: 1290MCFL27
- ISIN: INE545L07036 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,752.60
- Quantity: 1000 securities
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Interest Rate: 12.90% per annum
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE545L07036
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from January 3, 2026 to October 3, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 3, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing adds debt securities of Mangal Credit and Fincorp Limited to the BSE Debt segment for trading. The securities carry a CRISIL BBB/Stable rating and offer 12.90% annual interest with monthly payments. Impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in corporate debt securities.
