## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited (HLFL) issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 8% annual interest rate and mature on August 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest rate: 8% per annum paid annually
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA+/STABLE
- Scrip code: 977056, Scrip ID: 8HLFL28
- ISIN: INE146O07573
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 26, 2025
- Interest payment: Annually
- Maturity date: August 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. Provides additional investment option in the debt segment with AA+ rated paper from Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited.
