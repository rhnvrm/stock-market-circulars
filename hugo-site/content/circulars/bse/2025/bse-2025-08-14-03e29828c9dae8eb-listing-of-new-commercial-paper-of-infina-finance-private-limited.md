## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- 2000 commercial paper securities of Rs. 5 lakh each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Securities issued on private placement basis
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730018, ISIN: INE879F14LF7
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,966 per security
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Infina Finance Private Limited with high credit ratings indicating good creditworthiness.
