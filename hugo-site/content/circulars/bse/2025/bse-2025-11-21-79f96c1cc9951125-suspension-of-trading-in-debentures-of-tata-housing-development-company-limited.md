## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two non-convertible debenture series issued by TATA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in two debenture series: THDCL-8.27%-RESET RATE-14-8-27 (ISIN: INE582L08045) and THDCL-RESET RATE-28-10-27-PVT (ISIN: INE582L08060)
- Suspension effective from November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record date set as November 27, 2025
- Trading members prohibited from dealing in these securities from suspension date
- Notice number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 27, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to clients
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE582L08045 (Code: 975909) and ISIN INE582L08060 (Code: 976138) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 27, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects holders and potential traders of two specific debenture series of TATA Housing Development Company Limited. The suspension is temporary and related to a scheduled corporate action (redemption and interest payment).

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems prevent trades in these securities from November 27, 2025. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and interest as per the record date. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications beyond the specific securities involved.
