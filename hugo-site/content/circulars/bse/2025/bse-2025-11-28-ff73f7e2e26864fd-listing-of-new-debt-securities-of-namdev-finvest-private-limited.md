## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective November 28, 2025. The securities comprise 3,500 debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 11.30% p.a. interest rate with quarterly payments and maturing on September 30, 2028. The securities carry a CARE BBB+/Stable credit rating and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,500 debentures listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 977177, Scrip ID: 1130NFPL28
- ISIN: INE0IX207254 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,795.62
- Interest Rate: 11.30% per annum (quarterly payment)
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0IX207254
- Members should apply the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Members may refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Listing Date: November 28, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 30, 2025
- Interest Payment Schedule: Quarterly from December 30, 2025 to September 30, 2028
- Redemption Date: September 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with limited market impact. The listing provides NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED access to debt capital markets through private placement. The CARE BBB+/Stable rating indicates adequate safety with moderate credit risk. Impact is confined to trading members dealing in debt securities and investors in the debt segment. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
