## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on its Debt segment effective November 24, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total issuance of 500 instruments valued at Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crore). The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 18, 2026. Axis Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 500 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each (face value)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore
- Scrip Code: 730595
- Scrip ID: BGHP191125
- ISIN: INE09OL14ID8 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,845 per instrument
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14ID8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of commercial paper in the debt segment with limited broad market impact.

**Issuer**: Birla Group Holdings Private Limited adds short-term debt funding through highly-rated commercial paper.

**Investors**: Institutional investors gain access to A1+ rated short-term debt instruments with approximately 3-month tenure.

**Liquidity**: Trading restricted to Rs. 5 lakh denominations limits retail participation; primarily institutional market.
