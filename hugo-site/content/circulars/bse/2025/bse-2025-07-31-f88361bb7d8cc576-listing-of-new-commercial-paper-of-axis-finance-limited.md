## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Axis Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 5 lakh each (total 1000 units) are now available for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Scrip Code: 729947
- ISIN: INE891K14PG1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,364.50/-
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 30, 2025
- Trading Start Date: July 31, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
