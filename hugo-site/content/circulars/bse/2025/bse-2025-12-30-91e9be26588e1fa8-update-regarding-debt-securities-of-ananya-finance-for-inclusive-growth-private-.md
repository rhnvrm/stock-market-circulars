## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited. This circular continues from notice number 20251212-48 dated December 12, 2025. The debt securities under Scrip Code 976510 will be available for trading with revised terms effective December 31, 2025, involving a change in security mode from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs and a corresponding ISIN change.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976510 will have revised terms effective December 31, 2025
- Security mode changed from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs
- ISIN changed from INE774L08022 to INE774L07099
- Trading Members are requested to note these changes
- Contact person: Ms. Pooja Trivedi (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com)

## Regulatory Changes

**Mode of Security:**
- Existing Terms: Unsecured NCDs
- Revised Terms: Secured NCDs

**ISIN:**
- Existing ISIN: INE774L08022
- Revised ISIN: INE774L07099

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the revised security mode (Secured NCDs)
- Update ISIN from INE774L08022 to INE774L07099 for Scrip Code 976510
- Ensure proper handling of these securities from the effective date
- Contact BSE officials for clarifications if needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 31, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Previous Related Notice:** December 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20251212-48)

## Impact Assessment

This change enhances the security status of the NCDs from unsecured to secured, which may positively impact the credit quality and tradability of these instruments. Trading Members and investors holding these securities need to be aware of the ISIN change to ensure proper tracking and settlement. The conversion to secured status typically provides better protection to debenture holders in case of default or liquidation scenarios.
