## Summary

InterGlobe Aviation Limited (IndiGo) has clarified a news article published in Economic Times regarding a DGCA summons. The company confirmed that CEO Pieter Elbers and senior officials have been asked to participate in a review meeting with the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) on December 11, 2025. This clarification was provided in response to BSE's query about the news article titled "IndiGo Meltdown: India's aviation watchdog summons CEO Pieter Elbers, senior officials on Dec 11".

## Key Points

- CEO Pieter Elbers and senior officials requested to attend DGCA review meeting
- Meeting scheduled for December 11, 2025
- Clarification issued in response to BSE Surveillance query (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/RV/APJ/(2025-2026)/103)
- News article published in Economic Times on December 10, 2025
- Scrip Code: 539448

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a clarification regarding a regulatory review meeting.

## Compliance Requirements

Company officials required to participate in DGCA review meeting as requested by the aviation regulator.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: News article published in Economic Times
- **December 10, 2025**: BSE query issued to the company
- **December 11, 2025**: DGCA review meeting scheduled
- **December 11, 2025**: Company clarification issued

## Impact Assessment

The clarification confirms regulatory scrutiny of IndiGo's operations. While the meeting indicates DGCA's concern about operational issues, the immediate market impact is expected to be limited as this is a review meeting rather than enforcement action. The meeting may lead to operational changes or directives from DGCA depending on the outcome. Investors should monitor for any follow-up announcements regarding operational improvements or regulatory actions.
