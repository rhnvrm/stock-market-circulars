## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instruments consist of 2000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 100 crore), issued at discount price of Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with State Bank of India acting as Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit (discount pricing)
- **Total Quantity**: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore total)
- **Scrip Code**: 730666
- **Scrip ID**: SBIC271125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CT1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CT1
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a high-rated (A1+) short-term investment option from SBICAP Securities Limited with approximately 3-month maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these commercial papers on BSE's debt segment following standard protocols for dematerialized debt securities.
