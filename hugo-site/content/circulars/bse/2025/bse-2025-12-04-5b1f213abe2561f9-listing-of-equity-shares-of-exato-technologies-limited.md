## Summary

EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) following the listing of its equity shares on BSE. The total paid-up equity capital consists of 10,065,371 shares distributed between Promoter & Promoter Group (54.73%) and Public shareholders (45.27%). The company has not issued any partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, and has no locked-in shares or pledged promoter holdings.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 10,065,371 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 3 shareholders holding 5,508,924 shares (54.73%)
- Public shareholders: 736 shareholders holding 4,556,447 shares (45.27%)
- All promoter shares are in dematerialized form (100%)
- Public shareholding: 2,642,447 shares in demat form (58.00% of public holding)
- Overall dematerialization: 8,151,371 shares (80.98% of total)
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or otherwise encumbered
- No convertible securities, warrants, or depository receipts outstanding

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard disclosure requirement under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity has submitted the shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a)
- Declaration provided confirming no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares
- Shareholding pattern filed in the prescribed tabular format
- Disclosure includes voting rights distribution and dematerialization status

## Important Dates

- Filing date: December 4, 2025
- Report filed under: Regulation 31(1)(a)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a comfortable promoter holding above 50%, providing stability in management control. The public holding of 45.27% meets minimum public shareholding requirements. The relatively high dematerialization rate (80.98%) indicates good market liquidity. With 736 public shareholders, the stock shows reasonable distribution. The absence of pledged shares or encumbrances on promoter holdings is a positive indicator of financial health.
