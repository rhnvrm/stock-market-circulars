## Summary

JSW Energy Limited has listed new debt securities of Rs. 25 crore (25,000 securities of Rs. 1 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with an allotment date of November 12, 2025, carrying an interest rate of 8.05% per annum and maturing on November 10, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 25,000 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 977291
- **Scrip ID**: 805JSWEL28
- **ISIN**: INE121E08070
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-/STABLE, IND AA-/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 8.05% per annum (paid annually)
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE121E08070
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: November 10, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The private placement by JSW Energy Limited adds to the company's debt capital base and provides investors with rated debt instruments (ICRA AA-/STABLE). The listing enhances market depth in corporate debt segment but has limited broader market implications. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details.
