## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of RCRS Innovation Limited filed on December 10, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days for stakeholders to submit their feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by RCRS Innovation Limited on December 10, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide feedback on the Draft Offer Document must submit comments within 21 days from December 11, 2025
- Comments should be emailed to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- No mandatory compliance action required from existing market participants

## Important Dates

- **DRHP Filing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 11, 2025
- **Comment Deadline**: January 1, 2026 (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in RCRS Innovation Limited's upcoming public offering. There is no immediate market impact or compliance burden on existing listed entities or trading members. The notice facilitates transparency and public participation in the IPO approval process as mandated by SEBI's ICDR Regulations.
