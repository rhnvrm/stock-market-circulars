## Summary

Hindustan Construction Co. Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500185) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with December 5, 2025 as the record date. Eligible shareholders will be offered 277 equity shares of Re.1/- each for every 630 equity shares held, at an issue price of Rs.12.50 per share (including a premium of Rs.11.50). Trading on ex-rights basis will commence from December 5, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-771/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: Hindustan Construction Co. Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500185)
- Rights ratio: 277 equity shares for every 630 equity shares held
- Face value: Re.1/- per share
- Premium: Rs.11.50 per share
- Issue price: Rs.12.50 per share (payable on application)
- Ex-rights trading begins: December 5, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-771/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in the equity shares of Hindustan Construction Co. Ltd. on ex-rights basis from December 5, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ensure proper settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-771/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders holding shares as of the record date will be eligible to subscribe to the rights issue at the ratio of 277:630. Non-participation will result in dilution of their shareholding percentage.

**Market Impact**: The stock will trade ex-rights from December 5, 2025, which typically results in a price adjustment reflecting the value of the rights entitlement. The issue price of Rs.12.50 represents the pricing at which existing shareholders can acquire additional shares.

**Company Impact**: This rights issue represents a capital raising exercise for Hindustan Construction Co. Ltd., which will increase the company's equity capital base upon successful subscription and allotment.
