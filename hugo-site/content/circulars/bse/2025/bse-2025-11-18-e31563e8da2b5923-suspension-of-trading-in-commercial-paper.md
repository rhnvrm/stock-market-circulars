## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of 21 companies effective from their respective record dates between November 20-25, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities as the companies have fixed record dates for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- 21 companies have commercial paper redemptions scheduled between November 20-25, 2025
- Trading suspension commences from the respective record dates
- Earliest suspension: Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (November 20, 2025)
- Majority of suspensions effective November 24, 2025 (19 companies)
- Latest suspension: Two companies on November 25, 2025
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension for commercial paper redemptions as per existing BSE regulations on corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective suspension dates
- Members must comply with the no-dealings directive starting from the record dates mentioned
- Standard redemption procedures to be followed by respective issuers

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants dealing in commercial paper of major financial institutions and corporations. Temporary liquidity impact on these specific securities.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must adjust their portfolios and avoid transactions in the specified instruments during suspension period. No impact on underlying equity shares of these companies.

**Sectoral Impact:** Affects multiple sectors including financial services, retail, housing finance, cement, and industrial finance. Concentration of 19 suspensions on November 24, 2025 may impact short-term debt market activity on that date.
