## Summary

BSE has issued a circular suspending trading in a Treasury Bill (T-bill) that is scheduled to mature on December 15, 2025. The suspension becomes effective from December 11, 2025, and trading members are advised not to deal in the specified T-bill from this date onwards. This is a standard procedure for securities approaching their redemption date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with ISIN IN001225C050 (Scrip Code: 800353) designated as GS15DEC25C will mature on December 15, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from December 11, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified T-bill after the suspension date
- Circular reference: DR-775/2025-2026
- Notice applies to the Debt segment of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified T-bill (Scrip Code: 800353, ISIN: IN001225C050) from December 11, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members must ensure no new positions are created in this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 11, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 15, 2025**: T-bill redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting a single maturing T-bill. The suspension period is only 4 days before redemption, which is standard practice.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members holding positions in this T-bill should already be aware of the maturity date and have planned accordingly. The suspension prevents any last-minute trading activity before redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific T-bill who will receive redemption proceeds on December 15, 2025 as scheduled.
