## Summary

KSH International Limited (formerly KSH International Private Limited) has completed anchor investor allocation for its initial public offering (IPO). The IPO Committee, in consultation with book running lead managers Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and ICICI Securities Limited, allocated 55,46,874 equity shares of face value ₹5 each at an anchor investor allocation price of ₹384 per share (including share premium of ₹379 per share). The total amount raised from anchor investors is ₹212,99,99,616 (approximately ₹213 crores).

## Key Points

- **Total Equity Shares Allocated**: 55,46,874 shares
- **Anchor Investor Allocation Price**: ₹384 per equity share (face value ₹5 + premium ₹379)
- **Total Amount Raised**: ₹212.99 crores
- **Domestic Mutual Fund Allocation**: 41,40,591 shares (74.65% of total anchor allocation)
- **Number of Anchor Investors**: 13 investors
- **Number of Mutual Fund Schemes**: 8 schemes from 5 domestic mutual funds
- **Book Running Lead Managers**: Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and ICICI Securities Limited
- **Company CIN**: U28129PN1979PLC141032
