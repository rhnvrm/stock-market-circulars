## Summary

Bajaj Housing Finance Limited has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 500 crore (50,000 bonds of Rs. 10 lakh face value each) on the BSE Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with CRISIL AAA/Stable rating, carrying 7.10% annual interest and maturing on October 16, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 bonds with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977122 | Scrip ID: 71BHFL28 | ISIN: INE377Y07607 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per bond
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,352 (premium of Rs. 352 per bond)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 7.10% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard debt securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE377Y07607
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for detailed terms
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on October 16 from 2026 to 2028
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 16, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine private placement debt listing for institutional investors. The securities provide Bajaj Housing Finance Limited with medium-term funding at 7.10% interest rate. The CRISIL AAA rating indicates high credit quality. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. The listing enhances the company's debt capital market presence and provides liquidity options for investors in these securities.
