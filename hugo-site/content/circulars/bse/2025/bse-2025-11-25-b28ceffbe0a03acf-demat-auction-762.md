## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 762 dated November 25, 2025. The circular lists 117+ securities across various sectors that are subject to demat auction due to delivery shortages. Quantities range from 1 share to 999,999 shares depending on the scrip. This is a routine settlement mechanism to ensure completion of trades where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 762
- Settlement date: November 25, 2025
- Total scrips affected: 117+ securities
- Highest quantity: GV Films Ltd (700,000 shares) and Murae Organics (999,999 shares)
- Includes major stocks like Infosys (36 shares), Tata Steel (20 shares), Adani Power (100 shares), Adani Green (1 share)
- Also includes recent listings like Swiggy (2 shares), Waaree Energy (393 shares), Paytm/One 97 Communications (10 shares)
- Auction covers both large-cap and small-cap securities
- Quantities vary significantly across scrips indicating varying levels of delivery shortfalls

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular implementing existing auction settlement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members who have delivery shortages in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction or arrange securities
- Members must ensure availability of securities for auction settlement
- Successful auction participants must deliver securities as per settlement obligations
- Members failing to deliver may face buy-in consequences and penalties as per existing regulations

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 762
- Auction execution date: To be conducted as per standard BSE auction settlement timeline (typically within 2-3 trading days of circular issuance)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 117+ securities across market capitalizations. While most quantities are small (1-100 shares), some scrips have significant quantities (GV Films: 700,000, Murae Organics: 999,999, Epuja Spirits: 8,215, Orient Cement: 7,280).

**Trading Impact**: Securities under auction may experience price volatility during auction period. Members with short positions must arrange securities or face buy-in at potentially higher prices.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal direct impact on retail investors. This is a settlement mechanism between trading members to complete pending delivery obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members with delivery obligations in these scrips must ensure participation in auction process and arrange requisite securities to avoid penalties and financial consequences.
