## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 24,36,51,025 new equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 539216) effective from December 31, 2025. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Trading permitted from December 31, 2025
- Total new securities: 24,36,51,025 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Conversion from partly paid to fully paid shares
- Issued on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 539216
- ISIN: INE653S01028
- Distinctive Numbers: Two ranges provided (15347011-216115111 and 216156062-444460075)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to commence trading in these new securities from the effective date. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific event with minimal broader market impact. The listing enables trading in additional equity shares resulting from rights issue conversion, increasing the tradeable float of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and investors interested in this specific scrip.
