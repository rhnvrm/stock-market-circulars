## Summary

Tata Motors Limited (formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing shows the distribution of equity shares between promoter group and public shareholders, with total paid-up equity capital of 3,682,331,373 shares.

## Key Points

- Company name: Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Total number of shareholders: 6,647,799
- Total equity shares: 3,682,331,373 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 1,567,369,812 shares (42.56%)
- Public shareholding: 2,114,961,561 shares (57.44%)
- Number of promoter group shareholders: 10
- Company Secretary: Sudipto Kumar Das
- Outstanding ESOPs exist with 2,666,563 shares underlying convertible securities
- Total diluted share capital: 3,684,997,936 shares (including ESOPs)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard compliance filing under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 for shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration required on various aspects including partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, and significant beneficial owners
- Tata Sons Private Limited (TSPL), being a core investment company registered with RBI, is exempted under Significant Beneficial Ownership (SBO) Rules
- No other SBO exists under SBO Rules to be reported

## Important Dates

- Filing date: November 10, 2025
- No specific deadlines or effective dates mentioned in the circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This shareholding pattern filing provides transparency on ownership structure of Tata Motors Limited post-listing. The promoter holding at 42.56% indicates strong promoter commitment while maintaining adequate public float of 57.44%.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Routine compliance disclosure with no operational changes. The existence of outstanding ESOPs (2,666,563 shares) represents less than 0.1% potential dilution on a fully diluted basis.

**Key Observations:**
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities (except ESOPs), warrants, or depository receipts outstanding
- No shares in locked-in, pledged, or under non-disposal undertaking
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- Clean shareholding structure with standard promoter-public distribution
