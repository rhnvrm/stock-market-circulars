## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544641, symbol KVTOYS, and ISIN INE1IE301018. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544641
- Trading Symbol: KVTOYS
- ISIN: INE1IE301018
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice Number: 20251212-7

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new listing
- Members should await separate notice for exact listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Limited impact as this is a routine SME segment listing. The announcement provides preliminary information only, with actual trading to commence on a date specified in a future circular. Trading members need to update their systems with the scrip details once the listing date is confirmed.
