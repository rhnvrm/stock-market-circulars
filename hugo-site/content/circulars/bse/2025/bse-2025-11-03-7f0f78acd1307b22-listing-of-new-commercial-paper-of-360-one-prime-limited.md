## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 500 crores on the BSE Debt segment effective November 3, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling 1,000 units. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will mature on June 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 500 crores (1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,78,000 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Allotment date: October 31, 2025
- Maturity date: June 2, 2026
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730460
- ISIN: INE248U14SM5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions occur only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U14SM5
- Trades must be executed in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** October 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for 360 ONE Prime Limited and offers debt market participants an investment option with high credit ratings (A1+). The issue adds to the available commercial paper inventory on BSE's debt segment but does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
