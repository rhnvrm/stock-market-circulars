## Summary

B. D. Industries (Pune) Limited has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern as of August 5, 2025, following the listing of its equity shares. The company has 14,210,000 total equity shares with promoters holding 70.44% and public shareholders holding 29.56%.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 14,210,000 shares
- Promoter & promoter group holding: 10,010,000 shares (70.44%)
- Public shareholding: 4,200,000 shares (29.56%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 7
- Number of public shareholders: 690
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- Shares held in dematerialized form: 11,205,200 (78.85%)

## Regulatory Changes

None specified in this disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing under Regulation 31(1)(b) of SEBI regulations
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or differential voting rights
- Company has confirmed it has shares in locked-in status

## Important Dates

- Report date: August 5, 2025
- Post-issue filing date: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard post-listing compliance filing showing the shareholding structure of the newly listed company. The promoter holding of 70.44% is within regulatory limits, and the public shareholding of 29.56% meets minimum public shareholding requirements for listing.
