## Summary

BAJAJ FINSERV MUTUAL FUND is launching BAJAJ FINSERV BANKING AND FINANCIAL SERVICES FUND - NFO (New Fund Offer) on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from November 10, 2025 to November 24, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 01, 2025. Six scheme variants are available covering Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: November 10, 2025 to November 24, 2025
- Tentative Allotment Date: December 01, 2025
- Scheme Category: Equity Fund (Banking and Financial Services sector)
- 6 scheme options available: 3 Regular Plans and 3 Direct Plans
- Extended trading till midnight on November 24, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 01, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to receipt of clear funds by December 01, 2025
- Switches accepted during NFO period if funds received before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch following existing mutual fund regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Members/Brokers:**
- Ensure client-initiated funds are received by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Report CC with N flag to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Ensure clear funds availability in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 01, 2025
- Process SIP/XSIP registration authentication before 3:00 PM on November 24, 2025
- Pending transactions will be maintained till 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- For switches: Ensure funds for switch out scheme are received before December 01, 2025 for allotment consideration

**For Clients/Investors:**
- Initiate payment by 9:00 AM on T+1 to avoid order cancellation
- Complete SIP/XSIP authentication before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Ensure cheque/OTM payments result in clear funds by December 01, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: NFO Opening Date
- **November 24, 2025**: NFO Closing Date (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **November 24, 2025**: Extended trading till midnight for lumpsum purchases only
- **December 01, 2025**: Tentative Allotment Date
- **December 01, 2025**: Clear funds deadline in ICCL Account (6:00 PM)
- **T+1 Day**: Fund initiation deadline (9:00 AM) for order validation
- **T+5 or Last NFO Payout Day**: Pending transaction expiry (whichever is earlier, 3:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine NFO launch with no impact on secondary market trading or existing securities.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for interested mutual fund investors. Provides new investment opportunity in banking and financial services sector through equity fund. Investors have 15-day window to subscribe to the NFO.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Standard operational procedures for members and brokers on BSE StAR MF Platform. Established processes for NFO handling, fund collection, and order management apply.

**Sector Focus:** The fund focuses on banking and financial services sector, providing sectoral investment exposure for investors seeking concentrated exposure to this segment.
