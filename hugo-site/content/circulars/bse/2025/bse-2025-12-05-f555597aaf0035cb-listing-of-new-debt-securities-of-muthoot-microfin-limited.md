## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by Muthoot Microfin Limited on private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The securities consist of 75,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying a 9.70% annual interest rate and maturing on December 3, 2027. The securities are rated CRISIL A+/Positive and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 75,000 debt securities
- Scrip Code: 977342 | Scrip ID: 970MML27
- ISIN: INE046W07339
- Face Value & Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 9.70% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A+/Positive
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2027
- No Put/Call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities exclusively in dematerialized form under ISIN INE046W07339
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from January 1, 2026 to December 3, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings and provides institutional investors access to Muthoot Microfin Limited's debt instruments. The CRISIL A+/Positive rating indicates strong credit quality. There is no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
