## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using both BSE BOLT TWS and third-party trading platforms to test various trading functionalities including exceptional market conditions. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this session. Members are requested to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the mock trading day.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 08, 2025
- Available for all trading members including those using third-party platforms
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Testing covers various functionalities: call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals, and exceptional market conditions
- Comprehensive trading schedule from 11:15 AM login to 4:20 PM post-closing
- Feedback mandatory by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

**Login:** 11:15 AM - 11:45 AM

**Morning Block Deal Window:** 11:45 AM - 12:00 PM

**Pre-Open:**
- Order Entry period: 12:00 PM - 12:08 PM (random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- Matching period: 12:08 PM - 12:15 PM

**Continuous Trading T+1:** 12:15 PM - 4:00 PM

**Continuous Trading T+0:** 12:15 PM - 2:30 PM

**Special Pre-Open for IPO & Re-listed Scrips:**
- Order Entry period: 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM (random stoppage in last 10 minutes)
- Matching period: 12:45 PM - 1:00 PM

**Continuous Trading for SPOS Scrips:** 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

**Periodic Call Auction:** 12:30 PM - 3:30 PM (3 sessions of 1 hour each)

**Auction for Settlement Details:**
- Offer Entry and Matching period: 1:00 PM - 1:45 PM

**Afternoon Block Deal Window:** 1:50 PM - 2:05 PM

**Closing:** 4:00 PM - 4:10 PM

**Post-closing:** 4:10 PM - 4:20 PM

**Trade Modification T+1:** Until 4:20 PM

**Trade Modification T+0:** Until 2:45 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled mock trading session for testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Third-party trading platform users should utilize this opportunity to test their trading applications
- Members should test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025 (Saturday):** Mock trading session
- **November 08, 2025 by 5:00 PM:** Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Voluntary participation for testing and validation purposes.
