## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Navi Finserv Limited worth Rs. 250 crores issued on private placement basis. The securities carry CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will be traded in dematerialized form with effect from September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 500 units (Rs. 250 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 476,039.50 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730124, ISIN: INE342T14EB1
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption date: February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
