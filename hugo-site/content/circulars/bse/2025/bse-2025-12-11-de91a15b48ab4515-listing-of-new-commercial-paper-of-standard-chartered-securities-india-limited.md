## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis. The listing is effective from December 11, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment. A total of 1,300 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been admitted for trading.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited
- **Quantity**: 1,300 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,665 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730250
- **Scrip ID**: SCSI16925
- **Detail Name**: SCSIL-11-3-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE472H14730 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading under ISIN Number INE472H14730

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers have a short tenure of approximately 3 months and are rated ICRA A1+, indicating highest credit quality. Trading is limited to the debt segment with standard denomination requirements. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
