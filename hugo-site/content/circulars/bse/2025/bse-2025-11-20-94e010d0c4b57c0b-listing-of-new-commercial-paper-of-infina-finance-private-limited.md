## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. A total of 3500 securities with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 20, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 3500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,168.50
- Scrip Code: 730604
- Scrip ID: IFPL191125
- ISIN: INE879F14LZ5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14LZ5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 20, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: November 19, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 20, 2025
- Date of Redemption: May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine private placement listing of commercial paper in the debt segment. The listing provides Infina Finance Private Limited with short-term funding (approximately 6 months maturity) and offers institutional investors an additional A1+ rated debt instrument. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
