## Summary

BSE has listed 25,000 new equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) issued at a premium of Rs. 415/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, October 13, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until April 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 25,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 415/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 417.00/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 169987511 to 170012510
- Date of Allotment: September 5, 2025
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Scrip Code: 500463

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for additional securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in period applies to all 25,000 shares until April 25, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 13, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The issuance represents a small number of shares (25,000) issued through warrant conversion to non-promoters. The lock-in period until April 25, 2026 ensures these shares will not immediately impact trading liquidity. This is a routine corporate action with no significant implications for existing shareholders or market operations.
