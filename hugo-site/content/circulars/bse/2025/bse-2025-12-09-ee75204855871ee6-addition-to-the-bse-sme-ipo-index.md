## Summary

HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544630) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, December 10, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Tuesday, December 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED with exchange ticker 544630 is being listed on BSE on December 9, 2025
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index on December 10, 2025
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to prior Notice No. 20251208-28

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants should note the index addition for portfolio tracking and rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED listing on BSE
- **December 10, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index (effective at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index inclusion for a newly listed SME company. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their holdings. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
