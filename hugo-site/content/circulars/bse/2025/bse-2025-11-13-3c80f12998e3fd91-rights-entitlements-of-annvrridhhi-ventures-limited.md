## Summary

BSE has authorized trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LIMITED. REs will be tradable from November 17-20, 2025 on a T+1 rolling settlement basis, trade-for-trade. The underlying Rights Issue opens November 17-26, 2025. REs are tradable only in dematerialized form with ISIN INE075K20021.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading permitted from Monday, November 17, 2025 to Thursday, November 20, 2025
- Scrip Code: 751056, Scrip ID: ANVRDHI-RE, ISIN: INE075K20021
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Settlement basis: T+1 Rolling Settlement, trade-for-trade
- REs tradable in dematerialized form only
- Rights Issue opens November 17, 2025 and closes November 26, 2025
- Trading members must inform clients that REs are not ordinary shares
- Contract notes must include disclaimer about REs
- Unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification for rights entitlements trading under existing BSE framework for rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of REs trading and mention the ISIN (INE075K20021)
- Trading members must clarify that REs shall not be mistaken for ordinary shares
- Contract notes/bills containing purchase of REs must prominently include disclaimer stating:
  - Purchase of REs only gives right to participate in ongoing Rights Issue by making application with requisite money OR renounce the REs before issue closes
  - REs neither subscribed nor renounced on or before Issue Closing Date shall lapse and be extinguished
- Trading members should refer to notice 20200305-21 (March 05, 2020) and notice 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) for additional details

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 17, 2025**: REs trading period begins; Rights Issue opens
- **November 20, 2025**: REs trading period ends
- **November 26, 2025**: Rights Issue closes; last date to subscribe or renounce REs

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects existing shareholders of ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LIMITED who hold rights entitlements. Creates short-term trading opportunity for those wishing to renounce their rights.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - trading members must ensure proper client communication, contract note disclaimers, and settlement processes for T+1 trade-for-trade settlement. Only impacts traders dealing with this specific security.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders must decide by November 26, 2025 whether to subscribe to rights issue, renounce REs (tradable November 17-20), or allow REs to lapse. Unexercised REs will be extinguished after closing date.
