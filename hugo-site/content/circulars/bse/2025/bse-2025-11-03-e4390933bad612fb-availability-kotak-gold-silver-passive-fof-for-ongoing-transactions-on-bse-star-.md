## Summary

BSE announces that KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 04, 2025. This follows previous notice 20251003-38 and enables investors to resume subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) for both Regular and Direct plan variants of the equity fund.

## Key Points

- KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from November 04, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XU2) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174KA1XT4)
- Fund category: Equity (Fund of Funds)
- Facilities enabled: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- References previous notice 20251003-38

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about mutual fund availability on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 04, 2025 - KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice affects only investors and distributors interested in the KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF product. The reopening provides investment access to a gold-silver focused fund of funds, allowing retail and institutional investors to resume systematic investment plans and execute transactions through the BSE mutual fund platform. No impact on equity markets or listed securities.
