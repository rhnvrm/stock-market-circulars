## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis, effective December 19, 2025. The issuance consists of 34,000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings. The instruments were allotted on December 18, 2025 and will mature on March 18, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730814
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL181225
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EM3
- **Quantity**: 34,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,531 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EM3
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members must adhere to the tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing for a major corporate entity for working capital/treasury management purposes.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Commercial Paper is typically subscribed by institutional investors and sophisticated market participants. The 90-day maturity period (December 18, 2025 to March 18, 2026) indicates this is a standard short-term funding instrument.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. Only debt segment trading members need to be aware of the new listing and trading parameters.
