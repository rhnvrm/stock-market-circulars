## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the official change in name of Indiabulls Infraestate Ltd. The notification is based on the certificate received from the Registrar of Companies and is in accordance with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs records. The document has been digitally signed by MANISH RIYAL on July 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Official name change notification for Indiabulls Infraestate Ltd
- Certificate received from Registrar of Companies
- Notification issued under Ministry of Corporate Affairs authority
- Document digitally signed and authenticated
- Effective date documented as per MCA records

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a corporate name change as per Companies Act requirements and MCA filing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their records with the new company name
- Trading systems and databases should reflect the updated company name
- All future correspondence and filings should reference the new company name
- Stakeholders should note the change for documentation purposes

## Important Dates

- Document signing date: July 4, 2025
- Circular issue date: December 16, 2025
- Effective date: As per MCA certificate

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative corporate action involving a name change. There is no impact on the company's operations, shareholding structure, or trading arrangements. The change is purely nomenclature-related and requires only record-keeping updates by market participants and stakeholders. No trading disruption or special settlement procedures are required.
