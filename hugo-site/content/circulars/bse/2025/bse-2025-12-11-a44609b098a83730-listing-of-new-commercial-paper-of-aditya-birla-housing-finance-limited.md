## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instruments, totaling 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (Rs. 500 crore aggregate), are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The Commercial Paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and matures on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 10,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit
- **Aggregate Issue Size**: Rs. 500 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730747
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF101225
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FQ2
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FQ2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard market lot of 1 unit applies

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt market participants an additional investment option in housing finance sector paper with high credit ratings (A1+). The 91-day Commercial Paper offers institutional investors a liquid money market instrument. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader regulatory framework.
