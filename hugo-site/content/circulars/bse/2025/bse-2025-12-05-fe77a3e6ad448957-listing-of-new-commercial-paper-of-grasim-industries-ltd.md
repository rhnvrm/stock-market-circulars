## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on a private placement basis effective December 5, 2025. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh per unit with 10,000 units issued, maturing on March 5, 2026. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 5,000 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,710.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730710
- Scrip ID: GIL051225
- ISIN: INE047A14AP4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE047A14AP4
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members must use the specified scrip code 730710 for transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 5, 2025
- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 5, 2026
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper listing provides short-term funding to Grasim Industries Ltd and offers institutional investors a highly-rated short-term investment option. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities may utilize this instrument for their clients' portfolios.
