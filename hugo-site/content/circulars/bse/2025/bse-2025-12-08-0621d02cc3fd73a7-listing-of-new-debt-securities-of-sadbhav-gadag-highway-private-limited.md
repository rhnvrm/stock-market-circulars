## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Sadbhav Gadag Highway Private Limited on the BSE Debt segment effective December 8, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit, carrying a 20% annual interest rate and maturing on October 30, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity of securities listed: 13,500 units
- Scrip Code: 977348
- Scrip ID: 20SGHP28
- ISIN Number: INE04AP07032
- Credit Rating: ACUITE BB+
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 20% per annum
- Securities tradable only in dematerialized form
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE04AP07032
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Half-yearly from March 31, 2026 to October 30, 2028
- **First Interest Payment**: March 31, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing is specific to Sadbhav Gadag Highway Private Limited's debt securities and affects only trading members dealing with debt instruments. The BB+ credit rating indicates below investment grade, suggesting higher risk. The 20% interest rate reflects the credit risk profile of the issuer. No put/call options are available on these securities.
