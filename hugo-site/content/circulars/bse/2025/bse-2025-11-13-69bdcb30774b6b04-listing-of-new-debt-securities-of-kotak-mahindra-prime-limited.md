## Summary

BSE has listed three series of new debt securities issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 13, 2025. The total issuance comprises 89,500 units across three tranches with varying interest rates (7.09%, 7.288%, and 7.83% p.a.) and maturities in 2028. All securities carry AAA credit ratings from CRISIL and/or ICRA, indicating highest credit quality. The securities were allotted on November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three series of debt securities totaling 89,500 units listed on BSE Debt segment
- All securities rated AAA/STABLE by CRISIL and/or ICRA
- Face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit across all series
- Interest payment frequency: Annually for all three series
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit for all series

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities that adds to the debt segment inventory. The securities are restricted to institutional investors given the high face value of Rs. 1 lakh per unit.

**Issuer Profile**: Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, a subsidiary of Kotak Mahindra Bank, continues to access debt markets for funding. The AAA ratings reflect strong creditworthiness.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with additional high-quality debt investment options with yields ranging from 7.09% to 7.83% p.a. The issue prices above par for Series 2 and 3 indicate these are further issuances under existing series, priced to reflect prevailing market conditions.
