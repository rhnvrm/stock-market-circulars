## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 3,22,00,434 new equity shares of Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894) issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on December 22, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,22,00,434 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 15.35/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 5.35/- premium)
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE227G01018
- Distinctive Numbers: 128801737 to 161002170
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes from December 22, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event specific to Indowind Energy Ltd shareholders who participated in the rights issue. The new shares increase the company's equity capital base by approximately 3.22 crore shares. Market impact is limited to this specific scrip with no broader market implications.
