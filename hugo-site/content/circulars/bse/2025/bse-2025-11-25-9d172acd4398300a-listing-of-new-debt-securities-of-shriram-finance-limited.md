## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Shriram Finance Limited on its Debt segment with effect from November 25, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 9.15% per annum and maturing on January 19, 2029.

## Key Points

- Total quantity listed: 25,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 975339, Scrip ID: 915SFL29
- ISIN: INE721A07RY4 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,12,171.90
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+/Stable, IND AA+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.15% per annum payable annually
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE721A07RY4
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from January 19, 2025 to January 19, 2029
- **Redemption Date**: January 19, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds liquidity to Shriram Finance Limited's debt instruments on the BSE platform. The securities carry a strong credit rating (AA+) and offer a 9.15% annual return. There are no put/call options attached. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in corporate debt instruments.
