## Summary

BSE has announced the application of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective December 04, 2025. Three new securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the ST-ASM framework under Stage I, while two securities are moving out of the framework. The circular provides consolidated lists of all securities currently under the ST-ASM framework with their respective stages.

## Key Points

- 3 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework (all SME scrips)
- 2 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stages within the framework
- Changes effective from December 04, 2025
- Consolidated list includes 21+ securities currently under ST-ASM Stage I
- Framework includes SME scrips (marked @) and T+0 scrips (marked ~)

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

**New Inclusions (Stage I):**
1. Infinity Infoway Ltd (Scrip Code: 544567, ISIN: INE16XA01017) - SME
2. Rapid Multimodal logistics Ltd (Scrip Code: 544237, ISIN: INE0QA901010) - SME
3. Workmates Core2Cloud Solution Ltd (Scrip Code: 544610, ISIN: INE1I1301016) - SME

## Securities Moving Out of ST-ASM Framework

1. Mayur Leather Products Ltd (Scrip Code: 531680, ISIN: INE799E01011)
2. Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 511557, ISIN: INE732K01027) - Moving to ESM Framework

## Regulatory Changes

The Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework continues to operate with 5/15/30 days monitoring periods across different stages. Securities under this framework are subject to enhanced surveillance and may have specific trading conditions applied.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must be aware of securities under ST-ASM framework
- Enhanced monitoring required for transactions in these securities
- Specific margin and settlement requirements may apply
- Members should update their risk management systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 04, 2025
- Changes to ST-ASM framework applicability become effective from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects trading conditions for specific securities, primarily SME segment stocks. The addition of three SME securities and removal of two securities indicates ongoing surveillance activity.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or trading in affected securities should be aware of enhanced surveillance measures which may include additional margins, price bands, or trading restrictions.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update systems and ensure compliance with ST-ASM framework requirements for the newly included securities and remove restrictions for exiting securities.

## Additional Notes

- Securities marked with specific symbols indicate different frameworks:
  - @ = SME Scrips
  - ~ = T+0 Scrips (as per parent company)
  - $ = Moving to ESM Framework
  - \* = Moving to LT-ASM Framework
  - ** = Moving to Trade for Trade Framework
  - # = Moving to GSM Framework
  - ^ = Moving to Pledge Framework
