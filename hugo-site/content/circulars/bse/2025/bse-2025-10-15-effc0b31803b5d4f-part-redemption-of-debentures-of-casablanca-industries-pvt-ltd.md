## Summary

BSE has announced the partial redemption of debentures issued by CASABLANCA INDUSTRIES PVT LTD (Scrip Code: 976186, ISIN: INE834U07019). Following this partial redemption, the face value per debenture has been reduced to Rs. 94,500. Trading members are required to note this change and ensure trades reflect the revised face value.

- Company: CASABLANCA INDUSTRIES PVT LTD
- Debenture ISIN: INE834U07019
- Scrip Code: 976186 (CIPL-10%-22-11-30-PVT)
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 94,500 per debenture (post partial redemption)
- Circular Reference: DR-737/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving partial redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value of Rs. 94,500 per debenture
- All trades in these debentures must reflect the new face value
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the revised debenture parameters

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 16, 2025 - Trading begins with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting a single private placement debenture issue. The partial redemption reduces outstanding debt and adjusts the face value accordingly.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update face value in their systems. No impact on broader market operations or trading infrastructure.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders have received partial redemption proceeds, with remaining investment now reflected at the reduced face value of Rs. 94,500 per debenture.
