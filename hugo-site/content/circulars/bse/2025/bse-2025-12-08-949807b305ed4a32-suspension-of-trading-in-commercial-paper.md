## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the commercial paper (Scrip Code: 730205, ISIN: INE895B14388) of RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED effective December 09, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the commercial paper on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730205
- ISIN: INE895B14388
- Instrument: RCL-10-12-25-CP
- Effective date of suspension: December 09, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified commercial paper from December 09, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No dealings should be permitted in ISIN INE895B14388 after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 09, 2025
- **Commercial Paper Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 10, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity affecting only holders of this specific commercial paper.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this instrument. Standard procedure for maturing debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument terms. No impact on equity shareholders of RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED.
