## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by UGRO Capital Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 28, 2025. The issuance comprises 150,000 units with a face value of ₹10,000 each (total ₹150 crore), carrying a 9.50% p.a. interest rate with monthly payments, maturing on February 27, 2027. The securities are rated IND A+ and will trade in dematerialized form only under ISIN INE583D07620.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: UGRO Capital Limited
- **Total Quantity**: 150,000 units
- **Face/Paid-up/Issue Value**: ₹10,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: ₹150 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 977326 (Scrip ID: 950UCL27)
- **ISIN**: INE583D07620
- **Credit Rating**: IND A+
- **Interest Rate**: 9.50% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: February 27, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 27, 2025 (monthly thereafter)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: February 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement debt listing that expands UGRO Capital's debt securities available on BSE. The listing provides an additional fixed-income investment option for institutional and qualified investors in the debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and investors interested in UGRO Capital's credit profile. The IND A+ rating indicates adequate safety with monthly interest payments providing regular income. The 9.50% coupon is competitive for the rating category.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply with dematerialized trading only. No special operational considerations for trading members beyond normal debt segment operations.
