## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544621), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group effective December 16, 2025. This follows the exchange's notice dated December 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544621
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, December 16, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251202-4
- Previous Notice Reference: 20251201-60 dated December 01, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement mechanism, allowing for standard T+1 settlement cycles instead of the more restrictive trade-for-trade basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment change for their trading systems
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822
- Members should update their records and inform clients about the change in trading mechanism

## Important Dates

- **December 01, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251201-60)
- **December 02, 2025**: Current notice issued
- **December 16, 2025**: Effective date of segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED shares. The transition from Trade for Trade (where each trade requires specific matching) to Rolling settlement makes the stock more accessible to regular market participants and should enhance trading volumes. This is a standard progression for SME IPO listings that demonstrate compliance and stability post-listing.
