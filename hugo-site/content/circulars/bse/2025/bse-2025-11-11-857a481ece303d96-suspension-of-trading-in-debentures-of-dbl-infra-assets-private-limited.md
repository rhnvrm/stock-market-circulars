## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of DBL INFRA ASSETS PRIVATE LIMITED with effect from November 12, 2025. The suspension follows the company's notification to the exchange regarding early redemption of the debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Company: DBL INFRA ASSETS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: DBLIPL-13%-28-8-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE0IJE08011 (Scrip Code: 973405)
- Action: Early Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Trading suspension effective from November 12, 2025
- Reference: DR-754/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action related to early redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease dealing in the specified debentures from November 12, 2025
- All exchange members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new trades should be executed in ISIN INE0IJE08011

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete trading halt affects all market participants holding or intending to trade these debentures. Liquidity in these securities is eliminated from the exchange platform.

**Investor Impact:** High - Debenture holders cannot exit positions through exchange trading. Early redemption will be processed as per the company's redemption terms and timeline.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this scrip and inform clients holding these securities about the suspension and early redemption process.
