## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 10, 2025. The issue comprises 6,000 securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 300 crores, with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6,000 Commercial Paper securities
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 300 crores
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Scrip Code: 730734
- Scrip ID: ABHF091225
- ISIN: INE831R14FP4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FP4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 10, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 10, 2026
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the market. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard listing procedure with no special requirements. Only impacts debt market participants and trading members dealing with commercial paper instruments.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to institutional and high net-worth investors who participate in commercial paper markets, given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum trading denomination.
