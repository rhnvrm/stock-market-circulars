## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Navi Finserv Limited on a private placement basis with effect from November 17, 2025. The issue comprises 200 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 10 crore. The securities carry a CARE A1 credit rating and will mature on February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Navi Finserv Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 200 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 10 crore
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,88,497 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730581
- **Scrip ID**: NFL141125
- **ISIN**: INE342T14EZ0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14EZ0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment
- Applicable tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with limited broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Relevant primarily for institutional investors and debt market participants seeking short-term investment opportunities with CARE A1 rated paper.

**Liquidity**: Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible to qualified institutional buyers and high net worth investors in the debt market.

**Issuer**: Provides Navi Finserv Limited with short-term funding of Rs. 10 crore at a discounted rate for approximately 90 days.
