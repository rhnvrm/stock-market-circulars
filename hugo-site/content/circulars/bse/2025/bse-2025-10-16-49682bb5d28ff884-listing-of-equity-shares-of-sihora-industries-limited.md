## Summary

Sihora Industries Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated October 16, 2025. The company has a total of 5,327,400 equity shares held by 252 shareholders. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 69.97% while public shareholders hold 30.03% of the total equity.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 5,327,400 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 3,727,400 shares (69.97%) held by 7 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 1,600,000 shares (30.03%) held by 245 shareholders
- All shares are fully paid up equity shares
- No partly paid up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No shares in locked-in status
- No promoter shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner identified
- All promoter shares (3,727,400) are held in dematerialized form
- All public shares (1,600,000) are held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a standard disclosure filing under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, Regulation 31(1)(a) for shareholding pattern reporting.

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine shareholding pattern disclosure with no material impact on trading or market operations. The shareholding structure shows stable promoter holding at approximately 70% with no encumbrances, which indicates strong promoter commitment. The filing is part of regular compliance requirements for listed entities.
