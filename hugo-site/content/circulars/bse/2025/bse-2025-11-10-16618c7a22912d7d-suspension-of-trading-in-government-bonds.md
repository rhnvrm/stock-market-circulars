## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (SGBNOV25) effective November 11, 2025, as the bond is reaching its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this government bond from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (ISIN: IN0020170091, Scrip Code: 800275) will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-753/2025-2026
- Falls under Debt Segment and Corporate Actions category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for bond maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (SGBNOV25) from November 11, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members need to inform their clients about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: On or around November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single government bond series that has reached maturity. Sovereign Gold Bonds have a defined tenure and this suspension is part of the normal lifecycle.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure no trades are executed in this specific bond from November 11, 2025. Investors holding these bonds will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond terms.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific Sovereign Gold Bond series who will receive redemption at maturity as per the original bond terms announced in 2017.
