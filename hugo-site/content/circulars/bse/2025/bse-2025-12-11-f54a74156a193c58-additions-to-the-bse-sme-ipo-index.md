## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. Luxury Time Limited (ticker: 544635) and Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited (ticker: 544636) were listed on BSE on December 11, 2025, and will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index at the open of trading on December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Luxury Time Limited (Exchange ticker: 544635) listed on December 11, 2025
- Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited (Exchange ticker: 544636) listed on December 11, 2025
- Both companies to be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective December 12, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No. 20251210-77 and Notice No. 20251210-53

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following the listing of new SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index changes. Market participants tracking or benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should note the index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition of two SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index affects only index-tracking products and benchmarked portfolios specific to this index. No broader market implications or trading restrictions are associated with this change. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
