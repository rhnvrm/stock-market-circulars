## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Capital India Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities have a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each with 9.55% annual interest rate and 3-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 9.55% per annum
- Credit Rating: IVR A/STABLE
- ISIN: INE345H07054
- Scrip Code: 976963
- Market Lot: 1 security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from July 28, 2026 to July 28, 2028
- Date of Redemption: July 28, 2028
- Trading Effective: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing in the debt segment. The securities are institutional in nature with high face value and will primarily attract institutional investors.
