## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 728373, ISIN: INE523L14856) issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, effective December 04, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the commercial paper on its scheduled redemption date. Trading Members are instructed not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (NWIL-5-12-25-CP) to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 728373
- ISIN: INE523L14856
- Suspension effective from December 04, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Reference: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action taken for maturing commercial paper instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE523L14856) from December 04, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No trading or transactions should be executed in this security post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 05, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a standard procedure for commercial paper reaching maturity. The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of this specific ISIN.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading Members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific scrip code from December 04, 2025. This is a routine operational update.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing holders of this commercial paper who will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms. No impact on secondary market trading as the instrument is maturing.
