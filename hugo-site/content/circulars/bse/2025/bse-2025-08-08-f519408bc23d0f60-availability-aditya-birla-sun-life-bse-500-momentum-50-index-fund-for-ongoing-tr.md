## Summary

ADITYA BIRLA SUN LIFE BSE 500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening follows previous notice 20250718-21
- Four scheme variants available: Regular/Direct plans with IDCW Payout/Growth options
- All schemes categorized under Equity
- Facilities include subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Trading platform: BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - this is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the availability of the fund schemes for client transactions starting August 11, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 11, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for investors seeking exposure to BSE 500 Momentum 50 Index through this fund. Provides additional investment options with flexible transaction facilities including systematic investment and withdrawal plans.
