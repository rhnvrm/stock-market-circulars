## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Government Security GS19DEC25 (Scrip Code: 800591, ISIN: IN001225C092) effective December 17, 2025, due to the security maturing on its redemption date. This is a routine administrative action under notice DR-779/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Government Security GS19DEC25 will mature on the redemption date
- Scrip Code: 800591
- ISIN: IN001225C092
- Trading suspension effective from December 17, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-779/2025-2026
- Issued under Corporate Actions category for Debt segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for government securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the Government Security GS19DEC25 from December 17, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are advised to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in this security after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date of Suspension**: December 17, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 19, 2025 (implied from GS19DEC25 nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to a routine suspension of a single maturing government security. Trading members holding positions in GS19DEC25 should ensure settlement or redemption processes are completed before the suspension date. The suspension is a standard administrative measure and does not indicate any market disruption or systemic issue.
