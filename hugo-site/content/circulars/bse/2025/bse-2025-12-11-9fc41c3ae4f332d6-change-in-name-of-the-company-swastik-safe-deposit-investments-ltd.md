## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for CIN L82990MH1940PLC003151. The company name has been officially changed from THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD to E & E ENTERPRISES LIMITED, effective October 16, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD
- **New Name**: E & E ENTERPRISES LIMITED
- **CIN**: L82990MH1940PLC003151
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Certificate Issued By**: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- **Digitally Signed**: October 16, 2025 at 09:44:43 IST
- **Original Incorporation Name**: THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard name change procedure completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Dual Name Display**: The company must display the old name (THE SWASTIK SAFE DEPOSIT AND INVESTMENTS LTD) along with the new name (E & E ENTERPRISES LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act.
- **Registered Address**: 1st Floor, Piramal Tower, Peninsula Corporate Park, Ganpatrao Kadam Marg, Lower Parel, Delisle Road, Mumbai, Mumbai-400013, Maharashtra, India

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 16, 2025
- **Certificate Issuance Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Dual Name Display Period**: Two years from October 16, 2025 (until October 16, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change.

**Operational Impact**: The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new name.

**Stakeholder Action**: Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.

**Legal Continuity**: The company retains its original CIN and all legal continuity is maintained despite the name change.
