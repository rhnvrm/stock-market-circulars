## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited. The company has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, dated November 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Capillary Technologies India Limited
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Filing under: Regulation 31(1)(a) - Post Issue Shareholding Pattern
- As on Date: November 19, 2025
- Company has issued partly paid-up shares
- Company has issued convertible securities
- Company has shares in locked-in status
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters
- No depository receipts issued
- No warrants outstanding
- No outstanding ESOPs granted
- No differential voting rights shares
- No significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - This is a disclosure filing rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity has submitted shareholding pattern as required under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Disclosure includes detailed breakdown of promoter and public shareholding
- Declaration provided regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, and encumbrances
- Tabular format disclosure of holding of specified securities submitted

## Important Dates

- Shareholding Pattern Date: November 19, 2025
- Circular Date: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - New listing provides investment opportunity in Capillary Technologies India Limited. The shareholding pattern disclosure enables investors to understand the ownership structure, including the presence of locked-in shares and convertible securities.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors can review the shareholding composition including promoter holding, public shareholding, locked-in shares, and outstanding convertible securities. The absence of pledged shares and encumbrances indicates lower financial risk from promoter side.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard disclosure requirement for newly listed companies.
