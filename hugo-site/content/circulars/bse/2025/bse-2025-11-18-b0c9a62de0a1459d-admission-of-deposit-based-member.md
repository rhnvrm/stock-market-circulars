## Summary

BSE has admitted two new trading members under deposit-based membership category. Finverse Platform Private Limited (Member No. 6967) and Religo Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6968) have been granted trading rights on BSE Limited for Cash and Equity Derivatives segments. Both memberships are new admissions subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Finverse Platform Private Limited admitted as Member No. 6967
- Religo Broking Private Limited admitted as Member No. 6968
- Both members granted trading rights in Cash and Equity Derivatives segments
- Membership type: Deposit-based (trading rights only on BSE)
- Memberships subject to Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice regarding new member admissions.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 18, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251118-28

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification informing existing trading members about the admission of two new deposit-based members to the exchange. The addition of new trading members may marginally increase market participation but does not impose any obligations or changes for existing members. For clarifications, members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
