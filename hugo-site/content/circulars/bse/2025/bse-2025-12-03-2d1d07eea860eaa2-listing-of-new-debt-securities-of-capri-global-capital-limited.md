## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Capri Global Capital Limited for trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 3, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with quantity of 2,000 units at face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.90% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977340
- Scrip ID: 89CGL27
- ISIN: INE180C07247
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 8.90% per annum
- Credit Rating: ACUITE AA/Stable, IVR AA/Positive
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE180C07247
- Members must use the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- For detailed information, members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page
- Clarifications can be obtained from the debt department at contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 1, 2026 to June 1, 2027
- **Date of Redemption**: June 1, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis affecting only debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard trading procedures apply with securities available for trading in dematerialised form on BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and qualified investors participating in private placement debt markets. No put/call options available on these securities.
