## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Basket Repo and Special Repo segments for January 2026 (January 1-31, 2026). The circular provides detailed settlement schedules for Ready Leg T+0, Ready Leg T+1, and Forward Leg transactions, along with specific settlement timings for securities and funds pay-in and pay-out.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers 21 trading days in January 2026
- Settlement numbers range from 2526185 to 2526205
- Three settlement types: Ready Leg T+0, Ready Leg T+1, and Forward Leg
- Trading holidays observed on January 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-26, and 31, 2026
- Distinct settlement timings for Basket Repo and Special Repo transactions
- Reference made to ICCL Notice No.20180525-18 dated May 25, 2018 for additional details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants in Basket Repo and Special Repo segments must adhere to specified settlement timings
- Ready Leg T+0: Securities/Funds pay-in by 02:15 PM, pay-out by 03:00 PM for both Basket and Special Repo
- Ready Leg T+1: Basket Repo pay-in by 02:15 PM, pay-out by 03:00 PM; Special Repo pay-out by 01:00 PM
- Forward Leg: Securities/Funds pay-in by 02:15 PM, pay-out by 03:00 PM for both types

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- **First Settlement**: January 1, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526185)
- **Last Settlement**: January 30, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526205), with pay-out on February 2, 2026 for T+1 transactions
- **Trading Holidays**: January 3, 4, 10, 11, 17, 18, 24, 25, 26, and 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - routine operational schedule with no changes to existing settlement framework.

**Operational Impact**: Low - provides clarity on settlement dates for repo market participants for January 2026, enabling proper planning of liquidity and collateral management. The schedule maintains consistency with existing T+0 and T+1 settlement cycles for ready leg transactions and standard forward leg settlements.
