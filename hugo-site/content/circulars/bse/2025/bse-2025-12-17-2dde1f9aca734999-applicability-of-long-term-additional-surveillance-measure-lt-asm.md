## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective December 18, 2025. Five securities are being newly added to the LT-ASM framework, while three existing securities will be moved to higher ASM stages. No securities are being removed from the framework or moved to lower stages.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework (Stage I)
- 3 securities moved to higher ASM Stage II from Stage I
- No securities moved to lower ASM stages
- No securities removed from LT-ASM Framework
- No securities placed directly in Stage IV
- Effective date: December 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

**Part A - New Securities in LT-ASM Framework:**

1. 3B Films Ltd (544412, INE0TE101010)
2. Agri-Tech (India) Ltd (537292, INE449G01018)
3. Coromandel Engineering Company Ltd (533167, INE312J01012)
4. Deccan Bearings Ltd (505703, INE498D01012)
5. Shashwat Furnishing Solutions Ltd (543519, INE0JMN01019)

**Part B - Securities Moving to Higher ASM Stage:**

1. Aayush Wellness Ltd (539528) - Moved to Stage II
2. Mizzen Ventures Ltd (531537) - Moved to Stage II
3. Shlokka Dyes Ltd (544582) - Moved to Stage II

**Part C - Securities Moving to Lower ASM Stage:** Nil

**Part D - Securities in Direct Stage IV:** Nil

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with LT-ASM framework requirements for affected securities
- Securities under LT-ASM are subject to additional surveillance measures including trading restrictions
- Investors should be aware of enhanced surveillance applicable to these securities
- Market participants must adhere to specific margin and delivery requirements for LT-ASM securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Circular Date:** December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
The inclusion of 5 new securities in LT-ASM framework will subject them to stricter surveillance measures, potentially affecting liquidity and trading volumes. The movement of 3 securities to Stage II indicates continued concerns requiring heightened monitoring.

**Investor Impact:**
Investors holding or trading these securities should expect additional margin requirements and delivery obligations. The LT-ASM framework typically requires 100% upfront margin for purchase transactions.

**Market Operations:**
The consolidated list shows 11 securities currently under various ASM stages, indicating ongoing market surveillance efforts to protect investor interests and maintain market integrity.
