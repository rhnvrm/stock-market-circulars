## Summary

BSE has admitted new debt securities of Power Grid Corporation of India Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling 370,400 units (Rs. 3,704 crore). The debentures carry a 7.02% annual interest rate and mature on December 12, 2035.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977364, Scrip ID: 702PGCIL35
- ISIN: INE752E08809
- Total Quantity: 370,400 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 7.02% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/Stable
- Allotment Date: December 12, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 12, 2035
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE752E08809
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Refer to Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 12, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Annually on December 12 until 2035
- **Redemption Date**: December 12, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. Power Grid Corporation, a AAA-rated PSU, has successfully raised Rs. 3,704 crore through private placement. The 10-year tenure bonds offer competitive 7.02% annual returns. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors. No operational changes required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
