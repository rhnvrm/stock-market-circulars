## Summary

K V TOYS INDIA LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as on December 11, 2025, post-issue of IPO. The company has 7 promoter group shareholders holding 3,663,900 equity shares and 701 public shareholders holding 2,616,100 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shareholders: 708 (7 promoters + 701 public)
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 3,663,900 equity shares (58.30%)
- Public Shares: 2,616,100 equity shares (41.70%)
- Total equity shares: 6,280,000
- Filing date: December 11, 2025 (Post IPO)
- Company has locked-in shares (both promoter and public categories)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No differential voting rights or depository receipts issued
- No significant beneficial owner disclosed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) post-listing
- Declaration required regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, NDUs, other encumbrances, differential voting rights, and significant beneficial owners
- All shareholding data must be submitted in prescribed format for dissemination on Stock Exchange website

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Shareholding pattern reporting date (Post IPO)
- **December 12, 2025**: Circular publication date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine post-IPO shareholding disclosure for a newly listed company. The shareholding structure shows typical promoter holding above 50% (58.30%) with reasonable public float (41.70%).

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard compliance filing with no operational changes.

**Investor Impact**: Informational disclosure providing transparency on ownership structure post-IPO. Presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with minimum promoter contribution requirements. No encumbrances on promoter shares indicate clean shareholding structure.
