## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. The listing comprises 6000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 300 crores. These securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Godrej Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 6000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,754.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 300 crores (approx.)
- **Scrip Code**: 730607
- **Scrip ID**: GFL201125
- **ISIN**: INE02KN14648
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14648
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are traded on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 20, 2025
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term financing to Godrej Finance Limited and offers debt investors an additional investment avenue with high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+). The 90-day tenure indicates this is a short-term money market instrument. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.
