## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Capillary Technologies India Ltd (Exchange ticker: 544614) to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, November 25, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE on Friday, November 21, 2025, as referenced in Notice No: 20251120-42.

## Key Points

- Capillary Technologies India Ltd (ticker: 544614) is being listed on BSE on November 21, 2025
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective November 25, 2025 at market open
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and investors may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the updated index composition.

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Listing date for Capillary Technologies India Ltd on BSE
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine index inclusion following a new listing. The impact is primarily limited to index tracking funds and products that replicate the BSE IPO Index, which will need to include this stock in their portfolios.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard index rebalancing procedures apply. The action has been included in the corporate action file for automated processing by market systems.
