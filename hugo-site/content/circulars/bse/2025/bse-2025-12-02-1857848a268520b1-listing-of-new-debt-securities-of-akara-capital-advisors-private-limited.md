## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 2, 2025. The securities carry a 12.5% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on December 27, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 976868
- Scrip ID: 125ACAP28
- ISIN: INE08XP07324
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 95,034.25
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE08XP07324
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for additional details
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Listing Date: December 2, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from July 27, 2025 to December 27, 2028
- Redemption Date: December 27, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard debt security listing on private placement basis affecting only BSE debt segment participants. The securities will be available for trading exclusively in dematerialised form with no put/call options.
