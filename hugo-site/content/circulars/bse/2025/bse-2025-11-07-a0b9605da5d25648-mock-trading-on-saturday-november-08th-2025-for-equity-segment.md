## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. Trading members using third-party platforms can test their trading applications for various functionalities including exceptional market conditions such as call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release. Members are requested to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the same day.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity segment on November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Testing opportunity for third-party trading platforms
- Tests will cover various functionalities: call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals
- Login window: 11:15 AM to 11:45 AM
- Multiple trading sessions scheduled throughout the day
- Feedback required from all members by 5:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled mock trading session for testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025**: Mock trading session for Equity segment
- **5:00 PM, November 08, 2025**: Deadline for feedback submission
