## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Ugro Capital Limited on private placement basis on its Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The listing comprises 200 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 10 crores. The instruments carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 200 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- **Scrip Code**: 730497
- **Scrip ID**: UCL041125
- **ISIN**: INE583D14725
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 478,525.50 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Yes Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D14725
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Ugro Capital Limited with short-term funding through commercial paper issuance. For investors, it offers a rated debt instrument (IND A1+) with approximately 6-month maturity. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
