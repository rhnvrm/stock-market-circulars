## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,10,100 new equity shares of CWD Limited (Scrip Code: 543378) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading on October 29, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until May 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company**: CWD Limited (Scrip Code: 543378)
- **New Securities**: 1,10,100 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 747/- per share (premium of Rs. 737/-)
- **Allotment Type**: Preferential allotment to non-promoters through warrant conversion
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 4284857 to 4394956
- **Date of Allotment**: July 5, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE0H8H01019
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Lock-in Period**: Until May 14, 2026 for all 1,10,100 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities available for trading from October 29, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares (Dist. Nos. 4284857 to 4394956) until May 14, 2026
- The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 29, 2025
- **Lock-in End Date**: May 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The addition of 1,10,100 shares represents a relatively small increase in CWD Limited's equity base. The preferential allotment was completed through warrant conversion, indicating planned capital raising activity.

**Liquidity Impact**: Limited immediate impact on liquidity as all shares are locked in until May 14, 2026. Post lock-in expiry, there could be minor liquidity enhancement.

**Investor Impact**: Non-promoter allottees must adhere to lock-in restrictions. The issue price of Rs. 747/- reflects significant premium over face value, indicating investor confidence. Other shareholders should monitor any dilution effects from this preferential issue.
