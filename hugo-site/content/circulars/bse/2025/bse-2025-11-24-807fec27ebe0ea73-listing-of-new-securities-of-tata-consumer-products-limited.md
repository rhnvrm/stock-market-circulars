## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Tata Consumer Products Limited (Scrip Code: 500800) that were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, November 25, 2025. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each are being listed
- Shares were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme
- ISIN: INE192A01025
- Scrip Code: 500800
- Shares are pari passu with old equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 185 shares (632473163 to 632473347) and 91 shares (632706589 to 632706679)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of shares that were previously kept in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the exchange are informed about the listing and are permitted to trade these securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: Tuesday, November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the small quantity of shares (276 equity shares) being listed. This is a routine administrative listing of shares kept in abeyance pursuant to a corporate scheme. The shares will have the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares (pari passu), and the listing is not expected to materially affect the trading or market capitalization of Tata Consumer Products Limited.
