## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by NUVAMA Clearing Services Limited on private placement basis effective December 17, 2025. The issue comprises 5,500 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 275 crore. The commercial papers are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. ICICI Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: NUVAMA Clearing Services Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 5,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,942.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 275 crore (approx.)
- **Scrip Code**: 730796
- **Scrip ID**: NCSL171225
- **ISIN**: INE525L14166
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE525L14166
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded through BSE Debt segment only
- Market lot is 1 unit with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it represents a routine short-term debt instrument issuance by a clearing services entity. The 91-day commercial paper provides short-term funding for NUVAMA Clearing Services Limited at an implied discount rate. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial papers. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
