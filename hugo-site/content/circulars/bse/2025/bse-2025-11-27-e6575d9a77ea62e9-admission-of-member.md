## Summary

BSE has admitted Eklingji Broking LLP (Member No. 6970) as a new Trading Member of the exchange with trading rights in Cash/Equity Derivatives segments. The admission is effective November 27, 2025, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New member: Eklingji Broking LLP
- Member number: 6970
- Authorized segments: Cash and Equity Derivatives
- Type: New Membership
- Trading rights granted on BSE Ltd. only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- The new member must comply with all BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- No specific action required from existing trading members

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 27, 2025
- Effective Date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative notification about the expansion of BSE's member base. The addition of one new trading member has no direct effect on market operations, trading rules, or existing members' obligations. Trading members requiring clarification can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
