## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore. The instruments are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025, with a one-year tenure maturing on November 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 500 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,54,545.50 per paper (9.1% discount to face value)
- **Scrip Code**: 730612
- **Scrip ID**: MMFL201125
- **ISIN**: INE101Q14280
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 20, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE101Q14280
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions subject to standard BSE Debt segment rules and regulations

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds Rs. 25 crore of short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in CARE A1-rated commercial papers.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for trading members. Standard debt trading procedures apply with demat-only trading and specified lot sizes.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investment opportunity in one-year commercial paper of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited with CARE A1 rating, offering approximately 9.1% discount to face value at issue.
