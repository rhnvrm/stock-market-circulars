## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 7,50,000 new equity shares of Jyoti Structures Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513250) effective from Thursday, September 11, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 7,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 11.20/-
- Issue price: Rs. 13.20 per share
- Allotment date: June 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 11, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE197A01024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is a routine listing of new securities following warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in period applies until March 11, 2026
- Distinctive numbers assigned: 1189992805 to 1190742804

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 18, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a small equity dilution through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until March 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, reducing short-term supply pressure.
