BSE has announced that equity shares of PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544645, symbol PARKHOSPS, and ISIN number INE119201023. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544645
- Symbol: PARKHOSPS
- ISIN Number: INE119201023
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Specific listing date to be announced separately

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time. Members should await the separate notice containing the listing date and security details before commencing trading.

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine listing announcement for a new company joining BSE. The impact is limited to trading members who will have a new security available for trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate operational changes required. The healthcare sector will see one additional listed entity (PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED operates in the hospital/medical services sector based on the symbol naming).
