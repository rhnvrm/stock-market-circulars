## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Nanta Tech Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544668 and symbol NANTA. The exact date of listing and further details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Nanta Tech Limited
- Scrip Code: 544668
- Trading Symbol: NANTA
- ISIN Number: INE0YJA01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the upcoming listing of Nanta Tech Limited equity shares. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the detailed listing notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Listing Date:** To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The circular serves as preliminary information for trading members about the upcoming addition of Nanta Tech Limited to the SME segment. Investors and traders interested in this counter should await the subsequent notice containing the actual listing date and complete security details before any trading activity can commence.
