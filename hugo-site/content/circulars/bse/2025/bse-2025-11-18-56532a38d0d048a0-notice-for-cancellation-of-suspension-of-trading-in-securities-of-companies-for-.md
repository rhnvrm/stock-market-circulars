## Summary

BSE has issued a notice cancelling the suspension of trading in securities of two companies - Retro Green Revolution Ltd (Scrip Code: 519191) and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291). The suspension was originally imposed for non-compliance with provisions of SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018. Trading in these securities will resume from November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies will have their trading suspension cancelled effective November 20, 2025
- Retro Green Revolution Ltd (Scrip Code: 519191)
- Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291)
- This notice is in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19 dated October 20, 2025
- The suspension was imposed for non-compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018
- All other provisions of the original notice remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice represents the reversal of a previously imposed trading suspension, indicating that the affected companies have now rectified their non-compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the resumption of trading in the specified securities
- For clarifications, market participants can email bse.soplodr@bseindia.com
- Companies must continue to maintain compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018 to avoid future suspensions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Effective Date for Cancellation of Suspension**: November 20, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: October 20, 2025 (Exchange Notice No. 20251020-19)

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact**: Shareholders of both Retro Green Revolution Ltd and Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd will regain the ability to trade their securities, restoring liquidity. This indicates the companies have successfully addressed their compliance issues.

**Market Impact**: Limited to the two specific companies and their shareholders. The broader market impact is minimal as these are likely smaller-cap companies.

**Compliance Signal**: Demonstrates that companies can have suspensions lifted by rectifying non-compliance, encouraging other suspended companies to address their regulatory issues.
