## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of CHPL Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539335) will be moved to the 'X' group with effect from August 28, 2025. This change affects the trading classification and parameters for the stock.

## Key Points

- Company: CHPL Industries Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 539335
- New Classification: 'X' group
- Effective Date: August 28, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: Ms. Sonali Kurhade (+91-8779628213)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group, which typically indicates specific trading restrictions or surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must be aware of the new group classification and any associated trading parameters that apply to 'X' group securities.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for group change

## Impact Assessment

The move to 'X' group may result in different trading parameters, settlement procedures, or surveillance measures for CHPL Industries Ltd. shares. Trading members should review applicable rules for 'X' group securities.
