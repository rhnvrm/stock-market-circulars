## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of Adani Enterprises Ltd. and Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited effective August 26, 2025, due to record date fixation for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Two companies have fixed record date for commercial paper redemption
- Trading members advised not to deal in these commercial papers
- Suspension effective from August 26, 2025
- Both companies have same record date and suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine operational suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from August 26, 2025
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption process

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 26, 2025 (both companies)
- **Trading Suspension Date**: August 26, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only specific commercial paper instruments of two companies. Standard procedural suspension for redemption purposes with no broader market implications.
