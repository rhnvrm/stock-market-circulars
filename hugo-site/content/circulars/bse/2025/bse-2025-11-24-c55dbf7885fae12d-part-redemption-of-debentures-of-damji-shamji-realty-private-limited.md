## Summary

DAMJI SHAMJI REALTY PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE815R07022 (Code: 975254). The record date has been fixed as November 26, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: DAMJI SHAMJI REALTY PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE815R07022
- BSE Code: 975254
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (Zero Coupon)
- Maturity Date: December 15, 2026
- Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 38,422/- per Debenture
- Settlement Number: DR-764/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action related to part redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from November 26, 2025
- All trading and settlement systems must reflect the new face value of Rs. 38,422/- per debenture from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading**: November 26, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-764/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Debenture Holders**: Existing holders will receive partial redemption proceeds, and their remaining debenture holdings will be valued at the reduced face value of Rs. 38,422/- per debenture.

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this involves privately placed debt securities with restricted tradability. Trading members dealing with these specific debentures need to update their systems to reflect the new face value.

**Operational Impact**: Trading and settlement systems must be updated to reflect the reduced face value from November 26, 2025, to ensure accurate pricing and settlement of these securities.
