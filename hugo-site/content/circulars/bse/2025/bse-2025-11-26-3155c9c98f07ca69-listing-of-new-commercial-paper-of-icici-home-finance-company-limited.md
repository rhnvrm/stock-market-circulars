## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis. The listing consists of 1000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a tenure of 90 days from allotment to redemption.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Scrip Code: 730647, Scrip ID: IHF251125
- ISIN: INE071G14HG5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,606.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HG5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 26, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The instrument provides short-term financing options for ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and investment opportunities for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members and institutional investors in debt instruments may participate in secondary market trading of these commercial papers.
