## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ABCL) effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are instructed not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ABCL 6.25% debentures maturing December 25, 2025 (Private placement)
- ISIN: INE860H07HD0, Scrip Code: 960355
- Record date set for December 8, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 8, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-772/2025-2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified ABCL debentures (ISIN: INE860H07HD0) from December 8, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- Debenture holders as of the record date (December 8, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity Date**: December 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders and traders of this specific debenture series. No impact on equity trading or broader debt market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified debenture ISIN from December 8, 2025. Routine corporate action processing with standard suspension protocol.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their final interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the debenture terms. No action required from investors other than ensuring their holdings are properly recorded.
