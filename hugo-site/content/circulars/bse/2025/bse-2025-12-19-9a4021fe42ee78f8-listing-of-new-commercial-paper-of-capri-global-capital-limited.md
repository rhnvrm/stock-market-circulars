## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The instrument consists of 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, with a maturity period of 6 months.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Total issue size: Rs. 25 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,81,955.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE180C14649
- Scrip Code: 730813
- Scrip ID: CGCL191225
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Bank of Maharashtra
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14649
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Capri Global Capital Limited and offers investment opportunities in highly-rated (CRISIL A1+) debt instruments to institutional investors. The 6-month maturity period is standard for commercial paper, and the private placement nature limits broader market participation.
