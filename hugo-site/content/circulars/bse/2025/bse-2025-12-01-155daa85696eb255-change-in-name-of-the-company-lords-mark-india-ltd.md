## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of Lords Mark India Ltd. The name change has been approved by the Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- Company undergoing name change: Lords Mark India Ltd
- Approval received from Registrar of Companies
- Administrative corporate action with no material business impact

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of a corporate administrative change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to note the new company name for identification purposes
- Updated records to reflect the new company name in trading systems

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 1, 2025
- Effective Date: As approved by Registrar of Companies

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The company's trading symbol, operations, and business remain unchanged. Stakeholders should update their records to reflect the new company name.
