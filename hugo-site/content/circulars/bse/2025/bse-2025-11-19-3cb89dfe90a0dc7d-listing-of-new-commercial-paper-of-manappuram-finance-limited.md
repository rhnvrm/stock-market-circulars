## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on a private placement basis effective November 19, 2025. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, with 3,000 units issued. The commercial paper carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 3,000 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730587
- Scrip ID: MFL171125
- Detail Name: MFL-16-02-26-CP
- ISIN Number: INE522D14OX5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,337.50
- Issuing and Paying Agent: AXIS BANK LTD
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OX5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 19, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: November 18, 2025
- Listing Date: November 19, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term debt financing instrument for Manappuram Finance Limited with a 90-day tenure. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in Manappuram Finance securities. The issue provides additional liquidity options in the debt market but does not materially affect broader market operations.
