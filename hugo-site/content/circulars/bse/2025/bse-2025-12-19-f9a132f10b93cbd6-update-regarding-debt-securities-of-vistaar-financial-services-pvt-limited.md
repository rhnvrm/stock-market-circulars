## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Limited. The company has received call money from debenture holders, resulting in changes to the ISIN and paid-up value of the listed debt securities effective December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Notice is in continuation to Exchange Notice 20250926-4 dated September 26, 2025
- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- Changes are effective from Monday, December 22, 2025
- Scrip Code: 975778
- Old ISIN: INE016P07252
- New ISIN: INE016P07260
- Paid-up value increased from Rs. 65,000 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update reflecting the corporate action of call money receipt.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note the revised ISIN and paid-up value for the debt securities of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Limited for trading and settlement purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 22, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This update affects holders and traders of Vistaar Financial Services debt securities. The change in ISIN and increase in paid-up value from Rs. 65,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture reflects the call money received by the company. Market participants must update their records with the new ISIN (INE016P07260) for accurate trading and settlement of these securities.
