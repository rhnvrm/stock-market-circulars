## Summary

Muthoot Microfin Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000, carrying an interest rate of 9.70% per annum with monthly interest payments until maturity on December 3, 2027. The securities are rated CRISIL A+/Positive and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977342, Scrip ID: 970MML27
- ISIN Number: INE046W07339
- Quantity: 75,000 units
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: December 3, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A+/Positive
- Interest Rate: 9.70% per annum
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly from January 1, 2026 to December 3, 2027
- Maturity Date: December 3, 2027
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE046W07339
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 5, 2025**: Effective date of listing and admission to dealings
- **January 1, 2026**: First interest payment date
- **December 3, 2027**: Maturity and redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it involves private placement debt securities of Muthoot Microfin Limited. The impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and existing debenture holders of the company. The CRISIL A+/Positive rating indicates stable credit quality. The securities offer monthly interest payments at 9.70% per annum over a 2-year tenor, providing regular income opportunities for debt investors.
