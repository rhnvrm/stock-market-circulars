## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 1,000 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crore), issued at Rs. 4,86,081 per unit with CRISIL A1+ rating. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Capri Global Capital Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,86,081 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730794
- **Scrip ID**: CGCL171225
- **ISIN**: INE180C14631
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Bank of Maharashtra
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14631
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it represents a routine private placement of commercial paper in the debt segment. The instrument provides short-term funding to Capri Global Capital Limited with a tenure of approximately 4.5 months. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness and low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments with high credit ratings. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible to institutional and high net-worth investors.
