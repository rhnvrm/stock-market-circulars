## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd (ISIN: INE423A14XT1, Code: 729731) effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of the Commercial Paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Adani Enterprises Ltd
- Instrument: Commercial Paper
- ISIN: INE423A14XT1
- BSE Code: 729731
- Record Date: December 23, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: December 23, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Circular Reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified Commercial Paper from December 23, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding this instrument about the trading suspension
- Settlement of existing trades (if any) prior to suspension date to be completed as per normal settlement cycle

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 23, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific Commercial Paper issue. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in debt instruments must update their systems to block trading in this ISIN. Investors holding this Commercial Paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument terms.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of this Commercial Paper as of the record date will be eligible for redemption. No trading opportunity available from December 23, 2025 onwards as the instrument approaches maturity.
