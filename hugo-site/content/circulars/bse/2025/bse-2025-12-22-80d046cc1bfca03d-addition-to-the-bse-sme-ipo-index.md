## Summary

BSE announces the addition of Neptune Logitek Limited (Exchange ticker: 544663) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of December 23, 2025. The company was listed on BSE on December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited (ticker: 544663) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Company listed on BSE on December 22, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 23, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent addition.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and investors may need to adjust portfolios to reflect the new index composition.

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025**: Neptune Logitek Limited listing date on BSE
- **December 23, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index addition following an SME IPO listing. Impact is limited to:
- Index tracking funds that may need to acquire shares to match the index composition
- Investors monitoring the BSE SME IPO Index for investment decisions
- Potential marginal increase in trading activity for Neptune Logitek Limited shares
