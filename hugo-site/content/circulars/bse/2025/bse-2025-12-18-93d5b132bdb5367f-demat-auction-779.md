## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 779 dated December 18, 2025. The auction covers 87 scrips where delivery obligations were not met, requiring auction settlement. Notable quantities include Meesho Limited (283,122 shares), MANGALAM IND (89,634 shares), and RELIANCE POW (6,200 shares).

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 779
- Auction Date: December 18, 2025
- Total Scrips Affected: 87 securities
- Largest Quantities: Meesho Limited (283,122), MANGALAM IND (89,634), RELIANCE POW (6,200), AMPVOLTS LIM (6,670), Aditya Birla (4,919)
- Range: From 1 share (multiple scrips) to 283,122 shares (Meesho Limited)
- Covers equity securities across multiple sectors and market capitalizations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational auction settlement process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery shortfalls must participate in the demat auction as per BSE settlement rules
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism
- Members should ensure adequate arrangements to meet delivery obligations or participate in auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 18, 2025 (Settlement No. 779)
- Auction execution to be conducted as per BSE's standard settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Affects only specific scrips where delivery obligations were not met in Settlement 779.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to members and clients with pending delivery obligations in the listed scrips. Meesho Limited shows the highest quantity (283,122 shares), indicating significant delivery shortfall requiring auction settlement.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders awaiting delivery of these securities will receive shares through the auction process. Price discovery through auction may differ from normal market prices, potentially affecting settlement costs for defaulting members.
