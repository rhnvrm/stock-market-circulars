## Summary

Embassy Office Parks REIT has listed 8,000 units of Commercial Paper on BSE's Debt segment effective October 17, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,79,444, carrying CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings. The instruments will redeem on June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 730412
- **Scrip ID:** EOPR161025
- **ISIN:** INE041014072
- **Total Issue Size:** 8,000 units x Rs. 5,00,000 = Rs. 400 crores
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 4,79,444 per unit (discount to face value)
- **Credit Rating:** CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Tick Size:** 1 paise
- **Trading Mode:** Dematerialized form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent:** ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE041014072
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must comply with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Tenure:** Approximately 8 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing by Embassy Office Parks REIT for short-term funding needs. The instrument has strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL), indicating high credit quality.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members can now trade these Commercial Paper securities on the BSE Debt platform. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals.

**Liquidity Impact:** Addition of Rs. 400 crores worth of short-term debt securities to the market, providing another investment avenue in the REIT debt space.
