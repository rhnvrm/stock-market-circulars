## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on its Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The company has issued 23,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each on a private placement basis, with a total value of Rs. 1,150 crores. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730562, Scrip ID: RRVL131125
- ISIN: INE929O14EG5
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,522 per unit
- Quantity: 23,000 Commercial Papers
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Allotment Date: November 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EG5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Securities are listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term financing for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited for approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new instrument for potential investment opportunities.
