## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members about the partial redemption of debentures issued by PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED. Following the partial redemption, the face value per debenture has been reduced to Rs. 265,637.45. The debentures will trade with the reduced face value effective December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: PCPL-17-4-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE0NF907031
- Internal Code: 974311
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 265,637.45 per Debenture
- Effective Date for Trading: December 31, 2025
- Reference Number: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing debt securities regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- All trades in these debentures from December 31, 2025 onwards must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 265,637.45 per debenture
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders of specific PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE0NF907031).

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value. The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal amount, which is a routine corporate action for debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders have received partial redemption, reducing their investment amount while the remaining debentures continue to trade with adjusted face value.
