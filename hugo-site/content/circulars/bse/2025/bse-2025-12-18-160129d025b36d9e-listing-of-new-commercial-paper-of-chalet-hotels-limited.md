## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Chalet Hotels Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 18, 2025. The instrument consists of 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a 3-month maturity period. The Commercial Paper is rated CRISIL A1+ and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 per unit listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total issue size: 2,000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730809, Scrip ID: CHL171225
- ISIN: INE427F14027
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,351.50 per unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE427F14027
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the specified tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of a short-term debt instrument on private placement basis. The Commercial Paper provides Chalet Hotels Limited with short-term funding for 3 months. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. This listing has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations, and is relevant primarily to debt market participants and institutional investors.
