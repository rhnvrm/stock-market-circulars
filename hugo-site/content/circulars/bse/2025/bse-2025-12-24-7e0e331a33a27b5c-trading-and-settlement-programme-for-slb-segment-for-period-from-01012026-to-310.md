## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment for January 2026. The circular provides a detailed calendar of 21 settlement cycles (SL-189/25-26 through SL-209/25-26) covering the period from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026, including trading dates, depository settlement numbers, 6A7A entry and confirmation dates, and pay-in/pay-out dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers 21 trading days in January 2026
- First settlement: SL-189/25-26 on January 1, 2026
- Last settlement: SL-209/25-26 on January 30, 2026
- Pay-in time: 08:00 a.m. on designated pay-out dates
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks by 07:50 a.m.
- No trading on weekends (January 4-5, 11-12, 18-19, 25-26) and public holidays (January 26 - Republic Day)
- 6A7A entry and confirmation occur on the same day as trading date
- Pay-out follows T+1 settlement cycle (next business day)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Members are required to submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks latest by 07:50 a.m. on pay-in dates
- Pay-in must be completed by 08:00 a.m. on the designated pay-in/pay-out date
- All SLB trades must follow the specified 6A7A entry and confirmation timeline

## Important Dates

**Settlement Period:** January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026

**Key Trading Days:**
- January 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 27, 28, 29, 30 (21 trading days)

**Non-Trading Days:**
- Weekends: January 4-5, 11-12, 18-19, 25-26
- Republic Day Holiday: January 26, 2026

**Pay-in Deadline:** 07:50 a.m. daily
**Pay-in Time:** 08:00 a.m. daily

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine operational circular that maintains continuity in SLB segment operations.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard settlement cycle continues with T+1 settlement for all SLB transactions. Market participants can plan their lending and borrowing activities based on the published schedule.

**Participant Impact:** The schedule enables SLB segment participants to:
- Plan securities lending and borrowing transactions for January 2026
- Ensure adequate liquidity and securities availability for settlement
- Coordinate with depositories and banks for timely pay-in submissions

**Continuity:** The settlement cycle follows the established pattern with consistent timelines for 6A7A entries, confirmations, and pay-in/pay-out processes.
