## Summary

DSP Mutual Fund is launching DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025, with six plan variants covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options. The tentative allotment date is December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: November 24, 2025 to December 08, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Scheme Category: Equity (Index Fund tracking NIFTY SMALLCAP 250)
- Tentative Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 10, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- NFO extended till midnight on December 08, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt by December 10, 2025
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to funds receipt on or before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement following existing BSE StAR MF Platform procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on last day of NFO or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 10, 2025 for allotment
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be completed by 3:00 PM on December 08, 2025
- Switch transactions require funds receipt on or before allotment date for NFO consideration

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: November 24, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: December 08, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **SIP/XSIP Registration Deadline**: 3:00 PM on December 08, 2025
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on December 10, 2025
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch for a passive index fund tracking NIFTY SMALLCAP 250. It provides investors with another investment option in the smallcap segment through a low-cost index fund structure.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard NFO procedures apply with clear timelines for fund transfers, SIP registrations, and switches. Members and distributors need to ensure compliance with specified deadlines for client transactions to ensure successful allotment.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Provides new investment avenue for investors seeking exposure to smallcap stocks through a passive index fund strategy with both regular and direct plan options.
