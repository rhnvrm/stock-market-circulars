## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544614, trading symbol CAPILLARY, and ISIN number INE0ILV01024. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Capillary Technologies India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544614
- Trading Symbol: CAPILLARY
- ISIN Number: INE0ILV01024
- Listing venue: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Notice issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE are informed to note the new listing
- Members should prepare systems for trading in the new security
- Await separate notice for exact listing date and additional security details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
