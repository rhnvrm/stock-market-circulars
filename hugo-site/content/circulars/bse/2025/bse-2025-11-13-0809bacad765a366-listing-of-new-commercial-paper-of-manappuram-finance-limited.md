## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 3000 securities with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling Rs.150 crores. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025, with ISIN INE522D14OV9 and scrip code 730547. AXIS Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 3000 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,69,407.50 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730547
- **Scrip ID**: MFL121125
- **ISIN**: INE522D14OV9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 21, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: AXIS Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OV9
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 21, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 10 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Manappuram Finance Limited with short-term funding through the debt market. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The securities are available only to institutional and qualified investors trading in the debt segment, with no direct impact on retail equity markets.
