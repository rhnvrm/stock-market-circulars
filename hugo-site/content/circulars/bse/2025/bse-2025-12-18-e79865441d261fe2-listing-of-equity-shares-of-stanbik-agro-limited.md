## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of STANBIK AGRO LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544659 and trading symbol STANBIK with ISIN INE16QA01011. This listing pertains to the SME segment of BSE. The specific date of listing and complete security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: STANBIK AGRO LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544659
- Trading Symbol: STANBIK
- ISIN: INE16QA01011
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Complete security details to be provided in future notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the separate notice containing the listing date and detailed security information before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date:** To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low. This is a routine listing announcement for a single SME company. The impact is limited to investors and traders specifically interested in STANBIK AGRO LIMITED.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is announced. No immediate action required until the follow-up notice is issued.

**Investor Impact:** Provides investment opportunity for those interested in the agro sector within the SME segment. Investors should await the listing date announcement and review detailed security information before making investment decisions.
