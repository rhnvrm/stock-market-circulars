## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L55209DL2019PLC347492. The company name has been officially changed from SCHLOSS BANGALORE LIMITED to LEELA PALACES HOTELS & RESORTS LIMITED, effective September 23, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Schloss Bangalore Private Limited.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: SCHLOSS BANGALORE LIMITED
- **New Name**: LEELA PALACES HOTELS & RESORTS LIMITED
- **CIN**: L55209DL2019PLC347492
- **Effective Date**: September 23, 2025
- **Certificate Issued By**: Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- **Authorized By**: Sweety Kumar, Assistant Registrar of Companies
- **Registered Address**: The Leela Palace, Diplomatic Enclave, Africa Avenue, Netaji Nagar, New Delhi, South Delhi - 110023, Delhi, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SCHLOSS BANGALORE LIMITED) along with the new name (LEELA PALACES HOTELS & RESORTS LIMITED) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations continue under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 23, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 23, 2025, 13:23:23 IST
- **Display Requirement Period**: Two years from September 23, 2025 (until September 23, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are administrative corporate actions that do not affect business fundamentals, financial performance, or shareholder equity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update its signage, letterheads, and legal documents to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: None on rights and liabilities. All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest company status on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21). The name change reflects alignment with the Leela Palaces Hotels & Resorts brand identity in the hospitality sector.
