## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 1,421,054 equity shares of Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited (Scrip Code: 543273) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading from December 12, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until June 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,421,054 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,890
- Issue price: Rs. 1,900 per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 10801001 to 12222054
- ISIN: INE0CJD01011
- Scrip Code: 543273

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 1,421,054 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in until June 28, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: June 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential issue represents additional equity capital for Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited. The lock-in period ensures promoter/investor commitment. Impact is limited to the specific company and its stakeholders, with no broader market implications.
