## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are admitted to trading with effect from December 18, 2025. The Commercial Paper has a tenure of approximately 6 months with redemption scheduled for June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 50 crore (1000 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,78,933.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730804
- Scrip ID: ECLF171225
- ISIN: INE804I14YH8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE804I14YH8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term financing for ECL Finance Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The high credit rating (CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+) indicates low credit risk. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
