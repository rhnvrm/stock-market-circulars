## Summary

BSE has announced the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572). This circular notifies market participants that the suspension previously imposed on the security has been lifted, and normal trading operations can resume.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 531572
- Company: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Action: Revocation of trading suspension
- Trading status: Resumed to normal operations
- Notification applies to equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension that was previously in effect for Ranjit Securities Ltd. equity shares has been revoked. The security is now permitted for normal trading on the exchange platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can resume trading in the equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- All orders and transactions should be processed as per normal trading protocols
- Brokers and trading members should update their systems to reflect the revocation of suspension

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 19, 2025
- Effective Date: As per exchange notification

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of suspension has a medium impact on:
- Investors holding shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. can now trade their holdings
- Market liquidity for the scrip is restored
- Trading members can accept buy and sell orders for this security
- The company's equity shares are now accessible for normal market operations
