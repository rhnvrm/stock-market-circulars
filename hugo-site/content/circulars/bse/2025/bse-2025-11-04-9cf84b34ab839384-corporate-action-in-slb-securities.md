## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions affecting Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) for 16 companies scheduled for November 2025. The circular details dividend declarations, one buyback, and one stock split, along with their respective book closure/record dates, ex-dates, and shut periods during which SLB activities will be restricted.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have declared dividends with various record dates in November 2025
- GHCL Ltd. has announced a buyback of shares with record date November 14, 2025
- Websol Energy System Limited has announced a stock split with ex-date November 14, 2025
- SLB shut periods range from 1-4 days for each corporate action
- All corporate actions have ex-dates matching their book closure/record dates

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants must not lend or borrow the affected securities during the specified shut periods
- Market participants must adjust their SLB positions accordingly before shut periods commence
- Stock exchanges and clearing corporations must enforce restrictions on SLB activities during shut periods

## Important Dates

- **November 6-7, 2025**: Shut period for 7 companies (BPCL, Balkrishna, Shriram Finance, Godrej Consumer, Dr. Lal Pathlabs, Aptus, RR Kabel)
- **November 7-10, 2025**: Shut period for 3 companies (Godfrey Phillips, Ajanta Pharma, Power Grid)
- **November 12-13, 2025**: Shut period for ADF Foods
- **November 13-14, 2025**: Shut period for 3 companies (GHCL, ONGC, Websol Energy)
- **November 18-19, 2025**: Shut period for HUDCO
- **November 20-21, 2025**: Shut period for Gabriel India

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects liquidity in SLB market for 16 securities across multiple dates in November 2025.

**Operational Impact**: SLB participants must plan lending/borrowing activities around multiple shut periods throughout November, with peak impact on November 6-7 when 7 securities will be simultaneously restricted. The staggered dates allow for better operational planning but require continuous monitoring of SLB eligibility.
