## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be moved from their current trading group to the 'X' group with effect from December 09, 2025. This group change typically indicates that the scrip will be subject to specific trading conditions or surveillance measures.

- Scrip Code: 531572
- Company Name: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- New Group: 'X' group

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. will be transferred to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically includes securities that are under surveillance or have specific trading restrictions. This classification may impact trading parameters such as price bands, settlement cycles, or margin requirements.
