## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,59,82,092 new units of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust (Scrip Code: 540565) effective from November 14, 2025. These units were issued to non-sponsors for cash consideration of Rs. 168.77 per unit on a preferential basis and are subject to a one-year lock-in period until November 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,59,82,092 new units listed at issue price of Rs. 168.77 per unit
- Allotted to non-sponsors on preferential basis for cash consideration
- Date of allotment: November 11, 2025
- Trading commences: November 14, 2025
- New units rank pari-passu with existing units
- ISIN: INE219X23014
- Distinctive Numbers: 834558089 to 860540180

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- All 2,59,82,092 units are subject to lock-in restrictions until November 14, 2026
- Units cannot be transferred or traded by allottees during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: November 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The issuance increases the unit capital of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust by approximately 26 million units. The one-year lock-in period ensures no immediate selling pressure from the new allottees. The units are issued at Rs. 168.77, representing the agreed preferential pricing for non-sponsor investors. Overall impact is low as this is a standard corporate action for infrastructure investment trusts raising capital.
