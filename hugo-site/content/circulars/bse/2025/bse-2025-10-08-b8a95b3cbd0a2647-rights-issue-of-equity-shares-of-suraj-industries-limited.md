## Summary

Suraj Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 526211) has announced a rights issue with record date of 13th October 2025. Eligible shareholders will receive 21 equity shares of Rs.10/- each for every 13 equity shares held, at an issue price of Rs.40/- per share (including premium of Rs.30/-). Trading in the company's shares will be on ex-rights basis from 13th October 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights entitlement ratio: 21 equity shares for every 13 shares held
- Face value: Rs.10/- per equity share
- Premium: Rs.30/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs.40/- per share
- Payment structure: Rs.10/- on application, Rs.30/- on one or more subsequent calls
- Ex-rights basis trading commences from 13/10/2025
- Settlement number: DR-734/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action circular for rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Suraj Industries Limited shares are executed on ex-rights basis from 13th October 2025
- Members should adjust their systems to reflect the ex-rights trading status for scrip code 526211

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: 13th October 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: 13th October 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-734/2025-2026
- **Circular Date**: 8th October 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders holding shares as on record date will be eligible to subscribe to the rights issue in the ratio of 21:13. The staggered payment structure (Rs.10/- on application and Rs.30/- on calls) provides flexibility to shareholders.

**Market Impact**: Trading on ex-rights basis from 13th October 2025 will result in theoretical price adjustment to reflect the rights entitlement value. Trading members need to ensure proper handling of ex-rights transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard rights issue processing for brokers and depositories.
