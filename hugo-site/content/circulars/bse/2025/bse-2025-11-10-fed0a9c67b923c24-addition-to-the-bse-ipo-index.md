## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Lenskart Solutions Limited (Exchange ticker: 544600) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Monday, November 10, 2025, and will be added to the BSE IPO Index at the open of Wednesday, November 12, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- Lenskart Solutions Limited (ticker: 544600) listed on BSE on November 10, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective at market open on November 12, 2025
- This follows the earlier notice No: 20251107-66
- Corporate action file (*.SDE) updated with this inclusion
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index reconstitution process.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and passive investment strategies may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new index composition.

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Listing date of Lenskart Solutions Limited on BSE
- **November 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This index addition will primarily impact passive index funds and ETFs that track the BSE IPO Index, which will need to acquire shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited. The announcement provides two trading days between listing and index inclusion, allowing for orderly portfolio adjustments. The stock may experience increased trading volume and potential price support from index-tracking fund purchases around the inclusion date.
