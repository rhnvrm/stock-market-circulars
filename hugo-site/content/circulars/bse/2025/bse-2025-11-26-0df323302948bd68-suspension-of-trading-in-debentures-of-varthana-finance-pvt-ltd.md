## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures issued by Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd effective November 27, 2025. The suspension follows the company's notification regarding early redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) with coupon rate of 11.65% that were originally scheduled to mature on September 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to VFPL debentures with ISIN INE125T07295 (Scrip Code: 976016)
- Debenture details: VFPL-11.65%-16-9-26-PVT
- Reason for suspension: Early redemption of NCDs by the issuer
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date
- Notice reference: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action initiated by Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd for early redemption of its debt securities, resulting in administrative suspension of trading by the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE125T07295) from November 27, 2025 onwards
- All exchange trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- Members should inform clients holding or interested in these debentures about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date of Trading Suspension:** November 27, 2025
- **Original Maturity Date:** September 16, 2026 (now subject to early redemption)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to investors and traders holding or dealing in Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd debentures. The early redemption indicates the issuer's decision to retire debt before scheduled maturity, which may be driven by surplus liquidity or refinancing considerations.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive early redemption proceeds as per the terms communicated by the issuer. Trading suspension prevents secondary market transactions, requiring investors to hold until redemption.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and communicate the suspension to affected clients to avoid failed transactions or compliance issues.
