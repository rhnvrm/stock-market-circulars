## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251110-6 (DR-753/2025-2026) suspending trading in Government Bond 812MHSDL25 (ISIN: IN2220150121, Scrip Code: 810716) effective November 11, 2025, as the bond reaches its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date.

- Government Bond 812MHSDL25 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810716
- ISIN: IN2220150121
- Trading suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-753/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government bonds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in Government Bond 812MHSDL25 from November 11, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the suspension status
- Members holding positions should note the upcoming redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Bond Redemption Date**: On or around November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine maturity event affecting a single government bond. The suspension is standard procedure to prevent trading in securities about to be redeemed.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Trading members need to ensure internal systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended security. Bondholders will receive redemption proceeds as per standard settlement procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to current holders of 812MHSDL25 who will receive redemption payments. No impact on broader debt market operations.
