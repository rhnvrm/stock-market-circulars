## Summary

BSE has published the list of companies whose Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) will be visiting BSE premises during December 2025. The circular specifically lists companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd, one of the RTAs scheduled for the December visit. These visits facilitate investor grievance redressal, share transfer services, and other shareholder-related services at the exchange premises.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule published for December 2025
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd is the RTA listed in this circular
- 52 companies serviced by Adroit Corporate are covered
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, infrastructure, and textiles
- RTA visits enable direct investor services at BSE premises
- Includes companies like Hind Rectifiers, LKP Finance, Safari Industries, and Securekloud Technologies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an informational circular about scheduled RTA visits.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This circular is for information purposes only to notify investors and market participants about RTA availability at BSE.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period**: December 2025 (specific dates not provided in the excerpt)
- **Circular Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a routine operational circular.

**Investor Impact**: Low positive impact for shareholders of the listed companies. Provides convenient access to RTA services at BSE premises for grievance redressal, share transfers, and other investor services.

**Operational Impact**: Facilitates better investor servicing by bringing RTAs to exchange premises, improving accessibility for shareholders who need to resolve issues or complete transactions related to their holdings in the 52 companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services.
