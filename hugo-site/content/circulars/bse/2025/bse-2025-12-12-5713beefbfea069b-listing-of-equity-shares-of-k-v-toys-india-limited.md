## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544641, trading symbol KVTOYS, and ISIN number INE1IE301018. This is a SME segment listing, with the exact listing date and security details to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544641
- Trading Symbol: KVTOYS
- ISIN Number: INE1IE301018
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Notice Number: 20251212-7
- Listing date to be announced separately

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED and await the separate notice containing the listing date and additional security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This announcement has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME segment company listing. The impact is primarily relevant for:
