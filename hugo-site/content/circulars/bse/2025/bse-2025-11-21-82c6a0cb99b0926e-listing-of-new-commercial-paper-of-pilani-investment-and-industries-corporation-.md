## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The issue comprises 3000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 3000 Commercial Paper units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,664.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730613
- **Scrip ID**: PICL211125
- **ISIN**: INE417C14AF6
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE417C14AF6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Standard tick size of 1 paise applies
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited for a 91-day tenure. The instruments carry high credit ratings (A1+) indicating strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect broader equity markets or trading operations.
