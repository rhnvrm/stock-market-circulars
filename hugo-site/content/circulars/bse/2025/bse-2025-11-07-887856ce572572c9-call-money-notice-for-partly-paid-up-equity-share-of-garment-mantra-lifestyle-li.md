## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited to collect the second and final call on partly paid-up equity shares. The issue will be open from November 13-27, 2025, with a call money amount of Rs. 0.60 per share payable on 39,14,97,795 partly paid-up equity shares. This notice is issued in accordance with Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021.

## Key Points

- Second and final call on partly paid-up equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited
- Security symbol: GARMENTCM1 with market lot of 1
- Call money amount: Rs. 0.60 per partly paid-up equity share
- Total partly paid-up equity shares: 39,14,97,795
- Issue period: 15 days (November 13-27, 2025)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. during issue period
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Call Money Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice follows existing framework established by:
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding Call Money Module procedures

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Must submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through the Call Money Module on IBBS platform
- Only Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts and Self-Certified Syndicate Banks are eligible to use the module

**For Shareholders:**
- Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 0.60 per share during the issue period
- Payment must be made between November 13-27, 2025 during bidding hours (10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 7, 2025
- **Issue Opening Date:** Thursday, November 13, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Thursday, November 27, 2025
- **Bidding Period:** 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on each day during issue period
- **Issue Duration:** 15 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only holders of partly paid-up equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for involved intermediaries - Trading Members and SCSBs handling these shares will need to facilitate the call money collection process through IBBS platform.

**Shareholder Impact:** High for specific shareholders - Holders of partly paid-up shares must pay the second and final call amount of Rs. 0.60 per share or risk forfeiture of their shares.
