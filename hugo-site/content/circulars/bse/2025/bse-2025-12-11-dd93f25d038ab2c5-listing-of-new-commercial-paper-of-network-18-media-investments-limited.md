## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The issue comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, with a tenure of 91 days. The securities carry credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments. ICICI Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Total: Rs. 200 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730742, Scrip ID: NMI111225
- ISIN: INE870H14WM5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,643 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WM5
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Listing Date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 12, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides Network 18 Media & Investments Limited with working capital financing for 91 days. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. The listing enhances liquidity options for institutional investors in short-term debt instruments. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the high denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit.
