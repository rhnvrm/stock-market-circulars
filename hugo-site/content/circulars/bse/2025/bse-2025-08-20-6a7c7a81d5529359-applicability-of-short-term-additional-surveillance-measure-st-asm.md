## Summary

BSE has updated its Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 21, 2025. The update includes 7 new securities being added to the ST-ASM framework, 7 securities moving out of the framework, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under surveillance.

## Key Points

- 7 securities newly added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 7 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving between different ASM stages
- Framework includes regular equity scrips, SME scrips, and T+0 scrips
- Consolidated list shows securities with their respective ASM stages

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

**New Additions (Effective August 21, 2025):**
- Aryaman Financial Services Ltd (530245)
- Brightcom Group Ltd (532368) - As per NSE
- Cropster Agro Ltd (523105)
- Filmcity Media Ltd (531486)
- Pradhin Ltd (530095)
- Signpost India Ltd (544117) - As per NSE
- Spinaroo Commercial Ltd (544392) - SME Scrip

## Securities Removed from ST-ASM Framework

**Moving Out (Effective August 21, 2025):**
- Abram Food Ltd (544422) - SME Scrip
- Cryogenic Ogs Ltd (544440) - SME Scrip
- Cybele Industries Ltd (531472) - Moving to LT-ASM Framework
- Hit Kit Global Solutions Ltd (532359)
- National Securities Depository Ltd (544467)
- Pioneer Investcorp Ltd (507864)
- Quadrant Televentures Ltd (511116)

## Surveillance Framework Structure

- **Short Term ASM**: 5/15/30 Days framework for enhanced monitoring
- **Stage Classifications**: Securities categorized in different stages (Stage I shown in consolidated list)
- **Special Categories**: SME scrips (@), T+0 scrips (~), and NSE coordinated securities (*) included

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025
- All changes to surveillance measures become applicable from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Securities under ST-ASM face additional margin requirements and trading restrictions
- Enhanced surveillance applies to volatile or unusual price movement securities
- SME and T+0 scrips subject to same surveillance measures as regular equity

**Market Monitoring:**
- Continuous review process for securities meeting surveillance criteria
- Dynamic framework allowing movement between different surveillance categories
- Coordination with NSE for certain securities ensures uniform surveillance approach
