## Summary

BSE announces the resumption of trading in debentures of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD (Scrip Code 973204) effective December 09, 2025. This follows the partial exercise of put option by debenture holders after the record date of October 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD debentures (Scrip Code 973204) will resume from December 09, 2025
- Put option was partially exercised by debenture holders
- Previous record date was fixed as October 23, 2025 for payment of interest and redemption
- Reference number: DR-773/2025-2026
- Trading members are requested to take note of the resumption

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational update regarding trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that the debentures will be available for trading from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **October 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption (as per Notice No. 20251017-10)
- **December 08, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 09, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of trading in debentures

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a company-specific debt instrument update affecting only debenture holders and traders of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD debentures with Scrip Code 973204. The partial exercise of put option indicates normal corporate action processing, and the resumption allows continued market activity in these securities.
