## Summary

BSE announces the listing of further securities issued by 19 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- 19 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 3,421,841 shares across all companies
- Securities range from face value of ₹1 to ₹10
- All securities are not under lock-in period
- Largest issuance: SBFC Finance Limited with 2,309,228 shares

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Issuances:**
- SBFC Finance Limited: 2,309,228 shares (₹10 face value)
- Tata Motors Ltd: 306,071 shares (₹2 face value)
- Home First Finance Company: 254,550 shares (₹2 face value)
- J.B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals: 140,236 shares (₹1 face value)
- AXIS Bank Ltd: 131,047 shares (₹2 face value)
- Capital India Finance Limited: 125,000 shares (₹2 face value)

**Other Notable Companies:**
- Aarti Industries, Chalet Hotels, Cholamandalam Investment, Dr. Reddy's Laboratories, ICICI Lombard General Insurance, Navin Fluorine International, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for employee stock option securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Securities are listed with specified ISIN codes and scrip codes
- Distribution numbers assigned for tracking purposes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** September 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** Monday, September 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** Not applicable for all listed securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as these are employee stock option issuances rather than public offerings. The securities represent employee compensation and retention mechanisms. Trading volumes may see marginal increases due to additional shares in circulation for these companies.
