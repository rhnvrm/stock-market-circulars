## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures issued by MAS Financial Services Limited (ISIN: INE348L07191, Code: 975719) effective November 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of the debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: MAS Financial Services Limited
- Debenture Series: MFSL-8.55%-6-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE348L07191
- BSE Code: 975719
- Record Date: November 21, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 21, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of MAS Financial Services Limited from November 21, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- All transactions in the specified debentures must cease from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **November 21, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium impact, limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series (MFSL-8.55%-6-12-25-PVT). The suspension is temporary and linked to the corporate action process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record date (November 21, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption. Investors cannot trade these debentures from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN from November 21, 2025. Standard suspension procedure for corporate actions in debt securities.
