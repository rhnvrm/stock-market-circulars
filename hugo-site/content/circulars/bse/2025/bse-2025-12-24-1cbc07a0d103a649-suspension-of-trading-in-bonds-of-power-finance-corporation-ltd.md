## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of Power Finance Corporation Ltd with ISIN INE134E08LZ0 (Code: 974471) effective December 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of the 7.58% bonds maturing on January 15, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: PC-7.58%-15-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE134E08LZ0, Code: 974471)
- Record date for redemption: December 30, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 30, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds
- Notice reference: DR-787/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified bonds from December 30, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Bondholders on record as of December 30, 2025 will be eligible for redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 30, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 30, 2025
- **Bond Maturity**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific bond series. The suspension affects only the 7.58% bonds of Power Finance Corporation Ltd maturing in January 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems are updated to prevent any inadvertent trades in these bonds from December 30, 2025. Bondholders should prepare for redemption proceeds as per the bond terms.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders registered on the record date will receive redemption proceeds. Secondary market liquidity for these bonds ceases from the no-dealings date.
