## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of HDB Financial Services Limited effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is being implemented due to the company fixing a record date for the purpose of debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for HDB Financial Services Limited debenture series HDBFSL-ZC-13-01-26-PVT (ISIN: INE756I07EK0, Code: 974324)
- Suspension effective from December 29, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record date set as December 29, 2025
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified HDB Financial Services debentures (Code: 974324) from December 29, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No trades should be executed in the affected debenture series from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| December 24, 2025 | Circular issued |
| December 29, 2025 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| December 29, 2025 | Trading suspension effective (no dealings from this date) |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This suspension affects only a specific series of non-convertible debentures (HDBFSL-ZC-13-01-26-PVT) and is a routine corporate action for redemption purposes.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these specific debentures will receive their redemption amount and interest payment based on the record date. No trading will be possible from December 29, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in debenture code 974324 from the specified date. This is a standard operational requirement for such corporate actions.

**Duration:** The suspension is for the purpose of redemption, suggesting this is a terminal event for this debenture series.
