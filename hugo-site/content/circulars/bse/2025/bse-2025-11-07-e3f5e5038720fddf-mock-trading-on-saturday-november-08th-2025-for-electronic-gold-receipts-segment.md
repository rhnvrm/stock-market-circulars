## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms from empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will simulate various market functionalities including exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Electronic Gold Receipts segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Trading members can test third-party platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Session will simulate exceptional market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- All trades executed during mock trading will not attract any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or create rights/liabilities
- Feedback must be submitted by 5:00 PM on the same day
- Active participation from all members is requested

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine system testing and familiarization exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries or clarifications

## Important Dates

**Saturday, November 08, 2025 - Mock Trading Session Schedule:**

- **Log-in:** 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- **Pre-Open Order Entry Period:** 12:00 PM to 12:08 PM
- **Matching Period:** 12:08 PM to 12:15 PM
- **Continuous Trading:** 12:15 PM to 4:00 PM
- **Block Deal Window:** 1:00 PM to 1:15 PM
- **Closing:** 4:00 PM to 4:10 PM
- **Post-Closing:** 4:10 PM to 4:20 PM
- **Feedback Submission Deadline:** 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. This provides an opportunity for trading members to test and familiarize themselves with their trading systems and various market scenarios in a risk-free environment. No settlement obligations or margin requirements will be generated from the mock trades. The session serves as a platform for quality assurance and system readiness verification for members using both third-party and in-house trading platforms.
