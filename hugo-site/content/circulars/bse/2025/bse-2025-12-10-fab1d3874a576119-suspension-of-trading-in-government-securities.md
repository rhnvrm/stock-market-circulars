## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Treasury Bill (T-Bill) with ISIN IN001225C050 and Scrip Code 800353 (GS15DEC25C) effective December 11, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on December 15, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the suspension date onwards.

## Key Points

- T-Bill GS15DEC25C (Scrip Code: 800353) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: IN001225C050
- Trading suspension effective from December 11, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption Date: December 15, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-775/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for maturing government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified T-Bill (Scrip Code 800353) from December 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 11, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 15, 2025**: T-Bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The trading suspension affects only holders and potential traders of this specific T-Bill security. As the instrument is reaching maturity, this is standard market procedure to prevent trading in securities close to redemption. No broader implications for debt market operations or other government securities.
