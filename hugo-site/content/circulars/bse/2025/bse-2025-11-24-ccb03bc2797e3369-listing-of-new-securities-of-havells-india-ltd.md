## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 50,421 new equity shares of Havells India Ltd (Scrip Code: 517354) issued under two Employee Stock Purchase Schemes. The shares will commence trading from November 25, 2025, with a mandatory 12-month lock-in period from the date of credit to employee demat accounts.

## Key Points

- Total 50,421 equity shares of Re. 1/- each fully paid up
- 48,947 shares issued under Havells Employees Stock Purchase Scheme 2022
- 1,474 shares issued under Havells Employees Stock Purchase Scheme 2016
- All shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 25, 2025
- ISIN: INE176B01034

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All shares subject to mandatory 12-month lock-in period from credit date to employee demat accounts
- Shares cannot be traded during lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: 12 months from date of credit into employee demat accounts

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The 50,421 shares represent a negligible percentage of Havells India's total equity base. The 12-month lock-in period further minimizes any immediate supply impact on the market.

**Operational Impact**: Routine corporate action with no operational changes for trading members beyond awareness of the additional shares in circulation.

## Security Details

### Havells Employees Stock Purchase Scheme 2022
- **Shares**: 48,947 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 627206422 to 627255368
- **Lock-in**: 12 months from demat credit date

### Havells Employees Stock Purchase Scheme 2016
- **Shares**: 1,474 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 627255369 to 627256842
- **Lock-in**: 12 months from demat credit date
