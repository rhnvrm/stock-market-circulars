BSE has announced that equity shares of GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade under the symbol TRADEUNO with scrip code 544594 on the SME segment. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company Name: GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544594
- Trading Symbol: TRADEUNO
- ISIN: INE1GUJ01014

- Listing date to be announced separately

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.
