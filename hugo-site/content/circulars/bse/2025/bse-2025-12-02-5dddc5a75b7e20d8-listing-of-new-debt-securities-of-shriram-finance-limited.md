## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Shriram Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 2, 2025. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with scrip code 977335 (SFL011225) and will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE721A07SQ8.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977335
- Scrip ID: SFL011225
- Detail Name: SFL-3-5-29-PVT
- ISIN: INE721A07SQ8
- Quantity: 12,500 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+, IND AA+/Stable
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: December 1, 2025
- Date of Redemption: May 3, 2029
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number (INE721A07SQ8)
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE website for further details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 3, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued through private placement and cater to institutional investors. The high credit rating (AA+) indicates strong creditworthiness. The listing expands Shriram Finance's debt instrument options on BSE but has no direct impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
