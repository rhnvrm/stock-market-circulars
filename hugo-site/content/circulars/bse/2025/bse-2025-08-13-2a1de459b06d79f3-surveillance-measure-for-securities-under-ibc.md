## Summary

BSE has updated its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). One security, Lakshmi Precision Screws Ltd, has been moved to Stage 1 surveillance effective August 14, 2025. The circular provides consolidated lists of all securities currently under various stages of IBC surveillance.

## Key Points

- Lakshmi Precision Screws Ltd (Scrip Code: 506079, ISIN: INE651C01018) moved to Stage 1 ASM under IBC
- No securities moved between other surveillance stages
- No securities excluded from ASM framework
- Consolidated list shows 14+ companies currently under IBC surveillance at various stages
- Most companies remain at "Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure" stage

## Regulatory Changes

- Enhanced surveillance monitoring for Lakshmi Precision Screws Ltd under Stage 1 ASM
- Continued application of IBC surveillance framework with stage-wise progression
- Securities marked with ~ are T+0 scrips shortlisted based on parent company IBC status

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with additional surveillance requirements for listed securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting obligations for Stage 1 securities
- Compliance with IBC-related disclosure requirements for affected companies

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025 - New surveillance measures take effect
- All stage movements and surveillance measures applicable from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects trading patterns for securities under IBC proceedings with enhanced surveillance potentially leading to reduced liquidity and increased volatility for affected stocks.

**Operational Impact**: Limited - Only one security moves to higher surveillance stage, minimizing operational disruption while maintaining regulatory oversight of distressed companies.
