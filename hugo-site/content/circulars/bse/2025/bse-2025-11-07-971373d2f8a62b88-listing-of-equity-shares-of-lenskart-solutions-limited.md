## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544600, symbol LENSKART, and ISIN number INE956O01016. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Lenskart Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544600
- Trading Symbol: LENSKART
- ISIN Number: INE956O01016
- Notice Number: 20251107-20
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems for trading in LENSKART shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 7, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement marks the addition of Lenskart Solutions Limited to BSE's trading platform. Trading members should be aware of the new scrip for future trading activities. The actual market impact will be determined once the listing date and price details are announced. This represents a significant listing given Lenskart's prominence in the eyewear retail sector.
