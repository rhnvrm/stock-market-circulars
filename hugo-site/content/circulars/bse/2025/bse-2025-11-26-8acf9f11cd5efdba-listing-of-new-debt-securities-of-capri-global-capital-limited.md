## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE180C07239, carrying 9.25% p.a. interest rate with monthly payment frequency and maturity on May 24, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 5,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Scrip Code**: 977314
- **Scrip ID**: 925CGCL28B
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 9.25% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from December 24, 2025 to May 24, 2028
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE AA/Stable, IVR AA/Positive
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C07239
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 24, 2028
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (24th of each month from December 2025 to May 2028)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing that provides additional investment options in the debt segment. The securities are privately placed and will only affect investors interested in AA-rated corporate debt with monthly income streams.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for general market participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new scrip details for client queries and trading purposes.
