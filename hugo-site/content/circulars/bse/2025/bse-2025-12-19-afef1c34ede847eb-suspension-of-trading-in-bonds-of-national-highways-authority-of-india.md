## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for NHAI 7.39% tax-free bonds (ISIN: INE906B07EH2, Scrip Code: 935580)
- Record date fixed for December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 26, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified NHAI bonds from December 26, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 19, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 26, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of NHAI's 7.39% tax-free bonds maturing on January 11, 2026. This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact, primarily affecting bondholders who will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption. The suspension ensures proper settlement of redemption and interest payment to eligible bondholders as of the record date.
