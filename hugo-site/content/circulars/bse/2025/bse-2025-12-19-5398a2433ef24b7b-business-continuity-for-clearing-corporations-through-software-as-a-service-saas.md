## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL) will conduct a mock session on December 20, 2025 to test Two-way SaaS portability using the SaaS instance ICCL@NCL. The SaaS instance will be invoked at approximately 2:00 PM, after which all ICCL clearing members must log in exclusively through the ICCL@NCL instance for RTRMS system, N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe using their pre-provided credentials. This exercise is part of ICCL's business continuity planning for clearing corporations.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for December 20, 2025 to test Two-way SaaS portability
- SaaS instance ICCL@NCL will be activated at approximately 2:00 PM
- After activation, members cannot access regular RTRMS system and must use ICCL@NCL instance only
- All ICCL clearing members required to participate and test functionality
- Members must use previously provided login credentials for SaaS instance
- Access through specific URLs: https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/CIM/ (CIM) and https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/ (N-MASS)
- Applies to N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe systems

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements previously announced business continuity framework referenced in circulars dated March 29, 2023 (No. 20230329-18), April 13, 2023 (No. 20230413-49), April 21, 2023 (No. 20230421-41), and December 29, 2023 (No. 20231229-17).

## Compliance Requirements

- All ICCL clearing members must participate in the mock session on December 20, 2025
- Members must test SaaS functionality using ICCL@NCL instance
- Members must use designated SaaS URLs for accessing systems:
  - Cash deposit link (CIM): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/CIM/
  - Position and collateral allocation link (N-MASS): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/
- Members must refer to BSE exchange circulars No. 20251218-51, 20251218-52, and 20251218-53 dated December 18, 2025 for detailed mock timings
- Queries to be directed to: SaaS.Ops@icclindia.com and risk.monitoring@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 20, 2025**: Mock session date for testing SaaS portability
- **Approximately 2:00 PM on December 20, 2025**: ICCL@NCL SaaS instance activation time
- **December 18, 2025**: Date of reference BSE circulars (20251218-51, 20251218-52, 20251218-53) containing detailed mock timings

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All ICCL clearing members will experience a temporary shift from regular RTRMS system to SaaS instance during the mock session. Members must ensure technical readiness and staff availability to participate in testing.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a mock session for business continuity testing purposes and does not affect actual trading or settlement operations. However, successful testing is critical for disaster recovery preparedness.

**Member Preparedness**: Members need to ensure they have access to pre-provided credentials for ICCL@NCL instance and technical infrastructure to access the SaaS URLs. The exercise tests the portability framework that would be crucial in actual business continuity scenarios.

**System Scope**: Affects multiple systems including RTRMS, N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe, requiring comprehensive testing across all clearing member operational platforms.
