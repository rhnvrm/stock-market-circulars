## Summary

BSE has listed further securities issued by 15 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, December 16, 2025. A total of 1,482,075 shares across various companies are being listed with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 15 companies issuing further securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total shares listed: 1,482,075 shares across all companies
- Effective trading date: December 16, 2025 (Monday)
- No lock-in period applicable (all entries show 'NA')
- Face values range from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share
- Largest issuance: Wipro Ltd (567,778 shares)
- Companies span diverse sectors: banking, technology, manufacturing, jewelry, finance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems to reflect the newly listed securities
- Trading members must ensure proper tracking of the new share quantities from December 16, 2025
- Companies have fulfilled listing requirements for ESOP/ESOS issued securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 16, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: Not applicable (no lock-in period)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The additional shares represent routine ESOP/ESOS conversions and will cause marginal dilution. The staggered issuance across 15 companies limits individual stock impact.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible increase in tradable float. Largest additions are Wipro (567,778 shares), Moschip Technologies (314,536 shares), and ArisInfra Solutions (333,510 shares).

**Investor Impact**: No immediate impact on existing shareholders. These are employee compensation-related issuances already accounted for in company disclosures.
