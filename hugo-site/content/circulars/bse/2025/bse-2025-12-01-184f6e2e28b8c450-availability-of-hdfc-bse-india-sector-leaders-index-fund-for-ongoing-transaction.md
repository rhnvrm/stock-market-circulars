## Summary

HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund is reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. This includes facility for Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP). Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan with Growth option are available.

## Key Points

- Scheme reopening effective from December 02, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- Full transactional facilities including subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Scheme category: Equity
- ISIN for Regular Plan - Growth: INF179KC1JX8
- ISIN for Direct Plan - Growth: INF179KC1JY6
- References previous Notice No. 20251106-15

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. BSE members are informed about the scheme's availability for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Scheme Reopening Date:** December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors interested in transacting in HDFC BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund. The reopening provides investment opportunity in an equity index fund tracking BSE sector leaders. No impact on broader market operations or other securities.
