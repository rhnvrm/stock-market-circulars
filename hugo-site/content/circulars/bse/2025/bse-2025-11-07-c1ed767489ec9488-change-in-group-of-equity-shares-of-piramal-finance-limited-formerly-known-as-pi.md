## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Piramal Finance Limited (formerly known as Piramal Capital & Housing Finance Limited) with Scrip Code 544597 will be permitted to trade under Group 'B' starting Friday, November 21, 2025. This represents a change in the trading classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Piramal Finance Limited (Formerly Piramal Capital & Housing Finance Limited)
- Scrip Code: 544597
- New Trading Group: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Friday, November 21, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251107-22
- Category: Company related - Equity Segment

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified to trade under BSE's Group 'B' category. Group B typically includes companies that are required to provide additional disclosures or have specific trading requirements compared to Group A securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Be aware of the group change effective November 21, 2025
- Ensure their systems and processes are updated to reflect the new trading group classification
- Inform clients about the change in trading group status

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 21, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The change to Group 'B' may affect:
- Trading liquidity and settlement procedures
- Margin requirements for trading members
- Investor eligibility criteria
- Surveillance measures applicable to the scrip

Trading members should prepare for the transition and ensure compliance with Group 'B' trading requirements from the effective date.
