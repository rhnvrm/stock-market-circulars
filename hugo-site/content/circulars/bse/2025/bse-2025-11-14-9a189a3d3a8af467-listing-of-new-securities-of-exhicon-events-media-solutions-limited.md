## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 795,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) effective from November 17, 2025. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 315 per share (face value Rs. 10 with premium of Rs. 305).

## Key Points

- 795,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 305/-
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 543895
- ISIN: INE0O8901016
- Distinctive Numbers: 21740001 to 22535000
- Date of Allotment: August 6, 2025
- Issue Price: Rs. 315.00 per share

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from November 17, 2025. The allotted shares are subject to lock-in requirements as specified.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard preferential allotment of 795,000 shares with lock-in restrictions until May 31, 2026. The shares will increase the tradable float of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited after the lock-in period expires. No immediate trading implications as shares are locked-in for approximately 6 months from listing date.
