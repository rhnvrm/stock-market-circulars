## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Jagsonpal Finance and Leasing Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Jagsonpal Services Limited with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from "JAGSONPAL FINANCE AND LEASING LIMITED" to "JAGSONPAL SERVICES LIMITED"
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L65929DL1991PLC043182
- Certificate issued on September 29, 2025
- Company originally incorporated as "JAGSONPAL FINANCE AND LEASING PRIVATE LIMITED"
- Company is limited by shares
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative corporate action reflecting a name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name for a period of two years along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **September 29, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 29, 2025, 23:36:47 IST
- **Two-Year Period**: Old name must be displayed alongside new name until September 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect the fundamental business operations or financial position of the company.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all statutory documents, letterheads, signage, and official communications to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display with the old name for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of corporate identity.
