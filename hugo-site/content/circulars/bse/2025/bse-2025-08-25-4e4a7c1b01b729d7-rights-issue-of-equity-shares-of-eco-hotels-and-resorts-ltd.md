## Summary

ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD (Code: 514402) has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. Existing shareholders will be entitled to subscribe to new equity shares on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- Record date fixed as 29/08/2025
- Rights ratio: 1 equity share for every 4 equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs 15.20 per share (Rs 10 face value + Rs 5.20 premium)
- Ex-rights trading begins from 29/08/2025 (Settlement DR-704/2025-2026)
- Payment structure: Rs 3.80 on application, Rs 11.40 in calls as determined by Board

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard rights issue procedure being followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute transactions on ex-rights basis from 29/08/2025
- All equity share transactions must reflect the rights entitlement adjustment
- Trading members required to note the record date and payment terms

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: 29/08/2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: 29/08/2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-704/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact limited to ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD shareholders and trading. Existing shareholders gain subscription rights while share price will adjust for ex-rights trading. Payment in installments provides flexibility for subscribers.
