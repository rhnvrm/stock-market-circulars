## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Infinity Infoway Limited (Scrip Code: 544567), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Friday, October 24, 2025. This change follows the exchange's notice dated October 7, 2025, and represents a shift to a less restrictive trading mechanism.

## Key Points

- Company: Infinity Infoway Limited - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544567
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, October 24, 2025
- Reference Notice: 20251007-56 dated October 7, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from the Trade for Trade segment, which requires compulsory delivery and settlement for all transactions, to the Rolling settlement segment. This change allows for more flexible trading mechanisms including intraday trading and standard T+1 settlement cycles under M Group classification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the effective date of October 24, 2025 for the segment transfer
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain for any clarifications or additional details
- Inform clients about the change in trading mechanism for this scrip

## Important Dates

- **October 7, 2025**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20251007-56)
- **October 8, 2025**: Circular date
- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically improves liquidity and trading flexibility for the stock. Investors will be able to conduct intraday trading and benefit from standard settlement cycles rather than compulsory delivery.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Trading members need to update internal systems and classifications, but this is a routine operational change. The shift indicates the stock has met necessary criteria for graduating from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment.

**Investor Impact**: Positive - Enhanced trading flexibility and improved liquidity potential for shareholders of Infinity Infoway Limited.
