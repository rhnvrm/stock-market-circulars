## Summary

TATA MUTUAL FUND will offer TATA BSE MULTICAP CONSUMPTION 50:30:20 INDEX FUND - NFO along with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from December 09, 2025 to December 23, 2025. The scheme includes 6 plan options (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options). Tentative allotment date is December 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: December 09, 2025 to December 23, 2025
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes categorized as EQUITY with unique ISINs ranging from INF277KA1EM5 to INF277KA1ER4
- Tentative allotment date: December 29, 2025
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 26, 2025
- NFO extended till midnight on December 23, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds by December 26, 2025
- Switches accepted during NFO period with specific cutoff dates: Equity/Debt/Liquid/Overnight funds till December 23, 2025 (3:00 PM); FOF till December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a new product launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure transactions for which client has not initiated funds till 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period) will be cancelled and CC with N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending till 3:00 PM on last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (December 23, 2025)
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 26, 2025 for allotment
- Switch transactions subject to receipt of funds on or before allotment date to be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: December 09, 2025
- **NFO Closure Date**: December 23, 2025 (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **Extended NFO Period**: Till midnight December 23, 2025 (lumpsum only)
- **Switch Cutoff - Equity/Debt/Liquid/Overnight**: December 23, 2025 (3:00 PM)
- **Switch Cutoff - FOF**: December 22, 2025 (3:00 PM)
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: December 26, 2025 (6:00 PM)
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch affecting only mutual fund distribution channels and investors interested in consumption-themed index funds.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for BSE StAR MF Platform members who need to process NFO subscriptions and ensure compliance with payment timelines and switch matrices.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides new investment option for investors seeking exposure to BSE Multicap Consumption 50:30:20 Index through a passively managed fund with both Regular and Direct plan options.
