## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Shriram Finance Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 25, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a 9.15% p.a. interest rate, maturing on January 19, 2029.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 975339, Scrip ID: 915SFL29
- ISIN: INE721A07RY4 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 25,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 112,171.90
- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.15% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+/Stable, IND AA+/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE721A07RY4
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from January 19, 2025 to January 19, 2029
- **Redemption Date**: January 19, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine listing of debt securities by Shriram Finance Limited on BSE's debt segment. The listing provides additional fundraising information for market participants tracking the company's debt issuances but has no immediate impact on equity markets or trading operations. Relevant primarily for debt market participants and investors interested in AA+ rated corporate bonds.
