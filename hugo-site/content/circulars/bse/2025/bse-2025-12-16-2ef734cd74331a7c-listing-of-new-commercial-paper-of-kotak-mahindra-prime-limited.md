## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 16, 2025. The issue comprises 500 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 250 crore.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Size: 500 units (Rs. 250 crore total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,68,091.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730739
- Scrip ID: KMP101225
- ISIN: INE916D146I6 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE916D146I6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on the BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 358 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing in the debt segment affecting only debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Relevant only to institutional investors and entities dealing in short-term debt instruments of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Debt segment trading members can access these securities with standard trading parameters from December 16, 2025.
