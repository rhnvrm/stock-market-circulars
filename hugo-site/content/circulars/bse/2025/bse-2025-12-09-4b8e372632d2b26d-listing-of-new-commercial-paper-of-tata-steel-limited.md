## Summary

BSE has listed two series of commercial paper issued by Tata Steel Limited on private placement basis, effective December 9, 2025. The first series comprises 2,500 units worth Rs. 125 crores maturing on December 29, 2025, and the second series comprises 25,000 units worth Rs. 1,250 crores maturing on March 6, 2026. Both instruments are rated ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper series listed on BSE Debt segment
- Series 1: 2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (total Rs. 125 crores), maturing December 29, 2025
- Series 2: 25,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (total Rs. 1,250 crores), maturing March 6, 2026
- Allotment date: December 8, 2025 for both series
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ for both series
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should use the specified ISIN numbers for transactions
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 8, 2025 (both series)
- **Listing Date:** December 9, 2025 (both series)
- **Redemption Date (Series 1):** December 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 2):** March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Tata Steel with short-term funding and offers debt market participants additional highly-rated instruments for investment. The strong credit ratings (A1+) reflect the issuer's financial strength. The listing affects only debt segment participants and does not impact equity markets or broader trading operations.
