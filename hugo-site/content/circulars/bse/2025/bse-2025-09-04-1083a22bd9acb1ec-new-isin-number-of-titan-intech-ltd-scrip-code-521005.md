## Summary

BSE has announced a new ISIN number INE807M01031 for TITAN INTECH LTD (Scrip Code: 521005) following the subdivision of equity shares from Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share. The new ISIN will be effective for trades from September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: TITAN INTECH LTD (Scrip Code: 521005)
- New ISIN: INE807M01031
- Share subdivision: From Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share
- Effective date: September 8, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- Document reference: DR-710/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

The new ISIN number replaces the previous identifier for TITAN INTECH LTD equity shares following the approved share subdivision. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250828-57 dated August 28, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to recognize the new ISIN number INE807M01031 for all trades in TITAN INTECH LTD shares from the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 4, 2025
- Ex-Date (Effective Date): September 8, 2025
- Reference to prior notice: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This change affects trading systems, settlement processes, and investor demat accounts. Shareholders will receive 10 shares of Re.1 each for every 1 share of Rs.10 held previously. The subdivision improves affordability and liquidity of the stock while maintaining the same total value for existing shareholders.
