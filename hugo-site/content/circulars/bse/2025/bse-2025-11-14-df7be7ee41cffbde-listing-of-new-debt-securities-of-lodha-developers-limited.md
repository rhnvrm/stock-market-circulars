## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Lodha Developers Limited on private placement basis, effective November 14, 2025. The securities consist of 25,000 treasury bills with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying a CRISIL AA/Stable credit rating and offering 2.45% p.a. interest rate payable quarterly.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 25,000 treasury bills
- **Scrip Code**: 977293
- **Scrip ID**: LDL131125
- **Detail Name**: LDL-TBILLS-13-11-28-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE670K07349
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 2.45% per annum
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 13, 2028
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE670K07349
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from September 30, 2026 to November 13, 2028
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 13, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine debt security listing for institutional investors through private placement. The securities are available only in dematerialized form on the debt segment with limited retail participation expected.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to note the ISIN and scrip details for trading purposes. Standard debt trading procedures apply with 1 paise tick size and market lot of 1 unit.

**Investment Considerations**: The CRISIL AA/Stable rating indicates high degree of safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations. The 2.45% p.a. interest rate with quarterly payments provides predictable income stream over the 3-year tenure till November 2028.
