## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The listing comprises 700 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 35 crores. The securities are rated IND A1 and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- 700 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730651, Scrip ID: NFL251125
- ISIN Number: INE342T14FD4
- Credit Rating: IND A1
- Issue Price: Rs. 485,444.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14FD4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 19, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 114 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard private placement listing of short-term debt instruments. The commercial paper has a short tenure of about 4 months and is limited to institutional investors. Trading members can contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
