## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Corona Remedies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544644, trading symbol CORONA, and ISIN number INE02ZQ01018. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Corona Remedies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544644
- Trading Symbol: CORONA
- ISIN Number: INE02ZQ01018
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Specific listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code and symbol for Corona Remedies Limited in preparation for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members are informed in advance to prepare systems and processes for the new listing. The actual market impact will depend on the company's size, sector, and market conditions at the time of listing, which will be detailed in the subsequent notice containing the listing date and security details.
