## Summary

BSE has listed 6,000 new Commercial Papers issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis on the Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, were allotted on November 14, 2025, and will mature on February 13, 2026. The issuing and paying agent is HDFC Bank Limited.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 6,000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,423.65 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730577
- **Scrip ID**: SL141125
- **ISIN**: INE211H14AG0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE211H14AG0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Sharekhan Limited with short-term funding (91-day tenor) and offers debt market participants an A1+ rated investment option. The impact is limited to debt segment traders and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No effect on broader equity markets or retail investors.
