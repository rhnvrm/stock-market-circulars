## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors for the public issue of BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED. Anchor investor allocation is conducted one day prior to the issue opening and helps in price discovery and building confidence among retail investors.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation completed for BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED IPO
- Allocation details published by BSE as per regulatory requirements
- Anchor allocation typically occurs at or above the price band floor price
- One-third of anchor allocation subject to lock-in for 30 days, remaining two-thirds for 90 days
- Anchor portion limited to 60% of QIB category in book-built issues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is informational, notifying market participants about the anchor investor allocation process completion.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company and merchant bankers must ensure anchor allocation complies with SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Anchor investor details and allocation must be disclosed to the exchange
- Lock-in requirements must be adhered to as per regulations

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: December 21, 2025 (typically one day before issue opening)
- Issue Opening Date: Expected December 23, 2025 (or as per final prospectus)
- Lock-in Period: 30 days for one-third allocation, 90 days for two-thirds allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation indicates institutional confidence in the IPO and helps establish price benchmarks for retail investors. Successful anchor allocation often leads to better subscription rates in the public portion.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Retail and other investors can gauge institutional interest and pricing before subscribing to the issue. The anchor allocation price serves as a reference point for valuation.

**Company Impact:** High - For BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED, successful anchor allocation is crucial for IPO momentum and demonstrates institutional backing for their public market debut.
