## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL) has announced a mock session on December 20, 2025, to test Two-way SaaS portability using the SaaS instance ICCL@NCL. All ICCL clearing members are required to participate and test the functionality. The SaaS instance will be invoked at approximately 2:00 PM, after which members will not be able to log in to the RTRMS system and must use the ICCL@NCL instance for N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe with their existing credentials.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for December 20, 2025, to test Two-way SaaS portability
- SaaS instance ICCL@NCL will be invoked at around 2:00 PM
- All ICCL clearing members must participate in testing
- After invocation, RTRMS system login will be unavailable; members must use ICCL@NCL instance
- Login credentials previously provided to members remain valid
- Two SaaS links provided: CIM (cash deposit) and NMASS (position and collateral allocation)
- Detailed mock timings available in BSE circulars 20251218-51, 20251218-52, and 20251218-53

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements previously announced business continuity measures referenced in ICCL circulars from March 2023, April 2023, and December 2023.

## Compliance Requirements

- All ICCL clearing members must participate in the mock session on December 20, 2025
- Members must test functionality using the SaaS instance ICCL@NCL
- Members must access systems through designated SaaS links after 2:00 PM:
  - Cash deposit (CIM): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/CIM/
  - Position and collateral allocation (NMASS): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/
- Members must use existing login credentials for N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe
- Refer to BSE exchange circulars dated December 18, 2025, for detailed mock timings
- Direct queries to SaaS.Ops@icclindia.com or risk.monitoring@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 20, 2025**: Mock session for testing Two-way SaaS portability
- **Approximately 2:00 PM on December 20, 2025**: SaaS instance ICCL@NCL invocation time

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All ICCL clearing members will experience a mandatory system transition during the mock session. Regular RTRMS access will be unavailable after 2:00 PM, requiring members to switch to the SaaS instance.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a scheduled mock session for business continuity testing with no direct impact on trading or settlement operations.

**Member Impact**: Medium - Clearing members must ensure their teams are prepared to test the SaaS functionality and access systems through alternative links. Successful participation is critical for validating disaster recovery capabilities.

**Technical Impact**: Members need to verify they can access and operate through the SaaS instance URLs and that their existing credentials work properly in the ICCL@NCL environment across all relevant systems (N-MASS, CIM, NCMS.exe).
