## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of four newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of January 1, 2026. The companies—NANTA TECH LIMITED, ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED, BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED, and APOLLO TECHNO INDUSTRIES LIMITED—were listed on BSE on December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Four companies to be added to BSE SME IPO Index on January 1, 2026
- NANTA TECH LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544668)
- ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544669)
- BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544670)
- APOLLO TECHNO INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544671)
- All four stocks listed on BSE on December 31, 2025
- Corporate actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following the listing of new SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and fund managers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- **January 1, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. Routine index addition affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to rebalance portfolios to include these four stocks. The additions were included in the end-of-day corporate action file for automated processing.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors tracking or invested in the BSE SME IPO Index. The addition of four stocks represents standard index maintenance following new listings.
