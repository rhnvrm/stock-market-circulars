## Summary

BSE has listed 80,85,550 new equity shares of COVIDH TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 534920) issued on a rights basis. These shares will commence trading on the exchange from Thursday, November 13, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 80,85,550 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Mode of issue: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE899M01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 323423 to 8408972
- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Trading commencement date: November 13, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine rights issue listing that increases the paid-up share capital of COVIDH TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. The impact is limited to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue. The new shares carry the same rights and privileges as existing shares, ensuring no dilution of shareholder rights.
