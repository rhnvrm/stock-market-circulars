## Summary

BSE has extended the timeline for submission of System Audit reports for Trading Members (TYPE-III) who use algorithmic trading or provide algorithmic trading facilities to clients. The extension applies to the half-yearly audit period ending September 30, 2025, and was granted following representations from market participants in consultation with SEBI.

## Key Points

- Extension applies to stockbrokers using or providing algorithmic trading facilities
- Covers half-yearly audit period from April 2025 to September 2025
- Extended deadlines apply to all eligible members including Qualified Stock Brokers (QSBs)
- Decision made in consultation with SEBI based on market participant requests
- References original notice 20251017-29 dated October 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirements introduced. This circular provides timeline extensions for existing System Audit requirements under TYPE-III category for algorithmic trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members using algorithmic trading must complete:

1. **System Auditor Appointment & Audit Plan Submission** - Must be completed and submitted to BSE
2. **Preliminary Audit Report** - Comprehensive audit report covering the specified period
3. **Action Taken Report (ATR)** - If applicable based on audit findings

All submissions must be made to the Exchange by the respective extended deadlines.

## Important Dates

**Audit Period:** April 2025 - September 2025

- **December 10, 2025** - Deadline for completion of System Auditor Appointment and submission of Audit Plan
- **December 31, 2025** - Deadline for submission of Preliminary Audit Report
- **March 31, 2026** - Deadline for submission of Action Taken Report (if applicable)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Provides relief to trading members by extending compliance deadlines, allowing additional time for thorough system audits.

**Market Impact:** Low - Administrative extension affecting internal compliance processes of algorithmic trading members without direct market operations impact.

**Compliance Impact:** Medium - Trading members must ensure timely appointment of system auditors and submission of required reports within the extended timelines to maintain regulatory compliance.
