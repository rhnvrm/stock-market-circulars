## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of ENS Enterprises Limited for public comments. The document was filed on November 01, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can review and submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by ENS Enterprises Limited on November 01, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited on the draft offer document
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure notice as per ICDR Regulations requirements for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Interested parties may submit comments within 21 days from November 19, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available for public viewing for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **November 01, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by ENS Enterprises Limited
- **November 19, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **December 10, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to ENS Enterprises Limited's SME IPO process. The impact is limited to:

- Potential investors in ENS Enterprises Limited SME IPO
- Market participants interested in SME segment offerings
- Regulatory compliance observers

This is a procedural disclosure requirement with no immediate trading or operational implications for the broader market.
