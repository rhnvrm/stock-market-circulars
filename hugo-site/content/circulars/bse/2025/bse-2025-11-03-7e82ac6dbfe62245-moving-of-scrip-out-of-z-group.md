## Summary

BSE has announced that GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) will be moved out of the 'Z' Group and transferred to the 'X' Group effective November 14, 2025. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024, specifically Chapter-VII(A) relating to Penal Actions for Non-Compliance.

## Key Points

- GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) to be moved from Z Group to X Group
- Effective date: November 14, 2025
- Movement is based on SEBI Master Circular provisions on penal actions
- Normal trading restrictions of X Group will apply post-transfer
- Market participants can contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com for queries

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements provisions under SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024, Chapter-VII(A) which deals with Penal Actions for Non-Compliance. The movement from Z Group (most restrictive) to X Group indicates the company has addressed compliance issues that led to its initial placement in Z Group.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the group change for GV Films Ltd
- Trading in the scrip will be subject to X Group norms from November 14, 2025
- Any queries or clarifications should be directed to BSE Listing Compliance and Operations at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 14, 2025 - GV Films Ltd will be transferred from Z Group to X Group

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:** The movement from Z Group to X Group is a positive development for GV Films Ltd and its shareholders. Z Group is the most restrictive trading category, typically reserved for companies with serious compliance issues. Transfer to X Group indicates:

- Improved compliance status
- Reduced trading restrictions compared to Z Group
- Better liquidity prospects for shareholders
- Enhanced market confidence in the company

**Market Participants:** Traders and investors in GV Films Ltd should be aware of the group change and any associated trading rule modifications that apply to X Group securities versus Z Group securities.
