## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (ISIN: IN0020170091, Scrip Code: 800275) effective November 11, 2025, as the bond is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. Trading Members are advised not to deal in this Government Bond from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series VII (SGBNOV25) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: IN0020170091
- Scrip Code: 800275
- Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Reason: Bond maturity on redemption date
- Notice Reference: DR-753/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for bond maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified Government Bond from November 11, 2025
- Members should ensure their systems and processes reflect the trading suspension
- All stakeholders must take note of the suspension to avoid any inadvertent trading activity

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Bond Redemption Date:** November 2025 (specific date as per bond terms)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to medium. This affects only holders and traders of this specific Sovereign Gold Bond series issued in November 2017.

**Operational Impact:** Trading Members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this security. Bondholders will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond's terms and conditions.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding this bond will receive their principal amount along with applicable interest upon maturity. No further trading opportunity will be available after November 10, 2025.
