## Summary

BSE has notified members that JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND is reopening its JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF22M001093) for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will be available for ongoing transactions including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities effective from October 17, 2025. This notice references the previous Notice No. 20250922-10 which presumably announced the temporary closure of transactions.

## Key Points

- Scheme reopening for ongoing transactions effective October 17, 2025
- JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH available for subscription and redemption
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Scheme Category: EQUITY
- ISIN: INF22M001093
- Transactions available on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the resumption of trading activities for a specific mutual fund scheme that was previously closed for subscriptions.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members and participants should:
- Update their systems to enable transactions for the specified scheme from October 17, 2025
- Ensure the scheme is available for investors on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Process subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP requests for this scheme from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions including subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only one mutual fund scheme from JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors in JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND who can now resume or initiate investments through various transaction modes. The reopening provides investment flexibility through subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members need to enable the scheme for transactions. Standard operational update with no significant system changes required.
