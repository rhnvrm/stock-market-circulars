## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544621, symbol SSMD, and ISIN number INE1U2R01013. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544621
- Trading Symbol: SSMD
- ISIN Number: INE1U2R01013
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20251201-1
- Notice Date: December 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notification regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add one more company to the BSE SME segment, providing investors with additional investment opportunities in the agritech sector. The impact is limited to this single company and its stakeholders. Trading members should prepare their systems to accommodate the new scrip once the listing date is announced.
