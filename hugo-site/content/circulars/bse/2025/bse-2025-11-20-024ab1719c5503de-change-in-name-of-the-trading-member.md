## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member CREDAVENUE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6810) to ASPERO MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED, effective from Friday, November 21, 2025. This is an administrative update regarding member identification.

## Key Points

- Trading member number 6810 is changing its name
- Old name: CREDAVENUE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- New name: ASPERO MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective date: November 21, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative name change for an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only. Trading members dealing with this entity should update their records to reflect the new name.

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 21, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact on market operations. This is a routine administrative change affecting only the identification of one trading member. Other trading members are informed for their record-keeping purposes. No operational or trading changes are required.
