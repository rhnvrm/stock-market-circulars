## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Kalpataru Projects International Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 3,000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total value Rs. 150 crore), effective from November 14, 2025. The securities will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Kalpataru Projects International Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Quantity**: 3,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,519 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730563
- **Scrip ID**: KPIL131125
- **ISIN**: INE220B14BO6
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE220B14BO6
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing with no impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Affects only BSE debt segment trading members who wish to trade in Kalpataru Projects' Commercial Paper. The securities provide short-term investment opportunities for institutional investors.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants. The IND A1+ credit rating indicates adequate safety for short-term debt instruments. The issue price of Rs. 4,92,519 against face value of Rs. 5,00,000 implies an effective yield for the 90-day instrument.
