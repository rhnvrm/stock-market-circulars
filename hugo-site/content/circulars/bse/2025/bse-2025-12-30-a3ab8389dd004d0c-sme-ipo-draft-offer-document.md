## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED filed on December 29, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the Stock Exchange website for at least 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED on December 29, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- The document is hosted on BSE SME website at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx#
- Public stakeholders may submit comments within 21 days from December 30, 2025
- Comments should be emailed to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 30, 2025 (until approximately January 20, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine disclosure for a single SME company's IPO process.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to potential investors and stakeholders of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED who wish to review the draft offer document or submit comments during the public comment period.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency to potential investors interested in the SME IPO of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED.
