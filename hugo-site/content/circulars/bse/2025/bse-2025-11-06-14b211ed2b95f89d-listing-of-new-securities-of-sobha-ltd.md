## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,208 new equity shares of Sobha Ltd (Scrip Code: 532784) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each that were issued on rights basis. Trading members are permitted to trade in these securities effective November 7, 2025. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new securities listed: 1,208 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE671H01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 110135573 to 110136766 (1,194 shares) and 110143620 to 110143633 (14 shares)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Company: Sobha Ltd, Scrip Code: 532784

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for additional securities arising from corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new securities
- The additional 1,208 shares should be available for trading from November 7, 2025
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply to these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: November 7, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The addition of 1,208 shares represents a negligible increase in the total outstanding shares of Sobha Ltd and will have no material impact on trading volumes, liquidity, or share price.

**Operational Impact**: Routine operational update for trading members to recognize the new securities in their trading systems. The shares being pari-passu with existing equity means they carry identical rights and privileges, simplifying integration.
