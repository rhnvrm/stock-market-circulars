## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on its Debt segment effective August 25, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of ₹1,00,000 per unit and carry an interest rate of 13.30% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities (Market lot: 1)
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: ₹25 crore
- Scrip Code: 977052
- Scrip ID: 1330ACAP27
- ISIN: INE08XP07332
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB(Stable)
- Interest Rate: 13.30% per annum (paid monthly)
- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 21, 2027
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **August 25, 2025**: Listing effective date
- **February 21, 2027**: Redemption date
- **Monthly**: Interest payment frequency

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities offer monthly interest payments at 13.30% per annum with BBB(Stable) rating from ICRA, providing fixed income investment option for institutional investors.
