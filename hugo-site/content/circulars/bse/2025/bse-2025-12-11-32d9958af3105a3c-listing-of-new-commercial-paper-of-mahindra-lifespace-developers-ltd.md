## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd. on private placement basis. A total of 1500 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The securities carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will mature on March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1500 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Scrip Code: 730745, Scrip ID: MLDL101225
- ISIN: INE813A14391
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,721 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ 
- Allotment Date: December 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 6, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: YES BANK LTD
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE813A14391
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade under the detail name MLDL-6-3-26-CP on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025 (effective today)
- **Redemption Date**: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding (approximately 86 days) to Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd. The IND A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term debt instruments. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in Commercial Papers. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
