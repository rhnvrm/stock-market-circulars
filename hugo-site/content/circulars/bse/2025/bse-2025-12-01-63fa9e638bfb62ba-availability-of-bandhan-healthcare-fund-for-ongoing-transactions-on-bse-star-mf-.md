## Summary

BSE has announced that BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND will be reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. The fund will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This notice references earlier Notice No. 20251107-23 regarding the fund's previous status.

## Key Points

- BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND reopening for ongoing transactions from December 02, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Subscription and redemption facility enabled
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under EQUITY category
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251107-23

## Scheme Details

**Regular Plan Variants:**
1. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF194KB1KC2)
2. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF194KB1KD0)
3. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF194KB1KE8)

**Direct Plan Variants:**
4. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF194KB1KF5)
5. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF194KB1KG3)
6. BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF194KB1KH1)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and platform participants should note the reopening date
- Trading members should update their systems to enable transactions for these schemes from December 02, 2025
- Members should refer to previous Notice No. 20251107-23 for context on the fund's earlier status

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 02, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine operational notice affecting only the specific mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in healthcare sector equity funds through Bandhan Mutual Fund. Investors can now resume or initiate investments in this fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact:** BSE platform participants need to ensure the fund is available for transactions from the effective date. All transaction types (lumpsum, SIP, STP, SWP) should be enabled for the six scheme variants listed.
