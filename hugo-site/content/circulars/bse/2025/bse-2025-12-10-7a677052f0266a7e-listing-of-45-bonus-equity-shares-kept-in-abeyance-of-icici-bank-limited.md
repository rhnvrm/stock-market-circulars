## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the exchange with effect from Thursday, December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total shares being listed: 45 bonus equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2/- each
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Trading commencement date: December 11, 2025
- Distinctive number ranges: 15 shares (6412616651-6412616665) and 30 shares (6412615697-6412615726)
- Status: Previously kept in abeyance, now being released for trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of this listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures for the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The market impact is minimal as this involves only 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank being released from abeyance status. This is an administrative action that completes the bonus share distribution process for specific shareholders. The small quantity will have negligible effect on ICICI Bank's trading volume or stock price.
