## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 753 dated November 12, 2025. The auction includes 105 scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 25,000 shares across multiple sectors including oil & gas, infrastructure, financial services, manufacturing, and technology companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 753
- Auction Date: November 12, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 105
- Quantity range: 1 to 25,000 shares per scrip
- Includes major companies like ONGC (400 shares), NTPC (150 shares), BPCL (140 shares), Infosys (10 shares)
- Largest auction quantities: Standard Capital (25,000 shares), Atal Realtech (12,500 shares), Tuni Textile (10,000 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors: energy, telecom, automotive, financial services, pharmaceuticals, textiles, engineering

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational demat auction notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants with outstanding delivery obligations in the listed scrips must participate in or settle through the demat auction process
- Brokers and clearing members must ensure availability of shares for auction settlement
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 753

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The auction affects 105 different securities with varying quantities. While individual quantities are relatively small for most scrips, the breadth of affected securities is significant.

**Operational Impact**: Brokers and clearing members with outstanding positions in these scrips must arrange for shares or participate in the auction mechanism, potentially affecting their settlement obligations and penalties.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders of the listed companies may see temporary price volatility due to auction-related buying activity. Short sellers or those with failed delivery obligations will face auction settlement charges and potential penalties.
