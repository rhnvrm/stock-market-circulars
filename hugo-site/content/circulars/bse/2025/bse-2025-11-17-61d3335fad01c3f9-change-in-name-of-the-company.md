## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED. The company's name has been officially changed to IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED effective October 1, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED
- **New Name**: IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- **Effective Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Original Name**: GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED
- **CIN**: L62099MH1993PLC074167
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Office**: Z1-3092, Akshar Business Park, Sector 25, Vashi, Navi Mumbai, Sanpada, Thane, Thane-400703, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative corporate action for name change under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name in terms of Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, obligations, and legal standings continue under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 1, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Display Period for Old Name**: 2 years from October 1, 2025 (until September 30, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Shareholder rights and liabilities
- Existing contracts and obligations
- Company operations or business activities
- Stock trading (shares will continue to trade under new name)

Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
