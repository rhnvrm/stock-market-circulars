## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 600 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each with ISIN INE423A14YO0, trading under scrip code 730606 (AEL191125). The Commercial Paper was allotted on November 19, 2025 and will mature on January 30, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 600 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each
- Scrip Code: 730606, Scrip ID: AEL191125
- ISIN: INE423A14YO0
- Issue Price: Rs. 493,036.50/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YO0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- Securities are traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Tenure**: 72 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issuance is part of Adani Enterprises Limited's normal short-term funding operations. With high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+), the instrument represents low credit risk. The private placement basis limits broader market participation. Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE's debt department at the provided contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
