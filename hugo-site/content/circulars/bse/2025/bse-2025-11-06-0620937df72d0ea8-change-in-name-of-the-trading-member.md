## Summary

BSE has announced the name change of trading member LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD. (Member No. 3281) to "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD." This is an administrative change that will be effective from November 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member affected: LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD.
- Member Number: 3281
- New name: LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD.
- Change type: Private Limited to Limited company nomenclature
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the corporate name change of the trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the name change for their records. No action required from other market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is a routine administrative notification regarding the name change of a single trading member. The member number (3281) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in identification and operations. No impact on trading activities, market operations, or other market participants.
