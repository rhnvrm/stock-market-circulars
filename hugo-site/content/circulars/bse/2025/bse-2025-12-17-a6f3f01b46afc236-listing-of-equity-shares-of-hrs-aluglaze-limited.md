## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544656, symbol HRS, and ISIN number INE1E1V01017. This listing is under the SME segment. The specific date of listing and details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544656
- Symbol: HRS
- ISIN Number: INE1E1V01017
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be informed separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compli Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the upcoming listing of HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED equity shares for future trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. The impact is limited to trading members who will have a new security available for trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate action required. The SME segment typically caters to smaller companies, so market-wide impact is expected to be minimal.
