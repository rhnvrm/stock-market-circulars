## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Fusion Finance Limited's first and final call on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 6,10,58,392 partly paid-up equity shares (symbol: FUSIONPP) must pay Rs. 65.50 per share between November 27, 2025 and December 11, 2025. The call money collection will be facilitated through Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts.

## Key Points

- First and final call on partly paid-up equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited
- Total shares affected: 6,10,58,392 partly paid-up equity shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 65.50 per share
- Security symbol: FUSIONPP
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM during issue period
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)
- Module accessible to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts
- Circular follows BSE notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing framework under BSE notice no. 20210104-37 (January 4, 2021) and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money collection procedures for partly paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and SCSBs**: Must use the Call Money Module on IBBS platform to submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period
- **Shareholders**: Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 65.50 per share during the specified period
- **Eligible Intermediaries**: Only Self-Certified Syndicate Banks and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can facilitate call money collection

## Important Dates

- **Issue Opening Date**: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date**: Thursday, December 11, 2025
- **Issue Period**: 15 days
- **Bidding Period**: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily during issue period

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to specific shareholder group holding FUSIONPP partly paid-up shares. No impact on broader equity market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members and SCSBs must ensure their systems are configured to access the Call Money Module on IBBS platform. Total call money to be collected: approximately Rs. 39.99 crore (6,10,58,392 shares Ã— Rs. 65.50).

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding partly paid-up shares must arrange payment of Rs. 65.50 per share within the 15-day window. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as per company's articles of association.
