## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited. Anchor investor allocation is a key step in the IPO process where shares are allocated to institutional investors before the issue opens to the public.

## Key Points

- Company: Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited
- Event: Allocation to anchor investors in public issue
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Sector: Healthcare / Super Speciality Hospital Services
- Process Stage: Pre-IPO anchor allocation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investor allocation must comply with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018
- Anchor investors are subject to lock-in period as per regulatory requirements
- Disclosure of anchor investor details to the public before issue opening

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 19, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed on or before this date
- Public issue likely to open soon after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation indicates institutional confidence in the IPO. The allocation details provide insights into demand and pricing for the upcoming public issue.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - For retail investors interested in the Gujarat Kidney and Super Speciality Limited IPO, anchor allocation provides information about institutional interest and pricing. Successful anchor allocation typically signals positive sentiment.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard IPO process notification with no operational changes for existing market participants.
