## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Capri Global Capital Limited (CGCL) on its Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 9.25% per annum payable monthly. The securities are rated ACUITE AA/Stable and IVR AA/Positive, with redemption scheduled for May 24, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 5,000 debt securities
- Scrip Code: 977314 (Scrip ID: 925CGCL28B)
- ISIN: INE180C07239
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 9.25% per annum
- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from December 24, 2025 to May 24, 2028
- Redemption Date: May 24, 2028
- Credit Rating: ACUITE AA/Stable, IVR AA/Positive
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C07239
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 24, 2025
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly (24th of each month)
- Final Interest Payment: May 24, 2028
- Redemption Date: May 24, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities with limited quantity (5,000 units). The impact is primarily relevant to:

- Debt segment trading members who can now trade these securities
- Institutional investors and HNIs who may have subscribed to the private placement
- Existing investors in Capri Global Capital Limited debt instruments

The high credit ratings (AA/Stable and AA/Positive) indicate strong creditworthiness. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular income to investors. With a tenure of approximately 2.5 years and no put/call options, these are standard medium-term debt instruments.
