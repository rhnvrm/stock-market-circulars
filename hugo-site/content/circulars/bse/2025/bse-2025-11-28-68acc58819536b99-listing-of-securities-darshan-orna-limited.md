## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 50,029,335 new equity shares of Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be available for trading on the Exchange from December 1, 2025. The shares were allotted on November 25, 2025, at a face value of Rs. 2.00 per share with a premium of Rs. 1.00 per share.

## Key Points

- 50,029,335 equity shares of Rs. 2.00/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1.00/- on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 539884
- ISIN: INE671T01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 50029336 to 100058670
- Allotment Date: November 25, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 1, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine rights issue listing for Darshan Orna Limited. The new shares will increase the total outstanding equity and are available for trading from December 1, 2025. The shares carry the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares. Impact is limited to shareholders and investors of Darshan Orna Limited with no broader market implications.
