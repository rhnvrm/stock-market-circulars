## Summary

BSE has prohibited and suspended trading in the securities of Winpro Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531337) effective November 24, 2025. This action is an interim measure taken after the company failed to respond to a show cause notice and was found non-existent at its registered office address during a site visit conducted by the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Winpro Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531337)
- Trading suspension effective from November 24, 2025
- Reason: No response to show cause notice and non-existence at registered office
- Action type: Interim measure
- Site visit conducted by BSE confirmed company's absence at registered address

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing listing compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in securities of Winpro Industries Limited from November 24, 2025
- Investors should take note of the trading prohibition
- All market participants must comply with the suspension order

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Complete halt of trading activity in the company's securities, preventing all buy and sell transactions.

**Investor Impact:** Existing shareholders cannot exit their positions through the exchange. The suspension raises serious concerns about the company's operational status and governance.

**Compliance Risk:** The company's failure to respond to show cause notices and absence from its registered office indicates severe compliance violations and potential regulatory action beyond trading suspension.

**Trading Members:** Must update their systems to block any trading attempts in this security and inform affected clients.
