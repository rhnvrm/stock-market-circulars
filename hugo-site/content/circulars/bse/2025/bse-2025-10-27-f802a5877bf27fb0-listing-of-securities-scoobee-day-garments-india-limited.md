## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 3,420,028 new equity shares of SCOOBEE DAY GARMENTS (INDIA) LIMITED (Scrip Code: 531234) issued on a rights basis. The shares will be available for trading from October 28, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,420,028 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Shares issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- per share (total price Rs. 90/-)
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE962E01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 13500001 to 16920028
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading commences: October 28, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes effective October 28, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The rights issue increases the equity capital of Scoobee Day Garments (India) Limited by approximately 3.42 million shares. The impact is restricted to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue and will not significantly affect broader market operations or trading activities.
