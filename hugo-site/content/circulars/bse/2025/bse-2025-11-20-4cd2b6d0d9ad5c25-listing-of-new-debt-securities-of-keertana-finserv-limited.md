## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited issued on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 20, 2025. The securities are Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) with 11.20% annual interest rate, maturing on December 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Keertana Finserv Limited
- Scrip Code: 976776
- Scrip ID: 112KFL26
- ISIN: INE0NES07188 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 7,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,365 per debenture
- Interest Rate: 11.20% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07188
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 18, 2025
- Listing Date: November 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from July 11, 2025 to December 11, 2026
- Redemption Date: December 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for privately placed debt securities with limited market-wide impact. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt market segment for institutional and qualified investors. The securities carry an investment-grade credit rating (BBB+/Stable) and offer monthly interest payments at 11.20% per annum. No put or call options are available on these instruments.
