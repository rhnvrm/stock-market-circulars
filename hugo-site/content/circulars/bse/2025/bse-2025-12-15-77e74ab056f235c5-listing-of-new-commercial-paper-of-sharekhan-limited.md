## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment with effect from December 15, 2025. The issue comprises 8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 400 crores, with a maturity period of 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 8,000 units (Rs. 400 crores aggregate)
- Scrip Code: 730773 (Scrip ID: SL151225)
- ISIN: INE211H14AJ4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,447.73 per unit
- Allotment Date: December 15, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 16, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd
- Trading: Dematerialized form only, standard denomination Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Market Lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE211H14AJ4
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for price quotations

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 16, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in short-term debt instruments. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in the debt market, particularly those seeking short-term investment opportunities with 3-month tenure.
