## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Rachit Prints Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544503 and trading symbol RACHIT with ISIN number INE0ZN101029.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Rachit Prints Limited
- Scrip Code: 544503
- Trading Symbol: RACHIT
- ISIN Number: INE0ZN101029
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Status: Approved for listing, awaiting commencement date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance requirements mentioned beyond standard listing procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Trading Commencement: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will provide Rachit Prints Limited access to public capital markets and offer investment opportunities in the SME segment. Specific listing date and trading details will follow in subsequent notices.
