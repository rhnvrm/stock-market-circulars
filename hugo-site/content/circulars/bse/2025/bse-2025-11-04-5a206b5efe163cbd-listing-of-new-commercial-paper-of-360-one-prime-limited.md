## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed three new Commercial Paper securities on the BSE Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 300 crores across all three instruments. All securities were allotted on November 3, 2025, with redemption dates scheduled for October 2026. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent for all three instruments.

## Key Points

- Three new Commercial Paper instruments listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 6,000 units (2,000 units per instrument)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- All instruments carry high credit ratings (CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+)
- Allotment date: November 3, 2025
- Trading effective from: November 4, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact and is relevant primarily to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in short-term money market instruments. The high credit ratings (A1+) across multiple rating agencies indicate strong creditworthiness of 360 ONE Prime Limited. The instruments provide investment options with tenors of approximately 11 months from allotment date. The involvement of HDFC Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying agent adds operational reliability. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers for seamless trading operations.
