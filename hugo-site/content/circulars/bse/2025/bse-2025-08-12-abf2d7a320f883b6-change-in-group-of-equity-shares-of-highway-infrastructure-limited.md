## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of Highway Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 544477) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group), effective August 28, 2025. This change follows the exchange's previous notice dated August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Highway Infrastructure Limited
- Scrip Code: 544477
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective date: Thursday, August 28, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be shifted from T Group (Trade for Trade segment) to B Group under the Rolling segment, changing the trading and settlement mechanism for the stock.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading practices accordingly. No specific compliance actions required beyond awareness of the new trading segment.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 12, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 11, 2025**: Date of previous related notice (20250811-30)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment will improve liquidity and allow for normal T+1 settlement cycle instead of immediate delivery requirements. This is generally positive for trading activity and investor participation in the stock.
