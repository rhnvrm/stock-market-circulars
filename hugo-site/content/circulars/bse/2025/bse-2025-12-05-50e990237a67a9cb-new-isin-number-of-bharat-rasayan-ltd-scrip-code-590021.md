## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE838B01021 for Bharat Rasayan Ltd (Scrip Code: 590021) following the sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10 to Rs.5. The new ISIN will be effective for all trades executed on or after the ex-date of December 12, 2025. This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20251204-10 dated December 04, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Bharat Rasayan Ltd (Scrip Code: 590021)
- New ISIN: INE838B01021
- Corporate Action: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Rs.5/-
- Ex-Date: December 12, 2025
- Document Reference: DR-776/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251205-21
- Continuation of previous notice dated December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding ISIN change due to stock split.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN INE838B01021 for equity shares of Rs.5/- face value
- All trades executed on or after December 12, 2025 must use the new ISIN
- Members should ensure proper mapping of the new ISIN in their trading and settlement systems before the ex-date
- Depository participants and investors should update their records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Ex-Date**: December 12, 2025 (effective date for new ISIN usage)
- **Previous Notice**: December 04, 2025 (Notice No. 20251204-10)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The stock split increases liquidity by reducing share price, making shares more accessible to retail investors. Trading volume may increase post-split.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members, clearing corporations, and depository participants must update systems with the new ISIN before December 12, 2025. Failure to update may result in trade rejections or settlement issues.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive double the number of shares at half the price. No change in total investment value. Investors must ensure their demat accounts reflect the correct ISIN post-split for seamless trading.
