## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 17, 2025. Two series of Commercial Papers have been listed with different maturity dates and quantities.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Papers listed by Bajaj Finance Limited
- Series 1: 500 units, face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing January 28, 2026 (ISIN: INE296A14C63)
- Series 2: 11,000 units, face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing February 13, 2026 (ISIN: INE296A14D54)
- Both series rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Date of allotment: November 14, 2025
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must comply with standard denomination requirements of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2025**: Allotment date for both CP series
- **November 17, 2025**: Listing and admission to trading on BSE Debt segment
- **January 28, 2026**: Redemption date for Series 1 (Scrip Code: 730399)
- **February 13, 2026**: Redemption date for Series 2 (Scrip Code: 730586)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Bajaj Finance Limited with short-term funding through Commercial Paper issuance. Trading members can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. No significant operational or market-wide implications.
