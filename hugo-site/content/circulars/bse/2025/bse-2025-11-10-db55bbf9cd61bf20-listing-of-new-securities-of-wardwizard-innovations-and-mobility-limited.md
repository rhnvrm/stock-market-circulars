## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 4,01,23,546 new equity shares of Wardwizard Innovations and Mobility Limited (Scrip Code: 538970) issued through a rights offering. The shares are issued at face value of Re. 1/- each with a premium of Rs. 10/-, totaling Rs. 11/- per share. Trading in these new securities will commence from Tuesday, November 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: WARDWIZARD INNOVATIONS AND MOBILITY LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 538970
- Number of shares: 4,01,23,546 equity shares
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 11/- per share
- Basis: Rights issue
- ISIN: INE945P01024
- Distinctive Numbers: 262052304 to 302175849
- Date of Allotment: October 30, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 11, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities issued through rights offering.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- The new shares will trade under the same scrip code (538970) and ISIN (INE945P01024)
- All existing trading rules and regulations applicable to the company's equity shares will apply to these new shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 11, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The listing of over 40 million new equity shares represents significant dilution for existing shareholders who did not participate in the rights issue. This approximately increases the equity base substantially.

**Trading Impact**: Medium impact on stock liquidity and price. The increase in share count may lead to:
- Enhanced liquidity in the counter
- Potential short-term price pressure due to increased supply
- Rights issue proceeds strengthen company's capital base

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders who subscribed to the rights issue will see their shares listed for trading. Non-participating shareholders face dilution. The shares rank pari-passu, ensuring equal rights for all equity holders.
