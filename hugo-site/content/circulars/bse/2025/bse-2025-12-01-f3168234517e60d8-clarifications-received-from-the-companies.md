## Summary

BSE received clarification letters from three companies—KSB Limited (Scrip Code: 500249), Best Agrolife Ltd (Scrip Code: 539660), and Halder Venture Limited—in response to BSE Surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements in their securities. All companies confirmed that there is no undisclosed price-sensitive information and that they have been compliant with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015.

## Key Points

- KSB Limited responded to BSE's query about increased trading volume, confirming no undisclosed material information
- KSB attributed the volume increase to market conditions and market-driven factors
- Best Agrolife Ltd confirmed all necessary disclosures have been made under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- Best Agrolife stated no pending information or price-sensitive announcements are pending disclosure
- Halder Venture Limited was also queried regarding price movement clarification (letter dated November 28, 2025)
- All companies reaffirmed commitment to timely disclosure of material events per regulatory requirements
- Companies confirmed they will promptly disclose any future material information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular documents routine compliance with existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 30 regarding disclosure of material events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE Surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed price-sensitive information exists
- Companies must clarify compliance status with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- Ongoing obligation to promptly disclose material events and price-sensitive information
- Companies must maintain regular communication with stock exchanges regarding events affecting operations/performance

## Important Dates

- December 1, 2025: KSB Limited's clarification letter submitted
- November 29, 2025: Best Agrolife Ltd's clarification letter submitted
- November 29, 2025: Halder Venture Limited's clarification letter submitted (referenced)
- November 28, 2025: BSE Surveillance queries issued to companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications with no material disclosures or new information affecting market sentiment.

**Operational Impact:** None. The circular is informational, documenting companies' responses to standard surveillance queries.

**Investor Impact:** Low. The clarifications confirm that observed price/volume movements were market-driven with no undisclosed corporate developments. Investors can be assured that companies are compliant with disclosure norms.

**Regulatory Impact:** Demonstrates active surveillance by BSE and compliance by listed entities with transparency requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations.
