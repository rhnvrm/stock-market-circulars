## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 3, 2025. The issue comprises 1000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each, credit rating of CARE A1+, and redemption date of January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Vivriti Capital Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,88,669.50/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730475
- Scrip ID: VCL311025
- ISIN: INE01HV14AD5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AD5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: October 31, 2025
- Listing Date: November 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds Rs.50 crore worth of short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Paper has a tenor of 91 days and is rated CARE A1+, indicating high credit quality. There is no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the trading parameters for operational purposes.
