## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on its Debt segment effective November 19, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling 500 units. The commercial papers are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 500 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,66,287 per unit
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 730600
- Scrip ID: IFPL181125
- ISIN: INE879F14LY8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14LY8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine debt instrument listing that adds liquidity to the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors. The listing expands the available commercial paper options in the debt market but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Relevant primarily to debt market participants and investors in short-term debt instruments.
