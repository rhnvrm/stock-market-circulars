## Summary

BSE has admitted two trading members: Bansal Shares And Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6970) as a new member with trading rights in Cash/Equity Derivatives segments, and JRK Stock Broking Pvt.Ltd. (Member No. 6132) has been granted additional segment membership in the Debt segment. Both admissions are subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Bansal Shares And Securities Private Limited admitted as new trading member (Member No. 6970)
- Authorized segments for Bansal: Cash/Equity Derivatives
- JRK Stock Broking Pvt.Ltd. (Member No. 6132) granted additional Debt segment membership
- Both admissions effective December 10, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notification of member admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members about new member admissions and segment additions. It does not affect trading operations, compliance obligations, or market structure for existing participants. The addition of new trading members may marginally increase market participation but does not trigger any immediate action requirements.
