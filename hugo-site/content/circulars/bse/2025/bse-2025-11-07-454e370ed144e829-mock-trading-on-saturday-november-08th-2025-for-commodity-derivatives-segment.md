## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 8th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The session will include testing of various functionalities including exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release. All trades from this mock session are for testing only and will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 8th, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Trading members can test third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will cover various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- All trades are for testing purposes only with no real obligations
- Members requested to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the same day
- Active participation from all members is encouraged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback for the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on November 8th, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk or BSE Tech Support team for queries or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **November 8th, 2025 (Saturday)**:
  - Log-in: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
  - Continuous Trading: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 04:00 PM to 04:10 PM
  - Feedback Submission Deadline: 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None - This is a mock trading session that does not affect actual market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Optional testing opportunity for trading members to validate their systems and familiarize themselves with platform functionalities. No actual financial obligations or settlements will arise from this session.

**Member Impact**: Beneficial for members using third-party or in-house trading systems to test their applications under simulated market conditions including exceptional scenarios.
