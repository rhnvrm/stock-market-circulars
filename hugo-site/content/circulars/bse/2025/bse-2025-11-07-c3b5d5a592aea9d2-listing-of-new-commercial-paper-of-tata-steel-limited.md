## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by Tata Steel Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 7, 2025. The instrument consists of 15,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 7,500 crores. The Commercial Paper has a tenure of approximately 3 months, maturing on February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Instrument: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Issuer: Tata Steel Limited
- Quantity: 15,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 7,500 crores
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,130 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730510
- Scrip ID: TSL061125
- ISIN: INE081A14GI5
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE081A14GI5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 6, 2025
- Listing Date: November 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 10, 2026
- Notice Date: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Tata Steel Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Trading is restricted to institutional segments with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs, limiting retail participation.
