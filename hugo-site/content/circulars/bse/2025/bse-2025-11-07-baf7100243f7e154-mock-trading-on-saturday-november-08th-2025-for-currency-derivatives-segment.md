## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms from empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications and various functionalities including exceptional market conditions. The mock trading will not result in any actual obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Currency Derivatives segment on November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Trading members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems through ETI APIs
- Mock trades will not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or create rights and liabilities
- Members are encouraged to actively participate and provide feedback by 5:00 PM

## Trading Session Timings

- **Log-in**: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading for Currency Derivatives**: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading for Cross Currency Derivatives**: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Trade Modification (Currency)**: 04:10 PM
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency)**: 04:10 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback for the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on the same day
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries
- Members can also reach out to their respective Relationship Managers for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: November 08, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Feedback Deadline**: 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational activity with no actual market impact. The mock trading session serves purely as a testing and familiarization exercise for trading members. There are no financial obligations, settlement processes, or actual trading consequences. This allows members to ensure their systems are functioning properly and to test various scenarios including exceptional market conditions in a risk-free environment.
