## Summary

BSE has listed 60 lakh new equity shares of Goyal Associates Ltd (Scrip Code: 530663) with effect from August 14, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 2.50 per share (face value Re. 1 plus premium of Rs. 1.50) to non-promoters through preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 60,00,000 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 2.50 per share (Re. 1 face value + Rs. 1.50 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Securities created through conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 46937501 to 52937500
- ISIN: INE912B01032

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for new equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period applicable until February 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 31, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry:** February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares for a small company. The lock-in period until February 2026 means these shares cannot be traded immediately by allottees, providing stability to the stock price.
