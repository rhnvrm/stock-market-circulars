## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 17, 2025. Two series of Commercial Papers have been listed with different maturity dates and quantities.

## Key Points

- **First Series (ISIN: INE296A14C63)**
  - Quantity: 500 units
  - Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
  - Issue Price: Rs. 4,93,410
  - Scrip Code: 730399
  - Scrip ID: BFL151025B
  - Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
  - Redemption Date: January 28, 2026
  - Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+

- **Second Series (ISIN: INE296A14D54)**
  - Quantity: 11,000 units
  - Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
  - Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,026.50
  - Scrip Code: 730586
  - Scrip ID: BFL141125
  - Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
  - Redemption Date: February 13, 2026
  - Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+

- ICICI Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent
- Market Lot: 1 unit for both series
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members must use the designated scrip codes and ISINs for transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 17, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Both Series):** November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date (First Series):** January 28, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Second Series):** February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands the available Commercial Paper instruments in the BSE Debt segment for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can contact BSE Debt Department for clarifications.
