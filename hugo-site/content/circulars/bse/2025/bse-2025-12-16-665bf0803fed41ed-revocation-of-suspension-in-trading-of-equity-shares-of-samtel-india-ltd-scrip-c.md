## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371). This circular indicates that the previous trading suspension has been lifted and normal trading activities can resume for this security.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension has been revoked for Samtel India Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 500371
- Normal trading operations to resume
- Circular issued on December 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension of trading that was previously imposed on Samtel India Ltd.'s equity shares has been lifted. This restores normal trading status for the security on BSE platforms.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the revocation of suspension
- Resume normal trading operations for Samtel India Ltd. equity shares
- Ensure compliance with standard trading regulations applicable to the security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: As per BSE notification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revocation allows shareholders and investors to resume trading in Samtel India Ltd. shares, restoring liquidity and price discovery for the security.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and market participants need to update their systems to reflect the change in trading status for this scrip.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders can now trade their holdings in Samtel India Ltd., which was previously restricted due to the suspension.
