## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of further securities issued by 19 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading from Monday, September 8, 2025, with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 19 companies are listing additional equity securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total securities range from 2,384 shares (RateGain) to 2,309,228 shares (SBFC Finance)
- All securities have no lock-in period restrictions
- Face values vary from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share
- Securities span across various sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, finance, and technology

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Companies must ensure proper distribution of securities within specified ranges
- Standard listing compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Monday, September 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP listings. The securities represent employee compensation and are typically held for longer periods. No lock-in restrictions may provide immediate liquidity but volumes are generally low for such securities.
