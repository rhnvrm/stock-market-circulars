## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the 10% Non-Convertible Debenture of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (ISIN: INE0BUS07BB1, Code: 939889) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is imposed due to the company setting a record date of October 25, 2025 for the purpose of interest payment and redemption of the debenture. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE0BUS07BB1
- BSE Code: 939889 (IML-10%-9-11-25-NCD)
- Record Date: October 25, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debenture and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: 20251020-39
- Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure to suspend trading prior to record date for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debenture (INE0BUS07BB1) from October 24, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No dealing allowed in the security until further notice from the exchange

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **October 25, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture of INDEL MONEY LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and standard procedure for corporate actions.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of October 25, 2025 will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading opportunity available from October 24, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security and inform clients holding this debenture about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
