## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on a private placement basis. The listing is effective from November 11, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment. A total of 8000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been listed.

## Key Points

- Security: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Issuer: Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited
- Quantity: 8000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,363.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730529
- Scrip ID: ABRE111125
- Detail Name: ABREL-10-02-26-CP
- ISIN: INE055A14KJ9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: SBI
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE055A14KJ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides debt market participants with an additional short-term investment option (91-day commercial paper) from Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited with strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL). The impact is limited to debt market participants interested in commercial paper investments and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
