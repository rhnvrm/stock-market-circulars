## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL) has announced a mandatory mock session on December 20, 2025 to test the Two-way Software as a Service (SaaS) portability using the ICCL@NCL instance. This is part of the ongoing business continuity preparedness for clearing corporations. All ICCL clearing members must participate in testing the functionality.

## Key Points

- Mock session scheduled for December 20, 2025 to test Two-way SaaS portability
- SaaS instance ICCL@NCL will be invoked at approximately 2:00 PM
- After invocation, members cannot log into regular RTRMS system
- Members must use ICCL@NCL instance for N-MASS, CIM, and NCMS.exe with existing credentials
- Testing follows previous circulars from March 2023, April 2023, and December 2023
- Detailed mock timings available in BSE circulars 20251218-51, 20251218-52, and 20251218-53

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing business continuity framework for clearing corporations through SaaS mode established in earlier circulars from 2023.

## Compliance Requirements

- **All ICCL clearing members** must participate in the mock session
- Members must test functionality using the SaaS instance ICCL@NCL
- Access systems through specified SaaS links:
  - Cash deposit (CIM): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/CIM/
  - Position and collateral allocation (NMASS): https://rtrmssaas.icclindia.com/
- Use existing login credentials provided previously for ICCL@NCL instance
- Queries should be directed to SaaS.Ops@icclindia.com or risk.monitoring@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 20, 2025**: Mock session date
- **Approximately 2:00 PM (December 20)**: SaaS instance ICCL@NCL invocation time
- Refer to BSE circulars dated December 18, 2025 for detailed mock timings

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All ICCL clearing members will experience temporary system access changes during the mock session. Members will be unable to access the regular RTRMS system after 2:00 PM and must switch to the SaaS instance.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a planned testing exercise with no direct impact on trading activities. However, it is critical for ensuring business continuity capabilities.

**Preparation Required**: Members must ensure they have access to previously provided ICCL@NCL credentials and familiarize themselves with the SaaS portal links before the mock session.

**Purpose**: Tests the clearing corporation's ability to maintain operations through alternative infrastructure in case of primary system failure, ensuring market resilience and continuity of clearing and settlement operations.
