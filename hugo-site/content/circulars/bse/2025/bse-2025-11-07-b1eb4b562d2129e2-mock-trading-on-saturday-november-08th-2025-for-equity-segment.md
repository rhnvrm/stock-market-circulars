## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session provides trading members, including those using third-party platforms, an opportunity to test their trading applications and various functionalities under different market conditions. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS release accompanying this session
- Available for testing various functionalities including call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals
- Third-party trading platform users can participate
- Feedback requested from all members by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

**Login:** 11:15 AM - 11:45 AM

**Morning Block Deal Window:** 11:45 AM - 12:00 PM

**Pre-Open Session:**
- Order Entry: 12:00 PM - 12:08 PM (random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- Matching Period: 12:08 PM - 12:15 PM

**Continuous Trading:**
- T+1: 12:15 PM - 04:00 PM
- T+0: 12:15 PM - 02:30 PM
- SPOS Scrips: 01:00 PM - 04:00 PM

**Special Pre-Open for IPO & Re-listed Scrips:**
- Order Entry: 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM (random stoppage in last 10 minutes)
- Matching Period: 12:45 PM - 01:00 PM

**Periodic Call Auction:** 12:30 PM - 03:30 PM (3 sessions of 1 hour each)

**Auction for Settlement Details:**
- Offer Entry and Matching: 01:00 PM - 01:45 PM

**Afternoon Block Deal Window:** 01:50 PM - 02:05 PM

**Closing:** 04:00 PM - 04:10 PM

**Post-Closing:** 04:10 PM - 04:20 PM

**Trade Modification:**
- T+1: Until 04:20 PM
- T+0: 02:45 PM

## Testing Capabilities

Members can test various market conditions and functionalities including:
- Various types of call auction sessions
- Risk-reduction mode
- Trading halt scenarios
- Block deals execution
- Exceptional market conditions
