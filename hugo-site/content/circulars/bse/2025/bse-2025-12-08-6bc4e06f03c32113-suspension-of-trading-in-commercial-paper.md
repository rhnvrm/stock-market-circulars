## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 730205, ISIN: INE895B14388) effective from December 09, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730205
- ISIN: INE895B14388
- Instrument: RCL-10-12-25-CP
- Effective Date: December 09, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Reference: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE895B14388) from December 09, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new positions should be created in this instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 09, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: December 10, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - This is a single commercial paper instrument suspension due to natural maturity. It affects only holders and potential traders of this specific debt security.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members must ensure compliance systems block trading in this ISIN from the effective date. Holders of the commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds as per terms.

**Issuer**: RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED - This represents normal debt market operations as the commercial paper reaches its scheduled maturity date.
