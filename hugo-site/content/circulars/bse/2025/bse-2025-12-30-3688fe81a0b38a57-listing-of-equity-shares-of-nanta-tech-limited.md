## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of NANTA TECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544668 and symbol NANTA with ISIN INE0YJA01011. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- NANTA TECH LIMITED to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544668
- Symbol: NANTA
- ISIN: INE0YJA01011
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal market impact. The circular serves as advance notification to trading members about the upcoming addition of a new SME stock. Actual trading will commence only after the listing date is formally announced. Impact is limited to investors interested in SME segment stocks and market participants tracking new listings.
