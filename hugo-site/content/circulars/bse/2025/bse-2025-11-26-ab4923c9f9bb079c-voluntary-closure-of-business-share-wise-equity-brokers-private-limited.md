## Summary

BSE has announced that Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6762) will voluntarily cease business operations across all trading segments effective November 27, 2025. The closure affects the firm's operations in Cash, Equity derivatives, and Currency derivative segments.

## Key Points

- Member Name: Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited
- Member Number: 6762
- Effective Date: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Affected Segments: Cash, Equity derivatives, Currency derivatives
- Notice Number: 20251126-19
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a member exit notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for their records. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 - Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited ceases all trading operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine member exit affecting a single broker firm. Other trading members are notified for informational purposes. Clients of Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited will need to transfer their accounts to other brokers before the effective date.
