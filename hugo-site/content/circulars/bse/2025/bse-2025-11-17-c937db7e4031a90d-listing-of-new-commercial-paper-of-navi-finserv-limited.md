## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The listing comprises 200 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1 credit rating. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent for these instruments.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Navi Finserv Limited
- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 200 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,88,497 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 10 crore (200 Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Scrip Code**: 730581
- **Scrip ID**: NFL141125
- **ISIN**: INE342T14EZ0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14EZ0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarifications may contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds short-term debt instruments to BSE's debt segment, providing liquidity options for debt market participants. The CARE A1 rating indicates adequate safety for short-term obligations. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and institutional investors interested in short-term paper. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
