## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of KOTAK MAHINDRA INVESTMENTS LIMITED (ISIN: INE975F07IN7, Code: 975167, Series: KMIL-8.2134) effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for payment of interest and redemption of the non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: KOTAK MAHINDRA INVESTMENTS LIMITED
- Debenture Series: KMIL-8.2134
- ISIN: INE975F07IN7
- BSE Code: 975167
- Record Date: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- No Dealings From: December 4, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Members are advised to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the affected debenture series during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 4, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption; trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and necessary to facilitate the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems block trading in the affected debentures from the specified date. Clients holding these debentures should be informed about the redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from December 4, 2025 onwards, which is standard for instruments being redeemed.
