## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 50,029,335 equity shares of Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884) issued on a rights basis. The shares will be available for trading on the exchange starting Monday, December 1, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884)
- Number of shares: 50,029,335 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2.00 per share
- Issue premium: Rs. 1.00 per share
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE671T01028
- Distinctive numbers: 50029336 to 100058670
- Date of allotment: November 25, 2025
- Listing and trading effective date: December 1, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the listing of these new securities and should ensure their systems are updated to reflect the availability of these shares for trading from December 1, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 1, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action specific to Darshan Orna Limited with minimal market-wide impact. The rights issue increases the company's equity base by 50,029,335 shares. Trading members should be aware of the additional shares available for trading from December 1, 2025. Investors should note that these new shares carry the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares.
