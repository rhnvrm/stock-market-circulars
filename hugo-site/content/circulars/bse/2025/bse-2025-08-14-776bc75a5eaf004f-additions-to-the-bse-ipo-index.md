## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies - All Time Plastics Limited (ticker: 544479) and JSW Cement Limited (ticker: 544480) - to the BSE IPO Index, effective at market open on Tuesday, August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- All Time Plastics Limited (Exchange ticker: 544479) listed on BSE on August 14, 2025
- JSW Cement Limited (Exchange ticker: 544480) listed on BSE on August 14, 2025
- Both stocks will be added to BSE IPO Index effective August 19, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance action following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and fund managers tracking the BSE IPO Index may need to adjust portfolios accordingly.

## Important Dates

- August 14, 2025: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- August 19, 2025: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative action following new listings. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to incorporate these securities into their portfolios on the effective date.
