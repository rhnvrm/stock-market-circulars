## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED on its Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The company has issued 5,000 non-convertible debentures on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 10.85% per annum with monthly interest payments and a one-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 5,000 debentures
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code: 977318
- Scrip ID: 1085MGFL26
- ISIN: INE08KJ07159
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Interest Rate: 10.85% per annum (paid monthly)
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Market Lot: 1
- Securities must be traded in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07159
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 27, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from December 26, 2025 to December 26, 2026
- Date of Redemption: December 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to privately placed debt securities of a single entity. The impact is limited to:
- Debt market participants and institutional investors who may trade these securities
- Existing and potential investors in MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED debt instruments
- The securities provide a debt investment option with ACUITE A- rating and 10.85% annual return over a one-year period
- No put/call options are available on these securities
