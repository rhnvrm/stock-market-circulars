## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window - Takeover) for SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD. The offer will be conducted through a live OTB session from Friday, November 28, 2025 to Thursday, December 11, 2025. Trading members and custodian members can participate through iBBS and RTRMS platforms.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD
- **Symbol**: SOMAPPR
- **ISIN**: INE737E01011
- **Scrip Code**: 516038
- **Offer Size**: Up to 4,26,58,200 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Friday, November 28, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, December 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements existing OTB procedures as per Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodians:

1. **UCC Registration**: Ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
2. **Demat Requirement**: Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. **Platform Access**: Use designated URLs for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (Internet and Leased Line options available)
4. **File Processing**: Note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
5. **Confirmation**: Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids available only after confirmation received from Merchant Banker/RTA after issue closure

### Investor Categories for Bidding:

- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD shareholders. The offer does not impact broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians handling SOMAPPR shares need to ensure:
- Proper UCC registration and updates
- Platform access (iBBS/RTRMS) is configured
- Client demat requirements are verified
- Bidding processes are executed within specified timelines

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD shareholders who wish to participate in the takeover offer at Rs. 10 per share.
