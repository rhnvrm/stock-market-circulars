## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Shriram Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 9, 2025. The listing comprises 7,500 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 7.6% annual interest rate and maturing on December 8, 2032. The securities are rated CRISIL AA+/Stable and IND AA+/Stable, and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Listed**: 7,500 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 (at par)
- **Scrip Code**: 977349
- **Scrip ID**: 76SFL32
- **ISIN**: INE721A07SR6
- **Interest Rate**: 7.6% per annum (paid annually)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AA+/Stable, IND AA+/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE721A07SR6
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 8, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Annually on December 8 each year
- **Final Interest Payment & Redemption**: December 8, 2032

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt securities listing with limited market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for debt investors. Provides an investment opportunity in AA+ rated corporate debt from Shriram Finance Limited with a 7.6% annual return over a 7-year tenure.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members handling debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details (Scrip Code: 977349, ISIN: INE721A07SR6) for trading and settlement purposes.
