## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that T-Bill with ISIN IN002024Z370 (364TB261225, Scrip Code 805006) will be suspended from trading effective December 23, 2025, as it matures on December 26, 2025. This is a routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments.

## Key Points

- T-Bill with ISIN IN002024Z370 (364TB261225) to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 805006
- Trading suspension effective from December 23, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption Date: December 26, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from December 23, 2025
- All existing positions should be settled or closed before the suspension date
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single T-Bill approaching maturity. The 364-day treasury bill has completed its tenor and is being delisted as per standard procedures.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members have one business day notice to wind down positions. The suspension affects only one specific debt instrument in the T-Bills segment.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Holders of this T-Bill will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No trading disruption for other debt instruments.
