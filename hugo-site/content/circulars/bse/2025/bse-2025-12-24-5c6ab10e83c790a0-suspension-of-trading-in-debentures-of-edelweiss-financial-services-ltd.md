## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures effective from December 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to EFSL-GSEC-13-1-26-PVT series debentures
- ISIN: INE532F07BT8 (Code: 960419)
- Record date fixed for redemption: December 29, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 29, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-786/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension in accordance with corporate action requirements for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- All dealings in ISIN INE532F07BT8 must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This suspension affects only a specific series of non-convertible debentures and is part of the normal redemption process. Equity trading in Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. remains unaffected.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Only holders of the specific debenture series (INE532F07BT8) are impacted. The suspension facilitates the redemption process and ensures proper settlement for debenture holders as of the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Routine corporate action. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the suspended instrument from December 29, 2025.
