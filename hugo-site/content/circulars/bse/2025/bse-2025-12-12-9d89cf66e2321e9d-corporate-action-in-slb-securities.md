## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions for two securities available in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) scheme. Can Fin Homes Ltd. will undergo dividend distribution with a record date of December 19, 2025, while Nuvama Wealth Management Limited will implement a stock split effective December 26, 2025. Both actions will have associated shut periods during which SLB transactions will be restricted.

## Key Points

- Can Fin Homes Ltd. dividend action with record date and ex-date on December 19, 2025
- Nuvama Wealth Management Limited stock split with record date and ex-date on December 26, 2025
- Shut periods announced for both securities to facilitate corporate action processing
- Foreclosure activities will be restricted during specified shut periods
- Both corporate actions affect securities available in the SLB scheme

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine notification of corporate actions affecting SLB securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants must not enter into new lending/borrowing transactions for affected securities during shut periods
- Market participants should note the foreclosure restrictions during specified dates
- Brokers and clearing members must inform clients about the corporate action timelines
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled before the shut period commences

## Important Dates

**Can Fin Homes Ltd.:**
- Record Date: December 19, 2025
- Ex-Date: December 19, 2025
- Shut Period: December 18-19, 2025

**Nuvama Wealth Management Limited:**
- Record Date: December 26, 2025
- Ex-Date: December 26, 2025
- Shut Period: December 24-26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The corporate actions will temporarily restrict SLB activities for two securities during their respective shut periods. The dividend action affects Can Fin Homes shareholders, while the stock split will change the trading dynamics for Nuvama Wealth Management shares.

**Operational Impact:** SLB participants must adjust their lending/borrowing strategies around the shut periods. The stock split for Nuvama will require system adjustments for updated share quantities and prices. Shareholders of Can Fin Homes will receive dividend payments based on holdings as of the record date.

**Trading Impact:** Normal trading will continue, but SLB transactions will be suspended during shut periods. The ex-dates will see price adjustments reflecting the dividend payout and stock split respectively.
