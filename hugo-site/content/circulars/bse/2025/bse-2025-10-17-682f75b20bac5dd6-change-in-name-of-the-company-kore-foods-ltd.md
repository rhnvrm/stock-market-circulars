## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Kore Foods Limited. The company name has been officially changed to Team24 Consumer Products Limited effective August 5, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed by the Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L33208GA1983PLC000520
- Original name: KORE FOODS LIMITED
- New name: TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED
- Effective date of name change: August 5, 2025
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Certificate digitally signed by Brijesh Kain, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered address: H.No. 575/1C/G-1 Cujira, Santa Cruz, North Goa, Panjim, Santa Cruz, Tiswadi, North Goa- 403005, Goa, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Kore Foods Limited) along with the new name (Team24 Consumer Products Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **August 5, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Kore Foods Limited to Team24 Consumer Products Limited
- **August 5, 2027**: End of two-year period for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This name change has minimal market impact as it is purely administrative. The certificate explicitly states that the name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. The company will continue operations under the new name with all existing obligations and contracts remaining valid. Investors and trading members should update their records to reflect the new company name while being aware that both names will be displayed for the next two years.
