## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as KORE FOODS LIMITED. The company name has been officially changed to TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED with effect from August 5, 2025. The company maintains its Corporate Identification Number (CIN) L33208GA1983PLC000520 and remains a company limited by shares.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from KORE FOODS LIMITED to TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED
- Change effective from August 5, 2025
- CIN remains unchanged: L33208GA1983PLC000520
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered office location: H.No. 575/1C/G-1 Cujira, Santa Cruz, North Goa, Panjim, Santa Cruz, Tiswadi, North Goa- 403005, Goa, India
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Form approved by Brijesh Kain, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action processed under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (KORE FOODS LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name (TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED) at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: August 5, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Display Requirement Period**: Two years from August 5, 2025 (until August 5, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for future reference and transactions. The company will operate under the new name TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED while maintaining all existing legal obligations and shareholder rights.
