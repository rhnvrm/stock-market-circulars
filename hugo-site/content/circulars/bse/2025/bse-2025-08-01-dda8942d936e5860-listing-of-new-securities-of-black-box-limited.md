## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 2,27,626 new equity shares of BLACK BOX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 500463) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading from Monday, August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,27,626 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 415/-
- Issue price: Rs. 417/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 169565751 to 169793376
- ISIN: INE676A01027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Shares subject to lock-in restrictions until February 15, 2026
- All 2,27,626 shares under lock-in (Dist. Nos. 169565751-169793376)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 4, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected due to:
- Small issue size relative to overall market
- Complete lock-in of new shares until February 2026
- Preferential issue to non-promoters with no immediate trading liquidity
- Standard warrant conversion process with no unusual terms
