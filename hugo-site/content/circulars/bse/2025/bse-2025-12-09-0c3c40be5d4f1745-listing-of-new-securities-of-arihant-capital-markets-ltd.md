## Summary

BSE has listed 500,000 new equity shares of Arihant Capital Markets Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511605) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are listed with effect from December 10, 2025, and are subject to lock-in restrictions until June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 500,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 86/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 87/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 104112801 to 104612800
- ISIN: INE420B01036
- Date of allotment: September 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are permitted for trading from December 10, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until June 25, 2026
- All 500,000 shares are subject to lock-in provisions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 23, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment representing a relatively small number of shares. The lock-in period of approximately 6.5 months ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, preventing potential dilution effects in the near term. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining uniform rights across all shareholders.
