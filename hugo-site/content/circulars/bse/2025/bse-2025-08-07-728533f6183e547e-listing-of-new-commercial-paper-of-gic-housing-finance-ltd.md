## Summary

GIC Housing Finance Ltd has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 175 crores (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Paper was issued on private placement basis with allotment date August 6, 2025 and redemption date February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 3,500 units (Total value: Rs. 175 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729974
- ISIN: INE289B14JU1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,259/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 6, 2025
- Listing Date: August 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for GIC Housing Finance Ltd with strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA).
