## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 1, 2025. The instrument comprises 4000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 4000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,703.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730671
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF281125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FN9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FN9
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine debt instrument listing for short-term commercial paper. The listing provides ABHFL with short-term funding through the debt market and offers institutional investors a highly-rated (A1+) short-duration investment option. No impact on equity markets or trading members' operations beyond standard debt trading protocols.
