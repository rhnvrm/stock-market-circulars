## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SILVERSTORM PARKS AND RESORTS LIMITED on its SME website for public review and comments. The document was filed on December 28, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SILVERSTORM PARKS AND RESORTS LIMITED on December 28, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website (www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx) for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from December 29, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations for SME IPO Draft Offer Document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from December 29, 2025
- The company and intermediaries must ensure the document remains available for public access for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 28, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by SILVERSTORM PARKS AND RESORTS LIMITED
- **December 29, 2025**: Notice date - public comment period begins
- **January 19, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a procedural notice for a single SME IPO Draft Offer Document filing.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for prospective investors interested in SILVERSTORM PARKS AND RESORTS LIMITED SME IPO. Provides opportunity to review the offer document and submit comments before final offer document is published.
