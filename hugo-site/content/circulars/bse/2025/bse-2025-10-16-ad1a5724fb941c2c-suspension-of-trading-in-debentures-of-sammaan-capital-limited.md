## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in five series of non-convertible debentures issued by SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED effective October 20, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Five series of non-convertible debentures affected
- Trading suspension effective from October 20, 2025
- Record date set for October 20, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- All affected securities carry ISIN codes and BSE scrip codes
- Notice reference: DR-739/2025-2026

## Affected Securities

**SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED - Five Debenture Series:**

1. **SCL-8.80%-03-11-25-NCD** (ISIN: INE148I07MQ0, Code: 938240)
2. **SCL-9.3%-3-11-25-NCD** (ISIN: INE148I07MR8, Code: 938242)
3. **SC-03-11-25-NCD** (ISIN: INE148I07MT4, Code: 938244)
4. **SCL-03-11-25-NCD** (ISIN: INE148I07MS6, Code: 938248)
5. **SCL-8.94%-03-11-25-NCD** (ISIN: INE148I07MY4, Code: 938250)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption and interest payment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from October 20, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- Adherence to record date provisions for determining eligible debenture holders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 20, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** October 20, 2025
- **Purpose Date:** Redemption and interest payment as per debenture terms (November 3, 2025 based on security naming)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market segment only, specifically investors and traders holding or dealing in SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED debentures.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the five affected debenture series from October 20, 2025.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading opportunity available from the suspension date, ensuring fair treatment of all holders as of record date.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary removal of five debt securities from trading, though impact is limited given these are redemption-stage securities with imminent maturity dates.
