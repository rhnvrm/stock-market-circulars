## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement for Settlement No. 775 dated December 12, 2025. This circular lists securities across various companies that are subject to auction settlement, including the scrip codes, company names, and quantities involved in the auction process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 775
- Settlement Date: December 12, 2025
- Total securities listed: Over 115 different scrip codes
- Quantities range from 1 share to 199,999 shares depending on the security
- Notable large quantities include: Spright Agro (199,999 shares), Chartered Logistics (10,400 shares), Sattva Sukunj (54,354 shares)
- Auction covers diverse sectors including energy, pharmaceuticals, technology, metals, and FMCG

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must settle obligations for the listed securities as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed securities must participate in the auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per standard T+2 settlement cycle norms

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: 775

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction settlement with low market impact. The auction mechanism is a standard process to ensure settlement of trades where sellers fail to deliver securities. Impact is limited to:

- Specific trading members with outstanding delivery obligations
- Price discovery for the listed securities during the auction window
- Settlement guarantee fund may be utilized if auction is unsuccessful
- Routine operational process with no broader market implications
