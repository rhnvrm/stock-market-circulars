## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for company with CIN L27320GJ2022PLC135697. The company name has been changed from SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED to DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED with effect from September 29, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L27320GJ2022PLC135697
- Old Name: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED
- New Name: DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED
- Original Name at Incorporation: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved and digitally signed by Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company registered address: 5, Harekrishana Industrial Estate, Bakrol, Daskroi, Ahmedabad-382430, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action for name change under the Companies Act, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display its old name (SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name in terms of Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Digitally Signed**: October 8, 2025 at 15:12:01 IST
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from effective date (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative corporate action that does not affect:
- Company operations or business activities
- Rights and liabilities of shareholders and stakeholders
- Trading of securities
- Corporate identity number (CIN) remains unchanged

The name change appears to reflect a business focus shift from metals and alloys to kitchen utensils and appliances. Stakeholders should update their records with the new company name while noting that the old name must be displayed alongside the new name for the mandatory two-year period.
