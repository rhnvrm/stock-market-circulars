## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of MUTHOOT CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. (ISIN: INE296G07200, Code: 976157) effective November 19, 2025. The suspension is imposed due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MCSL-9.97%-05-12-25-PVT debentures
- ISIN: INE296G07200, BSE Code: 976157
- Suspension effective from November 19, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Number: 20251110-55 (DR-759/2025-2026)
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified MUTHOOT CAPITAL SERVICES debentures from November 19, 2025
- Trading members must ensure no transactions are executed in ISIN INE296G07200 from the specified date
- Members must inform their clients holding or trading these debentures about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 19, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders and traders of specific MUTHOOT CAPITAL SERVICES debentures. The suspension affects only one debenture series (MCSL-9.97%-05-12-25-PVT) and does not impact broader debt market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and principal redemption as per the company's announcement. Investors will be unable to trade these debentures from November 19, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and inform affected clients about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
