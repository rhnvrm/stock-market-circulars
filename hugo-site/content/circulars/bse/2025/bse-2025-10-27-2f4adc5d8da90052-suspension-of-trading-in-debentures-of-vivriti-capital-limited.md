## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED effective October 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for the redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Suspension applies to debenture series VCL-3M MIBOR+SPREAD-14-11-25-P (ISIN: INE01HV07486, Scrip Code: 975917)
- Record date fixed: October 30, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: October 30, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-746/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension procedure for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 30, 2025
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive
- Only applicable to the specific debenture series mentioned (INE01HV07486)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects only holders and traders of the specific VIVRITI CAPITAL debenture series. The suspension is temporary and directly related to the corporate action of redemption and interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading members dealing in this specific debt instrument must halt trading activities. Standard corporate action procedure with clear timeline and communication.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest as per the record date. Trading liquidity in this instrument ceases from the suspension date, but this is a planned corporate action with advance notice.
