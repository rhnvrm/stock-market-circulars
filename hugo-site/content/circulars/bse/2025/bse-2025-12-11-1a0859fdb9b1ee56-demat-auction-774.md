## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 774 dated December 11, 2025. The circular lists 90 scrips across various sectors where securities will be auctioned due to delivery obligations. The quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 432,980 shares for different companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 774
- Date: December 11, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 90 securities
- Includes companies across various sectors including power, sugar, chemicals, real estate, technology, and consumer goods
- Highest quantity listed: Magellanic Cloud (432,980 shares)
- Other significant quantities include: Spright Agro (300,002 shares), Captain Pipe (150,000 shares), SVS Ventures (12,000 shares)
- Mix of large-cap and small-cap stocks affected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and trading members must ensure settlement of the listed securities through the auction mechanism
- Parties with delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's standard auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 11, 2025
- Settlement Number: 774

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 90 different securities with varying quantities. While this is a routine auction settlement, the large number of scrips and some significant quantities (especially Magellanic Cloud with 432,980 shares) could create temporary price pressure.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers and members with open positions in these securities must participate in the auction mechanism, requiring operational coordination and potential additional margin requirements.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Primarily affects brokers and trading members with delivery obligations. Retail investors holding these stocks may see temporary volatility during auction settlement.
