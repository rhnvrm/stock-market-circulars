## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document for DIKSHA POLYMERS LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on December 16, 2025, is now available on the exchange website for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by DIKSHA POLYMERS LIMITED on December 16, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public may submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document will remain available on the SME website for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: December 16, 2025
- Notice date: December 17, 2025
- Public comment period: 21 days from December 17, 2025 (deadline: January 7, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it relates specifically to DIKSHA POLYMERS LIMITED's SME IPO process. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement for SME IPO filings under ICDR Regulations. Potential investors and market participants interested in this specific SME offering should review the document during the comment period.
