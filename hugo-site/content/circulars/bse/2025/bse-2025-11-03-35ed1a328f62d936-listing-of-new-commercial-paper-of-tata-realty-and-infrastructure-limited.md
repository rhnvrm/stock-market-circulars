## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 5,200 Commercial Papers issued by Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 3, 2025. Each Commercial Paper has a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,200 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,365
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 730474
- Scrip ID: TRIL311025
- ISIN Number: INE371K14DB1
- Credit Rating: CARE A+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE371K14DB1
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Securities will trade under Scrip Code 730474 and Scrip ID TRIL311025

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 31, 2025
- Listing Date: November 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term financing instruments for institutional and high-net-worth investors in the debt segment. The 91-day commercial paper tenure indicates standard short-term borrowing requirements for Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
