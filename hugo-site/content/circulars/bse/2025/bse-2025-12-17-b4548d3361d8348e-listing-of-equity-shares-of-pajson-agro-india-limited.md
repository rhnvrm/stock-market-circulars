## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Pajson Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544657, symbol PAJSON, and ISIN INE14LM01012. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Pajson Agro India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Trading Symbol: PAJSON
- ISIN Number: INE14LM01012
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and detailed security information to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for the new listing
- Await further notice regarding the specific listing date and security details
- Prepare systems for trading once the listing date is announced

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact. This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. Trading members and investors interested in the SME segment should watch for the subsequent notice with the actual listing date and security details before trading can commence.
