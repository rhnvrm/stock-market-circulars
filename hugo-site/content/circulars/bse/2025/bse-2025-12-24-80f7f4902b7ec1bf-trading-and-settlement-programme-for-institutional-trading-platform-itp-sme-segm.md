## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME Segment for January 2026. The circular provides the complete settlement calendar covering 21 trading days from January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026, with corresponding pay-in and pay-out dates following a T+1 settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Settlement period covers January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- 21 settlement cycles scheduled for the month (ITP-189/25-26 through ITP-209/25-26)
- T+1 settlement cycle maintained (trading day + 1 business day for settlement)
- Pay-in deadline: 9:50 AM (members must submit instructions to depositories/banks)
- Pay-in time: 10:00 AM
- Pay-out time: 11:00 AM (funds and securities)
- Depository settlement numbers: 2526189 through 2526209

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine settlement schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to depositories/banks latest by 9:50 AM on settlement day
- Pay-in must be completed at 10:00 AM
- Settlement applies to Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Important Dates

**Settlement Schedule (Selected dates):**
- January 1, 2026 (Trading) → January 2, 2026 (Settlement)
- January 2, 2026 (Trading) → January 5, 2026 (Settlement)
- January 23, 2026 (Trading) → January 27, 2026 (Settlement)
- January 30, 2026 (Trading) → February 2, 2026 (Settlement)

**Non-trading days observed:** January 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-26, 31 (weekends and holidays)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is a routine administrative circular providing the monthly settlement calendar for market participants trading on the ITP-SME platform. The schedule maintains standard T+1 settlement procedures with no changes to existing operational framework. Members can plan their settlement obligations and liquidity management for January 2026 based on this calendar.
