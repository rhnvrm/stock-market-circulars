## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 17, 2025. The issue comprises 9,200 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 460 crores, with a tenor of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 460 crores (9,200 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730582
- Scrip ID: BFSL141125
- ISIN: INE01C314DI2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under ISIN INE01C314DI2

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: November 17, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 13, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The commercial paper issuance provides short-term funding for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offers institutional investors a highly-rated debt instrument with a 91-day maturity. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
