## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5,00,000 each with 100 units listed, totaling Rs. 5 crores. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ rating and will mature on December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 100 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,642 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ 
- Scrip code: 730178
- ISIN: INE180C14599
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size maintained at 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 10, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The A1+ rating indicates good credit quality. Trading restricted to institutional and high-value investors due to Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
