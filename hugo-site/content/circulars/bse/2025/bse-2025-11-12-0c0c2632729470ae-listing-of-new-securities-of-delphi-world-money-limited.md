## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 5,223,295 new equity shares of Delphi World Money Limited (Scrip Code: 533452) issued on rights basis. The shares were allotted on November 10, 2025, at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 181/- per share, and will commence trading on November 13, 2025. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 5,223,295 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued
- Issue price: Rs. 191/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 181/- premium)
- Issued through rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE726L01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 11564358 to 16787652
- New shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 533452

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 13, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing following a rights issue. The share capital of Delphi World Money Limited will increase, resulting in dilution for shareholders who did not participate in the rights issue.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased number of tradable shares. The new shares will trade under the existing scrip code and ISIN with no operational changes required.
