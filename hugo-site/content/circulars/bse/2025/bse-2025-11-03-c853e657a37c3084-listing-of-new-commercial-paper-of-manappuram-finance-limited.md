## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper securities issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 1000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, with trading commencing on November 3, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,68,959
- Scrip Code: 730478
- Scrip ID: MFL311025
- ISIN: INE522D14OT3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OT3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it is a routine private placement of commercial paper in the debt segment. The listing provides short-term funding instruments for Manappuram Finance Limited with a maturity of approximately 10.5 months. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors seeking exposure to Manappuram Finance's short-term debt instruments.
