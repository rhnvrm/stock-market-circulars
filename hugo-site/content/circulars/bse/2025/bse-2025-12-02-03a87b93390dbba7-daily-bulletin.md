## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 167/2025-2026 dated December 2, 2025, provides a consolidated list of book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin covers multiple companies with scheduled activities including partial redemption of non-convertible debentures (NCDs), interest payments on privately placed NCDs, and redemption of commercial papers.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers record dates ranging from December 10, 2025 to February 26, 2026
- Multiple companies have scheduled NCD interest payments and partial redemptions in December 2025
- ARKA FINCAP LIMITED has three commercial paper redemptions scheduled between December 2025 and February 2026
- All securities listed maintain trading flag status of "1" indicating normal trading
- Corporate actions primarily involve debt securities rather than equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must honor the record dates specified for payment of interest and redemption
- Trading members should note the record dates for settlement purposes
- Investors holding securities as of record date are eligible for respective corporate actions

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Record date for CSA Investments Private Limited (Interest Payment)
- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for AAVAS Financiers Limited (Part Redemption & Interest)
- **December 12, 2025**: Record date for Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited (Interest Payment)
- **December 16, 2025**: Record dates for multiple Cube Highways Trust and Delhi International Airport Limited securities
- **December 17, 2025**: Record date for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED CP redemption
- **December 19, 2025**: Record dates for Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited (Interest Payments)
- **December 31, 2025**: Record date for Cube Highways Trust (Part Redemption & Interest)
- **January 7, 2026**: Record date for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED CP redemption
- **February 26, 2026**: Record date for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED CP redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine daily bulletin with scheduled corporate actions for debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Affects only holders of specific debt instruments and commercial papers as of respective record dates. No impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Standard settlement and payment processing for listed debt securities. No changes to trading operations or market infrastructure.
