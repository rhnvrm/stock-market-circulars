## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member CREDAVENUE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6810) will undergo a name change to ASPERO MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED effective Friday, November 21, 2025. This is an administrative change affecting member identification.

## Key Points

- Trading member number 6810 will retain the same membership number
- Old name: CREDAVENUE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- New name: ASPERO MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- No impact on trading operations or member privileges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update internal records to reflect the new name
- Use the updated name when referring to Member No. 6810
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Friday, November 21, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a purely administrative change that does not affect trading operations, market structure, or participant obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members may need to update their internal databases and contact lists to reflect the new trading member name. The member number (6810) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in system references.
