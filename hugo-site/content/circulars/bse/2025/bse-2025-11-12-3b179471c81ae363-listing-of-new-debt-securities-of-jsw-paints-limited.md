## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by JSW Paints Limited on private placement basis, effective November 12, 2025. The issuance comprises 330,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 3,300 crores. The securities carry an interest rate of 3.65% per annum with annual interest payments and maturity date of November 10, 2030. The securities are rated ICRA AA-/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 330,000 units of debt securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 3,300 crores
- Interest Rate: 3.65% per annum
- Scrip Code: 977287
- Scrip ID: 365JPL35
- ISIN: INE754Y08027
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA-/Stable
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Listing Date: November 12, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE754Y08027
- Members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for detailed information
- For clarifications, members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from November 10, 2026 to November 10, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement debt listing that does not affect equity markets or broad market operations.

**Issuer Impact**: Provides JSW Paints Limited with long-term debt funding of Rs. 3,300 crores at a competitive rate of 3.65% p.a., supporting the company's capital structure and financing needs.

**Investor Impact**: Offers institutional investors access to ICRA AA-/Stable rated debt securities from JSW Paints Limited with 5-year tenure and fixed annual interest payments.

**Liquidity**: Trading limited to BSE Debt segment with market lot of 1 unit, providing flexibility for institutional investors.
