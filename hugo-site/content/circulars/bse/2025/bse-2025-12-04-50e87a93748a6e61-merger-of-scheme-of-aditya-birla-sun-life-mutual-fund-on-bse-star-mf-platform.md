## Summary

BSE has announced the merger of Aditya Birla Sun Life CRISIL-IBX AAA NBFC-HFC Index Dec 2025 Fund (Transferor) into Aditya Birla Sun Life CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund (Transferee) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The merger involves four plan variants: Regular Plan Growth, Direct Plan Growth, Regular Plan IDCW Payout, and Direct Plan IDCW Payout.

## Key Points

- Four scheme variants are being merged from the AAA NBFC-HFC Index Dec 2025 Fund to the Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund
- Both Regular and Direct plans affected, including Growth and IDCW Payout options
- Merger involves debt index funds within the same fund house
- ISINs mapped for seamless transition of investors

## Scheme Mapping Details

**Transferor to Transferee Mapping:**

1. **Regular Plan - Growth**: INF209KC1274 → INF209KC1514
2. **Direct Plan - Growth**: INF209KC1258 → INF209KC1498
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF209KC1282 → INF209KC1522
4. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF209KC1266 → INF209KC1506

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheme rationalization by the asset management company.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF platform to update scheme details
- Investors holding units in the transferor scheme will automatically receive units in the transferee scheme
- Distributors and platforms to update their records with new ISINs

## Important Dates

Specific effective date of merger not mentioned in the circular. Investors should refer to the scheme merger document for detailed timeline.

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Investors:** Existing unitholders of the AAA NBFC-HFC Index Dec 2025 Fund will be transferred to the Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund. The merger consolidates similar debt index funds within the fund house.

**Operational Impact:** Low - routine scheme merger with automated transfer of holdings through ISIN mapping on BSE platform.
