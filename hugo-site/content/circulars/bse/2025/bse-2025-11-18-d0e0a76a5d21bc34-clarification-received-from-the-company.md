## Summary

BSE issued surveillance letters to three companies requesting clarification on significant price and volume movements in their securities. All three companies - Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited, Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited, and B.C. Power Controls Limited - responded that they have no pending price-sensitive information and have made all necessary disclosures in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015. The companies attributed the price/volume movements to market-driven factors.

## Key Points

- **Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited** (Scrip Code: 539551, 975516, 976418) responded to BSE surveillance query dated November 18, 2025 regarding volume movement
- **Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited** (Scrip Code: 544530) responded to BSE query dated November 17, 2025 regarding price movement
- **B.C. Power Controls Limited** (Scrip Code: 537766) submitted clarification on November 18, 2025 regarding price movement
- All companies confirmed no pending information or announcements with bearing on price/volume behavior
- All companies affirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated that price/volume movements are purely market-driven

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance activities and compliance responses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond promptly to BSE surveillance queries regarding abnormal price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any pending price-sensitive information exists
- Companies must affirm compliance with timely disclosure requirements under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- All material events and price-sensitive information must be disclosed to stock exchanges promptly

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: BSE surveillance query sent to Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited
- **November 18, 2025**: BSE surveillance query sent to Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited
- **November 18, 2025**: Clarification responses submitted by all three companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine clarifications with no new material information disclosed. The companies confirmed no undisclosed price-sensitive information exists.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides transparency that recent price/volume movements were market-driven and not due to undisclosed company-specific developments.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard surveillance and disclosure compliance process with no operational changes required.

**Companies Affected**: 
- Narayana Hrudayalaya Limited (healthcare sector)
- Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers Limited (financial services/broking sector)
- B.C. Power Controls Limited (power/electrical sector)
