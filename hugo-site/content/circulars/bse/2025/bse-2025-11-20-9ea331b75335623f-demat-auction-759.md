## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 759 dated November 20, 2025, covering 104 scrips across various sectors. The auction addresses delivery failures by members, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 54,435 shares. Notable scrips with significant quantities include Shanti Educa (54,435 shares), SPRIGHT AGRO (10,002 shares), Emcure Pharm (15,000 shares), SECUREKLOUD (6,643 shares), Swan Corp Li (5,000 shares), and VISTA PHARMA (5,000 shares).

## Key Points

- Total of 104 scrips included in Settlement No. 759
- Settlement date: November 20, 2025
- Auction covers delivery shortages across equity segment
- Quantities range from 1 share to 54,435 shares
- Multiple sectors affected including banking, technology, pharmaceuticals, utilities, and manufacturing
- Highest quantity: Shanti Educa with 54,435 shares
- Other significant quantities: SPRIGHT AGRO (10,002), Emcure Pharm (15,000), SECUREKLOUD (6,643), DANUBE INDUS (4,387), Tilak Ventur (4,170), ERAAYA LIFES (3,840), TRUE COLORS (3,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine demat auction process as per existing BSE regulations for handling delivery failures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery obligations must participate in the auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction rules
- Members failing to deliver securities must purchase them through the auction mechanism
- Auction participants must ensure sufficient funds and comply with auction timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 20, 2025
- Settlement Number: 759

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 104 different scrips, indicating widespread but relatively minor delivery failures across the market. Most quantities are small (under 500 shares), suggesting limited market disruption.

**Member Impact:** Members with delivery failures will face auction prices which are typically at a premium to market prices, resulting in financial penalties. The largest exposures are in Shanti Educa, SPRIGHT AGRO, and Emcure Pharm.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and maintains market integrity by penalizing delivery failures.

**Operational Impact:** Standard auction procedure with no exceptional operational requirements. Clearing and settlement infrastructure handles such auctions routinely.
