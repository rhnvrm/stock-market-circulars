## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debentures with ISIN INE321N07327 (Code: 975081)
- Debenture series: IFSL-9.50%-12-12-25-PVT (9.50% coupon rate)
- Record date set for November 27, 2025
- No dealings permitted from November 27, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-765/2025-2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for suspension of trading due to scheduled debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from November 27, 2025
- Members should note the record date and ensure no transactions are executed in these securities
- Proper communication to clients holding or intending to trade these debentures is required

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 12, 2025 (as per debenture series name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and tied to the scheduled redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. No trading liquidity available from suspension date until completion of redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these securities and inform affected clients about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
