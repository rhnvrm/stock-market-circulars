## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new Commercial Papers totaling Rs. 150 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on the BSE Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 3000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730584
- **Scrip ID**: NMIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WG7
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WG7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding instrument for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants. The strong credit ratings (CARE A1+ and IND A1+) indicate low credit risk. The 91-day tenor is typical for commercial paper instruments. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details.
