## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the equity derivatives segment on Saturday, November 29, 2025. The session will include selected index futures (SENSEX and BANKEX) and stock futures contracts expiring in December 2025. This is a routine testing exercise to ensure system readiness and does not involve actual trading.

## Key Points

- Mock trading scheduled for Saturday, November 29, 2025
- Covers equity derivatives segment only
- Includes SENSEX and BANKEX index futures
- Includes stock futures for KOTAKBANK, AXISBANK, BHARTIARTL, HDFCBANK, and ICICIBANK
- All contracts expire on December 24, 2025
- This is a simulation exercise with no real market impact

## Instruments Covered

### Index Futures
- **SENSEX** (Contract Token: 1102290) - Expiry: December 24, 2025
- **BANKEX** (Contract Token: 1104160) - Expiry: December 24, 2025

### Stock Futures
- **KOTAKBANK** (Contract Token: 1111953) - Expiry: December 24, 2025
- **AXISBANK** (Contract Token: 1115301) - Expiry: December 24, 2025
- **BHARTIARTL** (Contract Token: 1115246) - Expiry: December 24, 2025
- **HDFCBANK** (Contract Token: 1103080) - Expiry: December 24, 2025
- **ICICIBANK** (Contract Token: 1103455) - Expiry: December 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members may participate in the mock trading session to test their systems and familiarize themselves with trading procedures. Participation is voluntary.

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: Saturday, November 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This mock trading session has no impact on actual market operations or trading. It is purely a testing and simulation exercise designed to ensure system readiness and provide members an opportunity to test their connectivity and trading systems in a controlled environment.
