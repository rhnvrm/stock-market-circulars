## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of ICICI Home Finance Company Limited worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 18, 2025, with redemption scheduled for September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 4000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,96,873 per unit (discount to face value)
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip code: 730026
- ISIN: INE071G14HA8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading allowed only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- All trading members must use the specified ISIN number for transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 18, 2025
- Listing date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption date: September 26, 2025
- Maturity period: Approximately 39 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term nature (39 days) and private placement basis limit broader market implications. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
