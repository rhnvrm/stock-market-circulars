## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Seshachal Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 531794) will be moved to the 'X' group for trading purposes, effective December 10, 2025. This change affects the trading category and associated restrictions applicable to the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Seshachal Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 531794
- New Trading Group: 'X' group
- Effective Date: Wednesday, December 10, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251126-1
- Notice Date: November 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically represents securities with specific trading restrictions or surveillance measures imposed by the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- All trades in Seshachal Technologies Limited shares must comply with 'X' group trading rules from December 10, 2025
- Members should familiarize themselves with applicable restrictions and surveillance measures for 'X' group securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 10, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group suggests enhanced surveillance or trading restrictions for Seshachal Technologies Limited. This classification may:

- Impact trading volumes and liquidity
- Require additional due diligence from market participants
- Signal potential regulatory concerns or compliance issues
- Affect investor sentiment and stock performance

Traders and investors should be aware of the specific restrictions and requirements associated with 'X' group securities before trading in this scrip after the effective date.
