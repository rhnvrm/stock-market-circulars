## Summary

BSE has announced that QSIF EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective November 13, 2025. The scheme now supports regular transactions including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows the previous notice 20251023-19 and includes six plan variants across Regular and Direct categories.

## Key Points

- QSIF EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND reopened for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from November 13, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth) and Direct Plan (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth)
- Full facility for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions
- All variants classified under Equity category
- ISINs range from INF966L30159 to INF966L30209

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members and investors can now process transactions for this fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform using the standard procedures for mutual fund transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date for Ongoing Transactions**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it relates to the operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme. The reopening provides investors with access to a long-short equity fund focusing on stocks outside the top 100 companies. The impact is limited to:

- **Investors**: Can now invest in or redeem from QSIF EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND through BSE platform
- **Distributors/Members**: Can process client transactions for this fund
- **Market**: No direct impact on broader market operations or trading

The fund's long-short strategy and focus on ex-top 100 stocks makes it relevant for investors seeking alternative equity exposure with hedging capabilities.
