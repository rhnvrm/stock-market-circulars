## Summary

BSE has updated its surveillance measures for securities under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) framework effective November 7, 2025. One security, Seya Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 524324, ISIN: INE573R01012), has been shortlisted in Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) Stage 1 for companies relating to Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP). No securities moved to Stage 0, between stages, or were excluded from surveillance.

## Key Points

- Seya Industries Ltd (524324) enters IBC Stage 1 surveillance effective November 7, 2025
- No securities shortlisted in Stage 0 (Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure)
- No securities moved from Stage I to Stage II or vice versa
- No securities excluded from ASM for IBC framework
- Consolidated list contains 13 securities under various IBC surveillance stages
- Framework includes special markers for T+0 scrips (~) and recommencement scrips (^)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing IBC surveillance framework by updating the list of securities under different stages of surveillance based on insolvency proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants must note the surveillance stage classification for affected securities
- Enhanced monitoring and disclosure requirements apply to securities under IBC surveillance
- Market participants should be aware of increased risk associated with Stage 1 securities undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 7, 2025 - New surveillance classifications come into effect

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to one security entering Stage 1 surveillance. Seya Industries Ltd will face enhanced surveillance measures which may affect trading volumes and liquidity.

**Investor Impact**: Investors in Seya Industries Ltd should note the company is now under IBC Stage 1 surveillance due to insolvency resolution proceedings, indicating elevated financial distress risk.

**Trading Impact**: The surveillance designation may result in additional trading restrictions, price bands, or settlement requirements for the affected security.

## Additional Information
