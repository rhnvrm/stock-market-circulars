## Summary

AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, totaling 23,000 units (Rs. 23 crore). The debentures carry an interest rate of 11.5% p.a. with quarterly interest payments and mature on March 5, 2027. The securities have been rated CARE BBB- and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 23,000 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976467, Scrip ID: 115AFL27
- ISIN: INE0RU307155 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 9,965.34 per unit
- Interest Rate: 11.5% p.a. payable quarterly
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0RU307155
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 25, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Quarterly from June 5, 2025 to March 5, 2027
- Redemption Date: March 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional investment opportunities in the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors. The securities are rated investment grade (CARE BBB-) and offer a fixed return of 11.5% p.a. There is no impact on equity markets or general trading operations. Trading members requiring clarifications can contact BSE's debt department.
