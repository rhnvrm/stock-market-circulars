## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities and quantities subject to demat auction for Settlement No. 782 dated December 23, 2025. The auction covers 130 securities from various companies with specified quantities ranging from as low as 1 share to as high as 50,000 shares. This auction is conducted to settle short delivery obligations in the demat segment.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 782
- Settlement Date: December 23, 2025
- Total securities listed: 130 (approximately 65 entries displayed in two-column format)
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple securities) to 50,000 shares (STANDARD CAPITAL - Scrip Code 511700)
- Other high-quantity auctions include: AAYUSH WELLNESS (40,000 shares), QUADRANT FUTURE (8,214 shares), JAYASWAL NECO (5,000 shares), RIR POWER (4,347 shares)
- Major listed companies included: HDFC BANK, BHARTI AIRTEL, ITC, NTPC, SIEMENS, TATA MOTORS, BIOCON, MPHASIS, RADICO KHAITAN, UCO BANK, UNION BANK

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine auction notice as per existing BSE settlement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the demat auction process
- Securities must be delivered as per auction settlement procedures
- Members are required to honor their settlement obligations for the listed scrips and quantities
- Compliance with BSE's demat auction mechanism and timelines is mandatory

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 23, 2025
- Settlement Number: 782
- Auction Date: To be conducted as per BSE settlement calendar (specific auction timing to follow BSE guidelines)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 130 securities across various market segments including large-cap (HDFC BANK, ITC, NTPC, BHARTI AIRTEL), mid-cap, and small-cap stocks.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members with short delivery positions in any of the listed securities will need to participate in the auction to fulfill their obligations. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and maintains market integrity.

**Investor Impact:** Limited direct impact on retail investors. The auction primarily affects institutional participants and trading members who have failed to deliver securities in the normal settlement cycle.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary price impact possible during auction execution for securities with smaller quantities or lower liquidity, though impact is typically contained within the auction mechanism.
