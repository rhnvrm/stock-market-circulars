## Summary

360 ONE WAM Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crore (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 24, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with allotment date of November 21, 2025 and redemption scheduled for February 20, 2026. HDFC Bank acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crore (3,000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,279 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730625
- **Scrip ID**: OWL211125
- **ISIN**: INE466L14FG1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE466L14FG1
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing that provides short-term debt investment opportunity for institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal with Commercial Paper instruments. The listing expands the available universe of rated short-term debt instruments on BSE.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with an additional A1+ rated Commercial Paper option with 91-day maturity, offering returns of approximately 7.2% annualized based on the discount to face value.
