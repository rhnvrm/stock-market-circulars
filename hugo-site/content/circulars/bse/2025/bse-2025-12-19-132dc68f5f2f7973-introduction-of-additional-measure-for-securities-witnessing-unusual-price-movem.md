## Summary

BSE has placed 11 securities under Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) effective December 22, 2025, due to unusual price movements. SER Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 507984) is newly added to this list. The consolidated list includes securities that will face enhanced surveillance and trading restrictions to curb excessive speculation and protect investor interests.

## Key Points

- 1 new security added to ASM framework: SER Industries Ltd (ISIN: INE358F01013)
- Total of 11 securities now under additional surveillance measures
- Effective date: December 22, 2025
- Measure applies to securities witnessing unusual price movements
- Consolidated list includes previously listed securities continuing under surveillance

## Regulatory Changes

Securities under Additional Surveillance Measure typically face:
- Enhanced margin requirements
- Trading only in cash segment (no intraday trading)
- Price bands may be revised
- Additional disclosure requirements for market participants
- Periodic review of trading patterns

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers must inform clients about securities under ASM framework
- Enhanced due diligence required for transactions in listed securities
- Clients must provide additional margin/collateral as specified
- Trading members must ensure compliance with ASM conditions
- Market participants should monitor the consolidated list regularly

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 22, 2025 - Additional surveillance measures take effect for all listed securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Trading restrictions will reduce liquidity and speculative activity in the 11 affected securities. Investors holding these stocks may face limitations on intraday trading and higher margin requirements.

**Affected Securities**: 11 companies across various sectors including infrastructure, defense, semiconductor, and real estate.

**Investor Protection**: The measure aims to curb excessive volatility and protect retail investors from potential losses due to unusual price movements.
