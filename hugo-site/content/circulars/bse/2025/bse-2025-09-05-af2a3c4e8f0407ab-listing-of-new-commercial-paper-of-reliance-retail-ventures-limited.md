## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 1,675 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper worth Rs. 1,675 crore (33,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit against face value of Rs. 5,00,000
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EC4
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited and offers institutional investors a highly-rated short-term investment option with 91-day maturity.
