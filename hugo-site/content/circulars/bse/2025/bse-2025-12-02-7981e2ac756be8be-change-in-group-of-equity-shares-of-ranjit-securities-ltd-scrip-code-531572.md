## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be included under the 'X' group effective December 09, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be moved to 'X' group
- Change effective from December 09, 2025
- Trading members notified through Notice No. 20251202-12
- Contact person: Mrs. Kinjal More (+91-7304278788) for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically includes companies under special surveillance or with specific trading restrictions, which may affect liquidity and trading dynamics.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the group change effective December 09, 2025
- Update their systems and records accordingly
- Inform clients about the group change and its implications
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More for any clarifications needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group may result in:
- Increased trading restrictions or surveillance
- Potential impact on stock liquidity
- Possible changes to margin requirements
- Investor caution and reduced trading activity
- Enhanced monitoring by exchange authorities
