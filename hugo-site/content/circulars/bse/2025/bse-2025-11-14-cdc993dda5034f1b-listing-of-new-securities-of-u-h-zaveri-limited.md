## Summary

BSE has listed 20,388,000 new equity shares of U. H. ZAVERI LIMITED (Scrip Code: 541338) issued through a rights issue. The shares are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, November 17, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: U. H. ZAVERI LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 541338
- Number of Shares: 20,388,000 equity shares
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue Type: Rights issue at par
- ISIN: INE556Z01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 10194001 to 30582000
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: November 17, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
