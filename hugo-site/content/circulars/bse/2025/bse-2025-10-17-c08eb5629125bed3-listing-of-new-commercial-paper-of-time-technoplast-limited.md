## Summary

Time Technoplast Limited has listed new Commercial Paper instruments on BSE's Debt segment on October 17, 2025. The issue consists of 500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (total Rs. 25 crores) issued on private placement basis, with a maturity date of April 16, 2026. The instruments carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with IDBI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a routine commercial paper issuance by Time Technoplast Limited.
