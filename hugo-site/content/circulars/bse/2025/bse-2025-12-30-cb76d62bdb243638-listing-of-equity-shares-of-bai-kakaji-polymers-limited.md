## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544670, symbol BAIKAKAJI, and ISIN INE1IJQ01026. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company Name: BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED
