## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper of 16 companies effective from December 10-12, 2025. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 16 commercial paper instruments will be suspended from trading
- Suspensions range from December 10 to December 12, 2025
- Action taken due to record dates fixed for redemption of commercial paper
- Notice number: 20251209-5 issued on December 9, 2025
- All affected instruments are in the debt segment

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified commercial papers from the respective "No Dealings From" dates
- Members should ensure no transactions are executed in these securities post-suspension dates
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption purposes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited impact confined to debt market participants holding these specific commercial paper instruments. Does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers should prepare for redemption. Trading suspension prevents secondary market transactions during the redemption process.

**Operational Impact:** Standard corporate action requiring trading systems to block these securities. Redemption reference numbers DR-774, DR-775, and DR-776 for 2025-2026 assigned for processing.

**Sectoral Distribution:** Affected companies span financial services (securities, investment firms), infrastructure (power finance), telecom, manufacturing, and retail sectors, indicating normal market activity across multiple industries.
