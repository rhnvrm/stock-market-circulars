## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Dhaval Packaging Limited filed on December 30, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days for stakeholders to review and provide feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21-day period
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments solicited from stakeholders
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at: www.bsesme.com/download/312386/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHPDPL_20251231003527.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit their feedback within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document review is voluntary for public stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date and start of 21-day public comment period
- **January 21, 2026**: Expected deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)
