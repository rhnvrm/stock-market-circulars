## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544600, symbol LENSKART, and ISIN number INE956O01016. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Lenskart Solutions Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544600
- Trading Symbol: LENSKART
- ISIN: INE956O01016
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to the BSE trading platform. The impact is primarily informational at this stage, as the actual listing date has not been specified. Trading members and market participants should await the subsequent notice with specific listing date and security details before any trading activity can commence. This represents a new investment opportunity in the optical retail sector once trading begins.
