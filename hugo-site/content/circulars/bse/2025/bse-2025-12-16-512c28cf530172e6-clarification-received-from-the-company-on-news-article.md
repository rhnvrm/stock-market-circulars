## Summary

Vedanta Limited has clarified media reports regarding NCLT approval of its demerger scheme. The National Company Law Tribunal, Mumbai pronounced its order at 2:30 PM IST on December 16, 2025, sanctioning the scheme of demerger. The official order copy has not yet been uploaded on NCLT's website. The company will make a detailed disclosure once the order copy becomes available, in compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- NCLT Mumbai has sanctioned Vedanta's demerger scheme
- Order was pronounced at 2:30 PM IST on December 16, 2025
- Official order copy not yet available on NCLT website
- Detailed disclosure to be made upon receipt of official order copy
- Company confirms all material developments have been disclosed as required
- No unpublished price-sensitive information beyond what has been disclosed
- Clarification issued in response to BSE/NSE query on news article from CNBC TV18

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vedanta Limited will file detailed disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 once the official NCLT order copy is received
- Company has confirmed compliance with disclosure obligations and absence of undisclosed price-sensitive information

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025, 2:30 PM IST**: NCLT Mumbai pronounced order sanctioning demerger scheme
- **Pending**: Release of official NCLT order copy and subsequent detailed disclosure by company

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - The NCLT approval of the demerger scheme is a significant milestone for Vedanta's corporate restructuring. The news article reported stock surge following the announcement, indicating positive market sentiment.

**Corporate Impact**: High - Demerger represents major structural change for Vedanta Limited, potentially creating separate listed entities for different business verticals.

**Shareholder Impact**: High - Shareholders will be affected by the demerger structure, share allocation ratios, and creation of new listed entities once the scheme becomes effective.

**Next Steps**: Market participants should await the detailed disclosure with official order copy for complete understanding of demerger terms, timeline, and implementation requirements.
