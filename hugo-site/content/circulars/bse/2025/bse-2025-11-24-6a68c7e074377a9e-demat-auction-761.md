## Summary

BSE has published the list of scrips for demat auction under Settlement No. 761 dated November 24, 2025. The auction includes 92 scrips across various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 489,533 shares. This is a standard settlement mechanism for fulfilling delivery obligations where securities are not received from selling members.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 761
- Date: November 24, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 92 different securities
- Highest quantity: GV Films Ltd (523277) - 489,533 shares
- Multiple blue-chip and mid-cap stocks included
- Auction covers various sectors including banking, infrastructure, FMCG, and technology

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in the listed scrips must participate in the auction
- Members must ensure sufficient funds/securities for auction settlement
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanism rules
- Members should verify their obligation positions for the listed scrips

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 761

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - affects trading members with pending delivery obligations in the listed scrips. Members with shortages will need to procure shares through auction at potentially higher prices.

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - while 92 scrips are affected, the quantities for most securities are relatively small except for a few cases like GV Films (489,533 shares) and Catvision (2,149 shares).

**Financial Impact**: Varies by member - those with shortage positions may face higher procurement costs through auction mechanism, leading to potential losses compared to market prices.

**Affected Parties**: Trading members, clearing members, and institutional participants with open positions in the listed securities for Settlement 761.
