## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The listing comprises 4000 units of commercial paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crore. The instruments are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 4000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,703.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730671
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF281125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FN9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard listing notification for new debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FN9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarifications may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 28, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing with no broader market implications. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt segment trading procedures apply. The instruments carry strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating high credit quality and safety for short-term investments.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants interested in highly-rated commercial paper with approximately 91-day tenure. The discount to face value (issue price of Rs. 4,92,703.50 vs face value of Rs. 5,00,000) represents the implicit interest for investors.
