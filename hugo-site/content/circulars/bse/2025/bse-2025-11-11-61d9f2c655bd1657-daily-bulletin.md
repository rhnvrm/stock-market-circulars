## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 152 dated November 11, 2025 announces book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple securities. The bulletin covers rights issues, interim dividends, bonus issues, NCD redemptions with interest payments, and commercial paper redemptions scheduled between November 15-January 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Adani Enterprises has set record date November 17, 2025 for rights issue of equity shares (trading flag 0 and 1)
- Multiple interim dividends declared ranging from 10% to 350%
- Balrampur Chini Mills announces highest interim dividend of Rs. 3.50 per share (350%)
- Container Corporation (CONCOR) declares second interim dividend of Rs. 2.60 per share (52%)
- Autoriders International bonus issue record date: November 18, 2025
- BAID Finserv rights issue record date: November 17, 2025
- Several NCD redemptions and interest payments for Ashiana Housing and Axis Bank
- Commercial paper redemptions scheduled for CaratLane and CNH Industrial Capital

## Trading Flags

- Trading flag 0: Security will remain tradable on record date
- Trading flag 1: Security trading subject to ex-date adjustments

## Impact Assessment

This bulletin affects shareholders of multiple companies across various corporate actions. Investors must note the record dates to be eligible for dividends, rights/bonus entitlements. The trading flags indicate whether securities will trade cum or ex-entitlement. Notable high-value dividends from Balrampur Chini (350%) and EPL (125%) may attract investor interest. Rights issues by Adani Enterprises and BAID Finserv require shareholder attention for participation decisions.
