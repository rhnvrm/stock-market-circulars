## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ravelcare Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544629, symbol RAVEL, and ISIN number INE0W4D01018. The specific date of listing and additional details about the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Ravelcare Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544629
- Symbol: RAVEL
- ISIN: INE0W4D01018
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with access to a new investment opportunity in the SME segment. Market impact is limited to those specifically interested in Ravelcare Limited or the SME segment. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is confirmed.
