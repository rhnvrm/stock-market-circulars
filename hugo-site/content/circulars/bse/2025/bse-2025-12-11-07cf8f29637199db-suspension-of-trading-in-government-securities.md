## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Treasury Bill with ISIN IN001225C076 (Scrip Code 800448 - GS16DEC2025C) effective December 12, 2025, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on December 16, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill ISIN: IN001225C076
- Scrip Code: 800448
- Particulars: GS16DEC2025C
- Maturity/Redemption Date: December 16, 2025
- Trading suspension effective: December 12, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified T-bill from December 12, 2025
- Members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension directive
- All existing positions should be settled or managed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 16, 2025**: T-bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine suspension for a maturing government security. The suspension is a standard pre-maturity procedure to prevent trading in securities close to redemption. Only holders of this specific T-bill are affected, and they will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader debt market or trading operations impact anticipated.
