- Company Name: K K SILK MILLS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544624
- Trading Symbol: KKSILK
- ISIN Number: INE0EOJ01014
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice

No regulatory changes announced.

Trading members are required to take note of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time pending the separate notice with listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Minimal immediate market impact. This is a standard listing notification for a new SME company. Trading members and investors interested in the SME segment should await the follow-up notice containing the actual listing date and detailed security information before any trading activity can commence.
