## Summary

BSE has added 44 Aditya Birla Sun Life mutual fund schemes to the SIP for LIFE (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF platform. The addition includes both SIP and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) options across various fund categories including multi-asset allocation, balanced advantage, equity hybrid, flexi cap, frontline equity, midcap, and fund of funds schemes. Each scheme is available in both regular and direct plans, with L1 variants also included.

## Key Points

- 44 scheme codes added for SIP for LIFE SIP facility
- 4 scheme codes added for SIP for LIFE SWP facility (content appears truncated)
- Covers multiple fund categories: Multi Asset Allocation, Balanced Advantage, Equity Hybrid 95, Flexi Cap, Frontline Equity, GenNext, Midcap, and various Fund of Funds
- Both regular and direct plans included for each scheme
- L1 variants available for all schemes
- Fund house: Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement to expand the availability of mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform's SIP for LIFE facility.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular announcing the expansion of available investment options on the platform.

## Important Dates

No specific effective date or deadline mentioned. The schemes are presumed to be immediately available on the platform.

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a platform enhancement that provides additional investment options for retail investors.

**Operational Impact:** Positive for investors seeking long-term systematic investment options through the SIP for LIFE facility. The addition of Aditya Birla Sun Life schemes expands choice across multiple asset classes and investment strategies.
