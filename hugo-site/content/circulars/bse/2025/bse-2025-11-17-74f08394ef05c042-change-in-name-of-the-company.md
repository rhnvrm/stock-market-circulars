## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED. The company's name has been officially changed to IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED with effect from October 1, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED
- **New Name**: IKOMA TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- **Original Name**: GOOD VALUE IRRIGATION LIMITED
- **CIN**: L62099MH1993PLC074167
- **Effective Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Issuing Authority**: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- **Approved by**: M.Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is an administrative notification of a name change certificate issued under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (VUENOW INFRATECH LIMITED) for a period of **two years** along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Name Change Effective Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from October 1, 2025 (until October 1, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, rights, or liabilities.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update all statutory documents, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.
