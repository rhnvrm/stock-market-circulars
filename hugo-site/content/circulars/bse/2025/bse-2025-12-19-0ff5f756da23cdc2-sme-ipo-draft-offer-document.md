## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Mother Agri Food Limited filed on December 18, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on BSE's SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Mother Agri Food Limited on December 18, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure procedure for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from December 19, 2025 (by January 9, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 18, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Mother Agri Food Limited
- **December 19, 2025**: Notice published; public comment period begins
- **January 9, 2026**: Public comment period ends (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it is a routine procedural disclosure for an SME IPO. The impact is limited to prospective investors interested in Mother Agri Food Limited's upcoming public offering. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations before the company can proceed with its IPO process.
