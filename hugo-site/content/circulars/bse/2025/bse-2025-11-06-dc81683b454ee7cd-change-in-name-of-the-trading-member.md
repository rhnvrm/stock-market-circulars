## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Lakshmishree Investment & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3281) will change its name to "Lakshmishree Investment and Securities Ltd." effective Friday, November 7, 2025. This is an administrative change with no operational impact on trading activities.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD." to "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD."
- Member Number: 3281
- Change reflects conversion from private limited to public limited company
- No impact on trading operations or member code

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the corporate structure change of the trading member entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. For clarifications, members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notification Date:** November 6, 2025
- **Effective Date:** Friday, November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is a routine administrative notification regarding a trading member's corporate name change, likely reflecting a change in company structure from private limited to public limited. No operational changes or actions required from other market participants.
