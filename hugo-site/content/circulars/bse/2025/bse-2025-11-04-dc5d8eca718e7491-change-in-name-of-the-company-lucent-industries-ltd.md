## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Lucent Industries Limited (CIN: L73100MP2010PLC023011). The company name has been officially changed to Mobavenue AI Tech Limited with effect from October 1, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Sylph Education Solutions Limited.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Lucent Industries Limited to Mobavenue AI Tech Limited
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L73100MP2010PLC023011
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: Sylph Education Solutions Limited
- Certificate digitally signed by Sunidhi Matroja, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative corporate action reflecting a name change approved under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Lucent Industries Limited) along with the new name (Mobavenue AI Tech Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 1, 2025
- **Certificate Issued**: October 1, 2025, 14:25:50 IST
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from October 1, 2025 (until October 1, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all statutory records, letterheads, signage, and public documents to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights, liabilities, or obligations of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory identification.
