## Summary

BSE Ltd. has published the schedule for Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits for December 2025. The circular provides a list of companies whose share transfer and investor servicing operations are handled by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit BSE premises during December 2025. This is a routine administrative circular to inform market participants about RTA availability at the exchange.

## Key Points

- RTA visits to BSE Ltd. scheduled for December 2025
- List includes 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd.
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, textiles, and technology
- Each company is identified by its ScripCode and full corporate name
- RTAs handle shareholder services including share transfers, dematerialization, and investor grievances

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an informational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. The circular is informational in nature, providing details about RTA visit schedules for operational coordination.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period**: December 2025 (specific dates not provided in the content)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a routine administrative circular.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. The circular facilitates better coordination between BSE, RTAs, and listed companies for shareholder servicing activities. Investors and companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services may use this information to plan interactions during the RTA's visit to BSE premises.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral. The circular provides transparency about RTA schedules but does not affect trading or investment decisions.
