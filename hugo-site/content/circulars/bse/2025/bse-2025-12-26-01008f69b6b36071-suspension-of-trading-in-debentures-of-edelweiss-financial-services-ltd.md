## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific series of non-convertible debentures issued by Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. The suspension is effective from December 30, 2025, coinciding with the record date set by the company for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE532F07BS0 (Code: EFSL-GSEC-14-1-26-PVT, Series: 960418)
- Record date for redemption: December 30, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: December 30, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Notice reference: DR-787/2025-2026
- Issuer: Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action process where trading is suspended to facilitate clean settlement for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any transactions in the specified debentures (INE532F07BS0) from December 30, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction to avoid settlement issues

## Important Dates

- **December 26, 2025**: Circular issued by BSE
- **December 30, 2025**: Record date for debenture redemption
- **December 30, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only one specific debenture series of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. The suspension is temporary and related to the normal redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard corporate action procedure. Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN. Holders of these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption terms.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear in the records on December 30, 2025 will be eligible for redemption. No trading opportunity exists from the suspension date, which is standard practice for maturing/redeeming debt securities.
