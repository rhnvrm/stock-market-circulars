## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of EARNEST CONSTRUCTIONS PVT LTD (ISIN: INE0HCI07011, Code: 974942) effective November 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of NCDs and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ECPL-12%-25-6-27-PVT series debentures
- ISIN: INE0HCI07011, BSE Code: 974942
- Corporate action involves early redemption and interest payment
- Trading members explicitly advised to cease dealing in these securities
- Circular issued under notice number DR-754/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension triggered by corporate action event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 12, 2025
- All exchange participants must take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No dealings permitted in debentures with code 974942 (ECPL-12%-25-6-27-PVT)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 12, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: November 12, 2025
- **Purpose Effective Date**: November 12, 2025 (early redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Immediate Impact**: Complete halt on secondary market trading for the affected debenture series. Existing holders cannot exit positions through exchange trading from November 12, 2025 onwards.

**Investor Impact**: High impact for current NCD holders who may have planned to trade these securities. However, holders will receive early redemption proceeds and accrued interest as per the company's corporate action.

**Market Impact**: Limited to specific debenture series of EARNEST CONSTRUCTIONS PVT LTD. Does not affect broader debt market operations. Standard procedure for corporate actions involving early redemption.

**Liquidity Impact**: Trading liquidity for this specific debenture series reduced to zero from the suspension date until redemption completion.
