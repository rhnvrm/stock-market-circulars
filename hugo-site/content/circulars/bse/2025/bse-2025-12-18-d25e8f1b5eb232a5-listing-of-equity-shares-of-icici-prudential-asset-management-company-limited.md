## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ICICI PRUDENTIAL ASSET MANAGEMENT COMPANY LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544658 and trading symbol ICICIAMC with ISIN INE346A01027. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited's equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544658
- Trading Symbol: ICICIAMC
- ISIN Number: INE346A01027
- Listing date and detailed security information to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the new listing for future trading activity once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited to BSE's equity segment, providing investors access to shares of a prominent asset management company. The impact is moderate as it expands investment opportunities in the financial services sector, particularly in asset management. Trading members should prepare their systems to handle trading in this scrip once the listing date is announced.
