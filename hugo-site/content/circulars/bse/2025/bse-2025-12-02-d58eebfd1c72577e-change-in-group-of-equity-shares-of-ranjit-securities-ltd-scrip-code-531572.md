## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be moved to the 'X' group effective December 09, 2025. The 'X' group classification typically applies to securities that do not comply with listing requirements or have other regulatory concerns.

## Key Points

- Company: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531572
- New Group Classification: 'X' group
- Effective Date: December 09, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251202-12
- Notice Date: December 02, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group classification is a surveillance measure applied by BSE to securities that face compliance issues or fail to meet listing requirements. This classification typically results in restricted trading with stricter settlement requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the change in group classification for Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Update their systems to reflect the new 'X' group status
- Inform clients holding or trading in this security about the group change
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 02, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 09, 2025 - Trading in 'X' group commences

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group has significant implications:

**For Investors:**
- Restricted trading conditions may apply
- Reduced liquidity as many institutional investors avoid 'X' group stocks
- Potential difficulty in exiting positions
- Higher risk perception in the market

**For the Company:**
- Negative market sentiment
- Pressure to resolve compliance issues to move back to regular trading groups
- Reputational impact

**For Trading Members:**
- Need to update risk management systems
- Additional due diligence required for client trades
- Potential margin and settlement implications
