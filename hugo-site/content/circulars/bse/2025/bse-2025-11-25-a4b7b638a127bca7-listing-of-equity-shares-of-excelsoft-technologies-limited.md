## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Excelsoft Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544617, symbol EXCELSOFT, and ISIN number INE606N01019. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Excelsoft Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544617
- Trading Symbol: EXCELSOFT
- ISIN Number: INE606N01019
- Listing status: Approved, date to be announced
- Notice Number: 20251125-8
- Issued by: Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of Excelsoft Technologies Limited equity shares and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Security Details: To be provided in subsequent communication

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Trading members should update their systems with the security details. Market participants interested in Excelsoft Technologies Limited will be able to trade the stock once listing is completed. The impact is moderate, limited to participants interested in this specific security until further details and the listing date are announced.
