## Summary

BSE has updated the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) list, moving Vintage Securities Ltd. (Security Code: 531051, ISIN: INE153C01015) to GSM Stage I. The GSM framework is a surveillance mechanism to monitor securities that exhibit abnormal price movements or other concerning trading patterns.

## Key Points

- Vintage Securities Ltd. (Security Code: 531051) moved to GSM Stage I
- ISIN: INE153C01015
- Securities marked with (#) can move to lower GSM stages if included in ESM Framework
- Securities marked with ($) can move to lower GSM stages if included in IBC Framework
- Information aligned with NSE (as indicated by * marker)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the existing Graded Surveillance Measure framework by updating the list of securities under surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the GSM Stage I classification for Vintage Securities Ltd.
- Trading members must comply with applicable restrictions and requirements for securities under GSM Stage I
- Enhanced due diligence may be required for transactions in the affected security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: As applicable per GSM framework guidelines

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact confined to Vintage Securities Ltd. GSM Stage I classification typically involves additional surveillance and may include price bands, additional margins, or trading restrictions to prevent excessive speculation.

**Operational Impact**: Traders and investors dealing in Vintage Securities Ltd. should be aware of enhanced surveillance measures that may affect liquidity and trading conditions.

**Investor Impact**: Existing and potential investors in Vintage Securities Ltd. should exercise caution and be aware that the stock is under enhanced surveillance due to concerns about price movements or trading patterns.
