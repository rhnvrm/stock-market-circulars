## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Bajaj Finance Limited on its Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with 33,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, issued at Rs. 4,92,026.50 per unit. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will be redeemed on February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 33,000 Commercial Paper securities
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,026.50 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 16,500 crores (approximately)
- Scrip Code: 730564
- Scrip ID: BFL131125
- ISIN: INE296A14D47
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialised form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE296A14D47
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities listed and admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 14, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 13, 2025
- Listing Date: November 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2026
- Tenor: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Commercial paper issuances are standard short-term debt instruments used by highly rated corporates for working capital requirements. The listing affects only debt market participants dealing in short-term corporate debt. There is no impact on equity markets or general trading operations. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of Bajaj Finance Limited.
