## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730597, ISIN: INE01C314DK8) effective November 24, 2025. The suspension is being implemented as the Commercial Paper is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. This is a standard procedural action for maturing debt instruments.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited will mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 730597
- ISIN: INE01C314DK8
- Trading Members advised not to deal in this instrument from November 24, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-762/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for suspending trading in debt instruments approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the mentioned Commercial Paper from November 24, 2025
- Trading Members must take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in Scrip Code 730597 after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 25, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational matter affecting a single Commercial Paper instrument. The suspension is procedural and relates to the normal lifecycle of debt securities reaching maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects trading members dealing specifically in this Commercial Paper. Standard maturity process with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors holding this Commercial Paper will receive redemption proceeds as scheduled. No adverse impact on investment outcomes.
