## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Sharekhan Limited on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. The issue consists of 8000 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,91,700.26 with maturity on March 10, 2026. The securities carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 8000 commercial papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,700.26
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 730722
- Scrip ID: SL91225
- Detail Name: SL-10-3-26-CP
- ISIN: INE211H14AI6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE211H14AI6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 9, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: December 9, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with minimal market impact. The listing provides Sharekhan Limited with short-term financing for approximately 3 months. The strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate high creditworthiness. There is no impact on equity trading or broader market operations. The listing is relevant primarily for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers.
