## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed three new Commercial Paper securities on the BSE Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. All securities were issued on private placement basis with allotment date of November 3, 2025, face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, and redemption dates in October 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying agent for all three instruments.

## Key Points

- Three Commercial Paper securities listed with Scrip Codes: 730481, 730482, and 730483
- All securities issued on private placement basis with 2000 quantity each
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+ for first security; CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+ for second and third securities
- Issue prices range from Rs. 463,221.50 to Rs. 465,504.00
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing of Commercial Paper securities provides short-term financing options for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunities for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term corporate debt instruments.
