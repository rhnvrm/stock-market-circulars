## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of ENS Enterprises Limited for public comments. The document was filed on November 01, 2025 and contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days for public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by ENS Enterprises Limited on November 01, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document link: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comment period of at least 21 days as mandated by ICDR Regulations
- Draft Offer Document must be hosted on stock exchange website
- Interested parties can submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from November 19, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed**: November 01, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Comment Deadline**: 21 days from November 19, 2025 (December 10, 2025)
- **Minimum Display Period**: 21 days

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. The availability of the DRHP for public comments is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. Impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders of ENS Enterprises Limited. The public comment mechanism allows transparency and stakeholder participation in the IPO approval process.
