## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for January 2026. The circular provides the complete daily settlement schedule mapping trade dates to their corresponding settlement dates for the entire month.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- Daily settlement basis with T+1 settlement cycle (settlement occurs one business day after trade date)
- No trading/settlement holidays observed during January 2026 for F&O segment
- Total of 21 trading days in January 2026
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure adequate margin coverage for daily settlements
- Settlement obligations must be met as per the published schedule
- Members should plan their fund management according to the settlement calendar

## Important Dates

**January 2026 Settlement Schedule:**
- First trading day: January 1, 2026 (settles January 2, 2026)
- Last trading day: January 30, 2026 (settles February 2, 2026)
- Trading days: 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 27, 28, 29, 30 January 2026
- Non-trading days: 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-26, 31 January 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine operational schedule with standard T+1 settlement cycle.

**Operational Impact:** Low - provides advance notice for fund and resource planning by trading members and clearing members for January 2026 operations.

**Trading Impact:** None - maintains continuous daily settlement operations with no disruptions or special provisions.
