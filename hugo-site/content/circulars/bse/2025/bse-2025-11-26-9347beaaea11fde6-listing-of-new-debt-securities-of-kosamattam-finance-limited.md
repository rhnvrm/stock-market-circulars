## Summary

BSE notifies the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 26, 2025. The securities carry an 11% per annum interest rate with monthly payment frequency and mature on May 25, 2031.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 10,000 units
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 977316
- Scrip ID: 11KFL31
- ISIN: INE403Q08332
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Credit rating: IND A/Stable
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403Q08332
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- For clarifications, members should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: November 25, 2025
- Listing effective date: November 26, 2025
- First interest payment date: January 1, 2026
- Interest payment frequency: Monthly from January 1, 2026 to May 25, 2031
- Redemption date: May 25, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing affects only trading members dealing in debt securities and investors interested in Kosamattam Finance Limited's debt instruments. The securities are rated IND A/Stable, indicating adequate safety with stable outlook. The 11% interest rate reflects current market conditions for private placement debt securities with similar credit ratings and tenures.
