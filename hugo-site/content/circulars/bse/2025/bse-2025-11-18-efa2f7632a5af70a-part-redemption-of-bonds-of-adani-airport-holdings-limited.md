## Summary

Adani Airport Holdings Limited has announced part redemption of its bonds (ISIN: INE0GCN07047, Code: 976065) with a coupon rate of 9.35% maturing on September 8, 2028. The record date is set for November 25, 2025, for both interest payment and partial redemption. Post-redemption, the bonds will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 76,971 per bond, effective from November 25, 2025 in settlement DR-763/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Bond details: AAHL 9.35% bonds maturing on September 8, 2028
- ISIN: INE0GCN07047, BSE Code: 976065
- Record date: November 25, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Reduced face value: Rs. 76,971 per bond (post-redemption)
- Effective date: November 25, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-763/2025-2026
- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notice informing about bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure bonds trade with reduced face value of Rs. 76,971 per bond from November 25, 2025
- Bondholders must be on record as of November 25, 2025 to be eligible for interest payment and part redemption
- Trading systems must reflect the updated face value in settlement DR-763/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **November 18, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 25, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-763/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Bondholders**: Positive impact as bondholders receive partial principal repayment along with interest payment. The reduced face value of Rs. 76,971 indicates a partial redemption amount has been paid out.

**Market Operations**: Trading members must update systems to reflect the new face value for accurate trading and settlement. The bonds will continue trading but at the reduced face value.

**Liquidity**: Part redemption reduces outstanding debt and may affect bond liquidity and trading volumes going forward.

**Adani Airport Holdings**: Demonstrates debt management capability and adherence to bond covenants by executing scheduled partial redemption.
