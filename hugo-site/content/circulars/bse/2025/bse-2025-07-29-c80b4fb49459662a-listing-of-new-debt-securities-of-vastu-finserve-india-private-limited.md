## Summary

BSE has listed three new debt securities issued by Vastu Finserve India Private Limited on private placement basis, effective July 29, 2025. All securities carry a 9.25% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and are rated ICRA AA-/Stable.

## Key Points

- Three debt securities with face value of Rs. 100,000 each
- All securities carry 9.25% per annum interest rate
- Quarterly interest payment schedule
- Private placement basis issuance
- ICRA AA-/Stable credit rating
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Securities Details

**Security 1 (VFIPL-9.25%-28-7-28-PVT):**
- Scrip Code: 976959
- ISIN: INE08Z607091
- Redemption Date: July 28, 2028
- Quantity: 5,000 units

**Security 2 (VFIPL-9.25%-28-7-27-PVT):**
- Scrip Code: 976960
- ISIN: INE08Z607109
- Redemption Date: July 28, 2027
- Quantity: 5,000 units

**Security 3 (VFIPL-9.25%-28-1-28-PVT):**
- Scrip Code: 976961
- ISIN: INE08Z607117
- Redemption Date: January 28, 2028
- Quantity: 5,000 units

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: July 29, 2025
- First Interest Payment: October 28, 2025
- Redemption Dates: January 28, 2028; July 28, 2027; July 28, 2028

## Trading Information

- Trading segment: BSE Debt segment
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing for a private placement. The securities provide institutional investors with fixed-income investment options with quarterly interest payments and strong credit rating.
