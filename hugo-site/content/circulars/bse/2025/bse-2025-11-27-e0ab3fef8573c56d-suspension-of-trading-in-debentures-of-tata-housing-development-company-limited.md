## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of TATA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED (ISIN: INE582L08052, Code: 976013) effective December 2, 2025. The suspension is to facilitate redemption of debentures and payment of interest with a record date of December 2, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: THDCL-8.2175%-17-9-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE582L08052
- BSE Code: 976013
- Record date: December 2, 2025
- No dealings from: December 2, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice number: DR-768/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 2, 2025
- Trading members are required to note and comply with the suspension directive
- Debenture holders eligible as per record date will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 2, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 2, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only a specific series of private placement Non-Convertible Debentures of TATA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and routine for redemption purposes. Only holders of this particular debenture series (ISIN: INE582L08052) are affected. No impact on equity trading or other debt securities of the company.
