## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of DS Kulkarni Developers Limited shares. The OFS will be conducted on December 5th, 2025 (for Institutional/Mutual Fund/Other/Non-Institutional Investors) and December 8th, 2025 (for Retail Investors and unallocated bidders). A total of 5,00,000 shares will be offered at a floor price of Rs 10.00 per equity share, with 10% reserved for retail investors. All eligible bidders will be allotted shares at the floor price using proportionate allocation methodology.

## Key Points

- **Security**: DS Kulkarni Developers Limited (Symbol: DSKULKARNI, Code: 523890, ISIN: INE891A01022)
- **Total Offer Size**: 5,00,000 shares
- **Retail Reservation**: 50,000 shares (10% of issue size)
- **Non-Retail Offer Size**: 4,50,000 shares
- **Floor Price**: Rs 10.00 per share (Face Value: Rs 10/-)
- **Market Lot**: 1 share with tick size of Rs 0.01
- **Allocation Method**: Proportionate - all eligible bidders will be allotted at floor price only
- **Multiple Bidding**: Allowed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard OFS procedure following existing BSE guidelines for offer for sale of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodian Members:

1. **Margin Requirements by Investor Category**:
   - Retail Investors (RI/RIC) & Employees (EMP): 100% upfront margin in cash & cash equivalents
   - Non-Institutional Investors (NII): 100% upfront margin in cash
   - Institutional Investors (MF/IC/OTHS): 100% upfront margin in cash OR 0% margin option available

2. **Access URLs**:
   - iBBS Platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet), https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet), https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet), https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

3. **Bidding Categories**:
   - MF: Mutual Fund
   - IC: Insurance Company
   - NII: Non-Institutional Investors (Individual, HUF, Trust, Body Corporates, PMS)
   - OTHS: Others (FII, FI, Banks)
   - RI: Retail Investors (Individual, HUF, NRI)
   - RIC: Retail Investors bidding at cut-off
   - EMP: Employees (bid value up to Rs 5 lakhs)

4. **Important Notes**:
   - Retail Investor definition: Individual investor placing bids for total value not more than Rs 2 lakhs
   - Retail investors can bid in "RI", "RIC" and/or "NII" categories
   - Retail investors bidding in NII category will not be eligible for discount (if any)
   - Multiple bidding allowed

## Important Dates

### T Day - December 5th, 2025 (Thursday):
- **Institutional/Mutual Fund/Other/Non-Institutional Investors (IC/MF/OTH/NII)**
  - Bid Entry Period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Bid Modification and Cancellation: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Settlement: T+1 day

### T+1 Day - December 8th, 2025 (Monday):
- **Give up/Take up for Institutional Investors** (0% margin bids only): Up to 7:30 AM
- **Retail Investors/Employees (RI/RIC/EMP)** and unallocated T-day bidders:
  - Bid Entry Period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Bid Modification and Cancellation: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Settlement: T+2 day

### T+2 Day - December 9th, 2025 (Tuesday):
- Give up/Take up for unallocated T+1 day bidders: Up to 7:30 AM
- Settlement for T+1 day bids
