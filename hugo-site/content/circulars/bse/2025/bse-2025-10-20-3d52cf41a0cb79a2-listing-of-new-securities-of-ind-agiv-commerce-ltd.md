## Summary

BSE has listed 20,56,965 equity shares of Ind-Agiv Commerce Ltd. (Scrip Code: 517077) issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter. Trading commences on October 21, 2025. The shares were allotted on May 12, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 29 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 19) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 20,56,965 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- Issue price: Rs. 29 per share (premium of Rs. 19)
- Allotment date: May 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: October 21, 2025
- ISIN: INE115E01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 1000001 to 3056965
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the listing of new securities
- Lock-in requirements apply to the allotted shares as detailed below

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 21, 2025
- **Lock-in Period 1**: 1,55,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 1000001-1155000) locked until June 29, 2027
- **Lock-in Period 2**: 19,01,965 shares (Dist. Nos. 1155001-3056965) locked until June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing notice with minimal market-wide impact. The preferential allotment increases the paid-up capital of Ind-Agiv Commerce Ltd. Lock-in provisions ensure promoter commitment with 1.55 lakh shares locked for 2 years and 19.02 lakh shares locked for 1 year. Trading members can facilitate transactions in these new shares from October 21, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions.
