## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems to test their applications under various market conditions. The mock trades will not create any actual obligations, margins, or settlement requirements.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on November 08, 2025
- Intended for testing trading applications and various functionalities
- Testing scenarios include risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be deployed
- All trades are for testing only and carry no actual obligations
- Active participation requested from all members
- Feedback to be submitted by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

**November 08, 2025 Trading Sessions:**

- **Log-in:** 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- **Pre-Open Order Entry:** 12:00 PM to 12:08 PM
- **Matching Period:** 12:08 PM to 12:15 PM
- **Continuous Trading:** 12:15 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Block Deal Window:** 01:00 PM to 01:15 PM
- **Closing:** 04:00 PM to 04:10 PM
- **Post-Closing:** 04:10 PM to 04:20 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Third-party vendors and in-house system developers using ETI APIs should utilize this opportunity for testing

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, November 08, 2025
- **Feedback Deadline:** November 08, 2025 at 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a mock trading session with no real market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Provides testing opportunity for trading members to familiarize themselves with the EGR segment and validate their systems under various market scenarios. No actual trades, margins, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or rights and liabilities will be created.
