## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 54,71,101 partly paid-up equity shares of ACE Software Exports Ltd. issued through a rights issue. The securities will be listed and permitted to trade in the 'X' Group effective December 24, 2025. The shares have a face value of Rs. 10/- with Rs. 4.50/- paid-up and an issue price of Rs. 110/- (Rs. 49.50/- paid on application and Rs. 60.50/- payable on subsequent calls).

## Key Points

- 54,71,101 partly paid-up equity shares to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share, Paid-up value: Rs. 4.50/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 110/- (Rs. 49.50/- paid on application, Rs. 60.50/- on future calls)
- Scrip Code: 890230, Scrip ID: ACESEPP
- ISIN: IN9849B01026 (permanent), INE849B20028 (temporary - suspended)
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Group: X
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 5471101
- Date of allotment: December 19, 2025
- Dividend entitlement: Proportionate to amount paid-up

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following established procedures for rights issue securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the temporary ISIN No. INE849B20028 is suspended in accordance with SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012
- Members must update their systems with the new security details for trading from December 24, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Commencement Date**: December 24, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to ACE Software Exports Ltd. shareholders and traders. The partly paid-up nature means shareholders have outstanding payment obligations for future calls at the Board's discretion. Trading members need to update their systems to accommodate the new security. The listing in 'X' Group indicates these are newly listed securities subject to specific trading rules. Market lot of 1 share provides flexibility for small investors.
