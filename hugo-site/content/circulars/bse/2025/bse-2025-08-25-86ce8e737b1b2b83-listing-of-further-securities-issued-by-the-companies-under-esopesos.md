## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities from 9 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading from August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 9 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 1,302,570 new shares across all companies
- All securities are admitted for trading effective August 26, 2025
- None of the securities are under lock-in period
- Face values range from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **Black Box Limited**: 103,950 shares (Face Value: Rs. 2)
- **CreditAccess Grameen Ltd**: 45,725 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- **Dr. Reddy's Laboratories Ltd**: 4,160 shares (Face Value: Re. 1)
- **Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd**: 76,684 shares (Face Value: Re. 1)
- **IdeaForge Technology Limited**: 11,626 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- **Jana Small Finance Bank Limited**: 25,031 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- **Lupin Ltd**: 7,189 shares (Face Value: Rs. 2)
- **Paradeep Phosphates Limited**: 989,657 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- **Prataap Snacks Limited**: 27,504 shares (Face Value: Rs. 5)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: August 26, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP/ESOS share listings. The additional shares represent dilution for existing shareholders but are part of normal employee compensation programs. Paradeep Phosphates has the largest issuance with nearly 1 million shares.
