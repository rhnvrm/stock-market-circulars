## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of non-convertible debentures of Sakthi Finance Ltd effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of these securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to two NCD series: SFL-10%-29-8-25-NCD and SFL-ZC-29-8-25-NCD
- Both securities have ISIN codes INE302E07375 and INE302E07383 respectively
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from August 14, 2025
- Suspension is due to redemption corporate action

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Members required to note the suspension for compliance purposes

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 14, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 14, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as suspension is procedural for redemption. Affects holders of specific Sakthi Finance debenture series who will receive redemption proceeds. No broader market implications expected.
