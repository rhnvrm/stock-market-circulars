## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 12,000 units at a face value of ₹1,00,000 per unit. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.75% per annum and are rated ICRA AA- by the credit rating agency.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977193
- **Scrip ID**: 875360OP27
- **ISIN**: INE248U07GC5 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,01,059.38 per unit
- **Quantity Listed**: 12,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-
- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 8.75% per annum (payable annually)
- **Maturity Date**: October 7, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07GC5
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from October 7, 2026 to October 7, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 7, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt securities listing with no broader market implications. The listing affects only investors and trading members dealing in debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard trading procedures apply. The securities are available for trading in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with a standard tick size of 1 paise.

**Issuer Profile**: 360 ONE Prime Limited (formerly known as IIFL Wealth Prime) is the wealth management arm of the 360 ONE group. The ICRA AA- rating indicates high credit quality with adequate degree of safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations.
