## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. effective December 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of specific debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for EFSL-GSEC-14-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE532F07BS0, Code: 960418)
- Record date for redemption: December 30, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 30, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-787/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 30, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to clients holding these securities
- Ensure systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only one specific series of non-convertible debentures. Debenture holders scheduled for redemption will receive their principal and interest as per terms.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and inform affected clients. The suspension is procedural for facilitating redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them from December 30, 2025, but will receive redemption proceeds as per the debenture terms.
