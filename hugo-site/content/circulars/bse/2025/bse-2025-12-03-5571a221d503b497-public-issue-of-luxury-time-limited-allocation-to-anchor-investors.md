## Summary

Luxury Time Limited's Board of Directors finalized the allocation of 6,17,600 equity shares to anchor investors on December 03, 2025, at an anchor investor allocation price of Rs. 82 per equity share (face value Rs. 10, share premium Rs. 72). Three anchor investors participated in the allocation, with no mutual fund schemes applying.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 6,17,600 equity shares at Rs. 82 per share
- LRSD Securities Pvt Ltd received the largest allocation at 60.10% (3,71,200 shares)
- Viney Growth Fund allocated 1,23,200 shares (19.95%)
- Rajasthan Global Securities Private Limited allocated 1,23,200 shares (19.95%)
- No mutual fund schemes participated in the anchor investor portion
- Face value: Rs. 10 per equity share with premium of Rs. 72

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. The allocation follows SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2018 as amended.

## Compliance Requirements

- If the final offer price discovered through book building is higher than the anchor investor allocation price of Rs. 82, anchor investors must pay the differential amount by the specified pay-in date in the revised CAN
- Information disclosed to BSE for public dissemination on their website
- Notification sent to SEBI Corporation Finance Department, Division of Issues and Listing

## Important Dates

- Board Meeting Date: December 03, 2025
- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: December 03, 2025
- Differential payment (if applicable): As per revised CAN schedule

## Impact Assessment

This anchor investor allocation represents the pre-IPO institutional commitment for Luxury Time Limited's public issue. The successful allocation to three investors, though with no mutual fund participation, indicates institutional interest in the offering. The pricing at Rs. 82 per share sets a reference point for the final price discovery through the book building process. Market participants and retail investors will watch the final issue pricing and subscription levels to gauge overall market appetite for this IPO.
