## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of Punjab Infrastructure Development Board (PIDB) effective December 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for bond redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond ISIN: INE091D11089 (Scrip Code: 972366)
- Series: PIDB-STRPPH-27-12-2025-PVT
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Bonds and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: December 12, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action requiring temporary trading suspension per established procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified PIDB bonds from December 12, 2025
- Members must ensure no trades are executed in bond INE091D11089 on or after the suspension date
- Existing positions should be reconciled before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and price discovery for these bonds. Investors holding these securities will be unable to trade them from December 12, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems immediately to block trades in these bonds and inform clients holding positions about the suspension and upcoming redemption/interest payment.
