## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 164,125,000 new equity shares of Ashnisha Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 541702) issued on a rights basis. The shares will be available for trading from November 14, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 164,125,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2/- per share
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE694W01024
- Distinctive Numbers: 101000001 to 265125000
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 541702

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** November 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification specific to Ashnisha Industries Limited. The rights issue increases the company's equity capital base by 164.125 million shares. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and investors of this specific scrip. No broader market or regulatory implications.
