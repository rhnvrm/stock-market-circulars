## Summary

ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND is launching ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from December 08, 2025, to December 10, 2025, with extended hours until 6:00 PM on the last day. The tentative allotment date is December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO scheme: ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND (Liquid category)
- NFO period: December 08-10, 2025 (extended till 6:00 PM on December 10)
- Tentative allotment date: December 12, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Pending transactions will remain in system until 3:00 PM on last NFO payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to receipt of clear funds by December 11, 2025, 6:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated fund payments are made by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Transactions with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled orders without fund initiation
- Clear funds must be credited to ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025, for successful allotment
- Extended trading hours compliance: NFO acceptance until 6:00 PM on December 10, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 08, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **December 10, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till 6:00 PM)
- **December 11, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **December 12, 2025**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a routine mutual fund NFO launch. It affects only BSE StAR MF Platform members and investors interested in subscribing to the ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND. The liquid fund category is typically low-risk and used for short-term cash management. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
