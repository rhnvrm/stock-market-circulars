## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544625), currently listed in the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 19, 2025. This is a continuation of the earlier notice dated December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544625
- Current Classification: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Classification: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Notice Reference: Continuation of Notice No. 20251204-52 dated December 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, which allows for normal trading with T+2 settlement cycle instead of mandatory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the change in trading segment for this scrip
- Adjust internal systems and processes to reflect the new group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- **Previous Notice**: December 4, 2025 (Notice No. 20251204-52)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The shift from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling settlement) will increase liquidity and trading flexibility for LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED shares. Investors will no longer be restricted to mandatory delivery-based trading.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new group classification. The change indicates improved trading status for the company post-SME IPO listing.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors as rolling settlement provides better liquidity and ease of trading compared to the restrictive Trade for Trade segment.
