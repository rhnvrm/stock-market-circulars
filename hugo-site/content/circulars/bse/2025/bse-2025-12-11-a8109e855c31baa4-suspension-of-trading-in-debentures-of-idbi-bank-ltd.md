## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of IDBI BANK LTD (IDBI-8.62%-31-12-30-PVT, ISIN: INE008A08V00, Scrip Code: 953179) effective December 15, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for IDBI Bank debentures with ISIN INE008A08V00 (Scrip Code: 953179)
- Debenture series: IDBI-8.62%-31-12-30-PVT (8.62% coupon rate, maturity December 31, 2030)
- Record date for early redemption: December 15, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 15, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-777/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine corporate action notice for early redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified IDBI Bank debentures from December 15, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All dealings in these debentures must cease from the specified date to facilitate the early redemption process

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders of specific IDBI Bank debentures only. The early redemption will provide liquidity to debenture holders but removes a debt instrument from the market.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and upcoming early redemption. Debenture holders on record date will receive early redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for debenture holders as they will receive their principal back through early redemption, though they will lose future interest income from these 8.62% coupon debentures that were originally scheduled to mature on December 31, 2030.
