## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Standard Chartered Capital Limited on private placement basis effective November 14, 2025. The issue comprises 4,000 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, with CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings. The instruments will be redeemed on September 10, 2026.

- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores (4,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,72,247.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730568, Scrip ID: SCC141125
- ISIN: INE403G14TT8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G14TT8
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
- Listing Date: November 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 10, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 10 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The instrument provides short-term debt investment opportunity for institutional investors and adds to the available debt securities on BSE's platform. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. The discount to face value at issue price reflects the prevailing money market rates for highly-rated commercial paper.
