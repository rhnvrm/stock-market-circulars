## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited on its Debt segment effective December 4, 2025. The listing comprises 4,500 commercial papers of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, issued on private placement basis with a CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The securities were allotted on December 3, 2025, with redemption scheduled for December 2, 2026. IndusInd Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4,500 commercial papers listed
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,59,871.50 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730701
- **Scrip ID**: MFL031225B
- **ISIN**: INE549K14CG3
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CG3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents a routine addition of commercial paper to the BSE Debt segment. It affects only trading members dealing in debt securities and provides short-term funding instruments for Muthoot Fincorp Limited. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
