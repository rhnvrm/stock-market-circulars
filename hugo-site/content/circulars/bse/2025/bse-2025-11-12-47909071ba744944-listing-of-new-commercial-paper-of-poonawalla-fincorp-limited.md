## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 4000 securities with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 12, 2025. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh face value each
- Scrip Code: 730531 | Scrip ID: PFL101125
- ISIN: INE511C14ZA4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,519 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE511C14ZA4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 12, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments (84-day tenure) for trading on BSE's debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to participants interested in short-term debt securities of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
