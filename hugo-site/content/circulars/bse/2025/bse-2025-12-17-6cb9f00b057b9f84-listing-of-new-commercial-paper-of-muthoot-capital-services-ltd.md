## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Capital Services Ltd on private placement basis with effect from December 17, 2025. The instrument consists of 300 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 15 crore, with a tenure of approximately 85 days.

## Key Points

- Instrument: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 300 units (Rs. 15 crore)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,563.70 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730789
- Scrip ID: MCSL161225
- ISIN: INE296G14602
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: December 17, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 11, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 85 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper is rated CRISIL A1+, indicating strong creditworthiness. The listing provides another avenue for debt market participants but has no direct impact on equity trading or broader market operations. The instrument is suitable for institutional investors seeking short-term debt exposure with approximately 3-month maturity.
