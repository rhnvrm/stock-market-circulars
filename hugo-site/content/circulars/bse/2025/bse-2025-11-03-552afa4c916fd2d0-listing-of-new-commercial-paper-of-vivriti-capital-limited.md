## Summary

Vivriti Capital Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 50 Crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 Lakhs each) on BSE's Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis with a tenor of 3 months, maturing on 30th January 2026. The instruments carry a CARE A1+ credit rating and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 Crores aggregate)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,88,669.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730475
- Scrip ID: VCL311025
- ISIN: INE01HV14AD5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the designated ISIN Number INE01HV14AD5

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 31st October 2025
- Listing Date: 3rd November 2025
- Date of Redemption: 30th January 2026
- Circular Date: 3rd November 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issuance is a standard short-term funding instrument for Vivriti Capital Limited. The listing affects only debt market participants and those specifically interested in highly-rated short-term debt instruments. No broader market implications are expected from this listing.
