## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Papers issued by BOBCARD LIMITED on a private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. Series 1 consists of 1,500 papers worth Rs. 75 crores maturing on December 15, 2025. Series 2 consists of 1,000 papers worth Rs. 50 crores maturing on December 12, 2025. Both series carry IND A1+ credit rating and ICICI Bank acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total issue size: Rs. 125 crores (Rs. 75 crores + Rs. 50 crores)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper for both series
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ for both series
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Series 1 (BCL281125)**:
  - Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
  - Date of Redemption: December 15, 2025
  - Scrip Code: 730676
  - ISIN: INE027214902
- **Series 2 (BBL281125)**:
  - Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
  - Date of Redemption: December 12, 2025
  - Scrip Code: 730678
  - ISIN: INE027214910

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The short maturity periods (12-15 days) indicate these are standard short-term funding instruments. The IND A1+ rating reflects strong credit quality. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the high denomination requirement of Rs. 5 lakhs minimum. The listing provides additional liquidity options for debt market participants but does not affect broader market operations or equity trading.
