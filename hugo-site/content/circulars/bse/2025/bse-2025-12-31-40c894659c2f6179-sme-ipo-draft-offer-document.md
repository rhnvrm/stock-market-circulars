## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025, on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days for stakeholders to review and provide feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosures
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability on exchange website for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 31, 2025 (until January 20, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with minimal immediate market impact. The draft stage allows for public scrutiny and regulatory compliance before the actual IPO launch. Investors and market participants interested in KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED's SME IPO can review the offer document details during the comment period. The final IPO timeline will depend on resolution of comments and regulatory approvals.
