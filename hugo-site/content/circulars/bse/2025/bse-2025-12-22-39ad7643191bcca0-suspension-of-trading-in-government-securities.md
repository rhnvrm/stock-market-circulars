## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Government Security SGBDEC2513 (Scrip Code: 800281, ISIN: IN0020170158) effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the security reaching its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this government security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Government Security SGBDEC2513 (Sovereign Gold Bond December 2013 series) will mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 800281
- ISIN: IN0020170158
- Trading suspension effective from December 23, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with Government Security SGBDEC2513 from December 23, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new transactions should be executed in this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: On or around December 23, 2025 (specific date implied by suspension timing)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a single government security that has reached maturity. The suspension is a standard procedure and does not indicate any systemic issues.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only trading members dealing with this specific sovereign gold bond need to take action. The security will be redeemed as per normal maturity process.

**Investor Impact**: Routine - Holders of SGBDEC2513 will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond's terms and conditions. No trading disruption for other securities.
