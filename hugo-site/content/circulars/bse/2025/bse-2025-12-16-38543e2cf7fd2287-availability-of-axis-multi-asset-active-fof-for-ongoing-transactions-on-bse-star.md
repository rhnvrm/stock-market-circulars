## Summary

BSE has notified that AXIS MULTI-ASSET ACTIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 17, 2025. This follows the previous Notice No. 20251120-11 and makes available six plan variations (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options) for trading.

## Key Points

- AXIS MULTI-ASSET ACTIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 17, 2025
- Available transaction types: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variations available: 3 Regular Plans and 3 Direct Plans
- All schemes categorized under DEBT category
- Scheme variations include Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options
- Notice references previous communication No. 20251120-11

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about fund availability for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 - Reopening of AXIS MULTI-ASSET ACTIVE FOF for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This notice affects only investors and distributors dealing with Axis Mutual Fund products on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members can process subscription and redemption orders for the six AXIS MULTI-ASSET ACTIVE FOF scheme variations starting December 17, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking exposure to multi-asset allocation through fund-of-funds structure. Investors can now make fresh investments or redemptions through various modes (lumpsum, SIP, STP, SWP).
