## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy under Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SHREE PACETRONIX LIMITED. The takeover offer was conducted from October 31, 2025 to November 14, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025114). The revised obligation download date is November 18, 2025, and the revised settlement date is November 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2025114)
- Bid Period: October 31, 2025 to November 14, 2025
- Revised Obligation Download Date: November 18, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: November 19, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2025114 must be used by Members/Custodians in depository system
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the previously announced settlement schedule in Notice No. 20251030-1. The revision updates the obligation download and settlement dates for the takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2025114 in depository system entries
- Members must refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange
- Members should consult MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for applicable client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Bid End Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Revised Obligation Download**: November 18, 2025
- **Revised Settlement Date**: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This revision impacts members and custodians involved in the SHREE PACETRONIX LIMITED takeover offer. The updated settlement timeline provides one additional day for obligation processing and settlement completion. Members handling client transactions for this takeover must adjust their internal processes to accommodate the revised dates and ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements and proper depository system entries.
