## Summary

SMR Jewels Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on September 6, 2025. The document is now available for public comments for 21 days on BSE SME website with all required disclosures as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI.

## Key Points

- Company: SMR Jewels Limited
- Document filed on September 6, 2025
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comment period of 21 days from notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - standard SME IPO filing process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Document Filing Date: September 6, 2025
- Notice Date: September 8, 2025
- Public Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine SME IPO filing. The public comment period allows stakeholders to review the offer document before finalization. Impact limited to SME segment and potential investors interested in jewelry sector companies.
