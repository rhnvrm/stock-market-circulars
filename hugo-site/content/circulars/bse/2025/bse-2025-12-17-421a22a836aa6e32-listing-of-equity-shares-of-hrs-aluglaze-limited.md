## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544656, symbol HRS, and ISIN number INE1E1V01017. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544656
- Trading Symbol: HRS
- ISIN Number: INE1E1V01017
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Details of securities to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems to facilitate trading in HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The circular serves as an advance notification to trading members. Actual trading will commence only after the formal listing date is announced. Impact is limited to HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED and its stakeholders. No broader market implications.
