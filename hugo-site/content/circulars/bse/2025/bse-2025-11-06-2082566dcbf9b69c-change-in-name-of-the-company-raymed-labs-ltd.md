## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Raymed Labs Limited. The company name has been officially changed to Cresanto Global Limited with effect from April 30, 2025. This certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC under CIN L24111UP1992PLC014240.

## Key Points

- Old Name: RAYMED LABS LIMTED
- New Name: CRESANTO GLOBAL LIMTED
- Effective Date: April 30, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24111UP1992PLC014240
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, Registrar of Companies
- Authorized by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Registered Address: C-273, C Block, Sector 63, Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar-201301, Uttar Pradesh, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard administrative procedure under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (Raymed Labs Limited) and new name (Cresanto Global Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All legal obligations and contracts entered under the old name remain valid and enforceable

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2025**: Effective date of name change and certificate issuance
- **April 30, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. The name change is purely administrative and does not affect the company's business operations, assets, liabilities, or legal standing.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights, obligations, or ownership. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name.

**Market Impact**: Low. Investors and market participants should note the name change for identification purposes. The company's CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory filings and records.

**Compliance Obligations**: The company must update its letterheads, signage, documentation, and digital presence to reflect the new name while maintaining dual-name display for two years as mandated by Section 12 of the Companies Act.
