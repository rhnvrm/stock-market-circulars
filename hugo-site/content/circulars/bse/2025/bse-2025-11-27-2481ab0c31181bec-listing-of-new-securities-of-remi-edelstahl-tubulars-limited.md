## Summary

BSE has listed 9,62,893 equity shares of Remi Edelstahl Tubulars Limited (Scrip Code: 513043) issued on a preferential basis. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, November 28, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 9,62,893 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 129.33/- (including premium of Rs. 119.33/-)
- Allotment date: October 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 28, 2025
- ISIN: INE158G01015
- Distribution numbers: 10982401 to 11945293
- Issued to both Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the lock-in restrictions on the shares:
  - 2,67,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 10982401-11249400): Locked-in until May 31, 2027
  - 6,95,893 shares (Dist. Nos. 11249401-11945293): Locked-in until June 1, 2026
- Members must ensure compliance with lock-in provisions during trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 1)**: June 1, 2026 (6,95,893 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 2)**: May 31, 2027 (2,67,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares with minimal market impact. The lock-in periods ensure that a significant portion of shares (approximately 27.7%) will remain locked until May 2027, while the majority (72.3%) will be released by June 2026. The preferential allotment increases the paid-up capital of the company and the shares rank equally with existing shares, maintaining standard shareholder rights.
