## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED on private placement basis. The listing consists of 1,000 units of commercial paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crores. The securities are listed under scrip code 730626 and ISIN INE525L14158, with trading commencing on November 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (total Rs. 50 crores)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,066 per unit
- Scrip code: 730626
- Scrip ID: NCSL211125
- ISIN: INE525L14158
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Any clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department on specified contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption date**: December 22, 2025
- **Tenure**: 31 days

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents a routine short-term commercial paper issuance by NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED. The 31-day tenure indicates short-term funding requirements. The dual A1+ ratings from CRISIL and ICRA reflect strong credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors seeking short-duration corporate debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
