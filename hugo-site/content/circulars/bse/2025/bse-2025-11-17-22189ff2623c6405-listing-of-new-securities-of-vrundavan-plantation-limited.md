## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 856,872 new equity shares of Vrundavan Plantation Limited (Scrip Code: 544011) with effect from November 18, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 41/- per share (total issue price Rs. 51/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The allotment was completed on August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 856,872 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 51/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 41/-)
- Scrip Code: 544011
- ISIN: INE0Q6901013
- Distribution numbers: 5332729 to 6189600
- Date of allotment: August 1, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 18, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are available for trading on the Exchange. No specific compliance actions required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**:
  - 148,800 shares (Dist. Nos. 5332729-5481528): May 31, 2026
  - 420,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5769601-6189600): May 30, 2026
  - 288,072 shares (Dist. Nos. 5481529-5769600): May 30, 2027

## Lock-in Details

The newly listed shares are subject to lock-in periods as follows:

| Number of Shares | Distribution Numbers | Lock-in Until |
|-----------------|---------------------|---------------|
| 148,800 | 5332729-5481528 | May 31, 2026 |
| 288,072 | 5481529-5769600 | May 30, 2027 |
| 420,000 | 5769601-6189600 | May 30, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment represents standard corporate action for warrant conversion. The lock-in periods ensure orderly market participation. The shares are available for trading by non-locked-in holders and will contribute to the overall market capitalization of Vrundavan Plantation Limited.
