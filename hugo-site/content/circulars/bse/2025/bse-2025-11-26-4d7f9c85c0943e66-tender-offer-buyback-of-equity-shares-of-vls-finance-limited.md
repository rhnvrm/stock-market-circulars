## Summary

VLS Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 511333) has announced a buyback offer and fixed the record date as December 12, 2025 for determining eligible shareholders. The ex-entitlement date will also be December 12, 2025 under settlement number DR-776/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: VLS Finance Limited (BSE Code: 511333)
- Purpose: Tender offer (buyback) of equity shares
- Record date: December 12, 2025
- Ex-entitlement date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialized securities - rolling settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Only shareholders holding shares as of the record date (December 12, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade ex-entitlement from December 12, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This buyback offer will affect only VLS Finance Limited shareholders. Trading will continue on an ex-entitlement basis from December 12, 2025.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding VLS Finance shares as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. This may provide an exit opportunity at potentially premium prices.

**Trading Impact**: Shares will trade ex-entitlement from December 12, 2025, which may lead to price adjustments on that date.
