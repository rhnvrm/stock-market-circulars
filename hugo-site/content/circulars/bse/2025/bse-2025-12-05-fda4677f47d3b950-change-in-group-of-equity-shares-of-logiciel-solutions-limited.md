## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544625), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment under M Group effective December 19, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's previous notice dated December 04, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544625
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Notice Reference: Continuation of Notice No. 20251204-52 dated December 04, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED will transition from Trade for Trade settlement (which requires mandatory delivery) to Rolling settlement, allowing for intraday trading and T+1 settlement cycles. This upgrade from MT Group to M Group reflects improved trading eligibility.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- All pending positions in MT Group must be settled before the migration date
- Trading Members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain for implementation details and clarifications

## Important Dates

- **December 04, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251204-52)
- **December 05, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued
- **December 19, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

This change represents a positive development for LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED shareholders and traders. The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment increases liquidity and trading flexibility, allowing for intraday trading opportunities. Investors will benefit from improved price discovery and reduced settlement restrictions. Trading Members need to prepare their systems for the segment migration to ensure seamless transition.
