## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Access Point India Limited for their SME IPO, filed on September 9, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Access Point India Limited on September 9, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- Contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comment period of at least 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - standard SME IPO disclosure process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public must submit comments within 21 days if any
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 8, 2025
- Document Filing Date: September 9, 2025
- Comment Submission Deadline: Within 21 days from September 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to broader market - affects only Access Point India Limited and potential investors in their SME IPO. Standard regulatory disclosure process for SME listings on BSE.
