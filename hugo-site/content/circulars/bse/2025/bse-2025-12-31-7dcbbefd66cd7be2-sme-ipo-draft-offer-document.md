## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Dhaval Packaging Limited on its SME platform website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period. The document was filed on December 30, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21-day public review period
- Document accessible at: www.bsesme.com/download/312386/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHPDPL_20251231003527.pdf
- Public comments invited within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments to be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations requirements for SME IPO filings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must remain available on BSE website for at least 21 days
- Public comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of notice date
- Document must contain all disclosures as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice published and public comment period begins
- **January 21, 2026** (approximately): Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)
