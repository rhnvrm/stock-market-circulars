BSE has issued a notice regarding the Opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Bank of Maharashtra. This circular provides oversubscription details and important information for market participants regarding the OFS process.

- OFS announced for Bank of Maharashtra shares
- Oversubscription notice issued to inform market participants
- Government disinvestment initiative for public sector bank
- Trading and settlement procedures applicable for OFS

No specific regulatory changes mentioned in this circular. This is a notification of OFS commencement.

- Market participants must follow OFS bidding procedures
- Settlement process as per BSE OFS mechanism

- Bidders must adhere to specified timelines and procedures

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 2, 2025
- OFS opening date and bidding schedule as per the detailed circular
- Settlement dates to be followed as per BSE calendar

**Market Impact:** High - OFS for a public sector bank affects institutional and retail investors. Oversubscription indicates strong market demand.

**Operational Impact:** High - Trading members need to facilitate client participation in the OFS process.
