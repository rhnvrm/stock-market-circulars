## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including those using third-party platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs. The mock session will simulate various market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 08, 2025
- Open to members using third-party trading platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will cover various functionalities and exceptional market conditions
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Mock trades will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or rights/liabilities
- Active participation from all members is encouraged
- Feedback must be submitted by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in:** 11:15 AM - 12:15 PM
- **Continuous Trading:** 12:15 PM - 04:00 PM
- **Trade Modification:** 04:00 PM - 04:15 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance obligations arising from this mock trading session. Members are requested to:

- Participate actively in the mock trading session
- Test their trading applications for various functionalities
- Submit feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025:** Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment
- **November 08, 2025 by 5:00 PM:** Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions or obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Members with trading systems may use this opportunity to test and validate their platforms, particularly for exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. This is a voluntary testing exercise.
