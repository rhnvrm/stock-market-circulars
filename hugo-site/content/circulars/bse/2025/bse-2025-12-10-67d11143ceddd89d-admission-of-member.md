## Summary

BSE Limited has admitted two trading members effective December 10, 2025. Bansal Shares And Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6970) has been granted new membership for Cash and Equity Derivatives segments. JRK Stock Broking Pvt.Ltd. (Member No. 6132) has been granted additional segment membership for the Debt segment. Both members are admitted subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Bansal Shares And Securities Private Limited admitted as new trading member (Member No. 6970)
- Segments approved: Cash/Equity Derivatives
- JRK Stock Broking Pvt.Ltd. granted additional segment membership (Member No. 6132)
- Additional segment: Debt
- All admissions subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard administrative notification regarding member admissions under existing BSE membership framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from existing trading members. This is an informational notice about new member admissions. New members must comply with all applicable BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations for their respective trading segments.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 10, 2025 (admission date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. Addition of new trading members marginally increases market participation but does not affect trading operations, rules, or existing member obligations.

**Operational Impact:** No impact on existing trading members. New members will operate within their designated segments (Cash, Equity Derivatives, and Debt) under standard BSE regulatory framework.

**Member Impact:** Informational only for existing members. Indicates expanding broker network on BSE platform.
