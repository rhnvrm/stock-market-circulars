## Summary

BSE has notified that the equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip Code: 544446) will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Thursday, November 27, 2025. This is an administrative change in the stock's trading group classification.

## Key Points

- Company: Advent Hotels International Limited
- Scrip Code: 544446
- New Trading Group: 'B'
- Effective Date: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251113-1
- Category: Company related - Equity Segment

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will be moved from its current trading group to Group 'B'. Group 'B' stocks on BSE typically have different trading parameters and surveillance mechanisms compared to Group 'A' stocks. This reclassification may affect trading dynamics, liquidity, and circuit limits applicable to the stock.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the change in trading group classification. No specific action is required from trading members or investors, but they should be aware of the Group 'B' trading parameters that will apply to this scrip from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The reclassification to Group 'B' may result in:

- Different price band limits and circuit breakers
- Potential changes in trading hours or settlement cycles specific to Group 'B'
- Adjusted surveillance parameters

Investors and traders should familiarize themselves with Group 'B' trading rules before the effective date. The change does not affect the fundamental business operations or financial status of Advent Hotels International Limited.
