## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Sharekhan Limited on its Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. The issue comprises 8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 400 crores, issued on private placement basis with a tenor of 91 days maturing on March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 8,000 units (Rs. 400 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,700.26 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730722
- Scrip ID: SL91225
- Detail Name: SL-10-3-26-CP
- ISIN: INE211H14AI6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under ISIN INE211H14AI6

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: December 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 10, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on private placement basis and restricted to institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members can contact the debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
