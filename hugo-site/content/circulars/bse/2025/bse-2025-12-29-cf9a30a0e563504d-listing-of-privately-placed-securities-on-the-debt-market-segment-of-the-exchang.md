## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities of NJ Capital Private Limited on the debt market segment with effect from December 29, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE0MT207023, carrying 8.75% p.a. interest rate, maturing on December 26, 2027.

## Key Points

- Issuer: NJ Capital Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 977408, Scrip ID: 875NJCPL27
- ISIN: INE0MT207023
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Size: 2,500 debentures
- Interest Rate: 8.75% per annum (monthly payment)
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA-/STABLE
- Date of Allotment: December 26, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 26, 2027
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Market Lot: 1 debenture
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0MT207023
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 26, 2025
- Listing Date: December 29, 2025
- First Interest Payment: January 26, 2026
- Monthly Interest Payments: 26th of each month from January 26, 2026 to December 26, 2027
- Redemption Date: December 26, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing notification. The securities are privately placed and will trade only among qualified institutional buyers and sophisticated investors in the debt market segment. No impact on equity markets or general retail investors.
