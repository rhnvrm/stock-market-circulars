## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED on private placement basis. The listing comprises 300 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 15 crore. The instruments carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 300 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,88,408.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730609
- **Scrip ID**: UCL191125
- **ISIN**: INE583D14741
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Yes Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D14741
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 99 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine commercial paper listing representing short-term debt fundraising by UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED. The issue size of Rs. 15 crore is modest and targeted at institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to note the specific trading parameters (Rs. 5 lakh denomination, demat-only trading) but these are standard for commercial paper instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and qualified investors participating in the debt market. The IND A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness for short-term obligations.
