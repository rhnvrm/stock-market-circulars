## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Mirae Asset BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 ETF units effective December 19, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 Total Return Index with 1,884,753 units allotted at Rs.35.32 per unit. Trading will be in dematerialized form only in the B Group with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for a new ETF product. The impact on market operations is minimal as it simply adds another investment vehicle for market participants. The ETF provides exposure to dividend-yielding stocks from the BSE 500 universe. Trading members can facilitate client investments in this ETF starting December 19, 2025. The presence of six designated market makers ensures adequate liquidity for the ETF units.
