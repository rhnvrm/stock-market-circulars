## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Bandhan Silver ETF units effective December 12, 2025. The open-ended Exchange Traded Fund replicates and tracks the domestic price of silver. A total of 527,061 units have been allotted at an issue price of Rs. 178.35 per unit, with units trading in dematerialized form only in the 'E' Group with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Mutual Fund Name**: Bandhan Mutual Fund
- **Investment Manager**: Bandhan AMC Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 544639
- **ISIN**: INF194KB1KI9
- **Scrip ID**: SILVERBND
- **Total Units**: 527,061 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 178.35 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: E
- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Market Maker**: Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in Bandhan Silver ETF units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Units are classified under 'E' Group for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025 (Friday)
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands investment options for retail and institutional investors seeking exposure to domestic silver prices through an exchange-traded instrument. The presence of Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd as market maker ensures liquidity. Impact is limited to mutual fund segment participants and investors interested in commodity-linked ETFs.
