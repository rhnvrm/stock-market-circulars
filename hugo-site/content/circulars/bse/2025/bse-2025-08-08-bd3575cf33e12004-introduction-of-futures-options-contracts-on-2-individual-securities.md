## Summary

BSE announces the introduction of futures and options contracts for two individual securities - Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd and Suzlon Energy Ltd - in the Equity Derivatives segment effective August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new underlying securities added to derivatives trading
- Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd (Scrip Code: 543988)
- Suzlon Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532667)
- Trading to commence on August 18, 2025
- Contract details will be available in derivatives contract master file

## Regulatory Changes

Expansion of equity derivatives segment to include F&O contracts on two additional underlying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new contracts availability
- Contract master file to be referenced for detailed specifications
- File accessible through BSE extranet and website

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Contract master file generation (end of day)
- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commencement date for new F&O contracts

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact as it expands derivative trading opportunities for market participants. The addition of these contracts provides more hedging and speculation options for traders dealing with these underlying securities.
