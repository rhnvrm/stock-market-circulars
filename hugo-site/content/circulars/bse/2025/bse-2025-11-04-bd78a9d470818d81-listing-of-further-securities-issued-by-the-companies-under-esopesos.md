## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further equity shares issued by 11 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed with effect from Thursday, November 6, 2025. None of the shares are under lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 11 companies are listing additional shares issued under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 865,816 shares across all companies
- Effective date for trading: November 6, 2025
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed shares
- Largest issuance: Aditya Birla Fashion (225,779 shares)
- Notable companies include Wipro (42,156 shares), CarTrade Tech (187,800 shares), and Lupin (4,312 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for shares issued under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about these new listings for their operational awareness. No specific compliance actions required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Start Date**: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine ESOP issuances representing small percentages of total outstanding shares for each company. The absence of lock-in restrictions means shares can be traded immediately, but volumes are relatively small.

**Companies Affected**:
1. Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited (225,779 shares, Face Value: ₹10)

4. Lupin Ltd (4,312 shares, Face Value: ₹2)

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard listing process with no changes to trading procedures.
