## Summary

ASHIANA HOUSING LTD has partially redeemed its debentures, resulting in a reduced face value of Rs. 733,484.91 per debenture. The debentures will trade with this new reduced face value from September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ASHIANA HOUSING LTD
- ISIN: INE365D08026 (Scrip Code: 973220)
- Action: Part redemption of debentures
- New reduced face value: Rs. 733,484.91 per debenture
- Effective date for trading: September 5, 2025
- Reference: DR-709/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a corporate action notification for existing debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for debenture trading
- All trades from September 5, 2025 onwards must reflect the new face value
- Trading members are required to take note of this change

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 4, 2025
- Effective Date: September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This partial redemption affects the debt segment trading of ASHIANA HOUSING LTD debentures. Trading members and investors holding these debentures need to be aware of the significantly reduced face value when conducting transactions. The action demonstrates the company's debt management strategy through partial repayment to debenture holders.
