## Summary

BSE has issued notice (DR-753 to DR-757/2025-2026) suspending trading in 14 commercial papers due to scheduled redemptions. The suspension affects securities from major financial institutions and corporations including Can Fin Homes, Kotak Mahindra Prime, Sterlite Technologies, Jio Credit, Bajaj Finance entities, Standard Chartered Capital, ICICI Home Finance, and others. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective no-dealing dates.

## Key Points

- 14 commercial papers will be suspended from trading between November 11-17, 2025
- Record dates have been fixed for redemption purposes
- Trading suspension begins on the same date as the record date for each security
- Notice numbers DR-753/2025-2026 through DR-757/2025-2026 cover these actions
- Trading members are explicitly prohibited from dealing in these securities from specified dates

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for commercial paper redemptions following existing BSE debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the listed commercial papers from their respective no-dealing dates
- Members must update their systems to reflect trading suspension for affected ISINs
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption processing
