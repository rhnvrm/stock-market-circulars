## Summary

BSE has listed new perpetual debt securities issued by Aditya Birla Capital Ltd on private placement basis with effect from November 26, 2025. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 977312 under ISIN INE674K08059. The issue comprises 200 units with face value of Rs. 1 crore each, carrying an annual interest rate of 8.3765% with AAA rating from both CRISIL and ICRA.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- Quantity: 200 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,00,000 (Rs. 1 crore) per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crore
- Scrip Code: 977312
- Scrip ID: 838ABCLPER
- ISIN: INE674K08059
- Interest Rate: 8.3765% per annum
- Payment Frequency: Annually
- Security Type: Perpetual bonds with put/call options as per Key Information Document (KID)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA, ICRA AAA
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must follow the prescribed tick size of 1 paise
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Listing and Trading Commencement: November 26, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually (specific dates as per terms)
- Redemption: Perpetual (no fixed maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it pertains to institutional debt securities issued on private placement basis. The perpetual bonds are AAA-rated by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest credit quality. With face value of Rs. 1 crore per unit and perpetual nature, these securities are designed for institutional investors and do not affect retail markets. The listing provides Aditya Birla Capital with long-term capital at a competitive rate of 8.3765% annually. No impact on equity shares of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ABCAPITAL) is expected from this debt listing.
