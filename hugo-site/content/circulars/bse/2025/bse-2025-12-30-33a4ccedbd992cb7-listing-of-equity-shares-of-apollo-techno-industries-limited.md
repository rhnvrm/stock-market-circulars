BSE has announced that equity shares of Apollo Techno Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544671, symbol ATIL, and ISIN INE0X4A01013. The securities will be listed on the SME segment of BSE.

- Company Name: Apollo Techno Industries Limited
- Scrip Code: 544671
- Symbol: ATIL
- ISIN: INE0X4A01013
- Segment: SME
