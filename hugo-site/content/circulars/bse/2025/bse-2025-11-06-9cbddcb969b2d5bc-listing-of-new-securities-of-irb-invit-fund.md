## Summary

IRB InvIT Fund (Scrip Code: 540526) has listed 15,96,95,000 new units on BSE at an issue price of Rs. 62.95 per unit. These units were allotted on a preferential basis to sponsor and non-sponsor entities for cash consideration and rank pari-passu with existing units. The securities will commence trading from November 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total units listed: 15,96,95,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 62.95 per unit
- Allotment date: November 03, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 07, 2025
- ISIN: INE183W23014
- Distinguished Numbers: 1121905001 to 1281600000
- Units rank pari-passu with existing units

## Lock-in Details

- 1,79,54,250 units (Dist. Nos. 1121905001 to 1139859250) locked-in until November 06, 2028
- 14,17,40,750 units (Dist. Nos. 1139859251 to 1281600000) locked-in until November 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the listing and permitted to trade these securities with effect from November 07, 2025. Lock-in provisions must be observed as per the specified timelines.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 03, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: November 07, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (14.17 crore units)**: November 05, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (1.79 crore units)**: November 06, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment of units. The impact is limited to IRB InvIT Fund unitholders and involves capital raising through preferential issue. The lock-in periods indicate long-term commitment from certain holders, with approximately 11% of units locked for 3 years and 89% locked for 1 year.
