## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 80,000 new equity shares of Triton Valves Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505978) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from December 8, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 80,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1730/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 1740/- per share
- Issued to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 1200528 to 1280527
- Date of Allotment: October 1, 2025
- ISIN: INE440G01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in restrictions apply to the allotted shares as per details below
- Shares are subject to standard listing compliance requirements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 1, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement:** December 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (40,000 shares - Dist. Nos. 1240528 to 1280527):** June 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (40,000 shares - Dist. Nos. 1200528 to 1240527):** June 8, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (80,000) which represents minimal dilution. The preferential issue was completed through warrant conversion at Rs. 1740/- per share.

**Lock-in Structure:** Half of the allotted shares (40,000) are locked in until June 2026, and the remaining half until June 2027, which limits immediate trading supply.

**Investor Impact:** Standard preferential allotment with appropriate lock-in periods to prevent immediate market dumping. Trading members should update their records for accurate trading and settlement.
