## Summary

DSP Finance Private Limited has listed 4,000 units of Commercial Paper on BSE's Debt segment on a private placement basis. The instruments are valued at Rs. 5,00,000 per unit (total issue size Rs. 200 crore), with an allotment date of November 18, 2025, and redemption scheduled for February 16, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: DSP Finance Private Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (privately placed)
- **Total Units**: 4,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,278 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730596
- **Scrip ID**: DFRL181125
- **ISIN**: INE422H14107
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE422H14107
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term financing for DSP Finance Private Limited with a tenor of approximately 90 days. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates highest safety regarding timely payment. Trading is restricted to institutional and high-net-worth investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
