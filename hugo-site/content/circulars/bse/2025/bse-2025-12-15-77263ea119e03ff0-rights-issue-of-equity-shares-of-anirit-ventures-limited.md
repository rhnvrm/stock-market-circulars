## Summary

ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 530705) has announced a rights issue with record date set for December 18, 2025. The company will issue 2 equity shares of Rs. 10 each at a premium of Rs. 23 per share for every 1 equity share held. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-780/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED (Code: 530705)
- Rights ratio: 2 new equity shares for every 1 existing share held
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Premium: Rs. 23 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 33 per share
- Payment structure: Rs. 23 on application, balance Rs. 10 on subsequent call(s)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities - Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions on ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025
- Shareholders on record as of December 18, 2025 will be eligible for the rights entitlement
- Trading members must ensure proper settlement under DR-780/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-780/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Existing shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to additional shares at Rs. 33 per share. The ex-rights adjustment will impact the share price from December 18, 2025.

**Shareholder Impact:** Shareholders holding shares on the record date will receive rights entitlements in the ratio of 2:1. Payment can be made in installments with Rs. 23 payable on application and the balance on call.

**Trading Impact:** All trades from December 18, 2025 onwards will be conducted on an ex-rights basis, reflecting the rights entitlement separation from the share price.
