## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Game Changers Texfab Limited (Exchange ticker: 544594) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company is being listed on BSE effective November 4, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of Thursday, November 6, 2025. This is a routine index maintenance action following the company's listing.

## Key Points

- Game Changers Texfab Limited (ticker: 544594) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Company listing date: November 4, 2025 (Tuesday)
- Index addition effective date: November 6, 2025 (Thursday) at market open
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251103-44

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and investors monitoring the BSE SME IPO Index should note the addition for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Game Changers Texfab Limited listing on BSE
- **November 6, 2025**: Effective date of addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition affects:

- **Index Composition**: BSE SME IPO Index will include one additional constituent
- **Index Funds**: Passive funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to include this stock in their portfolios
- **Market Impact**: Limited to index-related trading activity during rebalancing
- **Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for SME IPO index investors and those tracking newly listed SME companies
