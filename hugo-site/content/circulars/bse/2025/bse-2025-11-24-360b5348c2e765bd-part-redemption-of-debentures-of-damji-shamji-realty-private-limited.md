## Summary

BSE has notified that DAMJI SHAMJI REALTY PRIVATE LIMITED will conduct a part redemption of its non-convertible debentures. The record date has been set as November 26, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value from the same date. The debentures (ISIN: INE815R07022, Scrip Code: 975254) maturing on December 15, 2026, will have their face value reduced to Rs. 38,422 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: DAMJI SHAMJI REALTY PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE815R07022
- Scrip Code: 975254
- Security Type: Zero-coupon non-convertible debentures (ZC)
- Maturity Date: December 15, 2026
- Record Date: November 26, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 38,422 per debenture
- Effective Date: November 26, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-764/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251124-17

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for part redemption of debentures as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the company's debentures will occur with the reduced face value effective from November 26, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 38,422 per debenture
- Debenture holders on record as of November 26, 2025, will be eligible for the part redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: November 26, 2025 (Settlement DR-764/2025-2026)
- **Original Maturity Date**: December 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine part redemption affecting a private limited company's debentures with limited market participation.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update face value in their systems. The two-day notice period provides adequate time for system adjustments.

**Investor Impact**: Moderate for debenture holders - Part redemption will provide early liquidity to debenture holders while reducing their remaining exposure. The reduced face value of Rs. 38,422 indicates a partial return of principal before maturity.
