## Summary

ZERODHA MUTUAL FUND is reopening the ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will be available with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities starting November 10, 2025. This follows the previous Notice No. 20251017-24.

## Key Points

- ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH is being reopened for transactions
- Scheme Category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01174
- Available facilities: Ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference to previous notice: 20251017-24

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of the scheme for processing investor transactions starting from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 - Reopening of scheme for ongoing transactions with SIP, STP & SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to:
- Investors interested in ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND
- Distributors and intermediaries using the BSE StAR MF Platform
- ZERODHA MUTUAL FUND operations

The reopening provides investors with renewed access to this equity index fund tracking the BSE SENSEX, along with systematic investment and withdrawal options. This is a routine operational matter affecting a single mutual fund scheme.
