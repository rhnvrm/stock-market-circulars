## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED on private placement basis effective December 8, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.75% per annum with half-yearly interest payments and mature on November 26, 2030. A total of 9,000 securities with face value of Rs. 100,000 each have been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED
- **Quantity**: 9,000 securities
- **Scrip Code**: 977347
- **Scrip ID**: 1175MGFL30
- **ISIN**: INE08KJ07167
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000.00 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000.00 per security
- **Interest Rate**: 11.75% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Half-yearly from May 26, 2026 to November 26, 2030
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-/Stable
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07167
- The tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at BSE on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Full details available in the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 8, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: May 26, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Half-yearly until November 26, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 26, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a routine listing of privately placed debt securities by a single issuer. The listing expands the BSE Debt segment offerings and provides investors access to ACUITE A-/Stable rated corporate bonds with 11.75% annual returns. Impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in corporate debt securities.
