## Summary

Allcargo Logistics Limited (532749) is demerging its International Supply Chain Business into a new entity, Allcargo Global Limited, effective from the start of trading hours on November 12, 2025. Shareholders will receive 1 equity share of Allcargo Global Limited for every 1 equity share held in Allcargo Logistics Limited. A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be conducted on November 12, 2025, to enable price discovery for Allcargo Logistics Limited post-demerger. Allcargo Global Limited will be added to multiple BSE indices at zero price without divisor adjustment.

## Key Points

- Demerger of International Supply Chain Business from Allcargo Logistics Limited into Allcargo Global Limited
- Share exchange ratio: 1:1 (1 equity share of Allcargo Global for every 1 equity share of Allcargo Logistics)
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) scheduled for price discovery of Allcargo Logistics post-demerger
- Allcargo Global Limited to be listed on BSE at a future date (date to be announced)
- Allcargo Global Limited will be added to 9 BSE indices at zero price after close of November 11, 2025
- No divisor adjustment will be made for index additions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notice regarding a demerger scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the Special Pre-Open Session on November 12, 2025, for Allcargo Logistics Limited
- Index tracking funds and portfolios need to adjust for the addition of Allcargo Global Limited to the following BSE Indices:
  - BSE SMALLCAP
  - BSE ALLCAP
  - BSE SERVICES
  - BSE 1000
  - BSE NEXT 500
  - BSE 250 MICROCAP
  - BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%)
  - BSE MIDSMALLCAP
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 11, 2025**: Last trading day before demerger becomes effective (close of trading)
- **November 12, 2025**: 
  - Demerger effective from start of trading hours
  - Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) for Allcargo Logistics Limited
  - Allcargo Global Limited added to BSE indices at zero price
- **Future date (TBA)**: Expected listing of Allcargo Global Limited equity shares on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- High impact on Allcargo Logistics shareholders who will receive additional shares in the demerged entity
- Price discovery through Special Pre-Open Session may result in significant price volatility for Allcargo Logistics post-demerger
- Addition of Allcargo Global Limited to 9 BSE indices at zero price will affect index composition and weighting

**Trading Impact:**
- Special Pre-Open Session on November 12, 2025, will affect normal trading operations for Allcargo Logistics
- Market participants need to adjust positions and strategies to account for the 1:1 share distribution

**Portfolio Impact:**
- Index funds tracking any of the 9 affected BSE indices will need to include Allcargo Global Limited in their portfolios
- No immediate divisor adjustment means index values will not be directly impacted at the time of addition
- Future price movements in Allcargo Global Limited will affect index performance once trading commences
