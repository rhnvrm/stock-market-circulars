## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Neptune Logitek Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544663, symbol NEPLOG, and ISIN INE1NQ501012. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544663
- Trading Symbol: NEPLOG
- ISIN: INE1NQ501012
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to prepare for the upcoming listing of Neptune Logitek Limited equity shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine new listing notification for the SME segment. Trading members and investors interested in SME stocks should watch for the follow-up notice with the actual listing date and trading commencement details. No immediate action required until listing date confirmation.
