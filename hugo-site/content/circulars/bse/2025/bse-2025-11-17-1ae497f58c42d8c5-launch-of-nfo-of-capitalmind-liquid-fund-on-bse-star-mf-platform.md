## Summary

CAPITALMIND MUTUAL FUND will launch CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from November 18, 2025 to November 21, 2025. Two plans are available: Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401034) and Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401042). The tentative allotment date is November 28, 2025, and clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 18, 2025 to November 21, 2025
- Two schemes offered: Direct Plan - Growth and Regular Plan - Growth
- Both schemes categorized as LIQUID fund
- Tentative allotment date: November 28, 2025
- NFO extended till midnight on November 21, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 27, 2025
- Clients must initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Pending transactions (where client initiated payment) will remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds pay-out or T+5, whichever is earlier
- For cheque and OTM payments, clear funds must be received in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on November 27, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 18, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **November 21, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 27, 2025**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account (by 6:00 PM)
- **November 28, 2025**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard NFO launch with minimal market impact. The circular provides operational guidance for BSE StAR MF platform members handling client subscriptions. The liquid fund category indicates a low-risk debt fund focused on short-term instruments. Impact is limited to mutual fund distributors and investors interested in liquid fund offerings from CAPITALMIND MUTUAL FUND.
