## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Meesho Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544632, symbol MEESHO, and ISIN number INE0VDM01015. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Meesho Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544632
- Trading Symbol: MEESHO
- ISIN: INE0VDM01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new listing and prepare for trading activity
- Members must use the designated scrip code (544632) and symbol (MEESHO) for all transactions
- Await separate notice for exact listing date and commencement of trading

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Meesho is a prominent e-commerce platform in India, and its listing is likely to attract significant investor attention and trading volume. This represents a major addition to the BSE equity segment.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details and prepare for potential high trading volumes upon listing. The staggered announcement (listing approval now, date later) allows members time to prepare their infrastructure.
