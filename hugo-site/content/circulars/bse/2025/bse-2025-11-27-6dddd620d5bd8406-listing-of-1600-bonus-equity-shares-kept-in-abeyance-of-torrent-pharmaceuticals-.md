## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,600 bonus equity shares of Torrent Pharmaceuticals Ltd (Scrip Code: 500420) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade effective Friday, November 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,600 bonus equity shares of Rs. 5/- each kept in abeyance are being listed
- ISIN: INE685A01028
- Trading to commence from November 28, 2025
- Two batches of shares: 800 shares (Distinctive No. 338437041 to 338437840) and 800 shares (Distinctive No. 338411241 to 338412040)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are involved. This is a standard listing procedure for shares previously kept in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the exchange are informed to note the listing of these securities for trading purposes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Effective Date:** November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal as this involves a small number of bonus shares (1,600) that were kept in abeyance. The listing is a procedural matter to enable trading of these shares and does not affect the overall market or company operations significantly. This is a company-specific administrative action with negligible market-wide implications.
