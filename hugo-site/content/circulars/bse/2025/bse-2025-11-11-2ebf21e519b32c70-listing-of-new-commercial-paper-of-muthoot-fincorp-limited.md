## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 11, 2025. The issuance consists of 5,000 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 250 crore, with a tenure of approximately 12 months.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Muthoot Fincorp Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 250 crore (5,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,60,887.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730534
- Scrip ID: MFL101125
- ISIN: INE549K14CE8
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CE8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Listing Date: November 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 354 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides short-term funding to Muthoot Fincorp Limited for working capital requirements. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates strong credit quality. Trading members dealing in debt securities can participate in trading these instruments from November 11, 2025. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
