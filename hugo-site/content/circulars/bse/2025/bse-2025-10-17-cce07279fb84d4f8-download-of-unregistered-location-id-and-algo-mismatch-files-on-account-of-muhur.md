## Summary

BSE has announced modified download schedules for unregistered Location ID and ALGO mismatch files specifically for the Muhurat Trading session scheduled on October 21, 2025. This is a procedural update for trading members regarding the availability timing of compliance files during the special festive trading session.

## Key Points

- UNLC1 and UNLC2 files will be available at 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM
- ALGO mismatch files (<SEG>_ALGO_MM_1) will be available at 2:30 PM
- This schedule applies specifically to the Muhurat Trading session on October 21, 2025
- Trading members can contact their Relationship Manager or BSE Helpdesk for queries
- Reference to previous notices: 20250922-21, 20250922-22, 20250922-23

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational adjustment for a special trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the modified file download timings for the Muhurat Trading session
- Members should download unregistered Location ID files (UNLC1, UNLC2) at the specified times: 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM
- ALGO mismatch files should be downloaded at 2:30 PM
- Members should ensure their systems are configured to handle the adjusted schedule

## Important Dates

- **October 21, 2025**: Muhurat Trading session
- **File availability times on October 21, 2025**:
  - 2:30 PM: UNLC1, UNLC2, and ALGO mismatch files
  - 3:15 PM: UNLC1 and UNLC2 files

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational adjustment for a special trading session. Trading members need to adjust their file download schedules for this single day only.

**Market Impact**: None - This does not affect trading activities or market operations directly; it only concerns backend compliance file downloads.

**Affected Parties**: Trading members who rely on automated systems to download unregistered Location ID and ALGO mismatch files need to adjust their schedules for October 21, 2025.

**Support Available**: BSE Helpdesk at 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 or bsehelp@bseindia.com, and respective Relationship Managers are available for assistance.
