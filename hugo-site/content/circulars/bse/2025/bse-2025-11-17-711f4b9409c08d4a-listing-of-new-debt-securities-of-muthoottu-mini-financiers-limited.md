## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited on its Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The company issued 5,000 non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of face value ₹1,00,000 each on a private placement basis, totaling ₹50 crore. The securities carry a 9.25% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on November 13, 2027. The securities are rated ICRA A (Stable) and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- **Scrip Code:** 977296
- **Scrip ID:** 925MMFL027
- **ISIN:** INE101Q07BT9
- **Quantity Issued:** 5,000 NCDs
- **Face Value:** ₹1,00,000 per NCD
- **Issue Price:** ₹1,00,000 per NCD
- **Total Issue Size:** ₹50 crore
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Credit Rating:** ICRA A (Stable)
- **Interest Rate:** 9.25% per annum
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly (10th of each month)
- **Date of Allotment:** November 13, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** November 13, 2027
- **Tenor:** 2 years
- **Put/Call Option:** Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode:** Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size:** 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE101Q07BT9
- The tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment:** December 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment Schedule:** Monthly from December 10, 2025 to November 13, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** November 13, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low. This is a routine private placement listing for debt securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited, a regional NBFC. The issue size of ₹50 crore is relatively small and will have minimal impact on overall debt market activity.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals who participated in the private placement or seek exposure to ICRA A-rated debt instruments with monthly income streams.

**Trading Impact:** The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with standard settlement procedures. The market lot of 1 unit (₹1 lakh face value) makes it accessible to qualified debt investors.
