## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 13, 2025. Seven securities have been newly added to the framework, while four securities are moving out due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks.

## Key Points

- 7 securities newly shortlisted in ST-ASM Framework (Stage I)
- 4 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Framework includes SME scrips and T+0 scrips
- Changes effective from August 13, 2025

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

**New additions (Stage I):**
- Adcounty Media India Ltd (544435) - SME scrip
- Binny Mills Ltd (535620)
- KBS India Ltd (530357)
- Khandelwal Extractions Ltd (519064)
- PG Electroplast Ltd (533581/133581) - NSE aligned, includes T+0 scrip
- Southern Magnesium & Chemicals Ltd (513498)

## Securities Removed from ST-ASM Framework

- Prostarm Info Systems Ltd (544410)
- RKD Agri & Retail Ltd (511169) - moved to GSM Framework
- Sunshield Chemicals Ltd (530845)
- Vuenow Infratech Ltd (531997)

## Compliance Requirements

- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to listed securities
- Special monitoring for price movements and trading volumes
- Additional disclosure requirements for companies under ST-ASM
- Stricter position limits and margin requirements

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 13, 2025
- Framework applies to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM stages

## Impact Assessment

The ST-ASM framework implementation will result in enhanced monitoring of the specified securities, potentially affecting their trading liquidity and investor participation. Companies moved out of ST-ASM to other frameworks (GSM) may face different regulatory requirements. The measure aims to maintain market integrity and protect investor interests through increased surveillance of volatile securities.
