## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The debt securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 5, 2025. The instruments comprise 2000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakhs each
- Scrip Code: 730695
- Scrip ID: MFL031225
- ISIN Number: INE522D14OZ0 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,64,515.50
- Allotment Date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2026
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands Manappuram Finance Limited's commercial paper offerings on BSE's debt segment. The securities carry strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL), indicating low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect broader equity market operations.
