## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd (Member No. 3129) will cease business operations in the Currency derivative segment due to voluntary closure. This is an administrative notification regarding member exit.

## Key Points

- DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd (Member No. 3129) is voluntarily closing business
- The closure affects only the Currency derivative segment
- Trading members are informed of this exit
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member exit notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Not specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single trading member's voluntary exit from the currency derivatives segment. No changes to trading rules, market operations, or other members' obligations. Standard administrative process for member exits.
