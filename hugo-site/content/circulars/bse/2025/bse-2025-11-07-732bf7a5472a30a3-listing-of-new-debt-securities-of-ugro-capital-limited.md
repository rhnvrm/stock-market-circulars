## Summary

Ugro Capital Limited has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 7, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 10,000 debentures, each having a face value of Rs. 1,00,000. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.65% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on May 6, 2031. The securities are rated IND A+ and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore
- Scrip Code: 977281
- Scrip ID: 1165UCL31A
- ISIN: INE583D08115
- Credit Rating: IND A+
- Interest Rate: 11.65% per annum
- Interest Payment: Monthly from December 6, 2025 to May 6, 2031
- Date of Allotment: November 6, 2025
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Put/Call Option: Not Available
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D08115
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 6, 2025
- Listing Date: November 7, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 6, 2025
- Monthly Interest Payments: 6th of every month from December 2025 to May 2031
- Redemption Date: May 6, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Ugro Capital Limited access to debt capital markets through private placement. For investors, these securities offer a fixed income opportunity with 11.65% annual returns and IND A+ credit rating. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular cash flows. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity trading operations or broader market dynamics.
