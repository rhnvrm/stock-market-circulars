## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Krazybee Services Ltd on private placement basis effective December 1, 2025. The securities comprise 50,000 units of non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crore. The debentures carry a 10.50% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on December 2, 2027. The securities are rated CRISIL A and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 units (market lot: 1 unit)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 500 crore
- Scrip Code: 977331
- Scrip ID: 1050KSL27
- ISIN: INE07HK07866
- Interest Rate: 10.50% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE07HK07866
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
- Listing Date: December 1, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 28, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from December 28, 2025 to December 2, 2027
- Maturity/Redemption Date: December 2, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds liquidity to the debt market segment for investors holding these securities. As a private placement with no public offer, there is no direct impact on retail investors or equity markets. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular income opportunity for institutional investors who participated in the private placement.
