## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in four government securities effective December 16, 2025, due to their maturity on December 18, 2025. The securities include three Treasury Bills (182-day, 364-day, and 91-day) and one Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for 4 government securities from December 16, 2025
- All securities are maturing on the redemption date of December 18, 2025
- Includes 182-day T-bill (ISIN: IN002025Y123)
- Includes 364-day T-bill (ISIN: IN002024Z362)
- Includes 91-day T-bill (ISIN: IN002025X257)
- Includes Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series (ISIN: IN0020170141)
- Notice issued under reference DR-778/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified T-bills and Sovereign Gold Bond from December 16, 2025
- Trading Members should note the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No transactions should be executed in these securities during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date:** December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has limited market impact as it follows standard procedures for maturing government securities. Debt market participants holding these securities should prepare for redemption. The suspension prevents any trading errors close to maturity and ensures orderly settlement. Investors expecting redemption proceeds should coordinate with their brokers for settlement on the maturity date.
