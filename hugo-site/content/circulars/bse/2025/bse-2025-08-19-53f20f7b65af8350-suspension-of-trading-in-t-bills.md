## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Treasury Bill 182TB22825 (ISIN: IN002024Y456, Scrip Code: 805029) effective August 20, 2025, as the instrument reaches its redemption date.

## Key Points

- Single T-Bill affected: 182TB22825 with ISIN IN002024Y456
- Scrip Code: 805029
- Trading suspension effective from August 20, 2025
- Reason: Natural maturity/redemption of the instrument

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from August 20, 2025
- Members are advised to take note of the suspension for their trading operations
- Reference number: DR-698/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one T-Bill reaching natural maturity. Routine administrative action with no broader implications for debt market operations or trading members' activities.
