## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ rating and are available for trading in the BSE Debt segment with effect from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (1000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,544.09 per unit
- CRISIL A1+ credit rating
- Scrip Code: 729923, ISIN: INE550X14AI4
- Allotment date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding requirements. The A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness. Trading will be limited to institutional and high-net-worth investors due to high denomination requirements.
