## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective December 29, 2025. Nine securities are being newly included in the ST-ASM framework, while 15 securities are being removed. No securities are being moved to higher or lower ASM stages within the framework. The consolidated list shows one security (Amines & Plasticizers Limited) currently in Stage I of the Short Term ASM framework.

## Key Points

- 9 securities newly shortlisted for Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework from December 29, 2025
- 15 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework effective December 29, 2025
- No securities moving to higher ASM stages
- No securities moving to lower ASM stages
- Securities exiting ST-ASM include moves to LT-ASM (1 security), Trade for Trade (4 securities), and others meeting exit criteria
- Framework includes both mainboard and SME scrips

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework application is being modified for 24 securities:

**New Inclusions (9 securities):**
1. Ceinsys Tech Ltd (538734)
2. Enterprise International Ltd (526574)
3. Global Capital Markets Ltd (530263)
4. Modi Rubber Ltd (500890)
5. My Money Securities Ltd (538862)
6. POCL Enterprises Ltd (539195)
7. Quantum Digital Vision India Ltd (530281)
8. Sasken Technologies Ltd (532663)
9. Vivid Mercantile Ltd (542046)

**Removals (15 securities):**
- 1 security moving to LT-ASM Framework: Teamo Productions HQ Ltd
- 4 securities moving to Trade for Trade Framework: Omni AXs Software Ltd, Relicab Cable Manufacturing Ltd, Swagtam Trading & Services Ltd, Valson Industries Ltd
- 10 securities meeting exit criteria including Asahi Songwon Colors Ltd, Bemco Hydraulics Ltd, Chemkart India Ltd, and others

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants must:
- Update trading systems to reflect new ST-ASM classifications effective December 29, 2025
- Apply appropriate surveillance measures and trading restrictions to newly included securities
- Remove ST-ASM restrictions from securities moving out of the framework
- Monitor securities moving to other surveillance frameworks (LT-ASM, Trade for Trade) and apply relevant restrictions
- Ensure proper disclosure and client communication regarding changed trading conditions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025 - All changes to ST-ASM framework become applicable
- **Circular Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Newly included securities will face enhanced surveillance and potential trading restrictions, likely affecting liquidity
- Securities exiting ST-ASM framework will see relaxed surveillance (unless moving to other frameworks)
- Four securities moving to Trade for Trade framework will experience continued restricted trading conditions
- One security moving to LT-ASM framework will remain under long-term surveillance

**Investor Impact:**
- Enhanced monitoring requirements for 9 newly included securities may result in price volatility
- Improved trading conditions for 10 securities completely exiting surveillance frameworks
- Continued restrictions for securities transitioning to other surveillance mechanisms

**Operational Impact:**
- Brokers and trading members must update risk management systems
- Margin requirements and position limits may be affected for securities in ST-ASM
- Enhanced reporting obligations for transactions in newly included securities
