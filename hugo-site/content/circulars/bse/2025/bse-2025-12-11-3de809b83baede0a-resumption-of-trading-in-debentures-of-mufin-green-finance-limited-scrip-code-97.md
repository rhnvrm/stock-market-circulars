## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code 977318) effective December 12, 2025. This follows clarification from the company that the previously notified record date of December 11, 2025 is only for interest payment purposes and not for redemption of debentures. Trading was previously suspended in anticipation of redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures with Scrip Code 977318 will resume from December 12, 2025
- Record date of December 11, 2025 is confirmed for interest payment only, not redemption
- This supersedes Exchange Notice No. 20251209-69 dated December 09, 2025
- Company clarified the purpose of the record date and requested trading resumption
- Reference number: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update correcting earlier communication regarding the purpose of the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note that debentures with Scrip Code 977318 will be available for trading from December 12, 2025
- No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading operations

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for interest payment on debentures
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading resumption date for Scrip Code 977318

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only a single debenture scrip. The clarification allows bondholders to continue trading these securities. Impact is confined to existing holders and potential investors in MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED debentures. Operational correction ensures market efficiency and prevents unnecessary trading suspension.
