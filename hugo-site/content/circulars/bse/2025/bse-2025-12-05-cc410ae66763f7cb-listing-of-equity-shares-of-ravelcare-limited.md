## Summary

Ravelcare Limited has listed its equity shares on BSE with a total of 68,59,000 shares. This circular discloses the initial shareholding pattern as per regulatory requirements under Regulation 31. The company has 5 promoter shareholders holding 72.94% and 465 public shareholders holding 27.06% of the total equity.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 68,59,000 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 50,02,998 shares (72.94%)
- Public holding: 18,56,002 shares (27.06%)
- Total shareholders: 470 (5 promoters + 465 public)
- Dematerialized shares: 55,30,000 shares (80.62% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants issued
- No depository receipts issued
- No shares in locked-in status
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a)/31(1)(b)/31(1)(c)
- Declaration required on partly paid shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and pledged shares
- Shareholding pattern to be disclosed in the prescribed tabular format
- Separate disclosure required for Promoter & Promoter Group and Public categories

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Pattern filing date: December 4, 2025 (if filed under Reg. 31(1)(b) for quarter ending)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure for a new listing with no material market impact. The shareholding pattern shows standard promoter-public distribution with promoters holding majority stake at 72.94%. The dematerialization rate of 80.62% is healthy for a new listing. The absence of pledged shares, locked-in shares, and convertible securities indicates a clean capital structure. This listing provides investment opportunity in Ravelcare Limited with reasonable public float of 27.06%.
