## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis. The securities, linked to NIFTY-50-INDEX, have been listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,500 securities with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, with a maturity date of February 11, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977375
- Scrip ID: IFSL121225
- ISIN Number: INE321N07608
- Detail Name: IFSL-NIFTY-50-INDEX-11-2-28-PVT
- Quantity: 2,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL PP-MLD AA+r/Stable
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07608
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at BSE (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Date of Redemption**: February 11, 2028
- **Date(s) of Payment of Interest**: Not Applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine private placement listing of debt securities with limited impact on broader market operations. The listing adds Rs. 25 crore worth of debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for institutional and qualified investors participating in debt markets, particularly those interested in NIFTY-50-INDEX linked structured debt products with AA+ credit rating.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard listing process with securities available for trading in dematerialized form through the existing BSE Debt segment infrastructure.
