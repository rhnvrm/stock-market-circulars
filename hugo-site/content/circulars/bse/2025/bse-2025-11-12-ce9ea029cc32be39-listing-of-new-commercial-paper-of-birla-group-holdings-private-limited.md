## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 2000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, effective from November 12, 2025. The instruments carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730543, Scrip ID: BGH121125
- ISIN Number: INE09OL14IC0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,664.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IC0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all transactions

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 12, 2025
- Listing Date: November 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 11, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instruments provide short-term financing to Birla Group Holdings Private Limited and offer investment opportunities for debt market participants. The dual A1+ ratings from CRISIL and ICRA indicate minimal credit risk. The 3-month maturity period is typical for commercial paper instruments used for working capital financing. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
