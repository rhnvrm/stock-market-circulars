## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd with Scrip Code 975892, effective December 03, 2025. The trading was previously suspended in connection with the record date fixed on July 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption of these debentures. The company has now requested resumption of trading following completion of the corporate action.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd (Scrip Code 975892) to resume from December 03, 2025
- Trading was previously suspended following Exchange Notice No. 20250721-46 dated July 21, 2025
- Record date for interest payment and redemption was July 23, 2025
- Reference number: DR-769/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for resumption of trading following completion of a corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note that the debentures having Scrip Code 975892 of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd will be available for trading from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **July 21, 2025**: Original suspension notice issued (Notice No. 20250721-46)
- **July 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 02, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption
- **December 03, 2025**: Trading resumption effective date

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to a specific debt instrument of a single issuer. The resumption allows normal trading activity to continue for holders and potential investors in these debentures. The impact is limited to participants interested in this particular scrip code and does not affect broader market operations or trading in other securities.
