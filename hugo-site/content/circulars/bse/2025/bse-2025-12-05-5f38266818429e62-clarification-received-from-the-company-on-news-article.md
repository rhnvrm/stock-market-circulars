## Summary

BSE has issued a surveillance notice seeking clarification from InterGlobe Aviation Ltd (IndiGo) regarding news articles published in Economic Times and other media channels. The exchange is verifying the accuracy of these reports to inform members and investors. As of December 5, 2025, the company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE surveillance department has sought clarification from InterGlobe Aviation Ltd (Scrip Code: 539448)
- News articles appeared in newspapers, websites, and television channels including economictimes.indiatimes.com
- Purpose is to verify accuracy and inform members/investors
- Reply from the company is currently awaited
- Notice issued by Surveillance Department on December 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine surveillance action under existing disclosure norms.

## Compliance Requirements

InterGlobe Aviation Ltd is required to provide clarification to BSE regarding the news articles in question. The company must respond to the exchange's query to ensure market transparency and investor awareness.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Reply Status**: Awaited (no deadline specified in circular)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard surveillance procedure with limited immediate market impact. The exchange routinely seeks clarifications when significant news articles appear about listed companies. Investors should await the company's official response for accurate information. The matter pertains to media verification rather than confirmed regulatory violations or material events.
