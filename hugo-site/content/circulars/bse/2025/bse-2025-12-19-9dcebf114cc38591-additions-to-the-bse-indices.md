## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (ticker: 544658) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective December 23, 2025, following its listing on December 19, 2025. Stanbik Agro Limited (ticker: 544659) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective December 22, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited (544658) listed on BSE on December 19, 2025
- Addition to BSE IPO index effective at market open on December 23, 2025
- Stanbik Agro Limited (544659) listed on BSE SME platform on December 19, 2025
- Addition to BSE SME IPO index effective at market open on December 22, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References previous notices: 20251218-56 and 20251218-50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine index rebalancing activity following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking funds and market participants using these indices should note the inclusions for portfolio adjustments.

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Listing date for both ICICI Prudential AMC and Stanbik Agro Limited
- **December 22, 2025**: Stanbik Agro Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **December 23, 2025**: ICICI Prudential AMC added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational update for index maintenance following new listings. Index-tracking funds may need to adjust holdings to reflect the new constituents. The staggered effective dates (December 22 and 23) allow for systematic inclusion. Investors and fund managers tracking these indices should be aware of the composition changes for rebalancing purposes.
