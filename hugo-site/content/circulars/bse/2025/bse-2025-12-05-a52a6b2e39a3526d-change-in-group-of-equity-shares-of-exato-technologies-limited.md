## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544626) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from December 19, 2025. Following this transfer, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted under M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544626
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251205-2
- Continuation of earlier Notice No. 20251204-53 dated December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment, which allows for more flexible trading including intraday trading and carry-forward positions, unlike the Trade for Trade segment which requires compulsory delivery.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the segment change effective December 19, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new M Group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 05, 2025
- Previous Related Notice: December 04, 2025 (Notice No. 20251204-53)
- Effective Date of Transfer: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This change enhances trading flexibility for EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment. This transition typically improves liquidity and allows for intraday trading and T+2 settlement, benefiting both trading members and investors. The move to M Group indicates improved trading status for the SME stock.
