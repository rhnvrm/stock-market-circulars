## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to dealings new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities totaling Rs. 500 Crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each) are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 11, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 Crores)
- Scrip Code: 730007
- Scrip ID: BHFL11825
- Detail Name: BHFL-09-02-26-CP
- ISIN: INE377Y14BP3 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,546 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Central Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE377Y14BP3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 10, 2025
- Listing Date: November 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 9, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact notification. This is a standard commercial paper listing on private placement basis in the debt segment. The listing provides short-term funding to Bajaj Housing Finance Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated paper (A1+). No impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading is restricted to debt segment participants dealing in wholesale debt instruments.
