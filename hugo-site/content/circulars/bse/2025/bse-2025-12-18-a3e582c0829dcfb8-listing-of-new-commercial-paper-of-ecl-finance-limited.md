## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective December 18, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 50 crore (1000 units at Rs. 5 lakh each). The instruments carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 1000 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,78,933.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730804
- **Scrip ID**: ECLF171225
- **ISIN**: INE804I14YH8
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE804I14YH8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members must ensure compliance with dematerialized trading requirements
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE platform. The impact is limited to participants in the debt segment who trade ECL Finance securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and qualified buyers who participate in commercial paper markets. The strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details for trading purposes.
