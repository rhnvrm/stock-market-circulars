## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Jio Credit Limited on a private placement basis. The instrument consists of 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5,000 crore. The CP is rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with allotment date of November 20, 2025 and redemption date of February 18, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,112.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730620
- Scrip ID: JCL201125
- ISIN: INE282H14063
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE282H14063
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size compliance: 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term financing for Jio Credit Limited with a tenor of approximately 90 days. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
