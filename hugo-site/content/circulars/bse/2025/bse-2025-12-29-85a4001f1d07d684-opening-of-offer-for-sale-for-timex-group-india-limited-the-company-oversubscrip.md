## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Timex Group India Limited and subsequent oversubscription notice. This circular provides information about the OFS mechanism and oversubscription status for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale opened for Timex Group India Limited
- The OFS has been oversubscribed
- Trading members and market participants need to be aware of the OFS schedule and procedures
- The circular provides details about bidding mechanism and settlement process

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard notification regarding an OFS event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform their clients about the OFS and oversubscription status
- Participants in the OFS must follow the prescribed bidding procedures
- Settlement will be carried out as per the OFS schedule
- Market participants should refer to the detailed circular for specific timelines and procedures

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 29, 2025
- OFS opening date and closure details to be referred from the detailed circular document
- Settlement schedule as per OFS mechanism

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact as it affects:
- Investors participating in the Timex Group India Limited OFS
- Trading members facilitating OFS bids
- Market operations specific to this security
- Price discovery and liquidity for Timex Group India Limited shares

The oversubscription indicates strong investor interest in the company's shares, which may influence secondary market trading post-OFS.
