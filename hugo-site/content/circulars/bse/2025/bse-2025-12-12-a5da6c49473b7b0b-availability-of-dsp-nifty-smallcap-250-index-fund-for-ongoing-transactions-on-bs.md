## Summary

BSE has announced that DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 15, 2025. This follows a previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251121-61. All plan types including Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options will be available for subscription, redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions from December 15, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: 3 Regular Plans and 3 Direct Plans
- All variants are equity category schemes
- Full transaction facilities restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- References previous suspension notice 20251121-61

## Scheme Details

Six ISIN codes activated:
1. Regular Plan - Growth (INF740KA1XE1)
2. Regular Plan - IDCW Payout (INF740KA1XF8)
3. Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (INF740KA1XG6)
4. Direct Plan - Growth (INF740KA1XH4)
5. Direct Plan - IDCW Payout (INF740KA1XI2)
6. Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (INF740KA1XJ0)

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems to enable transactions in these schemes
- Ensure all six ISIN codes are activated on the BSE StAR MF Platform by December 15, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 - Resumption of ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme on BSE's platform. Impact is limited to:
- Existing and prospective investors of DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND
- Mutual fund distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform
- No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations
- Positive for investors who were waiting to transact in this scheme during the suspension period
