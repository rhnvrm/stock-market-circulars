## Summary

BSE has listed two series of commercial paper issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 4, 2025. Series 1 consists of 5,500 units with redemption on December 3, 2026 (ISIN: INE522D14OZ0), and Series 2 consists of 7,500 units with redemption on March 2, 2026 (ISIN: INE522D14PB8). Both series have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+. Axis Bank acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Two new commercial paper series listed on BSE Debt segment
- Series 1: 5,500 units, Issue Price Rs. 464,425, Redemption December 3, 2026 (Scrip Code: 730695)
- Series 2: 7,500 units, Issue Price Rs. 491,727, Redemption March 2, 2026 (Scrip Code: 730696)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ for both series
- Date of allotment: December 3, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Series 1 Redemption**: December 3, 2026
- **Series 2 Redemption**: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt investment opportunities for institutional investors in Manappuram Finance Limited's commercial paper. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
