## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 21, 2025. The securities are rated CARE AA/Stable and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 17,500 debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 977307, Scrip ID: 895NWFL28
- ISIN: INE918K07QF6
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- Allotment Date: November 20, 2025
- Maturity Date: May 19, 2028
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QF6
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: May 19, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors access to CARE AA/Stable rated debt instruments from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited. The securities offer 8.95% annual interest with a tenure of approximately 2.5 years. Impact is limited to the debt market segment and relevant to investors seeking fixed-income instruments with strong credit ratings.
