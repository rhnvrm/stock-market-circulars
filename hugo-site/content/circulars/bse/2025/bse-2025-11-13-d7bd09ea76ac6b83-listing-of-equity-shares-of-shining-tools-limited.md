## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Shining Tools Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544607, symbol SHTL, and ISIN INE0D8001018. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shining Tools Limited
- Scrip Code: 544607
- Trading Symbol: SHTL
- ISIN: INE0D8001018
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the assigned scrip code and symbol for future trading activities once the listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 13, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will expand the BSE SME platform by one additional security.
