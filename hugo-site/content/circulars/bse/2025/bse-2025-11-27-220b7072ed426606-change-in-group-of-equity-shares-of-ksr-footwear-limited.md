## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of KSR Footwear Limited (Scrip Code: 544615) will be reclassified and permitted to trade under 'B' group effective Thursday, December 11, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: KSR Footwear Limited
- Scrip Code: 544615
- New Trading Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: December 11, 2025 (Thursday)
- Notice Number: 20251127-18
- Notice Date: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved to 'B' group, which typically indicates securities that meet certain criteria related to market capitalization, trading frequency, or corporate compliance standards as defined by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change and update their systems accordingly
- All trading in KSR Footwear Limited shares must comply with 'B' group trading rules from the effective date
- Members should ensure proper categorization in their trading terminals

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 11, 2025**: Effective date for group change - trading begins under 'B' group

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'B' group may result in changes to margin requirements, trading parameters, and settlement obligations for market participants trading in KSR Footwear Limited shares. Trading members should review the specific rules applicable to 'B' group securities and adjust their risk management frameworks accordingly.
