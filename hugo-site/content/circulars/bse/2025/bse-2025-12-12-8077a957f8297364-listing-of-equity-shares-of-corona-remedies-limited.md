## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Corona Remedies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544644, symbol CORONA, and ISIN INE02ZQ01018. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Corona Remedies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544644
- Symbol: CORONA
- ISIN: INE02ZQ01018
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new company joining BSE. Market impact is minimal until the actual listing date is announced and trading commences. Trading members should prepare their systems to accommodate the new scrip once the listing date is confirmed.
