## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding changes in scheme names of HDFC Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Systematic Transaction and Reporting for Mutual Funds) platform. This is an administrative update to reflect revised scheme nomenclature.

## Key Points

- Change in scheme names for HDFC Mutual Fund schemes
- Applicable to BSE StAR MF platform
- Administrative nomenclature update
- No impact on scheme operations or investor holdings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to scheme naming conventions on the BSE mutual fund platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and platforms to update scheme names in their systems
- No action required from investors
- Systems to reflect updated nomenclature

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 9, 2025
- Effective Date: December 9, 2025 (to be confirmed from circular content)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The scheme name changes on the BSE StAR MF platform are cosmetic updates that do not affect:
- Mutual fund scheme operations
- Investor holdings or NAV
- Trading or redemption processes
- Investment strategies or fund management

Market participants should update their systems to reflect the new nomenclature for accurate reporting and display purposes.
