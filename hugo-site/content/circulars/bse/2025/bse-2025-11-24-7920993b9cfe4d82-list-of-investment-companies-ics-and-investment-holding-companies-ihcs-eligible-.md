## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding Investment Companies (ICs) and Investment Holding Companies (IHCs) eligible for special call-auctions. Haryana Capfin Limited (Scrip Code: 532855) will be transferred from the trade-for-trade segment to Group X effective December 3, 2025. Easy Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code: 511074) will continue trading in the trade-for-trade segment with call auction until further notice.

## Key Points

- Haryana Capfin Limited (532855) to move from trade-for-trade segment to Group X
- Effective date for group change: December 3, 2025
- Easy Fincorp Limited (511074) remains in trade-for-trade segment with call auction
- This notice continues from previous circulars dated November 13, November 6, November 3, and October 15, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251124-11

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer of trading segment classification for Haryana Capfin Limited from the more restrictive trade-for-trade segment to Group X, allowing for normal trading operations. Easy Fincorp Limited continues under the trade-for-trade mechanism with call auction sessions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the segment transfer for Haryana Capfin Limited effective December 3, 2025
- Members should be aware that Easy Fincorp Limited continues in trade-for-trade with call auction
- For clarifications, members can contact Mr. Samit Mattoo at 022-2272 8103

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Effective Date for Group Change**: December 3, 2025 (Haryana Capfin Limited)
- **Previous Related Notices**: November 13, November 6, November 3, and October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The transfer of Haryana Capfin Limited from trade-for-trade to Group X will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for this security. The trade-for-trade segment typically has restrictions on intraday trading and requires full upfront delivery, while Group X allows normal trading.

**Market Participants**: Traders and investors in Haryana Capfin Limited can expect improved market access from December 3, 2025. Easy Fincorp Limited investors continue to face restrictions with trade-for-trade and call auction mechanisms.

**Limited Scope**: This circular affects only two specific companies and their trading mechanisms, representing a narrow market impact focused on investment company securities.
