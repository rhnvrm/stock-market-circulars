## Summary

NACL Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 524709) has announced a rights issue with a record date of December 12, 2025. The company will issue 5 equity shares of Re. 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share (total price Rs. 76.70) for every 31 existing equity shares held. Trading on an ex-rights basis will commence from December 12, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 5 new equity shares for every 31 existing shares held
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 75.70 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 76.70 per share
- Payment: Full issue price payable on application
- Scrip code: 524709
- Security type: Dematerialised securities in rolling settlement segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing SEBI guidelines for rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions on an ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025
- Trading members are required to note the record date and settlement details
- Shareholders must hold shares as of the record date to be eligible for rights entitlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive rights entitlements to subscribe to new shares at Rs. 76.70 per share. Shareholders can choose to exercise their rights, sell the rights entitlements, or let them lapse.

**Market Impact**: The stock will trade ex-rights from December 12, 2025, which typically results in price adjustment to reflect the dilution effect. The rights issue will increase the outstanding share capital of the company.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems correctly handle the ex-rights trading and settlement procedures from the specified date.
