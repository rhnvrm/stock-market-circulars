## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions for three securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. Can Fin Homes Ltd. and Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. have declared dividends, while Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited has announced a bonus issue. These corporate actions will result in shut periods for SLB transactions in these securities.

## Key Points

- Can Fin Homes Ltd. dividend with ex-date and record date of December 19, 2025
- Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. dividend with ex-date and record date of December 18, 2025
- Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited bonus issue with ex-date, record date, and foreclosure date of December 19, 2025
- SLB shut periods have been specified for all three securities
- These corporate actions will affect SLB lending and borrowing positions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a routine notification of corporate actions affecting SLB securities in accordance with existing SLB framework regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with SLB positions in these securities must note the shut periods
- No new lending or borrowing transactions will be permitted during the shut periods
- Existing SLB positions may be affected by the corporate actions
- Participants should adjust their positions before the shut period begins

## Important Dates

**Can Fin Homes Ltd.**
- Ex-Date: December 19, 2025
- Record Date: December 19, 2025
- Shut Period: December 18-19, 2025

**Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.**
- Ex-Date: December 18, 2025
- Record Date: December 18, 2025
- Shut Period: December 17-18, 2025

**Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited**
- Ex-Date: December 19, 2025
- Record Date: December 19, 2025
- Foreclosure Date: December 19, 2025
- Shut Period: December 18-19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects three actively traded securities in the SLB segment. Participants with lending or borrowing positions need to plan for the shut periods.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - SLB participants must ensure their positions are managed appropriately before the shut periods. The bonus issue for Dr. Lal Pathlabs will result in position adjustments.

**Trading Impact:** SLB transactions in these securities will be suspended during the specified shut periods. Participants should complete any required transactions before the shut periods commence.
