## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited (RRVL) on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The listing comprises 42,500 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+. The instruments were allotted on November 25, 2025 and will mature on February 24, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 42,500 Commercial Papers listed
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,582.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730658
- Scrip ID: RRVL251125
- ISIN: INE929O14EK7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that:
- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EK7
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applies to these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) with a tenor of approximately 91 days. The listing provides Reliance Retail Ventures Limited with additional short-term financing options and offers debt market participants investment opportunities in highly-rated commercial paper. The impact on broader market operations is minimal, representing standard corporate treasury management activity.
