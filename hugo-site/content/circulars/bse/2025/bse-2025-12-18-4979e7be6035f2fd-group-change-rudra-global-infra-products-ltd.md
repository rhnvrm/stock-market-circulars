## Summary

BSE has announced a group change for Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd (Scrip Code: 539226), which will be moved from Group X to Group B with effect from December 19, 2025. This reclassification typically indicates improved compliance standing or trading activity metrics.

## Key Points

- Company: Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd
- Scrip Code: 539226
- Current Group: X
- New Group: B
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251218-10

## Regulatory Changes

The company will be subject to Group B trading rules and surveillance mechanisms instead of Group X restrictions. Group B generally applies to companies with better compliance records compared to Group X.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- All trading in this scrip will follow Group B norms from December 19, 2025
- Members should ensure proper categorization in their trading platforms

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The migration from Group X to Group B is generally positive, indicating improved regulatory compliance or trading metrics. This change may result in:

- Reduced surveillance scrutiny
- Potentially improved liquidity
- Better investor confidence
- Changed margin requirements or trading limits as per Group B norms

Trading members and investors should note the reclassification when placing orders from December 19, 2025 onwards.
