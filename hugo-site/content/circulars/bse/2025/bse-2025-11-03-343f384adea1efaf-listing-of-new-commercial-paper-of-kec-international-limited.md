## Summary

KEC International Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 3, 2025. The issuance consists of 1,500 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, aggregating to Rs. 75 crores. The securities carry high credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ and will mature on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: KEC International Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 1,500 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 75 crores
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,643.50 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730476
- **Scrip ID**: KIL031125
- **ISIN**: INE389H14LM9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LM9
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum market lot is 1 security
- Tick size for price quotation is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Tenure**: 88 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of KEC International Limited. The short-term debt instrument is typically used for working capital requirements and does not affect the equity structure of the company. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt segment. No impact on equity shareholders or general trading operations.
