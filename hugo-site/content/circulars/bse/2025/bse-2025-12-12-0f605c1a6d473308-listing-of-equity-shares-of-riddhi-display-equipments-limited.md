## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Riddhi Display Equipments Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544640, symbol RDEL, and ISIN INE1DKT01011. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company Name: Riddhi Display Equipments Limited
- Scrip Code: 544640
- Trading Symbol: RDEL
- ISIN: INE1DKT01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be notified separately

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

Trading members should note the scrip details for Riddhi Display Equipments Limited and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced.
