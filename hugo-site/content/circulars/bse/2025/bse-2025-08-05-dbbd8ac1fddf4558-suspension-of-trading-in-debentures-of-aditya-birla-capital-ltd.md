## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD (Code: ABCL-9.25%-22-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE860H08DN6) effective August 7, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific debentures: ABCL-9.25%-22-8-25-PVT (Code: 957637)
- ISIN: INE860H08DN6
- Record date: August 7, 2025
- No dealings allowed from August 7, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 7, 2025
- Trading members are advised to take note of the suspension
- Compliance with corporate action procedures for debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Start**: August 7, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to holders and traders of specific ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL debentures. The suspension facilitates the corporate action process for interest payment and redemption. Trading members dealing in these debt securities must adjust their trading strategies accordingly.
