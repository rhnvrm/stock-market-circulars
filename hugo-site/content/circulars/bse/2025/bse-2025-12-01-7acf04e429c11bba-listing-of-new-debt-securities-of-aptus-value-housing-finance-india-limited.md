## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977324
- Scrip ID: 795AVHFL30
- ISIN: INE852O07188
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000.00
- Interest Rate: 7.95% per annum
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE852O07188
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly from December 27, 2025 to November 27, 2030
- **Redemption Date:** November 27, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on a private placement basis and will be traded on the BSE Debt segment. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates good credit quality. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular income to investors. No put/call options are available on these securities.
