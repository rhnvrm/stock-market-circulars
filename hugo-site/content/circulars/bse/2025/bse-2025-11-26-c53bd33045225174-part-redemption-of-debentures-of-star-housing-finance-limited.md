## Summary

STAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED has announced part redemption and interest payment on two series of its non-convertible debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values from the specified effective dates following the record dates.

## Key Points

- Two debenture series (ISIN: INE526R07017 and INE526R07025) are subject to part redemption
- SHFL-13.10%-19-4-27-PVT debentures will have reduced face value of Rs. 38,095.25 per debenture
- SHFL-13.35%-25-11-27-PVT debentures will have reduced face value of Rs. 75,000 per debenture
- Trading will commence with reduced face values from respective effective dates
- Notice issued by BSE on November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in face values effective from specified dates
- Debentures will trade at reduced face values post the effective dates

## Important Dates

| Debenture Series | ISIN | Record Date | Effective Date | Settlement Number |
|-----------------|------|-------------|----------------|-------------------|
| SHFL-13.10%-19-4-27-PVT | INE526R07017 | December 4, 2025 | December 4, 2025 | DR-770/2025-2026 |
| SHFL-13.35%-25-11-27-PVT | INE526R07025 | December 10, 2025 | December 10, 2025 | DR-774/2025-2026 |

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact restricted to debenture holders of STAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED. This is a routine corporate action for part redemption and interest payment on privately placed non-convertible debentures. The reduced face values will be effective from the specified dates for trading purposes. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.
