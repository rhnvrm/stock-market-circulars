## Summary

KOHIMA-MARIANI TRANSMISSION LIMITED has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE483Z08012, Code: 975831) with a Record Date of December 15, 2025. Following the redemption, the debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 95,650 per debenture effective from December 15, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-777/2025-2026). The company will also make interest payments to eligible debenture holders.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures (KMTL-30-6-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE483Z08012, BSE Code: 975831
- Record Date: December 15, 2025
- New reduced face value: Rs. 95,650 per debenture
- Effective date for reduced face value trading: December 15, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-777/2025-2026
- Debenture holders will receive interest payment on record date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action for part redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure debentures trade with reduced face value from December 15, 2025
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 95,650 per debenture
- Debenture holders must hold securities as on Record Date (December 15, 2025) to be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Notice Date
- **December 15, 2025**: Record Date for interest payment and part redemption
- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-777/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Holders as on Record Date will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. Post-redemption, their holdings will have reduced face value.

**Trading Impact**: From December 15, 2025, the debentures will trade at the reduced face value of Rs. 95,650, which trading members and investors must account for in valuation and settlement.

**Market Operations**: Limited impact confined to holders of this specific debenture series (KMTL-30-6-27-PVT). Trading members need to update systems for the new face value.
