## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of CHOICE FINSERV PRIVATE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective August 13, 2025. The securities carry an 11% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 11, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,500 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum paid monthly
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- ISIN: INE04SE07137
- Scrip Code: 977023

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed information
- Contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Monthly from September 11, 2025 to August 11, 2027
- Redemption Date: August 11, 2027
- Trading Effective: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options for debt market participants but does not affect broader market operations or equity trading.
