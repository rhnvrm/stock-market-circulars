## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities from 11 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading from August 13, 2025, with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total securities range from 1,000 shares (Arvind Fashions) to 4.38 million shares (Suzlon Energy)
- All securities have no lock-in period restrictions
- Trading commences August 13, 2025

## Companies and Share Details

- **Arvind Fashions Limited**: 1,000 shares (₹4 face value)
- **Fedbank Financial Services**: 50,687 shares (₹10 face value) 
- **Five-Star Business Finance**: 6,385 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Go Digit General Insurance**: 18,859 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Hind Rectifiers**: 21,132 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Monarch Networth Capital**: 31,000 shares (₹10 face value)
- **NIIT Ltd**: 149,999 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Sudarshan Chemical**: 23,584 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Suzlon Energy**: 4,385,250 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Wipro Ltd**: 198,402 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Yes Bank**: 920,629 shares (₹2 face value)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP/ESOS listings representing employee compensation. The securities have no lock-in restrictions, allowing immediate trading liquidity.
