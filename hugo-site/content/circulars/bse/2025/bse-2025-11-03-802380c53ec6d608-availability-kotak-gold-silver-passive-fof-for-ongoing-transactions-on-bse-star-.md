## Summary

BSE has announced that KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 04, 2025. This follows reference to earlier Notice No. 20251003-38.

## Key Points

- KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopening for all ongoing transactions
- Available facilities: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- Both plans classified under Equity category
- Notice Number: 20251103-31

## Scheme Details

**1. KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: EQUITY
- ISIN: INF174KA1XU2

**2. KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: EQUITY
- ISIN: INF174KA1XT4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members and investors should be aware of the scheme's availability for transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 04, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme. The reopening allows investors to resume transactions in the KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF through BSE's mutual fund platform. Impact is limited to existing and potential investors of this particular fund of funds scheme focused on gold and silver investments.
