## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for all schemes of Taurus Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. This facility is effective immediately following communication from Taurus Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP facility introduced for all Taurus Mutual Fund schemes
- Available on BSE StAR MF platform
- Effective immediately from November 18, 2025
- Communication received from Taurus Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform enhancement to existing mutual fund investment facilities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice regarding platform capability enhancement.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** November 18, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date:** November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This development provides investors with greater flexibility in their investment frequency for Taurus Mutual Fund schemes. The daily SIP option allows for more frequent, smaller investments compared to traditional monthly SIPs. The impact is limited to users of the BSE StAR MF platform who invest in Taurus Mutual Fund schemes. No operational changes required for BSE members.
