## Summary

Capillary Technologies India Limited has finalized the allocation of 68,28,001 equity shares to 21 anchor investors at an anchor investor allocation price of ₹577.00 per equity share (including share premium of ₹575.00) on November 13, 2025. The IPO comprises a fresh issue of up to ₹3,450.00 million and an offer for sale of up to 9,228,796 equity shares. The allocation was decided by the IPO Committee in consultation with the Book Running Lead Managers: JM Financial Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, and Nomura Financial Advisory and Securities (India) Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Total anchor investor allocation: 68,28,001 equity shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: ₹577.00 per equity share (face value ₹2, premium ₹575)
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: ₹394.18 crores (approximate)
- Number of anchor investors: 21 institutional investors
- IPO structure: Fresh issue up to ₹3,450 million + Offer for sale of 9,228,796 shares
- Largest allocations: ICICI Prudential Innovation Fund, Aditya Birla Sun Life Digital India Fund, and Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund (9.65% each)
- Allocation date: November 13, 2025
