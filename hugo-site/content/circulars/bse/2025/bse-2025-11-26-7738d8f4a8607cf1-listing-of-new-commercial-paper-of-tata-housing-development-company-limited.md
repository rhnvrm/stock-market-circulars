## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading in the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 26, 2025. The issue comprises 1000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, credit rated CARE A1+ and IND A1+, with a 91-day tenure maturing on February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Tata Housing Development Company Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 1000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,425 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,575)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730660
- **Scrip ID**: THDC251125
- **ISIN**: INE582L14JA0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14JA0
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard BSE Debt segment trading rules and compliance requirements apply
- Trading members requiring clarifications may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Standard debt segment trading operations apply. No special procedures or system changes required for trading members.
