## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by TATA Steel Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The listing comprises 22,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, assigned scrip code 730723 and scrip ID TSL081225. The instruments carry ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Quantity: 22,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,929 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730723
- Scrip ID: TSL081225
- ISIN: INE081A14GK1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- Trading Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE081A14GK1
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot is 1 unit with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 12, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides TATA Steel Limited with short-term funding through commercial paper issuance and offers institutional investors additional debt instruments for investment. The securities are limited to dematerialised trading on the debt segment and do not affect equity market operations. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
