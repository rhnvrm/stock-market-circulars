## Summary

ASCEND TELECOM INFRASTRUCTURE PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date has been set as November 21, 2025, and trading in the debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: ASCEND TELECOM INFRASTRUCTURE PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE889K08038
- Code: 974895 (ATIPL-11.35%-11-6-28-PVT)
- Record Date: November 21, 2025
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 80,000 per Debenture
- Effective Date: November 21, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 80,000 per debenture effective from November 21, 2025
- Trading Members should ensure proper processing of the part redemption and interest payment based on the record date

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 21, 2025 - Date for determining eligible debenture holders for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date**: November 21, 2025 - Date from which trading will be with reduced face value
- **Settlement**: DR-761/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The part redemption will reduce the outstanding face value of the debentures to Rs. 80,000 per debenture. Impact is confined to holders of these specific private company debentures (ISIN: INE889K08038). Trading members dealing with these securities need to ensure systems are updated to reflect the reduced face value from the effective date.
