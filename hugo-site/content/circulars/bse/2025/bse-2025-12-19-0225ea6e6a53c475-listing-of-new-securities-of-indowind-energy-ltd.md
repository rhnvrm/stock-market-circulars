## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 3,22,00,434 new equity shares of Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894) issued on a rights basis. The shares are issued at face value of Rs. 10 per share with a premium of Rs. 5.35, totaling Rs. 15.35 per share. These securities will be available for trading from Monday, December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894)
- Number of shares: 3,22,00,434 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue premium: Rs. 5.35 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 15.35 per share
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE227G01018
- Distinctive Numbers: 128801737 to 161002170
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 22, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date:** December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The rights issue increases Indowind Energy Ltd's equity base by 3.22 crore shares. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their holdings reflected with the new distinctive numbers. The shares being pari-passu ensures equal rights with existing shareholders. Market participants can trade these securities from December 22, 2025 onwards.
