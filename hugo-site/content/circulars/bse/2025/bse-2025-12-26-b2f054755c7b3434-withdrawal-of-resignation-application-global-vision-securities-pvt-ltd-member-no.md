## Summary

BSE has announced that Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application that was originally submitted on December 1, 2025. The trading member will continue its operations on the exchange. This reverses the resignation process for which a public notice was published in Financial Express and Navshakti newspapers on November 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application
- Original resignation application was submitted and notified via Exchange Notice No. 20251201-2 dated December 1, 2025
- Public notice regarding the resignation was published on November 29, 2025 in Financial Express and Navshakti newspapers
- The trading member will continue its membership with BSE
- All constituents and trading members have been informed of this withdrawal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reversing a previously announced resignation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other members. This is an informational notice.

For queries, constituents may contact BSE at membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- December 1, 2025: Original resignation application submitted (Exchange Notice No. 20251201-2)
- November 29, 2025: Public notice published in newspapers
- December 26, 2025: Withdrawal of resignation application announced

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative matter concerning a single trading member's decision to continue operations rather than resign from the exchange. No operational disruptions or changes to trading activities are expected.
