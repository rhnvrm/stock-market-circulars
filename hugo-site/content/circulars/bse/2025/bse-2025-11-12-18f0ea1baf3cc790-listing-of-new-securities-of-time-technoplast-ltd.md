## Summary

Time Technoplast Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532856, ISIN: INE508G01029) has listed 3,97,77,247 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each on BSE with effect from November 13, 2025. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 201.12 per share. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 3,97,77,247 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) through QIP route
- Issue price: Rs. 201.12 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 453858133 to 493635379
- Trading commences: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI regulations for QIP issuances.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance action required from members beyond awareness of the increased share capital.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 13, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The QIP of approximately 3.98 crore shares at Rs. 201.12 per share raises approximately Rs. 800 crore for Time Technoplast Ltd. This represents a material equity dilution and will increase the company's free float. The listing will increase liquidity in the stock.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal impact on trading operations. The new shares will be available for trading from November 13, 2025, with the same rights as existing shares. Contact person for clarifications: Mr. Pranav Rewale (Tel: 9653268851).
