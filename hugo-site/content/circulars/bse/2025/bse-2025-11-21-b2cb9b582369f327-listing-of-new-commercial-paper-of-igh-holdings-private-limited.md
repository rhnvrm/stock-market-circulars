## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 21, 2025. The Commercial Papers carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- 9000 Commercial Papers issued, each with face value of Rs.5,00,000/-
- Issue price: Rs.491640.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730622
- Scrip ID: IGHH211125
- ISIN: INE02FN14689
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14689
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds short-term debt securities to the BSE Debt segment, providing institutional investors with additional commercial paper investment options. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
