## Summary

BSE has notified that trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 6593) will be renamed to "RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This is a routine administrative change in the trading member's corporate name.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 6593)
- New Name: RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number remains unchanged: 6593
- Change is administrative in nature with no operational impact
- Queries to be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice about a member's name change.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 25, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 26, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the specific trading member undergoing the name change. No impact on trading operations, market structure, or other trading members. The member number remains the same, ensuring continuity in member identification and operations.
