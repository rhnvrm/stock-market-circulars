## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on the BSE Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The issue consists of 6000 units of Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with allotment date of November 4, 2025 and redemption date of February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 6000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,546 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730499
- Scrip ID: ABHF041125
- ISIN: INE831R14FI9
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FI9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice date: November 6, 2025
- Allotment date: November 4, 2025
- Listing effective date: November 6, 2025
- Redemption date: February 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides debt market participants access to short-term debt instruments of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited with a tenor of approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. The impact is limited to debt segment traders and institutional investors interested in short-term corporate debt instruments.
