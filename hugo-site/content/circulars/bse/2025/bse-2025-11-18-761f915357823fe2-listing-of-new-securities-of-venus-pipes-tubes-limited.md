## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 1,04,000 new equity shares of Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 543528) effective from November 19, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until May 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 1,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,690/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1,700/- per share
- Allotment date: September 5, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 19, 2025
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive numbers: 20492111 to 20596110
- ISIN: INE0JA001018
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- All 1,04,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until May 29, 2027
- No trading permitted in these locked-in shares until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 19, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a small preferential allotment to promoters representing minimal dilution. The shares are fully locked-in for approximately 18 months, ensuring no immediate market supply.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard listing procedure with no impact on trading operations for existing shares.
