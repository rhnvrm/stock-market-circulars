## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited effective December 31, 2025, as the security will mature on January 1, 2026. The affected security is AIHFPL-12.29%-1-1-26-PVT with ISIN INE0E2307146 and scrip code 974102. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited debt security to mature on January 1, 2026
- Trading suspension effective from December 31, 2025
- Affected security: AIHFPL-12.29%-1-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE0E2307146
- Scrip Code: 974102
- Notice reference: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt security from December 31, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant clients

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 31, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 1, 2026**: Maturity date of debt security

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine suspension affecting a single privately placed debt security of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited. Only holders of this specific debt instrument are affected. The suspension prevents trading one day prior to maturity, which is standard practice for maturing securities.
