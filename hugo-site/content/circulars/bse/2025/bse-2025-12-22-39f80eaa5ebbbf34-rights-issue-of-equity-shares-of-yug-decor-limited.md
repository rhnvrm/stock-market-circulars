## Summary

YUG DECOR LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540550) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with record date set for December 23, 2025. The company will issue 1 equity share of Rs.10 each for every 2 equity shares held by shareholders. The issue price is Rs.10 per share (at par), payable at the time of application. Trading will be conducted on ex-rights basis from December 23, 2025 under settlement number DR-783/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 1 equity share for every 2 equity shares held
- Face value: Rs.10 per share
- Issue price: Rs.10 per share (at par)
- Payment terms: Full amount payable at time of application
- Company: YUG DECOR LIMITED (BSE Code: 540550)
- Settlement: Dematerialised securities - rolling settlement segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must conduct transactions on ex-rights basis from December 23, 2025
- All trades in YUG DECOR LIMITED shares must reflect the rights entitlement adjustment
- Trading members are required to note the record date and settlement details

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-783/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will impact existing shareholders of YUG DECOR LIMITED who will be entitled to subscribe to additional equity shares in the ratio of 1:2. The ex-rights trading from December 23, 2025 will result in price adjustments to reflect the rights entitlement. Shareholders on record as of December 23, 2025 will be eligible to participate in the rights issue. The at-par pricing indicates the issue price equals the face value of the shares.
