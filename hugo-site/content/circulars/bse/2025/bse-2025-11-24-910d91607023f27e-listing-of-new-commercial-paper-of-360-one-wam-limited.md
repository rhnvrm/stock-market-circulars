## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The listing comprises 3000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under scrip code 730625.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: 360 ONE WAM LIMITED
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 3000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,279
- **Scrip Code**: 730625
- **Scrip ID**: OWL211125
- **ISIN**: INE466L14FG1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the designated ISIN Number INE466L14FG1 for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) on private placement basis. The securities provide an additional fixed-income investment option for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip code and trading parameters. Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to qualified institutional buyers and investors participating in private placements. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
