## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of SATIN CREDITCARE NETWORK LIMITED (ISIN: INE836B07782, Code: 975154) effective January 5, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture: SCNL-11%-13-01-26-PVT (INE836B07782)
- BSE Security Code: 975154
- Record Date: January 5, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 5, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-791/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from January 5, 2026
- Trading members should inform their clients holding or intending to trade in these debentures about the suspension
- No dealings permitted in debenture with ISIN INE836B07782 effective from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **January 5, 2026**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **January 5, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and relates to standard corporate action processing for redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of January 5, 2026 will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No new trading positions can be established from the suspension date.

**Liquidity Impact**: Complete halt in secondary market trading for this debenture series from January 5, 2026 onwards, preventing entry or exit for investors after the suspension date.
