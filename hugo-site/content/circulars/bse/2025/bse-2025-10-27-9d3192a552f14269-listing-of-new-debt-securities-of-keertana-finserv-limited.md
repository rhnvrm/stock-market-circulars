## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Keertana Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective October 27, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 10,000, carrying an interest rate of 11.40% per annum with monthly interest payments, and maturing on October 24, 2027. The debentures are rated IND BBB+/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977241, Scrip ID: 1140KRFL27
- ISIN Number: INE0NES07287
- Total Quantity: 101,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- Interest Rate: 11.40% per annum
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: October 24, 2025
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07287
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from November 24, 2025 to October 24, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 24, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing for a single issuer on private placement basis with limited quantity (101,000 units).

**Investor Impact**: Low. The debentures offer 11.40% annual returns with monthly interest payments over a 2-year tenure, rated BBB+/Stable, suitable for investors seeking fixed income instruments.

**Operational Impact**: None. Standard listing process with no changes to existing trading procedures or systems.
