## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 3,000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The instruments carry a CRISIL A1+ rating and will be redeemed on February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 3,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,92,531 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730579
- **Scrip ID**: ABLB141125
- **Detail Name**: ABLBL-12-02-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE14LE14046
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE14LE14046
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions subject to BSE Debt segment rules and regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market-wide implications. The instruments are short-term debt securities with a 90-day tenure.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members of BSE Debt segment can trade these instruments following normal protocols.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily to institutional and high-net-worth investors in the debt market seeking short-term investment opportunities with CRISIL A1+ rated paper. The high denomination (Rs.5 lakhs minimum) restricts accessibility to retail investors.
