## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the part redemption of non-convertible debentures issued by Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Limited. Two series of debentures (ISIN: INE657N07621 and INE657N07613) will undergo part redemption with a record date of December 12, 2025. Post-redemption, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value of Rs. 916,700 per debenture effective from December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two series of Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Limited debentures affected: ECSL-9.90%-30-6-27-PVT (ISIN: INE657N07621) and EAVCL-9.90%-30-6-27-PVT (ISIN: INE657N07613)
- Record date for part redemption: December 12, 2025
- Reduced face value post-redemption: Rs. 916,700 per debenture for both series
- Trading with reduced face value begins: December 12, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Both debentures carry 9.90% coupon rate with maturity on June 30, 2027
- Securities are privately placed (PVT designation)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade at reduced face value of Rs. 916,700 per debenture from December 12, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for both debenture series
- Ensure proper settlement procedures for DR-776/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This corporate action affects only holders of specific privately placed debentures of Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Limited. The part redemption is a scheduled event with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Moderate for debenture holders - Investors holding these debentures will receive partial redemption proceeds, and their holdings will continue with reduced face value of Rs. 916,700 per debenture.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard part redemption process requiring face value adjustment in trading systems. Trading members need to update debenture face values in their systems before the effective date.
