## Summary

BSE has listed 96,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of K&R Rail Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 514360) for trading effective November 28, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement between K&R Rail Engineering Limited (formerly Axis Rail India Limited) and KVR Rail Infra Projects Private Limited. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until November 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 96,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed and permitted for trading
- Shares issued pursuant to Scheme of Arrangement
- ISIN Number: INE078T01026
- Distinctive Numbers: 28509645 - 38109644
- Date of Allotment: October 15, 2024
- Trading commences: November 28, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Total 1,67,61,677 shares under lock-in until November 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a listing pursuant to an existing scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in compliance: 75% of total promoter shareholding post-scheme must remain locked in for 1 year from listing date
- Lock-in restrictions apply in accordance with Exchange observation letter dated December 19, 2013

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 15, 2024
- **Trading Commencement**: November 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine listing of shares pursuant to corporate restructuring. The lock-in period ensures price stability by restricting promoter share sales.

**Company Impact**: The scheme of arrangement represents a corporate restructuring between K&R Rail Engineering Limited and KVR Rail Infra Projects Private Limited. The significant lock-in of 1,67,61,677 shares (including the newly listed shares) demonstrates promoter commitment.

**Investor Impact**: Investors should note the lock-in restrictions on a substantial portion of shares until November 2026, which may affect liquidity and price discovery in the near term.
