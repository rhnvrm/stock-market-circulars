## Summary

VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective October 27, 2025. The instrument consists of 500 units with face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each, issued at Rs.477961.50/- per unit, maturing on April 22, 2026. The Federal Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 500 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.477961.50/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730439
- Scrip ID: VCL241025
- ISIN: INE01HV14AC7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AC7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: April 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper on private placement basis with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in the debt segment. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term commercial paper instruments.
