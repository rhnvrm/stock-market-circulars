## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,25,00,000 new equity shares of Reliance Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 500390) effective from Friday, August 8, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 240 per share.

## Key Points

- 1.25 crore equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each issued at Rs. 230 premium
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Trading permitted from August 8, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of allotment: June 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE036A01016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required for the newly issued shares
- Standard listing and trading procedures to be followed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expires**: February 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Reliance Infrastructure's paid-up capital and provides additional liquidity post lock-in period.
