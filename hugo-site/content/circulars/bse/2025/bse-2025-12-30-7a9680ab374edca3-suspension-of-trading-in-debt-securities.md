## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited effective December 31, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the company's 12.29% debt securities (ISIN: INE0E2307146, Scrip Code: 974102) on January 1, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE0E2307146
- Scrip Code: 974102
- Coupon Rate: 12.29%
- Maturity Date: January 1, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: December 31, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine suspension due to debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited from December 31, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and comply with the restriction

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 31, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 1, 2026**: Maturity date of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative action related to the natural maturity of a private company's debt instrument. The suspension affects only one specific debt security (12.29% notes) of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited. Trading members need to ensure compliance by ceasing all trading activity in this security from December 31, 2025. This does not reflect any credit event or default, but rather the scheduled maturity of the instrument.
