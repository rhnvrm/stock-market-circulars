## Summary

Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543978, ISIN: INE05CZ01011) has completed a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) and will list 12,793,102 new equity shares on BSE. The shares will be available for trading from December 9, 2025. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers at an issue price of Rs. 145 per share and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited
- Scrip Code: 543978
- ISIN: INE05CZ01011
- Number of shares: 12,793,102 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- Issue type: Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- Allottees: Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs)
- Issue price: Rs. 145 per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 53049390 to 65842491
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
