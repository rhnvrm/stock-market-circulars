## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 7,397,270 equity shares of Texmaco Rail & Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 533326) issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading on November 12, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. The allotment was completed on October 9, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 193/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 192/-).

## Key Points

- 7,397,270 new equity shares listed with effect from November 12, 2025
- Shares issued at Rs. 193/- per share (Rs. 1/- face value + Rs. 192/- premium)
- Issued on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distribution Numbers: 399467303 to 406864572
- Date of Allotment: October 9, 2025
- ISIN: INE621L01012
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI regulations for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

**Lock-in Obligations:**
- 3,886,010 shares (Dist. Nos. 399467303 to 403353312) are locked-in until May 12, 2027
- 3,511,260 shares (Dist. Nos. 403353313 to 406864572) are locked-in until May 12, 2026

**Trading Member Action:**
- Trading members must note the new securities for trading purposes from November 12, 2025
- Ensure lock-in restrictions are enforced for respective share tranches
