## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (Scrip Code: 977346, ISIN: INE523L07AR1) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 8, 2025. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.95% per annum with annual interest payments and mature on January 4, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977346, Scrip ID: 895NWIL28
- ISIN Number: INE523L07AR1
- Quantity: 10,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: December 4, 2025
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- Redemption Date: January 4, 2028
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AR1
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 4, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 8, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **January 4, 2028**: Redemption Date
- **Annual**: Interest payment frequency

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE's Debt segment, providing investors with additional fixed-income investment options. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants, investors in Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited securities, and trading members dealing in debt instruments. No broader market implications expected.
