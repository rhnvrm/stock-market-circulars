## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued by Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd on its Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The issuance consists of 1,500 units of commercial paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued on private placement basis. The instruments carry an IND A1+ credit rating and mature in approximately 86 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 1,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,721 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 75 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730745
- **Scrip ID**: MLDL101225
- **ISIN**: INE813A14391
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: YES BANK LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE813A14391
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Standard debt market compliance and reporting requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 6, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 86 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance represents short-term borrowing by Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd to meet working capital requirements. The A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Trading will be limited to institutional investors and qualified participants in the debt market due to the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
