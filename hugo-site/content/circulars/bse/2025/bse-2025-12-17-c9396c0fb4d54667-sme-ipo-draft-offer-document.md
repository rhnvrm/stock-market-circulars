BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Diksha Polymers Limited on its SME website for public comments. - Draft Offer Document filed by Diksha Polymers Limited on December 16, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
