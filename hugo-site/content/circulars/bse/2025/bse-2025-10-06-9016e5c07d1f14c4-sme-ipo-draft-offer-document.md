BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED filed on September 29, 2025, containing disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. The document is available for public comments for 21 days on the BSE SME website.

- Draft Offer Document filed by DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED on September 29, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from notice date

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document publication. The notice has minimal immediate market impact as it is part of the standard IPO process. Investors and stakeholders interested in DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED can review the document and provide feedback during the 21-day comment period. The actual listing and trading impact will depend on the final IPO execution after incorporating any comments and obtaining necessary approvals.
