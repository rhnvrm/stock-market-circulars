## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of units of Mirae Asset Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF effective November 13, 2025. The open-ended ETF will track the Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index and be listed in B Group with scrip code 544605. A total of 3,940,666 units were allotted on November 7, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 17.02 per unit.

## Key Points

- ETF will commence trading on Thursday, November 13, 2025
- Scrip Code: 544605, ISIN: INF769K01PQ4
- BOLT System ID: SMALL250
- Total units allotted: 3,940,666 units
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 17.02 per unit
- Date of allotment: November 7, 2025
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Five designated market makers appointed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification in compliance with existing SEBI guidelines requiring ETF units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades to be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Market makers: East India Securities Limited, Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited, Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited, Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited, Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited, and IRage Broking Services LLP

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a new product listing. The ETF provides investors with an additional instrument to gain exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index. The presence of five market makers should ensure adequate liquidity for the new ETF. The smallcap segment ETF adds to the suite of passive investment products available on BSE.
