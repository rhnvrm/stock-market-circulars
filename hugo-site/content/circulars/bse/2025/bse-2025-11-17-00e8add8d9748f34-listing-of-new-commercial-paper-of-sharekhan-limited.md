## Summary

Sharekhan Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The issue comprises 6,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued on private placement basis with allotment date of November 14, 2025 and redemption date of February 13, 2026. The securities carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 6,000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,423.65 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730577
- Scrip ID: SL141125
- ISIN: INE211H14AG0
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE211H14AG0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The 91-day maturity instrument provides short-term funding to Sharekhan Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is confined to debt market participants and institutional investors in commercial papers. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
