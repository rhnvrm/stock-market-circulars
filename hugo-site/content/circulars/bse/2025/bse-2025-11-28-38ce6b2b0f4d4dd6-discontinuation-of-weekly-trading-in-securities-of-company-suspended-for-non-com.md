## Summary

BSE announces the discontinuation of weekly trading facility for Seven Hill Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 511760) effective December 16, 2025. This follows the completion of the mandated six-month period during which the company's shares were allowed to trade on a Trade for Trade basis in Z group only on the first trading day of every week, following suspension for non-compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018.

## Key Points

- Seven Hill Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 511760) will no longer have weekly trading facility from December 16, 2025
- Company was initially suspended on April 24, 2025 (Exchange Notice No. 20250424-24) for non-compliance with SEBI Depositories Regulations
- After suspension, trading was allowed on Trade for Trade basis in Z group only on first trading day of each week
- Mandated six-month period has now been completed
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the final step of the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for suspension and revocation of equity shares as per:
- SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024
- Chapter-VII(A) - Penal Actions for Non-Compliance
- SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015

## Compliance Requirements

**For Market Participants:**
- Note the discontinuation of weekly trading facility for Seven Hill Industries Ltd
- No trading will be permitted in this scrip from December 16, 2025 onwards
- For clarifications, contact: bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

**Company Status:**
- Seven Hill Industries Ltd remains suspended for non-compliance with SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018
- Company must rectify non-compliances to seek revocation of suspension

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects single company already under suspension with restricted trading

**Trading Impact:** 
- Eliminates remaining trading opportunity (weekly Trade for Trade) for Seven Hill Industries Ltd
- Shareholders will have no trading avenue unless suspension is revoked
- Completes the penal framework for non-compliance as per SEBI guidelines

**Investor Impact:**
- Existing shareholders of Seven Hill Industries Ltd will not be able to trade shares after December 16, 2025
- Liquidity completely eliminated until company rectifies non-compliances

**Operational Impact:**
- Standard conclusion of six-month grace period as mandated by SEBI SOP
