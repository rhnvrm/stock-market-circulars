## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 159/2025-2026 dated November 20, 2025, announces book-closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions including dividend payments, interest payments on non-convertible debentures, and redemption of commercial papers and NCDs.

## Key Points

- Aryavan Enterprise Limited announces Rs. 0.50 per share (5%) interim dividend with record date November 28, 2025
- Multiple ICICI Home Finance Company Limited NCDs scheduled for interest payments between January-March 2026
- BOBCARD Limited commercial paper redemption scheduled for November 27, 2025
- Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited commercial papers maturing in February 2026
- JSW Steel Limited early redemption of NCD scheduled for December 8, 2025
- IKF Home Finance Limited partial redemption and interest payment on November 23, 2025
- Kerala Financial Corporation NCDs interest payment scheduled for December 16, 2025
- Hiren Wahen Buildtech Private Limited NCD redemption on November 30, 2025
- Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited interest payment record date December 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine disclosure bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must honor the announced record dates for determining eligible shareholders/debenture holders
- Trading flag set to 1 (cum-benefit) for all listed securities until record date
- Investors must hold securities on record date to be eligible for announced benefits

## Important Dates

- November 23, 2025: IKF Home Finance Limited - Interest payment and partial NCD redemption
- November 27, 2025: BOBCARD Limited - CP redemption
- November 28, 2025: Aryavan Enterprise Limited - Interim dividend record date
- November 30, 2025: Hiren Wahen Buildtech - NCD redemption
- December 2, 2025: Akara Capital Advisors - Interest payment record date
- December 8, 2025: JSW Steel Limited - Interest payment and early NCD redemption
- December 16, 2025: Kerala Financial Corporation - Interest payments
- January 12, 2026: ICICI Home Finance - Interest payment
- January 28-31, 2026: Multiple ICICI Home Finance interest payments
- February 7-19, 2026: Multiple ICICI Home Finance and Dalmia Cement actions
- March 10, 2026: ICICI Home Finance - NCD redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine corporate actions affecting specific securities only.

**Investor Impact:** Medium for affected shareholders/debenture holders who need to hold securities on record dates to receive dividends, interest, or redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard processing through depository systems for eligible investors.
