## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited on private placement basis. A total of 1,500 securities with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on December 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Quantity Listed: 1,500 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,67,148 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730772
- Scrip ID: KMIL151225
- Detail Name: KMIL-11-12-26-CP
- ISIN: INE975F14C74 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: December 17, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 11, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE975F14C74
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading effective from December 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure:
- All trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trading occurs in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Proper tick size of 1 paise is maintained

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper with minimal market impact. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE's platform and provides short-term funding to Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to institutional investors and entities participating in the commercial paper market.
