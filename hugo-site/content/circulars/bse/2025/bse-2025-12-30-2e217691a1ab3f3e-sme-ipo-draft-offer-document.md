## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Recode Studios Limited filed on December 27, 2025, for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the exchange website for at least 21 days to solicit public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Recode Studios Limited on December 27, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments are invited on the draft document
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders interested in providing feedback on the Draft Offer Document should submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from December 30, 2025
- The company and intermediaries must consider public comments as part of the IPO process

## Important Dates

- **Draft Document Filing Date**: December 27, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 30, 2025 (deadline: January 20, 2026)
- **Document Availability**: Minimum 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a procedural notice for an SME IPO draft document with no immediate impact on existing market operations or trading.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for potential IPO investors - Interested investors and stakeholders have the opportunity to review the draft offer document and provide feedback before the final offer document is filed.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard disclosure process with no changes to trading or operational procedures for market participants.
