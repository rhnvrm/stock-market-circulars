## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 24, 2025, as the debentures are scheduled to mature on December 26, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 975277
- ISIN: INE01YL07235
- Security: ESPLD-11.75%-26-12-25-PVT (11.75% coupon rate)
- Suspension effective date: December 24, 2025
- Maturity date: December 26, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-784/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension due to debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Earlysalary Services Private Limited from December 24, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 26, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine suspension affecting a single privately placed debenture issue from Earlysalary Services Private Limited. The suspension is procedural and relates to the normal maturity of the debt instrument. Trading members holding or dealing in this specific security need to cease trading one day prior to maturity.
