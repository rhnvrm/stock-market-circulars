## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis, effective November 25, 2025. The securities comprise 1,200 units of commercial paper with scrip code 730639 and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 1,200 units
- **Scrip Code**: 730639
- **Scrip ID**: FFSL241125
- **Detail Name**: FFSL-23-2-26-CP
- **ISIN Number**: INE007N14EI8
- **Face Value**: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 6.97% per annum (cumulative)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EI8
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Date**: February 23, 2026 (cumulative)
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper has a short tenure of 90 days and carries high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating strong creditworthiness. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors seeking short-term investment instruments. No put or call options are available. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
