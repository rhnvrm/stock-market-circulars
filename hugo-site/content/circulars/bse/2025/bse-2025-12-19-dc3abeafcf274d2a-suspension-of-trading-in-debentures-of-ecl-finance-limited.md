## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ECL Finance Limited effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its 9.60% debentures maturing on January 13, 2026. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures from the record date.

## Key Points

- ECL Finance Limited debentures (Code: 953285, ISIN: INE804I07J05) will cease trading from December 29, 2025
- The debentures carry a coupon rate of 9.60% with maturity date of January 13, 2026
- Record date for redemption purpose is set as December 29, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 29, 2025 onwards
- Notice issued under reference DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for scheduled debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients holding these debentures are informed about the redemption and trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only a specific series of privately placed debentures. The suspension is procedural and temporary, ending with redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the original terms. No trading opportunity available from December 29, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to block trading in this specific ISIN from the specified date to ensure compliance with the no-dealings instruction.
