## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis. The listing comprises 700 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 35 crore. These securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (short-term debt)
- **Issuer**: Adani Enterprises Ltd
- **Quantity**: 700 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,93,731 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 730606
- **Scrip ID**: AEL191125
- **ISIN**: INE423A14YO0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading allowed in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum trading unit is 1 security (market lot)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 65 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide Adani Enterprises with short-term funding for approximately two months. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong repayment capacity. Trading members engaged in debt markets can now trade these securities on BSE. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at the provided phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
