## Summary

BSE has announced the implementation of a resolution plan for RCI Industries & Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 537254) approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal, New Delhi Bench. The plan involves complete extinguishment of promoter shareholding and reduction of existing public shareholding to only 5% of the fully diluted capital structure. Trading in the company's equity shares will be suspended from November 28, 2025. The stock is currently under Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) restrictions.

## Key Points

- Record date set as November 28, 2025 for capital reduction implementation
- Complete cancellation and extinguishment of all promoter and promoter group shareholding
- Public shareholding to be reduced to only 5% of post-implementation fully diluted capital
- Trading suspension effective November 28, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-766/2025-2026)
- Resolution plan approved by NCLT New Delhi Bench
- Company currently under GSM (Graded Surveillance Measure) trading restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of NCLT-approved resolution plan involving capital restructuring under insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings. The plan mandates:

1. **Promoter Shareholding Extinguishment**: Complete cancellation of all equity shares held by promoters and promoter group
2. **Public Shareholding Reduction**: Drastic reduction of public shareholding through cancellation and extinguishment to retain only 5% of the restructured capital
3. **Trading Status**: Suspension of trading in equity shares following the capital reduction event

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Take note of the November 28, 2025 record date for capital reduction
- Cease trading in RCI Industries & Technologies Limited equity shares from November 28, 2025
- Inform clients about the trading suspension and capital restructuring
- Note existing GSM restrictions already in place

**For Company:**
- Implementation of NCLT-approved resolution plan
- Execution of share capital reduction and extinguishment as per tribunal order

**For Shareholders:**
- Promoters: Complete loss of shareholding through extinguishment
- Public shareholders: Significant dilution with 95% of shareholding to be cancelled and extinguished

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Notice date and circular issuance
- **November 28, 2025**: Record date for capital reduction
- **November 28, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (Settlement No. DR-766/2025-2026)
- **November 28, 2025**: No dealings/suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Immediate and complete trading suspension eliminates liquidity for existing shareholders
- Existing GSM restrictions indicate prior market concerns about the stock

**Shareholder Impact:**
- **Promoters**: Complete loss of shareholding with zero recovery
- **Public Shareholders**: Catastrophic impact with 95% shareholding extinguishment, retaining only 5% of restructured capital
- Severe wealth erosion for all existing equity holders

**Operational Impact:**
- Company undergoing resolution plan suggests financial distress or insolvency proceedings
- Capital restructuring indicates debt resolution through equity dilution/extinguishment
- Post-implementation capital structure will be fundamentally different with new shareholders likely entering as part of resolution plan

**Corporate Governance:**
- NCLT oversight ensures legal compliance in restructuring process
- Removal of existing promoters signals complete change in control and management potential
- Public shareholders face near-total loss despite minority retention in restructured entity
