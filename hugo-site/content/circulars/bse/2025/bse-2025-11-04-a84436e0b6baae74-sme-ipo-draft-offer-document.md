## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of MTE Structures Limited for its SME IPO, filed on October 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the stock exchange website for public comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Company: MTE Structures Limited
- Document Type: Draft Offer Document (DRHP)
- Filing Date: October 31, 2025
- Publication Date: November 04, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Compliance: Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Notice Number: 20251104-1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited on the draft document
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from the notice date
- The document will remain available for at least 21 days for public review

## Important Dates

- **October 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by MTE Structures Limited
- **November 04, 2025**: Notice published and document made available for public comments
- **November 25, 2025**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to a single SME IPO process. The publication is a standard regulatory requirement for transparency and investor protection. Impact is limited to:

- Potential investors in MTE Structures Limited's SME IPO
- Market participants interested in SME segment offerings

No immediate trading or operational impact on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
