## Summary

Purple Wave Infocom Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the listing of its equity shares post-IPO on December 4, 2025. The shareholding structure shows promoter and promoter group holding 6,649,955 shares (71.93%) across 3 shareholders, while public shareholders hold 2,596,045 shares (28.07%) distributed among 584 shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 9,246,000 (6,649,955 promoter + 2,596,045 public)
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 71.93% (3 shareholders)
- Public shareholding: 28.07% (584 shareholders)
- Locked-in shares are present in the promoter category
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No significant beneficial owners declared
- No equity shares with differential voting rights

## Regulatory Changes

This is a compliance disclosure under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015, which mandates listed entities to submit shareholding patterns. The filing is made under Regulation 31(1)(a) post-issuance of IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity required to disclose shareholding pattern post-IPO listing
- Declaration regarding various securities categories (partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, NDUs, other encumbrances)
- Disclosure of significant beneficial ownership
- Promoter and public shareholding breakdown with voting rights details

## Important Dates

- **December 4, 2025**: Shareholding pattern filing date (Post-IPO)
- Filing made under Regulation 31(1)(a) for post-IPO disclosure

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Purple Wave Infocom Limited adds a new equity to the BSE platform. The shareholding pattern shows a typical post-IPO structure with promoters retaining majority stake (71.93%) while maintaining minimum public float (28.07%). The presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with lock-in requirements for promoter holdings post-IPO. The relatively large number of public shareholders (584) suggests reasonable retail participation in the IPO. No encumbrances on promoter shares indicate a clean capital structure.
