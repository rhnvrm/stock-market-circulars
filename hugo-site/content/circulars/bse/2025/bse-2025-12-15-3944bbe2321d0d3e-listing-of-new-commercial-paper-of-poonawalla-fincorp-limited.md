## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 15, 2025. The issuance consists of 10,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crore. The Commercial Paper carries CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on March 13, 2026. ICICI Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total issuance: 10,000 Commercial Papers worth Rs. 500 crore aggregate value
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,906 per security
- Scrip Code: 730767, Scrip ID: PFL121225
- ISIN Number: INE511C14ZD8
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE511C14ZD8
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 13, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine Commercial Paper listing in the debt segment. The instrument has a short tenor of approximately 3 months and is rated A1+ by both CARE and CRISIL, indicating highest safety for short-term debt.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment participants and institutional investors dealing in Commercial Paper. The instrument provides a short-term investment avenue with high credit quality.

**Issuer Impact**: Provides Poonawalla Fincorp Limited with short-term funding of Rs. 500 crore at a discount rate (issue price Rs. 4,91,906 against face value Rs. 5,00,000), supporting working capital requirements.
