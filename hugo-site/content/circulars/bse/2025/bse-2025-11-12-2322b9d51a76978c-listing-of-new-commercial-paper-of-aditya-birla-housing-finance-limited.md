## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 12, 2025. The issuance comprises 8500 units with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with HDFC Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 8500 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs.4,92,546 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730545
- Scrip ID: ABHF111125
- ISIN: INE831R14FK5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: November 11, 2025
- Redemption date: February 10, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FK5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides short-term funding (approximately 3 months maturity) for Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
