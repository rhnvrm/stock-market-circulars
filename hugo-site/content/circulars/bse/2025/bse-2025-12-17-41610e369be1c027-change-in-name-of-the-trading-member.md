## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6795) will change its name to "SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, December 18, 2025. This is an administrative update regarding the trading member's official name.

## Key Points

- Trading member SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6795) is changing its name
- New name: SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED
- This is a name change only; member number remains unchanged
- Contact point for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a trading member's name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of this trading member for any future correspondence or dealings.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, December 18, 2025 - Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the official name of one trading member. There are no operational, trading, or market-wide implications. Other trading members need only update their internal records if they have dealings with this member.
