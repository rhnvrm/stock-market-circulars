## Summary

Shivamshree Businesses Limited (Scrip Code: 538520) has listed 3 crore new equity shares on BSE effective August 25, 2025. The shares were issued at Rs. 1.50 per share (face value Rs. 1 + premium Rs. 0.50) to non-promoters on preferential basis and are subject to lock-in until March 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed on BSE
- Issued at premium of Rs. 0.50 to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 1.50 per share
- Trading commences from Monday, August 25, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE857P01021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Shares subject to lock-in provisions as per regulations
- Standard compliance with BSE listing requirements

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 6, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 25, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: March 20, 2026
- Notice Date: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as all 3 crore shares are locked-in until March 2026. The preferential issue to non-promoters increases the public float but trading impact will only be realized post lock-in expiry. Represents expansion of equity base for the company.
