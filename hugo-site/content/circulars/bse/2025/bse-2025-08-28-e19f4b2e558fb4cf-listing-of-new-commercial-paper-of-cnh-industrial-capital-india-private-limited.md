## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by CNH Industrial Capital (India) Private Limited worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) for trading on the debt segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each (2000 units)
- Scrip Code: 730109, ISIN: INE0P4D14226
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,244 (Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for CNH Industrial Capital with a 3-month tenure. No direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
