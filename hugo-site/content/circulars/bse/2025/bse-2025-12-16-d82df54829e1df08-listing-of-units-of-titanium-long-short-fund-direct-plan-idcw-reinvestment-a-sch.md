## Summary

BSE has listed units of Titanium Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment, a close-ended equity scheme managed by Tata Asset Management Private Limited. The units will be available for trading in dematerialized form in the B Group effective December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund Name: Titanium Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment
- Investment Manager: Tata Asset Management Pvt Ltd
- Number of Units: 199,990 units
- Scrip Code: 544650
- ISIN: INF277K30054
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10.03 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 11, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading Group: B Group
- Scheme Type: Close-ended equity scheme investing in equity, equity-related instruments, debt, and money market instruments with limited short exposure through derivatives

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that units will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (i.e., one unit)
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: Computer Age Management Services (Private) Limited (CAMS), SEBI registration number INR000002813

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: December 17, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of mutual fund units for a specific direct plan scheme. The listing provides investors with trading access to 199,990 units of the close-ended fund. Only relevant to existing unitholders and potential investors interested in this specific Tata Mutual Fund scheme.
