## Summary

BSE has announced that AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF (both Regular and Direct plans) will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 01, 2026. The schemes will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopening for transactions after temporary suspension
- Both Regular Plan (ISIN: INF846KA1390) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF846KA1408) covered
- Facilities available: ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Effective date: January 01, 2026
- Scheme category: Debt (Fund of Funds)
- Reference to previous notice: 20251209-50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about fund availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 01, 2026 - Reopening of AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This notice affects only investors interested in AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF schemes.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard fund reopening process. BSE StAR MF Platform members can facilitate transactions in these schemes starting January 01, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors who wish to invest in or redeem from AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF schemes, as the fund will be accepting ongoing transactions and systematic investment/withdrawal facilities.
