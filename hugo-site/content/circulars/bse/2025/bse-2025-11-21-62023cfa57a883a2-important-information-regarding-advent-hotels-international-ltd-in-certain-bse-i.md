## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd has announced the exclusion of Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip code: 544446) from multiple BSE indices effective prior to trading on November 25, 2025. This action follows the company's scheme of arrangement for demerger and the established process for removal of spun-off scrips from indices. The exclusion was triggered because the scrip did not hit the lower circuit on November 20 and 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip code: 544446) to be removed from 9 BSE indices
- Effective date: Prior to open of trading on Tuesday, November 25, 2025
- Removal follows demerger scheme of arrangement process announced in Notice No: 20250403-46
- The scrip did not hit lower circuit on Thursday, November 20, 2025 and Friday, November 21, 2025, triggering exclusion
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action implements the existing process for removal of spun-off scrips from BSE Indices following a scheme of arrangement for demerger, as previously announced in Notice No: 20250403-46 dated April 3, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers tracking affected BSE indices must rebalance portfolios to exclude Advent Hotels International Limited
- Market participants should note the corporate action file update
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: First day scrip did not hit lower circuit
- **November 21, 2025**: Second day scrip did not hit lower circuit (trigger day)
- **November 25, 2025**: Effective exclusion date (prior to trading open)

## Affected Indices

Advent Hotels International Limited will be excluded from the following BSE indices:

1. BSE SMALLCAP
2. BSE ALLCAP
3. BSE CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY
4. BSE 1000
5. BSE NEXT 500
6. BSE 250 MICROCAP
7. BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%)
8. BSE MIDSMALLCAP

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The exclusion affects passive funds and ETFs tracking the affected indices, requiring mandatory rebalancing. The stock's presence in multiple indices including BSE SMALLCAP and BSE ALLCAP indicates moderate market capitalization.

**Operational Impact**: Index fund managers must execute selling orders before market open on November 25, 2025. The removal from 9 indices simultaneously may create temporary selling pressure.

**Investor Impact**: Passive investors in affected index funds will see automatic divestment from Advent Hotels International Limited. Active fund managers may reassess positions based on non-index investment thesis.
