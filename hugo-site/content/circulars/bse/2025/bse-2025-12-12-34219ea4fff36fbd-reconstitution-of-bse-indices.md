## Summary

BSE has announced the reconstitution of six indices effective December 22, 2025. The changes include 1 addition and 3 deletions in BSE Consumer Discretionary, 1 deletion in BSE Energy, 2 deletions in BSE Industrials, 10 additions and 9 deletions in BSE Diversified Financials Revenue Growth Index, and 6 additions and 6 deletions in BSE Select IPO Index.

## Key Points

- Total of 17 stocks being added across all indices
- Total of 21 stocks being dropped across all indices
- All changes effective from December 22, 2025
- BSE Diversified Financials Revenue Growth Index sees the most activity with 19 total changes
- BSE Select IPO Index sees 12 total changes with new IPO entrants

## Impact Assessment

This reconstitution will affect index funds and ETFs tracking these BSE indices. Passive funds will need to rebalance their portfolios to match the new index composition. The largest impact is on the BSE Diversified Financials Revenue Growth Index with net addition of 1 stock but significant composition change (10 in, 9 out). Index trackers should expect moderate trading activity on December 22, 2025 as funds adjust their holdings to align with the reconstituted indices.
