## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Reliance General Insurance Company Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to IndusInd General Insurance Company Limited with effect from September 22, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC, and digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: RELIANCE GENERAL INSURANCE COMPANY LIMITED
- **New Name**: INDUSIND GENERAL INSURANCE COMPANY LIMITED
- **CIN**: U66603MH2000PLC128300
- **Effective Date**: September 22, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Certificate Issued By**: Central Processing Centre, ROC (Sabari Raj)
- **Registered Address**: 6th Floor, Oberoi Commerz-1, International Business Park, Oberoi Garden City, Goregaon East, Mumbai City-400063, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

The name change has been approved and certified by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The certificate was digitally signed under rule 9(2) of the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Reliance General Insurance Company Limited) along with its new name for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 22, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from September 22, 2025 (until September 22, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Stakeholder Impact**: The name change does not affect the legal identity, rights, obligations, or liabilities of the company or its stakeholders. Existing contracts, policies, and agreements remain valid under the new name.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all official documentation, stationery, signage, and digital platforms to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Market Impact**: Minimal immediate market impact as this is an administrative change. Investors and policyholders should note the new corporate identity for future reference and correspondence.

**Brand Impact**: Significant rebranding from Reliance to IndusInd umbrella, potentially indicating change in ownership structure or strategic alignment with IndusInd group entities.
