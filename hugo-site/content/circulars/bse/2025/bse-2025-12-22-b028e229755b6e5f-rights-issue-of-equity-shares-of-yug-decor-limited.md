## Summary

YUG DECOR LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540550) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 23, 2025. The company will issue 1 equity share of Rs.10/- each for every 2 equity shares held by eligible shareholders. The shares will be issued at par value of Rs.10 per share, payable in full at the time of application. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 1 equity share for every 2 equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs.10/- per share (at par)
- Face value: Rs.10/- per equity share
- Payment: Full issue price payable at the time of application
- Trading settlement: DR-783/2025-2026
- Scrip code: 540550

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in YUG DECOR LIMITED shares on an ex-rights basis from December 23, 2025
- Shareholders holding shares as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the rights issue
- Full payment of Rs.10/- per share must be made at the time of application

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-783/2025-2026
- **Circular Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholders**: Existing shareholders as of the record date will receive rights entitlements in 1:2 ratio, providing an opportunity to increase their stake at par value. Non-participation will result in dilution of ownership percentage.

**Market Impact**: Share price may adjust downward on ex-rights date to reflect the dilution. The rights entitlement may be traded separately if the company provides for renunciation.

**Trading Impact**: All transactions from December 23, 2025 onwards will be on ex-rights basis, meaning buyers will not be entitled to participate in this rights issue.

**Company Impact**: Capital raise will strengthen the company's equity base, with funds to be utilized as per the company's disclosed objectives in the rights issue offer document.
