## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by Time Technoplast Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 10, 2025. The issue consists of 200 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 10 crores) with a redemption date of February 9, 2026. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with IDBI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Time Technoplast Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Scrip Code**: 730518
- **Scrip ID**: TTL101125
- **ISIN**: INE508G14IB5
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Size**: 200 units (Rs. 10 crores)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,062.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IDBI BANK LIMITED
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE508G14IB5
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must comply with BSE Debt segment regulations
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides Time Technoplast Limited with short-term funding through the debt market. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Affects only BSE debt segment trading members who deal in commercial paper and institutional investors interested in short-term debt instruments. The 91-day tenure makes this suitable for investors seeking near-term liquidity with reasonable returns.

**Investor Relevance**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals who participate in the debt market, as the minimum investment is Rs. 5 lakhs per unit.
