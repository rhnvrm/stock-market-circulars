## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6762) will cease operations in Cash, Equity derivatives and Currency derivative segments effective November 27, 2025, due to voluntary closure of business. This is a routine administrative notification with no broader market implications.

## Key Points

- Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6762) voluntarily closing business
- Cessation of operations in three segments: Cash, Equity derivatives, and Currency derivatives
- Effective date: November 27, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **November 26, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of business closure for Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The voluntary closure of a single trading member has negligible impact on overall market operations or trading activities.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to clients and counterparties of Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited who will need to transfer their accounts to other brokers. No impact on other exchange members or market infrastructure.

**Scope**: Affects only one trading member (Member No. 6762) across three segments (Cash, Equity derivatives, Currency derivatives).
