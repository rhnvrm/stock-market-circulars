## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The securities carry an 11% annual interest rate, mature on May 25, 2031, and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977316, Scrip ID: 11KFL31
- ISIN: INE403Q08332
- Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403Q08332
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Monthly from January 1, 2026 to May 25, 2031
- **Date of Redemption**: May 25, 2031

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of debt securities for a single issuer with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to trading members dealing in debt securities who may participate in trading these instruments. The securities add to the available debt instruments on BSE's debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for institutional and qualified investors who participated in the private placement or may trade these securities in the secondary market.
