## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371) will be transferred to the 'X' group with effect from December 31, 2025. This group classification change impacts trading parameters and indicates the stock is under specific surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- Company: Samtel India Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 500371
- New Group: 'X' Group
- Effective Date: December 31, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251224-21
- Contact: Mrs. Kinjal More (+91-7304278788)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically indicates stocks under special surveillance or with specific trading restrictions, often applied to companies with compliance issues, limited liquidity, or other regulatory concerns.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the group change effective December 31, 2025
- Update their systems and client communications accordingly
- Be aware of any additional trading restrictions applicable to 'X' group securities
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More for clarifications (Cell: +91-7304278788)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The transfer to 'X' group typically results in:
- Increased surveillance measures on the stock
- Potential trading restrictions or special margin requirements
- Reduced liquidity as some institutional investors may have restrictions on 'X' group stocks
- Signal to market participants about potential concerns with the company
- Impact on investor sentiment and trading activity
