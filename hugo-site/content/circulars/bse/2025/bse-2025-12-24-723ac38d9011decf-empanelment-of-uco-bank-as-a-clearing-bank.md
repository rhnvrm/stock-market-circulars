## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has empaneled UCO Bank as a designated Clearing Bank for settlement of funds obligations of members across various segments. This adds UCO Bank to the list of available clearing banks that members can use for their settlement activities.

## Key Points

- UCO Bank empaneled as designated Clearing Bank of ICCL
- Available for settlement of funds obligation across various ICCL segments
- Registered and Corporate Office located at 2nd Floor, 10 BTM Sarani, Kolkata - 700001
- Nodal Branch: UCO Bank, Mid Corporate D N Road Branch, Mumbai - 400001
- Complete list of ICCL empaneled Clearing Banks available at https://www.icclindia.com/Static/membership/List_of_Clearing_Banks.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update expanding the choice of clearing banks available to ICCL members.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members may choose to use UCO Bank as their clearing bank for funds settlement if desired.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 24, 2025
- Effective immediately upon empanelment
