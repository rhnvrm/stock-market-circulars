## Summary

Hari Govind International Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531971) has listed 6,67,500 new equity shares on BSE effective November 27, 2025. These shares were issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each to non-promoters on a preferential basis and are subject to lock-in restrictions until May 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,67,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to non-promoters
- Shares issued on preferential allotment basis
- Trading commences from Thursday, November 27, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished Numbers: 7857501 to 8525000
- ISIN: INE167F01018
- Scrip Code: 531971

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in period compliance required until May 30, 2026 for all allotted shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a routine preferential allotment of a relatively small number of shares (6.67 lakh). The shares are subject to standard lock-in restrictions preventing immediate selling pressure. No change to the fundamental rights or structure of existing shares as new shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares.
