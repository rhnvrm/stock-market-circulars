## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 91) will change its name to "ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED" effective Friday, December 5, 2025. This is an administrative change that requires trading members to update their records accordingly.

## Key Points

- Trading member ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 91) is changing its name
- New name: ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED
- Member number remains unchanged: 91
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- Trading members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change of an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new name
- Update any systems or databases that reference the old trading member name
- No other compliance actions required

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 5, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Nil. This is a name change only and does not affect trading operations, memberships, or market functioning.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. Trading members may need to update internal records and systems to reflect the new name. The member number (91) remains unchanged, which should minimize system impacts.

**Trading Impact:** None. All existing trading arrangements, positions, and obligations continue unchanged under the new name.
