## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 780 dated December 19, 2025. The auction includes 76 scrips with varying quantities available for auction to settle pending delivery obligations. This is a routine market operation mechanism to ensure settlement completion when sellers fail to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 780
- Auction Date: December 19, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 76 different securities
- Quantities range from 1 share to 146,000 shares depending on the scrip
- Notable large quantity auctions include Enbee Trade (146,000 shares), Indiabulls L (29,300 shares), and INDOWIND ENE (5,000 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must participate in the auction process as per BSE auction rules
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism for the listed scrips
- Participants must ensure availability of funds/securities for auction participation

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: December 19, 2025
- Settlement No: 780

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine settlement mechanism affecting only specific scrips where delivery obligations remain pending.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to clearing members and brokers with pending delivery obligations in the listed scrips. The auction ensures orderly settlement and reduces risk in the clearing system.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. The auction mechanism protects the integrity of the settlement system by ensuring delivery completion.
