## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited issued on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 5, 2025. The securities carry an 11.40% per annum interest rate with monthly payments and mature on May 12, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,250 debentures
- Scrip Code: 977029
- Scrip ID: 1140KFL27A
- ISIN: INE0NES07253 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,750.14
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07253
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 5, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period:** Monthly from January 12, 2026 to May 12, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** May 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The private placement debentures are limited to institutional and qualified investors. The listing provides additional debt market options for investors seeking fixed-income securities with monthly interest payments and BBB+ rating. No put/call options are available on these securities.
