## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the name change of trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 6593) to RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED, effective from Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect trading operations or member status.

## Key Points

- Trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. will change its name to RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number 6593 remains unchanged
- Name change is effective from November 26, 2025
- Trading members can seek clarifications at membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a corporate name change for an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice to update records and communications regarding the renamed trading member.

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 26, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The trading member's membership number, rights, and obligations remain unchanged. Other market participants should update their records to reflect the new name for future communications and transactions with this member.
