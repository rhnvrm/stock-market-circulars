## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by 360 ONE WAM Limited on private placement basis effective December 4, 2025. The instrument comprises 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5 crore, with maturity on June 1, 2026. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 5 crore
- Scrip Code: 730694
- Scrip ID: OWL31225
- ISIN: INE466L14FK3
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,396
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FK3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members must ensure compliance with dematerialization and lot size requirements

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 4, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 4, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 3, 2025
- Date of Redemption: June 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides 360 ONE WAM Limited with short-term funding (approximately 6 months tenure) through the debt market. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate low credit risk. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the high denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
