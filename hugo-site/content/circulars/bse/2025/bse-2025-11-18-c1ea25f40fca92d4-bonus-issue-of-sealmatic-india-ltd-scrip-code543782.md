## Summary

Sealmatic India Limited (Scrip Code: 543782) has submitted an undertaking to BSE regarding the credit of bonus shares following approval by the Board and members. The record date for determining eligible shareholders is November 21, 2025. A total of 18,10,000 bonus shares with face value of Rs. 10/- per share will be allotted, with distinctive numbers ranging from 90,50,001 to 1,08,60,000.

## Key Points

- Record date for bonus issue: Friday, November 21, 2025
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 18,10,000 shares
- Face value per share: Rs. 10/-
- Distinctive numbers: 90,50,001 to 1,08,60,000
- ISIN: INE0O4601016
- Scrip Code: 543782
- Company confirms compliance with all applicable SEBI, Stock Exchange, and Depository regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action under existing bonus issue framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bonus shares must be credited to shareholders' demat accounts by 12:00 P.M. on T+1 day (November 24, 2025)
- Company must comply with all applicable regulations of BSE, SEBI, and Depositories
- Proper disclosure of distinctive numbers, allotment details, and ISIN information

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: Friday, November 21, 2025 (T Day)
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: Monday, November 24, 2025 (T+1)
- **Credit Deadline**: Monday, November 24, 2025 by 12:00 P.M. (T+1)
- **Effective Listing Date**: Tuesday, November 25, 2025 (T+2)
- **Trading Availability**: Beginning of Day (BOD) on Tuesday, November 25, 2025 (T+2)
- **Circular Date**: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The bonus issue will increase the number of outstanding shares of Sealmatic India Limited, improving liquidity and making shares more accessible to retail investors through lower price per share.

**Shareholder Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who hold shares as of the record date (November 21, 2025), as they will receive additional bonus shares in proportion to their existing holdings.

**Trading Impact**: Bonus shares will be available for trading from November 25, 2025, with the stock price expected to adjust proportionally based on the bonus ratio.

**Operational Impact**: Standard corporate action with clear timeline - T+1 for credit completion and T+2 for trading commencement, ensuring smooth processing through the depository system.
