## Summary

BSE has listed 5,130,170 equity shares of NANTA TECH LIMITED following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shares are listed in demat form with varying lock-in periods ranging from 1 month to 3 years depending on the category of shareholders. The listing includes promoter holdings, anchor investor allocations, and public issue shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 5,130,170 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid and in demat form
- Multiple lock-in categories with different expiry dates
- Anchor investor allocations: 360,000 shares (180,000 + 180,000)
- Lock-in periods range from 29 January 2026 to 31 December 2028
- Shares allocated across distinctive number ranges from 1 to 5,130,170

## Share Allocation Details

### Lock-in Categories:

1. **384,069 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2026
2. **67,800 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2027
3. **851,200 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2026
4. **851,178 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2027
5. **1,100,000 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2028 (3-year lock-in)
6. **175,623 shares** - Lock-in from 22-12-2025 to 31-12-2026
7. **254,300 shares** - Lock-in from 23-12-2025 to 31-12-2026
8. **180,000 shares** - Anchor allocation, lock-in from 29-12-2025 to 28-01-2026 (1 month)
9. **180,000 shares** - Anchor allocation, lock-in from 29-12-2025 to 29-03-2026 (3 months)
10. **1,086,000 shares** - IPO shares, free for trading

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Lock-in Expiry (1 month)**: 28 January 2026
- **Anchor Lock-in Expiry (3 months)**: 29 March 2026
- **First Lock-in Expiry**: 31 December 2026
- **Second Lock-in Expiry**: 31 December 2027
- **Final Lock-in Expiry**: 31 December 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing of NANTA TECH LIMITED provides investors access to a new equity instrument on BSE. The staggered lock-in periods indicate promoter and early investor commitments, with the longest 3-year lock-in (1,100,000 shares) demonstrating strong long-term alignment. Approximately 1,086,000 shares are immediately available for free trading, providing initial liquidity. Anchor investors hold 360,000 shares with short-term lock-ins (1-3 months), which will add to tradable float in early 2026.
