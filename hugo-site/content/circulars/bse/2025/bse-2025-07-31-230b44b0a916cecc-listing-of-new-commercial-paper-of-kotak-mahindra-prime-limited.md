## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 4,000 units listed under scrip code 727700.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 727700, Scrip ID: KMPL23824
- ISIN: INE916D143S2 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 499,420.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Listing date: July 31, 2025
- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- Redemption date: August 7, 2025
- Maturity period: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 7-day maturity. The instrument provides short-term funding for Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors seeking very short-term debt instruments.
