## Summary

Capital Infra Trust (Scrip Code: 544338) has listed 4,32,61,000 new units on BSE effective November 18, 2025. The units were issued to the sponsor at Rs. 79.75 per unit on a preferential basis for cash consideration. The allotment date was November 13, 2025, and the units rank pari-passu with existing units. Different tranches have varying lock-in periods ending in 2026 and 2028.

## Key Points

- 4,32,61,000 new units issued at Rs. 79.75 per unit
- Issued to sponsor on preferential basis for cash consideration
- Trading commences on November 18, 2025
- Units rank pari-passu with existing units
- Distribution Numbers: 275400001 to 318661000
- ISIN: INE0Z8Z23013
- Two different lock-in periods apply to different tranches

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed:
  - 64,89,150 units (Dist. Nos. 275400001 to 281889150) locked until November 30, 2028
  - 3,67,71,850 units (Dist. Nos. 281889151 to 318661000) locked until November 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** November 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 1):** November 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 2):** November 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment to the sponsor of Capital Infra Trust with minimal market impact. The lock-in provisions ensure that approximately 15% of the issued units remain locked until 2028, while 85% will be unlocked by 2026. Trading members should note the new securities for their records. The pari-passu ranking ensures no dilution of rights for existing unitholders.
