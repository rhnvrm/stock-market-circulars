## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 198 bonus equity shares (face value Rs. 2 each) of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were kept in abeyance. These securities will be listed and permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, December 23, 2025. The shares are distributed across five distinctive number ranges.

## Key Points

- 198 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 2 per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- Distribution across 5 lots: 60, 100, 10, 15, and 13 shares
- Distinctive numbers assigned from 6412492908 to 6412647358

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the listing and should ensure these securities are available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 23, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 198 bonus equity shares kept in abeyance, representing an extremely small fraction of ICICI Bank's total equity. This is primarily an administrative action affecting specific shareholders whose bonus shares were previously held in abeyance. No material impact on trading volumes or stock price anticipated.
