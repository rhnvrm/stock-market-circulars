## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Timex Group India Limited. This circular provides details about the OFS mechanism, bidding process, and settlement procedures for institutional and non-institutional investors.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale mechanism opened for Timex Group India Limited
- OFS provides existing shareholders an opportunity to sell shares through exchange platform
- Separate bidding windows for institutional and non-institutional investors
- BSE platform to be used for conducting the OFS
- Settlement and allotment procedures as per standard OFS guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular implements existing OFS framework as per SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Selling shareholders must comply with OFS procedural requirements
- Bidders must meet eligibility criteria for respective investor categories
- Stock brokers must facilitate bidding process for clients
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per specified timelines
- Pre-bid and post-bid disclosure requirements to be met

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 26, 2025
- OFS opening date and bidding schedule to be as specified in the circular document
- Settlement dates as per T+2 settlement cycle or as specified

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - OFS creates significant trading activity and price discovery opportunity for Timex Group India Limited shares. May impact stock price based on demand-supply dynamics during bidding period.

**Investor Impact:** High - Provides opportunity for investors to acquire shares at discovered price. Existing shareholders may see dilution impact. Institutional and retail investors can participate based on allocation guidelines.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Stock brokers and trading members need to facilitate smooth bidding process. Exchange systems must handle OFS orders efficiently during bidding windows.
