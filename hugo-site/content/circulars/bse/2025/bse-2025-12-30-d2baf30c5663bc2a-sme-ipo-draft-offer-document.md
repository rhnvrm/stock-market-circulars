## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED filed on December 29, 2025, as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED on December 29, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public viewing and comments
- Public comment period: 21 days from December 30, 2025
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx#

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document during the 21-day period
- Comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from the notice date
- Company must comply with Schedule VI disclosure requirements of ICDR Regulations

- **Document Filing Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from December 30, 2025 (ending approximately January 20, 2026)
