## Summary

BSE has published details of shareholders subject to forfeiture proceedings for Nakoda Group of Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 541418) under case no. 252167. The circular provides comprehensive DNR (Distinct Number Range) details for shareholders whose equity shares are being forfeited.

## Key Points

- Company: Nakoda Group of Industries Limited (ISIN: IN9236Y01010)
- BSE Scrip Code: 541418
- Case Number: 252167
- Security Type: Equity shares with face value Rs. 2.50
- Multiple shareholder holdings listed with DNR ranges from 17679797 to 17719803
- Holdings range from as low as 1 share to as high as 2500 shares per entry

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This circular implements existing forfeiture procedures under securities law.

## Compliance Requirements

- Affected shareholders identified in the DNR ranges must take note of the forfeiture proceedings
- Company must comply with forfeiture procedures as per SEBI regulations and Companies Act
- BSE Listing Operations Department oversight of the forfeiture process

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 23, 2025
- Further action dates to be communicated as per case no. 252167 proceedings

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** High - Direct impact on shareholders within specified DNR ranges who face potential loss of equity holdings.

**Company Impact:** Significant impact on Nakoda Group of Industries Limited's shareholder register and capital structure following forfeiture completion.

**Market Impact:** Limited broader market impact but material for the specific scrip and affected shareholders who must respond to forfeiture notices.
