## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of BAJAJ HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are instructed not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: BAJAJ HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE377Y07391 (Code: 974422)
- Security: BHFL-7.83%-12-12-25-PVT
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification as per existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE377Y07391) from November 27, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the suspended debenture during the restriction period

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 27, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (BHFL-7.83%-12-12-25-PVT). The suspension is temporary and routine for debt redemption processes.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the terms. Investors cannot trade this security from November 27, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in this security.
