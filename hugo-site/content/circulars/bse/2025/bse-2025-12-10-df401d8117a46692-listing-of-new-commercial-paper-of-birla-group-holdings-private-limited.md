## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on its Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The instrument comprises 6,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 300 crore, issued on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and matures on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Issue Size: 6,000 units (Rs. 300 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730731
- Scrip ID: BGHP101225
- ISIN: INE09OL14IF3
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: December 10, 2025
- Maturity Date: March 11, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 91 days
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IF3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must use the designated scrip code 730731 and tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Birla Group Holdings Private Limited and offers investment opportunities for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members in the debt segment can access this instrument for client portfolios seeking short-term debt exposure with approximately 91-day maturity.
