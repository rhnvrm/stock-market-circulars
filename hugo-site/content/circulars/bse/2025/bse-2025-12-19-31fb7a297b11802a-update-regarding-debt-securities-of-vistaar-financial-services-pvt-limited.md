## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about changes to debt securities of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Limited. The company has received call money from debenture holders, resulting in a new ISIN and revised paid-up value effective December 22, 2025. This update follows the previous exchange notice dated September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 975778
- Original ISIN: INE016P07252
- New ISIN: INE016P07260
- Original paid-up value: Rs. 65,000 per debenture
- Revised paid-up value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- Changes effective from Monday, December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding existing debt securities following receipt of call money from debenture holders.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the ISIN change from INE016P07252 to INE016P07260
- Trading members must update records to reflect the new paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Systems must be updated before the effective date of December 22, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Monday, December 22, 2025
- **Previous Related Notice**: September 26, 2025 (Notice 20250926-4)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is primarily operational and affects holders of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Limited debentures. The call money receipt has increased the paid-up value per debenture by approximately 54% (from Rs. 65,000 to Rs. 1,00,000). Trading members dealing with these debt securities must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN and paid-up value to ensure accurate trading and settlement. The change is specific to this particular debt instrument and does not have broader market implications.
