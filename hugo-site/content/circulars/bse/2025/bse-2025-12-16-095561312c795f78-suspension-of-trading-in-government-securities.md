## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Government Security GS19DEC25 (Scrip Code: 800591, ISIN: IN001225C092) will mature on its redemption date and trading in this security will be suspended effective December 17, 2025. This is a standard procedure for maturing government securities.

## Key Points

- Government Security GS19DEC25 (ISIN: IN001225C092, Scrip Code: 800591) to mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from December 17, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date
- Circular reference: DR-779/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government securities in the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the affected government security (GS19DEC25) from December 17, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their systems and inform relevant stakeholders about the trading suspension
- All pending orders or positions in this security should be reviewed and managed appropriately before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: Notice date and circular issuance
- **December 17, 2025**: Effective date of trading suspension
- **Redemption Date**: Maturity date of GS19DEC25 (specific date to be noted as December 19, 2025 based on security naming convention)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a single government security approaching maturity. The suspension is a routine administrative action that follows standard market procedures.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended security. Investors holding this G-Sec will receive redemption proceeds as per standard settlement procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of GS19DEC25 who will receive their principal upon maturity. No action required from investors as redemption is automatic.
