## Summary

BSE has announced that DSM Fresh Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 544568) will undergo a Scrip ID change in the BOLT Plus System. The Scrip ID will be changed from 'DSM' to 'ZAPPFRESH' effective October 31, 2025, as per the company's request.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code remains unchanged: 544568
- Old Scrip ID: DSM
- New Scrip ID: ZAPPFRESH
- Company Name: DSM Fresh Foods Ltd
- Change applies to BOLT Plus System only
- Notice Number: 20251027-4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading system identifier at the company's request.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Scrip ID
- Trading members should take note of the change for order placement and tracking
- Members should contact BSE Listing Operations for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. The change affects only the display identifier in the BOLT Plus trading system. The scrip code (544568) remains unchanged, so existing systems using scrip codes for trading and settlement will not be affected. Trading members need to update internal references from 'DSM' to 'ZAPPFRESH' for order entry and tracking purposes.
