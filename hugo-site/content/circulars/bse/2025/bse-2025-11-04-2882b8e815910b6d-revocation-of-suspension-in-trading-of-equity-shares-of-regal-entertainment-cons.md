## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension of trading in equity shares of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Limited (Scrip Code: 531033, ISIN: INE101E01010). The company is an NBFC engaged in providing Micro Enterprise Loans, SME Loans, Other Structured Business, Personal Loans and ancillary services.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Regal Entertainment & Consultants Limited
- Scrip Code: 531033
- ISIN: INE101E01010
- CIN: L65923MH1992PLC064689
- Trading suspension has been revoked
- Company is listed only on BSE (not on any other exchange)
- Business Nature: NBFC providing Micro Enterprise Loans, SME Loans, Structured Business, Personal Loans
- Operational Status: Active
- Auditors: M/s. DBS & Associates Chartered Accountants, Mumbai

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The revocation allows shareholders and market participants to resume trading in the company's equity shares. This restores liquidity for existing shareholders and enables normal price discovery.

**Operational Impact:** The company can now operate with its shares actively traded on BSE, which may improve access to capital markets and enhance investor confidence.

**Investor Impact:** Existing shareholders can now exit or increase positions, and new investors can participate in the stock. The revocation suggests the company has addressed previous concerns that led to the suspension.
