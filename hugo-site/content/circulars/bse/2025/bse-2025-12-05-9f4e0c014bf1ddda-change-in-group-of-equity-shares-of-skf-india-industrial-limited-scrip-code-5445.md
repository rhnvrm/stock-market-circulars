## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SKF India (Industrial) Limited (Scrip Code - 544572) will be permitted to trade under 'B' group effective December 19, 2025. Trading members and market intermediaries have been notified of this group migration.

## Key Points

- Company: SKF India (Industrial) Limited
- Scrip Code: 544572
- New Trading Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251205-9
- Notice Date: December 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to 'B' group, which is typically designated for stocks with specific trading characteristics and settlement patterns as per BSE's group classification framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change for scrip code 544572
- Market intermediaries should inform their clients about the group migration
- All trading in these shares must comply with 'B' group trading norms from December 19, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The migration to 'B' group may affect trading dynamics for SKF India (Industrial) Limited shares. 'B' group stocks typically have different margin requirements, settlement procedures, and risk parameters compared to other groups. Trading members and investors should be aware of the modified trading conditions that will apply from the effective date.
