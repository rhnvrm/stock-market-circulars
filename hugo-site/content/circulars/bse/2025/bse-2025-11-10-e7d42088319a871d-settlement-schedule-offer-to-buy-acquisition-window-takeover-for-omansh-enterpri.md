## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy through Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OMANSH ENTERPRISES LIMITED. The bidding window will be open from November 11, 2025 to November 24, 2025, with settlement number 2025118. Obligation download is scheduled for November 28, 2025 and final settlement on December 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025118
- Bid Start Date: November 11, 2025
- Bid End Date: November 24, 2025
- Obligation Download: November 28, 2025
- Settlement Date: December 1, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement number 2025118 must be used by Members/Custodians in depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians
- Members can refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements existing guidelines as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Participants must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines
- Early pay-in process in Depository system must be completed sufficiently in advance before:
  - BSE OTB Bidding window closure for placing bids by members
  - Confirmation window closure for confirming bids by Custodians
- Members must use correct Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement number 2025118 in depository system
- Members should refer to designated file for stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 11, 2025**: Bid start date
- **November 24, 2025**: Bid end date
- **November 28, 2025**: Obligation download date
- **December 1, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it applies only to shareholders of OMANSH ENTERPRISES LIMITED and members/custodians participating in the takeover offer. The settlement schedule follows established procedures for acquisition windows. Members and custodians need to ensure timely early pay-in of securities to meet bidding and confirmation deadlines.
