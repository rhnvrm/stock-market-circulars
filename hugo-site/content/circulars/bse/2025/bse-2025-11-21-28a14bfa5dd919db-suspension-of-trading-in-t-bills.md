## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in T-bill scrip 815GUJ2025 (Scrip Code: 810586, ISIN: IN1520150088) effective November 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the T-bill reaching its redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the specified date.

- T-bill 815GUJ2025 to mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810586
- ISIN: IN1520150088

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill (815GUJ2025) from November 24, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems and inform relevant stakeholders
- Ensure no open positions or pending orders for this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date:** On or around November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single T-bill scrip upon maturity. T-bills regularly mature and are suspended from trading as part of normal debt market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Only affects holders and traders of this specific Gujarat government T-bill. No broader market implications. Trading members need to ensure compliance with the suspension to avoid trading in matured securities.
