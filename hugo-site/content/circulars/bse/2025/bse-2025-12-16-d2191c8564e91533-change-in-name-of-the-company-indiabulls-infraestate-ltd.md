## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying about the change in name of Indiabulls Infraestate Ltd. The change has been officially approved by the Registrar of Companies under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The document is digitally signed by Manish Riyal on July 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company name change approved for Indiabulls Infraestate Ltd
- Approval granted by Registrar of Companies, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Document digitally signed by Manish Riyal on 2025-07-04 at 15:54:28 +05:30
- Official notification carries the national motto "Satyameva Jayate" (Truth Alone Triumphs)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corporate action involving a change in the legal name of the company. The change has been formally approved by the regulatory authority (Registrar of Companies) as per the Companies Act requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must update all official documentation and communications with the new name
- Market participants should note the name change for records and trading purposes
- The company's stock symbol and ISIN may remain unchanged unless separately notified

## Important Dates

- Digital signature date: July 4, 2025, 15:54:28 +05:30
- Circular issue date: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal impact on trading activities. The name change does not affect the company's business operations, financial position, or shareholding structure. Investors and market participants should update their records accordingly. No immediate action is required from market participants beyond noting the name change for future reference.
