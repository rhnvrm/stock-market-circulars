## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 29, 2025. The circular includes securities being newly added to LT-ASM, securities moving between ASM stages, securities being removed from the framework, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM surveillance.

## Key Points

- 2 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework: Novateor Research Laboratories Ltd and Rajnish Wellness Ltd
- 1 security (Vantage Knowledge Academy Ltd) moved to higher ASM Stage II
- 4 securities removed from LT-ASM Framework due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks
- No securities moved to lower ASM stages or direct Stage IV
- Consolidated list shows securities across different ASM stages (Stage I primarily shown)

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term ASM Framework updates with specific stage assignments
- Securities moving between surveillance frameworks (LT-ASM to ESM, GSM, Trade for Trade, IBC)
- Maintenance of consolidated surveillance list with stage classifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to applicable surveillance measures for listed securities
- Securities under LT-ASM subject to enhanced monitoring and trading restrictions
- Compliance with stage-specific ASM requirements for affected securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025 - All LT-ASM framework changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Securities under LT-ASM face enhanced surveillance and potential trading restrictions
- **Market Participants**: Investors and traders must consider surveillance status when trading affected securities
- **Compliance Impact**: Brokers and trading members need to ensure adherence to surveillance measure requirements
- **Investor Protection**: Framework aims to protect investors by monitoring securities with specific risk characteristics
