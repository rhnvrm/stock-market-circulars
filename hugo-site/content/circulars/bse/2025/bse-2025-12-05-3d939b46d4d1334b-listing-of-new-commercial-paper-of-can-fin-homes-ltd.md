## Summary

Can Fin Homes Ltd has listed new Commercial Paper instruments on the BSE Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The issue consists of 10,000 units of Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis, with a total value of Rs. 500 crore (10,000 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakhs). The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10,000 units of Commercial Paper listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 500 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,631 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730707, Scrip ID: CFH041225
- ISIN: INE477A14EB5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE477A14EB5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded using the specified scrip code 730707 and scrip ID CFH041225

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides Can Fin Homes Ltd with short-term funding at a discount rate (implied yield based on issue price vs face value). For debt market participants, this adds another high-rated (A1+) commercial paper option with 3-month maturity. The listing is administrative in nature and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
