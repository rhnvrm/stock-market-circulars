## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED (Code: ECE-GSEC-23-12-25-PVT, ISIN: INE572O07FN0) effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED
- Security Code: ECE-GSEC-23-12-25-PVT (960361)
- ISIN: INE572O07FN0
- Record Date: December 8, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- No Dealings From: December 8, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice for debenture redemption as per existing BSE guidelines for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the specified debentures from December 8, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent dealings in the suspended securities
- Debenture holders should note the record date for redemption eligibility

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This suspension affects a single privately placed debt security with limited trading activity. The impact is confined to existing debenture holders of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard redemption procedure with clear timeline provided to market participants. Trading members have adequate notice period to update their systems and inform clients holding these debentures.
