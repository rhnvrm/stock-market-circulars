## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Capri Global Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 977340, ISIN: INE180C07247) on its Debt segment effective December 3, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 8.90% p.a., and maturing on June 1, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Capri Global Capital Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977340
- **Scrip ID**: 89CGL27
- **ISIN**: INE180C07247
- **Quantity**: 2,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 8.90% p.a.
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE AA/Stable, IVR AA/Positive
- **Date of Allotment**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 1, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notice for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE180C07247
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 1, 2026 to June 1, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: June 1, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities affecting only institutional investors who participated in the placement.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these debt securities on the BSE Debt segment. The securities add to the available debt instruments in the market.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to investors in Capri Global Capital's debt instruments. The AA credit rating indicates good creditworthiness with stable outlook.
