## Summary

BSE has issued a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 768 dated December 3, 2025. The auction involves 86 scrip codes across various companies where delivery obligations were not met. Quantities range from 1 share to over 89,000 shares depending on the security. This is a standard settlement mechanism to close out failed delivery positions.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 768
- Date: December 3, 2025
- Total scrips involved: 86 different securities
- Largest quantity: AVANCE TECH (89,925 shares), TRESCON LIMITED (46,993 shares)
- Includes major stocks like INFOSYS, TATA MOTORS, COAL INDIA, BIOCON, FEDERAL BANK
- Mix of large-cap and small-cap securities affected
- Auction triggered due to non-fulfillment of delivery obligations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational auction procedure under existing BSE settlement regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with short delivery positions must participate in the auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities for auction participation
- Auction results will determine final settlement prices for failed deliveries

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 3, 2025
- Auction Date: To be conducted as per standard settlement calendar (typically T+2 or T+3 from settlement date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 86 securities but represents routine settlement mechanism for delivery failures. Most quantities are small except for a few specific cases.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - trading members with short positions must arrange funds/securities for auction. Price volatility may occur in affected scrips during auction process.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - primarily affects institutional participants and brokers with failed deliveries. Retail investors holding these securities are not directly impacted unless their broker has short positions.
