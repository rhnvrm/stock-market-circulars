## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Efficient Fire and Security Solutions Limited, filed on December 30, 2025, for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Efficient Fire and Security Solutions Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at bsesme.com/download/340210/SME_IPO InPrinciple/DRHP_Efficient_30122025_20251230200639.pdf
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the draft offer document
- Any comments on the draft offer document must be submitted within 21 days (by January 20, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice issued and document made publicly available
- **January 20, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME IPO filing. It is relevant primarily to:
- Potential investors interested in Efficient Fire and Security Solutions Limited
- Market participants involved in SME segment offerings
- Regulatory observers tracking SME IPO pipeline

The 21-day comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations for transparency and public input on draft offer documents.
