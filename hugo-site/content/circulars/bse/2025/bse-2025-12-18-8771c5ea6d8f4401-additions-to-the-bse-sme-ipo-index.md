## Summary

BSE announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 19, 2025. HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED (Exchange ticker-544656) and PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker-544657) will be included in the index following their listing on December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED (ticker: 544656) listed on December 18, 2025
- PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (ticker: 544657) listed on December 18, 2025
- Index inclusion effective at market open on December 19, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference notices: 20251217-56 (HRS ALUGLAZE) and 20251217-55 (PAJSON AGRO)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new SME listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index constituents automatically updated in corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- **December 18, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **December 19, 2025**: Effective date for index inclusion (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action adding newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. The inclusion may generate minor trading activity from index-tracking funds or strategies focused on the SME IPO segment. Investors tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should note the composition changes. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
