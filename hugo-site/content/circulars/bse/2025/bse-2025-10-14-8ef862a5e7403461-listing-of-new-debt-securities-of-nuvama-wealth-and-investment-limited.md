## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on the BSE Debt segment with effect from October 14, 2025. The securities comprise 5000 units of 9.20% per annum non-convertible debentures issued on private placement basis with maturity date of December 20, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5000 units
- Scrip Code: 976911
- Scrip ID: 920NWIL27
- Detail Name: NWIL-9.20%-20-12-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE523L07AM2 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 102,564.1150
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: October 13, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.20% per annum (payable annually)
- Date of Redemption: December 20, 2027
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN INE523L07AM2
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 13, 2025
- Listing Date: October 14, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: December 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides debt market participants with an additional investment option in CARE AA/Stable rated instruments of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. The securities are available for trading exclusively in demat form on the BSE Debt segment. Impact is limited to debt segment investors and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
