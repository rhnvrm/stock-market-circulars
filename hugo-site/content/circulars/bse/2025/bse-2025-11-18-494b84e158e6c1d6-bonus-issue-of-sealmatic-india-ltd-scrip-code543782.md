## Summary

Sealmatic India Limited (Scrip Code: 543782) has submitted undertaking for bonus issue with record date fixed as 21st November 2025. The company will credit 18,10,000 bonus shares (face value Rs. 10/- per share) to eligible shareholders' demat accounts by 24th November 2025, with shares available for trading from 25th November 2025.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be issued: 18,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- ISIN: INE0O4601016
- Distinctive Numbers: 90,50,001 to 1,08,60,000
- Bonus issue approved by Board and shareholders
- Company confirms compliance with all SEBI, Stock Exchange and Depository regulations

## Compliance Requirements

- Credit of bonus shares to shareholders' demat accounts to be completed by 12:00 P.M. on T+1 day
- Company to comply with all applicable regulations of Stock Exchange, SEBI, and Depositories
- Undertaking submitted as per regulatory requirements for bonus share issuance

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: Friday, 21st November 2025 - for determining eligible shareholders
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: Monday, 24th November 2025 (T+1)
- **Credit of Bonus Shares**: By 12:00 P.M. on 24th November 2025
- **Effective Listing Date**: Tuesday, 25th November 2025 (T+2)
- **Trading Commencement**: Beginning of Day (BOD) on 25th November 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Shareholders**: Existing shareholders as on record date will receive bonus shares in their demat accounts, increasing their shareholding without additional payment. The bonus issue will increase the paid-up capital and total number of outstanding shares.

**Trading Impact**: Trading in bonus shares will commence from 25th November 2025. The stock price is expected to adjust proportionately based on the bonus ratio to reflect the increased share capital.

**Market Implications**: This corporate action affects the shareholding pattern and liquidity of Sealmatic India Limited shares on the BSE SME platform.
