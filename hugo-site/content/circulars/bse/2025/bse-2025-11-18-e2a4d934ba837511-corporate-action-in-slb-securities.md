## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions affecting Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) securities. Eight companies have declared dividends and one company (Thyrocare Technologies Limited) has announced a bonus issue. SLB trading will be suspended for these securities during their respective shut periods.

## Key Points

- 8 companies announcing dividend distributions with varying record dates between November 17-25, 2025
- 1 bonus issue by Thyrocare Technologies Limited with record date November 28, 2025
- SLB shut periods range from 1-2 days for each security
- All ex-dates align with respective book closure/record dates
- Thyrocare's bonus issue includes a foreclosure date of November 28, 2025

   - Record Date: November 25, 2025
   - Ex Date: November 25, 2025
   - Shut Period: November 24-25, 2025

   - Record Date: November 21, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB market participants must note the shut periods for affected securities
- No new SLB positions can be created during shut periods
- Existing positions must be managed considering the suspension periods
- Market participants should plan their SLB strategies around these corporate action dates

## Important Dates

- **November 14-17, 2025**: GMM Pfaudler shut period (earliest)
- **November 17-18, 2025**: Ashok Leyland shut period
- **November 19-20, 2025**: Natco Pharma and SUN TV shut period
- **November 20-21, 2025**: M.R.F., Polyplex Corporation, and Oil India shut period
- **November 24-25, 2025**: Ingersoll-Rand shut period
- **November 27-28, 2025**: Thyrocare Technologies shut period (latest)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects liquidity in SLB market for 9 securities over a two-week period. Participants engaged in securities lending and borrowing will need to adjust their positions or avoid these securities during shut periods.

**Operational Impact**: SLB desks and participants must track multiple shut periods across different dates. The staggered nature of shut periods (November 14-28) requires careful monitoring and operational planning for securities lending and borrowing activities.
