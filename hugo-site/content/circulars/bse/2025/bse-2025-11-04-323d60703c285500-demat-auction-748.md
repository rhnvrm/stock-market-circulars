## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction details for Settlement No. 748 dated November 4, 2025. The auction involves 104 different scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 31,060 shares. This is a routine settlement process where securities that failed to be delivered in previous trading sessions are auctioned to complete the settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 748
- Settlement Date: November 4, 2025
- Total Scrips Affected: 104 scrips from various companies
- Quantity Range: From 1 share (multiple scrips) to 31,060 shares (Vantage Know)
- Sectors Covered: Banking, manufacturing, finance, technology, automotive, metals, textiles, and others
- Largest Quantities: Vantage Know (31,060), Cressanda Ra (18,341), Dredging Cor (10,272), Laddu Gopal (10,000), Healthy Life (10,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members must ensure settlement of auctioned securities as per BSE guidelines
- Members with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per the prescribed timeline for Settlement No. 748
- Members should verify their obligations and ensure adequate funds/securities for settlement

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 748
- Specific auction timelines and settlement completion dates to be followed as per BSE's standard auction procedures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The auction affects 104 scrips across multiple sectors, which may lead to temporary price volatility in the affected securities during the auction period.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Clearing members and brokers with outstanding obligations must ensure timely settlement. Failure to settle may result in penalties and operational disruptions.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Investors in the affected scrips may experience temporary liquidity constraints or price movements during the auction settlement process. Most quantities are relatively small except for a few scrips with larger volumes.
