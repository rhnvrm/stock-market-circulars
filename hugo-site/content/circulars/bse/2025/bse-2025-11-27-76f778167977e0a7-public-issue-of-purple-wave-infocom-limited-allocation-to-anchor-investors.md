- Company: Purple Wave Infocom Limited
- Event: Allocation to anchor investors in public issue
- Notification date: November 27, 2025
- This is part of the pre-IPO allocation process

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure requirement for public issues.

- Part of the overall IPO disclosure framework

## Important Dates

- Notification Date: November 27, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed

The allocation to anchor investors typically indicates institutional investor interest in the IPO. This is a positive signal for the upcoming public issue as anchor investors provide price discovery and stability. The impact is limited to Purple Wave Infocom Limited and prospective retail investors in the IPO.
