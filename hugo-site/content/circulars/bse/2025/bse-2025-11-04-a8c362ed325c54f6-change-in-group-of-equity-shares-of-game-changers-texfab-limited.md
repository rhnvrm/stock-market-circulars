## Summary

BSE announces the transfer of equity shares of Game Changers Texfab Limited (Scrip Code: 544594) from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment under M Group, effective November 19, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a transition to normal rolling settlement.

## Key Points

- Company: Game Changers Texfab Limited (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544594
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, November 19, 2025
- Notice issued in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251103-44 dated November 03, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from compulsory delivery-based trading (Trade for Trade segment) to regular rolling settlement, allowing intraday trading and T+1 settlement cycle. This is a standard progression for SME IPO stocks after initial listing period.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Adjust trading terminals and risk management parameters accordingly
- Inform clients about the change in trading characteristics
- Contact the designated BSE official for clarification if needed

## Important Dates

- **November 03, 2025**: Previous related notice issued (Notice No. 20251103-44)
- **November 04, 2025**: Current notice date
- **November 19, 2025**: Effective date for group transfer

## Impact Assessment

This change will enhance liquidity and trading flexibility for the scrip by allowing intraday trading and margin trading facility. Market participants will benefit from reduced settlement risk and improved capital efficiency. The transition from MT to M Group indicates the stock has completed its initial stabilization period post-IPO.
