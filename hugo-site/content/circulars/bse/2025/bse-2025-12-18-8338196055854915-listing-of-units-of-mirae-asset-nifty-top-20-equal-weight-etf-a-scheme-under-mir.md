## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Mirae Asset Nifty Top 20 Equal Weight ETF units effective December 19, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme that replicates/tracks the Nifty Top 20 Equal Weight Total Return Index. A total of 6,484,327 units will be listed in the B Group with scrip code 544660, trading in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **ETF Name**: Mirae Asset Nifty Top 20 Equal Weight ETF
- **Investment Manager**: Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited
- **Total Units**: 6,484,327 units
- **Scrip Code**: 544660
- **ISIN**: INF769K01PZ5
- **Scrip ID**: TOP20
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 9.62 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: December 15, 2025
- **Group**: B
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following existing SEBI guidelines for mutual fund units trading in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that units will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines
- Trades will be executed in unit market lots (1 unit)
- Market makers have been appointed to provide liquidity

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- **Notice Date**: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine ETF listing that adds a new investment product tracking the Nifty Top 20 Equal Weight Total Return Index to the BSE platform.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors with access to a new passive investment vehicle offering equal-weight exposure to top 20 Nifty stocks, diversifying away from market-cap weighted concentration.

**Liquidity**: Five market makers have been appointed to ensure adequate liquidity: Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, East India Securities Limited, Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited, Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited, and IRage Broking Services LLP.
