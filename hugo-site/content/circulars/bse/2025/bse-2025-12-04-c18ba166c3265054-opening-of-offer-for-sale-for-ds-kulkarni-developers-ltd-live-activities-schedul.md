## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of DS Kulkarni Developers Ltd (Security Code: 523890, ISIN: INE891A01022). The OFS will be conducted on December 5, 2025 for institutional and non-institutional investors, and December 8, 2025 for retail investors. The total offer size is 5,00,000 shares at a floor price of Rs 10.00 per equity share, with 10% reserved for retail investors. All eligible bidders will be allotted shares at the floor price using proportionate allocation methodology.

## Key Points

- **Offer Size**: 5,00,000 shares (Non-Retail: 4,50,000 shares, Retail: 50,000 shares)
- **Floor Price**: Rs 10.00 per equity share (Face Value: Rs 10/-)
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Tick Size**: Rs 0.01
- **Allocation Method**: Proportionate allocation at floor price of Rs 10.00
- **Security Symbol**: DSKULKARNI
- **Security Code**: 523890
- **ISIN**: INE891A01022
- **Retail Reservation**: 10% of issue size (50,000 shares)
- **Retail Definition**: Individual investors with bid value up to Rs 2 lakhs
- **Multiple Bidding**: Allowed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard OFS procedure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and Custodian Members:**
- Note the OFS schedule and timings
- Ensure proper access to OFS modules (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet)
- Follow specified bidding categories and margin requirements
- Complete give-up/take-up activities within specified timeframes

**Bidding Categories and Margin Requirements:**
- **Retail Investors (RI/RIC)**: 100% upfront margin in cash and cash equivalents, bid value up to Rs 2 lakhs
- **Employees (EMP)**: 100% upfront margin in cash and cash equivalents, bid value up to Rs 5 lakhs
- **Non-Institutional Investors (NII)**: 100% upfront margin in cash (includes retail investors bidding above Rs 2 lakhs)
- **Institutional Investors (MF/IC/OTHS)**: Either 100% upfront margin in cash OR 0% margin option in cash

**Platform Access URLs:**
- iBBS: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) / https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) / https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) / https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

**Important Notes:**
- Retail investors can bid in RI, RIC, and/or NII categories
- Retail investors bidding in NII category are not eligible for discounts (if any)
- Multiple bidding is allowed across categories

## Important Dates

**T Day - December 5, 2025 (Thursday):**
- Bid opening for IC/MF/OTH/NII: 9:15 AM
- Bid closing for IC/MF/OTH/NII: 3:30 PM
- Bid modification and cancellation: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

**T+1 Day - December 8, 2025 (Monday):**
- Give-up/Take-up for institutional investors (0% margin bids): Up to 7:30 AM
- Bid opening for RI/RIC/EMP and un-allocated bidders: 9:15 AM
- Bid closing for RI/RIC/EMP and un-allocated bidders: 3:30 PM
- Bid modification and cancellation: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- Settlement day for T day institutional/non-institutional bids (100% margin)

**T+2 Day - December 9, 2025 (Tuesday):**
- Give-up/Take-up for un-allocated bidders from T+1: Up to 7:30 AM
- Settlement day for retail investors and T day institutional bids (0% margin)
- Settlement day for T+1 carried forward non-retail bids

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific OFS of moderate size (5 lakh shares) with limited market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Relevant for investors interested in DS Kulkarni Developers Ltd, particularly retail investors who have dedicated access on T+1 day with 10% reservation.
