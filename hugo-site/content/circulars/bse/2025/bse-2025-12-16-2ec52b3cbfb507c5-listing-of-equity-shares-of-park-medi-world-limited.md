## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544645, trading symbol PARKHOSPS, and ISIN number INE119201023. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544645
- Trading Symbol: PARKHOSPS
- ISIN Number: INE119201023
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes applicable. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the actual listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine new listing notification that expands the universe of tradeable securities on BSE. Trading members and investors should await the separate notice confirming the actual listing date and complete security details before any trading activity can commence in this scrip.
