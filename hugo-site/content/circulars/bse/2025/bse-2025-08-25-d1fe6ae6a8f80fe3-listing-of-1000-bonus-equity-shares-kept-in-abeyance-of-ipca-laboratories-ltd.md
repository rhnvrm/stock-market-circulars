## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1000 bonus equity shares of Ipca Laboratories Ltd (Scrip Code: 524494) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1000 bonus equity shares of Rs. 1/- each are being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE571A01038
- Distinctive numbers range: 1303453 to 1304452
- Company scrip code: 524494

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of previously issued bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: Tuesday, August 26, 2025
- Notice Date: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to the small number of shares (1000) being released from abeyance. This represents a routine administrative action for previously declared bonus shares of Ipca Laboratories.
