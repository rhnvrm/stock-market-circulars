## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd (Scrip Code 975892) effective December 03, 2025. Trading was previously suspended following the company's record date of July 23, 2025, which was set for interest payment and redemption of debentures. The company has now requested to resume trading operations.

## Key Points

- Trading in Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd debentures (Scrip Code 975892) to resume from December 03, 2025
- Previous suspension was linked to Exchange Notice No. 20250721-46 dated July 21, 2025
- Record date for interest payment and redemption was July 23, 2025
- Resumption request submitted by Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd
- Reference: DR-769/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard trading resumption notice following completion of corporate action procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the resumption of trading for Scrip Code 975892
- Trading members should ensure their systems are updated to allow trading from December 03, 2025

## Important Dates

- **July 21, 2025**: Original suspension notice issued
- **July 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 02, 2025**: Resumption notice issued
- **December 03, 2025**: Trading resumes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only holders and traders of specific debentures (Scrip Code 975892) of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to allow trading in these debentures from December 03, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for debenture holders who will regain liquidity and ability to trade these securities on the exchange.
