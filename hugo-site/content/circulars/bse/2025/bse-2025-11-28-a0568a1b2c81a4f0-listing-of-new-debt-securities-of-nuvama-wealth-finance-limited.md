## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 7,405 new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 28, 2025. The securities (NWFL-26-10-35-PVT) carry a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, with a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable credit rating and a maturity date of October 26, 2035.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 7,405 securities
- **Scrip Code**: 977322
- **Scrip ID**: NWFL251125
- **Detail Name**: NWFL-26-10-35-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE918K07QG4
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000 per security
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: October 26, 2035
- **Interest Rate**: As per Key Information Document (KID)
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QG4
- Members must use the tick size of 1 paise for trading these securities
- For further details, trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025 (effective immediately)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 26, 2035

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities affecting only the debt market segment. The securities are institutional in nature with high face value (Rs. 1 lakh per unit), limiting participation to institutional and high-net-worth investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on BSE's debt segment in dematerialized form with standard settlement procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and participants in the debt market who invested in this private placement. No impact on retail equity investors.
