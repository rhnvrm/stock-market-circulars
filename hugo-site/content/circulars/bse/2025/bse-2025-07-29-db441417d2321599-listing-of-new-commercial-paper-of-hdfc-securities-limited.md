## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 15 crores issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (3000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 15 crores
- Scrip Code: 729919
- ISIN: INE700G14PC1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,280/- per unit
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 29, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited and trading opportunity for debt market participants.
