## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Pine Labs Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544606 and symbol PINELABS with ISIN INE15B701018. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Pine Labs Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544606
- Symbol: PINELABS
- ISIN Number: INE15B701018
- Exact listing date to be announced through separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details for Pine Labs Limited
- Trading members should await further notice regarding the specific listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 13, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has medium impact on the market. It informs trading members about an upcoming new listing, allowing them to prepare for trading in Pine Labs Limited shares. Investors and market participants interested in this stock should monitor for the follow-up notice containing the actual listing date and additional security details. The listing will expand trading opportunities on BSE's equity segment.
