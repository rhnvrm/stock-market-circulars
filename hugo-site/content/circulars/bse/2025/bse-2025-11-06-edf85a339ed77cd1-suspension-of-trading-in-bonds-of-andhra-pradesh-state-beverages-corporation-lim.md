## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of Andhra Pradesh State Beverages Corporation Limited (APSBCL) effective November 13, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for APSBCL 9.62% bonds (ISIN: INE0M2307131, Code: 974446)
- Record date fixed for November 13, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from November 13, 2025 onwards
- Notice number: DR-755/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notice implementing standard procedures for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified APSBCL bonds from November 13, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealing restriction

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 13, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 13, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity and tradability of APSBCL bonds. Bondholders cannot exit positions after the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors holding these bonds must be aware that no trading will be possible from November 13, 2025. Those seeking to sell must do so before the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems and inform clients about the trading restrictions on this specific bond issue.
