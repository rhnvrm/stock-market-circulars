## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities (Treasury Bills) issued by Lodha Developers Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 14, 2025. The securities comprise 25,000 units with a total issue size of Rs. 250 crores (face value Rs. 1,00,000 per unit). The securities carry a credit rating of CRISIL AA/Stable and will be traded only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Lodha Developers Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977293
- **Scrip ID**: LDL131125
- **ISIN**: INE670K07349
- **Security Name**: LDL-TBILLS-13-11-28-PVT
- **Quantity**: 25,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crores
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 2.45% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from 30/09/2026 to 13/11/2028
- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 13, 2028
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE670K07349
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Further details are available in the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: September 30, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 13, 2028
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from September 30, 2026 to November 13, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing on the debt segment with no direct impact on equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Relevant only for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in private placement securities.

**Company Impact**: Neutral - Routine debt fundraising activity for Lodha Developers Limited. The AA/Stable rating indicates strong creditworthiness.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal - Trading restricted to dematerialized form on BSE Debt segment with standard settlement procedures.
