## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-bills) and one Sovereign Gold Bond effective December 09, 2025, as these securities will mature on the redemption date of December 11, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Four debt securities will be suspended from trading effective December 09, 2025
- Securities include three T-bills with different tenures (91-day, 182-day, and 364-day)
- One Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series also included in suspension
- Suspension due to upcoming maturity/redemption on December 11, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-773/2025-2026

| 800279 | IN0020170133 | Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series |

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified securities from December 09, 2025
- No new transactions should be executed in these T-bills and sovereign gold bond
- Members should note the suspension for operational compliance

## Important Dates

- **December 08, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 09, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 11, 2025**: Redemption/maturity date for the securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders of these specific T-bills and sovereign gold bond. Routine administrative action for maturing debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trades in these securities from December 09, 2025. Investors holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date.

**Segment**: Debt market segment only; no impact on equity markets.
