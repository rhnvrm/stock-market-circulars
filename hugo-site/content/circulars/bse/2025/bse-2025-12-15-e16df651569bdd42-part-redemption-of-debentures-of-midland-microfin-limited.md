## Summary

MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE884Q07715, Code: MML-8.69%-11-7-26-PVT, Scrip Code: 975816) along with payment of interest. Trading members are informed that trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value effective from December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of non-convertible debentures by MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED
- Record date fixed for payment of interest and part redemption
- Reduced face value of Rs. 28,570 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value effective from December 26, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the record date of December 27, 2025
- Trading members must ensure trading reflects the reduced face value of Rs. 28,570 per debenture from December 26, 2025 onwards
- Debenture holders must be aware of the part redemption and interest payment schedule

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: December 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 27, 2025 (for interest payment and part redemption)
- **Settlement Number**: DR-785/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting only holders of specific debentures (INE884Q07715) of MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 28,570 per debenture from December 26, 2025. This is a standard operational adjustment for debt securities undergoing part redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive interest payment and partial redemption as per the record date. The reduced face value reflects the return of principal through part redemption.
