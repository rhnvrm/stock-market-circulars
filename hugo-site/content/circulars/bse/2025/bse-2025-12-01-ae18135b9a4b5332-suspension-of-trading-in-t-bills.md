## Summary

BSE has issued a suspension of trading notice for three Treasury Bills (T-bills) and one Sovereign Gold Bond that are scheduled to mature on their respective redemption dates. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Four debt securities will be suspended from trading effective December 02, 2025
- Suspension is due to maturity/redemption of these securities
- Notice issued under reference DR-768/2025-2026
- Affects debt segment trading members

## Securities Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Description |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805074 | IN002025Y107 | 182T041225 (182-day T-bill) |
| 804997 | IN002024Z347 | 364TB41225 (364-day T-bill) |
| 805112 | IN002025X232 | 91TB041225 (91-day T-bill) |
| 800278 | IN0020170125 | Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the mentioned securities from December 02, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in these securities
- No new positions should be opened in these instruments

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** December 02, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 04, 2025 (implied maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific government securities. The suspension is routine and expected as securities approach maturity.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to ensure compliance by blocking trades in these securities from their trading systems. Investors holding these securities should prepare for redemption proceeds.
