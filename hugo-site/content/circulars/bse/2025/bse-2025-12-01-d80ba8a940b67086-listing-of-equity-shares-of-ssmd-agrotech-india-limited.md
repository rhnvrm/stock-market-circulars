## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange's SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544621 and symbol SSMD with ISIN INE1U2R01013. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544621
- Symbol: SSMD
- ISIN: INE1U2R01013
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Specific listing date: To be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions are required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 1, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for a single company on the SME platform. The impact is limited to trading members and investors interested in this specific stock. Market-wide impact is minimal. Trading members should await the separate notice with specific listing date and security details before commencing trading operations in this scrip.
