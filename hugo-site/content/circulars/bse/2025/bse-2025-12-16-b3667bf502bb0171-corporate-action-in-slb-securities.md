## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions for three securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. Can Fin Homes Ltd and Indian Oil Corporation Ltd have declared dividends, while Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited has announced a bonus issue. These corporate actions will have specific ex-dates, record dates, and shut periods that SLB participants must be aware of.

## Key Points

- Can Fin Homes Ltd: Dividend with ex-date and record date on 19-December-2025
- Indian Oil Corporation Ltd: Dividend with ex-date and record date on 18-December-2025
- Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited: Bonus issue with ex-date, record date, and foreclosure date on 19-December-2025
- Shut periods apply for all three securities during which SLB transactions will be restricted

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification for SLB segment participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants must note the shut periods during which lending and borrowing will not be allowed
- Participants holding positions must be aware of ex-dates to determine dividend/bonus entitlement
- Lenders and borrowers need to manage positions considering the foreclosure date for Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited

## Important Dates

**Can Fin Homes Ltd (Dividend)**
- Ex-Date: 19-December-2025
- Record Date: 19-December-2025
- Shut Period: 18-Dec-2025 to 19-Dec-2025

**Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (Dividend)**
- Ex-Date: 18-December-2025
- Record Date: 18-December-2025
- Shut Period: 17-Dec-2025 to 18-Dec-2025

**Dr. Lal Pathlabs Limited (Bonus Issue)**
- Ex-Date: 19-December-2025
- Record Date: 19-December-2025
- Foreclosure Date: 19-Dec-2025
- Shut Period: 18-Dec-2025 to 19-Dec-2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects SLB participants with positions in these three securities. The shut periods will temporarily restrict lending and borrowing activities.

**Operational Impact**: Participants must ensure their systems and processes account for the shut periods and adjust their SLB strategies accordingly. The bonus issue for Dr. Lal Pathlabs will require position adjustments post-record date.

**Investor Impact**: Investors using SLB facilities need to be aware that they cannot enter new SLB transactions during shut periods. Those holding borrowed securities will not be entitled to dividends/bonus shares unless they close positions before ex-dates.
