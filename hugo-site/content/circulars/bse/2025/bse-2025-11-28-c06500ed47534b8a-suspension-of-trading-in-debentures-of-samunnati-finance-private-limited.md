## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 01, 2025, due to the maturity of these securities. The affected debenture (SFPL-11.37%-2-12-25-PVT) with ISIN INE551U07316 and Scrip Code 975979 will mature on the specified date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture Series: SFPL-11.37%-2-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE551U07316
- Scrip Code: 975979
- Maturity Date: December 02, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: December 01, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-767/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for maturity-related trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Samunnati Finance Private Limited from December 01, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 01, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 02, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to holders of this specific debenture series of Samunnati Finance Private Limited. The suspension is a routine procedure for maturing debt securities. Debenture holders will receive their principal and final interest payment upon maturity. No impact on equity markets or other debt securities.
