## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by DAR CREDIT & CAPITAL LIMITED, effective November 25, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of DAR CREDIT & CAPITAL LIMITED will be suspended from trading
- ISIN INE04Q907066 (Code: 960472) - DCCL-12.25%-10-2-26-PVT series
- ISIN INE04Q907082 (Code: 960473) - DCCL-NIFTY-10-2-26-PVT series
- Record date for early redemption: November 25, 2025
- No dealings permitted from November 25, 2025
- Reference: DR-763/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture suspension due to early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 25, 2025
- All exchange members must take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in these securities
- Debenture holders should be aware that trading will cease before the record date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 25, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of two specific NCD series from a single issuer. No broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for affected debenture holders - they will not be able to trade these securities from November 25, 2025, though early redemption provides an exit opportunity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard suspension procedure for corporate action. Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in these ISINs from the specified date.
