## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that the surrender of trading membership by Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770) has been approved by SEBI. This is an administrative update with no operational implications for other market participants.

## Key Points

- Trading member Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770) has resigned
- The surrender of membership has been approved by SEBI
- Notice issued by BSE Membership Department on October 15, 2025
- Notice number: 20251015-2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative matter involving the resignation of a single trading member. There is no impact on market operations, trading systems, or other market participants. The approval by SEBI confirms the proper completion of the resignation process.
