## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Delisting) for MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED (Settlement No. 2025113). The original bidding period was conducted from August 14-21, 2024, and the revised settlement dates have been updated for obligation download and final settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Delisting (Settlement No. 2025113)
- Bid Period: October 30, 2025 to November 06, 2025
- Revised Obligation Download Date: November 07, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: November 10, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Available in both depositories with Market Type "Delisting" and Settlement No. 2025113

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the previous Notice No. 20251029-10 regarding the settlement calendar for MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED's delisting process. Members and custodians must follow the revised guidelines for acquisition of shares through stock exchange as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism for demat share delivery
- Must specify Market Type "Delisting" and Settlement No. 2025113 in depository system
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Contact ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it specifically pertains to the delisting process of MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED. The revision affects shareholders participating in the delisting offer and members/custodians handling the settlement process. The change primarily involves timeline adjustments for obligation download and final settlement, with no changes to the core delisting mechanism or requirements.
