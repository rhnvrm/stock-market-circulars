## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Yaashavi Jewellers Limited filed on December 25, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for a minimum period of 21 days for stakeholders to submit their comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Yaashavi Jewellers Limited on December 25, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Minimum 21-day comment period from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments on the Draft Offer Document must submit them within 21 days from December 26, 2025
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The company must keep the Draft Offer Document available on the SME website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 25, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **December 26, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 16, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it relates to a single SME IPO in the jewellery sector. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. Impact is primarily relevant to:

- Prospective investors interested in Yaashavi Jewellers Limited IPO
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the offer document
- SME segment observers tracking new listings

No immediate trading or operational impact on existing market participants.
