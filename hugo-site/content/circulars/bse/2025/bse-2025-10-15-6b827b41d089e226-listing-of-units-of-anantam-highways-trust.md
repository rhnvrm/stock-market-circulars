## Summary

BSE has listed the units of Anantam Highways Trust (InvIT) with ISIN INE1UA823019. The post-issue unit holding pattern shows total outstanding units of 21,75,00,000 with sponsor/investment manager group holding 37.54% and public holding at 62.46%. The compliance officer is Chandra Kant Sharma.

## Key Points

- Total outstanding units: 21,75,00,000 (21.75 crore units)
- Sponsor and Sponsor Group holding: 8,16,49,861 units (37.54%)
- Public holding: 13,58,50,139 units (62.46%)
- ISIN: INE1UA823019
- 100% of sponsor units are mandatorily held
- No units are pledged or encumbered
- Compliance Officer: Chandra Kant Sharma
