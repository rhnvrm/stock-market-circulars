## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The issuance consists of 2,500 units of Commercial Paper worth Rs.5,00,000 each (total Rs.125 crores) issued on a private placement basis. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form with a 91-day maturity period ending February 5, 2026.

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

- Trading members should use the specified ISIN Number (INE071G14HE0) for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides short-term financing to ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED for a 91-day period. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing expands the debt market inventory but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Impact is limited to institutional investors and debt market participants interested in short-term money market instruments.
