## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as TIRUPATI TYRES LTD. The company's name has been officially changed to TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre, and digitally signed on September 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: TIRUPATI TYRES LTD
- **New Name**: TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED
- **CIN**: L46209MH1988PLC285197
- **Original Name**: TIRUPATI TYRES PRIVATE LIMITED (at incorporation)
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Address**: Unit No 606, Reliables Pride, Anand Nagar, opp Heera Panna, Jogeshwari West, Mumbai-400102, Maharashtra, India
- **Certificate Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Approved By**: Prerna Panwar, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative change of company name processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (TIRUPATI TYRES LTD) along with its new name (TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 29, 2025, 12:13:36 IST
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from September 29, 2025 (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, business activities, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all stationery, signage, and documentation to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on stakeholders' rights and liabilities. Investors and creditors should note the new name for future correspondence and transactions with the company.
