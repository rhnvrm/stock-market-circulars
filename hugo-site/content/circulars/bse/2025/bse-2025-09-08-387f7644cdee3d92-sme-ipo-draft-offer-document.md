## Summary

Century Business Media Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on September 5, 2025. The document is available for public review and comments for a mandatory 21-day period as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Century Business Media Limited filed Draft Offer Document on September 5, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard compliance with existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public must submit comments within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Company must maintain document availability on exchange website for minimum 21 days
- All disclosures per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations must be included

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: September 5, 2025
- Notice date: September 8, 2025
- Comment submission deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for SME segment participants and investors interested in new listings. Standard IPO process with no unusual market disruption expected. Provides investment opportunity in SME segment once IPO proceeds.
