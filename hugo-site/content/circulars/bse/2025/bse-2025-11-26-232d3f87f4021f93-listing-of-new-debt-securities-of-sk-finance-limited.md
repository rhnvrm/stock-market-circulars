## Summary

SK Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 44,500 units at a face value of ₹1,00,000 per unit, representing a total issue size of ₹445 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 9.21% per annum with annual interest payments and will mature on November 25, 2030.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: SK Finance Limited
- **Quantity**: 44,500 units
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: ₹445 crore
- **Interest Rate**: 9.21% per annum
- **Scrip Code**: 977313
- **Scrip ID**: 921SKFL30
- **ISIN**: INE124N07788
- **Credit Rating**: IND AA-/Positive
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issue Type**: Private Placement
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE124N07788
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at BSE on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 25, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Annually through November 25, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 25, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt listing with no direct impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt investors interested in SK Finance Limited securities. The IND AA-/Positive credit rating indicates strong creditworthiness with stable outlook.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt trading procedures apply. Trading members can access additional information through the placement memorandum on BSE website.
