## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective December 10, 2025. The issuance consists of 500 units with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Quantity: 500 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,92,147 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730737
- Scrip ID: BFSL091225
- ISIN: INE01C314DN2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DN2
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting only members trading debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard listing with no special conditions. Trading members dealing with debt instruments can trade this commercial paper following regular debt segment procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and high net worth investors in debt markets who deal with commercial paper instruments. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness.
