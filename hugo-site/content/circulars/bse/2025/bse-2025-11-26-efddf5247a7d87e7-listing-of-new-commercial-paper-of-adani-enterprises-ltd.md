## Summary

BSE has listed 600 new Commercial Papers issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis. The instruments are denominated at Rs. 5,00,000 each with a 3-month maturity period. Trading commences on the BSE Debt segment effective from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 600 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,027 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730330
- Scrip ID: AEL26925
- ISIN: INE423A14YF8 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading to be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 26, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt financing for Adani Enterprises Ltd. The impact on broader market operations is minimal as this is a standard private placement instrument. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities can access this instrument for their clients seeking short-term corporate debt exposure.
