## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture series from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debenture series SML-11.76%-14-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE982X07218, Code: 973717)
- Record date fixed as December 29, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 29, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for trading suspension due to corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED from December 29, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific debenture series SML-11.76%-14-1-26-PVT. This is a routine corporate action affecting a single debt instrument. The suspension facilitates the orderly redemption process and ensures accurate determination of debenture holders entitled to interest payment and redemption proceeds. No broader market impact is anticipated.
