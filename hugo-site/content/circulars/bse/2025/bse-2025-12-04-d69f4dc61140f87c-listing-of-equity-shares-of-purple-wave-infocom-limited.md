## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Purple Wave Infocom Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544627, trading symbol PURPLEWAVE, and ISIN INE0VDK01019. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Purple Wave Infocom Limited
- Scrip Code: 544627
- Trading Symbol: PURPLEWAVE
- ISIN: INE0VDK01019
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice Number: 20251204-13

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems for trading in the new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to trading members who need to be aware of the new security. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business, IPO details, and listing date, which will be communicated separately. No immediate action required from market participants until the listing date is announced.
