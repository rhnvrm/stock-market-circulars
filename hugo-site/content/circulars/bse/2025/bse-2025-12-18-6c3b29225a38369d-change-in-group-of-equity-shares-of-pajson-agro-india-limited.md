## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544657), an SME IPO, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Friday, January 02, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251217-55 dated December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED is an SME IPO with Scrip Code 544657
- Stock currently trading in Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Will be shifted to Rolling segment under M Group
- Transfer effective from January 02, 2026
- Trading Members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED will transition from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for regular intraday trading instead of delivery-only transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the group change for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED
- Systems and risk management parameters should be updated to reflect Rolling segment status
- Members may contact the Exchange for clarifications if needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 02, 2026 (Friday)
- **Previous Notice**: December 17, 2025 (Notice No. 20251217-55)

## Impact Assessment

The migration from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment will enhance liquidity for PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED shares by allowing intraday trading, margin trading, and delivery-based transactions. This is a positive development for market participants as it reduces trading restrictions and provides greater flexibility. The impact is medium for existing shareholders and traders of this stock.
