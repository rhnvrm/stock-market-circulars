## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited on its Debt segment effective November 28, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 5,000 debentures, each having a face value of Rs. 100,000. The debentures carry an interest rate of 10.75% per annum payable monthly and will mature on November 27, 2027. The securities are rated ACUITE A- and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000
- Scrip Code: 977325
- Scrip ID: 1075MML27B
- ISIN: INE884Q07814
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Interest Rate: 10.75% per annum
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Put/Call Option: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE884Q07814
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: November 27, 2027
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds Rs. 50 crore worth of debt securities to BSE's debt segment. Impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing specifically with Midland Microfin Limited's debt instruments. The ACUITE A- rating indicates adequate safety with moderate credit risk. Monthly interest payments provide regular income for investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
