## Summary

BSE has listed 221,223 equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511644) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares will be permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from November 26, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions until June 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 221,223 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 118/-
- Issue price: Rs. 128/- per share
- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE113B01029
- Distinctive Numbers: 2276277 to 2497499

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and must ensure compliance with lock-in restrictions:
- All 221,223 shares (Dist. Nos. 2276277 to 2497499) are locked-in until June 29, 2027
- Shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The shares are issued to promoters and subject to complete lock-in until June 29, 2027, meaning they will not be available for trading in the near term. This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters following warrant conversion.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to be aware of the lock-in restrictions to prevent inadvertent trading attempts. The shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, so no special treatment required beyond lock-in compliance.
