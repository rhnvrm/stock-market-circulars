## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by JSW Paints Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 12, 2025. The issue comprises 330,000 bonds with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 3.65% per annum.

## Key Points

- Issuer: JSW Paints Limited
- Quantity: 330,000 bonds (total value: Rs. 3,300 crores)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per bond
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 977287
- Scrip ID: 365JPL35
- Detail Name: JSWPL-3.65%-10-11-35-PVT
- ISIN: INE754Y08027
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA-/Stable
- Interest Rate: 3.65% per annum (payable annually)
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE754Y08027
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Any clarifications may be obtained from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Listing Date: November 12, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from November 10, 2026 to November 10, 2030
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides JSW Paints Limited with access to debt capital markets for financing requirements. The ICRA AA-/Stable rating indicates high credit quality with adequate degree of safety for timely servicing of financial obligations. The securities are relevant only to debt market investors and institutional participants in the private placement. No put/call options are available on these securities.
