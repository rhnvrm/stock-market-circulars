## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of ICICI PRUDENTIAL ASSET MANAGEMENT COMPANY LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544658 and trading symbol ICICIAMC with ISIN number INE346A01027. The exact date of listing and additional details regarding the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: ICICI PRUDENTIAL ASSET MANAGEMENT COMPANY LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544658
- Trading Symbol: ICICIAMC
- ISIN Number: INE346A01027
- Listing to occur in due course with specific date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems for trading in this security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Details of securities: To be provided in subsequent communication

## Impact Assessment

This listing will provide market participants with access to trade equity shares of ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited on BSE. The impact is moderate as it expands investment opportunities in the asset management sector. Trading members should await the separate notice for the exact listing date and additional security details before commencing trading activities.
