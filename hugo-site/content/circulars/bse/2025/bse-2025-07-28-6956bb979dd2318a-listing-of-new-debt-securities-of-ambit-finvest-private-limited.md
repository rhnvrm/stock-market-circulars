## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Ambit Finvest Private Limited on its debt segment effective July 28, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 8.25% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and maturing on July 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities listed
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.25% per annum
- Credit Rating: ACUTE AA- Stable
- ISIN: INE985V07108
- Scrip Code: 976947
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue based on private placement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from October 25, 2025 to July 25, 2028
- Redemption Date: July 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities provide institutional investors with a medium-term investment option with stable credit rating. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
