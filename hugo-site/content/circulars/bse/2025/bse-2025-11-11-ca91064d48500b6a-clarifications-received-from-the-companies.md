## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from multiple companies regarding significant increase in trading volume of their securities. Three companies - Gallantt Ispat Limited (Scrip Code: 532726), Indigo Paints Limited (Scrip Code: 543258), and National Aluminium Co. Ltd. - responded confirming compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 and stating that volume movements are purely market-driven with no undisclosed price-sensitive information.

## Key Points

- BSE issued surveillance queries to companies regarding significant volume increase under reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/669 and 670
- All three companies confirmed full compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated no undisclosed material information or impending announcements exist
- Volume increase attributed to market conditions and various market factors
- Management of companies stated they are not connected with price/volume movements
- Companies committed to continue timely disclosures as per regulatory requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents company responses to routine surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies must disclose if any material events or price-sensitive information exists
- Continued obligation to make timely disclosures of material events within stipulated timeframes

## Important Dates

- November 11, 2025: BSE issued surveillance queries
- November 11, 2025: Companies submitted clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. The clarifications indicate normal market activity with no material undisclosed information. The volume increases are attributed to regular market forces.

**Investor Impact:** Neutral. Investors are informed that price/volume movements are market-driven and not based on undisclosed corporate developments.

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates active surveillance by BSE and responsive compliance by listed entities in providing timely clarifications on market movements.
