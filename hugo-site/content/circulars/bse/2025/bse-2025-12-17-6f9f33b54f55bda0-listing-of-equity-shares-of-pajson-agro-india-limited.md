## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Pajson Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544657, symbol PAJSON, and ISIN number INE14LM01012. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Pajson Agro India Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544657
- Trading Symbol: PAJSON
- ISIN: INE14LM01012
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds one more company to the BSE SME platform. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip code and symbol. The actual listing date and further details will be provided in a subsequent circular.
