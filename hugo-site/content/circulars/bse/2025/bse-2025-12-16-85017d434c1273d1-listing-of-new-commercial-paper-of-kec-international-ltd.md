## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by KEC International Ltd on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crore) with a 3-month tenure from December 16, 2025 to March 16, 2026. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings and will trade only in dematerialized form on BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: KEC International Ltd
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,397 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730783
- **Scrip ID**: KECIL16325
- **ISIN**: INE389H14LS6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LS6
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 16, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Standard operational procedures apply for BSE Debt segment trading members. The instrument provides institutional investors another short-term investment option from a rated corporate issuer.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong short-term creditworthiness of KEC International Ltd.
