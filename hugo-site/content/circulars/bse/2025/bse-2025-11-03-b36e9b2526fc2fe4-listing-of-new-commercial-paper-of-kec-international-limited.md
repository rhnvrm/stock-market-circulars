## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by KEC International Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 3, 2025. The listing comprises 1,500 securities of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (total value Rs. 75 crore) with a maturity period of approximately 3 months, maturing on January 30, 2026. The instruments carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crore (1,500 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Scrip Code: 730476, Scrip ID: KIL031125
- ISIN: INE389H14LM9
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,643.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- No Issuing and Paying agent specified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LM9
- Maintain standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof for trading
- Apply tick size of 1 paise for price movements
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Notice Date:** November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the BSE Debt segment for short-term fund raising by KEC International Limited. The instrument has high credit ratings (A1+) indicating strong creditworthiness. Trading is restricted to institutional and qualified investors given the high denomination requirement of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
