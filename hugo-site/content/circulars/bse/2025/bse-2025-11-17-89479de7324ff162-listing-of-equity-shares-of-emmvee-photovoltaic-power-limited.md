## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544608, trading symbol EMMVEE, and ISIN number INE1C6T01020. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited's equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544608
- Trading Symbol: EMMVEE
- ISIN Number: INE1C6T01020
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for the new listing
- Await separate notice for exact listing date and additional security details
- Prepare systems for trading once listing date is announced

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine new listing announcement with no immediate trading impact until the listing date is confirmed.

**Sector**: Renewable energy/photovoltaic sector, which may attract investor interest in clean energy companies.

**Trading Members**: Should update their systems with the new scrip details and monitor for the subsequent notice containing the listing date and complete security specifications.
