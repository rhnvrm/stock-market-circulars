## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Timex Group India Limited. The OFS mechanism allows existing shareholders to sell their shares to the public through the stock exchange platform.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale window opened for Timex Group India Limited
- Transaction to be conducted through BSE platform
- Applies to existing shareholders divesting stakes
- Standard OFS procedures and regulations apply

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular follows existing OFS framework under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must comply with standard OFS bidding procedures
- Eligible investors can participate during the specified OFS window
- Settlement to follow standard T+2 cycle as per exchange norms
- Selling shareholders and bidders must adhere to SEBI OFS guidelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: December 26, 2025
- OFS opening date and schedule details available in the full circular document

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The OFS provides liquidity opportunity for investors interested in Timex Group India Limited shares. The impact is company-specific and affects stakeholders of this particular security.

**Operational Impact:** Standard OFS process through BSE platform with established settlement mechanisms.
