## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Sammaan Capital Limited on its debt segment effective August 18, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying 9.60% annual interest rate and maturing on August 14, 2035.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 9,000 securities worth Rs. 90 crores total
- Interest rate: 9.60% per annum paid annually
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Maturity: 10 years (August 14, 2035)
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA, ICRA AA
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following existing BSE debt segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE148I07YD3
- Securities must be traded with minimum tick size of 1 paise
- Members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for additional details
- Clarifications available from BSE debt department

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 18, 2025
- Interest payment: Annually
- Redemption date: August 14, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities add to BSE's debt segment offerings but represent standard corporate financing activity with no broader market implications.
