## Summary

BSE has listed 6,47,449 equity shares of RAMINFO LIMITED (Scrip Code: 530951) issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading with effect from December 30, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 6,47,449 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 130.50
- Issue price: Rs. 140.50 per share
- Securities issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE357B01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 7542466 to 8189914
- Date of Allotment: September 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing procedure for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are permitted to trade on the Exchange. Lock-in requirements apply as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 30, 2025
- **Lock-in Period 1**: 2,47,449 shares (Dist. Nos. 7542466 - 7789914) locked until June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Period 2**: 4,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7789915 - 8189914) locked until June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine preferential allotment listing for a single company. The lock-in periods for all shares will restrict immediate trading activity. Investors should note the differential lock-in periods with approximately 38% of shares locked until June 2026 and 62% locked until June 2027.
