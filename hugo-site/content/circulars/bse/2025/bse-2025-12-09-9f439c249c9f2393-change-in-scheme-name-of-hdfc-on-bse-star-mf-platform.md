## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding changes in scheme names of HDFC Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform. This is an administrative update to reflect the revised nomenclature of HDFC mutual fund schemes.

## Key Points

- Changes in scheme names for HDFC Mutual Fund schemes on BSE StAR MF platform
- Administrative update to maintain accurate scheme information
- No impact on existing investments or operations
- Updated scheme names will be reflected on the BSE platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to scheme nomenclature.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and investors should note the updated scheme names when transacting on the BSE StAR MF platform. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Effective Date:** As per circular notification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is an administrative change with no impact on trading, investments, or fund operations.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Market participants should update their systems and records with the new scheme names for accurate reference and reporting on the BSE StAR MF platform.
