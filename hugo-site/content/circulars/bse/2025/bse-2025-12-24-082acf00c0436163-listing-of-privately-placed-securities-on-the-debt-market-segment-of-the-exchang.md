## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper securities issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its Debt Segment effective December 24, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited: 15,200 units, ISIN INE128M14AY9, 6.65% p.a., redemption on March 6, 2026
- Time Technoplast Limited: 2,000 units, ISIN INE508G14IE9, 7.60% p.a., redemption on March 20, 2026
- SBICAP Securities Limited: 2,000 units, ISIN INE212K14DE1, 6.72% p.a., redemption on March 24, 2026
- SEIL Energy India Limited: 3,000 units, ISIN INE460M14800, 6.20% p.a., redemption on March 24, 2026
- Solar Industries India Limited: 2,000 units, ISIN INE343H14212, 6.28% p.a., redemption on March 23, 2026
- Redington Limited: Details incomplete in circular
- All securities have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form with market lot of 1
- All issuers carry CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+ credit ratings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 23-24, 2025 (varies by issuer)
- **Redemption Dates**: March 6, 2026 to March 24, 2026 (varies by issuer)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact circular affecting only debt market participants. These are routine Commercial Paper listings on private placement basis with short-term maturities (approximately 90 days). No impact on equity markets or broader market operations. Relevant only for institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt instruments.
