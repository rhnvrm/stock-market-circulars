## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading Commercial Paper securities issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The issuance comprises 6000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 6000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per paper
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,59,871.50 per paper
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs.300 crores
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **ISIN**: INE549K14CD0
- **Scrip Code**: 730503
- **Scrip ID**: MFL041125
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE549K14CD0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at BSE

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 3, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 364 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Muthoot Fincorp Limited with short-term funding through the commercial paper route. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper instruments.
