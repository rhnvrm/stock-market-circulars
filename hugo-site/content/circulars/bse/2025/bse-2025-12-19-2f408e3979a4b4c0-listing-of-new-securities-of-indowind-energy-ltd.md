## Summary

BSE has listed 3,22,00,434 new equity shares of Indowind Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532894) issued through a rights issue. The shares are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective from Monday, December 22, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,22,00,434 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5.35/-
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE227G01018
- Distinctive Numbers: 128801737 to 161002170
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Trading commences: December 22, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 22, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for rights issue shares of Indowind Energy Ltd. The listing increases the company's equity base by approximately 3.22 crore shares. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific stock with no broader market implications. The shares being pari-passu means they carry the same rights and privileges as existing shares.
