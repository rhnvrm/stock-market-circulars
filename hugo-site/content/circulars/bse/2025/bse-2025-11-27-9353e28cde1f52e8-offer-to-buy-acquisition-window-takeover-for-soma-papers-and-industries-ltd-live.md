## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD (Symbol: SOMAPPR, Scrip Code: 516038, ISIN: INE737E01011). The offer will be live from Friday, November 28, 2025 to Thursday, December 11, 2025. The offer is for up to 4,26,58,200 equity shares at Rs. 10 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security:** SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD (SOMAPPR)
- **Scrip Code:** 516038
- **ISIN:** INE737E01011
- **Offer Size:** Up to 4,26,58,200 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 10 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Friday, November 28, 2025
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, December 11, 2025
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of investors must be registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a scheduled takeover offer following existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the schedule of activities
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- Access to OTB Module via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- Access to RTRMS (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Extranet access for files (Internet: https://member.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://memberll.bseindia.com/)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This takeover offer affects shareholders of SOMA PAPERS AND INDUSTRIES LTD who may wish to tender their shares at the offer price of Rs. 10 per share.

**Operational Impact:** Trading Members and Custodian Members need to facilitate bidding for their clients during the specified window. The offer size of up to 4.27 crore shares represents a significant acquisition activity.

**Investor Categories Eligible:** FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, NIB, Companies, and Individuals can participate through their Trading Members.

**Contact Support:** BSE has provided detailed contact information for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet support through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams for any technical or operational queries.

**Files for Acceptance:** Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids will be available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.
