## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crores issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument has a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit and will mature on September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 4000 units (Rs. 200 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,98,165.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730163, ISIN: INE212K14CE3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 5, 2025
- Listing Date: September 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 26, 2025
- Maturity Period: 21 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides SBICAP Securities with short-term funding for 21 days. State Bank of India acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.
