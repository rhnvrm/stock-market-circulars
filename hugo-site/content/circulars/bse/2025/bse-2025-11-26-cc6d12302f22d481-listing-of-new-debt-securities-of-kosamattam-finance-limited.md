## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The securities carry an 11% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and a redemption date of May 25, 2031. The securities are traded in dematerialized form only under ISIN INE403Q08332.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977316, Scrip ID: 11KFL31
- ISIN Number: INE403Q08332
- Quantity: 10,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value and Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum paid monthly
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- No put or call options available
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must use the specified ISIN Number INE403Q08332
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for trading

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Interest Payment Start Date:** January 1, 2026
- **Interest Payment Period:** Monthly from January 1, 2026 to May 25, 2031
- **Redemption Date:** May 25, 2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds Rs. 1,000 crore (10,000 units x Rs. 100,000) to the BSE Debt segment. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants and institutional investors interested in A-rated corporate debt
- Investors seeking fixed-income securities with 11% returns
- Trading members dealing in private placement debt securities

The securities are suitable for investors looking for monthly income streams over a 5.5-year investment horizon. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE's debt department at the provided contact numbers.
