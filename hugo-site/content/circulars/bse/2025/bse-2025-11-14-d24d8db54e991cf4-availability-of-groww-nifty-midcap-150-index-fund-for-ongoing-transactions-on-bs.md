## Summary

GROWW NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 17, 2025. The fund will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows the previous notice No. 20251027-42 and includes both Regular and Direct plan options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: November 17, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: 3 Regular Plan options and 3 Direct Plan options
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- All schemes are categorized as EQUITY funds
- Reference to previous notice: 20251027-42

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members should update their systems to enable transactions for the following ISINs:
- INF666M01MS1 (Regular Plan - Growth)
- INF666M01MT9 (Regular Plan - IDCW Payout)
- INF666M01MU7 (Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment)
- INF666M01MP7 (Direct Plan - Growth)
- INF666M01MQ5 (Direct Plan - IDCW Payout)
- INF666M01MR3 (Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions with SIP, STP & SWP facilities
- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Notice Number**: 20251114-46

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine operational update affecting only investors and distributors dealing with Groww mutual fund schemes.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members need to ensure their systems are configured to process transactions for all six scheme variants from November 17, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking exposure to Nifty Midcap 150 index through Groww's fund, as they will regain access to subscription, redemption, and systematic plan facilities.
