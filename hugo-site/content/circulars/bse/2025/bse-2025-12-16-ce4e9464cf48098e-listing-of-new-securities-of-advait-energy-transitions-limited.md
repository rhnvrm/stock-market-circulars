## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,408 new equity shares of Advait Energy Transitions Limited (Scrip Code: 543230) with effect from Wednesday, December 17, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 1,766 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,408 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1,766/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 1,776/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distribution Numbers: 10935370 to 10936777
- Date of allotment: November 13, 2025
- Trading commencement date: December 17, 2025
- ISIN: INE0ALI01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities
- All 1,408 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in until June 29, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing with minimal market impact. The issuance involves a small number of shares (1,408) representing a minor dilution for the company. The shares were allotted at a significant premium (Rs. 1,766 over face value of Rs. 10), indicating positive valuation. The 7-month lock-in period ensures these shares will not immediately impact trading liquidity. This listing is primarily informational for trading members and should not affect market operations or stock price significantly.
