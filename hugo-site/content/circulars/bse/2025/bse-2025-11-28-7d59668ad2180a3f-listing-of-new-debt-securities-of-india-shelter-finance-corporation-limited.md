## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited on a private placement basis effective November 28, 2025. The securities comprise 15,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 150 crore. The debentures carry an interest rate of 7.85% per annum with quarterly interest payments and mature on November 26, 2030. The securities are rated ICRA AA- and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 debentures with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 150 crore
- Scrip Code: 977323 (Scrip ID: 785ISFCL30)
- ISIN: INE922K07112
- Interest Rate: 7.85% per annum (payable quarterly)
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA-
- Allotment Date: November 27, 2025
- Maturity Date: November 26, 2030
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE922K07112
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: February 27, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from February 27, 2026 to November 26, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 26, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine private placement listing with limited impact on broader market operations. The securities are institutional in nature given the high face value of Rs. 1 lakh per debenture.

**Issuer Profile**: India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited is a housing finance company, and these securities represent a standard debt fundraising exercise.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt market investors seeking AA- rated securities with 7.85% annual return over a 5-year tenure. The quarterly interest payment structure provides regular income.

**Liquidity**: Market lot of 1 and dematerialized trading facilitates ease of trading, though private placement securities typically have lower liquidity compared to public issues.
