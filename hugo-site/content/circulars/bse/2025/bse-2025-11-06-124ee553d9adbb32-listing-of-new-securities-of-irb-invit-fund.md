## Summary

IRB InvIT Fund (Scrip Code: 540526) will list 15,96,95,000 new units on BSE from November 7, 2025. The units were allotted on November 3, 2025, at Rs. 62.95 per unit to sponsor and non-sponsor investors on a preferential basis for cash consideration. The new units rank pari-passu with existing units and carry lock-in requirements ranging from November 2026 to November 2028.

## Key Points

- Total new units listed: 15,96,95,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 62.95 per unit
- Allotment type: Preferential basis (cash consideration)
- Allotment date: November 3, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 7, 2025 (Friday)
- ISIN: INE183W23014
- Distribution numbers: 1121905001 to 1281600000
- New units rank pari-passu with old units

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- The new units are permitted for trading from November 7, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specific distribution number ranges
- Units must be traded in accordance with BSE listing requirements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** November 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial - 14,17,40,750 units):** November 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial - 1,79,54,250 units):** November 6, 2028

## Lock-in Details

- **1,79,54,250 units** (Dist. Nos. 1121905001 to 1139859250): Locked-in until November 6, 2028
- **14,17,40,750 units** (Dist. Nos. 1139859251 to 1281600000): Locked-in until November 5, 2026
