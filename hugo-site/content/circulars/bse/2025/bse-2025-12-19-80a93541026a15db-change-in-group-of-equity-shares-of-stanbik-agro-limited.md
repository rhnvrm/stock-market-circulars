## Summary

BSE announces the transfer of STANBIK AGRO LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544659) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling settlement segment (M Group) effective January 05, 2026. This change follows the exchange's previous notice dated December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- STANBIK AGRO LIMITED is an SME IPO listed company
- Scrip Code: 544659
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling settlement (M Group)
- Effective date: Monday, January 05, 2026
- Notice number: 20251219-1
- References prior notice: 20251218-50 dated December 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to Rolling settlement mechanism, allowing for T+2 settlement cycle and improved liquidity compared to the restricted trading environment of MT Group.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment change effective January 05, 2026
- Update their systems to reflect the new M Group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Previous Notice Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 05, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement is generally positive for market participants, as it increases liquidity and allows for intraday trading. This change indicates the stock has met BSE's criteria for graduation from the more restrictive MT Group.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members will experience improved flexibility in executing trades with T+2 settlement instead of immediate delivery requirements, potentially increasing trading volumes and market depth for STANBIK AGRO LIMITED shares.
