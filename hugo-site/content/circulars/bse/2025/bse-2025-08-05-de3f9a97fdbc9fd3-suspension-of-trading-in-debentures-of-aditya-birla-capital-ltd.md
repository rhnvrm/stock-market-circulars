## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific debentures of ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD (Code: ABCL-9.25%-22-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE860H08DN6) effective August 7, 2025, due to the company fixing record date for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD debentures (Code: 957637)
- Record date set for August 7, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from August 7, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-690/2025-2026

No regulatory framework changes. Standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 7, 2025
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension
- Compliance with corporate action timeline
