## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of DSP MSCI India ETF units effective December 1, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited that replicates/tracks the MSCI India Index. A total of 2,129,862 units have been allotted at an issue price of Rs. 30.37 per unit with a face value of Rs. 10.00 per unit.

## Key Points

- **Fund Name**: DSP MSCI India ETF
- **Investment Manager**: DSP Asset Managers Private Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 544620
- **ISIN**: INF740KA1WV7
- **Number of Units**: 2,129,862
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 30.37 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Underlying Index**: MSCI India Index
- **10 Market Makers** appointed including East India Securities Ltd, Kotak Securities Ltd, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in DSP MSCI India ETF units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Market makers must fulfill their obligations for providing liquidity in the ETF

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 1, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the ETF investment options available on BSE for investors seeking exposure to the MSCI India Index. The impact is limited to:

- **Market Participants**: Trading members and investors interested in passive index investment strategies
- **Liquidity**: 10 designated market makers will ensure orderly trading and liquidity
- **Investor Access**: Provides another avenue for investors to gain diversified exposure to Indian equities through a low-cost index-tracking vehicle

The listing follows standard procedures and does not introduce any market-wide operational changes. The ETF offers an additional instrument for portfolio diversification and index-based investment strategies.
