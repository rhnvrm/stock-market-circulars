## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis, effective December 17, 2025. Both securities carry dual AAA ratings from CRISIL and ICRA with STABLE outlook. The first security (NST-Rest Rate-14-12-35-PVT) has 50,000 units with 6.98% p.a. interest rate, while the second (NSL-Reset Rate-13-12-35-PVT) has 20,000 units with floating interest rate. Both securities have a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Nexus Select Trust listed on BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025
- Date of allotment: December 15, 2025
- Both securities rated CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Market lot: 1 unit for both securities
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
