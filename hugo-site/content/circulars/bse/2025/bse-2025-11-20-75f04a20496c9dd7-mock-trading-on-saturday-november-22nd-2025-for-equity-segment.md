## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, November 22nd, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading platforms and applications, including third-party systems, under various market conditions and functionalities. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this mock session. Members are encouraged to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the day of the mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 22nd, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Trading members using third-party platforms can test their applications
- Testing covers various functionalities including call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals
- Comprehensive trading schedule from 11:15 AM to 04:20 PM
- Feedback required from all members by 5:00 PM on the mock trading day

## Trading Schedule

**Login:** 11:15 AM - 11:45 AM

**Morning Block Deal Window:** 11:45 AM - 12:00 PM

**Pre-Open:**
- Order Entry period: 12:00 PM - 12:08 PM (with random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- Matching period: 12:08 PM - 12:15 PM

**Continuous Trading T+1:** 12:15 PM - 04:00 PM

**Continuous Trading T+0:** 12:15 PM - 02:30 PM

**Special Pre-Open for IPO & Re-listed scrips:**
- Order Entry period: 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM (with random stoppage in last 10 minutes)
- Matching period: 12:45 PM - 01:00 PM

**Continuous Trading for SPOS Scrips:** 01:00 PM - 04:00 PM

**Periodic Call Auction:** 12:30 PM - 03:30 PM (3 sessions of 1 hour each)

**Auction for Settlement Details:**
- Offer Entry and Matching period: 01:00 PM - 01:45 PM

**Afternoon Block Deal Window:** 01:50 PM - 02:05 PM

**Closing:** 04:00 PM - 04:10 PM

**Post-closing:** 04:10 PM - 04:20 PM

**Trade Modification T+1:** 04:20 PM

**Trade Modification T+0:** 02:45 PM

## Testing Capabilities

The mock session allows testing of:
- Various types of call auction sessions
- Risk-reduction mode
- Trading halt scenarios
- Block deals functionality
- Exceptional market conditions
- IPO and re-listed scrips trading
- Periodic call auction mechanisms

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members are encouraged to participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback on the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on November 22nd, 2025
- Third-party trading platform vendors and in-house system developers should utilize this opportunity for testing
