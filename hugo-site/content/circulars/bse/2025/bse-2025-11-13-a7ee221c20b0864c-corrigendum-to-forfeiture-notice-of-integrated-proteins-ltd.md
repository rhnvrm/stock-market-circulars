## Summary

BSE has issued a corrigendum (correction) to the forfeiture notice for Integrated Proteins Limited. The notice contains Annexure I listing 198 entries of share distinctive numbers that are subject to forfeiture. The shares listed range from distinctive number 1318001 to 1994101, encompassing various blocks of shares totaling different quantities per entry.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued for share forfeiture notice of Integrated Proteins Ltd
- Contains detailed listing of 198 entries of distinctive share numbers
- Share blocks range from 400 shares to 9,100 shares per entry
- Distinctive numbers range from 1318001 through 1994101
- Total shares subject to forfeiture across all entries listed in annexure

## Regulatory Changes

This is a correction to a previously issued forfeiture notice. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders holding shares with distinctive numbers listed in Annexure I should take note of the forfeiture proceedings
- Affected shareholders may need to clear outstanding dues to prevent forfeiture
- Company must follow SEBI and Companies Act provisions for share forfeiture process

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 13, 2025
- Specific forfeiture deadline dates not mentioned in the provided content

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Shareholders with distinctive numbers listed in the annexure face potential loss of shares if outstanding obligations remain unpaid. The forfeiture affects specific share certificates across 198 separate entries.

**Market Impact:** Limited broader market impact as this affects only specific shareholders of Integrated Proteins Limited who have not met their payment obligations. The corrigendum ensures accurate communication of which shares are subject to forfeiture proceedings.

**Operational Impact:** Company must complete forfeiture process according to legal requirements. Corrected information ensures proper identification of shares to be forfeited.
