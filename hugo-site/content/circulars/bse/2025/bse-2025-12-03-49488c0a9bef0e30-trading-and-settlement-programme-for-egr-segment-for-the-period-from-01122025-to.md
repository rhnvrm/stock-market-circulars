## Summary

BSE has published the comprehensive trading and settlement programme for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment for December 2025. The circular details 23 settlement cycles (DR-174 through DR-195) covering trading dates from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025, along with corresponding auction settlement schedules. Members must adhere to specific pay-in timings (by 2:00 PM for regular settlements, by 8:50 AM for auction settlements) and pay-out will be completed by 4:00 PM.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers December 1-31, 2025 with T+1 settlement cycle
- 23 regular settlement cycles (DR-174/2025-2026 through DR-195/2025-2026)
- 23 corresponding auction settlement cycles (RA-174/2025-2026 through RA-195/2025-2026)
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2526174 to 2526195
- Pay-in deadline: 2:00 PM for regular settlements
- Auction pay-in deadline: 8:50 AM (auction pay-in at 9:00 AM)
- Pay-out time: By 4:00 PM for funds and securities
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by specified deadlines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 2:00 PM for regular settlements
- For auction settlements, members must submit pay-in instructions latest by 8:50 AM
- Members must ensure timely submission to receive pay-out by 4:00 PM
- All settlements follow T+1 cycle with next business day pay-in/pay-out

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **First Settlement**: DR-174/2025-2026 - Trading Date: December 1, 2025, Pay-in/Pay-out: December 2, 2025
- **Last Settlement**: DR-195/2025-2026 - Trading Date: December 31, 2025, Pay-in/Pay-out: January 1, 2026
- **Holiday Adjustments**: Schedule accounts for December 25, 2025 (Christmas) with adjusted settlement dates
- **Daily Pay-in Deadline**: 2:00 PM (regular), 8:50 AM (auction)
- **Daily Pay-out Time**: By 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a standard operational circular providing the settlement calendar for EGR segment participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low - The circular provides necessary scheduling information for members trading in the EGR segment. The T+1 settlement cycle and timing requirements remain consistent with existing practices. Members need to plan their operations according to the published schedule, particularly around year-end dates and the December 25 holiday adjustment.

**Participant Action Required**: Members should incorporate this settlement schedule into their operational calendars and ensure compliance with pay-in/pay-out timings throughout December 2025.
