## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 769 dated December 4, 2025. The auction includes 98 securities from various companies with specified quantities to be auctioned for settlement purposes. This is a routine settlement mechanism for handling delivery shortages in the demat system.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 769
- Settlement Date: December 4, 2025
- Total securities listed: 98 scrips across various sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share to 41,484 shares depending on the security
- Notable large quantity auctions include Srestha Finvest (41,484 shares), Tilak Venture (14,066 shares), and Hikal Ltd (9,692 shares)
- Includes major stocks like ICICI Bank, Infosys, Adani Ports, Biocon, Indian Oil, Tata Power, and Vedanta

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational auction settlement process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanism
- Members should ensure availability of funds and securities for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 4, 2025
- Auction settlement to be completed as per BSE timelines for Settlement No. 769

## Impact Assessment

This auction settlement affects brokers and traders who have delivery shortages in the listed securities. The impact is operational and limited to entities with outstanding obligations in these specific scrips. Large quantity auctions in securities like Srestha Finvest, Tilak Venture, and Hikal may indicate significant delivery failures that need resolution through the auction mechanism. Routine auction settlements ensure market integrity and settlement discipline.
