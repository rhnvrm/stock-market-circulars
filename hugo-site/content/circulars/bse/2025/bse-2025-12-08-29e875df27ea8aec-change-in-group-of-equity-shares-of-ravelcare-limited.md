## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of RAVELCARE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544629), an SME IPO, will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 22, 2025. This follows Exchange Notice No. 20251205-57 dated December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- RAVELCARE LIMITED shares currently trade in Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 22, 2025
- Scrip Code: 544629
- Stock listed under SME IPO category
- Notice issued in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251205-57 dated December 5, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for RAVELCARE LIMITED equity shares will shift from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement basis, allowing for normal intraday trading and T+1 settlement cycle instead of the restrictive Trade for Trade framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Adjust trading and settlement processes for RAVELCARE LIMITED shares
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further clarification if needed
- Ensure readiness before the effective date of December 22, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 22, 2025 (Monday)
- **Previous Related Notice**: December 5, 2025 (Notice No. 20251205-57)

## Impact Assessment

This change is positive for RAVELCARE LIMITED as it moves from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment. The shift indicates improved regulatory standing and will likely enhance liquidity, allow intraday trading, and provide better price discovery. Trading members handling this scrip need to update their operational systems before December 22, 2025.
