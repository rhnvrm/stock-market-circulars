## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by SEIL Energy India Limited on private placement basis effective October 16, 2025. The issue comprises 2000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 494,263, maturing on December 24, 2025. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 2000 Commercial Papers listed with Scrip Code 730393 and Scrip ID SEIL161025
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 494,263 per unit
- ISIN: INE460M14792
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE460M14792
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Tenure:** 69 days (approximately 2.3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for SEIL Energy India Limited and offers institutional investors a highly-rated (A1+) debt investment option with approximately 2-month maturity. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.
