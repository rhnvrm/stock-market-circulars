## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Limited on private placement basis effective November 4, 2025. The instrument comprises 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 Crore, with a 100-day tenure maturing on February 11, 2026. The Commercial Paper carries CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will trade on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 50 Crore (100 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each)
- Scrip Code: 730492 | Scrip ID: AEL031125
- ISIN: INE423A14YM4
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,235.50 (discount of Rs. 9,764.50 per unit)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YM4
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 100 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt listing by Adani Enterprises Limited for working capital requirements. Commercial Paper listings are common market events with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Relevant only to debt market participants and institutional investors who invest in high-rated short-term commercial paper. The CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings indicate highest safety for short-term instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members can contact BSE debt department (022-22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
