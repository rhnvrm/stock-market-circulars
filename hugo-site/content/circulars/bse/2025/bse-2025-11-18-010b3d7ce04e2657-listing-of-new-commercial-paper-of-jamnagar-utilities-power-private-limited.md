## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing consists of 8000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 8000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,558 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730591
- Scrip ID: JUPL181125
- ISIN: INE936D14394
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1
- Trading in demat form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE936D14394
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Notice date: November 18, 2025
- Date of allotment: November 18, 2025
- Listing effective date: November 18, 2025
- Redemption date: February 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument is a short-term debt security with approximately 3-month maturity. Limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
