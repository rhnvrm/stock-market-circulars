## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251) in the Equity Derivative and Currency derivative segments with effect from December 15, 2025. Trading members requiring clarification may contact the Membership Department via email at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251)
- Segments affected: Equity Derivative and Currency Derivative
- Surrender approved by BSE
- Notice issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership Department
- Contact email for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding membership status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the change in membership status. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date of trading membership surrender

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action involving the voluntary surrender of trading membership by a single entity. The impact on overall market operations is minimal as it affects only the specific member's participation in equity derivative and currency derivative segments. Other trading members and market participants are not directly affected.
