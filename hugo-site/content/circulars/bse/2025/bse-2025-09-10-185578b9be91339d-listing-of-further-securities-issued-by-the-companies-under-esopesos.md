## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 15 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These shares will be admitted for trading on the exchange with effect from Thursday, September 11, 2025. All listed shares have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 15 companies issuing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 977,770 shares across all companies being listed
- Trading commences September 11, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the shares
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 across different companies

## Listed Companies and Share Details

- **Brainbees Solutions Limited**: 76,000 shares (₹2 face value)
- **City Union Bank Ltd**: 40,780 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Cyient Limited**: 2,106 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Fedbank Financial Services Limited**: 291,253 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Five-Star Business Finance Ltd**: 19,990 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Godawari Power and Ispat Ltd**: 213,355 shares (₹1 face value)
- **ICICI Prudential Life Insurance**: 11,476 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Intellect Design Arena Limited**: 42,489 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Max Healthcare Institute Limited**: 9,588 shares (₹10 face value)
- **NIIT Ltd**: 83,359 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Quick Heal Technologies Limited**: 56,575 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Star Health and Allied Insurance**: 5,300 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Stove Kraft Limited**: 15,000 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Tejas Networks Limited**: 108,699 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Vardhman Textiles Limited**: 500 shares (₹2 face value)

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: September 11, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Trading Commencement**: September 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The shares represent employee stock option exercises and will increase the free float of the respective companies marginally. No significant price volatility is expected as these are small quantities relative to the companies' total share capital.
