## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in 25 commercial papers issued by various companies effective from November 20-21, 2025. The suspension is due to scheduled redemptions with record dates set between November 20-23, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 25 commercial papers from different issuers are subject to trading suspension
- Redemptions scheduled between November 20-23, 2025
- No dealings permitted from November 20-21, 2025 depending on the security
- Includes major issuers like Bajaj Finance, Reliance Retail Ventures, Axis Finance, and others
- All securities covered under notice DR-760/2025-2026 and DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the listed commercial papers from the specified no-dealings dates
- Members must inform clients holding these securities about the redemption schedule
- Ensure settlement obligations are met before the suspension takes effect

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Record date and no-dealings date for 13 commercial papers (Bajaj Finance, UGRO Capital, IGH Holdings, Julius Baer Capital, Pilani Investment, Capri Global, Godrej Finance, Reliance Retail Ventures, Axis Finance, Fedbank Financial, Dalmia Cement, L&T Metro Rail, Nuvama Clearing Services)
- **November 21, 2025**: Record date and no-dealings date for 9 commercial papers (Nuvama Wealth, Krazybee Services, Navi Finserv, Vivriti Capital, Godrej Finance, SBICAP Securities, Infina Finance, 360 ONE Prime, Fedbank Financial, Tata Power, Manappuram Finance)
- **November 23, 2025**: Record date for 2 commercial papers (Navi Finserv, Fedbank Financial) with no-dealings from November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects debt market liquidity for these specific instruments but is a routine corporate action. Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as scheduled.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these securities and communicate with affected clients. The short notice period (3-4 days) requires prompt action.

**Issuer Coverage**: Diverse range of issuers including financial services companies (Bajaj Finance, Axis Finance, UGRO Capital), NBFCs (Manappuram Finance, Fedbank Financial), retail (Reliance Retail Ventures), infrastructure (L&T Metro Rail, Dalmia Cement), and fintech companies (Krazybee Services, Navi Finserv).
