## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Ltd. This corporate action updates the official company name registered with the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company name change notification for Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Ltd
- Formal announcement through BSE regulatory framework
- Updates to company records and trading symbols may follow

## Regulatory Changes

The circular notifies market participants of the approved change in the company's registered name as per regulatory requirements under SEBI and Companies Act provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the new company name in their records
- Trading members to update client databases with the new company name
- Investors should be aware of the name change for corporate correspondence

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 18, 2025
- Effective date of name change to be confirmed as per regulatory approval

## Impact Assessment

This name change represents a corporate identity update that will affect how the company is identified in market systems, investor communications, and regulatory filings. While it does not impact the company's business operations or shareholding pattern, investors and market participants should update their records accordingly. Trading in the company's securities will continue under the existing scrip code until any symbol changes are announced separately.
