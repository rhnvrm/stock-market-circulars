## Summary

BSE has announced the prohibition/suspension of trading in securities of LS Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 514446) effective December 9, 2025. This action has been taken as an interim measure in the interest of investors and the securities market.

## Key Points

- Trading in LS Industries Ltd securities is prohibited/suspended
- Scrip Code: 514446
- Effective Date: December 9, 2025
- Action taken as an interim measure
- Measure implemented to protect investor interests and market integrity

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is an interim trading suspension order specific to LS Industries Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in LS Industries Ltd securities from December 9, 2025
- Trading members and investors are required to take note of this suspension
- No buy or sell orders will be accepted for scrip code 514446

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete halt of trading activity in the security prevents price discovery and liquidity.

**Investor Impact:** High - Existing shareholders cannot exit positions and new investors cannot enter. All pending orders will not be executed.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block orders for scrip code 514446 and inform clients holding or interested in this security about the suspension.
