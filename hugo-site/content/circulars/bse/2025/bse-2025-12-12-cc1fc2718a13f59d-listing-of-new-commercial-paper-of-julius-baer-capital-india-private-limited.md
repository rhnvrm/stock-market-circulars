## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited on December 12, 2025. The total issuance comprises 4,000 units (3,000 + 1,000) of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, amounting to Rs. 200 crores. The securities are issued on a private placement basis and will be traded in dematerialized form under ISIN INE824H14RO8.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 4,000 units (issued in two tranches of 3,000 and 1,000 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,869.50 per unit (discount of approximately 1.62%)
- Scrip Code: 728983
- Scrip ID: JBCIP13325
- ISIN: INE824H14RO8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Tenure:** 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing with short-term maturity. The instrument is designed for institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to debt market investors seeking short-term, highly-rated instruments. The A1+ rating from both CRISIL and ICRA indicates the highest degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.

**Liquidity Impact:** Trading restricted to dematerialized form with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs, indicating this is institutional-grade paper with limited retail participation.

**Issuer Profile:** Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited is raising short-term funds through commercial paper at a competitive discount rate in the money market.
