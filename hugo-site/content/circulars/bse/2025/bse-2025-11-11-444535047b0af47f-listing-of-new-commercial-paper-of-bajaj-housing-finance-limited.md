## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. The instrument comprises 10,000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 492,546 per unit.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Housing Finance Limited
- **Securities**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 10,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 492,546 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730007
- **Scrip ID**: BHFL11825
- **Detail Name**: BHFL-09-02-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE377Y14BP3 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Central Bank of India
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE377Y14BP3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Bajaj Housing Finance Limited with a tenure of approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing expands debt market offerings but has no significant impact on broader market operations or trading practices.
