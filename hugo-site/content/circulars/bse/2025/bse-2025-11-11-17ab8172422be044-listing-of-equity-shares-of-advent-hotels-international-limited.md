## Summary

BSE has listed a total of 53,942,887 equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited. The shares are distributed across multiple tranches with varying lock-in periods, including free shares and locked-in shares with expiry dates ranging from May 2026 to November 2026.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares listed: 53,942,887
- Distribution across 10 different tranches with varying securities ranges
- Largest tranche: 28,512,109 shares (free from lock-in)
- Second largest tranche: 16,760,778 shares (free from lock-in)
- Multiple lock-in periods with expiry dates on 30-05-2026, 30-10-2026, and 05-11-2026
- Some tranches are immediately tradeable (marked as "Free")

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements outlined. This is a standard listing notification.

## Important Dates

- **30-05-2026**: Lock-in expiry for shares in ranges 31,960,110-31,960,109 and 37,182,110-37,182,109
- **30-10-2026**: Lock-in expiry for shares in ranges 28,512,110-30,520,109 and 35,742,110-36,742,109
- **05-11-2026**: Lock-in expiry for shares in range 33,597,110-35,742,109

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing provides liquidity for existing shareholders while maintaining lock-in provisions for certain tranches to ensure stability. Approximately 45.3 million shares (84% of total) are either free or will become tradeable by November 2026. The staggered lock-in expiry dates help prevent sudden supply shocks to the market.
