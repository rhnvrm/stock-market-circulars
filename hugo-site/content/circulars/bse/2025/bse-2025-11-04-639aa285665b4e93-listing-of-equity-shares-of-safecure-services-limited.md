## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Safecure Services Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544596, symbol SAFECURE, and ISIN number INE0SVZ01015. The specific date of listing and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Safecure Services Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544596
- Trading Symbol: SAFECURE
- ISIN Number: INE0SVZ01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date notification.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 04, 2025
- **Listing Date:** To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds one more security to the SME segment. Trading members and investors interested in SME stocks should await the separate notice with the actual listing date and complete security details before any trading activity can commence.
