## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544636, symbol WOSAL, and ISIN number INE1MF401019. This listing will occur on the SME segment of BSE. The specific listing date and further details about the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited
- Scrip Code: 544636
- Trading Symbol: WOSAL
- ISIN Number: INE1MF401019
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Separate notice will provide exact listing date and security details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems to handle trading in this new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing will add one more security to the SME segment of BSE. Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip code and symbol for when trading commences. Investors interested in SME stocks should watch for the follow-up notice containing the actual listing date and additional security details.
