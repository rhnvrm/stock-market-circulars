## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ghushine Fintrrade Ocean Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539864) will be included under the 'M' group effective October 27, 2025. This reclassification affects the trading segment and conditions applicable to this security.

## Key Points

- Company: Ghushine Fintrrade Ocean Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 539864
- New Group Classification: 'M' group
- Effective Date: October 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251020-9
- Category: Company related - Equity Segment

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved from their current group to the 'M' group. The 'M' group typically indicates securities under special surveillance or with specific trading restrictions, which may include higher margin requirements, limitations on price movements, or enhanced monitoring.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the group change effective October 27, 2025
- Adjust their trading systems and risk management parameters accordingly
- Inform clients about the change in group classification
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** Movement to 'M' group may result in reduced liquidity, stricter trading conditions, and potentially higher margin requirements for positions in this scrip. Investors and trading members should be aware of the changed trading parameters that will apply from the effective date.

**Market Operations:** Trading members need to update their systems and communicate the changes to their clients who hold or trade in this security.

**Contact Information:** Yogendra Daxini (Deputy Vice President) and Manish Raval (Manager, Listing Compliance) have issued this notice.
