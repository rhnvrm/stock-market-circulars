## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Axis Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 5, 2025. The issue comprises 5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 250 crores, with a maturity period of 8 days from allotment to redemption.

- **Issuer**: Axis Finance Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)

- **Scrip Code**: 730225
- **Scrip ID**: AFL12925
- **Detail Name**: AFL-12-12-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE891K14PN7 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891K14PN7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine short-term commercial paper listing for corporate treasury management purposes. The 8-day tenure indicates ultra-short-term funding requirements. The A1+ rating from both CRISIL and India Ratings reflects highest credit quality for short-term instruments. Trading members in debt segment can access this instrument for short-term investment strategies.
