## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND schemes for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR Mutual Fund Platform. A total of 10 schemes are now available, comprising both Direct and Regular plans across various IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options including Growth, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Quarterly reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- 10 ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND schemes are now available for transactions
- Includes 5 Direct Plan variants and 5 Regular Plan variants
- All schemes fall under the LIQUID category
- Options include Growth and IDCW reinvestment (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly)
- Each scheme has a unique ISIN for identification
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform for ongoing transactions

## Scheme Details

### Direct Plans
1. Growth (INF2JJD01029)
2. Daily IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01011)
3. Monthly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01037)
4. Quarterly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01052)
5. Weekly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01045)

### Regular Plans
1. Growth (INF2JJD01078)
2. Daily IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01060)
3. Monthly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01086)
4. Quarterly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01102)
5. Weekly IDCW Reinvestment (INF2JJD01094)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants and investors can now transact in these schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 12, 2025
- Schemes available for ongoing transactions effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational announcement. It expands investment options for mutual fund investors on the BSE platform by adding ABAKKUS liquid fund schemes. Liquid funds are typically used for short-term cash management and provide an alternative to bank savings. The availability benefits investors seeking liquid fund exposure through the BSE StAR MF Platform and enhances product diversity on the exchange.
