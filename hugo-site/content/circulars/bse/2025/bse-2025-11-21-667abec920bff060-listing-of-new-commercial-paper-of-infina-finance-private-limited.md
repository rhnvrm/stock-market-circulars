## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on the BSE Debt segment. The instrument comprises 2,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities are allotted on November 20, 2025, and will be redeemed on May 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 2,000 units (Rs. 100 crore total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,84,005 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730614
- Scrip ID: IFPL201125
- ISIN: INE879F14MA6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MA6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730614

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: May 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides an additional short-term commercial paper instrument for debt market participants. The A1+ credit ratings from both CRISIL and ICRA indicate high safety and liquidity. The 168-day tenor (approximately 5.5 months) offers a short-term investment option for institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt securities. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
