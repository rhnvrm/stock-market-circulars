## Summary

BSE has issued a circular detailing the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Mahamaya Lifesciences Limited. The circular provides information about the anchor investor bidding process, allocation details, and lock-in requirements.

## Key Points

- Public issue of Mahamaya Lifesciences Limited includes anchor investor portion
- Anchor investor bidding completed on specified date
- Allocation made to eligible anchor investors as per SEBI guidelines
- Lock-in period applicable as per SEBI regulations
- Details of allocation shared with market participants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular implements existing SEBI guidelines for anchor investor participation in public issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations
- Trading members to note allocation details for client accounts
- Depository participants to update records with lock-in details

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 10, 2025
- Anchor Investor Bidding Date: As mentioned in circular
- Lock-in Period: As per SEBI regulations (typically 30-90 days)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact on the market, specifically affecting:
- Investors participating in the Mahamaya Lifesciences Limited IPO
- Trading members handling anchor investor accounts
- Market participants tracking new listings

The anchor investor allocation provides price discovery and indicates institutional interest in the issue. The lock-in period ensures stability during the initial trading period.
