## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544636, symbol WOSAL, and ISIN number INE1MF401019. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited
- Scrip Code: 544636
- Symbol: WOSAL
- ISIN: INE1MF401019
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new listing and await further notification regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. Impact is limited to market participants interested in the SME segment. The listing will add one more counter to the BSE SME platform. No immediate market-wide implications.
