## Summary

BSE has listed 2,598,400 new equity shares of Hemant Surgical Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543916) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade from November 7, 2025. The shares were allotted at Rs. 197/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with premium of Rs. 187/-) on September 18, 2025, and are subject to lock-in periods extending through May 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,598,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 187/-
- Issue price: Rs. 197/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 10440001 to 13038400
- Date of allotment: September 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 7, 2025
- ISIN: INE0GYI01028
- Scrip Code: 543916

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing and lock-in restrictions:
- 1,049,600 shares (Dist. Nos. 10440001-11489600) locked-in until May 31, 2026
- 1,548,800 shares (Dist. Nos. 11489601-13038400) locked-in until May 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Batch 1)**: May 31, 2026 (1,049,600 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Batch 2)**: May 30, 2026 (1,548,800 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The lock-in periods ensure that the newly issued shares cannot be traded immediately, protecting against sudden supply increases. The preferential issue to non-promoters increases the public float of Hemant Surgical Industries Limited. Trading members should be aware of the lock-in restrictions when processing orders for this scrip.
