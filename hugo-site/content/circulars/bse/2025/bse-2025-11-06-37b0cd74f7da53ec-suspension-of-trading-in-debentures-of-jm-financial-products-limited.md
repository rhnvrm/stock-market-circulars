## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of JM Financial Products Limited (ISIN: INE523H07BX5, Code: 974922) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is due to partial redemption through investor-requested buyback and interest payment with a record date of November 8, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to JM Financial Products Limited 8.8% debentures maturing December 23, 2025
- ISIN: INE523H07BX5, BSE Code: 974922
- No dealings permitted from November 7, 2025
- Record date set for November 8, 2025
- Dual purpose: Partial redemption via buyback at investor request and interest payment
- Notice reference: DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealings directive

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **November 7, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **November 8, 2025**: Record date for partial redemption and interest payment
- **December 23, 2025**: Original maturity date of debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debt instrument. The suspension prevents trading activity until the corporate action is completed.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment based on November 8 record date. Liquidity temporarily unavailable due to trading halt.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this security and communicate the suspension to relevant clients and desk operators.
