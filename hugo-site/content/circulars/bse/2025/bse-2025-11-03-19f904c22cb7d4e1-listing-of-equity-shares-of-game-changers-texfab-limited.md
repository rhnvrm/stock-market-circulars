## Summary

BSE has listed 17,892,814 equity shares of GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED. The listing includes a combination of locked-in securities with varying lock-in periods ending between October 2025 and November 2028, anchor investor shares, and 4,480,800 free shares from the fresh IPO.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares listed: 17,892,814
- Free shares under fresh IPO: 4,480,800 (Demat)
- Anchor investor allotments: 447,600 shares (lock-in till 04.12.2025) and 447,600 shares (lock-in till 04.02.2026)
- Multiple tranches with lock-in periods ranging from October 2025 to November 2028
- Securities listed in both physical and demat form
- Distinctive numbers range: 1 to 17,892,814

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - this is a standard listing circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Promoters and locked-in shareholders must comply with the specified lock-in periods
- Lock-in periods vary by tranche: earliest ending 16.10.2025, latest ending 20.11.2028
- Anchor investors subject to lock-in until 04.12.2025 and 04.02.2026 respectively
- Securities must be maintained in specified form (Physical/Demat) as indicated

## Important Dates

- **16.10.2025**: Lock-in expiry for multiple tranches (30,815 + 52 + 74 shares in physical/demat)
- **17.10.2025**: Lock-in expiry for 73 shares (Demat)
- **31.10.2025**: Lock-in expiry for 103,057 shares (Demat)
- **20.11.2026**: Lock-in expiry date for several tranches
- **20.11.2027**: Lock-in expiry for one tranche
- **20.11.2028**: Lock-in expiry for multiple tranches
- **04.12.2025**: Anchor investor lock-in expiry (447,600 shares)
- **04.02.2026**: Anchor investor lock-in expiry (447,600 shares)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing adds liquidity to BSE with approximately 4.48 million freely tradable shares from the IPO, while the remaining shares are under lock-in. The staggered lock-in release schedule will gradually increase free float over the next 3 years.

**Investor Impact**: Investors can immediately trade the 4,480,800 free IPO shares. Anchor investors face a 30-60 day lock-in period. Promoter/pre-IPO shareholders have extended lock-in periods ensuring stability.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing process with no special trading restrictions beyond lock-in compliance.
