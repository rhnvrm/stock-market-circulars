## Summary

BSE has notified that BAJAJ FINSERV BANKING AND FINANCIAL SERVICES FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facility on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 03, 2025. The scheme offers six plan variants covering Regular and Direct plans with different dividend and growth options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from December 03, 2025
- Available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions
- SIP, STP and SWP facilities enabled
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All variants are equity category schemes
- Trading available on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members and investors can transact in the fund through BSE StAR MF Platform starting December 03, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions
- **December 02, 2025**: Notice date
- **Reference Notice**: 20251107-15 (previous notice regarding this fund)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. It provides investment opportunity for investors interested in the banking and financial services sector through this equity mutual fund scheme. The availability of multiple plan options (Regular/Direct with various dividend and growth options) provides flexibility to different investor segments. The notice ensures transparency and timely communication to BSE members and market participants about fund availability.
