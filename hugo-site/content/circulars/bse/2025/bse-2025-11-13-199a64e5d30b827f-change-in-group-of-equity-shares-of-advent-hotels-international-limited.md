## Summary

BSE has announced that trading in the equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip Code: 544446) will be permitted under Group 'B' with effect from Thursday, November 27, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Advent Hotels International Limited
- Scrip Code: 544446
- New Trading Group: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251113-1
- Notice Date: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited will be reclassified to trade under Group 'B' on BSE. This change in group classification may be due to the company meeting specific criteria or regulatory requirements for Group 'B' classification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in trading group for Advent Hotels International Limited
- All trades in the scrip must be executed under Group 'B' classification from November 27, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems and records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (Thursday) - Trading under Group 'B' commences

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group 'B' may affect the trading characteristics of Advent Hotels International Limited's equity shares. Group 'B' typically has different trading rules, settlement procedures, or eligibility criteria compared to other groups. Trading members and investors should be aware of this change to ensure smooth trading operations.
