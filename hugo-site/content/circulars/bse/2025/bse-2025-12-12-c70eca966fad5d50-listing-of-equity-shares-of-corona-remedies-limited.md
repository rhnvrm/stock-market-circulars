## Summary

Corona Remedies Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) for equity shares post-issue dated December 12, 2025. The total shareholding comprises 61,160,088 equity shares with promoter holding at 68.9959% (42,197,968 shares held by 6 promoters) and public holding at 31.0041% (18,962,120 shares held by 159,539 public shareholders). The company has a dematerialization rate of 92.9051% with 56,820,841 shares held in demat form.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 61,160,088
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 42,197,968 shares (68.9959%)
- Public shareholders: 159,539 shareholders holding 18,962,120 shares (31.0041%)
- Dematerialized shares: 56,820,841 (92.9051% of total)
- Promoter shares fully dematerialized: 42,197,968 (100%)
- Public shares in demat: 14,622,873 (77.1162%)
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares in locked-in status
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered
- No depository receipts issued
- No differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners declared

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard compliance filing under existing SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 and Listing Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Shareholding pattern disclosure required for post-issue listing
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or encumbrances
- Pattern filed as per SCRR, 1957 calculation methodology

## Important Dates

- **Post-issue date:** December 12, 2025
- **Filing date:** December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine post-listing shareholding disclosure with standard promoter-public shareholding distribution showing promoter holding within normal ranges.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - High dematerialization rate (92.9%) indicates good market infrastructure readiness. Clean shareholding structure with no encumbrances, pledges, or locked-in shares provides transparency to investors.

**Operational Impact:** None - Standard compliance filing with no operational changes or special conditions affecting trading or ownership transfer of shares.
