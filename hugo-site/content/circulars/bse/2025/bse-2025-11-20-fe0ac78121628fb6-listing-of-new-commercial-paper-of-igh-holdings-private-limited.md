## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 20, 2025. The listing comprises 6000 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 300 crore, maturing in 90 days on February 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6000 commercial papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,695 per paper
- Scrip Code: 730599
- Scrip ID: IGHH191125
- ISIN: INE02FN14671 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14671
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: November 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instruments are rated A1+ by both CARE and CRISIL, indicating highest safety for short-term debt. The listing provides institutional investors with an additional investment avenue in high-quality commercial paper with a 90-day tenure. Trading will be restricted to dematerialized form with standard debt market conventions.
