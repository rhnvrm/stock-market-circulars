## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 01, 2025. The debentures (ISIN: INE551U07316, Scrip Code: 975979) are scheduled to mature on December 02, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture: SFPL-11.37%-2-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE551U07316
- Scrip Code: 975979
- Maturity Date: December 02, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: December 01, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-767/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 01, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new positions should be created in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 01, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 02, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension for maturing private debentures with limited trading activity.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members holding these debentures should prepare for redemption on maturity. No trading will be permitted from December 01, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance on the maturity date.
