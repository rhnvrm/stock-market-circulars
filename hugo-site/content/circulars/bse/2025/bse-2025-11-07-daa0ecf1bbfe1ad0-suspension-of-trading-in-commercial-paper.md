## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers of 14 companies effective from their respective record dates fixed for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified "No Dealings From" dates, which range from November 11 to November 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- 14 commercial paper instruments will be suspended from trading
- Suspensions are due to companies fixing record dates for redemption
- First suspension begins November 11, 2025 (Can Fin Homes Ltd. and Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited)
- Last suspension begins November 17, 2025 (8 companies)
- All suspensions align with respective record dates
- Notice number: 20251107-10, dated November 07, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the "No Dealings From" dates
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect trading suspensions
- Members must comply with record date requirements for redemption processing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects debt market participants holding or trading these commercial papers. The staggered suspension dates minimize concentrated market disruption.

**Issuer Profile:** Includes major financial institutions (Bajaj Finance, Kotak Mahindra entities, ICICI Home Finance, Standard Chartered Capital) and corporates (Sterlite Technologies, Reliance group entities).

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update trading systems and communicate with clients holding these instruments. Investors should be aware of the upcoming redemptions and plan accordingly for reinvestment of proceeds.
