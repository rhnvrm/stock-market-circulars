## Summary

BSE has published an update regarding the debt securities of Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd (formerly known as Tata Motors Limited). This circular contains the shareholding pattern filed by Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing is signed by Company Secretary Sudipto Kumar Das.

## Key Points

- Listed Entity Name: Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Filing under: Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Total number of shareholders: 6,647,799
- Total shares held: 3,682,331,373 equity shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 1,567,369,812 shares (42.56%)
- Public shareholding: 2,114,961,561 shares (57.44%)
- The company has granted ESOPs with 2,666,563 shares underlying outstanding convertible securities
- Total shares on fully diluted basis: 3,684,997,936
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares
- Tata Sons Private Limited (TSPL), being a core investment company registered with RBI, is exempted under SBO Rules

## Regulatory Changes

This filing reflects the corporate restructuring where:
- TML Commercial Vehicles Limited has been renamed to Tata Motors Limited
- The original Tata Motors Limited has been renamed to Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd

The shareholding pattern disclosure is required under Regulation 31(1) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 to maintain transparency in shareholding structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities must file shareholding patterns under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration required on various securities types (partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, non-disposal undertakings)
- Disclosure of significant beneficial owners as per SBO Rules
- Separate reporting for Promoter & Promoter Group, Public shareholders, and Non Promoter-Non Public categories

## Important Dates

Filing Date: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This is a routine regulatory disclosure following corporate restructuring. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding at 42.56% and healthy public float at 57.44%, which meets regulatory requirements.

**Debt Security Holders:** Debt security holders of the erstwhile Tata Motors Limited (now Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd) need to note the name change and updated corporate structure for tracking their investments.

**Investor Confidence:** The transparent disclosure of shareholding pattern and the presence of Tata Sons as the promoter provides stability and confidence to investors and debt holders.

**Outstanding Securities:** The presence of 2,666,563 shares underlying outstanding ESOPs represents potential dilution of approximately 0.07% on the current equity base.
