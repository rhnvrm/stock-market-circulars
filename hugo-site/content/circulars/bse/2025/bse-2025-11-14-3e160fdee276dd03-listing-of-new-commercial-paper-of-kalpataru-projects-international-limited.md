## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kalpataru Projects International Limited (KPIL) on private placement basis with effect from November 14, 2025. The instrument comprises 3,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 150 crores, with a credit rating of IND A1+. The Commercial Paper will be traded in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Kalpataru Projects International Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 3,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crores
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,519 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730563
- **Scrip ID**: KPIL131125
- **ISIN**: INE220B14BO6
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE220B14BO6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must comply with the tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments with a tenure of approximately 90 days.

**Investor Impact**: Provides debt market participants with an additional short-term investment option rated IND A1+, indicating high credit quality and strong capacity for timely repayment.

**Issuer Impact**: Enables Kalpataru Projects International Limited to raise short-term funds of Rs. 150 crores through the commercial paper route for working capital requirements.
