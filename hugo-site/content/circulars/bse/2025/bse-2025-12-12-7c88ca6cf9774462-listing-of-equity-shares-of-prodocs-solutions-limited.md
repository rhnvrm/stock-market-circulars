## Summary

BSE has published the shareholding pattern for Prodocs Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: PRODOCS) filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) post-issue without PAN details. The company has a total of 7,050,000 fully paid-up equity shares with promoter group holding 51.77% and public shareholders holding 48.23%. The pattern shows 434 total shareholders with no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, or share encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 7,050,000 (100% fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 3,650,000 shares (51.77%) held by 6 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 3,400,000 shares (48.23%) held by 428 shareholders
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities, warrants, or outstanding ESOPs
- No shares held against depository receipts
- No locked-in shares
- No pledged or encumbered shares
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners declared
- All shares in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure requirement under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR
- Declaration submitted confirming no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or encumbrances
- Post-issue shareholding disclosure without PAN details

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 12, 2025
- Filing Type: Post-Issue shareholding pattern

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine post-listing compliance disclosure for a newly listed company.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard shareholding pattern reporting with clean capital structure. The 51.77% promoter holding provides controlling interest while meeting minimum public shareholding requirements of 25%. No encumbrances or complex securities indicate straightforward equity structure suitable for a newly listed entity.
