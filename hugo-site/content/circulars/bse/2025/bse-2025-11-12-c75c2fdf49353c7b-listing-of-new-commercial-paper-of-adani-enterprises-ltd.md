## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis on the debt segment effective November 12, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,64,684 and maturing on November 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 500 commercial papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,64,684
- **Scrip Code**: 730536
- **Scrip ID**: AEL111125
- **ISIN**: INE423A14YN2
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YN2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment that provides short-term funding to Adani Enterprises Ltd. The instrument has strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA), indicating highest safety for short-term debt. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No effect on equity markets or retail investors.
