## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper securities issued by 360 One Wam Limited on private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. Two tranches of Commercial Paper have been listed on the BSE Debt segment with face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, with credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+. HDFC Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two tranches of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- First tranche: 3,000 units (Scrip Code: 730605, ISIN: INE466L14FF3) redeeming on February 18, 2026
- Second tranche: 100 units (Scrip Code: 730593, ISIN: INE466L14FD8, further listing under same ISIN) redeeming on February 17, 2026
- Both tranches allotted on November 19, 2025
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue prices: Rs. 491,279/- (first tranche) and Rs. 491,516.50/- (second tranche)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd is the Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **November 19, 2025**: Allotment date for both tranches
- **November 20, 2025**: Effective listing date on BSE Debt segment
- **February 17, 2026**: Redemption date for second tranche (Scrip Code: 730593)
- **February 18, 2026**: Redemption date for first tranche (Scrip Code: 730605)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds short-term Commercial Paper instruments (approximately 90-day tenure) to the BSE Debt segment. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in highly-rated short-term debt instruments from 360 One Wam Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
