## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Access Point India Limited for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days for public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed on September 09, 2025
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Contains all required disclosures under ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Public comments invited within 21 days
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This follows standard ICDR process for SME IPO documentation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from notice date
- All feedback to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 08, 2025
- Draft Document Filed: September 09, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from September 08, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a standard procedural notice for a single SME IPO. Provides transparency in the IPO process by allowing public scrutiny of the offer document before finalization.
