## Summary

IRIS Business Services Limited has officially changed its name to IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited with effect from November 28, 2025. The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Central Processing Centre has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to the name change under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The company's CIN (L72900MH2000PLC128943) remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: IRIS Business Services Limited
- **New Name**: IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited
- **Effective Date**: November 28, 2025
- **CIN**: L72900MH2000PLC128943 (unchanged)
- **Original Name**: Company was originally incorporated as IRISBUSINESS.COM (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Registered Office**: T-231, Tower 2, 3rd Floor, International Infotech Park, Vashi Station, Vashi, Thane-400703, Maharashtra, India
- **Certificate Issued By**: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- **Company Secretary**: Santoshkumar Sharma (ICSI Membership No. ACS 35139)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action involving name change only.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (IRIS Business Services Limited) along with the new name (IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Effective date of name change (Certificate of Incorporation issued)
- **November 29, 2025**: Date of company secretary's digital signature on the circular
- **December 10, 2025**: BSE circular publication date
- **Two-year period**: Company must display both old and new names (from November 28, 2025 to November 27, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a cosmetic change that does not affect the company's business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative updates required for company stationery, documents, and branding materials. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory filings and corporate identity.

**Investor Impact**: No financial impact on investors. All existing shares, rights, and obligations remain unchanged. Investors should note the new name for future correspondence and document references.

**Compliance Impact**: The company must ensure dual-name display for two years across all statutory locations and documents as mandated by Section 12 of the Companies Act.
