## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 16 companies. The new shares will be available for trading from Thursday, November 27, 2025. The total includes 1,783,952 shares across major companies including ICICI Bank (623,293 shares), ICICI Prudential Life Insurance (286,880 shares), Ujjivan Small Finance Bank (327,767 shares), and Wipro (108,098 shares). None of the newly listed shares are under lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 16 companies listing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 1,783,952 new shares being listed
- Effective trading date: November 27, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable on any of the listed securities
- Major listings include ICICI Bank (623,293 shares), ICICI Prudential (286,880 shares), and Ujjivan SFB (327,767 shares)
- PG Electroplast has the highest number at 613,000 shares
- All shares have been allotted under respective company ESOP/ESOS programs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities for trading purposes
- Companies have complied with listing requirements for ESOP/ESOS issuances
- No additional compliance actions required from market participants

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: Not applicable (NA for all securities)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing represents routine ESOP/ESOS conversions with relatively small percentages of existing share capital for most companies. The absence of lock-in periods indicates these shares are immediately available for trading.

**Liquidity Impact**: Marginal increase in floating stock for the 16 companies. The largest absolute additions are for ICICI Bank, Federal Bank, ICICI Prudential, and Ujjivan Small Finance Bank.

**Investor Impact**: No material impact on existing shareholders. ESOP/ESOS issuances are standard corporate actions for employee compensation and typically well-anticipated by the market.
