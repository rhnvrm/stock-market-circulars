## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis effective December 18, 2025. The listing comprises 4000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each on the BSE Debt segment. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on March 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4000 Commercial Papers of Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Scrip Code: 730807
- Scrip ID: OPL171225
- Detail Name: 360OPL-18-3-26-CP
- ISIN Number: INE248U14SR4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs.500,000.00/-
- Issue Price: Rs.491,219.00/-
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U14SR4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the specified tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 18, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: December 17, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal impact on market operations. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications. No significant operational or compliance burden imposed on trading members.
