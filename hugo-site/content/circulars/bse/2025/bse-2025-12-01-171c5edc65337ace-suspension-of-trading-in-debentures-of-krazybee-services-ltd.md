## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07742, Code: 975750) of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD from December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- Debenture Series: KSL-10.20%-19-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE07HK07742
- BSE Code: 975750
- Record Date: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: December 4, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD from December 4, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No new trades should be executed in ISIN INE07HK07742 from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects specific debenture series only. Debt market participants holding or trading this particular NCD will be unable to trade from December 4, 2025.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and principal redemption based on holdings as of the record date. The suspension ensures proper settlement of the corporate action.

**Operational Impact:** Trading platforms and members must update systems to block trading in this ISIN from December 4, 2025. Standard corporate action procedure with limited operational complexity.
