## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills effective December 31, 2025, as they are scheduled to mature on January 2, 2026. Trading Members are advised not to deal with these T-Bills from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading effective December 31, 2025
- Scrip Code 800354 (ISIN: IN000126C010) - GS02JAN26C
- Scrip Code 805124 (ISIN: IN002025X273) - 91TB020126
- Both securities mature on January 2, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine suspension procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the mentioned T-Bills from December 31, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in these securities

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 31, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 2, 2026**: Redemption/Maturity date for both T-Bills

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard procedure for maturing Treasury Bills. Only affects holders and potential traders of these two specific securities. The suspension ensures orderly market operations ahead of the redemption date. No broader debt market implications expected.
