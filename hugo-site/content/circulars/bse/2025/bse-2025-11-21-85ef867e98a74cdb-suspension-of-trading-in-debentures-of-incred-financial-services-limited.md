## Summary

BSE has notified that trading in specific debentures of INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED will be suspended from November 27, 2025. The suspension is in effect due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture: IFSL-9.50%-12-12-25-PVT (ISIN: INE321N07327, Code: 975081)
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action-related trading suspension pursuant to existing BSE regulations for debt securities undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures starting November 27, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No dealings are permitted in the affected ISIN from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

This suspension directly impacts holders and traders of INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED debentures. The no-dealings restriction is critical for ensuring proper settlement of the redemption and interest payment process. Investors holding these debentures should note they cannot trade them from the suspension date. The impact is significant for debt market participants who hold or were planning to transact in these securities.
