## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 4000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling Rs.200 crore. Trading commenced on November 24, 2025 on the BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730628. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4000 Commercial Papers of Rs.5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730628
- Scrip ID: GFL241125
- ISIN Number: INE02KN14630
- Issue Price: Rs.491,664.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14630
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing with no significant impact on broader market operations. The listing adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members are informed of new securities available for trading. Standard trading protocols apply with dematerialized form requirement and specified lot sizes.

**Entity Impact**: Relevant only for Godrej Finance Limited and investors/traders interested in highly-rated (A1+) short-term debt instruments with 91-day maturity.
