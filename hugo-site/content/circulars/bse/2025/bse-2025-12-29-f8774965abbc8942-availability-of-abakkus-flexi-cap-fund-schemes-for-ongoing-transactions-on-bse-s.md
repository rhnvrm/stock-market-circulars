## Summary

BSE has announced that ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND will be reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 30, 2025. This follows previous notice 20251205-22 and allows investors to resume regular transactions in six schemes under the ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND.

## Key Points

- ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 30, 2025
- Six equity schemes available: 3 Regular Plans and 3 Direct Plans
- Each plan offers Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- All schemes fall under the EQUITY category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of trading activities for mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND schemes from December 30, 2025
- Ensure that subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP functionalities are operational for all six schemes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 30, 2025 - Resumption of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a mutual fund operational update affecting only ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium. Existing and potential investors in ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND can now resume regular subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities starting December 30, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Members and distributors need to ensure their systems are updated to facilitate transactions in these schemes from the effective date.
