## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 760 dated November 21, 2025. This circular lists 89 different securities scheduled for demat auction to fulfill settlement obligations. The auction includes securities from various sectors including banking, automobiles, IT, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing with quantities ranging from 1 share to 25,500 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 760
- Date: November 21, 2025
- Total number of securities listed: 89 scrips
- Highest quantity: South Indian Bank (532218) - 25,500 shares
- Auction includes major stocks like HDFC Bank, Infosys, Tata Motors, Coal India, Hero MotoCorp
- Covers diverse sectors: banking, IT, auto, pharma, manufacturing, media, real estate

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine demat auction circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled for the specified quantities
- Members should ensure availability of securities for delivery or participate in auction to close out positions

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 21, 2025 (Settlement No. 760)
- Auction execution date: To be conducted as per BSE auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 89 different securities across multiple sectors. While the quantities are generally small for most scrips, some securities like South Indian Bank (25,500 shares), ZEE Learn (16,259 shares), and Danube Industries (5,000 shares) have significant quantities.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members with settlement shortages in these securities must arrange for shares or participate in the auction. Price discovery in auction may be at premium to market prices.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Investors holding these securities may see temporary price volatility during auction process. Members with obligations need to fulfill settlement requirements.
