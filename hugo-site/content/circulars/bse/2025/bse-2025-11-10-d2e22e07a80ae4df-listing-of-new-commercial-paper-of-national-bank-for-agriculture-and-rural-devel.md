## Summary

National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective November 10, 2025. The listing comprises 11,000 Commercial Papers issued on a private placement basis, each with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000. The instruments carry credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, with Axis Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 11,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,618.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730524
- **Scrip ID**: NBAR071125
- **ISIN**: INE261F14ON6
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE261F14ON6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper listing provides NABARD with short-term funding and offers institutional investors a highly-rated debt instrument with approximately 3-month maturity. Trading is restricted to the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or retail investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
