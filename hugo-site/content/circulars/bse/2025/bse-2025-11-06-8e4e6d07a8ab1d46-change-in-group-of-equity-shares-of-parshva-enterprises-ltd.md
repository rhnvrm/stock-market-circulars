## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 542694) will be transferred from the 'T' Group to the 'B' Group effective November 19, 2025. This change follows Exchange Notice No. 20251031-64 dated October 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD
- Scrip Code: 542694
- Transfer: From 'T' Group to 'B' Group
- Effective Date: November 19, 2025
- Notice issued in continuation of earlier notice dated October 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The security is being moved from the 'T' Group (Trade-to-Trade segment with additional surveillance) to the 'B' Group (regular trading segment). This indicates the company has met the criteria for normal trading operations and reduced surveillance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the group change
- Adjustments to trading systems and processes may be required before November 19, 2025
- Normal trading protocols applicable to 'B' Group securities will apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 06, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Reference Notice:** October 31, 2025 (Notice No. 20251031-64)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from 'T' Group to 'B' Group is positive for the security as it indicates improved compliance and governance standards. This change will:

- Allow normal intraday trading (previously restricted in T Group)
- Remove trade-to-trade settlement restrictions
- Potentially improve liquidity and trading volumes
- Reduce transaction costs for investors
- Signal improved market confidence in the company
