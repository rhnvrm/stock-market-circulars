## Summary

BSE has notified that the TITANIUM HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND scheme has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions, including Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective from December 17, 2025. This follows the previous notice No. 20251121-68. Six plan variants are available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- TITANIUM HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND reopened for ongoing transactions from December 17, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform for subscription and redemption
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Six scheme variants available: 3 Regular Plan options and 3 Direct Plan options
- All variants classified as HYBRID (NC) type funds
- Each variant has unique ISIN numbers for identification

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification about the reopening of a mutual fund scheme for trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Members and investors can now transact in the reopened scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 - Scheme reopening for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a scheme-specific operational update affecting only investors interested in the TITANIUM HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors of this fund, as they can now make ongoing investments and redemptions through various modes including SIP and SWP.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can now process transactions for all six variants of the TITANIUM HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND scheme.

## Scheme Details

The following six variants are available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF277K30013)
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF277K30039)
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF277K30021)
4. **Direct Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF277K30047)
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF277K30062)
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF277K30054)
