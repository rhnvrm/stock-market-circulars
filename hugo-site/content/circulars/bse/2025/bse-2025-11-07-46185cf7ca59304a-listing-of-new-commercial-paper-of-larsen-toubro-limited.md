## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Larsen & Toubro Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 30,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, effective November 7, 2025. The securities will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with a redemption date of February 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 30,000 Commercial Papers issued
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,650.50 per security
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730516
- **Scrip ID**: LTL071125
- **ISIN Number**: INE018A14LS6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: State Bank of India
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE018A14LS6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing for a large, established corporate entity. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and IND, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members can now trade these securities on the debt segment following standard procedures. The listing expands available short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and provides Larsen & Toubro with short-term funding at competitive rates.

**Investor Impact**: The high credit rating and short tenure make these securities suitable for conservative institutional investors seeking short-term debt exposure to a blue-chip infrastructure company.
