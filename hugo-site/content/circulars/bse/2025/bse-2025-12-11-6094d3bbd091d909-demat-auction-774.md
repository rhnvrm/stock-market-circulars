## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 774 dated December 11, 2025. The auction includes 89 scrips from various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 432,980 shares. This auction is conducted to settle shortages arising from failed delivery obligations in the demat settlement system.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 774
- Auction Date: December 11, 2025
- Total Scrips Listed: 89 securities
- Quantity Range: From 1 share (multiple scrips) to 432,980 shares (Magellanic Cloud - scrip code 538891)
- Largest quantities include: Magellanic Cloud (432,980 shares), Captain Pipe (150,000 shares), SVS Ventures (12,000 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including power, infrastructure, chemicals, technology, housing finance, and consumer goods
- Mix of large-cap and small-cap securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular. This is a routine operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the auction
- Affected parties must settle their obligations through the auction mechanism
- Members must ensure adequate margins and funds for auction participation
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE settlement calendar and auction procedures

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: December 11, 2025
- Settlement No.: 774

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 89 scrips across various market segments. While routine in nature, large quantities in certain scrips (like Magellanic Cloud with 432,980 shares) could see price volatility during auction.

**Operational Impact**: Affects brokers and trading members with failed delivery obligations who must participate in the auction to close out positions. Clients with demat shortages may face additional costs if securities are purchased at auction premium.

**Liquidity Impact**: Temporary impact on specific scrips during auction hours. Small-cap and illiquid stocks may experience higher price discovery challenges during auction process.
