## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 100 crore) with CRISIL A1+ rating. The Commercial Paper was allotted on December 18, 2025 and will mature on December 16, 2026. IndusInd Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Units: 2,000
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,59,973 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730812
- Scrip ID: MFL181225
- ISIN: INE549K14CH1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CH1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 19, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** December 16, 2026
- **Tenor:** Approximately 363 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets. Commercial paper listings are regular occurrences in the debt market.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations, making it suitable for short-term debt portfolios.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to note the specific trading parameters (Rs. 5 lakh denomination, 1 paise tick size) for this instrument when facilitating client trades in the debt segment.
