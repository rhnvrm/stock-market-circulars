## Summary

Edelweiss BSE Sensex ETF, an open-ended exchange traded scheme managed by Edelweiss Asset Management Limited, will be listed and admitted to trading on BSE in the B Group effective November 27, 2025. The ETF replicates/tracks the BSE Sensex Total Return Index with 1,839,028 units issued at Rs. 85.63 per unit, allotted on November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544618, ISIN: INF754K01VY8
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name: ESENSEX
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 85.63 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Total Units: 1,839,028
- Trading: Dematerialized form only as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market Makers: Parvati Capital and Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt. Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard ETF listing following existing SEBI guidelines requiring units to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be in unit market lot (i.e., one unit)
- Market makers appointed: Parvati Capital and Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt. Ltd

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Circular Date**: November 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine ETF listing expanding investment options for market participants seeking passive exposure to BSE Sensex Total Return Index.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members can trade the ETF in single unit lots in dematerialized form through the BOLT system.

**Investor Impact**: Provides retail and institutional investors another avenue for passive index investing with the convenience of exchange trading.
