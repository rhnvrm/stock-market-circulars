## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in T-bill with ISIN IN002024Z321 (364TB211125, Scrip Code 804991) effective November 19, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with ISIN IN002024Z321 (364TB211125) to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 804991
- Suspension effective date: November 19, 2025
- Reason: Instrument maturing on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-759/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-bill (ISIN IN002024Z321) from November 19, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members must ensure clients are informed about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **November 18, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: T-bill maturity date (specific date not mentioned in notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single T-bill instrument approaching maturity. Standard procedure for debt securities redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects holders and traders of this specific T-bill. Trading members need to update systems and inform relevant clients. No broader market implications as this is part of normal debt instrument lifecycle management.
