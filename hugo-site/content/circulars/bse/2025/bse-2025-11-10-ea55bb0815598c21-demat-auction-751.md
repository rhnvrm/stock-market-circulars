## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 751 dated November 10, 2025. The auction covers 122 scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 33,500 shares. This auction is conducted to cover delivery shortfalls where sellers failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 751
- Auction Date: November 10, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 122 securities
- Quantities range from 1 share to 33,500 shares
- Largest quantities: WARDWIZARD (33,500 shares), STANDARD CAP (25,000 shares), IDEA (22,000 shares), SAGILITY (15,433 shares)
- Mix of large-cap, mid-cap, and small-cap stocks affected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine market operation process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery positions must participate in the auction or arrange for securities
- Buyers who did not receive delivery will receive securities through the auction process
- Settlement will be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 10, 2025 (Settlement No. 751)
- Auction Date: To be conducted as per BSE schedule for settlement 751

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects 122 securities across multiple sectors. While this is a routine process, the variety of stocks and quantities involved indicates delivery failures across the market.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members with short positions must arrange funds or securities for the auction. Buyers awaiting delivery will receive securities through auction mechanism.

**Notable High-Volume Auctions**:
- WARDWIZARD I: 33,500 shares
- STANDARD CAP: 25,000 shares
- VODAFONE IDE: 22,000 shares
- SAGILITY: 15,433 shares
- AERPACE: 6,462 shares

The auction ensures market integrity by completing failed deliveries, though it may result in higher costs for members who failed to deliver.
