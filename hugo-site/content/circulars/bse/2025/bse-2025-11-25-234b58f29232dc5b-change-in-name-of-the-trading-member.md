## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 6593) to RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED, effective from Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This is an administrative change requiring no action from other trading members.

## Key Points

- Trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. will change its name to RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member number 6593 remains unchanged
- Name change is effective from November 26, 2025
- Trading members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative notification of a trading member's name change.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. This is an informational circular for awareness purposes only.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, November 26, 2025 - Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The trading member's membership number (6593) remains the same, ensuring continuity in all systems and records. Other trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new name but no operational changes are required. The name change does not affect any trading operations, market access, or business relationships.
