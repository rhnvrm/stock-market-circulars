## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251104-5 announcing the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730472, ISIN: INE01C314DC5) effective November 06, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its maturity/redemption date of November 07, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730472
- ISIN: INE01C314DC5
- Instrument identified as BFSL-07-11-25-CP
- Suspension effective from November 06, 2025
- Reference: DR-750/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational measure for debt securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE01C314DC5) from November 06, 2025
- Members should ensure no new trades are executed in this instrument after the effective date
- Existing positions should be managed in accordance with maturity procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 07, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument. Only holders of this specific ISIN will be impacted.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members holding or tracking this commercial paper need to update their systems and ensure no further trades are executed. The suspension is procedural due to approaching maturity, which is standard practice in debt markets.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms on the maturity date. No trading opportunity exists after November 05, 2025.
