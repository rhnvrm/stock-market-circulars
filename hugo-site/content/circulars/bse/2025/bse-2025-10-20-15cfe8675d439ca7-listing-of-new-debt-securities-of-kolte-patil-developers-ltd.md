## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective October 20, 2025. The issuance comprises 13,996 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 139.96 crore. The securities carry a CARE AA-/Stable credit rating and have a 10-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 13,996 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 139.96 crore
- Scrip Code: 977231
- Scrip ID: KPDL161025
- ISIN: INE094I07080
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuer: Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd
- Placement Type: Private placement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE094I07080
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for detailed terms
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** October 15, 2035
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Not Applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine debt securities listing on private placement basis with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only for institutional investors who participated in the private placement. No put/call options are available, indicating limited liquidity features for early exit.
