## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis effective November 26, 2025. The instrument comprises 15,000 units with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 750 crore, with 91-day maturity period and credit rating of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

- **Issuer**: Poonawalla Fincorp Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 750 crore (15,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,881.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730653
- **Scrip ID**: PFL251125
- **ISIN**: INE511C14ZC0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE511C14ZC0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Transactions must adhere to tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term investment opportunity for debt market participants. The dual A1+ rating from CARE and CRISIL indicates high credit quality and safety for investors. The listing expands debt market investment options but does not affect broader market operations or equity trading.
