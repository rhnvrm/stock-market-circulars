## Summary

BSE has announced that NECTAR LIFESCIENCES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532649) has fixed December 24, 2025 as the Record Date for determining shareholder entitlement and eligibility to participate in the company's buyback offer. The ex-entitlement basis date is also December 24, 2025 under settlement number DR-784/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: NECTAR LIFESCIENCES LIMITED (BSE Code: 532649)
- Record Date: December 24, 2025
- Purpose: Determine entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible for buyback participation
- Ex-Entitlement Date: December 24, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-784/2025-2026
- Security Type: Dematerialised Securities under Rolling Settlement Segment
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification for a specific company's buyback process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Only shareholders holding shares as of the record date (December 24, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade on ex-entitlement basis from December 24, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-784/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects only shareholders of NECTAR LIFESCIENCES LIMITED who wish to participate in the buyback offer.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding equity shares as of December 24, 2025 will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback. Those purchasing shares on or after the ex-entitlement date will not be eligible.

**Trading Impact**: Shares will trade ex-entitlement from December 24, 2025, which may affect pricing as new buyers will not have buyback participation rights.
