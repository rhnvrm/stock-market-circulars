## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper securities issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis, effective August 25, 2025. Both securities have face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue 1: 4000 units, scrip code 730082, redemption June 9, 2026
- Issue 2: 1000 units, scrip code 730083, redemption March 13, 2026
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security for both issues
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ for both securities
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited is the issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: August 25, 2025
- Allotment date for both issues: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date (Issue 1): June 9, 2026
- Redemption date (Issue 2): March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide additional debt investment options for institutional investors in the BSE debt segment.
