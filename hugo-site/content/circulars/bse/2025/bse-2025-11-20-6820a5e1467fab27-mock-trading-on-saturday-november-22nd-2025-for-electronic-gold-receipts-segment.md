## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 22nd, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will simulate various market functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. No actual trades, margin obligations, or settlement obligations will result from this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 22nd, 2025 for EGR segment
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Trading session runs from 11:15 AM (login) to 4:20 PM (post-closing)
- Includes all standard trading phases: pre-open, continuous trading, block deals, and closing sessions
- No margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or creation of rights and liabilities
- Feedback requested from all participants by 5:00 PM on the same day
- Support available through BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500) and Tech Support (022-22728053)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a routine mock trading session announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Feedback submission requested by 5:00 PM on November 22nd, 2025 via contact numbers or email (bse.tech@bseindia.com, bsehelp@bseindia.com)
- Members should contact their Relationship Manager or BSE support teams for any clarifications

## Important Dates

**November 22nd, 2025 - Mock Trading Schedule:**
- 11:15 AM - 12:00 PM: Log-in period
- 12:00 PM - 12:08 PM: Pre-Open Order Entry period
- 12:08 PM - 12:15 PM: Matching period
- 12:15 PM - 4:00 PM: Continuous Trading
- 1:00 PM - 1:15 PM: Block Deal Window
- 4:00 PM - 4:10 PM: Closing
- 4:10 PM - 4:20 PM: Post-closing
- By 5:00 PM: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a mock trading session with no real financial impact.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Provides testing opportunity for trading members to validate their systems and familiarize with platform functionalities. Particularly useful for members to test exceptional market condition scenarios like risk-reduction mode and trading halts in a controlled environment without financial consequences.

**Member Benefits:** Trading members can use this session to ensure their trading applications are functioning correctly and prepare for actual market conditions, especially those related to the Electronic Gold Receipts segment.
