## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has issued the demat auction list for Settlement No. 756 dated November 17, 2025. The auction involves 92 securities across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 300,000 shares. This is a standard settlement mechanism used when physical securities need to be procured for delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 756
- Date: November 17, 2025
- Total securities involved: 92 scrips
- Quantity range: 1 to 300,000 shares
- Highest quantity: Murae Organi (542724) - 300,000 shares
- Other significant quantities: HARSHIL AGRO (79,145 shares), VODAFONE IDEA (11,000 shares), KHOOBSURAT L (7,405 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement participants must ensure availability of securities or arrange for procurement through the auction mechanism
- Compliance with standard BSE auction settlement procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 17, 2025
- Auction conducted as per Settlement No. 756 schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 92 securities across multiple sectors and market capitalizations. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of delivery obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Members with short positions in the listed securities will need to participate in the auction or face potential penalties. The wide range of quantities (from 1 share to 300,000 shares) suggests varying degrees of settlement failures across different scrips.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary price pressure may occur on securities with larger auction quantities, particularly Murae Organi, HARSHIL AGRO, VODAFONE IDEA, and KHOOBSURAT L, as members procure shares to meet delivery obligations.
