## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Stable Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6829) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Debt and OBPP (Odd Lot, Book Closure Period, and Post-IPO) segments effective November 27, 2025. This is an administrative closure with no immediate market-wide implications.

## Key Points

- Stable Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6829) is voluntarily closing operations
- Closure applies to Debt and OBPP segments only
- Effective date: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member-specific operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required for other trading members. This is an informational notice regarding one member's voluntary exit.

## Important Dates

- **November 26, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of business closure for Stable Broking Private Limited in Debt and OBPP segments

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice affects only the operations of a single trading member (Stable Broking Private Limited) in specific segments. Other market participants and trading activities remain unaffected. The voluntary nature of the closure indicates an orderly exit without regulatory concerns.
