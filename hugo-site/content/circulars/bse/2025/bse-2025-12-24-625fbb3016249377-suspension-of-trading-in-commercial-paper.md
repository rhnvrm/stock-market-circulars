## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 729918, ISIN: INE180C14557) issued by Capri Global Capital Ltd effective December 26, 2025. The suspension follows the company's decision to undertake early redemption (buyback) of the instrument. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper.

## Key Points

- Company: Capri Global Capital Ltd
- Scrip Code: 729918
- ISIN: INE180C14557
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (CGCL-29-7-26-CP)
- Action: Trading suspension due to early redemption/buyback
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- DR Reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for suspension of trading in debt instruments undergoing early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from December 26, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in this instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The suspension affects a single commercial paper instrument of Capri Global Capital Ltd.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of this commercial paper will undergo early redemption process. No trading opportunity available from December 26, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure the scrip is blocked from trading in their systems and clients are informed about the suspension.
