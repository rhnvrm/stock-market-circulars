## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 5,223,295 new equity shares of Delphi World Money Limited (Scrip Code: 533452) issued through a rights issue. The shares are issued at face value of Rs. 10 with a premium of Rs. 181 per share (total issue price Rs. 191 per share). Trading in these new securities will commence on November 13, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Delphi World Money Limited
- Scrip Code: 533452
- Number of shares: 5,223,295 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Premium: Rs. 181 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 191 per share
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE726L01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 11564358 to 16787652
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Trading commencement date: November 13, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes from November 13, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: Date of allotment of rights issue shares
- **November 12, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 13, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The rights issue increases the equity capital base of Delphi World Money Limited by approximately 5.22 million shares. The new shares will have the same rights and privileges as existing shares. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip with no broader market implications.
