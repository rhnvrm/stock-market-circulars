## Summary

BSE issued a revised notice regarding the part redemption of four debenture series of EDELWEISS ASSET RECONSTRUCTION COMPANY LIMITED. This notice follows an earlier exchange notice dated June 12, 2025, which had set the record date as June 17, 2025. The revised notice confirms that the post-redemption face value for all four affected debenture series is Rs. 1,000 per debenture.

## Key Points

- This is a continuation/revision of Exchange Notice No. 20250612-18 dated June 12, 2025
- Record date for part redemption was June 17, 2025
- Four debenture series of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited are affected
- All four series have the same post-redemption face value of Rs. 1,000 per debenture
- All debentures are private placement instruments

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This notice affects only holders of specific private placement debentures of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited. As these are non-convertible debentures with limited public trading, the market-wide impact is minimal.

**Operational Impact**: Low - The part redemption has already occurred (record date was June 17, 2025), and this is a revised informational notice confirming the post-redemption face values. Trading members need only note the updated face values for their records.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for affected debenture holders - While the partial redemption affects the outstanding principal amount for these specific debenture holders, all series maintain uniform post-redemption face values of Rs. 1,000 per debenture, providing clarity for position valuation.
