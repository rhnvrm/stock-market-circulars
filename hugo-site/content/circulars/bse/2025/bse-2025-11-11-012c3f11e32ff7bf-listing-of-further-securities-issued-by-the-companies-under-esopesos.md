## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of additional equity shares issued by nine companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). The shares will be available for trading from Wednesday, November 12, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in period and can be traded immediately upon listing.

## Key Points

- 9 companies listing additional equity shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 1,443,861 new shares being listed across all companies
- Effective date: November 12, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Companies span banking, technology, real estate, fashion, and industrial sectors
- Largest listing: Newgen Software Technologies Limited with 639,000 shares

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine ESOP share listings representing employee compensation conversions. The quantities are relatively small compared to the existing equity base of these companies.

**Liquidity Impact:** Marginal increase in tradable float for each company. The largest addition is Newgen Software with 639,000 shares, followed by V-Guard Industries with 345,853 shares.

**Investor Impact:** No immediate impact on existing shareholders. These shares dilute existing equity marginally but represent standard employee compensation practices.

**Trading Impact:** All shares are immediately tradable with no lock-in restrictions, allowing employees to liquidate positions if desired.
