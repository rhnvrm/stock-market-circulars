## Summary

HDFC Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (total 4000 units worth Rs. 20 crores) on BSE Debt segment effective July 30, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with HDFC Bank Ltd as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 4000 units (total value Rs. 20 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729928, Scrip ID: HSL30725
- ISIN: INE700G14PF4
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,280/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under ISIN INE700G14PF4

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited and additional debt investment opportunity for institutional investors. Trading will be conducted through standard BSE debt segment procedures.
