## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 24,812,000 (2.48 crore) new equity shares of Healthy Life Agritec Limited (Scrip Code: 543546) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, November 6, 2025. The shares were allotted on November 3, 2025, at par value of Rs. 10/- each and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Healthy Life Agritec Limited
- Scrip Code: 543546
- Number of shares: 24,812,000 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: At par (Rs. 10/-)
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE0L3501015
- Distinctive Numbers: 24812001 to 49624000
- Date of Allotment: November 3, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 6, 2025 (Thursday)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- The new shares are available for trading from November 6, 2025
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply to these securities

## Important Dates

- **November 3, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **November 4, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 6, 2025**: Trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide impact. The rights issue increases the total equity base of Healthy Life Agritec Limited by 2.48 crore shares. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their holdings reflected in their demat accounts. The listing may result in increased liquidity for the stock, though the overall market impact is minimal as this affects only one company. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the expanded share capital and new distinctive number ranges.
