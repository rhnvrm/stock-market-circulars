## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 778 dated December 17, 2025. The auction includes 96 scrips from various companies across different sectors, with quantities ranging from as low as 1 share to as high as 10,000,000 shares for Mangalam Industries.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 778
- Settlement Date: December 17, 2025
- Total scrips included: 96
- Highest quantity: Mangalam Industries (537800) - 10,000,000 shares
- Notable inclusions: Large quantities in Hindustan Copper (50,000 shares), Shish Industries (15,298 shares), Supertech EV (4,800 shares)
- Includes major companies like Infosys (3 shares), Trent (9 shares), Ashok Leyland (50 shares), Vedanta (22 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must participate in the demat auction settlement process
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per the auction schedule
- Parties involved in failed deliveries must settle their obligations through this auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 778

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 96 different scrips across various sectors. While most quantities are relatively small, some scrips have significant volumes (Mangalam Industries with 10 million shares, Hindustan Copper with 50,000 shares).

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers and clearing members with failed deliveries in these scrips must participate in the auction process to fulfill their settlement obligations.

**Investor Impact:** The auction mechanism ensures price discovery and settlement for failed deliveries, protecting investor interests and maintaining market integrity. Shareholders of the listed companies may experience temporary price volatility during the auction process.
