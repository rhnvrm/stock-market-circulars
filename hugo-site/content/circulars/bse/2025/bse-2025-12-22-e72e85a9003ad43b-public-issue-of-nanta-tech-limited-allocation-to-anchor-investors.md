## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors for the public issue of NANTA TECH LIMITED. This circular provides details about the anchor investor allocation process and results prior to the opening of the public issue.

## Key Points

- NANTA TECH LIMITED has completed the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors
- Anchor investor allocation occurs before the public issue opens
- The allocation details include number of shares allocated and price at which shares were allocated
- This is part of the standard IPO process for companies listing on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a notification of a specific corporate action.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 22, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed prior to public issue opening

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact for investors specifically interested in the NANTA TECH LIMITED IPO. Anchor investor participation often provides insight into institutional interest in a public issue. The allocation to anchor investors typically occurs at the issue price and represents a portion of the total shares on offer. This information is relevant for retail and institutional investors considering participation in the public issue when it opens for subscription.
