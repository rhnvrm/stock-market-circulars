## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper instruments issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. Two tranches totaling 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores) are now admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. Both instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- First tranche: 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 100 crores) under ISIN INE212K14DC5, maturing March 20, 2026
- Second tranche: 8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 400 crores) under ISIN INE212K14DB7 (further listing), maturing March 18, 2026
- Issue prices: Rs. 4,91,761 and Rs. 4,91,850 respectively against face value of Rs. 5,00,000
- State Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Tranche 1)**: December 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Tranche 2)**: December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 1)**: March 20, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 2)**: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of debt instruments on private placement basis. The listing provides institutional investors access to short-term debt instruments of SBICAP Securities Limited with approximately 90-day tenure. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
