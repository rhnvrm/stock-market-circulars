## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, November 22nd, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will allow participants to test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, November 22nd, 2025
- Applicable to Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment only
- Testing opportunity for third-party trading platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs
- Will simulate various market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Trades from mock session will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations or rights and liabilities
- Feedback requested from all members by 5:00 pm on the same day

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- All members must provide feedback for the mock trading session by 5:00 PM on November 22nd, 2025
- Members should contact their respective Relationship Manager or BSE support teams for any queries

## Important Dates

- **November 22nd, 2025**: Mock trading session for EGR segment
- **November 22nd, 2025 by 5:00 PM**: Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

This is a testing and familiarization exercise with no impact on actual market operations. The mock trading session creates no real obligations, rights, or liabilities for participants. It serves purely as an opportunity for trading members to verify their systems are functioning correctly and to familiarize themselves with various market scenarios in the Electronic Gold Receipts segment.
