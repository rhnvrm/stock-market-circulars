## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Jio Credit Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 1, 2025. The instrument comprises 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 500 crore, with a 91-day tenure maturing on February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 730675
- Scrip ID: JCL281125
- ISIN Number: INE282H14071
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,087/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new debt instrument listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE282H14071
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 1, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Listing Date: December 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE debt department at specified numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
