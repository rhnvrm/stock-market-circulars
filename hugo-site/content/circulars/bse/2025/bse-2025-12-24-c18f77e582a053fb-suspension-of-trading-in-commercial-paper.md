## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper (Scrip Code: 729918, ISIN: INE180C14557) issued by Capri Global Capital Ltd effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company's proposed early redemption (buyback) of the commercial paper instrument CGCL-29-7-26-CP.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified commercial paper from December 26, 2025
- Suspension applies to Scrip Code 729918 (Capri Global Capital Ltd)
- ISIN: INE180C14557
- Instrument: CGCL-29-7-26-CP
- Reason: Early redemption/buyback proposed by the issuer
- Reference: DR-785/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure implemented when an issuer proposes early redemption of debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealing in the specified commercial paper from December 26, 2025
- No fresh trades should be executed in Scrip Code 729918
- Members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific commercial paper instrument. Investors holding the instrument will need to await early redemption proceeds from Capri Global Capital Ltd.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to prevent trading in the suspended instrument and inform clients holding positions in this commercial paper about the suspension and forthcoming early redemption.
