## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 779 dated December 18, 2025. The auction covers 87 scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 280,000 shares (Meesho Limited being the highest at 283,122 shares).

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 779
- Auction Date: December 18, 2025
- Total Scrips Listed: 87 securities
- Highest Quantity: Meesho Limited (544632) - 283,122 shares
- Second Highest: Mangalam Industries (537800) - 89,634 shares
- Multiple scrips with single share quantities
- Covers diverse sectors including technology, finance, manufacturing, and infrastructure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine market operation circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction procedures
- Members must ensure availability of funds for auction purchases
- Delivery obligations for the specified quantities must be met

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 18, 2025
- Auction Settlement Number: 779

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 87 scrips with varying liquidity levels. High-volume auctions in Meesho Limited and Mangalam Industries may cause price volatility.

**Operational Impact:** Members with delivery failures in these scrips face mandatory auction participation and potential penalties. The auction mechanism ensures delivery completion and maintains market integrity.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of affected companies may experience temporary price movements during auction settlements. Members with outstanding obligations must arrange funds and securities promptly.
