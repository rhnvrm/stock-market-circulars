## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of MUTHOOT FINCORP LIMITED worth Rs. 150 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 3000 units (Total value: Rs. 150 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729931
- Scrip ID: MFL290725
- ISIN: INE549K14CB4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,65,450 per unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: INDUSIND BANK LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Muthoot Fincorp and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
