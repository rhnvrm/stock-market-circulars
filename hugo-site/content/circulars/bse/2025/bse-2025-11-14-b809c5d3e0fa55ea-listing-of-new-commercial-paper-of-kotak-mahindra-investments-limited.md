## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 14, 2025. The issue comprises 9000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, maturing on November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 9000 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,99,371 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730571
- **Scrip ID**: KMIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE975F14C41
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Listed on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The 7-day tenure indicates this is a very short-term debt instrument for meeting working capital requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+) reflect strong creditworthiness. Trading members can contact BSE debt department for clarifications.
