## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SHREE BALAJI (MALA) TEXTILES LIMITED on its website for public comments. The document was filed on September 29, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document will remain available for at least 21 days for public review and comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SHREE BALAJI (MALA) TEXTILES LIMITED on September 29, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
