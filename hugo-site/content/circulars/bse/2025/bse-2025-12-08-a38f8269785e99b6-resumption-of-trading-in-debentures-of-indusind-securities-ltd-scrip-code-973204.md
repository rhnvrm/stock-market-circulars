## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD (Scrip Code 973204) effective December 09, 2025. This follows the partial exercise of put option by debenture holders after the record date of October 23, 2025 set for payment of interest and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD (Scrip Code 973204) will resume from December 09, 2025
- Put option was partially exercised by debenture holders
- Previous record date for interest payment and redemption was October 23, 2025
- Reference to previous Exchange Notice No. 20251017-10 dated October 17, 2025
- Circular reference: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notification regarding resumption of trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the resumption of trading in the specified debentures
- Trading will be available for the debentures from December 09, 2025 onwards

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Original exchange notice issued
- **October 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 08, 2025**: Resumption notice issued
- **December 09, 2025**: Trading resumes in the debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only specific debenture instrument of INDUSIND SECURITIES LTD

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine resumption of trading after corporate action completion

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing debenture holders who can now trade the instrument; partial exercise of put option indicates some holders opted for redemption while others retained their holdings
