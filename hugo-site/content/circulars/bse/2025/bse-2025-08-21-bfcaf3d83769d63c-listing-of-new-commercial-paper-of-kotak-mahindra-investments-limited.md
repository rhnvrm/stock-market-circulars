## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited on private placement basis, effective August 21, 2025. The issue comprises 12,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 600 crores with a maturity period of 7 days.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 600 crores (12,000 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,99,411 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Maturity: August 28, 2025 (7-day commercial paper)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with standard terms. The 7-day maturity indicates short-term liquidity management by the issuer.
