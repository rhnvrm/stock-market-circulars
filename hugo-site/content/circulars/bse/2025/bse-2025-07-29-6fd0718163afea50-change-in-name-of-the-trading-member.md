## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. (Member No. 948) will change its name to "JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED" effective Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. to JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED
- Member number remains 948
- Change effective from July 30, 2025
- Notification issued to all trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their records with the new name
- Any queries should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, July 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other trading members may need to update their internal records for correspondence purposes.
