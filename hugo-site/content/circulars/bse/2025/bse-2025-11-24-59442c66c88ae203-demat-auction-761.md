## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 761 dated November 24, 2025. The auction includes 92 scrips with varying quantities available for auction to meet delivery obligations. This is a standard settlement mechanism where securities are auctioned when sellers fail to deliver shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 761
- Date: November 24, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 92 securities
- Quantities range from 1 share to 489,533 shares (GV FILMS LTD)
- Notable high-quantity auctions include GV FILMS LTD (489,533 shares), GLOBE CIVIL (3,142 shares), LAST MILE EN (2,500 shares), and CATVISION L (2,149 shares)
- Major companies included: BHARTI AIRTEL (388 shares), INFOSYS (420 shares), CANARA BANK (315 shares), INDIAN OIL (1,000 shares), GAIL (200 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery positions in listed scrips must participate in the auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism
- Trading members should note the quantities available for auction and plan accordingly

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 24, 2025
- Auction proceedings to be conducted as per BSE settlement calendar for Settlement No. 761

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 92 scrips with varying liquidity profiles. High-volume auctions like GV FILMS (489,533 shares) may see price volatility.

**Operational Impact:** Standard settlement procedure for members with delivery shortfalls. Members need to ensure adequate funds/securities for auction participation.

**Trading Impact:** Securities listed in the auction may experience temporary price movements during auction proceedings. The wide range of quantities indicates varied severity of settlement issues across different scrips.
