## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED for public review and comments. The document was filed on December 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The draft document will remain available on the exchange website for at least 21 days for public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED on December 30, 2025
- Document contains all required disclosures per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public viewing and comments
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has complied with Schedule VI disclosure requirements of ICDR Regulations
- Interested parties may submit comments on the draft offer document within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 31, 2025 (until January 20, 2026)
- **Document Availability**: Minimum 21 days on exchange website

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document filing.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for potential IPO investors - Provides opportunity to review and comment on the draft offer document before finalization.

**Company Impact**: Medium for SOTEFIN BHARAT LIMITED - Standard step in the IPO process; public feedback may influence final offer document.

**Regulatory Significance**: Standard compliance with ICDR Regulations for SME IPO process.
