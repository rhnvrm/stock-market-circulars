## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited issued on private placement basis. The listing includes 11,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 550 crores, with a redemption period of 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 11,000 units (total value Rs. 550 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730036
- ISIN: INE511C14YU5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,123 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Ltd serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Listing Date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term nature (3-month maturity) and private placement basis indicate standard corporate funding activity with limited broader market implications.
