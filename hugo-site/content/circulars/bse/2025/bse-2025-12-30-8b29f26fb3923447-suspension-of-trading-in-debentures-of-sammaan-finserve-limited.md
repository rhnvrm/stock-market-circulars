## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of SAMMAAN FINSERVE LIMITED (ISIN: INE244L07580, Code: 975838) effective January 1, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMMAAN FINSERVE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE244L07580
- BSE Code: 975838
- Debenture Series: SFIL-9.55%-16-1-26-PVT (9.55% coupon)
- Record Date: January 1, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 1, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Number: DR-789/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension pursuant to corporate action procedures for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 1, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- Debenture holders should note the record date for eligibility for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **January 1, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 1, 2026**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of SAMMAAN FINSERVE LIMITED debentures. No broader debt market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of January 1, 2026 will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. Secondary market liquidity ceases from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
