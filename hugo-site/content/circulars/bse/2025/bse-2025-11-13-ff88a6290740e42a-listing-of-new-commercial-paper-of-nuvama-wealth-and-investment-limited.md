## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1500 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited acting as Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 1500 Commercial Paper units listed with scrip code 730555 (NWIL121125)
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs.4,90,702 per unit
- ISIN: INE523L14AQ9
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L14AQ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for these securities
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** November 12, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt financing option for Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. The listing expands debt market trading options but has no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Low impact limited to debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial paper. The 91-day tenure (November 12, 2025 to February 11, 2026) represents standard short-term money market instrument.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only for institutional investors and debt market participants seeking highly-rated (A1+) short-term debt instruments. The discount of approximately 1.86% (issue price Rs.4,90,702 vs face value Rs.5,00,000) provides indicative short-term yield.
