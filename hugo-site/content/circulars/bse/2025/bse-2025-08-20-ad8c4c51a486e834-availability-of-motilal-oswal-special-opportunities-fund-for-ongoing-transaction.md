## Summary

MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across six scheme variants covering regular and direct plans with different dividend options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions after previous closure
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under EQUITY category
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities available
- Each scheme has unique ISIN codes for identification

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is a fund availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE can facilitate transactions in the specified mutual fund schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform starting August 21, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **August 20, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only mutual fund transactions on BSE platform. Provides additional investment options for investors through BSE StAR MF Platform for this specific equity fund from Motilal Oswal Asset Management.
