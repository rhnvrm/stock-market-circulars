## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has successfully listed new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. The issuance comprises 100 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5 crore, issued on a private placement basis. The securities carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating high credit quality for short-term debt instruments.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: 360 ONE WAM LIMITED
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (CP) issued on private placement basis
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 5,00,000 x 100 units = Rs. 5 crore
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,423.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730533
- **Scrip ID**: OWL101125
- **ISIN**: INE466L14FC0
- **Credit Ratings**: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FC0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance provides 360 ONE WAM LIMITED with short-term funding for working capital requirements. The dual A1+ credit ratings from CARE and ICRA reflect strong creditworthiness and low default risk. The impact is limited to institutional investors and debt market participants who trade in commercial papers. The 91-day maturity period and private placement nature make this a standard money market instrument with no broader market implications.
