## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Satin Creditcare Network Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities comprise 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (total Rs. 25 crore) with ICRA A1+ rating, issued at discount price of Rs. 457146/- per unit and maturing on November 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Total Quantity: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore aggregate)
- Issue Price: Rs. 457146/- per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730634
- Scrip ID: SCN211125
- ISIN: INE836B14333
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE836B14333
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 24, 2025 (effective today)
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 12 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Satin Creditcare Network Limited for approximately one year. The A1+ rating from ICRA indicates highest credit quality and minimal credit risk. Trading is restricted to BSE Debt segment with minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs, limiting participation to institutional and high net-worth investors. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
