## Summary

Samman Capital Limited has announced part redemption of three series of non-convertible debentures with a record date of December 15, 2025. The debentures will trade with reduced face value of Rs. 400 per debenture effective from December 15, 2025 (Settlement DR-777/2025-2026). This corporate action includes both part redemption and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Three series of debentures affected: SCL-9.8%-28-12-27-NCD, SCL-8.94%-28-12-27-NCD, and SCL-9.39%-28-12-27-NCD
- Record date fixed: December 15, 2025
- Reduced face value: Rs. 400 per debenture for all three series
- Effective date of reduced face value: December 15, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-777/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value from December 15, 2025
- All trading and settlement systems must reflect the new face value of Rs. 400 per debenture from the effective date
- Debenture holders as on record date will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: December 15, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-777/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects holders of three debenture series of SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED. The part redemption will return capital to debenture holders while reducing outstanding debt obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from December 15, 2025. The reduction in face value indicates partial capital repayment to investors.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment, reducing their principal exposure while maintaining their debt holdings at the new face value.
