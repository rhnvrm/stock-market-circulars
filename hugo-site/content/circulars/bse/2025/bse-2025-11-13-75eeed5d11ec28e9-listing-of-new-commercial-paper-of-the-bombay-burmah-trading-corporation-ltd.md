## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. on private placement basis. The securities comprise 500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 250 crore), issued at a discount with IND A1+ credit rating. The instruments are allotted on November 12, 2025, and will mature on November 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd.
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crore (500 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,61,894 per unit (discount of Rs. 38,106)
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Scrip Code**: 730548
- **Scrip ID**: TBBT121125
- **ISIN**: INE050A14887
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Trading Parameters

- Trading will be conducted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading segment: BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 1 year

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term debt funding for The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. and offers investment opportunities for debt market participants. The IND A1+ rating indicates strong credit quality and adequate degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.
