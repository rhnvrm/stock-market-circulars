## Summary

VLS Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 511333) has announced a buyback offer for its equity shares and has fixed December 12, 2025 as the Record Date to determine shareholder eligibility. The ex-entitlement date will also be December 12, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026). This is a corporate action in the dematerialized securities rolling settlement segment.

## Key Points

- Company: VLS Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 511333)
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Ex-Entitlement Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialized Securities - Rolling Settlement
- Trading members are required to take note of this corporate action

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE must take note of the buyback offer and record date
- Only equity shareholders holding shares as on the Record Date (December 12, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade on ex-entitlement basis from December 12, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025 - Date for determining shareholder eligibility for buyback participation
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: December 12, 2025 - Shares trade without buyback entitlement from this date
- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The buyback announcement may influence VLS Finance Limited's stock price and trading volumes as the ex-entitlement date approaches. Shareholders must hold shares before December 12, 2025 to participate.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders on record as of December 12, 2025 will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback offer. This provides an exit opportunity at potentially favorable prices.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the ex-entitlement status from the specified date and inform their clients about eligibility requirements.
