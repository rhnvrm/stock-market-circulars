## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 25, 2025. The circular details securities entering the framework, those moving between ASM stages, and securities exiting the framework.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly entering Long Term ASM Framework
- 4 securities moving to lower ASM Stage I from higher stages
- 15 securities exiting the Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher ASM stages
- Various reasons for exits including inclusion in Trade for Trade, GSM, ESM, and IBC frameworks

## Regulatory Changes

**New Securities in LT-ASM Framework:**
- Aditya Consumer Marketing Ltd (540146)
- Ahasolar Technologies Ltd (543941)
- Filtron Engineers Ltd (531191)
- Modern Thread India Ltd (500282)
- OTCO International Ltd (523151)

**Securities Moving to Lower ASM Stage I:**
- Aryan Share and Stock Brokers Ltd (542176)
- Ashika Credit Capital Ltd (543766)
- B-Right Realestate Ltd (543543)
- Vadilal Enterprises Ltd (519152)

**Securities Exiting LT-ASM Framework:**
- 15 securities including Ambitious Plastomac Company Ltd, Avro India Ltd, Banganga Paper Industries Ltd, and others
- Various exit reasons: Trade for Trade framework inclusion, GSM Framework inclusion, ESM Framework inclusion

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with LT-ASM framework requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to securities in the framework
- Specific trading restrictions and monitoring protocols for listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 25, 2025 - All changes to LT-ASM framework take effect

## Impact Assessment

The changes affect trading conditions for 24 securities across various categories. Securities entering or remaining in LT-ASM will face enhanced surveillance and potential trading restrictions. The movement of 4 securities to lower ASM stages indicates improved compliance, while 15 securities exiting suggests either improved market behavior or transfer to other regulatory frameworks with different oversight mechanisms.
