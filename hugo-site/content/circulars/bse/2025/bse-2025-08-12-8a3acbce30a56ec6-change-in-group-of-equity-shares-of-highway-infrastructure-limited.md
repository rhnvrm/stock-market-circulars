## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of HIGHWAY INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED equity shares (Scrip Code: 544477) from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group) effective August 28, 2025. This change will improve trading liquidity by moving the stock from restricted trading to normal rolling settlement.

## Key Points

- Stock transfer from T Group to B Group effective August 28, 2025
- Company: HIGHWAY INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544477)
- Continuation of previous exchange notice dated August 11, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685)

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment with B Group classification, allowing normal intraday trading and T+1 settlement instead of mandatory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the segment change and adjust their trading systems and processes accordingly for the new classification.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 12, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 11, 2025**: Previous related notice date

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on stock liquidity as the move from T Group to B Group allows for normal rolling settlement trading instead of compulsory delivery, potentially increasing trading volume and reducing settlement constraints for investors.
