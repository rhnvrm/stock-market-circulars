## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 19, 2025. The suspension is in connection with the early redemption of debentures (ISIN: INE12W707021, Code: 976204) with a record date set for December 19, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debentures ISIN INE12W707021 (Code: 976204)
- Debenture details: EIAPL-10.15%-6-2-29-PVT
- Purpose: Early redemption of debentures
- Record date: December 19, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 19, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-781/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notification regarding early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 19, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- All dealing activity in debentures with ISIN INE12W707021 must cease from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 19, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Debenture holders will be unable to trade these securities from December 19, 2025. This affects liquidity and exit options for investors holding these debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures will experience early redemption. Those wishing to exit their positions must do so before December 19, 2025, after which trading will be suspended.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these debentures and inform clients holding these securities about the early redemption and trading suspension.
