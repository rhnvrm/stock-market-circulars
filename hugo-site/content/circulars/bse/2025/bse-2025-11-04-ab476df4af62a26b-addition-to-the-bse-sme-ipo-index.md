## Summary

BSE announces the addition of GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544594) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE on November 4, 2025, and will be added to the index effective at the open of November 6, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED (ticker: 544594) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Stock listing on BSE: Tuesday, November 4, 2025
- Index addition effective: Thursday, November 6, 2025 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to previous notice: 20251103-44

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a routine index composition update following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index-tracking funds and investors monitoring the BSE SME IPO Index should note the composition change for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Stock listing date on BSE
- **November 6, 2025**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine index maintenance activity following the listing of a new SME stock. Impact is primarily relevant to:

- Index-tracking funds that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index composition
- Investors specifically monitoring SME IPO stocks
- The specific company being added (potential increase in visibility and liquidity)
