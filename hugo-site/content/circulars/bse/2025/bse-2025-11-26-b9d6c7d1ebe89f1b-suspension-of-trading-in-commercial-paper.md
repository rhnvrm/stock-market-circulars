## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 10 companies effective from their respective record dates. The suspension is due to scheduled redemptions of commercial paper, with record dates ranging from November 28, 2025 to December 7, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have commercial papers scheduled for redemption
- Trading suspension aligns with record dates for redemption
- Notice number: 20251126-5 issued under Corporate Actions - Debt segment
- Trading members must cease dealing from specified dates
- All suspensions relate to commercial paper redemption purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for scheduled commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the "No Dealings From" dates
- Market participants must observe the trading suspension for affected securities
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption processing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants holding or trading these commercial papers. The suspension is temporary and limited to specific securities undergoing scheduled redemption.

**Operational Impact:** Investors and trading members holding these commercial papers will need to process redemptions according to the scheduled dates. No new trading positions can be established from the suspension dates.

**Sectoral Coverage:** Diverse - includes financial services (Kotak Mahindra, Nuvama, Axis Securities, SBI Cards, Mirae Asset, Navi Finserv, Fedbank), retail (Reliance Retail), telecom (Reliance Jio), and manufacturing (Tata Steel) sectors.
