## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of ECL Finance Limited effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of specific debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ECL Finance Limited's 9.60% debentures maturing on December 19, 2025
- ISIN: INE804I07H64 (Code: 953139)
- Record date for redemption: December 4, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 4, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-770/2025-2026
- Trading members must refrain from dealing in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension
- Members must ensure their systems reflect the trading suspension to prevent inadvertent trades

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption and start of no-dealings period
- **December 19, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Holders of these debentures will be unable to trade them from December 4, 2025, affecting liquidity. Investors seeking to exit their positions must do so before the no-dealings date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and inform clients promptly to prevent attempted transactions during the suspension period.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders identified as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds. Those wishing to sell must act before December 4, 2025.
