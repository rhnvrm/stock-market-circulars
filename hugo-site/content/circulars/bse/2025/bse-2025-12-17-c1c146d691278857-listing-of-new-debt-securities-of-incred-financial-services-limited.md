## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 17, 2025. The securities are Nifty 50 Index-linked with a total quantity of 2,500 units and face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit. The securities carry a CRISIL PP-MLD AA+r/Stable rating and will mature on February 11, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977375, Scrip ID: IFSL121225
- ISIN Number: INE321N07608
- Quantity: 2,500 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit (at par)
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL PP-MLD AA+r/Stable
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07608
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for detailed terms
- Any clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 11, 2028
- **Interest Payment Date:** Not Applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine private placement debt listing with no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to institutional investors participating in the private placement. The securities are Nifty 50 Index-linked, providing exposure to benchmark equity index performance through debt instruments.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated with the new scrip details for debt segment trading.
