## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd (Code: AEL-24-12-25-CP, ISIN: INE423A14XT1, Scrip Code: 729731) effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of the Commercial Paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper ISIN: INE423A14XT1 (Scrip Code: 729731)
- Issuer: Adani Enterprises Ltd
- Record Date: December 23, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 23, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Notice Reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (INE423A14XT1) from December 23, 2025
- All market participants must comply with the trading suspension directive
- Settlement of existing positions must be completed as per applicable debt market settlement schedules

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: December 23, 2025
- **Purpose**: Redemption of Commercial Paper

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact confined to holders and traders of this specific Commercial Paper instrument. The suspension is temporary and relates to the natural maturity/redemption cycle of the debt instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in Adani Enterprises' Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE423A14XT1) must cease trading activities from December 23, 2025. Investors holding this instrument should prepare for redemption proceeds as per the terms of the Commercial Paper.

**Issuer**: Adani Enterprises Ltd - one of the flagship companies of the Adani Group operating in diversified sectors including infrastructure, energy, and resources.
