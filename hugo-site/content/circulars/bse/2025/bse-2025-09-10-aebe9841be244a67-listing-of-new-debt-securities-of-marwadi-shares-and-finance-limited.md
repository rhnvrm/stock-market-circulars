## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited on its debt segment effective September 10, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with zero interest rate and 2-year maturity.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 25,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 25 crore
- Zero coupon bonds with cumulative interest payment
- CRISIL A+/STABLE credit rating
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code:** 977093
- **Scrip ID:** 0MSFL27
- **ISIN:** INE138I08109
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 1,00,000
- **Date of Allotment:** September 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** September 9, 2027

## Trading Information

- Securities tradable only in demat form
- Market lot size: 1
- No put/call options available
- Placement memorandum available on BSE website

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** September 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** September 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** September 9, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal impact on broader market operations. Provides additional investment avenue for debt market participants seeking CRISIL A+ rated instruments with 2-year tenure.
