## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by India Infradebt Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The securities carry a 7.39% annual interest rate with a maturity date of May 27, 2031, and are rated AAA by both CRISIL and ICRA.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977332
- Scrip ID: 739IIL31
- ISIN: INE537P07877
- Quantity: 165,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 7.39% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE537P07877
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for complete details
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from May 27, 2026 to May 27, 2031
- **Redemption Date**: May 27, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities for institutional investors. The securities have high credit ratings (AAA) from both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating strong creditworthiness. No put/call options are available. The listing provides additional fixed-income investment opportunities in the debt segment but does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
