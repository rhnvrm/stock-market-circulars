## Summary

Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (Exchange ticker 544665) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, December 26, 2025, following its listing on BSE on Wednesday, December 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (ticker 544665) listed on BSE on December 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective December 26, 2025 at market open
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No. 20251223-35

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent addition following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and products may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Listing date for Global Ocean Logistics India Limited on BSE
- **December 26, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action affecting the BSE SME IPO Index. The addition follows the normal process for newly listed SME companies. Index funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to purchase shares of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited to maintain index alignment. For additional queries, market participants should contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
