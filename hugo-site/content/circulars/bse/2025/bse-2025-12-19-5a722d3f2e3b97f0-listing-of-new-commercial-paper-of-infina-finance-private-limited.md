## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 19, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crores, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crores
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,743 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730810
- Scrip ID: IFPL181225
- ISIN: INE879F14MF5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MF5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Listing Date: December 19, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. The issuance provides a 90-day funding instrument for Infina Finance Private Limited. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
