## Summary

JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 22, 2025. Five index fund schemes across equity and debt categories are included in this reopening.

## Key Points

- JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: August 22, 2025
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP & SWP
- Five schemes included: 4 equity index funds and 1 debt index fund
- All schemes are Direct Plan - Growth options

## Scheme Details

**Equity Schemes:**
- JIOBLACKROCK NIFTY 50 INDEX FUND (INF22M001044)
- JIOBLACKROCK NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND (INF22M001051)
- JIOBLACKROCK NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND (INF22M001077)
- JIOBLACKROCK NIFTY NEXT 50 INDEX FUND (INF22M001085)

**Debt Scheme:**
- JIOBLACKROCK NIFTY 8-13 YR G-SEC INDEX FUND (INF22M001069)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 22, 2025
- **Notice Date:** August 21, 2025
- **Reference Notice:** 20250804-32

## Impact Assessment

This reopening provides investors access to JIO BLACKROCK's index fund offerings through BSE's mutual fund platform. The availability of systematic investment and withdrawal facilities enhances investment flexibility for retail and institutional investors seeking exposure to various market segments including large-cap, mid-cap, small-cap equity and government securities.
