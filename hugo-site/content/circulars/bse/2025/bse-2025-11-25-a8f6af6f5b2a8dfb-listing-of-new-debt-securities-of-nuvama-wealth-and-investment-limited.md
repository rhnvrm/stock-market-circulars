## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited issued on private placement basis, effective November 25, 2025. The securities are debentures with a 9.20% annual interest rate maturing on December 20, 2027, rated CARE AA/Stable.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976911
- Scrip ID: 920NWIL27
- Detail Name: NWIL-9.20%-20-12-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE523L07AM2 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 10,000 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 103,596.16
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 9.20% per annum
- Trading: Only in dematerialised form
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE523L07AM2
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 25, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 25, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Annually from July 13, 2026 to December 20, 2027
- Date of Redemption: December 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on market operations. This is a routine listing of debt securities on the BSE Debt segment. The securities will be available for trading in dematerialised form with standard tick size of 1 paise. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new listing for their records and client servicing.
