## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1100 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, with CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 1100 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730592, Scrip ID: TBBT181125
- ISIN: INE050A14895
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 490,031.50
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE050A14895
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: February 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of commercial paper on private placement basis by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. The instrument provides short-term funding to the issuer (approximately 90-day maturity) and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly rated debt securities. No impact on broader market operations or trading regulations.
