## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of MTE Structures Limited on its SME platform for public review and comments. The document was filed on October 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from November 4, 2025 to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MTE Structures Limited on October 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public review for minimum 21 days
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted within 21 days from November 4, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Document Filed:** October 31, 2025
- **Notice Date:** November 4, 2025
- **Comment Deadline:** November 25, 2025 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability:** Minimum 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notification for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in MTE Structures Limited. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. No immediate market-wide impact expected.
