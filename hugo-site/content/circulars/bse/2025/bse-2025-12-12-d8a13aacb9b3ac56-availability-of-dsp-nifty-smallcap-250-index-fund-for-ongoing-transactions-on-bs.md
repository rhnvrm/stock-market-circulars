## Summary

BSE has announced that DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 15, 2025. The scheme will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows previous Notice No. 20251121-61 which presumably announced a temporary closure.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Effective date: December 15, 2025
- Available transaction types: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF
- Six plan variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options)
- All variants are equity category schemes

## Scheme Details

The fund offers six variants across Regular and Direct plans:

**Regular Plans:**
1. Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XE1)
2. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF740KA1XF8)
3. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XG6)

**Direct Plans:**
4. Growth (ISIN: INF740KA1XH4)
5. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF740KA1XI2)
6. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF740KA1XJ0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice for BSE StAR MF Platform members and investors.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 15, 2025 - Reopening for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This affects only investors and distributors dealing with this specific DSP index fund scheme.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members can resume accepting subscription and redemption orders for this scheme. Investors who were unable to transact during the closure period can now resume investments.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in gaining exposure to the NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 index through this DSP fund, as they can now make fresh investments or redeem existing holdings.
