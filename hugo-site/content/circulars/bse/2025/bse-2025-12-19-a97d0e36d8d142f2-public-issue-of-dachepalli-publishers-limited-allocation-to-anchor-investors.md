## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Dachepalli Publishers Limited. This circular details the anchor investor portion allocation ahead of the main public offering.

## Key Points

- Company: Dachepalli Publishers Limited
- Event: Allocation to Anchor Investors in Public Issue
- Anchor allocation completed prior to main public offering
- Provides institutional investor participation details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure for anchor investor allocation in public issues as per SEBI IPO regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and market participants to note the anchor allocation details
- Information relevant for price discovery and investor participation in the public issue
- Anchor allocation typically locked-in for 30 days from date of allotment

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 19, 2025
- Anchor allocation completed as per the circular

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation indicates institutional interest and provides price discovery mechanism for the IPO. The participation and allocation details help gauge market demand for the public issue.
