## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 125 crore issued by POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED on private placement basis, effective August 28, 2025. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with maturity on November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2,500 units (Rs. 125 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,135 per unit
- Scrip code: 730104 (PFL260825)
- ISIN: INE511C14YX9
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in dematerialized form only
- Minimum trading denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing effective: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for POONAWALLA FINCORP and investment option for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
