## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 1,500 commercial papers issued by Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd on private placement basis. Each paper has a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 and was issued at Rs. 4,92,123. The securities carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will be traded in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (privately placed)
- Quantity Issued: 1,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,123 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 75 crores
- Scrip Code: 730774
- Scrip ID: MLDL121225
- Detail Name: MLDL-13-3-26-CP
- ISIN: INE813A14409
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: YES Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE813A14409
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 15, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: December 15, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 13, 2026
- Maturity Period: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term debt financing. The issue carries a high credit rating (IND A1+) indicating low credit risk. The 3-month maturity period is standard for commercial paper instruments. Trading is restricted to institutional participants in Rs. 5 lakh denominations, limiting retail investor participation.
