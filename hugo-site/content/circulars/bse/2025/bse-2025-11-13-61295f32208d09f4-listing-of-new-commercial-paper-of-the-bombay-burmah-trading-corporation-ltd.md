## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crore), with issue price of Rs. 4,61,894 per unit, allotted on November 12, 2025, and maturing on November 12, 2026. Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,61,894 per unit
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- ISIN: INE050A14887
- Scrip Code: 730548
- Scrip ID: TBBT121125
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE050A14887
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 13, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2026
- Tenure: 365 days (1 year)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. and offers investment opportunity to debt market participants. The IND A1+ credit rating indicates high safety for principal repayment. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
