## Summary

MEHUL COLOURS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544472) equity shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: MEHUL COLOURS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544472
- Transfer from MT Group to M Group
- Segment change from Trade for Trade to Rolling
- SME segment company

## Regulatory Changes

Trading segment classification changed from restrictive Trade for Trade basis to normal Rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the segment transfer and adjust their trading operations accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, August 21, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for shareholders as shares move from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, improving trading flexibility and liquidity.
