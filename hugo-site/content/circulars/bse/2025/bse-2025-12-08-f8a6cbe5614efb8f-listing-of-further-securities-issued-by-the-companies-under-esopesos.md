## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 17 companies will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, December 9, 2025. All securities are listed without any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Total of 17 companies have issued further securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Securities include both equity shares with face values of Re. 1, Rs. 2, Rs. 5, and Rs. 10
- Total new shares across all companies: 3,213,508 shares
- No lock-in period applicable to any of the listed securities
- Largest issuances: Ujjivan Small Finance Bank (671,386 shares), Aditya Birla Capital (601,897 shares), Latent View Analytics (323,860 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification for listing of securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS schemes as per SEBI guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities
- Securities are available for trading from December 9, 2025
- No special compliance requirements as there are no lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Not Applicable (NA) for all securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. These are routine ESOP/ESOS conversions representing small percentages of outstanding equity for each company. The securities are immediately tradeable with no lock-in restrictions. This reflects normal employee incentive plan activity and demonstrates healthy employee participation in stock option schemes. The listing provides liquidity to employees who have exercised their stock options.
