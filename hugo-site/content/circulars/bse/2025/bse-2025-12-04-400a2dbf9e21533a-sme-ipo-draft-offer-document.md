## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED on its SME platform for public review and comments. The document was filed on December 03, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED on December 03, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period: 21 days from December 04, 2025
- Comments to be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested stakeholders and investors may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted within 21 days from December 04, 2025 (i.e., by December 25, 2025)
- Comments must be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document will remain available on the exchange website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED
- **December 04, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **December 25, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard procedural notice for an SME segment IPO. It does not affect existing market operations or trading.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for potential investors - The 21-day public comment period allows stakeholders to review the offer document and provide feedback before the final prospectus is issued. This is part of the regulatory process to ensure transparency and investor protection.

**Segment**: SME segment only, no impact on main board securities.
