## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed 3,000 units of Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 17, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit, totaling Rs. 150 crores. The Commercial Paper carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and IND A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 3,000 units of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit (Total: Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730584
- Scrip ID: NMIL141125
- ISIN: INE870H14WG7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WG7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing on private placement basis. The instrument has a short tenor of approximately 90 days and is rated A1+ by two rating agencies, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members will have access to trade these securities on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with standard trading parameters.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors who participated in the private placement. The high credit rating provides comfort on creditworthiness for the short investment horizon.
