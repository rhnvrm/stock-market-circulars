## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 19, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,000 units of face value Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling Rs.100 crore, with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Birla Group Holdings Private Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Total Issue Size: Rs.100 crore (2,000 units Ã— Rs.5,00,000)
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,91,664.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730595
- Scrip ID: BGHP191125
- ISIN: INE09OL14ID8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE09OL14ID8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 19, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 19, 2025
- Listing Date: November 19, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper listing provides short-term funding for Birla Group Holdings Private Limited and offers institutional investors a highly-rated (A1+) investment option with a 91-day maturity. The impact is limited to participants in the debt segment dealing with short-term commercial paper instruments.
