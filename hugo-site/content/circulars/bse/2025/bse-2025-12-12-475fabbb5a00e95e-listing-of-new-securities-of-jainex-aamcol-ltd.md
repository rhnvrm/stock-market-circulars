## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 7,48,169 new equity shares of Jainex Aamcol Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505212) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, December 15, 2025. The shares were issued at face value of Rs. 10/- each with a premium of Rs. 110/-, and are ranking pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 7,48,169 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 110/-
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- Scrip Code: 505212
- ISIN: INE280F01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 1510001 to 2258169
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the listing of these new securities and should update their systems accordingly to allow trading in these shares from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement Date**: December 15, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The rights issue has increased the equity capital of Jainex Aamcol Ltd. by 7,48,169 shares. The listing enables shareholders who subscribed to the rights issue to trade these shares on the exchange. The issue price of Rs. 120/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 110 premium) indicates capital raising by the company from existing shareholders. No impact on broader market operations or other listed entities.
