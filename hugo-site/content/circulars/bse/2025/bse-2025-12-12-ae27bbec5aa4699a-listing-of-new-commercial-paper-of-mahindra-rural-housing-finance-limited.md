## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 12, 2025. The instrument consists of 2000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total issue size Rs. 1000 Crores), carrying CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings, with maturity on December 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Quantity**: 2000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,67,635 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 1000 Crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730753
- **Scrip ID**: MRHF121225
- **ISIN**: INE950O14BY0
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE950O14BY0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 11, 2026
- **Tenor**: 364 days (approximately 1 year)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited with short-term funding through the commercial paper route. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
