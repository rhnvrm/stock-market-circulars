## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD (CIN: L99999MH1942PLC003556). The company name has been officially changed to STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED with effect from October 1, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on October 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company originally incorporated as CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD in 1942
- New name: STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L99999MH1942PLC003556
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Central Processing Centre
- Effective date of name change: October 1, 2025
- Certificate digitally signed under rule 9(2) of the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014

## Regulatory Changes

This is a certificate issued pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. The company must comply with Section 12 of the Companies Act regarding display of company name.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD) for a period of two years along with its new name (STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED) at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The company must update its registered address records: 5, Kumud Apartment CHS Limited, Karnik Road Chikan Ghar, Kalyan, NA, KALYAN, Thane- 421301, Maharashtra, India

## Important Dates

- **October 1, 2025**: Effective date of name change from CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD to STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED
- **October 1, 2025**: Date of issuance of Certificate of Incorporation
- **Two-year period from October 1, 2025**: Duration for which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the company's CIN, corporate structure, or stakeholder rights and liabilities. Investors and stakeholders should update their records to reflect the new company name. Trading symbol/scrip code changes, if any, will be communicated separately by BSE. The company's legal obligations and contractual commitments continue unchanged under the new name.
