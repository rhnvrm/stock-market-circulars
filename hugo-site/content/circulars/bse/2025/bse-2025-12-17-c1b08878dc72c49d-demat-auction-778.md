## Summary

BSE has released the demat auction list for Settlement No. 778 dated December 17, 2025. The auction includes 93 securities from various companies with specified quantities that failed to be delivered in the normal settlement process and will be auctioned to ensure settlement completion.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 778
- Settlement Date: December 17, 2025
- Total securities listed: 93 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 10,000,000 shares depending on the security
- Notable large quantity auctions: MANGALAM IND (10,000,000 shares), HINDUSTAN CO (50,000 shares)
- Includes major companies like INFOSYS LTD (3 shares), VEDANTA LIMITED (22 shares), TRENT LTD (9 shares), SPICEJET LTD (250 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members must participate in the auction to fulfill their delivery obligations
- Securities not delivered in normal settlement must be procured through this auction mechanism
- All participants must comply with BSE auction settlement procedures and timelines
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per exchange regulations

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 17, 2025
- Settlement Number: 778
- Auction execution timing as per BSE standard auction procedures

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects 93 securities with varying quantities. The impact is primarily operational, affecting clearing members and brokers who have pending delivery obligations. The large quantity for MANGALAM IND (10 million shares) and HINDUSTAN CO (50,000 shares) may have price impact during auction. Most other securities have relatively small quantities. This is a routine market operation to ensure settlement integrity and delivery fulfillment. Investors in affected securities may see temporary price volatility during auction execution.
