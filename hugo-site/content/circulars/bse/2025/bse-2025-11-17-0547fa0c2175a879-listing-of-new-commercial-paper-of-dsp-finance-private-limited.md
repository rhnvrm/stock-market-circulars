## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper instruments issued by DSP Finance Private Limited on private placement basis, effective November 17, 2025. Both instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carry ICRA A1+ credit rating, and will be traded only in dematerialized form with ICICI Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper instruments listed totaling 4,000 units (2,000 each)
- Instrument 1: DFPL-13-2-26-CP (ISIN: INE422H14099, Scrip Code: 730574)
  - Issue price: Rs. 491,183
  - Allotment date: November 14, 2025
  - Redemption date: February 13, 2026
- Instrument 2: DFPL-14-5-26-CP (ISIN: INE422H14081, Scrip Code: 730575)
  - Issue price: Rs. 482,186
  - Allotment date: November 14, 2025
  - Redemption date: May 14, 2026
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+ for both instruments
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN numbers (INE422H14099 and INE422H14081)

## Important Dates

- **Listing date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment date**: November 14, 2025 (both instruments)
- **Redemption date (Instrument 1)**: February 13, 2026
- **Redemption date (Instrument 2)**: May 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds Rs. 200 crores worth of commercial paper to BSE's debt segment. Trading members can contact the debt department (contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications. The instruments provide short-term investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
