## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 28, 2025. The securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 1500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 730668, Scrip ID: FFSL271125
- ISIN: INE007N14EK4
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,460 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EK4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified scrip code and ISIN

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instruments are short-term debt securities maturing in 3 months with strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term fixed income instruments of Fedbank Financial Services Limited.
