## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 11, 2025. The listing comprises 500 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,87,354 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730100
- Scrip ID: NWFL26825
- Detail Name: NWFL-10-03-26-CP
- ISIN: INE918K14CO4 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CO4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited with a maturity period of approximately 4 months. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate low credit risk. Trading members can contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
