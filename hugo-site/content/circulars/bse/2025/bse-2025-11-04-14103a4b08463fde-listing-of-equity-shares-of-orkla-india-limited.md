## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Orkla India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544595 and trading symbol ORKLAINDIA with ISIN number INE16NZ01023. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Orkla India Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544595
- Trading Symbol: ORKLAINDIA
- ISIN Number: INE16NZ01023
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a new company listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Orkla India Limited and await the separate notice regarding listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has medium impact for market participants. The listing will provide investors with a new investment opportunity in Orkla India Limited. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business fundamentals, issue size, and market conditions at the time of listing. Investors interested in participating should await the detailed listing notice for complete information on trading commencement.
