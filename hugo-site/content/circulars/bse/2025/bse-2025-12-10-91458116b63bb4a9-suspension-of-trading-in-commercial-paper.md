## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in 10 commercial papers issued by various financial services companies and corporations due to upcoming redemption. The suspension is effective from December 12-15, 2025, based on respective record dates. Trading members are explicitly instructed not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers suspended for redemption purposes
- Record dates range from December 12-15, 2025
- Issuers include SBICAP Securities, Godrej Finance, Vertis Infrastructure Trust, Julius Baer Capital India, HDFC Securities, Bajaj Financial Securities, DSP Finance, Tata Steel, and Redington Limited
- Trading suspension effective from respective record dates
- Notice number: 20251210-7, Category: Corporate Actions, Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the "No Dealings From" dates
- Members must ensure compliance with suspension for all 10 securities listed
- Suspension applies to commercial papers with ISINs: INE212K14CF0, INE02KN14408, INE0KXY14030, INE824H14SJ6, INE700G14PY5, INE01C314CT1, INE212K14CG8, INE422H14024, INE081A14GH7, INE891D14A87

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Record date and no dealings date for SBICAP Securities Limited CP (INE212K14CF0)
- **December 15, 2025**: Record date and no dealings date for remaining 9 commercial papers:
  - Godrej Finance Limited (INE02KN14408)
  - Vertis Infrastructure Trust (INE0KXY14030)
  - Julius Baer Capital India (INE824H14SJ6)
  - HDFC Securities Limited (INE700G14PY5)
  - Bajaj Financial Securities (INE01C314CT1)
  - SBICAP Securities Limited (INE212K14CG8)
  - DSP Finance Private Limited (INE422H14024)
  - Tata Steel Limited (INE081A14GH7)
  - Redington Limited (INE891D14A87)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 10 commercial paper instruments in the debt segment. Routine redemption activity with no unusual circumstances.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to prevent trading in these 10 securities from specified dates. Standard redemption process for short-term debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds on maturity. No secondary market trading available from suspension dates until redemption completion.
