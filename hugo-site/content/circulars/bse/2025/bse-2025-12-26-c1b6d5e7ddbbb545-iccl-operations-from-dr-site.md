## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) will operate from its Disaster Recovery (DR) Site from Friday, December 26, 2025, to Tuesday, December 30, 2025. This applies to all segments including Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR. Members and custodians will experience no changes in accessing ICCL applications or URLs during this period.

## Key Points

- ICCL operations shifting to DR site for 5 days (December 26-30, 2025)
- All segments covered: Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR
- No changes to member or custodian access procedures
- ICCL applications and URLs remain unchanged
- Reference to Exchange Circular No. 20251226-1 dated December 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a temporary operational shift to the Disaster Recovery site for business continuity purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from members or custodians. All existing access procedures and applications remain the same during the DR site operations period.

## Important Dates

- **December 26, 2025 (Friday)**: DR site operations begin
- **December 30, 2025 (Tuesday)**: DR site operations end

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. This is a planned operational shift to ensure business continuity testing and disaster recovery preparedness.

**Market Impact**: None. Trading and clearing operations continue normally with no disruption to members or custodians.

**Technical Impact**: None. All ICCL applications and URLs remain accessible without any changes to connection parameters or access methods.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Members and custodians can continue operations as usual without any modifications to their systems or processes.
