## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from three companies - West Leisure Resorts Limited (Scrip Code: 538382), Latent View Analytics Limited (Scrip Code: 543398), and Eldeco Housing and Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 523329) - in response to BSE surveillance queries regarding significant price and volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed that the movements were market-driven with no undisclosed information.

## Key Points

- **West Leisure Resorts Limited** clarified that price movement is purely market driven with no specific reasons
- **Latent View Analytics Limited** confirmed increased trade volume is due to market conditions and absolutely market driven
- **Eldeco Housing and Industries Limited** responded to surveillance query dated December 8, 2025
- All companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No undisclosed information or impending announcements from any of the three companies
- All required disclosures have been timely made to BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 30
- Companies must disclose if there is any undisclosed information that may affect price behavior
- All information must be hosted on company websites in addition to stock exchange submissions

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: BSE sent surveillance query to West Leisure Resorts Limited (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3188)
- **December 8, 2025**: BSE sent surveillance query to Eldeco Housing (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3186)
- **December 9, 2025**: BSE sent surveillance query to Latent View Analytics (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/745)
- **December 9, 2025**: All three companies submitted their clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications confirming no material undisclosed information exists. The price and volume movements were attributed to normal market conditions.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Provides transparency that recent trading activity in these three stocks is market-driven rather than based on undisclosed corporate developments.

**Operational Impact**: None. Companies confirmed they are maintaining good corporate governance practices and timely compliance with disclosure requirements.

**Surveillance Action**: BSE's proactive monitoring demonstrates exchange vigilance in ensuring fair markets and protecting investor interests through timely clarification requests.
