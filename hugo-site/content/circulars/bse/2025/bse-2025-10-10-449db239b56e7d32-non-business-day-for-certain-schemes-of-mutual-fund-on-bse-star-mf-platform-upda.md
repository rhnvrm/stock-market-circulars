## Summary

BSE has notified that Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund will observe a non-business day on October 13, 2025. During this day, transactions for this scheme will not be processed on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Affected Scheme: Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund
- Non-Business Date: October 13, 2025
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Fund House: Bandhan Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors should note that transactions in Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund cannot be placed on October 13, 2025
- BSE members and participants should inform their clients about this non-business day
- Alternative trading days should be used for transactions in this scheme

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Non-business day for Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and limited to investors of Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund. Investors planning transactions on October 13, 2025 will need to schedule them for alternative dates. This is a routine operational matter with no broader market implications.
