## Summary

BSE announces the launch of HELIOS SMALL CAP FUND - NFO (New Fund Offer) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from November 06, 2025 to November 20, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of November 27, 2025. Six scheme variants are available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- HELIOS MUTUAL FUND launching HELIOS SMALL CAP FUND as equity scheme NFO
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- ISINs assigned: INF0R8701350, INF0R8701376, INF0R8701368, INF0R8701384, INF0R8701400, INF0R8701392
- NFO period: November 06, 2025 to November 20, 2025
- Tentative allotment date: November 27, 2025
- Extended trading till midnight on November 20, 2025 for lump sum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on last NFO payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification following existing BSE StAR MF Platform procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 26, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on November 26, 2025
- Cancelled transactions (CC with N flag) must be reported to RTA/AMC for unfunded orders
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication to be completed before 3:00 PM on November 20, 2025
- Switch transactions subject to receipt of funds on or before allotment date; late receipts will not be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **November 06, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **November 20, 2025**: NFO closure date (3:00 PM for regular transactions; midnight for lump sum purchases)
- **November 20, 2025 by 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **November 26, 2025 by 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **November 27, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1 by 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (or transaction cancelled)
- **T+5 or NFO payout deadline by 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction processing deadline (whichever is earlier)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch for a new mutual fund scheme. It provides investors with additional investment options in the small-cap equity segment but does not affect secondary market trading or listed securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for BSE StAR MF Platform members - Members need to process NFO orders according to specified timelines and fund settlement requirements. Standard operational procedures apply with specific deadlines for fund collection and reporting.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for retail investors - Provides new investment opportunity in small-cap equity segment through both Regular and Direct plan options with flexible investment modes (lump sum and SIP).
