## Summary

Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited (Formerly known as J. Taparia Projects Limited) has extended the closing date of its ongoing rights issue from November 26, 2025 to December 10, 2025. The Rights Issue Committee approved this extension on November 25, 2025 to facilitate better participation from eligible shareholders and provide adequate time for subscription. All subsequent post-issue activities including allotment and listing dates have been adjusted accordingly.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue closing date extended by 14 days to December 10, 2025
- Issue originally opened on Monday, November 17, 2025
- Extension granted to facilitate better shareholder participation
- Last date for on-market renunciation remains Thursday, November 20, 2025
- Last date for submission of duly filled CAF with payment is now December 10, 2025
- No changes to Letter of Offer and Composite Application Form except closing date modification
- Rights Issue Committee meeting held on November 25, 2025 from 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural extension approved by the Rights Issue Committee under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible equity shareholders should take note of the revised Issue Closure Date of December 10, 2025
- Shareholders intending to subscribe must submit duly filled Composite Application Form (CAF) along with payment by December 10, 2025
- Corrigendum cum addendum available on BSE website (www.bseindia.com) and company website (www.annvrridhhi.com)

## Important Dates

- **Issue Opening Date:** Monday, November 17, 2025
- **Last Date for On Market Renunciation:** Thursday, November 20, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date (Extended):** Wednesday, December 10, 2025
- **Finalization of Basis of Allotment (on or about):** Thursday, December 11, 2025
- **Date of Allotment (on or about):** Thursday, December 11, 2025
- **Date of Credit of Rights Equity Shares (on or about):** Friday, December 12, 2025
- **Date of Listing (on or about):** Friday, December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Company Impact:** Provides Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited additional time to achieve better subscription rates for its rights issue, potentially improving the success of the capital raising exercise.

**Shareholder Impact:** Eligible shareholders receive 14 additional days to decide on and submit their subscription applications, allowing more time for financial planning and decision-making.

**Market Impact:** Limited market impact as this is a company-specific extension affecting only eligible shareholders of Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited (Script Code: 538539, ISIN: INE075K01013). The extension is a positive measure ensuring adequate participation in the rights issue.
