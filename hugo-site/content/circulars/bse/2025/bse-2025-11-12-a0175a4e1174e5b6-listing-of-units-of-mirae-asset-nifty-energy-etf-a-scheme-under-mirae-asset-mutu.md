## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Mirae Asset Nifty Energy ETF units effective November 13, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme that replicates/tracks the Nifty Energy Total Return Index. A total of 2,109,879 units have been listed in the B Group with scrip code 544604, trading under the symbol "ENERGY" on the BOLT system. The units will trade in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit, face value of Rs. 10.00, and issue price of Rs. 35.79 per unit.

## Key Points

- **ETF Name**: Mirae Asset Nifty Energy ETF
- **Scrip Code**: 544604
- **ISIN**: INF769K01PR2
- **Trading Symbol**: ENERGY
- **Total Units Listed**: 2,109,879 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 35.79 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B Group
- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Underlying Index**: Nifty Energy Total Return Index
- **Investment Manager**: Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited
- **Registrar**: KFIN Technologies Limited

## Market Makers

The following entities are designated as market makers for this ETF:
- East India Securities Limited
- Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited
- Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited
- Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited
- Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited
- IRage Broking Services LLP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification in compliance with existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, which mandate that mutual fund units must be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this ETF are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Members must use the designated scrip code 544604 and trading symbol "ENERGY" for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: November 13, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine ETF listing that provides investors with another instrument to gain exposure to the energy sector through a passive investment vehicle tracking the Nifty Energy Total Return Index.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking diversified energy sector exposure. The ETF offers a cost-effective way to invest in a basket of energy stocks with a minimum investment of 1 unit (approximately Rs. 35.79).

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard listing procedures apply. Six market makers have been designated to ensure liquidity in the ETF.
