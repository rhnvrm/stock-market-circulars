## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544665, symbol GLOBALLOG, and ISIN INE1ITY01011. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Global Ocean Logistics India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544665
- Trading Symbol: GLOBALLOG
- ISIN Number: INE1ITY01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Global Ocean Logistics India Limited and await the separate notice containing the listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 23, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a standard listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to trading members who will need to update their systems with the new scrip information once the listing date is confirmed. The announcement provides preliminary details while awaiting final listing particulars. Market impact is minimal as this affects only one SME segment company.
