## Summary

Robert Bosch GmbH (Acquirer) along with Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (PAC) has announced an open offer to acquire up to 70,00,355 fully paid-up equity shares representing 25.75% of the share capital of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. The offer price is ₹1,762.54 per equity share. This open offer is being made in accordance with SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer**: Robert Bosch GmbH, a German limited liability company (registered in Stuttgart, Germany)
- **Person Acting in Concert (PAC)**: Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (Indian company)
- **Target Company**: Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited (CIN: L29300GJ1984PLC007470)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 70,00,355 equity shares (25.75% of share capital)
- **Offer Price**: ₹1,762.54 per equity share
- **Face Value**: ₹10 per equity share
- **Offer Type**: Cash offer to public shareholders
- **Regulatory Framework**: SEBI (SAST) Regulations pursuant to Regulation 3(1), 4, and 5(1)
- **Conditional Nature**: This is NOT a conditional offer under Regulation 19 and has no minimum acceptance level
- **Competing Offer**: This is NOT a competing offer under Regulation 20

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - this is a disclosure of an open offer under existing SEBI (SAST) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Public Shareholders**: Must review the Letter of Offer and decide whether to tender their equity shares
- **NRI and OCB Holders**: Must obtain all requisite approvals (including from RBI) before tendering shares in the offer
- **Recent Sellers**: If equity shares were recently sold, the Letter of Offer and Form of Acceptance-cum-Acknowledgement should be handed over to the stock exchange member through whom the sale was effected
- **Shareholder Action**: Public shareholders should consult stockbrokers, investment consultants, the Manager, or Registrar to the Offer for clarification on actions to be taken

## Important Dates

- **Offer Calculation Date**: The 26% minimum offer requirement is calculated as of the 10th working day from closure of tendering period
- **Specific Dates**: Tendering period dates and other timeline details to be provided in the complete Letter of Offer

## Impact Assessment

**Corporate Impact**: This represents a significant takeover action where Robert Bosch GmbH is acquiring substantial stake in Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. The offer covers all remaining public shareholding (25.75%), indicating a potential delisting or very high promoter holding scenario post-offer.

**Shareholder Impact**: Public shareholders have an opportunity to exit at ₹1,762.54 per share. The offer price represents the valuation determined under SEBI (SAST) Regulations and provides liquidity to minority shareholders.

**Market Impact**: This is a major corporate restructuring in the air conditioning industry segment, involving a global manufacturing conglomerate (Bosch) taking control of operations previously associated with Johnson Controls and Hitachi brands in India.

**Strategic Significance**: The acquisition aligns with Bosch's strategy in the HVAC sector and represents consolidation in the air conditioning manufacturing industry in India.
