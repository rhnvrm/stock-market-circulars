## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The issue comprises 100,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying a 10.40% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and maturing on August 22, 2033. The securities are rated CRISIL AA- and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 100,000 debentures (Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 976712, Scrip ID: 1040MFL33
- ISIN: INE549K08541 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face value: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- Issue price: Rs. 10,011.40
- Interest rate: 10.40% per annum (monthly payment)
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA-
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: December 4, 2025
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K08541
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing effective date**: December 5, 2025
- **First interest payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Interest payment frequency**: Monthly from December 31, 2025 to August 22, 2033
- **Redemption date**: August 22, 2033

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Muthoot Fincorp Limited with additional debt capital and offers institutional investors fixed-income instruments with AA- credit rating. The securities are privately placed and restricted to qualified institutional buyers, limiting broader market participation. No put or call options are available on these debentures.
