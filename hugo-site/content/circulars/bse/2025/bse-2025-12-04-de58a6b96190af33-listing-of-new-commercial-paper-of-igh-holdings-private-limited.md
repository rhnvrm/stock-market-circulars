## Summary

IGH Holdings Private Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE Debt segment effective December 4, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each. A total of 9000 units have been listed with ISIN INE02FN14705, carrying credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+. The instruments will mature on March 5, 2026. Axis Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity listed: 9000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,749 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730698
- Scrip ID: IGHP41225
- ISIN: INE02FN14705
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14705
- Trading must be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions must adhere to the tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarifications should contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are short-term debt instruments maturing in approximately 3 months. Trading is restricted to the debt segment with standard denomination requirements of Rs. 5 lakhs, limiting participation to institutional and high-net-worth investors. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate low credit risk. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
