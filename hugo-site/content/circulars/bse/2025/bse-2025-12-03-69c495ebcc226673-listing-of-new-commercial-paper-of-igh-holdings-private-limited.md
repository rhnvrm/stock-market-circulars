## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis effective December 3, 2025. The issue comprises 7000 units with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, maturing on March 2, 2026. Axis Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: IGH Holdings Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 7000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,927.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730685
- **Scrip ID**: IGHP31225
- **ISIN**: INE02FN14697
- **Credit Ratings**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14697
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are listed and admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment with limited market impact. The instrument is short-term (90-day maturity) and targeted at institutional investors. The dual A1+ ratings from CARE and CRISIL indicate highest safety for short-term instruments. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for client portfolio considerations.
