## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED with scrip code 977347 on the BSE Debt segment effective December 8, 2025. The securities comprise 9,000 debentures issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 11.75% per annum. The securities are rated ACUITE A-/Stable and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 9,000 debentures
- Scrip Code: 977347
- Scrip ID: 1175MGFL30
- ISIN: INE08KJ07167
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Interest Rate: 11.75% per annum
- Interest Payment Frequency: Half-yearly
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Put/Call Option: Not available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07167
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Half-yearly from May 26, 2026 to November 26, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 26, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a private placement of debt securities by MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED. The impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants interested in trading these specific securities
- Investors holding or seeking exposure to MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED debt instruments
- The securities offer a relatively high interest rate of 11.75% with an A- credit rating, indicating moderate credit risk
- The 5-year tenure provides medium-term investment opportunities in the green finance sector
