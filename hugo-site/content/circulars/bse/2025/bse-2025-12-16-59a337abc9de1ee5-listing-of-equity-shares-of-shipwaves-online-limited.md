## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Shipwaves Online Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544646, symbol SHIPWAVES, and ISIN number INE0O9L01010. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shipwaves Online Limited
- Scrip Code: 544646
- Trading Symbol: SHIPWAVES
- ISIN Number: INE0O9L01010

- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
