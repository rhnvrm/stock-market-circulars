## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Yaashavi Jewellers Limited on its SME website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period. The document was filed on December 25, 2025 and contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Yaashavi Jewellers Limited on December 25, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments solicited and should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Comment deadline: 21 days from December 26, 2025 (January 16, 2026)
- Document link: DraftProspectusYaashviJewellersLimited_20251225155205.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must address public feedback as part of IPO filing process

## Important Dates

- **Draft Document Filed**: December 25, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from December 26, 2025
- **Comment Deadline**: January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - affects only prospective investors and stakeholders interested in the Yaashavi Jewellers Limited SME IPO. This is a procedural step in the IPO process allowing public scrutiny of the offer document before final approval.
