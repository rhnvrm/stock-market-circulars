## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED (ISIN: INE945W07423, Code: 974070) effective November 20, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (Private Placement)
- ISIN: INE945W07423
- BSE Code: 974070 (IFSL-NIFTY-5-12-25-PVT)
- Record Date: November 20, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 20, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-760/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for trading suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 20, 2025
- All trading members of BSE are required to take note of this suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction for debentures with ISIN INE945W07423

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 20, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 20, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High for debenture holders of INCRED FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED. Trading will be completely suspended in these specific debentures from November 20, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent any transactions in these debentures. Investors holding these debentures should be aware that they cannot trade them from the suspension date.

**Financial Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of November 20, 2025 will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. The suspension ensures clean settlement for the corporate action.
