## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Sidh Automobiles Limited (Scrip Code: 539983, ISIN: INE403101015). The company, a Non-Deposit Accepting Non-Banking Finance Company based in Greater Kailash, New Delhi, can now resume normal trading operations on the Bombay Stock Exchange. The company has provided updated company profile information including details of directors, management, and operational status.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension has been revoked for Sidh Automobiles Limited
- Scrip Code: 539983, ISIN: INE403101015
- Company is registered as Non-Deposit Accepting NBFC
- Listed exclusively on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)
- Operational status: Active
- All listing fees have been paid to the exchange
- Company recently changed statutory auditors from M/s. Sumit Ranka & Associates to M/s. B. M. Gattani & Co.

## Regulatory Changes

The revocation of trading suspension restores normal trading privileges for the security. No significant changes in the primary line of business have occurred during the last five years. The company has completed the required compliance formalities to warrant the suspension revocation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has updated its company profile with BSE
- Listing fees have been paid to the exchange
- New statutory auditors M/s. B. M. Gattani & Co. (FRN: 113536W) have been appointed to fill casual vacancy
- No action has been taken against the company by any other exchange in the last five years

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 9, 2025
- Effective Date: Trading resumes as per exchange notification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The revocation allows shareholders and investors to resume trading in Sidh Automobiles Limited's equity shares. This restores liquidity for existing shareholders who were unable to trade during the suspension period.

**Operational Impact:** Positive for the company as it regains access to capital markets and normal trading operations, improving corporate image and shareholder confidence.

**Investor Impact:** Beneficial for existing shareholders who can now exit or add positions, and potential investors who can now participate in the stock.
