## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Namra Finance Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The securities are 11.40% Non-Convertible Debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, maturing on November 11, 2027, rated ACUITE A- by credit rating agency.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977292
- **Scrip ID**: 1140NFL27
- **ISIN**: INE229U07152
- **Quantity**: 4,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Interest Rate**: 11.40% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from December 31, 2025 to November 11, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE229U07152
- Members must adhere to the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Clarifications can be sought from BSE debt department at contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 11, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing affecting only debt market participants interested in Namra Finance Limited's securities.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and qualified investors who participated in the private placement. The ACUITE A- rating indicates adequate credit quality with moderate credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details for trading purposes.
