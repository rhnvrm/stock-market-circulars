## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544623), currently listed on the SME platform, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 17, 2025. This follows the exchange's earlier notice dated December 2, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: December 17, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251203-1
- Previous Related Notice: 20251202-47 dated December 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from restrictive Trade for Trade settlement to standard Rolling settlement, allowing for normal intraday trading and delivery mechanisms instead of compulsory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Prepare for normal rolling settlement procedures from December 17, 2025
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for clarifications or further details

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251202-47)
- **December 3, 2025**: Current notice date confirming the change
- **December 17, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

This change enhances trading flexibility for MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED shares by moving them from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the normal Rolling segment. This typically indicates the company has met post-listing requirements and allows for regular intraday trading, improved liquidity, and standard settlement cycles. Trading members and investors will benefit from enhanced trading mechanisms and reduced restrictions.
