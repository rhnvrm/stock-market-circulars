## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has announced that AU Small Finance Bank will now provide Electronic Bank Guarantee (E-BG) and Electronic Fixed Deposit Receipt (E-FDR) facilities to members effective November 24, 2025. This expands the list of banks offering these electronic collateral services, continuing the initiative started in November 2023.

## Key Points

- AU Small Finance Bank added to list of approved banks for E-BG and E-FDR facilities
- Facility available to members from November 24, 2025
- This is a continuation of circular no. 20231113-18 dated November 13, 2023
- Members can contact their Relationship Managers or Collateral Management team for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an expansion of existing electronic collateral acceptance framework to include an additional banking partner.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members wishing to use E-BG or E-FDR facilities from AU Small Finance Bank can now do so starting November 24, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Effective date for E-BG and E-FDR facility from AU Small Finance Bank

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a positive development that provides members with additional banking options for electronic collateral submission. It enhances operational flexibility and convenience for members using AU Small Finance Bank.

**Market Impact**: Minimal - No direct impact on trading or market operations. This is an administrative enhancement to collateral management processes.

**Member Action Required**: Optional - Members currently banking with AU Small Finance Bank may choose to utilize this facility for submitting collateral electronically.
