## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 784 dated December 26, 2025. The auction covers 103 securities across various scrips with quantities ranging from 1 share to 19,824 shares. This is a standard settlement auction process where securities that have failed to be delivered are auctioned to complete the settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 784
- Auction Date: December 26, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 103 different scrips
- Largest Quantity: EXCEL REALTY - 19,824 shares
- Other Significant Quantities: SHYAMKAMAL I (10,022 shares), MODI RUBBER (6,505 shares), ITC Hotels L (5,760 shares), ERAAYA LIFES (3,855 shares)
- Smallest Quantities: Multiple scrips with 1 share each

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and brokers with short positions in the listed securities must participate in the auction process
- Securities must be procured through the auction mechanism to complete settlement obligations
- Standard auction rules and settlement procedures apply as per BSE regulations

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: December 26, 2025
- Settlement No: 784

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 103 securities but with relatively small quantities for most scrips. The auction indicates delivery failures in these securities which may be due to various settlement issues.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers and clearing members holding short positions in these securities must participate in the auction to fulfill their settlement obligations. Failed deliveries may result in higher costs due to auction prices potentially being higher than market prices.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Retail investors are generally not directly affected unless they are counterparties to the failed trades. The auction ensures settlement completion and market integrity.
