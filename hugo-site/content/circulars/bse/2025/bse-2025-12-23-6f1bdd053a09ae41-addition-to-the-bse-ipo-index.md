## Summary

BSE announced the addition of KSH International Limited (Exchange ticker: 544664) to the BSE IPO Index, effective at the open of Friday, December 26, 2025. The stock was listed on BSE on Tuesday, December 23, 2025, as referenced in Notice No: 20251222-39.

## Key Points

- KSH International Limited (ticker 544664) listed on BSE on December 23, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective December 26, 2025 (at market open)
- Addition included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and passive investment vehicles benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: KSH International Limited listing date on BSE
- **December 26, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action affecting only the BSE IPO Index. The primary impact will be on:

- Index tracking funds that replicate the BSE IPO Index composition
- Passive investment strategies benchmarked to this index
- The specific stock (544664) may see additional demand from index-related buying

No broader market implications or changes to trading rules.
