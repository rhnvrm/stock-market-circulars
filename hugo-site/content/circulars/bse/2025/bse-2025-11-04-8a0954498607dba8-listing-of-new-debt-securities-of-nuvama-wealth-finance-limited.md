## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 4, 2025. The issue consists of 2,579 units of CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable rated debentures with ISIN INE918K07QE9, having a face value and issue price of Rs. 100,000 each, maturing on April 30, 2029.

## Key Points

- Security Name: NWFL-GOLDFUTURE-30-4-29-PVT
- Scrip Code: 977266 | Scrip ID: NWFL301025
- ISIN: INE918K07QE9
- Quantity: 2,579 units
- Face Value/Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: October 30, 2025
- Date of Redemption: April 30, 2029
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call option available
- Interest payment dates: Not specified (NA)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE918K07QE9
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: April 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing provides liquidity options for investors holding these privately placed debt securities. The securities carry a strong credit rating (CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable), indicating high credit quality. The impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and trading members dealing in debt securities. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
