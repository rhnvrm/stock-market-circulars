## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited on a private placement basis. The instrument consists of 5,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 250 crore), issued at a discount of Rs. 4,66,114.50 per unit. The commercial paper is rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with allotment date December 3, 2025 and redemption on November 30, 2026. Trading commenced on BSE Debt segment effective December 3, 2025.

- Market lot: 1 unit, standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited at competitive rates given the A1+ ratings. The listing expands debt market offerings but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt securities can access this instrument for their clients' portfolios.
