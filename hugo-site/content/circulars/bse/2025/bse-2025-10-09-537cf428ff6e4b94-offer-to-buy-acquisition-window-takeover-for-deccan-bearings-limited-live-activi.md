## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of DECCAN BEARINGS LIMITED. The offer will be live from October 10, 2025 to October 27, 2025, allowing eligible investors to tender up to 52,00,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share through the BSE OTB platform.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: DECCAN BEARING LIMITED
- **Security Symbol**: DECANBRG
- **ISIN**: INE498D01012
- **Scrip Code**: 534796
- **Offer Size**: Up to 52,00,000 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 10.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Friday, October 10, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Monday, October 27, 2025

## Trading Schedule

### Session Timings

- **Bid Offering Period**: October 10, 2025 to October 27, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: October 10, 2025 to October 24, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: October 27, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: October 27, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

### Investor Categories

## Compliance Requirements

1. **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must note the OTB schedule
2. **UCC Registration**: Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
3. **Demat Requirement**: Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
4. **File Generation**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
5. **Reference Circulars**: Members should refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Important Dates

- **October 10, 2025**: OTB window opens, bid offering period begins
- **October 24, 2025**: Last day for give-up/take-up (except last day)
- **October 27, 2025**: OTB window closes, last day for give-up (until 3:30 PM) and take-up (until 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact, specifically affecting shareholders of DECCAN BEARINGS LIMITED who wish to participate in the takeover offer. Trading members and custodians facilitating client participation must ensure proper UCC registration and compliance with OTB operational procedures. The 18-day window provides adequate time for eligible investors to tender their shares at the fixed offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share.
