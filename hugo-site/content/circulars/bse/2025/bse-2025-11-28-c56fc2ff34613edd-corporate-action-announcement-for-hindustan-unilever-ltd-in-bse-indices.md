## Summary

Hindustan Unilever Limited (Exchange Ticker: 500696, HINDUNILVR) is demerging its Ice Cream business into Kwality Wall's (India) Limited effective from start of trading hours on Friday, December 5, 2025. Shareholders will receive 1 equity share of Kwality Wall's (India) Limited for every 1 equity share held in Hindustan Unilever Limited. A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be held on December 5, 2025 by BSE Limited to enable price discovery of Hindustan Unilever Limited post-demerger. Kwality Wall's (India) Limited will be added to 22 BSE indices at zero price without divisor adjustment.

## Key Points

- Demerger of Ice Cream business from Hindustan Unilever Limited into Kwality Wall's (India) Limited
- Effective date: Start of trading hours on Friday, December 5, 2025
- Share exchange ratio: 1:1 (1 share of Kwality Wall's for every 1 share of HUL)
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) scheduled for December 5, 2025 for price discovery of HUL post-demerger
- Kwality Wall's (India) Limited expected to list on BSE at a future date
- Kwality Wall's to be added to 22 BSE indices at zero price after close of December 4, 2025
- No divisor adjustment for index inclusions

## Regulatory Changes

This is a scheme of arrangement resulting in a demerger, requiring special market procedures including a Special Pre-Open Session for price discovery. The addition of Kwality Wall's (India) Limited to multiple BSE indices represents a significant change to index composition.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must prepare for the demerger effective December 5, 2025
- Index tracking funds and portfolios need to accommodate the addition of Kwality Wall's (India) Limited to 22 BSE indices
- Price discovery through Special Pre-Open Session must be followed on December 5, 2025
- For queries, clients should reach out to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 4, 2025 (Thursday)**: Last trading day before demerger; Kwality Wall's added to indices at zero price after market close
- **December 5, 2025 (Friday)**: Demerger effective from start of trading hours; Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) for HUL price discovery
- **Future date (TBD)**: Listing of Kwality Wall's (India) Limited equity shares on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This demerger affects a major SENSEX constituent and impacts 22 BSE indices including flagship indices like BSE SENSEX, BSE 100, BSE 200, and BSE 500. The Special Pre-Open Session is necessary to establish fair pricing post-corporate action.

**Investor Impact**: High - All shareholders of Hindustan Unilever Limited will receive equivalent shares in the newly formed entity, effectively splitting their investment across two companies. This creates a new investment opportunity in the standalone ice cream business.

**Index Impact**: High - Kwality Wall's (India) Limited will be added to 22 BSE indices including:
- BSE SENSEX
- BSE 100, BSE 200, BSE 500
- BSE FAST MOVING CONSUMER GOODS
- BSE INDIA MANUFACTURING INDEX
- BSE ALLCAP, BSE LARGECAP
- BSE SENSEX 50, BSE SENSEX SIXTY
- Various thematic and strategy indices

The zero price addition without divisor adjustment is a standard procedure for demerger situations to maintain index continuity.
