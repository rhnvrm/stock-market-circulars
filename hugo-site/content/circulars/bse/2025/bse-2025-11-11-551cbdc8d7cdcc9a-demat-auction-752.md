## Summary

BSE has announced the demat auction for Settlement No. 752 dated November 11, 2025. The auction covers 80+ securities across various scrip codes with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 1.4 million shares. This is a routine settlement auction for securities where delivery shortages have occurred.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 752
- Auction Date: November 11, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 80+ scrips
- Largest Auction Quantity: STANDARD CAP (1,490,020 shares)
- Other Significant Quantities: REMEDIUM LIF (586,981 shares), GACM Technol (88,267 shares), Vantage Know (35,000 shares)
- Multiple blue-chip stocks included: INFOSYS (30 shares), WIPRO (289 shares), BHARTI AIRTEL (1 share), BIOCON (10 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard demat auction settlement notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants with outstanding obligations in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement guidelines
- Brokers and clearing members must ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 11, 2025 (Settlement No. 752)
- Auction participants must complete their obligations by the settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The auction affects multiple securities with varying quantities, indicating delivery shortages across diverse market segments.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Brokers with short positions in the listed securities will need to participate in the auction, potentially at premium prices. The large quantities in certain scrips (STANDARD CAP, REMEDIUM LIF, GACM Technol) suggest significant settlement obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Individual investors are generally not directly affected unless their brokers have failed to deliver their securities. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and protects buyer interests.
