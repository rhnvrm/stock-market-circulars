## Summary

JM Financial Home Loans Limited has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE01A207153, Scrip Code: 975294) along with interest payment. The record date has been fixed as December 12, 2025, and trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: JM Financial Home Loans Limited
- Security: Reset Rate Debentures maturing on December 29, 2027 (Private)
- ISIN: INE01A207153
- Scrip Code: 975294
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- New Face Value: Rs. 50,000 per debenture (reduced)
- Effective Date of Reduced Face Value: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 50,000 per debenture effective from December 12, 2025
- All trading systems and records must be updated to reflect the new face value from the effective date
- Members should ensure proper processing of the part redemption and interest payment for debenture holders as per the record date

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-776/2025-2026)
- **December 29, 2027**: Original maturity date of debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects trading of specific debenture series of JM Financial Home Loans Limited. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. The face value reduction will affect the nominal value of holdings and future coupon calculations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading platforms and back-office systems must be updated to reflect the new face value from the effective date to ensure accurate trade processing and settlement.
