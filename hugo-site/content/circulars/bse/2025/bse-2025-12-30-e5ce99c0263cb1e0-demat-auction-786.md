## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 786 dated December 30, 2025. The auction includes 102 different securities from various companies where delivery obligations need to be fulfilled through the auction mechanism. The quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 263,431 shares for TIMEX GROUP, with significant volumes also noted for securities like PRAKASH STEELAGE (73,002 shares), PATEL CHEM SPECIALITIES (20,800 shares), and PULSAR INTERNATIONAL (14,740 shares).

## Key Points

- Total of 102 securities included in the demat auction
- Settlement number: 786
- Auction date: December 30, 2025
- Highest quantity: TIMEX GROUP with 263,431 shares
- Other significant volumes include PRAKASH STEELAGE (73,002), PATEL CHEM SPECIALITIES (20,800), PULSAR INTERNATIONAL (14,740), and K K SILK MILLS (9,000)
- Mix of large-cap stocks like INFOSYS, TITAN COMPANY, VEDANTA alongside mid and small-cap securities
- Includes shares from banking sector (BANK OF BARODA, CANARA BANK, UNION BANK, IDFC FIRST BANK, CITY UNION BANK)
- Mutual fund securities also included (HDFC MUTUAL, BANDHAN MUTUAL, ICICI PRUDENTIAL, SBI MUTUAL)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational auction process conducted by BSE for securities where delivery obligations could not be met in the normal settlement process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with delivery shortfalls must participate in the demat auction
- Securities must be delivered as per the quantities specified in the auction list
- Settlement must be completed through the auction mechanism for the listed securities
- Members are required to follow BSE's standard auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 786

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 102 different securities across various sectors. While most quantities are relatively small, some securities like TIMEX GROUP (263,431 shares), PRAKASH STEELAGE (73,002 shares), and others with large volumes could experience price volatility during the auction process.

**Operational Impact:** Affects trading members who have failed to deliver securities in the normal settlement cycle. These members will need to procure shares through the auction mechanism, potentially at premium prices, which may result in financial penalties or losses.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of the listed securities may observe temporary price movements during the auction. The inclusion of well-known stocks like INFOSYS, TITAN COMPANY, VEDANTA, and major banks suggests broader market participation in this settlement cycle.

**Sectoral Distribution:** The auction covers diverse sectors including banking, technology, metals, mutual funds, power, infrastructure, and consumer goods, indicating widespread delivery shortfalls across market segments rather than sector-specific issues.
