## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture series.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MRHFL debentures with ISIN INE950O08287 (Code: 974833)
- Debenture series: MRHFL-8.315%-12-12-25-PVT
- Record date set for November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from November 27, 2025
- Notice number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debenture from November 27, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Compliance with record date requirements for determining eligible holders for redemption and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date; trading suspension begins
- **December 12, 2025**: Implied maturity/redemption date based on debenture nomenclature

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This affects only holders of this specific debenture series and does not impact equity trading or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders. Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard corporate action procedure with no unusual requirements for market participants.
