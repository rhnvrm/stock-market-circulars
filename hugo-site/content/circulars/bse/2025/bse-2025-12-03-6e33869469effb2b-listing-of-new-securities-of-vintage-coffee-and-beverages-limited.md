## Summary

BSE has listed 14,355,000 equity shares of Vintage Coffee and Beverages Limited (Scrip Code: 538920) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from December 4, 2025. The shares were allotted at a premium of Rs. 114 per share on a face value of Rs. 10.

## Key Points

- 14,355,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each issued at premium of Rs. 114
- Total issue price: Rs. 124 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 130083410 to 144438409
- ISIN: INE498Q01014
- Trading commences: December 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the listing of new securities for trading purposes
- All 14,355,000 shares are subject to lock-in period restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment of shares. The lock-in period until June 30, 2026, means these shares cannot be traded freely until after that date. The listing increases the total tradable equity base of Vintage Coffee and Beverages Limited, though the immediate market impact is limited due to the lock-in restrictions. Trading members can facilitate transactions in these securities subject to applicable lock-in provisions.
