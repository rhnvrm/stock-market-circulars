## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (DR-788/2025-2026) informing trading members that debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited will be suspended from trading effective December 31, 2025, due to the securities reaching their maturity date on January 1, 2026. The affected instrument is AIHFPL-12.29%-1-1-26-PVT with ISIN INE0E2307146 and Scrip Code 974102.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from December 31, 2025
- Debt security maturing on January 1, 2026
- ISIN: INE0E2307146
- Scrip Code: 974102
- Instrument: AIHFPL-12.29%-1-1-26-PVT (12.29% coupon rate)
- Issuer: AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited
- Notice Number: 20251230-6
- Reference: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited from December 31, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and adjust their trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: January 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects a single privately placed debt instrument of a housing finance company. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this specific scrip code. Standard maturity procedure with one day advance notice.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing holders of this specific debt instrument who will receive redemption proceeds at maturity. No trading opportunity loss as securities are maturing imminently.
