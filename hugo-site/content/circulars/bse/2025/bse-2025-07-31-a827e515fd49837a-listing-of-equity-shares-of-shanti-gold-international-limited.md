## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Shanti Gold International Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544459, symbol SHANTIGOLD, and ISIN number INE06ZD01017.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shanti Gold International Limited
- Scrip Code: 544459
- Symbol: SHANTIGOLD
- ISIN Number: INE06ZD01017
- Listing venue: BSE Limited
- Status: Listing approved, trading to commence soon

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Trading Commencement: To be announced with listing date

## Impact Assessment

This listing will provide trading members and investors with access to trade Shanti Gold International Limited shares on BSE. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business fundamentals and market conditions at the time of listing commencement.
