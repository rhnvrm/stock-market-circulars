## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Paisalo Digital Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 11, 2025. The securities comprise 4,000 units with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 8.5% interest rate per annum, and maturing on December 9, 2028. The securities are rated IVR AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4,000 units
- Scrip Code: 977358
- Scrip ID: 85PDL28
- ISIN: INE420C07163
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 8.5% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: IVR AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C07163
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: December 11, 2025
- First Interest Payment: March 9, 2026
- Interest Payment Frequency: Quarterly (from March 9, 2026 to December 9, 2028)
- Maturity/Redemption Date: December 9, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of a single company. The impact is limited to:
- Investors holding or interested in Paisalo Digital Limited debt instruments
- Trading members dealing with corporate debt securities
- Enhanced financing options for Paisalo Digital Limited through debt market

The securities carry a strong credit rating (IVR AA/Stable), indicating low credit risk for investors.
