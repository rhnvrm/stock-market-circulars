## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of 26,66,66,663 equity shares of Swiggy Limited (Scrip Code: 544285, ISIN: INE00H001014) with effect from Tuesday, December 16, 2025. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 375/- per share. The newly issued shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 26,66,66,663 equity shares of Re. 1/- each face value listed
- Allotted to QIBs through Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- Issue price: Rs. 375/- per share
- Total capital raised: Approximately Rs. 10,000 crores
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 2493646893 to 2760313555
- Scrip Code: 544285
- ISIN: INE00H001014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for securities issued through QIP route as per SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities for trading purposes
- Standard trading procedures apply
- Shares have full fungibility with existing equity shares
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE officials (Mr. Tejas Tandel: 022 2272 8721)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 13, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 16, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The QIP represents a significant capital raise for Swiggy Limited, with approximately Rs. 10,000 crores raised through this institutional placement. The addition of 26.67 crore shares will result in material dilution for existing shareholders.

**Liquidity Impact**: The substantial increase in free float will improve liquidity in Swiggy's stock, making it more accessible for institutional and retail investors.

**Price Impact**: The issue price of Rs. 375/- per share provides a reference point. Market participants should monitor trading activity post-listing to assess demand-supply dynamics.

**Operational Impact**: The capital raised through this QIP will likely be used for business expansion, technology investments, and working capital requirements, strengthening Swiggy's competitive position in the food delivery and quick commerce sectors.
