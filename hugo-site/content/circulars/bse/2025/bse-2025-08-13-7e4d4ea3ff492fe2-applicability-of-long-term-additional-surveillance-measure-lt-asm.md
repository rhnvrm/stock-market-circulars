## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 14, 2025. The circular provides lists of securities entering the framework, moving between stages, and exiting the surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- 3 new securities added to Long Term ASM Framework
- 1 security moved to higher Stage II ASM
- 1 security (Spice Islands Industries Ltd) moved out of LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- Comprehensive consolidated list of all securities under various ASM stages provided

## Securities Entering LT-ASM Framework

**Part A - New Entries:**
- Jayaswal Neco Industries Ltd (522285)
- Spright Agro Ltd (531205) 
- Vintron Informatics Ltd (517393)

## Stage Changes Within Framework

**Part B - Moving to Higher Stage:**
- Ambitious Plastomac Company Ltd (526439) - moved to ASM Stage II

**Part C - Moving to Lower Stage:**
- Nil

**Part D - Direct Stage IV Entries:**
- Nil

## Securities Exiting LT-ASM Framework

**Part E - Moving Out:**
- Spice Islands Industries Ltd (526827) - moved out due to inclusion in ESM Framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Securities under LT-ASM framework face enhanced surveillance measures including potential price bands, margin requirements, and trading restrictions. The movement of securities between stages indicates ongoing monitoring of market behavior and compliance with regulatory requirements.
