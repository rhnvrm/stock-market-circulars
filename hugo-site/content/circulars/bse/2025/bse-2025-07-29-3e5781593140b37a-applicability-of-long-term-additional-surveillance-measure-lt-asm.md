## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective July 30, 2025. Three securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the LT-ASM framework, while no securities are moving out or changing stages within the framework.

## Key Points

- 3 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework
- Consolidated list includes multiple securities across different ASM stages
- Framework includes provisions for T+0 scrips and various exclusion criteria

## Regulatory Changes

The Long Term ASM framework continues with updated security lists. Securities under this framework face enhanced surveillance measures including potential trading restrictions and additional monitoring requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ASM framework requirements for listed securities
- Enhanced due diligence and monitoring for securities under surveillance
- Adherence to specific trading conditions as applicable to each ASM stage

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025 - New LT-ASM classifications come into effect

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of three securities in the LT-ASM framework will subject them to enhanced surveillance measures, potentially affecting trading volumes and investor participation. The measure is designed to protect investor interests by monitoring securities that may require additional oversight due to various risk factors.
