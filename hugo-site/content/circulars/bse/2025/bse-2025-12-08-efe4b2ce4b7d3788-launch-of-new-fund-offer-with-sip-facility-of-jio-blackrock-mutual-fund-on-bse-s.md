## Summary

BSE announces the launch of JIOBLACKROCK ARBITRAGE FUND - NFO (New Fund Offer) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from December 09-11, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 16, 2025. The circular provides detailed operational guidelines for transactions, payment timelines, and fund settlement requirements.

## Key Points

- JIOBLACKROCK ARBITRAGE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (Equity) with ISIN INF22M001101 will be available for subscription
- NFO period: December 09-11, 2025
- Tentative allotment date: December 16, 2025
- Lumpsum purchase extended till 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 16, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds receipt by allotment date
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification under existing BSE StAR MF Platform operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 16, 2025 for allotment
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Switch transactions require funds receipt under NFO scheme before the allotment date for consideration
- Pending transactions will remain in system till 3:00 PM on last payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Important Dates

- **December 09, 2025**: NFO launch date
- **December 11, 2025**: NFO closure date (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication, 6:00 PM for lumpsum purchases)
- **December 16, 2025**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds required in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
- **T+1 Day**: Deadline for client fund initiation at 9:00 AM (post-transaction)
- **T+5 or Last Payout Day**: Pending transaction retention deadline at 3:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational notification for BSE StAR MF Platform members regarding a new mutual fund offering. The impact is limited to:

- **Operational Impact**: Members and distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform need to follow specified timelines for transaction processing
- **Investor Access**: Provides new investment opportunity in arbitrage fund category for investors
- **Platform Activity**: Expected to generate transaction activity on BSE StAR MF Platform during the three-day NFO period
- **No Direct Market Impact**: Does not affect equity, debt, or derivative markets; limited to mutual fund distribution segment
