## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Reliance Retail Ventures Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities comprise 34,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 17,000 crores, with a 90-day tenure from September 9, 2025 to December 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 34,000 units (total value Rs. 17,000 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730168 (RRVL90925)
- ISIN: INE929O14EB6
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for corporate funding needs. The securities will provide short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
