## Summary

SBICAP Securities Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 8,000 units of commercial paper with face value Rs. 5,00,000/- each issued
- Scrip Code: 730726, Scrip ID: SSL91225
- ISIN Number: INE212K14CY1
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,954/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE212K14CY1

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term debt investment options for institutional investors with a 91-day maturity period. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
