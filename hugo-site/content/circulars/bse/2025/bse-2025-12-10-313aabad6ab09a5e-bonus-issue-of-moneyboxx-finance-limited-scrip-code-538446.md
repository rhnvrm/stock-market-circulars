## Summary

Moneyboxx Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 538446) has announced a bonus issue in the ratio of 1:1, meaning shareholders will receive 1 new equity share of Rs. 10/- for every 1 existing equity share held. The Board of Directors has fixed December 15, 2025 as the record date. A total of 3,64,42,345 bonus shares will be issued, including 37,37,745 shares reserved for convertible securities holders.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 1 new equity share for every 1 existing equity share (1:1)
- Total bonus shares to be issued: 3,64,42,345 shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Reserved shares for convertible securities holders: 37,37,745 shares
- Bonus shares will be credited to beneficiary accounts by 12 PM on allotment date
- Shares will be available for trading on the next working day after allotment

## Regulatory Changes

None specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company undertakes to credit bonus shares to beneficiary accounts by 12 PM on the deemed allotment date (December 16, 2025)
- Shareholders must hold shares as of the record date to be eligible for bonus shares

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: Monday, December 15, 2025
- **Deemed Allotment Date**: Monday, December 16, 2025 (by 12 PM)
- **Trading Commencement**: Wednesday, December 17, 2025
- **Circular Date**: December 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - The 1:1 bonus issue will double the number of outstanding shares, making the stock more liquid and accessible to retail investors. The share price will adjust proportionately post-bonus.

**Shareholder Impact**: Positive - Existing shareholders will see their shareholding doubled without any cash outlay. This is typically viewed as a positive signal of company confidence in future prospects.

**Liquidity Impact**: The increase in share count will improve liquidity and potentially increase trading volumes, making the stock more attractive to institutional and retail investors.
