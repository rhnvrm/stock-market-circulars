## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice regarding the partial redemption of debentures issued by Adani Airport Holdings Limited (AAHL). This circular updates the previous notice dated October 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20251020-33) with the actual reduced face value following the part redemption. The debentures with scrip code 976065 (ISIN: INE0GCN07047) will trade at the revised reduced face value of Rs. 77,515 per debenture effective November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Adani Airport Holdings Limited
- Scrip Code: 976065
- ISIN: INE0GCN07047
- Debenture Type: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT (9.35% coupon rate, maturity September 8, 2028)
- Revised Reduced Face Value: Rs. 77,515 per debenture
- This is a revision to the earlier notice dated October 20, 2025
- Reference Number: DR-752/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action notification updating previously communicated information about debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised reduced face value
- Trading Members must ensure debentures are traded at the revised face value of Rs. 77,515 per debenture from the effective date
- All trading systems and records must be updated to reflect the new face value

## Important Dates

- **Previous Notice Date**: October 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20251020-33)
- **Revised Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Effective Date for Revised Face Value Trading**: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This revision affects holders of Adani Airport Holdings Limited debentures and trading members who deal with these debt securities. The updated face value information ensures accurate pricing and settlement.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems with the corrected reduced face value before November 10, 2025 to ensure proper trading and settlement of these debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will see their holdings reflected at the revised reduced face value post-redemption, affecting the notional value of their investments while the actual redemption proceeds remain as per the terms of the debenture.
