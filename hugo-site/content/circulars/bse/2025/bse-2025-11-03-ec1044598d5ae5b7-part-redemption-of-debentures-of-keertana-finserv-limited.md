## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Keertana Finserv Limited has fixed record dates for part redemption and interest payment on two series of non-convertible debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values from the specified effective dates in November 2025.

## Key Points

- Two series of Keertana Finserv Limited debentures undergoing part redemption
- KFL-11.05%-20-2-26-PVT (ISIN: INE0NES07089) face value reduced to Rs. 12,500 per debenture
- KFL-11.40%-26-5-27-PVT (ISIN: INE0NES07147) face value reduced to Rs. 60,000 per debenture
- Both series include interest payment along with part redemption
- Trading will continue with reduced face values from effective dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for trading purposes
- Trading in affected debentures must reflect reduced face values from the effective dates
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face values for settlement purposes

## Important Dates

**First Series (KFL-11.05%-20-2-26-PVT):**
- Record Date: November 10, 2025
- Effective Date: November 10, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-752/2025-2026
- New Face Value: Rs. 12,500 per debenture

**Second Series (KFL-11.40%-26-5-27-PVT):**
- Record Date: November 11, 2025
- Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-753/2025-2026
- New Face Value: Rs. 60,000 per debenture

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only holders of specific Keertana Finserv Limited debenture series.

**Investor Impact:** Medium for affected debenture holders who will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payments. The reduced face values indicate capital return to investors.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face values for accurate trading and settlement of these securities from the respective effective dates.
