## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-bills) that are scheduled to mature on November 13, 2025. Trading Members are advised not to deal in these securities effective from November 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three T-bills will be suspended from trading due to upcoming maturity
- Suspension effective from November 11, 2025
- Maturity date for all three securities: November 13, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-753/2025-2026

## Securities Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 804988 | IN002024Z313 | 364TB131125 |
| 805065 | IN002025Y073 | 182T131125 |
| 805103 | IN002025X208 | 91TB131125 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the mentioned T-bills from November 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- All outstanding positions should be settled as per normal maturity procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational measure affecting the debt segment. Trading Members holding positions in these T-bills should prepare for maturity settlement. The suspension ensures orderly redemption without trading activity interfering with the maturity process. Impact is limited to participants holding or trading these specific T-bills.
