## Summary

BSE has announced that Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6762) will voluntarily cease all trading operations effective November 27, 2025. The closure affects the broker's participation in Cash, Equity derivatives, and Currency derivative segments.

## Key Points

- Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6762) is voluntarily closing its business
- Closure affects Cash segment, Equity derivatives segment, and Currency derivative segment
- Effective date: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)
- Trading members are informed of this closure
- Clarifications can be sought via email: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational circular.

## Important Dates

- **November 26, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of business closure

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative matter involving a single trading member's voluntary decision to cease operations. Other market participants are unaffected beyond awareness of the member's exit. Clients of Share Wise Equity Brokers Private Limited will need to transfer their accounts to other brokers.
