## Summary

LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, dated December 4, 2025, post IPO issue. The company has 2 promoter shareholders holding 4,406,300 equity shares and 1,285 public shareholders holding 3,061,600 equity shares. The company has locked-in shares held by promoters and public shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total promoter holding: 4,406,300 equity shares held by 2 shareholders
- Total public holding: 3,061,600 equity shares held by 1,285 shareholders
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Locked-in shares exist for both promoter and public shareholders (typical post-IPO requirement)
- No shares pledged or encumbered under non-disposal undertaking
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI LODR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR 2015 for post-IPO shareholding pattern
- Declaration of share capital structure including locked-in shares, pledged shares, and convertible securities
- Disclosure of promoter and public shareholding percentages
- Compliance with lock-in requirements as per IPO regulations

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** December 4, 2025 (Post IPO issue)
- Report pertains to shareholding structure immediately following IPO allotment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine disclosure for a newly listed company establishing its initial shareholding structure post-IPO.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency on ownership structure with approximately 59% promoter holding and 41% public holding (estimated based on share numbers). Locked-in shares ensure promoter commitment during the initial listing period.

**Operational Impact:** None. Standard compliance activity with no operational implications.
