## Summary

BSE has notified that Provident Cedar Private Limited will partially redeem its debentures (ISIN: INE0NF907031, Scrip Code: 974311) with a reduced face value of Rs. 265,637.45 per debenture. The debentures will trade at the new reduced face value from December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Provident Cedar Private Limited
- Debenture ISIN: INE0NF907031
- Scrip Code: 974311 (PCPL-17-4-28-PVT)
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 265,637.45 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 31, 2025
- Reference Number: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification for partial redemption of existing debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to:
- Take note of the part redemption
- Trade the debentures at the reduced face value of Rs. 265,637.45 per debenture from December 31, 2025 onwards

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading at Reduced Face Value**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact, affecting only holders of specific debentures of Provident Cedar Private Limited. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new reduced face value for accurate trading and settlement.
