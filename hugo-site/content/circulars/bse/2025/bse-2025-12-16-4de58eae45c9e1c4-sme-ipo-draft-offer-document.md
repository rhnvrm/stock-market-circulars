## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Peshwa Wheat Limited on the SME Exchange website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 and will be available for at least 21 days from December 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Peshwa Wheat Limited
- Date of Filing: December 16, 2025
- Document Type: Draft Offer Document for SME IPO
- Compliance: As per Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Public Comment Period: 21 days from notice date
- Document hosted on: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments to be submitted at: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard filing under existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 16, 2025
- Comments should be sent to email: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Public Comment Deadline**: January 6, 2026 (21 days from filing date)
- **Document Availability**: Minimum 21 days on SME Exchange website

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. The impact is confined to:

- Potential investors interested in Peshwa Wheat Limited
- Market participants tracking SME IPO pipeline
- Regulatory compliance stakeholders monitoring SEBI (ICDR) adherence

The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement allowing public scrutiny before final offer document approval.
