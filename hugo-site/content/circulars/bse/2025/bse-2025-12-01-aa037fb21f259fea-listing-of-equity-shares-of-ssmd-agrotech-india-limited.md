## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544621, symbol SSMD, and ISIN number INE1U2R01013. This is a standard listing notification for the SME segment, with the exact listing date to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544621
- Trading Symbol: SSMD
- ISIN Number: INE1U2R01013
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date will be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members. This is an informational notice about an upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 1, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate market impact as it is a standard listing announcement for an SME segment company. The listing will add one more tradeable security to the BSE SME platform. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business fundamentals and investor interest when trading commences.
