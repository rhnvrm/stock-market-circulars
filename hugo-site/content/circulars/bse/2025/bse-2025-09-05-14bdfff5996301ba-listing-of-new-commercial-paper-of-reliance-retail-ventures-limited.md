## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis effective September 5, 2025. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 33,500 units totaling Rs. 167.5 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 33,500 units
- Scrip code: 730153, Scrip ID: RRVL4925
- ISIN: INE929O14EC4
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Listing date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding instrument for institutional investors in Reliance Retail Ventures Limited commercial paper.
