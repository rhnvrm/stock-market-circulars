## Summary

BSE has prohibited and suspended trading in the securities of Quasar India Ltd (Scrip Code: 538452) effective December 3, 2025. The action follows the company's failure to respond to a show cause notice and findings that the company is non-existent at its registered office address during an exchange site visit.

## Key Points

- Company failed to provide any response to BSE's show cause notice
- Exchange conducted site visit and found company non-existent at registered office address
- Trading prohibition/suspension implemented as interim measure
- Action applies to all securities of Quasar India Ltd
- Scrip Code: 538452

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing compliance requirements for listed companies to maintain valid registered office and respond to exchange communications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in Quasar India Ltd securities
- Investors cannot buy or sell shares of Quasar India Ltd on BSE platform
- Trading members should inform their clients holding positions in this scrip

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Existing shareholders of Quasar India Ltd will be unable to liquidate their positions through BSE. The scrip will have zero liquidity on the exchange.

**Investor Impact**: High - Current investors face complete loss of trading ability. The non-existence at registered office raises serious concerns about company operations and asset recovery.

**Compliance Signal**: This action serves as a strong warning about the consequences of non-compliance with basic listing requirements including maintaining valid registered office and responding to exchange communications.
