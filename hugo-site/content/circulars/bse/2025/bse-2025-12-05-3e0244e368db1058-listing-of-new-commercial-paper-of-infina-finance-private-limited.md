## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on its Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The issue consists of 5000 Commercial Papers of face value Rs.5,00,000/- each issued on private placement basis with credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities are assigned Scrip Code 730706 and ISIN INE879F14MC2.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Issue Price: Rs.4,65,901/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730706
- Scrip ID: IFPL041225
- ISIN: INE879F14MC2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MC2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective December 5, 2025

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 5, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: December 4, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing on private placement basis affecting only debt market participants dealing with Infina Finance Private Limited instruments. The listing expands debt market offerings but does not affect broader market operations or equity segments. Trading members interested in debt instruments can contact BSE debt department at specified contact numbers for clarifications.
