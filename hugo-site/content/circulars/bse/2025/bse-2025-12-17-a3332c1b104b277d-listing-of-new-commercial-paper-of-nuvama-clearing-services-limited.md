## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED on private placement basis with effect from December 17, 2025. The instrument consists of 5500 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, with redemption scheduled for March 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 5500 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,942.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730796
- Scrip ID: NCSL171225
- ISIN: INE525L14166
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that:
- The securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE525L14166
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for debt instruments with minimal market impact. The commercial paper listing provides an additional short-term debt instrument for investors in the debt segment. The instrument has high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating low credit risk. Trading members are simply required to be aware of the new instrument's availability for trading on the BSE Debt segment.
