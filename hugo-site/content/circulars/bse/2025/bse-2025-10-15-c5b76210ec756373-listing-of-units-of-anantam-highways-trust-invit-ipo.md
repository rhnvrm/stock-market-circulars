## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that units of Anantam Highways Trust (InvIT - IPO) will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange. The trust has been assigned scrip code 544579, trading symbol ANANTAM, and ISIN INE1UA823019. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Anantam Highways Trust InvIT units approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544579
- Trading Symbol: ANANTAM
- ISIN: INE1UA823019
- This is an IPO listing for an Infrastructure Investment Trust
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details for Anantam Highways Trust and prepare their systems for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) segment on BSE, providing investors with additional options for infrastructure sector exposure. The listing of Anantam Highways Trust units will enable public market participation in highway infrastructure assets. Trading members should prepare for the upcoming listing once the specific date is confirmed. The medium impact reflects the addition of a new investment vehicle in the infrastructure sector.
