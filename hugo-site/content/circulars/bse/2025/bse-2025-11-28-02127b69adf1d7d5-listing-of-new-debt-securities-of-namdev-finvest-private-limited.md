## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED on its Debt segment effective November 28, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with allotment date of November 27, 2025. A total of 3,500 debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each have been listed under ISIN INE0IX207254 (further listing under same ISIN). The securities carry an interest rate of 11.30% per annum with quarterly interest payments and mature on September 30, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Quantity**: 3,500 debentures
- **Scrip Code**: 977177
- **Scrip ID**: 1130NFPL28
- **Detail Name**: NFPL-11.30%-30-09-28-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE0IX207254 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,01,795.62
- **Credit Rating**: CARE BBB+/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 11.30% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from December 30, 2025 to September 30, 2028
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE0IX207254
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 30, 2025
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Quarterly until September 30, 2028
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: September 30, 2028

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with limited market-wide implications. The impact is confined to specific investors and debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt investors seeking exposure to NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED with CARE BBB+/Stable rated instruments offering 11.30% annual returns with quarterly interest payments.
