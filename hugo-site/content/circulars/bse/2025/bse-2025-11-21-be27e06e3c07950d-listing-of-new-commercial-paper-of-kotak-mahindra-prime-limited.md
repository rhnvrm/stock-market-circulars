## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 21, 2025. The issue comprises 7,500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 375 crore. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 7,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,72,103 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 375 crore
- Scrip Code: 730616
- Scrip ID: KMPL211125
- ISIN Number: INE916D146C9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE916D146C9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 21, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 7, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 10.5 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper on BSE's debt segment with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and enhances liquidity in the debt market. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
