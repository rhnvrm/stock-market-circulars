## Summary

Jana Small Finance Bank Limited has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 250 crore on BSE's Debt segment effective October 20, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1 crore per unit, carrying an interest rate of 10.55% per annum with annual interest payments and maturity on October 16, 2035. The securities are rated IND A/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 250 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977229, Scrip ID: 1055JSFB35
- ISIN: INE953L08337
- Face Value/Paid-up Value/Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Interest Rate: 10.55% per annum
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE953L08337
- Members should apply the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Members can refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: October 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from October 16, 2026 to October 16, 2035
- Redemption Date: October 16, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Jana Small Finance Bank Limited with access to debt capital markets and offers institutional investors a rated debt instrument with a 10-year tenure. The private placement nature and institutional ticket size (Rs. 1 crore per unit) means this will not affect retail investors or broader market operations. No immediate action required by market participants beyond awareness of the new listing.
