## Summary

Three companies - Shyam Century Ferrous Limited, Genesys International Corporation Limited, and InfoBeans Technologies Ltd - responded to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed they have no undisclosed material information and that the movements are market-driven. The companies reiterated their compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations regarding timely disclosures.

## Key Points

- Shyam Century Ferrous Limited (Scrip Code: 539252, Symbol: SHYAMCENT) responded on December 6, 2025 to NSE and BSE queries about price movement
- Genesys International Corporation Limited (Scrip Code: 506109) responded on December 8, 2025 to BSE query about volume spurt
- InfoBeans Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 543644, Symbol: INFOBEAN) responded on December 8, 2025 to BSE query about price movement
- All companies confirmed no significant incident, news, or development that may affect security prices
- Companies stated that price/volume increases are purely market-driven
- All companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies confirmed no material information has been withheld that may have bearing on operations or performance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular documents routine surveillance clarifications under existing SEBI Listing Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed material information exists under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Companies must clarify if any price-sensitive information is pending disclosure
- Ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 regarding timely disclosure of material events

## Important Dates

- December 5, 2025: NSE and BSE sent surveillance queries to Shyam Century Ferrous Limited
- December 6, 2025: Shyam Century Ferrous Limited submitted clarification
- December 8, 2025: BSE sent surveillance queries to Genesys International and InfoBeans Technologies
- December 8, 2025: Both Genesys International and InfoBeans Technologies submitted clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications confirming no hidden material events. The unusual price/volume movements mentioned are attributed to normal market dynamics rather than company-specific developments.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency to investors that the recent price/volume movements in these three stocks are not due to undisclosed corporate events or material information. Reinforces that companies are complying with disclosure norms.

**Operational Impact:** None. This is standard surveillance mechanism by exchanges to ensure market integrity and proper information dissemination.

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates the surveillance framework is functioning as intended, with exchanges actively monitoring unusual trading patterns and companies responding promptly to clarification requests.
