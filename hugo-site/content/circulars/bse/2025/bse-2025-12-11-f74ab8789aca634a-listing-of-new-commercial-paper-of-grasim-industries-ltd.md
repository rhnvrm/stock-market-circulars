## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The issue comprises 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with a 90-day tenure maturing on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 10,000 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 500 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730743
- **Scrip ID**: GIL111225
- **ISIN**: INE047A14AQ2
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Format**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the Commercial Paper only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE047A14AQ2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) that provides additional trading options in the debt segment but has no direct impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard listing procedure for debt securities with no changes to existing trading infrastructure or processes. Trading members can access the new instrument through existing BSE Debt segment platform.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants - Institutional investors and debt market participants gain access to CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+ rated short-term paper from Grasim Industries with 90-day maturity.
