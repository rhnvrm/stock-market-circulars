## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective 25 November 2025. The issuance consists of 9000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, maturing in 91 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Birla Group Holdings Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 9000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 450 crores (9000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakhs)
- **Scrip Code**: 730646
- **Scrip ID**: BGH251125
- **ISIN**: INE09OL14IE6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IE6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded using the specified scrip code 730646 and scrip ID BGH251125

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 25 November 2025
- **Listing Date**: 25 November 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 24 February 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Birla Group Holdings Private Limited with strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating minimal credit risk. The listing expands debt market investment options for institutional investors but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading is restricted to the debt segment with standard Rs. 5 lakh denomination typical for Commercial Paper instruments.
