## Summary

BSE has issued notice regarding the name change of trading member ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 91) to "ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED" effective Friday, December 5, 2025. This is an administrative update for all trading members of the exchange.

## Key Points

- Trading member ALPIC SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 91) will change its name
- New name: ALLANZERS SECURITIES LIMITED
- Effective date: Friday, December 5, 2025
- Notice issued to all trading members of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only. Trading members requiring clarification should contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is an administrative name change affecting only the specific trading member (Member No. 91). Other market participants should update their records to reflect the new name but no operational changes are required.
