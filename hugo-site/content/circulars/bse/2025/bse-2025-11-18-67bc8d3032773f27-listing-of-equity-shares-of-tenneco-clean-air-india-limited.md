## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of Tenneco Clean Air India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544612, symbol TENNIND, and ISIN number INE19RI01016. The specific date of listing and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Tenneco Clean Air India Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544612
- Trading Symbol: TENNIND
- ISIN Number: INE19RI01016
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Trading members should await the separate notice for actual listing date and security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This announcement has minimal immediate market impact as it is an informational notice about a future listing.
