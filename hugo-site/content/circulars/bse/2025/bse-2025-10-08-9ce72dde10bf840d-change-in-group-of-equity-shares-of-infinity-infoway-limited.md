## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Infinity Infoway Limited (Scrip Code: 544567), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective October 24, 2025. This change allows for normal rolling settlement trading instead of the restrictive trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544567 (Infinity Infoway Limited - SME IPO)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling settlement (M Group)
- Effective date: Friday, October 24, 2025
- Notice issued in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251007-56 dated October 7, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from a restrictive trading framework (Trade for Trade) to a more flexible rolling settlement mechanism. This represents a relaxation of trading restrictions typically applied to newly listed SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to:
- Note the segment change effective October 24, 2025
- Update internal systems and trading platforms accordingly
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025 (segment transfer)
- **Previous Notice**: October 7, 2025 (Notice No. 20251007-56)

## Impact Assessment

This change is positive for market participants as it:
- Enhances liquidity by allowing rolling settlement trading
- Reduces settlement restrictions on the stock
- Indicates the company has met criteria for migration from restrictive segment
- Provides greater flexibility for investors and traders
- Standard progression for SME IPO listings demonstrating stability
