## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of FINSTARS CAPITAL LIMITED (Scrip Code: 977361, ISIN: INE08XA07052) on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a quantity of 1,500 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 11.07% p.a. with monthly interest payments and maturity date of December 9, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 1,500 securities
- Scrip Code: 977361, Scrip ID: 1107FCL27
- ISIN: INE08XA07052
- Face Value/Paid-up Value/Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Interest Rate: 11.07% per annum (monthly payments)
- Interest Payment Period: December 31, 2025 to December 9, 2027
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2027
- Credit Rating: IVR BBB-/Negative
- No Put/Call options available
- Securities tradable only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XA07052
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 9, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly from December 31, 2025 to December 9, 2027
