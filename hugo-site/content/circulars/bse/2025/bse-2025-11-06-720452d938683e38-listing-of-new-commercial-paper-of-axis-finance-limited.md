## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Axis Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 6, 2025. Two series of Commercial Papers totaling 4,500 units (Rs. 225 crore) have been listed on the BSE Debt segment with redemption dates in February 2026. Both instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- First series: 3,000 units with scrip code 730502 (AFL041125), ISIN INE891K14PO5
- Second series: 1,500 units with scrip code 729145 (AXFL28325), ISIN INE891K14OW1 (further listing)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Issue prices: Rs. 491,966 (first series) and Rs. 491,705.50 (second series)
- Allotment date: November 4, 2025
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: February 3, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of commercial paper. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt market. The instruments carry high credit ratings (A1+) indicating low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
