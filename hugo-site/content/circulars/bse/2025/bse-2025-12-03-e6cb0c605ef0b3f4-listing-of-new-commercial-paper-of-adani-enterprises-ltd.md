## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis. The listing comprises 500 securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 3, 2025. The Commercial Paper carries CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and matures on June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore
- Scrip Code: 730686
- Scrip ID: AEL21225
- ISIN: INE423A14YQ5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,301.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YQ5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade with tick size of 1 paise
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: December 3, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 2, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 1, 2026
- Notice Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard short-term debt instrument listing by Adani Enterprises Ltd. with 6-month tenure. The commercial paper is rated A1+ by both CARE and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments. The listing provides an additional debt trading avenue for institutional investors and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Standard procedural notification for debt market participants.
