## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 776 dated December 15, 2025. The auction includes 109 scrips from various companies across different sectors. The quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 25,695 shares for different securities. This is a standard settlement auction conducted by BSE to resolve delivery shortages.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 776
- Settlement Date: December 15, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 109 securities
- Highest quantity auctioned: 25,695 shares (Rajnish Wellness - Scrip Code 541601)
- Multiple securities with quantities of 1 share each
- Includes major companies like CIPLA, Hindalco, Indian Oil, Mahindra & Mahindra, Ultratech Cement, Vedanta
- Also includes mutual fund units from HDFC, ICICI Prudential, Kotak, Motilal Oswal, Nippon India, SBI, and UTI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with short delivery positions must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement of auction transactions to be completed as per BSE settlement procedures
- Members must ensure availability of securities or cash for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 776

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - This auction affects trading members who have delivery shortages in any of the 109 listed scrips. Members must arrange for securities or pay auction prices to fulfill delivery obligations.

**Market Impact:** Medium - While routine in nature, demat auctions can lead to temporary price volatility in affected scrips, particularly those with smaller quantities where procurement may be challenging.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Primarily impacts institutional investors and brokers with delivery obligations; retail investors generally not directly affected unless they are the receiving party in failed delivery transactions.
