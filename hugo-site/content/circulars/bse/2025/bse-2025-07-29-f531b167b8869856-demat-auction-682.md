## Summary

BSE has published Settlement No. 682 for demat auction dated July 29, 2025, listing securities and their respective quantities that are subject to auction settlement. The document contains details of approximately 90+ securities across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 to 25,000 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 682
- Settlement date: July 29, 2025
- Contains securities from multiple sectors including banking, pharma, energy, textiles, and technology
- Quantities vary significantly across securities, with IDFC FIRST BANK having the highest quantity at 25,000 shares
- CRESSANDA RAIL has 17,990 shares listed for auction
- Multiple securities have minimal quantities of 1-10 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must comply with demat auction settlement procedures
- Securities listed must be settled through the auction mechanism
- Participants holding these securities should ensure proper settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 29, 2025
- Settlement Number: 682

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction settlement affects a wide range of securities across various market segments. The impact varies by security based on the quantity involved. Securities with higher quantities like IDFC FIRST BANK (25,000), CRESSANDA RAIL (17,990), GHCL Textile (3,000), BELRISE INDU (1,500), and HIT KIT GLOB (1,240) may experience more significant settlement-related activity. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of these securities in the demat system.
