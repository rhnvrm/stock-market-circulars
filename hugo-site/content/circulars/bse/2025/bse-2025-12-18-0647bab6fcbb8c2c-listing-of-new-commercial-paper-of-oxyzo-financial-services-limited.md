## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by OXYZO Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The issue comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (totaling Rs. 25 crore) with ICRA A1+ credit rating. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025, with The Federal Bank Ltd acting as Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 500 units at Rs. 5 lakh face value each (Rs. 25 crore total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,78,977.50 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730808
- Scrip ID: OFSL171225
- ISIN: INE04VS14319
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE04VS14319
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified scrip code and ISIN

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: December 17, 2025
- Listing date: December 18, 2025
- Redemption date: June 15, 2026
- Notice date: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides OXYZO Financial Services Limited with short-term funding through the debt market. For trading members, this adds another debt instrument available for trading on the BSE platform. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. The 6-month tenure (December 2025 to June 2026) represents standard commercial paper maturity.
