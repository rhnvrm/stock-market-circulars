## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 17, 2025. The instrument comprises 1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with a maturity period of approximately 6 months. The Federal Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,77,392 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730790
- Scrip ID: VCL161225
- ISIN: INE01HV14AH6
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 16, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 25, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AH6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term debt investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations. Trading members involved in debt instruments should note the trading parameters and contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications.
