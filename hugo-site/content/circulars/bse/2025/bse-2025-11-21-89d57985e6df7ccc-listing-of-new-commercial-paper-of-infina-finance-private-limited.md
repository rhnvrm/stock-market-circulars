## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crores, with maturity period of approximately 167 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Infina Finance Private Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (privately placed)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crores (2000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,84,005 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730614
- **Scrip ID**: IFPL201125
- **ISIN**: INE879F14MA6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MA6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing on BSE's debt segment with no impact on equity markets. The Commercial Paper has strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicating high credit quality. The listing provides short-term funding to Infina Finance Private Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt market.
