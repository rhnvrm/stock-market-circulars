## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aarti Industries Ltd on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 14, 2025. The instrument has a face value of Rs.5,00,000/- per unit with a tenure of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 3000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,92,062.05/- per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730560
- **Scrip ID**: AIL131125
- **ISIN**: INE769A14AA5
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IndusInd Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE769A14AA5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with the specified tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Aarti Industries Ltd with short-term financing through the debt market. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can now trade these securities on the BSE platform. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
