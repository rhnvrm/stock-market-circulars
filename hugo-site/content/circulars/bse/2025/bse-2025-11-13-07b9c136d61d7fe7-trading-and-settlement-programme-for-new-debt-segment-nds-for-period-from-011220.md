## Summary

BSE has published the comprehensive trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for December 2025. The schedule covers 22 trading days from December 1 to December 31, 2025, providing settlement numbers, DP settlement numbers, trading dates, 6A/7A entry and confirmation dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, and auction dates with corresponding pay-in/pay-out timings.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule spans from NDS-162/25-26 (December 1) to NDS-183/25-26 (December 31, 2025)
- Each trading day follows T+1 settlement cycle for regular pay-in/pay-out
- Auction settlements follow T+2 cycle from trading date
- Regular pay-in timing: 11:00 AM with member instructions deadline of 10:50 AM
- Auction pay-in timing: 9:30 AM with member instructions deadline of 9:20 AM
- Schedule accounts for market holidays on December 6-7, 13-14, 20-21, 25, 27-28, 2025
- Pay-in instructions must be submitted to Depositories/banks before specified deadlines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit regular pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 10:50 AM for 11:00 AM pay-in
- Members must submit auction pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:20 AM for 9:30 AM auction pay-in
- All transactions must follow the published settlement calendar
- Members must ensure timely completion of 6A/7A entries and confirmations on specified dates

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **First Settlement**: NDS-162/25-26 with trading date December 1, 2025
- **Last Settlement**: NDS-183/25-26 with trading date December 31, 2025, auction pay-out on January 2, 2026
- **Year-end Settlements**: December 30 and 31 settlements will have pay-outs extending into January 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard monthly operational schedule that allows NDS market participants to plan their debt trading and settlement activities for December 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Market participants need to note the schedule and ensure compliance with specified timings. The year-end settlements extending into January 2026 require awareness for cash flow planning.

**Participant Impact**: Affects all members trading in the New Debt Segment who need to align their operations with the published settlement calendar and ensure timely submission of pay-in instructions to depositories and banks.
