## Summary

BSE has listed 31,00,000 equity shares of Kay Power And Paper Ltd (Scrip Code: 530255) effective December 12, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 20.10/- per share to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Number of shares listed: 31,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 30.10/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 20.10/-)
- Allotment date: October 11, 2025
- Trading commencement date: December 12, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 31840001 to 34940000
- ISIN: INE961B01013
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the lock-in requirements:
- 30,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 31840001 to 34840000) are locked-in until June 30, 2027
- 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 34840001 to 34940000) are locked-in until June 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,00,000 shares)**: June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (30,00,000 shares)**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a preferential issue of 31 lakh shares with strict lock-in provisions. The issue represents capital raising through warrant conversion. Trading members should be aware of the lock-in restrictions when facilitating trades. The shares being rank pari-passu ensures equal rights with existing shareholders.
