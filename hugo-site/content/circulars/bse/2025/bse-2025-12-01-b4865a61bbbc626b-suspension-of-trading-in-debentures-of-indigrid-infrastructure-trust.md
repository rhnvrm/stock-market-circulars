## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST debentures (ISIN: INE219X07132, Code: 960382)
- Debenture series: IGT-7.4%-26-12-25-INVIT
- Record date set for December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 11, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 11, 2025
- Members must ensure no buy/sell transactions are executed for ISIN INE219X07132
- Compliance with corporate action timelines for record date determination

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Purpose Execution**: Redemption and interest payment processing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects specific debt instrument holders of INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST. Investors holding these debentures will not be able to trade from December 11, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: High for debenture holders - those holding the securities as of record date will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. New investors cannot purchase these debentures after suspension.

**Operational Impact**: Trading platforms and members must update systems to block trading in the specified ISIN from the suspension date to ensure compliance with corporate action requirements.
