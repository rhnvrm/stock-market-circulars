## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The securities carry a 14.4% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and will mature on May 31, 2027. A total of 500 securities with face value of Rs. 100,000 each have been listed under ISIN INE065507BM2.

## Key Points

- Issuer: ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 977310
- Scrip ID: 144AFIL27
- Detail Name: AFIL-14.4%-31-5-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE065507BM2
- Quantity: 500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: November 19, 2025
- Credit Rating: IVR BBB-/Stable
- Interest Rate: 14.4% per annum
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN Number INE065507BM2
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for trading
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from November 30, 2025 to May 31, 2027
- **Date of Redemption**: May 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt securities listing on private placement basis with limited quantity (500 securities) and no put/call options. The impact is confined to debt market participants interested in BBB- rated corporate debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members dealing in debt securities need to note the new listing parameters. The securities follow standard BSE debt segment trading norms with dematerialised trading requirements.
