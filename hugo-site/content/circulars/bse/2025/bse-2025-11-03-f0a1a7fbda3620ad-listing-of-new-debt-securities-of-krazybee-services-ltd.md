## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Krazybee Services Ltd on private placement basis effective November 3, 2025. The securities comprise 12,500 units of non-convertible debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 11.50% interest rate per annum with monthly interest payments, maturing on March 29, 2027. The securities are rated CARE A and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Krazybee Services Ltd
- **Scrip Code**: 977262
- **Scrip ID**: 1150KSL27
- **ISIN**: INE07HK07858
- **Quantity**: 12,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 31, 2025
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A
- **Interest Rate**: 11.50% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Maturity Date**: March 29, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE07HK07858
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 29, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (dates not specified)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with no impact on secondary equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for institutional investors and qualified buyers in the debt market who participated in the private placement or may trade these securities.

**Operational Impact**: No significant operational changes for trading members. Standard debt trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading requirement.
