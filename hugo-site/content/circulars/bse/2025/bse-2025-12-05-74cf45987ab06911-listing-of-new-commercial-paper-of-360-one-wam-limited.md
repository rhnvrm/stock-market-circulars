## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment on December 5, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent. The commercial papers are rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a 3-month tenure maturing on March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3500 Commercial Paper securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,363.50 per security
- Scrip Code: 730704, Scrip ID: OWL41225
- ISIN: INE466L14FL1
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Allotment date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption date: March 5, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FL1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment. No impact on equity markets or trading operations for retail investors.
