## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251208-11 announcing the prohibition/suspension of trading in the securities of LS Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 514446) effective December 9, 2025. This action is taken as an interim measure in the interest of investors and the securities market.

## Key Points

- Trading in LS Industries Ltd securities is prohibited/suspended
- Action applies to scrip code 514446
- Suspension is an interim measure for investor protection
- Affects equity segment
- Trading members and investors are advised to take note

## Regulatory Changes

BSE has imposed a trading prohibition on LS Industries Ltd as an interim regulatory measure. This prevents all buy and sell transactions in the company's securities on the BSE platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in LS Industries Ltd securities from December 9, 2025
- No buy or sell orders can be executed for scrip code 514446
- Trading members must inform their clients holding or interested in this security
- Investors should note the suspension and adjust their portfolios accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors holding LS Industries Ltd shares will face immediate liquidity constraints as they cannot exit their positions through BSE. Pending orders will not be executed.

**Market Impact**: The suspension indicates potential regulatory concerns regarding the company. This is a serious action typically taken due to non-compliance, corporate governance issues, or market integrity concerns. Investors should exercise caution and await further clarification from the exchange or company regarding the reasons for suspension and expected duration.
