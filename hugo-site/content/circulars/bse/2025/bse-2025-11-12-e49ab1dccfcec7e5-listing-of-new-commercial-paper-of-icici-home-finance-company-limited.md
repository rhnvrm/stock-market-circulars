## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The listing consists of 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 200 crores) with a 3-month maturity period from November 12, 2025 to February 11, 2026. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crores (4,000 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakhs)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,534 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,466)
- **Scrip Code**: 730546
- **Scrip ID**: IHF121125
- **ISIN**: INE071G14HF7
- **Credit Ratings**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HF7
- Trading allowed only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Members requiring clarification should contact debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper issuance by a housing finance company for short-term funding requirements.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing process with no special trading conditions beyond normal debt segment requirements.

**Investor Impact**: Suitable for institutional investors seeking short-term debt instruments with high credit ratings (A1+). The discount of approximately 1.49% over 91 days indicates the yield on this instrument.

**Liquidity**: Trading permitted in Rs. 5 lakh denominations, making it accessible primarily to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals.
