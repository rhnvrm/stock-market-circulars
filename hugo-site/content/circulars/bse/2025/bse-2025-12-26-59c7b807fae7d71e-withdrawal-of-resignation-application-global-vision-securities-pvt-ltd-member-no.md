## Summary

BSE has informed all constituents that Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application that was originally submitted on December 1, 2025. This reverses the resignation process that had been publicly notified through newspapers on November 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application
- Original resignation application was submitted on December 1, 2025 (Notice No. 20251201-2)
- Public notice regarding resignation was published on November 29, 2025 in Financial Express and Navshakti newspapers
- The trading member will continue its operations with BSE
- Queries can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reversing a previously announced resignation.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members or market participants. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Original resignation application submitted
- **November 29, 2025**: Public notice published in newspapers
- **December 26, 2025**: Withdrawal of resignation announced

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one trading member and has no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. will continue normal trading operations. Constituents of the firm can continue business as usual without disruption.

**Contact**: For clarifications, contact BSE Membership department at membership.ops@bseindia.com
