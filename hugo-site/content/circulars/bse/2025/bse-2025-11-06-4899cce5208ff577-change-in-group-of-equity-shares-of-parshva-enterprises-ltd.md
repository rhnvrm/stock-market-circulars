## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 542694) will be transferred from the 'T' Group to the 'B' Group effective November 19, 2025. This change follows the previous exchange notice dated October 31, 2025, and allows trading in the company's shares under the 'B' Group category.

## Key Points

- Company: PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD
- Scrip Code: 542694
- Current Group: 'T' Group
- New Group: 'B' Group
- Effective Date: November 19, 2025
- Notice references previous Exchange Notice No. 20251031-64 dated October 31, 2025
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The security will be moved from the Trade-to-Trade ('T') Group to the regular 'B' Group, which allows for normal trading with intraday positions and delivery-based transactions. This represents a relaxation of trading restrictions previously applicable under the 'T' Group.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members of BSE must take note of this group change
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Normal trading rules applicable to 'B' Group securities will apply from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 19, 2025 (trading in 'B' Group commences)
- **Reference Notice**: Exchange Notice No. 20251031-64 dated October 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The migration from 'T' Group to 'B' Group is generally positive for the stock as it removes trade-to-trade restrictions. This allows for:

- Enhanced liquidity as intraday trading becomes possible
- Reduced settlement restrictions on trading members
- Improved market access for investors
- Greater flexibility in trading strategies

The change indicates that the company has met the criteria for graduation from the more restrictive 'T' Group, which is typically applied to securities with specific surveillance concerns or compliance issues.
