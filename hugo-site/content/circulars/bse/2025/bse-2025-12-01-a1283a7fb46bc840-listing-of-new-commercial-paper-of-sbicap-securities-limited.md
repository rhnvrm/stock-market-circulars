## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to dealings new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 1, 2025. The issue comprises 2,000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crores.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Quantity: 2,000 units (total value: Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730670
- Scrip ID: SSL281125
- ISIN Number: INE212K14CU9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CU9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment:** November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper listing provides SBICAP Securities Limited with short-term financing (90-day maturity) and offers debt market participants an additional investment option. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt segment trading members and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
