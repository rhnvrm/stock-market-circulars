## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instruments are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 10, 2025. The securities will trade under ISIN INE01C314DE1 with scrip code 730517.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,833.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730517, Scrip ID: BFSL061125
- ISIN: INE01C314DE1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and debt market participants. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt segment participants interested in Bajaj Financial Securities Limited instruments.
