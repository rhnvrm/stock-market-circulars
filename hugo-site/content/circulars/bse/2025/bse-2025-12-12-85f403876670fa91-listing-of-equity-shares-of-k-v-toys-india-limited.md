## Summary

K V TOYS INDIA LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as on December 11, 2025, following the listing of its equity shares post-IPO.

## Key Points

- Total shareholders: 708 (7 Promoter & Promoter Group, 701 Public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 3,663,900 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 2,616,100 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- Locked-in shares exist for both promoter and public categories
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts issued
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 post-listing
- Declaration required regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, non-disposal undertakings, other encumbrances, differential voting rights, and significant beneficial owners

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: December 11, 2025 (Post Issue of IPO)
- Filing date: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-IPO disclosure with no material impact on market operations. The filing provides transparency on the ownership structure of K V TOYS INDIA LIMITED following its initial public offering. The presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with standard IPO lock-in requirements for promoters and anchor investors.
