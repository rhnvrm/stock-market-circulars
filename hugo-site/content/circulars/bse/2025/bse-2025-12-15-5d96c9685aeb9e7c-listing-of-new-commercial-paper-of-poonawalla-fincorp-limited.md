## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 15, 2025. The instrument consists of 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crore. The Commercial Paper carries dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 500 crore (10,000 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,906 per unit (discount of Rs. 8,094 to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730767, Scrip ID: PFL121225
- ISIN: INE511C14ZD8
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE511C14ZD8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Tenure:** 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with 3-month maturity. Commercial Paper is a standard corporate funding tool for working capital requirements.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants. The instrument provides institutional investors with a short-term investment option backed by strong credit ratings (A1+).

**Issuer Benefit:** Poonawalla Fincorp Limited has raised Rs. 500 crore at an implied annualized discount rate of approximately 6.6%, providing cost-effective short-term funding for business operations.
