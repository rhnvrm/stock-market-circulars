## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 8000 new Commercial Papers issued by Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited on private placement basis, effective November 18, 2025. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment with ISIN INE936D14394, credit ratings CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 8000 Commercial Papers issued at face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,558 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 400 crores
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730591
- Scrip ID: JUPL181125
- ISIN: INE936D14394
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- ICICI Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 18, 2025
- Listing Date: November 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 17, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers provide short-term funding to Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited for a 91-day period. The instruments carry high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL), indicating strong credit quality and low default risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
