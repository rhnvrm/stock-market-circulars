## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of SRG Housing Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities have a face value of ₹1,00,000 each with 5,000 units totaling ₹50 crore, carrying 11.52% annual interest with monthly payments and maturing on March 1, 2031.

## Key Points

- 5,000 debt securities listed with scrip code 977076
- Face value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Interest rate: 11.52% per annum paid monthly
- Maturity date: March 1, 2031
- Credit rating: Acuité BBB+
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE559N07058
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 4, 2025
- Interest payment: Monthly
- Redemption date: March 1, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities by a housing finance company. The listing provides additional investment options for debt market participants but does not affect broader market operations or equity trading.
