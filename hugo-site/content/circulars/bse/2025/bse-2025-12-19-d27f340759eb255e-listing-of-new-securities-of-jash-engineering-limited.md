## Summary

BSE has listed 1,49,995 new equity shares of Jash Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 544402) effective from Monday, December 22, 2025. These shares were issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 305.50 per share (face value Rs. 2 with premium of Rs. 303.50). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 1,49,995 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Distinctive Numbers: 62755126 to 62905120
- Issue price: Rs. 305.50 per share (Rs. 2 face value + Rs. 303.50 premium)
- Allotment date: September 15, 2025
- ISIN: INE039O01029
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued through preferential allotment upon warrant conversion

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the lock-in restrictions on the newly listed shares:
- 75,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 62755126 - 62830125): Locked-in until June 20, 2027
- 74,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 62830126 - 62905120): Locked-in until June 22, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (74,000 shares)**: June 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (75,000 shares)**: June 20, 2027
- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The allotment represents a relatively small number of shares with extended lock-in periods (up to June 2027 for half the shares). The preferential allotment to Promoter and Non-Promoter through warrant conversion is a standard corporate action. Trading members should be aware of the lock-in restrictions when processing transactions in these shares.
