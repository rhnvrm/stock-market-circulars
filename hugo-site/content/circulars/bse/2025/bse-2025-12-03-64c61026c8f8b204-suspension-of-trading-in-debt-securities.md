## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 04, 2025, as the debentures are scheduled to mature on December 05, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: Earlysalary Services Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 975669
- ISIN: INE01YL07284
- Security Type: ESPL-11%-5-12-25-PVT (11% debentures)
- Maturity Date: December 05, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective: December 04, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026
- Notice Date: December 03, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 04, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 04, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **December 05, 2025**: Maturity date of debentures

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only holders of specific debentures of Earlysalary Services Private Limited. As this is a private company's debt instrument reaching maturity, the impact is limited to the specific investors holding these securities. No broader market implications are expected.
