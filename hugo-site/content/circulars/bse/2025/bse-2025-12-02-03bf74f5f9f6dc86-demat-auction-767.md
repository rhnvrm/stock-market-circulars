## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 767 dated December 2, 2025. The auction covers 96 scrips with quantities ranging from 1 share to 49,997 shares due for auction settlement. This is a standard market operation mechanism to complete settlement obligations where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 767
- Date: December 2, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 96
- Largest quantity: Mehai Techno (540730) - 49,997 shares
- Other significant quantities include Padam Cotton (27,232 shares), Steel Strips (7,288 shares), and Bank of Maharashtra (5,000 shares)
- Auction includes diverse sectors: banking, steel, textiles, infrastructure, technology, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular listing securities subject to auction for the settlement period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and participants must note the scrips listed for auction settlement
- Sellers who failed to deliver securities for Settlement No. 767 will be subject to auction mechanism
- Buyers will receive securities through the auction process
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction procedures

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: December 2, 2025 (Settlement No. 767)
- **Auction Date**: To be conducted as per BSE auction schedule for this settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 96 scrips with localized impact on specific securities rather than broad market disruption.

**Operational Impact**: The auction mechanism ensures delivery completion for failed settlements. Participants holding short positions in these scrips face auction risk and potential price volatility. Notable high-quantity auctions in Mehai Techno, Padam Cotton, and Steel Strips may see significant price movements during auction execution.

**Trading Impact**: Securities listed may experience increased volatility and liquidity pressure as auction participants enter the market to fulfill obligations or cover positions.
