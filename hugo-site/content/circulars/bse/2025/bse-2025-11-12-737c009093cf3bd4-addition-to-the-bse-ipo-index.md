## Summary

BSE announced that BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544603) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, November 14, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE effective Wednesday, November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED (544603) listed on BSE on November 12, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index on November 14, 2025
- Action included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- This follows Notice No: 20251111-37

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking funds and market participants should note the addition for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: Listing date of BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED on BSE
- **November 14, 2025**: Effective date of addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative action following a new listing. Index tracking funds that replicate the BSE IPO Index will need to include this stock in their portfolios. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
