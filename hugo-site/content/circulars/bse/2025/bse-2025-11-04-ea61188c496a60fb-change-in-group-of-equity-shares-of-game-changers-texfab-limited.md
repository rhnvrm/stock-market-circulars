## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Game Changers Texfab Limited (Scrip Code: 544594), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment and shifted under M Group effective November 19, 2025. This follows the company's SME IPO listing.

## Key Points

- Company: Game Changers Texfab Limited - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544594
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, November 19, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details
- Notice references previous circular dated November 03, 2025 (Notice No. 20251103-44)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for Game Changers Texfab Limited equity shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement basis, allowing for intraday trading and T+1 settlement instead of compulsory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Trading Members should familiarize themselves with Rolling segment trading rules for this scrip
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain for clarifications or assistance

## Important Dates

- **November 03, 2025**: Previous notice issued (Notice No. 20251103-44)
- **November 04, 2025**: Current notice issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This change enhances trading flexibility for Game Changers Texfab Limited shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment. The move is expected to improve liquidity, allow intraday trading, and reduce settlement timelines for investors and traders. This is a positive development typically indicating improved compliance or market readiness of the SME company post-IPO.
