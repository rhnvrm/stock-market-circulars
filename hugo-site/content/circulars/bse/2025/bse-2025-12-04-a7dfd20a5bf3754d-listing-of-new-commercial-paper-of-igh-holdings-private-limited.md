## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis effective December 4, 2025. The listing consists of 9000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,91,749 per unit, and maturing on March 5, 2026. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with Axis Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 9000 units
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,749 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730698
- Scrip ID: IGHP41225
- ISIN: INE02FN14705
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Allotment date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption date: March 5, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14705
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment from December 4, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The listing adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment for a 91-day tenure. Impact is restricted to institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt securities. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers.
