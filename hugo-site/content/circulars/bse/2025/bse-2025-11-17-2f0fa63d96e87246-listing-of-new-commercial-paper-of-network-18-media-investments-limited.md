## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 150 crore (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with a tenure of 91 days, maturing on February 13, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 150 crore (3000 units)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730584
- **Scrip ID**: NMIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WG7
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WG7
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that affects only debt market participants. The instrument provides short-term funding for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited.

**Investor Impact**: Available only to institutional and high-net-worth investors trading in debt markets. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt segment can now trade these instruments. Standard trading protocols apply with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs.
