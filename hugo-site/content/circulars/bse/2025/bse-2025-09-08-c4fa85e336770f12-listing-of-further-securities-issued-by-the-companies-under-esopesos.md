## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Tuesday, September 9, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies are listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total securities range from 218 shares (Godrej Properties) to 6,513,163 shares (Ather Energy)
- All securities will be available for trading from September 9, 2025
- No lock-in periods apply to any of the listed securities
- Face values vary from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **3i Infotech Limited**: 49,500 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Abans Financial Services**: 30,367 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Aditya Birla Capital**: 260,685 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Angel One Limited**: 30,418 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Ather Energy Limited**: 6,513,163 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Avalon Technologies**: 86,674 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Capital Small Finance Bank**: 39,250 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Go Digit General Insurance**: 10,830 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Godrej Properties**: 218 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Mphasis Limited**: 1,710 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Tata Consumer Products**: 3,192 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Tata Elxsi**: 1,924 shares (₹10 face value)
- **V.I.P. Industries**: 25,142 shares (₹2 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading. No additional compliance requirements specified.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: Tuesday, September 9, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP listings with relatively small quantities for most companies. The largest addition is from Ather Energy with over 6.5 million shares, which may have some liquidity impact for that specific stock.
