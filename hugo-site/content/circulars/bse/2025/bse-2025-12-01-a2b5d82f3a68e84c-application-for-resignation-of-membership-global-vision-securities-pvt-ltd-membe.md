## Summary

Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has formally applied for resignation of membership from the BSE in accordance with the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations. Trading members are notified of this application and given an opportunity to raise objections within a specified timeframe.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has applied for resignation of BSE membership
- Application submitted as per Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange
- Trading members can submit written objections to the Membership department
- Objection period is 15 days from December 1, 2025
- Clarifications can be requested via email at membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard notification of a membership resignation application.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members with Objections:**
- Submit objections in writing to the Membership department
- Objections must be submitted within 15 days from December 1, 2025
- Deadline for objections: December 15, 2025
- Contact email for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

**For Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd.:**
- Resignation process subject to completion as per Exchange regulations
- Any objections raised will be addressed as per standard procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Objection Deadline:** December 15, 2025 (15 days from notice date)
- **Objection Period Starts:** December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal to none. The resignation of a single trading member has limited direct impact on overall market operations.

**Operational Impact:** 
- Trading members who have outstanding transactions or obligations with Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. should take note and raise objections if necessary
- No impact on general trading operations or market liquidity
- Standard administrative process under Exchange membership regulations

**Member Impact:**
- Primarily affects Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and any members with direct business relationships
- Other trading members unaffected unless they have specific concerns warranting objections
- Resignation will be processed subject to clearance of any valid objections raised during the 15-day period
