## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 25, 2025. This session is intended for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. The mock session will allow testing of various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and other exceptional market conditions. No new BOLT TWS Release is scheduled.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on October 25, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Session available for testing third-party trading platforms, in-house systems, and ETI APIs
- Trading members can test exceptional market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- Mock trades will not create any real obligations, margins, or pay-in/pay-out requirements
- Feedback requested from all participants by 6:00 PM on session day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading session for testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 6:00 PM on October 25, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **October 25, 2025**: Mock Trading Session
  - 1:15 PM - 2:00 PM: Log-in period
  - 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Continuous Trading
  - 5:00 PM - 5:10 PM: Trade Modification
  - By 6:00 PM: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Optional testing opportunity for trading members to familiarize themselves with system functionalities and test their trading applications in simulated market conditions including exceptional scenarios. No real trades, obligations, or settlements will result from this session.
