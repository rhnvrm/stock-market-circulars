## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 40,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 285/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 295.00/- per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters through warrant conversion
- Trading commences from August 25, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0O8901016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities being available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to small number of shares (40,000) and lock-in restrictions until March 2026. The shares are issued through warrant conversion rather than fresh issue, representing completion of an existing commitment.
