## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 11, 2025. The instrument comprises 1,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 75 crores), with ISIN INE755K14FR6, maturing on March 9, 2026. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will trade only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,814 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,186)
- Scrip Code: 730741 | Scrip ID: DCL101225
- ISIN: INE755K14FR6
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE debt segment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FR6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: 89 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing affecting only the debt segment. The instrument provides short-term funding to Dalmia Cement and offers investment opportunity for debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Low risk instrument with strong credit ratings (A1+), suitable for investors seeking short-term debt exposure to a rated corporate issuer in the cement sector.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt segment need to configure the new scrip in their systems with specified parameters (tick size, lot size, denomination requirements).
