## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Industries Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The first series (RIL-29-12-25-CP) comprises 5,000 units maturing on December 29, 2025, while the second series (RIL-24-12-25-CP) comprises 9,000 units maturing on December 24, 2025. Both series have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment
- First series: 5,000 units with scrip code 730583 (RIL171125), maturing December 29, 2025
- Second series: 9,000 units with scrip code 730566 (RIL141125), maturing December 24, 2025
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Issue prices: Rs. 496,708 (first series) and Rs. 497,107 (second series)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ for both series
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers (INE002A14LO9 and INE002A14LN1)
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025 (both series)
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date (First Series)**: December 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Second Series)**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt instrument listing on the debt segment with no direct impact on equity markets or general trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in commercial papers. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. The instruments have strong credit ratings (A1+) and short tenures (36-41 days), making them suitable for short-term liquidity management.
