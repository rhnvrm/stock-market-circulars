## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of KSR Footwear Limited (Scrip Code: 544615) will be permitted for trading under 'B' group effective Thursday, December 11, 2025. This is a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: KSR Footwear Limited
- Scrip Code: 544615
- New Trading Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Thursday, December 11, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251127-18
- Notice Date: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current trading group to 'B' group. The 'B' group on BSE typically represents companies that meet certain criteria regarding market capitalization, trading frequency, and other regulatory parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Take note of the group change for KSR Footwear Limited
- Ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new group classification
- Apply appropriate trading rules and parameters applicable to 'B' group securities from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change in group classification. The impact is limited to KSR Footwear Limited's equity shares. Trading members need to update their systems accordingly, but this should not significantly affect market operations or trading patterns. The 'B' group classification may indicate improved liquidity or compliance with enhanced listing requirements.
