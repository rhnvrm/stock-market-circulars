## Summary

RJ Corp Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment with effect from December 11, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 99,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, representing a total issuance of Rs. 99 crore. The debentures carry a CRISIL AAA rating with 7.25% per annum interest rate and mature on December 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 99,000 units
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Scrip code: 977359
- Scrip ID: 725RJCL28
- ISIN: INE460K08053
- Credit rating: CRISIL AAA
- Interest rate: 7.25% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of allotment: December 10, 2025
- Maturity date: December 8, 2028
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE460K08053
- Trading must follow tick size of 1 paise
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing date**: December 11, 2025
- **First interest payment**: December 10, 2026
- **Final interest payment and redemption**: December 8, 2028
- **Interest payment frequency**: Annually from December 10, 2026 to December 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing notification with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on private placement basis and will be traded only among institutional investors in the debt segment. The CRISIL AAA rating indicates highest credit quality. This listing does not affect equity trading or retail investors. The securities provide fixed income investment opportunity for institutional participants in the debt market.
