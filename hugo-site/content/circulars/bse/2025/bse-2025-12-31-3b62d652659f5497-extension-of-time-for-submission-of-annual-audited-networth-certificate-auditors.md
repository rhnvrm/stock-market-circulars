## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has issued General Circular No. 08/2025 dated December 30, 2025, extending the deadline for filing annual returns and financial statements for FY 2024-25 to January 31, 2026 without payment of additional fees. This circular continues the relaxation provided under General Circular No. 06/2025 and responds to stakeholder representations requesting additional time for compliance.

## Key Points

- Companies can complete annual filings for FY 2024-25 up to January 31, 2026 without additional fees
- Applicable forms include: MGT-7, MGT-7A, AOC-4, AOC-4 CFS, AOC-4 NBFC (Ind AS), AOC-4 CFS NBFC (Ind AS), and AOC-4 (XBRL)
- Extension issued in continuation of General Circular No. 06/2025 dated October 17, 2025
- All other requirements of General Circular No. 06/2025 remain unchanged
- Circular issued by Deputy Director Dr. Amit Kumar with approval of competent authority

## Regulatory Changes

This circular provides temporary regulatory relief by:

- Extending the filing deadline for annual compliance forms pertaining to FY 2024-25
- Waiving additional fees that would otherwise apply for late filings until the extended deadline
- Maintaining continuity with previous circular (06/2025) while providing additional time based on stakeholder feedback

## Compliance Requirements

**Affected Entities:**
- All companies required to file annual returns and financial statements under the Companies Act, 2013

**Filing Forms Covered:**
- MGT-7 (Annual Return)
- MGT-7A (Annual Return for certain companies)
- AOC-4 (Financial Statements)
- AOC-4 CFS (Consolidated Financial Statements)
- AOC-4 NBFC (Ind AS) (NBFC Financial Statements under Ind AS)
- AOC-4 CFS NBFC (Ind AS) (NBFC Consolidated Financial Statements under Ind AS)
- AOC-4 (XBRL) (Financial Statements in XBRL format)

**Action Required:**
- Complete annual filings for FY 2024-25 by January 31, 2026 to avoid additional fees
- Ensure all other requirements from General Circular No. 06/2025 are met

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date:** December 30, 2025
- **BSE Publication Date:** December 31, 2025
- **Extended Filing Deadline:** January 31, 2026 (without additional fees)
- **Financial Year Covered:** FY 2024-25 (ended March 31, 2025)
- **Reference Circular:** General Circular No. 06/2025 dated October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:**
- Provides significant compliance relief to all companies by extending filing deadline
- Waives additional fees, reducing financial burden on companies
- Demonstrates regulatory responsiveness to stakeholder concerns
- Allows companies additional time to ensure accurate and complete filings

**Market Impact:**
- Affects all listed and unlisted companies required to file annual returns
- Particularly beneficial for companies facing resource constraints or operational challenges
- May delay public availability of FY 2024-25 financial information for some companies

**Compliance Impact:**
- Companies must track the extended deadline and ensure timely filing
- Failure to file by January 31, 2026 will result in additional fees
- No changes to other compliance requirements from previous circular
