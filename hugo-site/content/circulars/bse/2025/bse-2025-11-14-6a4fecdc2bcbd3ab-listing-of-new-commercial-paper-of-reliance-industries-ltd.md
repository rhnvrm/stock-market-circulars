## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Reliance Industries Ltd on a private placement basis, effective November 14, 2025. The instrument consists of 25,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 1,250 crore. The Commercial Paper has been issued at a discount with an issue price of Rs. 4,96,788 and will mature on December 24, 2025. The securities carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 25,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,96,788 per unit (issued at discount)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 1,250 crore
- Scrip Code: 730566
- Scrip ID: RIL141125
- Detail Name: RIL-24-12-25-CP
- ISIN: INE002A14LN1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE002A14LN1
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
- Listing Date: November 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 24, 2025
- Tenure: 40 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Reliance Industries has issued short-term debt at competitive rates (approximately 3.15% discount annualized) to meet working capital requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. This listing provides institutional investors and trading members with an additional short-term investment avenue backed by one of India's largest conglomerates. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible primarily to institutional investors.
