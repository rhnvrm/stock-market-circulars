## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 15, 2025. The listing consists of two tranches totaling 8,000 units (Rs. 40 crores) with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, maturing on March 13, 2026. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with State Bank of India acting as Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 8,000 units (2,000 units + 6,000 units under same ISIN)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,954 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730765
- Scrip ID: SBI121225
- ISIN: INE212K14CW5
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CW5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarifications should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice date: December 15, 2025
- Date of allotment: December 12, 2025
- Listing effective date: December 15, 2025
- Redemption date: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt market participants with an additional CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+ rated instrument for trading. The 91-day tenor commercial paper offers institutional investors a short-term investment option. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
