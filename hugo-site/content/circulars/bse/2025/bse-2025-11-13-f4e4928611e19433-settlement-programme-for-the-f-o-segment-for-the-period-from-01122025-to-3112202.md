## Summary

BSE has announced the daily settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for the period from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025. This is a routine operational schedule published by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers the entire month of December 2025
- Daily settlement occurs on T+1 basis (next trading day)
- Schedule accounts for market holidays including December 25, 2025 (Christmas)
- Last settlement of December 2025 trades occurs on January 1, 2026
- Total of 21 trading days in December 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should note the settlement dates for their F&O trades during December 2025 and ensure timely fulfillment of settlement obligations.

## Important Dates

- **Schedule Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **Market Holiday**: December 25, 2025 (no trading)
- **Last Trade Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Last Settlement Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Non-Trading Days**: December 6-7, 13-14, 20-21, 25, 27-28 (weekends and holiday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It provides necessary information for market participants to plan their derivatives trading and settlement activities for December 2025. The schedule follows standard T+1 settlement convention and accounts for the Christmas holiday on December 25, 2025.
