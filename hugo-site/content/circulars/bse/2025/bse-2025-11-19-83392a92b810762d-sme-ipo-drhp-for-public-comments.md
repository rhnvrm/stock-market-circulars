## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of ENS ENTERPRISES LIMITED, filed on November 01, 2025, for public comments. The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements. Stakeholders can submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the 21-day period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by ENS ENTERPRISES LIMITED on November 01, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document public comment period as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and stakeholders are invited to review the Draft Offer Document
- Public comments should be submitted within 21 days from November 19, 2025
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document can be accessed at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **November 01, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by ENS ENTERPRISES LIMITED
- **November 19, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **December 10, 2025**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the SME IPO process for ENS ENTERPRISES LIMITED. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. Impact is limited to potential investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the ENS ENTERPRISES LIMITED IPO. No immediate action required for general market participants.
