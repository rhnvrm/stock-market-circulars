## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Oscar Global Limited. The bidding window will operate from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026, with settlement number 2025138. Obligation download is scheduled for January 29, 2026 and final settlement on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025138
- Bid Period: January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Both CDSL and NSDL depositories support this mechanism
- Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2025138 must be used in depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular follows the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- Participants must ensure early pay-in effects are reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- No EPN file upload required for CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- December 31, 2025: Circular issued
- January 2, 2026: Bid start date
- January 15, 2026: Bid end date
- January 29, 2026: Obligation download date
- January 30, 2026: Final settlement date

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer impacts shareholders of Oscar Global Limited who wish to participate in the acquisition window. Trading members and custodians handling client securities must ensure compliance with early pay-in procedures and timelines. The 14-day bidding window provides adequate time for shareholders to tender shares. Members must coordinate with ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com) for clarifications on settlement procedures.
