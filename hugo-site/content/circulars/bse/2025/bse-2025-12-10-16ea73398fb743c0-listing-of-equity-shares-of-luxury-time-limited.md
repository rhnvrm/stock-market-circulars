## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Luxury Time Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544635, symbol LUXURY, and ISIN number INE1CJB01013. The specific date of listing and additional details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Luxury Time Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544635
- Trading Symbol: LUXURY
- ISIN: INE1CJB01013
- Exact listing date and security details to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the separate notice for the exact listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds one new company to the BSE SME segment. Trading members and investors interested in the SME segment should watch for the follow-up notice containing the specific listing date and complete security details. The impact is confined to participants interested in this specific company and the SME segment.
