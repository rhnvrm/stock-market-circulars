## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 4071) will cease business operations in the OBPP (Odd Lot, Block Deal & Put Through Platform) segment effective November 27, 2025. This action is due to the member's voluntary decision to close business operations.

## Key Points

- Member name: Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd.
- Member number: 4071
- Affected segment: OBPP (Odd Lot, Block Deal & Put Through Platform)
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of a member's voluntary exit from the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this closure for awareness. No specific compliance actions are required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (Thursday) - Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd. will cease OBPP segment operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification regarding one member's voluntary exit from the OBPP segment. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected. Members with pending transactions or queries related to Sunrise Gilts & Securities should contact BSE's Membership Department before the effective closure date.
