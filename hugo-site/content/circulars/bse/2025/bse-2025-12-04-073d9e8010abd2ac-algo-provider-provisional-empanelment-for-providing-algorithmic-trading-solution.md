## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and the updated list of Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an Algo Provider
- The vendor is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Office location: #1203, Cello Triumph, B-wing, IB Patel Road, off the W-E highway, Goregaon East, Mumbai-400063
- Contact number: +91 9013468987
- Complete list of empanelled Algo Providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational in nature, notifying trading members of the availability of a new provisionally empanelled Algo Provider.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (Immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is purely informational. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. The provisional empanelment provides trading members with expanded choice in selecting algo trading technology providers. No operational changes or compliance actions are required from existing market participants.
