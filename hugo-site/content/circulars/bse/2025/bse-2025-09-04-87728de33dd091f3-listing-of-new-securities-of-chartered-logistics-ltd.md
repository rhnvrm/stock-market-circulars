## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,09,00,000 new equity shares of Chartered Logistics Ltd (Scrip Code: 531977) effective September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 7.80 per share (Rs. 1 face value + Rs. 6.80 premium) to non-promoters through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 2,09,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1 each listed on BSE
- Issue price: Rs. 7.80 per share (face value Rs. 1 + premium Rs. 6.80)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 99340001 to 120240000
- ISIN: INE558F01026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for converted warrants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can commence trading these securities from September 5, 2025
- All shares subject to lock-in period until March 8, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents conversion of existing warrants rather than fresh equity dilution. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may provide some liquidity enhancement post lock-in period expiry.
