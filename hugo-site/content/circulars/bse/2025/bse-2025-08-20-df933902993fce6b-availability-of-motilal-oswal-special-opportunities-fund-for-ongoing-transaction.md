## Summary

MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND will be available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform starting August 21, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across six different plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective August 21, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Includes SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned. This is a platform availability update.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate transactions in the specified MOTILAL OSWAL SPECIAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025 - Fund availability for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: 20250724-24

## Impact Assessment

Low impact as this affects only mutual fund distribution through BSE's platform. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking exposure to special opportunities equity funds with systematic investment and withdrawal facilities.
