## Summary

BSE announces the commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED from December 26, 2025 to December 31, 2025. The Rights Issue will open on December 26, 2025 and close on January 05, 2026. REs will be traded on T+1 rolling settlement basis in dematerialized form only, with trade-for-trade settlement.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading period: December 26, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- Scrip Code: 751070, Scrip ID: ANIRIT-RE, ISIN: INE161F20011
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Settlement: T+1 Rolling Settlement on trade-for-trade basis
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- REs must be either subscribed with requisite application money or renounced before issue closing date
- Unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse and be extinguished after January 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing framework for rights entitlements trading as per previous notices 20200305-21 (March 05, 2020) and 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must inform all clients about commencement of REs trading mentioning the ISIN (INE161F20011)
- Trading Members must include prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills containing purchase of REs
- Disclaimer must inform clients that:
  - Purchase of REs only gives right to participate in Rights Issue by making application with requisite money or to renounce REs before issue closes
  - REs are not ordinary shares of the company
  - Unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date
- Trading Members must refer to notice 20200305-21 and 20200518-34 for additional details

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 26, 2025**: Trading in REs commences; Rights Issue opens
- **December 31, 2025**: Last day for REs trading/renunciation
- **January 05, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unexercised REs lapse

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Limited to shareholders and potential investors of ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard rights entitlements trading mechanism applicable only to specific company. Trading Members dealing with ANIRIT VENTURES clients need to ensure proper client communication and contract note disclaimers. The trade-for-trade settlement and dematerialized-only requirement is standard for rights entitlements trading.
