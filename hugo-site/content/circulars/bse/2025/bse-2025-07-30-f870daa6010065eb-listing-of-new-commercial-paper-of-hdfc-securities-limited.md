## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ and will mature on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,280 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729928, Scrip ID: HSL30725
- ISIN: INE700G14PF4
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 30, 2025
- Date of redemption: October 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides HDFC Securities Limited with short-term funding through the debt market and offers investors a rated investment option with approximately 3-month maturity.
