## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new commercial papers on the BSE Debt segment effective December 2, 2025. The listing involves 4000 commercial papers issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crore. The instruments carry credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4000 commercial papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,758.50 per paper (discount of Rs. 7,241.50)
- **Scrip Code**: 730681
- **Scrip ID**: NMIL21225
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WI3
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WI3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments of Network 18 Media & Investments Limited. The listing provides additional liquidity avenue for the issuer's commercial paper program.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for debt market participants seeking short-term investment opportunities with A1+ rated paper offering approximately 5.89% annualized discount yield.
