## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Arman Financial Services Limited on private placement basis effective December 5, 2025. The securities consist of 75,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 11.35% per annum with quarterly interest payments.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 75,000 debentures (Rs. 75 crore total)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- **Interest Rate**: 11.35% per annum (quarterly payments)
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 5, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-/Stable
- **ISIN**: INE109C07121
- **Scrip Code**: 977345
- **Scrip ID**: 1135AFSL27
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE109C07121
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Quarterly from March 5, 2026 to December 5, 2027
- **First Interest Payment**: March 5, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities for a single company with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these debt securities on BSE Debt segment. The securities are available for institutional and eligible investors in dematerialised form.

**Investor Impact**: Provides fixed-income investment opportunity with 11.35% annual return for investors holding until maturity (2-year tenure). Credit rating of ACUITE A-/Stable indicates moderate credit quality with adequate safety.
