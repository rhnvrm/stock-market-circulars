## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 500 Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis effective 16-Oct-2025. Each instrument has a face value of Rs.5,00,000 with issue price of Rs.4,98,668 and will mature on 30-Oct-2025. The securities carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will trade only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Papers of Rs.5 lakh face value each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 730392, Scrip ID: NWIL151025, ISIN: INE523L14AL0
- Issue Price: Rs.4,98,668 per instrument (discount of Rs.1,332 from face value)
- Allotment Date: 15-Oct-2025
- Redemption Date: 30-Oct-2025 (15-day tenure)
- Credit Ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market Lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt instruments issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L14AL0
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Members must use tick size of 1 paise for trading these instruments
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department on specified contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 16-Oct-2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** 16-Oct-2025
- **Allotment Date:** 15-Oct-2025
- **Redemption Date:** 30-Oct-2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a standard short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market-wide impact. Commercial papers are routine money market instruments used for short-term corporate funding.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Affects only debt segment trading members and institutional investors participating in commercial paper markets. The 15-day tenure indicates very short-term liquidity management instrument.

**Issuer Impact:** Neutral - Routine treasury operation for Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited to raise short-term funds at competitive rates backed by strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL).
