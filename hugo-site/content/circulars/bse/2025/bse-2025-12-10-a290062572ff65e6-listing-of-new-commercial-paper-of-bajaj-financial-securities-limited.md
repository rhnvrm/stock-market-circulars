## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on a private placement basis. The listing is effective from December 10, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment. The issue comprises 500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at a discount with redemption scheduled for March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 500 units of Commercial Paper listed with face value Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730737, Scrip ID: BFSL091225
- ISIN: INE01C314DN2
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,147 (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- ICICI Bank Limited acting as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DN2
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities available for trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing for institutional investors in the debt market.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading only. The commercial paper has a short tenure of approximately 3 months (December 2025 to March 2026).
