## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Edel Finance Company Limited on private placement basis, effective December 01, 2025. Two series of securities have been listed under different ISINs with face value of Rs. 100,000 each, both maturing on October 30, 2028, and rated CRISIL A+/STABLE.

## Key Points

- **Security 1 (ISIN: INE836K07304)**
  - Scrip Code: 976967 (EFCL30725)
  - Quantity: 500 securities
  - Issue Price: Rs. 101,192
  - Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
  - Interest: Cumulative as per KID
  - Put/Call option: As per KID

- **Security 2 (ISIN: INE836K07312)**
  - Scrip Code: 976968 (EFCL300725)
  - Quantity: 100 securities
  - Issue Price: Rs. 100,352
  - Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
  - Interest: Cumulative as per KID
  - Put/Call option: NA

- Both securities have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 100,000
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A+/STABLE
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Any clarifications required should be directed to BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Allotment Date (ISIN INE836K07304)**: November 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date (ISIN INE836K07312)**: July 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Both Securities)**: October 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with relatively small issue sizes (500 and 100 units). The securities are already allotted and this notification merely formalizes their trading on BSE's debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to specific debt investors who participated in the private placement. The CRISIL A+/STABLE rating indicates moderate credit quality with stable outlook.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt segment trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading and standard tick size of 1 paise.
