## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The listing comprises 600 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, traded in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment. The securities carry a CARE A1 credit rating and have a tenor of approximately 6 months.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 600 units of commercial paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,77,305 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730669
- Scrip ID: NFL281125
- ISIN: INE342T14FE2
- Credit Rating: CARE A1
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14FE2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at specified contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 1, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
- Listing Date: December 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it pertains to a routine commercial paper issuance in the debt segment. The listing provides short-term financing options for Navi Finserv Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The CARE A1 rating indicates adequate safety for the instruments. There is no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors.
