## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the 9.6% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE265J07498, Code: 974463) of JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Security affected: JM FINANCIAL ASSET RECONSTRUCTION COMPANY LTD 9.6% debentures
- ISIN: INE265J07498
- BSE Code: 974463
- Record date: December 4, 2025
- Trading suspension starts: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-770/2025-2026
- Security type: Non-Convertible Debentures (Private Placement)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for suspending trading during the redemption process of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in the affected security from the specified date

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| November 24, 2025 | Circular issued |
| December 4, 2025 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| December 4, 2025 | Trading suspension commences (no dealings from this date) |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This suspension affects a single privately placed NCD series. The impact is limited to holders of these specific debentures who will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard corporate action for debt securities reaching maturity. Trading members need to ensure the security is blocked from trading in their systems from the specified date.

**Investor Impact:** Neutral to positive for debenture holders who will receive their principal redemption amount plus accrued interest as per the terms of the debentures.
