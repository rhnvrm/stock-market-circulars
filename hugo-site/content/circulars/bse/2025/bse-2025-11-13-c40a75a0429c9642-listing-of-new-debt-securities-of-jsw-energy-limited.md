## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by JSW Energy Limited on private placement basis, effective November 13, 2025. The issuance comprises 25,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (total Rs. 250 crore), carrying an 8.05% per annum interest rate with annual payment frequency and maturing on November 10, 2028. The securities are rated ICRA AA-/STABLE and IND AA-/Stable, and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: JSW Energy Limited
- **Total Quantity**: 25,000 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 977291
- **Scrip ID**: 805JSWEL28
- **ISIN**: INE121E08070
- **Detail Name**: JSWEL-10-11-28-PVT
- **Interest Rate**: 8.05% per annum (payable annually)
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-/STABLE, IND AA-/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE121E08070
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for trading
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 10, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt securities listing that affects only the debt segment. There is no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard listing procedure for debt securities issued on private placement basis. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt investors and institutional participants in private placement. The securities carry investment-grade credit ratings (AA-) indicating strong creditworthiness of JSW Energy Limited.
