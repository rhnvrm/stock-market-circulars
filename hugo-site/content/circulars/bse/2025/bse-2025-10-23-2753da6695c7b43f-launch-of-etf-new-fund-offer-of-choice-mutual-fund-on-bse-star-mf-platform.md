## Summary

CHOICE Mutual Fund is launching CHOICE GOLD ETF - DIRECT PLAN as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from October 24, 2025 to October 31, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of November 04, 2025. The scheme is categorized under DEBT and will accept lumpsum purchases during the extended period until midnight on October 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- CHOICE GOLD ETF - DIRECT PLAN categorized as DEBT scheme
- NFO period: October 24, 2025 to October 31, 2025
- Tentative allotment date: November 04, 2025
- Extended trading available until midnight on October 31, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 04, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to receipt of clear funds by deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Transactions with initiated payments will remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 04, 2025 for unit allotment
- Cancelled transactions (where clients haven't initiated funds) will be reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **October 31, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended until midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 04, 2025**: Tentative allotment date and deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account (by 6:00 PM)
- **T+1 day**: Deadline for client fund initiation (9:00 AM)
- **T+5 or last day of NFO**: Pending transaction deadline (3:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it announces a routine NFO launch for a gold ETF product. The impact is limited to:

- **Market participants**: Members and investors interested in gold ETF exposure through CHOICE Mutual Fund
- **Operational impact**: Standard NFO procedures apply with specific timelines for fund settlement and allotment
- **Payment methods**: Flexibility provided through acceptance of cheques and OTM payments during NFO period, subject to clear funds requirement
- **Risk mitigation**: Clear cancellation policy for unfunded transactions protects against settlement failures
