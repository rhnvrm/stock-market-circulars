## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper (Scrip Code: 730205, ISIN: INE895B14388) of Rupa & Company Limited effective December 09, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the commercial paper on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rupa & Company Limited (Scrip Code: 730205) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE895B14388
- Commercial Paper designation: RCL-10-12-25-CP
- Suspension effective from December 09, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Reference: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper of Rupa & Company Limited from December 09, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 08, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date:** December 09, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** December 10, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument due to natural maturity. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard end-of-life process for debt instruments. Trading members need to ensure no deals are executed in this specific commercial paper from the effective date.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms.
