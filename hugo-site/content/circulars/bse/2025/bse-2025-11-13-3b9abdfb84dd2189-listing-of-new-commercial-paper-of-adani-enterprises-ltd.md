## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis with effect from November 13, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crore), issued at a discount with maturity of approximately 4.4 months. The instrument is rated ACUITE A1+ and CARE A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore aggregate)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,87,010 (issued at discount)
- Allotment Date: November 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 25, 2026
- Scrip Code: 729140
- Scrip ID: AEL28325
- ISIN: INE423A14WX5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A1+, CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing announcement for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14WX5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Settlement and trading procedures as per BSE Debt segment guidelines apply
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 13, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date:** March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. The instrument provides short-term funding avenue for Adani Enterprises Ltd.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in debt market, given the high denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit. The top-tier credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness for short-term obligations.
