## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED (ISIN: INE0RU307130, Code: 976225) effective December 5, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing December 6, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to AFL-10.50%-21-12-25 debentures (ISIN: INE0RU307130, Code: 976225)
- Record date set for December 6, 2025
- Purposes: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 5, 2025 onwards
- Notice number: DR-771/2025-2026
- Trading members must cease all transactions in the specified debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice implementing existing procedures for debenture redemption and record date compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED debentures from December 5, 2025
- All market participants must note the trading suspension and adjust their trading systems accordingly
- Compliance with the no-dealings directive is mandatory for all exchange members
- Trading members should inform their clients holding or intending to trade these debentures about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **December 5, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **December 6, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High impact on debenture holders and traders dealing in AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED securities. The trading suspension ensures orderly settlement and prevents trades that would not qualify for the upcoming redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures as of the record date will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment. No trading opportunity available after December 5, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in the specified ISIN and inform clients promptly to avoid failed transaction attempts.
