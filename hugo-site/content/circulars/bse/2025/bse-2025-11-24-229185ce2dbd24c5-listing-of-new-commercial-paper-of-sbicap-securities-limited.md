## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 2000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating the highest credit quality for short-term debt instruments. State Bank of India serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: SBICAP Securities Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 2000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730627
- **Scrip ID**: SSL211125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CQ7
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced through this circular. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN number
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the tick size of 1 paise for price quotations
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at the provided contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: 24th November 2025
- **Allotment Date**: 21st November 2025
- **Listing Date**: 24th November 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: 20th February 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal with commercial paper of SBICAP Securities Limited. The standard trading parameters align with existing BSE debt market practices.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors and high net-worth individuals with an additional short-term investment option in highly-rated commercial paper with a 3-month maturity period. The discount to face value (issue price of Rs. 4,91,845.50 versus face value of Rs. 5,00,000) represents the implicit interest return.
