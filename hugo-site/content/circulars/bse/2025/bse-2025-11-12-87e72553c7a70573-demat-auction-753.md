## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 753 dated November 12, 2025. The auction includes 106 different scrips with varying quantities available for auction to fulfill delivery obligations. This is a routine market operation to ensure settlement of trades where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 753
- Auction Date: November 12, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 106
- Quantities range from 1 share to 25,000 shares depending on the scrip
- Major scrips include SPICEJET (3,437 shares), STANDARD CAPITAL (25,000 shares), TUNI TEXTILE (10,000 shares), ATAL REALTECH (12,500 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including oil & gas, banking, technology, manufacturing, and textiles

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine auction process as per existing BSE settlement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the auction
- Buyers in the auction must ensure availability of funds
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement guidelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: November 12, 2025 (Settlement No. 753)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a standard settlement mechanism affecting only specific members with delivery shortfalls in the listed securities.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Routine auction process with no changes to existing procedures. Affects only members with open delivery obligations.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Does not affect general trading or investor positions. Only impacts members who failed to deliver securities and need to purchase from auction to fulfill obligations.
