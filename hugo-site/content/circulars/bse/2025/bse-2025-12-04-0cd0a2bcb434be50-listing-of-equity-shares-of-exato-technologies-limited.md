## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the upcoming listing of equity shares of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED on the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544626 and trading symbol EXATO with ISIN INE1E4401010. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544626
- Trading Symbol: EXATO
- ISIN: INE1E4401010
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Specific listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new company joining the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the new listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 4, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Details of securities: To be provided in subsequent circular

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact. This is a routine listing announcement for a single SME company. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is confirmed. The notice serves as an advance intimation to prepare for the commencement of trading in EXATO shares on the BSE SME platform.
