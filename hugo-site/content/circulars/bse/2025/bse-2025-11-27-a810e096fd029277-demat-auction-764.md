## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 764 dated November 27, 2025. The auction includes securities from 111 companies across various sectors, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 43,800 shares. This is a routine settlement mechanism for resolving demat delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 764
- Settlement Date: November 27, 2025
- Total companies involved: 111
- Largest quantity: Darshan Orna (43,800 shares)
- Other significant quantities: SVP Global (13,215 shares), Setco Auto (1,344 shares), Gujarat Mine (1,225 shares)
- Includes major companies like Infosys (105 shares), Tata Power (500 shares), Tata Steel (2 shares), Bajaj Auto (8 shares), BHEL (76 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including energy, pharmaceuticals, automobiles, IT, and infrastructure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard auction settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanisms
- Members should review their positions for the listed scrips and quantities

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 27, 2025
- Auction Settlement Number: 764

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on overall market operations. This is a routine demat auction settlement affecting small quantities of securities from various companies. The auction mechanism helps resolve delivery failures and maintain market integrity. Individual investors holding these securities are not directly affected unless they are party to the specific trades requiring auction settlement.
