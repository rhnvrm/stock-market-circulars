## Summary

AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE08XP07290, Code: 976585) along with interest payment. The record date has been set for November 13, 2025, and trading will commence with reduced face value from the same date under settlement number DR-755/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE08XP07290
- BSE Code: 976585
- Debenture Series: ACAPL-14.50%-29-3-26-PVT
- Record Date: November 13, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 33,333.36 per debenture
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Effective Date: November 13, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-755/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 33,333.36 per debenture effective from November 13, 2025
- Debenture holders on record as of November 13, 2025 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Circular notification date
- **November 13, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption
- **November 13, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-755/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a private placement of debentures by a private limited company. The part redemption will result in:

- Reduction in outstanding debenture face value to Rs. 33,333.36 per unit
- Return of capital to existing debenture holders
- Interest payment to eligible holders as per record date
- Adjustment in trading parameters for the affected debenture series

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series (ACAPL-14.50%-29-3-26-PVT) and does not affect broader market operations or equity trading.
