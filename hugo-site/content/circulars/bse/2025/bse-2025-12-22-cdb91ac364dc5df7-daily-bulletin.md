## Summary

BSE Bulletin No. 181/2025-2026 dated December 22, 2025 provides details of upcoming book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin includes information on capital reduction for Alps Industries Ltd. and extensive NCD interest payment and redemption schedules for AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED spanning from January 2026 to August 2027.

## Key Points

- Alps Industries Ltd. (Code: 530715) has set January 2, 2026 as record date for resolution plan involving capital reduction
- AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED (Code: 977341) has scheduled 22 separate record dates for privately placed non-convertible debenture interest payments
- Interest payment dates span from January 11, 2026 through August 11, 2027
- One NCD redemption scheduled for August 11, 2027
- All securities will continue trading (Trading flag: 1) during record date periods
- Capri Global Capital is partially listed but content appears truncated

## Corporate Actions Schedule

### Alps Industries Ltd. (530715)
- **Security Type**: Equity
- **Record Date**: January 2, 2026
- **Purpose**: Resolution Plan - Reduction of Capital
- **Trading Status**: Continues (flag: 1)

### AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED (977341)
- **Security Type**: Privately Placed Non-Convertible Debentures
- **Interest Payment Record Dates**: 
  - 2026: January 11, February 11, March 11, April 11, May 11, June 11, July 11, August 11, September 11, October 11, November 11, December 11
  - 2027: January 11, February 11, March 11, April 11, May 11, June 11, July 11, August 11
- **Redemption Record Date**: August 11, 2027
- **Trading Status**: Continues for all dates (flag: 1)

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: Alps Industries capital reduction record date
- **January 11, 2026 onwards**: Monthly interest payment record dates for AMBIUM FINSERVE NCDs
- **August 11, 2027**: Final interest payment and NCD redemption for AMBIUM FINSERVE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine informational bulletin that enables investors to track corporate action eligibility dates. For Alps Industries shareholders, the capital reduction under resolution plan may impact shareholding structure. For AMBIUM FINSERVE NCD holders, the bulletin provides clarity on the complete interest payment schedule through maturity and redemption in August 2027. No immediate trading restrictions are imposed as all securities remain tradeable during record date periods.
