## Summary

Simandhar Impex Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as on December 10, 2025, under SEBI Listing Regulations. The total issued equity shares stand at 3,056,925 with scrip code 3416431. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 74.64% while public shareholders hold 25.36% of the total equity.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 3,056,925 (fully paid up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 2,281,615 shares (74.64%)
- Public shareholding: 775,310 shares (25.36%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 9
- Number of public shareholders: 541
- Locked-in shares (Promoter): 27,000 shares (1.18% of promoter holding)
- Locked-in shares (Public): 12,000 shares (0.52% of public holding)
- Total dematerialized shares: 3,056,925 (100%)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated in this filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filed under Regulation 31(1) of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Shareholding pattern disclosure as on December 10, 2025
- Company has declared no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or depository receipts
- Company has significant beneficial owners
- Shares are held in locked-in status by both promoters and public
- Some promoter shares are pledged or encumbered

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: December 10, 2025
- Filing date: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure filing with no immediate market impact. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding above 74%, indicating strong promoter commitment. The presence of locked-in shares (both promoter and public) suggests recent listing or allotment activity. The filing is purely informational and meets regulatory compliance requirements under SEBI Listing Regulations.
