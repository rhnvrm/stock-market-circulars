## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) has issued a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 751 dated November 10, 2025. The circular lists securities and their quantities that are being put up for auction to settle outstanding obligations. The auction includes a diverse range of securities from various companies across different sectors, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 33,500 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 751
- Date of Circular: November 10, 2025
- Total securities listed: Over 100 different scrips
- Includes major stocks like HDFC Bank (50 shares), Reliance Industries (100 shares), Infosys (74 shares), and Hindustan Unilever (48 shares)
- Largest quantities include: Wardwizard I (33,500 shares), Standard Cap (25,000 shares), Vodafone Idea (22,000 shares), and Sagility Lim (15,433 shares)
- Covers multiple sectors including banking, FMCG, IT, pharmaceuticals, textiles, and infrastructure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding settlement obligations for the listed securities must participate in the auction process
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities are available for auction settlement
- Settlement participants must comply with BSE's standard auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 10, 2025 (Settlement No. 751)
- Auction date and time would be communicated separately as per BSE's standard auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine settlement auction that occurs regularly to clear outstanding obligations. The quantities involved are relatively small for most securities and unlikely to cause significant market movements.

**Operational Impact:** Low to Medium - Affects only those trading members who have failed delivery obligations in Settlement No. 751. These members will need to participate in the auction to fulfill their settlement obligations.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Regular investors are not directly affected unless they are settlement participants with outstanding obligations. The auction mechanism ensures smooth settlement and market integrity.
