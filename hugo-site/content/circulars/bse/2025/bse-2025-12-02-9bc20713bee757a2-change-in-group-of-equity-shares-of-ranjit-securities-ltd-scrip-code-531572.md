## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be moved to the 'X' group effective December 09, 2025. This group change affects trading parameters and surveillance for the scrip.

## Key Points

- Company: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531572
- New Group: X Group
- Effective Date: December 09, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251202-12
- Category: Company related - Equity Segment

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be transferred from their current group to the 'X' group, which typically indicates stocks under surveillance or with special trading restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change
- Enhanced due diligence may be required for transactions in this scrip
- Trading members requiring clarification can contact Mrs. Kinjal More at +91-7304278788

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Effective Date: December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Movement to X group typically results in:
- Enhanced surveillance measures
- Potential impact on liquidity
- Stricter monitoring of price movements
- Trading members need to adjust risk management parameters for this scrip
