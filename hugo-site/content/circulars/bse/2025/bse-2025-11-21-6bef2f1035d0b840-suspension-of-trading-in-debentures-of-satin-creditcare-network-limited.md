## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures (ISIN: INE836B07675, Code: 974417) of SATIN CREDITCARE NETWORK LIMITED effective November 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption on exercise of PUT option and interest payment.

## Key Points

- Debenture: SCNL-12.15%-12-12-27-PVT (INE836B07675)
- BSE Code: 974417
- Record Date: November 26, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption on exercise of PUT option and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-764/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for debenture trading suspension ahead of record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debenture from November 26, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension notice
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in SCNL debenture (Code: 974417)

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 26, 2025**: Record date for PUT option redemption and interest payment
- **November 26, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific non-convertible debenture. Debenture holders have PUT option exercise opportunity for redemption along with interest payment entitlement.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems block trading in specified debenture. Holders on record date will be eligible for interest payment and can exercise PUT option for early redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Only impacts investors holding or intending to trade this specific debenture instrument. Those on record will receive interest and redemption proceeds if PUT option exercised.
