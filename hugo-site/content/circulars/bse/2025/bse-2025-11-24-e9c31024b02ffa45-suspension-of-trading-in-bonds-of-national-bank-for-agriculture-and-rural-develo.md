## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in bonds of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) effective December 2, 2025. The suspension applies to the NABARD 7.50% bonds (ISIN: INE261F08DT8, Code: 974415) maturing on December 17, 2025, due to the record date being fixed for redemption and interest payment purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in specified NABARD bonds from December 2, 2025
- Suspension applies to NABARD-7.50%-17-12-25-PVT (ISIN: INE261F08DT8, BSE Code: 974415)
- Record date set for December 2, 2025 for both redemption and interest payment
- Circular reference: DR-768/2025-2026
- Notice issued November 20, 2025, effective for transactions from December 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealing in the specified NABARD bonds from December 2, 2025
- Trading Members must ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension
- No new buy or sell orders should be accepted for the specified bond from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 2, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 2, 2025
- **Bond Maturity**: December 17, 2025
- **Circular Issued**: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only one bond series of NABARD nearing maturity. Bondholders expecting redemption on December 17, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these bonds will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading liquidity available from December 2, 2025 onwards, though this is a standard practice close to maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific bond from December 2, 2025. Standard end-of-life process for maturing debt securities.
