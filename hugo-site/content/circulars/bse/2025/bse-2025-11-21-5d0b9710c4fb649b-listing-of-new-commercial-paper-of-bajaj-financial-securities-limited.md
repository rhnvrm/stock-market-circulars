## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities with scrip code 730615 (BFSL201125) are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The issue comprises 5,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 250 crores, with a maturity date of February 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,933.50 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 5,000 units (Rs. 250 crores)
- **Scrip Code**: 730615
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL201125
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DL6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members**:
- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DL6
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term funding instrument for institutional investors in the debt market.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt segment trading procedures apply. Trading members dealing in debt securities can participate using existing infrastructure.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net worth individuals in the debt market segment, given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination requirement.
