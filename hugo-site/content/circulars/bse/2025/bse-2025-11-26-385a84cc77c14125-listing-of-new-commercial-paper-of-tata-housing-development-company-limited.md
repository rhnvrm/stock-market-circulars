## Summary

BSE notifies trading members about the listing of new Commercial Papers issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 1000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh face value each
- Scrip Code: 730660, Scrip ID: THDC251125
- ISIN: INE582L14JA0
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,425 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these securities are conducted in dematerialised form only
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing for a specific issuer. The Commercial Paper is targeted at institutional investors and debt market participants. The 91-day maturity indicates short-term financing by Tata Housing Development Company Limited. The high credit ratings (A1+) reflect strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
