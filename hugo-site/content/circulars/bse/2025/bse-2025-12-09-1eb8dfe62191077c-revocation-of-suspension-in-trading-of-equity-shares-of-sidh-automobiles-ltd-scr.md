## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Sidh Automobiles Limited (Scrip Code: 539983). The company, which operates as a Non-Deposit Accepting Non-Banking Finance Company (NBFC), can now resume normal trading operations on the Bombay Stock Exchange. The company is registered in New Delhi and has maintained active operational status.

## Key Points

- **Company Name**: Sidh Automobiles Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 539983
- **ISIN**: INE403101015
- **Action**: Revocation of trading suspension
- **Business Nature**: Non-Deposit Accepting Non-Banking Finance Company
- **Registered Address**: R-13, S/F Greater Kailash-1, New Delhi, Delhi, 110048
- **Exchange**: Listed only on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)
- **Listing Fees**: Paid to all exchanges
- **Operational Status**: Active

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The revocation enables shareholders and potential investors to trade Sidh Automobiles Limited shares on BSE, restoring market liquidity for the security. This is particularly significant for existing shareholders who may have been unable to exit their positions during the suspension period.

**Investor Impact**: Existing investors can now trade their holdings, and new investors can purchase shares. The restoration of trading suggests the company has addressed the concerns that led to the initial suspension.

**Operational Impact**: The company can now access equity capital markets if needed, and the lifting of suspension removes a negative signal that may have affected the company's business relationships and credibility.
