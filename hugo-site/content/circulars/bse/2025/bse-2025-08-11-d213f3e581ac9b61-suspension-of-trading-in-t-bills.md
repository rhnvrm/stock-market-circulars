## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 12, 2025, as they approach their redemption date. The suspension affects 182TB14825, 91TB140825, and 364TB15825.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on their respective redemption dates
- Trading suspension takes effect from August 12, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-693/2025-2026
- Applies to all Trading Members of the Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard procedural suspension due to T-Bills reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 12, 2025
- Trading Members are advised to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 11, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: August 12, 2025
- T-Bills Maturity: On respective redemption dates

## Impact Assessment

Routine impact on debt market operations. Trading Members dealing in these specific T-Bills need to adjust their portfolios and trading strategies accordingly. Standard market procedure with no broader market implications.
