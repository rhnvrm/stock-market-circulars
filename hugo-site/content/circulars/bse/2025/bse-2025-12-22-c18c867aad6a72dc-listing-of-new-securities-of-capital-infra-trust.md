## Summary

Capital Infra Trust (Scrip Code: 544338, ISIN: INE0Z8Z23013) has listed 17,28,90,733 new units on BSE following an institutional placement to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs). The units were allotted at Rs. 72.30 per unit and will commence trading from December 23, 2025. These units rank pari-passu with existing units of the trust.

## Key Points

- Total units listed: 17,28,90,733 units
- Allotted exclusively to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs)
- Issue price: Rs. 72.30 per unit
- Distinctive numbers: 318661001 to 491551733
- New units rank pari-passu with existing units
- Scrip Code: 544338
- ISIN: INE0Z8Z23013

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following institutional placement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Note the listing of new securities effective December 23, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the increased number of tradable units
- Contact Mr. Pranav Rewale (9653268851) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 22, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 19, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 23, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 17.29 crore units represents a significant institutional placement for Capital Infra Trust. The institutional placement to QIBs indicates strong institutional interest. The units being pari-passu with existing units ensures no dilution of rights for existing unitholders. Trading members should expect increased liquidity and trading volumes following the listing. The relatively low issue price of Rs. 72.30 per unit makes it accessible for broader market participation once trading begins.
