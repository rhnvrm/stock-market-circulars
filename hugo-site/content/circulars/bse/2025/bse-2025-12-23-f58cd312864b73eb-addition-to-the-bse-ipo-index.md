## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of KSH INTERNATIONAL LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544664) to the BSE IPO Index effective December 26, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE on December 23, 2025, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- KSH INTERNATIONAL LIMITED (ticker: 544664) listed on BSE on December 23, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective at open on December 26, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to earlier Notice No: 20251222-39 regarding the listing
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to BSE IPO Index may need to rebalance to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Listing date for KSH INTERNATIONAL LIMITED on BSE
- **December 26, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action following a new IPO listing. Impact limited to:
- Index tracking funds benchmarked to BSE IPO Index will need to acquire shares
- Increased visibility and potential demand from passive investment strategies
- Standard corporate action processing for market participants
