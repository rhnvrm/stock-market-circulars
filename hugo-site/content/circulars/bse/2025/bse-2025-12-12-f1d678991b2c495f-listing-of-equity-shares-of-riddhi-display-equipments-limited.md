## Summary

Riddhi Display Equipments Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the listing of its equity shares post-IPO on December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total number of shareholders: 714 (6 Promoter & Promoter Group, 708 Public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 6,171,174 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 2,468,815 equity shares
- Total issued shares: 8,639,989 equity shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- Promoter shares are subject to lock-in restrictions
- No pledging or other encumbrances on promoter shares
- All shares held in dematerialized form
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial ownership issues

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable. This is a routine compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) for post-IPO shareholding disclosure
- Company has declared compliance with all applicable SEBI (LODR) requirements
- Shareholding pattern reflects the position as of December 12, 2025 (post-IPO)

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Date of shareholding pattern filing (post-IPO listing)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard regulatory filing with minimal market impact. The disclosure provides transparency on the ownership structure of Riddhi Display Equipments Limited following its IPO listing. The presence of lock-in on promoter shares (71.4% of total shares) indicates compliance with standard IPO regulations. No immediate trading restrictions or operational changes are indicated beyond normal post-IPO lock-in requirements.
