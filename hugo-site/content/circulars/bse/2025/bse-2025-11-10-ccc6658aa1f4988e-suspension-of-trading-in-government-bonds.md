## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Government Bond 812MHSDL25 (ISIN: IN2220150121, Scrip Code: 810716) effective November 11, 2025, as the bond is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this bond from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Government Bond 812MHSDL25 will mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810716
- ISIN: IN2220150121
- Trading suspension begins November 11, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-753/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for bond maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Government Bond (812MHSDL25) from November 11, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No transactions should be executed in this security post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Bond Maturity**: On redemption date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific government bond. This is a routine corporate action related to bond maturity with no broader market implications. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the suspension to avoid inadvertent transactions. The single bond suspension has minimal operational impact on the overall debt market.
