## Summary

Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has listed 8000 units of Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment on private placement basis. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings, with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and a tenure of 91 days from allotment to redemption.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 8000 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 400 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730541
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL111125
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DG6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DG6
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing with no significant market-wide implications. The instrument provides short-term funding to Bajaj Financial Securities Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members are notified of new debt securities available for trading on BSE Debt segment with standard trading parameters.

**Investor Impact**: Low - High credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk for investors in this short-term debt instrument.
