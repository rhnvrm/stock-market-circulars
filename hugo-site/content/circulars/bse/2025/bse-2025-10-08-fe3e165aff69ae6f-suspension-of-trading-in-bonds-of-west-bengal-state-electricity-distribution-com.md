## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of West Bengal State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd (WBSEDC) effective October 10, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for bond redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond ISIN: INE411L07023 (Code: 972599)
- Bond Series: WBSEDC-9.34%-II-25-10-25-PVT
- Record Date: October 10, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 10, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Bonds and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-733/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational procedure for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified WBSEDC bonds from October 10, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date provisions for bondholders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 08, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: October 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific WBSEDC bond series. The suspension prevents trading to ensure proper identification of bondholders eligible for final interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders on record as of October 10, 2025 will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Trading suspension is temporary and related to the maturity/redemption event.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in this security and inform clients holding or interested in these bonds.
