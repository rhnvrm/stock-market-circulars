## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). This circular covers 73,260 shares with certificate serial numbers ranging from 2082758719 to 2082831978, subject to a lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Total shares being listed: 73,260
- Share certificate serial number range: 2082758719 to 2082831978
- Lock-in status: Yes
- Lock-in period: December 19, 2025 to January 17, 2026 (29 days)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP/ESOS shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must adhere to the specified lock-in period for the listed shares
- Shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period from December 19, 2025 to January 17, 2026
- Standard ESOP/ESOS listing compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Lock-in End Date**: January 17, 2026
- **Circular Date**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing of 73,260 ESOP/ESOS shares represents a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The shares are subject to a short lock-in period of approximately one month.

**Operational Impact**: Low. This is a standard administrative process for companies issuing employee stock options. The lock-in period ensures compliance with regulatory requirements for ESOP/ESOS issuances.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible in the short term due to lock-in restrictions; minimal long-term impact given the relatively small number of shares.
