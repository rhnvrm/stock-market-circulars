## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 158/2025-2026 dated November 19, 2025, announces record dates and book closure information for various corporate actions across multiple securities. The bulletin covers commercial paper redemptions, non-convertible debenture interest payments and partial redemptions, privately placed bonds interest payments, and InvIT income distribution.

## Key Points

- Multiple commercial paper redemptions scheduled between November 25, 2025 and February 12, 2026
- Adani Enterprises Ltd has two CP redemption record dates: November 25 and November 27, 2025
- HDFC Bank Ltd has NCD interest payment record dates on December 23, 2025 and January 10, 2026
- Altius Telecom Infrastructure Trust has income distribution record date on November 24, 2025
- Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has three CP redemption dates in February 2026
- Piramal Finance Limited has combined interest payment and partial redemption on November 27, 2025
- Mahaveer Finance India Limited has two events: interest payment on December 16, 2025 and partial redemption with interest on December 19, 2025
- All securities will have trading flag "1" (trading continues) on their respective record dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities on the respective record dates to be eligible for announced corporate actions
- Trading continues for all listed securities on their record dates (Trading flag: 1)
- Issuers must ensure timely payment/redemption on scheduled dates
- No book closure periods announced; only record dates specified

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Altius Telecom Infrastructure Trust - Income Distribution
- **November 25, 2025**: Adani Enterprises Ltd - CP Redemption
- **November 26, 2025**: IFCI Ltd - Bond Interest Payment
- **November 27, 2025**: Adani Enterprises Ltd - CP Redemption; IKF Finance Limited - NCD Interest; Piramal Finance Limited - NCD Interest & Partial Redemption
- **December 1-2, 2025**: HDFC Securities Limited - CP Redemptions
- **December 4, 2025**: Manba Finance Limited - NCD Interest
- **December 16, 2025**: Mahaveer Finance India Limited - NCD Interest; Electrosteel Castings Ltd (December 21) - CP Redemption
- **December 19, 2025**: Mahaveer Finance India Limited - Partial NCD Redemption & Interest
- **December 23, 2025**: HDFC Bank Ltd - NCD Interest
- **January 10, 2026**: HDFC Bank Ltd - NCD Interest
- **January 21, 2026**: NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED - Partial NCD Redemption & Interest
- **February 9-12, 2026**: Network 18 Media & Investments Limited - CP Redemptions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational bulletin with no market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for specific security holders. Investors holding these securities must be aware of record dates to receive entitled payments, interest, or redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact**: Standard operational processing required by registrars, depositories, and issuers to process corporate actions on scheduled dates.

**Liquidity Impact**: No trading suspensions announced; all securities continue trading normally on record dates, maintaining market liquidity.
