## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Shipwaves Online Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544646, symbol SHIPWAVES, and ISIN number INE0O9L01010. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shipwaves Online Limited
- Scrip Code: 544646
- Symbol: SHIPWAVES
- ISIN: INE0O9L01010
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be informed separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Limited market impact as this is a routine SME segment listing announcement. The listing will add one more security to the SME platform. Trading members should prepare for the new listing once the specific date is announced. No immediate operational changes required.
