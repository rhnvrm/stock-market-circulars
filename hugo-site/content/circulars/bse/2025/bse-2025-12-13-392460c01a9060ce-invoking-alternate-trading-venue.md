## Summary

BSE has invoked the alternate trading venue mechanism, making BSE exclusive scrips in the Equity segment available for trading on NSE at 2:39 PM on December 13, 2025. This notice references exchange notice no 20251211-75 dated December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- BSE exclusive scrips in Equity segment will be available for trading on NSE
- Trading on NSE will commence at 2:39 PM on December 13, 2025
- This action is in reference to previous exchange notice no 20251211-75 dated December 11, 2025
- Notification applies to all trading members

## Regulatory Changes

Activation of alternate trading venue provisions allowing BSE-exclusive securities to be traded on NSE temporarily.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the availability of BSE exclusive scrips on NSE from the specified time and adjust their trading operations accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **December 13, 2025 at 2:39 PM**: BSE exclusive scrips become available for trading on NSE
- **December 11, 2025**: Date of referenced previous exchange notice (20251211-75)

## Impact Assessment

High impact on market operations as BSE exclusive scrips will be traded on an alternate venue (NSE). This provides continuity of trading and liquidity for these securities. Trading members need to be aware of this venue change to execute orders appropriately. The invocation of alternate trading venue suggests potential operational issues or circumstances requiring this contingency measure.
