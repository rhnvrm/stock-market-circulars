## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 14 equity scrips effective August 19, 2025. The revised price bands range from 2% to 20%, with most companies receiving moderate restrictions between 2% to 10%.

## Key Points

- 14 scrips affected with price band revisions
- Changes effective from August 19, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (lowest) to 20% (highest)
- Two companies (Repono Ltd and Umiya Mobile Ltd) receive the highest 20% price band
- Most companies receive moderate price bands between 2% to 10%

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions implemented as surveillance measure to control excessive volatility in the affected scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to revised price bands from August 19, 2025
- Any clarifications should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The price band changes will limit daily price movements for these 14 scrips, potentially reducing volatility and speculative trading while ensuring orderly market conditions.
