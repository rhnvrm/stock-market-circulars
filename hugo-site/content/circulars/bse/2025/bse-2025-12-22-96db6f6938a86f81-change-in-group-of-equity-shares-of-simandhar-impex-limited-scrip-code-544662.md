## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Simandhar Impex Limited (Scrip Code - 544662) will be transferred to the 'B' group effective Tuesday, January 06, 2026. This administrative change affects the trading classification of the company's securities on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Simandhar Impex Limited
- Scrip Code: 544662
- New Group: 'B' Group
- Effective Date: Tuesday, January 06, 2026
- Notice Number: 20251222-14
- Notice Date: December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be permitted to trade under the 'B' group classification starting January 06, 2026. The 'B' group typically represents securities that meet certain listing and compliance criteria as per BSE's classification framework.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants must note the group change and adjust their trading systems and procedures accordingly before the effective date of January 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The group change may affect trading dynamics, liquidity, and investor perception of Simandhar Impex Limited's equity shares. Market participants should update their records and systems to reflect the new group classification. The move to 'B' group generally indicates compliance with BSE's listing requirements and may positively influence trading activity.
