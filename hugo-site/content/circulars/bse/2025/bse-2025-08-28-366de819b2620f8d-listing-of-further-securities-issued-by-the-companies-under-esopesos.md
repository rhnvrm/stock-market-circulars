## Summary

BSE has listed additional equity shares issued by 13 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) for trading effective August 29, 2025. The total shares being listed amount to over 8.4 million shares across various companies with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have issued further securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All shares will be admitted for trading on BSE from August 29, 2025
- Total shares being listed: 8,448,555 shares
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed shares
- Largest issuance by Vishal Mega Mart Limited with 7.93 million shares
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Companies have completed ESOP issuance formalities
- Shares are assigned specific ISIN numbers and scrip codes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025 (Friday) - Trading begins
- **Notice Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Not Applicable (NA) for all companies

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings. The shares represent employee compensation and are typically issued at predetermined prices. No immediate price volatility anticipated, though increased float may marginally affect share prices of individual companies over time.
