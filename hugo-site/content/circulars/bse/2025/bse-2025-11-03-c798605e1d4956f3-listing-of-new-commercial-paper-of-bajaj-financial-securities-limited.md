## Summary

Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crore (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment. The paper was issued on private placement basis on October 31, 2025, and will mature on November 7, 2025 (7-day tenure). The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 150 crore (3,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Scrip Code: 730472, Scrip ID: BFSL311025
- ISIN: INE01C314DC5
- Issue price: Rs. 4,99,392 per unit (against face value of Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under ISIN Number INE01C314DC5

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 7-day maturity. The instrument provides short-term liquidity to Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offers investment opportunity for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading will be restricted to institutional and high-net-worth investors due to the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
