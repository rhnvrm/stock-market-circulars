## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the non-convertible debentures of GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE219O07362, Scrip Code: 974946) effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from December 4, 2025 onwards.

## Key Points

- Company: GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE219O07362
- Scrip Code: 974946
- Record Date: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: December 4, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026
- Circular issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification pursuant to existing regulations governing debt securities trading and corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients about the suspension
- No dealing or trading activity is permitted in the debenture (INE219O07362) starting from the record date

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 4, 2025**: Effective date for trading suspension (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited market impact as this affects a single private limited company's debenture issue. The suspension is temporary and related to a standard corporate action.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED debentures will receive interest payment and principal redemption based on the December 4, 2025 record date. No trading opportunity available from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance by blocking trades in this security from December 4, 2025. Systems should be updated to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended debenture.
