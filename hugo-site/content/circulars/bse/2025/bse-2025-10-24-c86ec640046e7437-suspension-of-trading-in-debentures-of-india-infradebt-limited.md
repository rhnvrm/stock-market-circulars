## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of India Infradebt Limited (ISIN: INE537P07588, Code: 960203) effective October 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to 7% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing on November 12, 2025
- ISIN: INE537P07588, BSE Code: 960203
- No dealings permitted from October 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and interest payment
- Circular reference: DR-744/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension pursuant to corporate action procedures for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from October 28, 2025
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in debenture ISIN INE537P07588
- Trading members must inform their clients about the suspension
- Compliance with record date procedures for determining eligible debenture holders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 28, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** October 28, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity:** November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and investor ability to enter/exit positions in these debentures.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders as of record date (October 28, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. New investors cannot purchase these debentures after suspension.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in these securities.

**Timeline Impact:** With only 4 days notice before suspension and 15 days until maturity, investors have limited time for any portfolio adjustments.
