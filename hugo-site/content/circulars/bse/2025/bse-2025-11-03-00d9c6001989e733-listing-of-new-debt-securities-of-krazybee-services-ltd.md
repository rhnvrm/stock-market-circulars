## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to dealings of new debt securities issued by Krazybee Services Ltd on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 3, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with an 11.50% interest rate and maturity date of March 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977262
- Scrip ID: 1150KSL27
- ISIN: INE07HK07858
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000.00
- Quantity: 12,500 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CARE A
- Interest Rate: 11.50% per annum (paid monthly)
- Date of Allotment: October 31, 2025
- Maturity Date: March 29, 2027
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE07HK07858
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **November 3, 2025**: Listing effective date
- **October 31, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **March 29, 2027**: Redemption date
- **Monthly**: Interest payment frequency

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for privately placed debt securities with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with tradable debt instruments from Krazybee Services Ltd with a CARE A credit rating and an 11.50% annual interest rate. The impact is limited to participants in the debt market interested in this specific issuer.
