## Summary

BSE has admitted two new entities as Deposit Based Trading Members with trading rights in Cash and Equity Derivatives segments. Finverse Platform Private Limited (Member No. 6967) and Religo Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6968) have been granted new membership status subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Two new trading members admitted to BSE
- Finverse Platform Private Limited assigned Member Number 6967
- Religo Broking Private Limited assigned Member Number 6968
- Both members have trading rights in Cash and Equity Derivatives segments
- Membership type: Deposit Based with trading rights only on BSE Ltd
- Admissions subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
