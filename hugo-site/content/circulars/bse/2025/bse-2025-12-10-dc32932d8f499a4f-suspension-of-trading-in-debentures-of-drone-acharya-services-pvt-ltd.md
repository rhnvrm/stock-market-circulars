## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the non-convertible debentures of Drone Acharya Services Pvt Ltd (scrip code 975944, ISIN: INE0WWM07010) effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: DASPL-21%-21-5-27-PVT (INE0WWM07010)
- Scrip code: 975944
- Record date: December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: December 11, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 11, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients holding these debentures are informed about the suspension and upcoming redemption

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series of Drone Acharya Services Pvt Ltd. As this is a private limited company's debt instrument, the market impact is minimal and affects only specific institutional or qualified investors holding these securities. The suspension is temporary and standard procedure for processing redemption and interest payments. Trading will resume after the corporate action is completed, unless the debentures are fully redeemed.
