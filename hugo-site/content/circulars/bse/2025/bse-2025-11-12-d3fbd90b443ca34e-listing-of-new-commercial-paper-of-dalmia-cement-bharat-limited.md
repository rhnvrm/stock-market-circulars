## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis, effective November 12, 2025. The debt securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with ISIN INE755K14FP0.

## Key Points

- 2000 commercial papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crores (2000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakhs)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,473.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730542, Scrip ID: DCL111125
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FP0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment for short-term funding by Dalmia Cement. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Trading is restricted to institutional participants in the debt market with minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs, limiting retail participation. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
