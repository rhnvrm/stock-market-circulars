## Summary

TILAK VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 503663) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 15, 2025. The company will issue 2 equity shares of Re.1/- each for every 1 equity share held, at par value. Trading in the company's shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 15, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-777/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: TILAK VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 503663)
- Rights Ratio: 2:1 (Two new shares for every one existing share)
- Face Value: Re.1/- per equity share
- Issue Price: Re.1/- per share (at par), payable on application

- Issue Type: Cash rights issue

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the record date and ex-rights date for proper settlement
- Shareholders holding shares as of the record date will be entitled to participate in the rights issue

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-777/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to 2 additional shares for every 1 share held, potentially doubling their shareholding at par value. Non-participation will result in dilution of their ownership stake.

**Market Impact**: The ex-rights trading from December 15, 2025 will result in price adjustment to reflect the rights entitlement. The 2:1 ratio represents significant equity expansion.

**Liquidity Impact**: Increased share capital will potentially improve liquidity in the stock post-rights issue, though immediate trading will reflect the ex-rights adjustment.

**Financial Impact**: The issue at par value (Re.1/-) suggests the company is raising capital for business operations, with full payment due on application.
