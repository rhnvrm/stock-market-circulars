## Summary

BSE has issued a correction notice regarding the transfer of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) to the 'X' group. The corrected transfer date is December 17, 2025, replacing the previously communicated date in Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025. All other contents of the original notice remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531572
- Group Transfer: Moving to 'X' group
- Corrected Transfer Date: December 17, 2025
- Original Notice Reference: Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025
- All other terms of the original notice remain valid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a correction to a previously announced group transfer, updating only the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to:
- Note the corrected transfer date of December 17, 2025
- Update their systems and records accordingly
- Ensure compliance with X group trading requirements from the corrected date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Corrected Transfer Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Original Notice Date**: December 02, 2025 (Notice No. 20251202-12)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this correction is minimal and limited to:
- Trading members who handle Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572)
- Requires a one-day adjustment from previously communicated transfer date
- No change to the fact of transfer to X group or other conditions
- X group classification typically indicates securities with specific trading restrictions or surveillance measures
