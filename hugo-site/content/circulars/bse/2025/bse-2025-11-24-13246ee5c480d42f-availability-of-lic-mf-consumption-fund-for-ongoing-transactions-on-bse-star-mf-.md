## Summary

LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND is reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 25, 2025. This follows the previous notice No 20251030-26 which had suspended these transactions.

## Key Points

- LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND reopening for all ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Effective date: November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a reversal of a previous suspension notice (Notice No 20251030-26).

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of these schemes for transactions from November 25, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 25, 2025 - LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND available for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational update affecting only investors and distributors dealing with LIC MF CONSUMPTION FUND schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The reopening restores normal trading functionality for these six specific scheme variants (ISINs: INF767K01SA6, INF767K01SC2, INF767K01SB4, INF767K01SD0, INF767K01SF5, INF767K01SE8).
