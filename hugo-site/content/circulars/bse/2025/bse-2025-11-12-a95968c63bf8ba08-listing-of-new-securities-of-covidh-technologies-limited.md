## Summary

BSE has listed 80,85,550 new equity shares of COVIDH TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 534920) issued on a rights basis. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, November 13, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 80,85,550 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each par value issued on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 534920
- ISIN: INE899M01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 323423 to 8408972
- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Trading Start Date: November 13, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The listing increases the outstanding share capital of COVIDH TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED by 80,85,550 shares through a rights issue. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue can now trade these shares on BSE.
