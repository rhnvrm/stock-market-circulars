## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L27320GJ2022PLC135697. The company's name has been changed from SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED to DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on September 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED
- **New Name**: DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED
- **CIN**: L27320GJ2022PLC135697
- **Effective Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Original Name**: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Registered Address**: 5, Harekrishna Industrial Estate, Bakrol, Daskroi, Ahmedabad-382430, Gujarat, India
- **Certificate Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Issued By**: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard certificate of incorporation pursuant to change of name under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED) for a period of **two years** along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 29, 2025 (digitally signed on October 8, 2025 at 15:12:01 IST)
- **Display Period for Old Name**: Two years from September 29, 2025 (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with minimal market impact. The name change:

- Does not affect the company's CIN, legal status, or corporate identity
- Does not impact existing rights, liabilities, contracts, or obligations of the company
- Does not affect shareholding patterns or trading operations
- Reflects a change in business focus from metals and alloys to kitchen utensils and appliances
- Requires stakeholders to update records with the new company name
- May require minor administrative adjustments for contractual documentation and correspondence
