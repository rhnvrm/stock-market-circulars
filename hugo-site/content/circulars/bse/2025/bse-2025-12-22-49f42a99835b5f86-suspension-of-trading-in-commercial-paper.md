## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE423A14XT1, Code: 729731) of Adani Enterprises Ltd effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is necessitated by the company fixing December 23, 2025 as the record date for redemption of the Commercial Paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Adani Enterprises Ltd
- Instrument: Commercial Paper
- ISIN: INE423A14XT1
- BSE Code: 729731
- Record Date: December 23, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 23, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Notice Reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification for debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper of Adani Enterprises Ltd from December 23, 2025
- Members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension to avoid regulatory violations
- No transactions should be executed in the instrument with ISIN INE423A14XT1 and Code 729731

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Suspension Effective From**: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific Commercial Paper instrument. The suspension is procedural and related to the natural maturity/redemption cycle of the debt instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt segment must update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in the suspended instrument. Investors holding this Commercial Paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of this Commercial Paper will be identified based on the December 23, 2025 record date for redemption purposes. The suspension prevents any changes in holdings that would affect redemption entitlements.
