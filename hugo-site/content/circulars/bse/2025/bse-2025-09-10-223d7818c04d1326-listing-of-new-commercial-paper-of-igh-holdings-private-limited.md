## Summary

IGH Holdings Private Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 150 crores (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE's Debt segment. The securities carry high credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ and will mature on December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,568 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730181, ISIN: INE02FN14648
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities traded under ISIN Number INE02FN14648

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 10, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low risk profile. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in the 3-month maturity segment.
