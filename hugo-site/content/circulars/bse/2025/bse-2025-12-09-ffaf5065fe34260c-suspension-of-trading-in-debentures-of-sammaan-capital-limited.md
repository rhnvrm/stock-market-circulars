## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in six series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED effective December 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing December 12, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Six debenture series of Sammaan Capital Limited affected by trading suspension
- Record date fixed: December 12, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reference number: DR-776/2025-2026
- All affected debentures have maturity date of December 27, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from December 12, 2025
- All trading activity in these six debenture series must cease from the suspension date
- Members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity Date**: December 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to debenture holders of Sammaan Capital Limited. The suspension is a standard procedure for processing redemption and interest payments.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will not be able to trade these securities from December 12, 2025. However, this is expected as the debentures are approaching maturity on December 27, 2025. Eligible holders as per the record date will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these six debenture series and communicate the suspension to affected clients.
