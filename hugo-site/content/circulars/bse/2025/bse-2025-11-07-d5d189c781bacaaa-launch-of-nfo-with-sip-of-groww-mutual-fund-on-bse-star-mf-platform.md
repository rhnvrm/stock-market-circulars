## Summary

GROWW Mutual Fund is launching GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from November 10-17, 2025, with tentative allotment on November 24, 2025. The scheme offers six variants covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options under the debt category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 10-17, 2025
- Tentative allotment date: November 24, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All variants are debt category money market funds
- SIP facility available during NFO period
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds by November 19, 2025 at 6:00 PM
- NFO extended till midnight on November 17, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Transaction Cancellation**: Transactions for which clients have not initiated funds till 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period transactions) will be cancelled and CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC
- **Payment Pending**: Transactions with initiated client payments will remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of making funds payout to AMC for the NFO or T+5, whichever is earlier
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 19, 2025
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication**: Client authentication for SIP/XSIP registrations must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (November 17, 2025)
- **Switch Transactions**: Funds for switch-out schemes must be received on or before the allotment date (November 24, 2025) to be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **November 17, 2025**: NFO closing date (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication; midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 19, 2025**: Clear funds deadline in ICCL Account (6:00 PM)
- **November 24, 2025**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch that expands product offerings on the BSE StAR MF Platform without affecting existing market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for BSE StAR MF Platform members who need to:
- Process NFO transactions during the specified period
- Ensure client fund transfers meet the specified deadlines
- Handle SIP/XSIP registrations with proper authentication before cutoff time
- Manage switch transactions with attention to fund receipt timing

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides additional investment option in the debt/money market category for investors seeking short-term debt instruments through both Regular and Direct plan routes with multiple payout options.
