## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the 10% Non-Convertible Debentures of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (ISIN: INE101Q07AN4, Code: 975197) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing October 26, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Company: Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- Debenture: MMFL-10%-10-11-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE101Q07AN4
- BSE Code: 975197
- Record Date: October 26, 2025
- Trading Suspension Start: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Dealing in ISIN INE101Q07AN4 is prohibited during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **October 26, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited. The suspension is a routine procedural measure to facilitate proper record keeping for corporate actions. Trading members holding or trading these debentures need to ensure no transactions are executed during the suspension period. The suspension protects the integrity of the redemption and interest payment process by freezing the holder registry.
