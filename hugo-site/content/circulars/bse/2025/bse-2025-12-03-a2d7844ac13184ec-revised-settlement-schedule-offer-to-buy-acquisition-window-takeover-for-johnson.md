## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. The takeover bid period ran from November 13, 2025 to November 26, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025119). The revised obligation download date is December 8, 2025, with settlement on December 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover, Settlement No. 2025119
- Bid Period: November 13, 2025 to November 26, 2025
- Revised Obligation Download: December 8, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: December 9, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No. 2025119 in the depository system
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the settlement schedule previously announced in Notice No. 20251112-9. Members and custodians should follow the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Must specify Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No. 2025119 in depository system transactions
- Members must refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Follow guidelines specified in Notice No. 20200528-32 for acquisition mechanism through Stock Exchange

## Impact Assessment

This revision impacts members and custodians who participated in the takeover offer for Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. They need to adjust their settlement timelines and ensure compliance with the revised schedule. The change provides additional time for obligation fulfillment and settlement completion.
