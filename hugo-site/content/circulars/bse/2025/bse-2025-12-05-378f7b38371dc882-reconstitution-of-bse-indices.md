## Summary

BSE has announced the reconstitution of its key indices effective December 22, 2025. The changes affect BSE India 150, BSE 150 Midcap Index, BSE 100 Largecap TMC, BSE 250 Largemidcap Index, and BSE Allcap Index. Multiple stocks will be added and removed from these indices as part of the periodic rebalancing process.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date**: December 22, 2025
- **BSE India 150**: 4 additions and 4 deletions
- **BSE 150 Midcap Index**: 10 additions and 10 deletions
- **BSE 100 Largecap TMC**: 3 additions and 3 deletions
- **BSE 250 Largemidcap Index**: 8 additions and 8 deletions
- **BSE Allcap**: Multiple deletions (Shri Jagdamba Polymers, Dolphin Offshore Enterprises, Hampton Sky - content truncated)
- Index reconstitution impacts passive fund rebalancing and stock weightages

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic index reconstitution by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Passive funds and ETFs tracking these indices must rebalance their portfolios to reflect the new composition
- Fund managers need to execute trades on or before December 22, 2025
- Disclosure requirements as per fund mandates may apply

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025**: Effective date for all index reconstitution changes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Stocks being added to indices may see buying pressure from passive funds, potentially increasing prices and liquidity
- Stocks being removed may face selling pressure as index funds divest holdings
- Highest impact expected on stocks moving in/out of BSE India 150 and BSE 100 Largecap TMC due to larger fund tracking

**Trading Impact:**
- Increased trading volumes expected around December 22, 2025 for affected stocks
- Potential volatility in affected securities during rebalancing period

**Investor Considerations:**
- Passive investors in index funds/ETFs will automatically see portfolio changes
- Active investors may anticipate rebalancing flows for tactical positioning
