## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper issues by Redington Limited on private placement basis, each with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs. Both securities carry high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) and will trade in dematerialized form with HDFC Bank as issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper issues listed: RL-7-10-25-CP and RL-4-11-25-CP
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 each with 4000 quantities per issue
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- First CP allotment date: August 8, 2025, redemption: October 7, 2025
- Second CP allotment date: August 7, 2025, redemption: November 4, 2025
- Trading effective from: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding options for Redington Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
