## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The issue comprises 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 300 crores, with a 91-day maturity period from December 4, 2025 to March 5, 2026. The instruments carry credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The 91-day maturity instrument provides short-term funding to Network 18 Media & Investments Limited at competitive rates (implied discount rate of approximately 6% annualized). The A1+ credit ratings from both ICRA and India Ratings indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing expands the debt market inventory but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
