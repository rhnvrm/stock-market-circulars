## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited worth Rs. 20 crores issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 9% p.a. interest rate and mature on July 22, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977055, ISIN: INE918K07PZ6
- Interest Rate: 9% p.a. payable annually
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,030 per security
- Credit Rating: CARE AA
- Market lot size: 1 security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members can refer placement memorandum on BSE website

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 22, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from August 24, 2026 to July 22, 2027
- Redemption Date: July 22, 2027
- Trading Effective: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt listing. The securities are issued on private placement basis with no put/call options, indicating standard institutional investment.
