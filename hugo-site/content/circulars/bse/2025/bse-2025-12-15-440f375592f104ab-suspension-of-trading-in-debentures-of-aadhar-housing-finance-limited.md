## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED. The company has fixed December 22, 2025, as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are prohibited from dealing in the specified debentures starting from the record date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debenture series AHFL-9.6%-6-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE538L07163, Code: 953177)
- Record date set for December 22, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 22, 2025, onwards
- Notice issued under reference number DR-782/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension related to corporate action events (debenture redemption and interest payment).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 22, 2025
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive
- Members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension
- All trading platforms and systems should reflect the suspension status

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 22, 2025 (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High impact for holders of these specific debentures as trading liquidity will be completely eliminated from the record date. Investors will not be able to buy or sell these securities on the exchange.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and inform clients about the suspension. The suspension is necessary to determine eligible holders for redemption proceeds and interest payments.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will be eligible for redemption proceeds and interest payment. Those wishing to exit positions must do so before December 22, 2025.
