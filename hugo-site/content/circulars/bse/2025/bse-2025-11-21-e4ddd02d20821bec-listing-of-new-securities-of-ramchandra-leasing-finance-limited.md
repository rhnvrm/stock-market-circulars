## Summary

BSE has listed 3,00,00,000 equity shares of Ramchandra Leasing & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 538540) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares are permitted to trade from Monday, November 24, 2025. The shares were allotted on September 2, 2025 at an issue price of Rs. 2.26 per share (face value Re. 1 plus premium of Rs. 1.26) and are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 3 crore equity shares of Re. 1 face value each listed
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis at Rs. 2.26 per share
- Issue price includes premium of Rs. 1.26 per share
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from November 24, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 51162001 to 81162000
- ISIN: INE516P01015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and trading permission for these new securities. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 24, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters with minimal market impact. All 3 crore shares are locked-in until June 30, 2027, preventing immediate trading by allottees. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining equal rights. The preferential issue strengthens promoter holding and capital base of the company.
