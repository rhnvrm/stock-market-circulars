## Summary

BSE has notified the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on a private placement basis. The securities totaling Rs. 375 crores (7,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) have been listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The Commercial Paper carries a maturity date of October 7, 2026, with credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- 7,500 units of Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed (total Rs. 375 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730616, Scrip ID: KMPL211125
- ISIN: INE916D146C9
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,72,103 per unit (Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: November 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 7, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE916D146C9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities must be traded through the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 7, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 10.5 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited with short-term funding through debt markets. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong credit quality. The securities are available for trading by institutional and qualified investors in the debt segment. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is expected.
