## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis. Two series of commercial papers have been admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with different maturity dates and quantities.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial paper listed with face value Rs. 5 lakh each
- Series 1: 1000 units maturing on 13/02/2026 (ISIN: INE248U14RZ9)
- Series 2: 3000 units maturing on 04/11/2025 (ISIN: INE248U14SA0)
- Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading in dematerialised form only with Rs. 5 lakh standard denomination

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: 06/08/2025
- Allotment Date: 05/08/2025
- Redemption Dates: 04/11/2025 and 13/02/2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term debt financing options for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunities for debt market participants.
