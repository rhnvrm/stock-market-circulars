## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from three companies - BAMPSL Securities Limited, DCM Shriram Limited, and Motisons Jewellers Ltd - in response to BSE queries regarding significant price and volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015, Regulation 30 disclosure requirements and stated they are not aware of any unpublished price-sensitive information that may have caused the recent movements.

## Key Points

- **BAMPSL Securities Limited** (Scrip Code: L65100DL1995PLC065028) responded on November 22, 2025 to BSE's November 21, 2025 query regarding price movement
- Confirmed all material and price-sensitive information has been promptly disclosed per Regulation 30 requirements
- Stated company is not aware of any event, development, or unpublished price-sensitive information causing the price movement
- **DCM Shriram Limited** (Scrip Code: 523367) responded on November 24, 2025 to volume movement query
- Confirmed all requisite information has been disclosed to exchanges pursuant to Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015
- **Motisons Jewellers Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544053) responded on November 24, 2025 regarding volume increase
- Stated company is not aware of reason for security movement
- Indicated movement is purely market driven and may be combination of various factors including market conditions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular relates to compliance with existing Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must provide clarifications when stock exchanges seek information regarding abnormal price or volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any unpublished price-sensitive information exists
- Companies must reaffirm their commitment to timely disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015
- All material events and information that are price-sensitive must be promptly disclosed to stock exchanges

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: BSE sent query to BAMPSL Securities regarding price movement
- **November 22, 2025**: BAMPSL Securities submitted clarification
- **November 24, 2025**: BSE sent volume movement query to DCM Shriram Limited (around 12:45 PM)
- **November 24, 2025**: DCM Shriram Limited and Motisons Jewellers Ltd submitted clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine compliance clarifications with no new material information disclosed. All three companies confirmed no unpublished price-sensitive information exists.

**Investor Impact**: Low - The clarifications provide transparency that recent price/volume movements are market-driven rather than based on undisclosed company-specific developments. Investors can continue trading with confidence that all material information has been disclosed.

**Regulatory Impact**: Low - Standard surveillance mechanism by BSE working as intended, with companies responding appropriately to exchange queries about unusual trading activity.

**Operational Impact**: None - No changes to business operations or compliance requirements beyond existing obligations under SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015.
