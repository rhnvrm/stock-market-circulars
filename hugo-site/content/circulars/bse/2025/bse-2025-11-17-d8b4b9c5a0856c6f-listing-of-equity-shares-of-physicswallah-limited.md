## Summary

BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) has announced the listing of equity shares of Physicswallah Limited on its exchange. Physicswallah is a prominent education technology company in India. This circular notifies market participants about the commencement of trading of the company's equity shares on the BSE platform.

## Key Points

- Physicswallah Limited's equity shares are now listed on BSE
- The company operates in the education technology sector
- Trading of the shares will commence as per the schedule specified in the circular
- Market participants can now trade Physicswallah shares on the BSE platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems to include the new scrip
- Brokers should ensure their trading terminals reflect the newly listed security
- Trading members should familiarize themselves with the security details

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: As specified in the detailed circular document

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing of Physicswallah Limited adds a significant education technology company to the BSE platform, potentially attracting investor interest in the education sector.

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing that follows standard procedures. Market participants need to update their systems to accommodate the new scrip.

**Investor Interest**: The education technology sector has seen substantial growth in India, and Physicswallah is a well-known brand in the online education space, which may generate considerable investor interest.
