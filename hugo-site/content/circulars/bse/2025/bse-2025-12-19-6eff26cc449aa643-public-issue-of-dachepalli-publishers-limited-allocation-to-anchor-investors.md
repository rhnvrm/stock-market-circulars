## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Dachepalli Publishers Limited. This allocation represents institutional investor participation ahead of the main public offering.

## Key Points

- Company: Dachepalli Publishers Limited
- Event: Anchor investor allocation for public issue
- Circular issued by BSE on December 19, 2025
- Anchor allocation is typically completed one day before the IPO opens to retail investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Disclosure of anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI guidelines
- Anchor investors subject to lock-in period as per regulations
- Pricing and allocation details to be made available to public

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 19, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation date would typically be one day before IPO opening

## Impact Assessment

Anchor investor allocation provides early price discovery and demonstrates institutional confidence in the issue. The quality and quantity of anchor participation often influences retail investor sentiment during the main IPO period. This is a standard step in the IPO process for companies seeking listing on BSE.
