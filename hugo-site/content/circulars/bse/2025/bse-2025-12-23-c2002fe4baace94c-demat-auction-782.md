## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 782 dated December 23, 2025. The auction includes 130 different securities across various scrip codes with quantities ranging from 1 share to 50,000 shares. This auction is conducted to close out outstanding delivery obligations where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 782
- Auction Date: December 23, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 130 scrips
- Largest auction quantity: STANDARD CAP (50,000 shares)
- Other significant quantities include: JAYASWAL NEC (5,000 shares), RIR POWER EL (4,347 shares), YUVRAAJ HYGI (3,370 shares)
- Includes major stocks like ITC, HDFC Bank, Bharti Airtel, Siemens, NTPC, Biocon, Tata Motors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the auction or arrange delivery
- Auction participants must ensure sufficient funds for bidding
- Settlement of auctioned securities must be completed as per BSE auction guidelines
- Defaulting members may face financial consequences for non-delivery

## Important Dates

- Settlement Number: 782
- Auction Notice Date: December 23, 2025
- Auction execution date to be determined per BSE auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 130 securities with varying liquidity profiles. Large auction quantities in certain scrips (STANDARD CAP, JAYASWAL NEC, RIR POWER EL) may cause temporary price volatility.

**Operational Impact:** Directly impacts brokers and trading members with unfulfilled delivery obligations in settlement 782. Auction costs will be borne by defaulting parties.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors unless they are clients of defaulting brokers. Institutional investors and market makers may participate in auction bidding.
