## Summary

BSE has announced that ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND schemes will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 30, 2025. This follows an earlier suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251205-22. All six variants of the scheme (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) will support full transactional facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND reopening for transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Replaces earlier suspension mentioned in Notice No. 20251205-22

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update restoring normal trading functionality for the affected mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 30, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of ongoing subscription and redemption transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Impact Level: Low**

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains only to investors and distributors dealing with ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND schemes. The resumption of transactions restores normal operations for this specific mutual fund, allowing investors to subscribe, redeem, and utilize systematic facilities. Market participants using the BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability for client servicing purposes.
