## Summary

BSE announces the detailed schedule for Blue Jet Healthcare Limited's Offer for Sale (OFS) to be conducted on September 10-11, 2025. The OFS includes 59.25 lakh shares as base offer size with an equal oversubscription option, totaling up to 1.18 crore shares.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Blue Jet Healthcare Limited (BLUEJET, Code: 544009)
- **Total Offer Size**: Up to 1,18,51,850 shares (including oversubscription option)
- **Floor Price**: Rs. 675.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Retail Reservation**: 10% of issue size reserved for retail investors
- **Allocation Method**: Price Priority Method

## Trading Schedule

- **Institutional/Non-Retail Bidding**: September 10, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Retail Bidding**: September 11, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give up/Take up Period**: Until 7:30 AM on T+1 and T+2 days

## Bidding Categories

- **Retail Investors (RI/RIC)**: Up to Rs. 2 lakh bid value - 100% upfront margin
- **Non-Institutional Investors (NII)**: Above Rs. 2 lakh - 100% upfront margin
- **Institutional Investors (MF/IC/Others)**: 100% or 0% margin options available

## Technical Requirements

- **Platform Access**: iBBS platform via internet or leased line
- **RTRMS Access**: Available for trading members
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Tick Size**: Rs. 0.05

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Institutional and non-retail bidding day
- **September 11, 2025**: Retail bidding day and carry-forward bids
- **Settlement**: T+1 and T+2 basis depending on investor category

## Impact Assessment

This OFS provides exit opportunity for existing shareholders while allowing new investors to participate. The two-day schedule accommodates different investor categories with appropriate margin requirements and settlement timelines.
