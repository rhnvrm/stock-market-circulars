## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for settlement number 755 dated November 14, 2025. The auction covers securities of 108 companies where delivery obligations were not met. The list specifies scrip codes, company names, and quantities of shares to be auctioned to complete the settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 755
- Auction date: November 14, 2025
- Total companies affected: 108
- Covers securities where delivery obligations failed
- Quantities range from 1 share to 10,000 shares depending on the scrip
- Major quantities include: Hindware Home (10,000 shares), SEPC Limited (1,100 shares), Tata Mutual Fund (1,300 shares), Kanani Industries (2,527 shares)
- Notable large-cap stocks included: Reliance Industries (234 shares), HDFC Bank (2 shares), Indian Oil Corporation (200 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational auction process under existing BSE settlement regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Selling brokers must ensure delivery of securities listed in the auction
- Buying brokers should note the auction process for completing their purchase transactions
- Members involved must comply with BSE's auction settlement procedures
- Payment and delivery obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Number: 755
- Auction Notice Date: November 14, 2025
- Auction execution and settlement to follow as per BSE's standard auction calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects specific settlement obligations for 108 securities

**Operational Impact:** 
- Brokers with failed delivery obligations must participate in auction or arrange securities
- Buying brokers will receive shares through auction mechanism
- Price discovery through auction may differ from market prices
- Settlement integrity maintained through auction process

**Financial Impact:**
- Selling brokers may face losses if auction prices exceed market prices
- Buying brokers protected through auction mechanism
- Quantities are relatively small for most scrips, limiting systemic impact

**Investor Impact:** Limited to specific transactions in settlement 755 where delivery failed
