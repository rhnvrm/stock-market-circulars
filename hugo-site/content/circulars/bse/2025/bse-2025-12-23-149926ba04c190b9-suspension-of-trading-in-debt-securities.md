## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of EARLYSALARY SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 975277, ISIN: INE01YL07235) effective December 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the debentures maturing on December 26, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: EARLYSALARY SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 975277
- ISIN: INE01YL07235
- Debenture: ESPLD-11.75%-26-12-25-PVT (11.75% coupon rate)
- Maturity Date: December 26, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: December 24, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-784/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 24, 2025 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new trades should be executed in these securities

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 26, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects a single privately placed debenture issue from a private limited company. The suspension is procedural and routine for maturing debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard pre-maturity trading suspension affecting only holders and potential traders of this specific debt instrument. No broader market implications expected.
