## Summary

BSE has issued a corrigendum to the earlier forfeiture notice for Integrated Proteins Ltd. This circular provides the corrected Annexure I containing revised details of share certificates with distinctive numbers that are subject to forfeiture. The annexure lists 198 entries with distinctive number ranges and corresponding share quantities.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued to correct earlier forfeiture notice for Integrated Proteins Ltd
- Contains revised Annexure I with detailed listing of share certificates
- Lists 198 serial numbered entries with distinctive number ranges
- Share quantities range from 400 to 9,100 shares per entry
- Distinctive numbers range from 1318001 to approximately 1994100
- Total affected shares span across multiple certificate ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a correction to previously issued forfeiture notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders holding shares with the listed distinctive numbers should take note of the corrected information
- The company and affected shareholders must refer to this corrected annexure for accurate forfeiture proceedings
- Previous annexure in the original notice should be disregarded

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 13, 2025
- This supersedes the annexure in the previously issued forfeiture notice

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has moderate impact limited to specific shareholders of Integrated Proteins Ltd whose shares fall within the listed distinctive number ranges. The correction ensures accuracy in the forfeiture proceedings and protects shareholder rights by providing correct information. Shareholders with certificates in the specified ranges should verify their holdings against the corrected annexure to understand if they are affected by the forfeiture proceedings.
