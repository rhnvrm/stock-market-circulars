## Summary

BSE has admitted Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities) as a Trading Member with trading rights exclusively on BSE Ltd in the Debt segment. The admission was effective December 29, 2025, and is subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New Trading Member: Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal operating as B M Securities
- Member Number: 6974
- Trading Segment: Debt
- Membership Type: New Membership
- Trading rights limited to BSE Ltd only
- Admission subject to Exchange Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice regarding the expansion of BSE's trading member base.

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Effective date of admission as Trading Member
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The admission of a new trading member in the debt segment expands BSE's participant base but does not affect existing trading operations, market structure, or regulatory requirements for current members. Trading members requiring clarification can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
