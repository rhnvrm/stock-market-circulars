## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to dealings new Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The issue comprises 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings, with redemption scheduled for September 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730547, Scrip ID: MFL121125
- ISIN: INE522D14OV9
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,69,407.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OV9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 13, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 13, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Date of Redemption: September 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term debt market participants with an additional investment instrument rated A1+ by both CARE and CRISIL. The listing expands debt market offerings but does not materially affect broader market operations. Impact is limited to debt segment traders and investors interested in highly-rated short-term corporate debt instruments.
