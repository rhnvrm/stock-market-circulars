## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in the Equity segment on December 13, 2025. This notice follows Notice No 20251213-1 dated December 13, 2025, which presumably announced a trading halt. Trading members are informed that normal equity trading operations have resumed.

## Key Points

- Trading has resumed in BSE Equity segment
- This is a continuation of Notice No 20251213-1 dated December 13, 2025
- Notice issued by Head of Trading Operations and VP Trading Operations
- Effective immediately upon issuance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational status update.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Resume normal equity trading operations
- Ensure systems are operational for equity segment trading
- Monitor for any follow-up communications regarding the earlier trading halt

## Important Dates

- **December 13, 2025**: Trading resumed in Equity segment
- Reference to earlier Notice No 20251213-1 dated December 13, 2025 (trading halt notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This notice affects all equity trading activity on BSE. The resumption allows normal market operations to continue after an interruption.

**Operational Impact**: High - Trading members can resume equity trading activities. Any pending orders or transactions can now be processed through the equity segment.

**Investor Impact**: High - Market participants can resume buying and selling equity securities on BSE after the temporary halt referenced in the earlier notice.
