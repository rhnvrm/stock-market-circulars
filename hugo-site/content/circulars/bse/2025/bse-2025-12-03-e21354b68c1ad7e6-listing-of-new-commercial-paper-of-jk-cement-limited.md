## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by J.K. Cement Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 3, 2025. The issue comprises 1,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a 90-day tenor maturing on March 2, 2026. The securities carry a CARE A1+ credit rating and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730690, Scrip ID: JKCL021225
- ISIN: INE823G14BB3
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: 1,000 securities (Rs. 50 crore)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,710.50 per security
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading permitted in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE823G14BB3
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Tenor:** 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement listing in the debt segment. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to J.K. Cement Limited and offers investment opportunities to debt market participants. The CARE A1+ rating indicates highest degree of safety for timely payment of financial obligations. Impact is confined to debt segment traders and institutional investors interested in high-quality short-term debt instruments.
