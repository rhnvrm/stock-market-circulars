## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 350 crores issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (7000 units totaling Rs. 350 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730051, Scrip ID: MFL20825
- ISIN: INE522D14OQ9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,576/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Tenor**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for Manappuram Finance Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
