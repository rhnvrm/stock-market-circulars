## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Magnum Ventures Limited worth Rs. 5 crores (5000 securities of Rs. 100,000 face value each) issued on private placement basis. The securities carry an 18% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and maturity date of August 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 debt securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 18% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: Acuite BB Stable
- ISIN: INE387I07013
- Scrip Code: 975493
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Maturity Date: August 31, 2027
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **August 14, 2025**: Listing effective date
- **Monthly**: Interest payment dates
- **August 31, 2027**: Redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities will provide additional investment options in the debt segment with relatively high interest rate of 18% annually, though rated at BB level indicating higher risk.
