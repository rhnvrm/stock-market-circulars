## Summary

BSE has announced that WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544610) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from December 01, 2025. Following this change, dealings in the equity shares of the company will be shifted under M Group. This notice is a continuation of Exchange's Notice No. 20251117-60 dated November 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544610
- Segment: SME
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: December 01, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251118-4
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED will transition from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group). This represents a change in the trading mechanism applicable to the scrip, moving from a more restrictive delivery-based segment to normal rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the segment transfer effective December 01, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- For further details or clarifications, trading members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 01, 2025 (Monday)
- **Previous Related Notice**: Notice No. 20251117-60 dated November 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment will provide enhanced liquidity and trading flexibility for the scrip. Trade for Trade segment typically has more restrictive trading conditions with compulsory delivery, while Rolling segment allows normal intraday trading and carry-forward positions.

**Market Participants**: Trading members and investors in WORKMATES CORE2CLOUD SOLUTION LIMITED will benefit from improved trading conditions. The change suggests the company has met the criteria for graduation from the more restrictive segment.

**Operational Impact**: Medium impact for trading members who need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the group change before the effective date.
