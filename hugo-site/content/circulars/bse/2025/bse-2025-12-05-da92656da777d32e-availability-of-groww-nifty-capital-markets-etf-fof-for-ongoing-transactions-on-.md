## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS ETF FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 08, 2025. The scheme includes six plan variants covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- GROWW NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 08, 2025
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes fall under Equity category
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251113-44
