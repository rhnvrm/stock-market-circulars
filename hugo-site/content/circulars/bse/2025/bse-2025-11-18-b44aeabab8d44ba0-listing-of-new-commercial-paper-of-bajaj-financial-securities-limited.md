## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis effective November 18, 2025. The instrument consists of 6,000 CPs with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, maturing on February 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,000 Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per CP
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per CP
- Scrip Code: 730588, Scrip ID: BFSL171125
- ISIN: INE01C314DJ0
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Allotment date: November 17, 2025
- Redemption date: February 16, 2026
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DJ0
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Securities available for trading on BSE Debt segment from November 18, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Allotment date
- **November 18, 2025**: Listing and commencement of trading
- **February 16, 2026**: Redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to debt instruments traded in the BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Papers provide short-term funding (approximately 3 months maturity) for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants seeking rated short-term instruments
- Institutional investors in Commercial Paper market
- No impact on equity markets or retail investors

The strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, IND A1+) indicate high credit quality and timely payment capability.
