## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD (ISIN: INE07HK07734, Code: 975736) effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KSL-12.5%-12-12-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07734)
- Security code: 975736
- No dealings permitted from November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-765/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for trading suspension aligned with existing corporate action procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE07HK07734) from November 27, 2025
- Members must inform their clients holding these securities about the trading suspension
- All pending orders for these debentures should be cancelled effective from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 27, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of specific debenture series of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine corporate action process for debt securities approaching redemption

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption as per the terms of the issuance. No trading opportunity available from the record date.
