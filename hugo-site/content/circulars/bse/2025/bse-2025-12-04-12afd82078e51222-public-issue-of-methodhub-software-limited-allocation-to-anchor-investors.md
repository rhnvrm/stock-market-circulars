## Summary

Methodhub Software Limited announced the allocation of 15,03,600 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each to anchor investors at Rs. 194 per share (including share premium of Rs. 184) on December 04, 2025. The total amount raised from the anchor investor portion is Rs. 29,16,98,400 (approximately Rs. 29.17 crore). The allocation was finalized by the Board of Directors in consultation with the Book Running Lead Manager. No mutual funds, life insurance companies, or pension funds participated in the anchor portion.

## Key Points

- **Total Equity Shares Allocated**: 15,03,600 shares
- **Anchor Investor Allocation Price**: Rs. 194 per equity share (Face value: Rs. 10, Share premium: Rs. 184)
- **Total Amount Raised**: Rs. 29,16,98,400 (Rs. 29.17 crore)
- **Number of Anchor Investors**: 17 investors
- **Largest Allocations**: SANSHI FUND – I and MINT FOCUSED GROWTH FUND PCC- CELL 1 each received 2,58,000 shares (17.16% each)
- **Board Meeting Date**: December 04, 2025
- **No institutional participation**: No mutual funds, life insurance companies, or pension funds applied in the anchor portion

## Anchor Investor Details

### Major Allocations (>10%)

1. **SANSHI FUND – I**: 2,58,000 shares (17.16%) - Rs. 5,00,52,000
2. **MINT FOCUSED GROWTH FUND PCC- CELL 1**: 2,58,000 shares (17.16%) - Rs. 5,00,52,000
3. **SILVER STRIDE INDIA GLOBAL FUND**: 1,80,000 shares (11.97%) - Rs. 3,49,20,000

### Other Notable Investors

- ABUNDANTIA CAPITAL VCC: 82,800 shares (5.51%)
- NAV CAPITAL VCC: 74,400 shares (4.95%)
- LILKIS CAPITAL AIF TRUST: 74,400 shares (4.95%)
- 11 additional investors each received 51,600-60,000 shares (3.43%-3.99%)

## Regulatory Compliance

- Allocation made in accordance with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2018, as amended
- If the final offer price discovered through book building is higher than the anchor investor allocation price of Rs. 194, anchor investors will be required to pay the differential amount by the pay-in date specified in the revised Confirmation of Allocation Note (CAN)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The successful anchor investor allocation indicates positive investor sentiment towards the IPO. The participation of 17 diverse anchor investors, though without traditional institutional investors like mutual funds or insurance companies, suggests confidence in the company's prospects.

**Investment Interest**: Total anchor book of Rs. 29.17 crore demonstrates substantial investor interest in the technology services sector. The anchor allocation typically sets a benchmark for the main IPO pricing and demand.

**Price Discovery**: The anchor allocation price of Rs. 194 per share will serve as a reference point for the price band discovery in the main book-building process. Any upward revision in final offer price will require additional payment from anchor investors.
