## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 9 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 9 companies are listing additional equity shares under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 1,302,526 new shares being listed across all companies
- All shares are being listed without any lock-in period restrictions
- Effective trading date: August 26, 2025
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Issuances:**
- Paradeep Phosphates Limited: 989,657 shares (₹10 face value)
- Black Box Limited: 103,950 shares (₹2 face value)
- Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd: 76,684 shares (₹1 face value)
- CreditAccess Grameen Ltd: 45,725 shares (₹10 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- Prataap Snacks Limited: 27,504 shares
- Jana Small Finance Bank Limited: 25,031 shares
- IdeaForge Technology Limited: 11,626 shares
- Lupin Ltd: 7,189 shares
- Dr. Reddy's Laboratories Ltd: 4,160 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listings
- All securities are admitted for regular trading without restrictions
- No special compliance requirements mentioned

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date:** August 26, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date:** August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The addition of ESOP/ESOS shares typically has neutral to slightly dilutive effects on existing shareholders but represents positive employee compensation practices by the companies involved. No lock-in restrictions means these shares are immediately tradeable, providing liquidity to employee option holders.
