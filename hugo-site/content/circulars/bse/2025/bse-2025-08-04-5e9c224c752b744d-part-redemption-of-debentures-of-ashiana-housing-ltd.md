## Summary

BSE has informed trading members about the partial redemption of debentures issued by ASHIANA HOUSING LTD. The company has reduced the face value per debenture following partial redemption, with the new reduced face value being Rs. 764,720.45 per debenture.

## Key Points

- ASHIANA HOUSING LTD (Scrip Code: AHL-8%-31-5-41-PVT, ISIN: INE365D08026, Code: 973220) is conducting partial redemption of debentures
- New reduced face value: Rs. 764,720.45 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value effective from August 5, 2025
- Reference: DR-688/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in face value
- Debentures will trade with the reduced face value from the effective date
- Trading members are required to take note of this corporate action

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 4, 2025
- Effective Date: August 5, 2025 (trading with reduced face value begins)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to ASHIANA HOUSING LTD debenture holders. This is a routine corporate action for debt securities involving partial redemption and face value adjustment. No broader market implications expected.
