## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) of RCRS Innovation Limited, filed on December 10, 2025, for public comments. The draft offer document will be hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations, with stakeholders invited to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- RCRS Innovation Limited filed its Draft Offer Document on December 10, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review and comments
- Stakeholders can submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- DRHP accessible at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per SEBI ICDR Regulations for SME IPO filings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comment period: 21 days from December 11, 2025
- Comments must be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft offer document must remain publicly accessible during the comment period

## Important Dates

- **DRHP Filing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days (December 11, 2025 to January 1, 2026)
- **Comment Submission Deadline**: January 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine procedural filing for an SME segment IPO.

**Affected Parties**: 
- Potential investors interested in RCRS Innovation Limited IPO
- Market intermediaries involved in the IPO process
- Regulatory authorities reviewing the offer document

**Operational Impact**: No immediate operational changes for existing market participants. Standard IPO process for SME segment companies seeking listing on BSE SME platform.
