## Summary

BSE has listed 2000 Commercial Papers of Manappuram Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective November 27, 2025. Each CP has a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 with total issue size of Rs. 100 crore. The instruments are issued at a discount (Rs. 464,425 issue price) and will mature on November 26, 2026. Trading will occur only in dematerialized form through the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2000 CPs of Rs. 5 lakh each (Total: Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730662, Scrip ID: MFL261125
- ISIN: INE522D14OY3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A+
- Issue Price: Rs. 464,425 per CP (discount to face value)
- Face Value/Paid-up Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per CP
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank
- Trading: Dematerialized form only, standard denomination Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OY3
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all trades
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: November 26, 2026 (365-day tenor)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of commercial paper for short-term funding needs.

**Operational Impact**: Standard debt segment listing with no special trading requirements beyond normal CP trading protocols. Trading members can access the instrument through regular BSE Debt segment operations.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term debt investment option in Manappuram Finance Limited with dual high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A+), indicating strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
