## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544603) to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, November 14, 2025. The stock was listed on BSE on Wednesday, November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED listed on BSE on November 12, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index on November 14, 2025 at market open
- Exchange ticker: 544603
- This action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Announcement references Notice No: 20251111-37

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing activity following a new stock listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their systems to reflect the index composition change effective November 14, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED listed on BSE
- **November 14, 2025**: Stock added to BSE IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard index addition following a new IPO listing.

**Operational Impact**: Index fund managers and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to rebalance their portfolios to include the new constituent. The inclusion will have proportional weight impact on existing index constituents.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for investors tracking or investing in BSE IPO Index-linked products. No direct impact on the broader market or other stocks.
