## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME segment for January 2026. The schedule covers 21 trading days from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026, with settlements numbered ITP-189/25-26 through ITP-209/25-26.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule spans from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- Total of 21 settlement cycles scheduled for the month
- Settlement numbers range from ITP-189/25-26 to ITP-209/25-26
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2526189 to 2526209
- T+1 settlement cycle maintained throughout the period
- Pay-in time: 10:00 a.m. (members must submit instructions by 9:50 a.m.)
- Pay-out time: 11:00 a.m. for both funds and securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:50 a.m.
- Pay-in must be completed by 10:00 a.m.
- Members must adhere to the published settlement schedule
