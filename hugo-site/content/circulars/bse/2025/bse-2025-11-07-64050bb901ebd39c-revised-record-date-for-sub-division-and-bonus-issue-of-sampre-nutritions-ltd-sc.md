## Summary

Sampre Nutritions Limited (CIN: L15499TG1991PLC013515, BSE Scrip Code: 530617) has revised the record date for its equity share sub-division and bonus issue from November 11, 2025 to November 14, 2025. The company will allot 43,693,710 bonus equity shares with a deemed allotment date of November 17, 2025, and shares will be available for trading from November 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Record date revised from November 11, 2025 to November 14, 2025
- Purpose: Split/sub-division and bonus issue of equity shares
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 43,693,710 equity shares
- Distinguished Number Range (DNR): 43693711 to 87387420
- Deemed date of allotment: November 17, 2025 (T+1 from record date)
- Credit of bonus shares to be completed by 12:00 PM on November 17, 2025
- Bonus shares available for trading from beginning of day on November 18, 2025 (T+2)
- Intimation made pursuant to Regulation 42 of SEBI Listing Regulations, 2015
- Compliance with SEBI Circular SEBI/CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated September 16, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. The intimation is made in compliance with existing regulations:
- Regulation 42 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- SEBI Circular SEBI/CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated September 16, 2024 regarding bonus share allotment timeline

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must ensure credit of bonus shares is completed in depository system by 12:00 PM on November 17, 2025 (T+1 day)
- Bonus shares must be available for trading at beginning of day on November 18, 2025 (T+2 day)
- Shareholders holding shares as of record date (November 14, 2025) will be eligible for the bonus issue and sub-division

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Date of circular intimation to BSE
- **November 14, 2025**: Revised record date for sub-division and bonus issue
- **November 17, 2025**: Deemed date of allotment (T+1)
- **November 17, 2025 by 12:00 PM**: Deadline for credit of bonus shares in depository system
- **November 18, 2025**: Bonus shares available for trading from beginning of day (T+2)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders on the record date will receive bonus shares, increasing their shareholding proportionately. The sub-division will reduce the face value per share while increasing the number of shares held.

**Trading Impact**: The revision of the record date provides additional time for investors to position themselves. Trading in bonus shares will commence from November 18, 2025, following the T+2 settlement cycle.

**Market Impact**: The stock split and bonus issue typically improve liquidity and make shares more affordable for retail investors. The revised timeline ensures proper settlement and credit processes are followed as per SEBI guidelines.

**Operational Impact**: The company and depository participants must ensure timely credit of 43,693,710 bonus shares to eligible shareholders within the prescribed timeline of T+1 day.
