## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of FINSTARS CAPITAL LIMITED on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 1500 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 11.07% per annum with monthly interest payments until maturity on December 9, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: FINSTARS CAPITAL LIMITED
- **Quantity**: 1,500 securities
- **Scrip Code**: 977361
- **Scrip ID**: 1107FCL27
- **ISIN**: INE08XA07052
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000
- **Credit Rating**: IVR BBB-/Negative
- **Interest Rate**: 11.07% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from December 31, 2025 to December 9, 2027
- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 9, 2027
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE08XA07052
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Any clarifications required should be directed to the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 9, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides debt market participants with a new investment instrument rated IVR BBB-/Negative. The securities offer monthly interest payments at 11.07% p.a. over a 2-year tenure. Impact is limited to institutional investors and participants in the debt segment interested in FINSTARS CAPITAL LIMITED's credit profile.
