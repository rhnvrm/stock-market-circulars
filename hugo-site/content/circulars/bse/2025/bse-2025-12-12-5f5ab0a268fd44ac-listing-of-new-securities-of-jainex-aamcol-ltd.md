## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 7,48,169 new equity shares of Jainex Aamcol Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505212) issued through a rights basis offering. The shares are listed and permitted for trading with effect from Monday, December 15, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 7,48,169 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 110/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 120 per share
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE280F01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 1510001 to 2258169
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a company-specific rights issue listing. The 7.48 lakh shares represent additional equity capital raised by Jainex Aamcol Ltd. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive number range. The rights issue at a significant premium (1100% over face value) indicates existing shareholder participation in the capital raise.
