## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 93,750 new equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511644) issued on a preferential basis to promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading from November 27, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until June 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 93,750 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each issued at a premium of Rs. 118
- Issue price: Rs. 128 per share
- Allotment date: August 22, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 27, 2025
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive numbers: 2497500 to 2591249
- ISIN: INE113B01029
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in compliance: All 93,750 shares are locked-in until June 29, 2027
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded by allottees during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The issue size is relatively small (93,750 shares) and will have minimal impact on overall liquidity or market capitalization. The shares are locked-in for approximately 19 months from the listing date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters following warrant conversion. The lock-in period ensures no immediate selling pressure on the stock.
