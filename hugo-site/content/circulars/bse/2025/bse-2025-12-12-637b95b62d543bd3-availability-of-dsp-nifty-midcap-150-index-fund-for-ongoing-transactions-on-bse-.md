## Summary

BSE has announced that DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 15, 2025. This follows earlier Notice No. 20251121-47 which had presumably suspended these transactions. The scheme offers both Regular and Direct plan options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment variants.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions from December 15, 2025
- All transaction types available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under Equity category
- Transactions will be processed through BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference made to earlier Notice No. 20251121-47

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transaction facilities for a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and participants of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND schemes
- Ensure all six ISIN codes are activated for trading from December 15, 2025
- Process subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP requests for the scheme as per normal procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 15, 2025 - Resumption of all transaction facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational matter affecting only investors and distributors of this specific DSP mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for existing and prospective investors of DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND who will regain ability to transact in the scheme through BSE platform.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members need to ensure system readiness to process transactions in the six scheme variants starting December 15, 2025.
