## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE debt segment effective August 18, 2025. The paper has a 91-day tenure with redemption on November 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 4000 units (Rs. 200 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730032, ISIN: INE466L14ET7
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 18, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk.
