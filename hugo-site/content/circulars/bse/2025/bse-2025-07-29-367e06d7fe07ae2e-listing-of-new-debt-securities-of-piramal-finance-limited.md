## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Piramal Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective July 29, 2025. The securities carry interest rates of 9% and 9.10% per annum with credit ratings of CARE AA/Stable and ICRA AA/Stable.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue 1: 70,000 units at 9% interest rate, maturing June 28, 2027
- Issue 2: 11,000 units at 9.10% interest rate, maturing June 28, 2030
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

**First Issue (ISIN: INE202B07JT0)**
- Scrip Code: 976955
- Quantity: 70,000 units
- Interest Rate: 9% p.a.
- Maturity: June 28, 2027
- Annual interest payment dates: July 28, 2026 to June 28, 2027

**Second Issue (ISIN: INE202B07JU8)**
- Scrip Code: 976956
- Quantity: 11,000 units
- Interest Rate: 9.10% p.a.
- Maturity: June 28, 2030
- Annual interest payment dates: July 28, 2026 to June 28, 2030

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- First Interest Payment: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of debt securities with minimal market impact. The securities are privately placed and will provide additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional investors.
