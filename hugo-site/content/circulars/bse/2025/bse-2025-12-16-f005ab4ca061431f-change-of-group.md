## Summary

BSE has issued a correction notice regarding the transfer of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) to the 'X' group. The corrected transfer date is December 17, 2025, superseding the date mentioned in the original notice dated December 02, 2025 (Notice No. 20251202-12). All other contents of the original notice remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572) will be transferred to 'X' group
- Corrected transfer date: December 17, 2025
- This notice corrects the date mentioned in Notice No. 20251202-12 dated December 02, 2025
- All other contents of the original notice remain valid
- Trading members are specifically requested to note this date correction

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corrective notice updating the effective date of a previously announced group transfer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the corrected transfer date of December 17, 2025
- Members should update their systems and records to reflect the accurate transfer date
- All trading and settlement activities for Ranjit Securities Ltd. should be conducted in accordance with 'X' group requirements from the corrected date

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Corrected date for transfer of Ranjit Securities Ltd. to 'X' group
- **December 02, 2025**: Date of original notice (Notice No. 20251202-12) that contained the incorrect date
- **December 16, 2025**: Date of this correction notice

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Ranjit Securities Ltd. and primarily affects trading members who need to be aware of the correct transfer date. Transfer to 'X' group typically indicates securities that are under special surveillance or have trading restrictions. Trading members and investors in Ranjit Securities should be aware of the specific trading conditions and restrictions applicable to 'X' group securities from December 17, 2025 onwards. The one-day correction may require adjustments to trading strategies and compliance systems.
