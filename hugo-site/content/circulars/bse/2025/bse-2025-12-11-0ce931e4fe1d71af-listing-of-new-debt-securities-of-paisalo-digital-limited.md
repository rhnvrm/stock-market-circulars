## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 11, 2025. The securities consist of 4,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.5% per annum with quarterly interest payments. The debentures are rated IVR AA/Stable and will mature on December 9, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4,000 units
- **Scrip Code**: 977358
- **Scrip ID**: 85PDL28
- **ISIN**: INE420C07163
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: IVR AA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 8.5% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly (from March 9, 2026 to December 9, 2028)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C07163
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: March 9, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 9, 2028
- **Interest Payment Period**: Quarterly from March 9, 2026 to December 9, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market-wide impact. The private placement of debentures by Paisalo Digital Limited adds to the company's debt instruments available for trading on BSE's Debt segment. The IVR AA/Stable rating indicates good credit quality. The listing provides institutional and eligible investors access to these debt securities for secondary market trading. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in Paisalo Digital Limited's debt instruments.
