## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 7,500 units at Rs. 1,00,000 face value each, amounting to Rs. 75 crore. The securities carry a 7.35% annual interest rate with a maturity date of November 21, 2030, and are rated AAA by both CARE and CRISIL.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977311
- **Scrip ID**: 735AFL30
- **ISIN**: INE891K07AJ1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 7,500 units
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Interest Rate**: 7.35% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE891K07AJ1
- Trading members must use the tick size of 1 paise for these securities
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from November 21, 2026 to November 21, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 21, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with no impact on broader market operations or retail investors.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to note the new scrip details for trading purposes. The securities will be available for trading on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialised form.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors who participated in the private placement. The AAA credit rating indicates high credit quality and low default risk.
