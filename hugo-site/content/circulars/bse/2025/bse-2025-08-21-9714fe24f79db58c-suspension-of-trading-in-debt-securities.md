## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of Infrastructure Leasing and Financial Services Limited (ILFS) effective August 22, 2025, due to their maturity on August 25, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from August 22, 2025
- Debt securities mature on August 25, 2025
- SCRIP CODE: 952662
- ISIN: INE871D07OJ9
- Security details: ILFS-8.69%-25-8-25-PVT

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified ILFS debt securities from August 22, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **August 25, 2025**: Maturity date of the debt securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to security maturity. Only affects holders and potential traders of the specific ILFS debt security (ISIN: INE871D07OJ9).
