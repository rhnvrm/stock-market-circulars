## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum to correct an error in the confirmatory order dated December 30, 2025 (reference number WTM/KV/CFID/CFID-TPD/31942/2025-26) concerning Pacheli Industrial Finance Limited. The correction changes a date mentioned at paragraph 40, page 17 of the original order from "May 14, 2025" to "January 16, 2025".

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued by SEBI's Whole Time Member Kamlesh C. Varshney
- Corrects date error in confirmatory order reference number WTM/KV/CFID/CFID-TPD/31942/2025-26
- Original order dated December 30, 2025 pertaining to Pacheli Industrial Finance Limited
- Date correction at paragraph 40, page 17: "May 14, 2025" to be read as "January 16, 2025"
- Copy to be served to all Noticees, Stock Exchanges, Registrar and Transfer Agents, and Depositories

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is purely a clerical correction to an existing order.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Noticees must take note of the corrected date
- Stock Exchanges, Registrar and Transfer Agents, and Depositories must ensure compliance with the corrected order
- The date "January 16, 2025" should be considered as the correct date in the original confirmatory order

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Date of original confirmatory order
- **December 31, 2025**: Date of corrigendum issuance
- **January 16, 2025**: Corrected date (replacing May 14, 2025 in the original order)

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has minimal market impact as it is a technical correction of a date in an existing SEBI order. The correction ensures accuracy in the official record but does not alter the substantive content or compliance obligations under the original confirmatory order. Stakeholders including Pacheli Industrial Finance Limited, Stock Exchanges, and relevant intermediaries should note the corrected date for reference purposes.
