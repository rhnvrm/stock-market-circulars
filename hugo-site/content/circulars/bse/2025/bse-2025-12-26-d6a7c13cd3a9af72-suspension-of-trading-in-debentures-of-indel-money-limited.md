## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (ISIN: INE0BUS07BC9, Code: 976097) effective December 30, 2025. The suspension is being implemented as the company has fixed the record date for redemption of these debentures on December 30, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading in INDEL MONEY LIMITED debentures (IML-10.50%-14-1-26-PVT) will be suspended from December 30, 2025
- ISIN: INE0BUS07BC9, BSE Code: 976097
- Suspension is due to debenture redemption corporate action
- Record date for redemption: December 30, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 30, 2025 onwards
- Notice reference: DR-787/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard trading suspension procedure for debt securities undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from December 30, 2025
- Members must ensure their trading systems reflect the suspension
- All pending orders for these debentures should be cancelled or blocked from execution
- Members should inform their clients holding or interested in these debentures about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| December 26, 2025 | Notice issued |
| December 30, 2025 | Record date for redemption |
| December 30, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date) |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of INDEL MONEY LIMITED debentures. The trading suspension affects only this specific debt instrument and does not have broader market implications.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will not be able to trade these securities from December 30, 2025. Investors seeking to exit their positions must do so before the suspension date. Those holding the debentures on the record date will receive redemption proceeds as per the debenture terms.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these debentures and inform affected clients about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
