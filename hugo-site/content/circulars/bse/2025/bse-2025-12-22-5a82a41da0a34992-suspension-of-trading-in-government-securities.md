## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Government Security SGBDEC2513 (Scrip Code: 800281, ISIN: IN0020170158) effective December 23, 2025, as the security will mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this security from the suspension date.

- Government Security SGBDEC2513 to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 800281
- ISIN: IN0020170158
- Suspension effective from December 23, 2025

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine operational notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal with the Government Security SGBDEC2513 from December 23, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure no new positions are created in this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Security Maturity**: Redemption date (specific date not mentioned in circular)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government securities. Only affects holders and potential traders of the specific Sovereign Gold Bond series SGBDEC2513. Trading members need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive to avoid operational issues.
