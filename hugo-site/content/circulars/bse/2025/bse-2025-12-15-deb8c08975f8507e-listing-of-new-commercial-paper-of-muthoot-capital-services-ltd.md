## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Capital Services Ltd on private placement basis with effect from December 15, 2025. The issue comprises 300 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 15 crore) with CRISIL A1+ rating, issued at Rs. 4,90,237 per unit and maturing on March 10, 2026. IndusInd Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Quantity: 300 units (total value Rs. 15 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730770
- Scrip ID: MCSL121225
- ISIN: INE296G14594
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,237 per unit
- Allotment Date: December 12, 2025
- Maturity Date: March 10, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296G14594
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 15, 2025
- Listing Date: December 15, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing on the BSE Debt segment. The instrument provides short-term funding for Muthoot Capital Services Ltd with a tenure of approximately 88 days. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness. Trading is restricted to institutional participants dealing in debt securities with minimum lots of Rs. 5 lakhs.
