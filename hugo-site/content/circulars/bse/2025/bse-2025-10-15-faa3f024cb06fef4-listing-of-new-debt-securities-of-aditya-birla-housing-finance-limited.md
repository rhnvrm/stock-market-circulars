## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective October 15, 2025. The securities comprise 33,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 7.9015% per annum with annual interest payments. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE, indicating highest credit quality. The issue was allotted on October 14, 2025, with a redemption date of August 11, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 33,000 debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 976488, Scrip ID: 790ABHFL28
- ISIN: INE831R07565 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,03,116.6685
- Interest Rate: 7.9015% per annum (annually payable)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Allotment Date: October 14, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R07565
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 14, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Annually from August 11, 2026 to August 11, 2028
- **Redemption Date:** August 11, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine private placement debt listing that adds to the existing debt securities of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited under the same ISIN. The listing provides institutional investors and debt market participants with additional tradable debt instruments.

**Operational Impact:** None for equity market participants. Debt market participants and trading members can now trade these securities on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form.

**Credit Quality:** The AAA rating from both CRISIL and ICRA indicates highest safety regarding timely payment of interest and principal, making these securities suitable for conservative debt investors seeking stable returns.
