## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the part redemption of non-convertible debentures issued by NAVI FINSERV LIMITED. The company has set a record date of December 13, 2025, for the redemption. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 6,666.67 per debenture effective from December 12, 2025 (settlement number DR-776/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Issuer: NAVI FINSERV LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE342T07585, Code: 976584)
- Security Description: NFL-11.16%-27-6-27-PVT
- Record Date: December 13, 2025
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 6,666.67 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in NAVI FINSERV LIMITED debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value from the effective date
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 6,666.67 per debenture
- Debenture holders on record as of December 13, 2025, will be eligible for the part redemption

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-776/2025-2026)
- **December 13, 2025**: Record date for part redemption eligibility

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action has a limited and specific impact:

- **Debenture Holders**: Holders of NAVI FINSERV LIMITED's 11.16% non-convertible debentures will receive partial redemption proceeds. The remaining investment will have a reduced face value.
- **Trading Impact**: Minimal market disruption expected as this is a single debt security with private placement characteristics (PVT designation)
- **Liquidity**: The reduced face value may affect trading dynamics, though debt securities typically have limited secondary market activity
- **Company Impact**: Part redemption reduces NAVI FINSERV LIMITED's debt obligations as per the original debenture terms
