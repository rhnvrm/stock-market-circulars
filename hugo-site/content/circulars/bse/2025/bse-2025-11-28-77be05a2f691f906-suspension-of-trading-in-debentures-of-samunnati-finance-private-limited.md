## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 01, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debentures (SFPL-11.37%-2-12-25-PVT) with ISIN INE551U07316 and scrip code 975979. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 975979
- ISIN: INE551U07316
- Debenture Series: SFPL-11.37%-2-12-25-PVT
- Coupon Rate: 11.37%
- Trading suspension effective from December 01, 2025
- Reference: DR-767/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from December 01, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 01, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Maturity Date**: The debentures are scheduled to mature around this time

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and related to the natural maturity of the debt instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders should prepare for redemption proceedings as the instruments approach maturity. No trading will be possible from December 01, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and ensure compliance with the suspension directive to avoid inadvertent trades in the suspended scrip.
