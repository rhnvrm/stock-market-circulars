## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crores) with CRISIL A1+ rating, issued at Rs. 4,91,484 per unit and maturing on March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730718, Scrip ID: MAF081225, ISIN: INE0JRU14347
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,484 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0JRU14347
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 8, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 8, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing for institutional investors. The instrument has a short tenure of approximately 3 months and represents standard short-term debt issuance by Mirae Asset Financial Services. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term debt instruments.
