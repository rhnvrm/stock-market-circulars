## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd, continuing from the previous notice dated October 1, 2025. Two scrip codes will be available for trading with revised terms and new ISINs effective December 1, 2025. The changes involve ISIN replacements and maturity date extensions for both securities.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd will trade under new ISINs from December 1, 2025
- Scrip Code 975859: Old ISIN INE0I0R07045 replaced by INE0I0R07078 (Quantity: 975)
- Scrip Code 973453: Old ISIN INE0I0R07052 replaced by INE0I0R07086 (Quantity: 2150)
- Both securities have maturity dates extended from October 1, 2025 to December 1, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251001-60 dated October 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an operational update regarding specific debt security terms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised terms for the affected scrip codes
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect new ISINs and maturity dates
- For further details, Trading Members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade (+91 22 2272 8547, debt.listing@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 1, 2025**: Effective date for trading with revised terms and new ISINs
- **December 1, 2025**: New maturity date for both securities (extended from October 1, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This update affects holders and traders of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd debt securities under the two specified scrip codes. The maturity extension by two months provides additional time for settlement. Market participants dealing with these specific instruments need to update their records with new ISINs. Limited broader market impact as this is company-specific and affects only debt segment trading members dealing with these particular securities.
