## Summary

BSE has admitted Eklingji Broking LLP as a new trading member with Member Number 6970. The firm has been granted trading rights in Cash and Equity Derivatives segments, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New trading member admitted: Eklingji Broking LLP
- Member Number: 6970
- Segments: Cash/Equity Derivatives
- Type: New Membership
- Trading rights are exclusively on BSE Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing members. The new member must comply with BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251127-21

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members about the addition of a new participant in the Cash and Equity Derivatives segments. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
