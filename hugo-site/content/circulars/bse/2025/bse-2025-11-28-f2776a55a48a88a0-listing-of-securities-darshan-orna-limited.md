## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading commencement of 50,029,335 new equity shares of Darshan Orna Limited (Scrip Code: 539884) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be available for trading starting December 1, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 50,029,335 equity shares of Rs. 2.00/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1.00/-
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE671T01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 50029336 to 100058670
- Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- New shares rank equally (pari-passu) with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 539884

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 1, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market-wide impact. The listing affects only Darshan Orna Limited and its shareholders who participated in the rights issue. The new shares will increase the company's outstanding equity base and are available for trading from December 1, 2025.
