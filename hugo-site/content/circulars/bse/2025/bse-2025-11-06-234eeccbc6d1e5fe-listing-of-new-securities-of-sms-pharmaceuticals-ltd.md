## Summary

SMS Pharmaceuticals Ltd (Scrip Code: 532815) has listed 50,00,000 new equity shares on BSE, effective November 7, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, with an allotment date of September 10, 2025. The shares carry a lock-in period until October 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 50,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed on BSE
- Issued at a premium of Rs. 126/- (total issue price: Rs. 127/-)
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 88652031 to 93652030
- ISIN: INE812G01025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from Friday, November 7, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes starting November 7, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: October 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The preferential allotment to promoters increases the paid-up capital but all shares are subject to a lock-in period of nearly 2 years, preventing immediate trading impact. The shares rank equally with existing shares, maintaining uniformity in shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing notification requiring trading members to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and lock-in restrictions for the specified distinctive number range.
