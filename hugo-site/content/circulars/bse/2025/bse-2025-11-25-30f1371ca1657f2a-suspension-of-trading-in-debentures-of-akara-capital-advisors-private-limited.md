## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED with ISIN INE08XP07233 (Code: 975766). The suspension is effective from December 5, 2025, as the company has fixed December 6, 2025 as the record date for debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Company: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture Series: ACAPL-10.01%-21-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE08XP07233
- BSE Code: 975766
- Record Date: December 6, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 5, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-771/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 5, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No trades should be executed in ISIN INE08XP07233 during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Circular issued by BSE
- **December 5, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **December 6, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to the corporate action of redemption and interest payment. This is a standard market practice to prevent trading during record date determination.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their interest payment and principal redemption. No impact on other securities or market segments.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this ISIN from December 5, 2025 onwards.
