## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement schedule for Settlement No. 754 dated November 13, 2025. The circular lists 103 securities from various companies with specific quantities to be auctioned in the dematerialized settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 754
- Settlement Date: November 13, 2025
- Total securities listed: 103 scrips across various sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share to 39,798 shares depending on the security
- Highest quantity: Money Masters Ltd (535910) with 39,798 shares
- Other significant quantities include: Mirae Asset (1,779 shares), Murae Organics (16,258 shares), Union Bank (4,200 shares), Cupid Ltd (4,023 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants holding positions in the listed securities must comply with auction settlement procedures
- Entities with obligations for the listed scrips must fulfill settlement requirements as per BSE auction mechanisms
- Clearing members must ensure timely settlement of auction obligations

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** November 13, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 754

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine auction settlement affecting specific securities with relatively small quantities in most cases. The auction mechanism is a standard procedure for addressing delivery shortfalls and does not indicate systemic market issues.

**Operational Impact:** Low to Medium for affected participants - Brokers and clearing members with positions in these specific securities will need to participate in the auction process. The impact is limited to entities with unsettled obligations in the listed scrips.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Most retail investors are unaffected. Only those with pending delivery obligations in the listed securities need to take action through their brokers.
