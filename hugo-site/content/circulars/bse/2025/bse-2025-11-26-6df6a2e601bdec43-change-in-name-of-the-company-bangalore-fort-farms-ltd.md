## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Bangalore Fort Farms Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Grameva Limited with effect from November 4, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, Manesar, Haryana under the authority of the Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Bangalore Fort Farms Limited** to **Grameva Limited**
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L51101WB1966PLC226442
- Effective date of name change: November 4, 2025
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate digitally signed by Prerna Panwar (Assistant Registrar of Companies)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action under the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Bangalore Fort Farms Limited) along with the new name (Grameva Limited) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All company documents, letterheads, and signage must be updated to reflect the new name while maintaining the old name display requirement

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Bangalore Fort Farms Limited to Grameva Limited
- **November 26, 2025**: BSE circular notification date
- **Two-year period**: Duration for mandatory display of both old and new company names

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard corporate administrative action with no material impact on business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative updates required across all company communications, documentation, and legal filings. The registered office address remains unchanged at Mani Square Mall, Kolkata.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The CIN and legal entity remain the same.

**Trading Impact**: Stock ticker and trading symbols may need to be updated on exchanges. Investors should verify current company status on MCA portal (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21).
