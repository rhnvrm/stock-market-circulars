## Summary

Global Ocean Logistics India Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(b) for the quarter ending December 22, 2025. The company has 14,442,691 total equity shares with promoter holding at 59.86% and public holding at 40.14%. The company has not issued any partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, and has no locked-in shares or pledged promoter shares.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 14,442,691
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 8,645,531 shares (59.86%)
- Public holding: 5,797,160 shares (40.14%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 6
- Number of public shareholders: 533
- Dematerialized shares: 11,652,291 (80.68% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants outstanding
- No depository receipts issued
- No shares in locked-in status
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard quarterly shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filed under Regulation 31(1)(b) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Quarterly shareholding pattern submission requirement fulfilled
- Company has made required declarations regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and pledged shares

## Important Dates

- Quarter ending: December 22, 2025
- Filing date: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with no material impact on trading or operations. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding above the minimum threshold and adequate public float. The 80.68% dematerialization rate indicates good liquidity potential. No concerns regarding share encumbrances, pledges, or lock-ins.
