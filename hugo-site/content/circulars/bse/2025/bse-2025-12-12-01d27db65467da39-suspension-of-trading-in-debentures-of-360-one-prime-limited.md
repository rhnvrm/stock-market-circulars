## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE248U07DV2, Code: 974688) of 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED effective December 17, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption on exercise of put option. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from December 17, 2025 onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debenture code 974688 (ISIN: INE248U07DV2)
- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures on exercise of put option
- Notice number: 20251212-75 / DR-779/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these securities from suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 17, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No dealings permitted in debenture code 974688 (INE248U07DV2) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 17, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of the specific debenture series. The suspension affects a single debt instrument and is procedural in nature for redemption processing.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders exercising the put option will have their securities redeemed as per the terms. No trading will be possible from December 17, 2025, which prevents secondary market transactions during the redemption process.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and redemption process.
