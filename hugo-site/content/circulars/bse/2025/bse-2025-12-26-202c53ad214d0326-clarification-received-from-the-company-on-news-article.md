## Summary

BSE has sought clarification from Coforge Ltd (Scrip Code: 532541) regarding news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. This is part of the exchange's standard surveillance procedure to verify news accuracy and keep members and investors informed. The company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE has requested clarification from Coforge Ltd regarding news articles
- The news articles appeared on Moneycontrol and potentially other media channels
- This is a routine verification process by the exchange's surveillance department
- Reply from the company is pending
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Coforge Ltd is required to provide clarification to BSE regarding the news articles
- The company must respond to the exchange's query as part of standard disclosure obligations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Reply Status**: Awaited

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine surveillance activity by the exchange. Clarification requests are standard procedure when news articles appear about listed companies.

**Investor Impact**: Low - The circular serves as an informational notice to investors that the exchange is verifying news reports. No trading restrictions or immediate actions are required.

**Company Impact**: Low - Coforge Ltd needs to respond to the exchange's query, which is a standard compliance requirement. The actual impact will depend on the content of the news articles and the company's response.
