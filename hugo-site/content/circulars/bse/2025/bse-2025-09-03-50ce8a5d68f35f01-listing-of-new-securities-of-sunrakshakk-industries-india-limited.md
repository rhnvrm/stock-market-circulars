## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 11,69,600 new equity shares of Sunrakshakk Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 539300) issued on preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 11,69,600 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 830/-
- Issue price: Rs. 840/- per share
- Allotment date: May 30, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 5031751 to 6201350
- ISIN: INE671K01019
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions apply as per specified schedule

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: May 30, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 31, 2026 (410,400 shares) and March 31, 2027 (759,200 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment. The lock-in periods indicate structured release of shares to market over time, preventing immediate dilution effects.
