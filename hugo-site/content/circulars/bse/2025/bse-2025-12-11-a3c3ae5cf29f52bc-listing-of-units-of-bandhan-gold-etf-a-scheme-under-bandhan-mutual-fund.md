## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Bandhan Gold ETF units effective December 12, 2025. The open-ended Exchange Traded Fund replicates domestic gold prices and will trade in E Group with scrip code 544638. A total of 710,898 units were allotted on December 5, 2025, at an issue price of Rs.129.05 per unit with a face value of Rs.10.00. Units will trade exclusively in dematerialized form with a market lot of 1 unit. Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd is designated as the market maker.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Bandhan Gold ETF (GOLDBND)
- Listing Date: December 12, 2025
- Scrip Code: 544638
- ISIN: INF194KB1KJ7
- Group: E
- Total Units: 710,898
- Face Value: Rs.10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.129.05 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 5, 2025
- Investment Manager: Bandhan AMC Limited
- Market Maker: Riddisiddhi Bullions Ltd
- Registrar: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, mandating that mutual fund units trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in Bandhan Gold ETF units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lots (minimum 1 unit)
- Members should note the ETF is classified under E Group for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **December 5, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **December 11, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **December 12, 2025**: Listing effective date and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited market-wide impact as this is a routine ETF listing. The ETF provides investors with another instrument to gain exposure to domestic gold prices through the exchange platform.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal operational changes for trading members. Standard dematerialized trading procedures apply with a simple market lot structure of 1 unit, making it accessible for retail investors.

**Investor Impact**: Provides retail and institutional investors with a transparent, cost-effective vehicle to invest in gold through the exchange mechanism, tracking domestic gold prices.
