## Summary

BSE has listed 61,500 new equity shares of DJ Mediaprint & Logistics Limited (Scrip Code: 543193) with effect from November 26, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 104/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until May 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares**: 61,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 114/- (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 104/- premium)
- **Allotment Basis**: Preferential allotment to non-promoters
- **Purpose**: Conversion of warrants
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 32818102 to 32879601
- **Date of Allotment**: June 11, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE0B1K01014
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the listing of these new securities
- The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions as specified
- All 61,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 32818102 to 32879601) are locked-in until May 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: June 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: May 30, 2026
- **Circular Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (61,500) from warrant conversion on a preferential basis. The shares are locked-in until May 30, 2026, preventing immediate trading by allottees.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing notification for trading members to update their records regarding the expanded share capital of DJ Mediaprint & Logistics Limited.

**Investor Impact**: The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, maintaining equal rights for all shareholders. The lock-in period provides stability by preventing immediate selling pressure.
