## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and the updated list of Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The company will provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Office located at #1203, Cello Triumph, B-wing, IB Patel Road, Goregaon East, Mumbai-400063
- Contact number: +91 9013468987
- Complete list of Algo Providers available at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding the empanelment of a new algo provider.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members interested in using algorithmic trading solutions may engage with the newly empanelled provider.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is an administrative announcement. It provides trading members with an additional option for algorithmic trading solutions. The provisional empanelment expands the choice of algo providers available to BSE trading members, potentially benefiting those looking to implement or upgrade their algorithmic trading infrastructure.
