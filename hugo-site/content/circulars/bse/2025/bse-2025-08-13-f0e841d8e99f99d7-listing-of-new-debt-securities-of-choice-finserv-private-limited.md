## Summary

Choice Finserv Private Limited has listed new debt securities worth ₹35 crore on BSE Debt segment effective August 13, 2025. The securities carry an 11% interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 11, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,500 securities
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Scrip Code: 977023
- ISIN: INE04SE07137
- Issue basis: Private placement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from September 11, 2025 to August 11, 2027
- Maturity Date: August 11, 2027
- Trading Commencement: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing for a private company. The listing adds to the available debt instruments on BSE but does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework.
