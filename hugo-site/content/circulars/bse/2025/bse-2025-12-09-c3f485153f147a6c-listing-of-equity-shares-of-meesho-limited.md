## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Meesho Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544632, trading symbol MEESHO, and ISIN number INE0VDM01015. The exact date of listing and additional details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Meesho Limited's equity shares will be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544632
- Trading Symbol: MEESHO
- ISIN Number: INE0VDM01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the assigned scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for Meesho Limited
- Await further notice regarding the exact listing date and security details
- Prepare systems for trading once the listing date is announced

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Meesho is a significant e-commerce platform in India, and its listing is expected to attract considerable investor interest. The listing will add a major technology/e-commerce stock to BSE's equity segment.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details and prepare for trading activity once the listing date is announced.

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors interested in the e-commerce and technology sector will have a new investment opportunity once trading commences.
