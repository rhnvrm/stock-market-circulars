## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code 977318) effective December 12, 2025. This follows a clarification from the company that the record date of December 11, 2025, previously communicated via Exchange Notice No. 20251209-69 dated December 09, 2025, is only for the purpose of interest payment and not for redemption of debentures. The company has requested resumption of trading based on this clarification.

## Key Points

- Trading in MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED debentures (Scrip Code 977318) will resume from December 12, 2025
- Record date of December 11, 2025 is confirmed to be for interest payment only, not redemption
- Previous notice dated December 09, 2025 had indicated the record date was for both interest payment and redemption
- Company has clarified the error and requested trading resumption
- Reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a clarification and correction of previously communicated information.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Note that trading in the specified debentures will be available from December 12, 2025
- Process trades in Scrip Code 977318 normally from the resumption date

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for interest payment on debentures (Scrip Code 977318)
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading resumption date for the debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The trading suspension was brief and related to administrative clarification. Normal trading activity will resume immediately.

**Investor Impact**: Low impact on debenture holders. The clarification confirms that redemption is not occurring on the record date, only interest payment. Investors can resume trading the debentures from December 12, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to enable trading in this scrip from the resumption date. The correction prevents unnecessary confusion about redemption versus interest payment events.
