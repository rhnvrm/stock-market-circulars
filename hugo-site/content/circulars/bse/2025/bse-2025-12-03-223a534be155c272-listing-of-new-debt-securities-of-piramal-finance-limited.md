## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Piramal Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 3, 2025. The securities comprise 19,723 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 9.30% p.a. interest rate and maturing on May 7, 2027. The securities are rated CARE AA/Stable and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976680, Scrip ID: 930PFL27A
- ISIN: INE202B07JP8 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 19,723 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,06,704.13
- Date of Allotment: December 2, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.30% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE202B07JP8
- Trading restricted to BSE Debt segment
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Annually from May 7, 2026 to May 7, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** May 7, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing with no impact on secondary market trading or retail investors. The securities are institutional-grade debt instruments.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members can now trade these debentures on the BSE Debt segment. The high face value (Rs. 1 lakh) indicates these are targeted at institutional investors.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals who participated in the private placement. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates strong creditworthiness with adequate safety for timely servicing of debt obligations.
