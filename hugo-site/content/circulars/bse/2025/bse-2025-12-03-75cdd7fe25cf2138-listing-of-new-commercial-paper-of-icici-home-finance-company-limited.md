## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) of Rs.125 crore on BSE's Debt segment on December 3, 2025. The CP has a face value of Rs.5 lakh per unit with 2,500 units issued on private placement basis. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will mature on December 29, 2025.

- Total issue size: Rs.125 crore (2,500 units Ã— Rs.5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs.4,97,854.50 per unit (discount of Rs.2,145.50 on face value)
- Scrip Code: 730693, Scrip ID: IHF031225
- ISIN: INE071G14HH3
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HH3
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples only
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 29, 2025
- **Tenure:** 26 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The 26-day commercial paper is a standard liquidity management tool for ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
