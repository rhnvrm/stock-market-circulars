## Summary

BSE has listed 73,260 further securities issued by companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities are subject to a lock-in period starting from December 19, 2025 and ending on January 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 73,260
- Share range: 2082758719 to 2082831978
- Lock-in status: Yes (restricted trading)
- Lock-in period: 29 days (December 19, 2025 to January 17, 2026)
- Securities type: ESOP/ESOS allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for employee stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must ensure compliance with lock-in restrictions during the specified period
- Shares cannot be traded or transferred during the lock-in period from December 19, 2025 to January 17, 2026
- Depositories and trading members must enforce lock-in restrictions on the specified share range

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Lock-in End Date**: January 17, 2026
- **Circular Date**: December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative action affecting employee stock option allotments.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard lock-in procedures apply to the specified share range. Trading platforms and depositories will automatically enforce restrictions.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to employees who received these stock options. The relatively short 29-day lock-in period suggests these are post-vesting exercise shares with temporary trading restrictions.
