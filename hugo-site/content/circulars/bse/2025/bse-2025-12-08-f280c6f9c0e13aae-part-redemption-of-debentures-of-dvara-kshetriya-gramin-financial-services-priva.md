## Summary

DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE179P07548, Scrip Code: 976048) along with payment of interest. The record date is set for December 12, 2025, and trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value of Rs. 5000 per debenture effective from the same date under settlement number DR-776/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (11%-27-12-26-PVT)
- ISIN: INE179P07548
- Scrip Code: 976048
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 5000 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from December 12, 2025
- Debenture holders registered as of the record date (December 12, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Debenture holders will receive partial redemption payment along with interest for holdings as of the record date. The face value of remaining debentures will be reduced to Rs. 5000 per debenture.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members should ensure their systems reflect the reduced face value from December 12, 2025 onwards to prevent settlement issues.

**Market Operations**: Routine corporate action with no broader market implications. This affects only holders of this specific debt security from DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED.
