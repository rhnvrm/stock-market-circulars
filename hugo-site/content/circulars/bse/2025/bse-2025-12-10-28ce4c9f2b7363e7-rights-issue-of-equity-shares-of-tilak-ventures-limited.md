## Summary

TILAK VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 503663) has announced a rights issue of equity shares. The company will issue 2 (two) new equity shares of Re.1/- each for every 1 (one) existing equity share held by shareholders. The issue price is Re.1/- payable on application. Trading will commence on an ex-rights basis from December 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 2 new equity shares for every 1 existing share held
- Issue price: Re.1/- per share (at par)
- Face value: Re.1/- per equity share
- Payment terms: Full issue price payable on application
- Scrip code: 503663
- Settlement: Dematerialized securities under rolling settlement segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions on an ex-rights basis from the specified date
- Shareholders as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the rights issue
- Trading members are required to take note of the ex-rights trading arrangement

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-777/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Significant dilution event with a 2:1 rights ratio, effectively tripling the share capital if fully subscribed. Existing shareholders who do not participate will face substantial dilution of their ownership percentage.

**Market Impact:** Shares will trade ex-rights from December 15, 2025, likely resulting in a price adjustment to reflect the rights offering. The theoretical ex-rights price will be lower than the cum-rights price.

**Trading Impact:** All equity transactions in TILAK VENTURES LIMITED must be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 15, 2025, affecting both institutional and retail trading activity.
