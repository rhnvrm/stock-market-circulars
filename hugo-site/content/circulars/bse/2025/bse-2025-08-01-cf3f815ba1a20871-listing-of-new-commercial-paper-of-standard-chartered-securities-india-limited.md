## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis, effective August 1, 2025. The issue comprises 300 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 15 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total issue size: 300 units (Rs. 15 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,65,983 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729943 (SCSIL31725)
- ISIN: INE472H14672

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- Listing effective: August 1, 2025
- Redemption date: July 31, 2026
- Maturity: 1 year

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Standard Chartered Securities India Limited with no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
