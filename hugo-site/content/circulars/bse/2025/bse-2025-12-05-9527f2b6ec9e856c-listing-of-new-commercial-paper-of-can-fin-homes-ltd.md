## Summary

Can Fin Homes Ltd has listed 10,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 500 crore) on BSE Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with an issue price of Rs. 4,92,631 per unit against face value of Rs. 5,00,000. The instruments carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 500 crore
- Scrip Code: 730707
- Scrip ID: CFH041225
- ISIN: INE477A14EB5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,631 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE477A14EB5
- Trading must be done in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 4, 2025
- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 5, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets or general trading operations. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for Can Fin Homes Ltd and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
