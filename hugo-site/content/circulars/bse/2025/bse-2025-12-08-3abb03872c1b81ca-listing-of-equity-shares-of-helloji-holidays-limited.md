## Summary

HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the completion of its IPO on December 8, 2025. The filing shows the post-issue shareholding structure with 4 promoters holding 2,400,000 shares and 188 public shareholders holding 1,030,000 shares.

## Key Points

- Company: HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Filing Date: December 8, 2025 (Post-IPO)
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 4 shareholders holding 2,400,000 shares
- Public Shareholders: 188 shareholders holding 1,030,000 shares
- Total Shares: 3,430,000 equity shares
- Promoter shareholding: Approximately 69.97%
- Public shareholding: Approximately 30.03%
- Locked-in shares: Yes (for Promoter, Public, and Non-Promoter-Non-Public categories)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, pledged shares, or non-disposal undertakings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard compliance filing under existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing required under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR 2015
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure mandatory for newly listed entities
- Declaration submitted confirming no outstanding convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or encumbrances except locked-in shares
- All shares held in dematerialized form

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: December 8, 2025
- IPO Completion: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-IPO disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a standard structure with promoters retaining majority stake (approximately 70%) and public shareholders holding the minimum required free float (approximately 30%). The presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with SEBI requirements for post-listing lock-in periods. No material concerns identified regarding share encumbrances or outstanding convertible instruments.
