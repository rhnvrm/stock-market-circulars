## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 15,73,000 equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676) effective from December 22, 2025. The shares were issued at Re. 1/- face value with a premium of Rs. 49/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The allotment date was October 16, 2025, and all shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 15,73,000 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 50/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 49/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from December 22, 2025
- ISIN: INE337M01021
- Scrip Code: 533676
- Distinctive Numbers: 121600191 to 123173190

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes
- All 15,73,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential issue represents a relatively small capital raise for the company. The lock-in period until July 31, 2026, ensures these shares will not create immediate selling pressure. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital. The shares rank equally with existing equity shares, maintaining uniform shareholder rights.
