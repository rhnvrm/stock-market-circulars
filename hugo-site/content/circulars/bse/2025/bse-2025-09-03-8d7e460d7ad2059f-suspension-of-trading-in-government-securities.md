## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two government securities effective September 4, 2025, as they approach their redemption/maturity date. The affected securities are 823GUJ2025 (ISIN: IN1520150047) and 823MH2025 (ISIN: IN2220150089).

## Key Points

- Two government securities will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from September 4, 2025
- Securities are approaching their redemption/maturity date
- Trading members advised not to deal in these securities
- Notice issued under reference DR-708/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is a standard operational procedure for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified government securities from September 4, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Notice issued
- **September 4, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: Securities mature (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension for maturing government securities. Only affects traders dealing specifically in 823GUJ2025 and 823MH2025 government securities. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
