## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of KSH International Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544664 and trading symbol KSHINTL with ISIN number INE987S01020. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- KSH International Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544664
- Trading Symbol: KSHINTL
- ISIN Number: INE987S01020
- Listing date to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 22, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members are informed in advance to prepare their systems for the new scrip. The actual trading impact will depend on the company's fundamentals, offer size, and market conditions at the time of listing, which will be detailed in the subsequent notice.
