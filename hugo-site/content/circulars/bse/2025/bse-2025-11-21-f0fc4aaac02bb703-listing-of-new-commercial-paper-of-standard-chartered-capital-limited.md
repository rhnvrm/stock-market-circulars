## Summary

Standard Chartered Capital Limited has listed new Commercial Paper on BSE's Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The issue comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 250 crores, issued on private placement basis with a 306-day tenure maturing on September 23, 2026. The instrument carries dual top-tier credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

- Issue size: 500 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000 = Rs. 250 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 471,723.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730623, Scrip ID: SCCL211125
- ISIN: INE403G14TV4
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G14TV4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** September 23, 2026
- **Tenure:** 306 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the market. The issue size of Rs. 250 crores is standard for corporate Commercial Paper programs.

**Operational Impact:** No significant operational changes required. Trading members only need to be aware of the new listing and standard trading parameters (dematerialized form, Rs. 5 lakh denominations, 1 paise tick size).

**Investor Impact:** Provides institutional investors with a highly-rated (A1+) short-term investment option from Standard Chartered Capital Limited with approximately 10-month maturity.
