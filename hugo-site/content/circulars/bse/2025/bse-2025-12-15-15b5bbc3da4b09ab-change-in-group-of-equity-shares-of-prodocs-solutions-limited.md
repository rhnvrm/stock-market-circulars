## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of equity shares of PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544643) from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective December 30, 2025. This follows the earlier notice dated December 12, 2025, and represents a transition from restricted trading to regular rolling settlement for this SME IPO stock.

## Key Points

- PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED equity shares (Scrip Code: 544643) will be transferred from MT Group to M Group
- Transfer effective date: Tuesday, December 30, 2025
- Current classification: SME IPO in Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New classification: Rolling segment (M Group)
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251212-59 dated December 12, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from Trade for Trade settlement (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group). This change allows for:
- T+1 settlement cycle instead of compulsory delivery-based trading
- Improved liquidity and trading flexibility
- Regular market operations consistent with other rolling settlement stocks

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment transfer in their systems
- All dealings in PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED shares will be under M Group from December 30, 2025
- Trading Members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain for clarification or additional information
- Existing positions in MT Group should be squared off or prepared for migration

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20251212-59)
- **December 15, 2025**: Current notice date
- **December 30, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The transition from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement is generally positive for market participants as it:
- Enhances liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Reduces settlement restrictions
- Enables carry-forward positions
- Makes the stock more accessible to retail and institutional investors

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to update their systems and inform clients about the change in trading mechanism. The stock will behave like regular rolling settlement securities post-December 30, 2025, allowing for more flexible trading strategies.
