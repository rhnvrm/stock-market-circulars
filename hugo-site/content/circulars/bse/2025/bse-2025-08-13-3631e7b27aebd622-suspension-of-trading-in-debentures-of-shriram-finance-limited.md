## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of SHRIRAM FINANCE LIMITED (Code: SFL-11%-30-08-2025-PVT, ISIN: INE721A08AH3) effective August 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing August 15, 2025 as the record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to SHRIRAM FINANCE LIMITED debentures (Code: 946997)
- ISIN: INE721A08AH3 (SFL-11%-30-08-2025-PVT)
- Record date: August 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings allowed from August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard procedure for trading suspension before record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Trading members are advised to take note of the suspension
- All transactions in the affected debentures must cease

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Notice issued
- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension begins
- **August 15, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine suspension affecting only specific debenture series of SHRIRAM FINANCE LIMITED. The suspension ensures proper settlement of interest payments and redemption to eligible holders as per the record date. Normal trading suspension procedure with no broader market implications.
