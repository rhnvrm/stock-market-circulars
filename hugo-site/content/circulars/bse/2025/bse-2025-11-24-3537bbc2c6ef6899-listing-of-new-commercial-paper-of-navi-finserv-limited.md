## Summary

BSE has listed three new Commercial Paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The securities carry IND A1+ credit rating and have a combined value of Rs. 35 crore (700 units at Rs. 5 lakh face value each). All three instruments mature in February 2026 and will trade in dematerialized form with HDFC Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Three Commercial Paper securities listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 700 units (300 + 200 + 200) aggregating Rs. 35 crore
- All securities allotted on November 21, 2025
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ for all three instruments
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying agent

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE Debt Department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and does not affect equity markets. The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors seeking exposure to Navi Finserv Limited's commercial paper. No operational changes required for equity market participants.
