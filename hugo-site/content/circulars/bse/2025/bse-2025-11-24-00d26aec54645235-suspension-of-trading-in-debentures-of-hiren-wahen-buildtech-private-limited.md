## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Hiren Wahen Buildtech Private Limited (ISIN: INE0S1Q07019, Code: 975302) effective November 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing November 30, 2025 as the record date for redemption of its 19% debentures maturing on November 30, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture: HWBPL-19%-30-11-25-PVT (ISIN: INE0S1Q07019, Code: 975302)
- Record date for redemption: November 30, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: November 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Notice number: 20251124-49 / DR-766/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 28, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to their clients accordingly
- No new buy or sell orders should be accepted for these debentures during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 28, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **November 30, 2025**: Record date for redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension for a private limited company's debenture redemption. The impact is limited to holders of these specific debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear in the records as of the record date (November 30, 2025) will receive redemption proceeds. Trading is suspended to ensure proper identification of eligible holders.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these debentures and inform clients holding these securities about the redemption process.
