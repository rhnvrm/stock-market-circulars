## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to dealings of new debt securities issued by Arman Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The securities are rated ACUITE A-/Stable with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, offering 11.35% per annum interest payable quarterly.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 75,000 units (total issue size: Rs. 75 crore)
- **Scrip Code**: 977345
- **Scrip ID**: 1135AFSL27
- **ISIN**: INE109C07121
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 11.35% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from March 5, 2026 to December 5, 2027
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 5, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE109C07121
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for detailed information
- Standard trading and settlement procedures for debt securities apply

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: March 5, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Quarterly until December 5, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: December 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal market-wide impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing.

**Investor Impact**: Provides an investment opportunity for debt investors seeking A-rated securities with 11.35% annual returns over a 2-year tenure. The quarterly interest payment structure offers regular income.

**Company Impact**: Enables Arman Financial Services Limited to raise Rs. 75 crore through debt issuance for business operations and expansion.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these securities on the BSE Debt segment. Contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.
