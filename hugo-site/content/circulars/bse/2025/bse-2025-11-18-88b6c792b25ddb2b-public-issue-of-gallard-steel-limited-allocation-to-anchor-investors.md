## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Gallard Steel Limited. Anchor investor allocation is typically conducted one day prior to the issue opening date and provides early price discovery and institutional validation for the IPO.

## Key Points

- Gallard Steel Limited's public issue has completed anchor investor allocation
- Anchor investors are qualified institutional buyers who bid for shares before the issue opens to public
- Allocation to anchor investors provides institutional validation and helps in price discovery
- This allocation is part of the pre-IPO process as per SEBI guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for IPO anchor investor allocation as per existing SEBI ICDR regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Disclosure of anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Transparency in allocation process and pricing for anchor portion
- Lock-in requirements for anchor investors as per regulatory norms

## Important Dates

- Anchor investor allocation date: November 18, 2025
- Public issue opening date to follow after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact as it relates to a specific company's IPO process. Anchor investor allocation typically indicates:

- Strong institutional interest in the IPO
- Price discovery and validation of issue price
- Sets the tone for retail investor participation
- Demonstrates market confidence in Gallard Steel Limited's public offering

For investors interested in Gallard Steel Limited's IPO, the anchor allocation details provide insights into institutional demand and pricing, which can inform investment decisions for the retail portion of the offering.
