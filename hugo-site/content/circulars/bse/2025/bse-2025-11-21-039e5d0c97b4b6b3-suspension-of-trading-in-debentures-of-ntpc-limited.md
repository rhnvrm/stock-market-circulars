## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251121-65 suspending trading in specific non-convertible debentures of NTPC LIMITED (NTPC-8.19%-15-12-25-PVT, ISIN: INE733E07JX0, Code: 953113) effective December 1, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NTPC LIMITED debenture series: NTPC-8.19%-15-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE733E07JX0, BSE Code: 953113
- Record date set for December 1, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings allowed from December 1, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-767/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 1, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealings directive immediately

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 1, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 1, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific NTPC debenture series. The suspension is procedural and expected for securities approaching redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. No trading opportunity available from December 1, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update systems to block trades in this ISIN. Routine corporate action with standard processing requirements.
