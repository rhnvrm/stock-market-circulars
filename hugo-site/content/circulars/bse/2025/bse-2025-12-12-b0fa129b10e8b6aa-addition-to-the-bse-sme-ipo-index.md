## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544637) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company is being listed on BSE effective December 12, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on December 15, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (ticker: 544637) listing on BSE effective December 12, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on December 15, 2025
- Addition effective at market open on December 15, 2025
- Corporate action file (*.SDE) updated with this change
- Announcement references Notice No. 20251211-50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent addition following the listing of a new SME company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED listing on BSE
- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME company to the BSE SME IPO Index. The impact is limited to investors and funds tracking this specific index.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Index tracking products will need to rebalance to include the new constituent, which is a standard operational process.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for investors focused on the SME IPO segment and those tracking the BSE SME IPO Index. No action required for general market participants.
