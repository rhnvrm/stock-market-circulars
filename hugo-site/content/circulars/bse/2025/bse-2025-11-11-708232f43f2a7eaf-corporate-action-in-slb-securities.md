## Summary

BSE has issued a circular detailing corporate actions for 23 securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. All listed companies have declared dividends with their respective record dates, ex-dates, and shut periods scheduled between November 11-26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 23 companies have announced dividend corporate actions
- All corporate actions are dividend-related (no other action types)
- Record dates range from November 11, 2025 to November 26, 2025
- Ex-dates align with record dates for all securities
- Shut periods are typically 1-2 days before the record date
- Indian Metals & Ferro Alloys Limited has the earliest action (November 11, 2025)
- Power Finance Corporation Ltd has the latest action (November 26, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard informational circular about scheduled corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB market participants must observe foreclosure dates for lending/borrowing positions
- Securities will be unavailable for new SLB transactions during shut periods
- Existing SLB positions must be squared off or rolled over before foreclosure dates
- Market participants must ensure compliance with corporate action timelines

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects liquidity in the SLB segment for 23 securities over a two-week period. Market participants with active lending/borrowing positions need to plan position management around these dates.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - requires SLB participants to track multiple foreclosure dates and shut periods. Trading and settlement teams must coordinate position squaring or rollover activities.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - primarily affects institutional investors and traders using SLB facilities. Retail investors holding underlying securities are not directly impacted but will receive dividends if holding shares on record dates.
