## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544630) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on December 9, 2025, and will be added to the index effective at market open on December 10, 2025. This is a routine index maintenance action following the company's listing on the BSE SME platform.

## Key Points

- HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED (ticker: 544630) listed on BSE on Tuesday, December 9, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective Wednesday, December 10, 2025 at market open
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Refers to prior Notice No. 20251208-28 regarding the listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index constituent update following new listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: Listing date for HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED on BSE
- **December 10, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. Index additions following SME IPO listings are standard procedures. Market participants tracking or replicating the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust portfolios to include the new constituent. The addition affects index funds, ETFs, and other products benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
