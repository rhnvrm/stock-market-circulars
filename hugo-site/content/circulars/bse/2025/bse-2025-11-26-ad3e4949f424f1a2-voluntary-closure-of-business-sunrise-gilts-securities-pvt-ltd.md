## Summary

BSE has announced that Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 4071) will voluntarily cease its business operations in the OBPP (Odd-Lot Buy Back and Pre-Open) segment effective November 27, 2025. This is an administrative closure with no broader market impact.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd.
- Member number: 4071
- Affected segment: OBPP (Odd-Lot Buy Back and Pre-Open)
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by: Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: November 26, 2025
- Effective date of closure: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This affects only one trading member's operations in the OBPP segment. Other trading members and market participants are not affected. The closure is voluntary and orderly, with no implications for market stability or operations.
