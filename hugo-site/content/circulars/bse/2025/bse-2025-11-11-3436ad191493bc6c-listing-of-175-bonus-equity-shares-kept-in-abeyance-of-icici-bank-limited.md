## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 175 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, November 12, 2025. The shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and are allocated under ISIN INE090A01021.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 175 bonus equity shares kept in abeyance
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Distribution: Three batches (75 shares, 50 shares, and 50 shares)
- Distinctive number ranges provided for all three batches

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 175 shares, which is an extremely small quantity relative to ICICI Bank's total outstanding shares. These are bonus shares that were previously kept in abeyance and are now being released for trading. This is an administrative matter with negligible impact on stock liquidity or trading volumes.
