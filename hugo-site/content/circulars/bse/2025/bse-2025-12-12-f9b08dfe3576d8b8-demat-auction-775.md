## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities and quantities for Demat Auction under Settlement No. 775 dated December 12, 2025. The auction includes 115 scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 199,999 shares across diverse sectors and market capitalizations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 775
- Date: December 12, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 115
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 199,999 shares (Spright Agro)
- Covers diverse sectors including infrastructure, pharmaceuticals, metals, IT, consumer goods, and financial services
- Highest quantities: Spright Agro (199,999), Sattva Sukun (54,354), Chartered Log (10,400), Droneacharya (6,000), SVS Ventures (6,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational auction settlement circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants with delivery obligations for the listed securities must participate in the demat auction process
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Members must fulfill their obligations for the specified quantities

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: 775

## Impact Assessment

This auction settlement affects trading members and custodians who have failed to deliver securities in the regular settlement cycle. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and maintains market integrity. The impact is localized to specific participants with delivery shortfalls in these 115 scrips. Large quantities in certain scrips (Spright Agro, Sattva Sukun, Chartered Log) may indicate significant delivery failures requiring attention.
