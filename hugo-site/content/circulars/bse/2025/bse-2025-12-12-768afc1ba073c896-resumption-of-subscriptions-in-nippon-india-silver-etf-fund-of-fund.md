## Summary

Nippon Life India Asset Management Limited has announced the resumption of all subscriptions in Nippon India Silver ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) effective December 12, 2025. This reverses the restrictions previously implemented via Notice cum Addendum No. 59 dated October 15, 2025. All subscription channels including fresh investments, additional subscriptions, switch-ins, and systematic plans (SIP/STP) are now available without any limits.

## Key Points

- All subscriptions in Nippon India Silver ETF Fund of Fund resumed without restrictions
- Fresh and additional subscriptions now allowed without limits
- Switch-ins to the scheme permitted
- Systematic Investment Plans (SIP) and Systematic Transfer Plans (STP) fresh registrations enabled
- Decision made considering current market conditions
- Supersedes previous restrictions from Notice cum Addendum No. 59 dated October 15, 2025
- Forms integral part of Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update reversing temporary subscription restrictions previously imposed by the fund house.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors can now subscribe to the scheme through all available channels
- Standard mutual fund investment procedures apply
- All other terms and conditions of the Scheme Information Document remain unchanged
- Investors must continue to read all scheme related documents carefully before investing

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 12, 2025 - Resumption of all subscriptions without restrictions
- **Previous Restriction Date:** October 15, 2025 - When limitations were originally imposed (Addendum No. 59)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This affects only investors in Nippon India Silver ETF Fund of Fund and represents a return to normal operations.

**Operational Impact:** Positive for investors seeking exposure to silver through this fund of fund structure. The removal of subscription restrictions indicates improved market conditions or liquidity in the underlying Silver ETF, allowing the fund to accept new investments.

**Investor Impact:** Investors who were previously unable to invest or increase their holdings in this scheme can now do so without limitations. This provides greater flexibility for portfolio allocation to silver as an asset class through the mutual fund route.
