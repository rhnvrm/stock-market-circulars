## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, both with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each and rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper securities listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 12, 2025
- Securities issued on private placement basis with Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Both securities have 10,000 quantity with market lot of 1
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+ for both instruments
- Central Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialised form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - BHFL-09-02-26-CP: February 9, 2026
  - BHFL-12-01-26-CP: January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding purposes. The securities provide institutional investors with additional debt investment options with high credit ratings.
