## Summary

BSE has notified that Stable Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6829) will voluntarily cease its business operations in the Debt and OBPP (Over the counter, Book Building, Private Placement) segments effective Monday, November 24, 2025. This is a voluntary closure decision by the member firm.

## Key Points

- Stable Broking Private Limited holds BSE Member No. 6829
- Business cessation applies to Debt and OBPP segments only
- Closure is voluntary in nature
- All trading members have been informed of this development
- Queries should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is an informational notice regarding a member firm's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from other trading members. This notice is for information purposes only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 24, 2025 (Monday) - Stable Broking Private Limited ceases operations in Debt and OBPP segments

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this closure is minimal and localized to one trading member. The voluntary cessation of Stable Broking Private Limited's operations in Debt and OBPP segments does not affect overall market operations or other trading members. This is a routine administrative matter related to membership management. Market participants dealing with this member should make alternative arrangements before the effective date.
