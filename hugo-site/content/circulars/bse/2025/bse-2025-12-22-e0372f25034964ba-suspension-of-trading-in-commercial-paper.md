## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd (ISIN: INE423A14XT1, Code: 729731) effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the redemption of the commercial paper, with the record date fixed as December 23, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper ISIN: INE423A14XT1
- BSE Code: 729731
- Issuer: Adani Enterprises Ltd
- Record Date: December 23, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 23, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Notice Reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any transactions in the specified commercial paper (INE423A14XT1) from December 23, 2025
- All pending orders and positions should be squared off before the suspension date
- Members must update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a single commercial paper instrument and is a routine redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard procedure for debt instrument redemption. Trading members dealing in this specific commercial paper need to ensure no trades are executed from the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Holders of this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading disruption for other securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd.
