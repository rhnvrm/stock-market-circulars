## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 19,822 new equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 19,822 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 509/-
- Issue price: Rs. 519/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 12349852 to 12369673
- Date of allotment: April 7, 2025
- ISIN: INE459Z01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in periods apply to different tranches of shares
- Shares subject to various lock-in periods ranging from March 31, 2026 to April 1, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 7, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 31, 2026 to April 1, 2026 (varies by tranche)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact given the small number of shares (19,822) and lock-in restrictions. Routine corporate action for warrant conversion with no significant operational changes for trading members.
