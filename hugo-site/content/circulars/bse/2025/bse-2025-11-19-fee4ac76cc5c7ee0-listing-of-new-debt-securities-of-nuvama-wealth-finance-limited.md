## Summary

BSE has listed 277 new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 19, 2025. The securities are gold future-backed bonds with a face value of Rs. 100,000, issued at Rs. 97,391, and carry a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable rating. The securities will mature on April 30, 2029.

## Key Points

- Quantity of securities listed: 277 units
- Scrip Code: 977266
- Scrip ID: NWFL301025
- Detail Name: NWFL-GOLDFUTURE-30-4-29-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE918K07QE9 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,391.00 (discount of approximately 2.6%)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE918K07QE9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: April 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities are institutional in nature with high face value (Rs. 1 lakh) and are gold future-backed. The AA/Stable rating indicates strong creditworthiness. This listing provides additional debt financing options for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited and does not affect equity markets or retail investors directly.
