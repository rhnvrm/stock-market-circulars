## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 12, 2025. The issue comprises 2,500 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 125 crore, maturing on February 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each (Total: Rs. 125 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730544
- Scrip ID: N18M121125
- ISIN: INE870H14WE2
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,490 to face value)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WE2
- Trading must be done in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members can contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Listing Date: November 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 11, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Network 18 Media & Investments Limited at an implied discount rate. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong short-term creditworthiness. The listing expands the debt trading options on BSE but has no direct impact on equity markets or trading operations. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can access this security for their clients' portfolios.
