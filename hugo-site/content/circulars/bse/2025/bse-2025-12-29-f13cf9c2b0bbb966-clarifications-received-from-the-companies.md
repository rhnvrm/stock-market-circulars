## Summary

BSE published clarifications received from multiple companies regarding significant movements in price and trading volume of their securities. Three companies - Sasken Technologies Limited, 5paisa Capital Limited, and Enterprise International Ltd - responded to BSE surveillance queries dated December 26, 2025. All companies attributed the price/volume movements to market conditions and confirmed compliance with disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Sasken Technologies Limited confirmed timely disclosures under SEBI LODR and stated no undisclosed events affecting operations/performance
- Sasken's trading window closed effective December 16, 2025 for designated persons ahead of Q3 FY2026 results (quarter ending December 31, 2025)
- 5paisa Capital Limited stated volume movement is purely market-driven with no management control or knowledge of specific reasons
- Enterprise International Ltd responded to BSE query (Ref No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3242) regarding price movement
- All companies confirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR for timely disclosures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies must disclose any material events/information that may impact stock price or operations
- Trading window closure must be implemented for designated persons during periods of financial result consideration

## Important Dates

- December 26, 2025: Date of BSE surveillance queries to companies
- December 16, 2025: Trading window closure date for Sasken Technologies designated persons
- December 31, 2025: Quarter end date for Sasken Technologies financial results
- December 27, 2025: Response date from Enterprise International Ltd

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - These are routine surveillance responses with no material information disclosed. The clarifications confirm that recent price/volume movements are market-driven rather than caused by undisclosed corporate events.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - No new material information provided. Investors should note that Sasken Technologies has closed its trading window ahead of Q3 results announcement, which is standard practice.

**Operational Impact:** None - No operational changes or corporate actions announced. Companies reaffirmed commitment to timely disclosures as per regulatory requirements.
