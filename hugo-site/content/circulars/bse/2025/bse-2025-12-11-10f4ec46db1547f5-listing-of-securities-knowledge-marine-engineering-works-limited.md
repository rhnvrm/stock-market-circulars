## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,421,054 new equity shares of Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited (Scrip Code: 543273) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on December 12, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until June 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,421,054 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value issued at a premium of Rs. 1,890
- Issue price: Rs. 1,900 per share
- Allotment date: October 30, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 12, 2025
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0CJD01011
- Distinctive Numbers: 10801001 to 12222054

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading from December 12, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 1,421,054 shares until June 28, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The locked-in shares represent additional capital raised by Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited and will not be available for trading until mid-2026. The listing increases the company's equity base and provides additional liquidity post lock-in expiry.
