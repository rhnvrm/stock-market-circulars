## Summary

OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE04VS07388, Scrip Code: 976577) with payment of interest. The record date is set for December 12, 2025, and trading will commence with reduced face value from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture Series: OFSL-9.75%-27-3-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE04VS07388
- Scrip Code: 976577
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 71,428 per Debenture (from previous face value)
- Effective Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 71,428 per debenture effective from December 12, 2025
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for settlement purposes
- All trades executed on or after the effective date must be at the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Existing debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment. The remaining investment will continue with reduced face value.

**Trading Impact**: Trading will continue with adjusted face value from December 12, 2025. Market participants must adjust their valuation and trading systems accordingly.

**Liquidity**: This part redemption reduces the outstanding debt obligation of OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED while maintaining debenture listing with reduced face value.

**Market Operations**: Standard corporate action with no disruption expected. Settlement will occur under DR-776/2025-2026.
