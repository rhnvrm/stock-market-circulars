## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED on its SME platform for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025, containing all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability for at least 21 days as per ICDR requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filing date and notice date
- **January 21, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)
- **Minimum 21 days**: Document availability period on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to SME segment and potential investors interested in KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED's IPO. Standard procedural filing with no immediate market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency and opportunity for public feedback on the proposed IPO, allowing stakeholders to review company disclosures before final offer document approval.

**Regulatory Impact**: Demonstrates compliance with ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements for SME IPO draft offer document disclosure and public comment period.
