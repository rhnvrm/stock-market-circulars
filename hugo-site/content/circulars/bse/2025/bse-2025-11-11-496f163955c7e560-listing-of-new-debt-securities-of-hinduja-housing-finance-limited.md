## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities issued by Hinduja Housing Finance Limited (HHFL) on private placement basis to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 11, 2025. The securities comprise 20,000 units with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.05% per annum with CARE AA+/Stable and CRISIL AA+/STABLE credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 debt securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Scrip Code: 977282
- Scrip ID: 805HHFL30
- ISIN: INE401Y07068
- Interest Rate: 8.05% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2030
- Credit Rating: CARE AA+/Stable, CRISIL AA+/STABLE
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE401Y07068
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 10, 2025
- Listing Date: November 11, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from November 10, 2026 to November 10, 2030
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings and provides investors access to HHFL's AA+ rated debt instruments. The securities offer a competitive 8.05% annual interest rate for a 5-year tenure. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
