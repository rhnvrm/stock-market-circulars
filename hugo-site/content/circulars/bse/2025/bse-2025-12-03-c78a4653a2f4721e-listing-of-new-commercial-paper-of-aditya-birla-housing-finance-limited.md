## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 3, 2025. The securities will be traded in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 8,000 units
- Scrip Code: 730687
- Scrip ID: ABHFL21225
- ISIN: INE831R14FO7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,703.50/-
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FO7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 3, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 2, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 3, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The 91-day maturity period represents standard commercial paper tenure.
