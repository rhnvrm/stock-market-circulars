## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers of 13 companies effective from November 24-26, 2025, due to record dates fixed for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective suspension dates.

## Key Points

- 13 commercial papers suspended for redemption
- Suspensions effective between November 24-26, 2025
- Companies include major corporates: Adani Enterprises, Reliance Retail Ventures, Tata Power, Tata Housing, Titan, UGRO Capital
- Financial institutions affected: Standard Chartered Securities, HDFC Securities, SBICAP Securities, IndoStar Capital Finance
- Industrial companies: Dalmia Cement, J.K. Cement, Electrosteel Castings
- All suspensions aligned with respective record dates for redemption

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from their respective suspension dates
- Standard compliance with redemption procedures as per record dates
- Trading members to ensure client portfolios are updated accordingly

## Important Dates

**November 24, 2025:**
- Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited CP - Record date and trading suspension

**November 25, 2025:**
- Adani Enterprises Ltd. CP - Record date and trading suspension

**November 26, 2025:**
- IndoStar Capital Finance Limited CP
- HDFC Securities Limited CP
- SBICAP Securities Limited CP
- Reliance Retail Ventures Limited CP
- Tata Housing Development Company Limited CP
- Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited CP
- J.K. Cement Ltd CP
- Tata Power Co. Ltd CP
- Electrosteel Castings Ltd. CP
- UGRO Capital Limited CP
- Titan Company Limited CP

All with record date and trading suspension on November 26, 2025.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market liquidity for these specific instruments temporarily

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per terms; no trading opportunity from suspension dates

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update systems to block trading in these 13 commercial papers from respective dates

**Sectoral Distribution:** Diversified impact across financial services, retail, power, cement, and manufacturing sectors, indicating broad market commercial paper redemption activity
