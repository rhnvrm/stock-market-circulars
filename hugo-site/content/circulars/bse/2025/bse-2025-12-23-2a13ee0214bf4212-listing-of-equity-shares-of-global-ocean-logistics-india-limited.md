## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544665, symbol GLOBALLOG, and ISIN number INE1ITY01011. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Global Ocean Logistics India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544665
- Trading Symbol: GLOBALLOG
- ISIN Number: INE1ITY01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN number for Global Ocean Logistics India Limited and await the separate notice with the listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine new company listing on the SME segment with minimal immediate market impact.
