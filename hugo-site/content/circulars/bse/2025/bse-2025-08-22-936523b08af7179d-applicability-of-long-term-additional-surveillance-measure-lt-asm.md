## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework with changes effective August 25, 2025. Five new securities have been added to LT-ASM, four securities moved to lower ASM stages, and fifteen securities removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- 5 new securities shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- 4 securities moved to lower ASM Stage I from higher stages
- 15 securities moving out of Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities moved to higher ASM stages
- No securities moved directly to Stage IV ASM

## Securities Added to LT-ASM

- Aditya Consumer Marketing Ltd (540146)
- Ahasolar Technologies Ltd (543941)
- Filtron Engineers Ltd (531191)
- Modern Thread India Ltd (500282)
- OTCO International Ltd (523151)

## Securities Moved to Lower ASM Stage I

- Aryan Share and Stock Brokers Ltd (542176)
- Ashika Credit Capital Ltd (543766)
- B-Right Realestate Ltd (543543)
- Vadilal Enterprises Ltd (519152)

## Securities Removed from LT-ASM

15 securities removed due to various reasons:
- 6 securities moved to Trade for Trade framework
- 1 security moved to ESM Framework (Sadbhav Infrastructure Project Ltd)
- Others removed from framework entirely

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM framework changes will affect trading conditions for the listed securities. Securities added to LT-ASM will face enhanced surveillance measures, while those moved to lower stages or removed will have reduced restrictions. This impacts liquidity and trading behavior for the affected stocks.
