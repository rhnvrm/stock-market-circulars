## Summary

Axis Services Opportunities Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective July 30, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP & SWP facilities across six plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: July 30, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- Full facility support: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP
- Reference to previous notice 20250703-15

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - operational reopening of existing fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their systems for fund availability
- Ensure proper handling of all transaction types from July 30, 2025

## Important Dates

- **July 30, 2025**: Fund reopening date for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Provides additional investment option for investors seeking equity exposure in services sector. Routine operational change affecting fund distribution platform availability.
