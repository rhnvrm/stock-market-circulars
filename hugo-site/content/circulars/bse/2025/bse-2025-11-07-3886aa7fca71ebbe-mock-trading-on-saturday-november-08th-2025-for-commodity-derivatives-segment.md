## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications under various market conditions including exceptional scenarios like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS release is scheduled.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on November 08, 2025
- Members can test third-party trading platforms and in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will include exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Mock trades will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities
- Feedback must be submitted by 5:00 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - Log-in: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
  - Continuous Trading: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 04:00 PM to 04:10 PM
- **Feedback deadline**: 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading session with no real market or financial impact. The session is purely for testing and familiarization purposes. No actual trading obligations will be created, and no margins, pay-ins, or pay-outs will be triggered. This is a standard operational activity to ensure system readiness and member preparedness.
