## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to dealings of new debt securities issued by Namdev Finvest Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The issue comprises 18,000 debentures with an 11.56% p.a. interest rate and maturity date of December 11, 2030.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Namdev Finvest Private Limited
- **Quantity**: 18,000 debentures
- **Scrip Code**: 977365
- **Scrip ID**: 1156NFPL30
- **ISIN**: INE0IX207262
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Credit Rating**: CARE BBB+/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 11.56% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Half-yearly
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0IX207262
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Queries should be directed to BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Half-yearly from June 11, 2025 to December 11, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: December 11, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing with no broad market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors in privately placed debt securities. The CARE BBB+/Stable rating indicates adequate safety with moderate credit risk.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal - These securities will trade on the debt segment with standard parameters (1 paise tick size, lot size of 1 debenture).
