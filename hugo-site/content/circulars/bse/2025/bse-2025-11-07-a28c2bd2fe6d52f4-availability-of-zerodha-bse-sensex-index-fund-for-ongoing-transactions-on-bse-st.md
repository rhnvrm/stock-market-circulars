## Summary

BSE has announced that ZERODHA MUTUAL FUND will reopen its BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities effective November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- ZERODHA MUTUAL FUND reopening scheme for ongoing transactions after initial closure period
- Scheme Category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01174
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251017-24

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about scheme availability.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only investors interested in the ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard mutual fund platform operations resuming for a single scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors as the scheme becomes available for ongoing transactions with multiple investment options (lump sum, SIP, STP, SWP).
