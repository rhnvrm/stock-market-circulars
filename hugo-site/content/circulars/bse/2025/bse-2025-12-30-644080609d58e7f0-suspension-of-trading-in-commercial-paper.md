## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 8 companies effective from their respective record dates between January 1-6, 2026. The suspension is due to scheduled redemption of these commercial papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in these instruments from the specified no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 8 companies' commercial papers affected by trading suspension
- Suspensions effective between January 1-6, 2026
- Action taken due to companies fixing record dates for redemption
- Trading members explicitly advised against dealing in these instruments
- Notice applies to debt segment only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for temporary trading suspension due to scheduled redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates
- Members must ensure compliance with suspension directives for debt segment instruments
- Adherence to record date timelines for redemption purposes

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: Trading suspension and record date for Kirloskar Ferrous Industries Ltd.
- **January 5, 2026**: Trading suspension and record date for IndoStar Capital Finance, Capri Global Capital, and Bajaj Financial Securities
- **January 6, 2026**: Trading suspension and record date for Infina Finance, ECL Finance, Samvardhana Motherson International, and Hindustan Media Ventures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and temporary impact confined to debt market segment. The suspension affects only commercial paper instruments of 8 specific companies and is part of normal redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members holding or dealing in these commercial papers need to cease trading activities from specified dates. Investors holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds as per scheduled maturity.

**Duration**: Short-term impact as suspensions are directly tied to redemption events occurring within the first week of January 2026.
