## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of ENS Enterprises Limited for public comments. The document was filed on November 1, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The draft offer document will remain available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days to solicit public feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by ENS Enterprises Limited on November 1, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review and comments
- Comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations requirement for public comment period on draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from November 19, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document is accessible at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **November 1, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by ENS Enterprises Limited
- **November 19, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **December 10, 2025** (approx.): Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it is a routine procedural step in the SME IPO process. It affects only potential investors and stakeholders interested in the ENS Enterprises Limited IPO. The public comment period is a regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations to ensure transparency and allow public feedback before the final offer document is approved.
