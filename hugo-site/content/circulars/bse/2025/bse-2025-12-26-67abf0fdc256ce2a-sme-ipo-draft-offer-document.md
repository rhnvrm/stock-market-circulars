## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Advance Technoforge Limited filed on December 24, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Advance Technoforge Limited on December 24, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Document file name: 26AdvanceTechnoforgeDraftProspectus_20251224211343.pdf
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure circular under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments on the Draft Offer Document must be submitted within 21 days from December 26, 2025 (by January 16, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The Draft Offer Document will remain available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Advance Technoforge Limited
- **December 26, 2025**: Notice published and public comment period begins
- **January 16, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to SME segment. Primarily affects prospective investors and market participants interested in the upcoming IPO of Advance Technoforge Limited.

**Operational Impact**: No operational impact on existing market participants. Standard procedural disclosure for SME IPO process.

**Investor Impact**: Provides opportunity for public scrutiny and feedback on the IPO offering before finalization, enhancing transparency in the SME IPO process.
