## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 04, 2025. Total issuance comprises 4500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, amounting to Rs. 225 crores. The Commercial Paper carries a CRISIL A1+ credit rating and matures on March 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nexus Select Trust
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 4500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,615 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 225 crores
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Scrip Code**: 730702
- **Scrip ID**: NST031225
- **ISIN**: INE0NDH14122
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Tenor**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NDH14122
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 03, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: March 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing that does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Commercial Paper is a short-term money market instrument typically accessed by institutional investors. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity traders. Only debt segment participants dealing in corporate debt securities need to be aware of the new listing for potential trading opportunities.
