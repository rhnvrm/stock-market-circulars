## Summary

ANNAPURNA FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED has announced a part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE515Q07640, Code: 976927) along with interest payment. The record date is set for December 7, 2025, with trading to commence at reduced face value from December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: AFPL-10.95%-22-07-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE515Q07640 (Code: 976927)
- Record date for eligibility: December 7, 2025
- Reduced face value: Rs. 79,166.65 per debenture
- Effective date of reduced face value trading: December 5, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-771/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action pursuant to debenture terms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value effective December 5, 2025
- Investors must hold debentures as of the record date (December 7, 2025) to be eligible for part redemption proceeds and interest payment
- Trading systems should reflect the updated face value of Rs. 79,166.65 per debenture from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular announcement date
- **December 5, 2025**: Effective date for trading at reduced face value (Settlement DR-771/2025-2026)
- **December 7, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

**Debt Instrument Holders**: Debenture holders on record date will receive part redemption proceeds, reducing their principal exposure. The face value reduction to Rs. 79,166.65 indicates partial capital return.

**Trading Impact**: From December 5, 2025, the debentures will trade with reduced face value, which may affect pricing calculations and yield computations.

**Liquidity**: Part redemption reduces outstanding debenture volume, potentially affecting secondary market liquidity for this instrument.

**Market Scope**: Impact is limited to holders of this specific ANNAPURNA FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED debenture series and does not affect broader market operations.
