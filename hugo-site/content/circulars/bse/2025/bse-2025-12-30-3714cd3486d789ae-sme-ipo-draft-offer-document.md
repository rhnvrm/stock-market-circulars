## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Taldar Hotels and Resorts Limited, filed on December 29, 2025, containing all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the Stock Exchange website for a minimum period of 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Taldar Hotels and Resorts Limited on December 29, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx#

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing as per existing ICDR Regulations.

- Public comments on the draft offer document must be submitted within 21 days from December 30, 2025
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The draft offer document must remain accessible on the exchange website for at least 21 days

- **December 30, 2025**: Notice issued and document made publicly available
- **January 20, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME IPO process. The impact is limited to:
