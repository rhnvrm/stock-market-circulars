## Summary

BSE has listed 5,84,000 new equity shares of Yuken India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522108) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from November 10, 2025. These shares were allotted at a premium of Rs. 1016/- (issue price Rs. 1026/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until May 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Yuken India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522108)
- **Number of Shares**: 5,84,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Type**: Preferential allotment to Promoters
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1026/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 1016/-)
- **Allotment Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: November 10, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE384C01016
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 13000001 to 13584000
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- All 5,84,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in expiry date
- Trading systems should reflect the lock-in status of these securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a preferential issue to promoters representing a capital raise for the company. The shares are locked-in for approximately 18 months from the trading start date, preventing any immediate market supply impact.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new shares and lock-in restrictions.

**Shareholding Impact**: Increases promoter holding in Yuken India Ltd. The preferential issue may indicate capital requirements for business expansion or operations.
