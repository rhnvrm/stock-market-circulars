## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHINING TOOLS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544607) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective November 28, 2025. This follows the company's SME IPO listing.

## Key Points

- Company: SHINING TOOLS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544607)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Friday, November 28, 2025
- Segment: SME platform
- Notice number: 20251114-4
- Continuation of previous notice: 20251113-51 dated November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment), which typically allows for more flexible trading mechanisms including intraday trading and delivery-based transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the segment change for their trading systems
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice date:** November 14, 2025
- **Effective date of segment transfer:** November 28, 2025
- **Previous related notice:** November 13, 2025 (Notice No. 20251113-51)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment increases liquidity potential and allows for more flexible trading mechanisms. This is a positive development for investors as it removes trading restrictions typically imposed on newly listed SME IPOs.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the segment change. The transition period provides adequate time for necessary adjustments before the effective date.
