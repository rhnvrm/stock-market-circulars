## Summary

BSE has admitted Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities) as a Trading Member of BSE Ltd. with trading rights in the Debt segment. The admission became effective on December 29, 2025, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New member: Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities)
- Member Number: 6974
- Trading Segment: Debt
- Membership Type: New Membership
- Trading rights are limited to BSE Ltd. only
- Admission subject to Exchange Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from existing trading members. This is an informational notice only. Members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Effective date of membership admission
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members of a new entrant in the Debt segment. The addition of one new trading member does not affect market operations, trading rules, or existing member obligations.
