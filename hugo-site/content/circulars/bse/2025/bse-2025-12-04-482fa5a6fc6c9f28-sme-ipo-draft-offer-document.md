## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED filed on December 03, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED on December 03, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from December 04, 2025
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard draft offer document filing process as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Public comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- Document to remain available on the website for minimum 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 04, 2025 (deadline: December 25, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains to a specific SME IPO filing. The draft offer document filing is a standard procedural step in the IPO process. Investors and stakeholders interested in VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED can review the document and provide feedback during the 21-day comment period. The actual market impact will depend on the IPO subscription and listing, which will occur after the final offer document is approved.
