## Summary

KEC International Ltd. has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) worth Rs. 75 crore (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the BSE Debt segment. The instruments were issued on private placement basis on December 8, 2025, with a maturity period of 88 days (redemption on March 6, 2026). The CPs carry a CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit rating and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crore (1,500 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,596.50 per unit (implied discount of ~1.68%)
- Scrip code: 730716, Scrip ID: KIL81225
- ISIN: INE389H14LR8
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LR8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing date**: December 8, 2025
- **Redemption date**: March 6, 2026
- **Tenure**: 88 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing for a reputed infrastructure company. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term investment option in a highly-rated corporate entity. The discount rate implies competitive yield for the 88-day tenure.

**Company Impact**: Enables KEC International to raise working capital through the commercial paper route at favorable rates, reflecting its strong credit profile in the infrastructure sector.
