## Summary

BSE has authorized trading in 68,06,08,792 new equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) with effect from December 29, 2025. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares of Rs. 1/- each that were originally issued on a rights basis. The new shares will be pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new shares being listed: 68,06,08,792 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares from rights issue
- Trading commencement date: December 29, 2025 (Monday)
- ISIN: INE483S01020
- Scrip Code: 539807
- Distinctive number ranges: 2789495347 to 3001101918 and 3001114078 to 3470116297
- Status: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification following corporate action completion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from December 29, 2025
- Members should update their systems to recognize the expanded share capital and new distinctive number ranges
- Normal trading rules and regulations apply to these shares

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 68.06 crore additional shares represents a significant increase in Infibeam Avenues' equity base. This conversion from partly paid to fully paid shares:

- Increases tradable float and potentially improves liquidity
- May cause temporary dilution impact on existing shareholders
- Brings additional paid-up capital to the company from rights issue subscribers completing payment
- No immediate price impact expected as this was anticipated corporate action from an existing rights issue
- Investors should monitor trading volumes and price movements post-listing on December 29, 2025
