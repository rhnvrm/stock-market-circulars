## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill 364TB211125 (Scrip Code: 804991, ISIN: IN002024Z321) effective November 19, 2025, as the instrument is set to mature on its redemption date. This is a routine suspension issued under notice DR-759/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- T-bill 364TB211125 with ISIN IN002024Z321 and Scrip Code 804991 will be suspended from trading
- Trading suspension effective from November 19, 2025
- Suspension due to maturity on redemption date
- Trading members advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified T-bill (Scrip Code 804991) from November 19, 2025 onwards
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and update their trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 18, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** November 21, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting a single Treasury Bill due to natural maturity. Such suspensions are standard practice in debt markets and do not indicate any market disruption.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members holding or dealing in this specific T-bill need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive. No broader operational changes required.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date through normal settlement procedures.
