## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the Market Maker for Davin Sons Retail Limited (Scrip Code: 544331). Aftertrade Broking Private Limited (Clearing Number 6669) will cease to act as Market Maker, and Rikhav Securities Limited (Clearing Number 3174) will assume this role effective December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Aftertrade Broking Private Limited (Clg. No. 6669) will no longer act as Market Maker for Davin Sons Retail Limited
- Rikhav Securities Limited (Clg. No. 3174) appointed as new Market Maker
- Change effective from December 26, 2025
- Company has entered into agreement with new Market Maker to comply with duties and obligations of Market Making
- Applicable to SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative change in Market Maker designation for a specific security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rikhav Securities Limited must comply with all duties and obligations of Market Making for Davin Sons Retail Limited
- Trading members should note the change in Market Maker for this scrip

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 26, 2025 - Rikhav Securities Limited begins acting as Market Maker

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine administrative change affecting only one security (Scrip Code: 544331). Market making continuity is maintained through the appointment of the new Market Maker.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members dealing with Davin Sons Retail Limited should be aware of the new Market Maker but no operational changes are required on their part.
