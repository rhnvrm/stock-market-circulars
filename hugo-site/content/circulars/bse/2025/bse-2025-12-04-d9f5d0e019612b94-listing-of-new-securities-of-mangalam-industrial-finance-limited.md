## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 46,34,86,428 new equity shares of Mangalam Industrial Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 537800) issued on a rights basis. The shares are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, December 5, 2025. These new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 46,34,86,428 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 537800
- ISIN: INE717C01025
- Distinctive Numbers: 961643501 to 1425129928
- Date of Allotment: December 1, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 5, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities in their systems. The shares are available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 5, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The rights issue represents a substantial increase in the share capital of Mangalam Industrial Finance Limited. With 46.35 crore new shares being listed, existing shareholders may experience dilution unless they participated in the rights issue.

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing notification with no operational changes required beyond updating trading systems with the new share count and distinctive numbers.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their holdings reflected from December 5, 2025. The pari-passu ranking ensures equal rights with existing shares.
