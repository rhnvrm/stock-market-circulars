## Summary

BSE has issued a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 779 dated December 18, 2025. The circular lists 87 securities from various companies that will be auctioned due to delivery defaults. The auction includes stocks ranging from small quantities of 1 share to large quantities such as 283,122 shares of Meesho Limited and 89,634 shares of Mangalam Industrial Finance.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 779
- Auction Date: December 18, 2025
- Total securities listed: 87 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 283,122 shares
- Largest quantity: Meesho Limited (544632) - 283,122 shares
- Second largest: Mangalam Industrial Finance (537800) - 89,634 shares
- Other significant quantities include Reliance Power (6,200 shares), Ampvolts Limited (6,670 shares), and Aditya Birla (4,919 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine market operations circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members holding short positions in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement of delivery obligations through auction mechanism
- Members must ensure sufficient securities or funds for auction participation

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: December 18, 2025 (Settlement No. 779)
- **Circular Issue Date**: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 87 different securities and their respective shareholders

**Operational Impact**: 
- Trading members with delivery obligations in these securities must participate in the auction
- Shareholders whose shares are being auctioned may face price volatility
- The auction mechanism ensures delivery completion for pending trades

**Affected Parties**:
- Trading members with short positions in listed securities
- Buyers awaiting delivery of shares
- Investors holding these securities may experience temporary price pressure
