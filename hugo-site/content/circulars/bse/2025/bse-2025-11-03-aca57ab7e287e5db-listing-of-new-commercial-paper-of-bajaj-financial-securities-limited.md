## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 3,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 150 crore, with a maturity period of 7 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Scrip Code**: 730472
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL311025
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DC5
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,99,392 per unit
- **Quantity**: 3,000 securities (Total: Rs. 150 crore)
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DC5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 3, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The 7-day tenure instrument is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and India Ratings, indicating highest credit quality and liquidity. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No impact on broader market operations or retail investors.
