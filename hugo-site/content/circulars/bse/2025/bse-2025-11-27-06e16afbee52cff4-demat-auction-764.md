## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 764 dated November 27, 2025. The document lists scrip codes, scrip names, and quantities of securities being auctioned in the demat segment. The auction includes 109 different securities from various companies across multiple sectors.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 764
- Settlement Date: November 27, 2025
- Total number of securities listed: 109 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 43,800 shares depending on the scrip
- Covers diverse sectors including energy, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, textiles, technology, and FMCG
- Notable large quantity auctions: Darshan Orna (43,800 shares), Excelsoft Te (10,000 shares), SVP GLOBAL T (13,215 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants involved in the specified securities must participate in the demat auction as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled for the listed securities and quantities
- Participants must comply with standard BSE demat auction settlement timelines and procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 27, 2025
- Settlement Number: 764

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market-wide impact. The demat auction settlement affects only specific market participants who have outstanding obligations in the listed securities. The quantities involved are relatively small for most scrips, suggesting limited liquidity impact. The auction mechanism helps ensure settlement discipline and completion of failed trades in the demat segment.
