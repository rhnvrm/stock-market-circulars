## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 22nd, 2025. This session is intended for trading members to test their trading platforms, including third-party systems and in-house developed systems using ETI APIs. The mock trading will simulate various market functionalities and exceptional conditions. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on November 22, 2025
- Trading members can test third-party platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Session will test various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- No new BOLT TWS release scheduled
- Mock trades will not create any actual obligations, margins, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or rights and liabilities
- Feedback requested from all participants by 5:00 pm on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 pm on November 22, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **November 22, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - Log-in: 11:15 am to 12:00 pm
  - Continuous Trading: 12:00 pm to 04:00 pm
  - Trade Modification: 04:00 pm to 04:10 pm
  - Feedback deadline: 5:00 pm

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading session with minimal market impact. It provides trading members an opportunity to test and familiarize themselves with their trading systems and exceptional market condition handling. No actual trading obligations or financial liabilities will arise from this session. The impact is limited to operational testing and system validation for Commodity Derivatives segment participants.
