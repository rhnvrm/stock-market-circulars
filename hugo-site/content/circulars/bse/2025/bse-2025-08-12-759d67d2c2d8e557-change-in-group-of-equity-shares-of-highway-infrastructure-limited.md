## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of Highway Infrastructure Limited's equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group), effective August 28, 2025. This change follows a previous exchange notice dated August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Highway Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 544477)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (T Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective date: Thursday, August 28, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be shifted from T Group (Trade for Trade segment) to B Group under Rolling segment, changing the trading mechanism for the stock.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this change and may contact the designated BSE official for further clarification on trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: August 12, 2025
- Effective date: August 28, 2025
- Previous related notice: August 11, 2025 (Notice no. 20250811-30)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from T Group to B Group will improve liquidity for Highway Infrastructure Limited shares by moving from a trade-for-trade basis to normal rolling settlement, potentially increasing trading volume and reducing settlement restrictions.
