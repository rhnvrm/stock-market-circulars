## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to specific indices. Orkla India Limited (ticker 544595) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective November 10, 2025, following its listing on November 06, 2025. Safecure Services Limited (ticker 544596) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective November 07, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on November 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- Orkla India Limited (544595) listed on BSE on November 06, 2025
- Orkla India Limited to be added to BSE IPO Index from market open on November 10, 2025
- Safecure Services Limited (544596) listed on BSE SME platform on November 06, 2025
- Safecure Services Limited to be added to BSE SME IPO Index from market open on November 07, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References previous notices 20251104-47 and 20251104-60

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolio managers tracking these indices should adjust their holdings accordingly to maintain benchmark alignment.

## Important Dates

- **November 06, 2025**: Listing date for both Orkla India Limited and Safecure Services Limited
- **November 07, 2025**: Safecure Services Limited added to BSE SME IPO Index (market open)
- **November 10, 2025**: Orkla India Limited added to BSE IPO Index (market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. Routine index additions following IPO listings have minimal market-wide impact.

**Affected Parties**:
- Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate BSE IPO Index and BSE SME IPO Index will need to purchase the respective securities
- Portfolio managers benchmarked against these indices may adjust positions
- The two newly listed companies may see modest additional demand from passive index funds

**Operational Impact**: Standard index rebalancing procedures apply. Corporate action files have been updated to reflect these changes.
