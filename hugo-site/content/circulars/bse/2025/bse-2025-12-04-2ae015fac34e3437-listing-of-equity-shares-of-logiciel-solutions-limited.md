BSE has announced that equity shares of Logiciel Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544625, symbol LOGICIEL, and ISIN number INE1BA901016. The specific date of listing and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.
