## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on private placement basis effective December 12, 2025. The issue comprises 5,000 securities of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 250 crore. The Commercial Paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on March 12, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

- Total issue size: Rs. 250 crore (5,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Face value per unit: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730756
- Scrip ID: GIL121225
- ISIN: INE047A14AR0
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE047A14AR0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all trades
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption date**: March 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides Grasim Industries with short-term working capital financing for 90 days. The strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate low credit risk. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade this instrument on BSE's debt segment. The listing expands Grasim's debt financing options and provides institutional investors with another rated short-term investment avenue.
