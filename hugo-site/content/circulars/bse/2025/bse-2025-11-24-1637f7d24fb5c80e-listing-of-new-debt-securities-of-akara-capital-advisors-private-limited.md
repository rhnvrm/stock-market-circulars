## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The securities comprise 40,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying a 12% annual interest rate with monthly payments, maturing on May 21, 2027. The securities are rated ICRA BBB (Stable) and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 40,000 units listed
- Scrip Code: 977309
- Scrip ID: 12ACAPL27B
- ISIN: INE08XP07423
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 12% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XP07423
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Clarifications can be sought from BSE debt department at contact numbers 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 24, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Redemption Date: May 21, 2027
- Notice Date: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities in the debt segment. The listing affects only debt market participants and investors specifically interested in Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited securities. The securities have a moderate credit rating (ICRA BBB Stable) and offer 12% annual interest with monthly payments over an approximately 18-month tenure. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
