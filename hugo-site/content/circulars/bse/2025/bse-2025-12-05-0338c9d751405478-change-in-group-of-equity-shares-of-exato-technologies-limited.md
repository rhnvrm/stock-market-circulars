## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544626), an SME IPO company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 19, 2025. This follows the previous notice dated December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544626
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251205-2
- Reference Notice: 20251204-53 dated December 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement basis. This represents a transition from restricted trading (where each trade must be settled individually) to normal market operations where trades are settled on a T+2 basis.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment change effective December 19, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain at 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- **Reference Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED shares. Moving from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) indicates the company has met BSE's criteria for normal trading operations. Investors will benefit from standard rolling settlement procedures rather than mandatory delivery-based trading. This is a positive development for the stock's market accessibility.
