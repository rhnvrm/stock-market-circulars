## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in nine debt securities effective December 5, 2025. The suspensions are due to redemption or delisting applications made by the issuing companies. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Nine debt securities from different issuers will be suspended from trading
- Effective date: December 5, 2025
- Reason: Delisting applications or redemptions initiated by companies/suspension in depository
- Reference: DR-771/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities from December 5, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this circular and implement the restrictions
- No trading activity should be executed in these securities post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 5, 2025 (trading suspension begins)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders of these specific debt securities and trading members who facilitate transactions in these instruments. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade them on the exchange from December 5, 2025. The impact is limited to debt market participants and specific security holders rather than the broader equity market. The suspensions are administrative in nature following company-initiated delisting applications or redemptions.
