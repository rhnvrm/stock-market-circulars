## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective December 19, 2025. These government securities will be listed in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS with varying maturity periods of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days. Trading members should note that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to their respective maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills to be listed effective December 19, 2025
- All T-Bills listed in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category
- Market lot size is 1 for all three securities
- Trading restrictions apply two working days before maturity date
- Contact numbers provided: 2272 8352/5753/8597 for clarifications

## New Treasury Bills Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenor |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| 805157 | IN002025X380 | 91TB190326 | March 19, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805158 | IN002025Y388 | 182TB18626 | June 18, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805159 | IN002025Z385 | 364T171226 | December 17, 2026 | 364 days |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing announcement for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must be aware of the new T-Bill listings effective December 19, 2025
- Trading members must note that securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip codes, ISINs, and symbols

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**:
  - 91TB190326: March 19, 2026 (last trading day: March 17, 2026)
  - 182TB18626: June 18, 2026 (last trading day: June 16, 2026)
  - 364T171226: December 17, 2026 (last trading day: December 15, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative circular for listing government-backed Treasury Bills. The listing expands the debt instrument options available on BSE for institutional and retail investors seeking short-term government securities. Trading members need only update their reference systems with the new security details. No operational changes or new compliance burden imposed.
