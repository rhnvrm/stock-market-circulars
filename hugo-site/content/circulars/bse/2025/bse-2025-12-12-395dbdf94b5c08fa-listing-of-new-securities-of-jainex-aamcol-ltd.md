## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 7,48,169 new equity shares of Jainex Aamcol Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505212) issued on a rights basis. These securities will be available for trading on the Exchange from Monday, December 15, 2025. The shares were allotted on December 10, 2025, at a face value of Rs. 10/- each with a premium of Rs. 110/- per share.

## Key Points

- 7,48,169 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 110/- per share
- Issue type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE280F01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 1510001 to 2258169
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Scrip Code: 505212
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to take note of the listing of these new securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 15, 2025 (Monday)
- **Circular Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The rights issue increases the equity base of Jainex Aamcol Ltd. by 7,48,169 shares. The new shares having pari-passu rights means they carry the same voting and dividend rights as existing shares. The issue price of Rs. 120/- (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 110 premium) represents the subscription price paid by existing shareholders exercising their rights. Impact is limited to shareholders and investors of Jainex Aamcol Ltd.
