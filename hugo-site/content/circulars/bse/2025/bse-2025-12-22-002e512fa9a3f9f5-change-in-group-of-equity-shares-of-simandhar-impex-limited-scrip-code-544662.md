## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Simandhar Impex Limited (Scrip Code 544662) will be moved to the 'B' group for trading purposes. This change will be effective from Tuesday, January 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Simandhar Impex Limited
- Scrip Code: 544662
- New Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Tuesday, January 06, 2026
- Notice Number: 20251222-14
- Notice Date: December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Simandhar Impex Limited will be reclassified to trade under the 'B' group. The 'B' group typically represents companies with specific trading characteristics or market capitalization criteria as per BSE group classification norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the group change for the scrip
- Trading systems and risk management parameters should be updated to reflect the new group classification
- Investors should be aware of the trading characteristics associated with 'B' group stocks

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The change to 'B' group classification may affect:
- Trading volumes and liquidity patterns
- Margin requirements for the stock
- Investor perception and participation
- Settlement and clearing processes specific to B group securities

Market participants should ensure their systems and operations are updated before the effective date to accommodate this group change.
