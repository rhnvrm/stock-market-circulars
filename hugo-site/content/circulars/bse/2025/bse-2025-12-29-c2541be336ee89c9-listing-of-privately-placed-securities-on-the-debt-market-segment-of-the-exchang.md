## Summary

BSE notified trading members about the listing of commercial paper issued on private placement basis by six companies on the BSE Debt Segment effective December 29, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial paper totaling 10,300 units (Rs. 515 crore)
- 360 ONE Prime Limited: 2,000 units at 7.00% p.a., maturing March 6, 2026
- UGRO Capital Limited: 300 units at 9.00% p.a., maturing June 25, 2026
- DSP Finance Private Limited: 2,000 units at 7.30% p.a., maturing June 25, 2026
- CNH Industrial Capital (India) Private Limited: 2,000 units at 6.45% p.a., maturing March 27, 2026
- SBICAP Securities Limited: 2,000 units at 6.67% p.a., maturing March 27, 2026
- Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd: Details partially provided in notice
- All securities allotted on December 26, 2025
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- All securities carry A1+ credit ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, or IND rating agencies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing debt market framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Members requiring clarification may contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 26, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 29, 2025
- **Redemption Dates:**
  - March 6, 2026: 360 ONE Prime Limited
  - March 27, 2026: CNH Industrial Capital, SBICAP Securities
  - June 25, 2026: UGRO Capital, DSP Finance

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for debt segment listings. The commercial paper listings provide short-term funding options for the issuing companies and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Interest rates range from 6.45% to 9.00% reflecting credit profiles and tenures.
