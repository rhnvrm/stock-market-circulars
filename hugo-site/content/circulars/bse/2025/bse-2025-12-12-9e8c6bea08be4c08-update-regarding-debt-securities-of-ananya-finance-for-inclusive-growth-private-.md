## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited. Following up on notice 20250620-29 dated June 20, 2025, the exchange has announced that specific debt securities will be suspended from trading effective December 17, 2025 due to ongoing restructuring of the company.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976510 with ISIN INE774L08022 will be suspended for trading
- Suspension effective from Wednesday, December 17, 2025
- Suspension is due to restructuring of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited
- This notice is in continuation of earlier notice 20250620-29 dated June 20, 2025
- Trading members can contact BSE officials for further details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action based on existing regulations governing debt securities during corporate restructuring events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the suspension and cease trading in the affected scrip code from December 17, 2025
- Trading Members should inform their clients holding these debt securities about the trading suspension
- For queries or clarifications, Trading Members should contact:
  - Ms. Pooja Trivedi at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Previous Related Notice**: June 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20250620-29)

## Impact Assessment

**Bondholders**: High impact - holders of these debt securities will not be able to trade them on the exchange from December 17, 2025, potentially affecting liquidity and exit options during the restructuring period.

**Trading Members**: Moderate to high impact - must update their systems to prevent trading in the suspended scrip and inform affected clients.

**Market Operations**: The suspension relates to specific debt securities of one issuer undergoing restructuring, indicating potential financial distress that may affect recovery prospects for bondholders.

- Company: Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited
