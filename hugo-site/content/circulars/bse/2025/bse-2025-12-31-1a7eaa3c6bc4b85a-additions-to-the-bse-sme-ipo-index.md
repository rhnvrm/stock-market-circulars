## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of four newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective January 1, 2026. The companies - Nanta Tech Limited, Admach Systems Limited, Bai-Kakaji Polymers Limited, and Apollo Techno Industries Limited - were listed on BSE on December 31, 2025 and will be included in the index at the open of trading on January 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four SME companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- All four stocks listed on BSE on December 31, 2025
- Index inclusion effective at market open on January 1, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- **January 1, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action following the listing of new SME companies. The addition of these four stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index will have minimal direct market impact but provides index tracking opportunities for investors focused on newly listed SME stocks. Index funds or ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to rebalance to include these securities.
