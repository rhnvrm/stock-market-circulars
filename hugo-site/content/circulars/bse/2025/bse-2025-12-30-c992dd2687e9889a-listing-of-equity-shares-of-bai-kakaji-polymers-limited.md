## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The scrip has been assigned code 544670, symbol BAIKAKAJI, and ISIN number INE1IJQ01026. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544670
- Symbol: BAIKAKAJI
- ISIN: INE1IJQ01026
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement on BSE's SME platform. The impact is limited to the addition of a new tradeable security once the listing becomes effective. No immediate market-wide implications. Investors and traders interested in SME stocks should await the separate notice containing the actual listing date and additional security details before trading becomes available.
