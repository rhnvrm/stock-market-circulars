BSE has admitted Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities) as a new Trading Member with trading rights in the Debt segment. The admission was effective December 29, 2025, and is subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New member name: Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities)
- Member number: 6974
- Trading segment: Debt
- Membership type: New Membership
- Effective date: December 29, 2025
- Trading rights limited to BSE Ltd. only

No regulatory changes. This is a standard membership admission notice.

No action required from existing trading members. This is an informational notice only.

- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date

Minimal impact. This is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members about the addition of one new member to the debt segment. No changes to market operations, trading rules, or compliance requirements for existing members.
