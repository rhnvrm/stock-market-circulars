## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of AVANSE FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED (ISIN: INE087P07378, Code: 975052) effective November 13, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: AVANSE FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture Series: AFSL-9.35%-28-11-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE087P07378
- BSE Code: 975052
- Record Date: November 13, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: November 13, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-755/2025-2026
- Coupon Rate: 9.35%

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from November 13, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in these debentures on or after the no-dealings date
- Members must inform clients holding these debentures about the redemption and suspension

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Circular issued by BSE
- **November 13, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 13, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 28, 2025**: Expected maturity date (based on debenture series name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects a single private placement debenture series with limited secondary market trading activity. The suspension is a routine corporate action related to maturity and redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the terms. No trading opportunity exists from November 13, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard suspension procedure for debt securities approaching maturity. Trading members need to update their blocked securities lists and inform affected clients.
