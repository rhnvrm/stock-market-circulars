## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited on its Debt segment effective November 10, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per debenture, issue price of Rs. 10,044, and carry an interest rate of 11.40% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 50,000 debentures listed
- **Scrip Code**: 977241
- **Scrip ID**: 1140KRFL27
- **Detail Name**: KRFL-11.40%-24-10-27-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE0NES07287 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,044 per debenture
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: IND BBB+/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 11.40% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from November 24, 2025 to October 24, 2027
- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 24, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not available
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07287
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 24, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 24, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (24th of each month)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a routine listing of privately placed debt securities of a single non-banking financial company. The securities are available for trading in the debt segment for interested investors. The BBB+ credit rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate capacity to meet financial commitments.
