## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice regarding the part redemption of debentures of Kinara Capital Private Limited (scrip code: 973862, ISIN: INE200W07415). This notice updates the earlier communication dated September 08, 2025, with a corrected reduced face value. The debentures will trade at the revised reduced face value of Rs. 654,893 per debenture effective December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Kinara Capital Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 973862
- ISIN: INE200W07415 (KCPL-13.74%-25-3-26-PVT)
- Revised Reduced Face Value: Rs. 654,893 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 02, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-768/2025-2026
- Supersedes: Exchange Notice No. 20250908-44 dated September 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revision to the previously communicated part redemption details. The actual reduced face value has been updated from the amount specified in the September 2025 notice to Rs. 654,893 per debenture.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised reduced face value for trading purposes
- All trades in these debentures from December 02, 2025 onwards must reflect the updated face value of Rs. 654,893 per debenture
- Trading members are required to update their systems and records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: December 02, 2025
- **Original Notice Date**: September 08, 2025 (Notice No. 20250908-44)

## Impact Assessment

This revised notice primarily impacts debenture holders and trading members dealing with Kinara Capital debentures. The correction to the reduced face value requires system updates and adjustments to trading parameters. The impact is limited to the specific debt security (scrip code 973862) and does not affect broader market operations. Trading members must ensure accurate pricing and valuation based on the corrected face value to maintain compliance and proper settlement of trades.
