## Summary

BANDHAN MUTUAL FUND will launch BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND as a New Fund Offering (NFO) with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from November 10, 2025, to November 24, 2025. The scheme offers 6 plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) under the Equity category. Tentative allotment date is November 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 10-24, 2025
- 6 scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes classified as EQUITY category
- Tentative allotment date: November 28, 2025
- Extended trading till midnight on November 24, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 28, 2025
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds are received by 9:00 AM on T+1 day, otherwise transactions will be cancelled with CC N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 28, 2025 for allotment
- For switches, funds must be received under NFO scheme before allotment date; funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: November 10, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: November 24, 2025 (3:00 PM for standard transactions)
- **Extended Period**: Till midnight on November 24, 2025 (lumpsum only)
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline**: Before 3:00 PM on November 24, 2025
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on November 28, 2025
