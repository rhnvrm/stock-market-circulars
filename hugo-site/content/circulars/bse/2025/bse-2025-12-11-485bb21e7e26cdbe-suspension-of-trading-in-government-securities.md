## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Government Treasury Bill (T-bill) with ISIN IN001225C076 (Scrip Code: 800448) that is scheduled to mature on December 16, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill effective from December 12, 2025. This is a standard procedure for securities approaching their redemption date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with ISIN IN001225C076 (Scrip Code: 800448) will mature on December 16, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from December 12, 2025
- Trading members must not execute any trades in this security from the suspension date
- Notice reference: DR-776/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the specified T-bill (ISIN: IN001225C076, Scrip Code: 800448) from December 12, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to block trades in this security
- Members holding positions in this T-bill should prepare for redemption on December 16, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 16, 2025**: T-bill redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine suspension affecting only one T-bill security approaching maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members holding or trading this specific T-bill need to ensure no trades are executed from December 12, 2025. Holders will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date (December 16, 2025) through standard settlement processes.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to current holders of this specific T-bill who will receive redemption value at maturity. No impact on broader debt market operations or other government securities.
