## Summary

BSE has listed four series of Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 17, 2025. The total issuance amounts to Rs. 750 crores (15,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with varying maturity periods. All securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Four Commercial Paper series listed totaling 15,000 units (Rs. 750 crores)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit across all series
- Allotment date: December 16, 2025 for all series
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025 (all series)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - December 24, 2025 (Series 2)
  - January 6, 2026 (Series 3)
  - March 16, 2026 (Series 1)
  - March 17, 2026 (Series 4)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors access to short-term debt instruments issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The staggered maturity dates from December 2025 to March 2026 provide liquidity options for investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is expected.
