## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in four government securities effective December 16, 2025, due to their scheduled maturity on December 18, 2025 (Redemption Date). The suspended securities include three Treasury Bills (91-day, 182-day, and 364-day) and one Sovereign Gold Bond from 2017 Series. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Four government securities will be suspended from trading on December 16, 2025
- Suspension applies to three T-Bills and one Sovereign Gold Bond
- Redemption date scheduled for December 18, 2025
- Notice number: 20251215-8, DR-778/2025-2026
- Applies to Debt segment only

## Securities Details

### Suspended Securities

1. **182T181225** - Scrip Code: 805080, ISIN: IN002025Y123 (182-day T-Bill)
2. **364TB181225** - Scrip Code: 805003, ISIN: IN002024Z362 (364-day T-Bill)
3. **91TB181225** - Scrip Code: 805118, ISIN: IN002025X257 (91-day T-Bill)
4. **Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series** - Scrip Code: 800280, ISIN: IN0020170141

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the listed securities from December 16, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in these securities
- No new positions or transactions should be executed in these instruments

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational measure affecting debt market participants holding or trading these specific government securities. The suspension ensures orderly market operations ahead of the redemption date. Impact is limited to holders of these four securities, with no broader market implications. Trading members should adjust their portfolios and inform clients holding these instruments about the impending maturity and redemption process.
