## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 13 securities effective July 29, 2025. The changes range from 2% to 20% price bands, indicating varying levels of surveillance measures applied to these stocks.

## Key Points

- 13 securities have revised price bands effective July 29, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 20%
- Glen Industries Ltd has the highest price band at 20%
- Three securities (Omnitex Industries, Shristi Infrastructure, Yarn Syndicate) have the lowest band at 2%
- Most securities are assigned 5% or 10% price bands

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions represent enhanced surveillance measures to control excessive volatility in these securities. Tighter bands indicate higher regulatory concern.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with revised price bands
- All trades must fall within the new band limits
- Clarifications can be sought from bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The price band changes will limit intraday volatility for these securities, potentially reducing speculative trading activity. Investors and traders should adjust their strategies accordingly for these stocks.
