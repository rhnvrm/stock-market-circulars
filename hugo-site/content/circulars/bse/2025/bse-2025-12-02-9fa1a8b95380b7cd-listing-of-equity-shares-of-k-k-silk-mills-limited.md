## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of K K SILK MILLS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544624, symbol KKSILK, and ISIN number INE0EOJ01014. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- K K SILK MILLS LIMITED to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544624
- Symbol: KKSILK
- ISIN Number: INE0EOJ01014
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing of K K SILK MILLS LIMITED on the SME segment expands the available trading universe for investors interested in small and medium enterprises. Trading members and investors should await the subsequent notice detailing the actual listing date and complete security specifications before any trading activity can commence.
