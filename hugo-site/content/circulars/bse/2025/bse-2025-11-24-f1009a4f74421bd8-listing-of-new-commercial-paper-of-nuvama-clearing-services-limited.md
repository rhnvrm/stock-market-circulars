## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED on private placement basis on its Debt segment effective November 24, 2025. The instrument comprises 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a maturity period of approximately one month.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crore aggregate)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,97,066 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730626
- Scrip ID: NCSL211125
- ISIN: INE525L14158
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: November 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 22, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Trading Specifications

- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only under ISIN INE525L14158
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Segment: BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no material impact on broader market operations. The Commercial Paper provides NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED with working capital financing for approximately one month. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading is restricted to institutional and high-net-worth participants given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination requirement.
