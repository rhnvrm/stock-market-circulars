## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-bill with ISIN IN1520220147 (747GUJSDL25, Scrip Code 810899) effective October 21, 2025, due to its upcoming maturity on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the effective date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 747GUJSDL25 (ISIN: IN1520220147, Scrip Code: 810899) will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from October 21, 2025
- Trading members must cease dealing in this instrument
- Notice reference: DR-740/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill from October 21, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the trading restriction

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 21, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: As specified in instrument terms

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity. Affects only holders and potential traders of this specific T-bill (747GUJSDL25). No broader market implications. Trading members need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive and update client communications accordingly.
