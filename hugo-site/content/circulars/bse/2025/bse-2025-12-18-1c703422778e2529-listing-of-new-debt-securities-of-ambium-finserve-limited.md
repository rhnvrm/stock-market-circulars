## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective December 18, 2025. The securities carry an 11.16% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and will be redeemed on August 26, 2027. The securities are rated CARE BBB- and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 securities listed
- Scrip Code: 977341
- Scrip ID: 1116AFL27A
- ISIN Number: INE0RU307213 (Further Listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000.00 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,045.86
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-
- Interest Rate: 11.16% per annum (monthly payments)
- Allotment Date: December 17, 2025
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0RU307213
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from January 26, 2026 to August 26, 2027
- **First Interest Payment**: January 26, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: August 26, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing in the private placement segment with limited market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Affects only trading members dealing in BSE Debt segment and investors in AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to specific debt security holders - The securities offer 11.16% annual returns with monthly interest payments over approximately 20-month tenure. CARE BBB- rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of financial obligations.

**Put/Call Option**: None available on these securities.
