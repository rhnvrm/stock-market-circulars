## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 20, 2025. The issue comprises 4000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings. The instruments will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with a maturity date of February 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730602
- Scrip ID: ABRE201125
- ISIN: INE055A14KK7
- Credit Ratings: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: SBI
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE055A14KK7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments with no broader market implications.

**Segment Impact**: Limited to BSE Debt segment participants and investors seeking short-term investment opportunities in CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+ rated commercial paper.
