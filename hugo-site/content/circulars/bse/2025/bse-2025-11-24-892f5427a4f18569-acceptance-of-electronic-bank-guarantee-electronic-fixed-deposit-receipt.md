## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has announced that AU Small Finance Bank will provide Electronic Bank Guarantee (E-BG) and Electronic Fixed Deposit Receipt (E-FDR) facilities to BSE members effective November 24, 2025. This is a continuation of circular no. 20231113-18 dated November 13, 2023, which initially introduced these electronic facilities.

## Key Points

- AU Small Finance Bank added as E-BG and E-FDR facility provider
- Facility becomes operational from November 24, 2025
- Electronic submission of bank guarantees and fixed deposit receipts streamlines collateral management
- Members can contact Relationship Managers or Collateral Management team for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular expands the list of banks participating in the existing electronic collateral submission framework established in November 2023.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members may opt to utilize AU Small Finance Bank for submitting electronic bank guarantees and fixed deposit receipts as collateral. This is a facilitative measure providing additional banking options.

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: E-BG and E-FDR facility through AU Small Finance Bank becomes available

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Positive but limited. Members gain an additional banking partner for electronic collateral submission, improving operational flexibility. The electronic process reduces paperwork and processing time compared to physical document submission.

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative enhancement to existing collateral management infrastructure with no direct impact on trading activities or market operations.
