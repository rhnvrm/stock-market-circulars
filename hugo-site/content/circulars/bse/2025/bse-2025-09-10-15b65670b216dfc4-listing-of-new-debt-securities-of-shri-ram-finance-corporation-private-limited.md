## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Shri Ram Finance Corporation Private Limited (SRFCPL) worth ₹30 crores on its Debt segment. The securities carry a 13.25% interest rate and have a 5-year tenure ending December 9, 2030.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: ₹30 crores
- Interest rate: 13.25% per annum paid monthly
- Credit rating: ACUITE A Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code:** 977091
- **Scrip ID:** 1325SRFC30
- **ISIN:** INE08E808041
- **Issue Price:** ₹1,00,000
- **Allotment Date:** September 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 9, 2030

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** September 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly from October 9, 2025 to December 9, 2030
- **Maturity Date:** December 9, 2030

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms
- No put/call options available on these securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide institutional investors access to a medium-term corporate bond with attractive yield in the current interest rate environment.
