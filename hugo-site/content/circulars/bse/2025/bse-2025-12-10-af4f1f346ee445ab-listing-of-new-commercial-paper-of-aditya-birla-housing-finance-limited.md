## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 10, 2025. The issuance comprises 6,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings. The securities were allotted on December 9, 2025 and will mature on March 10, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 6,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,308.50)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 3,000 million (Rs. 300 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730734
- Scrip ID: ABHF091225
- Detail Name: ABHFL-10-3-26-CP
- ISIN: INE831R14FP4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FP4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price quotations
- Securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment with immediate effect

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025 (effective immediately)
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026 (91-day tenure)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Provides debt market investors with a highly-rated (A1+) short-term investment option from Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited with a 91-day maturity period. The implicit yield is approximately 6% per annum based on the discount from face value.

**Liquidity**: Limited secondary market liquidity expected as commercial papers are typically held to maturity by institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on the BSE Debt platform. For clarifications, members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers.
