## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 5000 Commercial Papers issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. Each instrument has a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 with credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+. The securities will mature on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 5000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,643 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730727
- **Scrip ID**: NMIL101225
- **ISIN**: INE870H14WK9
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WK9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions must follow the tick size of 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) with approximately 91 days maturity. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors and debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. The securities are rated A1+ by both ICRA and India Ratings, indicating highest credit quality for short-term debt instruments.
