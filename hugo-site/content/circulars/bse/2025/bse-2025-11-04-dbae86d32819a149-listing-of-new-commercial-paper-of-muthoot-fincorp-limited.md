## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 5,500 new Commercial Papers issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 4, 2025. The instruments are valued at Rs. 5,00,000 each with a total issue size of Rs. 275 crore, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,63,757 per unit
- Total Quantity: 5,500 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730479
- Scrip ID: MFL031125
- ISIN: INE549K14CC2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K14CC2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 25, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 10.5 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The listing provides Muthoot Fincorp with access to short-term funding and offers institutional investors an additional A1+ rated investment option.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these commercial papers. The standard lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs makes these instruments suitable for institutional participants. For clarifications, trading members can contact the BSE debt department.

**Issuer Profile**: Muthoot Fincorp Limited is part of the Muthoot Group, engaged in financial services including gold loans and other lending activities.
