## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 12,805 new equity shares of Prime Fresh Limited (Scrip Code: 540404) on the exchange. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 164/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with premium of Rs. 154/-). The shares will commence trading from Monday, November 17, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until May 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Prime Fresh Limited (Scrip Code: 540404)
- **Number of Shares**: 12,805 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Type**: Preferential allotment to promoters pursuant to warrant conversion
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 164/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 154/- premium)
- **Allotment Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE442V01012
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 13645768 to 13658572
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities in their systems
- The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until the lock-in expiry date
- All 12,805 shares (Dist. Nos. 13645768 to 13658572) are locked-in until May 31, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 31, 2027
- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The preferential allotment involves a small number of shares (12,805) to promoters, representing negligible dilution to existing shareholders. The shares are locked-in for approximately 18 months, preventing immediate selling pressure.

**Operational Impact**: Routine listing activity with no operational changes for trading members. The shares will trade alongside existing equity shares with identical rights and benefits.

**Investor Impact**: Low impact for existing shareholders. The preferential allotment strengthens promoter holding and the funds raised (approximately Rs. 20.97 lakhs) may be utilized for business operations.
