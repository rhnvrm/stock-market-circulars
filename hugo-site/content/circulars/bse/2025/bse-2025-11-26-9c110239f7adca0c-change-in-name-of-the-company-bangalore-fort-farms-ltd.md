## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Bangalore Fort Farms Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Grameva Limited effective November 4, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Bangalore Fort Farms Limited** to **Grameva Limited**
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L51101WB1966PLC226442 remains unchanged
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Certified by Prerna Panwar, Assistant Registrar of Companies
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Originally incorporated as Bangalore Fort Farms Limited in 1966

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard name change process under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the **old name along with the new name for a period of two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- Update company name on all official documents, letterheads, and communications
- All stakeholders should verify the latest company status on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 4, 2025
- **Display Requirement**: Old name must be displayed alongside new name until November 4, 2027 (2-year period)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change with no effect on business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update its branding, stationery, and all official communications to reflect the new name.

**Stakeholder Impact**: None on rights and liabilities. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of all legal and financial obligations.
