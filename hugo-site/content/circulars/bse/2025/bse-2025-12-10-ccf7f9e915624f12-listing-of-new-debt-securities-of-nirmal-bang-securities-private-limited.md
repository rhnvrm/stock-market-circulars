## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nirmal Bang Securities Private Limited on private placement basis, effective December 10, 2025. The securities carry a 10.25% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on June 9, 2027. Total quantity of 5,000 securities with face value of Rs. 100,000 each have been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977356
- Scrip ID: 1025NBS27A
- ISIN Number: INE681R08141
- Quantity: 5,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Interest Rate: 10.25% per annum
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from March 9, 2026 to June 9, 2027
- Maturity Date: June 9, 2027
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A/STABLE
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE681R08141
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 10, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **March 9, 2026**: First interest payment date (quarterly thereafter)
- **June 9, 2027**: Maturity and redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds Rs. 50 crore worth of debt securities to the BSE Debt segment. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants interested in CRISIL A rated securities
- Investors seeking quarterly income instruments with 10.25% returns
- Nirmal Bang Securities' funding activities

The securities are privately placed with no put/call options, indicating limited secondary market liquidity expectations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the dematerialised trading requirement and tick size specifications.
