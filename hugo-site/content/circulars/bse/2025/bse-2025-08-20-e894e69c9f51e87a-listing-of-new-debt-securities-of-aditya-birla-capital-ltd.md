BSE has listed new perpetual debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.4232% per annum and have a face value of ₹1 crore each.

- Quantity: 200 securities listed
- Face Value: ₹1,00,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.4232% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+, IND AA+
- Scrip Code: 977034
- ISIN: INE674K08042
- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025

- **Scrip Name**: ABCL-8.42%-PERPETUAL-PVT

- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Redemption Date**: December 31, 2999 (Perpetual)
- **Put/Call Options**: As per Information Memorandum

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The perpetual nature and high credit rating (AA+) indicate stable institutional investment. Trading members can access placement memorandum details on BSE's debt section for complete terms and conditions.
