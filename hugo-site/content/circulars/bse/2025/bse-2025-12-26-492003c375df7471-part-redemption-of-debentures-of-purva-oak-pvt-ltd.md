## Summary

BSE has informed trading members about the partial redemption of debentures issued by PURVA OAK PVT LTD. The company has reduced the face value per debenture following the partial redemption. Trading members are required to note that the debentures will trade at the new reduced face value starting December 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PURVA OAK PVT LTD
- Scrip Code: POPL-12.50%-RESET RATE-19-11-2
- ISIN: INE0SRE07015 (975840)
- Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 57,575.76 per Debenture
- Effective Date: December 29, 2025
- Reference: DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding partial redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the reduced face value
- Debentures will be traded with the reduced face value of Rs. 57,575.76 per debenture from December 29, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate trading and settlement

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading at Reduced Face Value**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This partial redemption affects holders of PURVA OAK PVT LTD debentures (ISIN: INE0SRE07015). The reduction in face value from the original amount to Rs. 57,575.76 per debenture reflects the partial capital return to debenture holders. Trading members and investors holding these securities must account for the changed face value in their portfolios and trading activities from December 29, 2025 onwards. The impact is specific to this debenture series and does not affect broader market operations.
