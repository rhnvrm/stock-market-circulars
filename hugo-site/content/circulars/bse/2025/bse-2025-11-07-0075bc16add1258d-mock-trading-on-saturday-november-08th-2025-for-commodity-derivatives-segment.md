## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. The mock trading will simulate various market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts. No actual obligations or liabilities will be created from this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release planned
- Trading members can test third-party platforms, in-house systems, and ETI API integrations
- Session will simulate exceptional market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- Mock trades will not create any rights, liabilities, margin obligations, or settlement obligations
- Members requested to provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the session day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine mock trading session for testing and familiarization purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback on the session by 5:00 PM to BSE Helpdesk or Tech Support
- For queries, members should contact their Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com), or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **November 08, 2025**: Mock Trading Session
  - Log-in: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
  - Continuous Trading: 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 04:00 PM to 04:10 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Provides an opportunity for trading members to test their systems and familiarize themselves with various trading functionalities and exceptional market conditions in a risk-free environment. The session helps ensure system readiness and operational efficiency without any financial obligations or settlement processes.
