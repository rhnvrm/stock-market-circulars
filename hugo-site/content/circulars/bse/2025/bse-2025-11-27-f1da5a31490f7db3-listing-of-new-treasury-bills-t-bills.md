## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective November 28, 2025. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day instruments with varying maturity dates in 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills will be listed on BSE starting November 28, 2025
- All securities will be part of G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size is 1 for all three instruments
- Securities will be unavailable for trading two working days prior to maturity date
- Trading members should contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for clarifications

## New Instruments Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenor |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| 805148 | IN002025X356 | 91TB260226 | February 26, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805149 | IN002025Y354 | 182TB28526 | May 28, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805150 | IN002025Z351 | 364T261126 | November 26, 2026 | 364 days |

## Trading Restrictions

- Trading will be suspended two working days before the maturity/redemption date
- Suspension applies on Maturity Date (T) minus two Trading Days (excluding Bank Holidays)
- This ensures orderly settlement of maturing securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 28, 2025
- **91-Day T-Bill Maturity**: February 26, 2026
- **182-Day T-Bill Maturity**: May 28, 2026
- **364-Day T-Bill Maturity**: November 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing that provides additional government securities for debt market participants. The impact on overall market operations is minimal, as this is standard procedure for T-Bill listings. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should note the new scrip codes and ISINs for trading purposes.
