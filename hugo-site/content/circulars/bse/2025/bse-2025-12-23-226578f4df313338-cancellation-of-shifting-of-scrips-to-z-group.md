## Summary

BSE has cancelled the shift of Viji Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 537820) to 'Z' group that was scheduled for December 26, 2025. This notice supersedes the earlier Exchange Notice No.20251215-21 dated December 15, 2025, specifically for this company. All other provisions of the earlier notice remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Viji Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 537820) will NOT be shifted to 'Z' group
- This cancellation is effective from December 26, 2025
- Issued in continuation to Exchange Notice No.20251215-21 dated December 15, 2025
- All other provisions of the original notice remain valid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a cancellation of a previously announced trading status change.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should:
- Note that Viji Finance Ltd will continue trading in its current group
- Update their systems to reflect the cancellation of the 'Z' group shift
- Direct queries to bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 26, 2025 (cancellation takes effect)
- **Reference Notice**: Exchange Notice No.20251215-21 dated December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a cancellation of a previously announced shift. Viji Finance Ltd will maintain its current trading status, avoiding the restrictions and negative implications associated with 'Z' group classification. This is positive for the company and its shareholders as 'Z' group typically indicates companies with compliance or regulatory concerns.
