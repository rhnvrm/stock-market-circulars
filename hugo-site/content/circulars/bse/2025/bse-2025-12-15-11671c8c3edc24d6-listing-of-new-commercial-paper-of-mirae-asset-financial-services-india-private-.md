## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Papers issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing consists of 1500 securities of Rs.5,00,000/- each on the BSE Debt segment, effective December 15, 2025. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1500 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Issue Price: Rs.4,91,441.50/- per security
- Scrip Code: 730775
- Scrip ID: MAFS151225
- ISIN: INE0JRU14354
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- Trading Denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE0JRU14354
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: December 15, 2025
- Listing Date: December 15, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal impact on the broader market. The Commercial Paper is rated CRISIL A1+, indicating high credit quality. The listing is relevant only to institutional investors and debt market participants who trade in commercial papers. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. The 91-day maturity period is standard for commercial paper instruments.
