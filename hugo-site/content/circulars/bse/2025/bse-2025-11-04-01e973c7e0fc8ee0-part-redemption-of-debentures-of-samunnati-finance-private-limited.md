## Summary

BSE has notified that SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED has set the Record Date for payment of interest and part redemption of its non-convertible debentures. The debentures will trade with reduced face value effective November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE551U07332 (Code: 976049)
- Security: 12.50% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing September 27, 2026
- Record Date: November 12, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 41,666.62 per debenture (post-redemption)
- Effective Date: November 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-754/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value from the effective date
- Debenture holders on record date will receive interest payment and partial redemption proceeds
- Trading systems to be updated to reflect new face value from November 12, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: Record Date for interest payment and part redemption
- **November 12, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-754/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds and accrued interest. The reduced face value affects subsequent interest calculations and final redemption amount.

**Market Impact**: Limited impact confined to holders of this specific debenture series. Trading will continue with adjusted face value reflecting the partial redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading platforms and back-office systems need to update the face value for this ISIN to Rs. 41,666.62 per debenture from the effective date.
