## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for Infosys Limited's buyback through the Acquisition Window mechanism. The buyback bidding window will run from November 20-26, 2025, with obligation download on December 2, 2025 and final settlement on December 3, 2025. Settlement number 2025126 has been assigned for this transaction.

## Key Points

- Buyback bidding period: November 20-26, 2025
- Settlement Number: 2025126
- Obligation Download Date: December 2, 2025
- Settlement Date: December 3, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Both CDSL and NSDL depositories support the process
- Market Type "Buyback" to be used in depository systems
- Members not required to upload EPN file for early pay-in
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type and settlement number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements existing Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as referenced in Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must complete early pay-in of securities in depository system sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB Bidding window closure
- **Members/Custodians**: Must use Market Type "Buyback" and Settlement No. 2025126 in depository system
- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- **Members**: Should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- **Participants**: Early pay-in must be completed before prescribed timelines for BSE OTB platform bid acceptance and custodian confirmation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects Infosys Limited shareholders who wish to participate in the buyback offer through the stock exchange acquisition window mechanism.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Requires brokers, members, and custodians to process early pay-in of shares and follow specific settlement procedures. Proper coordination between depositories (CDSL/NSDL) and BSE OTB platform is essential.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders intending to tender shares in the Infosys buyback must work with their brokers to ensure timely early pay-in of demat shares before the bidding window closes on November 26, 2025.
