## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 3, 2025. The issue consists of 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with a tenor of 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Scrip Code: 730475
- Scrip ID: VCL311025
- ISIN: INE01HV14AD5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,88,669.50 per unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AD5
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: November 3, 2025
- Allotment Date: October 31, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 30, 2026
- Notice Date: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term debt investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment. The CARE A1+ rating indicates highest degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations. The listing expands available debt instruments on BSE platform but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
