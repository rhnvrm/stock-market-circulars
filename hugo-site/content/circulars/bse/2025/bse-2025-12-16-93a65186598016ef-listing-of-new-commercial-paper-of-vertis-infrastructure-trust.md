## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Vertis Infrastructure Trust on private placement basis effective December 16, 2025. The listing comprises 14,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling Rs.7,000 crore in value. The instruments carry a CRISIL A1+ credit rating and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Vertis Infrastructure Trust
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 14,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,84,807.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs.7,000 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730784
- **Scrip ID**: VIT151225
- **ISIN**: INE0KXY14055
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0KXY14055
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 5, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 172 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of short-term debt instruments with no impact on equity markets or broad market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these Commercial Papers on BSE platform. The listing adds to the debt market inventory but represents standard market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in private placement debt markets, given the high denomination of Rs.5 lakh per unit.
