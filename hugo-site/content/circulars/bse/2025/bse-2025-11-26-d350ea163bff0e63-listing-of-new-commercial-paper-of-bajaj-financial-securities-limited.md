## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment. The securities are admitted to trading with effect from November 26, 2025. The issue comprises 2,700 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2,700 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,906
- Scrip Code: 730649
- Scrip ID: BFSL251125
- ISIN: INE01C314DM4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE01C314DM4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 26, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 26, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 25, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for commercial paper issued on private placement basis. The impact is limited to debt market participants dealing with short-term debt instruments. The securities have a 3-month tenure and carry strong credit ratings (A1+), indicating low credit risk. No significant market-wide or operational impact is expected.
