## Summary

Fedbank Financial Services Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The securities are commercial papers issued on a private placement basis with ISIN INE007N14EI8, carrying a CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit rating. A total of 1,200 securities with face value of Rs. 500,000 each have been admitted for trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Security Name: FFSL-23-2-26-CP (Scrip Code: 730639, Scrip ID: FFSL241125)
- Quantity Listed: 1,200 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 500,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 500,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Interest Rate: 6.97% per annum (cumulative)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN Number: INE007N14EI8
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE Debt segment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EI8
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Date**: February 23, 2026 (cumulative)
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it represents a routine private placement of commercial paper by Fedbank Financial Services Limited. The securities are available for trading on BSE's Debt segment for institutional and qualified investors. The 90-day tenor (November 24, 2025 to February 23, 2026) and strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate short-term, high-quality debt instruments. Trading members dealing in debt securities can access these instruments for their clients' short-term investment needs. No put/call options are available on these securities.
