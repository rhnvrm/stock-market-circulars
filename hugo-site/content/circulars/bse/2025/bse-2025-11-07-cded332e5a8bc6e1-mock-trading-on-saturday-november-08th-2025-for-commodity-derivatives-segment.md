## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 08, 2025. This session is designed to allow trading members using third-party platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The session will simulate various functionalities including exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on November 08, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Session allows testing of trading applications and exceptional market conditions
- All trades from mock session will not create any actual obligations or liabilities
- Feedback requested from all members by 5:00 PM on the session day
- Trading members using third-party platforms or ETI APIs can participate

## Trading Session Schedule

- **Log-in:** 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading:** 12:00 PM to 04:00 PM
- **Trade Modification:** 04:00 PM to 04:10 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 5:00 PM on the mock trading day
- Members should contact their Relationship Manager or BSE support teams for any queries

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** November 08, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Feedback Deadline:** 5:00 PM on November 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine testing session with no market impact. The mock trading session:
- Creates no margin obligations
- Creates no pay-in or pay-out obligations
- Does not establish any rights or liabilities
- Is purely for testing, familiarization, and system validation purposes
- Allows members to test various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
