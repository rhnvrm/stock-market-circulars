## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on a private placement basis. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 5, 2025, with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 10,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each listed
- Scrip Code: 730710, Scrip ID: GIL051225
- ISIN Number: INE047A14AP4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,710.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities are traded under ISIN Number INE047A14AP4

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 5, 2025
- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) by Grasim Industries Ltd. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The 90-day maturity period is standard for commercial paper. This listing provides additional liquidity options in the debt market but has no significant operational or trading impact on the broader market.
