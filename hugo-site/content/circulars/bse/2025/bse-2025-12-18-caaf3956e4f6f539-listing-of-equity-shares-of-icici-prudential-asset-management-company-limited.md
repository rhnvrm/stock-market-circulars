## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ICICI PRUDENTIAL ASSET MANAGEMENT COMPANY LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544658, symbol ICICIAMC, and ISIN number INE346A01027. The exact date of listing and complete details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: ICICI PRUDENTIAL ASSET MANAGEMENT COMPANY LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544658
- Trading Symbol: ICICIAMC
- ISIN Number: INE346A01027
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the actual listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This announcement has medium impact for the market. ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company is a significant player in the asset management industry.
