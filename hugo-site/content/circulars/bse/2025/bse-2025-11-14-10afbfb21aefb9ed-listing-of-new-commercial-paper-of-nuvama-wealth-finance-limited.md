## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis effective November 14, 2025. The issuance consists of 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each, allotted on November 13, 2025, with a redemption date of February 12, 2026. ICICI Bank serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- **Securities Type**: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- **Quantity**: 3000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,90,702/- per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730559
- **Scrip ID**: NVF131125
- **ISIN**: INE918K14CT3
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CT3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper listing affects only institutional investors and trading members dealing in debt securities. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
