## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number INE142E01022 to BEMCO HYDRAULICS LTD (Scrip Code: 522650) following the subdivision of equity shares from Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share. The new ISIN will be effective for trades from August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: BEMCO HYDRAULICS LTD (Scrip Code: 522650)
- New ISIN Number: INE142E01022
- Share subdivision: From Rs.10 to Re.1 per equity share
- Effective date: August 22, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- DR Reference: 700/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

New ISIN number assignment following corporate action of share subdivision as per exchange regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN number
- All trades from August 22, 2025 onwards must use the new ISIN INE142E01022
- System updates required before the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Ex-date for share subdivision and new ISIN effectiveness
- **August 19, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 13, 2025**: Original notice date (20250813-66)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to BEMCO HYDRAULICS LTD shareholders and trading systems. Requires technical updates to trading and settlement systems to accommodate the new ISIN and adjusted share denomination. No broader market impact expected.
