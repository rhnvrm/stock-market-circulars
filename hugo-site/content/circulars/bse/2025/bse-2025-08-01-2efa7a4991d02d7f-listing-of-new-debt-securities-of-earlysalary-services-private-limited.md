## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of EARLYSALARY SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective from August 1, 2025. The securities carry a 10.70% interest rate and mature on March 5, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,500 securities
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 100,000 each
- Interest rate: 10.70% per annum
- Credit rating: CARE A-/Stable
- Market lot: 1 security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- Scrip Code: 976976
- Scrip ID: 1070ESPL27
- ISIN: INE01YL07383
- Security Name: ESPL-10.70%-5-3-27-PVT
- Date of allotment: July 30, 2025

## Important Dates

- Interest payment dates: Monthly from September 5, 2025 to March 5, 2027
- Redemption date: March 5, 2027
- Trading commencement: August 1, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The private placement adds to BSE's debt segment offerings but represents standard corporate financing activity.
