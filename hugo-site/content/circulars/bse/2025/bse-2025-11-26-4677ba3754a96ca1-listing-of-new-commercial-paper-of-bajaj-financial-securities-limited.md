## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities comprise 2700 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings with a 91-day tenure from allotment to redemption.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2700 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,906 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730649
- Scrip ID: BFSL251125
- ISIN: INE01C314DM4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE01C314DM4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 24, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no unusual features. The instrument has strong credit ratings (A1+) and follows normal BSE debt trading protocols. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No effect on equity markets or retail investors.
