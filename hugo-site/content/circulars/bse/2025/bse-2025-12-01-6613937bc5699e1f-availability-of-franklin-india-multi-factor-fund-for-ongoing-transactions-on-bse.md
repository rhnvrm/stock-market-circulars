## Summary

BSE has notified that FRANKLIN INDIA MULTI-FACTOR FUND will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. This reopening includes facility for Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP). The fund offers six plan variants covering both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from December 02, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY
- Full SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251107-55 indicating prior suspension or restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE Members and intermediaries should update their systems to enable transactions in these schemes from December 02, 2025
- Ensure all six ISIN codes are activated for trading on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Make SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available to investors for these schemes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 - Schemes available for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund, as they can now make fresh investments, redemptions, and set up systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: BSE Members and distributors need to ensure their systems are updated to process transactions in these schemes from the effective date. No significant operational burden expected as this is a standard scheme activation procedure.
