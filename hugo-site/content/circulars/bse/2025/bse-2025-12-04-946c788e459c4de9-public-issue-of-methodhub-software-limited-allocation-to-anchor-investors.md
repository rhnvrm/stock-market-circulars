## Summary

Methodhub Software Limited has finalized the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors as part of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The Board of Directors, in consultation with the Book Running Lead Manager, allocated 15,03,600 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each to 17 anchor investors at an allocation price of Rs. 194 per share (including share premium of Rs. 184), raising a total of Rs. 29,16,98,400 (approximately Rs. 29.17 crore). No mutual funds, life insurance companies, or pension funds participated in the anchor portion.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 15,03,600 shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs. 194 per equity share
- Face value: Rs. 10 per equity share
- Share premium: Rs. 184 per equity share
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: Rs. 29,16,98,400 (Rs. 29.17 crore)
- Number of anchor investors: 17
- Largest allocations: SANSHI FUND – I and MINT FOCUSED GROWTH FUND PCC- CELL 1 (each received 2,58,000 shares, 17.16%)
- Board meeting date: December 04, 2025
- No participation from mutual funds, life insurance companies, or pension funds
- Company CIN: U74900KA2016PLC085743

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular is a disclosure of anchor investor allocation as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- If the final offer price discovered through book building is higher than the anchor investor allocation price of Rs. 194, anchor investors must pay the difference by the pay-in date specified in the revised CAN (Confirmation of Allocation Note)
- Disclosure requirements as per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 are being fulfilled through this announcement

## Important Dates

- Board meeting for anchor allocation: December 04, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation date: December 04, 2025
- Pay-in date for price difference (if applicable): As specified in revised CAN

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The successful allocation of the anchor investor portion demonstrates investor interest in Methodhub Software Limited's IPO. The anchor book raised Rs. 29.17 crore from 17 investors, providing price discovery reference for the main IPO.

**Investor Composition:** The anchor investor base consists entirely of alternative investment funds and corporate investors, with no institutional investors like mutual funds, insurance companies, or pension funds participating. The largest allocations went to SANSHI FUND – I and MINT FOCUSED GROWTH FUND PCC- CELL 1 at 17.16% each.

**Price Benchmarking:** The anchor allocation price of Rs. 194 per share will serve as a reference point for the public offer price band. Anchor investors have a one-day price discovery advantage and lock-in requirements as per regulations.

**Company Profile:** Methodhub Software Limited (formerly Methodhub Software Private Limited) is a Bengaluru-based company incorporated in 2016, operating in the software services sector (CIN classification U74900KA).
