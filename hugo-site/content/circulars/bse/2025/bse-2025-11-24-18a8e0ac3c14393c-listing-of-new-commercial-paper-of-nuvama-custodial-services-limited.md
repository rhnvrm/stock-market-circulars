## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Custodial Services Limited on private placement basis. The instruments, totaling 2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (aggregate Rs. 100 crore), are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 24, 2025. The papers are rated ICRA A1+ and have a tenure of approximately 11 months.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Custodial Services Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 2000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,64,705.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730635
- **Scrip ID**: NCS211125
- **ISIN**: INE0H3Q14014
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0H3Q14014
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 21, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: October 21, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 11 months (334 days)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of commercial paper in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who may participate in secondary market trading of these instruments. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Nuvama Custodial Services Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants seeking highly-rated short-term instruments.

**Investor Relevance**: Relevant only for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals operating in the debt market segment interested in ICRA A1+ rated commercial papers with approximately 11-month tenure.
