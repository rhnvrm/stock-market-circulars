## Summary

BSE has notified that certain scrips will not be available for trading in the T+0 (same-day) rolling settlement cycle on Monday, December 22, 2025, and Friday, December 26, 2025. This affects 8 scrips across both dates, including major banking and financial stocks. Regular T+1 settlement trading will remain unaffected.

## Key Points

- 4 scrips restricted from T+0 trading on December 22, 2025
- 4 scrips restricted from T+0 trading on December 26, 2025
- Restriction applies only to T+0 rolling settlement cycle
- Regular T+1 settlement trading continues as normal
- Affects major stocks including InterGlobe Aviation, Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles, IndusInd Bank, and public sector banks

| Scrip Code | Symbol | Security Name |
|------------|--------|---------------|
| 139448 | INDIGO# | InterGlobe Aviation Limited |
| 100570 | TMPV# | Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited |
| 143220 | MAXHEALTH# | Max Healthcare Institute Limited |
| 132187 | INDUSINDBK# | IndusInd Bank Limited |

## Affected Scrips - December 26, 2025

| Scrip Code | Symbol | Security Name |
|------------|--------|---------------|
| 132483 | CANBK# | Canara Bank |
| 140611 | AUBANK# | AU Small Finance Bank Limited |
| 132461 | PNB# | Punjab National Bank |
| 132477 | UNIONBANK# | Union Bank of India |

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a temporary operational restriction for specific trading dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note these restrictions and adjust their trading systems accordingly
- Intraday traders should be informed that these scrips cannot be traded in T+0 settlement on the specified dates
- Trading in these scrips will continue to be available in regular T+1 settlement cycle

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025 (Monday)**: 4 scrips unavailable for T+0 trading
- **December 26, 2025 (Friday)**: 4 scrips unavailable for T+0 trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects intraday traders who utilize T+0 settlement for quick turnaround trades in these high-volume stocks.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and inform clients about the temporary restriction. The restriction is limited to T+0 settlement only, so regular T+1 trading remains available.

**Affected Parties**: Intraday traders, algorithmic trading systems utilizing T+0 settlement, and trading members offering same-day settlement services for these specific scrips.
