## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF, an open-ended scheme managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited. The units will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the 'B' Group effective December 18, 2025. The ETF replicates/tracks the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544654
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: SMALLADD
- ISIN: INF740KA1WX3
- Number of Units: 3,487,401 units
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 16.40 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Investment Manager: DSP Asset Managers Private Limited
- Registered Office: Mafatlal Centre, 10th Floor, Nariman Point, Mumbai 400 021

## Market Makers

The following entities have been designated as market makers for this ETF:

- East India Securities LTD
- KANJALOCHANA FINSERVE PRIVATE LIMITED
- KOTAK SECURITIES LTD
- PARWATI CAPITAL MARKET PVT LTD
- Share India Securities Ltd

- Achintya Securities Pvt Ltd
- CHOICE EQUITY BROKING PVT LTD
- IRAGE BROKING SERVICES LLP
- FYERS SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- MOTILAL OSWAL FIN SER LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Units of the Mutual Fund will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Trades effected in this scrip will be in unit market lot (i.e., one unit)
- Trading members must ensure compliance with dematerialization requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **December 17, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 18, 2025**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine addition of a new ETF product to BSE's trading platform. The ETF provides investors with exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index through a passively managed fund. The presence of 11 market makers should ensure adequate liquidity for trading.
