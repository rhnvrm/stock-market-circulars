## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07783, Code: 976231) along with interest payment. The record date is set for November 27, 2025, and trading will commence with reduced face value from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD (Code: KSL-10.30%-12-6-26-PVT)
- ISIN: INE07HK07783 (976231)
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 33,333 per debenture
- Effective Date: November 27, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from November 27, 2025
- All trading members are required to update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 33,333 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: November 27, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-765/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption impacts debenture holders of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD. The reduction in face value from the original amount to Rs. 33,333 per debenture reflects partial capital return to investors. Trading members must ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new face value to avoid settlement discrepancies. The simultaneous interest payment provides income to debenture holders while reducing their principal exposure.
