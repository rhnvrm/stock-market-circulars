## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 24,36,51,025 new equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 539216) effective December 31, 2025. These shares arose from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of Rs. 1/- each that were issued on a rights basis. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new shares listed: 24,36,51,025 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each
- Company: Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited
- Scrip Code: 539216
- ISIN: INE653S01028
- Conversion type: Partly paid shares to fully paid shares issued on rights basis
- Distinctive Numbers: Two ranges - 15347011 to 216115111 (200768101 shares) and 216156062 to 444460075 (228304014 shares)
- Trading Members are permitted to trade in these securities
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from December 31, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine corporate action involving conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares. The increase in tradeable shares may marginally affect liquidity, but since shares are pari-passu with existing equity, no material change in shareholder rights or company fundamentals is anticipated.
