## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 16,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 9% per annum
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- ISIN: INE918K07PY9
- Scrip Code: 977024
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Issue Details**: Private placement basis
- **Date of Allotment**: August 12, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: June 11, 2027
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 11, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE918K07PY9
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website
- For clarifications, contact debt department at specified phone numbers

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors.
