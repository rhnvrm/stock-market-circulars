## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited on the BSE Debt segment effective December 16, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a quantity of 13,525 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit, and carry an interest rate of 11.4% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976915
- Scrip ID: 114KF27
- Detail Name: KFL-11.40%-15-1-27-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE0NES07238 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 13,525 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 99,906.30
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,906.30
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.4% per annum (monthly payments)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07238
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: December 16, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from January 15, 2026 to January 15, 2027
- Date of Redemption: January 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing affects only investors and trading members dealing in debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited. The BBB+ credit rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate capacity to meet financial commitments. The 13-month maturity period and monthly interest payment structure provides short-term investment opportunities in the debt market.
