## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd issued on private placement basis, effective October 27, 2025. The securities comprise 36,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.03% per annum and maturing on May 4, 2035. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976676, Scrip ID: 803ABCL35
- ISIN: INE674K08018 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 36,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,04,088.10
- Date of Allotment: October 24, 2025
- Interest Rate: 8.03% per annum (payable annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE674K08018
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from May 6, 2026 to May 4, 2035
- **Redemption Date**: May 4, 2035
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands Aditya Birla Capital's debt fundraising through private placement and provides institutional investors with a AAA-rated investment option. The securities carry no put or call options and follow standard annual interest payment structure. No impact on equity markets or trading members' operations beyond standard debt segment procedures.
