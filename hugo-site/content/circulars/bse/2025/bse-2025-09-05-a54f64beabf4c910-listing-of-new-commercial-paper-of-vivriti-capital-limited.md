## Summary

BSE notifies trading members about the listing and admission to trading of new Commercial Paper securities issued by VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from September 5, 2025. The Federal Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 500 units (Total value: Rs. 250 crore)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730162
- Scrip ID: VCL4925
- Detail Name: VCL-02-01-26-CP
- ISIN: INE01HV14946
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,85,135 per unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading format: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14946
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be maintained

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 2, 2026
- Tenure: 120 days (approximately 4 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to Vivriti Capital Limited and offers institutional investors a debt instrument with CRISIL A1+ rating. Trading members can contact BSE's debt department at specified numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications. No impact on broader market operations or trading systems.
