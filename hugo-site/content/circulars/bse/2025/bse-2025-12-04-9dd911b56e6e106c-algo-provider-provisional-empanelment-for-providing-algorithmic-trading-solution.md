## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an approved vendor to provide algorithmic trading solutions to exchange trading members. This empanelment is effective immediately and expands the list of approved algo providers available to trading members.

## Key Points

- AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Office located at Cello Triumph, B-wing, Goregaon East, Mumbai-400063
- Contact number: +91 9013468987
- Complete list of algo providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update adding a new vendor to the approved algo provider list.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational, notifying members of an additional approved vendor option for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative notification that expands vendor options for trading members interested in algorithmic trading solutions.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing operations. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally empanelled vendor to consider.

**Reference**: The complete list of approved algo providers is maintained at https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
