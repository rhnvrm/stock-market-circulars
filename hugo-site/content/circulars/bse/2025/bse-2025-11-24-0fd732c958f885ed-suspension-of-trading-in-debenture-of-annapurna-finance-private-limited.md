## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of Annapurna Finance Private Limited effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debenture ISIN: INE515Q07228 (Code: 958467)
- Debenture series: AFPL-12.50%-19-12-25-PVT with 12.50% interest rate
- Record date set for December 4, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 4, 2025 onwards
- Circular reference: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard market practice notification for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All pending orders for the affected debenture should be cancelled

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to investors and traders holding or interested in this specific debenture of Annapurna Finance Private Limited. No broader market impact expected.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the suspended debenture. Investors holding these debentures will be eligible for redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading liquidity available from the suspension date.
