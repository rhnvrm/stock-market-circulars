## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 157/2025-2026 dated November 18, 2025, announces book closure and record dates for various corporate actions across 17 securities. Actions include NCD partial and full redemptions with interest payments, commercial paper redemptions, and capital reduction under resolution plan.

## Key Points

- 17 securities listed with upcoming record dates between November 20-December 23, 2025
- Multiple NCD (Non-Convertible Debenture) redemptions and interest payments scheduled
- Commercial paper redemptions announced for BHEL, Electrosteel Castings, and Redington
- LORDS MARK INDIA LIMITED has record date for capital reduction under resolution plan
- All securities will have trading flag set to 1 on respective record dates

- FRIEZA 06 2025 - Part redemption + interest (RD: Dec 01)

## Impact Assessment

This bulletin has targeted impact on holders of the listed securities. Investors must ensure their holdings are settled by the respective record dates to receive entitlements for interest payments, redemptions, or participate in corporate actions. The trading flag setting indicates ex-date adjustments will apply. Most significant for debt instrument holders expecting redemption proceeds and interest payments in the coming weeks.
