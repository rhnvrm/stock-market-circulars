## Summary

Mahindra Lifespace Developers Limited has issued new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis, which is now listed and admitted to trading on BSE debt segment effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1500 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,95,205 per unit
- Credit rating: IND A1+
- ISIN: INE813A14342
- Scrip code: 729997

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- YES Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Trading effective date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Mahindra Lifespace Developers Limited with 88-day maturity period.
