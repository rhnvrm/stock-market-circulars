## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 706 dated September 3, 2025, listing securities available for auction along with their respective quantities and scrip codes.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 706 effective September 3, 2025
- Multiple securities listed with specific auction quantities
- Includes major stocks like Nestle India (40 shares), Colgate-Palmolive (1000 shares), State Bank (1 share)
- Notable quantities include One Mobikwik (11,418 shares), UTI Mutual Fund (5,000 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, FMCG, technology, and mutual funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine operational settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the securities and quantities available for auction
- Standard demat auction settlement procedures apply
- Participants must comply with BSE auction settlement guidelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: September 3, 2025
- Settlement No: 706

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities as auction settlements typically involve delivery failures or other operational issues. Market participants holding positions in listed securities should monitor for potential price movements during auction process.
