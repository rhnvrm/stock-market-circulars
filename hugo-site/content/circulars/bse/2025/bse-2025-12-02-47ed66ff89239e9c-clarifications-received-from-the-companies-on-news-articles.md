## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department issued a notice seeking clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) regarding news articles published in Economic Times. This is part of BSE's routine process to verify the accuracy of news appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels to ensure members and investors are properly informed. The company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd regarding news articles
- Notice issued on December 2, 2025 (Notice No: 20251202-57)
- News article reference: Economic Times (https://economictimes.indiatimes.com)
- Reply from the company is pending
- This is a standard surveillance procedure by BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is part of existing surveillance procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Vodafone Idea Ltd is required to provide clarification/response to BSE regarding the news articles in question. Companies are expected to respond promptly to such surveillance queries to ensure market transparency.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Reply Status**: Awaited (as of notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine surveillance matter with no immediate trading implications.

**Investor Impact**: Informational notice to keep investors aware that BSE is actively monitoring news coverage and seeking verification from companies.

**Company Impact**: Vodafone Idea Ltd needs to respond to the surveillance query, but this is a standard procedural matter unless the underlying news contains material information.
