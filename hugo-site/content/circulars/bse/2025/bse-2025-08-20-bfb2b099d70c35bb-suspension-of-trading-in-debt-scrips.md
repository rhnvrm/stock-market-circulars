## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in ILFS 8.69% debentures (ISIN: INE871D07OI1, Scrip Code: 952658) effective August 21, 2025, due to maturity on August 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Single debt security affected: ILFS 8.69% debentures
- ISIN: INE871D07OI1
- Scrip Code: 952658
- Trading suspension effective from August 21, 2025
- Maturity date: August 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-699/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine administrative action for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified ILFS debentures from August 21, 2025
- All trading activities in this security must cease before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **August 24, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension for a single maturing debt security. Standard procedure for debt instruments approaching maturity to prevent trading in securities that will soon cease to exist.
