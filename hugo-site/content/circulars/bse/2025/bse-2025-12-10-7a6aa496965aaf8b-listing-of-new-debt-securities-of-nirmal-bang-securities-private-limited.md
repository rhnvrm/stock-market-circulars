## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nirmal Bang Securities Private Limited on its Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The securities consist of 5,000 units of 10.25% Non-Convertible Debentures issued on private placement basis with a total face value of Rs. 50 crore. The debentures carry a CRISIL A/STABLE rating and have a maturity period of approximately 18 months.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nirmal Bang Securities Private Limited
- **Quantity**: 5,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crore
- **Interest Rate**: 10.25% per annum
- **Scrip Code**: 977356
- **ISIN**: INE681R08141
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A/STABLE
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Date of Allotment**: December 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 9, 2027
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE681R08141
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for detailed terms
- Put/Call options are as per the Disclosure Documents

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from March 9, 2026 to June 9, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: June 9, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on private placement basis for institutional investors and high net-worth individuals. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading members interested in debt securities can access additional liquidity through this instrument. Contact BSE Debt Department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
