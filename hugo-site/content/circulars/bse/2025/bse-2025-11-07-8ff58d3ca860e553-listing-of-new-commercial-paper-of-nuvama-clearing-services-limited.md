## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by NUVAMA CLEARING SERVICES LIMITED on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective November 7, 2025. The issuance consists of 1500 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+. The instruments were allotted on November 6, 2025 and will mature on November 21, 2025, representing a 15-day tenure.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 1500 Commercial Paper units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,98,617 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730515
- **Scrip ID**: NVSL061125
- **ISIN**: INE525L14141
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE525L14141
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (022-22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term Commercial Paper listing with a 15-day maturity period. The issuance is on private placement basis in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members can trade these instruments following normal BSE debt segment protocols with specified denominations and tick size.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and qualified investors participating in the private placement. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate low credit risk for the short investment horizon.
