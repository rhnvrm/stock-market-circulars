## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 154/2025-2026 dated November 13, 2025, announcing book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple securities. The bulletin covers dividend payments, interest payments on non-convertible debentures, redemptions of NCDs and commercial papers, and a rights issue.

## Key Points

- **Banco Products (India) Ltd.**: Interim dividend of Rs. 7.00 per share (350%), record date November 19, 2025
- **Page Industries Ltd.**: Second interim dividend of Rs. 125.00 per share (1250%), record date November 19, 2025, payment date December 12, 2025
- **NBCC (India) Limited**: Second interim dividend of Rs. 0.21 per share (21%), record date November 19, 2025
- **Indowind Energy Ltd.**: Rights issue of equity shares, record date November 19, 2025
- Multiple NCD interest payments and redemptions scheduled across various dates in November-January 2026
- Commercial paper redemption for Axis Finance Limited on December 11, 2025
