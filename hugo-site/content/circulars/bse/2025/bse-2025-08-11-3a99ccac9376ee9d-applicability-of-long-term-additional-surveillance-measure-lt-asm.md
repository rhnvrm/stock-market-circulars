## Summary

BSE announces updates to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 12, 2025. The circular details securities entering LT-ASM, those moving between ASM stages, and securities exiting the framework.

## Key Points

- One security (Decorous Investment & Trading Co Ltd) newly shortlisted in LT-ASM Framework
- Aryan Share and Stock Brokers Ltd moved to higher ASM Stage II
- Gayatri BioOrganics Ltd moving out of LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- Consolidated list shows 18+ securities across various ASM stages
- Changes effective from August 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

- Updated LT-ASM Framework classifications
- Stage progression for existing securities under surveillance
- Integration with other surveillance frameworks (ESM, GSM, IBC, Trade for Trade)

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to enhanced surveillance measures for listed securities
- Trading restrictions apply based on ASM stage classification
- Compliance with specific surveillance parameters for each stage

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: All LT-ASM framework changes become effective

## Impact Assessment

- Limited impact on overall market as changes affect specific securities under surveillance
- Enhanced monitoring and trading restrictions for affected securities
- Investors in listed securities should review trading conditions and surveillance measures
