## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED filed on December 29, 2025 on its SME website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED on December 29, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations requirements for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted within 21 days from December 30, 2025 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available on exchange website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED
- **December 30, 2025**: Notice date and start of 21-day public comment period
- **January 20, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document.

**Investor Impact**: Low impact, relevant only to potential investors interested in the upcoming IPO of TALDAR HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED. The 21-day comment period allows public scrutiny before the final offer document is issued.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes for existing market participants. Standard IPO process documentation requirement.
