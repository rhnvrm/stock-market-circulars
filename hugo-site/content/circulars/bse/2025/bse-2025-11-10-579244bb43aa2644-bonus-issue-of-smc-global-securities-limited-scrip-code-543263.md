## Summary

SMC Global Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 543263, ISIN: INE103C01036) has provided details of its bonus share allotment. The company will allot 10,47,00,000 (Ten Crore and Forty Seven Lakhs) equity shares with distinctive numbers ranging from 113134451 to 217834450. The bonus shares will be credited to the depository system and made available for trading.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares allotted: 10,47,00,000 equity shares
- Distinctive number range: 113134451 to 217834450
- ISIN: INE103C01036
- Scrip Code: 543263
- Company confirms full responsibility for compliance with timelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- SMC Global Securities Limited must ensure bonus shares are credited in the depository system
- Company must ensure availability of shares for trading by the effective listing date
- Company has confirmed full responsibility to comply with the mentioned commitments and timelines

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 14, 2025 (Friday)
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 17, 2025 (Monday)
- **Effective Listing Date**: November 18, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The bonus issue will significantly increase the outstanding share count of SMC Global Securities Limited by 10.47 crore shares, which will dilute the share price proportionally while maintaining shareholder equity. This will improve liquidity and make the stock more accessible to retail investors.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders as of the record date (November 14, 2025) will receive bonus shares in proportion to their holdings. The bonus issue is typically positive for shareholder sentiment and indicates company's confidence in future prospects.

**Trading Impact**: Trading in the bonus shares will commence from November 18, 2025. Increased float may lead to enhanced trading volumes and improved market depth.
