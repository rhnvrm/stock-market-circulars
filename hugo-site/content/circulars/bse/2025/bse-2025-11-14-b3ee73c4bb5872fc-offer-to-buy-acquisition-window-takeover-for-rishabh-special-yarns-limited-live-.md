## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for shares of RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED. The offer will be live from Friday, November 14, 2025 to Thursday, November 27, 2025. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate in this OTB process through BSE's iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED
- **Security Symbol**: RISHYRN
- **ISIN**: INE351D01013
- **Scrip Code**: 514177
- **Offer Size**: Up to 9,25,782 Equity Shares
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 13.00 per share
- **Opening Date**: Friday, November 14, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- **Note**: Scrip is only listed at the Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India

## Session Timings

### Bid Offering Period
- **Duration**: November 14-27, 2025
- **Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

### Give-up / Take-up
- **Duration**: November 14-26, 2025
- **Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

### Give-up (Last Day)
- **Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

### Take-up (Last Day)
- **Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Timing**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM

1. Trading Members must ensure UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
2. Trading Members can bid only for Investors having shares in demat form
3. Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
4. Refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
