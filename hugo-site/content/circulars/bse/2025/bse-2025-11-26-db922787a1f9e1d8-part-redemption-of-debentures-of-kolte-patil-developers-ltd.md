## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd has fixed the record date for part redemption of four series of non-convertible debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values effective November 28, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-766/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Four series of debentures of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd will undergo part redemption
- Record date for part redemption: November 28, 2025
- Trading with reduced face values will be effective from November 28, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-766/2025-2026
- All four debentures are privately placed non-convertible debentures

## Debenture Details

### KPDL-ZC-17-4-33-PVT (INE094I07049)
- Security Code: 974771
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 65,345 per debenture

### KPDL-21-12-33-PVT (INE094I07064)
- Security Code: 975276
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 63,705 per debenture

### KPDL-ZC-20-9-34-PVT (INE094I07072)
- Security Code: 976030
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 68,438 per debenture

### KPDL-15-10-35-PVT (INE094I07080)
- Security Code: 977231
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 99,580 per debenture

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values effective from November 28, 2025
- All trading in these debentures from the effective date onwards will be at the reduced face values
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face values

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: November 28, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-766/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption affects holders of four series of privately placed debentures of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd. The reduction in face values reflects the partial return of principal to debenture holders. Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new face values from the effective date. This is a routine corporate action in the debt segment with limited broader market impact.
