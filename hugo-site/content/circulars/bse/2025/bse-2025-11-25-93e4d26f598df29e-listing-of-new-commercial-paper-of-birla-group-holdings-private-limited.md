## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis, effective November 25, 2025. The issuance consists of 9,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 450 crores, maturing on February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Birla Group Holdings Private Limited
- **Quantity**: 9,000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 450 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730646
- **Scrip ID**: BGH251125
- **ISIN**: INE09OL14IE6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IE6
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issuance provides short-term funding to Birla Group Holdings and offers institutional investors a highly-rated investment option. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading restricted to BSE Debt segment with standard commercial paper trading rules applicable.
