## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Supreme Industries Ltd on private placement basis. The listing comprises 900 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 45 crore. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment and are available for trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 900 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730580
- **Scrip ID**: SIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE195A14CE1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under ISIN Number INE195A14CE1

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Supreme Industries Ltd with short-term funding at competitive rates (implied discount rate based on issue price). The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term debt instruments. Trading members can offer these securities to institutional and high net-worth clients seeking short-duration debt investments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
