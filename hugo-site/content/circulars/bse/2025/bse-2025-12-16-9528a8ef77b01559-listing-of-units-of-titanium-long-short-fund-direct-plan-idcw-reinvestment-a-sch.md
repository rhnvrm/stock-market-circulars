## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of Titanium Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment, a close-ended equity scheme under Tata Mutual Fund, effective December 17, 2025. The scheme invests in equity and equity-related instruments as well as debt and money market instruments, with limited short exposure through derivatives. The units will trade in dematerialized form only in B Group.

## Key Points

- **Scheme Name**: Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment
- **AMC**: Tata Asset Management Pvt Ltd
- **Number of Units**: 199,990 units
- **Scrip Code**: 544650
- **ISIN**: INF277K30054
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10.03 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: December 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these units are executed in dematerialized form
- Trades will be in unit market lot (one unit)
- Members must use the designated scrip ID (TSIFDIR) and abbreviated name (TSIFDIR) on the BOLT system

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 17, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Circular Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine mutual fund unit listing with a small initial issuance of 199,990 units.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members gain access to a new close-ended equity scheme with hybrid long-short strategy. The scheme's unique feature is limited short exposure in equity and debt through derivatives.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors access to a direct plan IDCW reinvestment option with lower expense ratios compared to regular plans.

**Registrar Details**: Computer Age Management Services (CAMS) - SEBI Reg: INR000002813
