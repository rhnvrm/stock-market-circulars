## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 2000 Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 27, 2025. The instruments carry a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and will be traded on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,64,425
- Scrip Code: 730662
- Scrip ID: MFL261125
- ISIN Number: INE522D14OY3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A+
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: AXIS BANK
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OY3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 27, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 27, 2025
- Date of Redemption: November 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term funding instrument for Manappuram Finance Limited with a one-year tenure. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors. The strong credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A+) indicate high creditworthiness of the issuer.
