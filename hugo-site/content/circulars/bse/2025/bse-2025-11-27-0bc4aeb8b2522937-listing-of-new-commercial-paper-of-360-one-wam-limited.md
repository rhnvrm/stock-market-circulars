## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Papers totaling Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000/- each listed
- Scrip Code: 730661, Scrip ID: OWL261125
- ISIN Number: INE466L14FJ5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,279/- per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market lot of 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FJ5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot is 1 unit with tick size of 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent for these instruments

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025 (deemed date)
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025 (effective date)
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment with no impact on equity markets. The 3-month maturity instrument is part of normal corporate treasury operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these securities on BSE Debt segment. The instruments provide short-term investment opportunities for debt market participants. Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
