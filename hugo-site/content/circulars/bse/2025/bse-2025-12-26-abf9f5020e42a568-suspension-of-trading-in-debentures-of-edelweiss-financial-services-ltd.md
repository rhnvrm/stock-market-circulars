## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. effective December 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of the debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to EFSL debenture with ISIN INE532F07BS0 (Code: 960418)
- Suspension effective from December 30, 2025
- Record date for redemption set as December 30, 2025
- Trading members explicitly instructed not to deal in these debentures
- Circular issued under reference DR-787/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified EFSL debentures (INE532F07BS0) from December 30, 2025
- Members must take note of the trading suspension to avoid inadvertent transactions
- No new positions or trades should be executed in the affected security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 30, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific EFSL debenture issue. This is a routine corporate action for debt instrument redemption. Investors holding these debentures as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds. The suspension ensures orderly settlement and prevents trading during the redemption process. No broader market impact expected as this affects only one specific debt security.
