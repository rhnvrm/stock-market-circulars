## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership by Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251) in the Equity Derivative and Currency derivative segments, effective December 15, 2025. This is an administrative change affecting the member's participation rights in these trading segments.

## Key Points

- Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251) has surrendered its trading membership
- Surrender applies to Equity Derivative and Currency derivative segments only
- Approval granted by BSE with immediate effect from December 15, 2025
- Trading members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a membership status update notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice about a specific member's status change.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 - Surrender of trading membership approved and effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender of a single trading member's participation in derivative segments does not affect overall market operations or other members' trading activities. This is a routine administrative process for membership management at the exchange level.
