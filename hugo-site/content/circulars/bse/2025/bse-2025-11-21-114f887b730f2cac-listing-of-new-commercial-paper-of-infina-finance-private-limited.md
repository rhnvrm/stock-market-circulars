## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 21, 2025. The instrument consists of 2000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, and maturing on May 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,84,005 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730614
- Scrip ID: IFPL201125
- ISIN: INE879F14MA6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MA6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 21, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: November 21, 2025
- Date of Redemption: May 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it involves private placement of commercial paper by Infina Finance Private Limited. The instrument is rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt. The listing provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors holding these commercial papers. The 168-day tenure (November 20, 2025 to May 7, 2026) is typical for commercial paper instruments. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the standard denomination requirements and dematerialized trading mandate.
