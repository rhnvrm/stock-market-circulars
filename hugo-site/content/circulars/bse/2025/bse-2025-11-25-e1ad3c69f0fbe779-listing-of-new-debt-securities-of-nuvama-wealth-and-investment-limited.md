## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis, effective November 25, 2025. The securities comprise 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 9.20% annual interest rate and maturing on December 20, 2027. The securities are rated CARE AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976911, Scrip ID: 920NWIL27
- ISIN: INE523L07AM2 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,03,596.16
- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.20% per annum (payable annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Trading: Dematerialized form only with tick size of 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AM2
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed information
- Contact debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Annually from July 13, 2026 to December 20, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** December 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with limited market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Relevant for debt market investors seeking AA-rated securities with 9.20% annual returns over approximately 2-year tenure.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard listing procedure for trading members dealing in debt securities on BSE platform.
