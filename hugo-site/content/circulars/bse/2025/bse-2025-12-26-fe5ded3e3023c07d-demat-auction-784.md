## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 784 dated December 26, 2025. The auction includes 105 scrips from various companies with specified quantities that will be auctioned to meet delivery obligations. The quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 19,824 shares depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 784
- Auction Date: December 26, 2025
- Total Scrips Listed: 105 companies
- Largest quantity: EXCEL REALTY (19,824 shares)
- Notable high quantities: SHYAMKAMAL I (10,022 shares), MODI RUBBER (6,505 shares), ITC Hotels L (5,760 shares), ERAAYA LIFES (3,855 shares)
- Covers wide range of sectors including auto, pharma, technology, real estate, finance, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction procedures
- Clearing members are required to fulfill delivery obligations through the auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 26, 2025 (Settlement No. 784)
- Auction execution will follow BSE's standard settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 105 scrips across multiple sectors, indicating delivery failures that require auction settlement.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - trading members with short delivery positions in these scrips will need to participate in the auction or face penalties.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - shareholders expecting delivery may experience delays, and auction prices may differ from market prices affecting settlement costs.

The auction reflects delivery obligation failures in the normal course of trading operations and is a standard mechanism to ensure settlement completion.
