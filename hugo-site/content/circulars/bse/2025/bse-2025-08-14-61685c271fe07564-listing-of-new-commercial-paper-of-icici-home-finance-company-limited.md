## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 40 crores (8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment effective August 14, 2025. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with a 43-day tenure.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 8,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 40 crores
- Scrip Code: 730026, ISIN: INE071G14HA8
- Issue price: Rs. 4,96,554.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 26, 2025
- Tenure: 43 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and additional investment option for debt market participants.
