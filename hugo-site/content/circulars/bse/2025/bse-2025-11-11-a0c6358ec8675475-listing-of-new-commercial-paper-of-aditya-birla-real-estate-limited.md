## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 8000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, effective November 11, 2025. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 8000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,363.50 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 400 crores
- Scrip Code: 730529
- Scrip ID: ABRE111125
- ISIN: INE055A14KJ9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: SBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE055A14KJ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all trades

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Listing Date: November 11, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 10, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides an additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors in the debt segment. The commercial paper is rated A1+ by both CARE and CRISIL, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.
