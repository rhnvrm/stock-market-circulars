## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Studds Accessories Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544599 and trading symbol STUDDS with ISIN number INE00Q601028. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Studds Accessories Limited
- Scrip Code: 544599
- Trading Symbol: STUDDS
- ISIN Number: INE00Q601028
- Listing will occur in due course with date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Studds Accessories Limited in preparation for commencement of trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 6, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has minimal immediate impact as it is a preliminary notification. Trading members and investors interested in the company should await the subsequent notice containing the actual listing date and detailed security information. The listing will add a new equity option in the accessories/automotive components sector to the BSE platform.
