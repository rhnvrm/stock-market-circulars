## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. The listing comprises 4000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crore. The instruments carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 4000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730602
- Scrip ID: ABRE201125
- ISIN Number: INE055A14KK7
- Credit Ratings: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: SBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE055A14KK7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: November 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026
- Tenure: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it pertains to a routine debt instrument listing on private placement basis. The Commercial Paper is relevant only to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing with Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited securities. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong short-term creditworthiness. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
