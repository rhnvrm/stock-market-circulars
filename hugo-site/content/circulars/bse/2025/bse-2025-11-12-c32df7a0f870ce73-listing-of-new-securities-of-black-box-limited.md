## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 31,975 equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) effective November 13, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 415/- per share (face value Rs. 2/-) to a Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 31,975 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each listed
- Issued at premium of Rs. 415/- per share (total issue price: Rs. 417/-)
- Allotted to Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Distinctive Numbers: 170012511 to 170044485
- Trading commences: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange. All 31,975 shares are subject to lock-in requirements.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 13, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: May 25, 2026 (for all 31,975 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves a small number of shares (31,975) from warrant conversion on a preferential basis. The shares are subject to lock-in until May 25, 2026, preventing immediate trading of the entire allotment. This is a routine corporate action with no significant operational or market implications.
