## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number INE672A01026 to Tata Investment Corporation Ltd (Scrip Code: 501301) following the sub-division of equity shares from face value of Rs.10/- to Re.1/-. The new ISIN will be effective for trades executed on and from October 14, 2025 (Ex-Date).

## Key Points

- New ISIN: INE672A01026
- Company: Tata Investment Corporation Ltd
- Scrip Code: 501301
- Corporate Action: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Re.1/-
- Ex-Date for new ISIN: October 14, 2025
- Demat Request (DR) Number: 735/2025-2026
- Notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20250924-73 dated September 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a procedural ISIN assignment following corporate action approval.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN INE672A01026
- All trades executed on or after October 14, 2025 must use the new ISIN
- Settlement and clearing systems must reflect the sub-divided share structure (Re.1/- face value)
- Market participants should ensure proper mapping of old ISIN to new ISIN for continuity

## Important Dates

- **October 7, 2025**: Circular issuance date
- **October 14, 2025**: Ex-Date - new ISIN becomes effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects trading and settlement systems for Tata Investment Corporation Ltd shares. Trading members and investors must be aware of the ISIN change to avoid execution errors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems before the ex-date. The 10:1 sub-division increases liquidity by reducing per-share price, potentially broadening investor base. Shareholders will receive 10 shares of Re.1/- each for every 1 share of Rs.10/- held.
