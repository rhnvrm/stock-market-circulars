## Summary

BSE announces the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 8, 2025. The companies - Logiciel Solutions Limited (544625), Exato Technologies Limited (544626), and Purple Wave Infocom Limited (544627) - were listed on BSE on December 5, 2025 and will be included in the index at the open of trading on December 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Logiciel Solutions Limited (Exchange ticker: 544625)
- Exato Technologies Limited (Exchange ticker: 544626)
- Purple Wave Infocom Limited (Exchange ticker: 544627)
- All three stocks listed on BSE on December 5, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 8, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolio managers may need to adjust their holdings to reflect the index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 5, 2025**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **December 8, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine addition of three SME stocks to a specialized index following their successful listing. The BSE SME IPO Index tracks newly listed SME companies, and these additions are part of normal index maintenance.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal for general investors. May impact index fund managers and investors who track or invest in the BSE SME IPO Index, as they may need to include these stocks in their portfolios.

**Trading Impact**: Potential increased trading volumes in these three stocks as index funds rebalance their portfolios to include the new constituents.
