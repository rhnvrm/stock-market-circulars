## Summary

BSE has published the details of Demat Auction Settlement No. 786 dated December 30, 2025. The circular lists 100 scrips across various companies with their respective scrip codes and quantities to be auctioned. The auction includes major blue-chip stocks like Adani Enterprises (100 shares), Infosys (2 shares), Titan Company (5 shares), and Vedanta (252 shares), as well as numerous mid and small-cap stocks. Notable quantities include Timex Group (263,431 shares), Prakash Steel (73,002 shares), and Patel Chem (20,800 shares).

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 786
- Date: December 30, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 100 securities
- Includes major stocks: Adani Enterprises, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, HFCL, ICICI Prudential MF, IDFC First Bank, Indian Renewable Energy, Infosys, NMDC, Petronet LNG, Titan, Vedanta
- Largest quantity: Timex Group (263,431 shares)
- Other significant quantities: Prakash Steel (73,002 shares), Patel Chem (20,800 shares), Pulsar International (14,740 shares), Bandhan Mutual Fund (3,524 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine demat auction settlement notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and participants must settle obligations for the listed securities
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE settlement calendar and procedures
- Members holding short positions in listed scrips must participate in the auction or deliver securities

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 30, 2025
- Settlement Number: 786

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 100 different securities with varying quantities. While most quantities are relatively small, a few scrips have significant volumes (Timex Group with 263,431 shares, Prakash Steel with 73,002 shares). The auction mechanism helps ensure settlement discipline and price discovery for failed deliveries.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Clearing members with obligations in any of the listed securities must ensure adequate funds/securities availability for auction participation. The diverse range of scrips requires careful position monitoring.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Investors in affected securities may observe temporary price volatility during auction proceedings. The auction primarily affects members with failed delivery obligations rather than general market participants.
