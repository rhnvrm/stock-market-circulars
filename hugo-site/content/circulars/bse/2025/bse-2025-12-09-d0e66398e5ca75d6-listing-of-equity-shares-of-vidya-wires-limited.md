## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of VIDYA WIRES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544633 and trading symbol VIDYAWIRES with ISIN number INE14UN01029. The specific listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- VIDYA WIRES LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544633
- Trading Symbol: VIDYAWIRES
- ISIN Number: INE14UN01029
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be informed through a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The notification serves as an advance information circular to trading members. Market participants should await the subsequent notice detailing the exact listing date and additional security specifications before the stock becomes available for trading. This represents a standard expansion of BSE's listed universe with a new equity security.
