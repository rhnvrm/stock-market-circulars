## Summary

BSE has announced that K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544641), an SME IPO stock, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling settlement segment (M Group) effective December 30, 2025. This follows the exchange's earlier notice dated December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Stock: K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544641
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling settlement segment)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, December 30, 2025
- This is in continuation of BSE Notice No. 20251212-47 dated December 12, 2025
- Contact Person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be shifted from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement mechanism. This change upgrades the trading mechanism for the stock, allowing for more flexible trading compared to the restrictive T2T segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should take note of the group change
- Systems and trading terminals should be updated to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further clarification or details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Prior Notice Reference:** December 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20251212-47)
- **Effective Date:** December 30, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** The move from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group) is generally positive for the stock as it:
- Increases liquidity by allowing intraday and short-term trading
- Removes the restriction of compulsory delivery-based trading only
- May attract more market participants and trading volume

**Investor Impact:** Medium - existing shareholders and potential investors will benefit from improved trading flexibility. The stock will now be available for normal rolling settlement trading alongside other M Group stocks.

**Market Significance:** This transition typically occurs after an SME IPO stock demonstrates compliance and stability, marking a positive development for K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED.
