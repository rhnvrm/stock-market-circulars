## Summary

GREAVES FINANCE LTD has executed a partial redemption of its debentures (ISIN: INE1QWF07014, Scrip Code: 977162), reducing the face value per debenture to Rs. 95,833. Trading members are advised that these debentures will trade at the reduced face value effective from October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: GREAVES FINANCE LTD
- Security: GFL-10.50%-29-9-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE1QWF07014
- Scrip Code: 977162
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 95,833 per debenture
- Notice Number: DR-738/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the debentures at the reduced face value of Rs. 95,833 per debenture
- Trading members are required to take note of this change and adjust their systems accordingly
- All trades in these debentures from the effective date must reflect the new face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 17, 2025 - Debentures will be traded with reduced face value from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Debenture Holders:** Existing holders of GREAVES FINANCE LTD debentures will receive partial redemption proceeds, with the remaining investment reflected at the reduced face value of Rs. 95,833 per debenture.

**Trading Impact:** Medium - Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement. The debentures will continue to trade but at the adjusted face value.

**Market Significance:** This is a routine corporate action for debt securities where the issuer has opted for partial redemption rather than full maturity redemption. The 10.50% coupon rate debentures maturing on September 29, 2027, will continue to trade at reduced principal value.
