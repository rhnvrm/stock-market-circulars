## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and commencement of trading for 750,000 new equity shares of HB Estate Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 532334) effective December 12, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion at an issue price of Rs. 65.25 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 55.25). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to a lock-in period until June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 750,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each listed on BSE
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 65.25 per share (including premium of Rs. 55.25)
- Distinctive Numbers: 56301869 to 57051868
- Date of allotment: October 14, 2025
- Trading commences: December 12, 2025
- ISIN: INE640B01021
- Scrip Code: 532334
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- All 750,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Lock-in compliance must be maintained until June 29, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 14, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Start Date:** December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** June 29, 2026
- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing announcement for a preferential allotment of shares representing warrant conversion. The relatively small number of shares (750,000) and the complete lock-in restriction until June 29, 2026 means there will be no immediate trading impact or liquidity addition to the market.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members can recognize these securities in the system starting December 12, 2025, but must enforce lock-in restrictions on all shares.
