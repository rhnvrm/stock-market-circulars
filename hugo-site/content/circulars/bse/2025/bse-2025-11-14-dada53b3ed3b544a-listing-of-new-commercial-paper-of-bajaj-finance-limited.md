## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 14, 2025. The listing comprises 33,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each on the BSE Debt segment. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 33,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,026.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730564
- Scrip ID: BFL131125
- ISIN: INE296A14D47
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE296A14D47
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Bajaj Finance Limited regularly issues commercial papers as part of its short-term funding operations. The listing is limited to BSE Debt segment with no implications for equity markets or retail investors. Trading will be restricted to institutional participants dealing in debt instruments.
