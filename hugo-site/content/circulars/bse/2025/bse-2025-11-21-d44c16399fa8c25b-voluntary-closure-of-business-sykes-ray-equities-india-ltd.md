## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that SYKES & RAY EQUITIES (INDIA) LTD. (Member No. 189) will voluntarily cease operations in the Currency segment effective November 24, 2025. This closure is voluntary and affects only the currency trading segment.

## Key Points

- SYKES & RAY EQUITIES (INDIA) LTD. holds membership number 189
- Voluntary closure applies to Currency segment only
- Effective date: Monday, November 24, 2025
- Trading members are informed for awareness
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 24, 2025 - SYKES & RAY EQUITIES (INDIA) LTD. ceases currency segment operations

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one trading member's voluntary exit from the currency segment and does not impact overall market operations or other participants.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for other trading members. This is purely informational regarding one member's business decision to exit the currency trading segment.
