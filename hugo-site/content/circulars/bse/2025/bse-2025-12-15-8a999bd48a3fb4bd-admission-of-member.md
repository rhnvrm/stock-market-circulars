## Summary

BSE has admitted three entities as trading members with effect from December 15, 2025. Two entities (Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited and Allvest Securities Private Limited) have been granted new memberships with access to multiple trading segments. Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has been granted additional segment membership for commodity derivatives.

## Key Points

- Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited (Member No. 6972) admitted with Cash and Commodity Derivatives segments as new membership
- Allvest Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6973) admitted with Cash, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, and Debt segments as new membership
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Member No. 6706) granted additional segment membership for Commodity Derivatives
- All admissions are subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Notice Number: 20251215-20
- Queries to be directed to: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice regarding new member admissions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 (admission date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Addition of new trading members increases market participation but does not affect existing trading operations or rules.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on existing trading members. New members will operate under standard BSE regulations applicable to their respective segments.

**Member Impact**: Informational only. Existing members are notified of new participants in the exchange ecosystem for transparency purposes.
