## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,598,400 new equity shares of Hemant Surgical Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543916) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will commence trading from November 7, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 197 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 187). The securities are subject to lock-in periods ending in May 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,598,400 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 187
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Trading commences on November 7, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 197 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0GYI01028
- Distribution numbers: 10440001 to 13038400
- Date of allotment: September 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading from November 7, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions must be enforced as per specified schedule
- Securities must be properly identified by distribution numbers and ISIN

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,049,600 shares)**: May 31, 2026 (Distribution Nos. 10440001-11489600)
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,548,800 shares)**: May 30, 2026 (Distribution Nos. 11489601-13038400)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine preferential allotment listing for a single company representing capital raising activity. The locked-in nature of the shares means immediate trading volume impact will be limited.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new securities with specified distribution numbers and lock-in restrictions to prevent unauthorized trading during lock-in periods.
