## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment on November 11, 2025. The Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each has been issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 100 units (Rs. 5 crore total). The instrument carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730533, Scrip ID: OWL101125
- ISIN Number: INE466L14FC0
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,423.50 per unit
- Total Quantity: 100 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FC0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 11, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Allotment Date:** November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument has a short 90-day tenure and represents a relatively small issuance of Rs. 5 crore. It provides an additional short-term debt investment option for institutional investors and high net worth individuals in the debt segment. The instrument carries strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA), indicating high credit quality and minimal default risk.
