## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Delisting) of MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED (Settlement No. 2025113). The original acquisition window was conducted from August 14-21, 2024. The revised schedule updates the bid period to October 30 - November 6, 2025, with revised obligation download on November 7, 2025 and settlement date on November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Delisting (Settlement No. 2025113)
- Revised Bid Period: October 30, 2025 to November 6, 2025
- Revised Obligation Download Date: November 7, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: November 10, 2025
- Supersedes Notice No. 20251029-10
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Market Type "(Delisting)" and Settlement No. 2025113 must be used in depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular updates the settlement timeline for an existing delisting process in accordance with the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange (Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- **Members/Custodians**: Must specify Market Type "(Delisting)" and Settlement No. 2025113 in the depository system along with other details
- **Members**: Should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- **Members/Custodians**: Should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement procedures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific delisting settlement schedule revision affecting only MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED shareholders who are participating in the acquisition window.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for Members/Custodians handling transactions for MANPHOOL EXPORTS LIMITED shareholders, as they must ensure compliance with revised timelines and use correct settlement numbers in the depository system.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Shareholders participating in the delisting offer need to be aware of the revised bid period and settlement dates to ensure timely participation.
