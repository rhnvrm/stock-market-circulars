## Summary

InterGlobe Aviation Limited (IndiGo) has issued a clarification in response to a news article published in Economic Times on December 10, 2025. The company confirms that CEO Pieter Elbers and other senior officials have been asked to participate in a review meeting with the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) scheduled for December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification on news article titled "IndiGo Meltdown: India's aviation watchdog summons CEO Pieter Elbers, senior officials on Dec 11"
- Company confirms CEO Pieter Elbers has been asked to participate in DGCA review meeting
- Senior officials of the company will also attend the meeting
- Meeting scheduled for December 11, 2025
- Response provided by Company Secretary & Chief Compliance Officer Neerja Sharma
- Scrip Code: 539448

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a clarification regarding a regulatory review meeting.

## Compliance Requirements

This circular represents the company's compliance with BSE's requirement to clarify news items that may impact stock price or investor sentiment. The company has promptly responded to BSE Surveillance's query regarding the news article.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Economic Times news article published
- **December 10, 2025**: BSE Surveillance requested clarification (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/RV/APJ/ (2025-2026)/103)
- **December 11, 2025**: DGCA review meeting scheduled
- **December 11, 2025**: Company clarification issued

## Impact Assessment

The clarification confirms regulatory scrutiny of IndiGo's operations by DGCA. While the company has not disclosed the specific agenda of the review meeting, the involvement of the CEO and senior management suggests it is a significant matter requiring attention at the highest level. The news article's reference to "meltdown" suggests possible operational issues, though the company's brief response does not provide details. Investors should monitor for further disclosures regarding the outcome of the DGCA meeting and any resulting action items or regulatory directions.
