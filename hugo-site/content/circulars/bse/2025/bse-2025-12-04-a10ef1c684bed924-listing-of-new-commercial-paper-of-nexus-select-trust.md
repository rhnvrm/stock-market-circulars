## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis with effect from December 4, 2025. The issue comprises 4500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,92,615 per unit with CRISIL A1+ rating. ICICI Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4500 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,615 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730702
- Scrip ID: NST031225
- ISIN: INE0NDH14122
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NDH14122
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades subject to tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - routine listing of debt instruments. Relevant only to debt market participants, institutional investors trading in commercial paper, and those tracking Nexus Select Trust securities. The 90-day tenor and A1+ rating indicates short-term high-quality debt instrument with minimal market disruption.
