## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of IGH Holdings Private Limited on its debt segment effective August 14, 2025. The issue comprises 3,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 150 crores, issued on private placement basis.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Total quantity: 3,000 securities (Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,978 per security
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities must be traded under ISIN INE02FN14606

## Important Dates

- Listing date: August 14, 2025
- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for IGH Holdings Private Limited with 3-month maturity period. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
