## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Bangalore Fort Farms Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Grameva Limited with effect from November 4, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on November 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: Bangalore Fort Farms Limited
- **New Name**: Grameva Limited
- **CIN**: L51101WB1966PLC226442
- **Effective Date**: November 4, 2025 (Fourth day of November Two Thousand Twenty Five)
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Issuing Authority**: Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- **Approved By**: Prerna Panwar, Assistant Registrar of Companies/Deputy Registrar of Companies/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving a name change approved under the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name "Bangalore Fort Farms Limited" along with the new name "Grameva Limited" for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 4, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from November 4, 2025 (until November 4, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financials, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update its letterheads, signboards, website, and all official communications to reflect the new name while maintaining the old name display for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing agreements and contracts continue under the new name.
