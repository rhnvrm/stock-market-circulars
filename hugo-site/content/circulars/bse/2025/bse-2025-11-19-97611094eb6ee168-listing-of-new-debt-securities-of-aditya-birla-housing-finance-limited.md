## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 19, 2025. The securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only. The issuance comprises 20,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 7.2841% per annum with a 5-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 977299
- Scrip ID: 72841ABH30
- ISIN: INE831R07623
- Quantity: 20,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crore
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Interest Rate: 7.2841% per annum (payable annually)
- Listing Date: November 19, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Put/Call Option: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R07623
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 18, 2025
- Listing Date: November 19, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from November 18, 2026 to November 18, 2030
- Date of Redemption: November 18, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The high credit ratings (AAA/STABLE from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option in the housing finance sector. The 7.2841% interest rate reflects current market conditions for highly-rated 5-year debt instruments. No operational changes required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
