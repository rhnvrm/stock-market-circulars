## Summary

BSE has announced that the Draft Offer Document of AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED, filed on October 09, 2025, is now available on the BSE SME website for public review and comments. The document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED on October 09, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit their feedback within 21 days from October 13, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The company must maintain the document on the exchange website for at least 21 days as per ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **October 09, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED
- **October 13, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **November 03, 2025**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to a single SME IPO filing. The announcement follows standard SEBI procedures for draft offer documents under the ICDR Regulations. Impact is limited to:

- Potential investors interested in AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED
- SME segment participants
- Regulatory compliance stakeholders

The 21-day public comment period allows stakeholders to review disclosures and provide feedback before the final offer document is issued.
