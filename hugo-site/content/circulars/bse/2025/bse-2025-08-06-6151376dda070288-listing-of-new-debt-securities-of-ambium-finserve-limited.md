## Summary

AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED has listed new debt securities worth ₹20 crore on BSE's Debt segment through private placement. The securities carry an 11.16% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities of ₹10,000 face value each
- Scrip Code: 976992, Scrip ID: 1116AMFL26
- ISIN: INE0RU307197
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.16% per annum (monthly payments)
- Issue Price: ₹10,000 per security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 5, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: September 19, 2025 to August 19, 2026 (monthly)
- Date of Redemption: August 19, 2026
- Trading Effective: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard debt securities listing for a private placement. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option at 11.16% annual return with monthly interest payments.
