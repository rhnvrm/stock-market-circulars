## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL) will conduct operations from its Disaster Recovery (DR) Site from Friday, December 26, 2025, to Tuesday, December 30, 2025. This applies to all segments including Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR. Members and custodians will experience no changes in accessing ICCL applications/URLs during this period.

## Key Points

- ICCL operations shifting to DR Site from December 26-30, 2025
- All segments covered: Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR
- No changes required for members and custodians
- Access to ICCL applications/URLs remains unchanged
- Reference to Exchange Circular No. 20251226-1 dated December 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a temporary operational arrangement for disaster recovery purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required from members or custodians. The operational shift is transparent and requires no changes to existing access methods or procedures.

## Important Dates

- **December 26, 2025 (Friday)**: DR Site operations begin
- **December 30, 2025 (Tuesday)**: DR Site operations end

## Impact Assessment

Minimal to no impact on market participants. This is a routine disaster recovery exercise to ensure business continuity capabilities. Members and custodians can continue normal operations without any system access changes. The DR site operations cover all major trading and clearing segments, demonstrating comprehensive backup infrastructure readiness.
