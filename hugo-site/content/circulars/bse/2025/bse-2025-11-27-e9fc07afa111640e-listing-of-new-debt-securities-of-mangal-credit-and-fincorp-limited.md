## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Mangal Credit and Fincorp Limited on its Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 1,000 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit, and carry an interest rate of 12.90% per annum with monthly interest payments. The securities mature on October 3, 2027, and are rated CRISIL BBB/Stable.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976597, Scrip ID: 1290MCFL27
- ISIN: INE545L07036 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 1,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,752.60 per unit
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Interest Rate: 12.90% per annum (monthly payments)
- Interest Payment Period: January 3, 2026 to October 3, 2027
- Redemption Date: October 3, 2027
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- No Put/Call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE545L07036
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Queries may be directed to BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: January 3, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly from January 3, 2026 to October 3, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt securities listing affecting only participants in the BSE Debt segment and institutional investors in privately placed securities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing process for debt instruments. Trading members can trade these securities on the debt platform following normal procedures with 1 paise tick size.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Relevant only to investors who participated in the private placement and debt market participants. The CRISIL BBB/Stable rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate protection parameters.
