## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 8, 2025. The companies are Logiciel Solutions Limited (Exchange ticker 544625), Exato Technologies Limited (Exchange ticker 544626), and Purple Wave Infocom Limited (Exchange ticker 544627). These companies were listed on BSE on December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Logiciel Solutions Limited (544625) listed on December 5, 2025
- Exato Technologies Limited (544626) listed on December 5, 2025
- Purple Wave Infocom Limited (544627) listed on December 5, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 8, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index maintenance activity following new listings on the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 5, 2025**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **December 8, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact notice. This is routine index maintenance following new SME listings. The addition of these three stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index may result in minor trading activity from index-tracking funds or portfolios. Market participants tracking this index should note the composition changes for portfolio rebalancing purposes. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
