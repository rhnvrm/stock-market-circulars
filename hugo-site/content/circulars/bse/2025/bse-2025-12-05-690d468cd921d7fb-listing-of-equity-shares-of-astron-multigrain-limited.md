## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251205-12 informing trading members that equity shares of Astron Multigrain Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544628, symbol ASTRONMULT, and ISIN No. INE0RUY01012. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Astron Multigrain Limited
- Scrip Code: 544628
- Trading Symbol: ASTRONMULT
- ISIN Number: INE0RUY01012
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new company joining the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing details for future trading activities once the listing date is announced. No immediate compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 5, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine SME listing announcement. The notification is informational for trading members to prepare for the upcoming listing. Investors interested in SME stocks should await the separate notice containing the actual listing date and detailed security information before trading can commence.
