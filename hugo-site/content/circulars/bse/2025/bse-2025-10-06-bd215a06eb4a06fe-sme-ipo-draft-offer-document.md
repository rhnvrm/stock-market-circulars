## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Gabion Technologies India Limited for its SME IPO on the exchange website. The document, filed on September 30, 2025, contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for 21 days from October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Gabion Technologies India Limited on September 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from October 6, 2025

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: September 30, 2025
- Notice Date: October 6, 2025
- Public Comments Deadline: October 27, 2025 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure notice for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in Gabion Technologies India Limited's upcoming IPO. No immediate trading or operational impact on existing market participants.
