## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis with effect from November 26, 2025. The issue comprises 600 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 30 crore, with a 92-day tenor maturing on February 25, 2026. The securities carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ and will trade under ISIN INE423A14YF8.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 30 crore (600 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,027 per unit (discount of Rs. 8,973)
- Scrip Code: 730330, Scrip ID: AEL26925
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YF8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades subject to BSE Debt segment rules and regulations
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026
- **Tenor**: 92 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing by a large corporate entity. The Rs. 30 crore issue size is modest relative to the company's overall financing activities.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term, highly-rated debt investment option with 92-day maturity. The dual A1+ ratings from CARE and ICRA indicate highest safety for short-term instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now facilitate transactions in this commercial paper on BSE's debt segment platform in standard Rs. 5 lakh lots.
