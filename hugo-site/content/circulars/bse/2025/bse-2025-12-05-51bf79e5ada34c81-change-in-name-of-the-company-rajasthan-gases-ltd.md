## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of Rajasthan Gases Ltd. This is a standard corporate disclosure informing market participants about the updated corporate identity of the company.

## Key Points

- Company name change for Rajasthan Gases Ltd has been officially notified
- All securities of the company will continue to trade under updated company name
- Scrip code and ISIN remain unchanged
- This is an administrative update with no operational impact

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their records with the new company name
- The company must use the new name in all future communications and filings
- All existing securities will reflect the updated company name

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 5, 2025
- Effective Date: As per company's registration documents

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect:
- Trading operations
- Securities identification (scrip code/ISIN unchanged)
- Shareholder rights or obligations
- Company's business operations

Investors and market participants should simply note the updated company name for record-keeping purposes.
