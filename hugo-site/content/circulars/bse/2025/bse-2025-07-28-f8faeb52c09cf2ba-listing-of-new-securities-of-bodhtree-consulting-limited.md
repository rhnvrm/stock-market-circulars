## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 4,692,456 new equity shares of Bodhtree Consulting Limited (Scrip Code: 539122) issued through rights basis. The shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 4,692,456 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 20/-
- Rights basis issuance with allotment date of July 25, 2025
- ISIN: INE104F01029
- Distribution numbers: 17173115 to 21865570
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard new securities listing procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine rights issue listing for an existing company. The new shares will increase the company's share capital and provide additional liquidity.
