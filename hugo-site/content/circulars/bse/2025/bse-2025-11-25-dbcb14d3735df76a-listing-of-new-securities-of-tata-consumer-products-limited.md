## Summary

883 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Tata Consumer Products Limited (Scrip Code: 500800) that were previously kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme are now listed and permitted to trade on BSE with effect from November 26, 2025. These shares are pari passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total of 883 equity shares being listed from abeyance
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Scrip Code: 500800
- ISIN: INE192A01025
- Shares issued pursuant to a scheme
- Four separate batches of distinctive numbers
- Trading commences November 26, 2025

## Share Details

**Distinctive Number Ranges:**
- 285 shares: 631175900 to 631176184
- 63 shares: 632703437 to 632703499
- 125 shares: 632647396 to 632647520
- 410 shares: 632449937 to 632450346

**Total:** 883 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for shares previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are available for trading from the effective date. The shares carry same rights as existing equity shares (pari passu).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** November 26, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 883 shares from abeyance, representing an insignificant fraction of Tata Consumer Products' total equity capital. This is a routine administrative process related to a corporate scheme and does not affect the company's operations or fundamentals.
