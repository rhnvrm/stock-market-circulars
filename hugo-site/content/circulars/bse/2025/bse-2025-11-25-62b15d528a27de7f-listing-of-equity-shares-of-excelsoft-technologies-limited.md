## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of Excelsoft Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544617 and trading symbol EXCELSOFT with ISIN number INE606N01019. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Excelsoft Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544617
- Trading Symbol: EXCELSOFT
- ISIN Number: INE606N01019
- Listing status: Approved, pending date announcement
- Segment: Equity

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing approval notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the scrip details for future reference. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This announcement has limited immediate impact as it is a preliminary notification. Trading members should update their systems with the scrip details.
