## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Tata Motors Limited (formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) with Scrip Code 544569 will be permitted to trade under Group 'B' effective Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Tata Motors Limited (Formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Scrip Code: 544569
- New Trading Group: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Wednesday, November 26, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251112-15
- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Category: Company related
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition to trading under Group 'B' classification. Group 'B' typically indicates securities with specific trading parameters and surveillance measures as per BSE's classification framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for Tata Motors Limited shares
- Trading parameters applicable to Group 'B' securities will apply from the effective date
- Members should update their systems and processes to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 26, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group 'B' may affect:
- Trading margins and requirements for the scrip
- Surveillance measures applicable to the security
- Settlement cycles and trading parameters
- Market participants trading in Tata Motors Limited shares should be aware of the specific conditions and requirements applicable to Group 'B' securities

Trading members and investors should familiarize themselves with BSE's Group 'B' trading norms and adjust their trading strategies accordingly.
