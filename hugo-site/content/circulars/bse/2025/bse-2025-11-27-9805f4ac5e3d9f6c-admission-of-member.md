## Summary

BSE has admitted Eklingji Broking LLP as a new trading member of the exchange with member number 6970. The firm has been granted trading rights in Cash/Equity Derivatives segments under new membership category, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New trading member: Eklingji Broking LLP
- Member Number: 6970
- Segments authorized: Cash/Equity Derivatives
- Membership type: New Membership
- Trading rights limited to BSE Ltd. only
- Admission subject to exchange Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational notice regarding member admission.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This notice is for informational purposes only.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251127-21

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative announcement with no direct impact on market operations or existing trading members. The admission of a new trading member represents expansion of the exchange's member base but does not impose any obligations on existing participants. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
