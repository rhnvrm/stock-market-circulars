## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ONGC Petro additions Limited on private placement basis, effective November 17, 2025. The issue comprises 8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 400 crore, with a maturity period of 180 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: ONGC Petro additions Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 8,000 units (Rs. 400 crore)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,84,285.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730576
- **Scrip ID**: OPAL141125
- **ISIN**: INE163N14592
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE163N14592
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 13, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in short-term highly-rated commercial paper. The A1+ rating from both CRISIL and India Ratings indicates highest safety and strong credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members have access to debt department support for clarifications.
