## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities consist of 5,000 debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 11% annual interest rate with monthly payment, maturing on February 3, 2027. The securities are rated ACUITE A- and will trade in dematerialized form only under ISIN INE08KJ07142.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be observed for trading
