## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on private placement basis, effective December 2, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a 12.5% interest rate, maturing on December 27, 2028, with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Security Name: ACAPL-12.5%-27-12-28-PVT
- Scrip Code: 976868, Scrip ID: 125ACAP28
- ISIN: INE08XP07324
- Quantity: 5,000 debentures, Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: ₹95,034.25 (discount to face value)
- Interest Rate: 12.5% per annum (monthly payment)
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB
- Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 27, 2028
- No put/call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XP07324
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's website for complete details
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from July 27, 2025 to December 27, 2028
- **Redemption Date**: December 27, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market-wide impact as this is a private placement of debt securities by a private limited company. The listing provides additional trading avenue for institutional investors who participated in the placement. The ICRA BBB rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate capacity for timely payment obligations.
