## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544626), an SME IPO, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 19, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's Notice No. 20251204-53 dated December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544626
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Notice Reference: Continuation of Notice No. 20251204-53 dated December 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED equity shares will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. This represents a transition from a more restrictive trading framework to normal market operations, typically indicating successful completion of initial trading phase for SME IPO stocks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must prepare for the segment transition by December 19, 2025
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details and clarifications
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025 (Friday)
- **Reference Notice**: December 4, 2025 (Notice No. 20251204-53)

## Impact Assessment

This segment change is positive for EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED as it:
- Enhances liquidity by moving from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement
- Reduces trading restrictions associated with MT Group
- Allows for more flexible trading mechanisms under M Group
- Indicates successful transition from initial SME IPO listing phase
- May attract more traders and investors due to improved trading flexibility

Trading members should note the transition date and ensure their systems are updated accordingly. The move to Rolling segment typically results in better price discovery and improved market participation.
