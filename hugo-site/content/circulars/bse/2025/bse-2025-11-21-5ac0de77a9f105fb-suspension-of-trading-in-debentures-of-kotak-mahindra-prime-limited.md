## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for payment of interest and redemption of the debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Suspension applies to KMPL 7.8% debentures with ISIN INE916DA7SA6 (Scrip Code: 974426)
- Debentures are maturing on December 12, 2025
- Record date set for November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures
- No dealings permitted from November 27, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension in accordance with existing procedures for debt securities approaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE916DA7SA6) from November 27, 2025
- Members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members should inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and upcoming redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 27, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** November 27, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only holders of this specific debenture series.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard procedure for debt securities nearing redemption. Trading members need to update systems and notify affected clients.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the debenture terms. No trading opportunity available from suspension date, but this is expected given the proximity to maturity date.
