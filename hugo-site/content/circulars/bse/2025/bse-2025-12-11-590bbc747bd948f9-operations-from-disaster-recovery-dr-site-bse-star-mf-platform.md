## Summary

BSE has announced that the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform will operate from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, December 13, 2025, between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM. Members will experience limited access to the platform during environment switching activities from Primary to DR and back to Primary environment.

## Key Points

- BSE StAR MF platform will operate from DR site on December 13, 2025 from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM
- Platform access will be unavailable during two switching windows
- Primary to DR environment switch: 8:30 AM to 9:30 AM
- DR to Primary environment switch: 11:30 AM to 12:30 PM
- Members should plan their mutual fund activities accordingly
- Helpdesk support available on 022-45720450/650 and 022-69158550 (8:00 AM to 7:00 PM on business days)
- Queries can be raised on CRS portal: https://bsecrs.bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled operational activity for disaster recovery testing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must plan their BSE StAR MF platform activities to avoid the specified downtime windows
- Members should ensure alternative arrangements for critical mutual fund transactions during the switching periods
- No specific compliance actions required beyond operational awareness

## Important Dates

- **December 13, 2025**: DR site operations day
  - 8:30 AM - 9:30 AM: Primary to DR environment switch (platform unavailable)
  - 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: DR site operations window
  - 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: DR to Primary environment switch (platform unavailable)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - The BSE StAR MF platform will have limited availability during specific time windows on a Saturday, which typically sees lower trading volumes. The two one-hour switching windows (8:30-9:30 AM and 11:30 AM-12:30 PM) will result in complete platform unavailability.

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - Since the activity is scheduled for a Saturday and affects only the mutual fund platform, the overall market impact is limited. However, members conducting mutual fund transactions during these hours will need to plan accordingly.

**Member Impact**: Members actively using the BSE StAR MF platform on Saturday morning will need to adjust their operational schedules to accommodate the downtime windows. The advance notice allows for adequate planning.
