## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Muthoot Finance Limited. Trading will be suspended from December 26, 2025, with a record date of December 27, 2025 set for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of Muthoot Finance Limited affected by trading suspension
- MFL-II-11-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE414G07FK7, Code: 937351)
- MFL-VI-11-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE414G07FO9, Code: 937359)
- Both series subject to redemption and interest payment
- Trading members instructed to cease dealing in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debenture lifecycle events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Muthoot Finance NCDs from December 26, 2025
- Members are required to note and comply with the suspension directive
- No trading activity permitted in MFL-II-11-1-26-NCD (937351) and MFL-VI-11-1-26-NCD (937359) from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 27, 2025
- **Reference**: DR-785/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the two specific NCD series. No broader market implications expected.

**Investor Impact**: NCD holders with record date ownership will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Secondary market liquidity for these instruments ceases from December 26, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent transactions in the suspended NCDs. Routine corporate action processing for debt securities.
