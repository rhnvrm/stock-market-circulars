## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis. The instrument comprises 2,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crores. The CP carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will be redeemed on March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Fedbank Financial Services Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crores (2,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730705
- **Scrip ID**: FFSL041225
- **ISIN**: INE007N14EM0
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Federal Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EM0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding for Fedbank Financial Services Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate high creditworthiness. Trading members can contact BSE's debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
