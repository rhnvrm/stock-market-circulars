## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Finstars Capital Limited on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 11.07% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977361, Scrip ID: 1107FCL27
- ISIN Number: INE08XA07052
- Quantity: 1,500 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: IVR BBB-/Negative
- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XA07052
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum page for detailed terms
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from December 31, 2025 to December 9, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: December 9, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a private placement listing on the debt segment. The securities carry a sub-investment grade credit rating (IVR BBB-/Negative), indicating higher risk. The listing provides trading members with access to these debt instruments for their clients interested in high-yield debt securities. No put/call options are available on these securities.
