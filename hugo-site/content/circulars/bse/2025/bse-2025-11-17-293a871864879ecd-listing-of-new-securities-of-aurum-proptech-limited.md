## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 42,42,537 new equity shares of Aurum PropTech Limited (Scrip Code: 539289) effective from November 18, 2025. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 198.77 per share (face value Rs. 5) to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until May 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 42,42,537 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at premium of Rs. 198.77/-
- Issue price: Rs. 203.77 per share
- Allotment date: September 25, 2025
- Trading commences: November 18, 2025
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- ISIN: INE898S01029
- Distinctive Numbers: 73986553 to 78229089
- All shares subject to lock-in until May 20, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- The new securities are available for trading from November 18, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 42,42,537 shares until May 20, 2026
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The issuance represents capital raising by Aurum PropTech Limited for non-promoter investors. The lock-in period until May 20, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded freely for approximately 6 months, preventing immediate dilution impact. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and investors of Aurum PropTech Limited only.
