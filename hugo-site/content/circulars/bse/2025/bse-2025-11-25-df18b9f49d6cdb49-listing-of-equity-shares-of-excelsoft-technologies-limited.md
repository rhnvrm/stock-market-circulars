## Summary

BSE has issued lock-in details for the listing of equity shares of Excelsoft Technologies Limited (formerly Excelsoft Technologies Private Limited). The circular provides comprehensive information on 11,50,84,164 equity shares with varying lock-in periods ranging from 1 month to 3 years, including promoter shares, IPO public shares, and ESOP shares.

- Total equity shares detailed: 11,50,84,164 shares
- Multiple categories of shares: Promoter locked-in shares, IPO public shares, and ESOP shares
- Lock-in periods vary from no lock-in (ESOP) to 3 years for certain promoter holdings
- Shares available in both Demat and Physical form
- Distinctive numbers range from 1 to 11,50,84,164

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing circular providing lock-in details as per SEBI IPO regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Promoter shares subject to mandatory lock-in periods as per SEBI guidelines
- Lock-in shares marked distinctively with specific distinctive number ranges
- Shares held in both Demat and Physical form must comply with respective lock-in restrictions
- ESOP shares not subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start Date (Promoter Shares - 1 year)**: November 24, 2025 to November 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Start Date (Promoter Shares - 1 month)**: November 24, 2025 to December 23, 2025
- **Lock-in Start Date (Promoter Shares - 3 months)**: November 24, 2025 to February 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Start Date (Promoter Shares - 3 years)**: November 24, 2025 to November 25, 2028
- **ESOP Shares**: No lock-in applicable
- **IPO Public Shares**: No lock-in applicable

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - New technology company listing adds to BSE's IT sector offerings. The substantial number of locked-in shares (approximately 8.22 crore shares under various lock-in periods) will limit immediate free float.

**Liquidity Impact**: The lock-in structure ensures controlled supply of shares in the market over different time periods. ESOP and IPO public shares (approximately 3.28 crore shares) are immediately tradable, providing initial liquidity.

**Investor Impact**: Investors should note the lock-in schedules when assessing available float. The staggered lock-in expiry dates (1 month, 3 months, 1 year, and 3 years) will gradually increase tradable supply.

**Company Profile**: Excelsoft Technologies Limited is an education technology company with offices in Mysuru, Noida, and Hyderabad, operating in the software development sector (CIN indicates software publishing activities).
