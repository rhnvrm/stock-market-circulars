## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251223-7 (DR-784/2025-2026) suspending trading in debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debentures on December 26, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited to be suspended from trading
- SCRIP CODE: 975277
- ISIN: INE01YL07235
- Security particulars: ESPLD-11.75%-26-12-25-PVT
- Maturity Date: December 26, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: December 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-784/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Earlysalary Services Private Limited from December 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and comply with the directive

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 26, 2025**: Maturity date of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only a single debt security issue from a private limited company. The suspension is a routine procedural action taken before maturity. Market participants holding these debentures should prepare for maturity settlement. No broader market or systemic impact expected.
