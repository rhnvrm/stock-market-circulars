## Summary

BSE Limited has published the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) who will be visiting BSE premises during December 2025. The circular specifically lists companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit. This facility allows investors and shareholders to directly interact with RTAs for services related to share certificates, transmission, name changes, and other investor-related matters.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule for December 2025 at BSE Limited
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is among the visiting RTAs
- List includes 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services
- Companies span various sectors including steel, finance, textiles, pharmaceuticals, and technology
- Investors can avail RTA services during the scheduled visit period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification about scheduled RTA visits.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for investor convenience.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period**: December 2025 (specific dates to be confirmed by BSE)
- Investors should contact BSE or the respective RTA for exact visit dates and timing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Nil - This is purely an administrative and investor service announcement.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Facilitates direct investor-RTA interaction for shareholder services. Investors holding shares in the listed companies can utilize this opportunity for physical verification, document submission, and query resolution related to their holdings.

**Affected Entities**: Shareholders and investors of the 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., including notable names like Hind Rectifiers, LKP Finance, Safari Industries, Securekloud Technologies, and others listed in the circular.

## Companies Covered by Adroit Corporate Services

The circular lists companies across diverse sectors including industrial manufacturing, financial services, real estate, textiles, technology, and specialty chemicals. Investors in these companies can benefit from direct RTA interaction during the December 2025 visit period.
