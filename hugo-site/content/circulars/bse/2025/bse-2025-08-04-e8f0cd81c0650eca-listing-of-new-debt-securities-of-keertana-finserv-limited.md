## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to dealings of new debt securities issued by Keertana Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 4, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,500 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976776, Scrip ID: 112KFL26
- ISIN Number: INE0NES07188 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 101,129
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.2% per annum (monthly payments)
- Securities tradeable only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 1, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Monthly from August 11, 2025 to December 11, 2026
- Date of Redemption: December 11, 2026
- Effective Trading Date: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing on private placement basis. The securities will provide additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional investors.
