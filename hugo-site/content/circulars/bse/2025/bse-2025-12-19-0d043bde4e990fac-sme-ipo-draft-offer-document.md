## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Mother Agri Food Limited for public review and comments. The document was filed on December 18, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The draft is available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Mother Agri Food Limited on December 18, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website (www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx)
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and market participants can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 19, 2025 (i.e., by January 9, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The draft offer document will remain available for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 18, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Mother Agri Food Limited
- **December 19, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 9, 2026**: Last date for submission of public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single company's SME IPO process. The impact is limited to:

- Prospective investors interested in Mother Agri Food Limited's IPO
- Market participants and analysts tracking SME segment offerings
- Regulatory compliance stakeholders monitoring IPO documentation processes

The availability of the draft offer document for public comments is a standard pre-IPO regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations, ensuring transparency and allowing stakeholders to provide feedback before the final offer document is filed.
