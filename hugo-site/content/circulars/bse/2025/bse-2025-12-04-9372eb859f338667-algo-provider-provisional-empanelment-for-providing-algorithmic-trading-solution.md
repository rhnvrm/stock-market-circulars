## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solution provider for trading members of the exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and the list of approved algo providers is maintained on BSE's website.

## Key Points

- AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The vendor will provide algorithmic trading solutions to trading members
- This is a provisional empanelment, not final approval
- The empanelment is effective immediately from December 4, 2025
- Updated list of algo providers is available on BSE website

## Vendor Details

**Company Name:** AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited

**Address:** Office #1203, Cello Triumph, B-wing, IB Patel Road, off the W-E highway, Goregaon East, Mumbai-400063

**Contact:** +91 9013468987

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the list of empanelled algorithmic trading solution providers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may engage with AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited for algorithmic trading solutions
- Trading members should refer to the updated list of algo providers on BSE website: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
- No mandatory action required from trading members

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date:** December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine administrative notice that expands the options available to trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Trading members who wish to use algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. This does not impose any new obligations on existing trading members.

**Target Audience:** Trading members who use or are considering algorithmic trading solutions for their operations.
