## Summary

ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD (Scrip Code: 538539) has announced a rights issue with record date set for November 7, 2025. The company is offering 7 equity shares of Re.10/- each for every 3 equity shares held by existing shareholders. The issue price is Rs.10/- per share, payable in installments with Rs.2.50 on application and Rs.7.50 on subsequent call(s). Trading in the company's equity shares will be on ex-rights basis from November 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD (Scrip Code: 538539)
- Rights entitlement ratio: 7 equity shares for every 3 equity shares held
- Face value: Re.10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs.10/- per share at par
- Payment structure: Rs.2.50 on application and Rs.7.50 on one or more subsequent call(s)
- Record date: November 7, 2025
- Ex-rights date: November 7, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-751/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialised securities - Rolling settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD equity shares on ex-rights basis from November 7, 2025
- Eligible shareholders as on record date (November 7, 2025) will be entitled to participate in the rights issue
- Trading members are required to take note of the ex-rights trading arrangement

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-751/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The rights issue will affect existing shareholders of ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LTD who must decide whether to subscribe to additional shares. The ex-rights trading arrangement may cause price adjustment in the stock.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders as of the record date have the opportunity to maintain their proportional ownership by subscribing to the rights issue at par value. Non-participation will result in dilution of shareholding.

**Liquidity Impact**: The staggered payment structure (Rs.2.50 on application and Rs.7.50 on call) provides flexibility to shareholders in managing cash flows for subscription.

**Trading Impact**: All trades from November 7, 2025 onwards will be executed on an ex-rights basis, meaning new buyers will not be entitled to participate in this rights issue.
