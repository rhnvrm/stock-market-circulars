## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED on its Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit, carrying an 11% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments, and maturing on February 3, 2027. The securities have been assigned ACUITE A- credit rating and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977272
- Scrip ID: 11MGFL27
- ISIN Number: INE08KJ07142
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly
- Date of Allotment: November 3, 2025
- Maturity Date: February 3, 2027
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Market Lot: 1
- No Put/Call options available
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07142
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- The securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 3, 2025
- Listing Date: November 6, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Maturity/Redemption Date: February 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to private placement debt securities of a specific company. The impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants interested in MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED securities
- Investors seeking A- rated corporate bonds with 11% yield and monthly interest payments
- Trading members who may facilitate transactions in these securities

The securities provide an investment option with a 15-month tenure (approximately) from listing to maturity. For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
