## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Supreme Industries Ltd on private placement basis effective November 17, 2025. The issue consists of 900 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The papers were allotted on November 14, 2025 and will mature on February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 900 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730580
- **Scrip ID**: SIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE195A14CE1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE195A14CE1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Supreme Industries Ltd for approximately 90 days. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and low credit risk. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors seeking short-term, high-rated corporate debt instruments.
