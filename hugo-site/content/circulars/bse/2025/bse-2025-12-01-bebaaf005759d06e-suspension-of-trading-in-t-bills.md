## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills and one Sovereign Gold Bond effective December 2, 2025, as these securities are scheduled to mature on December 4, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these instruments from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from December 2, 2025
- Four debt securities affected: three T-bills and one Sovereign Gold Bond
- Maturity date: December 4, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-768/2025-2026
- Applies to debt segment trading members

| 804997 | IN002024Z347 | 364TB41225 (364-day T-bill) |
| 805112 | IN002025X232 | 91TB041225 (91-day T-bill) |
| 800278 | IN0020170125 | Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the listed securities from December 2, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No new positions should be opened in these securities

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 2, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **December 4, 2025**: Redemption/maturity date for all listed securities

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine maturity-related suspension affecting only the debt segment. Trading members holding positions in these securities should ensure settlement and redemption processes are in place. The suspension prevents any inadvertent trading close to maturity date, protecting market participants from settlement risks.
