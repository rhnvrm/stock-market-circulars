## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED (CIN: L46498GJ2011PLC064964). The company's name has been officially changed to VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED with effect from November 27, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED to VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L46498GJ2011PLC064964
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Registrar of Companies
- Company was originally incorporated as VEERAM T.V. NETWORK PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies (Sabari Raj)
- Registered office: Ground & First Floor, 7, Natvarshyam Co Op Ho S Ld, Opp. Orchid Park, Ramdevnagar Road, Ahmedabad- 380051, Gujarat
- Company is limited by shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a company-specific administrative action under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display its old name (VEERAM SECURITIES LIMITED) along with the new name (VIRAM SUVARN LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All company documents, letterheads, and communications must be updated to reflect the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 27, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: November 27, 2025 (digitally signed at 18:31:26 IST)
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from effective date (until November 27, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect trading operations or market dynamics.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new name.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative only. The company will need to update its branding, documentation, and statutory filings. Trading symbol and CIN remain unchanged for continuity.

**Investor Consideration**: Investors should note the name change for record-keeping purposes but no action is required. All securities held under the old name automatically transfer to the new name.
