## Summary

BSE has announced that Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd (scrip code 539226) will undergo a group change from Group X to Group B effective December 19, 2025. This reclassification represents an upgrade in the company's trading group status.

## Key Points

- Company: Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd
- Scrip Code: 539226
- Current Group: X
- New Group: B
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The company is being moved from Group X (typically reserved for companies under surveillance or with trading restrictions) to Group B (regular trading group for listed companies). This indicates that the company has met BSE's criteria for normal trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of this group change and update their systems accordingly to reflect the new group classification for trading purposes from December 19, 2025.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Effective Date: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This group change is positive for Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd as it represents a move from a restricted trading group (X) to a regular trading group (B). This upgrade may improve liquidity and investor confidence in the stock. The impact on broader market operations is minimal as this is a single-company reclassification.
