## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that debt securities of Solar Industries India Limited will be suspended from trading effective December 22, 2025, due to the maturity of the debenture issue on December 23, 2025. The affected security is a privately placed debenture with 8.20% interest rate.

## Key Points

- Company: Solar Industries India Limited
- Scrip Code: 974468
- ISIN: INE343H08016
- Security: SIIL-8.20%-23-12-25-PVT (Privately placed debenture)
- Interest Rate: 8.20%
- Maturity Date: December 23, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective: December 22, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-782/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 22, 2025 onwards
- Members are advised to update their systems and inform clients holding these securities
- Ensure no new orders are accepted for this security after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 23, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only holders of this specific privately placed debenture issue. Investors holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the debenture issue. No impact on equity trading of Solar Industries India Limited.
