## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. The fund, which was previously closed for subscriptions, will now accept ongoing subscription and redemption requests along with systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP). This notice supersedes the previous restriction communicated in Notice No. 20251107-55.

## Key Points

- Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund reopens for ongoing transactions from December 02, 2025
- All transaction facilities available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme plans available covering Regular and Direct plans with different IDCW options
- Fund category: Equity
- Previous closure notice: 20251107-55 dated November 07, 2025

## Scheme Details

The following six plan variants are now available for transactions:

1. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Regular Plan - Growth** (INF090I01YN8)
2. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (INF090I01YO6)
3. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (INF090I01YP3)
4. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct Plan - Growth** (INF090I01YQ1)
5. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (INF090I01YR9)
6. **Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (INF090I01YS7)

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems to enable transactions in Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund
- Investors can now place orders for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP through the BSE StAR MF Platform
- All six plan variants must be made available for investor transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Reference Notice**: 20251107-55 (previous closure notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational update specific to one mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors interested in Franklin India Multi-Factor Fund. The reopening provides access to a multi-factor equity strategy that was temporarily unavailable for new subscriptions.

**Operational Impact**: BSE members and distributors need to enable the fund on their platforms for transaction processing. The multi-factor fund strategy typically focuses on systematic factors like value, momentum, quality, and low volatility to generate returns.

**Notice Details**: Notice No. 20251201-36 issued by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).
