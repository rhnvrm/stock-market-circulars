## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 25 crores) with a 15-day tenure, maturing on October 30, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,98,668 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 500 units
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 25 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730392
- **Scrip ID**: NWIL151025
- **ISIN**: INE523L14AL0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L14AL0
- Trading shall occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on the BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Tenure**: 15 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The short 15-day tenure indicates working capital financing needs. The dual high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) reflect strong creditworthiness of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. The instrument provides short-term investment opportunity for debt market participants seeking high-rated money market instruments. No significant operational or market-wide implications.
