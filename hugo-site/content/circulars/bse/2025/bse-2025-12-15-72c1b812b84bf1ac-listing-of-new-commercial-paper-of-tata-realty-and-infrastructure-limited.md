## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited on a private placement basis. The listing is effective from December 15, 2025, and comprises 5,300 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Scrip Code**: 730768
- **Scrip ID**: TRIL121225
- **ISIN**: INE371K14DD7
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,510 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 5,300 units
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 13, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE371K14DD7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with limited market-wide impact. The Commercial Paper issuance provides short-term financing to Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited with a 3-month maturity period. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate high credit quality. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors seeking short-term, highly-rated paper. Impact is confined to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
