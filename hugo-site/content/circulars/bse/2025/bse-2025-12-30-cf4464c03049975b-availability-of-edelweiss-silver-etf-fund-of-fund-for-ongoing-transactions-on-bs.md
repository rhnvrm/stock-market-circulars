## Summary

BSE has announced that EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 31, 2025. The scheme will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP). Both Regular and Direct plan variants with Growth option are included.

## Key Points

- EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from December 31, 2025
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan (Growth) and Direct Plan (Growth)
- Both plans categorized as DEBT schemes
- Regular Plan ISIN: INF754K01WD0
- Direct Plan ISIN: INF754K01VZ5
- References previous Notice No. 20251205-58

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants can resume transactions in the specified fund schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform starting December 31, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 31, 2025 - Reopening of EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors interested in this specific Edelweiss Silver ETF Fund of Fund product.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for existing and prospective investors in this fund. Provides renewed access to ongoing investment and redemption facilities along with systematic investment options.

**Operational Impact**: Enables BSE StAR MF Platform to process transactions for this fund scheme, restoring full functionality for market participants interested in silver ETF exposure through a fund of fund structure.
