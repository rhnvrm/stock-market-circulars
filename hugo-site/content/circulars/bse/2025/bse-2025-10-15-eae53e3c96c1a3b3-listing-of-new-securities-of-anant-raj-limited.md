## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of 16,616,314 new equity shares of Anant Raj Limited (Scrip Code: 515055, ISIN: INE242C01024) with effect from Thursday, October 16, 2025. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 16,616,314 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted to QIBs
- Scrip Code: 515055
- ISIN: INE242C01024
- Issue Price: Rs. 662/- per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 449293362 to 465909675
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Total QIP proceeds: Approximately Rs. 10,999.99 crores (16,616,314 shares Ã— Rs. 662)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for securities issued through Qualified Institutional Placement route.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the listing of new securities
- Members should note the new distinctive number range for the allotted shares
- For clarifications, trading members may contact Mr. Akshata Mhatre on Tel. No. 022 2272 5042

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 16, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The listing of 16.6+ million new shares through QIP will increase the total outstanding shares of Anant Raj Limited, leading to dilution for existing shareholders. The QIP at Rs. 662 per share indicates strong institutional interest. The increased float may improve liquidity in the stock.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders will experience dilution. The capital raised (approximately Rs. 11,000 crores) suggests significant growth or investment plans by the company. QIB participation indicates confidence from institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive number range. The pari-passu status ensures equal treatment of new and existing shares for all corporate actions.
