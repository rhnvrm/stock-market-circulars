## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment for the period from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025. The circular provides a detailed schedule of 23 settlement cycles (DR-174 to DR-195 for 2025-2026) with corresponding trading dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, and auction settlement details.

## Key Points

- Settlement cycle covers December 1-31, 2025 with daily rolling settlements
- 23 settlement cycles detailed (DR-174/2025-2026 through DR-195/2025-2026)
- Each settlement has corresponding depository settlement numbers (2526174-2526195)
- Auction settlements run parallel with RA-174 through RA-195 designation
- Pay-in deadline is 2:00 PM with instructions to depositories/banks required by same time
- Pay-out of funds and securities completed by 4:00 PM
- Auction pay-in time is 9:00 AM with instructions required by 8:50 AM
- T+1 settlement cycle maintained throughout the period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational calendar publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to depositories/banks by 2:00 PM for regular settlements
- Members must submit auction pay-in instructions to depositories/banks by 8:50 AM for auction settlements
- Adherence to published settlement schedule is mandatory for all EGR segment participants

## Important Dates

**Settlement Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025

**Key Settlement Dates**:
- First settlement: DR-174 (Trading: Dec 1, Pay-in/out: Dec 2)
- Last settlement in December: DR-195 (Trading: Dec 31, Pay-in/out: Jan 1, 2026)

**Daily Timings**:
- Regular pay-in: By 2:00 PM
- Regular pay-out: By 4:00 PM  
- Auction pay-in: By 9:00 AM (instructions by 8:50 AM)

**Note**: Schedule accounts for weekends and December 25, 2025 (Christmas holiday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard operational circular that provides necessary scheduling information for EGR segment participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low to moderate for EGR segment members who need to plan their settlement operations and ensure timely submission of pay-in instructions.

**Participant Groups Affected**:
- EGR segment trading members
- Clearing members
- Depositories (NSDL/CDSL)
- Banks handling settlement transactions

**Business Continuity**: The publication ensures smooth continuation of EGR segment trading and settlement activities through December 2025 with clear timelines and expectations.
