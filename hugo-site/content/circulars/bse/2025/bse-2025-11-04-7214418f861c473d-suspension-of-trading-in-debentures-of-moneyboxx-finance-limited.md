## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE296Q07043, Code: 975220) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for payment of interest and redemption of the debentures. Trading members are strictly advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for MFL-13.15%-23-11-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE296Q07043)
- Security code: 975220
- Record date: November 7, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from November 7, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension mandated for corporate action processing related to debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE296Q07043 from the suspension date
- Trading members must inform their clients holding or intending to trade these debentures
- Compliance with record date procedures for interest payment and redemption eligibility

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Purpose Date**: November 7, 2025 (for redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity and any pending transactions in these debentures. Investors cannot exit or enter positions from November 7, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders must be aware of the suspension. Only investors holding the debentures as of the record date (November 7, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in security code 975220 and communicate the suspension to relevant clients and trading desks to prevent failed transactions.
