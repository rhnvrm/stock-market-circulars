## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED. The offer will be conducted from Friday, November 14, 2025 to Thursday, November 27, 2025. Trading Members and Custodian Members are requested to note the detailed schedule and operational requirements for participation.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: RISHABH SPECIAL YARNS LIMITED (Symbol: RISHYRN)
- **Scrip Code**: 514177
- **ISIN**: INE351D01013
- **Offer Size**: Up to 9,25,782 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 13.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Important Note**: Scrip is only listed at the Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (November 14-27, 2025)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (November 14-26, 2025)
- **Last Day Give-up**: November 27, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: November 27, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational details for an acquisition window under existing takeover regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- **Bidding Eligibility**: Trading Members can bid for Investors having shares in demat form only
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS for participation
- **File Access**: Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids will be available when confirmation is received from Merchant Banker/RTA after Issue closure
- **Reference Circulars**: Members must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The scrip is only listed at Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India, not BSE, limiting the direct impact on BSE trading activities. The offer size of up to 9,25,782 equity shares at Rs. 13.00 per share represents a relatively small transaction value of approximately Rs. 1.20 crores.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading Members and Custodian Members need to be aware of the schedule and ensure proper registration of investor UCCs. The 14-day window provides adequate time for participation.

**Investor Categories**: The offer is open to multiple categories including FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individual investors.

## Bidding Categories

- **FII**: Foreign Institutional Buyer
- **FI**: Financial Institution
- **MF**: Mutual Fund
- **IC**: Insurance Companies
- **OTH**: Other QIBs
- **NOH**: Other NIB
- **CO**: Company
- **IND**: Individual
