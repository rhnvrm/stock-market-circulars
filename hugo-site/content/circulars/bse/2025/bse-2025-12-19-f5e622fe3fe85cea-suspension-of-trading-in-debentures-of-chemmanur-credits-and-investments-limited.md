## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE051307AD4, Scrip Code: 938334) issued by CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is implemented to facilitate redemption of debentures and payment of interest, with the record date fixed as December 26, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED
- Debenture Series: CCIL-ZC-9-1-26-NCD
- ISIN: INE051307AD4
- Scrip Code: 938334
- Record Date: December 26, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt securities approaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures (INE051307AD4) from December 26, 2025
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. This is a routine corporate action related to debt servicing and redemption. Debenture holders whose names appear on the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. The suspension ensures orderly settlement without trading interference during the redemption process. No broader market impact expected as this affects only a single NCD series from one issuer.
