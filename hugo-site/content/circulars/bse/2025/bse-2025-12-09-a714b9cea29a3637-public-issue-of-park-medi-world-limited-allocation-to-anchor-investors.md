## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Park Medi World Limited. This allocation represents the first stage of the IPO process where qualified institutional buyers are allocated shares at a predetermined price before the issue opens to other investors.

## Key Points

- Company: Park Medi World Limited
- Allocation made to anchor investors as part of the IPO process
- Anchor investor allocation precedes the main public offering
- Details of anchor investors and allocation amounts are documented in the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- The allocated shares are subject to a minimum lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment
- Anchor investors cannot withdraw their bids after allocation

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 9, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation date: As specified in the circular
- Public issue opening date: To follow anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has medium market impact as it represents institutional investor interest in Park Medi World Limited's IPO. The anchor allocation provides price discovery and confidence to retail investors ahead of the main public issue. The success of anchor allocation often indicates the potential demand and reception of the IPO in the broader market.
