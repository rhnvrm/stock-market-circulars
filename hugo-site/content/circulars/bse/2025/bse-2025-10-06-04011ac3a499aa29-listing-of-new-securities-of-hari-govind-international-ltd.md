## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 28,57,500 new equity shares of Hari Govind International Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531971) issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each on preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters. Trading commences from Tuesday, October 7, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in provisions.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 28,57,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue type: Preferential allotment to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share (at par)
- Date of allotment: August 14, 2025
- Trading commencement: October 7, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 5000001 to 7857500
- ISIN: INE167F01018
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Lock-in Details

- 15,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5000001 to 6500000): Locked-in until April 15, 2027
- 13,57,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 6500001 to 7857500): Locked-in until April 15, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 7, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial)**: April 15, 2026 (13,57,500 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Full)**: April 15, 2027 (15,00,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares with minimal market impact. The lock-in provisions ensure promoter commitment over the medium term. Trading members can execute transactions in these securities from October 7, 2025.
