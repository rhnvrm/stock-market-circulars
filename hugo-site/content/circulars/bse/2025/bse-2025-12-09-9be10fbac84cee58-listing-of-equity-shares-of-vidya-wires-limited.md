## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of VIDYA WIRES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544633, symbol VIDYAWIRES, and ISIN number INE14UN01029. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: VIDYA WIRES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544633
- Symbol: VIDYAWIRES
- ISIN Number: INE14UN01029
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for VIDYA WIRES LIMITED and await the separate notice regarding the listing date and security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing to prepare for future trading activities. The announcement affects only VIDYA WIRES LIMITED and does not have broader market implications. Investors interested in this security should await the specific listing date announcement.
