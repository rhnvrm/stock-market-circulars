## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED's 13.15% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE296Q07043, Scrip Code: 975220) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Security affected: MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED MFL-13.15%-23-11-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE296Q07043
- Scrip Code: 975220
- Record date: November 7, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: November 7, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-751/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and record date
- No buy or sell transactions should be executed in these securities after the cut-off date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 7, 2025 (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension eliminates secondary market liquidity for these debentures. Existing holders cannot exit positions after November 7, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. No trading activity possible post-suspension.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members must update systems to prevent transactions in this security and inform clients holding these debentures about the redemption process.
