## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of equity shares of METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544637) from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 29, 2025. This follows the exchange's earlier notice dated December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED-SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544637
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, December 29, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251212-2
- Reference Notice: 20251211-50 dated December 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the regular Rolling segment, allowing for more flexible trading mechanisms including intraday trading, carry forward positions, and normal settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the segment change for METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025 (Monday)
- **Reference Notice Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This segment change is typically positive for the stock as it indicates improved compliance and market confidence. The move from Trade for Trade (restrictive segment) to Rolling segment allows:

- Enhanced liquidity through intraday trading
- Normal settlement cycles instead of compulsory delivery
- Increased trading flexibility for market participants
- Better price discovery mechanism

Trading members and investors should adjust their trading strategies accordingly from December 29, 2025.
