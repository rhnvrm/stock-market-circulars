## Summary

BSE has listed new non-convertible debentures issued by Standard Chartered Capital Limited on a private placement basis effective December 12, 2025. The issue comprises 25,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 7.3% per annum and maturing on January 9, 2029. The securities carry CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 25,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture (Total issue size: Rs. 250 crore)
- Scrip Code: 977366, Scrip ID: 73SCCL29
- ISIN: INE403G07111
- Interest Rate: 7.3% per annum (payable annually)
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Maturity Date: January 9, 2029
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G07111
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for complete details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 10, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: Annually until January 9, 2029
- **Redemption Date**: January 9, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine privately placed debt issuance by a well-rated financial institution (AAA rated). The securities are intended for institutional investors and will trade on the debt segment with limited retail participation.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members have access to new AAA-rated debt securities for portfolio diversification. The high face value (Rs. 1 lakh) and private placement nature indicate institutional-focused investment product.

**Issuer Profile**: Standard Chartered Capital Limited, part of the Standard Chartered Group, has raised Rs. 250 crore through this issuance at competitive rates given the AAA credit rating from both CRISIL and ICRA.
