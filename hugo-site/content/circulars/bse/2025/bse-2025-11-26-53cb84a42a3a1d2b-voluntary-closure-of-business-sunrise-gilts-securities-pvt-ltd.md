## Summary

BSE has notified that Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 4071) will voluntarily cease operations in the OBPP (Odd Lot Buying and Selling Platform) segment effective November 27, 2025. This is an administrative closure with no impact on other market participants.

## Key Points

- Sunrise Gilts & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 4071) is voluntarily closing business
- Closure applies to OBPP segment only
- Effective date: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This notice is informational only.

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of cessation of business in OBPP segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single member's voluntary closure in the OBPP segment, which handles odd lot transactions. No disruption expected to overall market operations or other trading members.
