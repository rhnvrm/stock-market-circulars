## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Limited worth Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (5,000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730031, Scrip ID: HSL140825
- ISIN: INE700G14PH0
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with no significant market impact. Provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited with 3-month tenure at competitive rates.
