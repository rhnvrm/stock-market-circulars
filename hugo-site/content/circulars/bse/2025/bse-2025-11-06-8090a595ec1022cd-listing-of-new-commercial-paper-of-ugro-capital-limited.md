## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Ugro Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective November 6, 2025. The listing comprises 200 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment. Yes Bank Limited is serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 200 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 10 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,78,525.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730497
- Scrip ID: UCL041125
- ISIN: INE583D14725
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ (indicating high credit quality)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D14725
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must comply with the specified tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarifications should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 6, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date:** May 5, 2026 (6-month tenure)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of short-term debt instruments affecting only debt segment participants.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Standard Commercial Paper listing with no special conditions or operational changes required for trading members.

**Investor Impact:** Provides debt market participants with an additional IND A1+ rated short-term investment option from Ugro Capital Limited with approximately 6-month maturity.
