## Summary

BSE Surveillance issued queries to three companies regarding significant price/volume movements in their securities. City Union Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532210), Contil India Limited (Scrip Code: 531067), and Hitachi Energy India Limited (Scrip Code: 543187, NSE: POWERINDIA) responded confirming they have made all necessary disclosures under SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 and have no undisclosed material information that could affect price/volume behavior.

## Key Points

- City Union Bank Limited confirmed timely disclosure of all material information under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- City Union Bank stated no undisclosed information/announcement exists that may bear on price/volume behavior
- Contil India Limited clarified the price movement is purely market-driven and management is not connected with share price movements
- Contil India Limited reiterated commitment to compliance with Listing Regulations and applicable laws
- Hitachi Energy India Limited confirmed it intimates exchanges of all events/information having bearing on operations/performance as per Regulation 30
- All three companies responded to BSE Surveillance letters dated November 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular documents routine surveillance monitoring and company responses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE Surveillance queries regarding abnormal price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies must disclose if any undisclosed material information exists that could impact share price/volume
- Timely and accurate disclosure of all material information affecting operations/performance is mandatory

## Important Dates

- November 6, 2025: Date of clarification letters from all three companies
- November 6, 2025: Date of BSE Surveillance queries (City Union Bank ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/657; Contil India ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/656; Hitachi Energy ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/658)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications confirming no undisclosed material information exists. The responses indicate normal market activity rather than company-specific developments.

**Operational Impact:** No operational changes for the companies or market participants.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency that recent price/volume movements are market-driven without underlying undisclosed corporate actions or material events. Confirms companies are compliant with disclosure norms.

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates BSE's active surveillance mechanism and companies' adherence to disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations.
