## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544608, symbol EMMVEE, and ISIN number INE1C6T01020. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited
- Scrip Code: 544608
- Trading Symbol: EMMVEE
- ISIN: INE1C6T01020
- Listing status: Approved, date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the scrip details for the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage beyond awareness of the new security that will be available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice
- **Details Communication**: Pending separate notification

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new solar/photovoltaic power company to BSE's equity segment. Medium impact for:
- Investors interested in renewable energy sector stocks
- Market participants tracking new IPO listings
- Portfolio managers considering green energy investments

Limited immediate trading impact as the exact listing date is pending announcement. Market participants should await the follow-up notice with specific listing date and trading commencement details.
