## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544585, symbol SIHORA, and ISIN number INE0SFA01011. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544585
- Trading Symbol: SIHORA
- ISIN: INE0SFA01011
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details for SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED and prepare systems for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a single SME company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in SME securities. No immediate trading or market-wide operational impact until the actual listing date is announced. Investors and traders should await further notification for the specific listing date and security details before any trading activity can commence.
