## Summary

BSE announces that equity shares of ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544500 and symbol ABRIL with ISIN number INE15MX01014.

## Key Points

- Company Name: ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544500
- Trading Symbol: ABRIL
- ISIN Number: INE15MX01014
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 4, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate impact as this is a standard new listing announcement. Impact will depend on the company's size, business model, and market reception once trading begins. Being in the SME segment, initial trading volumes may be moderate.
