## Summary

Purple Wave Infocom Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 following the listing of its equity shares post-IPO on December 4, 2025. The company has a total of 9,246,000 shares with promoter and promoter group holding 6,649,955 shares (71.92%) and public shareholders holding 2,596,045 shares (28.08%).

## Key Points

- Total number of shareholders: 587 (3 promoters/promoter group + 584 public shareholders)
- Total equity shares: 9,246,000 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 6,649,955 shares (71.92%)
- Public shareholding: 2,596,045 shares (28.08%)
- Locked-in shares exist for promoter holdings
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts issued
- No shares pledged or encumbered
- No significant beneficial owners declared

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard compliance filing under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Purple Wave Infocom Limited must maintain compliance with Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Company is required to file shareholding pattern post-IPO and subsequently on a quarterly basis
- Promoter shares are subject to lock-in requirements as per IPO regulations

## Important Dates

- **December 4, 2025**: Date of shareholding pattern filing (post-IPO)
- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) for post-issue shareholding disclosure

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine disclosure for a newly listed company completing its IPO process. The shareholding pattern shows standard post-IPO structure with majority promoter holding (71.92%) and minimum public float (28.08%) meeting SEBI requirements.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required. This is an informational disclosure providing transparency on ownership structure to market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides baseline shareholding information for investors interested in Purple Wave Infocom Limited. The locked-in promoter shares indicate commitment while meeting regulatory requirements for newly listed companies.
