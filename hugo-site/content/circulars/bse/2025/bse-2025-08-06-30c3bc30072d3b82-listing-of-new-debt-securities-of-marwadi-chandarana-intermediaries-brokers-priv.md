## Summary

BSE has listed new zero coupon debt securities of MARWADI CHANDARANA INTERMEDIARIES BROKERS PRIVATE LIMITED worth ₹20 crore on its Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are effective for trading from August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- 20,000 securities listed with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976985, Scrip ID: 0MCIBPL27
- ISIN: INE0K0Y08011
- Credit Rating: IVR A-/Stable
- Zero coupon bonds with cumulative interest payment
- Market lot size: 1 security
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members must use the specified ISIN number
- Reference Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 5, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 6, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: August 5, 2027 (Cumulative)
- Redemption Date: August 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a 2-year investment option from a rated issuer. No put/call options available, limiting flexibility for investors.
