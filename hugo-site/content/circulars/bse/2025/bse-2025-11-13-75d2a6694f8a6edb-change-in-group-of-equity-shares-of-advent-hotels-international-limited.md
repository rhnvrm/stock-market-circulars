## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip Code: 544446) will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Thursday, November 27, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Advent Hotels International Limited
- Scrip Code: 544446
- New Trading Group: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251113-1
- Notice Date: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved to BSE's Group 'B' trading classification. Group 'B' typically includes companies that meet certain criteria different from Group 'A' or other groups, which may affect trading parameters, settlement procedures, or liquidity conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for Advent Hotels International Limited
- Trading in the new group will commence from November 27, 2025
- Members should update their systems and processes to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date for trading under Group 'B'

## Impact Assessment

The change in trading group classification may affect:
- Trading conditions and parameters for the stock
- Liquidity and settlement requirements
- Risk management and margin requirements for trading members
- Investor access and trading strategies

Trading members and investors should be aware of the differences in trading conditions between groups and adjust their operations accordingly.
