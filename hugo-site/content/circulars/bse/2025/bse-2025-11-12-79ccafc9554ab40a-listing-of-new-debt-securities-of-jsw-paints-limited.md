## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by JSW Paints Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 12, 2025. The securities totaling 330,000 units (Rs. 3,30,00,000) with ICRA AA-/Stable rating will be traded in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 330,000 units
- Scrip Code: 977287
- Scrip ID: 365JPL35
- ISIN: INE754Y08027
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA-/Stable
- Interest Rate: 3.65% per annum
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE754Y08027
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from November 10, 2026 to November 10, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing of debt securities on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are restricted to institutional investors and do not impact retail trading.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors participating in the private placement. The ICRA AA-/Stable rating indicates high credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of financial obligations.

**Liquidity**: The securities have a standard 5-year tenure with annual interest payments and no put/call options, providing predictable cash flows for investors.
