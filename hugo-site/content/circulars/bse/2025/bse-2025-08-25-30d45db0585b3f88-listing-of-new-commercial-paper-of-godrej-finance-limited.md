## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, total 4000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 492,050.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730081, Scrip ID: GFL25825
- ISIN Number: INE02KN14580
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialised form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN Number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 25, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for a finance company. The securities provide short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment with high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA).
