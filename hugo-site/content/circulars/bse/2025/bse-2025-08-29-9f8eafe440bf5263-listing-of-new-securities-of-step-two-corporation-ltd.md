## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 31,47,500 new equity shares of Step Two Corporation Ltd (Scrip Code: 531509) issued on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on September 1, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 31,47,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 5/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 15/- per share
- Allotment date: April 9, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 1, 2025
- ISIN: INE623D01015
- Distribution numbers: 5052101 to 8199600

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for preferentially allotted shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new share listing
- Lock-in periods applicable as per SEBI regulations
- Shares subject to standard trading rules and regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 1, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (13 lakh shares)**: March 25, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (18.47 lakh shares)**: April 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares. The lock-in provisions ensure gradual release of shares into the market, preventing immediate dilution effects. Trading members can facilitate transactions in these new shares from September 1, 2025.
