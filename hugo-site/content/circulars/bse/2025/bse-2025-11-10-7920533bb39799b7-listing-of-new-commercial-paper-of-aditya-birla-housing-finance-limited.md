## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) on private placement basis. The listing comprises 7000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 3,500 crore. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 7000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,627 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730508
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF061125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FJ7 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FJ7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at BSE on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of commercial paper on the debt segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard debt trading procedures apply. The securities are available for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market.

**Liquidity**: The A1+ credit rating from both CRISIL and ICRA indicates high creditworthiness, which may attract institutional investors seeking short-term investment options. The 90-day maturity period provides a liquid short-term investment vehicle.

**Issuer Perspective**: Provides short-term funding at competitive rates for Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited to meet working capital requirements.
