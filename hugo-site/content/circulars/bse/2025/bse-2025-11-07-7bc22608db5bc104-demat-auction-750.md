## Summary

BSE has issued a demat auction notice for Settlement No. 750 dated November 7, 2025. The circular lists 109 securities from various companies with their respective scrip codes and auction quantities. This is a routine settlement auction for securities where delivery obligations were not met in regular settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 750
- Date: November 7, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 109 scrips across various sectors
- Includes major companies like TCS (516 shares), Tata Power (6,409 shares), Tata Steel (4,996 shares), Asian Paints (614 shares), and Standard Capital (5,750,963 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including IT, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, hotels, infrastructure, and financial services
- Quantities range from 1 share to over 5.7 million shares (Standard Capital)
- Notable large quantity auctions: Standard Capital (5,750,963 shares), Tata Power (6,409 shares), Saianand Comm (5,401 shares), Tata Steel (4,996 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the auction settlement
- Members must ensure settlement of auctioned securities as per BSE auction settlement guidelines
- Members failing to deliver securities will be subject to auction and associated charges
- Compliance with BSE settlement and clearing regulations mandatory

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** November 7, 2025 (Settlement No. 750)
- Auction participants must complete settlement as per BSE's standard auction timeline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 109 securities across multiple sectors, indicating delivery failures in regular settlement.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members with delivery obligations must arrange funds/securities for auction settlement. Late delivery charges and auction differentials applicable.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Investors who sold securities but whose brokers failed to deliver will see settlement through auction mechanism. May impact broker-client relationships if charges are passed on.

**Liquidity Impact:** Low to Medium - Large quantities in some scrips (particularly Standard Capital with 5.75 million shares) may temporarily affect market liquidity and prices during auction.

**Systemic Risk:** Low - Standard settlement mechanism to ensure market integrity and complete settlement cycles.
