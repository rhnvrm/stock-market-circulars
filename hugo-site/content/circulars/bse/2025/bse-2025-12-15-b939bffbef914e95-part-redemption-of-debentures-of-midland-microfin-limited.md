## Summary

MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED has fixed the record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures and payment of interest. Trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value from December 26, 2025. The debentures (ISIN: INE884Q07715, Code: 975816, Series: MML-8.69%-11-7-26-PVT) will have a reduced face value of Rs. 28,570 per debenture effective from settlement DR-785/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED
- Debenture Code: 975816
- ISIN: INE884Q07715
- Series: MML-8.69%-11-7-26-PVT
- Record Date: December 27, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 28,570 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from December 26, 2025
- All transactions from the effective date onwards must reflect the new face value of Rs. 28,570 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **December 26, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-785/2025-2026)
- **December 27, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders and traders of MIDLAND MICROFIN LIMITED debentures. The part redemption reduces the outstanding face value, which will impact the liquidity and trading characteristics of these debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the record date. Post-redemption, the reduced face value will affect future interest calculations and the principal amount outstanding.

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems and member platforms need to update the face value from December 26, 2025 to ensure accurate pricing and settlement of transactions.
