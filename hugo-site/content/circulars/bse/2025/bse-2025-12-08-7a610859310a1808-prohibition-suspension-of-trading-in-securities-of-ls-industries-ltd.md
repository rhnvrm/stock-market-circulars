## Summary

BSE has prohibited/suspended trading in the securities of LS Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 514446) effective from December 9, 2025. This action has been taken as an interim measure in the interest of investors and the securities market. All trading members and investors are advised to take note of this suspension.

## Key Points

- Company affected: LS Industries Ltd
- Scrip Code: 514446
- Action: Trading prohibited/suspended
- Effective date: December 9, 2025
- Nature: Interim measure
- Reason: In the interest of investors and securities market
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a temporary trading suspension imposed as an interim protective measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in LS Industries Ltd securities from December 9, 2025
- Investors are requested to take note of the suspension
- All market participants must comply with the trading prohibition immediately upon effectiveness

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 9, 2025 (trading suspension begins)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete halt in trading activity for LS Industries Ltd shares. Existing shareholders cannot exit positions and new investors cannot enter.

**Investor Impact**: High - Liquidity completely frozen for all shareholders. This indicates potential concerns about the company that warranted immediate regulatory intervention.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block orders for scrip code 514446. The interim nature suggests investigation or review is ongoing, with potential for resumption or further action based on findings.
