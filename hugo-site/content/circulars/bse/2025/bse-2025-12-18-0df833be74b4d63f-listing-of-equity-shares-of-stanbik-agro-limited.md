## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of STANBIK AGRO LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544659, symbol STANBIK, and ISIN number INE16QA01011. This is a company-related notice for the SME segment, with the exact listing date to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- STANBIK AGRO LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544659
- Symbol: STANBIK
- ISIN: INE16QA01011
- Exact listing date will be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the separate notice with listing date and security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The circular serves as an advance notification to trading members about the new SME listing. Actual trading will commence only after the listing date is formally announced in a subsequent notice. Impact is limited to traders and investors interested in SME segment stocks.
