## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Tata Consumer Products Limited (Scrip Code: 500800), which were previously kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade effective November 25, 2025. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 276 equity shares of Re. 1/- each being listed
- Shares were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme
- ISIN: INE192A01025
- Shares divided into two tranches: 185 shares (DN 632473163-632473347) and 91 shares (DN 632706589-632706679)
- New shares rank pari passu with old equity shares
- Trading permitted from November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for shares previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and should update their systems to reflect the additional shares available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Listing and Trading Effective Date**: November 25, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 276 shares out of Tata Consumer Products Limited's total outstanding equity, representing a negligible percentage. The shares were previously kept in abeyance as part of a corporate scheme and are now being released for trading. No dilution or fresh issuance is involved.
