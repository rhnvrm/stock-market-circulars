## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251219-8 suspending trading in commercial paper of 20 companies that have fixed record dates for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these instruments from the specified no-dealings dates between December 23-30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total of 20 commercial paper instruments affected across multiple issuers
- Suspensions scheduled between December 23, 2025 and December 30, 2025
- Record dates set between December 23, 2025 and December 30, 2025
- All suspensions are for redemption of commercial paper
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these instruments from respective no-dealings dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for scheduled commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial paper instruments from the specified no-dealings dates
- All trading activity in these instruments must cease as per the schedule provided
- Members should ensure their systems are updated to prevent trading in these securities

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Second set of suspensions (6 instruments)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects debt market liquidity temporarily for 20 commercial paper instruments from major corporate and financial entities including blue-chip companies like Larsen & Toubro, Adani Enterprises, Bajaj Finance, and Avenue Supermarts.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading members need to update systems and ensure compliance with no-dealings restrictions. Standard year-end redemption activity as companies settle their short-term debt obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Routine corporate action for maturing commercial paper. Investors holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds as per schedule. No unexpected disruptions anticipated.
