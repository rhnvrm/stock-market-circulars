## Summary

Admach Systems Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the allotment of equity shares post-IPO on December 30, 2025. The company has 6 promoters holding 3,586,366 shares (52.94%) and 952 public shareholders holding 3,186,434 shares (47.06%), with locked-in shares present for both promoter and public categories.

## Key Points

- Total shareholders: 958 (6 promoters + 952 public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 3,586,366 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 3,186,434 equity shares
- Total equity shares: 6,772,800
- Promoter shareholding percentage: 52.94%
- Public shareholding percentage: 47.06%
- Locked-in shares exist for both promoter and public categories
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts issued
- No shares pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure requirement under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities must file shareholding patterns under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern must be disclosed to stock exchanges
- Declaration regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, locked-in shares, pledged shares, and other encumbrances must be provided
- Shareholding data must specify promoter, public, and non-promoter-non-public categories

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Post-IPO allotment date and shareholding pattern filing date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory filing with no immediate market impact. The shareholding pattern shows standard post-IPO distribution with promoters retaining majority control at 52.94% and public shareholders holding 47.06%. The presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with IPO lock-in requirements. This information is primarily for transparency and investor awareness regarding the ownership structure of the newly listed Admach Systems Limited.
