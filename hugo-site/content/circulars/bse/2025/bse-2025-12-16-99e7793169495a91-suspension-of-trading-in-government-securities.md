## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Government Security GS19DEC25 (Scrip Code: 800591, ISIN: IN001225C092) effective from December 17, 2025, as the security is scheduled to mature on its redemption date of December 19, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal with this government security from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Government Security GS19DEC25 will mature on December 19, 2025
- Scrip Code: 800591
- ISIN: IN001225C092
- Trading suspension effective from December 17, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251216-5
- Reference: DR-779/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for the suspension of trading in maturing government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified government security (Scrip Code: 800591) from December 17, 2025 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 17, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **December 19, 2025**: Redemption/maturity date of the government security

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only one government security that is approaching its natural maturity date. Trading members holding or planning to trade this specific security need to be aware of the suspension timeline. No broader market implications are expected as this is standard procedure for securities reaching redemption.
