## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The listing comprises 40,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 20,000 crores. These securities carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730629
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL211125
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EJ9
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,583 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 40,000 units (Rs. 20,000 crores aggregate)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new debt instrument listing on BSE's Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these Commercial Papers only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EJ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size of 1 unit must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to BSE's Debt segment. The listing provides additional trading opportunities for debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now trade these Commercial Papers through BSE's Debt segment platform. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.

**Investor Impact**: Short-term investment opportunity for institutional and qualified investors in Commercial Papers of a major retail conglomerate with a 3-month maturity period. The discount-based issuance (issue price Rs. 4,92,583 vs. face value Rs. 5,00,000) provides implicit yield to investors.
