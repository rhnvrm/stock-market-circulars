## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 161/2025-2026 dated November 24, 2025 provides details of book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple debt securities. The bulletin covers interest payments and redemptions for privately placed non-convertible debentures, municipal bonds, and commercial papers scheduled between December 2025 and March 2026.

## Key Points

- A.K. Capital Finance Ltd. has multiple record dates for interest payments and partial redemptions across various NCD series
- Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation has record date of December 5, 2025 for interest payment and partial bond redemption
- HDFC Securities Limited commercial paper redemption scheduled for December 9, 2025
- Inbrew Beverages Private Limited has record dates for interest payments and partial NCD redemptions
- Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited has multiple commercial paper redemptions scheduled between December 1-29, 2025
- All securities will continue trading (trading flag = 1) during the record date periods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors holding eligible securities on the respective record dates will be entitled to receive interest payments or participate in redemptions
- No action required from investors; entitlements will be processed automatically based on holdings on record dates
