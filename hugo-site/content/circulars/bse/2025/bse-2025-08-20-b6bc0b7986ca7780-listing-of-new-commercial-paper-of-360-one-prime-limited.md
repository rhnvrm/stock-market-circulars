## Summary

BSE has listed two new series of commercial paper issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025. Both instruments have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper series listed on BSE Debt segment
- Series 1: 2,000 units, redemption on November 19, 2025 (ISIN: INE248U14SD4)
- Series 2: 6,000 units, redemption on November 18, 2025 (ISIN: INE248U14SC6)
- Issue price approximately Rs. 4.91 lakhs per unit for both series
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption Date (Series 1): November 19, 2025
- Redemption Date (Series 2): November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the BSE Debt segment.
