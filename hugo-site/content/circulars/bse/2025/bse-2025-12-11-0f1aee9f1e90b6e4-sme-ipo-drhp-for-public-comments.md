## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of RCRS Innovation Limited for public comments. The document, filed on December 10, 2025, contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days for stakeholder comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by RCRS Innovation Limited on December 10, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosure requirements
- Available on BSE SME website for public review and comments
- Stakeholders can submit comments via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Comment period: 21 days from notice date (December 11, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for IPO draft document review as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and stakeholders should review the Draft Offer Document available at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 11, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by RCRS Innovation Limited
- **December 11, 2025**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **January 1, 2026** (approximately): Last date for submission of public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal immediate market impact as this is a procedural notice for an upcoming SME IPO.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to prospective investors, merchant bankers, and stakeholders interested in RCRS Innovation Limited's public offering.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency and opportunity for public feedback on the draft offer document before finalization, enabling informed investment decisions for potential IPO participants.
