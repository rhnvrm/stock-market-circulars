## Summary

BSE has published the daily settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for January 2026. The notice outlines the settlement dates corresponding to each trading day during the month, with settlements typically occurring on T+1 basis.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers the period from January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- Daily settlements follow standard T+1 pattern (settlement on the next business day)
- Last settlement of January 2026 (for January 30 trades) occurs on February 2, 2026
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.
- No trading/settlement holidays observed during the covered period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational schedule notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should:
- Note the settlement dates for planning their F&O trading activities
- Ensure adequate funds/securities are available on settlement dates
- Align their internal systems with the published settlement calendar

## Important Dates

**Settlement Period:** January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026

**Key Settlement Dates:**
- First settlement: January 2, 2026 (for January 1 trades)
- Last settlement: February 2, 2026 (for January 30 trades)

**Trading days with weekend/holiday gap:**
- January 24-26, 2026: No trading on January 24-25 (weekend), next settlement on January 27

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine administrative notice

**Operational Impact:** Standard settlement procedures apply with no changes to existing processes. Market participants should incorporate this calendar into their operational planning for January 2026.

**Stakeholder Impact:** All F&O segment participants including traders, clearing members, and institutional investors should reference this schedule for settlement planning.
