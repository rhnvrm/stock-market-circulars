## Summary

BSE announced the addition of STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544599) to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, November 11, 2025. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Friday, November 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED listed on BSE on Friday, November 07, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544599
- Will be added to BSE IPO Index effective November 11, 2025 at market open
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to prior Notice No: 20251106-50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance action.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and passive funds tracking the BSE IPO Index may need to rebalance their portfolios.

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED listing effective date on BSE
- **November 11, 2025**: Addition to BSE IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard index maintenance procedure following a new IPO listing. Investors tracking or replicating the BSE IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios to include STUDDS ACCESSORIES LIMITED. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.
