## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Papers issued on private placement basis by six companies totaling Rs. 1,125 Crores. Five fresh listings include Julius Baer Capital (India) Pvt Ltd (Rs. 100 Cr), Tata Housing Development Company Ltd (Rs. 200 Cr), Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Ltd (Rs. 150 Cr), 360 ONE WAM Ltd (Rs. 175 Cr), and Bajaj Finance Ltd (Rs. 350 Cr). One further listing for DSP Finance Pvt Ltd (Rs. 150 Cr) is also included.

## Key Points

- All securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- All Commercial Papers carry A1+ credit ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, IND, or CARE
- Interest rates range from 6.29% to 7.21% p.a. (cumulative)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for all issuances

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard debt market listing notice for privately placed Commercial Papers by established financial institutions and corporates. All issuers have strong A1+ credit ratings. The Commercial Papers have short tenures (3-6 months) and will primarily be traded by institutional investors in the debt market segment. No impact on equity markets or general retail investors.
