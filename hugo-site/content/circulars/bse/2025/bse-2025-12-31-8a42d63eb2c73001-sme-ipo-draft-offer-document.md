## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days for stakeholder review and feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments on the Draft Offer Document must submit feedback within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- No compliance obligations for existing listed companies or market participants

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from December 31, 2025 (until January 21, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document filing with minimal market impact. The notice primarily serves informational purposes for potential investors and stakeholders interested in the upcoming KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED IPO. No immediate action required from existing market participants or listed companies. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations.
