## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy (Buyback) through Acquisition Window for Morganite Crucible India Ltd. The buyback will be conducted from December 31, 2025 to January 13, 2026, offering to purchase up to 14,00,000 equity shares at Rs. 1557.15 per share. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through the iBBS platform and RTRMS system.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Morganite Crucible India Ltd (Symbol: MORGANITE, Scrip Code: 523160)
- **ISIN**: INE599F01020
- **Offer Size**: Up to 14,00,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 1557.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Bid Period**: December 31, 2025 (9:15 AM) to January 13, 2026 (3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: December 31, 2025 to January 12, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 13, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 13, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action (buyback) being conducted through BSE's established Offer to Buy mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (dated September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Members should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Wednesday, December 31, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: December 31, 2025 to January 13, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: December 31, 2025 to January 12, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 13, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 13, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM extended)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific buyback affecting only Morganite Crucible India Ltd shareholders. The offer size of 14,00,000 shares represents a relatively small transaction.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Buyers (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Non-Institutional Investors (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).
