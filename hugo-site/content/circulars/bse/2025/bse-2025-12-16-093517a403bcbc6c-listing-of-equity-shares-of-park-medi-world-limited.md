## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that equity shares of PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544645, trading symbol PARKHOSPS, and ISIN number INE119201023. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544645
- Trading Symbol: PARKHOSPS
- ISIN Number: INE119201023
- Status: Approved for listing, date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN number for PARK MEDI WORLD LIMITED and await further notice regarding the actual listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice
