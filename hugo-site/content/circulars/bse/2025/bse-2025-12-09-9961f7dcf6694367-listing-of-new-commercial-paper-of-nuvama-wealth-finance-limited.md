## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 9, 2025. The listing consists of 3500 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 3500 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Scrip Code: 730724, Scrip ID: NWFL081225, Detail Name: NWFL-9-3-26-CP
- ISIN Number: INE918K14CV9
- Issue Price: Rs. 490942.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CV9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** December 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the BSE Debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The three-month tenure and private placement nature suggest this is a standard working capital instrument with no significant operational or market-wide implications.
