## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Gallard Steel Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544616, symbol GALLARD, and ISIN number INE0BUQ01012. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Gallard Steel Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544616
- Symbol: GALLARD
- ISIN: INE0BUQ01012
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued on November 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the forthcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement for the SME segment with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands investment opportunities in the steel sector for SME investors. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details once the final listing date is confirmed.
