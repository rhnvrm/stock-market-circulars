## Summary

DMR Hydroengineering & Infrastructures Limited (Scrip Code: 543410) has applied to BSE for record date approval to issue 63,82,216 bonus equity shares under SEBI regulations. The bonus shares will have distinctive numbers 39,88,886 to 1,03,71,101 with deemed allotment date of August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: DMR Hydroengineering & Infrastructures Limited
- BSE Scrip Code: 543410
- ISIN: INE07L501010
- Number of bonus shares: 63,82,216 equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 39,88,886 to 1,03,71,101
- Application submitted by Company Secretary Ravinder Kumar Bhatia

## Regulatory Changes

Bonus issue being conducted under SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Record date approval required from BSE SME Platform
- Compliance with SEBI bonus issue regulations
- Proper documentation and certification by Company Secretary

## Important Dates

- Deemed date of allotment: Friday, August 29, 2025
- Effective listing date: Monday, September 1, 2025
- Application date: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for existing shareholders who will receive additional shares at no cost. The bonus issue will increase the number of shares outstanding and may improve liquidity. Standard corporate action that demonstrates company's confidence in future prospects.
