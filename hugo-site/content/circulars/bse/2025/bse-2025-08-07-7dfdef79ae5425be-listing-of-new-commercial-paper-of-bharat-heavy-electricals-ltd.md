## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) worth Rs. 1,000 crores on private placement basis. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and IND A1+ with a 53-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 20,000 units (total value Rs. 1,000 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729975 (BHEL70825)
- ISIN: INE257A14AB9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 495,631.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 7, 2025
- Listing Date: August 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 29, 2025
- Maturity Period: 53 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Standard trading parameters apply with no special conditions.
