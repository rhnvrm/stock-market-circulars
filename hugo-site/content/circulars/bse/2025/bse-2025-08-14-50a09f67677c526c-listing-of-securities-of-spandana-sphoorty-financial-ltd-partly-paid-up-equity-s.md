## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 1,73,34,362 partly paid-up equity shares of Spandana Sphoorty Financial Ltd issued on rights basis. The securities will be listed and permitted to trade in the 'B' Group effective Monday, August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,73,34,362 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, partly paid-up of Rs. 5/-
- Issued on rights basis with allotment date of August 12, 2025
- Scrip Code: 890221, ISIN: IN9572J01019, Symbol: SSFLPP
- Issue price: Rs. 230/- (Rs. 115/- paid on application, Rs. 115/- in future calls)
- Distinctive numbers: 71305145 to 88639506
- Market lot: 1 share

## Regulatory Changes

Temporary ISIN No. IN8572J01028 suspended as per SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 regarding activation of ISIN in case of additional issue of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the new listing parameters and the suspension of temporary ISIN for compliance with SEBI regulations on additional share issues.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 18, 2025
- Notice Date: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds significant liquidity to Spandana Sphoorty Financial Ltd with over 1.73 crore partly paid-up shares. The partly paid-up nature means shareholders have future payment obligations, affecting trading dynamics and investor considerations.
