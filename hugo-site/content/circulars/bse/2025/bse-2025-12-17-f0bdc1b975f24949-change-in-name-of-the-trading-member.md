## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that the name of trading member SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6795) will be changed to "SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED" effective December 18, 2025. This is an administrative update with no impact on trading operations.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED to SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number 6795 remains unchanged
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- No impact on trading operations or member privileges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 18, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal to no impact on market operations or other trading members. This is a routine administrative name change that does not affect the member's trading rights, obligations, or member number. Other market participants should update their records accordingly for correspondence purposes.
