## Summary

HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as on December 16, 2025, following the completion of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The filing shows the company has 3 promoter/promoter group shareholders holding 13,978,115 equity shares and 992 public shareholders holding 5,304,010 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shareholders: 995 (3 promoters + 992 public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 13,978,115 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 5,304,010 equity shares
- Total equity shares: 19,282,125
- Filing date: Post-IPO as on December 16, 2025
- Company has locked-in shares (both promoter and public categories)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No pledged shares or non-disposal undertakings
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard compliance filing under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED is required to file shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) following the listing of equity shares
- The company must maintain updated records of promoter and public shareholding
- Locked-in shares must be disclosed as per regulatory requirements
- Declaration of various securities (partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, pledged shares, etc.) is mandatory

## Important Dates

- December 16, 2025: Reference date for shareholding pattern (Post-IPO)
- December 17, 2025: Circular publication date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure filing for a newly listed company following its IPO. The shareholding pattern provides transparency on ownership structure with promoters holding approximately 72.5% and public holding approximately 27.5% of total equity. The presence of locked-in shares indicates compliance with IPO lock-in requirements. No material market impact is expected as this is an informational disclosure for a new listing.
