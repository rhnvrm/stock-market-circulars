## Summary

Studds Accessories Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. This filing represents the post-issue shareholding pattern as on November 5, 2025, following the listing of equity shares on BSE.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Studds Accessories Limited
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Filing Date: Post-issue position as on 05.11.2025
- Regulation: Filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities outstanding
- No warrants issued
- No outstanding ESOPs
- No depository receipts issued
- No shares held by promoters are locked-in
- No shares held by promoters are pledged or encumbered
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure filing required under existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities are required to submit shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and encumbrances must be provided
- Post-issue shareholding pattern must be filed following IPO listing

## Important Dates

- Post-Issue Date: November 5, 2025
- Filing Date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This filing represents the completion of Studds Accessories Limited's IPO listing process on BSE. The shareholding pattern disclosure provides transparency to investors regarding ownership structure post-listing. The clean shareholding structure (no encumbrances, pledges, or outstanding convertible securities) indicates a straightforward capital structure. This is a routine compliance filing required for all newly listed companies and has no immediate market impact beyond confirming successful listing completion.
