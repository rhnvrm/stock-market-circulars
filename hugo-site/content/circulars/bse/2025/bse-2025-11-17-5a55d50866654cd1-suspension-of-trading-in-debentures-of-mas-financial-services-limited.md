## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific debentures of MAS Financial Services Limited effective November 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the affected securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debentures with ISIN INE348L07191 (Scrip Code: 975719)
- Debenture series: MFSL-8.55%-6-12-25-PVT
- Record date: November 21, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 21, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 21, 2025
- Members should inform their clients holding or interested in these securities about the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 21, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 21, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and related to a scheduled corporate action (redemption and interest payment).

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders identified as of the record date will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading liquidity available from November 21, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this security and communicate the suspension to relevant clients.
