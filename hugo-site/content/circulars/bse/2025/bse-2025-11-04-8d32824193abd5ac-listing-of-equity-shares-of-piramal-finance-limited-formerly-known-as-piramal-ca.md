## Summary

Piramal Finance Limited (formerly Piramal Capital & Housing Finance Limited) has filed its quarterly shareholding pattern report for the quarter ended September 30, 2025, under Regulation 31(1)(b). The report shows total shareholding of 225,557,813 equity shares with promoter holding at 46.39% and public shareholding at 53.61%. The company is not a Public Sector Undertaking and has dematerialized 226,677,700 equity shares.

- NSE Symbol: PFL
- ISIN: INE202B01038
- Scrip Code: 123456
- Total equity shares: 225,557,813
- Promoter and Promoter Group holding: 104,635,805 shares (46.39%)
- Public shareholder holding: 120,922,008 shares (53.61%)
- Total dematerialized shares: 226,677,700
- Number of shareholders: 27
- Report filed under Regulation 31(1)(b)
