## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new debt securities issued by Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited on private placement basis, effective November 4, 2025. The listing comprises 40,000 non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE647U08039, carrying a 10.20% per annum interest rate and maturing on June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 40,000 debentures listed on BSE Debt segment with Scrip Code 977267
- Face value and issue price of Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- CARE A+/Positive credit rating
- Market lot size of 1 debenture
- Allotment date: October 31, 2025
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE647U08039
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly (from December 31, 2025 to June 30, 2027)
- **Redemption Date**: June 30, 2027
