## Summary

BSE has granted provisional empanelment to Neutrino Trading Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider for exchange trading members. The empanelment is effective immediately, and the updated list of algo providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Neutrino Trading Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled by BSE
- The company will provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Contact: +91 9766071356
- Office Location: 601, Dhantak Plaza, Makwana Road, Andheri East, Mumbai â€“ 400059
- Empanelment status: Provisional
- Effective Date: Immediate (December 5, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update adding a new vendor to the approved algo provider list.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for trading members. This is an informational circular.

Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions may now engage with Neutrino Trading Private Limited as an exchange-approved vendor.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 5, 2025 (Immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative empanelment of a service provider.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members now have an additional approved vendor option for algorithmic trading solutions. The provisional empanelment status indicates the provider is subject to ongoing evaluation.

**Reference**: The complete list of empanelled algo providers is maintained at https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
