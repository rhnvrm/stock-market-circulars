## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice regarding the part redemption of debentures of Lodha Developers Limited (scrip code: 976923, ISIN: INE670K07307). The company has updated the reduced face value following the part redemption with record date of November 14, 2025. The revised reduced face value is Rs. 92,769/- per debenture, effective from December 02, 2025.

## Key Points

- Revised reduced face value for Lodha Developers debentures announced
- Scrip Code: 976923 (LDL-Reset Rate-21-01-28-PVT)
- ISIN: INE670K07307
- Revised reduced face value: Rs. 92,769/- per debenture
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251111-48 dated November 11, 2025
- Record date for part redemption was November 14, 2025
- Trading with revised reduced face value effective from December 02, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification updating previously communicated information regarding part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised reduced face value
- Trading Members are advised that debentures will trade with the reduced face value of Rs. 92,769/- per debenture from December 02, 2025
- Trading Members of the Exchange are required to take note of this revision

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2025**: Record date for part redemption (previously announced)
- **December 01, 2025**: Notice date for revised reduced face value
- **December 02, 2025**: Effective date for trading with revised reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This revision affects holders of Lodha Developers debentures with scrip code 976923. The updated reduced face value of Rs. 92,769/- per debenture will be reflected in trading from December 02, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to update their systems to reflect the revised reduced face value for these debentures. The revision notice (DR-768/2025-2026) ensures proper valuation and trading of these securities in the debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose record date was November 14, 2025 will receive part redemption based on the revised face value calculation.
