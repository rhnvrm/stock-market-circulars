## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251208-18 informing trading members that equity shares of Creative Newtech Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544631, symbol CNL, and ISIN number INE985W01018. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Creative Newtech Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544631
- Symbol: CNL
- ISIN: INE985W01018
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details for future trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members are informed in advance to prepare for the upcoming listing. The actual trading impact will depend on the company's fundamentals, offer details, and market conditions at the time of listing. No immediate action is required until the specific listing date is announced.
