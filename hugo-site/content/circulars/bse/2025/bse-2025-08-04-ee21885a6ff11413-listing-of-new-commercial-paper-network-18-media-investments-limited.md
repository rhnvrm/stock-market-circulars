## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs 225 crore issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading in the BSE Debt segment with effect from August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 4,500 units of Rs 5 lakh each
- Total value: Rs 225 crore
- Issue price: Rs 4,92,776 per unit (face value Rs 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729955
- ISIN: INE870H14VP0
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 4, 2025
- Redemption date: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited and investment option for debt market participants.
