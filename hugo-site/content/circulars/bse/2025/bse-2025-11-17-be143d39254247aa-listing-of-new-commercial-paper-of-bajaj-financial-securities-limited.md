## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing became effective on the BSE Debt segment on November 17, 2025. A total of 9,200 securities with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been admitted to trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 9,200 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730582
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL141125
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DI2
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DI2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offers trading members an additional debt instrument with high credit rating (A1+). The listing is limited to institutional and high-net-worth investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No broader market or regulatory implications.
