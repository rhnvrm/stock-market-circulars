## Summary

BSE announced the addition of Neptune Logitek Limited (Exchange ticker 544663) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, December 23, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE effective Monday, December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited (scrip code 544663) listed on BSE on December 22, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective December 23, 2025
- Addition follows reference to Notice No. 20251219-70
- Corporate action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard index reconstitution activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025**: Neptune Logitek Limited listing effective date
- **December 23, 2025**: Stock addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME company to the relevant index. Index-tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to incorporate the new constituent. The action has been included in the standard corporate action file for automated processing by market participants.
