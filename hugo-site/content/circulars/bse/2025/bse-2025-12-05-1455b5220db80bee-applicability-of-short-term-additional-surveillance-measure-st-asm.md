## Summary

BSE has updated the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Framework effective December 08, 2025. Four securities have been newly shortlisted under the 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework, while five securities are moving out of the framework due to inclusion in other surveillance or trading mechanisms. The circular provides a consolidated list of 17 securities currently under ST-ASM with their respective stage classifications.

## Key Points

- 4 new securities added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework effective December 08, 2025
- 5 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework due to inclusion in other frameworks (LT-ASM or Trade for Trade)
- No securities moving to higher or lower stage ASM at this time
- Consolidated list contains 17 securities under ST-ASM surveillance
- Framework includes both main board and SME scrips
- Securities marked with special indicators for SME scrips (@) and T+0 scrips (~)

## Regulatory Framework

The ST-ASM Framework applies additional surveillance measures based on:
- Price movements and volatility patterns
- Trading volumes and concentration
- 5/15/30 day monitoring periods
- Progressive stage-wise restrictions

Securities can move between frameworks:
- LT-ASM (Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure)
- Trade for Trade segment
- GSM (Graded Surveillance Measure)
- ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure)
- Pledge Framework

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025
- All changes to ST-ASM applicability take effect from market opening on December 08, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities under ST-ASM are subject to additional margin requirements
- Trading restrictions may apply based on stage classification
- Market participants must ensure compliance with enhanced surveillance norms
- Investors should be aware of liquidity constraints on affected securities
- Brokers must implement appropriate risk management controls

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects 9 securities (4 additions, 5 removals) with changes in trading conditions

**Liquidity Impact**: Securities entering ST-ASM may face reduced liquidity due to higher margins and surveillance measures. Securities exiting to Trade for Trade will face complete restriction on intraday trading.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding or trading these securities need to be aware of enhanced margin requirements and potential trading restrictions. Securities in higher stages face more stringent controls.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants must update risk management systems and surveillance parameters for affected securities effective December 08, 2025.
