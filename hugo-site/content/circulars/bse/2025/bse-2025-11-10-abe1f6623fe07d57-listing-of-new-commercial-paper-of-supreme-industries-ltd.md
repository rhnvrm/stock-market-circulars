## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Supreme Industries Ltd on private placement basis with effect from November 10, 2025. The instrument comprises 2000 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying a CRISIL A1+ credit rating. The papers were allotted on November 7, 2025 and will mature on February 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 commercial papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730523
- Scrip ID: SIL071125
- ISIN: INE195A14CD3
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE195A14CD3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Supreme Industries Ltd with short-term funding for approximately 90 days. The A1+ rating indicates highest safety and reflects strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and Supreme Industries stakeholders. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
