## Summary

MUTHOOT CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE296G07283, Code: 976898) with a record date of December 23, 2025. The debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 71,428.58 per debenture effective from the same date. This corporate action includes both interest payment and partial redemption of the debt securities.

## Key Points

- Debenture Code: MCSL-10.35%-3-4-27-PVT (976898)
- ISIN: INE296G07283
- Record Date: December 23, 2025
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 71,428.58 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 23, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-783/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice informing trading members of the part redemption event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value effective from December 23, 2025
- Record date eligibility applies to debenture holders as of December 23, 2025
- Settlement will be processed under DR-783/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption
- **December 23, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-783/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Direct impact on holders of MUTHOOT CAPITAL SERVICES debentures who will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. The face value reduction reflects the partial capital return.

**Market Impact**: Limited to specific debenture holders. Trading liquidity may be affected temporarily due to face value adjustment. No broader debt market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems and member records must be updated to reflect the new face value of Rs. 71,428.58 from December 23, 2025.
