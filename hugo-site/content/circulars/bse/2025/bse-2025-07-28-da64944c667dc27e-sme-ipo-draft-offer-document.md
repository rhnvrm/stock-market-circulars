## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of BharatRohan Airborne Innovations Limited for their SME IPO, filed on July 24, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by BharatRohan Airborne Innovations Limited on July 24, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - standard SME IPO process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public to review draft offer document available on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from July 28, 2025 notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: July 24, 2025
- Notice date: July 28, 2025
- Comment period: 21 days from July 28, 2025 (until approximately August 18, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard SME IPO process. Primary impact on potential investors in BharatRohan Airborne Innovations Limited and the SME segment. The public comment period allows stakeholders to provide feedback on the draft offer document before finalization.
