## Summary

BSE announced the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The issue comprises 6000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 300 crores) and carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,725 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 300 crores
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730665
- Scrip ID: GFL271125
- ISIN: INE02KN14655
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14655
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 27, 2025
- Listing Date: November 27, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides short-term funding instrument for Godrej Finance Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to BSE debt segment participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper.
