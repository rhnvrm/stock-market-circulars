## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, November 29th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems to test their trading applications and functionalities, including exceptional market conditions. No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented. Mock trades will not create any real obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for November 29th, 2025 (Saturday)
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- Trading members can test third-party or in-house trading systems through ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS Release scheduled
- Mock trades have no real financial implications (no margins, pay-in/pay-out, rights, or liabilities)
- Feedback requested by 3:00 PM on the same day
- Active participation from all members is encouraged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine testing and familiarization session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members should provide feedback by 3:00 PM on November 29th, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries
- Members may contact their respective Relationship Manager for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **November 29th, 2025 (Saturday)**: Mock Trading Session
  - 09:15 AM - 10:00 AM: Log-in
  - 10:00 AM - 01:00 PM: Continuous Trading for Currency Derivatives
  - 10:00 AM - 01:00 PM: Continuous Trading for Cross Currency Derivatives
  - 01:10 PM: Trade Modification (Currency)
  - 01:10 PM: Trade Modification (Cross Currency)
- **3:00 PM (November 29th, 2025)**: Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market activity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Provides an opportunity for members to test and familiarize themselves with trading systems and applications. Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can verify system functionality and prepare for exceptional market conditions. No actual trades, margin obligations, or settlement obligations will be created from this session.
