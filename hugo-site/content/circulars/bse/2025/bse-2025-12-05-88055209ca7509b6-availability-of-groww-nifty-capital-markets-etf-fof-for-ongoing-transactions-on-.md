## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS ETF FOF will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 08, 2025. The scheme will support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP). This follows the previous notice 20251113-44 regarding the scheme's status.

## Key Points

- GROWW NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available for both subscription and redemption
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six plan options available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All plans are categorized under Equity scheme category
- Transactions available through BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can process transactions in the specified scheme through BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 08, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a specific mutual fund scheme. The reopening provides investment opportunities for retail and institutional investors interested in gaining exposure to the Nifty Capital Markets index through a fund-of-funds structure. The availability of multiple plan options (Regular/Direct with different dividend options) and systematic investment facilities enhances accessibility for different investor preferences. Impact is limited to investors and distributors dealing with Groww Mutual Fund products on the BSE platform.
