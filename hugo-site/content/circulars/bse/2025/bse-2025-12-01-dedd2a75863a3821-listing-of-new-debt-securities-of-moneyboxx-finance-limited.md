## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED on private placement basis, effective December 01, 2025. The securities carry a face value of Rs. 10,000 with a 10.20% per annum interest rate and mature on November 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 25,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977330
- Scrip ID: 1020MFL27
- ISIN: INE296Q07159
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Interest Rate: 10.20% per annum (monthly payment)
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Maturity Date: November 28, 2027
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296Q07159
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Redemption Date**: November 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for a single issuer on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL BBB/Stable, indicating adequate credit quality. The listing expands the debt market offerings but is not expected to significantly affect broader market operations or trading patterns. Relevant primarily for debt market participants and investors interested in MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED's debt instruments.
