## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Bajaj Finance Limited on its debt segment effective September 5, 2025. The issue consists of 11,900 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 595 crores, issued on private placement basis.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 11,900 units (total value Rs. 595 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730155, Scrip ID: BFL40925
- ISIN: INE296A14C48
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,159.50 per unit
- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors with 3-month maturity period.
