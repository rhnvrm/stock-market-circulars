## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Unipro Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 540189) will be moved to the 'X' group effective November 11, 2025. This group change will affect the trading characteristics and surveillance measures applicable to the stock.

## Key Points

- Unipro Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 540189) will be included under 'X' group
- The change will be effective from November 11, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this group change
- Contact person for clarifications: Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788)

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will be subject to 'X' group trading rules and surveillance measures, which typically include stricter monitoring and may have different trading parameters compared to other groups.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change
- Members should ensure compliance with 'X' group trading rules
- Any clarifications needed should be directed to the designated BSE contact

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 04, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group indicates that the stock will be under enhanced surveillance. This may result in increased scrutiny of trading activities, potential impact on liquidity, and different price band or trade-to-trade settlement requirements. Trading members and investors should be aware of the specific conditions applicable to 'X' group securities.
