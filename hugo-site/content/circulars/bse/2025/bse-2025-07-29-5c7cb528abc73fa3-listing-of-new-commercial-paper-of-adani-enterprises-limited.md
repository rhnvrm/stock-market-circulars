## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 530 units totaling Rs. 26.5 crores, maturing on October 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 530 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,123 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729927
- ISIN: INE423A14XW5
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 28, 2025
- Listing date: July 29, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Adani Enterprises with a 3-month maturity period. Trading restricted to institutional participants due to high denomination requirement.
