## Summary

BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544603) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, November 14, 2025, following its listing on BSE on Wednesday, November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Stock being listed on BSE: BILLIONBRAINS GARAGE VENTURES LIMITED
- Exchange ticker: 544603
- Listing date: November 12, 2025
- Index addition effective date: November 14, 2025 (at market open)
- Index affected: BSE IPO Index
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational notice for market participants and index tracking funds.

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: Stock listing on BSE
- **November 14, 2025**: Addition to BSE IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition following a new listing. Impact is limited to:
- Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE IPO Index will need to add this stock to their portfolios
- Potential buying pressure from passive funds during rebalancing
- Informational for investors tracking IPO index constituents
