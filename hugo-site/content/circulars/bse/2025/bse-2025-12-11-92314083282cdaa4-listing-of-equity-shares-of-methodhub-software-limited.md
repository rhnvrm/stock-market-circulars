## Summary

METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, following the completion of its IPO on December 11, 2025. The filing shows the company has 2,060 total shareholders with Promoter & Promoter Group holding 5,061,885 shares and Public shareholders holding 13,791,516 shares.

## Key Points

- Total number of shareholders: 2,060 (4 Promoter & Promoter Group, 2,056 Public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 5,061,885 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 13,791,516 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or outstanding ESOPs issued
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Promoter shares have lock-in provisions applicable
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard compliance filing under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing made under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure mandatory for newly listed entities
- Declaration requirements confirmed for various categories of shares and encumbrances
- Promoter shares subject to lock-in requirements as per IPO norms

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Shareholding pattern filing date (post-IPO)
- **December 11, 2025**: Effective date of listing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine disclosure for a newly listed company completing its IPO process. The filing provides transparency on the ownership structure post-listing.

**Investor Impact**: Informational for investors tracking the company's shareholding distribution. The significant public shareholding (approximately 73% based on share count) indicates good public float post-IPO.

**Operational Impact**: None. This is a compliance disclosure with no operational changes to the company's business activities.
