## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. A total of 3000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each have been listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The instruments carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a tenure of 91 days maturing on February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,91,460/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730659
- Scrip ID: FFSL251125
- ISIN: INE007N14EJ6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Allotment Date: November 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 24, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EJ6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for these securities
- Securities are traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing provides short-term debt investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The dual A1+ credit ratings from CRISIL and ICRA indicate high credit quality and strong capacity for timely payment. The 91-day tenure makes these instruments suitable for short-term liquidity management. Impact is primarily relevant to debt market participants and investors seeking exposure to Fedbank Financial Services Limited's short-term debt instruments.
