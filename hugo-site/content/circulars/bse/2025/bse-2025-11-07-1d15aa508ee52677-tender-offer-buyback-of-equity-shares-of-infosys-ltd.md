## Summary

INFOSYS LTD (Scrip Code: 500209/100209) has announced a buyback of equity shares and set the record date as November 14, 2025. This date will determine which equity shareholders are entitled and eligible to participate in the buyback offer. The ex-entitlement basis will also be effective from November 14, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-756/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: INFOSYS LTD (Scrip Code: 500209/100209)
- Record Date: November 14, 2025
- Purpose: Determine entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible for buyback participation
- Ex-Entitlement Date: November 14, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-756/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251107-68
- Segment: Equity - Dematerialised Securities (Rolling Settlement)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification for a buyback offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members of the Exchange are required to take note of this corporate action
- Only equity shareholders holding shares as of the record date (November 14, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares purchased on or after the ex-entitlement date will not be eligible for the buyback

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-756/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This buyback announcement affects only INFOSYS LTD shareholders. Trading members and investors need to be aware of the record date to determine eligibility for participating in the buyback offer.

**Operational Impact**: Shareholders who wish to participate in the buyback must hold shares before the ex-entitlement date of November 14, 2025. Any shares purchased on or after this date will not qualify for the buyback offer.

**Investor Consideration**: Shareholders should review the buyback terms and conditions to make informed decisions about participation. The buyback may provide an exit opportunity at potentially favorable prices compared to market rates.
