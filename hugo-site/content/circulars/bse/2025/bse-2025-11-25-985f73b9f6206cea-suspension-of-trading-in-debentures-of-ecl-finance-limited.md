## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures of ECL Finance Limited (Code: 953154, ISIN: INE804I07H49) effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- **Company**: ECL Finance Limited
- **Security**: ECL-9.81%-22-12-25-PVT (Non-Convertible Debentures)
- **ISIN**: INE804I07H49
- **BSE Code**: 953154
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 8, 2025
- **Purpose**: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- **Reference**: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational circular for debt securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must not deal in the specified debentures of ECL Finance Limited from December 8, 2025
- **Trading Members**: Required to note the suspension and ensure compliance with no-dealing restriction
- **Debenture Holders**: Those holding the securities as of the record date (December 8, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| November 25, 2025 | Circular issued by BSE |
| December 8, 2025 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| December 8, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of this particular NCD series. The 9.81% coupon debentures maturing in December 2025 will cease trading to facilitate orderly redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption. No further trading opportunity available from December 8, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and inform clients holding these securities about the upcoming redemption.
