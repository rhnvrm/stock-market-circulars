## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,49,995 equity shares of Jash Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 544402) with effect from Monday, December 22, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in provisions based on the shareholder category.

## Key Points

- 1,49,995 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each being listed
- Issue price: Rs. 305.50/- per share (Rs. 303.50/- premium)
- Date of allotment: September 15, 2025
- Shares issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished numbers: 62755126 to 62905120
- ISIN: INE039O01029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are permitted to trade from December 22, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the specified schedule
- Shares cannot be transferred until the respective lock-in periods expire

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (74,000 shares)**: June 22, 2026 (Dist. Nos. 62830126 to 62905120)
- **Lock-in Expiry (75,000 shares)**: June 20, 2027 (Dist. Nos. 62755126 to 62830125)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (1,49,995) which represents minimal dilution to existing shareholders. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions preventing immediate selling pressure.

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all shares are under lock-in until June 2026 at the earliest.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral - Existing shareholders should note the expansion in equity base, though the lock-in provisions ensure no immediate market impact. The preferential allotment at Rs. 305.50/- per share indicates confidence from promoters and strategic investors.
