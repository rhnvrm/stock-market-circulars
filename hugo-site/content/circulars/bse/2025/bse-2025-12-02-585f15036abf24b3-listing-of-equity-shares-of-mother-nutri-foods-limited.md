## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Mother Nutri Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544623, symbol MNFL, and ISIN INE0UH001016. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Mother Nutri Foods Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Symbol: MNFL
- ISIN: INE0UH001016
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details for Mother Nutri Foods Limited and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. Impact is limited to investors and trading members interested in Mother Nutri Foods Limited. No immediate market-wide implications. Trading members should await further notice regarding the specific listing date and any additional security details before commencing trading activities.
