## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Indigrid Infrastructure Trust. Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of listed debt securities will be modified effective December 29, 2025. This is a continuation of the earlier exchange notice dated September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976972
- Old ISIN: INE219X07561 changing to New ISIN: INE219X07579
- Paid-up value increasing from Rs. 90,000 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Changes effective from Monday, December 29, 2025
- Call money has been received from debenture holders
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20250926-5 dated September 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational update following the standard process for debt securities when call money is received from debenture holders.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new ISIN (INE219X07579) for trading purposes
- All systems and records should be updated to reflect the new paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Debenture holders and trading members must ensure proper tracking of the ISIN change

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025 (Monday)
- **Reference Notice**: September 26, 2025 (Exchange Notice 20250926-5)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects specific debt security holders and traders dealing with Indigrid Infrastructure Trust debentures. The ISIN change requires system updates across trading platforms.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and debenture holders must update their records to reflect the new ISIN and increased paid-up value. The increase in paid-up value from Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture reflects the receipt of the final call money installment.

**Investor Impact**: Existing debenture holders who have paid the call money will see their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with updated paid-up value.
