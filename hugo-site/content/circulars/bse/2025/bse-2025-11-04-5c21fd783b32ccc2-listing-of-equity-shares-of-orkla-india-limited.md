## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Orkla India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544595, symbol ORKLAINDIA, and ISIN number INE16NZ01023. The specific date of listing and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Orkla India Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544595
- Symbol: ORKLAINDIA
- ISIN: INE16NZ01023
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced:
- Update trading systems with scrip code 544595 and symbol ORKLAINDIA
- Monitor for subsequent notice announcing the actual listing date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This announcement adds a new equity security to the BSE, expanding investment options for market participants. The actual market impact will depend on the company's profile, size, and investor interest once trading commences.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details but no immediate action required until the listing date is announced.
