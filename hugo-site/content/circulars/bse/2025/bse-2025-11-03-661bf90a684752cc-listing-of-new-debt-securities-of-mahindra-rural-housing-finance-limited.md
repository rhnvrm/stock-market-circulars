## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited (MRHFL) issued on private placement basis, effective November 3, 2025. The securities comprise 20,000 units with a face value of INR 1,00,000 each, carrying a credit rating of CRISIL AAA/stable and offering 7.3% p.a. interest with annual payment frequency.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977264 (Scrip ID: 73MRHF28)
- **ISIN**: INE950O07529
- **Issue Details**: 20,000 securities at INR 1,00,000 face value (Total: INR 200 crores)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA/stable
- **Interest Rate**: 7.3% per annum (paid annually)
- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: September 26, 2028
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE950O07529
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department at phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually starting October 31, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: September 26, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities are limited to institutional investors and do not affect retail equity markets. The CRISIL AAA rating indicates high credit quality. The listing provides Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited with additional funding for its housing finance operations through the debt segment of BSE.
