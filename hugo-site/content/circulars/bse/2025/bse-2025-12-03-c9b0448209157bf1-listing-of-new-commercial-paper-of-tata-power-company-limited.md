## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Tata Power Company Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 3, 2025. The instrument consists of 10,000 units of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 492,766.50 per unit with a redemption date of March 2, 2026. The issuing and paying agent is ICICI Bank Limited.

## Key Points

- Securities listed on BSE Debt segment effective December 3, 2025
- Quantity: 10,000 commercial paper units
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 492,766.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730691
- Scrip ID: TPCL031225
- ISIN: INE245A14KP3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE245A14KP3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term financing to Tata Power Company Limited with a tenure of approximately 90 days. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members can utilize this instrument for debt portfolio diversification. No significant operational or market structure changes are introduced.
