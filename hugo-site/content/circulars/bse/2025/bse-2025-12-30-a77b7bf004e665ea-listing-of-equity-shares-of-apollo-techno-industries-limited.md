## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Apollo Techno Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544671, trading symbol ATIL, and ISIN number INE0X4A01013. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Apollo Techno Industries Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544671
- Trading Symbol: ATIL
- ISIN Number: INE0X4A01013
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN number for Apollo Techno Industries Limited and await further notice regarding the listing date and trading commencement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for the SME segment with minimal market-wide impact. The listing will provide Apollo Techno Industries Limited access to capital markets and offer trading members and investors a new investment opportunity in the SME space. No immediate action required until the listing date is announced.
