## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 50,35,000 new equity shares of Sellwin Traders Limited (Scrip Code: 538875) effective from July 29, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 5.50 per share.

## Key Points

- 50.35 lakh equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 3.50
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences on July 29, 2025
- ISIN: INE195F01027
- Distribution numbers: 224887501 to 229922500

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new security listing
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine new security listing. The lock-in period until February 2026 restricts immediate trading liquidity. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may provide some additional market participation once lock-in expires.
