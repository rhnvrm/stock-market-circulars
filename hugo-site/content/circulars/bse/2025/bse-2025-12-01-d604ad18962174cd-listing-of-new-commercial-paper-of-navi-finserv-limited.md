## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The instrument comprises 600 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying a CARE A1 credit rating. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 600 commercial papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,77,305 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730669
- Scrip ID: NFL281125
- ISIN Number: INE342T14FE2
- Credit Rating: CARE A1
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14FE2
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: December 1, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 25, 2026
- Notice Date: December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing involving a limited issuance of 600 units totaling approximately Rs. 30 crores. The securities are rated CARE A1 and issued on private placement basis with a tenure of approximately 6 months. The listing provides short-term funding options for Navi Finserv Limited and offers institutional investors another CARE A1-rated debt instrument in the BSE Debt segment.
