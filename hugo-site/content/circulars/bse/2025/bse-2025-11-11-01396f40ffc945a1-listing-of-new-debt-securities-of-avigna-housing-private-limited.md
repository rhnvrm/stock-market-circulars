## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Avigna Housing Private Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Avigna Housing Private Limited
- **Quantity**: 32,500 debentures
- **Scrip Code**: 977283
- **Scrip ID**: 4AHPL29
- **ISIN**: INE0QJD07018
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE BB/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 4% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from November 30, 2025 to May 6, 2029
- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 6, 2029
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0QJD07018
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 30, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: May 6, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds liquidity options for holders of these privately placed debentures. The securities have a BB rating indicating higher credit risk, and the 4% interest rate reflects the private placement nature and credit profile of the issuer. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for client portfolio management purposes.
