## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Oraph Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and adds to the existing list of approved algo providers available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Oraph Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled to provide algorithmic trading solutions
- Trading members can now engage with this vendor for algo trading services
- The empanelment is effective immediately from December 4, 2025
- Complete list of algo providers is available on BSE website

## Vendor Details

**Oraph Private Limited**
- Address: D-393, Third Floor, Defence Colony, New Delhi - 110024
- Contact: +91 9810992306

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update adding a new vendor to the approved algo provider list.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members who wish to use algorithmic trading solutions may now engage with Oraph Private Limited as an approved vendor. Members should refer to existing guidelines for onboarding algo providers.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (Immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This expands the choice of algo providers available to trading members but does not change any trading rules or market structure.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. The provisional empanelment status indicates the vendor is approved for use subject to ongoing compliance monitoring.

## Additional Information

The complete and updated list of empanelled algo providers is maintained on the BSE website at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
