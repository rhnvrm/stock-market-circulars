## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544613 and trading symbol UTLSOLAR with ISIN number INE12UR01024. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Fujiyama Power Systems Limited
- Scrip Code: 544613
- Trading Symbol: UTLSOLAR
- ISIN Number: INE12UR01024
- Listing will occur in due course with date to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Fujiyama Power Systems Limited in their systems and await the separate notice regarding the actual listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will provide investors with a new investment opportunity in the power systems sector once trading commences. Trading members need to prepare their systems for the new security. The actual market impact will depend on the company's fundamentals, IPO pricing, and investor reception when the listing date is announced.
