## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership for Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) in the Currency derivative and Interest rate derivative segments, effective October 23, 2025. This is an administrative action following the member's voluntary decision to exit these trading segments.

## Key Points

- Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) has surrendered its trading membership
- Surrender applies to Currency derivative segment
- Surrender applies to Interest rate derivative segment
- Approval granted by BSE exchange authorities
- Effective date: October 23, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of membership status change.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 23, 2025 - Trading membership surrender becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single trading member's voluntary exit from derivative segments. No impact on market operations, trading systems, or other members' activities. Routine administrative change in membership status.
