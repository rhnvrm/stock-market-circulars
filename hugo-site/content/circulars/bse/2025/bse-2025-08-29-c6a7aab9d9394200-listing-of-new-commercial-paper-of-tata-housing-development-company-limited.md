## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 250 crore issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective August 29, 2025. The instrument carries CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (5000 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,558 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,442)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730115, Scrip ID: THDC280825
- ISIN: INE582L14IU0
- HDFC Bank acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding avenue for Tata Housing Development Company Limited and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
