## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Riddhi Display Equipments Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544640, trading symbol RDEL, and ISIN number INE1DKT01011. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Riddhi Display Equipments Limited
- Scrip Code: 544640
- Trading Symbol: RDEL
- ISIN: INE1DKT01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. It affects only trading members and investors specifically interested in Riddhi Display Equipments Limited. The listing expands the SME segment with one additional security. No immediate trading implications as the listing date has not been specified.
