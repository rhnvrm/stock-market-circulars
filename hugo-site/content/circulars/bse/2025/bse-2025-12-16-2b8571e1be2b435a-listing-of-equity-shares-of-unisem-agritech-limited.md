## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of UNISEM AGRITECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544648, symbol UNISEM, and ISIN number INE1FHV01026. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: UNISEM AGRITECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544648
- Symbol: UNISEM
- ISIN: INE1FHV01026
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the separate notice announcing the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for a new SME company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in this specific scrip. The listing will provide UNISEM AGRITECH LIMITED access to capital markets and offer investment opportunities to SME segment investors. Broader market impact is minimal as this affects only one new listing in the SME segment.
