## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 787 dated December 31, 2025. The auction includes 154 securities from various companies spanning multiple sectors, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 268,000 shares. This is a standard operational circular listing securities available for demat auction to settle shortages in delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 787
- Settlement Date: December 31, 2025
- Total number of securities listed: 154 scrips
- Largest quantity available: Rama Steel Tubes (268,000 shares)
- Includes major blue-chip stocks like Reliance Industries (2,150 shares), TCS (757 shares), HDFC Bank group companies, and others
- Covers diverse sectors: Banking, Automotive, Pharmaceuticals, Energy, Technology, FMCG, and Metals

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in delivery obligations must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's standard auction settlement procedures
- Members are required to fulfill their delivery obligations through the auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 31, 2025
- Settlement Number: 787

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The auction affects 154 securities across multiple sectors. While this is a routine settlement process, the quantities involved in certain securities (particularly Rama Steel Tubes with 268,000 shares, Suzlon Energy with 72,000 shares, and Vodafone Idea with 33,030 shares) could have temporary price impacts.

**Operational Impact**: This circular is standard operating procedure for settling delivery shortages. Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations in these securities must participate in the auction to close out their positions.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these securities may observe temporary volatility during the auction process, particularly in securities with larger quantities available for auction.
