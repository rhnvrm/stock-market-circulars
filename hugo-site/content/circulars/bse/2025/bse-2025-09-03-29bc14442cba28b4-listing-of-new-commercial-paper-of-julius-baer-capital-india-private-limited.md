## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crores (2000 units)
- Scrip Code: 730141
- Scrip ID: JBCIP3925
- ISIN: INE824H14SG2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,809.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Julius Baer Capital and investment avenue for institutional investors in the debt segment.
