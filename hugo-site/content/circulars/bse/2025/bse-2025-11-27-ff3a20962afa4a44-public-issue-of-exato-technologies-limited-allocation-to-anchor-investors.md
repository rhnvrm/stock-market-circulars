## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. This allocation is part of the standard IPO process where qualified institutional buyers are allotted shares prior to the issue opening to retail and other investors.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation completed for Exato Technologies Limited's public issue
- Allocation made as per SEBI ICDR Regulations for anchor investor participation
- Details of allocation provided in the circular
- This precedes the opening of the IPO to general public

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural circular for IPO anchor allocation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- One-day lock-in period applicable for 50% of anchor allocation
- 30-day lock-in period for remaining 50% of anchor allocation
- Anchor investors cannot sell allocated shares during lock-in period

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 27, 2025
- Anchor allocation date: November 27, 2025
- IPO opening date and other timelines would be as per issue prospectus

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation demonstrates institutional confidence in the IPO and typically influences retail participation. The quality and quantum of anchor allocation can affect listing performance.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Anchor allocation reduces shares available for retail and non-institutional investors. Strong anchor participation often viewed positively by market participants.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard IPO process step with no operational disruptions to market functioning.
