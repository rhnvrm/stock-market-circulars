## Summary

SBICAP Securities Limited has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The issue comprises 2000 units with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, issued on private placement basis. The CP carries credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with State Bank of India acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 2000 units of Commercial Paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,945.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730666
- **Scrip ID**: SBIC271125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CT1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: State Bank of India

## Trading Specifications

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CT1
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Listed on BSE Debt segment
- Detail Name: SBICAP-25-02-26-CP

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides SBICAP Securities with short-term funding for approximately 90 days. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors trading in commercial paper. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible primarily to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals.
