## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued by UGRO Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective December 8, 2025. The issue comprises 600 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 30 crores, with a tenure of 180 days maturing on June 3, 2026. Yes Bank Limited serves as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- 600 units of commercial paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 30 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,78,525.50 per unit
- Credit rating: IND A1+ 
- Scrip Code: 730715, Scrip ID: UCL51225
- ISIN: INE583D14766
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D14766
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be maintained

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding for UGRO Capital Limited and offers debt market participants an A1+ rated investment option with 180-day tenure. The listing enhances liquidity options in the debt segment but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
