## Summary

BSE has issued a corrigendum to the forfeiture notice for Integrated Proteins Ltd. The circular provides Annexure I containing detailed listing of share distinctive numbers (from and to) that are subject to forfeiture proceedings. The annexure lists 198 entries with distinctive share number ranges totaling approximately 150,000+ shares across various folios.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued to previous forfeiture notice for Integrated Proteins Ltd
- Annexure I details 198 entries of distinctive share numbers subject to forfeiture
- Share ranges span from distinctive number 1318001 to 19941
- Individual entries range from 400 shares to 9,100 shares per folio
- Total shares listed appear to exceed 150,000 shares subject to forfeiture

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corrigendum correcting or supplementing information in a previously issued forfeiture notice. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders holding shares with distinctive numbers listed in Annexure I must take note of the forfeiture proceedings
- Affected shareholders should verify their holdings against the distinctive number ranges provided
- Shareholders may need to take remedial action to prevent forfeiture (such as payment of calls due)
- Company must follow SEBI and Companies Act provisions for share forfeiture

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 13, 2025
- Specific deadlines for remedial action not mentioned in the extracted content

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** High - Shareholders with distinctive numbers listed face potential loss of equity holdings if calls remain unpaid or other forfeiture conditions are not remedied.

**Company Impact:** Significant impact on shareholding pattern and capital structure. Forfeiture of over 150,000 shares will alter the company's shareholder base.

**Market Impact:** May affect trading sentiment and liquidity for Integrated Proteins Ltd stock, particularly if large blocks of shares are being forfeited.
