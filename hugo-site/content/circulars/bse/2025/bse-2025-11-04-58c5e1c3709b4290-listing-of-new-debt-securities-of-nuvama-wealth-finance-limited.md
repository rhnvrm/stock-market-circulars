## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. The issue consists of 2,579 units of gold-future linked debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, maturing on April 30, 2029.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977266
- Scrip ID: NWFL301025
- Detail Name: NWFL-GOLDFUTURE-30-4-29-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE918K07QE9
- Quantity: 2,579 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options attached

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: April 30, 2029
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Not Applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds to the debt instruments available on BSE's debt segment but affects only specific debt market participants and investors interested in Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited's securities. The gold-future linked nature of these debentures may attract investors seeking exposure to gold-linked instruments. The AA/Stable rating indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
