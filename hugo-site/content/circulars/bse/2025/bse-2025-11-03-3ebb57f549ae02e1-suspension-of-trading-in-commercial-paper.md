## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of 9 companies effective from their respective no-dealings dates due to fixed record dates for redemption purposes. The suspensions affect commercial paper across multiple issuers including financial services companies and one retail venture.

## Key Points

- 9 companies' commercial paper suspended from trading
- One commercial paper (ECL Finance) suspended from November 4, 2025
- Eight commercial papers suspended from November 12, 2025
- Suspensions are for redemption purposes as record dates have been fixed
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified commercial papers
- Notice reference: DR-749/2025-2026 and DR-754/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from the no-dealings dates
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension for all listed commercial papers

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt segment trading for 9 commercial paper instruments. No impact on equity markets.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - investors holding these commercial papers will see redemption processed as per record dates. No trading possible from suspension dates.

**Operational Impact:** Low - routine procedural action by exchange. Standard redemption process for commercial paper instruments with defined timelines.
