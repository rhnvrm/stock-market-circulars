## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in commercial paper of 19 companies effective December 16-17, 2025. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption of commercial paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in these instruments from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 19 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- 10 companies have record date of December 16, 2025 with no dealings from December 16, 2025
- 9 companies have record date of December 17, 2025 with no dealings from December 17, 2025
- All suspensions are categorized under Corporate Actions in the Debt segment
- Notice references DR-778/2025-2026 and DR-779/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing in the debt market.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial paper from the specified no-dealings dates
- All affected commercial paper instruments are suspended from trading to facilitate redemption processing
- Trading members should inform their clients about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Record Date (Batch 1)**: December 16, 2025
- **No Dealings From (Batch 1)**: December 16, 2025
- **Record Date (Batch 2)**: December 17, 2025
- **No Dealings From (Batch 2)**: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects 19 commercial paper instruments across diverse sectors including financial services, infrastructure, cement, and retail.

**Operational Impact**: Routine - Standard suspension for redemption processing. Trading members and investors holding these instruments should prepare for redemption settlements.

**Sector Distribution**: Predominantly financial services companies (Bajaj Financial Securities, SBICAP Securities, Sharekhan, Infina Finance, UGRO Capital, Navi Finserv, ARKA FINCAP, DSP Finance), with representation from infrastructure (L&T Metro Rail), power (Tata Power), manufacturing (Motherson, Shree Cements, J.K.
