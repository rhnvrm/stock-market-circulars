## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 160/2025-2026 dated November 21, 2025, announces book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple securities including equity shares, non-convertible debentures, and commercial papers. The bulletin covers interest payments, redemptions, dividend announcements, bonus issues, and stock splits.

## Key Points

- **Apis India Ltd (506166)**: Bonus issue with record date December 5, 2025
- **Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS)**: Stock split from Rs.10/- to Rs.2/- with record date December 5, 2025
- **Engineers India Ltd (532178)**: Interim dividend with record date December 4, 2025
- Multiple Bajaj Financial Securities Limited commercial papers with redemption dates between November 24, 2025 and February 24, 2026
- Various private placement non-convertible debentures scheduled for interest payments and redemptions
- Akara Capital Advisors interest payment record dates: December 12 and December 16, 2025
- Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Limited partial redemptions scheduled for December 5 and December 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must be on record as of the specified record dates to be eligible for the respective corporate actions
- Trading flag status indicated for each security (0 = ex-date basis, 1 = cum basis)
- Companies must ensure book closure/record date announcements are communicated to shareholders and depositories

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: CP redemption - Bajaj Financial Securities
- **November 26, 2025**: Part redemption NCD - Damji Shamji Realty
- **November 27, 2025**: Interest payment - Aerogrid Advanced Hosting Solutions
- **December 4, 2025**: Interim dividend record date - Engineers India Ltd
- **December 5, 2025**: Multiple events - Apis India bonus issue, CAMS stock split, Crest Ventures interest & redemption, Edelweiss interest payment
- **December 12, 2025**: Interest payment - Akara Capital; Part redemptions - Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services
- **December 16, 2025**: Interest payment - Akara Capital
- **February 2-24, 2026**: Multiple CP redemptions - Bajaj Financial Securities

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impacts:**
- Bonus issue by Apis India will increase share liquidity and benefit existing shareholders
- CAMS stock split will improve share affordability and trading liquidity
- Interim dividend from Engineers India provides cash returns to shareholders

**Neutral Impacts:**
- Routine interest payments and redemptions for debt securities proceeding as scheduled
- Multiple commercial paper redemptions indicate normal debt market operations

**Scope:**
- Affects equity shareholders of three companies for dividend/bonus/split actions
- Impacts debt investors holding various NCDs and commercial papers for interest and redemption payments
- Broader market impact is limited as this is a routine administrative bulletin
