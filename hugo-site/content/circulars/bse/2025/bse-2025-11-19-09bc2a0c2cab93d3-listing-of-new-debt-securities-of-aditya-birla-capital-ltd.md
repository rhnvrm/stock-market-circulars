## Summary

Aditya Birla Capital Ltd has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 19, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and comprise 17,500 bonds with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 175 crore. The bonds carry an interest rate of 8.03% per annum and mature on May 4, 2035. The securities have been assigned AAA ratings by both CRISIL and ICRA.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 17,500 bonds
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per bond
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,04,638.30 (issued at premium)
- Scrip Code: 976676
- Scrip ID: 803ABCL35
- ISIN: INE674K08018 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Interest Rate: 8.03% per annum (paid annually)
- Maturity Date: May 4, 2035
- Allotment Date: November 18, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA, ICRA AAA
- Put/Call Option: Not available
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE674K08018
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: May 4, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate debt listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing adds Rs. 175 crore of AAA-rated debt securities to the BSE Debt segment, providing institutional investors with an investment option in Aditya Birla Capital's long-term debt (approximately 10-year tenor). The private placement nature means these securities are likely intended for qualified institutional investors rather than retail investors. No operational changes required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
