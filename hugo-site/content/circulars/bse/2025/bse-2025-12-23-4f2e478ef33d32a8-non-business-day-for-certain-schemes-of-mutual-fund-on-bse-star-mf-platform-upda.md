## Summary

BSE has notified non-business days for certain mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform during December 2025. The affected schemes are primarily international/overseas investment funds from Nippon India, ICICI Prudential, Bandhan, and Invesco. These schemes will not accept transactions on the specified dates due to holidays in their underlying markets.

## Key Points

- Multiple mutual fund schemes will observe non-business days on December 24 and December 31, 2025
- Affected fund houses: Nippon India, ICICI Prudential, Bandhan, and Invesco
- All affected schemes are international/overseas investment funds (Japan, Global, US markets)
- Transactions for these specific schemes will not be processed on the designated non-business days
- This is a routine operational update for the BSE StAR MF platform

## Affected Mutual Fund Schemes

**Nippon India:**
- Nippon India Japan Equity Fund - December 31, 2025

**ICICI Prudential:**
- ICICI Prudential Global Stable Equity Fund - December 24, 2025
- ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund - December 31, 2025

**Bandhan:**
- Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund - December 24 and December 31, 2025
- Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund - December 24 and December 31, 2025

**Invesco:**
- Invesco India - Invesco EQQQ Nasdaq-100 ETF Fund of Fund - December 31, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Stable Equity Fund, Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund, and Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund
- **December 31, 2025**: Non-business day for Nippon India Japan Equity Fund, ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund, Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund, Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund, and Invesco India - Invesco EQQQ Nasdaq-100 ETF Fund of Fund

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors should note that purchase, redemption, and switch transactions for the listed schemes will not be accepted on the specified non-business days
- Fund houses and distributors should ensure investors are informed about these non-business days
- The BSE StAR MF platform will not process transactions for these schemes on the designated dates

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - affects only specific international mutual fund schemes

**Operational Impact:** Low - routine holiday observance aligned with underlying international market closures (Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve)

**Investor Impact:** Limited - investors planning transactions in these specific schemes should plan around the non-business days; other mutual fund schemes remain unaffected and will operate normally
