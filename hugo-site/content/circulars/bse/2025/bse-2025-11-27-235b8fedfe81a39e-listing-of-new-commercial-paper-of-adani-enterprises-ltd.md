## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd on its Debt segment with effect from November 27, 2025. The company has issued 700 Commercial Papers of Rs.5,00,000 each on private placement basis, with a total value of Rs.35 crores. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- 700 Commercial Papers of face value Rs.5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs.4,93,731 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730606, Scrip ID: AEL191125, ISIN: INE423A14YO0 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: November 26, 2025
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot: 1 unit, Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YO0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Tenure:** Approximately 65 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers provide short-term financing to Adani Enterprises Ltd for approximately 65 days. The securities are rated at the highest short-term rating category (A1+) by both CARE and ICRA, indicating strong credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in highly-rated short-term corporate debt. The private placement nature means limited availability to retail investors.
