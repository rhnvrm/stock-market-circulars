## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of VERTIS INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST with effect from December 5, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of December 7, 2025 for the redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for VERTIS INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST debentures (INE0KXY07018)
- Debenture series: VIL-7.71%-22-12-25-PVT (Scrip Code: 974228)
- No dealings permitted from December 5, 2025
- Record date for redemption: December 7, 2025
- Circular reference: DR-771/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 5, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No dealing in VERTIS INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST debentures with ISIN INE0KXY07018

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 5, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **December 7, 2025**: Record date for redemption purpose

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it affects only a single debt security issue of VERTIS INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST. The trading suspension is a routine procedural measure to facilitate the redemption process. Debenture holders on record as of December 7, 2025 will receive redemption proceeds. The suspension prevents trading during the redemption window to ensure accurate beneficiary determination. No impact on equity markets or other securities.
