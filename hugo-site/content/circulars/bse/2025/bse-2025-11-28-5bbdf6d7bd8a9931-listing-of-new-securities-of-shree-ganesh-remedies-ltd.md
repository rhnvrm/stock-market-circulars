## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 968 new equity shares of Shree Ganesh Remedies Ltd (Scrip Code: 540737) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each issued on rights basis. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new shares to be listed: 968 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Shares resulted from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up
- Original issuance was on rights basis
- ISIN: INE414Y01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 950 shares (12836671 to 12837620) and 18 shares (12116308 to 12116325)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading Members permitted to trade in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are permitted to trade in the new securities effective from the specified date
- No additional compliance requirements specified

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine corporate action involving conversion of a small number of partly paid shares (968 shares) to fully paid shares for Shree Ganesh Remedies Ltd. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares and represent standard post-rights issue share conversion activity. Limited effect on overall trading volume or stock liquidity.
