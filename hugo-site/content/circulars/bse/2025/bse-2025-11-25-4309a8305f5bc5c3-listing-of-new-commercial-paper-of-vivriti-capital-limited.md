## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 25, 2025. The instrument comprises 200 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 10 Crore, with a 3-month tenure maturing on February 23, 2026. The Federal Bank Ltd acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 200 units (Rs. 10 Crore)
- Scrip Code: 730645, Scrip ID: VCL241125
- ISIN: INE01HV14AF0
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 489,564 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AF0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for trading
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 25, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing on private placement basis affecting only debt market participants dealing with short-term debt instruments. The listing provides institutional investors access to CARE A1+ rated 3-month commercial paper from Vivriti Capital Limited. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
