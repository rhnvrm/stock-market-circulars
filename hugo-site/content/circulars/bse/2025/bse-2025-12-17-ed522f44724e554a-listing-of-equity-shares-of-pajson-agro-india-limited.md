## Summary

Pajson Agro India Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated December 17, 2025. The company has a total of 23,809,595 equity shares with promoter and promoter group holding 62.47% and public shareholders holding 37.53%. The company has issued fully paid-up equity shares with no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, or encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 23,809,595
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 3 shareholders holding 14,874,995 shares (62.4748%)
- Public shareholders: 1,147 shareholders holding 8,934,600 shares (37.5252%)
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or under non-disposal undertaking
- No encumbrances on promoter shareholding
- Dematerialized shares: 23,809,595 (100% of total)
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration confirmed that the company has no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with no material impact on trading or operations. The shareholding pattern shows a stable promoter holding above 62% and reasonable public float of 37.53%. The absence of any encumbrances, pledges, or outstanding convertible securities indicates financial stability. All shares are in dematerialized form, facilitating smooth trading. This disclosure provides transparency to investors about the ownership structure of Pajson Agro India Limited.
