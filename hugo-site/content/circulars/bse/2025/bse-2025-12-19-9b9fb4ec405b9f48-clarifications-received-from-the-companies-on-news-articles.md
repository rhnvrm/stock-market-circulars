## Summary

BSE has issued a notice seeking clarifications from ITI Ltd (Scrip Code: 523610) and Waaree Energies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544277) regarding news articles appearing in various media channels. The exchange is verifying the accuracy of these news reports to inform members and investors. Replies from both companies are currently awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE seeks clarifications from two companies on media reports
- ITI Ltd (Scrip Code: 523610) - article from moneycontrol.com
- Waaree Energies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544277) - article from Economic Times energy section
- Replies are pending from both companies
- Part of BSE's routine surveillance to verify news accuracy

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- ITI Ltd and Waaree Energies are required to respond to BSE's clarification request
- Companies must verify or clarify information appearing in news articles
- Responses will be shared with market participants once received

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Reply deadline: Not specified (awaited)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance measure with minimal immediate market impact. The exchange is following standard procedures to ensure information accuracy in the market. Until clarifications are received, investors should be aware that news articles about these companies are under verification. The actual impact will depend on the nature of the news articles and the companies' responses.
