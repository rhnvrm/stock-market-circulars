## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed 500 units of Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment on private placement basis. The instruments are denominated at Rs. 5,00,000 each with a total issue size of Rs. 25 crore. The Commercial Paper carries credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 500 units (Total: Rs. 25 crore)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,78,999.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730522
- **Scrip ID**: ECLF71125
- **ISIN**: INE804I14YE5
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE804I14YE5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 11, 2026 (6-month tenure)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides ECL Finance Limited with short-term funding at competitive rates (implied yield approximately 8.76% annualized based on issue price). The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in short-term commercial paper instruments.
