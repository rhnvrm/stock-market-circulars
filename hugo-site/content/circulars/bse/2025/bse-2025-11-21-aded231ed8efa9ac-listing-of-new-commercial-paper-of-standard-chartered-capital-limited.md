## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Standard Chartered Capital Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 500 commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 500 commercial papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,71,723.50
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Scrip Code**: 730623
- **Scrip ID**: SCCL211125
- **ISIN**: INE403G14TV4
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403G14TV4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing affecting only participants in the debt segment who deal with short-term debt instruments. The commercial paper has high credit ratings (A1+) indicating low credit risk. Trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.
