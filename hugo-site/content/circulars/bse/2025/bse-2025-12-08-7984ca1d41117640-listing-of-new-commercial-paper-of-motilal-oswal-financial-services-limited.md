## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited on private placement basis effective December 8, 2025. The listing comprises 3000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment. The instrument is rated ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ with a redemption date of March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3000 units of Commercial Paper listed with face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 729774, Scrip ID: MOFSL30725, Detail Name: MOFSL-06-03-26-CP
- ISIN Number: INE338I14KT1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 6, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions are conducted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE338I14KT1
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** December 5, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options in the BSE Debt segment for institutional and qualified investors. The instrument has a tenure of approximately 3 months and is rated at the highest short-term rating category (A1+), indicating strong credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in Motilal Oswal Financial Services' short-term instruments.
