## Summary

BSE has announced changes in the trading groups for equity shares of Investment Companies (ICs) and Investment Holding Companies (IHCs). Nine companies will be transferred from the trade-for-trade segment to their respective groups (A, B, or X) between November 13-18, 2025. Additionally, five companies will continue trading in the trade-for-trade segment under call auction until further notice.

## Key Points

- 9 scrips moving from trade-for-trade segment to regular groups (A, B, or X)
- Group changes will be implemented in phases from November 13-18, 2025
- 5 scrips to remain in trade-for-trade segment (T, XT, Z groups) under call auction
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251015-50 dated October 15, 2025
- Changes affect Investment Companies and Investment Holding Companies specifically

## Regulatory Changes

The circular modifies the trading segment classification for equity shares of Investment Companies and Investment Holding Companies:

**Scrips Moving to Regular Groups:**
- Group B (6 companies): Pilani Investment, Nalwa Sons Investment, Kalyani Investment, GFL Limited, SIL Investments, Welspun Investments
- Group A (2 companies): TVS Holdings Limited, Maharashtra Scooters Limited
- Group X (1 company): Naperol Investments Limited

**Scrips Remaining in Restricted Trading:**
- Trade-for-trade segment with call auction: Vardhman Holdings (T), Kama Holdings (T), Elcid Investments (T), Haryana Capfin (XT), Easy Fincorp (Z)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the effective dates for group changes
- All trades in the specified scrips must follow the new group trading rules from respective effective dates
- For scrips continuing in trade-for-trade segment, call auction procedures must be followed
- Members should contact BSE officials (Shyam Bhagirath, Manu Thomas, or Shilpa Saboo) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Pilani Investment (539883), Nalwa Sons Investment (532256), Kalyani Investment (533302), GFL Limited (500173) move to Group B
- **November 14, 2025**: TVS Holdings Limited (520056) moves to Group A
- **November 17, 2025**: Naperol Investments (500298) moves to Group X; Maharashtra Scooters (500266) moves to Group A; SIL Investments (521194) moves to Group B
- **November 18, 2025**: Welspun Investments (533252) moves to Group B

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impacts:**
- Enhanced liquidity for 9 scrips moving from trade-for-trade to regular groups
- Improved trading flexibility with normal settlement cycles
- Better price discovery mechanism compared to trade-for-trade segment

**Operational Impacts:**
- Trading members need to update systems for group classification changes
- Investors in these scrips will experience different trading rules and settlement procedures
- Margin requirements may change based on new group classification

**Market Segment Impact:**
- Limited broader market impact as changes affect only Investment Companies/Investment Holding Companies
- Specific impact on investors and traders dealing with these 14 scrips
- Five scrips remaining in restricted segments may face continued liquidity constraints
