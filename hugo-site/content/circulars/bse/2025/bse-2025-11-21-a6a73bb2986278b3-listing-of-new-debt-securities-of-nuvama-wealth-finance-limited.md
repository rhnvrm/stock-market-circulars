## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective November 21, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.95% per annum with a tenure until May 19, 2028, and have received a CARE AA/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited
- Quantity: 17,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: November 21, 2025
- Maturity Date: May 19, 2028
- Scrip Code: 977307
- Scrip ID: 895NWFL28
- ISIN: INE918K07QF6
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QF6
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 20, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency:** Annually
- **Redemption Date:** May 19, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine debt listing on private placement basis with limited direct market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact:** Moderate for debt investors - Provides a new fixed-income investment opportunity with CARE AA/Stable rating, offering 8.95% annual returns over a ~2.5 year tenure.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members need to note the technical specifications (ISIN, tick size, demat-only trading) but this follows standard debt market procedures.
