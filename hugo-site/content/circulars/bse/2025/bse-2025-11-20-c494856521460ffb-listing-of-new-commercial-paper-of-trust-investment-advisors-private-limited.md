## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited on private placement basis effective November 20, 2025. The instrument consists of 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crores), with credit rating IVR A1+. The securities will trade only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Scrip Code**: 730611
- **Scrip ID**: TIAP201125
- **ISIN**: INE723X14LO2
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,77,670.50 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 500 units (Rs. 25 crores)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: IVR A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: The Federal Bank Limited
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN Number INE723X14LO2
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: June 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited for approximately 6.5 months. The IVR A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in short-term corporate debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
