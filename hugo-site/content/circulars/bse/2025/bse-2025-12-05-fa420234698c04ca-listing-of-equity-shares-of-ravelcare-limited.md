## Summary

RAVELCARE LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern showing the distribution of equity shares among promoters and public shareholders. The company has issued 68,59,000 fully paid-up equity shares with no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 68,59,000 (fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 50,02,998 shares (72.94%)
- Public holding: 18,56,002 shares (27.06%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 5
- Number of public shareholders: 465
- Dematerialized shares: 55,30,000 (80.62% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, or warrants issued
- No shares in locked-in status
- No shares pledged or encumbered by promoters
- No depository receipts issued against shares

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - this is a routine shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Listed entity has declared no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or encumbered shares
- Shareholding pattern disclosure requirement for newly listed equity shares

## Important Dates

- Filing date: December 4, 2025
- Circular publication date: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard regulatory filing for newly listed equity shares with no material market impact. The promoter holding of 72.94% indicates strong promoter control, while the public float of 27.06% meets minimum public shareholding requirements. The dematerialization rate of 80.62% is relatively high, indicating good investor preference for electronic holdings.
