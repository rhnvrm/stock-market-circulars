## Summary

BSE has announced the name change of trading member Lakshmishree Investment & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3281) to "Lakshmishree Investment and Securities Ltd." effective Friday, November 7, 2025. This is an administrative change relating to the corporate structure of the trading member.

## Key Points

- Trading member name changing from "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT & SECURITIES PVT.LTD." to "LAKSHMISHREE INVESTMENT AND SECURITIES LTD."
- Member Number: 3281
- Effective date: November 7, 2025 (Friday)
- For clarifications, trading members can contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a corporate name change for a trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. The notification is for information purposes to inform the exchange community of the name change.

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change reflects a corporate restructuring from a private limited company to a public limited company. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other market participants should note the new name for correspondence and record-keeping purposes.
