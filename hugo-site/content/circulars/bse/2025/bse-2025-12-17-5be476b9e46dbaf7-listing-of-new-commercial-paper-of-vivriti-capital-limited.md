## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The instrument consists of 1,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a maturity period of approximately 6 months.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 50 crore aggregate)
- Scrip Code: 730790, Scrip ID: VCL161225
- ISIN Number: INE01HV14AH6
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,77,392 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Allotment Date: December 16, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 25, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities to be traded under ISIN INE01HV14AH6

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper listing provides short-term funding to Vivriti Capital Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market. The CARE A1+ rating indicates adequate safety. There is no impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the trading parameters.
