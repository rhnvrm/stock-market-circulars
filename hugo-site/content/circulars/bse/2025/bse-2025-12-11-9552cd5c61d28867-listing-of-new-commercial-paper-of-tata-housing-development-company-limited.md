## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The issuance comprises 3,100 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,100 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730751
- Scrip ID: THDC101225
- ISIN: INE582L14JB8
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,474.00 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000.00 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14JB8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- Members requiring clarifications should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 11, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide Tata Housing Development Company Limited with short-term funding for approximately 91 days. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+, IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
