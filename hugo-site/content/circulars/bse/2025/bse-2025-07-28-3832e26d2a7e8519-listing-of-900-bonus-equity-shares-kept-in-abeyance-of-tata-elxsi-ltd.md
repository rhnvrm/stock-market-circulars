## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 900 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 900 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Shares distributed across 5 different distinctive number ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of previously issued bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to the small number of shares (900) being released. This represents previously allocated bonus shares now becoming tradeable, with no dilution effect on existing shareholders as the bonus shares were already accounted for in the company's share capital.
