## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper securities issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis effective November 27, 2025. The issue comprises 1500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each, rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a tenure of 91 days maturing on February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1500 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Total issue size: Rs.75 crore (1500 x Rs.5,00,000/-)
- Issue price: Rs.491,279/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730661, Scrip ID: OWL261125
- ISIN: INE466L14FJ5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FJ5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades must comply with BSE Debt segment regulations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Start Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members in the debt segment can now trade these instruments. Standard trading protocols apply with minimum denomination of Rs.5 lakhs.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term (91-day) commercial paper instruments of 360 ONE WAM LIMITED with high credit ratings (A1+).
