## Summary

IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED has announced part redemption of three series of non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date is set as December 12, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values from the same date under settlement number DR-776/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Three debenture series affected: 1063IOT28C, 1008IOT26C, and IOT-9.02%-20-10-28-PVT
- Record date for interest payment and part redemption: December 12, 2025
- Trading with reduced face value effective from December 12, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Face values will be reduced to Rs. 57,627.12, Rs. 25,000, and Rs. 20,438 respectively

## Debenture Details

### 1063IOT28C (ISIN: INE310L07AA9, Scrip Code: 950203)
- **Reduced Face Value**: Rs. 57,627.12 per debenture
- **Purpose**: Part redemption and interest payment

### 1008IOT26C (ISIN: INE310L07886, Scrip Code: 950212)
- **Reduced Face Value**: Rs. 25,000 per debenture
- **Purpose**: Part redemption and interest payment

### IOT-9.02%-20-10-28-PVT (ISIN: INE310L07AE1, Scrip Code: 952676)
- **Reduced Face Value**: Rs. 20,438 per debenture
- **Purpose**: Part redemption and interest payment

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values effective from December 12, 2025
- All trades from the effective date onwards will reflect the new reduced face values
- Members should update their systems to reflect the post-redemption face values

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: December 12, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-776/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as on record date will receive part redemption proceeds and accrued interest. Post-redemption, the face value of holdings will be reduced, affecting the principal amount outstanding.

**Trading Impact**: From December 12, 2025, all market trades in these three debenture series will be executed at the reduced face values. Trading members and investors must adjust their valuation and position tracking accordingly.

**Liquidity Impact**: Medium - affects three specific debenture series of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED in the debt segment.
