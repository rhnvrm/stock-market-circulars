## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment, effective November 4, 2025. The instrument consists of 1700 units of Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying credit ratings of CARE A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 1700 Commercial Papers listed
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,329 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730484
- **Scrip ID**: THDL031125
- **ISIN**: INE582L14IZ9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE582L14IZ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions subject to tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term financing for Tata Housing Development Company Limited with a tenure of approximately 3 months. The strong credit ratings (CARE A1+ and IND A1+) indicate high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial papers. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.
