## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251117-4 informing trading members that equity shares of Physicswallah Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544609, symbol PWL, and ISIN number INE0LP301011. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Physicswallah Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code assigned: 544609
- Trading Symbol: PWL
- ISIN Number: INE0LP301011
- Listing date and security details to be informed separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the company details for future trading
- Members should await separate notice for exact listing date and security specifications
- Standard listing compliance procedures to be followed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard listing procedure. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is confirmed.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Creates new investment opportunity in the education technology sector. Investors should await the detailed listing notice for trading commencement date and other specifications.
