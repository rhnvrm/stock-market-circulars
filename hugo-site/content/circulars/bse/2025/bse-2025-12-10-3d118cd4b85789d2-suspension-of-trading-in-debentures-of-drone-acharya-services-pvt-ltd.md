## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for the purpose of debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (DASPL-21%-21-5-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0WWM07010
- BSE Code: 975944
- Record Date: December 11, 2025
- Suspension Effective From: December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational suspension aligned with existing market practices for corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD from December 11, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in the suspended debenture security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The suspension affects a specific debenture issue of a private limited company. The impact is limited to existing debenture holders and does not affect broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders - Holders of these debentures will not be able to trade the securities from December 11, 2025. However, eligible holders as on record date will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard administrative suspension to facilitate the corporate action process. Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the trading suspension for the specified ISIN.
