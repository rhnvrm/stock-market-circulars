## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 250 crores) and is admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The securities carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 5,000 units (Rs. 250 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,933.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730615
- Scrip ID: BFSL201125
- ISIN: INE01C314DL6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DL6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all trades

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 21, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 21, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments (approximately 90-day tenure) for institutional investors and expands the available commercial paper options in the BSE Debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
