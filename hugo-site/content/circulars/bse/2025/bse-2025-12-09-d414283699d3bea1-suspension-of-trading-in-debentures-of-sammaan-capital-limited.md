## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in six series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED. The suspension is effective from December 12, 2025, coinciding with the record date set by the company for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Six NCD series of SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective date: December 12, 2025
- Record date for redemption and interest payment: December 12, 2025
- Affected ISINs: INE148I07RC9, INE148I07RD7, INE148I07RE5, INE148I07RI6, INE148I07RF2, INE148I07RK2
- BSE scrip codes: 939100, 939102, 939104, 939106, 939108, 939110
- Circular reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 12, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All trading activity in these NCDs must cease from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The suspension affects six NCD series but is limited to one issuer. Holders of these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and interest as per schedule.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure no transactions are executed in these securities from December 12, 2025. This is a routine corporate action with standard operational requirements.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their principal redemption and final interest payment. The trading suspension is procedural and necessary for settlement of redemption payments. Investors should contact their brokers for redemption proceeds.
