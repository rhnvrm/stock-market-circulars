## Summary

BSE announces the launch of New Fund Offering (NFO) for ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The offering includes 10 plan variants covering both Direct and Regular plans with different IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options. All plans are categorized under the LIQUID fund category with a tentative allotment date of December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Launch of ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND on BSE StAR MF Platform
- 10 plan variants available: 5 Direct Plans and 5 Regular Plans
- Direct Plan options: Growth, Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW
- Regular Plan options: Growth, Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW
- All plans have unique ISIN numbers assigned
- Tentative allotment date: December 12, 2025
- Fund category: Liquid Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular announcing the availability of a new mutual fund offering on the BSE platform.

## Important Dates

- **Tentative Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025 (for all 10 plan variants)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine announcement of a new mutual fund NFO launch. The offering provides investors with additional options in the liquid fund category through the ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND. Liquid funds are typically used for short-term parking of funds and maintaining liquidity. The availability of multiple plan variants (Direct vs Regular, and various IDCW options) provides flexibility to different investor segments. No impact on equity markets or trading operations.
