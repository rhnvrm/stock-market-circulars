## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of Adani Enterprises Ltd. and Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited effective August 26, 2025, due to record date fixation for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension affects commercial paper of two companies
- Record date fixed as August 26, 2025 for redemption purposes
- No dealings allowed from August 26, 2025
- Both companies' commercial paper will be redeemed

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 26, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 26, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 25, 2025

## Affected Securities

**Adani Enterprises Ltd.**
- Code: AEL-27-8-25-CP
- ISIN: INE423A14XO2 (729507)
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper

**Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited**
- Code: KMIL-28-8-25-CP
- ISIN: INE975F14B83 (730066)
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the mentioned commercial paper from August 26, 2025
- Companies have fixed record date for commercial paper redemption

## Impact Assessment

Routine suspension for commercial paper redemption with minimal market impact. Standard procedure for debt instrument maturity management.
