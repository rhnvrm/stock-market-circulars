## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544603 and trading symbol GROWW with ISIN INE0HOQ01053. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited
- Scrip Code: 544603
- Trading Symbol: GROWW
- ISIN Number: INE0HOQ01053
- Listing status: Approved, pending date confirmation
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the new listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date notification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 11, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The company will become available for trading once the formal listing date is announced. Trading members should prepare systems to accommodate the new scrip. Market impact will depend on the company's size, sector, and investor interest at the time of actual listing.
