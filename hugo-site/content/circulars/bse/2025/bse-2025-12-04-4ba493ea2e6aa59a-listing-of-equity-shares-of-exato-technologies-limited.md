## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544626, symbol EXATO, and ISIN number INE1E4401010. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544626
- Trading Symbol: EXATO
- ISIN Number: INE1E4401010
- Notice Number: 20251204-12
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the company details for future trading activities
- Members should await the separate notice for listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. The impact is limited to trading members who will need to update their systems with the new scrip details. There is no immediate market-wide impact. The announcement provides advance notice to prepare for trading once the listing date is formally declared.
