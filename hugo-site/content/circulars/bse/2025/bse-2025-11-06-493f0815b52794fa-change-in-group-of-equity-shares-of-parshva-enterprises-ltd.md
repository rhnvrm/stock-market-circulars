## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 542694) will be transferred from the 'T' Group to the 'B' Group, effective November 19, 2025. This change follows the exchange's earlier notice dated October 31, 2025, and represents a positive development in the trading status of the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD
- Scrip Code: 542694
- Transfer: From 'T' Group to 'B' Group
- Effective Date: November 19, 2025
- Trading will be permitted under 'B' Group with regular settlement
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251031-64 dated October 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip will move from the 'T' Group (Trade-to-Trade settlement with higher surveillance) to the 'B' Group (regular trading group with normal settlement). This change indicates that the company has met the necessary criteria for removal from enhanced surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Trading Members should inform their clients about the change in trading group
- Settlement procedures will revert to standard norms applicable to 'B' Group stocks

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Effective date for group transfer

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from 'T' Group to 'B' Group is generally positive for the stock and its investors. It indicates improved compliance and regulatory standing. Trading will become more flexible with regular settlement cycles instead of compulsory delivery-based settlement. This may lead to improved liquidity and reduced trading restrictions for PARSHVA ENTERPRISES LTD shares.
