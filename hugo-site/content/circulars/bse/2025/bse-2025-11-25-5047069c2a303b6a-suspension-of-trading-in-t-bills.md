## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251125-2 suspending trading in Treasury Bill (T-bill) 91TB281125 with effect from November 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of this T-bill on its redemption date. Trading Members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 91TB281125 (ISIN: IN002025X224, Scrip Code: 805109) to mature on redemption date
- Trading suspended from November 26, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-764/2025-2026
- Trading Members must cease all dealings in this T-bill

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill (Scrip Code 805109) from November 26, 2025
- All open positions should be settled before the suspension date
- Members must update their trading systems to reflect the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 26, 2025
- **T-bill Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 28, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard procedure for maturing T-bills affecting a single government security instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading Members holding positions in this T-bill need to ensure settlement before suspension. The 91-day T-bill is a short-term instrument with routine maturity cycles.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date as scheduled. No action required beyond normal settlement procedures.
