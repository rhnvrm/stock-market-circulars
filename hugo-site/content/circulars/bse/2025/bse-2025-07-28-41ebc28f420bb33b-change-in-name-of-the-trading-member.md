## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member Just Trade Securities Limited (Member No. 6421) will change its name to "Bajaj Capital Markets Limited" effective Tuesday, July 29, 2025. This is an administrative name change notification to all trading members.

## Key Points

- Trading member Just Trade Securities Limited (Member No. 6421) is changing name
- New name: Bajaj Capital Markets Limited
- Effective date: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- All trading members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is purely an administrative name change for an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Just Trade Securities Limited to Bajaj Capital Markets Limited

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number (6421) remain unchanged. Other market participants should note the new name for correspondence and identification purposes.
