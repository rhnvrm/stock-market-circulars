## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Shipwaves Online Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544646, symbol SHIPWAVES, and ISIN number INE0O9L01010. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shipwaves Online Limited
- Scrip Code: 544646
- Trading Symbol: SHIPWAVES
- ISIN: INE0O9L01010
- Segment: SME
- Listing to occur in due course with separate notice for exact date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for the upcoming listing. No immediate action required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for an SME segment company. Impact is limited to trading members who may participate in trading once the shares are listed. The exact listing date and security details will be provided in a subsequent notice, allowing market participants to prepare accordingly.
