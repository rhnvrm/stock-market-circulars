## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities worth Rs 99 crore issued by Earlysalary Services Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10.70% annual interest rate with monthly payments and will mature on August 6, 2027.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: 9,900 securities at Rs 1,00,000 face value each (Rs 99 crore total)
- Interest rate: 10.70% per annum with monthly payments
- Scrip Code: 977073
- ISIN: INE01YL07391
- Credit Rating: CARE A-
- Maturity: August 6, 2027
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for the securities is set at 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 29, 2025
- Listing Date: September 2, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from October 6, 2025 to August 6, 2027
- Maturity Date: August 6, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The CARE A- rating indicates adequate credit quality. Monthly interest payments provide regular income stream for investors.
