## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Titan Company Limited on private placement basis. The CP worth Rs. 1,000 crores (20,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) has been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The instrument carries a CARE A1+ credit rating and matures on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issue Size**: Rs. 1,000 crores (20,000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730755
- **Scrip ID**: TCL111225
- **ISIN**: INE280A14518
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions are conducted only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE280A14518
- Trading must be done in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Commercial Paper is a standard working capital financing tool used by corporates. The A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term debt instruments. This listing does not affect equity shareholders or general market operations. Only institutional investors and debt market participants engaged in money market instruments are impacted.
