## Summary

BSE has announced the detailed schedule for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of BANK OF MAHARASHTRA shares scheduled for December 2-3, 2025. The base offer size is 38,45,77,748 shares with an oversubscription option of 7,69,15,549 shares, totaling 46,14,93,297 shares. The floor price is set at Rs. 54.00 per share with a face value of Rs. 10. Institutional and Non-Institutional investors can bid on December 2, 2025 (T Day), while Retail Investors and Employees can bid on December 3, 2025 (T+1 Day). The allocation will follow price priority method.

## Key Points

- Security: BANK OF MAHARASHTRA (Symbol: MAHABANK, Scrip Code: 532525, ISIN: INE457A01014)
- Base Offer Size: 38,45,77,748 shares with oversubscription option of 7,69,15,549 shares
- Total OFS Size (including green shoe): 46,14,93,297 shares
- Floor Price: Rs. 54.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- Retail Reservation: 10% of issue size (3,84,57,775 base + 76,91,555 oversubscription = 4,61,49,330 total)
- Non-Retail Offer: 34,61,19,973 base shares + 6,92,23,994 oversubscription = 41,53,43,967 total
- Employee Reservation: 75,000 shares
- Market Lot: 1 share, Tick Size: Rs. 0.01
- Allocation Method: Price Priority Method
- Seller may exercise oversubscription option by 5 PM on December 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational details for an OFS transaction under existing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodian Members:

- Must facilitate bidding through designated platforms (iBBS for internet/leased line, RTRMS)
- Must ensure proper margin collection as per investor category
- Must process give up/take up requests within specified timelines

### Platform Access URLs:
- iBBS Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/
- iBBS Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/
- RTRMS Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/
- RTRMS Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/
- Extranet Internet: https://member.bseindia.com/
- Extranet Leased Line: https://memberll.bseindia.com/

### Margin Requirements by Category:
- **Retail Investors (RI/RIC)**: Bid value up to Rs. 2 lakhs - 100% upfront margin (Cash & Cash Equivalents)
- **Employees (EMP)**: Bid value up to Rs. 5 lakhs - 100% upfront margin (Cash & Cash Equivalents)
- **Non-Institutional Investors (NII)**: 100% upfront margin (Cash only)
- **Institutional Investors (MF/IC/OTHS)**: 100% upfront margin OR 0% margin option (Cash only)

## Important Dates

### December 2, 2025 (T Day):
- **9:15 AM - 3:30 PM**: Bid entry, modification and cancellation for IC/MF/OTH/NII categories
- **By 5:00 PM**: Seller to intimate stock exchanges regarding oversubscription option exercise

### December 3, 2025 (T+1 Day):
- **Up to 7:30 AM**: Give up/Take up window for Institutional Investor bids with 0% margin
- **9:15 AM - 3:30 PM**: Bid entry, modification and cancellation for RI/RIC/EMP categories
- **9:15 AM - 3:30 PM**: Unallocated bidders from T Day can carry forward and modify bids

### December 4, 2025 (T+2 Day):
- **Up to 7:30 AM**: Give up/Take up window for unallocated bidders from T+1 Day
- Settlement for various investor categories
