## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544628) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 22, 2025. This follows the company's SME IPO and represents a transition from restricted trading to normal trading operations.

## Key Points

- Company: ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544628
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, December 22, 2025
- Notice references prior circular dated December 05, 2025 (Notice No. 20251205-56)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement. This change allows for:
- Intraday trading (previously restricted under T2T)
- Normal delivery-based trading
- Standard rolling settlement cycle
- Removal of compulsory delivery requirements applicable under MT Group

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Trading Members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications
- No specific action required from investors, though they should be aware of the changed trading dynamics

## Important Dates

- **December 5, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251205-56)
- **December 8, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued
- **December 22, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically increases liquidity and trading flexibility for the stock. Investors will benefit from the ability to engage in intraday trading and normal rolling settlement procedures.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading Members need to update their systems and inform clients about the change. The transition is routine for SME IPO stocks that have completed their initial restricted trading period.

**Investor Impact**: Positive - Shareholders gain improved liquidity and reduced trading restrictions, making the stock more accessible for regular trading activities.
