## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis. The securities commenced trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 10, 2025. The issue comprises 10,000 debentures with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each, totaling ₹100 crore, carrying an interest rate of 8.80% per annum with quarterly interest payments and a maturity date of December 6, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 debentures
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: ₹100 crore
- Interest Rate: 8.80% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Scrip Code: 977353
- Scrip ID: 880IFSL27
- ISIN: INE321N07590
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE321N07590
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Members requiring clarification may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: March 9, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from March 9, 2026 to December 6, 2027
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 6, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a private placement debt issue with no public offer component. The listing provides liquidity to existing holders but has minimal impact on broader equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt market investors seeking AA-rated corporate bonds with quarterly interest payments. The 8.80% coupon rate provides a competitive yield for the rating category.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now facilitate trading in these securities on the BSE Debt segment for clients interested in InCred Financial Services' debt instruments.
