## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Neueon Corporation Limited (formerly Neueon Towers Limited) in T Group from December 23, 2025, following approval of a resolution plan by NCLT Hyderabad under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016. The company undergoes significant restructuring with 90% equity allotted to the Successful Resolution Applicant and public shareholding reduced to 10%. Trading will continue in Trade-to-Trade segment.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Neueon Towers Limited to Neueon Corporation Limited effective December 23, 2025
- Scrip Code: 532887, ISIN: INE333I01044, Symbol: NEUEON
- Listed in T Group with Trade-to-Trade segment trading only
- Total equity shares: 5,65,44,552 fully paid-up shares of Rs. 1/- each (reduced from Rs. 10/-)
- Face value reduced from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 1/- per share through capital reduction
- 90% shareholding transferred to Successful Resolution Applicant (SRA) or nominees
- Public shareholding reduced to 10% (including erstwhile promoters)
- Part of special pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Equity shares credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012

## Regulatory Changes

The listing follows NCLT-approved Resolution Plan under IBC provisions:
- Resolution Plan approved by NCLT Hyderabad Bench on October 23, 2024
- Complies with SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012, CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012)
- Complies with SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 (April 11, 2023) for pre-open session
- Dematerialized shares handled as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 (August 02, 2012)
- Trading continues under BSE Notice No. 20250702-33 dated July 02, 2025 for Trade-to-Trade segment

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing public shareholders face significant dilution, retaining only 10% of total equity while 90% transfers to the Resolution Applicant. Face value reduction from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 1/- affects per-share metrics.

**Trading Impact**: Classification in T Group with Trade-to-Trade segment means no intraday trading, delivery-based settlement only, and higher surveillance - typically indicating regulatory concerns or volatility.

**Market Perception**: Emergence from insolvency proceedings under IBC signals corporate restructuring. The "Corporate Debtor" designation and restrictive trading category may impact liquidity and investor sentiment negatively.

**Operational Impact**: Use of temporary ISIN for dematerialized shares requires coordination between depositories and market participants for proper credit to investor accounts post-restructuring.
