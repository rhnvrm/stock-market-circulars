## Summary

BSE notifies that NAVI NIFTY MIDSMALLCAP 400 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 11, 2025. The fund will support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan growth options will be available.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: December 11, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two scheme options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF959L01HS2) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF959L01HR4)
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Scheme category: Equity
- References previous Notice No. 20251121-66

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of a previously restricted mutual fund.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can resume normal transactions in the specified schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025 - Reopening of fund for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational update affecting only investors and distributors of NAVI NIFTY MIDSMALLCAP 400 INDEX FUND. The reopening provides liquidity and access to the fund after a temporary closure period. No impact on broader market operations or other securities.
