## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities of Hedge Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 3, 2025. The securities include a 10.50% coupon bond maturing in 2029 and an 11% coupon bond maturing in 2031, both with monthly interest payments and IVR BB+ Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities of Hedge Finance Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Both securities issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 100,000 each
- Allotment date: December 2, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit for both securities

## Security Details

### Security 1:
- **Scrip Code**: 977336
- **Scrip ID**: 1050HFL29
- **ISIN**: INE01ZK08638
- **Interest Rate**: 10.50% p.a.
- **Maturity Date**: August 2, 2029
- **Quantity**: 1,000 units
- **Credit Rating**: IVR BB+ Stable

### Security 2:
- **Scrip Code**: 977337
- **Scrip ID**: 11HFL31
- **ISIN**: INE01ZK08646
- **Interest Rate**: 11% p.a.
- **Maturity Date**: June 2, 2031
- **Quantity**: 1,000 units
- **Credit Rating**: IVR BB+ Stable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed information
- Contact debt department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly
- **Maturity Date (Security 1)**: August 2, 2029
- **Maturity Date (Security 2)**: June 2, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine debt security listing affecting only participants in the debt market segment and investors interested in Hedge Finance Limited's debt instruments. The securities carry BB+ credit rating indicating moderate credit risk. No put/call options are available on either security.
