## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730597, ISIN: INE01C314DK8) due to its upcoming maturity on November 25, 2025. Trading members are instructed not to deal in this commercial paper effective from November 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper: BFSL-25-11-25-CP
- Issuer: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- Scrip Code: 730597
- ISIN: INE01C314DK8
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: November 24, 2025
- Reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt instruments approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified commercial paper from November 24, 2025
- Members should ensure no new deals are executed in this instrument
- Existing positions should be settled as per standard settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 25, 2025**: Commercial paper redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension is a standard pre-redemption procedure to prevent trading in instruments about to mature. Only holders of this specific commercial paper are affected, and the impact is limited to orderly redemption processing. No broader market implications expected.
