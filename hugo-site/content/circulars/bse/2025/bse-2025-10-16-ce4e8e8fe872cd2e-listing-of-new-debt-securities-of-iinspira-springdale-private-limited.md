## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by IINSPIRA SPRINGDALE PRIVATE LIMITED on private placement basis, effective October 16, 2025. The securities comprise 10,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 20% annual interest rate and maturing on September 30, 2028. The securities are rated ACUITE B/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Issuer: IINSPIRA SPRINGDALE PRIVATE LIMITED
- Quantity: 10,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code: 977214
- Scrip ID: 20ISPL28
- ISIN: INE2C4407019
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: October 15, 2025
- Interest Rate: 20% per annum
- Interest Payment: Monthly from March 31, 2026 to September 30, 2028
- Maturity Date: September 30, 2028
- Credit Rating: ACUITE B/Stable
- Put/Call Option: Not Available
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE2C4407019
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: March 31, 2026 to September 30, 2028 (Monthly)
- **Redemption Date**: September 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The listing affects only specific debt market participants interested in private placement securities. The B credit rating indicates higher credit risk, suitable for risk-tolerant investors. The relatively high interest rate of 20% per annum reflects the risk profile of the issuer. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for client advisory purposes.
