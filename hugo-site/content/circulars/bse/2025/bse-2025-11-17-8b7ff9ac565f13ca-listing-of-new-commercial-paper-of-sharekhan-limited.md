## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to dealings 6000 new Commercial Papers issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. Each Commercial Paper has a face value of Rs.5,00,000 and carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+. The securities will be traded only in dematerialized form with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- 6000 Commercial Papers issued by Sharekhan Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs.4,91,423.65 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730577, Scrip ID: SL141125
- ISIN Number: INE211H14AG0
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE211H14AG0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)
