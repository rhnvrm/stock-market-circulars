## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of TRUHOME FINANCE LIMITED with ISIN INE432R07422 (Code: 975927) effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from December 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- TRUHOME FINANCE LIMITED debentures (8.94% coupon, maturity 26-12-25) suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE432R07422, BSE Code: 975927
- Record date set for December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members instructed to cease dealings from December 11, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational measure implemented when debentures approach redemption to ensure proper settlement and prevent trading after the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE432R07422) from December 11, 2025
- **Trading Members**: Required to take note of the suspension and inform clients holding or interested in these securities
- **Investors**: Cannot trade these debentures on BSE from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: Circular issued by BSE
- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 11, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective (no dealings from this date)
- **December 26, 2025**: Expected maturity date (based on debenture nomenclature 26-12-25)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects only holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and expected as part of the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: High for debenture holders - They must ensure their holdings are properly recorded before the record date to receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading opportunity available after December 10, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Standard procedure for debt securities approaching maturity. Trading members need to update their systems and communicate with affected clients to prevent attempted trades in the suspended security.
