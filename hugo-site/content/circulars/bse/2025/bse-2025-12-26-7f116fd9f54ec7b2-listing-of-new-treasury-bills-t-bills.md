## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the Debt Instruments segment (G GROUP) effective from December 29, 2025. The T-Bills have varying maturity periods of 91, 182, and 364 days.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills will be listed on BSE's Debt segment from December 29, 2025
- All T-Bills have a market lot size of 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each maturity/redemption date
- Securities classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS

## New Treasury Bills Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|
| 805160 | IN002025X398 | 91TB270326 | March 27, 2026 |
| 805161 | IN002025Y396 | 182TB26626 | June 26, 2026 |
| 805162 | IN002025Z393 | 364T251226 | December 25, 2026 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to the maturity/redemption date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Trading members should contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**:
  - 91-day T-Bill: March 27, 2026
  - 182-day T-Bill: June 26, 2026
  - 364-day T-Bill: December 25, 2026
- **Trading Suspension**: Two working days before each respective maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It provides standard listing information for new government treasury bills, affecting only debt market participants who trade in government securities. No significant operational or compliance burden is imposed on trading members.
