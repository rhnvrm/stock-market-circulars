## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by DLF Cyber City Developers Limited on private placement basis effective December 18, 2025. The securities consist of 100,000 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (total issue size Rs. 100 crores), carrying an interest rate of 6.98% p.a. with annual interest payments until maturity on June 17, 2030. The securities are rated AAA by both CRISIL and ICRA and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977384, Scrip ID: 698DLFC30
- ISIN: INE186K07148
- Quantity: 100,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value/Paid-up Value/Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 17, 2025
- Interest Rate: 6.98% p.a. (payable annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable
- Redemption Date: June 17, 2030
- Call Option: Available as per Key Information Document
- Trading: Dematerialized form only with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE186K07148
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 18, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Annually from December 17, 2026 to June 17, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** June 17, 2030

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of a single issuer. The securities provide institutional and qualified investors with access to AAA-rated debt instruments from DLF Cyber City Developers Limited. The listing expands the debt segment offerings on BSE and provides liquidity options for investors holding these securities. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
