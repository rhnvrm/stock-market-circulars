## Summary

BSE has notified members that GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF (Fund of Funds) will be reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions, along with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 24, 2025. This follows an earlier notice (20251202-21) regarding the scheme's status.

## Key Points

- GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from December 24, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under EQUITY category
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Notice references previous circular No. 20251202-21

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF666M01NF6)
2. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF666M01NG4)
3. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF666M01NH2)

### Direct Plan Options
4. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF666M01NC3)
5. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF666M01ND1)
6. **GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF666M01NE9)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transaction facilities for an existing fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems to enable transactions in these schemes from December 24, 2025
- Ensure all six ISIN codes are activated for trading on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Members should inform investors about the availability of subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Effective Date for Transactions**: December 24, 2025 (ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP facilities become available)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a scheme-specific operational update affecting only investors and distributors dealing with GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for existing and prospective investors in this fund. Investors can now resume or initiate investments through various modes (lump sum, SIP) and set up systematic plans (STP, SWP) starting December 24, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform participants need to ensure their systems are configured to accept transactions for all six scheme variants from the effective date.
