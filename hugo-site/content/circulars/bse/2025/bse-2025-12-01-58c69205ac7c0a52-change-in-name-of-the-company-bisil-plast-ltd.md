## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Bisil Plast Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Karnawati Innovation Limited with effect from October 23, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Bisil Plast Private Limited and holds CIN L17119GJ1986PLC009009.

## Key Points

- Old Name: BISIL PLAST LIMITED
- New Name: KARNAWATI INNOVATION LIMITED
- Original Name: BISIL PLAST PRIVATE LIMITED
- CIN: L17119GJ1986PLC009009
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved and digitally signed by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Registered Office: Office No. 702/3, 7th Floor, Shilp Aaron, Sindhu Bhavan Road, Thaltej, Daskroi, Ahmedabad-380059, Gujarat

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change processed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Bisil Plast Limited) along with the new name (Karnawati Innovation Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, obligations, and legal proceedings continue under the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status and view public documents on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 23, 2025
- **Certificate Digitally Signed**: October 23, 2025, 10:10:16 IST
- **Old Name Display Requirement**: Two years from October 23, 2025 (until October 23, 2027)
- **Circular Published**: December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect the fundamental business operations or financial position of the company. Investors and traders should note the new name for identification purposes.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative changes required for updating letterheads, signage, banking details, and other corporate documentation. All legal and financial obligations continue seamlessly under the new name.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights, existing contracts, or pending legal proceedings. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory records and filings.
