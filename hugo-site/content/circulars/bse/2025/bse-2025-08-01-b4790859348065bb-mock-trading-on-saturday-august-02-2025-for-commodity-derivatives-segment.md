## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 02, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and software in various market conditions including exceptional scenarios.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 02, 2025
- Log-in period: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Members can choose between mock trading or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Third-party trading platforms and in-house systems can test various functionalities
- Testing includes risk-reduction mode, trading halt scenarios

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Session conducted in compliance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 regarding testing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in mock trading
- Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Feedback must be provided by 4:30 PM on the same day
- Members must refer to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 for testing guidelines

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: August 02, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Log-in Window**: 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM
- **Trading Session**: 11:00 AM - 03:30 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 4:30 PM on August 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a mock trading session with no real financial obligations. Trades executed during the session will not create any rights, liabilities, margin obligations, or settlement requirements. The session serves purely for system testing and member familiarization with trading platforms and exceptional market condition scenarios.
