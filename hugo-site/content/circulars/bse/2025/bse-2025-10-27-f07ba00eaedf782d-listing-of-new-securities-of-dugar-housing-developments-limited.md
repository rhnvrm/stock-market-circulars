## Summary

BSE has listed 27,00,000 equity shares of Dugar Housing Developments Limited (Scrip Code: 511634) with effect from Tuesday, October 28, 2025. The shares were issued at Rs. 12/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 2/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to varying lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares**: 27,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 12/- per share (including premium of Rs. 2/-)
- **Allotment Category**: Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- **Purpose**: Conversion of warrants
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 7000001 to 9700000
- **Date of Allotment**: August 12, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE919M01026
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities issued pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

**Lock-in Period Compliance**:
- 4,86,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7000001-7486000): Locked-in until April 30, 2027
- 19,44,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7486001-9430000): Locked-in until April 30, 2026
- 54,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9430001-9484000): Locked-in until April 30, 2027
- 2,16,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9484001-9700000): Locked-in until April 30, 2026

Trading members must ensure compliance with the specified lock-in restrictions for the respective share tranches.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: 
  - April 30, 2026 (for 21,60,000 shares)
  - April 30, 2027 (for 5,40,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific listing event with minimal market-wide implications.

**Shareholder Impact**: The listing increases the outstanding shares of Dugar Housing Developments Limited by 27,00,000 equity shares, representing potential dilution for existing shareholders. However, the preferential allotment to Non-Promoters and the lock-in periods provide stability.

**Trading Impact**: The shares are available for trading from October 28, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions. Trading members should note the lock-in schedule when processing trades to ensure compliance.

**Liquidity**: The addition of new shares may improve liquidity in the scrip, though the lock-in periods on all shares will restrict immediate free float.
