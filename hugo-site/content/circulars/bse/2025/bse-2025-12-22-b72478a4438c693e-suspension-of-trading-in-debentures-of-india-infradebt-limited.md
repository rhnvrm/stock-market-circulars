## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of India Infradebt Limited (ISIN: INE537P07083, Code: 953237) effective December 24, 2025. The suspension is necessitated by the company's record date fixation for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Company: India Infradebt Limited
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (8.6% coupon, maturity 8-1-26)
- ISIN: INE537P07083
- BSE Code: 953237
- Record Date: December 24, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 24, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-784/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure aligned with existing BSE protocols for corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to note this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All pending orders for these debentures should be cancelled or managed appropriately

## Important Dates

- **December 22, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 24, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective (no dealings permitted)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and prospective traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature, facilitating the redemption process and interest payment distribution.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trading in the affected ISIN. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and interest payments as per the record date. No impact on other securities of India Infradebt Limited or the broader debt market.
