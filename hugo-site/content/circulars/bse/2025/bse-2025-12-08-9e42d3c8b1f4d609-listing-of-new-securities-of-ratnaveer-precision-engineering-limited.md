## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted 12,793,102 new equity shares of Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543978, ISIN: INE05CZ01011) for trading. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 145 per share. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and will commence trading on December 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- 12,793,102 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each listed
- Allotted to QIBs under Qualified Institutional Placement
- Issue price: Rs. 145 per share
- Distinctive numbers: 53049390 to 65842491
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 543978
- ISIN: INE05CZ01011

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a capital raising exercise by Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited through QIP. The addition of 12.79 million shares will increase the company's equity base and float. Impact is company-specific with no broader market implications. The QIP mechanism indicates institutional investor interest in the company. Trading members can facilitate transactions in these securities from December 9, 2025.
