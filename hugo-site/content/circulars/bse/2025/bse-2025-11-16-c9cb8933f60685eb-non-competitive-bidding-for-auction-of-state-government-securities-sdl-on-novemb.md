## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of non-competitive bidding facility for the auction of State Government Securities (SDL) scheduled by RBI on November 18, 2025. Trading members can participate in the auction for 14 SDL securities across 9 states with a total issue size of Rs. 13,600 crores through the NCB-GSec module of BSE's iBBS web-based system.

## Key Points

- 14 State Government Securities from 9 states (Arunachal Pradesh, Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh) available for non-competitive bidding
- Total issue size: Rs. 13,600 crores
- Bidding through NCB-GSec module on iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com)
- Minimum subscription: 100 units (Rs. 10,000)
- Maximum bid limits range from Rs. 2 crores to Rs. 15 crores per security
- Mix of new issues (13 securities) and one re-issue (7.01% TN 2032)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20191122-28 dated November 22, 2019 regarding the launch of non-competitive bidding facility for State Government Securities auctions.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Submit bids through NCB-GSec module by November 18, 2025, 8:00 AM
- Adhere to minimum subscription of 100 units (Rs. 10,000) and multiples thereof
- Comply with maximum bid limits specified for each security (Rs. 2-15 crores)
- Ensure timely settlement obligations and pay-in requirements

**For Direct Investors:**
- Submit bids by November 17, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025, 10:00 AM**: Bid collection start date (24-hour availability)
- **November 17, 2025**: Bid collection end date for direct investors
- **November 18, 2025, 8:00 AM**: Bid collection end date for trading members
- **November 18, 2025**: Auction date
- **November 19, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine debt market operational circular specific to government securities auction. No impact on equity markets or stock prices.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for debt market participants - Trading members dealing in government securities need to be aware of the bidding timeline and process requirements. The 24-hour bidding window provides adequate time for participation.

**Investor Impact:** Low - Limited to institutional investors and trading members who actively participate in government securities auctions. Retail equity investors are not affected.
