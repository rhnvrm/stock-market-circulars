## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 20,388,000 new equity shares of U. H. ZAVERI LIMITED (Scrip Code: 541338) issued through a rights issue. The shares will commence trading on Monday, November 17, 2025. These shares were issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each and are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 20,388,000 equity shares issued at par (Rs. 10/- per share) on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 541338
- ISIN: INE556Z01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 10194001 to 30582000
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: November 17, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities in their systems
- New shares are available for trading from the effective date
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action involving the listing of rights issue shares for a single company. The rights issue increases the equity base of U. H. ZAVERI LIMITED by approximately 20.39 million shares.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive number ranges. The new shares have the same rights and privileges as existing shares, requiring no special treatment in trading or settlement operations.
