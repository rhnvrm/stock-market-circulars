## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, September 6, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, with members able to test their trading applications and systems. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released during this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for September 6, 2025
- Covers Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 to be released
- Members can choose between Mock Trading Session or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Trading members using third-party platforms can test various functionalities
- No real trading obligations - no margins, pay-in/pay-out, or legal liabilities

## Trading Schedule

- Log-in: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives): 11:00 AM to 3:30 PM
- Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives): 11:00 AM to 3:30 PM
- Trade Modification (Currency): 3:40 PM
- Trade Modification (Cross Currency): 3:40 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate in testing as per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Feedback required by 4:30 PM on the same day
- Active participation encouraged for system testing

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Date: September 6, 2025
- Feedback Deadline: September 6, 2025 by 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine technical exercise with no market impact. The mock trading creates no real obligations or liabilities and is purely for testing trading systems and familiarization with new software versions.
