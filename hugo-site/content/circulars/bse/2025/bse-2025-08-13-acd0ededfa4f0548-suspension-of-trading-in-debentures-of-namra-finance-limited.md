## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date of August 15, 2025 for interest payment and debenture redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED debentures (Code: NFL-11.95%-20-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE229U07129)
- Record date set for August 15, 2025
- No dealings permitted from August 14, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and inform clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 13, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** August 14, 2025
- **Record Date:** August 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one company's debt securities. Routine corporate action with temporary trading suspension to facilitate proper record-keeping for interest payment and redemption process.
