## Summary

BSE has listed 1,73,48,70,187 fully paid-up equity shares of Lenskart Solutions Limited in demat form effective November 6, 2025. The listing includes various categories of shares with different lock-in periods ranging from 30 days to 18 months, with a substantial portion of shares subject to lock-in restrictions until May 2026 and May 2027.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares listed: 1,73,48,70,187 (all in demat form)
- Listing date: November 6, 2025
- Registered Office: Plot No. 151, Okhla Industrial Estate, Phase III, New Delhi â€“ 110 020
- Company Secretary: Preeti Gupta (Membership No.: A29209)
- All shares are fully paid-up and in dematerialized form
- Multiple tranches with varying lock-in periods
- Significant portion of shares under lock-in until May 2026 and May 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The listing of Lenskart Solutions Limited represents a significant addition to BSE's equity market. With over 173 crore shares listed, this is a major IPO that will impact market capitalization and trading volumes.

**Liquidity Considerations**: A substantial portion of the shares are under lock-in restrictions, which will significantly limit the free float available for trading in the initial months. The staggered lock-in release dates (December 2025, February 2026, May 2026, and May 2027) will gradually increase liquidity over 18 months.

**Investor Categories**: The listing includes diverse investor categories including anchor investors, promoters/early investors (long lock-ins), ESOP beneficiaries, and AIFs with varying lock-in requirements, reflecting a well-structured IPO allocation.

**Compliance**: Lock-in details are provided as per pages 193-196 of the Company's Prospectus and comply with SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.
