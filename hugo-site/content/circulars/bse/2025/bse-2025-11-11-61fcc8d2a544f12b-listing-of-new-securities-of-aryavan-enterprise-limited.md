## Summary

BSE has listed 8,75,000 new equity shares of Aryavan Enterprise Limited (Scrip Code: 539455) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from November 12, 2025, with a lock-in period until May 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8,75,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 33.60/-
- Issue price: Rs. 43.60/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE360S01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 6087901 to 6962900
- Date of Allotment: March 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now available for trading on the Exchange, subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: March 19, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares resulting from warrant conversion. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until May 15, 2026, preventing immediate trading by allottees. The addition represents new equity capital for Aryavan Enterprise Limited and minor dilution for existing shareholders.
