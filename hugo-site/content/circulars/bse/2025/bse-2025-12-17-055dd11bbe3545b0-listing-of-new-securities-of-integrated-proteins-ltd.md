## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,55,10,000 new equity shares of Integrated Proteins Ltd (Scrip Code: 519606) effective from December 18, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 11.25 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 1.25) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The allotment date was August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Integrated Proteins Ltd
- **Scrip Code**: 519606
- **ISIN**: INE177M01013
- **Number of Shares**: 1,55,10,000 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 11.25 per share (including premium of Rs. 1.25)
- **Allottees**: Non-Promoters
- **Basis**: Preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion
- **Trading Commencement**: December 18, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 3203601 to 18713600
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- All 15,510,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 3203601 to 18713600) are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2026
- Securities will trade under the existing scrip code 519606

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: December 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing action with limited market-wide impact. The preferential allotment increases the equity base of Integrated Proteins Ltd by 15.51 million shares. The lock-in period until June 30, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be sold immediately, providing stability. Trading members need to be aware of the increased share capital and lock-in restrictions for compliance purposes. The shares rank equally with existing equity shares in terms of rights and entitlements.
