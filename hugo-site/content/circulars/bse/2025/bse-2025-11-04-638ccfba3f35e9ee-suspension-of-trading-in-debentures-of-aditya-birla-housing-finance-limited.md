## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHF) effective November 13, 2025. The suspension is necessitated by the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture affected: ABHF-7.95%-28-11-25-PVT (ISIN: INE831R07342, Scrip Code: 974391)
- Record date set: November 13, 2025
- Trading suspension starts: November 13, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-755/2025-2026
- Notice date: November 4, 2025 (issued November 3, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure where trading is suspended to determine eligible debenture holders for redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified ABHF debentures from November 13, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No dealings permitted in scrip code 974391 from the suspension date

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| November 3, 2025 | Circular issued |
| November 4, 2025 | Notice date |
| November 13, 2025 | Record date for debenture redemption and interest payment |
| November 13, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects only holders and potential traders of specific ABHF debentures. The suspension is temporary and related to a scheduled corporate action.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders as of the record date (November 13, 2025) will be eligible for redemption proceeds and interest payment. No trading will be possible from that date.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified debenture and communicate the suspension to affected clients holding or interested in these securities.
