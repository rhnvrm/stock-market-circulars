## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new Commercial Papers issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 7, 2025. The instruments consist of 5,500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, with a maturity period of 90 days.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730506
- Scrip ID: IHF061125
- ISIN: INE071G14HE0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 5,500 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per instrument
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,627 per instrument
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HE0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 7, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 7, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 7, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE's debt segment for institutional investors and trading members dealing with short-term commercial papers. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of ICICI Home Finance Company Limited. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or general retail investors.
