## Summary

BSE has announced that ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will observe a non-business day on November 24, 2025 on the BSE StAR MF Platform. This is an update to the previously communicated non-business day schedule for mutual fund schemes.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will not be available for transactions on November 24, 2025
- This applies specifically to the BSE StAR MF Platform
- This is an update to the existing non-business day schedule

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding platform availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and investors should note the non-business day when planning transactions
- No transactions will be processed for this scheme on the specified date on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This affects only investors of ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund who wish to transact through BSE StAR MF Platform on November 24, 2025. Investors can plan their transactions accordingly on other available business days.
