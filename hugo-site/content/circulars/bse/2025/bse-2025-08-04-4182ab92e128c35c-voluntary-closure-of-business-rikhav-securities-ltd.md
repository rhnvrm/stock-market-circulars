## Summary

Rikhav Securities Ltd (Member No. 3174) has voluntarily ceased operations in the Currency derivative segment effective August 04, 2025. This is a business closure decision by the member firm.

## Key Points

- Rikhav Securities Ltd (Member No. 3174) ceases currency derivative operations
- Effective date: August 04, 2025
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a member business decision.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the cessation. No specific compliance actions required for other members.

## Important Dates

- **August 04, 2025**: Effective date of business cessation

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one member's operations in currency derivatives. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected.
