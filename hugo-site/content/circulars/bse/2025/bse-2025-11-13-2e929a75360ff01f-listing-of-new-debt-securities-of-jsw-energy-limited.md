## Summary

JSW Energy Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 25,000 units at a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling Rs. 250 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.05% per annum with annual interest payment and mature on November 10, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Issued**: 25,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 977291
- **Scrip ID**: 805JSWEL28
- **ISIN**: INE121E08070
- **Interest Rate**: 8.05% per annum (paid annually)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: November 10, 2028
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-/STABLE, IND AA-/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE121E08070
- The tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: November 10, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides JSW Energy Limited with additional debt financing of Rs. 250 crore at competitive rates (8.05% p.a.). The strong credit ratings (ICRA AA-/STABLE, IND AA-/Stable) indicate good creditworthiness. There is no impact on equity markets or trading operations. The securities are available for trading in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment for institutional and eligible investors.
