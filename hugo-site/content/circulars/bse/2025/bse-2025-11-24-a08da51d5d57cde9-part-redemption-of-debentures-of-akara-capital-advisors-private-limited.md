## Summary

AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED has announced a part redemption of its 13.30% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE08XP07332) along with interest payment. The record date has been set as December 6, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value from December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture: ACAPL-13.30%-21-02-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE08XP07332
- Scrip Code: 977052
- Record Date: December 6, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- New reduced face value: Rs. 66,666.73 per debenture
- Effective date for reduced face value: December 5, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-771/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value effective from December 5, 2025
- Trading members should ensure their systems reflect the reduced face value for these debentures from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **December 5, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-771/2025-2026)
- **December 6, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific debt instrument of a private limited company. Only debenture holders of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED will be affected. The part redemption will reduce the outstanding face value of the debentures to Rs. 66,666.73 per unit. Trading members dealing in this specific instrument need to update their records to reflect the reduced face value from the effective date to ensure proper settlement and valuation.
