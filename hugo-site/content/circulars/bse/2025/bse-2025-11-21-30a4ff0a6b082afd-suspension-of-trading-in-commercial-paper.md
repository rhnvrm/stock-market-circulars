## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the suspension of trading in commercial papers (CPs) of 13 companies due to upcoming redemptions. The suspension is effective from November 27-28, 2025, as these companies have fixed record dates for CP redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified CPs from the suspension date
- 12 CPs have suspension effective November 27, 2025
- 1 CP (CaratLane) has suspension effective November 28, 2025
- Notice category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment
- Notice number: 20251121-7

1. Credila Financial Services Limited (HCFSL-28-11-25-CP, ISIN: INE539K14BS1)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified commercial papers from the respective suspension dates
- No transactions should be executed in these CPs on or after the no-dealings date
- Reference notices: DR-765/2025-2026 (for 12 CPs) and DR-766/2025-2026 (for CaratLane)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Record Date (12 companies):** November 27, 2025
- **Trading Suspension (12 companies):** November 27, 2025
- **Record Date (CaratLane):** November 28, 2025
- **Trading Suspension (CaratLane):** November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Purpose:** Commercial Paper maturity and redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to debt market participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers. The suspension is a routine corporate action for CP redemption and does not indicate any credit concerns.

**Operational Impact:** Investors holding these CPs should note the redemption dates. Trading members must update their systems to prevent transactions in these securities from the specified dates. The suspension affects only the listed commercial paper segment and does not impact equity trading of these companies.
