## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis with effect from December 15, 2025. The issue consists of 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 200 crores. The Commercial Paper is rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a maturity date of March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: 360 ONE WAM LIMITED
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crores (4,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,562 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730769
- **Scrip ID**: OWL12125
- **ISIN**: INE466L14FM9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FM9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine commercial paper issuance on private placement basis. The impact is limited to:

- **Debt Market Participants**: Provides a new short-term debt instrument (88-day tenor) with high credit rating for investment
- **360 ONE WAM LIMITED**: Enhances liquidity and provides working capital funding at competitive rates
- **Trading Volume**: Expected to have negligible impact on overall market volumes given the private placement nature and institutional focus

The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
