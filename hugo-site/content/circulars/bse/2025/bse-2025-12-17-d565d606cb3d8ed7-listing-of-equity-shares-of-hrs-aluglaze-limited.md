## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544656, symbol HRS, and ISIN number INE1E1V01017. This is an SME segment listing, with the specific listing date and security details to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544656
- Trading Symbol: HRS
- ISIN Number: INE1E1V01017
- Listing date and security details will be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the company details for the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for the SME segment. The impact is limited to investors and traders interested in HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED. The company will provide additional liquidity options for shareholders once trading commences. Market participants should await the subsequent notice for exact listing date and trading commencement details.
