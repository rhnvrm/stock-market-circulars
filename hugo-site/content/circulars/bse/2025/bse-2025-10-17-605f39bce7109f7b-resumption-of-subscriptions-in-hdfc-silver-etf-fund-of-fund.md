## Summary

HDFC Mutual Fund has announced the resumption of all subscriptions in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund effective October 17, 2025. This follows an earlier addendum dated October 15, 2025 that had imposed restrictions. The decision to resume subscriptions was made in view of current market conditions. All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- All subscriptions in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund resume without restrictions
- Previous restrictions were imposed via addendum dated October 15, 2025
- Decision based on assessment of current market conditions
- All other scheme terms and conditions remain unchanged
- This addendum forms integral part of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update to existing scheme operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors should read all scheme related documents carefully
- Standard mutual fund investment risk disclosures apply
- Addendum to be incorporated into SID/KIM documentation

## Important Dates

- **October 15, 2025**: Previous addendum imposing restrictions
- **October 17, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of all subscriptions without restrictions
- **October 17, 2025**: Addendum date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a fund-specific operational update affecting only investors in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund. The resumption of subscriptions indicates normalization of fund operations and improved market conditions for silver investments. No impact on equity trading or broader market participants.
