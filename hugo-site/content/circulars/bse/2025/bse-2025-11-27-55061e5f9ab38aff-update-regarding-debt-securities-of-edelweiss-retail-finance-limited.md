## Summary

BSE has updated the scrip names and abbreviations for 8 debt securities of Edelweiss Retail Finance Limited (ERFL) to reflect a name change to ECL Finance (ECL). The update affects NCDs and perpetual bonds with maturity dates ranging from 2026 to 2028, with coupon rates between 8.88% and 10.40%.

## Key Points

- 8 debt securities affected by name change from ERFL to ECL
- Includes 5 private placements and 3 NCDs
- 2 perpetual bonds included in the update
- Scrip codes and ISIN numbers remain unchanged
- Only scrip names, abbreviations, and scrip IDs updated

## Affected Securities

### Private Placements
- 955156 (INE528S08027): ERFL-9.95%-9-10-26-PVT → ECL-9.95%-9-10-26-PVT
- 956802 (INE528S08035): ERFL-9.25%-31-7-27-PVT → ECL-9.25%-31-7-27-PVT
- 957083 (INE528S08043): ERFL-9.25%-6-10-27-PVT → ECL-9.25%-6-10-27-PVT
- 957246 (INE528S08050): ERFL-9.75%-PERPETUAL-PVT → ECL-9.75%-PERPETUAL-PVT
- 957463 (INE528S08068): ERFL-10.00%-PERPETUAL-PVT → ECL-10.00%-PERPETUAL-PVT
- 975514 (INE528S07169): ERFL-10.40%-18-3-27-PVT → ECL-10.40%-18-3-27-PVT

### NCDs
- 936130 (INE528S07110): ERFL-8.88%-22-3-28-NCD → ECL-8.88%-22-3-28-NCD
- 936132 (INE528S07128): ERFL-9.25%-22-3-28-NCD → ECL-9.25%-22-3-28-NCD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect corporate name change in BSE's debt securities listing database.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update internal systems to reflect new scrip names and abbreviations
- Investors holding these securities are not required to take any action
- All existing ISIN numbers and scrip codes remain valid

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 27, 2025
- Effective immediately upon circular issuance

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Negligible. This is purely an administrative change with no impact on:
- Trading operations
- Bond valuations or coupon payments
- Maturity dates or redemption schedules
- Investor holdings or entitlements

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. Market participants may need to update reference data systems to reflect new nomenclature for accurate reporting and reconciliation.
