## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME Segment for January 2026. The schedule covers 21 trading days from January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026, with settlements numbered ITP-189/25-26 through ITP-209/25-26. The programme follows a T+1 settlement cycle with specific pay-in and pay-out timings.

## Key Points

- Settlement cycle: T+1 (trade date plus one business day)
- Pay-in deadline: 9:50 a.m. (members must submit instructions to depositories/banks)
- Pay-in time: 10:00 a.m.
- Pay-out time: 11:00 a.m. (funds and securities)
- Total settlements in January 2026: 21 settlement cycles
- Settlement numbers: ITP-189/25-26 to ITP-209/25-26
- Depository settlement numbers: 2526189 to 2526209

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to depositories/banks by 9:50 a.m. on the settlement date
- Pay-in of funds and securities must be completed by 10:00 a.m.
- Members must ensure availability of funds and securities for timely settlement

## Important Dates

**January 2026 Trading Days:**
- January 1, 2, 5-9, 12-16, 19-23, 27-30 (21 trading days)

**Non-trading days in January 2026:**
- Weekends: January 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-25, 31
- January 26, 2026 appears to be a holiday (Republic Day)

**Settlement Timeline:**
- First settlement: ITP-189/25-26 (Trade: Jan 1, Settlement: Jan 2)
- Last settlement: ITP-209/25-26 (Trade: Jan 30, Settlement: Feb 2)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational schedule that enables market participants to plan their trading and settlement activities for the month.

**Operational Impact:** Standard procedures apply with no changes to existing settlement timelines or processes. The T+1 settlement cycle continues as per established norms for the ITP-SME segment.

**Stakeholder Impact:** Institutional investors and trading members operating in the SME segment through ITP need to align their operations with this schedule. The fixed pay-in deadline of 9:50 a.m. requires members to maintain operational discipline for timely instruction submission.
