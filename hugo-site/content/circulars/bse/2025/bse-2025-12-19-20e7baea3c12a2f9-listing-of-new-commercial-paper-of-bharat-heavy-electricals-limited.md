## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 250 crores, with a 28-day tenure maturing on January 16, 2026. The commercial paper carries CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will trade on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,595 per unit (discount of Rs. 2,405)
- Scrip Code: 730821
- Scrip ID: BHEL191225
- ISIN: INE257A14AE3
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE257A14AE3
- Adhere to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Tenure**: 28 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides BHEL with short-term working capital financing.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Available only to institutional investors through private placement. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt segment listing with no special trading conditions or restrictions.
