## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities aggregating Rs. 240 crores (24,000 securities of Rs. 1,00,000 face value each) on the BSE Debt segment effective December 4, 2025. These securities were issued on a private placement basis with an ICRA AA credit rating and carry an interest rate of 8.86% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 24,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 240 crores
- Interest Rate: 8.86% per annum (payable annually)
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA
- ISIN: INE248U07GE1
- Scrip Code: 977343
- Scrip ID: 886OPL28
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07GE1
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 3, 2026 to December 1, 2028
- **Redemption Date**: December 1, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of a single issuer. The securities are rated ICRA AA, indicating high credit quality. The listing provides additional liquidity options for holders of these debt instruments on the BSE Debt segment. This is a routine corporate action with no implications for equity markets or broader trading operations.
