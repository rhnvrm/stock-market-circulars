## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing that Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application as a Trading Member. The original resignation application was announced via Exchange Notice No. 20251201-2 dated December 1, 2025, and a public notice was published in Financial Express and Navshakti newspapers on November 29, 2025. The withdrawal means the trading member will continue its operations with BSE.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has withdrawn its resignation application
- Original resignation notice was issued on December 1, 2025 (Notice No. 20251201-2)
- Public notice was published on November 29, 2025 in Financial Express and Navshakti newspapers
- The trading member will continue its membership with BSE
- All constituents of Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and other Trading Members have been informed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding membership status.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements or actions needed from market participants. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Original resignation notice issued
- **November 29, 2025**: Public notice published in newspapers
- **December 26, 2025**: Withdrawal of resignation notice issued

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This administrative action affects only Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and its constituents. The withdrawal of resignation means business continuity for the trading member. No impact on trading operations, market structure, or other market participants. For clarifications, stakeholders can contact BSE membership department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.
