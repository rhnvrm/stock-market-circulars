## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in commercial papers of 13 companies due to upcoming redemptions. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified commercial papers from November 27-28, 2025, as the companies have fixed record dates for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- 13 companies' commercial papers will be suspended from trading
- Suspensions effective from November 27, 2025 (12 companies) and November 28, 2025 (1 company)
- All suspensions are for redemption of commercial papers
- Trading members must not deal in these instruments from the specified dates
- Notice number: 20251121-7, Reference: DR-765/2025-2026 and DR-766/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified commercial papers from the no-dealings date
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended instruments
- Ensure client communications regarding the suspension of these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Record Date (12 companies):** November 27, 2025
- **No Dealings From (12 companies):** November 27, 2025
- **Record Date (CaratLane):** November 28, 2025
- **No Dealings From (CaratLane):** November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market impact. The suspension is temporary and tied to scheduled redemptions of commercial papers. Investors holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No broader market disruption is anticipated as this is standard procedure for maturing debt instruments. The notice affects the debt segment only and includes major corporate issuers across financial services, infrastructure, and manufacturing sectors.
