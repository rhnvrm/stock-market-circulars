## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice regarding the part redemption of debentures of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited. This notice updates the earlier communication dated June 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20250612-18) which had set the record date as June 17, 2025. The post-redemption face value for four series of EARCL debentures has been revised to Rs. 1,000 per debenture.

## Key Points

- This is a revised notice updating earlier communication No. 20250612-18 dated June 12, 2025
- Record date for part redemption was June 17, 2025
- Four series of EARCL debentures are affected by this part redemption
- Post-redemption face value is Rs. 1,000 per debenture for all four series
- All affected debentures are privately placed debt securities
- Trading members are requested to take note of the revised information

## Debenture Details

### Affected Securities:

1. **EARCL-2%-28-03-27-PVT** (ISIN: INE015L07469, Code: 956041)
   - Post-redemption face value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture

2. **EARCL-2%-29-3-27-PVT** (ISIN: INE015L07477, Code: 956100)
   - Post-redemption face value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture

3. **EARCL-2%-27-4-27-PVT** (ISIN: INE015L07501, Code: 956233)
   - Post-redemption face value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture

4. **EARCL-2%-26-4-27-PVT** (ISIN: INE015L07493, Code: 956235)
   - Post-redemption face value: Rs. 1,000 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a revision to previously communicated corporate action details.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE must note the revised post-redemption face values
- Debenture holders affected by the part redemption should be aware of the updated face value of Rs. 1,000 per debenture
- No additional compliance actions required beyond noting the revised information

## Important Dates

- **Original Notice Date**: June 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20250612-18)
- **Record Date**: June 17, 2025 (already passed)
- **Revised Notice Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a clarification/revision of an already announced corporate action affecting privately placed debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing debenture holders of the four specified EARCL series. The revision confirms the post-redemption face value at Rs. 1,000 per debenture.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their records with the revised face value information for proper accounting and client reporting purposes.
