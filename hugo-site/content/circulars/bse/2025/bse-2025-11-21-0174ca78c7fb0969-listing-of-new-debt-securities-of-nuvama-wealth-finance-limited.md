## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 21, 2025. The company has issued 17,500 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.95% per annum. The securities are rated CARE AA/Stable and will mature on May 19, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 17,500 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code: 977307
- Scrip ID: 895NWFL28
- ISIN: INE918K07QF6
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: November 20, 2025
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note:
- These securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QF6
- Trading tick size is 1 paise
- Members requiring clarification can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Full details available in the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: May 19, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds Rs. 1,750 crore worth of debt securities to BSE's debt segment. The securities are rated AA/Stable, indicating high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in high-grade corporate debt instruments from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited. No material impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
