## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with CRISIL A1+ rating and will be traded in the debt segment from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 2000 units (Total value: Rs. 100 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730105
- ISIN: INE03W114716
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,73,490/- per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use specified ISIN number for all transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality.
