## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures of SHRIRAM FINANCE LIMITED effective August 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of August 15, 2025 for interest payment and redemption of debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with code SFL-11%-30-08-2025-PVT (ISIN: INE721A08AH3, Code: 946997)
- No dealings allowed from August 14, 2025
- Record date set for August 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **August 15, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **August 30, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (as indicated in code)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of specific SHRIRAM FINANCE LIMITED debentures. Impact is limited to this particular debt instrument and does not affect the company's equity shares or other securities. Standard procedure for corporate actions involving debt securities.
