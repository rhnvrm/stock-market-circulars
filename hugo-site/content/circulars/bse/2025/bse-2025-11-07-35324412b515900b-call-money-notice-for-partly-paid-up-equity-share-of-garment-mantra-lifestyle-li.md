## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited to collect the second and final call money on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders must pay Rs. 0.60 per share for 39,14,97,795 partly paid-up equity shares during the issue period from November 13 to November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Second and final call money notice for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited
- Total partly paid-up equity shares affected: 39,14,97,795 shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 0.60 per partly paid-up equity share
- Security symbol: GARMENTCM1
- Market lot: 1
- Issue period: 15 days (November 13-27, 2025)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Call Money Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in accordance with:
- Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money collection procedures

## Compliance Requirements

**Trading Members:**
- Must use BSE's IBBS platform Call Money Module to submit call money notice details
- Only Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can participate
- Must collect and submit details during the issue period

**Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs):**
- Must use the Call Money Module to submit collected call money details
- Must process payments within the specified bidding hours

**Shareholders:**
- Must pay Rs. 0.60 per partly paid-up equity share held
- Payment must be made during the issue period through authorized channels

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 7, 2025
- **Issue Opening Date:** Thursday, November 13, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Thursday, November 27, 2025
- **Bidding Period:** 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (daily during issue period)
- **Issue Duration:** 15 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
Moderate impact limited to shareholders of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited holding partly paid-up equity shares. This is the final call, meaning shareholders must pay to fully subscribe their shares or risk forfeiture.

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading Members and SCSBs need to ensure IBBS platform access is functional
- Registrar (Bigshare Services) will handle processing of approximately 39.15 crore shares
- Total call money collection amount: Rs. 23.49 crores (39,14,97,795 shares Ã— Rs. 0.60)

**Shareholder Impact:**
Shareholders holding partly paid-up equity shares must arrange payment of Rs. 0.60 per share within the 15-day window. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as this is the final call.
