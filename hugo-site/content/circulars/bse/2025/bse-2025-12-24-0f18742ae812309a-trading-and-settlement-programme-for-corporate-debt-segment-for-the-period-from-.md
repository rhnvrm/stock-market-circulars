## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Corporate Debt Segment (ICDM - Indian Corporate Debt Market) for January 2026. The circular provides comprehensive settlement schedules for three settlement cycles: T+0, T+1, and T+2, covering 21 trading days from January 1-31, 2026. Settlement numbers range from 2526185 to 2526205.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedules announced for T+0, T+1, and T+2 cycles in the Corporate Debt Segment
- Coverage period: January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- 21 trading days scheduled for the month
- Settlement numbers: 2526185 through 2526205
- Non-trading dates: January 3-4, 10-11, 17-18, 24-26, 31 (weekends and holidays)
- Market participants must adhere to depository settlement deadline times for respective market types

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must check depository settlement deadline times for respective market types
- Securities must be transferred to ICCL account within specified timelines
- Participants should refer to ICCL Notice No.20091206-01 dated December 6, 2009 for detailed settlement procedures
- Securities/funds pay-in must be completed on specified dates for each settlement number

## Important Dates

**Trading Period:** January 1-31, 2026

**T+0 Settlement:** Same-day pay-in and pay-out (21 settlements)

**T+1 Settlement:** Next business day settlement
- First settlement: Transaction 01-01-2026, Settlement 02-01-2026
- Last settlement: Transaction 30-01-2026, Settlement 02-02-2026

**T+2 Settlement:** Two business days settlement
- First settlement: Transaction 01-01-2026, Settlement 05-01-2026
- Last settlement: Transaction 30-01-2026, Settlement 03-02-2026

**Non-Trading Days:** January 3, 4, 10, 11, 17, 18, 24, 25, 26, 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine administrative circular providing advance notice of settlement schedules.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for debt market participants who need to plan their settlement operations, fund management, and securities transfer activities for January 2026.

**Participant Action Required:** Debt segment participants should integrate these dates into their operational calendars and ensure compliance with pay-in/pay-out timelines. Treasury and settlement teams should note the extended settlement dates for transactions executed on January 30, 2026, which will settle in February 2026.
