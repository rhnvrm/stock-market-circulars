## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities subject to surveillance measures for companies with high promoter and non-promoter encumbrance as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011. Effective December 10, 2025, Shalimar Paints Ltd has been added to this framework. No securities are being removed from the measure at this time. The consolidated list now includes 8 companies under enhanced surveillance.

## Key Points

- Shalimar Paints Ltd (Scrip Code: 509874, ISIN: INE849C01026) added to encumbrance surveillance framework
- Effective date: December 10, 2025
- No securities are exiting the framework in this update
- Total 8 companies currently under this surveillance measure
- Framework based on SEBI SAST Regulation 28(3) for high encumbrance levels

## Regulatory Framework

This measure is implemented under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011, which addresses situations where companies have high levels of promoter and non-promoter share encumbrance. The surveillance framework is designed to monitor and manage risks associated with pledged shareholdings.

## Securities Added (Annexure I)

**New Addition:**
1. Shalimar Paints Ltd - Scrip Code: 509874, ISIN: INE849C01026

## Securities Removed (Annexure II)

No securities are moving out of the framework as of this update. Securities may exit this framework if they are included in other surveillance measures such as:
- LT ASM (Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure)
- ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure)
- TT (Trade-to-Trade) framework

## Consolidated List (Annexure III)

All 8 securities currently under encumbrance surveillance:

1. B. L. Kashyap and Sons Ltd (532719 / INE350H01032)
2. Bedmutha Industries Ltd* (533270 / INE844K01012)
3. Brahmaputra Infrastructure Ltd (535693 / INE320I01017)
4. Future Market Networks Ltd (533296 / INE360L01017)
5. India Nivesh Ltd (501700 / INE131H01028)
6. Premier Energy and Infrastructure Ltd (533100 / INE429K01012)
7. Shalimar Paints Ltd (509874 / INE849C01026) - **NEW**
8. Sudal Industries Ltd (506003 / INE618D01015)

*Note: Bedmutha Industries Ltd is marked with asterisk as per NSE coordination.

- Addition of Shalimar Paints indicates elevated encumbrance levels detected by regulators
