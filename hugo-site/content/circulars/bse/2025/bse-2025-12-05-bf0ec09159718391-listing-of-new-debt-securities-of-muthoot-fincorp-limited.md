## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited on its Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 100,000 units (face value ₹10,000 each), representing a total issue size of ₹100 crore. The securities carry a 10.40% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 22, 2033.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoot Fincorp Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 976712
- **Scrip ID**: 1040MFL33
- **ISIN**: INE549K08541 (further listings under same ISIN)
- **Quantity**: 100,000 units
- **Face Value**: ₹10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹10,011.40
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AA-
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 10.40% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from December 31, 2025 to August 22, 2033
- **Redemption Date**: August 22, 2033
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for detailed terms and conditions
- Standard debt segment trading rules and procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: August 22, 2033

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a private placement listing with limited immediate impact on broader market operations. The securities are available for trading on the debt segment but are typically held by institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these instruments on the BSE platform. The availability of CRISIL AA- rated securities with 10.40% coupon provides additional investment options for debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals participating in debt markets. The securities offer regular monthly income with relatively high credit quality (AA- rating).

**Contact**: Trading members requiring clarifications may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
