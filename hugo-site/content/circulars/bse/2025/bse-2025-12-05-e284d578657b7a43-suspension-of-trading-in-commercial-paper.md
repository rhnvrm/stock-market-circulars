## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No 20251205-20 suspending trading in commercial papers of six companies effective December 9-11, 2025. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption of these commercial papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective no-dealing dates.

## Key Points

- Six companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension applies to specific ISIN codes for each company's commercial paper
- No dealings permitted from the specified dates for each security
- Notice issued under Debt segment Corporate Actions category
- Trading members must cease dealing from respective suspension dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the no-dealing dates
- Adherence to suspension applies to the following securities:
  - Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited - Two CPs (SCSL-10-12-25-CP and SCSL-11-12-25-CP)
  - PVR Inox Limited (PVRIL-12-12-25-CP)
  - Kirloskar Ferrous Industries Ltd (KFIL-12-12-25-CP)
  - BOBCARD Limited (BBL-12-12-25-CP)
  - Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited (KMPL-12-12-25-CP)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Affects only debt segment commercial paper market

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard redemption process for commercial papers nearing maturity

**Investor Impact:** Limited to commercial paper holders of the six specified companies who will receive redemption proceeds on respective record dates

**Trading Impact:** Temporary suspension of trading in six specific commercial paper ISINs only, not affecting broader market operations
