## Summary

Godrej Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 27, 2025. The listing comprises 6000 securities of face value Rs.5,00,000/- each issued on private placement basis. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 26, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- each
- Issue Price: Rs.4,91,725/- per security
- Total Issue Size: Approximately Rs.295.04 crore
- Scrip Code: 730665
- Scrip ID: GFL271125
- ISIN Number: INE02KN14655
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14655
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities listed and admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 27, 2025
- Listing Date: November 27, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The issuance is on private placement basis targeted at institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of Godrej Finance Limited. The listing provides short-term funding to the issuer and investment opportunity for debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
