## Summary

Tata Motors Limited (formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing was submitted by Company Secretary Sudipto Kumar Das and provides details of the company's shareholding structure at the time of listing.

## Key Points

- Company name: Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Total number of shareholders: 6,647,799
- Total shares outstanding: 3,682,331,373 fully paid-up equity shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 1,567,369,812 shares (42.56%)
- Public shareholding: 2,114,961,561 shares (57.44%)
- Outstanding ESOPs: 2,666,563 shares
- Total shares on fully diluted basis: 3,684,997,936
- Tata Sons Private Limited (TSPL) is exempted under SBO Rules as a core investment company registered with RBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure filing required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Company confirms no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged/encumbered promoter shares
- Company has outstanding ESOPs that need to be tracked
- No shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners other than TSPL (which is exempted)

## Important Dates

- Filing date: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** This shareholding pattern filing provides transparency on the ownership structure of Tata Motors Limited post-listing. The promoter holding at 42.56% indicates strong promoter commitment while maintaining adequate public float of 57.44%, which ensures good liquidity for trading.

**Investor Impact:** Investors can assess the distribution of shares between promoters and public shareholders. The presence of 2.67 million outstanding ESOPs indicates potential dilution upon exercise, representing approximately 0.07% of current equity.

**Operational Impact:** This is a routine compliance filing with no immediate operational changes. The shareholding structure appears stable with no encumbrances on promoter shares, indicating financial stability and absence of distress signals.
