## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of RIDDHI DISPLAY EQUIPMENTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544640), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment under M Group effective December 30, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing.

## Key Points

- Company: RIDDHI DISPLAY EQUIPMENTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544640)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: December 30, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251212-44 dated December 12, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for RIDDHI DISPLAY EQUIPMENTS LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement. This represents a relaxation of trading restrictions typically imposed on newly listed or volatile securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the segment change for RIDDHI DISPLAY EQUIPMENTS LIMITED
- Systems and trading terminals should be updated to reflect the new M Group classification
- Trading members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain for clarifications or additional information

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251212-44)
- **December 15, 2025**: Circular announcement date
- **December 30, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for RIDDHI DISPLAY EQUIPMENTS LIMITED shares. Under the Rolling segment, investors can engage in intraday trading and benefit from T+1 settlement cycles, whereas the Trade for Trade segment requires full upfront payment and delivery with no intraday trading allowed. This change typically indicates the exchange's confidence in the stock's stability post-IPO.
