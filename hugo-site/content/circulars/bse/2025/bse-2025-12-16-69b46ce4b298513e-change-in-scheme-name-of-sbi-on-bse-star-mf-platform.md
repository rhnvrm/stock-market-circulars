## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding changes in scheme names for SBI mutual fund schemes traded on the BSE StAR MF (Systematic Transaction Access & Reporting for Mutual Funds) platform. This is an administrative update to reflect revised naming conventions for SBI mutual fund schemes.

## Key Points

- Scheme name changes for SBI mutual funds on BSE StAR MF platform
- Administrative nomenclature update with no impact on scheme operations
- Affects mutual fund trading and reporting systems
- Market participants should update their systems with new scheme names

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is purely an administrative update to reflect scheme name modifications by SBI Mutual Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and intermediaries should update their systems to reflect the new scheme names
- Ensure accurate scheme identification in transaction reporting
- Update client-facing systems and documentation with revised nomenclature

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Effective Date: As specified in the circular (refer to PDF for specific implementation timeline)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal to none. This is an administrative change that does not affect scheme performance, holdings, or investor rights.

**Operational Impact:** Low. System administrators and market participants will need to update scheme master data in their systems to ensure correct scheme identification for trading and reporting purposes.

**Investor Impact:** None. Existing investments remain unaffected. Only the display names of schemes will change in system records and reports.
