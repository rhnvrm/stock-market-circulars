## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective December 8, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, listed on the BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Papers carry dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with ICICI Bank acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730719
- **Scrip ID**: NWF051225
- **ISIN**: INE918K14CU1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,862 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 500 Commercial Papers
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Ratings**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CU1
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 5, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE platform. The instrument is limited to 500 units with a total value of Rs. 25 crore.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.

**Investor Relevance**: Relevant primarily for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals dealing in short-term debt instruments in the BSE Debt segment.
