## Summary

BSE announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective November 21, 2025. The instruments include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with maturities ranging from February 2026 to November 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills will be listed effective November 21, 2025
- 91-day T-Bill (Scrip Code: 805145, ISIN: IN002025X349) maturing February 19, 2026
- 182-day T-Bill (Scrip Code: 805146, ISIN: IN002025Y347) maturing May 21, 2026
- 364-day T-Bill (Scrip Code: 805147, ISIN: IN002025Z344) maturing November 19, 2026
- All instruments have a market lot of 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to maturity/redemption date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new government debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these securities
- Securities will not be available for trading T-2 days before maturity (excluding bank holidays)
- Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 21, 2025
- **91TB190226 Maturity:** February 19, 2026 (trading stops February 17, 2026)
- **182TB21526 Maturity:** May 21, 2026 (trading stops May 19, 2026)
- **364T191126 Maturity:** November 19, 2026 (trading stops November 17, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for government securities listing. The addition of these T-Bills provides additional short-term investment options for debt market participants but does not affect equity markets or require operational changes from trading members.
