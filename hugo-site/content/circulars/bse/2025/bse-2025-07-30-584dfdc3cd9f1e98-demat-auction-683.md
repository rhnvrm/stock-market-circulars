## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 683 dated July 30, 2025. The auction includes 134 securities from various companies with specified quantities to be auctioned due to demat settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 683
- Settlement Date: July 30, 2025
- Total securities listed: 134 different scrip codes
- Quantities range from 1 share to 16,503 shares per security
- Includes major companies like Infosys, Larsen & Toubro, SpiceJet, Trent Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate in the demat auction process for the listed securities
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE demat auction procedures
- Specific quantities must be delivered or purchased as indicated

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 30, 2025
- Auction conducted as per BSE settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities and their shareholders. The auction may result in price volatility for the listed securities. Members holding positions in these securities need to ensure compliance with settlement requirements to avoid penalties.
