## Summary

BSE has listed new perpetual debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.4232% per annum and are rated CRISIL AA+/IND AA+.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 200 securities with market lot size of 1
- Scrip Code: 977034, ISIN: INE674K08042
- Face Value: Rs. 1 crore per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1 crore per security
- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Interest Rate: 8.4232% per annum paid annually
- Redemption Date: December 31, 2999 (perpetual)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+, IND AA+

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt security listing procedures followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms
- Put/Call options as per Information Memorandum

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Maturity**: December 31, 2999 (perpetual)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The perpetual nature and high credit rating make these institutional-grade securities with limited retail trading expected.
