## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of NANTA TECH LIMITED. This is part of the standard IPO process where a portion of shares are allocated to qualified institutional buyers (anchor investors) before the issue opens to other investors.

## Key Points

- Company: NANTA TECH LIMITED
- Transaction Type: Public Issue (IPO)
- Event: Anchor Investor Allocation
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Circular Date: December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard informational circular about an IPO anchor allocation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants should note the anchor investor allocation details
- Standard disclosure and reporting requirements for IPO process apply
- Details of the allocation would be available in the attached PDF document

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: December 22, 2025
- Specific anchor allocation date and IPO timeline would be detailed in the attachment

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact on market participants interested in the NANTA TECH LIMITED IPO. Anchor investor allocations typically indicate institutional interest in the offering and can influence price discovery during the IPO process. The allocation to anchor investors happens one day before the issue opens to the public and provides early price indication for the stock.
