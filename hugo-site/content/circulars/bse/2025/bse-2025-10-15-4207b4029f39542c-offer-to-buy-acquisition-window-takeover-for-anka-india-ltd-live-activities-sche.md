## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for ANKA INDIA LTD (Symbol: ANKIN, Scrip Code: 531673). The offer is for up to 69,24,902 equity shares at Rs. 17.00 per share, running from Tuesday, October 14, 2025 to Wednesday, October 29, 2025. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: ANKA INDIA LTD (ANKIN), ISIN: INE067C01025, Scrip Code: 531673
- **Offer Size**: Up to 69,24,902 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 17.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: October 14-29, 2025
- **Bid Offering Hours**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: October 10-29, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational guidelines for an existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Eligible Participants**: Trading members can bid only for investors having shares in demat form
- **Bidding Categories**: Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII), Financial Institution (FI), Mutual Fund (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Other NIB (NOH), Company (CO), Individual (IND)
- **Platform Access**: Members must use designated URLs for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet (both Internet and Leased Line options available)
- **File Availability**: Extranet files for bid acceptance available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- **No Securities Accepted**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, October 14, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, October 29, 2025
- **Circular Date**: October 14, 2025 (Notice No. 20251015-9, dated October 15, 2025)
- **Give-up/Take-up Start**: October 10, 2025
- **Last Day for Give-up**: October 28, 2025
- **Last Day for Take-up**: October 28, 2025 (extended to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only ANKA INDIA LTD shareholders. The offer involves a relatively small number of shares and will not impact broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians need to ensure proper UCC registration, coordinate with clients holding ANKA INDIA LTD shares in demat form, and manage bidding through designated platforms during the 16-day offer period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: 
- ANKA INDIA LTD shareholders with demat holdings can participate through their trading members
- Trading members must facilitate client participation and ensure technical readiness on iBBS/RTRMS platforms
- Custodian members involved in holding client securities need to process give-up/take-up transactions

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support (login IDs, password resets) and operational queries through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
