## Summary

Studds Accessories Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing pertains to equity shares and represents the post-issue shareholding position as on November 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Studds Accessories Limited
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Filing Date: November 6, 2025
- Shareholding Pattern Date: November 5, 2025 (Post-Issue)
- Filed Under: Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015

## Declaration Details

The company has declared the following regarding its share structure:

- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities issued
- No warrants issued
- No outstanding ESOPs granted
- No shares against which depository receipts are issued
- No shares held by promoters are pledged
- No shares held by promoters under Non-Disposal Undertaking
- No other encumbrances on promoter shares
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No shares in locked-in status
- No significant beneficial owner

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced through this circular. This is a standard disclosure requirement for newly listed companies.

## Compliance Requirements

This filing represents compliance with:

- Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Requirement to disclose shareholding pattern post-listing
- Standard format for disclosure of holding of specified securities as per SEBI guidelines

## Important Dates

- **November 5, 2025**: Post-issue shareholding pattern date
- **November 6, 2025**: Filing date of shareholding pattern with BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern filing is a mandatory requirement following the listing of equity shares. The declaration confirms a clean capital structure with no encumbrances, convertible securities, or outstanding options, which provides transparency to investors regarding the company's shareholding composition.
