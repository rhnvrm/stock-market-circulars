## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from Bajaj Auto Limited (Scrip Code: 532977) and Ganesh Housing Limited (Scrip Code: 526367) regarding significant increases in trading volumes of their equity shares across exchanges. Both companies responded that they have been making timely disclosures as required under SEBI Listing Regulations, have no unpublished price sensitive information, and the volume increases are purely market driven.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Auto clarified that it has been consistently making disclosures within stipulated timeframes under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Bajaj Auto recently announced the launch of Riki – 3-Wheeler E-Rickshaw segment on November 26, 2025
- Bajaj Auto stated there is no unpublished price sensitive information pending disclosure
- Ganesh Housing confirmed prompt disclosure of all material events within timelines required under Regulation 30
- Ganesh Housing stated no material event or price sensitive information needs to be disseminated to investors
- Both companies attributed the volume increases to market conditions and stated they are not privy to trading movements
- Both companies committed to continued adherence to disclosure requirements under SEBI Listing Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Both companies confirmed their commitment to:
- Continue making necessary disclosures as and when required under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Timely disclosure of any information/announcement likely to impact price/volume of equity shares
- Adherence to Regulation 30 regarding material events and price sensitive information

## Important Dates

- November 26, 2025: Bajaj Auto launched Riki – 3-Wheeler E-Rickshaw segment (press release issued)
- November 28, 2025: Date of clarification responses from both companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine clarifications confirming no undisclosed material information exists. The volume increases are attributed to normal market dynamics.

**Companies Affected:** Bajaj Auto Limited (BSE: 532977) and Ganesh Housing Limited (BSE: 526367)

**Investor Impact:** No action required. Companies have confirmed all material information has been disclosed and volume movements are market driven without any underlying undisclosed events.
