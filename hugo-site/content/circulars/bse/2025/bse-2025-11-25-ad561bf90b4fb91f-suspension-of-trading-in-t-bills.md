## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill scrip code 805109 (ISIN: IN002025X224 - 91TB281125) effective from November 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the T-bill reaching its maturity/redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill scrip code 805109 (91TB281125) will mature on its redemption date
- ISIN: IN002025X224
- Trading suspension effective from November 26, 2025
- Trading members must cease dealing in this instrument
- Notice reference: DR-764/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in scrip code 805109 from November 26, 2025
- Trading members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure no client orders are accepted for this T-bill after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 26, 2025**: Trading suspension begins
- **Redemption Date**: T-bill maturity (date not explicitly specified but imminent)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single maturing Treasury Bill instrument. Investors holding this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds as per standard maturity procedures. No broader market implications as this is a scheduled maturity event.
