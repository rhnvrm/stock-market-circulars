## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729888, ISIN: INE700G14PE7) due to its upcoming maturity on October 20, 2025. Trading Members are advised not to deal in this instrument effective from October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Limited to mature on October 20, 2025
- Scrip Code: 729888
- ISIN: INE700G14PE7
- Trading suspension effective from October 17, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-738/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all trading activities in the specified Commercial Paper
- No deals should be executed in this instrument from October 17, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice Date
- **October 17, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 20, 2025**: Redemption/Maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only one specific Commercial Paper instrument approaching maturity. Investors holding this instrument will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader market implications or systemic concerns.
