## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 22,00,000 new equity shares of Autoline Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 532797) effective November 20, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 102.50 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 92.50). The shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Autoline Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 532797)
- **Number of Shares**: 22,00,000 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 102.50 per share (including premium of Rs. 92.50)
- **Type of Issue**: Preferential allotment to promoters pursuant to warrant conversion
- **ISIN**: INE718H01014
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 43175402 to 45375401
- **Date of Allotment**: June 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 20, 2025
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to take note of the new securities admitted to trading
- All 22,00,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Shares must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: June 27, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: July 31, 2027 (for all 22,00,000 shares)
- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. The preferential allotment represents capital infusion through warrant conversion, strengthening the company's equity base. The issuance to promoters may indicate confidence in the company's prospects.

**Shareholding Impact**: The allotment to promoters will increase promoter shareholding and potentially alter the overall shareholding pattern of the company.

**Lock-in Implications**: The complete lock-in of all shares until July 2027 ensures no immediate supply pressure on the stock, providing stability for existing shareholders.

**Investor Considerations**: Investors should note the dilution from the new shares and monitor the company's utilization of funds raised through this preferential issue.
