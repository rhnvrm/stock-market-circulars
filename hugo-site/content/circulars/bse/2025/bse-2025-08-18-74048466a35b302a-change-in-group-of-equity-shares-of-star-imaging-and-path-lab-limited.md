## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of STAR IMAGING AND PATH LAB LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544482) from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 2, 2025. This follows a previous notice dated August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: STAR IMAGING AND PATH LAB LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544482
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: September 2, 2025
- Market Segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be transferred from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal trading operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 18, 2025
- Previous Notice: August 14, 2025
- Effective Date: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for the company as transfer to Rolling segment typically indicates improved compliance and allows for more flexible trading. This change should enhance liquidity and trading efficiency for the stock.
