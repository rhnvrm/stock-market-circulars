## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of FUJIYAMA POWER SYSTEMS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544613) to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Monday, November 24, 2025. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Thursday, November 20, 2025. The action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE).

## Key Points

- FUJIYAMA POWER SYSTEMS LIMITED listed on BSE on November 20, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index on November 24, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544613
- Addition reflected in corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No: 20251119-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index constituent update following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index trackers and passive funds benchmarked to BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Listing date of FUJIYAMA POWER SYSTEMS LIMITED on BSE
- **November 24, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact circular. This is a routine index maintenance action following a new listing. Impact is limited to:

- Index fund managers tracking BSE IPO Index who need to rebalance portfolios
- Investors and analysts monitoring the BSE IPO Index composition
- Potential increased liquidity for the stock due to index inclusion

No material impact on broader market operations or trading rules.
