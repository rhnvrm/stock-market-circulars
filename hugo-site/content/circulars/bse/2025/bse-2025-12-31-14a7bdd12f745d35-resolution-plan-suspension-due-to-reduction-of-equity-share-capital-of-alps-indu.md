## Summary

BSE has announced trading suspension for ALPS INDUSTRIES LTD. (Scrip Code: 530715) effective January 2, 2026 due to implementation of a resolution plan approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal, Allahabad Bench, Prayagraj. The plan involves a 99% reduction in equity share capital followed by a 10:1 share consolidation.

## Key Points

- Trading members advised not to deal in equity shares of ALPS INDUSTRIES LTD. from January 2, 2026
- Record date for capital reduction: January 2, 2026
- Suspension under Settlement No. DR-790/2025-2026
- Scrip Code: 530715
- NCLT Allahabad Bench has approved the resolution plan

## Regulatory Changes

**Capital Reduction (99% reduction):**
- Existing: 3,91,14,100 equity shares of Face Value Rs. 10/- each (Total: Rs. 39,11,41,000/-)
- Reduced to: 3,91,14,100 equity shares of Face Value Rs. 0.10 each (Total: Rs. 39,11,410/-)

**Share Consolidation (10:1):**
- Ten (10) equity shares of Face Value Rs. 0.10 each will be consolidated into One (1) equity share of Face Value Re. 1/- each
- Final number of shares after consolidation: 39,11,410 equity shares

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in ALPS INDUSTRIES LTD. equity shares from January 2, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and capital restructuring
- No transactions should be executed effective from the record date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 31, 2025
- **Record Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** January 2, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-790/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:**
- Existing shareholders will see face value reduced from Rs. 10 to Rs. 0.10 (99% reduction)
- Subsequently, every 10 shares will be consolidated into 1 share
- Net effect: Shareholders holding 100 shares will end up with 10 shares of Re. 1/- face value each

**Market Impact:**
- Complete trading suspension prevents any market transactions
- Resolution plan indicates the company is undergoing insolvency/restructuring proceedings under NCLT
- High severity corporate action affecting all existing shareholders
- Liquidity completely removed until further notice from the exchange
