## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs.150 crore issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs.5,00,000/- denomination each
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs.150 crore aggregate)
- Issue price: Rs.491,568 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730181
- ISIN: INE02FN14648
- Maturity: December 10, 2025 (91 days)

## Trading Details

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with minimal impact on broader market operations. Provides short-term funding option for IGH Holdings Private Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
