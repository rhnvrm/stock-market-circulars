## Summary

BSE has listed 13,689,000 equity shares of Apollo Techno Industries Limited in demat form. The shares are categorized into six distinct blocks with varying lock-in periods. The majority of shares (3,647,500 shares) are subject to lock-in until December 30, 2026, while smaller tranches have lock-in periods extending through 2027 and 2028. A portion of shares (2,639,000) are free from lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 13,689,000 equity shares
- All securities are fully paid (Type: F)
- All shares are held in demat form
- Six different blocks with distinctive number ranges from 1 to 13,689,000
- Lock-in periods vary from free trading to December 30, 2028
- Largest block: 3,647,500 shares locked until December 30, 2026
- Free float available: 2,639,000 shares with no lock-in restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable. This is an informational circular regarding completed listing.

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 520,000 shares (distinctive numbers 10,000,001 to 10,520,000)
- **March 28, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 530,000 shares (distinctive numbers 10,520,001 to 11,050,000)
- **December 30, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 3,647,500 shares (distinctive numbers 1 to 3,647,500)
- **December 30, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for 3,647,500 shares (distinctive numbers 3,647,501 to 7,255,000)
- **December 30, 2028**: Lock-in expiry for 2,745,000 shares (distinctive numbers 7,255,001 to 10,000,000)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a company-specific listing announcement with no broader market implications.

**Liquidity Impact**: Immediate liquidity is limited to 2,639,000 free shares (approximately 19% of total listed shares). Liquidity will gradually increase as lock-in periods expire between January 2026 and December 2028.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for Apollo Techno Industries shareholders and potential investors who need to understand the lock-in schedule and available float for trading purposes.
