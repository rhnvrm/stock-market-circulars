## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 18, 2025. The listing comprises 4,500 units of Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 4,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,091 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730589
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF171125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FM1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FM1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment affecting only participants in short-term debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard debt instrument listing with typical trading requirements. Only relevant to trading members dealing in commercial papers and institutional investors in debt markets.

**Duration**: Short-term instrument with 4-month maturity (November 2025 to March 2026).
