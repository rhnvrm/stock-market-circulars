## Summary

BSE has notified members that KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 04, 2025. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP). This notice references previous Notice No. 20251003-38.

## Key Points

- KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Two plans available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- Full transaction facilities enabled: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Both plans categorized under Equity scheme category
- Regular Plan ISIN: INF174KA1XU2
- Direct Plan ISIN: INF174KA1XT4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in KOTAK GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 04, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Reference:** Previous Notice No. 20251003-38

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to mutual fund operations on BSE StAR MF Platform. This notice primarily affects:

- Investors interested in gold and silver exposure through fund of funds structure
- Distributors and intermediaries facilitating mutual fund transactions
- BSE StAR MF Platform members who need to enable transaction capabilities

The reopening provides investment opportunities in precious metals (gold and silver) through a passive fund of funds approach for investors seeking diversification into commodity-linked equity instruments.
