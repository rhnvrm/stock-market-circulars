## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 18, 2025. The issue comprises 4,500 commercial papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 225 crore. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will mature on March 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4,500 commercial papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,091 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730589
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF171125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FM1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FM1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members must use the prescribed tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding to Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited for approximately 4 months. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new scrip code and trading parameters. For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers.
