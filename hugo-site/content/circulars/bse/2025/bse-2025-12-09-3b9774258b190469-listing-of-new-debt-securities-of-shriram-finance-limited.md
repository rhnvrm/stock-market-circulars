## Summary

Shriram Finance Limited has listed new debt securities of Rs. 750 crore (7,500 securities of Rs. 100,000 face value each) on BSE Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with allotment date of December 8, 2025. The securities carry a 7.6% annual interest rate with maturity on December 8, 2032 and are rated CRISIL AA+/Stable and IND AA+/Stable.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 7,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 750 crore
- Scrip Code: 977349
- Scrip ID: 76SFL32
- ISIN: INE721A07SR6
- Interest Rate: 7.6% per annum
- Maturity Date: December 8, 2032
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+/Stable, IND AA+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE721A07SR6
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually starting December 8, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: December 8, 2032

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Shriram Finance Limited with access to debt capital markets through private placement. For debt market participants, this adds another AA+ rated instrument for investment consideration. There is no impact on equity markets or broader market operations. Trading members in the debt segment can facilitate trading in these securities from December 9, 2025.
