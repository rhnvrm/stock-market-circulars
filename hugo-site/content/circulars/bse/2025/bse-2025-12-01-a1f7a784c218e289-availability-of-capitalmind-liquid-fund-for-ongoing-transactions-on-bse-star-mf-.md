## Summary

BSE has notified that CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. The fund will also support Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities. This notice follows previous communication (Notice No. 20251117-24) regarding the fund's temporary unavailability.

## Key Points

- CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND reopening for ongoing transactions effective December 02, 2025
- Both Direct Plan and Regular Plan variants available
- Full functionality including subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP enabled
- Two scheme variants: Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401034) and Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401042)
- Notice references previous communication dated November 17, 2024 (Notice No. 20251117-24)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice for BSE StAR MF Platform participants regarding the availability of CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND schemes for transaction processing.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 02, 2025 - CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors dealing with CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The reopening restores normal trading functionality for this specific liquid fund scheme. Members and investors can resume subscription, redemption, and systematic plan transactions from December 02, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors in CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND, as it restores full transaction capabilities including systematic investment options.
