## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from three companies regarding unusual price/volume movements in their securities. All three companies - Tata Investment Corporation Limited (Scrip: 501301), Eraaya Lifespaces Limited (Scrip: 531035), and Igarashi Motors India Limited (Stock Code: 517380) - confirmed that they have no undisclosed material information that could explain the observed market behavior and that they are in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 30 disclosure requirements.

## Key Points

- **Tata Investment Corporation Limited**: Responded to BSE surveillance query (L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/705) regarding spurt in volume, confirming no undisclosed information under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR affecting price/volume behavior
- **Eraaya Lifespaces Limited**: Clarified in response to BSE email L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3180 that the volume increase is market-driven with no material events requiring disclosure
- **Igarashi Motors India Limited**: Responded to BSE reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/707 confirming no impending material price sensitive information or announcements
- All companies confirmed timely and regular dissemination of events and information as per SEBI Listing Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Disclosure of any material events or price-sensitive information that may affect stock price/volume behavior is mandatory

## Important Dates

- **November 26, 2025**: Date of BSE surveillance email to Eraaya Lifespaces Limited
- **November 27, 2025**: Date of BSE surveillance emails to Tata Investment Corporation Limited and Igarashi Motors India Limited
- **November 27, 2025**: Date of responses from all three companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a routine surveillance exercise where companies have confirmed no undisclosed material information. The clarifications indicate that the observed price/volume movements are market-driven rather than based on company-specific undisclosed developments.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes or requirements for market participants. Companies have fulfilled their disclosure obligations by responding to the surveillance queries.

**Investor Impact**: No new material information disclosed that would warrant changes in investment decisions for these securities.
