## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF (Fund of Funds) will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 24, 2025. The fund will also support Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP). This follows the previous closure referenced in Notice No. 20251202-21.

## Key Points

- GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF reopens for transactions from December 24, 2025
- Both subscription and redemption transactions will be available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular and Direct Plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified under Equity category
- Fund previously closed as per Notice No. 20251202-21

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This notice affects only investors and distributors dealing with GROWW MULTI ASSET OMNI FOF on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The reopening restores normal trading functionality for this fund, allowing investors to resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities. This is a positive development for existing and potential investors in this multi-asset fund of funds scheme.
