## Summary

Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 531109) has listed 51,72,270 new equity shares on BSE with effect from November 27, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 63/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with premium of Rs. 53/-) to Non Promoters on a preferential basis. The shares are subject to lock-in until June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 51,72,270 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 53/-
- Issue price: Rs. 63/- per share
- Allotment to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: September 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 27, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 20968148 to 26140417
- ISIN: INE561M01018
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. The allotted shares are subject to mandatory lock-in requirements.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** June 15, 2026
- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment listing affecting a single company. The 51.72 lakh shares represent additional equity capital but are locked-in for approximately 7 months, preventing immediate trading by allottees. Standard corporate action with no broader market implications.
