## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited on private placement basis with effect from 06 November 2025. The issuance comprises 2000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 100 crore. The instruments are listed on BSE Debt segment with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth Management Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore (2000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,86,853.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730496
- **Scrip ID**: NWML041125
- **ISIN**: INE531F14FK6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE531F14FK6
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard tick size of 1 paise applies for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: 04 November 2025
- **Listing Date**: 06 November 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 12 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term debt. The instrument provides institutional investors with a 128-day investment option (04 Nov 2025 to 12 Mar 2026) in highly-rated paper. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Trading members requiring clarifications may contact BSE Debt Department at specified contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
