## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of Kosamattam Finance Limited effective September 4, 2025, due to the company setting a record date of September 5, 2025 for debenture redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific NCD series: KFL-ZC-23-9-25-NCD (ISIN: INE403Q08126, Code: 936310)
- No dealings permitted from September 4, 2025
- Record date fixed for September 5, 2025
- Purpose is redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Notice issued under reference DR-708/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard procedural suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from September 4, 2025
- All market participants must comply with the trading suspension
- No new transactions to be executed in the affected securities

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Notice issued
- **September 4, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **September 5, 2025**: Record date for redemption

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this affects only specific debenture series of one company. Standard procedural suspension to facilitate redemption process. Holders of these debentures will have their securities redeemed as per the company's redemption terms.
