## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective December 10, 2025. The listing comprises 75,000 debentures with a face value of ₹100,000 each, carrying a 7.51% annual interest rate and maturing on December 8, 2028. The securities are rated CARE AA/Stable and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 75,000 debentures
- Face Value: ₹100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: ₹100,000 per debenture
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 977357
- Scrip ID: 751THDC28
- ISIN: INE582L08078
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 7.51% per annum
- Allotment Date: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: December 10, 2025
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L08078
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE website for complete details
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 9, 2026 to December 8, 2028
- **Redemption Date**: December 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing affects only debt market participants and does not impact equity trading or broader market operations. The securities are issued on private placement basis with no put/call options, indicating a straightforward debt instrument for institutional investors. The CARE AA/Stable rating suggests high creditworthiness of the issuer.
