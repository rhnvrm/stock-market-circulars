## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis, effective November 24, 2025. The issue comprises 40,000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 2,000 crores. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 40,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730629
- Scrip ID: RRVL211125
- ISIN: INE929O14EJ9
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,583 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments on BSE's debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EJ9
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all transactions
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 24, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 24, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Papers are short-term debt instruments (91-day tenure) issued for institutional investors on private placement basis. Trading will be restricted to the debt segment with no impact on equity markets. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of Reliance Retail Ventures Limited. This listing provides additional short-term financing options for the issuer and investment opportunities for institutional participants in the debt market.
