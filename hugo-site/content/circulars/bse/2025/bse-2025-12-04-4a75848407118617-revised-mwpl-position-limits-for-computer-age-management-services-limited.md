## Summary

BSE has revised the Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) for stock derivatives contracts of Computer Age Management Services Limited (Scrip Code: 543232). The new limits apply to Stock Brokers, Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) Category I & II, and Mutual Funds (MFs), effective December 5, 2025. This circular partially modifies the earlier circular dated December 29, 2023.

## Key Points

- Market-wide position limit set at 3,71,06,075 shares for Computer Age Management Services Limited
- Client/NRI/Scheme of Mutual Fund limit: 37,10,250 shares
- Trading Member Proprietary limit: 74,20,500 shares
- TM (Proprietary + Client)/FPI (Cat I)/MF limit: 1,11,31,500 shares
- Scrip Code: 543232
- Applies to stock derivatives contracts

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in pursuance to notice No. 20250919-27 dated September 19, 2025, and partially modifies circular no. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023. The revision updates the position limit framework for Computer Age Management Services Limited stock derivatives.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stock Brokers must adhere to revised position limits when trading derivatives of Computer Age Management Services Limited
- Foreign Portfolio Investors (Category I & II) must comply with new limits
- Mutual Funds must ensure scheme positions remain within prescribed limits
- Trading Members must monitor both proprietary and client positions against new thresholds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revised position limits will affect the trading capacity of market participants in Computer Age Management Services Limited derivatives. Stock brokers, FPIs, and mutual funds will need to adjust their derivative positions to comply with the new limits. The changes are specific to this single scrip and impact only derivatives segment participants. Market participants should contact ICCL Risk Monitoring (Ms. Dhanashri Davane at 2272 8811 or Mr. Imran Ahmed at 2272 8614) for clarifications.
