## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544665) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE on December 24, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Global Ocean Logistics India Limited (ticker: 544665) listed on BSE on December 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective December 26, 2025
- This follows Notice No. 20251223-35
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Listing of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited on BSE
- **December 26, 2025**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index composition. The addition of a single stock to a specialized SME index has limited implications for broader market participants. Index fund managers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to rebalance portfolios accordingly.
