## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 250 crore issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 1, 2025. The instrument carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- 5,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each totaling Rs. 250 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 729948, ISIN: INE377Y14BN8
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Central Bank of India as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Must use specified ISIN number for all transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: August 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides Bajaj Housing Finance Limited with short-term funding at competitive rates given the high credit ratings. No significant impact on broader debt market operations expected.
