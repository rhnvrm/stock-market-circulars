## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by CREDITACCESS GRAMEEN LTD. effective October 31, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Two series of NCDs of CREDITACCESS GRAMEEN LTD. will be suspended from trading
- CAGL-9.6%-23-11-25-NCD (ISIN: INE741K07470, Code: 938274)
- CAGL-23-11-25-NCD (ISIN: INE741K07488, Code: 938276)
- Record date fixed: October 31, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: October 31, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-747/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 31, 2025
- Members should ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction for both NCD series
- Debenture holders as of record date (October 31, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders and potential traders of these specific debt securities. The suspension is temporary and procedural, facilitating the orderly redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to trading members dealing in these specific NCDs. Standard corporate action processing requiring system updates to prevent trades in suspended securities.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading opportunity available from suspension date, but this is standard practice for redemption processing.
