## Summary

BSE announces the availability of LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED's Draft Offer Document for SME IPO filed on August 12, 2025, for public comments over a 21-day period as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED on August 12, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21-day public comment period
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- Document hosted at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard ICDR Regulations Schedule VI compliance process being followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from August 13, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability for at least 21 days on exchange website

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Comment Deadline**: September 3, 2025 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability Period**: Minimum 21 days from August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard SME IPO documentation process. Affects potential investors in LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED and market participants interested in SME segment opportunities. No immediate trading or operational changes for existing market participants.
