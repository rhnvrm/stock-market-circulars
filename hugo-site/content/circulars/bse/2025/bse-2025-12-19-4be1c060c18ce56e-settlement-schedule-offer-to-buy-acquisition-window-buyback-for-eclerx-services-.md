## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the buyback of ECLERX SERVICES LTD through the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window mechanism. The bidding window will operate from December 22-29, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025132), with obligation download on January 1, 2026 and final settlement on January 2, 2026. Members and custodians must deliver demat shares through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback (Settlement No. 2025132)
- Bid Start Date: December 22, 2025
- Bid End Date: December 29, 2025
- Obligation Download: January 1, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 2, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in mechanism
- Market Type "Buyback" and settlement number 2025132 required in depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required by members and custodians
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements the existing revised guidelines for mechanism of acquisition of shares through stock exchange as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must deliver demat shares only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- **Members/Custodians**: Must use Market Type "Buyback" and settlement number 2025132 in the depository system along with other details
- **Participants**: Must ensure effect of early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- **Participants**: Must complete early pay-in process in depository system sufficiently in advance before closure of BSE OTB Bidding window
- **Members**: Should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts in requisite market type and settlement number
- No EPN file upload required

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 22, 2025**: Bid start date for buyback
- **December 29, 2025**: Bid end date for buyback
- **January 1, 2026**: Obligation download date
- **January 2, 2026**: Final settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This buyback affects only ECLERX SERVICES LTD shareholders and will temporarily impact trading activity during the acquisition window period.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members and custodians handling ECLERX shares must ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements and correct use of settlement number 2025132 in depository systems. The automatic earmarking/blocking mechanism in CDSL & NSDL simplifies the process by eliminating EPN file uploads.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Shareholders of ECLERX SERVICES LTD wishing to participate in the buyback must coordinate with their brokers/custodians to ensure timely delivery of shares through the early pay-in mechanism before bid confirmation deadlines.
