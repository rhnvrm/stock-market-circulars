## Summary

Network 18 Media & Investments Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE Debt segment effective December 2, 2025. The issuance comprises 4000 units of Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis, with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit (total issue size Rs. 200 crores). The securities carry credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 4000 units of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,758.50 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Credit ratings: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730681, Scrip ID: NMIL21225
- ISIN: INE870H14WI3
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE870H14WI3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: December 2, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 2, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 2, 2026
- Maturity Period: 3 months (90 days)

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents a routine short-term debt instrument issuance. Commercial Paper is a short-term unsecured promissory note used by corporations for working capital needs. The high credit ratings (ICRA A1+ and IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The 3-month maturity period and private placement nature limit broader market implications. This is a standard corporate treasury operation with no significant impact on Network 18's equity shareholders or market operations.
