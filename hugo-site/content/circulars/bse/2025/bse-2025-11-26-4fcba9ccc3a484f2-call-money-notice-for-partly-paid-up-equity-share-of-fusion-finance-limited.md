## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Fusion Finance Limited to collect the first and final call money on 6,10,58,392 partly paid-up equity shares. Trading members, SCSBs, and intermediaries can submit call money notice details through the platform during the issue period from November 27 to December 11, 2025. Shareholders must pay Rs. 65.50 per partly paid-up equity share.

## Key Points

- First and final call money notice issued for Fusion Finance Limited partly paid-up equity shares
- Total 6,10,58,392 partly paid-up equity shares subject to call
- Call money amount: Rs. 65.50 per share
- Security symbol: FUSIONPP
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bidding hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily during issue period
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)
- Call Money Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts
- Process conducted in accordance with Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements existing regulations under:
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money procedures

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through the IBBS Call Money Module
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts are authorized to use the module
- Ensure proper collection and submission of call money payments from shareholders

**For Shareholders:**
- Pay Rs. 65.50 per partly paid-up equity share during the issue period
- Contact registrar MUFG Intime India Private Limited for queries

**Contact Points for Technical Issues:**

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Issue Opening Date:** Thursday, November 27, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Thursday, December 11, 2025
- **Issue Period:** 15 days
- **Bidding Hours:** 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:**
Holders of 6,10,58,392 partly paid-up equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited must pay the outstanding Rs. 65.50 per share to fully paid-up their holdings. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as per company law provisions.

**Market Impact:**
Limited to existing shareholders of this specific security (FUSIONPP). This is the first and final call, meaning shares will convert to fully paid-up equity upon payment. The call money collection will bring approximately Rs. 39.99 crores (6,10,58,392 Ã— Rs. 65.50) into the company.

Trading members and SCSBs need to facilitate payment collection through the IBBS platform during the 15-day window. The registrar MUFG Intime India Private Limited will process the call money payments and update share records accordingly.
