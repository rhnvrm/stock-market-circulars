## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by ARKA FINCAP LIMITED effective December 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED will be suspended from trading
- AFL-9%-27-12-25-NCD (ISIN: INE03W107264, Code: 939142)
- AFL-9.3%-27-12-25-NCD (ISIN: INE03W107215, Code: 939144)
- Record date set for December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 12, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for record date compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 12, 2025
- All BSE trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date regulations for corporate actions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED's 9% and 9.3% NCDs maturing on December 27, 2025. Impact is limited to these specific debt instruments. Investors holding these debentures as of the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. The suspension is temporary and routine for facilitating corporate actions related to debt servicing and maturity.
