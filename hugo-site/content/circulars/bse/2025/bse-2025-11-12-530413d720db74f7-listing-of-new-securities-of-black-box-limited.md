## Summary

BSE has listed 31,975 equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) with effect from November 13, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 417/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 415/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Number of shares: 31,975 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 417/- (Rs. 2/- face value + Rs. 415/- premium)
- Allotment type: Preferential basis to non-promoter
- Basis: Conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 170012511 to 170044485
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in period applicable: All 31,975 shares (Dist. Nos. 170012511-170044485) are locked in until May 25, 2026

## Important Dates

- Notice date: November 12, 2025
- Date of allotment: September 30, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 13, 2025
- Lock-in expiry: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves a small number of shares (31,975) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are subject to lock-in until May 25, 2026, preventing immediate trading. This is a routine corporate action with no significant implications for market operations or trading activity.
