## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the Debt Instruments segment (G GROUP) effective November 21, 2025. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities with ISIN codes IN002025X349, IN002025Y347, and IN002025Z344 respectively. Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each maturity date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed with scrip codes 805145, 805146, and 805147
- All securities have a market lot size of 1
- Listing effective from November 21, 2025
- Securities listed under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Trading suspension applies two working days before maturity/redemption date
- Contact numbers provided: 2272 8352/5753/8597 for clarifications

## Treasury Bills Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenor |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| 805145 | IN002025X349 | 91TB190226 | February 19, 2026 | 91-day |
| 805146 | IN002025Y347 | 182TB21526 | May 21, 2026 | 182-day |
| 805147 | IN002025Z344 | 364T191126 | November 19, 2026 | 364-day |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new T-Bills in their systems
- Members must ensure trading is suspended two working days before maturity date (T-2)
- Bank holidays are excluded from the T-2 calculation
- Members requiring clarification should contact the specified helpline numbers

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **91-day T-Bill Maturity**: February 19, 2026 (Trading suspension from February 17, 2026)
- **182-day T-Bill Maturity**: May 21, 2026 (Trading suspension from May 19, 2026)
- **364-day T-Bill Maturity**: November 19, 2026 (Trading suspension from November 17, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative notification for listing government securities. The T-Bills provide additional short-term investment options for debt market participants with varying maturity profiles (3 months, 6 months, and 12 months). No impact on equity trading operations or margin requirements. Trading members engaged in debt segment will have access to these new government securities for client portfolios and proprietary trading.
