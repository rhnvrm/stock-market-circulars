## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 3, 2025. The securities comprise 2,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.90% per annum. The securities are rated ACUITE AA/Stable and IVR AA/Positive, and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 2,000 debentures (Rs. 20 crores aggregate value)
- Scrip Code: 977340, Scrip ID: 89CGL27
- ISIN: INE180C07247
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Interest rate: 8.90% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit ratings: ACUITE AA/Stable and IVR AA/Positive
- Allotment date: December 1, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C07247
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE website for further details
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 1, 2026 to June 1, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: June 1, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The listing adds Rs. 20 crores worth of AA-rated debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment, providing investors with an additional fixed-income investment option with tenure until June 2027. The securities are relevant primarily to institutional and qualified investors participating in the debt market, with no put/call options available.
