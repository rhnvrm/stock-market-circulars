## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED issued on private placement basis. The securities comprise 500 non-convertible debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying 14.4% annual interest rate and maturing on May 31, 2027. Trading commenced on November 24, 2025 under scrip code 977310.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 977310
- **Scrip ID:** 144AFIL27
- **ISIN:** INE065507BM2
- **Quantity:** 500 debentures
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 1,00,000
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Interest Rate:** 14.4% per annum (monthly payment)
- **Credit Rating:** IVR BBB-/Stable
- **Allotment Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** May 31, 2027
- **Put/Call Option:** Not Available
- **Trading Mode:** Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size:** 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE065507BM2
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly from November 30, 2025 to May 31, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** May 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued through private placement with limited quantity (500 units). The BBB- credit rating indicates moderate credit quality. Impact is limited to investors in private debt securities and does not affect broader equity markets or trading operations.
