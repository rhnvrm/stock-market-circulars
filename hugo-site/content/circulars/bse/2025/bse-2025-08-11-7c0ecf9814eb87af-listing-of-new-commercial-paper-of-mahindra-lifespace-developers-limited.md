## Summary

Mahindra Lifespace Developers Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 75 crores on BSE's debt segment effective August 11, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 1,500 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crores
- Scrip Code: 729997, Scrip ID: MLDL080825
- ISIN: INE813A14342
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,205 per security
- Market lot: 1 security
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 4, 2025
- Tenor: Approximately 88 days

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: YES Bank Ltd
- Trading restricted to BSE debt segment

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Mahindra Lifespace Developers Limited and offers institutional investors a rated investment option with 88-day maturity.
