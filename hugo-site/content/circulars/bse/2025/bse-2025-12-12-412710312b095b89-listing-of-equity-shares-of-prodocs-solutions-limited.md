## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544643 and symbol PRODOCS with ISIN INE10XK01019. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544643
- Trading Symbol: PRODOCS
- ISIN: INE10XK01019
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for PRODOCS SOLUTIONS LIMITED for future trading once listing is confirmed.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with limited immediate market impact. Trading members should prepare their systems for the upcoming listing. The exact date and additional security details will be provided in a subsequent circular. Impact is confined to those interested in SME segment trading and this specific company.
