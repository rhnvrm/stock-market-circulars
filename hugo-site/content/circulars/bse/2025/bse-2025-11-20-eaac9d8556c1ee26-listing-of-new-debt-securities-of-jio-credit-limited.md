## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Jio Credit Limited issued on private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. The securities consist of 50,000 units with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each (total ₹500 crore), carrying a 7.26% annual interest rate and maturing on November 18, 2030. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Jio Credit Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977304
- **Scrip ID**: 726JCL30
- **ISIN**: INE282H07042
- **Quantity**: 50,000 units
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA
- **Interest Rate**: 7.26% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually (November 18 each year from 2026 to 2030)
- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 18, 2030
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE282H07042
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 18, 2026
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: November 18 annually (2027, 2028, 2029, 2030)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 18, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of debt securities with no direct impact on secondary equity markets.

**Investor Segment**: Relevant only to institutional debt investors and high-net-worth individuals given the high face value of ₹1 lakh per unit.

**Credit Quality**: CRISIL AAA rating indicates highest degree of safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations, reflecting strong credit profile of Jio Credit Limited.

**Liquidity**: As a privately placed instrument with high denomination, secondary market liquidity may be limited compared to publicly issued bonds.
