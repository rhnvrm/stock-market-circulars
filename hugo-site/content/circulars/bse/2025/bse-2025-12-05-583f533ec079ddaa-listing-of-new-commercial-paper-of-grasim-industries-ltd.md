## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Grasim Industries Ltd on a private placement basis. The listing is effective from December 5, 2025, on the BSE Debt segment. The issue comprises 10,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crores.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 500 crores
- Scrip Code: 730710
- Scrip ID: GIL051225
- ISIN: INE047A14AP4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,710.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE047A14AP4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at BSE (contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 5, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 5, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. It provides Grasim Industries with short-term financing (90-day tenor) and offers debt market participants an additional investment instrument rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA. The listing affects only participants in the BSE Debt segment and does not impact equity markets or broader trading operations.
