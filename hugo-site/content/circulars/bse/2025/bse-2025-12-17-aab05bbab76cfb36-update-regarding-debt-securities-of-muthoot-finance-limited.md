## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Muthoot Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 974673). Following the company's receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of listed debt securities will be revised effective December 18, 2025. The ISIN will change from INE414G07HV0 to INE414G07JV6, and the paid-up value will increase from Rs. 1,000 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture.

## Key Points

- ISIN changes from INE414G07HV0 to INE414G07JV6
- Paid-up value increases from Rs. 1,000 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture (100x increase)
- Changes effective from Thursday, December 18, 2025
- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20230317-7 dated March 17, 2023
- Trading Members are requested to take note of these changes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational update following corporate action by Muthoot Finance Limited regarding their existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE414G07JV6)
- Trading Members must update records to reflect the new paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- All trading and settlement activities from December 18, 2025 onwards must use the revised parameters

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Notice date
- **December 18, 2025**: Effective date for new ISIN and paid-up value changes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects specific debt securities of Muthoot Finance Limited. The 100-fold increase in paid-up value per debenture will significantly impact position sizing, collateral calculations, and trading lot sizes.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and market participants holding or trading these securities must update their systems and records to reflect the new ISIN and substantially higher paid-up value. Failure to update could result in trade errors or settlement issues.

**Investor Impact**: Existing debenture holders will see their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with the revised paid-up value following the call money payment.
