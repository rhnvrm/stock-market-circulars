## Summary

BSE announces the schedule for Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of TWAMEV CONSTRUCTION AND INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED. The OFS will be conducted over two days: November 18, 2025 (T Day) for Institutional/Non-Institutional investors and November 19, 2025 (T+1 Day) for Retail investors. Total offer size is 1,57,03,800 shares with 10% reserved for retail investors at a floor price of Rs. 32.50 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: TWAMEV CONSTRUCTION AND INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED (Symbol: TICL, Code: 532738, ISIN: INE388G01026)
- **Total Offer Size**: 1,57,03,800 shares
- **Retail Reservation**: 15,70,380 shares (10% reserved for retail investors)
- **Non-Retail Offer Size**: 1,41,33,420 shares
- **Floor Price**: Rs. 32.50 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1 per share
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Tick Size**: Rs. 0.05
- **Allocation Method**: Price Priority Method
- **Retail Investor Definition**: Individual investor with total bid value not exceeding Rs. 2 lakhs

## Bidding Schedule

### Day 1 - November 18, 2025 (T Day)
- **Eligible Categories**: Institutional Investors (IC/MF/OTHS) and Non-Institutional Investors (NII)
- **Bid Entry Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Bid Modification/Cancellation**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM

### Day 2 - November 19, 2025 (T+1 Day)
- **Eligible Categories**: Retail Investors (RI/RIC) and unallocated bidders from T Day carrying forward bids
- **Bid Entry Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Bid Modification/Cancellation**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give up/Take up for IC bids**: Until 7:30 AM on November 20, 2025 (T+2)

## Investor Categories and Margin Requirements

### Retail Investors (RI/RIC)
- **Eligibility**: Individual, HUF, NRI with bid value up to Rs. 2 lakhs
- **Margin**: 100% upfront margin
- **Collateral**: Cash and Cash Equivalents
- **Settlement**: T+2
- **Bidding Days**: T+1 Day only

### Non-Institutional Investors (NII)
- **Eligibility**: Individual, HUF, Trust, Body Corporates, PMS with bid value above Rs. 2 lakhs
- **Margin**: 100% upfront margin
- **Collateral**: Cash
- **Settlement**: T+1 (or T+2 if carried forward)
- **Bidding Days**: T Day and T+1 Day

### Institutional Investors (MF/IC/OTHS)
- **Categories**: Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, FII, FI, Banks
- **Margin Options**: 100% upfront margin OR 0% margin
- **Collateral**: Cash
- **Settlement**: T+1 (or T+2 if carried forward)
- **Bidding Days**: T Day and T+1 Day

## Important Notes

- Multiple bidding is allowed
- Retail investors can bid in RI, RIC, and/or NII categories
- Retail investors bidding in NII category are not eligible for any discount offered by sellers
- If cumulative bid value of retail investor across all categories exceeds Rs. 2 lakhs, bids in RI/RIC categories may be subject to special treatment
- Unallocated bidders on T Day can choose to carry forward their bids to T+1 Day

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodian members must note the OFS schedule
- Members must ensure proper margin collection as per investor category
- Settlement obligations apply as per specified timelines (T+1 or T+2)
- Access to trading platforms requires proper credentials and network setup

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **November 18, 2025 (T Day)**: Bidding for IC/MF/OTH/NII categories (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **November 19, 2025 (T+1 Day)**: Bidding for RI/RIC and carry-forward bids (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **November 19, 2025**: Give up/Take up deadline for T Day bids (until 7:30 AM)
- **November 20, 2025 (T+2)**: Give up/Take up deadline for T+1 Day institutional bids with 0% margin (until 7:30 AM)

## Impact Assessment

This OFS provides an opportunity for various investor categories to acquire shares of TWAMEV CONSTRUCTION AND INFRASTRUCTURE LIMITED at a floor price of Rs. 32.50. The staggered bidding schedule allows retail investors a separate day (T+1) to participate, with 10% of the offer reserved for them. The price priority allocation method ensures fair distribution. Market participants should prepare necessary margin requirements and access credentials for the specified trading platforms. The two-day structure with carry-forward provision for unallocated bids provides flexibility for institutional and non-institutional investors.
