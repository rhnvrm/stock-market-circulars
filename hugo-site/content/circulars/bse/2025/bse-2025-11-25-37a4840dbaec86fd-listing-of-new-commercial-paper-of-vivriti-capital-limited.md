## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 25, 2025. The listing comprises 200 units of Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 10 crore. The instruments carry a CARE A1+ credit rating and will mature on February 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Vivriti Capital Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 10 crore (200 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,89,564 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730645
- **Scrip ID**: VCL241125
- **ISIN**: INE01HV14AF0
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Trading Specifications

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading segment: BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01HV14AF0
- Transactions must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding to Vivriti Capital Limited for a 3-month period. The CARE A1+ rating indicates adequate safety for investors. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in Commercial Papers.
