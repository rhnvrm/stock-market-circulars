## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED with effect from December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED
- ISIN: INE572O07FN0
- Security Code: 960361
- Series Code: ECE-GSEC-23-12-25-PVT
- Record Date: December 8, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 8, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Circular Reference: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 8, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new orders should be accepted for the affected debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects a single privately placed debenture series with limited market circulation.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the specified security.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Only holders of this specific debenture series are affected. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance.
