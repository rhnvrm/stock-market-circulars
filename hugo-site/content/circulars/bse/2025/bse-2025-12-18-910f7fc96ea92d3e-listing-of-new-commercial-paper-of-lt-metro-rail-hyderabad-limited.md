## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025, with trading permitted only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 12,000 Commercial Paper securities of Rs. 5,00,000/- each listed
- Scrip Code: 730802, Scrip ID: LTMR171225
- ISIN Number: INE128M14AX1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,96,802/-
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- State Bank of India is the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members must use the specified ISIN Number INE128M14AX1 for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper has a short tenure (approximately 37 days) and is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and IND, indicating highest safety. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in short-term high-rated paper from infrastructure sector issuers.
