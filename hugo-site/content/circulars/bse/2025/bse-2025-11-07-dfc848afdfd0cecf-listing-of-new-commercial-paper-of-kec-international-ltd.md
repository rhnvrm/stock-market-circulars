## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by KEC International Ltd on private placement basis on the Debt segment effective November 7, 2025. The instrument comprises 2000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on February 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total quantity issued: 2000 Commercial Papers
- Face value per unit: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730507
- Scrip ID: KECIL71125
- ISIN: INE389H14LO5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading tick size: 1 paise
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities tradable only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LO5
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing on BSE's debt segment with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding for KEC International Ltd with a maturity period of 90 days. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors but has no impact on equity markets or retail investors.
