## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on a private placement basis in the Debt segment, effective November 13, 2025. The securities are rated CRISIL AA-/Positive with a face value of Rs. 10,000 and carry an interest rate of 10.40% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 100,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976712, Scrip ID: 1040MFL33
- ISIN: INE549K08541 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Positive
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,034.19
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K08541
- Trading members should adhere to the tick size of 1 paise
- Members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)
- Further details available in the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 13, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Monthly from November 30, 2025 to August 22, 2033
- Date of Redemption: August 22, 2033

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional debt instruments for institutional and qualified investors in the private placement market. The AA-/Positive rating indicates stable creditworthiness of Muthoot Fincorp Limited. The 10.40% interest rate reflects current market conditions for similarly rated corporate debt with approximately 8-year tenure.
