## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251016-9 informing trading members that equity shares of SK Minerals & Additives Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544584, symbol SKM, and ISIN INE13YH01017. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SK Minerals & Additives Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544584
- Trading Symbol: SKM
- ISIN Number: INE13YH01017
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the specific listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing adds a new company to the SME segment, providing investors with access to SK Minerals & Additives Limited equity shares. Trading members should prepare their systems with the assigned scrip code and symbol. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business fundamentals and investor interest, which will become clearer once the listing date is announced and trading commences.
