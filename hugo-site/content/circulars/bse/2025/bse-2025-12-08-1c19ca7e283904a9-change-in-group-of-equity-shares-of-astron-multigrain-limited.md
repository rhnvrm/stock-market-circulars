## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544628) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 22, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a transition to normal rolling settlement trading.

## Key Points

- Company: ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544628
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, December 22, 2025
- Notice references continuation of Notice No. 20251205-56 dated December 05, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED shares will shift from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. In the MT Group, all trades require full upfront delivery and payment. Upon transfer to M Group, the shares will be eligible for normal rolling settlement with standard T+1 settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M
- Position limits and margin requirements applicable to M Group stocks will apply
- Trading Members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **December 05, 2025**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20251205-56)
- **December 08, 2025**: Current notice date
- **December 22, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The migration from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically enhances liquidity as it allows for intraday trading, short selling (subject to regulations), and leveraged positions. This is a positive development for market participants as it provides greater flexibility in trading the stock.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to adjust their risk management systems and client communication regarding the change in settlement mechanism. The stock will become more accessible to retail traders who prefer rolling settlement over compulsory delivery-based trading.

**Investor Impact**: Investors will benefit from improved liquidity and the ability to participate in normal market operations including potential carry-forward positions, subject to applicable margin requirements.
