## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 6593) will be renamed to "RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Wednesday, November 26, 2025. This is an administrative name change notification for all trading members of the exchange.

## Key Points

- Old Name: MONEYLICIOUS SECURITIES PVT. LTD.
- New Name: RAISE SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number: 6593
- Effective Date: November 26, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of administrative name change for a trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of the member effective November 26, 2025. No specific action required from other members unless they have ongoing business relationships with this entity.

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Notice issued
- **November 26, 2025**: Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal impact on market operations. The trading member's member number (6593) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of identification in systems. Other trading members need only update their internal records if they maintain direct business relationships with this member. No impact on trading activities, settlement processes, or client operations.
