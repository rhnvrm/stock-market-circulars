## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of LUXURY TIME LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544635), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 26, 2025. This change follows the earlier notice dated December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: LUXURY TIME LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544635
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling Segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, December 26, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251211-1
- Reference Notice: 20251210-77 dated December 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will transition from a more restrictive trading environment to a standard rolling settlement mechanism:

- **From MT Group (Trade for Trade)**: Requires compulsory delivery and payment, no intraday trading allowed
- **To M Group (Rolling Segment)**: Standard rolling settlement with T+1 settlement cycle, intraday trading permitted

This change indicates the completion of the initial post-IPO stabilization period for the SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change
- All trades from December 26, 2025 onwards will be under M Group rules
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 26, 2025 (Friday)
- **Reference Notice Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The shift to rolling segment will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for LUXURY TIME LIMITED shares. Investors will be able to engage in intraday trading and benefit from T+1 settlement instead of compulsory delivery.

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a standard procedural change for SME IPO listings after the initial stabilization period. Trading members need minimal system updates to accommodate the group change.

**Investor Impact**: Positive - Enhanced trading flexibility and liquidity, making the stock more accessible to a broader range of market participants.
