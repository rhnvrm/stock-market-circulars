## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 4,000 units listed under scrip code 730081.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total value: Rs. 200 crore
- Scrip Code: 730081, Scrip ID: GFL25825
- ISIN: INE02KN14580
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,050.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 25, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Godrej Finance Limited and investment option for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
