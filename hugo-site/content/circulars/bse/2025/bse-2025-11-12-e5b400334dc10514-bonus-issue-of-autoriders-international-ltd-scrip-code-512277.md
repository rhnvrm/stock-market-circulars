## Summary

Autoriders International Ltd (ISIN: INE340U01010, Scrip Code: 512277) has announced a bonus issue of 29,00,700 equity shares to existing shareholders. The company has set the record date as 18/11/2025, with deemed allotment on 19/11/2025 and shares available for trading from 20/11/2025. This undertaking complies with SEBI Circular CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated 16-09-2024.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be issued: 29,00,700 equity shares
- Record date for determining eligible shareholders: 18/11/2025
- Distinctive number range: 5,80,141 to 34,80,840
- ISIN: INE340U01010
- Company undertakes to submit documents and credit bonus shares to depository system by 12:00 PM on T+1

## Regulatory Changes

This bonus issue follows SEBI Circular CIR/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated 16-09-2024, which mandates specific timelines for bonus share allotment and trading availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must ensure deemed date of allotment is T+1 (next working day after record date)
- Documents must be submitted and bonus shares credited to depository system by 12:00 PM on T+1
- Bonus shares must be made available for trading from beginning of day (BOD) on T+2

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: 18/11/2025 - Eligibility date for existing shareholders
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: 19/11/2025 (T+1)
- **Credit to Depositories**: 19/11/2025 by 12:00 PM (T+1)
- **Effective Listing Date**: 20/11/2025 (T+2)
- **Trading Availability**: 20/11/2025 from beginning of day (T+2)

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue will increase the total equity share capital of Autoriders International Ltd by 29,00,700 shares. Existing shareholders as on the record date of 18/11/2025 will receive bonus shares in their demat accounts. The shares will be available for trading from 20/11/2025, potentially increasing liquidity in the stock. The bonus issue follows regulatory timelines, ensuring smooth credit and trading operations.
