## Summary

BSE has notified that SAMCO Small Cap Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 09, 2025. This follows the previous suspension notice (Notice No. 20251113-47). Both Regular and Direct plan variants will be available with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- SAMCO Small Cap Fund reopening for ongoing transactions from December 09, 2025
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF0K1H01305) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF0K1H01297)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Transaction platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251113-47

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding the resumption of transactions in a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. BSE members and mutual fund distributors should note the availability of the scheme for processing investor transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 09, 2025 - SAMCO Small Cap Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only investors and distributors dealing with SAMCO Small Cap Fund.

**Operational Impact**: Investors who were unable to transact in this scheme during the suspension period can now resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal plans through the BSE StAR MF Platform. This provides renewed access to a small-cap equity investment option for retail and institutional investors.
