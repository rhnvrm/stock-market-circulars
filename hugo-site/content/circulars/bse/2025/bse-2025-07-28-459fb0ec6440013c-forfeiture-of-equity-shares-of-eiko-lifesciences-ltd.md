## Summary

Eiko Lifesciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 540204) has forfeited 1,39,591 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to shareholders' failure to pay allotment/call monies. The forfeited shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 13720855 to 13860445.

## Key Points

- 1,39,591 equity shares forfeited by Eiko Lifesciences Ltd
- Face value of Rs. 10/- per share
- Shares were partly paid-up
- Forfeiture due to non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 13720855 to 13860445
- Company scrip code: 540204

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 28, 2025
- Notice Number: 20250728-11

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The forfeiture affects only specific shareholders who failed to meet their payment obligations on partly paid-up shares. The action reduces the company's share capital and outstanding obligations from defaulting shareholders.
