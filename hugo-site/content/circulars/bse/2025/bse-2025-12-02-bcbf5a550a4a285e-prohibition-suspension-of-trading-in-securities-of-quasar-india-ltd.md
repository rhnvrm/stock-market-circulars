## Summary

BSE has prohibited/suspended trading in the securities of Quasar India Ltd (Scrip Code: 538452) effective December 3, 2025. This action follows the company's failure to respond to a show cause notice and its confirmed non-existence at the registered office address during an exchange site visit. The suspension is implemented as an interim measure.

## Key Points

- Trading in Quasar India Ltd securities suspended from December 3, 2025
- Company did not respond to show cause notice issued by BSE
- Site visit by BSE confirmed company is non-existent at registered office address
- Scrip Code: 538452
- Suspension is an interim measure pending further action
- All trading members and investors are advised to take note

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This circular implements existing regulatory powers to suspend trading when a company is non-compliant and non-existent at its registered address.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in Quasar India Ltd securities from December 3, 2025
- Investors holding positions in this security should be aware that trading will not be possible
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading prohibition

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 2, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete halt in trading activity for Quasar India Ltd securities affects all existing investors and position holders.

**Investor Impact:** High - Investors holding shares cannot exit positions through market transactions during the suspension period. Liquidity is completely frozen.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must immediately update systems and notify clients holding positions in this security about the trading prohibition.

**Regulatory Significance:** This action demonstrates BSE's enforcement of compliance requirements and physical verification standards for listed companies. The company's non-existence at its registered address and failure to respond to regulatory notices represents serious compliance breaches that warrant immediate trading suspension to protect market integrity.
