## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 5.36 million equity shares of Niraj Cement Structurals Limited (Scrip Code: 532986) issued on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The new shares will commence trading on August 5, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 5,360,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 43
- Issue price: Rs. 53 per share
- Allotment date: March 21, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 54334341 to 59694340
- ISIN: INE368I01016
- Issued to both promoter and non-promoter categories

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing approval for preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading operations
- Lock-in provisions must be observed as specified
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: March 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (2.56M shares)**: February 4, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (2.8M shares)**: February 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The preferential issue represents a significant capital raise for Niraj Cement Structurals Limited. With differentiated lock-in periods, the shares will be released to the market in phases, potentially affecting liquidity and price dynamics. The warrant conversion indicates successful completion of the fundraising process initiated earlier.
