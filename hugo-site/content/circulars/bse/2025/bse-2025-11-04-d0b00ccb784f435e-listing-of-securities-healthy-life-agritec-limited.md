## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 24,812,000 new equity shares of Healthy Life Agritec Limited (Scrip Code: 543546) issued through a rights offering. The shares will be available for trading from November 6, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 24,812,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 543546
- ISIN: INE0L3501015
- Distinctive Numbers: 24812001 to 49624000
- Date of Allotment: November 3, 2025
- Trading Start Date: November 6, 2025 (Thursday)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing and trading commencement of the new securities
- The new shares are to be traded under the existing scrip code 543546

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 6, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide impact. The rights issue increases the paid-up capital of Healthy Life Agritec Limited by approximately 24.81 million shares. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights offering will see their holdings increase proportionately. The listing enables secondary market trading of these newly issued shares, providing liquidity to rights issue subscribers.
