## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of BAJAJ FINANCE LIMITED (Code: BFL-8.95%-8-1-26-PVT, ISIN: INE296A07QS1, Scrip Code: 958514) effective December 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading in the specified debentures will be suspended from December 24, 2025
- Record date set for December 24, 2025
- Debenture series: BFL-8.95%-8-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE296A07QS1
- Scrip Code: 958514
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-784/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debt securities undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to note this suspension and ensure compliance
- All affected parties must observe the record date of December 24, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** December 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. Trading suspension is a standard procedure for securities approaching redemption and does not indicate financial distress.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must ensure their systems are updated to prevent trading in the affected debentures from December 24, 2025.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Those holding on record date (December 24, 2025) will be eligible for the payment. Secondary market liquidity ceases from the suspension date.
