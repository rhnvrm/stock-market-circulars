## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has applied for resignation of its membership from the Exchange in accordance with the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations. Trading members are invited to submit any objections in writing within a 15-day period.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 6853) has applied for resignation of BSE membership
- Application submitted as per Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange
- Trading members may raise objections through the Membership department
- Objections must be submitted in writing within the specified timeframe
- Clarifications can be requested via email to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative process for member resignation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with objections must submit them in writing to the Membership department
- Objections must be submitted within 15 days from December 1, 2025
- Any clarifications should be requested via email to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Objection Deadline**: December 15, 2025 (15 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to a single member's voluntary resignation. The impact is primarily administrative and affects:

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and its internal operations
- Trading members who have existing relationships or outstanding matters with Global Vision Securities
- BSE Membership department for processing the resignation

The 15-day objection period provides sufficient time for any affected parties to raise concerns before the resignation is processed.
