## Summary

BSE has announced that AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF schemes will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 01, 2026. This follows the previous suspension notice (No. 20251209-50) and allows investors to resume transactions in these debt category fund-of-funds schemes.

## Key Points

- AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from January 01, 2026
- Both Regular Plan (ISIN: INF846KA1390) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF846KA1408) Growth options will be available
- Subscription and redemption facilities will be restored
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be available
- Schemes are categorized under DEBT category
- Notice references previous suspension notice No. 20251209-50

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of specific mutual fund schemes on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can resume transactions in the specified schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 31, 2025
- **Effective Date:** January 01, 2026 - Schemes reopen for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only investors and members dealing with AXIS GOLD AND SILVER PASSIVE FOF schemes. The reopening provides liquidity and transaction access to existing and potential investors in these gold and silver fund-of-funds products. No broader market implications beyond the specific mutual fund schemes mentioned.
