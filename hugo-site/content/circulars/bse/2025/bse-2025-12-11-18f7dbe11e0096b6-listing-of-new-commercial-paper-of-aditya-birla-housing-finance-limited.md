## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 500 crores) with ISIN INE831R14FQ2, allotted on December 10, 2025, and maturing on March 11, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730747
- Scrip ID: ABHF101225
- ISIN: INE831R14FQ2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,691.50
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FQ2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for price movements
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at BSE on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: December 11, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (91-day commercial paper) on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments. The listing provides Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited access to short-term funding through the debt market. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
