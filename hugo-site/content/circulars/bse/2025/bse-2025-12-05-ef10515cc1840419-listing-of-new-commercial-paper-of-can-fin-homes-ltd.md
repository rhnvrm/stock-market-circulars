## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Can Fin Homes Ltd on a private placement basis in the Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The instrument consists of 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on March 5, 2026. HDFC Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 10,000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,631 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730707
- Scrip ID: CFH041225
- ISIN: INE477A14EB5
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE477A14EB5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed

## Important Dates

- Notice date: December 5, 2025
- Date of allotment: December 4, 2025
- Listing effective date: December 5, 2025
- Date of redemption: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term financing for Can Fin Homes Ltd with a 3-month maturity. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+, ICRA A1+) indicate low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in short-term high-quality debt instruments.
