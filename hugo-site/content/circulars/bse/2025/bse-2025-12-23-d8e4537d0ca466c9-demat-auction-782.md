## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction Settlement No. 782 dated December 23, 2025. This auction includes 130 scrips with varying quantities available for settlement through the demat auction process. The auction covers securities across multiple sectors including banking, automotive, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, and manufacturing.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 782
- Settlement Date: December 23, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 130 scrips listed
- Quantities range from 1 share to 50,000 shares depending on the scrip
- Covers diverse sectors including financial services, automotive, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, and FMCG
- Notable high-volume scrips include STANDARD CAP (50,000 shares), JAYASWAL NEC (5,000 shares), and RIR POWER EL (4,347 shares)
- Includes major blue-chip stocks like ITC, HDFC Bank, NTPC, Siemens, and Tata Motors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants in the demat auction must follow BSE's standard auction settlement procedures
- Clearing members and brokers must ensure timely settlement of auctioned securities
- All transactions must be settled in dematerialized form as per SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 23, 2025
- Settlement Number: 782

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction with limited market-wide impact. The auction primarily affects:

- Specific shareholders who have pending delivery obligations in the listed scrips
- Clearing members responsible for settlement of these securities
- Buyers participating in the auction process

The quantities involved are relatively small for most scrips, suggesting minimal market disruption. However, holders of securities listed in this auction should be aware of potential settlement obligations. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of failed trades and maintains market integrity.
