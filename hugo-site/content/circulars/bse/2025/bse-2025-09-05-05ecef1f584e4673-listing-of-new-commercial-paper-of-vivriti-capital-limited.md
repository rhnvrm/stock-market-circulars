## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crore issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade in the BSE Debt segment with effect from September 5, 2025, under scrip code 730162 (VCL4925).

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,85,135 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE01HV14946
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions are in dematerialized form only
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Federal Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 5, 2025
- Redemption date: January 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides Vivriti Capital with working capital financing and offers institutional investors a rated investment option with 4-month maturity.
