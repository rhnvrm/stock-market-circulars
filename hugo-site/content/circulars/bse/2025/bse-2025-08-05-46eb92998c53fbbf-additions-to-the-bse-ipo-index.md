## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective August 7, 2025. Laxmi India Finance Limited (ticker 544465) and Aditya Infotech Limited (ticker 544466) will be included in the index following their listing on August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE IPO Index
- Effective date: August 7, 2025 at market open
- Both companies listed on BSE on August 5, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine index maintenance following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **August 5, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **August 7, 2025**: Effective date for BSE IPO Index addition at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine index addition following standard listing procedures. Index constituents and weightings will be updated accordingly for tracking funds and investment products linked to the BSE IPO Index.
