## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the name of Rajasthan Gases Ltd. This is an administrative corporate action that involves updating the company's registered name while maintaining continuity of its scrip code and ISIN numbers for trading purposes.

## Key Points

- Company name change notification for Rajasthan Gases Ltd
- Scrip code and trading symbol to be updated accordingly
- ISIN numbers may be revised or retained based on regulatory requirements
- Trading operations to continue without disruption

## Regulatory Changes

The name change follows standard corporate governance procedures and has been approved by the company's board and shareholders as required under Companies Act provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to update their records with the new company name
- Existing shareholders' holdings remain unaffected
- All existing securities continue to be valid under the new name
- Trading members to ensure system updates for client records

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 5, 2025
- Effective date for name change to be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The name change does not affect:
- Share capital structure
- Shareholder rights or holdings
- Trading operations or market timings
- Financial obligations or contracts

Investors and market participants need only update their records to reflect the new company name while all other trading parameters remain unchanged.
