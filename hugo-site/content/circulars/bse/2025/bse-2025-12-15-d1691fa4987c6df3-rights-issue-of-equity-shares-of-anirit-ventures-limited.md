## Summary

ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED (BSE Code: 530705) has announced a rights issue offering 2 equity shares for every 1 share held. The issue price is Rs. 33 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 23). Trading in the company's shares will be on ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 2 equity shares for every 1 existing equity share
- Issue price: Rs. 33 per share (Face value: Rs. 10, Premium: Rs. 23)
- Payment structure: Rs. 23 per share on application, balance Rs. 10 per share on subsequent call(s)
- Record date: December 18, 2025
- Ex-rights date: December 18, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-780/2025-2026
- Company code: 530705

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute transactions in ANIRIT VENTURES shares on ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025
- Shareholders on record as of December 18, 2025 will be eligible for the rights issue
- Application money of Rs. 23 per share payable upfront, with balance Rs. 10 per share on call(s)

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Circular notification date
- **December 18, 2025**: Record date for rights issue eligibility
- **December 18, 2025**: Ex-rights trading commences (Settlement DR-780/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will allow existing shareholders of ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED to subscribe to additional shares at a predetermined price. The 2:1 ratio means shareholders can potentially triple their holdings. The ex-rights adjustment will affect the stock's trading price from December 18, 2025. Shareholders must hold shares before the ex-rights date to be eligible for participation in the rights issue.
