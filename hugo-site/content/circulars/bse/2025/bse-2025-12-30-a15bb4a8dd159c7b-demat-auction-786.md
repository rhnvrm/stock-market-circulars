## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities under Demat Auction Settlement No. 786 dated December 30, 2025. This auction includes 100 securities from various companies with specified quantities to be auctioned to close out delivery shortfalls from trading members who failed to deliver securities on settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 786
- Auction Date: December 30, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 100 scrips
- Highest Quantity: TIMEX GROUP (263,431 shares), PRAKASH STEEL (73,002 shares), PATEL CHEM (20,800 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, mutual funds, infrastructure, metals, and consumer goods
- Quantities range from 1 share to over 260,000 shares depending on the security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard demat auction process as per existing BSE regulations for settlement of delivery obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Securities will be auctioned to fulfill delivery obligations
- Defaulting members will be liable for auction costs and price differences
- Clients and trading members affected should ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Settlement No.**: 786

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 100 securities across multiple sectors. Large quantities in select scrips like TIMEX GROUP (263,431 shares) and PRAKASH STEEL (73,002 shares) may experience temporary price volatility during auction.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members with delivery shortfalls will incur additional costs through the auction mechanism. Clients whose securities are being auctioned should monitor their holdings and demat accounts.

**Sector Coverage**: Broad impact across banking (BANKBARODA, CANBK, IDFCFIRSTB, UNIONBANK), metals (VEDL, HINDZINC, NMDC), infrastructure (NBCC, AFCONS), technology (INFY), and mutual funds (HDFCMF, SBIMF, ICICIPRUAMC).
