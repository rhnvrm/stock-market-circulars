## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 85,00,000 new equity shares of Royal India Corporation Limited (Scrip Code: 512047) with effect from December 12, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at par value of Rs. 10/- per share.

## Key Points

- 85,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences on December 12, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 109470001 to 117970000
- Date of Allotment: September 26, 2025
- ISIN: INE510H01015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in period applicable until June 29, 2025 for all 85,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 109470001 to 117970000)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: June 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment to promoters represents a dilution of approximately 85 lakh shares. All shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2025, preventing immediate liquidity impact. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining uniform shareholder rights. This is a standard corporate action with no expected significant effect on trading or market operations.
