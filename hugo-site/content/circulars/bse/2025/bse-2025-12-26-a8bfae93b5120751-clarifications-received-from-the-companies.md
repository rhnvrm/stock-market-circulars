## Summary

BSE issued clarifications from three companies - PH Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 500143), Sula Vineyards Limited (Scrip Code: 543711), and Castrol India Limited - in response to surveillance queries regarding significant price/volume movements in their securities. All companies confirmed they have made timely disclosures under SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 and stated that the observed movements are purely market-driven with no undisclosed material information.

## Key Points

- PH Capital Limited responded to BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/807 regarding significant price movement
- Sula Vineyards Limited addressed NSE letter NSE/CM/Surveillance/16227 and BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/806 regarding volume increase
- All companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated no material events or price-sensitive information has been withheld
- Price/volume movements attributed to market conditions and market-driven factors
- Companies reiterated commitment to timely disclosure of price-sensitive information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance responses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Confirmations required that all disclosures under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations have been made
- Companies must declare if any undisclosed price-sensitive information exists

## Important Dates

- December 24, 2025: NSE letter to Sula Vineyards
- December 26, 2025: BSE letters to PH Capital Limited and Sula Vineyards
- December 26, 2025: Company responses submitted

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine surveillance clarifications with no new material information disclosed. The companies confirm that observed price/volume movements are market-driven.

**Operational Impact**: None - No changes to operations or compliance requirements beyond standard disclosure obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Provides transparency that no undisclosed events are driving stock movements, confirming market efficiency and proper disclosure practices by the listed entities.
