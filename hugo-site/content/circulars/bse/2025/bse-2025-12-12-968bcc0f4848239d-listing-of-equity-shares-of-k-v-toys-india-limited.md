## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544641, symbol KVTOYS, and ISIN number INE1IE301018. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- K. V. TOYS INDIA LIMITED to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544641
- Symbol: KVTOYS
- ISIN: INE1IE301018
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing for operational readiness. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The announcement is relevant primarily to:

- Trading members who need to update their systems with the new scrip details
- Investors interested in the SME segment
- The company and its shareholders

The broader market impact is negligible as this concerns a single SME listing. Full trading details and the official listing date will be provided in a subsequent circular.
