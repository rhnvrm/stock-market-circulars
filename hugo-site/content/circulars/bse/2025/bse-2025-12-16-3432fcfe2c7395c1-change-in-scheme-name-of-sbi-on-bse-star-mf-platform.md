## Summary

BSE has announced changes in the scheme names of SBI Mutual Fund products available on the BSE StAR MF platform. This is an administrative update to align scheme nomenclature with regulatory requirements or fund house branding guidelines. The changes affect how the schemes are displayed and identified on the BSE trading platform.

## Key Points

- Changes apply to SBI Mutual Fund schemes listed on BSE StAR MF platform
- Administrative name change only - no impact on scheme characteristics, NAV, or investor holdings
- Updated scheme names will be reflected in platform systems
- Investors can continue transactions using updated scheme names

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative notification for scheme name updates. No regulatory compliance changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems with the new scheme names
- Distributors and intermediaries should use updated scheme names for transactions
- No action required from existing investors

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Effective Date: As specified in the circular (refer to PDF for exact implementation date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Negligible - This is purely an administrative change affecting scheme identification on the platform.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Existing investments remain unaffected. Investors should note the new scheme names for future transactions and record-keeping.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Platform intermediaries need to update scheme names in their systems and communications.
