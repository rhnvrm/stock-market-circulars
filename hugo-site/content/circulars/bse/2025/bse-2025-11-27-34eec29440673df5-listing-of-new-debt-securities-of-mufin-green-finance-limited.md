## Summary

MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling 5,000 securities (Rs. 50 crores). The securities carry a credit rating of ACUITE A- and offer 10.85% p.a. interest with monthly payments.

## Key Points

- Issuer: MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 977318
- Scrip ID: 1085MGFL26
- ISIN: INE08KJ07159
- Quantity: 5,000 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 50 crores
- Interest Rate: 10.85% per annum
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A-
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 26, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07159
- The tick size for these securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Further details available in the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 27, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from December 26, 2025 to December 26, 2026
- Redemption Date: December 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing adds Rs. 50 crores of debt securities to BSE's debt segment. The one-year maturity period and monthly interest payment structure provides investors with a short-term debt investment option. The ACUITE A- rating indicates moderate credit quality. No put/call options are available on these securities.
