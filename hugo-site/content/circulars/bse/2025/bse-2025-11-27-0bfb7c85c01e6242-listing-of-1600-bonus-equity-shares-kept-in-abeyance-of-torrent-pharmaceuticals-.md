## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,600 bonus equity shares of Torrent Pharmaceuticals Ltd (Scrip Code: 500420) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted for trading effective November 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,600 bonus equity shares of Rs. 5/- each being listed
- Shares were previously held in abeyance
- ISIN: INE685A01028
- Two batches of shares: 800 shares each
- Distinctive numbers: 338437041-338437840 and 338411241-338412040
- Effective trading date: Friday, November 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for shares released from abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to the small quantity of shares (1,600) being released. These bonus shares were kept in abeyance and are now being made available to eligible shareholders. The volume represents a negligible fraction of Torrent Pharmaceuticals' total equity capital and will not materially affect trading or market capitalization.
