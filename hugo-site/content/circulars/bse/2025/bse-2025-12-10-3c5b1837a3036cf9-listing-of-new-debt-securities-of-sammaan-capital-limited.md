## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Sammaan Capital Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment, effective December 10, 2025. The securities comprise 20,000 units of SCL-8.79%-09-12-30-PVT (Scrip Code: 977354) and 10,000 units of SCL-8.86%-09-12-30-PVT (Scrip Code: 977355), both with face value of Rs. 100,000 per unit.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Sammaan Capital Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- First security: 20,000 units with 8.7923% p.a. interest, monthly interest payment (ISIN: INE148I07YI2)
- Second security: 10,000 units with 8.857% p.a. interest, quarterly interest payment (ISIN: INE148I07YH4)
- Both securities allotted on December 9, 2025
- Credit ratings: CRISIL AA/Stable and ICRA AA/Stable (first security), ICRA AA-/STABLE (second security)
- Both securities mature on December 9, 2030
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 100,000 per unit for both securities
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit for both securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 10, 2025
- **First Interest Payment (SCL-8.79%):** January 9, 2026 (monthly thereafter until December 9, 2030)
- **First Interest Payment (SCL-8.86%):** March 9, 2026 (quarterly thereafter until December 9, 2030)
- **Redemption Date:** December 9, 2030 (both securities)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides institutional investors access to two rated debt instruments from Sammaan Capital Limited with investment-grade credit ratings (AA/Stable and AA-/Stable). The securities offer interest rates of approximately 8.79% and 8.86% respectively, with different payment frequencies. No put/call options are available on these securities. The listing expands BSE's debt market offerings but has no direct impact on equity markets or trading members' operations.
