## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Infibeam Avenues Limited to collect the first and final call money on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 69,99,85,723 partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 5 per share during the issue period from November 25 to December 9, 2025. The module will be available to Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts to submit call money notice details.

## Key Points

- Total partly paid-up equity shares subject to call: 69,99,85,723 shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 5 per partly paid-up equity share
- This is the first and final call on these shares
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Registrar: Alankit Assignments Limited (SEBI Registration No: INR000001385)
- Contact email: infibeamrights@alankit.com
- Rights issue website: https://rights.alankit.com
- Security symbol: INFIBPPCM
- Call money collection follows SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements existing framework under BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 for call money collection on partly paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through the Call Money Module on IBBS platform
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can submit details
- Must process call money payments during bidding hours (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM)

**For Shareholders:**
- Pay Rs. 5 per partly paid-up equity share during the issue period
- Make payments through designated SCSBs or Trading Members
- Contact the Registrar (Alankit Assignments Limited) for queries

**Contact Details for Support:**
- Regulatory/Business queries: listing.sales@bseindia.com (022-2272 8846/5063/8179)
- Bidding queries: ibbs.ops@bseindia.com (022-2272 8376/8436/8226)
- User ID/password issues: user.trdops@bseindia.com (022-2272 8005/8215)

## Important Dates

- **Issue Opening Date:** Tuesday, November 25, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, December 9, 2025
- **Bidding Hours:** 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (daily during issue period)
- **Issue Period Duration:** 15 days
- **Notice Date:** November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects holders of approximately 70 crore partly paid-up equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Limited who are required to pay the outstanding call amount.

**Operational Impact:** Trading Members and SCSBs must ensure their systems are configured to access and use the Call Money Module on the IBBS platform during the 15-day issue period. Shareholders must arrange funds to meet the call money obligation of Rs. 5 per share.
