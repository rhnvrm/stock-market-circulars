## Summary

SEBI has issued detailed compliance guidelines for implementing the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 across all regulated entities' digital platforms. This circular (SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/131) dated September 25, 2025, provides specific timelines and mechanisms for ensuring digital accessibility compliance, including mandatory accessibility audits by IAAP certified professionals.

## Key Points

- All regulated entities must comply with digital accessibility requirements for investor-facing platforms
- IAAP certified accessibility professionals must be appointed as auditors
- Accessibility audits must be conducted for all digital platforms
- Findings from audits must be remediated within specified timelines
- Annual accessibility audits will be mandatory going forward
- Different reporting mechanisms for stock brokers, depository participants, investment advisors, research analysts, and other regulated entities
- Submissions to SEBI must be made via email to digital_acc@sebi.gov.in
- Multiple digital platforms require consolidated audit reports

## Regulatory Changes

This circular provides implementation guidelines for the previously issued circular dated July 31, 2025 (SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/111) on Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The guidelines specify:

- Structured compliance requirements in five phases
- Reporting authority mechanism based on entity type
- Standardized formats for compliance submission
- Annual compliance reporting framework effective from FY 2026-27

## Compliance Requirements

### Phase 1 (By September 30, 2025)
- Submit list of digital platforms provided to investors (Table-C1 format)

### Phase 2 (By December 14, 2025)
- Appoint IAAP certified accessibility professionals as auditors (Table-C2 format)

### Phase 3 (By April 30, 2026)
- Conduct accessibility audit for all digital platforms (Table-C3 format)
- Submit consolidated initial audit reports for entities with multiple platforms

### Phase 4 (By July 31, 2026)
- Complete remediation of audit findings
- Ensure full compliance with circular requirements (Table-C4 format)
- Submit consolidated final audit reports

### Phase 5 (Annually from April 30, 2027)
- Conduct annual accessibility audits
- Submit final audit reports within 30 days of each financial year end

### Reporting Mechanism
- **Stock Brokers/Depository Participants**: Report to Stock Exchanges/Depositories
- **Investment Advisors and Research Analysts**: Report to BSE Limited
- **Market Infrastructure Institutions and other Regulated Entities**: Report directly to SEBI

## Important Dates

- **September 30, 2025**: Submit list of digital platforms
- **December 14, 2025**: Appointment of IAAP certified auditors
- **April 30, 2026**: Complete accessibility audits and submit initial reports
- **July 31, 2026**: Complete remediation and submit final audit reports
- **April 30, 2027 onwards**: Annual compliance submission (within 30 days of each financial year)

## Impact Assessment

### Operational Impact
- Regulated entities must allocate resources for hiring IAAP certified professionals
- Technical teams will need to conduct comprehensive accessibility audits
- Development teams must remediate identified accessibility issues
- Ongoing annual compliance will require sustained investment in accessibility

### Market Impact
- Enhanced accessibility will improve market access for persons with disabilities
- Increased investor inclusion and participation in securities markets
- Standardization of digital accessibility across the securities industry

### Compliance Impact
- Multiple deadlines require careful project management
- Entities with multiple platforms face consolidated reporting requirements
- Non-compliance may result in regulatory action
- Annual audits create ongoing compliance obligations

### Investor Impact
- Improved digital accessibility for investors with disabilities
- Better user experience across regulated entity platforms
- Increased ability to independently access securities market services
