## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited on its Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and comprise 5,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, carrying a 10% annual interest rate and maturing on August 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 debentures
- Scrip Code: 977065
- Scrip ID: 10KFLD27
- ISIN: INE403Q07GF7 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,465.75
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Interest Rate: 10% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Date of Allotment: December 18, 2025
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403Q07GF7
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Quarterly from March 1, 2026 to August 29, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: August 29, 2027
