## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 10 companies effective from their respective "no dealings" dates. The suspension is due to upcoming redemptions with record dates fixed between December 12-15, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension effective from respective "no dealings" dates
- All redemptions scheduled between December 12-15, 2025
- Notice issued under Category: Corporate Actions, Segment: Debt
- Notice Number: 20251210-7, dated December 10, 2025

## Affected Commercial Papers

**Effective December 12, 2025:**
- SBICAP Securities Limited (INE212K14CF0/730239) - Record Date: 12/12/2025

**Effective December 15, 2025:**
- Godrej Finance Limited (INE02KN14408/728463)
- Vertis Infrastructure Trust (INE0KXY14030/728473)
- Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (INE824H14SJ6/730243)
- HDFC Securities Limited (INE700G14PY5/730245)
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (INE01C314CT1/730246)
- SBICAP Securities Limited (INE212K14CG8/730248)
- DSP Finance Private Limited (INE422H14024/730288)
- Tata Steel Limited (INE081A14GH7/730409)
- Redington Limited (INE891D14A87/730417)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from their respective "no dealings" dates
- Members should ensure compliance with the suspension to avoid regulatory issues
- Internal systems should be updated to reflect trading restrictions

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Trading suspension begins for SBICAP Securities Limited CP; Record date for redemption
- **December 15, 2025**: Trading suspension begins for 9 companies' CPs; Record date for their redemptions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers

**Operational Impact:** Routine redemption process with standard trading suspension. No unusual circumstances reported.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. Trading suspension is temporary and specific to redemption record dates.
