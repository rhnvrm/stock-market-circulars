## Summary

BSE has announced shut periods for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment due to corporate actions in 23 securities. The shut periods are scheduled between November 13-21, 2025, affecting securities undergoing dividend distributions and one rights issue. During these shut periods, SLB transactions will not be permitted for the affected securities.

## Key Points

- 22 securities will have SLB shut periods due to dividend corporate actions
- 1 security (Adani Enterprises Ltd.) will have SLB shut period due to rights issue of equity shares
- Shut periods range from 2-4 days depending on the security
- Ex-dates and book closure/record dates vary from November 14-21, 2025
- ONGC and MOIL have the earliest shut periods starting November 13, 2025
- Multiple securities including Sonata Software, IRCTC, Info Edge, and Gabriel India have shut periods from November 20-21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular informing market participants about temporary restrictions in the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must not initiate new SLB transactions for the listed securities during their respective shut periods
- Participants should plan their SLB strategies accounting for these temporary restrictions
- Brokers and market intermediaries must inform their clients about the SLB unavailability during specified periods

## Important Dates

**Shut Periods by Date Range:**

- **November 13-14, 2025**: ONGC
- **November 13-15, 2025**: RITES, MOIL
- **November 14-17, 2025**: Balrampur Chini Mills, Pearl Global Industries, EPL Limited, Surya Roshni Ltd., Adani Enterprises (Rights Issue)
- **November 17-18, 2025**: Cochin Shipyard, Asian Paints, IRB Infrastructure, Precision Wires, MAN Infraconstruction
- **November 18-19, 2025**: NBCC, Jamna Auto Industries, Page Industries, CARE Ratings, Banco Products
- **November 19-20, 2025**: Container Corporation of India
- **November 20-21, 2025**: Sonata Software, IRCTC, Info Edge, Gabriel India

**Ex-Dates**: Range from November 14-21, 2025
**Book Closure/Record Dates**: Range from November 14-21, 2025
**Foreclosure Date**: November 17, 2025 (Adani Enterprises only)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects liquidity in the SLB segment for 23 securities, including several large-cap and mid-cap stocks. However, impact is limited to the SLB segment only and does not affect normal equity trading.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants using SLB for hedging, arbitrage, or short-selling strategies will need to plan around these shut periods. The staggered nature of shut periods (spanning November 13-21) helps distribute the impact.

**Investor Impact**: Limited direct impact on regular equity investors. Primarily affects institutional investors and traders using SLB mechanisms for short positions or securities lending activities.

**Notable Securities Affected**: Includes major companies across sectors - PSUs (ONGC, RITES, Cochin Shipyard, IRCTC, CONCOR, NBCC, MOIL), private sector leaders (Asian Paints, Info Edge, Sonata Software, Page Industries), and Adani Enterprises undergoing rights issue.
