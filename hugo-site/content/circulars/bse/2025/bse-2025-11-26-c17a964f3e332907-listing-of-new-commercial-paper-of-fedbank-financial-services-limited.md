## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The issue consists of 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 3000 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,460 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730659
- Scrip ID: FFSL251125
- ISIN: INE007N14EJ6
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Allotment date: November 25, 2025
- Redemption date: February 24, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Available on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The instrument provides short-term debt investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment. The A1+ credit ratings from both CRISIL and ICRA indicate the highest safety level for short-term debt instruments, reflecting strong creditworthiness of Fedbank Financial Services Limited.
