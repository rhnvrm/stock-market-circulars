## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 16,49,840 new equity shares of Coromandel Engineering Company Limited (Scrip Code: 533167) effective September 4, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 40.05 per share through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 16,49,840 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 30.05/-
- Issue price: Rs. 40.05 per share
- Issued through warrant conversion on preferential basis
- Distribution numbers: 33233599 to 34883438
- Date of allotment: May 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE312J01012
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in provisions applicable as per distribution details

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: May 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 15, 2026 (13,00,000 shares) and March 15, 2027 (3,49,840 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents additional shares of an existing listed company through warrant conversion. The lock-in periods provide market stability by restricting immediate trading of significant portions.
