## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of NANTA TECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544668 and symbol NANTA (ISIN: INE0YJA01011). The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Nanta Tech Limited
- Scrip Code: 544668
- Symbol: NANTA
- ISIN Number: INE0YJA01011
- Market Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing of NANTA TECH LIMITED shares for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in SME stocks. Trading members should prepare systems for the new scrip once the listing date is announced. No immediate action required as listing date is pending confirmation.
