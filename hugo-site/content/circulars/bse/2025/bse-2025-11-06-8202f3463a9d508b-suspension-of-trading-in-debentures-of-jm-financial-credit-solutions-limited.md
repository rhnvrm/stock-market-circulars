## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited (ISIN: INE651J07952, Code: 974918) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is implemented to facilitate partial redemption due to buyback at investor request and payment of interest, with a record date set for November 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading in JMFCSL debentures (8.80% coupon, maturity 19-12-25) to be suspended from November 7, 2025
- Record date for the corporate action is November 8, 2025
- Purpose: Partial redemption due to buyback at investor request and interest payment
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures
- Notice reference: DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension for corporate action execution.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All dealing activity in ISIN INE651J07952 must cease as per the specified timeline

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 7, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **November 8, 2025**: Record date for partial redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The suspension affects a single debenture series of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited. This is a temporary, procedural suspension to facilitate corporate action processing.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard corporate action procedure. Trading members need to ensure their systems block trading in the specified ISIN during the suspension period.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Debenture holders on record date will receive their partial redemption proceeds and interest payment as scheduled. The suspension is protective in nature to ensure accurate beneficiary determination.
