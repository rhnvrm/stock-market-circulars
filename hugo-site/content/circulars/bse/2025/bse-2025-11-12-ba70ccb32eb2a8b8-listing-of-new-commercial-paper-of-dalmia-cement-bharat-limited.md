## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis, effective November 12, 2025. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with IDBI Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 2000 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,473.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730542, Scrip ID: DCL111125
- ISIN: INE755K14FP0
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FP0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 11, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal broader market impact. The listing provides Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited access to short-term funding through the commercial paper market for approximately 3 months. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial paper instruments.
