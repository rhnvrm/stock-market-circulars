## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 4,00,000 new equity shares of Jyot International Marketing Limited (Scrip Code: 542544) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange from December 15, 2025. The shares were issued at Rs. 40/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 30/-) and are subject to lock-in until August 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 30/-
- Issue price: Rs. 40/- per share
- Allotted to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Date of Allotment: October 21, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 3221501 to 3621500
- ISIN: INE043R01016
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Complete lock-in on all 4,00,000 shares until August 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until August 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 15, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine preferential allotment of 4 lakh shares representing capital raising activity by Jyot International Marketing Limited. The shares are subject to standard lock-in provisions and will trade alongside existing equity shares. The issue size is relatively small and targeted at Non-Promoter investors.
