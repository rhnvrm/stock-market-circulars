## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited filed on September 29, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited on September 29, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document hosting.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from October 06, 2025 (ends October 27, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to a single SME company's IPO process. It follows standard regulatory procedures for SME IPO filings under ICDR Regulations. The public comment period allows stakeholders to provide feedback before the final offer document is approved. Impact is limited to potential investors and parties interested in Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited's public offering on the SME platform.
