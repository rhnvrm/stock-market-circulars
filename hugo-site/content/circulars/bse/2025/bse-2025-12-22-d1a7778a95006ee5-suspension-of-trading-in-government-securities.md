## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Government Security SGBDEC2513 (Scrip Code: 800281, ISIN: IN0020170158) effective December 23, 2025, due to the security's upcoming maturity on its redemption date. This is a standard administrative action taken for maturing debt securities.

## Key Points

- Government Security SGBDEC2513 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 800281
- ISIN: IN0020170158
- Trading suspension effective from December 23, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-783/2025-2026
- Trading members advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational measure for maturing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in SGBDEC2513 from December 23, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be taken in this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: To be announced (imminent)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Affects only a single Government Security (Sovereign Gold Bond series) that is reaching maturity. This is a routine administrative action with minimal market disruption.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of SGBDEC2513 will receive redemption proceeds as per the bond's terms. No trading opportunity after December 22, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific security. Standard end-of-life process for debt instruments.
