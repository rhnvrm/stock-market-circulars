## Summary

BSE has listed 46,07,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Inter Globe Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511391) issued at a premium of Rs. 36.50/- (total issue price Rs. 46.50/-) on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from December 31, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 46,07,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 46.50/- (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 36.50/- premium)
- Issued to: Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Basis: Conversion of warrants
- Scrip Code: 511391
- ISIN: INE661M01016
- Trading start date: December 31, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 8957296 to 13564295
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Allotment Details

**First Allotment (October 16, 2025):**
- Shares: 19,95,000
- Distinctive Numbers: 8957296 to 10952295

**Second Allotment (November 6, 2025):**
- Shares: 26,12,000
- Distinctive Numbers: 10952296 to 13564295

## Lock-in Requirements

- 16,20,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 8957296 - 10577295): Locked until August 31, 2027
- 3,75,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 10577296 - 10952295): Locked until August 31, 2026
- 4,87,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 10952296 - 11439295): Locked until August 31, 2027
- 21,25,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 11439296 - 13564295): Locked until August 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **First Allotment Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Second Allotment Date:** November 6, 2025
- **Notice Date:** December 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 31, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (shorter period):** August 31, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (longer period):** August 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with limited market impact. The preferential issue increases Inter Globe Finance Ltd's equity capital through warrant conversion. Approximately 35% of the newly listed shares have a lock-in period until August 31, 2027, while the remaining 65% are locked until August 31, 2026. Trading members can trade these securities from December 31, 2025, subject to applicable lock-in restrictions.
