## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Hiren Wahen Buildtech Private Limited effective November 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing November 30, 2025 as the record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Company: Hiren Wahen Buildtech Private Limited
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE0S1Q07019)
- Series Code: HWBPL-19%-30-11-25-PVT (975302)
- Record Date: November 30, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Circular Reference: DR-766/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 28, 2025
- All transactions in these securities must cease before the no-dealings date
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **November 28, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **November 30, 2025**: Record date for debenture redemption

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited as this affects a private limited company's debt securities with likely restricted investor base. The suspension is temporary and procedural, related to the normal course debenture redemption process. Market-wide impact is minimal. Trading members holding or dealing in these specific debentures need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive to avoid regulatory issues.
