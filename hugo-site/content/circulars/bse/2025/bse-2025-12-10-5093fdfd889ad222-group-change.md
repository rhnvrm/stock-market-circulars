## Summary

BSE has announced a group change for Modi Naturals Ltd (Scrip Code: 519003), which will be moved from Group X to Group B effective December 11, 2025. This is a routine reclassification of the security's trading group.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Modi Naturals Ltd
- Scrip Code: 519003
- Current Group: X
- Proposed Group: B
- Effective Date: December 11, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251210-3
- Notice Date: December 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative reclassification of the security's trading group on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note the group change and update their systems accordingly before the effective date of December 11, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine group reclassification with minimal market impact. The change from Group X to Group B may affect the trading and settlement procedures applicable to the security. Trading members should ensure their systems reflect the new group classification from the effective date.
