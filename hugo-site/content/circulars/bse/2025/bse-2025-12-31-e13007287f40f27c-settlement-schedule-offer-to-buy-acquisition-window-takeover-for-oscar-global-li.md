## Summary

BSE announces the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED. The bidding window will operate from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026, with settlement number 2025138. Obligation download is scheduled for January 29, 2026, and final settlement on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025138
- Bid Start Date: January 2, 2026
- Bid End Date: January 15, 2026
- Obligation Download: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Market Type "Takeover" and settlement number 2025138 must be used in depository system
- Members must refer to Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for revised guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI guidelines for acquisition of shares through stock exchange mechanism as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Participants must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- Early pay-in process must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload EPN file
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- Use Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2025138 in all depository transactions

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: Bid start date
- **January 15, 2026**: Bid end date
- **January 29, 2026**: Obligation download
- **January 30, 2026**: Final settlement date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact limited to shareholders and trading members dealing with Oscar Global Limited during the specified takeover period. The 14-day bidding window provides adequate time for shareholders to participate in the acquisition offer. Members and custodians handling Oscar Global shares must ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements to avoid settlement failures. The settlement schedule follows standard takeover procedures with clear timelines for bid submission, obligation determination, and final settlement.
