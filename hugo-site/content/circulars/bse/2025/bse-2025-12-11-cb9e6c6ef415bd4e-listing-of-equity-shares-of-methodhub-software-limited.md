## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544637 and symbol METHODHUB with ISIN INE0QZR01017. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Methodhub Software Limited
- Scrip Code: 544637
- Trading Symbol: METHODHUB
- ISIN: INE0QZR01017
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date:** To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement for the SME segment. The impact is limited to investors and trading members interested in SME securities. The listing will add one more security to the BSE SME platform. Market-wide impact is minimal as this affects only those specifically tracking or trading SME stocks or participating in the IPO.
