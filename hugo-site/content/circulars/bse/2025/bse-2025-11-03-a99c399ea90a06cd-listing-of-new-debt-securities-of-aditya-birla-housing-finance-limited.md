## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited (ABHFL) issued on a private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 3, 2025. The issue consists of 20,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an 8.03% annual interest rate and maturing on April 23, 2035.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 976628
- **Scrip ID**: 803ABHFL35
- **ISIN**: INE831R08100 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- **Quantity**: 20,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,04,286.7595 (at premium)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore (face value)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA, ICRA AAA (highest safety rating)
- **Coupon Rate**: 8.03% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: April 23, 2035
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R08100
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: April 23, 2035
- **Notice Date**: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard private placement listing on the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and high net-worth investors with access to AAA-rated debt instruments from Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited offering 8.03% annual returns with a 10-year tenure.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for trading members beyond awareness of the new listing for potential client inquiries.
