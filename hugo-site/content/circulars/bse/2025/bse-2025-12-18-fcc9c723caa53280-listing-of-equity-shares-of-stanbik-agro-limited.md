## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of STANBIK AGRO LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544659, symbol STANBIK, and ISIN INE16QA01011. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- STANBIK AGRO LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544659
- Trading Symbol: STANBIK
- ISIN Number: INE16QA01011
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the forthcoming listing and await the separate notice containing the specific listing date and security details before commencing trading in these securities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing will provide a new trading opportunity for investors interested in the SME segment. Trading members should update their systems with the security identifiers (scrip code, symbol, and ISIN) in preparation for the listing. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile, issue size, and investor response, which will be clearer once the detailed listing notice is released.
