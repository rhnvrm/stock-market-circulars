## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited. The OFS provides existing shareholders an opportunity to sell their shares to the public through the exchange platform.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale (OFS) opened for Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited
- Transaction to be conducted through BSE platform
- Applicable to institutional and non-institutional investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants in the OFS must comply with SEBI OFS regulations
- Trading members to follow BSE guidelines for OFS transactions
- Investors to complete bidding process as per specified timelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- OFS opening date and bidding schedule details available in the complete circular

## Impact Assessment

This OFS opening allows existing shareholders of Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited to divest their holdings through a regulated exchange mechanism. The impact is specific to investors interested in the construction and infrastructure sector. The transaction provides price discovery and liquidity for the stock while allowing broader investor participation.
