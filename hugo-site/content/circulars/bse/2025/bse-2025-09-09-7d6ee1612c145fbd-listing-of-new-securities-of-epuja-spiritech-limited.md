## Summary

EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092) is listing 29,39,706 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2.40/- to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. Trading begins September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 29,39,706 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 3.40/- (Rs. 1/- face value + Rs. 2.40/- premium)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 78639334 to 81579039
- ISIN: INE807D01030

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2024
- **Trading Start Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The warrant conversion increases the company's equity base and provides additional capital. All new shares are subject to lock-in until March 31, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
