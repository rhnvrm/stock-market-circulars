## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code 977318) effective December 12, 2025. This follows a clarification from the company that the previously announced record date of December 11, 2025 is only for interest payment purposes and not for redemption. Trading had been suspended in anticipation of redemption but will now resume as normal.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code 977318) to resume from December 12, 2025
- Record date of December 11, 2025 is only for interest payment, not for redemption as initially indicated
- This notice supersedes and clarifies Exchange Notice No. 20251209-69 dated December 09, 2025
- Trading suspension was implemented as a precautionary measure for expected redemption
- Notice issued under reference DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a clarification notice correcting the purpose of a previously announced record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the resumption of trading in Scrip Code 977318
- Normal trading protocols to apply from December 12, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the availability of this scrip for trading

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for interest payment on debentures (Scrip Code 977318)
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading resumption date for the debentures
- **December 09, 2025**: Date of original notice (No. 20251209-69) that is now being clarified

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only a specific debenture issue of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED and represents an administrative correction rather than a material change in trading status.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the availability of Scrip Code 977318 for trading from December 12, 2025. The suspension period was brief and limited to this specific instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for debenture holders as liquidity will be restored. The clarification ensures that holders expecting to trade these instruments can do so without delay, while still receiving their scheduled interest payment.
