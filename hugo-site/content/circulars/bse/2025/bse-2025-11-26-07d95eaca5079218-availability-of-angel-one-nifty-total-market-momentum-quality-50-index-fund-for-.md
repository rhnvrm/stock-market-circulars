## Summary

BSE has announced that ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 27, 2025. The fund will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows a previous notice (20251031-37) regarding the fund.

## Key Points

- ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions
- Effective date: November 27, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan (both Growth options)
- Facilities enabled: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Regular Plan ISIN: INF1J2R01130
- Direct Plan ISIN: INF1J2R01122
- Scheme Category: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

None specified. This is an informational notice for trading members and investors regarding the availability of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription and redemption transactions
- Reference to prior notice: 20251031-37

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single mutual fund scheme becoming available for ongoing transactions. The impact is limited to:

- Investors interested in the ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND
- Trading members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- The fund tracks the Nifty Total Market Momentum Quality 50 Index, providing exposure to momentum and quality factors across the total market

The reopening provides investors with renewed access to this thematic index fund, but does not affect broader market operations or create compliance obligations for market participants.
