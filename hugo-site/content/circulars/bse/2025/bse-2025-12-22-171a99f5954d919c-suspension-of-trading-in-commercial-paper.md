## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers of 10 companies with record dates set between December 24-26, 2025, for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these instruments from the specified suspension dates.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers suspended for redemption
- 6 instruments have record date of December 24, 2025 with suspension from December 24, 2025
- 4 instruments have record date of December 26, 2025 with suspension from December 26, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251222-48
- Segment: Debt
- Category: Corporate Actions

## Affected Instruments

**Suspensions from December 24, 2025:**
- Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (ISIN: INE975F14B00, Code: 729571)
- Capri Global Capital Limited (ISIN: INE180C14524, Code: 729753)
- Sterlite Technologies Limited (ISIN: INE089C14DR2, Code: 730116)
- SBICAP Securities Limited (ISIN: INE212K14CN4, Code: 730321)
- Navi Finserv Limited (ISIN: INE342T14EL0, Code: 730361) - Record date: December 25, 2025
- Kalpataru Projects International Limited (ISIN: INE220B14BN8, Code: 730413)

**Suspensions from December 26, 2025:**
- Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (ISIN: INE101Q14223, Code: 729913)
- Aarti Industries Ltd (ISIN: INE769A14981, Code: 730341)
- Tata Steel Limited (ISIN: INE081A14GF1, Code: 730355)
- Reliance Industries Ltd (ISIN: INE002A14LO9, Code: 730583)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice implementing standard procedures for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the respective suspension dates
- All transactions must cease before the record dates to ensure proper redemption processing
- Members must update their systems to reflect trading suspensions

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Record date and suspension date for 5 CPs (6 including Navi Finserv with different record date)
- **December 25, 2025**: Record date for Navi Finserv Limited CP
- **December 26, 2025**: Record date and suspension date for 4 CPs
- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers. Routine procedure with limited broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - trading members must update systems and cease trading in 10 commercial papers across two suspension dates. Standard redemption process for maturing debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - holders of these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from suspension dates.
