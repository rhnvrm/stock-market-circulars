## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill 182TB22825 (ISIN: IN002024Y456) effective August 20, 2025, due to its maturity on the redemption date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 182TB22825 with ISIN IN002024Y456 will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension takes effect from August 20, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the effective date
- Notice issued under reference DR-698/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard maturity-based trading suspension process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from August 20, 2025
- All trading members required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 19, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** August 20, 2025
- **T-Bill Maturity:** On redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational impact limited to holders of the specific T-Bill. Standard maturity process with no broader market implications beyond the cessation of trading in this particular treasury bill.
