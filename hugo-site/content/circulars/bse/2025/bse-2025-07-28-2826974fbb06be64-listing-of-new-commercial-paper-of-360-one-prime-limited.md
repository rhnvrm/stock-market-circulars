## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new Commercial Papers worth Rs. 150 crore on BSE Debt segment through private placement. The papers carry face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 3-month maturity and top credit ratings.

## Key Points

- 3,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per paper
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 729908, ISIN: INE248U14RV8
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use specified tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings indicate low risk for investors.
