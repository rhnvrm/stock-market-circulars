## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of DIVINITI EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND schemes for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 03, 2025. Six plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) will be available for transactions including SIP and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- DIVINITI EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND reopening for ongoing transactions from December 03, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes fall under Equity category
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251107-65
- Transactions available through BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants and investors can resume transactions in the specified fund schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

- **Effective Date**: December 03, 2025 - Schemes reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a single mutual fund scheme's operational status. The reopening allows existing and new investors to resume transactions in DIVINITI EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND. The fund employs a long-short equity strategy, which may be relevant for investors seeking hedged equity exposure. No impact on broader market operations or trading activities.
