## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis, effective December 17, 2025. The first security (Scrip Code 977372) has a quantity of 50,000 units with a fixed interest rate of 6.98% p.a., while the second security (Scrip Code 977376) has 20,000 units with a floating interest rate. Both securities are rated CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Both securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis
- Date of allotment: December 15, 2025
- Effective listing date: December 17, 2025
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 100,000 per unit for both securities
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE, ICRA AAA/STABLE for both securities

## Security Details

### Security 1:
- Scrip Code: 977372 (NST-Rest Rate-14-12-35-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0NDH07068
- Quantity: 50,000 units
- Interest Rate: 6.98% p.a. (fixed)
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from March 31, 2026 to December 14, 2035
- Redemption Date: December 14, 2035
- Put/Call Option: NA

### Security 2:
- Scrip Code: 977376 (NSL-Reset Rate-13-12-35-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0NDH07076
- Quantity: 20,000 units
- Interest Rate: Floating
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from March 31, 2026 to December 13, 2035
- Redemption Date: December 13, 2035
- Put/Call Option: NA

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing affects only debt market participants and investors interested in AAA-rated corporate bonds. The securities are issued on private placement basis with relatively small quantities, indicating limited broad market participation. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
