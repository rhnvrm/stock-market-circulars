## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of four newly listed companies to its indices. Park Medi World Limited (544645) and Nephrocare Health Services Limited (544647) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective December 19, 2025. Shipwaves Online Limited (544646) and Unisem Agritech Limited (544648) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective December 18, 2025. All four companies were listed on BSE effective December 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Four companies being added to BSE indices following their recent listing
- Park Medi World Limited (Exchange ticker: 544645) - added to BSE IPO index
- Nephrocare Health Services Limited (Exchange ticker: 544647) - added to BSE IPO index
- Shipwaves Online Limited (Exchange ticker: 544646) - added to BSE SME IPO index on SME platform
- Unisem Agritech Limited (Exchange ticker: 544648) - added to BSE SME IPO index on SME platform
- All listings effective December 17, 2025
- Corporate actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity following new company listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- **December 18, 2025**: Effective date for addition of Shipwaves Online Limited and Unisem Agritech Limited to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **December 19, 2025**: Effective date for addition of Park Medi World Limited and Nephrocare Health Services Limited to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a standard index maintenance activity following IPO listings. The additions to BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices are automatic for newly listed companies and do not represent unexpected market events.

**Index Impact**: Minimal impact on index composition and performance given these are new entrants with likely smaller market capitalizations relative to existing index constituents.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for passive funds and investors tracking these specific indices who will need to adjust their portfolios to match the updated index composition.
