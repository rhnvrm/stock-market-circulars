## Summary

BSE has released the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for January 2026. The circular provides a detailed schedule covering 21 settlement cycles (NDS-184/25-26 through NDS-204/25-26) from January 1, 2026 to January 30, 2026, including corresponding DP settlement numbers, trading dates, 6A/7A entry and confirmation dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, auction dates, and auction pay-in/pay-out dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers period from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- Total of 21 settlement cycles scheduled (NDS-184/25-26 to NDS-204/25-26)
- Regular pay-in time: 11:00 AM (members must submit instructions by 10:50 AM)
- Auction pay-in time: 9:30 AM (members must submit instructions by 9:20 AM)
- Pay-in instructions to be submitted to Depositories/banks
- Trading follows T+1 settlement cycle for regular transactions
- Trading follows T+2 settlement cycle for auction transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit regular pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 10:50 AM for 11:00 AM pay-in
- Members must submit auction pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks latest by 9:20 AM for 9:30 AM auction pay-in
- Adherence to scheduled trading dates, 6A/7A entry and confirmation dates, and pay-in/pay-out dates is mandatory

## Important Dates

**Settlement Period:** January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026

**First Settlement:**
- Settlement No: NDS-184/25-26
- Trading Date: January 1, 2026
- Pay-in/Pay-out: January 2, 2026
- Auction Pay-in/Pay-out: January 5, 2026

**Last Settlement:**
- Settlement No: NDS-204/25-26
- Trading Date: January 30, 2026
- Pay-in/Pay-out: February 2, 2026
- Auction Pay-in/Pay-out: February 3, 2026

**Non-Trading Days (observed in schedule):** January 26, 2026 (Republic Day)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine operational schedule with no changes to existing settlement procedures or timelines.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard monthly calendar enabling NDS segment participants to plan their trading and settlement activities. The schedule maintains consistency with established T+1 settlement for regular trades and provides clear timelines for auction settlements.

**Participant Impact:** Informational only - NDS segment members and clearing members need to note the schedule for operational planning and ensure compliance with specified pay-in instruction submission deadlines.
