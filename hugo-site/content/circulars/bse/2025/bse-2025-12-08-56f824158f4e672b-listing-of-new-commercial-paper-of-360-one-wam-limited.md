## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Papers issued on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective December 8, 2025. The securities consist of 1,500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 75 crore. The Commercial Papers carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a maturity period of 3 months from allotment date.

## Key Points

- Total issuance: 1,500 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 75 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730704
- Scrip ID: OWL41225
- ISIN: INE466L14FL1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FL1
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Listing Date: December 8, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: December 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial papers on private placement basis with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to 360 ONE WAM LIMITED for a 3-month period. The dual A1+ credit ratings from CARE and ICRA indicate high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. No significant operational or regulatory implications for broader market participants.
