## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective December 31, 2025. The suspension is due to the redemption/maturity of these instruments on January 2, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal with these T-Bills from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Notice Number: 20251230-7
- Notice Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment
- Two T-Bills are subject to trading suspension
- Suspension effective date: December 31, 2025
- Redemption date: January 2, 2026
- DR Reference: DR-788/2025-2026

## Affected Securities

| Sr. No. | Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|---------|------------|------|-------------|
| 1 | 800354 | IN000126C010 | GS02JAN26C |
| 2 | 805124 | IN002025X273 | 91TB020126 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard procedural suspension applied to maturing T-Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-Bills from December 31, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their systems and inform clients about the suspension
- No new positions should be created in these instruments

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 31, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 2, 2026**: Redemption/maturity date for both T-Bills

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational notice affecting only two specific T-Bill instruments nearing maturity. Investors holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No action required from holders other than awareness of the trading suspension. This is a standard market practice for debt instruments approaching maturity to prevent settlement issues.
