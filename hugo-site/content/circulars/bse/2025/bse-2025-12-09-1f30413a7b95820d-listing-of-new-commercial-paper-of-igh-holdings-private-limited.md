## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crores. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with allotment date of December 9, 2025 and redemption scheduled for March 10, 2026. Axis Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 500 units of Commercial Paper
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,749 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 25 crores (approximately)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading to be conducted only in dematerialized form
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 729003
- Scrip ID: IGHP17325
- ISIN: INE02FN14440 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14440
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded through BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 9, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Allotment Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Tenure:** Approximately 3 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis with limited circulation (500 units). The instruments are designed for institutional investors and will have no impact on retail equity markets.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only for debt market participants and institutional investors seeking short-term debt instruments with high credit ratings (A1+). The commercial paper offers approximately 3-month investment horizon.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip code and ISIN details for trading purposes.
