## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SKF India (Industrial) Limited (Scrip Code: 544572) will be moved from their current group to the 'B' group effective December 19, 2025. This is a routine group classification change that affects the trading segment for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: SKF India (Industrial) Limited
- Scrip Code: 544572
- New Group Classification: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Friday, December 19, 2025
- Trading Members and market intermediaries have been notified

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be permitted to trade under the 'B' group classification starting December 19, 2025. The 'B' group typically represents companies with specific characteristics related to trading volumes, market capitalization, or other BSE-defined criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market intermediaries should:
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Ensure proper handling of trades in the 'B' group category
- Inform clients about the group change if necessary

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The move to 'B' group does not halt trading but changes the classification under which the stock is traded. Trading members should update their systems accordingly, but no significant operational disruption is expected. Investors can continue trading the stock under the new group classification from the effective date.
