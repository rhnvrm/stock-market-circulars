## Summary

DLF Cyber City Developers Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 100,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit. The bonds carry a credit rating of CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable, indicating highest safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977384
- Scrip ID: 698DLFC30
- ISIN: INE186K07148
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 1,000 crore (100,000 units)
- Interest Rate: 6.98% per annum
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/Stable
- Date of Allotment: December 17, 2025
- Maturity Date: June 17, 2030
- Call Option available as per Key Information Document
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE186K07148
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Any clarifications required should be directed to BSE debt department at phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 17, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 18, 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **December 17, 2026 to June 17, 2030**: Interest payment dates (annually)
- **June 17, 2030**: Date of Redemption

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors access to AAA-rated debt instruments from DLF Cyber City Developers Limited. The high credit rating indicates strong creditworthiness, and the 6.98% interest rate reflects prevailing market conditions for top-rated corporate bonds. The private placement nature means limited retail investor participation. No significant operational changes or market disruptions are expected from this listing.
