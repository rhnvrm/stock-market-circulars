## Summary

Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064) has applied for resignation of BSE membership. Trading members have 15 days to raise objections.

## Key Points

- Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064) applied for BSE membership resignation
- Application made as per BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Trading members can object within 15 days of notice date
- Clarifications available via membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine membership resignation process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit written objections within 15 days if any
- Objections must be sent to BSE Membership department
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 20, 2025
- Objection Deadline: September 3, 2025 (15 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine membership resignation. May slightly reduce trading member count but unlikely to affect market operations significantly.
