## Summary

R Systems International Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 12, 2025. The company issued 27,500 debentures of face value Rs. 1,00,000 each on private placement basis, totaling Rs. 275 crore. The securities carry a 9.75% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on November 11, 2030. The debentures are rated CRISIL AA-/Stable.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 27,500 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 275 crore
- Scrip Code: 977286
- Scrip ID: 975RSIL30
- ISIN: INE411H08011
- Interest Rate: 9.75% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 debenture
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE411H08011
- Members must use the tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Members requiring clarification should contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 11, 2025
- Listing Date: November 12, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly
- Maturity/Redemption Date: November 11, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine debt listing notification affecting only debt market participants interested in R Systems International Limited's fixed income securities. The listing expands trading options in the debt segment but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. The CRISIL AA-/Stable rating indicates strong creditworthiness, making these securities suitable for institutional investors seeking fixed income instruments with moderate credit risk.
