## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper worth ₹200 crore issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: ₹200 crore (4,000 units of ₹5 lakh each)
- Issue price: ₹4,92,171.50 per unit (face value ₹5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730096, ISIN: INE212K14BX5
- Trading symbol: SBICAPSL-25-11-25-CP
- Issuing and paying agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for SBICAP Securities and additional debt instrument for investors seeking 91-day maturity paper.
