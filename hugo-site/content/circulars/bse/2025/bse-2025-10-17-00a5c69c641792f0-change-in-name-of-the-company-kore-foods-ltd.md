## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Kore Foods Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Team24 Consumer Products Limited with effect from August 5, 2025. The Corporate Identification Number (CIN) L33208GA1983PLC000520 remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from KORE FOODS LIMITED to TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC on August 5, 2025
- CIN remains L33208GA1983PLC000520
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered address: H.No. 575/1C/G-1 Cujira, Santa Cruz, North Goa, Panjim, Santa Cruz, Tiswadi, North Goa-403005, Goa, India
- Certificate approved and digitally signed by Brijesh Kain, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action pursuant to change of company name under the Companies Act, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (KORE FOODS LIMITED) along with the new name (TEAM24 CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All legal obligations and continuity of the company remain intact

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: August 5, 2025
- **Two-Year Dual Name Display Period**: August 5, 2025 to August 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update its letterheads, signage, and corporate communications to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for the mandated two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on rights and liabilities. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders retain all existing rights. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in identification and record-keeping.
