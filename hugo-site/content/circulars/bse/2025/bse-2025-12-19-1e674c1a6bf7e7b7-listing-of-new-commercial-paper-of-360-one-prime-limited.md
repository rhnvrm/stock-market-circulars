## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE's Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with HDFC Bank Limited acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent. The Commercial Paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and has a tenure of 90 days.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: 360 ONE Prime Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 1000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Size**: Rs. 50 crores total
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,314 per unit
- **ISIN**: INE248U14SR4 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Scrip Code**: 730807
- **Scrip ID**: OPL171225
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U14SR4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade on BSE Debt segment only
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard Commercial Paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The listing provides liquidity options for investors in 360 ONE Prime Limited's commercial paper.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade this instrument on the BSE platform. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and high-net-worth investors with a 90-day investment option in a highly-rated commercial paper with a minimum investment of Rs. 5 lakhs.
