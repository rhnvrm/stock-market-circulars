## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Game Changers Texfab Limited (Scrip Code: 544594) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective November 19, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a transition to more flexible trading conditions.

## Key Points

- Company: Game Changers Texfab Limited (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544594
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, November 19, 2025
- Notice continuation from previous circular dated November 3, 2025 (Notice No. 20251103-44)

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will transition from MT Group (Trade for Trade segment) to M Group (Rolling segment). Trade for Trade segment requires mandatory delivery of shares and does not allow intraday trading, while Rolling segment allows standard T+1 settlement with intraday trading capabilities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the segment change for proper order routing
- All trades post November 19, 2025 will be executed under Rolling segment rules
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Important Dates

- **November 3, 2025**: Previous notice issued (Notice No. 20251103-44)
- **November 4, 2025**: Current circular issued
- **November 19, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This change is positive for market participants as it moves the stock from a restrictive trading environment to a more liquid one. The Rolling segment allows intraday trading and standard settlement cycles, which typically increases trading volumes and liquidity. This is a standard progression for SME IPO stocks after initial listing stabilization period in Trade for Trade segment.
