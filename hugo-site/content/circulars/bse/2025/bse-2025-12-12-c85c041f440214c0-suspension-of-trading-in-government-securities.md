## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Treasury Bill (T-bill) with ISIN IN001225C035 (Scrip Code: 800395) effective from December 15, 2025. The suspension is due to the T-bill's scheduled maturity on December 17, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with Scrip Code 800395 (ISIN: IN001225C035) to be suspended from trading
- Security identifier: GS17DEC25C
- Suspension effective from December 15, 2025
- Maturity date: December 17, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-777/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill from December 15, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 15, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **December 17, 2025**: T-bill redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension prior to maturity of a single government security. Trading members dealing in government securities should note the suspension timeline to avoid execution errors. The suspension allows for orderly settlement and redemption process. No impact on equity markets or corporate securities.
