## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd on private placement basis, effective December 5, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a 91-day maturity period ending March 4, 2026. The instrument carries an ICRA A1+ credit rating and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- 2,000 units of Commercial Paper issued at face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakh)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,351.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730714, Scrip ID: HSCL41225
- ISIN Number: INE019C14656
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE019C14656
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025 (effective today)
- **Redemption Date**: March 4, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing on private placement basis affecting only debt market participants. The instrument provides short-term funding avenue for Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on equity trading. Only BSE Debt segment members dealing in commercial papers need to note the new listing. The A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and lowest credit risk for short-term obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for debt market investors and institutions dealing in money market instruments. Not applicable to retail equity investors.
