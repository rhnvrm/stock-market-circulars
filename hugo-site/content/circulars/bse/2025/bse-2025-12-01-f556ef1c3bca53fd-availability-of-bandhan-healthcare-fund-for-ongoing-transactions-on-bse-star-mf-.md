## Summary

BSE has notified that BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 02, 2025. This follows an earlier suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251107-23. Six plan variants covering regular and direct plans with different dividend options are available for trading.

## Key Points

- BANDHAN HEALTHCARE FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 02, 2025
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available: 3 Regular Plans and 3 Direct Plans
- All variants are equity category funds
- Plan options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) with Payout and Reinvestment options
- References previous Notice No. 20251107-23 which likely suspended transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification about the resumption of trading and transaction facilities for a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and market participants to note the availability of the fund for transactions from December 02, 2025
- Standard BSE StAR MF Platform procedures to be followed for processing subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction requests

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 01, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 02, 2025 - Resumption of ongoing transactions, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational matter affecting only a specific mutual fund scheme. The reopening of the fund allows existing and new investors to resume transactions.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in healthcare sector equity funds through Bandhan Mutual Fund, as they can now make fresh investments, redemptions, and set up systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal plans.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard mutual fund transaction processing will resume for this scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform from December 02, 2025.
