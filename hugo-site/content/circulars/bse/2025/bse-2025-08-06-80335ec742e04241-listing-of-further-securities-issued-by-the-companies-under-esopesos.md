## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These new securities will be available for trading starting Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 3,587,756 new shares across all companies
- Securities will be listed and admitted for trading on August 7, 2025
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share
- All securities are marked as having no lock-in period (NA)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of employee stock option securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability
- Companies must ensure proper disclosure and compliance with ESOP regulations
- Standard listing and trading procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Addition of 3.58 million shares across multiple companies may affect individual stock liquidity and market capitalization. Notable issuances include HDFC Bank (1.8M shares) and ICICI Bank (730K shares).

**Operational Impact**: Low - Routine corporate action with standard trading procedures.
