## Summary

BSE has notified that KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD will undertake part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE094I07080, Code: 977231). The record date is December 26, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 98,974 per debenture effective from the same date under settlement number DR-785/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD
- Debenture Code: KPDL-15-10-35-PVT (977231)
- ISIN: INE094I07080
- Record Date: December 26, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 98,974 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-785/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt Market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for part redemption of debentures as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value effective December 26, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 98,974 per debenture
- Ensure proper settlement processing under DR-785/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** December 26, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-785/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders:** Holders of these specific debentures as of the record date will receive part redemption proceeds. Post-redemption, the face value reduces to Rs. 98,974 per debenture.

**Trading Impact:** Trading members need to adjust their systems for the reduced face value. This affects pricing, valuation, and settlement processes for these specific debentures.

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders and traders of this specific debt instrument. No broader market implications expected.
