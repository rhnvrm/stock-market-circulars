## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) to the 'Z' group effective January 09, 2026, due to non-compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 - specifically Regulations 17(1) and 18(1) regarding composition of Board of Directors and Committees - for two consecutive quarters (June 2025 and September 2025). The company has until January 06, 2026 to rectify the non-compliance and avoid the transfer. Additionally, Amit International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531300) remains in Z group for similar non-compliance with Regulation 18(1).

## Key Points

- GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) will be transferred to 'Z' group effective January 09, 2026
- Non-compliance relates to Regulations 17(1) and 18(1) of SEBI LODR 2015
- Non-compliance period: Two consecutive quarters (June 2025 & September 2025)
- Last date to comply and avoid transfer: January 06, 2026
- Amit International Ltd (531300) already in Z group for Regulation 18(1) non-compliance for same quarters
- All trades in Z/ZP/ZY group securities will settle on Trade for Trade basis
- Action based on SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This action implements existing penal provisions under SEBI Master Circular dated November 11, 2024 for non-compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

**For GV Films Ltd:**
- Must comply with Regulation 17(1) - Composition of Board of Directors
- Must comply with Regulation 18(1) - Composition of Audit Committee and other Committees
- Deadline to rectify: January 06, 2026 (to avoid Z group transfer)

**For Market Participants:**
- All trades in Z/ZP/ZY group scrips must be settled on Trade for Trade basis
- No intraday trading or netting allowed for these securities

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Notice issued
- **January 06, 2026**: Last date for GV Films Ltd to comply and avoid Z group transfer
- **January 09, 2026**: Effective date of transfer to Z group (if non-compliance continues)
- **Non-compliance period**: June 2025 and September 2025 quarters

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Transfer to Z group severely restricts trading liquidity as all trades become Trade for Trade (no intraday positions)
- Increased settlement risk and reduced speculative activity
- Likely to cause price volatility and reduced investor interest

**Corporate Governance:**
- Indicates serious governance deficiencies in Board/Committee composition
- Two consecutive quarters of non-compliance suggests systemic issues
- Regulatory pressure increases significantly in Z group

**Investor Impact:**
- Existing shareholders face reduced liquidity and potential difficulty in exiting positions
- Higher transaction costs due to Trade for Trade settlement
- Reputational damage may lead to further price decline

**Market Sentiment:**
- Z group classification serves as warning to other listed entities about compliance importance
- Demonstrates exchange's enforcement of SEBI LODR requirements
