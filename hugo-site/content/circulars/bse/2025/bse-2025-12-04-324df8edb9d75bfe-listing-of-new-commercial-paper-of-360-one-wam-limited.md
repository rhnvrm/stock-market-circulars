## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE WAM Limited on private placement basis, effective December 4, 2025. The instrument consists of 100 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 5 Crore), rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a tenure from December 3, 2025 to June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 5 Crore
- Scrip Code: 730694
- Scrip ID: OWL31225
- ISIN: INE466L14FK3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,396 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE466L14FK3
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise to be observed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** June 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine debt instrument listing affecting only investors in 360 ONE WAM Limited's commercial paper. The short-term instrument (6-month tenure) provides working capital financing for the issuer and investment opportunity for debt market participants. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
