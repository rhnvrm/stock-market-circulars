## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 776 dated December 15, 2025. This circular lists securities and quantities available for auction to meet delivery obligations. The auction covers approximately 109 different scrips across various sectors including mutual funds, infrastructure, pharmaceuticals, banking, manufacturing, and other industries.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 776
- Date: December 15, 2025
- Total scrips listed: Approximately 109 different securities
- Quantities range from 1 share to 25,695 shares depending on the scrip
- Covers diverse sectors: mutual funds, banking, manufacturing, pharma, infrastructure, metals, energy
- Notable quantities: Rajnish Wellness (25,695 shares), Harshil Agro (6,000 shares), New Light Infraprojects (5,000 shares), RO Jewels (4,898 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations in settlement 776 must participate in the auction or arrange delivery
- Members must ensure settlement of securities as per exchange regulations
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 15, 2025
- Settlement Number: 776

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine settlement auction affecting only specific trading members with delivery failures. The auction provides a mechanism to close out unsettled positions. Individual investors holding these securities are not directly affected unless they have pending delivery obligations through their brokers in this specific settlement cycle.
