## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed two new commercial paper instruments on the BSE Debt segment effective November 12, 2025. Both securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, allotted on November 11, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+ credit ratings, with ICICI Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper instruments listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 500 units per instrument (Rs. 25 crore each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+
- Issuing and Paying agent: ICICI Bank Ltd
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Instrument Details

### Instrument 1 (Scrip Code: 730537)
- Scrip ID: ECLF111125
- ISIN: INE804I14YF2
- Issue price: Rs. 478,348
- Date of allotment: November 11, 2025
- Redemption date: May 21, 2026
- Tenure: ~6 months

### Instrument 2 (Scrip Code: 730538)
- Scrip ID: ECL111125
- ISIN: INE804I14YG0
- Issue price: Rs. 469,393
- Date of allotment: November 11, 2025
- Redemption date: August 10, 2026
- Tenure: ~9 months

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard commercial paper listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption date (Instrument 1)**: May 21, 2026
- **Redemption date (Instrument 2)**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and expands the debt market offerings. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk. Trading members can contact the BSE debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
