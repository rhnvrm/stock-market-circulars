## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Ironwood Education Limited. The OFS mechanism allows existing shareholders to sell their shares to institutional and retail investors through the exchange platform.

## Key Points

- Company: Ironwood Education Limited
- Mechanism: Offer for Sale (OFS)
- Exchange: BSE
- The OFS facility enables promoters/shareholders to divest stake through exchange mechanism
- Separate bidding windows for institutional and retail investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard OFS announcement following SEBI guidelines for share divestment through stock exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to ensure compliance with OFS bidding procedures
- Investors must follow prescribed bidding timelines and price bands
- Settlement as per standard OFS settlement cycle

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 3, 2025
- OFS bidding dates and timelines to be followed as per the detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact as this provides an opportunity for investors to acquire shares in Ironwood Education Limited through the OFS route. The OFS mechanism ensures price discovery and orderly divestment of shares by existing shareholders while providing investment opportunities to institutional and retail participants.
