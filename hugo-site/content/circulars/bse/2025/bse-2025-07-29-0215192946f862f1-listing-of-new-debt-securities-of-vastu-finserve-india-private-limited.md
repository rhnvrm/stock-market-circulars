## Summary

BSE has listed three new debt securities of Vastu Finserve India Private Limited on its debt segment effective July 29, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each and carry an interest rate of 9.25% per annum with quarterly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Three debt securities listed: VFIPL-9.25%-28-7-28-PVT, VFIPL-9.25%-28-7-27-PVT, and VFIPL-9.25%-28-1-28-PVT
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest rate: 9.25% per annum, paid quarterly
- Credit rating: ICRA AA-/Stable
- Date of allotment: July 28, 2025
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Placement memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: July 29, 2025
- Date of allotment: July 28, 2025
- First interest payment: October 28, 2025
- Redemption dates: January 28, 2028; July 28, 2027; July 28, 2028 (respective securities)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities provide institutional investors with AA-rated debt instruments from Vastu Finserve India Private Limited with competitive interest rates.
