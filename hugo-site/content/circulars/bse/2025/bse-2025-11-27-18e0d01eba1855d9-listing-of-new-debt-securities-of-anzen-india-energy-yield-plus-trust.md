## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Anzen India Energy Yield Plus Trust issued on a private placement basis, effective November 27, 2025. The securities carry a 7.39% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on November 25, 2036. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/Stable and IND AAA/Stable.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 77,500 units
- **Scrip Code**: 977319
- **Scrip ID**: 739AIEY36
- **ISIN**: INE0MIZ07046
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000.00
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000.00
- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA/Stable, IND AAA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 7.39% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from December 31, 2025 to November 25, 2036
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: NA

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0MIZ07046
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Clarifications can be sought from the debt department at contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 25, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for debt securities of an InvIT (Infrastructure Investment Trust) on a private placement basis. The impact is limited to investors in this specific instrument. The high credit rating (AAA/Stable from both CRISIL and IND) indicates strong creditworthiness. No broader market or operational impact anticipated.
