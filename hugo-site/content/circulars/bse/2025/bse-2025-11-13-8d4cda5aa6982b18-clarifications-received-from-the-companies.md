## Summary

Tejas Networks Limited submitted a clarification to NSE and BSE on November 13, 2025, in response to exchange queries dated November 12-13, 2025 regarding unusual volume activity in its scrip. The company confirmed it has been complying with Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations and stated there is no undisclosed information or impending announcement that may have a bearing on price or volume behavior in its securities.

## Key Points

- Company responded to queries from both NSE (November 12, 2025) and BSE (November 13, 2025)
- Clarification relates to spurt in trading volume observed in the company's scrip
- Company confirms full compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No undisclosed material information or impending announcements that could affect stock price or volume
- Trading symbols: NSE - TEJASNET, BSE - 540595
- Signed by Anantha Murthy N, Company Secretary & Compliance Officer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The circular confirms the company's ongoing compliance with existing SEBI Listing Regulation 30 requirements regarding timely disclosure of material information.

## Important Dates

- November 12, 2025: NSE query received
- November 13, 2025: BSE query received and company's clarification submitted

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine clarification statement issued in response to unusual trading activity. The company has confirmed no material undisclosed information exists.

**Investor Impact:** Low - Provides reassurance to investors that the volume spurt was not driven by undisclosed material information and that the company remains compliant with disclosure obligations.

**Operational Impact:** None - No operational changes or actions required. Standard disclosure practice when exchanges seek clarification on unusual trading patterns.
