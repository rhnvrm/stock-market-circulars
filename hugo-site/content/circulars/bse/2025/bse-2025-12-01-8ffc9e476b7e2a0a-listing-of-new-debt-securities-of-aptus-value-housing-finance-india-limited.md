## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities (Non-Convertible Debentures) of Aptus Value Housing Finance India Limited worth Rs. 150 crore (15,000 units of Rs. 1 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective December 1, 2025. These are monthly interest-paying NCDs with a tenure of approximately 5 years carrying a coupon rate of 7.95% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 units (face value Rs. 1,00,000 each)
- Total issue size: Rs. 150 crore
- Scrip Code: 977324
- Scrip ID: 795AVHFL30
- ISIN: INE852O07188
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 7.95% per annum (paid monthly)
- Allotment Date: November 27, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2030
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE852O07188
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (27th of each month)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 27, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing of debt securities with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Standard debt segment trading operations apply. The securities are available for trading by BSE members in the debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investment opportunity in CARE AA/Stable rated debt securities of a housing finance company with monthly interest payout for debt market participants.
