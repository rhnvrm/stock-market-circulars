## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific debenture series of Shriram Finance Ltd (scrip code: SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT, ISIN: INE721A07RF3, code: 974376) effective November 7, 2025. The suspension follows the company's notification that these debentures have been repurchased and extinguished. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Shriram Finance Ltd has repurchased and extinguished debentures with ISIN INE721A07RF3
- Trading suspension effective from November 7, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-751/2025-2026
- Debenture series: SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT (Scrip Code: 974376)
- All trading members must cease dealing in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving debenture repurchase and extinguishment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No fresh positions or dealings permitted in debenture ISIN INE721A07RF3

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of this particular debenture series. The repurchase and extinguishment represent normal debt redemption activity.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified debenture. Existing debenture holders have had their securities repurchased by the company.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders of this series have exited their positions through the company's repurchase program. No ongoing trading or liquidity concerns as the securities have been extinguished.
