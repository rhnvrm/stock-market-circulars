## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module of its Internet Based Book Building Software (IBBS) platform for Infibeam Avenues Limited to facilitate the first and final call on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 69,99,85,723 partly paid-up equity shares are required to pay Rs. 5 per share during the issue period from November 25, 2025 to December 09, 2025.

## Key Points

- First and final call money notice for Infibeam Avenues Limited partly paid-up equity shares
- Total partly paid-up shares affected: 69,99,85,723 shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 5 per partly paid-up equity share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Registrar: Alankit Assignments Limited (SEBI Reg: INR000001385)
- Call Money Module available to Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts
- Process follows Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements existing framework under:
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money module operations

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Submit details of call money notices collected through the Call Money Module on IBBS platform
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can access the module
- Process call money payments during bidding hours (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM)

**For Shareholders:**
- Pay Rs. 5 per partly paid-up equity share held
- Submit payment through authorized channels during the issue period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 18, 2025
- **Issue Opening Date:** Tuesday, November 25, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, December 09, 2025
- **Issue Period:** 15 days
- **Bidding Hours:** 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily during issue period

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects specific shareholder base holding partly paid-up shares of Infibeam Avenues Limited

**Operational Impact:** Trading Members and SCSBs need to ensure their systems are configured to access the Call Money Module and process submissions during the 15-day window

**Shareholder Impact:** Shareholders holding partly paid-up equity shares must arrange payment of Rs. 5 per share to fully pay up their holdings; failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as per company's terms
