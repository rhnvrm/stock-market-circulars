## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729888, ISIN: INE700G14PE7) effective October 17, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on the redemption date of October 20, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Ltd to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 729888
- ISIN: INE700G14PE7
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Reference: DR-738/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE700G14PE7) from October 17, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the suspension
- No transactions should be executed in this instrument after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 20, 2025**: Redemption date (maturity)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity affecting a single Commercial Paper instrument. Standard market procedure with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to prevent trades in the specified instrument. Only holders of this specific Commercial Paper are affected for redemption processing.
