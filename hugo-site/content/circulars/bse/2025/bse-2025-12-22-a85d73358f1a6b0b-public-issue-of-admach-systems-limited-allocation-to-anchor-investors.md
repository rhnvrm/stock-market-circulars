## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED. This announcement is part of the standard IPO process where shares are allocated to anchor investors prior to the issue opening for general public subscription.

## Key Points

- Public issue relates to ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Allocation made to anchor investors as per SEBI guidelines
- Anchor investor allocation occurs one day prior to issue opening
- Forms part of the book building process for the IPO

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard notification as per existing IPO procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED must comply with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Anchor investor allocation must follow prescribed guidelines
- Proper disclosure of allocation details to market participants

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 22, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed prior to issue opening
- Specific issue dates to be referred from the prospectus

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact as it provides transparency about the anchor investor allocation process for ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED's public issue. Anchor investor participation often indicates institutional interest in the IPO and can influence retail investor sentiment. The allocation details are important for market participants evaluating the IPO subscription and demand dynamics.
