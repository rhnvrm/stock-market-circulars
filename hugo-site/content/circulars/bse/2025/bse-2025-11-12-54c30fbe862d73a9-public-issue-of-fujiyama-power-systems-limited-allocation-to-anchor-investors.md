## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited. This allocation marks an important step in the company's IPO process, where institutional investors commit to purchasing shares at a determined price before the issue opens to the general public.

## Key Points

- Allocation to anchor investors has been completed for Fujiyama Power Systems Limited's public issue
- Anchor investor allocation typically occurs one day before the IPO opens to retail and other investors
- This process helps in price discovery and demonstrates institutional confidence in the issue
- The allocation details include the number of shares allocated and the price at which they were allocated

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard procedural notification as per SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- The issuer company must disclose anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI regulations
- Market participants should be aware of the anchor allocation while participating in the public issue
- The allocation information is made available to ensure transparency in the IPO process

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 12, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation has been completed as per the circular
- The public issue opening date would typically follow within one trading day
