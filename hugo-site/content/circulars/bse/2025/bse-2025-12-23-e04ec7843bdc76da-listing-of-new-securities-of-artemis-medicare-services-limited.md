## Summary

BSE has listed 1,89,62,247 equity shares of Artemis Medicare Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542919) issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs). The shares will be available for trading from December 24, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Number of shares listed: 1,89,62,247 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 174.03/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 173.03/- premium)
- Allotment basis: Preferential issue to Non-Promoters
- Purpose: Conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs)
- Distinctive numbers: 139238251 to 158200497
- ISIN: INE025R01021
- Scrip code: 542919
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance action required from members.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: November 15, 2025
- Trading commencement date: December 24, 2025
- Notice date: December 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on market operations. This is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares following CCD conversion. The shares increase the company's equity base by approximately 18.96 million shares. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital for Artemis Medicare Services Limited.
