## Summary

BSE has issued a circular detailing corporate actions affecting Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) positions for three companies. The circular specifies ex-dates, book closure dates, record dates, foreclosure dates, and shut periods for participants with SLB positions in Hindustan Unilever Ltd. (spin-off), Patel Engineering Ltd. (rights issue), and Engineers India Ltd. (dividend).

## Key Points

- Hindustan Unilever Ltd. undergoing spin-off with ex-date and book closure on December 5, 2025
- Patel Engineering Ltd. rights issue of equity shares with ex-date and record date on December 4, 2025
- Engineers India Ltd. dividend with ex-date and record date on December 4, 2025
- SLB positions will be foreclosed on respective dates to facilitate corporate actions
- Shut periods specified for each corporate action to prevent new SLB transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular provides operational guidance for handling existing SLB positions during corporate actions in accordance with established SLB framework guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with SLB positions in the affected securities must settle or rollover positions before foreclosure dates
- No new SLB transactions permitted during shut periods
- Lenders and borrowers must ensure compliance with foreclosure timelines
- Participants must adjust their SLB positions to account for corporate action entitlements

## Important Dates

**Hindustan Unilever Ltd. (Spin Off)**
- Shut Period: December 4-5, 2025
- Ex-Date: December 5, 2025
- Book Closure/Record Date: December 5, 2025
- Foreclosure Date: December 5, 2025

**Patel Engineering Ltd. (Rights Issue)**
- Shut Period: December 3-4, 2025
- Ex-Date: December 4, 2025
- Record Date: December 4, 2025
- Foreclosure Date: December 4, 2025

**Engineers India Ltd. (Dividend)**
- Shut Period: December 3-4, 2025
- Ex-Date: December 4, 2025
- Record Date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - affects liquidity in SLB market for three securities, particularly Hindustan Unilever which is a large-cap stock with significant SLB activity.

**Operational Impact:** Participants with open SLB positions must take immediate action to close or adjust positions before foreclosure dates. The spin-off action for Hindustan Unilever requires careful handling of entitlements and position adjustments.

**Trading Impact:** New SLB transactions in these securities will be restricted during shut periods. Short sellers using SLB mechanism must cover positions or face foreclosure.

**Investor Impact:** Rights issue participants in Patel Engineering must ensure their shareholding is not encumbered by SLB borrowing to receive entitlements. Dividend eligibility for Engineers India affected by record date positioning.
