## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Piramal Finance Limited (formerly Piramal Capital & Housing Finance Limited) with Scrip Code 544597 will be permitted to trade under Group 'B' effective Friday, November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Piramal Finance Limited (Formerly: Piramal Capital & Housing Finance Limited)
- Scrip Code: 544597
- New Trading Group: 'B'
- Effective Date: Friday, November 21, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251107-22
- Category: Company related - Equity Segment

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current trading group to Group 'B'. Group 'B' securities typically have different settlement and trading parameters compared to Group 'A' securities on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Take note of the group change for the scrip
- Update their systems to reflect the new trading group designation
- Ensure compliance with Group 'B' trading norms from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 21, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This administrative change may affect:
- Trading parameters and settlement cycles for the scrip
- Margin requirements for trading members
- Liquidity characteristics of the security
- Risk management processes for brokers and investors

The change from the current group to Group 'B' is a routine administrative action by the exchange.
