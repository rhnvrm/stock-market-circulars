## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Avanse Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, totaling Rs. 75 crore. The commercial paper carries a CRISIL A1+ rating and will be traded only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total Quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crore)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,85,658 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730805
- Scrip ID: AFSL171225
- ISIN: INE087P14770
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE087P14770
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to Avanse Financial Services Limited with a 177-day maturity period. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings but has no direct impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
