## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the resumption of subscription facility in Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund. This allows investors to once again subscribe to units of this fund of fund scheme.

## Key Points

- Subscription facility for Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund has been resumed
- Investors can now subscribe to units of this fund of fund scheme
- The fund invests in Tata Silver ETF
- This is an operational update for market participants and investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants and AMCs should note the resumption of subscriptions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it affects only investors interested in the Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund product. The resumption of subscriptions provides investors with renewed access to this investment vehicle for gaining exposure to silver through an ETF fund of fund structure.
