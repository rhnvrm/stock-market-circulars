## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Nexus Select Trust worth Rs. 20 crores (4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are effective for trading from August 11, 2025, in the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- 4,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,764 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: IND A1+ 
- ISIN: INE0NDH14072
- Scrip Code: 730001
- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Maturity date: November 7, 2025 (91 days)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Trading Effective**: August 11, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine short-term commercial paper issuance by Nexus Select Trust for working capital requirements. The 91-day tenor and A1+ rating indicate standard commercial paper characteristics with low credit risk.
