## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 560 crore issued by Mindspace Business Parks REIT on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, total 11,200 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,85,965 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730079, ISIN: INE0CCU14112
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a REIT. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. The short-term nature (6-month tenure) makes it suitable for money market investors seeking quality short-term instruments.
