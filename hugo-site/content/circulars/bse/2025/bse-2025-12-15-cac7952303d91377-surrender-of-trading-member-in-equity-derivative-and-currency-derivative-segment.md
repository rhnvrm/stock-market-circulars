## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership by Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251) in the Equity Derivative and Currency derivative segments, effective December 15, 2025. This is an administrative action where the member has voluntarily surrendered its trading rights in these specific segments.

## Key Points

- Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3251) has surrendered trading membership
- Surrender applies to Equity Derivative segment
- Surrender applies to Currency derivative segment
- Effective date: December 15, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action resulting from a member's voluntary decision to surrender trading rights.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this membership change. No specific compliance actions required from other members. For any clarifications, members may contact the Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date of surrender of trading membership for Ratnakar Securities Pvt. Ltd. in Equity Derivative and Currency derivative segments

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving the voluntary surrender of trading membership by a single member in specific derivative segments. Other market participants are unaffected beyond being informed of the membership status change.
