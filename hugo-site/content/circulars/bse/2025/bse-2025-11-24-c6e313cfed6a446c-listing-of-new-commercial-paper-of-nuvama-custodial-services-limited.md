## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Nuvama Custodial Services Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 2,000 units of commercial paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore. The securities are available for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Nuvama Custodial Services Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Scrip Code: 730635
- Scrip ID: NCS211125
- ISIN: INE0H3Q14014
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,64,705.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0H3Q14014
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 21, 2025
- Listing Date: November 24, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 21, 2026
- Tenure: Approximately 11 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt financing for Nuvama Custodial Services Limited and offers institutional investors an ICRA A1+ rated instrument with an 11-month maturity. The trading is restricted to the debt segment with no impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
