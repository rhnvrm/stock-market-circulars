## Summary

Northern Arc Capital Ltd has listed two new commercial paper series on BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. Series 1 consists of 100 securities with ISIN INE850M14CI7 (Scrip Code 730787) redeeming on March 16, 2026. Series 2 consists of 500 securities with ISIN INE850M14CJ5 (Scrip Code 730788) redeeming on March 17, 2026. Both series carry CARE A1+ rating with HDFC Bank Limited as issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper series listed on private placement basis
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security for both series
- Series 1 (NACL-16-3-26-CP): 100 securities, issue price Rs. 490,268.50, allotment date December 16, 2025
- Series 2 (NAC-17-3-26-CP): 500 securities, issue price Rs. 490,222.50, allotment date December 16, 2025
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ for both series
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date (Both Series):** December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1 - NACL-16-3-26-CP):** March 16, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2 - NAC-17-3-26-CP):** March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting institutional investors and trading members dealing in short-term debt instruments. The securities have a tenor of approximately 90 days and are rated CARE A1+, indicating adequate safety for short-term investments. The listing provides additional short-term funding options for Northern Arc Capital Ltd and investment opportunities for debt market participants.
