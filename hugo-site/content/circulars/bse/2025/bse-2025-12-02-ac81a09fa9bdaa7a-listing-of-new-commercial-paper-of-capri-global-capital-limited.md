## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 2, 2025. The instrument comprises 1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with a 6-month tenure maturing on June 2, 2026. YES Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730683
- Scrip ID: CGCL021225
- ISIN: INE180C14623
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,81,512.50 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14623
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 2, 2026
- **Tenure**: 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing in the BSE Debt segment. The instrument provides short-term funding for Capri Global Capital Limited and offers CRISIL A1+ rated investment opportunity for institutional investors. Trading is restricted to debt segment participants with no impact on equity markets or retail investors.
