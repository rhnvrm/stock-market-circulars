## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Tata Motors Limited (formerly known as TML Commercial Vehicles Limited) with Scrip Code 544569 will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Wednesday, November 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Tata Motors Limited (formerly TML Commercial Vehicles Limited)
- Scrip Code: 544569
- New Group Classification: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Wednesday, November 26, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251112-15
- Notice Date: November 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition to Group 'B' classification on BSE. Group B typically includes companies that meet certain listing requirements and trading criteria as per BSE's grouping norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the change in group classification from the effective date
- Update their systems to reflect the new group designation
- Ensure proper risk management parameters are applied for Group B securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 26, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This group change may affect:
- Trading margins and collateral requirements
- Risk categorization for brokers and investors
- Surveillance and monitoring parameters
- Clearing and settlement processes

The reclassification to Group B indicates the stock meets BSE's criteria for this classification, which may impact trading volumes and investor participation patterns.
