## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The privately placed securities consist of 26,000 units with a face value of ₹1,00,000 each, carrying an annual interest rate of 8.95% and maturing on June 4, 2027. The securities are rated ICRA AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 976768
- **Scrip ID**: 895OPL27
- **ISIN**: INE248U07FW5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Quantity**: 26,000 units
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,04,209.79
- **Total Issue Size**: ₹26 crore (approximately)
- **Interest Rate**: 8.95% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07FW5
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from June 6, 2026 to June 4, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: June 4, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing that expands 360 ONE Prime Limited's debt portfolio. The securities are privately placed and will have limited impact on broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for debt market investors. The ICRA AA/Stable rating indicates high credit quality, making these securities suitable for investors seeking relatively secure fixed-income investments with competitive yields.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Only debt market trading members and investors interested in corporate bonds are affected.
