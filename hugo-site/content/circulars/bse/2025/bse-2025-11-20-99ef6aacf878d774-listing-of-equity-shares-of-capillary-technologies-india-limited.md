## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544614, trading symbol CAPILLARY, and ISIN number INE0ILV01024. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Capillary Technologies India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544614
- Trading Symbol: CAPILLARY
- ISIN Number: INE0ILV01024
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await further communication regarding the listing date and complete security details before commencing trading in these shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 20, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will provide investors access to trade equity shares of Capillary Technologies India Limited on BSE. The actual market impact will be determined once the listing date and price band are announced. Trading members should prepare their systems to accommodate the new scrip once the final listing details are communicated.
