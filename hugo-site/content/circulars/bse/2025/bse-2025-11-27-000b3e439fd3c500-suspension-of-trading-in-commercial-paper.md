## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in 14 commercial papers issued by various companies including IGH Holdings Private Limited, L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited, Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, Standard Chartered Capital Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, HDFC Securities Limited, Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, and Sharekhan Limited. The suspensions are effective from the respective record dates set for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- 14 commercial papers to be suspended from trading
- Record dates range from December 2, 2025 to December 8, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from respective record dates
- All suspensions are for redemption purposes
- Notice number: 20251127-9
- Multiple financial institutions and holding companies affected

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the designated "No Dealings From" dates
- Trading members are required to adhere to the suspension dates for each listed security
- Record dates must be observed for redemption processing

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: Record date and trading suspension for IHPL-3-12-25-CP (INE02FN14630) and LTMR-3-12-25-CP (INE128M14AT9)
- **December 3, 2025**: Record date and trading suspension for IGHHPL-04-12-25-CP (INE02FN14622) and BGHPL-04-12-25-CP (INE09OL14HU4)
- **December 5, 2025**: Record date and trading suspension for BGHPL-08-12-25-CP (INE09OL14HV2)
- **December 8, 2025**: Record date and trading suspension for 9 commercial papers from various issuers including Standard Chartered Capital, IGH Holdings, Nuvama Wealth, Bajaj entities, HDFC Securities, Aditya Birla Housing Finance, and Sharekhan

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to debt market participants dealing in commercial papers. The suspensions affect short-term debt instruments from major financial institutions and holding companies. No impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in the specified securities from the respective suspension dates. Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the scheduled maturity dates.

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal, as commercial papers are typically held to maturity and the suspensions align with scheduled redemption dates.
