## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in five series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED, effective December 15, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing December 15, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Five NCD series of SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED will be suspended from trading
- Record date fixed: December 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from December 15, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-777/2025-2026
- All affected debentures have maturity date of December 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for suspension of trading due to scheduled debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED from December 15, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction
- All five series of NCDs listed above are subject to this restriction

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity Date**: December 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting only specific debt securities of SAMMAAN CAPITAL LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to the scheduled redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects trading members dealing in these specific NCDs. The suspension is standard procedure for securities approaching redemption with a fixed record date.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of these specific debentures who will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the record date eligibility.
