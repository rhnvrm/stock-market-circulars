## Summary

Capital Infra Trust (Scrip Code: 544338, ISIN: INE0Z8Z23013) has listed 17,28,90,733 new units on BSE following an institutional placement to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs). The units will commence trading from December 23, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 17,28,90,733 units allotted to QIBs through institutional placement
- Issue price: Rs. 72.30 per unit
- Distinguished numbers: 318661001 to 491551733
- Units rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Allotment date: December 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading operations commencing December 23, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 23, 2025
- **Circular Date**: December 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This institutional placement represents a significant capital raise for Capital Infra Trust. The addition of 172.89 crore units will increase the total outstanding units and may impact liquidity. Existing unitholders should note the dilution effect, though the units rank equally with existing shares. The QIB-focused placement indicates institutional confidence in the trust.
