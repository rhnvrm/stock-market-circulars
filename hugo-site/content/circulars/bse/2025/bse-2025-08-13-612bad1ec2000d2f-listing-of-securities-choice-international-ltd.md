## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 6 million new equity shares of Choice International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531358) effective August 14, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 6 million equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 290/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 300/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Allotment date: June 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 4, 2025
- **Trading Start**: August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard equity listing from warrant conversion. The shares are subject to lock-in until February 14, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
