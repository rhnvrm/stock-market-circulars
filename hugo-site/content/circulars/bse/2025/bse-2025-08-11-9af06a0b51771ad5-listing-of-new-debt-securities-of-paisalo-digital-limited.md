## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on a private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025. The securities carry a 9.75% annual interest rate and mature on August 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,400 securities with face value ₹1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: ₹34 crores
- Scrip Code: 977004, Scrip ID: 975PDL28
- ISIN: INE420C07148
- Credit Rating: IVR AA/Stable
- Issue price and paid-up value: ₹1,00,000 per security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from November 8, 2025 to August 8, 2028
- Maturity Date: August 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities add to BSE's debt segment offerings but represent standard corporate financing activity.
