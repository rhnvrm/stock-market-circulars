## Summary

CAPITALMIND MUTUAL FUND is launching a New Fund Offer (NFO) for CAPITALMIND LIQUID FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from November 18, 2025 to November 21, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of November 28, 2025. Both Direct Plan and Regular Plan with Growth options are being offered under the Liquid Fund category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 18-21, 2025
- Two schemes offered: Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401034) and Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401042)
- Both schemes categorized as Liquid Funds
- Tentative allotment date: November 28, 2025
- Extended trading till midnight on November 21, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions where client has not initiated funds till 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 27, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended period transactions) will be cancelled and CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions with initiated payment will remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds pay-out to AMC for NFO or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments must result in clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on November 27, 2025

## Important Dates

- **November 18, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **November 21, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 27, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **November 28, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1 day, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation to avoid cancellation
- **T+5 or last day of NFO, 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction deadline

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to a standard mutual fund NFO launch. It primarily affects:

- BSE StAR MF Platform members who may facilitate investor participation in the NFO
- Potential investors interested in liquid fund investments through CAPITALMIND MUTUAL FUND
- No direct impact on equity markets or listed securities
- Operational notification for mutual fund distributors and platform participants to manage NFO subscriptions and payment timelines
