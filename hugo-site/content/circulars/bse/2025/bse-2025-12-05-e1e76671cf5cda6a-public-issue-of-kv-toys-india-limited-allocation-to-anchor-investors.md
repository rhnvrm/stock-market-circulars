## Summary

K.V. Toys India Limited has finalized the allocation of 4,68,000 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs. 239.00 per equity share (including share premium of Rs. 229.00) on December 05, 2025. The allocation was made to 11 anchor investors as part of the company's public issue of equity shares with face value of Rs. 10 each.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 4,68,000 equity shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: Rs. 239.00 per equity share
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Share premium: Rs. 229.00 per share
- Number of anchor investors: 11 institutional investors
- Individual allocations range from 42,000 to 48,000 shares per investor
- No mutual fund schemes participated in the anchor portion
- CIN: U32409MH2023PLC407074
