## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for seven securities with effect from July 31, 2025. The changes range from 2% to 10% price bands across different scrips as part of surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- Seven securities affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from July 31, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 10%
- Part of BSE surveillance measures

## Securities Affected

1. **Covidh Technologies Ltd (534920)** - 2% price band
2. **Elegant Floriculture & Agrotech India Ltd (526473)** - 10% price band
3. **Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036)** - 10% price band
4. **Omax Autos Ltd (520021)** - 10% price band
5. **Panth Infinity Ltd (539143)** - 5% price band
6. **Vintage Securities Ltd (531051)** - 2% price band
7. **Yuranus Infrastructure Ltd (536846)** - 2% price band

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with revised price bands
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Price band changes will limit daily price movements for the affected securities, potentially reducing volatility and providing additional surveillance oversight for these stocks.
