## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL) worth Rs. 5 crore on its debt segment. The market-linked securities were issued on private placement basis and are effective for trading from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976954, Scrip ID: NWFL24725
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,000 per security (4% discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 24, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (Cumulative)
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The securities have a call option provision and are market-linked instruments with approximately 3.5 years maturity.
