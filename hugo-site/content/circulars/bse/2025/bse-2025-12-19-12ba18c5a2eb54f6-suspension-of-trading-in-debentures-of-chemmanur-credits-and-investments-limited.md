## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for payment of interest and redemption of the specified Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading members are advised not to deal in the mentioned debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE051307AD4, Code: 938334)
- Record date fixed for December 26, 2025
- Suspension effective from December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-785/2025-2026
- Debenture series: CCIL-ZC-9-1-26-NCD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice regarding trading suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 26, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE051307AD4 (Code: 938334) from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 19, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 26, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only a specific debenture series of one company

**Investor Impact:** Medium for debenture holders - holders as of record date will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds

**Operational Impact:** Low - routine trading suspension for corporate action processing. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trades in the specified ISIN from December 26, 2025.
