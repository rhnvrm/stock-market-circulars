## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension of trading in equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371). Trading in the company's equity shares will resume with immediate effect following this revocation.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension in Samtel India Ltd. equity shares has been revoked
- Scrip Code: 500371
- Revocation is effective immediately from December 16, 2025
- Normal trading operations can resume for the security

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension order previously imposed on Samtel India Ltd. equity shares has been lifted, restoring normal trading status for the security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can resume trading in Samtel India Ltd. equity shares
- All trading members should note the change in status
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Circular Issue Date**: December 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revocation enables shareholders to buy and sell shares, restoring liquidity and market access for Samtel India Ltd. securities.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who can now trade their holdings. The revocation suggests the company has met the necessary regulatory requirements that led to the initial suspension.

**Trading Impact**: Trading volumes and price discovery mechanisms will resume for the scrip, allowing normal market operations.
