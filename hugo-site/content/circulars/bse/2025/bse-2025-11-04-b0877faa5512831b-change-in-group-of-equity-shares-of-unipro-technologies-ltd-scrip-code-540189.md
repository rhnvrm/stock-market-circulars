## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Unipro Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 540189) will be moved from its current group to the 'X' group effective November 11, 2025. This group change is communicated to all trading members for necessary action.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 540189
- Company Name: Unipro Technologies Ltd.
- Action: Movement to 'X' group
- Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Contact Person: Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788) for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Unipro Technologies Ltd. will be included under the 'X' group from November 11, 2025. The 'X' group typically indicates securities under surveillance or those that do not meet certain listing/trading criteria, and may have trading restrictions or limitations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the group change effective November 11, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems and inform clients about the change in trading group
- Any clarifications required should be directed to Mrs. Kinjal More at +91-7304278788

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 4, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group may result in:
- Reduced trading activity and liquidity in the stock
- Increased scrutiny on trades
- Potential impact on investor confidence
- Trading restrictions that may apply to 'X' group securities
- Members and investors should exercise caution while trading in this security
