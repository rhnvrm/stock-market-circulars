## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 9,000 new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective December 19, 2025. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment with Scrip Code 730798 and ISIN INE01C314DZ6.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 9,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- **Scrip Code**: 730798
- **Scrip ID**: BFSL171225
- **Detail Name**: BFSL-18-3-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE01C314DZ6 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,874/-
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 18, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper on private placement basis with minimal market impact. The instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, indicating high credit quality. The listing provides debt market participants additional short-term investment options in Bajaj Financial Securities Limited paper maturing in March 2026.
