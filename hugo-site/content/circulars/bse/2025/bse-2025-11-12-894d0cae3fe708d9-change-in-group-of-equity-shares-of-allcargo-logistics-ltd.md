## Summary

BSE has announced that ALLCARGO LOGISTICS LTD (Scrip Code: 532749) will be transferred from 'T' Group to 'A' Group effective November 26, 2025. This follows the Exchange Notice No. 20251106-72 dated November 06, 2025. Trading in the equity shares will be permitted under 'A' Group from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: ALLCARGO LOGISTICS LTD
- Scrip Code: 532749
- Transfer from 'T' Group to 'A' Group
- Effective Date: November 26, 2025
- Trading will be permitted under 'A' Group
- This is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251106-72 dated November 06, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of ALLCARGO LOGISTICS LTD will be reclassified from 'T' Group to 'A' Group. The 'A' Group typically represents normal trading stocks with better liquidity and compliance standards compared to 'T' Group which is used for securities under surveillance or with trading restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are requested to take note of this group change and update their systems accordingly to reflect the new group classification for ALLCARGO LOGISTICS LTD effective November 26, 2025.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 12, 2025
- Effective Date: November 26, 2025
- Reference Notice: Exchange Notice No. 20251106-72 dated November 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from 'T' Group to 'A' Group is generally positive for the company and its shareholders as it indicates improved compliance status and removal of any surveillance measures. This change should enhance trading liquidity and investor confidence in ALLCARGO LOGISTICS LTD. Trading members should update their systems to accommodate normal trading conditions for this scrip from November 26, 2025.
