## Summary

BSE has listed 1,408 new equity shares of Advait Energy Transitions Limited (Scrip Code: 543230) effective December 17, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 1,776 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 1,766). The shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,408 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1,766/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1,776 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0ALI01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 10935370 to 10936777
- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the allotted shares until the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a small preferential allotment of 1,408 shares representing a routine capital raising activity through warrant conversion. The shares are subject to standard lock-in provisions until June 29, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume. The allotment to non-promoters does not alter promoter holding significantly.
