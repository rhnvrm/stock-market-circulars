## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 767 dated December 2, 2025. The auction includes 98 scrips with varying quantities available for auction, ranging from small quantities of 1 share to larger quantities of up to 49,997 shares. This is a standard settlement procedure for handling delivery shortfalls in the demat trading system.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 767
- Auction Date: December 2, 2025
- Total scrips included: 98
- Largest quantity: Mehai Techno (540730) - 49,997 shares
- Other significant quantities: PADAM COTTON (531395) - 27,232 shares; STARLINEPS E (540492) - 12,505 shares; STEEL STRIPS (513262) - 7,288 shares
- Includes major companies like BANK OF MAHA (5,000 shares), JSW STEEL, SIEMENS, Tata Motors, IOC, IDBI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and participants with obligations in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction process
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants should verify their obligations for the specified scrip codes and quantities

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 2, 2025
- Settlement Number: 767

## Impact Assessment

Low impact - This is a routine demat auction settlement affecting only specific market participants with pending delivery obligations. The auction provides a mechanism to close out unsettled positions and does not affect normal trading operations. Individual investors holding these securities are not directly impacted unless they have pending delivery obligations through their broker.
