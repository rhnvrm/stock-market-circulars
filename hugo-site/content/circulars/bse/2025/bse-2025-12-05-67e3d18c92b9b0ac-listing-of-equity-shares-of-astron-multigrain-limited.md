## Summary

Astron Multigrain Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of December 5, 2025. The company has total equity shares of 86,00,000 with promoter and promoter group holding 66.05% (56,79,940 shares) and public shareholders holding 33.95% (29,20,060 shares). The company has declared no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 86,00,000 fully paid up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 4 shareholders holding 56,79,940 shares (66.0458%)
- Public shareholders: 644 shareholders holding 29,20,060 shares (33.9542%)
- All shares are in dematerialized form (86,00,000 shares)
- No encumbrances on any shares (no pledges, NDU, or other encumbrances)
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares against depository receipts issued
- No locked-in shares reported
- No differential voting rights shares
- No significant beneficial owner reported

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

This filing is submitted in compliance with Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. Listed entities are required to file shareholding patterns at prescribed intervals to maintain transparency in ownership structure.

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: December 5, 2025
- Shareholding Pattern as of: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a standard promoter-public distribution with majority promoter holding at 66.05%. The absence of any encumbrances, pledges, or convertible securities indicates a clean capital structure. With 644 public shareholders, the stock maintains adequate public float as per listing requirements. The complete dematerialization of shares facilitates efficient trading and settlement.
