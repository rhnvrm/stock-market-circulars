## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of K K SILK MILLS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544624, symbol KKSILK, and ISIN INE0EOJ01014. The specific date of listing and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: K K SILK MILLS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544624
- Trading Symbol: KKSILK
- ISIN: INE0EOJ01014
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the separate notice containing the actual listing date and securities details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a standard new listing notification for the SME segment. Trading members are being notified in advance of the upcoming listing, but no trading can commence until the formal listing date is announced. The company will expand the investment universe for SME-focused investors once trading begins.
