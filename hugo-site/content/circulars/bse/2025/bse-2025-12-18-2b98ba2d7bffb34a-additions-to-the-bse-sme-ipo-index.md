## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 19, 2025. HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED (Exchange ticker-544656) and PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker-544657) are being listed on BSE on December 18, 2025, and will be included in the index at the open of the following day.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- HRS ALUGLAZE LIMITED - Exchange ticker 544656
- PAJSON AGRO INDIA LIMITED - Exchange ticker 544657
- Listing date: December 18, 2025
- Index inclusion effective: December 19, 2025 (at market open)
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new SME listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs may need to adjust holdings to reflect the updated index composition.

## Important Dates

- **December 18, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **December 19, 2025**: Effective date for index inclusion (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine addition of newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. Impact is primarily limited to:

- Index tracking products that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index
- Investors monitoring SME segment performance
- The two companies gaining index exposure and potential increased visibility

No broader market operational or trading changes are associated with this circular.
