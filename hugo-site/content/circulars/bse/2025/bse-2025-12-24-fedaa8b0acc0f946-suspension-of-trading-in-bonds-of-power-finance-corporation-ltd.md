## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in bonds of Power Finance Corporation Ltd (ISIN: INE134E08LZ0, Code: 974471) effective December 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of its 7.58% bonds maturing on January 15, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: PC-7.58%-15-1-26-PVT (Private placement)
- ISIN: INE134E08LZ0
- BSE Code: 974471
- Record date for redemption: December 30, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 30, 2025
- Circular reference: DR-787/2025-2026
- Notice number: 20251224-40

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified bonds from December 30, 2025
- Trading members must ensure their systems and operations reflect the trading suspension
- All pending orders in these bonds should be cancelled or withdrawn before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 30, 2025**: Record date for redemption; Trading suspension begins
- **January 15, 2026**: Bond maturity date (implied from bond name)

## Impact Assessment

**Bondholders**: Investors holding these bonds as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds. No action required beyond holding the bonds.

**Trading Members**: Must ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive to avoid regulatory issues. Any positions should be squared off before December 30, 2025.

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only one privately placed bond series nearing maturity. Standard redemption process with no systemic implications for the debt market.
