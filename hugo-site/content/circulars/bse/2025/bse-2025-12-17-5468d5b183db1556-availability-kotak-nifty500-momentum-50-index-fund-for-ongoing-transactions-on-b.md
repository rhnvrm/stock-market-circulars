## Summary

BSE has announced that KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 18, 2025. The scheme will also support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP). This follows the previous notice No. 20251119-30 regarding the scheme's availability.

## Key Points

- KOTAK NIFTY500 MOMENTUM 50 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions from December 18, 2025
- Available for subscription and redemption on BSE StAR MF Platform
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- Scheme category: Equity
- References previous notice No. 20251119-30

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1YF1
2. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1YH7
3. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1YG9

### Direct Plan Options
4. **Growth** - ISIN: INF174KA1YC8
5. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF174KA1YE4
6. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF174KA1YD6

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members can facilitate transactions in the reopened scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform following standard platform procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 18, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors interested in the Kotak Nifty500 Momentum 50 Index Fund.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking exposure to momentum-based equity strategies through the Nifty 500 Momentum 50 Index. The reopening provides investment opportunities in both regular and direct plan variants with multiple payout options.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can resume processing transactions for this scheme, including setting up systematic investment, transfer, and withdrawal plans for clients.
