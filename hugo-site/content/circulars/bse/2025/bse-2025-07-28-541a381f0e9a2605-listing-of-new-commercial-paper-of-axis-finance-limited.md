## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper securities issued by Axis Finance Limited on private placement basis, each with face value of Rs. 5,00,000. Both securities carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper securities listed with 2000 quantity each
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Security Details

**Security 1:**
- Scrip Code: 729909
- ISIN: INE891K14PF3
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,943.50
- Redemption Date: October 17, 2025

**Security 2:**
- Scrip Code: 729911
- ISIN: INE891K14PE6

- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Dates: October 17, 2025 and October 30, 2025

## Trading Information

Routine debt security listing with no significant market impact. Provides additional short-term funding instruments for institutional investors seeking high-rated commercial paper with 3-month maturity periods.
