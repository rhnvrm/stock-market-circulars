## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of LIGHT MICROFINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (Code: LMPL-13.79%-12-12-25-PVT, ISIN: INE366T07170, Security Code: 974421) effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading in LMPL debentures suspended from November 27, 2025
- Debenture series: LMPL-13.79%-12-12-25-PVT (INE366T07170)
- Security Code: 974421
- Reason: Record date fixed for redemption and interest payment
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for trading suspension due to corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from November 27, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and comply accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in security code 974421 (ISIN: INE366T07170) from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 27, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This suspension affects only a single series of private debentures from LIGHT MICROFINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED. The impact is limited to holders of this specific debt security.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems do not accept orders for this security from November 27, 2025. This is a routine operational requirement for corporate actions involving record dates.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their scheduled interest payment and principal redemption as per the fixed record date. Secondary market trading is suspended to facilitate proper corporate action processing.
