## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 769 dated December 4, 2025. The list contains securities of 97 scrips across various companies that are subject to demat auction for this settlement cycle. Quantities range from 1 share to 41,484 shares depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 769
- Settlement Date: December 4, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 97
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, pharma, energy, technology, and manufacturing
- Highest quantity: Srestha Finvest (539217) with 41,484 shares
- Several blue-chip stocks included: Adani Ports (10 shares), ICICI Bank (1 share), Infosys (107 shares), Tata Power (637 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure delivery obligations are met for the listed scrips
- Participants with shortages in these securities must participate in the auction or arrange for securities
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE settlement timelines
- Brokers with client obligations in these scrips must take necessary action

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 4, 2025
- Settlement Number: 769
- Auction execution timeline to follow standard BSE settlement procedures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects multiple scrips across sectors but with relatively small quantities for most securities. Routine settlement process.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers and clearing members with obligations in these 97 scrips need to arrange for securities or participate in the auction to fulfill settlement obligations. The range of affected securities spans from large-cap to small-cap stocks.

**Trading Impact:** Potential temporary price impact on smaller scrips with higher auction quantities (e.g., Srestha Finvest, Hikal, Tilak Venture) during auction execution.
