## Summary

BSE has listed 10 series of secured redeemable non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by ICL Fincorp Limited. The NCDs were allotted on December 2, 2025, with a face value of Rs. 1,000 each and issue price of Rs. 1,000 per NCD. The total issuance comprises 970,767 NCDs across different series with varying coupon rates (10.50% to 12.00% for interest-bearing NCDs), interest payment frequencies (monthly, annually, cumulative), and maturity dates ranging from January 2027 to October 2031. All NCDs carry a CRISIL BBB-/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Total Securities Listed:** 970,767 NCDs across 10 series
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1,000 per NCD
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 1,000 per NCD
- **Date of Allotment:** December 2, 2025
- **Nature:** Secured, Redeemable, Non-Convertible Debentures
- **Credit Rating:** CRISIL BBB-/Stable
- **Market Lot:** One NCD
- **Put/Call Option:** Not Applicable
- **Interest Payment Frequencies:** Monthly, Annually, and Cumulative options available

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides debt market investors with multiple investment options in ICL Fincorp Limited's secured NCDs across different maturity periods and interest payment structures. The CRISIL BBB-/Stable rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of financial obligations. The effective yields ranging from 11.00% to 12.68% reflect the risk-return profile of these investment-grade debt securities. The variety of interest payment frequencies (monthly, annual, cumulative) allows investors to choose based on their cash flow preferences. This listing enhances liquidity for NCD holders through exchange-based trading on BSE.
