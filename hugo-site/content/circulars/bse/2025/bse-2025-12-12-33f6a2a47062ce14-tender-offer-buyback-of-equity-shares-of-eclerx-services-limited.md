## Summary

ECLERX SERVICES LIMITED has announced a buyback of equity shares through a tender offer. The company has set December 17, 2025 as the record date to determine the entitlement and names of shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer. The ex-entitlement date is also December 17, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-779/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: ECLERX SERVICES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532927/132927)
- Record Date: December 17, 2025
- Ex-Entitlement Date: December 17, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-779/2025-2026
- Segment: Equity - Dematerialised Securities (Rolling Settlement)
- Purpose: Determine shareholders eligible to participate in buyback offer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the exchange are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement basis
- Only shareholders holding equity shares as of the record date (December 17, 2025) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade on ex-entitlement basis from December 17, 2025

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Circular announcement date
- **December 17, 2025**: Record date for determining eligible shareholders
- **December 17, 2025**: Ex-entitlement basis effective date
- **Settlement No.**: DR-779/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The buyback announcement typically has a positive impact on stock price as it indicates management confidence and provides an exit opportunity to shareholders at potentially premium prices. Trading will be on ex-entitlement basis from December 17, 2025.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders must hold shares by December 16, 2025 (T-1 date) to be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. Those purchasing shares on or after December 17, 2025 will not be eligible for the buyback.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure proper marking of ex-entitlement trading and inform their clients about eligibility criteria for participating in the buyback offer.
