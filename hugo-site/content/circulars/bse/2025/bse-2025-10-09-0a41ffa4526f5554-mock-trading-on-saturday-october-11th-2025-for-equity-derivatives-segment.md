## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 11th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading platforms, including third-party systems and in-house developed applications using ETI APIs. The mock trading will not result in any real obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 11th, 2025
- Available for members using BOLT TWS, third-party platforms, or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will include various functionalities and exceptional market conditions (risk-reduction mode, trading halt, etc.)
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- All trades are for testing purposes only - no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations
- No rights or liabilities will be created from mock trades

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine testing session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Feedback must be submitted by 2:00 PM on the same day
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **October 11th, 2025 (Saturday)**: Mock Trading Session
  - Login: 08:15 AM - 09:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 09:15 AM - 12:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 2:00 PM on October 11th, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Voluntary testing opportunity for trading members to familiarize themselves with platform functionalities and test their systems under simulated exceptional market conditions. Members can use this to ensure their trading applications are functioning properly without any financial risk or settlement obligations.
