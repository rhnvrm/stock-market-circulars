## Summary

BSE has announced the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 511632). The company profile submitted includes complete corporate information and compliance details.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited
- Scrip Code: 511632
- ISIN: INE091N01014
- Registered Office: 42, 199 Floor, Gopal Bhavan, Princess Street, Mumbai - 400 002
- CIN: L65990MH1984PLC033825
- Listed on: BSE and CSE
- Nature of Business: Management and Financial Services
- Whole-Time Director: Satyanarayan Ramchandra Agarwal
- Chairman: Pawan Kr Agarwal
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Prashant Chandrakant Vipani
- Statutory Auditor: M/S K.R. Khandelwal & Co. (appointed at AGM on 30.09.2025)

## Regulatory Changes

Trading suspension has been revoked for Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited, allowing normal trading to resume on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has submitted complete company profile as required
- Listing fees confirmed paid to BSE
- No change in main line of business in last five years
- No company name change in last five years
- Auditor change was due to statutory rotation as per Companies Act

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 2, 2025
- Auditor Appointment Date: September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of trading suspension allows shareholders to resume buying and selling equity shares of Olympic Management & Financial Services Limited on BSE. This restores liquidity and market access for the security. The company has demonstrated compliance with regulatory requirements by submitting the required company profile information.
