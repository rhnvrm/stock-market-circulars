## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited totaling Rs. 350 Crores (7,000 units of Rs. 5 Lakhs each) on private placement basis. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 12, 2025, under ISIN INE212K14CX3, maturing on March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total issuance: 7,000 units of Commercial Paper worth Rs. 350 Crores
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue prices: Rs. 4,91,954 and Rs. 4,92,026.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730752, Scrip ID: SSL111225
- ISIN: INE212K14CX3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: December 11, 2025
- Redemption date: March 12, 2026
- Tenor: 91 days
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety and is intended for institutional investors only. The 91-day short-term instrument provides working capital financing to SBICAP Securities Limited. No impact on equity markets or retail trading activities.
