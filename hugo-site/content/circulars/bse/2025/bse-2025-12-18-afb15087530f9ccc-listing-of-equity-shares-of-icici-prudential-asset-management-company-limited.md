## Summary

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of December 18, 2025. The total issued equity shares stand at 494,258,520 shares with Promoter & Promoter Group holding 87.59% and Public holding 12.41%.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares held: 494,258,520 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 432,929,855 shares (87.59%)
- Public shareholding: 61,328,665 shares (12.41%)
- Total number of shareholders: 2,958,295
- Dematerialized shares: 459,242,829 shares (92.92%)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares in locked-in, pledged, or encumbered status
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or encumbrances
- Standard shareholding pattern disclosure format followed

## Important Dates

- Post-issue date: December 18, 2025
- Filing date: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with no immediate market impact. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding at 87.59% with no encumbrances or pledges. High dematerialization rate of 92.92% indicates good market infrastructure adoption. No material changes in shareholding structure that would affect trading or investor sentiment.
