## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Axis Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 2500 units totaling Rs. 125 crores, and will be traded on BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2500 units (Rs. 125 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,268 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE891K14PH9
- Scrip Code: 730071
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading limited to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Issuing and Paying agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Listing effective: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors in the debt segment.
