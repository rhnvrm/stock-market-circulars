## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (scrip code 975784, ISIN: INE551U07308) effective December 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture series: SFPL-8.25%-27-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE551U07308
- Scrip code: 975784
- Record date: December 12, 2025
- No dealings from: December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice number: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational measure for debt securities approaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from December 12, 2025
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive
- All outstanding positions must be settled before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single series of privately placed debentures. The suspension is for standard corporate action purposes (redemption and interest payment) and only impacts holders of this specific debt instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the terms. The trading suspension prevents any transactions during the record date processing period.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure they do not execute any trades in this specific debenture series from the suspension date.
