## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Axis Finance Limited on a private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 19, 2025. The listing comprises 500 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, allotted on December 18, 2025, with redemption scheduled for October 23, 2026. The securities carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, with Axis Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Papers listed with scrip code 730797 and scrip ID AFL171225
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,71,914.50 per unit
- ISIN: INE891K14PQ0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891K14PQ0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to for all transactions
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 18, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: October 23, 2026
