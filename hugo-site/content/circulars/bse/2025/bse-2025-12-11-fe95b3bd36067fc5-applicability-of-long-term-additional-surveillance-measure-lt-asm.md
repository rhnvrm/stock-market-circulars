## Summary

BSE has issued updates to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective December 12, 2025. Two new securities have been added to the LT-ASM framework, two securities are being moved to higher ASM stages, one security is being directly placed in Stage IV, and no securities are being removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- 2 new securities shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework: Alps Industries Ltd and Raconteur Global Resources Ltd
- 2 securities moved to higher ASM stages: Dhyaani Tradeventtures Ltd and Droneacharya Aerial Innovations Ltd (both to Stage II)
- 1 security directly placed in Stage IV: CIAN Agro Industries & Infrastructure Ltd
- No securities moving to lower ASM stages
- No securities exiting the LT-ASM framework
- Changes effective from December 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The Long Term ASM framework is a surveillance mechanism applied to securities based on specific criteria. Securities under this framework face additional compliance and trading restrictions.

**New Additions (Part A):**
1. Alps Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 530715, ISIN: INE093B01015)
2. Raconteur Global Resources Ltd (Scrip Code: 541703, ISIN: INE00WY01013)

**Moving to Higher Stages (Part B):**
1. Dhyaani Tradeventtures Ltd (Scrip Code: 543516) - Moving to ASM Stage II
2. Droneacharya Aerial Innovations Ltd (Scrip Code: 543713) - Moving to ASM Stage II

**Direct Stage IV Placement (Part D):**
1. CIAN Agro Industries & Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 519477) - Direct to ASM Stage IV

## Compliance Requirements

Listed companies included in the LT-ASM framework must:
- Comply with enhanced surveillance requirements
- Accept trading restrictions as per their assigned ASM stage
- Higher ASM stages typically involve stricter price bands, position limits, and reporting obligations
- Stage IV represents the highest level of surveillance with most stringent controls

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** December 12, 2025 - All changes to LT-ASM framework take effect

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Securities under LT-ASM face restricted trading conditions including potential price bands and reduced liquidity
- Investors in affected securities may experience limited trading flexibility
- Movement to higher ASM stages indicates continued surveillance concerns

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect new surveillance measures
- Risk management systems need adjustment for affected securities
- Investors should be aware of enhanced surveillance status when trading these securities

**Affected Securities Summary:**
- Total new entries: 2 securities
- Securities with enhanced surveillance (moved to higher stages): 2 securities
- Direct Stage IV placements: 1 security
- Total securities continuing in framework: Multiple (consolidated list provided in Annexure III)

The absence of securities exiting the framework indicates ongoing surveillance concerns across the identified companies.
