## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Time Technoplast Limited issued on private placement basis effective December 1, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 units of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, with maturity date of March 25, 2026. The Commercial Paper is rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Time Technoplast Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 25 crore (500 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,88,555.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730317
- **Scrip ID**: TTL26925
- **ISIN**: INE508G14HZ6 (further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IDBI Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE508G14HZ6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for price movements
- Securities will be traded exclusively on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: March 25, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 114 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The instrument is part of Time Technoplast's normal treasury operations for working capital management.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in the debt market. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details for trading and settlement purposes. Contact debt department at specified phone numbers for clarifications: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
