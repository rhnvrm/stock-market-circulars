## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED effective December 16, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of its debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MMFL-10%-31-12-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE101Q07AO2, Scrip Code: 975320)
- Record date for redemption: December 16, 2025
- No dealings permitted from December 16, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-778/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 16, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing instruction for the affected security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 16, 2025 (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only a single debenture series of a private limited company in the debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as on the record date will receive their redemption proceeds. No new positions can be taken from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard corporate action that trading members regularly handle. Systems need to be updated to prevent inadvertent trades in the suspended security.

**Liquidity Impact**: The suspension removes this specific debt instrument from trading, but this is expected and routine for debentures approaching redemption.
