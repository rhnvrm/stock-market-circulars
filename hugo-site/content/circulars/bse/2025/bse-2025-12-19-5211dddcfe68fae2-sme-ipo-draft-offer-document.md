## Summary

BSE announces the availability of the Draft Offer Document of Mother Agri Food Limited filed on December 18, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is made available on the SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Mother Agri Food Limited on December 18, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must be hosted on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public is invited to submit comments on the draft offer document
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the specified period
- Company must comply with all disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Draft Filing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Public Comment Deadline**: Within 21 days from December 19, 2025 (January 9, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine SME IPO filing notification.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for prospective investors interested in the Mother Agri Food Limited IPO. The 21-day comment period allows stakeholders to review the offer document and provide feedback before finalization.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing market participants. This affects only the issuer company and potential IPO applicants.
