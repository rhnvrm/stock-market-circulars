## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of Tourism Finance Corporation of India Ltd (TFC) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond ISIN: INE305A09232 (Code: 952989)
- Bond Series: TFC-8.81%-9-11-25-PVT
- Record Date: October 24, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified bonds from October 24, 2025
- Members should inform their clients holding these bonds about the suspension
- All dealing activities in bond INE305A09232 must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 24, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** October 24, 2025
- **Expected Redemption Date:** November 9, 2025 (based on bond series name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects only bondholders of this specific series. The suspension is temporary and routine for corporate action processing.

**Investor Impact:** Bondholders of the TFC-8.81%-9-11-25-PVT series will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading will be possible from October 24, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific bond ISIN and communicate with affected clients regarding the redemption process.
