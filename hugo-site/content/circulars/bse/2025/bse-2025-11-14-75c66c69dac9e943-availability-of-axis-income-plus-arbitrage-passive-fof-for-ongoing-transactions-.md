## Summary

BSE has announced that AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 17, 2025. The scheme will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This notice follows reference to earlier Notice No. 20251027-59.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants are being made available: Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All variants are categorized under DEBT schemes
- Each plan has a unique ISIN for identification
- The scheme reopening applies to the BSE StAR MF Platform specifically

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members are informed to enable transactions for the specified scheme and its variants on the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions with SIP, STP & SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a scheme-specific operational update affecting only investors and distributors interested in the AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF. The reopening provides investors access to a debt fund-of-funds product with arbitrage strategy through various transaction modes on BSE's mutual fund platform.
