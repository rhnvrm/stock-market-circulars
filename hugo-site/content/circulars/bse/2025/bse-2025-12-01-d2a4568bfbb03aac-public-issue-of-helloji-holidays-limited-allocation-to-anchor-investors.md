## Summary

Helloji Holidays Limited's Board of Directors, in consultation with the Book Running Lead Manager, finalized the allocation of 2,64,000 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs. 118 per equity share (including share premium of Rs. 108 per share) on December 01, 2025. The total anchor investor allocation amounts to Rs. 3,11,52,000.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated: 2,64,000 shares
- Allocation price: Rs. 118 per equity share (including Rs. 108 share premium)
- Total allocation amount: Rs. 3,11,52,000
- Two anchor investors participated with equal allocation of 50% each
- Rajasthan Global Securities Private Limited allocated 1,32,000 shares for Rs. 1,55,76,000
- Longthrive Capital VCC-Trendview Capital Fund allocated 1,32,000 shares for Rs. 1,55,76,000
- Board meeting held on December 01, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure requirement for anchor investor allocation in SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Disclosure of anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI IPO regulations
- Public dissemination of allocation information through BSE website
- Compliance with Red Herring Prospectus terms and conditions

## Important Dates

- **December 01, 2025**: Board meeting date and anchor investor allocation finalization
- **December 01, 2025**: Disclosure submission date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure for an SME IPO with minimal market-wide impact. The anchor investor allocation represents the pre-IPO institutional placement that provides price discovery and stability for the public issue. Impact is limited to IPO participants and potential investors in Helloji Holidays Limited. The equal 50-50 allocation split between two institutional investors demonstrates balanced distribution.
