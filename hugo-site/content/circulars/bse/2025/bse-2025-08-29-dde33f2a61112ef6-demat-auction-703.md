## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities for demat auction under Settlement No. 703 dated August 29, 2025. The auction includes 89 different securities with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 97,500 shares across multiple companies and sectors.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 703
- Auction Date: August 29, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 89 different scrips
- Largest Quantity: DR. REDDY'S LABORATORIES with 97,500 shares
- Smallest Quantity: Multiple securities with 1 share each
- Major Companies Included: Reliance Industries (14,008 shares), NMDC Ltd (27,000 shares), Bank of India (10,400 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must settle the listed securities as per BSE demat auction procedures
- Settlement must be completed as per the specified quantities and scrip codes
- All auction participants must comply with BSE settlement guidelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: August 29, 2025
- Settlement Number: 703

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects a diverse range of securities across various sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, technology, and industrial companies. The auction facilitates the settlement of pending delivery obligations and ensures market integrity. The impact is primarily operational, affecting specific market participants who have outstanding settlement obligations for the listed securities.
