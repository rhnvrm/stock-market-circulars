## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 305 crores (6,100 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (6,100 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 305 crores approximately
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730010, ISIN: INE700G14PJ6
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by HDFC Securities Limited. The listing provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
