## Summary

KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD has announced a part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE094I07080, Code: 977231). Trading members are informed that trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issuer: KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD
- ISIN: INE094I07080 (Code: 977231)
- Debenture Series: KPDL-15-10-35-PVT
- Record Date: December 26, 2025
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-785/2025-2026
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 98,974/- per Debenture
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action following standard part redemption procedures for non-convertible debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the reduced face value for the specified debentures
- Trading in the debentures will reflect the reduced face value from the effective date
- All trading and settlement activities must account for the new face value of Rs. 98,974/- per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: December 26, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-785/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects holders of specific debenture series (KPDL-15-10-35-PVT) with reduced face value per debenture. Part redemption results in return of capital to debenture holders while reducing the outstanding principal amount.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new reduced face value of Rs. 98,974/- per debenture for all transactions from December 26, 2025 onwards. Settlement processes must account for the reduced face value in the specified settlement number.
