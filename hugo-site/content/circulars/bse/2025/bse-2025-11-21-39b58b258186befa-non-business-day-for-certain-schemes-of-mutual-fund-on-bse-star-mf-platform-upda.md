## Summary

BSE has announced a non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) Platform on November 24, 2025. This is a routine operational update affecting transaction processing for this specific mutual fund scheme.

## Key Points

- Non-business day scheduled for November 24, 2025
- Affects only ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund
- Applicable to BSE StAR MF Platform transactions
- No transactions will be processed for this scheme on the specified date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational schedule update.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and distributors should note that transactions for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund will not be processed on November 24, 2025
- Alternative trading days should be used for purchase, redemption, or switch transactions

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Non-business day for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact limited to investors and distributors planning transactions in ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund on November 24, 2025. Advance notice allows for transaction planning on alternative dates. No broader market impact expected.
