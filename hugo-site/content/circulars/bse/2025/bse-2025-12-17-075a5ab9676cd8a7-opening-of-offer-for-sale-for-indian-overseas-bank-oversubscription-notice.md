## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the oversubscription of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for Indian Overseas Bank equity shares. This circular provides information about the bidding status and oversubscription details.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale opened for Indian Overseas Bank equity shares
- The OFS has been oversubscribed
- Notice issued to inform market participants about subscription status
- Relates to government disinvestment program

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the oversubscription status
- Follow standard OFS procedures for allocation and settlement
- Comply with SEBI guidelines for OFS transactions

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 17, 2025
- OFS opening and closing dates as per the specific offering schedule

## Impact Assessment

The oversubscription indicates strong investor demand for Indian Overseas Bank shares. This notice is relevant for investors who participated in the OFS, as it may affect allocation ratios. Market participants should monitor for subsequent allocation and settlement notifications.
