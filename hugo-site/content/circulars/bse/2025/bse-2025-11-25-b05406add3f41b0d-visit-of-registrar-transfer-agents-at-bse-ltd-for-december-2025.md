## Summary

BSE has published the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) who will be visiting BSE premises in December 2025. The circular includes a list of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit. These visits are part of regular investor service operations where RTAs are available at the exchange premises to address investor queries and grievances.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule announced for December 2025 at BSE Ltd.
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is among the RTAs scheduled to visit
- The circular contains a comprehensive list of 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, logistics, textiles, and technology
- RTAs facilitate investor services including share transfers, dematerialization, dividend payments, and grievance resolution

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine administrative notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular for investor awareness and service facilitation.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Month**: December 2025 (specific dates to be communicated separately)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Nil. This is an administrative circular with no direct market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for shareholders of listed companies serviced by visiting RTAs. Investors can meet RTA representatives in person at BSE premises to resolve share-related queries, grievances, or service requests.

**Operational Impact**: Facilitates better investor servicing and grievance redressal by providing physical access to RTA representatives at the exchange premises.

## Companies Serviced by Adroit Corporate Services

The circular lists 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., including:
- Financial services companies (LKP Finance, IndiaNivesh, Dhansafal Finserve)
- Manufacturing and industrial companies (Hind Rectifiers, Bajaj Steel Industries, Safari Industries)
- Real estate companies (Marathon Nextgen Realty, Arihant Superstructures, Satra Properties)
- Technology companies (Securekloud Technologies, Netlink Solutions)
- Textile and paper companies (Soma Papers, Rishi Techtex, Banganga Paper Industries)
- Various other sectors
