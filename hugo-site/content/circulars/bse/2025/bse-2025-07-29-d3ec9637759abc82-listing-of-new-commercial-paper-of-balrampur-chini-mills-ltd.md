## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. worth Rs. 100 crore issued on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit, issued at a discount with 55-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 729924, Scrip ID: BCML29725
- ISIN Number: INE119A14864
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,631/- (discount to face value)
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025 (effective from today)
- Redemption Date: September 22, 2025
- Maturity Period: 55 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt market. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
