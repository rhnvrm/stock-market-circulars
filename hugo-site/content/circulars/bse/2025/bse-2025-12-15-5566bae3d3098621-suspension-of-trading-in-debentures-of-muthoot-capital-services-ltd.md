## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Muthoot Capital Services Ltd. The company has fixed December 19, 2025 as the record date for payment of interest and redemption of debentures bearing ISIN INE296G07093 (Code: 974550). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from December 19, 2025 onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debenture series MCSL-GSEC-6-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE296G07093
- Debenture Code: 974550
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-781/2025-2026
- Issuer: Muthoot Capital Services Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption and interest payment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debentures from December 19, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended debenture series

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 19, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action affecting a single debenture series of one issuer. The suspension is temporary and related to scheduled redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Only holders of this specific debenture series are affected. The suspension ensures proper settlement of redemption and interest payments to eligible holders as of the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Routine. Trading members need to update their systems and inform clients, but this is standard procedure for such corporate actions in the debt segment.
