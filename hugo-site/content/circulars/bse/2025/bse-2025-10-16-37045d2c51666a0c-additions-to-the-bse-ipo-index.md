## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective October 20, 2025. Rubicon Research Limited (Exchange ticker 544578) and Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited (Exchange ticker 544580) will be included in the index following their listing on BSE on October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE IPO Index: Rubicon Research Limited and Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited
- Both companies listed on BSE on Thursday, October 16, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on Monday, October 20, 2025
- Exchange tickers: 544578 (Rubicon Research) and 544580 (Canara Robeco)
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Related to prior notices: 20251015-42 and 20251015-44

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution following new IPO listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers tracking the BSE IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituents.

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with minimal market impact. The addition of these two stocks to the BSE IPO Index is procedural following their recent IPO listings. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to include these securities in their portfolios. The impact is limited to passive investors and index fund managers who track this specific index.
