## Summary

BSE has announced the trading holidays for the calendar year 2026 for Equity and Equity Derivatives Segments. The exchange will observe 15 trading holidays on weekdays, with 2 additional holidays falling on weekends. Special Muhurat Trading will be conducted on Sunday, November 8, 2026 (Diwali - Laxmi Pujan), with timings to be notified separately. The exchange reserves the right to alter or change any of these holidays with advance notice.

## Key Points

- 15 trading holidays scheduled on weekdays throughout 2026
- 2 holidays (Mahashivratri and Independence Day) fall on Saturday/Sunday
- Muhurat Trading scheduled for Sunday, November 8, 2026 (Diwali - Laxmi Pujan)
- Timings for Muhurat Trading to be announced separately
- Exchange may modify holiday schedule with advance circular
- Applies to both Equity and Equity Derivatives Segments

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an annual informational notice regarding the trading holiday calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note all scheduled holidays for operational planning
- Trading members should await separate circular for Muhurat Trading timings
- Trading members must monitor for any subsequent circulars altering the holiday schedule

## Important Dates

**Weekday Trading Holidays in 2026:**
- January 26, 2026 (Monday) - Republic Day
- March 3, 2026 (Tuesday) - Holi
- March 26, 2026 (Thursday) - Shri Ram Navami
- March 31, 2026 (Tuesday) - Shri Mahavir Jayanti
- April 3, 2026 (Friday) - Good Friday
- April 14, 2026 (Tuesday) - Dr. Baba Saheb Ambedkar Jayanti
- May 1, 2026 (Friday) - Maharashtra Day
- May 28, 2026 (Thursday) - Bakri Id
- June 26, 2026 (Friday) - Muharram
- September 14, 2026 (Monday) - Ganesh Chaturthi
- October 2, 2026 (Friday) - Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti
- October 20, 2026 (Tuesday) - Dussehra
- November 10, 2026 (Tuesday) - Diwali Balipratipada
- November 24, 2026 (Tuesday) - Prakash Gurpurb Sri Guru Nanak Dev
- December 25, 2026 (Friday) - Christmas

**Weekend Holidays:**
- February 15, 2026 (Sunday) - Mahashivratri
- March 21, 2026 (Saturday) - Id-Ul-Fitr (Ramzan Id)
- August 15, 2026 (Saturday) - Independence Day
- November 8, 2026 (Sunday) - Diwali Laxmi Pujan

**Special Trading Session:**
- November 8, 2026 (Sunday) - Muhurat Trading (timings to be notified)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - All trading members and market participants must plan their operations around these holidays. The 15 weekday holidays represent days when markets will be completely closed, affecting liquidity and trading strategies.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading firms, brokers, and investors need to adjust their trading calendars, settlement schedules, and operational planning for 2026. The special Muhurat Trading session on a Sunday requires separate operational arrangements.

**Planning Impact:** High for operational planning - Advance notice allows market participants to plan their trading strategies, settlement cycles, margin requirements, and resource allocation well in advance for the entire year.

**Note:** The exchange retains flexibility to modify the holiday schedule, requiring participants to monitor for any subsequent circulars regarding changes.
