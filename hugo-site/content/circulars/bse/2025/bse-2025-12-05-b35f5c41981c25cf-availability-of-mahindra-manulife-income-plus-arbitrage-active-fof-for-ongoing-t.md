## Summary

BSE announces that MAHINDRA MANULIFE INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 08, 2025. This notice follows reference Notice No. 20251120-10 and applies to six plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options).

## Key Points

- Scheme reopening date: December 08, 2025
- Platform: BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund)
- Fund category: Debt schemes
- Available facilities: Ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six plan variants covered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- Reference to previous notice: 20251120-10

## Scheme Details

The following six ISIN variants will be available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth**: INF174V01CR0
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment**: INF174V01CT6
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF174V01CS8
4. **Direct Plan - Growth**: INF174V01CU4
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment**: INF174V01CW0
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF174V01CV2

All variants are classified under the Debt category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational notice regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should:
- Update their systems to enable transactions for the specified scheme from December 08, 2025
- Ensure all six plan variants are available for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions
- Note the reference to previous Notice No. 20251120-10 for historical context

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Scheme Reopening Date**: December 08, 2025
- **Notice Number**: 20251205-61

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice affecting only investors and distributors dealing with MAHINDRA MANULIFE INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF. The reopening provides investment flexibility for existing and prospective investors of this specific debt-oriented fund of funds. No broader market or trading implications exist beyond mutual fund platform operations.
