## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Mac Charles Hub Projects Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are zero coupon bonds with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling 21,500 units. The securities carry an IND BB+/Stable credit rating and will mature on December 15, 2030. Trading commenced on December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 21,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Scrip Code: 977385
- Scrip ID: 0MCHP30
- ISIN: INE09T907019
- Security Name: MCHPPL-ZC-15-12-30-PVT
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000
- Credit Rating: IND BB+/Stable
- Interest Type: Zero Coupon (no periodic interest payments)
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: December 16, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE09T907019
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 15, 2030
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Not applicable (zero coupon bonds)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing adds debt securities of Mac Charles Hub Projects Private Limited to the BSE Debt segment, providing additional trading options for institutional and qualified investors. As these are privately placed zero coupon bonds with a sub-investment grade rating (BB+), they are likely to appeal to specialized debt investors. The impact is limited to participants in the debt market and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
