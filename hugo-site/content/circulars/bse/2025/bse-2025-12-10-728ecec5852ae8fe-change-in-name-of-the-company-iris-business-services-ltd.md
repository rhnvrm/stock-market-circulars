## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for IRIS Business Services Limited (CIN: L72900MH2000PLC128943). The company's name has been officially changed to IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited with effect from November 28, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of any stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from IRIS Business Services Limited to IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited
- Effective date of name change: November 28, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L72900MH2000PLC128943
- Company was originally incorporated as IRISBUSINESS.COM (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- No impact on existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate administrative action involving a name change as per Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (IRIS Business Services Limited) along with the new name (IRIS RegTech Solutions Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Effective date of name change as per Certificate of Incorporation
- **November 29, 2025**: Date of circular announcement and digital signature by Company Secretary
- **Two-year period**: Duration for which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a cosmetic change reflecting the company's business focus on regulatory technology solutions. No impact on trading operations or stock performance expected.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative only. The name change does not affect the company's operations, contracts, assets, liabilities, or legal standing.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing rights and obligations remain unchanged.
