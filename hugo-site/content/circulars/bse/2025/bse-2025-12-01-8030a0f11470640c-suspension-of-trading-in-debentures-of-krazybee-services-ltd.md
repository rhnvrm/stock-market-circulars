## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD with ISIN INE07HK07742 (Code: 975750) effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures (KSL-10.20%-19-12-25-PVT)
- ISIN: INE07HK07742, BSE Code: 975750
- Record date set for December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from December 4, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- Trading members are required to note and comply with the suspension directive
- All market participants should be informed about the trading halt

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Purpose Date**: December 4, 2025 (Redemption and Interest Payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of specific KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures. This is a scheduled corporate action with advance notice, minimizing market disruption.

**Investor Impact**: High for debenture holders who will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. Potential buyers/sellers will need to wait until the corporate action is completed.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard procedure for debt securities undergoing redemption. Trading members have clear instructions to halt dealings in the specified security.
