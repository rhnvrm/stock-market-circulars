## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Indigrid Infrastructure Trust (Scrip Code: 976972). Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN will change from INE219X07561 to INE219X07579, and the paid-up value will increase from Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture, effective December 29, 2025. This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice 20250926-5 dated September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- ISIN change: INE219X07561 → INE219X07579
- Paid-up value increase: Rs. 90,000 → Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code remains: 976972
- Changes effective from December 29, 2025 (Monday)
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20250926-5 dated September 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an update on existing debt securities following corporate action (call money receipt).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are requested to note the new ISIN and revised paid-up value for the debt securities
- Ensure systems are updated to reflect the ISIN change and new paid-up value before December 29, 2025
- Use new ISIN INE219X07579 for all transactions from December 29, 2025 onwards

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025 (Monday) - New ISIN and paid-up value become applicable
- **Previous Related Notice**: September 26, 2025 (Exchange Notice 20250926-5)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects holders and traders of specific debt securities of Indigrid Infrastructure Trust. The ISIN change requires system updates by trading members and brokers.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN and paid-up value. Debenture holders will see their holdings transition to the new ISIN with increased paid-up value from Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture.

**Financial Impact**: The increase in paid-up value from Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 represents call money paid by debenture holders, increasing their total investment by Rs. 10,000 per debenture.
