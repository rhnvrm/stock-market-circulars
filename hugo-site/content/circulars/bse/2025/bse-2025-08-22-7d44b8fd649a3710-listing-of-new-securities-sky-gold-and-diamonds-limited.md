## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new equity shares of Sky Gold And Diamonds Limited (Scrip Code: 541967). The listing includes 2,07,000 preferential equity shares issued through warrant conversion and 18,63,000 bonus shares, all effective from Monday, August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,07,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1,007/- to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- 18,63,000 equity shares allotted as bonus shares in 9:1 ratio
- All shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issue price: Rs. 1,017/- per share
- Date of allotment: June 26, 2025
- ISIN: INE01IU01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions applicable as specified
- All shares subject to standard listing compliance requirements

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 25, 2025 (Monday)
- **Allotment Date**: June 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Until February 28, 2027 for all shares

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected as this is a standard listing announcement for a relatively small company. The preferential allotment to promoters and bonus issue increases the total share capital but maintains existing shareholding proportions. Lock-in period ensures price stability during initial trading period.
