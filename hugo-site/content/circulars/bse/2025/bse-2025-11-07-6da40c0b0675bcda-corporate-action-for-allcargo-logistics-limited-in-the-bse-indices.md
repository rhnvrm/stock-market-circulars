## Summary

Allcargo Logistics Limited (Exchange Ticker: 532749) is demerging its International Supply Chain Business into a new entity, Allcargo Global Limited, effective from the start of trading hours on Wednesday, November 12, 2025. Shareholders will receive 1 equity share of Allcargo Global Limited for every 1 equity share held in Allcargo Logistics Limited. A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be conducted on November 12, 2025, to enable price discovery for Allcargo Logistics Limited post-demerger. Allcargo Global Limited will be added to nine BSE indices at zero price after the close of November 11, 2025, without divisor adjustment.

## Key Points

- Allcargo Logistics Limited (Ticker: 532749) is demerging its International Supply Chain Business
- New entity: Allcargo Global Limited
- Share allocation ratio: 1:1 (1 share of Allcargo Global for every 1 share of Allcargo Logistics)
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) scheduled for November 12, 2025, for price discovery
- Allcargo Global Limited to be listed on BSE at a future date (date not yet announced)
- Allcargo Global Limited will be added to 9 BSE indices at zero price without divisor adjustment
- Addition effective from November 12, 2025 (after close of November 11, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action under an approved scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE Limited will conduct a Special Pre-Open Session on November 12, 2025
- Index constituents will be updated to reflect the addition of Allcargo Global Limited
- Trading members and participants should prepare for the demerger event and SPOS process
- Investors holding Allcargo Logistics shares will automatically receive Allcargo Global shares in 1:1 ratio

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Circular announcement date
- **November 11, 2025**: Last day before demerger becomes effective (close of trading)
- **November 12, 2025**: Demerger effective date (start of trading hours); Special Pre-Open Session for price discovery; Allcargo Global Limited added to BSE indices
- **Future date (TBA)**: Expected listing date of Allcargo Global Limited equity shares on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This demerger affects a company listed in multiple major BSE indices including BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE SERVICES, BSE 1000, and BSE NEXT 500. The Special Pre-Open Session will determine the new price equilibrium for Allcargo Logistics post-demerger, which may result in significant price volatility.

**Index Impact**: The addition of Allcargo Global Limited to 9 BSE indices (BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE SERVICES, BSE 1000, BSE NEXT 500, BSE 250 MICROCAP, BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%), and BSE MIDSMALLCAP) will affect index composition and performance calculations.

**Shareholder Impact**: All existing shareholders of Allcargo Logistics will receive additional shares in Allcargo Global at 1:1 ratio, effectively splitting the business value between two entities. This may require portfolio rebalancing and tax considerations.

**Trading Impact**: The SPOS mechanism ensures fair price discovery in a controlled environment before regular trading begins, minimizing potential manipulation or extreme volatility.
