## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of VERTIS INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST (Security Code: VIL-7.71%-22-12-25-PVT, ISIN: INE0KXY07018, Code: 974228) effective December 5, 2025. The suspension is due to the scheduled redemption of these debentures with a record date set for December 7, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to VIL-7.71%-22-12-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE0KXY07018)
- Security code: 974228
- Purpose: Redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Trading members must not deal in these securities from the suspension date
- Notice reference: DR-771/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 5, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No transactions should be executed in the suspended security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 5, 2025 (no dealings from this date)
- **Record Date**: December 7, 2025 (for redemption purpose)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as on the record date will receive redemption proceeds. Trading is suspended to ensure proper settlement and to prevent transactions that would not settle before the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security and ensure compliance teams are aware of the suspension.
