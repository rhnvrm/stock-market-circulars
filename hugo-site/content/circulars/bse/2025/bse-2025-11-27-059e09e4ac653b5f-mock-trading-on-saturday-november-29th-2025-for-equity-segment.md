## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, November 29th, 2025. This session provides trading members using third-party platforms or proprietary systems an opportunity to test their trading applications under various market scenarios including exceptional conditions like call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halts, and block deals. There will be no new BOLT TWS release associated with this mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for November 29th, 2025 (Saturday)
- Available for all trading members including those using third-party platforms
- Testing covers various functionalities: call auctions, risk-reduction mode, trading halts, block deals
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Feedback requested from all members by 3:00 PM on the session day

## Trading Session Schedule

**Login:** 09:15 AM - 09:45 AM

**Morning Block Deal Window:** 09:45 AM - 10:00 AM

**Pre-Open Session:**
- Order Entry: 10:00 AM - 10:08 AM (random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- Matching Period: 10:08 AM - 10:15 AM

**Continuous Trading:**
- T+1 Settlement: 10:15 AM - 01:00 PM
- T+0 Settlement: 10:15 AM - 12:30 PM

**Special Pre-Open for IPO & Re-listed Scrips:**
- Order Entry: 10:00 AM - 10:45 AM (random stoppage in last 10 minutes)
- Matching Period: 10:45 AM - 11:00 AM

**Continuous Trading for SPOS Scrips:** 11:00 AM - 01:00 PM

**Periodic Call Auction:** 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM (2 sessions of 1 hour each)

**Auction for Settlement Details:**
- Offer Entry and Matching: 11:00 AM - 11:45 AM

**Afternoon Block Deal Window:** 12:30 PM - 12:45 PM

**Closing Session:** 01:00 PM - 01:10 PM

**Post-Closing:** 01:10 PM - 01:20 PM

**Trade Modification:**
- T+1: Until 01:30 PM
- T+0: 12:45 PM onwards

- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the mock trading day
- Third-party trading platform vendors and in-house system developers should utilize this opportunity for comprehensive testing
