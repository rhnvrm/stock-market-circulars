## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 729918, ISIN: INE180C14557) issued by Capri Global Capital Ltd, effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company's proposal to undertake an early redemption (buyback) of the instrument. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: Capri Global Capital Ltd
- Scrip Code: 729918
- ISIN: INE180C14557
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (CGCL-29-7-26-CP)
- Action: Trading suspension due to early redemption/buyback
- Effective Date: December 26, 2025
- Debt Resolution Reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action initiated by Capri Global Capital Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE180C14557) from December 26, 2025
- Trading members should take note of this suspension and inform their clients holding or intending to trade this instrument
- All pending orders and positions in this Commercial Paper should be reviewed prior to suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific Commercial Paper. The early redemption provides liquidity to existing holders but removes the trading option.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding this Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE180C14557) will not be able to trade it after December 25, 2025. They should expect redemption proceeds as per the company's buyback terms.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and communicate with clients who may hold or have interest in this instrument. Minimal broader market disruption expected as this affects a single debt instrument.
