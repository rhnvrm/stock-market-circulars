## Summary

KEC International Ltd has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 75 crore (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with 90-day maturity, credit rating of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crore (1500 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,456.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730490
- Scrip ID: KIL041125
- ISIN: INE389H14LN7
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE389H14LN7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption date**: February 2, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing by KEC International Ltd for short-term financing needs. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and India Ratings) indicate strong creditworthiness. The securities are available only to institutional and high net-worth investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
