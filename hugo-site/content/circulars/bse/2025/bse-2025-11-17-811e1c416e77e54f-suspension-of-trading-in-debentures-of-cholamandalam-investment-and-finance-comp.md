## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures issued by Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Ltd effective November 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for two NCD series of Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Ltd
- First series: CIFCL-8.40%-7-12-25-NCD (ISIN: INE121A07RJ8, Code: 939014)
- Second series: CIFCL-ZC-7-1-2-25-NCD (ISIN: INE121A07RL4, Code: 939016)
- Both debentures will undergo redemption and interest payment
- Trading members must cease dealings in these instruments from the no-dealings date
- Notice issued under DR-761/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 21, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended instruments
- Ensure client communications regarding the suspension for affected debenture holders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 21, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: November 21, 2025
- **Purpose Date**: Redemption and interest payment processing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of these specific debenture series. No impact on equity trading or other debt instruments of the company.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Investors cannot trade these securities from November 21, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems are updated to prevent trading in suspended ISINs. Standard corporate action processing for debt securities with no unusual operational challenges expected.
