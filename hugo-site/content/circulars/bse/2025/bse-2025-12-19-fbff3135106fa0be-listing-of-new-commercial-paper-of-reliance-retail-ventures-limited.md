## Summary

BSE notifies trading members about the listing and admission to trading of new Commercial Paper issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective December 19, 2025, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Reliance Retail Ventures Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 34,000 instruments
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per instrument
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,531
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 170 crores (34,000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakh)
- **Scrip Code**: 730814
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL181225
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EM3
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EM3
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all transactions
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited handles issuance and payment operations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 18, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 18, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting only institutional investors and debt market participants. The A1+ rating from both CARE and CRISIL indicates highest safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt instruments need to configure their systems with the new scrip details (Code: 730814, ISIN: INE929O14EM3). For clarifications, members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).

**Investor Impact**: Low. Commercial papers are short-term debt instruments typically accessible to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals. The 90-day maturity and high credit rating make this a low-risk, short-term investment option.
