## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371) will be moved to the 'X' group with effect from December 31, 2025. This group change is a trading segment reclassification that affects how the security is monitored and traded on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371) equity shares to be included under 'X' group
- Effective date: December 31, 2025
- Trading members have been notified of this change
- Contact person: Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788) for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically includes securities that are subject to additional surveillance or have specific trading restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Take note of the group change effective December 31, 2025
- Update their systems and processes accordingly
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More for any clarifications needed regarding this change

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The move to 'X' group may result in enhanced surveillance measures and potentially different trading parameters for Samtel India Ltd. shares. Investors and trading members should be aware of any additional requirements or restrictions that may apply to securities in the 'X' group. This reclassification could impact liquidity and trading activity in the scrip.
