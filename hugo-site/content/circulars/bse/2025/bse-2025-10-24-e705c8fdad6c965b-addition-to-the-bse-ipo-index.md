## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of MIDWEST LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544587) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE on Friday, October 24, 2025, and will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, October 28, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- MIDWEST LIMITED (ticker: 544587) listed on BSE on October 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index on October 28, 2025
- Addition becomes effective at market open on the specified date
- Corporate action file updated to reflect this change
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution following a new IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: MIDWEST LIMITED listing on BSE
- **October 28, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index addition following an IPO listing. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to incorporate MIDWEST LIMITED into their portfolios. The announcement provides advance notice allowing index fund managers to adjust their holdings accordingly.
