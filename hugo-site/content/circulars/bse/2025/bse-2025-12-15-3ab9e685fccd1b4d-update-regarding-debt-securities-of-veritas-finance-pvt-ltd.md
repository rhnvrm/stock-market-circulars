## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of VERITAS FINANCE Pvt Ltd, continuing from notice 20251104-24 dated November 4, 2025. The scrip code 974008 will be available for trading with revised terms effective December 16, 2025, including a significant coupon rate reduction from 10.35% to 8.50% and a new ISIN number.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974008 will continue trading with revised terms
- Old ISIN INE448U07208 replaced with new ISIN INE448U07323
- Coupon rate reduced from 10.35% to 8.50%
- Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name changed from 1035VFL28 to 850VFL28
- Scrip Long Name updated from VFL-10.35%-23-06-28-PVT to VFL-8.50%-23-06-28-PVT
- This is a continuation of previous notice dated November 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational update to existing debt security terms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised terms before trading commences on December 16, 2025
- All trading systems must be updated with new ISIN, coupon rate, and naming conventions
- Trading Members requiring clarification should contact Ms. Bhairavi Bondale at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, December 16, 2025 - Revised terms become effective for trading
- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Previous Notice**: November 4, 2025 (Notice number 20251104-24)

## Impact Assessment

**Debt Holders**: Significant impact due to coupon rate reduction from 10.35% to 8.50%, representing approximately 18% decrease in interest payments.

**Trading Members**: Operational impact requiring system updates for ISIN, scrip identifiers, and coupon rate information before December 16, 2025.

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects specific debt security trading and valuation. Existing holders of VERITAS FINANCE debt securities will experience reduced interest income.

**Contact**: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, and Ms. Bhairavi Bondale are designated contacts for queries.
