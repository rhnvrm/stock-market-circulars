## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Capital Services Ltd on private placement basis with effect from December 17, 2025. The instrument comprises 300 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 15 Crore) with ISIN INE296G14602, credit rating CRISIL A1+, and will trade on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoot Capital Services Ltd
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: 300 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000 = Rs. 15 Crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730789
- **Scrip ID**: MCSL161225
- **Detail Name**: MCSL-11-3-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE296G14602
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,563.70 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for a new debt security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the Commercial Paper only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296G14602
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Settlement and trading procedures as applicable to BSE Debt segment instruments apply

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 85 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard short-term debt instrument listing in the BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Paper is rated CRISIL A1+ indicating strong credit quality. Trading is restricted to institutional participants in the debt segment with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 Lakhs. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. Provides additional short-term debt trading opportunity for debt market participants.
