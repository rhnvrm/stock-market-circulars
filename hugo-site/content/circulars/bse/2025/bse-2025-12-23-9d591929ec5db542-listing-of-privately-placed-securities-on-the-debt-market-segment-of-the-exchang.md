## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on private placement basis by six companies on the debt market segment effective December 23, 2025. The securities will be traded only in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial papers totaling Rs. 1,000 crores on BSE debt segment
- All securities to be traded only in demat form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Contact: Debt department at 22728352/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications
