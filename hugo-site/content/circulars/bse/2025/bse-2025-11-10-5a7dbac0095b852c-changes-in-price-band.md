## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 18 securities effective November 11, 2025. The price bands have been tightened to levels ranging from 2% to 10%, restricting the maximum daily price movement for these scrips. This is a surveillance measure to manage volatility and protect investor interests.

## Key Points

- 18 securities affected by price band revisions
- Effective date: November 11, 2025
- Price bands set at 2%, 5%, or 10% depending on the security
- Notice Number: 20251110-48
- Issued by BSE Surveillance Department
- Trading members notified of changes

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands have been revised from existing levels as a surveillance measure. The tighter bands restrict daily price movement to protect against excessive volatility. Three stocks (Maitri Enterprises, Titan Intech, and Vaxfab Enterprises) have the most restrictive 2% band, while four stocks have 10% bands.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with revised price bands from November 11, 2025
- All trades must be executed within the specified price band limits
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will limit daily price volatility for these 18 securities. Traders and investors should note that price movements will be restricted to the specified percentages, which may impact liquidity and trading strategies. The measure is designed to curb excessive speculation and protect investor interests in these securities that may be experiencing unusual trading activity.
