## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective December 12, 2025. The T-Bills have varying maturities of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days. Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each security's maturity date.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills to be listed on BSE's Debt Market Segment
- Listed under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category
- Market lot size of 1 for all three securities
- Trading commences December 12, 2025
- Securities not available for trading two working days before maturity

## Securities Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenure |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|--------|
| 805154 | IN002025X372 | 91TB120326 | March 12, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805155 | IN002025Y370 | 182TB11626 | June 11, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805156 | IN002025Z377 | 364T101226 | December 10, 2026 | 364 days |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing effective date of December 12, 2025
- Trading members must ensure these securities are not traded two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- The two-day restriction excludes bank holidays from the calculation

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 12, 2025
- **91-Day T-Bill Maturity**: March 12, 2026
- **182-Day T-Bill Maturity**: June 11, 2026
- **364-Day T-Bill Maturity**: December 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of government treasury bills, which are standard money market instruments. The listing expands the available debt securities for institutional and retail investors seeking short-term government-backed investments. No impact on equity markets or specific companies. Trading members have standard procedures in place for handling T-Bill listings.
