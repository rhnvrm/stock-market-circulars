## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Aequs Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544634 and trading symbol AEQUS with ISIN number INE947N01017. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Aequs Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544634
- Trading Symbol: AEQUS
- ISIN Number: INE947N01017
- Specific listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued to all trading members of the Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems to handle trading in this new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This listing will provide investors with access to trade Aequs Limited equity shares on BSE.
