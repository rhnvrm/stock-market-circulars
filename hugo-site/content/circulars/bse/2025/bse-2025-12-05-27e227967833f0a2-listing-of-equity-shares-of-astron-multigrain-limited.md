## Summary

ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a). The company has total equity shares of 8,600,000 with promoter and promoter group holding 66.05% (5,679,940 shares held by 4 shareholders) and public holding 33.95% (2,920,060 shares held by 644 shareholders). The company has confirmed no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 8,600,000
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 66.05% (5,679,940 shares) held by 4 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 33.95% (2,920,060 shares) held by 644 shareholders
- All shares are fully paid up equity shares
- 60 shares (0.0021%) in locked-in category
- Total dematerialized shares: 8,600,000
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a mandatory shareholding pattern filing under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 - Regulation 31(1)(a). The Listed Entity has submitted the required declarations regarding various categories of shares and securities.

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with no material impact on trading or market operations. The shareholding pattern shows standard promoter and public distribution with promoter holding above minimum public shareholding requirements. No encumbrances, pledges, or other concerns are indicated in the filing.
