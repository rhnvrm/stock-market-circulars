## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 26,06,203 new equity shares of Windsor Machines Limited (Scrip Code: 522029) issued on a preferential basis to promoters. The shares are listed and permitted for trading with effect from Monday, December 29, 2025. All shares are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 26,06,203 equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 189.85/-
- Issue price: Rs. 191.85/- per share
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: October 7, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE052A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 84478319 to 87084521

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 26,06,203 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in requirements
- Shares cannot be traded or transferred until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry:** June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal immediate impact as all shares are locked in until June 2027 and cannot be traded during this period. The preferential issue to promoters increases their stake in the company. The issue represents approximately 26 lakh shares at a price of Rs. 191.85, totaling approximately Rs. 50 crores in capital raised. Trading members should note these shares in their systems but expect no trading activity until post lock-in period.
