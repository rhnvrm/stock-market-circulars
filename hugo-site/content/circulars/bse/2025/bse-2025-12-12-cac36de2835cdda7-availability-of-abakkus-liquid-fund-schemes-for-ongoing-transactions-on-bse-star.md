## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND schemes for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) Platform. A total of 10 schemes are now available, comprising both Direct and Regular plans with various Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options including Growth, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Quarterly reinvestment plans.

## Key Points

- 10 ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND schemes are now available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- 5 Direct Plan variants available with different IDCW options
- 5 Regular Plan variants available with different IDCW options
- All schemes are categorized under LIQUID fund category
- Each scheme has a unique ISIN for identification
- IDCW options include: Growth, Daily Reinvestment, Weekly Reinvestment, Monthly Reinvestment, and Quarterly Reinvestment

## Scheme Details

**Direct Plans:**
1. Growth (ISIN: INF2JJD01029)
2. Daily IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01011)
3. Monthly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01037)
4. Quarterly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01052)
5. Weekly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01045)

**Regular Plans:**
1. Growth (ISIN: INF2JJD01078)
2. Daily IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01060)
3. Monthly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01086)
4. Quarterly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01102)
5. Weekly IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2JJD01094)
