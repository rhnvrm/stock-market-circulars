## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 500 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, listed under Scrip Code 730674 (FFSL281125) with ISIN INE007N14EL2. Trading commenced on December 1, 2025, in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 500 units of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730674, Scrip ID: FFSL281125
- ISIN: INE007N14EL2
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Federal Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Securities must be traded under ISIN INE007N14EL2

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 28, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment affecting only debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members dealing in debt instruments need to note the instrument specifications for trading purposes. Standard procedures apply for dematerialized trading.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors participating in commercial paper markets. The instrument has high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating strong short-term credit quality.
