## Summary

BSE has announced that EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective from December 31, 2025. The reopening includes facilities for Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP). This notice references previous Notice No. 20251205-58 regarding the fund's availability.

## Key Points

- EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants will be available
- Facilities include: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 31, 2025
- Scheme category: DEBT
- Two plan options: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF754K01WD0) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF754K01VZ5)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of specific mutual fund schemes on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can resume transactions in the specified schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reopening**: December 31, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: No. 20251205-58

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors dealing with EDELWEISS SILVER ETF FUND OF FUND schemes.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors of these schemes, as they can now conduct ongoing transactions including systematic investment and withdrawal plans.

**Operational Impact**: Members of BSE StAR MF Platform need to enable these schemes for client transactions starting December 31, 2025. The availability of both subscription and redemption with systematic plan facilities provides full operational flexibility to investors.
