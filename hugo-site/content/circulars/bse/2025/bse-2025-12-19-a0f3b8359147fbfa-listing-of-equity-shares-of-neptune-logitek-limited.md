## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544663, symbol NEPLOG, and ISIN number INE1NQ501012. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: NEPTUNE LOGITEK LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544663
- Trading Symbol: NEPLOG
- ISIN Number: INE1NQ501012
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date notification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement for an SME segment company. The impact is limited to trading members who need to be aware of the new scrip for future trading once the listing date is confirmed. No immediate market or operational impact as the actual listing will occur at a later date to be specified.
