## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading approval of 11,89,259 new equity shares of HLE Glascoat Limited (Scrip Code: 522215) with effect from Tuesday, December 09, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Amalgamation between HLE Glascoat Limited (Transferee Company) and Kinam Enterprise Private Limited (Transferor Company) under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares:** 11,89,259 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 2/- per share
- **ISIN:** INE461D01028
- **Scrip Code:** 522215
- **Distinguished Numbers:** 68265481 to 69454739
- **Date of Allotment:** September 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** December 09, 2025
- **Purpose:** Scheme of Amalgamation between HLE Glascoat Limited and Kinam Enterprise Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard listing pursuant to corporate amalgamation approved under Companies Act, 2013 provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities available for trading from December 09, 2025
- No additional compliance requirements for market participants
- Standard trading rules and settlement procedures apply to these newly listed shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** September 27, 2025
- **Circular Date:** December 08, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine corporate action involving the listing of shares from an amalgamation scheme. The addition of 11,89,259 shares to the existing equity base represents a corporate restructuring activity.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members need to be aware of the additional shares available for trading. No changes to trading mechanisms or procedures.

**Investor Impact:** The amalgamation consolidates Kinam Enterprise Private Limited into HLE Glascoat Limited, with shareholders of the transferor company receiving shares in the transferee company as per the approved scheme.
