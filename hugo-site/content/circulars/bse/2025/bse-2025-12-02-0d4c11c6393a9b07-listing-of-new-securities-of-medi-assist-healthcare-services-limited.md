## Summary

BSE has listed 37,01,000 equity shares of Medi Assist Healthcare Services Limited (Scrip Code: 544088) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, December 3, 2025. All shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- 37,01,000 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 530/-
- Issue price: Rs. 535/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE456Z01021
- Distinctive numbers: 70649863 to 74350862
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. All 37,01,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 3, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This preferential issue represents a capital raise for Medi Assist Healthcare Services Limited. The lock-in period until June 2026 ensures these shares will not create immediate selling pressure. The shares rank equally with existing equity, maintaining uniform shareholder rights. Limited market impact expected as this is a standard preferential allotment procedure.
