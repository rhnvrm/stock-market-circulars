## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading commencement of 13,60,000 new equity shares of Vikram Kamat's Hospitality Limited (Scrip Code: 539659) effective from Monday, December 15, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 65/- on a preferential basis to non-promoters and are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 13,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 65/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 75/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: October 30, 2025
- Trading commencement date: December 15, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE564S01019
- Distinctive numbers: 15775866 to 17135865

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- All 13,60,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in period
- Lock-in period expires on June 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 15, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action involving preferential allotment to non-promoters. The market impact is expected to be minimal due to:

- Relatively small issue size of 13.6 lakh shares
- Complete lock-in of all allotted shares until June 30, 2026, preventing immediate market supply
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining equal rights structure
- Standard preferential allotment transaction with no unusual terms
