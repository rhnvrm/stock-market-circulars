## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 4,00,000 new equity shares of Jyot International Marketing Limited (Scrip Code: 542544) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, December 15, 2025. These shares were allotted at a premium and are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 40/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE043R01016
- Distinctive Numbers: 3221501 to 3621500
- Date of Allotment: October 21, 2025
- Trading commencement: December 15, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading commencement from December 15, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 15, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment of 4,00,000 shares represents a relatively small issuance for Jyot International Marketing Limited. All newly issued shares are subject to lock-in until August 30, 2026, which means they cannot be traded until that date despite being listed. The impact is limited to the specific company and its shareholders, with no broader market implications.
