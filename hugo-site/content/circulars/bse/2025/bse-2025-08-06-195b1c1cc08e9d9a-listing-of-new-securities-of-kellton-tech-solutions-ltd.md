## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 56,32,900 new equity shares of Kellton Tech Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 519602) issued through Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs) conversion. The shares will be available for trading from August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- 56,32,900 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences on Thursday, August 7, 2025
- ISIN Number: INE164B01030
- Scrip Code: 519602

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for securities issued through FCCB conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply to new shares
- Shares subject to existing compliance framework for equity securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 4, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 7, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The conversion represents significant equity dilution for existing shareholders of Kellton Tech Solutions Ltd. The addition of over 56 lakh shares to the tradeable float may impact stock liquidity and price dynamics. Investors should consider the dilution effect when evaluating their positions in the company.
