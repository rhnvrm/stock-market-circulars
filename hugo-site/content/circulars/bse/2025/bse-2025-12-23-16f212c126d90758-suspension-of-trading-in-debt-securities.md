## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251223-7 (DR-784/2025-2026) suspending trading in debentures of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 24, 2025, as the securities are scheduled to mature on December 26, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Earlysalary Services Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 975277
- ISIN: INE01YL07235
- Security Details: ESPLD-11.75%-26-12-25-PVT
- Interest Rate: 11.75%
- Maturity Date: December 26, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective: December 24, 2025
- Notice Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 24, 2025
- No new trades should be executed for ISIN INE01YL07235 after the suspension date
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 24, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **December 26, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects a single privately placed debenture issue. The suspension is a routine procedural step for maturing debt securities. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds upon maturity. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations. Only holders of this specific ISIN are affected.
