## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation for the name change of Sylph Technologies Limited to Sylph Industries Limited, effective October 24, 2025. The company, originally incorporated as Majjarrrsha Housing Finance Limited, is a company limited by shares with CIN L36100MP1992PLC007102. The certificate confirms that the name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Sylph Technologies Limited** to **Sylph Industries Limited**
- Effective date: **October 24, 2025**
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): **L36100MP1992PLC007102**
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company originally incorporated as Majjarrrsha Housing Finance Limited
- Registered office: Office No. 517, Sky Corporate Park, Scheme No. 78, A.B. Road, Vijay Nagar, Indore-452010, Madhya Pradesh
- Certificate digitally signed by Prerna Panwar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change processed under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is **obligated to display the old name (Sylph Technologies Limited) for a period of two years** along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)
- The name change does not affect existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date of name change (Certificate of Incorporation issued)
- **October 24, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name
- **December 16, 2025**: BSE circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update its corporate documentation, letterheads, and public communications to reflect the new name while displaying the old name for two years as required.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new name.
