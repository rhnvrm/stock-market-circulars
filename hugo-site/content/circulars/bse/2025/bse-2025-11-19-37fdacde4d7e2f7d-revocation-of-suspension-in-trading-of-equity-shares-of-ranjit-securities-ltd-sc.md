## Summary

BSE has announced the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Ranjit Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531572). This circular enables the resumption of normal trading activities for the company's equity shares on the BSE platform.

## Key Points

- Company: Ranjit Securities Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531572
- Action: Revocation of trading suspension
- Trading Status: Normal trading can resume
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading suspension previously imposed on Ranjit Securities Ltd. equity shares has been lifted. The company's shares are now eligible for normal trading operations on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can resume trading in Ranjit Securities Ltd. equity shares
- All trades will be subject to standard BSE trading rules and regulations
- Investors should exercise due diligence before trading

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 19, 2025
- Effective Date: As per BSE notification

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of suspension has a moderate impact on market operations. Shareholders and investors in Ranjit Securities Ltd. can now trade the company's equity shares through normal market mechanisms. This restores liquidity and allows investors to adjust their positions as needed. Market participants should monitor the stock's trading activity following the suspension revocation.
