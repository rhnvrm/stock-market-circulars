## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as TIRUPATI TYRES LTD. The company's name has been officially changed to TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED with effect from September 29, 2025. The company, which was originally incorporated as TIRUPATI TYRES PRIVATE LIMITED, is a company limited by shares with CIN: L46209MH1988PLC285197.

## Key Points

- Former Name: TIRUPATI TYRES LTD
- New Name: TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED
- Original Name at Incorporation: TIRUPATI TYRES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L46209MH1988PLC285197
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Prerna Panwar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered Address: Unit No 606, Reliables Pride, Anand Nagar, opp Heera Panna, Jogeshwari West, Mumbai- 400102, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative change under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29. The certificate confirms the formal approval of the name change by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs through the Central Processing Centre.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (TIRUPATI TYRES LTD) and new name (TIRUPATI INNOVAR LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- Effective Date of Name Change: September 29, 2025
- Certificate Issue Date: September 29, 2025
- Old Name Display Requirement: Two years from September 29, 2025 (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change is purely administrative and does not alter the company's legal structure, shareholding pattern, or business operations. All existing contracts, rights, obligations, and liabilities continue unchanged under the new name. Investors and stakeholders should note the new name for future correspondence and verification purposes.
