## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the resumption of subscription facility for Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund. This allows investors to resume making fresh investments in this mutual fund scheme.

## Key Points

- The subscription facility for Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund has been resumed
- Investors can now make fresh subscriptions to this fund
- This is a fund of fund that invests in Tata Silver ETF
- The circular is operational in nature and does not indicate any regulatory changes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard market operations update.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for market participants and investors.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 24, 2025
- Effective immediately upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is limited to investors and potential investors in the Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund. The resumption of subscriptions suggests that any previous temporary suspension has been lifted, allowing normal investment operations to continue. This has no broader market impact and is specific to this particular mutual fund product.
