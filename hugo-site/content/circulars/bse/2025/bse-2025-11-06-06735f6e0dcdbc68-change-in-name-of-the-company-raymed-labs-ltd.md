## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Raymed Labs Limited. The company name has been officially changed to Cresanto Global Limited with effect from April 30, 2025. This administrative change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Raymed Labs Limited** to **Cresanto Global Limited**
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24111UP1992PLC014240
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Registered address: C-273, C Block, Sector 63, Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar-201301, Uttar Pradesh, India
- Certificate digitally signed by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action involving name change approved under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is **obligatory required to display the old name (Raymed Labs Limited) for a period of two years** along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: April 30, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: April 30, 2025 (digitally signed at 11:15:45 IST)
- **Old Name Display Period**: April 30, 2025 to April 30, 2027 (2 years)

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Share structure or shareholding pattern
- Trading operations or stock listing status
- Legal obligations or contractual commitments

Investors and stakeholders should note the new name for correspondence and verification purposes. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in corporate identification.
