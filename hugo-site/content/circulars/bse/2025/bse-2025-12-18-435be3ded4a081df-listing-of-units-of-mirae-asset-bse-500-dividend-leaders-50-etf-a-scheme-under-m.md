## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Mirae Asset BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 ETF units effective December 19, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme that tracks the BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 Total Return Index. A total of 1,884,753 units have been allotted at an issue price of Rs. 35.32 per unit with a face value of Rs. 10.00 per unit.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544661, ISIN: INF769K01PY8
- Scrip ID on BOLT System: DIVIDEND
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Date of Allotment: December 15, 2025
- Trading Start Date: December 19, 2025
- Investment Manager: Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited
- Registrar: KFIN Technologies Limited
- Five market makers appointed including Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, East India Securities Limited, Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited, Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited, and IRage Broking Services LLP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure following SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure units are traded only in dematerialized form
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- No physical certificate trading permitted

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **December 19, 2025**: Trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine ETF listing that adds a new dividend-focused investment option tracking BSE 500 Dividend Leaders 50 Total Return Index. The listing provides investors with a passive investment vehicle focused on high dividend-yielding stocks from the BSE 500 universe. Five market makers have been appointed to ensure adequate liquidity.
