## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Ananya Finance For Inclusive Growth Private Limited. The securities under Scrip Code 976510 will undergo revision in terms, converting from unsecured to secured NCDs with a new ISIN, effective from December 31, 2025. This is a continuation of notice number 20251212-48 dated December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976510 will be available for trading with revised terms from December 31, 2025
- Security mode changing from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs
- ISIN changing from INE774L08022 to INE774L07099
- Trading members should note these changes for trading operations

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities are being upgraded in security status:
- **Previous Status**: Unsecured Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)
- **Revised Status**: Secured Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)

The ISIN identification has been updated:
- **Old ISIN**: INE774L08022
- **New ISIN**: INE774L07099

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE774L07099)
- Trading members should note the change in security mode from unsecured to secured
- For further details, trading members may contact:
  - Ms. Pooja Trivedi: +91 22 2272 8844
  - Email: debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 31, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Previous Related Notice**: December 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20251212-48)

## Impact Assessment

This change represents a positive development for NCD holders as the securities are being upgraded from unsecured to secured status, potentially improving credit quality and investor protection. Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated with the new ISIN before the effective date to ensure smooth trading operations. The change affects all existing holders of these debt securities under the old ISIN INE774L08022.
