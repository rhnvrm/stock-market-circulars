## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development (NBFID) on private placement basis, effective November 17, 2025. The securities comprise a 5-year bond with 6.86% interest rate and a 15-year bond with 7.15% interest rate, both carrying AAA credit ratings from CRISIL and ICRA.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment
- Security 1: ₹166 crore issuance (1,660 bonds) maturing November 13, 2030 at 6.86% p.a.
- Security 2: ₹246 crore issuance (2,460 bonds) maturing November 13, 2040 at 7.15% p.a.
- Both securities allotted on November 13, 2025
- Face value and issue price of ₹1,00,000 per bond for both securities
- Credit rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/STABLE for both securities
- Annual interest payment frequency
- No put/call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market lot size of 1 unit
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds ₹412 crore of AAA-rated debt securities to the BSE Debt segment. The securities are available only to institutional investors given the high face value of ₹1 lakh per bond. The private placement nature and debt segment focus limit broader market implications. Trading members participating in debt markets should note the new instruments for potential trading opportunities.
