## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kogta Financial (India) Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only under scrip code 977263 (ISIN: INE192U07376).

## Key Points

- Quantity: 12,000 debentures with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977263, Scrip ID: 95KFIL27
- Detail Name: KFIL-9.5%-30-10-27-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE192U07376 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A+/Positive
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,729
- Date of Allotment: November 27, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.5% per annum (payable annually)
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number (INE192U07376)
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on October 30 (2026 to 2027)
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds Rs. 120 crore worth of debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The securities carry a CARE A+/Positive rating indicating adequate safety for investors. The issuance is on a private placement basis with a limited quantity of 12,000 debentures. Impact is confined to Kogta Financial (India) Limited and its private placement investors. No implications for broader market operations or trading members beyond standard debt segment procedures.
