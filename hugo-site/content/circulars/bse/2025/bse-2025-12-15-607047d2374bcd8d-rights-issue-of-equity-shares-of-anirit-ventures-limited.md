## Summary

ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED (BSE Code: 530705) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 18, 2025. The company will issue 2 equity shares of Rs. 10 each for every 1 equity share held. The shares will be offered at a premium of Rs. 23 per share (total issue price Rs. 33 per share). Trading will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-780/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 2 new equity shares for every 1 existing equity share held
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Premium: Rs. 23 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 33 per share
- Payment structure: Rs. 23 on application, balance Rs. 10 on subsequent call(s)
- Ex-rights trading begins: December 18, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-780/2025-2026
- Scrip code: 530705

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in ANIRIT VENTURES LIMITED equity shares are conducted on an ex-rights basis from December 18, 2025
- Members must note the record date and settlement details for proper trade execution
- Shareholders as of the record date (December 18, 2025) will be eligible for the rights issue

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-780/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The rights issue will result in dilution for non-participating shareholders. The 2:1 ratio is significant and will substantially increase the company's equity base.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders need to decide whether to subscribe to the rights issue. The payment structure allows partial payment on application (Rs. 23) with the balance (Rs. 10) payable on call, providing some flexibility.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members must adjust their systems for ex-rights trading from December 18, 2025. The stock will trade without rights entitlement from this date, which typically results in a price adjustment.
