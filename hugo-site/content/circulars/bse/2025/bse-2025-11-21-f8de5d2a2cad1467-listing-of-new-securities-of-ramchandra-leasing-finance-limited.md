## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,00,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Ramchandra Leasing & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 538540) issued on preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, November 24, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 3 crore equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1.26/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 2.26 per share
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: September 2, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 51162001 to 81162000
- ISIN: INE516P01015
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences: November 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 2, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 24, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2027 (for all 3 crore shares)

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as all 3 crore preferentially allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 30, 2027. These shares cannot be traded until the lock-in expires. The issuance to promoters on preferential basis is a routine corporate action that increases the total equity base of the company while maintaining promoter commitment through the extended lock-in period.
