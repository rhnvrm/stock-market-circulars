## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Earlysalary Services Private Limited effective December 04, 2025, as the securities are scheduled to mature on December 05, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 975669
- ISIN: INE01YL07284
- Security: ESPL-11%-5-12-25-PVT (11% debenture)
- Maturity Date: December 05, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from December 04, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE01YL07284) from December 04, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and comply accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 03, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 05, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debt instrument from Earlysalary Services Private Limited. This is a routine suspension prior to maturity with no broader market implications. The suspension ensures orderly settlement as the debentures reach their scheduled maturity date.
