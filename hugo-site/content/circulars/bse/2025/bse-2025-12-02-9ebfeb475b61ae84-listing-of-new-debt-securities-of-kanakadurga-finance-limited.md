## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Kanakadurga Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective 02 December 2025. The listing comprises 2,500 secured redeemable non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 11.55% per annum payable monthly, maturing on 28 November 2027. The securities are rated CARE BBB/STABLE and will trade only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity listed: 2,500 debentures
- Scrip Code: 977333 | Scrip ID: 1155KFL27
- ISIN: INE104W07229
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Interest rate: 11.55% per annum
- Interest payment: Monthly
- Date of allotment: 28 November 2025
- Maturity date: 28 November 2027
- Credit rating: CARE BBB/STABLE
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE104W07229
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- Any clarifications may be sought from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: 02 December 2025
- Date of allotment: 28 November 2025
- Interest payment frequency: Monthly
- Redemption date: 28 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to private placement debt securities with limited quantity (2,500 units). The impact is confined to:

- **Debt market participants**: Additional trading opportunity in BBB-rated corporate debt with monthly interest payouts
- **Institutional investors**: Access to medium-term debt instrument with 11.55% yield
- **Kanakadurga Finance Limited**: Enhanced access to debt capital markets and improved liquidity for investors

The high face value (Rs. 1 lakh) indicates these securities are targeted at institutional and high-net-worth investors rather than retail participants. The CARE BBB/STABLE rating suggests moderate credit quality with adequate safety for debt servicing.
