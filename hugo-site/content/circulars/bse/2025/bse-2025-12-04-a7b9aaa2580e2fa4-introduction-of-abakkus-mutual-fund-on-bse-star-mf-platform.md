## Summary

ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND has been activated on BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 04, 2025. The platform will accept transactions from NRI and Minor clients under ABAKKUS Mutual Fund schemes, subject to country restrictions. Two New Fund Offer (NFO) schemes - ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND and ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND - will be available for order routing from December 08, 2025.

## Key Points

- ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND activated on BSE StAR MF Platform from December 04, 2025
- NRI and Minor client transactions accepted through the platform
- Investment applications not accepted from investors belonging to Democratic Republic of Korea, Iran, and Myanmar
- Two NFO schemes to be activated for order routing from December 08, 2025
- Platform accessible to BSE StAR MF members for transaction processing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform activation notice for a new mutual fund house.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must not accept NRI investment applications from Democratic Republic of Korea, Iran, and Myanmar
- Transactions for NRI and Minor clients can be processed through BSE StAR MF platform for ABAKKUS schemes
- Members should ensure compliance with country-specific investment restrictions

## Important Dates

- **December 04, 2025**: ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND activation on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **December 08, 2025**: NFO schemes (ABAKKUS FLEXI CAP FUND and ABAKKUS LIQUID FUND) available for order routing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine platform activation expanding the mutual fund options available on BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low - BSE StAR MF members gain access to distribute two new schemes from ABAKKUS MUTUAL FUND. Standard platform procedures apply with specific country restrictions for NRI investors.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking new mutual fund options, particularly the FLEXI CAP and LIQUID fund categories. NRI and Minor investors can participate subject to country exclusions.
