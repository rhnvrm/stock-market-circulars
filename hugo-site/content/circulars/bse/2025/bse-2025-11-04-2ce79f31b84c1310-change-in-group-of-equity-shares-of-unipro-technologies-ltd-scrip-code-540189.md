## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Unipro Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 540189) will be moved to the 'X' group with effect from November 11, 2025. The X group classification typically indicates securities that are under surveillance or have specific trading restrictions.

## Key Points

- Company: Unipro Technologies Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 540189
- Action: Inclusion under 'X' group
- Effective Date: November 11, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be classified under the 'X' group, which typically applies to:
- Securities under surveillance measures
- Companies with compliance issues
- Stocks requiring enhanced monitoring

This reclassification may result in modified trading conditions including potential restrictions on settlement, margin requirements, or trading frequency.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the group change effective November 11, 2025
- Inform clients about the reclassification and its implications
- Comply with any additional margin or trading requirements applicable to X group securities
- Contact BSE Listing Compliance for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Movement to X group typically reduces liquidity as many institutional investors and retail traders avoid such securities due to higher risk perception and potential trading restrictions.

**Trading Impact**: The reclassification may lead to:
- Reduced trading volumes
- Wider bid-ask spreads
- Higher margin requirements
- Potential price volatility
- Limited investor participation

**Investor Considerations**: Existing and potential investors should exercise increased caution and conduct thorough due diligence before trading in this security.
