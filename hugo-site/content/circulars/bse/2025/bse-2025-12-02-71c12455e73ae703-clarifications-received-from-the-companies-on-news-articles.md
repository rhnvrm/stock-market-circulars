## Summary

BSE issued this notice to inform members and investors about clarifications sought from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) regarding news articles published in Economic Times. The exchange routinely seeks such clarifications to verify accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels. The company's reply has been received and is attached to the circular.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd regarding news articles
- News article source: Economic Times (economictimes.indiatimes.com)
- Company's reply has been received and attached
- Notice issued by Surveillance Department
- Scrip Code: 532822 (Vodafone Idea Ltd)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond to BSE's clarification requests regarding news articles
- Companies must provide accurate information to verify media reports
- Replies must be submitted to the exchange for dissemination to market participants

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 2, 2025
- **Notice Number:** 20251202-57

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The impact depends on the content of the news article and the company's clarification. Such circulars help maintain market transparency by verifying information published in media sources.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Investors can review the company's official response to news reports, helping them make informed decisions based on verified information rather than unconfirmed media reports.

**Operational Impact:** Low - This is part of routine surveillance activities. The company has already submitted its response, indicating no operational delays or issues.
