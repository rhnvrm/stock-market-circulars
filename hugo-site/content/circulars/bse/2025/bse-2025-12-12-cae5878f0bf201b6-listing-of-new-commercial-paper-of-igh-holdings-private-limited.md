## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The instrument consists of 7000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, issued on private placement basis. The papers are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ and will mature on March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 7000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 491,749/- (for 5000 qty) and Rs. 491,592.50/- (for 2000 qty)
- **Scrip Code**: 730757
- **Scrip ID**: IHPL121225
- **ISIN**: INE02FN14713
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialised form
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE02FN14713
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for trading these securities

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with limited market impact. The listing adds a new short-term debt instrument (3-month commercial paper) to the BSE Debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. The impact is confined to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. There is no impact on equity markets or broader retail investors.
