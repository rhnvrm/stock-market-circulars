## Summary

KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD has announced part redemption of four series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) with a record date of November 28, 2025. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face values effective from the same date under settlement number DR-766/2025-2026. This corporate action affects privately placed debt securities across different maturity profiles.

## Key Points

- Four series of debentures are subject to part redemption
- Record date fixed as November 28, 2025
- Trading with reduced face values begins November 28, 2025
- All affected securities are privately placed non-convertible debentures
- Settlement number: DR-766/2025-2026

## Debenture Details

### KPDL-ZC-17-4-33-PVT (INE094I07049, Code: 974771)
- **New Face Value:** Rs. 65,345 per debenture
- **Security Type:** Zero Coupon

### KPDL-21-12-33-PVT (INE094I07064, Code: 975276)
- **New Face Value:** Rs. 63,705 per debenture

### KPDL-ZC-20-9-34-PVT (INE094I07072, Code: 976030)
- **New Face Value:** Rs. 68,438 per debenture
- **Security Type:** Zero Coupon

### KPDL-15-10-35-PVT (INE094I07080, Code: 977231)
- **New Face Value:** Rs. 99,580 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing debt securities framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for all four debenture series
- Trades executed from November 28, 2025 onwards must reflect the new reduced face values
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face values effective from the specified date
- Settlement processes must account for the part redemption amounts

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** November 28, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-766/2025-2026
- **Circular Issue Date:** November 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**For Debenture Holders:**
- Debenture holders on record as of November 28, 2025 will receive part redemption proceeds
- Outstanding investment amount will reduce to the new face values
- Different reduction amounts across the four series reflect varying coupon structures and maturity profiles

**For Trading Members:**
- System updates required to reflect new face values in trading and settlement systems
- Valuation and portfolio management systems need adjustment for affected securities
- Limited trading impact expected given these are privately placed securities with potentially limited secondary market activity

**Market Impact:**
- Minimal broader market impact as these are privately placed debt securities
- Action demonstrates ongoing debt servicing by KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD
- Reduction in outstanding debt burden for the issuer company
