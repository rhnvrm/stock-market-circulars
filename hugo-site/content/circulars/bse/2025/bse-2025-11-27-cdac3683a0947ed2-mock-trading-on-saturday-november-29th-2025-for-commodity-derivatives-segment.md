## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on November 29th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems to test their applications and familiarize themselves with various market functionalities including exceptional conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS release will be implemented. All trades from this session are for testing only and will not create any real obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on November 29th, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Members can test trading applications including exceptional market conditions
- Log-in window: 09:15 AM to 10:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM
- Trade modification: 01:00 PM to 01:10 PM
- Mock trades will not attract any margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations
- No rights or liabilities will be created from mock trading
- Feedback must be submitted by 3:00 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading session for testing and familiarization purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems through ETI APIs are encouraged to participate
- Members should actively participate in the mock trading session
- Feedback must be provided by 3:00 PM on November 29th, 2025
- Contact BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) or Help Desk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **November 29th, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - 09:15 AM - 10:00 AM: Log-in window
  - 10:00 AM - 01:00 PM: Continuous trading
  - 01:00 PM - 01:10 PM: Trade modification
- **3:00 PM (same day)**: Deadline for submitting feedback

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members have an opportunity to test their trading systems and ensure readiness for actual trading. This is beneficial for identifying and resolving technical issues in a non-production environment.

**Member Impact**: Optional participation for testing purposes. Members using third-party or in-house trading systems can use this session to verify functionality and test exceptional market scenarios without any financial obligations.
