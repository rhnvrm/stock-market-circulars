## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Ambium Finserve Limited issued on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 3, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.16% per annum with monthly interest payments and will be redeemed on August 26, 2027.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Ambium Finserve Limited
- Scrip Code: 977341
- Scrip ID: 1116AFL27A
- ISIN: INE0RU307213
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Size: 20,000 units (quantity)
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000
- Securities to be traded in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0RU307213
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 2, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from January 26, 2026 to August 26, 2027
- **Date of Redemption**: August 26, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with limited market impact. The listing provides trading members access to privately placed debt instruments of Ambium Finserve Limited rated CARE BBB-. The securities offer 11.16% annual interest with monthly payments, which may attract debt investors seeking regular income. No put or call options are available on these securities.
