## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Annapurna Finance Private Limited issued on private placement basis, effective December 11, 2025. The securities comprise 26,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 10.02% p.a. interest rate and maturing on December 10, 2030.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 26,000 debentures
- **Scrip Code**: 977360
- **Scrip ID**: 1002AFPL30
- **ISIN**: INE515Q07665
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Interest Rate**: 10.02% per annum (half-yearly payment)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A-/Stable
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 10, 2030
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE515Q07665
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for additional details
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Half-yearly from June 10, 2026 to December 10, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: December 10, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine private placement debt listing announcement affecting only debt segment participants. The securities are issued by a private limited company (Annapurna Finance Private Limited) and will be traded exclusively in the debt segment. The CRISIL A-/Stable rating indicates adequate credit quality with moderate credit risk.
