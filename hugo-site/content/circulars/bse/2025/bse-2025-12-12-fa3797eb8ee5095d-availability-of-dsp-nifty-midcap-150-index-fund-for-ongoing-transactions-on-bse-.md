## Summary

BSE has notified that DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from December 15, 2025. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) across all six plan variants - Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: December 15, 2025
- All six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Reference to previous notice: 20251121-47

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transactions for a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. BSE members are informed of the availability of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 - DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors interested in DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND.

**Operational Impact**: Low. BSE members can process subscription and redemption orders for this fund starting December 15, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of DSP NIFTY MIDCAP 150 INDEX FUND who can now resume or initiate transactions including systematic investment facilities.
