## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of Titanium Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan Growth, a close-ended equity scheme under Tata Mutual Fund, effective December 17, 2025. The scheme will be listed in the "B" Group with 62,491,849 units available for trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 62,491,849 units to be listed with scrip code 544649
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit, Issue price: Rs. 10.03 per unit
- ISIN: INF277K30047
- Trading in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Investment Manager: Tata Asset Management Pvt Ltd
- Registrar: Computer Age Management Services (Private) Limited (CAMS)
- Scheme invests in equity, equity-related instruments, debt, and money market instruments with limited short exposure through derivatives
- Units will only trade in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement following SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to trade only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be in unit market lots (minimum 1 unit)
- Members must use the designated scrip ID "TSIFDG" on the BOLT system

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it is a routine listing of a mutual fund scheme. It provides liquidity options for investors in this specific Tata Mutual Fund scheme. The close-ended nature means no new subscriptions, and the listing enables secondary market trading for existing unitholders. No impact on broader equity markets or other securities.
