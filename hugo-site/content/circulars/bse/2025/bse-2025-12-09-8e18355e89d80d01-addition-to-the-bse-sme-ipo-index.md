## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544630) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company is being listed on BSE effective Tuesday, December 9, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of Wednesday, December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED (ticker: 544630) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Listing on BSE effective December 9, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 10, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251208-28

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their holdings accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: HELLOJI HOLIDAYS LIMITED listing on BSE
- **December 10, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The addition affects primarily:

- Passive funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index, which will need to add the stock to their portfolios
- Index calculation and composition
- The newly listed company gains index membership visibility

No broader market or trading impact expected. Standard index reconstitution following new listing protocol.
