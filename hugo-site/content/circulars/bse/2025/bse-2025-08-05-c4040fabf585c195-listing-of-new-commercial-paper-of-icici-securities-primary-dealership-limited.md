## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ICICI Securities Primary Dealership Limited worth Rs. 40 crores (8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 40 crores (8,000 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,292 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729961
- ISIN: INE849D14HV0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing & Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 5, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 4, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a financial services entity. The short-term nature (3 months) and private placement basis limit broader market implications. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
