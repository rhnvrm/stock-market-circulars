## Summary

360 ONE WAM Limited has listed new commercial papers worth Rs. 175 crore (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the BSE Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are now available for trading in dematerialized form effective December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total issuance: 3,500 commercial papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730704, Scrip ID: OWL41225
- ISIN: INE466L14FL1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,363.50 per unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Transactions must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under ISIN INE466L14FL1

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date:** December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Tenor:** 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are available only to institutional investors and debt market participants. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate high credit quality. The listing provides short-term funding to 360 ONE WAM Limited and offers debt market participants additional investment options in highly-rated commercial papers.
