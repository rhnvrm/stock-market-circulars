## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Dhaval Packaging Limited for public comments. The document was filed on December 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The Draft Offer Document will be available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/download/312386/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHPDPL_20251231003527.pdf
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 31, 2025 (deadline: January 21, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document must remain available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice published and public comment period begins
- **January 21, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains specifically to a single SME IPO process. It is informational in nature, allowing stakeholders and the public to review and comment on the Draft Offer Document of Dhaval Packaging Limited. The impact is confined to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in this particular SME offering. Market-wide impact is negligible.
