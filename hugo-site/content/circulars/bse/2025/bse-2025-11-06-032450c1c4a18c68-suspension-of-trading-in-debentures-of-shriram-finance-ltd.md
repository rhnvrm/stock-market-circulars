## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in specific debentures of Shriram Finance Ltd effective November 07, 2025. The company has informed the exchange that the debentures with ISIN INE721A07RF3 (Scrip Code: 974376) have been repurchased and extinguished. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: Shriram Finance Ltd
- Debenture ISIN: INE721A07RF3
- Scrip Code: 974376
- Security Name: SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT
- Action: Repurchased by company and extinguished
- Trading suspension effective: November 07, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251106-18
- Reference: DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice following debt repurchase.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (INE721A07RF3) from November 07, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- All positions and settlements related to these debentures must be closed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 07, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT). This is a routine corporate action following scheduled debt repurchase.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members holding positions in these debentures need to ensure compliance with the suspension. No new trades can be executed after November 07, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders have already been repaid as part of the repurchase process. The suspension formalizes the extinguishment of the security from the exchange platform.
