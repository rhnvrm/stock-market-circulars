## Summary

SK Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment on November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 44,500 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (total issue size of Rs. 445 crore). The debentures carry a credit rating of IND AA-/Positive and offer an interest rate of 9.21% per annum with annual interest payments.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977313
- **Scrip ID**: 921SKFL30
- **ISIN**: INE124N07788
- **Issue Size**: 44,500 units (Rs. 445 crore)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 9.21% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: IND AA-/Positive
- **Date of Allotment**: November 25, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE124N07788
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from November 25, 2026 to November 25, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: November 25, 2030
- **Tenor**: 5 years

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine private placement debt listing that provides additional investment options for debt market participants. The issue size of Rs. 445 crore is moderate and the IND AA-/Positive rating indicates good credit quality.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for institutional and high net worth investors seeking fixed income instruments with 9.21% annual returns and 5-year maturity. The minimum investment requirement is Rs. 1,00,000 per unit.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details and ensure trading occurs only in demat form.
