## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has notified members and custodians of settlement holidays for calendar year 2026. The circular lists 19 settlement holidays falling on weekdays and 4 additional holidays falling on Saturday/Sunday. This advance notice enables market participants to plan their settlement and operational activities for the upcoming year.

## Key Points

- Total of 19 settlement holidays on weekdays during 2026
- 4 additional holidays falling on Saturday/Sunday (Mahashivratri, Ramzan-Id, Independence Day/Parsi New Year, Diwali Amavasya)
- Holidays include national, religious, and regional festivals
- Notice issued on 22 December 2025 for the entire calendar year 2026
- Applicable to all members and custodians

## Important Dates

### Weekday Settlement Holidays 2026

- 26 January 2026 (Monday) - Republic Day
- 19 February 2026 (Thursday) - Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti
- 03 March 2026 (Tuesday) - Holi (Second Day)
- 19 March 2026 (Thursday) - Gudi Padwa
- 26 March 2026 (Thursday) - Ram Navami
- 31 March 2026 (Tuesday) - Mahavir Jayanti
- 01 April 2026 (Wednesday) - Annual Bank Closing
- 03 April 2026 (Friday) - Good Friday
- 14 April 2026 (Tuesday) - Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti
- 01 May 2026 (Friday) - Maharashtra Din/Buddha Pournima
- 28 May 2026 (Thursday) - Bakri ID (Id-Uz-Zuha)
- 26 June 2026 (Friday) - Moharum
- 26 August 2026 (Wednesday) - Id-E-Milad
- 14 September 2026 (Monday) - Ganesh Chaturthi
- 02 October 2026 (Friday) - Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti
- 20 October 2026 (Tuesday) - Dussera
- 10 November 2026 (Tuesday) - Diwali (Bali Pratipada)
- 24 November 2026 (Tuesday) - Guru Nanak Jayanti
- 25 December 2026 (Friday) - Christmas

### Holidays Falling on Saturday/Sunday

- 15 February 2026 (Sunday) - Mahashivratri
- 21 March 2026 (Saturday) - Ramzan-Id (Id-Ul-Fitra)
- 15 August 2026 (Saturday) - Independence Day/Parsi New Year (Shahenshahi)
- 08 November 2026 (Sunday) - Diwali Amavasya (Laxmi Pujan)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must plan settlement activities considering these holidays
- No settlement operations will be conducted by ICCL on the specified holidays
- Participants should adjust their operational calendars and client communications accordingly
- Settlement cycles should be planned to accommodate these non-working days

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All clearing and settlement activities will be suspended on these days, requiring members to plan their operations, fund management, and client servicing around these dates.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine annual announcement that market participants expect and plan for. The holiday schedule aligns with national and religious observances.

**Planning Impact**: The advance notice (issued December 2025 for entire 2026) allows sufficient time for members and custodians to update their systems, inform clients, and plan liquidity and settlement schedules effectively.
