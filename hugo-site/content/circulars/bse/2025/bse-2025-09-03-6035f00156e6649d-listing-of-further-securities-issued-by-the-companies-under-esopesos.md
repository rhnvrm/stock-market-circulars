## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 15 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), which will be available for trading from September 4, 2025. The listing includes shares from major companies across banking, healthcare, technology, and other sectors.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of approximately 5.9 million shares across all companies
- Securities will be admitted for trading effective September 4, 2025
- All listed securities have no lock-in period restrictions
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- IDFC First Bank: 1,624,470 shares (₹10 face value)
- RBL Bank: 961,067 shares (₹10 face value)
- L&T Finance: 919,920 shares (₹10 face value)
- Yes Bank: 1,083,688 shares (₹2 face value)
- FSN E-Commerce (Nykaa): 494,334 shares (₹1 face value)

**Other Notable Companies:**
- 360 ONE WAM, Federal Bank, Mankind Pharma, SIS Limited, Somany Ceramics, Suven Life Sciences, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing of employee stock option securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply to the newly listed securities
- No special compliance requirements mentioned

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date:** September 4, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** September 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents routine listing of employee stock option securities. The addition of these shares to the tradeable float may provide minor liquidity increases for the respective companies, but volumes are relatively small compared to existing share capital.
