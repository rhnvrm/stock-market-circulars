## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 12 securities effective August 6, 2025, with bands ranging from 2% to 20% based on individual security risk profiles.

## Key Points

- 12 securities affected with revised price bands
- Changes effective from August 6, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20% (least restrictive)
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions for the following securities:
- Aditya Infotech Ltd: 10%
- Bisil Plast Ltd: 2%
- Chembond Chemicals Ltd: 20%
- Dharan Infra-EPC Ltd: 2%
- Gujarat Natural Resources Ltd: 2%
- Hindustan Fluorocarbons Ltd: 5%
- Monika Alcobev Ltd: 20%
- Neil Industries Ltd: 10%
- SDC Techmedia Ltd: 5%
- Spandana Sphoorty Financial Ltd: 20%
- Viji Finance Ltd: 2%
- Vineet Laboratories Ltd: 2%

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to revised price bands
- No additional compliance actions required
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 5, 2025
- Effective Date: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Price band changes will affect daily trading volatility limits for these securities. Securities with lower bands (2%) will have more restricted price movements, while those with higher bands (20%) will allow greater price fluctuations during trading sessions.
