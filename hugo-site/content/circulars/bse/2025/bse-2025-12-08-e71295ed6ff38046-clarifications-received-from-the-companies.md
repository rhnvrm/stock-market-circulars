## Summary

BSE published clarifications from three companies - Shyam Century Ferrous Limited (scrip code 539252), Genesys International Corporation Limited (scrip code 506109), and InfoBeans Technologies Ltd (scrip code 543644) - responding to surveillance queries about unusual price or volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed they have no undisclosed price-sensitive information and attributed the movements to market forces.

## Key Points

- Shyam Century Ferrous Limited clarified no significant incident/news/development affecting security price; movements are market-driven
- Genesys International Corporation Limited responded to volume spurt query, confirming no undisclosed information under SEBI Listing Regulations
- InfoBeans Technologies Ltd addressed price movement query, confirming all disclosures made timely under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- All companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No material information withheld that may have bearing on operations/performance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance activities under existing SEBI Listing Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed price-sensitive information exists
- Ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR for material event disclosures required
- Companies must inform exchanges if any development may affect security prices

## Important Dates

- December 05, 2025: NSE and BSE sought clarification from Shyam Century Ferrous Limited
- December 06, 2025: Shyam Century Ferrous Limited responded to exchanges
- December 08, 2025: BSE sought clarifications from Genesys International and InfoBeans Technologies
- December 08, 2025: Both companies provided responses on same day

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is routine surveillance activity. All three companies confirmed no material undisclosed information exists. The clarifications provide transparency to market participants that recent price/volume movements in these securities are market-driven rather than based on fundamental company developments. No trading restrictions or further surveillance measures indicated.
