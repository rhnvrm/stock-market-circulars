## Summary

Unisem Agritech Limited has finalized the allocation of 9,36,000 equity shares to anchor investors at Rs. 65.00 per share (including share premium of Rs. 60.00) ahead of its public issue. The Board of Directors approved this allocation on December 9, 2025, in consultation with the Book Running Lead Managers.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares allocated to anchor investors: 9,36,000
- Allocation price: Rs. 65.00 per equity share (face value Rs. 5.00 + premium Rs. 60.00)
- Four anchor investors participated in the allocation
- Universal Golden Fund received the largest allocation at 32.91% (3,08,000 shares)
- Getfive Opportunity Fund - I received 34.19% (3,20,000 shares)
- Resonance Opportunities Fund and J4S Venture Fund - I each received 16.45% (1,54,000 shares)
- Company CIN: U01100KA2016P1C096390

## Anchor Investor Details

### Allocation Breakdown:
1. **Universal Golden Fund**: 3,08,000 shares (32.91%)
2. **Resonance Opportunities Fund**: 1,54,000 shares (16.45%)
3. **J4S Venture Fund - I**: 1,54,000 shares (16.45%)
4. **Getfive Opportunity Fund - I**: 3,20,000 shares (34.19%)

## Regulatory Requirements

- As per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2009 (as amended), if the final offer price discovered through book building is higher than the anchor investor allocation price of Rs. 65.00, anchor investors will be required to pay the difference by the pay-in date specified in the revised Confirmation of Allocation Note (CAN)
- Information has been disclosed to BSE and SEBI as per regulatory requirements
