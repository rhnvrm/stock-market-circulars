## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement list for Settlement No. 787 dated December 31, 2025. The settlement covers 154 scrips across 77 companies with specific quantities required for delivery obligations. The auction includes major stocks like Reliance Industries (2,150 shares), Siemens (2,250 shares), Tata Consultancy Services (757 shares), and numerous other listed companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 787
- Settlement Date: December 31, 2025
- Total scrips covered: 154 (77 companies with dual entries in most cases)
- Includes blue-chip stocks, mid-cap, and small-cap companies
- Largest quantities include: Rama Steel Tubes (268,000 shares), Suzlon Energy (72,000 shares), Vodafone Idea (33,030 shares)
- Settlement covers delivery shortages requiring auction mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine settlement circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery obligations for listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed for the specified quantities as per BSE auction rules
- Participants must ensure timely delivery of auctioned securities

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** December 31, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 787

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction settlement affects 77 companies across market capitalizations. While routine in nature, the large quantities for certain scrips (Rama Steel Tubes: 268,000 shares, Suzlon: 72,000 shares) may create temporary price pressure.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers and clearing members with shortages must arrange securities through the auction mechanism. The settlement includes high-value stocks which may require significant capital deployment.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Primarily affects institutional participants and brokers with delivery failures. Retail investors are indirectly impacted through potential price movements during auction settlement.
