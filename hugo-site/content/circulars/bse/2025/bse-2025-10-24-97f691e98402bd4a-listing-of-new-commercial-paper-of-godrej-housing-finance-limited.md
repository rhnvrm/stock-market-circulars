## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Godrej Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective October 24, 2025. The listing comprises 2000 securities with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crores. These instruments will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730430, Scrip ID: GHFL231025
- ISIN Number: INE02JD14641
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,328.50 per security
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading denomination: Standard Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02JD14641
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term debt funding for Godrej Housing Finance Limited. The instruments are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments.

**Investor Impact**: The listing provides institutional investors access to highly-rated short-term debt instruments with a 91-day maturity. The issue price of Rs. 492,328.50 against face value of Rs. 500,000 implies an annualized yield for investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can now facilitate transactions in these instruments on the BSE Debt segment with standard debt market operational procedures.
