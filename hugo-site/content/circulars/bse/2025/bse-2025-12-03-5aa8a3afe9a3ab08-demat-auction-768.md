## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 768 dated December 3, 2025. The auction includes 88 scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 89,925 shares. This is a routine settlement auction where securities that failed delivery are made available for auction to complete settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 768
- Auction Date: December 3, 2025
- Total Scrips: 88 securities listed for auction
- Sectors covered include banking, pharmaceuticals, technology, manufacturing, and infrastructure
- Quantities range from single shares to thousands of shares
- Notable large quantities: AVANCE TECHN (89,925 shares), TRESCON LIMI (46,993 shares), MANGALAM GLO (19,622 shares), HINDUSTAN CO (16,000 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate in the demat auction as per BSE auction guidelines
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction process
- Members with shortages must cover their positions through the auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 3, 2025
- Auction Settlement No.: 768

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. Demat auctions are routine settlement mechanisms to resolve delivery failures. The quantities involved are relatively small compared to normal trading volumes for most securities. The auction ensures orderly settlement and does not indicate any systemic issues. Trading members with obligations in these securities will need to participate in the auction to complete their settlement requirements.
