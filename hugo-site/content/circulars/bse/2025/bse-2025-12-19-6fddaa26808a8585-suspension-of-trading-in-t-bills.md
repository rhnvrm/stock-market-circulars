## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are scheduled to mature on December 25, 2025. Trading Members are instructed not to deal in these T-Bills effective from December 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will mature on their redemption date (December 25, 2025)
- SCRIP CODE 805083, ISIN IN002025Y131: 182T251225 (182-day T-Bill)
- SCRIP CODE 805121, ISIN IN002025X265: 91TB251225 (91-day T-Bill)
- Trading suspension effective from December 22, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-782/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for T-Bill maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealing in the mentioned T-Bills from December 22, 2025
- Members should ensure no trades are executed in SCRIP CODE 805083 (ISIN IN002025Y131)
- Members should ensure no trades are executed in SCRIP CODE 805121 (ISIN IN002025X265)

## Important Dates

- **December 19, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **December 25, 2025**: T-Bills redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine pre-maturity trading suspension for two T-Bills in the debt segment. The suspension allows for orderly settlement and redemption process. Trading Members holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No impact on equity markets or other debt securities.
