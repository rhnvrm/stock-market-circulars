## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from December 10, 2025. The issue comprises 6,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on March 10, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Securities Description**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit
- **Quantity Issued**: 6,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730734
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF091225
- **ISIN Number**: INE831R14FP4
- **Detail Name**: ABHFL-10-3-26-CP
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FP4
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026 (91-day tenor)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing by a housing finance company for short-term funding. The instrument has high credit ratings (A1+) indicating strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: Standard debt market listing with no special trading conditions. Trading restricted to dematerialized form in Rs. 5 lakh denominations, which is typical for commercial paper instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Provides short-term investment opportunity (91 days) for debt market participants seeking highly-rated instruments with implicit yield of approximately 6% annualized based on issue price discount.
