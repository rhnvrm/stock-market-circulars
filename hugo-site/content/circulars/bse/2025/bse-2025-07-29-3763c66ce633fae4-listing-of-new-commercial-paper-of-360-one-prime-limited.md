## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 29, 2025, with face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit and 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, total 2000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 729921, ISIN: INE248U14RW6
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Adherence to 1 paise tick size requirement

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
