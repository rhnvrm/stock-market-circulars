## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 25,000 Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Bajaj Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 25,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,050.50
- **Scrip Code**: 730650
- **Scrip ID**: BFL251125
- **ISIN**: INE296A14D70
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296A14D70
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market impact. The listing expands short-term debt investment options for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The discount at issuance (Rs. 4,92,050.50 vs Rs. 5,00,000 face value) provides an implied yield for the 91-day instrument. No operational changes or compliance burden for general market participants.
