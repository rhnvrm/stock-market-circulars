## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for UDAAN PAPER INDUSTRIES LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on November 12, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a period of at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by UDAAN PAPER INDUSTRIES LIMITED on November 12, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has complied with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations for disclosures in the Draft Offer Document
- Stakeholders and public may submit comments on the draft document within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **November 12, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **November 13, 2025**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **December 4, 2025** (approximately): Last date for public comments (21 days from November 13, 2025)
