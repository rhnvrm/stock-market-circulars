## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544613 and trading symbol UTLSOLAR with ISIN number INE12UR01024. The specific date of listing and complete details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Fujiyama Power Systems Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544613
- Trading Symbol: UTLSOLAR
- ISIN Number: INE12UR01024
- Listing date and security details to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing of Fujiyama Power Systems Limited equity shares for future trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement with limited immediate market impact. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip information (code 544613, symbol UTLSOLAR). The actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile, issue size, and pricing, which will be detailed in the subsequent listing notice. Investors interested in the power systems sector may want to track the formal listing announcement for trading opportunities.
