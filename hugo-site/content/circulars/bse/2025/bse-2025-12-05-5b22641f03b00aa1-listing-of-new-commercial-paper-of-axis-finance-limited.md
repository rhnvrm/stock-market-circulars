## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective December 5, 2025. The instrument comprises 5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 250 crores, issued on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a very short tenure of 8 days, with allotment on December 4, 2025 and redemption scheduled for December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Axis Finance Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,99,310.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730225
- **Scrip ID**: AFL12925
- **Detail Name**: AFL-12-12-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE891K14PN7 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Limited

## Trading Details

- Trading will be exclusively in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading started on BSE Debt segment from December 5, 2025
- Securities traded under ISIN INE891K14PN7

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Tenure**: 8 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper has high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, IND A1+) indicating strong credit quality. The 8-day maturity suggests this is being used for very short-term working capital requirements. As a private placement, this is targeted at institutional investors and does not affect retail markets. The listing provides additional liquidity options for holders during the brief tenure.
