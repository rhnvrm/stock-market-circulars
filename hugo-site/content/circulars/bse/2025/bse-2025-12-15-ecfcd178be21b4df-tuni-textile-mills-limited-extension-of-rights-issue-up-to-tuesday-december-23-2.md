## Summary

Tuni Textile Mills Limited (Scrip Code: 531411) has extended the closing date of its ongoing Rights Issue from December 15, 2025 to December 23, 2025. The decision was made by the Board of Directors in a meeting held on December 13, 2025, to provide shareholders with additional opportunity to exercise their rights. The Rights Issue originally opened on November 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue closing date extended by 8 days from December 15, 2025 to December 23, 2025
- Board meeting held on December 13, 2025 (1:00 PM to 1:15 PM) approved the extension
- Issue opened on November 24, 2025 as per original schedule
- No changes to Letter of Offer (LOF) or Common Application Form (CAF) except closing date
- Subsequent post-issue activities timeline adjusted accordingly
- Extension aims to provide shareholders more time to participate in the Rights Issue

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural extension of an existing Rights Issue timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders entitled to participate must submit duly filled Common Application Form (CAF) with payment by December 23, 2025
- On-market renunciation of rights entitlements remains closed (last date was December 03, 2025)
- Off-market transfer of rights entitlements deadline extended to December 22, 2025

## Important Dates

**Revised Issue Schedule:**

- **Issue Opening Date:** Monday, November 24, 2025
- **Last Date for On-Market Renunciation:** Wednesday, December 03, 2025
- **Last Date for Off-Market Transfer:** Monday, December 22, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, December 23, 2025 (Extended from December 15, 2025)
- **Basis of Allotment Finalization:** Wednesday, December 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** Wednesday, December 24, 2025
- **Credit of Rights Equity Shares:** Friday, December 26, 2025
- **Listing Date:** Monday, December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Positive for existing shareholders who require additional time to arrange funds or complete application formalities. The 8-day extension provides more flexibility for participation in the Rights Issue.

**Market Impact:** Minimal broader market impact as this is company-specific. The extension indicates the company wants to maximize participation in its capital raising exercise.

**Operational Impact:** Post-issue activities including allotment, credit, and listing dates have been pushed back by approximately 8 days. The listing date is now scheduled for December 29, 2025, close to year-end.

**Liquidity Considerations:** The extension may help improve subscription rates by accommodating shareholders who need more time, potentially strengthening the company's capital raise outcome.
