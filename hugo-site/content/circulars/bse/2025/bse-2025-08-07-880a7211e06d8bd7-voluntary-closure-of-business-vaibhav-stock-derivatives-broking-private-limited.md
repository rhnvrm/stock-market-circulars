## Summary

Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6750) will voluntarily cease its currency derivative trading operations on BSE effective August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Member voluntarily closing currency derivative business segment
- Effective date: August 8, 2025 (Friday)
- Member number: 6750
- Only affects currency derivative segment operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a voluntary business closure by the member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the cessation
- Any clarifications should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **August 8, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single member's voluntary exit from currency derivatives segment only. Other market participants and segments remain unaffected.
