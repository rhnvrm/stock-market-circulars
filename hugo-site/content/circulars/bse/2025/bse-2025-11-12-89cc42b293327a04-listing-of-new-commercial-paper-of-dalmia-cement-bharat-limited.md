## Summary

BSE has listed 2000 Commercial Papers issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis with effect from November 12, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, with allotment date of November 11, 2025 and redemption date of February 10, 2026. IDBI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2000 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,473.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730542
- Scrip ID: DCL111125
- ISIN: INE755K14FP0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FP0
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited with short-term funding through the debt market. Impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants who may trade these instruments
- The issuing company's treasury operations
- No impact on equity markets or retail investors

The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk. The short 3-month tenure is typical for commercial paper used for working capital requirements.
