## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers of 8 companies effective from January 1-6, 2026. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 8 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension applies to debt segment securities
- No dealings dates range from January 1, 2026 to January 6, 2026
- Circular reference numbers: DR-789/2025-2026, DR-791/2025-2026, DR-792/2025-2026
- Notice number: 20251230-24

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the respective no-dealings dates
- Trading members should update their systems to prevent transactions in these securities from the specified dates

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers. No impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must ensure compliance with suspension directives to avoid regulatory issues. Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the respective terms.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary suspension affects liquidity in these specific instruments, though this is standard procedure for maturing commercial papers approaching redemption dates.
