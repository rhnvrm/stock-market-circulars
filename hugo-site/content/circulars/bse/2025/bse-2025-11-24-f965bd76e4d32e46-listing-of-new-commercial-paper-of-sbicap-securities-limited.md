## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis effective November 24, 2025. The instrument consists of 2,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total issue size Rs. 100 crore), carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Issuer: SBICAP Securities Limited
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Issue Size: 2,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730627
- Scrip ID: SSL211125
- ISIN: INE212K14CQ7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,845.50
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CQ7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 24, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 21, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 24, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides SBICAP Securities Limited access to short-term funding through the commercial paper market. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. This notification is relevant primarily for debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
