## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED effective November 25, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures with ISIN INE681R08091 and BSE code 975726. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE681R08091
- BSE Code: 975726
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (10.50% dated 10-12-25)
- Record Date: November 25, 2025
- No Dealings From: November 25, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-763/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from November 25, 2025
- No transactions should be executed in securities with ISIN INE681R08091 (BSE Code: 975726)
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This suspension affects only a single series of private placement debentures of NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED. As these are privately placed debt securities with limited trading activity, the market impact is minimal.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (INE681R08091) who will receive redemption proceeds as per the debenture terms. The 10.50% coupon rate debentures are reaching maturity/redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in these securities from November 25, 2025 onwards.
