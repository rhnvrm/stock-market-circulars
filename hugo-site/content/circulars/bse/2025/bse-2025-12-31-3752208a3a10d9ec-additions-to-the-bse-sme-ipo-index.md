## Summary

BSE announced the addition of four newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective January 01, 2026. The companies - NANTA TECH LIMITED (544668), ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED (544669), BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED (544670), and APOLLO TECHNO INDUSTRIES LIMITED (544671) - are being listed on BSE on December 31, 2025, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on January 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- NANTA TECH LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544668)
- ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544669)
- BAI-KAKAJI POLYMERS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544670)
- APOLLO TECHNO INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544671)
- Listing date: December 31, 2025
- Index inclusion effective: January 01, 2026 (at market open)
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following the listing of new SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Listing date for the four companies on BSE
- **January 01, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative update to the BSE SME IPO Index composition following the listing of four new SME companies. Index tracking funds and products linked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios to include these securities. The additions were included in the end-of-day corporate action file for automated processing by market systems.
