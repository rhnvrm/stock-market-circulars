## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors for the public issue of HRS Aluglaze Limited. This allocation represents the anchor book portion of the IPO, where institutional investors are allotted shares one day prior to the issue opening.

## Key Points

- Company: HRS Aluglaze Limited
- Event: Allocation to Anchor Investors in Public Issue
- Circular Date: December 10, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed as per SEBI ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- Minimum investment requirements for anchor investors as per ICDR norms
- Disclosure requirements for anchor investor allocations

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: December 10, 2025
- Public issue dates to follow

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation provides early price discovery for the HRS Aluglaze Limited IPO and demonstrates institutional investor interest. Successful anchor allocation typically indicates positive market sentiment towards the issue. This is a standard procedural step in the IPO process with medium market impact as it sets the stage for the retail portion of the offering.
