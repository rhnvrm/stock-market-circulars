## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective November 27, 2025. The issuance comprises 5,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (total Rs. 50 crore), carrying an interest rate of 10.85% per annum with monthly interest payments. The securities are rated ACUITE A- and will mature on December 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED
- **Scrip Code**: 977318
- **Scrip ID**: 1085MGFL26
- **ISIN**: INE08KJ07159
- **Issue Size**: 5,000 debentures (Rs. 50 crore)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Market Lot**: 1 debenture
- **Credit Rating**: ACUITE A-
- **Interest Rate**: 10.85% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly (26th of each month)
- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 27, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: December 26, 2026
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities on the BSE Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08KJ07159
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 26, 2025
- **Subsequent Interest Payments**: 26th of each month until maturity
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal market-wide impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities in a specialized segment.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt investors and institutional participants interested in A- rated green finance instruments with monthly income streams. The relatively short tenure (13 months) and monthly interest payments may appeal to investors seeking regular cash flows.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to trading members participating in the BSE Debt segment who need to update their systems with the new scrip information for trading and settlement purposes.
