## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies - BLT Logistics Limited (ticker 544474) and Essex Marine Limited (ticker 544475) - to the BSE SME IPO Index, effective from the market open on August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- BLT Logistics Limited and Essex Marine Limited listed on BSE SME platform on August 11, 2025
- Both companies will be added to BSE SME IPO Index from August 12, 2025 market open
- Index additions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Companies were previously announced in Notice No: 20250808-49 and Notice No: 20250808-47

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine index inclusion following standard listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index changes are automatically processed through corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- August 11, 2025: Companies listed on BSE SME platform
- August 12, 2025: Effective date for index addition (market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative action for index maintenance. The addition reflects the natural expansion of the SME IPO index as new companies complete their listing process on the BSE SME platform.
