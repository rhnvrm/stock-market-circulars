## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited on a private placement basis. The securities commenced trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The issue comprises 30,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, totaling Rs. 30 crore, carrying a 12% annual interest rate with monthly payments and maturing on June 11, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977367
- **Scrip ID**: 12ACAP27
- **ISIN**: INE08XP07431
- **Issue Size**: 30,000 units (Rs. 30 crore)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 12% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from January 11, 2026 to June 11, 2027
- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: June 11, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA BBB/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE08XP07431
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: January 11, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: June 11, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities from a private limited company with no equity listing. The issue size of Rs. 30 crore is relatively small and trading will be limited to institutional participants in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members dealing in debt securities need to be aware of the new scrip for trading purposes. The securities will trade in demat form only with standard tick size of 1 paise and market lot of 1 unit.
