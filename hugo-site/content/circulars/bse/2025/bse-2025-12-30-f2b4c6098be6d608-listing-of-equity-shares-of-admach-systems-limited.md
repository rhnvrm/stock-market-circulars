## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544669, symbol ADMACH, and ISIN number INE0XLO01010. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544669
- Symbol: ADMACH
- ISIN Number: INE0XLO01010
- Listing date to be announced via separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the listing information for ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED and await further notice regarding the specific listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The impact is specific to ADMACH SYSTEMS LIMITED and its stakeholders including investors, promoters, and trading members interested in SME segment stocks. Trading members should update their systems with the scrip code and symbol information in preparation for the listing. The announcement provides advance notice to allow market participants to prepare for trading once the listing date is confirmed.
