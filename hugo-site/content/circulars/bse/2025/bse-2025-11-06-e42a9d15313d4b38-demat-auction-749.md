## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 749 dated November 6, 2025. The auction includes 196 scrips with specific quantities of shares available for auction to cover settlement shortages. This is a routine market operation to facilitate settlement of trades where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 749
- Auction Date: November 6, 2025
- Total Scrips Listed: 196 securities
- Includes major stocks like Bajaj Finance (2,800 shares), Infosys (5,335 shares), Power Grid (19,774 shares), Yes Bank (18,992 shares), and NTPC (7,427 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, pharma, auto, energy, IT, and manufacturing
- Quantities range from 1 share to over 25,000 shares depending on the scrip

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate in the demat auction as per BSE auction rules
- Members with delivery obligations must ensure settlement compliance
- Auction participants must follow prescribed bidding procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 6, 2025 (Settlement No. 749)
- Auction conduct date would follow BSE's standard auction timeline for the settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine settlement mechanism to handle delivery shortfalls. While it includes major stocks, the quantities are relatively small compared to typical trading volumes.

**Operational Impact:** Affects specific trading members who have delivery obligations or wish to participate in the auction. The auction helps ensure smooth settlement and reduces systemic risk from failed deliveries.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. The auction mechanism is primarily a broker/trading member function to fulfill settlement obligations.
