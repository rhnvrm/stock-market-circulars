## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 182/2025-2026 dated December 23, 2025, announces book closure and record dates for various debt securities. The bulletin covers redemptions and interest payments for Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs), Commercial Papers (CPs), and Secured NCDs issued by multiple companies including Ashiana Housing Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, and Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers record dates ranging from January 5, 2026 to March 19, 2026
- Multiple securities of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Commercial Papers) have redemption dates between January and March 2026
- Ashiana Housing Limited has partial NCD redemption with interest payment scheduled for January 6, 2026
- Multiple secured NCDs of Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited have interest payment record date of March 17, 2026
- All securities listed have trading flag set to 1 (Record Date basis)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors holding securities on the respective record dates will be eligible for redemption proceeds or interest payments
- No book closure periods mentioned; all corporate actions are based on record dates
- Trading will continue without interruption as these are record date announcements

## Important Dates

**Ashiana Housing Limited:**
- January 6, 2026: Record date for partial NCD redemption and interest payment (Security codes: 973220, 974063)

**Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Commercial Papers):**
- January 5, 2026: CP redemption (Code: 730792)
- January 14, 2026: CP redemption (Code: 730799)
- March 16, 2026: CP redemption (Code: 730793)
- March 17, 2026: CP redemption (Code: 730798)
- March 19, 2026: CP redemption (Codes: 730833, 730837)

**Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited:**
- March 17, 2026: Interest payment for multiple secured NCDs (Codes: 938330, 938940, 938942, 939319, 939321, 939689, 939691, 939693, 940081, 940083, 940085, 940409, 940411)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational bulletin providing administrative information about debt securities.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for specific debt security holders who need to ensure they hold securities on record dates to receive redemption proceeds or interest payments.

**Operational Impact:** Standard operational processing for registrars, depositories, and paying agents handling the redemptions and interest payments for the listed securities.
