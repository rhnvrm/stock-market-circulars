## Summary

Tata Housing Development Company Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective December 10, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 75,000 units at a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling Rs. 75 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 7.51% per annum and mature on December 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977357
- Scrip ID: 751THDC28
- ISIN: INE582L08078
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 75,000 units
- Interest Rate: 7.51% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L08078
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 9, 2025
- Listing Date: December 10, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 9, 2026
- Last Interest Payment: December 8, 2028
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2028
- Interest Payment Frequency: Annually from December 9, 2026 to December 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors an opportunity to trade Tata Housing Development Company's debt securities on the exchange platform. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates strong credit quality. The impact is limited to the debt segment and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
