## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD (ISIN: INE0WWM07010, Code: 975944) effective December 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing December 11, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE0WWM07010 (DASPL-21%-21-5-27-PVT)
- BSE Code: 975944
- Record date: December 11, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-775/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures of DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD from December 11, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealing instruction effective from the record date

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 11, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 11, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings permitted from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The suspension affects a specific private company's debentures in the debt segment. The impact is limited to existing debenture holders of DRONE ACHARYA SERVICES PVT LTD.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action for debenture redemption. Trading members need to ensure compliance with the no-dealing instruction. Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the company's terms.

**Investor Impact**: Direct impact only on holders of these specific debentures who will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment based on holdings as of the record date.
