## Summary

BSE announces that MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND, which was launched as a New Fund Offering (NFO), has been reopened for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) starting December 08, 2025. This notice follows the previous announcement (Notice No. 20251114-10) regarding the NFO launch.

## Key Points

- MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND NFO scheme reopened for ongoing transactions
- Effective date: December 08, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity funds

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF769K01PS0)
2. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF769K01PU6)
3. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF769K01PT8)

### Direct Plan Options
4. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH** (ISIN: INF769K01PV4)
5. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT** (ISIN: INF769K01PX0)
6. **MIRAE ASSET INFRASTRUCTURE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT** (ISIN: INF769K01PW2)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational notification regarding the transition of a mutual fund scheme from NFO period to ongoing operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. BSE members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems to enable transactions in the reopened schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 05, 2025
- **Scheme Reopening Date:** December 08, 2025
- **Previous NFO Notice:** Reference Notice No. 20251114-10

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational update affecting mutual fund distribution through BSE StAR MF Platform. 

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in infrastructure-focused equity funds, as they now have access to ongoing investment options including systematic investment facilities.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - BSE members and distributors need to enable the scheme for transactions on their platforms starting December 08, 2025.

**Sector Focus:** The fund focuses on infrastructure sector investments, providing investors with a thematic equity investment option in this segment.
