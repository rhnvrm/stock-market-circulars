## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of FEDDERS HOLDING LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511628) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The new securities will commence trading with effect from Tuesday, December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Number of shares: 2,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 57.20/- (face value Re. 1/- + premium of Rs. 56.20/-)
- Allotment basis: Preferential allotment to Non-Promoters
- Source: Conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 201215971 to 201415970
- Date of allotment: October 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE417D01020
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of these new securities and the applicable lock-in restrictions when processing trades.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 23, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 30, 2026 (all 2,00,000 shares under lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential allotment represents a relatively small capital raise through warrant conversion. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 30, 2026, preventing immediate trading or liquidity impact. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining equal rights for all shareholders.
