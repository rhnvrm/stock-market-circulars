## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 773 dated December 10, 2025. The auction involves 96 scrips across various sectors where delivery obligations could not be met, requiring auction settlement. Quantities range from 1 share to 49,000 shares depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 773
- Settlement Date: December 10, 2025
- Total scrips involved: 96 companies
- Quantities vary significantly from single shares to thousands
- Highest quantity: B.C. POWER C (49,000 shares)
- Notable large quantities: Varun Beverages (10,000), Worth Invest (8,614), ASTEC Lifesciences (759)
- Covers diverse sectors including power, banking, manufacturing, technology, and FMCG

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational settlement through the auction mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in these scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction process
- Members are required to fulfill delivery obligations as per BSE auction rules

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 10, 2025
- Settlement Number: 773

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 96 scrips requiring auction settlement, indicating delivery failures that need resolution through auction mechanism.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members with open positions in these scrips must participate in the auction process to close out their obligations.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - May result in price volatility in affected scrips during auction settlement. Investors holding these securities may experience temporary liquidity constraints.
