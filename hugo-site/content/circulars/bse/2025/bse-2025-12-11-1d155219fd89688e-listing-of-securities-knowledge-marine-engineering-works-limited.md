## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,421,054 new equity shares of Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited (Scrip Code: 543273) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. Trading in these shares will commence on December 12, 2025. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,421,054 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,890/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1,900/- per share
- Allotment date: October 30, 2025
- Trading effective date: December 12, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 10801001 to 12222054
- ISIN: INE0CJD01011
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 1,421,054 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential issue listing with minimal market impact. The lock-in period of approximately 7.5 months ensures stability and prevents immediate dilution pressure. The issue represents capital raising activity by Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Limited for business expansion or operational requirements. Trading members should note the lock-in restrictions when processing any transaction requests for these distinctive numbers.
