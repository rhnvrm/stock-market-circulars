## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of JM Financial Products Limited (Scrip Code 974922) effective November 17, 2025. This follows a partial redemption where 7,500 debentures out of 25,000 were redeemed through buyback at investor request, leaving 17,500 debentures outstanding and available for trading.

## Key Points

- Trading in JM Financial Products Limited debentures (Scrip Code 974922) suspended for partial redemption
- Record date for buyback was November 8, 2025
- 7,500 debentures (30% of total) redeemed through buyback
- 17,500 debentures (70% of total) remain outstanding
- Trading to resume from November 17, 2025
- Reference: DR-757/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for resumption of trading following partial redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the resumption date of November 17, 2025
- Updated outstanding debenture count (17,500) should be reflected in trading systems
- Company completed investor buyback request process as per regulations

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Original exchange notice regarding record date
- **November 8, 2025**: Record date for partial redemption/buyback
- **November 14, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption
- **November 17, 2025**: Trading resumes for Scrip Code 974922

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The partial redemption reduces outstanding debentures by 30%, which may affect liquidity. However, 70% of debentures remain available for trading, maintaining reasonable market depth.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding the remaining 17,500 debentures will regain trading ability from November 17, 2025. The buyback provided exit opportunity for 30% of debenture holders.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update systems to reflect the reduced outstanding debenture count and prepare for resumption of trading activity.
