## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a non-convertible debenture (NCD) of Annapurna Finance Private Limited effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture from the record date.

## Key Points

- Debenture affected: AFPL-12.50%-19-12-25-PVT (ISIN: INE515Q07228, Code: 958467)
- Record date: December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: December 4, 2025
- Notice number: DR-770/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debenture from December 4, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All pending orders for the affected debenture should be cancelled or rejected from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 4, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 4, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited. This affects a single privately placed debenture series with restricted investor base. The suspension is temporary and related to standard corporate action processing.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will be identified as of the record date for receiving redemption proceeds and interest payment. No trading activity will be possible from December 4, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and inform clients holding or interested in this security about the suspension.
