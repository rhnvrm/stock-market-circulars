## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest on its 7.39% tax-free non-convertible debentures maturing January 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in specified NHAI bonds from December 26, 2025
- Record date set for December 26, 2025 for bondholders
- Applies to NHAI-7.39%-11-1-26-TAXFREENCD (ISIN: INE906B07EH2, Code: 935580)
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Notice issued under DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard suspension procedure for corporate action related to bond redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified NHAI bonds from December 26, 2025
- No new trades should be executed in these securities starting from the suspension date
- Members must inform their clients holding these bonds about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Bond Maturity Date**: January 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific NHAI bond series. The suspension is temporary and relates to the final redemption process as the bond approaches its January 2026 maturity date.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders as of the record date will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption. No trading opportunity available from suspension date onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading desks need to update their systems to prevent trades in this security from December 26, 2025.
