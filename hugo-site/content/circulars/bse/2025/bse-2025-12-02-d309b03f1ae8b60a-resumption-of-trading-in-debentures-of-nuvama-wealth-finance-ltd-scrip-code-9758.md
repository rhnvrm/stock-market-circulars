## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd (Scrip Code 975892) effective December 03, 2025. Trading was previously suspended following the company's record date of July 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption of debentures. The company has now requested resumption of trading operations.

## Key Points

- Trading in Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd debentures (Scrip Code 975892) to resume from December 03, 2025
- Previous suspension was related to record date fixed on July 23, 2025 for interest payment and redemption
- Notice reference: DR-769/2025-2026
- Original suspension notice: Exchange Notice No. 20250721-46 dated July 21, 2025
- Trading Members have been notified of the resumption

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for resumption of trading following completion of corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the availability of Scrip Code 975892 for trading from December 03, 2025
- All trading operations for these debentures can resume as per normal procedures

## Important Dates

- **July 21, 2025**: Original suspension notice issued
- **July 23, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **December 02, 2025**: Notice date for trading resumption
- **December 03, 2025**: Trading resumes for Scrip Code 975892

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Resumption allows debt market participants to trade these debentures again, restoring liquidity for this specific instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard resumption process with no special conditions or restrictions. Trading Members can resume normal trading operations for this scrip.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Debenture holders and potential investors can now trade the instrument, providing exit options and price discovery mechanism after the suspension period.
