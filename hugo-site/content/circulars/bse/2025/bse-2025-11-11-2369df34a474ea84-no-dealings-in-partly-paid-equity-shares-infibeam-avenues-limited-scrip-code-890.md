## Summary

BSE has announced a complete suspension of trading in partly paid equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Limited (Scrip Code 890219) effective November 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date to determine eligible shareholders for payment of first and final call money of Rs. 5 per share.

## Key Points

- Trading in partly paid equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Limited to be suspended from November 14, 2025
- Record date set for November 14, 2025 to identify shareholders eligible for call payment
- First and final call amount: Rs. 5 per share (Rs. 0.50 towards face value + Rs. 4.50 towards securities premium)
- Scrip code for partly paid shares: 890219
- Settlement number: DR-756/2025-2026
- Trading members advised not to deal in these shares from the specified date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for partly paid shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease all dealings in partly paid equity shares (scrip code 890219) of Infibeam Avenues Limited from November 14, 2025
- Trading members must ensure their systems and processes reflect the no-dealings status
- Members required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **November 11, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **November 14, 2025**: Record date and start of no-dealings period
- **Settlement Number**: DR-756/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: High - Complete suspension of trading in partly paid shares prevents any buy/sell transactions, affecting liquidity for current holders.

**Shareholder Impact**: High - Shareholders holding partly paid shares as of record date must pay the first and final call money of Rs. 5 per share to convert them to fully paid shares.

**Market Operations**: The suspension is limited to partly paid equity shares only and does not affect trading in fully paid equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Limited if listed separately.
