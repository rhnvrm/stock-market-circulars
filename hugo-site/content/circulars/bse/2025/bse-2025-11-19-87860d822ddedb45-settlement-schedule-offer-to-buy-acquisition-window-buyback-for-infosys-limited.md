## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for Infosys Limited's buyback offer through the Acquisition Window mechanism. The buyback bidding period will run from November 20-26, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025126), with obligation download on December 2, 2025 and final settlement on December 3, 2025. Shares must be delivered exclusively through early pay-in mechanism in depositories.

## Key Points

- Buyback settlement number: 2025126
- Bidding window: November 20-26, 2025 (7 days)
- Only early pay-in delivery mechanism allowed for demat shares
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts automatically
- No EPN file upload required by members/custodians
- Market type "Buyback" must be specified in depository systems
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

Members and custodians must follow the revised guidelines for mechanism of acquisition of shares through stock exchange as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must complete early pay-in process in depository system sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation
- **Depositories (CDSL & NSDL)**: Early pay-in securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in requisite market type and settlement number
- **Members**: Must use Market Type "Buyback" and Settlement No. 2025126 in depository systems
- **Members**: Should refer to designated file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **November 20, 2025**: Bid start date
- **November 26, 2025**: Bid end date
- **December 2, 2025**: Obligation download
- **December 3, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Infosys shareholders wishing to participate in the buyback must ensure timely delivery of shares through early pay-in mechanism. The 7-day bidding window provides adequate time for participation.

**Operational Impact**: Members and custodians must coordinate early pay-in activities to ensure shares are available before bid confirmation deadlines. The automatic earmarking process simplifies operations by eliminating EPN file uploads.
