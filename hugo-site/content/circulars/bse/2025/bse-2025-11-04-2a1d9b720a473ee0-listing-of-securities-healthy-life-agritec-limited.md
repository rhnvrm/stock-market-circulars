## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 24,812,000 equity shares of Healthy Life Agritec Limited (Scrip Code: 543546) issued on a rights basis at par value of Rs. 10/- each. These securities will commence trading on the Exchange from Thursday, November 6, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Company: Healthy Life Agritec Limited
- Scrip Code: 543546
- Number of shares: 24,812,000 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue type: Rights issue at par
- ISIN: INE0L3501015
- Distinctive Numbers: 24812001 to 49624000
- Trading commencement: November 6, 2025 (Thursday)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to take note of the listing and trading permission for these new securities effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 3, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with minimal market-wide impact. The rights issue increases the equity base of Healthy Life Agritec Limited. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their allotted shares become tradable from November 6, 2025. The new shares carry equal rights with existing equity shares.
