## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of Shriram Finance Ltd effective November 07, 2025. The debentures (SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT, ISIN: INE721A07RF3, Scrip Code: 974376) have been repurchased by the company and extinguished. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series SFL-GSEC-18-11-25-PVT (ISIN: INE721A07RF3) suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 974376
- Reason: Repurchased by company and extinguished
- Effective date: November 07, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-751/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding debt securities repurchase.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE721A07RF3) from November 07, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the extinguished debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 07, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series of Shriram Finance Ltd. Since the debentures have been repurchased and extinguished by the company, this represents a planned corporate action with minimal market disruption. Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the suspension to prevent inadvertent trading in these securities. The action affects only the debt market segment and does not impact the company's equity shares or other outstanding debt securities.
