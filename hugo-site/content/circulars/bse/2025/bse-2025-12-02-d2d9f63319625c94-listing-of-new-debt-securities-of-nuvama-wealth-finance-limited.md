## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of two new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 2, 2025. Both securities are rated CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- First security (NWFL-GOLDFUTURE-30-4-29-PVT): 225 units with face value Rs. 100,000, maturing April 30, 2029
- Second security (NWFL-NIFTY-13-10-28-PVT): 100 units with face value Rs. 100,000, maturing October 13, 2028
- Both securities allotted on November 27, 2025
- Credit rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable for both issues
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE website for detailed information
- For clarifications, members may contact the debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date (NWFL301025)**: April 30, 2029
- **Redemption Date (NWFL131025)**: October 13, 2028

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing adds to the available debt instruments on BSE's platform for institutional investors.
