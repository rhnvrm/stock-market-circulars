## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper of 10 companies effective from their respective no-dealings dates. The suspensions are due to record dates fixed for redemption of commercial paper between November 7-14, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension applies to debt segment commercial paper instruments
- No dealings permitted from the specified suspension dates
- Suspensions range from November 7 to November 14, 2025
- Notice issued under corporate actions category for debt segment

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified commercial papers from the no-dealings dates
- Members should update their systems to prevent trading in these instruments
- Ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension
- No new positions can be opened in these commercial papers from suspension dates

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Suspension begins for OXYZO Financial Services commercial paper
- **November 12, 2025**: Suspension begins for L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) commercial paper
- **November 13, 2025**: Suspension begins for Standard Chartered Capital, Nuvama Wealth Management, and Network 18 Media & Investments commercial papers
- **November 14, 2025**: Suspension begins for Capri Global Capital, Credila Financial Services, Vivriti Capital, Poonawalla Fincorp, and Reliance Retail Ventures commercial papers

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this is a routine corporate action for commercial paper redemption. The suspension affects only specific debt instruments and does not impact equity trading or other debt securities of these companies.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these commercial papers should be aware that secondary market trading will be suspended from the specified dates. Redemption will proceed as per the terms of the commercial paper.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure system updates to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended commercial papers. The staggered suspension dates require careful monitoring across different days.
