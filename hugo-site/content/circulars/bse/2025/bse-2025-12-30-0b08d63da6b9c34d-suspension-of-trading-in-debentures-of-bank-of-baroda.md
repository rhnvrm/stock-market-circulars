## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Bank of Baroda's 8.15% Perpetual AT1 (Additional Tier 1) Private Debentures (ISIN: INE028A08240, Scrip Code: 960413) effective from December 31, 2025. The suspension is due to the company's decision to redeem these debentures through the exercise of a call option. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Bank of Baroda to redeem perpetual AT1 debentures on exercise of call option
- ISIN: INE028A08240, Scrip Code: 960413
- Coupon rate: 8.15%
- Trading suspension effective from December 31, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-788/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notice regarding the suspension of trading due to security redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 31, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All pending orders or positions in these debentures should be managed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Redemption Event**: Exercise of Call Option on the specified debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of Bank of Baroda's specific AT1 perpetual bonds. The redemption through call option exercise is a standard corporate action for callable debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No further trading opportunity will be available after December 30, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive and manage client communications regarding positions in these securities. The suspension is routine for securities undergoing redemption.
