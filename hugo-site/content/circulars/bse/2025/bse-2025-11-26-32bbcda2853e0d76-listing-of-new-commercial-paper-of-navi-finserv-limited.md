## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective November 26, 2025. The listing consists of 700 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each on the BSE Debt segment. The securities carry IND A1 credit rating and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 700 Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,85,444.50 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730651
- Scrip ID: NFL251125
- ISIN: INE342T14FD4
- Credit rating: IND A1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note:
- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14FD4
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise to be followed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides an additional short-term debt investment option for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market. The commercial paper has a tenure of approximately 114 days (November 25, 2025 to March 19, 2026) and carries investment-grade credit rating (IND A1), indicating strong credit quality. No operational changes required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
