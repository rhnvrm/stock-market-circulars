## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 25 crore) and is admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 1, 2025. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 500 commercial paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each
- **Scrip Code**: 730674
- **Scrip ID**: FFSL281125
- **Detail Name**: FFSL-27-2-26-CP
- **ISIN Number**: INE007N14EL2
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,688.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Federal Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE007N14EL2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors and debt market participants. The instrument has strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating high credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in commercial paper. The standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs makes it accessible primarily to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.
