## Summary

BSE announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. ORKLA INDIA LIMITED (ticker 544595) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective November 10, 2025, following its listing on November 6, 2025. SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED (ticker 544596) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective November 7, 2025, following its listing on the SME platform on November 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- ORKLA INDIA LIMITED (544595) listed on BSE main board on November 6, 2025
- ORKLA INDIA LIMITED to be added to BSE IPO index at market open on November 10, 2025
- SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED (544596) listed on BSE SME platform on November 6, 2025
- SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED to be added to BSE SME IPO index at market open on November 7, 2025
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index maintenance action following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers and passive investors tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices should note the additions for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **November 6, 2025**: Listing date for both ORKLA INDIA LIMITED and SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED
- **November 7, 2025**: SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **November 10, 2025**: ORKLA INDIA LIMITED added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions following IPO listings. The additions will primarily affect:

- **Index Funds**: Passive funds tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to include these stocks in their portfolios
- **Market Participants**: Increased trading activity expected in these stocks as index funds rebalance
- **Liquidity**: Potential improvement in liquidity for both stocks due to index inclusion

The staggered addition dates (November 7 and November 10) allow for orderly inclusion without market disruption.
