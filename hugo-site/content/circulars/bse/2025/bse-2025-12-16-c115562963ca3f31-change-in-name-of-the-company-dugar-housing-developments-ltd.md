## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Dugar Housing Developments Limited (CIN: L62013TN1992PLC023689). The company's name has been officially changed to Axentra Corp Limited with effect from November 22, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dugar Housing Development Finance India Limited.

## Key Points

- Former name: Dugar Housing Developments Limited
- New name: Axentra Corp Limited
- CIN: L62013TN1992PLC023689
- Effective date of name change: November 22, 2025
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered office: Dugar Towers, 123 Marshalls Road, Egmore, Chennai-600008, Tamil Nadu

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name "Dugar Housing Developments Limited" along with the new name "Axentra Corp Limited" for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- Name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 22, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Dugar Housing Developments Limited to Axentra Corp Limited
- **December 2, 2025**: Date of digital signature on the certificate
- **Two-year period**: Duration for which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are administrative corporate actions that do not affect the fundamental business operations or financial position of the company.

**Shareholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights and liabilities. All existing shares, obligations, and contracts remain valid under the new name.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update all official documentation, letterheads, and branding materials to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years.

**Trading Impact**: Investors and traders should note the name change when searching for the company. Stock ticker and CIN remain unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading and record-keeping.
