## Summary

AXIS MUTUAL FUND is launching AXIS MULTI-ASSET ACTIVE FOF - NFO with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from November 21, 2025, to December 05, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of December 11, 2025. Six plan options are available including Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment variants.

## Key Points

- NFO period: November 21, 2025 to December 05, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes categorized under DEBT with unique ISINs assigned
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on NFO closure or T+5, whichever is earlier
- NFO extended till 6:00 PM on December 05, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds receipt
- Switches accepted during NFO period with conditions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 (including extended period transactions) will be cancelled and CC with N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- For switches, funds must be received under NFO scheme on or before allotment date for allotment consideration

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: NFO opens
- **December 05, 2025**: NFO closes (extended till 6:00 PM for lumpsum purchases)
- **December 05, 2025, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **December 11, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **December 11, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **T+1, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (or transaction cancellation)
- **T+5 or NFO last day, 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction deadline (whichever is earlier)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it announces a routine mutual fund NFO launch. It provides operational guidance for BSE StAR MF Platform members handling AXIS MUTUAL FUND transactions. The launch offers investors access to a multi-asset fund of funds strategy with both regular and direct plan options. Members need to ensure compliance with specified timelines for fund processing and client authentication to facilitate smooth allotment.
