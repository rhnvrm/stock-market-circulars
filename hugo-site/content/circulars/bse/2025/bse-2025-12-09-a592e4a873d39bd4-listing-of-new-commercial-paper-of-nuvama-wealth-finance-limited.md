## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 3,500 units of Commercial Paper, each with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000, totaling Rs. 175 crore. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 9, 2025, and will mature on March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Quantity**: 3,500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,90,942.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 175 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730724
- **Scrip ID**: NWFL081225
- **ISIN**: INE918K14CV9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K14CV9
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded exclusively on the BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a private placement of short-term Commercial Paper by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing provides debt market participants with an additional investment avenue in high-rated short-term instruments. The 91-day tenure and competitive pricing (implied discount rate) make it suitable for institutional investors seeking short-term treasury management options. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
