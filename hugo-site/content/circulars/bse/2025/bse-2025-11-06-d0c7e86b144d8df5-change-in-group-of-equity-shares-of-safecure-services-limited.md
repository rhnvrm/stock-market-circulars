## Summary

BSE has announced that SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544596), an SME IPO stock, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective November 20, 2025. This change follows the earlier notice dated November 04, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SAFECURE SERVICES LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544596
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Thursday, November 20, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251106-8
- Reference Notice: 20251104-60 dated November 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for T+1 settlement cycles instead of full delivery-based trading. This change typically indicates improved liquidity and trading confidence in the security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the change in settlement mechanism
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details
- Trading Members need to adjust their systems and processes for the new segment classification

## Important Dates

- Notice Issue Date: Thursday, November 06, 2025
- Effective Date of Transfer: Thursday, November 20, 2025
- Reference Notice Date: Tuesday, November 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment is generally positive for the stock as it:
- Improves liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Enables T+1 settlement instead of mandatory delivery
- Indicates the stock has met BSE's criteria for moving out of restrictive trading
- May attract more trading interest and volume
- Provides greater flexibility to market participants
