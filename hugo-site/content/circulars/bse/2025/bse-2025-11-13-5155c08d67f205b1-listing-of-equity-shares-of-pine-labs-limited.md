## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of Pine Labs Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544606, trading symbol PINELABS, and ISIN number INE15B701018. The specific date of listing and further details about the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Pine Labs Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544606
- Trading Symbol: PINELABS
- ISIN Number: INE15B701018
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems for trading in Pine Labs Limited equity shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will provide investors access to trade Pine Labs Limited equity shares on BSE platform. The company will join the equity segment of the exchange, offering new investment opportunities to market participants. Trading members should note the assigned scrip code and symbol for future trading operations. The actual market impact will depend on the final listing date and opening price details to be announced.
