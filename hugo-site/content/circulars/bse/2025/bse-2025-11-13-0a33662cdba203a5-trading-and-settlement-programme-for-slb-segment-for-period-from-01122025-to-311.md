## Summary

BSE has issued the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment for December 2025. The notice provides a complete schedule of 22 settlement cycles (SL-167/25-26 through SL-188/25-26) covering the period from December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025. Each settlement follows the standard T+1 pay-in/pay-out cycle.

## Key Points

- 22 settlement cycles scheduled for December 2025 (SL-167/25-26 to SL-188/25-26)
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2526167 to 2526188
- Trading date, 6A7A entry, and 6A7A confirmation occur on the same day for each settlement
- Pay-in/pay-out scheduled for T+1 (next business day after trading)
- Pay-in time: 08:00 a.m.
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks by 07:50 a.m.
- No trading scheduled for December 6-7, 13-14, 20-21, 25, 27-28 (weekends and holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks latest by 07:50 a.m. on pay-in day
- Pay-in must be completed by 08:00 a.m. on the scheduled pay-in date
- Members must follow the prescribed settlement schedule for all SLB transactions

## Important Dates

- **Schedule Period**: December 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025
- **First Settlement**: SL-167/25-26 - Trading on December 1, Pay-out on December 2
- **Last Settlement**: SL-188/25-26 - Trading on December 31, Pay-out on January 1, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Notice Number**: 20251113-32

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational schedule that enables market participants to plan their SLB activities for December 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The schedule follows standard T+1 settlement conventions with no changes to existing procedures. Members already familiar with SLB operations can continue with established workflows.

**Participant Action Required**: Market participants engaged in securities lending and borrowing should note the settlement dates and ensure timely submission of pay-in instructions as per the prescribed timeline (by 07:50 a.m.).
