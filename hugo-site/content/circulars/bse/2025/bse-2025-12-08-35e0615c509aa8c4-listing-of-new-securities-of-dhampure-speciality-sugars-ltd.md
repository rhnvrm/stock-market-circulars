## Summary

BSE has listed 4,00,000 equity shares of Dhampure Speciality Sugars Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531923) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from December 9, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until June 8, 2027.

## Key Points

- 4,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 82/-
- Issue price: Rs. 92.00 per share
- Allotment made to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 8331201 to 8731200
- Date of allotment: September 1, 2025
- ISIN: INE956B01013
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of new securities
- All 4,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until June 8, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 1, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** June 8, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters with minimal market impact. The shares represent warrant conversion and are subject to a lock-in period of approximately 18 months from the trading date, preventing immediate market availability. The issue size is relatively small and will not significantly affect the stock's liquidity or trading dynamics.
