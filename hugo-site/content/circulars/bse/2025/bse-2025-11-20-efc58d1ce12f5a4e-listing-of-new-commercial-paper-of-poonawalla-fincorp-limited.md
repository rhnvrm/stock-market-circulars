## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED on a private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. The listing comprises 15,000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each on the BSE Debt segment. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent for these instruments.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 15,000 Commercial Papers listed
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,893.50/- per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730603
- **Scrip ID**: PFL191125
- **ISIN**: INE511C14ZB2
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE511C14ZB2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI BANK LTD handles all administrative functions

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Tenor**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine Commercial Paper listing that expands short-term debt instrument availability on BSE's Debt segment. The impact is limited to institutional investors and debt market participants dealing in money market instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for debt securities with established trading protocols. Trading members have access to standard support through BSE's debt department for any clarifications.

**Investor Impact**: The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness, making these instruments suitable for institutional investors seeking short-term debt exposure with low credit risk.
