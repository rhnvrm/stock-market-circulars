## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Vidya Wires Limited. Anchor investor allocation is conducted one day prior to the issue opening and provides an indication of institutional demand and helps in price discovery.

## Key Points

- Company: Vidya Wires Limited
- Event: Anchor investor allocation for public issue
- Circular issued by BSE on December 2, 2025
- Anchor allocation precedes the main IPO opening

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for anchor investor allocation in accordance with SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity must ensure proper disclosure of anchor investor details
- Allocation details to be made available to public as per SEBI requirements
- Lock-in period applicable for anchor investors as per regulations

## Important Dates

- Anchor Allocation Date: December 2, 2025
- Main issue opening expected shortly after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation provides confidence to retail investors and indicates institutional interest in the IPO. The allocation details help in price discovery and gauge demand for the issue.

**Operational Impact:** This circular is informational for market participants interested in the Vidya Wires IPO.
