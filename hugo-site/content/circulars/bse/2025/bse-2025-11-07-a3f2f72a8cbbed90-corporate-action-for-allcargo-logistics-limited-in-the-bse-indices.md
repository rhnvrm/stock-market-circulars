## Summary

Allcargo Logistics Limited (Exchange Ticker: 532749) is demerging its International Supply Chain Business into a new entity, Allcargo Global Limited, effective from start of trading on Wednesday, November 12, 2025. Shareholders will receive 1 equity share of Allcargo Global Limited for every 1 equity share held in Allcargo Logistics Limited. A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be conducted on November 12, 2025, to enable price discovery for Allcargo Logistics Limited post-demerger. Allcargo Global Limited will be added to multiple BSE indices at zero price effective November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Demerger of International Supply Chain Business from Allcargo Logistics Limited to Allcargo Global Limited
- Share exchange ratio: 1:1 (1 equity share of Allcargo Global Limited for every 1 equity share of Allcargo Logistics Limited)
- Effective date: Start of trading hours on Wednesday, November 12, 2025
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) scheduled for November 12, 2025, for price discovery of Allcargo Logistics Limited post-demerger
- Allcargo Global Limited to be added to 8 BSE indices at zero price
- Allcargo Global Limited equity shares expected to list on BSE at a future date
- Notice Number: 20251107-25, dated November 07, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This circular announces a scheme of arrangement under corporate action regulations. The demerger represents a structural change in Allcargo Logistics Limited's business operations, with the International Supply Chain Business being separated into a distinct listed entity. The implementation follows applicable regulatory approvals for schemes of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE will conduct a Special Pre-Open Session on November 12, 2025, for Allcargo Logistics Limited
- Index constituents and market participants should note the addition of Allcargo Global Limited to specified BSE indices
- Market participants should account for the 1:1 share allocation in shareholder portfolios
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **November 07, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **November 11, 2025**: Last day before demerger becomes effective (close of trading)
- **November 12, 2025**: Demerger effective date, start of trading hours
- **November 12, 2025**: Special Pre-Open Session for Allcargo Logistics Limited
- **November 12, 2025**: Allcargo Global Limited added to BSE indices (after close of November 11, 2025, without divisor adjustment)
- **Future date**: Expected listing of Allcargo Global Limited equity shares on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The demerger will result in two separately traded entities, affecting portfolio compositions and index weightings. The Special Pre-Open Session is critical for establishing fair price discovery post-demerger for Allcargo Logistics Limited.

**Index Impact**: Allcargo Global Limited will be added to 8 BSE indices at zero price without divisor adjustment:
- BSE SMALLCAP
- BSE ALLCAP
- BSE SERVICES
- BSE 1000
- BSE NEXT 500
- BSE 250 MICROCAP
- BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%)
- BSE MIDSMALLCAP

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders of Allcargo Logistics Limited will automatically receive equal number of shares in Allcargo Global Limited (1:1 ratio), effectively splitting their investment across two entities.

**Trading Impact**: The SPOS mechanism ensures orderly price discovery and reduces potential volatility during the transition period for Allcargo Logistics Limited post-demerger.
