## Summary

NACL Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 524709) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 12, 2025. The company will issue 5 equity shares of Re 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share for every 31 equity shares held. Trading will be on an ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: NACL Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 524709)
- Rights Ratio: 5 new equity shares for every 31 existing shares held
- Face Value: Re 1/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 75.70 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 76.70 per share (payable on application)
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Ex-Rights Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in NACL Industries equity shares on an ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025
- Issue price is payable on application
- Only shareholders holding shares as of the record date (December 12, 2025) will be eligible for the rights issue

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium. The rights issue will potentially increase the share capital of NACL Industries by approximately 16.13% (5/31 ratio). The ex-rights trading may cause short-term price adjustments as the market factors in the dilution effect.

**Shareholder Impact:** Existing shareholders have the opportunity to maintain their proportional ownership by subscribing to the rights issue at Rs. 76.70 per share. Non-participation will result in dilution of their stake.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to ensure all transactions are executed on an ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025, to avoid settlement issues.
