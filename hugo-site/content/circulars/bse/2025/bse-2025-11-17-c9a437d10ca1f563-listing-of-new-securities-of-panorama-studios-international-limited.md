## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 35,00,000 new equity shares of PANORAMA STUDIOS INTERNATIONAL LIMITED (Scrip Code: 539469) effective from Tuesday, November 18, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 54.80 per share (face value Rs. 2 plus premium of Rs. 52.80). The shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 35,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 52.80 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 54.80 per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 70938751 to 74438750
- Date of allotment: August 13, 2025
- ISIN: INE258R01028
- Complete lock-in of all 35,00,000 shares until June 29, 2027
- Trading commences: November 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- The new securities are listed and permitted for trading from November 18, 2025
- All 35,00,000 shares are under lock-in until June 29, 2027
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment to promoters represents warrant conversion, and the extended lock-in period until June 29, 2027 ensures that these shares will not be available for trading in the near term. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, maintaining uniform shareholder rights. Market participants should be aware of the locked-in status when assessing available float.
