## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Mirae Asset Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF units effective November 13, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme replicating the Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index. A total of 3,940,666 units have been allotted and will trade in the B Group with scrip code 544605.

## Key Points

- Total units listed: 3,940,666 units
- Scrip code: 544605 (SMALL250)
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 17.02 per unit
- Date of allotment: November 7, 2025
- Trading commencement: November 13, 2025
- Market lot: 1 unit
- ISIN: INF769K01PQ4
- Group: B
- Trading format: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)

## Fund Details

- **Mutual Fund:** Mirae Asset Mutual Fund
- **Investment Manager:** Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited
- **Registered Office:** Unit No. 606, Windsor Building, Off. C.S.T Road, Kalina, Santacruz (East) 400098
- **Underlying Index:** Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index

## Market Makers

The following entities are designated as market makers for this ETF:
- East India Securities Limited
- Kanjolachana Finserve Private Limited
- Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited
- Parwati Capital Markets Privates Limited
- Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new ETF listing that provides investors with a passive investment vehicle to gain exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 index. The listing expands the range of investment products available on BSE. With six designated market makers, adequate liquidity support is expected. The ETF offers investors a cost-effective way to participate in the small-cap segment of the Indian equity market through a diversified portfolio tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index.
