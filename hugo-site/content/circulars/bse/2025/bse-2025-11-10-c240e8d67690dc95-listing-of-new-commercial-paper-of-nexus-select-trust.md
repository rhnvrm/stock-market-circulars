## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on a private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 10, 2025. The issue consists of 4000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated IND A1+ by credit rating agencies.

## Key Points

- 4000 Commercial Paper units listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,413 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730527, Scrip ID: NST71125
- ISIN: INE0NDH14098
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Allotment date: November 7, 2025
- Redemption date: February 6, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities must be traded under ISIN Number INE0NDH14098

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper securities with minimal market impact. The listing expands the debt instrument options available on BSE's debt segment. The 91-day tenor commercial paper is a short-term debt instrument that provides liquidity options for institutional investors. No significant operational or market-wide implications are expected from this listing.
