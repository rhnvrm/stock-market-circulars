## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Dhaval Packaging Limited filed on December 30, 2025, on its SME website for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Dhaval Packaging Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document hosted on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all mandatory disclosures per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document accessible at: www.bsesme.com/download/312386/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHPDPL_20251231003527.pdf
- Comments to be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must remain available on exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public comments can be submitted within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must address comments before finalizing the offer document

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from December 31, 2025 (ending approximately January 21, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a procedural notice for an upcoming SME IPO with no immediate impact on existing market operations or traded securities.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Relevant only for potential investors interested in the Dhaval Packaging Limited IPO and market participants who wish to provide feedback on the draft offer document.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing market participants. Standard IPO process for SME segment.
