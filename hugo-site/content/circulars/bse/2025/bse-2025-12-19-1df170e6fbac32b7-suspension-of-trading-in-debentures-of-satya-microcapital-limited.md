## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE982X07218 (Code: 973717)
- Debenture Series: SML-11.76%-14-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: December 29, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: December 29, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification requiring temporary trading suspension in accordance with existing debenture market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Trading Members are required to note and comply with the no-dealings restriction
- Debenture holders as per the record date (December 29, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of a specific debenture series of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to prevent trading in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025. The suspension is procedural and relates to a single debt instrument.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of December 29, 2025 will receive their scheduled interest payment and principal redemption. No trading will be possible in these debentures from the suspension date.
