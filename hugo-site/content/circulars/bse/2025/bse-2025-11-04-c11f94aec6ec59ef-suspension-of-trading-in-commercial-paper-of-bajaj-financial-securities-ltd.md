## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 730472, ISIN: INE01C314DC5) effective November 06, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument's scheduled redemption date of November 07, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper Scrip Code: 730472
- Issuer: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- ISIN: INE01C314DC5
- Effective suspension date: November 06, 2025
- Redemption/Maturity date: November 07, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251104-5
- Reference: DR-750/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for suspension of trading due to instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper (ISIN: INE01C314DC5) from November 06, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **November 04, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 06, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 07, 2025**: Commercial paper maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only a single commercial paper instrument approaching maturity. Trading members dealing in Bajaj Financial Securities Limited's debt instruments should note the suspension, but this does not affect equity shares or other securities of the company. The action is procedural and expected for maturing debt instruments.
