## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 17, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying a 9.25% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and maturing on November 13, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977296
- **Scrip ID**: 925MMFL027
- **ISIN**: INE101Q07BT9
- **Quantity Listed**: 5,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A (Stable)
- **Interest Rate**: 9.25% per annum
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly from December 10, 2025 to November 13, 2027
- **Date of Allotment**: November 13, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: November 13, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE101Q07BT9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact the debt department at phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: November 13, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing announcement with minimal market impact. The private placement of 5,000 debt securities by Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited adds to the available debt instruments on BSE's debt segment. The securities carry an ICRA A (Stable) rating indicating adequate safety with moderate credit risk. The impact is limited to investors in debt securities and does not affect broader equity market operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new listing for their client portfolios.
