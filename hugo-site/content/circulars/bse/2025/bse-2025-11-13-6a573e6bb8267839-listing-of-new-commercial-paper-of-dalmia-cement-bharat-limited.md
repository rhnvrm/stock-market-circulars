## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis, effective November 13, 2025. The instruments are admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730550, Scrip ID: DCBL121125
- ISIN: INE755K14FQ8
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,473.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE755K14FQ8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- Allotment Date: November 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term funding instruments for Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited. The listing expands debt market options for institutional investors and trading members dealing in corporate debt securities. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
