## Summary

BSE has listed 18,84,671 equity shares of Adani Green Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 541450) with effect from Friday, October 17, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 1470.75 per share to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until April 19, 2027.

## Key Points

- Number of shares listed: 18,84,671 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 1480.75 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 1470.75 premium)
- Allotment type: Preferential allotment to promoters
- Basis: Conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 2259400830 to 2261285500
- ISIN: INE364U01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** April 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as:
- All shares are allotted to promoters and subject to lock-in until April 2027
- No immediate free float dilution
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- This represents conversion of previously disclosed warrant allotment
- Promoter holding will increase but shares remain non-tradeable during lock-in period
