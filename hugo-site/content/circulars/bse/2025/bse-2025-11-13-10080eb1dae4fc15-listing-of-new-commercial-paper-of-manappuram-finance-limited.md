## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 13, 2025. The issuance consists of 3000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with AXIS Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Manappuram Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 3000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,69,407.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730547
- **Scrip ID**: MFL121125
- **ISIN**: INE522D14OV9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: AXIS Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OV9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- All transactions subject to debt segment trading rules and regulations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025 (effective immediately)
- **Redemption Date**: September 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing for Manappuram Finance Limited with a tenor of approximately 10 months. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial paper instruments. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
