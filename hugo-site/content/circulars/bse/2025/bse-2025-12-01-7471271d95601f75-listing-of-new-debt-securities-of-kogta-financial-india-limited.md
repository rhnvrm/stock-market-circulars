## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kogta Financial (India) Limited on private placement basis, effective December 1, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with ISIN INE192U07376 (further listing under same ISIN).

## Key Points

- Quantity: 12,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977263
- Scrip ID: 95KFIL27
- Detail Name: KFIL-9.5%-30-10-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE192U07376 (Further Listing)
- Credit Rating: CARE A+/Positive
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,729.00
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only

## Security Details

- **Interest Rate**: 9.5% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually on October 30 (2026 to 2027)
- **Date of Allotment**: November 27, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: October 30, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN INE192U07376
- Tick size of 1 paise applies to all trades
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: October 30, 2026 and October 30, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands trading options for institutional investors and debt market participants interested in Kogta Financial securities. The CARE A+/Positive rating indicates stable credit quality. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for client advisory purposes.
