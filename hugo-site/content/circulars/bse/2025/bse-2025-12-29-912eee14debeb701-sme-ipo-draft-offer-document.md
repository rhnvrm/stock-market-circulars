## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited, filed on December 28, 2025, for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited on December 28, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document must remain available on the exchange website for a minimum period of 21 days
- Public stakeholders may submit comments on the draft offer document to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- Document must contain all mandatory disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **December 28, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited
- **December 29, 2025**: Notice issued and document made available for public review
- **January 19, 2026** (approximately): Deadline for public comments (21 days from December 29, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with minimal immediate market impact. The announcement is primarily informational, notifying potential investors and stakeholders about the availability of the Draft Offer Document for Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited's proposed SME IPO. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. No trading implications or immediate compliance actions are required for existing market participants. Impact is limited to those interested in the SME IPO opportunity or wishing to provide feedback on the draft offer document.
