## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-bill 815GUJ2025 (Scrip Code: 810586, ISIN: IN1520150088) effective November 24, 2025, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the specified suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 815GUJ2025 will mature on its redemption date
- Scrip Code: 810586
- ISIN: IN1520150088
- Trading suspension effective from November 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-762/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill from November 24, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the suspension to avoid failed trade attempts

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 24, 2025 (effective from this date)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: On or around November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a single T-bill instrument in the debt segment. T-bill maturities are routine market events with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the suspension and avoid executing trades in this instrument. Standard maturity handling procedures apply.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific T-bill who will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms.
