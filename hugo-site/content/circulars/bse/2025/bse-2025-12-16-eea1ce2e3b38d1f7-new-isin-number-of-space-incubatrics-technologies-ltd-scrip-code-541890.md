## Summary

BSE has announced the new ISIN number for Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 541890) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-. The new ISIN INE797Z01028 will be effective for trades executed on and from the ex-date of December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- New ISIN number: INE797Z01028
- Company: SPACE INCUBATRICS TECHNOLOGIES LTD
- Scrip Code: 541890
- Corporate action: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- Effective date: December 19, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- Reference: DR-781/2025-2026
- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251208-9 dated December 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action implementation notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN number INE797Z01028 for equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- All trades from the ex-date onwards must use the new ISIN
- Members should ensure proper handling of the sub-divided shares in their trading and settlement systems

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Ex-Date**: December 19, 2025 (new ISIN becomes effective)
- **Previous Notice Reference**: December 08, 2025 (Notice No. 20251208-9)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only holders and traders of Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd shares.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new ISIN for the sub-divided shares. The share price will adjust proportionally due to the 10:1 split ratio (Rs. 10 to Re. 1), increasing liquidity and making shares more accessible to retail investors. No impact on total market capitalization of the company.
