## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies regarding unusual price or volume movements in their securities. Bonlon Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543211), Coromandel International Limited (Scrip Code: 506395), and Hind Aluminium Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531979) responded to BSE surveillance queries, confirming compliance with disclosure requirements and attributing the movements to market conditions.

## Key Points

- **Bonlon Industries Limited**: Responded to BSE ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3248 on December 30, 2025, confirming timely disclosure of all events under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015 with no information withheld
- **Coromandel International Limited**: Responded to BSE ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/822 on December 31, 2025, regarding volume movement, stating no pending material information and attributing volume increase to market conditions
- **Hind Aluminium Industries Limited**: Responded to BSE ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3249 on December 30, 2025, confirming compliance with Regulation 30 and attributing price changes to market circumstances
- All three companies confirmed regular compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- All companies stated there is no undisclosed price-sensitive information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular is routine disclosure of company responses to surveillance queries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015 regarding timely disclosure of material events
- Companies must clarify if any undisclosed information exists that could impact share price or trading volume

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Bonlon Industries Limited and Hind Aluminium Industries Limited submitted clarifications
- **December 31, 2025**: Coromandel International Limited submitted clarification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine surveillance responses with no new material information disclosed. All companies confirmed existing compliance and attributed movements to normal market dynamics.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral - Investors are informed that no undisclosed developments are driving price/volume changes in these stocks.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard surveillance and disclosure process with no operational changes required.
