## Summary

BSE has announced demat auction for Settlement No. 774 dated December 11, 2025. The auction covers 90 securities with various quantities that need to be auctioned due to delivery shortfalls. The securities range from individual shares to large quantities, with the highest being 432,980 shares of Magellanic Cloud and 300,002 shares of Spright Agro.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 774
- Auction Date: December 11, 2025
- Total Securities Involved: 90 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 432,980 shares
- Covers diverse sectors including power, consumer goods, real estate, technology, and manufacturing

## Impact Assessment

### Market Impact

- **Medium Impact**: Affects 90 securities across multiple sectors
- Auction mechanism ensures delivery obligations are met
- Price discovery through auction process may differ from market prices
- Participants with short positions bear the risk of auction premium

### Operational Impact

- Brokers and clearing members with shortfalls must arrange funds
- Large quantity auctions (Magellanic Cloud, Spright Agro, Captain Pipe) may face liquidity challenges
- Routine settlement process to address delivery failures
- Ensures market integrity and settlement discipline
