## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from November 25, 2025. The issue comprises 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Infina Finance Private Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Quantity: 3000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,85,020.50 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs.150 crore
- Scrip Code: 730644
- Scrip ID: IFL241125
- ISIN: INE879F14MB4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Trading Details

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading segment: BSE Debt Market

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 24, 2025
- Listing Date: November 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: April 30, 2026
- Tenor: Approximately 5 months

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14MB4
- Adherence to standard denomination requirements of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- All trades subject to 1 paise tick size

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper issuance adds short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment, providing investment options for institutional participants. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial paper instruments.
