## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment with effect from December 4, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 24,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, representing a total issuance of Rs. 240 crore. The bonds carry an interest rate of 8.86% per annum with ICRA AA credit rating and mature on December 1, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977343, Scrip ID: 886OPL28
- ISIN: INE248U07GE1
- Total Quantity: 24,000 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA
- Interest Rate: 8.86% per annum payable annually
- Date of Allotment: December 3, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07GE1
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 3, 2025
- Listing Date: December 4, 2025
- First Interest Payment: December 3, 2026
- Subsequent Interest Payments: Annually from December 3, 2026 to December 1, 2028
- Redemption Date: December 1, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis affecting only debt market participants. The ICRA AA rating indicates strong credit quality. The 8.86% interest rate reflects current market conditions for AA-rated corporate bonds with 3-year maturity. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
