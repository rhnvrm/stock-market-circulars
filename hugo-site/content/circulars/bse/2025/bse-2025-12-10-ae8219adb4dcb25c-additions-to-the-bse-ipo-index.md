## Summary

BSE announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective December 12, 2025. The companies are MEESHO LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544632), VIDYA WIRES LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544633), and AEQUS LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544634), which were listed on BSE on December 10, 2025. This is a routine index reconstitution following new company listings.

## Key Points

- Three companies being added to BSE IPO Index: MEESHO LIMITED, VIDYA WIRES LIMITED, and AEQUS LIMITED
- All three companies listed on BSE on Wednesday, December 10, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on Friday, December 12, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to rebalance their portfolios to include these three stocks.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium. The addition of three stocks to the BSE IPO Index will trigger buying activity from passive funds and ETFs that track this index. This typically results in increased liquidity and trading volumes for the newly added securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low. This is a routine index maintenance action. Market participants tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios, but no changes to trading mechanisms or compliance procedures are required.

**Investor Impact**: Investors in index funds or ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will gain exposure to these three newly listed companies as part of their passive investment strategy.
