## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific bonds of POWER GRID CORPORATION OF INDIA LIMITED effective December 8, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for bond redemption and interest payment. The affected bond series is PGCIL-8.32%-23-12-25-PVT with ISIN INE752E07NK9 (Code: 953417).

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for POWER GRID CORPORATION bonds from December 8, 2025
- Bond series: PGCIL-8.32%-23-12-25-PVT (INE752E07NK9)
- Record date set for December 8, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these bonds from the suspension date
- Circular reference: DR-772/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified bonds from December 8, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to clients
- No dealings permitted in bond series INE752E07NK9 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Purpose Effective Date:** December 8, 2025 (for redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The suspension affects only bondholders of the specific POWER GRID CORPORATION series (8.32% bonds). Investors holding these bonds will not be able to trade them from December 8, 2025, but will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in the affected ISIN and inform clients holding these bonds about the suspension and upcoming redemption.

**Investor Impact:** Bondholders as of record date (December 8, 2025) will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. This is a scheduled maturity event based on the bond series naming convention (23-12-25 indicating December 2025 maturity).
