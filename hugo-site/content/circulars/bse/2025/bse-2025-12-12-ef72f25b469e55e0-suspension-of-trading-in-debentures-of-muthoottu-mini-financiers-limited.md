## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited effective December 18, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MMFL 9.75% NCDs (ISIN: INE101Q07AF0, Code: 937817)
- Record date for redemption: December 18, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 18, 2025
- Circular issued under notice number DR-780/2025-2026
- Trading members must cease all transactions in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension aligned with existing debenture redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from December 18, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- All market participants must honor the record date for redemption purposes

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 18, 2025**: Record date for redemption
- **December 18, 2025**: No dealings commence (trading suspension effective)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of specific MMFL NCDs. The suspension is procedural and related to scheduled redemption rather than credit concerns.

**Investor Impact**: Medium impact for NCD holders who must hold securities through record date to be eligible for redemption proceeds. No trading flexibility available from December 18, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended securities. Standard corporate action processing applies for redemption settlement.
