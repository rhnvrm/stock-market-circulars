## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED (CIN: L27320GJ2022PLC135697). The company has officially changed its name to DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED effective September 29, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED
- **New Name**: DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED
- **Effective Date**: September 29, 2025
- **CIN**: L27320GJ2022PLC135697
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Address**: 5, Harekrishana Industrial Estate, Bakrol, Daskroi, Ahmedabad-382430, Gujarat, India
- **Certificate Issued By**: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- **Approved By**: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

The name change has been approved under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29. The certificate has been digitally signed by the Registrar of Companies under rule 9(2) of the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED) along with the new name for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Digitally Signed**: October 8, 2025 at 15:12:01 IST
- **Old Name Display Requirement**: Must be displayed for 2 years from effective date (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative corporate action that does not change the fundamental business operations or financial position of the company.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all its documents, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts and obligations continue under the new name.

**Compliance Impact**: The company must ensure proper disclosure and display of both old and new names as per statutory requirements for the next two years.
