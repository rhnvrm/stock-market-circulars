## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new debt securities aggregating Rs. 200 crore (20,000 securities at Rs. 1,00,000 face value) on BSE's Debt segment with effect from November 3, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with an allotment date of October 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 20,000 debt securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,443.40 per security
- **Scrip Code**: 977005
- **Scrip ID**: 804AFL35
- **ISIN**: INE891K08224 (further listings under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AAA/Stable, IND AAA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 8.04% per annum (payable annually)
- **Tenure**: ~10 years (redemption on August 8, 2035)
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891K08224
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Redemption Date**: August 8, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The securities are rated AAA by CRISIL and IND, indicating highest credit quality. The listing provides institutional investors with an additional fixed-income investment option. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
