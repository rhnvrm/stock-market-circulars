## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 2000 securities of Rs.5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs.100 crore. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 1, 2025, with a maturity period of 90 days until February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Issuer: SBICAP Securities Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Quantity: 2000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.4,91,945.50 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs.100 crore
- Scrip Code: 730670
- Scrip ID: SSL281125
- ISIN: INE212K14CU9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CU9
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be settled through the specified ISIN number
- Contact BSE Debt Department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Affects only debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial papers. The listing provides short-term funding avenue for SBICAP Securities and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated short-term debt instruments.

**Liquidity**: Trading restricted to institutional participants due to high minimum denomination of Rs.5 lakhs, limiting retail participation.
