## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Excelsoft Technologies Limited. This represents the anchor book portion of the IPO where qualified institutional buyers participate before the public offering opens.

## Key Points

- Public issue of Excelsoft Technologies Limited proceeding with anchor investor allocation
- Anchor investment portion completed as per SEBI IPO guidelines
- Allocation details disclosed to the market as per regulatory requirements
- Standard procedure before main IPO opens to retail and other investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and market participants to note the anchor allocation details
- Information to be disseminated as per listing agreement requirements
- Anchor investor lock-in period as per SEBI regulations will apply

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 18, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation: Completed as of circular date
- Main IPO dates to follow as per RHP schedule

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact - Anchor investor allocation is a key milestone in the IPO process that provides price discovery and demonstrates institutional investor confidence. Successful anchor allocation typically indicates strong demand for the public issue and sets the stage for the retail portion of the offering.
