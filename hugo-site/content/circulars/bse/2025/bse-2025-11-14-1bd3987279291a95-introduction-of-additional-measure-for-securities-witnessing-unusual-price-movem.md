## Summary

BSE has introduced additional surveillance measures for securities witnessing unusual price movements. One new security, Avishkar Infra Realty Ltd, has been added to the surveillance list effective November 17, 2025. The consolidated list now includes 10 securities subject to these additional measures.

## Key Points

- Avishkar Infra Realty Ltd (Scrip Code: 508929, ISIN: INE433O01024) newly added to surveillance
- Total of 10 securities currently under additional surveillance measures
- Measures target securities with unusual price movements
- Consolidated list includes securities from various sectors

## Regulatory Changes

BSE has implemented additional surveillance measures for securities exhibiting unusual price movements. This framework applies enhanced monitoring and potential trading restrictions to protect market integrity.

## Compliance Requirements

Traders and investors dealing in the following securities must be aware of additional surveillance measures:

1. Avishkar Infra Realty Ltd (508929)
2. Citizen Infoline Ltd (538786)
3. Colab Platforms Ltd (542866)
4. Dugar Housing Developments Ltd (511634)
5. EMA India Ltd (522027)
6. Mardia Samyoung Capillary Tubes Company Ltd (513544)
7. Omansh Enterprises Ltd (538537)
8. Oswal Overseas Ltd (531065)
9. RRP Defense Ltd (530929)
10. RRP Semiconductor Ltd (504346)

Market participants should exercise caution and be prepared for potential enhanced margin requirements or trading restrictions on these securities.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: November 17, 2025 - Additional surveillance measures take effect for newly added securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The additional surveillance measures may result in reduced liquidity and increased trading costs for the affected securities. Investors holding positions in these stocks should be prepared for potential margin increases and stricter monitoring.

**Operational Impact**: Brokers and trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the surveillance status of these securities. Enhanced due diligence and client communication regarding risks associated with these securities will be required.

**Investor Impact**: Retail and institutional investors should exercise heightened caution when trading these securities, as unusual price movements have triggered regulatory oversight. This may indicate higher volatility or potential manipulation concerns.
