## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis in the debt segment. The instrument consists of 100 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5 Crore, with an issue price of Rs. 4,64,774 per security. The commercial paper was allotted on November 28, 2025 and will be redeemed on November 27, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 100 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 5 Crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,64,774 per security (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730679, Scrip ID: AEL281125
- ISIN Number: INE423A14YP7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YP7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members can contact BSE debt department (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: December 01, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 28, 2025
- Date of Redemption: November 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of short-term debt instrument with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides Adani Enterprises with short-term financing for approximately one year. The high credit ratings (CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The listing expands the available debt instruments in BSE's debt segment but has no direct impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
