## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis effective August 20, 2025. The securities are market-linked with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 660 securities (Rs. 66 crores total)
- Scrip Code: 976954
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,888 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for further details
- Contact debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Date of Redemption: January 22, 2029
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (Cumulative)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing on private placement basis. The securities are market-linked and carry AA/Stable rating, indicating good credit quality for institutional investors.
