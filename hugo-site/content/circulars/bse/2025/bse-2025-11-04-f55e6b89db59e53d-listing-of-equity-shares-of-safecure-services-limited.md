## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Safecure Services Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544596, symbol SAFECURE, and ISIN INE0SVZ01015. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Safecure Services Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544596
- Trading Symbol: SAFECURE
- ISIN Number: INE0SVZ01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the final listing date notification.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 04, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for an SME segment company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in SME stocks. The listing will provide Safecure Services Limited with access to capital markets and offer investment opportunities in the SME segment. Trading members should await the separate notice with specific listing date and security details before any trading activity can commence.
