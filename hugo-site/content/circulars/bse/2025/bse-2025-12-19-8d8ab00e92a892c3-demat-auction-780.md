## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities for demat auction under Settlement No. 780 dated December 19, 2025. The auction includes 77 scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 to 146,000 shares. This auction is conducted to facilitate settlement of trading obligations where physical delivery has not been completed.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 780
- Date: December 19, 2025
- Total scrips included: 77 different securities
- Quantity range: 1 share (minimum) to 146,000 shares (maximum for Enbee Trade)
- Notable high-quantity auctions: Enbee Trade (146,000), Indiabulls Living (29,300), Indowind Energy (5,000), Avance Technologies (5,000), GEM Enviro Management (3,200)
- Covers diverse sectors including financial services, technology, manufacturing, hospitality, infrastructure, and retail

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for settlement auction.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement of trades must be completed through the auction mechanism for the specified quantities
- Members should ensure adequate funds/securities are available for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 19, 2025
- Auction Settlement: As per BSE auction settlement schedule for Settlement No. 780

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 77 scrips with varying liquidity profiles. Most quantities are relatively small except for a few securities.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - brokers and clearing members with obligations in these scrips need to participate in the auction to complete settlement. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of failed trades.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - primarily affects institutional participants and brokers with settlement obligations. Retail investors holding these securities are not directly impacted unless they are part of unsettled trades.

**Liquidity Impact:** Varied - securities with large auction quantities (like Enbee Trade with 146,000 shares) may experience temporary price pressure during the auction settlement period.
