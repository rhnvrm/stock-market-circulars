## Summary

BSE has announced that Stable Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6829) will cease to conduct business in the Debt and OBPP (Odd Lot Buy-Back and Payout Platform) segments effective November 27, 2025. This cessation is due to the voluntary closure of the member's business operations.

## Key Points

- Stable Broking Private Limited holds BSE Member Number 6829
- The member will cease operations in Debt and OBPP segments only
- Closure is voluntary and initiated by the member
- Effective date is Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Trading members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements are specified for other trading members. This is an informational notice about one member's voluntary exit from specific segments.

## Important Dates

- **November 26, 2025**: Notice date
- **November 27, 2025**: Effective date of cessation of business in Debt and OBPP segments

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this closure is minimal and localized. The voluntary exit of one trading member from the Debt and OBPP segments does not affect overall market operations or other trading members. The notice serves as an informational update to the trading community about changes in membership status. Other members can continue their operations without any changes to their business activities.
