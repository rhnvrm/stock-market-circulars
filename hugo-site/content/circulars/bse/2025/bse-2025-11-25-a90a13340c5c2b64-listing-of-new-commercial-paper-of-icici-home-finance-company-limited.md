## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 25, 2025. The listing comprises 4500 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, issued on a private placement basis. The securities carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will mature on February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Listed**: 4500 Commercial Paper units
- **Face Value**: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs.4,92,606.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730647
- **Scrip ID**: IHF251125
- **ISIN**: INE071G14HG5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI BANK LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE071G14HG5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing with limited market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Affects only debt market participants and investors interested in short-term debt instruments of ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED.

**Investor Relevance**: Relevant for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals seeking short-term investment opportunities in highly-rated commercial paper (A1+ rating from both CARE and ICRA indicates highest safety for short-term instruments).
