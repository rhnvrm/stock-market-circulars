## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Physicswallah Limited on the exchange. This circular provides details regarding the listing of the company's equity shares following the completion of regulatory requirements.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Physicswallah Limited are being listed on BSE
- The listing follows completion of necessary regulatory and compliance procedures
- Trading members and investors can now trade in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to include the new scrip
- Standard listing compliance requirements apply to Physicswallah Limited
- The company must comply with ongoing listing obligations as per SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing details as per exchange notification

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Physicswallah Limited equity shares expands the universe of tradable securities on BSE. As an education technology company, this listing provides investors access to the EdTech sector. Trading members should configure their systems to accommodate the new security for trading operations.
