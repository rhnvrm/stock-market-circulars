## Summary

BSE announced that LENSKART SOLUTIONS LIMITED (Exchange ticker - 544600) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, November 12, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE on Monday, November 10, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE).

## Key Points

- LENSKART SOLUTIONS LIMITED (ticker: 544600) to be added to BSE IPO Index
- Company listing on BSE: November 10, 2025
- Index addition effective date: November 12, 2025 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file
- Notice references prior notification No: 20251107-66

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to rebalance their portfolios to include LENSKART SOLUTIONS LIMITED effective November 12, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **November 10, 2025**: LENSKART SOLUTIONS LIMITED listing on BSE
- **November 12, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This addition will impact passive funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index, requiring them to purchase shares of LENSKART SOLUTIONS LIMITED as part of their rebalancing. The two-day gap between listing and index inclusion allows for price discovery and market stabilization. Index investors should expect typical rebalancing activity on November 12, 2025.
