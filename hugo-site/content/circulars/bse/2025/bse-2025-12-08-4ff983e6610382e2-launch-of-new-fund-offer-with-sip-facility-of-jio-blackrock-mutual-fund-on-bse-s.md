## Summary

JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND is launching JIOBLACKROCK ARBITRAGE FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from December 09, 2025, to December 11, 2025, with an extended window until 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025, for lumpsum purchases only. The tentative allotment date is December 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO for JIOBLACKROCK ARBITRAGE FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF22M001101)
- NFO period: December 09-11, 2025
- Tentative allotment date: December 16, 2025
- Extended hours until 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025, for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 16, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to receipt of clear funds by allotment date
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure transactions comply with fund payment timelines
- Clients must initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed by 3:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on December 16, 2025
- For switches, funds for switch-out scheme must be received before NFO allotment date

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: December 09, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: December 11, 2025 (3:00 PM for SIP registrations)
- **Extended Period for Lumpsum**: Until 6:00 PM on December 11, 2025
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on December 16, 2025
- **Payment Pending Deadline**: 3:00 PM on last day of NFO or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine NFO launch with no impact on secondary market trading or existing securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low impact limited to BSE StAR MF Platform members and mutual fund distributors who wish to participate in this NFO. Standard operational procedures apply for fund collection and allotment process.

**Investor Impact**: Provides new investment opportunity for investors seeking arbitrage fund exposure through JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND. Relevant only to those specifically interested in this fund category.
