## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of UNISEM AGRITECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544648, symbol UNISEM, and ISIN number INE1FHV01026. This listing will be on the SME segment of BSE.

## Key Points

- Company Name: UNISEM AGRITECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544648
- Symbol: UNISEM
- ISIN Number: INE1FHV01026
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the separate notice containing the listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new company on the SME segment. The impact is limited to trading members who will have a new security available for trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate market impact expected as this is a preliminary notification pending formal listing date announcement.
