## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited (HLFL) on its Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with ISIN INE146O08399, comprising 25,000 debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying a 9.25% annual interest rate and maturing on July 9, 2031.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977200, Scrip ID: 925HLFL31
- ISIN: INE146O08399 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 25,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,596.5753
- Date of Allotment: December 11, 2025
- Interest Rate: 9.25% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA+/STABLE, CRISIL AA+/STABLE
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE146O08399
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from October 9, 2026 to July 9, 2031
- **Redemption Date**: July 9, 2031

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis.

**Operational Impact**: Affects only BSE Debt segment participants and investors in Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited debt instruments. The securities carry strong credit ratings (AA+ from both CARE and CRISIL), indicating low credit risk. No put/call options are available on these debentures.
