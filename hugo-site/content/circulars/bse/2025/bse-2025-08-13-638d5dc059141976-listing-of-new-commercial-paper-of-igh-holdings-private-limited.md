## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of IGH Holdings Private Limited on the debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crores (2000 units).

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,978 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip code: 730014
- ISIN: INE02FN14598

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 13, 2025
- Date of redemption: November 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for IGH Holdings Private Limited with 91-day maturity period. Standard commercial paper offering with established credit ratings.
