## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Navi Finserv Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 520 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, with trading commencing on December 19, 2025. The Commercial Papers carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will mature on July 17, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 520 Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue price: Rs. 4,72,546.50 per security
- Scrip Code: 730811, Scrip ID: NFL181225
- ISIN: INE342T14FG7
- Credit Rating: IND A1+
- Market lot size: 1
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE342T14FG7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: July 17, 2026

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding for Navi Finserv Limited through the issuance of highly-rated (IND A1+) commercial paper.
