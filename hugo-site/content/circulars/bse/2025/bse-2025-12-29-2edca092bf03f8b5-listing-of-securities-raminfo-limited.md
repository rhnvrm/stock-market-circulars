## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 6,47,449 equity shares of RAMINFO LIMITED (Scrip Code: 530951) issued on preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading from December 30, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 6,47,449 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 140.50 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 130.50)
- Allotment type: Preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Allotted to: Promoters and Non-Promoters
- Distinctive numbers: 7542466 to 8189914
- Date of allotment: September 26, 2025
- ISIN: INE357B01022
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these securities
- Lock-in provisions apply to all allotted shares as per specified schedules

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 30, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial - 2,47,449 shares)**: June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Remaining 4,00,000 shares)**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected. The preferential allotment represents a modest capital raise through warrant conversion. Lock-in provisions for 2,47,449 shares until June 2026 and 4,00,000 shares until June 2027 will restrict immediate supply. The shares rank equally with existing equity, maintaining uniform shareholder rights.
