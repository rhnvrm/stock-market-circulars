## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 175 crores (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ with maturity date of October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: 3,500 units (Rs. 175 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,885 per unit
- Scrip code: 729956, ISIN: INE755K14FK1
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 1, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 4, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
