## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 8000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 400 crores. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 8000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 400 crores
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,363.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730529
- **Scrip ID**: ABRE111125
- **ISIN**: INE055A14KJ9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: State Bank of India (SBI)
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE055A14KJ9
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term funding to Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited. The instrument carries strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL), indicating highest safety for short-term debt.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. The instrument is available only in dematerialized form with a minimum investment of Rs. 5 Lakhs, restricting participation to institutional and high net-worth investors.

**Company Impact**: Provides short-term working capital funding of Rs. 400 crores to Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited at competitive rates, with redemption in approximately 3 months.
