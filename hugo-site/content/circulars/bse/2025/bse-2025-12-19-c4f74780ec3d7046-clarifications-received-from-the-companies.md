## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from two companies - Shish Industries Limited (Security Code: 540693) and Amines & Plasticizers Limited (Security Code: 506248) - in response to BSE's surveillance queries regarding significant movement in their equity share prices. Both companies confirmed they have no undisclosed material information and attributed the price movements to market-driven factors and general market conditions.

## Key Points

- Shish Industries Limited confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations and disclosed no pending material information
- Company stated the price movement appears purely market-driven and attributable to general market conditions and investor sentiment
- Amines & Plasticizers Limited clarified no withheld information or price-sensitive information pending disclosure
- Both companies confirmed continued adherence to disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- The clarifications were provided in response to BSE surveillance queries dated December 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding significant price movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies must disclose if any material information, events, or pending announcements exist that may impact share price
- Ongoing requirement to promptly intimate stock exchanges of any material events or information

## Important Dates

- December 18, 2025: BSE surveillance query sent to Amines & Plasticizers Limited
- December 18, 2025: Amines & Plasticizers Limited response date
- December 19, 2025: Shish Industries Limited response date
- December 19, 2025: Circular publication date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - These are routine clarifications with no new material information disclosed. Both companies confirmed no undisclosed events or announcements, indicating the price movements were due to normal market dynamics.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Investors receive confirmation that no material undisclosed information exists for these companies. The clarifications provide transparency but do not reveal any new substantive information affecting investment decisions.

**Operational Impact:** None - No operational changes or requirements for market participants. This is standard surveillance activity by BSE to ensure compliance with disclosure norms and market integrity.
