## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Namdev Finvest Private Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 12, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 11.56% per annum with half-yearly payments and maturing on December 11, 2030. The securities carry a CARE BBB+/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Listed**: 18,000 debt securities
- **Scrip Code**: 977365
- **Scrip ID**: 1156NFPL30
- **ISIN**: INE0IX207262
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 11.56% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: CARE BBB+/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE0IX207262
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department on specified telephone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Schedule**: Half-yearly from June 11, 2025 to December 11, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 11, 2030
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities that affects only participants in the debt market and specific investors interested in Namdev Finvest Private Limited securities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure with no special trading conditions beyond dematerialised trading requirements.

**Investor Relevance**: The securities offer a moderate return of 11.56% per annum with investment-grade credit rating (CARE BBB+/Stable), suitable for debt investors seeking fixed-income instruments with approximately 5-year maturity. The high face value of Rs. 1,00,000 indicates these are targeted at institutional or high-net-worth investors.
