## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD with ISIN INE07HK07734 (Security Code: 975736) effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Security affected: KSL-12.5%-12-12-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07734)
- Security Code: 975736
- Record Date: November 27, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 27, 2025
- Members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension
- All pending orders for these debentures should be cancelled before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issued
- **November 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **November 27, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only a single debenture series of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for specific debenture holders who will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. No trading opportunity available from November 27, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine corporate action procedure. Trading members need to ensure no orders are executed for this specific ISIN from the suspension date.
