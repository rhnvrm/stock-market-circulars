## Summary

BSE has listed 40,000 Commercial Papers issued by Tata Steel Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 2, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 20,000 crore in aggregate value, with credit ratings of ICRA A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Tata Steel Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Short-term debt)
- **Quantity**: 40,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,814 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730682
- **Scrip ID**: TSL011225
- **ISIN**: INE081A14GJ3
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE081A14GJ3
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard tick size of 1 paise applies
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 28, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 89 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine commercial paper issuance by Tata Steel for short-term working capital requirements. The private placement nature means it targets institutional investors rather than retail markets.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard debt instrument listing with no special trading conditions. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors participating in the private placement. The 89-day tenure makes this a short-term money market instrument with modest yield expectations based on the discount to face value.
