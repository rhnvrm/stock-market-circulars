## Summary

BSE has sought clarification from ITI Ltd (Scrip Code: 523610) and Waaree Energies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544277) regarding news articles appearing in various media platforms. The exchange initiated this action to verify the accuracy of news reports and to inform members and investors. As of December 19, 2025, replies are still awaited from both companies.

## Key Points

- BSE requested clarification from two companies regarding media reports
- ITI Ltd (Scrip Code: 523610) - news article from moneycontrol.com
- Waaree Energies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544277) - news article from Economic Times Energy
- Replies awaited from both companies as of notice date
- Action taken under BSE's surveillance function

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

The companies are required to:
- Respond to BSE's clarification request regarding media reports
- Provide accurate information to verify news article claims
- Ensure proper disclosure to investors and market participants

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Reply Status**: Awaited from both companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard surveillance procedure where exchanges verify media reports. No trading restrictions or regulatory action has been announced.

**Investor Impact**: Informational - Investors are made aware that certain news articles are under verification. Market participants should await official clarification from the companies before making investment decisions based on the referenced news articles.

**Companies Affected**: ITI Ltd and Waaree Energies Ltd are required to provide responses to address the news articles in question.
