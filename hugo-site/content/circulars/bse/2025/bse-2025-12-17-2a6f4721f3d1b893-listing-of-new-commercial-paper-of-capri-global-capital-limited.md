## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis effective December 17, 2025. The instrument consists of 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (total Rs. 50 crores) with CRISIL A1+ rating, issued at a discount of Rs. 486,081 per unit and maturing on April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Quantity: 1000 units (Total value: Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730794
- Scrip ID: CGCL171225
- ISIN: INE180C14631
- Issue Price: Rs. 486,081 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading: Dematerialised form only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Bank of Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE180C14631
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 4.5 months

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on market operations. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing on BSE's debt segment. The commercial paper provides Capri Global Capital Limited with short-term funding and offers debt market participants an A1+ rated investment option with 4-month maturity. Trading is restricted to debt segment participants with minimum investment of Rs. 5 lakhs.
