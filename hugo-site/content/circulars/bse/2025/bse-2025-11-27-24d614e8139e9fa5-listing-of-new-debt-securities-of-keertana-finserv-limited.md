## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Keertana Finserv Limited on a private placement basis with effect from November 27, 2025. The issuance consists of 5,000 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.40% per annum with monthly interest payments and will mature on October 24, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977241, Scrip ID: 1140KRFL27
- ISIN: INE0NES07287 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 50,000 units (5,000 debentures)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,006.25
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Clarifications can be obtained from BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 27, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: November 24, 2025 to October 24, 2027 (Monthly)
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it is a private placement of debt securities by Keertana Finserv Limited. The notification is primarily informational for trading members to facilitate trading on the BSE Debt segment. The BBB+ credit rating indicates moderate credit quality with adequate capacity to meet financial commitments.
