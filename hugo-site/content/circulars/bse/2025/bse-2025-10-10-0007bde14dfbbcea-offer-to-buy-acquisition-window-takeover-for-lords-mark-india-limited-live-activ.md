## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of LORDS MARK INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: LORDSMARK, ISIN: INE567L01017, Scrip Code: 501261). The offer is scheduled from Monday, October 13, 2025 to Tuesday, October 28, 2025, with an offer size of up to 2,60,000 equity shares at Rs. 30.00 per share (face value Rs. 10.00).

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: LORDS MARK INDIA LIMITED (LORDSMARK)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 2,60,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 30.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **ISIN**: INE567L01017
- **Scrip Code**: 501261
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC of investors must be registered/updated with correct PAN and Category in Exchange database

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for conducting an acquisition offer through BSE's OTB (Offer to Buy) module.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure investor UCC is registered with correct PAN Number and Category
- Trading Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- Members must access OTB module through specified URLs (iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet)
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Monday, October 13, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, October 28, 2025
- **Bid Offering Period**: October 13-28, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: October 13-27, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific acquisition offer with limited scope (2.6 lakh shares maximum). The takeover offer will only impact existing shareholders of LORDS MARK INDIA LIMITED who wish to participate.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading Members and Custodians need to ensure proper system access and investor registration compliance. The notice provides detailed contact information for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet access support.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Buyers (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Other NIBs (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).
