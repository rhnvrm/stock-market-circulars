## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of ONGC Petro additions Limited. The commercial paper with scrip code 729298 and ISIN INE163N14527 is scheduled to mature on its redemption date, and consequently, trading will be suspended from October 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of ONGC Petro additions Limited (Scrip Code: 729298) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE163N14527
- Suspension effective from October 27, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-743/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice regarding scheduled maturity of debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforesaid Commercial Paper from October 27, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** October 27, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** October 28, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine suspension due to scheduled maturity of a commercial paper instrument. Trading Members holding positions should be aware that the instrument will no longer be tradable from the specified date.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard debt instrument lifecycle event with no unexpected circumstances. Trading Members need to ensure compliance with the suspension directive.
