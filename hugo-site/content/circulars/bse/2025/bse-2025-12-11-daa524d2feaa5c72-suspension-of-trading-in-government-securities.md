## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in a specific Treasury Bill (T-bill) with ISIN IN001225C076 and scrip code 800448, identified as GS16DEC2025C. The suspension becomes effective from December 12, 2025, as the T-bill is scheduled to mature on December 16, 2025 (redemption date). Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date onwards.

- T-bill with scrip code 800448 and ISIN IN001225C076 (GS16DEC2025C) to be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from December 12, 2025
- Reason for suspension: upcoming maturity on December 16, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-776/2025-2026
- Trading members instructed not to deal in this security post-suspension

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension notice for a maturing government security.

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified T-bill (scrip code 800448, ISIN IN001225C076) from December 12, 2025
- Members must note and comply with the suspension to avoid trading in a security approaching maturity

## Important Dates

- **December 11, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of this specific T-bill as it approaches its scheduled maturity date. The short window between suspension and maturity (4 days) is standard practice to facilitate orderly redemption. No broader market implications or systemic impact expected. Trading members holding positions in this T-bill should prepare for redemption on December 16, 2025.
