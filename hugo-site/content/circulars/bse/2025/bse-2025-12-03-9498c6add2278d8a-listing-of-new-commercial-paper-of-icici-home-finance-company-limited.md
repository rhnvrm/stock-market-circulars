## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed Commercial Paper worth Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment effective December 3, 2025. The instrument is issued on private placement basis with a 26-day tenor, maturing on December 29, 2025. The paper carries CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings, indicating highest safety for short-term debt obligations.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units)
- Face value per unit: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,854.50 per unit (discount of Rs. 2,145.50)
- Scrip Code: 730693
- Scrip ID: IHF031225
- ISIN: INE071G14HH3
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard commercial paper listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HH3
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Adherence to tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Tenor**: 26 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument is a short-term debt facility used for working capital purposes. With top-tier credit ratings (A1+), it represents low credit risk. The private placement nature and institutional ticket size (Rs. 5 lakh minimum) limit participation to qualified institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or retail investors expected.
