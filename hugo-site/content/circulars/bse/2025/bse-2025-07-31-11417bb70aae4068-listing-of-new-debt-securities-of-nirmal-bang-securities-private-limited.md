## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of NIRMAL BANG SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED worth ₹49.65 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 10.25% p.a. interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on January 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4,965 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976970, ISIN: INE681R08125
- Interest Rate: 10.25% per annum paid quarterly
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A-/Stable
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Trading only in dematerialized form with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms and conditions

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 30, 2025
- Listing Date: July 31, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 30, 2027
- Interest Payment: Quarterly

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities provide institutional investors access to CRISIL A- rated debt instrument with attractive 10.25% yield. Trading members can contact BSE debt department for clarifications.
