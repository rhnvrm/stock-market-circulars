## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of ANZEN INDIA ENERGY YIELD PLUS TRUST (InvIT) effective November 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for AIEYPT-8.01%-01-12-25-INVIT debentures (ISIN: INE0MIZ07012, Scrip Code: 974399)
- Record date fixed as November 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from November 24, 2025 onwards
- Circular reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 24, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Record Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of ANZEN INDIA ENERGY YIELD PLUS TRUST debentures. This is a routine suspension for corporate action processing.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive interest payment and principal redemption. Secondary market trading is suspended to freeze the holder registry.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to block trades in this specific debt instrument from November 24, 2025. The suspension facilitates orderly processing of the redemption and interest payment.
