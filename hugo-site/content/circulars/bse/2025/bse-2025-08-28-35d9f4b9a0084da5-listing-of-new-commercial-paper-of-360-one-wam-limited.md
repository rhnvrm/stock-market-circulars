## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 275 crores issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading in the BSE Debt segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 275 crores (5,500 papers of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,508 per paper (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Maturity period: 3 months (August 26, 2025 to November 25, 2025)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE466L14EU5
- Scrip Code: 730108
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
