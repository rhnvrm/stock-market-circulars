## Summary

ZERODHA Mutual Fund is launching a New Fund Offer (NFO) for ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from October 20, 2025 to November 03, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of November 06, 2025. The scheme is an equity index fund with ISIN INF0R8F01174.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: October 20, 2025 to November 03, 2025
- Scheme: ZERODHA BSE SENSEX INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH
- Scheme Category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01174
- Tentative Allotment Date: November 06, 2025
- Extended trading hours available until midnight on November 03, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 06, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt by allotment date
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Pending transactions will remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 06, 2025 for unit allotment
- For switch transactions, funds must be received before the NFO allotment date; funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: NFO opens
- **November 03, 2025**: NFO closes (extended until midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 06, 2025**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds required in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
- **T+1 day, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation to avoid transaction cancellation
- **T+5 or last payout day, 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction deadline

## Impact Assessment

This NFO launch has minimal market impact and is relevant primarily to:
- Mutual fund distributors and members trading on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Investors interested in passive index funds tracking BSE SENSEX
- ZERODHA Mutual Fund customers

The launch follows standard NFO procedures with clear operational guidelines for fund collection and allotment. No impact on equity markets or existing listed securities.
