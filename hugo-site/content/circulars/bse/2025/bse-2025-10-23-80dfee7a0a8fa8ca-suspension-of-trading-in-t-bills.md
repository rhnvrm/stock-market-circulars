## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251023-5 suspending trading in Treasury Bill scrip code 810573 (ISIN: IN2220150113, Particulars: 799MAHSDL25) effective October 24, 2025, as the instrument reaches its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Suspension applies to T-bill scrip code 810573 with ISIN IN2220150113
- T-bill particulars identified as 799MAHSDL25
- Suspension effective from October 24, 2025
- Reason for suspension: maturity on redemption date
- Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill from October 24, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in this instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: On or around October 24, 2025 (maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only one T-bill scrip nearing maturity

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine administrative action for debt market participants

**Investor Impact**: Expected and planned - T-bill holders will receive redemption proceeds at maturity as per instrument terms

**Segment Affected**: Debt segment only, specifically Treasury Bills market
