## Summary

OXYZO Financial Services Limited has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each on BSE Debt segment on December 18, 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 500 units, amounting to Rs. 25 crore. The commercial paper carries ICRA A1+ rating and will mature on June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Total Quantity: 500 units
- Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,78,977.50/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730808
- Scrip ID: OFSL171225
- ISIN: INE04VS14319
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE04VS14319
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Date of Allotment: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing of commercial paper provides short-term funding to OXYZO Financial Services Limited and offers investment opportunity to institutional investors in the debt market. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term instruments. This listing does not affect equity markets or have broader market implications.
