## Summary

BSE has listed 51,72,270 new equity shares of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 531109) issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters. These shares will be available for trading with effect from Thursday, November 27, 2025. The shares were allotted on September 20, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 63/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 53/-) and are subject to a lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 51,72,270 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 53/-
- Issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 20968148 to 26140417
- ISIN: INE561M01018
- Issue Price: Rs. 63/- per share
- Trading commences: November 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the listing of these new securities and ensure proper identification using the ISIN INE561M01018 for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a capital raise through preferential allotment, increasing the equity base of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited by approximately 51.72 lakh shares. The lock-in period until June 15, 2026, means these shares cannot be sold before that date, limiting immediate supply in the market. This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact, primarily affecting shareholders and potential investors of the company.
