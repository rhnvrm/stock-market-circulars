## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crore issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading in the BSE Debt segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 75 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730107, ISIN: INE755K14FN5
- Trading only in dematerialized form with tick size of 1 paise
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The short-term nature (91 days) provides liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
