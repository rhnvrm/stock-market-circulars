## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aarti Industries Ltd on private placement basis on its Debt segment effective November 14, 2025. The listing consists of 3,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 150 crore. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Total Issue Size: 3,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 150 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730560
- Scrip ID: AIL131125
- ISIN: INE769A14AA5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,062.05 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IndusInd Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE769A14AA5
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 14, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 14, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing of commercial paper on private placement basis is a standard corporate treasury operation for short-term funding. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. There is no impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Only debt market participants dealing with commercial paper will be affected, and this represents normal course business activity.
