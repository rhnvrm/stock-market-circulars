## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new commercial papers on BSE's Debt segment effective November 6, 2025. Two tranches totaling Rs. 225 crores (3000 + 1500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) have been issued on private placement basis with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings. The papers mature in February 2026 with Axis Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- First tranche: 3000 units (Rs. 150 crores) maturing on February 3, 2026
- Second tranche: 1500 units (Rs. 75 crores) maturing on February 6, 2026
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both tranches
- Issue prices: Rs. 4,91,966 (first tranche) and Rs. 4,91,705.50 (second tranche)
- Allotment date: November 4, 2025
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers (INE891K14PO5 and INE891K14OW1)
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 1)**: February 3, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Tranche 2)**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding for Axis Finance Limited and offers debt investment opportunities to institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
