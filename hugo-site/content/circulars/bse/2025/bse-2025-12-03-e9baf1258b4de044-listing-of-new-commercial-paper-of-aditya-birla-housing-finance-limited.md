## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis effective December 3, 2025. A total of 8000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (totaling Rs. 400 crores) have been admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are allotted on December 2, 2025 and will mature on March 3, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 8000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakhs face value each
- Scrip Code: 730687, Scrip ID: ABHFL21225
- ISIN: INE831R14FO7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,703.50 per unit
- Date of Allotment: December 2, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 3, 2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FO7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** December 3, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** December 2, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** March 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited for approximately 3 months. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The private placement nature means limited direct impact on retail investors. Trading will be restricted to institutional and qualified investors dealing in debt securities. The listing adds liquidity options for holders of these commercial papers in the secondary market.
