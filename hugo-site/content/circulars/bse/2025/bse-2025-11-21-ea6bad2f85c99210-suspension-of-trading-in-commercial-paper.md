## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 730597, ISIN: INE01C314DK8) issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The suspension is effective from November 24, 2025, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on November 25, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (BFSL-25-11-25-CP) to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730597
- ISIN: INE01C314DK8
- Redemption/Maturity Date: November 25, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: November 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE01C314DK8) from November 24, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this instrument from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 24, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational matter affecting a single commercial paper instrument approaching maturity.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date. The suspension prevents new trading activity one day before maturity, which is standard practice.

**Operational Impact**: Standard procedure requiring trading members to update their systems and ensure compliance with the suspension directive for the specified ISIN.
