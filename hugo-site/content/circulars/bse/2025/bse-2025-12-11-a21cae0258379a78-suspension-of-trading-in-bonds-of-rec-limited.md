## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of REC LIMITED (India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited) effective December 16, 2025. The suspension applies to the bond series REC-5.81%-31-12-25-PVT (ISIN: INE020B08DH2, Scrip Code: 960313) due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are strictly advised not to deal in these bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: REC-5.81%-31-12-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE020B08DH2
- Scrip Code: 960313
- Record date for redemption: December 16, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 16, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds
- Notice reference: DR-778/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension pursuant to corporate action procedures when an issuer fixes a record date for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing activities in the specified REC LIMITED bonds from December 16, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these bonds about the suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted or executed for the affected bond series
- Members must ensure compliance with the suspension directive to avoid regulatory penalties

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 31, 2025 (as per bond nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Bondholders will be unable to trade these securities from December 16, 2025 onwards. The suspension effectively locks investors into holding the bonds until redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders eligible as per the record date of December 16, 2025 will receive redemption proceeds. Those wishing to exit their positions must do so before the suspension date.

**Liquidity Impact**: Complete elimination of secondary market liquidity for this bond series from the suspension date until redemption is completed.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and communicate the suspension to affected clients promptly.
