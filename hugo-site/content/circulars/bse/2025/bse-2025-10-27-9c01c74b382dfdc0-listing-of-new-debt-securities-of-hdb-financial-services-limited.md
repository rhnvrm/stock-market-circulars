## Summary

HDB Financial Services Limited has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment effective October 27, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 25,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, representing a total issuance of Rs. 250 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 7.3268% per annum with annual interest payments and mature on October 4, 2030.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: HDB Financial Services Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977244
- **Scrip ID**: 733HDBFS30
- **ISIN**: INE756I07FK7
- **Quantity**: 25,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 7.3268% per annum (paid annually)
- **Maturity Date**: October 4, 2030
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/STABLE
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE756I07FK7
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Any clarifications required should be directed to the debt department at contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Annually
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 4, 2030

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt securities listing that provides additional investment options in the debt market. The AAA credit rating indicates high credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in private placement debt securities. No impact on equity markets or retail trading operations.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and qualified investors with access to AAA-rated debt securities from HDB Financial Services with a tenure of approximately 5 years and competitive interest rate of 7.3268% per annum.
