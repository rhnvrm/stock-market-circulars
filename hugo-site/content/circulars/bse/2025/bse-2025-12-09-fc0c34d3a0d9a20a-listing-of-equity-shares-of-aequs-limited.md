## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Aequs Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544634, trading symbol AEQUS, and ISIN number INE947N01017. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Aequs Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544634
- Trading Symbol: AEQUS
- ISIN: INE947N01017
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in a subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the exchange are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will provide investors with a new investment opportunity once trading commences. Trading members should update their systems with the scrip code and symbol information. The actual market impact will depend on the company's fundamentals, pricing, and market conditions at the time of listing. Details about the nature of listing (IPO, direct listing, etc.) and number of shares are not provided in this preliminary notice.
