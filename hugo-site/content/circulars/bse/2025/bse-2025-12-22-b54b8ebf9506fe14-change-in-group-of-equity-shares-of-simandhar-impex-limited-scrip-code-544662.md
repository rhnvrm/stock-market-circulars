## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Simandhar Impex Limited (Scrip Code: 544662) will be transferred to the 'B' group effective Tuesday, January 06, 2026. This change in classification will alter the trading framework and surveillance parameters applicable to the scrip.

## Key Points

- Company: Simandhar Impex Limited
- Scrip Code: 544662
- New Classification: 'B' Group
- Effective Date: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Notice Number: 20251222-14
- Notice Date: December 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip will be moved from its current group classification to 'B' group. Trading in B group securities typically involves different surveillance measures and trading parameters compared to other groups.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants trading in Simandhar Impex Limited equity shares must:
- Ensure compliance with B group trading rules and regulations
- Adjust trading strategies according to B group parameters
- Adhere to settlement and delivery requirements applicable to B group securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Effective Date**: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to B group may impact:
- Trading volumes and liquidity patterns for the scrip
- Surveillance measures and monitoring parameters
- Settlement cycles and delivery obligations
- Investor participation based on B group trading characteristics

Market participants holding positions in Simandhar Impex Limited should review their strategies in light of the new group classification.
