## Summary

Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited has listed new Commercial Papers on the BSE Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 5000 Commercial Papers issued with face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Scrip Code: 730657, Scrip ID: JBCP261125
- ISIN Number: INE824H14SX7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,689/-
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under ISIN Number INE824H14SX7

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Listing Date: November 26, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands debt market offerings and provides short-term investment options for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate low credit risk. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
