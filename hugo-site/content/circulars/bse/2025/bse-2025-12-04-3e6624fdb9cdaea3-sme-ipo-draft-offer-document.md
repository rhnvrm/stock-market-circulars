## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of VAHH Chemicals Limited filed on December 03, 2025, containing all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH Chemicals Limited on December 03, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 04, 2025 (by December 25, 2025)
- Comments to be sent via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 03, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by VAHH Chemicals Limited
- **December 04, 2025**: Notice published and public comment period begins
- **December 25, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a procedural notice for a single SME IPO. Impact limited to investors specifically interested in VAHH Chemicals Limited.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for interested parties - Provides opportunity for prospective investors to review the draft offer document and submit comments before the final offer document is issued.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard disclosure and comment solicitation process as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPOs.
