## Summary

BSE has issued a notice seeking clarification from InterGlobe Aviation Ltd (Scrip Code: 539448) regarding certain news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels. The exchange is verifying the accuracy of these news articles to inform members and investors. As of December 5, 2025, the company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE has sought clarification from InterGlobe Aviation Ltd (Scrip Code: 539448)
- News articles appeared in various media channels including Economic Times website
- Purpose is to verify accuracy and inform market participants
- Company's response is currently pending
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine surveillance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- InterGlobe Aviation Ltd is required to respond to BSE's clarification request
- Company must verify or clarify the accuracy of news articles in question
- Response should address concerns raised by the exchange

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 5, 2025
- **Reply Status**: Awaited

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This is a standard surveillance procedure where the exchange seeks clarification on news articles to ensure market transparency. Until the company responds, there may be uncertainty among investors regarding the news article content. The actual impact will depend on the nature of the news articles in question and the company's clarification. No trading restrictions or penalties are currently in place.
