## Summary

BSE announces that GROWW NIFTY NEXT 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 26, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities across all plan variants.

## Key Points

- GROWW NIFTY NEXT 50 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from August 26, 2025
- Includes subscription and redemption facilities
- SIP, STP & SWP facilities available
- Six scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Standard mutual fund transaction procedures apply.

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational notice affecting the availability of a specific index fund scheme on BSE's mutual fund platform. Benefits investors seeking exposure to NIFTY Next 50 index through systematic investment options.
