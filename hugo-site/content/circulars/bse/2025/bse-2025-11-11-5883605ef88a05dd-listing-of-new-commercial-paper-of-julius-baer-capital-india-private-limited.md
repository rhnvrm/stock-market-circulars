## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited on private placement basis, effective November 11, 2025. The issue comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, with a maturity period of 91 days. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating highest degree of safety for timely payment.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crores (4,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730530
- **Scrip ID**: JBCP111125
- **ISIN**: INE824H14ST5
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE824H14ST5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities are listed and admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The instrument is available only for institutional investors and will be traded in demat form with a minimum ticket size of Rs. 5 lakhs. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
