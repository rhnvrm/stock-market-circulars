## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Titanium Long-Short Fund Regular Plan Growth units under Tata Mutual Fund, effective December 17, 2025. The close-ended equity scheme will be listed in the 'B' Group and will trade in dematerialized form only. A total of 164,969,929 units have been allotted at Rs. 10.01 per unit with a face value of Rs. 10.00.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 544651
- **ISIN**: INF277K30013
- **Total Units Listed**: 164,969,929 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10.01 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: December 11, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Group**: B Group
- **Scheme Type**: Close-ended equity scheme investing in equity/equity-related instruments, debt and money market instruments, with limited short exposure through derivatives

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular follows SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to trade only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades are executed only in dematerialized form
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (one unit)
- All standard BSE trading procedures for mutual fund units apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Trading Commencement**: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard mutual fund listing that adds another investment option for investors seeking exposure to long-short equity strategies. The scheme offers hybrid exposure with both long equity positions and short positions through derivatives.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The fund will trade like other mutual fund units on the platform.

**Investor Access**: The listing provides secondary market liquidity for unitholders of this close-ended scheme through the BSE platform.
