## Summary

BSE has updated the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective December 16, 2025. Nine securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the ST-ASM 5/15/30 Days framework, while two securities are moving out. The ST-ASM framework is designed to monitor securities exhibiting abnormal price movements or excessive volatility over short periods.

## Key Points

- 9 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 2 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher stage ASM
- No securities moving to lower stage ASM
- Changes effective from December 16, 2025
- Includes both mainboard and SME scrips
- One security (BRIJLAX) moving to ESM Framework

## Securities Entering ST-ASM Framework

The following securities are being placed under ST-ASM Stage I:

1. Fermenta Biotech Ltd (506414)
2. Global Longlife Hospital and Research Ltd (543520) - SME
3. Onix Solar Energy Ltd (513119)
4. Prashant India Ltd (519014)
5. S & T Corporation Ltd (514197)
6. S&S Power Switchgear Ltd (517273)
7. SMC Global Securities Ltd (543263)
8. Taylormade Renewables Ltd (541228)
9. Uniroyal Marine Exports Ltd (526113)

## Securities Exiting ST-ASM Framework

1. **AK Capital Services Ltd** (530499) - Moving out of ST-ASM
2. **Brijlaxmi Leasing & Finance Ltd** (532113) - Moving to ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure) Framework

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework applies additional surveillance measures to securities showing unusual price movements. Securities under this framework may face:

- Additional reporting requirements
- Enhanced monitoring by exchange
- Possible trading restrictions based on stage
- Price bands or other risk mitigation measures

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the revised surveillance framework for affected securities
- **Investors**: Should be aware of enhanced surveillance status when trading these securities
- **Listed Companies**: Must ensure compliance with heightened disclosure requirements
- All stakeholders should review the consolidated list of 54+ securities currently under ST-ASM framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Circular Date**: December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The inclusion of 9 securities in ST-ASM indicates concerns about short-term price volatility or unusual trading patterns. This may result in reduced liquidity and increased trading costs for these securities.

**Trading Impact**: Securities under ST-ASM may experience:
- Reduced trading volumes due to additional surveillance
- Potential margin increases by brokers
- Enhanced scrutiny of large orders
- Possible impact on institutional participation

**Investor Impact**: Retail and institutional investors should exercise caution when trading these securities and be aware of the heightened surveillance measures in place.
