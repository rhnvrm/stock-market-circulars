## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 12 securities effective December 12, 2025. The revised price bands range from 2% to 10%, representing changes from their existing levels. The circular is issued by the Surveillance department and trading members can seek clarifications at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions effective from December 12, 2025
- 12 scrips affected with varying price band percentages
- Price bands set at 2%, 5%, and 10% levels
- Three scrips (Bluegod Entertainment, Ramgopal Polytex, Wardwizard Foods) have 2% price bands
- One scrip (Ecoboard Industries, Seshachal Technologies) have 10% price bands
- Seven scrips have 5% price bands
- Issued under surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

Price band modifications are surveillance measures typically implemented to:
- Control excessive volatility in specific securities
- Protect investor interests
- Ensure orderly trading
- Address unusual price movements or market conditions

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands
- All trades in the affected scrips must comply with the new price band limits from December 12, 2025
- Members requiring clarifications should contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The revised price bands will limit the daily price movement range for these securities. Tighter bands (2%) indicate stricter controls, while wider bands (10%) allow more price discovery. This may affect:
- Intraday trading strategies
- Order execution for large trades
- Market liquidity in affected scrips

**Market Participants Affected**: Active traders, market makers, and investors in the 12 listed securities need to adjust their trading strategies according to the new price band limits.
