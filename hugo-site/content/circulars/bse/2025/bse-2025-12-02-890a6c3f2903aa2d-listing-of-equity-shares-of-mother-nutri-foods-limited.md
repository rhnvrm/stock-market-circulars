## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Mother Nutri Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544623, symbol MNFL, and ISIN number INE0UH001016. The specific listing date and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Mother Nutri Foods Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Trading Symbol: MNFL
- ISIN: INE0UH001016
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details for future trading activities
- Members should await separate notice for actual listing date and commencement of trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has minimal immediate market impact as it is a pre-listing notification. The actual listing date has not been specified. Trading members and investors interested in the SME segment should monitor for the follow-up notice containing the listing date and detailed securities information. The listing will add one more security to the BSE SME platform, providing additional investment opportunities in the food sector.
