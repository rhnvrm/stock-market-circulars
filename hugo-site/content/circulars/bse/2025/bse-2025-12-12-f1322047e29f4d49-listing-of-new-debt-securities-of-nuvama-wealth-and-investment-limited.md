## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis, effective December 12, 2025. The issue comprises 20,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 200 crore. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.95% per annum with annual interest payments and mature on September 10, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited
- **Quantity**: 20,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 977362
- **Scrip ID**: 895NWIL27
- **ISIN**: INE523L07AS9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AA
- **Interest Rate**: 8.95% per annum
- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: September 10, 2027
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities issued on private placement basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AS9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- Standard debt segment trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 10, 2026 to September 10, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: September 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt listing that expands the BSE debt segment inventory. The securities are rated CARE AA, indicating high credit quality.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and qualified investors with an additional fixed-income investment option offering 8.95% annual returns with approximately 2.75-year tenure.

**Company Impact**: Strengthens Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited's capital structure through debt financing of Rs. 200 crore, supporting business operations and growth initiatives.

**Operational Notes**: Trading members requiring clarifications can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).
