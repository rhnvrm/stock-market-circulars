## Summary

Admach Systems Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as on December 30, 2025 (post-issue of IPO) under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The company has 958 total shareholders with promoter and promoter group holding 3,586,366 equity shares and public shareholders holding 3,186,434 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shareholders: 958 (6 promoters & promoter group, 952 public shareholders)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 3,586,366 equity shares
- Public shareholding: 3,186,434 equity shares
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs issued
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Promoter shares are locked-in as per IPO requirements
- No pledged shares or non-disposal undertakings
- No differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard compliance filing post-IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing required under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure mandatory for newly listed entities
- Promoter shares subject to lock-in requirements as per IPO regulations

## Important Dates

- Filing date: December 30, 2025 (post-issue of IPO)
- Shareholding pattern as on: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory filing with minimal market impact. The disclosure provides transparency on post-IPO shareholding structure with standard lock-in provisions for promoter shares. No immediate trading or operational implications for market participants.
