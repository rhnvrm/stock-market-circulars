## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on private placement basis by six companies on the debt market segment effective December 30, 2025. The securities include commercial papers from Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (400 units), Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited (22,000 units), Time Technoplast Limited (200 units), J.K. Cement Ltd (1,500 units), Aarti Industries Ltd (3,000 units), and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial papers totaling 27,100+ units on BSE debt segment
- All securities trade only in dematerialized form under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 6.08% p.a. (J.K. Cement) to 9.25% p.a. (Muthoottu Mini Financiers)
- All listed commercial papers have CARE A1+ and/or CRISIL A1+ credit ratings
- Maturity periods range from 49 days to 182 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 29-30, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - J.K. Cement: February 17, 2026
  - Reliance Jio Infocomm: March 13, 2026
  - Aarti Industries: March 30, 2026
  - Muthoottu Mini Financiers: June 29, 2026
  - Time Technoplast: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt listing notification that does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt market participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these commercial papers on the BSE debt platform.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and qualified investors additional short-term debt investment options with high credit ratings (A1+) and varying maturity profiles from 49 to 182 days.
