## Summary

KSH International Limited (formerly KSH International Private Limited) has finalized the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors as part of its initial public offering. The IPO Committee, in consultation with book running lead managers Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and ICICI Securities Limited, allocated 55,46,874 equity shares of face value ₹5 each at an anchor investor allocation price of ₹384 per share (including share premium of ₹379), raising a total of ₹212,99,99,616.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 55,46,874 equity shares at ₹384 per share
- Face value: ₹5 per equity share
- Share premium: ₹379 per equity share
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: ₹212.99 crores
- Domestic mutual funds received 74.65% (41,40,591 shares) of anchor allocation through 8 schemes
- 13 anchor investors participated in the allocation
- Book running lead managers: Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and ICICI Securities Limited
- Allocation finalized on December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation demonstrates strong institutional interest in KSH International Limited's IPO, with significant participation from major domestic mutual funds (74.65% of allocation). The presence of marquee investors like HDFC Mutual Fund, Kotak Mahindra group entities, LIC Mutual Fund, and international investors like HSBC and Malabar India Fund indicates confidence in the company's prospects. The allocation provides price discovery and stability for the upcoming public offering, with the anchor allocation price of ₹384 per share serving as a reference point for retail and institutional investors in the main IPO.
