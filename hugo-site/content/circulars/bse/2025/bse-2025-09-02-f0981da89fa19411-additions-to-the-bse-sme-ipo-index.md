## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. GLOBTIER INFOTECH LIMITED (544494) and NIS MANAGEMENT LIMITED (544495), which were listed on the SME platform on September 2, 2025, will be added to the index effective at market open on September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- GLOBTIER INFOTECH LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544494) listed on BSE SME platform on September 2, 2025
- NIS MANAGEMENT LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544495) listed on BSE SME platform on September 2, 2025
- Both stocks will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on September 3, 2025
- Index changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index addition following the listing of new companies on the SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust holdings accordingly.

## Important Dates

- September 2, 2025: Both companies listed on BSE SME platform
- September 3, 2025: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine addition to the SME IPO Index following new listings. The addition will affect index-tracking funds and passive investment strategies benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index. The changes have been communicated through the standard corporate action file for automated processing by market participants.
