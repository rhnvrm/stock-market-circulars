## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE219O07362, Scrip Code: 974946) of GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to GIPL-ZC-30-4-26-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE219O07362)
- Scrip Code: 974946
- Suspension effective from December 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-770/2025-2026
- Trading members must not deal in these securities from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture suspension due to record date fixation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified debentures from December 4, 2025
- No dealings permitted in GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE219O07362) from the specified date
- Trading members are required to note and implement this suspension immediately

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 2, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 4, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited market impact as this affects debt securities of a private limited company with likely restricted trading volumes. The suspension is temporary and related to standard corporate action procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders cannot trade these securities from December 4, 2025. Holders as of the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. Investors planning to trade these debentures must complete transactions before the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these securities and inform clients holding or interested in these debentures about the suspension.
