## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Luxury Time Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544635, symbol LUXURY, and ISIN number INE1CJB01013. This listing will occur on the SME segment of BSE. The specific date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Luxury Time Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544635
- Trading Symbol: LUXURY
- ISIN: INE1CJB01013
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to prepare for the listing and trading of Luxury Time Limited equity shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 10, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company on BSE. The impact is limited to investors and traders interested in Luxury Time Limited specifically. The SME segment listing indicates this is a smaller company, and market-wide impact will be minimal. Traders should await the separate notice with the exact listing date and security details before trading.
