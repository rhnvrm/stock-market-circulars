## Summary

BSE has published Settlement No. 689 dated August 7, 2025, listing securities to be auctioned in the demat auction. The list contains over 75 securities with their respective scrip codes, names, and quantities to be auctioned.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 689
- Date: August 7, 2025
- Contains diverse range of securities across sectors
- Quantities vary significantly from 1 share to over 19,000 shares
- Includes major companies like Adani Ports (140 shares), Bharat Heavy Electricals (700 shares), and Grasim Industries (3 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the securities and quantities listed for auction
- Relevant parties must comply with demat auction settlement procedures
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE regulations

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: August 7, 2025
- Settlement Number: 689

## Impact Assessment

The auction affects shareholders and brokers holding positions in the listed securities. Impact varies by security based on quantity being auctioned - ranging from minimal impact for single-share auctions to potentially significant impact for larger quantities like Monotype India (19,526 shares) and Inventure Growth (6,170 shares). Regular demat auctions are part of standard market operations to resolve settlement obligations.
