## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Efficient Fire and Security Solutions Limited, filed on December 30, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available on the SME website for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Efficient Fire and Security Solutions Limited on December 30, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public review and comments for at least 21 days
- Hosted on BSE SME website at bsesme.com
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from December 31, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document must remain publicly available for minimum 21 days as per ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 20, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects potential investors, merchant bankers, and market participants interested in the SME segment. The 21-day public comment period allows stakeholders to review the offer document and provide feedback before the final prospectus is filed. Standard procedural impact for SME IPO process with no immediate market-wide implications.
