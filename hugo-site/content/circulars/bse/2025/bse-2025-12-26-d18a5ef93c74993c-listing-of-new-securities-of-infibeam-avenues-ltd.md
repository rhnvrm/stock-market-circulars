## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 68,06,08,792 new equity shares of Infibeam Avenues Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) effective from Monday, December 29, 2025. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 1/- each that were issued on a rights basis. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new securities being listed: 68,06,08,792 equity shares
- Securities arise from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares
- Face value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Original issuance: Rights basis
- Scrip Code: 539807
- ISIN: INE483S01020
- Status: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Two tranches with distinctive numbers: 21,16,06,572 shares (DN: 2789495347 to 3001101918) and 46,90,02,220 shares (DN: 3001114078 to 3470116297)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing circular for new securities arising from corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities
- No additional compliance requirements specified for members
- Standard trading rules and regulations applicable

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement Date: Monday, December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The listing of over 68 crore equity shares represents a significant addition to the free float of Infibeam Avenues Ltd. This substantial increase in share count may impact:

- Liquidity: Increased number of shares available for trading
- Market Capitalization: Proportional increase based on current market price
- Shareholding Pattern: Dilution for existing shareholders who did not participate in the rights issue
- Price Discovery: Potential short-term volatility as market absorbs the increased supply

**Operational Impact:** Minimal operational impact on trading members as this is a routine listing of converted shares. The shares being pari-passu means they carry identical rights and privileges as existing shares.
