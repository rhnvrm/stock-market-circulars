## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited (MFL) on its Debt segment effective December 15, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities
- Scrip Code: 977368
- Scrip ID: 880MFL2028
- ISIN: INE549K07HD3
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA- / Positive
- Interest Rate: 8.80% per annum
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K07HD3
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: December 15, 2025
- Date of Redemption: December 12, 2028
- Interest Payment Frequency: Monthly

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. It provides debt market participants with a new investment option in Muthoot Fincorp Limited's securities rated CRISIL AA-/Positive. The securities offer an 8.80% annual return with monthly interest payments over a 3-year tenure. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in AA-rated corporate debt instruments.
