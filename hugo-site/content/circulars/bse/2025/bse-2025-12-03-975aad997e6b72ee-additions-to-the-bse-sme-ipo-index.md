## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 4, 2025. MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544623) and K K SILK MILLS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544624) were listed on BSE on December 3, 2025 and will be included in the index at the open of trading on December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- MOTHER NUTRI FOODS LIMITED (ticker: 544623) - listed December 3, 2025
- K K SILK MILLS LIMITED (ticker: 544624) - listed December 3, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 4, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to prior notices: 20251202-47 and 20251202-48

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking products and investors following the BSE SME IPO Index should note the constituent changes for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **December 4, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with limited market impact. The addition of these two SME stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index primarily affects:

- Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index
- Passive investment strategies following this index
- Index calculation and dissemination

The impact is confined to the SME segment and does not affect broader market operations or trading rules.
