## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company originally known as Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Ratnakar Securities Limited with effect from October 24, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed by Sunidhi Matroja.

## Key Points

- Original company name: Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited
- New company name: Ratnakar Securities Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L66120GJ1992PLC017564
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Effective date of name change: October 24, 2025
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved and digitally signed by: Sunidhi Matroja, Assistant Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action reflecting the company's name change as per the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited) and new name (Ratnakar Securities Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited to Ratnakar Securities Limited
- **Two-year period from October 24, 2025**: Company must display both old and new names during this period

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action involving a name change. Trading operations and business activities continue without interruption.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update its branding, documentation, and signage to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years. The CIN remains unchanged (L66120GJ1992PLC017564).

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. The name change is purely administrative and does not alter the legal status or obligations of the company.
