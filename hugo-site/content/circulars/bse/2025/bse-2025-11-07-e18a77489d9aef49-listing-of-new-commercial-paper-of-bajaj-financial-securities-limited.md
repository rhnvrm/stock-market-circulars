## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective November 7, 2025. The issuance consists of 6000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment under scrip code 730517 with ISIN INE01C314DE1.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 6000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,757.50
- Scrip Code: 730517
- Scrip ID: BFSL061125
- ISIN: INE01C314DE1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 6, 2026
- **Maturity Period**: 92 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides short-term funding instrument for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The high credit rating (CRISIL A1+, IND A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing with conventional trading parameters. Trading members can access these debt securities through BSE Debt segment with standard tick size and lot requirements.

**Investor Impact**: The commercial paper offers a short-term investment option (3-month maturity) with discount pricing (issue price Rs. 4,91,757.50 against face value Rs. 5,00,000), providing implicit yield for investors seeking highly-rated short-term debt instruments.
