## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 16, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a quantity of 13,525 units, face value of Rs. 100,000 each, and carry an interest rate of 11.4% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Keertana Finserv Limited
- **Security Type**: Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (further listings under same ISIN)
- **Quantity**: 13,525 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 99,906.30 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 976915
- **Scrip ID**: 114KF27
- **ISIN**: INE0NES07238
- **Credit Rating**: IND BBB+/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 11.4% per annum (paid monthly)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07238
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Monthly from January 15, 2026 to January 15, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: January 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing with no put/call options. The securities are privately placed and will trade on the BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional and HNI investors participating in debt markets. The securities carry a BBB+ rating indicating moderate credit quality with stable outlook.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for debt securities with established trading infrastructure.
