## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20251201-56 suspending trading in commercial paper of 17 companies due to upcoming redemptions. The suspensions are effective from the respective record dates, which range from December 3 to December 9, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified commercial paper instruments during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- 17 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension periods: December 3, 2025 to December 9, 2025 (various dates)
- Notice applies to debt segment corporate actions
- Trading members instructed to cease dealing in affected commercial paper
- Each company has specific ISIN codes and scrip codes listed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for scheduled commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from the respective record dates
- Members must ensure proper settlement of existing positions before suspension dates
- Adherence to the no-dealings directive is mandatory for all affected securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 1, 2025
- **First Suspension Date:** December 3, 2025 (Muthoot Housing Finance)
- **Second Suspension Date:** December 8, 2025 (Titan Company)
- **Third Suspension Date:** December 9, 2025 (15 companies)
- **Record Dates:** Align with respective suspension dates for redemption purposes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited and localized to debt market participants holding these specific commercial paper instruments. The staggered redemption dates help minimize concentrated market impact.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in 17 commercial paper securities across three different dates. Firms holding these instruments need to prepare for redemption settlements.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary reduction in tradable commercial paper universe, though this is expected and planned by market participants given the scheduled nature of redemptions.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds on the respective record dates. No action required from investors as redemptions are automatic.
