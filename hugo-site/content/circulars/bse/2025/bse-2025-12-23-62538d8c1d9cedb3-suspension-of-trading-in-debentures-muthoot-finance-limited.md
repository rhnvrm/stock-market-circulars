## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Muthoot Finance Limited (scrip code: 937355, ISIN: INE414G07FM3) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is necessitated by the company's record date of December 27, 2025, fixed for the purpose of redemption and interest payment on these debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures of Muthoot Finance Limited
- The debenture series affected is MFL-IV-11-1-26-NCD with ISIN INE414G07FM3 (Code: 937355)
- Suspension applies to the Debt segment of BSE
- Corporate action involves both redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 26, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No dealings permitted in scrip code 937355 (INE414G07FM3) starting from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Notice date - circular issued
- **December 26, 2025**: No dealings from this date (trading suspension begins)
- **December 27, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific Muthoot Finance debenture series. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature, related to the corporate action of redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear on the record date (December 27, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. Trading suspension prevents last-minute transactions that could affect entitlement determination.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to block trades in this security and inform clients holding or interested in this debenture about the suspension and upcoming redemption event.
