## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Neutrino Trading Private Limited as an Algo Provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to exchange trading members. The empanelment is effective immediately, and the updated list of Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Neutrino Trading Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an Algo Provider
- Vendor is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Contact: +91 9766071356
- Address: 601, Dhantak Plaza, Makwana Road, Andheri East, Mumbai – 400059
- Complete list of empanelled Algo Providers available at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update expanding the list of approved algorithmic trading solution providers available to trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members seeking to use algorithmic trading solutions may engage with the newly empanelled provider as per existing BSE guidelines.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 5, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular provides trading members with an additional vendor option for implementing algorithmic trading strategies. The provisional empanelment expands member choice but does not alter trading rules, risk frameworks, or operational requirements.
