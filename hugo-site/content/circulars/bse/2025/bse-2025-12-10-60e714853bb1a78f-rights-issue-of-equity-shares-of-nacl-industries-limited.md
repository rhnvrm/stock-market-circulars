## Summary

NACL Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 524709) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of December 12, 2025. The company will issue 5 equity shares of Re 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share (total issue price Rs. 76.70) for every 31 equity shares held by eligible shareholders. Trading in the equity shares will be on ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 5 equity shares for every 31 equity shares held
- Face value: Re 1/- per equity share
- Premium: Rs. 75.70 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 76.70 per share (payable on application)
- Record date: December 12, 2025
- Ex-rights date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Scrip code: 524709

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action under existing SEBI guidelines for rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in NACL Industries Limited shares on ex-rights basis from December 12, 2025
- Shareholders on record as of December 12, 2025 will be eligible to participate in the rights issue
- Issue price is payable at the time of application
- Trading must comply with dematerialized securities rolling settlement segment requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **December 12, 2025**: Record date for rights issue eligibility
- **December 12, 2025**: Ex-rights trading begins (Settlement No. DR-776/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to additional shares at Rs. 76.70 per share, maintaining their proportional ownership. The rights ratio of 5:31 represents approximately 16.13% dilution if fully subscribed.

**Market Impact**: Shares will trade ex-rights from December 12, 2025, which typically results in a theoretical price adjustment to reflect the value of the rights separated from the stock. Trading members need to ensure proper settlement procedures.

**Company Impact**: The rights issue will enable NACL Industries to raise capital from existing shareholders for business expansion, debt reduction, or other corporate purposes as disclosed in the rights issue prospectus.
