## Summary

Robert Bosch GmbH (Acquirer) alongwith Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (PAC) have launched an open offer to acquire up to 70,00,355 fully paid-up equity shares representing 25.75% of the share capital of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited at ₹1,762.54 per equity share. This open offer is made in compliance with SEBI (SAST) Regulations pursuant to Regulation 3(1), Regulation 4, and Regulation 5(1).

## Key Points

- **Acquirer**: Robert Bosch GmbH, a German limited liability company (Registration: HRB 14000)
- **PAC**: Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (CIN: U72400KA1997PTC023164)
- **Target Company**: Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited (CIN: L29300GJ1984PLC007470)
- **Offer Shares**: 70,00,355 equity shares (25.75% of share capital)
- **Face Value**: ₹10 per equity share
- **Offer Price**: ₹1,762.54 per equity share (Indian Rupees One Thousand Seven Hundred Sixty-Two and Fifty-Four Paise)
- **Offer Type**: Cash offer to public shareholders
- **Non-conditional**: Not subject to minimum level of acceptance
- **Not a competing offer** as per Regulation 20 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

This open offer is made in accordance with:
- Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011
- Regulation 3(1), Regulation 4, and Regulation 5(1) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations

Note: While SEBI (SAST) Regulations typically require open offers to be for at least 26% of total share capital, the public shareholding is only 25.75%, hence the offer is for all available public shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Public Shareholders**: Must review the Letter of Offer and accompanying Form of Acceptance-cum-Acknowledgement
- **NRI and OCB holders**: Must obtain all requisite approvals (including RBI approval) to tender their equity shares in this offer
- **Recent sellers**: If equity shares were recently sold, the Letter of Offer should be handed over to the stock exchange member through whom the sale was effected
- **Shareholders requiring clarification**: May consult stockbrokers, investment consultants, the Manager to the Offer, or Registrar to the Offer

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date**: November 11, 2025
- **10th Working Day Calculation**: Offer size calculated as of 10th working day from closure of tendering period (as per SEBI SAST Regulations)

## Impact Assessment

**Corporate Impact**: This represents a significant takeover transaction where Robert Bosch GmbH (a major German multinational) is acquiring control of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. The acquisition of 25.75% stake at a substantial premium suggests strategic consolidation in the air conditioning manufacturing sector.

**Shareholder Impact**: Public shareholders holding 70,00,355 equity shares have an exit opportunity at ₹1,762.54 per share. The offer price represents the valuation determined as per SEBI (SAST) Regulations and provides liquidity to minority shareholders.

**Market Impact**: High significance due to the involvement of Robert Bosch GmbH, a Fortune Global 500 company, entering the Indian air conditioning market through this acquisition. The total offer size amounts to approximately ₹123.37 crores (70,00,355 shares × ₹1,762.54).

**Strategic Implications**: The transaction indicates consolidation in the HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) sector in India, with Bosch acquiring significant stake in a joint venture company that was previously associated with Johnson Controls and Hitachi.
