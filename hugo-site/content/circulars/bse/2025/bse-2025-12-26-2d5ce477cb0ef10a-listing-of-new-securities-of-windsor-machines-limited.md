## Summary

BSE has listed 26,06,203 new equity shares of Windsor Machines Limited (Scrip Code: 522029) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from December 29, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions until June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 26,06,203 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 189.85/-
- Issue price: Rs. 191.85 per share
- Allotment date: October 7, 2025
- New shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE052A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 84478319 to 87084521

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new securities in their systems
- All 26,06,203 shares are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2027
- Shares issued on preferential basis to promoters only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as the preferential allotment is to promoters and all shares are locked-in for approximately 18 months. The shares rank equally with existing equity, maintaining standard shareholder rights. No trading activity expected until lock-in expires in June 2027.
