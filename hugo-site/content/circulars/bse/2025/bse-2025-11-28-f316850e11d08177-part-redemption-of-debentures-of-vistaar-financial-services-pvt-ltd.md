## Summary

VISTAAR FINANCIAL SERVICES PVT LTD has announced part redemption of its debentures with ISIN INE016P07195 (Scrip Code: 975474). The debentures with coupon rate of 9.75% maturing on May 3, 2027, will have a reduced face value of Rs. 41,666.67 per debenture following the part redemption and interest payment. Trading in these debentures will commence with the reduced face value from December 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: VISTAAR FINANCIAL SERVICES PVT LTD
- ISIN: INE016P07195
- Scrip Code: 975474
- Debenture: VFSPL-9.75%-5-3-27-PVT
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 41,666.67 per Debenture
- Trading with reduced face value begins: December 1, 2025
- Reference Number: DR-767/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251128-2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action relating to debt securities part redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the reduced face value of Rs. 41,666.67 per debenture
- Trading Members must ensure trading reflects the reduced face value from December 1, 2025
- All market participants should update their records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Notice date and announcement
- **December 1, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Partial capital return through redemption reduces outstanding principal amount. Face value reduction from original amount to Rs. 41,666.67 indicates substantial partial redemption has occurred.

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific privately placed debenture series. No broader market implications. Trading liquidity may be affected due to reduced outstanding amount.

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems and records must be updated to reflect new face value. Valuation and reporting systems need adjustment for the reduced principal amount.
