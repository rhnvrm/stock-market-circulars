## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Physicswallah Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544609, symbol PWL, and ISIN number INE0LP301011. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Physicswallah Limited
- Scrip Code: 544609
- Symbol: PWL
- ISIN Number: INE0LP301011
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new listing and prepare for trading in Physicswallah Limited equity shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced in separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity instrument to BSE's trading platform. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details. Investors will have the opportunity to trade Physicswallah Limited shares once listing is completed. The actual market impact will depend on the listing date and opening price details to be announced.
