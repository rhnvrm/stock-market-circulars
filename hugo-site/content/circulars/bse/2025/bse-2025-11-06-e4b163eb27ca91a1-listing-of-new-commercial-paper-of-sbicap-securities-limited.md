## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 2,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 125 crore) with a tenure of 94 days. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: SBICAP Securities Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Quantity**: 2,500 units (Rs. 125 crore aggregate)
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,519 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730500
- **Scrip ID**: SSL041125
- **ISIN**: INE212K14CP9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: State Bank of India Financial Institution Branch

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE212K14CP9
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: November 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument is designed for institutional investors and wholesale debt market participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the trading parameters. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
