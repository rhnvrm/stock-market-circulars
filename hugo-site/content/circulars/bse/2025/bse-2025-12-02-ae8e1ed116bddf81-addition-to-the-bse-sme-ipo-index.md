## Summary

BSE announced the addition of SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544621) to the BSE SME IPO INDEX effective at the open of December 3, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE on December 2, 2025.

## Key Points

- SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED (ticker: 544621) to be added to BSE SME IPO INDEX
- Company listing date: December 2, 2025
- Index addition effective date: December 3, 2025 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking the BSE SME IPO INDEX should note the composition change for portfolio management and rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **December 2, 2025**: SSMD AGROTECH INDIA LIMITED lists on BSE
- **December 3, 2025**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO INDEX at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition following the listing of an SME company. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO INDEX will need to include this stock in their portfolios. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
