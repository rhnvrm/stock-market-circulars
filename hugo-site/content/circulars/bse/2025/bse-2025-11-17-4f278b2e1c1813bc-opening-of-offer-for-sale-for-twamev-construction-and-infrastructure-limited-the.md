## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited. The OFS provides an opportunity for institutional investors, non-institutional investors, and retail investors to participate in the bidding process for equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Company: Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited
- Type: Offer for Sale (OFS)
- Exchange: BSE
- Investor categories: Institutional, Non-Institutional, and Retail investors
- Circular issued on November 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible investors must follow the prescribed bidding process
- Participants must adhere to the settlement schedule as specified by BSE
- All bids must comply with BSE's OFS framework and guidelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 17, 2025
- OFS Opening Date: To be confirmed (refer to settlement calendar circular)
- Settlement dates and timelines as per BSE settlement schedule

## Impact Assessment

The OFS provides liquidity opportunity for existing shareholders of Twamev Construction and Infrastructure Limited to sell their holdings, while allowing new investors to acquire shares in the company. The impact is medium as it affects specific investors interested in this company and creates short-term trading activity. Institutional and retail investors should review the offer documents and participate based on their investment criteria.
