## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited. This is a corporate disclosure regarding a company name change.

## Key Points

- Company name change notification for Johnson Controls-Hitachi Air Conditioning India Limited
- Administrative corporate action with no material business impact
- Information disseminated to market participants and investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a disclosure of a corporate administrative action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and investors should note the new company name for future reference
- Trading symbol and securities identification may be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Company name changes are administrative in nature and do not affect the underlying business operations, financial position, or shareholder value. Investors should update their records with the new company name for identification purposes.
