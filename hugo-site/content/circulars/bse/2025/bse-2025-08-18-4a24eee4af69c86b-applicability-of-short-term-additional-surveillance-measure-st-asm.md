## Summary

BSE has updated the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 19, 2025. The circular provides three annexures detailing securities being added to, removed from, and continuing in the ST-ASM framework with stage changes.

## Key Points

- 7 new securities added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 5 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving between ASM stages
- Framework includes regular securities, SME scrips, and T+0 scrips
- Consolidated list shows current ASM Stage I securities

## Regulatory Changes

- Short Term ASM framework updated with new security inclusions and exclusions
- Securities marked with specific symbols indicating NSE coordination, SME status, or T+0 classification
- Framework continues to operate on 5/15/30 day cycles

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to additional surveillance measures for listed securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for securities under ST-ASM
- Compliance with stage-specific trading restrictions and procedures

## Important Dates

- **August 19, 2025**: Effective date for all ST-ASM framework changes
- All new inclusions and exclusions become operational from this date

## Impact Assessment

- Direct impact on trading activities for 12 securities (7 new inclusions, 5 exclusions)
- Enhanced surveillance may affect liquidity and trading volumes for included securities
- Market participants need to adjust trading strategies for securities under surveillance measures
- Regular framework updates ensure market integrity and investor protection
