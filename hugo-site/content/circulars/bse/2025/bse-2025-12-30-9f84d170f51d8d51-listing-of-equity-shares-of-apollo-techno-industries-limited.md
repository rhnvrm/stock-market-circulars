## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Apollo Techno Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544671, symbol ATIL, and ISIN INE0X4A01013. The exact date of listing and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Apollo Techno Industries Limited
- Scrip Code: 544671
- Trading Symbol: ATIL
- ISIN: INE0X4A01013
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Separate notice will provide listing date and security details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Apollo Techno Industries Limited ahead of its listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. The impact is limited to trading members who need to prepare their systems for the new scrip. Market participants interested in SME segment opportunities should watch for the follow-up notice with the actual listing date and security details.
