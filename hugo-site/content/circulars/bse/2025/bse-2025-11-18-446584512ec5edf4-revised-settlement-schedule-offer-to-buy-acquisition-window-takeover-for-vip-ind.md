## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for V.I.P. Industries Limited. The bidding period ran from November 3-17, 2025, with the obligation download date revised to November 19, 2025 and settlement date to November 20, 2025. Settlement number is 2025115.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025115
- Bid Period: November 3, 2025 to November 17, 2025
- Revised Obligation Download Date: November 19, 2025
- Revised Settlement Date: November 20, 2025
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251030-10
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Available through both depositories

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular updates the settlement timeline for an ongoing takeover offer under existing acquisition guidelines referenced in Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must use Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2025115 in the depository system
- **Share Delivery**: Demat shares must be delivered exclusively through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- **Stamp Duty**: Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- **Guidelines Reference**: Members/Custodians must refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Bid End Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Revised Obligation Download**: November 19, 2025
- **Revised Settlement Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Members and custodians involved in the VIP Industries takeover offer need to adjust their settlement operations according to the revised timeline. The one-day delay in settlement may require coordination with clients and back-office systems.

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - Limited to participants in the specific takeover offer for VIP Industries. Does not affect regular market operations or broader trading activities.

**Compliance Impact**: Medium - Participants must ensure proper use of settlement number and market type in depository systems, and verify stamp duty calculations from the designated file.
