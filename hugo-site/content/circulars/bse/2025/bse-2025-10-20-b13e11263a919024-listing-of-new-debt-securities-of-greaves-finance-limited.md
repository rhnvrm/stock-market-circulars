## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Greaves Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, carrying an interest rate of 10.50% per annum with monthly interest payments. The securities have been assigned a credit rating of IND A-/Stable and will mature on October 20, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Greaves Finance Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977225
- **Scrip ID**: 1050GFL027
- **ISIN**: INE1QWF07030
- **Issue Size**: 2,500 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 (at par)
- **Interest Rate**: 10.50% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from November 20, 2025 to October 20, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: IND A-/Stable
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE1QWF07030
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 20, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 20, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (20th of each month)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing in the debt segment affecting only participants in the debt market.

**Investor Impact**: Provides debt market participants with an additional fixed-income investment option rated IND A-/Stable with a 10.50% annual return and monthly interest payments.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems to accommodate the new scrip code and ISIN for trading purposes.
