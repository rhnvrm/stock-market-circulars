## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Mangal Credit and Fincorp Limited on a private placement basis with effect from November 27, 2025. The securities will be traded on the BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form only. The non-convertible debentures carry a 12.90% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on October 3, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976597
- Scrip ID: 1290MCFL27
- Detail Name: MCFL-12.90%-3-10-27-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE545L07036 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 1,000 debentures
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Paid-up Value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,752.60
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Interest Rate: 12.90% per annum (monthly payment)
- Date of Allotment: November 26, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call option available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE545L07036
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Monthly from January 3, 2026 to October 3, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing adds liquidity to Mangal Credit and Fincorp Limited's debt instruments for investors holding these securities. The CRISIL BBB/Stable rating indicates adequate credit quality with moderate credit risk. The relatively high coupon rate of 12.90% reflects the credit profile of the issuer. Impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and existing investors in the company's debt securities.
