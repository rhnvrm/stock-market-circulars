## Summary

BSE has listed 13,39,000 new equity shares of Ganesha Ecosphere Limited (Scrip Code: 514167) issued on preferential basis to promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, October 28, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions until April 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 13,39,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issued at premium of Rs. 1,025/- per share (total issue price: Rs. 1,035/-)
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: July 17, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 25456985 to 26795984
- ISIN: INE845D01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- All shares under complete lock-in until April 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until April 30, 2027
- Standard preferential allotment compliance already completed by the company

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 17, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all shares are locked-in until April 2027. The preferential allotment to promoters indicates promoter commitment and capital infusion into the company.
