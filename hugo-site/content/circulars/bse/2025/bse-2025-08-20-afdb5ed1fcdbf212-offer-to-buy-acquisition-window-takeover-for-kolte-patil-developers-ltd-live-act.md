## Summary

BSE announces the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of KOLTE PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD. The offer is scheduled from Thursday, August 21, 2025 to Monday, September 8, 2025, targeting up to 2,30,56,825 equity shares at Rs. 329.00 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: KOLTE PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD (Symbol: KOLTEPATIL, ISIN: INE094I01018)
- Offer size: Up to 2,30,56,825 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 329.00 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10.00
- Market lot: 1
- Opening date: August 21, 2025
- Closing date: September 8, 2025

## Session Timings

- **Bid Offering Period**: August 21 - September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: August 21 - September 5, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (last day)**: September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (last day)**: September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)
