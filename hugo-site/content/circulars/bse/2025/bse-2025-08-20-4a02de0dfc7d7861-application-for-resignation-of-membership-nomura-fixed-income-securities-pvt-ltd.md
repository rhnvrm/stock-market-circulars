## Summary

BSE has received a resignation application from trading member Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064). Other trading members have until September 3, 2025 to raise objections to this membership resignation.

## Key Points

- Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064) has applied for BSE membership resignation
- Application filed under BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- 15-day objection period for other trading members
- Any clarifications can be sought via membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - standard membership resignation procedure being followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit any objections in writing to BSE Membership department
- Objections must be received within 15 days of notice date
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 20, 2025
- Objection Deadline: September 3, 2025 (15 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative membership change. May reduce number of active trading members but does not affect trading operations or market structure significantly.
