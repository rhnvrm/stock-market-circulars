## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective November 7, 2025, in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category. The securities include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with specific maturity dates in 2026. Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each maturity date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed on BSE from November 7, 2025
- All securities classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size of 1 for all three T-Bills
- Trading suspension applies two working days before maturity date
- Securities identified by unique scrip codes and ISIN numbers

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing details of the three T-Bills
- Members must ensure trading is halted two working days before maturity/redemption date
- Clarifications can be obtained by contacting BSE on 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 7, 2025
- **91TB060226 Maturity**: February 6, 2026 (trading stops February 4, 2026)
- **182TB08526 Maturity**: May 8, 2026 (trading stops May 6, 2026)
- **364TB61126 Maturity**: November 6, 2026 (trading stops November 4, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of government securities that expands debt instrument trading options on BSE.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems with new scrip codes and ISIN numbers. The pre-maturity trading suspension rule is standard practice.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional short to medium-term government securities investment options with maturities ranging from 3 months to 12 months.
