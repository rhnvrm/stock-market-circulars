## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 21, 2025. Both securities carry 10% annual interest with monthly payment schedules and are rated IND A/Stable.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities listed: KFL-10%-30-11-27-PVT (100,000 quantity) and KFL-10%-31-05-28-PVT (50,000 quantity)
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10,000 each
- Interest rate: 10% per annum with monthly payments
- Credit rating: IND A/Stable for both securities
- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms and conditions
- Contact BSE debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date (Security 1): November 30, 2027
- Redemption Date (Security 2): May 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for a private placement. The securities provide fixed income investment options with stable credit rating and regular monthly interest payments.
