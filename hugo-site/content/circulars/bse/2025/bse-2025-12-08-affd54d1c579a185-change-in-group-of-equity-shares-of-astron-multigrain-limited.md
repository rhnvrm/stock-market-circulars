## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544628), an SME IPO company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective December 22, 2025. This follows the exchange's earlier notice dated December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544628
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Monday, December 22, 2025
- Notice continuation from: Notice No. 20251205-56 dated December 5, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from the restrictive Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to the more flexible Rolling settlement system. This change upgrades the stock's trading category from MT Group to M Group, allowing for normal T+1 settlement instead of trade-for-trade basis.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change by December 22, 2025
- Adjust trading and settlement procedures for the scrip accordingly
- Contact BSE officials for clarification if needed

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 8, 2025
- Effective Date: December 22, 2025
- Prior Notice Reference: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment indicates improved liquidity prospects for ASTRON MULTIGRAIN LIMITED shares. This change typically occurs when exchange requirements are met and suggests normalized trading operations. Trading members will experience enhanced flexibility in trading this scrip, and investors will benefit from standard rolling settlement instead of compulsory delivery-based trading.
