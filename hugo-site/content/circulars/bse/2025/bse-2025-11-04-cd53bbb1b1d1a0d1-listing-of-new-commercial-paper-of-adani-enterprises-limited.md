## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total value Rs. 5 crore), issued at a discount with allotment date November 3, 2025 and redemption date February 11, 2026. The Commercial Paper carries credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ and will be traded on BSE's Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Quantity: 100 units (Total: Rs. 5 crore)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,90,235.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730492
- Scrip ID: AEL031125
- ISIN: INE423A14YM4
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YM4
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for all trades
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 100 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no significant market-wide implications.

**Issuer Impact**: Standard treasury operation for Adani Enterprises Limited to meet short-term funding requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in the debt market. The instrument offers short-term investment opportunity with tenor of approximately 100 days at an implied discount yield.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. Only relevant to debt market trading members dealing with commercial paper instruments.
