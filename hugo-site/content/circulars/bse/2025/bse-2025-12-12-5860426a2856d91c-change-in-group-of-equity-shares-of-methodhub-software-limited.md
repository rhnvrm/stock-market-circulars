## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544637) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling settlement segment (M Group) effective December 29, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a transition to normal trading operations.

## Key Points

- Stock will move from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling Settlement)
- Scrip Code: 544637
- Change is effective from Monday, December 29, 2025
- Applies to METHODHUB SOFTWARE LIMITED listed on SME platform
- Previous notice reference: 20251211-50 dated December 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism will change from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement. In T2T segment, shares must be delivered compulsorily and are subject to trading restrictions. The move to Rolling settlement (M Group) allows normal trading with standard settlement cycles and no mandatory delivery restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change
- Investors can trade the stock under normal rolling settlement rules from December 29, 2025
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025 (Monday)
- **Previous Notice**: December 11, 2025 (Notice No. 20251211-50)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The transition from T2T to Rolling settlement is generally positive for stock liquidity as it removes trading restrictions typical of the Trade for Trade segment. Investors will have more flexibility in trading without compulsory delivery requirements.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their internal systems and processes to accommodate the group change. The stock will be accessible for normal intraday and delivery-based trading activities from the effective date.
