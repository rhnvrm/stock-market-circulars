## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 781 dated December 22, 2025. The auction covers 107 scrips from various sectors including major companies like Adani Enterprises, Bajaj Auto, ICICI Bank, Tata Motors, Siemens, and others. The quantities range from 1 share to 8,756 shares depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 781
- Date: December 22, 2025
- Total scrips affected: 107
- Sectors covered: Automobiles, technology, pharmaceuticals, energy, financial services, real estate, and manufacturing
- Major companies included: Adani Enterprises (30 shares), Adani Green (18 shares), Bajaj Auto (27 shares), ICICI Bank (1 share), Tata Motors (20 shares combined), Reliance Power (291 shares)
- Largest auction quantity: HDB Financial (8,756 shares) and Healthy Life (8,000 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard demat auction notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must comply with BSE demat auction procedures for Settlement No. 781
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled for the listed scrips and quantities
- Standard auction participation rules apply as per BSE guidelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 22, 2025
- Settlement Number: 781

## Impact Assessment

The auction affects a diverse range of 107 scrips across multiple sectors. While the quantities for most scrips are relatively small (ranging from 1 to a few hundred shares), some scrips like HDB Financial (8,756 shares), Healthy Life (8,000 shares), Ola Electric (5,760 shares), and Vikran Engineering (2,921 shares) have larger quantities. The auction represents routine settlement procedures for delivery failures and has medium-term impact on the affected securities' settlement process. Market participants holding these securities should ensure compliance with auction procedures.
