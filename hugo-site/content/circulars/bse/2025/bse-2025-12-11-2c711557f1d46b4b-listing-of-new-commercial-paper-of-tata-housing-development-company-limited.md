## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective December 11, 2025. The instruments consist of 3100 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on March 11, 2026. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Total quantity: 3100 Commercial Papers
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,474 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730751
- Scrip ID: THDC101225
- ISIN: INE582L14JB8
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14JB8
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 11, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing of Commercial Papers on private placement basis provides short-term debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt market. The 91-day maturity period and high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low-risk short-term financing by Tata Housing Development Company Limited. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
