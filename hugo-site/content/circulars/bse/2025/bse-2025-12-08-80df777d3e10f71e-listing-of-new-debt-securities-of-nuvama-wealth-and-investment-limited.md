## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis, effective December 8, 2025. The securities carry an 8.95% annual interest rate with a face value of Rs. 100,000 and will mature on January 4, 2028. A total quantity of 10,000 units has been issued with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977346
- **Scrip ID**: 895NWIL28
- **ISIN Number**: INE523L07AR1
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AA/Stable
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000.00
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000.00
- **Interest Rate**: 8.95% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Quantity Issued**: 10,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AR1
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 4, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: January 4, 2028
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Annually

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with limited direct impact on broader equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debt market participants - The securities offer an 8.95% annual return with CARE AA/Stable rating, providing a debt investment option for institutional and qualified investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for trading members dealing in debt securities. The securities are available for trading in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

**Credit Profile**: The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates high degree of safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations, with very low credit risk.
