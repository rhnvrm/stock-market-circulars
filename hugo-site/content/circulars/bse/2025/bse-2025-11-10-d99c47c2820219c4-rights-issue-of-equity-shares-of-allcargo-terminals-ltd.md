## Summary

ALLCARGO TERMINALS LTD (Scrip Code: 543954) has announced a Rights Issue with a Record Date of November 14, 2025. The company is offering 3 Equity Shares of Rs.2 each for every 19 Equity Shares held at an issue price of Rs.20 per share (including premium of Rs.18). Trading in the equity shares will be done on Ex-Rights basis from November 14, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-756/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights Ratio: 3 new Equity Shares for every 19 existing Equity Shares held
- Face Value: Rs.2 per share
- Issue Price: Rs.20 per share (Face Value Rs.2 + Premium Rs.18)
- Record Date: November 14, 2025
- Ex-Rights Date: November 14, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-756/2025-2026
- Payment Structure: Rs.5 per share on application, balance payable within 12 months from allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure transactions in ALLCARGO TERMINALS LTD equity shares are executed on Ex-Rights basis from November 14, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-rights arrangements
- Proper settlement procedures must be followed for Settlement No. DR-756/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Trading Begins**: November 14, 2025
- **Application Payment**: Rs.5 per share on application
- **Balance Payment Deadline**: Within 12 months from date of allotment

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will impact existing shareholders of ALLCARGO TERMINALS LTD who hold shares as of the record date. Shareholders will have the option to subscribe to 3 additional shares for every 19 shares held. The flexible payment structure (Rs.5 on application with balance due within 12 months) provides shareholders with extended payment terms. The ex-rights trading adjustment will affect the stock's market price from November 14, 2025 onwards. Trading members need to ensure proper handling of ex-rights transactions to maintain market integrity.
