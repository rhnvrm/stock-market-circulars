## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 15,73,000 new equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676) effective from December 22, 2025. These shares were issued at Re. 1/- each with a premium of Rs. 49/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 15,73,000 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 50/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 49/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading commences: December 22, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 121600191 to 123173190
- ISIN: INE337M01021
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from December 22, 2025
- All 15,73,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 31, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with minimal market-wide impact. The preferential allotment represents a capital raising activity through warrant conversion. The lock-in period ensures stability of the new shareholding. Trading members of BSE can trade these securities alongside existing shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited from December 22, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions.
