## Summary

BSE has announced that AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 17, 2025. This follows the previous notice No. 20251027-59. The fund offers six plan variants across Regular and Direct plans with different dividend and growth options.

## Key Points

- AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF reopens for ongoing transactions on November 17, 2025
- Available transaction types: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct Plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All variants categorized as DEBT funds
- Transactions available through BSE StAR MF Platform
- References previous notice No. 20251027-59

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF are available:

**Regular Plans:**
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF846KA1291)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF846KA1317)
- Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1275)

**Direct Plans:**
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF846KA1309)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF846KA1325)
- Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1283)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational notice regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about mutual fund scheme availability.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 14, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 17, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors interested in the specific Axis Mutual Fund scheme.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for existing and prospective investors of AXIS INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE PASSIVE FOF who can now resume or initiate transactions including systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal plans.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members can facilitate transactions in all six variants of this fund of funds scheme from November 17, 2025.
