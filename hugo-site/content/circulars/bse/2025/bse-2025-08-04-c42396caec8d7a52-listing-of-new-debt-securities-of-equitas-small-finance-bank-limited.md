## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited worth Rs. 50 crore on private placement basis. The securities carry 9.60% annual interest rate and mature on July 31, 2030.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976979, Scrip ID: 960ESFBL30
- ISIN: INE063P08120
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 9.60% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-, IND AA-
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for complete details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: August 4, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: July 31, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option backed by Equitas Small Finance Bank's credit rating.
