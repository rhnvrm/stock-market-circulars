## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis effective November 13, 2025. The instrument comprises 1000 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value each with CRISIL A1+ rating, issued at Rs.491,423.50 per unit, allotted on November 12, 2025 and maturing on February 11, 2026. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.491,423.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730549
- Scrip ID: MAFS121125
- Detail Name: MAFS-11-2-26-CP
- ISIN: INE0JRU14339
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 11, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0JRU14339
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: November 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard commercial paper listing that adds liquidity to the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors. The 91-day tenor instrument provides short-term investment opportunity.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members can now execute trades in this instrument on BSE Debt segment in standard lots of Rs.5 lakhs.

**Credit Quality**: CRISIL A1+ rating indicates highest degree of safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations, reflecting strong credit quality of Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited.
