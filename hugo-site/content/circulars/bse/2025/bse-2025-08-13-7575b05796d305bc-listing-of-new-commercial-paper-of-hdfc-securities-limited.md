## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis effective August 13, 2025. The issue comprises 3,600 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 18 crores, with maturity date of November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 3,600 securities
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 18 crores
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per security
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Scrip Code: 730015
- ISIN: INE700G14PI8
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must comply with debt segment trading rules

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 13, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for HDFC Securities Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper with 91-day tenure.
