## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Mahip Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 542503) will be moved from its current trading group to the 'M' group effective October 15, 2025. This change in classification may impact trading parameters and investor participation in the stock.

## Key Points

- Company: Mahip Industries Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 542503
- New Group: 'M' group
- Effective Date: October 15, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251008-18
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified under the 'M' group, which is typically used by BSE for companies with specific characteristics such as lower market capitalization, limited trading volumes, or those requiring additional surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Note the change in group classification for Mahip Industries Ltd.
- Update their systems and records accordingly before October 15, 2025
- Contact Ms. Sonali Kurhade (Cell: +91-8779628213) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'M' group may result in:
- Changed trading parameters and margin requirements
- Potential impact on liquidity and trading volumes
- Modified surveillance measures
- Investors and trading members should review their positions and strategies accordingly
