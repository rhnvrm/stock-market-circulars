## Summary

BSE has announced the commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for 3I INFOTECH LIMITED. The REs will be available for renunciation trading from October 07, 2025 to October 20, 2025. The Rights Issue itself will open on October 07, 2025 and close on October 27, 2025. Trading will be conducted on T+1 rolling settlement basis in dematerialized form only, with trade-for-trade settlement.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading period: October 07, 2025 to October 20, 2025
- Rights Issue open period: October 07, 2025 to October 27, 2025
- Scrip Code: 751041, Scrip ID: 3IINFO-RE
- ISIN: INE748C20012
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Settlement: T+1 rolling settlement, trade-for-trade basis
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- REs must be either subscribed with application money or renounced before issue closing date, otherwise they will lapse

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing framework for Rights Entitlements trading as per notices 20200305-21 (dated March 05, 2020) and 20200518-34 (dated May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must inform all clients about commencement of trading in REs with proper ISIN identification
- Trading Members must ensure clients do not mistake REs for ordinary shares
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include disclaimer explaining:
  - Purchase of REs only gives right to participate in ongoing Rights Issue or renounce the REs
  - Application requires requisite application money
  - Un-subscribed and un-renounced REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date
- Trading Members must refer to previous notices (20200305-21 and 20200518-34) for complete details

## Important Dates

- **October 06, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **October 07, 2025**: 
  - RE trading for renunciation commences
  - Rights Issue opens
- **October 20, 2025**: Last day for RE renunciation trading
- **October 27, 2025**: Rights Issue closes
- **Post October 27, 2025**: Un-subscribed/un-renounced REs will lapse and be extinguished

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects existing shareholders of 3I INFOTECH LIMITED who wish to participate in the rights issue or trade their entitlements.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading Members need to update systems for RE trading, ensure proper client communication, modify contract notes with mandatory disclaimers, and handle T+1 trade-for-trade settlements in demat form only.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders must decide within the specified window whether to subscribe to the rights issue, renounce their entitlements for trading, or let them lapse. Time-sensitive decision required between October 07-27, 2025.
