## Summary

BSE has announced that OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective November 14, 2025. This follows previous Notice No. 20251104-9 and includes availability of SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND reopened for ongoing transactions effective November 14, 2025
- All six plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) are now available
- Subscription and redemption facilities enabled
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities available for all plans
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Notice supersedes previous Notice No. 20251104-9

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND are available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF0S5R01091)
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF0S5R01109)
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF0S5R01117)
4. **Direct Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF0S5R01125)
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF0S5R01075)
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF0S5R01083)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notification regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF Platform members should enable the OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND schemes for client transactions
- All six plan variants must be made available for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP from November 14, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: 20251104-9 dated November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notification affecting only investors and distributors of OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND.

**Investor Impact**: Investors can now resume or initiate transactions in OLD BRIDGE ARBITRAGE FUND schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform with full access to systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal facilities.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members need to ensure the fund is enabled for transactions from November 14, 2025.
