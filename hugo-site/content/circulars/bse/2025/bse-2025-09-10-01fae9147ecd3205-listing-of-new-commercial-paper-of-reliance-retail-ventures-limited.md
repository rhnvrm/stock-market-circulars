## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 1,700 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 34,000 units (Rs. 1,700 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730168, ISIN: INE929O14EB6
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Listing date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited and adds to the available debt instruments for institutional investors.
