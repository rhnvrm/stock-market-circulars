## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Bisil Plast Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Karnawati Innovation Limited with effect from October 23, 2025. The company, originally incorporated as Bisil Plast Private Limited, is a company limited by shares with CIN L17119GJ1986PLC009009.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Bisil Plast Limited** to **Karnawati Innovation Limited**
- Effective date: October 23, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L17119GJ1986PLC009009
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: Bisil Plast Private Limited
- Certificate issued under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014
- Approved and digitally signed by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate administrative action processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs' Central Processing Centre.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display its old name (Bisil Plast Limited) along with the new name (Karnawati Innovation Limited) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new company name

## Important Dates

- **October 23, 2025**: Effective date of name change and certificate issuance
- **October 23, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate administrative change that does not affect the company's business operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative only. The company will need to update its letterheads, signage, and official documents to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on stakeholders. All existing rights, obligations, and liabilities continue unchanged. Stakeholders are advised to verify the company's latest status on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
