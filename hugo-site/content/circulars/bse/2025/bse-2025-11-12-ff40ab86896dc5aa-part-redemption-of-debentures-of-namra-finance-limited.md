## Summary

NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date has been fixed as November 19, 2025. Post redemption, the debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 70,000 per debenture effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE229U07137
- Scrip Code: 975677
- Debenture Series: NFL-11%-24-5-26-PVT
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record Date: November 19, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 70,000 per debenture
- Effective Date: November 19, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-759/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value from the effective date
- All trading and settlement activities must reflect the new face value of Rs. 70,000 per debenture from November 19, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for accurate pricing and settlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: November 19, 2025 - for determining eligibility for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: November 19, 2025
- **Settlement**: DR-759/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: November 11, 2025 (issued November 12, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Eligible debenture holders as on the record date will receive interest payment and partial redemption proceeds. The face value per debenture will be reduced to Rs. 70,000.

**Market Impact**: Trading will continue with the reduced face value from November 19, 2025. Market participants should adjust their valuation models and trading strategies accordingly.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new face value for accurate trade execution and settlement in the debt segment.
