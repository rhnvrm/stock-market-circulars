## Summary

Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited has listed new debt securities on the BSE Debt segment effective November 4, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 40,000 units at a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling Rs. 400 crores. The securities carry a credit rating of CARE A+/Positive and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977267
- **Scrip ID**: 1020CMEE27
- **Detail Name**: CMEESL-10.20%-30-6-27-PVT
- **ISIN Number**: INE647U08039
- **Quantity**: 40,000 units
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 31, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 10.20% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A+/Positive
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE647U08039
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from December 31, 2025 to June 30, 2027
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on a private placement basis and will only affect participants in the debt market. The CARE A+/Positive rating indicates stable credit quality. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new listing for their records and client advisory purposes. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
