## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 174/2025-2026 dated December 11, 2025, announces record dates and book closure dates for various companies' debt securities. The bulletin covers scheduled interest payments and partial redemptions for privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs) and commercial paper redemptions across multiple issuers.

## Key Points

- Record dates set for interest payments on privately placed NCDs from multiple finance companies
- Partial redemption schedules announced for various NCD issuers
- Commercial paper redemption date specified for Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited
- Record dates range from December 2025 to March 2026
- All listed securities will continue trading (Trading flag: 1) during record date determination

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must be registered holders on the specified record dates to be eligible for interest payments or redemption proceeds
- Companies must process payments/redemptions as per scheduled dates
- Trading members should note record dates for client servicing purposes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine administrative bulletin that documents scheduled corporate actions for debt securities. The information is primarily relevant to bondholders and debenture investors of the specific issuers mentioned.

**Operational Impact:** Standard processing required by registrars and transfer agents to ensure eligible holders receive payments on due dates. No special operational changes required.

**Investor Impact:** Affects holders of specific debt securities who need to be registered on record dates to receive interest payments or redemption proceeds. Trading continues normally as all securities maintain trading flag status.
