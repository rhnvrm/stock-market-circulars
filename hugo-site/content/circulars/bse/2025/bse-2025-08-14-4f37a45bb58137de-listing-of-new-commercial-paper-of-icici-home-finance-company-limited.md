## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED worth Rs. 40 crores (8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ with a maturity period of 43 days.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 8,000 units (Total value: Rs. 40 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 496,554.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730026
- ISIN: INE071G14HA8
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 26, 2025
- Maturity Period: 43 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides ICICI HOME FINANCE with short-term funding at competitive rates given the A1+ ratings from both CARE and ICRA.
