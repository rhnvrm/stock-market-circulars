## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities scheduled for demat auction settlement number 758 dated November 19, 2025. The auction includes 98 different scrips with varying quantities to be settled through the auction mechanism. This is a routine market operation to clear pending delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 758
- Settlement Date: November 19, 2025
- Total Scrips Listed: 98
- Largest quantity: RPOWER (Reliance Power) - 200,000 shares
- Second largest: SPRIGHT (Spright Agro) - 499,754 shares
- Notable large caps included: Infosys, NTPC, Tata Motors, Tata Steel, Axis Bank, Adani Group companies
- Quantities range from 1 share to several hundred thousand shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard demat auction settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants with obligations in the listed securities must ensure settlement through the auction mechanism
- Members must comply with standard auction settlement procedures as per BSE regulations
- Timely payment and delivery obligations must be met as per settlement schedule

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: November 19, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 758

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine demat auction settlement affecting specific delivery obligations. While the list includes some large-cap stocks like Infosys, Tata Motors, NTPC, and Adani group companies, the quantities involved are relatively small compared to their daily trading volumes. The largest quantities are in mid and small-cap stocks (Reliance Power - 200,000 shares, Spright Agro - 499,754 shares, Shanti Educational - 37,983 shares). Members with outstanding delivery obligations in these scrips will need to participate in the auction to fulfill their settlement requirements.
