## Summary

BSE has announced that ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 27, 2025. The fund will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Two growth plan options with separate ISINs
- References previous notice 20251031-37

## Fund Details

**Available Plans:**

1. **ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH**
   - Category: Equity
   - ISIN: INF1J2R01130

2. **ANGEL ONE NIFTY TOTAL MARKET MOMENTUM QUALITY 50 INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
   - Category: Equity
   - ISIN: INF1J2R01122

## Transaction Facilities

- Ongoing subscription (purchases)
- Redemption (withdrawals)
- Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)
- Systematic Transfer Plan (STP)
- Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Previous Related Notice:** October 31, 2025 (Notice No. 20251031-37)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. It affects only investors interested in this specific Angel One mutual fund scheme. The reopening provides investment access to a Nifty Total Market Momentum Quality 50 index-tracking fund with systematic investment facilities, expanding options for retail and institutional investors on the BSE StAR MF platform.
