## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the company's debt securities will have a new ISIN and revised paid-up value effective October 17, 2025. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250915-28 dated September 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has received call money from debenture holders for specific debt securities
- Scrip Code 977103 affected by this change
- Old ISIN INE674K07085 will be replaced with new ISIN INE674K07127
- Paid-up value increased from Rs. 50,000/- per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000/- per debenture
- Changes are in continuation of previous notice dated September 15, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update reflecting corporate action on existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new ISIN (INE674K07127) for transactions from October 17, 2025
- Update systems to reflect revised paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000/- per debenture
- Ensure proper recording of the ISIN change in trading and settlement systems

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 (Friday) - New ISIN and paid-up value come into effect
- **Previous Related Notice**: September 15, 2025 (Exchange Notice No. 20250915-28)

## Impact Assessment

This update has moderate impact limited to holders and traders of Aditya Birla Capital's specific debt securities (Scrip Code 977103). The doubling of paid-up value from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture reflects call money received from debenture holders. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN to ensure smooth trading and settlement. The change does not affect equity markets or broader debt market operations.
