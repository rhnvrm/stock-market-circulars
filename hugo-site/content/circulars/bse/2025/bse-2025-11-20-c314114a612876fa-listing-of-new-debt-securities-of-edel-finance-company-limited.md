## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Edel Finance Company Limited issued on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective November 20, 2025. The securities have a face value of Rs. 100,000, issue price of Rs. 98,782, and carry a CRISIL A+/STABLE credit rating with redemption scheduled for October 30, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1,000 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976968
- Scrip ID: EFCL300725
- Detail Name: EFCL-30-10-28-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE836K07312 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A+/STABLE
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 98,782.00 (issued at discount)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE836K07312
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 20, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 19, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 20, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 30, 2028
