## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Neptune Logitek Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544663, symbol NEPLOG, and ISIN number INE1NQ501012. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Neptune Logitek Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544663
- Trading Symbol: NEPLOG
- ISIN: INE1NQ501012
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new listing and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact notice providing advance information about a new SME listing. Trading members are informed of the company details for reference, but no immediate market impact until the actual listing date is announced. This is a standard procedural announcement for new listings on the SME platform.
