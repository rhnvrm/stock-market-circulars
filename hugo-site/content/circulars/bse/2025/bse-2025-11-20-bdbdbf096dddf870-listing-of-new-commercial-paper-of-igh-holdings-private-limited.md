## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis effective November 20, 2025. The issue comprises 6000 units of Commercial Paper with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 300 crores. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. The issue is priced at Rs. 4,91,695 per unit with a redemption date of February 18, 2026, representing a tenure of approximately 90 days.

## Key Points

- 6000 units of Commercial Paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,695 per unit (discount of Rs. 8,305 per unit)
- Total issue size: Rs. 300 crores
- Scrip Code: 730599, Scrip ID: IGHH191125
- ISIN: INE02FN14671 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Allotment date: November 20, 2025
- Redemption date: February 18, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing announcement for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02FN14671
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 90 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis affecting only the debt market segment. The listing adds Rs. 300 crores of short-term debt instruments to the BSE platform.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for institutional and high net-worth investors in debt market who invest in commercial papers. The dual A1+ rating from CARE and CRISIL indicates highest credit quality for short-term instruments.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on equity markets or general trading operations. Debt segment participants can trade these instruments in standard lots of Rs. 5 lakhs.
