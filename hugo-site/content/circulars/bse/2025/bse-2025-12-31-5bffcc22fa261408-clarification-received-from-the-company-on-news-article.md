## Summary

Vodafone Idea Limited issued a clarification under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 in response to a news article that appeared in mainstream media regarding AGR (Adjusted Gross Revenue) dues of Rs. 87,695 crore being frozen by Cabinet. The company stated it has not received any official communication from the Government on this matter and will make appropriate disclosures when any development requiring disclosure occurs.

## Key Points

- News article titled "Vodafone Idea AGR dues of Rs. 87,695 crore frozen by Cabinet; Stock gives up gains" appeared in mainstream media
- The news caused material price movement in Vodafone Idea's stock on 31 December 2025
- Company has not received any communication from the Government regarding the reported matter
- Company will disclose information as and when there is any development requiring disclosure
- Clarification issued in compliance with SEBI listing regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes were announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This circular represents the company's compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, which requires listed entities to clarify material news reports that may impact stock prices.

## Important Dates

- **31 December 2025**: Date of news article publication and stock price movement
- **31 December 2025**: Date of clarification issued to stock exchanges

## Impact Assessment

The news article caused material price movement in Vodafone Idea's stock (Symbol: IDEA, Scrip code: 532822). The company's clarification that it has not received any official government communication on the matter may help address market speculation. The AGR dues issue is significant for Vodafone Idea as it pertains to substantial regulatory liabilities. Investors should note that no official development has been confirmed by the company despite media reports.
