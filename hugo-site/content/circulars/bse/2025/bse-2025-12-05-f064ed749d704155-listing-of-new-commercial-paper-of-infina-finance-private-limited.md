## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 5, 2025. The Commercial Paper consists of 5000 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at a discount price of Rs. 4,65,901 per unit, maturing on November 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 5000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit (total Rs. 250 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,65,901 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730706
- Scrip ID: IFPL041225
- ISIN Number: INE879F14MC2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 30, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited
- Trading format: Dematerialised only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE879F14MC2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 5, 2025
- Listing Date: December 5, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are short-term debt instruments available only to institutional and high-net-worth investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for their records. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
