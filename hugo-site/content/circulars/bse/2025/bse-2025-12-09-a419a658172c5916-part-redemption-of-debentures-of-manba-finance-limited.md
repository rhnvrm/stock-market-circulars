## Summary

MANBA FINANCE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date is fixed as December 14, 2025, and trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value from December 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issuer: MANBA FINANCE LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE939X07242, Code: 977173)
- Coupon Rate: 11%
- Record Date: December 14, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 90,000 per debenture
- Effective Date: December 12, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-776/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value of Rs. 90,000 per debenture effective December 12, 2025
- All trading systems and records should be updated to reflect the new face value from the effective date
- Investors holding debentures as of record date (December 14, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **December 9, 2025**: Notice issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-776/2025-2026)
- **December 14, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting only holders of specific debentures of MANBA FINANCE LIMITED.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders - Part redemption will return capital to investors, reducing their outstanding investment in these debentures. The face value reduction from original value to Rs. 90,000 indicates partial principal repayment.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from December 12, 2025. Standard operational procedure for debt instrument redemptions.
