## Summary

BSE has listed 26,06,203 new equity shares of Windsor Machines Limited (Scrip Code: 522029) issued on preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from December 29, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until June 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 26,06,203 equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 189.85/-
- Issue price: Rs. 191.85 per share
- Allotment date: October 7, 2025
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished Numbers: 84478319 to 87084521
- ISIN: INE052A01021
- Scrip Code: 522029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 26,06,203 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions
- Shares cannot be traded until lock-in period expires on June 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The preferential allotment increases the paid-up capital of Windsor Machines Limited, but all shares are locked in until June 2027, preventing any immediate market liquidity impact. The allotment to promoters may indicate confidence in the company's prospects. Trading members should note these shares in their systems but they will not be available for trading until the lock-in period expires.
