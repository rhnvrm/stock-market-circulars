## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for debt securities of Aethon Developers Private Limited effective October 20, 2025. This action follows the earlier notice dated August 6, 2025 (notice number 20250806-13) and is being implemented due to ongoing restructuring of the company. The suspension applies to Scrip Code 976099 bearing ISIN INE15S307015.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from Monday, October 20, 2025
- Suspension relates to debt securities with Scrip Code: 976099
- ISIN affected: INE15S307015
- Reason for suspension: Company restructuring
- This is a continuation of earlier notice 20250806-13 dated August 6, 2025
- Trading Members can contact BSE officials for further details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements a trading suspension measure as part of the exchange's operational procedures during corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the suspension and cease trading in the specified scrip code from October 20, 2025
- Trading Members should contact BSE officials for any clarifications or additional information needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Effective Date of Trading Suspension**: October 20, 2025 (Monday)
- **Reference to Previous Notice**: August 6, 2025 (notice number 20250806-13)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The suspension affects trading in specific debt securities of Aethon Developers Private Limited. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade them on the exchange from October 20, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the suspension and ensure compliance. The suspension is tied to the restructuring process, which may indicate financial difficulties or strategic reorganization.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these debt securities will face liquidity constraints as trading will be suspended. The restructuring nature suggests potential changes to the terms or structure of these debt instruments.
