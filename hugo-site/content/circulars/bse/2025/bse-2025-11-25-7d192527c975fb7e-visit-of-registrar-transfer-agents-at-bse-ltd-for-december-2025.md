## Summary

BSE has published the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) visiting BSE premises during December 2025. The circular provides a list of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit. These visits enable investors to address grievances and service requests related to share transfers, dematerialization, and other investor services directly at BSE premises.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule for December 2025 published by BSE
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is one of the visiting RTAs
- List includes 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate, covering diverse sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, steel, textiles, and technology
- Investors can utilize these visits for resolving share-related grievances and service requests
- Companies range from established names to smaller listed entities across various BSE scrip codes (501270 to 524606 range)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for investor convenience.

## Important Dates

- **December 2025**: Month during which Registrar & Transfer Agents including Adroit Corporate Services will visit BSE premises (specific dates not provided in the excerpt)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for shareholders of the listed companies, providing direct access to RTA services at BSE premises for resolving transfer, demat, and other share-related issues.

**Operational Impact**: Facilitates investor grievance redressal mechanism and enhances investor service delivery for companies serviced by visiting RTAs.
