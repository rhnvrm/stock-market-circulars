## Summary

BSE announced the addition of Studds Accessories Limited (Exchange ticker 544599) to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, November 11, 2025. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Friday, November 7, 2025, as referenced in Notice No: 20251106-50.

## Key Points

- Studds Accessories Limited listed on BSE on November 7, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544599
- Will be added to BSE IPO Index effective November 11, 2025
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index-tracking funds and portfolio managers may need to adjust their holdings to reflect the updated BSE IPO Index composition.

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Studds Accessories Limited listing date on BSE
- **November 11, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative action affecting index composition. Impact is primarily on:

- Index-tracking funds that replicate the BSE IPO Index
- Passive investment vehicles tied to the BSE IPO Index
- Investors monitoring newly listed companies

The addition reflects standard index maintenance procedures following new listings and does not indicate any broader market or regulatory changes.
