## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 5 crores), with allotment date of November 28, 2025 and redemption date of November 27, 2026. The commercial paper carries dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 730679
- **Scrip ID**: AEL281125
- **ISIN**: INE423A14YP7
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,64,774 per unit
- **Quantity**: 100 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14YP7
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for trading
- Trading is restricted to BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 01, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 27, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 12 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that adds one more short-term debt instrument to the BSE Debt segment. The instrument is relatively small in size (Rs. 5 crores) and will primarily be of interest to institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness, making it a low-risk short-term investment option.

**Investor Impact**: Provides an additional short-term investment avenue for debt market participants seeking highly-rated commercial paper with approximately 12-month maturity.
