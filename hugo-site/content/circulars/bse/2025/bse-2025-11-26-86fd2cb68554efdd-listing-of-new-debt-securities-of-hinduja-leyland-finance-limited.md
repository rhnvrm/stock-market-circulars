## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited (HLFL) on its Debt segment effective November 26, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, totaling 30,000 units (Rs. 300 crore). The debentures carry a 9.25% annual interest rate with CARE AA+ and CRISIL AA+ credit ratings, maturing on July 9, 2031.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 30,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,165.75
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 300 crore approximately
- Scrip Code: 977200
- Scrip ID: 925HLFL31
- ISIN: INE146O08399 (Further listings under same ISIN)
- Interest Rate: 9.25% per annum
- Credit Rating: CARE AA+, CRISIL AA+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE146O08399
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from November 26, 2025
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually from October 9, 2026 to July 9, 2031
- **Redemption Date**: July 9, 2031

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt listing with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Affects only debt segment investors interested in Hinduja Leyland Finance securities. The AA+ rating indicates strong credit quality.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard listing procedure with demat-only trading as per existing norms. No put/call options attached to these securities.
