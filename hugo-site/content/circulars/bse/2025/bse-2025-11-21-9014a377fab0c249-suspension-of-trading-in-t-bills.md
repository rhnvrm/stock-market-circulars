## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for T-bill 815GUJ2025 (Scrip Code: 810586, ISIN: IN1520150088) effective November 24, 2025, as the security approaches its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 815GUJ2025 to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 810586
- ISIN: IN1520150088
- Suspension effective from November 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-762/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification for T-bill maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-bill from November 24, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- All pending orders for this security should be reviewed and cancelled as necessary

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** November 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** On or shortly after November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension of a single T-bill ahead of its scheduled maturity. Trading members holding positions in this security should plan for redemption. No broader market implications expected.
