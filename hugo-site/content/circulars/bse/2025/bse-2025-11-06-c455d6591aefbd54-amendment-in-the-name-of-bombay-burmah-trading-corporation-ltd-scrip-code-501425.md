## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding an amendment in the name of Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd (Scrip code: 501425). This is an administrative update to the company's name registration with the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd
- Scrip Code: 501425
- Nature: Name amendment
- Exchange: BSE

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the official name of the company as registered with BSE. The name amendment will be reflected in all exchange systems and records.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the updated name for the company
- Systems and records should be updated to reflect the name change

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The name amendment does not affect the scrip code, ISIN, or trading operations. Market participants should update their internal records to reflect the new name.
