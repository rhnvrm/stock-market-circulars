## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific debentures (ISIN: INE651J07952, Code: 974918) of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited effective November 7, 2025. The suspension is to facilitate partial redemption due to investor buyback requests and interest payment processing, with a record date set for November 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are prohibited from dealing in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Record date for corporate action: November 8, 2025
- Purpose: Partial redemption due to buyback at investor request and payment of interest
- Affected debenture: JMFCSL 8.80% maturing December 19, 2025 (Private Placement)
- ISIN: INE651J07952
- BSE Code: 974918
- Notice Reference: DR-751/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from November 7, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of the suspension and inform relevant clients holding these securities
- No dealings permitted until further notice from the exchange

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 6, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective From:** November 7, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 8, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity Date:** December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only a specific privately placed debenture series of JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited. The suspension is temporary and related to routine corporate actions.

**Investor Impact:** Moderate for holders of this specific debenture series - no trading allowed during suspension period, but this facilitates timely processing of partial redemption and interest payment.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard procedure for record date-based corporate actions in debt securities. Trading Members need to update their systems to block trades in this security.
