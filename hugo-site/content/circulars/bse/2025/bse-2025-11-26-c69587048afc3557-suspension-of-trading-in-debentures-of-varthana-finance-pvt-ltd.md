## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in debentures of Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to early redemption of the company's Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd
- Scrip Code: 976016
- ISIN: INE125T07295
- Security: VFPL-11.65%-16-9-26-PVT
- Reason: Early Redemption of NCD
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard suspension notice due to corporate action (early redemption).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures of Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance mandatory from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (trading suspension begins)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only a single privately placed debenture series of Varthana Finance Pvt Ltd.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - holders of these specific NCDs will experience early redemption. No trading opportunity available from November 27, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard corporate action processing. Trading members need to update their systems to prevent transactions in the suspended security.
