## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544585 and symbol SIHORA with ISIN number INE0SFA01011. The listing will occur on the SME segment, with the specific listing date and security details to be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544585
- Trading Symbol: SIHORA
- ISIN Number: INE0SFA01011
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20251016-17
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions are required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for the SME segment. The impact is limited to trading members who need to be aware of the new security being added to the Exchange. No immediate market operations are affected as the listing date has not yet been specified. The announcement provides advance notice to market participants to prepare for the upcoming listing.
