## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Sharp Agricom Limited for public review and comments. The document was filed on July 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Sharp Agricom Limited on July 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document contains all ICDR Regulations Schedule VI disclosures
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard IPO process following ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public has 21 days to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: July 30, 2025
- Notice Date: July 31, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from July 31, 2025 (until August 21, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

Routine SME IPO process with standard public comment period. Limited immediate market impact as this is the draft stage. Potential future impact depends on successful completion of IPO process and company's market performance.
