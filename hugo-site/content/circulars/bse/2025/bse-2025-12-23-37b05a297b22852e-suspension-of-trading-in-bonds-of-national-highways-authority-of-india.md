## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) effective December 26, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NHAI 7.14% Tax Free NCD bonds (ISIN: INE906B07EG4, Code: 935578)
- Suspension effective from December 26, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Record date set for December 26, 2025
- Notice number: DR-785/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for bond redemption and interest payment processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified NHAI bonds from December 26, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No dealings permitted in INE906B07EG4 (Code: 935578) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects bondholders and traders of NHAI 7.14% tax-free NCDs. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trades in this specific bond from December 26, 2025. Bond holders on record date will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders as of record date will benefit from interest payment and principal redemption. No trading opportunity available from suspension date onwards for this instrument.
