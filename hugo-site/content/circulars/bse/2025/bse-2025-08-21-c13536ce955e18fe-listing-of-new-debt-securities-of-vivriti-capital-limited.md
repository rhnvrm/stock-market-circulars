## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Vivriti Capital Limited worth Rs. 175 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10.10% annual interest rate and will mature on August 19, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 17,500 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 175 crore
- Interest rate: 10.10% per annum paid quarterly
- Credit rating: CARE A+/Stable
- ISIN: INE01HV07601
- Scrip Code: 977042
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 19, 2025
- Interest payment: Quarterly basis
- Maturity date: August 19, 2027
- Trading commencement: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment with investment-grade rating.
