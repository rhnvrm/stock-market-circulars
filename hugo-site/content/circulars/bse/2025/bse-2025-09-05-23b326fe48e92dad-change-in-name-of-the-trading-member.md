## Summary

BSE has notified that trading member ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD. (Member No. 912) will change its name to "ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED" effective Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD. to ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED
- Member number 912 remains unchanged
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- Contact point provided for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the new corporate name of the trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the name change for their records
- No specific compliance actions required from other market participants
- Queries can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Monday, September 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is a routine administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unaffected. Other market participants need only update their records to reflect the new name.
