## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 4, 2025. Mother Nutri Foods Limited (ticker: 544623) and K K Silk Mills Limited (ticker: 544624) are being added following their listing on BSE on December 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two stocks to be added to BSE SME IPO Index: Mother Nutri Foods Limited (544623) and K K Silk Mills Limited (544624)
- Both companies listed on BSE on Wednesday, December 3, 2025
- Index inclusion effective at market open on Thursday, December 4, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No. 20251202-47 and 20251202-48 for respective company listings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust holdings to reflect the new composition.

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Listing date for Mother Nutri Foods Limited and K K Silk Mills Limited on BSE
- **December 4, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index rebalancing event following new SME listings. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to purchase shares of the newly added companies. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
