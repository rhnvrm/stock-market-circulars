## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective October 24, 2025. The issue comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 200 crore, issued on private placement basis. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a 91-day tenor maturing on January 23, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HD2
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be settled through the designated Issuing and Paying Agent (ICICI Bank Limited)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Tenor:** 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are available only to institutional and high-net-worth investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. The strong credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate low credit risk. The listing provides ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED with short-term funding at competitive rates, with an implied discount rate of approximately 1.5% for the 91-day period. Trading members dealing in debt securities can access these instruments for their clients' short-term debt portfolios.
