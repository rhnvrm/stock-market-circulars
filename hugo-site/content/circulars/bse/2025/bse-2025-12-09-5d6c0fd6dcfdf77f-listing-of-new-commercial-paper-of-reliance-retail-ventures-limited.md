## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 9, 2025. The issue comprises 13,800 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on March 9, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- **Issuer**: Reliance Retail Ventures Limited
- **Quantity**: 13,800 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730721
- **Scrip ID**: RRVL81225
- **Detail Name**: RRVL-9-3-26-CP
- **ISIN**: INE929O14EL5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE929O14EL5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: December 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of commercial paper by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on the BSE Debt segment. The listing provides short-term debt market participants with additional investment options in highly-rated (A1+) commercial paper from a major retail conglomerate. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers.
