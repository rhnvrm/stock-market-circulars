## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited on private placement basis, effective November 21, 2025. The securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with 500 units issued at Rs. 5,00,000 face value each.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity**: 500 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,54,545.50
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730612
- **Scrip ID**: MMFL201125
- **ISIN**: INE101Q14280
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 20, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE101Q14280
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 20, 2026
- **Tenure**: 1 year
