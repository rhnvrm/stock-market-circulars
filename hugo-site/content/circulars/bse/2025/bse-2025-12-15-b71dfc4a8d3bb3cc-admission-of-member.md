## Summary

BSE has admitted three trading members effective December 15, 2025. Two entities (Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited and Allvest Securities Private Limited) have been granted new memberships, while Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has been granted additional segment membership for commodity derivatives.

## Key Points

- Parshwa Prism Gems and Jewellery Limited (Member No. 6972) admitted for Cash and Commodity Derivatives segments as new member
- Allvest Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6973) admitted for Cash, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, and Debt segments as new member
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Member No. 6706) granted additional segment membership for Commodity Derivatives
- All memberships subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Contact email for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard membership admission notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025 (admission effective)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The admission of new trading members expands the BSE member base and provides additional market participants across cash, derivatives (equity, currency, commodity), and debt segments. No action required from existing trading members.
