## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities available for demat auction under Settlement No. 749 dated November 6, 2025. The auction includes 194 scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 25,000 shares per scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 749
- Date: November 6, 2025
- Total number of scrips: 194
- Largest quantities include: YES BANK (18,992), POWERGRID (19,774), CANARA BANK (25,787), STEEL AUTHORITY (14,762), and INDUS TOWERS (13,288)
- Covers major stocks across banking, power, oil & gas, telecom, pharmaceutical, and industrial sectors
- Auction conducted to settle short delivery obligations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate in the demat auction for settlement of their short delivery obligations
- Members must ensure settlement of the listed quantities for the specified scrips
- Auction process to be completed as per BSE's standard demat auction procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: November 6, 2025
- Settlement Number: 749

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement. The impact is localized to trading members with short delivery positions in the listed scrips. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of trades where sellers failed to deliver securities. Large quantities in major stocks like YES BANK, POWERGRID, and CANARA BANK may indicate significant short delivery positions that need resolution. The auction provides a mechanism for buyers to receive their securities and for the market to maintain settlement discipline.
