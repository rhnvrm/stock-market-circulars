## Summary

Swastika Castal Ltd (Scrip Code: 544452) equity shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective Monday, August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Swastika Castal Ltd
- Scrip Code: 544452
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: August 11, 2025
- Notice continuation from July 26, 2025 circular

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal Rolling segment allows for regular intraday trading and settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. Contact Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685) for further details.

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for liquidity as shares move from restrictive Trade for Trade to normal Rolling segment, enabling regular trading and settlement cycles.
