## Summary

EXATO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) for equity shares. The company has a total of 10,065,371 fully paid-up equity shares held by 739 shareholders. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 54.73% while Public shareholders hold 45.27%. The company has declared no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, depository receipts, locked-in shares, or pledged shares.

## Key Points

- Total paid-up equity shares: 10,065,371
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 5,508,924 shares (54.73%) held by 3 shareholders
- Public holding: 4,556,447 shares (45.27%) held by 736 shareholders
- Dematerialized shares: 8,151,371 shares (80.98% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants outstanding
- No depository receipts issued
- No shares in locked-in period
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The listed entity has submitted the required declaration confirming the absence of partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, depository receipts, locked-in shares, and pledged shares.

## Important Dates

- Filing date: December 4, 2025
- Report filed under: Regulation 31(1)(a)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows a stable promoter holding above 50%, indicating strong promoter control. The high dematerialization rate of 80.98% indicates good market participation. The absence of pledged shares by promoters suggests financial stability. No immediate action required by market participants.
