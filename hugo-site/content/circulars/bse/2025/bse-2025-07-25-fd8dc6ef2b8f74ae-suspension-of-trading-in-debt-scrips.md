## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a debt security (ISIN: INE408S07016, Scrip Code: 952566) identified as IND-29-7-25-PVT effective July 28, 2025, due to its maturity. Trading members are advised not to deal in this debenture from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Single debt security affected: INE408S07016 (Scrip Code: 952566)
- Security identifier: IND-29-7-25-PVT
- Trading suspension effective from July 28, 2025
- Reason: Security maturity
- Reference number: DR-682/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debenture from July 28, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **July 25, 2025**: Notice date
- **July 28, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Maturity Date**: Security reaches maturity (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only one debt security reaching natural maturity. This is a routine administrative action with no broader market implications. Trading members need to adjust their systems to reflect the suspension of this specific debt instrument.
