## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The privately placed bonds totaling 26,000 units (₹260 crore at face value) carry an 8.95% annual interest rate with ICRA AA/Stable rating and mature on June 4, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 26,000 units listed
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: ₹1,04,209.79 per unit
- Scrip Code: 976768
- Scrip ID: 895OPL27
- ISIN: INE248U07FW5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: ICRA AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Allotment Date: November 14, 2025
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No put/call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U07FW5
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from June 6, 2026 to June 4, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: June 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis affecting only the debt market segment.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Relevant only for debt investors and institutional participants in 360 ONE Prime bonds.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for general market participants; debt segment trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details.
