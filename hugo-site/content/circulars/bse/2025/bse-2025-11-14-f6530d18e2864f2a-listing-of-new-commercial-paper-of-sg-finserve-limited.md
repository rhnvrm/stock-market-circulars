## Summary

SG FINSERVE LIMITED has listed 500 units of Commercial Paper on BSE Debt segment through private placement. Each paper has a face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- with an issue price of Rs. 4,95,344/-. The instruments carry an ICRA A1+ rating and will mature on 30th December 2025.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Papers listed with Scrip Code 730565 and Scrip ID SGF131125
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,344/- per unit
- ISIN Number: INE618R14026
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: YES BANK LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE618R14026
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for price movements

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 13th November 2025
- Listing Date: 14th November 2025
- Redemption Date: 30th December 2025
- Notice Date: 14th November 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The short tenure (47 days from allotment to redemption) and private placement nature limit broader market implications. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Trading members in the debt segment can participate in these instruments following standard debt trading protocols.
