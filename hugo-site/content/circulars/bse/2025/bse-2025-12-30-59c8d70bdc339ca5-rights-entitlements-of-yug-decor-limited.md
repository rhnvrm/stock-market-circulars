## Summary

BSE has announced the commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of YUG DECOR LIMITED. Trading will be permitted from January 02, 2026 to January 20, 2026, with the Rights Issue opening period from January 02, 2026 to January 23, 2026. The REs will be settled on T+1 rolling settlement basis with trade-for-trade settlement and are tradable in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Company: YUG DECOR LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 751073
- Scrip ID: YUG-RE1
- ISIN: INE796W20027
- Market Lot: 1725
- Group: R
- Trading Period for Renunciation: January 02, 2026 to January 20, 2026
- Rights Issue Period: January 02, 2026 to January 23, 2026
- Settlement: T+1 Rolling Settlement, trade-for-trade basis
- Trading Form: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notice for rights entitlements trading as per existing SEBI framework.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs with the ISIN number (INE796W20027)
- Clarify that REs should not be mistaken for ordinary shares of the company
- Include a prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases stating:
  - Purchase of REs only gives the right to participate in the ongoing Rights Issue by making an application with requisite application money OR renounce the REs before the issue closes
  - REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced on or before the Issue Closing Date shall lapse and be extinguished
- Refer to notice no. 20200305-21 dated March 05, 2020 and notice no. 20200518-34 dated May 18, 2020 for further details

## Important Dates

- **January 02, 2026**: Trading in Rights Entitlements begins; Rights Issue opens
- **January 20, 2026**: Last day for trading/renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **January 23, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced will lapse

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific rights issue announcement with limited market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for Trading Members - Requires proper client communication and disclaimer inclusion in contract notes for RE purchases to ensure clients understand the nature and limitations of Rights Entitlements.

**Investor Impact:** Medium for YUG DECOR shareholders - Existing shareholders need to be aware of the trading window for REs and take action (subscribe, renounce, or trade) before the expiry dates to avoid lapsing of their entitlements.
