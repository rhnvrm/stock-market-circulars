## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new debt securities issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 17, 2025. The securities consist of 50,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, carrying an 8% annual interest rate and maturing on December 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Manappuram Finance Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977377
- **Scrip ID**: 8MPFL27
- **ISIN**: INE522D07CI5
- **Quantity**: 50,000 debentures
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Market Lot**: 1 debenture
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL AA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 8% per annum
- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D07CI5
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on December 15 (2026 and 2027)
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities. The listing provides:

- Additional debt investment options for institutional and qualified investors
- Enhanced liquidity for Manappuram Finance's debt instruments on the BSE platform
- No impact on equity markets or retail investors
- Relevant primarily for debt market participants, institutional investors, and existing Manappuram Finance stakeholders

The CRISIL AA/Stable rating indicates high credit quality with adequate degree of safety regarding timely servicing of financial obligations.
