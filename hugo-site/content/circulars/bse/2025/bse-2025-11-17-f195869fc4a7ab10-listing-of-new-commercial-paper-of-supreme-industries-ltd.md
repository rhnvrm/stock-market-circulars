## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Supreme Industries Ltd on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 900 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 45 crore. The securities carry a CRISIL A1+ credit rating and will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 900 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730580
- **Scrip ID**: SIL141125
- **ISIN**: INE195A14CE1
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Tenor**: 90 days (approximately)
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE195A14CE1
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded exclusively on the BSE Debt segment
- Market participants must adhere to the specified tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Investor Impact**: Low. The commercial paper is rated CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating), indicating strong credit quality. Available only to institutional and high net worth investors due to the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.

**Company Impact**: Provides Supreme Industries Ltd with short-term funding for approximately 90 days at a discounted rate (issue price Rs. 4,92,471.50 vs face value Rs. 5,00,000).
