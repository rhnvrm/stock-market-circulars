## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis. The issue consists of 500 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, totaling Rs. 25 crore. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from December 3, 2025, and will mature on June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Quantity: 500 units
- Total issue size: Rs. 25 crore
- Scrip Code: 730686
- Scrip ID: AEL21225
- ISIN: INE423A14YQ5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,301.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 3, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: December 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Adani Enterprises Ltd. for approximately 6 months. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading will be restricted to dematerialized form in standard lots of Rs. 5 lakhs, making it accessible primarily to institutional investors.
