## Summary

BSE has released the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME Segment for December 2025. The schedule covers 22 trading sessions from December 1, 2025, to December 31, 2025, following a T+1 settlement cycle. Pay-in is scheduled at 10:00 AM with instructions due by 9:50 AM, and pay-out occurs by 11:00 AM on settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Total of 22 trading sessions scheduled for December 2025
- Settlement numbers range from ITP-167/25-26 to ITP-188/25-26
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2526167 to 2526188
- T+1 settlement cycle maintained throughout the month
- No trading on weekends (Saturdays and Sundays)
- December 25, 2025 (Christmas) appears to be a trading holiday (gap in schedule)

## Trading Schedule Details

### Settlement Timings
- **Pay-in Time**: 10:00 AM
- **Pay-in Instruction Deadline**: 9:50 AM (members must submit instructions to Depositories/banks)
- **Pay-out Time**: By 11:00 AM (for both funds and securities)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard monthly settlement schedule notification.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members
- Submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by 9:50 AM on settlement day
- Ensure timely submission of funds and securities for pay-in at 10:00 AM
- Be prepared to receive pay-out of funds and securities by 11:00 AM

### Operational Requirements
- Follow T+1 settlement cycle for all ITP-SME trades
- Coordinate with depositories and banks for timely settlement
- Adhere to specified timelines for pay-in and pay-out processes
