## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (total 2,500 units aggregating Rs. 125 crore) on BSE Debt segment effective November 21, 2025. The commercial paper is issued on private placement basis with credit ratings of CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, carrying a 90-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Total issuance: 2,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (Rs. 125 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730605, Scrip ID: OWL191125
- ISIN: INE466L14FF3 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,373.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE debt segment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 18, 2026
- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard commercial paper listing in the debt segment with limited market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members dealing in debt instruments can now trade this commercial paper under the specified ISIN. The instrument provides a short-term investment option (90 days) for institutional and qualified investors.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt market investors seeking short-term highly-rated instruments. The dual A1+ ratings from CARE and ICRA indicate highest credit quality for short-term obligations.
