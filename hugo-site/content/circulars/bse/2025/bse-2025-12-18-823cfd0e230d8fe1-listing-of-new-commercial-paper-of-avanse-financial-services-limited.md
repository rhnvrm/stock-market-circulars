## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Avanse Financial Services Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment effective December 18, 2025. The instrument consists of 1500 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,85,658, carrying CRISIL A1+ rating. The securities are allotted on December 17, 2025 and will mature on June 12, 2026. Axis Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1500 Commercial Papers
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,85,658 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730805
- Scrip ID: AFSL171225
- ISIN: INE087P14770
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE087P14770
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 18, 2025
- Allotment Date: December 17, 2025
- Listing Date: December 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment with no implications for equity markets or broader trading operations. The listing provides short-term funding instrument for Avanse Financial Services Limited and adds to available debt instruments for institutional investors in the BSE Debt segment.
