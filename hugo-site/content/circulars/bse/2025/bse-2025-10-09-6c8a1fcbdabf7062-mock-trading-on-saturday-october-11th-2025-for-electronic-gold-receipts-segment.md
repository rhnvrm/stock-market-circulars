## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, October 11th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems to test their trading applications under various market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halts. The mock trading will not create any actual margin, pay-in, pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 11th, 2025
- Applicable to Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment only
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Trading members can test their applications including third-party platforms and in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing will cover various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts
- Trades from mock session will not attract any obligations or create rights/liabilities
- Members requested to provide feedback by 2:00 pm on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine system testing exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participation is voluntary but members are encouraged to participate actively
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 2:00 pm on October 11th, 2025
- Feedback can be submitted via BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

**October 11th, 2025 - Mock Trading Schedule:**
- **08:15 am - 09:00 am:** Log-in period
- **09:00 am - 09:08 am:** Pre-Open Order Entry period
- **09:08 am - 09:15 am:** Matching period
- **09:15 am - 12:00 pm:** Continuous Trading
- **11:00 am - 11:15 am:** Block Deal Window
- **12:00 pm - 12:10 pm:** Closing period
- **12:10 pm - 12:20 pm:** Post-closing period
- **By 2:00 pm:** Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions or obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Low. This is an optional testing opportunity for trading members to verify their system functionality and familiarize themselves with trading operations in the EGR segment. Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can use this opportunity to ensure their applications are functioning correctly under various market scenarios. No actual trades, margins, or settlement obligations will result from this session.
