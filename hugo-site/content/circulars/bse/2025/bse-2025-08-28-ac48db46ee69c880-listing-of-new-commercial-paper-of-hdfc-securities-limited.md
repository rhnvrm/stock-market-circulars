## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. Two tranches totaling Rs. 365 crores have been admitted for trading in the debt segment with HDFC Bank Ltd as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- First tranche: 4300 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each (ISIN: INE700G14PM0, Scrip Code: 730095)
- Second tranche: 3000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each (ISIN: INE700G14PN8, Scrip Code: 730110)
- Both tranches rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must follow standard denomination requirements
- Market lot size is 1 for both securities

## Important Dates

- First tranche allotment date: August 26, 2025
- First tranche redemption date: November 25, 2025
- Second tranche allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Second tranche redemption date: November 27, 2025
- Effective trading date: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors with 3-month tenure and competitive pricing.
