## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of a company. A new Certificate of Incorporation has been issued subsequent to the name change.

## Key Points

- Company has undergone a name change
- New Certificate of Incorporation issued
- Annexure I contains the new Certificate of Incorporation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a notification of corporate name change as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the new company name for all future correspondence and transactions
- Updated records should reflect the new company name

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative notification with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the company's securities, trading symbol, or ISIN. Investors and market participants should update their records accordingly.
