## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 500 crores issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment from August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (10,000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,232 per unit
- Total issue size: Rs. 500 crores
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730049
- ISIN: INE700G14PL2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption date: November 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors.
