## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of NANTA TECH LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544668 and symbol NANTA with ISIN number INE0YJA01011. The specific date of listing and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: NANTA TECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544668
- Trading Symbol: NANTA
- ISIN: INE0YJA01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be informed separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code and symbol for NANTA TECH LIMITED for future trading operations once the listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for an SME segment company. The impact is limited to the addition of one new scrip to the SME platform. Trading members need to update their systems with the scrip details once the actual listing date is announced. No immediate market-wide impact expected.
