## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6795) to "SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, December 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from SADRAVYA MONEY MANAGEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED to SADRAVYA MONEY PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number: 6795
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- No impact on trading operations or member services
- Contact point for queries: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the revised legal name of the trading member entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only. The affected trading member must update their records and documentation with the new name.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 18, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with no operational, trading, or market impact. Other trading members and market participants should note the updated name for correspondence and record-keeping purposes. The member number remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in all systems and processes.
