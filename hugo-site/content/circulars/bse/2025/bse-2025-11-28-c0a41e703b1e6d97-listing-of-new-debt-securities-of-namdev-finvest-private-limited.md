## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new debt securities issued by NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED on private placement basis, effective November 28, 2025. The company has issued 3,500 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an 11.30% annual interest rate and maturing on September 30, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 3,500 debentures
- **Scrip Code**: 977177
- **Scrip ID**: 1130NFPL28
- **ISIN**: INE0IX207254 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,01,795.62
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL BBB+/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 11.30% per annum (paid quarterly)
- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0IX207254
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 30, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from December 30, 2025 to September 30, 2028
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: September 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing for a private limited company on private placement basis. The securities will trade only in the debt segment and have no impact on equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional/high net worth investors participating in private placement. The CRISIL BBB+/Stable rating indicates adequate credit quality with moderate credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Only debt segment trading members need to be aware of the new listing for trading purposes.
