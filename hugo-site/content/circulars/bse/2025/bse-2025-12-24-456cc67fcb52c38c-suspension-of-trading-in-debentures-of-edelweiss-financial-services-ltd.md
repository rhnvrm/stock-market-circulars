## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. effective December 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures with ISIN INE532F07BT8 (Code: 960419). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd.
- Debenture Code: EFSL-GSEC-13-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE532F07BT8
- BSE Code: 960419
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Record Date: December 29, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 29, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-786/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from December 29, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate appropriately with clients holding these debentures
- Ensure all dealing systems reflect the trading suspension for ISIN INE532F07BT8

## Important Dates

- **December 24, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 29, 2025**: Record date for redemption; trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited - affects only holders of this specific debenture series. No impact on equity trading or other debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders - investors holding these debentures will have their instruments redeemed as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Low - routine corporate action that trading members regularly handle. Standard suspension protocol applies.
