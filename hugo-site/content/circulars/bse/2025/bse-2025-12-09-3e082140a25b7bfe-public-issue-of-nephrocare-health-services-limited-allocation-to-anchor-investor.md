## Summary

Nephrocare Health Services Limited announced the allocation of 5,657,919 equity shares to anchor investors at ₹460 per equity share (face value ₹2, share premium ₹458) on December 9, 2025. The anchor investor portion raised ₹2,602.64 crore from 14 institutional investors. The allocation was finalized by the board of directors in consultation with book running lead managers ICICI Securities Limited, Ambit Private Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, and Nomura Financial Advisory and Securities (India) Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 5,657,919 equity shares at ₹460 per share
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: ₹2,602,642,740 (₹2,602.64 crore)
- Face value: ₹2 per equity share; Share premium: ₹458 per equity share
- 14 anchor investors participated in the allocation
- Domestic mutual funds received 2,582,944 shares (45.65% of anchor portion) through 6 schemes from 5 mutual funds
- Life insurance companies and pension funds received 434,815 shares (7.69% of anchor portion) from 2 entities
- Largest allocation: SBI Healthcare Opportunities Fund (760,896 shares, 13.45%)
- Book running lead managers: ICICI Securities, Ambit Private, IIFL Capital Services, Nomura Financial Advisory

## Anchor Investor Allocation Details

**Major Allocations:**
- SBI Healthcare Opportunities Fund: 760,896 shares (13.45%) - ₹350.01 crore
- DSP Healthcare Fund: 619,584 shares (10.95%) - ₹285.01 crore
- ICICI Prudential Pharma Healthcare and Diagnostics Fund: 543,488 shares (9.61%) - ₹250.00 crore
- Fidelity Funds - Pacific Fund: 492,288 shares (8.70%) - ₹226.45 crore
- Multiple international funds: The Prudential Assurance Company Limited, Eastspring Investments, Polar Capital Funds, Manulife Global Fund (543,488 shares each, 9.61% each)

**Domestic Mutual Fund Participation:**
- 5 domestic mutual funds applied through 6 schemes
- Total allocation to mutual funds: 2,582,944 shares (45.65%)
- Amount allocated to mutual funds: ₹1,188.15 crore

**Insurance and Pension Funds:**
- 2 life insurance companies and pension funds allocated 434,815 shares (7.69%)
- Includes SBI Life Insurance Company Limited and HDFC Life Insurance Company Limited
