## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED. The company's name has been officially changed to DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED with effect from September 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L27320GJ2022PLC135697
- Old Name: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED
- New Name: DOLPHIN KITCHEN UTENSILS AND APPLIANCES LIMITED
- Original Name at Incorporation: SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate Date: September 29, 2025
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Registered Address: 5, Harekrishana Industrial Estate, Bakrol, Daskroi, Ahmedabad-382430, Gujarat, India
- Approved by: Sweety Kumar, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a name change certificate issued pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SAI SWAMI METALS AND ALLOYS LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: September 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: September 29, 2025 (digitally signed on October 8, 2025 at 15:12:01 IST)
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from effective date (until September 29, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or stakeholder rights and liabilities. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for identification purposes. The company will operate under both names during the two-year transition period as mandated by regulatory requirements.
