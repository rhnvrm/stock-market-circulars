## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) will operate from its Disaster Recovery (DR) Site from Friday, December 26, 2025, to Tuesday, December 30, 2025. This applies to all segments including Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR Segment. Members and custodians will experience no changes in accessing ICCL applications or URLs during this period.

## Key Points

- ICCL operations shifting to DR site for planned period
- All segments covered: Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR
- No changes to member or custodian access procedures
- ICCL applications and URLs remain unchanged
- Related to Exchange Circular No. 20251226-1 dated December 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational shift to the Disaster Recovery site.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for members and custodians. Normal operations continue with existing access procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Start Date**: Friday, December 26, 2025
- **End Date**: Tuesday, December 30, 2025
- **Duration**: 5 days of DR site operations

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a planned operational shift to the DR site with no expected disruption to clearing and settlement activities.

**Member Impact**: No impact on members or custodians as all ICCL applications and URLs remain accessible without any changes to connection procedures.

**Market Impact**: No market impact expected as this is a backend operational change that maintains business continuity across all trading segments.
