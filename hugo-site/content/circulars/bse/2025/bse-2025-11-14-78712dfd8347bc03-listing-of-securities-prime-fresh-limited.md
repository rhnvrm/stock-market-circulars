## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 12,805 equity shares of Prime Fresh Limited (Scrip Code: 540404) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading from Monday, November 17, 2025, with a lock-in period extending until May 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 12,805 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 154/-
- Issue price: Rs. 164/- per share
- Allotment type: Preferential basis to promoters
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE442V01012
- Distinguished numbers: 13845768 to 13858572
- Date of allotment: September 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 12,805 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until May 31, 2027
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold during the lock-in period as per SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The allotment of 12,805 shares represents a small addition to the existing equity base and will not significantly impact trading volumes or stock liquidity.

**Dilution Impact**: Low dilution effect as the allotment is relatively small in size.

**Investor Impact**: No immediate impact on existing shareholders. The preferential allotment to promoters may be viewed positively as it demonstrates promoter commitment, with shares locked in for approximately 18 months from the listing date.
