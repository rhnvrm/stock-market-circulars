## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and commencement of trading of 60,752 new equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) effective from November 28, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 417 per share. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 60,752 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 415/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 170044486 to 170105237
- Date of Allotment: October 18, 2025
- Issue Price: Rs. 417 per share
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Scrip Code: 500463

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. The securities are subject to mandatory lock-in requirements as specified.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 28, 2025 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 10, 2026 (all 60,752 shares under lock-in until this date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment represents a small issuance of 60,752 shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares are subject to lock-in until June 2026, limiting immediate trading impact. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new securities for the company.
