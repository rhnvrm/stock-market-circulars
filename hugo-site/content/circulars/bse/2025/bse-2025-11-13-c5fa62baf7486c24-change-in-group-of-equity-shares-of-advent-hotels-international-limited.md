## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Advent Hotels International Limited (Scrip Code: 544446) will be permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Thursday, November 27, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Advent Hotels International Limited
- Scrip Code: 544446
- New Group Classification: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Thursday, November 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251113-1
- Notice Date: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' instead of their current group classification. Group 'B' on BSE typically includes companies that have fulfilled certain listing requirements but may have different trading or settlement characteristics compared to Group 'A' stocks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for Advent Hotels International Limited
- All trading in these equity shares must be conducted according to Group 'B' regulations and parameters from the effective date
- Members should update their systems and records to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group 'B' may affect:
- Trading parameters and margins for the stock
- Settlement processes
- Risk management requirements for brokers and traders
- Investor perception and liquidity patterns

Trading members and investors should familiarize themselves with Group 'B' trading norms before the effective date to ensure smooth transition and compliance.
