## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Wakefit Innovations Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544642, trading symbol WAKEFIT, and ISIN number INE0E7301029. The exact date of listing and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Wakefit Innovations Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544642
- Trading Symbol: WAKEFIT
- ISIN Number: INE0E7301029
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing for their records and operations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 12, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement that provides trading members with advance notice of Wakefit Innovations Limited's upcoming debut on BSE. The impact is primarily informational for market participants. Investors and traders interested in this new listing should await the subsequent notice containing the exact listing date and complete security details before the stock becomes available for trading.
