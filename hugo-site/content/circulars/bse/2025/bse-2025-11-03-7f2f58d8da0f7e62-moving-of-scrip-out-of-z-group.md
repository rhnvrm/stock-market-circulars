## Summary

BSE has announced that GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) will be moved out of the 'Z' Group and transferred to the 'X' Group effective November 14, 2025. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024, specifically under Chapter-VII(A) relating to Penal Actions for Non-Compliance.

## Key Points

- GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) will be transferred from Z Group to X Group
- The transfer is based on SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024
- Movement relates to Chapter-VII(A) covering Penal Actions for Non-Compliance
- Market participants can contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com for queries

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements provisions under SEBI's Master Circular regarding penal actions for non-compliance. The movement from Z Group to X Group typically indicates that the company has addressed previous compliance issues that led to its placement in the more restrictive Z Group.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. This is an informational notice regarding the group classification change for GV Films Ltd.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 3, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 14, 2025 - GV Films Ltd will be moved from Z Group to X Group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The transfer from Z Group to X Group is generally positive for the affected security. The Z Group typically contains securities with compliance issues or those under surveillance, while X Group is for regular listed companies. This movement indicates improved compliance status.

**Trading Impact:** The reclassification may improve trading conditions for GV Films Ltd, as Z Group securities often face trading restrictions and investor caution. Movement to X Group should normalize trading activity.

**Investor Impact:** Limited impact on broader market participants. For investors in GV Films Ltd, this represents a positive development indicating resolution of previous compliance concerns.
