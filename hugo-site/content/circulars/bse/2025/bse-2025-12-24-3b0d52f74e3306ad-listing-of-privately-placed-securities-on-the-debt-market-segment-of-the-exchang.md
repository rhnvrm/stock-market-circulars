## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities issued by Greaves Finance Limited on its Debt Segment effective December 24, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, bearing interest at 10.50% p.a. with monthly payment frequency.

## Key Points

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE1QWF07048
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Market lot is 1 unit
- Total quantity listed: 2,500 securities
- Scrip Code: 977394, Scrip ID: 1050GFL27A
- Credit Rating: IND A-/Stable
- Issue price equals face value at Rs. 1,00,000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note that securities trade only in demat form
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from December 23, 2025 to December 23, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: December 23, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities affecting only institutional investors who participated in the private placement.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard debt trading procedures apply with no special requirements beyond dematerialized trading.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors holding these specific securities. Put/call options are as per the disclosure document.
