## Summary

State Bank of India has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective October 23, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis, comprising 7,500 bonds with a face value of Rs. 1 crore each, carrying an interest rate of 6.93% per annum. The bonds mature on October 20, 2035, with a call option available from October 20, 2030. The securities are rated CARE AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: 7,500 bonds of Rs. 1 crore each (Rs. 75,000 crore)
- Scrip Code: 977234, Scrip ID: 693SBI35
- ISIN: INE062A08488
- Interest rate: 6.93% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of allotment: October 20, 2025
- Maturity date: October 20, 2035 (10-year tenure)
- Call option available from: October 20, 2030
- Credit ratings: CARE AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable
- Market lot: 1 bond
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE062A08488
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date**: October 20, 2025
- **Listing date**: October 23, 2025
- **Interest payment dates**: Annually from October 20, 2026 to October 20, 2035
- **Call option date**: October 20, 2030
- **Maturity/Redemption date**: October 20, 2035

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine debt security listing that provides additional fixed-income investment opportunities. The securities are privately placed and will trade on the debt segment.

**Issuer Profile**: State Bank of India, India's largest public sector bank, continues to access debt markets for funding. The AAA ratings reflect the bank's strong creditworthiness.

**Investor Impact**: Suitable for institutional and high-net-worth investors seeking long-term fixed-income instruments with sovereign-backed credit quality. The 6.93% coupon offers predictable returns with a call option providing issuer flexibility after 5 years.
