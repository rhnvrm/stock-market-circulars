## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Mother Nutri Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544623, symbol MNFL, and ISIN number INE0UH001016. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Mother Nutri Foods Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544623
- Trading Symbol: MNFL
- ISIN: INE0UH001016
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the security details for Mother Nutri Foods Limited. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the listing date announcement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact.
