## Summary

BSE has announced that MAHINDRA MANULIFE INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF (Fund of Funds) will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 08, 2025. The scheme will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This notice supersedes the earlier communication dated November 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20251120-10).

## Key Points

- MAHINDRA MANULIFE INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF reopening for transactions from December 08, 2025
- Available for both ongoing subscriptions and redemptions
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Scheme category: DEBT (Fund of Funds)
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- References previous notice dated November 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20251120-10)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members and investors should note the reopening date for transaction planning.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 05, 2025
- **Reopening Date**: December 08, 2025 (for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. It affects only investors who wish to invest in or redeem units from the MAHINDRA MANULIFE INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF scheme. The reopening provides liquidity and investment opportunity for interested mutual fund investors on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Scheme Details

The following six ISIN variants are available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth**: INF174V01CR0
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment**: INF174V01CT6
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF174V01CS8
4. **Direct Plan - Growth**: INF174V01CU4
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment**: INF174V01CW0
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout**: INF174V01CV2
