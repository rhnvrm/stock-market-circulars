## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities, totaling 6,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (aggregate Rs. 300 crores), are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Quantity: 6,000 units (Rs. 300 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730588
- Scrip ID: BFSL171125
- ISIN: INE01C314DJ0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,845.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DJ0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: November 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing in the debt segment for short-term funding purposes. The instruments have a tenure of 91 days and are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and India Ratings, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment. Only relevant for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers.
