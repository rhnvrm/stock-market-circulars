## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of AEQUS LIMITED. The anchor investor portion was allocated on December 2, 2025, one day prior to the issue opening date. This allocation provides details of institutional investors who have committed to invest in the company at the anchor investor price.

## Key Points

- Company: AEQUS LIMITED
- Allocation Date: December 2, 2025
- Allocation Type: Anchor Investor Portion
- The anchor investor allocation was conducted as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Allocation was made to qualified institutional buyers (QIBs) prior to issue opening
- Details include investor names, number of shares allocated, and allocation amounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process disclosure as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must complete the anchor investor allocation one day before the issue opens
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment
- The anchor investor price is disclosed and used as a reference for the issue price band
- Public issue will open for subscription after anchor allocation

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: December 2, 2025
- Issue Opening Date: December 3, 2025 (expected)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Anchor investor participation provides an indication of institutional investor interest and helps in price discovery for the public issue. Strong anchor allocation typically signals positive sentiment for the IPO.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Retail and other investors can assess the quality and quantity of anchor investor participation to inform their investment decisions. The anchor investor price serves as a reference point for the issue pricing.

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a standard IPO process step with no operational changes required for market participants.
