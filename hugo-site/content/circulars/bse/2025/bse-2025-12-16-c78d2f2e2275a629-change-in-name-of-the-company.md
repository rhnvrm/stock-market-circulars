## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to the change of name for CIN L36100MP1992PLC007102. The company name has been officially changed from **Sylph Technologies Limited** to **Sylph Industries Limited** with effect from October 24, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Majjarrsha Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: Sylph Technologies Limited
- **New Name**: Sylph Industries Limited
- **CIN**: L36100MP1992PLC007102
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Original Name**: Majjarrsha Housing Finance Limited (at incorporation)
- **Registered Office**: Office No. 517, Sky Corporate Park, Scheme No. 78, A.B. Road, Vijay Nagar, Indore, Indore-452010, Madhya Pradesh, India
- **Certificate Issued By**: Prerna Panwar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change approved by the Registrar of Companies under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the **old name for a period of two years** along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All company documents, letterheads, and official communications must be updated to reflect the new name

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 24, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all statutory documents, letterheads, signboards, and official communications. For two years, both old and new names must be displayed wherever required by law.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights or liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of corporate identity for regulatory and compliance purposes.
