## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Balkrishna Industries Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment effective December 4, 2025. The issuance comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 100 crore) with a maturity period of 88 days, maturing on March 2, 2026. The instruments carry a CRISIL A1+ credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,811.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730703, Scrip ID: BIL041225
- ISIN: INE787D14219
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Dematerialized trading only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE787D14219
- Trading must occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE Debt Department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 4, 2025 (effective for trading)
- **Redemption Date**: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard commercial paper listing that provides Balkrishna Industries Limited with short-term funding through the debt market. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality and strong capacity for timely payment. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and investors seeking short-term corporate debt instruments. No effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
