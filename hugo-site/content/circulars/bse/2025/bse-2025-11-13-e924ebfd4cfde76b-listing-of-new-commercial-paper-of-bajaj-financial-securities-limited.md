## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument totaling Rs. 1,250 crores (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) is admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025. The Commercial Paper has a tenure of 91 days with maturity on February 11, 2026, and carries dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 1,250 crores (2,500 units)
- Face value per unit: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,845.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730551
- Scrip ID: BFSL121125
- ISIN: INE01C314DH4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314DH4
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise for price quotes

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: November 12, 2025
- Listing Date: November 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents standard corporate funding activity through short-term commercial paper. The instrument provides Bajaj Financial Securities Limited with working capital for approximately three months. For trading members, this adds another debt instrument to the BSE platform but requires no operational changes beyond standard debt trading procedures. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
