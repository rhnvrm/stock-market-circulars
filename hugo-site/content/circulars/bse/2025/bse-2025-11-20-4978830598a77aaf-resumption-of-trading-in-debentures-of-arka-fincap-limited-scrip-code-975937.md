## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED (Scrip Code 975937) effective November 21, 2025. The debenture holders did not exercise the Put Option that was available to them with a record date of November 4, 2025, and have requested to resume trading in these securities.

## Key Points

- Trading was previously suspended for the Put Option exercise period
- Record Date for Put Option redemption was set as November 4, 2025
- Debenture holders chose not to exercise the Put Option
- Trading will resume on November 21, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-761/2025-2026
- Previous notice reference: 20251030-60 dated October 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice regarding trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note that:
- Debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED with Scrip Code 975937 will be available for trading from November 21, 2025
- Normal trading operations should resume for this security

## Important Dates

- **October 30, 2025**: Original notice issued regarding Put Option
- **November 4, 2025**: Record Date for Put Option redemption
- **November 20, 2025**: Circular date announcing trading resumption
- **November 21, 2025**: Trading resumes in the debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only holders and potential traders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures with Scrip Code 975937.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to ensure their systems reflect the availability of these debentures for trading from November 21, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders who chose not to exercise the Put Option can now trade their holdings in the secondary market. This provides liquidity to existing holders who may wish to exit their positions.
