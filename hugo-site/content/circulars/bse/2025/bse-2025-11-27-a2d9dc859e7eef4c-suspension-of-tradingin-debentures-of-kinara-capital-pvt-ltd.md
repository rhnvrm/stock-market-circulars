## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in three debentures of KINARA CAPITAL PVT LTD effective November 28, 2025, following the company's notification that these debentures have been fully redeemed. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Three debentures of KINARA CAPITAL PVT LTD have been fully redeemed
- Trading suspension effective from November 28, 2025
- Reference: DR-766/2025-2026
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Compliance and Operations

## Affected Securities

**1. KCPL-12%-17-9-29-PVT**
- ISIN: INE200W07449
- Scrip Code: 976014
- Status: Fully redeemed

**2. KCPL-13.10%-7-8-27-PVT**
- ISIN: INE200W07423
- Scrip Code: 975887
- Status: Fully redeemed

**3. KCPL-27-8-27-PVT**
- ISIN: INE200W07431
- Scrip Code: 975954
- Status: Fully redeemed

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from November 28, 2025
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action following the successful redemption of privately placed debentures. The impact is minimal as these are private placements with limited market participation. Debenture holders have been fully redeemed, and the suspension is a procedural requirement to prevent inadvertent trading in redeemed securities.
