## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Dachepalli Publishers Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544667 and trading symbol DACHEPALLI with ISIN INE1F9M01018. This listing is on the SME segment of BSE.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Dachepalli Publishers Limited
- Scrip Code: 544667
- Trading Symbol: DACHEPALLI
- ISIN: INE1F9M01018
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details to be announced via separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required. Members should await separate notice for listing date and security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. Impact is limited to trading members who will have a new security available for trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate market-wide implications. Investors interested in the SME segment should watch for the subsequent notice with listing date and security details.
