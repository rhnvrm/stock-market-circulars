## Summary

BSE has listed 84,80,735 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Nimbus Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 511714) effective December 31, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement for Amalgamation involving eight transferor companies merging into Nimbus Projects Limited. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 84,80,735 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed
- Scrip Code: 511714, ISIN: INE875B01015
- Trading permitted from December 31, 2025
- Shares issued under amalgamation scheme involving nine companies total
- Distinctive Numbers: 10838001 to 19318735
- Allotment Date: May 16, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should note the listing of additional equity shares of Nimbus Projects Limited for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 31, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The listing follows a scheme of arrangement involving the amalgamation of eight transferor companies (Gupta Fincaps Private Limited, Urvashi Finvest Private Limited, Intellectual Securities Private Limited, Happy Graphics and Exhibition Private Limited, Link Vanijya Private Limited, Dynamo Infracon Private Limited, Pushpak Trading & Consultancy Private Limited, Mokha Vyapaar Private Limited, and Padma Estates Private Limited) into Nimbus Projects Limited. The additional shares will increase the company's equity capital base but represent a single-company corporate restructuring with no broader market implications.
