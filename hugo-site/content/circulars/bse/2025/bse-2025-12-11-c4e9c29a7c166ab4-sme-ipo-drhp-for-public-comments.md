## Summary

BSE has announced that the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) for RCRS Innovation Limited's SME IPO, filed on December 10, 2025, is now available for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by RCRS Innovation Limited on December 10, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from December 11, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document is made available as per ICDR Regulations requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **December 11, 2025**: Notice date and start of comment period
- **January 1, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only RCRS Innovation Limited and potential investors in the SME IPO.

**Operational Impact**: This is an informational notice that facilitates transparency in the IPO process by allowing public scrutiny of the draft offer document before the final IPO launch. Standard procedure for SME listings on BSE.
