## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited. The debentures with ISIN INE975F08CR9 (KMIL-9%-31-12-25-PVT, Scrip Code: 953176) will not be available for trading from December 16, 2025, as the company has fixed this date as the record date for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Debenture Series: KMIL-9%-31-12-25-PVT (Private Placement)
- ISIN: INE975F08CR9
- BSE Scrip Code: 953176
- Record Date: December 16, 2025
- No Dealings From: December 16, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-778/2025-2026
- Issuer: Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard market operation procedure when debentures approach redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE975F08CR9) from December 16, 2025
- Members are advised to update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended instrument
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for this specific debenture series from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Record Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** December 16, 2025
- **Maturity Date:** December 31, 2025 (as indicated by instrument name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This affects only a single series of privately placed debentures with a maturity coupon rate of 9%. The suspension is temporary and directly related to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. The trading suspension prevents new positions from being created close to maturity.

**Operational Impact:** Routine administrative action with no broader market implications. Trading members should ensure compliance systems are updated to block trades in this specific ISIN from December 16, 2025.
