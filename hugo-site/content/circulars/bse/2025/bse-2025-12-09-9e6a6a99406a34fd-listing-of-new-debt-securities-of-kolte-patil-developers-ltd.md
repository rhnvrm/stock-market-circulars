## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd on private placement basis, effective December 9, 2025. The zero-coupon bonds have a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each with 10,994 units issued, totaling Rs. 109.94 crore. The securities carry a CARE AA- credit rating and mature on October 30, 2035.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd
- **Quantity**: 10,994 units
- **Scrip Code**: 977351
- **Scrip ID**: 0KPDL35
- **ISIN**: INE094I07098
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Credit Rating**: CARE AA-
- **Interest Rate**: 0% p.a. (zero-coupon bond)
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE094I07098
- Members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Members can refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: October 30, 2035
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Not applicable (zero-coupon bond)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with no immediate impact on equity markets or retail investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt security listing procedures apply. Trading members have access to placement memorandum and BSE support for queries.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors who participated in the private placement. The securities are zero-coupon bonds with a 10-year maturity period and carry a CARE AA- rating, indicating adequate safety with moderate credit risk.
