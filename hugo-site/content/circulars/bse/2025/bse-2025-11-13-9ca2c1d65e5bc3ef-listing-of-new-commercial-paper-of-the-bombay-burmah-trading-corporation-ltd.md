## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper worth Rs. 250 crore issued by The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. on private placement basis. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective November 13, 2025, with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, issue price of Rs. 4,61,894, and redemption date of November 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd.
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crore (500 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,61,894 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730548
- **Scrip ID**: TBBT121125
- **ISIN**: INE050A14887
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 12, 2026
- **Tenure**: 1 year
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE050A14887
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading effective from November 13, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term financing to The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd. and offers institutional investors an IND A1+ rated debt instrument with approximately 1-year tenure. The listing expands debt market offerings but has no impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
