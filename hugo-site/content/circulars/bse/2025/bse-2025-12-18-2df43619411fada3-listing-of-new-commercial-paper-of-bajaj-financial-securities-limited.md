## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper instruments issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective December 18, 2025. The instruments have face values of Rs. 5,00,000 each with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent for both instruments.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper instruments listed on BSE Debt segment
- First instrument (ISIN: INE01C314DZ6): 15,000 units with redemption on March 18, 2026
- Second instrument (ISIN: INE01C314DX1): 1,000 units with redemption on January 15, 2026
- Both instruments rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent
