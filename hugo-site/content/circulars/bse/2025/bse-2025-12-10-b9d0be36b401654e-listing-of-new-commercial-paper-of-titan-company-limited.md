## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Titan Company Limited on a private placement basis. The listing comprises 30,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 1,500 crores), effective December 10, 2025. The commercial paper carries a CARE A1+ credit rating and will mature on March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 30,000 units of commercial paper
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,650.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 1,500 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730728
- **Scrip ID**: TCL91225
- **ISIN**: INE280A14500
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE280A14500
- Trading permitted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 9, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing representing short-term borrowing by Titan Company Limited to meet working capital requirements.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a short-term debt instrument from a highly rated issuer (CARE A1+). The 90-day maturity offers liquidity with minimal interest rate risk.

**Operational Impact**: Standard operational procedures apply for BSE debt segment trading members. No special arrangements required.
