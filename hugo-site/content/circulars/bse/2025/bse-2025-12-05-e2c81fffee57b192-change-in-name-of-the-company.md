## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of a listed company. The circular provides updated corporate identification details including the new company name while maintaining the existing scrip code and ISIN for continuity in trading and settlement.

## Key Points

- Company name has been officially changed as per regulatory approvals
- Scrip code remains unchanged for trading continuity
- ISIN number remains the same
- All other corporate details remain unaffected
- Change is effective from the date specified in the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a notification of corporate name change pursuant to applicable provisions of the Companies Act and SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to update their systems with the new company name
- All future correspondence and filings to reference the new company name
- Trading members to inform their clients about the name change
- No action required from investors as scrip code and ISIN remain unchanged

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 5, 2025
- Effective Date: As specified in the company's resolution and regulatory approvals

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal to none. Name changes do not affect trading operations as the scrip code and ISIN remain unchanged.

**Operational Impact:** Low. System updates required by market participants to reflect the new company name in their databases and trading platforms.

**Investor Impact:** No direct impact on shareholders. All shareholdings, rights, and entitlements remain unchanged. The name change is purely administrative and does not affect the company's business operations or financial position.
