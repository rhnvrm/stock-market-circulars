## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of NHPC LIMITED with ISIN INE848E07AT3 (Code: 959190) effective December 18, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: NHPC-7.38%-03-01-26-PVT (ISIN: INE848E07AT3, Code: 959190)
- Record date set: December 18, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: December 18, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-780/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for bond redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified NHPC bonds from December 18, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and adjust their trading activities accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the affected bond series from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **December 18, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **December 18, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of NHPC LIMITED bonds (7.38% coupon, maturity 03-01-26). Bondholders registered on the record date will receive the scheduled interest payment and bond redemption proceeds. The trading halt is temporary and specific to this bond series undergoing maturity/redemption. Market impact is limited to investors holding or planning to trade this specific debt instrument. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications.
