## Summary

BSE has admitted Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities) as a new trading member on December 29, 2025. The member has been granted trading rights only on BSE Ltd. in the Debt segment under member number 6974.

## Key Points

- New member admitted: Sunil Rajkumar Khandelwal (B M Securities)
- Member number: 6974
- Segment: Debt
- Type of membership: New Membership (trading rights only)
- Admission date: December 29, 2025
- Notice number: 20251231-2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

The newly admitted member must comply with the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of BSE Ltd. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Admission date of new trading member
- **December 31, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The admission of a single new trading member in the Debt segment does not affect existing trading operations or market structure. This is informational for existing trading members to be aware of new participants in the exchange.
