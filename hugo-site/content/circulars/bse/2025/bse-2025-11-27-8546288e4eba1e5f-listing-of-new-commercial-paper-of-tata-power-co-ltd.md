## Summary

BSE has listed 10,000 Commercial Papers of Tata Power Co. Ltd on its Debt segment effective November 27, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total issue size Rs. 500 crores), carrying credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Issued**: 10,000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,534 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 500 crores
- **Scrip Code**: 730667
- **Scrip ID**: TPCL271125
- **ISIN**: INE245A14KO6
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE245A14KO6
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing that provides Tata Power with short-term financing and offers debt market participants an additional A1+ rated instrument with 91-day maturity.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for institutional investors and debt market participants seeking highly-rated short-term debt instruments. The A1+ rating indicates highest safety for short-term investments.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations; limited to BSE Debt segment participants.
