## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544594) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE on November 4, 2025, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on November 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED listed on BSE effective Tuesday, November 4, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective Thursday, November 6, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544594
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251103-44

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constitution update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and investors tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should note the addition.

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Listing date for GAME CHANGERS TEXFAB LIMITED on BSE
- **November 6, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine index addition following an SME listing. Impact is primarily limited to index tracking funds and investors specifically following the BSE SME IPO Index.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Index funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios to include the new constituent. The action has been included in the standard corporate action file for automated processing.
