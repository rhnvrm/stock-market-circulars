## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 500 crores issued by Tata Steel Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with 10,000 units listed, carrying ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,95,616 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Scrip code: 729973, Scrip ID: TSL60825
- ISIN: INE081A14GA2
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 6, 2025
- Listing date: August 7, 2025
- Redemption date: September 30, 2025
- Maturity period: Approximately 55 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a large corporate entity. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The short-term nature (55 days maturity) provides Tata Steel with working capital financing at competitive rates.
