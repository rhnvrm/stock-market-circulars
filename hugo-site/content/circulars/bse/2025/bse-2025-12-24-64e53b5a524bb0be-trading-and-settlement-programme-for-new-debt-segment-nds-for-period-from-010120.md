## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for January 2026. The schedule covers 21 settlement cycles (NDS-184/25-26 through NDS-204/25-26) starting from January 1, 2026 and ending January 30, 2026, with the final pay-out on February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- 21 settlement cycles scheduled for January 2026 (NDS-184 to NDS-204)
- Trading occurs on T day with same-day 6A/7A entry and confirmation
- Regular pay-in/pay-out on T+1 at 11:00 a.m.
- Auction pay-in/pay-out on T+2 at 9:30 a.m.
- Members must submit pay-in instructions 10 minutes before settlement time
- Settlement cycles follow T+1 pattern for regular settlements and T+2 for auctions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by 10:50 a.m. for regular pay-in (11:00 a.m. settlement)
- Members must submit auction pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by 9:20 a.m. for auction pay-in (9:30 a.m. settlement)
- All participants in NDS segment must follow the prescribed settlement schedule

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: First trading day and settlement cycle begins (NDS-184/25-26)
- **January 30, 2026**: Last trading day for the month (NDS-204/25-26)
- **February 2-3, 2026**: Final pay-in and pay-out dates for January settlements
- **Settlement holidays**: January 26, 2026 (Republic Day) - no settlement scheduled

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Low - This is a standard monthly schedule that enables NDS market participants to plan their trading and settlement activities. The schedule maintains the consistent T+1 and T+2 settlement patterns.

**Market Impact**: Minimal - Routine operational circular with no effect on market structure, trading rules, or participant obligations beyond normal settlement procedures.

**Participant Action Required**: NDS segment members should update their internal calendars and settlement systems with the provided schedule to ensure timely compliance with pay-in deadlines.
