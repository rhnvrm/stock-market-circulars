## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 795,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading from Monday, November 17, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until May 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 795,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 305/-
- Issue price: Rs. 315.00 per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 21740001 to 22535000
- ISIN: INE0O8901016
- Date of allotment: August 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- Lock-in period must be observed until May 31, 2026 for all 795,000 shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: November 17, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in End Date**: May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with minimal market-wide impact. The preferential allotment represents warrant conversion, adding 795,000 shares to the existing float of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited. The 18-month lock-in period ensures these shares will not be available for trading until mid-2026, limiting immediate liquidity impact.
