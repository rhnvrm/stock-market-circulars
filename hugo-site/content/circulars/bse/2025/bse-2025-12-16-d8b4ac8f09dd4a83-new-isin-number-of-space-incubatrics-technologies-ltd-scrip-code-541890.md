## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number INE797Z01028 for Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 541890) following the subdivision of equity shares from face value of Rs.10 to Re.1. The new ISIN will be effective for trades executed on and from the ex-date of December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- New ISIN: INE797Z01028 for subdivided shares
- Company: Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd
- Scrip Code: 541890
- Subdivision ratio: Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share
- Ex-date for new ISIN: December 19, 2025
- Divisional Reference: DR-781/2025-2026
- Notice is in continuation to Exchange Notice No. 20251208-9 dated December 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine corporate action notification for share subdivision.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN INE797Z01028 for all trades in Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd equity shares from December 19, 2025 onwards
- The new ISIN corresponds to equity shares with face value of Re.1 each
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ISIN change before the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **December 08, 2025**: Original exchange notice date (Notice No. 20251208-9)
- **December 16, 2025**: Current notice date
- **December 19, 2025**: Ex-date - new ISIN becomes effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only shareholders and traders of Space Incubatrics Technologies Ltd. The share subdivision increases liquidity by reducing per-share price, making shares more accessible to retail investors.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems are updated with the new ISIN before the ex-date to avoid settlement issues. The subdivision will result in proportionate increase in share count (10x) for existing shareholders while maintaining total value.
