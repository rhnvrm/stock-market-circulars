## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 730205, ISIN: INE895B14388) issued by RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED. The suspension is effective from December 09, 2025, due to the instrument's scheduled maturity on December 10, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of RUPA & COMPANY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 730205) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE895B14388
- Redemption/Maturity Date: December 10, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: December 09, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-773/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt instruments approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper with effect from December 09, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 08, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** December 09, 2025
- **Commercial Paper Maturity Date:** December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument prior to its scheduled redemption. Standard market procedure with no broader implications.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Only affects trading members dealing with this specific commercial paper. No impact on equity trading or other debt instruments.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who should expect redemption as per the instrument's terms on the maturity date.
