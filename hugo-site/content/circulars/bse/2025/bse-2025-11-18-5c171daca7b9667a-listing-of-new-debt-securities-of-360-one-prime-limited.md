## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 18, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 26,000 units at a face value of ₹1,00,000 each, representing a total issuance of ₹260 crore. The debentures carry an interest rate of 8.95% per annum and are rated ICRA AA/Stable.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 976768 (Scrip ID: 895OPL27)
- **ISIN**: INE248U07FW5 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Quantity**: 26,000 units
- **Face Value**: ₹1,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,04,209.79 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA/Stable
- **Interest Rate**: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialised form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE248U07FW5
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from June 6, 2026 to June 4, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: June 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors access to ICRA AA/Stable rated debt instruments of 360 ONE Prime Limited. The securities offer a fixed return of 8.95% per annum over an approximately 18-month tenure. The impact is limited to debt market participants interested in high-rated corporate debt securities.
