## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of DSP Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF, an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index, effective December 18, 2025. The ETF will be listed in the 'B' Group with 3,487,401 units available for trading in dematerialized form only, with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Mutual Fund**: DSP Mutual Fund
- **Investment Manager**: DSP Asset Managers Private Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 544654
- **Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name**: SMALLADD
- **ISIN**: INF740KA1WX3
- **Total Units**: 3,487,401 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 16.40 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B
- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Members should contact the designated persons at AMC or BSE for any clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new ETF listing that provides investors with another investment vehicle to gain exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index. The listing expands the range of passive investment options available on BSE. With 11 market makers appointed, adequate liquidity should be available for trading. The impact is limited to market participants interested in smallcap index exposure through ETFs.
