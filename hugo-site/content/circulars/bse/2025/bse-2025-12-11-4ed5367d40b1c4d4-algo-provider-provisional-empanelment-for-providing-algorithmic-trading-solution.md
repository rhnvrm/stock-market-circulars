## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Quantiply Technologies Private Limited as an Algo provider to offer algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Quantiply Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Contact: Plot no: 344 Dharamshi Bhuvan, 4th floor, Chandavarkar crossroad, Matunga E, Mumbai – 400019
- Phone: +91 99202 66899
- Updated list of Algo Providers is available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members may choose to engage with the newly empanelled Algo provider if they require algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Immediate effect from December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular is informational in nature and provides trading members with an additional vendor option for algorithmic trading solutions. The empanelment expands the available choices for members seeking algo trading services. The complete list of empanelled Algo Providers can be accessed at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
