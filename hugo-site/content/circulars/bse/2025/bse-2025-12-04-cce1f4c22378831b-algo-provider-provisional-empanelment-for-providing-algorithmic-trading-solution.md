## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited as an approved vendor for providing algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately and adds to the existing list of approved algo providers available to trading members.

## Key Points

- AlgoBulls Technologies Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an algo provider
- The company is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Empanelment is on a provisional basis
- Office location: Office #1203, Cello Triumph, B-wing, IB Patel Road, off the W-E highway, Goregaon East, Mumbai-400063
- Contact number: +91 9013468987
- Complete list of algo providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update to the list of approved algorithmic trading solution providers.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. This circular is informational in nature, allowing trading members to optionally engage with the newly empanelled algo provider if they choose to use algorithmic trading solutions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: December 4, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine vendor empanelment that expands the options available to trading members who wish to use algorithmic trading solutions.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for trading members. The empanelment provides an additional vendor option for members seeking algorithmic trading capabilities.
