## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by J.K. Cement Ltd on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,770.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+
- Scrip code: 730112
- ISIN: INE823G14AU5
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis. The listing provides additional debt instrument option for institutional investors with 3-month maturity period.
