## Summary

BSE has listed 3,420,028 new equity shares of SCOOBEE DAY GARMENTS (INDIA) LIMITED (Scrip Code: 531234) issued on a rights basis. The shares are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, October 28, 2025. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Company: SCOOBEE DAY GARMENTS (INDIA) LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 531234
- Number of Shares: 3,420,028 equity shares
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 90/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 80/- premium)
- Issue Type: Rights basis
- ISIN: INE962E01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 13500001 to 16920028
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading Commencement: October 28, 2025
- Ranking: Pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes
- The new shares are available for trading from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of rights issue shares for a single company. The addition of 3.42 million shares will increase the company's equity capital base.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to be aware of the new shares available for trading. The shares have the same rights as existing shares (pari-passu), so no special treatment is required.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their allotted shares available for trading from October 28, 2025.
