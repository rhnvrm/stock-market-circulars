## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment effective December 26, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Time Technoplast Limited: 1,500 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE508G14ID1, 7.60% p.a., redemption on June 25, 2026
- Muthoot Capital Services Ltd: 500 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE296G14610, 8.85% p.a., redemption on May 25, 2026
- Avanse Financial Services Limited: 1,200 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE087P14788, 8.37% p.a., redemption on March 18, 2026
- HT Media Ltd: 1,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE501G14EN4, 6.48% p.a., redemption on March 20, 2026
- Infina Finance Private Limited: 2,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE879F14MH1, 6.78% p.a., redemption on March 25, 2026
- All securities rated CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: December 24-26, 2025 (varies by issuer)
- **Redemption Dates**: March 18, 2026 to June 25, 2026 (varies by issuer)
- **Interest Payment**: Cumulative basis on redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Low impact on overall market. This is a routine administrative notification affecting only debt market participants and institutional investors trading in commercial paper. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors with maturity periods ranging from 3 to 6 months. Interest rates range from 6.48% to 8.85% p.a., reflecting current market conditions and issuer credit profiles.
