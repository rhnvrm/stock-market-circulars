## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of MTE Structures Limited for public review and comments. The document was filed on October 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MTE Structures Limited on October 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Document will be hosted for at least 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments on the Draft Offer Document must be submitted within 21 days from November 4, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document is available for review at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **October 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by MTE Structures Limited
- **November 4, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **November 25, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the SME IPO process of MTE Structures Limited. The primary impact is on:

- Potential investors interested in MTE Structures Limited's IPO
- Market participants and analysts who wish to review the company's disclosures
- Regulatory stakeholders monitoring SME IPO compliance

The document availability for public comments is a standard transparency measure in the IPO process and does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations.
