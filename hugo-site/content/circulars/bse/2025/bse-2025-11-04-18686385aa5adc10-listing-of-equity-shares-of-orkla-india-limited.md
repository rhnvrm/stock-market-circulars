## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Orkla India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544595, symbol ORKLAINDIA, and ISIN number INE16NZ01023. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Orkla India Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544595
- Trading Symbol: ORKLAINDIA
- ISIN Number: INE16NZ01023
- Listing date to be announced via separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new listing details
- Members to await separate notice for exact listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 4, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for market participants. This listing adds a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Trading members need to prepare systems for the new scrip code and symbol. Investors interested in Orkla India Limited should monitor for the listing date announcement to participate in trading.
