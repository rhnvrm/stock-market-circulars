## Summary

BSE has announced that trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of JAINEX AAMCOL LIMITED will commence on December 1, 2025. The REs trading window will be open for 4 days from December 1-4, 2025, while the actual Rights Issue will remain open from December 1-9, 2025. Trading will be conducted on T+1 rolling settlement basis in dematerialized form only, with trade-for-trade settlement.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading period: December 1-4, 2025 (Monday to Thursday)
- Rights Issue open period: December 1-9, 2025
- Scrip Code: 751062
- Scrip ID: JAINEX-RE
- ISIN: INE280F20019
- Market Lot: 1
- Group: R
- Settlement: T+1 rolling settlement on trade-for-trade basis
- Trading form: Dematerialized only
- REs not subscribed or renounced before issue closing date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular announces standard rights entitlements trading procedures in accordance with existing BSE framework.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Inform all clients about commencement of REs trading, mentioning the ISIN (INE280F20019)
- Clarify to clients that REs are not ordinary shares of the company
- Include prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases stating:
  - Purchase of REs only provides right to participate in ongoing Rights Issue or renounce REs
  - Application must be made with requisite application money
  - REs not subscribed or renounced before issue closing date will lapse
- Refer to notice no. 20200305-21 (dated March 5, 2020) and notice no. 20200518-34 (dated May 18, 2020) for additional details

**For Investors:**
- REs must be held in dematerialized form for trading
- Investors must either subscribe with application money or renounce REs before December 9, 2025
- Failure to act will result in lapse of entitlements

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| December 1, 2025 | REs trading begins; Rights Issue opens |
| December 4, 2025 | REs trading ends |
| December 9, 2025 | Rights Issue closes; Last date to subscribe or renounce REs |
| After December 9, 2025 | Unsubscribed/unrenounced REs will lapse and be extinguished |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Limited to existing shareholders of JAINEX AAMCOL LIMITED and potential investors interested in the rights issue.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for trading members - Requires proper client communication, disclaimer inclusion in contract notes, and adherence to T+1 settlement procedures for RE trades.

**Investor Impact:** Medium for JAINEX shareholders - Shareholders have a 4-day window to trade their rights entitlements and a 9-day window to either subscribe to the rights issue or renounce their entitlements. Inaction will result in loss of entitlements.
