BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Dachepalli Publishers Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544667, symbol DACHEPALLI, and ISIN number INE1F9M01018. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.
