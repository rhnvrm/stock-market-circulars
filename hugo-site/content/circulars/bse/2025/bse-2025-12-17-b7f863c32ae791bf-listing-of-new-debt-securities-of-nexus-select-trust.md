## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on a private placement basis, effective December 17, 2025. Both securities carry AAA ratings from CRISIL and ICRA, with one having a fixed interest rate of 6.98% p.a. and the other having a floating rate. The securities will be traded only in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities of Nexus Select Trust listed on BSE Debt segment
- Security 1 (Scrip Code 977372): 50,000 units at face value Rs. 100,000, fixed rate 6.98% p.a.
- Security 2 (Scrip Code 977376): 20,000 units at face value Rs. 100,000, floating rate
- Both securities rated CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Date of allotment: December 15, 2025
- Both securities mature in December 2035 (10-year tenure)
- Quarterly interest payment schedule starting March 31, 2026
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit for both securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for private placement debt securities with minimal market impact. The listing affects only specialized debt market participants and institutional investors involved in private placements. The AAA rating indicates high credit quality. There is no impact on equity markets or general retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new scrip codes and trading parameters.
