## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on private placement basis effective October 24, 2025. The securities carry a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, interest rate of 10.62% per annum with monthly interest payments, and maturity date of February 7, 2028. The securities are rated IND A/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 7,500 units
- Scrip Code: 977001
- Scrip ID: 1062KFLD28
- ISIN: INE403Q07GC4 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,592.6575 per unit
- Interest Rate: 10.62% per annum (monthly payments)
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403Q07GC4
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum section for further details
- For clarifications, trading members can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly
- **Maturity Date**: February 7, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement debt listing with no broader market implications. The securities are privately placed and will have limited trading activity on the exchange.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Only relevant to institutional investors who participated in the private placement or those interested in debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard debt listing procedures apply. Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip code and trading parameters.
