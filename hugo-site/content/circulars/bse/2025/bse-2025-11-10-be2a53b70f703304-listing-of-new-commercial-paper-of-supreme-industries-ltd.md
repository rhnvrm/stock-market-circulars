## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Supreme Industries Ltd on private placement basis on the Debt segment. The listing comprises 2000 units of Commercial Paper with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crore. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with scrip code 730523 (SIL071125) and carry a CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Supreme Industries Ltd
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 100 crore (2000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730523
- **Scrip ID**: SIL071125
- **ISIN**: INE195A14CD3
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized only
- **Issuing & Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE195A14CD3
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 07, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 05, 2026
- **Tenure**: 90 days
