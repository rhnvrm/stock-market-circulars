## Summary

BSE has deferred the system enhancement/upgradation activity that was scheduled to be undertaken on Friday, December 12, 2025, from 03:00 PM to 06:00 PM on the BSE StAR MF Platform. This activity was previously announced via Exchange Notice No. 20251208-27 dated December 08, 2025. As a result of the deferment, the BSE StAR MF Platform will remain available to members for normal transaction execution during the originally scheduled maintenance window.

## Key Points

- System enhancement activity originally scheduled for December 12, 2025 (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM) has been deferred
- BSE StAR MF Platform will be available for normal operations during this time
- Members can continue to execute transactions without interruption
- This is an update to the previous notice dated December 08, 2025 (Notice No. 20251208-27)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. Members should note that the BSE StAR MF Platform will remain operational as usual on December 12, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **December 08, 2025**: Original notice issued announcing system enhancement activity
- **December 11, 2025**: Notice issued deferring the scheduled activity
- **December 12, 2025**: Originally scheduled maintenance date (now deferred) - Platform will remain operational

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. The deferment of the maintenance activity is positive for market participants as it ensures uninterrupted access to the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members who had planned for the downtime period can now continue normal operations. No adjustments to trading schedules or workflows are required. The platform will maintain full functionality during the originally scheduled maintenance window.
