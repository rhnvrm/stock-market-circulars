## Summary

Housing & Urban Development Corporation Ltd. (HUDCO) has listed new secured redeemable non-convertible debentures on BSE's Debt segment effective December 15, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each, totaling 1,90,500 debentures (Rs. 1,905 crore aggregate value). The debentures carry a 6.98% per annum interest rate with AAA rating from CARE, ICRA, and India Ratings.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977370 | Scrip ID: 698HUDC32 | ISIN: INE031A08AA2
- Total Quantity: 1,90,500 debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Market Lot: 1 unit (minimum trading quantity)
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 (at par)
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA, ICRA AAA, IND AAA (highest safety rating)
- Interest Rate: 6.98% per annum payable annually
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Trading: Demat form only under specified ISIN
- Tick Size: 1 paise for price movements
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Members must use the designated ISIN (INE031A08AA2) for all transactions
- Adherence to 1 paise tick size for price quotations
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- For clarifications, contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 15, 2025 (effective for trading)
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from December 12, 2026 to December 12, 2032
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: December 12, 2032 (7-year tenure)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard debt instrument listing that primarily affects institutional investors and debt market participants. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are not available for retail subscription.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors with a AAA-rated long-term debt instrument backed by a government-owned financial institution. The 6.98% yield offers a benchmark for similar 7-year government-backed debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members gain access to trade HUDCO debentures on the BSE Debt platform with standard settlement procedures.
