## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Mirae Asset Capital Markets (India) Private Limited on private placement basis, effective December 10, 2025. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with ISIN INE0T2V14087 and Scrip Code 730736.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 6000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,660.50/-
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Scrip Code**: 730736
- **Scrip ID**: MACM091225
- **ISIN**: INE0T2V14087
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading**: Dematerialised form only
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE0T2V14087
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be adhered to

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: December 09, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. The commercial papers are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, indicating strong credit quality. The listing expands debt market instruments available to institutional investors but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading members in the debt segment can contact BSE debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
