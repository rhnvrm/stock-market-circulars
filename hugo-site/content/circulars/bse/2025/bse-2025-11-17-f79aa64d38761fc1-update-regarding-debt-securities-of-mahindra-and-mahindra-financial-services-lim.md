## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Mahindra And Mahindra Financial Services Limited (MMFSL). Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of certain listed debt securities will be revised effective November 18, 2025. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20230329-35 dated March 29, 2023.

## Key Points

- ISIN will change from INE774D07UU9 to INE774D07VM4
- Paid-up value will increase from Rs. 100/- per Debenture to Rs. 1,00,000/- per Debenture
- Scrip Code 974727 remains unchanged
- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- Changes are in continuation of earlier notice dated March 29, 2023

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a procedural update following corporate action on existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised ISIN: INE774D07VM4
- Trading Members must update systems to reflect new paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000/- per Debenture
- All trading and settlement activities post-effective date must use revised parameters

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 17, 2025
- **Effective Date:** November 18, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20230329-35 dated March 29, 2023

## Impact Assessment

This update has moderate impact on holders and traders of MMFSL debt securities with the specified ISIN. The substantial increase in paid-up value from Rs. 100 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture (1000x increase) represents a consolidation following call money receipt. Trading systems and records must be updated to reflect the new ISIN and denomination to ensure accurate trading and settlement operations.
