## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Thursday, December 11, 2025. The shares have a face value of Rs. 2/- each and are under ISIN INE090A01021.

## Key Points

- 45 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- Two batches: 15 shares (Distinctive Nos. 6412616651-6412616665) and 30 shares (Distinctive Nos. 6412615697-6412615726)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: December 11, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This circular addresses a small administrative matter involving 45 bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance. The quantity is insignificant relative to ICICI Bank's total outstanding shares. This is a routine listing notification completing the bonus share distribution process for specific cases that were pending.
