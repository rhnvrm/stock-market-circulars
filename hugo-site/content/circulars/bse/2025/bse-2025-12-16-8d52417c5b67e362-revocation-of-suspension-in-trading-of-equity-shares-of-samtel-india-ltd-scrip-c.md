## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension on trading of equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371). This action allows normal trading activity to resume for the company's shares on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension on Samtel India Ltd. equity shares has been revoked
- Scrip Code: 500371
- Normal trading can now resume on BSE
- Action taken by BSE in accordance with regulatory framework

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the revocation of a previously imposed trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can resume trading in Samtel India Ltd. equity shares
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply
- All participants must ensure compliance with applicable securities laws

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: December 16, 2025
- Effective Date: As per circular notification

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of trading suspension has a medium impact on:

- **Shareholders**: Existing shareholders can now trade their holdings
- **Market Liquidity**: Resumption of trading improves liquidity for the scrip
- **Price Discovery**: Normal market mechanisms for price discovery can resume
- **Investor Access**: Investors can now buy or sell shares of Samtel India Ltd. on BSE
