## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of HIREN WAHEN BUILDTECH PRIVATE LIMITED effective November 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of November 30, 2025 for payment of interest and redemption of debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from November 28, 2025
- Record date set for November 30, 2025 for redemption purposes
- ISIN: INE0S1Q07019 (Code: 975302)
- Debenture series: HWBPL-19%-30-11-25-PVT
- Circular reference: DR-766/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative action for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from November 28, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Circular issuance date
- **November 28, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **November 30, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of these specific debentures issued by HIREN WAHEN BUILDTECH PRIVATE LIMITED. This is a routine corporate action for debt securities nearing maturity. The suspension ensures proper settlement of redemption obligations without market interference. Given the private limited nature of the issuer, market-wide impact is minimal.
