## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crore issued by Shree Cements Ltd on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each totaling 3000 units (Rs. 150 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,95,527/- per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE070A14810
- Scrip Code: 729917
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Shree Cements Ltd with high credit ratings ensuring investor confidence.
