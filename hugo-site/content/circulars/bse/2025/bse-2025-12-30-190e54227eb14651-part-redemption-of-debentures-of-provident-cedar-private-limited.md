## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the partial redemption of debentures issued by PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED. Following the partial redemption, the face value per debenture has been reduced to Rs. 265,637.45. The debentures will trade with the new reduced face value from December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 974311
- ISIN: INE0NF907031
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 265,637.45 per Debenture
- Effective Date: December 31, 2025
- Reference: DR-788/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange must:
- Take note of the reduced face value
- Ensure debentures are traded with the new face value of Rs. 265,637.45 per debenture from December 31, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The partial redemption affects only the debenture holders of PROVIDENT CEDAR PRIVATE LIMITED. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for accurate pricing and settlement of these debt securities.
