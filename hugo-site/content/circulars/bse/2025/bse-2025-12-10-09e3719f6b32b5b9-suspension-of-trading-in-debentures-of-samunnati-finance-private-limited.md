## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective December 12, 2025. The suspension is due to the record date being fixed for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE551U07308
- Scrip Code: 975784
- Series: SFPL-8.25%-27-12-25-PVT
- Record Date: December 12, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: December 12, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-776/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of SAMUNNATI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE551U07308) from December 12, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- All transactions in these debentures must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **December 12, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects a specific series of private placement debentures, limiting broader market impact.

**Investor Impact**: High for debenture holders - redemption and interest payment will be processed based on the December 12, 2025 record date.

**Trading Impact**: Complete trading suspension for the specified debenture series from December 12, 2025. Trading members cannot execute any buy or sell orders for these securities.
