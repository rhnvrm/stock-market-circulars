## Summary

BSE has published the draft offer document of Yaashavi Jewellers Limited filed on December 25, 2025, as per Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft offer document filed by Yaashavi Jewellers Limited on December 25, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Public comments solicited via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments on the draft offer document within 21 days from December 26, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on SME website: DraftProspectusYaashviJewellersLimited_20251225155205.pdf

## Important Dates

- **Draft Filing Date**: December 25, 2025
- **Notice Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from December 26, 2025 (until January 16, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. It is relevant primarily for:
- Prospective investors interested in Yaashavi Jewellers Limited
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the draft offer document
- Regulatory compliance tracking for SME listings

The circular follows standard procedure for SME IPO filings and does not indicate any changes to market operations or trading rules.
