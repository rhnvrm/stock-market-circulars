## Summary

BSE has announced that GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective November 20, 2025. This follows a previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251107-54. All facility options including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) will be available for trading.

## Key Points

- GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND reopens for ongoing transactions from November 20, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform for subscription and redemption
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options
- All schemes classified under DEBT category
- Previous suspension was notified via Notice No. 20251107-54

## Scheme Details

Six variants of GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND are available:

**Regular Plan:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF666M01MZ6)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF666M01NA7)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF666M01NB5)

**Direct Plan:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF666M01MW3)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF666M01MX1)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF666M01MY9)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE can resume processing transactions for GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND through the BSE StAR MF Platform from November 20, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** November 20, 2025 - GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date:** November 19, 2025
- **Notice Number:** 20251119-31
- **Reference Notice:** 20251107-54 (previous suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

This notification has minimal market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a single mutual fund scheme on BSE's trading platform. The reopening allows investors to resume normal investment, redemption, and systematic plan activities in GROWW MONEY MARKET FUND. This is a routine operational update affecting only investors and distributors dealing with this specific debt fund scheme.
