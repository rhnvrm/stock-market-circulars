## Summary

BSE has informed trading members about the part redemption of debentures issued by Grand Hills Developments Pvt Ltd. The debenture with ISIN INE0DWX08015 (Scrip Code 977285) will have a reduced face value of Rs. 96,563 per debenture effective from November 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Grand Hills Developments Pvt Ltd
- Security Type: GHDPL-RESET RATE-31-10-29-PVT
- ISIN: INE0DWX08015
- Scrip Code: 977285
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of Debentures
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 96,563 per debenture
- Reference: DR-765/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised face value for trading purposes
- All trades in these debentures must reflect the reduced face value from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 27, 2025 - Debentures will trade with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard debt market corporate action with minimal market impact. The part redemption affects only investors holding these specific debentures of Grand Hills Developments Pvt Ltd. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
