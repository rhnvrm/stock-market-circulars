## Summary

BARODA BNP PARIBAS MUTUAL FUND is launching the BUSINESS CONGLOMERATES FUND NFO with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from September 02, 2025 to September 15, 2025. The scheme offers 6 different plan variants with tentative allotment on September 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: September 02-15, 2025
- 6 scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- All schemes are equity category funds
- SIP facility available during NFO period
- Tentative allotment date: September 22, 2025
- Extended trading till midnight on September 15, 2025 for lumpsum purchases

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on September 19, 2025
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Cheques & OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt by deadline

## Important Dates

- NFO Start: September 02, 2025
- NFO End: September 15, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum)
- Fund Availability Deadline: September 19, 2025 (6:00 PM)
- Tentative Allotment: September 22, 2025
- SIP Registration Deadline: September 15, 2025 (3:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Provides additional investment option for retail investors interested in business conglomerates theme. No direct impact on individual stock prices or market operations.
