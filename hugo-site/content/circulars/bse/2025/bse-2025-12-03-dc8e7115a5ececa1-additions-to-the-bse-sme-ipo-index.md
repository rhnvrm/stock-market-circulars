## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective December 4, 2025. Mother Nutri Foods Limited (Exchange ticker: 544623) and K K Silk Mills Limited (Exchange ticker: 544624) were listed on BSE on December 3, 2025 and will be included in the index at the opening of the next trading day.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index: Mother Nutri Foods Limited and K K Silk Mills Limited
- Both companies listed on BSE on December 3, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on December 4, 2025
- Exchange tickers: 544623 (Mother Nutri Foods) and 544624 (K K Silk Mills)
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Referenced prior notices: 20251202-47 and 20251202-48

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new SME listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and passive investment vehicles benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituents.

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **December 4, 2025**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine index maintenance activity affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Index funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to purchase shares of the two new constituents to maintain index alignment. The corporate action file has been updated to facilitate automated processing by market systems.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors specifically tracking or invested in the BSE SME IPO Index. The addition of these two stocks increases the index's diversification within the SME IPO segment.
