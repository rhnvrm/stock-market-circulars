## Summary

Annapurna Finance Private Limited has announced a part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE515Q07640) along with interest payment. The record date is set for December 7, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value effective December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Annapurna Finance Private Limited
- Debenture Code: AFPL-10.95%-22-07-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE515Q07640 (BSE Code: 976927)
- Record Date: December 7, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- New reduced face value: Rs. 79,166.65 per debenture
- Effective date for reduced face value trading: December 5, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-771/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification as per debt market operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value from December 5, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 79,166.65 per debenture
- All transactions from December 5, 2025 onwards must be conducted at the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **November 24, 2025**: Circular notice date
- **December 5, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-771/2025-2026)
- **December 7, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Investors holding these debentures as of the record date will receive part redemption proceeds and interest payment. The remaining investment will continue with reduced face value.

**Trading Impact**: From December 5, 2025, all market participants must trade these debentures at the reduced face value of Rs. 79,166.65, which represents a partial return of principal to investors.

**Market Operations**: This is a routine corporate action in the debt market segment with limited broader market impact, affecting only holders and traders of this specific debenture series.
