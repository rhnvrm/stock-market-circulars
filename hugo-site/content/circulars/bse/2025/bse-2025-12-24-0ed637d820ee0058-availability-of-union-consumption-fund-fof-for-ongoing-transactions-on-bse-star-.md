## Summary

BSE has notified members that UNION CONSUMPTION FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective December 26, 2025. This follows the previous suspension notice (Notice No. 20251128-39). All facility types including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) will be available.

## Key Points

- UNION CONSUMPTION FUND reopening for transactions from December 26, 2025
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options
- All transaction facilities enabled: ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Fund category: Equity
- Previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20251128-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members can facilitate transactions in the specified schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 26, 2025 - Resumption of ongoing transactions
- **Reference Notice**: Notice No. 20251128-39 (suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one mutual fund scheme and its variants.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members and distributors can resume processing transactions for UNION CONSUMPTION FUND through BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors who were waiting during the suspension period can now conduct subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transactions.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of UNION CONSUMPTION FUND as transaction facilities are restored.
