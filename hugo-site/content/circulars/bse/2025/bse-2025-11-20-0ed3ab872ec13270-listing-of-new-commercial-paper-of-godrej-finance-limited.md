## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 6000 Commercial Papers issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective November 20, 2025. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment with Scrip Code 730607 (Scrip ID: GFL201125) and ISIN INE02KN14648. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Size: 6000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 491,754.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730607
- Scrip ID: GFL201125
- ISIN: INE02KN14648
- Allotment Date: November 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02KN14648
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** November 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** November 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for short-term debt instruments with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper has a tenure of 90 days and is rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments. Trading is restricted to institutional participants in standard lots of Rs. 5 lakhs. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
