## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of LIGHT MICROFINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE366T07170, Code: 974421) effective November 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: LMPL-13.79%-12-12-25-PVT (INE366T07170)
- BSE scrip code: 974421
- Record date fixed: November 27, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: November 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice number: DR-765/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debenture series (INE366T07170) from November 27, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding or intending to trade these debentures about the suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Record Date:** November 27, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and relates to a standard corporate action (redemption and interest payment). Trading will remain suspended until the redemption and interest payment processes are completed.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. No trading opportunity exists from November 27, 2025 onwards for this instrument.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this ISIN and communicate the restriction to relevant clients and trading desks.
