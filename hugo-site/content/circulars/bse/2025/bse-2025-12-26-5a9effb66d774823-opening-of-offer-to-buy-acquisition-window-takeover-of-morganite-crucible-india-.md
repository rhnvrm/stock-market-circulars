## Summary

Foseco India Limited (Acquirer) along with Persons Acting in Concert (PACs) - Foseco Overseas Limited, Vesuvius Holdings Limited, and Foseco (UK) Limited - have launched an open offer to acquire up to 14,00,000 fully paid-up equity shares of Morganite Crucible (India) Limited. The offer price is INR 1,557.15 per equity share of face value INR 5 each, representing 25.00% of the voting share capital. This offer is made pursuant to Regulation 3(1) and Regulation 4 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Offer Price**: INR 1,557.15 per equity share
- **Face Value**: INR 5 per equity share
- **Offer Size**: Up to 14,00,000 equity shares representing 25.00% of voting share capital
- **Offer Type**: Cash offer, unconditional, not subject to minimum acceptance level
- **Acquirer**: Foseco India Limited (CIN: L24294PN1958PLC011052)
- **Target Company**: Morganite Crucible (India) Limited (CIN: L26920MH1986PLC038607)
- **PACs**: Foseco Overseas Limited, Vesuvius Holdings Limited, and Foseco (UK) Limited (all UK registered entities)
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Made under SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011
- **Not a Conditional Offer**: Does not require minimum acceptance as per Regulation 19
- **Not a Competing Offer**: As per Regulation 20 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

This is a compliance action under SEBI (SAST) Regulations rather than a regulatory change. The offer follows the mandatory requirements of Regulations 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations for substantial acquisitions and takeovers.

**Note on Offer Size**: While Regulation 7(1) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations typically requires an offer size of at least 26% of total voting share capital, this offer is for 25% as it represents the total number and percentage of equity shares held by public shareholders as on the date of the Letter of Offer.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Public Shareholders**:
- Review the Letter of Offer immediately upon receipt
- Consult stockbroker, investment consultant, Manager to the Offer, or Registrar to the Offer for clarification if needed
- If shares have been recently sold, hand over the Letter of Offer and accompanying Form of Acceptance-cum-Acknowledgement to the stock exchange member through whom the sale was effected
- Submit Form of Acceptance-cum-Acknowledgement if intending to participate in the offer

**For the Acquirer and PACs**:
- Obtain all statutory and other approvals as required under SEBI (SAST) Regulations
- Ensure compliance with disclosure and documentation requirements
