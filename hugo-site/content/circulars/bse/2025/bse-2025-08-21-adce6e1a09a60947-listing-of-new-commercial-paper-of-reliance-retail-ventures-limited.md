## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper worth ₹675 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade in the BSE Debt segment with face value of ₹5 lakh each and 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 13,500 units of ₹5,00,000 each (total ₹675 crore)
- Issue price: ₹4,92,812.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Trading in dematerialized form only under ISIN INE929O14DW4
- Standard trading denomination of ₹5 lakh and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of ₹5 lakh and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Listing date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption date: November 19, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. The short-term nature and private placement basis limit broader market implications. Trading will be restricted to institutional investors given the high denomination requirement.
