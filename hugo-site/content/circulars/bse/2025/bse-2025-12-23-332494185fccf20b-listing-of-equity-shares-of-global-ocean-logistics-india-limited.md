## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Global Ocean Logistics India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544665, symbol GLOBALLOG, and ISIN number INE1ITY01011. The exact listing date and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Global Ocean Logistics India Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544665
- Symbol: GLOBALLOG
- ISIN: INE1ITY01011
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Detailed trading information will be provided in a subsequent notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: December 23, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The addition of one new SME company to the exchange expands investment opportunities in the SME segment but does not affect existing market operations or trading procedures. Impact is limited to traders and investors interested in the logistics sector and SME segment investments.
