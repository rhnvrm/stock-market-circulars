## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the part redemption of debentures issued by Ashiana Housing Ltd. The debentures with ISIN INE365D08026 (Scrip Code: 973220, Series: AHL-8%-31-5-41-PVT) will trade at a reduced face value of Rs. 646,663.793 per debenture effective December 11, 2025, following partial redemption.

## Key Points

- Company: Ashiana Housing Ltd
- Security: 8% Debentures maturing May 31, 2041 (Private Placement)
- ISIN: INE365D08026
- Scrip Code: 973220
- New reduced face value: Rs. 646,663.793 per debenture
- Effective date for trading at reduced face value: December 11, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-775/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving partial redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the debentures at the reduced face value of Rs. 646,663.793 per debenture from December 11, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this change and update their systems accordingly
- All trades executed after the effective date must reflect the new face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 11, 2025 - Debentures will trade at reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only holders of specific privately placed debentures of Ashiana Housing Ltd. The part redemption is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Moderate for affected debenture holders - Investors holding these debentures will receive partial redemption proceeds, and the remaining debenture holdings will have a reduced face value. The interest and redemption calculations will be based on the new reduced face value going forward.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement of these securities.
