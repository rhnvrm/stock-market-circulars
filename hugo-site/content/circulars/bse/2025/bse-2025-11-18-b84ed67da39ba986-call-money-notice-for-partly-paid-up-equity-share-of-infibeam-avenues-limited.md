## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module of the IBBS platform for Infibeam Avenues Limited to collect the first and final call on 69,99,85,723 partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders must pay Rs. 5 per share during the bidding period from November 25 to December 9, 2025. The registrar for this issue is Alankit Assignments Limited.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Infibeam Avenues Limited
- **Type of Call**: First and Final Call on Partly Paid-up Equity Shares
- **Call Amount**: Rs. 5 per partly paid-up equity share
- **Total Shares**: 69,99,85,723 partly paid-up equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Security Symbol**: INFIBPPCM
- **Registrar**: Alankit Assignments Limited (SEBI Reg: INR000001385)
- **Contact**: infibeamrights@alankit.com, Tel: +91-011-42541966/952
- **Module**: Call Money Module of IBBS platform available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This notice operates under existing framework per Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding Call Money Module procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and SCSBs**: Must use the Call Money Module of IBBS platform to submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period
- **Eligible Participants**: Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members having 3-in-1 type accounts
- **Shareholders**: Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 5 per share to complete their subscription
- **Bidding Hours**: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. during the issue period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Issue Opening Date**: Tuesday, November 25, 2025
- **Issue Closing Date**: Tuesday, December 09, 2025
- **Bidding Hours**: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily
- **Issue Period**: 15 days

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing holders of Infibeam Avenues partly paid-up equity shares must pay the final call amount of Rs. 5 per share to avoid forfeiture. Total call amount to be collected is approximately Rs. 350 crore (69.99 crore shares Ã— Rs. 5).

**Market Operations**: Trading Members and SCSBs need to facilitate the call money collection process through the IBBS platform during the specified 15-day window.

**Company Impact**: Successful collection will result in all partly paid-up shares becoming fully paid-up, strengthening the company's equity capital base and completing the rights issue process initiated earlier.
