## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective November 10, 2025. The instruments were issued on private placement basis with a total quantity of 500 securities at Rs. 5,00,000 face value each. The Commercial Papers are rated CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+ with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 500 Commercial Papers listed
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,78,999.50
- Scrip Code: 730522
- Scrip ID: ECLF71125
- ISIN Number: INE804I14YE5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE804I14YE5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 10, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: November 7, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: November 10, 2025
- Date of Redemption: May 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for debt instruments on private placement basis. The impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term commercial paper instruments. The listing expands the available debt securities on BSE's platform but has no broader market or regulatory implications. Trading members can contact BSE's debt department for clarifications on contact numbers provided.
