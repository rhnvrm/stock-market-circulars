## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in four debt securities that are scheduled to mature on December 11, 2025. The suspension becomes effective from December 9, 2025. This includes three Treasury Bills and one Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series.

## Key Points

- Four debt securities will mature on the redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from December 9, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-773/2025-2026
- Issued by Listing Compliance & Operations department
- Trading members advised not to deal in these securities post suspension date

## Securities Affected

**Treasury Bills:**
- 182T111225 (SCRIP CODE: 805077, ISIN: IN002025Y115)
- 364TB111225 (SCRIP CODE: 805000, ISIN: IN002024Z354)
- 91TB111225 (SCRIP CODE: 805115, ISIN: IN002025X240)

**Sovereign Gold Bond:**
- Sovereign Gold Bond 2017 Series (SCRIP CODE: 800279, ISIN: IN0020170133)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the mentioned securities from December 9, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in these maturing securities
- Members must take note of this suspension for their operational compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** December 8, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date:** December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market impact. The suspension affects only holders and traders of the specific securities mentioned. Investors holding these securities until maturity will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. The advance notice of one day provides adequate time for market participants to adjust positions or complete any pending transactions before the suspension takes effect.
