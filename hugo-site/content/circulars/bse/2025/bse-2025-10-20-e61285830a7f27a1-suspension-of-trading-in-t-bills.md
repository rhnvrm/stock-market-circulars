## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-bill with ISIN IN1520220147 (747GUJSDL25, Scrip Code 810899) effective from October 21, 2025, due to its maturity on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security.

## Key Points

- T-bill ISIN: IN1520220147 (747GUJSDL25)
- Scrip Code: 810899
- Trading suspension effective from October 21, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-740/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-bill from October 21, 2025 onwards
- Members are requested to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 21, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: On or around October 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational suspension for a maturing T-bill. Only affects holders of this specific security who should prepare for redemption. No broader market implications.
