## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three series of non-convertible debentures issued by Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. effective December 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing December 23, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Three debenture series of Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. will be suspended from trading
- EFSL-9.39%-8-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE532F07BM3, Code: 937339)
- EFSL-9.8%-8-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE532F07BN1, Code: 937341)
- EFSL-ZC-8-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE532F07BO9, Code: 937343)
- Record date set for December 23, 2025 for both redemption and interest payment
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these debentures from December 23, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-783/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from December 23, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in the three specified debenture series after the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Record Date**: December 23, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: December 23, 2025
- **Purpose**: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders of three specific debenture series of Edelweiss Financial Services. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process, which is a standard corporate action.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debenture holders and trading members dealing in these specific instruments. The zero-coupon debenture (EFSL-ZC) and two interest-bearing debentures (9.39% and 9.8%) will be redeemed, providing liquidity to holders while removing these instruments from trading.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear on the record date (December 23, 2025) will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Trading opportunity in these instruments ceases from the no-dealings date.
