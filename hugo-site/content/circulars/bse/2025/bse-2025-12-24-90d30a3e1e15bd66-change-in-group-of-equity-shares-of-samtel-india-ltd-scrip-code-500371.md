## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500371) will be moved to the 'X' group effective December 31, 2025. This group change is a significant classification update that will affect the trading conditions for this security.

## Key Points

- Company: Samtel India Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 500371
- New Group: X Group
- Effective Date: December 31, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251224-21
- Notice Date: December 24, 2025
- Category: Company related
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Samtel India Ltd. will be reclassified from their current group to the 'X' group. The 'X' group typically indicates securities under special surveillance or trading restrictions at BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Take note of the group change effective December 31, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Be aware of any trading restrictions or conditions applicable to 'X' group securities
- Contact Mrs. Kinjal More on +91-7304278788 for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group indicates that the stock is being placed under enhanced surveillance. This may result in:
- Stricter trading conditions
- Potential impact on liquidity
- Additional scrutiny on trading activities
- Possible trading restrictions or special margin requirements

Trading members and investors should exercise caution and be aware of the implications of this group change when trading in Samtel India Ltd. shares from December 31, 2025 onwards.
