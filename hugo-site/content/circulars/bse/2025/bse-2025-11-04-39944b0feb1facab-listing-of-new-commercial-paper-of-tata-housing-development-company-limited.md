## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Papers issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective November 4, 2025. The issue comprises 1700 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on February 2, 2026. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and IND A1+, with HDFC Bank serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Quantity Issued**: 1700 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,329 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730484
- **Scrip ID**: THDL031125
- **ISIN**: INE582L14IZ9
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 2, 2026
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14IZ9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: November 3, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine Commercial Paper listing with minimal market impact. The short-term debt instrument (approximately 3-month tenor) provides Tata Housing Development Company Limited with working capital financing. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors interested in short-term corporate debt instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
