## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on a private placement basis, effective December 12, 2025. The securities are rated CARE AA with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.95% per annum, and maturing on September 10, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977362, Scrip ID: 895NWIL27
- ISIN: INE523L07AS9
- Quantity: 20,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Interest Rate: 8.95% per annum (paid annually)
- Date of Allotment: December 10, 2025
- Maturity Date: September 10, 2027
- Credit Rating: CARE AA
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE523L07AS9
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Trading members requiring clarifications may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **December 12, 2025**: Listing effective date
- **December 10, 2026 to September 10, 2027**: Interest payment dates (annually)
- **September 10, 2027**: Redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to privately placed debt securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. The impact is limited to:

- Debt market participants and institutional investors holding or trading these specific securities
- Enhanced liquidity for holders of these debentures through BSE Debt segment
- No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations
- The CARE AA rating indicates high credit quality with adequate safety for timely servicing of debt obligations
