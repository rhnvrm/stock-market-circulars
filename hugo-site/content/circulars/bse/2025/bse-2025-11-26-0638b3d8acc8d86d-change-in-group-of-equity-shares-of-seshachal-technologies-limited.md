## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Seshachal Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 531794) will be transferred to the 'X' group effective Wednesday, December 10, 2025. This change in grouping affects the trading parameters and surveillance framework applicable to the security.

## Key Points

- Company: Seshachal Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 531794
- New Group: X Group
- Effective Date: Wednesday, December 10, 2025
- Current trading to continue in existing group until the change date

## Regulatory Changes

The security will be subject to the trading rules and surveillance measures applicable to 'X' group securities. The 'X' group typically includes securities that require enhanced surveillance or have specific trading restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Investors should be aware of the different trading parameters applicable to 'X' group securities
- All trades must comply with the rules governing 'X' group securities from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** November 26, 2025
- **Effective Date:** December 10, 2025 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group indicates enhanced surveillance or regulatory monitoring. This may result in:
- Modified trading parameters such as price bands or order limits
- Increased scrutiny of trading activity
- Potential impact on liquidity as some institutional investors may have restrictions on trading in certain groups
- Investors should exercise caution and review the specific trading rules applicable to 'X' group securities
