## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 750,000 new equity shares of HB Estate Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 532334) effective December 12, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 65.25 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 55.25) pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 750,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value issued at premium of Rs. 55.25
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Trading permitted from December 12, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 65.25 per share
- Distinctive numbers: 56301869 to 57051868
- Date of allotment: October 14, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE640B01021
- Scrip Code: 532334

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 750,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions until June 29, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: December 12, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a company-specific listing announcement affecting HB Estate Developers Ltd only. The preferential allotment represents a modest increase in share capital.

**Shareholder Impact**: The 750,000 new shares will increase the outstanding share count and may cause minor dilution for existing shareholders. Non-promoter allottees are subject to 18-month lock-in period.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing procedure with no operational changes for trading members. The shares trade under existing scrip code 532334.
