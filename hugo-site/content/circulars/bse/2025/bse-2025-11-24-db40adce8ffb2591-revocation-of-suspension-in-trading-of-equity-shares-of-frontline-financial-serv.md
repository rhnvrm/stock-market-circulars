## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Frontline Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 531685, ISIN: INE776R01011). The company, which provides financial advisory services, is listed on BSE and Ahmedabad Stock Exchange. The company profile shows it is operational with registered office at Office No 803, Eighth Floor Wallstreet II, Ellisbridge, Ahmedabad 380006, Gujarat.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension has been revoked for Frontline Financial Services Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 531685
- ISIN: INE776R01011
- Company business: Financial advisory services in service sector
- Managing Director: Sandeep Chhaganlal Mathur (also serving as Compliance Officer)
- Company Secretary Ms. Isha Badruka resigned w.e.f. 31st July 2025
- Listed on Bombay Stock Exchange and Ahmedabad Stock Exchange
- Listing fees paid to all exchanges
