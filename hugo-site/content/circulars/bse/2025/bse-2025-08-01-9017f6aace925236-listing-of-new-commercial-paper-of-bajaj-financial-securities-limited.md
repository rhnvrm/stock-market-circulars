## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective August 1, 2025. Both securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ with ICICI Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper securities listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- First security: 2000 quantity, maturing October 27, 2025 (Scrip Code: 729886)
- Second security: 5000 quantity, maturing August 14, 2025 (Scrip Code: 729390)
- Both securities traded only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 1, 2025
- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- First Security Redemption: October 27, 2025
- Second Security Redemption: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of commercial paper securities. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt segment.
