## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by JM Financial Services Limited on private placement basis, effective 03 December 2025. The securities consist of 5,250 non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.30% per annum and maturing on 27 May 2027. The securities are rated CRISIL AA/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977328, Scrip ID: 830JMFSL27
- ISIN: INE012I07116 (further listing under existing ISIN)
- Total quantity: 5,250 debentures
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Allotment date: 02 December 2025
- Trading commenced: 03 December 2025
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA/Stable
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE012I07116
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 02 December 2025
- **Listing Date**: 03 December 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from 27 November 2026 to 27 May 2027
- **Redemption Date**: 27 May 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The securities are privately placed and not publicly offered, limiting participation to institutional investors. The transaction adds Rs. 52.5 crore to JM Financial Services Limited's debt capital but does not affect equity markets or create any new compliance obligations for broader market participants. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details.
