## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company originally known as Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Ratnakar Securities Limited with effect from October 24, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- **Original Name**: Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited
- **New Name**: Ratnakar Securities Limited
- **CIN**: L66120GJ1992PLC017564
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Certificate Issuer**: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Address**: Shop-304, Sankalp Square-2, Nr. Delux Appartment, Nr. Kalgi char rasta, Jalaram Temple, Paldi, Ahmedabad-380006, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate name change processed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (Mangalya Soft-Tech Limited) and new name (Ratnakar Securities Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing obligations and commitments continue under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 24, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from October 24, 2025 (until October 24, 2027)
- **Circular Date**: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Stakeholders should update their records to reflect the new company name. All contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes. The company continues its business activities under the new identity. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory filings and identification.

**Note**: Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
