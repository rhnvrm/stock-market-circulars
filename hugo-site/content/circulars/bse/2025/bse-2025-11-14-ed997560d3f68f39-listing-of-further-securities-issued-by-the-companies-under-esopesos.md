## Summary

BSE has listed 4,313 shares issued under Employee Stock Option Plans/Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOP/ESOS). The shares are subject to lock-in periods with release dates of February 3, 2026 and February 3, 2027.

## Key Points

- Total shares allotted: 4,313
- Eight tranches of shares with varying allotment sizes
- Distinctive numbers range from 20678934 to 20683246
- Lock-in release dates: February 3, 2026 (for first 4 tranches) and February 3, 2027 (for last 4 tranches)
- First tranche lock-in: 2,653 shares (623+503+394+1,133)
- Second tranche lock-in: 1,660 shares (389+315+247+709)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shares subject to lock-in restrictions until specified release dates
- Cannot be traded or transferred until lock-in period expires on February 3, 2026 or February 3, 2027 respectively

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Lock-in release date for 2,653 shares (tranches 1-4)
- **February 3, 2027**: Lock-in release date for 1,660 shares (tranches 5-8)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The total number of shares (4,313) is relatively small and represents routine ESOP/ESOS allotments. The lock-in periods ensure these shares will not immediately affect market liquidity. This is a standard administrative listing notice with no immediate trading implications.
