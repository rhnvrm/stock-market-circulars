## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 300 crore issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE's debt segment with effect from August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- 6,000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each listed
- Securities issued on private placement basis
- Trading effective from August 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE211H14AD7, Scrip Code: 729991
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding instrument for investors in debt segment with 3-month maturity period.
