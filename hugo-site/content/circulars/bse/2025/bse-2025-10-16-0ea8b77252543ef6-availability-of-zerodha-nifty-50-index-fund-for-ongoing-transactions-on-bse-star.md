## Summary

BSE has notified members that ZERODHA MUTUAL FUND is reopening the ZERODHA NIFTY 50 INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) starting October 17, 2025. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20250925-10.

## Key Points

- ZERODHA NIFTY 50 INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH is being reopened for transactions
- Scheme Category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01158
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference to previous notice: 20250925-10

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of this scheme for processing client transactions effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It affects only investors and distributors interested in the ZERODHA NIFTY 50 INDEX FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The reopening provides investors with renewed access to subscription, redemption, and systematic investment/transfer/withdrawal facilities for this specific equity index fund scheme. No broader market or operational implications exist beyond this specific mutual fund product.
