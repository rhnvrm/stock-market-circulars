## Summary

KEC International Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective September 9, 2025. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings with redemption date of December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,391 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730165, Scrip ID: KIL090925
- ISIN: INE389H14LH9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 5, 2025
- Tenure: Approximately 87 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading limited to institutional and high-net-worth investors due to Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
