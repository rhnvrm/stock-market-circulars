## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding an amendment in the corporate name of Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd (Scrip code: 501425). This is an administrative update to reflect changes in the company's official registered name.

## Key Points

- Company affected: Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd
- Scrip code: 501425
- Nature: Corporate name amendment
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Circular date: November 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This circular notifies market participants of the formal name change or amendment for Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Ltd as registered with the stock exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems and records with the amended company name
- Trading members should ensure proper identification of the security using the scrip code 501425
- All future references and transactions should use the updated company name

## Important Dates

- Circular issue date: November 6, 2025
- Effective date: To be determined based on regulatory approval

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an administrative change that does not affect the trading, valuation, or operations of the company.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Market participants need to update internal systems and databases to reflect the new company name.

**Investor Impact:** No direct impact on shareholding, rights, or obligations. Existing shares continue to trade under the same scrip code 501425.
