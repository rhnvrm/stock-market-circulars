## Summary

BSE has listed the equity shares of SKF India (Industrial) Limited (Scrip Code: 544572) following the submission of shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing shows a total of 49,437,963 equity shares with promoter group holding 52.58% and public holding 47.42% as of October 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 49,437,963 (100% in dematerialized form)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 25,992,059 shares (52.58%)
- Public holding: 23,445,904 shares (47.42%)
- Total number of shareholders: 64,256
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or depository receipts issued
- No shares in locked-in status
- No promoter shares pledged or encumbered
- Record date: October 15, 2025 (NSDL and CDSL)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entity has submitted shareholding pattern under Regulation 31 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration provided confirming no partly paid shares, convertible securities, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or depository receipts
- All shares held in dematerialized form as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- Record Date (NSDL): October 15, 2025
- Record Date (CDSL): October 15, 2025
- Circular Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure for the listing of SKF India (Industrial) Limited equity shares. The shareholding pattern shows compliance with minimum public shareholding requirements with 47.42% public holding. No pledging or encumbrance of promoter shares indicates financial stability. The listing enables trading of the company's equity shares on BSE platform. No immediate market impact expected as this is an informational filing.
