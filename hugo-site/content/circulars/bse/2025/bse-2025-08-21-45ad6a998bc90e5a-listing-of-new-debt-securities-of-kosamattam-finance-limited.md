## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 21, 2025. Both securities carry 10% annual interest with monthly payments and are rated IND A/Stable.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities listed: KFL-10%-30-11-27-PVT (₹100 crore) and KFL-10%-31-05-28-PVT (₹50 crore)
- Interest rate: 10% per annum with monthly payments
- Face value and issue price: ₹10,000 per security
- Credit rating: IND A/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Date of allotment: August 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date (First Security): November 30, 2027
- Redemption Date (Second Security): May 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities provide institutional investors with fixed-income investment options with monthly interest payments and A-grade credit rating.
