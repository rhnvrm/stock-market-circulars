## Summary

BSE has notified that DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd (Member No. 3129) will voluntarily cease operations in the Currency derivative segment. This is a routine notification regarding member exit from the exchange.

## Key Points

- DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd (Member No. 3129) is ceasing business in Currency derivative segment
- Closure is voluntary in nature
- Notification issued to all trading members of the exchange
- Contact email provided for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding member exit.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other members. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Not specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a single member firm voluntarily exiting the currency derivatives segment. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to clients of DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd who will need to transfer their currency derivative positions to other brokers.

**Member Impact**: Affects only DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd and its clients in the currency derivatives segment.
