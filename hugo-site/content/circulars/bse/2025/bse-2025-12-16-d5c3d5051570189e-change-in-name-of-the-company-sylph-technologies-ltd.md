## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Sylph Technologies Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Sylph Industries Limited with effect from October 24, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Majjarsha Housing Finance Limited and is a company limited by shares with CIN L36100MP1992PLC007102.

## Key Points

- **Previous Name**: SYLPH TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- **New Name**: SYLPH INDUSTRIES LIMITED
- **Original Name**: MAJJARRRSHA HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED
- **CIN**: L36100MP1992PLC007102
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Office**: Office No. 517, Sky Corporate Park, Scheme No. 78, A.B. Road, Vijay Nagar, Indore, Indore-452010, Madhya Pradesh, India
- **Issuing Authority**: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- **Approved by**: Prerna Panwar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative name change process under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Sylph Technologies Limited) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 24, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from October 24, 2025 (until October 24, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update all statutory documents, letterheads, and signage to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Neutral. No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The certificate clarifies that this is purely a nomenclature change.

**Compliance Impact**: Company must ensure dual-name display compliance for the mandatory two-year period and update all registrations and filings with the new name.
