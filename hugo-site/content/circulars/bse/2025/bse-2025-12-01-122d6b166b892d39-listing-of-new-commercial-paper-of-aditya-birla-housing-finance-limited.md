## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The securities totaling 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 200 crore) are now admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective December 1, 2025. The commercial papers carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Quantity**: 4,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,92,703.50 per unit
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore
- **Scrip Code**: 730671
- **Scrip ID**: ABHF281125
- **ISIN**: INE831R14FN9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited

## Trading Details

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Listed on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 91 days

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note:
- All trading must be conducted in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FN9
- Standard denomination requirements must be followed (Rs. 5 lakhs minimum)
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper issuance represents short-term funding for Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited with strong credit ratings (A1+). The listing provides institutional investors with an additional debt investment option in the housing finance sector. No changes to trading rules or compliance requirements for the broader market.
