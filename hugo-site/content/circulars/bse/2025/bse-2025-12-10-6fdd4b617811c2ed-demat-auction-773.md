## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 773 dated December 10, 2025. The circular lists 118 scrips from various companies along with their respective scrip codes and quantities being auctioned. This is a standard operational procedure for settlement of demat obligations where delivery shortages have occurred.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 773
- Settlement Date: December 10, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 118 securities across various sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share to 49,000 shares depending on the scrip
- Major quantities include: B.C. Power C (49,000 shares), Varun Bevera (10,000 shares), Worth Invest (8,614 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including power, banking, manufacturing, technology, pharmaceuticals, and more

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with outstanding delivery obligations in the listed scrips must participate in the auction process
- Members must settle their demat obligations as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Entities with short positions in listed scrips should ensure compliance with auction settlement requirements

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** 773

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational process affecting only specific scrips where delivery obligations remain unsettled.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to brokers and clearing members who have outstanding delivery obligations in the listed securities. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of failed delivery obligations.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal for general investors. Only impacts participants with open positions or delivery obligations in the specifically listed scrips. The auction quantities are relatively small for most scrips, indicating limited systemic delivery failures.
