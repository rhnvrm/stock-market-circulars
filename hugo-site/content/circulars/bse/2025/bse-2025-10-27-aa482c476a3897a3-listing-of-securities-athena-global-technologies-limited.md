## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 650,000 new equity shares of Athena Global Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 517429) effective from Tuesday, October 28, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 87 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 77 premium). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to a lock-in period until April 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 650,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE
- Issued at a premium of Rs. 77/- (total issue price: Rs. 87/-)
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 14061589 to 14711588
- Date of allotment: August 11, 2025
- Trading commences: October 28, 2025
- ISIN: INE576B01019
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities available for trading
- All 650,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until April 29, 2027
- The shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: April 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The listing is routine in nature and involves preferential allotment to promoters. The shares are locked in until April 2027, preventing any immediate market circulation or price impact.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive numbers. The preferential issuance strengthens promoter holding.

**Liquidity Impact**: None in the near term due to the lock-in period extending until April 2027.
