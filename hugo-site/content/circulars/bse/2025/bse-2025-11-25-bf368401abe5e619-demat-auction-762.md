## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 762 dated November 25, 2025. The circular lists 117 securities (scrip codes and names) along with their respective quantities available for auction. This is a routine operational notice for the exchange's settlement and clearing process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 762
- Date: November 25, 2025
- Total securities listed: 117 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 999,999 shares depending on the security
- Notable large quantity items include: GV Films Ltd (700,000 shares) and Murae Organi (999,999 shares)
- Major companies included: Infosys, Tata Steel, Tata Consumer, Bosch, Indian Hotels, Adani Power, Adani Green, HEG, Suzlon Energy

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This circular is informational. Members participating in the demat auction should:
- Review the list of securities and quantities available for auction
- Follow standard BSE auction procedures for Settlement No. 762
- Ensure compliance with exchange auction guidelines and timelines

## Important Dates

- **November 25, 2025**: Settlement date for demat auction No. 762

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational notice for demat auction settlement. The auction process helps resolve delivery shortages and maintains market integrity.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Relevant primarily to clearing members and trading members who have delivery obligations or wish to participate in the auction process.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Individual investors are generally not directly affected unless they are involved in specific delivery failures. The auction mechanism is part of the exchange's risk management and settlement assurance framework.
