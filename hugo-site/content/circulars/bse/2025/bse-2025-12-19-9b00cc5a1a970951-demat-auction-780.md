## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 780 dated December 19, 2025. This circular lists securities across 77 scrips that are part of the demat auction settlement process. The auction involves varying quantities across different companies, ranging from 1 share to 146,000 shares for Enbee Trade Ltd.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 780
- Settlement Date: December 19, 2025
- Total scrips covered: 77 different securities
- Highest quantity: Enbee Trade Ltd (146,000 shares)
- Other significant quantities include: Indiabulls L (29,300 shares), INDOWIND ENE (5,000 shares), AVANCE TECHN (5,000 shares), GEM Enviro M (3,200 shares), REAL ECO-ENE (1,792 shares), ALOK INDUSTR (1,571 shares)
- Companies include major names like Vedanta, Tata Elxsi, Delta Corp, Edelweiss Financial, IIFL Capital, Intellect Design, IRCON International, Ola Electric, Shriram Finance, Thyrocare, and Whirlpool

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine settlement auction publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants involved in these scrips must complete their settlement obligations as per BSE auction procedures
- Brokers and clearing members must ensure delivery of securities or fulfill auction obligations for the listed quantities
- Participants should refer to BSE's standard auction settlement mechanism and timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 19, 2025
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's settlement calendar and auction schedule for Settlement No. 780

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: This demat auction affects 77 scrips with varying settlement obligations. Market participants holding positions in these securities need to ensure timely settlement to avoid penalties.

**Market Impact**: The auction involves a wide range of companies across sectors. The highest quantity (146,000 shares of Enbee Trade) and other large positions may indicate settlement failures that require auction mechanisms. This is a routine market operation to ensure settlement integrity and does not indicate broader market concerns.

**Participant Impact**: Brokers and clearing members with outstanding obligations in these scrips must participate in the auction process or fulfill delivery requirements to complete the settlement cycle.
