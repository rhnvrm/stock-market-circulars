## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 773 dated December 10, 2025. The circular lists securities across various scrip codes where delivery obligations remain unfulfilled, requiring auction settlement. The list includes approximately 118 securities with quantities ranging from 1 share to 49,000 shares across diverse sectors.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 773
- Auction Date: December 10, 2025
- Total securities affected: Approximately 118 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple securities) to 49,000 shares (B.C. Power C - scrip code 537766)
- Major quantity auctions include Varun Beverages (10,000 shares), Worth Invest (8,614 shares), and CIAN Agro (3,691 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including power, banking, technology, manufacturing, and financial services

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed for Settlement No. 773
- Members should ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement
- Auction procedures as per BSE regulations must be followed

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: December 10, 2025 (Settlement No. 773)
- Auction execution date and timings to be confirmed as per BSE auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 118 securities with varying liquidity profiles. Large quantity auctions in B.C. Power C (49,000 shares) and Varun Beverages (10,000 shares) may impact prices temporarily.

**Operational Impact:** Members with delivery obligations must arrange securities or funds urgently. The broad range of securities affected indicates potential settlement issues across multiple trading members.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of affected securities may see temporary price volatility during auction proceedings. Members facing shortages may incur auction costs and penalties.
