## Summary

AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE08XP07332, Code: 977052) along with interest payment. The record date has been set for December 6, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value from December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED 13.30% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing on February 21, 2027
- ISIN: INE08XP07332
- BSE Code: 977052
- Action: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 66,666.73 per Debenture
- Segment: Debt Market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE are required to:
- Note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value from the effective date
- Update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 66,666.73 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: December 6, 2025 - Date to determine eligible debenture holders for part redemption and interest payment
- **Effective Date**: December 5, 2025 - Trading commences with reduced face value
- **Settlement Number**: DR-771/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting a single privately placed debt security. The part redemption will reduce the outstanding face value of the debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of December 6, 2025 will receive part redemption proceeds and interest payment. Post-redemption, each debenture will have a reduced face value of Rs. 66,666.73.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from December 5, 2025 onwards.
