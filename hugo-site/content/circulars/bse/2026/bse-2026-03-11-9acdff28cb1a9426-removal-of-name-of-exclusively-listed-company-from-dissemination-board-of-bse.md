## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260311-45 informing trading members that PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective Thursday, March 12, 2026. The removal is pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, and is triggered by the company exercising the exit option.

## Key Points

- PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited (CIN: U24232MP1992PLC007026) will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board w.e.f. March 12, 2026.
- The company is registered at Plot No. 323, Sector (E), Industrial Area, Sanwer Road, Indore - 452006.
- Reason for removal: Exit Option exercised by the company.
- Post-removal, shares of the company will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

The removal is carried out in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the framework for Exclusively Listed Companies on the Dissemination Board and their exit mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note that transactions in shares of PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited on the Dissemination Board will no longer be possible from March 12, 2026.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company (Mr. Ashish Jayant Shah, Director; panindia1992@gmail.com) or BSE (Mr. Parag Jain, Manager - Listing Compliance & Operations; Ph: 022-2272 8685; direct.listing@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **March 12, 2026 (Thursday)**: Effective date of removal of PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited from the BSE Dissemination Board.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and limited to investors or trading members who held or transacted in shares of PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited on the Dissemination Board. Since the company was exclusively listed on the Dissemination Board (not on the main exchange), the broader market is unaffected. Existing shareholders should be aware that the shares will no longer be available for trading on BSE's Dissemination Board following the removal.
