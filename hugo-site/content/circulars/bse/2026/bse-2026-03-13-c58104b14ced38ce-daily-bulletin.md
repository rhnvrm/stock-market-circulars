## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 237/2025-2026 dated March 13, 2026, listing record dates and book closure information for interest payments on privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs), commercial paper (CP) redemptions, secured NCD interest payments, and an equity dividend announcement. The bulletin covers multiple issuers across debt and equity segments.

## Key Points

- Banco Products (India) Ltd. has declared a Second Interim Dividend of Rs. 8.00 per share (400%) with record date March 19, 2026
- Bajaj Finance Limited has record dates for redemption of three commercial paper tranches in May-June 2026
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has record dates for redemption of four commercial paper tranches in May-June 2026
- Multiple companies have scheduled interest payment record dates for privately placed NCDs
- Ashoka Buildcon Limited has three NCD redemption record dates in April 2026
- Capri Global Capital Limited has multiple interest payment dates on Secured NCDs from May through August 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies and their registrars/transfer agents must ensure shareholder/debenture holder records are updated before the respective record dates
- Trading members should note record dates for ex-date calculations and settlement obligations
- Investors holding relevant securities should ensure demat accounts are updated before respective record dates to be eligible for dividends, interest, or redemption proceeds
