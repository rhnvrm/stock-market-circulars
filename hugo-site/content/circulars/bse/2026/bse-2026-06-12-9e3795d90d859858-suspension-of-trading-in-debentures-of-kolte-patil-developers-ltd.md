## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd. has fixed a record date of 15 June 2026 for the redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) under series KPDL-21-12-33-PVT DR-651/2026-2027 (ISIN: INE094I07064, BSE Code: 975276). Trading in these debentures is suspended with effect from 15 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in KPDL NCDs (ISIN INE094I07064, BSE Code 975276) is suspended from 15 June 2026.
- The suspension is triggered by the company fixing 15 June 2026 as the record date for NCD redemption.
- Debenture series affected: KPDL-21-12-33-PVT DR-651/2026-2027.
- All trading members are directed not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice is issued under existing BSE listing and debt segment operational guidelines governing trading halts ahead of corporate action record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in the specified KPDL NCD (ISIN INE094I07064) on and from 15 June 2026.
- Members should update their systems to block trades in BSE Code 975276 from the effective date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 12 June 2026 |
| Record Date (Redemption) | 15 June 2026 |
| No Dealings From | 15 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This suspension directly affects holders of KPDL NCDs under the specified series who wish to exit or transact on the secondary market. From 15 June 2026, no secondary market trades will be permitted. Investors holding these instruments should note that redemption proceeds will be processed as per the record date. Equity shareholders of KPDL are unaffected. The impact is confined to fixed-income investors in this specific debenture tranche.
