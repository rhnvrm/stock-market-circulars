## Summary

BSE has issued a notice prohibiting and suspending trading in the securities of Alstone Textiles (India) Limited (scrip code 539277) as an interim measure. The action follows a site visit by the Exchange during which the company was found to be non-existent at its registered office address.

## Key Points

- BSE conducted a site visit to Alstone Textiles (India) Ltd. and found the company absent at its registered office address.
- As an interim measure, trading in the company's securities is prohibited/suspended effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 539277 — Alstone Textiles (India) Ltd.
- All trading members and investors are advised to take note of the suspension.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. The action is taken under BSE's existing powers to protect market integrity and investor interests when a listed company cannot be located at its registered office.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease trading in scrip code 539277 from May 26, 2026 onwards.
- Investors holding positions in this security should be aware that trading is not permitted until further notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension:** Tuesday, May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension of trading will prevent all market participants from buying or selling shares of Alstone Textiles (India) Ltd. on BSE. Existing holders of the scrip will be unable to exit their positions until the suspension is lifted. This is a high-impact action as it signals potential serious compliance or governance issues with the company, including the possibility that it may be a shell or defunct entity.
