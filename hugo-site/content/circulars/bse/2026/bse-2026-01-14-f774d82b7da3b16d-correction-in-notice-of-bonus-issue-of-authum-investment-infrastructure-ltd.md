## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited has issued an undertaking to BSE regarding the listing of 67,93,80,400 bonus equity shares issued under Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. This is a correction to the original notice of bonus issue. The company confirms that bonus shares will be credited to the depository system by 12 PM on Wednesday, January 14, 2026 (next working day after record date) and will be available for trading from the beginning of day on Friday, January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be listed: 67,93,80,400 equity shares
- Bonus shares issued under Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Crediting deadline: 12 PM on January 14, 2026 (next working day after record date)
- Trading commencement: Beginning of day on January 16, 2026
- This is a correction to the original bonus issue notice
- Undertaking signed by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer Dipyanti Jaiswar

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is a correction notice reaffirming compliance with existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 requirements for bonus share issuance and listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must credit bonus shares to depository system by 12 PM on January 14, 2026
- Bonus shares must be available for trading from beginning of day on January 16, 2026
- Formal undertaking to BSE as per listing requirements under SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026 (12 PM)**: Deadline for crediting bonus shares to depository system (next working day after record date)
- **January 16, 2026 (BOD)**: Bonus shares available for trading (beginning of day)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The issuance of approximately 68 crore bonus shares will significantly increase the outstanding share count of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited, affecting share price, liquidity, and market capitalization calculations.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders on the record date will receive bonus shares in their demat accounts by January 14, 2026, increasing their shareholding proportionately without additional investment.

**Trading Impact**: Enhanced liquidity expected from January 16, 2026 when bonus shares become tradeable. Share price will adjust proportionately to reflect the increased share count.
