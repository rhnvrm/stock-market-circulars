## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (MFL-13.15%-21-2-26-PVT, ISIN: INE296Q07050, Code: 975415) of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED has fixed record date for debenture redemption and interest payment
- ISIN: INE296Q07050, BSE Code: 975415
- Debenture series: MFL-13.15%-21-2-26-PVT
- Trading suspension begins February 6, 2026
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these debentures from suspension date
- Notice reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE296Q07050) from February 6, 2026 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients holding or intending to trade these debentures
- No dealings permitted in the affected debenture series during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to the record date for corporate action processing.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN. Clients holding these debentures will be unable to exit their positions during the suspension period.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds based on holdings as of the record date. Liquidity is temporarily frozen for this instrument.
