## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,08,604 new equity shares of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) are permitted for trading effective Friday, March 06, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis, and carry ISIN INE483S01020.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807)
- **Total New Shares:** 14,08,604 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Nature:** Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- **ISIN:** INE483S01020
- **Pari-passu:** These shares rank equally with existing equity shares of the company
- **Trading effective from:** Friday, March 06, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice permitting trading members to trade in the newly listed securities upon conversion of rights-basis partly paid shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Friday, March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of new equity shares resulting from a rights issue conversion. The addition of 14,08,604 shares to the tradeable float of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) may have a minor dilutive effect on existing shareholders, but the conversion from partly paid to fully paid shares was a pre-announced corporate action. Market-wide impact is negligible; impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders in this specific scrip.
