## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Omnitech Engineering Limited (Exchange ticker: 544720) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on March 05, 2026, and will be included in the index effective at the open of trading on Monday, March 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited (ticker: 544720) is newly listed on BSE effective March 05, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on March 09, 2026
- This action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for March 05, 2026
- Reference Notice No. 20260304-44 for the original listing announcement
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following the IPO listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to include Omnitech Engineering Limited (544720) effective March 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Omnitech Engineering Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file (*.SDE) updated
- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544720 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of Omnitech Engineering Limited to the BSE IPO Index is a standard post-listing index inclusion event. Funds and products benchmarked or tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to incorporate this stock from March 09, 2026. The impact is limited to index-linked investment vehicles; broader market impact is minimal.
