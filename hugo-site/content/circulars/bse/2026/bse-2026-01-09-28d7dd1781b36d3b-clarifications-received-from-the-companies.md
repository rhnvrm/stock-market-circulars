## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three companies - Signatureglobal (India) Limited, Alkyl Amines Chemicals Limited, and GNA Axles Limited - in response to surveillance queries regarding significant increases in trading volume. All companies confirmed that the volume increases are market driven and that they have no pending price-sensitive information to disclose.

## Key Points

- Signatureglobal (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 543990) clarified that volume increase is purely market driven
- Alkyl Amines Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 506767) confirmed significant volume increase across exchanges is market driven
- GNA Axles Limited (Scrip Code: 540124) responded to queries about price movement
- All companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- No undisclosed price-sensitive information or pending announcements from any company
- Companies not aware of any specific information leading to volume/price movements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine surveillance compliance disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether all material disclosures under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 have been made
- Companies must declare if any price-sensitive information is pending disclosure

## Important Dates

- January 9, 2026: Date of clarifications submitted to BSE
- Surveillance queries received on January 8-9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - These are routine clarifications confirming no undisclosed material information

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard surveillance compliance process

**Investor Impact:** Provides assurance that volume increases are market driven without underlying undisclosed corporate developments

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates companies' adherence to disclosure obligations under SEBI (LODR) Regulations
