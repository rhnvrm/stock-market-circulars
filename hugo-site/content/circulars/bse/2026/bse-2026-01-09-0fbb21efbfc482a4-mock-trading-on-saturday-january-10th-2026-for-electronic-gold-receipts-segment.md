## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, January 10th, 2026. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems via ETI APIs. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released, with details to follow in a separate circular. All trades during the mock session will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, January 10th, 2026 for EGR segment
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released
- Trading members can test various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Trades executed during mock trading will not attract any obligations or create rights/liabilities
- Members must submit feedback by 3:00 PM on the same day
- Session covers all trading phases from login to post-closing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine system testing and familiarization exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on January 10th, 2026
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries
- Third-party vendors and in-house system developers should utilize this opportunity to test their trading applications

## Important Dates

- **January 10, 2026**: Mock Trading Session
  - Login: 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 11:00 AM - 11:08 AM
  - Matching Period: 11:08 AM - 11:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM - 2:00 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM
  - Closing: 2:00 PM - 2:10 PM
  - Post-Closing: 2:10 PM - 2:20 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 3:00 PM on January 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Provides testing opportunity for trading members to familiarize themselves with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 and verify their trading systems' functionality. Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can test various exceptional market scenarios including risk-reduction mode and trading halts. Participation is voluntary but encouraged for ensuring system readiness.
