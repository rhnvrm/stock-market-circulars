## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on designated securities effective June 08, 2026. The notice also details securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, securities exiting the framework, and updates to price bands, all effective from June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will be applicable from June 08, 2026 on all open positions as on June 05, 2026 and new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework effective June 04, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable on stage-elevated securities effective June 04, 2026
- Certain securities to continue in LT-ASM but transferred to T / XT / MT / TS trading groups effective June 08, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework effective June 04, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and is not an adverse action against the listed company
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and builds upon the LT-ASM framework originally established by Exchange notice no. 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) and subsequent revisions, including:
- Exchange notice no. 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018)
- Exchange notice no. 20201204-56 (December 04, 2020)
- Exchange notice no. 20240809-46 (August 09, 2024)
- Exchange notice no. 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)

The revised LT-ASM framework governs the imposition of heightened surveillance measures including enhanced margin requirements and lower price bands on securities exhibiting unusual market behavior.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers and members**: Must apply 100% margin on all open positions in LT-ASM-listed securities from June 08, 2026
- **Market participants**: Must note the group reclassification (T / XT / MT / TS) for affected securities effective June 08, 2026
- **All participants**: Must observe the lower price bands for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages from June 04, 2026
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 04, 2026 | Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; Lower Price Band applicable; Securities exiting LT-ASM framework take effect |
| June 05, 2026 | Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin |
| June 08, 2026 | 100% margin applicable on open positions as of June 05, 2026 and all new positions; Group transfer (T/XT/MT/TS) effective |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **high** market impact for securities placed under or elevated within the LT-ASM framework:

- **Liquidity impact**: 100% margin requirement will significantly increase the cost of holding or creating positions, likely reducing liquidity in affected securities
- **Price impact**: Lower price bands restrict downside trading range, potentially limiting price discovery
- **Group transfer impact**: Movement to T/XT/MT/TS groups typically implies trade-to-trade settlement, eliminating netting benefits and increasing settlement risk for participants
- **Investor caution**: While BSE clarifies that shortlisting is not adverse action against companies, inclusion in LT-ASM typically signals elevated risk and market concerns about the security
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework will have their prior price bands reinstated, subject to not being part of any other surveillance measure
