## Summary

BSE has issued a live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window buyback of shares of WIPRO LTD. The buyback is open from Thursday, 11th June 2026 to Wednesday, 17th June 2026, at an offer price of Rs. 250 per share, for up to 60,00,00,000 (60 crore) equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** WIPRO LTD (Symbol: WIPRO, ISIN: INE075A01022, Scrip Code: 507685)
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 250.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 60,00,00,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 2.00 per share
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Bid Offering Period:** June 11–17, 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Bid Modifications/Cancellations:** June 11–17, 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Give-up / Take-up:** June 11–16, 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Give-up (last day):** June 17, 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- **Take-up (last day):** June 17, 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is an operational schedule referencing existing OTB framework per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of investors is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- For new login IDs and password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet, contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational circular directed at trading members and custodians participating in the Wipro buyback via the BSE OTB acquisition window. The offer size of up to 60 crore shares at Rs. 250 represents a significant buyback transaction. Members across multiple investor categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, other QIBs, NIB, Company, Individual) are eligible to bid. The schedule is a standard OTB notice with no changes to existing participation rules; impact is primarily on trading operations teams managing order placement and settlement for the offer period.
