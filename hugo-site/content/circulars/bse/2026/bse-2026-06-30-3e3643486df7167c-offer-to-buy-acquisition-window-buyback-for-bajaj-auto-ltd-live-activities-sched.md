## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for BAJAJ AUTO LTD. The buyback window is open from Wednesday, 1st July 2026 to Tuesday, 7th July 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 12,000 per share for up to 46,94,000 equity shares. Trading Members and Custodian Members are requested to participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms.

## Key Points

- **Security:** BAJAJ AUTO LTD (Symbol: BAJAJ-AUTO, ISIN: INE917I01010, Scrip Code: 532977)
- **Offer Size:** Up to 46,94,000 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 12,000.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 1st July 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 7th July 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. for most activities; Take-up on the last day (7th July) closes at 4:00 p.m.
- Bid Offering, Modifications/Cancellations, and Give-up/Take-up sessions run from 1st July to 7th July 2026 (Give-up ends 6th July)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice follows existing OTB operational instructions per Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Bidding is available for investors holding shares in demat form only.
- Members may access the OTB module via iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/) platforms, or via leased line alternatives.
- For new login IDs or password resets, contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational notice primarily impacting Trading Members and Custodian Members of BSE who manage client portfolios in Bajaj Auto Ltd shares. The buyback at Rs. 12,000 per share for up to 46,94,000 shares represents a significant corporate action. Eligible investor categories include FIIs, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, NIBs, Company, and Individual investors. Members should prepare their systems and ensure UCC compliance ahead of the 1st July 2026 opening date.
