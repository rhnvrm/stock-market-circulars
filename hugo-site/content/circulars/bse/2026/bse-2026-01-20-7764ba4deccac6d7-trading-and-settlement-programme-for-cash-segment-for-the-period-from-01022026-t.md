## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Cash Segment covering February 1-28, 2026. The circular provides detailed schedules for both T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles, including settlement numbers, trading dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, and auction settlement timelines. The schedule includes 21 T+1 settlements (2526810 to 2526830) and 19 T+0 settlements (DR-505 to DR-523).

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers February 1, 2026 to February 28, 2026
- T+1 cycle: 21 settlements starting from settlement number 2526810
- T+0 cycle: 19 settlements starting from DR-505/25-26
- Pay-in timing: 10:30 a.m. with members required to submit instructions to Depositories/Banks by 10:30 a.m.
- OTR allocation window: up to 8:00 p.m. on T day
- OTR confirmation window: up to 7:30 a.m. on T+1 day
- Settlement holidays observed on February 7-8, 14-15, 21-22, and 28, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard monthly settlement schedule publication as per existing SEBI and BSE regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks latest by 10:30 a.m. on pay-in day
- Entry of 6A/7A data by members must be completed on specified dates
- Custodians must confirm 6A/7A data by specified deadlines
- OTR allocation (Give-up entry) to be completed by 8:00 p.m. on T day
- OTR confirmation (Take-up entry) by custodians to be completed by 7:30 a.m. on T+1 day
- Auction offers must be submitted on designated auction settlement dates
- Members should refer to Notice No. 20221223-17 for detailed OTR procedures

## Important Dates

- February 1, 2026: Programme start date (Settlement 2526810, DR-505)
- February 2, 2026: First pay-in/pay-out date with @ marking (special timing)
- February 19, 2026: Mahashivratri - trading day with next day settlement
- February 20, 2026: Pay-in/pay-out for February 18-19 trades
- February 28, 2026: Programme end date
- March 2, 2026: Extended pay-out date for settlement 2526829
- March 4, 2026: Final auction pay-out for settlement 2526830

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - All equity market participants including brokers, custodians, and clearing members must align their operational schedules with the published calendar. The schedule is critical for fund and securities management planning.

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine settlement schedule with no changes to existing T+1 and T+0 frameworks. Markets continue to operate under established settlement cycles.

**System Impact:** The @ symbol on certain dates (February 2 and 20) indicates special pay-in/pay-out timings that members must note for system configurations and operational readiness.
