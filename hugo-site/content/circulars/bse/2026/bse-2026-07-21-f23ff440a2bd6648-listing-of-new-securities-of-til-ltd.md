## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new equity shares of TIL Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505196) with effect from Wednesday, July 22, 2026. The new securities comprise 97,39,346 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis pursuant to the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up status.

## Key Points

- 97,39,346 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each become eligible for trading.
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid-up shares.
- ISIN: INE806C01018.
- Distinctive numbers: 70352057 to 80091402.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations enabling trading in newly converted fully paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional shares under ISIN INE806C01018 as eligible for trading from the effective date. No specific action required from investors beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for trading: Wednesday, July 22, 2026.
- Notice date: July 21, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of TIL Ltd. by 97,39,346 shares following conversion from partly paid to fully paid status. As the shares are pari-passu with existing equity, no differential trading treatment applies. Impact on market activity is expected to be limited and specific to TIL Ltd. shareholders and traders.
