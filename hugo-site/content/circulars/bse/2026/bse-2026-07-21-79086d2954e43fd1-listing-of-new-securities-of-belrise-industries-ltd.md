## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 7,72,72,727 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each of Belrise Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544405, ISIN: INE894V01022), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), are listed and admitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 7,72,72,727 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each allotted to QIBs under QIP
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 220/- per share
- Distinctive numbers: 889879193 to 967151919
- Date of allotment: July 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification for newly issued equity shares following a QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond noting the new listing date. Queries may be directed to Ms. Sahana Shetty at BSE (Tel: 022 2272 8600).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 17, 2026
- Listing and admission for trading: Wednesday, July 22, 2026
- Notice Date: July 21, 2026 (Notice No. 20260721-23)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Belrise Industries Limited by 7,72,72,727 shares, which rank pari-passu with existing shares. This reflects capital raised via QIP at Rs. 220/- per share and may have a modest dilutive effect on existing shareholders, though it is a routine post-QIP listing event with limited immediate market impact.
