## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD (ISIN: INE385W07034, Code: 975834) effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest on these debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from January 29, 2026
- Record date set for January 29, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest
- Debenture: DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT (INE385W07034)
- Notice Reference: DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action-driven trading suspension to facilitate proper settlement of put option redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must refrain from dealing in the specified debentures (INE385W07034, Code: 975834) from January 29, 2026
- All trading positions should be settled before the suspension date
- Members must note the record date for determining eligible debenture holders

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 29, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective from this date (no dealings)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. Trading activity in this security will cease, affecting liquidity for debenture holders who may wish to exit positions.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders with put options will have the opportunity to exercise redemption rights. Those holding positions on record date will receive interest payments. No trading possible from suspension date onward.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to block trades in this security and inform clients holding positions in these debentures about the suspension and corporate action.
