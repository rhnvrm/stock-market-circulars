## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260703-37 dated July 03, 2026, the MIRAE ASSET BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF will reopen for ongoing transactions—including subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities—on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme covered is MIRAE ASSET BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF, available in both Regular and Direct Plans (Growth option).
- Regular Plan - Growth: ISIN INF769K01QW0
- Direct Plan - Growth: ISIN INF769K01QX8
- The scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions.
- This follows an earlier notice (20260703-37 dated July 03, 2026) that presumably suspended or paused these transactions temporarily.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational update regarding platform availability of a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and participants dealing in this scheme should update their systems to allow ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions from the effective date. No specific filing or compliance action is required beyond operational readiness.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 23, 2026
- Effective Date for Reopening of Transactions: July 24, 2026
- Reference Notice: July 03, 2026 (Notice No. 20260703-37)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market-wide impact. It affects only investors, distributors, and members transacting in the MIRAE ASSET BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF scheme via the BSE StAR MF Platform, enabling them to resume ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including systematic investment facilities, from July 24, 2026.
