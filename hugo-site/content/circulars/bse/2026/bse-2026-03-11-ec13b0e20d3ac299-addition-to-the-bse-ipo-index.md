## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544723) to the BSE IPO Index. The company was listed on BSE effective March 11, 2026, and will be included in the BSE IPO Index at the open of trading on March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (ticker: 544723) listed on BSE on March 11, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on March 13, 2026 (Friday)
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated March 11, 2026
- Reference notice: No. 20260310-24

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following the listing of a new IPO on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF operators tracking the BSE IPO Index must incorporate SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED into their portfolios effective March 13, 2026
- Clients with queries may contact bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED listed on BSE
- **March 13, 2026 (Friday)**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This addition is a standard index inclusion following a new IPO listing. It primarily impacts investors and fund managers tracking the BSE IPO Index, who will need to adjust their portfolios to include SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED. The broader market impact is limited, but the inclusion may drive incremental buying interest in the stock from index-linked funds at the open on March 13, 2026.
