## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund has fixed **July 10, 2026** as the record date and ex-date for a face value split of units of the **Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF** (Scrip Code: 590107, ISIN: INF373I01023). Each unit with a face value of Rs. 10 will be split to Re. 1 per unit. A new ISIN for the split units will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF (Scrip Code: 590107) will undergo a face value split from Rs. 10/- per unit to Re. 1/- per unit.
- Record Date and Ex-Date are both set for **July 10, 2026**.
- The ETF will trade with the new face value effective **July 10, 2026** (DR-669/2026-2027).
- Existing ISIN INF373I01023 will be replaced; the new ISIN will be announced separately.
- Issued under DR-669/2026-2027 by Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

The face value of Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF units is being reduced from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 per unit through a split. This is a corporate action by Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund communicated to BSE under its listing obligations. The new ISIN post-split will be separately notified.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Re. 1 per unit effective July 10, 2026.
- Members should monitor the subsequent BSE notice for the new ISIN number to ensure accurate settlement and record-keeping.
- Positions and margins related to this ETF may need to be recalculated based on the new face value.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 30, 2026 |
| Record Date | July 10, 2026 |
| Ex-Date / Settlement | July 10, 2026 |
| New Face Value Effective | July 10, 2026 |
| New ISIN Announcement | To be notified separately |

## Impact Assessment

The face value split of Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF from Rs. 10 to Re. 1 will result in a 10x increase in the number of units held by investors, with a proportional decrease in the NAV per unit, keeping the overall investment value unchanged. This improves affordability and liquidity of the ETF on the exchange. Trading members and clearing systems must update unit counts, face value references, and await the new ISIN before July 10, 2026 to avoid settlement discrepancies.
