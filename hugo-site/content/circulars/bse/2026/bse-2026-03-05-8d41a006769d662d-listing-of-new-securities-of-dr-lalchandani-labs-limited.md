## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 43,04,000 new equity shares of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited (Scrip Code: 541299) issued on a rights basis at par are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 43,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis are now listed on BSE
- ISIN: INE871Z01013
- Distribution Numbers: 4333069 to 8637068
- Shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these new securities are now available for trading. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 2, 2026
- Trading Commencement Date: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 43,04,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited via a rights issue at par value of Rs. 10/-. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no differential rights or dilution concerns beyond the standard rights issue dilution already known to the market.
