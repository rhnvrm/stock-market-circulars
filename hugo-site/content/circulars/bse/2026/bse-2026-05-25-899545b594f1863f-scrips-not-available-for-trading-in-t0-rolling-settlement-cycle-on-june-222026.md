## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that four equity scrips will not be available for trading under the T+0 rolling settlement cycle on Monday, June 22, 2026. Normal T+1 settlement trading in these scrips is unaffected.

## Key Points

- Four scrips are excluded from T+0 rolling settlement on June 22, 2026.
- The restriction applies only to the T+0 settlement cycle; standard settlement trading continues normally.
- Affected scrip codes and symbols: 132343 (TVSMOTOR#), 112599 (ADANIENT#), 140115 (LTTS#), 132175 (CYIENT#).
- Notice issued by BSE Trading Operations department (Notice No. 20260525-22).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is an operational notice restricting T+0 settlement availability for specific scrips on a specific date.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure that orders in the four listed scrips are not placed under the T+0 rolling settlement cycle on June 22, 2026. Orders intended for same-day settlement should be rerouted or deferred.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026 (Monday):** T+0 rolling settlement unavailable for TVSMOTOR#, ADANIENT#, LTTS#, and CYIENT#.

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and their clients holding or intending to trade in TVS Motor Company, Adani Enterprises, L&T Technology Services, or Cyient under the T+0 settlement mechanism will need to plan accordingly for June 22, 2026. The impact is limited in scope — confined to one trading day and four scrips — but requires proactive system or order-routing adjustments to avoid failed or rejected T+0 trades.
