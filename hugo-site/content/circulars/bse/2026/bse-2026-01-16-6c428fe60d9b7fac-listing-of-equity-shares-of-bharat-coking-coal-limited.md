BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited on the exchange. This circular provides details about the listing of the company's equity shares, marking its entry into the public markets.

- Bharat Coking Coal Limited equity shares are being listed on BSE
- The listing enables public trading of the company's shares
- This is a new listing announcement for the exchange
- The company operates in the coal sector
