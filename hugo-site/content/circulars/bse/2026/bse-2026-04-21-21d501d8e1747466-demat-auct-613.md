## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in a Demat Auction under Rolling Settlement No. DR-613/2026-27. Auction offer entry is scheduled for April 21, 2026, with pay-in on April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction is for securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-613/2026-27 in the Equity segment
- Offer entry window: 02:00 PM to 02:45 PM on April 21, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 PM to 03:00 PM on April 21, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 03:00 PM to 03:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 PM to 05:30 PM
- Auction pay-in (securities): Wednesday, April 22, 2026 at 08:00 AM
- Members and custodians must complete securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership Structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The previous restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the specified session timings
- Members and custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities and complete pay-in within depository cut-off timings on April 22, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should consult notices regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips before submitting offers
- No corrections will be permitted after punching; utmost care must be exercised

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Auction offer entry (02:00 PM – 02:45 PM), Auction matching (02:45 PM – 03:00 PM), Delivery statement (03:00 PM – 03:30 PM), Final reports (03:35 PM – 05:30 PM)
- **April 22, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 08:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational auction notice affecting trading members who have short-delivered securities in Rolling Settlement DR-613/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they short-delivered introduces greater flexibility under the Uniform Membership Structure. Members with outstanding auction obligations must act promptly within the defined windows to avoid penalties or settlement failures.
