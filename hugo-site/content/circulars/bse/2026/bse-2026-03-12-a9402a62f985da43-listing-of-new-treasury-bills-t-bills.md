## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G GROUP – DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment, effective March 13, 2026. The T-Bills have maturities of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective March 13, 2026 under G Group – Debt Instruments
- Market lot for all three securities is 1
- Trading will be suspended two (2) working days prior to each T-Bill's Maturity/Redemption Date (Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Contact for clarifications: 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued government Treasury Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: the listed T-Bills will not be available for trading two (2) working days before their respective maturity/redemption dates.

| 805191 | IN002025Y495 | 182TB10926 | March 13, 2026 | September 10, 2026 |
| 805192 | IN002025Z492 | 364TB11327 | March 13, 2026 | March 11, 2027 |

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing of government-issued Treasury Bills, which are low-risk, short-term debt instruments. Trading members dealing in the debt segment should update their systems to reflect the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers. The trading halt two days prior to maturity is a standard exchange practice to ensure orderly settlement.
