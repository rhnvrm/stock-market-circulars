## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that LTIMindtree Ltd (Scrip Code: 540005) is officially changing its company name to **LTM Limited**. As a result, the Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective April 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company LTIMindtree Ltd is being renamed to **LTM Limited**
- Scrip Code **540005** remains unchanged
- Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System changes from existing identifier to **LTM**
- Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System changes to **LTM**
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company has formally informed BSE of its name change. BSE is updating the official Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name in the BOLT Plus trading system to reflect the new corporate identity. No changes are made to the scrip code or segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their internal systems and references to reflect the new scrip name **LTM LIMITED** and Scrip ID **LTM** on BOLT Plus System from April 17, 2026
- Order entries and reports referencing the old name should be updated to avoid confusion post-effective date

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **April 17, 2026**: Effective date for name change and BOLT Plus System identifier update

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative change affecting how the stock is identified on BSE's BOLT Plus trading system. The underlying scrip code (540005) is unaffected, so existing positions, orders, and holdings remain valid. Trading members and market participants who reference LTIMindtree by scrip name or abbreviated identifier in their systems will need to update to LTM LIMITED / LTM. The change reflects a corporate rebranding and has no direct impact on trading, settlement, or compliance obligations beyond identifier updates.
