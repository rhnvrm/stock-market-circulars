## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's standard demat auction settlement process for handling short deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and brokers with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Securities must be tendered as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants should refer to BSE's standard auction guidelines for bidding and settlement timelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 5, 2026
- Settlement No.: 832

## Impact Assessment

This auction affects brokers and clearing members with short delivery obligations in 81 listed scrips. The largest exposures are in LEADING LEASING (51,001 units), VINNY OVERSEAS (10,100 units), RELIANCE INDUSTRIES (3,437 units), and BHARAT COKING COAL (4,189 units). Routine in nature; no broader market impact expected. Counterparties with short positions in these scrips will face auction prices which may differ from market prices, potentially resulting in additional costs.
