## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to all Futures and Options contracts on Life Insurance Corporation of India (Derivative Asset Code: LICL, Equity Scrip Code: 543526) in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The adjustments are triggered by LIC's declaration of a Final Dividend of Rs. 10/- per equity share for the financial year 2026-27, with June 25, 2026 set as the Record Date.

## Key Points

- LIC has declared a Final Dividend of **Rs. 10/- per equity share** for FY 2026-27
- Record Date: **June 25, 2026**; Ex-date: **June 25, 2026**
- Exchange will make necessary adjustments to all available F&O contracts on LICL on **June 24, 2026**
- For Options: Rs. 10/- will be deducted from all strike prices generated at end of day June 24, 2026
- For Futures: Adjusted futures price = Daily MTM settlement price on June 24, 2026 minus Rs. 10/-
- Adjusted prices apply as base rate/previous close for trading from June 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are made in pursuance of BSE Notice No. 20180710-26 (Review of Adjustment of corporate actions for stock options) and in line with SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members in the Equity Derivatives Segment are informed to account for the adjusted strike prices and futures prices effective June 25, 2026
- Members requiring further details should contact their respective relationship manager at BSE

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Exchange makes adjustments to all F&O contracts on LICL; reference MTM settlement price determined
- **June 25, 2026**: Ex-date and Record Date; adjusted strike prices and futures base rates become effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

All open Futures and Options positions on LICL will be automatically adjusted by BSE to reflect the Rs. 10/- dividend. Options strike prices will be reduced by Rs. 10/-, and futures contract base prices will similarly be adjusted downward by Rs. 10/- from the June 24 MTM settlement price. This is a standard mechanical adjustment and does not alter the economic value of existing positions but traders should be aware of the revised price levels effective June 25, 2026.
