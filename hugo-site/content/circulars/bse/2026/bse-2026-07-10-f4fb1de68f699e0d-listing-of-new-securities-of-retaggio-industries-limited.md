## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 16,00,000 new equity shares of Retaggio Industries Limited (Scrip Code - 544391), each of face value Rs. 10/- issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- per share to a non-promoter on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company and are subject to a lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 16,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- each, total issue price Rs. 26/- per share
- Shares allotted to a non-promoter entity on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 15566161 to 17166160
- Date of Allotment: 05/03/2026
- ISIN: INE0KWJ01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Entire allotment of 16,00,000 shares is under lock-in until 19/02/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed shares as tradable from the effective date, subject to the applicable lock-in restrictions on the allotted shares until 19/02/2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 05 March 2026
- Listing/Trading effective date: 13 July 2026 (Monday)
- Lock-in expiry: 19 February 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases Retaggio Industries' free-float equity base by 16,00,000 shares, though these shares remain locked in until February 2027, limiting immediate market impact. The move reflects conversion of previously issued warrants into equity, a standard capital-raising and shareholding formalization event with limited near-term trading impact given the lock-in.
