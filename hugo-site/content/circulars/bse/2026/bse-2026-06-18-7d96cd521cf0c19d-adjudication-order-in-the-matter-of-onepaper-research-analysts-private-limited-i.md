## Summary

SEBI has passed an Adjudication Order (No. Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32446 dated June 16, 2026) against OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited, a SEBI-registered Research Analyst holding Registration No. INH000008093. BSE has disseminated this notice to all Research Analysts and investors via Notice No. 20260618-11.

## Key Points

- SEBI Adjudication Order No. Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32446 dated June 16, 2026 passed against OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited
- Entity is a registered Research Analyst with SEBI Registration No. INH000008093
- The order is available on the SEBI website (https://www.sebi.gov.in) and attached to this BSE notice
- BSE has requested all Research Analysts and investors to take note of the order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations governing Research Analysts.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Research Analysts are advised to take note of this adjudication order
- Investors dealing with or relying on research from OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited should review the full order available on the SEBI website
- The order details specific violations and penalties; entities should review to ensure their own compliance with applicable Research Analyst regulations

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Date of SEBI Adjudication Order (Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32446)
- **June 18, 2026**: Date of BSE Notice (20260618-11) disseminating the order

## Impact Assessment

This adjudication order is directed specifically at OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited and does not have broad market-wide operational impact. However, it serves as a regulatory signal to the research analyst community regarding compliance expectations. Investors who may have relied on research or recommendations from this entity should review the full order to understand the nature of violations found by SEBI. The enforcement action reinforces SEBI's oversight of the research analyst segment.
