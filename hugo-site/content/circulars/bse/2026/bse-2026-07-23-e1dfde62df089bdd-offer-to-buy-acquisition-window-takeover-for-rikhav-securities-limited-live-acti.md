## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of RIKHAV SECURITIES LIMITED, to be conducted via the iBBS platform from Friday, 24th July 2026 to Thursday, 6th August 2026.

## Key Points

- Security Name: RIKHAV SECURITIES LIMITED; Symbol: RIKHAV; ISIN: INE0CFH01028; Scrip Code: 544340
- Offer Size: Up to 99,55,920 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1600 shares; Face Value: Rs. 5.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 47.75 per share
- Offer opens Friday, 24th July 2026 and closes Thursday, 6th August 2026
- Bid Offering Period, Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 24th July to 6th August 2026, 9.15 a.m. to 3.30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 24th July to 5th August 2026, 9.15 a.m. to 3.30 p.m.
- Give-up on last day (6th August 2026): 9.15 a.m. to 3.30 p.m.
- Take-up on last day (6th August 2026): 9.15 a.m. to 4.00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual
- Access via iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/), RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/), and Extranet (https://member.bseindia.com/) platforms, with leased line alternatives available

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the offer to buy under the existing Acquisition Window (Takeover) framework, referencing Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017 for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Members and Custodians requiring new login IDs or password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet should contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Opening Date: Friday, 24th July 2026
- Closing Date: Thursday, 6th August 2026
- Give-up/Take-up window: 24th July to 5th August 2026
- Final day give-up: 6th August 2026 (9.15 a.m. – 3.30 p.m.)
- Final day take-up: 6th August 2026 (9.15 a.m. – 4.00 p.m.)

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/procedural notice targeted at Trading Members and Custodian Members facilitating the takeover offer for Rikhav Securities Limited shareholders. It has direct relevance to shareholders and market participants engaging in the offer but does not carry broader systemic or market-wide impact.
