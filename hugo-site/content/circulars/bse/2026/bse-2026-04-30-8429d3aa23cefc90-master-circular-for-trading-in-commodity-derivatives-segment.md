## Summary

BSE has released Notice No. 20260430-22, a Master Circular for Trading in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. This circular consolidates all previously issued notices and circulars by the Trading Operations Department into a single reference document, enabling members to access all applicable rules and guidelines at one place.

## Key Points

- BSE has compiled a Master Circular covering all relevant notices and circulars for the Commodity Derivatives Segment
- The circular is issued by the Trading Operations Department
- It serves as a comprehensive single-point reference for exchange members operating in the Commodity Derivatives Segment
- Attachments include: Commodity Derivatives Master Circular and Part III Annexure File Format

## Regulatory Changes

This circular does not introduce new regulations. It is a compilation of all previously issued notices and circulars from the Trading Operations Department related to the Commodity Derivatives Segment, providing members with consolidated access to existing rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in the Commodity Derivatives Segment should refer to this Master Circular as the primary reference for applicable trading rules and guidelines
- Members should review the attached annexure (Part III Annexure File Format) for file format specifications relevant to the segment

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 30 April 2026
- No specific implementation deadlines mentioned; this is a reference consolidation circular

## Impact Assessment

The Master Circular has a moderate operational impact as it simplifies regulatory navigation for members active in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. By consolidating all applicable notices and circulars into one document, it reduces compliance complexity and improves accessibility. No new trading restrictions, margin changes, or instrument modifications are introduced.
