## Summary

Aritas Vinyl Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as required under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated January 22, 2026. The company has a total of 19,688,680 equity shares with promoter and promoter group holding 27.99% (5,511,472 shares) and public holding 72.01% (14,177,208 shares). All shares are fully paid up with no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 19,688,680
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 5,511,472 shares (27.99%)
- Public holding: 14,177,208 shares (72.01%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 8
- Number of public shareholders: 1,104
- Dematerialized shares held by promoters: 5,511,472 (100% of promoter holding)
- Dematerialized shares held by public: 14,177,208 (100% of public holding)
- All shares carry equal voting rights

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

The company has confirmed compliance with Regulation 31 requirements by declaring:
- No partly paid up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants issued
- No depository receipts against shares
- No employee stock options (ESOPs) outstanding
- No locked-in shares
- No pledged shares by promoters
- No non-disposal undertakings
- No other encumbrances on promoter shares
- No differential voting rights shares
- No significant beneficial owners beyond declaration

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: January 22, 2026
- Regulation: Filed under Reg. 31(1)(a)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows:
- Stable promoter holding at 27.99% with no encumbrances
- Healthy public float of 72.01% meeting minimum public shareholding requirements
- 100% dematerialization of all equity shares
- Clean shareholding structure with no pledges, lock-ins, or outstanding convertible instruments
- Total of 1,112 shareholders indicating reasonable distribution
