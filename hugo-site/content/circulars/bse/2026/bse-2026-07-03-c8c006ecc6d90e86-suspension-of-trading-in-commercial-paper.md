## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that several companies have fixed Record Dates for redemption of their Commercial Paper (CP) instruments. Trading members are advised not to deal in the listed Commercial Paper of these companies from the specified record/ex-dates onward, as part of routine debt segment settlement operations ahead of maturity.

## Key Points

- The notice lists numerous Commercial Paper ISINs across various issuers with their respective Record Dates, Ex Dates, and Settlement DR (Designated Record) numbers.
- Issuers include HT Media Ltd, Redington Limited, Axis Finance Limited, 360 ONE Prime Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Vivriti Capital Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited, Grasim Industries Ltd, Tata Power Co. Ltd, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited, Capri Global Capital Limited, Krazybee Services Ltd, and Network 18 Media & Investments Ltd (list truncated in source document).
- Record Dates for the listed CPs range from 06 July 2026 to early August 2026, coinciding with Ex Dates in most cases.
- Settlement numbers (e.g., 665/2026-2027, 667/2026-2027, 677/2026-2027 through 684/2026-2027) are referenced for each batch of instruments.
- No attachment was provided with this notice; all details are contained within the notice text itself.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for the Debt segment, implementing standard pre-redemption trading suspension procedures for Commercial Paper.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper ISINs of the listed companies on and after the respective Record/Ex Dates mentioned in the notice, to ensure orderly settlement ahead of redemption.

## Important Dates

- HT Media Ltd (ISIN INE501G14ER5): Record/Ex Date 06 July 2026
- HT Media Ltd (ISIN INE501G14ES3): Record/Ex Date 08 July 2026
- Redington Limited: Record/Ex Date 22 July 2026
- Axis Finance Limited: Record/Ex Date 23 July 2026
- 360 ONE Prime Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited: Record/Ex Date 24 July 2026
- Vivriti Capital Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited, Grasim Industries Ltd, Tata Power Co. Ltd: Record/Ex Date 27 July 2026
- 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited: Record/Ex Date 28 July 2026
- Capri Global Capital Limited (INE180C14888): Record/Ex Date 29 July 2026
- Krazybee Services Ltd, Capri Global Capital Limited (INE180C14730): Record/Ex Date 30 July 2026
- Network 18 Media & Investments Ltd and subsequent entries: early August 2026 (details truncated in source)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to the Debt segment and specifically to Commercial Paper instruments nearing redemption, not equity shares. The suspension is a standard pre-maturity administrative measure affecting only holders and traders of these specific debt instruments. No impact on equity trading, company operations, or broader market sentiment is expected.
