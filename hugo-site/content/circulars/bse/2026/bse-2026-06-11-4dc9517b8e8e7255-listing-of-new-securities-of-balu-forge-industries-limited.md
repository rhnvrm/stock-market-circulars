## Summary

BSE has notified that 20,60,000 new equity shares of Balu Forge Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531112, ISIN: INE011E01029) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, June 12, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 360/- each (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 350/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 20,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 350/- (issue price Rs. 360/-)
- Issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders via warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 117621901 to 119681900
- Date of Allotment: March 6, 2026
- Trading commences: June 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 12, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to the newly listed shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 6, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement:** June 12, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (5,60,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 117621901–118181900):** December 31, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (15,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 118181901–119681900):** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific event with limited broader market impact. The preferential allotment increases Balu Forge Industries' total equity share count by 20,60,000 shares. The majority of the new shares (15,00,000) are locked in until end of 2027, limiting near-term free-float impact. The remaining 5,60,000 shares are locked until end of 2026. Market participants should note the expanded share capital when assessing dilution effects.
