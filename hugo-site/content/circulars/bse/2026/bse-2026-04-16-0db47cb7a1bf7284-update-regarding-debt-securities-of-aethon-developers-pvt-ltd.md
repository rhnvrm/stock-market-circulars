## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Aethon Developers Pvt Ltd, continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20251216-25 dated 16th December 2025. Trading members are informed that Scrip Code 976099 (ISIN: INE15S307023) will be available for trading under revised terms as detailed in Annexure I, effective Wednesday, April 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of earlier notice No. 20251216-25 dated December 16, 2025
- Scrip Code **976099** with ISIN **INE15S307023** (Aethon Developers Pvt Ltd) is subject to revised trading terms
- Revised terms are specified in Annexure I attached to the notice
- Effective date for trading under revised terms: **Wednesday, April 20, 2026**
- Segment: Debt | Department: DCS-Listing

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Aethon Developers Pvt Ltd will trade under revised terms as outlined in Annexure I of the notice. The specific nature of the revisions (e.g., coupon rate, maturity date, or other terms) is detailed in the annexure referenced but not reproduced in the main notice body.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the revised terms applicable to Scrip Code 976099 / ISIN INE15S307023 before executing trades from April 20, 2026 onwards

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a targeted impact on trading members dealing in the debt securities of Aethon Developers Pvt Ltd (Scrip Code 976099). The revision of terms for a specific ISIN is a routine debt segment operation but requires trading members to update their records and ensure trades executed from April 20, 2026 reflect the new terms. The broader market impact is low, as this affects a single private limited company's debt instrument.
