## Summary

BSE has listed 1,46,45,000 new equity shares of Kamdhenu Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 543747, ISIN: INE0BTI01037) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are available for trading on BSE with effect from Thursday, May 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,46,45,000 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5.80/- per share (issue price Rs. 6.80/-)
- Allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 314355001 to 329000000
- Date of allotment: March 30, 2026
- All 1,46,45,000 shares are subject to lock-in until November 20, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange from May 14, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 1,46,45,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 314355001–329000000) must be observed until November 20, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 30, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 14, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 1.46 crore new equity shares to the tradeable pool of Kamdhenu Ventures Limited, representing a dilution to existing shareholders. However, since all newly issued shares are subject to lock-in until November 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is expected to be moderate; the dilution effect is already factored in from the warrant issuance stage. Retail investors and traders should note the scrip code (543747) and the pari-passu ranking of new shares with existing equity.
