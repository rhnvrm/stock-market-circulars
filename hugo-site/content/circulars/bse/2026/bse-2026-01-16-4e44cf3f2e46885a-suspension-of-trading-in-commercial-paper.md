## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (Scrip Code: 730922, ISIN: INE975F14C82) effective January 19, 2026. The suspension is due to the commercial paper reaching its redemption/maturity date on January 20, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730922
- ISIN: INE975F14C82
- Instrument Type: Commercial Paper (KMIL-20-1-26-CP)
- Suspension effective from January 19, 2026
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Reference: DR-801/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified Commercial Paper from January 19, 2026
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be opened in this instrument

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 19, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 20, 2026**: Redemption/Maturity date (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument that has reached maturity. Standard procedure for debt instruments approaching redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure compliance by not executing trades in this specific commercial paper. No broader market implications as this is an expected maturity event.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors holding this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date as per original terms.
