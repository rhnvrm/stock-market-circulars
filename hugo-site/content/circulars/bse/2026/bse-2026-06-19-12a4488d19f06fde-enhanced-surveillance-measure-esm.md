## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260619-27. Securities meeting ESM criteria will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement effective June 23, 2026, and will be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade segment. Stage II securities will additionally face a 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin w.e.f. June 23, 2026 on all open positions as on June 22, 2026 and new positions from June 23, 2026.
- Certain securities will be moved to a lower stage (Stage I) under the ESM framework w.e.f. June 22, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. June 23, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 22, 2026.
- Securities moving out of the ESM framework w.e.f. June 22, 2026 are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework as established under earlier notices dated June 02, 2023, July 18, 2023, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, October 04, 2024, and July 25, 2025. The current update adds new securities to the framework and moves certain existing securities to lower stages or out of the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all open and new positions in ESM-listed securities from June 23, 2026.
- Trading in qualifying securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules.
- Stage II securities must be traded within the 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction.
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework will revert to the band applicable before ESM inclusion, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective; securities exit from ESM framework effective.
- **June 23, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin requirement effective on all open positions (as on June 22, 2026) and new positions; Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade shift effective.

## Impact Assessment

Securities included under ESM face significantly restricted trading conditions — mandatory 100% margins increase the capital requirement for traders holding or initiating positions, reducing speculative activity. The shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement obligations. Stage II securities face the added constraint of a 2% daily price band under Periodic Call Auction, which severely limits intraday price discovery and liquidity. Market participants should review the attached annexures to identify affected securities and adjust their risk and margin frameworks accordingly.
