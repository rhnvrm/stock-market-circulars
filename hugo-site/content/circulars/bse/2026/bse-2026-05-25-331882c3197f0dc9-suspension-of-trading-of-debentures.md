## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260525-29 suspending trading in two debt securities of Hari and Company Investments Madras Private Ltd with effect from May 26, 2026. The suspension is due to redemption or an application for delisting made by the company (Reference: DR-638/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Two debenture series of Hari and Company Investments Madras Private Ltd are suspended from trading on BSE.
- Scrip Code 730439 (ISIN: INE01HV14AC7) is suspended.
- Scrip Code 730344 (ISIN: INE01HV14AA1) is suspended.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective May 26, 2026.
- The suspension is due to redemption or the company's application for delisting.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an operational notice under existing listing and trading regulations governing debt segment securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 730439 (INE01HV14AC7) and Scrip Code 730344 (INE01HV14AA1) from May 26, 2026 onwards.
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in these securities post the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only trading members who hold or deal in these two specific debenture series of Hari and Company Investments Madras Private Ltd, a private company. Market-wide impact is minimal. Members with open positions or pending trades in these securities must settle or wind down exposure before May 26, 2026. The suspension is likely permanent given the redemption or delisting context.
