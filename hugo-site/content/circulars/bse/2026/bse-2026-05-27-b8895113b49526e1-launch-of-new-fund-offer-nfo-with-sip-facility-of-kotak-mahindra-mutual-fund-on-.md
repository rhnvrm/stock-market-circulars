## Summary

Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund is launching the **Kotak Nifty Alpha Low-Volatility 30 Index Fund** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on May 29, 2026, and closes on June 12, 2026. SIP facility is available. Six scheme variants are offered across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options. Tentative allotment is scheduled for June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Fund category: Equity (Index Fund tracking Nifty Alpha Low-Volatility 30)
- Six scheme variants: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- ISINs: INF174KA1A52 (Regular-Growth), INF174KA1A60 (Regular-IDCW Payout), INF174KA1A78 (Regular-IDCW Reinvestment), INF174KA1A29 (Direct-Growth), INF174KA1A37 (Direct-IDCW Payout), INF174KA1A45 (Direct-IDCW Reinvestment)
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled; CC with 'N' flag will be reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions remain in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to funds receipt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice for a new product launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by **6:00 PM on June 16, 2026** for allotment eligibility
- Members must report CC with 'N' flag to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Cheque and OTM payment instructions must result in clear funds received in ICCL account by the June 16, 2026 deadline

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **June 12, 2026**: NFO subscription closes
- **June 16, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account
- **June 17, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new fund offer announcement with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant to BSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, particularly those with clients interested in passive equity index funds. Members need to ensure operational processes align with the T+1 fund initiation deadline and the June 16 clearing deadline to avoid transaction cancellations and client allotment failures.
