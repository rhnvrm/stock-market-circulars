## Summary

BSE has notified a revision of the applicable price band (daily circuit filter) percentages for 22 listed scrips, effective July 9, 2026. The changes are part of the Exchange's ongoing surveillance measures under the Department of Surveillance (DOSS) and apply to the Equity segment.

## Key Points

- 22 scrips have had their price bands revised to new percentage limits ranging from 5% to 20%.
- Notable revisions include Faalcon Concepts Ltd (10%), Diksha Polymers Ltd (20%), Leapfrog Engineering Services Ltd (20%), and Liotech Industries Ltd (10%).
- The majority of scrips (17 of 22) have been set to a 5% price band, indicating tighter volatility controls.
- The revised bands apply uniformly to the equity segment for the listed scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the revised price band limits to the listed scrips from the effective date. Any clarification can be sought by writing to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 9, 2026
- Notice Date: July 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision tightens or adjusts daily price movement limits for these 22 scrips, which affects intraday and daily trading volatility allowances. Traders and investors holding or trading these scrips should note the updated circuit limits to avoid order rejections and to recalibrate trading strategies around the new bands.
