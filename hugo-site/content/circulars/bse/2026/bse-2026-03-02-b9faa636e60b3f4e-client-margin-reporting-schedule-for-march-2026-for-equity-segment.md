## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Segment for March 2026. The circular specifies, for each trade date, the last date by which members must submit client margin reports and the corresponding penalty date if reporting obligations are not met.

## Key Points

- Applies to all BSE members trading in the Equity Segment.
- For each trade date, members have a window of approximately 6–8 calendar days to complete client margin reporting.
- Failure to report by the last date triggers a penalty on the following business day.
- The schedule covers trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026, with reporting deadlines extending into early April 2026.
- Issued jointly by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule circular under the existing client margin reporting framework for the Equity Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the specified last date of margin reporting.
- Non-compliance by the deadline will attract a penalty effective from the penalty date listed for that trade date.
- Members should ensure their back-office and reporting systems are aligned with the schedule below.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium operational impact on all equity segment members and their compliance teams. While it introduces no new rules, adherence to the schedule is mandatory to avoid financial penalties. Members with high trading volumes across multiple trade dates must track multiple concurrent reporting deadlines. The schedule accounts for market holidays, with penalty dates skipping non-trading days (e.g., gaps around Holi and other March holidays).
