## Summary

BSE (Notice No. 20260410-40) has published clarifications received from companies where significant price and/or volume movements were observed. The Exchange wrote to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Replies from 48 companies are attached, while responses from 17 additional companies are still pending.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in multiple scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies
- 48 companies have submitted their replies (attached as Replies1 through Replies10)
- 17 companies have not yet responded and their replies are awaited
- Notice falls under the Equity segment, Category: Company related, Department: DOSS

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a surveillance-driven disclosure under BSE's existing framework for monitoring abnormal price and volume movements in listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet submitted their clarifications must respond to BSE promptly
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies for relevant disclosures before making investment decisions in the mentioned scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 10, 2026
- Replies attached as of April 10, 2026; pending replies from 17 companies are expected to follow

## Impact Assessment

This is an informational disclosure notice. The replies provide context around recent price and volume anomalies in 48 scrips, helping investors make informed decisions. Companies still awaiting reply may face further regulatory scrutiny if they do not respond. No trading restrictions are imposed by this notice; however, continued non-response could escalate to stricter surveillance measures.
