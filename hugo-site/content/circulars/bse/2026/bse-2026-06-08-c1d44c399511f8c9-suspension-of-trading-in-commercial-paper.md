## Summary

BSE has issued a notice instructing trading members to suspend dealings in the commercial paper of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited (Scrip Code: 731457, ISIN: INE128M14BE9) with effect from June 9, 2026, as the instrument reaches its redemption date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper scrip code 731457 (LTMR-10-6-26-CP) of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited is maturing on its redemption date.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper effective June 09, 2026.
- Reference number: DR-647/2026-2027.
- ISIN: INE128M14BE9.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon maturity of a commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

All trading members of BSE must cease trading in the commercial paper of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited (ISIN: INE128M14BE9, Scrip Code: 731457) from June 9, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension; commercial paper redemption date.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only the specific commercial paper instrument of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited and is a standard procedure upon instrument maturity. Existing holders should ensure redemption proceeds are settled as per exchange norms.
