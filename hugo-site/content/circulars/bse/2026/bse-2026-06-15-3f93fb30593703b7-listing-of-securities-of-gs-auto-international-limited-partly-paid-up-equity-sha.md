## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of 2,90,29,160 partly paid-up equity shares of GS Auto International Limited, issued on a rights basis, effective Wednesday, June 17, 2026. The shares will trade under the 'X' Group with scrip code 890238 and scrip ID GSAILPP.

## Key Points

- **2,90,29,160 equity shares** of Rs. 5/- each, partly paid-up at Rs. 2.50/- per share, issued on rights basis
- Effective listing date: **June 17, 2026**
- Scrip Code: **890238** | Scrip ID: **GSAILPP** | Group: **X**
- ISIN: **IN9736H01014** | Face Value: Rs. 5/- | Paid-up value: Rs. 2.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- (Rs. 5/- paid on application; remaining Rs. 5/- payable in one or more subsequent calls at Board's discretion)
- Distinctive numbers: 14514581 to 43543740
- Date of allotment: June 11, 2026
- Dividend entitlement is proportionate to the amount paid-up
- Temporary ISIN IN8736H01023 is suspended per SEBI Circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The suspension of temporary ISIN IN8736H01023 follows existing SEBI guidelines on activation of ISIN for additional issues (SEBI Circular dated August 2, 2012 and September 11, 2012).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new scrip code (890238) and ISIN (IN9736H01014) for the partly paid-up shares
- The temporary ISIN IN8736H01023 must not be used for trading as it stands suspended
- Future call payments by shareholders will be determined by the company's Board

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Date of allotment of partly paid-up shares
- **June 17, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing circular with limited market-wide impact. It affects holders of GS Auto International Limited rights entitlements who subscribed to the rights issue. The shares trade in the 'X' Group, indicating they are partially paid securities with future call obligations. Investors should be aware that dividend entitlement is proportionate to the paid-up amount (Rs. 2.50 out of Rs. 5 face value) until full payment is made.
