## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures of PIRAMAL FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE140A07799, Code: 975446) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: PFL-9.35%-27-2-26-PVT (ISIN: INE140A07799)
- BSE Security Code: 975446
- Record date set: February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: February 12, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-819/2025-2026
- Issuer: PIRAMAL FINANCE LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure where trading is suspended ahead of redemption to ensure proper settlement and payment to rightful holders as on record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures (INE140A07799) from February 12, 2026
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for this security from the suspension date
- Members must inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and upcoming redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Purpose Date**: February 12, 2026 (redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of the specific PIRAMAL FINANCE debenture series (INE140A07799). This is a standard corporate action with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of record date (February 12, 2026) will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment. Post-suspension, secondary market trading will not be possible.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update systems and ensure no trades are executed in this security from February 12, 2026. The 2-day notice period allows adequate time for system updates and client communication.

**Liquidity Impact**: Complete liquidity freeze for this specific debenture series from suspension date, which is expected as the instrument is reaching maturity and being redeemed.
