## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260511-50 informing trading members that Kshitij Investments Ltd (Scrip Code: 503626) has officially changed its corporate name to Manglam Global Corporations Limited, effective May 15, 2026. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated but remain as MANGLAM.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 503626 previously traded as Kshitij Investments Ltd
- New company name: Manglam Global Corporations Limited
- BOLT Plus System Scrip ID: MANGLAM (unchanged)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: MANGLAM (unchanged)
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The company name change has been registered and communicated to BSE by the company. BSE is formally notifying all trading members of the updated corporate identity. No changes to scrip code, segment, or trading identifiers beyond the legal name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new company name — Manglam Global Corporations Limited — for Scrip Code 503626. No additional action is required as the BOLT Plus System identifiers (SCRIP ID and abbreviated name) remain MANGLAM.

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **May 15, 2026**: Effective date of name change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Impact is minimal. The name change is purely administrative; the scrip code (503626), BOLT Plus SCRIP ID (MANGLAM), and abbreviated name (MANGLAM) all remain unchanged. Trading members will see no disruption to order entry or settlement workflows. Investors and members should note the updated legal name for documentation and compliance purposes only.
