## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 203/2025-2026 dated January 23, 2026 announces record dates and book closure dates for various corporate actions across multiple companies. The bulletin covers dividend payments, commercial paper redemptions, non-convertible debenture (NCD) redemptions and interest payments, and a rights issue.

## Key Points

- Aarti Industries has two commercial paper redemption dates: February 11, 2026 and February 25, 2026
- Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone has multiple NCD-related record dates throughout 2026 for interest payments and redemptions
- Advani Hotels & Resorts announces first interim dividend of Rs. 1.0000 per share (50%) with record date January 30, 2026
- Apollo Tyres has interim dividend record date of February 10, 2026
- Arunis Abode Limited has record date January 30, 2026 for rights issue of equity shares
- Berar Finance Limited has two interest payment dates on February 10, 2026 for privately placed NCDs
- All securities will continue trading (Trading flag = 1) except Apollo Tyres scrip 100877 (Trading flag = 0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities on the specified record dates to be eligible for respective corporate actions
- Companies must process dividend payments, interest payments, and redemptions as per the announced schedule
- Trading members should note the trading flags for affected securities

## Important Dates

**January 2026:**
- January 30, 2026: Record date for Advani Hotels interim dividend and Arunis Abode rights issue

**February 2026:**
- February 10, 2026: Apollo Tyres interim dividend; Berar Finance interest payments
- February 11, 2026: Aarti Industries CP redemption
- February 20, 2026: Adani Ports NCD partial redemption and interest
- February 25, 2026: Aarti Industries CP redemption

**March 2026:**
- March 28, 2026: Adani Ports NCD interest payment

**May 2026:**
- May 12, 2026: Adani Ports NCD interest and redemption
- May 15, 2026: Adani Ports NCD interest payment

**June 2026:**

**October 2026:**
- October 16, 2026: Adani Ports NCD partial redemption and interest

**November 2026:**
- November 12, 2026: Adani Ports NCD redemption and interest

**December 2026:**
- December 25, 2026: Adani Ports NCD interest payments (two securities)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine daily bulletin providing standard corporate action notifications.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Investors holding affected securities need to be aware of record dates to claim dividends, interest, and participate in corporate actions. Missing record dates will result in loss of entitlements.

**Trading Impact:** Minimal - Most securities continue normal trading. Only Apollo Tyres scrip 100877 has trading flag 0, while its duplicate listing 500877 continues trading.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Clearing corporations, depositories, and brokers must process multiple corporate actions across different security types (equity, commercial papers, NCDs) throughout the year.
