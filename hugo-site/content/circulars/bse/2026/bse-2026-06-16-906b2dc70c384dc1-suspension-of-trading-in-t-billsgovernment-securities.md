## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the 91-day T-bill with scrip code 805193 (91TB190626, ISIN IN002025X505) will mature on its redemption date. Trading in this security is suspended with effect from June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 805193
- Security: 91TB190626
- ISIN: IN002025X505
- Trading suspended effective: June 17, 2026
- Reason: Maturity/redemption of the T-bill
- Reference: DR-653/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued when a government security reaches its redemption date.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid T-bill (scrip code 805193, ISIN IN002025X505) with effect from June 17, 2026. Members must ensure no transactions are executed in this security on or after the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 16, 2026
- Suspension Effective Date: June 17, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine maturity-driven suspension affecting a single 91-day T-bill. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per normal settlement procedures. No discretionary trading decisions are required by market participants.
