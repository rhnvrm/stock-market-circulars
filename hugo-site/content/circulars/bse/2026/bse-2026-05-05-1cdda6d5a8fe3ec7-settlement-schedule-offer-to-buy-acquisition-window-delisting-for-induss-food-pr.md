## Summary

ICCL has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Delisting) pertaining to INDUSS FOOD PRODUCTS & EQUIPMENTS LIMITED. The acquisition window will operate from 06 May 2026 to 12 May 2026, with obligations downloadable on 13 May 2026 and final settlement on 14 May 2026 under Settlement No. 2026046.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Delisting | Settlement No.: 2026046
- Bid window open from 06/05/2026 to 12/05/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available on both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Delisting)" and Settlement No. 2026046 in the depository system
- Members & Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to file MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under the existing framework established by Notice No. 20200528-32 dated 28 May 2020 regarding the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance of the BSE OTB Bidding window closure and the Custodian confirmation window
- **CDSL**: Clients must transfer securities directly to the early pay-in account of the member/custodian
- **NSDL**: Securities must be earmarked/blocked in the client's account; clients must execute transfer instructions to the member's NSDL pool account using the correct market type and settlement number

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | 06 May 2026 |
| Bid End Date | 12 May 2026 |
| Obligation Download | 13 May 2026 |
| Settlement Date | 14 May 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to stock brokers (Members) and Custodians participating in the delisting acquisition window for INDUSS FOOD PRODUCTS & EQUIPMENTS LIMITED. The impact is limited in scope — affecting only those holding or transacting in the company's shares during the delisting process. Participants must coordinate early pay-in through the correct depository channels to avoid rejection of bids. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
