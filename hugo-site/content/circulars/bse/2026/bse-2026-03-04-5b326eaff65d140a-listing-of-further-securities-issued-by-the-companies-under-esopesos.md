## Summary

BSE has listed additional equity shares issued by 7 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These securities are admitted for trading effective Thursday, March 5, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 7 companies have listed fresh securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All shares are effective for trading from March 5, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed shares
- Total new shares span a range of 6,000 (CG Power) to 716,314 (Infosys)
- Infosys contributes the largest tranche with 716,314 shares (ISIN: INE009A01021)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing norms.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from market participants. The notice is informational, advising all participants to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective distribution number ranges.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 5, 2026 (Thursday)
