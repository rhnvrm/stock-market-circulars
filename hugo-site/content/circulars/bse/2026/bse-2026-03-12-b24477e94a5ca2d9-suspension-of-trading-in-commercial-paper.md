## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260312-38 informing trading members that 13 companies have fixed 16 March 2026 as the record date for redemption of their respective commercial papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial paper of these companies effective 16 March 2026 (DR-840/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- 13 companies have set 16 March 2026 as the record date for commercial paper redemption
- Trading in commercial paper of all listed companies is suspended from 16 March 2026
- Notice reference: DR-840/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations
- Segment: Debt Market

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the commercial paper of the listed companies from 16 March 2026 onwards
- All 13 companies' commercial papers are covered under notice DR-840/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 16 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Commercial Paper for all 13 companies

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects commercial paper instruments of 13 companies across diverse sectors including financial services (ICICI Securities, Bajaj Financial Securities, SBICAP Securities, Northern Arc Capital, UGRO Capital), housing finance (Mahindra Rural Housing Finance, Aditya Birla Housing Finance), diversified conglomerates (Adani Enterprises), and others (Time Technoplast, 360 ONE Prime, Muthoot Capital Services, Chalet Hotels, Mahindra Lifespace Developers). The impact is limited to debt market participants holding or intending to trade these specific commercial paper instruments. Equity trading in these companies is unaffected.
