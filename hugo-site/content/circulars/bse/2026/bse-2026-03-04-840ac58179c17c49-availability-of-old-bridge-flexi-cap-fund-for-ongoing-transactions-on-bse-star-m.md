## Summary

BSE has informed members that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform. This reopening is effective from March 04, 2026, following the earlier notice (No. 20260212-36).

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026
- Both Regular and Direct plan variants are available across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also enabled alongside regular subscription and redemption
- This notice supersedes/follows up on BSE Notice No. 20260212-36

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction availability for an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. BSE members and distributors transacting through BSE StAR MF may now process orders for the listed OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 — Fund reopened for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260212-36 (February 12, 2026) — original closure/suspension notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact. Investors and distributors who were previously unable to transact in OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform can now resume normal activity. The reopening restores access to six scheme variants (three Regular Plan, three Direct Plan) covering Growth and IDCW options. No broader market or regulatory implications.
