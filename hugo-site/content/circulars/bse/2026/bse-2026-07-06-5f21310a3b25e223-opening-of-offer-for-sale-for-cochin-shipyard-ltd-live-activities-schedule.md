## Summary

BSE has released Notice No. 20260706-39 dated 06 Jul 2026, announcing the live activities schedule for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of Cochin Shipyard Limited. - BSE notified trading members about the live activities schedule for the OFS window of Cochin Shipyard Limited.
- The notice is operational in nature, intended to inform trading members and system participants of the timeline for OFS-related activities.
- No additional document or attachment accompanies this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an operational/procedural notice supporting the ongoing OFS process for Cochin Shipyard Limited.

Trading members and system participants should align their systems and processes with the published live activities schedule to ensure timely and compliant participation in the OFS.

## Important Dates

Notice issued and effective 06 Jul 2026, coinciding with the opening of the Offer for Sale for Cochin Shipyard Limited.

The notice has limited direct market impact but is operationally important for trading members and market participants involved in the Cochin Shipyard OFS, ensuring smooth execution of the offer window on BSE's trading systems.
