## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) has notified that certain securities listed in Annexure_GSM shall be moved to their respective higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective May 25, 2026. The move triggers progressively stricter trading restrictions depending on the GSM stage assigned to each security.

## Key Points

- Securities in the attached Annexure will be moved to higher GSM stages effective **May 25, 2026**
- Surveillance actions intensify from Stage I through Stage IV
- All affected securities will be subject to settlement on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off allowed
- Trading members are advised to take adequate precautions while trading in these securities
- References earlier GSM framework notices dated February 23, 2017; March 3, 2017; July 20, 2018; November 29, 2019; and November 17, 2023

## Regulatory Changes

The GSM framework categorizes securities into four stages based on surveillance concerns, with each stage attracting progressively stricter measures:

| Stage | Surveillance Actions |
|-------|---------------------|
| I | 100% applicable margin rate; price band of 5% or lower |
| II | Trade-for-trade with 5% or lower price band; Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value to be deposited by buyers |
| III | Trade-for-trade with 5% or lower price band; trading permitted once a week (every Monday/1st trading day); ASD of 100% of trade value by buyers |
| IV | Trade-for-trade with 5% or lower price band; trading permitted once a week (every Monday/1st trading day); ASD of 100% of trade value by buyers; **no upward price movement** |

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the GSM stage assignment of securities in the Annexure and apply corresponding surveillance restrictions from May 25, 2026
- **Buyers**: Must deposit the applicable Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) — 50% of trade value for Stage II, 100% for Stages III and IV
- **Settlement**: All affected securities must be settled on a trade-to-trade basis; no netting off is permitted
- Members may refer to the GSM FAQ at: http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/graded_surveil_measure.aspx?expandable=6
- Queries can be directed to: bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date**: May 25, 2026 — securities moved to higher GSM stages and new surveillance actions become applicable

## Impact Assessment

Movement to higher GSM stages significantly restricts trading activity in affected securities. Stage III and IV securities are particularly impacted, as trading is limited to once per week (Mondays), buyers must deposit 100% of trade value as ASD, and Stage IV securities cannot move upward in price. These measures are designed to protect investors from potentially manipulated or fundamentally weak securities, but effectively reduce liquidity and may cause sharp price adjustments. Market participants holding or considering positions in GSM-listed securities should review the Annexure to assess exposure.
