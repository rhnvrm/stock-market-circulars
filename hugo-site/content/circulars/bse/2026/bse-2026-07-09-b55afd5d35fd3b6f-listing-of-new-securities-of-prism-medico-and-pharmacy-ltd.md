## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 75,00,000 new equity shares of Prism Medico and Pharmacy Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512217), each of face value Rs. 10/- issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- (issue price Rs. 20/-), allotted on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 10, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 75,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- (issue price Rs. 20/- per share)
- Shares allotted to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Distinctive numbers: 6,063,429 to 13,563,428
- Date of allotment: 05/05/2026
- ISIN: INE730E01016
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Shares are subject to lock-in: 25,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 6,063,429–8,613,428) locked-in until 31/01/2028; 49,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 8,613,429–13,563,428) locked-in until 31/01/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for securities issued via preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the newly listed shares as tradeable from the effective date; shares under lock-in are not available for trading until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 05/05/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Friday, July 10, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (49,50,000 shares): 31/01/2027
- Lock-in expiry (25,50,000 shares): 31/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing modestly increases the tradeable float of Prism Medico and Pharmacy Ltd. once lock-in periods lapse, but a significant portion of the newly allotted shares remain locked-in for 6-19 months, limiting immediate market impact. The event is administrative in nature and primarily relevant to shareholders and market participants tracking the company's capital structure.
