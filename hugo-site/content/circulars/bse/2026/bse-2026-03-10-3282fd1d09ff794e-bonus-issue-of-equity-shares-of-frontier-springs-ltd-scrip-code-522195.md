## Summary

Frontier Springs Ltd (BSE Scrip Code: 522195) has announced a bonus issue of equity shares with a record date of March 13, 2026. The company will issue 2 (two) bonus equity shares of Rs.10 each for every 1 (one) equity share of Rs.10 held. Trading in the equity shares will be on an ex-bonus basis effective March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 2:1 (2 bonus shares for every 1 share held)
- Face value of bonus shares: Rs.10 each
- Record date: March 13, 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: March 13, 2026
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 7,877,022 equity shares
- Allotment date: March 16, 2026
- Reference: DR-839/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action circular notifying trading members of an upcoming bonus issue and ex-bonus date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the ex-bonus date of March 13, 2026 and ensure all transactions in Frontier Springs Ltd equity shares are processed accordingly.
- Trades executed on or after March 13, 2026 will be on an ex-bonus basis.

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Bonus Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Allotment Date:** March 16, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders of Frontier Springs Ltd holding shares as of the record date (March 13, 2026) will receive 2 additional equity shares for every 1 share held, effectively tripling their shareholding. The share price is expected to adjust proportionally on the ex-bonus date. A total of 7,877,022 new equity shares will be allotted on March 16, 2026, increasing the total paid-up share capital of the company. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the ex-bonus adjustment.
