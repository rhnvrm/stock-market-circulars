## Summary

IFCI Limited has responded to BSE's surveillance department regarding significant increases in trading volume and price movement of its securities. The company has confirmed that it has no undisclosed information or events that would explain the abnormal market activity. This clarification was provided in response to BSE's surveillance letter dated January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- BSE surveillance department queried IFCI Limited about significant volume increases across exchanges
- Company confirmed no unpublished price-sensitive information in its possession
- No material events or developments that would affect price/volume behavior
- Standard surveillance response to unusual market activity
- Letter issued by Company Secretary & Compliance Officer Priyanka Sharma

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has fulfilled its obligation to respond to exchange surveillance queries
- Disclosure confirms compliance with continuous disclosure requirements under SEBI regulations
- No further action required from the company at this time

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Date of BSE surveillance letter (Reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/863)
- **January 13, 2026**: Date of company's clarification response

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine clarification that does not provide new material information to the market. The company's statement that there are no undisclosed events suggests the volume and price movements may be due to general market conditions or investor sentiment rather than company-specific developments.

**Operational Impact**: None. No changes to company operations or business activities.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral. Investors are informed that the recent trading activity is not based on any material undisclosed information, which provides transparency but does not offer new insights into company fundamentals or prospects.
