## Summary

BSE has notified inclusion of certain securities under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II effective June 04, 2026. Stage I securities attract a minimum 50% margin (capped at 100%), while Stage II securities attract a minimum 100% margin. The measures apply to all open positions as on June 03, 2026 and all new positions created from June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I are placed under ST-ASM Stage I with a margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (maximum capped at 100%)
- Securities listed in Annexure I are also placed under ST-ASM Stage II with a margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (maximum capped at 100%)
- Effective date for both stages: June 04, 2026
- Applies to open positions as on June 03, 2026 and new positions from June 04, 2026
- ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company
- On exit from the framework, a scrip's price band is reinstated to its pre-ASM level, unless it remains under another surveillance measure

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of earlier ASM framework revisions notified via:
- Exchange notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Exchange notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Exchange notice 20201204-56 dated December 04, 2020
- Exchange notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Exchange notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework to the newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must collect enhanced margins (50% for Stage I; 100% for Stage II) from clients on all affected securities from June 04, 2026
- **Market Participants**: Must account for increased margin obligations on open positions held as of June 03, 2026
- **Clearing Members**: Must ensure margin compliance for both open and new positions in the listed securities
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins
- **June 04, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I will face at least 50% margin requirements, significantly increasing the cost of carrying positions and likely reducing trading volumes and liquidity. Stage II securities face the maximum 100% margin, effectively requiring full upfront payment of the position value, which may sharply curtail speculative activity. The measure affects both existing holders (open positions as of June 03) and new entrants from June 04 onwards. The actual list of affected securities is in the attached Annexure and would need to be referenced to identify specific stock-level impact.
