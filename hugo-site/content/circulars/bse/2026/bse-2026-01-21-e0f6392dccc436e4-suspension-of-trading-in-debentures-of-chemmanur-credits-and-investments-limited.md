## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to CCIL-10.75%-13-2-26-NCD (ISIN: INE051307AZ7, Code: 939687)
- Record date set for January 29, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from January 29, 2026 onwards
- Notice issued under reference DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 29, 2026
- Trading members are required to note and comply with the suspension directive
- No buy or sell transactions should be executed for the mentioned NCD series

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders and potential traders of the specific NCD series only. The suspension is temporary and related to routine corporate action of redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: High for existing debenture holders who need to be aware of the record date for receiving redemption proceeds and interest. No trading opportunity available after January 29, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified security code (939687) from the effective date to ensure compliance.
