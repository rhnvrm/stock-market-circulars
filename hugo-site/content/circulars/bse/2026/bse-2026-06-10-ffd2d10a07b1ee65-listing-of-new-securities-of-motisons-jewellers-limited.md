## Summary

BSE has listed 54,00,000 new equity shares of Motisons Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 544053, ISIN: INE0FRK01020) with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 17/- each (face value Rs. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 16/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 54,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- (issue price Rs. 17/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 996360001 to 1001760000
- Date of allotment: 26 February 2026
- Trading permitted from 11 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares under an existing preferential warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. No separate compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 26 February 2026
- **Effective date for trading:** 11 June 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** 15 December 2026 (all 54,00,000 shares under lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 54,00,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Motisons Jewellers Limited, though all newly listed shares remain under lock-in until 15 December 2026 and are therefore not freely tradeable in the near term. The preferential issue at Rs. 17/- per share reflects the warrant conversion price. Impact on the broader market is minimal; existing shareholders should note the incremental dilution to the equity base.
