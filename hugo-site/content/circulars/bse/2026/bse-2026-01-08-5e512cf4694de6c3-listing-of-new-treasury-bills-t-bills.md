## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective January 9, 2026. The T-Bills have tenures of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with maturity dates in April 2026, July 2026, and January 2027 respectively.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills to be listed on BSE starting January 9, 2026
- All securities classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size is 1 for all three instruments
- Trading will be suspended two working days before maturity date
- Contact provided for clarifications: 2272 8352/5753/8597

## New Instruments Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenure |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|--------|
| 805163 | IN002025X406 | 91TB090426 | April 9, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805164 | IN002025Y404 | 182TB90726 | July 9, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805165 | IN002025Z401 | 364TB70127 | January 7, 2027 | 364 days |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to the maturity/redemption date
- The two-day suspension excludes bank holidays
- Trading members should ensure their systems are updated with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **April 7, 2026**: Last trading day for 91TB090426 (T-2 from April 9)
- **April 9, 2026**: Maturity date for 91TB090426
- **July 7, 2026**: Last trading day for 182TB90726 (T-2 from July 9)
- **July 9, 2026**: Maturity date for 182TB90726
- **January 5, 2027**: Last trading day for 364TB70127 (T-2 from January 7)
- **January 7, 2027**: Maturity date for 364TB70127

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification regarding the listing of government-backed treasury bills. The addition of these debt instruments provides more short-term investment options for debt market participants but does not affect equity trading or overall market operations. The notice is informational in nature and requires no action from equity market participants.
