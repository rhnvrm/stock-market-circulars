## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260701-28 dated July 01, 2026, the SUMMIT EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND of Invesco Mutual Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme was previously closed and will now reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Ongoing transactions include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.
- Six scheme/plan variants are covered under Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational follow-up to BSE Notice No. 20260701-28 dated July 01, 2026, reopening the scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members dealing in mutual fund units via BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems/records to allow ongoing transactions in the listed ISINs effective the reopening date, and inform relevant desks/clients accordingly.

## Important Dates

- July 01, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260701-28 issued.
- July 27, 2026: SUMMIT EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund platform operational notice with minimal market impact. It affects only members and investors transacting in this specific Invesco scheme via BSE StAR MF and has no bearing on listed equity trading or broader market activity.
