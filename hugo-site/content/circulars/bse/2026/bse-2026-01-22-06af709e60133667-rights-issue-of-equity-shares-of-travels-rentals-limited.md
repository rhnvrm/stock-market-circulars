## Summary

TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544242) has announced a rights issue with record date fixed as 28th January 2026. Eligible shareholders will receive 1 equity share of Rs.10/- face value for every 1 equity share held, at an issue price of Rs.15/- per share (including premium of Rs.5/-). Trading in the company's equity shares will be conducted on ex-rights basis from 28th January 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544242)
- Rights Ratio: 1:1 (1 new equity share for every 1 existing equity share)
- Face Value: Rs.10/- per share
- Premium: Rs.5/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs.15/- per share (payable in full at application)
- Record Date: 28th January 2026
- Ex-Rights Date: 28th January 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-807/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities - Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue in accordance with existing securities regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED equity shares on ex-rights basis from 28th January 2026
- Eligible shareholders must pay the entire issue price of Rs.15/- per share at the time of application
- Trading members are required to take note of the ex-rights trading arrangement

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 22nd January 2026
- **Record Date:** 28th January 2026
- **Ex-Rights Trading Begins:** 28th January 2026 (Settlement No. DR-807/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Existing shareholders holding shares on the record date will be entitled to subscribe to additional equity shares on a 1:1 basis at Rs.15/- per share, which represents a premium of Rs.5/- over face value. Full payment required at application.

**Trading Impact:** From 28th January 2026, all trades in TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED will be executed on ex-rights basis, meaning buyers after this date will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue.

**Market Impact:** The rights issue will result in dilution of existing shareholding for those who do not subscribe. The 1:1 ratio indicates significant equity expansion.
