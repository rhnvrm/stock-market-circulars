## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Jaihind Synthetics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 514312) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated effective April 22, 2026, reflecting the new name Jaihind Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Jaihind Synthetics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 514312) is officially changing its name to **Jaihind Industries Limited**.
- The SCRIP ID on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new company name.
- The abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will remain **JAIHIND** (unchanged).
- Change is effective from **April 22, 2026**.
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

In accordance with the company's intimation to the Exchange, the following identifiers on the BOLT Plus System will be revised:

| Field | Old Value | New Value |
|---|---|---|
| Company Name | Jaihind Synthetics Ltd. | Jaihind Industries Limited |
| SCRIP ID (BOLT Plus) | JAIHIND (Jaihind Synthetics) | JAIHIND INDUSTRIES LIMITED |
| Abbreviated Name | JAIHIND | JAIHIND |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the updated SCRIP ID on the BOLT Plus System effective April 22, 2026.
- Any internal systems or references using the old company name should be updated accordingly.
- No change in scrip code (514312) or abbreviated name; only the full SCRIP ID designation is amended.

## Important Dates

- **April 16, 2026** – Date of BSE notice (Notice No. 20260416-16).
- **April 22, 2026** – Effective date for the change in SCRIP ID and company name on BOLT Plus System.

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is low. This is a routine corporate action involving a company name change. The scrip code (514312) remains unchanged and the abbreviated ticker JAIHIND on the BOLT Plus System is also retained, ensuring continuity in day-to-day trading. Trading members and investors need only be aware of the updated full company name and SCRIP ID designation for record-keeping and compliance purposes. No changes to trading lot, price band, or other trading parameters are indicated.
