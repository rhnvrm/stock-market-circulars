## Summary

BSE has listed 69,43,691 new equity shares of Borosil Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 502219) issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters. The shares will be available for trading from January 8, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 69,43,691 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 534/-
- Issue price: Rs. 535/- per share
- Allotted to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: October 17, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE666D01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 138179134 to 145122824

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. All 69,43,691 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in provisions until July 15, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The shares represent an increase in equity capital for Borosil Renewables Limited and will be subject to lock-in restrictions for approximately six months from the trading commencement date. The issuance to Non Promoters at Rs. 535/- per share will add to the company's freely tradable equity base after the lock-in period expires.
