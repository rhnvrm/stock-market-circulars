## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by four companies on the BSE Debt Segment, effective May 13, 2026. The securities are admitted to trading in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four fresh Commercial Paper listings admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment from May 13, 2026
- All securities trade only in dematerialised form with a standard lot of 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size: 1 paise
- All CPs carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (A1+ from ICRA, CRISIL, CARE, or IND)
- Queries to be directed to BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard admission-to-trading notice under existing BSE debt segment listing framework for privately placed Commercial Papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Trades must be executed in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should note the respective scrip codes and ISIN numbers for order entry
