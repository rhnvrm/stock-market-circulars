## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for securities satisfying criteria for inclusion under Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 23, 2026, on both open positions as on June 22, 2026, and new positions created from June 23, 2026. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- The enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on June 22, 2026, and all new positions from June 23, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip will be reinstated to its pre-ASM level, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued further to Exchange notice no. 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024) regarding revision of the ASM framework. No new framework changes are introduced; this is a periodic application of the existing ST-ASM framework to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) are collected on all open and new positions in the shortlisted securities from June 23, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I (attached to the original notice) for the full list of securities under each stage.
- Members may direct clarification queries to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Cut-off date for existing open positions subject to new margin requirements.
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin collection on open positions and all new positions in shortlisted securities.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I will face a minimum 50% margin requirement, while those in Stage II securities will face 100% margin — effectively requiring full upfront capital for new positions. This is likely to reduce speculative activity in the affected scrips and may cause short-term price volatility or liquidity reduction. The framework is a standard surveillance tool and does not reflect fundamental issues with the listed companies. Price bands will revert to pre-ASM levels once securities exit the framework, provided they are not under any other surveillance measure.
