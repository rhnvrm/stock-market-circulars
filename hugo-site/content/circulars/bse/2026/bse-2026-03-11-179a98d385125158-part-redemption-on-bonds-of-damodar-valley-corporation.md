## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Damodar Valley Corporation (DVC) has fixed a record date of 13 March 2026 for the purpose of part redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading in the bonds will be with a reduced face value of Rs. 4,00,000 per bond effective from 13 March 2026 under settlement DR-839/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Two DVC bond series are affected: DVC-14A-9.3%-30-3-27-A-PVT (INE753F07046, Code: 972730) and DVC-14B-9.3%-30-3-27-B-PVT (INE753F07038, Code: 972731)
- Both series carry a coupon rate of 9.3% and mature on 30 March 2027
- Record date for part redemption and interest payment is 13 March 2026
- Reduced face value post-redemption: Rs. 4,00,000 per bond
- Effective date for trading at reduced face value: 13 March 2026
- Settlement reference: DR-839/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification pursuant to existing BSE listing and debt market operational guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value effective from 13 March 2026 for both bond series
- All transactions in these bonds on and after the effective date must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 4,00,000 per bond

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 13 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** 13 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-839/2025-2026
- **Notice Date:** 11 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt corporate action impacting holders of two privately placed DVC bond series. The part redemption reduces the outstanding face value of each bond to Rs. 4,00,000, reflecting a partial return of principal alongside scheduled interest payment. Impact is limited to bondholders and trading members dealing in these specific instruments on BSE's debt market segment.
