## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to trading members that NCL Seccolor Ltd, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. Following removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will no longer be available for trading on the Dissemination Board of BSE.

## Key Points

- NCL Seccolor Ltd (CIN: U21029TG1986PTC007033) is being removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective March 11, 2026.
- The removal is in pursuance of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016.
- Post removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will not be available on the Dissemination Board of BSE.
- The company is registered at 10-3-162, 5th Floor, NCL Pearl, Sarojini Devi Road, Secunderabad, Telangana – 500 026.

## Regulatory Changes

This action is carried out pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the framework for Exclusively Listed Companies and their presence on the Dissemination Board.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members dealing in NCL Seccolor Ltd shares should note the removal and cease transactions on the Dissemination Board from March 11, 2026.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company secretary M. Ravindra at companysecretary@nclbuildtek.com or BSE's Mr. Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Compliance & Operations) at direct.listing@bseindia.com / Ph: 022-2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **March 11, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of removal of NCL Seccolor Ltd from the BSE Dissemination Board.

## Impact Assessment

The removal affects only NCL Seccolor Ltd, a single exclusively listed company with limited market participation. Investors or trading members holding or transacting in this company's shares via the Dissemination Board should take note that trading access will cease from March 11, 2026. The broader market impact is minimal given the niche nature of ELC-based trading on dissemination boards.
