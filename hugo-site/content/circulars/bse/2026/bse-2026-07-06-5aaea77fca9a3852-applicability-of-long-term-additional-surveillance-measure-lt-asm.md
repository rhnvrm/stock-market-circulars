## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dating back to 2018. The circular specifies securities newly added to LT-ASM, securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages, securities continuing in LT-ASM but shifting to different trading groups (T/XT/MT/TS), and securities exiting the framework. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- 100% margin applies from July 9, 2026 on all open positions as of July 8, 2026 and on new positions created from July 9, 2026 onwards, for securities listed in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect July 7, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B).
- Lower price band applies from July 7, 2026 for the relevant securities.
- Securities under the June 2021 LT-ASM framework (Annexure I, Part D) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups effective July 9, 2026.
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework effective July 7, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands revert to pre-shortlisting levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III for reference.
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is a surveillance measure only, not an adverse action against any company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic operational update applying the existing LT-ASM framework (established via notices from 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2024) to a revised set of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin requirements on affected securities from the specified effective dates.
- Members should apply the revised (lower) price bands to newly added LT-ASM securities.
- Members should track group transfers (T/XT/MT/TS) for securities continuing under the 2021 LT-ASM framework.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 7, 2026: Higher LT-ASM stage movements and lower price bands take effect; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II).
- July 8, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 9, 2026: 100% margin applies to new positions; securities transfer to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups.

## Impact Assessment

The update directly affects trading conditions—margin requirements, price bands, and trading group classification—for the specific securities named in the annexures, which are not detailed in the visible text. Impact is significant for holders and traders of the affected scrips (higher margin costs, restricted price movement) but is a routine, recurring surveillance action rather than a broad market event.
