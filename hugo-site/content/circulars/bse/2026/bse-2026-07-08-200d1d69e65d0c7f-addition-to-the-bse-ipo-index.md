## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Knack Packaging Limited (Exchange ticker: 544814) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE effective Wednesday, July 8, 2026, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on Friday, July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Knack Packaging Limited (ticker 544814) listed on BSE effective July 8, 2026
- Stock to be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on July 10, 2026
- The addition references an earlier Notice No. 20260707-41
- Effective date noted for the index action is July 6, 2026
- This action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine index constituent update following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from listed companies or market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index should update portfolio holdings to reflect the addition of Knack Packaging Limited.

## Important Dates

- July 6, 2026: Effective date referenced for the index action
- July 8, 2026: Knack Packaging Limited listed on BSE
- July 10, 2026: Stock added to BSE IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine index maintenance notice affecting index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index, which will need to rebalance holdings to include Knack Packaging Limited. Direct market impact on other listed securities is minimal, though inclusion may generate incremental passive-fund buying interest in the newly listed stock.
