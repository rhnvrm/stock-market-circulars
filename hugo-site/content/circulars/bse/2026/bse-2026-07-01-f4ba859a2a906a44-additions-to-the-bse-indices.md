## Summary

BSE has announced additions to its indices following the listing of four companies. Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited (544802) and Advit Jewels Limited (544803) were listed on the BSE mainboard on July 1, 2026, and will be added to the BSE IPO index effective the open of trading on July 3, 2026. Separately, Riyaasat Lifestyle Limited (544804) and Jivial Industries Limited (544805) were listed on the BSE SME platform on July 1, 2026, and will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective the open of trading on July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited (Ticker: 544802) listed on BSE mainboard effective July 1, 2026; added to BSE IPO index effective July 3, 2026
- Advit Jewels Limited (Ticker: 544803) listed on BSE mainboard effective July 1, 2026; added to BSE IPO index effective July 3, 2026
- Riyaasat Lifestyle Limited (Ticker: 544804) listed on BSE SME platform effective July 1, 2026; added to BSE SME IPO index effective July 2, 2026
- Jivial Industries Limited (Ticker: 544805) listed on BSE SME platform effective July 1, 2026; added to BSE SME IPO index effective July 2, 2026
- These actions will be reflected in the day's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative index maintenance notice consistent with BSE's standard practice of adding newly listed companies to relevant IPO indices.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO or BSE SME IPO indices should ensure portfolio rebalancing reflects these additions on the stated effective dates.

## Important Dates

- July 1, 2026: Listing date for all four companies
- July 2, 2026 (open): Effective addition of Riyaasat Lifestyle and Jivial Industries to BSE SME IPO index
- July 3, 2026 (open): Effective addition of Waterways Leisure Tourism and Advit Jewels to BSE IPO index

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine index maintenance action affecting only newly listed companies and passive funds tracking the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices. No impact on broader market indices or existing constituent stocks.
