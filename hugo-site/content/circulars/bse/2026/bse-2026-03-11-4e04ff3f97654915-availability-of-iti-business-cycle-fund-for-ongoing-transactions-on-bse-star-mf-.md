## Summary

BSE has notified members that ITI Business Cycle Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 12, 2026. This notice references the earlier Notice No. 20260212-38.

## Key Points

- ITI Business Cycle Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 12, 2026
- Six scheme variants are available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All transaction types enabled: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- All schemes are categorized under Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction availability for an existing mutual fund.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and distributors on BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed ITI Business Cycle Fund schemes from March 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 12, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ITI Business Cycle Fund for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

Low market impact. This notice affects mutual fund distributors and investors transacting through BSE StAR MF. The six scheme ISINs available are:
