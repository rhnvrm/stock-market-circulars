## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 729011, ISIN: INE04VS14244, Series: OFSL-12-3-26-CP) is set to mature on March 11, 2026. As a result, trading in this instrument has been suspended with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (ISIN: INE04VS14244) matures on March 11, 2026.
- Trading in the scrip (Code: 729011, Series: OFSL-12-3-26-CP) is suspended effective March 11, 2026.
- All trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.
- Reference: DR-837/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon the maturity of a commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 729011, ISIN: INE04VS14244) from March 11, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Date of circular issuance.
- **March 11, 2026**: Maturity date of commercial paper and effective date of trading suspension.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This notice pertains solely to the maturity of one commercial paper instrument issued by OXYZO Financial Services Limited. Trading members holding or dealing in this paper must stop transactions from March 11, 2026. There is no broader market impact beyond the affected instrument.
