## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has published the client margin reporting schedule for the Currency Derivatives (CDX) segment for June 2026. The schedule specifies the last date for margin reporting and the corresponding penalty date for each trade date during the month.

## Key Points

- Covers all trade dates in June 2026 for the Currency Derivatives Segment (CDX)
- Reporting window is typically T+5 to T+7 business days
- Penalty dates follow one business day after the last reporting date
- Schedule extends into early July 2026 for late-month trade dates (22-Jun to 30-Jun)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly schedule circular issued by ICCL.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in the Currency Derivatives segment must submit client margin reports by the specified last date for each trade date
- Failure to report by the last date will attract penalties effective from the listed penalty date
