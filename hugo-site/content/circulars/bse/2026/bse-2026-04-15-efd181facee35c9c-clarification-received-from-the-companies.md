## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260415-15 dated 15 April 2026, listing companies that experienced significant price/volume movement. The Exchange had written to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Clarifications have been received from 28 companies, while 17 companies have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 45 equity scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies.
- 28 companies have submitted their replies, which are attached to the notice (Replies1 through Replies5).
- 17 companies have not yet responded; the Exchange is awaiting their replies.
- The notice is issued under the Surveillance function to protect investor interests and maintain market integrity.
- Signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a routine surveillance action under existing BSE frameworks for monitoring unusual market activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 17 companies listed as "reply awaited" are expected to submit clarifications to BSE promptly: Benchmark Computer Solutions Ltd (544052), Citichem India Ltd (544324), Goel Food Products Ltd (543538), K&R Rail Engineering Ltd (514360), Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd (523475), Mefcom Capital Markets Ltd (531176), Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd (511018), Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Ltd (544673), Narmada Macplast Drip Irrigation Systems Ltd (517431), Naturite Agro Products Ltd (538926), Ovobel Foods Ltd (530741), Saffron Industries Ltd (531436), Sampat Aluminium Ltd (544520), Supertech Ev Ltd (544428), Tamilnadu Telecommunications Ltd (523419), Vishal Bearings Ltd (539398), ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640).
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached company replies before making investment decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 15 April 2026
- Replies attached as of 15 April 2026 for the 28 responding companies.
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; companies are expected to respond at the earliest.

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects investors and trading members holding positions in 45 listed equity scrips that witnessed unusual price/volume activity. The 28 companies that have responded provide transparency into the reasons behind price movements, enabling informed trading decisions. The 17 non-responding companies remain under surveillance scrutiny, and continued non-compliance may attract further regulatory action. Overall market impact is contained to these specific scrips; there is no systemic or broad market implication.
