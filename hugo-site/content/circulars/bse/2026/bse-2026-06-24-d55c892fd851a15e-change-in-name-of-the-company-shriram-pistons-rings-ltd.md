## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Shriram Pistons & Rings Ltd (Scrip Code: 544344) has changed its corporate name to **SPR Auto Technologies Limited**, effective **July 01, 2026**. The scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will remain unchanged as SHRIPISTON.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Shriram Pistons & Rings Ltd** to **SPR Auto Technologies Limited**
- Scrip Code remains **544344**
- Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **SHRIPISTON** (no change)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: **SHRIPISTON** (no change)
- Effective date: **July 01, 2026**
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company has formally notified BSE of its name change, triggering an update to the exchange's records under the Listing Operations department. No changes to trading identifiers or ISIN are indicated in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the new company name (SPR Auto Technologies Limited) for record-keeping and client communication purposes. No changes to trading systems are required as the scrip ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change:** July 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact on trading operations is minimal. The scrip code (544344), scrip ID (SHRIPISTON), and abbreviated name (SHRIPISTON) on the BOLT Plus System remain unchanged, so no system-level adjustments are needed by trading members. Investors and brokers should update internal records to reflect the new legal name — SPR Auto Technologies Limited — for compliance and documentation purposes.
