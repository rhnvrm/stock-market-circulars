## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) effective June 12, 2026, on all open positions as of June 11, 2026 and new positions thereafter. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and a lower price band is being applied from June 10, 2026. Some securities are also being transferred out of the LT-ASM framework.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 12, 2026 on all open and new positions for securities in Annexure I (Part A)
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) are being moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework from June 10, 2026
- Lower Price Band applicable from June 10, 2026 for securities moved to higher stages
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group from June 12, 2026
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 10, 2026
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework is in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is a market surveillance action, not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice refers to the revised LT-ASM framework as updated through multiple prior notices:
- Original framework: Exchange notice 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018)
- Key revisions: notices dated October 27, 2018; December 04, 2020; August 09, 2024; September 20, 2024
- Framework for T/XT/MT/TS group transfers: Exchange notice 20210604-41 (June 04, 2021)

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margins are collected/maintained for affected securities from June 12, 2026
- Members must apply the lower price band for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages from June 10, 2026
- Price bands for scrips exiting the framework will be reinstated to pre-shortlisting levels, unless the scrip is under another surveillance measure
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages; lower price band applicable; securities exiting LT-ASM framework
- **June 11, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margins
- **June 12, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions (as of June 11) and all new positions; affected securities transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a high market impact for traders and investors holding or planning to trade in the affected securities. The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding positions, potentially reducing liquidity and increasing selling pressure on shortlisted scrips. Securities moved to T/XT/MT/TS groups face further trading restrictions. The lower price band limits downside volatility but also restricts price discovery. Market participants should review the attached annexures to identify specific affected securities and adjust their risk and margin management accordingly.
