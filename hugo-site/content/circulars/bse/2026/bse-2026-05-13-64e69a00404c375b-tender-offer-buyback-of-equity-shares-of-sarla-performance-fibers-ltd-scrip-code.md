## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd (Scrip Code: 526885) has fixed **May 15, 2026** as the record date for its equity share buyback offer. The record date will be used to determine the entitlement and names of shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd (Scrip Code: 526885) is conducting a tender offer (buyback) of its equity shares
- Record date fixed at **May 15, 2026** under the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment
- Settlement reference: DR-631/2026-2027
- Ex-entitlement date is also May 15, 2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date (May 15, 2026) for the buyback offer
- Members should inform eligible clients (shareholders as of record date) of their entitlement to participate in the buyback

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | May 13, 2026 |
| Record Date | May 15, 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | May 15, 2026 |
| Settlement No. | DR-631/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited, company-specific impact. Only existing shareholders of Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd (526885) as of May 15, 2026 are eligible to participate in the buyback. Trading members should ensure client positions are correctly reflected ahead of the record date. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
