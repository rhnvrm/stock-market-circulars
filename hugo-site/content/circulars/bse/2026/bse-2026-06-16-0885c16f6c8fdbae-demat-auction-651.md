## Summary

BSE Limited, through the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 651 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-651/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities with auction sessions scheduled on June 16, 2026 and pay-in on June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 16, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on June 16, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Pay-in of auction securities: June 17, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they have short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The notice reiterates the existing framework under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, where settlement is routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the specified session timings
- Members and Custodians must check auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute pay-in within depository cut-off timings on June 17, 2026
- Trading members must exercise caution when entering securities codes and rates — no corrections are permitted for punching errors
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Members should check notices regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) before submitting offers

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Auction session day — offer entry (2:00–2:45 PM), matching (2:45–3:00 PM), reports (3:00–5:30 PM)
- **June 17, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice limited to trading members with short delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-651/2026-27. Impact is narrow and transactional, affecting only members who need to participate in the auction to cover short deliveries. No broad market or regulatory implications.
