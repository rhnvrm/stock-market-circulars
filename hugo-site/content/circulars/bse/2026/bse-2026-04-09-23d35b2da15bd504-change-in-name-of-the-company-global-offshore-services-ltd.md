## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Global Offshore Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 501848) has changed its corporate name to **Garware Offshore Services Limited**. Accordingly, the Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated effective **April 16, 2026**.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Global Offshore Services Ltd
- New company name: Garware Offshore Services Limited
- Scrip Code remains unchanged: **501848**
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **GARWAOFFS**
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: **GARWAOFFS**
- Segment: Equity
- Notice issued by: Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME of the company on the BOLT Plus trading system will be changed from the existing references for Global Offshore Services Ltd to **GARWAOFFS** to reflect the new corporate name, Garware Offshore Services Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System and update their internal records, trading terminals, and references accordingly before the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **April 09, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **April 16, 2026**: Effective date for the name change and updated BOLT Plus System identifiers

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate name change with moderate operational impact on trading members. The Scrip Code (501848) remains unchanged, so historical data and orders linked to the scrip code are unaffected. However, any systems or workflows referencing the scrip by its abbreviated name or Scrip ID will need to be updated to **GARWAOFFS** before April 16, 2026 to avoid disruption in trading operations.
