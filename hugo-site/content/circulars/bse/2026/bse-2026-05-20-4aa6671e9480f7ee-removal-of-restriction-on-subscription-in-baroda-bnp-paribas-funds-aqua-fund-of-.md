## Summary

BSE has notified members that the restriction on subscriptions in Baroda BNP Paribas Funds Aqua Fund of Fund has been removed effective May 20, 2026. This reverses the earlier suspension communicated via Notice No. 20220701-4 (July 1, 2022). The fund is now open for fresh/additional purchases, Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), and Systematic Transfer Plans (STP) on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Key Points

- Subscription restrictions on Baroda BNP Paribas Funds Aqua Fund of Fund are lifted effective May 20, 2026
- Fresh purchases, additional purchases, SIP, and STP are now permitted on the BSE StAR MF platform
- The restriction was originally imposed via BSE Notice No. 20220701-4 dated July 1, 2022
- Communication was received directly from Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund
- An addendum notice issued by the fund house is attached to the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice reinstates normal subscription access that had been suspended since July 2022, aligning with the fund house's updated policy on subscriptions for the Aqua Fund of Fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members on the BSE StAR MF platform should update their systems to reflect the restoration of subscription access for this scheme
- Members should refer to the attached addendum issued by Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund for full scheme-level details

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026**: Effective date for removal of subscription restrictions
- **July 1, 2022** (Notice No. 20220701-4): Original date when subscription restrictions were imposed

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact for mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform who previously could not invest in Baroda BNP Paribas Aqua Fund of Fund. Investors and advisors interested in fund-of-fund strategies with a water/aqua theme can now resume or initiate investments via BSE's platform. The broader market impact is minimal as this affects a single niche fund scheme.
