## Summary

BSE has listed 76,112 new equity shares of TCC CONCEPT LIMITED (Scrip Code: 512038) with effect from Thursday, January 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 547.94 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to a share swap arrangement. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 76,112 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 557.94 per share (premium of Rs. 547.94)
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to share swap
- Distinctive numbers: 47451950 to 47528061
- ISIN: INE887D01016
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- All shares subject to lock-in until July 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are permitted to trade on the Exchange from January 8, 2026, subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a small preferential allotment of 76,112 shares issued pursuant to a share swap to non-promoters. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 14, 2026, preventing immediate trading. The allotment represents routine corporate action with limited liquidity impact due to the lock-in period and relatively small number of shares involved.
