## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, February 07, 2026. This session is intended for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their software systems and trading platforms. Members can participate either in the mock trading session or use the UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020. No new BOLT TWS release is planned for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, February 07, 2026
- Members can choose between mock trading session or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Opportunity to test trading applications, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs can test their applications
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Mock trades will not create any margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities
- Feedback requested from all participants by 4:30 PM on the mock trading day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular references existing SEBI requirements:
- SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of software used in trading and risk management

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems should utilize this opportunity to test their applications
- Testing should cover various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Feedback must be submitted by 4:30 PM on February 07, 2026 via provided contact channels

## Important Dates

- **February 07, 2026 (Saturday)**
  - Log-in: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
  - Feedback submission deadline: 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Voluntary participation for testing purposes only. No margin obligations or settlement obligations will arise from mock trades. Members can use this as an opportunity to test their trading systems in a simulated environment without financial risk.

**Member Impact:** Beneficial for members to test and familiarize themselves with trading platforms, especially for testing exceptional market conditions and system readiness.
