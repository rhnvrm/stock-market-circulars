## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that meet the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM), pursuant to its ongoing ASM framework established through prior circulars (dated September 20, 2024, October 27, 2018, July 19, 2019, December 4, 2020, and September 25, 2023). Securities listed in the attached Annexure I have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II based on satisfying respective inclusion criteria.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The specific list of affected securities is provided in Annexure I (Annexure_STASM), which is not included in the notice text.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip moves out of the ASM framework, its price band will revert to the band applicable prior to shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework (as revised via prior circulars) to a new set of securities meeting the stage-specific inclusion criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified higher margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on all open positions as of July 10, 2026, and on new positions created from July 13, 2026, for the securities listed in Annexure I.
- Members should refer to the Annexure for the exact list of affected securities and apply margins accordingly.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 9, 2026
- Reference date for open positions: July 10, 2026
- Effective date for margin applicability: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) will raise the cost of carrying positions in the affected securities, likely dampening speculative trading activity and reducing liquidity in these scrips. Market participants holding or trading the listed securities should reassess position sizing and funding requirements ahead of the July 13, 2026 effective date. Since the affected securities are not named in the notice body (only via a separate annexure), impacted brokers and investors should consult the annexure directly to determine specific exposure.
