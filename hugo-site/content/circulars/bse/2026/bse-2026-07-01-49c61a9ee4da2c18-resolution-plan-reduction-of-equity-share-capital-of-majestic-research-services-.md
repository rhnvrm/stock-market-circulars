## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Majestic Research Services and Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 539229) has fixed a Record Date for reduction of its equity share capital, pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Bengaluru Bench. Under the plan, the entire equity shareholding of promoters/related parties/promoter group will be fully extinguished and cancelled with no payment to these shareholders, while the residual paid-up equity capital held by public shareholders will be written down to 5% of total equity shareholding (after allotment of shares to the Resolution Applicant), in accordance with the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957.

## Key Points

- Company: Majestic Research Services and Solutions Limited, Scrip Code 539229
- Action arises from a Resolution Plan approved by NCLT, Bengaluru Bench, under insolvency proceedings
- Promoter/related party/promoter group equity shareholding to be fully extinguished and cancelled — no consideration payable
- Public shareholders' residual paid-up equity to be written down to 5% of total equity shareholding post allotment to the Resolution Applicant, per SCRR 1957
- Trading in the company's equity shares is currently under suspension

## Regulatory Changes

This is a company-specific corporate action implementing an NCLT-sanctioned resolution plan under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code framework, not a broad regulatory/policy change. It applies the capital reduction provisions of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 to bring public shareholding to the prescribed minimum threshold.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the Record Date and Ex-Date/Suspension effective date for reference in back-office and settlement records.
- No action required from public shareholders to receive extinguishment treatment for promoter shares; the capital write-down for public shareholders is automatic per the approved plan.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 08/07/2025
- Ex-Date/Suspension effective date: 08/07/2025 (Settlement No. DR-667/2026-2027)
- Notice Date: 01 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The corporate action results in complete extinguishment of promoter equity and a substantial write-down of public shareholders' equity capital to the statutory minimum public float level, as part of the company's insolvency resolution. Since trading in the scrip is already suspended, direct market trading impact is limited, but the action materially affects existing public shareholders' equity value and capital structure post-resolution.
