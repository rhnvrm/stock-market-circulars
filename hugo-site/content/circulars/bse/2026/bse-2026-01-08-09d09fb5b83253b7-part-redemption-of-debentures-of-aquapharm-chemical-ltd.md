## Summary

BSE has notified that AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD has completed a partial redemption of its debentures, resulting in a reduced face value. The debentures with ISIN INE0SYQ07015 (Scrip Code: 975354) will trade at the new reduced face value of Rs. 85,000 per debenture starting January 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD
- Debenture Series: ACL-8.90%-29-1-29-PVT
- ISIN: INE0SYQ07015
- Scrip Code: 975354
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 85,000 per debenture
- Effective Date for Trading: January 09, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-795/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action involving partial redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the reduced face value of the debentures
- All trading in these debentures must reflect the new face value of Rs. 85,000 per debenture from January 09, 2026 onwards

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 08, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date**: January 09, 2026 - Debentures will trade at reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide impact. The partial redemption affects only holders of AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD debentures. Trading members dealing in this specific debt instrument need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
