## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Gujarat Gas Ltd (Scrip Code: 539336) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name to **Gujarat Energy Limited**. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company Gujarat Gas Ltd is being renamed to **Gujarat Energy Limited**
- Scrip Code remains unchanged: **539336**
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **GUJENERGY**
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: **GUJENERGY**
- Change is effective from **July 1, 2026**
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME for Scrip Code 539336 on the BOLT Plus System will change from Gujarat Gas Ltd to GUJENERGY to reflect the new corporate name Gujarat Energy Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the new company name, Scrip ID, and abbreviated name effective July 1, 2026. Any references to Gujarat Gas Ltd in trading systems should be updated to Gujarat Energy Limited / GUJENERGY.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 1, 2026 — new name, Scrip ID, and abbreviated name become active on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change affecting how the security appears in trading systems. The Scrip Code (539336) remains the same, minimizing disruption. Trading members and investors tracking this security by name or symbol need to update references from Gujarat Gas Ltd to Gujarat Energy Limited (GUJENERGY). No change in ownership, shareholding, or trading terms is indicated.
