## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE090W08038, Code: 975718) effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company exercising its call option for redemption along with interest payment, with the record date set for January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in specified debentures of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE090W08038, BSE Code: 975718
- Debenture Series: LFL-16%-20-5-26-PVT
- Record date for redemption: January 13, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption on exercise of call option and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-787/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- Trading Members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED debentures (INE090W08038) from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders of specific debenture series of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED in the debt market segment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to block trading in the affected debentures. Investors holding these debentures will be unable to trade from the suspension date and should prepare for redemption on the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds plus interest payment based on the January 13, 2026 record date. Secondary market liquidity ceases from the suspension date.
