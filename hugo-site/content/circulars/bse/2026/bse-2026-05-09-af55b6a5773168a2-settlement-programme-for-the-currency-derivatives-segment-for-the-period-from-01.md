## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has issued the settlement programme for the Currency Derivatives (CDX) segment on BSE for the period from 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. This notice (Notice No. 20260509-8, dated 09 May 2026) provides the scheduled settlement dates and associated details for all currency derivatives transactions during June 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the Currency Derivatives segment for June 2026 (01.06.2026 to 30.06.2026)
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category
- Attachment reference: CDX062026 (contains the detailed settlement schedule)
- Notice Number: 20260509-8, dated 09 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational notice providing the monthly settlement calendar for the Currency Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members and clearing members participating in the Currency Derivatives segment on BSE must adhere to the settlement dates and timelines specified in the CDX062026 attachment.
- Members should ensure adequate funds and securities are available on respective settlement dates as per the programme.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 09 May 2026
- Settlement Programme Period: 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026
- Detailed settlement dates are provided in the attached schedule (CDX062026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a routine operational impact on participants in the BSE Currency Derivatives segment. Clearing members and trading members need to plan their liquidity and settlement obligations in accordance with the June 2026 schedule. No extraordinary settlements or market structure changes are indicated. The impact is limited to scheduling awareness and operational planning for the month of June 2026.
