## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities subject to Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). New securities satisfying the IBC surveillance criteria are being added (Annexure I), certain securities are being moved out of the framework (Annexure II), and a consolidated list of all securities under the framework is provided (Annexure III). All changes are effective June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under the IBC Additional Surveillance Measure framework, effective June 11, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure II are being removed from the IBC framework, effective June 11, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the full consolidated list of securities currently under the framework.
- The framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion in this framework is purely a market surveillance action and should not be interpreted as an adverse finding against the company.
- For securities exiting the framework, price bands will be reinstated to their pre-inclusion levels, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the IBC surveillance framework in continuation of prior notices:
- Exchange notice no. 20180608-34 (June 08, 2018)
- Exchange notice no. 20220520-52 (May 20, 2022)
- Exchange notice no. 20230210-55 (February 10, 2023)
- Exchange notice no. 20250905-47 (September 05, 2025)

The IBC framework is reviewed periodically; this notice reflects the latest review cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised list of securities under the IBC surveillance framework and apply the applicable trading restrictions (e.g., price bands, trade-for-trade settlement) for each affected scrip.
- For securities exiting the framework, members should apply the reinstated pre-framework price band, unless another surveillance measure overrides it.
- Members with queries may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Effective date for new inclusions (Annexure I) and exclusions (Annexure II) from the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine periodic update to the IBC surveillance framework. Securities newly added to the framework (Annexure I) will be subject to enhanced surveillance conditions, which typically include restrictive price bands and/or trade-for-trade settlement, limiting speculative activity. Securities being removed (Annexure II) will have their price bands reinstated to pre-inclusion levels, potentially increasing trading flexibility for those scrips. The framework does not constitute an adverse regulatory action against the listed companies; it is a market integrity measure aligned with IBC proceedings. Market participants trading in IBC-listed companies should review the attached annexures to identify affected securities.
