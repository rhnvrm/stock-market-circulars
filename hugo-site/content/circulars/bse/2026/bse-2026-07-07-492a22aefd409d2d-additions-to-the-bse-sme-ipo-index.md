## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced that four recently listed SME companies - Sampark India Logistics Limited (544810), Kratikal Tech Limited (544811), Atharva Poly-Plast Limited (544812), and Seemax Resources Limited (544813) - will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index. These companies were listed on BSE effective July 7, 2026, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on July 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four SME companies listed on BSE on July 7, 2026 will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index
- Sampark India Logistics Limited - Exchange Ticker 544810 (referenced in Notice No. 20260706-28)
- Kratikal Tech Limited - Exchange Ticker 544811 (referenced in Notice No. 20260706-34)
- Atharva Poly-Plast Limited - Exchange Ticker 544812 (referenced in Notice No. 20260706-33)
- Seemax Resources Limited - Exchange Ticker 544813 (referenced in Notice No. 20260706-30)
- These actions will be reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for the notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine index maintenance action following new SME listings on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the additions effective the specified date. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 7, 2026: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- July 8, 2026: Effective date of addition to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice affecting index composition. The primary impact is on passive funds and index-tracking products that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index, which will need to rebalance to include these four new constituents. Direct market impact on the broader exchange is minimal, though the newly listed stocks may see incremental demand from index-tracking flows.
