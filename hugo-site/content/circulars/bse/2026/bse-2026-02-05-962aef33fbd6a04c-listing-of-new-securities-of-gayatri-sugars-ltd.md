## Summary

BSE has listed 166,668 new equity shares of Gayatri Sugars Ltd (Scrip Code: 532183) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis following warrant conversion. The shares will be available for trading from February 6, 2026, and are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 166,668 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to non-promoters
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 67526966 to 67693633
- Date of Allotment: November 22, 2024
- Issue Price: Rs. 10/- per share
- ISIN: INE622E01023
- Trading commences: February 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 22, 2024
- **Trading Commencement Date:** February 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** August 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential allotment represents a small quantity of shares (166,668 shares) and is subject to lock-in restrictions until August 30, 2026, limiting immediate trading activity. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, maintaining uniformity in shareholder rights.
