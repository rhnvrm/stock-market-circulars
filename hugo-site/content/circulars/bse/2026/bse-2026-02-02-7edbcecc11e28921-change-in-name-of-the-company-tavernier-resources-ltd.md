## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Tavernier Resources Limited (CIN: L93290MH1994PLC193901). The company's name has been officially changed to Eforu Entertainment Limited with effect from October 27, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dewan Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Old Name: Tavernier Resources Limited
- New Name: Eforu Entertainment Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L93290MH1994PLC193901
- Original incorporation name: Dewan Industries Limited
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered address: Level 4, A-Wing, Dynasty Business Park, Andheri Kurla Road, Andheri East, J.B. Nagar, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a completed name change process under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Tavernier Resources Limited) along with the new name (Eforu Entertainment Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new company name

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Effective date of name change (Certificate of Incorporation issued)
- **February 2, 2026**: Circular notification date
- **October 27, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action involving a name change. Trading in the company's securities will continue under the new name.

**Stakeholder Impact**: The name change does not affect the rights, obligations, or liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts and agreements remain enforceable.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update its letterheads, signage, website, and all official documentation to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period. Stakeholders are advised to verify the company's current status on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21).
