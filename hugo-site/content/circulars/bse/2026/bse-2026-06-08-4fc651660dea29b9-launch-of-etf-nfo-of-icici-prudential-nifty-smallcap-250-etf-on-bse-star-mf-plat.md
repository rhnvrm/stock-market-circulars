## Summary

BSE has informed members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund is launching the ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from June 9, 2026 to June 16, 2026. Both regular and direct plans are available, with a tentative allotment date of June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 9, 2026 to June 16, 2026 (extended till midnight on June 16 for lumpsum purchases)
- Two schemes offered: ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF and its Direct Plan (both Equity category)
- Tentative allotment date: June 19, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 18, 2026
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance by June 18 deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notice for a new ETF scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions for which clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled, and a CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Members must ensure clear funds are credited to the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 18, 2026 for allotment eligibility.

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: NFO opens
- **June 16, 2026**: NFO closes (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF)
- **June 18, 2026 by 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account
- **June 19, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is an operational notice relevant to BSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the StAR MF platform. Investors and distributors placing NFO orders must adhere to fund initiation and clearance deadlines to avoid cancellation. No impact on equity or derivative markets.
