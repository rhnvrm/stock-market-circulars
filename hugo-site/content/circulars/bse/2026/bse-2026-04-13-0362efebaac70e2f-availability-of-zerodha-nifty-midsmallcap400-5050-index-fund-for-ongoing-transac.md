## Summary

BSE has notified members that the Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund has been reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective April 13, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260320-44 dated March 20, 2026. SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also available under this reopening.

## Key Points

- Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Effective date: April 13, 2026.
- Facilities enabled: Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Scheme: Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund – Direct Plan – Growth; ISIN: INF0R8F01190; Category: Equity.
- This notice follows BSE Notice No. 20260320-44 dated March 20, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. BSE members offering mutual fund transactions through BSE StAR MF may now process purchase, redemption, and systematic transaction requests for this scheme.

## Important Dates

- **April 13, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of the fund for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **March 20, 2026**: Original BSE Notice No. 20260320-44 referenced in this circular.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine fund availability update enabling retail and institutional investors to transact in the Zerodha Nifty MidSmallcap400 50:50 Index Fund through the BSE StAR MF platform. The fund tracks a blended midcap-smallcap index (Nifty MidSmallcap400) with a 50:50 allocation, offering passive equity exposure. No changes to fund structure, fees, or regulatory framework are involved.
