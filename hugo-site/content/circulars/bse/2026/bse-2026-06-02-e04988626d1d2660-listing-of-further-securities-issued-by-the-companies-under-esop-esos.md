## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further equity shares issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) by 12 companies are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 3, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have listed additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All new shares are effective for trading from June 3, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed shares
- Total new shares across all companies exceed 2.6 million
- Wakefit Innovations has the largest allotment at 1,364,949 shares
- Le Travenues Technology (ixigo) follows with 485,584 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly admitted securities and update their systems accordingly. All listed shares are available for trading from June 3, 2026 without any lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 3, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. ESOP/ESOS listings are routine corporate events representing employee compensation conversions. The new shares are small relative to each company's total equity base. Details of newly listed shares:
