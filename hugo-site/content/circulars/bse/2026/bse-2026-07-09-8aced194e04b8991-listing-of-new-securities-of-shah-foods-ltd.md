## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new securities of Shah Foods Ltd (Scrip Code - 519031) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 10, 2026. The securities comprise two tranches of equity shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis, ranking pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 1,58,85,037 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 52.50/- to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- 68,32,463 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 100/- to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Total of 2,27,17,500 new equity shares listed, distinctive numbers 7,07,501 to 2,34,25,000
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE455D01012
- Date of allotment: 27/03/2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new securities as listed and permitted to trade from July 10, 2026, subject to the applicable lock-in restrictions on the allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 27 March 2026
- Listing/Trading effective date: 10 July 2026 (Friday)
- Lock-in expiry for 1,58,85,037 shares (issue price Rs. 62.50, Dist. Nos. 7,07,501-1,65,92,537): 31 January 2028
- Lock-in expiry for 68,32,463 shares (issue price Rs. 110, Dist. Nos. 1,65,92,538-2,34,25,000): 31 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Shah Foods Ltd's tradable equity share capital via preferential allotment to non-promoters. Since the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in periods extending to January 2027 and January 2028, immediate free-float impact is limited, though the preferential issue reflects capital raising activity by the company that existing shareholders and prospective investors should note.
