## Summary

BSE has issued a circular detailing corporate action schedules for 17 securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. All listed companies are declaring dividends with specified shut periods during which SLB positions will be restricted. The shut periods range from January 28, 2026 to February 20, 2026, with ex-dates and record dates varying by company.

## Key Points

- 17 companies have declared dividend corporate actions affecting SLB operations
- Shut periods range from 1 to 4 days depending on the security
- Ex-dates span from January 29, 2026 to February 20, 2026
- All corporate actions are dividend-related
- SLB participants must close or adjust positions before shut period commencement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification of corporate actions affecting SLB segment operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB market participants must ensure positions in affected securities are closed or adjusted before respective shut periods
- Lenders and borrowers must account for dividend entitlements during shut periods
- Trading members should inform clients about shut period restrictions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects liquidity in SLB segment for 17 securities during respective shut periods. Participants with open positions must plan exits or rollovers.

**Operational Impact:** SLB participants must monitor multiple shut periods across different securities, requiring careful position management and dividend adjustment calculations.

**Investor Impact:** Borrowers of these securities during shut periods will not be able to establish new positions, and existing positions may require settlement before shut period commencement.
