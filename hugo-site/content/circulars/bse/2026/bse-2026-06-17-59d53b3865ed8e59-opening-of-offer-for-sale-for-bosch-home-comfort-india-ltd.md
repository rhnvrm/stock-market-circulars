## Summary

Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited, the promoter of Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd, is conducting an Offer for Sale (OFS) through BSE and NSE. The base offer size is up to 1,963,994 equity shares (7.22% of total paid-up equity share capital) at a floor price of Rs. 1150 per share, with an oversubscription option of up to 202,634 additional equity shares (0.75%).

## Key Points

- Seller: Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited (Promoter)
- Base Offer Size: Up to 1,963,994 equity shares (7.22% of paid-up capital), face value Rs. 10 each
- Oversubscription Option: Up to 202,634 equity shares (0.75% of paid-up capital)
- Floor Price: Rs. 1150 per equity share
- OFS conducted via separate designated windows on BSE and NSE
- Purpose: To achieve minimum public shareholding requirement in the Company
- Applicable OFS guidelines referenced: Notice Nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, 20240701-19 and SEBI circulars dated December 28, 2018, January 10, 2023, and June 14, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The OFS is conducted under existing SEBI and BSE OFS guidelines, including:
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 (December 28, 2018) – Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 (January 10, 2023) – Comprehensive Framework on OFS
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 (June 14, 2024) – Framework for OFS to Employees

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must follow existing OFS bidding guidelines as referenced in prior notices
- Non-Retail Investors may participate on T Day (June 18, 2026)
- Retail Investors may participate on T+1 Day (June 19, 2026); Non-Retail Investors may also carry forward un-allotted bids from T Day to T+1 Day
- Market participants should refer to BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for disseminated OFS details and attached particulars

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 18, 2026 (T Day)**: OFS open for Non-Retail Investors only
- **June 19, 2026 (T+1 Day)**: OFS open for Retail Investors and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids

## Impact Assessment

The OFS will increase public float in Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd by up to 7.97% (base + oversubscription), facilitating compliance with SEBI's minimum public shareholding norms. The floor price of Rs. 1150 per share sets the minimum transaction price. Retail investors gain access on T+1 Day, providing wider participation. The offer is limited to two trading days and is expected to have a moderate near-term impact on Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd's share price and liquidity.
