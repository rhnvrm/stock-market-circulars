## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for company CIN L67120MH1983PLC031614. The company name has been officially changed from SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED to MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED with effect from October 14, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Previous name: SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED
- New name: MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED
- Original incorporation name: SAVANI INVESTMENT & LEASING LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L67120MH1983PLC031614
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Effective date of name change: October 14, 2025
- Certificate issued by: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Registered office address: 602, Samarth Vaibhav Co-op Hsg Society Ltd., Off - Link Road, Lokhandwala Complex, Andheri (West), Mumbai - 400053, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

No substantive regulatory changes. This is an administrative action confirming the legal change of company name as per the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name (MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED) at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act, 2013
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **October 14, 2025**: Effective date of name change from SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED to MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED
- **October 14, 2027**: End of two-year period for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- The company's corporate identity (CIN remains unchanged)
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders including shareholders, creditors, and other parties
- Existing contracts, agreements, or legal obligations
- Trading operations or listing status

Stakeholders should update their records to reflect the new company name for future correspondence and transactions. The company will continue operations under the new name while maintaining all existing legal obligations and relationships.
