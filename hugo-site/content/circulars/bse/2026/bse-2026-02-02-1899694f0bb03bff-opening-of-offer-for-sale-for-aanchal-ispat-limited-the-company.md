## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of an Offer for Sale (OFS) for shares of Aanchal Ispat Limited (ISIN: INE322R01022). The seller, Mukesh Goel, is part of the promoter and promoter group. The OFS will be conducted through a separate trading window on BSE (designated stock exchange) with NSE Clearing Limited as the designated clearing corporation. The offer follows SEBI's Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements Regulations, 2015 and SEBI OFS Circulars.

## Key Points

- Seller: Mukesh Goel (Promoter category)
- Company: Aanchal Ispat Limited
- ISIN: INE322R01022
- Designated Stock Exchange: BSE
- Designated Clearing Corporation: NSE Clearing Limited
- Two-day OFS structure with separate sessions for Non-Retail and Retail investors
- Non-Retail investors can carry forward un-allotted bids to the second day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular announces an OFS pursuant to paragraph 5(b) of the SEBI OFS Circular and follows existing SEBI regulations for offer for sale mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Prospective investors and brokers must read the entire Offer Notice dated February 02, 2026 along with SEBI OFS Circulars before participating
- Investors must comply with the specific bidding windows based on their category (Retail/Non-Retail)
- Orders must be placed through the designated stock exchange (BSE) during specified trading hours

## Important Dates

- **T Day (February 03, 2026)**: Non-Retail Investors bidding window
  - Trading hours: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM IST
  - Non-Retail investors may indicate willingness to carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day

- **T+1 Day (February 04, 2026)**: Retail Investors and carry-forward bids window
  - Open to Retail Investors and Non-Retail investors with carried forward un-allotted bids
  - Allocation from unsubscribed portion of Retail Category

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The OFS provides an opportunity for promoter stake sale in Aanchal Ispat Limited, potentially increasing public float and providing liquidity. The two-day structure allows broader investor participation across retail and non-retail categories.

**Operational Impact**: Investors interested in Aanchal Ispat Limited shares have a structured opportunity to acquire shares through the OFS mechanism. The carry-forward option for Non-Retail investors ensures efficient price discovery and allocation. Trading will occur through a separate BSE window, ensuring dedicated focus on this transaction without affecting regular market operations.
