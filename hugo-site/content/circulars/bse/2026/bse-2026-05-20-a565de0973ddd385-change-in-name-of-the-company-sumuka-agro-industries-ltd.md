## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Sumuka Agro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 532070) has officially informed the Exchange of a change in its company name to **Gujjubhai Industries Limited**. The change is effective **May 26, 2026**, and will be reflected in the Scrip ID and abbreviated name used on the BOLT Plus trading system.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Sumuka Agro Industries Ltd** to **Gujjubhai Industries Limited**
- Scrip Code **532070** remains unchanged
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **GUJJUBHAI**
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: **GUJJUBHAI**
- Notice issued by the Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for Scrip Code 532070 on the BOLT Plus trading system will be updated from the identifiers associated with Sumuka Agro Industries Ltd to **GUJJUBHAI**, reflecting the company's new legal name, Gujjubhai Industries Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to update their internal records and systems to reflect the new company name and updated BOLT Plus identifiers effective May 26, 2026. No changes to the Scrip Code (532070) are required.

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026** – Date of BSE Notice (Notice No. 20260520-35)
- **May 26, 2026** – Effective date of name change and Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name update on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and operational in nature. The company's Scrip Code (532070) remains unchanged, so existing orders, holdings, and references by code are unaffected. Trading members using the Scrip ID or abbreviated name (e.g., in search or order entry by name) will need to use **GUJJUBHAI** from May 26, 2026 onwards. This is a routine corporate action with no direct market or financial impact on investors.
