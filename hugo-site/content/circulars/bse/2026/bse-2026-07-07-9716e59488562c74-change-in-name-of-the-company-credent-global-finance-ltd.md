## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Credent Global Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 539598) has informed the Exchange about a change in its company name to AMPL Capital Limited. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: Credent Global Finance Ltd
- New Name: AMPL Capital Limited
- Scrip Code: 539598 (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: AMPL
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: AMPL
- Change effective from July 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to reflect the company's new legal name as informed to the Exchange, supported by attached Certificate of Incorporation (COI_CREDENT).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name (AMPL) for BOLT Plus System trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change limited to company name and associated trading identifiers. There is no change in the underlying business, scrip code, or shareholder rights. Trading members and investors should note the new scrip ID (AMPL) to avoid confusion when trading post the effective date.
