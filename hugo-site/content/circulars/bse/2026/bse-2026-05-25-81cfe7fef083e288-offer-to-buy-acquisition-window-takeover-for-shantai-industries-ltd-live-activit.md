## Summary

BSE has issued a live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SHANTAI INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 512297, ISIN: INE408F01024). The offer is open from 29th May 2026 to 9th June 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 21.00 per share, covering up to 19,20,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** SHANTAI INDUSTRIES LTD | Symbol: SHANTAI | ISIN: INE408F01024 | Scrip Code: 512297
- **Offer Size:** Up to 19,20,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 21.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 2.00 per share
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Tuesday, 29th May 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 9th June 2026
- Bid Offering Period runs from 26th May 2026 to 9th June 2026 (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM)
- Give-up / Take-up sessions available from 26th May to 8th June 2026 (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM)
- On the last day (9th June 2026), Give-up closes at 3:30 PM and Take-up closes at 4:00 PM
- OTB module accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms on internet and leased line

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular provides operational scheduling information for an existing takeover offer framework under BSE's Acquisition Window mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- For operational instructions, members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).
- For leased line access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to trading members and custodians transacting in SHANTAI INDUSTRIES LTD shares. Eligible investor categories include FII, FI, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, other QIBs, NIBs, Companies, and Individuals. The offer price of Rs. 21.00 provides a defined exit opportunity for existing shareholders. Members must ensure UCC compliance and demat holdings before submitting bids through the iBBS platform.
