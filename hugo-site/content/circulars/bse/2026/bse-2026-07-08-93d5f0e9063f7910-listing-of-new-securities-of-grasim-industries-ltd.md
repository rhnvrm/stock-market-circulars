## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of Grasim Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500300) with effect from Thursday, July 9, 2026. The new securities comprise 5,967 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 5,967 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each became eligible for trading following conversion from partly paid to fully paid-up status.
- These shares were issued on a rights basis.
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680463939 to 680469905
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the Company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for newly fully-paid-up shares arising from a prior rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to recognize and permit trading in the newly listed shares from the effective date; no additional action required from investors as the shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 8, 2026
- Effective Date of Trading: Thursday, July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the small quantum of shares (5,967) relative to Grasim Industries' total outstanding equity. This listing simply reflects the completion of a call-money/partly-paid conversion process under an earlier rights issue and does not materially affect the stock's float, valuation, or trading dynamics.
