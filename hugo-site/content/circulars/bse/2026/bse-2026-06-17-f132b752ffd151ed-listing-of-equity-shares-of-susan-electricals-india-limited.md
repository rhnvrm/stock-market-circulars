## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260617-9) dated 17 June 2026 regarding the listing of equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited on the SME segment of the exchange. This is a company-related notice issued by the DCS-Listing department.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited are being listed on BSE's SME segment.
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department on 17 June 2026.
- Classified under the SME segment, indicating this is a small and medium enterprise listing.
- No attachments are included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard listing announcement for a new SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members should note the new listing and update their systems accordingly. Investors who participated in the IPO should expect their shares to be available for trading from the listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 17 June 2026
- **Listing Date:** 17 June 2026 (same-day listing implied by notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This listing is limited in scope to investors and trading members dealing in the SME segment. The listing of Susan Electricals India Limited on BSE SME is a routine event with moderate impact on IPO allottees who can now trade their shares. Broader market impact is low.
