## Summary

BSE Limited has issued Notice No. 20260708-2 inviting offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction 666, conducted in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-666/2026-27. This is a routine settlement/RMS auction process to address short delivery of securities, administered on behalf of the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Key Points

- Auction offers will be accepted on trading terminals by the Exchange on Wednesday, July 08, 2026.
- The auction pertains to Rolling Settlement No. DR-666/2026-27.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- The prior restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- No corrections are permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate; the Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning a reason.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and any relevant corporate action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) before submitting offers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice; it reiterates the existing framework under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, including the removal of the earlier restriction barring trading members from offering securities in auctions where they had short-delivered.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings.
- Members must exercise care while entering securities codes and rates during auction offer entry, as punching errors cannot be corrected after submission.
- Members should cross-check corporate action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split) affecting the relevant securities before placing auction offers.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Wednesday, July 08, 2026 (02:00 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Thursday, July 09, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring operational notice affecting only trading members and custodians involved in the specific auction settlement (DR-666/2026-27). It does not pertain to any specific listed company or have broader market-wide implications; impact is limited to procedural compliance for the trading members participating in this settlement cycle.
