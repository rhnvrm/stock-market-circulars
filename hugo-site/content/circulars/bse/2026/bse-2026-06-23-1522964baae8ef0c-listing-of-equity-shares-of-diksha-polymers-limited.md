## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Diksha Polymers Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Diksha Polymers Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544798
- Trading Symbol: DIKSHA
- ISIN: INE1OSI01014
- Actual listing date and security details to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required; members should await the separate notice with the exact listing date and trading details before executing any transactions in this security.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as this is a preliminary listing notice for an SME-segment company. The actual trading impact will be assessed once the listing date notice is issued. Investors and trading members interested in Diksha Polymers Limited should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date.
