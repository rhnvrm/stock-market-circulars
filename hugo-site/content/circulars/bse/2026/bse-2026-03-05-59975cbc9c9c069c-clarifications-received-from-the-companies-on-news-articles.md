## Summary

Bharat Forge Limited (BSE: 500493, NSE: BHARATFORG) issued a clarification to BSE and NSE on March 5, 2026, in response to exchange queries regarding a news article published on cnbctv18.com. The article related to monthly industry-level data and market trends for the North American Class 8 truck market. The company confirmed there are no specific undisclosed negotiations, material developments, or regulatory proceedings that require disclosure under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- The news article referenced by exchanges was based on industry-level data about North American Class 8 truck orders, which is part of Bharat Forge's ordinary course of business.
- The company confirmed no specific negotiations or material developments are underway that require disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- Bharat Forge confirmed it is not aware of any undisclosed material information that could explain price/volume movement in its shares.
- No regulatory or legal proceedings beyond what has already been intimated to exchanges.
- The news article has no material impact on the company's operations or financial position.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a compliance response under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bharat Forge reaffirmed its commitment to timely disclosure of all material events as required under applicable regulations.
- Response submitted by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer Tejaswini Chaudhari (Membership No. A18907).

## Important Dates

- **March 5, 2026**: Date of CNBC TV18 news article and exchange query letters (BSE ref: L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/(2025-2026)/207; NSE ref: NSE/CM/Surveillance/16538).
- **March 5, 2026**: Date of Bharat Forge's clarification response.

## Impact Assessment

No material market or operational impact. The clarification is a standard regulatory response confirming the absence of undisclosed price-sensitive information. The North American Class 8 truck market exposure is part of the company's normal business operations and was already publicly known. Investors should note no new material information has been disclosed.
