## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised price bands for 8 equity scrips, effective May 7, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, replacing existing levels. This action is part of BSE's ongoing surveillance measures to manage volatility and protect market integrity.

## Key Points

- 8 scrips have had their price bands revised effective May 7, 2026
- Revised price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 10%
- Change is initiated by BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS)
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com
- No attachments accompanying the notice

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands have been revised from their existing levels for the following scrips:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|------------|------------|--------------------|
| 533260 | CP Capital Ltd | 5% |
| 517170 | Edvenswa Enterprises Ltd | 5% |
| 500183 | HFCL Ltd | 10% |
| 532894 | Indowind Energy Ltd | 5% |
| 541983 | Innovative Ideals and Services (India) Ltd | 2% |
| 512267 | Media Matrix Worldwide Ltd | 10% |
| 541945 | Ranjeet Mechatronics Ltd | 10% |
| 543244 | Shine Fashions (India) Ltd | 5% |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all orders in the above scrips comply with the revised price band limits from May 7, 2026 onwards
- No specific filing or reporting obligation is placed on members beyond adherence to new band limits
- Members with queries should contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 6, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 7, 2026 (revised price bands come into force)

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects 8 scrips across varied market segments. Innovative Ideals and Services (India) Ltd faces the tightest restriction at 2%, indicating heightened surveillance concern for that scrip. HFCL Ltd, the most widely tracked stock in this list, is revised to a 10% band — potentially a loosening or tightening depending on its prior band. Trading members will experience constrained intraday movement in all listed scrips. This is a standard surveillance tool used by BSE to curb excessive speculation or abnormal price movement and does not indicate any corporate action or fundamental event.
