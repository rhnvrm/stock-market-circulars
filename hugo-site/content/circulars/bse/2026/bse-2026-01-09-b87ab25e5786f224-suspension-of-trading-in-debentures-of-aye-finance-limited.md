## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in specific debentures of AYE FINANCE LIMITED (Code: AFL-11.60%-24-1-26-PVT, ISIN: INE501X08081, Scrip Code: 975348) effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of January 17, 2026 for the purpose of debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE501X08081 (Scrip Code: 975348)
- Debenture series: AFL-11.60%-24-1-26-PVT (11.60% coupon rate)
- No dealings permitted from January 16, 2026
- Record date set for January 17, 2026
- Purpose: Final redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice issued under DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture maturity and redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified debentures from January 16, 2026
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Members must ensure no transactions are executed in these securities during the suspension period
- Debenture holders as of the record date (January 17, 2026) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **January 16, 2026**: Trading suspension becomes effective (no dealings from this date)
- **January 17, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium impact limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is a routine corporate action associated with debt maturity.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their principal redemption amount plus final interest payment. Those holding the debentures on record date will receive proceeds as per the terms of issuance.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members must ensure systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in these suspended securities. The suspension is temporary and related to the settlement process for redemption.

**Issuer Impact**: This represents the scheduled maturity and redemption of AYE FINANCE LIMITED's debt obligations, reducing the company's outstanding debt portfolio.
