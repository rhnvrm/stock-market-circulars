## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,58,10,276 new equity shares of Blue Jet Healthcare Limited (Scrip Code: 544009, ISIN: INE0KBH01020), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,58,10,276 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted to QIBs under a Qualified Institutional Placement
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of allotment: July 9, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 506/- per share
- Distinctive numbers: 173465426 to 189275701
- Scrip Code: 544009; ISIN: INE0KBH01020

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the increased tradable equity from the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 9, 2026
- Listing/Trading effective date: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Notice date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Blue Jet Healthcare Limited following its QIP. As the new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares and were placed with institutional investors at a fixed issue price, near-term market impact is limited, though it modestly expands free float and institutional ownership in the stock.
