## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260311-67) informing trading members that Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd has fixed a record date for redemption and interest payment on its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are directed not to deal in the specified debentures with effect from 13 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd
- Debenture Series: VFSPL-3MTBILL-30-3-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE016P07187 (Scrip Code: 973892)
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Record Date: 15 March 2026
- No Dealings From: 13 March 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-839/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's debt segment procedures for suspending trading prior to corporate action record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in ISIN INE016P07187 (Scrip Code 973892) from 13 March 2026.
- Members must take note of the no-dealings instruction and ensure compliance to avoid any trades in the debenture series ahead of the record date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 11 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 13 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 15 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only holders and prospective traders of the specific NCD series (VFSPL-3MTBILL-30-3-26-PVT) issued by Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd. The trading halt is temporary and limited in scope, impacting liquidity of this particular instrument until after the record date. Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds and interest as scheduled on or around the record date of 15 March 2026.
