## Summary

BSE has notified members (Notice No. 20260304-1) that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 04, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260212-36.

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct Plans are available, covering Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are enabled alongside standard subscription and redemption.
- Six scheme variants are available in total across Regular and Direct plans.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Brokers and distributors on the BSE StAR MF Platform may now process transactions in the listed OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 — Schemes reopened for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice is a routine operational update enabling transactions in OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND on the BSE StAR MF platform. The six available schemes are:
