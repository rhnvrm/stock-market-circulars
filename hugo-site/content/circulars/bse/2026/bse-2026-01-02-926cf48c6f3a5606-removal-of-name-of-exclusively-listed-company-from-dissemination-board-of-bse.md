## Summary

BSE has announced the removal of Premium Linkers Limited from its Dissemination Board effective January 5, 2026. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Following removal, the company's shares will no longer be available on BSE's Dissemination Board. The removal is due to the completion of an exit option process.

## Key Points

- Premium Linkers Limited (CIN: U74899DL1982PLC014454) will be removed from BSE Dissemination Board
- Removal effective from Monday, January 5, 2026
- Company is an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC) on BSE
- Registered address: 102, Palco House, 2162/T-10, Main Patel Road, New Delhi â€“ 110008
- Reason for removal: Exit option
- Action taken under SEBI circular dated October 10, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

This removal is implemented pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the removal of Exclusively Listed Companies from the Dissemination Board.

## Compliance Requirements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Effective Date of Removal:** January 5, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this removal is limited as it affects only one Exclusively Listed Company. The removal follows the completion of an exit option process, indicating that shareholders have been provided an opportunity to exit their holdings. Trading Members will no longer be able to access Premium Linkers Limited shares through BSE's Dissemination Board after January 5, 2026.
