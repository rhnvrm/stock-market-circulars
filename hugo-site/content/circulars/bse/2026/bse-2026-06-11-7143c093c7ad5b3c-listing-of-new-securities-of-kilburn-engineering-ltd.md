## Summary

BSE has listed 5,10,000 new equity shares of Kilburn Engineering Ltd. (Scrip Code: 522101, ISIN: INE338F01015), effective Friday, June 12, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 415/- (Issue Price Rs. 425/- per share, face value Rs. 10/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 5,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE with effect from June 12, 2026
- Shares issued via preferential allotment on conversion of warrants at issue price of Rs. 425/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 52962859 to 53472858
- Allotment done in two tranches: 2,00,000 shares on 17/04/2026 and 3,10,000 shares on 27/04/2026
- Lock-in applies to all 5,10,000 shares with varying expiry dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to a preferential allotment already approved and executed by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the listing and permitted trading effective June 12, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed by holders of these shares per the details below

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date 1:** 17 April 2026 — 2,00,000 shares allotted
- **Allotment Date 2:** 27 April 2026 — 3,10,000 shares allotted
- **Effective Listing Date:** 12 June 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,10,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 53362859–53472858):** 31 December 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (4,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 52962859–53362858):** 31 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 5,10,000 new equity shares to Kilburn Engineering Ltd.'s tradeable pool, though the immediate float impact is limited given that all shares are subject to lock-in until at least December 2026. The preferential allotment at Rs. 425/- per share reflects capital raised from promoters and select non-promoters via warrant conversion, which may signal confidence in the company's growth trajectory. Broader market impact is low given the company's mid-cap status.
