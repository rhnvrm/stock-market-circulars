## Summary

BSE has listed fresh commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by four companies — 360 ONE Prime Limited, HT Media Ltd (two separate CPs), SBICAP Securities Limited, and Can Fin Homes Ltd — on the BSE Debt Segment, effective May 26, 2026. All securities are available for trading exclusively in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four issuers have listed fresh commercial papers on the BSE Debt Segment under private placement.
- All CPs carry top-tier short-term credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.
- Trading is permitted only in demat form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples.
- Tick size for all listed CPs is 1 paise.
- Face value and paid-up value for all CPs is Rs. 5,00,000 per unit.
- Allotment date for all CPs is May 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under the BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form.
- All trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational listing notice with low market impact. The listed commercial papers are short-duration instruments (maturing between July and August 2026) with top-tier credit ratings (A1+), primarily relevant to institutional investors and debt market participants. The listing adds liquidity to these privately placed instruments by making them tradeable on the BSE Debt Segment. No broad market impact is expected.
