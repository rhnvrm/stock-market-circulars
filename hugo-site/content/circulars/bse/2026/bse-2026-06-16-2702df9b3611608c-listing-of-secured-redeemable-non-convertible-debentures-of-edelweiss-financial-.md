## Summary

BSE has admitted Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Edelweiss Financial Services Limited to dealings on the Exchange effective Wednesday, June 17, 2026, under the 'F Group'. Trading members are informed of the listing details, trading conditions, and relevant contact information.

## Key Points

- Securities admitted: Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures of Edelweiss Financial Services Limited
- Listed under the **F Group** segment on BSE
- Effective date: **Wednesday, June 17, 2026**
- Trading is permitted only in **dematerialized form** (per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)
- Market lot: **One Bond** (unit market lot)
- Financial year of the company ends on **31st March**

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The notice reiterates the existing SEBI guideline (February 16, 2000) that bonds must be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be in the unit market lot of one bond per transaction
- For clarifications, members may contact Edelweiss Financial Services Limited (Mr. Tarun Khurana, Compliance Officer & Company Secretary, Tel: +91 22 4079 5199, Email: efslncd@edelweissfin.com) or BSE's Mr. Hardik Bhuta (Tel: 022-22728814)

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Date of BSE notice
- **June 17, 2026**: Effective date for commencement of dealings on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with limited market impact. It provides Edelweiss Financial Services Limited's NCDs access to BSE's debt segment (F Group), enabling secondary market trading for debenture holders. The impact is confined to investors and trading members dealing in this specific NCD instrument. Registrar and Transfer Agent services are handled by KFIN Technologies Limited, Mumbai.
