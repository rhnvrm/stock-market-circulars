## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of GRD Securities Ltd. (Member No. 3007) in the Currency Derivative segment with effect from June 5, 2026. This is an administrative notice informing all trading members of the exchange about the change in membership status.

## Key Points

- GRD Securities Ltd. (Member No. 3007) has surrendered its trading membership in the Currency Derivative segment.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE effective June 5, 2026.
- The notice is issued by the Membership Compliance Department.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action reflecting a member's voluntary surrender of its trading rights in the Currency Derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required of other trading members. Those needing clarification on this matter may contact BSE Membership Operations at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 5, 2026 — Surrender of trading membership of GRD Securities Ltd. in the Currency Derivative segment.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to GRD Securities Ltd. and its clients in the Currency Derivative segment. Other trading members and the broader market are not affected. Clients of GRD Securities Ltd. who trade in currency derivatives should seek alternative trading arrangements through another registered member.
