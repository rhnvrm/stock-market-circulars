## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for June 2026 for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment. The notice specifies the last date for margin reporting and the penalty date for each trading day in June 2026.

## Key Points

- Covers all 22 trading days from 01-06-2026 to 30-06-2026 in the EGR segment.
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically 5 business days later) and a penalty date (one business day after the reporting deadline).
- The schedule extends into early July 2026 for penalty dates on trades at month-end.
- Issued by ICCL on behalf of BSE under the Settlement/RMS category.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

Members trading in the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment must report client margins by the specified last date for each trade date. Failure to report by the deadline will attract penalties on the corresponding penalty date.

## Important Dates

- First trade date covered: 01-06-2026 (margin reporting deadline: 08-06-2026; penalty date: 09-06-2026)
- Last trade date covered: 30-06-2026 (margin reporting deadline: 07-07-2026; penalty date: 08-07-2026)
- Penalty dates extend up to 08-07-2026 for June month-end trades.

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This is a standard monthly compliance calendar for EGR segment members. Members should update their internal compliance trackers with the June 2026 deadlines to avoid margin reporting penalties.
