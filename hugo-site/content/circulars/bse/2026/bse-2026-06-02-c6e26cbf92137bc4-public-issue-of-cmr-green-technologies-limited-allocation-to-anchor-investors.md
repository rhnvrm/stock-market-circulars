## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of CMR GREEN TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED has been subscribed. A total of 98,14,393 equity shares were allotted to Anchor Investors at a price of Rs. 192/- per equity share on June 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- CMR GREEN TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED is conducting a public issue (IPO) on the BSE Equity segment.
- Anchor Investor subscription took place on June 2, 2026.
- Total shares subscribed by Anchor Investors: 98,14,393 equity shares.
- Anchor Investor allotment price: Rs. 192/- per equity share.
- The announcement was issued by the company and is enclosed with the circular.
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural notice regarding anchor investor allocation as part of the IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the anchor investor allocation for their records and client communication purposes. No specific compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the allocation details.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Subscription Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation at Rs. 192/- per share signals institutional interest in CMR GREEN TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED's IPO. Successful anchor investor participation is typically a positive signal for retail and non-institutional investor confidence in the issue. The allotment of over 98 lakh shares to anchor investors at the issue price establishes the price benchmark for the public issue opening.
