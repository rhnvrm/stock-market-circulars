## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Hari and Company Investments Madras Private Limited is changing its registered name to Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited. The change takes effect on June 11, 2026 and applies across 16 debt scrip codes listed on the BOLT Plus system. The SCRIP IDs and abbreviated names remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Hari and Company Investments Madras Private Limited** to **Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited** (conversion from private to public limited company)
- Change is effective **June 11, 2026**
- Applies to 16 scrip codes in the **Debt segment**
- SCRIP IDs and abbreviated names on BOLT Plus System remain the same — no changes required to existing orders or references
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

The BOLT Plus System records for all 16 affected scrip codes will be updated to reflect the new company name. The abbreviated names (e.g., HCI161225, HCI191225, HCI090126, etc.) are unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new company name where applicable. No changes to scrip IDs or system identifiers are required.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 5, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The conversion from private limited to limited company status results only in a name update; all BOLT Plus scrip IDs and abbreviated names remain identical. Debt holders and trading members face no change in settlement, pricing, or identification codes. Sixteen scrip codes across the debt segment are affected.
