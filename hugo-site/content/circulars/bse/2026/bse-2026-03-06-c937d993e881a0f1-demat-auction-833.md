## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities scheduled for Demat Auction No. 833, dated 06/03/2026. A total of 87 scrips are included in this auction, covering a wide range of sectors. Demat auctions are conducted by exchanges to settle failed delivery obligations where sellers have not delivered securities on time.

## Key Points

- Auction Settlement No.: 833
- Date: 06/03/2026
- Total scrips listed: 87
- Covers both large-cap and SME securities
- Includes notable stocks such as Reliance Industries, Infosys, Asian Paints, ONGC, Vedanta, Yes Bank, and Lupin
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 27,542 shares (Route Mobile)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's standard auction settlement mechanism for failed deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of the auction process
- Buyers of securities with failed deliveries will receive auction proceeds or close-out rates as per BSE settlement rules
- Members should ensure settlement obligations are met to avoid inclusion in future auction lists

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 06/03/2026
- Settlement No.: 833

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard settlement mechanism circular with moderate operational impact. The majority of scrips have small quantities (1–200 shares), indicating minor short-delivery incidents. However, a few scrips have significantly large quantities:
- Route Mobile (543228): 27,542 shares
- SOFCOM SYSTEMS (538923): 17,625 shares
- Chandrima Medicare (540829): 9,762 shares
- TRANSGENE BIOTEK (526139): 12,213 shares
- Vikran Engineering (544496): 5,607 shares

These larger quantities may see more price impact during the auction process. Members holding positions in these scrips should monitor auction outcomes closely.
