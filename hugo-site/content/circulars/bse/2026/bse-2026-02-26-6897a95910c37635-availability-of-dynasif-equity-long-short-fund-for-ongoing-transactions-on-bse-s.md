## Summary

BSE has informed its members that DYNASIF EQUITY LONG - SHORT FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 27, 2026. This notice follows the earlier Notice No. 20260205-30 and covers six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- DYNASIF EQUITY LONG - SHORT FUND is reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 27, 2026.
- Both subscription and redemption facilities, along with SIP, will be available.
- Six scheme variants are covered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment).
- All six schemes fall under the Equity category.
- This notice is a follow-up to Notice No. 20260205-30.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an operational notice regarding platform availability of an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems and workflows to reflect the availability of DYNASIF EQUITY LONG - SHORT FUND schemes for ongoing transactions from February 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of DYNASIF EQUITY LONG - SHORT FUND for ongoing subscription, redemption, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has low market impact and is limited to operational implications for BSE StAR MF Platform participants. Investors and distributors using the platform will gain access to six DYNASIF EQUITY LONG - SHORT FUND scheme variants for transactions. The ISINs for the schemes are:
