## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members informing them that Government Security GS17JUN26C (scrip code 800376, ISIN IN000626C035) is approaching its redemption date and trading in this security must cease effective June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in GS17JUN26C (scrip code 800376, ISIN IN000626C035) is suspended from June 15, 2026
- The suspension is due to the security reaching its maturity/redemption date
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this security on or after June 15, 2026
- Reference: DR-651/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to existing BSE listing and debt segment rules for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must refrain from dealing in GS17JUN26C (scrip code 800376, ISIN IN000626C035) with effect from June 15, 2026
- Members holding or facilitating client positions in this security should ensure no fresh trades are executed on or after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 15, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective (DR-651/2026-2027)
- **June 17, 2026**: Implied redemption/maturity date based on the scrip name GS17JUN26C

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. This notice concerns a single government security nearing its natural maturity. Trading members and investors holding GS17JUN26C must ensure no transactions are initiated from June 15, 2026 onward. Redemption proceeds will be settled per standard RBI/BSE debt segment procedures. No broader market disruption is expected.
