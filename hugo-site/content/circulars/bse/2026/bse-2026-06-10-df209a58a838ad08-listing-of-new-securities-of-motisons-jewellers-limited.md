## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 40,00,000 new equity shares of Motisons Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 544053, ISIN: INE0FRK01020) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 11, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 17/- each (face value Rs. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 16/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 40,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- (issue price Rs. 17/-) to non-promoters
- Issued on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 984460001 to 988460000
- Date of Allotment: December 11, 2025
- Lock-in applies to all 40,00,000 shares until December 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice for listing of newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities and their eligibility for trading from June 11, 2026. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond acknowledging the listing.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 11, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 11, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 40,00,000 equity shares to the tradeable float of Motisons Jewellers Limited on BSE, though these remain under lock-in until December 15, 2026 and cannot be traded during that period. The impact on the broader market is minimal; this is a company-specific corporate action. Post lock-in expiry, the release of these shares may modestly increase available float for the stock.
