## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in 22 commercial papers issued by various companies effective from their respective record dates (February 10-11, 2026). Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers as the companies have fixed record dates for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- 22 commercial papers from different companies will suspend trading
- 12 securities have record date and suspension date of February 10, 2026
- 10 securities have record date and suspension date of February 11, 2026
- All suspensions are due to commercial paper redemption
- Notice references DR-817/2025-2026 and DR-818/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective suspension dates
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction from the specified dates
- Trading suspension applies to the debt segment only

## Important Dates

**February 10, 2026:**
- Record date and trading suspension for 12 commercial papers (Muthoot Capital Services, Time Technoplast, Adani Enterprises, Birla Group Holdings, Network 18 Media, ICICI Home Finance, Mirae Asset Financial Services, Dalmia Cement, Bajaj Financial Securities, Nuvama Wealth and Investment, Reliance Retail Ventures, Kalpataru Projects International)

**February 11, 2026:**
- Record date and trading suspension for 10 commercial papers (Embassy Office Parks REIT, Bajaj Financial Securities, Nuvama Wealth Finance, Aarti Industries, Godrej Housing Finance, Reliance Retail Ventures, Bajaj Finance, Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands, Supreme Industries, Navi Finserv)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants dealing in commercial papers but does not impact equity trading.

**Operational Impact:** Routine commercial paper redemption process; investors holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds on maturity. Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these specific ISINs from the suspension dates.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers will have their investments redeemed at maturity as per the original terms. No action required from investors except to expect redemption proceeds.
