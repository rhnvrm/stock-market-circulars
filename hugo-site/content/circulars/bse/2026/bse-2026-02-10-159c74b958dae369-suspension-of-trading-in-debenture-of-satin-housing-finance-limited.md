## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of SATIN HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED (Code: 975970, ISIN: INE02YC07018) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- ISIN: INE02YC07018
- BSE Code: 975970
- Debenture: SHFL-10.90%-27-2-26-PVT
- Interest Rate: 10.90%
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- No dealings allowed from: February 12, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **February 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 12, 2026**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **February 12, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

The trading suspension affects holders of SATIN HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED's 10.90% non-convertible debentures maturing on February 27, 2026. This is a routine corporate action to facilitate the redemption process and interest payment to eligible debenture holders as per the record date. The impact is limited to investors holding this specific debt instrument, and the suspension is temporary in nature to ensure proper settlement of the redemption and interest payment process.
