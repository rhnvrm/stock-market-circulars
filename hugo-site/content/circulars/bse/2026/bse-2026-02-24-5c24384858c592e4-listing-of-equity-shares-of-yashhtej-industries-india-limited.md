## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited
- Scrip Code: 544708
- Trading Symbol: YASHHTEJ
- ISIN: INE1O6P01016
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing admission notice under BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the upcoming listing. Further details including the listing date and security specifics will be provided in a follow-up circular, and members should act accordingly once that notice is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 24, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited immediate market impact as it is a preliminary listing announcement for a SME-segment company. The actual trading commencement and price discovery will occur on the listing date, which is yet to be specified. Relevant primarily to traders and investors tracking BSE SME IPOs and new listings.
