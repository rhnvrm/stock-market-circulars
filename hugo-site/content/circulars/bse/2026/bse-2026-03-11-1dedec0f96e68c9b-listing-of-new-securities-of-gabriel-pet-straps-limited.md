## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 18,13,996 new equity shares of Gabriel Pet Straps Limited (Scrip Code: 544108) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 256/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 246/-).

## Key Points

- 18,13,996 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 246/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 256/- per share
- Allotted to Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 5598341 to 7412336
- ISIN: INE0QZF01012
- Trading effective from March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from March 12, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies: all 18,13,996 shares (Dist. Nos. 5598341 to 7412336) are locked in until September 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 18,13,996 shares to the tradable float of Gabriel Pet Straps Limited, though the lock-in period until September 30, 2026 means these shares cannot be sold by allottees until that date. The preferential allotment to non-promoters at Rs. 256/- per share indicates a capital raise event for the company. Overall impact is confined to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip.
