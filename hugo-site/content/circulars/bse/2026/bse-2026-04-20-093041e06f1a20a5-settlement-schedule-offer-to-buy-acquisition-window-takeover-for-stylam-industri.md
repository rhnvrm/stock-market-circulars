## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has issued Notice No. 20260420-5 dated 20 April 2026 providing the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for Stylam Industries Limited. This notice governs the clearing and settlement timelines for eligible shareholders tendering shares in the open offer.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category on 20 April 2026.
- Pertains to the Offer to Buy via the BSE Acquisition Window under SEBI Takeover Regulations for Stylam Industries Limited.
- Specifies the settlement schedule that market participants and custodians must follow for the tender offer process.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the existing SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations framework for the specific Stylam Industries open offer via the BSE Acquisition Window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and custodians handling client tenders for Stylam Industries shares must adhere to the settlement schedule communicated in this notice.
- Participants must ensure timely delivery of shares and funds as per the prescribed settlement cycle.
- Any entity participating in the Acquisition Window tender must comply with ICCL's settlement and margin obligations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 20 April 2026
- Settlement schedule dates for the Acquisition Window tender: as specified by ICCL in the detailed schedule (refer to the source PDF for exact dates).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a targeted impact on shareholders of Stylam Industries Limited who are tendering shares in the open offer, as well as their brokers and custodians. It does not affect the broader market. Participants must align their back-office and settlement operations with the prescribed ICCL schedule to avoid settlement failures or penalties.
