## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that, under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited, 31,83,961 equity shares have been subscribed at Rs. 424/- per equity share on July 16, 2026. The notice encloses a copy of the company's announcement dated July 16, 2026 confirming this allocation.

## Key Points

- 31,83,961 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited public issue
- Anchor allocation price: Rs. 424/- per equity share
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship Department
- Signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine disclosure notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members beyond being informed of the anchor allocation for the purposes of trading in the IPO once listed.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 16, 2026
- Anchor investor subscription date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation at Rs. 424/- per share provides an early price signal for Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited's IPO and can influence subscriber sentiment in the retail and QIB portions of the issue. The impact is limited to participants and prospective investors in this specific IPO, with no bearing on existing listed securities.
