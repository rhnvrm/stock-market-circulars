## Summary

BSE has listed 53,94,075 new equity shares of Yug Decor Limited (Scrip Code: 540550) issued on a rights basis at par value of Rs. 10 each. The shares will commence trading from January 29, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. Additionally, the market lot size has been revised from 3,450 to 5,175 shares.

## Key Points

- 53,94,075 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 540550
- ISIN: INE796W01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 10789270 to 16183344
- Date of Allotment: January 27, 2026
- Trading commencement: January 29, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot revised from 3,450 shares to 5,175 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should update their systems to reflect the new distinctive number range and increased share capital

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: January 29, 2026
- **Market Lot Change Effective Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to Yug Decor Limited shareholders and traders. The rights issue increases the company's equity base by approximately 50% (from existing base implied by distinctive numbers). The market lot change to 5,175 shares will affect minimum trading quantities and may impact liquidity and accessibility for retail investors.
