## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two non-convertible bonds (NCDs) of Housing & Urban Development Corporation Ltd. (HUDCO) effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest on the same date.

## Key Points

- Two tax-free non-convertible bonds of HUDCO will be suspended from trading
- HUDCO 7.02% bond (ISIN: INE031A07AL1, Code: 935636) - suspended from January 16, 2026
- HUDCO 7.27% bond (ISIN: INE031A07AN7, Code: 935640) - suspended from January 16, 2026
- Record date set as January 16, 2026 for both bonds
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Reference: DR-800/2025-2026
- Trading members advised not to deal in these bonds from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified HUDCO bonds from January 16, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and record date
- No dealings permitted in ISIN INE031A07AL1 (935636) and INE031A07AN7 (935640) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 9, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 16, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** January 16, 2026
- **Purpose Date:** January 16, 2026 (redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only bondholders of two specific tax-free NCD series of HUDCO

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - routine suspension for corporate action purposes. Trading members need to ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction from January 16, 2026.

**Investor Impact:** Bondholders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment on the record date. No action required from investors who hold these bonds as they will automatically receive their dues.
