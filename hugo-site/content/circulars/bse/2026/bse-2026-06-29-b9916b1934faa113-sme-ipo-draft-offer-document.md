## Summary

BSE has published a notice informing the public that the Draft Offer Document of Infrax Renewable Limited, filed on June 26, 2026, is now available on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Infrax Renewable Limited on June 26, 2026
- Document made available on BSE SME website as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of notice (i.e., by July 20, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260629-27

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Any interested party or public stakeholder may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from June 29, 2026 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The Draft Offer Document is hosted at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 26, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it is a routine procedural step in the SME IPO process for Infrax Renewable Limited. It signals the company's intent to list on the BSE SME platform and invites public scrutiny of the draft offer document. Investors and analysts interested in the renewable energy SME space may wish to review the document and submit comments before the deadline.
