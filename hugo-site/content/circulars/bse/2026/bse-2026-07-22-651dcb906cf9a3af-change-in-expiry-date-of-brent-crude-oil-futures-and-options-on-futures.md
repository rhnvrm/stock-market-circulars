## Summary

BSE has issued a notice revising the expiry dates for certain contract months of Brent Crude Oil Futures and Options on Futures contracts. This follows an earlier notice (20260312-20 dated March 12, 2026) that had informed market participants of the contract launch calendar. Trading members are advised to note the updated expiry dates for the August 2026 and December 2026 contract months.

## Key Points

- Exchange notice number 20260722-41 dated July 22, 2026 revises previously announced expiry dates.
- Changes apply to both Brent Crude Oil Futures Contracts and Brent Crude Oil Options on Futures Contracts.
- Revised expiries are earlier than the originally scheduled dates by 1 to 3 days.
- Specification annexure for both futures and options contracts is attached to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

Brent Crude Oil Futures Contract:
- August 2026 contract (launched April 1, 2026): expiry moved from 31st August 2026 to 28th August 2026.
- December 2026 contract (launched August 3, 2026): expiry moved from 31st December 2026 to 30th December 2026.

Brent Crude Oil Options on Futures Contract:
- August 2026 contract (launched June 1, 2026): expiry moved from 27th August 2026 to 26th August 2026.
- December 2026 contract (launched October 1, 2026): expiry moved from 29th December 2026 to 28th December 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants dealing in Brent Crude Oil Futures and Options on Futures must update their systems and trading strategies to reflect the revised expiry dates. Participants should refer to the attached annexure for full updated contract specifications.

## Important Dates

- Brent Crude Oil Futures - August 2026 contract: revised expiry 28th August 2026 (previously 31st August 2026)
- Brent Crude Oil Futures - December 2026 contract: revised expiry 30th December 2026 (previously 31st December 2026)
- Brent Crude Oil Options - August 2026 contract: revised expiry 26th August 2026 (previously 27th August 2026)
- Brent Crude Oil Options - December 2026 contract: revised expiry 28th December 2026 (previously 29th December 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This change primarily impacts trading members and participants active in BSE's commodity derivatives segment, specifically those trading Brent Crude Oil futures and options contracts. The expiry date shifts are relatively minor (1-3 days earlier) but require attention to avoid settlement or position management errors. No direct impact on equity markets or listed stocks is expected, as this is confined to the commodity derivatives trading operations.
