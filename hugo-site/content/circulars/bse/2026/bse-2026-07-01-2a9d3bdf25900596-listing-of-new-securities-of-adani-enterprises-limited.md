## Summary

BSE has informed market participants that 90,11,048 new equity shares of Re.1/- each of Adani Enterprises Limited (Scrip Code: 512599) are listed and permitted for trading with effect from Thursday, July 2, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement among Adani Green Technology Limited (Amalgamating Company 1), Adani Emerging Businesses Private Limited (Amalgamating Company 2) and Adani Enterprises Limited (Amalgamated Company), and Adani Tradecom Limited (Transferor Company) and Adani New Industries Limited (Transferee Company), along with their respective shareholders and creditors.

## Key Points

- 90,11,048 new equity shares of Re.1/- each of Adani Enterprises Limited are listed and permitted for trading effective July 2, 2026.
- Shares were issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving Adani Green Technology Limited, Adani Emerging Businesses Private Limited, Adani Enterprises Limited, Adani Tradecom Limited, and Adani New Industries Limited.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of Adani Enterprises Limited.
- ISIN Number: INE423A01024 (existing ISIN of Adani Enterprises Limited).
- Distinctive Numbers: 1347514433 – 1356525480.
- Date of Allotment: April 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing approval notice issued under BSE Listing Operations following completion of a scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members; the new shares are automatically listed and tradable under the existing ISIN alongside currently listed shares of Adani Enterprises Limited.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 15, 2026
- Listing and trading effective date: July 2, 2026 (Thursday)
- Notice Date: July 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited given the modest scale of the new share issuance (90,11,048 shares) relative to Adani Enterprises Limited's total outstanding equity. Since the shares rank pari-passu with existing shares and use the same ISIN, there is no new instrument creation or trading disruption. This listing formalizes completion of a corporate restructuring (composite scheme of arrangement) among several Adani group entities, which may be of interest to shareholders tracking the group's internal reorganization but is unlikely to materially affect Adani Enterprises' share price or liquidity.
