## Summary

BSE received clarifications from four companies - Yajur Fibres Ltd., Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Ltd., India Tourism Development Corporation Ltd., and Gala Precision Engineering Ltd. - in response to surveillance queries regarding significant price movements or volume increases in their securities. All companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 and stated they are not aware of any undisclosed material information that could explain the market movements.

## Key Points

- Yajur Fibres Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544676) clarified it is not aware of reasons for significant price movement and attributes it to market-driven forces
- Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Ltd. (Scrip Symbol: 532872) confirmed compliance with disclosure requirements regarding volume increase
- India Tourism Development Corporation Ltd. stated no announcement has been made and no information is due for disclosure
- All companies confirmed they have made all material disclosures required under SEBI Listing Regulations
- Companies stated management is not connected with the price/volume movements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies committed to continue making adequate and timely disclosures to stock exchanges
- All material information with bearing on operations/performance has been disclosed within stipulated time

## Important Dates

- Clarifications dated: February 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine surveillance responses where companies confirm no undisclosed material events exist. The clarifications indicate that recent price and volume movements in these securities are market-driven rather than based on fundamental developments. Investors should note that no new material information has been identified by the companies to explain the trading activity.
