## Summary

MOTILAL MUTUAL FUND is launching MOTILAL OSWAL DIVERSIFIED EQUITY FLEXICAP PASSIVE FUND OF FUNDS as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 02, 2026 to January 15, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of January 21, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan options with Growth variants will be available.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: January 02, 2026 to January 15, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: January 21, 2026
- Two scheme variants available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GM7) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GL9)
- Scheme Category: Equity Fund of Funds
- Extended trading till midnight on January 15, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 21, 2026
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clearing conditions
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds are received by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Pending transactions will remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 21, 2026 for unit allotment
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on January 15, 2026
- For switches, funds under NFO scheme must be received on or before allotment date (January 21, 2026) for allotment consideration
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for transactions where client has not initiated funds by specified deadline

## Important Dates

- **January 02, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **January 15, 2026**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **January 15, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **January 21, 2026**: Tentative allotment date
- **January 21, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **T+1, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (post-transaction)
- **T+5 or last payout day, 3:00 PM**: Pending transaction system retention limit

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch that expands investment options for mutual fund investors on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for BSE members and mutual fund distributors who will need to facilitate client transactions during the NFO period. Standard NFO procedures apply with specific timelines for fund collection and settlement.

**Investor Impact**: Provides new investment opportunity in a diversified equity flexicap passive fund of funds structure, offering both regular and direct plan options with growth variants. Investors can participate through lumpsum or SIP routes during the NFO period.
