## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a redistribution of scrips across products in the Equity segment. The revised scrip configuration will take effect from Monday, July 27, 2026. Members must use the updated Security Master file, to be shared by BSE on the evening of July 24, 2026, which will apply from July 27, 2026 onward.

## Key Points

- BSE is redistributing scrips across products within the Equity segment.
- Revised scrip configuration becomes effective Monday, July 27, 2026.
- An updated Security Master file will be shared by BSE on July 24, 2026 (end of day).
- Members are required to use this latest Security Master file mandatorily.
- Members should test the revised configuration during the mock trading session on Saturday, July 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational/administrative notice from BSE's Trading Operations department rather than a regulatory rule change. It pertains to internal reclassification of scrips across trading products.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must download and mandatorily use the updated Security Master file shared by BSE EOD on July 24, 2026.
- Members must participate in and test the revised configuration during the mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 25, 2026, to ensure readiness for live trading.
- Queries can be directed to BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) or the BSE Help Desk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026 (EOD): Updated Security Master file to be released.
- July 25, 2026 (Saturday): Mock trading session for testing the revised scrip configuration.
- July 27, 2026 (Monday): Revised scrip configuration becomes effective for live trading.

## Impact Assessment

The change is operational in nature, affecting how scrips are distributed across trading products on BSE's systems. Trading members must update their systems with the new Security Master file and validate readiness through the mock session to avoid disruptions when the new configuration goes live on July 27, 2026. Failure to update systems in time could result in trading errors or mismatches for affected scrips, but the overall market impact is limited to operational/technical readiness rather than affecting specific listed companies or investors directly.
