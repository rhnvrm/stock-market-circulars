## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window – Takeover) for AVI Products India Limited. PPMS Real Estates LLP (Acquirer) is making an open offer to public shareholders of AVI Products India Limited at Rs. 33.00 per equity share to acquire up to 8,59,769 fully paid-up equity shares, representing 26% of the voting share capital of the target company. The offer is conducted under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: PPMS Real Estates LLP
- Target Company: AVI Products India Limited
- Offer Price: Rs. 33.00/- per equity share (payable in cash)
- Offer Size: 8,59,769 equity shares (26% of voting share capital)
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00/- per equity share
- Regulatory Basis: SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 – Regulations 3(1) and 4
- Mechanism: BSE Acquisition Window as per SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated Dec 09, 2016
- Letter of Offer attached to the BSE notice for participant reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The offer operates under the existing framework established by:
- SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016
- BSE notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62 (Revised Guidelines for acquisition via Stock Exchange)

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians**: Must note the open offer window and facilitate client participation in tendering shares through the BSE acquisition mechanism.
- **Public Shareholders of AVI Products India Limited**: May tender up to 26% of the company's voting share capital during the offer period at the stated price of Rs. 33.00 per share.
- Offer details are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: May 11, 2026
- **Offer Opening Date**: Wednesday, May 13, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date**: Tuesday, May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer is significant for existing public shareholders of AVI Products India Limited, who must evaluate whether to tender shares at Rs. 33.00/- per share. The offer represents a potential change in control, with the acquirer seeking 26% of voting capital. Trading members and custodians handling accounts with AVI Products India Limited holdings must process client instructions through the BSE acquisition window within the specified offer period. The offer has no broad market impact but is material to the specific stock and its shareholders.
