## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 2,460 new equity shares of Bosch Ltd (Scrip Code: 500530), issued to promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,460 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 35,190/- per share to Promoters on a preferential basis.
- Issue price: Rs. 35,200/- per share.
- Distinctive numbers: 32051461 to 32053920.
- Date of allotment: 10/06/2026.
- ISIN: INE323A01026.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing approval notice; no regulatory or procedural changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members or investors beyond recognizing the new shares as listed and tradeable from the effective date, subject to the lock-in restriction noted below.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 10/06/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 30/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing involves a small quantum of shares (2,460) issued to promoters on a preferential basis and is immaterial relative to Bosch Ltd's overall share capital. The shares are locked in until January 30, 2028, limiting any near-term impact on free float or trading volumes. Overall market and operational impact is minimal.
