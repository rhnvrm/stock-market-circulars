## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Purple Entertainment Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited with effect from November 4, 2025. The certificate was digitally signed by the Assistant Registrar of Companies at the Central Processing Centre, Manesar.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L65100GJ1974PLC084389
- Original name: Purple Entertainment Limited
- New name: Purple Agrotech Industries Limited
- Effective date of name change: November 4, 2025
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC Manesar
- Approved by: Mitika Raja, Assistant Registrar of Companies
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered office: 30-B, Third Floor, Ajanta Complex, Income Tax, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad-380009, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes applicable. This is a standard administrative procedure for corporate name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (Purple Entertainment Limited) and new name (Purple Agrotech Industries Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act, 2013
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, agreements, and legal documents remain valid under the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Purple Entertainment Limited to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited
- **November 4, 2027**: End of two-year period during which both old and new names must be displayed

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a routine corporate administrative action that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update its letterheads, signage, website, and other branding materials to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years as mandated.

**Legal Impact**: The name change does not affect any existing rights, liabilities, contracts, or obligations of the company or its stakeholders. All legal relationships continue under the new corporate identity.

**Investor Consideration**: Shareholders should note the new company name for future correspondence and documentation. The stock symbol and CIN remain unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading and corporate identification.
