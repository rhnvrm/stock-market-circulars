## Summary

BSE has released the penalty report for client code modifications of non-institutional trades for January 2026. The report (file name: PM0126.Clg no.) is available in the Equity Transaction Folder on the BSE Extranet platform. This penalty framework was established under Exchange Notice Nos. 20110729-24 (July 29, 2011) and 20110826-4 (August 26, 2011).

## Key Points

- Penalty report covers client code modifications for non-institutional trades during January 2026
- Report file name: PM0126.Clg no.
- Available in Equity Transaction Folder on BSE Extranet
- Members must save files for future reference
- Framework established under 2011 Exchange Notices
- Applies to all trading segments

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is a routine notification regarding the availability of penalty reports under existing framework established in 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must download the penalty report from the Equity Transaction Folder on Extranet
- Members are required to save the downloaded files for future reference
- Any affected members should review penalties imposed for client code modifications during January 2026
- Contact BSE Trading Operations for queries at 022-2272 8435/5785 or bse.reports@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Report Period**: January 2026
- **Download Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Reference Notices**: Exchange Notice No. 20110729-24 (July 29, 2011) and 20110826-4 (August 26, 2011)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine monthly report release affecting only trading members who modified client codes for non-institutional trades during January 2026.

**Market Impact**: None. Administrative circular with no impact on trading, pricing, or market operations.

**Member Impact**: Low. Affects only those trading members who modified client codes during the reporting period. Members must review applicable penalties and maintain records for compliance purposes.
