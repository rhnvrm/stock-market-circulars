## Summary

BSE's Index Cell has announced the addition of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited (Exchange ticker: 544794) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company was listed on BSE on June 19, 2026, and will be included in the index effective at the open of trading on Monday, June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited (ticker: 544794) listed on BSE on June 19, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective June 22, 2026 (market open)
- This action references and follows from Notice No. 20260618-28
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 19, 2026
- Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action triggered by the new listing of an SME IPO company on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should note the updated composition effective June 22, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited listed on BSE; corporate action included in end-of-day *.SDE file
- **June 22, 2026**: Stock addition to BSE SME IPO Index becomes effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine index maintenance action. The addition of a newly listed SME IPO stock to the BSE SME IPO Index is a standard process following every eligible new listing. Impact is limited to index-linked products (funds, ETFs) tracking the BSE SME IPO Index, which will need to adjust their portfolios to include Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited before the June 22, 2026 open.
