## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further equity shares issued by seven listed companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- ESAF Small Finance Bank Limited (ISIN INE818W01011, Scrip Code 544020): 51,391 shares of face value ₹10 listed.
- IDFC First Bank Limited (ISIN INE092T01019, Scrip Code 539437): 21,67,979 shares of face value ₹10 listed.
- Jana Small Finance Bank Limited (ISIN INE953L01027, Scrip Code 544118): 3,73,436 shares of face value ₹10 listed.
- Max Estates Limited (ISIN INE03EI01018, Scrip Code 544008): 16,140 shares of face value ₹10 listed.
- Metro Brands Limited (ISIN INE317I01021, Scrip Code 543426): 3,873 shares of face value ₹5 listed.
- Shriram Finance Limited (ISIN INE721A01047, Scrip Code 511218): 33,311 shares of face value ₹2 listed.
- ZIM Laboratories Limited (ISIN INE518E01015, Scrip Code 541400): 1,09,451 shares of face value ₹10 listed.
- None of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated distinctive number ranges and share counts for the respective ISINs for trading and settlement purposes. No further action is required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective Listing Date: Wednesday, July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a minor dilution from ESOP/ESOS allotments and does not materially alter free-float or market capitalization for any of the seven companies. This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing update relevant mainly to back-office and settlement processes.
