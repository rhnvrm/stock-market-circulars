## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies - Genus Prime Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 532425), Indo Count Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 521016), and RNIT AI Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 517286) - in response to BSE Surveillance queries regarding significant price and/or volume movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed that there is no pending or impending price sensitive information that could have caused the movements, and attributed the price/volume changes to market-driven factors. The companies reiterated their commitment to comply with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- **Genus Prime Infra Limited** responded to BSE surveillance query dated February 05, 2026 (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/901)
- **Indo Count Industries Limited** responded to NSE query dated February 4, 2026 (Ref. No.: NSE/CM/Surveillance/16429) and BSE query dated February 5, 2026 (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/903)
- **RNIT AI Solutions Limited** responded to BSE query dated February 04, 2026 regarding price movement
- All three companies confirmed no unpublished price sensitive information or pending announcements
- Companies stated that price/volume movements are purely market driven
- All companies confirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance and disclosure compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond promptly to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any undisclosed price sensitive information exists
- Companies must continue timely disclosure of all material events under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies should maintain materiality policies as laid down by their Board of Directors

## Important Dates

- **February 04, 2026**: NSE surveillance query sent to Indo Count Industries
- **February 04, 2026**: BSE surveillance query sent to RNIT AI Solutions
- **February 05, 2026**: BSE surveillance queries sent to Genus Prime Infra and Indo Count Industries
- **February 05, 2026**: All three companies submitted their clarification responses

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine clarifications confirming no material undisclosed information. The price/volume movements in these securities are attributed to normal market dynamics rather than corporate actions or pending announcements.

**Investor Impact**: Low - The clarifications provide transparency that no price sensitive information is being withheld, which may help prevent speculation. Investors can be assured that the companies are maintaining disclosure discipline.

**Operational Impact**: None - No operational changes or actions required from market participants. This is part of standard exchange surveillance procedures to ensure market integrity and prevent information asymmetry.
