## Summary

BSE has introduced the Daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for The Wealth Company Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) Platform. This enhancement provides investors with the option to make daily investments in schemes offered by The Wealth Company Mutual Fund through the BSE platform.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP facility is now available for The Wealth Company Mutual Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Investors can now opt for daily investment frequency in addition to existing SIP options
- The facility enables more frequent and flexible investment options for retail investors
- Applicable to schemes offered by The Wealth Company Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform enhancement to provide additional investment frequency options.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an operational update for the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 2, 2026
- Effective Date: As per the circular issuance

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a platform feature enhancement that provides additional flexibility for mutual fund investors.

**Operational Impact:** Low - The update affects only investors who wish to use daily SIP options for The Wealth Company Mutual Fund schemes.

**Investor Impact:** Positive - Provides more granular investment frequency options for retail investors seeking daily investment discipline.
