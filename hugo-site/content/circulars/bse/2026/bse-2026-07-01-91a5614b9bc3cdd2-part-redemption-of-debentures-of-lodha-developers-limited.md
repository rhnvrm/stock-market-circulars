## Summary

BSE, in continuation to its earlier Notice No. 20260609-50 dated June 09, 2026 (which fixed the Record Date for part redemption), has now informed the actual reduced face value for two debenture series of Lodha Developers Limited. Trading Members are advised that these debentures will trade at the reduced face value with effect from July 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Lodha Developers Limited
- Series 1: LDL-Reset Rate-21-01-28-PVT, ISIN INE670K07307, Scrip Code 976923 — Reduced Face Value: Rs. 78,261/- per debenture
- Series 2: LDL-TBILLS-13-11-28-PVT, ISIN INE670K07349, Scrip Code 977293 — Reduced Face Value: Rs. 59,247/- per debenture
- This follows the part redemption process for which the Record Date was earlier notified via Notice No. 20260609-50 dated June 09, 2026
- Reference: DR-663/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational follow-up notice implementing the part redemption already announced, specifying the actual reduced face values post-redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the reduced face values and ensure trades in these debentures are executed and settled at the revised face value from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- June 09, 2026: Original notice (20260609-50) fixing Record Date for part redemption
- July 01, 2026: Notice date informing actual reduced face value
- July 02, 2026: Effective date from which debentures will trade at reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the two specific Lodha Developers Limited debenture series (scrip codes 976923 and 977293). Trading Members must update systems to reflect the reduced face values to ensure accurate pricing and settlement from July 02, 2026 onward. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
