## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies — Lime Chemicals Limited, Siddha Ventures Limited, and True Green Bio Energy Limited (formerly CIL Nova Petrochemicals Ltd.) — in response to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price and/or volume movements in their respective securities. All three companies confirmed they are in compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, and that no material price-sensitive information has been withheld.

## Key Points

- **Lime Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 507759)** responded to BSE email No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/953 dated 05th March 2026, stating no significant price-sensitive information is currently pending disclosure.
- **Siddha Ventures Ltd (Scrip Code: 530439)** responded to BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/3406 dated 04th March 2026, confirming all material disclosures are current and compliant.
- **True Green Bio Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 533407)** responded to BSE email No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/950 dated 04th March 2026, confirming no material information has been withheld regarding the spurt in volume and price.
- All three companies affirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.
- No material events or information were identified as responsible for the price/volume movements.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. The companies reference existing obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to continue monitoring and disclosing any material price-sensitive information in accordance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.
- No new compliance actions are required from market participants as a result of these clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **05th March 2026**: Lime Chemicals Ltd and True Green Bio Energy Ltd submitted clarifications to BSE.
- **05th March 2026**: Siddha Ventures Ltd submitted clarification (in response to BSE query dated 04th March 2026).
- **04th March 2026**: BSE issued surveillance queries to Siddha Ventures Ltd and True Green Bio Energy Ltd.
- **05th March 2026**: BSE issued surveillance query to Lime Chemicals Ltd.

## Impact Assessment

These are routine surveillance-driven clarifications with no material market impact. The disclosures reassure investors that no undisclosed price-sensitive events are pending for these three companies. Traders and investors in Lime Chemicals (507759), Siddha Ventures (530439), and True Green Bio Energy (533407) should note that the companies themselves attribute the price/volume movements to factors other than undisclosed corporate events. No trading restrictions or regulatory actions have been announced.
