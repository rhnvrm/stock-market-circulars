## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of 10 companies effective from January 20-21, 2026, due to upcoming redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified no-dealings dates, which coincide with the respective record dates for redemption.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- 6 securities suspended from January 20, 2026
- 4 securities suspended from January 21, 2026
- Suspension applies to debt segment trading
- Notice issued under category: Corporate Actions

## Securities Suspended (January 20, 2026)

1. **Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited** (PICL-21-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE417C14850, Code: 728621)
2. **Standard Chartered Capital Limited** (SCCL-21-01-26-CP, ISIN: INE403G14TL5, Code: 729250)
3. **ONGC Petro additions Limited** (ONGCPAL-21-01-26-CP, ISIN: INE163N14550, Code: 729903)
4. **Bajaj Financial Securities Limited** (BFSL-21-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE01C314EB5, Code: 730878)

## Securities Suspended (January 21, 2026)

1. **Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited** (JBCIPL-22-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE824H14RH2, Code: 728628)
2. **Vivriti Capital Limited** (VCL-22-01-26-CP, ISIN: INE01HV14979, Code: 730308)
3. **Godrej Housing Finance Limited** (GHFL-22-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE02JD14641, Code: 730430)
4. **Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited** (NWIL-22-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE523L14AN6, Code: 730431)
5. **Navi Finserv Limited** (NFL-22-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE342T14ES5, Code: 730438)
6. **Bajaj Finance Limited** (BFL-22-1-26-CP, ISIN: INE296A14E04, Code: 730822)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial paper from the respective no-dealings dates
- Suspension is mandatory for all securities listed above
- Applies to all trading activity in the debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date (Batch 1)**: January 20, 2026 (4 securities)
- **No Dealings From (Batch 1)**: January 20, 2026
- **Record Date (Batch 2)**: January 21, 2026 (6 securities)
- **No Dealings From (Batch 2)**: January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide impact. The suspension affects short-term debt instruments nearing maturity and is standard procedure for commercial paper redemption. Trading members holding these securities should prepare for redemption settlement. The action affects multiple financial sector entities including NBFC, investment companies, and housing finance companies. No broader debt market implications expected as this is a scheduled maturity event.
