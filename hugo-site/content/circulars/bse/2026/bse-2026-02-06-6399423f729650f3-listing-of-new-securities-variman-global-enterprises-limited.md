## Summary

VARIMAN GLOBAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540570) has listed 5,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 9.50/- on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters. These shares will be permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Monday, February 9, 2026. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until August 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 1/- each issued at Rs. 10.50/- (including premium of Rs. 9.50/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares
- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE717F01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 194581001 to 195081000
- All 5,00,000 shares subject to lock-in until August 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 5,00,000 shares until August 15, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded by allottees during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 9, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: August 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares for VARIMAN GLOBAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED. The 5,00,000 shares represent additional equity capital but are subject to lock-in restrictions until mid-August 2026, preventing immediate supply increase in the market. Trading members dealing with scrip code 540570 should be aware of the expanded share capital and lock-in constraints.
