## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,24,772 new equity shares of Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543978, ISIN: INE05CZ01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 148.27/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 138.27/-).

## Key Points

- 1,24,772 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 138.27/- (total issue price Rs. 148.27/-) to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 67870464 to 67995235
- Trading permitted from June 9, 2026
- Lock-in applies on all allotted shares until January 31, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities pursuant to a preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities. The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded by the promoter allottees until January 31, 2028.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 12, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The preferential allotment of 1,24,772 shares represents a small addition to the company's share capital. The shares are locked in until January 2028, so there is no near-term increase in tradeable float. Routine corporate action with no broader market or systemic implications.
