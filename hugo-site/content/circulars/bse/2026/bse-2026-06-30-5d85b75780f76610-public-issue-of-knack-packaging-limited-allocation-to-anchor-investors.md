## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that in the public issue of KNACK PACKAGING LIMITED, 77,20,587 equity shares have been allocated to Anchor Investors (AIs) at a price of Rs. 161 per equity share on June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 77,20,587 equity shares subscribed under the Anchor Investor portion of the Knack Packaging Limited public issue
- Anchor Investor price fixed at Rs. 161 per equity share
- Announcement issued by the company on June 30, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural intimation as required under SEBI guidelines for public issues involving anchor investor allocations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the anchor investor allocation details for KNACK PACKAGING LIMITED. No specific action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the allocation.

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Date of anchor investor allocation and company announcement

## Impact Assessment

This notice is informational in nature, confirming the anchor investor subscription for Knack Packaging Limited's public issue at Rs. 161 per share. Anchor investor allocations indicate institutional interest ahead of the broader public subscription window and may signal price discovery for retail investors. Impact is limited to prospective investors and trading members handling IPO-related orders for this company.
