## Summary

BSE has published Notice No. 20260611-24 announcing that the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Q&T Foods Limited, filed on June 10, 2026, is now available on the BSE SME website for a mandatory public comment period of at least 21 days, as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Q&T Foods Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE for an SME IPO.
- The document was filed on June 10, 2026, and is hosted on the BSE SME website.
- The public comment window is open for a minimum of 21 days from the date of notice (June 11, 2026).
- Public comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.
- The notice is issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued in compliance with the existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations), which mandate that draft offer documents be made public for comment before an IPO proceeds.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is available at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Interested parties, analysts, and the public may review the document and submit comments within 21 days from June 11, 2026.
- Comments must be emailed to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before July 2, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural disclosure with no immediate trading or market-wide impact. It concerns Q&T Foods Limited, a company seeking to list on the BSE SME platform. The notice provides an opportunity for the public to review and comment on the company's disclosures before the IPO proceeds. Impact is limited to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in the SME IPO segment.
