## Summary

Matrimony.com Limited has announced a buyback offer for up to 8,93,129 fully paid-up equity shares of face value ₹5 each at a buyback price of ₹655 per share. The buyback represents 4.14% of the total number of equity shares in the paid-up equity share capital as on March 31, 2025. The aggregate buyback amount is ₹5,850 lakhs (excluding transaction costs). The buyback is being conducted through the tender offer route on a proportionate basis from all eligible equity shareholders/beneficial owners as on the record date of January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Buyback quantity: Up to 8,93,129 equity shares (4.14% of paid-up equity capital)
- Buyback price: ₹655 per equity share
- Buyback size: ₹5,850 lakhs (excluding transaction costs)
- Face value: ₹5 per equity share
- Method: Tender offer route on proportionate basis
- Two categories: Reserved category for small shareholders and general category for other eligible shareholders
- Record date: January 30, 2026
- Manager to the Buyback: Saffron Capital Advisors Private Limited
- Registrar to the Buyback: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable. This is a corporate action circular announcing a buyback offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Buyback conducted in accordance with Article 16 of the Articles of Association
- Complies with Sections 68, 69, 70, 110 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013
- Complies with Companies (Share Capital and Debentures) Rules, 2014
- Conducted as per SEBI (Buy-Back of Securities) Regulations, 2018
- Complies with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Subject to approvals from statutory/regulatory authorities including SEBI, NSE, BSE, ROC, and RBI as required

## Important Dates

- Record Date: January 30, 2026
- Financial statements reference date: March 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Financial Impact:** The buyback size of ₹5,850 lakhs represents 24.68% of aggregate paid-up equity capital and free reserves per standalone audited financials and 24.81% per consolidated financials as on March 31, 2025.

**Shareholder Impact:** Eligible shareholders as on record date can participate in the buyback on proportionate basis. Small shareholders have a reserved category allocation. The buyback price of ₹655 per share provides an exit opportunity at a premium for participating shareholders.

**Capital Structure Impact:** The buyback will reduce the paid-up equity share capital by up to 4.14%, potentially improving earnings per share and return ratios for continuing shareholders.

**Market Impact:** High importance corporate action that may impact stock liquidity and price dynamics. Shareholders need to respond to the tender offer if they wish to participate.
