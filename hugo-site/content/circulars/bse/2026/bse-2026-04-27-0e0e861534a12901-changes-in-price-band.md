## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 24 equity scrips effective April 28, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, reflecting updated circuit limits based on surveillance assessments. Trading members are required to note the new limits immediately.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 24 scrips across various sectors effective April 28, 2026
- Revised bands: 2% (3 scrips), 5% (11 scrips), 10% (7 scrips), 20% (2 scrips)
- Action taken by DOSS (Department of Surveillance and Supervision)
- Notice No. 20260427-26 issued April 27, 2026
- Clarifications to be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions are a surveillance tool used by BSE to control excessive volatility in individual scrips. The revised limits replace the existing circuit filters for the listed securities. Two scrips — Emiac Technologies Ltd (544747) and Safety Controls & Devices Ltd (544746) — have been assigned the widest band of 20%, while Hindusthan Insulators & Industries Ltd (539984), Indsoya Ltd (503639), and Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd (507968) have been restricted to a 2% band.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new price bands before market open on April 28, 2026
- No further action required from listed companies; this is a trading-side restriction
- Members with queries must contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: Notice issued
- **April 28, 2026**: Revised price bands come into effect
