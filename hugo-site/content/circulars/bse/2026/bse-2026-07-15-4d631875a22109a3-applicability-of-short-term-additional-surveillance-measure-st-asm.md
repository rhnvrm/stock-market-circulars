## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that satisfy the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, under both Stage I and Stage II. This follows a series of earlier exchange notices establishing and revising the ASM framework since 2018. The notice specifies increased margin requirements applicable to open and new positions in the identified securities effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion.
- Securities listed in Annexure I have also met the criteria for ST-ASM Stage II inclusion.
- ST-ASM Stage I: applicable margin rate is 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- ST-ASM Stage II: applicable margin rate is 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse against the concerned company/entity.
- Price bands for scrips exiting the framework will revert to the pre-shortlisting price band, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework (established via prior notices dated September 20, 2024, October 27, 2018, July 19, 2019, December 4, 2020, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024) to a fresh set of identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the revised margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II securities, subject to the higher of the specified rate or existing margin, capped at 100%) on all open positions as of July 16, 2026, and on new positions created from July 17, 2026, for securities listed in Annexure I. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 16, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margins.
- July 17, 2026: Effective date for revised margin rates on open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements will raise the cost of holding or creating positions in the identified securities, likely reducing speculative trading activity and liquidity in these scrips. Market participants holding or trading the affected securities should reassess position sizing and funding requirements ahead of the July 17, 2026 effective date. The specific list of affected securities is contained in the referenced Annexure, which was not included in the extracted text.
