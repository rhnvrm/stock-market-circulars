## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Batlivala & Karani Securities India Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 736) has officially changed its name to 360 One Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 736) following a change in management. The name change has been approved by SEBI and is effective from 20 April 2026.

## Key Points

- Member No. 736, previously known as Batlivala & Karani Securities India Pvt. Ltd., is now named 360 One Capital Market Pvt. Ltd.
- The change is due to a change in management at the member firm.
- SEBI has formally approved the name change.
- The member number (736) remains unchanged.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an administrative update reflecting SEBI-approved name change of a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name — 360 One Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. — for Member No. 736 when dealing with this entity on or after 20 April 2026.

## Important Dates

- **20 April 2026**: Effective date of the name change from Batlivala & Karani Securities India Pvt. Ltd. to 360 One Capital Market Pvt. Ltd.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice. The name change affects only the identity of a single trading member (No. 736) and has no implications for broader market operations, trading rules, or listed securities. Counterparties and trading members transacting with this entity should update their internal records accordingly.
