## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260603-1 dated 03 June 2026 announcing a change in the group classification of equity shares of Rajnandini Fashion India Limited, listed on the BSE SME segment. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260603-1 dated 03 June 2026
- Affects equity shares of Rajnandini Fashion India Limited
- Applicable to the SME segment
- Issued by Department: DCS-Listing
- No attachments provided with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

The group classification of the equity shares of Rajnandini Fashion India Limited has been changed on the BSE SME platform. Group classifications on BSE (e.g., A, B, T, XT, M, MT) determine trading conditions such as settlement type, applicable margins, and circuit filter limits.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants should note the revised group classification for Rajnandini Fashion India Limited
- Applicable margin and settlement requirements as per the new group should be followed with effect from the notified date

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 03 June 2026
- Effective date of group change: Not explicitly stated; participants should refer to BSE systems for the operative date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action affecting a single SME-segment company. Group reclassification may alter margin requirements, circuit filter limits, and settlement cycles for trades in the scrip. Impact is limited to traders and investors holding or transacting in Rajnandini Fashion India Limited shares on the BSE SME platform.
