## Summary

BSE has notified members that the Mirae Asset BSE India Defence ETF FOF, previously launched as an NFO (referenced in Notice No. 20260130-26), has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption along with SIP, STP & SWP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- The Mirae Asset BSE India Defence ETF FOF NFO scheme is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Both Regular and Direct plans are available across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are also enabled.
- Six scheme variants are now live with distinct ISINs.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational activation notice enabling ongoing transactions for an existing NFO scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond ensuring the platform is updated to reflect the availability of these schemes for transactions from February 24, 2026.
