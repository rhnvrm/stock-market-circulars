## Summary

BSE has admitted Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited as a new Trading Member with Member Number 6975. The firm has been granted trading rights on BSE Ltd. in the Cash segment, subject to the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- New member admitted: Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited
- Member Number: 6975
- Segment: Cash
- Type of Membership: New Membership
- Trading rights granted on BSE Ltd. only
- Admission subject to Exchange Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. The new member must comply with all applicable BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 2, 2026
- Effective Date: January 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with no operational impact on existing trading members or market participants. The admission of a new trading member expands the BSE trading community but does not affect trading rules, settlement procedures, or compliance obligations for existing members. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
