## Summary

BSE has updated the applicability of the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Effective June 25, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as of June 24, 2026 and new positions created from June 25, 2026. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and some securities are being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups or exiting the framework, both effective June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 25, 2026 on all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages w.e.f. June 23, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable w.e.f. June 23, 2026 for stage-upgraded securities
- Securities continuing in LT-ASM but transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group w.e.f. June 25, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM Framework w.e.f. June 23, 2026 (Annexure II)
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely for market surveillance purposes

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references revisions to the LT-ASM framework per the following prior notices: 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20240809-46 (August 9, 2024), 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024), and 20210604-41 (June 4, 2021). The current update applies the revised framework to updated security lists.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margins are maintained on affected securities from June 25, 2026
- Members holding positions in securities shortlisted under LT-ASM must comply with the applicable margin and price band requirements
- The LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Price band of securities exiting the framework reverts to the pre-shortlisting band, unless subject to another surveillance measure
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; Lower Price Band applies; Securities exit from LT-ASM Framework
- **June 24, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 25, 2026**: 100% margin requirement becomes effective; Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding positions in affected securities, reducing leverage and liquidity for market participants. Movement to higher LT-ASM stages and lower price bands restricts upside trading potential. Securities exiting the framework will see restoration of normal price bands (subject to no other surveillance measures), improving trading conditions. This circular affects equity segment participants, particularly those trading in smaller or flagged scrips under surveillance.
