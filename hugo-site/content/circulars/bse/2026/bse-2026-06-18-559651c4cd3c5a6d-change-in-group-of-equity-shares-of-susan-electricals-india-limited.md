## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260618-1 dated 18 June 2026 notifying a change in the group classification of equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department and pertains to the SME segment.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260618-1 dated 18 June 2026.
- Subject concerns a change in the group of equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited.
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.
- Applicable to the SME segment of BSE.
- No attachments are included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

The group classification of the equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited on BSE has been revised. Group changes on BSE typically affect the trading settlement cycle, circuit filters, or surveillance category applicable to a security.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants — including brokers, traders, and investors — holding or trading in Susan Electricals India Limited equity shares should take note of the revised group classification and ensure trading activity conforms to the updated parameters applicable to the new group.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 18 June 2026
- Effective date of the group change is not explicitly stated in the notice; participants should refer to the BSE website or contact DCS-Listing for confirmation.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to traders and investors in Susan Electricals India Limited on the BSE SME segment. A group change may alter the applicable circuit limits, settlement type (e.g., rolling versus trade-for-trade), or surveillance framework. The broader market impact is minimal given the company's SME listing status.
