## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 21,43,095 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each of Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 540879), issued at a premium of Rs. 113/- per share to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- 21,43,095 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 113/- (issue price Rs. 114/-) to promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion.
- Distinctive numbers: 369456418 to 371599512.
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- ISIN: INE713T01028.
- Allotment date: 23/06/2026.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 23/01/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE Listing Operations for securities arising from a preferential allotment/warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members other than updating trading systems to reflect the new tradable quantity from the effective date; the allotted shares remain locked-in until 23/01/2027 and cannot be traded until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 23/06/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 23/07/2026 (Thursday)
- Lock-in Expiry: 23/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 21,43,095 shares to Apollo Micro Systems Limited's tradable equity base, a relatively small incremental quantity issued to promoters via warrant conversion. Since the shares are locked-in until January 2027, there is no immediate free-float dilution or selling pressure; the impact on market liquidity and price is expected to be minimal in the near term.
