## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) schedule for Sanmitra Commercial Limited. The window will be extended from Monday, January 19, 2026 to Tuesday, January 20, 2026. This revision accounts for the exchange holiday on January 15, 2026 (Thursday) due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Acquisition window for Sanmitra Commercial Limited extended by one trading day
- Extension from January 19, 2026 to January 20, 2026
- January 15, 2026 excluded as exchange holiday
- Holiday declared due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra
- Notice issued in continuation to notice no. 20260101-15 dated January 01, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative schedule adjustment to accommodate the exchange holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the revised acquisition window dates
- Participants in the Sanmitra Commercial Limited takeover must adjust their timelines accordingly
- Trading activities related to this acquisition window must be conducted on the revised dates

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 15, 2026**: Exchange holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- **January 19, 2026**: Original acquisition window end date (now extended)
- **January 20, 2026**: New acquisition window end date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a one-day administrative extension affecting only the acquisition window for a single company.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Market participants involved in the Sanmitra Commercial Limited takeover need to adjust their schedules by one day to account for the exchange holiday. This is a routine adjustment that does not affect regular trading operations or other securities.
