## Summary

Hexagon Nutrition Limited's equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Friday, June 12, 2026, under the 'T' Group of Securities. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012, the scrip will remain in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days from the listing date.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Friday, June 12, 2026
- **Group:** 'T' Group of Securities
- **Trade-for-Trade Segment:** 10 trading days from listing
- **Scrip Code:** 544785 | **Scrip ID:** HEXAGON
- **ISIN:** INE0JUI01012
- **Total Securities:** 12,29,18,109 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 122918109
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 45/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 44/-)
- **Date of Allotment:** June 10, 2026
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- Trading only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, which mandates the Trade-for-Trade segment for newly listed IPO scrips for 10 trading days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form.
- Lock-in details for shares are as per Annexure I of the notice.
- Shareholding pattern is as per Annexure II of the notice.
- Share certificates for physical form shares have been enfaced with non-transferability; demat shares have lock-in period marked in depository records.
- Registrar to the Issue: KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221).

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 11, 2026:** Notice date
- **June 12, 2026:** Listing date and commencement of trading; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) held
- **10 trading days from June 12, 2026:** Trade-for-Trade segment period

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing notice with moderate market impact. The scrip enters under the 'T' Group with Trade-for-Trade restrictions for 10 days, limiting speculative intraday trading and requiring mandatory delivery settlement. The issue price of Rs. 45/- (44x premium over face value of Rs. 1/-) reflects the IPO valuation. With over 12.29 crore shares outstanding, the company has a meaningful public float. Trading members and new investors should note the SPOS on June 12, 2026 before regular trading commences. Physical share transfers are restricted; only demat trading is permitted.
