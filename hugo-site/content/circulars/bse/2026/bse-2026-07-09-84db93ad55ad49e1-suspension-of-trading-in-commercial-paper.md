## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed record dates for the redemption of their Commercial Paper (CP) instruments. Trading members are advised not to deal in the Commercial Paper of the listed companies from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- Five Commercial Paper instruments across three issuers are affected by this suspension notice.
- ECL Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 730936, ISIN: INE804I14YI6, Scrip ID: EFL-16-7-26-CP) has a record date of 15/07/2026.
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited has three CP instruments affected:
  - Scrip Code 731522 (ISIN: INE918K14DJ2, Scrip ID: NWFL-21-7-26-CP), record date 20/07/2026
  - Scrip Code 731222 (ISIN: INE918K14DE3, Scrip ID: NWFL-22-07-26-CP), record date 21/07/2026
  - Scrip Code 731780 (ISIN: INE918K14DO2, Scrip ID: NWFL-28-7-26-CP), record date 27/07/2026
- CNH Industrial Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 731571, ISIN: INE0P4D14275, Scrip ID: CICPL-29-07-26-CP) has a record date of 28/07/2026.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these Commercial Papers as their respective record/ex/settlement dates approach.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under Listing Operations for corporate actions related to debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper instruments from the notified record dates onward, in line with standard redemption settlement procedures.

## Important Dates

- ECL Finance Limited (EFL-16-7-26-CP): Record/Ex/Settlement Date - 15 July 2026 (DR: 2027)
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL-21-7-26-CP): Record/Ex/Settlement Date - 20 July 2026 (DR: 2027)
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL-22-07-26-CP): Record/Ex/Settlement Date - 21 July 2026 (DR: 2027)
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL-28-7-26-CP): Record/Ex/Settlement Date - 27 July 2026 (DR: 2027)
- CNH Industrial Capital (India) Private Limited (CICPL-29-07-26-CP): Record/Ex/Settlement Date - 28 July 2026 (DR: 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact and is confined to the debt segment, specifically affecting holders and traders of these Commercial Paper instruments. It ensures orderly settlement ahead of redemption and does not affect equity trading or the broader market. Debt market participants holding these specific CP instruments should note the trading suspension dates to avoid settlement issues.
