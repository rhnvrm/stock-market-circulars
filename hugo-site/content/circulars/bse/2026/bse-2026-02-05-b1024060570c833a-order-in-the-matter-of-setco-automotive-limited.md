## Summary

SEBI has issued an order under multiple sections of the SEBI Act, 1992 (Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A), 11B(1), 11B(2) read with Section 15HA and 15HB) in the matter of Setco Automotive Limited. The order involves 10 noticees including the company, a private limited entity (Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd.), and 8 individuals. The order references SEBI's powers to prohibit fraudulent and unfair trade practices relating to securities markets as mandated under Section 11(2)(e) of the SEBI Act.

## Key Points

- Order issued under reference number QJA/SS/CFID/CFID-SEC2/32034/2025-26
- 10 noticees involved: Setco Automotive Ltd., Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd., and 8 individuals (Harish Sheth, Udit Harish Sheth, Arun Arora, Ashok Kumar Jha, Suhasini Somesh Sathe, Rovinder Kumar Singla, Urja Harshal Shah, Jatinder Bir Singh Gujral)
- Order invokes SEBI's powers under Section 11(2)(e) to prohibit fraudulent and unfair trade practices
- Case involves voluminous records including a 40-page show cause notice with 26 annexures and 3 CDs of documents
- Order references Supreme Court judgment in Clariant International v. SEBI regarding SEBI's wide-ranging legislative, executive and judicial powers
- The document is 73 pages long (only introductory portion provided)
