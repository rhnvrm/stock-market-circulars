## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Anand Seamless Limited, filed on February 27, 2026, on the SME exchange website. The document is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Anand Seamless Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE SME on February 27, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from the date of this notice (March 02, 2026).
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The disclosure complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website.
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of the notice date.

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is routine in nature and has no direct impact on broader market operations or existing listed securities. It pertains solely to the pre-IPO disclosure process for Anand Seamless Limited on the SME platform. Investors and analysts interested in the upcoming SME IPO may review the draft document and submit comments during the open period.
