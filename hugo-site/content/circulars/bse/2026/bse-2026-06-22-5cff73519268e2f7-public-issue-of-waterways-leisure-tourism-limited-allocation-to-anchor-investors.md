## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the Public Issue of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited, 32,58,045 equity shares have been subscribed on June 22, 2026 at a price of Rs.808/- per equity share.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation for Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited IPO completed on June 22, 2026.
- Total shares allocated to anchor investors: 32,58,045 equity shares.
- Anchor investor price: Rs.808/- per equity share.
- Notice issued by Vice President Mangesh Tayde, BSE Listing Business Relationship Department.
- A copy of the company's announcement dated June 22, 2026 is enclosed as attachment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural notice under the public issue process.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required of trading members. This notice is informational, apprising members of the anchor investor subscription details ahead of the public issue opening.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Anchor investor allocation date and notice date.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. Anchor investor allocation is a standard pre-IPO step and signals institutional interest in the Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited IPO at Rs.808/- per share. The allocation size of approximately 32.58 lakh shares provides a reference price point for retail and non-institutional investors ahead of the public issue opening.
