## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of securities under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II as per Annexure I. Enhanced margin requirements will apply effective June 15, 2026, on all open positions as of June 12, 2026, and new positions created from June 15, 2026. This notice builds on the revised ASM framework established through multiple prior exchange notices dating back to October 2018.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company
- Price band of scrips moving out of the framework will be reinstated to the pre-ASM price band, subject to no other surveillance framework applying

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the revised ASM framework referenced in:
- Exchange notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Exchange notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Exchange notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Exchange notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Exchange notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM framework to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers/trading members) must ensure applicable margins are collected on all open positions in ST-ASM securities as of June 12, 2026
- Enhanced margin requirements must be applied to new positions created on or after June 15, 2026
- Members should refer to Annexure I (attached) for the specific list of securities included under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Notice date; ST-ASM inclusion announced
- **June 12, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins
- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements on both open and new positions

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the affected securities will face significantly higher margin requirements starting June 15, 2026. Stage I securities require at least 50% margin (up from potentially lower existing rates), while Stage II securities require 100% margin, effectively requiring full cash/collateral coverage. This may trigger margin calls, forced position reductions, or reduced liquidity in the affected scrips. The measure is temporary and surveillance-driven; affected companies are not being penalized from a regulatory compliance standpoint.
