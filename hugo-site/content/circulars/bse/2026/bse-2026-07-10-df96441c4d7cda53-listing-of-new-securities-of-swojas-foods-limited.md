## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 39,84,500 new equity shares of Swojas Foods Limited (Scrip Code - 530217), each of face value Rs. 10/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 6.5/- per share to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Total of 39,84,500 equity shares allotted across three tranches: 13,21,000 shares on 25/05/2026, 13,31,500 shares on 30/05/2026, and 13,32,000 shares on 09/06/2026.
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis following conversion of warrants.
- Issue price of Rs. 16.5/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 6.5/-).
- ISIN for the securities: INE295B01016.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- All three tranches carry lock-in requirements with distinct expiry dates.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing for operational awareness; no specific compliance action is required beyond recognizing the new shares as tradable from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: 25 May 2026 (13,21,000 shares), 30 May 2026 (13,31,500 shares), 9 June 2026 (13,32,000 shares).
- Listing/trading effective date: Monday, 13 July 2026.
- Lock-in expiry dates: 19 January 2027 (first tranche), 24 January 2027 (second tranche), 29 January 2027 (third tranche).

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Swojas Foods Limited by 39,84,500 shares, though the newly listed shares remain under lock-in until January 2027, limiting immediate float impact. The preferential allotment via warrant conversion reflects capital raised from non-promoter investors at a fixed issue price of Rs. 16.5/- per share. Market impact is limited to shareholders and traders tracking this specific scrip.
