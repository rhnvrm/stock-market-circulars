## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window under Takeover regulations for SENTHIL INFOTEK LIMITED (Symbol: SENINFO, Scrip Code: 531980, ISIN: INE564B01015). The offer is open from Wednesday, 18th June 2026 to Thursday, 2nd July 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 8.00 per share for up to 13,13,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** SENTHIL INFOTEK LIMITED | Symbol: SENINFO | ISIN: INE564B01015 | Scrip Code: 531980
- **Offer Size:** Up to 13,13,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 8.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 18th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, 2nd July 2026
- Bid session timings: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily; Take-up on the last day closes at 4:00 PM
- Bids can be placed only for shares held in demat form
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with the correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises an existing takeover offer under applicable SEBI Takeover Regulations via BSE's Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window mechanism, referencing Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (Sep 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (Feb 3, 2017) for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database before placing bids.
- **Members and Custodians** should note that End of Issue files on Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted.
- Extranet files for acceptance of bids will be available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA after issue closure.
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.
- Contact Trading Operations (022-22728005 / user.trdops@bseindia.com) for new login IDs and password resets on iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet systems.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to trading members and custodians facilitating participation in the takeover offer for SENTHIL INFOTEK LIMITED. Investors holding shares of SENINFO in demat form may tender shares through their trading members during the offer window. The offer price of Rs. 8.00 per share should be compared against the prevailing market price to assess attractiveness. The offer covers up to 13,13,000 equity shares across multiple investor categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, NIBs, Companies, and Individuals). Broad market impact is limited given the small-cap nature of the issuer.
