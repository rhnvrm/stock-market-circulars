## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13,15,715 new equity shares of Virinchi Limited (Scrip Code: 532372) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, May 20, 2026. These shares were issued to the Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 28/- per share.

## Key Points

- 13,15,715 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 18/- (issue price Rs. 28/-) to Promoter
- Issued on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE539B01017
- Distribution numbers: 107481182 to 108796896
- All 13,15,715 newly issued shares are subject to lock-in until 31 December 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities under the preferential allotment route.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from May 20, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies to all 13,15,715 shares (Dist. Nos. 107481182 to 108796896) until 31/12/2027; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 24 February 2026
- **Trading commencement:** 20 May 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** 31 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 13,15,715 shares to Virinchi Limited's tradeable equity base, though the entire allotment is locked in until end of 2027, meaning no immediate increase in free-float. The preferential allotment to the Promoter at Rs. 28/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 18/-) strengthens promoter holding. Market impact is limited in the near term given the full lock-in on these shares.
