## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for seven equity scrips effective June 16, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20%, reflecting varying degrees of surveillance concern for each security.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions are effective from **June 16, 2026**
- Seven scrips are affected across a range of price band levels: 2%, 5%, 10%, and 20%
- Sizemasters Technology Ltd (513496) receives the most restrictive band at **2%**
- Harikanta Overseas Ltd (544769) and Yaashvi Jewellers Ltd (544770) are revised to a broader **20%** band
- Trading members are advised to contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands for the following scrips have been revised:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|---|---|---|
| 531533 | Accedere Ltd | 5% |
| 524516 | Bacil Pharma Ltd | 5% |
| 543500 | Evoq Remedies Ltd | 5% |
| 544769 | Harikanta Overseas Ltd | 20% |
| 544396 | Network People Services Technologies Ltd | 10% |
| 513496 | Sizemasters Technology Ltd | 2% |
| 544770 | Yaashvi Jewellers Ltd | 20% |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the revised price bands for the affected scrips starting June 16, 2026
- Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members should update their risk management systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The tightening of price bands — particularly the 2% restriction on Sizemasters Technology Ltd — signals increased surveillance scrutiny and will limit intraday price movement for affected scrips. Traders holding positions in these stocks may face reduced liquidity and constrained exit/entry points. The 20% bands on Harikanta Overseas Ltd and Yaashvi Jewellers Ltd represent a loosening that may reflect reduced surveillance concern or a prior overly restrictive band. Overall impact is moderate and confined to holders of these seven scrips.
