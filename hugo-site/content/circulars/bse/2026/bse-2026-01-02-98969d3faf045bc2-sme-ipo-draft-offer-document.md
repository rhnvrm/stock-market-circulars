## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Craftech Paper Mills Limited on its SME website for public review. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide feedback must submit comments within 21 days from January 02, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document must remain publicly accessible for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **Draft Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 02, 2026
- **Comment Deadline**: January 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact primarily affecting:
- Potential investors interested in the Craftech Paper Mills Limited SME IPO
- Merchant bankers and intermediaries involved in the IPO process
- Regulatory compliance teams monitoring SME public issues
- Market participants tracking upcoming SME listings

The document publication is a mandatory procedural step in the IPO process with no immediate market-wide implications.
