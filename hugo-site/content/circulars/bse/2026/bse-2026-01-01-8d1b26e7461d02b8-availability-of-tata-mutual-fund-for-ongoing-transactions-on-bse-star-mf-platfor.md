## Summary

BSE announces that TATA BSE MULTICAP CONSUMPTION 50:30:20 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF platform effective January 01, 2026. The scheme offers 6 plan variants across Regular and Direct plans with IDCW and Growth options.

## Key Points

- TATA BSE MULTICAP CONSUMPTION 50:30:20 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions from January 01, 2026
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- 6 scheme variants available: Regular Plan (IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, Growth) and Direct Plan (IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, Growth)
- Specific STP investment amount ranges and minimum installment requirements apply
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20251208-12

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of a previously closed mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Members:**
- Ensure STP registrations comply with minimum installment requirements based on investment amount ranges
- Daily/Monthly/Quarterly STP with Rs. 500-999: minimum 12 installments
- Daily/Monthly/Quarterly STP with Rs. 1000-1999: minimum 6 installments  
- Daily/Monthly/Quarterly STP with Rs. 2000 or above: minimum 3 installments
- Non-compliance with minimum installment numbers may result in rejections from AMC

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 01, 2026 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF platform

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational notice affecting a single mutual fund scheme. 

**Operational Impact:** Low to Medium for members dealing with TATA AMC schemes - Members must ensure proper configuration of STP parameters to avoid rejections. The STP minimum installment requirements based on investment ranges need to be carefully followed during transaction setup.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors interested in this consumption-focused multicap index fund - Provides renewed access to subscription, redemption and systematic transaction facilities for this specific TATA AMC scheme.
