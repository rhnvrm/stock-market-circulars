## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 40,000 new equity shares of Fredun Pharmaceuticals Limited (Scrip Code: 539730) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, July 9, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 1,240/- per share (issue price Rs. 1,250/-, face value Rs. 10/-) to promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,240/- per share to promoters on a preferential basis.
- Issuance is pursuant to conversion of warrants held by promoters.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers: 5516991 to 5556990.
- Date of allotment: April 9, 2026.
- ISIN: INE194R01017.
- Issue price: Rs. 1,250/- per share.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until January 15, 2028.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members or investors beyond noting the updated listed share capital and applicable lock-in on the new shares. The company has complied with preferential allotment and listing approval requirements for the new securities.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: April 9, 2026
- Listing/trading effective date: Thursday, July 9, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for the allotted shares: January 15, 2028

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and stock-specific. The addition of 40,000 shares via preferential allotment to promoters is a marginal increase to Fredun Pharmaceuticals' total equity base and does not affect trading in existing shares. Since the new shares are locked in until January 15, 2028, there is no immediate free-float or supply impact on the market. Overall market and sector impact is negligible.
