## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that four schemes under SBI Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) Series 81 (1157 Days) will mature on their fixed Redemption Date. Trading in all four schemes is suspended with effect from May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Four SBI FMP Series 81 (1157 Days) schemes are maturing on their scheduled Redemption Date
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these schemes from May 29, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-640/2026-2027
- Issued by Marian DSouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Affected Schemes

| Scrip Code | Scheme Name | ISIN |
|---|---|---|
| 543883 | SBI FMP - Series 81 (1157 Days) - Regular Plan - Growth | INF200KA15X8 |
| 543884 | SBI FMP - Series 81 (1157 Days) - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout | INF200KA16X6 |
| 543885 | SBI FMP - Series 81 (1157 Days) - Direct Plan - Growth | INF200KA17X4 |
| 543886 | SBI FMP - Series 81 (1157 Days) - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout | INF200KA18X2 |

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a procedural notice for the orderly wind-down of maturing fixed maturity plan schemes in accordance with standard BSE listing and operations guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in all four listed SBI FMP Series 81 scrip codes (543883, 543884, 543885, 543886) from May 29, 2026
- Members should inform their clients about the trading suspension ahead of the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** May 29, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to existing unitholders of SBI FMP Series 81 (1157 Days). As a fixed maturity plan reaching its scheduled end, investors will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in these four scrip codes from May 29, 2026.
