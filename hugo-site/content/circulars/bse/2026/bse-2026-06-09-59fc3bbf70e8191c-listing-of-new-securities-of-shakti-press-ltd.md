## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,46,41,400 new equity shares of Shakti Press Ltd (Scrip Code: 526841) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,46,41,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- on rights basis
- ISIN: INE794C01016
- Distribution numbers: 3520201 to 28161600
- Date of allotment: June 05, 2026
- Trading commences: June 10, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice following a rights issue allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed securities of Shakti Press Ltd from June 10, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 10, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares and result from a rights issue. Existing shareholders who subscribed to the rights issue will have their shares available for trading from June 10, 2026. This is a standard post-allotment listing process with no broader market implications.
