## Summary

BSE has issued a live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SHARP INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: SHARP, Scrip Code: 523449, ISIN: INE207B01011). The offer runs from 16th June 2026 to 30th June 2026, with bidding sessions open from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on all offer days.

## Key Points

- Offer to buy up to 64,86,000 equity shares of SHARP INDIA LIMITED at Rs. 10.00 per share (face value Rs. 10.00)
- Offer period: Tuesday, 16th June 2026 to Tuesday, 30th June 2026
- Bid Offering Period: 16th–30th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up / Take-up period: 16th–29th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- On the last day (30th June 2026), Give-up closes at 3:30 p.m. and Take-up closes at 4:00 p.m.
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading members must ensure investor UCC is registered with correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Bids are accepted only for shares held in demat form
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises an existing takeover/acquisition window mechanism under SEBI Takeover Regulations, referencing earlier Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the session schedule and ensure timely bid submissions via the iBBS platform
- Trading Members must verify that investor UCC records in the Exchange database reflect the correct PAN Number and Category
- Only demat shares are eligible for bidding
- Members must use designated BSE platforms (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet) for bid submission and file access
- For new login IDs or password resets on iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet, members must contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for trading members and custodians holding or managing SHARP INDIA LIMITED shares on behalf of clients. The offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share equals the face value, indicating the acquirer is making a minimum price offer. Eligible investor categories include FIIs, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, NIBs, Companies, and Individuals. The offer size of up to 64,86,000 shares could impact liquidity in the scrip during the offer period. Members should prepare systems access via iBBS and RTRMS ahead of the 16th June opening.
