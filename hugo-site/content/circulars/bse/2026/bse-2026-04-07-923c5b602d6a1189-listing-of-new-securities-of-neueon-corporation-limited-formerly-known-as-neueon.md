## Summary

BSE has notified that 50,89,00,968 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each of Neueon Corporation Limited (formerly Neueon Tower Limited, Scrip Code: 532887, ISIN: INE333I01044) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, April 08, 2026. These shares were issued and allotted pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Hyderabad Bench.

## Key Points

- 50,89,00,968 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each are newly listed on BSE
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 56544553 to 565445520
- Date of allotment: November 07, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 1/- per share
- ISIN: INE333I01044
- All newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in until April 14, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing follows the NCLT, Hyderabad Bench order dated October 23, 2024, approving the Resolution Plan for Neueon Corporation Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are informed that these securities are now available for trading from April 08, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 50,89,00,968 shares until April 14, 2027; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** October 23, 2024
- **Date of Allotment:** November 07, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** April 08, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** April 14, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 508.9 million new equity shares significantly increases the total share capital of Neueon Corporation Limited. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until April 14, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or tradable supply. The shares were allotted as part of an insolvency resolution process under NCLT, indicating the company underwent restructuring. Investors should note the lock-in constraints and monitor the company's post-resolution operational performance.
