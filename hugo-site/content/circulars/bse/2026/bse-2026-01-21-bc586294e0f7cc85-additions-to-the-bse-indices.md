## Summary

BSE announces the addition of four newly listed companies to its indices. AMAGI MEDIA LABS LIMITED (ticker 544679) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 23, 2026. Three SME platform stocks - NARMADESH BRASS INDUSTRIES LIMITED (544680), INDO SMC LIMITED (544681), and GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED (544682) - will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- AMAGI MEDIA LABS LIMITED listed on BSE on January 21, 2026
- AMAGI MEDIA LABS to be added to BSE IPO index at open on January 23, 2026
- Three companies listed on BSE SME platform on January 21, 2026: NARMADESH BRASS INDUSTRIES LIMITED, INDO SMC LIMITED, and GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED
- All three SME stocks to be added to BSE SME IPO index at open on January 22, 2026
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References previous notices: 20260120-39, 20260120-48, 20260120-49, and 20260120-47

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing announcement following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index constituents are updated automatically in corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- **January 22, 2026**: Effective date for addition of three SME stocks to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **January 23, 2026**: Effective date for addition of AMAGI MEDIA LABS LIMITED to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard index maintenance activity following new IPO listings. Index fund managers tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to adjust holdings accordingly. The staggered effective dates (January 22 for SME stocks, January 23 for mainboard stock) allow for orderly index rebalancing. No broader market or regulatory implications.
