## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the upcoming auction of three Treasury Bills by the Government of India on February 04, 2026. The non-competitive bidding facility will be available through BSE's iBBS web-based system (NCB-GSec module) for direct investors and trading members. The auction includes 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with a combined issue size of ₹29,000 crore.

## Key Points

- Three Treasury Bills to be auctioned on February 04, 2026 through RBI
- Non-competitive bidding available via BSE's NCB-GSec module on iBBS platform
- Total issue size: ₹29,000 crore across three tenors
- Bid collection starts February 02, 2026 at 10:00 AM (24-hour availability)
- Different bid collection deadlines for direct investors and trading members
- Minimum lot size: 100 units (₹10,000) for all three T-Bills
- Settlement on February 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular references the existing non-competitive bidding facility launched via Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Submit bids through NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com by February 04, 2026, 8:00 AM
- Ensure compliance with minimum lot requirements and maximum amount limits
- Contact Trading Operations for new user ID creation or password reset issues

**For Direct Investors:**
- Submit bids by February 03, 2026, 5:00 PM
- Adhere to minimum investment of ₹10,000 and maximum limits per security type

## Important Dates

- **February 02, 2026, 10:00 AM**: Bid collection starts
- **February 03, 2026, 5:00 PM**: Bid collection deadline for direct investors
- **February 04, 2026, 8:00 AM**: Bid collection deadline for trading members
- **February 04, 2026**: Auction date
- **February 05, 2026**: Settlement date
