## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,06,62,786 new equity shares of EFC (I) Limited (Scrip Code: 512008) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026. The shares were issued on a rights basis at a premium of Rs. 148/- over a face value of Rs. 2/- per share.

## Key Points

- 1,06,62,786 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 148/- (i.e., issue price Rs. 150/-) on rights basis
- ISIN: INE886D01026
- Distribution numbers: 137283377 to 147946162
- Date of allotment: May 25, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed shares from May 27, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: May 25, 2026
- Effective date for trading: May 27, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the equity share capital of EFC (I) Limited. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares and will be freely tradable from May 27, 2026. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and prospective investors in EFC (I) Limited; no broader market implications.
