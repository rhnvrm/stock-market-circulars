## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 539177, ISIN: INE206F01022) has announced a bonus issue of 67,93,80,400 equity shares of Re. 1 each. The new shares will be listed effective January 15, 2026, with deemed date of allotment on January 14, 2026. The distinctive numbers for the new shares range from 169845101 to 849225500.

## Key Points

- Company: Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- Scrip Code: 539177
- ISIN: INE206F01022
- Number of Bonus Shares: 67,93,80,400 equity shares of Re. 1 each
- Distinctive Number Range: From 169845101 To 849225500
- Corporate Action: Bonus share issuance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action of bonus share issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares as per the approved bonus ratio
- The company has completed all necessary regulatory approvals for the bonus issue
- Disclosure made by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer Dipyanti Jaiswar

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: January 14, 2026
- **Effective Listing Date**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The bonus issue will significantly increase the number of outstanding shares, improving liquidity and making the stock more accessible to retail investors. The share price will adjust proportionately to reflect the increased share count.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive additional shares without any cash outlay, increasing their total shareholding while maintaining their proportional ownership in the company.

**Trading Impact**: Post-listing on January 15, 2026, trading will commence with the adjusted share price reflecting the bonus issue ratio.
