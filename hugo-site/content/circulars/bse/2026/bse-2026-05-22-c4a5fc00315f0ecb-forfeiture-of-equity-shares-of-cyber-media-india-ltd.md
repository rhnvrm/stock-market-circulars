## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Cyber Media India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532640) has forfeited 1,71,329 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each. These shares were partly paid-up and were forfeited due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by the concerned shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company: Cyber Media India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532640)
- Number of shares forfeited: 1,71,329 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies on partly paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers of forfeited shares are listed in Annexure-I attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action notice under listing operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and to update client holdings accordingly. Members should refer to Annexure-I for the distinctive numbers of the forfeited shares to ensure accurate reconciliation.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- Notice No.: 20260522-24

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture of 1,71,329 partly paid-up shares reduces the outstanding partly paid share count for Cyber Media India Ltd. Holders of these specific shares (identified by distinctive numbers in Annexure-I) lose their investment in these shares. The impact is limited to affected shareholders and does not alter the company's fully paid-up equity capital.
