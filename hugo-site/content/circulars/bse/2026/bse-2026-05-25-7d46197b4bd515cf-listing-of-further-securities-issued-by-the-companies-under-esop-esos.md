## Summary

BSE has listed additional equity shares issued by 8 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026. No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities.

## Key Points

- 8 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed for trading from May 26, 2026
- Yes Bank has the largest allotment at 50,89,979 shares
- Dixon Technologies follows with 2,84,028 shares
- No shares are subject to lock-in restrictions
- All listings are in the Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective ISIN and scrip codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP/ESOS allotments representing employee compensation conversions to equity. The share quantities are small relative to the total outstanding shares of each company. No lock-in applies, so shares are freely tradable from the effective date. The largest allotment (Yes Bank at ~50.9 lakh shares) is modest relative to Yes Bank's large outstanding share capital.
