## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED (Security Symbol: FILTRON). The offer period runs from Monday, January 12, 2026 to Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The offer is for up to 1,65,14,290 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 0.50 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED
- **Security Symbol**: FILTRON
- **ISIN**: INE589N01017
- **Scrip Code**: 531191
- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,65,14,290 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 0.50 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: January 12, 2026 to January 27, 2026
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC registration with correct PAN and Category is mandatory

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule circular. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must note the revised schedule of activities
- **Trading Members** must ensure UCC of the investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- **Trading Members** can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members and Custodians should refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This revised schedule affects shareholders of SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED who wish to participate in the takeover offer. The low offer price of Rs. 0.50 per share (against face value of Rs. 10.00) indicates significant financial distress or restructuring.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and custodians need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the revised timeline. Members must ensure investor UCC details are updated before participating.
