## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-4 informing trading members that the equity shares of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment under scrip code 544710 and symbol ACCORDTS.

## Key Points

- Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE's SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544710
- Trading Symbol: ACCORDTS
- ISIN: INE132201018
- The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard listing notification informing trading members of an upcoming equity share listing on the SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited. No immediate compliance action is required; members should await the subsequent notice confirming the exact listing date and associated security details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME segment listing announcement with limited broader market impact. The circular affects trading members who may wish to prepare for dealing in ACCORDTS shares once the listing date is formally announced. The listing adds a new equity to BSE's SME platform, providing investors access to Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited shares.
