## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260611-36 dated 11 June 2026 informing trading members about corporate actions applicable to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of the affected securities and the nature of the corporate actions are provided in the annexure attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on 11 June 2026.
- Pertains to corporate actions in the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment.
- Trading members are directed to refer to the attached annexure for the full list of affected securities and action details.
- Signed by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice communicates existing corporate action events as they apply to open SLB positions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must review the annexed details of corporate actions in the SLB segment.
- Members should take appropriate action on their SLB positions as required by the corporate action events listed in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 June 2026
- Effective dates for individual corporate actions are specified in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a medium operational impact on trading members with open positions in the SLB segment. Corporate actions such as dividends, bonus issues, splits, or rights entitlements can affect the terms of outstanding lending/borrowing agreements, requiring adjustments to positions or collateral. Members with active SLB positions should consult the annexure to assess whether any of their holdings are affected.
