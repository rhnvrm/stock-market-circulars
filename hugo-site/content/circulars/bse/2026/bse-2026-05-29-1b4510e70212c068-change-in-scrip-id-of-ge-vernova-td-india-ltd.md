## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a change in the Scrip ID for GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275) in the BOLT Plus System. The Scrip ID will be updated from **GVTD** to **GVT&D**, effective June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 522275 (GE Vernova T&D India Ltd) will have its BOLT Plus System Scrip ID changed from `GVTD` to `GVT&D`
- The change takes effect from June 01, 2026
- This is a system-level identifier change only and does not affect the company's securities or trading fundamentals
- Trading members are advised to update their systems and references accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update to the Scrip ID in BSE's BOLT Plus trading system to better reflect the company name (GE Vernova T&D India Ltd).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the new Scrip ID (`GVT&D`) for GE Vernova T&D India Ltd in the BOLT Plus System
- Members should update any internal systems, order templates, or references that use the old Scrip ID (`GVTD`) before June 01, 2026
- For clarifications, contact Mr. Tushar Shetty (+91 22 2272 8634) or Ms. Asmita Chavan (+91 22 2272 8266) at Listing.crd@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 01, 2026**: New Scrip ID (`GVT&D`) becomes effective in BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. This is a routine identifier update in the BSE BOLT Plus trading system. Trading members need to update their records to use the new Scrip ID `GVT&D` instead of `GVTD` when referencing or placing orders for GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275). No impact on share prices, holdings, or corporate fundamentals.
