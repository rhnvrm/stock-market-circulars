## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MITCON Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd has communicated a reduced face value following partial redemption of Pass-through Certificates (PTCs) issued under TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025. The PTCs will be traded at the revised reduced face value with effect from June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- MITCON Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd notified BSE of partial redemption of TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 PTCs.
- Scrip: TTS25-11.75%-21-9-27-PTC | Scrip Code: 977166 | ISIN: INE2JU715017
- Reduced face value post partial redemption: **Rs. 3512.70 per PTC**
- Effective date for trading at reduced face value: **June 23, 2026**
- Reference: DR-657/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action under the debt segment — partial redemption results in a revised (reduced) face value at which the PTC will subsequently trade on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revised face value of Rs. 3512.70 per PTC for TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 (Scrip Code: 977166, ISIN: INE2JU715017).
- All trades in this instrument from June 23, 2026 onwards must reflect the reduced face value.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 22, 2026
- **Effective Date (Reduced Face Value):** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt segment corporate action with limited market impact. Only trading members holding or dealing in TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 PTCs are affected. The reduced face value reflects the partial principal repayment passed through to PTC holders, which is standard practice for securitised debt instruments. No broader equity or derivatives market implications.
