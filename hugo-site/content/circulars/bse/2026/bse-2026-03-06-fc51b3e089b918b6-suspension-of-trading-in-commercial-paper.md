## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) under the identifier JBCPL-10-3-26-CP will mature on March 09, 2026. Trading in this commercial paper is suspended effective March 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733) is set to mature on March 09, 2026.
- ISIN: INE824H14SZ2; Instrument: JBCPL-10-3-26-CP.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 09, 2026 onwards.
- Notice reference: DR-835/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for the maturity and consequent suspension of trading in a commercial paper instrument on the Debt segment of BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in Scrip Code 730733 (JBCPL-10-3-26-CP, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) with effect from March 09, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and alert relevant desks to prevent any inadvertent trades post the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Suspension / Maturity Effective Date:** March 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This suspension applies solely to the specific commercial paper (JBCPL-10-3-26-CP) of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited. Upon maturity, holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
