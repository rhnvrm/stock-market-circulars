## Summary

BSE Limited, through the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 640 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-640/2026-27. Trading members of the Exchange are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities that were short-delivered in the relevant settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Auction is for Rolling Settlement No. DR-640/2026-27
- Offers accepted on trading terminals on Monday, June 01, 2026
- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction) due on Tuesday, June 02, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they have short-delivered such securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors; the Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

The notice reiterates that following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is conducted through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member. Additionally, the earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for the same settlement in which they had short-delivered has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on June 02, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must consult notices regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) issued by the respective departments
- Utmost care must be taken when punching securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be permitted after submission

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Auction matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Delivery statement and reports (3:00 PM – 3:30 PM), Money statement and final reports (3:35 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 02, 2026**: Auction pay-in of securities at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians with obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-640/2026-27. Members who have short-delivered securities in this settlement cycle are directly impacted and must participate in or monitor the auction. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in the same settlement where short delivery occurred provides greater flexibility to members. Broader market impact is limited as this pertains to a specific settlement cycle's auction process.
