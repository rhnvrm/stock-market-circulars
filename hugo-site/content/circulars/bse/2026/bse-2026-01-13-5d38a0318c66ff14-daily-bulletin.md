## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 196/2025-2026 dated January 13, 2026, announces book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple companies. The bulletin includes interim dividend announcements, interest payments on non-convertible debentures, partial and full NCD redemptions, and a bonus issue.

## Key Points

- Bank of Maharashtra declares 10% interim dividend of Rs. 1.0000 per share with record date January 20, 2026
- Axita Cotton Limited announces bonus issue with record date February 13, 2026
- Multiple NCD interest payments and redemptions scheduled for Aadhar Housing Finance, Ananya Finance, Cholamandalam Investment, and Greaves Finance
- Greaves Finance Limited has multiple record dates for interest payments and NCD redemptions spanning from February 2026 to April 2027
- Trading flags vary by security type (equity trading flag 0 or 1, NCDs flag 1)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must maintain proper records of shareholders/debenture holders as of the specified record dates
- Trading members must ensure proper settlement for securities with book closure or record dates
- Investors must hold securities before the record date to be eligible for declared benefits

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Record date for Aadhar Housing Finance (interest payment and partial NCD redemption)
- **January 20, 2026**: Record date for Bank of Maharashtra interim dividend
- **January 23, 2026**: Record date for Ananya Finance interest payment
- **January 28, 2026**: Record date for Cholamandalam Investment interest payment
- **February 12-13, 2026**: Record dates for Greaves Finance interest payments and Axita Cotton bonus issue
- **Multiple dates through April 2027**: Various Greaves Finance NCD interest payments and redemptions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. This is a routine daily bulletin that provides essential information for investors holding these securities. The dividend and bonus announcements may generate positive sentiment for respective stocks.

**Investor Impact**: Medium. Investors must be aware of these dates to ensure eligibility for dividends, interest payments, and bonus shares. Those trading near record dates need to factor in ex-date implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard operational processes for book closure and record date management apply. No extraordinary measures required.
