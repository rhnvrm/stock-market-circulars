## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for MEGASOFT LIMITED. The company's name has been officially changed to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED with effect from January 12, 2026. The company is a company limited by shares with CIN L24100TN1999PLC042730, originally incorporated as MEGASOFT LIMITED.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from MEGASOFT LIMITED to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Effective date: January 12, 2026
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24100TN1999PLC042730
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Registered address: No.85, Kutchery Road, Mylapore, Chennai-600004, Tamil Nadu

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate name change processed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (MEGASOFT LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name (SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED) at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change:** January 12, 2026
- **Certificate Issue Date:** January 12, 2026
- **Old Name Display Requirement:** Two years from January 12, 2026 (until January 12, 2028)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders pursuant to such change. The company's CIN, registration status, and legal obligations remain unchanged. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders should note the new name for future correspondence and documentation, but no financial or operational impact is expected from this administrative change.
