## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Gujarat Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 539336) will be moved from the 'T' Group to the 'A' Group with effect from July 16, 2026. This is a continuation of the Exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260625-49 dated June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip: Gujarat Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 539336)
- Trading will be permitted under 'A' Group, transferred from 'T' Group
- This notice follows and continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260625-49 dated June 25, 2026
- No attachments accompanied this notice

## Regulatory Changes

The group reclassification changes the settlement mechanism applicable to the scrip. 'T' Group securities are typically traded on a trade-for-trade basis (no intraday trading, compulsory delivery), whereas 'A' Group securities are part of the normal rolling settlement segment and are typically more liquid, higher-market-cap stocks eligible for intraday trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange are requested to take note of the group change and update their trading systems and client communications accordingly to reflect the new settlement and trading rules applicable under 'A' Group from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- June 25, 2026: Original notice (20260625-49) referenced
- July 14, 2026: Date of this confirmatory notice
- July 16, 2026: Effective date of group change from 'T' to 'A'

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'A' Group is generally viewed positively as it signals improved trading flexibility, including eligibility for intraday trading and normal rolling settlement, which can enhance liquidity for Gujarat Energy Limited shares. Trading members and investors holding or trading this scrip should adjust their trading strategies to align with 'A' Group rules effective July 16, 2026.
