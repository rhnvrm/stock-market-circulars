## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Kusumgar Limited, 46,28,877 equity shares have been subscribed by anchor investors at a price of Rs. 419 per equity share. This allocation was confirmed on July 7, 2026, based on the company's announcement of the same date.

## Key Points

- 46,28,877 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in Kusumgar Limited's public issue
- Allocation price: Rs. 419 per equity share
- Subscription confirmed on July 7, 2026
- Notified via BSE Notice No. 20260707-49, issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President
- Company's intimation letter (Kusumgar_Ltd_Anchor_Intimation_Letter) enclosed with the notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine disclosure under SEBI ICDR regulations governing anchor investor allocations ahead of a public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond noting the anchor allocation for reference in the context of the upcoming public issue.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor subscription date: July 7, 2026
- Notice issuance date: July 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Anchor investor allocation at Rs. 419 per share provides an early price benchmark for Kusumgar Limited's public issue and signals institutional investor confidence ahead of the broader public offering. This is a standard pre-listing procedural step with limited direct market impact but is informative for prospective retail and institutional subscribers evaluating the issue.
