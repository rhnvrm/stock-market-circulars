## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 4,74,65,031 new equity shares of Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited (formerly Innovassynth Investments Limited, Scrip Code: 533315). These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Merger by Absorption under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The new shares will commence trading on January 30, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 4,74,65,031 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE
- Shares issued under merger scheme: Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited merged into Innovassynth Investments Limited
- Company renamed from Innovassynth Investments Limited to Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited
- New shares rank equally with existing equity shares (pari-passu)
- Scrip Code: 533315
- ISIN: INE690J01011
- Distinctive Numbers: 27984286 to 75449316
- Allotment Date: December 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements existing regulations under the Companies Act, 2013 (sections 230-232) regarding schemes of merger and absorption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must recognize the increased share capital of Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited
- Trading members should update records to reflect the new outstanding shares
- Existing shareholders should note the company name change and merger completion

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Allotment date for new equity shares
- **January 29, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **January 30, 2026**: New shares listed and permitted for trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium. The listing of approximately 4.75 crore additional shares represents a substantial increase in the company's outstanding equity. This will impact the company's market capitalization calculation and may affect liquidity and price discovery.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders of the merged entity will see their ownership percentage adjusted based on the merger swap ratio. The shares rank pari-passu, ensuring equal rights for all equity holders.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal for market participants. Standard listing process for post-merger securities with no special trading conditions or restrictions applied.
