## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II, effective June 12, 2026. Stage I securities attract a minimum 50% margin (capped at 100%), while Stage II securities attract a mandatory 100% margin. The measure applies to all open positions as of June 11, 2026 and all new positions from June 12, 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin rate of 50% or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin rate of 100% or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Revised margins apply to open positions as on June 11, 2026 and new positions created from June 12, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates concurrently with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is a market surveillance action and does not constitute an adverse finding against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip will be reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, provided the scrip is not part of any other surveillance measure.
- Affected securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of earlier ASM framework revision circulars:
- Notice no. 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice no. 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice no. 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice no. 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice no. 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice no. 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM framework to a new set of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the revised margin rates (Stage I: minimum 50%; Stage II: 100%) on affected securities from June 12, 2026.
- Members must apply margins on all open positions held as of end of day June 11, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I of the notice for the list of securities included under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 11, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margins.
- **June 12, 2026**: Revised margin rates become effective for both open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II will significantly increase the capital cost of holding or trading the affected securities. Stage II securities, requiring 100% margin, effectively restrict leveraged positions entirely. This can reduce liquidity and trading volumes in shortlisted scrips and may trigger position unwinding by participants unable to meet the higher margin calls. The measure is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance to curb excessive speculation or abnormal price movements in select securities.
