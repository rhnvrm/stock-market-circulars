## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Catalyst Trusteeship Ltd has communicated reduced face values per PTC (Pass Through Certificate) on account of partial redemption for five series under INDIA UNIVERSAL TRUST AL1 and AL2. These PTCs will trade at the updated reduced face values effective June 25, 2026 (DR-659/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Partial redemption applies to five PTC series across INDIA UNIVERSAL TRUST AL1 and AL2.
- Catalyst Trusteeship Ltd is the trustee communicating the reduced face values.
- All affected PTCs will trade at their respective new reduced face values from June 25, 2026.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a scheduled partial redemption event resulting in revised face values for the listed PTCs under the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated reduced face values and ensure these PTCs are traded at the revised values from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date for Revised Face Values:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment
