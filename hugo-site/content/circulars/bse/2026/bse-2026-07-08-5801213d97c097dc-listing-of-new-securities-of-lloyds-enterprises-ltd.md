## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512463) with effect from Thursday, July 9, 2026. The new securities comprise 2,10,62,615 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, issued pursuant to the conversion of partly paid-up rights shares into fully paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512463)
- 21,062,615 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up, issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE080I01025
- Distinctive numbers: 1503044791 to 1524107405
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Notice issued by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President, BSE Listing Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for shares arising from rights-issue call money conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize and permit trading in the new fully paid-up shares under the existing ISIN from the effective date. No separate action required from investors as the shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 8, 2026
- Effective trading date for new securities: Thursday, July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the tradable equity base of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. by 2,10,62,615 shares as partly paid rights shares become fully paid and tradable. Since the new shares are pari-passu with existing shares under the same ISIN, there is no separate trading symbol or price discovery process; the impact is limited to a modest increase in free float and total outstanding shares.
