## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up notice to its earlier circular dated June 16, 2026 (Notice No. 20260616-13), informing trading members of the actual reduced face value of the debentures of ADANI AIRPORT HOLDINGS LIMITED following part-redemption. The debentures, identified by scrip code 976065 (ISIN: INE0GCN07047, AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT), will now trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 62,581 per debenture.

## Key Points

- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260616-13 dated June 16, 2026, which had fixed the Record Date for part-redemption of these debentures.
- The company has now confirmed the exact reduced face value: Rs. 62,581 per debenture.
- Scrip Code: 976065; ISIN: INE0GCN07047; Security Name: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT.
- The debentures will trade with the revised (reduced) face value effective July 07, 2026.
- Reference: DR-666/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational update to reflect the post-redemption face value of an existing listed debt security in accordance with the earlier corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are required to take note of the revised reduced face value and ensure debentures are traded accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- June 16, 2026: Original notice (20260616-13) announcing Record Date for part-redemption.
- July 06, 2026: Notice confirming reduced face value.
- July 07, 2026: Effective date from which debentures will trade at the reduced face value of Rs. 62,581 per debenture.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture (scrip code 976065) of Adani Airport Holdings Limited. Post part-redemption, the face value per debenture is reduced, which will proportionately affect the traded price and outstanding principal value of the instrument. There is no broader equity market impact, but debt market participants and depositories need to update records to reflect the new face value from July 07, 2026.
