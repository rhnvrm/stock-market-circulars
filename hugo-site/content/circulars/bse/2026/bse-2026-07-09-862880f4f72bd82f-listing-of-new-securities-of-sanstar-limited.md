## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,80,24,157 new equity shares of Sanstar Limited (Scrip Code - 544217), each of face value Rs. 2/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 108/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 1,80,24,157 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 108/- each
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive Numbers: 182244251 to 200268407
- Date of Allotment: 24/06/2026
- Issue Price: Rs. 110/- per share
- ISIN: INE08NE01025
- Shares are subject to lock-in up to 14/01/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares already allotted on a preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new shares as eligible for trading from the effective date, and observe the applicable lock-in restrictions on these specific distinctive numbers until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 24, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: July 10, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: January 14, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Sanstar Limited's total outstanding equity share capital, resulting in dilution for existing shareholders. Since the new shares are locked in until January 14, 2027, there is no immediate free-float impact on market liquidity, though the preferential allotment reflects capital raised from non-promoter investors at a defined issue price of Rs. 110/- per share.
