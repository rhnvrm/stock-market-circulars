## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to Meera Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 540519) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- per share to Rs.5/- per share. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260217-10 dated February 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for Meera Industries Ltd (540519) is **INE343X01026**
- Sub-division is from face value Rs.10/- per equity share to Rs.5/- per equity share
- New ISIN is effective for all trades on and from the ex-date: **06-03-2026**
- DR reference: 834/2025-2026
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260217-10 dated February 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update arising from a corporate action (stock sub-division) already approved under DR 834/2025-2026. The old ISIN ceases to be applicable for trades from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN **INE343X01026** for all trades in Meera Industries Ltd equity shares on and from 06-03-2026.
- Back-office and depository records should reflect the new ISIN post ex-date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Ex-Date (new ISIN effective):** 06 March 2026
- **Reference Notice:** 20260217-10 dated 17 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to trading members and depository participants dealing in Meera Industries Ltd (BSE: 540519). Operationally, systems referencing the old ISIN must be updated before the ex-date of 06-03-2026. No broader market or regulatory impact is anticipated. The sub-division increases share affordability but does not change the company's overall market capitalisation.
