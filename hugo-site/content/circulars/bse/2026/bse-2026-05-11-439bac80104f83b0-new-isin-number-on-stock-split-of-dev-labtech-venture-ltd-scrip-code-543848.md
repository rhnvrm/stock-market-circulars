## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260511-51 (in continuation of Notice No. 20260507-6 dated 07 May 2026) informs trading members that DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD (Scrip Code: 543848) has been assigned a new ISIN number following a sub-division of its equity shares from face value Rs.10/- per share to Re.5/- per share. The new ISIN becomes effective from the ex-date of 15 May 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD: **INE0NIJ01025**
- Stock split type: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Re.5/- per share
- Scrip Code: **543848**
- New ISIN is effective for trades on and from ex-date **15 May 2026** (DR-631/2026-2027)
- This notice continues from BSE Notice No. 20260507-6 dated 07 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The existing ISIN for DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD equity shares is replaced with INE0NIJ01025 as a direct consequence of the corporate action (stock split). The old ISIN will cease to be valid for trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN **INE0NIJ01025** for all trades in DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD equity shares on and from 15 May 2026.
- Depository participants and custodians should ensure holdings are reflected under the new ISIN post the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 11 May 2026
- **Continuation of:** Notice No. 20260507-6 dated 07 May 2026
- **Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN):** 15 May 2026
- **DR Reference:** DR-631/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update arising from a board-approved stock split. The face value reduction from Rs.10/- to Re.5/- effectively doubles the number of shares outstanding, which typically improves liquidity for retail investors. Trading members and back-office systems must ensure the ISIN change is captured before the ex-date to avoid settlement failures. The impact is confined to holders and traders of DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD (Scrip Code: 543848) and carries no broader market-wide implications.
