## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in the partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Limited (scrip code 890226) as of March 06, 2026. A total of 2,08,92,394 partly paid-up equity shares are available for trading following the receipt of first/second call money.

## Key Points

- Total partly paid shares issued as on March 06, 2026: 2,99,25,394
- Partly paid shares on which first/second call money received (as on February 16, 2026): 2,09,08,655
- Shares available for trading on resumption: 2,08,92,394
- Paid-up value per share: Rs. 5/-
- ISIN for shares available for trading: IN9170U01035
- Lock-in period: Not Applicable
- Distinctive number range: From 18649681 To 39558335 (Total: 20,892,394 shares)
- Difference of 16,261 shares between call money received and shares available for trading is due to one case where debit could not be executed due to insufficient balance in the concerned account

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular pertains to an operational update on trading resumption for partly paid-up shares following receipt of call money.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and depositories should note the updated ISIN (IN9170U01035) for the partly paid-up shares available for trading
- Trading members should ensure transactions reference the correct distinctive number range (18649681 to 39558335)

## Important Dates

- March 06, 2026: Date of circular and resumption of trading
- February 16, 2026: Date as of which first/second call money receipt was tallied (2,09,08,655 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate, company-specific impact. Holders of Suraj Industries partly paid-up equity shares can now resume trading approximately 2.09 crore shares. The minor discrepancy of 16,261 shares (due to insufficient demat balance in one account) is flagged for transparency. No lock-in restrictions apply, allowing free trading of eligible shares.
