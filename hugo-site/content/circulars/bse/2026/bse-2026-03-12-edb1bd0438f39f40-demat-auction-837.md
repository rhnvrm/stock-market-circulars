## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction notice for Settlement No. 837, dated 12 March 2026. The auction covers approximately 70 securities across various sectors where delivery obligations could not be fulfilled, requiring auction settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 837 dated 12/03/2026
- Approximately 70 scrips listed for demat auction
- Largest quantity: SPICEJET LTD (Scrip 500285) — 883,228 shares
- Other significant quantities: ROYAL INDIA (512047) — 122,593 shares; EVEXIA LIFEC (524444) — 47,197 shares; FERTILIZERS (590024) — 24,008 shares
- Covers equities across sectors including pharma, energy, finance, infrastructure, and FMCG

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing auction settlement framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery positions in listed scrips must participate in the auction settlement process
- Buyers of auctioned securities are obligated to accept delivery at auction-determined prices
- Applicable penalties for short deliveries apply as per existing BSE bye-laws

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: 12/03/2026
- Settlement Number: 837

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction with medium market impact. The large SPICEJET quantity (883,228 shares) is notable given the stock's trading volumes and may cause minor price impact. The majority of other scrips have small quantities (single digits to a few thousand) with minimal individual impact. Overall market impact is low to medium, confined to the specific securities listed.
