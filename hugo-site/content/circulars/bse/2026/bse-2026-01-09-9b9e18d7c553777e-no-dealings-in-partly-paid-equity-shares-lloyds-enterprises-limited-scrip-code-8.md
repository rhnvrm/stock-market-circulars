## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in partly paid equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (Scrip Code 890224) effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date to determine shareholders eligible to receive call notice for payment of the first and final call of Rs. 19.50 per partly paid share (Rs. 0.50 towards face value and Rs. 19 as premium).

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in partly paid equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited
- Record date set for January 16, 2026
- No dealings from January 16, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-800/2025-2026)
- Call amount: Rs. 19.50 per partly paid share (Rs. 0.50 face value + Rs. 19 premium)
- Purpose: To ascertain shareholders eligible to pay the first and final call
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for partly paid shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in partly paid equity shares (Scrip Code 890224) from January 16, 2026
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Shareholders holding partly paid shares will receive call notice for payment of Rs. 19.50 per share

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 16, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 16, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-800/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of partly paid equity shares of a single company. Does not impact fully paid shares or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to ensure systems are updated to prevent trading in the specified scrip code from the effective date. Shareholders of partly paid shares need to prepare for call payment obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders holding partly paid equity shares (Scrip Code 890224) will be required to pay the first and final call amount to convert their shares to fully paid. Those on record as of January 16, 2026 will receive the call notice.
