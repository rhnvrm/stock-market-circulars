## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that the price bands applicable to six scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 22, 2026. Four scrips have been revised to a 5% price band, and two scrips have been revised to a 2% price band.

## Key Points

- Six scrips have had their price bands revised by BSE Surveillance (DOSS - Trading, Equity segment).
- IITL Projects Ltd (Scrip Code: 531968) - revised to 5% price band.
- Swastika Investmart Ltd (Scrip Code: 530585) - revised to 5% price band.
- Best Eastern Hotels Ltd (Scrip Code: 508664) - revised to 5% price band.
- B & A Ltd (Scrip Code: 508136) - revised to 5% price band.
- Zelio E Mobility Ltd (Scrip Code: 544563) - revised to 2% price band.
- Looks Health Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 534422) - revised to 2% price band.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine surveillance action by BSE under its price band monitoring framework, revising the daily permissible price movement (price band percentage) for the listed scrips based on their trading behavior and risk profile.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their trading systems to reflect the revised price bands for these scrips and ensure orders placed comply with the new band limits from the effective date. Any clarifications can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective Date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will constrain the maximum daily price movement permitted for these six scrips, which may reduce intraday volatility but also limit trading opportunities for participants expecting sharp price moves. This is a standard surveillance measure and does not indicate any fundamental change to the underlying companies, but traders and investors holding these scrips should adjust their order placement strategies accordingly.
