## Summary

BSE has compiled clarifications from three listed companies — Kirloskar Brothers Limited, Loyal Equipments Limited, and Silicon Rental Solutions Limited — in response to exchange queries about significant price movement in their respective securities. All three companies have confirmed compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and stated that no undisclosed or price-sensitive information exists that could explain the price behaviour.

## Key Points

- Loyal Equipments Ltd (Gandhinagar, Gujarat) responded to BSE surveillance letter ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/3409 dated around 07/03/2026, denying any undisclosed corporate actions or price-sensitive announcements.
- Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 543615, CIN: L74999MH2016PLC272442, formerly Silicon Rental Solutions Private Limited) cited Regulation 30(10) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and confirmed no pending price-sensitive information.
- Kirloskar Brothers Limited also submitted a clarification as part of this consolidated circular.
- All companies assert price movements are purely market-driven and not connected to management actions.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Companies are responding under existing provisions of Regulation 30 and Regulation 30(10) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies reaffirm ongoing obligation to promptly disclose all material events and information to stock exchanges as required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- No new compliance actions required by investors or market participants.

## Important Dates

- Loyal Equipments Ltd clarification date: 07 March 2026
- Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd clarification date: 07 March 2026
- Circular published on BSE: 09 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are standard surveillance-driven clarification filings with no new material information disclosed. Investors should note that all three companies have explicitly stated no impending corporate actions or undisclosed events. Price movements in these scrips remain unexplained by fundamentals as per management submissions.
