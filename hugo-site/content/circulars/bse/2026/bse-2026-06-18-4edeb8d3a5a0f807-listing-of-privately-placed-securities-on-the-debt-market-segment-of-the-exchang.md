## Summary

BSE has listed two privately placed debt instruments of Marwadi Chandarana Intermediaries Brokers Private Limited on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 18, 2026. Both instruments are NIFTY 150-linked structured products allotted on June 15, 2026, and redeemable on June 14, 2029, carrying a CRISIL PPMLD A credit rating.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities (Scrip Codes 977874 and 977875) admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment from June 18, 2026.
- Both securities are issued by Marwadi Chandarana Intermediaries Brokers Private Limited on a private placement basis.
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form under respective ISINs.
- Tick size for both securities is 1 paise.
- Market lot is 1 for both instruments.
- Further details available via the Placement Memorandum on the BSE debt memorandum portal.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE debt market framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form.
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.
- Refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 15, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement:** June 18, 2026
- **Date of Redemption:** June 14, 2029
