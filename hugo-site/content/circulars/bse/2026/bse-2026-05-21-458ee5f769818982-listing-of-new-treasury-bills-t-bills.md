## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS effective May 22, 2026. The securities include a 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bill with maturities ranging from August 2026 to May 2027.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective May 22, 2026 under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Scrip codes: 805217, 805218, and 805219
- Market lot for all three securities is 1
- Trading will be restricted two working days prior to the maturity/redemption date (T minus 2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Clarifications may be directed to BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing notice for newly issued government Treasury Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the trading restriction: the listed T-Bills shall not be available for trading two working days prior to the respective maturity/redemption dates (excluding bank holidays).

## Important Dates

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| 805217 | IN002026X073 | 91TB20826 | August 20, 2026 |
| 805218 | IN002026Y071 | 182T191126 | November 19, 2026 |
| 805219 | IN002026Z078 | 364TB20527 | May 20, 2027 |

- **Listing effective date:** May 22, 2026
- **Last trading day** for each security: Two working days before respective maturity date (excluding bank holidays)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing of government-issued Treasury Bills providing short-term debt instruments (91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors) for trading on BSE's debt segment. These instruments carry sovereign backing and are primarily relevant to institutional investors and trading members active in the government securities market.
