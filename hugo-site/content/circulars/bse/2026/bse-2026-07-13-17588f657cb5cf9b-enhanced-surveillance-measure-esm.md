## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under ESM and will attract a minimum 100% margin effective July 15, 2026. Some securities already under ESM will move down to Stage I, while others will exit the framework entirely per Annexure II. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I newly qualify for ESM and will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of July 14, 2026, and new positions from July 15, 2026
- These securities will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 15, 2026
- Some securities continuing under ESM will move to a lower stage (Stage I) effective July 14, 2026
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective July 14, 2026
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) will have their price band reinstated to pre-ESM levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure
- ESM operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under ESM reflects market surveillance criteria only and is not to be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic review and update applying the existing ESM framework (established via prior notices from June 2023 through July 2025) to a revised list of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on open and new positions in the newly added ESM securities from the specified dates
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded only in the Trade-for-Trade segment, with Stage II securities subject to a 2% price band and periodic call auction
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Stage movements and exits from ESM framework take effect; 100% margin applies to open positions as of this date
- July 15, 2026: New ESM inclusions take effect with Trade-for-Trade shift and 100% margin on new positions

## Impact Assessment

The update affects trading conditions for a specific set of securities named in the annexures, imposing higher margin requirements and Trade-for-Trade restrictions that reduce liquidity and increase transaction costs for holders of newly included stocks. Securities exiting the framework benefit from reinstated normal price bands and settlement conditions. Since the annexure listing specific security names is not included in the extracted text, exact stock-level impact cannot be determined from this notice alone; investors and members must consult the referenced annexures directly.
