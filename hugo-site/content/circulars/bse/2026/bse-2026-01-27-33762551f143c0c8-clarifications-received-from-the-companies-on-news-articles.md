## Summary

BSE Limited issued clarifications received from Adani Green Energy Limited and Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited in response to media reports published on January 27, 2026. Both companies provided responses under Regulation 30(11) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, addressing specific news articles about their operations.

## Key Points

### Adani Green Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 541450/ADANIGREEN)

- Clarification regarding media report titled 'Adani Green targets 7 GWh BESS in Gujarat & up capex to Rs. 40,000 crore in FY27' published on www.thehindubusinessline.com
- Company reiterates previous announcements from November 11 and 13, 2025 regarding Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) project implementation
- Currently on track to deploy one of the world's largest single location BESS in coming months
- Plans to potentially enhance BESS capacities to more than 2x the current year's capacity in subsequent financial year
- Commissioning expected to significantly enhance value for all stakeholders
- Company confirms compliance with necessary disclosures under SEBI Listing Regulations

### Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited (Scrip Code: 532921/ADANIPORTS)

- Rumour verification under Regulation 30(11) of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Response to media report published on https://economictimes.indiatimes.com
- Details of clarification not fully provided in extracted content

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine disclosure requirement under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must provide clarifications on media reports that may impact stock prices under Regulation 30(11) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Stock exchanges (BSE and NSE) actively monitor media reports and seek clarifications from listed companies
- Companies must respond promptly to clarification requests from stock exchanges

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: Date of media reports and stock exchange clarification requests
- **January 27, 2026**: Date of company responses to stock exchanges
- **November 11, 2025**: Original press release regarding BESS project by Adani Green Energy
- **November 13, 2025**: Subsequent communication regarding BESS implementation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are clarifications reiterating previously announced information rather than new material disclosures.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - No changes to existing operational plans or timelines.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides transparency and addresses potential market speculation, but contains no material new information for investors beyond previous announcements.

**Compliance Impact**: Demonstrates adherence to SEBI disclosure requirements and proactive engagement with stock exchanges on market-sensitive information.
