## Summary

BSE's Listing Operations department has issued a notice regarding the listing of new securities of Sammaan Capital Limited on the equity segment, effective April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- New securities of Sammaan Capital Limited are being listed on BSE's equity segment.
- Notice issued by the Listing Operations department under the company-related category.
- No attachments accompanying this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements stated beyond the standard obligations applicable to newly listed securities on BSE.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 22, 2026
- **Listing Date:** April 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard administrative notice for the admission of new securities of Sammaan Capital Limited to trading on BSE's equity segment. Existing shareholders and market participants in SAMMAANCAP should note the availability of these new securities for trading.
