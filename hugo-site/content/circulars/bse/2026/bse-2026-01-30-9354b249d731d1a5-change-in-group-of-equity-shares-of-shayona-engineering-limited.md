## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544686) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective February 12, 2026. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a shift to normal trading operations.

## Key Points

- Company: SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544686
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Thursday, February 12, 2026
- Notice follows continuation from Notice No. 20260129-49 dated January 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from the restrictive Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to the regular Rolling settlement mechanism. This transition allows for standard T+1 settlement cycles and improved trading flexibility compared to the compulsory delivery-based trading in the MT Group.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Inform clients about the change in trading segment
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 12, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Reference Notice**: 20260129-49 dated January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change has a positive impact on liquidity and trading flexibility for SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED shares. The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group) indicates the stock has completed its initial post-IPO phase and is moving to normal trading operations. This typically improves market liquidity, allows for intraday trading, and reduces settlement obligations for traders. Investors will benefit from standard settlement cycles and improved price discovery mechanisms.
