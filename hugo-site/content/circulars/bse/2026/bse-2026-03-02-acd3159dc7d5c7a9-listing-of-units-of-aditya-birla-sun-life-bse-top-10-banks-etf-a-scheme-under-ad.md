## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE Top 10 Banks ETF, an open-ended exchange traded fund tracking the BSE Top 10 Banks Total Return Index, effective Wednesday, March 4, 2026. The ETF is admitted to dealings on BSE under the 'B' Group.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544719
- **Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT System:** ABSL10BANK
- **ISIN:** INF209KC1670
- **Number of Units Listed:** 2,657,518
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 182.46 per unit
- **Date of Allotment:** February 26, 2026
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Group:** B
- **Fund Manager / AMC:** Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent:** Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS), Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular reiterates the SEBI guideline dated February 16, 2000, requiring that units of mutual funds be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this ETF are conducted in dematerialized form only.
- Trades must be in unit market lots (minimum 1 unit).
- Members requiring clarifications may contact Mr. Parag Jain (BSE Listing Compliance, Tel: 022-2272-8685, Email: direct.listing@bseindia.com) or the AMC directly.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 26, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement Date:** March 4, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Circular Date:** March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with low market-wide impact. Trading members gain access to a new ETF instrument tracking the BSE Top 10 Banks Total Return Index. The ETF provides investors with a passive, demat-only vehicle for exposure to the top 10 banking stocks on BSE. No changes to existing trading rules or compliance frameworks are introduced.
