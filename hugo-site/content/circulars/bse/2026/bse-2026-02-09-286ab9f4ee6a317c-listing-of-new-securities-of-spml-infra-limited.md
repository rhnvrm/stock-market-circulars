## Summary

BSE has listed 32,05,128 new equity shares of SPML Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 500402) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares are permitted to trade from February 10, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until August 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 32,05,128 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 116.56/-
- Issue price: Rs. 118.56/- per share
- Allotment date: November 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: February 10, 2026
- Distribution Numbers: 72251772 to 75456899
- ISIN: INE937A01023
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 32,05,128 shares until August 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 20, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as the entire allotment is locked-in for approximately 18 months. The preferential allotment to promoters at Rs. 118.56/- strengthens promoter holding. No dilution impact on trading float until lock-in expiry in August 2027.
