## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) has announced the reconstitution of several BSE indices. The changes are effective at the open of trading on Monday, June 22, 2026. Affected indices include BSE 100, BSE SENSEX 50, BSE SENSEX NEXT 50, and BSE Focused IT. BSE SENSEX and BSE BANKEX see no changes.

## Key Points

- Reconstitution is effective at market open on June 22, 2026
- BSE SENSEX and BSE BANKEX have no additions or deletions
- BSE 100 sees three additions and three deletions
- BSE SENSEX 50 sees one addition and one deletion, with the dropped stock moving to BSE SENSEX NEXT 50
- BSE Focused IT sees one addition (L&T Technology Services) and one deletion (Cyient)
- Contact for queries: bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a periodic index reconstitution based on BSE Index Services' standard methodology review.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for listed companies. Market participants — particularly passive funds, ETFs, and index-tracking portfolios — must rebalance holdings in affected indices to reflect the new compositions by the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 22, 2026 (open of trading)

## Impact Assessment

**BSE 100**
- Additions: Ashok Leyland Ltd. (500477), One 97 Communications Ltd. / Paytm (543396), CG Power and Industrial Solutions Ltd. (500093)
- Deletions: Ambuja Cements Ltd. (500425), Tube Investments of India Ltd. (540762), Colgate-Palmolive (India) Ltd. (500830)

**BSE SENSEX 50**
- Addition: TVS Motor Company Ltd. (532343)
- Deletion: Adani Enterprises Ltd. (512599)

**BSE SENSEX NEXT 50**
- Additions: Adani Enterprises Ltd. (512599), Ashok Leyland Ltd. (500477), One 97 Communications Ltd. (543396), CG Power and Industrial Solutions Ltd. (500093)
- Deletions: TVS Motor Company Ltd. (532343), Ambuja Cements Ltd. (500425), Colgate-Palmolive (India) Ltd. (500830), Tube Investments of India Ltd. (540762)

**BSE Focused IT**
- Addition: L&T Technology Services Ltd. (540115)
- Deletion: Cyient Ltd. (532175)

Stocks being added to indices are likely to see buying pressure from passive and index-tracking funds ahead of the effective date, while stocks being removed may face selling pressure. The move of Adani Enterprises from BSE SENSEX 50 to BSE SENSEX NEXT 50, and the corresponding inverse move of TVS Motor, is notable for large-cap fund allocations.
