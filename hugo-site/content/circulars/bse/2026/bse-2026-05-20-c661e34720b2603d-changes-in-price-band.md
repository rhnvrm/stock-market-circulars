## Summary

BSE's Department of Stock Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 12 equity scrips effective May 21, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, representing tightened controls on intraday price movement for these securities.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 12 scrips under BSE surveillance action
- Effective date: May 21, 2026
- Revised bands are 2%, 5%, or 10% depending on the scrip
- Three scrips (Mayur Floorings Ltd, Mcleod Russel India Ltd, Shankara Building Products Ltd) face the most restrictive 2% band
- Two scrips (Jain Resource Recycling Ltd, Noida Toll Bridge Company Ltd) revised to 10% band
- Remaining seven scrips revised to 5% band

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from May 21, 2026
- Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will be rejected by the exchange
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision tightens daily price movement limits for all 12 scrips, which restricts volatility but also reduces trading flexibility. The 2% band applied to Mayur Floorings, Mcleod Russel India, and Shankara Building Products is the most restrictive level, signaling heightened surveillance concern for these stocks. Traders holding or monitoring positions in these scrips should update risk parameters and order management systems before the May 21 market open.
