## Summary

SHRIRAM Mutual Fund is launching SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 19, 2026 to January 21, 2026, with two plan variants - Regular Plan (Growth) and Direct Plan (Growth). The tentative allotment date is January 27, 2026. Clear funds must be available in the ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on the allotment date.

## Key Points

- SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND NFO offered in two variants: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01489) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01471)
- Both plans categorized as Debt schemes
- NFO period: January 19-21, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: January 27, 2026
- Extended trading till midnight on January 21, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending till 3:00 PM on last day of NFO or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification following existing BSE StAR MF platform procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 27, 2026 for unit allotment
- SIP/XSIP/ISIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (January 21, 2026)
- Cancelled transactions (without client fund initiation by T+1 9:00 AM) will be reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- For switch transactions, funds must be received under NFO scheme before allotment date to be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **January 21, 2026**: NFO closure date (3:00 PM for SIP registrations; extended to midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **January 27, 2026**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds deadline in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
- **T+1 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (transactions without funds will be cancelled)
- **T+5 or last NFO payout day**: Whichever is earlier - deadline for pending transactions with initiated payments

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine NFO launch for a money market debt fund, one of many mutual fund offerings on the platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard NFO procedures apply with clear timelines for fund transfers, allotments, and transaction processing. Only affects BSE StAR MF platform members and investors interested in SHRIRAM Money Market Fund.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional investment option in the money market debt category for investors seeking short-term debt exposure through SHRIRAM Mutual Fund with both regular and direct plan options.
