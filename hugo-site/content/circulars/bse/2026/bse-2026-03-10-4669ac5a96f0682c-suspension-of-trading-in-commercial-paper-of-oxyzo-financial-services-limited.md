## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 729011, ISIN: INE04VS14244) is set to mature on March 11, 2026. Trading in this commercial paper is suspended effective March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (OFSL-12-3-26-CP) is maturing on March 11, 2026
- Scrip Code: 729011; ISIN: INE04VS14244
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 11, 2026 onwards
- Reference: DR-837/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding the scheduled maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the commercial paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 729011) with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Date of notice
- **March 11, 2026**: Maturity date; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard maturity-driven trading suspension limited to a single commercial paper instrument in the debt segment. Only trading members and investors holding this specific instrument (ISIN: INE04VS14244) are affected. No equity market implications.
