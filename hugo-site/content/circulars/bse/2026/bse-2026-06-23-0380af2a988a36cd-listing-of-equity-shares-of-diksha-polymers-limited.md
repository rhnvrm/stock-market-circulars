## Summary

Diksha Polymers Limited will be listed on BSE in the MT Group of Securities effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026. The scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012. The IPO was issued at Rs. 112 per share (face value Rs. 10, premium Rs. 102), with allotment completed on June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544798 | Scrip ID: DIKSHA | ISIN: INE1OSI01014
- Total securities: 5,196,600 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 5,196,600
- Issue price: Rs. 112/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 102/-)
- Market lot: 1,200 equity shares
- Trading in dematerialised form only
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) scheduled for June 24, 2026
- Market Maker: Shreni Shares Limited (SEBI Reg: SMEMM0621909112018)
- Registrar: Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai (SEBI Reg: INR000003753)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is subject to existing SEBI guidelines including the mandatory Trade-for-Trade segment requirement for new SME listings (SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012) and dematerialisation-only trading (SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade in minimum market lot of 1,200 equity shares; Exchange may modify lot size with at least one month's prior notice.
- All trades must be in dematerialised form only.
- Trading members must participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 24, 2026, per Exchange notice 20120216-29.
- Lock-in details and shareholding pattern to be observed as per Annexure I and Annexure II respectively.
- Corporate action for lock-in marking in depository records has been executed by the company.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Date of allotment in public issue
- **June 23, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 24, 2026**: Listing date; SPOS session held; trading commences in MT Group
- **10 trading days from June 24, 2026**: Trade-for-Trade segment restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME segment listing with limited broad market impact. Diksha Polymers Limited (plastics/polymers sector, Gwalior) is a small-cap issuer raising capital via the BSE SME platform. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading sessions reduces speculative intraday activity and requires delivery-based settlements. Market makers (Shreni Shares Limited) provide liquidity support. Impact is confined to investors and trading members dealing in this specific scrip.
