## Summary

BSE Mumbai has published the list of securities and quantities for Demat Auction under Settlement No. 809 dated February 2, 2026. The auction covers 94 different securities with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 25,000 shares. This auction is part of the regular settlement process for securities where physical delivery obligations need to be fulfilled through the demat auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 809
- Date: February 2, 2026
- Total Securities: 94 different scrips
- Quantity Range: From 1 share (multiple securities) to 25,000 shares (Restaurant Brands Asia)
- Includes major blue-chip stocks like Adani Ports (50 shares), Ambuja Cement (20 shares), Asian Paints (1 share), Cipla (3 shares), Nestle India (10 shares), State Bank of India (20 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including cement, paints, banking, oil & gas, textiles, pharmaceuticals, and mutual funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure availability of funds and securities for settlement on the scheduled date
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's demat auction procedures and timelines
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per the quantities specified in the circular

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: As per Settlement No. 809
- Circular Date: February 2, 2026
- Auction execution date to be as per BSE's standard settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 94 securities across multiple sectors, but quantities are relatively small for most securities

**Operational Impact:** Routine settlement process; brokers with outstanding obligations must participate in auction

**Investor Impact:** Limited direct impact on retail investors; primarily affects institutional participants and brokers with delivery shortfalls

**Liquidity Impact:** Minimal - auction quantities are generally small relative to daily trading volumes for most securities

**Notable Securities:** Includes several high-value stocks (Nestle India, Asian Paints, Hindustan Unilever, BEML) but in small quantities
