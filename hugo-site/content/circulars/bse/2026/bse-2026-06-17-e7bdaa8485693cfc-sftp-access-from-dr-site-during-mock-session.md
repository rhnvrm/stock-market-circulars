## Summary

BSE Index Services Pvt. Ltd. (BISPL) will conduct a Disaster Recovery (DR) mock session on Saturday, June 20, 2026, operating its SFTP server from the DR site between 10:30 AM and 2:30 PM to test Business Continuity Planning (BCP) preparedness. All clients who access end-of-day (EOD) downloadable files via SFTP are requested to connect to the DR SFTP server and verify successful access.

## Key Points

- BISPL will operate its SFTP server from the DR site on June 20, 2026 (10:30 AM – 2:30 PM)
- DR SFTP Server IP: 103.47.198.38 | External Port: TCP 3313 (shared previously)
- June 20 is a non-trading Saturday; no new EOD files will be generated — available files will be for June 19, 2026 only
- Clients are strongly urged to proactively participate and confirm they can download files without issues
- Contact BSE Helpdesk / BISPL Team for issues: 022-2272 8500 or 022-2272 5872 / 2272 8131
- Email queries to: bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a scheduled BCP/DR drill.

## Compliance Requirements

- All BISPL SFTP clients should access the DR SFTP server (103.47.198.38, TCP port 3313) during the mock session window (10:30 AM – 2:30 PM on June 20, 2026)
- Clients should verify they can successfully download EOD files from the DR site
- Any access issues must be reported to BSE Helpdesk or the BISPL team promptly

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** DR Mock Session — 10:30 AM to 2:30 PM
- EOD files available during the session will be for **June 19, 2026** (previous trading day)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to clients of BISPL who use SFTP to download index-related EOD files. Since June 20 is a non-trading day, there is no disruption to live trading or settlement. This is a routine BCP test; participation is encouraged to validate DR readiness.
