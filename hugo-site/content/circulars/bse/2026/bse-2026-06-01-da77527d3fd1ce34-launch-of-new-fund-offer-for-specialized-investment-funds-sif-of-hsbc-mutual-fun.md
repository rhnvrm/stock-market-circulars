## Summary

BSE has informed members that HSBC Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for **REDHEX HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND (SIF)** — a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) — on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 2, 2026, and closes on June 16, 2026, with an SIP facility also available. This notice references and supplements an earlier circular (20260601-16) introducing HSBC Mutual Fund SIF schemes on the platform.

## Key Points

- NFO period: **June 2, 2026 to June 16, 2026** (extended till midnight on June 16 for lumpsum purchases)
- Scheme: **REDHEX HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND (SIF)**, categorized as DEBT
- Tentative allotment date: **June 19, 2026**
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before **3:00 PM on June 16, 2026**
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled; CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalizes the introduction of HSBC Mutual Fund SIF schemes on BSE StAR MF Platform as previously communicated in notice 20260601-16.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by **9:00 AM on T+1 day** for transactions not to be cancelled
- SIP/XSIP authentications must be completed by **3:00 PM on June 16, 2026** (NFO closure date)
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by **6:00 PM on June 19, 2026** for allotment eligibility
- Members must report cancelled transactions to RTA/AMC with CC 'N' flag

## Important Dates

| Event | Date / Deadline |
|---|---|
| NFO Open Date | June 2, 2026 |
| NFO Close Date | June 16, 2026 (midnight for lumpsum) |
| SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline | June 16, 2026, 3:00 PM |
| Tentative Allotment Date | June 19, 2026 |
| Clear Funds Deadline in ICCL | June 19, 2026, 6:00 PM |

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard NFO operational notice with medium operational impact on BSE StAR MF Platform members and mutual fund distributors. Members facilitating transactions for HSBC Mutual Fund SIF must adhere to strict payment and authentication timelines to avoid cancellations. The introduction of a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category on BSE StAR MF represents an expanded product offering for sophisticated investors. No broad market impact is expected.
