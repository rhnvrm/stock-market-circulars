## Summary

BSE has listed 1,87,612 bonus equity shares of CWD Limited (Scrip Code: 543378) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, April 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,87,612 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each (face value) are now listed
- Shares were previously held in abeyance and are now released for trading
- ISIN: INE0H8H01019
- Distribution Numbers: 22021684 to 22209295
- Effective trading date: Monday, April 20, 2026
- Scrip Code: 543378

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard notice informing trading members of the availability of new securities for trading following resolution of abeyance status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to update their systems to reflect the listing of these new securities of CWD Limited from April 20, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, April 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The release of 1,87,612 bonus shares from abeyance represents a minor increase in the tradable float of CWD Limited. Impact is limited to existing shareholders who were previously unable to trade these shares. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
