## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 16,918 new equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited (Scrip Code: 532454, ISIN: INE397D01024) are admitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, April 15, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, originally issued on a rights basis. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 16,918 equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited (Scrip Code: 532454) are now tradeable on BSE from April 15, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid rights shares into fully paid up shares of face value Rs. 5/- each
- ISIN: INE397D01024
- Distinctive numbers: 5883025770 to 5883042687
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for newly admitted securities following a rights issue conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities and are permitted to trade in them from the effective date. No specific compliance action is required beyond ensuring trades reference the correct ISIN (INE397D01024) and scrip code (532454).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** April 15, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 16,918 shares is a very small increment relative to Bharti Airtel's existing large share capital, resulting in negligible dilution. This is a routine administrative listing arising from the completion of a rights issue partly-paid share conversion. No disruption to normal trading is anticipated.
