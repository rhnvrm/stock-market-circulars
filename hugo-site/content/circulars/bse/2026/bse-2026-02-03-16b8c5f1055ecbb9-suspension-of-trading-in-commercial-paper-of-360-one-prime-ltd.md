## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of 360 ONE PRIME LTD (Scrip Code: 360OPL-16-06-26-CP, ISIN: INE248U14RD6, Code: 729020) effective February 04, 2025. The suspension is due to the company's buyback of the Commercial Paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LTD
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE248U14RD6)
- Scrip Code: 360OPL-16-06-26-CP (729020)
- Reason: Buyback of Commercial Paper
- Effective Date: February 04, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification for suspension of trading due to buyback.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper of 360 ONE PRIME LTD from February 04, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 03, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: February 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension impacts investors and trading members holding or intending to trade the specific Commercial Paper (INE248U14RD6) of 360 ONE PRIME LTD. The buyback indicates the company is redeeming this debt instrument. Trading members must ensure no trades are executed in this instrument from the effective date. Impact is limited to this specific CP series and does not affect other securities of the company.
