## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 11,00,000 new equity shares of Associated Alcohols & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507526, ISIN: INE073G01016) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 679/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 669/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 11,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 669/- (issue price Rs. 679/-)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 18979201 to 20079200
- Date of allotment: March 12, 2026
- Trading commences: June 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to preferential allotment and warrant conversion under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and their lock-in status. Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 12, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (9,00,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 18979201–19879200):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,00,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 19879201–19979200):** November 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,00,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 19979201–20079200):** November 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 11,00,000 new equity shares to the tradable float of Associated Alcohols & Breweries Ltd., though the majority (9,00,000 shares) remain under lock-in until November 30, 2027. Only 2,00,000 shares will be freely tradable from June 1, 2026 once lock-in periods expire in late November 2026. The overall market impact is minimal given the relatively small size of the issuance relative to typical float.
