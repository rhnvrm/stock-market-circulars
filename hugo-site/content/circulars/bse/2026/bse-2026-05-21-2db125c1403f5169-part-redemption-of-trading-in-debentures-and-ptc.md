## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Tulip Growth 12 and Moneyboxx Finance Limited have fixed record dates for part redemption of their respective non-convertible debentures. Trading in these debentures will be conducted at reduced face values effective May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two companies have fixed record dates for part redemption of NCDs
- Trading in both debentures will be with reduced face value effective May 22, 2026
- Tulip Growth 12 (Scrip Code 975299): NCD with ISIN INE0SDO15017, face value reduced by Rs. 322.75 per debenture
- Moneyboxx Finance Limited (Scrip Code 976440): NCD with ISIN INE296Q07084, face value reduced by Rs. 60,000 per debenture
- Settlement series for both: 636/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice under existing debt market listing compliance procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for the affected debentures effective from the ex-date (May 22, 2026)
- All trades executed on or after the ex-date must reflect the reduced face value
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face values for settlement purposes

## Important Dates

| Security | Record Date | Ex Date |
|---|---|---|
| Tulip Growth 12 (INE0SDO15017) | 24 May 2026 | 22 May 2026 |
| Moneyboxx Finance Limited (INE296Q07084) | 23 May 2026 | 22 May 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects holders and traders of these two specific debt instruments. The part redemption reduces the outstanding principal (face value) of each debenture, which directly impacts the trading price and settlement value. Investors holding these NCDs will receive partial principal repayment, and subsequent trading will reflect the lower face value. Impact is limited to participants in the debt segment trading these specific securities.
