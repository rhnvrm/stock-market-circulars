## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260527-13) informing trading members that three schemes of Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund XLV Series 5 are maturing on their Redemption Date. Trading in all three schemes will be suspended with effect from May 29, 2026 (Reference: DR-640/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Three Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund XLV Series 5 schemes are maturing and will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective date: May 29, 2026
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these schemes from the effective date
- Issued by Marian DSouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Affected Schemes

| Sr. No. | Scrip Code | Scheme Name | ISIN |
|---------|------------|-------------|------|
| 1 | 543891 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund XLV Series 5 – Regular Plan – Growth Option | INF204KC1BH7 |
| 2 | 543892 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund XLV Series 5 – Regular Plan – IDCW Payout Option | INF204KC1BI5 |
| 3 | 543893 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund XLV Series 5 – Direct Plan – Growth Option | INF204KC1BJ3 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding the scheduled maturity and consequent suspension of trading in these fixed-horizon mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the above-mentioned Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes from May 29, 2026 onwards
- Members are requested to take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 29, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact. This is a standard suspension of trading notice issued upon maturity of closed-ended fixed horizon fund schemes. Investors in these schemes will receive redemption proceeds as per the scheme terms. No impact on broader markets or other securities.
