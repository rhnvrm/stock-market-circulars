## Summary

BSE has notified that MOTILAL OSWAL DIVERSIFIED EQUITY FLEXICAP PASSIVE FUND OF FUNDS will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 29, 2026. This reopening follows a previous closure referenced in Notice No. 20260101-31. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) in both Regular and Direct plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants available
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities enabled
- Two ISIN codes available: INF247L01GM7 (Regular Plan - Growth) and INF247L01GL9 (Direct Plan - Growth)
- Fund category: Equity Fund of Funds
- Previous suspension reference: Notice No. 20260101-31

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. BSE members and distributors can resume offering this fund to investors through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors and distributors interested in the specific Motilal Oswal Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds scheme.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The reopening restores normal transaction capabilities for this fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors who were waiting to invest or redeem can now proceed with their transactions. The availability of systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) provides flexibility for regular investors.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors in this scheme, as they regain access to subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities.
