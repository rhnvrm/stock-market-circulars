## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 6,05,494 new equity shares of Shree Hari Chemicals Export Ltd. (Scrip Code: 524336) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 4, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs).

## Key Points

- 6,05,494 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 69/- (issue price Rs. 79/-) to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued via conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 4986141 to 5591634
- ISIN: INE065E01017
- All 6,05,494 shares are subject to lock-in until June 4, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's listing and trading admission process for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility for trading from March 4, 2026
- The lock-in restriction must be observed: all 6,05,494 shares (Dist. Nos. 4986141–5591634) are locked in until June 4, 2027 and cannot be traded during this period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 4, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** June 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with limited market impact. The newly listed shares represent a modest addition to the company's equity capital. Since all shares are under lock-in until June 2027, there is no immediate increase in tradable float. Market participants trading Shree Hari Chemicals Export Ltd. (Scrip Code: 524336) should note the updated share distribution numbers and the lock-in status of the newly listed securities.
