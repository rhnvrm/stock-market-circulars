## Summary

BSE has issued a revised live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P M TELELINNKS LIMITED. The offer is for up to 26,19,500 equity shares at Rs. 6.81 per share, running from January 13, 2026 to January 28, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security**: P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (Symbol: PMTELELIN, ISIN: INE092C01015, Scrip Code: 513403)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 26,19,500 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.81 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 13-28, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 12-27, 2026)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a revised schedule notice for an existing takeover offer process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Custodian Members must note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period begins (9:15 AM)
- **January 13, 2026**: Bid offering period opens (9:15 AM)
- **January 27, 2026**: Last day for regular give-up/take-up (until 3:30 PM)
- **January 28, 2026**: Bid offering and offer closing date
  - Give-up (last day): 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Take-up (last day): 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of P M TELELINNKS LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for participating members - Trading Members and Custodians need to use iBBS and RTRMS platforms for processing bids and ensure proper UCC registration.

**Investor Categories**: The offer is open to multiple investor categories including FII, FI, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals.

**Platform Access**: Members can access the OTB module through:
- iBBS platform (Internet and Leased Line URLs)
- RTRMS platform (Internet and Leased Line URLs)
- Extranet for files (for Members & Custodians)

**Support Contacts**: Detailed contact information provided for Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams for iBBS, RTRMS, login issues, and password resets.
