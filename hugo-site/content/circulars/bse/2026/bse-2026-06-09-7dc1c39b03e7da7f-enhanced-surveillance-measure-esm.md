## Summary

BSE has issued a notice updating the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, effective June 10–11, 2026. Securities listed in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin from June 11, 2026 and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade. Certain securities will be moved down to Stage I, while Stage II securities will be subject to a 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction. A separate list (Annexure II) details securities exiting the ESM framework.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on June 10, 2026 and new positions from June 11, 2026
- Qualifying securities will be moved from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. June 11, 2026
- Certain securities will be moved to lower Stage I under ESM w.e.f. June 10, 2026
- Stage II securities will be under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 10, 2026
- A consolidated list of all securities under ESM is provided in Annexure III
- Securities exiting the ESM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 10, 2026
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance and is not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework originally established by notice no. 20230602-44 (June 2, 2023) and subsequently amended by notices in July 2023, August 2024, September 2024, October 2024, and July 2025. The current update adds new securities, moves securities between stages, and removes eligible securities from the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure 100% margin collection on ESM-listed securities in Annexure I from June 11, 2026
- Members must adhere to Trade-for-Trade settlement rules for all securities shifted under ESM
- Stage II securities must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band
- Members should note the securities exiting ESM and the reinstated applicable price bands upon exit
- For queries, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Stage movement to Stage I takes effect; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction takes effect; securities listed in Annexure II exit the ESM framework
- **June 11, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin requirement effective on all open positions; shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade effective for newly included securities

## Impact Assessment

The ESM framework update significantly restricts trading conditions for included securities by imposing 100% margin requirements and moving securities to Trade-for-Trade settlement, which reduces liquidity and increases cost of trading. Stage II securities face additional constraints via a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism. Market participants holding or planning positions in affected securities will face higher margin obligations and limited price movement. The exit of securities from ESM (Annexure II) restores normal price bands and settlement conditions for those scrips, subject to no other surveillance measure being applicable. The update does not constitute adverse regulatory action against the listed companies.
