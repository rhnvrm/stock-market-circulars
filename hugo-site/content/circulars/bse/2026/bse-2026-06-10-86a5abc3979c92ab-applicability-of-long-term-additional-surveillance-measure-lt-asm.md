## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) effective June 15, 2026, applicable to all open positions as on June 12, 2026 and new positions thereafter. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and some are exiting the framework effective June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable w.e.f. June 15, 2026 on all open positions as on June 12, 2026 and new positions created from June 15, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages w.e.f. June 11, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable w.e.f. June 11, 2026 for newly staged securities
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) to continue in LT-ASM but transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group w.e.f. June 15, 2026
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM framework w.e.f. June 11, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the company
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references the revised LT-ASM framework established through:
- Exchange notice 20180321-46 dated March 21, 2018 (original framework)
- Exchange notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice 20201204-56 dated December 04, 2020
- Exchange notice 20240809-46 dated August 09, 2024
- Exchange notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024 (latest revision)
- Exchange notice 20210604-41 dated June 04, 2021 (T-group transfer provisions)

The current notice applies these frameworks to a new batch of securities per the attached annexures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on open positions in affected securities as on June 12, 2026, effective June 15, 2026
- Members must comply with the lower price band restrictions for newly staged securities from June 11, 2026
- LT-ASM framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com
- Price band of securities exiting the framework will revert to pre-shortlisting price band, unless the scrip is part of another surveillance measure

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Securities move to higher LT-ASM stages; Lower Price Band applicable; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II)
- **June 12, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 15, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions (as of June 12) and new positions; affected securities transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding or creating positions in affected securities, effectively reducing leverage and liquidity for these stocks. This is a high-impact surveillance action that will:
- Deter speculative trading in shortlisted securities
- Increase capital requirements for market participants holding these securities
- Restrict price movement via lower price bands for newly staged securities
- Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups face trade-for-trade settlement, further reducing tradability

The full list of affected tickers is contained in the attached annexures (not reproduced in the notice text). Periodic review means securities may be added or removed from the framework over time.
