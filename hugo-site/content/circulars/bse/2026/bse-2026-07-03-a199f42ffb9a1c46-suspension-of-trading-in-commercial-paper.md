## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Commercial Paper issued by Ashoka Buildcon Limited (Scrip Code: 731435, Particulars: ABL-07-07-26-CP, ISIN: INE442H14527) is set to mature on its redemption date. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper with effect from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Ashoka Buildcon Limited
- Scrip Code: 731435
- Instrument Particulars: ABL-07-07-26-CP
- ISIN: INE442H14527
- Segment: Debt
- Reference: DR-665/2026-2027
- Trading in this Commercial Paper will be suspended from July 06, 2026, due to maturity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice regarding cessation of trading due to scheduled maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN INE442H14527) of Ashoka Buildcon Limited on or after July 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective From: July 06, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact notice affecting only trading members dealing in this specific Commercial Paper instrument in the debt segment. It has no bearing on equity trading or broader market operations, and reflects standard exchange practice upon maturity of a debt security.
