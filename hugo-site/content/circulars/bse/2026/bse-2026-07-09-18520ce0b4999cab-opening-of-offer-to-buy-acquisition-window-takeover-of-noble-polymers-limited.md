## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members and custodians about the opening of an open offer to acquire shares of NOBLE POLYMERS LIMITED through the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism. The offer is made by Acquirers Mr. Mahesh Alabhai Odedra ("Acquirer-1") and Mr. Hiren Rambhai Odedra ("Acquirer-2") to the public shareholders of the Target Company at a price of Rs.5.00 per equity share, payable in cash, to acquire up to 22,76,406 equity shares of face value Rs.5/- each, representing 26.00% of the Emerging Voting Share Capital of the Target Company.

## Key Points

- Offer made pursuant to Regulations 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, involving a change in control and management of the Target Company.
- Offer price: Rs.5.00 (Rupees Five Only) per equity share, payable in cash.
- Offer size: Up to 22,76,406 equity shares (face value Rs.5/- each), representing 26.00% of the Emerging Voting Share Capital.
- Acquirers: Mr. Mahesh Alabhai Odedra and Mr. Hiren Rambhai Odedra.
- Target Company: Noble Polymers Limited.
- Mechanism follows SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015 and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016, along with BSE's Revised Guidelines issued via notices dated Feb 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; Nov 2, 2020; and Aug 25, 2021.
- The Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for market participants' reference.
- Further details on the Offer to Buy are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; the notice reiterates applicability of existing SEBI SAST Regulations, 2011 and the Revised Guidelines for the Acquisition Window mechanism for tender-offer based takeovers.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and custodians must process the acquisition of shares in accordance with the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism and the Revised Guidelines referenced in the notice. Public shareholders wishing to tender shares should refer to the attached Letter of Offer for participation procedures.

## Important Dates

- Offer opens: Friday, July 10, 2026
- Offer closes: Thursday, July 23, 2026
- Notice date: July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The open offer represents a change in control and management event for Noble Polymers Limited, directly affecting its public shareholders who may choose to tender shares at Rs.5.00 per share. Trading members and custodians handling this security must ensure operational readiness for processing tenders through the Acquisition Window during the offer period. Broader market impact is limited given the small scale of the offer.
