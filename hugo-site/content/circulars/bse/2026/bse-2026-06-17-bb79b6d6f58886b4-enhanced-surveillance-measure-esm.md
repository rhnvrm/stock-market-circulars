## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260617-31 dated June 17, 2026. Securities listed in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement effective June 19, 2026, and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to the Trade-for-Trade segment. Some securities are being downgraded to Stage I, while others are exiting the ESM framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on June 18, 2026 and new positions from June 19, 2026
- Certain securities will be moved to lower Stage I under the ESM Framework effective June 18, 2026
- Securities qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective June 19, 2026
- Securities shifting to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective June 18, 2026
- A list of securities exiting the ESM Framework is provided in Annexure II (effective June 18, 2026)
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This circular references and builds upon prior ESM notices: 20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61. The current update modifies the composition of ESM stages and adjusts margin and settlement requirements for newly added or re-staged securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all open and new positions in ESM-listed securities from the effective dates
- Trading in affected securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules
- Stage II securities are subject to a 2% price band and must be traded via Periodic Call Auction
- Shortlisting under ESM is a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company
- Price bands for securities exiting ESM revert to pre-ESM levels unless another surveillance measure applies

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Stage I downgrade takes effect; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction begins; securities exit ESM framework (Annexure II)
- **June 19, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin requirement on new positions; shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement for newly included securities

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities face significantly restricted trading conditions including mandatory 100% margin requirements, migration to Trade-for-Trade settlement (eliminating netting benefits), and for Stage II securities, a tight 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction. These measures substantially reduce liquidity and increase trading costs for market participants holding or transacting in these securities. Investors and members should review the Annexures to identify specific scrips impacted. Securities exiting the ESM framework will see price band reinstatement, improving their tradability.
