## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) effective February 3, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for NHAI 7.02% Bonds (ISIN: INE906B07EK6, Code: 953489)
- Suspension effective from February 3, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- Record date set for February 3, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-812/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified NHAI bonds from February 3, 2026
- Members must take note of the suspension and update their trading systems accordingly
- No dealings permitted in the bonds from the record date

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Circular issued by BSE
- **February 3, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **February 3, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only holders and traders of the specific NHAI bond series. The suspension is routine for redemption purposes and bondholders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction from the specified date. Systems should be updated to prevent inadvertent trades in these securities.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders whose names appear on the record date will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. No trading opportunity available from February 3, 2026 onwards.
