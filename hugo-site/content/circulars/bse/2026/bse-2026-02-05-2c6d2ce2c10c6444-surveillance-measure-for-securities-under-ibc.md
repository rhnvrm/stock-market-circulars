## Summary

BSE has updated its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective February 6, 2026, Alka India Ltd (Scrip Code: 530889, ISIN: INE061B01038) has been added to Stage 1 surveillance for companies in Insolvency Resolution Process. No securities were moved between stages or excluded from surveillance during this update.

## Key Points

- Alka India Ltd added to Stage 1 Additional Surveillance Measure under IBC framework
- Effective date: February 6, 2026
- No securities added to Stage 0 (Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure)
- No securities moved from Stage 1 to Stage 2 or vice versa
- No securities excluded from ASM framework
- Consolidated list includes 14+ securities under various IBC surveillance stages

## Regulatory Changes

The surveillance framework operates in multiple stages:
- **Stage 0**: Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure from company regarding insolvency
- **Stage 1**: Additional Surveillance Measure for companies in Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP)
- **Stage 2**: Enhanced surveillance with stricter trading restrictions

Alka India Ltd has been newly classified under Stage 1, indicating the company is undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process under IBC.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must be aware of enhanced surveillance on Alka India Ltd
- Securities under IBC surveillance may have additional margin requirements
- Price-to-price delivery-based settlement may apply
- Market participants should exercise caution when trading securities under ASM-IBC framework
- T+0 scrips marked with "~" are shortlisted based on parent company IBC status
- Recommencement scrips marked with "^"

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 6, 2026 - Alka India Ltd enters Stage 1 surveillance
- **Circular Date**: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: High - Alka India Ltd will face additional trading restrictions and scrutiny. Investors may experience reduced liquidity, higher margin requirements, and potential price volatility.

**Market Participants**: Brokers and traders dealing in Alka India Ltd must update risk management systems and inform clients about enhanced surveillance measures.

**Investor Risk**: Elevated risk profile for Alka India Ltd due to insolvency proceedings. The Stage 1 classification signals ongoing resolution process under IBC, which may result in restructuring, liquidation, or other material changes to the company.

**Broader Market**: The consolidated list shows 14+ securities remain under IBC surveillance framework, reflecting ongoing stress in certain segments of the market. Securities include companies from various sectors undergoing insolvency proceedings at different stages.
