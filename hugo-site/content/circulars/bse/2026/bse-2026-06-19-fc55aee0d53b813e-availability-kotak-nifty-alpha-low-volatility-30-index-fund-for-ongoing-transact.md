## Summary

BSE has informed members that the KOTAK NIFTY ALPHA LOW-VOLATILITY 30 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from June 22, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260527-44 dated May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 22, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct plans are available across three options each: Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment.
- All six scheme variants fall under the Equity category.
- The notice references prior Notice No. 20260527-44 dated May 27, 2026, which originally addressed this fund.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enablement notice informing members of the platform availability of the scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond awareness of the scheme's availability for transaction processing on the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of KOTAK NIFTY ALPHA LOW-VOLATILITY 30 INDEX FUND for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **May 27, 2026**: Date of the original related notice (20260527-44).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to the operational availability of a single index fund scheme on the mutual fund platform. Investors and distributors transacting through BSE StAR MF can place subscription, redemption, and SIP orders in the KOTAK NIFTY ALPHA LOW-VOLATILITY 30 INDEX FUND from June 22, 2026.
