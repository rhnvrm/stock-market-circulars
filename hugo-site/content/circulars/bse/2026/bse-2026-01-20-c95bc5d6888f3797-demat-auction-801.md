## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities involved in demat auction settlement number 801 dated January 20, 2026. The auction includes 84 scrips across various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 55,046 shares. This auction is conducted to settle short deliveries in the dematerialized segment.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 801
- Date: January 20, 2026
- Total scrips listed: 84
- Quantities range from 1 share to 55,046 shares
- Notable large quantities include Teamo Produc (55,046 shares), Murae Organi (10,854 shares), AMD INDUSTRI (6,500 shares), and MUKAT PIPES (3,707 shares)
- Auction includes securities from major companies like RELIANCE IND, INFOSYS LTD., ICICI BANK L, STATE BANK O, INDIAN OIL C, and BANK OF INDI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with short delivery positions in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds and securities for settlement
- Settlement obligations must be met as per the exchange's clearing and settlement regulations

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 20, 2026
- Settlement Number: 801

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to specific trading members who have short delivery positions in the listed securities. This is a routine market operation to ensure orderly settlement of trades where delivery obligations were not met. The auction mechanism helps maintain market integrity by providing a framework to close out unsettled positions. Market-wide impact is minimal as this affects only specific scrips and participants with settlement failures.
