## Summary

BSE has informed members that Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund has communicated a change in scheme name and features for one of its schemes listed on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The change takes effect from June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Motilal Oswal AMC has notified BSE of a scheme name change effective June 22, 2026.
- The scheme 'MOTILAL OSWAL ASSET ALLOCATION PASSIVE FUND OF FUND - AGGRESSIVE' will be renamed to 'MOTILAL OSWAL MULTI ASSET PASSIVE FUND OF FUND'.
- A notice cum addendum issued by Motilal Oswal AMC is attached to the BSE circular.
- This change applies to the scheme as listed and traded on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

The scheme name change reflects an update in the fund's classification or branding from 'Asset Allocation Passive Fund of Fund - Aggressive' to 'Multi Asset Passive Fund of Fund'. Members and intermediaries should update their records to reflect the revised scheme name from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors should update internal systems, records, and client-facing materials to reflect the new scheme name.
- Refer to the Motilal Oswal AMC addendum for details on any changes to scheme features beyond the name.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 22, 2026 — New scheme name and features come into effect on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the name (and potentially minor features) of one Motilal Oswal mutual fund scheme. Existing investors in the scheme are not required to take any action; however, intermediaries and members should ensure their systems reflect the updated scheme name from June 22, 2026 to avoid operational discrepancies.
