## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Central Processing Centre has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L67120WB1982PLC035452. The company's name has been officially changed from CONSECUTIVE INVESTMENTS & TRADING CO LTD to CONSECUTIVE COMMODITIES LIMITED, effective from December 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company originally incorporated as CONSECUTIVE INVESTMENTS & TRADING CO LTD
- New name: CONSECUTIVE COMMODITIES LIMITED
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L67120WB1982PLC035452
- Certificate issued by Assistant Registrar of Companies/Deputy Registrar of Companies/Registrar of Companies
- Approved by Ibson Shah, Central Processing Centre
- Certificate digitally signed under rule 9(2) of the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014

## Regulatory Changes

This certificate is issued pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders pursuant to such change of name.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name for a period of two years along with its new name at all places wherever the company is required to display its name in terms of Section 12 of the Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors and view public documents on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change:** December 16, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date:** December 16, 2025
- **BSE Circular Date:** January 21, 2026
- **Old Name Display Requirement:** Two years from effective date (until December 16, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change is administrative in nature and does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or stakeholder rights and liabilities. The company's registered office address remains at 23 Ganesh Chandra Avenue, 3rd Floor, Kolkata-700001, West Bengal, India. Stakeholders should update their records with the new company name while noting that the old name will continue to appear alongside the new name for the mandatory two-year period.
