## Summary

BSE has notified trading members to refrain from dealing in the non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of nine companies that have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or redemption of their debentures. This is a standard pre-record-date trading suspension applicable to the listed debt instruments.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified NCD scrips on and around their respective record dates.
- Nine NCD issuers are covered across three settlement cycles (DR 640, 641, and 642 of 2026-2027).
- Record dates range from 29 May 2026 to 2 June 2026.
- Ex-dates coincide with record dates in all cases.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing BSE listing and settlement rules governing debt segment trading suspensions prior to corporate action record dates.

## Important Dates

- **29 May 2026**: Record date and ex-date for ANS Private Limited (APL-ZC-13-6-26-PVT) and Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL-9.62%-16-6-27-PVT).
- **1 June 2026**: Record date and ex-date for ICL Fincorp (two series), Sammaan Capital Limited, ECL Finance Limited, and ONGC Petro additions Limited.
- **2 June 2026**: Record date and ex-date for L&T Finance Limited (LTF-8.8000%-17-06-26-PVT) and Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific NCD scrips listed. This is a standard pre-record-date suspension to facilitate accurate determination of beneficial owners entitled to interest payments or redemption proceeds. No broader market disruption is expected. NCD investors should note they cannot trade these instruments around the specified dates.
