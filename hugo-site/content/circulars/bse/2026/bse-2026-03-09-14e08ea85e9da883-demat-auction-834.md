## Summary

BSE has announced a Demat Auction for Settlement No. 834, dated March 9, 2026. The auction covers 76 securities across various listed companies where delivery obligations were not met. Participants with outstanding buy positions in these scrips will receive shares through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 834 demat auction scheduled by Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., Mumbai
- 76 scrips are listed for auction covering a broad range of sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 27,111 shares (Franklin Industries)
- Notable high-quantity auctions include: Franklin Industries (27,111), Leading Lease (18,402), Sofcom Systems (13,798), DSM Fresh Food (8,400), Sellwin Traders (9,800), LT Foods (6,900), Deccan Gold (5,000)
- Large-cap names included: Reliance Industries (15), HDFC Life (1,550), Yes Bank (1), Spicejet (1,000), Exide Industries (13), Marico (77)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine market operations notice under BSE's settlement and auction framework for handling non-delivery of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sellers who failed to deliver the auctioned securities are liable for any price difference (auction price vs. settlement price) as per exchange rules
- Buyers with unmatched deliveries will receive shares through the auction or cash compensation if auction is unsuccessful
- Members must ensure their clients are informed of auction outcomes and corresponding debits/credits

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 9, 2026
- Settlement No.: 834

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with medium operational impact. The auction affects a wide range of scrips across sectors including FMCG, banking, infrastructure, technology, and financial services. High-quantity auctions in companies like Franklin Industries, Leading Lease, and Sofcom Systems may lead to price discovery differences. Sellers responsible for non-delivery face potential financial penalties based on the difference between auction and contract prices. Overall market impact is limited and contained to the specific securities listed.
