## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Catalyst Trusteeship Limited has communicated reduced face values on account of partial redemption of five Pass-Through Certificates (PTCs) traded on the Debt segment. These PTCs will trade at the revised reduced face values with effect from May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Five PTCs are undergoing partial redemption with revised face values effective May 25, 2026
- Trustee: Catalyst Trusteeship Limited
- Reference circular: DR-637/2026-2027
- Trading members must take note of the revised face values for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

The face values of the following PTCs are reduced due to partial redemption:

| Company Name | ISIN | Scrip Code | Reduced Face Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 PL DAN (2024PLDAN-25-7-29-PTC) | INE1KIF15019 | 976384 | Rs. 0.21 per PTC |
| 2024 PL DIANA (2024PLD-9.80%-18-4-28-PTC) | INE0TWW15018 | 975459 | Rs. 0.01 per PTC |
| Stellar 1.5 2024 (STELLAR-9.5%-19-4-27-PTC) | INE1UUF15013 | 976595 | Rs. 0.36 per PTC |
| NASHVILLE 03 2024 (NVL03-9.90%-17-2-34-PTC) | INE0UV115016 | 975584 | Rs. 0.07 per PTC |
| Picasa 06 2025 (PICASA-9.5%-23-2-27-PTC) | INE231315016 | 976801 | Rs. 0.13 per PTC |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revised reduced face values for each PTC
- Trades in these PTCs from May 25, 2026 onwards must reflect the updated reduced face values

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: Notice date
- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date for trading at reduced face values

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact, affecting only holders and traders of these five specific debt PTCs in the Debt segment. The partial redemptions are routine operations for securitisation/pass-through vehicles. Trading members dealing in these instruments must update their systems to reflect the new reduced face values from May 25, 2026.
