## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, May 30, 2026. The session covers trading from both the Primary Site (PR) and a Disaster Recovery Site (DR) switchover, allowing trading members to test their systems and familiarize themselves with various market conditions.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, May 30, 2026, for the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Sessions will run from Primary Site (PR) in the morning and switch to Disaster Recovery Site (DR) in the afternoon.
- Trading members using third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house ETI API-based systems can participate.
- Testing covers exceptional market conditions including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios.
- Trades from mock trading sessions carry no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights and liabilities.
- Members are requested to provide feedback by 4:00 pm on the same day.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session.
- Members using third-party or in-house trading systems via ETI APIs should use this opportunity to validate their applications.
- Feedback on the session should be submitted by 4:00 pm on May 30, 2026, via the contact numbers or email provided.

## Important Dates

- **May 30, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment.
  - Log-in: 09:15 am – 10:00 am
  - Pre-Open Order Entry (PR): 10:00 am – 10:07/08 am
  - Pre-Open Order Matching (PR): 10:08 am – 10:12 am
  - Buffer Period (PR): 10:12 am – 10:15 am
  - Continuous Trading (PR): 10:15 am – 12:00 pm
  - Switchover to DR: 12:00 pm – 12:45 pm
  - Pre-Open Order Entry (DR): 12:45 pm – 12:52/53 pm
  - Pre-Open Order Matching (DR): 12:53 pm – 12:57 pm
  - Buffer Period (DR): 12:57 pm – 01:00 pm
  - Continuous Trading (DR): 01:00 pm – 02:45 pm
  - Trade Modification: until 03:00 pm
- **Feedback deadline:** 4:00 pm on May 30, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. The mock trading session is purely for testing purposes and does not create any real financial obligations or legal liabilities. It is operationally relevant only for trading members who need to validate their trading systems, particularly ETI API integrations and third-party platform setups, ahead of live market sessions. The DR switchover component is significant from a business continuity perspective, ensuring members are prepared for disaster recovery scenarios.
