## Summary

BSE has released the Financial Disincentive file for February 2026 (filename LCS0226.xxxx) for trading members who failed to upload or incorrectly registered TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminal location details. A charge of Rs.1,000 per non-compliant terminal location will be debited to the General Charges Account upon completion of 60 days from the date of this notice (March 10, 2026).

## Key Points

- Financial Disincentive file for February 2026 (LCS0226.xxxx) has been made available in the Equity Transaction Folder on Extranet
- Rs.1,000 charge applies per unregistered or incorrectly registered terminal location from which orders were placed
- Charges will be debited to the General Charges Account after 60 days from March 10, 2026 (i.e., around May 9, 2026)
- Members can apply for waiver/review of the financial disincentive
- Waiver requests must be sent to Waiver-Review@bseindia.com with subject line: "Waiver / review of Financial Disincentive_<TM Name>_<Clg No.>_LOCATION MISMATCH-FEB-2026"

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice enforces existing directives under:
- Exchange Notice No. 20070531-8 (May 31, 2007)
- Exchange Notice No. 20070828-28 (August 28, 2007)
- Exchange Notice No. 20131204-9 (December 4, 2013)
- Exchange Notice No. 20131213-26 (December 13, 2013)
- Exchange Notice No. 20180925-4 (September 25, 2018)
- Exchange Notice No. 20250924-54 (September 24, 2025)
- Exchange Notice No. 20251010-20 (October 10, 2025) — defines the Rs.1,000 disincentive
- Exchange Notice No. 20251008-67 (October 8, 2025) — waiver/review process details

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminals are registered with BSE before placing orders from those locations
- Members must upload correct terminal location details to the Exchange proactively
- Members seeking waiver must contact Waiver-Review@bseindia.com with required details including TM Name, Clearing Number, and reference to LOCATION MISMATCH-FEB-2026
- Processing fees apply for waiver requests (refer Exchange Notice No. 20251008-67)
- For queries, contact BSE Enforcement at 022-69158540, 022-45720640, 022-45720440 or Enforcement@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Notice date; LCS0226.xxxx file available on Extranet
- **~May 9, 2026**: Deadline before which charges are debited (60 days from notice date)
- Members should submit waiver requests well before the 60-day debit deadline

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a targeted impact on trading members who placed orders from unregistered or incorrectly registered terminal locations during February 2026. The Rs.1,000 per-location penalty is relatively modest but can accumulate for members with multiple non-compliant terminals. Members have a 60-day window to seek waivers before charges are automatically debited. The circular reinforces BSE's ongoing enforcement of terminal registration compliance to maintain audit trails for order origination.
