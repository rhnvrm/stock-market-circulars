BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Susan Electricals India Limited on the BSE SME website. - Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

This notice has limited broader market impact as it pertains solely to the SME IPO process for Susan Electricals India Limited. It is a routine regulatory step in the SME listing process.
