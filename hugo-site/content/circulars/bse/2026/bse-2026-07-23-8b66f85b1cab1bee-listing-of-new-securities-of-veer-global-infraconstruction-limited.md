## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 8,00,000 new equity shares of Veer Global Infraconstruction Limited (Scrip Code - 543241) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to conversion of a loan.

## Key Points

- 8,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 75/- (issue price Rs. 85/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis.
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of loan.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers: 16243421 to 17043420.
- Date of allotment: 22/06/2026.
- ISIN: INE244W01010.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 23/01/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members other than updating systems to reflect the new listed quantity effective the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 22 June 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 24 July 2026 (Friday)
- Lock-in expiry: 23 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases the free-float/outstanding share count of Veer Global Infraconstruction Limited, but with lock-in in place until January 2027, these shares will not immediately add to tradable supply. Overall market impact is minimal and specific to this single scrip.
