## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that units of ICICI Prudential Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF, an open-ended index exchange traded fund tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index, will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE in the 'B' Group effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- ETF listed under ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, managed by ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited
- Scrip Code: 544795; Scrip ID on BOLT: SMALLIETF; ISIN: INF109K1A476
- Total units listed: 4,968,883
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit; Issue price: Rs. 17.73 per unit
- Date of allotment: June 19, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit; Group: B
- Units will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- 11 market makers designated including Kotak Securities, ICICI Securities, HDFC Securities, Motilal Oswal, Mirae Asset Capital Markets, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is governed by existing SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000) requiring ETF/mutual fund units to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialized form
- All trades must be in market lot of 1 unit
- Members requiring clarification may contact Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Operations IPO) at BSE: 022-2272-8685 or mf.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: June 19, 2026
- Listing effective date: June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces a new ETF instrument on BSE providing retail and institutional investors exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index. The presence of 11 market makers should ensure adequate liquidity. Impact is moderate for investors seeking smallcap index exposure through the ETF route on BSE; no existing scrips or market operations are affected.
