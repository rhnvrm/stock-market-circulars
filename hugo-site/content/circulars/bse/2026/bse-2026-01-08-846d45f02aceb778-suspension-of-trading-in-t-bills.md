## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective January 09, 2026. The suspension applies to scrip codes 810582 (ISIN: IN1520150104, 827GUJ2026) and 810595 (ISIN: IN1020150117, 829AP2026) as these instruments will mature on their respective redemption dates. Trading members are advised not to deal with these T-Bills from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills to be suspended from trading: 827GUJ2026 and 829AP2026
- Scrip Code 810582 (ISIN: IN1520150104) - 827GUJ2026
- Scrip Code 810595 (ISIN: IN1020150117) - 829AP2026
- Trading suspension effective from January 09, 2026
- Reason: Instruments maturing on redemption date
- Reference: DR-795/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with the specified T-Bills from January 09, 2026
- Members should note the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No transactions should be executed for scrip codes 810582 and 810595 after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 08, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** January 09, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** On or around January 09, 2026 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational matter affecting only two specific T-Bill instruments in the debt segment. The suspension is due to natural maturity of the instruments.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Trading members need to ensure these specific scrip codes are blocked from trading in their systems from January 09, 2026. This is a standard end-of-life process for government securities.

**Investor Impact:** Low - Holders of these T-Bills will receive redemption proceeds as per normal maturity process. No action required from investors beyond cessation of trading.
