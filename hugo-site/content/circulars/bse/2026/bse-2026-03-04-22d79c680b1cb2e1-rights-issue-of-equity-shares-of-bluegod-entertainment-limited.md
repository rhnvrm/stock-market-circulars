## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED (Scrip Code: 539175) has fixed **March 6, 2026** as the record date for its rights issue. Trading in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from the same date under the Rolling Settlement segment.

## Key Points

- **Company:** BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED (BSE Code: 539175)
- **Record Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Rights Ratio:** 9 (Nine) equity shares for every 47 (Forty-Seven) equity shares held
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 3/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 2/- premium)
- **Payment Terms:** Entire issue price of Rs. 3/- per share is payable in full on application
- **Settlement:** DR-834/2025-2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework, requiring trading members to execute transactions on an ex-rights basis from the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED (539175) equity shares are carried out on an **ex-rights basis with effect from March 6, 2026**.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights pricing adjustment from the record date.

## Important Dates

|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue dilutes existing shareholding by offering 9 new shares for every 47 held at a modest price of Rs. 3/- per share. The impact is limited to existing shareholders and trading members dealing in this scrip. The ex-rights adjustment from March 6, 2026 will reflect in the market price. Overall market impact is low given the small size of the company and the standard nature of this corporate action.
