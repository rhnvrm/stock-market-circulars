## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two debt securities of Jana Capital Limited effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026, on account of debt restructuring. This notice continues from earlier Exchange Notices No. 20230602-25 (June 2, 2023) and No. 20231229-9 (December 29, 2023).

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Jana Capital Limited will be suspended from trading effective June 30, 2026.
- Scrip Code **974868** (ISIN: INE028U08032) is suspended.
- Scrip Code **975230** (ISIN: INE028U08040) is suspended.
- The suspension is due to debt restructuring of Jana Capital Limited.
- This is a continuation of prior Exchange Notices dated June 2, 2023 and December 29, 2023.

## Regulatory Changes

The Exchange is suspending trading in the specified scrip codes pursuant to the debt restructuring process of Jana Capital Limited. This action is taken under BSE's authority to manage listing and trading of debt securities in the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are informed of the suspension and must cease trading in the affected scrip codes from June 30, 2026 onwards.
- For further details, Trading Members may contact:
  - **Ms. Bhairavi Bondale**: +91 22 2272 8597 | debt.listing@bseindia.com
  - **Hardik Bhuta**, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 30, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Effective date of trading suspension for both scrip codes.

## Impact Assessment

Holders of Jana Capital Limited's debt securities under ISIN INE028U08032 and INE028U08040 will no longer be able to trade these instruments on BSE from June 30, 2026. The suspension reflects the ongoing debt restructuring of Jana Capital Limited, which has been under regulatory watch since at least June 2023. Market impact is limited to investors specifically holding these two debt instruments.
