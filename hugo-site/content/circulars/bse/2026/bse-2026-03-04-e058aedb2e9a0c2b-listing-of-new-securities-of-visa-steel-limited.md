## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,35,00,000 new equity shares of Visa Steel Limited (Scrip Code: 532721) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 5, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 40/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 30/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Visa Steel Limited (Scrip Code: 532721, ISIN: INE286H01012)
- **Number of Shares:** 1,35,00,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 40/- per share (premium of Rs. 30/-)
- **Allottee:** Promoters (preferential basis via warrant conversion)
- **Distribution Numbers:** 115789501 to 129289500
- **Date of Allotment:** December 22, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** March 5, 2026
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's procedures for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from March 5, 2026.
- No additional compliance action required from general market participants beyond awareness of the increased share count.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | December 22, 2025 |
| Trading Commences | March 5, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry | November 30, 2027 |

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,35,00,000 equity shares to Visa Steel's tradeable float, though these shares are subject to a lock-in until November 30, 2027, meaning they cannot be sold by the promoters until that date. The allotment represents a preferential issue to promoters via warrant conversion at Rs. 40/-, which increases promoter shareholding. The lock-in restricts immediate selling pressure. Existing shareholders face dilution from the expanded share capital. Overall market impact is moderate and limited to Visa Steel specifically.
