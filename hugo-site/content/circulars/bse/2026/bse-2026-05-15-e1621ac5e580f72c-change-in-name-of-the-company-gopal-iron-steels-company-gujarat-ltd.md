## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Gopal Iron & Steels Company Gujarat Ltd (Scrip Code 531913) has changed its corporate name to Castora Agri Commodities Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company scrip code 531913 remains unchanged
- Existing name: Gopal Iron & Steels Company Gujarat Ltd
- New name: Castora Agri Commodities Limited
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: CASTORA
- New abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: CASTORA
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name for Scrip Code 531913 on the BOLT Plus System will be changed from the existing identifiers associated with Gopal Iron & Steels Company Gujarat Ltd to 'CASTORA', reflecting the company's new legal name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records and systems to reflect the new company name (Castora Agri Commodities Limited) and new SCRIP ID/abbreviated name (CASTORA) for Scrip Code 531913 effective May 21, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 15, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The scrip code (531913) remains the same, ensuring continuity in trading. Trading members and investors need to be aware of the new company name and updated SCRIP ID (CASTORA) when referencing this security after May 21, 2026. No changes to trading conditions, lot sizes, or other parameters are indicated.
