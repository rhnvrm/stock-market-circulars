## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures (ISIN: INE109C07097) of Arman Financial Services Limited effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: Arman Financial Services Limited
- ISIN: INE109C07097 (Code: 975626)
- Debenture: AFSL-10.20%-25-1-26-PVT (10.20% coupon rate)
- Record date: January 16, 2026
- No dealings period: From January 16, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Arman Financial Services Limited from January 16, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- All transactions must be halted for ISIN INE109C07097 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 16, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of specific debentures (ISIN: INE109C07097) of Arman Financial Services Limited. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Debt segment trading members must ensure their systems prevent trading in these debentures from January 16, 2026. Investors holding these debentures should note they will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of January 16, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and redemption. Secondary market trading will not be possible from the suspension date.
