## Summary

BSE notifies trading members that 2,60,409 equity shares of GTL Infrastructure Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532775) will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, January 05, 2026. These shares were issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each pursuant to conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs).

## Key Points

- Company: GTL Infrastructure Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 532775
- Number of shares: 2,60,409 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: At par
- Purpose: Conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs)
- ISIN: INE221H01019
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 4370345347 to 4370605755

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the listing of these new securities and update their systems accordingly for trading commencing January 05, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 09, 2017
- Listing Date: January 05, 2026 (Monday)
- Notice Date: January 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine listing notification for shares issued pursuant to FCCB conversion that were allotted in 2017. The number of shares (2,60,409) represents a relatively small addition to the company's equity base. The shares will rank pari-passu with existing shares, ensuring equal rights for all equity holders.
