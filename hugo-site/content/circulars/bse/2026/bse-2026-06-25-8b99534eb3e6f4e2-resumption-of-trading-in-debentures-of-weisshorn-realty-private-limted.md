## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited (Scrip Code 974625) effective June 29, 2026. Trading was previously suspended in connection with a Record Date of June 18, 2026 set for Redemption of NCD on Exercise of Put Option. Since the Put Option was not fully exercised, the company has requested resumption of trading.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974625 (Weisshorn Realty Private Limited NCD) will resume trading from June 29, 2026
- This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260616-9 dated June 16, 2026
- The Record Date of June 18, 2026 was fixed for Redemption of NCD on Exercise of Put Option and Payment of Interest
- The Put Option was not fully exercised, prompting the request to resume trading
- Reference: DR-660/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational notice restoring normal trading status for a debt security following a corporate action event that was partially exercised.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note the resumption of trading in Scrip Code 974625 of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited with effect from June 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260616-9 issued
- **June 18, 2026**: Record Date for Redemption of NCD on Exercise of Put Option
- **June 25, 2026**: This resumption notice issued
- **June 29, 2026**: Trading in Scrip Code 974625 resumes

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited debentures (Scrip Code 974625). Since the Put Option was not fully exercised, the outstanding debentures remain available for secondary market trading. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the reinstatement of this scrip for trading from June 29, 2026.
