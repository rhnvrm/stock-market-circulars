## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd (HPCL, Scrip Code 500104, Derivative Asset Code HPCL) has fixed August 14, 2026 as the record date for payment of a final dividend of Rs. 19.25 per equity share for FY 2026-27. In line with SEBI's Master Circular on adjustment of corporate actions for stock options, the Exchange will adjust all available Futures & Options contracts on the HPCL underlying on Thursday, August 13, 2026, with the ex-date being Friday, August 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- HPCL has fixed August 14, 2026 as record date for payment of final dividend of Rs. 19.25 per equity share for FY 2026-27
- Exchange will adjust all available F&O contracts on HPCL on August 13, 2026 (ex-date: August 14, 2026)
- Adjustment made in line with Exchange notice 20180710-26 and SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational adjustment under existing SEBI/BSE corporate action adjustment framework for stock derivatives.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment must apply the revised strike prices and futures base rates from August 14, 2026 onward. Members should contact their respective relationship manager for further details.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 14, 2026
- Adjustment Date: August 13, 2026 (contracts adjusted at end of day)
- Ex-Date: August 14, 2026 (adjusted rates effective for trading)

## Impact Assessment

**Options Contracts:** The full dividend value of Rs. 19.25 will be deducted from all strike prices generated at the end of August 13, 2026; adjusted strikes become available for trading from August 14, 2026.

**Futures Contracts:** The adjusted futures price will be the August 13, 2026 daily mark-to-market settlement price less Rs. 19.25; this adjusted rate becomes the base/previous close for trading on August 14, 2026.

This is a standard mechanical adjustment ensuring derivative contract values are not distorted by the dividend payout; it does not alter the economic position of existing contract holders but requires trading systems and members to apply correct adjusted reference prices from the ex-date.
