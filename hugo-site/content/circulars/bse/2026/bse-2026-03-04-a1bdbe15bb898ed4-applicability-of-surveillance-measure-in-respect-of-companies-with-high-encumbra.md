## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the Surveillance Measure for companies with high promoter encumbrance as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. Effective March 05, 2026, four new securities are added (Annexure I), four securities are removed (Annexure II), and the consolidated active list comprises 13 securities (Annexure III).

## Key Points

- **4 securities added** to the high encumbrance surveillance list effective March 05, 2026
- **4 securities removed** from the list effective March 05, 2026
- **13 securities** remain on the consolidated active surveillance list
- Securities marked with (*) are listed as per NSE data
- Removals may be due to inclusion in LT-ASM, ST-ASM, or high Promoter & non-Promoter Encumbrance Framework

## Regulatory Changes

This update is made under **SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 — Regulation 28(3)**, which mandates surveillance measures when promoter encumbrance levels are deemed high. The circular reflects a periodic review and rebalancing of the watchlist.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and investors should note the revised surveillance status of the affected securities
- Additional margins or trading restrictions may apply to securities on the surveillance list
- Market participants holding or trading in these scrips must factor in the surveillance framework conditions
- Companies on the list with (*) marking are identified based on NSE data

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 05, 2026 — all additions and removals come into force
- **Circular Date:** March 04, 2026
