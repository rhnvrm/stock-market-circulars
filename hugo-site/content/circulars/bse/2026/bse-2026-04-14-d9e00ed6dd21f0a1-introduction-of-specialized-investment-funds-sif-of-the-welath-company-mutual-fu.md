## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260414-2 dated April 14, 2026, announcing the introduction of Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) offered by The Wealth Company Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. The notice is categorized under Trading – Mutual Fund segment and is accompanied by three annexures (Annexure I, II, and III) providing detailed fund and operational information.

## Key Points

- BSE StAR MF platform will now list Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) from The Wealth Company Mutual Fund.
- The circular is issued by the Trading Operations department under the Mutual Fund segment.
- Three annexures accompany the notice, likely detailing scheme information, SIP/transaction parameters, and operational guidelines.
- SIFs are a distinct product category, separate from regular mutual fund schemes, and are targeted at sophisticated/high-net-worth investors.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rules are introduced. The circular operationalizes the availability of the SIF product category from The Wealth Company Mutual Fund on BSE's mutual fund distribution platform (BSE StAR MF), consistent with SEBI's framework for Specialized Investment Funds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual fund distributors and registered investment advisers using BSE StAR MF should take note of the new SIF schemes available for transacting.
- Participants should review Annexure I, II, and III for scheme codes, ISIN details, transaction cut-off times, and any platform-specific requirements.
- Ensure client eligibility criteria for SIF products are met before facilitating subscriptions, as SIFs typically have higher minimum investment thresholds.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 14, 2026
- Effective Date: As specified in the accompanying annexures (refer to Annexure I, II, III for NFO/subscription open dates).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on mutual fund distributors, RIAs, and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform. The introduction of SIF products expands the product range available on the platform. Investors interested in higher-risk, potentially higher-return specialized strategies from The Wealth Company Mutual Fund will gain access through BSE's distribution infrastructure. No adverse market impact is anticipated; the circular is routine in nature for new fund onboarding.
