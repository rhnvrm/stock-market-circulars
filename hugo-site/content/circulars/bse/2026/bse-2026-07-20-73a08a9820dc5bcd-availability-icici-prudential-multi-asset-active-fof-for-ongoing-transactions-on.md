## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260629-39 dated June 29, 2026, the ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF scheme will reopen for ongoing transactions—including subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP facilities—on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- The reopening applies to ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF under both Regular and Direct plans (Growth option).
- Ongoing transaction facilities being restored include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- This follows an earlier BSE notice (20260629-39) dated June 29, 2026, which likely announced a temporary suspension or restriction on transactions for this scheme.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update reflecting reactivation of transaction facilities for a specific mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Mutual fund distributors and members transacting on the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems/processes to allow ongoing transactions for the specified scheme codes effective the stated date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 20, 2026
- Effective Date for Reopening: July 21, 2026
- Reference prior notice: June 29, 2026 (Notice No. 20260629-39)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative/operational notice with minimal market impact. It affects mutual fund investors and distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform for this specific ICICI Prudential scheme, enabling them to resume ongoing transactions. There is no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader trading activity.
