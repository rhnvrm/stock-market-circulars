## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544680, symbol NARMADESH, and ISIN number INE0S1B01014. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited
- Scrip Code: 544680
- Symbol: NARMADESH
- ISIN: INE0S1B01014
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: Approved, date to be announced separately

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Standard trading rules and regulations for SME segment will apply once listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Minimal immediate market impact. This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. Trading members should note the assigned scrip code and symbol for future reference.
