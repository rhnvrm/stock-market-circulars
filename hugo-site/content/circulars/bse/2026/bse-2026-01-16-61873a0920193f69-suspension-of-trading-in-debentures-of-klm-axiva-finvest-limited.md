## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is implemented as the company has set a record date for redemption of its 9.85% debentures maturing on February 6, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KAFL-9.85%-6-2-26-NCD (ISIN: INE01I507943, Scrip Code: 939669)
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 22, 2026
- Coupon rate: 9.85%
- Notice reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026
- Members must ensure their systems are updated to reflect the trading suspension
- Compliance with the record date requirements for redemption purposes

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 22, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **February 6, 2026**: Expected debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting a single debenture series. The suspension is procedural and relates to the normal redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in the specified security. Standard procedure for debt market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral to Positive - Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds as per the scheduled maturity. The trading suspension ensures proper settlement and prevents complications during the redemption process.
