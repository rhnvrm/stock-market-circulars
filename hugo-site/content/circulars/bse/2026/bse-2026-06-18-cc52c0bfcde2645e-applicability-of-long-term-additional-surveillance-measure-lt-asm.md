## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) pursuant to the revised framework established via various prior Exchange notices. Securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) will be subject to 100% margins effective June 23, 2026, on all open and new positions. Securities in Annexure I (Part B) are being moved to higher stages of LT-ASM and will have a Lower Price Band imposed from June 19, 2026. Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will continue in the LT-ASM framework but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group from June 23, 2026. Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 23, 2026 on all open positions as on June 22, 2026 and new positions created thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) move to higher stages of LT-ASM with Lower Price Band effective June 19, 2026
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) continue in LT-ASM but shift to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 23, 2026
- Securities in Annexure II exit the LT-ASM framework effective June 19, 2026
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely for market surveillance purposes
- A consolidated list of all securities under the framework is provided in Annexure III
- Price band of exiting scrips reinstated to pre-LT-ASM level, subject to no other surveillance measure applying

## Regulatory Changes

The LT-ASM framework continues to operate under the revised structure established by Exchange notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024. This circular updates the list of securities under various stages of the framework and introduces stage movements effective June 19 and June 23, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin is collected/deposited for affected securities (Annexure I, Part A) from June 23, 2026
- Members must observe the Lower Price Band for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I, Part B) from June 19, 2026
- Trading in securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group (Annexure I, Part D) must comply with the respective group rules from June 23, 2026
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Lower Price Band applicable for securities in Annexure I (Part B); securities in Annexure II exit the LT-ASM framework
- **June 22, 2026**: Last date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement
- **June 23, 2026**: 100% margins effective for Annexure I (Part A) securities; Annexure I (Part D) securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding and creating positions in affected securities, reducing leverage and potentially impacting liquidity. Stage movements to higher LT-ASM levels with Lower Price Bands further restrict downside trading flexibility. Securities exiting the framework will see their price bands restored, which may improve trading activity. The LT-ASM framework is a surveillance tool and does not constitute a finding of wrongdoing against the listed companies involved.
