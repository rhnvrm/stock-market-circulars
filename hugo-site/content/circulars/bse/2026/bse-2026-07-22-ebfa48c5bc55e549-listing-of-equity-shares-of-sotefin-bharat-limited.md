## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Sotefin Bharat Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment (MT Group of Securities) effective Thursday, July 23, 2026. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade under the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Sotefin Bharat Limited, registered in Kolkata, West Bengal
- Number of securities: 1,81,60,307 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 18,160,307
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: SOTEFIN
- Scrip Code: 544832
- ISIN: INE12Z301012
- Market Lot: 600 shares
- Issue Price: Rs. 187/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 177/-)
- Date of allotment in the public issue: July 21, 2026
- Shares will trade only in dematerialised form; minimum market lot is 600 equity shares
- Scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on the listing day, July 23, 2026
- Market Maker: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited
- Registrar to the issue: Bigshare Services Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational listing notice implementing SEBI's existing framework for SME IPO listings and Trade-for-Trade requirements under CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in dematerialised form and in the prescribed minimum market lot of 600 shares.
- The company has enfaced non-transferability on physical share certificates and executed the necessary corporate action to mark the lock-in period in depository records, as detailed in Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern details are provided in Annexure II.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 23, 2026: Effective date of listing and start of dealings; also date of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)
- Trade-for-Trade status applies for 10 trading days from the listing date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notice for a new SME IPO. It is directly relevant to allottees, market makers, and trading members handling the scrip, but carries limited systemic market impact given its SME-segment scope and temporary Trade-for-Trade restriction designed to ensure orderly price discovery in the initial trading period.
