## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260505-22 informs trading members that 3,25,000 new equity shares of SPML Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 500402) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, May 6, 2026. The shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 215/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 213/-).

## Key Points

- 3,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 213/- (total issue price Rs. 215/-) to Promoters via preferential allotment
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 77831336 to 78156335
- Date of allotment: 02 March 2026
- ISIN: INE937A01023
- All 3,25,000 shares are subject to lock-in until 14 November 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the listing of these new securities effective May 6, 2026. The lock-in restriction on all 3,25,000 shares must be observed until 14 November 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** 06 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 14 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 3,25,000 equity shares to SPML Infra Limited's tradable equity base; however, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until November 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is minimal in the near term. The preferential allotment to Promoters at Rs. 215/- per share reflects a warrant conversion, which may marginally increase Promoter shareholding percentage.
