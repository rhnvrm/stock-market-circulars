## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE538L07221 (Code: 953501)
- Debenture Series: AHDL-9.55%-1-3-26-PVT
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-820/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debentures from February 13, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform relevant stakeholders
- Debenture holders should be aware of the record date for redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED's 9.55% debenture series maturing on March 1, 2026. This is a standard procedure for debt securities approaching redemption. Impact is limited to this specific debt instrument and does not affect the company's equity shares or other debenture series. Trading members dealing in corporate debt should update their systems to block trading in this ISIN from the specified date.
