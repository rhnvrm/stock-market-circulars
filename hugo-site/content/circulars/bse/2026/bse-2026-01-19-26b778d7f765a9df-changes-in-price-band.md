## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 10 securities effective January 20, 2026. The changes include tightening of price bands ranging from 2% to 10% across various companies. This is a surveillance measure to control price volatility in these scrips.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions effective from January 20, 2026
- 10 scrips affected by the change
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 10%
- Ashnisha Industries and Chandni Machines have the tightest band at 2%
- Four companies (Bosch Home Comfort India, JTL Industries, Mukat Pipes, ZR2 Bioenergy) set at 10% band
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

Revised price bands for affected securities:

1. Ashnisha Industries Ltd (541702) - 2%
2. Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd (523398) - 10%
3. Chandni Machines Ltd (542627) - 2%
4. Chandra Prabhu International Ltd (530309) - 5%
5. JTL Industries Ltd (534600) - 10%
6. Manaksia Aluminium Company Ltd (539045) - 5%
7. Modi Rubber Ltd (500890) - 5%
8. Mukat Pipes Ltd (523832) - 10%
9. United Van Der Horst Ltd (522091) - 5%
10. ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640) - 10%

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must observe the revised price bands for the listed scrips
- All trades must be executed within the new price band limits
- Members requiring clarification should contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 19, 2026
- Effective Date: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions will limit intraday price movement in these 10 securities. Tighter bands (2-5%) suggest heightened surveillance concerns regarding volatility or market activity. Traders and investors in these stocks will experience restricted price discovery within the new bands. The changes may reduce liquidity in the short term as market participants adjust to the new trading parameters.
