## Summary

BSE has decided not to proceed with the suspension of trading in securities of Shree Securities Ltd (scrip code: 538975), which had been announced via Notice No. 20260422-16 dated April 22, 2026. The cancellation is effective May 25, 2026. The original suspension was triggered by non-compliance with Regulation 76 of SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018 for two consecutive quarters — September 2025 and December 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for Shree Securities Ltd (scrip code: 538975) stands cancelled effective May 25, 2026.
- Original suspension was announced on April 22, 2026 via Notice No. 20260422-16.
- Non-compliance involved Regulation 76 of SEBI (Depositories & Participants) Regulations, 2018 for Q2 and Q3 FY2025-26 (September 2025 and December 2025 quarters).
- All other provisions of the original notice remain unchanged.
- Contact for clarifications: bse.soplodr@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reverses the enforcement action (suspension) that was set to take effect under the earlier notice dated April 22, 2026, for Regulation 76 non-compliance under SEBI (Depositories and Participants) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance action is required by market participants for Shree Securities Ltd as a result of this notice. The company's securities will remain tradeable. Market participants should note that all other terms of the original notice (No. 20260422-16) remain in force for any other companies listed therein.

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Original suspension notice (No. 20260422-16) issued.
- **May 21, 2026**: This cancellation notice issued.
- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date — trading suspension for Shree Securities Ltd will not be enforced.

## Impact Assessment

The cancellation benefits holders and traders of Shree Securities Ltd (scrip code: 538975) by preventing a trading halt that would have restricted liquidity. The broader market impact is minimal as this affects only one scrip. The decision suggests the company has either remedied its non-compliance with Regulation 76 (related to reconciliation of records of dematerialized securities) or otherwise satisfied the Exchange's requirements. Investors in this stock should note continued monitoring by BSE for ongoing compliance obligations.
