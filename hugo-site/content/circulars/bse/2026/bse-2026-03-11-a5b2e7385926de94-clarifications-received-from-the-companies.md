## Summary

BSE has compiled clarifications received from multiple listed companies in response to surveillance queries regarding significant volume or price movement in their shares. Three companies — Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip: 539177), Fabtech Technologies Ltd (Scrip: 544558 / NSE: FABTECH), and Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd (Scrip: 500186) — responded to BSE's reference no. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026, confirming compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and denying any pending undisclosed price-sensitive information.

## Key Points

- Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd confirmed all information is in the public domain and no price-sensitive information is pending disclosure under SEBI LODR Regulation 30; volume increase attributed to market forces.
- Fabtech Technologies Ltd confirmed no pending price-sensitive announcement that could explain the share price movement; movement deemed purely market-driven.
- Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd stated it has been periodically disseminating updates on all material events and is compliant with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.
- All three companies affirmed ongoing compliance with disclosure obligations to stock exchanges.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. These are routine clarification responses under the existing framework of Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies are required to respond to BSE surveillance queries promptly under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- Companies must ensure all price-sensitive information is disclosed to exchanges as and when it arises.
- No new compliance actions are required from investors or other market participants as a result of this circular.

## Important Dates

- March 10–11, 2026: BSE issued surveillance queries to the companies.
- March 11, 2026: All three companies filed their clarification responses.

## Impact Assessment

The impact on the broader market is low. These are standard surveillance-driven clarifications with no material adverse disclosures. Investors in Authum Investment & Infrastructure (539177), Fabtech Technologies (544558/FABTECH), and Hindustan Oil Exploration (500186) should note that the companies have confirmed no undisclosed material information, suggesting recent price/volume movement is speculative or sentiment-driven rather than fundamentals-based.
