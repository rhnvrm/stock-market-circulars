## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that representatives of nine Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) will visit the Department of Investor Services (DIS) at BSE's Cama Building, Dalal Street, Mumbai during the first week of May 2026. Members and investors with complaints against client companies of these RTAs may approach the representatives during the scheduled hours.

## Key Points

- Nine RTA representatives scheduled to visit DIS across five days (4–8 May 2026)
- Visits held at Department of Investor Services, 1st Floor, Cama Building, Dalal Street, Mumbai–400001
- Investors/members with grievances against RTA client companies may meet representatives during scheduled slots
- Contact officers: Mr. Akshay Kulthe (2272 8282) and Mr. Anjappa Kadmur (2272 5770)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Trading members are informed to relay this information to investors with pending grievances against RTA client companies so they may utilize the in-person visit opportunity.
