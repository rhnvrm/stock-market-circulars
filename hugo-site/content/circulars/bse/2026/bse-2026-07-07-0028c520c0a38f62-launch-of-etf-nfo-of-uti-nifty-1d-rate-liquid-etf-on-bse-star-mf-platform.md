## Summary

BSE has notified members that UTI Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for UTI NIFTY 1D RATE LIQUID ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 8, 2026 to July 15, 2026. The notice outlines scheme details and operational instructions for members regarding fund pay-in, cancellation rules, and payment acceptance for the NFO period.

## Key Points

- UTI Mutual Fund is launching UTI NIFTY 1D RATE LIQUID ETF as an NFO on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Two scheme variants are available: UTI NIFTY 1D RATE LIQUID ETF and UTI NIFTY 1D RATE LIQUID ETF - Direct Plan - Growth, both classified under the Debt category.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is July 16, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those executed during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled, with a CC 'N' flag reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM (One Time Mandate) payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026.
- The NFO is extended until 6:00 PM on July 15, 2026, exclusively for Lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice communicating a fund house's NFO launch and associated platform procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026, for allotment eligibility, including for cheque and OTM payments.
- Members handling Lumpsum purchases must track the extended cutoff of 6:00 PM on July 15, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 8, 2026 to July 15, 2026
- Extended NFO period (Lumpsum purchases only): until 6:00 PM, July 15, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 16, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund product launch notice affecting only members and investors dealing in mutual fund units via BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct bearing on listed equity securities or broader market trading activity, and primarily serves as an operational/compliance reminder for member firms handling the NFO subscription process.
