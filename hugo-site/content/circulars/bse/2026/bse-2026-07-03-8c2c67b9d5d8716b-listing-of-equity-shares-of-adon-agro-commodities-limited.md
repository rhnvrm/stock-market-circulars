## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The date of listing and further security details will be announced through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544809
- Symbol: ADON
- ISIN: INE1SME01017
- Segment: SME
- Department: DCS-Listing
- No attachment accompanies this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's SME segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN once trading commences. No immediate action is required until the formal listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

Notice Date: July 3, 2026. The actual date of listing and dealing commencement will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact as this pertains to a single SME listing. Relevant primarily to investors and market participants tracking ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED and SME segment activity.
