## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Vans Electroengineerings Limited, filed on July 17, 2026, has been made available on the Exchange's website for a period of at least 21 days under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations, inviting public comments ahead of the company's proposed SME IPO.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document pertains to Vans Electroengineerings Limited's proposed SME IPO.
- The document was filed with BSE on July 17, 2026.
- It contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME public issues page for public review.
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine disclosure requirement under SEBI ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments on the draft offer document should be emailed to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from the notice date.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: July 17, 2026
- Notice date: July 20, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date (until approximately August 10, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural disclosure step in the SME IPO process for Vans Electroengineerings Limited and does not affect currently listed securities. It signals an upcoming SME IPO that investors and market participants may wish to track, but has no immediate trading or price impact.
