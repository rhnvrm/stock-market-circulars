## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the new ISIN number allotted to Mangalam Worldwide Ltd (Scrip Code: 544764) following the sub-division of the company's equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260630-19 dated June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for Mangalam Worldwide Ltd: INE0JYY01029
- The new ISIN reflects the sub-division (stock split) of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Re.1/-
- The new ISIN will be effective for trades done on and from the ex-date
- Reference: DR- 669/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy change; this is an operational/administrative update implementing a previously approved corporate action (sub-division of shares) by assigning a new ISIN.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE0JYY01029) for Mangalam Worldwide Ltd for all trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 7, 2026
- Ex-Date (new ISIN effective from): July 10, 2026
- Prior related notice: June 30, 2026 (Notice No. 20260630-19)

## Impact Assessment

The sub-division increases the number of outstanding shares proportionally while reducing the face value per share, which typically improves liquidity and affordability for retail investors without changing the underlying market capitalization. Trading members and depository participants need to ensure accurate mapping of the new ISIN to avoid settlement errors post ex-date.
