## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Basket Repo and Special Repo segment for February 2026. The circular provides detailed settlement schedules for Ready Leg (T+0 and T+1) and Forward Leg transactions, covering 19 settlement numbers from February 2 to February 27, 2026. Settlement timings remain consistent with existing frameworks.

## Key Points

- Settlement period: February 1, 2026 to February 28, 2026
- 19 settlement cycles scheduled (Settlement No. 2526206 to 2526224)
- Three settlement schedules provided: Ready Leg T+0, Ready Leg T+1, and Forward Leg
- Standard settlement timings apply for Basket Repo and Special Repo transactions
- No trading on February 7, 8, 14, 15, 19, 21, 22, and 28 (weekends/holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine settlement schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to specified pay-in times: 2:15 PM for all settlement types
- Securities/Funds pay-out timings vary by settlement type:
  - Ready Leg T+0: By 3:00 PM
  - Ready Leg T+1: By 1:00 PM (Special Repo) or 3:00 PM (Basket Repo)
  - Forward Leg: By 3:00 PM
- Participants should refer to ICCL Notice No. 20180525-18 dated May 25, 2018 for detailed guidelines

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: February 1, 2026 to February 28, 2026
- **First Settlement**: February 2, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526206)
- **Last Settlement**: February 27, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526224)
- **Final Pay-out for Last Settlement**: March 2, 2026 (for T+1 Ready Leg)

## Settlement Schedule Details

### Ready Leg T+0
Same-day settlement with transaction, pay-in, and pay-out occurring on the same date.

### Ready Leg T+1
Next-day settlement with pay-in and pay-out occurring one business day after the transaction date.

### Forward Leg
Settlement occurs on T+1 basis matching the transaction maturity date.

### Settlement Timings
- **Pay-in Time**: 2:15 PM (all types)
- **Basket Repo Pay-out**: By 3:00 PM (all legs)
- **Special Repo Pay-out**: By 3:00 PM (T+0 and Forward), By 1:00 PM (T+1)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational circular that provides necessary scheduling information for repo market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Market participants in the repo segment will use this schedule for planning their February 2026 transactions and settlements. The schedule follows standard settlement conventions with no deviations from normal practice.

**Participant Action**: Market participants should update their settlement calendars and ensure compliance with specified timings for securities and funds pay-in/pay-out.
