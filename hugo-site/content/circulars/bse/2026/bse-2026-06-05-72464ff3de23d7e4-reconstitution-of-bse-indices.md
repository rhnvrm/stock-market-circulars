## Summary

BSE Index Services Pvt. Limited announced the June 2026 reconstitution results for 31 BSE indices. Changes take effect at the open of Monday, June 22, 2026. The BSE CPSE, BSE METAL, and BSE PSU indices remain unchanged. The reconstitution has been included in tonight's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE).

## Key Points

- Reconstitution affects 31 indices spanning large-cap, mid-cap, small-cap, micro-cap, and sectoral categories
- Largest changes: BSE NEXT 500 and BSE NEXT 250 MICROCAP each see 51 adds and 51 drops
- BSE 1000 sees 40 adds and 40 drops; BSE 250 MICROCAP sees 47 adds and 47 drops
- BSE SMALLCAP 500 sees 42 adds and 42 drops
- BSE CPSE, BSE METAL, and BSE PSU indices are unchanged
- BSE CONSUMER DURABLES, BSE POWER, BSE ALLCAP, BSE MIDSMALLCAP, BSE SMALLCAP, and BSE SME IPO see only drops (no adds)
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) tonight

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a periodic index reconstitution carried out by BSE Index Services Pvt. Limited (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.), a wholly owned subsidiary of BSE Ltd, in accordance with established index methodology.

## Compliance Requirements

- Fund managers and ETF providers tracking affected BSE indices must rebalance portfolios to reflect new constituents by the effective date
- Clients with queries should contact bseindex@bseindia.com
- Market participants should update their systems with the revised index compositions from the corporate action file (*.SDE) published tonight

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 22, 2026 (at market open)
- **Corporate Action File:** Available end-of-day June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This reconstitution is one of the most extensive in scope, touching 31 indices simultaneously. Stocks being added to major indices like BSE 200, BSE 500, or BSE 1000 are likely to see increased demand from passive funds and ETFs tracking those benchmarks ahead of the effective date. Conversely, stocks dropped from indices may face selling pressure. The large-scale micro-cap and small-cap index changes (BSE 250 MICROCAP, BSE NEXT 250 MICROCAP, BSE SMALLCAP 500, BSE NEXT 500) indicate significant market-cap shifts in the lower end of the market. Index-tracking funds will need to execute rebalancing trades before June 22, 2026 open.
