## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Consecutive Investments & Trading Co Ltd. The company's name has been officially changed to Consecutive Commodities Limited, effective from December 16, 2025. This certificate was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre on December 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: Consecutive Investments & Trading Co Ltd
- **New Name**: Consecutive Commodities Limited
- **Corporate Identification Number (CIN)**: L67120WB1982PLC035452
- **Effective Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Office**: 23 Ganesh Chandra Avenue, 3rd Floor, Kolkata - 700013, West Bengal, India
- **Certifying Authority**: Assistant Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- **Approved by**: Ibson Shah, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved. This is a standard corporate name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name alongside the new name for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 16, 2025
- **Effective Date of Name Change**: December 16, 2025
- **Old Name Display Requirement**: Must be maintained until December 16, 2027 (two years)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, business activities, or financial position.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights or liabilities. Existing shares, contracts, and legal obligations continue under the new name without interruption.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update its stationery, signage, and documentation to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display of old and new names for the mandatory two-year period.
