## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260407-6 announces the resumption of trading in debentures of PTC India Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Codes 972889 & 972893) effective April 8, 2026. The trading was previously suspended in connection with a Record Date of March 13, 2026 set for redemption on exercise of a buyback option and payment of interest. Since the buyback option has not been fully exercised, the company has requested resumption of trading.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of PTC India Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Codes 972889 & 972893) will resume from **April 8, 2026**.
- The suspension was related to Exchange Notice No. 20260311-63 dated March 11, 2026, which intimated a Record Date of March 13, 2026 for redemption on exercise of buyback option and payment of interest.
- The buyback option was **not fully exercised**, prompting the company to request resumption.
- Reference: DR-606/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice reinstates normal trading status for the specified debentures following partial completion of a buyback event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are required to note the resumption of trading in Scrip Codes 972889 and 972893 effective April 8, 2026.
- No additional compliance action is required beyond awareness of the revised trading status.

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260311-63 issued, announcing Record Date.
- **March 13, 2026**: Record Date fixed for redemption on exercise of buyback option and interest payment.
- **April 7, 2026**: Current notice issued announcing resumption.
- **April 8, 2026**: Trading resumes in Scrip Codes 972889 & 972893.

## Impact Assessment

The resumption of trading restores liquidity for holders of PTC India Financial Services Ltd debentures under Scrip Codes 972889 and 972893. The partial non-exercise of the buyback option means some debenture holders will continue to hold these instruments and can now trade them on the BSE debt segment. Impact is moderate and confined to participants in these specific debt instruments.
