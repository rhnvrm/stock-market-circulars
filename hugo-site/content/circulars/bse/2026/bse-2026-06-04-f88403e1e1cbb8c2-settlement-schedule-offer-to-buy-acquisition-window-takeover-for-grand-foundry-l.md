## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) pertaining to Grand Foundry Limited. The bidding window runs from June 9, 2026 to June 22, 2026, with final settlement on July 7, 2026. Members and custodians must deliver demat shares exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism and ensure the early pay-in is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed bidding/confirmation window deadlines.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No. 2026064
- Bidding window: June 9, 2026 – June 22, 2026
- Obligation download date: July 6, 2026; Settlement date: July 7, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism (available on both CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026064 must be used in depository system entries
- CDSL and NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members are directed to continue following Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must complete early pay-in of securities in the depository system sufficiently in advance of the BSE OTB Bidding window closure (for member bids) and the Custodian confirmation window closure.
- Early pay-in must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines.
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026064 when submitting in depository systems.
- No EPN file upload is required.
- For queries, contact ICCL Help Desk at 022-69158590 / 45720490 / 45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for stock exchange members and custodians participating in the takeover acquisition window for Grand Foundry Limited. The requirement to use early pay-in exclusively (no EPN file needed) and to ensure timely reflection in BSE/ICCL systems places a process-compliance burden on participants. Retail investors tendering shares should coordinate with their brokers/custodians well before the June 22, 2026 bid closure to avoid rejection. The settlement lag (bid end June 22 → settlement July 7) is standard for takeover windows.
