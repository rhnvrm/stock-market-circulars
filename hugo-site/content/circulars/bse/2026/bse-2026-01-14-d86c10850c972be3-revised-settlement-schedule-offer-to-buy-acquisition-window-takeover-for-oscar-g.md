## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy through Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED. The bid end date has been extended by one day from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026. The settlement will be processed under Settlement No. 2025138 with obligation download on January 29, 2026 and settlement date on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bid period: January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026 (revised from January 15, 2026)
- Settlement Number: 2025138
- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Obligation Download Date: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- This circular supersedes Notice No. 20251231-8
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Available in both depositories (NSDL and CDSL)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular revises the timeline for an existing takeover offer, extending the bid acceptance period by one trading day.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2025138 in the depository system
- Demat shares should be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Members must refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- All delivery instructions must comply with the revised settlement calendar

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Revised Bid End Date:** January 16, 2026 (extended from January 15, 2026)
- **Obligation Download:** January 29, 2026
- **Settlement Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Notice Date:** January 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The one-day extension of the bid end date provides additional time for shareholders of OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED to participate in the takeover offer. This revision affects:

- **Shareholders:** Additional day to tender shares in the acquisition window
- **Members/Custodians:** Need to update their settlement calendars and client communications
- **Market Operations:** Minor adjustment to settlement processing timeline

The impact is limited to participants in this specific takeover offer. Members and custodians can contact ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com) for clarifications.
