## Summary

Fairchem Organics Limited (Scrip Code: 543252) has announced a buyback of equity shares with the record date set for January 5, 2026. The record date will determine which shareholders are eligible to participate in the tender offer. The ex-entitlement date is also January 5, 2026, with settlement number DR-791/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Fairchem Organics Limited (Scrip Code: 543252)
- Record Date: January 5, 2026
- Ex-Entitlement Date: January 5, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-791/2025-2026
- Purpose: Determine entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in buyback offer
- Notice Number: 20260101-39
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities - Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the exchange are required to take note of the buyback announcement
- Only shareholders holding shares as of the record date (January 5, 2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade on ex-entitlement basis from January 5, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-791/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This buyback announcement has moderate impact limited to shareholders of Fairchem Organics Limited. Shareholders holding shares on the record date will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback offer. The buyback may provide an exit opportunity to shareholders at potentially attractive prices and could result in value accretion for continuing shareholders. Trading members need to ensure proper tracking of ex-entitlement date for settlement purposes.
