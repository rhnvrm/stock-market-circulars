## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,08,604 new equity shares of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) are available for trading effective Friday, March 06, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807)
- **ISIN:** INE483S01020
- **Total New Shares:** 14,08,604 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Nature:** Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, issued on rights basis
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Ranges:**
  - 10,955 shares: 3001101919 – 3001112873
  - 13,83,199 shares: 3470116298 – 3471499496
  - 125 shares: 3476101502 – 3476101626
  - 14,325 shares: 3479604908 – 3479619232

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly created securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions are required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Friday, March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 14,08,604 equity shares to the tradeable float of AvenuesAI Ltd., resulting from a rights issue conversion. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution differential applies. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and traders in this specific scrip.
