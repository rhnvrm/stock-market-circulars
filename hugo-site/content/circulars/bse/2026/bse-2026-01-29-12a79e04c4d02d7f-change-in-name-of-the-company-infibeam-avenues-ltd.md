## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Infibeam Avenues Limited. The company name has been officially changed to AvenuesAI Limited with effect from January 20, 2026. The company was originally incorporated as Infibeam Incorporation Limited and retains its CIN L64203GJ2010PLC061366.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Infibeam Avenues Limited** to **AvenuesAI Limited**
- Effective date: **January 20, 2026**
- CIN remains unchanged: L64203GJ2010PLC061366
- Certificate approved and digitally signed by Ibson Shah, Assistant Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Company is limited by shares
- Registered address: 28th Floor, GIFT Two Building, Block No. 56, Road-5C, Zone-5, GIFT City, Gandhinagar-382355, Gujarat

## Regulatory Changes

Name change approved under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The certificate has been issued by the Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the **old name (Infibeam Avenues Limited) along with the new name (AvenuesAI Limited) for a period of two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This applies to all locations where company name display is mandatory
- The name change does not affect rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: January 20, 2026
- **Certificate Issuance Date**: January 20, 2026 (digitally signed at 13:13:36 IST)
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from effective date (until January 20, 2028)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name change reflects a strategic rebranding initiative, likely signaling the company's focus on AI-related business. Stakeholders should update records and documentation to reflect the new company name.

**Operational Impact**: The certificate clarifies that all rights and liabilities remain unaffected by the name change. Investors, creditors, and business partners should note the new name for all future correspondence and transactions.

**Verification**: Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
