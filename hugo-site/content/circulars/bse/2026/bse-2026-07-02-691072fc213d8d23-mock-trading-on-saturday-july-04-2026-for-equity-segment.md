## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, July 04, 2026. This session allows trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms, across various functionalities such as call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halts, and block deals. The exercise also fulfills SEBI's regulatory requirement for periodic testing of trading and risk management software. Additionally, BSE will release a new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 during this session, with further details to be published separately.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for Equity segment scheduled on Saturday, July 04, 2026
- Members may participate in either the Mock Trading Session or the UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements per SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Trading members using third-party trading platforms can use this session to test their applications for various functionalities including exceptional market conditions
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 will be released during this session; details to follow via a separate circular
- Members must submit feedback on the mock trading session by 4:30 pm on the session day
- Reference to earlier Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020, regarding testing of trading and risk management software

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing existing SEBI requirements (SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020) for periodic software testing by trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must log in during the specified window (10:15 am to 10:45 am) to participate
- Members are expected to test their trading and risk management software as part of regulatory compliance
- Feedback on the mock trading session must be submitted by 4:30 pm on the day of the session via provided contact numbers/email
- Members may alternatively use the UAT environment to satisfy the same regulatory testing requirement

## Important Dates

- Mock trading session: Saturday, July 04, 2026
- Login window: 10:15 am – 10:45 am
- Morning Block Deal Window: 10:45 am – 11:00 am
- Pre-Open Order Entry: 11:00 am – 11:08 am; Matching: 11:08 am – 11:15 am
- Continuous Trading T+1: 11:15 am – 03:30 pm
- Continuous Trading T+0: 11:15 am – 01:30 pm
- Special Pre-Open for IPO & Re-listed scrips: Order Entry from 11:00 am, Matching 11:45 am – 12:00 pm
- Continuous Trading for SPOS Scrips: 12:00 pm – 03:30 pm
- Periodic Call Auction (4 sessions of 1 hour): 11:30 am – 03:30 pm
- Auction for Settlement Details (Offer Entry and Matching): 12:00 pm – 12:45 pm
- Afternoon Block Deal Window: 01:30 pm – 01:45 pm
- Closing: 03:30 pm – 03:40 pm
- Post-closing: 03:40 pm – 03:50 pm
- Trade Modification T+1: until 04:00 pm
- Trade Modification T+0: until 01:45 pm
- Feedback submission deadline: 4:30 pm on session day

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with no direct impact on listed securities or the broader market. It is relevant primarily to trading members and their technology teams, who should ensure system readiness and participate in testing to remain compliant with SEBI's software testing requirements. The release of BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 during this session may require members to update their trading workstation software.
