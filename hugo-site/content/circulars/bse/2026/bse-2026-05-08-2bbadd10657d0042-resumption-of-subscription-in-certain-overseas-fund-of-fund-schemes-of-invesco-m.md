## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260508-1 informing members that Invesco Mutual Fund has resumed subscriptions in three overseas fund of fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective May 08, 2026. This follows a temporary suspension of fresh subscriptions that was implemented via Exchange Notice No. 20260102-26 dated January 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Subscriptions are resumed effective May 08, 2026 in three Invesco overseas fund of fund schemes
- Resumption covers Lump Sum investments (fresh and additional purchases), Switch-ins, and fresh SIP and STP registrations
- The resumption applies specifically on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Requisite system changes have already been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform by Invesco AMC
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Invesco AMC is attached to the circular for reference

## Regulatory Changes

The temporary suspension of fresh subscriptions in these Invesco overseas fund of fund schemes, originally imposed on January 02, 2026, has been lifted. Subscriptions are now permitted across all modes including lump sum, switch-in, SIP, and STP.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers/distributors) are advised to take note of the resumption while executing transactions in the affected schemes
- No separate compliance action is required beyond awareness; platform changes have already been implemented

## Important Dates

- **January 02, 2026**: Original suspension of subscriptions (Exchange Notice No. 20260102-26)
- **May 08, 2026**: Effective date of resumption of subscriptions in the listed schemes

## Impact Assessment

This circular restores investor access to three Invesco overseas fund of fund schemes after approximately four months of suspension. The impacted schemes are:
- INVESCO INDIA – INVESCO Global Consumer Trends Fund of Fund
- INVESCO INDIA – INVESCO Global Equity Income Fund of Fund
- INVESCO INDIA – INVESCO Pan European Equity Fund of Fund

Investors and distributors who were unable to make fresh investments, switch-ins, or register new SIP/STP mandates during the suspension period can now resume these activities. The impact is moderate, affecting participants with exposure or interest in overseas equity markets via these fund of fund vehicles.
