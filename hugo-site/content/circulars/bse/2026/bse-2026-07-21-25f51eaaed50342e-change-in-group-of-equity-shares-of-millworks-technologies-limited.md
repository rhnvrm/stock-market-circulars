## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260720-24 dated July 20, 2026, informing trading members that the equity shares of MILLWORKS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544826), an SME-listed company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Tuesday, August 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544826 (Millworks Technologies Limited) shifts from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment.
- The stock will be dealt under the M Group following the transition.
- This notice is a continuation of an earlier BSE notice (No. 20260720-24) dated July 20, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading segment for the specified scrip under existing BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and processes to reflect the change in settlement group for this scrip from the effective date, ensuring correct order routing and settlement handling under the Rolling segment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Prior Notice Reference: July 20, 2026 (Notice No. 20260720-24)
- Effective Date of Segment Change: Tuesday, August 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transition from Trade for Trade (which mandates delivery-based settlement without intraday trading) to the Rolling segment generally increases trading flexibility and liquidity for the stock, as it permits normal rolling settlement. This is a routine but stock-specific administrative change relevant primarily to shareholders, traders, and brokers dealing in Millworks Technologies shares, with limited broader market impact.
