## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Vahh Chemicals Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Vahh Chemicals Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544778, Trading Symbol: VAHH, ISIN: INE0H3U01013
- Exact listing date and security details to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing and await the separate notice confirming the listing date and full security details before initiating dealings in the scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 10, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact as this is a routine SME segment listing notice. The listing of Vahh Chemicals Limited (VAHH) will provide SME investors and trading members access to trade the scrip once the formal listing date is confirmed. Market participants should monitor for the follow-up notice specifying the listing date.
