## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Segment for March 2026. The notice (No. 20260302-8) specifies the last dates for margin reporting and corresponding penalty dates for each trade date in March 2026.

## Key Points

- Covers trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026 in the Equity Segment.
- Each trade date has a designated last date for margin reporting and a penalty date if reporting is missed.
- The schedule accounts for market holidays (e.g., no trade dates on certain days, penalty dates extending into April 2026).
- Issued by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic schedule circular issued by ICCL for operational compliance purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the specified last date of margin reporting.
- Failure to report by the last date will attract penalties effective from the listed penalty date.
- Members should ensure their back-office and reporting systems align with the published schedule.
