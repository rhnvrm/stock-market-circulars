## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective January 20, 2026. Two securities - Ashnisha Industries Ltd and Chandni Machines Ltd - will be moved to higher ESM stages. No new securities are being added to the ESM framework, and no securities are being removed or moved to lower stages.

## Key Points

- No new securities added to ESM framework
- 2 securities moving to higher ESM stages: Ashnisha Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 541702, ISIN: INE694W01024) and Chandni Machines Ltd (Scrip Code: 542627, ISIN: INE01GZ01011)
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages
- No securities exiting the ESM framework
- Consolidated list shows 30+ securities across ESM Stage I and Stage II (list truncated in provided content)
- Ashnisha Industries Ltd moving to ESM Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

The ESM framework continues to operate as a surveillance tool to alert investors about securities exhibiting abnormal price movements or other concerns. Securities in higher ESM stages face stricter trading restrictions and surveillance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the ESM stage changes for affected securities
- Trading members must ensure compliance with ESM framework requirements
- Enhanced due diligence required for securities under ESM surveillance
- Investors should exercise caution when trading ESM securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 20, 2026
- Changes to ESM classifications become applicable from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Only 2 securities affected by stage changes

**Trading Impact**: Medium for affected securities - Higher ESM stages typically result in additional margins, reduced price bands, and trade-for-trade settlement

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding or trading Ashnisha Industries Ltd and Chandni Machines Ltd should be aware of increased surveillance and potential trading restrictions
