## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for March 2026 for the Equity Segment. The circular specifies the trade dates, corresponding last dates for margin reporting, and penalty dates applicable to all BSE members.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers trade dates from 02-03-2026 through 30-03-2026.
- Each trade date has a designated last date for margin reporting (typically T+6 trading days) and a subsequent penalty date.
- Members must ensure timely margin reporting to avoid penalties.
- The schedule is issued by Deputy Managers Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar on behalf of ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine periodic schedule notice under existing client margin reporting obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All BSE members trading in the Equity Segment must report client margins by the specified last date for each trade date.
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties assessed on the corresponding penalty date.
- Members should align their internal reporting workflows with the published schedule for the full month of March 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate operational impact on all BSE member brokers participating in the Equity Segment. While it introduces no new rules, timely adherence to the reporting schedule is mandatory to avoid penalties. Members with large client books should ensure back-office systems are configured to meet each deadline. The schedule extends into early April 2026 for trades executed in the last week of March, requiring cross-month planning.
