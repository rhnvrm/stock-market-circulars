## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,00,000 new equity shares of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912, ISIN: INE557Z01018) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 83.75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 73.75/-).

## Key Points

- 3,00,000 equity shares (Rs. 10/- face value) issued at a premium of Rs. 73.75/-, total issue price Rs. 83.75/-
- Issued to Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 20868901 to 21168900
- Date of allotment: March 19, 2026
- Effective trading date: June 9, 2026
- Entire 3,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 25, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational listing notice pursuant to an already-approved preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and are permitted to trade these securities from June 9, 2026. No specific action required from members beyond awareness of the lock-in restriction on all 3,00,000 shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 19, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 9, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 25, 2027 (applicable to all 3,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 20868901 to 21168900)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine promoter preferential allotment via warrant conversion for a small-cap company. All newly listed shares carry a lock-in period until December 25, 2027, meaning they cannot be sold in the open market until that date, which further limits any near-term trading or price impact. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no change in dividend or voting rights structure.
