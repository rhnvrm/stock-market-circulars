## Summary

BSE has updated its encumbrance-based surveillance measure (continuation of Notice No. 20220131-43 dated January 31, 2022). Securities with high promoter and/or non-promoter encumbrance that meet the inclusion criteria will attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments from June 18, 2026. Separately, certain securities have been identified as eligible to exit the framework from June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in **Annexure I** have met the inclusion criteria and will attract a minimum **75% margin** in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The 75% margin applies to all open positions as on **June 17, 2026**, and to all new positions created from **June 18, 2026**.
- Securities in **Annexure II** are eligible to **exit** the encumbrance framework effective **June 16, 2026**.
- A consolidated list of all securities under this framework is provided in **Annexure III**.
- This measure operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion in this measure should **not** be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the list of securities subject to BSE's encumbrance-based surveillance framework, originally established under Notice No. 20220131-43 (January 31, 2022). Securities are added or removed from the framework based on periodic review of promoter and non-promoter encumbrance levels. The encumbrance framework is reviewed periodically by the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure that a minimum margin of 75% is collected/maintained for securities listed in Annexure I.
- The margin requirement applies to **all open positions** in affected securities as of June 17, 2026.
- The margin requirement also applies to **all new positions** in affected securities created from June 18, 2026 onwards.
- Members should take note of the updated Annexure III for the complete consolidated list of securities under the framework.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 15, 2026 | Notice issued |
| June 16, 2026 | Annexure II securities exit the encumbrance framework |
| June 17, 2026 | Reference date for open positions subject to 75% margin |
| June 18, 2026 | 75% minimum margin becomes effective for Annexure I securities |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **high impact** on trading activity in the affected securities. The 75% minimum margin requirement substantially increases the cost of taking or maintaining positions in these stocks, which may reduce liquidity and increase bid-ask spreads. Market participants — particularly those holding leveraged positions in affected securities — will need to provide significantly higher collateral. The measure targets companies where promoter and/or non-promoter shareholding is heavily encumbered, signalling elevated financial risk. Traders and investors in affected securities should review the annexures carefully to determine whether their holdings are impacted.
