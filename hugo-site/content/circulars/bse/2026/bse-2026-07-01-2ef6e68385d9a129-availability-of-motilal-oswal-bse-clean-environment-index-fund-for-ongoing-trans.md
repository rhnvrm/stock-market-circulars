## Summary

BSE has informed members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260604-26 dated June 04, 2026, the Motilal Oswal BSE Clean Environment Index Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- The reopening applies to both the Regular Plan and Direct Plan (Growth option) of the scheme.
- Regular Plan - Growth: ISIN INF247L01HD4 (Equity category)
- Direct Plan - Growth: ISIN INF247L01HC6 (Equity category)
- Ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP and SWP facilities are being restored on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- The notice references and follows up on prior Notice No. 20260604-26 dated June 04, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational update regarding platform availability of an existing mutual fund scheme, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and participants should update their systems/processes to allow ongoing transactions (subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, SWP) for the specified ISINs from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for reopening: July 02, 2026
- Reference prior notice date: June 04, 2026
- Notice date: July 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine administrative notice enabling resumption of ongoing transactions for a specific index fund scheme on BSE's mutual fund platform, relevant primarily to trading members and investors dealing in this specific fund rather than the broader market.
