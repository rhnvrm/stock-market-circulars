## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD (ISIN: INE860H07CL4, Code: 957542) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD
- Debenture Series: ABCL-8.85%-23-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE860H07CL4
- BSE Code: 957542
- Record Date: February 6, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Number: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension aligned with corporate action processing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- No dealings are permitted in the debenture series ABCL-8.85%-23-2-26-PVT from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 6, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series (ABCL-8.85%-23-2-26-PVT). This is a routine corporate action with no trading impact on the equity shares of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd. Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the terms. The suspension ensures clean settlement of the redemption process by preventing trading activity after the record date.
