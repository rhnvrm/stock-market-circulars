## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by five companies on its debt market segment effective January 12, 2026. The securities are admitted for trading in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- **Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited**: 200 CPs of Rs. 5 lakhs each, ISIN INE101Q14322, 9.25% p.a., maturing May 14, 2026, CARE A1 rated
- **Tata Housing Development Company Limited**: 3,700 CPs of Rs. 5 lakhs each, ISIN INE582L14JG7, 6.20% p.a., maturing February 27, 2026, CARE A1+/IND A1+ rated
- **360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited**: 200 CPs of Rs. 5 lakhs each, ISIN INE0TQI14020, 7.30% p.a., maturing July 8, 2026, CARE A1+ rated
- **Vivriti Capital Limited**: 500 CPs of Rs. 5 lakhs each, ISIN INE01HV14AK0, 9.30% p.a., maturing June 18, 2026, CRISIL A1+ rated
- **KEC International Ltd**: 3,000 CPs of Rs. 5 lakhs each, ISIN INE389H14LT4, 7.10% p.a., maturing March 30, 2026, CRISIL A1+/IND A1+ rated
- All securities trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 9, 2026 (MMFL, THDC, 360ONE, VCL); January 12, 2026 (KECIL)
- **Redemption Dates**: 
  - February 27, 2026 (Tata Housing)
  - March 30, 2026 (KEC International)
  - May 14, 2026 (Muthoottu Mini Financiers)
  - June 18, 2026 (Vivriti Capital)
  - July 8, 2026 (360 ONE Alternates)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine debt market listing with no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard commercial paper listings following established BSE debt segment procedures. Trading members have access to contact information for clarifications.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for institutional debt investors and trading members dealing in short-term debt instruments. The commercial papers offer interest rates ranging from 6.20% to 9.30% p.a. with tenures between 49 to 180 days.
