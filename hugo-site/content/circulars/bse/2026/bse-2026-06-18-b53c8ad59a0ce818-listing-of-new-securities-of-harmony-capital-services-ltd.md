## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 91,26,000 new equity shares of Harmony Capital Services Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530055) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 19, 2026. The shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 91,26,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 3000901 to 12126900
- Date of Allotment: April 15, 2026
- Issue Price: Rs. 10/- per share
- ISIN: INE264N01017
- Trading permitted from June 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new securities and their lock-in restrictions before executing any trades involving these shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 15, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 19, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (55,00,000 shares):** December 31, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (36,26,000 shares):** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 91.26 lakh new equity shares to the tradeable float of Harmony Capital Services Ltd., subject to lock-in restrictions. Of the total, 55,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 3000901–8500900) are locked in until December 31, 2027, and 36,26,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 8500901–12126900) are locked in until December 31, 2026. The freely tradeable portion will be limited until these lock-in periods expire. Impact is confined to this scrip with no broader market implications.
