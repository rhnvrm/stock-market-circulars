## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 34,01,360 new equity shares of Manorama Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 541974, ISIN: INE00VM01036), allotted pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), are listed and admitted for trading with effect from Tuesday, July 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 34,01,360 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) under a QIP.
- These new shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.
- Date of Allotment: July 02, 2026.
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,470/- per share.
- Distinctive Numbers: 59708531 to 63109890.
- Scrip Code: 541974; ISIN: INE00VM01036.
- Queries can be directed to Mr. Pranav Rewale (Tel: 9653268851).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department for shares already allotted via QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members; the notice is informational, confirming admission of the new shares to trading.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 02, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective From: July 07, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Notice Date: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Manorama Industries Ltd.'s tradable equity base by 34,01,360 shares at an issue price of Rs. 1,470/- each, reflecting successful capital raising via QIP. The impact is limited to the company's shareholding structure and liquidity, with negligible effect on the broader market.
