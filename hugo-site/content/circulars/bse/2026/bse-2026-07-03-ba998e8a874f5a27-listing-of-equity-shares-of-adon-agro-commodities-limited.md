## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Monday, July 6, 2026, the equity shares of ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED, registered office in Vashi, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra
- Number of securities: 23,017,270 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 23,017,270
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: ADON
- Scrip Code: 544809
- ISIN: INE1SME01017
- Market lot: 2000 shares
- Issue price: Rs. 70/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 60/-)
- Date of allotment in public issue: July 2, 2026
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on the listing day, July 6, 2026
- Market Maker: Shreni Shares Limited
- Registrar to the issue: KFIN Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the notice implements existing SEBI circulars on Trade-for-Trade segment classification for newly listed SME scrips and Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) rules for IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades are executed only in dematerialised form and in minimum market lots of 2000 shares.
- Members must account for the scrip's inclusion in the Special Pre-open Session on the listing date.
- The company has completed corporate action to mark the lock-in period in depository records as per Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 2, 2026
- Listing and Trading Commencement: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable: First 10 trading days from listing

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with limited market-wide impact, primarily relevant to trading members and investors dealing in ADON AGRO COMMODITIES LIMITED shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the initial 10 days is a standard safeguard to curb speculative trading in newly listed SME scrips.
