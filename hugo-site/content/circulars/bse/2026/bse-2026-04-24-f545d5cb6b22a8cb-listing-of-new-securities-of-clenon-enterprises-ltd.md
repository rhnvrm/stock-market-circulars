## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that 23,25,000 equity shares of Clenon Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 517564, ISIN: INE769B01028) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, April 27, 2026. These shares were allotted to non-promoters pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Hyderabad Bench.

## Key Points

- 23,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are newly listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to non-promoters under NCLT-approved Resolution Plan (order dated March 24, 2023)
- Distribution numbers: 8615317 to 10940316
- Date of allotment: December 30, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the Company
- All 23,25,000 shares are subject to lock-in until April 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to an existing NCLT Resolution Plan order dated March 24, 2023.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are informed of the new listing for awareness and record-keeping
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 23,25,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 8615317 to 10940316) are locked in until April 30, 2027 and cannot be traded during this period

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** March 24, 2023
- **Date of Allotment:** December 30, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 27, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** April 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing has limited immediate market impact as all newly listed shares are under lock-in until April 30, 2027. Post lock-in expiry, 23,25,000 additional shares will become freely tradeable, which could modestly increase the floating stock of Clenon Enterprises Ltd. The shares were issued at Rs. 10/- face value as part of a debt resolution process under NCLT supervision.
