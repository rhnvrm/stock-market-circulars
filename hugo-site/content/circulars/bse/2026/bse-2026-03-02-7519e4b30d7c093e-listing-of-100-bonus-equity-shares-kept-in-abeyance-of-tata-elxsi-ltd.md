## Summary

BSE has listed 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are permitted for trading effective Wednesday, March 4, 2026. The shares are face value Rs. 10/- each under ISIN INE670A01012.

## Key Points

- 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) are now listed on BSE
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance and are now being admitted to trading
- Face value of each share: Rs. 10/-
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive number range: 31165821 to 31165920 (100 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification pursuant to the listing of bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance pending resolution of associated legal or procedural matters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to update their systems to permit trading of these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from general market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The market impact of this circular is minimal. Only 100 shares are being released from abeyance, representing a negligible addition to Tata Elxsi Ltd's total outstanding share capital. This is a routine administrative listing action and does not signal any material change in the company's capital structure or business operations.
