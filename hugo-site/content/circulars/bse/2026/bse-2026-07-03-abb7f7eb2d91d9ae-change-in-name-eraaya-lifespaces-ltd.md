## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (Scrip Code: 531035) has changed its name to Ebix Limited, following submission of a Certificate of Incorporation reflecting the new name. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd
- New Name: Ebix Limited
- Scrip Code: 531035 (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: EBIX
- Change effective from July 09, 2026
- Supporting document: Certificate of Incorporation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a corporate name change communicated to the Exchange and updated in trading systems.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their records and systems to reflect the new Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name (EBIX) for Scrip Code 531035 from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change with no effect on the company's financials, operations, or shareholder rights. Trading members and investors should note the new Scrip ID (EBIX) to avoid confusion when placing orders or tracking the security post-effective date.
