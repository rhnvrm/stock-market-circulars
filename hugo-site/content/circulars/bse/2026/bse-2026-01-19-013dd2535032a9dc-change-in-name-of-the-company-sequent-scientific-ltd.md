## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of Sequent Scientific Ltd. This is an administrative corporate action that updates the company's official registered name.

## Key Points

- Company affected: Sequent Scientific Ltd
- Corporate action: Change in company name
- Exchange: BSE
- Circular date: January 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a disclosure circular informing market participants of the company's name change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the updated company name for all future references
- The scrip code and ISIN will remain unchanged
- Trading in the security continues unaffected

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 19, 2026
- Effective Date: As per company records

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. Company name changes are administrative in nature and do not affect the trading, settlement, or business operations of the company.

**Operational Impact:** Market participants and investors should update their records with the new company name. The scrip code remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading operations.
