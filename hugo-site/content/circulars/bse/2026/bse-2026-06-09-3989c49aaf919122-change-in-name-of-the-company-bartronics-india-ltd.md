## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bartronics India Ltd (scrip code 532694) is changing its name to Avio Smart Market Stack Limited. Effective June 15, 2026, the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to ASMS.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Bartronics India Ltd
- New company name: Avio Smart Market Stack Limited
- Scrip code remains unchanged: 532694
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: ASMS
- New abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: ASMS
- Change is effective from June 15, 2026
- Certificate of Incorporation attached as supporting document

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name for scrip code 532694 on the BOLT Plus System are being updated to reflect the new corporate name. No change in scrip code or ISIN has been announced in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the name change and update their systems accordingly to reference the new SCRIP ID (ASMS) and abbreviated name (ASMS) when trading in this scrip from June 15, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 09, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative name change with moderate operational impact. Trading members and investors holding or tracking Bartronics India Ltd must update their records and trading systems to reflect the new name, Avio Smart Market Stack Limited, and the updated BOLT Plus identifiers (ASMS). The scrip code 532694 remains unchanged, providing continuity for position tracking.
