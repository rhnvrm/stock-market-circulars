## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260312-36) directing trading members to suspend dealings in the Non-Convertible Debentures of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited. The suspension applies from 16 March 2026, which is also the record date fixed by the company for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading in Aadhar Housing Finance Limited's 9.55% NCD (AHFL-9.55%-31-3-26-PVT, ISIN: INE538L07270, BSE Code: 953764) is suspended effective 16 March 2026.
- The record date for redemption and interest payment is 16 March 2026.
- No dealings are permitted from 16 March 2026 onwards in these debentures.
- The circular reference is DR-840/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE procedures for NCD redemptions on the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE538L07270, BSE Code: 953764) from 16 March 2026.
- Members are required to take note of and comply with the no-dealing instruction issued by BSE Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 16 March 2026
- **Debenture Maturity:** 31 March 2026 (as indicated in the debenture name AHFL-9.55%-31-3-26-PVT)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to this specific NCD series of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited and does not affect equity trading. Existing holders of these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment around the record date of 16 March 2026. Market impact is low as this is a routine pre-redemption trading halt on the BSE Debt Market Segment.
