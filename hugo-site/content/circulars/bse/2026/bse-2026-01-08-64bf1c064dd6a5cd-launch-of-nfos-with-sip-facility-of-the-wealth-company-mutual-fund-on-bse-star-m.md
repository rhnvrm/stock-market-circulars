## Summary

THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND will offer THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from January 09, 2026 to January 23, 2026. The NFO includes 6 scheme options across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment variants, all categorized as DEBT schemes with tentative allotment date of January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND NFO launches with 6 scheme options
- NFO period: January 09, 2026 to January 23, 2026
- All schemes categorized as DEBT with unique ISINs assigned
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 29, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight on January 23, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds receipt
- Switches accepted during NFO period with conditions on fund receipt timing

## Scheme Details

The following schemes are being launched:

1. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF2F0001388)
2. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF2F0001396)
3. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF2F0001404)
4. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF2F0001412)
5. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF2F0001420)
6. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF2F0001438)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on January 23, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 29, 2026
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds payout or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Switch transactions require funds receipt on or before allotment date for consideration

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: January 09, 2026
- **NFO Closure Date**: January 23, 2026 (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **Extended NFO Period**: Till midnight January 23, 2026 (lumpsum only)
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: January 29, 2026 by 6:00 PM in ICCL Account
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with standard operational impact limited to BSE StAR MF platform participants. The launch expands investment options for mutual fund investors seeking gold ETF fund-of-funds exposure. Members need to follow prescribed timelines for fund collection and authentication to ensure smooth allotment process. No market-wide or regulatory impact expected beyond normal NFO operational procedures.
