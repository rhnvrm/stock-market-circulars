## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Zebion Infotech Limited, filed on March 07, 2026, on the SME platform. The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Zebion Infotech Limited on March 07, 2026
- Document is hosted on BSE SME website as per ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI)
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from March 09, 2026
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure requirement under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is available on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Market participants, investors, or any stakeholders may submit comments or objections on the draft document to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the stipulated period

## Important Dates

- Document Filing Date: March 07, 2026
- Notice Date: March 09, 2026
- Public Comment Deadline: March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine procedural disclosure for the upcoming SME IPO of Zebion Infotech Limited. Relevant primarily to potential investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the SME IPO segment. No changes to trading rules, compliance frameworks, or market operations.
