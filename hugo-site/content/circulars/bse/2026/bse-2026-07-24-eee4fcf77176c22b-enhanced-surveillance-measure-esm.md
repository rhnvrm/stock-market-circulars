## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. The circular identifies securities newly qualifying for ESM inclusion, securities moving between ESM stages, and securities exiting the framework, with detailed lists provided in Annexures I, II, and III attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under ESM and will attract a minimum 100% margin.
- The 100% margin applies to all open positions as on July 27, 2026, and to new positions created from July 28, 2026 onward.
- Some securities already under ESM will move to a lower stage (Stage I) effective July 27, 2026, per Annexure I.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Securities newly qualifying under ESM will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 28, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, effective July 27, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework effective July 27, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands will revert to pre-ESM levels unless another surveillance measure applies.
- ESM operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ESM is a market surveillance action and not to be construed as adverse to the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is a periodic update applying the existing ESM framework (as established via BSE notices dated June 02, 2023, July 18, 2023, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, October 04, 2024, and July 25, 2025) to a revised set of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on open and new positions in securities newly added to ESM.
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded only in the Trade-for-Trade segment where applicable, with the specified price bands and auction mechanisms.
- Members should refer to Annexures I, II, and III for the specific list of affected securities and their applicable stage/status.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 27, 2026: Stage movement to Stage I for continuing ESM securities; exit of securities per Annexure II; Stage II securities begin Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction.
- July 28, 2026: 100% margin applies to open positions as of July 27, 2026, and new positions; newly qualifying securities shift to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has high impact on trading in the affected securities, as it imposes stricter margin requirements (100%), restricts trading to Trade-for-Trade (eliminating intraday trading), and applies tighter price bands for certain stages. Investors and traders holding or planning to trade in newly listed ESM securities face significantly higher capital requirements and reduced liquidity. Since the specific security names are contained only in the referenced annexures and not in the notice text itself, the precise market impact cannot be quantified from this content alone, but the framework itself is a standard, periodically-applied surveillance tool aimed at curbing excessive speculation in specific scrips rather than a broad market event.
