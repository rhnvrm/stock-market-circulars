## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 36 equity scrips effective June 11, 2026, as part of its ongoing surveillance function. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, indicating varying levels of surveillance concern for each scrip. This notice was issued by the Department of Surveillance (DOSS) under Notice No. 20260610-28.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 36 scrips across the equity segment effective June 11, 2026.
- Revised bands range from a low of 2% (most restrictive) to a high of 20% (least restrictive in this list).
- Two scrips — Suraj Industries Ltd (523411 / 890226) and Vertex Securities Ltd (531950 / 890234) — appear under dual BSE codes, covering both equity and debt/other series.
- Members may seek clarifications at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands for the listed scrips have been revised from their existing levels by BSE's Surveillance department. This is a discretionary measure that restricts daily price movement to the specified percentage above or below the previous close.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must note the revised price bands and ensure that orders placed in these scrips from June 11, 2026 onward comply with the new limits. No further action is required from members beyond awareness and system updates.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands is a standard surveillance tool used by BSE to moderate volatility in specific scrips. A tighter band (2%) signals heightened surveillance concern and restricts intraday price movement significantly, potentially reducing liquidity. A wider band (10%–20%) indicates less restriction but still marks the scrip for monitoring. Traders and investors holding positions in any of the 36 affected scrips should be aware that price movement will be capped at the revised band levels starting June 11, 2026. This is a medium-impact routine measure with no systemic implications.
