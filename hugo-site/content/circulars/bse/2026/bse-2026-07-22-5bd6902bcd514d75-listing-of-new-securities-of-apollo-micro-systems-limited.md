## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 87,65,615 new equity shares of Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 540879) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 113.00 per share (issue price of Rs. 114.00 for shares of Rs. 1.00 face value each) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 8,765,615 equity shares of Rs. 1.00 each issued at a premium of Rs. 113.00 per share.
- Shares allotted to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers: 360690803 to 369456417.
- Date of allotment: 17/06/2026.
- Issue price: Rs. 114.00 per share.
- ISIN: INE713T01028.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 23-01-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares arising from preferential allotment/warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new securities as listed and tradeable effective July 23, 2026, and observe the lock-in restriction on the allotted shares (87,65,615 shares) until 23-01-2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: 17 June 2026
- Listing/trading effective date: 23 July 2026 (Thursday)
- Lock-in expiry: 23 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Apollo Micro Systems' equity share capital by 87,65,615 shares, which may have a modest dilutive effect on existing shareholders. Since the shares are locked in until January 2027, there is no immediate impact on free float or trading liquidity from these specific shares. The move reflects completion of a preferential warrant conversion, generally viewed as a routine corporate action with limited near-term market impact.
