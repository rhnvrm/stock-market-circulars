## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Seemax Resources Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange from Tuesday, July 7, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. As per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade under the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Seemax Resources Limited, Registered Office at 403, Mayfair Corporate Park, Behind DPS School, Kalali, Vadodara, Gujarat, India - 390012
- Listing effective date: July 7, 2026
- Group: 'MT' Group of Securities (SME segment)
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable for 10 trading days
- Number of securities: 4,400,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 4,400,000
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: SEEMAX
- Scrip Code: 544813
- ISIN: INE1EVO01017
- Market Lot: 1000 shares
- Issue Price: Rs. 141/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 131/-)
- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 3, 2026
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form, with minimum market lot of 1000 shares
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 7, 2026
- Market Maker: Sunflower Broking Private Limited, Rajkot, Gujarat
- Registrar to the issue: Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements existing SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (trade-for-trade for newly listed scrips) and the February 16, 2000 SEBI guideline mandating dematerialised trading for newly listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in SEEMAX are executed only in dematerialised form and in the prescribed minimum market lot of 1000 equity shares.
- The company has completed the necessary corporate action to mark the lock-in period on demat shares as per Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern details are available in Annexure II.
- Members with queries may contact the company's Compliance Officer or the Exchange.

## Important Dates

- July 3, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 7, 2026: Listing and commencement of trading; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) held
- 10 trading days from July 7, 2026: Trade-for-Trade segment applicability

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with impact confined mainly to investors and market participants involved in Seemax Resources Limited shares. The trade-for-trade restriction for the initial 10 days is a standard investor-protection measure for newly listed SME scrips and does not indicate any adverse company-specific issue. Broader market impact is minimal given the small issue size (44 lakh shares) and SME segment classification.
