## Summary

Liotech Industries Limited will be listed on the BSE SME segment effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities. The scrip will be subject to Trade-for-Trade (T2T) settlement for the first 10 trading days as mandated by SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012. The issue price was Rs. 321 per share.

## Key Points

- Listing effective date: June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Scrip Code: 544796 | Scrip ID: LIOTECH | ISIN: INE0TKX01011
- Group: MT Group of Securities (SME segment)
- Total securities: 39,00,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 39,00,400
- Issue price: Rs. 321/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 311/-)
- Market lot: 400 equity shares
- Date of allotment in public issue: June 22, 2026
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 24, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Pari passu with existing shares
- Financial year end: March 31

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is governed by existing SEBI guidelines including the T2T requirement under SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 and dematerialisation mandate per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in demat form
- Minimum market lot of 400 equity shares must be maintained; any modification requires at least one month's prior market notice
- Trading members should note SPOS participation guidelines as per Exchange notice 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I; Shareholding pattern as per Annexure II
- Market Maker: Aikyam Capital Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000321232)
- Registrar: KFIN Technologies Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221)

## Important Dates

- June 22, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- June 23, 2026: Notice date
- June 24, 2026: Effective listing date and SPOS session
- 10 trading days from June 24, 2026: Trade-for-Trade restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with limited broader market impact. The scrip is restricted to Trade-for-Trade settlement for 10 trading days, meaning no speculative intraday positions can be carried. Investors who received allotments must ensure shares are in demat form prior to trading. The minimum lot size of 400 shares at Rs. 321 per share implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,28,400, limiting retail participation to allottees and HNIs. Market maker Aikyam Capital Private Limited will provide liquidity support as required under SME listing norms.
