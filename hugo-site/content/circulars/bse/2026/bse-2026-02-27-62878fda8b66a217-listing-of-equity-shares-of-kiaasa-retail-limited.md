## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260227-3) informing trading members that the equity shares of Kiaasa Retail Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Kiaasa Retail Limited
- Scrip Code: 544711
- Symbol: KIAASA
- ISIN: INE1C3F01018
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Category: Compliance
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the forthcoming listing of Kiaasa Retail Limited. Members should await the separate notice from BSE that will provide the exact listing date and detailed security information before facilitating trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate, localised impact relevant to trading members active in the BSE SME segment. Kiaasa Retail Limited's shares will soon be available for trading, but no immediate action is required until the listing date notice is published. Investors and traders interested in SME IPOs should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and complete security details.
