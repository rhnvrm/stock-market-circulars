## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260519-30 informing members that the Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from May 20, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260426-2 dated April 26, 2026, which had previously addressed the fund's status.

## Key Points

- Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 20, 2026
- Transactions enabled include subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility
- Six scheme variants are covered across Regular and Direct plans
- All six schemes are classified as Equity type funds
- This notice follows BSE Notice No. 20260426-2 dated April 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction facility for an existing mutual fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Members and distributors on the BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of the fund for processing investor transactions from May 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2026**: Notice date
- **May 20, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **April 26, 2026**: Date of prior related Notice No. 20260426-2

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact for investors and distributors transacting in Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund through BSE StAR MF. The six scheme variants affected are:

| Sr. No. | Scheme Name | Type | ISIN |
|---------|-------------|------|------|
| 1 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - Growth | Equity | INF277K30070 |
| 2 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | Equity | INF277K30088 |
| 3 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout | Equity | INF277K30096 |
| 4 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - Growth | Equity | INF277K30104 |
| 5 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout | Equity | INF277K30120 |
| 6 | Titanium Equity Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | Equity | INF277K30112 |

Investors previously unable to transact in this fund during its closure period will regain access to all transaction types from May 20, 2026. No broader market impact anticipated.
