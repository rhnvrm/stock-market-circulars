## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 644 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-644/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers for the purchase of securities that were short-delivered in the settlement. The auction offer entry session is scheduled for June 05, 2026, with pay-in on June 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction is for Rolling Settlement No. DR-644/2026-27
- Auction offer entry open on Friday, June 05, 2026 from 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching will take place from 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports available from 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports available from 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of auction securities scheduled for Monday, June 08, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate shown in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should refer to notices issued for Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) before submitting offers
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities code and rate as no corrections will be allowed

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: Auction offer entry session (02:00 p.m. – 02:45 p.m.)
- **June 05, 2026**: Auction matching (02:45 p.m. – 03:00 p.m.)
- **June 05, 2026**: Delivery statement and accepted offers report (03:00 p.m. – 03:30 p.m.)
- **June 05, 2026**: Money statement and final reports (03:35 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- **June 08, 2026**: Auction pay-in of securities at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-644/2026-27. It primarily affects trading members who have delivery shortfalls and those wishing to participate in the auction by offering securities for purchase. The removal of restrictions on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short deliveries (under the Uniform Membership structure) may increase liquidity and participation in auction sessions. Members must ensure timely securities pay-in on June 08, 2026 to avoid penalties.
