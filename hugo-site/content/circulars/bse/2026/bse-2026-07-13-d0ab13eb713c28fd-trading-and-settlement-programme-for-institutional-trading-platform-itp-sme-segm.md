## Summary

BSE has issued the standard monthly trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME segment, covering the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. This is a routine administrative notice under the Settlement/RMS category issued by the BSE Clearing (BSECL) department.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains exclusively to the ITP - SME segment of BSE
- Covers the settlement schedule for the entire month of August 2026
- Issued by BSE Clearing (BSECL) department under the Settlement/RMS category
- Detailed schedule is provided in the attached document (ITP082026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a periodic operational notice consistent with BSE's regular practice of publishing monthly settlement calendars for each trading segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and clearing members dealing in the ITP-SME segment should refer to the attached settlement calendar to ensure timely pay-in and pay-out of funds and securities in accordance with the prescribed schedule.

## Important Dates

Schedule effective from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. Specific settlement dates are detailed in the attachment (ITP082026), not reproduced in the notice text.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is a routine operational notification affecting only settlement scheduling for the ITP-SME segment. It does not affect trading activity, pricing, or any specific listed securities, and is primarily relevant to back-office and compliance teams at trading/clearing member firms.
