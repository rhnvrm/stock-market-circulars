## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of two newly listed SME stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index. VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544778) and UHM VACATION LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544779) were listed on BSE on June 11, 2026, and will be included in the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- VAHH CHEMICALS LIMITED (ticker: 544778) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 12, 2026
- UHM VACATION LIMITED (ticker: 544779) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 12, 2026
- Both stocks were listed on BSE effective June 11, 2026
- The corporate actions are included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 11, 2026
- References prior notices: No. 20260610-34 (VAHH Chemicals) and No. 20260610-44 (UHM Vacation)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index reconstitution notice reflecting the addition of newly listed SME IPO stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

Index-tracking funds, ETFs, and portfolio managers benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index should account for these additions effective at the open of June 12, 2026. Clients may direct queries to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Both stocks (544778 and 544779) listed on BSE
- **June 12, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative action reflecting the graduation of two newly listed SME IPO stocks into the BSE SME IPO Index. Impact is confined to index-tracking products and participants referencing the BSE SME IPO Index.
