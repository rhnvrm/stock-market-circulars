## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued Notice No. 20260723-10 dated July 23, 2026, prescribing the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of Rikhav Securities Limited. The acquisition window will run from July 24, 2026 to August 6, 2026, under Settlement No. 2026091.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No.: 2026091
- Bid Start Date: 24/07/2026; Bid End Date: 06/08/2026
- Obligation Download Date: 12/08/2026
- Settlement Date: 13/08/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026091 must be quoted by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- Members/Custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for revised guidelines on the mechanism for acquisition of shares through the stock exchange
- Early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number; no EPN file upload is required
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes existing takeover/acquisition window settlement guidelines (per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020) for this specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed bid confirmation timelines on the OTB platform
- Members and Custodians must complete early pay-in in the depository system well in advance of the bidding window closure and confirmation window deadlines
- Members/Custodians must use the correct Market Type and Settlement Number when processing early pay-in in the depository system
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians for early pay-in

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: July 24, 2026
- Bid End Date: August 6, 2026
- Obligation Download: August 12, 2026
- Settlement Date: August 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice is procedural, providing operational clarity to Members, Custodians, and Depository Participants involved in the Rikhav Securities Limited takeover offer. It does not affect broader market trading but is important for participants tendering shares to ensure timely early pay-in and settlement compliance, avoiding rejection of bids or settlement delays.
