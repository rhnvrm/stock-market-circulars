## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under SEBI Master Circular (HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, last updated January 30, 2026) announcing the suspension of trading in securities of **Murae Organisor Ltd** (Scrip Code: 542724) effective **May 29, 2026**, due to non-compliance with Regulation 27(2) of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for two consecutive quarters — September 2025 and December 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading in Murae Organisor Ltd (Scrip 542724) will be suspended from **May 29, 2026** for failure to comply with Regulation 27(2) of SEBI LODR Regulations.
- Non-compliance spans two consecutive quarters: **September 2025** and **December 2025**.
- The entire shareholding of promoters in the non-compliant entity, as well as all other securities in the promoter's demat account(s), will be **frozen** during the suspension period.
- Suspension can be avoided if the company achieves full compliance to the Exchange's satisfaction on or before **May 25, 2026**.
- After 15 days of suspension, trading will be allowed only on a **Trade for Trade basis in Z group**, on the first trading day of every week, for six months.
- Revocation of suspension requires the company to comply with all prescribed procedures and extant norms.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This action is pursuant to the existing SEBI Master Circular on penal actions for non-compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations and the Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading of specified securities of listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Murae Organisor Ltd** must comply with all provisions of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015 — specifically Regulation 27(2) — to the satisfaction of BSE on or before **May 25, 2026** to avoid suspension.
- The company must also pay any applicable fines for the suspension to be revoked after it takes effect.
- Promoters of the non-compliant entity must note that their entire demat holdings will remain frozen during any suspension period.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| September 2025 & December 2025 | Quarters for which Regulation 27(2) non-compliance was recorded |
| May 25, 2026 | Deadline for company to comply and avoid suspension |
| May 29, 2026 | Effective date of trading suspension if non-compliance persists |
| 15 days post-suspension | Trade for Trade (Z group) weekly trading begins |
| 6 months post-suspension | Period during which weekly Z-group trading is permitted |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **high impact** on investors and market participants holding securities of Murae Organisor Ltd (Scrip 542724). A trading suspension will severely restrict liquidity in the stock, and promoter shareholding will be frozen, limiting their ability to transact. If the company fails to remedy the non-compliance by May 25, 2026, shareholders will face illiquidity from May 29, 2026 onward. Post-suspension, the shift to Trade for Trade settlement in Z group further signals regulatory distress and typically results in significant price discovery challenges. Market participants should monitor BSE announcements for any compliance update from Murae Organisor Ltd before the deadline.
