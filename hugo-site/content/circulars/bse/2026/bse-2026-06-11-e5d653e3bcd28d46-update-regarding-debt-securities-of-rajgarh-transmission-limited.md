## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260611-25) in continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260424-14 dated April 24, 2026, regarding the debt securities of Rajgarh Transmission Limited. The key change involves a revision in the mode of security from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs, accompanied by an ISIN change, effective from June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976760 of Rajgarh Transmission Limited will trade under revised terms from June 12, 2026.
- The mode of security is revised from **Unsecured NCDs** to **Secured NCDs**.
- The ISIN is revised from **INE0N1Z08013** to **INE0N1Z07015**.
- This notice is a continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260424-14 dated April 24, 2026.
- For queries, trading members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade at +91 22 2272 8597 or debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

The terms of the listed debt securities are being revised as follows:

| Particulars | Existing Terms | Revised Terms |
|---|---|---|
| Mode of Security | Unsecured NCDs | Secured NCDs |
| ISIN | INE0N1Z08013 | INE0N1Z07015 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised ISIN (INE0N1Z07015) and security mode (Secured NCDs) for Scrip Code 976760 before placing orders from June 12, 2026.
- Members should update their internal systems to reflect the new ISIN to avoid settlement or identification errors.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026** – Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 12, 2026 (Friday)** – Revised terms become effective; securities available for trading under new ISIN and security mode.
- **April 24, 2026** – Original notice (20260424-14) issued.

## Impact Assessment

The change from Unsecured to Secured NCDs improves the credit quality and risk profile of the instrument for investors, as secured creditors have priority claim on assets in the event of default. The concurrent ISIN change requires trading members and depository participants to update records. Existing holders should verify their demat account reflects the correct ISIN. The impact is moderate and operational in nature, confined to holders and traders of this specific debt instrument.
