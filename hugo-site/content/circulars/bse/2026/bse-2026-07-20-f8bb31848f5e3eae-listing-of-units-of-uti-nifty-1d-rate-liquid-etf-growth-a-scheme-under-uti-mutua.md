## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that units of UTI Nifty 1D Rate Liquid ETF – Growth, an open-ended Exchange Traded Fund tracking the NIFTY 1D Rate Index, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the 'F' Group with effect from Tuesday, July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: Units of UTI NIFTY 1D RATE LIQUID ETF - GROWTH
- Number of units: 305,012
- Scrip Code: 544828
- Group: F
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Face Value: Rs. 1000.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1000.00 per unit
- Date of Allotment: 16/07/2026
- Scrip ID on BOLT System: LIQUIDBETA
- ISIN: INF789F1AG68
- Fund House: UTI Mutual Fund; Investment Manager: UTI Asset Management Company Limited
- Market Makers: East India Securities Ltd, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, Parwati Capital Market Private Limited, IRAGE Broking Services LLP, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational listing notification, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- As per SEBI guidelines dated 16th February 2000, units of the Mutual Fund will only be traded in dematerialized form.
- Trades in this scrip will be executed in unit market lot (i.e., one unit).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 16 July 2026
- Listing effective from: Tuesday, 21 July 2026
- Notice issued: 20 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with low market impact. It enables trading members and market participants to trade the new ETF units on the Exchange from the effective date, with designated market makers ensuring liquidity. No impact expected on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
