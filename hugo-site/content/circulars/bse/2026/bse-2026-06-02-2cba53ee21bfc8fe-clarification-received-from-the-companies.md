## Summary

BSE's Surveillance (DOSS) department observed significant price and/or volume movements in seven listed equity scrips. The Exchange sought clarifications from the respective companies to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. Replies have been received from three companies, while four others have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed unusual price/volume activity in seven equity scrips and initiated clarification requests.
- Infobeans Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 543644), NIIT Ltd (Scrip Code: 500304), and Spice Lounge Food Works Ltd (Scrip Code: 539895) have submitted their replies.
- Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Nexxus Petro Industries Ltd (544265), Colinz Laboratories Ltd (531210), and York Exports Ltd (530675) have not yet responded.
- Replies received are attached as annexures to the notice for investor reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is part of BSE's ongoing surveillance mechanism under which companies are queried about abnormal market activity in their securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four companies yet to respond — Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, Colinz Laboratories, and York Exports — are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE's Surveillance department promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies from the three responding companies before taking trading decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 02 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; response is deemed overdue given the notice has been issued.

## Impact Assessment

The notice signals active surveillance scrutiny on all seven scrips. For the three companies that have replied, the attached clarifications may help reduce information asymmetry for investors. The four non-responding companies remain under active surveillance watch, and continued non-response could lead to further regulatory action such as trade-for-trade classification or additional surveillance measures. Investors holding or considering positions in these scrips should exercise caution and review the BSE notice and attachments.
