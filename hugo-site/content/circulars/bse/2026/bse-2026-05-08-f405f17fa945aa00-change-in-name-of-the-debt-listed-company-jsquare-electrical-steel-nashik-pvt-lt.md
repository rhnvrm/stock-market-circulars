## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that JSQUARE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK Pvt Ltd (Scrip Code: 976360), a debt-listed company, has formally changed its name to JSW JFE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK PRIVATE LIMITED. The change is effective May 14, 2026, and will be reflected in the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System.

## Key Points

- Company scrip code 976360 is changing its registered name from JSQUARE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK Pvt Ltd to JSW JFE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK PRIVATE LIMITED
- The Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System is 945J2ESN28 and will be updated accordingly
- The name change is supported by a Certificate of Incorporation (COI) pursuant to change of name dated April 16, 2026
- The notice was issued by the Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name of the company and its listed debt securities on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new legal name. No structural or ownership changes are indicated; this is solely a name change pursuant to the Companies Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the new company name: JSW JFE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK PRIVATE LIMITED
- All references to JSQUARE ELECTRICAL STEEL NASHIK Pvt Ltd in debt market dealings should be updated to the new name effective May 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **May 8, 2026**: Notice date issued by BSE
- **April 16, 2026**: Date of Certificate of Incorporation for name change
- **May 14, 2026**: Effective date for the name change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and operational in nature. This is a routine name change for a privately held company listed on the debt segment. Trading members and counterparties holding or dealing in this company's debt securities (Scrip Code: 976360, BOLT Plus ID: 945J2ESN28) should update their internal records. No impact on trading activity, pricing, or settlement is expected.
