## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has published the trading and settlement programme for the Cash (Equity) segment for June 2026. The circular (Notice No. 20260509-1, dated 09 May 2026) outlines the schedule under both T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles for the period 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026.

## Key Points

- The trading and settlement schedule covers the entire month of June 2026 (01.06.2026 to 30.06.2026).
- Applicable to the Equity (Cash) segment on BSE.
- Settlement is governed under both T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles.
- Issued by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), the clearing and settlement arm of BSE.
- Reference attachment: EQ062026 (detailed settlement calendar).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic circular publishing the settlement calendar for the upcoming month in line with existing SEBI-mandated T+1 and T+0 settlement frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members, clearing members, and custodians operating in the BSE Cash (Equity) segment must adhere to the settlement dates specified in the EQ062026 attachment.
- Members should plan fund and securities obligations in line with the published T+1 and T+0 settlement schedule for June 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 09 May 2026
- **Programme Period:** 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026
- Specific trading days, settlement days, and holidays are detailed in attachment EQ062026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with medium impact limited to clearing and settlement planning. Brokers, clearing members, and custodians must update their internal settlement calendars for June 2026. No market disruption is expected; the circular ensures orderly settlement continuity under the established T+1 and T+0 frameworks.
