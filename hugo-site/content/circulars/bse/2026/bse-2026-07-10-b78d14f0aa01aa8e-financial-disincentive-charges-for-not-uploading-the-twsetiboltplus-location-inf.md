## Summary

BSE has issued its monthly notice regarding financial disincentive (charges) imposed on Trading Members for failing to upload TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminal location information for June 2026. Orders punched from terminal locations that are unregistered or incorrectly registered attract a penalty of ₹1,000 per instance, as per Exchange Notice no. 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026. The disincentive file for June 2026 (LCS0626.xxxx) has been made available to members via Extranet, and the charges will be debited to the General Charges Account after 60 days from the date of this notice.

## Key Points

- Applies to Trading Members whose TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminals are not registered, or incorrectly registered, with the Exchange.
- Financial disincentive of ₹1,000 per instance is levied for each order punched from a non-compliant terminal location.
- The relevant data file (LCS0626.xxxx) for June 2026 has been placed in the Equity Transaction Folder on Extranet.
- Members seeking a waiver or review of the disincentive must follow the process outlined in Exchange Notice No. 20251008-67 dated October 8, 2025.
- Waiver/review requests must be emailed to Waiver-Review@bseindia.com with a specified subject line format including TM Name, Clearing No., and "LOCATION MISMATCH-JUNE-2026".

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this notice implements the penalty framework established under earlier BSE notices (20070531-8, 20070828-28, 20131204-9, 20131213-26, 20180925-4, 20250924-54, 20251223-22) and the applicable penalty structure under Notice no. 20251010-20 and 20260417-45.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must ensure all TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminal locations are registered with the Exchange before punching orders from those locations. Members disputing the charges must submit a waiver/review request via email within the prescribed timeline, including applicable processing fees as detailed in Notice No. 20251008-67.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 10, 2026
- Applicable Period: June 2026
- Debit to General Charges Account: 60 days from the date of this notice (on or about September 8, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring monthly compliance enforcement notice with low market impact. It affects only Trading Members with non-compliant terminal registrations and imposes a modest financial penalty (₹1,000 per instance). It does not affect listed companies, stock prices, or broader market operations.
