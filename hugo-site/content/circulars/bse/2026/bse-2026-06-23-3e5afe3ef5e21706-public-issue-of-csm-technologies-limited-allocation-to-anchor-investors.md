## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the Public Issue of CSM Technologies Limited, a total of 17,70,120 equity shares have been subscribed on June 23, 2026, at a price of Rs. 113 per equity share.

## Key Points

- 17,70,120 equity shares allotted to Anchor Investors in CSM Technologies Limited's public issue
- Anchor Investor price fixed at Rs. 113 per equity share
- Announcement issued by the company on June 23, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE Listing Business Relationship Department
- Relevant company letter (Company_Letter_Anchor) enclosed as attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational notice regarding the completion of anchor investor allocation as part of a standard IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the anchor investor allocation details for CSM Technologies Limited's public issue. No specific compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allotment Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation at Rs. 113 per share establishes a price signal for the CSM Technologies Limited IPO. Anchor investor participation at this price level may influence retail and institutional investor sentiment during the public offer period. The allocation of 17,70,120 shares indicates meaningful anchor investor interest in the issue. This notice is informational in nature and has no direct operational impact on secondary market trading.
