## Summary

BSE Limited (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities through Demat Auction 646, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-646/2026-27. The auction offer entry session is scheduled for June 9, 2026 with securities pay-in due on June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM – 2:45 PM on June 9, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM – 3:00 PM on June 9, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports available: 3:00 PM – 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM – 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction) due: June 10, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member
- The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements in which they had short deliveries has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The notice reiterates the existing Uniform Membership structure under which auction settlements flow through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member, and confirms the removal of the restriction on offering securities in auctions where a member has short delivered.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the specified session window
- Members and Custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings on June 10, 2026
- Trading members must exercise caution when entering security codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted after entry
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate shown on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Members should also check notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by relevant departments

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Auction matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Reports available (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 10, 2026**: Securities pay-in for Auction at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant only to trading members who have short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-646/2026-27 and those willing to offer securities in the auction. No stocks are specifically identified in the notice; the list of securities subject to auction is attached separately (off646 and ARR 646 attachments). Standard auction rules apply and no systemic changes are introduced.
