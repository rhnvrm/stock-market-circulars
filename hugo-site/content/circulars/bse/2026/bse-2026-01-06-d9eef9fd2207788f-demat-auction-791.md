## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities and quantities subject to demat auction for Settlement No. 791 dated January 6, 2026. The auction includes 88 securities from various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 233,250 shares. This is a standard market operation to complete settlement obligations where physical delivery was not completed.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 791
- Auction Date: January 6, 2026
- Total securities listed: 88 scrips
- Highest quantity: YES BANK LTD (532648) - 233,250 shares
- Other significant quantities: WELCURE DRUG (524661) - 50,000 shares, WELSPUN SPEC (500365) - 10,000 shares
- Mix of large-cap and small-cap securities included
- Covers diverse sectors including ports, technology, banking, steel, and consumer goods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine market operation notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with short deliveries in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Participants must ensure funds availability for auction settlement
- Auction process to follow standard BSE demat auction procedures
- Settlement obligations must be met as per exchange timelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 6, 2026
- Settlement No.: 791

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects brokers and investors with pending delivery obligations in 88 securities. Large quantities in YES BANK (233,250 shares), TOURISM FINA (18,255 shares), and WELCURE DRUG (50,000 shares) may see price volatility during auction.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers with short positions in listed securities must arrange for auction participation and fund allocation. The wide range of securities (from blue-chips like RELIANCE, ICICI BANK, HCL TECH to smaller companies) indicates settlement issues across market segments.

**Investor Impact:** Existing shareholders unaffected unless involved in the specific settlement. Auction prices may differ from market prices, potentially affecting cost basis for acquiring brokers.
