## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code - 544517) will be transferred to 'B' group for trading purposes. This reclassification will become effective from Friday, January 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Shankara Buildpro Limited
- Scrip Code: 544517
- New Trading Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Friday, January 23, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260109-9
- Notice Date: January 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited will be moved from their current trading group to the 'B' group. The 'B' group typically comprises companies with medium capitalization and trading volumes that meet BSE's specified criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants must:
- Take note of the group change for Shankara Buildpro Limited
- Update their trading systems and records accordingly
- Ensure proper classification of the scrip in their platforms before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 23, 2026**: Effective date for trading in 'B' group

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change in the trading classification of Shankara Buildpro Limited. The move to 'B' group may affect certain trading parameters such as price bands, margin requirements, or settlement cycles as per BSE's group-specific regulations. Market participants should update their systems to reflect this change before January 23, 2026.
