## Summary

BSE has informed its members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund has communicated a change in the scheme name for one of its funds on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The change is effective from May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Equity Arbitrage Fund has been renamed to ICICI Prudential Arbitrage Fund.
- The change applies specifically to the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- A notice cum addendum issued by ICICI Prudential AMC is attached to the circular.
- Communication is from the Trading Operations department under the Mutual Fund segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are involved. This is an administrative scheme name revision initiated by ICICI Prudential AMC and communicated to BSE members via a notice cum addendum.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their records and systems to reflect the revised scheme name: **ICICI Prudential Arbitrage Fund** (previously ICICI Prudential Equity Arbitrage Fund).

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and operational in nature. Investors' holdings, NAV, and fund structure remain unaffected. The name change may require updates in platform displays, order entry systems, and member back-office records on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
