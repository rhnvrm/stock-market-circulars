## Summary

BSE has issued a notification regarding corporate actions in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The circular announces dividend declarations for 17 securities with their respective record dates, ex-dates, and shut periods scheduled between January 29, 2026 and February 20, 2026. During the shut periods, these securities will not be available for lending and borrowing activities.

## Key Points

- 17 companies have declared dividends affecting their securities in the SLB segment
- Shut periods range from 1 to 3 days depending on the security
- Earliest ex-date is January 29, 2026 (Zensar Technologies)
- Latest ex-date is February 20, 2026 (IRCTC)
- All corporate actions are dividend-related
- Securities will be unavailable for lending/borrowing during specified shut periods

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine notification of corporate actions affecting SLB operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the shut periods when these securities cannot be lent or borrowed
- SLB transactions must be planned considering the foreclosure dates and shut periods
- Participants should ensure no lending/borrowing positions are initiated during the shut periods for affected securities

## Important Dates

**January 2026:**
- January 29, 2026: Ex-date for Zensar Technologies (Shut: Jan 28-29)
- January 30, 2026: Ex-date for CG Power, Godrej Consumer Products, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Kirloskar Pneumatic, Sona BLW, COFORGE (various shut periods Jan 29-31)
- January 31, 2026: Record date for Motilal Oswal Financial Services and COFORGE

**February 2026:**
- February 2, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for Bharat Petroleum, Siyaram Silk Mills, Share India Securities, Metro Brands (Shut: Feb 1-2)
- February 5, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for GAIL (Shut: Feb 4-5)
- February 9, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for Power Grid Corporation (Shut: Feb 6-9)
- February 10, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for Apollo Tyres (Shut: Feb 9-10)
- February 12, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for Repco Home Finance (Shut: Feb 11-12)
- February 13, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for MRF (Shut: Feb 12-13)
- February 20, 2026: Ex-date and Record date for IRCTC (Shut: Feb 19-20)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects liquidity in the SLB segment for 17 securities during their respective shut periods. Market participants engaged in securities lending and borrowing activities need to plan their positions accordingly.

**Operational Impact:** SLB participants must adjust their lending/borrowing strategies to account for the unavailability of these securities during shut periods. This may temporarily affect liquidity and borrowing costs for these specific securities.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding positions in these securities through SLB arrangements need to be aware of the restrictions during shut periods. Dividend eligibility will be determined based on the record dates specified.
