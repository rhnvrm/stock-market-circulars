## Summary

BSE has notified corporate actions in Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) securities effective 13-March-2026. Three securities are impacted: eClerx Services Limited (bonus issue with foreclosure), RailTel Corporation of India Limited (dividend), and Indian Railway Finance Corporation (dividend). Participants with open SLB positions in these securities must take note of the shut periods and foreclosure dates.

## Key Points

- eClerx Services Limited is undergoing a **Bonus Issue** with a foreclosure date of 13-Mar-2026 and a shut period from 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026
- RailTel Corporation of India Limited is paying a **Dividend** with record date 13-March-2026 and shut period from 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026
- Indian Railway Finance Corporation (IRFC) is paying a **Dividend** with record date 13-March-2026 and shut period from 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026
- All three securities share the same book closure/record date and ex-date of 13-March-2026
- Shut period for all three securities is 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an operational notification under existing SLB framework rules that require BSE to notify participants about upcoming corporate actions affecting securities available in the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants holding open positions in ECLERX, RAILTEL, or IRFC must ensure compliance with shut period restrictions from 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026
- For eClerx Services Limited, open SLB positions must be foreclosed by 13-Mar-2026 due to the bonus issue
- No new lending/borrowing transactions in these securities should be initiated during the shut period
- Borrowers must account for dividend entitlements for RailTel and IRFC positions

## Important Dates

| Security | Corporate Action | Record/Ex Date | Foreclosure Date | Shut Period |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| eClerx Services Limited | Bonus Issue | 13-Mar-2026 | 13-Mar-2026 | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |
| RailTel Corporation of India Limited | Dividend | 13-Mar-2026 | N/A | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |
| Indian Railway Finance Corporation | Dividend | 13-Mar-2026 | N/A | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |

## Impact Assessment

**eClerx Services Limited (ECLERX):** The bonus issue triggers a mandatory foreclosure of all open SLB positions by 13-Mar-2026. This is significant as bonus issues alter the share capital structure, making outstanding lending contracts non-viable without adjustment. Participants must close positions before the foreclosure date.

**RailTel Corporation of India Limited (RAILTEL) and Indian Railway Finance Corporation (IRFC):** Dividend actions affect SLB positions as borrowers become liable to compensate lenders for dividends received during the borrowing period. The shut period restricts new SLB transactions to ensure clean settlement around the record date.

Overall market impact is moderate-to-high for SLB participants specifically exposed to these three securities. Broader market impact is limited to these counters around the record date window.
