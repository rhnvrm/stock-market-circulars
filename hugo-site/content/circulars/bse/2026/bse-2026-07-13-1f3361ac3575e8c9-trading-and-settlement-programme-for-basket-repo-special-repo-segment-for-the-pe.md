## Summary

BSE has notified the trading and settlement programme for the Basket Repo and Special Repo segments for the period from 1 August 2026 to 31 August 2026. The notice, issued by BSECL under the Settlement/RMS category, provides the detailed schedule (attachment REPO082026) that market participants dealing in repo transactions should follow.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains exclusively to the Basket Repo and Special Repo segments on BSE.
- Covers the trading and settlement calendar for August 2026 (01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026).
- Issued by the BSECL (BSE) department under Settlement/RMS category.
- Detailed schedule is provided in the attachment REPO082026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic notification of the settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and clearing members active in the Basket Repo and Special Repo segments should align their trading and settlement operations with the published schedule for August 2026.

## Important Dates

Applicable period: 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with no material impact on stock prices or broader market sentiment. It primarily serves as an operational reference for repo market participants to plan settlement activities during August 2026.
