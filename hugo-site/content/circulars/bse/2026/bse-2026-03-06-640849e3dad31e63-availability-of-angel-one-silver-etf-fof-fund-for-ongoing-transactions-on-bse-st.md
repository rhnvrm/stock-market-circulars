## Summary

BSE has informed members that Angel One Silver ETF FOF Fund is reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from March 06, 2026. This follows the earlier notice No. 20260206-19.

## Key Points

- Angel One Silver ETF FOF Fund is available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 06, 2026.
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan (Growth option) are available.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are available.
- The scheme category is DEBT for both plans.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding fund availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE are informed to note the availability of the schemes for transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 06, 2026 — Reopening for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in Angel One Silver ETF FOF Fund via BSE StAR MF Platform. The two available schemes are:
