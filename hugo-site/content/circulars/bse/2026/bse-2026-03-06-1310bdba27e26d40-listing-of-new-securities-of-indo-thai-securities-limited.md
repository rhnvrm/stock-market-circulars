## Summary

BSE has listed 30,95,000 new equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676, ISIN: INE337M01021) for trading effective Monday, March 9, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 30,95,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at an issue price of Rs. 37.60/- (Rs. 1 face value + Rs. 36.60 premium)
- Issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 124923191 – 128018190
- Date of allotment: December 31, 2025
- ISIN: INE337M01021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from March 9, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specified tranches and must be observed until the respective lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 31, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** March 9, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates:**
  - 5,95,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 124923191–125518190): Lock-in until September 15, 2026
  - 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 125518191–126018190): Lock-in until September 15, 2027
  - 20,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 126018191–128018190): Lock-in until September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 30,95,000 shares to the tradeable float of Indo Thai Securities Limited, though a majority (25,95,000 shares) remain under lock-in until at least September 2026. Only 5,00,000 shares are locked in until September 2027. The immediate freely tradeable impact is limited given the lock-in constraints. This is a routine corporate action with minimal broader market significance.
