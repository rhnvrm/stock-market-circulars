## Summary

BSE has informed members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260612-28 dated June 12, 2026, the INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND (managed by Kotak Mahindra AMC) will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- The scheme covers six plan/option combinations, all classified under DEBT scheme type:
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174K30046)
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174K30053)
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174K30061)
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF174K30012)
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174K30020)
  - INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174K30038)
- As communicated by Kotak AMC, Redemption, Switch-out, SWP and STP-out transactions under this SIF (Specialised Investment Fund) scheme will be accepted only twice a week: every Monday and every Wednesday.
- If Monday or Wednesday falls on a non-business day, the AMC will process redemption, switch-out, SWP and STP-out requests on the next working/business day.
- Redemption payouts will be released by the AMC/RTA as per the payout Turnaround Time (TAT) specified in the Scheme Master.
- Notice signed by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update implementing the scheme's reopening as previously announced via Notice No. 20260612-28 dated June 12, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and distributors handling transactions in this scheme must ensure that redemption, switch-out, SWP and STP-out requests are routed in accordance with the twice-weekly (Monday/Wednesday) processing window communicated by Kotak AMC, and must inform investors accordingly.

## Important Dates

- June 12, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260612-28 issued regarding the scheme.
- July 9, 2026: Scheme reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Ongoing: Redemption/switch-out/SWP/STP-out transactions accepted every Monday and Wednesday (or next business day if these fall on a holiday).

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and investors dealing in this specific Kotak SIF scheme via BSE StAR MF Platform. It does not affect listed equity trading or broader market operations, but distributors should update their systems/processes to reflect the restricted redemption window for this scheme.
