## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in 2,000 new equity shares of Jtekt India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 520057) with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. These shares arose from the conversion of partly paid-up shares to fully paid-up shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 2,000 equity shares of Jtekt India Ltd. converted from partly-paid to fully paid-up status.
- Face value of each share is Re. 1/-.
- Shares issued originally on a rights basis.
- ISIN: INE643A01035.
- Distinctive Numbers: 2,000 shares numbered 198660113 to 198662112.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing permission notice issued under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly fully-paid shares resulting from a prior rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the permission to trade the new securities; no specific action is required from trading members beyond updating trading systems to recognize the additional shares under the existing ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Trading: Monday, July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the very small quantity of shares (2,000) being listed relative to the company's likely total outstanding shares. This reflects routine administrative processing of a rights issue call-money conversion rather than a fresh capital raise, and is unlikely to have any material effect on share price, liquidity, or trading volumes for Jtekt India Ltd.
