## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 945 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174, ISIN: INE090A01021) that were previously kept in abeyance are now listed and permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 945 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each (face value) are being admitted to trading
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Effective trading date: Tuesday, May 26, 2026
- Two tranches of distinctive numbers assigned:
  - 45 shares: Distinctive Nos. 6412512764 to 6412512808
  - 900 shares: Distinctive Nos. 6412587077 to 6412587976

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational listing notice permitting previously withheld bonus shares to enter the market.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Tuesday, May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 945 shares represent a negligibly small quantity relative to ICICI Bank's total issued capital. This is a routine administrative action to release bonus shares that had been held in abeyance, likely pending resolution of entitlement disputes or pending documentation from the original shareholders.
