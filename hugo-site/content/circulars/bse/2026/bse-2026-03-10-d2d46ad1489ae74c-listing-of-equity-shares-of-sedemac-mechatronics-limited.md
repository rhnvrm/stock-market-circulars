## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544723
- Trading Symbol: SEDEMAC
- ISIN: INE00XB01019
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details and await the separate notice confirming the listing date and security specifics before initiating trades.
- Members must ensure compliance with all applicable BSE trading rules once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 10, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals an upcoming new equity listing on BSE for SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited, a company in the mechatronics sector. Market participants and investors interested in this segment should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. The listing will provide a new tradeable security on the BSE Equity segment.
