## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED (ISIN: INE982X07440, Code: 975946) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date of February 8, 2026 for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the SML-10.40%-23-2-26-PVT series.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- Record date set for February 8, 2026
- Debenture series: SML-10.40%-23-2-26-PVT (10.40% coupon rate)
- ISIN: INE982X07440
- BSE Code: 975946
- Circular Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealing in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- No transactions permitted in the affected NCD series until further notice
- Members must comply with the no-dealings restriction to facilitate redemption process

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular notification date
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects holders and potential traders of SATYA MICROCAPITAL LIMITED's 10.40% NCDs maturing in 2026. The trading halt is temporary and procedural, enabling the company to process redemption and final interest payment to debenture holders on record. Impact is limited to this specific debt security and does not affect the company's equity shares or other debt instruments. Investors holding these debentures will receive their principal redemption amount plus accrued interest as per the debenture terms.
