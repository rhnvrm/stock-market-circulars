## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260608-24 detailing the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for **BABA ARTS LTD** (Symbol: BABA, Scrip Code: 513343, ISIN: INE534A01028). The offer is open from **Tuesday, 9th June 2026 to Monday, 22nd June 2026** at an offer price of **Rs. 6.00 per share**, for up to **1,32,92,000 equity shares**.

## Key Points

- **Security:** BABA ARTS LTD | Symbol: BABA | ISIN: INE534A01028 | Scrip Code: 513343
- **Offer Size:** Up to 1,32,92,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 6.00 per share | Face Value: Rs. 10.00 | Market Lot: 1
- **Bid Offering Period:** 9th June 2026 to 22nd June 2026, 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Give-up / Take-up Period:** 9th June 2026 to 19th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Last Day Give-up (22nd June 2026):** 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Last Day Take-up (22nd June 2026):** 9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs (OTH), Other NIB (NOH), Company (CO), and Individual (IND)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice follows existing OTB operational guidelines as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the schedule and participate via the iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Members can bid only for investors holding shares in **demat form**
- End-of-Issue files on Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted
- RTRMS accessible at https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- Extranet for files accessible at https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a **medium, stock-specific impact** limited to BABA ARTS LTD (BABA) and the trading/custodian members participating in the OTB acquisition window. The offer price of Rs. 6.00 per share against a face value of Rs. 10.00 reflects a below-par offer. The offer size of up to 1,32,92,000 shares represents a significant acquisition stake. No broad market or regulatory impact is expected; operational impact is confined to members setting up bids on the iBBS platform within the specified schedule.
