## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Wiscon Industries Limited, filed on July 23, 2026, on the exchange's SME website. The document, containing disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, is open for public comments for a period of at least 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document of Wiscon Industries Limited filed on July 23, 2026
- Document contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Hosted on the BSE SME IPO website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of the notice
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine disclosure notice under existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public may review the draft offer document at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments on the draft offer document must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from the notice date

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: July 23, 2026
- Notice date: July 24, 2026
- Public comment period: minimum 21 days from date of notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural disclosure related to the pre-listing process for Wiscon Industries Limited's SME IPO. It has no direct market or trading impact on currently listed securities, but is relevant for market participants tracking upcoming SME IPO listings and for stakeholders wishing to submit comments on the draft offer document.
