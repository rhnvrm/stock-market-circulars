## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED effective February 4, 2026. The suspension is implemented because the company has fixed the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealing date.

## Key Points

- Company: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE08XP07241 (Code: 975926)
- Debenture Series: ACAPL-12.5%-19-2-26-PVT
- Record Date: February 4, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 4, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension implemented in accordance with existing BSE regulations for corporate actions involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE08XP07241) from February 4, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All outstanding positions and settlements must be completed before the no-dealing date

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 4, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **February 4, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of the particular debenture series (ACAPL-12.5%-19-2-26-PVT). This is a scheduled corporate action with no broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment. Trading suspension ensures fair treatment of all investors by preventing transactions that would not settle before the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN from February 4, 2026. Standard operational procedure for scheduled debt redemptions.
