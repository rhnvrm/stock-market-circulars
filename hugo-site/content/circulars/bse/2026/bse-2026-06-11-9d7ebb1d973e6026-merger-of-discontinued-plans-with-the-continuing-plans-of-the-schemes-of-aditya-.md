## Summary

BSE has notified its members that Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund will merge certain discontinued scheme plans/options into their respective continuing plans on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The merger becomes effective after close of business hours on June 19, 2026. Investors unwilling to participate in the merger may exit without exit load within the specified window.

## Key Points

- Merger of discontinued Aditya Birla Sun Life MF plans into continuing plans, effective after close of business hours on June 19, 2026.
- Investors may redeem or switch to other ABSL MF schemes without exit load between May 21, 2026 and June 19, 2026 (both days inclusive).
- Fresh subscriptions (lumpsum, additional investment, Switch In, AMC STP In) and new SIP/XSIP registrations will not be accepted in the Transferor scheme after close of business hours on June 19, 2026.
- Existing systematic registrations (SIP/XSIP, AMC STP, SWP) in the Transferor scheme will automatically migrate to the respective plans/options of the Transferee scheme after close of business hours on June 19, 2026.
- Redemptions and switch-outs will continue to be allowed in the Transferor scheme up to the effective timeline permitted by the AMC.

## Regulatory Changes

The scheme restructuring is driven by Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC's Notice-cum-Addendum. Discontinued plans are being consolidated into continuing plans as part of SEBI-mandated scheme rationalization norms. Members are directed to refer to the attached Annexure and Notice-cum-Addendum for the complete list of Transferor and Transferee schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members must inform their clients/investors about the merger and the no-exit-load redemption window.
- Members must ensure no fresh subscription or new systematic registrations are processed for the Transferor schemes after June 19, 2026 close of business.
- Members should note that existing systematic registrations will be migrated automatically and should update their records accordingly.
- Refer to the attached Annexure for scheme-specific mapping between Transferor and Transferee plans.

## Important Dates

- **May 21, 2026**: Start of no-exit-load exit window for dissenting investors.
- **June 19, 2026**: Last date for no-exit-load redemption/switch; merger effective after close of business hours; fresh subscriptions and new SIP/XSIP registrations cease for Transferor schemes.
- **After June 19, 2026**: Existing systematic registrations (SIP/XSIP, AMC STP, SWP) migrate to Transferee scheme plans/options.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact on BSE StAR MF platform members and their clients invested in the affected Aditya Birla Sun Life MF discontinued plans. Investors must act before June 19, 2026 if they wish to exit without incurring an exit load. Systematic investment registrations will be automatically migrated, reducing manual intervention required, but members should validate that SIP/XSIP/SWP mappings align with investor intent post-migration. No broad market impact is anticipated.
