## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has sought clarification from Maruti Suzuki India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532500) in response to news articles appearing across various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The exchange initiated this inquiry to verify the accuracy of the reported news and to keep members and investors informed. As of the notice date, the company's reply is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260630-33 dated 30 June 2026 issued by the Surveillance Department
- Clarification sought from Maruti Suzuki India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532500) regarding news articles reported on Moneycontrol
- The exchange sought the clarification to verify accuracy of media reports and protect investor interest
- Reply from Maruti Suzuki India Ltd is still awaited as of the notice date
- Notice signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice. This is a standard surveillance action under BSE's monitoring framework for listed companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Maruti Suzuki India Ltd is required to submit a clarification to BSE regarding the news articles referenced in this notice. The company is expected to respond promptly to enable BSE to inform market participants about the accuracy of the reported news.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 30 June 2026
- Reply deadline: Not specified; reply is currently awaited

## Impact Assessment

The immediate market impact is low as this is a routine surveillance query with no punitive action taken. Maruti Suzuki India Ltd is a large-cap, high-visibility stock; any pending clarification may cause minor uncertainty until the company responds. Once the clarification is received, BSE is expected to disseminate the information to members and investors to ensure market transparency.
