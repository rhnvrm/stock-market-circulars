## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED effective January 21, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: ARKA FINCAP LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)
- ISIN: INE03W107165
- Scrip Code: 974525
- Series: AFL-5-2-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 21, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 21, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED from January 21, 2026
- Trading members must note the suspension and communicate to relevant clients holding or trading these securities
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Circular issue date
- **January 21, 2026**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **January 21, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact confined to holders and traders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED's specific debenture series (AFL-5-2-26-PVT). The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear in records on January 21, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and redemption. No trading activity possible from the record date, preventing any last-minute transfers.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN (INE03W107165) and ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction from January 21, 2026.
