## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited (Scrip Code: 543597, ISIN: INE0I0T01010) will be migrated from the BSE SME Platform to the BSE Mainboard Platform under the 'B' Group, effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Migration is effective from **July 1, 2026**; shares will be admitted to dealings on the Mainboard under the **'B' Group**
- Total securities: **31,833,079 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each**, fully paid-up (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 31,833,079)
- Market lot remains **1 share**; scrip abbreviated name and ID on BOLT System: **VOEPL**
- Trading will continue only in **dematerialized form** per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Audited financial results for Q4 and FY ended March 31, 2026 are enclosed as Annexure I
- Shareholding pattern as on March 31, 2026 is enclosed as Annexure II

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The migration follows SEBI's existing guidelines requiring demat-only trading. The shares move from SME segment listing to Mainboard listing under the standard 'B' Group classification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Mainboard segment classification for VOEPL from July 1, 2026
- All trades must be executed in unit market lot (1 share) in dematerialized form only
- Members requiring clarifications may contact the Company Secretary Mr. Prasad Zinjurde at +912532350461 / info@voepl.com, or BSE's Parag Jain / Hardik Bhuta at 022-22728685

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Notice date
- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date of migration to BSE Mainboard 'B' Group

## Impact Assessment

The migration elevates Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited from the SME segment to the Mainboard, potentially improving liquidity and broadening investor eligibility (institutional investors restricted from SME scrips can now participate). Trading members should update scrip classifications in their systems before market open on July 1, 2026. The Registrar and Transfer Agent (Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai) remains unchanged. No change to ISIN, face value, or total share capital.
