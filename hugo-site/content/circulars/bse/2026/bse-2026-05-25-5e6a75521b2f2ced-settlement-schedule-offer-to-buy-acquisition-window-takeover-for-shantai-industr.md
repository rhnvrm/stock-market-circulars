## Summary

BSE's clearing corporation ICCL has issued the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) through the Acquisition Window under the Takeover (open-offer) mechanism for Shantai Industries Limited. This notice (No. 20260525-14, dated 25 May 2026) provides members and participants with the key settlement timelines they must adhere to during the offer period.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) under the Settlement/RMS category.
- Pertains to the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) specifically for Shantai Industries Limited.
- Settlement schedule governs the pay-in and pay-out obligations of brokers and investors participating in the open offer.
- No attachment was included; schedule details are contained within the notice body.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operationalises existing SEBI Takeover Regulations by specifying the settlement timeline for the current offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and participants must follow the prescribed settlement schedule for all transactions executed through the Acquisition Window for Shantai Industries Limited.
- Brokers facilitating client bids in the open offer must ensure timely pay-in of securities and funds as per the dates specified by ICCL.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 25 May 2026
- Specific settlement dates (pay-in/pay-out) are detailed within the circular; participants should refer to the full notice for exact timelines.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with medium impact, affecting brokers, investors, and custodians participating in the Shantai Industries Limited takeover offer. Compliance with the settlement schedule is mandatory for all parties transacting through the BSE Acquisition Window. Non-adherence may result in settlement penalties from ICCL.
