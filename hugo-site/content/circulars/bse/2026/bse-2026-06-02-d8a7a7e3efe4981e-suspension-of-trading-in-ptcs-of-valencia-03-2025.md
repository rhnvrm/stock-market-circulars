## Summary

BSE has issued a notice directing trading members to suspend all dealing in Pass-through Certificates (PTCs) of Valencia 03 2025 with effect from June 03, 2026. The suspension follows a redemption notification from Vardhman Trusteeship Private Limited, the trustee for the securitisation trust.

## Key Points

- Vardhman Trusteeship Private Limited notified BSE of the redemption of PTCs issued under Valencia 03 2025
- Affected scrip: V032025-10.10%-17-08-26-PTC | Scrip Code: 976588 | ISIN: INE1TQQ15010
- Trading members are instructed not to deal in these PTCs effective June 03, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-643/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an operational notice under existing debt segment listing and operations guidelines, triggered by the trustee's redemption communication.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must cease trading in PTCs of Valencia 03 2025 (ISIN: INE1TQQ15010, Scrip Code: 976588) from June 03, 2026 onwards
- Members must take note of this notice and ensure no transactions are executed in the affected security post the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of Valencia 03 2025 PTCs. The suspension is a standard procedure following the trustee's redemption notification, indicating the underlying securitised assets have been redeemed. Investors holding these PTCs should expect the redemption proceeds to be processed through the normal settlement mechanism. No broader market impact is anticipated.
