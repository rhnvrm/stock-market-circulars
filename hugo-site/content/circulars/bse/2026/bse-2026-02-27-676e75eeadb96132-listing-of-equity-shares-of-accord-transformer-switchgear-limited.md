## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-4 informing trading members that the equity shares of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The exact date of commencement of trading and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited
- Scrip Code: 544710
- Trading Symbol: ACCORDTS
- ISIN: INE132201018
- Segment: BSE SME
- Actual listing date to be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the upcoming listing of ACCORDTS on the SME segment and await the subsequent notice for the specific listing date and security details before initiating any dealings.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate BSE notice

## Impact Assessment

Low broad market impact. This notice is relevant specifically to trading members and investors interested in the SME IPO of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited. No trading can commence until the follow-up notice confirming the listing date is issued.
