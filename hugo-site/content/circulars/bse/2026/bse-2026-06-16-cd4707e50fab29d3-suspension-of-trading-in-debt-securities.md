## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260616-5) informing trading members that debt securities of LOANX SILVERBELL (11.85% coupon, ISIN: INE1UUH15019, scrip code 976596) are maturing. Trading in these securities is suspended with effect from June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976596
- Security: LOANX SILVERBELL MAR 2025 – 11.85% – 18-6-
- ISIN: INE1UUH15019
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the debt security
- Reference: DR-653/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice regarding the maturity and consequent suspension of trading in a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are advised not to deal in the aforementioned debt securities of LOANX SILVERBELL from June 17, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 16, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: June 17, 2026 (maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine suspension triggered by the maturity of a single privately placed debt security (LOANX SILVERBELL). Only holders of ISIN INE1UUH15019 are directly affected. No broader market or systemic implications are anticipated.
