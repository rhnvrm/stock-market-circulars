## Summary

BSE has notified that GROWW SMALL CAP FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 02, 2026. This notice supersedes the previous notice No. 20260107-20 and covers six variants of the scheme across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options.

## Key Points

- GROWW SMALL CAP FUND reopening for all ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: February 02, 2026
- All facilities restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Reference to previous notice No. 20260107-20

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a specific mutual fund scheme on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice for members and investors regarding the availability of the mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 02, 2026 - GROWW SMALL CAP FUND reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This notice affects only investors interested in GROWW SMALL CAP FUND and does not have broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The reopening allows investors to resume normal transaction activities including fresh subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP/STP/SWP) for this specific mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors of GROWW SMALL CAP FUND who can now execute transactions that may have been restricted during the closure period.
