## Summary

BSE has announced that the BSE StAR MF platform will observe a non-business day for all Mutual Fund schemes on Sunday, February 1, 2026, in line with all Mutual Funds observing a non-transaction day on Union Budget Day. No mutual fund transactions will be processed on this date.

## Key Points

- All Mutual Funds are observing a non-business/non-transaction day on Union Budget Day
- BSE StAR MF platform will be closed for all mutual fund schemes on February 1, 2026
- The closure coincides with the presentation of the Union Budget on Sunday, February 1, 2026
- This applies to all mutual fund schemes across the platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding a scheduled non-business day.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members should inform their clients about the non-business day for mutual fund transactions
- No mutual fund purchase, redemption, or switch transactions will be processed on February 1, 2026
- Members should plan their operations and client communications accordingly

## Important Dates

- **February 1, 2026 (Sunday)**: Non-business day for BSE StAR MF platform and all Mutual Fund schemes due to Union Budget presentation

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Mutual fund investors will not be able to transact on February 1, 2026. This is a one-day operational closure that aligns with industry practice during Union Budget presentation.

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this is a scheduled non-business day with advance notice. Investors planning transactions should complete them before February 1 or schedule them for February 2, 2026 onwards.

**Investor Impact**: Investors should be aware that mutual fund NAV-based transactions will not be processed on this date, and they should plan their investment activities accordingly.
