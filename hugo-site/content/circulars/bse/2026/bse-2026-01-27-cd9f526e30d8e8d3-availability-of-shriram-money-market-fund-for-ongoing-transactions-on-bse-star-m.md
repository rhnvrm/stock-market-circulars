## Summary

BSE has announced that SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 28, 2026. This follows earlier notice No 20260116-58 regarding the fund.

## Key Points

- SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND reopening for transactions from January 28, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants available
- Full facilities include: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two scheme variants with separate ISINs

## Scheme Details

**SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: Debt
- ISIN: INF680P01489

**SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: Debt
- ISIN: INF680P01471

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 28, 2026 - Reopening for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting investors in SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND. The reopening provides liquidity options for existing and new investors. Market impact is minimal as this pertains to a single debt fund scheme. Investors seeking money market exposure through Shriram Mutual Fund can now transact through the BSE StAR MF platform.
