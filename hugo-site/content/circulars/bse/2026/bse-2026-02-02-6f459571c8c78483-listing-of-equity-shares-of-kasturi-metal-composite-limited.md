## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of KASTURI METAL COMPOSITE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544688, symbol KASTURI, and ISIN number INE0T1301023. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company name: Kasturi Metal Composite Limited
- Scrip code: 544688
- Trading symbol: KASTURI
- ISIN: INE0T1301023
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Kasturi Metal Composite Limited and await further notice regarding the listing date and detailed security specifications.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: February 2, 2026
- Listing date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. It affects only trading members who will trade this security once listed on the SME segment. The announcement does not affect existing market operations or other securities.
