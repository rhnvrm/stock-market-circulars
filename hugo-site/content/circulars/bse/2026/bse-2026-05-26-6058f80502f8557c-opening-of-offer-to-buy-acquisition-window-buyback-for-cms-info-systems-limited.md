## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of the opening of the Offer to Buy (Buyback) acquisition window for CMS Info Systems Limited. The company is repurchasing up to 49,39,126 equity shares at Rs. 340 per share through the Tender Offer route, representing 3% of its total outstanding paid-up equity share capital. The buyback window opens May 29, 2026 and closes June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** CMS Info Systems Limited
- **Buyback Quantity:** Up to 49,39,126 (Forty Nine Lakh Thirty Nine Thousand One Hundred Twenty Six) fully paid-up equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Buyback Price:** Rs. 340/- per equity share
- **Total Consideration:** Up to Rs. 167,93,02,840/- (approx. Rs. 167.93 Crore), excluding transaction costs
- **Buyback Route:** Tender Offer via BSE acquisition window
- **Buyback Percentage:** 3% of total outstanding equity shares; constitutes 7.56% (standalone) and 7.17% (consolidated) of aggregate paid-up capital and free reserves/securities premium as at March 31, 2026
- **Statutory Compliance:** Within the 10% limit under Section 68 of the Companies Act; board approval route
- **Record Date:** Friday, May 22, 2026
- **Small Shareholders:** Reservation applicable on a proportionate basis

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The offer is conducted under the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchange pursuant to Tender Offers:
- SEBI Circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI Circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE Notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62 (Revised Guidelines)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate eligible shareholders/beneficial owners (as on the record date of May 22, 2026) in tendering shares through the BSE acquisition window.
- Participation is on a proportionate basis with reservation for small shareholders.
- Market participants must follow the Revised Guidelines per SEBI Circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE Notices 20170210-16 and 20190424-35.
- The Letter of Offer (attached to the notice) must be reviewed by relevant participants.

## Important Dates

|---|---|

| Buyback Window Closes | Thursday, June 4, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to existing shareholders of CMS Info Systems Limited (BSE: CMSINFO) who are eligible to participate in the buyback based on the May 22, 2026 record date. The buyback at Rs. 340 per share provides an exit opportunity for shareholders. Trading members and custodians handling the tender process need to be operationally ready for the five-day window (May 29–June 4, 2026). The buyback size of ~Rs. 167.93 Crore is modest relative to the company's capital base and is within the 10% statutory board-approval limit, limiting broader market impact.
