## Summary

BSE has notified members that Edelweiss Mutual Fund will launch the New Fund Offer (NFO) of EDELWEISS NIFTY METAL ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 6, 2026, to July 20, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 27, 2026. The circular outlines the scheme details and operational instructions for members regarding fund pay-in, cheque/OTM payments, and switch transactions.

## Key Points

- Scheme: EDELWEISS NIFTY METAL ETF and EDELWEISS NIFTY METAL ETF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF754K01XG1)
- NFO period: July 6, 2026 to July 20, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 27, 2026
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026, for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of funds prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered
- NFO scheme extended till midnight July 20, 2026, only for Lumpsum purchases on the BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new fund offer listing on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure client funds are initiated and cleared within the specified timelines (9:00 AM T+1 for initiation; 6:00 PM July 23, 2026 for clear funds in ICCL account) to ensure valid allotment, and must correctly report cancelled transactions to RTA/AMC.

## Important Dates

- NFO Opens: July 6, 2026
- NFO Closes: July 20, 2026 (extended till midnight for Lumpsum purchases)
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 23, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice relevant primarily to BSE StAR MF platform members and distributors handling mutual fund transactions. It does not affect listed equity securities or trading in the cash/derivatives market.
