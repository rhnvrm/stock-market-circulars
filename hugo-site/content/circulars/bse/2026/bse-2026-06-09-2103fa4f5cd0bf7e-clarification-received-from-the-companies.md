## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movements in seven listed companies and wrote to them seeking clarifications. As of 09 June 2026, three companies have responded and their replies are attached to the notice. Four companies have not yet submitted their replies.

## Key Points

- BSE observed unusual price/volume activity in seven scrips and invoked its standard investor-protection procedure of seeking company clarifications.
- Replies received from BCL Industries Ltd (524332), Bhageria Industries Ltd (530803), and CCL Products (India) Ltd (519600) — attachments available in the notice.
- Replies still awaited from Colinz Laboratories Ltd (531210), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), and SRU Steels Ltd (540914).
- Notice issued by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued under BSE's existing surveillance framework to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest material information.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four companies yet to reply — Colinz Laboratories, Elango Industries, CMX Holdings, and SRU Steels — are expected to submit clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors should review the attached replies from BCL Industries, Bhageria Industries, and CCL Products before making trading decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 09 June 2026
- No deadline explicitly stated for pending replies; companies are expected to respond at the earliest.

## Impact Assessment

Investors holding or considering positions in any of the seven scrips should note that BSE has flagged unusual price/volume activity. The three companies that have replied provide disclosed clarifications, which may reduce uncertainty. The four non-responding companies remain under surveillance scrutiny, and continued non-response could attract further regulatory action such as trade-for-trade migration or ASM/GSM inclusion. Overall market impact is limited to these specific scrips.
