## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment effective January 23, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form only with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial paper on BSE debt segment
- Infina Finance: 5,000 units, ISIN INE879F14MK5, 7.95% p.a., redemption April 20, 2026
- Muthoot Fincorp: 4,000 units, ISIN INE549K14CJ7, 8.83% p.a., redemption January 21, 2027
- 360 ONE WAM: 800 units, ISIN INE466L14FR8, 8.40% p.a., redemption January 21, 2027
- Krazybee Services: 1,000 units, ISIN INE07HK14458, 10.45% p.a., redemption October 21, 2026
- 360 ONE Alternates: 100 units, ISIN INE0TQI14061, 7.5% p.a., redemption April 22, 2026
- All securities have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Allotment Date** (all securities): January 22, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Infina Finance: April 20, 2026
  - 360 ONE Alternates: April 22, 2026
  - Krazybee Services: October 21, 2026
  - Muthoot Fincorp: January 21, 2027
  - 360 ONE WAM: January 21, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to debt market segment participants. This is a routine administrative listing notice for privately placed commercial paper. The listings provide additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt market. Credit ratings range from A1+ (highest short-term rating), indicating high credit quality. Interest rates range from 7.5% to 10.45% p.a., reflecting varying risk profiles and tenures. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
