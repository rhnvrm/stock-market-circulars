## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Vaghani Techno-Build Limited. The company name has been officially changed to Emrock Corporation Limited with effect from December 1, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre and confirms that the company remains a company limited by shares.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from VAGHANI TECHNO-BUILD LIMITED to EMROCK CORPORATION LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L41001GJ1994PLC168513
- Certificate issued on December 1, 2025
- Company originally incorporated as Dhruv Makhan (India) Ltd on October 6, 1994
- Name was previously changed to Vaghani Techno-Build Limited on October 10, 2006
- Company remains a company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by M.Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Vaghani Techno-Build Limited) along with its new name (Emrock Corporation Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and view public documents on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Vaghani Techno-Build Limited to Emrock Corporation Limited
- **October 10, 2006**: Previous name change from Dhruv Makhan (India) Ltd to Vaghani Techno-Build Limited
- **October 6, 1994**: Original incorporation date as Dhruv Makhan (India) Ltd

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Trading operations or stock listing
- Shareholder rights or liabilities
- Company's legal obligations or contracts
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN)

The company's registered office is located at 7th Floor, 715, Anushri Accolade-2, Nr. Ugati Lakeview, Science City Road, Sola, Ahmedabad-380060, Gujarat. Stakeholders should note the name change for correspondence and documentation purposes.
