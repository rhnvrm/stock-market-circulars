## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260409-34 informs trading members that five new commercial paper (CP) issuances have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective April 9, 2026. All securities were allotted on April 8, 2026, issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, tradeable only in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five fresh commercial paper listings across four issuers admitted to BSE Debt Segment on April 9, 2026
- All CPs issued at a discount to face value of Rs. 5,00,000; traded in demat form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples; tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agents include HDFC Bank, Bank of Maharashtra, and ICICI Bank
- Contact for clarifications: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under existing BSE debt market framework for privately placed commercial papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities exclusively in dematerialised form using the respective ISIN numbers
- Trading must occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all transactions
