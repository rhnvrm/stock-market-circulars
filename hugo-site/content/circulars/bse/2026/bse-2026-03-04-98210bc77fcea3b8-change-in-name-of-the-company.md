## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying a change in the name of a listed company. The name change has been certified by the Registrar of Companies (RoC) under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, as digitally signed by M. Nagaraju on February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- A listed company has officially changed its name as per certification from the Registrar of Companies.
- The change is backed by a digitally signed certificate from the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).
- The circular was issued on March 4, 2026.
- The RoC certificate was signed by M. Nagaraju on February 10, 2026 at 18:59 IST.

## Regulatory Changes

The company's registered name has been updated in the records of the Registrar of Companies under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. BSE is notifying market participants of this official name change as required under listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, brokers, and investors should update their records to reflect the new company name.
- The company's scrip name on BSE may be updated to reflect the new name.
- Any contracts, filings, or communications should reference the new name going forward.

## Important Dates

- **RoC Certificate Date:** February 10, 2026
- **BSE Circular Date:** March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with low market impact. The name change does not affect the company's fundamentals, trading status, or compliance standing. Investors holding shares are unaffected in terms of their holdings; only the company's display name and associated identifiers may be updated on the exchange.
