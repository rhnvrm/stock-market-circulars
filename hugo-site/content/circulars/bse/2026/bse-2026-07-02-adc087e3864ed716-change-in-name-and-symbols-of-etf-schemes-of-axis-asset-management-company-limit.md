## Summary

BSE has informed members that the trading symbols of six Exchange Traded Fund (ETF) schemes managed by Axis Asset Management Company Limited will change with effect from July 03, 2026. Additionally, the scheme name "AXIS S&P BSE SENSEX ETF" will be renamed to "AXIS BSE SENSEX ETF" from the same date. The underlying ISINs and scrip codes remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Six Axis Mutual Fund ETF schemes are undergoing symbol changes on BSE.
- Scrip Code 543347 (ISIN INF846K01Y96): Axis NIFTY IT ETF — symbol changes from AXISTECETF to ITAXIS.
- Scrip Code 543357 (ISIN INF846K016C7): Axis NIFTY India Consumption ETF — symbol changes from AXISCETF to CONSUMAXIS.
- Scrip Code 543348 (ISIN INF846K01Z12): Axis NIFTY Healthcare ETF — symbol changes from AXISHCETF to HEALTHAXIS.
- Scrip Code 543853 (ISIN INF846K010Q0): Axis S&P BSE SENSEX ETF, renamed to AXIS BSE SENSEX ETF — symbol changes from AXSENSEX to SENSEXAXIS.
- Scrip Code 544382 (ISIN INF846KA1119): Axis Nifty500 Value 50 ETF — symbol changes from AXISVALUE to VALUEAXIS.
- Scrip Code 533570 (ISIN INF846K01W80): Axis Gold ETF — symbol changes from AXISGOLD to GOLDAXIS.
- ISINs and scrip codes remain the same; only trading symbols (and one scheme name) are changing.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative listing update by BSE's DCS-Listing department reflecting Axis Asset Management Company Limited's decision to rebrand/rename its ETF scheme symbols and, for one scheme, its name. No changes to scheme structure, investment objective, or ISIN are involved.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants should update their systems, order routing, and reference data to reflect the new symbols from July 03, 2026.
- Depositories, custodians, and back-office systems should map old symbols to new symbols using the unchanged ISINs to avoid settlement or reporting errors.
- Investors holding these ETF units should note the new symbols when placing orders after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: Notice issued by BSE.
- July 03, 2026: Effective date for symbol changes and the scheme name change (AXIS S&P BSE SENSEX ETF to AXIS BSE SENSEX ETF).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational rather than financial — no change to NAV, holdings, or investor entitlements. However, failure to update trading and settlement systems with the new symbols could result in order rejections or reconciliation issues immediately after the effective date. Market participants dealing in these six Axis ETFs should ensure timely system updates.
