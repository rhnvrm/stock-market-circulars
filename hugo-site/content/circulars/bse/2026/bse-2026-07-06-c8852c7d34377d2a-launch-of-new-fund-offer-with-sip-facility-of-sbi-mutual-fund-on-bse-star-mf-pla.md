## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the launch of a New Fund Offer (NFO) with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for a scheme of SBI Mutual Fund, to be made available on the BSE StAR MF platform. The notice falls under the Trading category and Mutual Fund segment, issued by the Trading Operations department.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260706-41 dated 06 Jul 2026 issued by BSE Trading Operations department.
- Pertains to the Mutual Fund segment under the Trading category.
- Enables launch of a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility for SBI Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform.
- No attachment was provided with this notice; details are limited to the notice text itself.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational/product notice enabling trading members to offer the new SBI Mutual Fund scheme with SIP facility via the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and participants dealing in mutual funds via BSE StAR MF should note the availability of this new scheme and ensure their systems/processes support subscription and SIP registration for the SBI Mutual Fund NFO as per standard StAR MF operating procedures.

## Important Dates

Notice date: 06 Jul 2026. Specific NFO subscription opening/closing dates and SIP registration timelines were not specified in the notice text.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice facilitating access to a new mutual fund product on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, corporate actions, or market-wide operations, and is primarily relevant to mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform.
