## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544768) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) with effect from Monday, June 15, 2026. This notice continues from the Exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260529-40 dated May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited (SME IPO)
- **Scrip Code:** 544768
- **Current Segment:** Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- **New Segment:** Rolling (M Group)
- **Effective Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Prior Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260529-40 dated May 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is being reclassified from the Trade for Trade settlement mechanism (MT Group) to the normal Rolling settlement mechanism (M Group). Under Trade for Trade, each transaction must be settled individually without netting; the move to Rolling settlement allows standard T+1/T+2 netting and settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems and workflows to reflect the new segment classification (M Group) for Scrip Code 544768, effective June 15, 2026.
- For further details, trading members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026:** Effective date for transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment).

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard post-IPO segment transition for SME-listed shares. Moving from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement typically improves liquidity and price discovery as it enables netting of buy and sell positions. Trading members dealing in M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited shares must note the segment change to avoid settlement errors. The impact is moderate for active traders in this scrip but is operationally routine for the broader market.
