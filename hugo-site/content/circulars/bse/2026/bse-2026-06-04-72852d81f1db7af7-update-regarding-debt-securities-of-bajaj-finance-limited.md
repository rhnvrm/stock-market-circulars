## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260604-17) regarding the debt securities of Bajaj Finance Limited. Following receipt of final call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of the listed debt security (Scrip Code 973965) will change effective Friday, June 5, 2026. This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20250606-40 dated June 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finance Limited has received the final call money from debenture holders for Scrip Code 973965.
- A new ISIN (INE296A08AA1) replaces the existing ISIN (INE296A08995) effective June 5, 2026.
- The paid-up value per debenture increases from Rs. 8,00,000 to Rs. 10,00,000.
- This update is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250606-40 dated June 6, 2025.

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN for Scrip Code 973965 is being revised from INE296A08995 to INE296A08AA1 following full payment of the called amount by debenture holders. The paid-up value is correspondingly updated to reflect the fully-paid status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the updated ISIN (INE296A08AA1) and revised paid-up value (Rs. 10,00,000 per debenture) for Scrip Code 973965 and ensure all systems and records are updated accordingly from June 5, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 5, 2026 (Friday)**: New ISIN (INE296A08AA1) and revised paid-up value of Rs. 10,00,000 per debenture become effective.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-call-money administrative update. The change in ISIN and paid-up value affects trading members and investors holding Scrip Code 973965 (Bajaj Finance debentures). Members must update their back-office and trading systems to reflect the new ISIN before trading resumes on June 5, 2026. The increase in paid-up value from Rs. 8,00,000 to Rs. 10,00,000 per debenture reflects the final installment payment, completing the partly-paid debenture structure.
