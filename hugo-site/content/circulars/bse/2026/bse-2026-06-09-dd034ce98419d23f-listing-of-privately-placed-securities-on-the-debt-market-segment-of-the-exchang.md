## Summary

BSE has listed five new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on its Debt Segment, effective June 9, 2026. The issuers are Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, Tata Housing Development Company Limited, Infina Finance Private Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, and Titan Company Limited. All CPs are dated securities maturing in September 2026 and are available for trading in dematerialised form.

## Key Points

- All securities are traded exclusively in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for all listed CPs is 1 paise.
- Allotment date for all five CPs: June 8, 2026.
- Redemption dates range from September 4 to September 7, 2026.
- Interest is cumulative for all issuances.
- Credit ratings are high-grade (A1+) across all issuers from agencies including CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, and IND.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice informing trading members of new securities admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form.
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof must be observed.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.
