## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Thursday, February 05, 2026. The companies - KANISHK ALUMINIUM INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544693), ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544694), and MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544695) - were listed on BSE on February 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- KANISHK ALUMINIUM INDIA LIMITED (544693)
- ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (544694)
- MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED (544695)
- Listed on BSE on February 04, 2026
- Index addition effective at open of February 05, 2026
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine index maintenance action following the listing of new SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking or trading products linked to the BSE SME IPO Index should note the composition change.

## Important Dates

- **February 04, 2026**: Listing date for the three companies on BSE
- **February 05, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with medium impact. The addition of three newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index will affect:

- Index composition and weightings
- Investment products and funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index
- Trading strategies based on index constituents
- Market participants monitoring SME IPO performance

The companies referenced in previous notices (20260203-43, 20260203-44, 20260203-45) provide additional context for the listings. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
