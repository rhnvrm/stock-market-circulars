## Summary

BSE has informed members that Zerodha Mutual Fund is launching the Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 NFO with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window runs from June 19, 2026 to July 7, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: **Zerodha Life Cycle Fund 2041 – Direct Plan – Growth** (ISIN: INF0R8F01216)
- Category: Equity
- NFO period: June 19, 2026 to July 7, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 10, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 7, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF are extended till midnight on July 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for the launch of a new mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 10, 2026 for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 10, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date / Deadline |
|---|---|
| NFO opens | June 19, 2026 |
| NFO closes | July 7, 2026 (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP; midnight for lumpsum) |
| Clear funds deadline in ICCL | July 10, 2026 by 6:00 PM |
| Tentative allotment date | July 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational notification for BSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on BSE StAR MF. The scheme is a single equity NFO from Zerodha AMC and does not affect broader market operations, listed equities, or existing mutual fund schemes.
