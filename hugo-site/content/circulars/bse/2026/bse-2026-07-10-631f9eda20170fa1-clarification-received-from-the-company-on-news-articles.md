## Summary

BSE Ltd, as part of its routine surveillance process to verify the accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels, sought clarifications from Premier Explosives Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd regarding media reports about them. Premier Explosives Ltd has submitted its reply, which is attached to the notice, while Rajesh Exports Ltd's response is still pending.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Premier Explosives Ltd (Scrip Code: 526247) regarding a news article published on economictimes.indiatimes.com; the company has replied.
- BSE sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding a news article published on thehindubusinessline.com; reply is awaited.
- This is a standard exchange surveillance measure to inform members and investors about the veracity of circulating news reports.
- Notice issued by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) of BSE, signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President) and Shreya Ghose (Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine clarification request under BSE's ongoing surveillance framework, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to submit its clarification response to BSE regarding the referenced news article. Investors and members should treat unconfirmed news reports with caution until formal clarifications are issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 10 July 2026
- Rajesh Exports Ltd reply: awaited (no deadline specified in notice)

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is limited since the specific content of the news articles and the substance of Premier Explosives' reply are not detailed in this notice. Investors in Premier Explosives Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd should review the attached reply and monitor for further updates, as unclarified news reports can occasionally contribute to short-term price volatility.
