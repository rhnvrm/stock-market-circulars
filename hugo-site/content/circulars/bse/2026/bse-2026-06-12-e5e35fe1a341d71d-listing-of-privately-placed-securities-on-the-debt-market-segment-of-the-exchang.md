## Summary

BSE has listed five new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt Segment, effective June 12, 2026. The issuers are Tata Steel Limited, Nuvama Clearing Services Limited, Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, and Infina Finance Private Limited. All securities are available for trading in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment effective June 12, 2026
- All CPs issued on private placement basis at Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form in denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- Contact for clarifications: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational listing notice under the BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should use the respective ISIN numbers for each security
