## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260311-61 directing trading members to refrain from dealing in the commercial papers of 15 companies listed below. The suspension is in connection with record dates fixed for redemption of commercial papers, effective from 13 March 2026, under circular DR-839/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in commercial papers of 15 companies is suspended effective 13 March 2026
- Record dates for redemption range from 13 March 2026 to 15 March 2026
- All suspensions are governed under circular reference DR-839/2025-2026
- Trading members are advised not to deal in the listed commercial papers from the specified 'No Dealings From' date
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an enforcement of existing commercial paper redemption suspension norms under DR-839/2025-2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the commercial papers of the listed companies from 13 March 2026 onwards
- Members must ensure no transactions are executed in these instruments post the specified suspension date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular affecting the debt segment. Trading in 15 commercial paper instruments is suspended ahead of their redemption dates. The impact is limited to debt market participants holding or intending to trade these specific instruments. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations. Holders of these commercial papers should note the upcoming redemption dates and ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive from 13 March 2026.
