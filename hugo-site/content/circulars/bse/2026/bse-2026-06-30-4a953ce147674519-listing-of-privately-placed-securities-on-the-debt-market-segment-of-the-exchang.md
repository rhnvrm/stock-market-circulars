## Summary

BSE has admitted five commercial papers (CPs), issued on a private placement basis, to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 30, 2026. The securities belong to SBICAP Securities Limited, Infina Finance Private Limited, Jio Credit Limited, Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, and ARKA FINCAP LIMITED. All securities trade in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- **SBICAP Securities Limited** (Scrip: 732005, ID: SSL290626, ISIN: INE212K14EN0) — Qty 2,000 CPs at Rs. 5,00,000 face value; Issue Price Rs. 4,91,423.50; Rate 7.00% p.a.; Allotment 29/06/2026; Redemption 28/09/2026; Rated CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+; IPA: State Bank of India
- **Infina Finance Private Limited** (Scrip: 732007, ID: IFPL29626, ISIN: INE879F14NG1) — Qty 2,000 CPs; Issue Price Rs. 4,91,123.00; Rate 7.25% p.a.; Allotment 29/06/2026; Redemption 28/09/2026; Rated CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+; IPA: ICICI Bank
- **Jio Credit Limited** (Scrip: 732008, ID: JCL29626, ISIN: INE282H14139) — Qty 4,000 CPs; Issue Price Rs. 4,97,200.50; Rate 6.85% p.a.; Allotment 29/06/2026; Redemption 29/07/2036; Rated CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+; IPA: ICICI Bank
- **Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited** (Scrip: 732013, ID: KMPL30626, ISIN: INE916D146U1) — Qty 4,000 CPs; Issue Price Rs. 4,65,561.00; Rate 7.50% p.a.; Allotment 30/06/2026; Redemption 25/06/2027; Rated CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+; IPA: Kotak Mahindra Bank
- **ARKA FINCAP LIMITED** (Scrip: 732010, ID: AFL29626, ISIN: INE03W114732) — Qty 2,000 CPs; Issue Price Rs. 4,90,887.00; Rate 7.70% p.a.; Allotment 29/06/2026; Redemption 25/09/2026; Rated CRISIL A1+; IPA: (not fully disclosed in source)
- All securities trade only in dematerialised form in denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof with a tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE debt market segment framework for privately placed commercial papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof must be adhered to for all trades
- Queries may be directed to BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular for debt segment trading members. The five commercial papers provide short- to medium-term debt instruments from highly rated issuers (all rated A1+ by at least one major rating agency), offering yields between 6.85% and 7.70% p.a. The listing expands the universe of tradeable debt instruments on BSE's debt market segment. Impact is limited to debt segment participants; no equity market or systemic regulatory impact.
