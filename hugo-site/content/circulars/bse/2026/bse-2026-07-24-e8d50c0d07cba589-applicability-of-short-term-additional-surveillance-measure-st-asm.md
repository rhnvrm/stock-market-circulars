## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities, listed in the attached Annexure I, have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities meeting Stage I criteria will attract an additional margin of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher), while securities meeting Stage II criteria will attract an additional margin of 100% (or existing margin, whichever is higher), both capped at a maximum of 100%. These measures take effect from July 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria: applicable margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Securities in Annexure I satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria: applicable margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on July 27, 2026, and new positions created from July 28, 2026 onward.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Price bands of a scrip moving out of the ASM framework will be reinstated to the pre-shortlisting price band, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band prevails.
- This notice references and updates prior circulars: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is a periodic update applying the existing ST-ASM framework (as revised via prior circulars) to a fresh set of securities identified in Annexure I.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified higher margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on all open and new positions in the identified securities from July 28, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure_STASM for the specific list of affected securities.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 27, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to new margin requirements.
- July 28, 2026: Effective date for revised margin rates under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (50%-100%) will raise the cost of trading in the affected securities, likely reducing speculative activity and liquidity in these names. Market participants holding or trading these securities should expect higher capital requirements. The impact is stock-specific and limited to securities listed in the (unprovided) Annexure I, with no broader market-wide implications.
