## Summary

BSE has announced the removal of Premium Linkers Limited from its Dissemination Board effective January 5, 2026. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Following this removal, shares of Premium Linkers Limited will no longer be available on BSE's Dissemination Board.

## Key Points

- Premium Linkers Limited (CIN: U74899DL1982PLC014454) to be removed from BSE Dissemination Board
- Removal effective from Monday, January 5, 2026
- Reason for removal: Exit option provided to shareholders
- Company registered address: 102, Palco House, 2162/T-10, Main Patel Road, New Delhi â€“ 110008
- Action taken under SEBI circular dated October 10, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

This removal follows the framework established by SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the delisting process for Exclusively Listed Companies (ELCs) on stock exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note that Premium Linkers Limited shares will not be available for trading on BSE's Dissemination Board from January 5, 2026
- No action required from trading members; this is an informational notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 2, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 5, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Premium Linkers Limited, an exclusively listed company on BSE. The removal affects only this single company and follows the exit option mechanism. Trading members and investors holding shares should note that the scrip will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board after January 5, 2026.
