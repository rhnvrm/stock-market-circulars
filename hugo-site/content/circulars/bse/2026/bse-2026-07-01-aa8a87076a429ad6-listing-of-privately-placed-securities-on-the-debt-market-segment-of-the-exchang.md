## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by four companies - Chennai Petroleum Corporation Ltd., Godrej Finance Limited, Navi Finserv Limited (two issues), and Credila Financial Services Limited - have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper instruments have been freshly listed on the BSE Debt Segment.
- Chennai Petroleum Corporation Ltd.: ISIN INE178A14HR8 (CPCL-30-7-26-CP), Rs. 500,000 face value, quantity 10,000, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, interest rate 6.30% p.a., redemption on 30/07/2026.
- Godrej Finance Limited: ISIN INE02KN14762 (GFL-28-9-26-CP), Rs. 500,000 face value, quantity 3,000, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, interest rate 7% p.a., redemption on 28/09/2026.
- Navi Finserv Limited: ISIN INE342T14GX0 (NFL-30-12-26-CP), Rs. 500,000 face value, quantity 200, rated CARE A1, interest rate 9.50% p.a., redemption on 30/12/2026.
- Navi Finserv Limited (second issue): ISIN INE342T14GW2 (NFL-25-11-26-CP), Rs. 500,000 face value, quantity 400, rated CARE A1, interest rate 10% p.a., redemption on 25/11/2026.
- Credila Financial Services Limited: ISIN INE539K14CC3 (CFSL-21-12-26-CP), Rs. 500,000 face value, quantity 10,000, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, interest rate 7.80% p.a.
- All securities will trade only in dematerialised form, in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Actual/deemed date of allotment for all instruments is 30/06/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these commercial papers comply with the specified standard denomination (Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples), tick size (1 paise), and dematerialised form requirement. Any clarifications can be sought from BSE's debt department via the listed contact numbers.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (all issues): 30/06/2026
- Listing/Effective Date: 01/07/2026
- Chennai Petroleum Corporation redemption: 30/07/2026
- Godrej Finance redemption: 28/09/2026
- Navi Finserv (NFL-30-12-26-CP) redemption: 30/12/2026
- Navi Finserv (NFL-25-11-26-CP) redemption: 25/11/2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to debt market participants and holders of these specific commercial papers. It provides operational clarity on trading parameters for these newly listed instruments but does not affect equity markets, broader trading operations, or listed company stock prices.
