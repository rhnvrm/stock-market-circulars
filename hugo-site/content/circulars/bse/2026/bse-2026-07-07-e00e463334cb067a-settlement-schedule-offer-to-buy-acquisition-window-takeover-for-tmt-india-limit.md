## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for TMT (INDIA) LIMITED. The acquisition window will be conducted from 08 July 2026 to 21 July 2026, under Settlement No. 2026084.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement No.: 2026084
- Bid Start Date: 08/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 21/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 24/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 27/07/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories (CDSL & NSDL).
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026084 in the depository system.
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file.
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice references the existing framework under Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed bidding and confirmation window timelines.
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance in the Depository system, well before the OTB bidding window closes and the Custodian confirmation window closes.
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number.

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 08 July 2026
- Bid End Date: 21 July 2026
- Obligation Download: 24 July 2026
- Settlement Date: 27 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural settlement notice affecting Members, Custodians, and Depository Participants involved in the TMT (India) Limited takeover offer. It does not directly affect broader market trading but is important for entities and shareholders participating in the acquisition window to ensure compliance with pay-in timelines and avoid settlement failures.
