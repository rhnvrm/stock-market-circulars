## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 29,70,000 new equity shares of Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519604) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 13, 2026. The shares were issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 10/- per share.

## Key Points

- 29,70,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- on rights basis are now listed
- Trading commences from March 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE565E01016
- Distribution numbers: 3960001 to 6930000
- Date of allotment: March 9, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading of the new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions are required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 9, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** March 13, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of rights issue shares for Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no change in shareholder rights. The rights issue increases the total share count, which may have a minor dilutive effect on existing shareholders.
