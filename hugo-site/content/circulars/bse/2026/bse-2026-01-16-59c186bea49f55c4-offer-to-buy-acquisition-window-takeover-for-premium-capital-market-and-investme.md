## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LTD (PREMCAPM). The acquisition window will operate from Friday, January 16, 2026 to Thursday, January 30, 2026. The offer is for up to 17,04,000 equity shares at Rs. 6.40 per share through the BSE's OTB (Offer to Buy) platform.
