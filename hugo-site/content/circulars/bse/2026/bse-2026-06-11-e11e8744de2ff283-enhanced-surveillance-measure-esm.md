## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, adding new securities to ESM (with 100% margin requirements), downgrading certain securities to Stage I, and removing others from the framework. Changes are effective June 12 and June 15, 2026. Stage II securities will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction.

## Key Points

- New securities listed in Annexure I are brought under ESM with a minimum 100% margin requirement effective June 15, 2026, on all open positions as on June 12, 2026, and new positions from June 15, 2026.
- Certain securities currently in ESM will be moved to a lower stage (Stage I) under the ESM Framework effective June 12, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective June 15, 2026.
- Securities shifted to Stage II will be subject to Trade-for-Trade settlement with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective June 12, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework effective June 12, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands will revert to pre-ESM levels unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM shortlisting is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice amends the ESM framework previously established under notices dated June 02, 2023; July 18, 2023; August 09, 2024; September 20, 2024; October 04, 2024; and July 25, 2025. The update revises the list of securities subject to ESM, including new inclusions, stage changes, and exclusions in line with periodic review criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of minimum 100% margin on all open positions (as on June 12, 2026) and new positions (from June 15, 2026) for securities newly placed under ESM.
- Members must adhere to Trade-for-Trade settlement norms for ESM-listed securities from the effective dates.
- For Stage II securities, members must comply with the 2% price band restriction and Periodic Call Auction mechanism from June 12, 2026.
- Members may direct clarification queries to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Notice date.
- **June 12, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective; ESM exclusions effective.
- **June 15, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective; shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective for newly included securities.

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities will face significantly tighter trading conditions: mandatory 100% margins substantially increase the cost of holding positions, while the shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk for traders. The 2% price band on Stage II securities limits intraday price movement, reducing liquidity. Securities exiting ESM will see their price bands reinstated, potentially improving liquidity. The periodic review mechanism means the list can change again in subsequent cycles. The circular affects an undisclosed number of securities across BSE's equity segment as detailed in the attached annexures.
