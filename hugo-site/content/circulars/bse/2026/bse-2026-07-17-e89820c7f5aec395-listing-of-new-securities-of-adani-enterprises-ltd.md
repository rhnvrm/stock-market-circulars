## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512599) with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. The new securities comprise 4,80,344 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, arising from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares, originally issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- New securities: 4,80,344 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, fully paid-up.
- These shares arise from conversion of partly paid rights shares into fully paid equity shares.
- ISIN: INE423A01024 (same ISIN as existing equity shares).
- Distinctive numbers span four blocks: 1,21,144 shares (1408554617–1408675760), 1,20,666 shares (1408675907–1408796572), 1,46,888 shares (1408797090–1408943977), and 91,646 shares (1408943978–1409035623).
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.
- Notice signed by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President, BSE Listing Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new securities as tradable and pari-passu with existing equity shares from the effective date; no additional action required from investors or other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for trading: Monday, July 20, 2026.
- Notice date: July 17, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,80,344 fully paid-up equity shares to Adani Enterprises Ltd.'s tradable float following conversion of partly paid rights shares. Given Adani Enterprises' large existing share base, this incremental listing is unlikely to have a material impact on price or liquidity, but it completes the rights issue call-money process for the affected shareholders.
