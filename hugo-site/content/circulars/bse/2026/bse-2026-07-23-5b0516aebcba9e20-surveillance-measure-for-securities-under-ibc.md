## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Certain securities have been newly added to this surveillance framework, while others have exited, effective July 24, 2026. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided for reference.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I now satisfy the criteria for Additional Surveillance Measure under the IBC/IRP framework, effective July 24, 2026
- Securities listed in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC Framework, effective July 24, 2026
- Annexure III provides the consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework
- This framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange
- Placement under this framework is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company
- This circular references and updates prior notices dated June 08, 2018, May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework has been introduced; this is a periodic update to the existing IBC-related Additional Surveillance Measure that has been in effect since 2018. Securities meeting IRP-related criteria are added, and those no longer meeting criteria are removed, on an ongoing review basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated list of securities under the IBC surveillance framework
- Members should apply the relevant price band and trading restrictions applicable to securities under this framework
- For securities exiting the framework, the price band will revert to the level applicable prior to shortlisting, unless the security remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band will prevail
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Effective date for additions and exits from the IBC surveillance framework: July 24, 2026
- Notice issued: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to specific securities named in the annexures (not detailed in the notice text) that are undergoing insolvency resolution proceedings. Securities added to the framework will face tighter trading restrictions including modified price bands, while securities exiting will see their standard price bands reinstated. This is a routine, recurring surveillance exercise with no broader market-wide implications beyond the affected scrips.
