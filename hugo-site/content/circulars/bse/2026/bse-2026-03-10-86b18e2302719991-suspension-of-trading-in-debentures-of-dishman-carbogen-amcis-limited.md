## Summary

BSE has directed trading members not to deal in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited effective March 12, 2026. The suspension is in connection with the company's fixed record date for redemption on exercise of put option on its debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading in the specified NCD series of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited is suspended from March 12, 2026
- The debenture series affected: DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT with ISIN INE385W07034 (Scrip Code: 975834)
- Record date for redemption on exercise of put option is March 12, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-838/2025-2026
- BSE Notice No: 20260310-50 dated March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE rules governing suspension of trading in debt instruments ahead of corporate action record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not buy or sell the debentures of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited (ISIN INE385W07034) from March 12, 2026 onwards
- Members are advised to inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: BSE notice issued
- **March 12, 2026**: Record date for redemption on exercise of put option
- **March 12, 2026**: No dealings permitted from this date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific NCD series (INE385W07034) of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited. Investors holding these debentures will be eligible for redemption as per the put option terms at the coupon rate of 10.04%. No broader market or equity segment impact is anticipated.
