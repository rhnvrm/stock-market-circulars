## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Quantum Mutual Fund has communicated changes to scheme names for three of its funds on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The changes are effective June 29, 2026, and are purely nomenclature updates with no change to fund features.

## Key Points

- Three Quantum Mutual Fund schemes are being renamed effective June 29, 2026
- The changes affect scheme names only on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Quantum AMC is attached to the circular
- No structural or financial changes to the schemes are indicated

## Regulatory Changes

The following scheme name changes have been approved and will take effect on June 29, 2026:

| Sr. No. | Existing Name | Revised Name |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | QUANTUM DYNAMIC BOND FUND | QUANTUM DYNAMIC TERM FUND |
| 2 | QUANTUM NIFTY 50 ETF FUND OF FUND | QUANTUM NIFTY 50 ETF FOF |
| 3 | QUANTUM GOLD SAVINGS FUND | QUANTUM GOLD ETF FOF |

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and distributors operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems and records to reflect the new scheme names from June 29, 2026
- Members should refer to the attached Notice-cum-Addendum from Quantum AMC for complete details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and operational in nature. Investors in these three Quantum Mutual Fund schemes will see updated scheme names reflected on the BSE StAR MF Platform from June 29, 2026. There is no change to fund strategy, investment objectives, or financial terms. Distributors and members may need to update internal records or systems to map to the new scheme names.
