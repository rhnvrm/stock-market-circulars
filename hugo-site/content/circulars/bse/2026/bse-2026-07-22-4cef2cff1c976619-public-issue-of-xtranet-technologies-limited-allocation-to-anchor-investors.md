## Summary

BSE informed trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Xtranet Technologies Limited, 39,40,200 equity shares were subscribed on July 22, 2026 at a price of Rs. 127 per equity share. The notice encloses the company's announcement dated July 22, 2026 confirming this anchor allocation.

## Key Points

- Anchor Investors subscribed to 39,40,200 equity shares of Xtranet Technologies Limited.
- Subscription price was Rs. 127 per equity share.
- Allocation occurred on July 22, 2026, ahead of the public issue opening for other investor categories.
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department, signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond being informed of the anchor allocation ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- Anchor allocation date: July 22, 2026
- Notice issued: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation provides an early signal of institutional demand for Xtranet Technologies Limited's public issue, which can influence investor sentiment ahead of the IPO's broader subscription period and eventual listing. Market impact is limited to IPO-interested participants and does not affect existing listed securities.
