## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) effective June 02, 2026. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and some are exiting the framework, both effective May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will apply from June 02, 2026 on all open positions as of June 01, 2026 and on new positions created from June 02, 2026 onwards
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) are moved to their respective higher stages of the LT-ASM framework effective May 29, 2026
- Lower Price Band is applicable w.e.f. May 29, 2026 for securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group w.e.f. June 02, 2026
- Securities in Annexure II are moving out of the LT-ASM framework effective May 29, 2026
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework is in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely a market surveillance action, not an adverse finding against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and builds upon prior BSE notices:
- Notice 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) — original LT-ASM framework
- Notice 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018) — revision
- Notice 20201204-56 (December 04, 2020) — revision
- Notice 20240809-46 (August 09, 2024) — revision
- Notice 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024) — revision
- Notice 20210604-41 (June 04, 2021) — group transfer provisions

The revised LT-ASM framework continues to use staged surveillance with increasing restrictions as securities progress through stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin is collected/deposited for securities in Annexure I (Part A) from June 02, 2026
- Members must apply Lower Price Band restrictions for securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages from May 29, 2026
- Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group must be traded accordingly from June 02, 2026
- Price band for securities exiting the framework reverts to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless covered by another surveillance measure
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Securities move to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I Part B); Lower Price Band applies; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II)
- **June 01, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 02, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions (as of June 01) and new positions; securities in Annexure I (Part D) transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital requirement for trading in affected securities, reducing leverage and liquidity for market participants holding or entering positions in these stocks. The transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS groups further restricts trading mechanics. Securities exiting the framework will see price band restoration, potentially improving trading flexibility. The framework is applied solely based on XBRL data surveillance and runs in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
