## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 10,00,000 new equity shares of Balu Forge Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531112, ISIN: INE011E01029) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 360/- per share.

## Key Points

- 10,00,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 350/- (total issue price Rs. 360/-) per share.
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distribution numbers: 114991901 to 115991900.
- All 10,00,000 newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in until 30 November 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may trade in these securities from May 25, 2026 onwards.
- The lock-in on all allotted shares (114991901–115991900) must be observed until 30 November 2026; these shares cannot be transferred or sold before that date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 09 February 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** 25 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 30 November 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 10 lakh shares to the tradeable float of Balu Forge Industries Limited, though all newly issued shares remain locked in until November 2026, limiting immediate dilution impact on the open market. The preferential allotment at Rs. 360/- represents the issue price benchmark for these shares. Existing shareholders should be aware of the potential overhang once the lock-in expires at end of November 2026.
