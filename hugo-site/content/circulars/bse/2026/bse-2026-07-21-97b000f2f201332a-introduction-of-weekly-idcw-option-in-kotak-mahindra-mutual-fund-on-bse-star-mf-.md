## Summary

BSE has informed its members that it has received communication from Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund regarding the introduction of a Weekly IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) option, with both Reinvestment and Payout facility, on the BSE StAR MF platform. This option is effective immediately for four specified Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund schemes.

## Key Points

- The Weekly IDCW option (with Reinvestment & Payout facility) is now available on BSE StAR MF platform for the following Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund schemes:
  - Kotak Savings Fund
  - Kotak Corporate Bond Fund
  - Kotak Money Market Fund
  - Kotak Bond Short Term Fund
- The change is effective with immediate effect.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund is attached to the circular for members' information.
- Notice issued jointly by BSE's Trading and Operations departments under the Mutual Fund segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational/product update enabling a new IDCW frequency option (weekly) for existing debt fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF platform members and mutual fund distributors should update their systems/processes to reflect the new Weekly IDCW option for the specified Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund schemes and refer to the attached Notice-cum-Addendum for scheme-specific terms.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 21 July 2026
- Effective Date: Immediate effect (21 July 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine operational update relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors and BSE StAR MF platform members dealing in the named Kotak Mahindra debt schemes. It provides investors an additional weekly income distribution option but does not affect listed equity trading, market structure, or broader investor base.
