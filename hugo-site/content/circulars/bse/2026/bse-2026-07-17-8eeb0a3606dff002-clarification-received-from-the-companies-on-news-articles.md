## Summary

BSE, as part of its surveillance process to verify news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites and television channels, sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) and Pfizer Ltd (Scrip Code: 500680) regarding recent news reports. As of the notice date, replies from both companies are awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification to verify accuracy of news articles and inform members/investors accordingly.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) — clarification sought on a news article from The Hindu BusinessLine; reply awaited.
- Pfizer Ltd (Scrip Code: 500680) — clarification sought on a news article from The Hindu BusinessLine; reply awaited.
- No specific details of the underlying news articles are provided in this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd and Pfizer Ltd are required to respond to BSE's clarification request regarding the news articles in question.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 17 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260717-18). No specific deadline for company response is mentioned in the notice.

## Impact Assessment

Low immediate market impact as this is a standard surveillance measure with no confirmed material information disclosed. Investors should monitor for follow-up company clarifications, which could affect sentiment depending on the nature of the underlying news articles.
