## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members of the contract launch calendar for WTI Crude Oil Futures and Options on Futures contracts in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. This is a follow-up to earlier exchange notices dated 07 Aug 2025, 16 Sep 2025 and 08 Jan 2026, and lists launch and expiry dates for contract months spanning January 2027 through December 2027.

## Key Points

- The Exchange will launch additional WTI Crude Oil Futures contracts and WTI Crude Oil Options on Futures contracts as per the published calendar.
- Contract specifications are provided via attachments: WTI Crude Oil Futures Contract Specification and WTI Crude Oil Options of Futures Contract Specification.
- Futures contracts for January 2027 through December 2027 will be launched progressively between August 2026 and July 2027.
- Options on Futures contracts follow a similar staggered launch schedule, each tied to an underlying futures contract with its own expiry.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is an operational notice under the Trading Operations department implementing a previously communicated contract launch schedule for the Commodity Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the launch and expiry dates to plan trading strategies, system readiness, and client communication for the new contract months. No specific filing or compliance action is required beyond awareness.
