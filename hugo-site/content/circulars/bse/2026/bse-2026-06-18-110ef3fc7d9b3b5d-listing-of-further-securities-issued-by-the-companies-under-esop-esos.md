## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by Aether Industries Limited, Axis Bank Limited, and Go Digit General Insurance Limited under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three companies have had new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted to trading on BSE
- All newly listed shares carry no lock-in restrictions (NA)
- Effective date for trading: June 19, 2026
- Total new shares: 3,677 (Aether Industries), 148,481 (Axis Bank), 21,559 (Go Digit General Insurance)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's listing operations for ESOP/ESOS share allotments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and update their systems accordingly for trading from June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 19, 2026 (Friday)
