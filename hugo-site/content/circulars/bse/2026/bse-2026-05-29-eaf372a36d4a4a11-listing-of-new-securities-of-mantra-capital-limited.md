## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 38,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- (issue price Rs. 20/-) to Non Promoters of Mantra Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 511577) on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 38,25,000 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- (total issue price Rs. 20/-)
- Issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE304E01010
- Distribution Numbers: 32054559 to 35879558
- Date of Allotment: April 8, 2026
- Trading commences: June 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their lock-in restrictions before facilitating trades. Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 8, 2026
- Trading Commencement: June 1, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (2,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 32054559–32254558): November 29, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (11,25,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 32254559–33379558): November 30, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (25,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 33379559–35879558): November 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 38,25,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Mantra Capital Limited. However, the majority of shares (38,25,000 out of 38,25,000) are subject to lock-in until November 2026, meaning only a limited number of shares will be freely tradeable at the time of listing commencement. The preferential allotment to Non Promoters at Rs. 20/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a 100% premium) reflects the company's capital-raising activity. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in constraints.
