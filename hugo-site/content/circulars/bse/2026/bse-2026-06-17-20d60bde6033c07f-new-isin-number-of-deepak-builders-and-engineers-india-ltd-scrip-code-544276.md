## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd (Scrip Code: 544276) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- per share to Re.1/- per share. This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260611-47 dated June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd (544276): **INE0OPA01027**
- Sub-division is from Rs.10/- face value to Re.1/- face value (10:1 split)
- The new ISIN is effective for trades executed on and from the ex-date
- Reference: DR-655/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

The existing ISIN for Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd equity shares has been replaced with INE0OPA01027 to reflect the sub-divided shares of Re.1/- each. Trading members must update their systems to reference the new ISIN for all transactions from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN (INE0OPA01027) for all trades in Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd equity shares on and from the ex-date.
- Back-office and settlement systems should be updated accordingly prior to June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN):** June 19, 2026
- **Predecessor Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260611-47 dated June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update affecting trading members and investors holding or trading Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd shares. The share count per investor will increase tenfold while the face value decreases proportionally, with no change in the economic value held. Members must ensure systems are updated before June 19, 2026 to avoid settlement issues.
