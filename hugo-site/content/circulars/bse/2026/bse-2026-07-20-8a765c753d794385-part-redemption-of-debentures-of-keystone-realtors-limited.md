## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260720-4, dated 20 July 2026, informs Trading Members that Keystone Realtors Limited has fixed a Record Date for the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Effective from the Ex Date, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value on the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Keystone Realtors Limited
- Instrument: Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD), Scrip ID: KRL-9.5%-29-9-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE263M07109
- Scrip Code: 977174
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 73,134.33 per debenture
- Trading in the debenture will occur at the reduced face value from the Ex Date onward

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable; this is an operational/corporate action notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must ensure trades in the specified ISIN (INE263M07109) are executed and settled using the reduced face value of Rs. 73,134.33 per debenture from the Ex Date onward.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 21 July 2026
- Ex Date: 21 July 2026
- Settlement: 676/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only holders and traders of Keystone Realtors Limited's specific NCD series (ISIN INE263M07109). The part redemption reduces the outstanding principal per debenture, requiring Trading Members to adjust trading and settlement systems to reflect the new reduced face value. No broader market impact is expected.
