## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 29,70,000 new equity shares of Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519604) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 13, 2026. The shares were issued at a face value of Rs. 10/- each at a premium of Rs. 10/- (total issue price Rs. 20/-).

## Key Points

- 29,70,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 519604 | ISIN: INE565E01016
- Distribution Numbers: 3960001 to 6930000
- Date of Allotment: March 09, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade the new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 09, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** March 13, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine post-rights-issue listing for Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. The new shares (29,70,000) increase the tradable float and are fungible with existing shares due to pari-passu ranking. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and traders of scrip code 519604.
