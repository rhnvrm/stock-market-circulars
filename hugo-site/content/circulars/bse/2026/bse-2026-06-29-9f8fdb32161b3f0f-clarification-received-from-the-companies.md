## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price/volume movements in 19 scrips and wrote to the respective companies seeking clarification. Replies have been received from 11 companies, while 8 companies' responses remain pending.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume activity in 19 listed equity scrips
- Exchange wrote to all companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information
- 11 companies have submitted their clarifications (attached as Replies 1 & 2)
- 8 companies have not yet responded as of the notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a disclosure and transparency measure by BSE's Surveillance department to keep market participants informed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have responded: clarification replies are attached to this circular for reference by trading members and investors
- Companies with pending replies: expected to submit clarifications to the Exchange promptly

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29 June 2026
- Notice Number: 20260629-20

## Impact Assessment

Investors and trading members holding or dealing in the 19 listed scrips should review the attached company clarifications before making trading decisions. The 8 companies yet to respond may face follow-up action from the Exchange's Surveillance department. The notice signals heightened scrutiny of these scrips, which may influence short-term trading sentiment.
