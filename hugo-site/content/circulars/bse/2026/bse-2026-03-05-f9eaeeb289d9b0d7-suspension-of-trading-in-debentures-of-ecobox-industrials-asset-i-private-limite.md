## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED will be suspended from 10 March 2026, as the company has fixed a Record Date for Early Redemption of its debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading in the specified debentures of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED is suspended effective 10 March 2026.
- The company has fixed 10 March 2026 as the Record Date for Early Redemption of these NCDs.
- ISIN: INE12W707013 (Scrip Code: 976482), instrument: EIAIPL-10.15%-RESET RATE-6-2-2.
- Notice reference: DR-836/2025-2026.
- Issued under Notice No. 20260305-22 dated 05 March 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements standard BSE procedure for suspending trading ahead of a corporate action (early redemption) to protect investors and ensure orderly settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members of BSE must not deal in the debentures of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE12W707013 / Scrip Code: 976482) from 10 March 2026 onwards.
- Members are required to take note of the trading suspension and inform their clients accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 10 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 10 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Early Redemption of Debentures
- **Circular Date:** 05 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Holders of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED NCDs (ISIN: INE12W707013) will be unable to trade these instruments from 10 March 2026. Investors holding these debentures as of the Record Date will be eligible for early redemption proceeds. The suspension is limited to this specific instrument in the debt segment and does not affect other securities.
