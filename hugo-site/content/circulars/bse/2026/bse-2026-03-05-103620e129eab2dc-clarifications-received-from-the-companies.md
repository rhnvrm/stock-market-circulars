## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies — Lime Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 507759), Siddha Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 530439), and True Green Bio Energy Limited (formerly CIL Nova Petrochemicals Ltd., Scrip Code: 533407) — in response to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price and/or volume movements in their respective securities. All three companies confirmed that there is no undisclosed material or price-sensitive information that could explain the observed movements, and that they remain compliant with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- **Lime Chemicals Ltd (507759)**: Responded to BSE email L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/953 dated 05 March 2026; confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) 2015 and stated no price-sensitive information is pending disclosure.
- **Siddha Ventures Ltd (530439)**: Responded to BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/3406 dated 04 March 2026; confirmed no material or price-sensitive information exists that has not been disclosed to the exchange.
- **True Green Bio Energy Ltd (533407)**: Responded to BSE email L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/950 dated 04 March 2026; confirmed no material information or event has been withheld that is required to be disclosed under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) 2015.
- All three companies reaffirmed their commitment to timely disclosure of any future material events.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. These are company responses to standard BSE surveillance queries under the existing framework of Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must continue to monitor for and disclose any material/price-sensitive information as and when it arises, per Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- No new compliance obligations arise from these clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **05 March 2026**: Lime Chemicals Ltd and True Green Bio Energy Ltd submitted clarifications.
- **05 March 2026**: Siddha Ventures Ltd submitted clarification (in response to a query dated 04 March 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. These are routine surveillance-driven clarifications with no material disclosures made. Investors should note that all three companies have denied knowledge of any undisclosed price-sensitive developments. The filings do not indicate any forthcoming corporate actions, regulatory actions, or significant business events. Market participants should exercise independent due diligence given the observed price/volume anomalies that triggered the BSE queries.
