## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of Harikanta Overseas Limited (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544769) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Tuesday, June 16, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260601-23 dated June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544769 (Harikanta Overseas Limited – SME IPO)
- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Tuesday, June 16, 2026
- Contact for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822
- Continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260601-23 dated June 01, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Harikanta Overseas Limited will move out of the Trade for Trade (TfT) mechanism under the MT Group and be placed under the M Group (Rolling settlement). Under TfT, each trade must result in delivery; the shift to Rolling segment allows normal netting and settlement under T+1 rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in Harikanta Overseas Limited (Scrip Code: 544769) must update their systems and inform clients of the change in trading segment effective June 16, 2026. No trade netting is permitted in TfT; members should ensure compliance with the new M Group settlement rules post-migration.

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from MT Group to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard post-IPO procedural migration for an SME-listed company. The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) typically improves liquidity and eases trading for retail and institutional participants, as netting of trades becomes permissible. Impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or trading Harikanta Overseas Limited shares.
