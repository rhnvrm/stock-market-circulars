## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of ATHARVA POLY-PLAST LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544812) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Tuesday, July 21, 2026. Following this transition, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted to the M Group.

## Key Points

- This notice is issued in continuation of BSE's earlier Notice No. 20260706-33 dated Monday, July 06, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 544812, listed under the SME segment.
- Trading will move from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group).
- Effective date of transition: Tuesday, July 21, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational/administrative reclassification of the trading segment for the specified scrip under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in trading segment (from MT Group Trade for Trade to M Group Rolling) for this scrip effective the transition date. No specific action required from investors beyond awareness of the change in settlement mechanism.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Prior related notice: July 06, 2026 (Notice No. 20260706-33)
- Effective date of segment shift: Tuesday, July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (settlement on a trade-to-trade basis, restricting intraday trading) to the Rolling segment (allowing normal rolling settlement) typically indicates improved trading liquidity conditions and eases restrictions previously imposed on the scrip. This is a positive operational development for shareholders and traders of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited, though the overall market impact is limited to this single SME-listed company.
