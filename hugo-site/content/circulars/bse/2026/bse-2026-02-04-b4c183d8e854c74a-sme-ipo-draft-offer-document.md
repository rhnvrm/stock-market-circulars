## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Panchatv Bharat Limited, filed on February 02, 2026, on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days for stakeholder feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited on February 02, 2026
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure notice for SME IPO draft documents as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from February 04, 2026
- Company must keep the document available for at least 21 days for public review

## Important Dates

- **February 02, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **February 04, 2026**: Notice issued and document made public
- **February 25, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. It is informational in nature, allowing stakeholders to review and comment on the draft offer document before the final IPO launch. The impact is limited to potential investors and market participants interested in Panchatv Bharat Limited's upcoming SME IPO.
