## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of CMR Green Technologies Limited (Exchange ticker: 544777) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on June 10, 2026, and will be added to the index at the open of trading on June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- CMR Green Technologies Limited (ticker: 544777) is being added to the BSE IPO Index
- The stock was listed on BSE effective June 10, 2026
- Index inclusion is effective at the open of Friday, June 12, 2026
- The action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for tonight
- Reference is made to Notice No. 20260609-44 regarding the original listing notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action by BSE Index Services Private Limited to include a newly listed IPO stock in the BSE IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs referencing the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of CMR Green Technologies Limited effective June 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: CMR Green Technologies Limited listed on BSE
- **June 12, 2026**: Effective date of addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index composition update with limited market-wide impact. Funds and portfolios tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to incorporate CMR Green Technologies Limited (544777). The addition follows standard BSE IPO Index methodology, which includes newly listed IPO stocks. Passive investors and index-linked products referencing the BSE IPO Index are the primary entities affected.
