## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member No. 3299) will cease to conduct business in the Currency derivative segment effective January 9, 2026. This closure is voluntary and initiated by the member itself.

## Key Points

- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited holds BSE membership number 3299
- Closure applies specifically to Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: Friday, January 9, 2026
- Nature of closure: Voluntary business cessation
- All trading members have been informed of this development

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the closure for awareness
- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited must cease currency derivative operations from the effective date
- Members requiring clarification should contact membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 9, 2026 (Friday) - Currency derivative segment operations cease

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal as this affects only one trading member's operations in a specific segment. Other market participants and segments remain unaffected. The voluntary nature indicates an orderly closure process. Trading members are notified for informational purposes to update their records regarding active participants in the currency derivative segment.
