## Summary

BSE has issued notice (No. 20260310-6) informing trading members that NCL Seccolor Ltd, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026. This action is pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Post removal, the shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board of BSE.

## Key Points

- NCL Seccolor Ltd (CIN: U21029TG1986PTC007033) is being removed from the BSE Dissemination Board
- Removal is effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026
- The action is in pursuance of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016
- After removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will not be available on the Dissemination Board
- Registered address: 10-3-162, 5th Floor, NCL Pearl, Sarojini Devi Road, Secunderabad, Telangana - 500 026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements the existing SEBI framework for exclusively listed companies under circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the treatment of ELCs on the Dissemination Board.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trades in NCL Seccolor Ltd shares on the Dissemination Board will not be possible from March 11, 2026 onwards
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company (M Ravindra, Company Secretary, companysecretary@nclbuildtek.com) or BSE (Mr. Parag Jain, Manager - Listing Compliance & Operations, Ph: 022-2272 8685, direct.listing@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Date of Removal:** Wednesday, March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The removal of NCL Seccolor Ltd from the BSE Dissemination Board has limited market impact as it affects only shareholders and traders dealing in this single exclusively listed company. Once removed, shares will no longer be tradeable on the BSE Dissemination Board. Existing shareholders should be aware of this change and take necessary action before the effective date if required.
