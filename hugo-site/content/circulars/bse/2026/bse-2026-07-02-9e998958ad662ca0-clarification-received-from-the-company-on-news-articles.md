## Summary

The BSE, as part of its surveillance function, sought clarification from Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 505537) regarding news articles that appeared in various newspapers, websites, and television channels, specifically referencing a report from The Economic Times (economictimes.indiatimes.com). The company's reply has been received and is attached to the notice for members and investors.

## Key Points

- The Exchange routinely seeks clarifications from listed companies to verify the accuracy of news reports circulating in media
- Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 505537) was asked to clarify a news article published by The Economic Times
- The company has submitted its reply, which is attached to this notice
- The notice was issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance Services (DOSS)
- Signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Arpita Joshi (Manager, Surveillance)

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from investors or market participants. Listed companies are required to respond promptly to exchange queries regarding news articles under standard disclosure obligations.

## Important Dates

Notice Date: 02 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine clarification notice with limited direct market impact. It informs investors that the exchange has verified the accuracy of a specific news report concerning Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd. Investors should refer to the attached company reply for specific details on the clarification provided.
