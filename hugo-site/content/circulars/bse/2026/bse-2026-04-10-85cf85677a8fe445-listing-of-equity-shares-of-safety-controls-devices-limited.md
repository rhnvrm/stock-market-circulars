## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Safety Controls & Devices Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment under scrip code 544746 and symbol SCDL with ISIN INE0UMH01018. The specific listing date and further securities details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Safety Controls & Devices Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544746 | Symbol: SCDL | ISIN: INE0UMH01018
- Exact listing date and security details to be announced through a subsequent notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to note the forthcoming listing of SCDL on the SME segment and await the separate notice that will confirm the exact listing date and full security details before initiating any dealings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 10, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME segment listing announcement with low immediate market impact. The circular serves as an advance notification to trading members. Market participants interested in Safety Controls & Devices Limited should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and tradeable security details.
