## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260304-5 informing trading members that Mizuho Capsave Finance Private Limited has fixed a Record Date of March 11, 2026 for the purpose of Part Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Payment of Interest. Trading in these debentures will continue with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: Mizuho Capsave Finance Private Limited
- Debenture Series: MCFPL-7.35%-25-9-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE0DBJ07226 (Scrip Code: 977154)
- Record Date: March 11, 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value post-redemption: Rs. 83,332/- per debenture
- Effective Date of Reduced Face Value: March 11, 2026
- Settlement Reference: DR-837/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notification under existing BSE framework for debt segment instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from March 11, 2026, all trades in the specified debenture (ISIN INE0DBJ07226) must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 83,332/- per debenture.
- Members should update their systems to account for the changed face value effective the settlement date DR-837/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** March 11, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-837/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt corporate action with limited market impact. It affects only holders and traders of the specific NCD series (MCFPL-7.35%-25-9-28-PVT, ISIN INE0DBJ07226). Post part-redemption, the face value per debenture will reduce to Rs. 83,332/-, which will affect pricing and settlement calculations for any trades executed on or after March 11, 2026. Broader market impact is negligible as this is a privately placed instrument.
