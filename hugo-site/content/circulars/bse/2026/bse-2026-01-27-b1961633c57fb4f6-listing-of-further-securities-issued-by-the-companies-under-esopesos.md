## Summary

BSE has listed 269,610 additional equity shares issued by companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). The listing includes four distinct share ranges with varying lock-in requirements, where approximately 36% of the shares (155,610 shares) are subject to a one-month lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 269,610 equity shares
- Four separate share ranges listed with different lock-in statuses
- Share range 1: 188,400 shares (no lock-in)
- Share range 2: 86,000 shares (lock-in applicable)
- Share range 3: 97,600 shares (no lock-in)
- Share range 4: 86,010 shares (lock-in applicable)
- Lock-in period: January 15, 2026 to February 14, 2026 (one month)
- Share number ranges: 12528959772 to 12529229381

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

N/A - This is an informational circular regarding completed listing activity. No specific compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Lock-in start date: January 15, 2026
- Lock-in end date: February 14, 2026
- Circular date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing represents a relatively small number of shares issued under employee stock option plans. The partial lock-in on 155,610 shares (approximately 58% of the total) will temporarily restrict their trading until mid-February 2026. This is a routine corporate action with no significant implications for overall market operations or liquidity.
