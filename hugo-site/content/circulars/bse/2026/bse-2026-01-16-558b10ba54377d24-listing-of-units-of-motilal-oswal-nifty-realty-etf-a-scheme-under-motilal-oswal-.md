## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Motilal Oswal Nifty Realty ETF units effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The open-ended scheme replicates the Nifty Realty TR Index and will trade in the 'B' Group. A total of 720,000 units with face value of Rs.10 per unit and issue price of Rs.84.96 per unit were allotted on March 15, 2024. Trading will be in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.
