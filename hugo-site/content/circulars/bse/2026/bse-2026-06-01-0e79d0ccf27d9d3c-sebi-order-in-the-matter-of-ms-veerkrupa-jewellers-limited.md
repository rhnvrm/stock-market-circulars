## Summary

SEBI, vide order no. QJA/SS/IVD-2/ID13/32429/2026-27 dated May 29, 2026, has restrained 17 individuals and entities from accessing the securities market in connection with M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited. The restraint periods range from 3 to 5 years and take effect immediately from the date of the order.

## Key Points

- 11 noticees (Nos. 1–11) are restrained for 5 years, including M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited, M/s Vivid Mercantile Limited, and M/s First Overseas Capital Limited.
- 6 noticees (Nos. 12–17) are restrained for 3 years, including M/s NNM Securities Private Limited and M/s Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited.
- All restrained parties are prohibited from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities directly or indirectly.
- Noticees with open derivative positions may close/square off within 3 months from the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Existing pay-in/pay-out obligations for transactions executed before the order date may be settled.
- For Noticees 6, 16, and 17, the new restraint period runs consecutively after prior SEBI restraint orders (October 23, 2025 and May 30, 2024 respectively).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's investigative authority, issuing specific directional orders against named individuals and entities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must take note of the restrained entities and ensure these parties are not permitted to trade or access the securities market.
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the prohibition on dealing in securities for all 17 named noticees.
- Members should monitor for any association of restrained entities with securities market activities in any manner.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Immediate (from May 29, 2026)
- **Derivative Position Close-out Deadline:** Within 3 months of order date (by August 29, 2026) or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier
- **BSE Notice Date:** June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly restricts 17 entities and individuals from participating in securities markets for multi-year periods. Trading members must immediately block these parties from market access. The consecutive nature of restraints on Noticees 6, 16, and 17 suggests repeat regulatory violations. The case involves M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited and an interconnected network of individuals and corporate entities, indicating a coordinated enforcement action likely related to market manipulation or fraudulent trading practices.
