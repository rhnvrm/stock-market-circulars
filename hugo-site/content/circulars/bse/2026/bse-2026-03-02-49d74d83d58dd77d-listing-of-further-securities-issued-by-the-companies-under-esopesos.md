## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that further new securities issued by 10 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from **Wednesday, March 4, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have had new ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective March 4, 2026
- All newly listed shares have **no lock-in period** (NA)
- The largest issuance is by **HDFC Bank Limited** with 906,722 shares (face value ₹1)
- **ICICI Bank Limited** issued 376,461 shares (face value ₹2)
- **RBL Bank Limited** issued 235,707 shares (face value ₹10)
- **Wipro Limited** issued 129,574 shares (face value ₹2)
- Smaller issuances from Aarti Pharmalabs (10,457), Balaji Telefilms (25,000), Carborundum Universal (16,702), Indegene (13,332), Quess Corp (14,918), and Solara Active Pharma Sciences (1,500)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice regarding the listing of securities already issued under approved ESOP/ESOS schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective ISIN numbers and distribution ranges for settlement and trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026
