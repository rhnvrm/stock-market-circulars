## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that several companies have fixed Record Dates for payment of interest and/or redemption of their listed Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Bonds. Trading Members are advised not to deal in the debentures/bonds of these companies around the specified record and ex-dates, as trading in these instruments will be suspended for settlement purposes.

- Notice covers multiple NCD/Bond issuances across several companies, each identified by Scrip Code and ISIN
- Krazybee Services Ltd — KSL-10.95%-23-7-26-PVT (ISIN: INE07HK07791) — Record/Ex-Date: 08/07/2026
- Kosamattam Finance Limited — KFL-28-7-26-NCD (ISIN: INE403Q07DM0) — Record/Ex-Date: 13/07/2026
- Sammaan Capital Limited — five NCD series (9.40%, 9.90%, 9.02%, 9.48%, Zero Coupon), ISINs INE148I07ON3, INE148I07OR4, INE148I07OQ6, INE148I07OP8, INE148I07OS2 — Record/Ex-Date: 13/07/2026

- Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited — TIAPL-8.70%-28-7-26-PVT (ISIN: INE723X07109) — Record/Ex-Date: 13/07/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine periodic notice under BSE's Listing Operations/Corporate Actions process for debt instruments, issued ahead of scheduled interest payment and/or redemption dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the listed debentures/bonds of the specified companies on and around the stated Record Date/Ex-Date for each ISIN.
- Back-office and settlement systems should be updated to reflect the applicable Settlement/DR numbers referenced for each instrument (e.g., DR-667/2026-2027, DR-670/2026-2027, DR-671/2026-2027, DR-672/2026-2027, DR-673/2026-2027).

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only trading in the specific listed debt instruments (NCDs/bonds) of the named companies around their interest payment/redemption record dates. It has no bearing on equity trading or broader market activity, and is a standard operational advisory for debt-segment Trading Members and bondholders to ensure accurate settlement of interest and redemption proceeds.
