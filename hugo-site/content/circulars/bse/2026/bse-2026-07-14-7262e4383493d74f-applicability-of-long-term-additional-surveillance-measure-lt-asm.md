## Summary

BSE has issued an updated list of securities subject to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, and June 04, 2021. The notice details securities newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, securities being transferred to restricted trading groups (T/XT/MT/TS), and securities being removed from the framework, along with associated margin and price band changes. Detailed lists of affected securities are provided in Annexures I, II, and III (not reproduced in this notice text).

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) will face 100% margin requirements on all open and new positions.
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) will move to higher stages within the LT-ASM framework.
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will remain in the LT-ASM framework but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups.
- Securities in Annexure II will be removed from the LT-ASM framework.
- Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- LT-ASM measures apply in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under this framework is purely a surveillance action and is not to be construed as adverse action against any company.
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework will revert to pre-shortlisting levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice implements the revised LT-ASM framework as established through prior BSE circulars, applying escalated surveillance (100% margins, group transfer, price band revision) to specific securities based on periodic review.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members must apply 100% margin requirements on affected securities from the effective date and adjust trading behavior for securities moved to restricted groups (T/XT/MT/TS). Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Lower price band applicable; securities move out of LT-ASM framework (Annexure II); securities move to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I Part B) effective.
- July 16, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions.
- July 17, 2026: 100% margin applicable on all open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups.

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM measures significantly increase the cost of trading (100% margin) and restrict trading mechanisms (group transfer) for the affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and increasing volatility in these stocks. Investors and traders holding positions in the listed securities should review the annexures to determine specific impact and adjust risk exposure ahead of the effective dates.
