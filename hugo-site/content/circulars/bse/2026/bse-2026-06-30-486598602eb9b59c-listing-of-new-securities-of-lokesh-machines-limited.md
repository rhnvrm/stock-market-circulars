## Summary

BSE has listed 13,00,000 new equity shares of Lokesh Machines Limited (Scrip Code: 532740) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 13,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 171.71/- (Issue Price: Rs. 181.71/-)
- Shares allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- ISIN: INE397H01017
- Distribution Numbers: 19996771 to 21296770
- Date of Allotment: May 6, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the newly listed shares are available for trading from July 1, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified below.

## Important Dates

- **Effective trading date:** July 1, 2026
- **Date of allotment:** May 6, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (6,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 19996771–20596770):** December 30, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (7,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 20596771–21296770):** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing results in a modest increase in the tradeable float of Lokesh Machines Limited. However, the majority of the newly issued shares (all 13,00,000) are subject to lock-in until end of December 2026, limiting immediate selling pressure. Market impact is expected to be minimal in the near term.
