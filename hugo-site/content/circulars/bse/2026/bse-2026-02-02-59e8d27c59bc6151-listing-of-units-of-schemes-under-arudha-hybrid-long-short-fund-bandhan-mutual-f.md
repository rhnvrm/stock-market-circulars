## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of four schemes under Arudha Hybrid Long-Short Fund, managed by Bandhan AMC Limited, effective from February 3, 2026. The fund employs an interval investment strategy investing in equity and debt securities, including limited short exposure through derivatives. All schemes will be listed in the 'B' Group and traded in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Four schemes listed: Regular Plan-Growth, Regular Plan-Monthly IDCW, Direct Plan-Growth, and Direct Plan-Half yearly IDCW
- All units have a face value of Rs. 10.00 per unit and issue price of Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Date of allotment: January 28, 2026
- Trading effective date: February 3, 2026
- Investment Manager: Bandhan AMC Limited
- Registrar: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS)
- All units must be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market lot: 1 unit for all schemes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, mandating that mutual fund units be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these schemes are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (i.e., one unit)
- Trading members should contact the listed persons for any clarifications regarding the listing
