## Summary

BSE has observed significant price/volume movement in a set of listed scrips and, as part of its surveillance process, sought clarifications from the respective companies to ensure trading members and investors have access to relevant information. Replies have been received from eight companies and are attached to this notice, while clarifications remain pending from six other companies.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume activity in 14 scrips and wrote to each company seeking an explanation.
- Companies that have responded (replies attached):
  1. Arkade Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 544261)
  2. Dhanashree Electronics Ltd (Scrip Code: 542679)
  3. Digitide Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 544413)
  4. National Standard (India) Ltd (Scrip Code: 504882)
  5. NIIT Learning Systems Ltd (Scrip Code: 543952)
  6. SG Mart Ltd (Scrip Code: 512329)
  7. Taj GVK Hotels & Resorts Ltd (Scrip Code: 532390)
  8. Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri Ltd (Scrip Code: 534369)
- Companies whose replies are still awaited:
  1. CMX Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 532217)
  2. LE Lavoir Ltd (Scrip Code: 539814)
  3. Mapro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 509762)
  4. SC Agrotech Ltd (Scrip Code: 526081)
  5. Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 539117)
  6. VJTF Eduservices Ltd (Scrip Code: 509026)
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS), signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a rule change.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies listed under "reply awaited" are expected to respond to BSE's clarification request. Trading members and investors should review the attached replies for context before trading in the affected scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 08 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260708-21). No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This notice provides transparency on price/volume anomalies for the named scrips and may influence short-term trading sentiment for these specific stocks, particularly those where clarifications are still pending. It does not carry broader market-wide implications.
