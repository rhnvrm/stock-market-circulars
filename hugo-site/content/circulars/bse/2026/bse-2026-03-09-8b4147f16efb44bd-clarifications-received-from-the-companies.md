## Summary

BSE received and published clarifications from three listed companies — Kirloskar Brothers Ltd, Loyal Equipments Ltd (Scrip: unlisted in letter but Gujarat-based ASME-certified company), and Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 543615) — in response to BSE surveillance queries regarding significant price movements in their respective securities. All three companies have stated that the price movements are purely market-driven and that there is no undisclosed or price-sensitive information pending disclosure.

## Key Points

- Loyal Equipments Ltd responded to BSE surveillance letter ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/3409 dated around 07/03/2026, confirming compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- Loyal Equipments Ltd confirmed no undisclosed/price-sensitive information or impending corporate action that could explain the price movement.
- Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd (formerly Silicon Rental Solutions Private Limited, CIN: L74999MH2016PLC272442, Scrip Code: 543615) responded under Regulation 30(10) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd confirmed no pending price-sensitive information or announcements that could have caused significant price movement.
- Kirloskar Brothers Ltd also submitted a clarification (content partially redacted in source).
- All companies assured exchanges of continued timely disclosure of material events as required under applicable regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. These are responses to BSE surveillance queries under the existing framework of Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding significant price movements under Regulation 30(10) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- All three companies have confirmed ongoing compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and committed to timely disclosure of all material events.

## Important Dates

- Loyal Equipments Ltd clarification date: 07/03/2026
- Silicon Rental Solutions Ltd clarification date: 07th March, 2026
- BSE circular publication date: 09/03/2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are standard surveillance-driven clarifications with no corporate actions, earnings surprises, or regulatory penalties involved. The disclosures serve to inform market participants that the recent price movements in KIRLOSBROS, Loyal Equipments, and Silicon Rental Solutions (543615) are not backed by any undisclosed fundamental developments. Investors should treat the price movements as speculative or sentiment-driven until further company disclosures.
