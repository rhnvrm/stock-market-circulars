## Summary

BSE Index Services announced additions to BSE indices following the listing of three newly issued stocks. Alpine Texworld Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544825) and SBI Funds Management Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544829), both listed on BSE effective July 21, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO index effective the open of trading on July 23, 2026. Additionally, Millworks Technologies Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544826), listed on the SME platform of BSE effective July 21, 2026, will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective the open of trading on July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Alpine Texworld Limited (544825) added to BSE IPO index, referencing Notice No. 20260720-17
- SBI Funds Management Limited (544829) added to BSE IPO index, referencing Notice No. 20260720-31
- Millworks Technologies Limited (544826) added to BSE SME IPO index, referencing Notice No. 20260720-24
- All three stocks began trading on BSE effective July 21, 2026
- These index actions will be reflected in the night's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine index maintenance action following new listings, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action required from market participants. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO index or BSE SME IPO index should ensure portfolio rebalancing reflects these additions on the specified effective dates. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Alpine Texworld Limited, SBI Funds Management Limited, and Millworks Technologies Limited listed on BSE/BSE SME
- July 22, 2026: Millworks Technologies Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (effective at market open)
- July 23, 2026: Alpine Texworld Limited and SBI Funds Management Limited added to BSE IPO index (effective at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low direct market impact; this is a routine administrative index update following recent IPO listings. Impact is primarily relevant to passive funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE IPO or BSE SME IPO indices, which will need to adjust holdings to reflect the additions on the stated effective dates.
