## Summary

BSE Surveillance issued queries (Ref. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SG/2025-2026/947, 948, 949) on March 2, 2026, to three listed companies seeking clarification on significant increases in trading volume of their equity shares. All three companies — Avadh Sugar & Energy Ltd., Polyplex Corporation Ltd., and Sarda Energy & Minerals Ltd. — responded confirming full compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 and stated that no undisclosed material events exist.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance queried three companies on the same date (March 2, 2026) regarding unusual volume surges in their equity shares.
- Avadh Sugar & Energy Ltd. (CIN: L15122UP2015PLC069635) stated volume increase appears to be purely market-driven with no pending material disclosures.
- Polyplex Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 524051, CIN: L25209UR1984PLC011596) confirmed all price-sensitive information has been filed timely and nothing remains pending.
- Sarda Energy & Minerals Ltd. (Security Code: 504614) confirmed ongoing compliance with disclosure obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR.
- All three companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 and other applicable provisions of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- Companies committed to intimating stock exchanges promptly if any future disclosable event arises.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. These are company responses to BSE Surveillance queries under the existing surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must continue to disclose material information/events under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 within prescribed timelines.
- Any future events requiring disclosure must be intimated to stock exchanges in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements.

## Important Dates

- March 2, 2026: BSE Surveillance emails issued (Ref. Nos. 947, 948, 949) and company responses submitted on the same date.

## Impact Assessment

No material market impact expected. All three companies have confirmed there are no undisclosed price-sensitive developments driving the volume increases, characterizing the movements as market-driven. Investors and market participants should note that BSE Surveillance's queries are a routine monitoring mechanism, and the companies' responses indicate no fundamental or regulatory trigger behind the recent volume surges.
