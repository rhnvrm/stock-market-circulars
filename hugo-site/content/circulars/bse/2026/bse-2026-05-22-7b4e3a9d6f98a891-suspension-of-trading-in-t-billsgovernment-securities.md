## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three T-bills/Government Securities will mature on their redemption dates and trading in them is suspended effective May 25, 2026. Members are advised not to deal in these securities from that date onwards (DR-637/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in three T-bills effective May 25, 2026
- Securities are maturing at their scheduled redemption dates
- Scrip codes affected: 805072 (364-day T-bill), 805149 (182-day T-bill), 805184 (91-day T-bill)
- No attachment; standard operational notice

## Securities Affected

| Sr. No. | Scrip Code | Particulars | ISIN |
|---------|------------|-------------|------|
| 1 | 805072 | 364TB28526 | IN002025Z096 |
| 2 | 805149 | 182TB28526 | IN002025Y354 |
| 3 | 805184 | 91TB280526 | IN002025X471 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under the exchange's standard procedure for maturing government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the above-mentioned T-bills/Government Securities with effect from May 25, 2026. Members holding these securities should note the impending redemption and refrain from executing any trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Suspension Effective From:** May 25, 2026
- **Redemption Reference:** DR-637/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. The three T-bills are approaching their scheduled maturity; suspension of trading is a standard pre-redemption procedure. Holders will receive par value upon redemption. No broader market disruption is expected.
