## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of revisions to the daily price band (circuit filter) applicable to eight scrips, effective from July 24, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% for stricter scrips to 10% for less restricted ones, reflecting the Exchange's ongoing volatility surveillance process.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260723-33 dated July 23, 2026, issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS).
- Applicable to the Equity segment, effective July 24, 2026.
- Price bands revised as follows:
  - 7Nr Retail Ltd (Scrip Code 540615) — revised to 2%
  - Aspira Pathlab & Diagnostics Ltd (Scrip Code 540788) — revised to 2%
  - Elitecon International Ltd (Scrip Code 539533) — revised to 10%
  - Lippi Systems Ltd (Scrip Code 526604) — revised to 2%
  - Panacea Biotec Ltd (Scrip Code 531349) — revised to 5%
  - Polymac Thermoformers Ltd (Scrip Code 537573) — revised to 5%
  - Seemax Resources Ltd (Scrip Code 544813) — revised to 5%
  - Sonalis Consumer Products Ltd (Scrip Code 543924) — revised to 5%

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine surveillance-driven adjustment to the applicable price band (circuit filter) percentage for the listed scrips, as part of BSE's ongoing monitoring under its volatility and surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for these scrips from the effective date and ensure client orders comply with the new limits. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 23, 2026
- Effective Date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will directly constrain the maximum permissible intraday price movement for the eight named scrips. Tighter bands (2%) for 7Nr Retail, Aspira Pathlab and Lippi Systems indicate closer surveillance and reduced volatility tolerance, while the wider 10% band for Elitecon International signals comparatively relaxed monitoring. Traders and market participants in these scrips should adjust order placement strategies accordingly to avoid rejections beyond the new limits.
