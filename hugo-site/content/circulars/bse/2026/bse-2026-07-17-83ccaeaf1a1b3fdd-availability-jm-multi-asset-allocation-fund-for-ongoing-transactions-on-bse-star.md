## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260624-2 dated June 24, 2026, the JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP, STP and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund transactions resume on BSE StAR MF Platform from July 20, 2026.
- Six scheme variants are covered, spanning Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- All schemes are categorized as Equity under the JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund.
- STP investment amount ranges and minimum installment requirements have been specified by JM Financial AMC.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice reinstating trading availability for a specific mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members handling JM Mutual Fund transactions should update their systems to reflect resumed availability of the listed ISINs and ensure STP transactions comply with the specified amount ranges and minimum installment counts:
- Monthly/Weekly STP of Rs. 500 to Rs. 999: minimum 12 installments
- Monthly/Weekly STP of Rs. 1,000 and above: minimum 6 installments

## Important Dates

- June 24, 2026: Original notice (20260624-2) referenced
- July 17, 2026: Current notice issued
- July 20, 2026: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP transactions

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update relevant to mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and platform members facilitating BSE StAR MF transactions. It restores full transaction capability for JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund investors but does not affect listed equity securities or broader market operations.
