## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further equity shares issued by four companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ISIN: INE674K01013, Scrip Code: 540691): 290,715 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 2735816481 to 2736107195
- Kalpataru Limited (ISIN: INE227J01012, Scrip Code: 544423): 8,100 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 205913994 to 205922093
- Karnataka Bank Limited (ISIN: INE614B01018, Scrip Code: 532652): 74,042 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 378229886 to 378303927
- R Systems International Limited (ISIN: INE411H01032, Scrip Code: 532735): 6,213 shares of face value ₹1, distinctive numbers 166501453 to 166507665
- None of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing approval under existing BSE listing procedures for shares allotted pursuant to ESOP/ESOS exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members beyond noting the increase in listed share capital for the respective companies. Companies must ensure ISIN records and shareholding disclosures reflect the additional allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 24, 2026
- Effective Listing/Trading Date: Monday, July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The additional shares represent a marginal increase in the free-float equity capital of each company arising from employee stock option exercises. Given the small quantum relative to each company's total outstanding shares, the market impact is expected to be minimal, with no material effect on stock price or trading liquidity.
