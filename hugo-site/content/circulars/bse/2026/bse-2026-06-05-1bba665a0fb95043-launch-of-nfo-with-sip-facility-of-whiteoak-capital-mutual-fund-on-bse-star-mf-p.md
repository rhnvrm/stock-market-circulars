## Summary

BSE has notified members that WHITEOAK CAPITAL MUTUAL FUND is launching the WHITEOAK CAPITAL - AGGRESSIVE HYBRID FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 8, 2026, and closes on June 22, 2026, with SIP/XSIP facility available. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options are offered, with a tentative allotment date of June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 8, 2026 to June 22, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two scheme variants available: Regular Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AG6) and Direct Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF03VN01AF8)
- Both schemes are categorised as Equity (Aggressive Hybrid Fund)
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on June 22, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight on June 22, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be eligible for allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notice following existing BSE StAR MF operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 29, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payment instruments are accepted during the NFO period subject to the same fund-clearance deadline
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, provided funds are received before the allotment date
- Members must communicate the SIP/XSIP authentication deadline (3:00 PM, June 22, 2026) to clients

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026**: NFO opens
- **June 22, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication by clients
- **June 22, 2026 (midnight)**: NFO closes (lumpsum purchases extended until midnight)
- **June 29, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be available in ICCL account
- **June 30, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily affects BSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Members need to ensure operational readiness for the new NFO schemes (both ISIN codes) and communicate key deadlines to investors. The fund falls in the Aggressive Hybrid (Equity) category, making it suitable for investors seeking a mix of equity and debt exposure. No market-wide trading impact is expected; impact is limited to mutual fund distribution operations.
