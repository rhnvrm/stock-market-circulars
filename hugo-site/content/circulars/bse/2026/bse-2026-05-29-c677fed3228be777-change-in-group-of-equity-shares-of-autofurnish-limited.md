## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of Autofurnish Limited (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544767) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Friday, June 12, 2026. Dealings will subsequently be classified under M Group.

## Key Points

- Autofurnish Limited (Scrip Code: 544767) is currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment under MT Group
- Transfer to Rolling segment (M Group) is effective June 12, 2026
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260527-47 dated May 27, 2026
- Applicable segment: SME
- Contact for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Autofurnish Limited will move from the Trade for Trade (MT Group) settlement mechanism to the standard Rolling settlement (M Group) mechanism. This is a standard progression for SME IPO stocks after the initial mandatory Trade for Trade period.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and client-facing information to reflect the group change for Autofurnish Limited (Scrip Code: 544767) from the effective date of June 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 12, 2026 (transfer to Rolling segment / M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural change affecting only Autofurnish Limited (SME segment). The transition from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement increases trading flexibility for investors, as they are no longer required to deliver securities on the same day of purchase. Impact is limited to traders and investors holding or trading this specific SME scrip.
