## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Axis Mutual Fund will launch the Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 130 (92 Days) NFO on the BSE StAR MF Platform from March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026. Four scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW Payout options. Tentative allotment date is March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: March 09, 2026 to March 11, 2026
- Four scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth & IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth & IDCW Payout)
- All four schemes are categorized as DEBT with tentative allotment date of March 12, 2026
- NFO extended till 6:00 PM on March 11, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Transactions where clients have not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Pending transactions (with initiated payment) will remain in system till 3:00 PM on the last day of funds payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on March 12, 2026
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine NFO launch notification under the existing BSE StAR MF operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation of transactions
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on March 12, 2026 for unit allotment
- Cheque and OTM payments must result in clear funds reaching ICCL account by the stated deadline
