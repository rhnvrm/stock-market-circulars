## Summary

BSE (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a revised settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED. This notice revises the obligation download and settlement dates for Settlement No. 2026070, which covered a bidding period from June 10 to June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026070)
- Bidding window ran from 10 June 2026 to 23 June 2026
- Revised Obligation Download Date: 30 June 2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 01 July 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism (available at both depositories)
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026070 in the depository system

## Regulatory Changes

This notice revises the settlement schedule originally communicated in Notice No. 20260609-38. No new regulatory framework is introduced; it is an operational revision to existing settlement dates for this specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism at both depositories
- The Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026070 must be specified in all depository system entries
- Members should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 (dated May 28, 2020) for settlement guidelines under the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange
- Members must refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | 10 June 2026 |
| Bid End Date | 23 June 2026 |
| Revised Obligation Download | 30 June 2026 |
| Revised Settlement Date | 01 July 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This revision has a targeted operational impact on members and custodians participating in the IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED takeover acquisition window. The shift of obligation download to June 30 and settlement to July 1 requires affected parties to update their processing timelines accordingly. Broader market impact is minimal as this applies to a single settlement for a specific company's takeover offer.
