## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260623-35 dated June 23, 2026, announcing the postponement of the issue opening of Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited's SME IPO. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.

## Key Points

- The issue opening of Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited SME IPO has been postponed.
- Notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.
- Notice Number: 20260623-35, dated June 23, 2026.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice informing market participants of a scheduling change for the Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Investors and intermediaries involved in the Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited SME IPO should take note of the postponement and await further communication from BSE or the company regarding the revised issue opening date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Revised Issue Opening Date:** Not specified in this notice; further communication expected.

## Impact Assessment

The postponement of the Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited SME IPO issue opening directly affects prospective retail and institutional investors who had planned to subscribe during the originally scheduled period. Intermediaries such as brokers and registrars involved in the IPO process will need to update their timelines accordingly. The broader market impact is limited given the SME segment classification of this IPO.
