## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260421-3) informing trading members that T-bill 182TB23426 (Scrip Code: 805134, ISIN: IN002025Y305) will mature on its redemption date. Trading in this security is suspended with effect from April 22, 2026 (DR-615/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to T-bill 182TB23426 (Scrip Code: 805134, ISIN: IN002025Y305)
- Suspension is effective from April 22, 2026
- The suspension is due to the security reaching its maturity/redemption date
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date
- Reference: DR-615/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice in accordance with existing BSE rules for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading in Scrip Code 805134 (ISIN: IN002025Y305) from April 22, 2026 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in the aforesaid T-bill after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 22, 2026 (redemption/maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or trading in 182TB23426. As the security is maturing, holders will receive redemption proceeds as scheduled. This is a routine administrative action with no adverse market impact; it simply closes out the trading lifecycle of the instrument on the exchange.
