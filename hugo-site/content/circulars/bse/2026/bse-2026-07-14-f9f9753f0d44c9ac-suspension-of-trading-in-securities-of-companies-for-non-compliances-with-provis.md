## Summary

BSE has notified that trading in the securities of Advance Syntex Ltd (Scrip Code: 539982) will be suspended with effect from August 14, 2026, due to non-compliance with Regulation 31 (Submission of Shareholding Pattern) of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, for two consecutive quarters — December 2025 and March 2026. This action follows the SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated July 11, 2023 (last updated January 30, 2026) on penal actions for non-compliance with LODR provisions.

## Key Points

- Advance Syntex Ltd (Scrip 539982) failed to submit its shareholding pattern for two consecutive quarters: December 2025 and March 2026.
- Trading suspension is scheduled to take effect on August 14, 2026, unless the company complies before the cutoff.
- The entire shareholding of the promoter, along with all other securities held in the promoter's demat account(s), will be frozen during the suspension period.
- If suspension proceeds, trading will resume only on a Trade for Trade basis in Z group, restricted to the first trading day of each week, for six months, 15 days after the suspension is effected.
- Revocation of suspension will require the company to comply with the prescribed procedure and all extant norms for revocation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice enforces existing provisions under Regulation 31 of the SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, and the SEBI Master Circular's Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading of specified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Advance Syntex Ltd must submit its shareholding pattern for the pending quarters (December 2025 and March 2026) to the satisfaction of the Exchange on or before August 11, 2026, to avoid suspension. Failure to comply will also require payment of applicable fines before suspension can be revoked, along with adherence to the full revocation procedure.

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2026**: Deadline for the company to comply with Regulation 31 requirements to avoid suspension.
- **August 14, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension if non-compliance continues.
- **15 days post-suspension**: Trade for Trade window in Z group begins, limited to the first trading day of each week for six months.

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders and market participants dealing in Advance Syntex Ltd securities face an imminent trading halt starting August 14, 2026, significantly reducing liquidity and marketability of the stock. Promoters will have their entire shareholding and demat holdings frozen, restricting any transfer or pledge activity. Investors should monitor the company's compliance status closely before the August 11, 2026 deadline, as continued non-compliance will materially impair the stock's tradability for an extended period under the restrictive Trade for Trade mechanism.
