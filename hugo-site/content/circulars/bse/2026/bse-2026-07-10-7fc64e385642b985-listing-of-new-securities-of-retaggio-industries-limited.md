## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 3,02,000 new equity shares of Retaggio Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544391) will be listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 26/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 16/-).

## Key Points

- New securities: 3,02,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Issue price: Rs. 26/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 16/- premium)
- Distinctive numbers: 17866161 to 18168160
- ISIN: INE0KWJ01014
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of allotment: 12/03/2026
- Lock-in applicable on all 3,02,000 shares until 23/02/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the additional listed quantity and note the lock-in status of the newly listed shares until 23/02/2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 12/03/2026
- Listing/Trading effective from: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 23/02/2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action involving a relatively small share count relative to typical listed float, arising from warrant conversion. Impact on overall trading and liquidity is expected to be minimal, though it marginally increases the non-promoter shareholding base of Retaggio Industries Limited. The lock-in on these shares until February 2027 limits any near-term supply overhang.
