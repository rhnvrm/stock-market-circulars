## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities meeting the criteria have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I or Stage II, with enhanced margin requirements taking effect on June 18, 2026. This is in continuation of the revised ASM framework notices issued between 2018 and 2024.

## Key Points

- Securities in **ST-ASM Stage I**: Applicable margin rate raised to **50%** or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Securities in **ST-ASM Stage II**: Applicable margin rate raised to **100%** or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Enhanced margins apply to all **open positions as on June 17, 2026** and all **new positions created from June 18, 2026**.
- Full list of affected securities is in the attached Annexure I.
- ASM inclusion is a surveillance action and should **not** be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.
- ST-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Price band of scrips moving out of the framework will be reinstated to the band applicable before ASM shortlisting, subject to no other surveillance measure applying.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the list of securities under the ST-ASM framework as per provisions of Exchange notices 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024) concerning revision of the ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** must ensure enhanced margins are collected from clients holding open positions in the identified securities from June 18, 2026.
- Members should refer to **Annexure I** attached to the notice for the complete list of affected securities.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Notice date.
- **June 17, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **June 18, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements (ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II).

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the securities listed in Annexure I will face substantially higher margin requirements. ST-ASM Stage I securities require at least 50% margin, while Stage II securities require 100% margin, effectively requiring full cash backing for positions. This can trigger forced liquidations for participants unable to meet the enhanced margin calls, increasing volatility in the affected scrips. Market participants should review their portfolios against the Annexure I list before June 17, 2026 to assess exposure and plan margin funding accordingly.
