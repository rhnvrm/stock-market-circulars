## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SAI PRIMUS LIFEBIOTECH LIMITED, filed on June 27, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the notice date of June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SAI PRIMUS LIFEBIOTECH LIMITED on June 27, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website in accordance with ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from June 29, 2026 (deadline: July 20, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, analysts, and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days of the notice date to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed:** June 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact. It is a routine disclosure requirement for SME IPOs under ICDR Regulations, allowing the public to review and comment on the draft offer document of SAI PRIMUS LIFEBIOTECH LIMITED before the IPO proceeds. Impact is confined to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in this specific company's upcoming SME IPO.
