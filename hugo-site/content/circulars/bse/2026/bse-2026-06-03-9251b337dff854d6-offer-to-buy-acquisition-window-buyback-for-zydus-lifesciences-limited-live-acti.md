## Summary

BSE has published the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for Zydus Lifesciences Limited (ZYDUSLIFE, Scrip Code: 532321). The buyback window is open from Thursday, 4th June 2026 to Wednesday, 10th June 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 1,260 per share and an offer size of up to 87,30,158 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Zydus Lifesciences Limited | Symbol: ZYDUSLIFE | ISIN: INE010B01027 | Scrip Code: 532321
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 1,260.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 87,30,158 equity shares
- **Opening Date:** Thursday, 4th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Wednesday, 10th June 2026
- **Session Timings:** Bid Offering and Modifications/Cancellations from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. throughout the offer period
- **Take-up on last day (10th June):** Extended until 4:00 p.m.
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1.00 | Market Lot: 1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is an operational schedule circular under existing OTB framework. Members are directed to refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Only investors holding shares in demat form are eligible for bidding.
- Members and Custodians should note that End of Issue files on the Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted.
- For Leased Line URL access setup, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opens:** Thursday, 4th June 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Wednesday, 10th June 2026
- **Give-up / Take-up:** 4th June to 9th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Give-up (last day):** 10th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
- **Take-up (last day):** 10th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is primarily operational in nature, directed at Trading Members and Custodian Members participating in the Zydus Lifesciences buyback. The buyback offer of up to ~87.3 lakh shares at Rs. 1,260 per share represents a significant corporate action for ZYDUSLIFE shareholders. Investors holding ZYDUSLIFE shares in demat form may tender shares through their trading members during the specified window. The impact is moderate, limited to participants in this specific buyback offer.
