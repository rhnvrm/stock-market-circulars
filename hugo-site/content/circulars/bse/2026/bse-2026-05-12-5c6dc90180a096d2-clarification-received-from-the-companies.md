## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260512-24 on 12 May 2026 regarding significant price/volume movements observed in 15 scrips. The Exchange wrote to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Replies have been received from 9 companies, while 6 others are yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 15 listed scrips in the recent past
- The Exchange reached out to all 15 companies for clarification under its surveillance mandate
- 9 companies have submitted replies (attached as Replies1 and Replies2 to the notice)
- 6 companies' replies are still awaited
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose, Surveillance division

## Companies That Replied

1. ADC India Communications Ltd (Security Code: 523411)
2. Astal Laboratories Ltd (Security Code: 512600)
3. Black Box Ltd (Security Code: 500463)
4. Crystal Business System Ltd (Security Code: 540821)
5. Droneacharya Aerial Innovations Ltd (Security Code: 543713)
6. Jindal Worldwide Ltd (Security Code: 531543)
7. Mirc Electronics Ltd (Security Code: 500279)
8. Munjal Auto Industries Ltd (Security Code: 520059)
9. Quick Heal Technologies Ltd (Security Code: 539678)

## Companies Awaiting Reply

1. Adhata Global Ltd (Security Code: 531286)
2. Anuroop Packaging Ltd (Security Code: 542865)
3. Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd (Security Code: 511018)
4. Sheshadri Industries Ltd (Security Code: 539111)
5. Takyon Networks Ltd (Security Code: 544471)
6. ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (Security Code: 506640)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an informational notice disclosing the status of company clarifications sought by BSE under its standard surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 6 companies yet to respond (Adhata Global, Anuroop Packaging, Milgrey Finance, Sheshadri Industries, Takyon Networks, ZR2 Bioenergy) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached company replies before taking trading decisions in these scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 May 2026
- No specific deadlines mentioned for pending replies

## Impact Assessment

This notice serves as an investor protection measure by BSE's Surveillance team. The flagging of 15 scrips for unusual price/volume activity signals heightened regulatory scrutiny. Investors and traders in these securities should exercise caution and review the company-submitted clarifications (attached to the notice) before taking positions. Companies still awaiting submission of replies may face further surveillance action. No immediate trading restrictions are announced.
