## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) of JIO BLACKROCK Mutual Fund have been activated on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 25, 2026, pursuant to SEBI's Regulatory Framework for SIF (circular dated February 27, 2025). The PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND SIF - NFO scheme will be available for order routing from June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND_SIF is live on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 25, 2026
- Transactions for NRI and Minor investors are permitted under JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes
- NRI applications are NOT accepted from investors in: Democratic Republic of Korea, Iran, Myanmar, United States of America, and Canada
- PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND SIF - NFO scheme will go live for order routing from June 29, 2026
- Detailed transaction acceptance process is provided in Annexure III

## Regulatory Changes

This activation is in accordance with SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025, which established the Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF). The notice references three annexures:
- Annexure I: Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF)
- Annexure II: Monitoring of Minimum Investment Threshold under SIF
- Annexure III: Detailed Process for Transaction Acceptance under SIF on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure NRI applications from the five restricted countries (Democratic Republic of Korea, Iran, Myanmar, United States of America, Canada) are not accepted under JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes
- Members should familiarise themselves with the transaction acceptance process detailed in Annexure III before routing orders
- For queries, members may contact the Mutual Fund Helpdesk at 022-45720450 / 022-45720650 on business days between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m., or raise queries via the CRS portal

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **June 29, 2026**: PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND SIF - NFO scheme activated for order routing on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

This circular is primarily operational in nature and directly affects BSE members and distributors who route mutual fund transactions via the BSE StAR MF Platform. The introduction of JIO BLACKROCK's SIF products expands the range of specialized investment options available on the platform. The NRI country restriction list and transaction process guidelines require member compliance. Broader market impact is limited to participants in the mutual fund distribution ecosystem.
