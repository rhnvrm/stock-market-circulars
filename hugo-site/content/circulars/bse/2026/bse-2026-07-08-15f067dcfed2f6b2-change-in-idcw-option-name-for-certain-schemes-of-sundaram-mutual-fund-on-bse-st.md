## Summary

BSE has notified its members of a communication received from Sundaram Mutual Fund regarding a change in the IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) option name for two of its schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective July 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Sundaram Mutual Fund has renamed the 'Monthly IDCW' option to simply 'IDCW' for two schemes.
- Affected schemes: Sundaram Arbitrage Fund and Sundaram Overnight Fund.
- The change is reflected on the BSE StAR MF platform.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Sundaram AMC is attached to the circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a regulatory change but an operational update communicated by the AMC and disseminated by BSE for member awareness on the StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF platform members and distributors should update their systems/records to reflect the revised scheme option name to ensure accurate transaction processing for the affected schemes.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and purely administrative, affecting only the naming convention of the IDCW option for two Sundaram Mutual Fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform. There is no change to the underlying scheme structure, NAV, or investor holdings. Distributors and members should ensure their systems recognize the updated option name to avoid transaction processing errors.
