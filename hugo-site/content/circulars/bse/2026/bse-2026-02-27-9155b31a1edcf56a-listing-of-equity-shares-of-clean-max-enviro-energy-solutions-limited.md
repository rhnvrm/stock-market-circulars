## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-17 informing trading members that the equity shares of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The scrip has been assigned code 544717 with symbol CLEANMAX and ISIN INE647U01026. The precise listing date and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE.
- Scrip Code: **544717**, Symbol: **CLEANMAX**, ISIN: **INE647U01026**.
- Exact date of listing will be announced via a separate notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's standard listing process for newly admitted equity securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details and await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date.
- Members should update their systems with the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN ahead of the listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals an imminent listing of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited on BSE. The company operates in the clean/renewable energy sector, which may attract investor interest aligned with ESG and green energy themes. Market participants, particularly those tracking new listings or the energy sector, should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and any additional security details.
