## Summary

Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (BSE: 533632 | NSE: ONELIFECAP) has extended the closing date of its ongoing Rights Issue following a Rights Issue Committee meeting held on March 2, 2026. The issue closing date has been pushed from March 6, 2026 to March 16, 2026. Additionally, the last date for on-market renunciation has been extended from March 2, 2026 to March 10, 2026, providing eligible shareholders more time to apply or renounce their rights entitlements.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue opening date remains unchanged: Monday, February 23, 2026
- Issue closing date extended by 10 days: from Friday, March 6, 2026 to Monday, March 16, 2026
- Last date for on-market renunciation extended: from Monday, March 2, 2026 to Tuesday, March 10, 2026
- Last date for receiving requests for Application Form and Rights Entitlement Letter also extended to Tuesday, March 10, 2026
- No changes to the terms and conditions of the Letter of Offer (LOF), Abridged Letter of Offer (ALOF), or Application Form
- An addendum to the LOF with the revised timeline will be published on company and exchange websites
- Rights Issue Committee meeting was held on March 2, 2026 (1:45 PM – 2:15 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This extension is made under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The company has disclosed the revised schedule as a material event. No regulatory framework changes are involved; this is a company-level scheduling adjustment within the existing rights issue framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is required to publish the addendum to the Letter of Offer on its website (www.onelifecapital.in), the Registrar's website (www.kfintech.com), and the BSE and NSE websites
- Eligible shareholders must submit their applications or renunciation requests by the revised deadlines
- No additional compliance obligations for brokers or other market participants beyond updated timeline awareness

## Important Dates

|---|---|---|

| Last Date for On-Market Renunciation | Monday, Mar 2, 2026 | Tuesday, Mar 10, 2026 |
| Last Date for Application Form / RE Letter Requests | Monday, Mar 2, 2026 | Tuesday, Mar 10, 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | Friday, Mar 6, 2026 | Monday, Mar 16, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides eligible equity shareholders of Onelife Capital Advisors an additional 10-day window to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce their entitlements on the market. This is a moderate-impact event limited to existing shareholders of the company. The broader market impact is minimal as no terms, pricing, or entitlement ratios have changed. Shareholders who had already applied are not affected. The extension may improve subscription levels for the issue by allowing more time for shareholder participation.
