## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713). The offer will be open from January 2-15, 2026, allowing investors to tender up to 8,58,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 12.15 per share. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: OSCARGLO, ISIN: INE473F01010, Scrip Code: 530713)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 2-15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)
- **Investor Categories**: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Non-Institutional), CO (Company), IND (Individual)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational details for a specific takeover offer following existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Bidding Eligibility**: Trading members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use designated URLs for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet access (both Internet and Leased Line)
- **File Availability**: Acceptance confirmation files on Extranet will be available only after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- **No Securities Accepted**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 2, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 2-15, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2-14, 2026
- **Last Day for Give-up**: January 15, 2026
- **Last Day for Take-up (Extended Hours)**: January 15, 2026 (until 4:00 PM)
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only OSCAR GLOBAL LTD shareholders. The offer size of 8.58 lakh shares represents a relatively small transaction.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians need to ensure their systems and login credentials for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms are functional. Contact information provided for technical support (Trading Operations: 022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com) and specific contacts for OTB module queries (Sandeep Pujari, Rushi Bhoir, Hormazdiar Mobedji).

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to OSCAR GLOBAL LTD shareholders who wish to tender their shares at Rs. 12.15 per share during the offer period.
