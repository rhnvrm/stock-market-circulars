## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,62,000 new equity shares of Cospower Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543172) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, March 12, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters at a premium of Rs. 980/- per share (issue price Rs. 990/-) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,62,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 980/- (issue price Rs. 990/-)
- Issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0CQB01010
- Distinctive numbers: 1674501 to 1836500
- Date of Allotment: 09/02/2026
- Trading permitted from: March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities. All 1,62,000 shares are subject to lock-in until 30/09/2026 and should be traded accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 09/02/2026
- **Trading Commencement:** 12/03/2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 30/09/2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 1,62,000 shares represents a modest increase in the tradeable float of Cospower Engineering Limited. All newly listed shares carry a lock-in restriction until September 30, 2026, further limiting near-term liquidity impact. Relevant only to existing shareholders and traders dealing in Scrip Code 543172.
