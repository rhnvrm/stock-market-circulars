## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for securities that have satisfied the inclusion criteria under Stage I and Stage II of the ASM framework. The enhanced margin requirements become effective June 25, 2026, applicable to all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions created from June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- The ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company
- Specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to Exchange notice nos. 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024) regarding revision of the ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the revised margin rates for affected securities from June 25, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I: Minimum 50% margin on all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions from June 25, 2026 (max cap: 100%)
- ST-ASM Stage II: Minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions from June 25, 2026 (max cap: 100%)
- Price Band upon exit from the framework shall revert to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure
- Members with clarifications may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 24, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margins
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I face a minimum 50% margin requirement, while those under Stage II face 100% margin, significantly increasing the cost of holding or building positions. This can reduce speculative activity, lower liquidity, and increase trading costs for market participants holding these securities. The measure is a standard surveillance tool applied to securities exhibiting abnormal price or volume behaviour. The affected securities are detailed in the separately attached Annexure I.
