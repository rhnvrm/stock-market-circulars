## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL), invites offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 649, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-649/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Friday, June 12, 2026, with pay-in on Monday, June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 12, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): Monday, June 15, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement will be processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member following the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements in which they short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The circular reiterates that under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and the earlier restriction prohibiting trading members from offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must verify the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must take utmost care when punching securities code and rate — no corrections will be permitted for punching errors
- Trading members should monitor notices issued by respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) for relevant scrips

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026 (Friday):** Demat Auction 649 session (2:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 15, 2026 (Monday):** Auction securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational auction notice with no broad market impact. It affects only trading members with short-delivery obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-649/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auctions for settlements with short deliveries provides trading members greater flexibility in participation.
