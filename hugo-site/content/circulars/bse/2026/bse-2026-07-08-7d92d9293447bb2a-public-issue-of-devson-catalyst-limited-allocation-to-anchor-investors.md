## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the public issue of DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED, 10,00,800 equity shares have been subscribed at Rs. 118 per equity share. This follows the company's announcement dated June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10,00,800 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED public issue
- Allocation price: Rs. 118 per equity share
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship Department
- Company's original announcement dated June 29, 2026, attached as reference

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational disclosure regarding anchor investor subscription in an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members; the notice is for information purposes regarding the anchor investor allocation.

## Important Dates

- Company announcement date: June 29, 2026
- BSE notice date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine pre-IPO disclosure confirming anchor investor participation ahead of the public issue's subscription period. It provides an early price signal (Rs. 118 per share) for the DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED IPO but has limited direct impact on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
