## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for purchase of securities in Auction in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-661/2026-27. This is a routine demat auction conducted to settle short deliveries in the settlement cycle, held on Wednesday, July 01, 2026, with pay-in scheduled for Thursday, July 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction to be held on Thursday, July 02, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Settlement to be routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.
- The prior restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they have short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while punching security codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted for punching errors.
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Members are advised to refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for related corporate action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) before placing offers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reflects the continued application of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which settlement is routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and reiterates the removal of the earlier restriction on short-delivering members participating in auctions for the same settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings. Members must accurately enter security codes and rates during the offer entry window, as punching errors cannot be corrected after submission.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Wednesday, July 01, 2026 (02:00 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Thursday, July 02, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational/settlement notice affecting trading members and custodians involved in the DR-661/2026-27 rolling settlement auction process. It carries low market-wide impact as it pertains to routine short-delivery settlement mechanics rather than any specific security or broader trading policy change.
