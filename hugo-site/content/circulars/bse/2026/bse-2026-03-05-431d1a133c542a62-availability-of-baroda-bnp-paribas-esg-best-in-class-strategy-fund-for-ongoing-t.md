## Summary

BSE has informed members that the Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best-in-Class Strategy Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 6, 2026. This notice references an earlier communication (Notice No. 20260211-41).

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from **March 6, 2026**.
- Both Regular and Direct plan variants are available across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are included.
- All six schemes fall under the **Equity** category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice enabling an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these six schemes for ongoing subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities from March 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 6, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions, SIP, STP, and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a low market impact, as it is a routine operational update enabling an ESG-themed equity mutual fund for ongoing investor transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors interested in ESG-focused equity funds can now transact in all six plan variants through the platform. No changes to market structure, trading rules, or compliance obligations are involved.
