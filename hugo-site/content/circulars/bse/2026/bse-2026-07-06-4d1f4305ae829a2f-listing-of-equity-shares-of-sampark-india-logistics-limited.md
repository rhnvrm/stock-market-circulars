## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Sampark India Logistics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'MT' Group of Securities effective Tuesday, July 7, 2026. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Sampark India Logistics Limited, listed on BSE SME platform
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: SILL
- Scrip Code: 544810
- ISIN: INE147701010
- Total securities: 1,22,58,750 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (distinctive number range 1 to 1,22,58,750)
- Market Lot: 1600 shares
- Issue Price: Rs. 84/- per share (Face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 74/-)
- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 3, 2026
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on the listing day
- Market Maker: Rikhav Securities Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000157737)
- Registrar to the issue: Maashitla Securities Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INR000004370)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an implementation notice under existing SEBI framework for Trade-for-Trade segment (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012) and Special Pre-open Session (Exchange notice 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in the minimum market lot of 1600 equity shares, subject to future revision by the Exchange with at least one month's prior notice.
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I must be adhered to; the company has arranged for lock-in to be marked in depository records for demat holdings.
- Shareholding pattern is disclosed as per Annexure II.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 3, 2026
- Listing and start of trading: Tuesday, July 7, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable: 10 trading days from listing
- Special Pre-open Session: Tuesday, July 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with limited direct impact on the broader market. It is significant for allottees and prospective investors in Sampark India Logistics Limited, who should note the Trade-for-Trade restriction (which limits intraday trading and requires delivery-based settlement) for the initial 10 trading days, as well as the minimum market lot requirement of 1600 shares.
