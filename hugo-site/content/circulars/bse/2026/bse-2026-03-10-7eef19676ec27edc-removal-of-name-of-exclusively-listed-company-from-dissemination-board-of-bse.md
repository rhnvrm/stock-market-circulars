## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that NCL Seccolor Ltd, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026, pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Following this removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board of BSE.

## Key Points

- NCL Seccolor Ltd (CIN: U21029TG1986PTC007033) will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective March 11, 2026.
- The removal is in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016.
- Post removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will not be available for trading on the Dissemination Board.
- The company is registered at 10-3-162, 5th Floor, NCL Pearl, Sarojini Devi Road, Secunderabad, Telangana – 500 026.

## Regulatory Changes

This action is carried out pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the framework for Exclusively Listed Companies and their presence on the BSE Dissemination Board. No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should take note of the removal and cease facilitating trades in NCL Seccolor Ltd on the Dissemination Board from March 11, 2026.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company's Company Secretary M. Ravindra at companysecretary@nclbuildtek.com or BSE's Mr. Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Compliance & Operations) at direct.listing@bseindia.com / Ph: 022-2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **March 11, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of removal of NCL Seccolor Ltd from the BSE Dissemination Board.

## Impact Assessment

The removal of NCL Seccolor Ltd from the BSE Dissemination Board has limited broader market impact, as dissemination board companies are exclusively listed entities not traded on the main exchange. Investors or shareholders holding shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd should note that the shares will no longer be accessible through the BSE Dissemination Board post March 11, 2026. The impact is confined to existing shareholders of this specific company.
