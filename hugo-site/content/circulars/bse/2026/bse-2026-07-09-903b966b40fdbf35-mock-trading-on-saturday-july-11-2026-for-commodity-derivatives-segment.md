## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026. The session allows trading members using third-party trading platforms of empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications, including functionality for exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 will also be released, with details to follow via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for the Commodity Derivatives segment on July 11, 2026
- Open to trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Testing covers functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 to be released; further details via separate circular
- Trades from mock session carry no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no rights or liabilities
- Members requested to submit feedback on the mock trading session by 3:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational notice for system testing, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session and submit feedback via provided contact numbers or email by 3:00 PM on the day of the session. No mandatory compliance action is required.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Session: Saturday, July 11, 2026
- Log-in: 09:15 AM to 10:00 AM
- Continuous Trading: 10:00 AM to 02:00 PM
- Trade Modification: 02:00 PM to 02:10 PM
- Feedback deadline: 3:00 PM on session day

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice intended solely for system testing and familiarization. It does not affect live trading, securities pricing, or investor holdings, and trades executed during the mock session do not carry any financial obligations.
