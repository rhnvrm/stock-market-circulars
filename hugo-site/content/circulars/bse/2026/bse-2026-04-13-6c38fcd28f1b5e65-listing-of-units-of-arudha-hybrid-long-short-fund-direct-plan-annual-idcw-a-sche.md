## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the units of Arudha Hybrid Long-Short Fund Direct Plan - Annual IDCW, a scheme under Bandhan Mutual Fund, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, April 15, 2026. The fund is an interval investment strategy investing in equity and debt securities, including limited short exposure through derivatives, and will trade in Group B.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544749, listed under Group B on BSE
- ISIN: INF194K30259
- Scrip ID on BOLT System: ARSIFHDAD
- Total units listed: 99,995 units
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Date of allotment: April 7, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Units will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS), Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing complies with existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, which mandate that mutual fund units be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that all trades in this scrip must be executed in dematerialized form only
- Minimum application amount during NFO: Rs. 10,00,000 and in multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter, across all investment strategies under Arudha SIF at the PAN level
- Minimum initial investment on continuous basis: Rs. 10,00,000 and in multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 7, 2026
- Effective listing date on BSE: April 15, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Notice date: April 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with limited broader market impact. The fund targets a niche investor segment given the high minimum investment threshold of Rs. 10 lakhs. The hybrid long-short strategy with derivatives exposure may interest sophisticated investors seeking equity and debt exposure with downside hedging. With only 99,995 units listed, liquidity on the exchange may be limited.
