## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by four companies — Chennai Petroleum Corporation Ltd, Grasim Industries Ltd, UGRO Capital Limited, and Redington Limited — have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- All securities will trade only in dematerialised form under their respective ISIN numbers.
- Standard trading denomination is Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise.
- Chennai Petroleum Corporation Ltd: Scrip Code 732031, ISIN INE178A14HS6, 13,000 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value, issue price Rs.4,94,663.50, interest rate 6.25% p.a., allotted 02/07/2026, redemption on 03/09/2026.
- Grasim Industries Ltd: Scrip Code 732035, ISIN INE047A14BD8, 10,000 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value, issue price Rs.4,96,154.50, interest rate 6.15% p.a., allotted 03/07/2026, redemption on 18/08/2026.
- UGRO Capital Limited: Scrip Code 732033, ISIN INE583D14949, 800 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value, issue price Rs.4,83,200.50, interest rate 9% p.a., allotted 02/07/2026, redemption on 20/11/2026.
- Redington Limited (RL-10-9-26-CP): Scrip Code 732036, ISIN INE891D14C69, 2,000 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value, issue price Rs.4,93,903.00, interest rate 6.53% p.a., allotted 03/07/2026, redemption on 10/09/2026.
- Redington Limited (RL-11-9-26-CP): Scrip Code 732037, ISIN INE891D14C77, 4,000 units of Rs.5,00,000 face value, issue price Rs.4,93,816.00, interest rate 6.53% p.a., allotted 03/07/2026, redemption on 11/09/2026.
- All papers carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+, or IND A1+).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's Debt Segment listing process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new ISINs and scrip codes for trading in the Debt Segment. Queries can be directed to BSE's debt department at the listed contact numbers.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: July 3, 2026
- Redemption dates range from 18/08/2026 to 20/11/2026 depending on the specific instrument.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative notice relevant primarily to debt market trading members and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. It has no direct bearing on equity trading or broader market operations, though it reflects ongoing short-term borrowing activity by the named corporates.
