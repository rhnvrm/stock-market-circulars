## Summary

BSE has notified that 52,66,537 equity shares of Rnit Ai Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 517286) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Strategic Investors (non-promoters) at Rs. 44/- per share pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by the NCLT, Jaipur Bench.

## Key Points

- 52,66,537 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 34/- (total issue price Rs. 44/-) to Strategic Investors on a preferential basis
- Issuance is pursuant to the Resolution Plan approved by NCLT, Jaipur Bench vide order dated September 23, 2024
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 79525547 to 84792083
- ISIN: INE335Q01026
- All 52,66,537 shares are subject to lock-in until May 29, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a listing notification consequent to an NCLT-approved Resolution Plan under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are informed of the listing effective May 26, 2026
- The newly listed shares carry a lock-in obligation until May 29, 2027; trading in these shares by the allottees is restricted until that date

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date:** September 23, 2024
- **Date of Allotment:** March 28, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement:** May 26, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** May 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds approximately 52.66 lakh new shares of Rnit Ai Solutions Limited to the tradeable universe, though the entire allotment is under lock-in until May 2027. The preferential issuance to strategic investors under an IBC resolution plan signals the company's ongoing restructuring. Existing shareholders see dilution but shares are pari-passu. No immediate trading activity is expected from the newly allotted shares given the lock-in period.
