## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260708-19, dated 08 Jul 2026) announcing the listing of units of the Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund, a scheme under Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund, on the exchange's Mutual Fund segment.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to the listing of units of a new mutual fund scheme, Infinity Hybrid Long-Short Fund.
- The scheme is managed by Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund.
- Category: Company related; Segment: Mutual Fund; Department: DCS-Listing.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification and does not introduce any regulatory changes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from market participants beyond standard trading procedures applicable to newly listed fund units.

## Important Dates

Notice Date: 08 Jul 2026. No further specific dates (e.g., listing effective date) were disclosed in the notice content.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice confirming the listing of a new hybrid mutual fund scheme's units on BSE. It expands the range of mutual fund products available for trading on the exchange but does not affect existing listed securities or require action from other market participants.
