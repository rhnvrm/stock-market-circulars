## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance & Supervision (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movement in the scrips of 32 listed companies. To ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information, the Exchange wrote to these companies seeking clarification. Replies have been received from 22 companies and are attached to the notice, while responses from the remaining 10 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 32 listed company scrips
- Exchange initiated outreach to all 32 companies for clarification as part of its surveillance mandate
- 22 companies have submitted their replies (attached as Replies1, Replies2, Replies3)
- 10 companies have not yet responded and their replies are awaited
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260624-25 by the Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance function under the Surveillance department (DOSS), aimed at maintaining market integrity by seeking disclosures from companies with unusual trading activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet responded (10 companies listed above) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly
- Investors and trading members in these scrips should review the attached company replies for material information that may explain recent price/volume movements

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 24 June 2026
- Replies attached cover responses received as of 24 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies

## Impact Assessment

This is an informational surveillance notice. The replies from 22 companies are publicly disclosed and may contain material information explaining recent price or volume anomalies in those scrips. Investors holding or considering positions in any of the 32 listed companies should review the attached replies. Companies still awaiting response (10 firms) may face further exchange scrutiny if replies are not submitted. No trading restrictions have been imposed as part of this notice.
