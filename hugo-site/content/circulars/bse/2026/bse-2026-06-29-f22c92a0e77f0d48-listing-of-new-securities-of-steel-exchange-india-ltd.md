## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,82,97,870 new equity shares of Steel Exchange India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 534748, ISIN: INE503B01021) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **2,82,97,870 equity shares** of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 13.10/- per share (issue price Rs. 14.10/-)
- Shares issued on **preferential basis to Non-Promoters** through conversion of warrants
- New shares rank **pari-passu** with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: **1247220543 to 1275518412**
- Date of allotment: **April 30, 2026**
- Trading effective from: **June 30, 2026**

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to an approved preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to enable trading of the newly listed shares from June 30, 2026. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 30, 2026
- **Trading commencement:** June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** January 14, 2027 (all 2,82,97,870 shares under lock-in until this date)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of approximately 2.83 crore shares expands the equity base of Steel Exchange India Ltd. However, the entire new issue is under lock-in until January 14, 2027, meaning these shares cannot be sold in the open market until that date. The immediate impact on free float is therefore negligible. Existing shareholders may note the dilution in equity ownership resulting from this preferential allotment to non-promoters via warrant conversion.
