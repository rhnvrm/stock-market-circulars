## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260610-17, in continuation of Notice No. 20240807-22 dated 07 August 2024) notifying amended covenant terms for Muthoot Microfin Ltd's listed debt security (Scrip Code 975885, ISIN INE046W07271). The revised terms will be effective for trading from Friday, 12 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **975885** (ISIN: **INE046W07271**) will trade with revised covenant terms from 12 June 2026.
- Two financial covenants have been amended: the Write-Off Ratio threshold and the Return on Assets (RoA) threshold.
- The Write-Off Ratio covenant has been restructured into a tiered schedule with a significantly relaxed threshold of 6% applicable from October 1, 2025 to March 31, 2027.
- The Return on Assets covenant has been relaxed to 0% from April 1, 2026 until Maturity Date.
- Trading members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade (+91 22 2272 8597 / debt.listing@bseindia.com) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

**Write-Off Ratio Covenant — Amended Schedule:**

| Period | Original Threshold | Amended Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Until March 31, 2025 | ≤ 2% | ≤ 2% |
| Until September 30, 2025 | ≤ 1.75% | ≤ 1.75% |
| October 1, 2025 – March 31, 2027 | ≤ 1.75% (until March 31, 2026) | ≤ **6%** |
| April 1, 2027 – Maturity | ≤ 1.5% | ≤ **4%** |

**Return on Assets Covenant — Amended Schedule:**

| Period | Original Threshold | Amended Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Until March 31, 2026 | > 1% | > 1% |
| April 1, 2026 – Maturity | > 1% | > **0%** |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the revised covenant terms applicable to Scrip Code 975885 from 12 June 2026.
- No attachment or separate document has been issued; the revised terms are fully specified within this notice.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026** — Notice issuance date.
- **June 12, 2026 (Friday)** — Effective date for revised covenant terms to apply to trading.

## Impact Assessment

The amendments represent a material relaxation of financial covenants, particularly the Write-Off Ratio (raised from 1.75% to 6% for the period October 2025–March 2027), reflecting deterioration in the issuer's asset quality metrics. The Return on Assets floor being reduced to 0% from April 2026 further signals stress in Muthoot Microfin's microfinance portfolio. Existing NCD holders should reassess credit risk exposure. Prospective buyers in the secondary market should factor in the relaxed covenants when pricing this instrument.
