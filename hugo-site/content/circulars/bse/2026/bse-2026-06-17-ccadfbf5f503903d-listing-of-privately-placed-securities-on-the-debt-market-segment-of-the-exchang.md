## Summary

BSE has listed two new privately placed debt securities issued by National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development (NBFID) on the BSE Debt Segment, effective June 17, 2026. Both instruments carry AAA ratings from CRISIL and ICRA, are available in dematerialised form only, and have a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit.

## Key Points

- Two NBFID debenture series admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment from June 17, 2026
- Both securities issued on a private placement basis and tradeable in demat form only
- Tick size for both securities is 1 paise
- Quantity: 2,50,000 units each; Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE for both instruments
- Face Value, Paid-up Value, and Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Date of Allotment for both: June 15, 2026
- Placement Memorandum available at BSE debt memorandum data portal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Members to observe the tick size of 1 paise for these scrips
- For queries, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Important Dates

| Security | Allotment Date | Interest Payment | Redemption Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| NBFID 7.37% (Scrip Code 977870, ISIN INE0KUG08142) | 15-Jun-2026 | Annually 16-Jul-2026 to 16-Jul-2029 | 16-Jul-2029 |
| NBFID 7.66% (Scrip Code 977871, ISIN INE0KUG08159) | 15-Jun-2026 | Annually 15-Jun-2027 to 16-Jun-2036 | 16-Jun-2036 |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to institutional and wholesale debt market participants. Both securities are AAA-rated instruments issued by NBFID, a government-backed infrastructure financing institution, signalling low credit risk. The listing expands the tradeable debt universe on BSE's Debt Segment but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should update their systems with the new scrip codes (977870 and 977871) and ISIN numbers.
