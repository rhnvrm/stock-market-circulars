## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), invites offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 638 pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-638/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Wednesday, May 27, 2026, with pay-in on Friday, May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 PM to 02:45 PM on May 27, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 PM to 03:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 03:00 PM to 03:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 PM to 05:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): Friday, May 29, 2026 at 08:00 AM
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. Additionally, the earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers through trading terminals within the specified session window
- Members and custodians are required to verify auction pay-in obligations and complete securities pay-in in depositories before depository cut-off timings on May 29, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should consult relevant departmental notices on corporate actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) before placing auction offers

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026** — Auction session day; offer entry from 02:00 PM to 02:45 PM
- **May 29, 2026** — Auction pay-in at 08:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with low market impact. It affects trading members and custodians who have short-delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-638/2026-27 and must participate in or monitor the auction process. The removal of the restriction allowing short-delivering members to offer in the same settlement auction is a procedural change that improves market flexibility under the Uniform Membership structure.
