## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members of the new ISIN number allotted to Indian Toners & Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 523586) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10 to Rs.2 per share. This is a continuation of the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260618-41 dated June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN allotted: INE826B01026
- The new ISIN corresponds to the sub-divided equity shares of face value Rs.2 each (previously Rs.10 each)
- This notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260618-41 dated June 18, 2026, which originally approved the sub-division
- The new ISIN becomes effective for trades executed on and from the ex-date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational/administrative update implementing the previously approved stock split by assigning a new ISIN to reflect the revised face value of the equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and depositories must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE826B01026) for trades in Indian Toners & Developers Ltd shares executed on or after the ex-date. Investors' demat holdings will be updated by depositories to the new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Ex-Date: July 17, 2026 (DR- 674/2026-2027)
- Notice Date: July 15, 2026
- Original sub-division notice: June 18, 2026 (Notice No. 20260618-41)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action follow-through with limited market impact. The stock split increases the share count while proportionally reducing the price per share, and the new ISIN ensures accurate tracking and settlement of the sub-divided shares. Existing shareholders and traders need to ensure their records reflect the new ISIN post ex-date to avoid settlement discrepancies.
