## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Laser Power & Infra Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company: Laser Power & Infra Limited
- Scrip Code: 544822
- Symbol: LASERPOWER
- ISIN: INE17IR01028
- Notice issued by BSE Limited, DCS-Listing Department
- Signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members at this stage; the listing and trading date will be announced via a subsequent notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 15 July 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced in a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low immediate market impact as trading has not yet commenced. Once listed, LASERPOWER will become available for trading on the Exchange, which may be of interest to investors tracking new equity listings and IPO-linked stocks.
