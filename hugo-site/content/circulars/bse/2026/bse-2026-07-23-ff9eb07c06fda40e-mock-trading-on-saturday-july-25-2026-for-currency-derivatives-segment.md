## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments on Saturday, July 25, 2026. The session will be conducted from both the Disaster Recovery (DR) site and Primary (PR) site to allow trading members, including those using third-party or in-house ETI API-based trading platforms, to test their trading applications under various functionalities, including exceptional market conditions. No new BOLT TWS release is associated with this mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, July 25, 2026, for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments.
- Session tests both Disaster Recovery (DR) and Primary (PR) site systems with a switchover between the two.
- Trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs can test functionality, including exceptional market conditions.
- No new BOLT TWS release accompanies this mock session.
- Trades from the mock session carry no margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to actively participate and submit feedback on the session.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational notice for a scheduled system test, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and vendors should log in during the 07:30 am–08:00 am window and participate in the scheduled sessions.
- Members must submit feedback on the mock trading session by 3:00 pm on July 25, 2026, via the contact numbers/email provided (BSE Tech Support: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com; Help Desk: 022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com).
- Queries may be directed to the respective Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk, or BSE Tech Support team.

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, July 25, 2026
- **Log-in:** 07:30 am – 08:00 am
- **Continuous Trading (Currency & Cross Currency Derivatives, DR):** 08:15 am – 10:45 am
- **Switchover to PR:** 12:30 pm – 01:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading (Currency & Cross Currency Derivatives, DR - second session):** 01:15 pm – 02:00 pm
- **Trade Modification (Currency & Cross Currency):** 02:10 pm
- **Feedback Deadline:** 3:00 pm, July 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal to no direct market impact as it pertains solely to a system testing exercise. Trades executed during the mock session do not create actual financial obligations, margin requirements, or legal rights/liabilities. The impact is confined to trading members and technology vendors who need to validate system readiness ahead of live trading, ensuring operational continuity and reducing the risk of technical disruptions during actual currency derivatives trading.
