## Summary

BSE has listed 33,33,333 equity shares of Choice International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531358) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, January 6, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- 33,33,333 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 290/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 300/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 205655501 to 208988833
- Date of Allotment: November 12, 2025
- ISIN: INE102B01014
- All shares subject to lock-in until July 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now available for trading from January 6, 2026, subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The shares represent warrant conversion and will be subject to lock-in restrictions until July 2026, limiting immediate trading activity. No broader market implications.
