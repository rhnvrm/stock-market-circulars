## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Lorven International Limited, filed on February 25, 2026, on its SME platform in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations. The document is available for public review for at least 21 days, during which stakeholders may submit comments.

## Key Points

- Lorven International Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for an SME IPO with BSE dated February 25, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website and complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations.
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (i.e., by March 19, 2026).
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is a standard procedural step under SEBI's ICDR Regulations, which mandate public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs prior to listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and public stakeholders wishing to provide feedback on the Draft Offer Document of Lorven International Limited must submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from February 26, 2026.
- No action is required from existing market participants or listed companies.

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 19, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is a routine disclosure specific to the SME IPO process of Lorven International Limited. The primary audience is prospective investors and analysts interested in evaluating the company ahead of its proposed listing on the BSE SME platform. No immediate trading or compliance obligations arise for existing market participants.
