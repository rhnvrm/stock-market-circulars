## Summary

BSE SME Exchange has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Quanto Agroworld Limited, filed on 28 February 2026. The document is made available on the BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days to solicit public comments, as mandated under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.

## Key Points

- Quanto Agroworld Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with the BSE SME Exchange on 28/02/2026
- The draft is hosted on the BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of this notice (02 March 2026)
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, AVP, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, which requires draft offer documents to be made publicly available for comment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of 02 March 2026 (i.e., by 23 March 2026)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Filing:** 28 February 2026
- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** 23 March 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It relates solely to the pre-IPO disclosure process for Quanto Agroworld Limited on the SME platform. Investors and analysts interested in participating in or evaluating the upcoming SME IPO may review the draft prospectus and submit comments. No trading or compliance action is required from existing market participants.
