## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of equity shares of ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544694) from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective February 18, 2026. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a progression in trading mechanisms for the stock.

## Key Points

- Company: ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544694
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: February 18, 2026
- Notice references previous BSE Notice No. 20260203-44 dated February 03, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement mechanism, allowing for intraday trading and standard T+1 settlement cycles instead of the restrictive T2T framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the group change
- Members must ensure proper categorization of the scrip under M Group from the effective date
- Trading members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 4, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 18, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This change positively impacts trading by moving the stock from a restrictive Trade for Trade segment to a more liquid Rolling segment. Investors will benefit from:
- Ability to conduct intraday trading
- Standard settlement cycles
- Improved liquidity and price discovery
- Reduced trading restrictions typical of T2T segments

The transition indicates regulatory confidence in the stock's trading behavior post-IPO and should enhance market participation.
