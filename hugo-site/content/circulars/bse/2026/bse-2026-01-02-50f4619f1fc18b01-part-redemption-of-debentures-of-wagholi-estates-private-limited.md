## Summary

BSE has notified that WAGHOLI ESTATES PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed a record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value effective from January 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: WAGHOLI ESTATES PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE0Z1N07017, Code: 975855)
- Purpose: Part redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Record Date: January 7, 2026
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 80,000 per debenture
- Effective Date: January 7, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-793/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260102-37

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the company's debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value from the effective date
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 80,000 per debenture from January 7, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 2, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 7, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-793/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects only holders and traders of WAGHOLI ESTATES PRIVATE LIMITED debentures. The part redemption will reduce the outstanding principal amount of the debentures.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in these specific debentures need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from the effective date. This is a routine corporate action for debt securities with limited broader market implications.
