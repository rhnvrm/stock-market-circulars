## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of FX MULTITECH LIMITED for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The draft offer document will be hosted on BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by FX MULTITECH LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations framework for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- The draft offer document will remain available for public viewing for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026 (deadline: January 22, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document that affects only prospective investors interested in FX MULTITECH LIMITED.

**Investor Impact**: Prospective investors in FX MULTITECH LIMITED SME IPO can review the draft offer document and provide feedback during the 21-day comment period.

**Regulatory Impact**: Standard compliance with ICDR Regulations Schedule VI disclosure requirements for SME public issues.
