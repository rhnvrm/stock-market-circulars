## Summary

Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has submitted its shareholding pattern as of January 30, 2026, under Regulation 31(1)(a). The company has a total of 22,698,363 equity shares with promoter and promoter group holding 68.83% and public shareholders holding 31.17%. The company has confirmed no outstanding convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, partly paid shares, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or other encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,698,363 (all fully paid-up)
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 15,624,400 shares (68.83%) held by 6 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 7,073,963 shares (31.17%) held by 622 shareholders
- Dematerialized shares: 18,404,363 shares (81.08% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No shares locked-in or pledged
- No differential voting rights shares
- No significant beneficial owners identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Listed entity required to submit declaration regarding various types of securities and encumbrances
- All declarations confirmed with 'No' responses, indicating standard shareholding structure

## Important Dates

- Report Date: January 30, 2026
- Filing Date: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine periodic disclosure with no material impact on trading or market operations. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding above 68% with no encumbrances, indicating strong promoter commitment. The relatively high public shareholding of over 31% ensures adequate free float for trading. The dematerialization rate of 81% is reasonably high, facilitating electronic trading.
