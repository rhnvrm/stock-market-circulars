## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that 1,71,792 equity shares of Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited (Scrip Code: 543350) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 19, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation of Medinova Diagnostic Services Limited (Transferor Company) with and into Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited (Transferee Company).

## Key Points

- 1,71,792 equity shares of Re. 1/- each are newly listed on BSE effective June 19, 2026
- Shares issued under the Scheme of Amalgamation: Medinova Diagnostic Services Limited merged into Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited
- ISIN: INE043W01024
- Distribution Numbers: 102724937 – 102896728
- Date of Allotment: January 08, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following the completion of a court/NCLT-approved Scheme of Amalgamation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. Brokers and trading members should update their systems to reflect the availability of these shares for trading from June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 08, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** June 19, 2026 (Friday)
- **Notice Date:** June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total outstanding tradeable equity shares of Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Limited by 1,71,792 shares. The addition is relatively small compared to the existing share capital and is a direct result of the previously announced amalgamation with Medinova Diagnostic Services Limited. Shareholders of Medinova who received these shares as part of the scheme will now be able to trade them on BSE. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the modest size of the new issuance.
