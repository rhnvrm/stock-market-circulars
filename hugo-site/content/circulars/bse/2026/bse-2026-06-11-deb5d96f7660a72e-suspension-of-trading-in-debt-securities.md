## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260611-14 informing trading members that the privately placed debentures of Green Gold Animation Private Limited (scrip code 974917, ISIN: INE0NZ807013) will mature as per the maturity date fixed by the company. Trading in these debentures is suspended with effect from June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: GAPL-7%-15-6-26-PVT (Green Gold Animation Private Limited, 7% debenture maturing June 15, 2026)
- Scrip Code: 974917
- ISIN: INE0NZ807013
- Trading suspended effective June 12, 2026
- Reference: DR-650/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice triggered by the scheduled maturity of a privately placed debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid debentures (GAPL-7%-15-6-26-PVT, ISIN: INE0NZ807013) with effect from June 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension
- **June 15, 2026**: Maturity date of the debenture (as indicated by the instrument name GAPL-7%-15-6-26-PVT)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders of this specific privately placed debenture of Green Gold Animation Private Limited. The suspension is a routine procedural step ahead of maturity redemption and does not affect broader market operations or other securities.
