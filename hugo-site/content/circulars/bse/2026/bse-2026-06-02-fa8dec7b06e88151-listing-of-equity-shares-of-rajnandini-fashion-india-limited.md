## Summary

Rajnandini Fashion India Limited (Scrip ID: RFIL, Scrip Code: 544771) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment effective Wednesday, June 3, 2026, under the 'MT' group of securities. The scrip will be in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** June 3, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Scrip Code:** 544771 | **Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name:** RFIL
- **ISIN:** INE1GM501015
- **Total Securities:** 10,370,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 10,370,000
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 63/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 53/-)
- **Market Lot:** 2,000 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 1, 2026
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- **Segment:** SME | **Group:** MT
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only, in minimum market lot of 2,000 shares
- Scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 3, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI guidelines:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012): Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000: Mandatory dematerialised trading
- BSE Notice 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012): Special Pre-open Session for IPOs and relisted scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip in dematerialised form only
- All trades must be in the minimum market lot of 2,000 equity shares
- Trading members must participate in SPOS on June 3, 2026 for this scrip
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I; Shareholding pattern as per Annexure II
- Corporate action for lock-in period has been executed in depository records

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 3, 2026:** Effective listing date; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)
- **10 trading days from June 3, 2026:** Trade-for-Trade restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with moderate operational impact for trading members dealing in SME securities. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days means all trades must result in delivery — no netting or intraday squaring off is permitted during this period, which may limit speculative activity. The market lot of 2,000 shares at Rs. 63/- implies a minimum trade value of Rs. 1,26,000, consistent with SME segment norms. The designated Market Maker is Giriraj Stock Broking Private Limited, which is required to provide liquidity. Registrar is Bigshare Services Private Limited. The company is a Rajasthan-based fashion company (registered office in Jaipur's Sitapura Industrial Area) with CIN U51109RJ2010PLC033059.
