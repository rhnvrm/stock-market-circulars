## Summary

BSE has listed 1,399,415 new equity shares of Automobile Products of India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505032, ISIN: INE0NY101012) issued at par on a rights basis. These shares are available for trading on the Exchange effective April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,399,415 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis
- ISIN: INE0NY101012
- Distribution numbers: 4817657 to 6217071
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 505032

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 28, 2026
- Effective date for trading: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing of rights issue shares is a routine corporate action for Automobile Products of India Ltd. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, meaning no change in shareholder rights or dividend entitlements relative to existing shareholders.
