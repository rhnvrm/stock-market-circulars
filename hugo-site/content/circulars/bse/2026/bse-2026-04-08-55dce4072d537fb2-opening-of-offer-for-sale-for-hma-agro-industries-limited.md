## Summary

BSE has announced an Offer for Sale (OFS) by promoters of HMA Agro Industries Limited. The sellers — Zulfiqar Ahmad Qureshi, Mohammad Ashraf Qureshi, Mohammad Mehmood Qureshi, Gulzar Ahmad, and Wajid Ahmed — propose to sell up to 3,31,81,173 equity shares (approximately 6.63% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital) at a floor price of Rs.18 per equity share through BSE's designated OFS window.

## Key Points

- Up to 3,31,81,173 equity shares of face value Re.1 each are being offered for sale
- The offer represents approximately 6.63% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital
- Floor price is set at Rs.18 per equity share
- OFS will be conducted through BSE's separate, designated OFS window
- Five promoters collectively act as sellers in this OFS
- Trading members and custodians should refer to revised OFS guidelines per earlier BSE notices

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The OFS is conducted under existing frameworks:
- BSE notice nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, and 20240701-19 (Revised Guidelines for Bidding in OFS Segment)
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 (Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism)
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 (Comprehensive Framework on OFS)
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 (OFS to Employees Framework)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must follow existing OFS bidding guidelines as outlined in referenced BSE notices
- Non-retail investors may participate on T Day (April 09, 2026)
- Retail investors may participate on T+1 Day (April 10, 2026)
- Non-retail investors with unallotted bids from T Day may carry forward bids to T+1 Day
- Market participants should monitor BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for OFS details disseminated by the Exchange

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 08, 2026
- **T Day (Non-Retail Investors only):** April 09, 2026
- **T+1 Day (Retail Investors + Non-Retail carryforward):** April 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This OFS represents a moderate market event. The sale of ~6.63% promoter stake at a floor price of Rs.18 per share could exert short-term downward pressure on HMA Agro Industries Limited's stock price. Retail investors gain access on T+1 Day, broadening participation. The offer size of over 3.31 crore shares is significant and may affect liquidity in the stock during the OFS window. Investors should assess the floor price relative to prevailing market price before participating.
