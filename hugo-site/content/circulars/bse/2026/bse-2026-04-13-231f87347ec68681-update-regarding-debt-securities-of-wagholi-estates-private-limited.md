## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260413-26) continuing the suspension of trading for debt securities of Wagholi Estates Private Limited. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20240726-17 dated 26 July 2024. The scrip bearing ISIN INE0Z1N07017 (Scrip Code: 975855) remains suspended for trading due to restructuring until further notice.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for Wagholi Estates Private Limited debt securities remains in effect
- ISIN: INE0Z1N07017 | Scrip Code: 975855
- Suspension is due to ongoing restructuring of the issuer
- No end date specified — suspended "till further notice"
- Original suspension initiated via Notice No. 20240726-17 dated 26 July 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reaffirms and continues the existing suspension order originally placed in July 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure no trades are executed in Scrip Code 975855 (ISIN: INE0Z1N07017)
- Members seeking further clarification may contact:
  - **Mr. Akash Shinde** — +91 22 2272 8597 | debt.listing@bseindia.com
  - **Hardik Bhuta**, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **Original Suspension Notice:** 26 July 2024 (Notice No. 20240726-17)
- **Current Notice Date:** 13 April 2026
- **Suspension End Date:** Not specified — till further notice

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and trading members holding or dealing in the specific debt security of Wagholi Estates Private Limited (Scrip Code: 975855). The ongoing restructuring of the issuer means this security remains illiquid with no trading permitted on BSE. Holders of this instrument face continued inability to exit their positions on the exchange until the restructuring is resolved and BSE lifts the suspension.
