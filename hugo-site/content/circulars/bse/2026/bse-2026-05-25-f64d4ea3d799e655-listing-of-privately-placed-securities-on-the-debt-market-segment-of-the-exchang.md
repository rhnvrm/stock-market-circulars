## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by four companies on the BSE Debt Segment effective May 25, 2026. The securities are admitted to trading in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four companies have listed Commercial Papers under fresh listing on the BSE Debt Segment.
- All CPs are traded in dematerialised form only, in denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples.
- Tick size is 1 paise for all listed securities.
- Redemption dates range from August 21, 2026 to August 24, 2026.
- Interest rates range from 7.35% to 8.09% p.a. on a cumulative basis.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of new debt securities admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these securities are conducted in dematerialised form.
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof must be observed.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.
