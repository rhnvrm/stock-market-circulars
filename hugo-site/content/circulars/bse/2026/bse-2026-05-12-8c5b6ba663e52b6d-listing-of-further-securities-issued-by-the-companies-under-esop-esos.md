## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260512-23 informs all market participants that further new securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on BSE with effect from Wednesday, May 13, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed for trading from May 13, 2026
- No lock-in period applicable to any of the newly listed securities
- Segment: Equity; Department: Listing Operations
- Issued under Notice No. 20260512-23 dated May 12, 2026
- Largest allotments are Ujjivan Small Finance Bank (544,021 shares) and Sai Life Sciences (244,281 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new ISINs and distribution number ranges for the listed securities. No specific compliance action is required from investors beyond awareness of the updated share counts for the affected companies.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, May 13, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not applicable (NA for all 13 companies)
