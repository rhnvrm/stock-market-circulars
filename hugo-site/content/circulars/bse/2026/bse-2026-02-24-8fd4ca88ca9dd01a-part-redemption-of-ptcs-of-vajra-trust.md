## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260224-14 informs trading members that Axis Trustee Services Limited has communicated reduced face values on partial redemption of nine Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) issued under various Vajra Trust series. The PTCs will trade at the revised reduced face values with effect from February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Nine Vajra Trust PTC series are undergoing partial redemption simultaneously
- Axis Trustee Services Limited is the trustee communicating the reduced face values
- All PTCs trade on the Debt Market Segment of BSE
- Trading members are required to note the revised face values for trading purposes
- Reference notice: DR-828/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action (partial redemption) under existing debt market regulations. The reduced face values replace previous face values for the listed PTCs from February 25, 2026 onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values for all nine PTC scrips effective February 25, 2026
- Members must ensure trades are executed at the revised reduced face values post the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Values:** February 25, 2026
