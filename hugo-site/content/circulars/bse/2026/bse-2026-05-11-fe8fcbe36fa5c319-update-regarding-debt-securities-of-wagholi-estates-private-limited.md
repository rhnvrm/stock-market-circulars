## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Wagholi Estates Private Limited, continuing from an earlier notice (No. 20260413-26 dated April 13, 2026). Scrip Code 975855 (ISIN INE0Z1N07017) will be available for trading with revised terms effective Tuesday, May 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of Notice No. 20260413-26 dated 13th April 2026
- Scrip Code 975855 with ISIN INE0Z1N07017 will trade under revised terms
- Effective date: Tuesday, May 12, 2026
- Revised terms are detailed in Annexure I attached to the notice
- Trading Members are requested to take note of the changes

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Wagholi Estates Private Limited (Scrip Code 975855, ISIN INE0Z1N07017) will be subject to revised terms as specified in Annexure I, superseding or supplementing the original terms communicated in the April 13, 2026 notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised terms for Scrip Code 975855 (ISIN INE0Z1N07017) and ensure all trades from May 12, 2026 onwards are executed in accordance with the updated terms outlined in Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- **April 13, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260413-26) issued
- **May 11, 2026**: Current update notice issued
- **May 12, 2026**: Revised terms become effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

This update has a moderate impact limited to trading members who deal in the debt securities of Wagholi Estates Private Limited. The revision of trading terms may affect pricing, yield, or other contractual parameters for Scrip Code 975855. Trading members should review Annexure I carefully before executing any trades from May 12, 2026.
