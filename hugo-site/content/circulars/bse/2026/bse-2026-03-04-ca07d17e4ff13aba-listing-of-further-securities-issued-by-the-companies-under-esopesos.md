## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading further new securities issued by 7 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) arrangements. These securities are effective for trading from Thursday, March 5, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 7 companies have listed new ESOP/ESOS shares effective March 5, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Total shares listed range from 6,000 (CG Power) to 716,314 (Infosys)
- Notice No: 20260304-26, issued March 4, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice informing market participants of newly listed ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective ISIN codes and scrip codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date for Trading:** Thursday, March 5, 2026
- **Notice Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. These are routine ESOP/ESOS share listings with no lock-in restrictions. The largest listing is Infosys (716,314 shares, face value ₹5, ISIN INE009A01021, Scrip 500209), which is negligible relative to its total outstanding shares. Details of all listed securities:
