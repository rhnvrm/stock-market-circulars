## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited on its SME platform website for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments on the Draft Offer Document must submit them within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The Draft Offer Document will remain available on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date:** January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the IPO process of Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited on the BSE SME platform. It is a procedural requirement under ICDR Regulations to make draft offer documents available for public comments before proceeding with the IPO. The impact is limited to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in this specific SME offering.
