## Summary

BSE has listed 16,30,000 new equity shares of Balu Forge Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531112, ISIN: INE011E01029) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 16,30,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 350/- (issue price Rs. 360/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 115991901 to 117621900
- Date of allotment: February 25, 2026
- ISIN: INE011E01029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from May 25, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to all 16,30,000 shares until November 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 25, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 16,30,000 shares to the tradeable float is a minor increase in equity capital for Balu Forge Industries Limited. The shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2026, limiting immediate market impact. This is a routine corporate action with no material effect on broader market operations.
