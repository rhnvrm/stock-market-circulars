## Summary

BSE has issued a notice updating members on a revised communication received from ABAKKUS Mutual Fund regarding restrictions on NRI (Non-Resident Indian) applications for its mutual fund schemes traded on the BSE StAR MF platform. This notice supersedes an earlier communication on the same subject and is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- ABAKKUS Mutual Fund has revised its list of restricted countries for NRI transactions.
- NRI applications from investors residing in the USA shall not be accepted in any scheme of ABAKKUS Mutual Fund.
- The restriction applies with immediate effect on the BSE StAR MF platform.
- This notice references and updates prior notices: No. 20251204-44 (introduction of ABAKKUS Mutual Fund on BSE StAR MF) and No. 20260526-30 (earlier restricted countries communication).

## Regulatory Changes

No change in SEBI/BSE regulations; this is an operational restriction imposed by the fund house (ABAKKUS Mutual Fund) itself, communicated through BSE to platform members.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF platform members and distributors must ensure that NRI applications from investors residing in the USA are not accepted or processed for any ABAKKUS Mutual Fund scheme, effective immediately.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 14 July 2026
- Effective: Immediate effect
- Prior related notices: 04 December 2025 (No. 20251204-44) and 26 May 2026 (No. 20260526-30)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to NRI investors based in the USA seeking to invest in ABAKKUS Mutual Fund schemes via the BSE StAR MF platform; such applications will now be rejected. There is no impact on listed equity securities, trading operations, or the broader market. Distributors and members handling NRI mutual fund transactions need to update their onboarding checks accordingly.
