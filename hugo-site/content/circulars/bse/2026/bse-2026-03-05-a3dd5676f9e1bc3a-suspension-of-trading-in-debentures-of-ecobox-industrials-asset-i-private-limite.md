## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed a Record Date of 10 March 2026 for the early redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are directed not to deal in the specified debentures from 10 March 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Company: ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture: EIAIPL-10.15%-RESET RATE-6-2-2
- ISIN: INE12W707013 (Scrip Code: 976482)
- Record Date: 10 March 2026
- Purpose: Early Redemption of Debentures
- No dealings permitted from: 10 March 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-836/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational notice issued under existing BSE framework for debt segment suspension ahead of debenture redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in ISIN INE12W707013 (Scrip Code 976482) from 10 March 2026.
- Members must take note of the no-dealings instruction and ensure compliance within their trading systems and client advisories.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 05 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 10 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The trading suspension is limited in scope, affecting only one specific NCD series (ISIN INE12W707013) of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED. Existing holders of this debenture will proceed to early redemption as of the record date. Market impact is contained to debt segment participants and investors holding this specific instrument.
