## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number to MOBAVENUE AI TECH LTD (Scrip Code: 539682) following the sub-division of its equity shares from Rs.10/- per share to Rs.2/- per share. The new ISIN INE622Q01027 becomes effective from the ex-date of June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for sub-divided equity shares: **INE622Q01027**
- Sub-division ratio: Rs.10/- face value reduced to Rs.2/- face value per share
- Effective ex-date: **June 12, 2026** (DR-650/2026-2027)
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260526-40 dated May 26, 2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The old ISIN for MOBAVENUE AI TECH LTD equity shares is superseded by the new ISIN INE622Q01027 for all trades executed on or after the ex-date. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN for trades in this scrip from June 12, 2026 onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN INE622Q01027 for all trades in MOBAVENUE AI TECH LTD equity shares from the ex-date onwards.
- Back-office and settlement systems should be updated to recognize the new ISIN prior to June 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 12, 2026**: Ex-date; new ISIN INE622Q01027 becomes effective for trades (DR-650/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update following a stock split approval. The impact is limited to trading members handling MOBAVENUE AI TECH LTD (539682) shares, who need to update their systems with the new ISIN before the ex-date. Shareholders will see their share count increase proportionally (5x) with a corresponding reduction in face value, with no change in overall investment value at the time of split.
