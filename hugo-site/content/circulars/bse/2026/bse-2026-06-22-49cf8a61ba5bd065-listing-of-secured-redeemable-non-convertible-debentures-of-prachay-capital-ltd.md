## Summary

BSE has admitted Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Prachay Capital Ltd to dealings on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 23, 2026, under the 'F Group' segment. Trading will be in dematerialized form only, with a unit market lot of one bond.

## Key Points

- Securities admitted: Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures of Prachay Capital Ltd
- Effective date of trading: June 23, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Listed under: F Group (Debt Market Segment)
- Trading form: Dematerialized only, as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market lot: One bond (unit market lot)
- Financial year end: March 31
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: KFIN Technologies Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Trading in these NCDs is governed by existing SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000) requiring bonds to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must trade in these NCDs in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be executed in unit market lots (one bond per lot)
- Members requiring clarification may contact Prachay Capital Ltd (Divya Raizada, +91 22 4079 5199, info@prachay.com) or BSE (Hardik Bhuta, AVP, 022-22728814)

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date — NCDs admitted to dealings on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing notice with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant only to trading members who intend to transact in Prachay Capital Ltd's NCDs on the BSE debt market segment. The listing provides investors access to these instruments in a regulated exchange environment with mandatory dematerialization.
