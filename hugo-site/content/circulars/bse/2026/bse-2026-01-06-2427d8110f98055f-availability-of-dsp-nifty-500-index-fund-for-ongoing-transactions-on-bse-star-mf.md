## Summary

BSE has notified that DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 07, 2026. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan). This applies to all six plan variants of the scheme covering both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from January 07, 2026
- Facilities enabled: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes are equity category funds
- Reference made to previous Notice No. 20251218-12

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational notification affecting a single mutual fund scheme on the BSE platform.
