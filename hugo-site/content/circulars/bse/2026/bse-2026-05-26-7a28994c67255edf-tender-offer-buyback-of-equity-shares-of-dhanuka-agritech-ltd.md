## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Dhanuka Agritech Ltd (Scrip Code: 507717) has fixed **29 May 2026** as the record date for determining the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in its buyback offer under the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement segment.

## Key Points

- Record date and ex-entitlement date are both set to **29 May 2026**.
- Settlement number: **DR-640/2026-2027**.
- Applicable segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement (Equity).
- Shareholders on record as of 29 May 2026 will be eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing BSE listing and buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and communicate relevant details to their clients holding Dhanuka Agritech equity shares.
- Ensure client positions are settled correctly prior to the ex-entitlement date to determine buyback eligibility.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 26 May 2026 |
| Record Date | 29 May 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | 29 May 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a targeted impact limited to shareholders of Dhanuka Agritech Ltd (BSE: 507717). Shareholders holding equity shares as of 29 May 2026 will be eligible to tender shares in the buyback offer. No broad market-wide operational or regulatory impact is expected.
