## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice (No. 20260223-5) informing trading members that the equity shares of Fractal Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company will trade under scrip code 544707 with symbol FIL on the SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code assigned: **544707**
- Trading Symbol: **FIL**
- ISIN Number: **INE1W3Q01011**
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework for SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details (Code: 544707, Symbol: FIL, ISIN: INE1W3Q01011) in preparation for the upcoming listing
- Members should await the subsequent notice from BSE for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a preliminary informational notice with limited immediate market impact. The actual trading opportunity will only arise once BSE issues the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. Investors and market participants interested in SME segment listings should monitor for the subsequent announcement. The notice covers only the SME segment, so impact is contained to participants active in that space.
