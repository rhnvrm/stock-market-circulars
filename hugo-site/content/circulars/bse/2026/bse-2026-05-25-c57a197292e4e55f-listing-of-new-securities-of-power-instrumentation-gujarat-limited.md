## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade of 4,84,600 new equity shares of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912) effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders pursuant to the conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 83.75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 73.75/-).

## Key Points

- 4,84,600 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 73.75/- (total issue price: Rs. 83.75/-)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE557Z01018
- Date of allotment: March 10, 2026
- Trading permitted from: May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to an already completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade from May 26, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 10, 2026
- **Trading commencement:** May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in expiry (2,85,000 shares, dist. nos. 19486431–19771430):** December 1, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (1,99,600 shares, dist. nos. 19771431–19971030):** December 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,84,600 shares to the tradeable float of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912). However, the majority of the allotted shares (2,85,000) are locked in until December 2027, and the remaining 1,99,600 shares until December 2026, limiting immediate dilution impact on the free float. Overall market impact is minimal and confined to existing shareholders and traders in this scrip.
