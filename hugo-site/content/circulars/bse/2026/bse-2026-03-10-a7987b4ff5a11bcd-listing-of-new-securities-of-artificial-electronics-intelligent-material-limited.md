## Summary

BSE has listed 97,53,750 new equity shares of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited (Scrip Code: 526443) with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 40/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 30/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited (Scrip Code: 526443)
- **Number of Shares:** 97,53,750 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 40/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 30 premium)
- **Allotment Type:** Preferential allotment to Non-Promoters
- **ISIN:** INE072B01027
- **Distribution Numbers:** 16965841 to 26719590
- **Pari-passu:** Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listing and permitted to trade these securities from March 11, 2026.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 97,53,750 shares (Dist. Nos. 16965841–26719590) until **September 30, 2026**; these shares cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 17, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 97.54 lakh new equity shares to the tradable float of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited, though all shares are under lock-in until September 30, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is minimal and localized to this scrip. Existing shareholders may note the dilution from the preferential allotment dated November 17, 2025.
