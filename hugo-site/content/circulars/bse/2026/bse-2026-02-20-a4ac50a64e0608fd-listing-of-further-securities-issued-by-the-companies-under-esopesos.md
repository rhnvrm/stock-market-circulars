## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that further new equity securities issued by 8 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, February 23, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 8 companies have listed new shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes effective February 23, 2026
- Total shares listed range from 5,500 (Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands) to 58,80,060 (Lloyds Engineering Works)
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Face values range from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share depending on the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new securities admitted for trading. No specific compliance action is required from market participants beyond updating records for the newly listed share ranges.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 20, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP/ESOS listings representing employee compensation shares. Details of newly listed securities:
