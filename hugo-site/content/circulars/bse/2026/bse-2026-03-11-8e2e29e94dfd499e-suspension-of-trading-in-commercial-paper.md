## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260311-61 informing trading members that 15 companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their respective Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial papers of these companies from March 13, 2026, in accordance with circular DR-839/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in commercial papers of 15 listed companies is suspended effective March 13, 2026
- All companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their commercial papers
- The suspension applies to the Debt segment of BSE
- All suspensions are issued under circular reference DR-839/2025-2026
- Most companies have a record date of March 13, 2026; UGRO Capital has a record date of March 15, 2026 but no-dealings effective from March 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing framework (DR-839/2025-2026) governing suspension of trading in commercial papers ahead of redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the commercial papers of the listed companies from March 13, 2026 onwards
- Members must update their systems to block transactions in the specified ISIN codes effective the stated no-dealings date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market action affecting commercial paper holders and traders on BSE's debt segment. The suspension prevents trading in these instruments ahead of their redemption, which is standard practice to ensure orderly settlement. Investors holding these commercial papers should expect redemption proceeds on or around the respective record dates. No impact on the equity segments of these companies. Applicable to all trading members active in the debt segment.
