## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for June 2026 for the Equity Segment. The schedule specifies the last date by which members must submit margin reports for each trade date, along with the corresponding penalty dates for late or non-reporting.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers all trading days from 01-06-2026 to 30-06-2026.
- For each trade date, the last date of margin reporting is generally 5 trading days later.
- Penalty dates follow one trading day after the last reporting date.
- The schedule extends into early July 2026 for trade dates at the end of June.
- Issued by the Department of ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule notice under existing margin reporting framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit client margin reports by the specified "Last Date of Margin Reporting" for each trade date.
- Failure to report by the deadline will result in penalties levied on the "Penalty Date" as listed in the schedule.
- Applicable to all members trading in the Equity Segment.
