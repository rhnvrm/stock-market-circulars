## Summary

BSE has revised the effective date for trading of Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code: 544517) equity shares under 'B' group from the originally scheduled date to January 27, 2026. This revision is necessitated by the declaration of January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday on account of Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares (Scrip Code: 544517) will now trade under 'B' group from Tuesday, January 27, 2026
- Original schedule has been revised due to trading holiday on January 15, 2026
- Trading holiday declared for Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra
- This notice is in continuation of Notice Nos. 20260112-8 (January 12, 2026) and 20260109-9 (January 09, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative schedule adjustment to accommodate a trading holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are informed to note the revised effective date for the group change of Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares.

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections)
- **January 27, 2026**: Revised effective date for Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares to trade under 'B' group

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a procedural adjustment affecting the timing of a single scrip's group migration. The delay of approximately 12 days (accounting for the holiday) allows for proper settlement schedules to be maintained. Market participants trading in Shankara Buildpro Limited should note the revised date for the group change implementation.
