## Summary

CMR Green Technologies Limited has been listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Wednesday, June 10, 2026, under the 'B' Group of Securities. The listing follows the company's public issue with an issue price of Rs. 192 per share (face value Rs. 2 + premium Rs. 190), with shares allotted on June 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Scrip ID / Code:** CMRGREEN / 544777
- **ISIN:** INE00WV01027
- **Total Securities:** 21,90,55,489 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 2212683 to 221268171
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 192/- per share (face value Rs. 2/-, premium Rs. 190/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Trading only in demat form** as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- **Registrar to the Issue:** KFIN Technologies Limited, Hyderabad (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is pursuant to existing SEBI guidelines and BSE listing norms. Lock-in details and shareholding pattern are provided as per Annexure I and Annexure II respectively.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in CMRGREEN are executed only in dematerialised form.
- Trading members must participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 10, 2026, as per Exchange Notice No. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012.
- Lock-in period as marked in depository records must be respected; corporate action for lock-in has been executed by the company.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 8, 2026
- **Listing & Trading Effective Date:** June 10, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of CMR Green Technologies Limited introduces a new equity scrip (CMRGREEN, code 544777) to BSE's 'B' Group, making it tradeable for retail and institutional investors. With over 219 million shares admitted to dealings, liquidity will depend on the market's reception post-SPOS. The SPOS mechanism helps establish a fair opening price on day one. Investors who received IPO allotments at Rs. 192/share can trade from June 10, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to certain categories of shareholders as detailed in Annexure I.
