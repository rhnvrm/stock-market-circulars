## Summary

BSE has issued an updated notice on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing multiple prior notices dating back to 2018. Under the revised framework, securities identified in Annexure I (Part A) will be subject to 100% margin requirements effective July 21, 2026, applicable to all open positions as of July 20, 2026 and new positions created thereafter. A revised lower price band will apply to these securities from July 17, 2026. Additionally, securities listed in Annexure I (Part B) will move to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework effective July 17, 2026. Separately, securities under Annexure I (Part D) will continue in the LT-ASM framework but will be transferred to the T/XT/MT/TS trading group effective July 21, 2026. Securities listed in Annexure II will exit the LT-ASM framework effective July 17, 2026. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- LT-ASM framework revised per prior notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, and June 04, 2021.
- 100% margin applicable on all open positions as of July 20, 2026 and new positions from July 21, 2026 onwards, for securities in Annexure I (Part A).
- Lower price band applicable from July 17, 2026 for securities in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) move to higher LT-ASM stages effective July 17, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) continue in LT-ASM but shift to T/XT/MT/TS trading group effective July 21, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure II exit the LT-ASM framework effective July 17, 2026.
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting under the framework is based on surveillance criteria (including XBRL submissions by listed companies) and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the company.
- Price band for a scrip exiting the framework will be reinstated to its pre-shortlisting level, unless it remains subject to another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

The notice applies BSE's revised LT-ASM framework (as previously communicated via notices in 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2024) to specific securities, imposing enhanced margin requirements, modified price bands, and trading group reclassification as surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and market participants must apply 100% margins on affected securities per the effective dates, observe the revised price bands, and account for the trading group transfer for securities moving to T/XT/MT/TS groups. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 17, 2026: Lower price band effective for Annexure I (Part A) securities; securities move to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I Part B); securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II).
- July 20, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 21, 2026: 100% margin effective on open and new positions (Annexure I Part A); securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS group (Annexure I Part D).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a high impact on trading in the affected securities due to the imposition of 100% margins, which significantly raises the cost of taking or holding positions and reduces leverage. The price band revisions and trading group transfers further constrain trading flexibility and could reduce liquidity in the affected scrips. Investors and traders holding positions in securities named in the annexures should review their exposure ahead of the effective dates.
