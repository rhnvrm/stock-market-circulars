## Summary

BSE has invoked the alternate trading venue for the Equity Derivative Segment, making NSE exclusive scrips available for trading from 12:40 PM on January 10, 2026. This follows the earlier exchange notice dated January 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Alternate trading venue invoked for Equity Derivatives segment
- NSE exclusive scrips in equity derivatives now available for trading on BSE
- Trading commenced from 12:40 PM on January 10, 2026
- This action follows exchange notice 20260109-18 dated January 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is an operational intervention to ensure market continuity through alternate trading arrangements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the availability of NSE exclusive derivative scrips on BSE platform
- Members should adjust their trading systems and operations to accommodate the alternate venue
- Standard trading rules and compliance requirements remain applicable

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Original exchange notice issued (20260109-18)
- **January 10, 2026, 12:40 PM**: NSE exclusive scrips made available for trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

The invocation of the alternate trading venue has significant operational impact on the equity derivatives market. Trading members gain access to NSE exclusive derivative instruments through BSE's platform, ensuring market continuity and providing redundancy in trading infrastructure. This affects all participants trading equity derivatives and requires immediate operational adjustments to trading systems and workflows.
