## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 20, 2026. The session is intended for testing and familiarization purposes; trades executed during the session carry no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no legal rights or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be conducted from BSE's DR site for the Currency Derivatives segment on June 20, 2026 (Saturday).
- Members may participate in this session or the UAT environment to fulfil regulatory requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.
- Trading members using third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house systems via ETI APIs can test their applications during this session.
- Mock trades will not attract any margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or create any rights and liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 1:00 PM on the day of the mock session.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This notice references existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 (November 24, 2020) and BSE Notice No. 20201201-22 (December 1, 2020) regarding testing of trading and risk management software.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may participate in the mock session or the UAT environment to meet their regulatory obligations for DR readiness testing.
- Members using third-party or in-house trading systems via ETI APIs are encouraged to test various functionalities, including exceptional market conditions.
- Feedback must be submitted to BSE by 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026, via the contact details provided.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session from DR site.
  - Log-in: 8:15 AM – 9:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives): 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  - Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives): 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  - Trade Modification (Currency): from 12:10 PM
  - Trade Modification (Cross Currency): from 12:10 PM
- **Feedback deadline:** 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational exercise with no market impact. Participation is voluntary and mock trades carry no financial obligations. The session is relevant primarily to trading members active in the Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments who wish to validate their DR readiness or meet regulatory testing requirements.
