## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF units effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 TR Index, managed by Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited. The units will be listed in the 'B' Group and traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Motilal Oswal Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF
- Listing Date: January 19, 2026
- Total Units: 4,200,000 units
- Scrip Code: 590148
- ISIN: INF247L01CH6
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 16.34 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- BOLT System ID: MOSMALL250
- Date of Allotment: March 15, 2024
- Trading: Dematerialized form only as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification following existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade ETF units in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Members should contact designated personnel for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Effective listing and trading commencement date
- **March 15, 2024**: Date of allotment

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it is a routine ETF listing. The ETF provides investors exposure to the Nifty Smallcap 250 TR Index through a passively managed fund. Three market makers have been appointed (East India Securities Ltd., Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited) to ensure liquidity. The listing expands investment options for market participants seeking smallcap exposure through an ETF structure.
