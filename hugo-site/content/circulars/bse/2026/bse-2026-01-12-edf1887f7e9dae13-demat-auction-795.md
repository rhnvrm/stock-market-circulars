## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities and quantities subject to Demat Auction under Settlement No. 795 dated January 12, 2026. The auction lists approximately 103 scrips across various sectors with their respective quantities that need to be auctioned due to delivery failures or shortages in the settlement system.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 795
- Settlement Date: January 12, 2026
- Total scrips listed: Approximately 103 securities
- Includes major stocks like ITC Ltd (95 shares), Tata Steel (100 shares), Vodafone Idea (5,800 shares)
- Highest quantity: Jaiprakash Power (9,999 shares)
- Wide range of companies from banking, manufacturing, pharma, telecom, and other sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine auction settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities for auction settlement
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's auction settlement procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: 795

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on overall market operations. This is a routine operational activity to close out failed delivery obligations through the auction mechanism. Impact is confined to:

- Trading members with delivery shortfalls in the listed securities
- Clients whose trades are subject to auction settlement
- Price discovery may be affected for the specific quantities under auction
- Securities include both high-value stocks (ITC, Siemens, HCL Tech) and smaller companies

The auction mechanism ensures settlement finality and maintains market integrity by addressing delivery failures.
