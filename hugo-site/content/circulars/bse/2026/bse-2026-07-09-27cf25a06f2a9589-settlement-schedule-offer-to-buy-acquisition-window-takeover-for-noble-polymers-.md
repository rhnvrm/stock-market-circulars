## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL) has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Noble Polymers Limited. The bidding window runs from July 10, 2026 to July 23, 2026, with obligation download on August 5, 2026 and final settlement on August 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2026087
- Bid Start Date: 10/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 23/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 05/08/2026
- Settlement Date: 06/08/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026087 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- Members/Custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for Revised Guidelines on Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members & Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational settlement notice under existing acquisition window mechanisms, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in of securities in the depository system must be completed sufficiently in advance of the closure of the BSE OTB bidding window and the custodian confirmation window
- Members/Custodians must use the correct Market Type and Settlement Number when processing early pay-in

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: July 10, 2026
- Bid End Date: July 23, 2026
- Obligation Download: August 5, 2026
- Settlement Date: August 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, operational notice affecting only members, custodians, and participants involved in the Noble Polymers Limited takeover acquisition window. It has no broader market-wide impact but is essential for accurate settlement processing by involved parties within the specified timelines.
