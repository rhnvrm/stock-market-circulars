## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned code 544718 with symbol PNGSREVA and ISIN INE1RDG01013. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544718
- Trading Symbol: PNGSREVA
- ISIN: INE1RDG01013
- Actual listing date to be announced via a subsequent notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard pre-listing intimation circular informing trading members of an upcoming new equity listing on the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the upcoming listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited. They should await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and security details before initiating any trading activity in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broad market impact as it pertains solely to the upcoming listing of one new company, PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited. Trading members should prepare their systems to accommodate the new scrip (PNGSREVA, code 544718) once the listing date is formally confirmed. Investors interested in this jewellery sector company should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice detailing the listing date and any additional security information.
