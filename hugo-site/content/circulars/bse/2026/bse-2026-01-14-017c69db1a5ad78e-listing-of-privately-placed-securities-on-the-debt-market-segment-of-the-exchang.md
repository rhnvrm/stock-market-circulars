## Summary

BSE has listed new privately placed debt securities from Aditya Birla Capital Ltd on the debt market segment effective January 14, 2026. Two debt instruments have been admitted to trading: ABCL-7.60%-20-7-35-PVT (20,400 units) and ABCL-7.24%-18-2-31-PVT (500 units), both rated AAA by multiple rating agencies.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities from Aditya Birla Capital Ltd listed on BSE Debt Segment
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Tick size for both securities is 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1 for both instruments
- Both securities carry AAA credit ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, and IND

## Security Details

**Security 1: ABCL-7.60%-20-7-35-PVT**
- ISIN: INE674K07051
- Scrip Code: 976931
- Quantity: 20,400 units
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 103,005.53
- Interest Rate: 7.6% p.a. (annually)
- Allotment Date: January 12, 2026
- Interest Payment: Annually from July 23, 2026 to July 20, 2035
- Redemption Date: July 20, 2035
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA, ICRA AAA

**Security 2: ABCL-7.24%-18-2-31-PVT**
- ISIN: INE860H07HE8
- Scrip Code: 960492
- Quantity: 500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,000,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,056,002.82
- Interest Rate: 7.24% p.a. (annually)
- Allotment Date: January 12, 2026
- Interest Payment: Annually from February 18, 2022 to February 18, 2031
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2031
- Credit Rating: ICRA AAA, IND AAA

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Refer to Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: January 12, 2026
- Listing and Trading Start Date: January 14, 2026
- First Interest Payment (Security 1): July 23, 2026
- Redemption Date (Security 2): February 18, 2031
- Redemption Date (Security 1): July 20, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The listing affects only participants in the BSE debt market segment and provides additional trading opportunities in AAA-rated debt instruments from Aditya Birla Capital Ltd. The securities are privately placed and primarily relevant to institutional investors and debt market participants.
