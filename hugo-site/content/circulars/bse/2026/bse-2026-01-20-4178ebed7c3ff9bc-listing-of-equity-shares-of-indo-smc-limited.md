## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of INDO SMC Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade under scrip code 544681 and symbol INDOSMC with ISIN INE0WKY01013. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- INDO SMC Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544681
- Symbol: INDOSMC
- ISIN: INE0WKY01013
- Notice Number: 20260120-10
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to prepare for the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine SME listing announcement. The listing will add one more security to the BSE SME platform. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details once the listing date is confirmed.
