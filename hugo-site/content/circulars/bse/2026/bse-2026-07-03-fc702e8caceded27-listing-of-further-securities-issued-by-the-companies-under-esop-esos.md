## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 10 listed companies have been approved for listing and trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 06, 2026. The notice details the ISIN, scrip code, number of shares, face value, and distinctive number ranges for each company's newly issued shares.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have new ESOP/ESOS-issued equity shares listed and admitted for trading effective July 06, 2026
- Companies covered: Max India Limited (60,000 shares), Bliss GVS Pharma Limited (267,250 shares), HDB Financial Services Limited (28,108 shares), IDFC First Bank Limited (1,510,378 shares), Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited (90,975 shares), Rainbow Children's Medicare Limited (7,483 shares), Renaissance Global Limited (34,590 shares), Sansera Engineering Limited (71,520 shares), UTI Asset Management Company Limited (843 shares), Valiant Organics Limited (15,000 shares)
- All shares are listed with no lock-in restrictions (marked "NA" for lock-in status)
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing of these additional securities for accurate trade processing, settlement, and record-keeping from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: Monday, July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and largely operational. These are incremental share issuances under employee stock option programs, resulting in modest equity dilution for the respective companies. The volumes involved are small relative to overall market capitalization for most of these companies, so no material price impact is expected. Trading members should ensure their systems reflect the updated share counts and distinctive number ranges for accurate settlement from July 06, 2026 onward.
