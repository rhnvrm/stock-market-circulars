## Summary

Advit Jewels Limited (Scrip Code: 544803, Symbol: RAMBHAJO, ISIN: INE1SJO01012) will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026, under the 'T' Group of Securities. The scrip will be in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** July 1, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Scrip Code:** 544803 | **Scrip ID / Symbol:** RAMBHAJO
- **ISIN:** INE1SJO01012
- **Group:** T Group (Trade-for-Trade for 10 trading days)
- **Total Shares Listed:** 4,58,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 45,810,000
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 138/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 128/-)
- **Date of Allotment:** June 29, 2026
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Pari Pasu:** Yes
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only, as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Scrip will participate in Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days post-listing
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring dematerialised trading only
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) as per BSE notice 20120216-29

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must trade Advit Jewels Limited shares only in dematerialised form
- During the 10-day Trade-for-Trade period, trades must result in actual delivery; netting/squaring off intraday is not permitted
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I of the notice
- Shareholding pattern is as per Annexure II of the notice
- Corporate action for lock-in marking in depository records has been executed by the company

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **July 1, 2026:** Listing and commencement of trading on BSE (Wednesday)
- **July 1, 2026:** Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for the scrip
- **10 trading days from July 1, 2026:** Trade-for-Trade restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing of a small jewellery company. The T Group classification and Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 days restricts intraday speculation, ensuring delivery-based trading only in the initial period — a standard SEBI-mandated safeguard for newly listed securities. The issue price of Rs. 138/- against a face value of Rs. 10/- reflects a premium of Rs. 128/-. The registrar is Bigshare Services Private Limited. Impact is limited to investors who participated in the IPO and trading members who handle this scrip.
