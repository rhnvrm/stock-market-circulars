## Summary

BSE announces the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for shares of Aanchal Ispat Limited (ISIN: INE322R01022). The seller, Mukesh Goel, who is part of the promoter and promoter group, will conduct the OFS over two trading days. BSE Ltd has been declared as the designated stock exchange, with NSE Clearing Limited as the designated clearing corporation. The offer is being conducted pursuant to SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 and SEBI OFS Circulars.

## Key Points

- Seller: Mukesh Goel (Promoter category)
- Company: Aanchal Ispat Limited
- ISIN: INE322R01022
- Designated Stock Exchange: BSE Ltd
- Designated Clearing Corporation: NSE Clearing Limited
- Two-day trading window with separate sessions for Non-Retail and Retail Investors
- Non-Retail Investors have option to carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard OFS procedure conducted under existing SEBI OFS Circulars and SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Prospective investors and brokers must read the entire Offer Notice dated February 02, 2026 along with SEBI OFS Circulars before participating
- Investors must adhere to category-specific bidding timelines
- Non-Retail Investors must explicitly indicate willingness to carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day if desired

## Important Dates

- **T Day (February 03, 2026)**: Non-Retail Investors bidding session
  - Trading hours: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM IST
  - Non-Retail Investors can indicate willingness to carry forward un-allotted bids

- **T+1 Day (February 04, 2026)**: Retail Investors and carry-forward Non-Retail Investors bidding session
  - Available for Retail Investors and Non-Retail Investors who chose to carry forward un-allotted bids
  - Allocation from unsubscribed portion of Retail Category

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Promoter stake dilution through OFS may impact share price and liquidity of Aanchal Ispat Limited. The two-day structure allows price discovery and broader participation across investor categories.

**Investor Impact**: Non-Retail Investors get first opportunity on T Day with option to participate in Retail Category unsubscribed portion on T+1 Day. Retail Investors have dedicated access on T+1 Day along with carry-forward bids from Non-Retail category.

**Operational Impact**: Standard OFS trading window mechanism on BSE platform with NSE Clearing handling settlement operations.
