## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,00,000 new equity shares of Atal Realtech Limited (Scrip Code: 543911) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, March 10, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to the Promoter pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 9,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 14/- (issue price Rs. 16/-) to Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 122996251 to 123896250
- ISIN: INE0ALR01029
- Trading effective from March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from March 10, 2026
- Lock-in applies to all 9,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 122996251–123896250) until September 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 10, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 9,00,000 shares expands the total share capital of Atal Realtech Limited. However, all newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period until September 30, 2027, meaning they cannot be traded until that date. No immediate liquidity impact on the open market is expected.
