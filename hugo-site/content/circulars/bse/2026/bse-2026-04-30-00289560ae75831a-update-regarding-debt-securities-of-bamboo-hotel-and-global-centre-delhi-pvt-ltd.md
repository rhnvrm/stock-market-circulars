## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Bamboo Hotel And Global Centre (Delhi) Pvt Ltd, continuing from earlier notices dated February 4, 2025 and February 18, 2025. The amendment revises the Project Commercial COD (Commercial Operation Date) clause under the Debenture Trust Deed (DTD), extending the deadline by six months. The revised terms will be effective from Monday, May 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities (Scrip Codes 976378 and 976414) are affected by this amendment
- ISINs involved: INE755L07015 and INE755L07023
- The Project Commercial COD has been extended from March 31, 2026 to September 30, 2026
- Amendment applies to clause 1.1 under the Debenture Trust Deed (DTD)
- This is a continuation of earlier Exchange Notices 20250204-37 and 20250218-20

## Regulatory Changes

Amendment to clause 1.1 under the Debenture Trust Deed (DTD):

| Condition | Existing | Amended |
|---|---|---|
| Project Commercial COD | March 31, 2026 | September 30, 2026 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are requested to note the revised terms for the affected scrip codes
- Members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade at +91 22 2272 8597 or debt.listing@bseindia.com, or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) for further details

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Monday, May 4, 2026 — revised terms become applicable for trading
- **Notice Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Amended Project Commercial COD:** September 30, 2026 (previously March 31, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the two specific debt securities issued by Bamboo Hotel And Global Centre (Delhi) Pvt Ltd. The six-month extension of the Project Commercial COD signals a delay in the company's commercial operations, which may affect the risk profile of these instruments. Trading members dealing in these securities should update their records to reflect the revised terms effective May 4, 2026. Broader market impact is minimal given the niche nature of these private company debentures.
