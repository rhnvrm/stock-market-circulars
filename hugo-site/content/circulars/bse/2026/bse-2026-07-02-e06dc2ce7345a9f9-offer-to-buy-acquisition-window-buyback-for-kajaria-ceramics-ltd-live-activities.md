## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of live activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of KAJARIA CERAMICS LTD, to be conducted from Friday, 3rd July 2026 to Thursday, 9th July 2026 on the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Security: KAJARIA CERAMICS LTD (Symbol: KAJARIACER, ISIN: INE217B01036, Scrip Code: 500233)
- Offer Size: Up to 21,50,000 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Rs. 1.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 1380.00 per share
- Offer Period: Friday, 3rd July 2026 to Thursday, 9th July 2026
- Bid Offering Period and Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 3rd–9th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 3rd–8th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up on last day (9th July 2026): 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up on last day (9th July 2026): 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual
- Access is via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (Internet and Leased Line URLs provided)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational schedule notice referencing existing Exchange notices (20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017) for operational instructions on OTB participation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Investor's UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for Investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet.
- Members/Custodians needing new login IDs or password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet should contact Trading Operations (022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Opening Date: Friday, 3rd July 2026
- Closing Date: Thursday, 9th July 2026
- Give-up (last day of offer): 9th July 2026, 9:15 a.m.–3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 9th July 2026, 9:15 a.m.–4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is primarily operational, informing Trading and Custodian Members of the timing windows for bid placement, modification, cancellation, and give-up/take-up activities related to the KAJARIA CERAMICS LTD buyback. It ensures smooth execution of the buyback process on the Exchange platform but does not alter the terms of the buyback itself.
