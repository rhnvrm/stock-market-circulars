## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260602-34 reminding Research Analysts of their obligation under the Ease of Doing Investment (EoDI) framework to disclose their registered name and SEBI registration number on all social media platforms (SMPs). The notice references Exchange master circular No. 20260430-46 dated April 30, 2026, and specifically calls on Research Analysts to keep their social media details updated promptly whenever any changes occur.

## Key Points

- Research Analysts must display their SEBI-registered name and registration number on their social media profiles/platforms.
- This obligation is part of the broader EoDI (Ease of Doing Investment) initiative, governed by BSE master circular No. 20260430-46 dated April 30, 2026 (point 23).
- Research Analysts must promptly update their social media details on their platform whenever any changes occur.
- The notice is issued by the Membership Department and is directed at the Research Analysts segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice serves as a reminder and reinforcement of existing requirements under the EoDI framework as set out in the Exchange master circular No. 20260430-46 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Research Analysts must ensure their registered name and SEBI registration number are displayed on all social media platforms where they maintain a presence.
- Any changes to social media details must be updated promptly on the respective platform.
- Research Analysts are required to ensure full compliance with point 23 of Exchange master circular No. 20260430-46.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Reference Circular:** Master Circular No. 20260430-46 dated April 30, 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned; compliance is expected on an ongoing basis.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It is a targeted compliance reminder addressed solely to Research Analysts, requiring them to maintain accurate and updated disclosures on social media platforms. No trading, listing, or operational disruptions are expected. Non-compliance could attract regulatory scrutiny from SEBI or BSE's Membership Compliance department.
