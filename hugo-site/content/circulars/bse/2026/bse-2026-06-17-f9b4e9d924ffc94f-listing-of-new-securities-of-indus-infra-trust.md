## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,52,10,084 new units of Indus Infra Trust (Scrip Code: 544137, ISIN: INE0NHL23019) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 18, 2026. The units were allotted to the Sponsor at Rs. 119/- per unit on a preferential basis for cash consideration, with allotment dated June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,52,10,084 units allotted to Sponsor at Rs. 119/- per unit on a preferential basis
- Units listed and available for trading from June 18, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 44,29,38,606 to 46,81,48,689
- Units rank pari-passu with existing units of the trust
- Significant lock-in restrictions apply to the newly listed units

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the lock-in restrictions before executing transactions in the newly listed units:
- 37,81,513 units (Dist. Nos. 44,29,38,606–44,67,20,118) are locked in until June 19, 2029
- 2,14,28,571 units (Dist. Nos. 44,67,20,119–46,81,48,689) are locked in until June 19, 2027

Locked-in units cannot be traded until their respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 18, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (37,81,513 units):** June 19, 2029
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,14,28,571 units):** June 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 2.52 crore new units to the tradable float of Indus Infra Trust, though the effective free float increase is limited due to lock-in restrictions on all newly issued units until 2027 or 2029. The preferential allotment to the Sponsor at Rs. 119/- reflects the trust's unit price at the time of allotment. Market impact is expected to be moderate as lock-in periods prevent immediate secondary market trading of these units.
