## Summary

BSE Ltd, through its Surveillance Department (DOSS), sought clarifications from four listed companies to verify the accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels. This is done to inform members and investors about the veracity of such news reports. Replies have been received from two companies, while two others have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- Companies that have replied to BSE's clarification request:
  - Happiest Minds Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 543227) — news source: moneycontrol.com
  - Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 542904) — news source: bfsi.economictimes.indiatimes.com
- Companies whose reply is still awaited:
  - Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) — news source: thehindubusinessline.com
  - Gensol Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 542851) — news source: bfsi.economictimes.indiatimes.com
- The actual reply letters from Happiest Minds and Ujjivan Small Finance Bank are attached to the notice.
- Issued for and on behalf of BSE Ltd by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine exchange communication under BSE's ongoing market surveillance process rather than a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rajesh Exports Ltd and Gensol Engineering Ltd are required to submit their clarification replies to BSE regarding the referenced news articles.
- Companies receiving such queries are expected to respond in line with exchange surveillance and disclosure norms (e.g., SEBI LODR requirements on market rumour verification).

## Important Dates

- Notice date: 13 July 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for the pending replies from Rajesh Exports Ltd and Gensol Engineering Ltd.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is a standard surveillance measure and does not itself indicate any confirmed wrongdoing or material development. However, pending clarifications from Rajesh Exports Ltd and Gensol Engineering Ltd may keep these stocks under heightened market and investor scrutiny until their responses are received and disclosed. The replies already received from Happiest Minds Technologies and Ujjivan Small Finance Bank provide clarity to the market on the specific news reports concerning them.
