## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned Scrip Code 544718, trading symbol PNGSREVA, and ISIN INE1RDG01013. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE.
- Scrip Code: **544718**, Symbol: **PNGSREVA**, ISIN: **INE1RDG01013**.
- Exact listing date has not yet been announced; a separate notice will follow.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details and await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date before initiating trades.
- Members should update their systems with the assigned Scrip Code (544718) and ISIN (INE1RDG01013) in preparation for commencement of trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited on BSE, relevant to investors and trading members tracking new IPO listings in the gems and jewellery sector. The immediate market impact is limited as trading cannot commence until the official listing date notice is issued. Members should monitor BSE announcements for the follow-up circular confirming the effective listing date.
