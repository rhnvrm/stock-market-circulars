## Summary

BSE has notified market participants of an Offer for Sale (OFS) by the President of India, acting through the Ministry of Finance (Government of India), as promoter of General Insurance Corporation of India (GICRE). The OFS will occur on June 16–17, 2026, offering up to 35,088,000 equity shares (2.00% of total paid-up capital) at a floor price of Rs. 352 per share, with an option to sell an additional 5,26,32,000 shares (3.00%) if oversubscription is exercised.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** President of India via Ministry of Finance (promoter of GICRE)
- **Base Offer Size:** 35,088,000 equity shares — 2.00% of total paid-up equity share capital
- **Oversubscription Option:** Additional 5,26,32,000 equity shares (3.00%); if exercised, total offer = 8,77,20,000 shares (5.00%)
- **Floor Price:** Rs. 352/- per equity share
- **T Day (June 16, 2026):** Open exclusively for non-Retail Investors
- **T+1 Day (June 17, 2026):** Open for Retail Investors, Employees, and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids from T Day
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 20,000 equity shares available to eligible employees of GICRE, with a per-employee application cap of Rs. 5,00,000, subject to competent authority approval
- The OFS will be conducted through the designated OFS window on both BSE and NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is governed by existing SEBI frameworks:
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 (December 28, 2018) — Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 (January 10, 2023) — Comprehensive Framework on OFS
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/6 (January 23, 2024) — OFS Framework for Employee Shares
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 (June 14, 2024) — Updated Employee OFS Framework
- BSE notice nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, 20240221-47, 20240701-19 (Revised OFS Bidding Guidelines)

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians** must follow the revised OFS bidding guidelines as referenced in prior BSE notices
- **Non-Retail Investors** must submit bids only on T Day (June 16, 2026); they may carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day
- **Retail Investors** may bid only on T+1 Day (June 17, 2026)
- **Eligible Employees** must bid within the Rs. 5,00,000 application limit and subject to competent authority approval
- All participants must use the designated OFS window on BSE/NSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant government divestment in a major public sector general insurer. The offer represents 2–5% of GICRE's paid-up capital depending on whether the oversubscription option is exercised, introducing notable secondary market supply. The floor price of Rs. 352/- per share sets a reference point for institutional pricing. Retail investors, who can only participate on T+1 Day, will have visibility into T Day price discovery before bidding. Employee participation is limited and capped, reducing dilution risk for that tranche. Overall market impact on GICRE's share price is expected to be moderate-to-high in the short term given the size of the offer relative to typical daily volumes.
