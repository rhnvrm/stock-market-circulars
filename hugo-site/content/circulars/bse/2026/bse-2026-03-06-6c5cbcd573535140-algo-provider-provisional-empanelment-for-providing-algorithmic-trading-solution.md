## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as an Algorithmic Trading solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately as of Notice No. 20260306-5 dated 06 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Stratzy Fintech Private Limited has been granted provisional empanelment as an Algo provider on BSE.
- The vendor is located at Flat No 1501, Center Point, Mulund East, Mumbai – 400081.
- Contact number: +91 8291113955.
- The circular is applicable with immediate effect.
- The updated list of all empanelled Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative update to the list of approved Algo providers on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members wishing to use algorithmic trading solutions may now engage Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as their Algo provider. No mandatory compliance action is required from trading members as a result of this circular.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 06 March 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This circular expands the pool of available Algo providers for BSE trading members by adding one new vendor. It does not affect existing trading operations, regulations, or compliance obligations. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions have an additional option to consider.
