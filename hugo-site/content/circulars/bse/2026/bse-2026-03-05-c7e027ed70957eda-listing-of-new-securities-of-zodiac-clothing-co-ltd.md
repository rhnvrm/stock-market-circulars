## Summary

BSE has listed 1,464,414 new equity shares of Zodiac Clothing Co. Ltd. (Scrip Code: 521163, ISIN: INE206B01013) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 6, 2026, subject to a lock-in period until October 14, 2027.

## Key Points

- 1,464,414 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 92.43 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 102.43 per share
- Allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 25993718 to 27458131
- Effective date for trading: March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading from March 6, 2026. No additional compliance action is required from general trading members beyond awareness of the lock-in restriction on all 1,464,414 shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 16, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 14, 2027 (all 1,464,414 shares: Dist. Nos. 25993718 to 27458131)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The newly listed shares are entirely under promoter lock-in until October 2027, meaning they will not be available for trading in the open market during this period. The listing is a routine administrative action following a preferential allotment. General market liquidity for this scrip is unaffected in the short term.
