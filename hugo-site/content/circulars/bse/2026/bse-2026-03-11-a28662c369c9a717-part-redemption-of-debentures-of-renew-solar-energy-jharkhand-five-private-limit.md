## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ReNew Solar Energy (Jharkhand Five) Private Limited has fixed a record date for payment of interest and part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in these debentures will occur with a reduced face value from 13 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: ReNew Solar Energy (Jharkhand Five) Private Limited
- Debenture series: RSEJFPL-8.44%-31-8-29-PVT (ISIN: INE154Z07011, Scrip Code: 976003)
- Record date: 15 March 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced face value: Rs. 92,210/- per debenture
- Effective date for reduced face value trading: 13 March 2026
- Settlement: DR-839/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action under existing BSE debt market norms for part redemption of privately placed NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that from 13 March 2026, trades in the debentures of ReNew Solar Energy (Jharkhand Five) Private Limited (ISIN: INE154Z07011) will be executed at the reduced face value of Rs. 92,210/- per debenture.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 15 March 2026
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading: 13 March 2026 (Settlement DR-839/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine part redemption event for a privately placed NCD. Impact is limited to holders of the specific debenture series (ISIN: INE154Z07011). Trading members holding or dealing in these securities must update their systems to reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 92,210/- per debenture from 13 March 2026. No broader market impact is anticipated.
