## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued an updated Master Circular for the Equity Cash Segment, consolidating all applicable circulars and notices up to March 31, 2026. This circular is issued in compliance with SEBI's Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/POD 3/CIR/P/2023/58 dated April 20, 2023, which mandates stock exchanges, clearing corporations, and depositories to issue master circulars. The master circular supersedes all prior notices and the existing master circular relevant to the Equity Cash Segment.

## Key Points

- ICCL has published a consolidated Master Circular for the Equity Cash Segment, updated till March 31, 2026
- The circular is effective from the date of its issue (April 29, 2026)
- All prior notices and the existing master circular for the Equity Cash Segment stand rescinded upon issuance of this master circular
- The full consolidated circular is attached as Annexure 1 (Annexure1EqCashMasterCircularTill31Mar2026)
- In case of inconsistency between the master circular and any applicable notice, the relevant notice takes precedence
- The master circular is published on ICCL's website under Home > Downloads > Master Circulars

## Regulatory Changes

This master circular rescinds all previously issued notices and the existing master circular for the Equity Cash Segment. However, a savings clause ensures continuity:
- Any action taken or contemplated under rescinded guidelines before this circular's commencement remains valid
- Rights, privileges, obligations, liabilities, and penalties accrued under rescinded guidelines remain unaffected
- Ongoing investigations, legal proceedings, or remedies are preserved as if the rescinded guidelines were never rescinded

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must take note of the updated Master Circular for the Equity Cash Segment
- Members should refer to the relevant notice in case of any inconsistency with the master circular
- The master circular serves as the primary reference document for all Equity Cash Segment transactions and settlement operations going forward
- Members can contact ICCL for clarifications via phone (022-69158590 / 45720490 / 45720690) or email (iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 29, 2026 (date of issue)
- **Content Updated Till:** March 31, 2026
- **Reference SEBI Circular Date:** April 20, 2023 (SEBI/HO/MRD/POD 3/CIR/P/2023/58)

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant regulatory document for all clearing members and market participants operating in the BSE Equity Cash Segment. As a master circular, it consolidates all operational rules, procedures, and compliance requirements into a single reference document, reducing ambiguity and improving regulatory clarity. The rescission of prior notices simplifies compliance by establishing a single authoritative source. Market participants should review the full Annexure 1 to ensure their operations align with the consolidated guidelines. The savings clause protects ongoing proceedings and previously accrued rights, minimizing disruption during the transition.
