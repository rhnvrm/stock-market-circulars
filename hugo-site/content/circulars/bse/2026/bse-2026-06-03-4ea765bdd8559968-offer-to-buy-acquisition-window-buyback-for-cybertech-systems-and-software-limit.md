## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for Cybertech Systems and Software Limited (Scrip Code: 532173, Symbol: CYBERTECH). The buyback window runs from Thursday, June 4, 2026 to Wednesday, June 10, 2026, at an offer price of Rs. 170 per share for up to 8,50,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Cybertech Systems and Software Limited (CYBERTECH), ISIN: INE214A01019, Scrip Code: 532173
- **Offer Size:** Up to 8,50,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 170.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Thursday, June 4, 2026
- **Closing Date:** Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (with Take-up on last day until 4:00 p.m.)
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalises an existing buyback mechanism under BSE's OTB (Offer to Buy) platform framework, referencing prior operational circulars (20150930-1 and 20170203-29).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- For new login IDs or password resets on iBBS and RTRMS platforms, contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant for trading members and custodian members participating in the Cybertech Systems buyback. The offer is limited in scope — up to 8,50,000 shares at Rs. 170 — and targets demat shareholders across eligible investor categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, QIB, NIB, Company, Individual). Market impact is contained to participants in this specific OTB window. Members must verify UCC details and platform access before the opening date of June 4, 2026.
