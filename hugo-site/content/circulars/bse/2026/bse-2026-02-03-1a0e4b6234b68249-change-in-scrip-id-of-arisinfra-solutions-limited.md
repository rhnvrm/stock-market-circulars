## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the Scrip ID for Arisinfra Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544419) will be changed in the BOLT Plus System from "ARISINFRA" to "ARIS" effective February 09, 2026, as per the company's request.

## Key Points

- Company affected: Arisinfra Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544419
- Old Scrip ID: ARISINFRA
- New Scrip ID: ARIS
- System: BOLT Plus
- Change requested by the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading system identifier.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the new Scrip ID
- Update internal systems to reflect the new identifier
- Ensure proper routing of orders using the new Scrip ID from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is purely an administrative change affecting the trading system identifier.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to use the new Scrip ID "ARIS" instead of "ARISINFRA" from February 09, 2026. No impact on existing holdings or trading operations.
