## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544684, symbol DIGILOGIC, and ISIN INE1OOT01028. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Digilogic Systems Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544684
- Trading Symbol: DIGILOGIC
- ISIN Number: INE1OOT01028
- Notice Number: 20260127-3
- Category: Company related (SME)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new listing and prepare their systems for trading when the listing date is announced
- No immediate compliance actions required pending the separate notice with listing date details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Details of securities: To be informed separately

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a single company listing on the SME segment. Impact is primarily relevant to:
- Investors interested in the SME segment
- Trading members who need to update their systems for the new scrip
- Digilogic Systems Limited shareholders who will gain liquidity through exchange trading

The listing expands investment opportunities in the SME space but does not affect broader market operations or existing securities.
