## Summary

BSE has issued an update, in continuation of its earlier notice dated January 31, 2022, regarding the surveillance measure applicable to companies with high Promoter as well as non-Promoter 'Encumbrance' as per Regulation 28(3) of the SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011. The notice specifies securities newly added to this framework, securities being removed from it, and provides a consolidated list of securities currently under the measure.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under this encumbrance-based surveillance measure and will attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The enhanced margin applies from July 14, 2026, covering all open positions as of July 13, 2026, as well as any new positions created on or after July 14, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are eligible to exit the framework effective July 10, 2026.
- Annexure III contains the consolidated list of all securities currently covered under this measure.
- This measure operates alongside all other surveillance measures currently imposed by the Exchange and will be subject to periodic review.
- BSE clarifies that inclusion of a security under this measure should not be interpreted as an adverse action against the company or entity concerned.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update/review under the existing surveillance measure first introduced via BSE notice no. 20220131-43 dated January 31, 2022, which tracks companies with high combined promoter and non-promoter share encumbrance under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must apply a minimum 75% margin on securities listed in Annexure I for both Equity and Equity Derivatives segments starting July 14, 2026.
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the removal of Annexure II securities from the enhanced margin framework effective July 10, 2026.
- Members must refer to the consolidated Annexure III list to track all securities currently under this measure.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Securities in Annexure II become eligible to exit the framework.
- July 13, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions in Annexure I securities.
- July 14, 2026: Minimum 75% margin becomes effective for Annexure I securities on open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

This measure increases trading costs and reduces leverage for the specific securities newly added to the high-encumbrance surveillance list, potentially dampening trading volumes and liquidity in those stocks. Conversely, securities exiting the framework will see reduced margin burdens, potentially improving trading activity. The overall market impact is limited to the affected securities rather than being systemic, though it signals heightened regulatory scrutiny on companies with significant promoter and non-promoter share encumbrance.
