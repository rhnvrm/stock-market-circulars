## Summary

THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 04, 2026. The fund will support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP). This notice references previous Notice No. 20260108-42 regarding the fund's availability.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: February 04, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options)
- All schemes categorized as DEBT funds
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- Three Regular Plan variants: Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment
- Three Direct Plan variants: Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plan Options
1. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF2F0001388)
2. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT (ISIN: INF2F0001396)
3. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT (ISIN: INF2F0001404)

### Direct Plan Options
4. THE WEALTH COMPANY GOLD ETF FOF FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (ISIN: INF2F0001412)
