## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE00ZD07603, Code: 974349) effective January 19, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures. Trading members are explicitly advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Complete trading suspension for ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (Nifty50Index-3-2-26-PVT series)
- ISIN: INE00ZD07603, BSE Code: 974349
- Record date set for January 19, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members strictly advised against dealing in these securities
- Notice reference: DR-801/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure implemented when a company sets a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified debentures from January 19, 2026
- No dealings permitted in ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED debentures with ISIN INE00ZD07603
- Trading members must inform their clients about the suspension
- Compliance mandatory for all exchange members

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 19, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 19, 2026
- **Purpose Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 (for interest payment and redemption)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and investor access to these securities. Investors holding these debentures cannot exit positions through secondary market from January 19, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption schedule. However, no trading opportunity exists from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this ISIN and communicate the suspension to affected clients holding or interested in these debentures.
