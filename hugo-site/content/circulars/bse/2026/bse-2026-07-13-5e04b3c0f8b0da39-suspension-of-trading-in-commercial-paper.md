## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that certain companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their listed Commercial Paper (CP). Members are advised not to deal in the CP instruments of these companies from the specified record/ex dates.

## Key Points

- Can Fin Homes Ltd. (Scrip Code 731504, Scrip ID CFHL-17-07-26-CP, ISIN INE477A14EF6) — Record/Ex Date 16 July 2026, Settlement DR 673/2026-2027
- Time Technoplast Limited (Scrip Code 730960, Scrip ID TTL-23-07-26-CP, ISIN INE508G14IH2) — Record/Ex Date 22 July 2026, Settlement DR 677/2026-2027
- Tata Steel Limited (Scrip Code 731830, Scrip ID TSL-23-7-26-CP, ISIN INE081A14HB8) — Record/Ex Date 22 July 2026, Settlement DR 677/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice under Listing Operations for suspension of trading ahead of Commercial Paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the listed Commercial Paper of the above companies on and after the respective record/ex dates mentioned.

## Important Dates

- Can Fin Homes CP: Record/Ex Date - 16 July 2026
- Time Technoplast CP: Record/Ex Date - 22 July 2026
- Tata Steel CP: Record/Ex Date - 22 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to the debt segment; only holders/traders of these specific Commercial Paper ISINs are affected. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
