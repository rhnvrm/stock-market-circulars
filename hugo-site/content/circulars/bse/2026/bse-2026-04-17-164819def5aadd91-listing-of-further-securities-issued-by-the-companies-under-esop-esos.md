## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that further new securities issued by 9 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, April 20, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 9 companies have issued new securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All new securities are effective for trading from April 20, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed shares
- Shares span face values of ₹1, ₹2, and ₹10
- Total new shares range from 1,250 (IndoStar Capital) to 3,472,439 (Bajaj Finance)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice informing market participants of new securities admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. The notice is informational — market participants are requested to take note of the new securities and their respective distribution number ranges.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 20, 2026 (Monday)
