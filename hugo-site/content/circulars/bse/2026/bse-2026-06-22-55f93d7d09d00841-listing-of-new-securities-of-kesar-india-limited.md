## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,55,235 new equity shares of Kesar India Limited (Scrip Code: 543542, ISIN: INE0L1C01019) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 23, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 340/- (issue price Rs. 350/-) on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Total New Shares:** 14,55,235 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 350/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 340/-)
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Date of Allotment:** May 2, 2026
- **Distribution Nos.:** 28573230 to 30028464
- **ISIN:** INE0L1C01019
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following a preferential allotment under applicable SEBI regulations governing warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities and their availability for trading. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond standard settlement obligations for these newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 2, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 23, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (6,20,953 shares — Dist. Nos. 28573230–29194182):** December 31, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (8,34,282 shares — Dist. Nos. 29194183–30028464):** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 14,55,235 shares to the tradeable float of Kesar India Limited, subject to lock-in restrictions on the entire allotment until at least December 31, 2026. The locked-in shares will not immediately contribute to free-float supply. The issue price of Rs. 350/- serves as a reference point for market pricing. Impact is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of this scrip; no broader market or regulatory implications.
