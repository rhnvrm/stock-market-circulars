## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that two series of Bajaj Finance Limited privately placed debt securities have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 16, 2026. The listing includes one fresh listing and one further listing, both allotted on June 12, 2026. All securities are tradeable only in dematerialised form with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Two Bajaj Finance NCD series are now live on BSE Debt Segment as of June 16, 2026.
- Both are issued on a private placement basis and must be traded in dematerialised form only.
- Tick size for both securities is 1 paise.
- Full placement memorandum details are available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Clarifications may be sought from the BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's debt market operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these securities are executed only in dematerialised form.
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on the BSE website for further details before trading.
