## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three T-bills/Government Securities (182-day, 364-day, and 91-day T-bills) are maturing on their respective redemption dates. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-bills/Government Securities are set to mature: 182TB23726 (Scrip Code 805170, ISIN IN002025Y420), 364TB23726 (Scrip Code 805096, ISIN IN002025Z179), and 91TB230726 (Scrip Code 805205, ISIN IN002026X032)
- Trading members are advised not to deal with these securities with effect from July 21, 2026
- Reference: DR-676/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice related to scheduled maturity of government debt instruments, not a regulatory policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the listed T-bills/Government Securities (Scrip Codes 805170, 805096, 805205) from July 21, 2026 onwards, as these instruments reach maturity/redemption.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 20, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective: July 21, 2026 (post redemption/maturity)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice affecting only the debt segment. It pertains to standard maturity processing of short-term government securities (T-bills) and does not affect equity markets, listed companies, or broader trading activity. Trading members holding positions in these specific instruments need to ensure no further dealing occurs after the suspension date.
