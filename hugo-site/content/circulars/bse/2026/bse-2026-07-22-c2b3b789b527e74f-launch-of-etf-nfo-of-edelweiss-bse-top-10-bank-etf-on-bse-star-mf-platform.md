## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Edelweiss Mutual Fund will offer a New Fund Offer (NFO) of EDELWEISS BSE TOP 10 BANK ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 23, 2026 to July 30, 2026. The notice covers both the regular plan and the direct plan-growth option, along with operational instructions for fund pay-in, cheque/OTM payments, and switch transactions during the NFO period.

## Key Points

- Two schemes are being launched: EDELWEISS BSE TOP 10 BANK ETF and EDELWEISS BSE TOP 10 BANK ETF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH, both under the Equity category.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is August 06, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those executed during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled, with a CC 'N' flag reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by the specified deadline.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of funds under the NFO scheme prior to allotment; switch-out funds received on/after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- The NFO remains extended till midnight on July 30, 2026, only for lumpsum purchases on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice regarding a new mutual fund scheme launch on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 04, 2026 for cheque/OTM payments to be eligible for allotment.
- Members must ensure client fund initiation occurs by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation.
- Switch transactions must have funds received under the NFO scheme prior to the allotment date to be considered.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 23, 2026 to July 30, 2026 (extended till midnight July 30, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only)
- Fund clearance deadline for cheque/OTM payments: 6:00 PM, August 04, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: August 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice relevant primarily to BSE trading/clearing members handling mutual fund transactions via the StAR MF platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, pricing, or any specific stock, and carries no broader market or compliance risk beyond standard NFO processing procedures.
