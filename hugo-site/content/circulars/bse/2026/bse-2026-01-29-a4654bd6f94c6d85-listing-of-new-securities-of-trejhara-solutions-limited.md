## Summary

BSE has listed 89,89,344 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Trejhara Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 542233) with effect from January 30, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of amalgamation between LP Logistics Plus Chemical SCM Private Limited (Transferor Company) and Trejhara Solutions Limited (Transferee Company) under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 89,89,344 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 542233
- ISIN: INE00CA01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 14516299 to 23505642
- Shares issued under scheme of amalgamation with LP Logistics Plus Chemical SCM Private Limited
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding the listing of new securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing of equity shares resulting from a corporate amalgamation. The shares increase the total outstanding equity of Trejhara Solutions Limited but represent a standard corporate action with no immediate impact on trading operations or market participants. The shares are fungible with existing equity shares and will trade under the same scrip code.
