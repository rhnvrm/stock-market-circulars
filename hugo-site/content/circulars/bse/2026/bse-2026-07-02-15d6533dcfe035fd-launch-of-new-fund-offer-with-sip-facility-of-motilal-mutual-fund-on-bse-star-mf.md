## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund will launch the Motilal Oswal BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 Index Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 3, 2026, to July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: MOTILAL OSWAL BSE MIDCAP 150 MOMENTUM 30 INDEX FUND, available in Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HF9) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HE2)
- Category: Equity (Index Fund)
- Tentative allotment date: July 23, 2026
- NFO period: July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026 (extended till midnight July 17 for lumpsum purchases only)
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new product launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period transactions); otherwise transactions will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of funds prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- NFO open date: July 3, 2026
- NFO close date (SIP/XSIP authentication deadline, 3:00 PM): July 17, 2026
- NFO extended close (lumpsum only, midnight): July 17, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 23, 2026
- Fund availability deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for trading members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct impact on listed equity securities, market indices, or broader trading operations, and primarily concerns fund distributors and members handling MF transactions for this specific NFO.
