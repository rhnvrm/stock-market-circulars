## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to trading members regarding the non-competitive bidding facility for the auction of Government of India (GoI) dated securities scheduled on June 25, 2026. The Government of India will sell four dated securities through auction as announced by RBI on June 22, 2026. Bidding is available via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- RBI press release dated June 22, 2026 announced the GoI auction of four dated securities on June 25, 2026.
- Bidding is facilitated through BSE's iBBS web-based system (https://ibbs.bseindia.com) under the NCB-GSec module.
- This notice is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018, which launched the non-competitive bidding facility.
- Two G-Sec instruments are available for bidding with a minimum subscription of 100 units and minimum amount of Rs. 10,000.
- Settlement date for the auction is June 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice pursuant to the existing non-competitive bidding framework established under circular no. 20180423-42 (April 23, 2018).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com) to place bids.
- Members must submit bids by June 25, 2026, 8:00 AM.
- Direct investors must submit bids by June 24, 2026, 5:00 PM.
- Contact Trading Operations for new user ID creation, password reset, or bidding-related queries.
- Contact ICCL Operations for obligation, pay-in, and collateral-related queries.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |
|---|---|
| Bid Collection Start | June 23, 2026, 10:00 AM onwards (24-hour availability) |
| Bid Collection End (Members) | June 25, 2026, 8:00 AM |
| Bid Collection End (Direct Investors) | June 24, 2026, 5:00 PM |
| Auction Date | June 25, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 29, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is limited to trading members and direct investors participating in the government securities debt segment via the non-competitive bidding route. The two securities on offer are:

- **6.68% GS 2040** (Symbol: 668GS40J26) — Re-issue, Issue Size: Rs. 17,000 Cr, Min: 100 units / Rs. 10,000, Max: Rs. 2 Cr
- **7.43% GS 2076** (Symbol: 743GS76J26) — Re-issue, Issue Size: Rs. 11,000 Cr, Min: 100 units / Rs. 10,000, Max: Rs. 2 Cr

The notice has no direct impact on equity markets. Eligible participants in the debt segment should ensure timely bid submission through the designated platform.
