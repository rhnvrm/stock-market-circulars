## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new equity shares of HDFC Life Insurance Company Limited (Scrip Code: 540777) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at a premium.

## Key Points

- 1,45,23,906 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 678.52/- (issue price Rs. 688.52/-) to promoters on a preferential basis
- Distinctive numbers: 2157951076 to 2172474981
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE795G01014
- Date of allotment: 16/06/2026
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 13/01/2028

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for a preferential issue already allotted.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond recognizing the new securities as eligible for trading from the effective date; the company must observe the stipulated lock-in period for the allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 16 June 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 13 July 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 13 January 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases HDFC Life's total outstanding equity share capital since the new shares are pari-passu with existing shares and were issued to promoters (not the public), it has limited near-term float or price impact, though it modestly raises promoter shareholding. The two-year lock-in period restricts these shares from being sold until January 2028, limiting supply-side pressure on the stock in the near term.
