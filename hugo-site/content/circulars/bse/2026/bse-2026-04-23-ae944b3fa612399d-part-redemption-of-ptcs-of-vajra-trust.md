## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260423-15 informs trading members that Axis Trustee Services Limited has communicated reduced face values on partial redemption of Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) across 10 Vajra Trust series. These PTCs will trade at the new reduced face values with effect from April 24, 2026 (Reference: DR-617/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Axis Trustee Services Limited has notified BSE of partial redemption across 10 Vajra Trust PTC series
- All affected PTCs are in the Debt segment under Listing Operations
- Reduced face values are effective from April 24, 2026
- Trading members must note and apply the new reduced face values for these instruments
- The notice covers PTCs with coupon rates ranging from 9.25% to 9.75%

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a corporate action notice reflecting partial redemption of existing debt instruments, resulting in revised (reduced) face values for the listed PTCs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revised face values for all 10 affected PTC scrips
- All trades in these PTCs from April 24, 2026 onwards must reflect the reduced face values as specified

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value:** April 24, 2026
- **Reference:** DR-617/2026-2027
