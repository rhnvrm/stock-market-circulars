## Summary

BSE has announced that Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (Exchange ticker: 544673) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Thursday, January 08, 2026. The company is being listed on BSE effective Wednesday, January 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (ticker: 544673) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Company listing date: January 07, 2026
- Index addition effective date: January 08, 2026 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to Notice No. 20260106-16 for listing details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index constituent addition following the listing of a new company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **January 07, 2026**: Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited listing on BSE
- **January 08, 2026**: Stock addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index maintenance activity affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index. Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their holdings to reflect the new constituent. No impact on broader market operations or trading mechanisms.
