## Summary

BSE Limited sought clarifications from Technopack Polymers Limited (Scrip Code: 543656) and Trent Limited (Scrip Code: 500251, NSE Symbol: TRENT) regarding significant movements in share price and volume respectively. Both companies confirmed that they have been complying with disclosure obligations under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, particularly Regulation 30, and have no undisclosed material information that could affect share price or volume.

## Key Points

- **Technopack Polymers Limited** responded to BSE reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3266 dated January 2, 2026 regarding price movement
- **Trent Limited** responded to BSE reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/836 dated January 5, 2026 regarding volume movement
- Both companies confirmed regular compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- No pending announcements or undisclosed material information exists
- Price/volume movements are market-driven with no specific reason known to management
- All material information affecting operations or performance has been disclosed in timely manner

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange queries regarding unusual price or volume movements
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 disclosure obligations
- Companies must state if any material undisclosed information exists that could impact share price
- Responses must be submitted in a timely manner to the exchange

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: BSE sought clarification from Technopack Polymers Limited
- **January 3, 2026**: Technopack Polymers Limited submitted response
- **January 5, 2026**: BSE sought clarification from Trent Limited (email received at 12:44 PM)
- **January 5, 2026**: Trent Limited submitted response

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine clarifications with no new material information disclosed. Both companies attributed movements to market forces.

**Investor Impact**: Low - No new information for investors to act upon. Confirms companies are meeting disclosure obligations.

**Operational Impact**: None - No changes to company operations or business activities.

**Compliance Impact**: Standard - Demonstrates normal surveillance process by BSE and compliance response by listed companies.
