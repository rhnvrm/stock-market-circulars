## Summary

NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE0IX207189, Scrip Code: 975879) along with interest payment. The record date has been fixed as January 21, 2026. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 50,000 per debenture effective from January 21, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-803/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Part redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures of NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE0IX207189, BSE Scrip Code: 975879
- Record date fixed for determining eligible debenture holders
- Reduced face value will be Rs. 50,000 per debenture after redemption
- Debenture series: NFPL-11.75%-5-8-27-PVT
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notification as per existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in face value effective from January 21, 2026
- Trading in the debentures will continue with the reduced face value from the effective date
- Settlement number DR-803/2025-2026 applies to transactions with reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 21, 2026 (for interest payment and part redemption eligibility)
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: January 21, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-803/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only holders of specific debentures of a private limited company

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 50,000 per debenture from January 21, 2026

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record as of January 21, 2026 will receive interest payment and partial principal repayment, resulting in reduced face value of their holdings
