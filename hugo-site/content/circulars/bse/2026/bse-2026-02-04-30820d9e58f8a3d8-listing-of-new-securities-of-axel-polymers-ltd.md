## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 24,84,444 new equity shares of Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642) with effect from Thursday, February 5, 2026. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 45/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 35/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until August 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Number of shares listed: 24,84,444 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 45/- per share (including premium of Rs. 35/-)
- Allotment basis: Preferential allotment to Non-Promoters
- Distinctive numbers: 8516681 to 11001124
- Trading permitted from: February 5, 2026
- ISIN: INE197C01012
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from February 5, 2026
- All 24,84,444 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until the lock-in expiry date
- The locked-in shares fall under distinctive numbers 8516681 to 11001124

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific listing of preferential shares with minimal market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Routine listing process for existing company; no changes to trading operations or market structure.

**Liquidity Impact**: Limited initially due to 100% lock-in on all newly issued shares until August 10, 2026. Post lock-in expiry, these shares will add approximately 10% to the free float depending on existing share capital.
