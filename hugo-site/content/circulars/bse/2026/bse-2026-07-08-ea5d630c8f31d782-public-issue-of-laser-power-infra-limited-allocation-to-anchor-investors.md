## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Laser Power & Infra Limited, 1,04,01,867 equity shares have been subscribed on July 08, 2026 at a price of Rs. 214 per equity share. The notice encloses the company's Anchor Allocation Intimation Letter dated July 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- Anchor Investors subscribed to 1,04,01,867 equity shares in the Laser Power & Infra Limited public issue.
- The anchor allocation price was fixed at Rs. 214 per equity share.
- The intimation is based on the company's announcement dated July 08, 2026.
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department under Notice No. 20260708-35.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine disclosure of anchor investor allocation as part of the IPO process, consistent with SEBI ICDR regulations governing anchor investor participation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond being informed of the allocation; the notice is for reference ahead of the public issue proceeding to the broader subscription and listing stages.

## Important Dates

- Anchor allocation date: July 08, 2026
- Notice date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor allocation signals initial institutional demand and provides a price reference (Rs. 214) ahead of the public issue opening for other investor categories. Market impact is limited to participants tracking the Laser Power & Infra Limited IPO, with broader significance if subscription trends or listing performance draw wider investor interest.
