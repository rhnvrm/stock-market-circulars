## Summary

Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited has been listed on the BSE SME platform with scrip code YASHHTEJ and ISIN INE1O6P01016. This circular presents the post-issue shareholding pattern as on 24 February 2026, filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Key Points

- Company: Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited
- Scrip Code: YASHHTEJ (BSE SME)
- ISIN: INE1O6P01016
- SME listing: Yes
- Class of securities: Equity Shares
- Report type: Post-Issue shareholding pattern
- Report date: 24 February 2026
- Promoter and Promoter Group shares are subject to lock-in
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No pledged or NDU-encumbered promoter shares
- No depository receipts or differential voting rights issued
- No significant beneficial owner declared

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard post-issue disclosure filing under SEBI LODR Regulation 31(1)(a).

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has complied with post-issue shareholding pattern disclosure requirements.
- Promoter shares are locked in as required under SME IPO norms.
- No additional compliance action is required from other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Report Date / Listing Date: 24 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is a routine administrative filing confirming the post-issue capital structure of a newly listed SME company. Investors in YASHHTEJ should note that promoter shares are currently under lock-in, restricting their tradability for the applicable lock-in period under SEBI SME IPO guidelines.
