## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that Catalyst Trusteeship Limited has communicated the reduced face value per Pass Through Certificate (PTC) following part redemption of two series of India Universal Trust AL1 - Series A3 and Series A2. The revised face values will apply with effect from July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Debt segment, Corporate Actions category, issued by the Listing Operations department.
- India Universal Trust AL1 - Series A3 (IUTA1-8.20%-20-9-30-PTC, ISIN INE16J715035, Scrip Code 976052): reduced face value of Rs. 8,623,972.25 per PTC following part redemption.
- India Universal Trust AL1 - Series A2 (IUTA1-8.12%-20-7-27-PTC, ISIN INE16J715027, Scrip Code 976051): reduced face value of Rs. 5,748,044.74 per PTC following part redemption.
- Reference: DR-677/2026-2027.
- No attachment was provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update reflecting a corporate action (part redemption) already executed by the trustee.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange are required to take note of the reduced face values and ensure trades in these PTCs from the effective date reflect the updated per-PTC value.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading at Reduced Face Value: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to market participants holding or trading India Universal Trust AL1 Series A3 and A2 PTCs. The face value reduction reflects principal already redeemed to certificate holders, requiring trading systems and members to adjust reference prices accordingly from July 22, 2026. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
