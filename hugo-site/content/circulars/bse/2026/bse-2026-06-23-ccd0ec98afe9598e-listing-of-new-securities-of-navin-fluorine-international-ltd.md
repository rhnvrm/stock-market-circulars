## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 860 new equity shares of Navin Fluorine International Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532504, ISIN: INE048G01026) are permitted for trading effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 860 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each are newly listed on BSE with effect from June 24, 2026.
- Shares were issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up on a rights basis.
- ISIN: INE048G01026 (same as existing equity shares).
- These shares are pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers assigned span multiple allotment tranches ranging from 49201861 to 49213210.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to an existing rights issue conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date for Trading:** Wednesday, June 24, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 860 equity shares is a very small quantity relative to the existing share capital of Navin Fluorine International Ltd. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no differential rights or pricing implications arise. This is a routine administrative listing following a rights issue partly paid conversion.
