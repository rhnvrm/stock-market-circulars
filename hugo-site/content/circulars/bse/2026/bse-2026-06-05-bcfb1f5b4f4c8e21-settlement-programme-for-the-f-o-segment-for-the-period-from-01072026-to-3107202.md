## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has published the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment on BSE for the period 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. This notice provides the scheduled settlement dates and cycles applicable to derivatives contracts expiring or settling during the month.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the full month of July 2026 (01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026)
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the Derivatives segment
- Attachment FNO072026 contains the detailed settlement schedule
- Applicable to all F&O segment participants on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly issuance of the settlement calendar as required by ICCL operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members must adhere to the settlement dates and pay-in/pay-out schedules specified in the attached programme (FNO072026)
- Members should plan their funds and securities obligations in line with the published settlement calendar for July 2026

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 05 June 2026
- Programme Period: 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- Detailed settlement dates are available in the attached document FNO072026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has routine operational impact on derivatives market participants. Clearing and trading members of the F&O segment must align their settlement obligations — including margin calls, pay-in of funds/securities, and pay-out expectations — with the published schedule. No extraordinary settlement dates or holiday disruptions are indicated in the notice itself; refer to attachment FNO072026 for the full schedule.
