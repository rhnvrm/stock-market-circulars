## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of CSM Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Thursday, July 2, 2026, under the 'T' Group of Securities. In accordance with SEBI circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- CSM Technologies Limited's equity shares to be listed and admitted to dealings from July 2, 2026
- Listed in the 'T' Group of Securities on BSE
- Scrip will be in Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Total securities: 51,603,472 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (Distinctive Number range 1 to 51,603,472)
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: CSM
- Scrip Code: 544806; ISIN: INE0ZK601013
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue price for the public issue: Rs. 113/- per share (Face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 103/-)
- Date of allotment in the public issue: June 30, 2026
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated 16 February 2000
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 2, 2026
- Registrar to the issue: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice implementing existing SEBI circulars (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 on Trade-for-Trade segment and the February 2000 dematerialisation guidelines).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure the scrip is traded only in demat form.
- The company has executed necessary corporate action for marking the lock-in period in depository records, as detailed in Annexure I.
- Trading members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012 for Special Pre-open Session procedures.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 1, 2026: Notice date
- July 2, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of listing, admission to dealings, and Special Pre-open Session
- 10 trading days from July 2, 2026: Trade-for-Trade segment applicability

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables trading in CSM Technologies Limited shares on BSE. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 trading days limits intraday speculative trading, requiring actual delivery of shares, which is standard practice for newly listed IPO scrips to curb early volatility. Market impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in this specific scrip.
