## Summary

BSE, as part of its routine surveillance to verify the accuracy of news articles circulating in newspapers, websites, and television channels, sought clarifications from three listed companies regarding specific news reports. Swiggy Ltd (Scrip Code: 544285) and Tata Motors Ltd (Scrip Code: 544569) have submitted their replies, which are attached to the notice. A reply from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding a news article published on The Hindu BusinessLine is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Swiggy Ltd on a news article published by Economic Times; reply received.
- BSE sought clarification from Tata Motors Ltd on a news article published by The Hindu BusinessLine; reply received.
- BSE sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd on a news article published by The Hindu BusinessLine; reply is awaited.
- This is a standard surveillance measure undertaken by the Exchange (DOSS - Department of Surveillance) to inform investors about the veracity of market-moving news.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to submit its clarification/reply to BSE regarding the referenced news article. Swiggy Ltd and Tata Motors Ltd have already complied by submitting their replies.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 16 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260716-18). No specific deadline mentioned for Rajesh Exports Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is generally limited to short-term investor sentiment and price discovery for the three named stocks, as clarifications help confirm or dispel market rumors. Investors and members should review the attached replies from Swiggy Ltd and Tata Motors Ltd for specifics, and monitor for Rajesh Exports Ltd's forthcoming clarification.
