## Summary

BSE has announced that the Draft Offer Document of WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED, filed on December 31, 2025, is now available on the stock exchange website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from January 05, 2026
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification as per ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document must be made available for public viewing for at least 21 days
- All disclosures must comply with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments must submit them to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **January 05, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 26, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural announcement for an SME IPO. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement to ensure transparency in the IPO process. General market impact is minimal as this affects only the specific SME issuer.
