## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. The session is intended for trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs to test their trading applications, including exceptional market condition scenarios such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts.

## Key Points

- Mock trading is scheduled for May 23, 2026, for the Commodity Derivatives segment
- Applicable to members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS release will accompany this session
- Trades executed during mock trading carry no margin, pay-in, pay-out obligations, or legal rights/liabilities
- Members are requested to actively participate and provide feedback by 1:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate in the mock session to test their systems
- Members must submit feedback on the mock trading session by 1:00 PM on May 23, 2026, via BSE Tech Support or Help Desk contacts

## Important Dates

- **May 23, 2026**: Mock trading session day
  - Log-in: 9:15 AM – 10:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Feedback deadline**: 1:00 PM on May 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This session has no market or financial impact. It is a purely technical exercise for members to validate trading system functionality. Participation is voluntary and results in no real obligations. Contact details for support: BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 / bse.tech@bseindia.com; BSE Help Desk at 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 / bsehelp@bseindia.com.
