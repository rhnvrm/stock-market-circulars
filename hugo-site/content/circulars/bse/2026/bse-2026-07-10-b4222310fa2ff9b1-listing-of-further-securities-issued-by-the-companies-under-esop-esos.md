## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 10 listed companies are listed and admitted for trading with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Additional shares issued under ESOP/ESOS by the following companies will be listed for trading:
  - Angel One Limited (ISIN: INE732I01021, Scrip Code: 543235) — 169,561 shares, face value ₹1
  - Avalon Technologies Limited (ISIN: INE0LCL01028, Scrip Code: 543896) — 24,058 shares, face value ₹2
  - CreditAccess Grameen Limited (ISIN: INE741K01010, Scrip Code: 541770) — 26,102 shares, face value ₹10
  - EPL Limited (ISIN: INE255A01020, Scrip Code: 500135) — 18,000 shares, face value ₹2
  - Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited (ISIN: INE281B01032, Scrip Code: 512455) — 30,888 shares, face value ₹1
  - One 97 Communications Limited (ISIN: INE982J01020, Scrip Code: 543396) — 347,475 shares, face value ₹1
  - Sedemac Mechatronics Limited (ISIN: INE00XB01019, Scrip Code: 544723) — 6,000 shares, face value ₹10
  - Transport Corporation of India Limited (ISIN: INE688A01022, Scrip Code: 532349) — 87,345 shares, face value ₹2
  - VA Tech Wabag Limited (ISIN: INE956G01038, Scrip Code: 533269) — 62,680 shares, face value ₹2
  - Wipro Limited (ISIN: INE075A01022, Scrip Code: 507685) — 333,850 shares, face value ₹2
- None of the newly listed shares are under lock-in.
- Distinctive number ranges for each allotment are specified in the notice for record-keeping purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing and admission of these additional shares for trading purposes; no specific action is mandated beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 10, 2026
- Effective/Listing Date: Monday, July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of incremental shares arising from employee stock option exercises. The quantities involved are small relative to the total outstanding shares of each company, resulting in minor equity dilution with negligible near-term price impact. The notice is primarily informational for trading members to ensure accurate settlement and trading of the newly listed shares.
