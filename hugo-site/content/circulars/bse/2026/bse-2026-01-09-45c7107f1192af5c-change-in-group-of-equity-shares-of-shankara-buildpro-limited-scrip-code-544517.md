## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code - 544517) will be permitted to trade under 'B' group effective Friday, January 23, 2026. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Shankara Buildpro Limited
- Scrip Code: 544517
- New Group: 'B' group
- Effective Date: Friday, January 23, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260109-9
- Notice Date: January 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The trading classification for Shankara Buildpro Limited shares will change to 'B' group. Stocks in the 'B' group typically have different settlement and trading characteristics compared to other groups on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are required to take note of this group change and ensure their trading systems and operations are updated to reflect the new classification effective January 23, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The change to 'B' group classification may affect trading behavior, liquidity patterns, and settlement procedures for Shankara Buildpro Limited shares. Market participants should familiarize themselves with the characteristics and requirements of 'B' group securities before the effective date.
