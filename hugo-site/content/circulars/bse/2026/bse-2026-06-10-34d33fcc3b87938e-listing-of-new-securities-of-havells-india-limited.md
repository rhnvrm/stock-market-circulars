## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and trading permission for 3,04,810 new equity shares of Havells India Limited (Scrip Code: 517354, ISIN: INE176B01034) allotted under three Employee Stock Purchase Schemes. These shares are ranked pari-passu with existing equity shares and are available for trading from June 11, 2026, subject to applicable lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- 3,04,810 new equity shares of Re.1/- each fully paid up listed on BSE
- Shares allotted on 22 May 2026 under ESPS 2014, ESPS 2015, and ESPS 2016
- Trading permitted from Thursday, 11 June 2026
- All shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Significant portion of shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for shares allotted under existing employee stock purchase schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the lock-in restrictions on the newly listed shares. Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in periods expire.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 22 May 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** 11 June 2026
- **Lock-in Period 1 (12 months):** From date of credit to employee demat accounts
- **Lock-in Period 2 (2 years):** Up to 21 May 2028

| Scheme | Total Shares | 12-Month Lock-in | 2-Year Lock-in |
|---|---|---|---|
| ESPS 2014 | 1,14,191 | 57,044 (Dist. 627256843–627313886) | 57,147 (Dist. 627313887–627371033) |
| ESPS 2015 | 1,50,000 | 1,17,000 (Dist. 627371034–627488033) | 33,000 (Dist. 627488034–627521033) |
| ESPS 2016 | 40,619 | 40,619 (Dist. 627521034–627561652) | None |

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 3,04,810 shares represent a small addition to Havells India's equity base. A significant portion is under lock-in, limiting immediate tradeable supply. This is a routine corporate action with no material effect on price discovery or trading activity.
