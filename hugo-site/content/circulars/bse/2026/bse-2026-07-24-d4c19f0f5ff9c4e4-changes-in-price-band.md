## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the price bands applicable to a list of scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 27, 2026. The circular (Notice No. 20260724-21) lists over 30 scrips with their revised price band percentages, ranging from 2% to 20%, reflecting periodic recalibration of trading bands typically tied to volatility and surveillance criteria.

## Key Points

- Revised price bands apply to a broad list of scrips including Ahmedabad Steelcraft Ltd, Anik Industries Ltd, Arvaya Healthcare Ltd, Astonea Labs Ltd, Avadh Sugar & Energy Ltd, Bfl Asset Finvest Ltd, Chatterbox Technologies Ltd, Choksi Asia Ltd, Cosmo Ferrites Ltd, Crystal Business System Ltd, Daikaffil Chemicals India Ltd, Dcm Financial Services Ltd, Diamond Power Infrastructure Ltd, East West Freight Carriers Ltd, Emami Paper Mills Ltd, Filmcity Media Ltd, Globe Civil Projects Ltd, Gujarat Lease Financing Ltd, Highway Infrastructure Ltd, Investment & Precision Castings Ltd, Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd, Kabsons Industries Ltd, Kalyani Cast-Tech Ltd, Kesoram Industries Ltd, Khaitan Chemicals & Fertilizers Ltd, Kirloskar Electric Company Ltd, Kriti Nutrients Ltd, Manaksia Steels Ltd, Mangal Electrical Industries Ltd, Manoj Ceramic Ltd, and Meera Industries (list continues in the source notice, which was truncated).
- Revised price band percentages vary by scrip, ranging from as tight as 2% (e.g., Arvaya Healthcare Ltd) to as wide as 20% (e.g., Anik Industries Ltd, Avadh Sugar & Energy Ltd, Chatterbox Technologies Ltd, Globe Civil Projects Ltd, Highway Infrastructure Ltd, Kabsons Industries Ltd, Kalyani Cast-Tech Ltd, Mangal Electrical Industries Ltd, Manoj Ceramic Ltd).
- The change is issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance Supervision (DOSS) under the Trading category, Equity segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine application of BSE's existing price band revision mechanism, under which the Exchange periodically adjusts circuit filter limits for individual scrips based on volatility, trading pattern, and surveillance considerations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their trading systems to reflect the new price bands for the listed scrips and ensure orders are placed within the revised circuit limits from the effective date. No filing or reporting action is required from listed companies.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 24, 2026
- Effective Date: July 27, 2026 (revised price bands come into force)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and stock-specific rather than market-wide. Scrips moving to tighter price bands (e.g., 2% or 5%) will see reduced daily price movement potential, which can affect liquidity and short-term trading strategies for those names. Scrips moving to wider bands (e.g., 20%) may experience greater intraday volatility. Trading members and algorithmic trading desks should recalibrate order validation logic for the affected scrips ahead of the July 27, 2026 effective date.
