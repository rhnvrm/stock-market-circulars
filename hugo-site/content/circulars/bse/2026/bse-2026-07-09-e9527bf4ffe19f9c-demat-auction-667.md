## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Demat Auction 667 (ARR 667), pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-667/2026-27. This is a routine auction conducted to settle short deliveries in the settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Auction relates to Rolling Settlement No. DR-667/2026-27.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Thursday, July 9, 2026.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of auction securities to be completed by Friday, July 10, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they have short-delivered has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while entering security codes and rates; no corrections permitted after punching errors.
- Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for any Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split) affecting the securities.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine procedural/operational notice implementing existing auction settlement mechanisms under the Uniform Membership structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within the specified depository cut-off timings.
- Members must ensure accuracy while punching security codes and rates during the auction offer entry window, as no corrections are allowed post-entry.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: Thursday, July 9, 2026, 02:00 p.m. – 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. – 03:00 p.m., July 9, 2026
- Delivery statement/report: 03:00 p.m. – 03:30 p.m., July 9, 2026
- Money statement & final reports: 03:35 p.m. – 05:30 p.m., July 9, 2026
- Auction pay-in: Friday, July 10, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard, recurring operational notice for settling short deliveries via auction under the exchange's clearing and settlement framework. It affects trading members and custodians involved in the specific rolling settlement cycle (DR-667/2026-27) but has no material impact on broader market activity, specific listed companies, or investors outside the affected settlement participants.
