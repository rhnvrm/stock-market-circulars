## Summary

BSE has notified members of a communication received from Quant (SIF) Mutual Fund regarding a revision in the minimum application amount for five Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) schemes. The revised amounts are effective immediately, as per the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Quant AMC.

## Key Points

- The revision applies to five Quant SIF schemes:
  1. QSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund
  2. QSIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund
  3. QSIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund
  4. QSIF Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund
  5. QSIF Equity Long-Short Fund
- Changes cover minimum/additional application amount for Purchase/Switch, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- The relevant Annexure and Notice-cum-Addendum from Quant AMC is attached to the circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory (SEBI/exchange) rule change; this is an operational update communicated by the AMC (Quant Mutual Fund) and disseminated by BSE to its trading members for the relevant SIF schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members dealing in these Quant SIF schemes must update their systems/processes to reflect the revised minimum and additional application amounts for purchase, switch, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions, and inform clients accordingly.

## Important Dates

Effective immediately from the date of the notice, 01 Jul 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to operational/procedural changes for BSE members and investors transacting in these specific Quant SIF schemes. There is no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader market trading.
