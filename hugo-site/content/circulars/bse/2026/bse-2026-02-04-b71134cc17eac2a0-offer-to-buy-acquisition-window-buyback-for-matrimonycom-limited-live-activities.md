## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/BuyBack) for MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED. The buyback offer will be conducted from February 5-11, 2026, offering to purchase up to 8,93,129 equity shares at Rs. 655 per share. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform and RTRMS system during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security**: MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED (Symbol: MATRIMONY, ISIN: INE866R01028, Scrip Code: 540704)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,93,129 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 655 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Thursday, February 5, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, February 11, 2026
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN number and category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular provides operational guidelines for the buyback process following existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and Custodian Members:**
- Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Access iBBS platform or RTRMS for bid placement and modifications
- Only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Monitor Extranet for acceptance files after issue closure (files available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA)
- Note: No End of Issue files will be generated if no securities are accepted

**Bidding Categories Available:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

**Bid Offering Period**: February 5-11, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Bid Modification/Cancellation**: February 5-11, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up/Take-up**: February 5-10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up (Last Day)**: February 11, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Take-up (Last Day)**: February 11, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED shareholders. The buyback of up to 8,93,129 shares represents a specific corporate action that provides an exit opportunity to shareholders at Rs. 655 per share.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and custodians need to ensure proper UCC registration and access to iBBS/RTRMS platforms. Members should coordinate with their clients holding MATRIMONY shares in demat form who wish to participate in the buyback offer.

**Platform Access Requirements**:
- Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (iBBS), https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (RTRMS)
- Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (iBBS), https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (RTRMS)
- Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet), https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

**Support Contacts**: Multiple contact points provided for iBBS login issues, password resets, and OTB module queries through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
