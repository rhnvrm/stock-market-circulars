## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Prayatna Developers Private Limited has fixed a record date of 16 March 2026 for the purpose of payment of interest and part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in the debentures will continue with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: Prayatna Developers Private Limited
- Debenture Series: PDPL-7.30%-31-12-26-PVT (ISIN: INE925U07023, Code: 973756)
- Record Date: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 380,280 per Debenture
- Effective Date: 16 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE debt segment operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note that trading in the debentures of Prayatna Developers Private Limited (ISIN: INE925U07023) will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 380,280 per debenture with effect from 16 March 2026 under Settlement No. DR-840/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 16 March 2026
- **Circular Date:** 12 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains to a single privately placed NCD series of a private limited company. Holders of ISIN INE925U07023 will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest on the record date. Post-redemption, trading will reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 380,280 per debenture. The impact is confined to existing debenture holders and relevant trading members in the debt segment.
