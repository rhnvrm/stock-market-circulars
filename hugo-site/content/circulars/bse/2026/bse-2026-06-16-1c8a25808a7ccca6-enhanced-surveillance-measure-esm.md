## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Effective June 17–18, 2026, certain securities are being added to ESM (attracting 100% margin), some existing ESM securities are being downgraded to Stage I, securities under Stage II will move to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, and a separate list of securities will exit the ESM framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on June 17, 2026 and on new positions from June 18, 2026.
- Some securities in Annexure I are being moved to a lower stage (Stage I) under ESM w.e.f. June 17, 2026.
- All securities qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. June 18, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 17, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) will have their pre-ESM price band reinstated, unless they are part of another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities under ESM is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Inclusion in ESM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and operates under the ESM framework established via earlier notices: 20230602-44 (June 2, 2023), 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023), 20240809-42 (August 9, 2024), 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024), 20241004-65 (October 4, 2024), and 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic review and update under the existing ESM structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members/Brokers**: Must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all open positions in ESM securities as on June 17, 2026 and on all new positions from June 18, 2026.
- **Members**: Must note the shift of qualifying ESM securities from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment from June 18, 2026.
- **Members**: Must adhere to the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism for Stage II securities from June 17, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Notice date.
- **June 17, 2026**: Securities move to Stage I (downgrade within ESM); Stage II securities shift to Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; securities exit ESM framework (Annexure II) and revert to prior price bands.
- **June 18, 2026**: New and existing ESM securities attract minimum 100% margin; qualifying securities shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but operationally significant update. Trading members must adjust margin collection and settlement procedures for affected securities immediately. Securities moving into ESM face reduced liquidity due to the Trade-for-Trade shift and 100% margin requirement, which can compress trading activity. Stage II securities with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction face the most restrictive trading conditions. Securities exiting the ESM framework will see their price bands and settlement modes restored, which may improve liquidity for those scrips. The overall market impact is moderate and confined to the specific securities listed in the Annexures.
