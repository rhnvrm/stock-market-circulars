## Summary

BSE has designated two dedicated escalation email IDs for handling unresolved matters related to Investment Advisers (IAs). These are to be used strictly for escalation purposes and must not be included in routine correspondence.

## Key Points

- Two-tier escalation email system established for Investment Adviser (IA) related matters
- Escalation Level 1: `iara.escalation1@bseindia.com` — use if primary email is not replied/resolved within 7 days
- Escalation Level 2: `iara.escalation2@bseindia.com` — use if Escalation 1 email is not replied/resolved within 7 days
- Escalation email IDs must not be marked in routine correspondence
- Regular communication should continue through designated operational email channels

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational/administrative notice establishing a formal escalation framework for Investment Adviser matters within the BSE Membership Compliance department.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from using escalation email IDs for routine correspondence
- Escalation emails should only be raised after the primary channel has failed to respond or resolve within 7 days
- For unresolved Escalation 1 matters, Escalation 2 may be used after a further 7-day wait

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: 22 May 2026 (immediate, as per notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market or operational impact. This notice is administrative in nature, aimed at improving tracking and timely resolution of escalated Investment Adviser matters. It benefits members by providing a clear two-step escalation path when standard communication channels are unresponsive.
