## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of units under iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund managed by ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, effective February 10, 2026. Two schemes are being listed: the Growth plan with 23.87 crore units (Scrip Code: 544696) and the Direct Plan - Growth with 19.76 crore units (Scrip Code: 544697). Both schemes invest in equity and debt securities with limited short exposure through derivatives and will trade in dematerialized form only in the "B" Group with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Effective date of listing: February 10, 2026
- Fund type: iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund with equity, debt securities, and limited short exposure through derivatives
- Two schemes listed: Growth plan and Direct Plan - Growth
- Growth plan: 23,86,78,214 units, Scrip Code 544696, ISIN INF109K30018
- Direct Plan Growth: 19,75,61,096 units, Scrip Code 544697, ISIN INF109K30026
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit for both schemes
- Issue price: Rs. 10.00 per unit for both schemes
- Date of allotment: February 4, 2026
- Trading group: "B" Group
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification following existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in these units are executed only in dematerialized form
- Trades must be effected in unit market lots (1 unit)
- Members should contact the Registrar and Transfer Agents (Computer Age Management Services Limited - CAMS) for operational queries

## Important Dates

- **February 4, 2026**: Date of allotment of units
- **February 10, 2026**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides liquidity to investors in ICICI Prudential's iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund on the BSE platform. Impact is limited to:

- Investors in these specific schemes who can now trade units on the exchange
- Trading members who will facilitate transactions in these schemes
- No direct impact on equity markets or other listed securities
