## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Segment for March 2026. The circular specifies the last date for margin reporting and the associated penalty date for each trade date in the month, covering the period from 02 March 2026 to 30 March 2026.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers all Equity Segment trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026.
- Each trade date has a defined "Last Date of Margin Reporting" and a "Penalty Date" by which non-compliant members face penalties.
- The reporting window is typically 6–8 trading days after the trade date.
- The final trade date in this schedule (30-Mar-2026) has a last reporting date of 08-Apr-2026 and a penalty date of 09-Apr-2026.
- The circular is issued by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL, on behalf of ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic notification establishing the operational calendar for client margin reporting obligations under existing SEBI/ICCL margin framework rules for the Equity Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the specified "Last Date of Margin Reporting".
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties being levied on the "Penalty Date".
- Members should ensure their back-office and reporting systems are aligned with the published schedule to avoid penalties.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on all trading members active in the Equity Segment. Members must align their compliance and reporting workflows with the published schedule throughout March 2026. Late or missed margin reports will attract financial penalties. The schedule extends into early April 2026 for trade dates at the end of March, requiring members to maintain reporting vigilance beyond the month end.
