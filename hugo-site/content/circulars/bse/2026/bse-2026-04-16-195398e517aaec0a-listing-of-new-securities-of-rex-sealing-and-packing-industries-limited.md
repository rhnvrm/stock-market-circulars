## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 94,000 new equity shares of Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543744, ISIN: INE0MFT01014) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, April 17, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 200/- (issue price Rs. 210/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 94,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 200/- (issue price Rs. 210/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 2380001 to 2474000
- Date of allotment: February 10, 2026
- ISIN: INE0MFT01014
- All 94,000 new shares are subject to lock-in until October 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from April 17, 2026 onwards
- The lock-in restriction on all 94,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 2380001 to 2474000) must be observed until October 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 17, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** October 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing is limited in scope, involving 94,000 shares at Rs. 210/- each (total issuance value ~Rs. 1.97 crore). Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until October 31, 2026, there is no immediate free-float addition to the market. Impact on trading volumes and price discovery for Scrip Code 543744 is expected to be minimal in the near term.
