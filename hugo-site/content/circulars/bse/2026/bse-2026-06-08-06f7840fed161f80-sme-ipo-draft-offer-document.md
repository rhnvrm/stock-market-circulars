## Summary

BSE has published a notice that the Draft Offer Document of **Vivekanand Cotspin Limited**, filed on June 05, 2026, is now available on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Vivekanand Cotspin Limited on June 05, 2026
- Document made available on BSE SME website in compliance with ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI)
- Public comments invited for at least 21 days from the date of this notice (June 08, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued in accordance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring public availability and comment period for draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Public comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from June 08, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** June 05, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 08, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before June 29, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO. The impact is limited to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in Vivekanand Cotspin Limited. No broader market or trading impact is anticipated.
