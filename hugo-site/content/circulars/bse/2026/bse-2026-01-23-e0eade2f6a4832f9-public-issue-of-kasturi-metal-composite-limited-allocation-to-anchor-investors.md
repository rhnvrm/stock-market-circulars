BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Kasturi Metal Composite Limited. Anchor investor allocation is the first phase of an IPO process where institutional investors are allocated shares before the issue opens to retail investors.

- Event: Allocation to anchor investors for public issue
