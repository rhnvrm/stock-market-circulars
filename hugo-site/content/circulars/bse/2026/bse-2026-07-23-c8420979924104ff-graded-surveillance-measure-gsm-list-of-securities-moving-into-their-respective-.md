## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that certain securities, listed in the attached Annexure, will be moved to their respective higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework with effect from July 24, 2026. This action is taken under the GSM Framework originally published on February 23, 2017, and subsequently referenced in various Exchange notices. Securities moved to higher GSM stages will be subject to enhanced surveillance actions including higher margins, restricted price bands, trade-for-trade settlement, and additional surveillance deposits.

## Key Points

- Securities specified in the Annexure_GSM attachment will move to higher GSM stages effective July 24, 2026.
- The action stems from the GSM Framework framework established via Exchange notice no. 20170223-44 (Feb 23, 2017) and updated through subsequent notices (20170303-29, 20180720-55, 20191129-23, 20231117-63).
- Four GSM stages (I through IV) apply progressively stricter surveillance actions.
- Stage I: 100% margin and price band of 5% or lower.
- Stage II: Trade-for-trade with 5% or lower price band, plus 50% Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) on trade value from buyers.
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade with 5% or lower price band, trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or first trading day), plus 100% ASD from buyers.
- Stage IV: Same as Stage III, but with no upward price movement permitted.
- Settlement for securities under GSM will be done strictly on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off allowed.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing GSM Framework (in effect since February 2017) to specific securities identified in the Annexure, moving them to higher surveillance stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with the applicable margin, price band, ASD, and trade-for-trade settlement requirements for the affected securities from the effective date.
- Members must take adequate precautions while trading in the identified securities given the trade-to-trade settlement basis with no netting.
- Queries regarding GSM can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com or the published FAQ link.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 24, 2026 — securities move to their respective higher GSM stages.
- Notice Date: July 23, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a high trading impact on the specific securities named in the Annexure, as they will face significantly reduced liquidity due to trade-for-trade settlement, restricted price bands, higher margin/ASD requirements, and in some cases weekly-only trading windows. Investors and traders holding or considering these securities should review the Annexure for exact scrip names and adjust trading strategies accordingly. The broader market impact is limited since this is a routine, periodic surveillance action affecting only specifically flagged securities rather than the market as a whole.
