## Summary

Groww BSE Hospitals ETF, an open-ended scheme tracking the BSE Hospitals Index – TRI, managed by Groww Asset Management Limited, has been listed on BSE effective Thursday, March 5, 2026 under Group B. A total of 1,786,452 units with scrip code 544721 are admitted to dealings, with trading restricted to dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Scrip Code:** 544721 | **Scrip ID on BOLT:** GROWWHOSPI
- **ISIN:** INF666M01OD9
- **Group:** B | **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Face Value:** ₹10.00 per unit | **Issue Price:** ₹48.88 per unit
- **Total Units Listed:** 1,786,452
- **Date of Allotment:** March 2, 2026
- **Market Makers:** Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd, East India Securities, Parwati Capital Market Pvt. Ltd, IRAGE Broking Services LLP
- **Registrar & Transfer Agent:** KFin Technologies Ltd, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, mandating that mutual fund units be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only.
- Trades must be in unit market lot (i.e., one unit per lot).
- Members requiring clarification may contact Mr. Parag Jain at BSE (mf.listing@bseindia.com, Tel: 022-2272-8685) or Mr. Krishnam Thota (Head Operations & IRO) at Groww Asset Management Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 2, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** March 5, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Circular Date:** March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces a new ETF instrument on BSE providing exposure to the Indian hospitals/healthcare sector via the BSE Hospitals Index – TRI. With an issue price of ₹48.88 and 1.79 million units listed, liquidity is supported by four designated market makers. The ETF is placed in Group B, making it accessible to a broad investor base. Impact is moderate — relevant primarily to investors seeking sectoral healthcare exposure through a passive ETF structure.
