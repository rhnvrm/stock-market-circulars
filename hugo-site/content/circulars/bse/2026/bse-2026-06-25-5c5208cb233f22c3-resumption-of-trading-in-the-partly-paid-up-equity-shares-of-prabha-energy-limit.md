## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in 88,97,316 partly paid-up equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited (scrip code 890236) effective 2nd July 2026. Trading was previously suspended and is being resumed following the receipt of First Call Money, as referenced in Exchange Notice No. 20260505-41 dated 5th May 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading will resume in 88,97,316 partly paid equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited (scrip code 890236) from 2nd July 2026
- New ISIN assigned: IN90I0M01022
- Market lot: 1 share
- Shares were issued on a Rights basis with face value of Re. 1/- each
- Shares are partly paid up to Re. 0.67 per share
- First Call Money received: Rs. 47.52 per share (comprising Re. 0.33 towards face value and Rs. 47.19 towards premium)
- DR reference: DR-663/2026-2027
- Synopsis containing details of partly paid shares available for trading is attached to the original notice

## Regulatory Changes

A new ISIN (IN90I0M01022) has been assigned to the partly paid-up equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited. This follows the continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260505-41 dated 5th May 2026, which had previously addressed the partly paid share status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members of BSE are required to take note of the resumption of trading effective 2nd July 2026
- Members should update their systems with the new ISIN (IN90I0M01022) for Prabha Energy Limited partly paid shares
- Members should refer to the attached synopsis for complete details of partly paid shares available for trading

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 25th June 2026
- **Trading Resumption Date:** 2nd July 2026
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260505-41 dated 5th May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly impacts holders of partly paid-up equity shares of Prabha Energy Limited issued under the rights issue. With trading set to resume on 2nd July 2026, shareholders holding these partly paid shares will be able to buy or sell them on the exchange. The receipt of First Call Money (Rs. 47.52 per share) has triggered this resumption. The new ISIN (IN90I0M01022) must be used for all transactions in these shares. The impact is limited to existing rights shareholders and traders interested in this specific scrip.
