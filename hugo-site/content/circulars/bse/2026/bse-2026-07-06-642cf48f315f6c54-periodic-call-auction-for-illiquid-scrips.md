## Summary

BSE has issued a periodic review of scrips subject to the Periodic Call Auction (PCA) mechanism for illiquid securities, as mandated under SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/38/2013 dated December 19, 2013. Based on trading activity analysis for the period January 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026, BSE has identified securities to be added to and removed from the PCA mechanism, effective July 9, 2026. The full lists are provided in Annexure I (scrips moving into PCA) and Annexure II (scrips moving out of PCA) attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- This is a periodic (recurring) surveillance review conducted in consultation with SEBI and applied uniformly across stock exchanges.
- The review period covered trading activity from January 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026.
- Scrips meeting illiquidity criteria will be moved into the Periodic Call Auction mechanism.
- Scrips no longer meeting illiquidity criteria will be moved out of the Periodic Call Auction mechanism.
- Changes take effect from July 9, 2026.
- Trading members are advised to exercise additional due diligence when trading in these securities, whether on own account or on behalf of clients.
- Detailed scrip lists are contained in Annexure_PCAS (Annexure I for additions, Annexure II for removals), which is a separate attachment not included in the notice text.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine periodic application of the existing SEBI-mandated Periodic Call Auction framework established via SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/38/2013 (December 19, 2013) and BSE Notice No. 20131220-26 (December 20, 2013).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must identify securities newly added to the PCA list and adjust trading practices accordingly, since these securities will only trade via periodic call auction rather than continuous trading.
- Members should exercise enhanced due diligence for both incoming and outgoing PCA scrips.
- Members should refer to Annexure_PCAS for the specific list of affected securities.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Review period analyzed: January 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026
- Effective date of changes: July 9, 2026
- Notice date: July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific illiquid scrips listed in the annexures, which will experience reduced trading frequency (periodic call auctions rather than continuous trading) if added, or resumption of normal continuous trading if removed. This affects price discovery and liquidity for holders and traders of these specific securities, though the overall market impact is low given these are typically low-volume, illiquid names. Trading members handling affected securities should update systems and client communications accordingly.
