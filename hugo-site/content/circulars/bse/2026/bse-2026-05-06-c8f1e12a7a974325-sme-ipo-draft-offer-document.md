## Summary

BSE Limited has published a notice (No. 20260506-5) making available the Draft Offer Documents of two SME companies — Bipin Offset Limited and Shree TNB Polymers Limited — on the BSE SME Exchange website. These documents are hosted for a mandatory public comment period of at least 21 days as required under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Documents for two SME IPOs have been filed with BSE SME Exchange
- Both companies filed their documents on 05 May 2026
- Documents are available at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from the date of this notice (by 27 May 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued in compliance with existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018, which mandates public availability of Draft Offer Documents for a minimum of 21 days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and market participants may review the Draft Offer Documents on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of this notice
- The companies and their advisors should ensure documents remain accessible on the exchange website throughout the comment period

## Important Dates

- Date of Filing (both companies): 05 May 2026
- Notice Date: 06 May 2026
- Public Comment Deadline: 27 May 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant to prospective retail and institutional investors interested in the upcoming SME IPOs of Bipin Offset Limited and Shree TNB Polymers Limited. The 21-day public comment window provides an opportunity for stakeholders to raise concerns before these companies proceed with their public offerings on the BSE SME platform.
