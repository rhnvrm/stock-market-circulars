## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department issued Notice No. 20260407-19 on 7th April 2026, informing members and investors that it had sought clarification from FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Scrip Code: 543384) regarding a news article published on economictimes.indiatimes.com. The company has responded, and the reply is attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Scrip Code: 543384) about a news article on Economic Times
- The company has provided its reply, which is attached to the circular
- The notice is issued to ensure accuracy of information available to members and investors
- Issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose of the Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice providing the company's clarification on a news article.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 7th April 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine surveillance clarification notice. Investors in FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Nykaa) should review the attached company reply for any material information related to the referenced Economic Times news article.
