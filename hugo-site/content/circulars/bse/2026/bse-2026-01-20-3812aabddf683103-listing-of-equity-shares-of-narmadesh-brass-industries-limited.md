## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544680, symbol NARMADESH, and ISIN number INE0S1B01014.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited
- Scrip Code: 544680
- Symbol: NARMADESH
- ISIN: INE0S1B01014
- Segment: SME
- Specific listing date to be announced through separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details for when trading commences. No immediate compliance actions required until listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate impact. This is a standard listing announcement for a new SME segment company. Trading members and investors interested in the SME segment should await the separate notice that will provide the actual listing date and additional security details before trading can commence.
