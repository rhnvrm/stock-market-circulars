## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 197 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) are permitted for trading effective Monday, February 23, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, originally issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 197 equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited admitted to trading on BSE from February 23, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, of face value Rs. 10/- each
- Original issuance was on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Two tranches: 25 shares (Distinctive Nos. 107803612–107803636) and 172 shares (Distinctive Nos. 161365796–161365967)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for newly converted shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No specific compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 20, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** Monday, February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Market impact is minimal. Only 197 equity shares are being listed, representing a negligible addition to the float of Fusion Finance Limited. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so there is no dilution or preferential treatment differential. This is a routine administrative listing following completion of the rights issue conversion process.
