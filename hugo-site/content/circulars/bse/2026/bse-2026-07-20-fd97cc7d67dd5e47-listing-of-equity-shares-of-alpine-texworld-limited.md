## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Alpine Texworld Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Tuesday, July 21, 2026, under the 'T' Group of Securities. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Alpine Texworld Limited, registered office in Paldi Kankaj, Ahmedabad, Gujarat
- Number of securities: 38,247,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 38,247,000
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: ALPINETEX
- Scrip Code: 544825
- ISIN: INE1JCQ01037
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 105/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 95/-)
- Date of allotment in public issue: July 17, 2026
- Financial Year end: March 31
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 21, 2026
- Registrar to the issue: Kfin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notice implementing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 on Trade-for-Trade segment applicability for newly listed scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the scrip will trade only in demat form.
- The company has executed necessary corporate action to mark the lock-in period in depository records per Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern is disclosed as per Annexure II.
- Trading members must account for Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) procedures on the listing day.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 17, 2026
- Listing and dealing effective from: Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS): Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable for: first 10 trading days from listing

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice for a newly issued IPO stock. Market impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in ALPINETEX shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 days is a standard SEBI safeguard to curb speculative trading in newly listed scrips and does not affect broader market operations.
