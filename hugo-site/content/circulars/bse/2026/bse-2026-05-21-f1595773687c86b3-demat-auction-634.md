## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 634, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-634/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for May 21, 2026, with pay-in obligations due on May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 21, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on May 21, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on May 21, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on May 21, 2026
- Auction pay-in will be held on Friday, May 22, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections permitted for punching errors; trading members must exercise utmost care with securities code and rate entry
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning any reason

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. Notably, the previous restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the specified session timings
- Members and custodians are required to verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories before depository cut-off timings on May 22, 2026
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Trading members should also refer to relevant notices regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments

## Important Dates

- **May 21, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), and final reports (up to 5:30 PM)
- **May 22, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational auction notice affecting trading members with short-delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-634/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auction for settlements with short deliveries (post Uniform Membership structure) provides greater flexibility to trading members. The notice has limited broad market impact but is operationally significant for affected clearing and trading members who must adhere to strict session timings and depository cut-off deadlines.
