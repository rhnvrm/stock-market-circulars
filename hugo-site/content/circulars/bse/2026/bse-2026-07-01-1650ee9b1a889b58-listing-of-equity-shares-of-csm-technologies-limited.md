## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that the equity shares of CSM Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544806, trading symbol CSM, and ISIN INE0ZK601013.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of CSM Technologies Limited are approved for listing and dealing on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 544806
- Symbol: CSM
- ISIN: INE0ZK601013
- The exact date of listing and further security details will be announced through a separate notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN ahead of the listing date once announced.

## Important Dates

Notice dated July 1, 2026. Specific listing date to be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new tradable security to the Exchange, providing investors an opportunity to trade CSM Technologies Limited shares once dealings commence. Market impact is limited until the listing date and trading details are confirmed.
