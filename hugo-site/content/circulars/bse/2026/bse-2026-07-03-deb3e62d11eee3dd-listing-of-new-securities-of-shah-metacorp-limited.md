## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 97,163,362 new equity shares of Shah Metacorp Limited (Scrip Code - 533275), issued on a rights basis, have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 97,163,362 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.86/- on rights basis.
- ISIN: INE482J01021
- Distinctive numbers: 885210867 to 982374228
- Date of allotment: 30/06/2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members other than updating systems to permit trading of the newly listed shares from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 30 June 2026
- Listing and trading effective date: 6 July 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Shah Metacorp Limited following its rights issue. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip, with no broader market implications.
