## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in commercial paper of 10 companies effective from their respective record dates (January 20-21, 2026) for the purpose of redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial paper instruments from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to 10 commercial paper instruments
- Record dates set for January 20, 2026 (4 instruments) and January 21, 2026 (6 instruments)
- Trading suspension begins on the same date as the record date for each instrument
- Purpose of suspension is redemption of commercial paper
- Notice issued under reference numbers DR-802/2025-2026 and DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial paper from the specified "No Dealings From" dates
- Market participants should note the record dates for redemption purposes
- Compliance with suspension directive is mandatory for all exchange members

## Important Dates

**Record Date: January 20, 2026 (No Dealings From: January 20, 2026)**
- Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited (INE417C14850)
- Standard Chartered Capital Limited (INE403G14TL5)
- ONGC Petro additions Limited (INE163N14550)
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (INE01C314EB5)

**Record Date: January 21, 2026 (No Dealings From: January 21, 2026)**
- Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (INE824H14RH2)
- Vivriti Capital Limited (INE01HV14979)
- Godrej Housing Finance Limited (INE02JD14641)
- Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (INE523L14AN6)
- Navi Finserv Limited (INE342T14ES5)
- Bajaj Finance Limited (INE296A14E04)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific commercial paper instruments. The suspension is temporary and relates to scheduled redemptions.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to prevent dealing in these instruments from the specified dates. Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms.

**Issuer Profile:** The list includes financial services companies (Bajaj Finance, Bajaj Financial Securities, Standard Chartered Capital), housing finance (Godrej Housing Finance), wealth management (Nuvama), and industrial corporations (Pilani Investment, ONGC Petro additions).

**Investor Action:** Holders of these commercial papers should prepare for redemption and expect settlement of proceeds according to the instruments' terms and conditions.
