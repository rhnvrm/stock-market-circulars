## Summary

Oberoi Realty Limited has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE093I07066, Code: 976126). The debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 20,750 per debenture effective January 8, 2026. Trading members are notified to account for the reduced face value in trading operations from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures of Oberoi Realty Limited
- ISIN: INE093I07066
- Scrip Code: 976126 (ORL-7.95-24-10-26-PVT)
- Reduced face value: Rs. 20,750 per debenture
- Record date: January 8, 2026
- Effective date for trading with reduced face value: January 8, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-794/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for partial redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading in these debentures reflects the reduced face value of Rs. 20,750 per debenture from January 8, 2026
- Members should update their systems to account for the face value reduction in settlement DR-794/2025-2026
- Debenture holders as per record date (January 8, 2026) will receive partial redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 8, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-794/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Partial redemption will result in cash receipt while remaining investment continues at reduced face value. This improves liquidity for investors seeking partial exits.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal disruption expected as the reduced face value will be systematically updated in trading systems. Price adjustments will reflect the lower face value from the effective date.

**Market Significance**: Limited broader market impact as this affects only specific debenture holders of Oberoi Realty Limited. Standard corporate action in debt market operations.
