## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Kiaasa Retail Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The scrip has been assigned code 544711 under the symbol KIAASA on the SME segment. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Kiaasa Retail Limited equity shares will be listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544711 | Symbol: KIAASA | ISIN: INE1C3F01018
- Actual listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice issued under BSE compliance procedures for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing of KIAASA RETAIL LIMITED
- Members should await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine pre-listing notification for a new SME IPO on BSE. The market impact is limited to investors and trading members with exposure to or interest in Kiaasa Retail Limited. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated. Members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date.
