## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260310-26 informs trading members that new commercial paper (CP) securities issued on a private placement basis by multiple companies have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective March 10, 2026. The issuers include DSP Finance Private Limited, Reliance Retail Ventures Limited, Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited (two tranches), Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, and Bajaj Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- All listed securities are Commercial Papers issued on a private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination for trading is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise for all listed securities
- Interest payment basis is cumulative for all instruments
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's existing framework for privately placed debt securities on the BSE Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these securities occur only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be executed in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers listed below
