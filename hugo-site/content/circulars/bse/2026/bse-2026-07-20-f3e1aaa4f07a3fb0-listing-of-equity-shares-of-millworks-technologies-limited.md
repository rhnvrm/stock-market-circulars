## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that the equity shares of Millworks Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The notice provides the scrip code, trading symbol, and ISIN for the company but does not specify the exact listing date.

## Key Points

- Company: Millworks Technologies Limited
- Segment: SME
- Scrip Code: 544826
- Symbol: MILLWORKS
- ISIN: INE1SRC01010
- The actual date of listing and further securities details will be communicated through a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from market participants at this stage; trading members should await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date before enabling trading in the scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 20 July 2026
- Listing date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low immediate market impact as this is a procedural pre-listing notification for an SME company. Impact will become more relevant once the actual listing date and trading commence, at which point price discovery and liquidity effects may be observed for the scrip.
