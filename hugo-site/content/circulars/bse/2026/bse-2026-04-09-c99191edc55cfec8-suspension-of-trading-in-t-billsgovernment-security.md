## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that government security GS15APR26C (Scrip Code: 800649, ISIN: IN000426C055) is set to mature on redemption. Trading in this security is suspended with effect from April 10, 2026 (Reference: DR-608/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 800649 (GS15APR26C, ISIN: IN000426C055) will mature on redemption
- Trading in the above government security is suspended from April 10, 2026
- This falls under the Debt segment, Corporate Actions category
- Reference number: DR-608/2026-2027
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a standard operational notice for the suspension of trading in a maturing government security under existing BSE listing and debt segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in Scrip Code 800649 (GS15APR26C) on or after April 10, 2026
- Members are advised to update their systems and inform relevant desks to avoid any erroneous transactions in the suspended security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 9, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** April 10, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** April 15, 2026 (as indicated by the scrip name GS15APR26C)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to participants holding or trading in GS15APR26C on BSE's debt segment. Upon redemption, holders will receive the par value of the security. Trading members must ensure no new positions are taken from April 10, 2026 onwards. The suspension is a routine administrative action with no broader market impact.
