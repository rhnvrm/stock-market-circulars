## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,497 new equity shares of Sammaan Capital Ltd. (Scrip Code: 535789) are permitted for trading effective Tuesday, April 21, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 3,497 equity shares of Sammaan Capital Ltd. admitted for trading on BSE
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up (Rs. 2/- each) on rights basis
- ISIN: INE148I01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 733942452 to 733945948
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 20, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: April 21, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 3,497 shares is a small incremental increase to the existing share count of Sammaan Capital Ltd. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, meaning no dilution preference or special rights are attached. Routine corporate action with negligible effect on liquidity or price discovery.
