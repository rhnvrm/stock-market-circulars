## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework. Securities newly meeting ESM criteria will attract a minimum 100% margin from June 30, 2026 and will be moved from Rolling Settlement to the Trade-for-Trade segment. Certain securities already in ESM will be downgraded to Stage I, Stage II securities will be subject to a 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction, and some securities will exit the framework entirely—all effective June 29–30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I meeting ESM criteria will attract **minimum 100% margin** on all open positions as on June 29, 2026, and on new positions from June 30, 2026.
- Securities meeting ESM criteria will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to **Trade-for-Trade (T4T) segment** w.e.f. June 30, 2026.
- Some securities currently in ESM will be moved to a **lower Stage (Stage I)** w.e.f. June 29, 2026 (listed in Annexure I).
- **Stage II** securities will operate under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** and **Periodic Call Auction** w.e.f. June 29, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework are listed in **Annexure II**, effective June 29, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in **Annexure III**.
- ESM placement is a surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company.
- Securities moving out of ESM will have their previous price band reinstated, unless under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice amends and continues the ESM framework established under prior BSE notices (20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61). It updates the list of securities across ESM stages and adjusts margin and settlement conditions accordingly.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/brokers** must ensure 100% margin is collected on positions in newly included ESM securities from the effective date.
- Members must note the shift to Trade-for-Trade for affected securities and adjust settlement processes.
- For Stage II securities, members must comply with the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction requirements.
- Clarifications can be directed to **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 29, 2026 | Securities move to Stage I (downgrade within ESM); Stage II T4T + 2% price band + PCA effective; securities exit ESM framework |
| June 30, 2026 | New ESM securities: 100% margin on open positions; shift to Trade-for-Trade segment |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **high market impact** for traders and investors holding positions in the affected securities. The imposition of 100% margin requirements significantly increases the cost of holding positions, likely reducing liquidity in these securities. The shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk awareness. Stage II securities face additional constraints via price bands and call auctions, further limiting intraday price discovery. Securities exiting the framework will see trading conditions normalize, with price bands restored to pre-ESM levels (subject to other surveillance measures). The specific securities affected are listed in the attached Annexures, which should be reviewed for portfolio exposure.
