## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that further new securities issued by six companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Friday, March 6, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS issuances
- All new securities are effective for trading from March 6, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed shares
- Notice issued under BSE Notice No. 20260305-18 dated March 5, 2026
- Category: Company Related | Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for ESOP/ESOS share listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. This notice is for informational purposes to ensure awareness of newly tradeable securities.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, March 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not applicable (no lock-in on any of the listed shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The share quantities involved are relatively small compared to the total outstanding shares of these large-cap companies. Intellect Design Arena sees the largest issuance at 1,59,601 shares, while ICICI Bank's issuance of 8,906 shares is the smallest. Since there are no lock-in restrictions, all newly issued shares are immediately available for trading. This is a standard operational event with no significant price or liquidity implications.
