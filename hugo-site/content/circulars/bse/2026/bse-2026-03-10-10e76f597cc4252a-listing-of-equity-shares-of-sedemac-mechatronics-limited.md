## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned Code 544723, Symbol SEDEMAC, and ISIN INE00XB01019. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544723; Symbol: SEDEMAC; ISIN: INE00XB01019
- Exact listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine new listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the subsequent notice confirming the listing date before initiating trades in SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited equity shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 10, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice marks the upcoming market debut of SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited on BSE. Trading members and investors interested in the mechatronics sector should monitor the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date. Impact is limited to participants in this specific scrip; no broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
