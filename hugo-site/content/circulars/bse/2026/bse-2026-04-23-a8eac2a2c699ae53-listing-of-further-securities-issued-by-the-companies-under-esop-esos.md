## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that further new securities issued by 7 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from **Friday, April 24, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 7 companies have had additional ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective April 24, 2026
- Total new shares across all companies: approximately 367,553 shares
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities
- Notice issued under BSE Notice No. 20260423-16, Category: Company Related, Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing of securities already issued to employees under approved ESOP/ESOS schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new ISIN distribution ranges for each company. No additional compliance action is required from investors or brokers beyond updating records to reflect the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 23, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, April 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares represent a minor increase in the tradeable float for each of the seven companies. The individual tranches are small relative to total outstanding shares, so price impact is expected to be negligible. Details of shares listed per company:
