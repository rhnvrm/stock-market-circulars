## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED (ISIN: INE0NES07089, Code: 975395) effective February 10, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED
- Debenture Series: KFL-11.05%-20-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE0NES07089
- BSE Code: 975395
- Record Date: February 10, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 10, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-817/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from February 10, 2026
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Compliance with record date provisions for interest payment and redemption eligibility

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity for debenture holders who may need to exit positions before the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders must ensure their holdings are recorded by the record date to be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading opportunity available from February 10, 2026 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems and block trading in these debentures from the specified date, and communicate the suspension to affected clients.
