## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, splitting them into Stage I and Stage II. Both stages carry enhanced margin requirements effective June 17, 2026, covering all open positions as on June 16, 2026, and any new positions created from June 17, 2026 onwards. Specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM criteria have been split into Stage I and Stage II based on the degree of surveillance concern.
- Stage I imposes a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Stage II imposes a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- The enhanced margins apply to open positions as on June 16, 2026 and all new positions from June 17, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and must not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a security exits the ASM framework, its price band is reinstated to the pre-ASM level, provided it is not part of any other surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to several prior exchange notices revising the ASM framework:
- Notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies existing ST-ASM provisions to the newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure that the prescribed enhanced margins (Stage I: ≥50%, Stage II: 100%) are collected on all affected open positions as on June 16, 2026, before the start of trading on June 17, 2026.
- Enhanced margins must also be applied to all new positions in shortlisted securities created from June 17, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I (attached to the notice) for the complete list of securities under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026** – Notice date; ST-ASM applicability announced.
- **June 16, 2026** – Reference date for open positions on which enhanced margins apply.
- **June 17, 2026** – Effective date for enhanced margin requirements on open positions and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I or Stage II face an immediate and significant increase in margin obligations, potentially requiring additional capital infusion within one trading day. Stage II securities at 100% margin effectively require full upfront funding, severely limiting leveraged positions. Market liquidity in affected scrips may reduce as leveraged participants are forced to reduce or close positions. The measure is temporary and surveillance-driven; scrips that meet exit criteria will have margins and price bands restored. Brokers need to promptly update risk management systems and notify clients holding affected securities.
