## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued a notice (No. 20260630-30) dated June 30, 2026, informing trading members of corporate actions affecting securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of the affected securities and the nature of the corporate actions are provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised to refer to the annexure for the full list of SLB securities subject to corporate actions.
- The notice is issued by the Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) on behalf of BSE Clearing (BSECL).
- The circular falls under the Corporate Actions category for the SLB segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice serves as an operational communication regarding existing corporate action procedures in the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in the SLB segment must review the attached annexure to identify affected securities and take appropriate action as per ICCL's standard corporate action handling procedures for SLB positions.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 30, 2026
- Specific effective dates for individual corporate actions are detailed in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market-wide impact. Members with open SLB positions in the affected securities may need to adjust their lending/borrowing arrangements in line with the corporate action outcomes. The scope is limited to participants in the SLB segment.
