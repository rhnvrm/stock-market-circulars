## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026, the equity shares of Kusumgar Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'B' Group of Securities, following completion of its initial public offering (IPO).

## Key Points

- Company: Kusumgar Limited, Registered Office at 101 Manjushree, V M Road Corner of N S Road No 5, JVPD Scheme, Vile Parle West, 400056
- Number of Securities: 104,991,372 Equity Shares of Rs. 1/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive Number range: 1 to 104,991,372
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: KUSUMGAR
- Scrip Code: 544821
- ISIN: INE0ISX01025
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 419/- per share (Face Value Rs. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 418/-)
- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 13, 2026
- Pari Passu status: Yes
- Financial Year: Ends March 31
- Lock-in details specified as per Annexure I
- Shareholding Pattern specified as per Annexure II
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form, per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 15, 2026, as per Exchange notice no. 20120216-29
- Lock-in period has been marked in depository records via corporate action
- Registrar to the Issue: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a listing notification pursuant to completion of an IPO process, consistent with existing SEBI dematerialisation and listing norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in Kusumgar Limited shares are executed only in dematerialised form.
- Trading members should note participation of the scrip in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on the listing day per existing exchange procedures for IPO/relisted scrips.
- Queries may be directed to the company (Devanand Parshottam Mojidra, Company Secretary and Compliance Officer) or to BSE contacts Mr. Parag Jain and Hardik Bhuta.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 13, 2026
- Listing and commencement of dealings: Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- Notice issued: Tuesday, July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces a new tradable scrip (KUSUMGAR, Scrip Code 544821) into the 'B' Group of Securities on BSE. Trading members and investors need to be aware of the new ISIN and trading parameters ahead of the listing date. Impact is limited to market participants trading or tracking this specific company and does not affect broader market operations or existing listed securities.
