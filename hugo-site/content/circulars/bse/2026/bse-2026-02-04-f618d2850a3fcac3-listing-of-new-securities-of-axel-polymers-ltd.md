## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading of 24,84,444 new equity shares of Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642) effective from February 5, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 35/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis and are subject to lock-in until August 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24,84,444 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 35/-
- Issue price: Rs. 45/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: December 13, 2025
- Trading commences: February 5, 2026
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE197C01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 8516681 to 11001124

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- All 24,84,444 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 10, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine preferential allotment listing for Axel Polymers Ltd. The shares represent an increase in the company's equity capital base. The lock-in period until August 10, 2026 ensures these shares will not immediately impact market liquidity. Trading members should update their records accordingly.
