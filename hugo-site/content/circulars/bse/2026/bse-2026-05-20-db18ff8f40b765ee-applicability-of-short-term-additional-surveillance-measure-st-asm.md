## Summary

BSE has shortlisted securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework under both Stage I and Stage II. Elevated margin requirements become effective May 22, 2026 for open positions held as of May 21, 2026 and for all new positions created from May 22, 2026 onwards. The Annexure I attached to the notice lists the specific securities covered.

## Key Points

- Securities in ST-ASM Stage I are subject to a minimum 50% margin rate (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%
- Securities in ST-ASM Stage II are subject to a minimum 100% margin rate (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%
- Both stages apply to open positions as of May 21, 2026 and new positions from May 22, 2026
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity
- Specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior Exchange notices revising the ASM framework:
- Notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM criteria to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) are collected on all open positions in shortlisted securities as of May 21, 2026
- Enhanced margins must be applied to all new positions in shortlisted securities from May 22, 2026 onwards
- Members may seek clarifications by writing to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026**: Notice issued
- **May 21, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margin
- **May 22, 2026**: Effective date for ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II margin requirements

## Impact Assessment

The enhanced margin requirements will significantly increase the cost of holding or initiating positions in the shortlisted securities. Stage II securities face a full 100% margin, effectively requiring traders to fund positions entirely from their own capital with no leverage. This is likely to reduce liquidity and trading volumes in the affected scrips. When a security exits the framework, its price band reverts to the pre-ASM level, unless another surveillance measure is in force. Market participants with existing positions in shortlisted securities should ensure sufficient margin availability before May 22, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation.
