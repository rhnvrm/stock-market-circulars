## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Elfin Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company will trade on the SME segment under scrip code 544724 and symbol ELFIN. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544724
- Trading Symbol: ELFIN
- ISIN: INE1FEW01013
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification for the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are hereby informed and should prepare for the admission of Elfin Agro India Limited shares to dealings once the listing date is confirmed. Members should await the follow-up notice for the specific listing date and security details before executing trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces Elfin Agro India Limited as a new publicly tradable entity on the BSE SME platform. The impact is primarily relevant to investors and trading members interested in the SME segment. Broader market impact is limited given the SME-segment scope, but the listing provides new investment opportunities in the agro sector.
