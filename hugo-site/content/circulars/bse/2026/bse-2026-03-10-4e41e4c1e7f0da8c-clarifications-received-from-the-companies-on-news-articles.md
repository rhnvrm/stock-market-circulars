## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from two listed companies — RailTel Corporation of India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543265) and Uno Minda Limited (BSE: 532539, NSE: UNOMINDA) — regarding news articles published on 9 March 2026 and any potential connection to share price/volume movements. Both companies responded confirming the news and asserting that no undisclosed material information exists.

## Key Points

- RailTel confirmed it signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with GAIL on 5 March 2026 for building a digital infrastructure backbone.
- RailTel clarified that the MoU is **non-binding** and does not qualify as a material event under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015.
- RailTel attributed share price movement to general market conditions, not the GAIL MoU news.
- RailTel confirmed no undisclosed material information exists that could explain trading movement.
- Uno Minda responded to queries regarding a news item titled "Uno Minda launches advanced trumpet horn range for aftermarket" published on ET Auto on 9 March 2026.
- Both companies affirmed commitment to timely and complete disclosure of all material events under applicable regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The circular is informational, reflecting compliance with BSE's standard surveillance query process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Both companies are required to respond to BSE surveillance queries under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.
- RailTel confirmed continued compliance with timely disclosure norms.
- Uno Minda confirmed awareness of disclosure obligations and provided clarification on the product-related news.

## Important Dates

- **5 March 2026**: RailTel signed MoU with GAIL for digital infrastructure backbone.
- **9 March 2026**: News articles published — GAIL-RailTel MoU (The Hindu BusinessLine) and Uno Minda trumpet horn launch (ET Auto).
- **9 March 2026**: BSE issued surveillance query emails to both companies.
- **10 March 2026**: Both companies submitted clarifications to BSE.

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal. RailTel's MoU with GAIL is explicitly non-binding with no finalized terms, and has been classified as a non-material event under SEBI LODR. Share price movement was attributed to broader market conditions. Uno Minda's clarification pertains to a routine product launch in the aftermarket segment. No new compliance obligations, penalties, or trading restrictions arise from this circular. Investors should note that neither disclosure alters the fundamental outlook of either company in a material way.
