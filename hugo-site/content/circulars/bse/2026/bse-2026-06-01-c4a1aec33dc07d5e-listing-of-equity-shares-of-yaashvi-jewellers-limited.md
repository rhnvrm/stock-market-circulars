## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The scrip carries code 544770, symbol YAASHVI, and ISIN INE1T6L01010. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544770 | Symbol: YAASHVI | ISIN: INE1T6L01010
- Listing date not specified in this notice; will be communicated separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new SME IPO entrant.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. Further trading and settlement details will be provided in the subsequent notice announcing the effective listing date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 1, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact as this is a small-cap SME segment listing. Relevant primarily to investors and trading members dealing in SME securities. The scrip will become available for trading on the date announced in the follow-up notice.
