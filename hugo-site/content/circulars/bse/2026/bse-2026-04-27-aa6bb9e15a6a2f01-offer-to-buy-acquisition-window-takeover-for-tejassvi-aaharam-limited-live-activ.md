## Summary

BSE has announced the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) schedule for TEJASSVI AAHARAM LIMITED (Symbol: TEJASSVI, Scrip Code: 531628, ISIN: INE173E01019). The offer window runs from Tuesday, 28th April 2026 to Tuesday, 12th May 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share at face value of Rs. 1.00, for up to 70,00,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** TEJASSVI AAHARAM LIMITED | Symbol: TEJASSVI | Scrip Code: 531628 | ISIN: INE173E01019
- **Offer Size:** Up to 70,00,000 Equity Shares at Rs. 10.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 1.00)
- **Offer Window:** 28th April 2026 (Opening) to 12th May 2026 (Closing)
- **Live OTB Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily during the issue period
- **Give-up Period:** 28th April 2026 to 11th May 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Last Day Give-up:** 12th May 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Last Day Take-up:** 12th May 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.)
- Bidding is available across investor categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NOH, CO, IND
- Only shares held in **demat form** are eligible for bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under existing OTB framework as referenced in Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017). Leased Line access instructions refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members may only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after the Exchange receives confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.
- Members and Custodians should use the iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/) for participation.

## Impact Assessment

This notice directly impacts trading members, custodian members, and retail/institutional investors holding shares of TEJASSVI AAHARAM LIMITED (531628). The acquisition window provides an exit opportunity at Rs. 10.00 per share — 10x the face value of Rs. 1.00. Trading members must act within the specified session timings and ensure UCC compliance before submitting bids. The relatively large offer size of up to 70 lakh shares signals a significant ownership change. All participants should refer to the iBBS and RTRMS platforms for bid submission and monitor the Extranet for post-closure acceptance files.
