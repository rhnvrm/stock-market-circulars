## Summary

BSE issued surveillance queries to three companies - Laxmi Dental Limited, Hindustan Tin Works Limited, and Capri Global Capital Limited - regarding unusual price or volume movements in their securities. All three companies responded that they have no unpublished price sensitive information pending disclosure under SEBI LODR Regulation 30, and confirmed that the movements are purely market-driven.

## Key Points

- Laxmi Dental Limited (Scrip Code: 544339, Symbol: LAXMIDENTL) clarified volume increase inquiry dated January 21, 2026
- Hindustan Tin Works Limited (Scrip Code: 531595) clarified price movement inquiry dated January 20, 2026
- Capri Global Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 531595) clarified volume increase inquiry dated January 21, 2026
- All companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- No unpublished price sensitive information or events due for disclosure
- All companies attributed price/volume movements to normal market behavior

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies affirmed they have disclosed all material information affecting operations and performance
- All requisite disclosures to stock exchanges have been made within stipulated timeframes

## Important Dates

- January 21, 2026: Date of BSE surveillance queries and company responses for Laxmi Dental Limited and Capri Global Capital Limited
- January 20, 2026: Date of BSE surveillance query for Hindustan Tin Works Limited

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it contains routine surveillance clarifications with no new material information. The companies confirmed that recent price and volume movements are market-driven with no underlying corporate developments. This is a standard regulatory procedure by BSE to ensure transparency and prevent information asymmetry in the market. No action required by market participants.
