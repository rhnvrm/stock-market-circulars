## Summary

BSE has listed 9,987,258 new equity shares of Vineet Laboratories Limited (Scrip Code: 543298) issued through a rights offering. The shares are listed with effect from February 5, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 9,987,258 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 20/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 30/- per share
- Shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE505Y01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 9219009 to 19206266
- Date of Allotment: February 2, 2026
- Trading commences: February 5, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading from February 5, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: February 2, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 5, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The rights issue increases the equity capital of Vineet Laboratories Limited by approximately 10 million shares. The listing enables shareholders who subscribed to the rights issue to trade their allotted shares. No impact on broader market operations or other securities.
