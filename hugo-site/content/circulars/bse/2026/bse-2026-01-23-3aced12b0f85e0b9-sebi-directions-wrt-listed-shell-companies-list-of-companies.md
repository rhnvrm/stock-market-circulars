## Summary

BSE announced that Alka India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530889) will be removed from Stage 3 of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective January 29, 2026. This action follows an NCLT approved resolution plan dated February 07, 2025. The trading suspension on the company's securities will also be revoked on the same date. The stock will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs and Relisted scrips upon resumption.

## Key Points

- Alka India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530889) to be removed from Stage 3 of GSM framework
- Action follows NCLT approved resolution plan dated February 07, 2025
- Trading suspension will be revoked on the same date as GSM removal
- References previous Exchange notices dated May 06, 2022 (20220506-12) and February 14, 2024 (20240214-11)
- Stock will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs & Relisted scrips
- Trading members should refer to notice 20120216-29 for SPOS procedures

## Regulatory Changes

The company is being removed from the shell company surveillance framework (Stage 3 of GSM) following successful completion of a resolution plan approved by the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT). This represents a positive regulatory status change, moving the company from enhanced surveillance back to normal trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the removal of Alka India Ltd. from Stage 3 GSM framework
- Trading members should familiarize themselves with Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) procedures as outlined in notice 20120216-29
- Market participants must take note of the trading resumption and updated status
- For clarifications, contact BSE at 022-2272 8083

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 29, 2026 (Thursday)**: Effective date for removal from Stage 3 GSM framework
- **January 29, 2026 (Thursday)**: Trading suspension revoked
- **February 07, 2025**: NCLT order date approving resolution plan

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to single stock. Positive development for Alka India Ltd. shareholders as trading restrictions are lifted.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to prepare for stock's participation in SPOS. Normal trading will resume after pre-open session.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders of Alka India Ltd. will regain ability to trade the stock after suspension period. The NCLT-approved resolution plan suggests successful corporate restructuring.

**Precedent**: Demonstrates the exit pathway for companies classified as shell companies under SEBI framework when they successfully implement resolution plans.
