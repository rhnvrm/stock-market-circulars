## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) will officially change its name to Flattrade Broking Private Limited effective Monday, March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) is changing its name.
- New name: Flattrade Broking Private Limited.
- Effective date: Monday, March 9, 2026.
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative name change for a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members who interact with or reference Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. should update their records to reflect the new name, Flattrade Broking Private Limited, effective March 9, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 9, 2026**: Effective date of name change from Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. to Flattrade Broking Private Limited.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an internal administrative update affecting only the name of Member No. 6524. No changes to membership status, trading rights, or operational functions are indicated.
