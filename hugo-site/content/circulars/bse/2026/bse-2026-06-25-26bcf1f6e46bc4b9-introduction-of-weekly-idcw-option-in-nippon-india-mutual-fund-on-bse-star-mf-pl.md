## Summary

BSE has notified members that Nippon India Mutual Fund has introduced a Weekly Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) Payout option under two of its debt schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The new option is available with effect from June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Weekly IDCW Payout option introduced for **Nippon India Ultra Short Duration Fund**
- Weekly IDCW Payout option introduced for **Nippon India Money Market Fund**
- Both options are available on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: June 24, 2026
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Nippon India Mutual Fund is attached to the circular for member reference

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a product enhancement by Nippon India Mutual Fund, adding a weekly frequency IDCW distribution option to existing debt fund schemes already available on BSE StAR MF.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members. The notice is informational. Members dealing in mutual fund transactions on BSE StAR MF should refer to the attached Nippon India Mutual Fund Notice-cum-Addendum for scheme-specific details.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 24, 2026 — Weekly IDCW Payout option available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine addition of a distribution frequency option for two existing Nippon India debt schemes. Investors in Nippon India Ultra Short Duration Fund and Nippon India Money Market Fund who prefer weekly income payouts now have access to this option via the BSE StAR MF platform. No changes to existing scheme structures, mandates, or other investor categories.
