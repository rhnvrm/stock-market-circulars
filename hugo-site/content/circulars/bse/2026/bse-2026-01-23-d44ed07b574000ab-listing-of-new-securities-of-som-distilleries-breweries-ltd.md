## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 287,880 equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from January 27, 2026, and are subject to a lock-in period until July 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- 287,880 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 108/-
- Issue price: Rs. 110/- per share
- Allotment date: February 22, 2025
- Securities issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Distinctive Numbers: 203473273 to 203761152
- All shares subject to lock-in until July 28, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Shares cannot be traded by allottees until lock-in period expires on July 28, 2027
- Securities rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Allotment Date**: February 22, 2025
- **Trading Commences**: January 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expires**: July 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential allotment represents warrant conversion to promoters with approximately 30-month lock-in period from allotment date. The shares cannot be sold in the open market until July 2027, preventing immediate supply pressure. This is a routine corporate action typical of preferential allotments to promoters for capital raising purposes.
