## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260615-36 informs trading members that Mirc Electronics Ltd (scrip code 500279) has officially changed its corporate name to ONIDA ELECTRONICS LIMITED. The Scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 500279 previously listed as **Mirc Electronics Ltd** is being renamed to **ONIDA ELECTRONICS LIMITED**.
- The Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System changes to **ONIDA**.
- The abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System will be **ONIDA**.
- A Certificate of Incorporation has been attached to confirm the name change.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

The company's trading identifiers on BSE's BOLT Plus System are being updated to reflect the new legal name. The Scrip ID and abbreviated name are revised from references to "Mirc" to "ONIDA".

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants must update their internal systems, records, and references to reflect the new company name (ONIDA ELECTRONICS LIMITED) and the revised Scrip ID/abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System effective June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational update with moderate impact. Traders, brokers, and back-office systems referencing Mirc Electronics Ltd or the old scrip identifiers will need to update their records to avoid confusion or failed lookups. The underlying scrip code (500279) remains unchanged, limiting the scope of disruption. Retail investors holding the stock are unaffected in terms of holdings but should note the new trading name ONIDA ELECTRONICS LIMITED.
