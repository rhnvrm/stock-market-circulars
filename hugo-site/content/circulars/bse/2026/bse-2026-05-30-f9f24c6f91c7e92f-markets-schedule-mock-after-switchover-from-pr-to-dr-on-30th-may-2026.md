## Summary

BSE has initiated a switchover of its trading systems from the Primary (PR) location to the Disaster Recovery (DR) location on 30th May 2026, in continuation of Notice Nos. 20260529-7,8,9,10,11 dated 29th May 2026. Trading will commence in a Pre-Open session at the DR location from 12:45 pm for Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segments.

## Key Points

- Exchange-wide switchover from PR to DR location effective 30th May 2026
- All segments (Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, EGR) affected
- Pre-Open session begins at 12:45 pm at DR location
- Continuous trading sessions across all segments run from 1:00 pm to 2:45 pm
- Trade Modification deadline is 3:00 pm for most segments
- This is a Mock Trading Session under DR conditions

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is an operational switchover to the Disaster Recovery (DR) site for mock trading purposes, resulting in revised intraday session timings for all segments on 30th May 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to the revised DR Mock Trading Session timings
- All orders and trades must be placed within the revised session windows
- Trade Modifications must be completed by 3:00 pm (or 2:30 pm for T+0 Equity)

## Important Dates

- **30th May 2026**: PR to DR switchover; DR Mock Trading sessions active
- **12:45 pm**: Pre-Open session begins at DR location for all segments
- **1:00 pm**: Continuous Trading commences (Equity, Equity Derivatives)
- **2:45 pm**: Continuous Trading closes for most segments
- **3:00 pm**: Trade Modification deadline for Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Cross Currency Derivatives
- **2:30 pm**: Trade Modification T+0 deadline for Equity

## Impact Assessment

**Equity Segment:**
- Pre-Open Order Entry: 12:45 pm – 12:53 pm
- Pre-Open Matching: 12:53 pm – 1:00 pm
- Continuous Trading T+1: 1:00 pm – 2:45 pm
- Continuous Trading T+0: 1:00 pm – 2:15 pm
- Afternoon Block Deal Window: 2:00 pm – 2:15 pm
- Auction Offer Entry and Matching: 1:30 pm – 2:00 pm
- Closing: 2:45 pm – 2:55 pm
- Post-Closing: 2:55 pm – 3:05 pm

**Equity Derivatives Segment:**
- Switchover to DR: 12:00 pm – 12:45 pm
- Pre-Open Order Entry: 12:45 pm – 12:52/53 pm
- Order Matching: 12:53 pm – 12:57 pm
- Buffer Period: 12:57 pm – 1:00 pm
- Continuous Trading: 1:00 pm – 2:45 pm
- Trade Modification deadline: 3:00 pm

**Currency Derivatives Segment:**
- Continuous Trading (Currency): 12:45 pm – 2:45 pm
- Continuous Trading (Cross Currency): 12:45 pm – 2:45 pm
- Trade Modification deadline: 3:00 pm (both)

**Commodity Derivatives Segment:**
- Continuous Trading: 12:45 pm – 2:45 pm

All market participants trading on BSE across all segments are impacted by the compressed and shifted trading schedule. Normal trading hours are suspended for the day in favor of these DR mock session timings.
