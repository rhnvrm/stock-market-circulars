## Summary

BSE has informed members that TRUST Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility for TRUSTMF Large & Mid Cap Fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 3, 2026, to July 17, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: TRUSTMF Large & Mid Cap Fund (Regular Plan - Growth, ISIN INF0GCD01909; Direct Plan - Growth, ISIN INF0GCD01891)
- Category: Equity
- NFO period: July 3, 2026 to July 17, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 24, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform with SIP facility

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new mutual fund scheme launch, not a regulatory amendment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period trades) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag.
- Transactions with payment initiated remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026 for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- NFO extended till midnight July 17, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on July 17, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 3, 2026 – July 17, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 17, 2026
- ICCL clear funds deadline: 6:00 PM, July 23, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular relevant primarily to BSE members, mutual fund distributors, and investors participating in the NFO via BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader market trading and carries low overall market significance.
