## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 660, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-660/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Tuesday, June 30, 2026, with pay-in on July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 30, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): Wednesday, July 1, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member following implementation of Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in an auction in the settlement in which they short delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member. The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction in the same settlement where they had short delivery has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings on July 1, 2026
- Trading members must exercise utmost care while entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted for punching errors
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Trading members should monitor notices regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) for relevant scrips

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), auction matching, delivery statements
- **July 1, 2026**: Auction securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational auction notice affecting trading members with short delivery obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-660/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in an auction for the same settlement in which short delivery occurred provides additional flexibility to trading members. Overall market impact is limited and procedural in nature.
