## Summary

BSE has listed two privately placed debt securities on its debt market segment effective January 7, 2026. Ambium Finserve Limited issued NCDs worth Rs. 30 crore (3,000 units of Rs. 10,000 each) at 11.16% p.a. interest with monthly payments until redemption on January 19, 2028. Lucina Land Development Limited issued NCDs worth Rs. 210 crore (2,100 units of Rs. 1,00,000 each) at 13% p.a. interest with monthly payments until redemption on January 30, 2029.

## Key Points

- Two companies' privately placed debt securities admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment
- Ambium Finserve Limited: 30,000 units, Face Value Rs. 10,000, Total Rs. 30 crore
- Lucina Land Development: 21,000 units, Face Value Rs. 1,00,000, Total Rs. 210 crore
- Both securities trade in dematerialized form only
- Tick size for both securities is 1 paise
- Market lot is 1 for both securities
- Ambium Finserve rated CARE BBB-/Stable
- Lucina Land Development rated IVR A-(CE)/Stable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- Put/call options as per respective Disclosure Documents must be observed

## Important Dates

**Ambium Finserve Limited (Scrip Code: 977439, ISIN: INE0RU307221)**
- Allotment Date: January 6, 2026
- Listing Date: January 7, 2026
- Interest Payment: Monthly from February 19, 2026 to January 19, 2028
- Redemption Date: January 19, 2028

**Lucina Land Development Limited (Scrip Code: 977440, ISIN: INE0JZO07040)**
- Allotment Date: January 5, 2026
- Listing Date: January 7, 2026
- Interest Payment: Monthly from January 31, 2026 to January 30, 2029
- Redemption Date: January 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification informing trading members about newly listed privately placed debt securities. The listing provides liquidity options for existing investors in these debt instruments. The securities are restricted to institutional and qualified investors as per private placement norms. Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.
