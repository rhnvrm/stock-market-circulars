## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the applicable price bands for 12 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 07, 2026. The revisions were made by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) as part of routine periodic price band adjustments.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 12 scrips across the Equity segment.
- Bharat Gears Ltd (505688) - revised to 5%
- Capital Trade Links Ltd (538476) - revised to 10%
- CLN Energy Ltd (544347) - revised to 5%
- Digitide Solutions Ltd (544413) - revised to 10%
- Galaxy Bearings Ltd (526073) - revised to 5%
- Kaiser Corporation Ltd (531780) - revised to 2%
- Lokesh Machines Ltd (532740) - revised to 2%
- National Standard (India) Ltd (504882) - revised to 10%
- OnMobile Global Ltd (532944) - revised to 5%
- Seshachal Technologies Ltd (531794) - revised to 5%
- Shikhar Consultants Ltd (526883) - revised to 5%
- Telge Projects Ltd (544544) - revised to 5%

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine surveillance action under BSE's price band monitoring framework, which periodically adjusts the maximum permissible daily price movement (circuit limits) for select scrips based on volatility and other exchange criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for the listed scrips and ensure orders are placed within the new circuit limits from the effective date. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 07, 2026
- Notice Date: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will constrain or expand the daily trading range for the affected scrips, directly impacting intraday volatility and order placement strategies for traders holding or trading these 12 stocks. Impact is limited to these specific scrips rather than the broader market.
