## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,78,20,426 new equity shares of Viram Suvarn Limited (Scrip Code: 540252) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 6, 2026. These shares were issued on a rights basis at a price of Rs. 8/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium of Rs. 6/-).

## Key Points

- 3,78,20,426 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 6/- (total issue price Rs. 8/-) on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE607V01028
- Distribution numbers: 7,56,40,853 to 11,34,61,278
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to facilitate trading in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions required beyond standard trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing increases the total share count of Viram Suvarn Limited through a rights issue, which is dilutive to existing shareholders. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, meaning no preferential rights. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and traders of this specific scrip (540252).
