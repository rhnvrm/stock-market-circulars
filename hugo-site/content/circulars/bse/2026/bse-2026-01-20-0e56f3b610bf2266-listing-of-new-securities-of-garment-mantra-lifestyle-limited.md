## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 40,950 equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 539216) arising from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 1/- each issued on rights basis. These shares will be available for trading from Wednesday, January 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 40,950 new equity shares listed for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares
- Face value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE653S01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 216115112 - 216156061
- Scrip Code: 539216
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities with effect from January 21, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 20, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** Wednesday, January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The listing of 40,950 shares from conversion of partly paid shares represents a small increase in the tradable float of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited. The shares carry the same rights as existing equity shares (pari-passu status), ensuring no dilution of shareholder rights.
