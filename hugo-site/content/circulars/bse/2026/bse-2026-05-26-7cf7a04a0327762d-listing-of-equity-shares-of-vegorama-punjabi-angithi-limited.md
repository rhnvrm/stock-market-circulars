## Summary

Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited (Scrip ID: VPAL, Scrip Code: 544765) is listed on BSE SME effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities. The scrip will be in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Wednesday, May 27, 2026
- **Scrip ID / Code:** VPAL / 544765
- **ISIN:** INE1L3801017
- **Group:** MT (SME segment)
- **Total Securities:** 16,610,994 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 16,610,994
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 77/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 67/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,600 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** May 25, 2026
- **Trade-for-Trade:** Yes, for 10 trading days per SEBI guidelines
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** Applicable on May 27, 2026
- **Trading Form:** Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is conducted under existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 (dematerialised trading), SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 (trade-for-trade), and the Special Pre-open Session framework (Notice No. 20120216-29).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip exclusively in dematerialised form.
- All trades must be in minimum market lot of 1,600 equity shares until modified by the Exchange with at least one month's prior notice.
- Lock-in shares in physical form have been enfaced as non-transferable; lock-in in demat form has been marked in depository records.
- Members should refer to SPOS guidelines for pre-open session procedures on the listing date.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **May 26, 2026:** Notice date
- **May 27, 2026:** Effective listing date; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)
- **10 trading days from May 27, 2026:** Trade-for-Trade segment restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with a moderate market impact confined to participants trading in the VPAL scrip. The trade-for-trade restriction for 10 days limits speculative activity and requires settlement on a gross basis. The market lot of 1,600 shares restricts retail participation to higher capital thresholds. Market maker Pace Stock Broking Services Private Limited is designated to provide liquidity. Registrar services are handled by Bigshare Services Private Limited. Overall impact on broader market operations is low.
