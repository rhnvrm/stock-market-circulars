## Summary

IIFL Finance Limited submitted a clarification to BSE regarding a news article titled "IIFL Finance Q3 Results: Stock tanks 15% despite sharp surge in Gold loans; Here's why" published on CNBCTV18.com on January 22, 2026. The company confirmed that it is not aware of any undisclosed information that could explain the stock price movement and stated that the price decline appears to be market sentiment driven.

## Key Points

- Company confirms no undisclosed information or events that could explain the stock price movement
- Q3 FY2026 financial results (quarter and nine months ended December 31, 2025) were announced in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations
- Stock price movement appears to be market sentiment driven or due to factors beyond company's control
- No ongoing negotiations or events beyond those disclosed in Board Meeting outcome dated January 22, 2026
- No initiation of any new regulatory or legal proceedings against the company
- No material event under Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of SEBI LODR Regulations warranting disclosure

## Regulatory Context

This clarification was provided in response to BSE's surveillance query (Ref No.: L/SURV/ONL/RV/APJ/(2025-2026)/144) under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has complied with disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations
- Company commits to promptly inform stock exchanges of any further material developments
- All existing disclosures are available in public domain

## Important Dates

- January 22, 2026: News article published and clarification submitted to BSE
- December 31, 2025: Quarter-end for financial results announced

## Impact Assessment

The clarification addresses investor concerns about significant stock price decline following quarterly results announcement. The company's statement that no material undisclosed events exist provides transparency but acknowledges market sentiment as the driver of price movement. This is a routine surveillance response with no material impact on trading or company operations.
