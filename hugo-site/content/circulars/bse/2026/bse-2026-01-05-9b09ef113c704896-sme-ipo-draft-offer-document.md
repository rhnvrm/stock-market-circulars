## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for Larisa Hotels and Resorts Limited's SME IPO, filed on December 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Company: Larisa Hotels and Resorts Limited
- Draft Offer Document filing date: December 31, 2025
- Notice date: January 05, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments to be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by: Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document must be hosted on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- All disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations must be included
- Public comments are invited and must be submitted within 21 days from January 05, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: December 31, 2025
- Notice publication date: January 05, 2026
- Public comment deadline: January 26, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the SME IPO process for Larisa Hotels and Resorts Limited. The impact is limited to:

- Potential investors interested in this specific SME IPO
- The company seeking to list on BSE SME platform
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the draft offer document

The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement to ensure transparency and allow stakeholders to review the offering details before the IPO proceeds.
