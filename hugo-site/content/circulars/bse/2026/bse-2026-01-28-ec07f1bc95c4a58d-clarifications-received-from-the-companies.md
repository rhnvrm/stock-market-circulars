## Summary

BSE has observed significant price and volume movements in the securities of Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd (Security Code: 523475) and Bharat Rasayan Ltd (Security Code: 590021). To ensure trading members and investors have access to latest relevant information, the Exchange has written to both companies seeking clarifications. Responses from the companies are awaited.

## Key Points

- Two companies identified with significant price/volume movements in recent trading
- Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd (Security Code: 523475)
- Bharat Rasayan Ltd (Security Code: 590021)
- BSE has written to both companies requesting clarifications
- Responses from companies are pending
- Action taken to ensure transparency for trading members and investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

The listed companies (Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd and Bharat Rasayan Ltd) are required to provide clarifications to BSE regarding the observed price/volume movements in their securities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 28, 2026
- **Clarification Response:** Awaited from both companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This surveillance action indicates unusual trading activity in the two securities which may affect investor confidence and trading decisions until clarifications are received.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or considering positions in Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd and Bharat Rasayan Ltd should await company responses before making trading decisions. The price/volume movements suggest potential material information or market speculation that requires official clarification.

**Compliance Impact:** The companies are under regulatory scrutiny and must provide timely responses to maintain compliance with disclosure norms and preserve market integrity.
