## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1946 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500300) are permitted for trading effective Friday, March 06, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 1946 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500300) are listed for trading
- Shares are of face value Rs. 2/- each, issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680461587 to 680463532
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted effective March 06, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly converted fully paid-up equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted (and informed) to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Friday, March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Only 1946 shares are being added to the tradeable float of Grasim Industries Ltd., a large-cap company. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares, meaning no dilution differential or preferential rights. This is a routine administrative listing arising from a rights issue partly paid share conversion.
