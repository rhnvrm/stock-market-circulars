## Summary

BSE has informed members that the Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund of Mirae Asset Mutual Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 11, 2026. This follows a prior suspension communicated via Notice No. 20260519-31 dated May 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund reopens for ongoing transactions (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP) from **June 11, 2026**
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Special NAV processing rules apply: bi-weekly NAV cutoffs (Monday and Thursday at 3:00 PM)
- If Monday or Thursday is a non-business day, redemption is processed on the next business day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice reinstates availability of the fund on the BSE StAR MF platform following a prior suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members dealing in mutual funds must note the NAV cutoff schedule for redemption processing
- Redemption requests received after Thursday 3:00 PM through Monday 3:00 PM are processed at Monday NAV
- Redemption requests received after Monday 3:00 PM through Thursday 3:00 PM are processed at Thursday NAV

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **Reference Notice**: No. 20260519-31 dated May 19, 2026 (original suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update restoring access to a single mutual fund family's scheme variants on the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors in the Platinum Hybrid Long-Short Fund who were unable to transact during the suspension period can resume normal subscription, redemption, and SIP activity from June 11, 2026.
