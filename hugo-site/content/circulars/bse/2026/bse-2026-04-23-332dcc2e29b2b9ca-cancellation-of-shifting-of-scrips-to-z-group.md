## Summary

BSE has cancelled the proposed shifting of two companies — Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (scrip code 531035) and Vikas Lifecare Ltd (scrip code 542655) — to the 'Z' group. This reverses the earlier announcement made via Exchange Notice No. 20260415-13 dated April 15, 2026. The cancellation is effective from April 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- The shifting of Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (531035) to 'Z' group has been cancelled.
- The shifting of Vikas Lifecare Ltd (542655) to 'Z' group has been cancelled.
- This notice supersedes the earlier Notice No. 20260415-13 dated April 15, 2026, with respect to these two scrips.
- All other provisions of the original notice remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

The earlier directive to shift Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd and Vikas Lifecare Ltd to the 'Z' group (as per Notice No. 20260415-13) is withdrawn for these two companies. Their group classification will remain as previously assigned and will not be changed to 'Z' group from April 27, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note that these two scrips will not move to the 'Z' group.
- No specific action is required from investors or brokers beyond acknowledging the cancellation.
- For queries or clarifications, market participants may write to bse.soplodr@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **April 15, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260415-13) issued announcing shifting to 'Z' group.
- **April 23, 2026**: Current notice issued cancelling the shift.
- **April 27, 2026**: The originally proposed effective date for the 'Z' group shift, which is now cancelled.

## Impact Assessment

The cancellation is a positive development for shareholders of Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd and Vikas Lifecare Ltd. Being placed in the 'Z' group typically implies non-compliance with listing requirements and results in restricted trading conditions. The reversal suggests that the compliance issues previously identified may have been resolved. The broader market impact is limited to these two scrips and their respective investor base.
