## Summary

BSE has notified its trading members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for the ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 20, 2026 to July 28, 2026, covering both the regular and direct plan schemes, with a tentative allotment date of July 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF (Equity category) and ICICI Prudential BSE Insurance ETF - Direct Plan (Equity category)
- NFO period: July 20, 2026 to July 28, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 31, 2026
- Platform: BSE StAR MF
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period trades) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag
- Transactions with payment initiated remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 30, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight July 28, 2026, only for lumpsum purchases

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice regarding a new scheme launch and associated transaction/payment processing instructions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by the specified cut-off times to avoid transaction cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 30, 2026, for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payment instruments must comply with the clear funds deadline

## Important Dates

- NFO opens: July 20, 2026
- NFO closes: July 28, 2026 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 30, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular affecting BSE StAR MF Platform members and their handling of NFO subscription transactions for a new ETF product. It has no direct impact on listed equity trading or existing stock prices; relevance is limited to mutual fund distributors and members processing this specific NFO.
