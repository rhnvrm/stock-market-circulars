## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 56,25,000 new equity shares of RDB Infrastructure and Power Limited (Scrip Code: 533285) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, April 23, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 40.50 per share (face value Re. 1/- with a premium of Rs. 39.50/-).

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 533285 (RDB Infrastructure and Power Limited)
- **Number of new shares:** 56,25,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 40.50/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 39.50/- premium)
- **Allottees:** Non-Promoters, via preferential basis on conversion of warrants
- **Listing effective date:** April 23, 2026
- **ISIN:** INE245L01028
- **Distribution numbers:** 204384001 to 210009000
- **New shares rank pari-passu** with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in the new shares from April 23, 2026 onwards.
- All 56,25,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 204384001 to 210009000) are subject to a lock-in and cannot be freely traded until October 31, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 25, 2026
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** April 23, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** October 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 56,25,000 additional equity shares results in share capital dilution for existing shareholders of RDB Infrastructure and Power Limited. Since these shares are issued to non-promoters via warrant conversion at Rs. 40.50/-, it signals prior fundraising activity now materializing into tradeable equity. The lock-in until October 31, 2026 limits immediate selling pressure from the newly allotted shares. Overall market impact is moderate and localized to existing shareholders and traders in this scrip.
