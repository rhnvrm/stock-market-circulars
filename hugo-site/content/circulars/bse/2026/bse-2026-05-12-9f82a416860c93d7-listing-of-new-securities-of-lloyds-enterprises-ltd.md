## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 7,244 new equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512463) are permitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, May 13, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 7,244 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. admitted for trading on BSE from May 13, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid up shares of face value Re. 1/- each
- ISIN: INE080I01025
- Distinctive Numbers: 1,494,194,859 to 1,494,202,102
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly eligible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Wednesday, May 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. Only 7,244 shares are being listed, which is a small addition to the existing equity capital of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. The conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid shares is a standard corporate action. These shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution impact beyond what was already anticipated at the time of the rights issue. Market impact is expected to be negligible.
