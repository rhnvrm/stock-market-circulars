## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in commercial papers of 24 companies that have fixed record dates for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective 'no dealings' dates, which range from January 22 to January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24 commercial paper instruments will be suspended from trading
- Suspension period: January 22-30, 2026 (depending on individual securities)
- Reason: Record date fixed for redemption of commercial papers
- Trading members must not deal in these securities from the specified dates
- Notice categorized under Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the listed commercial papers from their respective 'no dealings' dates
- Members must ensure compliance with suspension to facilitate smooth redemption process
- No transactions should be executed in these instruments during suspension period

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects debt market participants and commercial paper investors. Multiple issuers across financial services, infrastructure, and manufacturing sectors impacted.
