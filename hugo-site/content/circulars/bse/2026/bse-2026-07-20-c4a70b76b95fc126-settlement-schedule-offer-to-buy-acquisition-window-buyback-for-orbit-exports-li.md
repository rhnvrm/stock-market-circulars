## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of ORBIT EXPORTS LIMITED, to be conducted from 21/07/2026 to 27/07/2026. The notice specifies the buyback settlement number, bidding window, obligation download date, and settlement date, along with early pay-in procedures for participating members and custodians.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback (Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window)
- Settlement Number: 2026090
- Bid Start Date: 21/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 27/07/2026
- Obligation Download Date: 31/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 03/08/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism (available in both CDSL and NSDL)
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026090 in the depository system
- Reference to earlier Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding revised guidelines for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange mechanism
- Early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number; no EPN file upload is required
- Members must refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes existing buyback settlement guidelines under the previously issued Notice No. 20200528-32 (May 28, 2020) on Revised Guidelines for Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through the Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform.
- Early pay-in of securities in the depository system must be completed sufficiently in advance of the BSE OTB bidding window closure and the custodian confirmation window.
- No EPN file upload is required by Members/Custodians for early pay-in shares.
- Members must reference the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for stamp duty charges applicable to clients.
- For clarifications, Members/Custodians may contact the ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690, iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 21 July 2026
- Bid End Date: 27 July 2026
- Obligation Download Date: 31 July 2026
- Settlement Date: 03 August 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/settlement notice affecting members, custodians, and shareholders tendering shares in the Orbit Exports Limited buyback through the Acquisition Window mechanism. It ensures orderly settlement of the buyback tender process but does not carry broader market-wide implications beyond the participating stock and its intermediaries.
