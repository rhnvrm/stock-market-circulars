## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to dealings of Motilal Oswal Nifty India Defence ETF units in the 'B' Group effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended fund that replicates/tracks the Nifty India Defence Total Return Index. Trading will be conducted in dematerialized form only, with units traded in market lots of one unit.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 590152
- **ISIN**: INF247L01DJ0
- **BOLT System ID**: MODEFENCE
- **Total Units Listed**: 750,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 85.86 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: August 21, 2024
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B
- **Investment Manager**: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited
- **Registered Office**: 10th Floor, Motilal Oswal Tower, Rahimtullah Sayani Road, Opp. Parel ST Depot, Prabhadevi, Mumbai-400025

## Market Makers

Three market makers have been appointed:
1. East India Securities Ltd.
2. Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
3. Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
4. Irage Broking Services LLP

## Compliance Requirements

- Units must be traded in dematerialized form only, as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Trades will be executed in unit market lots (minimum 1 unit)
- Trading members must ensure compliance with dematerialization requirements

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 (Monday)
- **Date of Allotment**: August 21, 2024

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors access to a thematic ETF focused on India's defence sector through the Nifty India Defence Total Return Index. The presence of four market makers ensures adequate liquidity for the ETF. Impact is limited to mutual fund segment participants and investors interested in defence sector exposure through ETF instruments.
