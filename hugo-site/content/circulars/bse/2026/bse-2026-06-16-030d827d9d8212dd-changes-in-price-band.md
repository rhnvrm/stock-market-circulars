## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 15 equity scrips effective June 17, 2026, via Notice No. 20260616-26 issued by the Department of Surveillance (DOSS). The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, with most scrips being moved to tighter bands of 2% or 5%, indicating heightened surveillance monitoring.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 15 scrips effective June 17, 2026
- Bands revised downward to 2% for 4 scrips (Cosmo Ferrites, Duke Offshore, Parle Industries, Parmax Pharma, Shentracon Chemicals)
- Bands set at 5% for 6 scrips (Amanta Healthcare, Cemantic Infra-Tech, Deccan Health Care, Flexituff Ventures, Orosil Smiths India, Sunrise Efficient Marketing)
- Bands set at 10% for 2 scrips (Gautam Exim, MSTC Ltd)
- Bands set at 20% for 2 scrips (Rajnandini Fashion India, Shah Metacorp)
- Issued by DOSS Surveillance department; queries to be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band limits have been revised from their existing levels to new percentage limits as specified. Tighter bands (2–5%) restrict daily price movement more severely and are typically associated with surveillance actions or illiquidity concerns.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure all orders in the affected scrips comply with the revised price band limits from June 17, 2026 onwards. No action is required from listed companies; this is an exchange-level trading restriction.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 17, 2026
