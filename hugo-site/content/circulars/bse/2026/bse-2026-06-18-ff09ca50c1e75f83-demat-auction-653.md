## Summary

BSE Limited (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a notice inviting auction offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Demat Auction 653, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-653/2026-27. The auction offer entry session is scheduled for June 18, 2026, with securities pay-in due on June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on Thursday, June 18, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on June 18, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on June 18, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on June 18, 2026
- Securities pay-in (auction): Friday, June 19, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member following the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in an auction for settlements where they short-delivered has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The notice reiterates that under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and the prior restriction on offering securities in auction for self-short-delivered settlements has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before placing offers
- Members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM–2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM–3:00 PM), and reports
- **June 19, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice limited to trading members with short-delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-653/2026-27. Impact is confined to the specific members involved in this auction cycle. No broad market or investor-level impact anticipated.
