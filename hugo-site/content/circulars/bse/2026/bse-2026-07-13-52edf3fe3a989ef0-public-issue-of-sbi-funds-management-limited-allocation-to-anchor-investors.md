## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that, under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the public issue of SBI Funds Management Limited, 4,63,93,095 equity shares were subscribed on July 13, 2026, at a price of Rs. 574 per equity share. The notice encloses a copy of the company's announcement dated July 13, 2026, regarding the anchor investor allocation.

## Key Points

- 4,63,93,095 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the SBI Funds Management Limited public issue
- Allocation price: Rs. 574 per equity share
- Subscription occurred on July 13, 2026
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship department
- Company announcement dated July 13, 2026 enclosed as attachment (AnchorAllocationIntimation)

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members; the notice is for informational purposes ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Anchor investor allocation date and notice issuance date

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation signals strong institutional interest in the SBI Funds Management Limited IPO ahead of its public subscription window, which can influence retail and institutional investor sentiment. The Rs. 574 per share anchor price may serve as a reference point for pricing expectations in the broader public issue.
