## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for June 2026 for the Equity Derivatives segment. The notice specifies trade dates, the corresponding last dates for margin reporting, and penalty dates applicable in case of non-compliance.

## Key Points

- Covers all trading days in June 2026 (01-Jun to 30-Jun 2026) for the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Each trade date has a designated last date for margin reporting, typically 5 trading days after the trade date.
- Penalty dates follow the last reporting date by one trading day.
- Reporting deadlines extend into early July 2026 for late-June trade dates.
- Issued by ICCL Deputy Managers Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule notice under existing client margin reporting requirements for the Equity Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE must report client margins for each trade date in June 2026 by the corresponding last date of margin reporting. Failure to report by the last date will attract penalties as per the penalty dates listed in the schedule.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a routine operational impact on brokers and clearing members participating in the Equity Derivatives segment. Members must ensure timely client margin reporting to avoid penalties.
