## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Prayatna Developers Private Limited has fixed a record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) along with payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will continue with a reduced face value effective 16 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Prayatna Developers Private Limited
- Debenture Series: PDPL-7.30%-31-12-26-PVT (ISIN: INE925U07023, Scrip Code: 973756)
- Record Date: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 380,280 per debenture
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value: 16 March 2026
- Settlement: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's debt market segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures of Prayatna Developers Private Limited will trade with the reduced face value of Rs. 380,280 per debenture effective 16 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value post record date.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 16 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-840/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this pertains to a single privately placed NCD series of a private limited real estate developer. Debenture holders will receive part redemption proceeds along with interest on the record date. Post-redemption, the face value per debenture is reduced to Rs. 380,280. This is a routine debt corporate action with no broader market implications.
