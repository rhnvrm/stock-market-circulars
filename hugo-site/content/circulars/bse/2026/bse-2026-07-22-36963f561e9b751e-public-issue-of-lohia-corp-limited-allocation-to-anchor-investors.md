## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the public issue of Lohia Corp Limited, 1,15,79,133 equity shares have been subscribed on July 22, 2026 at a price of Rs. 425 per equity share. The notice encloses a copy of the company's announcement dated July 22, 2026 regarding this anchor allocation.

## Key Points

- Anchor Investors subscribed to 1,15,79,133 equity shares of Lohia Corp Limited.
- Anchor allocation price was Rs. 425 per equity share.
- Subscription occurred on July 22, 2026, one day ahead of the public issue opening for other investor categories, as per standard anchor investor timelines.
- The notice was signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation under SEBI ICDR regulations governing public issues.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond noting the anchor allocation for the upcoming Lohia Corp Limited IPO.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor subscription/allocation date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor allocation at Rs. 425 per share provides a price reference point for the Lohia Corp Limited public issue and signals institutional investor interest ahead of the IPO opening to other categories of investors. This is a routine pre-listing disclosure with limited direct market impact beyond informing prospective IPO subscribers and existing market participants tracking the issue.
