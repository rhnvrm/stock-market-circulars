## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. New securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under ESM and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions starting July 20, 2026. Certain securities already under ESM will be moved down to Stage I, while others will exit the framework entirely per Annexure II. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin.
- Some existing ESM securities are being moved to a lower Stage I under the framework effective July 17, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 20, 2026.
- Securities newly shifting to Stage II will be under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective July 17, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) will have their price bands reinstated to pre-ESM levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ESM is based purely on market surveillance criteria and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice implements periodic review and reclassification of securities under the existing ESM framework established via earlier notices (20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on open positions (as of July 17, 2026) and new positions (from July 20, 2026) for securities newly added to ESM.
- Members must ensure affected securities are traded under Trade-for-Trade settlement rather than Rolling Settlement from the effective dates.
- Members must apply the revised 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism for securities moving to Stage II effective July 17, 2026.
- Queries should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 17, 2026**: Stage revisions (including move to Stage I or Stage II) and exclusions from ESM take effect; open positions as of this date are subject to new margin requirements.
- **July 20, 2026**: 100% minimum margin applies to new positions; affected securities shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

The ESM update significantly affects trading conditions for the listed securities by imposing steep margin requirements and shifting them to Trade-for-Trade settlement, which reduces liquidity and discourages speculative trading. Securities moving to Stage II face further restrictions with tighter price bands and periodic call auctions, limiting intraday price movement and trading flexibility. Conversely, securities exiting the framework will see restored price bands and improved trading conditions, potentially increasing their liquidity. Market participants holding positions in affected scrips should reassess margin requirements and settlement implications ahead of the effective dates.
