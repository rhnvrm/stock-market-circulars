## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MANCHERIAL REPALLEWADA ROAD Pvt Ltd, a debt-listed company, has informed the Exchange of a change in its corporate name to Mancherial Repallewada Road Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name for the company's listed debt securities on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from MANCHERIAL REPALLEWADA ROAD Pvt Ltd to Mancherial Repallewada Road Limited
- Two debt securities (Scrip Codes 976124 and 976125) are affected
- SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System will be updated for both securities
- Change is effective June 29, 2026
- Certificate of Incorporation is attached as supporting document

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's legal name change in BSE's BOLT Plus System records.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to update their systems and records to reflect the new name and updated SCRIP IDs for Scrip Codes 976124 and 976125 effective June 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 29, 2026
