## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Shadowfax Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544685, trading symbol SHADOWFAX, and ISIN number INE12UN01015. The exact date of listing and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Shadowfax Technologies Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544685
- Trading Symbol: SHADOWFAX
- ISIN Number: INE12UN01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code and symbol for Shadowfax Technologies Limited in preparation for trading commencement. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new trading instrument to the BSE equity segment. Market participants interested in the logistics technology sector should watch for the subsequent notice announcing the actual listing date and security details. The company's admission to trading will provide investors with access to Shadowfax Technologies Limited shares on the exchange platform.
