## Summary

BSE has updated its outbound call numbers for the BSE StAR Mutual Fund segment, implementing the TRAI-mandated 1600 series in compliance with the amended Telecom Commercial Communications Customer Preference Regulations (TCCCPR), 2018. Members should expect outbound calls from the new numbers with immediate effect.

## Key Points

- New outbound call numbers for BSE StAR Mutual Fund: **1600313680** and **1600113188**
- Change is effective immediately from the date of this notice (14 May 2026)
- Mandated by TRAI's amendment to TCCCPR, 2018 to curb Unsolicited Commercial Communication (UCC)
- Reference notice: Exchange Notice No. 20251027-18 dated 27 October 2025
- Helpdesk support continues on 022-45720450/650 & 022-69158550 on business days, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

The Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has amended the TCCCPR, 2018 to strengthen consumer protection against Unsolicited Commercial Communication. As a result, BSE has adopted the 1600 series for all outbound calls to members in the Mutual Fund segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their records to recognise **1600313680** and **1600113188** as legitimate BSE StAR Mutual Fund outbound call numbers.
- No action required beyond awareness; members should not block or reject these numbers.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 14 May 2026 (immediate effect)
- **Reference Notice Date:** 27 October 2025 (Notice No. 20251027-18)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational update. There is no effect on trading, settlements, or compliance obligations. The sole impact is on member communication: calls from BSE's Mutual Fund helpdesk will now originate from the 1600 series numbers instead of previous numbers. Members who whitelist or monitor inbound call sources should update their records accordingly.
