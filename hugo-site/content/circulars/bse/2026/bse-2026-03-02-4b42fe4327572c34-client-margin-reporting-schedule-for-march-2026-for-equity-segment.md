## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Segment for March 2026. The circular outlines the trade dates, corresponding last dates for margin reporting, and penalty dates applicable throughout the month.

## Key Points

- Issued under Notice No. 20260302-8 dated 02 March 2026.
- Covers the Equity Segment margin reporting obligations for March 2026.
- Each trade date has a specific last date for margin reporting (generally T+6 to T+8 trading days) and a subsequent penalty date.
- The schedule accounts for market holidays, with some penalty dates falling in April 2026.
- Signed by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a periodic operational notice reaffirming the existing margin reporting framework for the Equity Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All BSE members must report client margins for each trade date by the specified last date of margin reporting.
- Failure to report within the deadline will result in penalties effective from the penalty date.
- Members must ensure their back-office and reporting systems are aligned with the schedule below.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on BSE members and brokers who are required to report client margins for the Equity Segment. Non-compliance with the stated deadlines will attract financial penalties. The schedule extends into early April 2026 due to end-of-month trade dates, requiring members to plan their reporting cycles accordingly. There is no direct impact on stock prices or broader market participants.
