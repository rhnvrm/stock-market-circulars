## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited. The OFS allows existing shareholders to offer their shares for sale to the public through the stock exchange platform. This circular provides details about the bidding process, settlement procedures, and important dates for investors.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale opened for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited
- Trading facility provided through BSE platform
- Settlement calendar and procedures specified for the OFS
- Bidding process details outlined for retail and non-retail investors
- Price band and lot size information provided

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing SEBI guidelines for Offer for Sale transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must follow the specified bidding timelines and procedures
- Brokers must ensure proper client code modifications if required
- Settlement obligations must be met as per the calendar specified
- All trading members must comply with OFS mechanism guidelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: January 13, 2026
- OFS bidding dates and settlement schedule as specified in the detailed circular
- T+2 settlement cycle applicable as per standard BSE norms

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The OFS provides an opportunity for investors to acquire shares of East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited at a discovered price through the exchange mechanism. This impacts potential investors looking for investment opportunities.

**Liquidity Impact:** Positive - OFS transactions typically improve price discovery and provide liquidity to existing shareholders while allowing new investors to participate.

**Operational Impact:** Standard operational procedures apply for trading members facilitating OFS bids. Proper systems and processes must be in place to handle OFS transactions.

**Investor Impact:** Provides investment opportunity for retail and institutional investors to participate in the offering at market-determined prices.
