## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper securities issued by six companies on private placement basis on the BSE Debt Segment effective January 23, 2026. The securities include commercial papers from Infina Finance Private Limited, Muthoot Fincorp Limited, 360 ONE WAM Limited, Krazybee Services Ltd, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, and Time Technoplast Limited. All securities will be traded only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Six companies' commercial papers listed on BSE Debt Segment
- All securities trade in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Face value for all instruments: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Interest rates range from 7.5% to 10.45% per annum
- Redemption periods vary from April 2026 to January 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of privately placed commercial paper on the debt market segment with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional trading instruments for debt market participants but does not affect equity markets or require any operational changes beyond standard debt trading procedures. All securities carry high credit ratings (A1+ from various agencies), indicating low credit risk. Total value of listed instruments is approximately Rs. 527 crores across all six issuers.
