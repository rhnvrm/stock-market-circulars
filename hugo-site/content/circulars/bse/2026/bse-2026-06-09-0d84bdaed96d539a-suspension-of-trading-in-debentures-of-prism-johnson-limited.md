## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Prism Johnson Limited has fixed a record date of 18 June 2026 for the redemption of its 8.50% Non-Convertible Debentures on exercise of a call option and for payment of interest. Trading in these debentures is suspended with no dealings permitted from 18 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in Prism Johnson Limited's NCDs effective 18 June 2026
- Record date fixed at 18 June 2026 for redemption on exercise of call option and interest payment
- Debenture series: PJL-8.50%-5-7-28-PVT DR, Script Code 654/2026-2027
- ISIN: INE010A08149 (BSE Code: 975809)
- Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations framework for debt segment securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in Prism Johnson Limited debentures (ISIN: INE010A08149, Code: 975809) from 18 June 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems and alert clients holding this debenture series accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 09 June 2026
- **Record Date / No-Dealings Date:** 18 June 2026 — Redemption on exercise of call option and payment of interest

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a limited, targeted impact confined to holders and traders of Prism Johnson Limited's 8.50% NCD series maturing in 2028 (PJL-8.50%-5-7-28-PVT DR). The call option exercise indicates early redemption, meaning bondholders will receive principal and accrued interest as of the record date. Secondary market liquidity for this instrument ceases from 18 June 2026. Broader equity trading in Prism Johnson Limited is unaffected.
