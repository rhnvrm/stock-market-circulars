## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (PREMCAPM, Scrip Code: 511660). The offer window will be open from Friday, January 16, 2026 to Thursday, January 30, 2026. The acquirer is offering to buy up to 17,04,000 equity shares at Rs. 6.40 per share through the OTB mechanism on BSE.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (PREMCAPM)
- **ISIN**: INE555D01019, Scrip Code: 511660
- **Offer Size**: Up to 17,04,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.40 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: January 16-30, 2026
- **Bid Offering Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- **Last Day Take-up Extended**: Until 4:00 PM on January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard OTB process following existing BSE procedures for takeover offers as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and Custodian Members:**

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in BSE UCC database
2. Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. Access OTB module through iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
4. Use RTRMS for transaction management (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/)
5. Access Extranet for acceptance files after issue closure confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA
6. Note: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated

**Bidding Categories Available:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a standard takeover offer for a small-cap company with limited trading volumes. The offer is for a maximum of 17,04,000 shares at a fixed price of Rs. 6.40 per share.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members familiar with OTB procedures need to ensure their systems and investor UCC details are updated. The process follows established BSE protocols with standard session timings and platform accessibility.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to existing shareholders of Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd who hold shares in demat form and wish to tender their shares through the acquisition window. Shareholders must work with their trading members to participate.

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support related to iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet access, including Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
