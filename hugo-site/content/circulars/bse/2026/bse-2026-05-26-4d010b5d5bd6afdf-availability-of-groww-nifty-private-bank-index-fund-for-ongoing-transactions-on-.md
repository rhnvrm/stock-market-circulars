## Summary

BSE has informed members that the GROWW NIFTY PRIVATE BANK INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from May 27, 2026. This follows an earlier Notice No. 20260505-32 dated May 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 27, 2026
- Transactions available: subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans
- Scheme category is Equity for all variants
- Issued by VP and AVP of Mutual Fund division at BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice announcing the resumption of transaction availability for an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond noting the updated availability of this scheme for client transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: GROWW NIFTY PRIVATE BANK INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **May 05, 2026**: Original Notice No. 20260505-32 issued (referenced as the basis for this reopening)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in the GROWW NIFTY PRIVATE BANK INDEX FUND through the BSE StAR MF Platform. Distributors and investors who had been unable to place orders due to the suspension will now be able to resume subscriptions, redemptions, and SIPs in all six plan variants of this equity index fund.
