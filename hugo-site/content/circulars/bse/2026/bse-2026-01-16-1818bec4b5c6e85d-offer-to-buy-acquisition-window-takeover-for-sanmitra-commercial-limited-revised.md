## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Scrip Code: 512062, Symbol: ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014). The acquisition window is scheduled from Tuesday, January 6, 2026 to Tuesday, January 20, 2026. The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 15.00 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.00 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014, Scrip Code: 512062)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 15.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily from January 6-20, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM from January 6-19, 2026
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule notice for an existing takeover offer. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must note the revised schedule of activities
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Files for acceptance of bids will be available on Extranet after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- No End of Issue files will be generated if no securities are accepted

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: January 6, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Closing Date**: January 20, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 6-20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day for Give-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day for Take-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)
