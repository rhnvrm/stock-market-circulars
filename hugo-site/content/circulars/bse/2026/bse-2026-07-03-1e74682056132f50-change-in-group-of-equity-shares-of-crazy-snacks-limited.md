## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260702-44 dated July 02, 2026, informing trading members that the equity shares of Crazy Snacks Limited (Scrip Code: 544807) will be shifted from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544807 (Crazy Snacks Limited) is being moved out of the Trade for Trade (MT Group) segment.
- The stock will be dealt under the Rolling segment, specifically the M Group, going forward.
- This is a follow-up action referencing Notice No. 20260702-44 dated July 02, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational reclassification of the trading segment/group for a specific security under BSE's existing surveillance and settlement categorization framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in Crazy Snacks Limited shares must update their systems to reflect the change from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment) settlement rules effective the stated date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Segment Change: Friday, July 17, 2026
- Reference Notice: July 02, 2026 (Notice No. 20260702-44)

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates reduced regulatory restriction, as Trade for Trade requires compulsory delivery-based settlement without intraday trading. Moving to the Rolling (M Group) segment under normal settlement rules may improve liquidity and trading flexibility for Crazy Snacks Limited shares, benefiting existing shareholders and potential traders, though impact is limited to this single scrip.
