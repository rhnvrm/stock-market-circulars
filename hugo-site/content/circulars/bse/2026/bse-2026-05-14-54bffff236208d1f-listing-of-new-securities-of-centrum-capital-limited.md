## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,72,50,000 new equity shares of Centrum Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 501150, ISIN: INE660C01027) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, May 15, 2026. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 28.52/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- with a premium of Rs. 27.52/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants allotted on March 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,72,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed effective May 15, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 28.52/- per share (premium of Rs. 27.52/-)
- Issued to Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 459579195 to 486829194
- Date of allotment: March 27, 2026
- All 2,72,50,000 shares are under lock-in until November 21, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the new securities now available for trading from May 15, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 2,72,50,000 shares must be observed until November 21, 2027 — these shares cannot be sold or transferred by the promoters during this period

## Important Dates

- **March 27, 2026**: Date of allotment of new shares
- **May 15, 2026**: Effective date for listing and trading on BSE
- **November 21, 2027**: Lock-in expiry date for all 2,72,50,000 promoter shares

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 2.725 crore shares to Centrum Capital Limited's equity base, resulting in dilution for existing shareholders. However, since all newly listed shares are under a lock-in period until November 21, 2027, there is no immediate increase in tradable float. The preferential allotment at Rs. 28.52/- indicates promoter confidence in the company at that price level. Market impact is expected to be moderate, primarily reflected in the adjusted share capital and diluted EPS metrics.
