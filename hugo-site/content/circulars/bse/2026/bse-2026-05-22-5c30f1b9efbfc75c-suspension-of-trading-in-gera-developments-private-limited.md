## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Gera Developments Private Limited. The company has fixed a record date of 25 May 2026 for the purpose of payment of interest and redemption of the NCD.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the debentures of Gera Developments Private Limited effective from 25 May 2026
- The suspension is in connection with interest payment and full redemption of the NCD (ISIN: INE965R07330)
- Scrip Code: 975217; Scrip ID: GDPL-9.60%-11-6-26-PVT
- Settlement DR: 637/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance and Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in the NCD (Scrip Code: 975217, ISIN: INE965R07330) of Gera Developments Private Limited on and from the ex-date of 25 May 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 22 May 2026
- **Record Date:** 25 May 2026
- **Ex Date:** 25 May 2026
- **Settlement:** DR 637/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific 9.60% NCD issued by Gera Developments Private Limited (a private company). Trading in this debenture will be suspended from the ex-date to facilitate orderly processing of interest payment and redemption. NCD holders should ensure their holdings are correctly recorded as of the record date to receive the interest and redemption proceeds.
