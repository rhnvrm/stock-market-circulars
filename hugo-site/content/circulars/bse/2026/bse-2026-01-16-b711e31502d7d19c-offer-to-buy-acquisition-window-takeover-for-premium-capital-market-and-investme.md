## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (PREMCAPM). The offer is for up to 17,04,000 equity shares at Rs. 6.40 per share and will run from Friday, January 16, 2026 to Thursday, January 30, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (Symbol: PREMCAPM, Scrip Code: 511660)
- **ISIN**: INE555D01019
- **Offer Size**: Up to 17,04,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.40 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 16, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 16-30, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 16-29, 2026)
- **Last Day Take-up**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (January 30, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard takeover offer notice following existing OTB framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
- **Participation**: Only for investors having shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Use iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) or RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/)
- **Reference Circulars**: Members should refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- **Files**: Acceptance files on Extranet available after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date**: Friday, January 16, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date**: Thursday, January 30, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 16-30, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up Period**: January 16-29, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd. The offer size is relatively small (17.04 lakh shares at Rs. 6.40 per share, total consideration approximately Rs. 1.09 crore).

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard OTB process using existing BSE infrastructure and platforms. Trading members familiar with takeover procedures can participate without additional system changes.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Buyers (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs, Non-Institutional Buyers (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support regarding iBBS login, password resets, and operational queries through Trading Operations team (022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com).
