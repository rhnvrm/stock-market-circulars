## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Lodha Developers Ltd has informed the Exchange of reduced face values per debenture following partial redemption of two of its privately placed debentures. The revised face values will be applicable for trading with effect from June 2, 2026 under DR-642/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Two Lodha Developers Ltd debentures are undergoing partial redemption with revised (reduced) face values.
- Scrip **LDL-Reset Rate-21-01-28-PVT** (ISIN: INE670K07307, Code: 976923): Reduced face value set at **Rs. 79,443 per debenture**.
- Scrip **LDL-TBILLS-13-11-28-PVT** (ISIN: INE670K07349, Code: 977293): Reduced face value set at **Rs. 62,399 per debenture**.
- Both debentures are privately placed debt instruments listed on BSE's Debt segment.
- The notice is issued under DR-642/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a corporate action (partial redemption) resulting in a reduction of the face value of the specified debentures as communicated by the issuer to BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the updated reduced face values for both debenture scrips.
- All trades executed on or after June 2, 2026 in these debentures must reflect the new reduced face values.
- Members should update their systems accordingly to ensure accurate settlement and reporting.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal (face value) of both debentures. This is a routine debt corporate action with moderate impact on debenture holders, as the reduced face value changes the valuation basis for trading and settlement. Equity shareholders of Lodha Developers Ltd are not directly affected. Trading members dealing in these scrips must update reference data before market open on June 2, 2026 to avoid settlement discrepancies.
