## Summary

BSE has announced the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window under Takeover regulations for **PH CAPITAL LTD** (Symbol: PHCAP, Scrip Code: 500143, ISIN: INE160F01013). The offer window runs from **Tuesday, 19th May 2026 to Tuesday, 2nd June 2026**, with an offer price of **Rs. 206.66 per share** for up to **7,80,026 equity shares** (face value Rs. 10).

## Key Points

- Security: PH CAPITAL LTD | Symbol: PHCAP | ISIN: INE160F01013 | Scrip Code: 500143
- Offer size: Up to 7,80,026 equity shares at Rs. 206.66 per share
- OTB session timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily; final day (2nd June 2026) Take-up closes at 4:00 p.m.
- Bid offering period: 19th May 2026 to 2nd June 2026
- Give-up period: 19th May 2026 to 1st June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- Eligible investor categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NOH, CO, and IND
- Platform: iBBS (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS for bid submission
- Extranet files for acceptance will be available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes an existing OTB/Takeover framework under SEBI regulations. Members are directed to refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and investor category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in **demat form**.
- Members and Custodians must note that End of Issue extranet files will not be generated if no securities are accepted.
- For Leased Line access configuration, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| OTB Opening Date | Tuesday, 19th May 2026 |
| OTB Closing Date | Tuesday, 2nd June 2026 |
| Give-up Period End | Sunday, 1st June 2026 (3:30 p.m.) |
| Take-up Final Session | Tuesday, 2nd June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.) |

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly affects **Trading Members and Custodian Members** of BSE who facilitate bids on behalf of clients holding shares in PH CAPITAL LTD. The offer is limited to up to 7,80,026 shares at Rs. 206.66, making the total offer size approximately Rs. 16.11 crore. Impact is moderate and operationally specific — members need to verify UCC compliance, ensure demat holdings, and use the designated iBBS/RTRMS platforms within the stipulated session timings. Broader market impact is low given the size and single-stock scope of the takeover offer.
