## Summary

BSE has listed 14,00,000 new equity shares of MARG TECHNO PROJECTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540254) issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, February 11, 2026. The shares were allotted on December 12, 2025 at an issue price of Rs. 50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with premium of Rs. 40/-) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 14,00,000 equity shares listed on BSE
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 50/- per share (including premium of Rs. 40/-)
- Distribution Numbers: 12800001 to 14200000
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE245H01018
- Date of Allotment: December 12, 2025
- Trading commences: February 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the listing and permitted trading of these new securities. The lock-in requirements must be adhered to:

- 8,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 12800001-13650000): Lock-in until August 31, 2027
- 5,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 13650001-14200000): Lock-in until August 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date (Partial)**: August 31, 2026 (for 5,50,000 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date (Remaining)**: August 31, 2027 (for 8,50,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment of shares in MARG TECHNO PROJECTS LIMITED. The impact is limited to this specific scrip with no broader market implications. The lock-in provisions ensure that a significant portion of shares (8,50,000) remain locked until August 2027, limiting immediate supply pressure. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distribution numbers.
