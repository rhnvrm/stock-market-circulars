## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 532626) has fixed 21 July 2026 as the Record Date for sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.5/- each to Rs.2/- each.

## Key Points

- Company: Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 532626)
- Corporate action: Sub-division (stock split) of existing equity shares from face value Rs.5/- each to Rs.2/- each
- Record Date: 21 July 2026
- Effective from (w.e.f.): 21 July 2026
- Reference: DR-676/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural notice implementing a corporate action already approved by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- The existing ISIN INE063E01053 (Rs.5/- paid-up) will not be valid for transactions on the Exchange on or after 21 July 2026.
- A new ISIN for the Rs.2/- paid-up shares will be communicated to the market via a separate notice.
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the sub-division and cease trading in the old ISIN from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Effective Date: 21 July 2026
- Notice Date: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The sub-division will increase the number of outstanding shares and proportionally reduce the per-share price and face value, improving liquidity and affordability for retail investors without changing the company's overall market capitalization. Trading members and depository participants must ensure timely ISIN transition to avoid settlement disruptions. Impact is specific to Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd shareholders and traders rather than the broader market.
