## Summary

BSE has notified members that DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND will be reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 07, 2026. This follows the previous Notice No. 20251218-12 which had temporarily restricted transactions.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND reopening for all transaction types on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct Plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes are categorized as Equity schemes
- Subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Transactions resume from January 07, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform operational update reversing a previous temporary restriction on transactions for this specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice for BSE members and investors about the availability of the fund for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 06, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 07, 2026 - DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND reopens for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a scheme-specific operational update affecting only investors and potential investors in DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND.

**Operational Impact:** Positive for DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND investors who can now resume normal investment and redemption activities including systematic plans. Trading members facilitating mutual fund transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes.

**Investor Impact:** Beneficial for existing and prospective investors who were waiting to transact in this index fund tracking the NIFTY 500 benchmark.
