## Summary

WHITEOAK CAPITAL MUTUAL FUND is launching a New Fund Offering (NFO) for WHITEOAK CAPITAL - CONSUMPTION OPPORTUNITIES FUND on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 20, 2026 to February 03, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of February 10, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options are available under the equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: January 20, 2026 to February 03, 2026
- Fund Name: WHITEOAK CAPITAL - CONSUMPTION OPPORTUNITIES FUND
- Two Plans Available: Regular Plan (ISIN: INF03VN01AE1) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF03VN01AD3)
- Scheme Category: Equity
- Tentative Allotment Date: February 10, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 09, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight on February 03, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt by deadline
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch announcement following existing BSE StAR MF Platform procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 09, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on February 03, 2026
- For switch transactions, funds under NFO scheme must be received before allotment date
- Cancelled transactions (CC with N flag) will be reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions will remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds payout or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: NFO Opening Date
- **February 03, 2026**: NFO Closing Date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **February 03, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **February 09, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **February 10, 2026**: Tentative Allotment Date

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific mutual fund NFO launch. The impact is primarily operational for:

- BSE StAR MF Platform members who facilitate mutual fund transactions
- Investors interested in consumption-focused equity funds
- Mutual fund distributors managing client NFO subscriptions

The announcement follows standard NFO procedures with clear timelines and operational requirements. No significant changes to existing processes or market structure.
