## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260612-10 dated June 12, 2026, announcing a change in the group classification of equity shares of Hexagon Nutrition Limited. Such group changes are administrative reclassifications by BSE that can affect trading settlement, surveillance, and eligibility for certain trading mechanisms.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260612-10 dated June 12, 2026
- Equity shares of Hexagon Nutrition Limited are subject to a group reclassification
- Administered by the DCS-Listing department under the Equity segment
- No attachment or additional documentation provided with the notice

## Regulatory Changes

The group designation of Hexagon Nutrition Limited's equity shares has been revised by BSE. BSE classifies listed equities into groups (e.g., A, B, T, X, Z) based on criteria such as market capitalisation, trading volume, compliance record, and corporate governance standards. A change in group can move a security into or out of trade-for-trade (T-group) settlement or other surveillance-linked categories.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification for Hexagon Nutrition Limited.
- Investors and brokers should note any resulting change in settlement type (e.g., rolling settlement vs. trade-for-trade) that may apply post-reclassification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 12, 2026
- Effective date of group change: Not explicitly stated; typically the next trading day following the notice unless otherwise specified by BSE.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading mechanics for Hexagon Nutrition Limited shares. If the reclassification moves the stock into a trade-for-trade or surveillance group, intraday trading benefits (netting) would be removed and mandatory delivery would apply, potentially reducing liquidity. Conversely, an upgrade in group could improve liquidity and broaden eligible investor base. The change does not affect the company's operations or financial standing directly.
