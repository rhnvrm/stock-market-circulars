## Summary

BSE has announced the revocation of suspension in trading of equity shares of Typhoon Holdings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512307). Trading will resume effective May 26, 2026, in the 'XT' group, pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for Typhoon Holdings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512307) will be revoked with effect from May 26, 2026.
- The scrip will be traded under the **'XT' group** upon resumption.
- The revocation is pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated January 30, 2026 (erstwhile SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/CMD/CIR/P/2020/12 dated January 22, 2020).
- A company profile (Annexure I) is attached to the notice.
- The Information Memorandum will be available on BSE's website under: Corporates → Corporate Filings → Information Memorandum → Revocation.

## Regulatory Changes

The revocation is governed by SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated January 30, 2026, which supersedes the earlier SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/CMD/CIR/P/2020/12 dated January 22, 2020. Trading members should refer to BSE Notice No. 20120216-29 for details on the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs and Relisted Scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revocation and the applicable 'XT' group classification.
- Members must be aware of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) applicable to this relisted scrip on May 26, 2026.
- Trading members should review the Information Memorandum available on BSE's website prior to trading.

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **May 26, 2026**: Effective date for revocation of suspension; trading resumes in 'XT' group.
- **May 26, 2026**: Special Pre-open Session (Relist session) for the scrip.

## Impact Assessment

The revocation allows investors to resume trading in Typhoon Holdings Ltd. equity shares after a period of suspension. The scrip will be placed in the 'XT' group, which typically indicates additional surveillance or specific trading conditions for relisted securities. The Special Pre-open Session on the effective date may see heightened activity as market participants react to the resumption. Impact is limited to investors and trading members with exposure to or interest in this specific scrip.
