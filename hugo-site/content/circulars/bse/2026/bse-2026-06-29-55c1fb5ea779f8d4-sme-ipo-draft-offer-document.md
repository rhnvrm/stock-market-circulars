## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Quessentials Limited, filed on June 25, 2026, on the BSE SME website in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of this notice (June 29, 2026).

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document of Quessentials Limited was filed on June 25, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for a mandatory public comment period of at least 21 days.
- Stakeholders and public are invited to submit comments on the draft offer document.
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from June 29, 2026 (deadline: approximately July 20, 2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- The draft offer document is made publicly available per ICDR Regulations requirements.
- Any person wishing to submit public comments on the DRHP of Quessentials Limited must do so within 21 days of this notice via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is of limited market-wide impact. It is relevant to prospective investors and analysts interested in Quessentials Limited's upcoming SME IPO. The 21-day public comment window is a standard regulatory requirement before the company can proceed with the IPO process.
