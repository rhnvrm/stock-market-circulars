## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer (acquisition window/takeover) for Fabino Enterprises Limited. The acquirers — R G Family Trust, Sameer Gupta, and Sundeep Gupta — are offering to purchase up to 5,46,000 fully paid-up equity shares representing 26% of the target company's equity share capital at Rs. 20 per share, in compliance with SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirers: R G Family Trust (Acquirer No. 1), Sameer Gupta (Acquirer No. 2), and Sundeep Gupta (Acquirer No. 3)
- Target Company: Fabino Enterprises Limited
- Offer Price: Rs. 20/- per fully paid-up equity share (face value Rs. 10/-)
- Offer Size: Up to 5,46,000 equity shares (26% of equity share capital)
- Offer Period: June 29, 2026 to July 10, 2026
- Market Lot: 3,000 equity shares
- Offer made under Regulation 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011
- Letter of Offer and Pre-Offer Advertisement attached

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under the existing mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges pursuant to tender-offers under Takeover regulations, as per SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), along with BSE revised guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate tendering of shares by eligible public shareholders during the offer window.
- In case of oversubscription, acceptance will be on a proportionate basis ensuring no non-marketable lots result.
- Minimum acquisition per public shareholder shall not be less than the minimum marketable lot of 3,000 equity shares.
- Details of the Offer to Buy are available on the BSE website.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 25, 2026
- Offer Opening Date: June 29, 2026
- Offer Closing Date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer directly impacts public shareholders of Fabino Enterprises Limited, providing them an exit opportunity at Rs. 20 per share. The offer covers 26% of the equity share capital, which is a significant portion. Shareholders holding lots of 3,000 or more shares can participate. If the offer is oversubscribed, allotment will be proportionate. Trading members must ensure their clients are informed and capable of tendering shares through the BSE acquisition window mechanism within the specified dates.
