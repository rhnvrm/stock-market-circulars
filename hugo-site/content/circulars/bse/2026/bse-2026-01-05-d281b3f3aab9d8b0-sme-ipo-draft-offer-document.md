## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED on its SME website for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 05, 2026
- Document will remain available on the website for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED
- **January 05, 2026**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **January 26, 2026**: Last date for submission of public comments (21 days from notice date)

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to the proposed SME IPO of WAGONS LEARNING LIMITED.
