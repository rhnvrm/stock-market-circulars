## Summary

BSE has notified members that Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund will launch the **Apex Hybrid Long-Short Fund (SIF)** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from **March 6, 2026 to March 18, 2026**. This is a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) offering six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options. This notice references earlier circular 20260304-55 regarding the introduction of SIFs on the platform.

## Key Points

- NFO period: March 6, 2026 to March 18, 2026
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment options)
- All schemes are categorized as **Equity** under Specialized Investment Funds (SIF)
- Tentative allotment date: **March 25, 2026**
- Clear funds must be in ICCL Account by **6:00 PM on March 25, 2026**
- NFO extended till midnight on March 18, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions where clients have not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled with CC N-flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions (with initiated payment) remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt by 6:00 PM on March 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes the SIF framework previously introduced via notice 20260304-55, extending it to Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund's Apex Hybrid Long-Short Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- **BSE Members** must inform clients of the NFO window and fund initiation deadlines
- Client funds must be initiated before **9:00 AM on T+1**; failure results in transaction cancellation and CC N-flag reporting to RTA/AMC
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by **6:00 PM on March 25, 2026** for allotment eligibility
- Members must ensure Cheque and OTM payment timelines align with the 6:00 PM March 25, 2026 deadline

## Important Dates

- **March 6, 2026**: NFO opens
- **March 18, 2026**: NFO closes (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF)
- **March 25, 2026**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds deadline at 6:00 PM in ICCL Account
- **T+1 at 9:00 AM**: Deadline for clients to initiate funds, else transaction is cancelled
- **T+5 or 3:00 PM on last payout day** (whichever earlier): Pending transactions with initiated payment remain active until this time

## Impact Assessment

This circular is of **medium impact** for mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform. It introduces a new hybrid long-short equity SIF from a major AMC (Aditya Birla Sun Life), which may attract interest from sophisticated investors seeking alternative strategy funds. Platform members need to operationally manage the strict fund-initiation and clear-funds deadlines to avoid transaction cancellations. No broader market or regulatory impact is expected beyond the specific NFO participants.
