## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bijoy Hans Ltd (Scrip Code: 524723) has informed the Exchange of a change in the company's name to Arvaya Healthcare Limited. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: Bijoy Hans Ltd
- New Name: Arvaya Healthcare Limited
- Scrip Code: 524723 (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: ARVAYA
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: ARVAYA
- Attachment: Certificate of Incorporation (COI) confirming the name change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update reflecting the company's approved change of name, evidenced by the Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal systems to reflect the new Scrip ID (ARVAYA) and company name (Arvaya Healthcare Limited) for Scrip Code 524723 effective the change date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the company's name and associated trading identifiers (Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name). There is no change to the scrip code, shareholding structure, or underlying business operations. Trading members and investors should note the new identifiers to avoid confusion when trading or tracking the security post-effective date.
