## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 11,00,000 new equity shares of Nutricircle Limited (Scrip Code: 530219) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, April 28, 2026. These shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) to the promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants, allotted on February 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- 11,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to promoters via warrant conversion
- Shares listed and tradeable from April 28, 2026
- ISIN: INE536C01029
- Distribution numbers: 10000001 to 11100000
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Entire allotment of 11,00,000 shares is subject to lock-in until November 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new share listing from April 28, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on the allotted shares (dist. nos. 10000001–11100000) must be observed until November 30, 2027
- No action required from retail investors beyond awareness of the increased share count

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 13, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** April 28, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 11,00,000 shares to the tradeable universe of Nutricircle Limited (Scrip Code: 530219), representing a promoter preferential allotment. Since all newly listed shares are locked in until November 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or selling pressure from this allotment. Market impact is minimal and confined to this scrip. Existing shareholders should note the dilution in equity base.
