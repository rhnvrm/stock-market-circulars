## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE403Q07EN6
- Scrip Code: 939637
- Series: KFL-6-2-26-NCD
- Record Date: January 22, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- No Dealings From: January 22, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No dealings should be executed in scrip code 939637 (ISIN: INE403Q07EN6) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension has limited market impact as it affects only specific non-convertible debentures of Kosamattam Finance Limited in the debt segment. Debenture holders on record as of January 22, 2026 will receive redemption proceeds. Trading members must ensure compliance to avoid regulatory issues. The suspension is temporary and directly related to the redemption corporate action.
