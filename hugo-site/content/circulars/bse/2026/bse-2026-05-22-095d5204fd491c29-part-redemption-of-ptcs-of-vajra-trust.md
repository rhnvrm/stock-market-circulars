## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Axis Trustee Services Limited has notified the exchange of reduced face values arising from partial redemption of Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) across 10 series under multiple Vajra Trusts (007 through 016). The PTCs will trade at the revised reduced face values effective May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 PTC series across Vajra Trusts 007, 008, 009, 010, 011, 012, 014, 015, and 016 are undergoing partial redemption
- Axis Trustee Services Limited is the trustee communicating the reduced face values
- All revised face values take effect from May 25, 2026 (DR-637/2026-2027)
- PTCs span coupon rates of 9.25%, 9.50%, and 9.75% with maturities ranging from 2027 to 2034

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard partial redemption event under existing debt listing regulations, resulting in updated face values for the listed PTC instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the revised reduced face values and ensure all transactions in these PTC scrips from May 25, 2026 onwards reflect the updated face values as listed below.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date for Revised Face Values:** May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment
