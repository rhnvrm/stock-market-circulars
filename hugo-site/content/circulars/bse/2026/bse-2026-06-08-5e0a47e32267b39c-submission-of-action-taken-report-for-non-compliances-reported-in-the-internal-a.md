## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to all Trading Members regarding the submission of Action Taken Reports (ATR) for non-compliances reported in the Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026. The due date for ATR submission is July 31, 2026. The notice reiterates procedural requirements, penalty provisions, and a detailed electronic filing process.

## Key Points

- Trading Members must close all non-compliances reported in the Internal Audit Report within 2 months from the end of the due date for Internal Audit Report submission.
- For the half year ended March 31, 2026, the Action Taken Report must be submitted by **July 31, 2026**.
- The ATR must be certified by an Internal Auditor empanelled with the Exchange and must cover at least one month of sample verification.
- ATR submission is only considered complete after the Member submits via BEFS (BSE Electronic Filing System) and receives an acknowledgment email; saved or auditor-submitted drafts are not final.
- Prescribed monetary penalties and disciplinary actions per Exchange Notice No. 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026 will be initiated for non-submission or unresolved observations.
- Critical non-compliances (shortfall of client funds/securities/commodities/networth, incorrect networth reporting, offering fixed/assured returns, mobilizing deposits) require immediate action without waiting for ATR submission.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements the framework prescribed under Exchange Notice No. 20251202-5 dated December 02, 2025 on "Ease of Doing Compliance - Submission of Action Taken Report."

## Compliance Requirements

- All Trading Members must submit the Action Taken Report electronically through BEFS by July 31, 2026.
- ATR must be certified by an Exchange-empanelled Internal Auditor confirming compliance status.
- Members must bring these provisions to the attention of their respective internal auditors.
- In cases of critical non-compliances, immediate remedial action is required under Exchange Notice No. 20260417-45, independent of ATR timelines.

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2026**: Deadline for submission of Action Taken Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026.
- **December 02, 2025**: Original framework circular (Exchange Notice No. 20251202-5).
- **April 17, 2026**: Penalty/disciplinary action framework circular (Exchange Notice No. 20260417-45).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has medium operational impact on Trading Members, requiring coordination between compliance teams and Exchange-empanelled internal auditors to prepare and submit ATRs through BEFS before the July 31 deadline. Failure to comply risks monetary penalties and disciplinary action. Members with critical non-compliances (client fund/securities shortfalls) face immediate regulatory action irrespective of ATR timelines.
