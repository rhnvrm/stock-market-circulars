## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 11,50,000 new equity shares of Novelix Pharmaceuticals Limited (Scrip Code: 536565) have been listed and are permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026. The shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 11,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- each (issue price Rs. 20/-) to Promoters on preferential basis
- Shares arise from conversion of warrants allotted to Promoters
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE314I01036
- Distribution Numbers: 19935001 to 21085000
- Date of Allotment: 30 March 2026
- All 11,50,000 new shares are under lock-in until 30 November 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities following conversion of warrants under SEBI preferential allotment guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their lock-in status. The entire 11,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 19935001–21085000) are locked in until 30 November 2027 and cannot be traded until that date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 30 March 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** Monday, 25 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 30 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 11,50,000 shares to Novelix Pharmaceuticals' equity base (Scrip Code 536565), representing a capital increase from warrant conversion at Rs. 20/- per share. Since all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until November 2027, there is no immediate addition to the tradable float, minimising short-term market impact. The preferential allotment to Promoters may indicate continued promoter confidence in the company.
