## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has issued the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment covering the period from 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260605-5).

## Key Points

- Covers the full trading and settlement schedule for the SLB segment for July 2026.
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category.
- The detailed programme is available as attachment SLB072026 in the linked PDF.
- Applicable to all participants in the BSE SLB segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule notification.

## Compliance Requirements

SLB segment participants — borrowers, lenders, and clearing members — should refer to the attached schedule (SLB072026) to plan lending, borrowing, and settlement activities in accordance with the prescribed dates for July 2026.

## Important Dates

- Effective period: 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- Notice date: 05 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This is a standard monthly calendar notice for SLB participants. No changes to existing rules or margin requirements are indicated. Participants should update their internal systems with the July 2026 settlement dates as provided in the attached schedule.
