## Summary

BSE has notified market participants of the opening of an open offer under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for SJ Corporation Limited. Four acquirers — Mr. Pintu Kanjibhai Kalavadia, Mr. Prashant Kanjibhai Kalavadia, Mr. Umang Kantilal Savani, and Mr. Kalpesh Patel — are jointly making the offer to public shareholders to acquire up to 1,12,72,300 equity shares (face value Rs. 1.00 each), representing 26.00% of the Expanded Voting Share Capital of the Target Company, at a price of Rs. 12.00 per share, payable in cash.

## Key Points

- Offer made pursuant to Regulations 3(1) and 4 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, involving substantial acquisition of equity shares and voting share capital along with a change in control and management of the Target Company.
- Offer price: Rs. 12.00 per equity share, payable in cash.
- Maximum shares to be acquired: 1,12,72,300 equity shares (26.00% of Expanded Voting Share Capital).
- Mechanism follows SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 9, 2016), as further revised by BSE notices dated Feb 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; Nov 2, 2020; and Aug 25, 2021.
- Letter of Offer has been made available to trading members and custodians.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements the existing SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 and the Revised Guidelines for the Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, Buy Back and Delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and custodians must process the tender offer through the Acquisition Window mechanism in accordance with the Revised Guidelines, referring to the attached Letter of Offer for procedural details and eligibility criteria for tendering shares.

## Important Dates

- Offer opens: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Offer closes: Friday, July 24, 2026
- Notice date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The open offer affects public shareholders of SJ Corporation Limited who may choose to tender shares at the offer price of Rs. 12.00 per share, and results in a change of control and management of the company. Trading members and custodians need to ensure operational readiness to support tendering through the Acquisition Window during the offer period. Broader market impact is limited given the company's scale.
