## Summary

BSE has informed its members that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 04, 2026. This notice is a follow-up to an earlier notice dated February 12, 2026 (No. 20260212-36).

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct Plans are available across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are enabled along with regular subscription and redemption.
- Six scheme variants are available spanning Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Distributors and members using BSE StAR MF Platform may now facilitate transactions in the listed OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 — Schemes reopened for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260212-36 (February 12, 2026).

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard platform availability notice enabling investors to transact in OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND through the BSE StAR MF Platform. The six available schemes cover all major plan and option combinations:
