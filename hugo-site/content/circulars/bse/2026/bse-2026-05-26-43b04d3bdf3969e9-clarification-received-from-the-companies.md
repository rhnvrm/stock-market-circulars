## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260526-18 dated 26 May 2026 noting significant price/volume movement in six listed companies. The Exchange had previously written to these companies seeking relevant information for trading members and investors. Clarifications have been received from two companies, while four others have not yet responded.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in six scrips and sought clarification from the respective companies.
- Rain Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 500339) has submitted its reply to BSE.
- Gennex Laboratories Ltd (Scrip Code: 531739) has submitted its reply to BSE.
- Replies are still awaited from ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Gyan Developers & Builders Ltd (530141), and Cargotrans Maritime Ltd (543618).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard surveillance action under BSE's monitoring framework for unusual price/volume activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four companies that have not yet responded — ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd, Elango Industries Ltd, Gyan Developers & Builders Ltd, and Cargotrans Maritime Ltd — are expected to submit clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to take note of the clarifications filed by Rain Industries Ltd and Gennex Laboratories Ltd (attached to the circular).

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 26 May 2026
- Replies already received: Rain Industries Ltd and Gennex Laboratories Ltd (as of 26 May 2026)
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is of moderate relevance to investors holding positions in the six named scrips. The receipt of clarifications from Rain Industries and Gennex Laboratories may provide market participants with context for the recent price/volume anomalies. The continued absence of replies from the remaining four companies (ZR2 Bioenergy, Elango Industries, Gyan Developers & Builders, Cargotrans Maritime) may sustain uncertainty around those counters until responses are filed.
