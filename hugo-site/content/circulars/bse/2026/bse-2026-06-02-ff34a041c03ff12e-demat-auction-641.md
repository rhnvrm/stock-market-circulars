## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice for Demat Auction 641 under Rolling Settlement No. DR-641/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers on June 02, 2026 for the purchase of securities that were short-delivered in the respective settlement.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: June 02, 2026, 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM
- Auction matching session: June 02, 2026, 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports available: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports available: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in scheduled for June 03, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Offers are to be submitted through trading terminals
- Attachments include off641 and ARR 641 documents

## Regulatory Changes

Trading members are reminded that following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings by June 03, 2026
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted for punching errors
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the relevant departments

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), and report availability
- **June 03, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational auction for settlement shortfalls in the equity cash segment. The impact is limited to trading members and custodians with outstanding short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-641/2026-27. The removal of restrictions on offering auction securities in the same settlement as short-delivery (under the Uniform Membership structure) provides greater flexibility to clearing members.
