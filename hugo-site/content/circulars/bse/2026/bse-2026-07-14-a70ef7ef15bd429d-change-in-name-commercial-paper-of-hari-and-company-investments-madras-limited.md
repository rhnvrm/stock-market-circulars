## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260623-41 dated June 23, 2026, informing Trading Members that the Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name of a debt-listed Commercial Paper scrip in the BOLT Plus System will change effective July 15, 2026. The scrip, previously listed under Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited, will now reflect the name Vivriti Capital Limited.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 731975
- Existing Name: Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited
- New Name: Vivriti Capital Limited
- New Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: VCL220626
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: VCL220626
- This is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260623-41 dated June 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements a previously announced name change for the debt segment scrip identifiers in the BOLT Plus trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should update their internal systems and records to reflect the new Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name (VCL220626) for this debt security effective the change date.

## Important Dates

- Original Notice Date: June 23, 2026 (Notice No. 20260623-41)
- Current Notice Date: July 14, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update affecting only the identification of a debt-listed Commercial Paper scrip on the BOLT Plus System. There is no impact on equity markets or trading operations beyond ensuring correct scrip identification for the affected debt security going forward.
