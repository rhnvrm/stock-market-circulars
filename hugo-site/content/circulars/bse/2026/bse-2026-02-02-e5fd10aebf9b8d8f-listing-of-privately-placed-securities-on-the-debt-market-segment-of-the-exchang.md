## Summary

BSE has listed multiple commercial paper securities issued on private placement basis by six companies on the debt market segment, effective February 2, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise. The issuances include papers from Tata Power Co. Ltd (10,000 units), Navi Finserv Limited (800 units), Axis Finance Limited (4,000 units across two tranches), Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited (500 units), and Birla Group Holdings Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Six companies' commercial paper securities now listed on BSE debt segment
- All securities traded only in dematerialized form under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- All securities allotted on January 30, 2026
- Trading effective February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors with additional short-term debt investment options with varying maturity profiles (45 to 180 days) and interest rates (6.70% to 10% p.a.). The high credit ratings (primarily A1+) indicate low credit risk. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
