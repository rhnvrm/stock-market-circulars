## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of record dates fixed for redemption of Commercial Paper (CP) issued by 16 companies. Trading members are advised not to deal in the listed CP instruments as their respective record/ex dates approach, ahead of redemption settlement.

## Key Points

- 16 Commercial Paper instruments across various issuers are covered under this notice.
- Issuers include Standard Chartered Securities (India), Jio Credit Limited, Chennai Petroleum Corporation, Chalet Hotels, National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development, Bharat Petroleum Corporation, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management, ICICI Home Finance Company, Aditya Birla Housing Finance, Fedbank Financial Services, Grasim Industries, Navi Finserv, Tata Power, Bajaj Finance, Bajaj Financial Securities (two separate CPs), and Mirae Asset Financial Services (India).
- Trading members are directed not to deal in these Commercial Papers from their respective record/ex dates.
- Record dates range from 28 July 2026 to 6 August 2026, with settlement numbers spanning 681/2026-2027 to 688/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Corporate Actions/Listing Operations department for the Debt segment, implementing standard CP redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper ISINs on or after the respective record/ex dates listed for each instrument, to ensure clean settlement ahead of redemption.

## Important Dates

- 28 July 2026: Record/Ex date for Standard Chartered Securities (India) CP (INE472H14854) and Jio Credit Limited CP (INE282H14139)
- 29 July 2026: Record/Ex date for Chennai Petroleum Corporation CP (INE178A14HR8)
- 30 July 2026: Record/Ex date for Chalet Hotels Limited CP (INE427F14035)
- 3 August 2026: Record/Ex date for National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development CP (INE0KUG14025) and Bharat Petroleum Corporation CP (INE029A14BW6)
- 5 August 2026: Record/Ex date for 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management, ICICI Home Finance, Aditya Birla Housing Finance, Fedbank Financial Services, Grasim Industries, Navi Finserv, Tata Power, Bajaj Finance, and Bajaj Financial Securities CPs
- 6 August 2026: Record/Ex date for Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) CP and a second Bajaj Financial Securities CP

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains solely to the debt segment and specific Commercial Paper instruments nearing redemption. It affects institutional debt market participants and holders of these specific CPs, with no bearing on equity trading or broader market operations.
