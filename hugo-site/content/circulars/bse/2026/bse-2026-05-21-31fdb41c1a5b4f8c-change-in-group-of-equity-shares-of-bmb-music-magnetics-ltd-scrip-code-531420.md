## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of BMB Music & Magnetics Ltd (Scrip Code: 531420) will be reclassified and included under the 'X' group with effect from May 29, 2026. The 'X' group on BSE typically denotes securities that are under trade-to-trade settlement or have specific surveillance/compliance concerns.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of BMB Music & Magnetics Ltd (Scrip Code: 531420) will be moved to the 'X' group.
- The change is effective from May 29, 2026.
- Trading members with queries are directed to contact bse.revocation@bseindia.com.
- The notice was issued by the Listing Compliance department.

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is being reclassified from its current trading group to the 'X' group on BSE. The 'X' group is a trade-to-trade settlement group where each trade must result in delivery, and intraday squaring off of positions is not permitted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems and client communications to reflect the group change for Scrip Code 531420.
- Any clarifications should be directed to bse.revocation@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Moving to the 'X' group restricts intraday trading, requiring all trades to result in compulsory delivery. This typically reduces liquidity and increases the cost of trading for market participants. Investors and traders holding or planning to trade in BMB Music & Magnetics Ltd shares should be aware of the changed settlement obligations from May 29, 2026 onwards.
