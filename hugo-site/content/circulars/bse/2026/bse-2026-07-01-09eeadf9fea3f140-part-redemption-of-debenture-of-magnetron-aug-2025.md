## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that Mitcon Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd. has reported a reduced face value on partial redemption of Magnetron Aug 2025 Pass Through Certificates (PTCs). The revised face values will apply for trading effective July 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two ISINs under the MA2025-11.50%-28-07-28-PTC series are affected: INE2GPJ15012 (Scrip Code 977078) and INE2GPJ15020 (Scrip Code 977079).
- INE2GPJ15012 (977078): reduced face value set at Rs. 3792.12 per PTC following part-redemption.
- INE2GPJ15020 (977079): reduced face value set at Rs. 7051.02 per PTC following part-redemption.
- This action pertains to Pass Through Certificates (PTCs), a debt segment instrument, not equity shares.
- Reference: DR-663/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation introduced; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Corporate Actions/Debt segment listing operations process reflecting a partial redemption event reported by the debenture trustee.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to take note of the reduced face values and ensure these PTCs are traded at the revised (reduced) face value from the effective date. No further action is needed from listed companies or investors beyond awareness of the updated trading value.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 01, 2026
- Effective date for trading at reduced face value: July 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it is confined to two specific debt instruments (PTCs) in the debt segment. It primarily affects existing holders and traders of these particular PTC ISINs, who need to be aware of the revised face values for accurate valuation and trading from the effective date. No broader market or equity impact is expected.
