## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 97,53,750 new equity shares of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited (Scrip Code: 526443) have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 40/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value at a premium of Rs. 30/-).

## Key Points

- 97,53,750 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- (issue price Rs. 40/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 16965841 to 26719590
- ISIN: INE072B01027
- Trading permitted from March 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from the effective date. The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period until September 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 17, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 97,53,750 shares to the tradable float of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited, though all newly listed shares are under lock-in until September 30, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in freely tradable supply. Relevant only to existing shareholders and traders in this scrip.
