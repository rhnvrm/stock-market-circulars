## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Groww Mutual Fund will launch the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on May 29, 2026, and closes on June 12, 2026, with the tentative allotment date of June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two schemes are being offered: the regular plan and the Direct Plan, both under the EQUITY category.
- The NFO period runs from **May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026**; lumpsum purchases are accepted until midnight on June 12, 2026.
- Clients must initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without fund initiation will be cancelled and a CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in the ICCL account.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a new fund offer on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day for all transactions, including those executed during the extended NFO period.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by **6:00 PM on June 16, 2026** for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payment transactions are contingent on receipt of clear funds in the ICCL account by the same deadline.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| NFO Opens | May 29, 2026 |
| NFO Closes (Lumpsum on BSE StAR MF) | June 12, 2026 (midnight) |
| Clear Funds Deadline in ICCL Account | June 16, 2026, 6:00 PM |
| Tentative Allotment Date | June 19, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact. It is a standard operational communication directing trading members on how to handle transactions for the Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Momentum Quality 100 ETF NFO. Members active in mutual fund distribution on BSE StAR MF should note the funds initiation and ICCL clear funds deadlines to avoid transaction cancellations.
