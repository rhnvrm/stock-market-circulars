## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260312-8 informs trading members that five new commercial paper instruments issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective March 12, 2026. The issuers are Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, Reliance Retail Ventures Limited, Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, and 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited. All securities are to be traded in dematerialised form with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper instruments freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment effective March 12, 2026
- All securities trade in dematerialised form only; tick size is 1 paise per unit
- Face value for all instruments is Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Placement memorandum details available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Issued on allotment date of March 11, 2026
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's existing framework for privately placed debt instruments on the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- The tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all listed instruments
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE's debt portal for further details on each instrument
