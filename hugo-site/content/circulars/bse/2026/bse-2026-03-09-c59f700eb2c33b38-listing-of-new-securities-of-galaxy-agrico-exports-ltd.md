## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260309-26 informs trading members that 1,31,59,102 new equity shares of Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531911) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, March 10, 2026. These shares were issued on a rights basis at a premium.

## Key Points

- 1,31,59,102 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 25.87/- on rights basis
- ISIN: INE803L01016
- Distribution Numbers: 5000021 to 18159122
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of Allotment: March 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to enable trading in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 5, 2026
- Trading Commencement Date: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The listing adds new shares to the tradeable float of Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531911). Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will be able to trade their newly allotted shares from March 10, 2026. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, meaning no differential rights or preferential treatment.
