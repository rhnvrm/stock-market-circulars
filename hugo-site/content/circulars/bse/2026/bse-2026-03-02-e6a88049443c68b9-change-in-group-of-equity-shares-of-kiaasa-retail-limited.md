## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of KIAASA RETAIL LIMITED (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544711) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) with effect from Tuesday, March 17, 2026. This is a continuation of Notice No. 20260227-40 dated February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544711 (KIAASA RETAIL LIMITED - SME IPO)
- Transfer from: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Tuesday, March 17, 2026
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022 – 2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of KIAASA RETAIL LIMITED will move from Trade for Trade (T+1 compulsory delivery with no netting) settlement under MT Group to normal Rolling settlement under M Group. This transition is a standard post-SME-IPO process once the initial mandatory Trade for Trade period concludes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems and records to reflect the group change effective March 17, 2026.
- Members should inform their clients about the change in settlement type for this scrip.
- For queries or clarifications, members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at 022 – 2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, March 17, 2026 (migration from MT Group to M Group)
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260227-40 dated February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change has a low market impact and is a routine administrative transition for a newly listed SME IPO. Post the migration, KIAASA RETAIL LIMITED shares will trade under normal Rolling settlement (M Group), allowing netting of positions and standard T+1 settlement. Traders who previously had to maintain full delivery margins under Trade for Trade rules will benefit from the standard margining system applicable to M Group scrips.
