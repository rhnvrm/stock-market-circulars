## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by five companies - Adani Enterprises Ltd., Can Fin Homes Ltd., ICICI Home Finance Company Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, and Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. - have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 8 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Five fresh commercial paper listings admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment.
- Adani Enterprises Ltd.: ISIN INE423A14ZI9, Scrip ID AEL70726, 500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 7.20% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption on 25/09/2026, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- Can Fin Homes Ltd.: ISIN INE477A14EL4, Scrip ID CFHL70726, 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 6.30% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption on 22/09/2026, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- ICICI Home Finance Company Limited: ISIN INE071G14IB4, Scrip ID IHFC070726, 2,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 6.22% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption on 22/09/2026, IPA ICICI Bank Limited.
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited: ISIN INE01C314GI5, Scrip ID BFSL070726, 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 6.70% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption on 18/09/2026, IPA ICICI Bank Limited.
- Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd.: ISIN INE257A14AI4, Scrip ID BHEL8726, 15,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 6.65% p.a., allotted 08/07/2026 (redemption/IPA details not fully specified in extracted content).
- All securities will trade only in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department for debt segment admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the ISIN numbers, scrip codes, denomination (Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples), and tick size (1 paise) for trading these instruments. Queries can be directed to the BSE debt department at the listed contact numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: 08 July 2026.
- Adani Enterprises CP: allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 25/09/2026.
- Can Fin Homes CP: allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 22/09/2026.
- ICICI Home Finance CP: allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 22/09/2026.
- Bajaj Financial Securities CP: allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 18/09/2026.
- BHEL CP: allotted 08/07/2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notice for privately placed debt instruments (commercial papers) and has minimal impact on equity markets or broader trading activity. It is primarily relevant to institutional debt market participants and trading members dealing in the BSE Debt Segment.
