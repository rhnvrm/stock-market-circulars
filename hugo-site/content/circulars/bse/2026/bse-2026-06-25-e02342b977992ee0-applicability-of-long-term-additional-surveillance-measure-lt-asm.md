## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, placing securities under 100% margin requirements effective July 1, 2026, and lower price bands effective June 29, 2026. The notice also identifies securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages and securities exiting the framework. The detailed lists of affected securities are provided in the attached annexures.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from July 1, 2026 on all open positions as of June 30, 2026, and all new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities placed under higher stages of LT-ASM framework effective June 29, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable on certain securities effective June 29, 2026
- Some securities continuing in LT-ASM but transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective July 1, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework effective June 29, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting of securities is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued with reference to BSE notice no. 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) and subsequent notices, including the revised LT-ASM framework updates in notices 20181027-1, 20201204-56, 20240809-46, and 20240920-63. The framework now reflects the latest periodic review, with securities being added to, escalated within, or removed from the LT-ASM structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on open positions in LT-ASM securities from July 1, 2026
- Members must ensure compliance with the lower price band restrictions for applicable securities from June 29, 2026
- The LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges
- Clarifications may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages and lower price band become effective
- **June 29, 2026**: Securities exit from LT-ASM framework
- **June 30, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin
- **July 1, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions and new positions; transfer to T/XT/MT/TS Group for eligible securities

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM framework imposes significant trading restrictions on affected securities, including 100% upfront margin requirements that substantially increase the capital needed to trade these stocks. The lower price band limits downside price movement. These measures restrict liquidity and increase costs for traders holding or entering positions in LT-ASM securities. The framework is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the listed companies. Securities exiting the framework will have their previous price bands reinstated, subject to no other surveillance measures being applicable.
