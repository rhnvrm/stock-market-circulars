## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260609-34 informing trading members that certain securities (listed in the attached Annexure_GSM) will be moved to their respective higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective **June 10, 2026**. The move triggers progressively stricter surveillance actions depending on the GSM stage assigned to each security.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure_GSM will be moved to higher GSM stages from June 10, 2026.
- Four GSM stages are defined, each with escalating surveillance restrictions.
- Settlement for all affected securities will be on a **trade-to-trade (T2T) basis** — no netting off is permitted.
- Trading members are advised to exercise adequate caution while trading in these securities.
- Queries can be directed to [bse.surv@bseindia.com](mailto:bse.surv@bseindia.com).

## Regulatory Changes

This notice operates under the GSM Framework published on February 23, 2017, and subsequent amendments. The applicable surveillance actions by stage are:

| Stage | Surveillance Actions |
|-------|---------------------|
| **I** | Applicable margin rate of 100%; price band of 5% or lower as applicable. |
| **II** | Trade-for-trade with price band of 5% or lower; Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of **50% of trade value** to be deposited by buyers. |
| **III** | Trade-for-trade with price band of 5% or lower; trading permitted **once a week (every Monday/1st trading day)**; ASD of **100% of trade value** to be deposited by buyers. |
| **IV** | Trade-for-trade with price band of 5% or lower; trading permitted **once a week (every Monday/1st trading day)**; ASD of **100% of trade value** to be deposited by buyers; **no upward price movement** permitted. |

Reference notices: 20170223-44 (Feb 23, 2017), 20170303-29 (Mar 3, 2017), 20180720-55 (Jul 20, 2018), 20191129-23 (Nov 29, 2019), 20231117-63 (Nov 17, 2023).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** must ensure buyers in Stage II, III, and IV securities deposit the required ASD before or at the time of trade.
- **No netting off** is allowed; all trades settle on a gross/trade-to-trade basis.
- Members must take adequate precautions and communicate restrictions to their clients.
- Members should consult the GSM FAQ available at: [http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/graded_surveil_measure.aspx?expandable=6](http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/graded_surveil_measure.aspx?expandable=6)

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026** — Effective date for movement of securities to their respective higher GSM stages.

## Impact Assessment

Securities moved into higher GSM stages face significantly reduced liquidity and increased cost of trading. Stage III and IV securities are especially impacted, as trading is restricted to once a week with full ASD requirements. Stage IV securities additionally carry a no-upward-movement restriction, making them effectively inaccessible for speculative buying. Investors and traders holding or planning to trade in these securities should review the Annexure_GSM list and reassess their positions prior to June 10, 2026. The enforcement reflects BSE's ongoing effort to protect retail investors from abnormal price movements in fundamentally weak scrips.
