## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that new equity shares of Galactico Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542802) issued under a Rights Issue are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, February 27, 2026. A total of 3,42,72,605 equity shares of Re.1/- each were issued at a premium of Rs.0.75/- per share.

## Key Points

- 3,42,72,605 new equity shares of Re.1/- face value issued at a premium of Rs.0.75/- on a Rights Basis
- Scrip Code: 542802 | ISIN: INE906Y01028
- Distribution Numbers: 149011331 to 183283935
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from February 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed securities of Galactico Corporate Services Limited from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** February 27, 2026 (Friday)
- **Circular Date:** February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a company-specific listing event affecting Galactico Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542802). The addition of 3,42,72,605 shares to the tradeable float may marginally increase liquidity for the scrip. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will be able to trade their newly allotted shares from February 27, 2026. The pari-passu ranking ensures no differentiation between new and old shares.
