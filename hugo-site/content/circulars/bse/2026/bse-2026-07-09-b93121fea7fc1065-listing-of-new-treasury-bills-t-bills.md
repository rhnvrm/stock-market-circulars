## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G Group - Debt Instruments category, effective July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills will be listed effective July 10, 2026 in the G Group - Debt Instruments segment.
- Scrip Code 805238: ISIN IN002026X149, Symbol 91TB081026, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 08/10/2026 (91-day T-Bill).
- Scrip Code 805239: ISIN IN002026Y147, Symbol 182T070127, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 07/01/2027 (182-day T-Bill).
- Scrip Code 805240: ISIN IN002026Z144, Symbol 364TB08727, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 08/07/2027 (364-day T-Bill).
- These securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to their respective maturity/redemption dates (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notice for newly issued government Treasury Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip codes, ISINs, and symbols, and ensure trading in these instruments ceases two working days before each respective maturity date. Queries can be directed to the Exchange at 2272 8352/5753/8597.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: July 10, 2026
- Maturity date for 91TB081026: October 8, 2026
- Maturity date for 182T070127: January 7, 2027
- Maturity date for 364TB08727: July 8, 2027
- Trading in each T-Bill ceases 2 working days prior to its maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact, affecting only debt segment trading members who deal in government Treasury Bills. No impact on equity markets or listed companies.
