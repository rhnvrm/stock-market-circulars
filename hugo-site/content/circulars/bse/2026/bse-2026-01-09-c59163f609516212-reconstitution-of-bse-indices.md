## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the monthly reconstitution of BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO Indices, effective from the market opening on Monday, January 19, 2026. A total of 24 stocks will be dropped from these indices - 13 from BSE IPO Index and 11 from BSE SME IPO Index.

## Key Points

- Monthly reconstitution affects BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO Indices
- 13 stocks being removed from BSE IPO Index
- 11 stocks being removed from BSE SME IPO Index
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO Indices should adjust their portfolios accordingly
- Market participants should note the reconstitution for trading and investment strategies
- Changes reflected in corporate action files for automated processing

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 09, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 (market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium impact on affected stocks and index-tracking funds. Stocks being dropped may experience selling pressure from passive funds that track these indices. The reconstitution is part of regular index maintenance to ensure indices reflect recent IPO activity and maintain their intended composition. Index funds and ETFs tracking BSE IPO and SME IPO Indices will need to rebalance their holdings, potentially creating trading volume in the affected securities.
