## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited effective January 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its 9.85% debentures maturing on January 28, 2036. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE128M08037 (Code: 953370)
- Coupon Rate: 9.85%
- Maturity Date: January 28, 2036
- Security Type: Private Non-Convertible Debentures
- Notice Number: DR-796/2025-2026
- Issuer: L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any transactions in the specified debentures from January 12, 2026
- All market participants must observe the trading suspension from the no-dealings date
- Compliance mandatory for all BSE trading members dealing in debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 12, 2026
- **Original Maturity**: January 28, 2036

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects a single privately placed debenture issue. Holders of the debenture will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. Trading suspension is temporary and standard procedure for redemption-related corporate actions. No broader debt market implications expected. Impact confined to existing debenture holders and potential buyers who cannot trade during the suspension period.
