## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 23,00,00,000 (23 crore) equity shares of JSW Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 543994, ISIN: INE880J01026) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 1, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP).

## Key Points

- 23,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted to QIBs via QIP are now listed on BSE
- Effective trading date: Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 285/- per share
- Date of allotment: June 26, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 2100001568 to 2330001567

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following a completed Qualified Institutional Placement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new shares for trading purposes. No specific compliance action is required from members. For clarifications, members may contact Ms. Mayuri Visaria at Tel. 022 2272 8901.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 26, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 23 crore new shares via QIP increases the total share count of JSW Infrastructure Limited, which may have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. At the issue price of Rs. 285/- per share, the QIP raised approximately Rs. 6,555 crore. The new shares are fungible with existing shares and will trade normally from July 1, 2026. Trading members should update their records to reflect the expanded share capital.
