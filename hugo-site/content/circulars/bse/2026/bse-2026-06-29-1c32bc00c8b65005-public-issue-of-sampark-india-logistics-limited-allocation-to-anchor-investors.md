## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Sampark India Logistics Limited, a total of 9,18,400 equity shares were subscribed on June 29, 2026, at a price of Rs. 84 per equity share.

## Key Points

- 9,18,400 equity shares allotted to Anchor Investors in the Sampark India Logistics Limited IPO.
- Anchor allotment price fixed at Rs. 84 per equity share.
- Allotment took place on June 29, 2026.
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship Department (Notice No. 20260629-17).
- A copy of the Company's announcement dated June 29, 2026, is enclosed with the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in a public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required of trading members. This notice is for information and record purposes only.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Allocation Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it relates solely to the anchor investor allocation phase of the Sampark India Logistics Limited IPO. The anchor allotment at Rs. 84 per share establishes the issue price benchmark. Trading members are informed for reference; no action is required on their part.
