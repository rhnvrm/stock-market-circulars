## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544676) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, with dealings shifted to M Group, effective from Friday, January 30, 2026. This change follows the Exchange's previous notice dated January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544676
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, January 30, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260114-10
- Continuation of Notice: 20260113-25 dated January 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement basis. This represents a transition from restricted trading with compulsory delivery to regular market segment with standard settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment transfer and adjust their trading systems accordingly
- All dealings in the equity shares must be conducted under M Group from the effective date
- Trading Members requiring further details should contact Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** Tuesday, January 14, 2026
- **Previous Notice Date:** Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- **Effective Date of Transfer:** Friday, January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This group change from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment) will:

- **Increase Liquidity:** Moving to Rolling segment typically improves trading liquidity and market participation
- **Enhanced Trading Flexibility:** Investors will no longer be restricted to compulsory delivery-based trading
- **Improved Settlement:** Standard rolling settlement cycles will apply instead of Trade for Trade settlement
- **Positive Market Signal:** Transfer to Rolling segment often indicates improved compliance and stability post-SME IPO listing
- **Broker Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems and risk management parameters for the scrip
