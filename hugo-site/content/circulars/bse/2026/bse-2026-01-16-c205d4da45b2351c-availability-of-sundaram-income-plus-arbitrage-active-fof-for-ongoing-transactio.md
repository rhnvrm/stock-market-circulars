## Summary

BSE has announced that SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF (Fund of Funds) scheme will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 19, 2026. The scheme will also offer systematic investment facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan). This notice supersedes the previous Notice No. 20260102-20.

## Key Points

- SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF reopens for transactions from January 19, 2026
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Trading will be conducted on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Notice issued by VP - Mutual Fund Arunkumar Ganesan and AVP - Mutual Fund Mahesh Tare

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement regarding the reopening of a specific mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate impact for investors specifically interested in arbitrage-oriented fund of funds schemes. Existing and potential investors in Sundaram Mutual Fund schemes can now access this FOF scheme for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members need to enable this scheme for client transactions from January 19, 2026. The availability of SIP, STP, and SWP facilities provides flexibility for investors pursuing systematic investment strategies in arbitrage funds.
