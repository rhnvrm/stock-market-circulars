## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective indices. Bharat Coking Coal Limited (ticker 544678) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 21, 2026, following its listing on January 19, 2026. Defrail Technologies Limited (ticker 544677) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 20, 2026, following its SME platform listing on January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bharat Coking Coal Limited (544678) listed on BSE on January 19, 2026
- Addition to BSE IPO index effective at market open on January 21, 2026
- Defrail Technologies Limited (544677) listed on BSE SME platform on January 19, 2026
- Addition to BSE SME IPO index effective at market open on January 20, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference notices: 20260116-39 and 20260116-41

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard index constituent update following new IPO listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required. Market participants should note the index additions for portfolio rebalancing and index-tracking purposes. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Listing date for both Bharat Coking Coal Limited and Defrail Technologies Limited
- **January 20, 2026**: Defrail Technologies Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **January 21, 2026**: Bharat Coking Coal Limited added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions following new IPO listings. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to include these stocks in their portfolios. The additions reflect the ongoing activity in India's primary market with one mainboard IPO and one SME IPO.
