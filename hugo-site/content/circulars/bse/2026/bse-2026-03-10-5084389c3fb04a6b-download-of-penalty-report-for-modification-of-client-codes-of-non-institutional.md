## Summary

BSE has released the penalty report for client code modifications of non-institutional trades conducted during February 2026. The report has been made available in the EQUITY TRANSACTION FOLDER on the BSE Extranet under the filename PM0226.Clg. Trading members are advised to download and retain this file for future reference.

## Key Points

- Penalty report for client code modifications of non-institutional trades for February 2026 is now available
- File name: **PM0226.Clg**
- Available in the EQUITY TRANSACTION FOLDER on BSE Extranet
- Applicable to all segments
- Issued in accordance with Exchange Notice Nos. 20110729-24 (July 29, 2011) and 20110826-4 (August 26, 2011)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued pursuant to existing guidelines established under Exchange Notice Nos. 20110729-24 dated July 29, 2011 and 20110826-4 dated August 26, 2011 regarding penalties for client code modifications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must download the penalty report file (PM0226.Clg) from the EQUITY TRANSACTION FOLDER in Extranet
- Members are required to save the file for future reference
- Members who modified client codes for non-institutional trades during February 2026 should review their penalty details

## Important Dates

- **Report Period:** February 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Availability:** File uploaded on March 10, 2026 (today's Extranet upload)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine monthly compliance notice with limited market impact. Only trading members who carried out client code modifications for non-institutional trades during February 2026 are directly affected. Members should review penalties applicable to them and maintain records accordingly. For queries, members may contact BSE at 022-2272 8435 or 022-2272 5785, or email bse.reports@bseindia.com.
