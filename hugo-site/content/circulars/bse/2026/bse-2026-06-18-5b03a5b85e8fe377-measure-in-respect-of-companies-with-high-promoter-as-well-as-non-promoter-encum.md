## Summary

BSE has updated its encumbrance surveillance measure under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulation 2011, Reg. 28(3). New securities meeting the high encumbrance criteria are being added to the framework with a minimum 75% margin requirement effective June 23, 2026, while certain securities have been made eligible for exit from the framework effective June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I are newly included under the encumbrance measure and will attract a minimum **75% margin** in both Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The 75% margin applies to all open positions as on June 22, 2026, and all new positions created from June 23, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are eligible to exit the framework effective June 19, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the updated consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- This measure is in continuation of Exchange notice no. 20220131-43 dated January 31, 2022.
- The measure operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.

## Regulatory Changes

The framework applies Reg. 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulation 2011 to securities where both promoter and non-promoter encumbrance levels are high. The periodic review mechanism ensures securities can enter or exit the framework based on updated encumbrance disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure minimum 75% margin is collected for all positions in Annexure I securities in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- Members must apply the margin requirement to existing open positions as of June 22, 2026, and all new positions from June 23, 2026 onwards.
- The shortlisting of securities under this measure should **not** be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 18, 2026 | Notice issued |
| June 19, 2026 | Annexure II securities exit the encumbrance framework |
| June 22, 2026 | Reference date for existing open positions subject to new margin |
| June 23, 2026 | 75% margin requirement effective for Annexure I securities |

## Impact Assessment

The 75% minimum margin requirement is significantly higher than standard margin levels, increasing the cost of holding or taking positions in affected securities for both retail and institutional participants. This acts as a deterrent to excessive leveraged trading in stocks with high promoter/non-promoter encumbrance, which can signal elevated risk. Traders with existing positions in Annexure I securities must arrange additional margins before June 23, 2026 to avoid margin shortfall penalties. The simultaneous exit of Annexure II securities provides relief to traders holding those stocks.
