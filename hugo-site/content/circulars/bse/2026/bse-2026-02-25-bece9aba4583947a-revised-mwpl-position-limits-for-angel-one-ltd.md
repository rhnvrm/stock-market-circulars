## Summary

BSE, via Notice No. 20260225-40, has revised the Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) for stock derivatives contracts on Angel One Ltd (Scrip Code: 543235) following a stock split. The revision is issued under ICCL authority and is effective from February 26, 2026. This is a partial modification of circular no. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023.

## Key Points

- MWPL for Angel One Ltd has been revised across all participant categories due to a stock split
- Scrip Code: 543235 | Scrip: Angel One Ltd
- Market Wide Limit (total shares): 9,67,33,080
- Client / NRI / Scheme of Mutual Fund limit: 96,72,500
- TM Proprietary limit: 1,93,45,000
- TM (Proprietary + Client) / FPI (Category I & II) / MF limit: 2,90,17,500
- Issued by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on behalf of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

This circular partially modifies circular no. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023, and is issued in pursuance of notice No. 20251219-72 dated December 19, 2025. The revision adjusts position limits to reflect the increased share count resulting from the Angel One Ltd stock split.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members and Participants in the derivatives segment must adhere to the revised MWPL limits for Angel One Ltd
- Stock Brokers, Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) â€“ Category I & II, and Mutual Funds must ensure their positions remain within the newly specified limits
- Any existing positions exceeding revised limits must be reviewed and adjusted accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision directly impacts traders, FPIs, and mutual funds holding derivatives positions in Angel One Ltd. The adjusted limits reflect the higher share count post-split, maintaining proportional position caps. Participants must recalibrate their derivatives exposure to Angel One Ltd by market open on February 26, 2026.
