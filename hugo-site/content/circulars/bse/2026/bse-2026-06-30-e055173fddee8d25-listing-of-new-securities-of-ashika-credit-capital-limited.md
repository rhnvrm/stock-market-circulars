## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that 4,03,52,586 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 543766, ISIN: INE094B01013) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange effective Wednesday, July 01, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving the amalgamation of Ashika Commodities and Derivatives Private Limited (Transferor Company) with Ashika Global Securities Private Limited (AGSPL), and subsequently AGSPL with Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Amalgamated Company).

## Key Points

- 4,03,52,586 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are newly listed on BSE
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Ashika Credit Capital Limited
- ISIN: INE094B01013; Scrip Code: 543766
- Distribution Numbers: 44731146 – 85083731
- Date of allotment: May 28, 2026
- Trading permitted from July 01, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operationalises the listing outcome of a court/NCLT-approved Composite Scheme of Arrangement under applicable SEBI and Companies Act provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and brokers should update their systems to reflect the newly listed securities under Scrip Code 543766
- Trading members must ensure trades in these new shares are processed from July 01, 2026 onwards
- No additional compliance action is required from investors holding existing shares, as new shares rank pari-passu

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 28, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing & Trading:** July 01, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total share capital of Ashika Credit Capital Limited on BSE by 4,03,52,586 shares. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing equity, they carry identical rights and will trade under the same scrip code and ISIN. The amalgamation consolidates Ashika Commodities and Derivatives Pvt Ltd and Ashika Global Securities Pvt Ltd into Ashika Credit Capital Limited, potentially increasing the free float and liquidity of ACCL shares. Market impact is expected to be moderate, confined to existing shareholders of the constituent entities who receive these shares as consideration under the scheme.
