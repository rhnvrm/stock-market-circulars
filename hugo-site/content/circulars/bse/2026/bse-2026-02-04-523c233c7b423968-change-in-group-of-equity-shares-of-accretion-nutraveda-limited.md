## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544694), an SME IPO company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective February 18, 2026. This change follows the exchange's earlier notice dated February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544694
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Wednesday, February 18, 2026
- Notice references prior communication dated February 3, 2026 (Notice No. 20260203-44)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement. Trade for Trade segment typically requires full upfront payment and delivery, while Rolling segment allows for T+1/T+2 settlement cycles with margin trading facility.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the segment transfer for ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED
- Trading Members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications
- All dealings in the company's equity shares will be executed under M Group from the effective date

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 4, 2026
- Effective Date: February 18, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Prior Notice Date: February 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment indicates improved liquidity and regulatory compliance status for ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED. This change allows for:

- Enhanced trading flexibility for investors
- Access to margin trading facilities
- Standard settlement cycles instead of immediate delivery requirements
- Potential increase in trading volumes and market participation

The move suggests the company has met BSE's criteria for graduation from the more restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal rolling settlement, which is generally positive for market liquidity and investor accessibility.
