## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260407-21 informing trading members that the equity shares of Asian Hotels (West) Ltd. (Scrip Code: 533221) will be reclassified and included under the 'B' group with effect from April 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Asian Hotels (West) Ltd. (Scrip Code: 533221) are being moved to the 'B' group.
- The change is effective from April 09, 2026.
- Trading members with queries are directed to email bse.revocation@bseindia.com.
- Notice issued by the Listing Compliance department.

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is being reclassified into the 'B' group on BSE. The 'B' group on BSE typically comprises companies that do not meet the criteria for higher-tier groups, which may indicate changes in compliance status, trading volumes, or other listing-related metrics.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems and records to reflect the new group classification for Scrip Code 533221 effective April 09, 2026.
- Any queries regarding the reclassification should be directed to bse.revocation@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 07, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** April 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification of Asian Hotels (West) Ltd. to 'B' group may affect the trading dynamics of the scrip, as 'B' group securities are subject to different trading conditions compared to higher-tier groups. Investors and traders holding this scrip should be aware of the change, as it may influence liquidity, margin requirements, and overall market sentiment around the stock.
