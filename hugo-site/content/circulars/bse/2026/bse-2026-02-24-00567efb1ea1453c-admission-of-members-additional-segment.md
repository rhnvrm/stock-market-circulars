## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260224-3 informs trading members that four firms have been admitted as Trading Members (trading rights only) on BSE Ltd. under additional segments, effective 24 February 2026. The admissions are subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Four trading members admitted to additional segments on BSE Ltd.
- FRR Shares & Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 6207) admitted to the **Debt** segment.
- Microcurves Trading Private Limited (Member Code: 6868) admitted to the **Debt** segment.
- Wealth India Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 6521) admitted to the **Debt** segment.
- Composite Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 3145) admitted to the **Equity Derivatives** segment.
- All admissions confer trading rights only on BSE Ltd.
- Admissions are subject to Exchange Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice announcing new segment admissions for existing trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for existing trading members. Newly admitted members must operate in accordance with the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of BSE Ltd. for their respective segments. Members seeking clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 24 February 2026
- **Effective Date:** 24 February 2026 (implied from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine membership expansion notice. The addition of three members to the Debt segment and one to the Equity Derivatives segment marginally increases participation and liquidity potential in those segments but has no direct effect on securities prices, investor obligations, or broader market operations.
