## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 14,765 equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) arising from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each that were issued on a rights basis. Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities with effect from Thursday, February 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Fusion Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 543652
- Number of shares: 14,765 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 161351031 to 161365795
- Status: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice for additional securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date
- The shares rank pari-passu with the old equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 04, 2026
- Trading Commencement: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves a small number of shares (14,765) resulting from conversion of partly paid to fully paid shares from a rights issue. This is a routine corporate action with no material effect on the company's capital structure or trading operations. The shares will have the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares.
