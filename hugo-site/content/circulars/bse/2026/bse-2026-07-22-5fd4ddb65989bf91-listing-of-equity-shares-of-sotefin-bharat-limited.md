## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Sotefin Bharat Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course, under the SME segment. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Sotefin Bharat Limited
- Scrip Code: 544832
- Symbol: SOTEFIN
- ISIN: INE12Z301012
- Segment: SME
- Category: Company related, DCS-Listing department
- No attachments accompanied this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notification rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code and symbol once trading commences. No immediate action is required until the formal listing date notice is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice formalizes the upcoming admission of Sotefin Bharat Limited's equity shares to trading on BSE's SME platform. Impact is limited to market participants tracking new SME listings and the company's shareholders/investors awaiting tradability. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
