## Summary

Omnitech Engineering Limited (BSE ticker: 544720) was listed on BSE on March 05, 2026. Effective at market open on Monday, March 09, 2026, the stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index. This follows Notice No. 20260304-44 and is part of the standard post-listing index inclusion process.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited (ticker: 544720) listed on BSE on March 05, 2026
- Stock to be added to the BSE IPO Index effective March 09, 2026 (market open)
- Action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated March 05, 2026
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following a new listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index must update their portfolios to include OMNITECH ENGINEERING LIMITED (544720) effective March 09, 2026.
- Clients should refer to the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for system updates.

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Omnitech Engineering Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file published
- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544720 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of Omnitech Engineering Limited to the BSE IPO Index will require passive funds, ETFs, and index-linked products tracking the BSE IPO Index to include this stock in their portfolios from March 09, 2026. This may generate buying interest in the stock around the effective date. The impact is contained to index-tracking investment vehicles and the specific stock; broader market impact is minimal.
