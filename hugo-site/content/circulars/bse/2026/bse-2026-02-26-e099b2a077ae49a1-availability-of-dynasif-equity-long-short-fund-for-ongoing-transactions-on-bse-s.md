## Summary

BSE has informed its members that DYNASIF EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from February 27, 2026. This notice references an earlier Notice No. 20260205-30 and covers six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- DYNASIF EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 27, 2026
- Both subscription and redemption facilities are available
- SIP facility is also enabled alongside lump-sum transactions
- Six scheme variants are available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All six schemes fall under the Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update following the fund's NFO period, transitioning it to ongoing transaction availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond ensuring the fund is enabled for transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of DYNASIF EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND for ongoing subscription, redemption, and SIP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **Reference Notice**: 20260205-30 (original NFO notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine operational notice enabling existing investors to transact and new investors to subscribe to DYNASIF EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND through the BSE StAR MF Platform. The fund's availability across six ISIN variants (covering growth and income distribution options under both Regular and Direct plans) provides flexibility for distributors and investors transacting via BSE.
