## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (Notice No. 20260612-17) observed significant price/volume movement in 13 scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. Six companies have submitted replies, while seven companies' replies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance wrote to 13 listed companies regarding unusual price/volume activity in their scrips
- Replies have been received from 6 companies and are attached to the notice
- 7 companies have not yet responded and their replies remain awaited
- Notice is issued to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's price/volume movement monitoring framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet replied (Elango Industries, SRU Steels, Phaarmasia, Raunaq International, Amrapali Industries, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery, Blue Jet Healthcare) are expected to submit clarifications to BSE Surveillance
- Trading members and investors should review the attached company replies before transacting in the listed scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies
