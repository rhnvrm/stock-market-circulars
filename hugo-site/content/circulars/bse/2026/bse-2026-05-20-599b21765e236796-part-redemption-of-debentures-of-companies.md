## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that IKF Home Finance Limited and Ratnaafin Capital Private Limited have fixed record dates for part redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in these debentures will occur at reduced face values from the respective ex-dates.

## Key Points

- Two companies have fixed record dates for part redemption of NCDs under the Debt Segment.
- Trading members must note the reduced face values effective from the ex-dates.
- Both debentures are privately placed (PVT category).
- Settlement reference is 636/2026-2027 for both instruments.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a standard corporate action communication under BSE's Listing Operations framework for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in the affected debentures reflect the reduced face values from the respective ex-dates.
- Members should update their systems to account for the new reduced face values per debenture for both scrips.

## Important Dates

| Company | Scrip Code | ISIN | Record Date | Ex Date | Reduced Face Value |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| IKF Home Finance Limited | 975067 | INE02VP08022 | 24 May 2026 | 22 May 2026 | Rs. 8,333.37 per debenture |
| Ratnaafin Capital Private Limited | 977619 | INE0MJR07033 | 22 May 2026 | 22 May 2026 | Rs. 95,833 per debenture |

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily impacts holders of the specified NCD instruments and trading members active in the BSE Debt Segment. The reduced face values reflect partial principal repayment to debenture holders. Investors holding these instruments should expect adjusted market prices post ex-date. No broader market-wide impact is anticipated as these are privately placed debt instruments with limited trading activity.
