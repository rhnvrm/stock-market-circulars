## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-17 informing trading members that the equity shares of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544717
- Trading Symbol: CLEANMAX
- ISIN: INE647U01026
- Segment: Equity
- Listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural listing announcement under BSE's standard new-listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the forthcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required until the listing date and security details are confirmed in the subsequent notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium market impact as it signals the imminent debut of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited on BSE's equity segment. The company operates in the clean/renewable energy space, a sector of growing investor interest. Trading members and investors should watch for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and full security details before initiating any trading activity.
