## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that certain securities will be moved to higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective June 18, 2026. The securities are listed in the attached Annexure (Annexure_GSM). Affected securities will face progressively stricter surveillance actions depending on their assigned GSM stage (I through IV).

## Key Points

- Securities in the Annexure will move to their respective higher GSM stages from June 18, 2026.
- GSM Stage I: 100% applicable margin rate and price band of 5% or lower.
- GSM Stage II: Trade-for-trade settlement, 5% price band or lower, and Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value from buyers.
- GSM Stage III: Trade-for-trade, 5% price band, trading permitted once a week (every Monday/1st trading day), and ASD of 100% of trade value from buyers.
- GSM Stage IV: Same as Stage III with the additional restriction of no upward movement in price.
- No netting off is permitted; all settlements are on a trade-to-trade basis.
- FAQs and details available at the BSE GSM page.

## Regulatory Changes

The move is under the GSM Framework published on February 23, 2017, and subsequent amendments (notices dated March 3, 2017; July 20, 2018; November 29, 2019; and November 17, 2023). Affected securities are escalated to higher surveillance stages based on their trading and fundamental parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the securities listed in Annexure_GSM and apply the corresponding surveillance actions from June 18, 2026.
- Buyers in Stage II must deposit ASD of 50% of trade value; in Stages III and IV, ASD is 100% of trade value.
- No netting off is permitted in any GSM stage; all trades must be settled on a trade-to-trade basis.
- Members are advised to take adequate precautions while trading in these securities.
- For clarifications, members may refer to the FAQ at http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/graded_surveil_measure.aspx?expandable=6 or email bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 18, 2026**: GSM stage upgrades become effective for securities listed in the Annexure.

## Impact Assessment

Securities moved to higher GSM stages will face significantly reduced liquidity due to trade-for-trade settlement, restricted price bands (5% or lower), and weekly trading limitations (Stages III and IV). The mandatory ASD requirements (50%–100% of trade value) will increase the cost of trading for buyers, further dampening activity. Investors holding these securities may find it difficult to exit positions quickly, particularly under Stages III and IV where trading is limited to once a week with no upward price movement allowed at Stage IV. Trading members should inform clients holding or planning to trade in these securities about the new restrictions.
