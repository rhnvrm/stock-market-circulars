## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Oxyzo Financial Services Limited has fixed a Record Date for the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Following the redemption, trading in the debentures will continue but with a reduced face value effective March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Oxyzo Financial Services Limited
- Debenture Series: OFSL-9.75%-22-3-27
- ISIN: INE04VS07339 (Scrip Code: 975561)
- Record Date for Part Redemption: March 7, 2026
- Reduced Face Value post-redemption: Rs. 40,000 per debenture
- Effective Date for trading with reduced face value: March 6, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-834/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action under existing BSE debt market guidelines for part redemption of listed NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from March 6, 2026, debentures of Oxyzo Financial Services Limited (OFSL-9.75%-22-3-27) will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 40,000 per debenture.
- Members should update their systems and communicate this change to clients holding these debentures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Record Date (Part Redemption):** March 7, 2026
- **Effective Date (Reduced Face Value trading begins):** March 6, 2026
- **Settlement Reference:** DR-834/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. Only investors holding the specific NCD series (INE04VS07339 / scrip code 975561) are directly affected. Post-redemption, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value of Rs. 40,000 per unit. Trading members need to update their systems accordingly before the effective date of March 6, 2026.
