## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260617-34) announcing the opening of an Offer for Sale (OFS) for Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd, along with the live activities schedule for the offer. The notice is categorized under Trading Operations for the Equity segment.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale (OFS) is being opened for Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd
- A live activities schedule has been published by BSE Trading Operations department
- Notice falls under the Trading category, Equity segment
- No additional attachments were included with the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice governing the conduct of the OFS offer window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants (brokers, investors) intending to participate in the OFS must adhere to the live activities schedule published by BSE
- Trading members should follow the timelines and procedures outlined in the live activities schedule for submitting bids during the OFS window

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 17 June 2026
- OFS Opening: 17 June 2026 (as per the notice date and subject)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to investors and trading members wishing to participate in the Bosch Home Comfort India Ltd OFS. The live activities schedule provides the operational framework for the offer, including bid submission timelines. Impact is moderate and limited to participants in this specific OFS transaction.
