## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260223-7) informing trading members that the 91-day Treasury Bill with scrip code 805148 (ISIN: IN002025X356, particulars: 91TB260226) is set to mature on its redemption date. Accordingly, trading in this T-Bill is suspended effective February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- T-Bill scrip code 805148 (ISIN: IN002025X356, 91TB260226) will mature on its scheduled redemption date
- Trading suspension is effective from **February 24, 2026**
- Reference number: DR-827/2025-2026
- All trading members are advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the effective date onwards
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the normal maturity lifecycle of a Treasury Bill instrument listed on BSE's Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in scrip code 805148 (ISIN: IN002025X356) with effect from February 24, 2026
- Members should ensure no buy or sell orders are placed for this instrument on or after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** February 24, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the routine maturity and consequent trading suspension of a single 91-day Treasury Bill. Investors holding this instrument will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
