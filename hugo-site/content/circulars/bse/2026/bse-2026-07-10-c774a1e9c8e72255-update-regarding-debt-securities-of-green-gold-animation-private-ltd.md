## Summary

BSE has issued an update in continuation of its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260611-35 dated 11 Jun 2026, regarding the debt securities of Green Gold Animation Private Ltd. The scrip (Scrip Code 974917) will be available for trading with revised terms, including a new ISIN and an extended maturity date, effective Monday, July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974917, previously listed under ISIN INE0NZ807013, will trade under a revised ISIN with effect from July 13, 2026.
- The old ISIN INE0NZ807013 (Scrip Long Name: GAPL-7%-15-6-26-PVT) is being replaced with new ISIN INE05RZ07024 (Scrip Long Name: GAPL-7%-15-9-26-PVT).
- The maturity date has been extended from 15-06-2026 to 15-09-2026.
- This is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260611-35 dated 11 Jun 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an administrative/operational update reflecting revised terms (ISIN and maturity date) of an already listed debt security.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note the revised ISIN and maturity date details and update their systems/records accordingly ahead of the effective date. Queries can be directed to Ms. Bhairavi Bondale, Assistant Vice President Hardik Bhuta's office (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Original Notice: 11 Jun 2026 (Notice No. 20260611-35)
- Effective Date of Revised Terms: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Original Maturity Date: 15-06-2026
- Revised Maturity Date: 15-09-2026
- Notice Date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or trading this specific debt security. The maturity extension and ISIN change require trading members to update their systems to ensure accurate trade processing from July 13, 2026 onward. No broader market impact is expected.
