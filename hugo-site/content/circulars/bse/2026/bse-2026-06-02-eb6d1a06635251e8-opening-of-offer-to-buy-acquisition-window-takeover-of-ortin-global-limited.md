## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260602-36 announces the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) under the Takeover regulations for ORTIN GLOBAL LIMITED. Mr. Parveen Satija (Acquirer) has made an open offer to acquire up to 21,14,162 equity shares representing 26% of the fully paid-up equity and voting capital of the Target Company at Rs.14.65 per share in cash, pursuant to SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer:** Mr. Parveen Satija
- **Target Company:** ORTIN GLOBAL LIMITED
- **Offer Price:** Rs.14.65 per equity share (face value Rs.10.00 each), payable in cash
- **Shares Offered For:** Up to 21,14,162 fully paid-up equity shares
- **Stake Acquisition:** 26.00% of fully paid-up equity share capital and voting capital
- **Regulatory Basis:** Regulation 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 — offer for control over the Target Company
- **Offer Window:** Friday, June 5, 2026 to Thursday, June 18, 2026
- **Mechanism:** SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 09, 2016), along with BSE Revised Guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer follows the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, as revised through multiple BSE notices (20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must note and comply with the Offer to Buy mechanism as per SEBI and BSE Revised Guidelines.
- All transactions must be executed via the BSE Acquisition Window during the specified offer period.
- Participants should refer to the attached Letter of Offer for complete terms and conditions.
- Offer details are available on the BSE website.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Offer Opens:** Friday, June 5, 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Thursday, June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer will directly impact public shareholders of ORTIN GLOBAL LIMITED, who may tender their shares at Rs.14.65 per share — a cash offer for up to 26% of the company's equity. Trading Members and Custodians facilitating client participation must ensure compliance with BSE's Acquisition Window mechanism. The offer may affect the stock's trading volume and price discovery during the offer period (June 5–18, 2026).
