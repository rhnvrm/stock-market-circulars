## Summary

Skirpper Limited (Scrip Code: 538562) has forfeited 33,198 equity shares of Re. 1/- each that were partly paid-up, due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders. The forfeited shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 112904036 to 112937233.

## Key Points

- Company: Skipper Limited (Scrip Code: 538562)
- Number of shares forfeited: 33,198 equity shares
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Share status: Partly paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers: 112904036 to 112937233

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and to update their systems accordingly. No specific action required from trading members.

- Notice Number: 20260120-24

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture affects a relatively small number of shares (33,198) and is limited to shareholders who failed to pay allotment/call monies on partly paid-up shares. This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The forfeited shares may be reissued by the company as per applicable regulations. No material impact on the company's operations or trading activity is expected.
