## Summary

BSE has listed 18,01,801 new equity shares of JHS Svendgaard Laboratories Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532771, ISIN: INE544H01014), issued at Rs. 27.75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 17.75/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to the conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 16, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **18,01,801 new equity shares** listed on BSE effective June 16, 2026
- Issued to **non-promoters** on a preferential basis via **conversion of warrants**
- Issue price: **Rs. 27.75/-** per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 17.75/-)
- Distribution numbers: **85603967 to 87405767**
- Date of allotment: **February 1, 2026**
- All newly listed shares carry a **lock-in until December 31, 2026**
- Shares are ranking **pari-passu** with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational listing notice informing trading members of the availability of new securities for trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are now available for trading from June 16, 2026 onwards. No additional compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the new share count and distribution numbers.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 1, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 18 lakh new shares to the tradeable float of JHS Svendgaard Laboratories Ltd., though all are locked in until December 31, 2026, limiting any immediate dilution impact on the stock price. The warrant conversion at Rs. 27.75/- per share reflects the price at which non-promoter investors exercised their warrants. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in restriction and the relatively modest size of the issuance for this small-cap company.
