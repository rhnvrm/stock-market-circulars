## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 835 dated March 10, 2026. This auction involves securities of multiple listed companies that are subject to auction due to short delivery or related settlement obligations in the BSE settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 835 auction conducted on March 10, 2026
- A total of 64 scrips across various sectors are listed for auction
- Quantities range from 1 unit (e.g., AEGIS VOPAK, GE Vernova T) to 9,027 units (SVARAJ TRADING)
- Covers equities from sectors including pharma, infrastructure, IT, FMCG, chemicals, and financial services
- Notable large-quantity scrips include: SVARAJ TRADING (9,027), TATA CHEMICALS (2,000), DANUBE INDUSTRIES (227), GAGAN GASES (226), MAHARASHTRA (343), PREMIER SYNTHETICS (175)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine settlement auction notification under existing BSE auction framework for short deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/participants with outstanding short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of this auction
- Buyers of auctioned securities will receive shares or close-out as per BSE settlement rules
- Sellers failing to deliver will be subject to auction price-based close-out charges

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 10, 2026
- Settlement No.: 835
