## Summary

BSE has issued a revised live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) of P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (Security Symbol: PMTELELIN, Scrip Code: 513403, ISIN: INE092C01015). The offer period runs from Tuesday, January 13, 2026 to Wednesday, January 28, 2026, with an offer to acquire up to 26,19,500 equity shares at Rs. 6.81 per share. Trading members and custodian members are required to facilitate bidding through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (PMTELELIN), Scrip Code 513403, ISIN INE092C01015
- **Offer Size**: Up to 26,19,500 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.81 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 13-28, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 12-27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule circular. No new regulatory changes are introduced; the circular updates operational timings and procedures for the takeover offer in accordance with existing OTB framework referenced in Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Trading Members**: Can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- **Trading Members & Custodians**: Access OTB module through iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- **Trading Members & Custodians**: Use RTRMS platform for reporting (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- **Trading Members & Custodians**: Access Extranet for bid acceptance files (Internet: https://member.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://memberll.bseindia.com/)
- **Note**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- **Investor Categories**: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other), CO (Company), IND (Individual)

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 12-27, 2026
- **Last Day for Give-up**: January 28, 2026 (until 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day for Take-up**: January 28, 2026 (extended until 4:00 PM)
- **Bid Offering Hours**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (throughout offer period)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to shareholders of P M TELELINNKS LIMITED who wish to tender their shares in the acquisition window. The offer size of 26.19 lakh shares represents a specific takeover transaction.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and custodian members must ensure their systems and processes are aligned with the revised schedule. Members need to verify UCC registrations are current and facilitate bidding through designated BSE platforms (iBBS and RTRMS). The extended take-up window on the last day (until 4:00 PM vs. 3:30 PM) provides additional time for final transaction processing.

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support across Trading Operations and Listing Sales departments for any queries related to login credentials, password resets, and OTB module operations (contact numbers: 022-22728005, 022-22728376, 022-22728226, 022-22725626, 022-22728846, 022-22725046).
