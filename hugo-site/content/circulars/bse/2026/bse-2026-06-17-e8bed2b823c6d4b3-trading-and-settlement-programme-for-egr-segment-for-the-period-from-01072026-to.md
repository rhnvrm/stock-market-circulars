## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has published the trading and settlement programme for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment on BSE for the period 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. The calendar covers 23 settlement cycles (DR-067/2026-2027 through DR-089/2026-2027), each with a T+1 pay-in/pay-out cycle, along with corresponding auction settlement numbers.

## Key Points

- The programme covers settlement numbers DR-067/2026-2027 to DR-089/2026-2027 (depository numbers 2627067–2627089).
- All settlements follow a T+1 cycle: trading on day T, pay-in and pay-out on T+1.
- Pay-in deadline is 3:00 p.m.; members must submit instructions to depositories/banks by this time.
- Pay-out of funds and securities is effected by 4:00 p.m.
- Auction pay-in is at 9:00 a.m.; members must submit auction pay-in instructions by 8:50 a.m.
- Each settlement has a corresponding auction settlement number (RA-067/2026-2027 through RA-089/2026-2027).
- The final settlement (DR-089) trades on 31 July 2026 with pay-in/pay-out on 03 August 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational calendar notice issued by ICCL for the EGR segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- EGR segment members must adhere to the stated pay-in deadlines (3:00 p.m. for regular settlements; 8:50 a.m. for auction pay-in).
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to the relevant depositories and banks within the prescribed timelines to avoid settlement failures.

## Important Dates

- **First trading day covered:** 01 July 2026 (Settlement DR-067/2026-2027)
- **Last trading day covered:** 31 July 2026 (Settlement DR-089/2026-2027)
- **Last pay-out date in the period:** 03 August 2026
- **Regular pay-in deadline:** 3:00 p.m. on each pay-in date
- **Auction pay-in deadline:** 8:50 a.m. on each auction pay-in date

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard monthly operational circular relevant to brokers, clearing members, and custodians participating in BSE's EGR segment. The T+1 settlement cycle is consistent with the established framework. No changes to settlement norms are introduced. Participants should update their internal settlement calendars to reflect these dates for July 2026.
