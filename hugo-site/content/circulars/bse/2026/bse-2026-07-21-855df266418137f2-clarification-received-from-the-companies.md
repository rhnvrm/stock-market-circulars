## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 16 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. Replies have been received from 5 companies and are attached to the notice, while replies remain awaited from the other 11 companies.

## Key Points

- Exchange wrote to companies showing unusual price/volume activity to ensure trading members and investors have access to relevant information.
- Replies received from: Dhruva Capital Services Ltd (531237), J.G.Chemicals Ltd (544138), Liberty Shoes Ltd (526596), NCL Research & Financial Services Ltd (530557), Umiya Tubes Ltd (539798).
- Replies awaited from: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Crazy Snacks Ltd (544807), Darjeeling Industries Ltd (539770), Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (544156), Kaarya Facilities and Services Ltd (540756), Kinetic Trust Ltd (531274), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Praruh Technologies Ltd (544538), Rander Corporation Ltd (531228), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117), VJTF Eduservices Ltd (509026).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS).

## Compliance Requirements

Companies for which replies are awaited are expected to submit clarifications to BSE regarding the price/volume movement in their scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 21 July 2026; no specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

Investors and trading members in the 16 named scrips should note that these securities are under exchange surveillance for unusual price/volume activity. Companies that have not yet responded may face continued scrutiny or additional surveillance measures until clarifications are provided.
