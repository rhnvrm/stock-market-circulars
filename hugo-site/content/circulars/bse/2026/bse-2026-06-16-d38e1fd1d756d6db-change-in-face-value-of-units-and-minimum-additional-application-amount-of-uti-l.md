## Summary

BSE has notified members that UTI AMC will revise the face value of UTI Liquid Fund units from Rs. 1,000 per unit to Rs. 100 per unit, effective June 22, 2026, on the BSE StAR MF platform. The scheme will be temporarily unavailable for dematerialized transactions from June 17–19, 2026, and revised units will be allotted under a new ISIN. Additional transaction parameter revisions and introduction of SIP facility will follow on June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Face value of UTI Liquid Fund units revised from Rs. 1,000/- to Rs. 100/- per unit, effective June 22, 2026
- Dematerialized mode transactions suspended on BSE StAR MF platform from June 17 to June 19, 2026
- Post face value conversion, revised units will be allotted under a new ISIN assigned by UTI AMC/RTA
- Annexure with new ISIN details to be made available; transactions re-enabled from June 22, 2026
- Revision in minimum/additional application amounts for purchase, switch, and redemption effective June 24, 2026
- Introduction of Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility under UTI Liquid Fund from June 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to a Notice-cum-Addendum issued by UTI AMC, the following structural changes are being made to UTI Liquid Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform:

- **Face Value Revision:** The per-unit face value is reduced from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100, which will result in a proportional increase in the number of units held by investors.
- **ISIN Change:** A new ISIN will be issued post the face value conversion process by UTI AMC/RTA. The existing ISIN will be replaced.
- **Transaction Parameter Revision:** Minimum and additional application amounts for purchase, switch-in, switch-out, and redemption will be revised.
- **SIP Introduction:** A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility is being newly introduced under UTI Liquid Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members are advised to take note of all changes and ensure necessary actions are completed at their end.
- Members must refrain from executing dematerialized mode transactions in UTI Liquid Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform from June 17 to June 19, 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ISIN once announced by UTI AMC/RTA.
- Members should communicate changes in transaction parameters to their clients as applicable.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 17, 2026 | Start of dematerialized transaction suspension for UTI Liquid Fund |
| June 19, 2026 | End of dematerialized transaction suspension |
| June 22, 2026 | Revised face value (Rs. 100/unit) effective; new ISIN enabled on BSE StAR MF platform |
| June 24, 2026 | Revised transaction parameters (purchase/switch/redemption amounts) effective; SIP facility introduced |

## Impact Assessment

This change primarily impacts BSE StAR MF platform members and their clients who hold or transact in UTI Liquid Fund in dematerialized mode. The face value reduction from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100 per unit is a structural change that will increase the unit count proportionately without affecting the overall investment value. However, the accompanying ISIN change requires system-level updates by members and brokers. The three-day transaction blackout (June 17–19) may temporarily restrict liquidity access for investors using the demat route on BSE StAR MF. The introduction of SIP in a liquid fund and revised minimum application amounts broadens accessibility for retail investors.
