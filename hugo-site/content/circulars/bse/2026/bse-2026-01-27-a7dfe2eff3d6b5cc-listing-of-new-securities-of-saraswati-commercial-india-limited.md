## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and trading permission for 6 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Saraswati Commercial (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 512020). These shares, which were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme, will be available for trading from Wednesday, January 28, 2026. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 6 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being listed
- Shares were kept in abeyance pursuant to a scheme
- ISIN: INE967G01019
- Distinctive numbers: 640346 to 640351
- Shares are pari passu with old equity shares
- Scrip Code: 512020

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures for the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves only 6 equity shares of Saraswati Commercial (India) Limited, representing an insignificant quantity that will not materially affect trading volumes or market dynamics. This is a routine administrative listing of shares previously kept in abeyance.
