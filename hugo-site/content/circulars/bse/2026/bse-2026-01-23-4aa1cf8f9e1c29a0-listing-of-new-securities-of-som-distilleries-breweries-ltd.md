## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 6,06,060 new equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis following warrant conversion. Trading will commence on January 27, 2026. The shares were allotted on February 12, 2025, at Rs. 110/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 108/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until July 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- 6,06,060 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 108/-
- Issued to Promoter on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Allotment date: February 12, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 110/- per share
- Trading commencement: January 27, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Distinguished Numbers: 200442973 to 201049032
- Complete lock-in until July 28, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities for Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd.
- All 6,06,060 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in and cannot be traded until July 28, 2027
- Shares are issued under preferential allotment regulations for promoter subscription

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** February 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** January 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** July 28, 2027
- **Circular Date:** January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal immediate market impact as all shares are locked in until July 2027, preventing any trading activity. This is a promoter strengthening move through warrant conversion.

**Shareholding Impact:** Increases promoter holding by 6,06,060 shares, demonstrating promoter confidence in the company. The preferential allotment route via warrant conversion indicates planned capital infusion.

**Liquidity Impact:** No near-term liquidity impact due to complete lock-in. Post-July 2027, these shares may enter the trading float depending on promoter decisions.
