## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED. Anchor investor allocation is conducted prior to the opening of the public issue and helps in price discovery and building confidence among retail investors.

## Key Points

- Company conducting IPO: GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED
- Allocation made to anchor investors as per SEBI regulations
- Anchor allocation precedes the public issue opening
- Details of specific allocations, investor names, and amounts available in the full circular

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

- Anchor investors subject to lock-in period as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Disclosure requirements for anchor investor allocation met through this circular
- Company and lead managers must ensure compliance with anchor allocation norms

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 5, 2026
- Anchor allocation date and public issue dates available in detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation is a positive indicator for the upcoming IPO, showing institutional interest. The allocation helps establish a price reference point for retail investors and can influence subscription levels in the public issue.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency on pre-IPO institutional participation and helps retail investors assess the quality of investor interest in GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED.
