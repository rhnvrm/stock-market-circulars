## Summary

BSE has announced the launch of THE WEALTH COMPANY SMALL CAP FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window opens March 5, 2026, and closes March 18, 2026, with tentative unit allotment on March 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: March 5, 2026 to March 18, 2026
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All schemes are categorized as Equity with tentative allotment date of March 25, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on March 18, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions where clients have not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last fund payout day to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on March 25, 2026
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds on or before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification under existing BSE StAR MF Platform operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 to avoid transaction cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on March 25, 2026, for unit allotment
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed by clients before 3:00 PM on March 18, 2026
- Switch transactions must have funds received on or before the allotment date to be eligible

## Important Dates

- **March 5, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **March 18, 2026**: NFO subscription closes (midnight for lumpsum; 3:00 PM deadline for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **March 25, 2026**: Tentative unit allotment date; clear funds must be in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
