## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 22,00,000 new equity shares of BONLON INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543211) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 33/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 23/-).

## Key Points

- 22,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 23/- (issue price Rs. 33/-) to promoters via preferential allotment
- Allotment was made on conversion of warrants; shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 14183359 to 16383358
- ISIN: INE0B9A01018
- All 22,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these new securities from June 30, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 30, 2026
- **Effective date of listing/trading:** June 30, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in expiry:** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. The addition of 22,00,000 shares increases the promoter holding in BONLON INDUSTRIES LIMITED but the lock-in until end of 2027 prevents these shares from being traded, limiting any near-term dilution effect on the open market. Existing shareholders may note the expanded share capital on a pari-passu basis.
