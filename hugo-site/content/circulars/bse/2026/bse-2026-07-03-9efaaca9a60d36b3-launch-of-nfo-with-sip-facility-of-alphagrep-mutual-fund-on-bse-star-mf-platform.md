## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ALPHAGREP Mutual Fund will launch the New Fund Offer (NFO) of ALPHAGREP Multi Asset Allocation Fund, along with SIP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026, covering six plan/option variants of the debt-category scheme, with a tentative allotment date of July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND is launching ALPHAGREP MULTI ASSET ALLOCATION FUND as an NFO with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- The scheme falls under the DEBT category and is offered in six variants: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout).
- ISINs assigned: INF2VOA01046 (Regular Growth), INF2VOA01053 (Regular IDCW Reinvestment), INF2VOA01061 (Regular IDCW Payout), INF2VOA01012 (Direct Growth), INF2VOA01020 (Direct IDCW Reinvestment), INF2VOA01038 (Direct IDCW Payout).
- Tentative allotment date for all variants is July 24, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including during the extended period) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026.
- The NFO remains extended till midnight on July 20, 2026, only for lumpsum purchases; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 20, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice enabling a specific AMC's NFO and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated on time to avoid transaction cancellation and 'N' flag reporting to RTA/AMC.
- Members must ensure clear funds are credited to the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026, for both cheque/OTM and other payment modes to be eligible for allotment.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients no later than 3:00 PM on July 20, 2026; lumpsum purchases have an extended window till midnight on the same day.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 06, 2026 to July 20, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 20, 2026
- Lumpsum purchase extended deadline: Midnight, July 20, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 24, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular relevant primarily to BSE trading members and mutual fund distributors handling transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct bearing on listed equities or broader market trading activity, but members dealing in mutual fund distribution must adhere to the specified fund pay-in and SIP authentication timelines to avoid transaction cancellations for client NFO subscriptions.
