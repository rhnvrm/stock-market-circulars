## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Vivriti Capital Limited has fixed a record date of 09 March 2026 for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (series VCL-8.74%-24-01-31-PVT, ISIN INE01HV07544, scrip code 976532) along with payment of interest. Post-redemption, trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value.

## Key Points

- Company: Vivriti Capital Limited
- Debenture Series: VCL-8.74%-24-01-31-PVT
- ISIN: INE01HV07544 (Scrip Code: 976532)
- Record Date: 09 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 82,864 per debenture
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value: 09 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-835/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under BSE's debt segment operational guidelines for part redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from 09 March 2026, all trades in the specified debentures will be executed at the reduced face value of Rs. 82,864 per debenture.
- Members should update client records and system parameters to reflect the reduced face value effective from the settlement date DR-835/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 09 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 09 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-835/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** 24 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to trading members and investors holding the specific Vivriti Capital NCD series (INE01HV07544). From the effective date, the debenture will trade at a reduced face value of Rs. 82,864 per debenture, reflecting the partial principal repayment. This is a routine event with no broader market implications.
