## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 80,50,000 new equity shares of Comfort Fincap Limited (Scrip Code: 535267), issued at Rs. 2.00 face value with a premium of Rs. 7.00 per share to Promoter and Non-Promoter category on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, July 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 80,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 2.00 each issued at a premium of Rs. 7.00 (issue price Rs. 9.00 per share)
- Allotment made to Promoter and Non-Promoter category on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE274M01026
- Distinctive numbers: 90006501 to 98056500
- Date of allotment: 17/04/2026
- Lock-in details: 22,11,300 shares (Dist. Nos. 90006501-92217800) locked in until 29/02/2028; 58,38,700 shares (Dist. Nos. 92217801-98056500) locked in until 28/02/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares arising from warrant conversion on a preferential allotment basis.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new shares as listed and eligible for trading from the effective date; entities holding locked-in shares must observe the applicable lock-in periods (until February 2027 and February 2028 respectively) before any transfer or trading of those specific tranches.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 17 April 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 8 July 2026 (Wednesday)
- Lock-in expiry (58,38,700 shares): 28 February 2027
- Lock-in expiry (22,11,300 shares): 29 February 2028

## Impact Assessment

This increases Comfort Fincap Limited's listed equity share capital by 80,50,000 shares, expanding the free-float base gradually as lock-in periods expire. The immediate market impact is limited since a significant portion of the new shares remain locked in until 2027-2028, restricting near-term supply overhang. This is largely a procedural/administrative listing update relevant to shareholders and capital structure tracking rather than an event with broad market-moving implications.
