## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Liotech Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned code 544796 under the symbol LIOTECH in the SME segment. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a subsequent separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Liotech Industries Limited are approved for listing on BSE's SME segment.
- Scrip code: 544796; Symbol: LIOTECH; ISIN: INE0TKX01011.
- The exact listing date and security details will be announced via a separate notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date before initiating dealings in LIOTECH shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited to SME segment participants and investors tracking Liotech Industries Limited. No broad market or systemic impact. Trading members should monitor BSE for the subsequent notice confirming the listing date and additional security details.
