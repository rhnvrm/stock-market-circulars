## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up notice (continuation of Notice No. 20260304-6 dated March 04, 2026) informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 506854) consequent to the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- to Rs.5/- per share.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 506854): **INE639B01023**
- Sub-division ratio: Equity shares sub-divided from Rs.10/- face value to Rs.5/- face value
- The new ISIN is effective for all trades executed on and from the ex-date
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260304-6 dated March 04, 2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN for TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD equity shares has been updated from the previous identifier to INE639B01023 to reflect the sub-divided shares of Rs.5/- each. Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN for all transactions from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN number **INE639B01023** for all trades in TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD equity shares on and from the ex-date (09-03-2026)
- Reference DR-835/2025-2026 for record-keeping purposes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Ex-Date (Effective Date for New ISIN):** March 09, 2026
- **Previous Notice:** March 04, 2026 (Notice No. 20260304-6)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update following a corporate action (share sub-division). Trading members and depository participants need to update their systems to reference the new ISIN (INE639B01023) for TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD from March 09, 2026. Investors holding shares under the old ISIN will have their holdings migrated to the new ISIN by the depository. The share sub-division doubles the number of shares held while halving the face value, which may improve liquidity for the stock.
