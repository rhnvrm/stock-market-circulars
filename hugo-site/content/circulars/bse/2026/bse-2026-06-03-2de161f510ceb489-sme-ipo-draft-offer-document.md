## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Avi Lifestyles Limited**, filed on June 02, 2026, on the BSE SME platform. The document contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Avi Lifestyles Limited on June 02, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website in compliance with ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI)
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (June 03, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under SEBI's ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of the DRHP ahead of an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days of June 03, 2026 (deadline: approximately June 24, 2026)
- Comments must be sent via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed:** June 02, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** June 24, 2026 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine pre-IPO disclosure for Avi Lifestyles Limited on the SME platform. It is relevant primarily to prospective investors, analysts, and advisors evaluating the upcoming SME IPO. No existing listed securities are affected.
