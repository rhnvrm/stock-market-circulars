## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window – Takeover) for IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED. The acquirer, ASPECT GLOBAL VENTURES PRIVATE LIMITED, is making an open offer to public shareholders at Rs. 8.50 per equity share to acquire up to 26,98,298 shares representing 26% of the voting share capital, in compliance with SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer:** ASPECT GLOBAL VENTURES PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Target Company:** IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 8.50/- (Rupees Eight and Fifty Paise Only) per equity share, payable in cash
- **Shares to be Acquired:** Up to 26,98,298 equity shares (face value Rs. 5.00 each)
- **Percentage of Voting Capital:** 26% of the total voting share capital
- **Regulatory Basis:** SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 – Regulations 3(1) and 4 (substantial acquisition with change in control)
- **Mechanism:** BSE's Acquisition Window mechanism per SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131
- **Letter of Offer** attached to the notice for reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under the existing framework established by:
- SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE revised guidelines via notices dated February 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; November 2, 2020; and August 25, 2021

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians** must note the opening of the acquisition window and facilitate client participation as per SEBI's tender-offer mechanism guidelines.
- Participants must follow the Revised Guidelines for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers as detailed in BSE notice no. 20170210-16 and 20190424-35.
- Full offer details are available on the BSE website at www.bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date:** Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date:** Tuesday, June 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer provides public shareholders of IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED with a cash exit opportunity at Rs. 8.50 per share. The offer represents a change-in-control transaction, meaning the acquirer (ASPECT GLOBAL VENTURES PRIVATE LIMITED) is seeking to gain substantial voting rights and management control. The 26% acquisition target is significant as it may materially alter the ownership structure and corporate governance of the target company. Shareholders should review the attached Letter of Offer carefully to make an informed tendering decision within the June 10–23, 2026 window.
