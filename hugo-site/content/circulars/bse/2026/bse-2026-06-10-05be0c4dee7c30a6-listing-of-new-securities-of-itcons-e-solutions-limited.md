## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,70,200 new equity shares of ITCONS E-Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543806, ISIN: INE0O6A01019) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. The shares were allotted on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 71.00/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 61/-).

## Key Points

- 8,70,200 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed effective June 11, 2026
- Issued at a premium of Rs. 61/- (issue price Rs. 71.00/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter via preferential allotment on conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 7303127 to 8173326
- Date of allotment: January 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE0O6A01019

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares following preferential warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these securities from June 11, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to all newly listed shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 29, 2026
- **Trading commencement:** June 11, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (5,74,200 shares):** December 21, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (2,96,000 shares):** December 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification affecting only ITCONS E-Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543806). The addition of 8,70,200 shares to the tradable pool represents a modest increase in the company's listed equity. The majority of newly listed shares (5,74,200) remain under lock-in until December 2027, limiting near-term free float impact. Existing shareholders should note dilution from the preferential warrant conversion at Rs. 71.00/- per share.
