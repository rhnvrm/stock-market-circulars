## Summary

BSE has listed five new Commercial Papers (CPs) on its Debt Market Segment under private placement, effective June 24, 2026. The issuers are Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited, ECL Finance Limited, Time Technoplast Limited, and DSP Finance Private Limited. All securities will trade exclusively in dematerialised form in standard denominations of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five Commercial Papers admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 24, 2026
- All CPs issued on a private placement basis with face value of ₹5,00,000 per unit
- Trading permitted only in demat form; standard denomination ₹5 lakhs and multiples; tick size 1 paise
- Redemption periods range from September 2026 to June 2027
- Credit ratings are A1+ across all issuers (CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, IND ratings)
- Queries to be directed to BSE Debt Department: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under existing BSE Debt Segment listing norms for privately placed Commercial Papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- All trades must be in standard denominations of ₹5,00,000 and multiples thereof
- Trading members should use the assigned Scrip Codes and ISINs for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (NWFL-21-9-26-CP):** 6,000 units at issue price ₹4,90,268.50; rate 8.05% p.a.; rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+; paying agent ICICI Bank.

**Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited (SCSL-23-12-26-CP):** 200 units at issue price ₹4,83,047.00; rate 7.00% p.a.; rated ICRA A1+; paying agent HDFC Bank.

**ECL Finance Limited (ECLFL-21-12-26-CP):** 540 units at issue price ₹4,78,411.00; rate 9.10% p.a.; rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+; paying agent ICICI Bank.

**Time Technoplast Limited (TTL-24-06-27-CP):** 1,000 units at issue price ₹4,62,321.00; rate 8.15% p.a.; rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+; paying agent IDBI Bank.

**DSP Finance Private Limited (DFPL-22-12-26-CP):** 400 units at issue price ₹4,82,091.50; rate 7.45% p.a.; rated ICRA A1+; paying agent not fully captured in source.

Impact is limited to institutional and professional investors active in the BSE Debt Segment. All issuers carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (A1+), indicating low credit risk. No equity market impact anticipated.
