## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in commercial papers of 22 companies due to upcoming redemption. The suspension takes effect from the record dates of February 10-11, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 22 companies have fixed record dates for redemption of commercial papers
- 12 companies have record date on February 10, 2026
- 10 companies have record date on February 11, 2026
- No dealings permitted from respective record dates
- Notice reference: DR-817/2025-2026 and DR-818/2025-2026
- Includes major corporations like Adani Enterprises, Reliance Retail Ventures, Bajaj Finance, ICICI Home Finance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from the specified record dates
- Trading suspension effective from February 10, 2026 for 12 issuers
- Trading suspension effective from February 11, 2026 for 10 issuers
