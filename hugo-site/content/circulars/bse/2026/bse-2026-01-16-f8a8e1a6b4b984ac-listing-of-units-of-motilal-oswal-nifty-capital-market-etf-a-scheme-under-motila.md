## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Nifty Capital Market ETF units effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Capital Market Total Return Index. A total of 1,725,000 units have been allotted at an issue price of Rs. 47.54 per unit with a face value of Rs. 10.00. The units will trade in dematerialized form only in the 'B' Group with scrip code 590149.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Total Units**: 1,725,000 units
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 47.54 per unit
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 590149
- **ISIN**: INF247L01EV3
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B Group
- **Date of Allotment**: March 7, 2025
- **Investment Manager**: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited
- **Registered Office**: 10th Floor, Motilal Oswal Tower, Rahimtullah Sayani Road, Opp. Parel ST Depot, Prabhadevi, Mumbai-400025

## Market Makers

Three authorized market makers have been designated:
1. East India Securities Ltd.
2. Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
3. Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
4. Irage Broking Services LLP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that units will only be traded in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Standard BSE trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: March 7, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: January 19, 2026

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market impact.
