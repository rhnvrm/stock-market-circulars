## Summary

BSE has issued new ISIN numbers for two DSP Mutual Fund ETFs — DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Quality 50 ETF (scrip code 543438) and DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (scrip code 544109) — following a split/change in face value from Rs.10 per unit to Re.1 per unit. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260616-11 dated June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Quality 50 ETF (543438) is assigned new ISIN **INF740KA1ZF3** following face value split from Rs.10 to Re.1.
- DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (544109) is assigned new ISIN **INF740KA1ZG1** following face value split from Rs.10 to Re.1.
- The new ISINs are effective from the ex-date of **July 3, 2026** (DR-664/2026-2027).
- This notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260616-11 dated June 16, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

New ISIN numbers have been allotted to reflect the change in face value (unit split) of the two DSP ETFs. The old ISINs will no longer be valid for trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN numbers (INF740KA1ZF3 and INF740KA1ZG1) for trades in the respective ETFs from July 3, 2026 onwards.
- Back-office and settlement systems should reflect the new ISINs effective the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** 20260616-11 dated June 16, 2026
- **Ex-Date (effective date for new ISINs):** July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational change impacting holders and traders of DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Quality 50 ETF and DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF. The face value split from Rs.10 to Re.1 increases the number of units proportionally, making units more affordable. Trading members and custodians must update ISIN references in their systems before July 3, 2026 to ensure uninterrupted settlement. Failure to update may lead to settlement failures or mismatches in trade records.
