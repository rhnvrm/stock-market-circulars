## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed commercial papers (CPs) of four companies — Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited — on the BSE Debt Segment, effective June 10, 2026. All securities were allotted on June 9, 2026, and are available for trading exclusively in dematerialised form.

## Key Points

- All commercial papers are traded in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all listed securities is 1 paise
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form under respective ISINs
- All securities were allotted on 09/06/2026 (deemed date)
- Face value and paid-up value for all CPs: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Signed off by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational listing notice under BSE's debt market segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
