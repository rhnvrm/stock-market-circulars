## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about a change in the Scrip ID for Arisinfra Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544419) in the BOLT Plus System. The Scrip ID has been changed from ARISINFRA to ARIS, effective January 09, 2026, as per the company's request.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code remains unchanged: 544419
- Old Scrip ID: ARISINFRA
- New Scrip ID: ARIS
- Change applies to BOLT Plus System only
- Effective date: January 09, 2026
- Notice issued on February 03, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative modification to the trading identifier in BSE's BOLT Plus trading system as requested by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Take note of the Scrip ID change in their trading systems
- Update any internal references from ARISINFRA to ARIS
- Ensure proper identification when placing orders for this security

- Ms. Asmita Chavan: +91 22 2272 8266 (Listing.crd@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 09, 2026
- **Notice Date**: February 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a technical change in the trading identifier that does not affect the scrip code, trading operations, or investor holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new Scrip ID (ARIS) instead of the old one (ARISINFRA). The scrip code 544419 remains unchanged, minimizing confusion.

**Investor Impact**: None. Investors' holdings and trading activities are not affected by this administrative change in the trading system identifier.
