## Summary

BSE has announced index-related actions arising from the demerger of Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited's (TRIVENI, Exchange Ticker 532356) power transmission business into Triveni Power Transmission Limited (TPTL). The demerger takes effect from the start of trading hours on Wednesday, July 22, 2026, with shareholders of TRIVENI receiving 1 equity share of TPTL for every 3 equity shares held. A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be conducted on July 22, 2026 for price discovery of TRIVENI post-demerger, and TPTL will be added to nine BSE indices at zero price without divisor adjustment.

## Key Points

- Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited is demerging its power transmission business into a new entity, Triveni Power Transmission Limited.
- Shareholders receive 1 TPTL share for every 3 TRIVENI shares held.
- TPTL equity shares are expected to list on BSE at a future date.
- A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be held on July 22, 2026 to enable price discovery for TRIVENI post-demerger.
- TPTL will be added to the following BSE indices at zero price, without divisor adjustment: BSE SMALLCAP, BSE FAST MOVING CONSUMER GOODS, BSE ALLCAP, BSE 1000, BSE NEXT 500, BSE 250 MICROCAP, BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%), BSE SMALLCAP 500, and BSE MIDSMALLCAP.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rules are introduced; this is an index maintenance action arising from a corporate scheme of arrangement (demerger).

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action is required from market participants. Index fund managers and passive investors tracking the affected BSE indices should account for the zero-price addition of TPTL and the absence of divisor adjustment in their portfolio rebalancing.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026 (close of trading): TPTL added to indices at zero price.
- July 22, 2026: Demerger becomes effective from start of trading hours; Special Pre-Open Session held for TRIVENI price discovery; TPTL effective addition date to BSE indices.

## Impact Assessment

The demerger and subsequent index addition affect index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the nine listed BSE indices, as they will need to account for TPTL's inclusion. Since TPTL is added at zero price without divisor adjustment, immediate index value impact is minimal, but tracking portfolios will need to adjust holdings once TPTL shares are credited and begin trading. TRIVENI shareholders should expect price adjustment in TRIVENI shares during the SPOS on July 22, 2026 to reflect the value transferred to the demerged entity.
