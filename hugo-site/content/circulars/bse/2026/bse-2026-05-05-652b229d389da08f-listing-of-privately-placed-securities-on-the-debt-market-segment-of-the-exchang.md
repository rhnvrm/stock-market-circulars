## Summary

BSE has listed five new commercial papers issued on a private placement basis, admitting them to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective May 5, 2026. The securities are issued by Infina Finance Private Limited, Network 18 Media & Investments Limited, Chalet Hotels Limited, SBICAP Securities Limited, and DSP Finance Private Limited. All securities trade in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment with effect from May 5, 2026
- All securities trade exclusively in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size: 1 paise
- Ratings across listed papers range from CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+/CARE A1+/IND A1+, indicating highest short-term credit quality
- Redemption dates range from June 2026 to October 2026
- Issuing and paying agents: ICICI Bank Limited, State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of new admissions to the BSE Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these securities are conducted in dematerialised form only
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
