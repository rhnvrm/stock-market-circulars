## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that 1,24,38,296 equity shares of One Global Service Provider Limited (Scrip Code: 514330, ISIN: INE670O01013) issued under a Scheme of Amalgamation are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange effective Wednesday, March 04, 2026. The shares were issued to shareholders of Plus Care International Private Limited, the transferor company, as part of its merger into One Global Service Provider Limited under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- 1,24,38,296 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are listed on BSE effective March 04, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation of Plus Care International Private Limited (Transferor) with One Global Service Provider Limited (Transferee)
- Amalgamation approved under Sections 230 to 232 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of One Global Service Provider Limited
- ISIN: INE670O01013
- Distribution numbers: 7321208 to 19759503
- Date of allotment: July 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the listing of shares previously allotted under a court/NCLT-approved scheme of amalgamation as permitted under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their records to reflect the addition of 1,24,38,296 new equity shares of One Global Service Provider Limited (Scrip Code: 514330) available for trading from March 04, 2026
- Brokers and depository participants should ensure these shares are correctly reflected under ISIN INE670O01013

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** July 04, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Circular Date:** March 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables shareholders of the erstwhile Plus Care International Private Limited who received shares under the amalgamation scheme to trade their holdings on BSE. The addition of approximately 1.24 crore shares to the tradable float of One Global Service Provider Limited may affect liquidity and share price dynamics. The impact is moderate and contained to existing shareholders of both companies involved in the merger.
