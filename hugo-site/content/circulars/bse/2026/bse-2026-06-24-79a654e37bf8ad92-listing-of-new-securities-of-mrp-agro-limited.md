## Summary

BSE has listed 3,91,730 new equity shares of MRP Agro Limited (Scrip Code: 543262) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. These shares are available for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,91,730 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 120/-, making the issue price Rs. 130/- per share
- Shares allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 11108271 to 11500000
- ISIN: INE0D7801012
- All 3,91,730 shares are subject to a lock-in period until August 27, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to an approved preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility for trading from June 25, 2026. No additional compliance action required from members beyond updating scrip data.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 2, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 27, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Market impact is minimal. The 3,91,730 newly listed shares are entirely under promoter lock-in until August 2028 and will not be available for trading in the secondary market during this period. The listing is procedural and does not add to the tradeable float immediately.
