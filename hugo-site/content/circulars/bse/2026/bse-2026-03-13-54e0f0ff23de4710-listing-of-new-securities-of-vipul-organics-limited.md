## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13,05,400 new equity shares of Vipul Organics Limited (Scrip Code: 530627) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, March 16, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 211/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 201/-).

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 13,05,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 211/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 201 premium)
- **Allotted to:** Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- **Date of allotment:** 22 December 2025
- **Trading effective from:** Monday, March 16, 2026
- **ISIN:** INE834D01018
- **Distribution numbers:** 17749167 to 19054566
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the lock-in restrictions on the newly listed shares before executing trades
- Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | 22 December 2025 |
| Trading commences | 16 March 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (12,15,000 shares: Dist. 17749167–18964166) | 30 October 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (60,000 shares: Dist. 18964167–19024166) | 31 October 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (20,000 shares: Dist. 19024167–19044166) | 30 October 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (10,400 shares: Dist. 19044167–19054566) | 31 October 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 13,05,400 new equity shares increases the tradeable float of Vipul Organics Limited. However, the majority of shares (approximately 12,05,400 out of 13,05,400) are under lock-in until October 2026, limiting immediate supply impact on the market. The free-float addition is therefore minimal in the near term. This is a routine corporate action with low broader market significance.
