## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 792 dated January 7, 2026. The list contains 88 scrips from various companies with specified quantities that are subject to demat auction settlement. This is a routine market operations circular for settlement of outstanding delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 792
- Date: January 7, 2026
- Total scrips listed: 88 companies
- Quantities range from 1 share to 114,296 shares depending on the scrip
- Covers diverse sectors including pharmaceuticals, financial services, metals, automobiles, textiles, and engineering

## Scrips Under Auction

The circular lists all scrips with their codes and auction quantities, including:

- High quantity auctions: MIRC ELECTRO (114,296 shares), GOURMET GATE (10,307 shares), GOYAL ALUMIN (6,001 shares)
- Medium quantity auctions: BLISS GVS PH (5,385 shares), SHANKARA BUI (5,137 shares), BARODA EXTRU (4,500 shares)
- Low quantity auctions: Multiple scrips with quantities ranging from 1 to 100 shares

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must settle their demat auction obligations for the listed scrips
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's standard demat auction procedures
- Members should ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 7, 2026
- Settlement Number: 792

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with medium impact. The demat auction affects trading members who have failed delivery obligations. The wide range of scrips and quantities indicates normal market settlement activity. Members with positions in the listed scrips must ensure timely settlement to avoid penalties. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement of failed trades and maintains market integrity.
