## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial papers, issued on a private placement basis by five companies, have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 21, 2026. The notice details the ISIN, credit rating, issue price, interest rate, and redemption date for each instrument.

## Key Points

- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN numbers.
- Trading will occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise.
- Fresh listings include:
  - **360 ONE Prime Limited**: Scrip Code 732113, ISIN INE248U14TS0, Rs. 500,000 face value, Rs. 463,485 issue price, 7.90% p.a., allotted 20/07/2026, redemption 19/07/2027, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
  - **Kirloskar Ferrous Industries Ltd.**: Scrip Code 732114, ISIN INE884B14788, Rs. 500,000 face value, Rs. 491,659.30 issue price, 7.20% p.a., allotted 20/07/2026, redemption 14/10/2026, rated ICRA A1+.
  - **KEC International Ltd.**: Scrip Code 732115, ISIN INE389H14MR6, Rs. 500,000 face value, Rs. 493,236 issue price, 7.05% p.a., allotted 21/07/2026, redemption 30/09/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+.
  - **Bajaj Finance Limited**: Scrip Code 732116, ISIN INE296A14I34, Rs. 500,000 face value, Rs. 491,183 issue price, 7.20% p.a., allotted 20/07/2026, redemption 19/10/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
  - **Infina Finance Private Limited**: Scrip Code 732117, ISIN INE879F14NI7, Rs. 500,000 face value, Rs. 488,481 issue price, 7.55% p.a., allotted 20/07/2026, redemption date per detail name 11/11/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's debt segment listing process; no new regulatory requirements are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from equity market participants. Trading members dealing in the debt segment should note the ISIN, denomination, and tick size details for these newly listed commercial papers. Queries can be directed to BSE's debt department at the numbers provided (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).

## Important Dates

- Listing effective: July 21, 2026
- Allotment dates: 20/07/2026 (360 ONE Prime, Kirloskar Ferrous, Bajaj Finance, Infina Finance) and 21/07/2026 (KEC International)
- Redemption dates range from 30/09/2026 to 19/07/2027 depending on the instrument

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, procedural listing notice relevant only to debt segment trading members and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. It does not affect equity trading, pricing, or broader market operations for the underlying companies' listed equity shares.
