## Summary

BSE has published an updated list of 31 securities subject to the Add-on Price Band Framework. These securities face additional price movement restrictions as a surveillance measure to protect investors and maintain market stability.

## Key Points

- 31 securities currently under Add-on Price Band Framework
- Covers companies across various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, and infrastructure
- Securities subject to stricter price band limits compared to normal trading
- Framework acts as enhanced surveillance measure

## Regulatory Changes

This is an updated list reflecting the current status of securities under the Add-on Price Band Framework. No new regulatory changes are introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply applicable price bands for listed securities
- Investors should be aware of restricted price movements in these securities
- Price movements limited to prescribed bands during trading sessions

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 01, 2026
- Effective immediately for all listed securities in the table

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects liquidity and price discovery for 31 securities by restricting intraday price movements.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Provides protection against excessive volatility but may limit trading opportunities. Investors holding or trading these securities should exercise caution.

**Trading Impact:** Price bands limit maximum upward/downward movement per session, requiring multiple sessions for significant price adjustments.
