## Summary

BSE has listed 5,00,000 new equity shares of Orient Tradelink Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531512, ISIN: INE681D01039) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, April 13, 2026. Each share has a face value of Rs. 10/- and was issued at a premium of Rs. 6/-, making the issue price Rs. 16/- per share.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 6/- (issue price: Rs. 16/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 34732001 to 35232000
- ISIN: INE681D01039
- Effective trading date: Monday, April 13, 2026
- All allotted shares are under lock-in until October 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities following a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the addition of 5,00,000 new equity shares to the tradeable securities of Orient Tradelink Ltd.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 5,00,000 shares are locked in until October 15, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date.

## Important Dates

- **April 13, 2026**: Effective date for listing and trading of new securities
- **October 15, 2026**: Lock-in expiry date for all allotted shares
- Allotment dates and distribution:
  - 26/11/2025: 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 34732001–34832000), lock-in upto 15/10/2026
  - 28/11/2025: 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 34832001–34932000), lock-in upto 15/10/2026
  - 08/12/2025: 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 34932001–35032000), lock-in upto 15/10/2026
  - 09/12/2025: 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 35032001–35132000), lock-in upto 15/10/2026
  - 10/12/2025: 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 35132001–35232000), lock-in upto 15/10/2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 5,00,000 new equity shares to Orient Tradelink Ltd.'s share capital, representing a preferential allotment to non-promoters via warrant conversion. Since all new shares are under lock-in until October 15, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float or liquidity. Broader market impact is minimal given the small size of the issuance. Existing shareholders may note a dilution in equity, while the company receives proceeds at Rs. 16/- per share.
