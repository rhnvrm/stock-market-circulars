## Summary

Vedanta Limited, the promoter of Hindustan Zinc Limited, has issued a notice for an Offer for Sale (OFS) of equity shares through the stock exchange mechanism. The Seller proposes to sell up to 3,35,00,000 equity shares (0.79% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital) with an option to additionally sell up to 1,10,00,000 equity shares (0.79% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital). The OFS is structured in accordance with SEBI OFS Circulars and Stock Exchange operational guidelines.

## Key Points

- **Base Offer Size**: Up to 3,35,00,000 equity shares (0.79% stake) of Hindustan Zinc Limited
- **Additional Option**: Up to 1,10,00,000 equity shares (0.79% stake)

- **Seller**: Vedanta Limited (Promoter)
- **Method**: Stock exchange mechanism as per SEBI OFS framework
- **Regulatory Framework**: Conducted under SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024
- **Stock Exchanges**: BSE Limited and National Stock Exchange of India Limited

## Regulatory Changes

The OFS is conducted under the comprehensive framework on Offer for Sale of Shares through Stock Exchange Mechanism as notified by SEBI, including:

- Master circular for stock exchanges (SEBI/HO/MRD-PoD2/CIR/P/2024/00181 dated December 30, 2024)
- Framework for OFS to Employees (SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/6 dated January 23, 2024)
- Modification in Framework for OFS to Employees (SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024)
- BSE Revised Operational Guidelines (Notice 20240701-19 dated July 1, 2024)
- NSE Revised Operating Guidelines (Circular 93/2024 dated July 12, 2024)

## Compliance Requirements

- Notice issued to BSE Limited and National Stock Exchange of India Limited as per regulatory requirements
- Compliance with SEBI OFS Circulars and Stock Exchange operational guidelines
- Adherence to disclosure norms for offer for sale transactions
- Subject to limited exceptions regarding release and distribution in United States and other restricted jurisdictions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **T Day (Non-Retail Investors only)**: January 28, 2026
- **T+1 Day (Retail Investors and carry-forward bids)**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The OFS involves a substantial stake sale by promoter Vedanta Limited, which may lead to increased trading volumes and potential price volatility in Hindustan Zinc Limited shares during the offer period.

**Ownership Structure**: Post-OFS, Vedanta Limited's promoter holding in Hindustan Zinc Limited will reduce by up to 1.58% (if greenshoe option is fully exercised), which may affect market perception of promoter commitment.

**Liquidity**: The OFS will provide institutional and retail investors an opportunity to acquire shares through the stock exchange mechanism, potentially improving free float and liquidity.

**Investor Categories**: The two-day structure allows non-retail investors to bid on T day with retail investors and carry-forward bids on T+1 day, ensuring broader participation across investor segments.
