## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of PVV Infra Ltd. (Scrip Code: 536659) with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. The new securities comprise 59,55,513 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued pursuant to the conversion of partly paid-up shares (issued on a rights basis) into fully paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- Company: PVV Infra Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 536659
- ISIN: INE428B01021
- New shares: 59,55,513 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid-up rights shares to fully paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers: 58,41,285 shares (Nos. 124328233 to 130169517) and 1,14,228 shares (Nos. 130169518 to 130283745)
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Notice No. 20260717-15 dated July 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notice under BSE Listing Operations enabling trading in shares that became fully paid-up following call money payment on rights-issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to recognize and permit trading in the additional fully paid-up shares under the existing ISIN INE428B01021 from the effective date. No further action is required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: Monday, July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the fully paid-up equity share capital and free float of PVV Infra Ltd. by 59,55,513 shares. As these shares carry pari-passu rights with existing equity, the impact on per-share metrics may be marginally dilutive, but the overall market impact is limited given this is a routine administrative listing following a rights issue call-money conversion.
