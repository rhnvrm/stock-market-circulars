## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L29220TG2012PLC082904. The company name has been officially changed from STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED to STANDARD ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED with effect from December 29, 2025. The certificate was digitally signed by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED
- **New Name**: STANDARD ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED
- **CIN**: L29220TG2012PLC082904
- **Effective Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Original Name**: STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Registered Address**: D.12, Phase I, IDA, Jeedimetla, Hyderabad-500055, Telangana, India
- **Certificate Issued By**: Sunidhi Matroja, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative procedure under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically rule 29 for change of name certification.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Display Requirement**: The company is obligated to display the old name (STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED) along with its new name for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act.
- **Rights and Liabilities**: The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.
- **Stakeholder Verification**: All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: December 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from December 29, 2025 (until December 29, 2027)
- **Circular Publication Date**: January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Company name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder equity. Trading symbol/ticker may be updated by the exchange.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative changes required in company stationery, signage, contracts, and legal documents. The company must comply with the two-year dual name display requirement.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights, obligations, or benefits. Existing shares, contracts, and legal agreements remain valid under the new name.
