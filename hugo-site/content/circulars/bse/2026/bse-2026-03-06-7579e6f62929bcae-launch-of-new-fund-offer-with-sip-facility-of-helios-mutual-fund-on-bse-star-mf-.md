## Summary

BSE has informed members that HELIOS MUTUAL FUND will launch the HELIOS ARBITRAGE FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window opens March 09, 2026, and closes March 13, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of March 20, 2026. Six scheme variants are available under Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- NFO period: March 09, 2026 to March 13, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- Tentative allotment date: March 20, 2026
- Lump sum purchases extended till midnight on March 13, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on the NFO closure date
- Transactions where client has not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Pending transactions (where payment is initiated) remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds payout or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a new fund launch.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on March 18, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable during the NFO period, subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on March 18, 2026
- Switch transactions must have funds received under the NFO scheme on or before the allotment date; funds received after the allotment date will not be considered
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on March 13, 2026

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **March 13, 2026**: NFO subscription closes; lump sum purchases accepted till midnight; SIP/XSIP authentication deadline at 3:00 PM
- **March 18, 2026**: Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
- **March 20, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to BSE members and distributors who operate on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The launch of the HELIOS ARBITRAGE FUND adds a new equity arbitrage scheme for investors seeking low-risk returns with tax efficiency. Operational impact is moderate for members who need to manage client transactions, fund clearances, and SIP registrations within the specified timelines. No broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
