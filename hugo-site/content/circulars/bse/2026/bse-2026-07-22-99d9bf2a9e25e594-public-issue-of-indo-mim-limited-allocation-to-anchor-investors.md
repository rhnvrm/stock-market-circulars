## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the public issue of INDO MIM Limited, 2,35,25,656 equity shares were subscribed on July 22, 2026, at a price of Rs. 485 per equity share. A copy of the company's announcement dated July 22, 2026 was enclosed for reference.

## Key Points

- 2,35,25,656 equity shares allotted under the Anchor Investors portion of INDO MIM Limited's public issue
- Anchor allocation price fixed at Rs. 485 per equity share
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department
- Signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members; notice is for information purposes.

## Important Dates

- Anchor investor subscription and allocation: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor allocation confirms investor demand ahead of INDO MIM Limited's public issue and sets a reference price point (Rs. 485) for the broader offer. This is a routine pre-IPO disclosure with limited direct market impact beyond informing participants tracking the issue's progress and pricing.
