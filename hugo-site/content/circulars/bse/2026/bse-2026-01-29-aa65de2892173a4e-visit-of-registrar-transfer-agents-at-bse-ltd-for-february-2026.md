## Summary

BSE has published the schedule for Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) visits to BSE premises during February 2026. The circular contains a list of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd, one of the RTAs scheduled to visit. These visits facilitate direct interaction between investors and RTAs for resolution of grievances and service requests related to share transfers, dematerialization, dividend payments, and other investor services.

## Key Points

- RTA visits scheduled for February 2026 at BSE premises
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd is among the RTAs visiting
- List includes 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, textiles, and technology
- Investors can utilize these visits for grievance redressal and service requests
- Covers scrip codes ranging from 501270 to 524606

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural circular for scheduling RTA visits.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for listed entities. This is an informational circular for investor benefit.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Facilitates investor servicing through direct RTA interaction at exchange premises.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for shareholders of the listed companies who can utilize RTA visits for resolving service issues related to share certificates, dividend payments, address changes, and other corporate actions.
