## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade for 6,40,000 new equity shares of Ramkrishna Forgings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532527) with effect from Friday, June 12, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 2100/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 2098/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Shares Listed:** 6,40,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2098/-
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 2100/- per share
- **Issued to:** Promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- **Pari-passu:** Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **ISIN:** INE399G01023
- **Distribution Numbers:** 181030605 to 181670604
- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading Effective:** June 12, 2026
- **Lock-in Period:** All 6,40,000 shares locked in until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to BSE Listing Operations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 12, 2026. The lock-in restriction on all 6,40,000 shares must be observed until December 31, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 12, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these preferential allotment shares has minimal immediate market impact as all 6,40,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 31, 2027, preventing promoter selling in the near term. The issue price of Rs. 2100/- per share reflects the premium at which warrants were converted. The new shares are fungible with existing equity (same ISIN), so no separate tracking is required after the lock-in expires.
