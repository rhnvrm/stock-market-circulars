## Summary

BSE has listed 30,95,000 new equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676) issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Monday, March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- 30,95,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 36.60/- per share (issue price Rs. 37.60/-)
- Shares issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE337M01021
- Distribution Numbers: 124923191 – 128018190
- Date of Allotment: December 31, 2025
- Trading permitted from March 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing and lock-in details before executing trades in these securities. Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 31, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,95,000 shares | Dist. Nos. 124923191–125518190):** September 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,00,000 shares | Dist. Nos. 125518191–126018190):** September 15, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (20,00,000 shares | Dist. Nos. 126018191–128018190):** September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 30,95,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Indo Thai Securities Limited, though a significant portion (30,95,000 shares) is subject to lock-in until September 2026 or September 2027. Only shares not under lock-in will be freely tradeable from March 9, 2026. The issue price of Rs. 37.60/- per share reflects the warrant conversion terms. Market impact is limited to Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676).
