## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the Equity Shares of Crazy Snacks Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Friday, July 3, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME segment. In line with SEBI circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Crazy Snacks Limited, listing on BSE SME platform in 'MT' Group
- No. of Securities: 2,39,37,830 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (Distinctive Number range 1 to 23937830)
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: CRAZY
- Scrip Code: 544807
- ISIN: INE0RBX01014
- Market Lot: 3,000 shares
- Issue Price: Rs. 42/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 32/-)
- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 1, 2026
- Financial Year end: March 31
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form; physical shares carry non-transferability endorsement
- Scrip will participate in Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 3, 2026
- Market Maker: Alacrity Securities Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000215936)
- Registrar to the issue: KFin Technologies Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (dated January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade settlement for newly listed scrips for the first 10 trading days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in dematerialised form and in minimum market lot of 3,000 equity shares.
- Trading members must account for Trade-for-Trade settlement conditions for the first 10 trading days from listing.
- Any modification to market lot size will be communicated via a separate market notice with at least one month's prior notice.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 1, 2026
- Listing and trading commencement: Friday, July 3, 2026
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS): July 3, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable: First 10 trading days from July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice affecting trading members and investors dealing in Crazy Snacks Limited shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction and minimum lot size of 3,000 shares will limit speculative trading and reduce liquidity risk during the initial listing period. Broader market impact is minimal given the SME segment classification and limited issue size.
