## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has released the client margin reporting schedule for July 2026 for the Currency Derivatives segment. Members must report client margins by the specified last dates to avoid penalties.

## Key Points

- Covers 23 trade dates from 01-Jul-2026 to 31-Jul-2026
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically T+5 working days) and a penalty date (T+1 after reporting deadline)
- Penalty dates extend into August 2026 for late-July trade dates
- Issued by BSECL department under Settlement/RMS category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly schedule notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Members trading in the Currency Derivatives segment must submit client margin reports by the last date specified for each trade date. Failure to report by the deadline will result in penalties applied on the designated penalty date.
