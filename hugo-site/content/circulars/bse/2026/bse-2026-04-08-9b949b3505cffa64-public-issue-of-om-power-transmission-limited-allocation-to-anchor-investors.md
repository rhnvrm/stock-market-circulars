## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260408-31 dated April 8, 2026, regarding the public issue of OM Power Transmission Limited and the allocation of shares to anchor investors. The notice falls under the Company Related category for the Equity segment, handled by the Listing Business Relationship Department.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260408-31 issued on April 8, 2026
- Pertains to the public issue (IPO) of OM Power Transmission Limited
- Details the allocation made to anchor investors prior to the public subscription opening
- Anchor allocation document attached as 'Anchor_Allocation'
- Classified under Equity segment, Company Related category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an informational notice in accordance with SEBI's requirement for exchanges to publish anchor investor allocation details for public issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company and lead managers must ensure anchor investor allocations comply with SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment as per SEBI norms
- BSE members and market participants are advised to refer to the attached anchor allocation document for specific details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 8, 2026
- Anchor allocation precedes the public issue opening; refer to the attached document for exact subscription and allotment dates

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate and targeted impact, primarily relevant to investors and market participants involved in the OM Power Transmission Limited IPO. Anchor investor allocation details provide insight into institutional interest in the issue, which can serve as a sentiment indicator for retail investors. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
