## Summary

BSE has published Notice No. 20260302-20 announcing that the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Quanto Agroworld Limited has been filed with the BSE SME Exchange. The document is available on the exchange website for at least 21 days for public review and comments, in compliance with Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.

## Key Points

- Quanto Agroworld Limited filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE SME Exchange on 28 February 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (i.e., by 23 March 2026).
- Comments should be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The notice was issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, AVP, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in accordance with existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, which mandates public availability of draft offer documents for a minimum of 21 days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and other stakeholders wishing to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document of Quanto Agroworld Limited must do so within 21 days from 02 March 2026.
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Date of Filing by Company:** 28 February 2026
- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** 23 March 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with limited broad market impact. It is relevant primarily to investors and analysts interested in the upcoming SME IPO of Quanto Agroworld Limited. The 21-day public comment window provides transparency and allows stakeholders to flag concerns before the IPO proceeds further.
