## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities scheduled for Demat Auction under Settlement No. 835, dated 10 March 2026. The auction covers shortfall deliveries across a broad range of listed equity securities, with quantities ranging from 1 to 9,027 shares per scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 835 auction conducted on 10/03/2026
- 62 scrips listed across diverse sectors including pharma, energy, IT, FMCG, and finance
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 9,027 shares (SVARAJ TRADING)
- Large quantity auctions include: SVARAJ TRADING (9,027), TATA CHEMICALS (2,000), DANUBE INDUSTRIES (227), GAGAN GASES (226)
- Covers both large-cap (Infosys, Tata Consultancy Services, Dabur, Kotak Mahindra Bank) and small/mid-cap securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's existing demat auction framework for settlement shortfalls.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short deliveries in the listed securities must participate in the auction process
- Buyers of auctioned securities should ensure timely receipt and settlement
- All participants must comply with BSE auction settlement procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 10/03/2026 (Settlement No. 835)

## Impact Assessment

The auction primarily impacts trading members and clients involved in the specific delivery shortfalls for Settlement No. 835. The presence of large-cap names (Infosys, TCS, Dabur, Tata Chemicals, Kotak Mahindra Bank) alongside smaller scrips is notable but quantities are modest for blue-chips. The large quantity in SVARAJ TRADING (9,027 shares) and TATA CHEMICALS (2,000 shares) may see price impact in the auction. Overall market impact is low as this is a standard settlement auction mechanism.
