## Summary

BSE has announced that DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 07, 2026. The fund will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This notice reverses the previous restriction notified vide Notice No. 20251218-12. All six plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options) will be available for transactions.

## Key Points

- DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND reopening for transactions from January 07, 2026
- All transaction types available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Supersedes previous restriction under Notice No. 20251218-12

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update reversing a previous transaction restriction on the specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can resume normal transactions in the specified fund schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 06, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 07, 2026 - Reopening of DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a fund-specific operational change affecting only DSP NIFTY 500 INDEX FUND investors.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for existing and prospective investors in this specific fund. Investors who were unable to transact in this fund can now resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic plans.

**Operational Impact:** Members of BSE StAR MF Platform need to ensure their systems reflect the availability of this fund for transactions from January 07, 2026.
