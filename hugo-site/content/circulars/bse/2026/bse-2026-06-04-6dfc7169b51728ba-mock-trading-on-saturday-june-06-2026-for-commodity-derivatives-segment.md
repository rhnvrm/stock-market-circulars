## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. The session is intended for testing and familiarization purposes only. Trades executed during the mock session will not attract any margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and will not create any rights or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, June 06, 2026, for the Commodity Derivatives segment
- Log-in window: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM; Continuous Trading: 11:00 AM to 3:30 PM
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Members may alternatively use the UAT environment to fulfill regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Third-party trading platform users and in-house system developers via IML/ETI APIs can test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- Feedback to be submitted to BSE by 4:30 PM on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This notice operationalises the existing SEBI requirement for periodic system testing as per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020, and Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session or the UAT environment to fulfil their regulatory system-testing obligations
- Members using third-party or in-house trading platforms should use this session to test functionalities under exceptional market conditions
- Feedback must be submitted to BSE by 4:30 PM on June 06, 2026
- Contact channels: BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 / bse.tech@bseindia.com; BSE Help Desk at 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 / bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 06, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for Commodity Derivatives segment
  - Log-in: 10:15 AM – 11:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:00 AM – 3:30 PM
  - Feedback deadline: 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

Low impact. This is a routine operational exercise with no financial consequences for participants. Trades executed during the session carry no margin, settlement, or liability obligations. Participation is voluntary, and members may alternatively use the UAT environment. No changes to live trading rules, securities, or market structure are involved.
