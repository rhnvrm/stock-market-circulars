## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260424-18 prohibiting and suspending trading in the equity securities of Tiaan Consumer Limited (scrip code: 540108) with effect from Monday, April 27, 2026. The suspension follows a site visit by the Exchange during which the company was found to not exist at its registered office address.

## Key Points

- Trading in securities of Tiaan Consumer Ltd (scrip code 540108) is prohibited/suspended effective April 27, 2026
- The suspension is imposed as an interim measure following an Exchange-conducted site visit
- During the site visit, the company was found not to be present/operational at its registered office address
- All trading members and investors are directed to take note of this suspension
- Notice issued by Jayshree Soni (Deputy Vice President, Listing Compliance) and Shyam Bhagirath (Manager, Listing Compliance)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulations are introduced. The suspension is exercised under BSE's existing authority to impose interim measures when a listed company fails to maintain a valid registered office — a fundamental listing compliance requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members:** Must immediately cease facilitating trades in Tiaan Consumer Ltd (scrip code 540108) from April 27, 2026 onwards
- **Investors:** Must take note that trading in this security will not be permitted from the effective date
- No further action is specified for the company in this notice; additional regulatory proceedings may follow

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 24, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** Monday, April 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact regulatory action. Investors holding shares of Tiaan Consumer Ltd will be unable to exit their positions through normal exchange trading once the suspension takes effect. The company's inability to be located at its registered office is a serious compliance breach that typically signals deeper governance or financial distress. The interim nature of the suspension suggests further investigation and potentially permanent delisting proceedings may follow. Trading members should update their systems to block orders in scrip code 540108 before market open on April 27, 2026.
