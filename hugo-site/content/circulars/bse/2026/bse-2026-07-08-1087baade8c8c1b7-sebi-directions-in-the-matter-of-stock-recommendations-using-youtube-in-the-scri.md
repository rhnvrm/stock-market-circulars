## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all Trading Members informing them of SEBI's communication dated July 08, 2026 regarding the matter of stock recommendations using YouTube in the scrip of Sadhna Broadcast Limited. This is a continuation of earlier Exchange notices dated November 01, 2023 and May 29, 2025. SEBI has confirmed that a Final Order dated May 29, 2025 has been passed in this matter, and as a result, the directions contained in the earlier Confirmatory Order have ceased to exist.

## Key Points

- Matter pertains to alleged unauthorized stock recommendations made via YouTube concerning Sadhna Broadcast Limited.
- SEBI's Final Order dated May 29, 2025 has been passed, superseding the earlier Confirmatory Order.
- The directions under the Confirmatory Order no longer apply following issuance of the Final Order.
- This notice references two prior Exchange notices: No. 20231101-12 (dated November 01, 2023) and No. 20250529-47 (dated May 29, 2025).
- Attachments include the Confirmatory Order and the Final Order for Sadhna Broadcast Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice formalizes the closure/supersession of the Confirmatory Order previously in effect, following SEBI's issuance of a Final Order in the matter.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the update and ensure compliance with the current status of directions applicable to Sadhna Broadcast Limited, i.e., that the Confirmatory Order's directions are no longer in force following the Final Order.

## Important Dates

- November 01, 2023: Original Exchange Notice No. 20231101-12 issued.
- May 29, 2025: Exchange Notice No. 20250529-47 issued; SEBI Final Order dated same day.
- July 08, 2026: SEBI email confirming Final Order status and cessation of Confirmatory Order directions; current BSE notice issued.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative/compliance update for trading members concerning a single scrip (Sadhna Broadcast Limited) related to a specific enforcement matter around unauthorized YouTube-based stock recommendations. It does not affect broader trading operations, market structure, or other listed securities.
