## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures.

- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 21, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective From:** January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and potential traders of the specific debenture series (INE051307AZ7) of CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED. This is a routine corporate action for debt redemption and interest payment. Debenture holders on record as of January 29, 2026 will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment. The suspension ensures orderly settlement of the redemption process.
