## Summary

BSE Index Services has announced the addition of two newly listed stocks to BSE indices. Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited (ticker: 544799) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective July 1, 2026, and Anubhav Plast Limited (ticker: 544800) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective June 30, 2026. Both stocks are listed on BSE effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited (544799) listed on BSE mainboard effective June 29, 2026, and added to the BSE IPO index at the open of July 1, 2026.
- Anubhav Plast Limited (544800) listed on the SME platform of BSE effective June 29, 2026, and added to the BSE SME IPO index at the open of June 30, 2026.
- Both additions are reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 29, 2026.
- These additions are in reference to earlier notices: No. 20260625-29 (Turtlemint) and No. 20260625-25 (Anubhav Plast).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Clients with queries may contact bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Listing date for both Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited and Anubhav Plast Limited on BSE.
- **June 30, 2026**: Anubhav Plast Limited (544800) added to BSE SME IPO index at market open.
- **July 1, 2026**: Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited (544799) added to BSE IPO index at market open.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. These are standard post-listing index inclusions for newly debuted stocks on the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices. Index-tracking funds or ETFs benchmarked to these indices may rebalance their portfolios accordingly on the respective effective dates.
