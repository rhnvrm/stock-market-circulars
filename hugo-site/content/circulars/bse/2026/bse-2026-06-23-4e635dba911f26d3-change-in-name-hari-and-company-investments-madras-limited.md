## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited (a debt-listed company) has changed its name to **Vivriti Capital Limited**. The Scrip IDs and abbreviated names on the BOLT Plus System for all 22 listed debt securities will be updated effective **June 29, 2026**.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited** to **Vivriti Capital Limited**.
- A total of **22 debt security scrip codes** are affected across the name change.
- Scrip IDs and abbreviated names on BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new company name (prefix changes from legacy identifiers to VCL-based codes).
- The change is supported by a Certificate of Incorporation filed with the Exchange.
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations, BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new company name, Scrip IDs, and abbreviated names on the BOLT Plus System.
- References to "Hari and Company Investments Madras Limited" in internal records for these 22 scrip codes should be updated to "Vivriti Capital Limited".

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative change with moderate operational impact for trading members transacting in these 22 debt securities. The underlying securities and their terms remain unchanged; only the company name, Scrip IDs, and abbreviated names are updated. Members should ensure their trading and back-office systems are updated before June 29, 2026 to avoid order entry or settlement discrepancies.
