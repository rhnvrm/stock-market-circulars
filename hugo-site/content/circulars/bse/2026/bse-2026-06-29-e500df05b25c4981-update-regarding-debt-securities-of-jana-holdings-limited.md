## Summary

BSE has directed the suspension of trading in a debt security of Jana Holdings Limited (Scrip Code: 974867, ISIN: INE682V08158) with effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026. The suspension is on account of debt restructuring and follows the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20230601-29 dated June 1, 2023.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974867 (ISIN: INE682V08158) belonging to Jana Holdings Limited will be suspended for trading effective June 30, 2026.
- The suspension is triggered by ongoing debt restructuring of the issuer.
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20230601-29 dated June 1, 2023.
- Trading Members are advised to take note and inform their clients accordingly.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This action is an administrative measure by BSE's DCS-Listing department under its authority to suspend debt securities undergoing restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease trading in Scrip Code 974867 (ISIN: INE682V08158) from June 30, 2026 onwards.
- Members may contact Mr. Akash Shinde (+91 22 2272 8728) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com for further clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Date of this notice.
- **June 30, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Effective date of trading suspension for Scrip Code 974867.

## Impact Assessment

Holders of Jana Holdings Limited debt security (ISIN: INE682V08158) will be unable to trade on BSE from June 30, 2026. The suspension is linked to a debt restructuring process that has been ongoing since at least June 2023. Market impact is limited in scope as it pertains to a single debt instrument, but investors holding this security face illiquidity until the restructuring is resolved and trading is restored.
