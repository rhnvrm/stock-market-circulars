## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited (Exchange ticker: 544768) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE effective June 01, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of June 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited (ticker: 544768) lists on BSE on June 01, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on June 02, 2026
- This addition references prior Notice No. 20260529-40
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 01, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index composition update following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-linked fund managers and ETF operators tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of ticker 544768 effective June 02, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited listed on BSE
- **June 02, 2026**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine addition to the BSE SME IPO Index following a new SME listing. Impact is confined to passive funds and structured products benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index. No effect on broader equity indices or trading conditions.
