## Summary

Indian Toners & Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 523586) has announced a sub-division (stock split) of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of Rs.10/- face value will be split into five equity shares of Rs.2/- each. The record date for this corporate action is July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Indian Toners & Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 523586)
- Corporate action: Sub-division of equity shares (5-for-1 stock split)
- Existing face value: Rs.10/- per share
- New face value: Rs.2/- per share (five shares issued for each existing share)
- Record date: July 17, 2026
- Effective date: July 17, 2026
- Reference: DR-674/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a standard corporate action under BSE Listing Operations, notified to trading members via Notice No. 20260618-41.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the existing ISIN No. INE826B01018 (Rs.10/- paid-up) will cease to be valid for exchange transactions on or after July 17, 2026.
- A new ISIN for the Rs.2/- paid-up shares will be communicated by BSE through a separate notice prior to the record date.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ISIN once announced.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** July 17, 2026
- **Effective Date of Sub-Division:** July 17, 2026
- **ISIN INE826B01018 invalidity date:** On and after July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The 5-for-1 stock split will increase the number of equity shares outstanding fivefold while reducing the face value from Rs.10/- to Rs.2/-. This typically improves share liquidity and affordability for retail investors. Existing shareholders' economic value remains unchanged. The old ISIN will be deactivated on the record date, and a new ISIN will be assigned; trading members and depositories must update their systems accordingly to avoid settlement issues.
