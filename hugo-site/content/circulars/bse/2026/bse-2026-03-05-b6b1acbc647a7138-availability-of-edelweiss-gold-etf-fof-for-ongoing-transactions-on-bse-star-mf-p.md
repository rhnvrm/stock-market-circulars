## Summary

BSE has informed members that EDELWEISS GOLD ETF FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective March 06, 2026. This includes availability of SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- EDELWEISS GOLD ETF FOF reopens for ongoing subscription and redemption from March 06, 2026
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two scheme variants are covered: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- This notice references prior Notice No. 20260211-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice about the resumption of ongoing transactions for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the scheme availability for processing client transactions through BSE StAR MF Platform from March 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 06, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of EDELWEISS GOLD ETF FOF for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice is relevant to mutual fund distributors and investors who wish to transact in EDELWEISS GOLD ETF FOF via the BSE StAR MF Platform. The two affected schemes are:
