## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in the scrips of several listed companies and had written to them seeking clarification. Replies received from 11 companies are attached to this notice, while replies from 5 other companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- Companies that submitted clarifications (attached as Replies1 and Replies2):
  1. Anjani Foods Ltd (Scrip Code: 511153)
  2. BF Utilities Ltd (Scrip Code: 532430)
  3. CarTrade Tech Ltd (Scrip Code: 543333)
  4. Container Corporation of India Ltd (Scrip Code: 531344)
  5. Hinduja Global Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 532859)
  6. Shalimar Wires Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 532455)
  7. Shankara Buildpro Ltd (Scrip Code: 544517)
  8. Unjha Formulations Ltd (Scrip Code: 531762)
  9. La Tim Metal & Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 505693)
  10. Best Eastern Hotels Ltd (Scrip Code: 508664)
  11. C.E. Info Systems Ltd (Scrip Code: 543425)
- Companies from which replies are still awaited:
  1. CMX Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 532217)
  2. Mapro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 509762)
  3. Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 539117)
  4. VJTF Eduservices Ltd (Scrip Code: 509026)
  5. Kinetic Trust Ltd (Scrip Code: 531274)
- Notice issued to inform trading members/investors of the latest relevant information regarding these companies.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action required from trading members or investors. Companies from which replies are awaited may face continued exchange follow-up until clarifications are submitted.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 15 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260715-12).

## Impact Assessment

This notice provides transparency on companies under exchange surveillance for unusual price/volume activity. Investors in the 11 companies with submitted clarifications should review the attached replies for material information affecting valuation. The 5 companies with pending replies may warrant closer monitoring given the lack of disclosed explanation for price/volume movements.
