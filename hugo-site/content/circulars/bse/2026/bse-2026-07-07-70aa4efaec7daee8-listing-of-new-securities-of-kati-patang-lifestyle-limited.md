## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 17,36,093 new equity shares of Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 531126) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, July 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 14 per share (Rs. 10 face value, Rs. 24 issue price) to non-promoters on a preferential allotment basis.

## Key Points

- Company: Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited (Scrip Code: 531126)
- 17,36,093 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 14/- (issue price Rs. 24/-) to non-promoters on preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 51589932 to 53326024
- ISIN: INE237C01016
- Date of Allotment: 20/05/2026
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 15/01/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares already allotted on a preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond updating trading systems to reflect the newly listed shares effective the stated date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 20/05/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 08/07/2026 (Wednesday)
- Lock-in Expiry: 15/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited by 17,36,093 shares, though these shares remain under lock-in until January 15, 2027, limiting immediate free-float impact. The event is company-specific with no broader market implications.
