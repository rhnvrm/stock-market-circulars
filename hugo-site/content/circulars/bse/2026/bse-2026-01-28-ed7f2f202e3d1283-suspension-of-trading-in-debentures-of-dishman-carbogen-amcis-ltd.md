## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE385W07034, Code: 975834) of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the company's record date fixation for redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD
- Debenture ISIN: INE385W07034
- Debenture Code: 975834
- Debenture Type: 10.04% Non-Convertible Debentures
- Record Date: January 29, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 29, 2026
- Reference Number: DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture suspension due to put option redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD from January 29, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All trades in these debentures must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Circular issue date
- **January 29, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and potential traders of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD's Non-Convertible Debentures (INE385W07034). Existing debenture holders will have their put option exercised and receive interest payment based on the January 29, 2026 record date. Trading suspension prevents any transactions from this date, which is standard procedure for corporate actions involving redemption. This does not affect the company's equity shares or other securities.
