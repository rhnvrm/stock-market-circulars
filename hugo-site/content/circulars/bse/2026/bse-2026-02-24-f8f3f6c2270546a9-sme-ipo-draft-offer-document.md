## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Sham Foam Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document is available for public review for a minimum period of 21 days in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations. Members of the public are invited to submit comments on the draft document.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Sham Foam Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website as required under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are invited for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice (February 24, 2026)
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Full document accessible at the BSE SME portal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations, which mandates public availability of SME IPO draft offer documents for a minimum of 21 days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and investors may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections on the draft offer document must be submitted within 21 days from February 24, 2026 (i.e., by approximately March 17, 2026)
- Comments to be directed to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is a standard pre-IPO disclosure requirement for Sham Foam Limited's proposed listing on the BSE SME platform. The primary audience is prospective investors, analysts, and industry participants who may wish to review the company's offer document and provide feedback before the IPO proceeds. No immediate trading or compliance action is required from existing market participants.
