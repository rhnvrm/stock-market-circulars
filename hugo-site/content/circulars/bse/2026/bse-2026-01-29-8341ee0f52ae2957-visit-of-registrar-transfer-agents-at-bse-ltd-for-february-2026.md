## Summary

BSE has published the schedule for Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs) visiting BSE premises during February 2026. The circular lists companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd as their RTA. These visits facilitate coordination between RTAs, the exchange, and companies for handling investor grievances and related administrative matters.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule published for February 2026
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd is the RTA featured in this notice
- List includes 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate
- Companies span multiple sectors including finance, manufacturing, real estate, and technology
- Visit facilitates investor service coordination and grievance handling

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is informational for coordination purposes between BSE, RTAs, and listed companies.

## Important Dates

- Scheduled Month: February 2026
- Circular Date: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is an administrative notice.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. Facilitates routine coordination between RTAs and the exchange for investor services.

**Investor Services:** Positive for investor grievance resolution as it ensures RTA availability at exchange premises for handling complaints and queries.

The circular includes a comprehensive list of 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd, including:

- Financial Services: LKP Finance, IndiaNivesh, Milgrey Finance
- Manufacturing: Hind Rectifiers, Permanent Magnets, Safari Industries
- Real Estate: Marathon Nextgen Realty, Arihant Superstructures, Satra Properties
- Technology: Securekloud Technologies, Netlink Solutions
- Steel & Metals: Bajaj Steel Industries, Sharda Ispat, Scan Steels
