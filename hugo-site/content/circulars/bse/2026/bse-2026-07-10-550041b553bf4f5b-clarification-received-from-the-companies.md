## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in the securities of several listed companies and wrote to them seeking clarification. Replies received from four companies have been published as attachments, while replies from five other companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume movement in the recent past for nine listed companies.
- Replies have been received and attached from: C.E Info Systems Ltd (Scrip Code: 543425), KPT Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 505299), TCI Express Ltd (Scrip Code: 540212), and TVS Electronics Ltd (Scrip Code: 532513).
- Replies are still awaited from: CMX Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 532217), Mapro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 509762), SC Agrotech Ltd (Scrip Code: 526081), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 539117), and VJTF Eduservices Ltd (Scrip Code: 509026).
- Notice issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance Operations and Supervision (DOSS).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

The five companies with pending replies are expected to respond to BSE's clarification request regarding price/volume movement in their securities.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 10 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260710-25).

## Impact Assessment

This disclosure is intended to keep trading members and investors informed about companies under surveillance scrutiny for unusual price/volume activity. It signals heightened monitoring of the listed scrips but does not itself impose trading restrictions; investors should note that clarifications from four companies are now available while five remain pending.
