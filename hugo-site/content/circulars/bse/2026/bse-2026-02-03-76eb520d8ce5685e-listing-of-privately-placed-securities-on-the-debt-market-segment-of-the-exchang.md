## Summary

BSE has listed new privately placed debt securities on its Debt Segment effective February 3, 2026. The circular details fresh listings of commercial paper by Bajaj Finance Limited, Network 18 Media & Investments Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, and Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, along with further listing by 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited. Trading will be conducted in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- All securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The listing of privately placed debt securities on the BSE Debt Segment provides institutional investors access to short-term debt instruments issued by established financial and non-financial companies. The securities are rated A1+ by major rating agencies (CRISIL, ICRA, CARE, IND), indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments. Interest rates range from 7.47% to 8.45% p.a., reflecting current market conditions for commercial paper. No action is required by equity market participants or retail investors.
