## Summary

BSE has listed 20,27,972 new equity shares of Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543978, ISIN: INE05CZ01011) allotted to promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, February 25, 2026. The shares carry a lock-in restriction until September 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Total Shares Listed:** 20,27,972 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 143/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 133/- premium)
- **Allotment Type:** Preferential basis to promoters
- **Pari-Passu:** Shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Distribution Numbers:** 6,58,42,492 to 6,78,70,463
- **ISIN:** INE05CZ01011
- **Scrip Code:** 543978

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to the allotment of shares on a preferential basis to promoters under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these shares are available for trading from February 25, 2026.
- The 20,27,972 shares allotted to promoters are subject to a mandatory lock-in period and cannot be traded until September 30, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** February 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 20.28 lakh new shares on a preferential basis to promoters increases the total share capital of Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited. Since these shares are subject to a lock-in until September 2027, there is no immediate impact on free-float supply. Existing shareholders may note a dilution in their percentage shareholding. The preferential allotment at Rs. 143/- reflects the agreed valuation at the time of allotment in December 2025.
