## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260428-13 dated 28 April 2026 informing market participants about the surrender of trading membership by Vyanktesh Securities IMF Pvt. Ltd., holding Member No. 6504. This is an administrative notice under the Membership & Compliance department.

## Key Points

- Member Name: Vyanktesh Securities IMF Pvt. Ltd.
- Member Number: 6504
- Action: Surrender of Trading Membership
- Notice Number: 20260428-13
- Category: Others | Segment: General
- Department: Membership & Compliance
- No attachments included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a routine administrative communication regarding the cessation of trading membership for the specified member.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on other market participants. Counterparties and clients of Vyanktesh Securities IMF Pvt. Ltd. should be aware of the membership surrender and make alternative trading arrangements if applicable.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 28 April 2026
- Effective date of membership surrender: Not explicitly stated in the notice content provided.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to clients and counterparties of Vyanktesh Securities IMF Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6504). Existing clients will need to transfer their accounts or positions to another BSE member. There is no broader market or systemic impact anticipated from this single member surrender.
