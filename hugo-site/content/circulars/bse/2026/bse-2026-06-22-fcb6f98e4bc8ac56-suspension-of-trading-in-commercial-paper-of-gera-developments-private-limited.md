## Summary

BSE has directed trading members to cease dealing in the commercial paper of Gera Developments Private Limited. The company has fixed 23 June 2026 as the record date for redemption of this instrument, triggering a trading suspension effective the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading in the commercial paper of Gera Developments Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730989) is suspended
- Record date for redemption is set for 23 June 2026
- Ex-date coincides with the record date: 23 June 2026
- ISIN: INE965R14013; Scrip ID: GDPL-29-7-26-CP
- Settlement reference: 657/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE Listing and Debt Segment rules requiring suspension of trading in a commercial paper ahead of its redemption record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the commercial paper of Gera Developments Private Limited (ISIN: INE965R14013) on or after the ex-date of 23 June 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date / Ex-Date:** 23 June 2026
- **Settlement:** 657/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt market participants holding or intending to trade this specific commercial paper. The suspension is a routine procedural step ahead of full redemption; no broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
