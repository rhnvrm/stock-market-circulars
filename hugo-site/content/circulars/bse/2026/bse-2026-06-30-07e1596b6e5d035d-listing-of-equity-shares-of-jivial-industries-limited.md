## Summary

Jivial Industries Limited equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME segment. The scrip will be subject to Trade-for-Trade restrictions for the first 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Scrip Code:** 544805 | **Scrip ID:** JIVIAL
- **ISIN:** INE0V3T01017
- **Total Securities:** 46,69,600 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 196/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 186/-)
- **Market Lot:** 600 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 29, 2026
- **Group:** 'MT' Group under SME segment
- **Trade-for-Trade:** Applicable for first 10 trading days
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** Applicable on July 1, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in accordance with existing SEBI guidelines:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days post-listing
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring trading only in dematerialised form
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) as per Exchange notice 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade in minimum market lot of 600 equity shares; lot size may be modified by the Exchange with at least one month's prior notice
- All trades must be executed in dematerialised form only
- Trading members should be aware of Trade-for-Trade restrictions for the first 10 trading days
- Market Maker: **Sunflower Broking Private Limited** (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000195131; Market Maker Reg. No. SMEMM0328816102012)
- Registrar: **Bigshare Services Private Limited** (SEBI Reg. No. INR000001385)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Listing Date:** July 1, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Trade-for-Trade Period:** July 1, 2026 through approximately July 14, 2026 (10 trading days)
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** July 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing notice with medium impact on trading members dealing in SME securities. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days limits speculative trading and requires delivery-based settlement, which may constrain liquidity in the initial period. The SPOS on listing day is mandatory for price discovery. The Market Maker (Sunflower Broking Private Limited) is obligated to provide liquidity. Investors and trading members should note the minimum market lot of 600 shares and mandatory demat trading requirements. The company is based in Rajkot, Gujarat, and operates in the industrial/manufacturing sector (railing products).
