## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, June 20, 2026. The session is intended for testing and familiarization purposes, and trades executed during the session will not result in any margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be conducted from BSE's Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, June 20, 2026, for the EGR segment.
- Trading members using third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house systems via ETI APIs can use this session to test their trading applications.
- Members may participate in either the Mock Trading Session or the UAT environment to fulfill SEBI regulatory requirements per circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.
- Trades from the mock session carry no margin obligation, pay-in/pay-out obligation, or legal rights and liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 1:00 PM on the day of the session.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular references existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 (November 24, 2020) regarding testing of software used in or related to Trading and Risk Management, and BSE Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session to fulfill regulatory testing requirements under the referenced SEBI circular.
- Members must provide feedback by 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026, via the contact numbers or email IDs provided.
- For queries: BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 or bse.tech@bseindia.com; BSE Help Desk at 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 or bsehelp@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session from DR site for EGR segment.
- **08:15 AM – 09:00 AM:** Log-in window.
- **09:00 AM – 09:08 AM:** Pre-Open order entry period.
- **09:08 AM – 09:15 AM:** Pre-Open matching period.
- **09:15 AM – 12:00 PM:** Continuous trading session.
- **11:00 AM – 11:15 AM:** Block Deal Window.
- **12:00 PM – 12:10 PM:** Closing session.
- **12:10 PM – 12:20 PM:** Post-closing session.
- **By 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026:** Feedback submission deadline.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational exercise with no financial or market impact. The mock trading session does not create any trading obligations, margin requirements, or settlement liabilities. It is relevant only to EGR segment trading members who use third-party or in-house trading platforms and wish to test system connectivity and functionality against BSE's DR infrastructure.
