## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities, as listed in the attached Annexure I, have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II frameworks. Increased margin requirements will apply to these securities effective July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The specific list of affected securities is provided in Annexure I (not included in extracted text).
- ASM measures apply in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip moves out of the ASM framework, its price band will be reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains under another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the existing ASM framework established via prior BSE circulars (20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024; 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018; 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019; 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020; 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023; and 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic application of existing ST-ASM criteria to newly identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II, subject to a 100% cap) on all open positions as of July 22, 2026, and on new positions created from July 23, 2026 onward for securities listed in Annexure I.
- Members should monitor the annexure for the specific list of affected securities.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective Date of Revised Margins: July 23, 2026 (applicable to open positions as of July 22, 2026 and new positions thereafter)

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements will raise the cost of trading in the affected securities, potentially reducing liquidity and trading volumes for those specific stocks. Since the exact list of impacted securities is contained in an annexure not available in the extracted text, the overall market impact cannot be precisely quantified, but such ST-ASM actions typically affect small-cap or thinly traded stocks exhibiting high volatility or price movement patterns flagged by surveillance criteria.
