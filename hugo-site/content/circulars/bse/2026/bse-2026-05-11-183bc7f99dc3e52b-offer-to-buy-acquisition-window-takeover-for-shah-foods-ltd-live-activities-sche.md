## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Shah Foods Ltd (Scrip Code: 519031, Symbol: SHAHFOOD, ISIN: INE455D01012). The offer window is open from Wednesday, 13th May 2026 to Tuesday, 26th May 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 62.50 per share for up to 60,61,900 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Shah Foods Ltd | Symbol: SHAHFOOD | ISIN: INE455D01012 | Scrip Code: 519031
- **Offer Size:** Up to 60,61,900 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 62.50 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- **Offer Period:** 13th May 2026 (Opening) to 26th May 2026 (Closing)
- **Bid Offering Session Timings:** 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- **Give-up/Take-up (last day except closing):** 13th May 2026 to 25th May 2026, 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Give-up (last day of offer – 26th May 2026):** 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Take-up (last day of offer – 26th May 2026):** 9:15 AM – 4:00 PM
- Bidding is available for investors holding shares in demat form only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular provides the operational schedule under the existing OTB Acquisition Window framework as outlined in Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet.
- New login IDs and password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet must be requested through Trading Operations (022-22728005 / user.trdops@bseindia.com).
- Bidding categories supported: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (QIBs), NOH (NIB), CO (Company), IND (Individual).

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for trading members and custodians involved in the Shah Foods Ltd takeover offer. Members must ensure UCC compliance and use the designated iBBS/RTRMS platforms for bid submission. The offer price of Rs. 62.50 against a face value of Rs. 10.00 represents a significant premium. Broader market impact is limited to participants trading in SHAHFOOD (Scrip Code: 519031) during the defined offer window. Extranet files for bid acceptance will only be generated upon confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure.
