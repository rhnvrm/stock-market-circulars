## Summary

BSE has informed its members about SEBI's revised nomination norms for demat accounts and mutual fund folios, issued vide SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676 dated May 29, 2026. This follows earlier SEBI directions from January 10, 2025, and subsequent exchange notices, including BSE notice no. 20250316-3 dated March 16, 2025, all aimed at facilitating ease of doing investments for investors.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued modified norms for nomination in demat accounts and mutual fund folios to further ease the investment process for investors.
- The original SEBI directions on this subject were issued on January 10, 2025 (circular no. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/ON/2025/01650).
- BSE had previously communicated these directions via notice no. 20250316-3 dated March 16, 2025.
- Members are advised to take note of the updated SEBI circular and ensure necessary compliance wherever applicable.
- Any further changes or clarifications will be communicated by the Exchange.

## Regulatory Changes

SEBI has issued updated/modified nomination norms for:
- Demat accounts
- Mutual fund folios

The regulatory basis is SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676 dated May 29, 2026, which supersedes or supplements earlier directions on the same subject.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (trading members/brokers) must review the SEBI circular dated May 29, 2026, in its entirety.
- Members must ensure compliance with the modified nomination norms wherever applicable to their operations.
- Members dealing in BSE StAR Mutual Fund platform should align their processes with the updated norms.
- For queries related to BSE StAR Mutual Fund Operations, members may contact the Mutual Fund Helpdesk at 022-45720450/650 and 022-69158550 on business days between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m., or raise queries through the CRS portal.

## Important Dates

- **January 10, 2025**: Original SEBI circular (SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/ON/2025/01650) issued on nomination norms.
- **March 16, 2025**: BSE communicated the original SEBI directions via notice no. 20250316-3.
- **May 29, 2026**: SEBI issued modified/updated nomination norms circular (SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676).
- **June 12, 2026**: BSE issued this notice communicating the updated norms to members.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on mutual fund intermediaries and depository participants. Members operating on the BSE StAR Mutual Fund platform or managing demat accounts will need to update their processes to align with the revised nomination norms. The changes are part of SEBI's ongoing ease-of-doing-investments initiative and are investor-friendly in nature, aimed at simplifying nomination procedures. No immediate market-wide trading disruption is expected, but non-compliance could attract regulatory scrutiny.
