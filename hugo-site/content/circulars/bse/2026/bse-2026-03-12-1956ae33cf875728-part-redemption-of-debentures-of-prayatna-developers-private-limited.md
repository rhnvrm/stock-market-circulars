## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Prayatna Developers Private Limited has fixed a record date of 16 March 2026 for the purpose of part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will be with a reduced face value from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: Prayatna Developers Private Limited
- Debenture Series: PDPL-7.30%-31-12-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE925U07023 (BSE Code: 973756)
- Record Date: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 380,280 per debenture
- Effective Date: 16 March 2026
- Settlement: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under BSE's debt segment operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures of Prayatna Developers Private Limited will trade with the reduced face value of Rs. 380,280 per debenture with effect from 16 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from the effective settlement date DR-840/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 16 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-840/2025-2026
- **Debenture Maturity Date:** 31 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine part redemption event for privately placed NCDs of a single private limited company. The impact is limited to holders of the specific debenture series (ISIN: INE925U07023). Trading members dealing in this instrument must update face value records effective 16 March 2026. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
