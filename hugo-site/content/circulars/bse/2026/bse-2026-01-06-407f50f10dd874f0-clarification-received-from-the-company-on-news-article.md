## Summary

BSE received clarifications from two companies regarding news articles: (1) Reliance Industries Limited clarified that Bloomberg corrected their news report about Russian oil cargo deliveries to Jamnagar refinery, confirming vessels discharged elsewhere; (2) Indian Energy Exchange Limited confirmed the news article referenced a public hearing at APTEL scheduled for January 6, 2026, regarding their petition challenging CERC's market coupling order dated July 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Reliance Industries denied receiving Russian oil at Jamnagar refinery in recent weeks
- Bloomberg initially published incorrect report claiming three vessels laden with Russian oil heading to Reliance's refinery
- Bloomberg subsequently corrected their report, clarifying Russian oil cargo discharged elsewhere
- Reliance confirmed no linkage between stock price movement and news article
- Indian Energy Exchange clarified news about APTEL hearing on market coupling norms
- IEX hearing relates to petition challenging CERC's suo-motu order dated July 23, 2025
- Both companies confirmed no undisclosed information that could explain trading movements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Both companies stated no information withheld that should have been announced to stock exchanges
- Companies will continue making disclosures as per regulatory obligations

## Important Dates

- **January 6, 2026**: Date of BSE query to companies
- **January 6, 2026**: Date of company clarifications
- **January 6, 2026**: APTEL hearing date for IEX petition
- **July 23, 2025**: Date of CERC suo-motu order on market coupling (referenced by IEX)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are routine clarifications addressing news articles. Reliance corrected misinformation about Russian oil deliveries, while IEX provided context about a public legal proceeding. Both companies confirmed no undisclosed material information.

**Operational Impact**: None - No operational changes or requirements arising from these clarifications.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Clarifications provide transparency but contain no new material information affecting investment decisions. Stock price movements, if any, are not linked to undisclosed information per company statements.
