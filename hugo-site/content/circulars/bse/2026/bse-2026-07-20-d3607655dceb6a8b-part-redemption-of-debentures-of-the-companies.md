## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that seven companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or part redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Following the respective ex-dates, these debentures will trade with a reduced face value as specified for each security.

## Key Points

- Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited (12%-16-9-27-PVT): Two ISINs (INE08XP07357, INE08XP07365), Record Date 01/08/2026, Ex Date 31/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 59,086/- per debenture
- Vivriti Capital Limited (8.74%-24-01-31, ISIN INE01HV07544): Record Date 09/08/2026, Ex Date 07/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 75,724/- per debenture
- Annapurna Finance Private Limited (10.95%-22-07-27-PVT, ISIN INE515Q07640): Record Date 07/08/2026, Ex Date 07/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 45,833.29 per debenture
- Laxmi India Finance Limited (10.50%-24-02-29-PVT, ISIN INE06WU07072): Record Date 09/08/2026, Ex Date 07/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 8,333.34 per debenture
- Keertana Finserv Limited (11.40%-26-5-27-PVT, ISIN INE0NES07147): Record Date 11/08/2026, Ex Date 11/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 30,000/- per debenture
- OXYZO Financial Services Limited (9.75%-27-3-27-PVT, ISIN INE04VS07388): Record Date 12/08/2026, Ex Date 12/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 33,333/- per debenture
- Pahal Financial Services Private Limited (12.4%-23-1-28-PVT, ISIN INE514Q07395): Record Date 12/08/2026, Ex Date 12/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 73,913.04 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine notice under BSE Listing Operations for corporate debt actions on already-listed NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members holding or transacting in these debentures must ensure trades reflect the reduced face value from the respective ex-dates and settle transactions per the record dates specified above.

## Important Dates

- 31 Jul 2026: Ex-date for Akara Capital Advisors NCDs (both ISINs)
- 01 Aug 2026: Record date for Akara Capital Advisors NCDs
- 07 Aug 2026: Ex-date and Record date for Annapurna Finance NCD; Ex-date for Vivriti Capital and Laxmi India Finance NCDs
- 09 Aug 2026: Record date for Vivriti Capital and Laxmi India Finance NCDs
- 11 Aug 2026: Record date and Ex-date for Keertana Finserv NCD
- 12 Aug 2026: Record date and Ex-date for OXYZO Financial Services and Pahal Financial Services NCDs

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains only to debt segment instruments (NCDs) of specific privately-placed issuers, not listed equity shares. Impact is limited to bondholders and trading members dealing in these specific debt securities, who need to account for the reduced face value and adjusted settlement mechanics from the respective ex-dates.
