## Summary

BSE has notified the listing of 98,16,000 new equity shares of ACE MEN ENGG WORKS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 539661) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from January 5, 2026, and are subject to a lock-in period until July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 98,16,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 54/-
- Issue price: Rs. 64/- per share
- Allotment date: November 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 5, 2026
- ISIN: INE023R01018
- Distinctive Numbers: 3098501 to 12914500
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the new securities available for trading and the associated lock-in restrictions when processing trades.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a company-specific listing notification. The preferential allotment increases the company's equity base by 98.16 lakh shares, which will be locked-in until July 2026, preventing immediate trading of these shares by allottees.
