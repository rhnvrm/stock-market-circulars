## Summary

Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 497) has applied to surrender its membership of BSE in accordance with the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations. Trading members who wish to object to this surrender must do so in writing within 15 days of this notice.

## Key Points

- Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 497) has formally applied to surrender its BSE membership.
- The application is being processed in accordance with BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.
- Any trading member may raise objections in writing to the Membership Department.
- Objections must be submitted on or before **March 18, 2026**.
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice follows the standard BSE membership surrender procedure under existing Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to object to the membership surrender must submit written objections to the BSE Membership Department within 15 days from the date of this notice (i.e., by March 18, 2026).
- Objections and clarifications should be directed to: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Objection Deadline:** March 18, 2026 (15 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

The surrender of Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd.'s BSE membership has limited direct market impact. Clients and counterparties of the member should make alternative arrangements for trading on BSE. The 15-day objection window provides an opportunity for any affected trading members to raise concerns before the surrender is finalised.
