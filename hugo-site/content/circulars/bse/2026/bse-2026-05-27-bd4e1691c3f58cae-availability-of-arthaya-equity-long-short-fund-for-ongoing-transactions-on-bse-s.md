## Summary

BSE has informed members that ARTHAYA EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from May 29, 2026. This is pursuant to Notice No. 20260430-42 dated April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- ARTHAYA EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 29, 2026
- Transactions available: subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans
- All six variants are classified under the EQUITY scheme category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This notice is an operational update following the prior Notice No. 20260430-42 dated April 30, 2026, which had initially addressed the fund's availability status.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of ARTHAYA EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND schemes for ongoing transactions from May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Notice issued
- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **April 30, 2026**: Prior related notice (No. 20260430-42)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice affecting only ARTHAYA EQUITY LONG SHORT FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors and distributors using BSE StAR MF can resume transacting in the fund's six scheme variants (Regular and Direct plans, across Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options) from May 29, 2026. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
