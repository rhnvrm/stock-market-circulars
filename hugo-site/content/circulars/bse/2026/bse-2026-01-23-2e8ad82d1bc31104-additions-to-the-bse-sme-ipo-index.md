## Summary

ARITAS VINYL LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544683) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE on Friday, January 23, 2026. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- ARITAS VINYL LIMITED listed on BSE effective January 23, 2026
- Exchange ticker: 544683
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Addition effective at market open on January 27, 2026
- Action included in corporate action file (*.SDE)

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index constituent addition.

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs may need to adjust holdings to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Listing date of ARITAS VINYL LIMITED on BSE
- **January 27, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index. Index funds tracking this index will need to include ARITAS VINYL LIMITED in their portfolios. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.
