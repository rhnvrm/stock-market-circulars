## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260608-25 dated June 8, 2026, noting significant price/volume movements in 9 equity scrips. The Exchange had written to these companies seeking relevant information for trading members and investors. Replies have been received from 6 companies, while 3 companies have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 9 listed companies' scrips
- Exchange wrote to these companies to ensure investors have access to latest relevant information
- 6 companies have submitted their clarifications (replies attached to the notice)
- 3 companies' replies are still awaited
- Notice issued by Surveillance department under Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance communication requiring companies to explain unusual market activity in their securities.

## Compliance Requirements

**Replies Received:**
1. Man Industries (India) Ltd — Scrip Code: 513269
2. Network 18 Media & Investments Ltd — Scrip Code: 532798
3. RITES Ltd — Scrip Code: 541556
4. Tata Teleservices (Maharashtra) Ltd — Scrip Code: 532371
5. Kwality Walls (India) Ltd — Scrip Code: 544622
6. York Exports Ltd — Scrip Code: 530675

**Replies Awaited:**
1. Elango Industries Ltd — Scrip Code: 513452
2. Colinz Laboratories Ltd — Scrip Code: 531210
3. SRU Steels Ltd — Scrip Code: 540914

The three companies with pending replies are expected to submit clarifications to BSE Surveillance.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 8, 2026
- No explicit deadline mentioned for pending replies; companies are expected to respond promptly to BSE's surveillance queries.

## Impact Assessment

Investors and trading members in the 6 companies that have responded now have access to official management clarifications explaining the recent price/volume movements. For the 3 companies with pending replies (Elango Industries, Colinz Laboratories, SRU Steels), traders should exercise caution as no official explanation has been provided yet for the unusual market activity. This notice serves as a transparency measure by BSE to protect retail investors from uninformed trading decisions.
