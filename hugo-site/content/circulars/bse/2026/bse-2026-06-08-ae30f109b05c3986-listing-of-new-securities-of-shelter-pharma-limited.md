## Summary

BSE has listed 2,25,000 new equity shares of Shelter Pharma Limited (Scrip Code: 543963, ISIN: INE013V01011) for trading with effect from Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters via conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 42.25 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 32.25).

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 2,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 42.25/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 32.25 premium)
- **Basis of issue:** Preferential allotment to non-promoters via conversion of warrants
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Distinctive numbers:** 16611793 to 16836792
- **Trading effective from:** June 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to preferential allotment under warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 9, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the portions detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment (1,14,000 shares):** May 1, 2026
- **Date of allotment (1,11,000 shares):** May 5, 2026
- **Trading effective date:** June 9, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (72,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 16611793–16683792):** January 30, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (42,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 16683793–16725792):** January 29, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (1,11,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 16725793–16836792):** January 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 2,25,000 new shares to the tradeable float of Shelter Pharma Limited, representing a preferential allotment to non-promoters. All newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until late January 2027, so the immediate free-float impact is negligible. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders and active traders of this scrip.
