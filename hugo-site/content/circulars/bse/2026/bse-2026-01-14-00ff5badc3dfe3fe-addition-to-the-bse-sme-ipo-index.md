## Summary

YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544676) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, January 16, 2026. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Wednesday, January 14, 2026. The addition is a standard index maintenance action following a new SME listing.

## Key Points

- YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (544676) listed on BSE on January 14, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective January 16, 2026 at market open
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Index queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and investors following the BSE SME IPO Index should note the addition for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED listing date on BSE
- **January 16, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index inclusion following an SME IPO listing. Impact limited to:
- Index tracking funds that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index may need to include this stock
- Increased visibility for YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED among index-focused investors
- No trading restrictions or operational changes for market participants
