## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd has fixed a record date for the payment of interest and redemption of its 9.61% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE918K07PN2). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures effective April 8, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd's NCD series NWFL-9.61%-23-4-26-PVT (Scrip Code: 976140, ISIN: INE918K07PN2) is suspended.
- Record date and ex-date are both fixed at April 8, 2026.
- Settlement reference: DR 606/2026-2027.
- The suspension is for the purpose of interest payment and full redemption of the debentures.
- Issued by Listing Compliance and Operations department, signed by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's listing compliance framework for corporate actions in the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (Scrip Code 976140) on or after the ex-date of April 8, 2026.
- Members should ensure all pending trades in this security are settled in accordance with BSE settlement procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 7, 2026
- **Record Date:** April 8, 2026
- **Ex-Date:** April 8, 2026
- **Settlement:** DR 606/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited in scope, affecting only the single NCD series NWFL-9.61%-23-4-26-PVT issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Ltd. Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and principal redemption proceeds as per the company's schedule. Market impact is low as this is a privately placed debt instrument approaching maturity, and trading activity in such securities is typically low.
