## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department issued Notice No. 20260514-24 dated 14 May 2026, informing members and investors that the Exchange had sought a clarification from Adani Power Ltd (Scrip Code: 533096) concerning a news article published on Moneycontrol. The clarification has been received from the company.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance Department sought clarification from Adani Power Ltd (Scrip Code: 533096) regarding a news article on moneycontrol.com.
- The clarification has been received from the company.
- The notice is categorized under Company Related, Equity Segment, and issued by the DOSS department.
- Signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Arpita Joshi (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice to members and investors regarding the verification of news accuracy.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 14 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine surveillance action by BSE to verify media reports about Adani Power Ltd and ensure accurate information is available to investors. The receipt of clarification from the company indicates the matter is resolved at the exchange level. No trading restrictions or penalties are mentioned.
