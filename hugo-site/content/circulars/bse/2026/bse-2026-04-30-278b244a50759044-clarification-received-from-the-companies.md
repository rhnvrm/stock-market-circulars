## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has issued Notice No. 20260430-17 dated April 30, 2026, regarding significant price and volume movements observed in 22 listed companies. In the interest of informed trading, BSE had written to these companies seeking clarifications. Replies have been received from 12 companies, while 10 others are yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed unusual price/volume movement in 22 equity scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies.
- Replies have been received from 12 companies: Abans Enterprises Ltd, Accretion Nutraveda Ltd, AuSom Enterprise Ltd, Cohance Lifesciences Ltd, HCP Plastene Bulkpack Ltd, Marg Techno Projects Ltd, Nagreeka Exports Ltd, PCS Technology Ltd, Prima Agro Ltd, Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd, Sampat Aluminium Ltd, and Tenneco Clean Air India Ltd.
- Replies are awaited from 10 companies: Archidply Decor Ltd, Deep Polymers Ltd, Dhillon Freight Carrier Ltd, Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd, Greenpanel Industries Ltd, Hindustan Appliances Ltd, Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd, Resourceful Automobile Ltd, ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd, and Dharani Finance Ltd.
- The notice is issued under the Equity segment by the Surveillance Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard surveillance-driven disclosure mechanism under BSE's existing framework to ensure market transparency.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 10 companies for which replies are still awaited must submit their clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies from companies before making investment decisions in the affected scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 30, 2026
- Replies already received as of: April 30, 2026
- No explicit deadline stated for pending replies, but prompt submission is implied.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to investors and traders holding or considering positions in the 22 named scrips. The receipt of clarifications from 12 companies provides some transparency, but the 10 pending responses may leave uncertainty around those stocks. Investors should review the attached company replies before trading. No broader market-wide impact is expected; effect is limited to the specific securities mentioned.
