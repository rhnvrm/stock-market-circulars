## Summary

BSE has listed 1,14,00,000 new equity shares of Nitco Limited (Scrip Code: 532722) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, May 22, 2026. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 1,14,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 82.25/- (issue price Rs. 92.25/-)
- Shares allotted to Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 22,91,16,106 to 24,05,16,105
- ISIN: INE858F01012
- All 1,14,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2027
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility for trading from May 22, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies to all 1,14,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 229116106 to 240516105) until November 30, 2027; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 23, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total equity share count of Nitco Limited by 1,14,00,000 shares, resulting in dilution of existing shareholders. Since all newly listed shares are under promoter lock-in until November 2027, there is no immediate floating stock addition. Market impact is moderate and limited to existing shareholders' dilution; no near-term supply-side pressure is expected from these shares.
