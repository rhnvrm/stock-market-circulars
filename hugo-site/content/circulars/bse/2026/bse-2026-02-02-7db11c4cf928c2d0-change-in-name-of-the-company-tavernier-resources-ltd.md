## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Tavernier Resources Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Eforu Entertainment Limited with effect from October 27, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dewan Industries Limited and maintains its CIN L93290MH1994PLC193901.

## Key Points

- Old Name: Tavernier Resources Limited
- New Name: Eforu Entertainment Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L93290MH1994PLC193901
- Original incorporation name: Dewan Industries Limited
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered Address: Level 4, A-Wing, Dynasty Business Park, Andheri Kurla Road, Andheri East, J.B. Nagar, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure completed in accordance with the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Tavernier Resources Limited) along with the new name (Eforu Entertainment Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 27, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 27, 2025 (digitally signed at 23:33:23 IST)
- **Dual Name Display Period**: October 27, 2025 to October 27, 2027 (2 years)

## Impact Assessment

This name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. It is purely an administrative change with no operational or financial impact on the company's business activities or its obligations to shareholders, creditors, or other parties. The company continues to operate under the same CIN and registered address.
