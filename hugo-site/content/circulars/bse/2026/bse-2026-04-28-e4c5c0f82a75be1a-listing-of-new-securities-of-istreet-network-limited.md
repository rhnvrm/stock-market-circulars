## Summary

BSE has listed 4,51,66,668 new equity shares of iStreet Network Limited (Scrip Code: 524622) effective April 29, 2026. These shares, issued at Rs. 6/- per share (face value Rs. 4/- with a premium of Rs. 2/-), were allotted on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters across three allotment dates in late 2025.

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 4,51,66,668 equity shares
- **Issue price:** Rs. 6/- per share (Rs. 4/- face value + Rs. 2/- premium)
- **ISIN:** INE532B01020
- **Scrip Code:** 524622
- **Trading commencement:** Wednesday, April 29, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in details applicable as per annexure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities available for trading from April 29, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specified shares as per the attached annexure; trading members must ensure compliance with lock-in periods

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| First allotment (93,83,332 shares) | 28 November 2025 |
| Second allotment (1,37,83,335 shares) | 30 November 2025 |
| Third allotment (2,20,00,001 shares) | 04 December 2025 |
| Trading commencement on BSE | 29 April 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 4.52 crore new equity shares on a preferential basis represents a significant capital raise for iStreet Network Limited. Existing shareholders face equity dilution. The shares are allotted at Rs. 6/-, which provides context for market pricing. Lock-in restrictions on a portion of these shares (as per annexure) will limit near-term selling pressure from allottees. Overall market impact is moderate and company-specific.
