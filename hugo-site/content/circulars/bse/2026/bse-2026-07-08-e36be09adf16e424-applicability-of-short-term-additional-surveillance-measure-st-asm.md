## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II, as per the attached Annexure I. Enhanced margin requirements will apply to these securities effective July 10, 2026, in line with the ASM framework established through prior exchange notices.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I.
- Securities listed in Annexure I have also satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage II.
- ST-ASM Stage I: applicable margin shall be 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- ST-ASM Stage II: applicable margin shall be 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on July 9, 2026, and new positions created from July 10, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band will be reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the continuing ASM framework established via BSE notices dated September 20, 2024 (20240920-71), October 27, 2018 (20181027-1), July 19, 2019 (20190719-33), December 4, 2020 (20201204-56), September 25, 2023 (20230925-49), and September 20, 2024 (20240920-63). No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic application of existing ST-ASM criteria to newly identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II securities, subject to the 100% cap) on all open and new positions in the listed securities from July 10, 2026. Members should refer to Annexure I for the specific list of affected securities and direct any clarifications to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 9, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to the new margin requirements.
- July 10, 2026: Effective date for enhanced ST-ASM margin rates on all open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II will face significantly higher margin requirements (50% and 100% respectively), which typically reduces trading leverage and liquidity in these scrips and may curb speculative activity. The impact is limited to the specific securities named in Annexure I rather than the broader market, and the measure is a standard, recurring surveillance mechanism rather than a novel regulatory change.
