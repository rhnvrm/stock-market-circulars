## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,00,000 new equity shares of Emkay Global Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 532737) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, July 22, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 1,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 229.5/- (issue price Rs. 239.5/-) to promoters on a preferential basis.
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers: 27130132 to 27230131.
- Date of allotment: 27/05/2026.
- ISIN: INE296H01011.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members other than updating systems to reflect the additional listed quantity effective the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: 27 May 2026
- Effective date of listing/trading: 22 July 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 23 January 2028

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 1,00,000 shares is a small incremental increase to Emkay Global Financial Services Ltd's listed equity base and represents a preferential allotment to promoters rather than a public issue. The shares are subject to lock-in until 23 January 2028, limiting near-term sale pressure. Overall market impact is expected to be minimal given the limited share count involved.
