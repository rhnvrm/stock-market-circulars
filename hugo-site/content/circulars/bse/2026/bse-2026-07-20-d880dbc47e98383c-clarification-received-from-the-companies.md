## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in a set of scrips and had written to the respective companies seeking clarification. Replies have been received from four companies and are attached to the notice, while replies from eight other companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE flagged unusual price/volume activity in 12 listed scrips and sought clarification from each company.
- Companies that have responded (replies attached): B & A Ltd (Scrip Code: 508136), Global Surfaces Ltd (Scrip Code: 543829), Responsive Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 505509), Schneider Electric President Systems Ltd (Scrip Code: 544786).
- Companies whose replies are still awaited: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Darjeeling Industries Ltd (539770), Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (544156), Kinetic Trust Ltd (531274), Liberty Shoes Ltd (526596), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117), VJTF Eduservices Ltd (509026).
- The notice is issued for investor and trading member awareness, not as a regulatory penalty or trading restriction.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action required from trading members or investors. Companies whose replies are awaited are expected to respond to BSE's queries; investors should review the attached replies for context before trading in the flagged scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 20 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260720-20).

## Impact Assessment

The disclosure provides transparency around unusual trading activity in the listed scrips and may influence short-term investor sentiment or trading behavior in these stocks. The scrips with pending replies warrant closer monitoring given the lack of company clarification on the observed price/volume movement.
