## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that REC Limited's privately placed non-convertible debentures (Scrip Code 977856, ISIN INE020B08GD4) have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 11, 2026. The instrument carries a coupon of 7.38% p.a. and matures on February 28, 2029.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** REC Limited
- **Scrip Code / ID:** 977856 / 738RECL29 (Detail Name: RECL-7.38%-28-2-29-PVT)
- **ISIN:** INE020B08GD4
- **Quantity Listed:** 3,00,000 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (Market Lot: 1)
- **Issue / Face / Paidup Value:** Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- **Coupon:** 7.38% per annum, paid annually
- **Credit Rating:** CARE AAA/Stable | CRISIL AAA/STABLE | ICRA AAA/STABLE | IND AAA/Stable
- **Tick size:** 1 paise
- **Trading form:** Dematerialised only
- For placement memorandum details, refer to: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's debt market admission process for privately placed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this security exclusively in dematerialised form under ISIN INE020B08GD4.
- Members requiring clarification may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing with no broader market impact. The instrument is issued by REC Limited (a AAA-rated public sector NBFC) on a private placement basis. Institutional and eligible investors can now trade the NCD on the BSE Debt Segment. Put/call options, if any, are governed by the Disclosure Document.
