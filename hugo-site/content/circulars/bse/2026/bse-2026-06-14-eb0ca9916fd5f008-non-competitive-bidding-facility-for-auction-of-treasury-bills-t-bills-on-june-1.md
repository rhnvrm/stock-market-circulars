## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding facility for the Government of India's auction of three Treasury Bills on June 17, 2026. Trading members and direct investors can place bids through the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com). The auction covers 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with a combined issue size of Rs. 24,000 crore.

## Key Points

- GoI is auctioning three Treasury Bills on June 17, 2026, as announced by RBI on June 12, 2026.
- Bidding is available via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based system.
- All three T-Bills are new issuances (not re-issues).
- Minimum lot size is Rs. 100 and minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 for all securities.
- This circular is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established by circular no. 20180423-42 (April 26, 2018).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on the iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com) to place bids.
- Members must submit bids by June 17, 2026, 8:00 am.
- Direct investors must submit bids by June 16, 2026, 5:00 pm.
- Bids must conform to the minimum lot (Rs. 100) and maximum amount limits per security.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |
|---|---|
| Bid Collection Start | June 15, 2026, 10:00 am (24-hour availability) |
| Bid Collection End (Direct Investors) | June 16, 2026, 5:00 pm |
| Bid Collection End (Members) | June 17, 2026, 8:00 am |
| Auction Date | June 17, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 18, 2026 |
