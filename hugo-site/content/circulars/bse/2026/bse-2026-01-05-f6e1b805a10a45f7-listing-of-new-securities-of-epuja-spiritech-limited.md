## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 23,67,647 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092) effective from January 6, 2026. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 3.40 per share (face value Re. 1/- with premium of Rs. 2.40). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to a lock-in period until July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 23,67,647 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 3.40 per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 2.40 premium)
- Allotment in two tranches: 15,14,706 shares on July 18, 2025 and 8,52,941 shares on July 26, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 86679043 to 89046689
- ISIN: INE807D01030
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Mode: Conversion of warrants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from January 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date (Tranche 1):** July 18, 2025
- **Allotment Date (Tranche 2):** July 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** January 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** July 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact. The preferential allotment represents a capital raising exercise through warrant conversion. All 23,67,647 shares are subject to lock-in until July 9, 2026, preventing immediate selling pressure. The shares will rank equally with existing equity shares, maintaining uniform shareholder rights.
