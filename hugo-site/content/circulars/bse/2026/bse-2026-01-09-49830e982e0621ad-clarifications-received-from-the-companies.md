## Summary

BSE published clarifications from three companies - Signatureglobal (India) Limited, Alkyl Amines Chemicals Limited, and GNA Axles Limited - responding to surveillance queries regarding significant increases in trading volume and price movements in their securities. All companies confirmed compliance with disclosure obligations and stated that the volume/price movements were market-driven with no undisclosed price-sensitive information pending.

## Key Points

- Signatureglobal (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 543990) responded to BSE letter regarding volume increase, confirming movements are purely market-driven
- Alkyl Amines Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 506767) clarified significant volume increase across exchanges, stating no pending announcements
- GNA Axles Limited (Scrip Codes: BSE 540124, NSE GNA) addressed price movement queries from both BSE and NSE
- All companies confirmed full compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No price-sensitive information pending disclosure by any of the companies
- Companies are not aware of specific information causing the volume/price movements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular documents company responses to routine surveillance queries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Confirmation of compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015 required
- Disclosure of any pending price-sensitive information or announcements mandatory
- Companies must state whether movements are market-driven or due to specific corporate developments

## Important Dates

- January 9, 2026: Date of clarifications from all three companies
- January 8, 2026: Date of surveillance queries from BSE/NSE to companies

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine surveillance clarifications confirming no undisclosed material information. The confirmations provide transparency to market participants that recent volume/price increases in these securities are market-driven and not due to pending corporate announcements. This type of disclosure helps maintain market integrity and investor confidence by ensuring all material information has been properly disclosed per regulatory requirements.
