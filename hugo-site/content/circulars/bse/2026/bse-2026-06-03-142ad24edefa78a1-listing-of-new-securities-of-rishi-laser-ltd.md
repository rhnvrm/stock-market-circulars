## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,00,000 new equity shares of Rishi Laser Ltd. (Scrip Code: 526861, ISIN: INE988D01012) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from June 4, 2026. The shares were issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 150/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 140/-).

## Key Points

- 8,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 140/- (total issue price Rs. 150/-) on a preferential basis via warrant conversion.
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distribution numbers: 9192601 to 9992600.
- Date of allotment: May 7, 2026.
- Trading permitted from June 4, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and applicable lock-in restrictions before facilitating trades in these shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: May 7, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: June 4, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,25,000 shares; Dist. Nos. 9192601–9317600)**: December 12, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (6,75,000 shares; Dist. Nos. 9317601–9992600)**: December 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 8,00,000 shares represents a modest increase in the free float of Rishi Laser Ltd. The majority of shares (6,75,000) are locked in until December 2026, limiting immediate market impact. The smaller tranche (1,25,000 shares) carries a longer lock-in until December 2027, likely representing the promoter portion. Overall market impact is minimal given the small size of the issuance.
