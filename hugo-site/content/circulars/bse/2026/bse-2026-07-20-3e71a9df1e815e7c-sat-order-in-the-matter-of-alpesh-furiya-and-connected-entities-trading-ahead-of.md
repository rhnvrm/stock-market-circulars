## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members updating them on the SAT (Securities Appellate Tribunal) order in the matter of Alpesh Furiya and connected entities, who were found trading ahead of stock recommendations made by Mr. Pradeep Pandya, an anchor of various shows on CNBC Aawaz. This is a continuation of an earlier Exchange Notice (No. 20240611-76 dated June 11, 2024) on the same matter. SAT, in its order dated January 29, 2026, reduced the monetary penalty to ₹25 lakhs and held that the period of debarment already suffered by the parties was sufficient. Subsequently, SEBI via email dated July 20, 2026, directed the defreezing of PAN AIIPP0780C, belonging to Pradeep Baijnath Pandya, with immediate effect.

## Key Points

- Case relates to front-running/trading ahead of stock recommendations made by CNBC Aawaz anchor Mr. Pradeep Pandya by Alpesh Furiya and connected entities.
- SAT order dated January 29, 2026 reduced the monetary penalty to ₹25 lakhs.
- SAT held that the period of debarment already served by the involved parties is sufficient, effectively ending further debarment.
- SEBI's email dated July 20, 2026 directed immediate defreezing of PAN AIIPP0780C (Pradeep Baijnath Pandya).
- Notice is signed by officials from BSE's Investigation department (Deputy Vice President and Deputy Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an update on enforcement action outcome. The penalty originally imposed has been reduced by SAT on appeal, and the debarment period is deemed complete, resulting in the release of the PAN freeze that was previously in effect.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the SAT order and SEBI's directive, and ensure compliance by recognizing that PAN AIIPP0780C is no longer frozen and can resume normal trading/market activity, subject to the reduced penalty of ₹25 lakhs.

## Important Dates

- June 11, 2024: Original Exchange Notice No. 20240611-76 issued.
- January 29, 2026: SAT order reducing penalty to ₹25 lakhs and confirming sufficiency of debarment period served.
- July 20, 2026: SEBI email directing defreezing of PAN AIIPP0780C, and date of this BSE notice.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific individuals and entities involved in this enforcement case rather than the broader market. Trading members must ensure their systems and records reflect the defreezing of the specified PAN. This serves as a reminder of BSE's and SEBI's ongoing surveillance of front-running and unauthorized trading practices linked to media-driven stock recommendations.
