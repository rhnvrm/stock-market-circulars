## Summary

BSE announced the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544685) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 30, 2026. DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544684) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 29, 2026. Both stocks have been listed on BSE on January 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (544685) listed on BSE main board on January 28, 2026
- SHADOWFAX to be added to BSE IPO index at market open on January 30, 2026
- DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED (544684) listed on BSE SME platform on January 28, 2026
- DIGILOGIC to be added to BSE SME IPO index at market open on January 29, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20260127-50 (SHADOWFAX) and Notice No: 20260127-49 (DIGILOGIC)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational notice about routine index additions following new listings.

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Listing date for both SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED and DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED
- **January 29, 2026**: DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED added to BSE SME IPO index (at market open)
- **January 30, 2026**: SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED added to BSE IPO index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Routine administrative action for newly listed companies to be included in respective IPO indices. Index tracking funds and passive investment vehicles benchmarked to BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices may see minor portfolio adjustments. No impact on trading operations or market participants beyond standard index rebalancing procedures.
