## Summary

BSE has listed 2,39,76,000 new equity shares of Capricorn Systems Global Solutions Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512169) issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each on a rights basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,39,76,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis are now listed on BSE
- ISIN: INE968E01012
- Distribution numbers: 3996001 to 27972000
- Date of allotment: February 25, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are now available for trading. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures for newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 25, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** February 27, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of rights issue shares for Capricorn Systems Global Solutions Ltd. The addition of approximately 2.40 crore new equity shares increases the company's tradable float. Since the shares are issued at par and rank pari-passu with existing shares, there is no structural change to shareholder rights. Impact is limited to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue and trading members dealing in Scrip Code 512169.
