## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the listing of equity shares of Mehul Telecom Limited on the SME segment. The notice was issued by the DCS-Listing department on 23 April 2026 under Notice No. 20260423-10.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Mehul Telecom Limited are being listed on the BSE SME segment
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department
- Category: Company related
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes outlined in this notice. This is a standard listing announcement under BSE's SME platform framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company (Mehul Telecom Limited) must comply with BSE SME listing requirements and ongoing disclosure obligations
- Market participants and brokers should update their systems to reflect the new listing

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 23 April 2026
- Listing effective as per BSE SME segment commencement procedures

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Mehul Telecom Limited on the BSE SME segment provides the company access to public capital markets and offers investors an opportunity to participate in the telecom SME space. The impact is confined to investors specifically tracking BSE SME listings and those interested in the telecom sector. No broad market impact is anticipated.
