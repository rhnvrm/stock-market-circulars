## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Citurgia Biochemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 506373, ISIN: INE795B01031). The company is a manufacturer and dealer of citric acid, registered in Mumbai with its plant located in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand. The company profile indicates active operational status and listing only on BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

The revocation of trading suspension has high impact on the stock as it restores normal trading activity. Investors can now buy and sell shares of Citurgia Biochemicals Limited on BSE.
