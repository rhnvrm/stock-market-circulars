## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,78,20,426 new equity shares of Viram Suvarn Limited (Scrip Code: 540252) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,78,20,426 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 6/- (total issue price Rs. 8/- per share) on rights basis
- ISIN: INE607V01028
- Distribution Numbers: 7,56,40,853 to 11,34,61,278
- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- Effective Date of Trading: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing expands the tradeable float of Viram Suvarn Limited (Scrip Code: 540252) by 3,78,20,426 shares. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution of rights occurs for existing shareholders beyond what was already effected through the rights issue allotment.
