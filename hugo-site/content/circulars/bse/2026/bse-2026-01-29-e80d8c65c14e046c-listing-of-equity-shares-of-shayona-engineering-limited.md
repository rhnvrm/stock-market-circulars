## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Shayona Engineering Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544686, symbol SHAYONAENG, and ISIN INE0UCL01011. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shayona Engineering Limited
- Scrip Code: 544686
- Trading Symbol: SHAYONAENG
- ISIN: INE0UCL01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is announced.
