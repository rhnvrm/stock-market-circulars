## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis on its debt market segment, effective February 5, 2026. The circular provides details of six commercial paper issuances from Bajaj Finance Limited, Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Adani Enterprises Ltd., ECL Finance Limited, and Nuvama Wealth Management Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form only, with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Six new commercial paper instruments listed on BSE debt segment
- All securities trade in dematerialized form only under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise for all securities
- Interest rates range from 7.25% to 8.65% p.a.
- Tenures vary from 72 days to 364 days
- All commercial papers have CRISIL A1+ or equivalent ratings
- Trading effective immediately from February 5, 2026

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

- Trading members must note that securities trade only in dematerialized form
- Standard lot size: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size: 1 unit for all securities
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed commercial paper with no impact on equity markets or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects debt market trading members dealing with commercial paper instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in the debt market segment. These are privately placed instruments not available to retail investors through public subscription.

**Total Issue Size**: Approximately Rs. 1,912.5 crores across the five detailed issuances, representing short to medium-term funding for the respective issuers.
