## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 14 equity scrips effective June 03, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, impacting trading limits for these securities on the exchange.

## Key Points

- 14 scrips have their price bands revised effective June 03, 2026
- Price bands revised to levels of 2%, 5%, 10%, and 20% depending on the scrip
- Two scrips (Mihika Industries Ltd and Softtech Engineers Ltd) face the most restrictive 2% band
- Three scrips (Goldline Pharmaceutical Ltd and Integra Essentia Ltd) receive the widest 20% band
- Issued by BSE Surveillance department under Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Arpita Joshi

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure all orders placed in the affected scrips comply with the newly revised price bands from June 03, 2026 onwards. Members requiring clarification may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision tightens trading limits for Mihika Industries Ltd and Softtech Engineers Ltd (narrowed to 2%), which may signal heightened surveillance concern. Goldline Pharmaceutical Ltd and Integra Essentia Ltd receive relatively wider 20% bands, allowing greater price movement. Traders holding or transacting in any of these 14 scrips must account for the new circuit limits from the next trading session.
