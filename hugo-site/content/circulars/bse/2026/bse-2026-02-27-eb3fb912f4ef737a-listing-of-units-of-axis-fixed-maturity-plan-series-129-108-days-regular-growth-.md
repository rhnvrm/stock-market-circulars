## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that units of Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 129 (108 Days) Regular Growth, a close-ended debt scheme under Axis Mutual Fund, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Monday, March 2, 2026, under the 'F' Group.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 129 (108 Days) Regular Growth (Close-ended debt scheme)
- Risk Profile: Relatively Low Interest Rate Risk and Moderate Credit Risk
- Scrip Code: 544714 | Scrip ID on BOLT: AXISRGG129
- ISIN: INF846KA1432
- Total Units: 74,299,461
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit | Issue Price: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: F
- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- Listing Effective Date: March 2, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is in compliance with existing SEBI guidelines mandating that mutual fund units be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that all trades in this scrip will be executed in unit market lots (one unit per lot)
- All trades must be settled in dematerialized form only; physical trading is not permitted
- Members may contact BSE Listing Operations (Mr. Parag Jain, 022-2272 8685, mf.listing@bseindia.com) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- Listing and Trading Commencement: March 2, 2026 (Monday)
- Circular Date: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact. The scheme involves 74.3 million units at Rs. 10 face value (total corpus approximately Rs. 742.99 crore). As a close-ended debt FMP with low interest rate risk and moderate credit risk, it primarily affects investors who subscribed during the NFO period. Trading members should update their systems to recognize scrip code 544714 (AXISRGG129) from March 2, 2026. Registrar and Transfer Agent is KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai.
