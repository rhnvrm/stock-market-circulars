## Summary

BSE Ltd. has issued a circular announcing the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to BSE premises for March 2026. The circular includes a list of companies serviced by each RTA, identified by BSE scrip code. This facilitates investor grievance redressal and transfer-related services.

## Key Points

- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is among the RTAs scheduled to visit BSE Ltd. during March 2026.
- The RTA services a large number of listed companies across various sectors including finance, steel, textiles, chemicals, and real estate.
- Notable companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services include: Marathon Nextgen Realty Ltd (503101), Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036), Safari Industries India Ltd (523025), Arihant Superstructures Ltd (506194), LKP Finance Ltd (507912), Scan Steels Ltd (511672), and Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161), among others.
- The full list covers over 50 companies with scrip codes ranging across BSE-listed entities.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational communication regarding scheduled RTA visits.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors with grievances or transfer requests related to companies serviced by the listed RTAs may approach BSE during the scheduled visit period for March 2026.
- No specific compliance action required from listed companies or market participants.

## Important Dates

- Visit period: March 2026 (specific dates to be confirmed per individual RTA schedule)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It is an administrative notice intended to inform investors about the availability of RTA representatives at BSE premises for resolving transfer and investor service matters. No trading, pricing, or compliance implications for listed companies or investors.
