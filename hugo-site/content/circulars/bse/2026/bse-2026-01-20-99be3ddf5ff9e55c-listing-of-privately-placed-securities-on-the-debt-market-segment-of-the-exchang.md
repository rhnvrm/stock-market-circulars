## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper securities issued on private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment, effective January 20, 2026. The listed securities include commercial papers from 360 ONE Prime Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, Muthoot Fincorp Limited, Infina Finance Private Limited, and Adani Enterprises Ltd. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial paper securities on BSE debt segment
- All securities trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Total quantity listed: 34,000 commercial papers (excluding partial Adani data)
- Interest rates range from 6.95% to 8.83% per annum
- Redemption dates range from April 2026 to January 2027
- All issuers carry CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+ credit ratings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 19-20, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Bajaj Finance: April 20, 2026
  - 360 ONE Prime: January 18, 2027
  - Julius Baer Capital: January 19, 2027
  - Infina Finance: January 18, 2027
  - Muthoot Fincorp: January 18, 2027
