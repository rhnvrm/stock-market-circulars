## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Commercial Paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 729011, ISIN: INE04VS14244) bearing the particulars OFSL-12-3-26-CP will mature on March 11, 2026. Accordingly, trading in this instrument is suspended with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 729011
- Company: OXYZO Financial Services Limited
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (OFSL-12-3-26-CP)
- ISIN: INE04VS14244
- Maturity Date: March 11, 2026
- Trading suspended effective March 11, 2026
- Reference: DR-837/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to the maturity of a commercial paper instrument listed on BSE's Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid Commercial Paper of OXYZO Financial Services Limited with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Date of notice issued by BSE
- **March 11, 2026**: Maturity date; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific commercial paper instrument. The suspension is a standard procedural action triggered by the instrument's maturity date. No broader market disruption is anticipated. Trading members should ensure no transactions are executed in this scrip on or after March 11, 2026.
