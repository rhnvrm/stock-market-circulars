## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Friday, July 24, 2026, the equity shares of CALIBER MINING AND LOGISTICS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'B' Group of Securities, following completion of its public issue (IPO).

## Key Points

- Company: Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited, registered office at MIDC Chandrapur Industrial Area, Chandrapur, Maharashtra
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: CMLL
- Scrip Code: 544833
- ISIN: INE11XY01018
- Number of securities listed: 6,53,75,785 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (distinctive numbers 1 to 65375785)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Issue price: Rs. 424/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 414/-)
- Date of allotment in the public issue: July 22, 2026
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form as per SEBI's February 16, 2000 guidelines
- Registrar to the issue: KFin Technologies Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice following an IPO allotment, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading in CMLL shares only in dematerialised form
- The scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 24, 2026, per Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012
- Lock-in period for applicable shares has been marked in depository records via corporate action executed by the company

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 24, 2026 (Friday): Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE, including Special Pre-open Session

## Impact Assessment

This notice formalizes the listing of a new IPO scrip and is primarily relevant to shareholders who received allotment, prospective investors, and trading members handling the scrip's debut. Market-wide impact is limited, though the Special Pre-open Session mechanism may cause some initial price discovery volatility on the listing day.
