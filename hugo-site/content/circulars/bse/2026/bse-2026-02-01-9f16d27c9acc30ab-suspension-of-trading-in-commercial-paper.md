## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730495, ISIN: INE824H14SQ1) effective February 02, 2026. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its redemption date. Trading members are instructed not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited will mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 730495
- ISIN: INE824H14SQ1
- Trading suspension effective from February 02, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-811/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt Market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified commercial paper (ISIN: INE824H14SQ1) from February 02, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure no orders are placed or executed for Scrip Code 730495

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 01, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: February 02, 2026 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a single commercial paper instrument in the debt segment. The suspension is routine due to instrument maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard process for maturing debt instruments. Only holders of this specific commercial paper and trading members dealing in debt securities need to take note.

**Issuer**: Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited - Private entity, not a publicly traded stock, so no equity market impact.
