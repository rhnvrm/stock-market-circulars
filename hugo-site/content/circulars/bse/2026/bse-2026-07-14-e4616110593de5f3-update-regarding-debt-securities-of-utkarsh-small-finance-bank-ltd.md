## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Ltd bearing Scrip Code 959644 and ISIN INE735W08038 will be available with revised terms effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026. The revision follows debt restructuring and modifies the Call Option Date definition and the notice period required for exercising the Call Option.

## Key Points

- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20200702-24 dated July 2, 2020 regarding the same debt securities.
- Scrip Code 959644 (ISIN INE735W08038) will trade with revised terms from July 15, 2026.
- The Call Option Date definition is changed from a fixed date (60 months after Deemed Date of Allotment, i.e., June 26, 2025) to any date on or after that 60-month mark.
- The advance notice period required before exercising the Call Option is reduced from 30 days to 21 days, aligned with SEBI (Issue and Listing of Non-Convertible Securities) Regulations, 2021.
- No prepayment penalty or prepayment interest will be charged by the Bank on redemption under this clause.

## Regulatory Changes

Annexure I revises two clauses of the debenture terms:
1. "Call Option Date" is redefined from a specific fixed date to any date falling on or after 60 months from the Deemed Date of Allotment (June 26, 2025).
2. The Bank's Call Option redemption notice period to Debenture Trustee/Holders is shortened from 30 days to 21 days prior to the Call Option Date, in line with SEBI NCS Regulations 2021 timelines. The clarification on no prepayment penalty/interest is retained.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in this ISIN must update their systems/records to reflect the revised terms effective July 15, 2026. Queries can be directed to BSE's Debt Listing officials (Ms. Rutuja Mhasade / Hardik Bhuta, debt.listing@bseindia.com, +91 22 2272 8597).

## Important Dates

- Deemed Date of Allotment: June 26, 2025
- Effective date of revised terms: Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- Notice issued: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and trading members dealing in this specific NCD ISIN of Utkarsh Small Finance Bank. The change shortens the redemption notice window and provides flexibility on the exact call option exercise date, which could allow earlier or more flexible redemption by the Bank, a modest positive for debt investors seeking clarity but a minor operational update for market participants overall.
