## Summary

IRCON International Limited (Scrip Code: 541956/IRCON), a Navratna Government of India Undertaking, has issued a clarification to BSE and NSE in response to a news article published on NDTV Profit on March 6, 2026, titled "Ministry of Railways Proposes Merger of IRCON And RVNL as Shares Surge". The Company states that the news is not based on any information or details available with it and that it is not engaged in any such discussions or negotiations.

## Key Points

- The clarification is in response to a BSE/NSE query dated March 6, 2026 regarding a news item on NDTV Profit.
- The news article was captioned: "Ministry of Railways Proposes Merger of IRCON And RVNL as Shares Surge".
- IRCON International Limited is a Government Company under Section 2(45) of the Companies Act, 2013.
- The Company explicitly states it is not engaged in any negotiations or discussions related to the proposed merger.
- The Company reaffirmed its commitment to promptly informing stock exchanges of any material events.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This is a voluntary disclosure/clarification under the listing obligations framework (SEBI LODR), made in response to an exchange query regarding unusual price movement or market speculation.

## Important Dates

- March 6, 2026: Date of the NDTV Profit news article and BSE/NSE clarification request.
- March 6, 2026: Date of IRCON's clarification letter (Ref: IRCON/SECY/STEX/124).

## Impact Assessment

The clarification is likely to moderate speculative sentiment around a potential IRCON-RVNL merger. Investors who bought shares on the basis of the merger news may reassess their positions. No operational or structural impact on IRCON is expected. The stock may see short-term volatility as the market digests the denial.
