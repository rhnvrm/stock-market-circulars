## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of Lendingkart Finance Limited (ISIN: INE090W07675, Code: 975428) from February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing February 8, 2026 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the mentioned debentures during the no-dealings period.

## Key Points

- Company: Lendingkart Finance Limited
- Debenture Series: LFL-11.33%-23-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE090W07675
- BSE Code: 975428
- Record Date: February 8, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Lendingkart Finance Limited from February 6, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction
- The suspension applies specifically to debentures with ISIN INE090W07675 (BSE Code: 975428)

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings from this date)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of the mentioned debenture series of Lendingkart Finance Limited. The trading suspension is a standard procedure before record dates for corporate actions.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear in the records on February 8, 2026 will be eligible for redemption proceeds and interest payment. Investors cannot trade these debentures from February 6, 2026 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date to ensure compliance with BSE directives.
