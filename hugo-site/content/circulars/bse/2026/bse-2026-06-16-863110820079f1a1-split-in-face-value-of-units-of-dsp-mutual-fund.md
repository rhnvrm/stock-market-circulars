## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that DSP Mutual Fund has announced a face value split for units of two ETF schemes. The split reduces the face value from Rs. 10/- per unit to Re. 1/- per unit, with a record date of July 3, 2026. New ISIN numbers will be communicated separately.

## Key Points

- DSP Nifty Midcap 150 Quality 50 ETF (Scrip Code: 543438, ISIN: INF740KA1QL0) units will undergo a 10:1 face value split.
- DSP Nifty Healthcare ETF (Scrip Code: 544109, ISIN: INF740KA1UF4) units will undergo a 10:1 face value split.
- Face value reduced from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- per unit for both schemes.
- Both schemes share the same record date and ex-date of July 3, 2026.
- New ISIN numbers for the post-split units will be announced via a separate BSE notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a corporate action (face value split) under existing listing and mutual fund regulations. The split is processed under BSE notice reference DR-664/2026-2027.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the ex-date of July 3, 2026, for both affected ETF schemes.
- Unit holders should await a separate notice from BSE communicating the new ISIN numbers for the split units.
- Systems and records referencing the old ISINs (INF740KA1QL0 and INF740KA1UF4) should be updated once new ISINs are announced.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** July 3, 2026
- **Ex-Date / Effective Date for new face value trading:** July 3, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The face value split from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- will result in a 10x increase in the number of units held by each investor, with a proportional decrease in NAV per unit. This makes the ETF units more affordable and accessible to retail investors. Traders should update price references and lot-size calculations accordingly from July 3, 2026. New ISIN assignments will require downstream updates in trading systems, depository records, and fund platforms.
