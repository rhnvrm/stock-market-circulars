## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260612-16 informing members and investors that it had sought clarifications from MTAR Technologies Ltd and Nestle India Ltd regarding news articles published in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The notice confirms that clarifications have been received from both companies.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively monitors news articles across media channels to verify accuracy and protect investor interests.
- MTAR Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 543270) was asked to clarify a news item sourced from CNBC TV18.
- Nestle India Ltd (Scrip Code: 500790) was asked to clarify a news item sourced from Moneycontrol.
- Clarifications have been received from both companies and are available for member/investor reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an informational disclosure under BSE's standard surveillance practice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants. Companies have already responded to BSE's clarification requests.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine informational notice with minimal market impact. Investors in MTAR Technologies Ltd (BSE: 543270) and Nestle India Ltd (BSE: 500790) should review the respective company clarifications to assess accuracy of any news they may have encountered. No trading restrictions or price band changes are associated with this notice.
