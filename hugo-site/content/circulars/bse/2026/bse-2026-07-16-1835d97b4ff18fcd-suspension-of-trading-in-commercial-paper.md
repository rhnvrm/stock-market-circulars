## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their Commercial Paper (CP) instruments. Trading members are advised not to deal in the Commercial Paper of these companies from the specified ex-dates, as these instruments are approaching redemption.

## Key Points

- ICICI Securities Primary Dealership Limited (Scrip Code 731531, ISIN INE849D14HZ1, Scrip ID ISPDL-21-7-26-CP): Record/Ex-Date 20/07/2026, DR 675/2026-2027
- Adani Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code 730967, ISIN INE423A14YW3, Scrip ID AEL-23-7-26-CP): Record/Ex-Date 22/07/2026, DR 677/2026-2027
- Nexus Select Trust (Scrip Code 731559, ISIN INE0NDH14155, Scrip ID NST-27-7-26-CP): Record/Ex-Date 24/07/2026, DR 679/2026-2027
- TATA Steel Limited (Scrip Code 731598, ISIN INE081A14GX4, Scrip ID TSL-30-7-26-CP): Record/Ex-Date 29/07/2026, DR 682/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice under existing Listing Operations procedures for handling Commercial Paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in the listed Commercial Paper instruments of the above companies on and after their respective ex-dates, in line with standard practice ahead of redemption/maturity.

## Important Dates

- 20 July 2026: Record/Ex-Date for ICICI Securities Primary Dealership Limited CP
- 22 July 2026: Record/Ex-Date for Adani Enterprises Ltd. CP
- 24 July 2026: Record/Ex-Date for Nexus Select Trust CP
- 29 July 2026: Record/Ex-Date for Tata Steel Limited CP

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains only to specific short-term debt instruments (Commercial Papers) nearing redemption, not the equity shares of the underlying companies. Trading members dealing in these particular CP ISINs need to cease trading from the respective ex-dates, but there is no effect on equity trading, pricing, or broader market operations for ADANIENT, TATASTEEL, or other listed equities of these issuers.
