## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 58,00,000 equity shares of Choice International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531358) issued at Rs. 300 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 290) on preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. Trading commences on January 29, 2026. The shares are subject to lock-in periods with 40 lakh shares locked until August 1, 2026 and 18 lakh shares locked until August 2, 2027.

## Key Points

- 58,00,000 new equity shares listed and permitted for trading
- Scrip Code: 531358, ISIN: INE102B01014
- Issue price: Rs. 300 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 290 premium)
- Shares issued to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Date of allotment: November 28, 2025
- Distinguished numbers: 208988834 to 214788833
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed:
  - 40,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 208988834-212988833) locked until August 1, 2026
  - 18,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 212988834-214788833) locked until August 2, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 29, 2026
- **First Lock-in Expiry**: August 1, 2026 (40 lakh shares)
- **Second Lock-in Expiry**: August 2, 2027 (18 lakh shares)

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a capital raising exercise through preferential allotment for Choice International Ltd. The impact is company-specific with no broader market implications. The staggered lock-in periods indicate a mix of promoter and non-promoter participation in the preferential issue. The shares being fungible with existing equity may result in increased liquidity for the scrip post lock-in expiry.
