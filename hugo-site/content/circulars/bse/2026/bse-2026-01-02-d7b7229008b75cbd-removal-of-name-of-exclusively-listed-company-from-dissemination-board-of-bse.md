## Summary

BSE has announced the removal of Premium Linkers Limited from its Dissemination Board effective January 5, 2026. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Following this removal, the shares of Premium Linkers Limited will no longer be available on BSE's Dissemination Board.

## Key Points

- Premium Linkers Limited (CIN: U74899DL1982PLC014454) will be removed from BSE Dissemination Board
- Reason for removal: Exit option
- Company registered address: 102, Palco House, 2162/T-10, Main Patel Road, New Delhi â€“ 110008
- Shares will not be available on the Dissemination Board post-removal
- Action taken under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the provisions of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016 regarding removal of exclusively listed companies from the Dissemination Board when they opt for exit.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this change. No specific compliance action required from members, but they should note that Premium Linkers Limited shares will no longer be available on BSE's Dissemination Board after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Effective Date of Removal:** January 5, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to a single exclusively listed company. Trading members and investors should note that Premium Linkers Limited shares will not be accessible through BSE's Dissemination Board after January 5, 2026.
