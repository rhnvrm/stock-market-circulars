## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movements in seven listed companies and wrote to each seeking clarification. As of Notice No. 20260527-25 dated 27 May 2026, replies have been received from two companies and are attached to the notice. Replies remain awaited from five companies.

## Key Points

- BSE initiated clarification requests due to unusual price/volume activity in seven equity scrips.
- Replies received from Gyan Developers & Builders Ltd (Scrip Code: 530141) and Go Fashion (India) Ltd (Scrip Code: 543401); attachments available with the notice.
- Five companies have not yet submitted clarifications to the Exchange.
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President and Manager, Surveillance, BSE Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance action under BSE's price/volume monitoring framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Pending companies** — ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Cargotrans Maritime Ltd (543618), Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Ltd (526443), and Global Ocean Logistics India Ltd (544665) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE at the earliest.
- Investors and trading members in these scrips should review the attached replies from the two responding companies for the latest material information.

## Important Dates

- **27 May 2026** — Notice date; clarifications from Gyan Developers & Builders Ltd and Go Fashion (India) Ltd received and attached.
- No specific deadline mentioned for the five pending companies.

## Impact Assessment

The circular is stock-specific and limited in scope. Investors holding or trading in any of the seven flagged scrips should note the elevated surveillance attention. The receipt of clarifications from two companies may help explain recent price movements in those stocks. The five pending companies remain under scrutiny, and further notices or trading restrictions could follow if replies are not received promptly.
