## Summary

BSE has published the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to BSE premises for January 2026. This circular lists companies whose share transfer and investor servicing is handled by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. The RTA visit facility enables investors to meet RTA representatives in person at BSE to resolve queries and grievances related to share transfers, dematerialization, and other investor services.

## Key Points

- RTA visiting BSE in January 2026: Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd.
- Circular contains list of 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, textiles, and infrastructure
- Notable companies include Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036), LKP Finance Ltd (507912), Safari Industries India Ltd (523025), and Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161)
- Investors can meet RTA representatives at BSE premises during scheduled visit days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for investor convenience.

## Important Dates

- Visit Month: January 2026
- Specific visit dates not provided in the extracted content

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an operational circular designed to facilitate investor grievance redressal by enabling face-to-face meetings between investors and RTA representatives at BSE premises. Benefits retail investors holding shares in the listed companies by providing easier access to investor services. No impact on trading activities, pricing, or compliance obligations.
