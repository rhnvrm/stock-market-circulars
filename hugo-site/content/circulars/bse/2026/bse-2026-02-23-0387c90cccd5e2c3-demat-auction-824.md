## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 824, dated 20 February 2026. This auction covers 47 securities where sellers failed to deliver shares in the normal market settlement. The exchange will conduct a demat auction to source these shares and complete delivery obligations to buyers.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 824 dated 20/02/2026 covers 47 securities across various sectors
- Largest quantity lots: GOKUL REFOIL (2668 shares), MOIL LTD (1000 shares), VISTAR AMAR (1001 shares), GODFREY PHILLIPS (649 shares), QUICK HEAL TECHNOLOGIES (496 shares)
- Auction includes large-cap names such as HCL Technologies (140 shares), Bandhan Bank (100 shares), Bharat Electronics (25 shares), Eicher Motors (3 shares), and ITC (1 share)
- New-age listings also included: Eternal Limited/Zomato (99 shares), CarTrade Tech (5 shares), Meesho (4 shares)
- Swan Corporation has a significant 750-share lot; Hemant Surgical has 400 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular under existing BSE bye-laws governing demat auction procedures for settlement shortfalls.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members who have open delivery obligations for the listed securities in Settlement No. 824 must participate in or monitor the auction process
- Buyers with unresolved delivery claims will receive shares or close-out proceeds as per BSE auction settlement rules
- Members should ensure proper reconciliation of their positions for all 47 listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** 20 February 2026 (Settlement No. 824)
- **Circular Publication Date:** 23 February 2026
- Auction date and final settlement date to follow as per BSE's standard auction settlement schedule
