## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department sought clarification from IIFL Finance Limited (BSE: 532636) and Piramal Finance Limited (BSE: 544597) regarding a news article titled "Piramal Finance and IIFL in exploratory talks for an MFI deal: Sources" published on Moneycontrol.com on March 4, 2026. Both companies responded on the same date, denying any undisclosed material information that would account for share price movements.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance queried both IIFL Finance and Piramal Finance about a Moneycontrol article dated March 4, 2026, reporting alleged exploratory MFI deal talks between the two firms.
- IIFL Finance stated it evaluates various strategic opportunities periodically but confirmed no information requiring disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015 exists as of the date.
- IIFL Finance attributed its share price movement to market sentiment or factors beyond the company's control.
- IIFL Finance confirmed no new regulatory or legal proceedings have been initiated against the company.
- Piramal Finance stated the news article is "factually incorrect" and declined to comment on market speculation.
- Piramal Finance reaffirmed its commitment to comply with Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations and will disclose any material developments promptly.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. The clarifications are in response to BSE Surveillance queries under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 30 governing disclosure of material events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Both companies have fulfilled their obligation to respond to exchange surveillance queries.
- Both companies reaffirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.
- IIFL Finance committed to promptly notify stock exchanges of any future information requiring mandatory disclosure.

## Important Dates

- **March 4, 2026**: Moneycontrol article published; BSE surveillance queries issued; clarification letters submitted by both IIFL Finance and Piramal Finance.

## Impact Assessment

The clarifications indicate that the reported MFI deal talks are either non-existent (per Piramal Finance) or not yet at a stage requiring disclosure (per IIFL Finance). Shareholders and market participants should note that no confirmed transaction or negotiation has been acknowledged. Share price movements in these scrips tied to this news article may lack fundamental basis. Investors should await any formal Regulation 30 disclosures before drawing conclusions about potential deal activity.
