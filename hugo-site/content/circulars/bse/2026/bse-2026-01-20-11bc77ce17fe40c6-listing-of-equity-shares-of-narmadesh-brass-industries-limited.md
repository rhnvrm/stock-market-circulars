## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544680, symbol NARMADESH, and ISIN No. INE0S1B01014. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Narmadesh Brass Industries Limited
- Scrip Code: 544680
- Symbol: NARMADESH
- ISIN Number: INE0S1B01014
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME segment company. Impact is limited to trading members who need to be aware of the new security for future trading. The listing will add one more equity share to the SME segment. Actual market impact will depend on the listing date and security details to be announced separately.
