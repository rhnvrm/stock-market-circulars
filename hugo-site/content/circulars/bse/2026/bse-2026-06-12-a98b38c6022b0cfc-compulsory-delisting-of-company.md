## Summary

BSE has announced the compulsory delisting of Aroma Enterprises (India) Ltd (scrip code 531560) effective June 16, 2026. The company is being delisted as it has remained suspended for more than 6 months, pursuant to the order of the Delisting Committee of the Exchange under SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021.

## Key Points

- Aroma Enterprises (India) Ltd (scrip code 531560) will be compulsorily delisted from BSE effective June 16, 2026
- Delisting is triggered by the company remaining suspended for more than 6 months
- Action is taken under SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021
- The company will be moved to the Dissemination Board of the Exchange after delisting
- Promoters are required to provide an exit option to public shareholders at fair value determined by an independent valuer

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The action is taken in accordance with existing SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021, specifically Regulation 34(1) and Regulation 34(2).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Promoters**: Must purchase shares from public shareholders at fair value determined by an independent valuer appointed by the Exchange. A Public Notice will be issued shortly with details.
- **Promoters / Promoter Group**: Transfers (sale, pledge, etc.) of equity shares held by promoters/promoter group are frozen. Corporate benefits (dividend, rights, bonus, split) are also frozen until exit option is provided to public shareholders.
- **Company and Depositories**: Shall not effect any transfer of promoter/promoter group equity shares until exit compliance is certified by the Exchange.
- **Whole-time Directors and Compliance Officers**: Not eligible to become directors of any listed company until the exit option is provided.
- **Company, Directors, Promoters**: Barred from directly or indirectly accessing the securities market or seeking listing of any equity shares, or acting as intermediary for 10 years from the date of delisting per Regulation 34(1).

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of compulsory delisting; securities cease to be listed and available for trading
- **Post-delisting**: Public Notice to be issued shortly with fair value details for the promoter buyout

## Impact Assessment

Public shareholders of Aroma Enterprises (India) Ltd will lose the ability to trade shares on BSE from June 16, 2026. However, they are entitled to an exit opportunity at fair value as determined by an independent valuer. Until promoters provide this exit, their own shareholding is frozen and they face restrictions on corporate benefits. The 10-year securities market bar on the company, its promoters, and directors is a significant regulatory consequence. Queries can be directed to bse.delistscn@bseindia.com.
