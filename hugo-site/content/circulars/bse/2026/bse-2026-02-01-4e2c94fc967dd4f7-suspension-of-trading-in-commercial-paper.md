## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No 20260201-5 directing Trading Members to suspend trading in the Commercial Paper (CP) of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited with Scrip Code 730495 (ISIN: INE824H14SQ1, JBC-3-2-26-CP) effective February 02, 2026. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its redemption/maturity date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper: JBC-3-2-26-CP issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 730495
- ISIN: INE824H14SQ1
- Trading suspension effective from February 02, 2026
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Reference: DR-811/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (Scrip Code 730495) from February 02, 2026 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in this instrument after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 01, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: February 02, 2026 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational matter affecting a single commercial paper instrument. The suspension is procedural and relates to natural maturity of the debt instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only Trading Members dealing with this specific commercial paper need to ensure no further transactions are processed. Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the instrument's terms.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who will receive redemption on maturity. No trading opportunity exists post-suspension date.
