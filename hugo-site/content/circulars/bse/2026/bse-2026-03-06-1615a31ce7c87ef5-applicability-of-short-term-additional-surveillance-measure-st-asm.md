## Summary

BSE has issued its updated Short Term (ST) Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) list effective March 09, 2026. The circular details securities newly shortlisted under the ST 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework, securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages, and securities exiting the framework entirely. A total of 6 securities are newly added, 1 is moved to a higher stage, and 10 securities are removed from the ST-ASM framework.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly shortlisted in ST 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework effective March 09, 2026, including 4 SME scrips (marked @)
- 1 security (Aritas Vinyl Ltd) moved to a higher ASM stage within the framework
- No securities moved to a lower ASM stage (NIL)
- 10 securities removed from the ST-ASM Framework effective March 09, 2026
- 2 securities (KMF Builders & Developers Ltd, Panabyte Technologies Ltd) moving out due to inclusion in the Trade for Trade Framework
- SME scrips marked @ are subject to ST-ASM Framework applicability
- Consolidated list of securities currently under ST-ASM is provided in Annexure III

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM Framework is updated periodically by BSE to apply heightened surveillance on securities exhibiting unusual price movements. Securities placed under ST-ASM are subject to revised price bands (5%, 15%, or 30% depending on stage) and may be moved to trade-for-trade settlement. This circular reflects the latest reconstitution of the framework with additions, stage changes, and exits.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated ST-ASM list and apply applicable price bands and settlement conditions for affected securities from March 09, 2026
- Members dealing in securities newly added to ST-ASM must comply with applicable margin and settlement norms
- Securities exiting ST-ASM due to inclusion in Trade for Trade (KMF Builders & Developers Ltd, Panabyte Technologies Ltd) will be subject to Trade for Trade settlement rules instead

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 09, 2026 — all changes (additions, stage movements, removals) take effect
- **Circular Date:** March 06, 2026
