## Summary

BSE has informed market participants that, in continuation of Notice No. 20260710-36 dated July 10, 2026, the equity shares of Birla Cotsyn (India) Limited ("the Company" or "Corporate Debtor") will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026. The shares will be placed under the XT Group and will continue to trade in the Trade-to-Trade segment, in line with Exchange Notice No. 20230210-55 dated February 10, 2023. This listing follows a National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) order dated January 9, 2025, approving a revised capital structure for the Company under insolvency resolution.

## Key Points

- Total listed securities: 26,43,39,940 fully paid-up equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Comprises 1,43,39,940 new equity shares allotted post capital reduction and 25,00,00,000 new equity shares allotted on preferential basis
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 264339940
- Scrip Code: 533006; Scrip ID on BOLT: BIRLACOT
- Group: XT; Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Re. 1/- each fully paid up
- ISIN No.: IN8655I01023
- Lock-in: 23,79,05,946 shares (distinctive nos. 14339941–252245886) locked in until July 31, 2027
- Company will be part of the special pre-open session for IPO/other category scrips per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to the NCLT order dated January 9, 2025, the entire shareholding of the erstwhile Promoter & Promoter Group (48,60,83,760 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, aggregating Rs. 48,60,83,760/-) stands cancelled. Public shareholders continue to hold 1 equity share in the Company for every 153 existing equity shares previously held.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants must recognize the Company's shares under the new capital structure, distinctive number range, and XT Group classification from the effective listing date. Trading will remain restricted to the Trade-to-Trade segment as per existing exchange norms for this scrip.

## Important Dates

- NCLT Order: January 9, 2025
- Listing effective date: July 15, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Lock-in expiry for 23,79,05,946 shares: July 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The relisting formalizes the outcome of Birla Cotsyn's insolvency resolution process, wiping out prior promoter holdings and introducing new shareholders via capital reduction and preferential allotment. Trading restricted to Trade-to-Trade segment and continued XT Group classification signal ongoing surveillance due to the company's insolvency history, limiting speculative trading activity. Impact is confined to this single stock and its existing/new shareholders rather than the broader market.
