## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,08,604 new equity shares of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) are permitted for trading effective Friday, March 06, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 14,08,604 equity shares of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) admitted for trading from March 06, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up at Re. 1/- each on rights basis
- ISIN: INE483S01020
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Four tranches of distinctive numbers covering the total allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly converted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted (and implicitly required to note) that these new securities are available for trading from March 06, 2026 under ISIN INE483S01020.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 05, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Friday, March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing of 14,08,604 shares represents a routine conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid equity. The shares carry equal rights to existing shareholders (pari-passu). Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new distinctive number ranges under ISIN INE483S01020. Distinctive number ranges: 3001101919–3001112873 (10,955 shares), 3470116298–3471499496 (13,83,199 shares), 3476101502–3476101626 (125 shares), 3479604908–3479619232 (14,325 shares).
